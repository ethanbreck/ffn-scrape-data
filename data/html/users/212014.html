Greetings:

Hi and thanks for viewing my profile! The name's Marilyn and as you can see my pen-name is LiLAqUaMaRiE, but I also have other fics under aquaangel. So if you see either name used as the author, well that’s me.

I've been writing for a couple of years now on and off, and I know my writing skills aren't that great but I've come a long way these past years and I am proud of myself and of my work. However, I always try to improve my writing skills whenever possible, so if you like to leave me tips and/or constructive criticism in your reviews, feel free and it's appreciated; anything that helps my writing improve is okay in my book. As well, if you like my work, then thank you and hope it continues to please, but if not, then that's fine as well. I simply write for the fun of it.

I'm also trying to update my fics whenever I can, but for the most part real life gets in the way, that and/or a writer block. So sorry if some fics seem to be on a long hiatus. One day I'm going to complete all the works that are left hanging - at least that is what I hope to do.

Disclaimer: I do not own any fandom/character (or anything related to it) that I write about nor do I attempt to make any sort of profit from them. I just write for fun and for those plot bunnies to leave me alone. They’re evil I tell you, evil!
