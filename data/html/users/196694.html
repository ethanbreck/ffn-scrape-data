5th Update

I'm 21 now!

Due to that lovely institution called COLLEGE, during the fall, there will be VERY limited updates. I'm talking even less limited than normal. So, I haven't dropped off the face of the earth. I'm just studying for some b-s test or some nonsense.

If you put me on your favorites list, thanks and please e-mail me and I'll thank you in my stories. Look out for it. Also, to those who seem to read too much into things... Please don't flame me. If you don't like my story, keep it to your damn self.(you KNOW who YOU are)I don't need to hear it, nor do I wanna hear it. Although may think it's for my benefit, everyone else likes my stories. I'm not going to change my stories because two disturbed people think that my creative writing sucks. I do this for my enjoyment, as well as others. It's for fun, not a college paper that has to be perfect. I do take the critcism well, though. IF IT IS WRITTEN IN AN INTENTION AS THOUGH NOT TO FLAME ME! I didn't like the way those people came at me, so don't take it personal. "lighten up on the cursing" will serve me just fine. I try to do better and if it looks as though I don't, sorry fo' ya. But, I have five stories that I myself am absolutely proud of and I have another on fictionpress.net, so feel free to check that out. Now, on to the stories!

I have taken up writing Harry Potter stories also. I LOVE Harry Potter. He's great. I know, I sound like a big kid, but I know grown people who love Harry Potter. So all those over the age of 21 that read Harry Potter books faithfully, more power to ya!

I have made my Harry Potter story fit to my liking...Meaning that I changed the names into something I might like, 'kay? I quite enjoy the names I gave them and I, personally, think they're cute. This is FANFICTION, people! I'm not J. K. Rowling and I don't mean to be, but this IS my story. So, it is not a BLATANT error, as that person adequately put it and refuse to change it. Thank you and much love to those who don't mind it. Plus, since Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows came out on July 21st, I think it is safe to say that my Harry Potter stories are now ALTERNATE UNIVERSE fics...SWEET! By the way, it is an AWESOME book! I feel as though I'm losing a dear friend... I've been hooked on Harry since like I was 12 or 13 or something like that. I'll be 21 in December.

ATTENTION! ATTENTION!

Semper Atqui Nunquam, the Lord Voldemort/Giovanna Zaviera story, has just been released. So, please R & R. I'd like some feedback on it.

I've also been posting my HPstories on other sites. Some are under different pen names, so don't get the wrong impression that someone is plagiarising me. That's not the case. I know I'm on Schnoogle.com/Fictionalley.org, The Petulant Poetess, and Sink Into Your Eyes (On the Harry/Ginny Network). Semper Atqui Nunquam is posted on Schnoogle.com/Fictionalley.org only as well as this one.

I have also started Inuyasha stories...My first one is a little fic I like to call Concrete Rose. Don't worry, I have not abandoned my other stories...

Another added plus is my new Samurai Champloo story...Dancing Cherry Blossoms. Check that out too...

Plus, some people send me emails asking if I will continue from The End into their adult lives... Yes, I will and it will be sort of a long saga of stories, about four or five different ones that are set over a long period of time...the equivalent of a very serious war. We can just say that a certain someone will gather followers and start a war of his/her own with a very unexpected slew of evil-doers known as the... Wait and see. So, there will be a whole lot of original characters introduced (children and enemies), but they will not overshadow the others, mainly Harry. He will play a MAJOR part in the stories.

I have named the first in the saga...Life, Love, and Family. I hope ya'll like it!

My two babies, The Opposite and The End were added to C2s! I'm so happy. You like me! You REALLY like me! LOL

Read on, read on!
