Previous user names:

I seem to have this habit of changing my pseudonyms every two years or so.

Ophelia Winters: Ophelie Winter is the name of a French singer. It's also a Shakespearean name so I latched onto it.

Blushing Sigh: I used to be a militant shipper and I was a heck of het enthusiast. And the penname was romantic, since it's a reference to infatuation.

Atia of the Julii: I loved Atia in 'Rome' and Atia is the name of one of my cousins :).

Favourite movies: 'Mr. and Mrs. Iyer' and 'Jetlag'. Even if you're not into foreign films, you should go ahead and give these two a try :). You might like them.

List of ships: You can find my list of anti-shippings and shippings in my LJ userinfo page. It's in my homepage.

Favourite bands: Black, Coldplay, Creed, Depeche Mode, Deep Forest, Good Charlotte, Gorillaz, Heather Nova, Indochine, Interpol, Iron Maiden, Lifehouse, Linkin Park, Mandy Moore, Michelle Branch (Spirit Room era), Mylene Farmer, Nine Inch Nails, Oasis, Savage Garden, The Calling, The Corrs, Strings.

Music: If it's accessible, some form of rock or pop, electronica, ethnic fusion, trip hop, tasteful and Asian, then I probably listen to it.

Favourite books: Most South-East Asian literature, L. M. Montgomery’s 'Anne' series, 'The Catcher in the Rye', 'Rising Sun', The Discworld series, 'Coraline', 'Anansi Boys'. the early 'Harry Potter' books.

Favourite TV shows: 'CSI', 'Veronica Mars', 'House, M.D.', 'Everybody hates Chris', 'Rome'.
