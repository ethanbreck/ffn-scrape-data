08--04-12

So. My sister passed away after a very long battle. And my muses have been silent for a long time. I'm doing my best to revive them, but I can't make any promises. There is an additional difficulty in that a week after her passing, we had a huge black out in my state. I lost power for 4 days. When it came back on, my computer was fried. I lost everything. So now I'm starting all my WIPs again from scratch.

In summery, no the stories aren't dead. They're just stalled and I can't promise when they'll get completed or updated next. I do apologize, but things happen. Hopefully, I'll get the swing back and put something out soon.

Thanks to all my readers. I adore you guys.

-Eve
