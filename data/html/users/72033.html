I'm still working on that new InuYasha fanfiction as well as a few other new ones like this one.

Kagome receives a letter from her father-- an explorer that many have given up for dead. As she and her friend Sango prepare to take their younger brothers to meet their long gone fathers, disaster strikes. To save themselves, they must hide in an old ship in the harbor. Too bad for them that it leaves port before they can state their case to the captain. Kagome's only chance is to strike a deal with the captain. (The rest is omitted, for now, because it gets complicated. Plus, I can't write summaries to save my life.)

Blame "Pirates of the Caribbean." I got this latest idea for an IY fanfic involving pirates... but when I realized that there were at least two (probably more on other sites as well as that one) IY/PoftheC fusions on mm.org... I kind of lost the spark. It had nothing to do with the fact that my story would have almost nothing to do with that movie. It would just be an IY pirate story. However, historical fiction is NOT my forte. I thought I would give up on this story. But then I recently aquired "Skies of Arcadia: Legends" to play on my Christmas Nintendo Gamecube. So THEN I got to thinking... who needs ocean to be a pirate? I'm not planning on doing a total IY/SofA fusion either. (I'd probably have to describe moonstones and the basic plot of the game) But it sure sounded interesting...

So it's come down to these options for the InuYasha pirate fanfiction:
1) historical
2) post-apocalyptic Earth
3) fusion with "Skies..." universe; set before or after the game

NOW can you see why I can't get started?? I can't even decide what time/world to set the story in...

Pathetic.
