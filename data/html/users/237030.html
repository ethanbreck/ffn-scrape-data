Hey, what's happining? (is it just me or was that as lame as it sounded...) Anyway, about me? I'm a dumb kid who thinks she can write. I wrote a little back in 2001 with the penname "Kari Kamiya aka Hikari Yagami" and the userid of something like 52523(?). Trust me, my stories were like less than 900 words most of the time and involved davis bashing. I live on the east coast of the US. Age? Doesn't matter. I am a teenager though.

I occasionally watch Anime. Well only if its on networks in the US. I can't get a hold of any Japanese tapes and even if I could I would just sit there, dumfounded, and not have a clue of what anybody was saying. Anyway, like I said, I occasionally watch Anime. Digimon and Sailor Moon. Sad, isn't it? And I never get to see Sailor Moon because its on Cartoon Network, and I dont have cable so I dont get the channel. Digimon, I loved back in 1999, 2000, and 2001. I got seriously confused when season 3 came on. And I season 4 comes on at freakin 7:30 in the morning, and on UPN of all channels. Come on now. Card Captors I think got cancelled. I'm not 100% sure about that, but it doesn't come on WB anymore. Hamtaro, again its on cartoon network, but I saw it once and its soo cute! Yeah ok, getting to a point, I only remember the first and second seasons of Digimon, so don't expect me to write season 3 or 4 stories.

Mind my writings, I'm not fully exposed to the Japanese culture. In fact, I'm still getting exposed to the American culture. I can speak 3 words of Japanese, and I know they put their last names first. The only way that I get Japanese names is by looking through my "Bust A Groove" video game booklet, and I really cant tell which names are boys and which are girls. I could use plenty of help understanding the culture. You could just send me a big, fat email explaining everything you know about Japan (not including like "Japan is on the northeast hemisphere!").

Slap me if I don't update. I'm really busy because I just got my Learner's Permit to Drive, plus I have school, homework, dance, and cheerleading. And the VERY occasional party.

I support Taiora, Mimato, and especially Takari. Yes, I refuse to believe that season 2 episode where Sorato hints are dropped. I do believe in "His Master's Voice", that was my absolute favorite. The first time it came on, I recorded it and watched it 6 times straight! It would've been 7+ if my mom didn't tell me to stop watching it. There might be hints of other couples in my stories, but they will be just hints.

Ok, now on to the writing.

Why Do I Feel So Sad - Planning a sequal since a reader requested one. Like what, 2 people read the story? whoop de frikken do.

Just Listen To Me - Ehh, needs major work. I just added an alternate ending, and if that doesn't go well, then I'll have to take it down and rewrite the whole thing, cause I have this nagging feeling that I can make that story much better.

Misery - Going through MAJOR revisements, the problem is everytime I try to replace the text of a chapter, FF.Net doesn;'t do anything. I'm looking into that...

The Other Prince Charming - That needs to be finish. I'm happy with all of the chapters, they don't need revising. Just editing.
