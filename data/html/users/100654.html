Author has written 4 stories for Star Ocean, and Warcraft.

A drifter and a dreamer, plus a twenty-something years old university student with interest in role-playing and creative self expression. I read fan fiction in rather infrequent bouts but enjoy delving into it every once, and I have even sometimes found myself reading fiction about storyverses I barely know in a spur of a moment.
As a writer, I enjoy lengthy descriptions, dramatic events and following one or few characters inside their minds, so my texts are very likely to contain each of the mentioned at least to some degree. Most of my own stories on this site are ridiculously old, however, so I apologise in advance for the poor language and amounts of teenager's fangirlism in them. I'm not a native speaker of English, but I hope that doesn't show too much at least in my newer texts.
