Author has written 11 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Ninja Turtles.

Wow. So a big white space can be really daunting! I havn't written a bio because I honestly didn't know what I would fill the space with. For some reason when I get an idea to write a story it comes really easily, but then you see this and...blank.

But anyhoo. I suppose I can fill it with a little bit about myself. I'm a girl in her mid twenties who is tredding her way through graduate school. One year left, whoo hoo! I am a huge animal lover and show it off with a house full of animals. Two dogs, two cats, and two turtles (one named Leo, the other is Mikey ;) ) and a large tortoise. When I'm not in school I spend my limited free time with my animals, watching movies, watching my favorite football teams, running, and much much more! Wow, I feel like an infomercial.

The stories that I've written are almost all complete and pretty much explain themselves as you read them. I'm hoping to write more as inspiration hits me. Most of my future writings will probably continue to be TMNT related, since I have taken a strong interest in it ever since the movie reconnected me with my childhood...delightful.

Well, that's all I got. Enjoy my stories!
