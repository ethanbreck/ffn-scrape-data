So, hi
My name is Fay and yes I know really old fashioned name but I love it since it means fairy in French, my parents actually gave me a special name when I was born like Fay because I was the second daughter. Some quick facts about me is that I was born on the 22nd of September 1989 which makes me 22 years old right now. I am also from England and grew up there for 12 years in Knebworth which is in Hertfordshire right now though, and for the previous ten years, I've been living in the San Francisco Bay Area in California. I guess when I finally get citizenship I'll have to say I'm half American XP

In my opinion I've had quite a challenging life and although many of my stories are depressing or have depressing themes it's my life which has really helped me write them and add the depth which I hopefully provide. Many of my fics are discontinued and probably will never ever ever be continued.

So about my Fanfiction.net life:
I've been writing fanfiction since I was nine I think, although these were about random series' the majority of which I can't remember. The first series' I do remember writing about though were FRIENDS, Pokemon, Futurama and Digimon, this was all in 1998 and I thought these were the most original things in the world, guess what…they weren't, not even close! In 2000 I started to write Harry Potter fanfics and I continued this until 2004, Harry Potter and a girl named Luna were the reasons for me joining fanfiction.net. After I got over my Harry Potter phase, when Sirius Black died, I started writing Fruits Basket fanfiction as well as some other fanfics, I've written for things from Moulin Rouge to Mink, from Love Hina and Sailor Moon to Fight Club but I've mainly stuck with my favorite series and until 2008 that was Fruits Basket. During my time writing Fruits Basket fanfiction I was also writing with many different people and grew to love roleplaying, it became a major part of my life. In 2007 however, one of my online friends blocked me, on my birthday none the less, and I grew scared of RPng.

I started to write on my own mainly finishing Fruits Basket stories and then I grew to love Maki Minami's work Special A, I started many different fanfics that was until the series ended in 2009 and I fell in love with Skip Beat. It was also in 2009 that I started roleplaying again with another great writer, unfortunately, heartbreaking to me who has had roleplaying and writing joint stories as a major part of their life since they were 14 I have had confirmed to me that this will come to an end quite soon. Two years and 12 great RPs, I know heartbreaking right. Right now many of my stories are on hiatus if not discontinued but when I lose writing joint stories from my life I will probably pick one or two or even more of them back up.
--

So I guess that's enough about me, if you have any questions feel free to ask.
