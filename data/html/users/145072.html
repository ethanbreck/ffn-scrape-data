"Tale of a Dreamer"

They said i was a prophet
i heard voices none other heard
and saw things none other glimpsed.
Men flocked to me when i walked by
holding communion with myself.
"You are a prophetess!" they cried. "Prophesy to us!
You are a prophet!"
weary, weeping
world-worn, i went on my way
wary into the wild world-night.

They said i was mad
i heard voices none other heard
and saw things none other glimpsed.
Men drew away when i walked by
holding communion with myself.
"she is a madwoman!" they cried. "drive her away!
she is mad!"
weary, weeping
world-worn, i went on my way
wary into the wild world-night.

Sitll i, a mad and dreaming prophet
hear things that other heard
and saw things none other glimpsed.
Men pay me no mind when i walk by
holding communion with myself.
Nothing they say of me, nothing they ask
and i am left alone, a mad and dreaming prophetess.
still
weary, weeping
world-worn, i went on my way
wary into the wild world-night.
