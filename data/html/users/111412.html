Things I Like in Fic: Angst, slash, good dialogue, good characterization, original storylines, hurt/comfort, PWP, well-done AUs, believability, character introspection, Dark!Harry, Dark!Xander, Buffy bashing, love/hate relationships, RPS, crossovers that work, epics

I am no longer in the Rent fandom, so any unfinished Rentfics you find here will remain that way. I still read BtVS and Harry Potter fic, but I haven't written any in awhile. My current obsession is Firefly. All the fic found here, plus a couple WIPs and some original stuff, can be found on my website. I also have a livejournal for fic (link above).
