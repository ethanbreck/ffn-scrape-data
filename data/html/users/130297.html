My name is Florencia, but you can call me Flower . I'm a 16 year-old girl from Argentina.

I came across fanfiction.net while looking for Digimon fanfics in my crazy obesession with the show some years ago. Actually, it's the only anime show I have ever seen - only seasons 1 & 2 - and I absolutely loved it. I can say I have some favourite characters - Sora, Matt, TK, Ken - but I like them all, with the possible exclusion of Cody - but I don't know why however. I'm obsessed with Sorato, they look extremely cute together! But that's not the only reason I like this couple, I believe -as many others out there - that Friendship and Love go together and, of course, they had a strong, trusting relationship as friends which was one of the things that made them be the adorable and perfect couple they are.

I haven't written any fanfic yet as I found it really hard to get some creative and original ideas. I did have one little idea some months ago, but found it really hard to write since I'm still learning English at my school - my first language is Spanish.

However I love to read here on FF. Of course I just read Digimon fics, as I clearly stated before it's the only show I've seen. Mainly, I read fanfics which have Sorato as the central couple but I don't have any preferences for the other couples - could be Mimi-Tai, Mimi-Joe, Mimi-Izzy, TK-Kari, Davis-Kari and almost every other couple.

I also love to read books, my favorite ones are The Lord of the Rings and Harry Potter, and I recently read The Da Vinci Code, it was amazing. In my free time I like to draw, and lately I've been writing poems - you can read them at http://www.fictionpress.com/~royalflower ,would love it if you guys checked them out!

For all those Neopets-lovers out there, you can find me as flower_x2x - I love to make new neofriends ;) just send a neomail!

This is pretty long, so I won't bore you any longer. See you around! Bye =)
