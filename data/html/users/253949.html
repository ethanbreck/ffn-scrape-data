My name is Michelle (aka Michey :D), and I currently write Chandler and Monica fanfiction. Though the purpose of "Friends" is to make people laugh, my fics will rarely do that. Sorry, guys - if you want to laugh, watch your DVDs :p

List of Michelle's beliefs:

1. "Friends" is the perfect show to make you laugh
2. "Alias" is the perfect show to make you cry
3. Chandler/Monica and Sydney/Vaughn are the epitome of soul mates
4. The readers put life into a story - the writer merely puts the words onto paper
5.Broadway was created solely to entertain me for decades
6. The Phantom of the Opera well deserves its praise, and should live on eternally
7. Never in life will I need to know the formula for slope intercept forms

Reviews always mean the world to a writer, and especially to me. I especially love receiving reviews from my past stories - you guys may think such reviews are pointless to me, but they make me feel like my stories haven't disappeared once I've finished. I love all of your praise and criticism. The readers are what make me strive for better writing, and I thank all of you :

R.I.P., our beloved Thursdays.
