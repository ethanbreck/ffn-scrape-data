My stories.. how you say.. suck. v.v May Death Do Us Part is by far the most humiliating fanic I have ever left available to the public. I wanted so badly to delete it... To delete both of those stories... But, sometimes, despite how horrible I know my writing was.. -and yes, I'm sure I've improved since then-.. I still get compliments every so often. There's probably a 3% chance I would ever continue MDDUP, and that's only because I'm so frigging ashamed of it... As that shadowgir person said, and I agree, I never really went anywhere with it... And I'm sorry. I'm sorry that I made people want to read more, which to me seems insane, but.. I'm sorry. I just don't think I can. I think low of Azka, and how much I seriously screwed up her character, to the point where I would do almost anything to lock her away from memory and throw away the key.

I don't want to remember her. I just.. don't. Most of you understand. I just don't know whether or not I should delete my fics and leave this place permanently... I am such a fool... And I fear that my friends will eventually find my horrible fanfics.. That would only lead to more embarrassment. So, nobody who reads this, I don't know what to do. If anyone could give me advice here, anyone, it would be most appreciated in every way. I've overstayed my visit, and maybe it's just time for me to move on.

And I will always be sorry for what I said, DeadLegato. I am ashamed. SO ashamed. And I don't believe you would ever forgive me. Igg... I'll just shut up now.

I will always remember all of your nice reviews. I will; I promise. Even if I do delete the fics, which is most likely. You guys mean a lot to me, and gave me the strength to continue. Thank you. But this is the end.

...I'll be back, I swear. You just won't know what name I'm under.

Thanks for your time and support... And thanks for showing me how much improvement I need. I owe you all much. Have fun.. on ff.net. I need a break from this place. To think. Goodbye... everyone.

--RebelliousBluePudding.
For one last time.
