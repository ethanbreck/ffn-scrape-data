Who am I?

Good, question, complicated answer, but I like to compare myself to the seven deadly sins in following order:

• acedia - sloth (Yes, laziest being ever... just believe me. xD)
• luxuria - lust (I'm not only perverted myself and proud of it, I also spread it like a disease... no matter how innocent the people, I taint them. xD)
• avaritia - greed (especially regarding money... one can never have enough of that. 8D)
• gula - gluttony (let's just say, I love food... very much so! :3)
• superbia - pride (never ever injure my pride... it might be the last thing you'll do. ôo)
• ira - rage (I'm rarely getting pissed, but if so, then beware... it's explosive...)
• invidia - envy: (none existant, I rather replace that sin with addiction (nicotine, coffee, anime/manga, videogames, food... *insert endless list in here*))

Summing up, we might say, I'm never gonna end up in heaven, rather hell... well, if the devil isn't that picky... I'm being serious here. ôo
