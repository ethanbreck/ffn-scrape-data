Hello everyone! I'm a Florida teenager, and I've been writing for at least six or seven years...however everything really took off when I got into fanfiction! My first fandom was Star Wars, but since then I've read and dabbled in Harry Potter, The Mummy, and various others. In Star Wars fics, I'm a big fan of Han/Leia, Luke/Mara and Jaina/Jag- I primarily stick to the original trilogy as I don't have too much interest in the PT, though I have read the NJO books. In Harry Potter, I'm a strong proponent of James/Lily, Ron/Hermione and Harry/Ginny- however I'm pretty open to anything as long as its well written and romantic!

Likes: Nailpolish, shopping, theatre, SUMMER BREAK, the color pink, all things French, the beach, and good friends!

Dislikes: People who call too often, irresponsibility, styrofoam cups, the outdoors, and black pens :-)

Don't forget to REVIEW any of the stories you read here- I love feedback, good or bad...although I have to admit I prefer good ;-) And if you're THAT interested in my work, you can head on over to fictionpress.net and type 'Miska' into the author search engine for an original, non-fanfic-related story :-D I'd be flattered ;-)

On a final note, I *strongly* recommend all the authors/stories in my favorites! I'm a pretty discriminating reader, and all of these are the tops!
