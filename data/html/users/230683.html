BIO OF A LANCER WHO LIKES TO STICK SPEARS IN HIS PC BECAUSE HE WANTS A MAC.

A warning of sorts: Some of my stuff are...um, suicidal. And, uh, disturbing. (At least, to me.) So, stay away from my poems if you don't like stuff like that. And remember, flames will be treated as flames. Unless you tell me what I did wrong, you WILL get a piece of my mind. I'm sure as hell I can be a WHOLE lot meaner than you are.

In an attempt to take myself lightly, the random fiction "That's Not Fair!" has been turned into an insanity series! It's pretty much random stuff I write when I'm bored, downloading big, BIG files over my stupidly slow dial-up connection and when I don't have anything else to do. So, enjoy! Oh, and looyah means insane kids with weird almost-magical powers that lets them escape from the long, hideous arm of the Law(And I mean, EVERY Law). That's it. Oh, and I made that word up. Haha. You're not gonna find it anywhere but here!

As you can see, I've changed my e-mail address. BUT, I still have the old one. So, e-mail me to whichever address.

Ahem, here begins my bio section. Be ready for one helluva crap-filled, humorless(though I intend to make it as funny as possible.Or at the very least, interesting.) bio section you'll ever read. Don't ever say that you haven't been warned!

Date of Birth: January 14th,1988. (Soon to be 15! Woo-hoo!)
Gender: Male.
On the most-annoying-friend-ever scale?: Yep, at
the very top.
Most-likely-to-be-hated-for-bad-poetry?: Hell, yeah.

Enough of that, here's my fav stuff.

Fav games: The Final Fantasy series, Megaman Zero, Sonic the Hedgehog games,Resident Evil:Code Veronica(I love the Magnum! Bwahahaha!),Chrono series,Super Metroid,Metal Gear Solid and VR Missions. Well, I have more but I couldn't think of any right now.

Fav anime:Boys Be,Vandread. That's it. I don't watch a lot of animes.

Fav music: Well, I listen to a lot of stuff. Mostly stuff that are easy to listen such as Jazz, melancholy Brit Pop(Coldplay!Coldplay!) and some weird funk-heavy metal bands. Namely Incubus. Oh, and I like songs from games. Mostly Final Fantasy stuff. But I do like the songs from Sonic Adventure 1 and 2.(Yes, I own a Dreamcast)

Fav characters:Claire Redfield from RE:CV, Selphie from FFVIII, Cloud from FFVII, Garnet til Alexandros(a.k.a Sarah) from FFIX, Will Parry and Kirjava from His Dark Materials, Legolas from LOTR, Hermione Granger(I'm not sure if this is how you spell her name...) from Harry Potter, Xianghua, Mitsurugi and Taki from Soul Calibur. There's more but, I can't think of them right now. Later!

Notice that my poem "You" is short. It's really just an experimentation. I read this poem by AuroraDannon titled "Life" and it was so good! So, uh, I tried to do the same thing. It's funny to think that people actually liked it. But, I think it's a good thing, right? Well, whee~! to that!

NEW PROJECT!!! Okay, so it isn't really a project. Actually, I'm trying to do this a-poem-a-week thing...so, I am trying my best to do it. Right now, I KNOW that I have poems to last me another 3 weeks...but I'm still gonna write more anyway. Wish me luck!!

Why is it that NO ONE noticed that my haiku Alaska has two chapters? Please, for my sake, read the second chapter!

Oh, AND I HAVEN'T FORGOTTEN ABOUT MY FIC. IT'S JUST THAT I'M HAVING A HUGE WRITER'S BLOCK RIGHT NOW! HELP ME...
