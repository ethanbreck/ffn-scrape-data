Hmmm, let's see... 10 facts about me:

1) I love watermelon. Seriously, I can make myself sick and still want more.

2) I will listen to the same song repeatedly until I make myself sick.

3) I'm obsessed with Harry Potter fan fiction. I would read more of other genre's, but I can never find the pairings I like. If you know of a great Toph/Zuko or Lassie/Jules pairing, let me know.

4) My favorite characters to read: Snape, Luna, and Percy. Unfortunately, most of the stories for the latter two suck. Luckily for me, there are quite a few quality Snape-fics though.

5) Not much of a fan of slash. However, Irresistible Poison is one of the best romances I've read, regardless of the fact that it falls into this category.

6) I refuse to read anything Harry/Hermione. Its just wrong.

7) I don't really like Ginny. Not sure why, but I just find 'firey redheads' annoying.

8) I work way too much.

9) I encourage my husband to watch sports so he won't care if I take really long naps and then stay up all night to write.

10) I think the Harry Potter Lexicon is awesome.
