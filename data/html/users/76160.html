... It hasn't been five years since I last updated this profile.. really.. I swear! .

So.. yeah. I haven't written in over seven years. All my posted stories are from way back in the day when I was a HUGE Ronin Warriors fangirl. These days I find myself content simply to lurk and read the fabulous writings of various authors on this site. Not to mention my tastes have changed drastically. Anymore I am content to wile away the hours reading stories for Labyrinth, Thor, Ouran, Star Trek (the new movie), and Aliens.

If you're looking for news on updates or anything of the sort, I'm sorry to disappoint. What is posted is posted. It's not that I don't have ideas for stories, Lord knows I have a ton, I'm just sorely out of practice and have little time around being a productive member of society to actually type anything other then a drabble .;

Thank you for stopping by, nonetheless. Please check out my favorite stories for links to some really good ones!
