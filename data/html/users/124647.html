Author has written 6 stories for Akazukin Chacha, Neverwinter Nights, and Final Fantasy VII.

10 April 2017

The last time I touched this page would be about 7 years ago, back when I was floating, lost and disoriented in this ocean called life. How nostalgic to see my earlier writings, the response that it had generated (still generates, though now its more of a trickle).

I realize that I owe something to that younger version of myself who wrote in earnest; constructed worlds that she could never inhabit but could imagine in her mind as naturally as dreams come in sleep. I realize that I can write, still. And because I had taken a break for so long, that there is much much more to draw from now. I've lived, and now, it's time for me to write.

After years of silence, I've come back to finish the stories I began. (Just as well, because they are also remaking Final Fantasy VII now).

Thank you for your patience, and let the good times roll once again.
