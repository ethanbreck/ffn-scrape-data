(1/7/03)As it is a new year I have decided to update this yet again! Let's see, I live in IL and am 16 (go me!)! My hobbies are reading (regular books AND FF,GT), playing flute, piccolo, violin, and piano, more reading, trying to figure out my latin homework(Mea vita est legere libros. My life is to read books.), talking on im, and doing stuff with my friends and whatnot.

I am a total bookworm. I LOVE the Harry Potter books. (I don't understand why some of my friends don't want to read them. They must be crazy to NOT want to read them. No matter how many times I say "READ THE HARRY POTTER BOOKS!!!" some still don't listen. *sigh*

My best friendin FL, aka greeneyes, introduced me to fanfiction and I have been hooked since.(Ok it might be more of an obsession now.)She has introduced me to some of my fav. stories (the H/G, R/H ones...she doesn't read as much L/J as I do) You might have figured from my last statement that my ship is Harry/Ginny and Ron/Hermione. I think these make total sense so don't even try to tell me otherwise.

greeneyes and I have a pen name called Soar and Stealth, on which we have a short R/H songfic, go review it! Our story Strange Coincidences WILL, I repeat, WILL be updated...Ch2 is in the works now. It Siriusly is! But first we are going to repost Ch1, after tweaking a few more little things, nothing too major. But we DID spend lots of time talking about the story on New Years Eve. So hopefully it should get going again soon.

As for my stories,

Glimpses of Truth- My second story, I've had the idea for quite awhile, but I never acted on it, until recently. I know this is going to be really good, because I have great plans for it. For anyone who hasn't read it yet: Lily and James start their seventh year at Hogwarts, on their way they meet up with trouble. When they finally reach school, Dumbledore drops some big news on them...but doesn't explain that much, except that they are destenied to be great together. To add more confusion to the mess, Lily's best friend is able to see things in the future, will she tell Lily what is in store for her, or tell no one. Old and new friends, enemies, and those who we don't know yet, will be poppong up all over the place.

I have also been trying to make a Harry Potter section on my website, but with school and stuff it has been impossible to work on...I'll update when I have more.

One last note...Go read 'The Order of the Phoenix'and it's sequel 'The Well of Shadows' by ruskbyte. It is so totally good. He deserves to be a King of H/G on GT...I'm Sirius!, well not really, but this is a totally brilliant story!
