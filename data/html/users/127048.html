UPDATE 6-29-2008: I am very sorry that I haven't written in such a long time. My life has changed drastically and so have many of my activities. I was writing and finishing part 4 and 5 to The Ashes of Wonderland on the school computer and saving it to my personal account there. At one point, the school re-did their system and I lost all my work. I was so frustrated that I hadn't bothered to write since. I know that I should have made back ups in case, but I didn't think of that. It is my fault that my work was lost... but I'm hoping that I'll be able to sit down and write again.

If anyone still actually cares, that is.

I am a female writer who enjoys anime, Harry Potter, and Doctor Who. I'm a geek. That's about all you really need to know about me... xD

When I begin writing again, I shall begin writing again on The Ashes of Wonderland as well as beginning a Doctor Who fanfiction, but only after this season is over... which is another seven days. It shall be about the adventures of the Doctor and a new companion, named Amelia.

Wish me luck!
