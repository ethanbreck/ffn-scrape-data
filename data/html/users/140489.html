Ok so considering you've reached this page I'm presuming that you're either lost, in search of some fics to read, or hopping to find out more about the person behind the identity willowwood. I'm hoping it's the second option and maybe even a little bit of the third, either way I should probably tell you something about myself considering you're here right? Ok so what do you wanna know?

Well my real name is Paula; even though most of my net buddies call me Will, (I’m happy either way). I'm from a city called Wolverhampton, in England, where I’ve lived all my life and have just completed University, earning a 2.1-degree in Creative and Professional Writing combined with Film Studies.

Much like the majority of the people of this site, I've loved writing all my life, but as you can see, I've only been posting fanfiction on here since 2001, after having caught the bug from reading other peoples for about a year before that. I will admit that for 4 years worth of work there aren't really as many stories on here as you'd probably expect, but the reason for that is because my muse often takes the form of a donkey, planting it's hooves into the sand, and not budging until it's good and ready lmao.

As you can see, I originally started writing stories for the Buffy and Angel fandom, after having been a fan of both shows since series 3 of Buffy. Two years ago, I fell in love with the new forensic drama Bones and now write fanfiction for that as well. With Bones, I'm a HUGE Hodgela (Jack Hodgins/Angela Montenegro) shipper but you can probably figure that out from my stories considering that the majority of them revolve around this paring lol. As for Buffy and Angel, I'm pretty flexible although some my favourites are Buffy/Angel, Spike/Anya, Spike/Tara, Wesley/Fred, and Wesley/Lilah.

Well I’ll leave you in peace now, thanks for coming to check out my stories and my profile. I hope you enjoy reading them as much as I have enjoyed writing them, and even though it's not mandatory, I really would love it if you could spare the time to leave me a review and tell me what you think.

If you'd like to say Hey, or have got a question feel, free to PM me or come and check out my LJ (which I don't update half as much as I should) by clicking on the Homepage link.

Thanks (again) and Hugs
P.
