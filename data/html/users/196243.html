Author has written 6 stories for Pearl Harbor, Moulin Rouge, Titanic, and Red Eye.

Well...it's been quite a while. The profile I deleted states that I am 15...and now I'm 23. Obviously, it's been about eight years since I posted anything on ff.net. But I guess I'm back now.

I noticed that "Not As She Appears" is still getting hits and reviews. I am VERY sorry, but it's been so long since I started writing that story that I have no idea where I was going with it anymore or who half the original characters are supposed to be. Maybe I'll watch Pearl Harbor over and over again until the ideas come back, but until then...I don't see how I can write more chapters. I do promise to try my best, though. I might have to re-write the current chapters to do it since I'm obviously in a different mindset now than I used to be.

The reason I came back to ff.net was mainly to publish a Red Eye fanfic that I'm working on. I recently saw Red Eye for the first time and I was blown away by the chemistry and tension between Jackson and Lisa. Please take the time to read and review that one, since I'm a little nervous about being back in the fanfiction world. Thanks!!

Also, I'm a little bit of a grammar nut. If anyone needs a beta, feel free to ask. :)
