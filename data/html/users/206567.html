Hello, my name is April! I am an elementary art teacher. I love to create art, read books, travel, go to church and listen to music. I have been on fanfiction forever and love to read ffics in my free time.

Here is a list of the types of fanfiction stories I like the most! I read mostly romance stories and believe it is important for a story to have good grammar and punctuation. I also appreciate it when authors spend time developing their characters and the story has a plot that develops over time.

The Labyrinth (Jareth & Sarah) All time favorite ffics!

Hunger Games (Peeta & Katniss) 2nd favorite!

Lord of the Rings- book (my favorite characters are Aragorn, Frodo, Merry, Pippin, Gandalf, & Tom Bombadil)

Harry Potter (Harry & Ginny, James & Lily)

Twilight (Alice & Jasper, Jacob & Bella)
