I'm completely redoing my bio! I was tired of simply updating, so I'm just going to ramble on for a while here, and hope you stick around long enough to read the info on my stories.

For those of you who were wondering, I have removed 2 of my stories. Yes, I know, it's sad, but one I didn't like, and one wasn't being read (seemingly). So they're gone.

Where to begin? School has started, which means, of course, fewer posts and longer breaks between. I will try my hardest to get the new stuff to you, I promise. Projects, homework, and general teacher anal-ness will have me bogged down for a while.

Ah, Harry Potter. Thou art mine passion and mine destruction. I now have *way* too many fics in the Potterverse. Oh, well, they're all being reviewed, so I won't be mean and take them off. My personal favorite is HP and the Journey Across Time. Of course, I've worked the longest on that one so I may just be biased.

Also, don't forget to just read my stories, but also go to my favorites and check them out. There are some really good ones in there that deserve attention. Not that most of them aren't getting it, they just deserve as much as possible. Drop a review by, too.

Now on to the stories themselves. I won't bore you with stats as I have in the past, but I will let you know this: if it's finished, don't expect it to be updated. If it's at the top of the list, it's been updated. The ones at the bottom haven't. Simple, and I don't mean to sound sarcastic or anything. Have a great day, and happy reading!

Love and butter,
~*~Evie~*~
