Well I've been a member of this site since December of 2001... so over a decade at this point. Crazy how I still love fan fictions... not that the show or manga hasn't changed over the years... I went from Tenchi Muyo, to Cardcaptor Sakura to Hey Arnold... then I took a break from the site for a while, I'm not sure what it was but I guess it was the whole high school thing that took over my life. Then I came back in 2006 and was addicted to Avatar: The Last Airbender fan fiction, and that show became a major obsession for me, followed soon after with a Naruto Obsession, then came the Kyle XY Obsession then my Twilight Obsession, co-mingled with a Scorpius/Rose Obsession, which morphed into a Dramione Obession and now my OTP's are Dair and Klaroline, and I intend to spend the entire summer of 2012 reading Klaroline Fanfiction! ]

A little about me...

I'm going to try to find an interesting way to tell you that I'm a girl, I'm twenty four, and that I love being apart of things that are so much bigger than just me. I think that's why I love this site so much, knowing that there are so many other people out there who love writing and love creating amazing works of art that they allow others to enjoy makes my heart swell. I know that may sound cheesy but whats wrong with that? It is one of my favorite things in life to find people in the real world who know what fanfiction is, and actually read and/or write it! I hug people when that happens! ]
