Hey Minna! It's been a while hasn't it?

About me hmmmm...lets see...

The names Ko-chan, though obviously thats not my real name, it's my nickname my sis Ki-chan gave me. It's also short for my middle name (which I like much better then my first). I'm half Jap. and part Americian and well...way too many others to sit and name. I was born and raised in America and know nothing of my Jap. heritage (how sad is that?! Unfortuneately I was too young to care when I was younger and it was too late to care when I got older since my grandmother died, sadly my Father and his siblings never cared to find out either T_T). It's my life long dream to fully and completely fix that problem. I usually come off as a very quiet shy person, but once I get to know someone, if I must say so myself, I can't seem to shut up (sometimes anyways).

I love to do many things and can never stay focused on one thing sometimes. For the past seven years I've been completely and totally fixated on Manga/Anime (or asian stuff in general), I love learning about other countires, and hope to travel to many different countries in the future. I love writing, and sometimes drawing; and reading is my favorite past time besides swimming. I have an extremely large collection of Manga (spanning about 650+) and still going though a good portion belongs to my good friend Cho_cho, seeing as how we combine our collections. Many of my friends and family tell us we could open a Manga library, lol, though I'm not sure I could part with them even for a short time. My favorite colors are any shade of green and purple, I also like silver, white, grey, black, and dark blues.

My favorite Anime and/or Manga: Yu Yu Hakusho, W Juliet, Skip Beat!, Whistle!, Prince of Tennis, Hana-Kimi, Rurouni Kenshin, Gundam Wing, Gundam Seed, Sailor Moon, Outlaw Star, PetShop of Horrors, Genju no Seiza, Tokyo Babylon, Spiral, Case Closed/Detective Conan, CLAMP works in general, Naruto, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Hikaru No Go, Fruits Basket, FullMetal Alchemist, and well many, many, many more; though it would take to long to sit and name them all.

I really don't have too many dislikes I usually name as they come, though its the same with my likes. I do dislike snobby and rude people and those who judge on looks alone. I don't like certain anime mainly becasue I perfer the Manga over it. Sometimes the anime just doesn't do it justice. . ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

All right! Now to the main point of re-doing this (aren't you glad I'm done rambling!!)! Ok! So my faithful readers...I'm so incredibly happy that you've put up with my wish-washyness so...THANK YOU+bows deeply+ My hats off to you for waiting for so long. But I had to take a couple of steps back from writing. I'm the type of person that gets bored with something easily or I lose intrest in something easily. So I had to move two space back in order to be able to move forward once again. It's been a while since I've written anything, then one night the feeling to write just hit me once again! I almost stayed up all night in order to do just that, but alas I had to get up early the next day so my writing had to wait. Now though I've got plenty of free time (though I should be using it to sign up for college -_-; I took a year off so I really should get at it so my dad will get off my back) though I'm not sure how much that'll change in the near future with work and (hopefully) school coming, but I will try to make more time to write and less to read. With my motavation to write again I hope it won't take so long to get new chapters up. I plan on adding new stories and revising old ones, also if anyone is intrested I have some original stories up on FictonPress.com (Pen name: Asian-hime) though I only have one story up right now, I do plan on adding more and revising the one that is there. In any case thank you all for being so patient, I hope in the next few weeks you'll see that it payed off!!
