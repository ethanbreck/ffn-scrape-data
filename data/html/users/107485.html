May 19, 2003-- Chapter 7 of "Borrowed Time" is coming. Hopefully by this weekend I'll have it up. Thanks for all the great reviews. You guys are great!

"Out of the Element" should also be up in fictionpress.net by the end of this week too.

Any of my LOTR stories fans--I've been working on a sequel to "On the Beach" and even one to "Hostile Takeover". They will sort of be connected in a plot where a dark force is trying to abduct a Ring-bearer to recreate the One Ring. In Enai's story it will be after her ability as a warden to travel to the Blessed Realm. In my "Hostile Takeover" story, Aragorn's reincarnation is given the task of escorting Sally to the Grey Havens. The universes are coming together.

Please refer to fpn for any of my original works. I just added a new one called "Warm Advocate". Thanks.

Bio:

I'm a early thirties mother of two and a "retired" librarian married to a wonderful man who happens to be a semi-world-famous fantasy sculptor. I write fanfiction because it's fun and it has really helped me build my writing skills. I used to write comic books but that industry has gotten too tough for me. I have written a couple of children's book that I am currently querying publishers to get it in print. I tend to be a perfectionist so it might take me a while to update.

I love to get reviews and will make a great effort to read and review the work of those who read and review mine. We're in this together.

I may not post as often as my readers would like me to but my writing time is usually limited to about an hour a day. Sometimes I have good days and sometimes I have bad days.

I'm going to remove my unfinished (never to be finished) stories soon.
