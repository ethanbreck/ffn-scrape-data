8/05/04: IMPORTANT NOTICE
How interesting. Two stories that I have had up here for more than two years have suddenly been reported and removed. So as a result, I am removing every story I have on this website. It has been nearly three years since I joined FF.net, and it seems that they just don't care about loyalty. I am not a child to be slapped on the hand for a supposed 'violation'. Yes, the two stories that were banned were in script format, which up until recently I did not know was not allowed. One was a script for an actual play with stage directions and everything. So playwrights are not worthy of publishing on FF.net? Fine. I can and will take my stories somewhere else.
My deepest apologies to those of you whom actually enjoyed my work. I certainly enjoyed my time on this site. Thank you all so much for your support. :)
With love, Rachel (a.k.a. RaiaGirl)
