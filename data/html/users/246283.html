Updates:

08 Sep 2018
I am still alive. I voraciously consume fanfiction now rather than writing or drawing. I have some older works on ArchiveOfOurOwn under the same username that are not published at FFN. If the pseud changes, I'll remember to update it here, too. Please message me instead on AO3 if you want to talk.

Depression is a horrible thing. You don't trust what your eyes see, what your ears hear, what your heart feels. I regard my younger self, and - for all her flaws - she was the better person. Depression takes everything away - it is the most quiet murderer of the soul.

Be kind to each other. You never know what devils gnaw their bones.

04 Feb 2014
Uploaded the last chapter of Musing that I finished on 29 Jan 2014. I'm not proud of its quality, but it's enough that it lifts one part of my burden. Also, I think I may look into AO3 (Archive Of Our Own) if I decide to publish anything else. FFN is far too restrictive in the types of formatting it allows in chapters.

27 Oct 2009
Tidied the profile page a bit. Uploaded an old work that was originally published to TokyoPop.com.

25 Jan 2009
Chapter Five of Musing is completed and posted. I look below at the previous update information and snicker sadly to myself. Difficult, indeed. I feel I must apologize to everyone for what occurred. I hesitate to put something so very personal on such a public forum, but I owe it to my readers. There are many reasons that I took so long, but the two major ones are these: I suffer from mental and emotional health issues, complicated by the illness of depression that runs in my family; and the fact that I gained a boyfriend only to have the aforementioned problems cause me to leave him, along with an extremely stressful confrontation with someone else a few days before that break-up. I had an emotional and near mental breakdown. I was having difficulty finishing writing beforehand, and after all that... crap, I couldn't bear to look at any of my writings. I could not afford to invest myself emotionally in anything other than strictly myself. Occasionally, as a way to stay in touch in some manner, I created a new account ( Transformer Author Spotlights) and posted old Transformers stories. (See more information on that on the user profile page for TAS.)

Then just a few days ago, I was on the laptop and thought, Why not? I opened the file and... just started typing. It didn't, well, hurt is the closest approximation I am able to arrive at. The chapter quality is not entirely to my standard, but I attribute that to being rusty. I can only go forward and do my best to do better.

So there it is. I wish I could make up for the promise to have a chapter every two weeks, but I am unable to. What I can promise you is at least one more chapter (it will deal with the concept of the power of names) that will be finished--I do not know when, though. I ask for your forgiveness and your patience. I will do better.

P.S. Thank you Nightmusic22 for adding me to your favorite authors list. I think I smiled in a way I have not for months.
