I thought I'd update a little since I'm now 32, wow did I really say that? I am reworking some of my fics, very slowly. I may even post them when I am done. I don't think anyone still reads these! If you do don't be afraid to say 'hello'

Well the last time I was here I was 18, I am currently 22, yeah 26 now.That shows how much I update around here! I haven't written anything in years, four years to be exact. I have started writing again, most of the stories that I wrote four plus years ago I have started rewrites to go with them. Rereading what I have written I apologize for how terrible most of it is. I was young and didn't know what I was doing! I was blinded by newsies! I also noticed I tend to kill some one off in every story!! I am working on keeping people alive in my fics now! I see a reoccurring theme going on here. I write for awhile and then I stop. It's been almost a year-ish really. I did post one thing during 2008 and the year is almost over! I plan on continuing a few pieces. Specially Buttons' Story!!

Just like many of the other newsies authors around here I am a member of the NML. I have been on there since 2002, seven years this Jan 2009. I also have the Newsies Mailing List C2 on here so if you are on the NML and want your story added to it please contact me or one of the staff for the list with the link to the story you want in there.

Newsies- My favorite newsie is Skittery. I used to like Spot, but all the teeny bopper romances out there have just ruined him for me, but he will still hold a special place in my newsies heart. Don't get me wrong I still like to write about him, after all I did write The Spot Clause for him. All the news boys are cute in their own way. My favorite pair to write about are Olivia and Skittery because they just seem to get on each others nerves so much.
The Tribe- Lex may be short, but he is my favorite badass from The Tribe. He is also my favorite to write and RP about. There's just something about his stupid rough ways that makes me love him so much, even though he is the biggest jerk in the series. Pride is also quite hot with his eco loving ways. My favorite female characters from series is Tai San (also my favor pairing TaiSan/Lex) and Ruby even though I haven't seen all of season five yet.
Mighty Ducks- There's a small theme going here, Fulton and Portman are my favorite characters. You just have to heart the bash brothers. However, including those two I like to RP Charlie, Guy, and Banks. I don't really have a favorite pairing there since it is a bit limited.
