Ok, in response to reading OotP:

I think I agree with almost everything in this here( She didn’t review the het ships, though. A shame too, as most of her readers probably enjoy slash and het equally.

There was one point when I was almost sure Draco was giving Harry “The eye”. It was a little scary. My friend said she believed Draco was quite obsessed with Harry. I think so, but more in the Stalker way. o.O

I thought the book was a little bland and did leave me empty after I read it, but it answered a lot of questions that I was hoping would be answered.

And Sirius falling through the veil?! Omg, no! That arch thing was SO creepy. The whispers and mummers…*shudder* I think Sirius will be back, though. He plays such a big role in Harry’s life that if her were to leave, Harry would be completely and utterly suicidal. Think about it! How would YOU feel? I would have beat Remus down and followed Sirius, if it were me. But I know doing that would be really stupid and I’m glad Harry didn’t. Remus…*sniff* poor Remus! Man, after reading red’s “My immortal” dead!SiriusAngsty!grieving!Remus slash fic, I think I MIGHT write something too.

I loved Ginny’s character at first, but then realized that she got over Harry really fast and maybe she’s just trying to convince herself that she doesn’t’ like him anymore. She was really brave in the Department o Mysteries, though.

I loved Neville! And in St. Mungo’s(Spell?) was SUCH a tearjerker! Not as much as when Sirius died, but still.

Luna was cool, but Tonks was kind of annoying. I’m still hoping for a good Tonks/Remus fic to come out soon.

Seamus was a complete wanker, as usual, and the incident with Percy shocked the hell out of me. I new something like this was bound to happen. Percy was so in love with Crouch, and when he died I was sure Percy was going to become Fudges lapdog.

Tortured!Angst!Suicidal!Harry shocked me too. I thought of Harry as a person above self-pity, but apparently not.

I loved Snape in this and fell in love with the flash back scene. James was such an ass back then, I loved it. And I was drooling over the description of Sirius in his youth. *Drools on her keyboard* *wipes mouth with back of sleeve*

And, to anyone who read the Draco Trilogy, did you notice that it incestuous?! If you didn’t notice, Narcissa is(Was *Sniff*) Sirius’s Cousin(Second cousin?).

I always loved Remus and Siruis(not in the slashy way) and thought that the scene where Sirius is walking down the hall and singing “Bless Ye Marry Hippogriffs”(I think that was it) was perfect.

And I totally agree with Rhysenn. I think that Remus is in need of a good, hard, shag. After all he’s been through he, definitely, deserves one. (Go on! Use a lasso if you have to, Tonks! *Wink*)

Now for PAIRINGS!(Judged by personal experience in reading fics with such pairings)

Pairings that I still love, even after reading OotP!

Draco/Ginny!!!!! The entire time, I was sniffing around for any hints. Come on, JKR! You KNOW they’re perfect for each other!

Pairings that are ok…

Ron/Hermione JKR didn’t put any hint in this book about them getting together, but it could still happen.
Harry/Gabriel (Sp?) Quite unlikely, yet still intriguing.
Harry/Draco I think I’m starting to get a thing for slash. And all those years of loathing it…Down the drain.
Harry/Hermione After reading the Draco Trilogy, who could not at least like this paring, if not love it to death.

Pairings that I hate

Ron/Harry They’re just friends, damn it! *Cries*
Hermione/Draco I don’t see how this would work. But, you never know.
Ron/Draco This is probably the MOST unlikely I’ve ever read.
Sirius/Harry This is just sick! Not to mention, he’s dead now so any, teeny, tiny, fraction of a chance this would happen is gone. I still think Siri is coming' back.

Pairings I’ve ditched since OotP.

Ginny/Harry Just not working for me anymore. I’m not sure if it ever was…
Harry/Cho Well, obviously Cho hates Harry now. At least that’s what we’re lead to believe…Harry is sure over her though.

Fics I’m working on at the moment

Never Really Existed – My very first D/G and I do plan on finishing it. (Taken down for re-writing)

A Very Odd Day – It’s really…Erm…weird… I just recently added a new chapter and I’m working on the next.

My Secret Keeper – My very first FanFic. I’ve ditched this. It was my first and it was one of the WORST things I’ve ever written. It’s one of those fics you find in MST’s, so I took it down.

More coming soon… I’m going to wait a month or two before I write any new stuff so all the other authors can work out all the cliché’s. I just KNOW people are going to use the Room of Requirement for Snogs and Shags.
