I'm 29 now and I've had this account since I was 13 or 14. So if you read one of my stories and it sounds like a 13 year old wrote it, I was probably 13 when I wrote it lol. I don't write for all of my ships anymore, just a few.

I'm a horseback rider in Canada. I have 6 horses; 5 geldings and 1 mare. Sonny is a grey selle francais horse I showed in the 3' - 3'6" hunters. Kelpie is the favourite and knows it. He's a bay welsh/arab large pony with an amazing show record and absolutely hilarious attitude lol. Shooter is a palomino welsh that is essentially a pet at this point, but hopefully he'll get back into work soon. King is a chocolate palomino German Riding Pony who shows in the hunters. Harkin is the newest, and he's a liver chestnut welsh and should be a big winner when his training is done. PK is the mare and she's... unique. There's no pony quite like my Peek. I also have a dog, Sydney (chihuahua), a former barn cat (Jack), and I just got a new Scottish Straight kitten (Avery). Animals sort of run my life lol.

Other than animals, I have a diploma in Travel and Tourism from Sheridan College and I graduated from Brock University with a BA in sociology and minor in psychology. I love music; I took 7 years of vocal lessons and I still love to sing, though I'm admittedly not that good anymore. My favourite artists are Delta Goodrem and Lady Gaga.
