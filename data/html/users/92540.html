Author has written 14 stories for Slayers, Lost Universe, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Matantei Loki Ragnarok, Avengers, and Captain America.

Well, let me see... I'm Irony, I like to be mysterious about my real name, I'm the same age as Britney Spears, have the same birthday as Tupak Shakur, was born in the same city as Jim Carrey and live in the same city as Jann Arden. My hobbies include fanfiction, sewing, cartooning, beadwork, anime, and godzilla movies. My tastes in clothing, music, food, and entertainment are probably best described as eclectic. I hate hockey, bubblegum pop, network news, and movies that think they're deep when they're not. If I grow up, I want to be either a history professor or a novelist, and I'm going to marry either Josh Groban, Ethan Embry, or Paul Bettany.

All this is still true, but I'm now working on a Master's degree in classics at Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario. This is the major reason I haven't updated anything in two years. I'm hoping to do some catch-up this summer, in between doing massive amounts of reading in three languages and working on an archeological site near Tito, Italy. Busy busy.
