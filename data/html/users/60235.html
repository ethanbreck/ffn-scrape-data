girl, born in late April 1987, who likes to read and write in her spare

(ain't life fun?) I've found this really cool site for people who
SI(self injure)/are depressed/have eating disorders/whatever. It could
be triggering, but it could also be really helpful.

I like getting e-mail, and reviews. You can now find several of my
lists on soupfiction.net, on the incredibly off chance that you liked
my lists and feel like reading more :) Please review my fics, and
thanks to people who do review my fics. And please give me constructive
(note: constructive) criticism, I really want to improve, which I can't
do if I don't know what needs improving.
I'm really interested in dragons, thanks to Anne Mccaffery's Pern
books, and I've now read all of the Pern books, the Pit Dragon trilogy,
and the Enchanted Forest Chronicles. I read all the time, so if anyone
has a book they'd recommend...I usually read sci-fi/fantasy or
religious books. The Case for Christ is an awesome book, I recommend
it. I am also hopelessly obsessed with Gillian Rubinstein's Circus
books (Galax-arena, Terra-farma), and am waiting, not so patiently, for
Universercus, the final Circus book, to come out, although God alone
knows if and when it will since it was supposed to come out in 2003.
On 5/16/03 I got my learner's permit, and on 6/24/04 I got my license
so I CAN DRIVE! All you Massachusetts people should be terrified :)
My profile on FP.net is http://www.fictionpress.com/~spunkiegirl in
case anyone wants to read my original fiction.
I joined Yahoo groups on 8/8/03 so I now have a second e-mail address
-- spunkie_girl2003@yahoo.com. So, you can email me at either one of
them.. I always love talking to people online, so feel free to email me
whenever you want.
