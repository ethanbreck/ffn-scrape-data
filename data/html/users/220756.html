Author has written 36 stories for Harry Potter.

poetic licence also goes by: 'Queen of Morbidity', 'Kryptonite' and 'Amy' (on her boring days). Loves her family, adores her friends. Her main accomplishment in life is working for the Government, being published, getting away with having a messy room. Lives with a madwoman,a nuroticcat and her imagination. Likes to: sing with the radio (badly), listen to the rain, 3am, calm midnight walks, indulging in chocolate. Dislikes: hurting people, people who think they're better than she is just because they have money / status / reputation, being taken for granted. Has firms views on politics, inter-racial tolerance, cruelty to children & animals, sport, art and literature. All of which are ignored by everyone she knows.

Strengths: Has photographic memory when it comes to small details: can recount fights down to the last detail. Born under Cancer (Water) and devotes her life to living up to it. Is emotional, can be manipulative, clingy at times, independent at others, home-bound, has a natural charm that most people hate her for. Is intelligent, witty, energetic, persistent, friendly, subtle as a sledgehammer and has no sense of tact whatsoever. When she's not talking, she's writing.

Weaknesses: Is more critical on self than anyone could ever be. When in doubt: hide behind the nearest rock until the question goes away. And then there's chocolate...

Special Skills: Ability to talk herself in, and out, of every situation (even when underwater). Can lie straight to your face. Will read your soul at any opportunity. Persistent beyond call of duty.

Weapons: A personality that can maim at thirty paces - and her nails are classified as deadly weapons. More patience than the dead, more sparks than a dragon, more stubbornness than a mountain.

Mottos: "Don't wait for the light at the end of the tunnel…stride right up there and light the bloody thing yourself." - Cassandra Hutchinson-Kern
"Imagination is more important than knowledge" - Albert Einstein.
"Realisation: What I see, is; what I believe, I am." - Amy Crosby

Natural Habitat: Australia. This explains her madness, her insanity, her relaxed attitude and the way the spells pyjamas with a 'y'. Also Australian for her obsession with Aussie Rules Football, Barbies on Sundays, 40° Christmas days, creatures that can kill you in remarkably nasty ways, and the Australian Swim-Team (Especially Ian Thorpe).
