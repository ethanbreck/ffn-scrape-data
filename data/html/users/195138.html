Author has written 4 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Naruto, Final Fantasy VII, and Office.

I have read more fanfics and unfinished stories than there are episodes of General Hospital. True story. I love reading crossovers. They are by far my favorite type of fanfiction. If it's epic in length, even better. But ya gotta love the oneshot!

My fandoms of choice range from video games (particularly Zelda and Final Fantasy) and anime to my latest movie or sci-fi binge. I've been a giant sucker for Xander stories from Joss Whedon's Buffy the Vampire Slayer for years. I just can't seem to break the habit.

But here's the real message. I wish I were a fast writer, or capable of epic writing for that matter. Y'know what, I'd settle for posting more amature-ish short stories. In the meanwhile, I'm just going to read your fanfiction. I hope I left you a nice review, and if I did, you can bet your knickers that I was ultra enthused by your work. :D
