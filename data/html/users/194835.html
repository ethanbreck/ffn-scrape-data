Author has written 9 stories for Guild Wars, Sailor Moon, Final Fantasy X-2, and Final Fantasy X.

I've been around here for quite some time, but when it comes to actually writing, Im a little slow at that. Recently, getting to know one of the authors here has sparked my creativity and given me the slight push of bravery I needed in order to actually submit a story. Im dreadful and do need a bit of prodding sometimes to keep writing, but hopefully now that I've put up a 'muse wanted' sign, I'll be able to keep going with some proper motivation.

A bit of general info about me. I'm 27, lesbian, and live in Minnesota. I dont wear flannel, so don't think it, nor do I own a motorcycle, though I do want a truck. ;) I'm a romantic at heart, and tend to veer towards stories that have a girl/girl relationship in them, though I do enjoy a well written m/f romance, a fair amount of the story favorites on my list are of that type. But hey, can't blame a girl for dreamin.

I do enjoy getting to know people, so if you'd like, please feel free to e-mail me at the address provided and we'll go from there with IM's or whatnot.

Fandoms I'll likely write in:

Guild Wars -- Im the first one here, yay! It's a fairly new game, so that's not too surprising, but, still. Will most likely primarally focus on Cynn, perhaps some stories with original characters as my personal character in the game advances.

SailorMoon -- My first and true anime love. I've several stories which I've started, and may stick up here, though they've stalled for lack of motivation and responce.

Final Fantasy X -- Rikku. Come on, look at my story favorites.

Final Fantasy X-2 -- Rikku again. Did I really have to say it?
