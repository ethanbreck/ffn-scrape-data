The rest I either don't have ships for or I haven't been watching long enough.

For the record, while I'm a proud shipper, I'm not into 'ship wars. Live and let ship, as far as I'm concerned. It's all down to personal preferences and what involves you. I'll read mostly anything provided it is well written.

I'm a grammar and spelling Nazi when it comes to what I will and will not read, I also believe in good characterisation, some semblance of a reasonable plot (though I'm not averse to a bit of plotless smut occasionally), and that well-written dialogue can be the making or breaking of a story. I'm a beta reader - email me if you need my services - I do all ratings, most of the shows above, and only draw the line at anything involving underage sex, non-con (unless it's necessary for plot purposes and not explicit), and certain bodily functions.

I am a writer too. Mostly ER - with 2 WIPs currently in progress (Second Time Around and Behind These Hazel Eyes) - most of my other WIP (Give Me You, Divided We Fall, Blood Ties, Choices etc etc...) may finally have fallen by the wayside but never say never. I'm also getting into writing for CSI - particularly NY - and hope to have a multi-chapter for CSI:NY up soon.

Anything you wanna ask about my stories email me. Feedback is better than chocolate, after all.

If you're still reading, well done :)
