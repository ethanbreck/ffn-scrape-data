I've only started posting RE works last year even though I've been playing the games since I was 14. I have browsed through the RE for many years so I figured it's time to start contributing. :)

Another note: Some of you guys might notice that my older stories do not have quotation marks. This is because my older stories were written on Appleworks and fanfiction.net changed a lot of its formatting over the years so my stories written on Appleworks did not carry their original format over. I must warn you to beware of my older stuff because they are FILLED with grammatical errors and nonsense. Hopefully, there has been a visible progression in terms of quality over the years!
