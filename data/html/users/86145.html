I am the one and only Evil Overlady. Hear my maniacal laughter! Mwah-ha-ha! I have been writing for over twelve years (which you can imagine has turned me maniacal). Often I assist Vathara, but I am not responsible for what she does with the ideas we put together. ;) I also draw. I post the drawings on my Homepage, so drop by! And if you know somewhere where I can post my art send an e-mail.

Be sure, also, to check out my FictionPress Shelf here.

My stories: I prefer to do short pieces (There are novel-writers, like Vathara, and short-story-writers. I'm the latter.). My longest piece was a sixty-two page original sci-fi (which you can find on my FictionPress Shelf).

The most important part to a good story, in my opinion, is good dialogue. Take these favorite lines of mine for example...

Yu-Gi-Oh:
Yugi (flicks coin at door after Yami warns to check for traps first; coin gets zapped): "Checking for traps sounds like a good idea..."

ep.45
Mokuba: Don't be mad, but I came here with Yugi and his friends. ...I didn't know who else to call.
Kaiba: Anyone but them!

ep.180
Yami Yugi: Kaiba! Say something!
Kaiba: Don't screw up! Got it?

Viper:
Frankie: "Geez! Joe! You nearly scared my hair flat!"
"Come on, guys! Don't leave me holding the bag on this one! How do I explain this (the bullet holes)? Termites?!"

Gargoyles:
Elisa: "I think your head stays rock hard even at night."

Action Man:
Alex: "Time to stop screaming... now."
Rikki (stops screaming then looks): "Actually, time to start!"

Coach Grey: "It's on a need-to-know basis."
Alex: "Guess what? I need to know _now_." (Armed helicopter hovers in front of them) "Oh... Okay, maybe not right this second..."

NightWalker:
Shido: "Hey! Shame on you. It's not nice to pull out guns in the middle of a crowd."

Inuyasha:
Villager's daughter clinging to Miroku as a monster attacks: "I'm scared!"
Miroku (eyeing jealous Sango): "So am I, but not for the same reason."

Avatar: The Last Airbender:
Prince Zuko (looking at map): “How am I going to find him, Uncle? He is clearly a master of evasive maneuvering.”
Sokka (holding his own map): “You have no idea where you’re going, do you?”
