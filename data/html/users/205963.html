-REAL SUMMARIES FOR MY STORIES GO HERE-

Frozen Flame is about a princess. But not any princess. It's about THE princess of Hyrule, of the Legend of Zelda Series. In Ocarina of Time, Link(our hero) is locked away within the Sacred Realm for seven years. Not much is known about what exactly happened during his seven year 'sleep'. This story is meant to elaborate on the details, trying to stick to the game's own plot as closely as possible, even though barely anything was revealed. It's realistic. It's angst. There will be action.
And everything revolves around our main character, and heroine. The princess, Zelda.

Burnt Roses is my way of making fun of all the wrong things in society. The Zelda cast seemed perfect for it, though I can think of tons of other characters that will suit my purpose as well. Basically, everything is to an -extreme-, everyone will cling to a stereotype. It's high school, but the kind of high school where if you wore a black shirt that had "I see stupid people" on front in red letters, you would be branded a freak. Extremely close minded people, extremely stereotypical, and overall BS, as in being true to a certain degree, and SHOULDN'T HAPPEN in real life, but DOES ANYWAY. Read it, you might learn something.

Clouded Eyes
Let's just say its LIKE a Mary Sue, but not. Because the main character is similar to me, but not me, and NOT PERFECT. The girl falls into the Legend Of Zelda game, and BAM. You have an exciting, dramatic adventure awaiting. And romance too. Maybe.
