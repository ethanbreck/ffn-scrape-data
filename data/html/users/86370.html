Hey everyone- First I just wanted to say that all my stories are discontinued. All my stories were started in my high school days, and now I simply don't have the time for them. Plus, I simply believe my own writing from that time could use some work, but, it's part of my history of growing up so I could never delete them.

But I LOVE to read others stories. I am a Big fan of Kenshin, Naruto, Escaflowne, Twilight, DBZ, Sookie, LotR, Alpha and Omega and Harry Potter stories. I pretty much only read T or M cuz i simply find the quality in them better then K rated stories. I'm very annoyed by slash stories because most (not all!) are way overdone and with the writing quality of a repressed thirteen year old. My favorite stories tend to have damsels in distress becoming fighters. And I will admit i spend the majority of my down time at home shifting through stories looking for yet another story to add to my list of stories i keep tabs on daily.

About me: I'm a 30-something year old girl form Santa Cruz, California. A x-WoW player, a lover of mass effect and city builder games. I love the arts, dogs, the outdoors and coffee at all times of the day. Random facts about my life, i know, but i figured i needed to say something to describe myself.

My last note: spelling is my downfall in life, so trust me I know; at least i have improved some over the years.
