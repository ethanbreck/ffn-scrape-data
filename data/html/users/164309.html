Erm.. what can I say...?

I write for fun and as a stress relief. Am old enough to know better and am a Brit so beware my spelling.

I don't post WIPs, so once the story starts to goes up it is finished. I just post in sections to make sure the formatting is right and it gives me a chance to cast another eye over the stories.

The stuff posted here will included HP/SS slash. A ship only Snarry in that fandom. I write only for challenges/fest/ficathons in HP (so I'll never have that many HP fics) and I do that solely because without I deadline I wouldn't finish a fanfiction... :-)
