Author has written 2 stories for Sailor Moon X-overs.

Well, it's certainly been quite a long time since I logged into my account, much less updated anything. For those of you still reading, I'm have a rather eclectic fictional palate. That is, I'm interested in things from Star Wars/ Trek to Once Upon a Time, Harry Potter to Bones and Castle. I think a shorter list would be the genres that I'm not interested in. Namely, horror and reality TV (pretty much the synonyms in my book).

As for my stories, I'd love to say that I'll be finishing them soon, but the truth is that my muse has moved beyond the Japanese manga/ anime phase and my reference "texts" are currently in storage. They're still in the back of my mind, and I hope one day my muse will wander back into their genre so I can finish. For now, I'm just a lurker who enjoys reading the worker of my fellow authors.
