Hello. My name is MordorianNazgul, as you probably know. I'm a 19-year-old college student, currently undecided/undeclared, with insane friends and a horrible complex about my writing, which I will someday start putting back up here.

My majorfandoms are Star Wars, Halo, Forgotten Realms, Baldur's Gate, and Knights of the Old Republic, though I also cruise through other sections on occasion. I hate Mary Sues, and if I've left you a not-nice review, I will not apologize; I'm looking for quality fanfics with three-dimensional characters, not flat and super-powerful girls with angsty pasts that everyone loves to death. Feel free to voice your opinions on the matter to me.

If I have a fanfic that does not agree with ToS out here, then it will go on my LJ. The link to it has been provided above. As well, I have a deviantART account that is currently still undergoing construction, but when I finish it and start uploading pictures, I will put a link to it on my LJ, and probably here as well.

When I finally start putting stories back up here, feel free to check their progress on this page. Until then, take a look at my other stuff, have a great day, and aluve ust.
