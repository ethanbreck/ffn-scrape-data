4. Constructive criticism is preferred. Not necessary though. (grin)
5. When advertising your own fic, give a full summary along with a REVIEW. Spam is not appreciated.
6. No phrases repeated n times. (I.e. Please update! x5 billion) I call that spam.
7. Pointing out mistakes and giving pointers is luv. (heart)
8. Please check to make sure your Caps Lock is OFF.
9. Spam = you being whacked on the head with a rotting, maggot-infested fish.
