Author has written 4 stories for Diablo, Resident Evil, and Xena: Warrior Princess.

I'm a huge flake. My motto in life is 'try everything once, go back to it if you like it.' Writing's been something that I've done one and off, but I've always found a way back to. I've explored different types, forms, layers, plots and I do enjoy a range of different universes. Here you find some of my more experimental, if not slightly beaten and raw, forms of what I do with part of my spare time.

NOTE: Please be aware that my fanfics are extremely spaced out in their time periods. My writing skills as well as inspirational whims may vary greatly from each of them.
