Hi! I'm Kayura or Nezie or... (insert name here) I'm 17 (almost 18 now)... Umm...

If you want me to update my fics, send me e-mails or review (either method gets to me). Currently, I'm really busy so I have to be really inspired. :D I have been really busy for 2 years. *sigh* But as you might be able to see I'm a veteran of ff.net ... I'm going on my 5th year. (Once under pen name Kayura)

The kind of fics I write & fav. characters:

1)Gundam Wing AC - oh yes. oh yes. I was one of the originals that loved this show when it debuted in the US. That's right. :) I'm a fan of all the pilots, but I've never been really inspired by Trowa. *sigh* I used to have a lovely epic up here (at least I thought it was lovely) but no one else seemed to like it, so I took it down.

2) Ronin Warriors / Samurai Troopers: Once again, veteran. Watched this show as a kid on the SciFi channel. Heehee. I'm a HUGE Rowen/Touma fan. Also like Dais and Kale. Of course, I like Kayura - its what my friends call me. Kayura-san, Pissed Off Ancient/Wise One. LOL!

I don't watch that much anime anymore. Actually, I don't watch that much TV anymore in general. Just so you know.

3) Lord of the Rings - Writing before it was the movies again. :D For some reason I try to be a stickler for dates with this. Don't know why. *shrug* Anyway... contemplating an Eomer fic. (sounds weird, yes) Or... I dunno. I try to find really obscure characters, because Tolkien's so complete with his descriptions! I don't want to taint his work...

4) The Outsiders - Huge fan. Read it for the first time 10 years ago, read it every six months since. :D I like Steve (way to go me), and of course - Dally, tragic hero, and Soda, pretty boy.

5) Harry Potter - Yes, I'm a potterhead. I say nothing more except. Oliver Wood (before the movie), Fred & George Weasley (ditto to Oliver's), and (after the movie) Draco Malfoy. Hermione is also my home-dog.

6) Peter Pan - My favorite book of all time!!! Weeeee...

As you can tell, there are 1.95 rules to my fic writing: -No yaoi/slash- AND -Generally has an O.C.- However, I do agree with certain couples and detest others. Example: Arwen and Aragorn are always together, same with Noine/Zechs, but mostly its secondary. If they have a romantic liason that is exactly definitive in the story/movie they have it in my fic. I'm also partial to Duo/Hilde, but sometimes I get sadistic and put her with Trowa (some weird reason).

Anyway, I'll quit boring you.

Feel free to flame away or praise away, or better yet contribute constructive criticism. Ja ne!

-Nezie-
