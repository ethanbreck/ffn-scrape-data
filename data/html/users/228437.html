IF YOU WANT TO KNOW THE STATUS OF MY STORIES, PLEASE READ BELOW! I'M TIRED OF BEING ASKED WHEN SOMETHING IS GOING TO GET DONE!!

I write mostly poetry, so a fic is a change for me. I began writing "Gone Away" After school got out, and it progressed into a story I wanted to share with people. I plan on writing more Buffy, it's all a matter of being inspired.
i'm very picky about when and where I write, I have to be listening to the right music, sitting in the right light, and be on the brink of sleep. Why sleepy? I tend to think more clearly about how Buffy, Spike, and all the other characters feel, and speak.
I read a lot of essays about how to write a convincing Spike, but I quickly discovered that because of the way my fic was set up, I could write Spike differently.
I spend my spare time keeping my websites up, writing poetry, shopping, and spending time with my family.

Right now my main Babies are Thoughts of Kagome and Thinking of Him. Maybe i'll get around to some of the others... if you're interested in adopting one of my fics-- let me know... But if you didn't know... Low Down Ache has been taken over by the beautiful Dreamcatcher5. She writes better than I do-- so please continue to read, though I might let her move the entire thing over to her profile. It's her baby now. *nods*

Status of My Stories:

Soul Mates: Currently working on it, I'm a little stuck on how to link the story arch, and how to end it. I like sappy, but not so sappy that it cheapens the whole story.(Stalled Indefinately. Buffy Ended-- and i'm feeling alittle spazzed. Once Season Six comes out in June, I might find some inspiration again.)

Gone Away: As you can see I haven't updated it in a while. I'm in the process of re-writing some chapters, and figuring out a ending. That's the part I'm always stuck on. Hee hee. *blushes* (Stalled Indefinately. See Above.)

There is: I started this one on a whim, and I had begun my next chapter of it, but then I got all side tracked and what not-- anyway. It may progress into being strictly a mail story (ie: Just Letters being passed back and forth, and little to no action outside of the letters) (Stalled, until i can find some Inspiration)

Thoughts of Kagome: This fiction has no beginning and no end. And thus, no middle really either. I'll keep doing it as long as i keep getting good reviews from it. *shrugs* i'm a selfish author. It's a smattering of thoughts, as long as I feel like I'm feeling Inuyasha, It'll keep going.

Thinking of Him: Still just a tiny baby pre-me. Though I think I may have finally gotten Kagome's brain down finally, who knows. That's what the Read and Review is for, though, right? it also has no beginning no middle and no end. :)

LOW DOWN ACHE: This fic is no longer posted. I'm sorry if you miss it, or you thought it was great. I hated it. I thought it sucked. (cept what Dreamcatcher5 did, that isn't applicable here) if you really want it to continue, i'd suggest talking to her about it. :P

Anyway, If you want to Help me, by being a beta, or have a great idea for an ending, email me, I'm more than willing to hear your ideas, and I'll credit you with the idea! Happy day!
