I would love it if I didn't have to be an adult and hold down my soul crushing job and could focus more on doing what I want...which is reading, drawing, and video games. But one needs to eat, which requires money...which equals job o.

I also enjoy creative writing, though I am rusty from so many years of unused creative juices. Hopefully, I'll get back into the swing of things.
