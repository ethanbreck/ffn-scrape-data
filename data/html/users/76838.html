~~WELCOME TO KATLYNN'S WORLD~~

I swear, the world is going to explode people because I'm back and I'm posting! So sorry to all of you who value your lives.

Where have I've been you may ask??? Well the answer to that one is doing my homework like a good little first year university student should. Other than that, I've been a silent lurker around here, reading as many good stories as possible (which is getting harder to do with with what little spare time I have) and the odd time adding a review if I'm not in a hurry to be somewhere.

Instead of just studying and hiding around here to fill in my free time, I've been hardcore reading graphic novels (magna). The series I'm reading right now are:

1) Ceres Celestial Legend (also known as Ayashi no Ceres): I'm am totally obsessed with this series. I so for have up the the 6th book in this series. It is so good, that I can't even stress it enough!

2) Mars - This is a romance novel that keeps taking my breath away! It's so good and I can hardly put the any of the books I own down... I keep rereading them. So far I have 1-12 but I still have more to get!

4) Gravitation: Well, we can thank Galene from here for getting me addicted to this series! But I love her still! Definately worth reading!

If you would like personal opinion about these novels or what just feel like talking about them, just add me to your msn list or email me. My msn is sunprincess_kate@hotmail.com

So as you can tell, if you don't already know me and have yet to talk to me, I'm an anime nut! Sue me! I own all the Sailor Moon Magna's, watched every season except the Stars because Canada hasn't aired it and I don't own a credit card to buy it. I love Escaflowne and laugh my ass off in every Inyuyasha episode I watch.

My favourite pairings are;

Usagi/Mamoru

Van/Hitomi

Inyuyasha/Kagome

Aya/Toya/Yushi which ever one... I love those guys with her!

If you would like to know more radom yet important things about me, keep reading below. If not,well... I dunno what to say to that!

First of all, my name is Katlynn.
Pronounced like Kaitlynn even though the spelling is different. I have yet to encounter anybody who even spells their name like mine even though there are so many different ways to spell my name.

I'm 18. I'm female. I have my drivers liscence.

I'm a first year student at the University of Western Ontario, in an undergraduate program earning a BSC. Other than that, I'm not entirely sure what I'm going to do with my life... I still have to figure that out!

Ummm... I play the fiddle (it's a violin but I play different music! NOT CLASSICAL! Eeek.. I don't do classical, it hurts my ears and tries my patients, and makes me bored!), piano, and used to play the clainet.

Two years ago, I went to Ottawa on a free trip with Enounters With Canada which was the best thing that has ever happened to me. I LOVED the entire week, and the 147 people that I met and lived with for a week will always be apart of me.

I also went to PEI that same year and ate a french fri at one of their potatoe factories! ~_~

Oh and if it weren't for my Big'sis who shall remain nameless (I wounder if she still checks up on me here...) I would never have turn my obsession for watching Sailor Moon into what it is today. I still have to agree with you Big'sis, the episode with where Umino decides to become Tuxedo Melvin is priceless... just priceless!

So there you have it. You've finally finished reading my bio. Hope it was somewhat interesting for you, I tried my best! ~_~

~Katlynn~
