My biography needs a serious revision. So here it is.

My name is Acey, I write fanfiction of the Harry Potter kind because I enjoyed the series and couldn't resist tinkering with the characters. There is nothing special about my childish attemps at creating my own fantasies - but at least it's done in proper grammar and spelling, non? =) I'd be really glad if you'd just take a peek and review, though.

Righty-ho. I enjoy reading, very much, and I adore dragons. A more recent addiction of mine would have to be LiveJournal. My username is embryophyte. Everything else I fail to include in this little description should be found there, although I suspect that the introduction there is due for a change as well. Still, I can say that the interests tell all.

My favourite books are:
Harry Potter
The Lord of the Rings
Ella Enchanted
The Enchanted Forest Chronicles
Pride and Prejudice

Charmed is probably the only television serial I watch faithfully, except now that I've been introduced to Clay Aiken I pay attention to American Idol as well. My favourite singers, besides him, include Barratt Waugh, Natalie Imbruglia, Enya, Westlife and the Corrs. I enjoy Kevin Kern's and Andrew Lloyd Webber's music. Most music I enjoy lean towards the melancholic, ballad-like songs, although exceptions are not at all rare. Blue is quite possibly the only colour I can stand on a long-term basis, although I hold a special affection for lime green and hot pink. =p

My favourite pairings:
Harry/Draco
Draco/Hermione
Fred/George
Occasional Remus/Sirius and Harry/Lucius
and of course,
Tang/Kuo and
Cole/Phoebe

Possibly that is all that can be said about me. I suppose more is obvious down at LJ though. And you might want to refer to my Fiction Press account - the UserID is exactly the same.
