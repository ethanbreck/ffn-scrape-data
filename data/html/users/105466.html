I am nobody. Who are you? Are you nobody, too?

How's that for cryptic? OK, in Harry Potter terms, I'm as prickly as canon!Snape, serious as Remus, with a pinch of Percy and the occasional Sirius tendency. I love reviews (particularly the ones that employ good grammar and intelligent criticism).

Important notice regarding the rest of my work: Trust is a WiP, and I hope to revise chapter one before continuing, as I have had a Creative Writing course since I began writing it. As to A Father's Loss, well, I'm still debating re-writing it. My concepts of the characters have since changed (particularly where Narcissa is involved!), as well, I would like to think, as my overall quality of writing.

Important notice regarding "Harry Potter, werewolf": It's gone, it's gone, it's gone! I've deleted the sucker and feel so much better for it.
