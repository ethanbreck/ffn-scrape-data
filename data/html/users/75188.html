Updated: 20/12/04:

Well, after re-reading through the works of my 11 year old self (and cringing in utter horror...), I've decided to update my profile! I'm thinking of deleting my unfinished works, as I've completely lost interest in them and still have a slight case of writer's block. Anyway, after reading my author's notes and some of those song parodies...I feel like (to borrow a phrase from a British friend of mine) a right wazzick (and no, I'm not sure if that's how you spell it)!

So, since I'm sure all who read my "stories" three years ago are still waiting with baited breath for any form of update that might appear, I am here to tell you...it's time for some spring(winter) cleaning! I'm going to delete a few of the "bad" song parodies (before anyone comments that they're all bad, just remember...that's not nice! Ha.). There might be some updates...but I wouldn't bet on it. Anyway, that's about it. I didn't even remember I had an account on here until I was cleaning out the favorites on my old computer! Good-bye, all! Happy Christmas!
