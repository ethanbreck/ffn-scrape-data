About The Author

Sailor Sky, Jaded Lee -- yes I am that person. I'm an old writer here with past author accounts ranging back to nearly a decade ago.

I tend to have very large ambitions and not always enough time to allocate to them. No matter how long a story goes without an update, if it is posted here, it will be finished in time. This is a hobby and unfortunately there are several other things in my life that take precedence over writing.

I will admit that my grammar isn't always up to par to where it should be, but I am human, and I do make mistakes (sometimes several times over). Suggestions, criticism, praise and the like are always welcome and encouraged. I'm here to improve, not digress.

"My art is my humor, my stories are a much darker tale." Instabilitas

To The Readers

To all who have read and reviewed any one of my stories, or most/all for that matter, thank you very much! I appreciate your interest in my work and hope to keep up in the best of spirits.
