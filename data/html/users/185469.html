Well, it's about time I update my profile...

I currently write for three fandons: Charmed, Nip/Tuck and Angel. I know that pairings can be a rather sensitive subject, so let me give you an idea of what to expect:

Charmed:

Pairings: Cole/Phoebe, Piper/Leo, no exceptions. Ever.

The "Redemption" series started at the end of Season 4, radically changed the outcome and lost all contact with the TV show after that. The "Comedy of Errors" series started somewhere around the first half of Season 4 and took a path of its own, no possession by the Source whatsoever. And, no, I didn't give up on the "Comedy of Errors" series, even though I haven't updated "Roll the Dice, Run for Your Life" in ages. I'll come around. I promise.

All my other Charmed fics are stand alones.

Redemption series:

1) Redemption
2) The Demon's Advocate
3) So Mote It Be
4) Fruit of My Womb
5) The Coleville Horror
6) Season of Hope
7) Cursed Be
8) A Matter of Balance (work in progress)

Comedy of Errors series:

1) Whose Power Is It, Anyway?
2) Roll the Dice, Run for Your Life (on hiatus)

Nip/Tuck

Pairings: Christian/Grace, always. All others may vary according to my mood.

As of now I only have own story ("Turning Point"), but I intend to keep on writing after I finish it.

Angel

"Half the Size, Twice the Trouble" is a response to Pyro’s “mini-Spike” challenge posted on the Chocolate Covered Strawberries board, and I don't plan on writing a sequel. My Charmed "Redemption" series already has an ocasional cross-over with BtVS/Angel, so I plan on dealing with my plot issues there.
