STORY STATS

New- New Erebos: Shizuru is looking for nothing else except a fresh start in her new home. She finds herself within the embrace of a dangerous organization and on a catapult to fame. She is bombarded with lies and power struggles. Within her reach is everything she's dreamed of and more but...what is the true cost? And, was she even in control of what choosing her own life?

Shizuru struggles to find a place in New Erebos and within her new "family". Her passion for her art keeps her excited and moving but that passion soon finds the eyes of the Fangslinger, Natsuki Kruger. There are tastes of power and prosperity but trade off can be more than she bargained for.

A work based on the aspects of noir film and fiction with strong gangland elements. The Sinfonia and the metropolis of New Erebos enter a new era, centuries after the world was flung into state of chaos and depravity.
