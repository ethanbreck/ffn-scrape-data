Well, hello... Welcome to my profile, I suppose. I don't really have much to tell you; I'm a huge Doctor Who fan, well New Who anyway, so I'm not exactly hardcore but I do love watching it over and over again. I'm currently writing my own epic Doctor Who Saga called The Time Child about a girl called Danielle Fielding who sort of travels with the Doctor. The link is just down there *points downwards* if you want to check it out. No pressure or anything, but if you do and you like it drop a review, and any questions you may have throw them my way and I'll be happy to answer them :)

Anyway, I may add more things to this if and when they come to me, but for now enjoy! :)

My tumblr account is also dannifielding, for those who are interested. I try and answer anything sent to me, plus I tend to talk about the more spoilery stuff over there. So be warned XD

I also post stories *cough*smut*cough* on there that I don't on here, so if you go there and check the sidebar, then you'll be able to see what I post :)

It is also a good place to look for the things that people have made/written me. Check them out and send them all the love for it.

Basically, I live on Tumblr, so get over there and say hello!!
