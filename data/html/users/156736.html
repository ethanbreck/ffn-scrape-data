Rest In Peace Richard, you will always be our favorite Albus Dumbledore, we love you!

Hello all you wonderful people out there who have now wandered into the miraculous world of me, citywitch110. Well, now that I've said that, I am in a bit of a slump right now and as you can see the last time I updated my story was in March. I've discovered I like reading fics more than I like writing them which is ok, some people like reading more, some like writing more and most like both. As you can see if you looked at my fave stories list, I LOVE Harry Potter fics and I encourage you to read all of the stories I have there because they are the best of the best of the best, etc. Hopefully i will find my muse soon and get out of this dead end so i can focus on a really good story. But until then I will just have to continue my search through the bazillion HP fics and find as many as I can to keep me occupied until by creativity comes back. I hope you have enjoyed my rantings and as Porky the Pig would say, "That's All Folks!"
