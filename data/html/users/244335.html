Author has written 8 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, and Teen Titans.

Geeze, how long has it been?!

Anyway, sorry I haven't update most of my fics here. Lot's of other issues have taken priority and I've had so many other ideas running around in my head screaming for my attention, things like fanfiction have sadly fallen to the wayside...

But I AM trying! I have a 'Teen Titans' fanfic posted up for those fans. It's a mature one-shot of Beast Boy and Terra, one of my favorite couples. I hope you fans of the animated show (namely those of you who are old enough to read mature works) will take the time to read and review it.

Hopefully, now that that story is dealt with, I can focus on other stories I'm working on.

Now, to address a question someone asked in a review;

The website TitansGo. net, which has been around for 8 years and providing news and updates of the animated series, along with an oekaki, forums and boasting a host of info such as character bios, screenshots and transcripts of the show, will be shutting down as of December 1st. So the forums, oekaki, transcripts, everything will be gone as of that date.

I find this extremely disheartening, especially with the new animated Teen Titans shorts coming out in 2012! Talk about timing...

So if there is something on the site that you want to save, do it quickly, because after December 1st, it's gone. So go now and grab whatever you can.

Screenshots and info on the new series (which features our favorite teen heroes in chibi form!) can be seen here.

Also; a preview!

BUT! There IS hope!

Some members, who were as disappointed as I that the site was shutting down, took it upon ourselves to make a new place! It's called TGN-Memory and its a temporary set-up to what (we hope!) will be a promising, fun site! Follow the link here.

I hope all you Titans fans will take the time to look and maybe sign up and be part of our community!

See ya soon! ^_^
