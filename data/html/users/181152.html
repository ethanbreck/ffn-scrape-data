Hello to you all!

I decided to update my bio...finally. So here goes.

I'm a 15 year old girl in grade 10, who is absolutely obsessed with anime and Japanese culture in general, as well as Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, and about 50 other assorted genres. ^_^' My favorite anime will always be Gundam Wing (of course, as you can tell by my list of stories) with...many others coming in close second.

I have a few bad habits, which I won't give out here, but my worst is not updating...or rather, not writing any more on stories until I get struck by inspiration or bitten by an evil plot bunny. (Usually one of the pink or black variety.) I realize many of my stories haven't been updated in, like, forever, *coughelvespilotgundamscough* but I'm really trying. However, I'm not usually one of those people who just sits down and trys to write SOMETHING in continuation, I won't add to fics until I'm struck by an evil plot bunny. So it can take a while to update.

Let me introduce you to my muses:

Domen: A miniscule, four-legged demon with extremely pointy (if small) fangs, I met him when writing my first evilly sadistic fic, Death's Rampage. I experienced a pain in the back of my hand, and when I looked down, he was clinging to it, his fangs sunk into my skin. I later would come to realize that he is my evil/sadistic muse, who will kill plot bunnies, infuse himself with their ideas, then bite me and transfer the plots to me. However, he only will attack black plot bunnies- the others he tends to try and destroy, as they are either pink/purple (romance/tragedy types) or flourescent green/deep purple (insane-type humour/fantasy adventure, respectively), both of which are genres he abhors.

Colby is my romance tragedy muse, who is also an alter ego of the guy I'm in love with. (literally). He helps he out with my romance/tragedy type ficlets, at least until the point they cross over into angst, at which point Domen takes over. Domen absolutely hates him- considering he is al;l about the romance, it is not surprising.

Kyliandria is an Elvish priestess who can be found in most of my fantasy/LOTR fics as my alter ego. (None of those fics have been posted yet.) She is a high priestess of Rivendell and has a tendency to get a little sugar-high. (She was the muse responsible for When elves Pilot Gundams.)

Well, that's just about it. Below is the order in which I will try to update, from top to bottom priority:

Set Me Free
Love Knows No boundaries
Of Death, Love, and Enemies of Old
When Elves Pilot Gundams

As well, I take fic requests as long as they are in a fandom I am familiar with, and preferably dark romance/tragedy types. I am also looking for someone to do a Gundam Wing/Harry Potter crossover with. You can email me at the address above, or use Dracos_Girl@mugglenet.com (I think that's it, anyways)

I am also a huge Duet Maxwell supporter (as she is written by Tori Yuki Ichimura- RNDM is my life!) now will you put me in a story

Ja ne and many hugs!!
Tseecka Akeunah
