THE KH SURVEY

(Please copy and paste this onto your profile and answer the questions! Axel says to spread the KH fever!)

SECTION ONE: The "Favorite" Questions

1. Your favorite KH guy?

Sora... then Riku... then Axel and Roxas... then Demyx... Gah!

2. Your favorite KH girl?

Namine

3. Your least favorite KH guy? Why?

DEFINITELY Diz. What a self-righteous @&). I don't care that he SEEMED nice at the end.

4. Your least favorite KH girl? Why?

Alice from Wonderland. No particular reason, I just hate that movie.

5. Favorite World? (Includes both KH1 and KH2)

I'll say Land of Dragons, because it was the best place for leveling up. Most of the worlds were equally fun.

6. Least Favorite World?

Atlantica and Wonderland, hands down. They are such a pain in the first game. XD

7. Favorite Weapon? (Includes both KH1 and KH2)

I nabbed Oblivion as SOON as I found it in KH. Especially after Kairi was doing her insecure should-we-trust-Riku dance. After that I was all "Oh, I am SO using Oblivion instead of Oathkeeper!" Also, the Ultima Weapon is very, very awesome.

8. Least Favorite Weapon?

Well, I didn't use all of the weapons, but I didn't dislike any of them.

9. Fav. Summon? (includes both KH1 and KH2)

Stitch is pretty awesome. Not to mention the cutest.

10. Fav. Form? (aka. Sora's Forms)

Final Form, Valor being close second. But I only used the forms so I could level them all up to 7 and be the MOST awesome as my ordinary self! I mean... Sora's ordinary self. XD

11. Favorite Pairing? (includes yaoi coupling) Why?

SoRiku and Akuroku. Adorableness, complex dynamics, and hotness. Need I say more?

12. Least Fav. Pairing? (includes yaoi couples) Why?

Any animal-like Disney characters with human characters. Seriously, what the heck?

13. Any cool crack pairings you've heard of? List 'em.

Most of the Org. XD Demyx/Xigbar, Demyx/Saix, Demyx/Luxord, Marluxia/Xemnas (thanks to OrgLIX XD), Xemnas/Saix... there's billions, really. Also, Demyx seems to be the Organization's b!tch. XDD

14. Weirdest Pairing(s) You've Ever Heard Of?

Riku/Mickey. -_-;

15. Any "Kh-pet-peeves" you have?

Diz being a huge jerk, and how often Kairi is bashed needlessly... and her RIDICULOUSLY girly Keyblade. (Seriously, WTF?)

16. Fav. Partner in KH? (includes both KH1 and KH2)

Riku, definitely. He and Sora just work so awesomely (I'm MAKING it a word XD) together. Plus they're adorable. I squee.

SECTION TWO: Do you believe it, or not believe it?

17. Do you believe in the Xemnas/Saix theory?

I guess it could happen - I wouldn't be SURPRISED if it did, but I don't quite get that vibe from them. EVERYone in the Org can't be gay. XD

18. Do you believe that Zexion is emo?

He definitely has the Emo-hair. I wouldn't be surprised if his Somebody was emo. But, no, I don't think of him as emo, just a very contemplative person.

19. Do you believe that Marluxia is gay?

Crack!Marly is DEFINITELY gay - canon Marly could go either way, I think.

20. Do you believe that Kairi is the most annoying character in KH?

Not at all. Kairi, ESPECIALLY considering that she's a plot device, is an AWESOME character. So she's a teeny bit selfish in the first game. SHE'S A PRE-TEEN GIRL WITH A CRUSH. It's incredible that that's all she did. PLUS, she acknowledges gracefully that she'd be in Sora's way (+10 points) though she still wants to help (+5 points). Then she grows emotionally by the next game and MAKES SURE that Sora finds Riku. And kicks Heartless butt since she's there anyways. More people need to give Kairi props.

SECTION THREE: Answer Yourself!

21. If Roxas had to choose either Namine or Olette, who would you root for? Why?

If he wound up with anyone besides Axel, I think it would be Namine. Except that I see their dynamic as very familial. So that doesn't work. But I can't see him with Olette... gah. Whatever.

22. What's your theory on KH: Birth by Sleep?

I haven't really thought much about it - I imagine I'll find out after it comes out and fans crack the code.

23. Was Chain of Memories a waste of time?

Not really, I think it's interesting for Organization XIII backstory. I hate the way Quinton Flynn interpreted Axel's character in it.

24. If you had the choice of meeting ONE (and ONLY ONE!) KH character, who would it be?

Demyx... because the poor dear needs a hug. ;_;

I'd say Axel, but I can't help unless I'm Roxas. XD

25. Which KH character do you relate to the most? Why?

I think I can relate to Sora a lot, actually. I care a lot about my friends and like looking at things optimistically, and I always try as hard as I can to do the right thing. Plus I'm short. XD

26. What's the most embarrassing moment that ever happened to you that had something to do with KH?

Um. No comment. XD My only story has spoilers for "Homecoming".

27. Have you ever cosplayed as a KH character? If so, who? If not, who would you like to cosplay as?

I've never cosplayed, but it would be so cool to cosplay as Axel. It wouldn't be as fun as it could be with a Roxas, though... =3

28. The Funniest Moment in all of KH would be...?

THE funniest thing? In Space Paranoids, where Sark goes "You're very persistent, Tron." and Tron goes "I'm also BETTER THAN YOU! 8D" and then Tron!Pwns Sark right in the face with that disk! XD Priceless. You don't expect it because he's so humble at first.

29. The Hardest Enemy/Boss was...?

Demyx the second time. SERIOUSLY. Axel was the second hardest for me. But I had to beat them both TWICE because my game crapped out without my having saved ANYTHING right after I beat Demyx after trying, like, 40 times (I'm stubborn with memory cards). @_@

I'll put Sephiroth here once I beat him.

30. What was a good edition in KH2 that made it oh-so-addictive?

Better system, graphics and storylines... but KH is better for forcing you to use strategy. KH2 was a lot more fun AFTER finishing KH.

Note: You MUST only choose one! "Both" or "Neither" in unacceptable!!

31. Hayner or Pence?

Pence is adorable (especially with Ollette) But Hayner's pretty cool, too.

32. Zexion or Marluxia?

Zexion

33. Riku or Roxas?

Riku, but Roxas is such a close second!

34. Roxas or Sora?

Oh, god, MUST you kill me in this way? ;_;

Sora, but I LOVE ROXAS. I'm sorry, Roxy!

35. Axel of Demyx?

Axel. glomps Demyx I'm sorry, Dem Dem! D=

36. Kairi or Larxene?

Kairi

37. AkuRoku or SoRiku?

I DEFY THE RULES OF THIS SECTION. BOTH, I SAY.

38. Namixas or Namiku?

Namiku if we're talking about Repliku. Poor guy - poor Namine, too. They could be happy together.

39. Zemyx or AkuRoku?

AkuRoku.

40. SoKai or SoRiku?

SoRiku.

41. Sea Salt Ice Cream or Paopu Fruit?

Paopu fruit, cause I'm a romantic. =3

But OMG, Sea Salt Ice Cream is SO FUNNY. It's like the replacement for sex. 358/2 SPOILER AHEAD: "Axel, I've been eating ice cream with someone else..."

42. Cloud or Leon?

Leon. He's less whiny. :) But I like what I've seen of Cloud in Crisis Core clips on youtube. That's about all I know of the FF series.

43. CloTi of Clerith?

CloTi. Zerith all the way. I can't believe Zack isn't in KH. That's a travesty. TT_TT

44. Simple and Clean or Passion?

Simple and Clean, I suppose.

SECTION FIVE: The Last Section!!

45. List all the KH character you've fallen for. (This includes Final Fantasy charcter as well)

I don't fall for any characters. XD But I do adore Sora, Riku, Axel, Roxas, and Demyx.

46. What crossovers would you like to see with KH?

It would be SO AWESOME for KH characters to meet up with the cast of Tsubasa! I'd be very psyched for that! Besides that... hmm... Finding Nemo would be cute. grin

47. Does anyone in KH look like another character? List 'em all!

Roxas looks similar to Cloud and Ven. Axel looks a little bit like Reno. That's about all I know of, though.

48. Which new KH game can you abosolutely NOT wait for?

358/2 DAYS! I'm sittin' on pins and needles for that one.

49. Do you like KH1 or KH2 better? Why?

I love KHII and think that it's DEFINITELY the best... but I also enjoyed KH.

50. LAST QUESTION! What makes Kingdom Hearts one of the best games in the world!?

The character and story designs, the graphics (for KHII, at least XD), DISNEY (=D), and definitely the yaoi/WAFFy best friends. X)
