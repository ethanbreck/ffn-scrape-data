Hello All!

Right now I am NOT writing new chapters for either "The Wrong One" or "Son of a Death Eater."

However, I am revising "Son of a Death Eater," for three reasons:
~ The first two or three chapters are filled with odd symbols. Click on it and you'll see what I mean.
~ It's not up to date with "HP and the Order of the Phoenix," the fabulous 5th part to JK Rowling's series.
~ The writing is rusty. I uploaded the last chapter over a year ago when I knew almost nothing about writing well. Now, I know more.

Note that although I do not have "writer's block" (which doesn't exist), I am NOT adding a new chapter. Sorry, folks.
But there WILL be new and different content in order to tally with OotP.

And, by the way- due to lots of homework, etc. it might be a VERY long time before I've revised the whole story- maybe months, I don't know.
