Grindylow: Hello! I'm Grindylow, and this is my friend Hinkypunk! We are the colaborated version of Lovely Lily and MaluElvellon. We are insane. Huh Hinkypunk?
Hinkypunk: Yeppers! Complete basket cases!
Grindylow: I never really understood the term "basket cases"... what a strange selection of words to mean crazy... It kinda reminds me of the Easter Bunny when people say "basket cases"... whoever made that up should get sat on.
Hinkypunk: You're right! That is kind of irritating... heehee, the Easter Bunny makes me think of chocolate... *drool*
Grindylow: Any-who, we are not in a basket... but we are quite crazy.
Hinkypunk: Heehee! I don't wanna be a chicken I don't wanna be a duck, so I shake my butt!!! *does chicken dance*
Grindylow: alrighty then... getting back to business. Our stories are not for the normal, but the insane.
Hinkypunk: I'm OUT-sane! See, you'd think that if you were IN-sane, that you'd actually be sane, but if you were not sane you would really be OUT of sane, so therefor, you'd be OUT-sane!
Grindylow: Magnificent! What a wonderful discovery... from now on we will use the word out-sane, to stand for what we so recently called insane... teeheehee. By the way we're not very good spellerers (and yes, I mispelled speller on purpose!)
Hinkypunk: Though I am a freak when it comes to punctuation... it MUST be done RIGHT!!
Grinylow: Yeah, that's really the only irritating quality you have... next to your raging temper... and constant complaining... but that's it...
Hinkypunk: RAGING TEMPER!?!? WHAT DO YOU MEAN 'RAGING TEMPER'?! *looks at what she just typed* Oh... wait.. teeheehee, sorry!
Grindylow: Right... moving on... well maybe you've heard enough about us. What about you?! You can review our stories and let us know just how you are doing... if we haven't sent you away screaming yet... BWAHAHAHA * laughs evily*
Hinkypunk: *joins in the laughing like a maniac* BWAHAHAHAHAAAAAA!
Grindylow: pssst... you like me better, right? teeheehee.
Hinkypunk: Are you trying to turn the nice reviews against me?
Grindylow: Hey! You weren't supposed to be reading that!
Hinkypunk: Well, I did!
*they leave room arguing insanely- Oops! I mean OUT-sanely.*
