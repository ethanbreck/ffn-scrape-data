So, I'm Kaya Wolfclaw.™

For things that interest me. I love wolves. They're amazing creatures, and it upsets me to no end that humans don't respect them. I LOVE video games, call me a FPS gamer, I mostly have all the Halo Games, Dead Space 1 & 2, Left 4 Dead 1 & 2, BioShock 1 & 2, and CoD Black Ops and World at War. I love reading, mostly sci-fi and manga but I think my manga days are well behind me as newer anime doesn't hold much of an appeal anymore.

For my anime interest [as minimal as they are anymore] are Fushigi Yugi (all time favorite), Tenchi Muyo, FullMetal Alchemist, Fruits Basket, Kodocha, Sailor Moon, Dead Space: Downfall & Aftermath, Halo Legends, and Azumanga Daioh. My major interests in this category are more Fushigi Yugi, Dead Space and FullMetal Alchemist. I guess you can throw Red vs Blue in there too that is another favorite I adore. Music for me ranges to just about anything, I'm VERY diversified when it comes to music. I tend to stay away from modern rap though, I have mostly soundtracks and comedians on my iPod at the moment just because in class all day you need something to distract you from the monotone voice of lectures. I'm also in college as of right now for computer networking, sooo any comp problems you can ask. :3
