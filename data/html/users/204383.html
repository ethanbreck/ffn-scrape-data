What can I say? I LOVE TO READ. :) I love reading books, and when I'm not satisfied with books, I turn to fanfiction. I also like watching TV, and when I'm not satisfied with what I watch, then I also turn to fanfiction. Thankfully, my anime geek phase is over. It's been mangled by my Harry Potter inner geek. I am now also addicted to Western Pop Culture (though I really try to curb it, being an unhealthy thing, that obsession. Hee.) and how the entertainment business works, not only in the West but the whole world. I hate loving to watch American Idol, America's Next Top Model, Project Runway, Survivor, Amazing Race, and all the other reality TV shows I can watch out there. Tsk, tsk. It just amazes/baffles me to the core how these shows work. Too bad my choices of shows are limited here in my country (the Philippines).

I like to lurk. And I love, love, love reading weecaps/recaps/forums of shows from the Televison Without Pity website.

Check it out if you haven't yet. Love the snark.

I'm a college student, taking up Literature (English) at a University here in my country. I love reading Classical Lit, and a bit of Pop Fiction here and there.

Here at FFNet, I specifically like reading stories and support ships from the following:

1. Harry Potter: Draco/Hermione (love the possibilities) Harry/Hermione (kind of predictable storylines, but sweet all the same and some of the greatest written fanfics ever come from their ship, along with Draco/Hermione) RemusorSirius/Hermione (also full of possibilities, but very rarely good). Basically, I love Hermione not with Ron or Snape.

2. Heroes: Claire/Peter - that was before I found out they were relatives. But hey, there's still AU, right?

3. X-Men: Kitty/Pyro - they're cute but rare

4. Flame of Recca: Fuuko/Mikagami

5. Rorouni Kenshin: Misao/Aoshi and Misao/Enishi (See White Tiger, Jade Concubine. It's great but sadly abandoned.)

6. Fushigi Yuugi: Yui/Suboshi or anything Yui-centered. I love her character possibilities.

7. Hana Yori Dango: Domyouji/Tsukushi/Rui

I tried writing a story (an HP fanfic, of all things), as you can see below; but that didn't work out too well. I am my worst critic, you see, so I could not write without thinking it had to be absolutely perfect - to the point, accurate and sensible. Then HP book 5 and 6 happened, which effectively crushed all my dreams and inspiration of continuing (not to mention book 7! And the Epilogue! Gah!); plus real life got in the way. And that was the end of my fanfiction writing career.

Some of my favorite fanfiction HP fanfic writers/stories of all time are:

Lissanne (spelling?) of the Love is a Crime & Love is a Battlefield series (Harry/Hermione) - remains unfinished until this time. Abandoned. Sad.

Melissa D of We'll Always Have Paris (Draco/Hermione).

Cassandra Claire of the Draco Dormiens, Draco Sinister & Draco Verita series (Draco/Hermione/Harry - Which I unfortunately was not able to finish reading before it was pulled off the internet. Argh.) Yes, there was some controversy about it and the author, but that's too heavy to contemplate for a light fanfiction reader like me. It's quite intriguing though, and meaty for a thing such as the FanDom Universe of Harry Potter. It boggled my mind. And also makes me quite sad because I think it's one of the reasons (aside from the significant story of a certain Scribe) enthusiasm for the fantastic universe of the HP FanDom significantly dwindled. If you want to find out what happened then, do some research on Google. Haha.

Lori of the Paradigm of Uncertainty, Hero with a Thousand Faces, & The Show that Never Ends series (Harry/Hermione) - also not ended properly though, just with a summary and some notes.

And they're not even here on FFNet! (They can all be found in the Schnoogle / Fiction Alley website: or dot org) Haha. Other stories that are here can be found on my Favorites List. :) I'm currently addicted to inadaze22's Broken (DHr) because it's so freaking brilliant, what with the drama, intrigue, and all that. Go read it if you haven't yet.

And! A friend of mine wrote the ever lovely Twist Through Time (HHr - also in my Favorite Stories). Check it out too if you want some Hermione/Harry or Hermione-centered time-travel action. And her Dare Me is modern AU, sort of parody, also HHr and can all be found in the Portkey website: . :D
