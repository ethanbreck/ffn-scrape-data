I'm pretty much on hiatus. Either ideas for my popular fics don't come to mind or that I'm trying to get a medical transcriptionist job.

Seeing as how school has started, I know that a lot of kids are scared or don't quite fit in. I too was like this. I'm going to put a poem that I recently wrote about my time at school and perhaps those kids can find someone in their corner.

You’re probably expecting something sappy

Something that melts or makes me happy.

How abouts would I know?

A skull, a poem, a black crow?

Hearing that final school bell?

The answer I have, I cannot tell.

Could you set me free

Turn my darkness into glee?

People sneer, bait and drain

Laugh, snicker and cause pain

They leave my voice in a strangle

While trying to undo the social tangle.

So have fun, have a ball!

While I wither, within the wall.
