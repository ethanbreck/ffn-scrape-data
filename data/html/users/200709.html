What to write?

I'm thirthy something...I have been reading fanfiction since my introduction to the internet (too long ago). I have not written, well rephrase that, I have not published in over six years. A few weeks ago I got bitten again. I do not know how it will go... Wait a see.

For those of you reading or waiting for me to finish 'Untold Future, Untold Past', I do not know. It was started between book 4 and 5 of Harry Potter and after reading The Order of the Phoenix (not my favourite) I could not keep my mind in my story. I still have the ending written. Yes, much like JK. But the chapters inbetween are in bits and pieces.

English is not my native language, mistakes will be made, I can always use the help (how else will one learn) So I apologize in advance.

That you will/will not like the stories, can't help you there... tastes do differ! The mind works in mysterious ways...
