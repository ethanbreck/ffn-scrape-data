Why hellooooooo. I don't write anymore. I'm really quite terrible at weaving a story. The subtleties of plot construction are completely beyond my comprehension. However! I'm fairly adept at identifying grammatical errors and errors in characterization. If you're looking for some help with that, I'm the person to turn to ].

If you're here because I left you an unpleasant review, understand that I left it with the best intent. I don't try to be mean. I want people to know I'm actually paying attention and I want people to consider the quality of what they're posting. A mindless review does nothing for an author except reaffirm that nothing could be improved upon. A real writer should strive for constant improvement--and pointing out the most egregious faults shows an author where they need to focus their energies first and foremost. As said, I'm not trying to be mean and I really try to get that across when I review.

I do occasionally feel the need to suffice my appetite for HPDM slash or anything Severitus/Sevitus's challenge. Lately I've been more on the SS/HP track; I've sort of dropped Severitus in the meanwhile because it's too awkward going from father to lover. o_O abused!Harry is probably my favorite Harry... but it's getting pretty difficult to find anything original on that tract. I don't even need it to be SUPERsuper new, I just want something well-written T_T. That isn't too very much to ask, is it? Make me cry! Haha. Seriously though, I love a good tearjerker.

I used to go by chochang913. If you knew me from that era, I am truly and deeply sorry. I was an obnoxious little brat. Sometimes I glance through old reviews and cringe in shame.

I'm 23 now (2012) and I've been reading fanfiction on this site for over a decade. I would feel a bit embarrassed... but... nothing tugs the same heartstrings as my beloved HP series. I graduated from uni with a bachelor's in Bio/Psych last year and am currently employed at Children's Division.
