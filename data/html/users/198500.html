Author has written 26 stories for Final Fantasy X, Chrono Trigger, Lunar: Eternal Blue, Kingdom Hearts, Suikoden, Final Fantasy: Tactics, Final Fantasy I-VI, Final Fantasy VII, and Naruto.
Things change, as does my profile.

I've cranked out several stories, wondering if it's all worth it. I mean, sure I don't have nearly the writing expertise such as people like Nanaki, Mox Jet, Weilia, and Mintbaby (to name a few), but I know each person has their own unique style.

What's mine? Well, I think about what could have been, what a character may have turned out like had the inclusion of a tiny difference completely changed their outlook. One such example of this is Altered Destiny, one of my many Final Fantasy fics, though not the only one about a certain silver haired swordsman.

My best work? I consider some of my best writing to be stories like Runes and Revelations, The Cause of Death, Altered Destiny, For a Dark Knight, and Sin's Design, but I know my writing truly does not stop there.

Where does my writing stop? When the readers stop reading. Let me know you read, and let me know that you care to take the time to read whatever's on my mind, from Magus being angsty about turning Glenn into a frog, to Spekkio not being cheerful. Think it's crazy? Read a few stories and then think about it.

Feedback induces work, if more work is to be done, there must be more feedback. Maybe I'll start up my old Kingdom Heart's Fanfic: Heart of an Ocean of Stars, or finish up Cause of Death, who knows?
