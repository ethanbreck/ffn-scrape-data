So is Roman Dirge, author of the Lenore comics. Gotta love the little dead girl. ^_^ Oo, can't forget Nightmares and Fairy Tales. That's a new one - just bought the first one, but damn, is it GOOD!!

Okay. Now...anime! Inu-Yasha, Dirty Pair, Yu Yu Hakusho, Candidate For Goddess, Cowboy Bebop, the little I've seen of Vampire Hunter D...I love them all! Though, admittedly, Inu-Yasha especially. I'm looking into getting Evangeline and Escaflowne (The DVDs are sold for twenty-five to thirty FUCKING BUCKS!! What the FUCK?!?), because what I've heard about them is damn good. I also want to check out everything else my Animerica magazine talks about. ^_~ Or, most of it, at least. ^_^

Now, onto...Normal types of music! ^_~ Offspring. Boy Hits Car. Blind Guardian. Sum41, Lifehouse, SOME of Eminem, some Avril Lavigne, Good Charlotte, all the soundtrack stuff of Final Fantasy. One of my favourite songs is 'Eyes On Me.' I don't know who it's by, but I think it's from Final Fantasy Eight. ^_^

Which leads me to my games! Playstation Two rocks. ^_^ Simply. Currently I've obbsesed with Kingdom Hearts, and I'm somewhere near the end, I believe. Before that was Final Fantasy Ten, whch Is still, by far, the BEST MOTHERFUCKING GAME out there! Although, to be sure, Kingdom Hearts comes VERY fucking close.

Yes. I like to say 'fuck.'

I also have Final fantasy Seven and Eight, though I have not played them yet. I also have Draken, but I'm not overly fond of that. Oh, I've got Teken Four, too, but I don't really play that unless I need a two-player game. Still pretty cool, though - escept for the fact that I CAN'T FUCKING SAVE WHEN I UNLOCK NEW FIGHTERS!! Grr grr, I say. _
