Author has written 7 stories for Chronicles of Narnia.

I'm returning to the fandom community after a rather long absence, this time primarily working in the world(s) of The Chronicles of Narnia. And, really, the first time of seriously writing anything, too.

More info about my published stories and of my next project, titled Fuel to Fire, can be found at my LiveJournal page. (It seems they have removed the link... I have the same moniker over there!)

All stories are of the same Dichotomy universe. It is preferable that the stories are read in order of publication, and not chronologically. I do have a master plan for the entire series and those following the entire arc.

However, as a novice at all this, I am hoping to try out different styles, and yes, different ratings. Not every story will be everyone's cup of tea...but to each their own, and I hope to put out a little something for everybody. Eventually.

It is recommended the stories are read in this order:

1. Dichotomy (March, 1008; March, 1948)

2. Splintered (April, 1012)

3. Hail to the Days (December, 1009)

4. Forgiveness (October, 1947)

5. Interregnum (October, 1947)

6. The Ambassador (April-June, 1012)

7. Windmills and Walls (July, 1940) NEW PIECE 9/15!!

Thanks so much for stopping by! I'm still new at this and would appreciate any and all feedback, insight, whatever!
