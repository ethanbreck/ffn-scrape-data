Hey everyone in FanFiction land! My name is Liz and I'll be your host for this insightful bio!

Name: Liz, just in case you didn't know

Age: Most likely either older or younger than you; but I'll be nice and say between 18 and 25.

Any other details, you will have to ask me personally, cuz I'm too lazy to type them right now.

Besides personal details, like height, hair color, and active sex life, there are few things I feel like sharing with the world.

One, I'm usually hungry. I don't have any brothers, so in retaliation, I got an appetite like a guy. My mother often wonders about it.

Also, I've been dancing most of my life, and before you get any of the wrong thoughts, I mean ballet dancing. Geez.

Three, penguins rock my world.

Four, I collect cheap toys, like from Happy Meals and the like. My day was made when Burger King started selling those DBZ toys again. I have a Goku so far, but I plan on collecting the entire series.

My favorite DBZ couples are Gohan/Videl and Trunks/Pan, although lately the idea of Bra/Uubu has been sounding fun. I don't really write lemons, although I suppose I could if I wanted to, and I don't like yaoi at all. I'm not one of those people who will pledge against, and I don't really care if people write it, but I have no interest in reading it.

My other favorite animes are pretty much everything, with the exception of Gundam. Doesn't matter which Gundam, I don't really like any of them. But if I had to list them, my favorite animes would be: Excel Saga, Love Hina, Evangelion, Cowboy Bebop, Trigun, Fushisi Yugi, and some more I can't remember.

Best moment of life (kinda): The day I saw a cloud shaped like Goku. I'm not joking. Even my boyfriend thought so. I would've killed for a camera.

Random notes: Everyone should watch independent movies. They have alot going for them. Especially if they are about Irish men killing in the name of God (Boondock Saints. Great movie.)

Lint brushes are a Microsoft conspiracy to take over the world. You have been warned.

Don't be fooled by deodorant that says goes on clear. Yes, it will go on clear, but it comes off white as anything else.

I do believe that is all. I would apprectiate it if you would read any of my stories now, even if they say they are out of commision. I'm just too lazy to fix it. :)
