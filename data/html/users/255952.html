I am a yaoi fan (thanks to llamajoy and Tenshi of bishonenink, I read my first yaoi on their site - FF8). My favourite pairings are in this order, two in the number one spot, 1. Squall/Zell, Aya/Youji, no exceptions. 2. Heero/Duo 3. Cloud/Vincent 4. Sephiroth/Vincent. So if there is a pairing I am going to write about you can bet it will be one of them.

I will also tend to write in favour of Zell, Youji or Duo because I am obsessed with them. They are all my Angels, Zell is the Angel of sunshine, Youji the Angel of spring and Duo is my innocent Demon.

Like Squell I'm not into threesomes and will never write one or read one for that matter. They don't do a thing for me. Me I am boring but I read anything that has the above pairings and there are so many good fanfic writers out there that always keep me satisfied.
