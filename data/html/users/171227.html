Author has written 28 stories for Harry Potter.

About my fics: Mostly romance and a lot of oneshots. I like writing about characters who aren't frequently written about. Sometimes, though, I'll fall into the LJ trap and write about them. If you don't know who the main characters are in one of my stories check the top information and it'll usually say where it has the title. If there are no characters listed then just pick whoever you want:)

Fics I read: I love romance. No slash and no super harry. I really like the major AU fics that take place in different time periods or kingdoms and stuff like that. I also like fics that are based on other movies/books. You know like following the story lines of Phantom of the Opera, the Notebook, Titanic, etc. I also like the next generation ones. I think some really good ones will come out because of the epilogue of DH. I'm really interested in any romance of the new Malfoy. If you know of any fics like these please suggest them to me! I'm always looking for a good read!

Fave ships: LE/JP, HG/RL, GW/DM

I'll read all ships EXCEPT: HP/GW (the book is enough for me), HP/HG and all slash pairings
