Hello to all. Allow me to introduce myself, I am Ice Eyes *bows*. For all you curious readers I am currently editing Where Loyalty Lies and working on the last ten or so chapters. Hopefully there will be a new one in a couple of weeks or so. I apologize for my mostly atrocious writing skills and generally awful and transparent plot line. I know it's bad, but I also don't care. I had fun writing it, and honestly, I still do. But we aren't here to win a Pulitzer now are we?

I realize it's been a long time since I updated and I offer you an explanation of sorts. I fell in love and then I got my heart broken. Then I dropped off the face of the earth. Then I got busy with college. Then I got out of the habit of writing. I fell in love again. But between paying rent and writing papers, college killed my creative process.

Now I have a BA in English and I'm in my last year for my master's in Library Science and I'm getting back into writing. I guess the best place to start is right where I left off.

Other than that I have a wonderful finance, we're getting married next summer. An adorable cat, Lilanthe. Who is not Gem for those of you who have followed WLL. Gem passed about five years ago. Poor dear. :(
