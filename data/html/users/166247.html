Author has written 36 stories for Power Rangers, Angel, and Smallville.

Name: "Hey you" seems to be what I'm called the most recently.

What I write: I suppose I could be considered a slash writer. It's what I do, or did. Don't really write much anymore.

Bio: Right so I felt it was time to update all this jazz. Geez I’ve been a member of this site since 02, been writing for much longer though. Sadly my early stories truly suck, the characters are rather out of character and the writing leaves much to be desired, but I like to think I’d gotten better over the years and on occasion I find the drive to go back and fix some of the older ones that are really a mess.

TV: I don’t really watch much TV so I can’t give favorite TV shows, and on shows I do watch it’s only one or two specific seasons that I like; for example MMPR and DinoThunder (only because of Tommy and Kira really) where ‘Power Rangers’ is concerned, and season 5 of ‘Angel’ (only because of Spike). Was never really into Smallville past season 2, though I was rather fond of Lex.

Movies: I tend to prefer horror, you'd think with the sappy crap I used to write I'd like those romance chick flicks, but really I don't at all.

Favorite Stories I've Written: It would have to be 'The Zedd Chronicles'. I'm well aware it's a little odd with the focus being on Zedd and Tommy, but I really do feel even if it's not my best work it was the series I had the most fun writing. Though the third installment 'A Red Delusion' was basically a space filler. The stories were as follows if anyone cares:

The Zedd Chronicles;

'A Green Nightmare' (complete)
'A White Dream' (complete)
'A Red Delusion' (complete)
'A Black Reality' (complete)
'A Purple Illusion' (complete)

Stories I've Decided Not to Finish: A lot. Sorry, just not really my thing anymore. I don't mind helping out someone else, and if someone wants me to I don't mind turning out a story on request, but not that into my own stuff anymore and too busy now a days to bother with it. Still though enjoy what I have done if you like if it can amuse someone I'm happy to leave it up.
