Author has written 3 stories for Sailor Moon X-overs, and Harry Potter.

Hey everyone, I'm 19, a freshman at Rice University in Houston, TX, and a member of Brown College. I love to swim and write or read stories. My favorite animes are Sailormoon, Dragonball-Z-GT, Slayers, Tenchi-muyo, Gundam Wing, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Ranma 1/2, Bubblegum Crisis, Ah! My Goddess, Revolutionary Girl Utena, Princess Mononoke, and Yu Yu Hakusho. My fave manga are X/1999 (my all time favorite, it's really good, you people should read it!), Sailormoon (in my opinion the manga is better than the anime), and Fushigi Yuugi.

As you can see, this place is a little bare at the moment, but I'm working on that. I've got stories in progress posted below. I've finally finished my Sailor Jupiter fic, and that was a fun one, it's nice to see how much my writing has grown since the start of the story. As for the Harry Potter story, that just sort of hit me. I have no idea where my inspirations come from, but when I got the idea in my head, I couldn't stop until I had it written down. I think it sounded better in my head, but you know how those things go.

Updates (12-3-03) Updated my bio... yes I am still alive, however, my first college exams start next week, so those'll be occupying a lot of my time. But (and this is a big but) the semester's almost over and then I get an entire month off. Can you believe it? An entire month. Hopefully an inspiring month for all of our sakes.

Oh, go look at this one story I posted, New Moon. I was requested to do a SMxHP crossover, and this was what that developed into. Tell me what you think of the plot thus far, and then maybe I'll write some more on it.

Go check out my stuff at fictionpress.net (http://www.fictionpress.net/profile.php?userid=127827). I've worked really hard on all of them, and I'd love some input from ya'll.

Stories in the Works:

Baby Mine - I have seven chapters in this story written, but I'm not sure if I want to post it simultaneously with NM or not. It's a cross-over with DBZ and SM, and I hope you find it original and enjoy it as well. Expect it to hit you local posting areas sometime soon.

Fallen Angel - This one doesn't quite have the first chapter done, but I've got a nice outline and direction for it. I plan on this being my masterpiece, so don't expect any quick updates on it. I plan on several and frequent revisions. It's about a recently turned vampire in case you want a little background on it. This one will be going up at Fictionpress.Net by the way.

New Moon - This is my HP/SM cross-over, and right now, I've only posted a prologue (if you would even call it that, it's more like a teaser). You should be able to figure out the pairing from the story, and I'm sorry if it scares you away, but I'm a strange girl. I'm thinking of adding some slash in there too, but let me know what you think about the first part of it, and we'll go from there. If no one really likes what I have written, I may just give it up completely.

One last thing. I'm thinking of creating a yahoo group thingy for all my stories and maybe anything else anyone wants to talk about. So let's see a show of hands for people that would join or be interested in joining.

All inspiration is attributed to my muse, Miki Chen, these are for you.

--Writer's Fest * Silence's Siren
