A Little Bit About Myself...

Writing is my life. I write every day whether it's a little blurb or 6 paragraphs. For those aspiring writers, KEEP WRITING. The more you write, the better you'll become. Also, another key thing is to read everything you can. Step out of you box, read different genres, adapt to different styles, read with a dictionary, read things you wouldn't normally read...I can't stress enough how important this four letter word really is.

I am 23 years old. Gosh, I remember I became a member on this site when I was entering high school as a freshman. That's kinda funny. I used to be such a nerd (i love nerds :P). I'm currently working on my own novel right now. It is very precious to me and it is always on my mind, so a lot of the time I am preoccupied with that. I have a very interesting tendency to not update for a very long time, because I'm focused on writing my novel I plan to publish when it's finished. So please bear with me. I promise, although updates will be sporadic, I will NEVER give up on stories. Some (NOT the Stolen Trilogy) have been taken down to be revamped, but other than that, never think that you have been forgotten.

My New Years resolution is to try to update my stories more often (give it any where from one to two weeks, but do keep in mind I have other priorities to attend to).

Wanna keep in touch? Visit my blog: where you can get all the details about what I'm working on, the novel I'm currently working on and the fanfictions you love so much! Please join me! ;)

To get even more info on the STOLEN Trilogy or my novel, please visit me at http://www.facebook.com/seriouslyskye You can get all the latest hooblah on what going on in my world as well as little snippets of what's to come in STOLEN. You'll also be kept up to date with the current goings on of my original series The Mystical Knights Trilogy! (Think of it this way...you request me, and you'll get an update...)

Also, for I made a playlist for STOLEN! Give it a listen, my lovelies!!

I'm taking ideas for my SPEAK playlist! Shoot me a reply in a review!

Also, I'd be glad to have some betas on board. I really need all the help I can get, so if you're interested--and interesting--please give me a shout via email. That's 'bout it for now. I guess I'm just your average, crazy young adult with too many dreams and crazy things rambling through her head (sighs) Oh well, life is too short to be taken seriously...Enjoy and Happy Reading!

STOLEN TRILOGY--Book 2: SPEAK

Speak: Sequel to STOLEN Bella cannot deal with her trauma, so she takes a vow a silence. As she tries to come to terms with her past by herself, her family urges her to find solace with the ones she loves. Although in her heart Bella knows they are right, her mind just can't seem to shake the rigid fright that haunts her every move--until the day she receives the worst news imaginable. A vampire army is rising--and their bloodlust for Bella is undeniable. Can Bella find the strength that kept her going for so long so that she can learn to speak? Danger is lurking down every dark corner--and there is another that sings for Bella's blood...
