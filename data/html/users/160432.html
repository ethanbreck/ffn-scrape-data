"Who am I? A poet. What do I do? I write. How do I live? I live." -La Boheme OK, well, I attempt to write poetry that turns out to be decent some of the time. Anywho...am odd, slightly crazed teenager who is currently attempting to make fanfiction not take over her life. I read in several fandoms: Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Les Mis, RENT, Moulin Rouge, Star Wars, Buffy, AAR...and write in a few of those. I am a slash addict and still maintain that Severus/Remus is the BEST SHIP IN THE WORLD. I am also known to not update for, oh, several months. This is not because I want readers to hate me, but because I am a lazy bum. I also intend to write an original fiction short or novel of my own sometime. I hope.
