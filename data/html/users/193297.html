Author has written 1 story for Final Fantasy VIII.

In 2009 I began writing 'One Day You Will Ache Like I Ache' with a rather ambitious time line and plot. At chapter 8/9 life happened, as it does, and this writing was put to the way side.

Recently I have taken to re-reading some of my old favourite fan fictions on quiet nights. And this has gotten me geared up to re-writing and hopefully finally completing this fan fiction. Maybe some new readers will come across it, maybe some old. Everyone hates an unfinished fan fiction with potential (and I think mine has some!) So here's to a second crack at it.

If you have read the original of old, I have changed a number of scenes and chapters significantly. Nida's role in the story at the beginning is pointless and has been cut out. I've edited down some of the more overly dramatic scenes (not the tragedy though) so that they will fit better with the serious tone of the story.

If you've made it up to the rift in time chapters - yes I am a very big fan of the creative and artistic stylisations of the 'inner mind' in The Cell.

Also - if you are an active beta reader/good at editing hit me up - I try to hold off uploading new chapters until reviewing them twice, but I still find the odd mistake here and there after uploading - which I am sure annoys readers.
