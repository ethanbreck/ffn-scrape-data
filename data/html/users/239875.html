OMG. I just realized I haven't updated this in 3 years.

I am the best Procrastinator I know!! W00T!

In these past years, I've suffered, been overjoyed, overworked, lazy as can be, and bored.

Groovy huh?!

Yeah, I've only written 3 stories, but there's a reason for that. I have had writer's block. Really Bad. And now, I'm taking up as much space as I can, so it'll seem like I'm writing a lot.

I don't like rap or anything, but HELLO to my friends at drama and biology class, if you ever get on here, its me, LJ. (heh)

In the near future, I'd like to write more stories like my 'Bob' story, but please let it be known; it's only humor. I have nothing against gays, drug addicts, or the elderly. I know a lot of people like that, and that doesn't mean I am one. So nyah.

It's chocolate chip), pop bands, Paris Hilton, anything on MTV right now, (Except for Bam Margera. He's cute.) And, last but not least, INSOLENCE!!!

Thrift Stores are great, because you can do anything to the stuff, and not feel too bad about cutting it up! And Hot Topic is alright becauseusually there's a lot of cute guys there. You can get so wound up in trying to be more "Insert random label" than your peers, trying to listen to music you hate so you can fit into a label just so you can wear the clothes.
