Hello all! I’m Chrissie-chan! Some of you know me and some of you don’t, so here’s a bit about myself. I'm 20-ish and a university student, but if you met me you wouldn't believe that because I don't really look like it. I adore anime and manga, really I do. Some of my favorites are Magic Knight Rayearth, Card Captor Sakura, Angelic Layer, Real Bout High School, DearS, Rozen Maiden, Lucky Star, NANA, and Shinshi Doumei Cross. And there might be more in the future, who knows? I sure don’t.

About My Writing: Most of the fics I write are generally having to do with any series that I've really taken a liking to. I've (hopefully) improved my writing style since the last time I updated (which was nearly four years ago!), so I'm looking forward to being able to share new stories with everyone. Please read them and review! Reader feedback is important to me; it inspires me to write more and do better. That and I love hearing what people think about my writing and my characters.

Oh and if you'd like an update on any of my stories, just leave a review on the last chapter of that story, and I will get back to ya, k?

For your sake, I will finish the current version and have the revision available on my personal website, never mind my innaccurate author's note about it. -_-;; Hang in there all you AAA fans!
