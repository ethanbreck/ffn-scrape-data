Ihave now entered the great and barren wasteland known as college. I go to IUB here in good ol' Indiana. While I like to think of myself as a sophisticated college student (if there ever was one) I still remain true to my roots by loving Labyrinth! I don't know why, but I think ever since I was little I've always had an obsession for fantasy, and I guess Labyrinth was the perfect fantasy to me at the time. I've liked it forever. I don't really need to watch the movie anymore though, considering that I have it memorized. But I do every once in a while just so I won't go into withdrawals or something crazy like that. Here's a list of my favorite fantasy movies: The Last Unicorn, Willow, Labyrinth, Legend, The Princess Bride, Merlin, Alice in Wonderland (not the cartoon), Flight of Dragons, Wizard of Oz, Return to Oz, The Tenth Kingdom, Photographing Fairies, Snow White: A Tale of Terror, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and I can't think of anymore right now. Check them out if there's one you haven't heard of before. They're all great. Thanks for reading this, even though I can't even imagine why you'd be here in the first place. Oh, and also, if you are a fic writer of fantasy, feel free to email me and I will Beta Read your story for FREE. I wasn't even aware that other people charge money for that type of thing until recently, which I think is so dumb. But, that's just my opinion. Oh, and I'm fairly fluent in French, so if you want to send me a fic in that language, that's OK, too. I have stopped writing fanfics for awhile, due to the large amount of homework loaded onto me. *sigh* College isn't all it's cracked up to be. However, I have been lurking and reviewing, so if you need help with a story or just feedback, I would be more than happy to help.
