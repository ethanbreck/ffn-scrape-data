2-6-2011

It's been way to long since I have posted or said anything. I am not even sure if anyone even reads this to see if I am still around. On the off chance that I do have a fan or two here is a long overdue update on my stories.

My first priority for the year is going to be my story Unexpected the original is still up but the new version I think will be 100% better. More details longer chapters and it is being Beta by a new friend that I have been a fan of Cheska. This is a story that I have had on the back of mind for awhile and wanted to make better. The original was set only in SM universe, but for the re-write it had become a crossover with HP world.

Now I have read all the books and I have seen all the movies and I am not a fan of book seven so I will be shaking up the HP world. This story will however not have Usagi moving to Hogwarts and suddenly becoming a witch nor will it be a Harry/Usagi pairing. I hope that you all can give it a chance.

My other story The Secret is Out and the re write version will be placed on hold. I am not certain where I want to take this story when I first wrote I loved it, but now I'm not sure how I feel about it. Sorry to disappoint those that did like it.

I may in the next few days post a few of my written one shots. The ones I have are from the Marvel universe so if you like comics. Well I think I have written more now for this site since well forever. If anyone reads this and wants to talk feel free to drop me a PM or an email.
