My name is Christine, but everyone calls me Chri. Or Chri Chri. Or Hooker, if you feel so inclined. I'm a 21 year old student at the Douglas J Aveda Institute for cosmetology.

I've been obsessively reading fanfiction for a very long time, so please don't judge me for some of the random (and possibly shitty) things in my favorites list. They probably got added when I was 13 and I'm too sentimental to take them down. I write my own fanfiction, but I rarely post it. The stories that had been up have been taken down so my apologies to those of you who had them favorited. I've grown a lot in my writing and the quality was no longer up to my standards. Plus, does anyone actually read Dark Angel fanfiction anymore?

My obsessions are many and include but are not limited to: Twilight, Battlestar Galactica, Star Wars, Firefly, Lord of the Rings, Buffy the Vampire Slayer/Angel, Bones, One Tree Hill, and Dark Angel. Basically I'm the biggest nerd you'll ever meet.
