My favorite anime is Rurouni Kenshin and my favorite anime couple is Kenshin and Kaoru. I love writing fanfics about Kenshin and the gang. It's so much fun_

Okay, everyone knows that my first choice in an anime series is Rurouni Kenshin, but I also like: Naruto, DBZ and my newest anime Saiunkoku Monogatari. My fav character in Saiunkoku is Shi Seiran (hottie!!) and fav pairing is Shi Ryuuki/Kou Shuurei.

Arigatou for all the comments I have received on my stories and for all the helpful writing tips. I've used them to improve my writing style. Arigatou also to those who selected me as their favorite authors and to those who has visited my website.

P.S. I like to rotate my stories. So, if you don't see one of your favorite stories that I've written on ffnet, visit my website. All the stories that I have ever written are kept there.

Updates: Sorry for not updating my older ficcies but I got to a certain point within my stories where my brain just froze up. It was hard trying to come up with what I really wanted to happen in the story. I hate just writing anything just to get the story out because the story loses its flavor. Anyhoo, I will finish those stories eventually. So please be patient.
