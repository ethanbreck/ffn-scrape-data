One last note: if you read my fics, please REVIEW! Good or bad, reviews do help authors, such as myself, stay motivated. If you like a story, we need to know so we are motivated to finish said story quickly instead of the years some of us take to complete our works. If there is something left to be desired, let me know! I am always hoping to grow as an author and any constructive criticism is welcomed! You can even email it to me if you fear being publicly negative. I will not bite. I promise! I will mention, however, that flames will be completely ignored once I finish laughing at them. Point out legitimate complaints. Do not tell me that you simply did not like the story or flame it because you did not agree with my artistic vision. The only thing I will do is laugh at the review and keep on writing. xD Lol.

To those who received a review from me and are angry/upset: I did not leave the review to incite your anger. However, I feel if I read a story/chapter, it is only polite to leave some feedback for the author. I always try my best to point out the best parts of your writing and highlight a few areas where you can improve. I do this, because I wish to have the type of reviews for my stories. This site is not here to feed people's egos. This site is here to help us all learn and grow as writers. If you feel my review was unjust or you do not understand something I wrote, please send me a PM. If you are simply angry and want to be a brat, don't bother.

Peace and Out!
