Hello, I am Renegade Seraph. I've been MIA from the Final Fantasy VIII world for a while now, hijacked by other fandoms. However I haven't given up fanfiction, and when I'm not lurking on this pen-name, you'll find me over here: RedStockings

Currently I am super grateful to Ashbear, (and her gang) for pulling me back into the ff8 fandom via the 'Where I Belong' Challenge August 2012. My Entry is A Voice In The Dark currently only one chapter, but who knows? I was reluctant to take it further, since I fear if I did it would require a lot more plot planning, and as a result, might never be finished. But saying that, I was surprised after so long, how easy it felt to write Squinoa again. So, never say never.

As of now, will stalk my way through everyone else' submissions :D

Edit 23/8/12: I have now posted a continuation of A Voice in the Dark, called Sail into the Dark. :D

Note: I would like to thank everyone who has recently left feedback on my older works, I am very grateful. But I would like to ask you politely, not to spend valuable time pointing out suggestions or improvements which I could make to my writing, plot, grammar, ect. Simply because I will not be returning to re-edit those fics. I however welcome any critiques, ect on anything published After July 2012 and fics being updated. Thank you very much :D
