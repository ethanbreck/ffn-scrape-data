Sex: Female

Age: 27

Interests/fandoms: ShuuheixRenji, YumichikaxIkkaku from Bleach although I am no longer a fan of Bleach as a whole.
Riley and Zane from Degrassi. I'm loving Zane's confidence and Riley's tentativeness despite his aggressive nature.
Heero and Quatre from Gundam Wing. My first yaoi pairing.
Jackie and Hyde from That 70s Show
AkihikoxHiroki from Junjou Romantica. I haven't written anything with this pairing yet and there is hardly any of it out there but I believe that they are best suited for one another.

Music: I've been really enjoying J-Pop lately. I think it's because I don't understand it. I like the idea just enjoying the music without having to dedicate any effort to understanding the
context. I have little mental capacity to spare on analyzing other peoples lives.

Guilty Pleasure: Baseball. I used to hate it but it grows on you and I'm absolutely hooked now.

Pet Peeve: Flames but not in the way you expect. I don't agree with being rude to people but at the same time I don't agree with stopping people from voicing an opinion. If a reader doesn't like a story then they are entitled to say so. Preferably in a constructive manner but since the writer is posting the story on the internet, they really should be open to the fact that flames can happen and it's a part of being a writer. Even published authors have their share of flames. Sure it hurts but learning to brush it off is part of an authors development. It's about maturity.

Want to know something? Ask me.
