To your left, my three babies, they are my biggest love before any thing/one else

(previously known as George-Julianna-Fan1)
My Real name is Maya, and you can say i'm quite smitten with George Clooney (then again who isn't?), have been since I was 14 years old.

I'm currently in my Ugly Betty shipping stage, Mainly Daniel/Betty (in my heart I know they ended up together even if the ending of the show showed a more ambiguous resolution). I do also kindda like Daniel/Wilhelmina, but only as an AU fanfiction couple, I am very happy they didn't go down that path in the show, besides conner is much more suited for her character.
What else?.. ohh yea: Mama Meade rocks!!

I don't ship couples very easily, which is why my ship list is kind of short, but. When i finally do relinquish my loving i tend to ship that couple even after their show ends.

So, hands down with no competition whatsoever the number one ship in TV history and for me in general is: Doug and Carol from ER. you can say what you want, but they had, not only the most honest and electrifying chemistry, but their relationship was so beautifully written, that i never met an ER fan who didn't love this couple. you knew right from the pilot that there was something special about this two and as the show progressed and they got back together you couldn't help but want them to end up together. like Carol said, they are soul-mates.

The next ones are: Josh and Donna from The West Wing, Daniel and Betty AKA Detty, from Ugly Betty, Harry and Ginny - Harry Potter.
And I am slowly becoming more and more affectionate to the idea of Annie and Jeff from Community.
Other then that Detty deserves a big applause for being the first ship since Doug and Carol to awaken up my writing muses. and they have been working almost non-stop. in the last couple of months.

So sit back relax, I have a really long favorite list so enjoy it; and who knows maybe one day i'll post my stories on the website again. (BTW if you're wondering why i don't have any D/C fics in my favorites that's because all my favorite fics and authors have taken down their stories).
