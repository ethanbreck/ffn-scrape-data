I don't spend much time on here anymore, but I still get notifications for reviews and read and respond to each one.

Attention currently devoted to SlightlySmaller (born June 2014) and SignificantlySmaller (born December 2015), as well as writing original fiction. :)

I grew up with Harry Potter. I read the first book when I was 11, and probably the second as well. But then I had to start waiting for them to come out to read the next. So I read Harry Potter between ages 11 and 18. I AM the Harry Potter generation. Despite this, I wasn't completely obsessed with them (the way I veritably am now) until a few years ago.

I'm American, but I bought my copy of the series in Scotland. This leads to some strange inconsistencies the few times I quote...

I'm a prolific journal writer. And a bit obsessive about how I write in my journals. Actually, I'm a bit obsessive about a lot of things.

This story is what I am most proud of. Complete at 50,000 words.
