It's May 2016, six long years since I've updated anything, let alone my profile. If any of you for some reason came to check and see if there's been any activity here--well, I appreciate the thought!

For those of you who haven't, which I imagine to be most of you, my name's Amm. I used to write fanfiction and take on more challenges than I apparently could handle or finish. I lost interest, dabbled in RP for a few years, but started taking an interest in writing again. I may revisit some of the challenges I abandoned, as some of those fandoms are still near and dear to my heart, but I'd prefer not to get anyone's hopes up, so I won't make any promises.

Obviously (or should I say, hopefully), six years will do a number on my writing style. Hopefully it's changed for the better! I welcome all feedback, positive or critical.

And for those of you who PMed me over the years and never got a response: I apologize.

Projects Revisited (2016)
Big maybes/ifs, but here are a few writing challenges I may revisit. No promises; I tried to order them from most to least likely to be picked up again.

One Hundred Fandoms of Hell (various)
- Progress: 8/100
- Write 100 one shots/drabbles for 100 different fandoms. Since the stories aren't related in any way, in fandom nor character, I feel like I could pick this up again quite easily.

40 Nights: Shinichi and Ran (Detective Conan)
- Progress: 31/40
- I still love Detective Conan, plus this challenge was the one I got closest to completing. It may take some rewatching old favorites, and catching up on newer material . . .

30 Kisses: Jaden and Alexis (Yu-Gi-Oh! GX)
- Progress: 20/30
- Last year (2015) I got into Zexal and I loved it so much (although I still have to finish it), I entertained the idea of giving GX another chance. If I do, I may pick this up again. It was my work with the highest traffic as well, so I'm hoping to pleasantly surprise you guys? Maybe.
