August 8th, 2013 So it's been 5 years since I remembered Fanfiction.net even existed, much less that I had STORIES that I had written at one point in time. Therefore, I started to re-read them and was quite horrified with a few of them and will, in fact, be deleting them shortly but there were two or three others that I still had that twinge of "this could have potential" and I have, once again, started the long process of re-writing the stories. I'm still not a fabulous writer anymore than when I was 18-21 years old (I'm 29 now xD) but I do believe I'm better at my words and describing things. Still horrible at run-ons and crap though.. sucks.

Now, let me try to update these "bullets" the best I can without deleting TOO much.

Ahem.

a) I currently live in Fort Worth with my boyfriend and two super fat cats (spoiled). I pretty much love it; I love my life right now. I used to work for Wal-Mart for 7 years, then Toys R Us for 1 year (trust me, TRU isn't as grand as you'd think it was.. unless you like having management that are all retards, minus one or two gems), and now I've just started working the for MET (Merchandising Execution Team) for Home Depot. I'm only part-time so I get a LOT more time home now and I get paid more than when I was at either of the last jobs. Go figure.

b) Cleburne, Texas is the hometown I was reared in and parts of me love it but that time to move back is not now.

c) I have not role-played nor written anything concerning Remus Lupin in a very, very long time but that does not make him any less of a person in my head. He's still the voice of reason at the back of my head.. or the voice of rage when I'm angry (and ultimately he is too). We are one.

d) Outside of work, I don't do much outside of play Minecraft, play with my cats, cook, shower, sleep and play some other things (mainly Torchlight 2, Left 4 Dead, Guild Wars 2), or talk with the boyfriend.

e) I am very competitive but I'll always pretend that I'm not. It's, like, kinda a goal for me. Something for me to strive towards. But I don't speak about it to others so they can't do the "Heh, I knew you were going to fail" when I do indeed fail. But then again, I'd never get any satisfaction from it from other people since they don't know about it either but that's okay.

f) I think I actually have a phobia of things possibly being "undead"

g) I love playing video games :/ Nathan (bf) calls me "filthy casual" all the time, but it's okay cuz he says it with love and a kiss.

h) I'm extremely introverted.

j) I obsess over things. Lately, it's been a health obsession.

"I guess what I'm saying is that this all feels very familiar. But it's not mine to be familiar about. I just know that another kid has felt this. This one time when it's peaceful outside, and you're seeing things move, and you don't want to, and everyone is asleep. And all the books you've read have been read by other people. And all the songs you've loved have been heard by other people. And that girl that's pretty to you is pretty to other people. And you know that if you looked at these facts when you were happy, you would feel great because you are describing 'unity.' It's like when you are excited about a girl and you see a couple holding hands, and you feel so happy for them. And other times you see the same couple, and they make you so mad. And all you want is to always feel happy for them because you know that if you do, then it means that you're happy, too." - The Perks of Being a Wallflower - Stephen Chbosky

How-so-effing-true :

"Drunken words means sober thoughts." One of the reasons why I prefer being sober because I always say to much.

Not sure if I'll write much or not. But my fav pairings are: Remus/Lily. Remus/Severus. Remus/James. Remus/Sirius. Lily/James. James/Remus (kinda)
