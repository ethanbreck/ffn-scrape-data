Hello, and welcome to my profile page.

There's not much to say about myself. I'm a tomboy and love football (soccer) and anime/cartoons. I enjoy writing and drawing which is posted on here and on deviantART under the same username.

I'm a huge Digimon fan so all my work will be Digimon related at present. I'm probably best known for my Digimon 2.5 series on here, and on deviantART. If you want to check out my artwork of 2.5, and other seasons work go here:

Favourite couple is obviously Taiora for anyone who knows me. Of course I was rather disappointed with how Adventure 02 ended, which I think inspired 2.5 all those years ago. Other couples I support are Takari, Jyoumi and Kenyako, although I don't really mind any coupling.

Digimon 2.5 actually began way back in 2002 when I first joined here, and since then it has gone through a massive transformation. When I last looked back on the original story from 02 I actually cringed. I was 13 and naturally my writing sucked. I could definitely see how far my writing had come since then.

As well as being on here and deviantART, I am also on twitter and tumblr. Feel free to follow me:
https://twitter.com/Cherrygirluk19
