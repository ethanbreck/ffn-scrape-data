Dang, It's been a while since I updated this! I'm a anime-loving boy, who likes Pokémon, Digimon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Zoids, and Tenchi Muyo! However, I only watch official translations. I don't know Japanese, after all.
I love ZIM! It was on Nick, but since it was canned, I'm boycotting Nick!
I appreciate any company that stared making video games systems before 1990. So for me, it's Nintendo all the way!
I'm a dog lover, through and through! I have two dogs. A fat german sheperd named Max, and a brownish mutt named Cocoa! They are not my passwords!
I also have a cat. My dad, who races lawnmowers, named her MowJoe. Weird name? Yes. But I love her regardless. I enjoy sleeping on the couch with her climbing next to me and laying next to me. And my mother enjoys this "catnapping" too.
Oftentimes, I have to use the skool's crappy computers to upload, since AOL's parental controls restrict me from the document manager.
And I'm a boy! And I love the New York radio station known as Z100. Anyone who didn't know that should hit yourself with the Steel Pipe of Dishonor!
I'm no longer revealing my AOL IM Screen Name because o advertisers keep IMing me!
I love writing crossovers! I was planning on writting a ZIM/Digimon Crossover, but it got boring. As did manyof my attempts at a ZIM crossover.
Anyway, I also like "The Simpsons", and I'm a teenage, hot chocolate (and Pepsi Twist) loving boy with a cardboard tube for a mouth! And I'm extremely choked up about ZIM being canned! And extremely angered that Jimmy Moron is replacing it!
