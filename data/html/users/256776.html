Hello, hello... =)

Some basic introduction: I'm a 17-year-old Portuguese girl, and my penname, Aldarwen, means 'lady of the trees' in elvish. :)
I like writing and reading in the fantasy genre. My all-time favourite is "The Lord of the Rings" by J.R.R. Tolkien, but I alsoliked the Harry Potter series, Garth Nix, the "Sevenwaters" trilogy by Juliet Marillier, "Sophie's World" by Jostein Gaarder...:) I also enjoy drawing and painting.

About my stories: "A fading spring" is a short fic, LotR fandom. :) "How I became a witch" is Harry Potter fandom, and it is a story written on Hermione's point of view, about how she went to Hogwarts. I had some previous Lord of the Rings stories here on ff.net, but I wrote them when I was younger and they weren't very good. =P
If you read my stories, please review. I'm always interested to know your opinions on them. :)
