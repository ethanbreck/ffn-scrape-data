Digimon: Liberation Force
A government agency called the D.S.A. (Digital Safety Agency) now controls the Digital World. From home, using new Digitalization Vizors, anyone on Earth can visit the Digital World, have their own Digimon partner and battle against other Tamers. However, there is one big rule. No Digimon may be able to Digivolve past the Champion level, for safety reasons. Only certain people and agents have clearance to pass over this rule. Though, there are others who choose to ignore this rule. That's when the D.S.A steps in and apprehends these troublemakers. Although it seems the D.S.A. is doing good work, they are holding a dark secret. "Digimon: Liberation Force" is a story about a ragtag group of Tamers who feel like the Digital World has become to overpopulated and Digimon are treated like slaves, more than friends. This group must stand up for Digimon rights and discover the secret behind the D.S.A.

The Fairly Odd Phantom III: The Pixie Conspiracy (Project on hold for now)

The Head Pixie and Sanderson have been spying on Timmy and Danny through their multiple exploits. After gathering some information on Danny's enemies, the Pixies pays a visit to Vlad. They strike a deal and from a plan to use Danny to take over Fairly World.

My Crossover (Possible future project)
(Scrubs/ My Name is Earl crossover)
Earl and Randy leave town for a Lumberjack Competition in California. After a slight mishap at the competition, Randy accidentally cuts one of his fingers off and is rushed to Sacred Heart Hospital. While Randy is tended to by Turk, Earl discovers that Dr. Cox is on his list and sets out to correct what he did.
