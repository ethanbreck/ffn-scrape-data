Hello! My name is Ally and I have been a writing on FF.net for nearly 5 1/2 years. I am mostly into Harry Potter fanfiction with an emphasis on Harry/Ginny. That is almost all I write with a few exceptions. I am currently a Psychology Major getting ready to finish my Bachelor's degree next winter (2009). I also work at a preschool and am in the Wind Ensemble at the universit I attend. If you don't see me for periods on end, you know where I am.

Beta Reading

Besides my busy schedule, I do make time to beta read! It usually takes me around 24-48 hours to do it so if you have that kind of patience, feel free to send me an e-mail. I am an accredited beta reader on Perfect Imagination (perfectimagination.co.uk). My new PI number is 5604525670 and my screenname is pinkshades05.
