Yo. My name's Molly, but the voice in my head keeps telling me to call her Roni (short for Veronica), so I answer to either. I'm a senior at EKU, majoring in creative writing. Because of that, I like to read as much as possible to help me develop my own style and see what works and what doesn't. A few years back I was pushed into checking some stuff out on here and I've been hooked ever since.

These days I mostly read Bleach fics, but I've been known to check out fics about wrestling, InuYasha, Buffy and Angel. So ya never know where I'll pop up.

As a writer (and I'm sure most of you understand), I'm obsessed with revising. Because of that, I haven't actually posted anything on here myself... yet. I've got countless fics that I've been working on for years, literally, that I'm still not satisfied with. I know I'll never be completely happy with anything I write, as most writers aren't, but I'd like to get anything I post up to at least a 75-80 satisfaction rate with myself before I subject anyone else to it. Fingers crossed that I get there sometime in the next decade.

I'm on AIM if anyone wants to chat or bounce ideas off of me or aything. My screenname is RebelRoni666. Feel free to hit me up.
