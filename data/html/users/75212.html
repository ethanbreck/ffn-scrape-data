And we all will fly away...

Fly away...

Away...

So I do have two completed stories under my belt. I am glad to say that they both have done me proud:

1. I Swear- my first attempt at a Power Rangers story that takes place during Ninja Storm

2. The Divine Magic Revolution- my second attempt at Power Rangers, this time taking place during Mystic Force.

I tend to write under the drama/romance or supernatural/drama genre. My stories tend to have some romance, but at times is full of drama--as in the case of I Swear. Then there is the T.D.M.Revolution which is filled with the drama from beginning to end, and supernaturally, I think it does justice to the category...

I still do watch the show, so far I think I haven't missed a season...It's just that there's so many of them. Well, Power Rangers aren't the only fictions I produced. I tried my hands at Harry Potter, Beyblade, and Sky High.

For those who read those stories: everyone is entitled to their own ideas and concepts. Just because in the show or movie one character hooks up with this one, doesn't necessarily mean that I'll abide by those standards. It's really simple: if you don't like the story, then don't review...
