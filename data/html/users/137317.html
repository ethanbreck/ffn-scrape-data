IMPORTANT:

I really enjoy writing fanfiction. Always have and always will. Even when I was younger I made up stories that went along with the movies, shows, and books I liked. I got into fanfiction fairly late in the game but have enjoyed the time spent here. Be it Sailor Moon and the unpublished, but still largest and proudest project,"Lexy Chronicles" or my current love of Xiaolin Showdown fanfiction has really helped me to grow as a writer. Since I was in the 7th grade and realized that I wanted to be a writer it was all I dreamed about. I love to write fiction and have always wanted to write books people would love, like the books that were so important to me growing up.

That being said I've come to a time where fanfiction needs to take a back burner. Frankly it should have happened a long time ago but I'm finally putting my foot down. Come New Years fanfiction will officially (sadly) be a second priority. I hope, but will not promise to have the majority of stories written, but not published, at that point in time so I can keep updates some what frequent. I really want to write professionally and fanfiction has become an excuse for me not to pursue it due to my desire to please the readers here.

So to sum up, come the end of the holiday season, right after New Years, I will be focusing on my original fiction more than fanfiction. Its easier said then done so your support and understand in appreciated.
