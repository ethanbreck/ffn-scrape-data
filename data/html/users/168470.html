The third installment into my Tarrant/Alice series. Tarrant and Alice are the proud parents of rambunctious twins, Ashtant and Straleigh. When Straleigh is abducted by the once-thought comatose Knave of Hearts, how will the celebrated couple of Underland cope?

The Entomologist and The Loose Cannon
(On Hiatus) A GSR-centric fic... I'm kind of lost the mojo for this fic, and I would love to see some ideas floating around of what the fans of this story would like to happen next... PM me!

The Times Of Your Life
I've only recently gotten into the Twilight fandom and this began as a Twilight/Repo!
