Author has written 9 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
I love TV. I used to sit in class and day dream about new stories from my favorite shows. I came across some fanfic and just haven't been able to stop reading, and since I am a writer, I figured I should try my hand at it.

I write Buffy stories, almost always Xander centric, but I challenge myself once in a while. I love B/X, because I always root for the underdog to get the girl of his dreams. There isn't going to be B/S (at least portrayed in a good way) ever. Not because I'm a B/Xer, just cause I can't stand the abusive relationship that is B/S. So now you know what to expect from my stories. Hope you enjoy.
