Author has written 8 stories for Sailor Moon, and Inuyasha.

It's strange to see what one review will do for a person. I was seriously considering calling all my fiction dead after my last computer died and took the last almost finished chapter of Catch Me with it. Then, at random, one person reviewed my story "Catch Me" and it got me thinking. Even though the actual Inuyasha series had ended, someone still liked my side view rendition and felt it was worth reading.

I have just graduated college with a Bachelor of Fine Arts and am engaged at the age of 23. Since then, I have not really done anything creative aside from costume commissions and painting commissions since I had graduated. My days have turned to a monotony of get up, go to work, and go to bed. This lather rinse repeat lifestyle is rather sickening and now that I think of it, returning to my writing may be just the escape I need. Thank you hoping2improve for inadvertantly giving me the little nudge I needed to continue.

So, it is official. I will be continuing my stories, Catch Me being the first. I do not know how long it will be until my next update, but it will most certainly be far faster than my last.

~Angela
