June 11, 2009

Yes, I am still alive...

Updated my stories information in accordance to new crossovers sorting, and will probably even update some of them (yes, I know that my updates come slowly, but I am too busy and overall tired to write most of the time).

If you want me to update a particular story, leave me a review; the amount of reviews will be taken into account when I will be deciding which story I should focus on. (And I am overall unhappy that I get more “story’s favored” and “story alerts” then actual reviews; if you are looking forward to the next chapter, why not leave an author a few words of encouragement?)

The stories I am currently working on:

Status: In progress, approx. 1/3 complete

Status: In progress, approx. 1/2 complete

School Wars

Status: On hold, approx. 1/3 complete

The rest of there stories I published are either complete or I stopped working on them completely.

Note:

I must say that my earlier stories (i.e. the ones that were published/updated before the year 2006) are nowhere near the standard I am trying to maintain these days. But if you decide to read one or few of my earlier stories anyway, I would certainly appreciate it if you review them (including complete or discontinued stories), even if your reviews won’t be all that positive.
