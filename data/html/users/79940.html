Author has written 113 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Blue Seed, Harry Potter, Angel, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Song of the Lioness, Buffy X-overs, Smallville, Firefly, Supernatural, Dark Angel, Stargate: SG-1, Stargate: Atlantis, Doctor Who, Torchwood, Justice League, Heroes, and Merlin.

Twenty-two, bisexual, female. Pro-choice, pro-gay rights (for obvious reasons.) Over-educated liberal deist, just like the rest of my family. History major. I love to read, love to write- as you could probably tell from the sheer number of stories I've written. If you ask me how it got to be that many, I swear I don't know, because last I looked there were only five down there and now there's over a hundred. This can probably be explained by the creativity that I use to avoid doing any actual studying for my classes. Ah, well.

This is a SLASH ZONE, though not slash-only, and some stories may contain graphic material, usually for sexual content or implied sexual content. There are also some incest, threesome, gender-bender (and more!) stories, so if this squicks you, please, stop here, and stop now. The back button exists for a reason.

I love constructive criticism, but keep in mind that I rarely answer messages, and almost never answer reviews, so don't feel offended if you don't get a reply. To be cliche: it's not you, it's me. I'm almost never on this site to do anything but update. However, I do get your replies, and certainly enjoy receiving any and all comments that are given, unless they are obviously prejudiced and unpleasant, in which case I ignore them.

One last thing: People, if a story is marked "Complete," it means that it won't be updated. Posting to ask me for more is flattering, but ultimately useless. I may, however, be moved to write a sequel, which is an entirely different thing, and also depended on the whims of my capricious brain.

Alright, that said, enjoy the show. I'd be wary of some of my older fic, though- if you read from the bottom up, you can, um, definitely see the evolution of my writing. From, y'know, horrible to current. Just a warning.

"I've always been the first one to settle for second-best."

"By the time you swear you're his, shivering and sighing, and he vows his passion is infinite, undying- Lady, make note of this: one of you is lying."
