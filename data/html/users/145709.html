G'day!

My name is PeaceKeeper, but I also answer to PK or Fabian, and God if you are really worthy. I primarily write Digimon fanfics, focusing either on my OC's Liam and Nyamon, or Kari and Gatomon. What can I say, I've got a thing for smart-* cat-digimon.

For those of you who have read some of my stuff back in spring, let me assure you that I am not dead. Over the summer I got swept away in things and was unable to post stories (cursed dial-up connections...), but I shall be resuming to writing as soon as I get resettled in my new pad.

Although I do refer to the following in my stories, I do not own them: Digimon, MTV, various Pennsylvania companies, my soul. Digimon is owned by Toei in Japan, and in America is owned by Disney's Bandai *winces* and Fox Kids. Anyone else, I don't know who is owned by.

Basically, I'm a poor college student. And I mean POOR! I don't own the computer I write this stuff on, I don't own "my" car, I don't own my soul, etc. So please don't sue. I don't have any money to give you.

MY POLICY ON FLAMERS:
Honest and intellectual criticism is always welcomed. If it's long or in depth, feel free to IM me. But I do not tolerate flaming (meaningless insults to authors or readers, without substinance and often just meant to piss people off).

Flamers are the lowest beings on earth and, in my opinion, are worthless. If you don't like Digimon, then don't read a Digimon fanfic. I make my stories clear at the beginning on what they are about (mostly Digimon).

They call me Peacekeeper because I keep the peace and beat down flamers in the forums and chatrooms. Wanna try to flame me? I guarantee you'll be the one to get burned.
