Updated 4/3/2006.

I got into the Digimon fandom back in ~1999. I liked Taito, but thought Matt/Tai friendship fics were even better. Later I was seduced to Harry Potter, especially Cassandra Claire's fics, and Harry/Draco. Then came my Stargate phase, with Jack/Daniel. A few years ago I started going in reverse order and got into HP again, only with Harry/Ron this time.

After a two year fandom hiatus, I'm starting to get into Digimon again (still only Digimon Adventure, the other seasons don't exist to me). I have plans for a Matt-centric fic taking place during the Myotismon arc with darkish themes and Matt/Tai friendship. Will this actually happen? Dunno.
