ABOUT ME:

Ah, the section that you've all been waiting for. *not* All about little ol' me.

I am a girl, and I live on the east coast of the United States. You're not getting a more particular location than that, and I'm not telling you my real name, so don't ask - 'cause I'm not stupid enough to give out that information online. I am 14; tall and thin with short, semi-spiky brown hair that sticks up all over the place. I have glasses and pale skin. My eyes change from blue to green to gray depending on my mood. Nowadays they tend to be gray.

I don't currently have any pets. I have a few aquaintances, but only one true friend. This friend is just as shadowy as me.

Shadowy? Yes, I am. I flirt with darkness and often withdraw alone to sit in the shadows in my mind, heart, and soul and ponder death. I am depressed, or so they say. I may be, and I may not. However, I think that 'normality' is just as much of a 'disease' as this thing that they call 'depression.' I love the darkness and hate it at the same time. It is my enemy and yet my friend. I fight both against it and for it at the same time. I am walking along the edge of the cliff of sanity in my mind even as the part behind me shatters forever. One of these days, the ground under my feet will shatter as well, or I will step over the cliff's edge. Either way, I shall fall into darkness eventually.

The hour has come to dance again-
I, too, know how to fight.
Though panic rises, the wings of a demon,
I will not give in this night.

Unfathomed darkness slowly creeps its way in-
The storm’s lightning blindingly white.
Though I cry with the rain, I will still stand come morning.
You will not conquer my soul this night.

Wearily, I laugh, among friends, fallen angels.
Like me, they are tired of sight.
Though the days have grown long and my steps are uncertain,
I will not be broken this night.

Familiar sights of madness still seize my being as I scream-
Wings of heaven will no longer take flight.
Even as stars cease to hold magic, and flames merely burn down,
I will continue to dance this night.

One more battle won, but war still looms ahead-
Never to be won, though I may fight.
Into the sunset, I ride on wings of a demon.
I will not give in this night.

- "This Night" by Midnight Corruption

A light caress of poison
The slightest touch of black
A haunting echo from the lips
Of a girl who won't come back.

Her strength gave 'way out in the depths
Of this sorrow-broken place;
An angel died upon her knees
Leaving but a memory of her face.

Who knew? -- everyone
But who saw past her guise?
Who saw the agony within
Her deeply haunted eyes?

Did any of her friends see her as they left?
Did any of them look back?
Nobody seemed to see the girl
Who was inwardly living in black.

Her heart broke on a sunny day
In a supposedly safe place
Innocence drowned in the falling tears
That stained her bewildered face.

She tried to love but got broken
Tries now to laugh but only can cry
Pale slashes blaze across her wrists,
Signs she tried in vain to die.

The air around her crackles
With the anguish she tries to hold back;
This world where no one sees her -
Everyone's in black.

She sits alone to cry awhile
In her only stable place
Of all those who have hurt her -
No one said it to her face.

Tattered pieces of paper
Clutched tightly as she cries
Promises still bind her here
But part of her spirit dies.

Just a kiss of poison,
Just a hint of black,
I burned my bridges and I know
It's too late to turn back.

Just something else gone wrong again
In this nightmarish place;
No one else around can see
The shadows on my face.

And if you saw me in a dream
Would you pass me by?
Would you stop and talk awhile
Or would you let me die?

- "Black" by Chupaflor
