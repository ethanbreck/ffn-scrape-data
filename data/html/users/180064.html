Victory Means Life (finished)- My first Zelda fic. The evil Ganondorf has attacked the City of Hyrule. Impa and the Hylian Army will make him pay...

What Women Want (6 and 1/2chapters so far)- A Tenchi Muyo fic. Here's the basic jist of it. Tenchi is struck by lightning and thouroughly electrocuted. After passing out, finds he can read the minds of all the women in his house! A blatent adaptation of the Mel Gibson movie "What Women Want".

The Legend of Zelda: Facade of the Living (11 chapters so far)- Another Legend of Zelda fic. With Ganondorf gone, there is a new Gerudo King. His ambitions endanger the fragile peace of the kingdom. Only one hero can stop him... (good god, maybe I'll finish this damn thing one day. 12.04.11))

The King of Hyrule's Daughter (finished)- The very beginning of the legend, and the story of the lady princess who lost her life protecting the Triforce of the Gods.
