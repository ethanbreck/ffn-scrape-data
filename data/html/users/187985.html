Hiya there and welcome to my profile.
I'm Luke and I'm from Manchester, England.
When not writing I "work" (if you can call it that) as a model, I also enjoy football (soccer), X-box, kick boxing, cooking and driving far too fast. I live with my chinchilla, and three other people, and am kinda romanticallly involved with one of them. One day I'll write a story about us, but I doubt anyone would belive it.

In terms of my fanfiction My favourite fandoms to write about are
Digimon: Especially Matt, Kari, Davis, Gatomon and Blackwargreymon
My favourite pairings are Tai/Matt, Matt/Davis, TK/Davis or some complex three way triangle. Saying that I like reading most stories yaoi stuff just appeals to me more to write about. My personal favourite Digimon story I've written are my Blackwargreymon sagas.

Final Fantasy series I'm a big fan of Squall/Riona and I like Yuffie but can't really get a handle on her.

Suikoden 1 and 2 I like Tir Mcdohl/Kasumi and Riou/Jowy but also like the minor lights from that series. I'm very proud of my "Judgement" story in this area.

I'm a big pokemon fan too and want to do a Mewtwo story on day (It's on my to do list) but also really want to do one on my favourite of all pokemon, Ivysaur!

I love getting reviews (doesn't everyone) so if you've like (or hated) any of my stories feel free to review or message me I've certainlty enjoyed hearing from people who have read my fics in the past and have met some good net buddies from it. :-)
