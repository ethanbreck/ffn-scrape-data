Hey there! Thanks for stopping by. Unfortunately, there isn't a lot to see here, especially since I no longer write fan fiction. The stories that I had uploaded years ago, I removed simply because I wasn't happy with the quality of them. Around the time that I was writing fan fiction, I also founded the first Palletshipping C2 community here on FanFiction.net, the Palletshipper Paradise. I still maintain that C2, off and on, and plan to continue to do so. I set up the C2 back during the tail end of 2004 (where has the time gone!?) and shortly after, when forums were introduced on FanFiction.net, established the Palletshipper Paradise Parlor to go along with the C2 community. Although I don't feel that I have a good enough handle on storytelling to write fan fiction, I do still enjoy palletshipping and I want to make it easier for others that enjoy it to have access to the fan fiction. That is why the C2 community exists, and the forum is there for fans to come together to discuss their favorite stories and palletshipping in general. I hope that the collection of palletshipping fan fiction continues to grow for the enjoyment of the readers and I hope that the collection inspires others to tell the kind of stories that they want to tell.

If you have a palletshipping story, and you don't see it in the Palletshipper Paradise C2 community, please send me a private message so that I can add it! I try to find and add stories on my own, but it's possible for me to miss some too, so if that happens, please give me a heads up!

Happy reading and writing!
