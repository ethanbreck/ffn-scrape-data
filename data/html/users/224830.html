Hey guys... you might have noticed my lack of writing for the fandom lately. I think I'm officially done with it... I'm keeping my account open so I can still review my favorites, but if you're looking for updates... let me just point out that most of these are YEARS old. They've kinda been left behind. It still makes my day to get reviews and to see that you all are SOMEHOW finding my fics though I haven't exactly been around, but please, the pleas for updating are really pointless. Untouchables will not be finished. Anything But That will not be finished. Sold will not be finished. They are destined to die off as tragically abandoned fictions.

Also, keep in mind that these are (for the most part) old stories. Yes, I have grown as a writer since them. Isn't that what college is for? So don't be too hard on them. I look back at them with a nostalgic air. You should do the same.

Thanks... I've had some great years here. The NJL will always be with me. We were a lovely bunch, eh?

Cheers to those who came before me, those who were here with me, and those of you yet to come. We do have the best fandom. You may have to search a little, but the good authors are there. And when they're on, they're ON. Check out my "Favorite Authors/Stories" tabs if you don't believe me.

I look back with smiles and nothing but good feelings. I seriously adore all of the friends I've made here.

Viva la Blush (and a little side of Skush, too :P)!
