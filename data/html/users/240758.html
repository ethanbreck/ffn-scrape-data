Well, with a new year coming I thought I'd update this again.

I'm Cori, I'm 22, I've been writing on FF.net for over 3 years now, probably about 5 years at least.

I have several different stories up, from Passions to Charmed to Smallville, but my main fandom right now is Power Rangers.

I've just recently gotten back into the series and yes, I'm 22, so sue me, I'm a kid at heart and not afraid to admit it. I just like the show, it's a good show.

Feel free to stop by and take a look at my stuff but I will warn you straight away: I am a slash writer, that means m/m or f/f relationships. If this does not tickle you the right way, please do not read my stories, I do not want to get flamed which is why I have warned you. Other than that, if this is something you're interested in, please stop by and remember, reviews are an author's best friend, I'm glad you're reading my stories but I'll like you even better if you review them. Author's are their own worst critics.
