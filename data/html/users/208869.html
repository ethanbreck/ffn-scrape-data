Well, a new year calls for a profile redo. My old one was getting a little long in the tooth (so to speak) and really needed to be changed. So, without further ado, here it is...

Thanks to Trev as always for his above the call help when it comes to reading my work.

I've got kind of addicted to a lot of anime shows recently, so they're gaining the majority of my attention. Plus, I've also become kinda disillusioned with Buffy the Vampire Slayer as a series. Don't worry, I haven't abandoned any of my fics and I am in fact slowly writing more to each of them but my career now has a stranglehold on most of my time, and as such what time I get tends to get used up pretty fast by all my other commitments. I'm sure you don't really want to know about all that nonsense, though, so I'll stop now.

Anime I've been watching recently:

Bleach
Full Metal Panic
Full Metal Panic: Fumoffu!
Hikaru no Go
Rurouni Kenshin
Samurai Deeper Kyo

Manga I've been reading recently:

Bleach
Full Metal Panic
Hikaru no Go
Rurouni Kenshin

This is a more recent list, now. I'm really enjoying Full Metal Panic, it's a really funny show. The manga is excellent, too. Hikaru no Go is a little weird for my tastes, but so far it's amusing. I want to get my hands on the Samurai Deeper Kyo manga, as I'm sorry, but the anime sucked bawls. Kenyou? Uh... and Migeira? What on earth was he even in the thing for?

Never mind. Have a good year, all. Don't be afraid to drop me a PM if you want - I'll try my best to reply to 'em.

Believe it or not, I am trying to write more of my started fics. I've got about 1000 words of the final chapter of 'Every Angel' written - but that chapter looks to be shaping up to be 7000 words or so, so it's nowhere near finished. Due to popular demand (although I'll probably regret it later) I've also got about 1500 words of another chapter written for 'I'm the Doctor' but I'm not greatly impressed with it so I'm still trying to sort that out. Oh, and I've got another Naruto fic which has maybe a couple of chapters written. I'll see how that one pans out...
