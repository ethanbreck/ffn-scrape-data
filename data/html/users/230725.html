Hello, I'm EneriRenie.

Credit to KyteAura for the pen name.

I prefer to be called Reen. Or Irene. Or Reenie. Whatever floats your boat--just NOT Joy plzkthxbai (my second name).

Turning 22...very soon.

I live in the Philippines.

I spent the first 13 years of my education in a coed private Catholic school before studying and graduating as a film major in my country's most radical university. (I feel this is an explanation as to why I am the way I am. XD)

I was compelled to write my first fanfiction because I was properly appalled at the insufficient epilogue of Grandia 2.

The rest of my work are inspired by crack. Mostly.

I'm no longer unemployed. Yay! I currently work as a copywriter for an all-anime cable channel. 8D (2 guesses where, and the first one doesn't count.)

...And my soul has succumbed to the evil that is called Johnny's Entertainment. My ultimate goal is to go to Japan and marry Nishikido Ryo.

I am a rabid shipper of the following pairings: Harry/Ginny, Eriol/Tomoyo, Van/Hitomi, Seifer/Quistis, Kurama/Botan, Ron/Hermione, Inuyasha/Kagome, Yoh/Anna, Ranma/Akane, Naruto/Hinata, Arnold/Helga, Shikamaru/Ino, Vincent/Yuffie, Satoshi/Risa, Robin/Raven.

I don't know if I like Sasuke/Sakura. I don't think I can write Sasuke/Sakura.

I cannot write a lemon. Or slash.

For inquiries/suggestions/comments/violent reactions (especially on my Yoh/Anna C2), email me at cutesypatootsie(at)gmail(dot)com.
