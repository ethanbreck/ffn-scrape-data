Hi!

Welcome to my profile! Being as though you're probably here to learn a little bit about me, I'll say that I'm a tea-drinking Sagittarius who loves to laugh and have fun. An aspiring illustrator, I hope to find a happy career in comics, children's books, or animation. I'm an avid reader, and though the term is horribly clichéd, it is a rather apt description. I like to study languages, even though I can't seem to actually learn one, and I travel whenever the opportunity presents itself.

My hobbies include, but are not limited to, daydreaming, reading, writing short stories, playing D&D and video games, photography, tarot, hanging out with my friends and family, and starting things that are never to be finished. ;~P

Anyway, my art and fanart are displayed on my homepage, so please feel free to check it out as well. And if you happen to play Harvest Moon: FoMT / MoFo, I have a Fishing guide and a Relationship guide up on GameFAQs (dot) com and Neoseeker (dot) com under the moniker Arashinonatsu. For now though, thanks for visiting!

Best wishes,

CalypsoTea
