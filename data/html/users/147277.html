Remembered My Log-in
March 30, 2010

It took three days and two reminders from two fellow ex-writers for me to remember how to log into my account. It's not easy logging in when one cannot remember both the ID and the password. I figured it out, however, and I am making an official announcement that I'm done writing. It was fun while it lasted. :)

MAILING LIST is available!
Because the frequency of my updates are...not so frequent these days, I decided that initiating a mailing list will be the easiest way to notify readers of new posts. For anyone interested, please e-mail me and let me know that you'd like to join. I will most gladly add you onto the list. :)
