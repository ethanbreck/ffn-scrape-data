Recent Updates: Alanka: CHAPTER SIX UP! (onlyafter another year!)

I have had many different accounts on ff.net... for what reason, I don't know. But I was first introduced to ff.netat the end of2000, and have been reading Draco/Hermione since there were only6 pages of it. Extremely long, extended DM/HG fics are my weakness, and when I read them, I get completely into them.

For Fics:I support Draco/Hermione,Hermione/Blaise, Hermione/Lucius, Hermione/Oliver and the rare odd ones like Sirius/Hagrid heheh. I really have only ventured out of HP fanfiction once or twice... didn't really take that muchof a liking towards it. Today is August 30, 2005 (Umm.. now its March 4, 2007... so much for determination. But hey, I got another one out!) and I haven't been on fanfiction.net to update anything, or very often at all since exactly one year ago. But now that I've come back, I'm ready and rather determined to finish Alanka, and maybe possibly the others. I've also started an untitled fic to which I have two chapters already. I don't plan to post that one until it is completely finished. (...))

For Music: I have a wide variety of tastes ranging from Pink Floyd to Peaches. I love Iron & Wine, the Shins, Finch, Bright Eyes, Rilo Kiley, Tilly and the Wall, Sublime, Metric, the Dresden Dolls, The Unicorns, Le Tigre, Ima Robot, Hot Hot Heat, The Used, Interpol, InMemory, and Norma Jean are some of my favorites.

For Books: Currently my favorites are (of course) the HP series, Life of Pi, The Perks of Being A Wallflower, the Georgia Nicolson series, etc.

My fic Alanka (the only one I'm reallyfocusing on) was started before Book 6 came out, so bear with the fact that a certain someone is still alive and active, and so on.
