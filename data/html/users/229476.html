I'm bored so I'm going to write a new bio! Woohoo! Not that I'm a new person...well, let's see here. I have various personalities:

Lucy. Lucy is eccentric, she is really perverted and doesn't really care much about anything. She hardly ever worries about anything.

Deborah. Deborah worries all the time. She worries about her grades far too much. She always expects the worse. She ponders life and other philosophical things. She is occasionally suicidal, but usually just deep. She hardly ever laughs.

Aralya. Aralya was created by Deborah trying to write a romance. She was princess of Gondor, and now finds her only possible dwelling is in this body. She can be a little perverted, but not too bad. She likes to laugh and have fun. She doesn't care too much about anything, but tries to do well at things.

Nicole: High-pitched, really nasty, hygenically obessive, snotty.

Todd: Doesn't care about hygene at all, likes to have fun, sportsy--oddly enough, because of my P.E. grade...

Yep, that's all of me. Well, most. I also have a few others, plus Estel and Galadriel each inhabit my brain at times. When I write, it gets strange because sometimes when Deborah's mid-sentence Lucy takes over or some such thing.

Namarie!

Oh, and I did not say my age so you would not judge on that.
