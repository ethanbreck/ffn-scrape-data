Author has written 2 stories for Twilight.

My first tale is called The Bella and Edward Show. It's my attempt at allowing the reader a view of what it might have felt like to witness the developing relationship between Bella and Edward. I do this through my own character, Raymond Kulp, who happens to be in Edward and Bella's graduating class. I believe I stayed canon in this story and I challenge you to dispute it. My second tale is called Breaking Dawn, the Battle! This was a major undertaking; as I provide the reader with an alternate ending to the confrontation the Cullens had with the Volturi. I recapture the exact tempo and dialogue from Book 3 of Breaking Dawn; starting at the moment when Eleazar labelled Bella a shield. It's clearly a what if tale, answering the question; How would the Cullen fare if they had gone in with the intention of fighting versus Stephanie Meyer's diplomatic route. This tale is mostly told through Jasper, Kate and Garrett's POVs, although I provide the POVs of several others near the end; which include Carlisle, Alice, Edward and Bella.

I've started (and failed to finish) a sequel to The Bella and Edward Show which I hadn't titled. I brought back Raymond Kulp's character and his immediate family. The tale is very dark and the theme is not suitable for everyone. Ray is forced to go back to Forks, Washington in hopes of contacting Edward and Bella Cullen. He knows that if he doesn't contact them that he could very well lose his oldest daughter.
