Hey all!

If you look at the stories I've written, you'll notice that it's been quite awhile since I've posted anything on here. I've decided it is high time to write again, and I needed to change this little profile thingie because it said before that I was 18, and now I'm nearly 20. I'm a second year English major, and someday I'd like to write full time, although time will tell on that one. So anyway, the story I'm working on now is The Marauder Chronicles, and I'm also attempting to continue Good-Bye to You, though I'm thinking of changing that title. My stories can get a little smutty sometimes, so if you don't like that sort of thing, don't say I didn't warn you! It's part of life! I'm primarily a hardcore Ron/Hermione writer, but I sometimes venture into Draco/Hermione...it's so forbidden, I can't resist. So please stay tuned and keep reading my stories, because sooner or later I always come back. :)

Thanks for reading!

Lindsay (Scabberssucks)
