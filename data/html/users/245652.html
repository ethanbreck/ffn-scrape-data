CSI freak, (though frightened of attempting any such fanfiction), fan of the Chronicles of Faerie by OR Melling, and in awe of Tolkien, and Robert Jordan. These words are mine, the concepts and some of the characters belong to the greats before me. Thank you kindly to all of them-you've given me some of the best things I've ever written. My apologies to them all fo butchering their characters and worlds. *bows head in shame* Forgive me. I try. Honest.

As you are here, please do take the time to read some of what I've posted. It's not that great, but a lot of people seem to enjoy it. Go figure. I give my solemn word that I will read and review at least one of your pieces as long as it's in one of the fandoms I'm familiar with. And to all of you who review-Many thanks and cookies to you all. Final thanks goes out to all the people out there who belive in me, who have inspired me, and to anyone who really gives a damn. Hugs to all...
