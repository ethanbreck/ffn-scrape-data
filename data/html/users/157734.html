Whats the SECRET to my writing!

To me, you can't force someone who is currently inspiration-dead to write a good story. The result will be as good as a few days coffee and stale bread (trust me its not nice at all, i tried it). There are a few times when I was forced to write a good story within an hours time frame and guess what I got, 55/100 and yes that was one of my exam criterias. Heck, it was extremely devastating on me and my self-confidence.

But anyway my writings are all inspired by listening to good musiclike Padi (an Indonesian Band, they're good. Listen to them if you can.). To me different music brings out different styles of writing from me. I can also be inspired by calming sceneries, reading up on the reference side of the library and learning to understand the real me. Does that sounds weird? hehehe..

I also need lots of good coffee and munchies while I work on the stories. Sometimes I like to write while I'm in Starbucks/Coffee Bean, yeah once again good coffee is the main reason here. Sitting there by myself with coffee beside me, I get lots of ideas on the story I work on especially when I watch other people's behaviours and yes I'm more of an observer than a participant. God I sound boring don't I? hehehe...
