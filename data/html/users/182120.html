June 18, 2014. 4:54 PM. Wow, so long since I updated my profile. I just got into TMNT 2k12 and have so many ideas for different stories. Already got two written...currently working on my third. I started thinking about how much this site has expanded since I first started writing here. I think readers (and writers alike) have become desensitized to how important reviewing stories is. When the site was new, everyone was reviewing what they read and it was wonderful. Now, with those follow and favorite buttons, people don't find it as necessary. But...guess that's how it goes. So many stories, so little time. Carry on.

August 9, 2014. 10:22 AM. I've successfully moved from Michigan to Texas. My life is a bit busy right now and I'm unfortunately suffering from a little of writer's block. January 5, 2018: 9:19 AM. New year!! And I realize I hardly wrote anything in 2017. Shame on me, but at the same time, 2017 was a horrible year for me. But that's all in the past now! I still want to finish Contagious. Also feeling inspired by The Gifted and Runaways...and certain episodes of Black Mirror. Trying to get myself more motivated, because I know once I start, then it will be hard to quit again (which sounds amazing right now). Anyway, thank you all for sticking by me and continuing to read and review all my work. It really means a lot and this site has been like a 2nd home to me for over 17 years now and I do not want to lose anything about it. Love you all!
