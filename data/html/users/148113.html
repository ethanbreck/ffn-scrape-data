My name is Katy, I'm 14 years old, I live in the United States, and I am a dedicated otaku-of-many-things, including but not limited to: Skies of Arcadia, Final Fantasy X, Magi-Nation, Zoids: Century Zero, Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball Z, Yu-Gi-Oh - and the list goes on and on. I have an older brother who's currently in his first year at college, an annoying younger sister who's a pain in the rear, and nice, slightly insane parents. (But really, aren't all parents that way?)
I'm currently in the process of rewriting all my fics, as coffee was spilled on the only floppy disk that housed my stories. Don't worry, though, I think they'll turn out better for it. Scratch that, they HAVE turned out better for it.
They're Into the Otherworlds (A self-insertation), Ligress Nine (obviously a Zoids fic), and Guardians of Memory (a crossover). If ya wanna read one of the two, just email me, topic Fanfic, and if somebody else in my family doesn't delete it I'll send a copy to you. If I feel like it, maybe. Don't count on it, I can be pretty fickle.
And that's about it. Oh yeah, I forgot to tell you. I have a yami. Her name's SilverShadow, and she's the daughter of Rune, highborn Prince of their kind, the Lesser Powers. She's got control over most basic kinds of telepathic, kinetic, and metabolic powers. She's got white hair, silvery-gray eyes, and usually wears light blue and leather, with a cut-out phoenix made of solid gold. She's pretty much everything I'm not, and is better in the categories I'm simply okay in, like sarcasm.
-I- have her 'cause I have a (high-functioning) autism. Aspergers, although I bet only about two of you who ever read this will know anything about it or what the term "high-functioning" means in the world of autism. *pause* Although I could be wrong, but I'm pretty sure I'm not.
*sigh* And if you've read this far, which I seriously doubt, then I'll let you know that Into the Otherworlds and Ligress Nine are probably never going to be added here. Pity.
