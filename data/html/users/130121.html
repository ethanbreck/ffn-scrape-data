Lets say my computer isn't safe either. Profession wise I was in the Air Force and am now going to school to be a middle grades teacher. I am 28 and act between 16 and 18 (ok maybe more like 12 and 13) because I can. My favorite colors are blue and black. I like to listen to Godsmack, Rob Zombie, anime music (Japanese not English versions), country (has to have a fast beat I hate the slow ones), Some rap, and the Oldies. But I warn you now I am protective of my friends and tend to be hot headed towards those that say or do anything that I perceive bad to them. Oh and I have very very bad spelling. And that's pretty much me. I hope you like my stories and if you have any suggestions feel free to e-mail me. O yes I just remembered! Sorry for the disappearing act. A lot has been going on in my life and I just haven't had time for my stories.

Updated Profile: 15 August 11

I finally graduated and have been hunting for a teaching job, but until then I actually have free time to continue my stories! I had hoped to post some in October of '07, but life happened and I just couldn't write from all the stress. I am updating this profile for those that have been waiting and hopefully haven't forgotten me.

I am now 30 years old and will be 31 in October. I still act younger than I am around my peers though. While student teaching I can tell you that though I was professional I knew how to have fun with my students and my energetic personality clearly showed through. Some of the things I did with my students ranged from reenacting trench warfare with paper balls, math games (Bingo, Jeopardy, Sticky Ball), a variety of science experiments (Film Canister Detective, Plate Boundary Sign Language), fun with prepositions and complex sentences, poetry notebooks, performing a skit from The Outsiders, and creating a soundtrack for A Solitary Blue. I am proud of all my students that I have taught during my student teaching program, and I hope that they will remember that though school is hard it can be fun, even Math.

I still can't stand seeing someone depressed and will go out of my way to make them happy. I listen to a variety of music with the more current hits being my favorite. I would have to say my favorite artists will always be the ones mentioned above with the inclusion of Owl City, Iyaz, Forgotten People, Korn, Rihanna, Lady Gaga, Pitbull, Bruno Mars, and Mumford & Sons.

I am happy to report that my spelling and grammar has greatly improved. I will be leaving my original profile unedited to attest to this. As I start to write again and start posting my stories I do not know how different they will be due to by experience as a teacher and the development of my own maturity, but I will do my best to provide you with captivating chapters and overall interesting stories.
