Finally removed my horrible Trigun fan
fiction I wrote a few years ago. It was so bad that I got
GAFFed! I was actually happy about it! I can officially say
I wrote a God Aweful Fan Fiction! I also removed my X-Men: Evolution
fan fiction because I've lost interest in the series and doubt I'll be
updating it anytime soon.

I have no plans right now
for a new story and am trying to work on my writing skills before
producing anything else.

Don't know who provided
the link to the GAFFed post, but it was fun to see my work being
pulverized. Heh, I'm one of those people who have a sense of humor and
don't like to start arguements about people's opinions, but then again,
I completely agree with the people at GAFF: my fanfic was God
Aweful! Thanks for ripping it apart for me! A writer needs
that to keep them in place!
