Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Sailor Moon.

Reading is without a doubt my favourite pastime, both fanfiction and published work! I love all genres, though I tend to lean more towards fantasy and sci-fi, like A Song of Ice and Fire by George RR Martin or Lord of the Rings by JRR Tolkien.

In the world of fanfiction, Sailor Moon stories will forever hold a special place in my heart. SM stories introduced me to fanfics and I've been a dedicated fan since I watched Sailor Moon as a child. Usagi and Mamoru (aka Serena and Darien) are, for me, the perfect couple and I can't get enough of their story. Feel free to recommend any Usagi and Mamoru stories to me, your personal work or another author's...I'm always ready to delight in a UsaMamo story.

For those new to SM Fandom, I recommend you read the stories by Alicia Blade (my absolute favourite SM author...she's now a published author, see Cinder by Marissa Meyer), Antigone, Aria, Chibi J, Crystal Rose, Dejana Talis, EightofSwords (Subject to Change in particular, she's also published here by her friend/editor JadeEye), Gorecki (I especially liked "Untitled" which was housed on ASMR, but due to their server crash is no longer available online...pm me if you would like to read this!), Iamthelizardqueen (Not as Planned), Jason Ulloa, Jennifer Wand, Karisma, Lady M Harris, Lady Solo, Luthien82, Marsh Angel, MG, Patch, Reyus, Sexylyon, Stormlight, and Tennyo...all of whom you can find here at FF.Net or at Aria's Ink. If you're looking for SM fics with serious themes or dark storylines, I suggest LeVar Bouyer's Innocence In A Minor, Angus MacSpon's Sailor Moon 4200, and His Lordship Chaos' 1101. Also, I highly recommend Crystal Heart's Masquerade, Aimee's The Coldest December, Lilac Summer's First Truths, Lois Fogg's Fantasy, as well as Sailor Jes' Butterfly, Unfolding...those were the first SM stories that I read, and they are still timeless and incredible when I re-read them today!

Recently, I've also gotten into The Vampire Diaries fanfics. I'm a huge fan of the show and am partial to Damon Salvatore / OC stories, probably because Damon reminds me so much of Mamoru from Sailor Moon. They're both dark-haired, blue-eyed and seemingly such jerks, but beneath all their sarcasm they've got a hidden sweetness. My favourite TVD story, actually my favourite all-time fanfic at the moment, is Bits of Sunshine by Terans-DinosaurCostume. It's funny, provokes a lot of emotions and well written...definitely a must read!

I have had a few pathetic attempts at writing, but have definitely failed miserably. If by some divine intervention I find myself inspired, I might go back and edit or rewrite my stories...or maybe, heaven forbid, write something new. If you're looking for a beta reader, feel free to send me a pm :)

I'm always on the hunt for wonderful stories and update my favourite stories list regularly.
