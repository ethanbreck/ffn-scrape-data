Dear All,

I'm no longer writing very much fanfiction anymore, alas, for I have too many projects going on in my life, and fanfiction has been moved far down on my list of things to do. I would love to go back and write all the ideas I have, and to do nothing but read, review, and write fanfiction all day... but alas, Real Life sucks away at my time, and I have to allot what little I have accordingly. When inspiration hits and the timing is right, I'll post a one-shot or two on here. However, I'm also working on several original projects, which might be posted piecemeal on Tumblr.

For those who have stuck with me for all this time, thank you. I hope to continue to entertain you with eventual fanfics and original pieces alike. Those who have just come across my work, I'm sorry you've got to read this quasi-goodbye message. Know that I write it sadly.

I'm still around, art-wise, as fanart is a bit easier to pick up and put down when I will, and I also have original art pieces up for viewing. To see this work, just go to my deviantart page, or my Tumblr,

Thank you all for your support, your faves, your critiques and your dedication! Here's to many more years of writing and art, wherever it may be found!

SirLadySketch
