Yayers!! Revampin' my bio! Okay, now let's see here... What should I let the peoples know about myself:

Well, my pen name is Zett (a character in one of my fics) though I am called Zoa (not my REAL name, but might as well be, and also the name of a character I use as a trademark in many fics, hehe). I live in good ol' Canada! and am a young lady of 17 years. I love DBZ, Final Fantasy, LOTR, and Linkin Park!! Writing is one of my major hobbies along with drawing (especially anime), talking to my boy friend, and loafing.

Bwahahahaha, I seem to develop likings for the villans as my favorite character from DBZ is Perfect Cell (hottie!!) and from FF7, Sephiroth (also hottie!!) I also really like Squall (FF8) and Gohan (DBZ).

Alright, enough about me and my craziness, let's see about some of my ficies:

1. The Key To My Heart - This is my serious fic, well, mostly serious. I don't get chapters out on it alot, because I only write it when I know I'll do my best work. It's dedicated to Cell, and how love can be so much more powerful than we might think.

2. Something Worth Fighting For - Well, This is kind of my beginning fic, for Gohan, and how things in the DBZ world may have changed if a female Saiyan was to show up. Kind of humor based, cutesy, romantic, you know the deal.

4. An Insight on Vegeta's Dating Life - LOL, my madness fic. Kind of started when I would be in a really crazy or comical mood. Good for a laugh, lol, kinda my vent. INSANE!

5. Gohan's Resolution - My new, ultimate Gohan fic. Hehe, a new outlook on the Buu saga. My boy friend and I had some interesting ideas for what MIGHT have happened in the Buu saga, and how Gohan's character might have developed differently. And, I dedicate this fic to him, so, thanks for everything Andy baby! You're most of my inspiration for this fic. *kisses*

6. Gohan Undercover - Okay, this fic is basically just crazy, even more so that my fic on Veggie's dating life. Inspired by my love of James Bond movies, this fic is a bit of a parody on what kind of agent our innocent Saiyan hero, Gohan, would have made. Plus, what DBZ lovin' girl doesn't like the thought of Gohan wearing shades and a tux? ;)

And* (drum roll)

Guilty by Association* - This fic will consist of all my original ideas and my two most prized Original Character creations. The story revolves around a young teen girl named Zoa who befriends Gohan and the Z Fighters. After the Cell Games, Cell's biological son (born in a way similar to Nameks) makes his presence known and attacks the Z Warriors. But, will he prove to be completely his father's son? Or will he learn to open up his heart? (giggle!!) This is to be my most prized fic, so I hope to get it up soon. But it will be requiring a lot of work, so I may not have it ready for a while yet.

Continuing, I have also started a joint fic with my bf which is also about Gohan, this time in GT. You can check it out under our joint account, pen name 'Fritz/Zett'. It's called Gohan's Sacrifice. Oh, and don't worry, this fic is more of a side thing, and will not be taking time away from my others. I will continue to get chapters out on all my fics as quickly as I can!

Hehe, and I also write some DBZ poetry, I've got two up so far, one for Trunks and one for Veg, might post more later. And I'm REALLY tempted to write a LOTR fic in the future, but we shall see.

So in closing, I'd like to thank you for all of your reviews and support! I'm glad you like my fics, and I hope you will continue to! Happy readings!!

2 finger wave*
