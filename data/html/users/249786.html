It's been a while since I've logged in, several years I think. And although I still read fanfiction a lot, I've given up on writing altogether. Guess I'm just not writer-material. I'm sorry to everyone who have been, or were, enjoying my stories.

It's been a long time since I've looked at my account; let alone reviewwith the penname because I am lazy. So I guess I just wanted to update my profile because it was becoming a nagging burden in the back of my head. But I don't have the heart to delete my account, because it documents a part of my silly romantic self.

I got a review several weeks ago, and that simple fact alone, makes me wonder.. are people still reading what I wrote a long time ago? I hardly recognizethe fics myself. Anyway, there won't be any stories or whatever coming from me again, but know that I still enjoy reading a lot.
