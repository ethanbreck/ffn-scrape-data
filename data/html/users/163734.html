Hey everybody! You actually decided to take a look here? Well, anyway, I'm kinda boring. School's a drag, especially math class. I hate math! Ahem. Sorry. With that outta the way, ON TO THE BIO!
All of the stories I write are usually inspired from playing too many video games, watching too much TV, and my overactive imagination.

My current interests are:

Fire Emblem- Okay, so I haven't actually PLAYED it yet, but I hope to try it soon. I love RPGs and a strategy RPG would be more of a challenge than the arcade fighters I'm so used to.

Soul Calibur 2-A great fighting game. If you don't have it, go get it! For me it's tied with SSBM. Great graphics, gameplay, and if you get good you can kick major butt!

SSBM- One of my favorites. I never get tired of this game. The only thing I can complain about is the Lv. 9 COM cheapness.

LOTR- The books and the movies; I love em both. I love the action, the swordfights, the male characters (what? I'm a girl. So sue me if I think the male characters look cool).

Anime- Most anime have great plot and a lot of action. Plus the drawing style's cool.

Other bits of info:

Romance- I like to read the occaisional romance story, but nothing too mushy. Unfortunately, I have trouble writing romance myself seeing as I've never had a boyfriend or gone on a date or anything.

Horrible spelling/grammar- I'll admit, even I make stupid mistakes; no one's perfect after all. The occasional mistake is fine, but it gets a little frustrating when a story is riddled with em.

Mary-Sues/Gary-Stus- It didn't take long for me to figure out what these were. Basically they're self-insertion fics where an original character, who is absolutely perfect, falls for a canon character. I try to avoid creating one of these whenever I can.

Original characters: Everyone's done it. Even I've created an original character or two, but only to help the story along.

Well, that's enough of my ranting. See ya'll later!
