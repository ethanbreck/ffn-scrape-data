Dear Readers,

After very careful consideration, I have made the decision to stop posting any of my fics on FFnet. I'm still mulling over the idea of removing everything from this site, period, but for now, I'll leave everything up. Be forewarned that that could change. That being said, I'm still actively writing and posting to Archive of Our Own under the same pen name, i.e. ariaadagio on Ao3. Lucifans, you can find many more of my Lucifer stories on Ao3!

Personal recommendations:

And There Was Light - My favorite! Bringing it all together. Plot-y urban fantasy with a generous helping of romance.

Recover/Request/Reclaim/Recite - Bar none, the best thing I've ever written. I am so so proud of this for lots of reasons, but primarily due to the challenging subject matter I tackled. As an added bonus, this narrative allowed me to bring back my favorite original character from Lightning Strikes Twice, Stewart, and writing it helped me worked through a lot of emotions, as well. It was super cathartic to write.

Some Say in Ice - This was the fic that reminded me why I loved writing so much, after a very dark time when I'd written hardly anything at all. I loved being able to write a story for Bangel that I could enjoy as an adult, and I'm very proud of it.

Current projects:

Posting to Ao3 only. Check there!
