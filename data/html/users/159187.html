Hello! Interested readers. My name is Amp, and although I adore to write, I usually don't have time to pursue it to its extent. What I do have an opportunity to do is to READ!! I love ff.net, because I can read so many works of some promising authors out there.

Well, lets begin, with a little of my likes.

From television, series, my absolute favorite of all times is DAWSON'S CREEK. I live to see Joshua Jackson act out the outstading and adorably cute character of Pacey Witter. But unfortunaly the WB channel in my city has been bought by another channel and now I can't see it anymore, sniff. If it wasn't for my best friend who has cable, write now I would be dead. Buffy the vampire slayer was also one of my favorites specially when James Masters a.k.a Spike made a more important role. I also indulge in certain comedies my favorites being "That 70's show", "Scrubs", "Frasier" and on occasions "Sabrina".

On the anime side of TV, yes I do like anime. I was a great fan of Sailor Moon. My favorite season was when the starlights arrived. I loved the character of Seiya and the relationship he created with Sailor Moon. I also like Dragon Ball Z, only, the whole Dragon Ball GT was a total bore. Ranma 1/2 has its episodes.

And in books I love Harry Potter, you know I thought that they were only kids novels, but as soon as I started reading them I was totally hooked with J.K. Rowling narrating capacities. She has an extreme talent of making something as surreal as a wizarding world seem realistic and wonderful. An applause for J.K.

Well, I think that just about sums it up. If you asked for movies. My all time favorites are "Gone with the wind", "Titanic", "Men in Black", "10 things I hate about you" and another ones I can't remember.

Well I hope you enjoy my stories and please give me reviews so that I can improve my writting. See you at the review page.

Sayonara
Amp
