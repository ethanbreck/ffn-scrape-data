Fanfiction rants
I however do not like all fanfiction such as betrayal stories (one in which one character is betrayed by all of the other characters and leaves the show to find love someplace else.) They have been done countless times. If you are going to write stories at least make them orginal please, I'm begging you on my knees here. Also another type of story that has been popping up a lot lately is stories in which stories in which one character is bashed so that another character can be held to a higher regard. Lastely, stories in which one character instantly becomes a magnet for the oppisite sex (or the same sex depending on the character),instantly becomes an unstopable force and possess ever single trait possible and is good at everything. Writing said stories proves that you have no individuality and shouldn't be writting to begin with.

Why I write
I write because it keeps me sane. I tend to write a lot of Makoto Kino stories. Why do I write stories with Makoto Kino? She is such a complex character and one I feel connected to. A strong independant woman constantly missunderstood for a rebel and troublemaker. Why not? She doesn't take lip from anyone and stands up for herself and her friends. She's everything I want to be and currently am. We all feel a little missunderstood sometimes.

My Policy
I do update stories at my own pace, because i usually hate it when my chapters come out rushed which they used to do a lot. I work on whatever story i feel like working on at the time, but when i'm stuck whatever story has more reviews typically gets updated more often because more people are reading it. So if you want a story updated review people! I am open to suggestions, and even flames if they help me improve my writing. But I do ask if you do have a suggestion on a story or future story i'm thinking about writing simply email me otherwise it's no fun to the other wonderful readers if they know what's going to happen does it?

I take requests, I just recieved my most first one recently.
