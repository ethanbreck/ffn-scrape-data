I pray for an idea, and a way I cannot see.

It's too heavy to carry, and impossible to leave.

- Sara Groves

I'm Ashley, and welcome to my strange and twisted little world.

I first joined FanFiction.net when I was in eighth grade. At that point, I wrote a story for Back to the Future called The Return. What a road it has been since then. As I look back at what I have written, I take pride in the evolution I have gone through as a writer and as a human being. That being said, I also see a back-log of unfinished works of art which I am still ashamed of. However, I also see fingerprints of the significant turns my life has taken in the past decade I have been on this site. I feel like I am now an excellent writer who makes story her priority. I have made it my pledge to finish a story before publishing.

Right now, I am in graduate school for Social Work, and enjoying every minute of it. What I am learning will bleed though into my work, as well as my current fixations. I hope you enjoy reading as much as I enjoy writing.
