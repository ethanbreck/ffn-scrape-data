Author has written 38 stories for Silmarillion, Harry Potter, Trigun, Fullmetal Alchemist, Baldur's Gate, Kyo kara Maoh!/今日からマ王！, Elder Scroll series, Lord of the Rings, and Kingdom Hearts.

About me: German software developer, currently living in Berlin, previously lived elsewhere in Germany as well as the UK and the US. Singular they pronouns, erring on the side of female.

You can also find me on Dreamwidth (as Kaz), AO3 (as Kaz) and Tumblr (as Kazaera), although I'm not particularly active on any of those sites these days.

About my fics: I've been writing fanfic for around fifteen years at this point, although my writing pace has always been rather slow and erratic and I bounce between fandoms a lot. The main ones I return to semi-regularly are Silmarillion (also my first fandom) and Morrowind, which contains the Fic Of My Heart Sleeper in the Cave, a novelisation which I've been chipping away at for over ten years and is the only longfic WIP I continually return to.

Warning: my early fics are here solely out of nostalgia and in case some people like them. I don't recommend them and would like to think I've gotten better at writing since.
