Author has written 6 stories for Smallville, Harry Potter, Buffy X-overs, and Twilight.

Well, here I am, updating my profile after who knows how long.

I figured I should do it as I am beginning to write again. For those of you who read my fics here is some information about my writing.

I have been writing since Season 1 of Roswell. I didn't like how it ended, saw mention of something called fanfiction on the forum boards, read some, and decided to write my own. I began with the Problem-Solving 101 series (no longer online) and finally after a few years ended my Roswell writing with the last part of it.

I have written Roswell, Xena, Buffy, Smallville, BSB/Nysnc, Harry Potter, original, and have now moved onto Twilight. Most of my stories are not online anymore after I took down my website. There are a few posted here. I am sure you can find them if you run a search on my user name; I've been using it since the beginning.

In regards to Twilight my favorite character is Leah Clearwater and so far all my stories focus on her. I know, I know; you're like what stories; you only have one Twilight fic. Well, that's true. I have one completed Twilight fic. I have four others in progress; I learned a long time ago to not post anything but a complete story.

My pairings are Callwater and Blackwater though I do enjoy the idea of Paul/Leah and Jared/Leah. Imprinting sucks but it makes a good plot device. Especially delayed imprinting or double-imprinting or imprinting when a person is truly whole...

I read and write slash, het and gen though Twilight fans are prolly safe as there are no females I can see myself writing Leah with. I was the first LifeSaver in Roswell (Maria/Tess) and remember my stories of it fondly.

Bad news: I am a writer who goes through spurts. I move between reading fic, writing fic, reading fic and starting tons of fics and never finishing them. I have over 20 WIP's; most over 10 pages long but never completed. Right now I am in a reading and writing spurt. Who knows how long that will last. Another reason I only post completed works; I never know when I will get tired of writing.

I generally do not respond to reviews but if you ask me too I will attempt it. I don't give away plot ideas. I do not have a beta. I try to comb through and make sure there are no mistakes but sometimes I miss them.

If you want me to look over your story I will try. I will also tell you if it sucks or if you sound like a 13 year old fan girl when you write so make sure you really want an honest opinion.

I work 40 plus hours a week, spend a lot of time reading solely Twilight fic-I have all but abandoned my poor gmail account where all my HP and Buffy group mail gets sent- and am currently involved in re-modeling my room. It's a long process because I dislike spending time off the internet.

If anyone knows any yahoo groups for Twilight fanfiction please email me the links. I have no Twlight groups and I want some; specifically I want some Leah Clearwater groups.

SM needs to get over the leaking of MS and write Leah a real story; preferably one where she and Embry get together.
