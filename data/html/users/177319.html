last updated 1.01.05

Ugh. I was writing a biography to this fucking thing as I just had to press the back button.

Anyway, my name is Jennifer. Other psuedonyms I've had for this site include The Digital One, H.M.L. Mad Wolfy, and Dragoos. I mainly write and read out of the FFVIII, FFX, and KH sections. Although I support several couples, I'm a die-hard Quall fan, which is evident in my A/Ns to some of my stories and some stories all by themselves. Special.

WORKING/FORMULATING ON:

- This is a fire door never leave open. Meh. I planned out how I wanted to go with this fic, but I'm not sure if the plot will actually satisfy me. I usually write chapter by chapter without planning things, which may be the reason for the evident lack of plot in pretty much all of my stories.
- a Tikku. The showdown with Sin is breathing down everyone's necks and they know it. Tidus, for a change, is even more aware about the who whats when where and whys and is scared. A cerain innocent someone with nothing better to do decides to get to know her favorite person a bit better. Meant to be fluffingly fluffy, hopefully in a good way :)
- a Quall. Instead of those MILLIONS of Quistis and her agnst fics, I want Quistis to actually, you know, GET with Squall? Still working on the plot and everything though, ha. I won't be resorting to AUs since I passionately hate those motherfuckers, and hopefully it won't be steadfast. Perhaps I'll tackle the challenge of taking on Squall's POV into making a Quall? OR MAYBE BOTH. AHAHA.
- a fic tentatively titled "Exhibitionism," starring Zell and taking place a few years after the ending of FFVIII. The heroes (and villains) lives are scattered. Fishermans Horizon has always been that sort of place. That place that stays the same to conform to the changes.

DESCRIPTIONS OF WORK FEATURED:
- alchemy destinies - my first fic, a fic I wrote at the grand age of 11. To my surprise, it isn't as embarrassing as I thought it would be and it actually has a plot. heh.
- slight warmth - horrible fic, no plot, horrible portrayal of unrequitted love in a 15yr old. but I still like the coupling and hopefully i'll write a redeemable tikku to compensate.
- how to clean everything - a humorless fic which i somehow thought was funny, oddly enough. It probably only got that many reviews because I begged some of my friends to review it for me, but I love reading the criticisms for the fic. 3
- washing machine - first stab at shounen-ai. I thought I protrayed riku really well, but i remember rereading it sometime before and thinking that the unrequited-ness of it sounded awfully like slight warmth and made riku sound like a 11 year old nsync fan diagnosed with depression. oh well.
- mounted poster - so painfully mushy. i wrote the first 3 chapters of it when i was 12 then sporadically added a new chapter... yeah. ok. NEXT.
- squinoa parody - i like the response to this fic the best. although i have to admit i really don't think the story is funny :( but you know, me and dry sexual innuendo. i'll definately update this.
- this is a firedoor never leave open is definately my favorite fanfic i have attempted to write. full of references to the weakerthans, the BEST BAND in the whole fucking world. my fic at least won't be a waste of time if you've learned about them from it :) and um... yeah. I don't want to play kingdom hearts chain of memories, so just to hell with it if my fic doesn't match up with anything that happens in that stupid game. gr.

That's it. If you want to talk fanfiction with me, I'd love that. AIM = lamPhugger no1. Check out my favorite stories list if you want to read something outside the hookless high school and AU fics littering the sections. I highly recommend A Woman's Harbor, although it's showing signs of a crossover (le sigh) and the author obviously seems to have a life or periodically gets resurrected from death. Oh well.
