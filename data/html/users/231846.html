Hi I’m Orefish, previously Fish, well Fish1, I wanted to be just Fish but some yahoo took the name so I had to be numbered. And now I'm Orefish since my boyfriend got ahold of my name and wanted to read my stuff. Now he can't. AHAHA. Anyways, I enjoy writing fan fiction and I try, note the word "try", to make it seem like an actual novel - you know, something you’d actually want to waste your time on. I’ve grown up some since I’ve started reading and writing fan fiction and I find my tastes have grown up too. I can’t stand mangled half Japanese, half English, all dialogue, stuff I see here and I think other people might feel the same way. So I make it a point to avoid it.

As for genres I enjoy writing romance of all types; girls with girls, boys with boys, and the good old fashioned boys with girls. I feel that most of our decisions and actions in life are done with love in mind, even if it’s controlling things indirectly. I find over coming this catalyst or giving in to it can make for very good entertainment.

Anyways, please enjoy my fiction. : )

5.14.08: Currently, I have a beta version of Everybody Else's Girls up. I wont be updating it, but I will be posting a polished finished peice in the future - and with luck a sequel or expansion to it. So please sit tight.
