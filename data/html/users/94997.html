Hello!

I'm more of a casual fanfic writer than I used to be. My interests tend to randomly shift from one fandom to another, and I know I have a very bad track record when it comes to leaving stories unfinished. Unfortunately, due to the passing of a close friend, I can no longer bring myself to go back to my 'Mario' stories. That doesn't mean that I never will, you understand, but those stories are on a permanent hiatus. To those few who were hoping that I would continue 'Under the Weather' and any others that I've left unfinished, I sincerely apologize. At this time, it's just too painful to go there.

As for my 'Care Bears' series? Wellll...I don't know if I'll ever consider it 'over', but it's on the back burner for now. The muses don't speak to me much anymore, but they poke me every time I think about throwing in the towel, as if to say 'Hey, we're still here!'. There have been a lot of personal setbacks, so it's getting harder and harder to get in the right frame of mind for writing.

So, this account will be updated very rarely, but I haven't quit writing. 'The Hollow' is back off hiatus, for the time being!

I don't post a whole lot of art, what little I do post can be found in my DeviantArt gallery. I go by contraryheartliger over there. Unfortunately, FFN doesn't seem to support links. :P
