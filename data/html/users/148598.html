Hello everyone!

I don't want to bore you with insignificant details of myself...that's why I've listed all my favorite movies and books and pairings below! You should be so excited.

Name: Angel Kitten

Personality: in-betweenish, people say I tend to have mood-swings

Likes: Japanese animation, cats, the internet, Harry Potter, Orlando Bloom & Garrett Hedlund

Dislikes: outer space, big dogs and my (ex) math teacher

Favorites

Movies: Spirited away, Howl's moving Castle, Princess Mononoke, the Birdcage, Forrest Gump, Love actually, Lord of the Rings, Star Wars, Austin Powers, X-men, the Mummy, Bedazzled, the Last Samurai, Four Brothers

Books: Memoirs of a Geisha, To Kill a Mockingbird, Pride and Prejudice, Wuthering Heights, Harry Potter

Anime: Card Captor Sakura, Rurouni Kenshin, Inu yasha, Fruits Basket

Pairings: Sakura/Syaoran, Tomoyo/Touya, Kenshin/Kaoru, Enishi/Kaoru, Kouga/Kagome, Sesshoumaru/Kagome, Tomoyo/Kurogane

I pretty much like my favorite characters paired op with everyone who deserves them, so you could say I enjoy alternative pairings. I've read some yaoi and shouen ai which I liked, but I mostly prefer hetero pairings. (nothing against homosexuals though!)

Characters: Syaoran from Card Captor Sakura, Saitou from Rurouni Kenshin, Kouga and Sesshoumaru from Inu yasha, Shigure and Hatsuharu from Fruits Basket.

As for the Harry Potter series: (it gets its own section : )

Favorite characters: Remus Lupin & Severus Snape

To those of you who are still in denial: no, Snape is not evil. He's one of the most enigmatic and interesting characters I've ever read! Snape has yet a part to play, mark my words. And Lupin...who doesn't love him? He's such a sad and beautiful character. I'm so happy that he and Tonks are together!

Favorite pairings: Remus/Tonks, Ron/Hermione

I used to like Harry/Ginny, but after reading HBP I've changed my mind. I wouldn't want to pair Harry up with a snotty popularity princess. Go Harry/Luna!

Stories

The Power of Two: on hiatus

The Emperor and I: on hiatus

Only Time: on hiatus

The sad truth people! I am a very lazy person and writing doesn't come easy to me, so that is why I prefer one-shots. I am currently busy 'fixing' all my ficlets, as to make them a bit more pleasing to the eye and correcting my grammar. You'll notice the improvement once I'm done.

My newest fic For the man he almost is is a one-shot with Tomoyo and Kurogane in the starring roles. I'm pretty content with the way it turned out.

Only Time and The Emperor and I have been renovated and look a lot better than they used to. The Power of Two is currently under construction.

Ciao for now!

- Angel Kitten ;)
