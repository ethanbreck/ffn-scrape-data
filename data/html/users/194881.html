Hi! I'm Kalloway, it's nice to meet you. I don't post here often - I post on my own site and my livejournal.

I write for Kingdom Hearts, the Complication of FFVII, FFVIII, FFIX, FFIV, Twin Signal, Legacy of Kain, Shadow of Destiny, Grandia 2, Fullmetal Alchemist, Disgaea, Dragonball Z, Arc the Lad, Chaos Legion, Suikoden IV, Ehrgeiz, and quite a few others.

Mostly I write yaoi/shounen-ai/boys love, with a healthy mix of yuri and het.

Please visit my site for the hundreds of pieces that aren't archived here. I've added a few links below.

Also, feedback really does make me write quicker. Or at least pay attention to a story. Sometimes when I finish a chapter, I totally forget that I need to keep removing the words from my head for other people to read and when I get reviews/e-mail, that reminds me.
