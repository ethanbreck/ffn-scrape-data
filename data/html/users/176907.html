Hi everybody!

I'm Danielle from Austria (you don't have to know the city - but it's called Leoben).

I'm a total Wrestling-addict! I'm a proud Chain Gang member (I am the only one in Leoben going outside in WWE t-shirts everyday) and Edgehead. I can't help myself - I just love the two guys.

My hobbies are reading and writing fanfics (my biggest German WWE story has over 1100 pages at the moment with no end in sight ...), watching sports, surfing the internet, shopping, working, sleeping ...

Now I finally got to post my oneshots I wrote recently after Survivor Series gave me some ideas. Now I posted my new story, with a very dark content. I was very unsure about it, to be honest, but I'll give it a try.

I'd love to get to know other Wrestling fans or fanfiction writers, so feel free to e-mail me at daniellecena@gmail.com :-)

Everybody else has a list of his/her favourite wrestlers, so I decided that I can't stand behind.

Wrestlers:

John Cena: coughs At first I absolutely didn't like him, but during the last two years he totally grew on me, and now I am a proud Chain Gang member (you should see my apartment - totally Cena hehe!!). He really has developped, and just like Edge he is a great entertainer. And now I find him sexy as hell. :-) What can I say? Many many people will understand me. He doesn't need to be champ, but okay. As long as he doesn't hold the belt for another year I can live with it. Randy sometime had to lose the title, but I would loved to happen it with another person whose name is not John Cena or Triple H. Okay, he wasn't champ for long, so woohoo! He's better as a contender. Great for Randy that he has the belt again. But sad that this storyline seems to go on ... again and again and again ... meh.

Edge: I don't care which stupid things he has done in his private life (although I use some of the stuff in my stories), but he's a hell of an entertainer! He's one of the best technicians in the business and I totally respect what he has done for the company (and that he came back after that terrible injury!). And he's damn sexy too and plays an absolute fantastic heel! Sad he's injured, but it seems like he will be back at the Rumble (and hopefully win the damn thing hehe).

Randy Orton: Rated-RKO were fantastic and he also was a great champion. And he will always be a great champion. I love his psycho image. Yeah for another Age Of Orton reign! He's one of the best heels in the business. I really like him.

Chris Jericho: Love his book, love Fozzy, love him. Great heel, totally entertaining and a wonderful technician who can have a good match with everybody. The perfect wrestler, imo. Was great to see him wrestle in Vienna. I totally marked out. hehe

Shawn Michaels: Fantastic storyline with Chris, totally entertaining. He's just great. Enough said. And DX is the only thing I like about Triple H. hehe

Divas:

Beth Phoenix: I just LOVE this woman. She's fantastic. Love her looks and especially her hard wrestling technique. Very unique.

Jillian: Fantastic wrestler, sad that she's so underrated. :-(

Christy Hemme: Sad that she had to leave, but she's doing great in TNA.

Melina: If there wasn't her screaming ... but she definitely has some good wrestling skills.

Mickie: Fantastic in-ring-work. She's so sweet.

Trish: Will be missed forever. Loved her as guest host of RAW.

I also watch TNA regularly, and after I saw some clips of Christopher Daniels, I began to totally adore him. This guy is just awesome and fantastic (yay, no more Curryman! woohoo). Other favourites are Samoa Joe, Kurt Angle (I liked him in WWE and I still like him in TNA) ... there are definitely more, but I can't remember right now. Tara comes to mind, she was great as Victoria and is even greater as Tara.

Kisses,

Danielle :-)
