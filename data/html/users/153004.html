I'm also a HUGE Firefly fan. Mal is my favorite character. Love River as well.

There are several other shows that I really like and may, eventual, dabble in.

7/16/06
Do to a reply posted to one of my fic's, I feel I need to make something perfectly clear, I am NOT a Jack/Sam shipper, never have been and never will be. I may, on occassion, write a UST fic, but that's about as far as it goes. I just don't see anything more than a strong friendship and attraction between them. So, if you see Sam and Jack as the characters in any of my fic's, just know right off the bat that it will NOT be a ship fic!

Don't get me wrong. I have nothing against shippers. Everyone has a right to their opinion. Heck, I've even read a few ship fic's ('Supporting Rumors' by Jackie O'Neill Nut and 'Wives and Other Follies' by Flatkatsi immediately come to mind here) that I thought were extremely well written and handled the relationship in an enjoyable and believable manner.
