Hello, you poor misguided person who does not have anything better to do than to read my bio! :) (Some people have so much free time...)

Well, what can I say about myself...
I'm a big DBZ-fan, but I actually found the site when I was looking for Dexter's laboratory fics. Besides these two I also read some .HACK/SIGN (a really cool new anime), X-men Evolution (a great new take on the old comic) and Serial Experiments Lain fics nowadays. (If you're looking for something you have to watch at least 3 times before it starts to make sense, this is for you.)

I'm writing for many years now, and I have some amount of fanfiction stories also written. (Mostly DBZ.) Why am I not posting them, you ask? Well, they are all written in hungarian, and I don't want to run into another hungarian-english-translating pit, as I did with the poor "A hero is born" fic. No matter how hard I tried, I couldn't translate it correctly, that is why I didn't post it entirely. I do have some english stories in mind, I just don't have the time to write them, taking my huge original hungarian fantasy-fiction projects into account.
