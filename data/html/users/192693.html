Favorite shows to watch-Bones, Burn Notice, CSI, MASH, Castle, NCIS, Law & Order (all but SVU)-and I absolutely loved Leverage when it was still on.

Weird fact about me/my family-my older sister (there are 4 of us) and I have a birthday 2 days apart. Mine's on April 21st and hers is on April 23rd. Scary but true-we're almost exactly two years apart.

I've been addicted to reading for forever-and I'll read just about anything except horror. I admit romance novels are my guilty pleasure. My favorite authors are Agatha Christie, Alison Weir, Jane Austen,Terry Goodkind, Diana Gabaldon (her Outlander series may be one of my favorite things to read of all time) Janet Evanovich, Nancy Martin--and probably a lot of others -too many to list here probably.

My dad passed away 10 years ago, and there are times I still miss him dreadfully-I was always a "Daddy's girl"
