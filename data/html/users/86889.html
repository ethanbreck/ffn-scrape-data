Hi, I'm Lucky Chan (yup...it's actually a pun in Filipino...yes, I'm from the Philippines, and damn proud of it), although I'm also known as Misty on the net.

As you can probably see, I love anime. I love Yu Yu Hakusho, Hunter X Hunter, Rurouni Kenshin, Groove Adventure Rave, Naruto, Yakitate! Japan...and that's just to name a few.

I'm picky with the pairings I write for (I tend to have one main OTP for each series, and I remain faithful for that pairing forever and ever, etc), and I write only whenI feel like it. Every now and then, I can take requests, too.

Comments are always very much appreciated. (I love getting reviews. Really. So please do.)

Disclaimer (since I always forget) : The characters and places used in my stories are not mine, for theybelong to their respective creators.

My sites:

It's exactly what the name says it is.You can find everything here from book and movie reviews,attempts at making decent icons and graphics, my writings(original or fanfiction), details on my real (but not personal) life. I wonder why you'll want to go here, though.
