Hello, my friends!

Since what people write reflects what they believe, I supose I should clear some things up for you.

There may or may not be some boy/boy and girl/girl pairings; it all depends whether I like the couple or not. I have no problems whatsoever with those couples.

Eventually I will run across some religious grounds. I will keep it as unbiased as I possibly can. I think of myself as an open-minded person (hopefully!) so I hope no one takes offense.

I have no problems with using history or taking an artistic liscence (sp?) to it. If it is about a tragedy, such as the Holocaust or 9/11, understand that I am writing it with no disrespect towards the dead.

I will also not write or read fics with sex in them. Some suggestiveness is okay (Hey, I'm a Friends fan, too!) but that's as far as it goes.

There will be constant reminders of this in the stories I write.

I've taken down "Runaways", "The Life and Times of 18", and "Love Is Blind". I'm so sorry, Deadly Beauty!

Also, I do not flame or appreciate them. If you flame me, I can either block you or delete you. I don't see the point in wasting your time flaming a story you didn't even like to begin with. And I also don't get why flamers put "Have a nice day" at the end of their reviews. How am I supposed to have a nice day now that you've bashed the story I worked so hard on?

A note: I can't e-mail you, so I have to communicate in reviews. If you have no account, look for an A/N in the story or check back here.

Well, enough of that serious stuff, let's get on to some other stuff!
I'm Anime/Manga-hungry and I watch too much TV. I also like to read, sing, draw, and obviously, write. I love history, too. I'm also interested in psychology and philosophy.
