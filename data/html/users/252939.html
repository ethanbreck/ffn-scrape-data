Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Silmarillion.

I'm Cerasi, and I'm pretty sure it's time to change this profile, since it previously said I'm fourteen... but it's been five years since that update. Wow, that's a ridiculously long time.

I'm a sophomore at Ohio State University (go bucks!) and I'm returning as a writer for the first time in many years. I regret to inform anyone who was still waiting on White Angel that I will never finish it. I reread it recently and it is kind of terrible, even for my thirteen year-old self. I have just found several notebook full of completed fanfics, chapters of fics that I don't remember writing, and short ficlets that I may or may not post.

I am currently working on revising and expanding "In the Murk of Mirkwood Forest," a Legolas/Aragorn fic that I wrote when I was thirteen. Over the next few weeks I will be completing and posting that. Once I finish it, I have ideas for a sequel floating around in my mind, but we shall see if I can manage my time enough to write a whole new story, one that would probably take a long time to complete.

As far as being a reader goes, the only category I have been reading lately is Lord of the Rings. My fave pairings are:

Legolas/ Aragorn

Legolas/OC (but good ones are always hard to find)

Legolas/Elladen/Elrohir

Elladen/Elrohir

Legolas/Haldir

Aragorn/Eowyn

Aragorn/Haldir

...so I'm pretty much a slash fan at the moment.

Please check out my stories and leave me reviews so that I can check out yours!!
