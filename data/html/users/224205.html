I guess I better put something up here. I'm never sure if anyone actually reads these things or not but still.

I've been writing on and off for over 15 years.

I'm a gamer and have Baldur's Gate I and II, Neverwinter Nights and Neverwinter Nights 2, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic as well as Dragon Age: Origins and Inquisition, and The Witcher 3. I also have Skyrim, Grim Dawn, Icewind Dale, Titan Quest: Anniversary Edition, Diablo III and Mass Effect: Andromeda. All of which I have on my gaming PC.

I've been playing SW:TOR on and off since its launch which is very unusual for me as I tend to avoid MMO's like the plague. However I got the chance to play the Beta and I was sold. :)

My avatar is a picture of D1041 Western Prince which is one of the seven persevered D1000 class 'Western' Diesel-Hydraulics. Yes I like my trains, both the real thing and in model from.
