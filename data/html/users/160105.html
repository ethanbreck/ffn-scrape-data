Greetings, salutations, and a general, good old-fashoned howdy! I decided it was high time to update my bio...so... here goes!

HP: As always, my favorite Harry Potter character is the one and only Severus Snape. (Yay to the might house of Slytherin!) I have successfully read all five of the wizarding-wonder books, but you'll get no spoilers in this bio!

For those of you who have visited here before, this may be a repeat, but I don't like to keep anyone out in the cold, so humor me for a few lines! =) I just recently graduated from college with my RN, BSN. This is the reason that it took forever for me to post anything. Now that I only have to contend with the "real world" I should be able to post more regularly. (Yay!) I am but a lowly nurse working in surgery, writing short stories so that the plot-bunnies do not take over my humble home.

Travelling tops my list of favorite things to do. (It ranks on a list that includes singing, writing, reading, canoeing, hiking, movies, and goofing off with friends.) I have been blessed to travel to Mexico, Canada, Honduras, Nicaragua, Spain and Germany...and for those who are wondering GERMANY WAS AWESOME...so was Spain...alright, so I thought they were all pretty cool. As far as local travel, my friend Calliope and I just went to a Renaissance fair about two hours away from our city. For those of you who like fantasy and have never been to a Renaissance fair of any kind, here is my advice to you...GO! GO NOW!! GO IN COSTUME!! BUY COOL THINGS!!! TELL THEM MADAM ARIANNA SENT YA! Okay, honestly, they won't have a clue who I am, but you'll have fun, none the less. Oh, and be sure you take your camera. Swipe all of the costume ideas that you can without actually having to swipe the costumes. =)

I am currently working on my second fan-fic (HP route, of course), and in doing so everyone will finally be introduced to my friend, Calliope. (Yay!) Okay, so that isn't her real life name, but she has created a super-cool character in her stead. And yes, I am rambling...

Well, now that I have added a bit of spice to my bio, I leave you all with a quote worth pondering...

Choose your battles wisely. Will your fight make a difference in ten years, or will it just be wasted time that could have been spent constructively on something that would have enhanced your life or the life of another?

Hmmm...stay happy, keep reading, and long live pasta!

Love,

~Madam Arianna
