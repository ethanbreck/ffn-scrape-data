Author has written 5 stories for M*A*S*H, and Supernatural.

After over ten years of being unable to write I am thrilled to say that I'm back at the keyboard! Or, ok, at least tinkering at it when my brain decides to cooperate.

Completing a story that was the length of a typical published work of fiction was a first for me, and boosted my confidence enough to get me back into writing. Becoming somewhat sane again (as in, largely winning the constant battle against my bipolar disorder) might've been a bit helpful as well.

I'd like to apologize to all the reviewers who left notes on my unfinished stories, which I've removed from this site because 1) my writing style has vastly improved since I was in high school, and after poking at what I already written I judged them irredeemable and frankly embarrassing, and 2) I felt guilty every time someone begged for an update, knowing that there's a 99.98% certainty I will never finish those fics. My interests have shifted in the decade since I've written for those fandoms, and I no longer find myself immersed in TYR, LotR, or SVU. I will always look back on the time I spent in those universes fondly, but I don't see myself jumping back into them.

Still, let me say, to all of those have reviewed: you have no idea how much it's meant to me.

The reviews I received for those stories were saved in multiple formats before the fics were deleted, and still encourage me to this day.

I hope that you can enjoy my more recent works in the MASH and Supernatural fandoms and would love to hear your thoughts on any of those. Thanks for reading, and, most of all, thanks to everyone who have left reviews on my works.

Note that I've taken a lot of my fanfic-related activity to AO3, due to the much wider array of filters, and the kudos feature, which makes it a much more reliable way to judge readers' reactions to your works. While most readers won't take the time to leave a comment on a work, even if they truly enjoy it, a click of a button can at least tell the author that someone out there appreciated the time and effort they put into a story.

You can find me on AO3 under the same name - lastarael.
