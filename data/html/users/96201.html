Misc: I've got a pretty bad temper, but I'm able to laugh at myself easily. Nothing drives me more crazy than someone who calls me childish because of my enjoyment of writing for a series I enjoy, like Inuyasha. I'm open for suggestions on most fanfiction, and I love reader input. I also love comments, and not surprisingly, they tend to make me write faster. Even so, usually it takes me anywhere from 6 months to a year to finish a multi chapter fanfic, because I sometimes tend to burn out on fanfiction and decide to work on my original stories or manga. I have roughly twenty original stories in the works, and I love all the characters, but due to the demands which are sometimes placed upon me by my adoring fans on this web site, none are completed, inspite of my 200+ completed works of fanfiction. This is a rough guestimation number, based on what is not posted on ff.net, but instead resides on a hard drive that was in my old computer.

A general notice for all of my readers:

I'm sorry for how long it has been since I have updated several of my stories. Although this page makes it seem otherwise, I've not been inactive in the past few years. My love and interest in the fandoms posted here have simply waned, and while I still love them and hope to continue a few of the stories (probably not all of them), most of this is currently in it's final form. I've been doing a great deal of work on my deviantart page, as well as under my other pen name "chibi-no-oneesan". I have not forgotten you all, and I greatly appreciate all of those who have kept up with my stories. I will try very hard to continue them, or at least give them endings which don't seem tacked on.

The fact is, my writing style and particular preference of 'hero' character have changed a bit. I don't have much free time anymore, either, due to my personal life. I'm not leaving the fandoms. Let's just say, I'm now dormant.

Thanks all.

-DS
