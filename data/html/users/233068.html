Author has written 17 stories for Slam Dunk, Diablo, Trigun, Weiss Kreuz, and Rurouni Kenshin.

I'm probably dead to the world right now, having too much to do in so little time. And if you expect me to write the continuation of my stories without even having people to read it, then you're ready to wipe your ass clean yourself. But to think of it, the main purpose of writing a story is not to have people read it, it's pointless if that is the case. For me, I've settled that my purpose in writing is to actually satisfy MYSELF and not other people. Reviews are just assessments to how I am progressing with the coherence and interest factor of the story. So far I have only satisfied myself with my writing only ONCE, and that's with one chapter of metallseele and the one-shot fic: winter night's rain. It's 2005 now and my stories are waaaaay back in 2003. How do I assess myself now? Did I develop? WHAT WHAT? Will the people like my incoherent attempt at telling a bleak story?

I tell you this, my friends. Probably after I've satisfied myself with my studies, I will satisfy you with my writing. Writing a chapter at this time would perhaps not satisfy you. Who would be satisfied with a chapter brought by the laziness of the writer? Perhaps not. And that wouldn't satisfy me either.

I guess I still have to lend myself time to improve, seeing my style isn't really appealing to myself and to others yet.

Watch out, people, just watch out.

as of now, I'm trying my best to write a vampire story and attempting to write a new chapter of metallseele.
