It is with my own mind that I shall never submit a story onto this site. My skills will never amount to the greatness I've preveiwed on this site of tales and stories. I have decided I shall only read and browse through the site, leaving my mark behind with a reveiw if I feel up to it. I have chosen to nevermore post a story again, though I doubt it would matter. It is now that I say my tales and stories shall never leave my imagination nor mind but will never be touch the blue-lined colleged rule paper beside my desk or the computer I am now typing on...

Perhaps I will change that idea, because I have the sudden erge to continue my story of my Feline Royals. This is the only inspiration that will drive me to continue writing and because I seem to draw alot more then I write. Poems and such are an exception because I usually engulf too much depression I let my feelings flow out of my ink pen or graphite pencil tip.

Forever I will remain faithfull to this site, visiting those very few topics I enjoy reading over. (Fruits Basket being the new favorite.)

I still pray I will get back into my writing habit even though it was never popular in public. So until then, my school work shall continue to drop until I am caught in that 'hole' of which they speak. Whatever the hell that is. I wish for help because my mind is everywhere but focused on work.

So until again my daydreams will forever remain episodes of what could've been and if by chance shall be once more someday.
