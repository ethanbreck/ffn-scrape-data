Author has written 6 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Transformers/Beast Wars, One Piece, and Ninja Turtles.

Hey all! I'm a crazed anime fan to the core. XD

Anyway, in my fics I prefer to have characters get into relationships gradually, none of that love-at-first-sight garbage. Just about all of my fics include my own character so don't say I didn't warn you.

My latest obsession is One Piece. But I also like sCRYed, Full Metal Alchemist, Transformers (all of them), Ultimate Muscle, DBZ, Gundam Wing, G Gundam, Rurouni Kenshin, CardCaptor Sakura (subbed & dubbed), Fushigi Yuugi. I like just about any kind of anime I can get my hands on; execpt hentai. That stuff is just wrong. I'm not a big fan of yaoi but I will read it and post comments if I like it.

I currently have about 20+ fanfics going at once so forgive me if I'm a little slow on updating. Once I get an idea in my head for a story my mind won't let me be until I write it down.

I am open to all types of CONSTRUCTIVE criticism. Flames will be occassionally read then thrown into the fireplace. Oh the warmth!
