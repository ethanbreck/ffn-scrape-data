About me:

Occupation: Animator In-Training

Interests: Creative stuff, cartoons, music, animals, Disneyland, whatever.

Where you can usually find me: Disneyland or Starbucks (That should tell you I'm in California)

I started writing fictions early in my life. Granted they weren't any good, but it was a start for my creativity. My "Crazy-Fan-Girl" (yes, I'm a girl) status started with Lion King, when I first saw Timon. He was great! Funny, witty, small... okay, it was his voice that caught my attention. Since that day, I had wanted to lend my voice for a character. It was around this same time that I started drawing (and the drawing actually looked like the object I drew). I eventually gave up on voice acting after realizing it wasn't for me, and turned my attentions toward a career in animating. I figured if I couldn't voice those cute little characters, I would bring them to life instead. A part of me still hopes to someday lend my voice to a character though. I think it'd be cool.

Well, as of today, I still draw and write, except now I'm better at both. I'm still a big kid (even though I'm over 21 now) and I never let my age get me down. I still go to Disneyland on a weekly basis. If you go, chances are you've probably seen me. Haha!

Click the "Homepage" link and you'll be taken to my DeviantArt page.

Favorite characters:

I'm obsessed with cartoons! Ask anyone I know, they'll tell you I'm a loser. (No, I'm serious, I am.) And I'm damn proud of it! Ha ha. Here's the list of my top 5 favorites (as of 7/21/09):

- Buck (Ice Age Dawn of the Dinosaurs)

- Dr. Cockroach (Monsters vs. Aliens)

- Beast Boy (Teen Titans)

- Tinker Bell

- Toph (Avatar)

Dreams & Goals!

Animator/writer! That's what I'm aiming for. It's always been my dream to see my name on the big screen.

I'm very close to accomplishing this goal.

My Words of Advice!

KEEP THE CHILD ALIVE IN YOUR HEART. (I can't stress this enough, people.)
