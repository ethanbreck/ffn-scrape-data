Author has written 5 stories for Warcraft.

I only write as I get the urge. I love being creative and coming up with various stories, but have not really written them down much. Although, I've put other stories up on the web via my own website, but that was awhile ago and the website is now down. Maybe I'll upload them here some day.

I strive to make the characters real, and enjoy being descriptive. I also enjoy the English language and try to keep it consistent, though I may make some typo or grammatical errors from time to time. Having two young children can keep me distracted enough to not notice. :)

3/19/12 - Well, I've finished Of Thorns and Wilted Roses. It was an interesting challenge dealing with four main characters and numerous supporting characters. Thanks much to my sister-in-law for her edits and beta reading for this story. She was a big help.

While I was writing OTAWR, I glanced over Defiant Vengeance and notice quite a number of errors. I plan to fix those as I have time, one chapter at a time. I also have Unseen sitting around, waiting for it's turn to be fleshed out into a full length story. I'm still undecided which direction I want to take it, though, so it may be a little bit before I update it. And when I do, it'll be updated slowly because I have about three original novel ideas that I have been wanting to work on.

12/13/11 - After reviewing chapter 14 of Ileeya's Thorn, I've found that I agree with some of my reader's that Ileeya was too laid back. And I decided that I had not revealed enough information about what had happened between her and Kirlothenin. So, I rewrote the chapter. The outcome is the same. But now it has more...emotion and details. This unfortunately has lead the chapter to being twice as long as before, but the longevity of it is worth the change, in my opinion. :)

12/07/11 - I've gotten a few inquiries about sequels to my stories. I just wanted to let anyone know who might happen to glance at this profile page that I am in process of putting together a sequel...to both stories. Yes, Ileeya, Kirlothenin, Adorei, Adam, Kristi, and Amy will all come together for another unexpected adventure. Half of the first chapter is already written, but I have some research I need to finish before I can complete it. :) The chapter will be published...soon. :)

11/30/11 - My second story, "Defiant Vengeance" is finished. The inspiration for this story came from back when I had run the Deadmines instance. For some reason I wanted to see the juggernaut leave the cavern. I knew Adam was going to star in his own fic after creating him for Ileeya's Thorn. As I was doing research for story ideas to involve these two, I came across the profile for Marisa du'Paige and pretty much came up with the plot line from there.

I have some small ideas for my next fic, but nothing substantial, yet. There's a few character's from Defiant Vengeance I wouldn't mind delving deeper into (Maggie, Ishana, Quentin Bandor), but first I think I want to review Ileeya's Thorn and clean it up from spelling and grammar mistakes, as well as update some small bits with a little more description for those of my family and friends who wish to read the story but don't know the Warcraft terms and story. Perhaps I'll get inspiration for a sequel. We'll see. :)

09/11 - Finished with my first full fic here, "Ileeya's Thorn". It was a learning experience. I set two goals for myself on this one. The first was to see if I could get my readers to think one thing about a character, then turn around and feel a different way. The second was to see if I could write two people together who have the odds stacked against them. It's up to the individual reader if I was successful or not. :)
