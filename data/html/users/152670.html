October 16, 2012


Okay, it has been way too long since I last even updated this. I haven't honestly abandoned any of my fics or just disappeared without a trace. I've more been trying to find time as well as motivation to figure out where to go with some of my stories. I have also thought about redoing some of them like my Gravitation fic Writer's Winter. I like the story, but it really could use a lot of editing. Especially now that I have gotten loads better with writing. I'll eventually get back to my Naruto and Yu Yu Hakusho fics, but I need to figure out what I really want to do with them and I just cannot really remember everything I wanted to do with them.

Meanwhile, I am definitely planning to do some Harry Potter fics. Don't know why, but Harry Potter has been an addiction lately. So eventually I will start writing for them. I also might try writing another Gravitation story, because I really do like the characters of Gravi, even they are pretty crazy. So, basically, I will be getting to writing something some time soon. Just not sure when yet. So stayed tuned, and sorry for the long wait. I'll try to keep you all posted, or anyone that is actually interested.

For anyone interested in role-playing for Gravitation, a friend of mine and I have a forum for such a thing. Or if you are just interested in checking it out, you can visit it here.

December 20, 2006
