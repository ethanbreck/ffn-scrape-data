Okay, just because I can't get people to leave me alone after annoying the crap out of me after I tell them a few things...

I lost a whole bunch of outlines for the stories I write. Because of that, I am posting the criteria I have for finishing my stories.

1) Interest. Does anyone even care about this story?

2) Heck, do I even remember the original plot?

3) Do I want to invest the time in this stuff anymore?

4) Hmm... let's see... at the end of those 18 hour work days on this film set, after holding up either a large boom mic, or a very heavy shoulder camera on my already stiff shoulder because I did exactly that yesterday, and the day before that, and the day before that, would I like to write a fanfiction that I may or may not care about, work on my real novel that my editor thinks could make me enough money that I won't have to work 18 hour days under hot film lights, or sleep because I know that I have another 18 hour day to get that documentary finished in time to make some money so I can take a break to work on the novel that will make me real money?

Fics that didn't make the cut and the criteria I lost them at:

-Heroes Past and Heroes Present (lost at criteria 2. And then 4 when I thought of a new way this could go.)

-If Only They Were Dreams (lost at criteria 1 for a while, but suddenly I had a wave of interest. On the attempt for another chapter, it crossed into criteria 2)

-Pampered to Death (Criteria 1. I actually have the outline for this. Nobody's interested.)

-Power Rangers: Final Force (Criteria 4. I Think it could be cool, but I'd rather sleep)

-Secret Weapon (Criteria 1-4. Who cares? I don't care! Why would you care if I don't?)

-Wreaking Havoc on the Family (Criteria 1-4. Actually, I think it got flagged once, for reasons I don't know. No comments were left)

So, please, to those of you who were stalking me on myspace (I got rid of that account) and who are trying to stalk me on facebook (here's a hint: my profile is locked, and D.E. Lewis isn't my real name), STOP ASKING ME TO FINISH THESE FICS!!

Seriously.

It's getting old.
