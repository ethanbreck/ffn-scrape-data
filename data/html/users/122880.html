Soooo... Hi. ;; If you're actually reading this, you've either noted that I've posted a new story, or you're checking up on me because you're reading one of my old ones. Either way, I'm happy you're reading this at all! Hehe.

My newest story is Sunset, and it is a fanfic set in the Twilight-spawned series by Stephenie Meyer. They are awesome books - I bought the first one on a whim after a friend (Jade-chan) bugging me to read it for months. Well, I was hooked. I brought it back to college with me that same day, finished it, and borrowed the next two from my roommate. They're so good! Ms. Meyer really is an amazing author. Her characters are so real, so compelling... and I hope to have brought you at least a small degree of that in my OC-narrated fanfiction. Please don't turn away because it's narrated by an original character - if you read my author's notes within said story, you'll understand why I'm asking you to give it a chance. And if you do read it, don't forget to review. - Constructive criticism is great, but flames will be used to toast marshmallows for s'mores. And do try to use some proper grammar. "i rly liked it, plz rite more 4 me!!!!!!!" is not really hearty incentive. .~

For those of you who are trying to check up on old stories, bear with me. I apologize, but a lot of them are going to be disappearing or going through some major re-writing. In the few years I've been away from posting, I've developed a lot as a writer, and I'd like to bring you some of the same characters and storylines with a lot more maturity and a much better flow. So don't give up hope! Keep checking back. Oh, and I do check my e-mail at least every other day, so feel free to drop me a line there if you have any questions or comments. Okay?

I wish you all pens that never run out of ink, stories that never grow old, and imaginations that know no limits. Soar with grace.
