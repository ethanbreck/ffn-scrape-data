[Omg where are the updates?! / Author's Excuses Section ]

October 6, 2011 --

Think I may be done with Fanfiction. I thought, perhaps, rewriting Yaoi High would re-inspire me to work on fanfics...but we all see how well that turned out with the lack of updates. I didn't want to say everything was discontinued because I thought I would eventually want to come back to them to finish everything I started. I know I said I would finish Yaoi High, and when I said that, I really did believe it. But as of late -- with college, life, and wanting to work on original stuff-- I think I may be done with Fanfiction.

I like to think I grew as a writer because of this site--because of my readers. So thank you everyone. Thank you for the fanart. Thank you for the reviews. Thank you for just reading. Thank you for the encouragement. Thank you for the inspiration. I do apologize for being a fickle writer. But I don't think I would have ever started writing if it wasn't for this site and the people in it.

So, thank you.
