Hello. I'm ltnikki. I am a very not so average 14 year old girl. My personality is not what you would call "nice" or "sweet." Actually, it is the exact opposite. I am just brutally honest. I usually don't care much about what people think and i just love being me. It doesn't matter if i'm liked or not, i just want to be the person that i am and not anything more or less.

My interest pretty much revolves around Anime. I won't bore you with specifics but i do believe i am a pretty rabid anime fan, although i don't tend to read too many fics on it.
I also love Harry Potter. I am obessed with the marauders and everything to do with them. I love Lily and James fics and if you like them, take a stroll at my favorites. Most of them are amazingly good.

I am not a fan of fluff or sap at all.

I have offically written my first real fic, which is more of a poem anyways. I just wrote it in the dead of night and R/hr fan forced me into uploading it on ff.net at 1:30 in the morning. Its called Veiled. I won't bore you with summaries because its right there at the bottom.
