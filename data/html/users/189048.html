The Me Stuff:

I was always an artist before a writer, and a penciller before I could paint. Now I tend to fill my sketchbooks with scribbled scenes from daily life on campus and random cartooning as I remember how to draw.

Incidentally, I read A LOT of fanfiction. Sporadically write it as well. And by sporadic I mean sporadic. I make no excuses about this. My writing is not great or mind blowing, and it's not the air I breathe. But I have fun doing it and for me that is what counts.

I have a pet peeve and it is this: author's notes in the middle of a story, especially when the author is explaining some key plot point. If your story can't do that on its own, I'm out. It breaks the flow of a story and you're being a lazy author. Fix it. Fix it now. Love you.

The story stuff:

If I haven't updated a story in a while chances are it will not get updated. Sorry! However, you may cling fast to that slender thread of hope. There is always hope.

Sometimes I post up one-shots. And I would like to take this moment to point out that if it is labeled 'complete' or says 'ONE-SHOT' in the summary, that means that I will not be writing more and a review asking for more is superfluous, pointless, unnecessary, without purpose... get the idea? I don't mind the reviews, just don't get all upset when there isn't more, eh?

"Forget all the rules. Forget about being published. Write for yourself and celebrate writing." -- Melinda Haynes
