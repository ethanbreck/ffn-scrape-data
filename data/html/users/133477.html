Hi, welcome to my profile, I'm Breeze and have been wandering around this website since 2001!

I've recently got back into writing in a major way and I absolutely love it!

As a writer I like to fill in the gaps, especially with my full length fics. If the author has left a gap I love expanding and exploring what that gap - be it culture, character or land - might be like.

I primarily write in the Chronicles of Narnia at the moment, but have plans to return to Middle Earth in future fiction I write.

Here's a little bit about me:

I'm imaginative, bubbly, a bit of a dreamer sometimes, a teacher, a reader, a writer, a walker, a horse rider and a sailor.

I love so many random and wonderful things - earl grey tea, the first blue bells in Spring, mangoes, the feel of the sun on my face, walking barefoot on grass, Narnia, the Lord of the Rings, Merlin, fantasy, a great book that I can't put down, cantering on a horse through the countryside, theatre, singing for the pure joy of it, laughter, a warm hot chocolate on a snowy day, sunlight through the leaves of a tree, sailing, teaching, English, seeing children achieve and realise their accomplishment, Arthurian legend, laughing with my friends, baking cupcakes, the love of my life, our amazing creator and saviour.

In 2015 I have made myself a promise that I will never not finish a story again, so if you have reviewed any of my work in the past or are going to, thank you so much.

In the mean time, thank you so much for reading and reviewing my writing, it really does mean the world to me.

Breeze :)
