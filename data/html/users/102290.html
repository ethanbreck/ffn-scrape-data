Author has written 65 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Harry Potter, Buffy X-overs, Laurie R. King, Doctor Who, Babylon 5, Avatar: Last Airbender, and Legend of Korra.

LizBee likes to escape reality by playing with other people's characters.

She likes clever dialogue, well-constructed plots, intelligent humour and stories that, in general, don't suck. She dislikes stories that take perfectly good existing characters and make them stupid, weak or vapid for no good reason. And bad spelling. And stories with no point. Stories that don't make sense. Look, she may not know art, but she knows what she hates.

In short, LizBee is the cantankerous old woman of fandom. You will find her on her porch, nursing a shotgun and throwing stray cats at passers by. Or on Tumblr at liz-squids.tumblr.com.
