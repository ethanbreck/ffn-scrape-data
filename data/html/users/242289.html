Author has written 27 stories for Yu Yu Hakusho, Death Note, Ouran High School Host Club, and Supernatural.

It's probably best not to expect much from me. Most of my writing these days is original fiction, so I can't post it on this site. What little fanfic I do write never gets finished. Regardless, I may post or update something in the future. I'd really like to write more stories for Yu Yu Hakusho or even continue the ones that are still listed as WIP. My old fics would probably need some serious overhauling, though. Even the ones from just five years ago make me cringe a little.

Anime that I watched when I was younger and still enjoy today include Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing, Inuyasha, and Card Captor Sakura. That should give you an idea of how old I am. I like my anime serious but with a happy ending or humorous but with a legitimate plot—not too heavy and not too silly. I prefer same-sex couples, but only if they are more than just fanservice. Too many anime these days have jumped on the shipteasing bandwagon, and I am tired of it. I want to see romantic relationships that actually mean something and go somewhere. This is probably why I'm so leery of watching anime that haven't already finished airing. I don't want to start shipping a couple and then find out in the last few episodes that, just kidding, they were straight after all.

When it comes to live-action tv shows, I virtually never ship gay or lesbian pairings. Canon gay and lesbian couples tend to get together fast and become boring even faster, and they invariably end in breakups or death. I like Destiel from Supernatural, though.

WIPs

Yu Yu Hakusho Saisei Project Honestly, I hadn't even intended to rewrite the entire series in the first place. I only meant to flesh out the Dark Treasures case. It turned into a full-series endeavor because my best friend adored That Which Comes Between Two Mountains and urged me to write more. I tried to continue Saisei without her, but feedback from people on the internet just couldn't replace her encouragement. When I received no feedback at all? It didn't feel worth it. Regardless, I'll try to finish the stories that are already in-progress, and I might re-post the one that I deleted out of frustration. I intend to complete the first season and give this project some closure, but I doubt that I'll continue on with the planned second and third seasons.

Be But Sworn My Love I'm actually working on the second and third chapters of this one at the same time. Progress is slow, but I have real hope for completing it.

Guard I'm working on all the remaining chapters, so it probably won't be updated for a while. When I do update it, you can expect several chapters in a relatively short period. This is pretty much the only fic that I've written that involves an intersex character, and I probably botched it horribly, but it was the best that I could do at the time.

Perceptions This...probably wouldn't have had a happy ending, so I doubt that I'll finish it. I've rather lost my taste for tragedies.

Once Upon a Time... Um? Maybe someday? I won't say that I've abandoned it, but I wouldn't hold my breath.

Like the Red Rose... Definitely not going to be finished.
