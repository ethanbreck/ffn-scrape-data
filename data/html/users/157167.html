Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter.

I'm 19 years old, a chronic bookworm, and addicted to coffee. I also happen to be a first year English major. :O I pretty much live in Borders. It's scary the amount of money I spend in that place.

I usually go through a book or two a week during the school year, more during the summer. I'm also legally blind, thanks to many a late night reading session and staring at a monitor until the wee hours of the morning reading fanfiction. Some of my favorite books include Harry Potter, His Dark Materials, Pride and Prejudice, the Abhorsen trilogy, Lord of the Rings, The Truth About Forever, anything else by Sarah Dessen really, The Alchemist, The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, and The Princess Bride.

Harry Potter is mainly the only fandom I will write here, but I might consider writing some LOST or maybe Pirates or something. Who knows? I'm currently obsessed with The Office as well, so maybe some fics for that fandom, too.

Ship wise, in regards to Harry Potter, I avidly support most of the cannon ships: Ron/Hermione, Lily/James, Harry/Ginny. Luna and Neville are very cute as well.I also ship Remus/Sirius. Is anyone else saddened by the Remus/Tonks thing? Sooo random. sigh Alas.

Anyhoo, I suppose that's enough for now. I'd loooove if you would check out some of my fics and maybe, just maybe, review. Reviews are gooood.
