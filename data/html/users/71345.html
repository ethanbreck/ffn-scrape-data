I'm a 16 year old authoress currently residing in the great and almighty Edmonton ("Losing the Stanley Cup since Gretzky left!") in the great and almighty Alberta ("Home of three-quarters of the world's redneck population!") in the great and almighty Canada ("We're really passive...until you piss us off. Then we have to kill you. Sorry.").

I used to write for FF8, now I write for Harry Potter. I wrote a Buffy fic once, which was about fat camp and got mixed reviews. Notice my writing get progressively more horrible as you near the bottom of the list! That was back in the good old days, when I had a life, and more to do than think up a coherent plot and fret over dialogue. But my loss is your gain, dear reader.

For some reason, recently I wandered back into the FF8 portion of the site...and was amazed by the sheer number of Squinoas. It was abominable. So I said hey! I'll write an FF8 fic again! Just for kicks! A Queifer! Like old times! So there you go.

Blood & Daisies: Making Squall a cardboard character since 1987.
