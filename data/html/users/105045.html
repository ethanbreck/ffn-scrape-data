Author has written 28 stories for Hercules: The Legendary Journeys, Stargate: SG-1, Sentinel, Xena: Warrior Princess, Mummy, Torchwood, and Inspector Rex/Kommissar Rex.

Okay, so here are the collective works of my time not spend horseriding, whielding a sword or jousting! Overall, I enjoy all fandoms, but my favourite inspirations over the years came from Stargate SG-1, The Mummy, Hercules: The Legendary Journeys, CSI, The Sentinel and Torchwood.

I'm the car mechanic of the TV fandom - most of my stories are inspired by things, the writers and creators of the individual TV show have either messed up, missed out or simply got wrong. The rest of my stories are written to be episodes, I would have liked to see.

I'm also always up for a writing challenge and I try to write as much as possible and promise to only post completed stories. TV characters, for me, are transcendent. They are things to take and mold and so there are some of my stories, which border on the insane - but they are designed to make the reader laugh.

Unfortunately the day only has so many hours ... Enjoy!
