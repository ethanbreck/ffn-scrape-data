*Spooky announcer voice* Imagine it: A short girl, human (sadly) in all ways. A five-foot measuring stick is taller than she is, but she doesn't let it bother her--much. She's naught but at the tender age of fifteen. A mass of brown hair covers her head, braided in the back with unruly little wisps all hanging around in the front. Her eyes are the same color, and rather boring. She wears a bandanna, one emblazoned with images of an alien named Zim, a robot named G.I.R., and the symbols of the Irken Empire. Earrings also bearing that tiny Invader dangle from her ears. She wears a huge black T-shirt, secured by a belt around her waist and sporting two-eyed the symbol of the Irken Empire in a pale purple. A plushie of G.I.R. hangs from her belt, smiling at the world in general. Her jeans are normal and boring, as are her shoes. One wrist is wrapped in a black terry cuff, this too sporting the image of G.I.R., this time in his doggie suit. The other jangles with the sounds of a charm bracelet, the four metal charms showing Zim, Dib, Gaz, and G.I.R. A bracelet of amethyst and a rubber one also bearing Zim and G.I.R. also occupy this wrist. A shoelace (guess who's on it--G.I.R.) is wrapped like a choker around her throat. She looks odd, for sure, and a bit eccentric as well. But most of all, she looks like...a fangirl.

Yep, that's me. Janella K. Baduini. I live in New Jersey! *Nods sagely* That particular description is of me in all my IZ-fangirl splendor! ^.^ *Is proud of her obsessions* So...yeah...hi. I suppose you'd like to hear more, right? Sure you do!

I live with one alter-ego, a mutant known as Mythos, and roughly...*counts*...nine or ten head voices. They're cool...they inspire me. Inspiration is fun! All of them have their little assignments, though some of them also don't do a very good job at it! *Glares* Anyway, there's Lightwing, a Dae-uial; ZaiFae, Red, Purple, and Reno, Irkens all; Dazdemordres and Kyrrill, a pair of dragons, and mythos, Nightcrawler, and Gambit, a trio of mutants.

Mythos is my alter-ego! She's so lucky! *Coughsarcasmcough*

Feel bad for me...I'm the poor innocent *Cough cough* vessel for all these peeps. I enjoy sugar. And fire. And pointy objects. I am prone, if you provoke me, to destructive fits of anger. Destruction is good^.^ I like to draw and read and write and play with my toys. Dinosaur toys. The Parasaurolophus herd is taking over my kitchen table!!! Whoa! *blink* I have an active imagination and it makes me afraid of the dark.
