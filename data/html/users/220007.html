Hello! My name is Leanne, and I'm a thirtysomething computer scientist. My hobbies include acting, singing, costuming, fantasy roleplaying, anime, and writing fanfic. :)

I have a particular passion for Fushigi Yuugi and Escaflowne, and a relatively new-found one for Harry Potter. I'm also a fan of X-Files (oooo, Krycek! Ooooo, Skinner!), Stargate SG-1, Farscape (waaaaaaah!), Star Trek of various sorts, and I *love* action/adventure movies, especially X-Men!

I have a couple of little Otaku, a scientist husband, a weinerdog, various birds, and a van with the license plate "JGLYPUF".

And I recently got my first screen credit in an independent film - "Max Magician and the Legend of the Rings", where I play a female Elvish warrior! (If anyone has happened to see it, I'm the redheaded one!)

I have set up a yahoo group for the discussion of the Fires of Beltane universe, for anyone who would like to join. You may check it out at Drop in and say hello!
