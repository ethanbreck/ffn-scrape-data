Author has written 6 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Fruits Basket, Naruto, D N Angel, and Hana Yori Dango.
Let’s admit it, at heart we are all fan girls whether we like it or not. I don’t write fan fiction as much as I’d like to. The only ‘inspiration’ I base these stories on all come from bursts of emotion after watching a heart-rendering episode or reading the manga. Therefore, I’m reluctant to actually write anything besides one-shots.

I try not to write OOC stories but as you can see from one of my earlier stories, Hime no Ame I’m not immune to OOCness. I love reading other people’s fanfiction. And I have to admit that I tend to enjoy odd pairings and support characters that are not part of the ‘main cast’ in the original stories.

Okay, and maybe I am weird and have emotional issues but I love angst. Angst, romance, and a dash of ironic humor. And it doesn’t hurt for the main character to have a horrible dark past. ^o^

Eeeek. Shameless plug~ if anyone likes J-rock, please visit my art site. It’s a j-rock fanart/original sitey but I have the intent to load some anime fanart on it. And just a trivial fact but I’m a major Visual Kei fan. RYUTARO!! PlaTree lyrics are so pretty. ;_; Um, I like most classic J-rock bands (Luna Sea, PlaTree, X, Malice Mizer) except for GLAY and I adore most indie bands (Baroque, Gazette, Kagrra, Fatima, Vidoll). Besides J-rock, I like listening to J-pop, British rock, and classical music except if it’s really cheerful.
