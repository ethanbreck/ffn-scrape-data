Hello there!

I just realized that people are checking out my profile, and it’d just be rude of me to leave you with a blank page.

So, first, I have a confession to make: I’m not a writer by nature. I write only when I have ran out of things to read or when an idea smack me so hard in the face that I have to write it down to get it out of my system.

That being said, I do enjoy writing very much. It’s not easy, but it’s satisfying and fun and it gives me something to nitpick on at the end of the day.

I like writing dialogues, missing-scenes, character-studies and vignettes. My fics tend to be canonically compliant. Characterization is a big turn-on, and plots kill me dead every time.

At the moment, my main fandom is Supernatural, very occasionally Full-metal Alchemist and House. The West Wing was my first fandom ever, and it’ll always be special to me.

What else… Hm. I'm a Scorpio. I enjoy sunsets, long walks on the beach, and tend to be a one-fandom writer. You can usual find me on Yahoo IM squeeing/complaining about one thing or another. Feel free to come and say hi!

That’s all for now. :)
