CONTROL FANS, HERE IS A LINK TO SAGE'S RING:

Ok, I've been a member of FF.Net for a LONG time. Over the past 9 years I've written, posted, deleted, all across the board on a bunch of FF topics. Here's a head-turner for you, just so you know how long I've been on this site: I remember when "Music" was a category and so was "Poetry"... before Music was taken away entirely and FictionPress.net was created (Yes, I'm on there too).

So... about me... I'm a mother of a 18mo. old little girl and the wife of an amazing man. I'm a stay-at-home mom and a Jasper's Darlin'. Most of my day usually consists of entertaining my kiddo, writing, reading, and the occasional nap. I'm a Harry Potter fanatic (ask me about The Harry Potter Theory sometime). I'm also a Twi-Hard (Team Jasper all the way baby... but I'm not always cool with Alice's perkiness...).

Right now, I have two Twilight Fics. Losing You, Losing Me has kind of stalled, but I'm hoping I'll be able to finish it soon.

Control is my baby, right now, and my primary focus. I've been planning it for months and I'm actually nearing the finish line on it, which is a new experience altogether for me. If I were to pick any of my stories for someone to read to get to know my writing, I would pick this one. By far, it's my best work and I have RemyKilday and HammerHips to thank for that.

RemyKilday was kind enough to make me three banners for "Control". You can check them out using the links below. She does amazing work.

I'm also a fan of the show Dark Angel (yes, I am aware it only lasted 2 seasons) and I have an Alec/OC story for that. From the reviews, I guess it's pretty good, though I've stagnated on it since I got preggers and had a kid (nothing kills creativity like having to pee every 5 mins). I guess I'll have to work on that.

Anyhow, please let me know what you think of my stories. I love constructive criticism. Thanks! I am a registered Beta Reader, so if you need one and have something on my pref list, let me know!
