Hi! If you're reading this, welcome! If not, well, I know there are plenty of other entertaining things out there to do than read about me. :)

It's been a while since I've updated anything on my profile. And I really do mean a while. I realize there's not much to tell, but here it goes anyway:

I've been a member of this site for a long time now. PLEASE don't read any of my early works as I don't think they're very good. At all. However, should you choose to do so, don't say you weren't warned. :)

I am the eldest of three, enjoy reading, writing, song collecting, singing, having a good time, civil debates and the company of loved ones. Oh, and talking about anything, everything and nothing... sometimes all at once.

I'm a better reader than writer. It's what brought me to fanfiction long before I gave writing a thought. Fandoms I've written for include X-Men: Evolution, X-Men: The Movie (not the 3rd one), Harry Potter, Supernatural, and Fantastic Four.

I think this quote best describes my writing process: I don't like writing, I like having written by Ernest Hemingway. Good ideas are usually few and far between so I can't promise a story at a certain time. However, when I do start a story that I post here, I promise to finish it. Unless, of course, I die or a horrendous disaster or emergency happens. :D
