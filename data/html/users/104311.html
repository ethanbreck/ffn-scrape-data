Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter.

I became hooked on fanfiction when my friend was reading a Backstreet boy story at my house. It then progressed to Nsync that I read. Now I really only read Anime, Labyrinth,Harry Potter fiction and very recently Twilight. I love angst. Jareth is the greatest!! My favorite HP pairs are HP/DM, HG/SS, HG/DM but I really have a thing with reading the twins angst. NOT necessarily twincest. I'm also on the look out for a really good separation story about the twins. I'm very much a fan of fiction and I spend a lot of time on the computer reading. I have written fiction before but I've never posted it to ff.net I started with 'When you're gone' because I was absolutely compelled to write it. I hope you give it a chance and if you read it I hope you enjoy it.

A little bit about me:

I'm canadian.

Favorite books: The flowers in the attic series Harry Potter (Prisoner of Azkaban esp.) The Vampire Lestat, Pandora, Anything by Patricia Cornwell

Occupation: University student
Likes: H/D, FW and GW separation fics, anime (fruits basket is my favorite show right now), music, good fanfiction, sitting around reading fanfiction, writing

Um that's it for now. If you're looking for good stories try out some of my favorites or some of my favorite authors!!!
