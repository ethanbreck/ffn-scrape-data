Welcome!

I've had this account since I was thirteen, and naturally there are many things published here that I'm embarrassed by and not particularly fond of. I thought for a while about abandoning this account for a new one when I decided I did still have some fanfics left in me, but I eventually decided to press forward here. I suppose this place serves as an easily accessible archive of my progress as a writer over the last seven years. Just be weary of anything published before 2004.
