Beta Service =)!

I'm offering beta-reading service for anyone who wants me to offer advice, tips, correcting grammar, etc. or whatever. I'm available for doing so, so if you would like to have your story beta-read or even all of it, I'm open :)! Reserve me by contacting me through my e-mail listed at the top. However, the requirements for this beta service are...

1. An understand author willing to take advice and reasonable criticism
2. An author willing to put visible effort in their literary work
3. An author wanting to improve in all areas of writing
4. An author who loves to write!

I tell you what I think. Not what you want to hear. I will tell you truth even if it makes your eyes burst or your comp screen crack. I don't know you, I won't offend you. Your writing and you are different. You decide if you want it to be the same.

I won't run nor will I flame and hide. My e-mail is there. Got a problem with me, e-mail me. My fanfics aren't places to post your trash or beg for repairs for your slaughtered ego.

To my current haters who want me dead for insulting their ego, talk to me if you have a problem with what I said. We can work it out and I'm totally up for it.

To those who are spamming my review board, whoever you are, get the fuck out of my review board even if it's my fault. That's what my e-mail is for. I'm not running from you but you must be so freaking scared if you left your message anonymously for me to make my point.

Anyhow.
