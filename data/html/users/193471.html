Hi. Please ignore my untidyness... It's a littly dusty here too. So this is an old hang out of mine. I read a lot, and write a little. I'm told I'm a skilled writer, but at times I can be a very undedicated one as you can see by a few unfinished works. I write for fun according to my moods and the process is often dictated by what's going on in my life, so please be a patient reader with me. I mostly come here for casual reading and also to meet people with similar interests. I've had the pleasure of meeting a couple of my very good friends here.

If you'd like to know me a little better, all I can say is I'm laid-back, a bit of a thrill-seeker, intelligent, and a terrible procrastinator. I'm very idea-oriented, so I play around in my own head a lot. Some people consider me quiet because of that little fact, but if you happen to strike my interests and passions, that would totally blow that notion out the , coming up with ideas is what I do best, but I can be less motivated at executing them.Ah yes...and I'm a girl. I forget, people always ask me that first.

I'm more into art then writing, so I suppose I could blame some of my neglect here on that. I've been polishing my art all my life in hopes of soon becoming professional. So with that said, I'd love it if you visited my art page as well, but I completely understand if you came here for a good read. -hint- Look below =)
