And if you ever said you missed me, then don't say you never lied...

July 16, 2006: It's been a while, hasn't it? I'm back on the internet for a few weeks, and I've decided to focus on re-writing many of the chapters in Chanson D'Amour and Carpe Diem, because my writing style has changed a lot over time. (I was 13 when I originally wrote them, for goodness sakes!) If I get inspired, look out for an update or maybe even a new story? Who knows. Thanks for anyone who has been watching my work... it means the world to me.
What is there to know about me? Well, for starters, my name is Alli (hi!). I'm in high school, a Pisces, and a little insane. How’s that for an introduction? I obviously love writing fiction and poetry, and equally adore reading whatever I can.

My favorite main genre is Romance, especially in the Power Rangers catagory. Call it lame or whatever, but it takes skill to find moments in a half-hour kids' show! I basically support every couple mentioned in my stories, especially Jason/Kat and Billy/Trini. I dabble in other fandoms, most recently RPS and band slash. What can I say? Tastes change. Be expecting more mature works in the future, or at least, more mature than some of the stuff I've done before! Also, check out some of my original writings on fictionpress.com. It's mostly poetry right now, but I will be starting a pretty epic historical fiction soon. Keep tuning in for more!
