Hmmm... I don't think I really have anything worth being said, at this point. Beyond the fact that my name is Lexi (aka "DD") there isn't much to tell about me, myself.

My stories seem to be on a semi-permanent hold right now, due to a lot of shit happening in my life. For some reason I don't feel like writing light hearted humor/romance stories... *sighs* I'll keep working on "Dance Among the Stars" because Kat has taken to standing over me with a whip, but until further notice don't expect updates of anything else. (If you really want to know why, you can email me and I will proceed to bitch and moan about my life - I won't subject you to that here, however. I have too much respect for your intelligence. ^_~)

"Dance Among the Stars" is my current favorite story, though it's only partly mine - it's mostly Kat's. We've been working hard on it, and I think I'm drawn to the exploration of Gohan's budding psyche... You'll find me poking around inside his head as time goes on, methinks. ^_^n If you guys want any updates on what's in the works, Kat decided to start a mailing list - email zsillysis@hotmail.com if you want to be on it. (there'll also be an advertisement in the story, of course. ^_~) Just don't email me! ^_^n

"The Field Trip" is frusterating me to no end. I /want/ to finish it, I really do - but the terrible writing at the beginning drives me nuts! There's no real plot, and everything is barely held together with a few laughs. For an author, it isn't a pretty sight. I'll probably try cleaning it up at some point, then trying to move on with it...

"The Subs" is another one that seems daunting. I have ideas for it, but no will to write. *sighs* I /will/ continue this however, I promise! (part of my problem is that my muse, T-chan, has officially abandoned me, and Kitty is too busy being an author to be my muse... .)

"Invasion" is one that has the potential to be one of my favorites, given that it is not mired down with terrible writing, but *shrug* it's a funny story. I haven't been in a "funny" mood lately. It's the most likely to be re-begun, however...

"Getting Together" is only inactive for the time being. I'm still enjoying this story, and I have plans for it - it isn't highly in demand, though, so it keeps getting shunted back behind the more daunting tasks, such as Field Trip.

"Lonely Girl" will be updated as soon as I can find the illusive "perfect song" for the next chapter. I know /exactly/ what happens, though... *grins* And plus I'm in the mood to write this sort of thing.

Well, I'm sure I've just told you more than you cared to know, but oh well. If you read all that, it's your own fault. ^_~ Have fun reading my stories, and go check out my original poetry at fictionpress!
