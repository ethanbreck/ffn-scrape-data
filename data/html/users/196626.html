Author has written 38 stories for Naruto, Fire Emblem, Dragaera, Batman, Harry Potter, X-Men: The Movie, and Kingdom Hearts.

If something squicks you, I am sorry for that, and wish you better luck on your next read.

If I have improperly labelled a fic for triggery content or forgot to put a warning on it, please don't hesitate to tell me.

Warning policy: I don't "warn" for het/slash/subtext. I don't warn for romantic relationships between cousins. I won't budge on that.

I try to warn for everything else, whether the subject is dicussed or graphically detailed - self-harm, abuse, racism/sexism/ableism/homophobia/transphobia...

Fandoms: mostly an unholy mix of Homestuck and the Batfamily these days, though I promise to try and update this with my older Naruto fics. My DCU is firmly pre-reboot.

On shipping: I am very much a shipper.Most of my gen fics contain thinly-disguised ships. I can't help it, if I ship something it's always going to be in the back of my head, in the way I write certain characters. I really like OT3s.

DCU ships: Bruce/Jason, Steph/Cass, Babs/Dick for the Batfam OTPs. Also Bruce/Dick, Tim/Steph, Cassie/Rose, Dinah/Ollie, Slade/Dick, variations on The Good OT6 of Cass/Steph/Tim/Kon/Cassie/Rose (god, Titans, you are so incestuous), and too many others to describe.

Homestuck ships: Generally speaking, I really like the idea of black/pale flips, which is one reason I adore Vriska and Terezi, Feferi and Eridan.

Naruto ships: NaruSakuSasu is The OT3. Team 7 OT3 4ever! OTPs are NejiHina and ShikaTema. Other OTPs are NaruSasu and InoSaku. Other ships I like are mostly messed-up variation on the theme "Sasuke is messed up and makes terrible life choices" (ItaSasu is the least terrible thing on that list, which should tell you something about the rest. I still really like Sound-time OroSasu.) or pretty crack - IruAnko, TemaIno, KibaHanabi...

Themes: family, identity, masks, city, conflicting loyalties.
