Author has written 3 stories for Dark Angel.

Fan Fiction started with The Nanny for me. Silly, I know. But it's where it all begins. I inhaled alternate stories that my beloved characters might somehow find themselves in.

And then it moved onto Dark Angel. I loved that show so much that I actually became a fan fic writer. I stopped when the show stopped really, I'm planning on one day writing originals or turning my fan fics into originals.

After DA died out for me Buffy became my Fandom of Choice.

And then Stargate. I'm still completely head over heals in love with Stargate SG-1 as a fandom. I ship like crazy. You can find a story I'm currently coauthoring over at: http://www.fanfiction.net/s/4012076/1/

Since you're here, feel free to take a look at my stories, thanks for dropping by, and drop me some lines if you're up for some interesting yet random conversation.
