+Who's Kasumi-chan?+

Hi there! I'm Kasumi-chan. Before anyone get angry, I'm gonna warn that I'm from Brazil and my English isn't the best. I can commit mistakes, as well as anyone else. Now, let's go on with the Bio.
I'm a 13 year old girl from Brazil. I like drawing, writting, computers, TV and video games. I'm a huge anime fan, but I also like Harry Potter, Invader Zim and The Powerpuff Girls. My favorites animes are Dragon Ball Z, Dr. Slump, CCS and Digimon. Although I haven't been much into them lately, you can see that mainly cuz of the lack of stories about those in my list.
If there's something in the world I can do in a good quality, this is writting. I love it! That love for writting just keep making me always more, and more "up to" learn English well. I don't want that basic English, I want some advanced English! I want to be able to talk to Americans, Canadians or whoever speaks English. And I've been doing quite a good job, speacially in my fanfics, in which I work SO hard to make it understandable. But I can't say how well/bad I'm going. So, please, if you want to be nice and tell me if I'm doing it good, review in one of my stories. I'll read it and take it. It doesn't matter if you hated my English or the story! But I want to know!
Okay, I'm a bit off topic right now. So, let's stop here and move on to something else.
