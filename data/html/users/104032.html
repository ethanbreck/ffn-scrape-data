Author has written 7 stories for Final Fantasy X, Sailor Moon, and Haruhi Suzumiya series.

Hello everybody, and welcome to my author's profile. I have been writing for as far back as I can remember, and writing fan fiction for over 14 years. Only a very small fraction of what I've ever written is posted on this site; most of it having been lost over the years. For fan fiction, I enjoy writing mostly Sailor Moon stories, though I have occasionally written ones for other shows and games as well.

Besides fan fiction, I also write my own original fiction. Unlike my fan fiction, I usually write horror or adventure stories. This is in contrast to my fan fiction, which is usually romance and drama.

I try to update as often as possible, but have unfortunately left a lot of you hanging with several of my stories due to my extremely hectic job. For that I am truly sorry. Fortunately, I still have my original notes for some of them and plan on continuing them as soon as possible. Below are the stories that I will be continuing:

The Vexation of Haruhi Suzumiya

An Unexpected Vacation

As for my other incomplete stories, I am sorry to say that they will probably never be completed. I apologize to anybody who has put time into reading them. Currently, I am working on 'Going Home', an alternate universe Sailor Moon story. If you are interested, please check it out.
