Things You More Than Likely Won't See in My Fanfics

1. Out of Control OC’s
(Note: In case you may not know (or are new), OC stands for Original Character. Meaning they where never in the actual story the fanfiction is based off of, but where placed there for whatever reason.)
You ever read a fanfiction that had so many Original Characters that it doesn’t even seem like the same thing it’s based on? While I may place one or two, four tops, I’ll try my hardest not to put so many in that the story doesn’t even seem like the material it’s based from.

2. Out of Control OOC
(Note: OOC means Out of Character)
While a character in my story may do something he hadn’t in the actual story, I promise I won’t have the said character go WAY off base--mind control obviously not included. But under the same token, I’m not going to kill myself trying to make them to a tee exactly like they are in the story–but I’ll try and get close.

3. Shadow/Amy Pairings in my Sonic fanfiction. EVER.
I will be polite and let said author go unnamed but they have completely and FOREVER ruined my idea for even coming to terms with this ship, let alone ever liking it. This is what happens when you completely write characters so out of character that it literally pisses me off, turn Amy into a Mary Sue and...hell, have the nerve to make comments and insult other authors of the same ship about being 'original' when the first chapter of said story is so unoriginal that I literally started laughing because I thought the author's notes about said unoriginal authors was a joke. :Growls:
