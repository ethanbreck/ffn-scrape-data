I'm a story whore. I love to read other people's stories (especially Buffy, Harry Potter, Stargate, Firefly, and Dark-Hunter ones, or crossovers of any) but you would have to pay me to write one. Not that I'm a bad writer. I just lack, shall we say, motivation, attention span, time management, and follow through skills. Kind of puts a crimp in the fiction writing process...

Anyhow, just to feel like there's something actually in my profile, here are some quick facts about me, in case anyone cares. :)

-I used to play college hockey...until it put a cramp in my tv, book reading, and social schedules. That, and I kind of sucked. The only thing I was good at was checking and getting the puck out of the corner. Let's just say I learned how to skate during our first on ice practice.

-I am an Anthropology and Ancient Studies double major, meaning I will never get a paying job after I graduate unless I want to, god forbid, teach. update I graduated...and work in a middle school now. How's that for depressing?..

-Favorite books include The Sevenwaters Trilogy by Juliet Marillier, Kushiel's Dart by Jacqueline Carey, Queen's Own trilogy by Mercedes Lackey, Nightrunner series by Lynn Flewelling, and of course, Harry Potter, particularly books three and six.

-Favorite tv shows include, but are not limited to, Buffy, Heroes, Queer as Folk, Battlestar Galactica, Stargate SG-1, Lost, ER, and 24.

-I would be content to live off Ben and Jerry's Cherry Garcia the rest of my life (which wouldn't be long, actually considering the fat content...)

-I want to spend at least 3 months doing nothing but reading and sleeping on some topical island. And drinking cocktails. With a cabana boy.

-Oh, and I have a strange affinity for squirrels...except when they throw nuts at me. Seriously. It's happened. More than once.
