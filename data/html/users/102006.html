Author has written 9 stories for Rurouni Kenshin, and Transformers/Beast Wars.

Let's start with the basics: I'm a professional artist (yup, BFA and everything) who's been an anime fan since high school. I love animation in general. I now have a degree in animation from the Columbus College of Art and Design in Columbus, Ohio. My ultimate goal is to work for a major company like EA Games or Pixar. Let the job hunt begin!

My claim to fame (such as it is) is the year I spent in Japan from July 2003-July 2004 with the JET Programme teaching English in high schools. It was an amazing experience, I will never forget it. For those interested in my Japan adventures I kept a LiveJournal of my travels (with pictures!), feel free to to have look: www.livejournal.com/users/calger459

In regards to past stories, I want to offer my profound thanks to everyone who voted for me in the 2002 RKRC awards. "Prism" was the winner of the 2002 Action/Adventure category. Thanks also go to the voters for "Clearing Skies", which won runner-up in the Drama category for 2005.

Clearing Skies status: I'm sorry everyone, but it seems like my fanfiction writing days are at an end, at least for now. I simply have other priorities in my life now. Clearing Skies is therefore on indefinite hiatus; my sincere apologies to everyone who's faved the story and added it to their alert lists. I hope someday to complete the fic, but right now I can't say when that will be. Thank you for reading!
