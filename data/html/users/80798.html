My name is Trinity D'Canto (just a pen name, you know). I'm 21 years old and I live in Cockeysville, Maryland with my parents. I attend the Community College of Baltimore County at Essex where I major in Criminal Justice. "GouGou Sentai Boukenger: The Next Generation":

In 2031, 25 years after the Original Boukenger, a new team is born, a new generation of Endless Bouken Spirits and Dreams. The children of the Boukenger are here and they're seeking new adventures!

While taking care of a favor for SGS Japan head Satoru Akashi, Eiji Takaoka mysteriously vanishes. His mission was to investigate an old tomb of Ashu. Almost a month later, SGS is informed that a Precious signal has appeared at his last known location. Determined to solve the mystery, Satoru sends the New Boukenger out to investigate.

The Precious is one that sends the new team through time back to 2006, immediately after the end of the Quester where they encounter their parents, still battling the Negative Syndicate. Can the New Boukenger find the missing Eiji, stop the Negative who followed them through time and keep the time stream intact long enough to go home?

The New Boukenger

Kei Takaoka: BoukenRed: the daughter of SGS Museum curator Sakura Nishihori-Takaoka and Former Boukenger turned rescue worker turned occult bookshop owner, Eiji Takaoka, Kei was handpicked for the BoukenRed spot by Satoru Akashi. Apparently, he liked her spirit of adventuring. Kei is very good at her "Chiefu" role, enjoying her new lease on life, following the tragic death of her fiance, Kai Tomasu in an accident involving a very dangerous Precious.

Belle "Rin" Inou: BoukenBlack: The oldest daughter of Masumi and Natsuki Inou, Rin was handpicked by Satoru to fill the spot her father left behind when he retired to write books about his experiences. Every bit her father's daughter, Rin has the position of Kei's right hand woman, which she fills rather nicely, considering the two are the best of friends.

Tsubasa Mogami: BoukenBlue: Souta and Kaze no Shizuka's only son, Tsubasa (also called "Tsu-kun" by Kei), is the information expert his father was and also, the playboy...although he only has eyes for Kei. Tsubasa has established himself as Kei's right hand man, able to do anything Rin can't.

Satoshi Akashi: BoukenYellow: the son of Satoru and Mini-Skirt Santa (Eve), Satoshi strives not to fill his father's footsteps and to find his own path...his own adventure. Satoshi harbors a secret crush on Kei, obivious to everyone but Kei herself.

Aurora "Hikari" Inou: BoukenPink: Natsuki and Mausmi's second daughter. If Rin is like Masumi, then Hikari is like Natsuki, eager and cheerful, always up for a little excitment. Hikari, like most younger siblings, wants to be just like her big sister, but learns to find her own treasure.

Kando Takaoka: BoukenSilver: Kando, Kei's baby brother, joins the team after inheriting Sargess Rescue from his father. He strives to overcome the Ashu heritage and legacy he shares with his father and sister.

GouGou Sentai Boukenger: The Next Generation: Coming 2/11/07 exclusively to fanfiction.net and rangerboard!
