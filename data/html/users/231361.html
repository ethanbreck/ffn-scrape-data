Well, it's time and past to revamp this thing, because all my prorities have changed.

The name's Dee, at least around the Internets. I also go by Scribbler, though that's mostly in Newsies areas.

I'm a slasher. Yeah, that's right. Equal opportunity femmeslash, too. And I also enjoy het, while we're at it. I'm an omnishipper in every fandom I've ever been in, because why restrict myself? Sure, I have OTPs, but I'll read anything with anyone.

Writing is pretty much my life, because it's cheaper than therapy and I'd much rather write about two boys (or girls, or what have you) going at it than blather on about my problems. I do, in fact, plan to write in that magical time 'when I grow up', or rather, when I grow older, because I doubt I'll ever really grow up.

I'm currently doing a lot of writing in the Newsies fandom (especially since I signed up for the Fanfic100for the characters Specs and Dutchy), and my favorite pairing is Sputchy. But write me good Specs/Skittery/Dutchy and I will love you for forever and a day. I've dabbled in several fandoms (as you can see), but most of what I've written lately (in other words, most of the stuff that actually shows some skill and some level of emotional maturity) is RPS of people I know, and as such is located at my LJ (or, if you just want the fics and not random stuff about my life, at my website).

And that, I suppose, is all. Unless you want the creepy, stalker versions of my life, which is...creepy and stalkerish.
