I formerly used this profile to post Buffy the Vampire Slayer fanfic. I deleted all my stories in 2002 or so. Geez, has it been 10 years, really? Since then, I've bounced through a lot of different fandoms. Currently obsessed with professional wrestling.

I started watching wrestling in January 2010, so my knowledge of anything before that comes from DVD sets and the internet. I started watching because Dule Hill and James Roday were going to be on Raw, so I guess the guest host gimmick worked? I've been dabbling in Wrestling fic for about two years, but I never managed to finish anything up or post it before now. I will be trying to post some of my older stuff and new things I'm working on.

A Few of My Favorite Things...

Edge: The first Raw I ever watched was the Raw before the Rumble in 2010 when Edge returned from his Achilles injury, so basically my love for Edge and my love for wrestling went hand in hand. I actually got kind of depressed when he had to retire, and now it's hard for me to watch him because I remember how much I love him and it makes me sad all over again. ... and my first Smackdown show happens to be the one airing 9/21/2012, where Edge appeared. And I always complain that awesome things never happen to me.

Punk: Also a hot angle in the winter of 2010. Punk seriously creeped me out to begin with. I always liked him, but I think I fell in love with him when he started doing commentary. And I went crazy for him after his Las Vegas pipebomb, just about when everybody did, I guess. I was actually at the Raw in Boston on 7/11/2011, before MITB.

John Cena: There is absolutely nothing ironic about my love for John Cena. That's all I really have to say about that.
