Current Projects

I've recently returned to writing fanfic after a looooooooong writer's block, and I don't think I ever want to look at my previous works again. Some of them are bad, very bad. But if I see something worth saving, I might work on it again.

And as my reemergence from God knows where, I bring with me my latest long running project - A series of Cedric/Hermione fics set in GoF that are inspired by the keywords of the song 'Bitch' sung by Meredith Brooks and by morethenitseems aka PotterChick1010's Fanvid which can be found here. They can be read chronologically, OR stand alone.

'His Cheshire Cat Grin' (aka Bitch) is the 1st- set after Krum asks Hermione to the ball and before Ron asks her.

'Her Storm-Gray-Boy' is the 2nd story and is based on Lover - set both before the Yule ball. And while this time the word isn't said, it's still implied.

'His Diamond in the Rough' is the 3rd and is still WIP. Based on Child - set during and just after the Yule Ball.

Remember, reviews are LUV!! I'm holding some of the stories hostage here! I've already thought of the storylines to Child, Mother, Sinner and Saint,so if you want them sooner, you'll REVIEW!!!

Future Projects

I've been inspired by a Cedric/Hermione AMV called 'Missing' and I've been fleshing out the plot outline. It'll be an almost love triangle between Hermione, Cedric and an unwilling Harry. Also set during GoF, I'm on holidays now, so I'll probably be able to start on it.
