Became a cadet in Civil Air Patrol for four years, ranking to Technical Sergeant. He was stationed for the 104th Composite Squadron at Barnes Air National Guard in Westfield, Massachusetts. There is where he learned his martial arts skills.

Started to play and teach himself guitar because he loved the music and arts of his favorite music artist Bruce Springsteen.

His biggest influence who got him into martial arts was Steven Seagal.

Ever since he was 10, he wanted to act and produce feature films like his heroes Steven Spielberg and George Lucas.

Taught himself to speak and write in Japanese.

Taught himself how to fight, military Special Forces style, by ordering books in the Military Book Club.
