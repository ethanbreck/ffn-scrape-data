My pen name comes from the coriander plant. The seeds are used for cooking and the oil from its seeds is sometimes used in aromatherapy. I like to think I share some of its characteristics, personality wise. I live in Ontario, Canada and have for most of my life, although I spent four months in Alberta a few years ago.
