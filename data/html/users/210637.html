I decided that I'm going to update this since it's been over two years that I've been writing.

I am a high school teacher who still feels 24 years old even though my driver license claims I'm older than 30! (I demand a recount). I'm happily married for 14 years and have two daughters. Also have one hyper Welsh Corgi.

I love to read and also watch tv. So when I found out about fan fiction in 2001, I was hooked. I have thought about writing my own hundreds of times and so I decided to try my hand at it.

Favorites include:

Television: Big Valley, A-Team, SWAT, Airwolf, Third Watch, CSI:NY, CSI, Bones, Numb3rs, Crossing Jordan, Medium, Criminal Minds, Moonlight, Rat Patrol

Movies: Pirates of the C, Harry Potter, The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe, National Treasure, Gone with the Wind, Speed Racer!

Books: Stephan King, Clive Cussler, James Patterson, Rita Mae Brown
