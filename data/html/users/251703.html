Author has written 4 stories for Naruto.

All right, let's beef this up a little. *rolls up imaginary sleeves*

I'm a 25 year old who is hard of hearing; has a love for history and the written word; spazzes all over the place when something awesome is found (and sometimes, it's so small and insignificant to other people, I'm the only one spazzing). Like anyone else, I have my issues but I'm trying my best not to let them bring me down. They have, though, in the form of the Writing Drought that's lasted from 2007 until now. I'm hoping the fics will keep rolling now, but I can't promise anything.

Things I like in terms of fandom-esque things: NCIS, Naruto, Bleach, Bakuman, Fairy Tail, Katekyo Hitman Reborn, Fruits Basket, Gundam Wing (which will forever be one of my favorites), Saiyuki, Ouran High School Host Club, Cardcaptor Sakura, Law and Order: SVU and Criminal Intent, Family Guy, Robot Chicken, How I Met Your Mother, Big Bang Theory.

Actively reads fanfics in: NCIS and Naruto.

Boring? Maybe. I promise that it's totally not intentional. I do try to be one of the cool kids but it just isn't happening. No matter how many awesome people I hang out with.

I do hope you enjoy what I have been able to write. It would mean the world to me if you do.
