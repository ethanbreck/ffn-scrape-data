About my writing

As much as it may seem like I'm not doing anything, I am reading to get research to actually finish that DOA story. It needs a total rewrite so I deleted it. It shall be written in due time.

I mostly write fanfiction that contains fan characters, because I somehow seem to find that amusing.You may consider my stories Mary Sues, although for that to be true I'd have to have thought of any possible pairings with my characters myself. Usually, however, this is not the case. My friends think of pairings for them when I roleplay online using my characters, and eventually I come to like them. This may lead to inserting the pairing in a story I'm working on.
If this is appalling to you, I suggest you turn and run.
I certainly don't care if you read my stories or not - I write for myself, just like you read for yourself and not because someone told you to.
At least I hope you have a choice in what you read.
