Hi there!

My name is Vanessa. I live in Florida, USA. I like to write, when I can. Being a university student usually means I either have a LOT of free time or NO free time, depending on the time of year. I started this account on ffn years ago, when I sucked at writing even more than I do now. I've deleted most of the stories from back then. I don't write as much fanfiction as I used to, but I decided to update and see if I can get back into it.

My favorite fandoms to read/write for include: The Legend of Zelda; Labyrinth; The Phantom of the Opera; Avatar: The Last Airbender; Axis Powers Hetalia; and other stuff.

I'd love for you to drop me a review if you enjoyed (or hated) something I wrote. Getting reviews is the only way for me to know if I'm doing this right.

Stories:

Sprite -- A "Labyrinth" fanfic, and my newest published fanfic in a long time. Sarah is forced to deal with the rather rude intrusion of the Underground into her life--but not from the source she expected. Currently in the works. Reviews are appreciated.

Where Shadows Seem to Wither -- Old, written back in 2003. It's a clumsy and ungraceful attempt at exploring emotions in writing, but at the time I was very proud of it. I don't encourage anyone to read it, but I'm keeping it up for old time's sake, as well as to remind myself that I always need to improve. This story involves rape--there aren't any graphic descriptions, but it's there. You've been warned.
