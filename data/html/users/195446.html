disclaimer

Just an update since this site is so old...

Arslan is one of my favorite anime/stories. I don't plan on watching the new series as I think the new art pales in comparison to the art of the original 3 movies.

I wrote this in HS. I have no idea why the uploader at ff.net stripped all my punctuation, but I swear it was there before. I have no time or plans to edit.

If you don't like it, don't read it or review it. It's not gonna hurt my feelings. If you have comments or questions feel free to email me.

Yes, woman is misspelled on purpose. Look up sex bias inherit in language and you'll understand why a 15yo me wrote womyn with a Y.

If you think the romance is rushed, I don't understand why you like anime. Most shoujo romance gets straight to the point. But there are reasons in there, however teenagery they may be. Every watch fushigi yuugi? There you go. (You should totally watch it, it's great.)

Anyway, thanks for stopping by. Please do tell me your thoughts on the stories if you care to.
