Author has written 13 stories for Gilmore Girls, Laverne and Shirley, Harry Potter, and StarTrek: Voyager.

Hey all.

I've been reading and writing fanfiction for about 15 years.

I mainly write for Book of Mormon, Star Trek, and Harry Potter. I have stories in the works for Book of Mormon and Star Trek: Voyager that I am excited about. I used to write for Laverne Shirley and Gilmore Girls when I was younger. While I still appreciate my favorite pairings for those shows, I no longer write for those 2 shows.

For Harry Potter, I focus on stories centering around Severus Snape as a guardian and/or mentor for Harry. I don't know why...it's just one of those things. I believe Snape to be a very complex individual and deep inside, I believe that he truly had the capacity to care for Harry if put into certain circumstances.

For Star Trek: Voyager, I only read and write Janeway/Chakotay fics. It's obvious from the romantic tension on the show and the intense caring they displayed for one another that they should have ended up together. Even in the novel "Full Circle", Chakotay admits his love for Kathryn, but she dies before they can be together. However, "The Eternal Tide" rectified that. =)

For Book of Mormon, I like McPriceley / Price & McKinley (on occasion I like Pricingham). And even though he's super gay, I have a fangirl crush on Andrew Rannells.
