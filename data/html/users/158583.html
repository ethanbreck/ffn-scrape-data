Author has written 24 stories for Sailor Moon, Game X-overs, Final Fantasy VII, and Castlevania.

Well it's been awhile again hasn't it? Although I've been more active on my site and Youtube. So it's not like I disappeared. For some reason I never get around to putting anything up here lately. Believe me, I have the motivation to write, finish/edit things and put up older things. It's just that the current stress in my life makes it hard for me to be able to do the things that I want to do.

I'll leave this here for now as it's still relevant:

Why did you delete stuff?: Other than deleting a few really badly written fics from over ten years ago it was for grammatical error, spelling and editing issues. My horrible magnum opus, Showdown in Midgar Gulch is full of these. Anything I could not finish might as well go too. If they ever get finished I'll put them back up. I'd hate to leave fics lingering here covered in dust. I kept the two old Castlevania fics up because while they are terrible I couldn't stop laughing when I read them.

What are you going to put up in their place?: Well, probably things of a similar vein. I just can't seem to get super serious. So parodies and comedy mostly. I might mix genres together. I just wanted a fresher start.

Newest fic, Everybody Farts, is actually new. I wrote it over the course of a few hours last night (10/9-10/18).

It's a silly fic about Reno trying to tell Elena that everybody farts. Even Tseng. Why did I write this? No idea. XD
