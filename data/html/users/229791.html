I do apologize for the abandoned WIPs, something I used to foam out at the mouth about. Other things I've softened my stance on: going to movies solo, wine coolers, leaving a club before last call. Things I still hold strongly- marshmallows (ew) Butterfingers (EW) and smarm, because I don't effin see the point. Slash or het folks, or keep it moving!
