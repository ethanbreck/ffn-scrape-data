Author has written 16 stories for Harry Potter, Labyrinth, Lord of the Rings, X-Men: The Movie, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Peter Pan.

So, it has been two years since I last updated. Two years since my computer crashed. Two years since the ideas floating in my head and down through the keyboard were lost in the inky e-abyss.

Most of the plots herein contained were long since finished in my dreams, or as a verbal yarn woven for friends during late night fiction sessions. I'm sure that not a one of these stories would have been finished as I was more fond of talking through plots than actually setting them down on paper.

However, I'm never true to my e-word. I'll say these are done, never to be updated and some day down the road inspiration will strike. Send me an e-mail about any of these stories if you wish. I'll talk you through what I'd pictured, or dreamed if it will give you closure. Look for updates, they may happen eventually. Thanks for reading and look for the new wave.

Nowadays I write stories with friends through chats- an auto-transcript of plot if you will. Allowing me to flesh out my details and cash in on sudden inspiration.

If I've reviewed your stories, feel loved. I don't do it often. In fact, I hardly visit ff.net anymore. If I've read ye, if I've reviewed, you're worth it.

I'm 20 years old. I'm in university. I have an unclear future and one hell of an easy past. I love culture, language, people. I hate rudeness, dishonorable intentions, and the dark. Love me, hate me, I'm me. J'écris- ergo sum. =D
