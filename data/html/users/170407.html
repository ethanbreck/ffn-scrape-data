My name is Christopher Rodriguez, and if you are reading this... you should put some clothes on... because I can see you.

Yes... that's right, put down the Cheetos and close your robe. Thank you, now I will dispense with my introduction.

For the comfort of others I have turned on the "No Flaming" sign. Fire is detrimental to the internet and the stories I submit are prone to crispiness. If you have something to say, say it constructively so that I may take what is being said as advice to improve my future works.

... No, I don't want any cheetos, but thank you for offering. Please refasten the belt on your robe, there may be children about. Thank you.

I first was introduced to this sight thanks to my (soon to be) ex-wife. She has several works published on the site as well, under the pen name "Pannychan". Hers are early works of Dragon Ball Alternate Universes, but her recent works are focused on her World of Warcraft passion.

I can promise two things, that (1) I will write the best I can about what I know and like, (2) I may break my promises from time to time.

I am formerly a member of the US Army, and I am very proud of my seven years of service. No, I am not interested in hearing your opinion about how the "War on Terrorism" is "A waste of time and money." Unless you were there, you've not earned the right to tell me what I and my brothers/sister do to protect your freedoms.

In the event of fanboy geekgasm over something you feel is "Totally Awesome" that I've submitted, your seat cusion may be used as a drool sponge. Feel free to share positive comments with me once the slobber has been cleaned up or is at no risk to your computer.

I'm an anime and comic book fan, that plays World of Warcraft and any video game relating to Spider-man... as well as a few unrelated games (such as DDR, Rock Band, or Smash Bros.). No, my family and I do not live in my parent's basement... we are planning to move into their attic where there is more space and fewer spiders.

Thank you if you spent time to read this, and I'm sorry if you now need therapy... but I cannot pay your bills.
