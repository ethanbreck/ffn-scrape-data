DO YOU WRITE FANFICTION: Yes, and rather badly, to boot.

New Update: I am not writing fanfiction that I plan to post anytime soon -- All the stuff on this page is woefully old, or dropped, with the exception of Lina's Quest, which I’m lazy about, as no one seems to like it. : (

That, and I wrote it while lonely in abroad in Norway one winter without anyone i knew, or a car, or any knowledge of the language, waaaaay back in 1999. Thankfully my stepfather was kind enough to print two copies of the thing, since the files and computer it was composed and saved on are now lost and gone.

So, working from the paper version, with tons and tons of all kinds of crazy notes and edits written on top of it, I last worked on it a year ago -- that is, typing the monster up on my old desktop computer (now kinda broken... sob, and editing it at the same time... ) ... anyway, it's a 366 page monster, in total, without my additional add ons to it, and edits, that no one seems to like. sniff

Anyhoo, I’m planning to get back to it, someday… but right now it just makes me depressed and sad whenever I think about it. But if I ever get any responses, or even figure out how to find the hit counts on this site work… Maybe I’ll start posting the new chapters here again? Hopefully…: )

The other fanfiction I’m working very heavily done is actual a huuuuge bunch of RP’s (RP’s stand for Role Plays, by the way, in case you didn’t know : ) ) that I do almost every night with my best buddies online. So far though there are over 700 posts for one of them…. Said seven hundred posts that I have to go through and label and number, even –before- I get down to editing them and preparing them along with my friends, for readers, and in lit. format. Eesh! But rest assured -- they’re –awesome-.

Old Update:

Um... right now I'm peddling some Harry Potter crud involving the "old crowd", ie. the Marauder era, but focusing on the future minions of Voldemort instead during their weird and "awkward" teenage years.

Another piece I'm seriously revising is a real piece of whored out as shit multi-crossover called b Lina's Quest /b that takes the idea of recasting the mantles of the sailor senshi (sailor scouts of the Sailor moon Fame) and passing off the roles to various over-powered denizens from other series.

So far it's just a Slayers, Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball Z, Ranma 1/2, X/1999, No Need For Tenchi!, Urisei Yatsura, Devil Hunter Yhoko, Inuyasha, Magic Knight Rayerath, etc. Already a huge ulti-crossover so far, but the cast and used series list just keeps growing and growing.

I'm also seriously considering spinning a semi-humorous, shoujo-ai flavored DBZ 'fic as well. Ho-hum.

Favorite Parings: Let's just say that I adore the unusual. Unless it really squeaks me. I love AU, or alternate universe stuff…. Or just a recasting of what’s “normal” in fanfiction. Also, I’m a serious Crack Couples! Fan.

DBZ Ships I like; I'm a fan of murai Trunks x Goten, Vegeta x Chichi, Vegeta x Yamcha, shoujo-ai (and I've found a total of three of 'em in the DBZ universe... a new high record!), polygamy (jealousy and angst!), any relationships or pairings with Brolli, Turlus, Raddiz an' Nappa in them, anything remotely fair to Chichi, anything with the before mentioned saya-jins and/or amazon... the odd stuff, I guess. I myself admit to growing tired of the same old B/V and G/V pairing 'fics, so i thought I'd mention a few of my favorite "alternatives" to 'em to remedy this.

Harry Potter Parings I like:

My favorite pairings in the Harry Potter 'verse are the more, uh, explosive couples... I like strangeness, I like lots of chemistry and verbal sparring, and play by plays…

I like Ron x Draco, and Sirius x Severus an' such... also anything that has to do with Tom Riddle/ Voldemort, too. And please, I am VERY TIRED of the same old, same old Harry x Draco, or even worse, Draco x Harry… Harry x Ron, Hermione and Ron, Hermione and Draco, and even worse, Remus and Sirius. Done far, far too many times for me already! Eesh!

Naruto Parings I like:

Again, I LOVE the unusual, and explosive couples and stuff. Yosh!

I LOOOOVE neji x sasuke, but will read sasuke x neji…. I’m a HUGE sasuke uke fan, really… and have a really hard time reading any fic where sasuke’s seme. I really, really dislike Sakura x sasuke… just wrong and icky and unhealthy on sakura’s part…. Or Hinata x sasuke… that’s sort of like trying to pair up a mouse with a python. Doesn’t really work! I don’t like reading Hinata mary-sues, or sakura mary-sues…. And if you know anything about fanfiction in general, you should –definitely- know what a ‘mary-sue’ is… and if you don’t, look it up! There’s tons of awesome articles on the subject!

Oh yeah… on a last note, I’m a huge sucker for Spock x McCoy fanfiction…. Mmmm! 3 3

Oh yesI like ANYTHING Trigun, and don't even have a preference. I love it all!
