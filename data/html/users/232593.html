First of all I hate the world. Everything's just so f*cked up right now that it doesn't even make sense anymore.

Second of all, I hate that stupid hunky unbelievably drop-dead gorgeous jerk next door. He's not worth my time.

And...well, let me make a list...

-I hate whining. Hell, I don't even do it. And I dislike people that whine.

-I hate complete and absolute morons, who think they don't have to give a damn about the world, and just blabber on all day in school. Don't get me wrong, I love dorks, but I hate idiots.

-I DON'T hate my life, even though it's kinda shitty right now, yet I'm trying to make the best of it. Though I do hate it when people don't bother to even have a taste of the future and decide to end it by killing themselves. If you ask me, they're cowards and forever will be after they're dead.

-I DON'T hate my best friend. I can only dislike what she does, but I could never hate her.

-And last but not least (believe me) I ABHOR the way a stupid dazzling smile from a guy can melt my heart in a matter of seconds!

Okay, that's it, I'm done. For now.
