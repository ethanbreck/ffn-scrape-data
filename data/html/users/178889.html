Hello, It's very nice to know that someone is looking at this.

Anyways, let's do a little introduction of myself. First of all, I am a HUGE anime and manga otaku (and am highly proud of it), I enjoy reading fanfics (though, of course, pertaining to the couplings I like), and I like to draw (though I totally suck at it, that's why I stick to writing fics. Ahh, I really admire those manga artists.)

And since English is not my first language, I would like to say to please forgive me if there are any grammar or spelling errors in my fics. I do try to get used to it.

I have been reading manga and watching anime since...ever since! I don't even remember myself. And I basically like every manga I read (even if it's stupid).

Here are the list of couplings I like from the mangas/animes/gamesthat are ranked top in my head.

Based on the listing by Seme x Uke:

(Sou-uke is when everyone is after one character/person, so that person will always most likely be the uke)

(Sou-seme is the opposite of sou-uke. Obviously, one character is always the seme with any character)

When there is a '&' in between, that means that I see them as partners/friends...not coupling.

Inuyasha:

Inuyasha x Kagome: The official couple, or not really, not sure. But this is indeed a cute pairing. I like the strong-willed, pretty, and can kick some YOUKAI ASS heroine type of girl. I personally think Inuyasha needs someone like Kagome to support him.

Inuyasha x Kikyou: Although I don't like them much as I like InuKago,I think they are okay. I don't hate Kikyou, really, I actually feel sorry for her. If she really knew what happened, then maybe she wouldn't be mean like how people see her and won't have to suffer all these things she's going through like, carrying the burden of the fact that she is only a mere walking corpse, and she is not suppose to be in the world of living. She really IS beautiful from my point. Just, if she would smile more often, things would have been better.

Miroku x Sango: A recent couple that's been made official. I just love the fact that Miroku still insists to rub Sango's butt, even if he knows what the consequences are(a.k.a. earning a heavy slap from her). Also, even having the courage to actually ask out a different girl even after confessing to Sango. These two are just so lovable and cute.

Kagura & Kanna : Okay...I'm not any of the lesbian type. I just think that they fit together as a sister x sister relationship.Although, Kanna hardly talks and Kagura seems to be annoyed at Kanna...I think they really care for each other(in a sister way). I just wonder what is on Kanna's mind now that Kagura is dead. But there's a high possibility that she's not even thinking of anything. I mean, she is made of 'nothingness'.

Kouga & Kagome: I think they work pretty good as a couple too.But , since Kagome likes Inuyasha more than him, it'll just be a unrequited love for our poor wolf boy. Nevertheless, they make a good partner and friends.

Kouga x(or &) Kagura: Hmm...there is a lot of debate whether Kouga should be with Kagura or Ayame(anime original character). But since Ayame only exists in the Anime, I think I would choose Kagura. But I don't really mind if I see them as a coupling or just partners. Hmmm...I should say, fluff is good for them...not too much love-making.

Inuyasha x Sesshoumaru: Yes,this is yaoi. Brotherly love,you call it? Inuyasha, the cute one and Sesshoumaru, the beautiful one. Nice. But I see Sesshoumaru as an uke (I guess that means my mind is weird).

Hiten x Yura: Though they are a really minor coupling and they both are actually dead(in the story), I love this couple. I got actually influenced by someone's fanart drawing of them! They were so cute! But being minor characters, it's really hard to find this coupling, but hey, isn't that what fanfics are for? I imagine Yura strong willed, kind(not like from the manga), and very...naive ( I know, I know). And my view of Hiten is way off from the manga so I won't say any further.
