I'm Tiffari, and I've been on the fanfiction scene for over a decade now. I don't write much anymore, but I do put something up now and then, when inspiration decides to strike. There's no specific fandom that I stick with anymore. I'm just as likely to write a fic for Merlin as I am to write a fic for Detroit Metal City or Buffy the Vampire Slayer. (I have not written any fics for any of those fandoms thus far, but they might be coming at some point in the future.)

IMPORTANT NOTE ABOUT BETA REQUESTS

I am currently on hiatus. Unfortunately, I don't have the time to beta-read any fics at the moment. I might be able to help with brainstorming, but I can't make any promises. Feel free to send me a message, but please don't take it personally if I don't respond. There are many other things demanding my attention at the moment, and I can be very forgetful. (I apologize sincerely for any cases in which I don't respond.)
