Hi there. This is the author formally known as Lolo Winner. I changed to this more mature name, which I will use for my pen name if I ever become published. I am currently editing and re-evaluating most of my fictions, so they will all be updated in due time. Gundam Wing is one of my obsessions, and the main subject for my stories. I mainly write drama stories, but I also write a bit of poetry (both original and GW), and the occasional humor fiction. I am exploring the area of original fictions, but don't hold your breath on those ones. I can be controversial and experimental at time, so beware of some odd pairings or very original plot lines. I hope you enjoy my works.
