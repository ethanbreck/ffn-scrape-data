Author has written 3 stories for Final Fantasy VII, and Kuroshitsuji.

After years and YEARS of absence (and deleting almost all of my fanfiction from here for some reason), I've come back, mostly because I'm a much better writer, and much more bored. I'm kinda over the otaku-ness of my teenage years, and I feel like I can explore characters in a story that is going to be an actual story, and not just some strange rambling of an obsessed teenager.

Letters from Moira

LFM is mostly going to be an exploration of Sebastian's character. I was inspired by Tatum's (Sebastian in the English dub) commentary where he said he feels like Sebastian is only a reflection of what Ciel needs him to be, that he's so obscure and otherworldly that we as humans cannot recognize any sense of personality or what-have-you that he may have, as human. Of course, he said it more eloquently, but I wanted to take that and see just how far I could possibly push Sebastian's character in lieu of not only the anime but the manga. There will be a plot, however, I will not be following any of the already established plot lines, but I will be connecting to them at some points.

Beach

I'd really love to give and explanation for this little one-shot, but I wrote it forever ago, and I honestly do not remember. I think, for the most part, I was just playing with the characters and making them horribly OOC, but it was cute, so I think I'll let it pass.

Photographic Memory

The extended form of Beach. I had originally planned to leave it a oneshot, but inspiration struck, and what can I say? I am conflicted about the AU, as it's hard to stay true to characters when you reformulate their lives completely, but hopefully I don't make them too disgustingly malformed. This is still going to follow the "plot" of Beach, just a little revised and longer.

untitled

Well, I got a Neon Genesis Evangelion piece for you guys! Well, not yet, as I haven't even made a title. But I figured it would be a good piece to work on when I'm in a rut with the longer, more-things-I-need-to-keep-in-my-head Kuroshitsuji fic. Besides, who doesn't love some Shinji action? Though, poor boy, I'm probably going to ruin his life yet again, but we'll see where this goes. There also might be some Shinji/Kaworu smut going on. Who knows. Maybe it's implied. We'll jump that bridge when it comes.

Don't forget to review guys! I love them, I really do. It's good to know when readers are enjoying my works, so please let me know!
Also, I'm always on the lookout for a nice beta reader! If you wanna beta, or even just talk about some plot ideas, hit me up!
