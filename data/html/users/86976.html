Hi. 1980s kid here. I love to watch Jem and Sailor Moon. I like '80s cartoons, '80s ABC sitcoms, anime, video games, computer games, comic books, and reading and writing stories.

10/17/18: Okay, time for another update regarding my stories.

"Betty and Cheryl" has been moved to AO3. You can check out my AO3 profile here.

Fics in Progress:

All Hail Calla - a post-series Gummi Bears fic

Limbo/Unsure:

Betty Cooper sequel fic - Maybe - MAYBE - I'll someday write a sequel to Betty Cooper: This Is Who I Am and Betty Cooper: Woman for All Seasons, turning it into a trilogy - if - IF - I can come up with a good enough story for it. The title that I have in mind is "Betty Cooper: Darkness and Light", but I really don't have many ideas for it. Might happen, might not.

Jem fic - Those of you that have read Mistress of Magic (the sequel to Stormer Switches Sides) know it ends on a bit of a cliffhanger, since the Misfits now have their own Synergy-like computer. Well, that will be resolved - but not in a Jem fic. I'm writing a Transformers fanfic (more info below) that will have an extended Jem flashback sequence that resolves the loose ends.

Vampire Hunter D - I've had the first draft of the final story in the trilogy (which began with Second Chance and was followed up on with Complicated Girls) done for years now. I've just been too lazy to do the final draft. More scenes need to be written to flesh things out. I might or might not finish it. Any demand for it? Let me know!

Escaflowne - I might write a sequel to To Fade Away that deals with Hitomi's return to Earth and her confessing her love for Yukari. MAYBE. I had never intended to write a sequel when I wrote this story (which is a prequel to the movie). We'll see. The title that I have in mind is "To Hope Again". Let me know!

Star Trek - I might write a sequel to my TNG fic, Cold Deal. It would deal with Tasha experiencing the events of season 1's "Conspiracy". However, all that I'd originally set out to do was undo Tasha's death, which I've accomplished. Is there any demand for a sequel in which she takes on the parasitic aliens? Let me know!

Transformers - Fueled by inspiration and sadness, I've written a prototype draft of a sequel to Dream Machine. It deals with Carly living through the events of the Movie, season 3, and season 4. The draft has all of the present-day stuff and one flashback scene written out. Numerous flashback scenes have yet to be written to flesh the story out. This story is much more dramatic (probably depressing) than Dream Machine. It will include an extended Jem flashback sequence to resolve the plot threads from my Jem fanfics. I no longer really have the inspiration to finish this (mostly because I was depressed at the time that I wrote it), but I can if there's demand for a Carly-centric story dealing with her marriage and friendships. Let me know!

Discontinued:

Sailor Moon - I was writing the prologue of a new fanfic series. However, my motivation for writing it no longer exists (I was depressed at the time). Only the prologue would have been original. It would have resulted in a restoration of the original timeline (before Chibiusa's interference), and the rest of the chapters would have been novelizations of each episode of Sailor Moon S (from the heroes' perspectives only, meaning no Death Busters scenes), rewritten slightly to omit Chibiusa. As such, it wasn't really fanfic. If anyone REALLY wants to see this, I MIGHT resurrect it. Let me know! As for my old fics, I Wanna Be A Star! and Lunarock (where the heck did that one go?! it got deleted?!), no future entries in that series.

FF: The Adventures of Mark, Mike, Matt, and Bill - I have long since lost the save state for the game that I was using for this fic. Besides, if I ever do write another Final Fantasy fanfic, it will be with original or in-game characters, not self-insertions.

Spitfire Racers - This is my NASCAR Racers fanfic series focusing on an all-girl racing team. I had planned to make it a 13-episode "season", but the Spitfire Racers are back together. That's all that matters. It really doesn't matter who ends up on Team Fastex in Megan's old spot. This series is so open-ended that it could have conceivably gone on for decades, so it might as well just end now.

Captain N: The Game Master -I still like the series, but I'm no longer in the mood to write about a male protagonist (as you could probably tell in my old stories, where Kevin got increasingly marginalized in favor of Lana). This includes the unresolved side series where the N Team goes to different cartoon universes (Filmation's Ghostbusters, BraveStarr). I assure you that they got back to Videoland safely. No more If Samus Was Around cartoon episode adaptations either. No more CaptainNity either (what a loss!).

Linda Danvers - This is my Supergirl fanfic series. The next story had been delayed since 2007 (the series' timeline covers up to the end of 2006). I'd slowly been writing the next story since 2007. It would have covered 2007-2010. However, I've decided to officially discontinue the series for a few reasons: 1) With the series being so far behind schedule, the real-time aspect of commenting on current events has been lost. 2) It's open-ended with no real goal, so it could have conceivably gone on for decades, so it might as well just end now. 3) It's become an obligation that's been hanging over my head, not something that I'm excited and passionate about, so discontinuing it will lift a huge weight off my shoulders and give me much more time to focus on other fiction. Rest assured that Linda, Lara, Kioko, and the others live happily ever after.

Resident Evil - Who, What, How? is my short follow-up to the first Resident Evil movie. That's all that I ever intended to write. I've never had any plans to add Rain to the events of the later films.

The Journal of Cantara Samira Fallon - This is my Forgotten Realms OC series. I've decided creating an original medieval fantasy setting would be better.

Gundam - I had originally planned to condense Mobile Suit Gundam Tournament into a trilogy of "movies", but I've decided it's not worth the effort. Just enjoy the 50 "episodes" as they are.
