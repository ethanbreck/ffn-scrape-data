Good evening, ladies and gentlemen. Yes, it has been many a year since my writing has graced (cough) the pages of what some have named the Pit o' Voles (sometimes I could agree with them, but there are actually some brilliant writers on here. Like Fimbrethil and Enchanted April, among many others.)

Fandoms include: (Fav shippy couples in brackets ~_)

House, M.D. (House/Cam)

NCIS (Gen Gibbs/Abby, some shippy Gibbs/Abby)

Frasier (Niles/Daphne... Niles angst is total love)

Batman [comics and B:TAS only (Joker/Harley, Gen Batman)

I rather enjoy writing Joker/Harley fics, but unfortunately they're an incredibly difficult pairing to nail. There has to be shippiness, but NO fluff or OOC, and with those two that's particularly difficult. It's easier to get into Harley's head than the Joker's; those who have managed good Joker POV shippy fics should be damn proud of themselves.
