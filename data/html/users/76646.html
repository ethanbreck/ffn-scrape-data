Author has written 13 stories for Gatekeepers, Fushigi Yuugi, Escaflowne, Roswell, Zoey 101, Twilight, Lizzie McGuire, and Hana Yori Dango/花より男子.

I can't believe I've been writing since 2001! It's amazing. Fanfiction.net continues to be the best place to post fanfics.

Anyway, a little somethin' somethin' about me: I love a handful of anime series - Escaflowne, Rurouni Kenshin, Vampire Knight, Ultra Maniac, and Saiunkoku Monogatari. Most of my fanfics are about Escaflowne - even after all these years, the show still inspires me to write. Lately, I've been hugely into writing fanfics for Twilight as well, particularly for the Jacob/Bella ship.

I am also a BIG fan of Veronica Mars. I was absolutely heartbroken when they canceled the show, and in the middle of a season no less! JDramas are my weakness, too. Pride, Hana Yori Dango, Love Revolution, Hana Kimi, etc. Ikuta Toma is one of my favorite actors.

Music? Hell yes. I am a huge music junkie. Indie, rock, alternative are my choices.

UPDATES: I am still working on the epilogue of Unexpected. And Not a Monster is definitely on the top of my to-write list.

2012-Feb-18: Just want you guys to know I'm still here and I'm still working on the 2 fics above as well as "Too Long".
