Who am I?

I used to be a Digimon/Pokémon fanfic writer, but I've now laid those two giants to rest and have spanned into the enormous world of Naruto and the spaz-tastic show Codename: Kids Next Door. My updating pattern is fairly slow (it takes a while for a chapter) but the chapters I chock out tend to be pretty long as a result of that. I also value character development and plot over action and character derailment (unless there is a reason for the derailment. A VALID reason, not a deus ex machina or just because you happen to hate the character). So no matter how much I actually dislike a character, I will NOT result to character bashing.

As far as pairings go, I do have personal preferences which would take too long to list, but it doesn't mean I won't be open to reading about others. I mean, whatever floats your boat. I'm fine with same sex pairings (although yuri doesn't really interest me that much) but I am as picky about how they're written as I am when the pairing is hetero. I'm VERY anal about characters morphing out of character just to pairing them with someone else...unless it's cited as a crack pairing. Then it's all good! XD

Review Status:

I’m kinda on the fence about reviews. While I do like sending reviews, I usually don’t have the time to write them. ‘Cause of that, a lot of great fics I’ve read tend to go review-less (is that even a word?). That said, when I write a review, I won’t write simple mindless praise (unless the story really does deserve the endless praise). I also will NEVER flame. It doesn’t matter whether the author deserves it or not. I won’t write things like “Your story sux and you’re a dumb @ss for writing it! Nyeh! Nyeh!” I don’t have the heart or energy to spew vitriol. I can be nit-picky and I’ll ask questions. But I won’t be overly harsh or rude. If I find something I find annoying (like character bashing) or I don’t like what’s happening, I’ll just move on.

On the flipside, I won’t demand reviews. I used to do that during my Digimon/Pokémon stories and, looking back, it seems kinda silly. I started this whole thing for fun and to get away from my headaches in real life. Feedback is great and I like hearing from you readers, but it isn’t a requirement for me. It’s enough that someone out there is willing to put up with me and what I turn out. ; Plus, who am I to talk when I barely have time to give out reviews myself?

I also have no problem if you don’t like something in my fics. I have no business being a law student if I can’t take criticism or see another side, heheh. However, what I said about flames applies here too. I appreciate constructive criticism as long as it’s not something nonsensical like “You’re not following my OTP! So you are crap!” Constructive criticism HELPS but only if it really is constructive. Flaming does nothing but make you look annoying and immature… or like a troll (but even then, it takes skill to be a good troll). Actually, you might not even get the reaction you hope from me since I tend to view flames as a source of snark and lulz (especially if you’re anonymous). So yeah.
