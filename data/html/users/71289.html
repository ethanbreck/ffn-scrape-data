I need a new profile. Seriously.

I'm known as Gladiaria Alata in most English-speaking forums, ChibiSecchan in one, and Silver Wing in the Chinese-speaking ones. But for pen name, I'm keeping this one. I'm too attached to "Chou Nuriko" to get rid of it completely.

I have a messed-up biological clock.

I write better when I'm not in my room.

I'm still a huge shoujo ai fanatic. But I've tuned down a bit: I'm only fussy on forums but not at real life.

My current obsession (which would be subjected to change every few months or so) is Touma x Mikoto from To aru Majutsu no Index. No special reason: I love Mikoto and wish she could be happy with whom she likes, even if it's in the world of fandom.

I've been a beta-reader for years. But I figure the word needs more fanfic for my current obsession, so I come active again.

Look at my work list and you can figure out I love writing crossovers. See if I can pull one off for Railgun...
