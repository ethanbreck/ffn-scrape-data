Started with fanfiction back in the 1980's before I even knew there was a term for fanfiction, let alone others that wrote stories about their favorite books, tv shows, movies, etc... Started with cartoons, but never took off with public postings/publications on the internet until late 1990's. It all started with Gundam Wing and everything spiraled away from there... (writing and posting, joining communities, creating groups, joining fanfiction sites, browsing for other stories, and maintaining a public website..)

Now my interests have grown to include other fandoms, including Harry Potter, and my most recent fascination, Star Trek XI...

Also, I am an avid slash fan. Even though I can (and often do) appreciate a well written heterosexual story/fic, I write more slash/yaoi/homosexual fiction than I do straight fiction... there is something fascinating about this and I am sure that I could give a psychologist hours of intriguing material to study, but there it is. I am part of that group of straight women that love to read and write about my favorite male characters (and sometimes female characters) living, loving, and growing together.

I am a 32 year old female who works full time as a manager in security. When I am not at work protecting my company's assets, catching shoplifters, and prosecuting internals, I am often out with my few close friends relaxing, or at home on the internet or reading. My friends always joke with me that I have way too many books, but I will NEVER go with a Kindle or devices like it (until it becomes a last resort)... I love the feel of paper books and the atmosphere of reading one too much. My favorite genres are paranormal romances, manga/graphic novels, and science fiction... also I will admit I read some of the young adult series as well (of course, as I mentioned to my best friend once... why weren't they writing these kind of young adult books when I was a teenager??)

My current favorite fandoms are (in no particular order): Gundam Wing (2x1 and 3x4 pairings) - Harry Potter (DracoxHarry, LuciusxHarry, SeverusxHarry, well written threesomes... and even sometimes a well written VoldemortxHarry) - Star Trek XI (SpockxKirk, McCoyxKirk, SpockxMcCoyxKirk) - Thunderbirds (fav character is a well written Alan Tracy) - Transformers (BeexSam - I don't know why, but it works for me!) - Yu Gi Oh (YamixYugi/AtemuxYugi) - any more than I can't remember right now...

I currently write and post fiction for Gundam Wing. I write fiction for Star Trek XI and Harry Potter, but haven't posted any fics yet - they still lurk on my hard drive.

I welcome friendly, considerate, constructive criticism, but do not tolerate flamers and idiots - all my stories always have appropriate warnings and I cannot help it if an idiot ignores the warnings and reads the story anyway and is appalled at the content.
