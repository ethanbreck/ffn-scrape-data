Hello! I write these things mainly because I get images in my head that demand
some sort of expression, and then poof, there they are. I am constantly amazed when
something my brain came up with happens to make sense to someone else. Writing is
amazing thing-- you or I can write something we think or feel, and then, suddenly,
when someone reads it. they are thinking and feeling something a lot like it. Or so we
aim. I hope you enjoy reading about what I think and feel. Thank you for trying.
