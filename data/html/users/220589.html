Right; well I suppose if you're here it's 'cause you want to know something about me. I'll try to keep it short...I tend to ramble.

There are three things I could never live without: one is water, the other is air, and the third is my computer. That said, I'm constantly on ff.net reading stories or adding-on to my own. If I review your work, it's because I really love it, and if I don't it just means that you caught me on a lazy day.

Harry Potter is my primary genre although I'm tentatively working on a Smallville fic. I've got tons of ideas for stories up my sleeve, so if by some miracle you like what I write, then stay on the look-out :P

I'm just some twenty-something college student trying to make it in this world, and as my life holds a lot of angst, it will undoubtedly reflect in my characters; but, I think I write a mean bit of lactose-intolerance-inducing fluff every once in a while, though you be the judge. Reviews are what make MY world go 'round. xxoo
