Author has written 18 stories for Resident Evil, Silent Hill, Titan Maximum, Mortal Kombat, A song of Ice and Fire, and Evil Within.

7/19/2018: This is my oldest account, one I've had since middle school. I've come and gone, but at this point, I'm tempted to "reclaim" my oldest account to say the least. My Harry Potter fics are old, childish, and cringeworthy upon reading. I'm not saying I'm a writing genius at this point,or that I've truly improved, but all have been deleted because I don't like to look or think of them anymore. My apologies for all those who read, reviewed, and enjoyed them.

The few that I left up were ones I had done more recently, or ones that I enjoyed writing at one time. Some of the older ones, like the goofy Resident Evil ones or the Titan Maximum ones, might be removed one day. If not, I might rewrite them.

I am also considering changing my pen name for what feels like the millionth time. It doesn't quite work if I focus on a lot of other things than HP, now, does it?

As of now, I am writing for myself. I spent a lot of time in middle school and beyond writing for and following advice on reviews. I have a disability that leaves me stuck at home, and so I spend a lot of time writing and thinking of ideas for games, books, movies, and series in general that I love. It helps me explore, and it allows me the freedom to leave the house without really leaving the house. Escapism at its best, I suppose.

Of course I still listen to and love reviews but I can't say they'll affect my fics like they used to. I hope that does not offend anyone as I don't mean it to come off as utterly rude even though it sounds that way. I love anyone who reads and reviews or favorites or anything like that. Thank you to those who check my work out!

If you see my fics anywhere else, they are NOT ME! I only post on fanfiction.net. I have a deep loyalty to the site, I've been here a very, very long time!
