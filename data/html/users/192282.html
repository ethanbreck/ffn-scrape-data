All the right friends in all the right places

What is there to know about me? Well, aside from my life being completely being hectic at the moment, there are lots of things that I love doing. I'm am currently engaged to a wonderful man, and I couldn't ask for anyone better in my life.

As far as writing goes, I love doing it but wish that I had more time. As you can tell, when it comes to wrestling I absolutely love writing fics that include Randy Orton and Shane McMahon. I've also done a short drabble that features Ted, and I have a funny feeling he might make an appearance in the future in a fic or two. I did a bit when it came to the Harry Potter fandom, and they always contained Draco and Hermoine because for some reason I just think those two make an awesome couple. However, I've been out of that fandom for so long I'm not sure I could even pull off another fic. The other fandom that I write in is for NCIS and of course those fics are always going to be centered around the one and only totally worthy of all my love and attention Tim McGee. I mean seriously...how can you not love the man?

Other then that, I'm not sure that there is much else to really know about. I mean, it's pretty much as basic as it gets, just like me. Please feel free to PM me if you have any thoughts or questions, I'm always up for answering. Even if you have an idea for a fiction or challenge, I'm open to giving it a try.
