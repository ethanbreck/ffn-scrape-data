Hi everyone!

This bio was written 6 years ago, but I'm not going to change it now.

So I’ve finally got around to starting a decent bio. To start it off I think I’m freaking mad, so does many of the people around me.

I’m born and bred in Norway, live in a town called Trondheim. I go to school, and that gives me far more homework than I’ll ever get around to do (might be because I write all the time, but what is a girl to do?). I’ve got dogs, Chow Chows to be precise, I’ve had Chows all my life. My big hobby is (it isn’t writing) scouting, done that most of my life, scary waking up and realising you’ve been doing something most of your life. Writing is my passion, I love it. Most of what I write ends up in one of my overfilled drawers, but some of it is put here (a very small part of it, but some.). I also love reading, not the way I love writing. I read Harry Potter (as you might see from what I write) but I’m also a big Tolkien fan. I thought of writing a Tolkien fanfiction once, but I can’t, what Tolkien wrote is what Tolkien wrote and there is nothing to do about it. Otherwise, I read anything I come across.

Now I’ve started reading comics too, I read most of what Marvel publishes, so I started writing a bit of X-Men to, some based on the comics and some based on the movies.

So to say something about my fanfiction stories:

“When love isn’t easy” is a series made up of “Sleeping Ginny”, “Slipping away” and “Stupid boys, stupid book”. It’s mostly based on the 6th movie (for now at least), and is about Ginny and Draco’s relationship.

“Change my life” is a story that popped into my head, I don’t have a clue where it came from; it just popped up with plot, dialogue and everything, it was meant to be published. It is post-Hogwarts, mostly about my two favourite characters in the HP books; Herm and Gin.

“If tomorrow newer comes” is a songfic, my idea of what might happen the last night before the big battle. And it will not have a Sequel; find out what you think will happen.

“Red as fire, green as emeralds” the story I’m really in love with. A story from James and Lilly’s time at Hogwarts. It is now complete, there might be an epilogue

“All I’m afraid of, is you” I like writing angst Ginny/Draco but this is the only one I have published.

“Without a fight” normally I’m a Herm/Rem girl, but this time I wrote a Herm/George. It’s a different kind of brake-up story.

“I don’t care” my very very screwed up Rouge Logan non-relationship.

“Sleepless nights, vodka and a self-portrait” my first Kitty Colossus story based on some of one of my comics favourite Colossus Bloodline and Astonishing X-Men. Took me forever to write, I kept messing it up but now I think I’m happy with it. Hopefully I haven’t messed up their relationship as I normally mess up every other relationship I have written recently.

I have a Norwegian story to, but I won’t bother to write about it in English so I’ll write about them in Norwegian.

Hope you enjoy, Love siri

Vel, alt å si om meg er allerede sagt på engelsk der oppe, men jeg skulle skrive tre ord om den norske fortelingen mine. “If tomorrow newer comes” har også en norsk versjon, den ble skrevet først og er derfor bedre enn den engelske. Og det blir ingen oppfølger, la fantasien jobbe.
