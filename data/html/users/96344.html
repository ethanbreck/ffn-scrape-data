Author has written 6 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Digimon, Gravitation, Final Fantasy IX, Beyblade, and Mass Effect.

Between NC17 and songfics being taken down and through my own fickle nature my published list is rather short. I also have an account on AO3 where you will find the un-moderated versions of some of my adult fiction and other fiction that is not accepted here.

Please leave me reviews and feel free to give any constructive criticism - it's always welcome! Though some things you should take into account is that 1) I am Australian and I therefor mostly adopt the British spelling of words, and 2) the pure age of some of my fics here.

Most of my published stories here act purely as archive. They are very old. I have no intention of editing them, even for the sake of formatting (which has changed so much over the years). I apologize for general crappy quality and how difficult they are to read now, but hey, it's fun to start back in 2002 and work your way through to 2011 if you're bored!

My ambitions include being a published (like, for real) author when I finish my book, finally putting my degree in editing to good use, and buying several cats and a goat in my old age. In that horrible real life thing I spend all my time writing and playing video games.

Hobbies: Writing, video games, being a complete tool and avoiding the world.

Favorite book: Martin Middleton - The Circle of Light
Favorite video game: Dragon Age
Favorite movie: The Fifth Element
Favorite color: Red
Favorite word: Probably
