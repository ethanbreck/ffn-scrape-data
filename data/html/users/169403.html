Hello,

This will be the final time that you will hear from me.

There has been someone reporting my stories for various reasons, and so they keep getting taken down. Due to this, I have stopped using this pen-name and am now writing under a different active pen-name.

Do not be alarmed if you recognize my work taken down from this account and uploaded under a different pen-name starting with 'Bluer' as the first five letters sometime in the future. That is my new account.

It has been my pleasure to provide you with some (hopefully) interesting stories, and I send a thank you to all of those of you who have supported me throughout my ff writing journey. I wish you all the best.

Signing out permanently,

Thank you so much for your amazing support through my time here in ff.net, I've learnt a lot because of fantastic constructive remarks, and I have felt as though I have grown into a better writer than when I first started.
