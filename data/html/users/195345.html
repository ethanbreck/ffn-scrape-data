Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter.
Well, I got hooked to fanfiction thanks to an old fetish with Cardcaptor Sakura (NOT cardcaptors, mind you, and yes, there is a BIG difference). I still love the coupling S+S and T+E from that section...
Icebreakers by Suppi-chan has to be one of the best fanfictions ever written. If you know anything about CCS you simply MUST check out Suppi-chan's works...extreme talent right there.

So anyways, I got a little into Digimon thanks to theultimatesora as well and found some really cute fics, but I never actually completed any fics I wrote about the digidestined (normally Takari or Taiora), but my hopes are one day to complete a Takari...very best-friendy, but not precisely in the R/Hr (they're not incredibly stubborn or proud, lol -_-')

Then, I actually ventured into the Harry Potter section of ff.net, and much to my (pleasent) surprise...I found some good stories. I had been scared that it would only be filled with fics that disgraced the book -- and well, yes, some are less than wonderful, I did find some wonderfully written stories about one pairing I had become rather suspicious of -- R/Hr. The one that got me hooked on this couple and actually got me to consider the prospect of H/G (before I had thought the idea of Harry with his "best friend's little sister" a bit absurd) was How Long Will It Take? by Heather-Bether. Great story ^^. And I can't forget Chatting by meeker which got me interested in Fred/Angelina

And after months of reading and just a bit of side-writing... I got a penname and completed "Me Too." Not my best work, but something that was done, so I posted. A few weeks later... I got an interesting idea of a story that would include BOTH of my couples and that believed (and I discovered I was right) would be fun to write. So, in the end, I don't think I've done half-bad with "Ginny & Harry's Plot." And I've discovered how much a simple review can mean (and I've been doing more reviewing myself =) ). I've found there are some awesome ppl on ff.net like Adnap Nottap that happen to be not only brilliant writers (You can't not read "Snowball Fight", you just can't) but also wonderful reviewers!

--On a note about "Ginny and Harry's Plot":

4/2: Um...so much for posting in December... i'm sorry! I'm glad I posted it anyway, now, though. I should post it FINISHED soon... I hope. It's been really really really busy lately, but I hope to be getting some time soon.

I actually made a chapter outline for a new fic I plan on writing. It should be about 9 chapters long and it focuses mainly on Ron (it's R/Hr). I plan to write A LOT of that before posting so that I post in even amounts of time. Not two days here, a month there, but once a week.

hmmmm, well, I saw this fan art of them about to kiss and Hermione had this particular look on her face that sort of inspired that story. Also, I've recently watched movies like The Cutting Edge (read sunshyndaises fic..that's why I got the movie and LOVED it) and other romantic movies that are getting me *back* into the writing mood. I'm a bit worried about keeping my characters in character though b/c I haven't read through the whole series in a few months. Oh well, I think I'm just going to try to enjoy to write this one... (i won't post anything until G&H 's plot is uploaded *complete and edited*).
