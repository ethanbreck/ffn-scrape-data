Okay. I have deleted some stories that I did not like, and am no longer fond of keeping up. I deleted those stories because I no longer like how I have written them, and I feel somewhat embarrassed by them. Any previous works that were WIP's have been abandoned. Sorry to all the people who enjoyed the fiction, and wanted it continued. I have lost all inspiration for the fandom and have moved on to others.

I don't upload my fanworks onto this site any more. If you are interested in seeing much more improved work, then go to Peach Allgeretto.

Just in case you want to know what fandoms I dabble in before checking out my works, they are: Star Wars, Weiss Kreuz, Yuugiou, .hack//DUSK, .hack//GU, Final Fantasy VIII, Eternal Sonata, Naruto, Kyou Kara Maou, Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann, and Original. Some major warnings are that I write het, slash, and femmslash. Fics are labeled accordingly, please do not read if the subject matter does not appeal to you.

Also, please do not come to my journal ranting that what I write is wrong, and so on. The only opinion I care about is whether or not you can critique my work in a civilized manner or just want to compliment it. You can even leave a message saying that you didn't like it, but just don't be rude about it. That's all I ask.

Thanks for your understanding.
