I've changed my pen name (again, slightly)! Turns out if you wait a few years, your dream-name CAN become available. Previously "Linzy" and "SpookiestMoose". Sorry for any confusion!

Things About Me:

I don't upload much because I want my writing to be very good when I post it. Other update-delayers include cosplay, sleep, and being a grown-up. (XP)

I do read a lot of what other people write, and I try to leave constructive reviews. Please PM if you wish to reply!

I provide warnings when needed, including but not limited to: pairings, obeying or ignoring of cannon, out-of-character-ness, stupid jokes, and elves in panda suits.

REVIEWS MAKE ME HAPPIER THAN AN ANGEL IN AN OLD BOOKSHOP. WITH COCOA.

Things I Like:

Anime: Black Butler, Yu-Gi-Oh! (and Yu-Gi-Oh! Abridged), Final Fantasy VII (original and Advent Children), Paranoia Agent, Soul Eater, Yu Yu Hakusho

Not Anime: The Nightmare Before Christmas, Invader Zim, Avatar: The Last Airbender, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Joss Weeden's Firefly, Disney's Gargoyles & The Mighty Ducks, Every classic Disney Movie, Good Omens, Welcome to Night Vale

Pairings: Anything interesting with a bit of conflict. Slash, het, asexual, polyamorous, platonic, fluff, or otherwise.

Inspiration: Tim Burton, Neil Gaiman, the small discorporated moose plush floating over my shoulder, and whatever book I'm currently reading

Thanks to: My wonderful beta readers, everyone who has reviewed, and my most fantastical Avian!
