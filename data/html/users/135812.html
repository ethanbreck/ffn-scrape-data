A random seventeen girl from California who is a miserable Biology and Art History major and who secretly wants a shot at Comparative Literature and raises little dust bunnies under her bed and grows tomatoes from the keyboard of her laptop. the world's all good...

Life is like a river that flows downstream from the mountain to the sea. Sometimes the paths are wide and smooth, with gentle flowing waters that run gleaming under the sun like a plane of flawless glass; other times, the bottom rises up, filled with rocks, and the water breaks against the surface in rapid, unpredictable waves. And as a river, you have no choice but to go along with the current, your will lost, always moving away, always searching, always moving forward but never understanding where you are or where you are going, your life a mockery of what semblence of control you play to keep...

In other words, updates are sporadic. Sorry about that.
