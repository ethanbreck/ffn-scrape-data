Author has written 2 stories for White Wolf, and Dungeons and Dragons.

I don't write a lot of fanfiction, although I enjoy reading it. What I do write here is based on role-playing games, and the characters within are original, although the setting is not. I'm more comfortable writing my own characters than risking mangling other people's.

I'm thirty-nine years old, and I live in Colorado. I'm about as hardcore left and feminist as they get, and that is reflected in my writing, be warned. I have two small children, and have been very neglectful of my writing since they were born. I'm trying to get myself back into practice again, but because it's been so long, I don't expect what I do to be terribly good.

I'm really out of practice with my writing, and have been ever since I had kids. I'm trying to jump back in with nanowrimo, and we'll see how it goes.
