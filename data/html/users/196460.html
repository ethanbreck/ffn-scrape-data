About Me:

It has been ages since I've visited this site. I've become a member to new fandoms, left a few, rejoined some others, although Sailor Moon will always remain my constant. I haven't written any fics for SM yet, sadly. My obsession for Fire Emblem has been rekindled, no thanks to the latest installment. (I'm looking at you Awakening.) What can I say? I really enjoy the multiple pairing feature it hosts, and the tactician class is my favorite.

I'm a hardcore multishipper; I like a lot of pairings, and I also like to mix and match. I am particular to unusual ships, but I'm game for most. And I do enjoy harems, though in my mind, only a few characters can have the privilege of being the harem focus (usually a female *cough*).

I look forward to posting more fics and finishing the ones saved in my files. (Seriously, they've been sitting there for years, waiting for me to work on them.)
