Author has written 8 stories for Cowboy Bebop, and Sailor Moon.

As you can see, I've been writing fanfics for a long time (some not always so great). At the present moment, the only one I'm updating is Resonating Light, so apologies to those who have asked me to update Kodoku no Fortress. I may come back to that one someday, but it's been so many years that I'm just not sure if I'd be able to do it anymore. Um, unless you bribe me. I accept cookies and promises of fresh chapter ideas.

If you are interested in reading more of my recent work, please check out my writing journal linked as my homepage.

I was also one of the co-authors for the much more popular Bleed, which you can find here on FF.net under Evil Authoress, Inc. (or see my favorites).
