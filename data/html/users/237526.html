A wacky parody of SM seasons 1 through SuperS that I had to write for journal entries. Is my first fiction. Read with caution. Is Finished.

My take on a Harry Potter/Yu-Gi-Oh crossover. I'm thinking of redoing it because it is a bit fast paced. It'll be a while before I actually do it if I decide to. Is Finished.

To Find a Wizard-
May also go under construction. Is the sequel to Ten Souls and You're Free. Once again, Harry Potter/Yu-Gi-Oh crossover. Is Finished.
See Past Me-
A closer look at one of my favourite Harry Potter characters, Fleur Delacour. There are a bunch of other stories like this, but that's okay. Is Finished.
A Change of Character-
I think it's my best written one yet, but that's just me. It's Tamora Pierce. Alanna is rescued by someone you'd probably never suspect, and as a result that saviour starts to rethink his/her ways. 2nd chapter is an Author's Note. Is Finished.
Harry Potter and The Plot Parody-
A parody of plots used over and over and over again. Has plenty of guest appearances and reappearances. Read with caution. Is Finished.
Delia's Folly-
Was meant to be just one chapter, but now may have 1 or 2 more as well. Is Tamora Pierce. Is about Delia of Eldorne during the Lioness Quartet, and how she gets a crush on someone who might not be her true love after all. Finished.
Thoughts After Murder-
A kind of creepy one shot about how the Dream Teams's killer feels after the deed. Is Finished.
How Times Will Change-
A Harry Potter AU where James is the Boy-Who-Lived and Harry is his father. Is being written.
