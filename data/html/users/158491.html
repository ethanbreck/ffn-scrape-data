Author has written 10 stories for Monster Rancher series/モンスターファーム, Ginga series, and Transformers/Beast Wars.

Thanks for checking out my profile! I also go by "Eclipse-Wolven" and tend to write more serious stories for the series I'm interested in. If you read my stories, please comment on them, whether its good or bad (provided it's constructive criticism if you don't like it), as I really like hearing feedback from people so I can improve the story. Right now, I'm focusing on Ginga series (GDW & GNG) fanfics, but I also have some much older Monster Rancher ones that frankly aren't as good. That said, feel free to comment on them anyway though :).

Note:

My full length stories tend to be pretty long, but I ask you to please be patient with them and read them all the way through, as I often include plot points that are important and come back in later in the story. Also, I keep the canon characters in character as much as possible and plan the plot carefully to make it as interesting as I can for the readers.

Hope you find something you like!
