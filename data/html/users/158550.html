I am a 31 year old patent examiner in the Washington, D.C. area.

I've loved fan fiction since I discovered it at a convention in my teens. Back then, for all you youngsters, it was printed in zines. I've still got a number of those, particularly for those fandoms that don't seem to have a lot of internet sites.

I like UFO, Rat Patrol, Buffy, V, Babylon 5, Starman, and Robin of Sherwood, as well as Blake's Seven, Dark Angel, Doctor Who, Hogan's Heroes, Star Wars and the list goes on and on.

I think you can figure out the fandoms with little net representation easily. Which ones don't you recognize? :-)

I only started writing a little over a year ago, as I found a fandom where there were scenes I wanted to see, but they'd never show.

And yes, I know the fandom that I write in is not on the list above. I love the characters of Earth Final Conflict, but the episodes... don't get me started. I'm writing these because we'll never see scenes like this in the series.
