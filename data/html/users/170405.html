Ok, so I figured that I would update this bio so people don't get confused. I'm, erm, a cyclic obsessive. So there is a logic to my bizarre reviews, however random they may seem. I've actually started reading fics written for other anime's just because the plot looks interesting, or because I read a crossover and want to find out more about the characters. In the end, I don't neccesarily need to like the actual cannon plot to enjoy a fic, being instead attracted to the persepective of the characters and the cannon that fan writers imbue into their writing. You'd be amazed at how prefering one character over another can radically change a cliched from another of similar ilk.

Fic Reading: DBZ, Inuyasha, Labrynth, Good Omens, Dogma, Discworld, Harry Potter, Van Helsing, Yu Yu Hakusho, Gundam Wing/AC, Rurouni Kenshin, Yu-gi-oh, Stargate, Hellsing, Trigun, Wolf's Rain, Final Fantasy 7 and 10, Spirited Away, Fullmetal Alchemist, Naruto (hey, went to Otakon and had to keep punting these weirdos wearing headbands out of the way, there was enough there to launch a fullscale invasion of... downtown Baltimore; fun thought that.) and no doubt many more to come.

Not sure if that's a compliment but I'll take what I can get. Speaking of which, I ran across a similar fic that was published before mine a few years ago, people might enjoy it, on favorite's.
