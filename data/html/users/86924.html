Joined a long time ago with intentions to write. I have lots of started and half finished fiction written over the last decade but have never succeeded in finishing any of it.

I decided very early on that I would never post unfinished work as I have read too many really good stories that just stop half way through and years later are still not updated, or even have a message from the author saying 'abandoned', and find this really frustrating.

I currently work as a contract trainer and spend a lot of my time reading fanfiction and reviewing old writing projects. I may actually get something completed before I retire.
