What is there to say about me? I'm unique. Quite odd if I do say so myself.

I'm 17 and my birthday is November 18, 1987. I pride myself on my height of 5'3. And I love my green 1995 Nissan 200 SX.

I go to an incredibly preppy high school in Arizona. I am also enrolled in the AP program and I truly question my sanity when I signed up for those classes. I'm a nerd.

I play softball and like to think I'm pretty good at it. Right field is quite a lovely place.

I'm obsessed with movies and everything Harry Potter related. Yes, even the movies. I adore the third one! True, it didn't stay true to the book, but Alfonso Cuaron is a genius! I love it!

I'm addicted to Lily/James fanfictions and have been for quite some time now. I'm a sucker for fluff and would definitely classify myself as a hopeless romantic. I have terrible luck in the guy department.

I didn't lie to you. I am, indeed, an odd person. Deal with it! =)
