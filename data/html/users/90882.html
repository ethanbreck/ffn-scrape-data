Author has written 14 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Fushigi Yuugi, D N Angel, Harry Potter, and Death Note.

Hello again, all. I'll be updating again, but only with stories I've been working on recently-- meaning, none of that tripe I was working on... :checks dates: Oh, dear. Six years ago was the most recent for this site, and most are seven years. Meaning I was in early high school, so don't read them! Please! They're embarrassing, but still a reminder of how I used to write, so I'll leave them for history's sake.

Also, I write more consistently on adultfanfiction.net under my general pen name 'Duomi', which I much prefer. That's also my pen name for deviantart.com, if you're curious-- I'm a much better artist than I am a writer.

CURRENT STORIES: (Stories that aren't half a decade old, and are being worked on. All are Death Note at the moment...)

Goodnight Moon: COMPLETE. Light and L grow closer during their time chained together as they hunt the Yotsuba group, but what will happen when Light regains his memories...?

Tear You Apart: WiP. Sequel to Goodnight Moon. Kira and L are on the run from the world with Mello, Near and the NPA hot on their trail... but with logic against him, will L stay with Light? It's a game of shifting alliances where anyone can win.

(Heh, doesn't that sound dramatic? Tear You Apart is my baby, though. One of these days I want to overhaul Goodnight Moon and play around with it a little, but I started it after not writing fanfiction for years, so to me it changes markedly as it progresses. TYA was started when I'd had practise with fanfiction for months of GM, so it doesn't embarrass me-- I just get worried that I'll lose direction!)

Thanks For the Memories: Co-written with Gloria B., this is a prequel to Goodnight Moon and to Tear You Apart. It's centered on the Wammy's House and how Mello, Near and Matt came to be the characters we're familiar with in the series. Not as light and fluffy as it might sound: we're of the mind that the Wammy's House is much more sinister in design than it appears to be, and, of course, Beyond Birthday was alive and well at the time, developing into the character he became.

Arcadia: AU Death Note with an attempt at a film noir feel. Light is a private investigator moonlighting as a vigilante serial killer. When a case he's working catches L's attention, however, the two will need to cooperate to survive.
