Hi! My name's Megan. I'm just a college student, doing all that figuring-out-what-to-do-with-my-life stuff. Every once in a while, though, I need to get away from it all and sink into the world of Digimon, where reality can't hurt me. ;) It's also where Taichi and Yamato are deeply in love with each other. And, of course, Ken and Daisuke are also deeply in love with each other. Expect a lot of that here. It's basically all I do now.

I have been off, enjoying others' works. Writing doesn't come as naturally as it did in the days where I could wake up and watch Digimon on Fox Kids every morning. :is sad: But I do have a few things to offer that have come about in the two years that I haven't written any fanfiction! I hope you'll enjoy my new stories.

NEW as of 9/19/06:

The Letter -- Just uploaded today. A little bit of Taito/Yamachi fluffy-ness I hope you'll enjoy. :)

Good Thing There's a Bad Cat -- Also just uploaded today, though written over a year ago! More Taito/Yamachi fluffy-ness, with a mischievous cat thrown in for good measure.

Stories I HOPE to Continue Work On:
Bewitching the Dragon -- This little puppy's been on the back burner for quite a while. I'm actually hoping to revamp what little I've written of it so far. If I do (and I have started doing so), then I don't think I'll have any more couples than Daiken/Kensuke and Taito/Yamachi. I might include the yuri couple I was planning on. But we'll have to see how that goes. It's a work in slow, slow progress!
