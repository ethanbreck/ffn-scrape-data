Author has written 17 stories for Digimon, Card Captor Sakura, and Harry Potter.
After a lengthy absence from the world of Fanfiction.net (make that about three years, according to my last update), I may, or may not be making a return. I've recently started reading fanfiction again. And I'm starting to think about writing again.

It's funny, the last time I updated this profile, I was 16. I'm 20 now and this is just...kind of funny. =)

I'm not really sure how many of you are still following my work. Considering the fact that I still recieve reviews in my inbox once every blue moon (mostly friendly threats about continuing Sock-Sucking Vortex), I assume that my stuff is still being read.

Some of my most popular stories, unfortunately, are in formats that aren't allowed on FF.net anymore, so I'm going to have to get rid of them. This means a few of the stories will be removed. I'm thinking of placing them somewhere else. I believe my fanfiction Yahoo! Group is still in existence. If not, I may just post them in my livejournal.

I have removed several stories, most notably The Sock-Sucking Vortex and the Angela et Diabolus series. This is especially important for SSV, I contacted everyone who has ever left me a review, listed the story as a favourite, put the story on alert and put the story into their C2 communities of the story's removal and asked them to e-mail me back if they wished for further information. If anyone else wants to be informed, e-mail me and let me know. I'm very appreciative of your support and I feel bad about leaving the stories for the last three years without a peep. Keep in mind that their republication does not necessarily mean their continuation, though I feel I want to attempt it.

Lastly, I'm not particularly proud of most of my listed fanfiction. Most of these are pre-Harry Potter and some pre-awareness of actual writing conventions. I've removed quite a few stories that were not on C2, favourite and alert lists. I kept a record of them for my own devices, but to my present knowledge, no one will ever read such atrocities to not only literature, but the series they tried to depict, again. I'll leave a little description in my profile of those stories as a tribute but that's the last trace of them on the internet. =P
