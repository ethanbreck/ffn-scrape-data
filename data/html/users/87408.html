This is a collection of my older fics from 2005 to about 2010 or so. I am no longer active in fandom and do not use this website. I personally no longer support many of these stories. Some of them are very painful for me, and a few have themes that may be disturbing. Some of that was intentional; most of it was not. I was dealing with issues. I leave them in archive here solely because I see little sense in removing them after all these years. Some people enjoy them. Some people enjoy using them as a signpost of things they dislike. Take them as you will.
