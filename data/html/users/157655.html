(February 29, 2016)

With 'After Story: Devotion' under my belt, it's time to move on to Kyou's After Story, voted on by the "Clannad ~After Story~" group on Facebook. The tentative title will be 'Reconciliation'.

I'll still work on Fuuko's AS, but it will be editing for typos, as well as looking for any continuity problems. The story is complete, though I have been considering adding a post-epilogue-type one-shot.

If any reviewers find any of those kinds of errors, please PM me so I can fix them.

Take care and God Bless you all.

Magnus Prime

(December 19, 2015)

Not as much of a gap between updates, but still...

Thanks to my recent exposure to a most excellent visual novel, I have been motivated to write again.

'Fuuko After Story: Devotion' is the result of that.

Merry Christmas (and Happy Birthday to Furukawa Nagisa-chan) and Happy New Year!

Magnus Prime

(March 7, 2015)

It has been a LONG TIME!

In February 2006 I married my Wifey, then joined the Army that same year due to financial difficulties. Got out in 2009 on a Medical Discharge (honorable) and immediately started attending seminary. Earned an MDIV in 2012, and am now about to earn a Christian Counseling degree.

But, I miss the fanfiction, so I'll be trying to update my works over the next few weeks. In fact, I have just posted a huge update to Slayers: Loss.

Take care and God Bless you all.

Magnus Prime

2004 was an interesting year for me.

At this point, I don't know what's going to become of my incomplete works. I have too many things on my plate, many responsibilities. As a result, I cannot be available for any editing work.

Feel free to e-mail me with any questions regarding spelling or grammar, but as far as reviewing any works...I'm sorry, I just don't have the time or energy to spare any more.

Many thanks to those who have taken the time to read my works, and more thanks to those who took the time to submit a review.

If I find myself with the appropriate resources (time, etc.) I'll try to get some of this done ('A New Challenge' comes to mind).

Take care and God Bless you all.

Magnus Prime
