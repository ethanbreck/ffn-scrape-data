Hi, my name's Clare, I'm 28 although my brain has yet to catch up to the fact I'm that old. It still thinks I'm meant to be 15 at the youngest and 21 at the oldest. Haven't written fanfiction in ages but thinking of giving it another go whenever I don't have anything orignal I'm working on. Or just to stop me struggling with anything original as I have been doing for a while.

Will probably be writing fanfiction for a lot of the fandoms I'm in, whatever I get an idea for really. Could be anything from How To Train Your Dragon, anything Doctor Who related, Kickin It, Grojband, The Marvel Cinematic Universe, just a lot of random stuff really. And yes, even though I'm on the wrong side of 25 I do watch a lot of stuff that's meant for kids (a lot of them have a lot to make them enjoyable for adults too). I read a lot of Young Adult novels too because I find the characters far more relatable in then than in anything meant for adults.
