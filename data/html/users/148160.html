Back to the random "about me" stuff

... I love things and people that are slightly off the norm like racially ambiguous people, people who are anomalies in their environment, (like me!), different languages--Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, Italian, Japanese--people who love and embrace their own culture, diversity. I also love things that are organic and sometimes artificial, being contrary just for the sake of arguing, people who analyze and question and don't just go with the crowd. I just like people that are interesting. I love true freedom, but don't feel enough of it on a regular basis.

As far as my writing, I pretty much only write about the "minority" characters ... anyone who isn't the main character because you can open a book or watch TV to see them. I think "women of color" (like me) are so under-represented everywhere so I write featuring people like Leah, Bonnie, Claire, and Manny. When I don't, I usually find it hard to finish--hence the Lizzie McGuire/Higher Ground/Roswell/Smallville stories.
