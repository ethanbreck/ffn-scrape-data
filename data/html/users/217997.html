Updated Oct. 2010: I am currently a graduate student enrolled in a doctoral program somewhere in the USA. I plan to continue my stories and, hopefully, finish them up eventually. A lot of things have changed over the last seven or so years, as has my writing style. I appreciate that many of you still seek updates for my stories and send me encouragement to continue. As I've written in author's notes updates before, I plan to do just that. I am still mostly an anime fan, as I feel Japanese animation provides a lot more depth in terms of storyline and artwork than American cartoons do today. However, I am a huge fan of comics (DC and Marvel) because, aside from growing up with them, I feel they are one of the few American animation groups that have managed to use "cartoons" to their full potential. I'm first and foremost an X-Men fan in this medium (and as such, a Gambit fan). I would say, that the Dark Knight and Night Wing (the original Robin) capture my attention the most next. In terms of anime, my interests are varied, so I will not get into them here. Especially as none of my stories currently focus on any anime genre. I am a fan of any story as long as it is well written and well thought out. Action and a little bit of romance (any kind) is also a selling point for me. I have many other interests outside of these two genres (I will shortly mention Anne Rice's Vampire Chronicles and Josh Whedon's Buffy here), but I do not feel it is necessary to get into that for the same reasons given above for anime. I hope you enjoy my stories and come back to read them when I myself come back to write them!
