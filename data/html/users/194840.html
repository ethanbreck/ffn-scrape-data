Esteemed Readers:

I am sorry to say that both of my stories that I have posted here on Fanfiction.net will no longer be continued. I'm sure you all had more than an inkling that this would happen, considering neither story has been updated in at least a year's time. I no longer have inspiration or much of an interest in either, and, as such, it would be an injustice to you all to continue these fanfictions when I no longer can give them my absolute best while writing them. I don't want to give you work that I've done with only my brain and not my heart.

I know that I said a long time ago (when I first started, I believe) that I would never leave a story unfinished, and I feel guilty for not being able to keep my promise to you all. I ask for no flames or exceptionally angry reviews. I know I'm not the only one by far who has done this, so don't bother trying to make me feel even worse about this. You won't succeed.

Moving on, I truly do enjoy writing, and I am confident that I will post another plot-bunny sometime within the near future. In fact, there is one that is constantly jumping around in my head, incessantly demanding to be written to completion. However, this time I will make sure that I can finish what I've started and have a clear plan of exactly where I'm going from beginning to end.

As for Anything Would Be Better than Your Goodbye on a Bad Day, I can safely say that, in a way, at least, it is already complete. It has been in this state since the very first chapter was posted. So I am taking the additional chapters off of the site, as my initial idea was not to actually continue this story. I feel it has become irritatingly redundant, and I also do not know how to end it. Nor am I very interested to the Gundam Wing series anymore. As for Fields of Gold, I've lost inspiration for that story, as well as interest in the series, for the most part. I will keep the story posted, at least for the time being, but it is highly unlikely that it will ever move to the status of complete.

Thank you for all your support and nice reviews. They were really encouraging, at least most of them. Like Bertie Bott's Every Flavor Beans, I occasionally encountered a review that tasted suspiciously like earwax. But it most certainly has beengreat fun so far, everyone! Thank you!

In light of recent plot-bunnies, I have one that wishes to manifest itself as a one-shot songfic (Wow, can I really write an honest-to-God one-shot?), and the other wants to be an epic of sorts that involves time travel. Both are ideas stemming from the works of the absolutely wonderful J.K. Rowling, so be expecting something not that far from now. The multi-chapter story will have a more...shall I say...unconventional kind of pairing. And the other will probably involve James and Lily. I might tell you guys a little more about it if you send me a PM.

Oh, and if you actually read this in its entirety, or even any of it at all, much kudos to you!

Thanks for all your time and patience,

Yurilune (Yes, I changed my name again)
