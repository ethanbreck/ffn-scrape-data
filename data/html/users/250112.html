Been a while since I last poked at this, hasn't it?

While I haven't stopped writing, I've slowed down in posting stuff or writing longer stuff these days, and I'm not as active in fandoms as I was when I was younger. While I've not stopped writing altogether, I've shifted mostly to shorter drabbles or stuff I can wrap up in a chapter or two over the longer "epics" I tried when I was younger. I've come to realize over the last three or four years that I actually prefer short formate over long.

This has, naturally, led to the result that anything here that I was working on that was intended to be a longer story can effectively be considered dead. I'm not beyond letting someone take over them, but some I'm not.

In the meantime, welcome. This is my little corner of FF.Net. I've been here a long time. Enjoy what's here.
