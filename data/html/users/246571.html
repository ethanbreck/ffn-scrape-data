Hi. I’m going to do this because a) i think you all have a kind of right to know who is writing these stories, and b) I’m INCREDIBLY bored.

Let’s start with the basics. My name is Rosie, actually my full name is Rosanna, but no one ever calls me that (in fear of their own life, I expect). I'm about 5"4" tall, with green eyes and golden hair which is always tied up and with a ribbon in it. I'm 15 and i live in "The land of Sweeping plains" - for anyone who is not familiar with that poem, that means Australia. I live In Sydney now, but i was actually born in Dorset, England.

I like to do lot's of things - mainly things that don't interest the majority of girls my age. My favourite places are generally not man made. I love the Royal National Park in Bundeena – yes, where all the fires were in 2000. I love going down the South Coast to all the little coastal towns, and I love bushwalking. I also like going to Forests and woods, or lying on the grass in the middle of some field (or soccer pitch) and looking up at the sky. Among my favourite man made/ built places are all the historical buildings i.e. the Governor’s Cottage on Clark Island, the Tilba-Tilba sweet shop (and cheese factory!) and my grandparent’s house in the Lake district. However, I do enjoy shopping and going to places that are VERY modern. I play Soccer for the Leichhardt Saints – we’re not doing very well in the comp. This year.

I love to read. It’s one of the best things to do in any situation in my opinion. Books can change your mood completely, as well as your view on life. I’ve been changed by the literature that surrounds me, I know that.

Favourite books: too many to name, but here are some. Sophie’s world, Children of the Dust, Harry potter, Artemus Fowl, Red wall, Owl service, the Obernewton, Lord of the Rings, Watership Down, The Tomorrow series, Bridge to Wiseman’s Cove, Goodnight Mister Tom (Old favourite since I was eight), Looking for Alibrandi, 48 Shades Of Brown, Wierdstone of Brisingamen, Sea of May, Narnia (it’s a old favourite as well), The Hobbit, Winter (anything by John Marsden is Great – besides Dear Miffy), Fat Boy Saves World, Hitch Hiker’s Guide series, Bridge to Terabithia (another old classic favourite), Northern Lights and the whole His Dark Materials Trilogy, Confessions of Georgia Nicholson (I laughed like a loon on loon tablets), Adrian Mole, Wild Swans, Pillars of the Earth, anything by John Grisham or Stephen King, The Gathering, Playing Beattie Bow, Peter Rabbit, anything by Roald Dahl, A Brief History of Time, Borrowed Light….. The list just goes on and on and on…. I have over 200 books in the bookshelf in my bedroom…

Favourite Movies: Bend it Like Beckham, Sixth Sense, The Truman Show, any animated Disney Movie that is not a sequel, Monty Python (Life of Brian and Now for Something Completely Different are my all time favourites), Wallace and Grommit The Wrong Trousers, Chicken Run, I am Sam, A.I, The Quiet American, Billy Elliot, Looking for Alibrandi – although the book is way, way, way, way better, Murphy’s Law, Snow falling on Cedars, Little Women, Apollo 13, Toy Story 1 & 2, Shrek, Aussie Rules (that movie is so good. So sad, yet so very well made), the Matrix, The Matrix Reloaded, Lord Of the Rings Fellowship of the Ring and Two Towers, Il Cinema Paridiso…

Likes: Sunshine, Rainy days (I know those two completely contradict each other), food, happy people, people who are their own self rather than following the trends, lateral thinking puzzles, shiny apples, cute words eg. Did you know that puggle is the term for a baby platypus?, writing, drawing, anything involving craft or making a mess, sitting in the middle of a park, parks, seafood, little kids who have out grown the grotty stage, olives, my friends, TELEVISION, hats, ribbon, formula one racing, swimming, soccer, CRICKET (ha ha to all you English supporters out there!! Australia Rules the ASHES!!), rowing (head of the river is so fun. but we lost :-()smiles, beaches, waves, ice cream, junk food in general (what can I say, I’m addicted to sugar), fireplaces, Nova 96.9, politics, Delphiniums, philosphy…

By the way, I like the colours blue, orange and green, my favourite animals are pandas, penguins and Weedy Sea Dragons, and I support the Inner West Rabbitohs in the Rugby League (that’s my, that’s my, that’s my that’s my team! Sorry you wouldn’t know that song unless you live here), and my favourite bands are REM, Oasis, Living End, SIMON AND GARFUNKEL (what can i say, i'm a freak)

Hi everyone... If you're a What It Is fan... then I'm so sorry. I'f you haven't read the latest chapter, then i suggest you do, and if you have... well, I'm sorry.
