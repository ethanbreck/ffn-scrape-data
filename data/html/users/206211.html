Hello.

A few of you might be wondering where my fan fictions went--

I deleted my work. This is not because of a few stupid flamers; those didn’t touch me. I’m proud to say I’ve only had four of those in total. (Yes, I counted)

I wanted to thank you for 328 members that put me on their favorite author’s lists. I want to thank you for outstanding numbers of reviews on Cat Scratches and Cuts, Take A Dive, Pepper ‘Me’ Up, Sunburn. My prime time in writing for this site.

A few of you will think it’s not fair.

A few of you will think it’s a loss.

A few of you might be pissed and not understand me.

Hell, maybe a few of you will be glad.

That’s fine. I know what I’m doing is unexpected and heavily misunderstood.

It’s my own reasons however, and my words wont justify my feelings enough so I’m not even going to try and explain it. I decided that it’s enough and I’m done. Easy come, easy go. Imagine I was never here. Enjoy what I had written for the time that I had written it. I feel like I’ve improved enough to be content with writing on my own for a while again.

It’s been a great year and a half. I’ve learned a lot, improved a bunch and met with amazing people.

And just for the record, I met a lot of shitheads. Well, not a lot. A specific two - I'm sure you know who you are - pink, i mean, think..and if you're anoymous i'm sorry you never got the guts to say it to my face.

I certainly enjoyed writing it. I enjoyed feedback.

But it’s over now.

I’m sorry if I’ve disappointed any of you. I’m sorry if I angered you.

I just want you to know that I did and still appreciate you guys and all your responses.

I’m going to new places, discovering new things--I hope my fics have touched you in some way.

Always,
-Ace of Spades
