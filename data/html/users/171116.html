CLASSIC VS. ROCK HAS BEEN UPDATED AS OF 2:30 pm Pacific Northwest Time on February 1, 2008.

Questions?

What are your feelings about the new additions to the site? First of all, I think before anything is offered, there should be a department that actually checks (omg, actually caring about the policies-watch out ff.net, you're actually participating!) the reports and gives specific reasons as to why we are being blocked. Other than that issue, I think they are dandy- but make me feel old D: back in my day we didn't have forums or communities. When we wanted a beta, we begged one of our reviewers who had critically analyzed our stories. They would then go over the stories before we posted. Now we have a list!? Who isn't up for simplicity? And every time I see that Beta sign I keep thinking to myself, "Why the freaking heck is there an s-set on the fanfiction site?" This is what happens when you learn old school German. Long live the s-set!

What is the next chapter in Classic vs. Rock going to be about? I have started it and sent it to my beta. Almost called her an ss set. It's five pages right now and I'm worried that it is too slow. But then I remind myself that I don't want to rush. It takes people a long time to trust others (unless you're Steffen, "oh! we have to be friends? okay here, these are the family secrets!" srsly). However, those secrets are much more prominent than before. But I am also concerned about the Artemis-i-ness. It needs more him. Mooorrreeeee. Arg.

Everyone keeps wondering if Holly and everyone else will come into play? Holly, for me, is one the hardest characters to write. She's just so on top of the ball and that trait comes so naturally for her, it is hard to make it sound as if she didn't plan ahead. She doesn't stop and think about comebacks, she just has them and they sound fluent. But yes, she will be appearing to Artemis. To anyone else? Doubtful. However...there is an exception to the rule. She will not, however, play a major role. She simply check up on Artemis and talks to him. Well, laughs at him and his teenage ways. But the chapter she does appear in needs to be re-written D: I feel silly for that one.

What about the Labyrinth? Um. Good question. I would like to yield the answer to...

Why are you so unsure? I have begun to write it, however, things need to be in certain places. I'm trying to have a cast of characters that are world known. I want everyone to be able to spot at least one or two characters they know from various subjects. However, being an English/Political Science major, the other subjects are giving me a run for my money. That is one of the reasons. Another is that Classic vs. Rock precedes this story. Now that I've read Moby-Dick, that almost precedes CvR. D: Also, the more classes I take here, the more people I find who fit the criteria. It really is snowballing.

Why do you never have one-shots: ...I use to for other genres, but they were childish so I took them down. I also can't say all I need to say in a one-shot. Those take some serious effort and dedication. However, I might work on some one-shots for Moby-Dick. The death of Ahab, the Parsee, and Starbuck haunt me. What can I say?

Why do you hold the Name "Genghis Khan"? Wow. This is an old story. The reason being when a bunch of my friends in AP World History were playing cards we took countries. I didn't have a country, but I had all the cards so, I became Genghis Khan. I have the name on the back of my sweatshirt. Which gets a lot of strange looks because I'm a girl.

What is your Writing Process? Going to school. I write in class and usually not at home. If I am at home it would be in the early morning or late at night. Usually I write it and read over it. Then I have "Readme.com" or Patrick read it out loud to me. That goes on for awhile and then I post it. Titles are usually picked from the play list I have set up. Yeah. Nothing special. Really. If I write it on paper I make generalizations and then when I'm typing I add more, or even edit.

"Classic vs. Rock", is there anything we should be looking for? Yes. But when I write, I usually (though I try not to) put very obvious clues into what could happen, or go on. Also, watch what I say at the end, because not ALWAYS am I telling the truth. There could be no 'danger' or anything like that. But then again, even when you are in your house there are many dangers surrounding you.

That's about all I can give you for now. And if you see any others, or would like to guess, E-mail me with them. I'd love to hear the speculations. Also, this helps me see if I am being sneaky enough.

Also, you can spot the lyrics of the title song in the chapter. Each one has them. If you can find them, I'll be pleasantly surprised.You must also be aware that not all the songs are originally in English and have been translated.

There are a lot of OCs in CvR. Do you think this makes it hard for the reader? Yes. Especially when I mess up on their names. Li ana, John, and Temair are constantly being changed for Kelli, Rune, and Feb. This makes it easier for me, but much harder for the reader...which sucks for the both of us. Not only the name change, but just how many friends and family members Steffen has. The only consolation that I can give anyone is that a lot of characters are one timers (like Vaughn and the leaving ASB). It will be noticable who will stay and who will go. Even some of Steffen's siblings will only be mentioned in passing. Another part of this is that Artemis is being slowly introduced to the family and making his own set of friends so he's going to know people, but not know some at the same time. It will become apparent who is more dominant in the story than other (clearly Steffen and Jared at this point). I also have to take into consideration Artemis' parents.

So you don't take criticism well? That's a load question for me. I can take criticism well, if it is phrased properly. If someone wrote, "I like this and this, but this really confused me, try to do this", I would be like "Hey! Of course, I'm totally up for explain that and trying that". I realize that I'm not a prefect writer. Nor am I the best. I'm still improving (that's why I am going to school) so I have no trouble with feed back.

If someone thinks you got it wrong, what should they do? Send me and e-mail or write it in a signed review, now that I can reply to it. Make sure to sign in. And when you write that I got something messed up, take into a count that I'm not stupid. Don't be condescending. I'm not asking you to sugar coat it. What I am asking is that you help me.

Talk to me like I was in your position, and you were in mine. I don't like "know-it-all" attitudes, because I'm sure there is something I know that you don't.

Why did you simply call the story "The Labyrinth"? That's what it is. The Labyrinth.

What about "Classic vs. Rock"? Why does it hold that title. Honestly? I've been trying to figure it out myself. Perhaps Artemis is more Classic and Steffen is more of a Rocker kid. But I could be wrong. In fact, the more I think about it the more you notice that Rock stems from Classic. Besides, who makes the rules for what is Classic and what is not?

On the subject of music, what do you listen too? I really think the question should be what do I not listen too. I really don't care what it is unless it has fallout boy. Oh yes, make sure you write that with lower case letters, because it is too much of an effort to capitalize their name...thing. When he learns to sing, call me, and maybe I'll give him the time of day. I also don't listen to all that screaming stuff. I have a brother, a cat, and neighbors and I really don't need to pay for ear damage. However, Dir en Grey doesn't count. Oh, and I don't like the Beatles. Sorry, I know, I just smashed someone's favorite group. I'm a Rolling Stones fan, though. Oh! MC Lars xD Remember, HotTopic is not punk rock D:!

What does the Labyrinth hold for Artemis? Dangers untold. Well, just as Sarah had her motely crew, Artemis will as well. Exciting huh? There will be many characters from other books, history, hard sciences, and other things that I can't explain. Since Artemis is more grown-up in the head than Sarah, he will be faced with more challenges.

What is the reason behind your picture: Mr. Smith only fights for lost causes. It makes me happy.

What about Favorite books? Blithedale Romance (Hollingsworth ftw), Scarlet Letter (Chillingwoth), Hamlet (Horatio), King Lear (Edmund pwns), Lord of the Rings (Faramir uber alles), Dark is rising sequence (Bran), Artemis Fowl (Artemis Sr.) My constitutional law book, Dr. Faustus (Mephy!) and Moby-Dick. Be on the look out for a Moby-Dick fic. I am not happy with Starbuck dying. -_- Ahab and I are having a little chat about that.

Are there any books you won't read? Yes. I refuse to read Narnia and the Twilight series. The first one I will get to, just not yet. The last one is just painful in itself to read. "I like watching you sleep". Honestly? You're sneaking into my bedroom to watch me sleep. That's called breaking and entering, my friend. It's illegal. And creepy. It isn't just that either. It disgusts me.

Why hasn't the Labyrinth been updated? Because I haven't figured out the whole plot yet. This story has a maze in it, so I need to visualize what is in what in the maze. As well as the character make up and how everything is viewed.

What is with Moby-Dick Stealing the spot light? Well, first of all, I want to give Starbuck and Ahab a second chance. Also. It's all for the Starbuck. Really, it is.

Will we be seeing any of these things in the near future? Unfortunately, I have camp and therefore cannot write anything because I am constantly on the go. However, I will be going to England, so I probably will not be writing.

You're in school as we speak, what is it like? I am currently on hold as I am studying abroad in England.
