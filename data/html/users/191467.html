I’m Callia and/or Nonni. I like reading Gilmore Girls, One Tree Hill, Mighty Ducks, Peter Pan and Harry Potter stories. I’m friends with Seabreeze and RK-128 and give them props for their anime stories. Although I have to admit that’s not my favorite subject to read, they make their stories interesting.

I'm a complete computer dunce, and I can't seem to figure out how to get my stories on this site, so till I get one of my friends to help me out, you'll just have to make believe there are stories here.

I have been writing fan fics since I was in the 7th grade and have written over 45 stories (some of which have been forever lost due to computer problems and were not included in this count). I will admit that I get a lot of ideas at the same time and often don't finish stories b/c I’m too ready to start the next one. I have however, been trying to finish some of my old ones recently.

I have currently been trying to work on some stories that will actually be allowed on this site since the majority of what I write is band/musician fan fiction and they took that section off the site. (check out my site if you like that stort of thing b/c i've got some stories up there...)

Hopefully I’ll have something up soon but until then, let your imagination run wild!
