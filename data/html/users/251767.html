I'm an overachiever, and hense had more classes than I probably should have most of the time.

I just graduated from undergrad a week ago now, and I actually have time! It's the first summer that I am not taking courses, so, as work will only take up about 20 hours a week, I have plenty of time now. I will revise all In Progress stories before I add any new chapters to them.

I have no idea how long it will take, but I'll start revising either James Meet James, or Through Time after KoS is done. I may work on the revision while I am adding new chapters to KoS, but my main goal is to get KoS done.
