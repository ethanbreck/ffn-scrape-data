Ok, So I haven't been active on here in a good long time! The last time I was here was when I was still in College and still had time to write for fun. Now after years of not writing and juggling a full time job, Grad School and my personal life, I have decided to start writing again. I will be finishing the two fanfics I already have posted and I hope to write some new ones! I am by no means the best writer, but I hope that Fanfiction.Net will give me the practice I need and feedback to make me even better. Please read and review my stories with constructive criticism. Criticism only makes you better when it's something you can change or make better. I can't change it if you just don't like my stories ;-) BEWARE: I could choose to use perfect grammar, but quite frankly I'm too lazy and too busy to go back and re-read all my chapters. Earlier chapters especially have some...issues. If you don't mind being my editor, just make the corrections and send them to me. Otherwise sorry for mistakes! P.S. if I have some super mean reviews out there floating around, I'M SO SORRY I started writing on this site when I was a kid and I was mean and bitchy at times just like a kid. I still have reviews I wish I could take down :-(
