Author has written 4 stories for Bible, Lord of the Rings, and Good Omens.
"Saellys", also occasionally called by such diverse names as Hannah Solo, undisclosed last name, Bean, Hey You, and many others, is the pen name of Hannah, an 21-year-old female college senior.

In her spare time (what's that?) she enjoys writing, reading, surfing the Internet, watching TV shows such as The Simpsons and Lost (along with the occasional episode of Buffy), and listening to music that runs the gamut from U2 to Iron & Wine. She also keeps a weblog (see link) that covers basically everything that's going on in her life at any given moment.

Favorite profic authors include J. R. R. Tolkien, Ray Bradbury, Neil Gaiman, and many others.

She has written more in the way of Star Wars fanfiction than much of anything else, but lately has been working on an original novel that she may or may not decide someday to attempt to publish.

She knows there's not much here now, but if you're bored, check out the stuff in the "Favorite Stories" and "Favorite Authors" sections--she highly recommends everything in there.

She despises writing in the third person, but for the sake of literary tradition, she'll stoop even to that.
