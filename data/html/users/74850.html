Author has written 6 stories for Hellsing, Saiyuki, and Trigun.

Sometimes you just have to say "EFF IT" and walk away from something you love. Yes, I did that. After spending 20 years literally slaving away at a job in which they refused to give a payraise for at least 15 years. I had enough. I have a new position, better pay, and more free time to my name so...

The Lady Blackhat is back after yet a THIRD multi year absence - Yes, I know that I still have stories not finished, but the final outlines are now on my laptop and I have been diligently crafting the next chapter(s) for Again Master.

In the meantime, I am re-uploading all of my Trigun works. There is a specific order to these as later stories will spin off the anime series. If you care to read them as they are uploaded, you may want to start with Ecstasy, Just for Tonight, Burgundy and Blood, and Genesis for starters. These will be followed by Ticket to the Future and Generation Gunsmoke.

Love and Peace,
