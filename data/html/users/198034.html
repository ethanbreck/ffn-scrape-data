Author has written 17 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, and Firefly.

I started writing fanfic when I was young and bored. Some of the stories I wrote were good, many were...less so. But it taught me a lot about writing and gave me the confidence to start writing original stories. I met some great people through fandom, read a lot, and learned a lot. My fanfic days are, sadly, behind me now. It's been four years since I really wrote anything. I would like to finish Torches someday, just because I'm stubborn and hate leaving things unfinished, but I can't make any promises. So thanks to everyone who's read my stuff and supported me through the years. It's been a blast. (July 2008)
