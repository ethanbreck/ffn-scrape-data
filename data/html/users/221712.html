Howdy; Titanium Gold here. If ya know me or have read my works before, good to see ya and welcome back; if not, then welcome aboard.

At the time of this latest update, it's been four years since any sort of constant update here on my part; three years since the last update at all - and for that I apologize. Much pain, loss and struggle has happened to beat the muse into submission over that time (you want to know the details, feel free to PM me); but recently, much has also happened to bring that muse back into the game. With some help from a great TV show or two and a few good friends (among others, I'm looking at you, whatif-ifonly), the muse is back. Hopefully, with spare time and energy having returned to my life after over a year, that'll be permanent.

I am in the process of writing a fun little project called Resident Evil 6: Rampage; it's my take on what would've happened if the President (in the video game Resident Evil 6) called in a little support for his Raccoon City revelations - and that support turned out to be an old friend of a certain Secret Service Agent, who would spend the duration of the game backing her and Leon to the end, saving the life of a character who should never have died in that game to begin with... with a little help from a certain group of tough FBI Agents, including Jennifer Jareau. (Yes, it's a Criminal Minds crossover - sort of. You'll know what I mean when you read it.)
Take a read as it's updated; review, and let me know what you think. It's self-beta'd and (hopefully) being updated biweekly. Until I finish that project (and any sequels that follow), all others are on hiatus for now - and some of my older, more amateur projects are being deleted in the process. Enjoy the reading, folks; whether you review or not you have my thanks for reading.

One more thing: to those with the disposition, please, don't waste your breath flaming me if you feel the desire; all you'll do is fuel the heater. Any concerns? Send me a message.
