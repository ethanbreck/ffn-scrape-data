I'm currently a high school senior and as shown, I love to write. Like many, I blame the universe's inablity to allow me to have a life (translated, means my parents inability to let me have a car) for my sometimes unsettling obsession for the TV show Buffy the Vampire Slayer. The only way I can deal with it is to waste my time writing fanfic and cram my head with visions of Spike. Overall, it's rather a pleasant affliction.
