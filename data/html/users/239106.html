What's there to say about me? Hmm...well personally, I like reading the stories more then writing them. I usually start stories and never end them. For the most part they are about Kane, Undertaker, Jeff Hardy, and Trish Stratus. Often times it's a love story between Trish and a partner of my choosing. LoLz, hey, I can dream, can't I?

But for me, I like to think of Kane and Undertaker as 'My Babies' Most females I talk to, can't believe I prefer Kane and Taker over people like The Rock, HHH, and Chris Jericho. But then again, there are a few females like myself who are totally, and completly obbsessed with Taker and/or Kane.

There's nothing wrong with that. Oh no there isn't. You should see my room. Posters...pictures...everywhere. Mostly of...Kane, and Taker. Ha. Go figure. I love to talk on aim, I'm usually on cuz I'm usually grounded from everything else. :-/

I'm only 16, and with already been on a funny farm, and suicidal, I don't think I wanna try that again. Wrestling saved my life...Kane and Taker...the loves of my life. Their wives are lucky bitches, right? Haha.

You know, when I run off, and get married, there will always be a place in my heart for Kane and Undertaker. Always. No matter what...if you feel the same way please...talk to me about it. :-D
