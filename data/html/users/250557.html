So here's a little information on what's going on with my writings, but I must warn everyone I'm not great at summaries, at all. So bare with the ones I provide, please. Thanks.

Harry Potter Stories:
FINISHED: Secrets Untold, Secrets Are Never To Be Spoken, Secrets Taken
IN THE WORKS: Lost Heir, The Plot
OUTLINED: Vanishing, The Bracelet, Perfect, Seer
WHAT'S GOING ON: Well the Secrets Trilogy is completed, and I've moved on to another Harry Potter story called "The Plot" the first chapter has been posted.
SUMMARIES:
Secrets Untold - Chanse has found out she's a witch and has to deal with leaving her home, Maui, and her friends. She goes to Hogwarts and meets someone special, along with finding out the truth about her family.
Secrets Are Never To Be Spoken - This is a story of Chanse's summer after Hogwarts.
Secrets Taken - Chanse goes back for her last year at Hogwarts and is forced to choose between Ravenclaw or Gryffindor, life or death, her brother or her life, and many other choices. Chanse has been put up against a lot the last year, but this year is the true test of her will.
Lost Heir - Rogue was a normal girl, until the Gryffindor Quidditch team came to her front door, on Halloween. She finds out that the world she's been living in is all a lie, and now she must live where she is meant to live...in the magic world.
Vanishing - Samantha (Sam) has a cloudy memory after she finds herself in the hospital wing on the first night back to Hogwarts. She can't remember anything about herself, or anything that's happened to her...except she remembers her name. Later she remembers somethiings, and it seems to attract a certain Gryffindor redhead. But Dumbledore seems to know all about Sam’s past.
The Bracelet - A magical bracelet binds a daughter to her father so she can't ever love a man without remembering the night when he almost raped her. But can Oliver save her from her father and the bracelet?
Perfect - Desiree is the fetural twin sister of Fleur Delacour. Desiree leaves Beauxbatons and goes to Hogwarts. She has never been a good Veela and ever since she was little, she wanted to be different. When she leaves for Hogwarts it gives her the perfect chance to be different, which would make Desiree truly happy...but Fleur, their grandma, and their family are going to give Desiree some trouble. Will Desiree ever be truly happy?
Seer - A short story. Harry has a weird encounter with an older Ravenclaw student (NO NOT CHO). He's told by her that he's to die, but Harry's not afraid. She tells him that he needs to leave otherwise he and someone else will die. This mysterious student turns out to be a Seer...but could she be more then just a Seer?
The Plot – Sirius wants Lily. Lily is with James. James and Sirius are best friends. Ever since Sirius was younger he’s liked Lily, and so has James, and finally Lily choose her man…James. Now Sirius is jealous of James, and in being jealous, Sirius finds someone unlikely, Vanora. Sirius has never noticed Vanora before, but with the combined powers of her and him, Lily will see what she’s lost.

X-Men:
OUTLINED: Lady Luck
SUMMARIES:
Lady Luck – A girl wakes up to find herself in the X-Men Mansion. She later finds out her name is Kat, but her parents have died, so Psylocke renames her, Katana. Katana learns of her mutant powers are to have the ability to wish for anything and have it for 24 hours. She finds out her past, and as soon as she finds it out, she has to destroys her future, with the X-Men, to gain her past, or choose another path.
Frozen Fire - A new mutant is on the scene with the powers to control both fire and ice. The X-Men want her on their side but they soon find out she’s on Magneto’s side. Even though she’s evil she seemed to have melted Iceman’s heart. Based off of the messed up storyline of the movie.
Shadow of the Past - What is Colossus loved someone before Kitty? What if Kitty and Colossus relationship was all some secret plot? Colossus is dying of the Legacy Virus and has a flashback to his early years with Professor X and the X-Men.

Zelda:
OUTLINED: Zelda’s Legend
SUMMARIES:
Zelda’s Legend – Zelda has always seemed to be a quiet frail little princess, but no one knows her side of the story...until now. She has to deal with the death of her father, battles, finding Link, helping Link in disguise, and all doing this while finding who she really is…a fighter? Or a princess?

Degrassi:
OUTLINED: Take On Me
SUMMARIES:
Take On Me - What if Jimmy and Ashley were broken up? What if Sean and Emma were now just friends? What if Craig and Manny never got together? And new girl, from Kansas City, appears after a snowing night, and Craig finds her. After she spends the night at Craig’s with Spinner, Jimmy, and Sean they all become good friends. Saria becomes the girl all the guys hang out with and all the other girls don’t really like. What if Craig starts to have feelings for Saria? Would their friendship change? What if Sean also starts to have feelings for Saria? Would their friendship change? What is Saria to do?

Inuyasha:
OUTLINED: My Strength, Untitled
SUMMARIES:
My Strength - Short story. Sango finally admits to Mirokyu she loves him, but shortly afterwards Sango is fatally wounded. Mirokyu must deal with loosing the women he loves.
