Author has written 42 stories for Young Hercules, Shakespeare, Book X-overs, Roswell, Sailor Moon, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Power Rangers, Supernatural, and Joe Versus the Volcano.

I was posting for a little while under the name BrynSpikess, but that account suddenly got deleted, so I'm back to this one. I've deleted some of my older fic from this account, but it's still up on my website. Also, some of my higher rated fic is on my site. Right now I'm just writing Buffy, Angel, Young Hercules, Sailor Moon, and Roswell fanfic, although I might branch out. I have a weakness for Spander and an addiction to slash, so beware. May start writing some Firefly, Supernatural, and Power Rangers soon too.

As you have probably noticed, several of my fanfics haven't been updated in years. Not to worry, I still have my notes, and as long as I'm not killed suddenly without warning (wink), they all will eventually be updated and even (gasp!) finished. So be patient, feel free to email me to let me know that you're still impatiently waiting, and I'll eventually get them done.

Basic facts about me: I'm a 26 year old female Sagittarius, an INFP. I have one cat. I love to write even though my writing sometimes sucks. I'm a little bit crazy, but one would have to be, wouldn't one, in order to get any enjoyment out of this world? Fave color is green, and as soon as I find a new job, I'll be dying my hair dark green again. I like fire and snowflakes and roses.
