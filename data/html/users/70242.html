Yo. This is Penfreak.

I work with three primary fandoms - Sonic the Hedgehog, Yu-Gi-Oh, and Fullmetal Alchemist. There's also a chance I will write Final Fantasy related fics in future, as I am a fan of that series and particularly of 5, 7 and 9.

Recently my writings have been leaning towards the pairingless kind, but I make no secret of my fondness for shounen-ai/YAOI, which I both read and write. My primary genres for writing are angst and drama, and I consider myself to fit quite snugly into the category of AU writer. That's alternate universe, to those who don't know. I have the decency not to use it as an excuse for writing the characters out of character, at least.

When I'm not writing AUs, I have preferred canon sources for my writings. For Sonic it's STC, the British comic. Fullmetal Alchemist, I prefer the manga - please note the spoiler warnings! Dedicated scanlation groups means I'm up to chapter 56! And for Yu-Gi-Oh, I mix and match.

Several of my fics have been removed. Some have gone for good, others will return in a shiny new version.

Current priorities are Shrapnel (a Fullmetal Alchemist AU with WW2 influences), and Mind and Conscience (a Yu-Gi-Oh! AU). Somewhere in the sidelines is Balance of Power (a Fullmetal Alchemist AU with cyberpunk tones)Also being plotted are a Harry Potter/Fullmetal Alchemist crossover that may never find its way to the Internet, and a humour YGO AU featuring Malik Ishtar and many, many dead things.

The versions seen on ff.net are not the final edits of the fic chapters. For that, you should visit my writing livejournal. ...it has pictures.
