About Me:

Hi there! I sometimes retread my stories and think about if I will finish them... Maybe I will someday! 1-2-15

My name is Elizabeth and I am a 25 year old college student from Florida. I am a wedding Photographer who enjoys working with people and being able to experience the joy of others through my lense! I live with my beloved husband, Mark, who has been a critical part of my life and healing since my father died in 2005. Together we have a small but joyous life with our two dogs, Momo and Melfina. It’s been an insurmountable feat to get to this point in my life but now I truly feel like I’ve finally gotten to that special place that we humans refer to as happiness. I’m stronger then ever before and I am proud of my life and grateful for the wonderful man that God sent me when he knew I needed it the most. We are simple people, with simple dreams, but it’s that part of us that makes us so understanding and our lives so cherished. We’ve had our many trials, our fights and our quarrels, but we’ve always made it in the end due to our personal strengths and our endless devotion to each other's happiness. We joined destinies on January 17th, 2009 and it was a beautiful ceremony and a very lovely day, our time in Mexico was a dream of bliss and relaxation that we will never forget. Even though my father was not there physically, I felt his spirit with me as I walked down the isle-- guiding me as he always has and always will through the happiest and the hardest trials of my life. I miss him terribly and sometimes I find it hard to smile during the times where I feel I have no more strength to lift up my world and let the sun kiss it's darkened corners-- but then I realize that my "Atlas" is there at my side, reassuring me with his rubber face and gentle hands that everything will indeed be okay, holding my world up for me when I am too weak to do it myself. Mark is the light of my life, the "pep" in my step, the rockstar in my scuba diving and the laughter in my heart-- he is my motivation and my every happiness (Momo too!). I love my little sister like I love the world and my nephews are always and will forever be dear to my heart. I often feel like I am a 45 year old woman trapped in a 20 year olds body but the expierence suits me just fine. I love the Lord, who strengthens my fortitude and liven's my spirit, sending me forth into the unknown where I expierence all of this world he has offered to us; misery, love, pain, hope, rejection, acceptance...! He has always been in my heart and provides me with all that I need and more. Other then all that, I spend a lot of my time surfing the web, watching movies, playing with my dogs, drawing and other things. I spend the majority of my time playing the video Game WOW where i have my own very successful guild with friends from all over the country that I love and cherish. We make so many happy memories and enjoy all of our time together.

Between daily life and WOW I have absolutely zero time to write. That being said, it's been more then a year since i updated "Wake Up" and I feel guilty/happy everytime i get a new review/alert for the story. I'll be rereading the entire story from start to finish soon so I can maybe get back to work while i babysit my nephews -- But I cant promise anything. To those who have stuck around and are waiting for an update-- I have no died and i have not forgotten .

Thanks again,

-Luky 8-30-2011

Please enjoy my stories!!
