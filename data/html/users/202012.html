Hello! My name's Quinara, and I've been around in the Buffy fandom since approximately 2001, but in the beginning I was so inactive that it probably doesn't count. I've watched the show ever since the second episode was aired on BBC2 (having forgotten the week before) and wrote my first, truly atrocious, fanfic in 2002. Ever since I've alternated between trying to forget and trying to write something decent.

The fanfic here is a collection of my more recent attempts, ie. the fics I'm not so much ashamed of. I'm a firm Spuffy shipper, and that sentiment tends to leak out, even when I'm not writing romance or the two characters aren't even together.

UPDATE 05/2009: It’s been a while since I’ve posted anything here, but I thought I’d put my one Dollhouse fic up. If you’re interested in any of my more recent Buffy stuff, of which there’s quite a lot, please check out the link to my "homepage" or my LiveJournal (quinara.livejournal.com/profile). :)
