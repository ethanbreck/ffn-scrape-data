Here to just read, have never considered myself a good writer. Just wanted an actual account with an email to leave reviews, instead of being one of that "anonomyous reviewers".

Currently I really only read Supernatural fanfic, as that's really the only show and characters that have managed to capture my interest enough to really want more than what I just see on the show. I'm especially a huge fan of the Dean-Castiel relationship as well, and pretty much any of the fics I read feature those two. I do NOT mean I'm a fan of the slash pairing involving those two, because frankly, I find slash pathetic, ridiculious, fabricated and OOC 99.9 percent of the time. Dean and Castiel share a deep emotional bond, like brothers, and yes they do care and love each other, but in a platonic way; nothing sexual about it.
