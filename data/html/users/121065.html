Well, Where do I begin? Lets start with the basics.

Age:19

D.O.B. 9/16/83

Sex:Male

Ocupation: Internal helpdesk employee for Henry Schein INC.

Marital Status: Let me just quote Squall from FF8, "Why do people depend on eachother? In the end your on your own..."

And now onto me as a person...

Life, has been better, but its been worse too. The Job (witch I tolerate I guess, its not so bad...) and my freinds are keeping me going again... Its funny how life has a tendency to keep you locked in a similar pattern...

I owe my friends a lot, since they have always been there for me when I really needed them. They helped me through some rough times, and continue to do so. No matter how much of a jerk I am to them some times...

Writing, like video games, never fails me as an outlet for pain, stress, and frustration. Im back, with avengence, as my writing helps me deal with shit once again...

Well, thats about as much into my self as Im going into. Oh there is one more thing I want to say to writers on the site. if you put your emotion into it when you write, then its always going to be good...
