Aside from Criminal Minds, I also love XME, Ninja Turtles, CSI, Justice League, Merlin, Glee, Once Upon a Time, and Teen Titans!

I now have fifty-three fics, but two of them will likely never be updated again ('Our Not-So-Perfect World' & 'Troubled Teens'). They were the first two fics I ever attempted, and my inexperience rather showed in some places. I'll be leaving them up because I like to think they serve as an example of how far I've come in my writing over the last eight years.

My most successful fic thus far (and, coincidentally, my very favourite) is 'Grimm'. It's a Flash-centered Justice League fic that I am very proud of. My other semi-fave story is 'For Tonight', a Teen Titans Beast Boy/Raven-fic.

My one and only Inuyasha fic, started almost a year ago and recently posted, is called 'Does He Love You', and is inspired by a song of the same name (sung by Reba Macintire and Kelly Clarkson). It's a Kagome/Inuyasha/Kikyo fic, offering Kagome's reflections on their relationship.

I also started some TMNT fiction, but most of it remains incomplete on my hardrive. The one I did finish, 'The Truth', has been posted.

At the start of May 2007, I began writing Reid-centric oneshots for what I hoped would be a Criminal Minds FF100 story. However, as my laziness won out again, I finished only 20 or so of them and still have many half-finished ones to work on. So, I've been posting them individually every once in a while.

And most recently, I've dipped my pen in the Merlin fandom.
