Here's some info about myself for all you curious people out there:

Name: Cairo (like the city in Egypt as everyone is fond of pointing out)
Currently Living: In the good ol U.S. of A

Livejournal:I know some chapters of my have 17+ rating. I keep getting emails asking for the unedited chapters, so I decided to post them on my LJ: http: //cairo-39. livejournal.com (just delete the spaces). I apologize for getting angry at those who have asked me about sending them the unedited versions. I did not realize that the link wasn't working for some people.

Interests: running, reading, drawing, writing, Harry Potter (slash--it goes w/o saying), Kuroshitsuji (I ADORE the anime. Although it is not shounen-ai, it is absolutely amazing but a little confusing so you got to read the manga as well), Prince of Tennis, Mirage of Blaze, most anime in general.

Favorite Pairings: HP/DM, HP/LM, HP/SS, HP/TM(LV). I'm not really into reading threesomes or PWP. I am very picky on Harry being paired with LM, SS, or TM(LV). Sometimes people make the relationship between them so very fluffy and it's quite annoying. Anyway, those are my main favorite pairings. I refuse to read anything that doesn't have Harry as one of the main pairings. I'm pretty Harry-centric so fics that are Draco-centric or whatever and totally bash on Harry I refuse to read.

Currently Working On:

Butterfly Kisses- A slash fic involving Harry and Draco.

Power- HP/LM

Right now, I'm more concerned with getting Butterfly Kisses done with. I'll post the chapters as soon as I get done writing them.

Don't Have a Title Yet- Well, I still have a non-magic AU fic saved on my external hard drive, but I figure I should focus on getting Power and Butterfly Kisses done before I start any new projects. God knows I have enough trouble motivating myself to write the chapters for these two fics.

What I Hate About Most Fics- I am not a cheesy or fluffy type of girl. In fact if you knew me personally, you'll find that I'm not a terribly emotional person. If any of my stories are in danger of becoming fluffy and cheesy, please tell me. In fact, I beg you to tell me!

Favorite Author Thus Far: It's so hard to really give you a favorite. There's so many good ones out there! Maya (Underwater Light is a total must read!), switchknife, Hijji, Empathic Siren (Artificial Life has everything! Vampire!Snape, Harry as the hot guy everyone wants, and it even has a plot! I love it), Fearless Diva (I'm totally in love with her fic Tissue of Silver), and silentauror (On the Wings of the Storm is my favorite fic of hers), kafeinyaro (One That Stood Alone), sparklespiff (Attrition is a PoT slash fic and it's just GAH in a good way!). There's way too many to name!

I've been away from ff.net for a while so sorry. Real life has a way of ruining time for writing. Or rather my motivation is low because I am intent on just reading amazing slash fics. I love reading comments and critiques regarding my fics so please feel free to share your opinions with me :). Flames will, of course, get an eyeroll and be ignored.
