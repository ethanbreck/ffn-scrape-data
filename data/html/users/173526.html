Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter, Saiyuki, and Bleach.

Well, what to say. It's been a few years since I've given any time to this place. I had so many plans, so many ideas. I still do, but I seem to have lost the ability to communicate them for the past while. I'm hoping to change this.

If any of you are coming here to find out what I'm doing with my existing stories, this is for you:

I have deleted my one FFIX fanfiction. It was not going anywhere when I started writing it. Moreover, it was a thinly veiled self-insert fic, and I cannot have that on my conscience any longer.

Of my two Harry Potter fanfics, Unexpected Needs will need a major overhaul and rewrite before I even think of continuing, and I'm not too hopeful on that front. The other, which is a one shot, will remain, although I may never write a sequel to it. Having just reread it, I feel like it is still a strong example of my writing, even if it is a songfic.

For Partial Differentials, I really want to continue writing this work, but I will probably rework the beginning before doing so. Somebody has got to get that group into a full blown orgy.

I am still amazed that I get favorites for The Art of Destruction once every couple months. I just reread that as well, and while I can see some errors, I probably will just leave it as is. I had a plan to write a multichapter Bleach fic, and I was waiting for the Deicide arc to end to do so. Well, the arc has ended, and Bleach has taken an entirely different turn, so we'll see if what I had in mind is still workable or not.

Also, I may start writing My Little Pony: Friendship is magic fics.

Thanks to all of you who ever gave my writing some time. I really appreciate it, and wish I could have done more to satiate the appetites I seem to have stoked in some of you. I have a pet peeve about authors like me who start fics they don't finish even when people think they're good. I'd like to not be a hypocrite about that anymore. ;-p
