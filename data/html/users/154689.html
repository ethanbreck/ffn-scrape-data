I'm a girl who likes to unwind after a hard day of bossing people around (yeah right!), dealing with unruly people and uptight higher-ups. As you may have guessed, I love to write. I always have, ever since I was a little girl. My first writing experience was to re-right the ending to a cartoon I used to watch ... I was a critic at the tender age of 6 XD. Anyway, as I stated previous, my passion is writing. I find nothing more wonderful than sitting down in front of my laptop and typing about random things that pop into my head. The only thing better than that is sitting in the forestry area where I used to hide-away in (sigh - come back to me new forest) with a notepad and pen - so many wonderful inspirations.

I will admit that my writing is not perfect, but I try to improve. Additional help is always appreciated :)
