Author has written 3 stories for Sailor Moon, and Magic Knight Rayearth/魔法騎士レイアース.

My real name is Erika, for anyone out there who cares. I came up with the name Raifuno Maki when I was 14 years old, and in 9th grade. My friends at that time were into fanfiction, something that I had never heard of till then. It was thanks to them that I decided that I needed to come up with a 'pen name'. I guess I didn't want anyone to know who was really writing my stories at the time.

I'm22, born June 07, 1982. I think since I was born in the summer time, I really like the warm weather. It helps that I live in sunny California. I'm always turning up the heat at my house or at work, I don't know how everyone else survives around me. lol

I really like computers. For a while, I was really into web pages, I even bought my own domain name, ichidou.org. But since spending a lot of money just keeping my site open, I've sort of moved on. Maybe if I ever get another spare minute, I might work on it again. I pay for it... might as well. In the meantime, I've been writing sporadically in my a href=""Live Journal /a. Fell free to drop in if you want to see what is new with me.

Besides computers, I really like Anime, duh! I watch all kinds. My favorites will always be Sailor Moon, Ranma 1/2, Magic Knight Rayearth, X/1999, Ai, Orange Road, etc. I love anime!

I play a lot of video games in my spare time as well, like .hack, xenosaga, final fantasy... I like rpg games.

I also like to read.^.^ Didn't used to when I was younger. Mostly I ready Harry Potter, and Dragonlance. Plus... I read fanfiction.. another duh.

Anyway, thanks for reading this little profile. If you read any of my fanfics or other works, send me a review, maybe it will help cure this on-going writer's block.
