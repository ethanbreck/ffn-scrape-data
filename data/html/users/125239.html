07 OCTOBER 2007

It's been nearly eight months since I last updated here, and it was all excuses. Welcome to more of the same.

Right now, I'm looking out my window into a park in South Kensington. Yep, London. Yet another reason I haven't even really been alive on here. The others remain the same - Eight characters on Paixao and one (soon to be two) on Template (both Livejournal RPs), absolute terror of writing anything that would suck majorly, and just a complete lack of inspiration.

I actually will post that little blurb, just to see what people think. I don't know if I'll post it in Kingdom Hearts or Chrno Crusade, though, seeing as it is a crossover. The Kingdom Hearts section has quite a bit more traffic, but the Chrno Crusade section is a tad bit closer to my heart. Both sections have authors I admire in them.

Well, I suppose I'll make that decision when I post it in the next five minutes.

As always, feel free to PM or email me. I love to chat. :3
