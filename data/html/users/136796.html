Author has written 70 stories for Gilmore Girls, General Hospital, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Passions, ER, Dark Angel, NCIS, Life Unexpected, and Revenge.

See I have this thing called a fiction addiction formed many years ago when I was just a little kiddie (all grown up now, though sadly still the same height :-P) its cause was no cable and an overactive imagination. While the cable aspect was resolved the disease had already taken hold, though it waxes and wanes in its severity.

If you’re reading his trying to figure out when any GG or DA fics are going to be finished may I refer you to their story summaries because try as I might with the DA stuff, the idea in my head will just not appear on the screen (I tried for 2 years and I mean trust me I seriously tried). If you just want a general ending for an unfinished story, you can PM me and I will tell you the path I intended to take the story and its resolution.

If you’re reading this regarding any GH fics: anything that isn’t Brazen isn’t going to be finished. You should also know I hate Liz and Sonny and can make that pretty clear in my stories.

Now if you have any general curiosities feel free to ask. If you’re curious about my favorite stories of own works (others you know where to find them), I prefer my shorts to my longer stories (I have a lot of oneshots).

For DA (all and always ML):

When Grey Was Yellow

If you're looking for a massive read that would be Moving On, if you're like me and once you stop reading something you can't stop, do not start this story at night. :-P

Seriously feel free to PM me if anything confuses you in my stories. Thanks for reading. Please don't forget to review if you like the story (otherwise it leads to the demise of the story), I'm a cruddy reviewer, I am the queen of 'Great job. More please.' Honestly you can type the word, 'More' in and I'll be happy, it will give me the encouragement to write the next part or start the next story because well, the story's already in my head, I know how it turns out. Typing it out is for you guys. =D

~Mia
