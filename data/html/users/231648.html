If you want me to read your stuff, just ask. I do not flame people. Why? Because flaming serves no constructive place in the world of literacy and I really think that most of the time it's akin to jealousy. There's some fantastic works of fan fic out there - read and enjoy!

Yes, I am obsessed with wrestling! I also like other stuff though - singing, writing (duh), training my dog, God (yes, I'm a Christian. How scary!!!) and I love my job and future career. Pharmacy rules!

Oh yeah, the stories. Please read 'Rogue Angel'. I know, it's very long, but it's my labour of love, my baby. And for those who've read it, yes it is a classic Mary Sue, and yes, Angel is based on me, in the loosest terms possible. As they say in the wrestling, she's me with the volume way up! We do definitely have differences, though. I'm all brains and no guts, she's all guts and no brains, lol. She SAYS the things I THINK. Plus, she's athletically talented and gets to hang out with some chronically hot guys...and gets to kiss some of them too...lucky girl.

My new series is called 'The Right Guy' and is based on the fact that in the WWE, the guys greatly outnumber the girls and yet our divas still can't find a good man! It will include stories about the following divas and WWE chicks: Trish Stratus, Ivory, Molly Holly, Lita, Victoria, Gail Kim, Lilian Garcia, Jessie (from Tough Enough 2), Dawn Marie, Nidia and Stephanie McMahon. Keep an eye out for them - I hope you enjoy!

Finally, everything I write is first posted (pilot tested) in my Yahoo group and will eventually find its way to my webpage.

Thanks for everyone who cares enough to read, you guys rock!
