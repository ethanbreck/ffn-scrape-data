Author has written 19 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Harry Potter, CSI, Torchwood, Hawaii Five-0, and Teen Wolf.

Stats: 31 year old woman from Michigan (I'm getting so old. Had this account since I was 17. Yeesh)

Update 1 - So, I started a new fic in a new fandom. Because I fell in love. With a character. Of course. So, Teen Wolf is my current "obsession." So I guess I am back in the game after a year out, though I am still working on my original. Which is sadly slower going than this new fanfiction...which is just kind of sad.

Update 2: To all of those reading my older stories and looking into updates on them, I have to say that I am sorry that most of them seem to be abandoned. It's not a case of abandoned so much as that I've outgrown not only that writing style but also the story-line's themselves. I've had reviews recently for Baby of our Dreams asking me to continue...and I'm sorry, but I won't. Now that there is over ten years between now and when I last updated that story, I can honestly say that the story is something that doesn't appeal to me to continue. To be honest, the premise just makes me cringe and I've written better and similar stories where Harry did the right thing and went to a social service of some kind...rather than hide out at a "worst enemy" home, no matter how he feels. I was a bit too far into the realm of artistic license at 18 and now tend to stick a little closer to reality.

So, in terms of most of the stories on here, I very much doubt that I will continue them. However, I will also not put them into "completed" status, because that irks me when other writers do that to abandoned stories. I am not going to do it to my own readers. I encourage you to read them if you like them, just go in expecting that they will not be finished and feel free to infer your own endings! That, however, does not give you permission to steal my stories and post them as your own. I may not like them all that much anymore but they are still mine.

Thanks for taking the time to read this...and for reading my stories. You all are awesome!
