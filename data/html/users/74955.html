i'm a gentle soul living in the weirdest city in texas, going to college and obsessing over all sorts of things. I've been reading all sorts of fanfiction for years, but I'm just now brave enough to try my hand in it. (Some of these stories have been gathering dust on my digital shelves for years.)

I've been working very hard on a whole bunch of stories that are prequels to Starry Starry Night, but am experiencing the frustration so many of you fellow writers have also experienced when trying to post your stories.

I want to thank everyone that has reviewed, because it really does help! Thank you! thank you! thank you!

I also want to thank my muse, because she's so cruel and demanding.
