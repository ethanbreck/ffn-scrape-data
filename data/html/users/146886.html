AKA Far Waters AKA Darkwaters-I'm having a bit of an identity crisis (not!). My attention wanders constantly, but do follow some fandoms with a bit of consistancy, namely Bleach, Naruto, Harry Potter, and Samurai Champloo. Most of my writing will probably be for one of said fandoms, though there is always the possibility that I may throw a curve ball and write something for some really, really obsecure fandom. If you check my LiveJournal it will have updates on story status because I don't post author's notes seperately.

Update: 04.07.2005.

Re: House of Leaves

I've had several people ask if I'm going to continue HoL, and it needs to be known that I have absolutely no plans to abandon it. It may take a while for the chapters to get out, because while I'm not a slow writer once I get the story in my head, I am slow on getting it done on paper. Chapter five is started, and I have the outline finished, and I am about 45-percent into it, but it may still take a while before I get it finished. So, don't worry, it will be continued, but maybe not as fast as I'd like.
