Author has written 3 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

10 years is a long time. 10 years ago I made an account on this site after a friend pointed it out to me. I had always enjoyed writing and had a pretty active imagination so it only made sense to really start putting it to use and continue the stories of shows I loved as a kid. I was only 15 when I started doing this, now I'm 25 and it's been about 7 years since I last updated. It's kind of disappointing that it I let this fall by the wayside. Especially since some of my last updates make mention that I'd finish the story even if it took me 10 years, well ten years has come and gone and I haven't finished it yet. But someday I know I will, until then if anyone stumbles upon this enjoy what I have up and please feel free to shoot me a private message if you have any question!
