I am Maria Lorenzo. You talkin' smacke? You'll sleep with the fishes, emer efer. Actually I am a delusional and manic depressive teenager whose alter ego is a mob boss woman from either Italy or Russia,name Maria Lorenzo. I used to write Buffy fanfiction, but now I mostly read my friend Jill Valentine's stuff. I Would read TUTU's, but she hasn't written anything is soooo very long. I have completly given up on "Balancing the Scales". Tutu can have it if she would want to finish it. She expressed an intrest in saving that story from me a long time ago.

Um...I like drama and acting, my friends (what would I do without them?), writing, short stories, reading, history, speech and debate, singing, nerdy things, and pineapples. I don't like it when my boyfriend ignores me, I don't like cottage cheese, weather that's too hot, being forced to grow up into a mature adult, running into hard things and bruising my legs, making an ass of myself, and being hit in the face by Gerry.

My favorite quote is "God must love stupid people, because he made so many of them." I want to move away to the British Isles. Anywhere. Scotland, Ireland, Wales, Britain, the isle of sky, the isle of man, anywhere, as long as I can move there.

I was born in a cathedral while the rings of Saturn was high in the sky. I had a toe, an eyeball, and also a hip. My parents were very anxious. Rhinos began to howl when I was born and librarians in London were pitiful to discover that the letter ­­­t now came after h in the dictionary. Teachers at the school I attended were often astounded by my fascination-a word which here means fixation -for all things round. In Spring of last year it was reported that I was killed in a tornado. This is, of course, true. My current whereabouts-which here means about where you are-are certainly not Westminster Abby.

I have decided I want to marry either: 1) a hotsoccer-playing latin 2) a liberal cowboy or 3) a hot Russian named Dimitri. (he doesn't actually have to be Russian ethnically, just speak Russian and be from Russia. As long as he has an accent and speaks english and is hot, I'm good)

Or, I could just find and marry a hot liberal latin Russian-accented soccer-playing cowboy named Dimitri. However, something tells me those aren't dime-a-dozen.
