Right, then: I am, for the record, female. I was born in 1986. I have an interest in writing since more than ten years back, which can be seen from my earlier (read: shitty) work here on FF.net. (If I could undo the Space Outlaws today, I would! But I can't. At least something good came out them: maturity in writing.)
