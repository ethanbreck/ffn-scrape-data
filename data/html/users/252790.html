Well...
My name's Adenara Yatman. Well, not really.
That would be a pen name... but you aren't getting my real name, so stop while you're ahead.

I am obsessed with Harry Potter... and Lord of the Rings. I am currently working on getting my friends addicted to fanfiction. Let's see... two down, not many left to go.
I figure skate, which is pretty much what I live for, and I take piano lessons and play clarinet in the band at school. I also play field hockey in the spring. Played softball last year, then quit. Big, scary ball. *Shudders*.

And I'm Canadian. A very patriotic Canadian... who loves to make cracks about politics and Americans. No offence, I'm also one myself. An American. Not a politician. *Shudders once again.

Anyhow, enough about me. *Pets her beaver whom is seated on her lap*

Don't read 'Where Water Meets Land' expecting an update, because, unless I have a sudden urge to continue it, it will never be finished. Especially since it is now an AU with OotP out... I think... wait. Maybe it itsn't.
But still, though I am slowly writing chapter two, it most likely never will be completed. In fact, I'd bet quite a bit of money on it.

And, for your information, 'starkers' means naked. Not mad, crazy, insane, or anything else. It means naked.

You'd be surprised at how many people use 'starkers' in the wrong context. Makes me laugh though, and goodness knows I could use a laugh every now and then.
