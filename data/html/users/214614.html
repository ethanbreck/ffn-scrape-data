Author has written 2 stories for Rurouni Kenshin.

What can I say about myself really? I love to do a great deal of things anything from writing and acting to sailing and scuba diving. I have a passion for writing, but I can never seem to come up with any decent material... I am currently in college for International Management. If I was creative enough, I would love to be a full-time writer. Instead I am currently in the process of figuring out how to start my own publishing company, which is why I am so behind on updating my story. Hopefully by the time I graduate this December, I will have something started.

My obsessive hobbies seem to include the study of Japanese and Chinese history, though for China it is more specifically the study of the Three Kingdoms era - and no, that research does not include playing lots of Dynasty Warriors. Though if you want a quick introduction to the history, it is a fun game.

I did an intensive language study in Japan in the fall of 2006, one of the best experiences of my life and I recommend to anyone who is interested in studying abroad that they DO IT.

One thing I believe though, is that no matter how much I study I will always learn something new from others. So if you have an itching to share some information with me, by all means, please email me. I would love to hear about what you've learned and experienced. Or if you feel that I have said something that is misinformed, you are welcomed to correct me.

I have some of my photography up from my trip to Japan on DeviantArt. If you're interested, check it out:

I suppose I can give some basic stats about moi:
Age: 21
Gender: Female
Location: New Mexico

"Dance, as if no one was watching; sing, as if no one was listening; and live each day as if it were your last." - Irish saying

Finally, on a last note: if I post anyting, please bear with my writing style. It may seem choppy at times or even outrageously bad, but it's because I am still experimenting. There is usually method behind my madness.

Thank you!
