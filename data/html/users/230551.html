Author has written 29 stories for X-Files, CSI, Young and the Restless, and Ripper Street.

Greetings! I'm Juliet, sometimes known as Susie, formerly of the Graveyard Shift. After a nearly 10 year hiatus (for a variety of legitimate and made-up reasons), I returned to fandom and fanfiction in 2014 and haven't looked back since. Currently I'm writing (and RPing) exclusively in the Ripper Street fandom and fairly exclusively about Susan and Jackson though other muses in other fandoms may eventually creep in. I do my best to write regularly, though I have one more than full time job and one part job along with other freelance work and projects. So while there may be periods where I'm a little quiet, I have no intention of disappearing completely again.

Blogging about fandom, writing and a lot of Ripper Street at madameinsecte.tumblr.com
RPing as Susan Hart from Ripper Street at onlyonecharm.tumblr.com
All Ripper Street fics are cross posted to AO3.
