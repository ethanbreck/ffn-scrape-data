Heya,

waves

How're you all? I'm good thanks for asking. Feeling a little mental today but hey! I'm tired. I'm allowed to be mental... I think.

OK. So I pretty much just read fic and post reviews. I'm debating posting my own fic but a. Its not very good (me thinks) and b. There's not much of it. I prefer to write my own orig stories with my own orig characters so I generally don't write fanfic. Also I am never happy with the way the characters react when I play with them. They always say things that make me go "huh? that sounds wrong" but I can never decide why. So I simply don't write them.

I mainly read Buffy and Angel fic because, well, I'm supposedly obsessed- at least I'm told I am. Now just because I come up with theories all the time and can quite easily hold hours long conversations picking the characters, or the plots, or the philosophy/mythology of the show to peices... does that really make me obsessed? Oh well. On Angel I don't really have a favourite pairing (I have a least favourite, which would be Connor/Cordy) but on Buffy I have several: Wills/Faithy, Spike/Xan, Dawn (not with anyone in partic, I just like Dawnie) and there's a fic at the moment that I like that has Willow/Wesley elements. I generally don't like Buffy (as in the character) centric fics cos, well, she pouts too much and whines too much and generally annoys me.

Other shows I read the fic of are: Dark Angel (I miss Dark Angel); Farscape (How could they have done that to me!?- for explaination see last episode); Smallville (seem to like Chlex); and Star Trek (was raised on Star Trek).

I think I've rambled enough for one night (grin) so I'm gonna leave this at that.

Bye bye,

S.
