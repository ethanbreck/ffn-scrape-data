Never Tomorrow -

Memories, they wash my mind
Like the frozen rain.
I'm numb here but I can't forget the pain.
Death was yesterday
And somewhere I have ever seen.
So never mind tomorrow, boy,
Tomorrow's never been.
~ River of Styx, High Holy Days

Other Names: Lindsay, Cosmos, Figgy
Age: 16
Interests: Writing, Reading, Anime, Manga, Video Games, Mythology, Movies, Web Design, Drawing.
Currently: Grade 12 in High School
Future Goals: Software Programming, possibly Medicine, possibly Law. Undecided, really.

Additionally:
I greatly enjoy writing, both fanfiction and original stories. I love to read, and I really love web design. I'm also a big movie and video game fanatic... And don't even get me started on anime and manga.

Anyway, what kind of fanfiction are you going to see from me? It will probably mostly be serious, though every once and a while I get a bit of a funny idea that I have to write about. When it comes to subject... I think you're mostly going to see video game and anime fanfiction from me, but I do write some book fanfiction and movie fanfiction, so you never know.

Well... I've been pretty undedicated when it comes to any type of writing as of late. I'm hoping that revising my entire persona here on FF.Net will help it somewhat, and I'll be able to update my fanfiction again. Hopefully I can get back into the swing of things and stop leaving my fics for years at a time _;;

By the way, in case you're looking for them... I'm going to delete a few of my stories that I have no intention on finishing. There are quite a few that sort of just came to me, with no ideas on how they would end. Now they're going to end in a cloud of smoke XD

Well, thank you for reading my long and pointlessly boring profile. Hopefully it hasn't deterred you from my writing at all... _
