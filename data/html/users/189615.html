Hello All,

As you can see, I joined this site quite some time ago. It was with the intention of publishing the 10 or so Charmed fan fictions I had written. Somehow, life must have gotten way, because I never did publish a single thing back in 2002.

Covert Affairs has reawakened my creativity and I have started writing again. I almost forgot how much fun it is. Some readers might find my writing style a bit different. The only excuse I can come up with is that my first job out of college was in radio writing news, PSAs and commercials. The style required was quick and to the point in a conversational way. That style is evident in my stories. I also try to make my stories seem like an episode of the show, most if not all, will be multi-chapter stories.

Other stats:

Current Residence: Wisconsin, home of the Super Bowl Champion Green Bay Packers!! There's just something magical about having Lambeau Field in your backyard (okay, not exactly, but you get the point).

Past Lives: I am an Air Force brat, I grew up all over the country: California, Texas, Arkansas, Florida, Michigan, Oklahoma and now Wisconsin.

Thanks for stopping by and I hope you enjoy my work.
