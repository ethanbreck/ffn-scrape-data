Author has written 6 stories for Inuyasha, and Trigun.

I'm in Southern California. Embarrassed about writing fanficiton. (none of my friends know, so shhh!) I'm bulletproof. Criticisms and flames are welcome. My fics don't exceed a PG-13 rating in content, but I put them at "M" FOR STANDARD ANIME ACTION, just to play it safe. I won't write lemons. It's a decision I made before it made me, and I believe my writing is better off for it.

I wrote the trilogy after only 3 seasons had aired in Japan. Lots of stuff hadn't happened back then, so it left plenty of room to speculate.Don't forget to click on my website to see the fanart submitted for these fics. I was really lucky. The artists rock.

1.BESO - fluff city, Inuyasha/Kagome
2.SOLEDAD - a bonafide love story, Sango/Sesshoumaru
3. VENGANZA - love/hate story Kouga/Kagura

Links: DBZ bio Blog (my yahoo website died on me, so go buy the Trigun manga if you get a chance. Good stuff.)
