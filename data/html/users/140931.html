You know, I just realised, I've never uploaded any of my "real" writing. :S By that, I mean not all the morose stuff you see below you. Yes, I enjoy writing the odd depressing poem, and I relished writing something totally unlike me like "Me, myself, and the guy in the corner", but I am first and foremost a comic writer. Anyone who knows me - Truly, Loopylass, Dally - will tell you that I'm not half as depressing, or depressed, as I sound. I'm barmy really. :)
But none of you know that! And bizarrely, the only pieces of comic literature I have stored on my computer at the moment are the opening chapters of two novels, 'Attatchments' and 'Heaven Inc.' - and neither of them are particularly good. -big sigh-
So, as from now on, I won't upload any more depressing stuff, okay? Well, maybe one or two pieces if I have a bad day. But I'm going to work on my comic side instead, and upload some of that stuff. :)
As if you didn't already know that, that means 'One Step Closer' is now officially DEAD. It was never alive anyway.
Have a good one,
