Author has written 13 stories for Legend of Zelda, Fire Emblem, Voltron: Legendary Defender, and Super Smash Brothers.

Hey guys! I'm experiencing a mild resurgence in my urge to write fanfic, which greases the wheel for some of my other creative endeavors. I'm a big fan of familial drama and relationship drama, especially the latter, and that probably shows in nearly all of my work. I have a special affection for action scenes, though writing them is difficult for me.

I'm a big fan of The Legend of Zelda, Fire Emblem, Mass Effect, The Witcher, Metroid, various Final Fantasies, Ogre Battle, the Mother/EarthBound series, Smash Bros., and too many other game properties to list. Anime's also high up on the interest list but I haven't been keeping up with more recent stuff (outside of My Love Story!) so you may not see much of that out of me.

If you like my work and want to contact me for any reason, please feel free to drop me a line. I'm open to comments, criticism, commissions, suggestions, or just a nice friendly conversation about what informs the way I write fic. I always appreciate hearing from other creatives or from people who like to read what I write, so don't be shy; I'd love to hear from you.

You can also find me elsewhere:

tumblr: theloudguy

AO3: TheLoudGuy
