For those wondering you should probably consider The Price of Redemption as dead. Generally I say to myself "Never say never" and that I might, might, revisit it, but its looking less likely as the months roll by. I apologise for this. I am just not interested in Bleach or in YoruSoi any more. Speaking of fics that I am going to finish, Kill the Lights has not been abandoned. I know that my updates are slow as fuck but I do have every intention of finishing it.

If it feels like I am taking far too long to update then please feel free to drop me a PM and tell me so. Sometimes I need a friendly kick up the bum.
