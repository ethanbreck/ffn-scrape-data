Once upon a time, there was me.. Don't really know what to say. I'm a girl totally obsessed with Sephiroth. If someone ever makes the mistake of asking me why I like him, I'll never stop talking. He's walking perfection!! Anyway, I'm also a hopeless romantic, and I firmly believe in Aer/Seph pairing. That's why I begun writing 'Pieces of Us'.. My main inspiration in writing were the lovely works of other fanfic writers!! If you have any questions, feel free to e-mail me. I never bite strangers. *grins*

And about the stories.. My main priority right now is 'Pieces of Us', though I'm also working on a sideproject I affectionately call 'The Devil You Know'. I've only posted two chapters so far, but I've got big plans for it once I finish 'PoU' first. Let me know what you think of my stories (I treasure all opinions!) and check out my Fav lists for better quality fiction! ^_^

In addition, I've put up the first chapter + a prologue to a story based on the Matrix, called "Been Here Before". It's not that good but hey, at least it's an update. In my book, that's good in the big way. ^_^
