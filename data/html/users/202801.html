Author has written 4 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Threads of Fate, Resident Evil, and Danny Phantom.

So it's been a long time since I've been on FF.net, and my profile was horribly outdated. At the same time, I'm too lazy to do another. : )

In regards to my stories: As you might already know, My ToF and RE stories have been discontinued. Why? I didn't like where they were going, they were just for my entertainment to write, and I started them when I was in, like, fifth grade or whatever. As for Spoiled Yet Selfless, it's in the midst of being rewritten. That, too, has been done over the years, there are plenty of spelling mistakes, and once again, I didn't like where it was going. But this one I won't throw out.

I am still writing, though at a slower pace, but I won't be posting anything until I actually finish it. I just don't see the point of adding another unfinished story to fanfiction.net. I have a couple Teen Titans ones, and Iron Man one, a Star Ocean one, an Oblivion one, a Dragon Age Origin one, a Mass Effect one... All in varying levels of connectedness. And Spoiled Yet Selfless is being heavily rewritten still (that one I will never give up on!) and I still like my Danny Phantom one.

Even though I'm not posting anymore, I'm still active on ff.net, and anyone is welcome to message me. I am always happy to discuss stories, mine or others :)
