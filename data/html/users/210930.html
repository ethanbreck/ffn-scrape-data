Author has written 22 stories for Legend of Zelda, SpongeBob SquarePants, Assassin's Creed, Breaking Bad, and Witcher.

What is there to say about me? I'm addicted to stories. Be they movies, books, shows, video games, or a new friend opening up to me about why they moved to this town, I love a good story just about more than anything in the world. I like to hear them, and I like to tell them.

I am, admittedly, still into fanfiction at a much later age than most. It's not something I can help, so I never tried too hard to shed it. When I get into a new fandom, new "what-if" scenarios are almost guaranteed to flood my head and demand to come out. Besides that, I enjoy the challenge of doing justice to a pre-existing universe and/or characters. My approach to fanfiction is sort of like being an impression artist, or being in a tribute band. The more people tell me "That sounds exactly like them!" the more I'm going to keep at it.

On the flip side, since I am older than most fanfic authors, I also have a crap-ton more adulting to do. I'm notoriously slow to finish multi-chapter sized projects, and I am even more notorious for losing interest in them when I pick up a new fandom. I'm decent about tossing up a one-shot here and there, but many many apologies to all those followers I've left hanging on chapter stories in the past.

The ideas I have kicking around in my head right now are mostly for Borderlands, Star Wars, and Legend of Zelda. Stay tuned if that sounds like your thing!
