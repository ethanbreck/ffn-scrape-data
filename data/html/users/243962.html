Hello, there, everyone! I guess you know that I'm Gwen, strictly from the fact that it's spread above this now (look up!). I mean, it's there at least twice! Really, your laziness just won't be tolerated anymore! But then again, I've recently become known as the Great Sarcasmo, not to mention the older aliases.

Oh, joy, Sweet Sixteen has it. And, no, I didn't get the Jag.

I mainly do the whole Harry Potter fanfiction thing, but I've been known to dip into other areas, such as the Princess Diaries and a few video games.

Okay, my ONE poem I've posted so far, woo hoo!
