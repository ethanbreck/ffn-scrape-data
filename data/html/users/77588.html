I have been an avid fan, reader, and writer of fanfiction since I first discovered it back in 1998. When I first discovered what the word 'fanfiction' meant, it was like opening a wide, light-filled door for me :3

I was captivated. I was entranced. I would spend literally hours and hours during the day, after school, on the weekdays, the weeknights, during the nighttime, any time really to read and devour fanfiction XD

It was my window into a whole new bright and wonderful world, and to this day I still love it with everything that I am, and still love to read and write it.

All of my stories are yuri (girl x girl) =3

This is my favourite type of story, and I truly love and enjoy reading and writing stories that focus on that. It just fills my heart with joy to read yuri stories. I hope that you all will take the journey with me, and enjoy the stories that I write for you =3
