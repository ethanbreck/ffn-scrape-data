Hey,

I'm the author formerly known as Sparkles the Cat and I tend to be a huge slacker in the update's department. I haven't actually uploaded any new fics since '02, but my love of writing has finally decided to show it's head once again. I'm a sociology major in a small Florida college and I'm going to be graduating in a little over a year, so most of my free time is used to lounge around and read. I try to stick as closely to the what really happened in a series as possible, so feel free to tell me if I mess up a few facts.

A Second Chance is just a one shot, mostly because I lost a lot of interest in doing it. But if the demand is great enough, I may just be inspired for another chapter or two.

Stiff Competition is a work in progress, and I'll be getting updates out as soon as possible. This was just an idea that I've been toying around with for a while now and just finally had the time and energy to write. Updates aren't going to be very frequent, finals are just around the corner. I know a lot of people might not like the amount of time that's passed since the end of the series, but you all can just suck it.

All comments and reviews are welcome. Just try not to make them too harsh, kay?
