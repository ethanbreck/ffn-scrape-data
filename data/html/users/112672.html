IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT!

After sixteen years on this site, I'm sorry to announce that I will be leaving FFnet.

This decision doesn't come easily or lightly, and has been something I've been considering for about a year.

The fact is that, while the majority of the interactions that I've had with readers and reviewers have been overwhelmingly positive, as the years have gone on, I've received more and more negative, harassing or just plain unpleasant messages. From language police to self-important bullies masquerading as content vigilantes, I've gotten more irritating messages from this site than any other.

Unfortunately, because of how FFnet is set up, when I receive such harassment, there's not really any recourse I can take, either by blocking the users giving me trouble or reporting them to admins.

I have a reasonably thick skin and can handle such interactions as just part of what one can expect when doing anything on the internet. I've been here for sixteen years and on the internet in general for much longer. I know what to expect and have plenty of practice at it. That being said, I write fanfic for pleasure, as an outlet and to enjoy the company of other fans and share ideas with them. Writing fic at all is an obligation that no one holds me to but myself, and if I feel uncomfortable or frustrated with any aspect of it, there is nothing holding me to what is creating that frustration.

FFnet has gone through many, many changes over the last decade and a half, and I've ridden through them all. I've been here for more than half my life, for longer than a good number of my readers have even been alive, and had expected to remain here until the day came when, for whatever reason, I left the internet or fanfic writing entirely. Unfortunately, that's not how things have turned out.

WHAT'S GOING TO BE HAPPENING TO THIS ACCOUNT.

My two continuing stories, Haunted and The Ironmonger's Heart will continue to update until they are completed. I plan to finish Haunted and then move on to Ironmonger, and hopefully they will both be finished before too long.

Beyond those two exceptions, no new works will be appearing on this account. This includes any new installments into my two series, Loki's Brood and Pins & Needles.

This does not mean I am disappearing from the internet entirely.

For anyone wishing to continue to follow my works, read new works and see what I'm up to, I'll still be active on Archive of Our Own under the name Raven_Ehtar. Please go there if you want to see any of my new works.

This account, while becoming inactive, will not be disappearing. It will remain up as an archive for anyone who wants to come back and read here rather than migrating over to a new site, and I'll be checking once in a while to make sure everything still works. I will also still be receiving alerts for reviews and messages from this site, and will continue to respond to them as I always have. The only change is that I won't be adding to what is already here.

To all my readers, especially to any who have managed to ride through the years as I have, thank you all so much. Thank you for taking the time to read what I have written, for any kind words you've given to me, for any times you've shared something you've enjoyed with friends. I appreciate every one of you, and I truly regret that my issues with this site have led me to this point.

Thank you all, and I wish you all luck and love in the future.

Herein you will find the sometimes lighthearted, sometimes serious, sometimes funny and sometimes heartbreaking adventures of Tony, Loki and his many children, most notably Fenrir, Hela and Jörmungandr, and of course the extended family of the Avengers team. Sometimes the stories will transition smoothly from one to the next, sometimes there will be huge jumps. Sometimes they will be incredibly long, other times very short. I plan to write scenes that are widespread across the timeline, from the time the children are truly children all the way until they are fully grown. Occasionally I may put in parts that are sandwiched between stories that are already complete and posted, rearranging the continuity - but hopefully never breaking it.

Basically writing these out as a series instead of a very long, multi-chaptered fic is so I can write those parts that interest me without having to spend months fleshing every little piece out. You'll find places where I'm hinting at things we don't see directly, and may not mention again for some time. Most likely I will get back to it, though perhaps not. This, as well as offering me a measure of convenience, is to give the whole thing a greater sense of realism. After all, you never really know everything that's going on in real life, right? ;)

(No worries though, major and plot necessary details I will come back and cover. If anyone has any really anxiety over something, check out all the installments and if it's not covered yet, leave me comment to remind me about it. :3)
