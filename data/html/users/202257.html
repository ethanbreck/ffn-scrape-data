the Ten Commandments of FF.N
1) Thou shall not upload without first using a spellchecker or a beta reader.
2) Thou shall not write incest fics.
3) Thou shall not read Cynthia, Piedmon's Lady's fics.
4) Thou shall not be a tattle tale. The owners of FF.N shall take care of abuses.
5) Thou shall not flame. Flamers shall be smote. if you don't like it, don't be an ass.
6) Thou shall post pairings in the opening of a story so we don't waste our time.
7) Thou shall not pair up main characters with your alternate egos. Mary Sues must die.
8) Thou shall not mix up shounen ai and yaoi, or shoujo ai and yuri. There is a difference.
9-10) Thou shall not fault me for not having 10 yet. they will come.
