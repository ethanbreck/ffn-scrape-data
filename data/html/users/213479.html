Author has written 6 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, and Harry Potter.
Monday, November 22 '07

Hello! I am an 18 year old college student who used to be a 12 year old fanfiction writer.

As a way to slink away from the information overload that I am constantly prone to, I dabble in writing. This time, my dabbling led me back to my Herminone x Draco stories that I had previously abandoned. After sifting through the garbage that I wrote years ago, I returned to the one story that I did not want to ditch. I then discovered that my old love for the pairing led me not to care too much what the real books did to my lovely blond boy (receding hairline!).

I refused to accept it, and therefore typed a new chapter to what was once my most popular story.

As one of my favorite authors once said, "All the stories are fictions. What matters is which fiction you believe."

Enjoy! Read and review as you like.

Love,

The Reanimated Corpse of The Spangled Pandemonium
