Hello. I used to be known as Yami13. I don't know if anyone remembers me. Though I have been a member here for quite some time, you may notice that I have not posted any stories.

I am an avid reader, but do not deem my writing as anything more than average. I seem to be able to edit others' work while my own remains plain. I am not creative, but if you have an interesting story, I will read from beginning to end without a problem.

I hope to read stories that are fantastic and imaginitive, while helping others learn some tricks to fixing their personal writing style.
