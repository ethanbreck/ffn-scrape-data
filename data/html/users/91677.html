Well, I've decided to re-vamp this bugger...again...mainly due to a combination of irritation, amusement, and boredom.

And the first topic on the chopping block is...non-conformity.

It amuses and irritates me how far people will go to 'non-conform'. Some of the best examples of this can be found on various profile pages across this site. You've all seen the ones that I'm talking about. They generally go something like "99.9 of people go with the crowd. If you're one of the 0.1 who don't, put this on your profile." I mean, "HELLO!!" by placing this statement on your profile you're conforming.

It irritates me greatly.

I do, however, love the variety of random quote that I've found on various profile pages. Probably my favorite one was "I use to have super powers, but my therapist made them go away." This one made me cackle manically.

Well, a bit about me I suppose:

I'm a 23 year old female who lives in the Southern US. I just graduated with a BA in May '08. Thinking about going for a Masters and mentally bemoaning the fact that there's no such thing as a Masters of Fanfic Reading...and I point to my favorites list as evidence that I could pull it off.

I 'discovered' this site in the late '90's and remember with mild fondness how irritated I used to be trying to find the next chapter of a fic that I was reading. Ah, the days before chapters were linked together. (At least a few of you hopefully know what I'm talking about).

Before I was on this site, I was an avid reader at amandaswiftgold.com (I don't even know if this is still around...) and I remember when theria.net was a huge archive of Ronin Warriors fanfic. Which means that yes, Ronin Warriors was my original fandom. (BTW, if you're ever looking for a really good RW fic series, check out Mieren's fics!)

Oh! Before I forget...

Seriyuu's Chosen is based on a challenge from KingOfLoosePages, challenge 2 to be exact.

Anyways, read, enjoy, review, the standard stuff :)
