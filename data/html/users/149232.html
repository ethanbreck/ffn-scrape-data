Quick 2014 update here.

About the Author: I'm in my late twenties, and I live in middle America, on the outskirts of a little town that's kinda near a big city. But not near enough, apparently, because a visiting cousin, describing her current situation, has been quoted as saying "I'm stuck in the middle of nowhere, with my hillbilly cousins, and nothing to do. But we did go swimming in a river yesterday, and it wasn't even polluted!"

I'm quite average, I love country music, classic rock, some oldies, and the soundtracks to musicals (like Chicago, the Buffy musical ep, and Newsies). I love horror, sci-fi, fantasy, and action-adventure movies. People have called me gothic and punk, but I'm not. I just used to dress kind of that way and for some people, the image stuck.

I really love tattoos, and I currently have a bakers' dozen. And as soon as I get the money, I'm going to get many, many more. I have a list.

I'm a Mommy of two beautiful blue-eyed blonde boys who I was once told could pass for a Malfoy.

As I'm sure you know, I'm nearing the point of unhealthy obsession with all things Walking Dead, Harry Potter and Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and I absolutly adore Firefly/Serenity, Lord of the Rings, Ranma 1/2, Twilight and Newsies. I also have a few fics about a smattering of other subjects.

I collect quotes and one-liners and can't always remember (or be arsed to look up) who said it first. If I include a direct quote of yours in any of my writings and don't give you credit, it's because I either didn't realize I quoted or I have forgotten where I heard it. Let me know and I'll add in the appropriate disclaimer.

Technically, I am no longer on hiatus, but I just haven't been able to get into the old fics that I have left unfinished since I went onto hiatus. That pretty much means that anything that's not a new fic, and isn't already finished, has probably been abandoned indefinitely.

That's all for now, folks. And here's one last parting shot of questionable wisdom:

An apple a day will keep ANYONE away, if thrown hard enough.

Ciao!

D.S.L.*
