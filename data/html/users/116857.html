Basics

Heystella is a girl, 23 years old, born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area but is currently attending nursing school in Hawaii. She writes for funsies. :)

If I could spend my life traveling the world and drinking tea and listening to jazz music without working or going to school, I’d be a happy camper. Unfortunately, I haven’t built a working robot replica of myself, so my desire to shirk all life responsibilities isn’t in the cards. Yet. Cue evil cackle. I tend to be drawn to things (fandoms, ships) that aren’t quite popular. I’m also weirdly fascinated by death and things that go bump in the night, idk.
