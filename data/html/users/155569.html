Moon Child Fanfiction

I've been giving this careful consideration and all current Moon Child fanfics are to be discontinued.

No feedback whatsoever (good or bad) makes me feel that there is no point and that my time would be better spent on other things. Also most fics in this fandom have been deleted. The main ones that have been deleted are really old ones or the ones that I am no longer going to continue writing.
