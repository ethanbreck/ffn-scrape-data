About Me:

Hello everyone!

Writing horror stories is my specialty...strangely enough since Romance used to be my favorite genre. Horror has taken that place now. I mostly write disturbing Pokemon stories.

Current Stories.-

Maggots of Society: I was tired of all the unoriginal journey stories that I've read over the years and decided to deconstruct the journey genre in its entirety...with a VERY dark turn. It's entirely up to you, reader, if you think I have failed or not in my quest.

Welcome to the Internet: I very much like this story. I wrote it for the Tragedy Contest in SPPF, and I'm overall pleased with the result. An upsetting parody of trainers who don't get their Pokemon on time.

Contaminated Freedom: One of my proudest works. =)

Two of a Kind: Two of a Kind is up on Serebii...and it's been on hiatus since September! x_x I also have something really important to say who's critical on this story here. Your efforts are appreciated, but this version - which is the original version - is just pure shit. It's boring as hell and the one on serebii is much better. Still, I like constructive crticism since it makes the revised version much better.

Making Two Dreams Come True: Getting two reviews this year from a story who I have abandoned 4 years ago frankly worries me. I love reviews, and they keep me going, but it's just wasted in a story that will never be updated. So a message to you all: Review Two of a Kind or one of my one-shots, not this piece of crap! Thank you.

A Life's Worth: One-shot. My first horror story and I'm quite proud of it. I try to demonstrate a bit the terrors of war with a supernatural feel. Hope it doesn't disturb people too much :)

Within One's Reach: Another horror one-shot (though it has more drama in it)! I try to show how jealousy and hatred can transform one person. Hope you read it! ;)

Blessed Liquid: My entry for a writing contest held at Serebii.net. It had to have a battle between a Sandshrew and a Cacnea and an evolution had to take place. One of my bloodiest works up to date. ;)

Future Works.-

None. :(

Well, that's all guys. Always check for the 'Updated' tag that's above this message, and alongside the sections for updates (everything that's bolded are the new changes). In other words, bold = new. See you.
