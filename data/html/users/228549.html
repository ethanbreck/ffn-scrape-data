It's been two years since I last logged in here...time flies.

But anyway. I am a 15-year old male reader from Turkey, and that's it, just a reader. I don't have any skill at writing, so I just read the amazing work that people have in here.

Among my favourite writers are Tolkien, David Eddings and J.K Rowling. I especially like the second one. Michael Moorcock is pretty good too. I have read the Belgariad and the Malloreon with great pleasure, and I'm reading them again. Also, after a good reread of the Tamuli trilogy, I've concluded that it's the best work David Eddings has done.

Ack, I haven't been visiting here for a MONTH! School is pretty 'terrifying' at the moment...that means I haven't been spending any time on my fics either, but I will...hopefully, hehe, fortunately my stories (if you could call them so) aren't really known at the moment so I don't really have to satisfy anyone's expectations of swift updating :P
