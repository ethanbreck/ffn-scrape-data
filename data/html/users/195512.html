Hey everyone!

I thought I would revamp my bio to make it look a bit more professional, so here's a little bit about me. My name is actually Trina. I use this account to write most of my Harry Potter fanfiction; I use my other account - Tuppence - to write most of my non-Harry Potter fanfiction. I also have an account on fictionalley.org - Midnight Green - for my Harry Potter fanfiction that doesn't come under these titles.

Name: Trina. Pseudonyms I go under are Tuppence, for most of my non-Harry Potter fanfiction and Sweet Darkling, for Harry Potter fanfiction. Midnight Green on fictionalley.org for other Harry Potter fanfiction that doesn't come under the titles here.

Age: Currently 23.

Harry Potter fics in progress:

Snowflakes and tea: Harry Potter. 3 years after Harry finally defeated Voldemort, he faces some other personal demons - starting with Cho. Innocently sitting in a cafe with Hermione, Ron and Ginny, he looks up to realise that Cho is in the doorway, and he wonders what will happen.

Black Memories: Harry Potter. A series of non-chronological chapters, dealing with memories past, present and future of the last generation of Blacks; Bellatrix, Andromeda, Narcissa, Sirius and Regulus. "He looked past the hollowed cheeks and he ignored the thick black hair that could have belonged to other girls.He looked into her eyes – eyes that glittered strangely and seemingly without sanity – and he remembered the cousin he had loved."

Fervescere: Harry Potter. Just like the bubbling foam when chemicals combust in Potions, things boil and effervescence is created when Draco, Pansy and Slytherins are mixed together. A series of non-chronalogical drabbles that are exactly 500 words long. Draco/Pansy; others decided later.

From Before And Beyond The Universe: A series of random, non-chronological one-shots inspired by quotes from my beloved Doctor Who, mostly humourous and always Slytherin. Second quote is "It won't be quiet, it won't be safe and it won't be calm." "She was screaming at him and gods did her throat hurt..."

Just A Hundred Words: A series of random, non-chronological drabbles exactly 100 words long. These will be either solely Draco/Pansy or Slytherin. Inspired by the drabbles created by Sea Isle Witch (find her on livejournal - her fanfics are brilliant). "...she still looked like herself, and, gods, she was still a sight for sore eyes"

A Word Or Two: A series of dialogue only, non-chronological, random fics. Draco/Pansy. Exactly 600 words long.

Puppets in the Shadows: Not all that glitters that is gold...wars may have been won but when some finish, others begin. What starts out as good and rightous can become twisted and bigoted... And this is true of when Voldemort falls as of any other time... What happens if the good guys start greying? First chapter is up.

Lucius and Narcissa the First Year: Dialogue-only, fluffy and ludicrously out of character comedic fanfics, centred on Lucius and Narcissa, as the name suggests, and in the year that Draco first leaves for Hogwarts.

When We Met: Based entirely and inspired entirely by the amazing Bollywood movie, Jab We Met, it's a story that looks at how Padma and George met outside Hogwarts... Want some comedy? Here it is!

Beyond The White Kings Cross. Snape dies but that's never the end of a story. His next chapter begins in a White Kings Cross, where he grumbles about the clichés that make up the (after)world and begins his 'next great adventure'.

"Her words feel permanently branded into his brain, mocking him and taunting him to distraction. It takes him a few more seconds to realise that he couldn’t even imagine Addison’s face anymore. And she hated the fact that she kept getting those tingles. But, fortunately for her sanity, she's found a friend who seems just as twisted and bitter as her, and it's friends that help you survive unbearable hell like college and murders piling up, after all...

And sometimes, you have to screw everything up to really understand and appreciate what you had all along. Guess Leah will have to leave home to realise all this...
