Hello! I'm Ailsa! Feel free to email me and chat away. I'm afraid I've been absent from FF.net for so long, to my own grief, but I hope that you enjoy the stories here. I do come back once in a while! There are such terrific people and authors here and there are plenty of links to them under my Favourite Stories and Favourite Authors.
I write predominantly LOTR fanfiction, with a bit of other stuff thrown in now and then. I've got nothing at all against slash fics, but I'll never be brave enough to write one. The dominant characters in my fics (by faaaar) are Frodo and Sam, but all the Fellowship make an appearance at one time or another!
