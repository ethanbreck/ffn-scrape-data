Who am I?
I've actually been on Fanfiction.net for the past ten years, although most of that time this account has been dead. I've kind of wandered around, read stories and the like, but haven't really seen the need to write again. Until now. I'm making a return to FF.net!
Even though I've "stopped" writing fanfiction for a while, I haven't stopped writing. Some of my real-life writing pieces have even made it into contests. Now, I'd like to take my writing experience back into the fanfiction world.

Typically with fanfiction I'm a bit looser, as I can never own the story itself (with it just being a fan thing; also, I myself own copyrights and know how it works). I may make slight alterations to a story if I end up messing up a fact, but I'm never going to perfect a fanfiction. This is mostly because I spend too much time perfecting my own work for publication. Fanfiction is an outlet, a release, for me in ways that my original writing sometimes cannot be because I have to promote it. Keep that in mind as you review.

My favorite fandoms to review/write for for are Pokemon, Tokyo Mew Mew, Angelic Layer, Digimon, MegaMan (mostly NT Warrior), My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic, this new movie that popped up on my radar called How to Train Your Dragon, and anything Pretty Cure. If your fic is outside that, I can read and review, but I probably won't get most of it. I tend to focus mostly on technical stuff when I review. Each chapter of a story I review gets a rating -- one for technical (did you spell words right, make the story flow), one for story construct (was it original, did it keep me involved, are your characters and story well built), and one for repeat readability (will I come back and read the next chapter?). Each can get a score of ten, making a possible thirty points for each chapter/story. But don't think just because you got a sucky score doesn't mean your story sucks! If you have any questions, you can ask.

I also take requests. Especially now when I'm trying to get writing. I'd love to have a full library here for everyone to read again, and I'd like that as soon as possible, but I also know that I can't handle all of that. I don't really have any favorite pairings, so I will write anything in the aforementioned fandoms -- ANYTHING. The one exception is I won't write requested M fics; I will eventually write M fics, but I'd rather not do everybody's fetishes. That just sounds creepy. Anything else is fair game, but for right now, I'm only taking one-shots. I'm simply not that free to do full fics that people request. And I get them done on my time. Don't request something and say you want it the next day, because it just won't happen. I want to do your fic right.

Eventually up here you should be able to see a bunch of my fanfictions; I've been working on them, and they should be up soon. I hope that soon there will be a lot for you to read here!
