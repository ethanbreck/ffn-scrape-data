Author has written 7 stories for Inuyasha, Star Ocean, Mega Man, and Bleach.

Fanfiction Notice: Do not expect regular updates for these stories. I don't think I have the ability to continuously write stories, so updates will come when they come.

Complete the Circle was the first fanfic that I ever wrote. I cringe when I look back at it now. It's utterly horrible and cliche, not to mention poorly written. Don't read. Please. I only keep it up for those nostalgic people. I know I sometimes go back to find a not-so-well written piece out of nostalgia (and get very irritated when the author decides to remove it).

God's Game is a very long story that draws heavily on Heian Japanese culture, as well as general (and I mean VERY general) Japanese history and myths. It was fun when I was writing it while watching a Japanese drama that reenacted a lot of myths and ghost stories, but now, I'm afraid that inspiration for the story has dried up. I also find aspects of the story very childish now.

Ocean of Hearts The title will probably change once I write another version of this story. I have the general outline, and frankly, it sounds better than the current sad chapter up, so don't read until I've changed this fic a bit.

Protected Wrote it when I was in the Hitsugaya craze, especially before that terrible Bleach movie came out. Now, I'm just waiting for Gin's voice to come back to me.

Zero Purpose really serves no purpose. It's an exercise in writing for me. I was trying to experiment with massive narrative and few dialogues, since one of the main flaws I felt in my writing was too much dialogue. (I usually revert to dialogue when I'm particularly uninspired, yet still forcing myself to write.)
