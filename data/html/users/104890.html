Author has written 7 stories for Greek Mythology, and Misc. Books.

Hello. :) My (internet) name is Squishie and I've been writing fiction for a long time now, but nothing I've been able to publish proudly. I have several projects that are large investments and I thought I'd do something more exciting to get me started first. One of my biggest passions in life has been Greek Mythology. I've studied it, taking many classes in it, read about it and love writing about it. My biggest influence to write this story has been the Percy Jackson series (as I love the idea of the gods existing in the present day world) and my best friend, who also has a strong passion for Greek Mythology.

I wanted to thank everyone who has been commenting on my current (and only) story. It's a huge inspiration to know that other people are reading and actually enjoy it, rather than just me and my nerdy friends. ;) In response to certain comments: the chapters are off because I started with a prologue, instead of the first chapter. So the numbers are off.

I would like to put myself out there for a writing challenge: if anyone would like to send me characters, a challenge plot, an ending or anything to do with Greek Mythology and see if I can pull it off to your satisfaction, let me know! I would love to give it a try. I am all about coming up with new concepts and meeting challenges. I look forward to what you guys can send me!
