Writing has been a passion of mine for quite some time, yet these days I find that I have very little time to work. I am in my mid twenties and will be graduating from college in June of this year with a Bachelor's degree in English, specializing in Elementary Education. My "hope" is to be teaching in a fourth grade classroom next school year. However, writing fanfiction is quite calming and relaxing, so I don't see myself quitting this venture any time soon. My favorite fandoms include Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, The Patriot, Stargate, and the Dragonriders of Pern.
