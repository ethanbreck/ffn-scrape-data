Author has written 56 stories for Andromeda, StarTrek: Enterprise, StarTrek: Voyager, Titanic, Howl's Moving Castle, Stargate: Atlantis, Firefly, and Forever Knight.

I'm a sci-fi fan that's addicted to fanfic. Mix that with a bit of borderline insanity and you get me and the stuff I come up with. On a (slightly) more serious note, I read and write fanfic for a few sci-fi shows, along with some original stuff.

03/01/2019: It's been a very busy couple of years for me! And not much chance of that changing soon. I've been trying to type up short stuff written over a long period of time and get it up online. Mostly Andromeda, so there'll be a bit of a deluge in that category. With any luck I'll get some more, longer fic or fic in other fandoms written up and posted at some point soon, but... I wouldn't recommend holding your breath, unfortunately, with as slowly as I write. Thank you all for your patience!
