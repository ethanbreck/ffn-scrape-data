Ok... Let's get this straight... I'm not a Sora fan girl. -waves at rabid Sora fan girls- I came up with this name before I even knew about Kingdom Hearts! I promise!

Why must all my favorite characters die? I think I'm cursed... I miss Sirius...

FF8 is better than FF7! So there! I mean, does 7 have Irvine or Squall? I don't think so! Especially Irvine since Elfie has Squall now... Tehehe...

I love anime! My favorites include: Fruits Basket, Alice 19th, Full Metal Alchemist, Sorcerous Stabber Orphen, D. N. Angel, Azumanga Daioh, E's Otherwise... and several more
