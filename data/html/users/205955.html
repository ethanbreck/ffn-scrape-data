Hey everyone!

Website: Dreamers by Day
Blog: Pourquoi Pas?!

"Dream Writer Experiences" = my almost-patented one-five chapter stories. You know, in case you were confused.

Alias: Sydney/Vaughn shipper. My thought process is if I can't have Michael Vaughn, then Sydney Bristow should be next in line. Weiss rocks my world too.
Glee: STRONG Finn/Rachel. I cannot stomach that other one.
Harry Potter: Harry/Hermione, canon be damned. Ginny gives me hives, and I think the movies made my affliction worse.
House: While Hugh Laurie is one sexy bastard, I only like watching him. I read Chase/Cameron.
P&P: Darcy/Elizabeth (um, duh); and for some reason, Col. Fitzwilliam and pretty much anybody makes me salivate. I don't examine that one too closely, as I'm usually too busy saving my laptop from drool shock.
The West Wing: Donna/Josh and C.J./Danny, though it's hard to find good WW fic anywhere.

This place and I don't always get along. Whenever I try to upload my Alias fics, the formatting goes all goofy, and if you know me, you know that my formatting is usually very, very important. As a result, I'll only sometimes be posting my Alias fics here; a complete list can be found on SD-1. (My library's called Cloud 47 -- I know, I'm so clever.) Works from other fandoms will always be posted here. You can find my P&P fics at A Happy Assembly and Austen Underground also. All of my fan fics (and PDFs of them!) can be found at my personal website, Dreamers by Day. In conclusion, I'm accessible from a variety of different places. If you would like to toss me a line, hit me up! I'm always willing to talk! Your feedback really does matter! Your constructive criticism and encouragement ALWAYS counts, as I undergo a continual growth process. It's kinda like breathing.

As of 9/9/11: Wow, it's been a while. Couple o' things. I am working on a new Glee project that I wanted to complete before I posted, and it's almost there. I have to finish the prewrite, then I'll type it up and post! The reason why I haven't gotten much done around here is because I'm in the process of moving to France for the year. I'll be teaching English in a small town in the Loire Valley area, and the preparations have taken up most of my life. In fact, I've started a blog about it, and I'd be tickled pink if you read and commented! Here's the link: . I hope to get my Glee project done soon so I can get that monkey off my back and start working on some original stuff, 'cause Momma needs some brand new shoes. Well, at least some money to think about buying brand new shoes.

:D Becky, the Dream Writer 4 Life
