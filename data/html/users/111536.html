I know this might suck, and it does suck and I've sent plenty of irritated messages to Amazon on this issue, but I have no control over it. Sorry guys! I might not respond to personal messages, but Mara Miller is my real name (and I'm not shy about this fact). I will sometimes post fics when I can't help it, but I am moving toward possibly starting my own publishing press. I am very sorry if this upsets any of you, but I can't make a living with writing if I'm not producing something that will get sales.
