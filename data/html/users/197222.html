Joined 04-09-02, id: 197222, Profile Updated: 09-28-11

I wanted to take the time to apologize for disappearing off the face of the earth. I left two unfinished stories and I know I may have disappointed a few fans, I've removed them from the site. I had a lot happen to me, adult life seems to come out of no where. I was married for six months to a high school boyfriend, then divorced, and then I found the most amazing man that I have been happily married to for almost 2 years now. I went to college, graduated, I'm working now, thinking of having children. I'm 21 years old now and life has completely changed for me. I don't watch anime anymore, I have grown out of it, it was a huge part of my childhood and I'll always remember writing on this website. I still write as a hobby but I use my own characters and I'm working on two novels, one is the story Sunshine with a few changes. I hope to someday get them published. I will no longer be writing on this website but I wanted to thank everyone for the support they did give me.

Farewell and thank you again :)
