Just a college graduate trying to make my way in the world.

I've been involved in the world of fanfiction for quite some time now, more so than my own original writing. So far what's posted is what I have to show for it, ignoring the plot bunnies in my head that hop around from time to time. Unfortunately it usually takes me a great deal of time for an idea to brew and finally make its way to actual words, but I try my best.

For those of you who follow, I appreciate your time and your readership. I hope my writing makes it worthwhile.
