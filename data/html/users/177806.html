Author has written 53 stories for Lunar: Silver Star, Ronin Warriors, Chrono Cross, Legend of Zelda, One Piece, Avengers, and Dangan Ronpa Kibou no Gakuen to Zetsubou no Koukousei.

I have been writing fanfiction for many years and posting online since 2000. My current fanfiction interests include the Marvel Cinematic Universe and One Piece, though there may be forthcoming My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic titles as well. Past interests have included the Lunar series of video games, Ronin Warriors, and Chrono Cross (I apologize that 2/3 are damn near impossible to find anymore) and although none of those projects are active, let's agree to never say never. There's a lot of other anime, video games, and television shows that have had my strong interest as well.

I was born July 31, 1986 in New Hampshire, where I've lived for most of my life. I graduated high school in 2005 and pursued a degree in creative writing, which I obtained in May of 2009. Most of my career work has been with temp agencies doing things like data entry (my degree paired with a poor economy at the time of my graduation didn't leave me with a lot of open doors) but I think I'd like to find permanent work in the non-profit sector or otherwise working towards helping those in need. I love attending anime conventions, with my big conventions consisting of Anime Boston and Otakon. Other things I enjoy include food, going to the movies, arts and crafts, board and card games, CBS' The Big Bang Theory, Comedy Central's news programs, ABC's Once Upon a Time, Tumblr, and almost anything on HGTV.

My current major fanfiction project is the Laws of Motion series, a multi-story series set in the One Piece universe. In the series, Sanji and Nami are captured by a rival pirate crew whose captain has been experimenting with Devil Fruits and makes them the next subjects of his research. The series will consist of six stories - three short stories and three multi-parters. The current recommended reading order is "Vanished", A Force Against Inertia"Sought", and the still in-progress Mass x Acceleration. The third short and multi-parter won't be out until after Mass x Acceleration is completed.

In the early months of 2013 I was diagnosed with Stage 2 Hodgkin's Lymphoma. Since then I have entered remission and will be considered "cured" if I don't have a recurrence before May of 2018. I was out of work for the vast majority of the year due to my illness but recently got back on track. My current position is set to end before the end of the year, which is a tough time to be a temp, but hopefully things will pick up in January and I won't be out of a job for too long :)

All stories whose titles end with "by Soulblighter2" were authored by a fanfiction author who went by soulblighter2. SB2 passed away in late 2002 and was a major loss to the Chrono Cross fanficton community. Communications between myself and the person in charge of getting his online affairs in order resulted in the following: the removal of all stories from his account (despite the account itself not being deleted) and the storing of his writing on my account.
