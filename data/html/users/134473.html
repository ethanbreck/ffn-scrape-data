Hiya!

Have no idea why anybody would be interested in my profile as am hardly on ff.net anymore but, meh, who knows? :) If anybody is interested in chatting, you're far more likely to find me on lj (livejournal) as ferrous_wheeler.

Have been reading fanfiction for a long time, am a frustrated writer and truly enjoy editing. Unfortunately don't get as much time as I'd like to do any of that due to pesky needs like making money to pay bills.

My reading tastes are varied. Fantasy, AU (love crossovers!), RPF, gen, slash, whatever, though I am picky and certain genres/pairings are no-go zones. Basically, with exceptions, I like my stories to have a happy ending, and it's an added bonus if there's a heaping helping of h/c (hurt/comfort) along the way.

I'm sure that there's more that I should put here, but I'm not a big fan of spilling my life onto the screen. So, if you want to find me, I'm on lj :)

Favourite shows: Supernatural, NCIS (pre-Bishop), Stargate Atlantis, Stargate SG1, Agent Carter, The Big Bang Theory, Emergency.
