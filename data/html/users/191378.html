Update 9-02-2013: Updated Blue Child list and few minor edits

Well my friends think I should try posting here (imagine my surprise I apparently did sign up years ago but I complete forgot about it)

I'm in multiple fandoms *cough*addict*cough* but I've only written in Torchwood, they are all Janto make no mistake, despite the characters.

As my homepage is not showing up you can find my archive here

I am also on AO3, my username is cyberdigi (attempt at link here)

As it seems like at least some people here like my stories I'll be posting my stories here as well. Now before I get to the nitty gritty info about the stories, there are some things you should know. I am a SLOW writer, well lazy might be a better word, regardless, there are can be long periods between new stories. Now as I'm reposting here that means you guys won't see this for awhile, however once their all up ;)

I DO NOT LIKE GWEN. There I said it. In Butterfly I try to be fair with her, however I feel in the show she gets away with things she should not, so I try to treat her as she should be. Blue Child on the other hand, she's off her rocker, but there's a REASON, really. The concept for the universe requires her dreaming the whole thing.

I am still going to continue to post to LJ first but I will get everything up here as well.

Now for my stories:

I have 2 main series, Blue Child and Butterfly Series.

Butterfly Series is my post COE RTD you are an idiot I'm fixing this mess, solution. It follows through just after Adam through COE, with the concept Ianto made a choice that made him immortal and that one choice changes everything (get it Butterfly Effect? ;) )

As I've been writing this one pretty much chronologically it'll be all posted in one story called Butterfly Series following My Jack.

Now Blue Child, my baby. It started as a tickle when someone on an anti-gwen livejournal comm posted about the idea Gwen dreamed the whole thing. Then the Long Live Ianto comm had a challenge that was basically the same thing and a tickle became a biting bunny and didn't leave. It's become my universe for challenges :)

I haven't got very far in the series as far as covering the actual show, but I plan for more :D

Now this one I'm NOT writing chronologically for the most part so each installment will be posted in a separate story below are the current installments (in chronological order):

(as far as reposting, I've opted with the exception of Delusional Obsession [which is the premise of the universe, to repost in chronological order...mostly)

Blue Child and Butterfly are connected together, My Jack (for the most part) takes place in both universes, the TARDIS just does different things, Dreams of What Could Have Been helps explain this.

Now some stories are not appropriate for this site, and these are part of a couple mini series, My solution will be an edited version here and giving you a link to the unedited version (ie my archive above). Now on that note, my livejournal comm is set as explicit adult content which means if you do not have an account you have to confirm your age, but I'll repeat PLEASE DO NOT GO THERE IF YOU ARE NOT OF AGE. (and I will REPEAT this when these stories are posted)

I have a couple stories that are not in either of this series and I'll be posting them as well, but with very few exceptions I just write in my main series.

Well I do believe that's everything, please enjoy the stories :D (and maybe review?)
