Author has written 10 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy X-2, and Final Fantasy VII.

In the process of updating or re-writing older stories that remain unfinished. The current project is "History Never Repeats." It's not a story that was terribly popular or one that got much readership, however I still love the story and I'm resurrecting it because it's the one I just couldn't let go. It's already longer than any of my finished works at about 100k words at roughly the halfway point, so there's a lot invested in it.

I can't make any promises on which additional stories or how often I might update, being that things get busy and complicated. But I'll try my best to finish at least a few. On some of them, I've totally lost my place and am not quite sure anymore where I was going with them. Others are finished and stand alone as they are, though may be in need of some editing. I may or may not get to it.

I'm also updating a FF VII story called Seraphim, set post Dirge of Cerberus. I found a few hand written notes a while back and decided to resurrect it. Being that I don't have any actual old chapters completed, from chapter one on, everything is new, based on those old notes. I don't remember exactly how it played out, but I have a pretty solid idea of the big picture.

I'm also considering an update to "Strange Blood," which was supposed to be half murder mystery, half naughty vampire themed Selfer. I really love these two together, especially the versions of them I wrote in the opening chapter, but I really don't remember where I was going with it. All chapters and notes I may have had are long gone. But, I'm thinking about it, as I've had multiple requests for an update.

For those asking about "I Hate Everything About You," which I co-wrote with Wench of Diablos (under her account): I don't know if or when there will be an update. Short of re-posting the entire thing on my account, there will not be updates until she decides to come back. While I would love to see the whole thing posted, since it is finished and there are another 15 or so chapters to go, it is her story in the end. I wrote approximately half, but the idea, the settings and the scenario are all hers. I have been in touch, so we will see.
