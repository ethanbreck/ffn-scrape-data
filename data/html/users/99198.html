January 2007

What in heaven's name is S.C. Preclarus Noctis? And how did you come up with it?
It was my horrible attempt in Latin when I was in...8th grade? To be honest, I just used one of those online translation things (that never work, by the way) to generate my name. I'm too lazy to think of a better one, so I'll stick with this lame name for now. S.C. are my initials. Preclarus Noctis is supposed to mean "beautiful by night," but I bet it means "there is a cat in my pants" and I just don't know.

When will you update Misconception?
It will take a while. (as stated in the author's note). Long story short: school is painful. It has taken over my life and my soul...and yes. And I need to work on my writing skills, once again.

While you're waiting, I encourage you to look through my favorite authors/stories. They have so much more to offer! Remember to review them too!

When will you update your other stories?
Never.

Do you need/want a beta?
No. Not really. It's a pet peeve. I just don't like people reading my story before the others. I bet I could use one for my grammatical errors and what not, but I just get very irked by sending my story to a beta. I don't know why. I was just born that way. I'm sorry, Grammar Nazis for ruining your reading. I do proofread my story as best I can though...I bet it's very conceited of me. Or something.

When you read a fanfic, please review; it would mean a lot to me. Do not flame; if you don't like my story, don't review. Only use constructive criticism when you see something that could make the story better (grammatical, spelling, etc.)
