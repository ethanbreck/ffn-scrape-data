Konnichiwa minna-san! Thank you for visiting BluePenguin's humble abode here on ff.net. ~bows~. Since you went through all the trouble to find out something about yours truly, it would not be nice to leave you empty-handed, ne? (n_n)

About me:

BluePenguin is a bored college sophomore who lives in the Philippines. She is older than most people in the fandom she is currently in, but still is a child at heart. She enjoys reading books, stories and fanfiction, instead of doing homework and studying. She is a full-time procrastinator (well, old habits die hard) and a master crammer. She hates evil professors and hellish exams and undecipherable source codes and programs. She is both a nocturnal and a morning person.

BluePenguin is an avid anime/manga fan. She prefers reading shounen manga than shoujo manga, because sometimes shoujo manga are just too mushy and too cliche (although they have wonderful bishies). But one shoujo manga she particularly likes is Sunadokei (so if someone knows where she can download it, please do PM her). She loves writing stories, but her muse always runs away from her just when she is in the prospect of developing an idea. She loves analysing characters and doing introspective stories and she worships genius authors that can do stories like that, because she fails miserably when she tries her hand at it.

BluePenguin is also fascinated by the Japanese culture and the Japanese language and can't wait to take her Japanese language elective some time next year. She hopes to visit Japan someday too.

BluePenguin loves third person. (n_n)

If readers notice some information mistakes or grammar mistakes in BluePenguin's stories, or if they have comments and suggestions on how she can improve her writing, she urges you to PM or email her and point out her mistakes and share your suggestions. She would very much appreciate it. Also, if you just want to talk or ask something, please feel free to PM BluePenguin as well. She loves making new friends. (n_n).

Anime/Manga that BluePenguin has already seen/read: (this list is not complete though)

Tennis no Oujisama. Rurouni Kenshin. Ouran High School Host Club. Slam Dunk. Death Note. Perfect Girl Evolution. YuYu Hakusho. Bleach. Card Captor Sakura. Shaman King. Sunadokei. Naruto. Beyblade. Gundam Wing. Flame of Recca. Gensoumaden Saiyuki. Monster. Inuyasha... and others which BluPenguin will add on later...

BluePenguin is very much addicted to Tennis no Oujisama at the moment. She is also a Tenimyu fangirl and adores the Rikkai Myu actors to bits (she used to fangirl over the Hyoutei boys in Hyoutei Myu, but has had a change of heart when she saw Rikkai Myu). She worships the Rikkai Daigaku Fuzoku Regulars in the manga, but doesn't like their anime counterparts very much, because she thinks the Rikkai boys were butchered in the anime (they are so much cooler in the manga.. so for all Rikkai fans who have not yet read the manga, you're missing on a lot!).

BluePenguin reads all sorts of pairings in the PoT fandom but she adores SanaYu pairing the most because, seriously, those two are just as good as married. But she equally loves all Rikkai pairings.

Pairings Preferences: BluePenguin adores Rikkai pairings (she can't get enough of those boys)... Troika tops her list.. But anything Rikkai makes her a happy and giddy girl... Aside from that, BluePenguin has also come to appreciate odd pairings such as Jackal/Akazawa and Niou/Tezu... (She blames I-chan for this! xD)

BluePenguin hopes that you will enjoy reading her stories. She thanks you for dropping by! (n_n)...

An observation on the Rikkai Troika's name references based on the manga:

-- Yukimura sometimes calls Yanagi and Sanada by their first names.

-- Yanagi always calls Yukimura and Sanada by their first names.

-- Sanada always calls Yanagi by his first name but never calls Yukimura by his first name.

The Troika are such interesting personalities.
