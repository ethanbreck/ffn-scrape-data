Well, it's been about eight years since I've updated this, so it's about time, I did, isin't it?

Probably best to start with something about myself, I suppose:

I'm 21, barely 5'2", and my hair colour is natually hovering somewhere between dark blond and light brown.

What? That not enough? Alright, I go by Arrin pretty much everywhere on the net, but really, any bastarsization of that nickname will work.

...and yes, I'm a girl.

I enjoy reading, writing, playing video games, and just generally mucking about on the computer.

I don't actually watch much television, the only times I really actively go to watch it, is for House, Stargate, and more recently Sanctuary.

Currently, House is in the forefront of my fanfic-writing mind, and I'm sad to say my Harry Potter fic Marionettus dosen't get nearly enough love as it should.

Favorate pairings:

House MD:

House/Cuddy - They've been dancing around eachother since season one, and the bicker like an old married coupple, just put us out our misery and pair them together already.

Cameron/Chase - It kinda startled me when Cameron jumped Chase for the first time, but hey, it works.

Kutner/Thirteen - This needs to happen! If the writers should ever actually do anything about feedback and comments from fans, let it be about this. Please don't pair her with Foreman. With that rant out of the way, I can really see them together. Kutner's so awkward and sweet, and Thirteen's kinda closed off and rough around the edges, I think Kutner's just what she needs to mellow out and open up a bit.

Cameron/Thirteen: My indulgance pairing, I know it isn't going to happen, but I read the fics anyway. Chase is a optional, but welcome addition to this duo, if only just to have have Cameron say the line: "She followed me home, can we keep her?"

Stargate Atlanits:

Sheppard/Weir: C'mon people, how can you not see that?

Rodney/Keller: I was a bit skeptical over this one, but it's grown on me.

Rodney/Teyla: I was rooting for this one, right up untill I realizes they brought Kenan back with them in the dart. I still hope for this, but I know it's not going to happen anywhere else but in fandom.

Stargate SG-1:

Sam/Daniel: Yeah, more wishfull thinking, but c'mon people, give the geeks some love!

Sam/Jack: It does work, dispite my wishing Jack was Daniel.

Any Kudley fans who are reading this, should join the Kudley Army as soon as you can; help fight the fight against all this Foreteen nonsense.

Also: You should have a look at RemytheReaper and Revengent's fics, not only are they awesome, they're Kudley Army members too.

Well, that sums things up nicely, I think. See you all next update.

~Arrin
