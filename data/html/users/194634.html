Author has written 1 story for Twilight.

I've been on hiatus lately due to school and life but hope to be back to writing very soon.

*UPDATE* 9/22/11- After several months of self- debating and writing, I have decided to put an indefinite hold on "A Time to Change, A Time to Heal"; if there is anyone still reading that poor thing. I started it with very good intentions, but also when I was at a different point in my life. As I have gone through several things lately, I no longer feel a pull to the story or a passion for it. In order to turn out good stories I feel one must be passionate with their plot and I have lost that passion. I may very well come back it one day with lots of revisions, but for now it is going to expire.

I enjoy reading and writing well-written and intriguing fan fiction, especially anything Twilight and more recently, Harry Potter related.

I also don't mind a new character being introduced, as long as there is character development. I also love a good lemon or two. :-)

If you love Draco/Hermione stories then you need to check out, It's All Uncharted by redhead414. This author is absolutely brilliant in her writing and character development.

I recently stumbled upon The Cullen Campaign by belladonna1472 and it's amazing! I normally won't touch an Edward/Bella story, but this one has converted me. It's well-written, witty, smart, and fabulous, please check it out.
