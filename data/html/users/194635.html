Current Works Set in BBC Sherlock Fandom (Sherlock works are mirrored at AO3, under the same user name):

The Minor Fall - First part in the Composing Hallelujah series (trilogy). Dark, AU. Sherlock Holmes is hailed as the world's greatest concert violinist who can't leave off the cocaine. MAJOR WARNINGS: Drug/Alcohol Abuse, Physical/Sexual Abuse, Torture, Suicide/Attempted Suicide, M/M Sex, and other (sometimes extreme) adult themes contained within. Somebody That I Used To Know- Spoilers for both Series 1 & 2 - set Post-The Reichenbach Fall. Sherlock shows back up at 221B. How does John cope? What do they say to each other? And what if the danger isn't over?

Friend - One-shot, spoilers for Series 1, Episode 2: The Blind Banker. Sherlock is very deliberate about his use of the word friend when introducing John to Sebastian. Everyone seems surprised. Internal Sherlock Monologue-ing.

...Do You?- One-shot, spoilers for Series 2, Episode 1: A Scandal in Belgravia. It seems to take John a while to get back to 221B after he hears Sherlock's phone in the abandoned building. Why? My take on what we didn't see while Sherlock contemplated murdering an American.

See It All - Prequel One-Shot. Sherlock Holmes and Sally Donovan have some kind of history – it’s easy to see that when we first see them interacting with each other. My take on what might have happened.
