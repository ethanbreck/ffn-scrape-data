The Bio

If you've been watching me, chances are, you're aware I've not done any updates here in a long, LONG time, instead concentrating on the artistic side of things.

The stories in here that were chaptered, I'm probably not going to update anytime soon. I've found that I work better when doing a single, long piece instead.

My interests have broadened over the course of time. I am about to upload two new pieces in here, that you probably weren't expecting from me. Nevertheless, I've gotten good reviews from them on another site, so up here they go.

To everyone who's read my stuff, thank ye all for the support. Wheither it's digital dimension creatures, mystical mechanical warriors, or miniature technicolor equines. If you enjoy it, that's good enough for me.

If any of you have questions of any sort, feel free to email me at rebound@gosdin.net!
