Author has written 3 stories for Matrix, and Stargate: Atlantis.

Who am I? A scientist and sometimes-writer who plays out her crazy ideas in the world of fiction. I typically read everything, so long as an author can weave a good story. If you can tell a good story, I can lend my ear (eyes?) for a bit.

In regards to Deep Freeze, I am deeply sorry if someone waited all this time for an update on it that never came. I just can't foresee me being able to go back and finish it at this point.

I often receive comments about Prophecy, so let me assure you, yes, I will finish it. I am aware that it has been seven years since it's seen an update, but it is not abandoned. It's a bit of a complicated issue on why I pulled much of the later chapters, and why I've considerably diced up what's still posted. However, it is not dead, and though it's been a while, I will finish it sometime before the next Ice Age. It is a story very near and dear to my heart. It will be finished.

Other than the rest of Prophecy, nothing new will be added to this account. I believe the time has come to jump ship to Archive of Our Own (AO3), so to speak, but don't worry. What's already here will stay here. For that matter, if you have accounts on both sites, I would prefer you follow the link below and check it out over there, as I will be spending less time over here on FFN (which is a shame, because if you look up at my profile, you will see I've been a part of FFN for more than 12 years. I'm an old timer!). But I'll always be around as an avid reader and reviewer! :)

Check out my account on Archive of Our Own, both for the few items you see here, and all the many newer items you won't:
