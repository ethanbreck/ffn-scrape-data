:Introductions:

Might I be so obliged to make your acquaintance? It was only a matter of time before we would meet, of course. I drop in once in awhile between the "real world" and writing to update my work and read others' material. Perhaps you are looking for a good distraction from everyday agenda, a chance to share your own interests regarding X-Men Evolution in particular, or probably stumbled across my profile by mere chance. Whatever the circumstance, I am more than happy to invite you to read my work. Could I interest you in a little Rogue and Remy? Probably a good Jonda to pass the time? Or maybe it is your own work that should reach my attention? Whatever it may be, I hope you believe me when I say Time is not wasted when spent together. Enjoy.

And it was my pleasure to meet you, really.

:Snipets:

I do not enjoy writing essays as much as I like fanfiction. Perhaps it is because I am not fighting for a grade on this site o.O I guess it keeps me imaginative, a place to escape when the world doesn't make sense to me or deserves interpretation.

:Favorite Evo Pairings:

Romy--I've never met someone who could say different

Jonda--This is simply an Evo couple. I mean the Brotherhood to me was obsolete before Wanda became the poster girl for it. I've always imagined their love being the passionate kind because they're both pretty crazy.

Kiotr--There was something between them before the show cut off. In the comics, I read she dumped him for another Peter. So, this is before all that drama and after the drama with Lance.

Jubes/Sam--So many think she should be with Bobby and Sam with Rahne. I don't care: I want them together.

Rolo (Ororo/Logan)--because in Evo, they're two people that don't seem to need anyone. Except each other.

Jott--they're supposed to be together even though I couldn't care for them on an individual basis

I have a blog focused on spreading the glory of the old days and finding inspiration for writing my fic, Back in the Day: The Legend of Logan's Kin. So if you're so inclined, please pop in there!
