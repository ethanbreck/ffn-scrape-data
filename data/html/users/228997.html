My name is maureen. I'm 17 and I live in pennsylvania.

Iam helplessly addicted and obsessed with harry potter. my favorite house is ravenclaw- and my favorite colors are silver, blue and black. i am in love with remus, sirius, charlie, harry, draco, ron...well, just about everyone.

Let's see... i spend a lot of my free time reading, but i like to go on the computer and play games (and read fanfics as well -).

I don't like school this year, but i used to.I like creative writing and french, and I like to learn random useless phrases in other languages. Dites à votre kangoorou ne pas me chatouiller, s'il vous plaît.

A couple of my favorite books are Daughter of the Blood, Heir to the Darkness, Queen of the Shadows- that's the Black Jewels Trilogy- Dreams Made Flesh, Into the Land of the Unicorns, Alanna The First Adventure, Trickster's Queen...um, ok, i'll just list my favorite authors. JK Rowling (obviously), David Eddings, Anne Bishop (she wrote The Black Jewels Trilogy mentioned above), Nora Roberts, Bruce Coville, Darby Conley, and Bill Waterson. Ok- the last two wrote/drew comics- i still like them.

Anything else I need to mention? If I think of it I'll add it later.
