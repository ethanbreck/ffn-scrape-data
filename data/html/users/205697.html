Welcome to my profile and thank you for reading my stories. The success of my stories and plots depend a lot on the readers and their reviews. So I sincerely thank you for reading my story and I greatly appreciate all the reviews.

A little about myself:

I am 26 years old and live in Texas. English is not my first language and has never been my strongest subject in school. But somehow I love writing and also reading fanfictions. I do apologize for any typo and grammatical mistake in my stories. Hopefully they do not affect the story too much.

I try to watch anime whenever I have time. I enjoy watching all of CSI, Numb3rs, and House. I love writing. It is one of my life's greatest passions. My other passion is drawing. I also enjoy playing pc/video games. Right now i am addicted to WoW. I also love art, history and reading fanfictions.

My stories

I try to develop the characters base on their origin personalities, traits, and behaviors. However, I also want to show the aspects of the characters’ lives and persona that the characters have never portrayed on shows. I want to develop the characters in depth, yet at the same time stay true to what is already there. I believe that is the challenge of fanfiction, to create something completely different base on something that have already been molded, sculpted, and finalized. It is an entirely new form of art.

fanfiction is an art of its own, one must truly understand, appreciate, and love the art to enjoy reading and writing it.
