Hm, I don't think I like bios very much... (Cuz I'm dull...)

What useless information can I provide?

I'm female and from the UK, I'm a gamer and that, music and the internet take up most of my time. I'm not really into TV and movies, I get too bored and restless watching them (though not at the cinema). I do like anime, if I can ever be bothered to watch it anymore, as my fics no doubt show.

I don't write too often any more, as life and work gets in the way and while I do try to keep my hand in, lately I seem to get stuck before I finish them...I started working on an original story, the first chapter for which is on fictionpress.

I can't think of anything else at the moment, so I think that'll do...

Oh, I'll put a disclaimer here, since I always forget to write one. Since these are 'fanfics' I obviously don't own any of it, bar my artistic license. Excuse my sarcasm, but I don't see the point with these things...

And of course, many thanks to anyone who reviews - it's always nice to get feedback (faves are of course appreciated too)!

Anyway, I think I'd better shut up before I bore everyone to death.

Ja ne minna!
