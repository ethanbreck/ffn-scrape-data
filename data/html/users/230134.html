A Farewell, & Some Final Notes:

Hello, hello! This is Lethargy again, back with a final hurrah. It's been really fun writing these stories, (short as some of them are, heh), but I've realized over time that I've moved on from these fandoms and well, fanfiction.net in general.

I think I mentioned a year ago that I'd post my final outlines for the stories I planned out. Well, now I'm making good on that idea, and I plan to do it all by the end of Summer 2016. This way, readers will at least get an idea of what I had in mind, and possibly some closure to the stories they were following (as far as I wrote them, anyways). I remember one of my favorite stories, Scorpion's Disciple (Naruto) did the same thing, and while I'm sad that author never wrote out the last chapter, I felt better knowing what they planned to do.

Not that I expect everyone will like the directions I intended for my stories, but giving you guys something seems like the cool thing to do.

On an added note, I'd like to add here that all story ideas are up for adoption! You don't need to message me for permission or ever cite me if you do use them--I just want some of these ideas to get new life, or possibly a facelift, wherever they might go instead. I think that's the best part of fanfiction--building up on possibilities to create something familiar but new.

I'm still dropping in here sporadically to read stuff (there's nice things here in between all the chaos), so chances are if you message me, I'll see it within a year or so, especially if it's during this summer. But yeah, if I do start writing again, it'll be with a different fandom and probably on AO3. Maybe we'll meet again if we're into the same things!

Anyways, that's about it. Happy Reading everyone, and Write On! Or something of that nature.

Best regards,

Lethargy (June 15, 2016)
