Do you write additional/sequels to stories?

Unless planned, no. If it says "Complete", then I won't. You are welcome to add on yourself!

Yup. Email jf_sindel@yahoo.com and be prepared to WAIT. I have a real job that is 40 in an office.

What won't you do?

Pedophilia is against my ethical code. So don't bother with shota/loli. I usually hear it out before deciding to take a request.

Typically, it has to be a very immoral request for me not to do. I don't need exposure, my friend.

AO3, HentaiFoundry, AdultFF, and FF.Net.

Yes. That's my actual penname. This was a very old account before I adopted it.

So you write creepypasta too?

I wrote Kosciusko, Texas. So yes.

What do you want? (Besides money?)

My dream is to find more Chun Li/Bison fanfics. Artwork. So writing me that earns quite hefty praise.
