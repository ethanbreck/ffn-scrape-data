I am the Jew in Gryffindor, more commonly know as Jig, that weird girl, or that person who writes uncommonly good fanfiction about Jewish people with brown hair and blue eyes. I am a Potterholic, Phan, and a METMA member. No flaming please, I do my best to get the facts straight and spell correctly.
