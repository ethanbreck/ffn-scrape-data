The Author

l a masaana

Where did it come from? First note it is all in lowercase letters, as e. e. cummings' name was and is written. There are no periods because they frankly are not very attractive. The l is used because it is very pretty when it is handwritten in cursive uppercase. The a comes from the first letter of my first name. The term masaana (pronounced mass-ay-AHN-uh) comes from one of my favorite book series, Wheel of Time, in which one of the characters is named Mesaana. I just tweeked her name.
