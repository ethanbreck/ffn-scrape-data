I am one of those persons who enjoys reading profiles. So I decided to change mine. So I can read it myself, of course, hee.

about me:
My name on here is Chibi-Cola which is a hiding name, of course. It's also such an old nickname that I'm quite ashamed of it, but for the sake of nostalgia I shall keep using it. I hail from the Netherlands, which means that I am one of those persons whose first language is, INDEED, not English. I'm currently 22 years of age. I aspire to become 23 someday and maybe to finish university (which means I'm MIA a lot because of school). Oh, and to avoid any confusion: I am female.

what i like to read (and maybe even write ~gasp~):
I like loads of things. Not so likely pairs, canon, more populair pairings - you name it, I probably like it. I have no qualms with slash either, but I'm not very much into it, either.

My favourite pairings include: KaoruKenshin (Rurouni Kenshin), Mimato (Digimon), Romy, Rogan (X-Men), TomoyoTouya (Cardcaptor Sakura), NarcissaLucius, FleurBill, LilyJames, SiriusRemus, NevilleLuna and most canon couples (Harry Potter), Malink (Zelda), ÉomerLothiriel (Lord of the Rings).
I'm also very fond of BSSM crossover stories, especially whenver Rei Hino plays a major role (I can't help it, she's my favourite)! So I find myself liking lots of crossover couples as well, I'm quite openminded and like to read whatever people come up with! :)

currently:
Reading: Lots of different stuff! :D
Writing: I'm suffering from no creativity whatsoever, ZEE HORROR! I'm thinking about forcing myself to write a BSSM/HP crossover (probably an oneshot) in the near future.
Reviewing: Whenever I feel the need to.

Much love to LoveKittyPaw, and GunslingerBlok, who are both truly awesome. Check them out whenever you have the time!
