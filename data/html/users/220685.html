Author has written 6 stories for Wheel of Time.

Instead of yammering about myself, I figured I'd put a few tidbits here about my fanfic preferences. If anyone finds any stories that fit these criteria, please let me know in case I missed them!

Favorite 'worlds': Star Wars, Wheel of Time, BTVS/Angel, Avatar (the Last Airbender, not those bloody blue people)

Favorite characters to read about: Anakin, Vader, Logain, Lan, Nynaeve, Mat, Rand, Spike, and Toph

Pairings I like: Anakin/any, Vader/any, Logain/any, Mat/Aludra (or Mat/anyone besides Tuon/Aes Sedai), Rand/Min, Rand/other (anyone but Avi, Elayne, and any other Aes Sedai), Spike/non-canon Buffy characters, Toph/Zuko

Characters I dislike (I love to read fics where they get tormented!): Egwene (aka, Egweenie), Tuon, Elaida, Cadsuane. Let's just be safe and say almost all of the Aes Sedai. I also dislike Buffy when she's in "kick the Spike" mode.

I'm a sucker for happy endings, especially where my favorite characters are concerned. I also really like humorous stories. Angst, however, is not really my cup of tea. If I want to be depressed, I can watch the bloody news...I prefer my entertainment to be more enjoyable. Of course, if the angst is experienced by the characters I hate, it makes me smile. I'm a bit sadistic that way. ;-)
