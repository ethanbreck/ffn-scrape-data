Konnichiwa! I'm Jess the Dragonkeeper, previously known as Sunny Aino. I'm a sixteen-year-old Australian otaku, and a terrible procrastinator...

My favourite TV show is Slayers (although I haven't seen that much of it yet) and I also like Digimon (especially 02 & Tamers), Pokémon, CardCaptors, Sailor Moon and Evangelion. I also recently became hooked on Ranma 1/2, and Princess Mononoke is the best movie EVER! I wanna see it again...
I also love to read! My favourite series (at the moment) are Harry Potter, Discworld, and His Dark Materials by Philip Pullman (haven't read it? Read it. It rocks.)

My friends PrincessSwifty, Chiccachilli and Rocke&Roll are FF.N authors too, so check their stories out too! (They're on my Favourite Authors list.)
