Author has written 12 stories for Dragonlance, Skies of Arcadia, Power Rangers, Valdemar universe, and Dragon Age.

Author is disabled and house-bound, and lives with two other disabled people, six cats, as many finches, a great pyrenese, and an aibo. Some favorite things that Author does not currently write in include Boston Legal, Bubblegum Crisis 2032 and 2040, Claymore (manga), Cromartie High, Dexter, NCIS, Saiyuki (Minekura's version, manga), and Speed Grapher. Author's all-time favorite author is Elizabeth Moon.

Author tries not to take shit too seriously, and is sick and tired of being sick and tired.

“If we are blinded by darkness, we are also blinded by light. When too much light falls on everything, a special terror results.”
-- Annie Dillard

AroWrites.net -- Mostly self-explanatory, isn't it? Contains links to other places to find me, as well as contact information.

Please note: I am incurably ill, and it has a strong effect on my writing output. TAF, IO, and S&S (located on AO3) are all definitely getting more done to them over time. I'd like to work more on RftD eventually as well, but I can't currently handle a television and thus can't re-play Skies of Arcadia.

That said, I don't mind the occasional "I miss this story!" poke. Just know that even if it's been a couple of years, there's good reasons and the story isn't forgotten.
