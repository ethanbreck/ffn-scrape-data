Well, here I am. I've revamped and reposted Choosing Sides. Choosing Sides will be twelve chapters. There are no more original characters, because I think that in reality, Ms. Rowling gave us plenty to work with. There will be a sequel. Actually, this might be a Four Part story, depending on how I break it up.
