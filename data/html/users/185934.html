Hello~

My name is Falke. I am more of a lurker than an author, but I sometimes do get random bouts of inspiration. They don't last long (as reflected in my long list of incomplete/dropped works). But if you follow me, thank you for reading anyway. Series I follow include: Harry Potter, Rurouni Kenshin, Teen Titans, DBZ, Digimon, CCS, ATLA, and several other various categories, though not as avidly. I enjoy anime, though I haven't watched a lot in the past few years. I also like video games, and my current obsession lies with watching Asian dramas (particularly Chinese and Korean ones.) Unfortunately (or fortunately, depending on how one looks at it), K/Cdrama doesn't seem to have a large fanfiction base, otherwise I'd be a goner.

If you were a follower of my old stories (anything written before 2011), I apologize; it is highly likely that those stories will never be updated again. I am tentatively back as I would like to try my hand at writing again, but I will not be picking up where I left off. If anyone is interested in picking up one of my dropped stories, I have no objections-- ask away. I also apologize to any of those who may follow my future stories. Updates for those are likely to be few and far between. I am currently working two jobs (one full time, one part-time) and will soon be starting school again as well. I hate reading stories that never get updated, so it's be hypocritical of me to expect others to do the same. However, I am grateful for those who do. Thank you for your patience.
