Now...I'm a self-proclaimed Freya (from Matantei Loki Ragnarok), as I loved Loki but he didn't feel the same way. Also I look a bit like her...

Loki is good, Közi is good, Schwarz Stein is good. Sophia/Sif and Jacob/Heimdall are better.

I eat flowers, not much else. And I tend to be obsessive. Frighteningly so, in fact. I've been reading too much Norse mythology lately, and...irony is my God. If you understand me, then your aura is purple, or something like that. Whatever Jacob/Heimdall said.

What more to say about me? Go to my livejournal if you care.
