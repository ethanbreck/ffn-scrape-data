Author has written 8 stories for Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Pitch Black / Riddick, and Twilight.

I'm really surprised by the number of hits my fics get, yet the lack of reviews. I can't help but wonder if people open them in a tab while searching and then just never read them? I rarely read a fic without leaving a review. I may not leave one for every chapter. I suppose if it's a single chapter fic I might blow through a few of those without leaving reviews, actually.

At any rate, I know my LOTR fic is a MS style, but I think it's fun anyway. I even got pinned on a Mary Sue lookout sight a long time ago for it.

Blue Moon has an entire plot sitting in my Google docs, I've got chapters lined up and the whole bit. I'm not feeling pleased with the lack of dialog in the 2nd chapter though and I know this is my weak point. So I'm trying to decide right now whether to push myself to focus on conversations to tell the story or continue with the narrative style I'm comfortable with. Feedback on that topic is welcome because I fear it may be dense and difficult the read the way I write it.

So I'm a fan of Twilight, Riddick, Harry Potter, Artemis Fowl, and Gossip Girl.

My favorite ships are: Harry/Draco, Justin Finch-Fletchley, the Bloody Baron, Jasper/Alice, Blair/Chuck, and Pansy Parkinson. Send them my way if you've got them.

Thanx for stopping by.
-Jade-
