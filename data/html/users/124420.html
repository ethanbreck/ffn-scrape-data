Author has written 10 stories for Inuyasha, Naruto, Transformers, and Fullmetal Alchemist.

Some updates regarding my stories:

'Sanctuary' has been officially discontinued. After considerable debate with Damp, my editor for this story, we decided that instead of deleting chapters and forcing readers to reread the same story over (which is unfair and, frankly, boring), we will instead revamp the plot, keeping the elements fans found positive. Please be on the look out for 'Sanctuary 2.0' (working title only) in the coming months. Any title suggestions are welcome as are any plot elements that fans liked in the original but have not been previously listed in reviews. I am listening- your reviews are not ignored.

'Banks of the River Styx' is also being discontinued until further notice and no rewrite has been planned for the foreseeable future. It pains me to do it and I may come back to it when I have more time but I am in my senior year of college and have very little time to write. If I am rewriting 'Sanctuary' and keeping 'Bared Naked,' something else had to go.

'Scale' has a new chapter as of 5/19, 12:15 pm PST.

'Bared Naked' will be rewritten for cosmetic reasons though the plot will remain overall intact with minor corrective tweaking. I've been rereading it and cringing over structural issues and then someone wrote me a review that said there was a truck sized plot-hole in it...grr...

The 'Round Robins' on AdultFanfiction.net (originally posted as collaborative launching pads for other fanfiction writers who may have good story ideas but are unsure about developing a story) may disappear within the coming months since no one has touched them for a LONG time and I have enough stories to keep me busy. If, dear reader, you are interested in participating or wonder what I am referring to, please check out the first chapter of each RR for an explanation and appropriate rules on AFF.net provided you are over 18.

So this is whats going on for those who are interested. Not terribly exciting but updates rarely are unless you are 'expecting' or something.
