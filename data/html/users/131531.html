Author has written 12 stories for Digimon, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and Web Shows.

Well its been 8 years since I've been here, but better late than never.

I still bear a passion for Star Wars though my stories have dried up years ago. I still love Digimon and I sorely wish I could find those episodes so I can savor them again, especially my dearest Takato and Juri! I'm a HUGE Tim Burton fan and adore all his films. I could probably name all the films I love, but I'll probably just bore you all to death, so let's just say anything that's fantasy, whimsical, funny, anime-ish, romantic, or just plain weird touches my sweet tooth. And um...Twillight doesn't do anything for me. I kind of like my vampires to be dangerous and frightening. However I can appreciate a book that's doing more for abstinence then the lame-brain abstinence only sex education classes some states are teaching.

Moving on...

I'm currently on a Ninja Turtle binge and I hope that this time I see it through to the end. I hope to meet some good people and read some excellent stories again.
