These fics are not a WIP, but a series of 6 interconnected stories, which veer off BtVS canon after the sixth season finale "Grave" but follow AtS canon until the end of season 4. They can, for the most part, be read as stand-alones but will make more sense in context. The first three are primarily Buffy/Spike, the others are Buffy/Spike/Angel, all of them being mainly character studies. As they're the first fics I wrote they do get progressively better, so stick with them if you happen to be passing by.

If you wish to read more of my stories - or just these ones in their correct format - please visit: archiveofourown.org/users/netweight
