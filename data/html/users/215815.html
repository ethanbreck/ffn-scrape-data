Stuff I am grieving over:

Sayo's death in the RK series. Actually every loved character's death makes us, fanfiction writers, limited stuff to write about. I mean, if Sayo didn't die, Sano could've kissed her. And if Sano kissed her it would make it easier to write a fic about them. And that means more stuff to write about. Okay, I'm blabbing now. Somebody slap me, please. My dad not wanting to give me a new cell phone. Why Shigure had to die that way. Why they didn't kiss. Why Toki had to be left all alone, without Shigure to take good care of her. Not enough Sayo/Sano and Toki/Shigure fics out on the net. Too much Kaoru/Kenshin and Sano/Megumi (no offense meant). How much I try to make my bio have at least a bit of sense in what I say. Why I lose inspiration so fast. The fact I can only use the computer when I have no homework (which rarely happens), or if it's a weekend. Why Pyro and Rogue will never get together. Why he had to be bad. =(

Did Toki and Shigure ever love each other in a romantic way, like never before? Does Eriol love Tomoyo more than a friend? Will Touya ever learn to love Nakuru? Does Arwen have feelings for Legolas somehow?
