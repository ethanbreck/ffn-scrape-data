Age: 25 (Egad!)

Location: I live in a very small town in southeast Indiana once called Philanthropy, now named after a Roman General. My mailbox lives across the street, in Ohio.

Hobbies: On the off chance that I'm not writing, you'll probably find me doing one of the following: reading; drawing; painting; creating digital art (via Photoshop and/or Gimp. Oh, and sometimes I eat and sleep.
