Hello

So, I'll admit that I've been a long time reader of fanfic, but have just finally decided to try my hand at writing some.

Hopefully this will work out.

I have some little plot bunnies bouncing for most of these, but I'll just be sticking with my Merlin story at this point. Currently I have plans to make this story into a trilogy and will try to update once a week. I will finish this, as nothing drives me more nuts than incomplete stories.
