Short (yeah right...) Bio: Well, I first got interested in the whole pokemon craze when the games for the gameboy( yes, those monumentally gigantic old school gameboys that could double as bricks) were first introduced. After that, I was addicted to the series and watched every episode after school and during the Saturday morning cartoon block on Kids WB. And, upon watching the episode 'Pokeball Peril' where TR first introduced the whole idea of pokeshipping to the world in what is probably some of the most AAML-y lines in the whole series, I realized that, 'hey, Ash and Misty makes a lot of sense to me' and the rest is pretty much history! I took to the internet to find anything and everything Ash and Misty and found the AAML website that soon became one of the first of its kind to promote this coupling. They had fanfics, fanart, hints, and just about everything you could possibly think of to connect Ash and Misty to one another. I was hooked! My original fanfiction adventure began when I found the 'Pokemon Tower' website. I started writing random, no plot, mediocre fanfiction in the hopes of getting my name (which I had been using since I started writing) out in the open. Soon, I switched gears and found ff.net and I never really looked back. My first fic 'The Fight For Love' really cemented my feelings for this awesome coupling and now I can no longer picture any two people that are more meant for each other than Ash and Misty. I started this profile in the fall of 02' and I steadily improved my writing skills since then (as evidenced if you read my very first fic, to my very latest one). Today, I remain a strong believer in all things pokeshipping and I am still awaiting the day when those two finally admit their feelings for one another and we can all breathe a HUGE sigh of relief. Until then, though, keep the AAML spirit alive and happy reading!

R.I.P. Maddie Blaustein (1960-2008)
