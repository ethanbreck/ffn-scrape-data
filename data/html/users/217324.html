'Sup?

I'm a writer of fanfics and an artist. I'm obsessed with Gerard Way and My Chemical Romance in general, Manic Street Preachers, Spike (aka. William the Bloody), Pacey Witter and, most recently, Dr. Sheldon Cooper of The Big Bang Theory. I'm notoriously slow with updating, but don't let that put you off. I will not leave any story unfinished!

Read me, REVIEW ME!

UPDATES

APRIL 21, 2010: GOOD LORD! I hadn't realized so much time had passed! (dies of embarrassment)

I recently purchased my first home, and have been super busy with everything that goes with such a milestone. Unfortunately, moving in to my new place means that I lose my regular computer access and I can only do a minimum of personal stuff when I'm at work. I'm continuing to write the old fashioned way (longhand on a legal pad) and promise to upload new chapters when I can. :)

Cheers,
