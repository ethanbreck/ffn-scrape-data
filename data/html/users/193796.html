Author has written 26 stories for Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Andromeda, Star Wars, Troy, Tin Man, and Dragon Age.

Rewriting a bio I haven't looked at in six years is an odd experience, let me tell you. I guess I'll say thank you for taking the time to read anything I may have written. Even though it may seem like I disappear for a while (years at a time apparently), I am always around and always reading. My fandoms tend to shift frequently, including some I don't even write for, so you can never quite tell where I might be hanging out or where I'll appear next!

I like to only post completed works if possible--which generally means oneshots are a series of stories that can stand on their own. I worry about losing interestin a fandom and leaving my readers hanging, so I try to mitigate that possibility.

Anyway, please click on a story I have written below and send me some feedback; praise and/or concrit is happily accepted! I try to respond to all my reviewers, whether you're reviewing a story that was published a week ago, or two years ago. I adore you all and am happy to talk!
