Let me start by saying that Sailor Saturn is my favorite anime character of them all, mainly, because she and I have so many things in common, particulary, our past.
As for my other favorite characters, they're those with either an intriguing, difficult or mysterious past; those who are somehow trying to redeem themselves; and those who simply excell for one reason or another. Here's a list:
The Crow, Spawn, Rei Ayanami, The Darkness, Witchblade, Batman, Wolverine, Urd, Nabiki Tendo, Ukyo Kuonji, Spiderman, Sam Burke, Twitch Williams, Lara Croft, Zeta, Sil, the Predator, the aliens, and the list increases day by day.

As for anime, my likings go from the really dark like "Vampire Hunter D" to the light hearted like "Card Captor Sakura". I'm the kind of guy who goes from dark to light in a flash and you never know when the change will happen.
"Too high or too low, there ain't no in-betweens" Billy Joel

Last I will explain my name. I am a Gemini and born in the year of the snake, but that's not all.
Snake in nahuatl is coatl; since snakes usually appeared in pairs, coatl became cuate, a Spanish word for twin; and the twins are the symbol for Gemini. Interesting, huh?
