Hey ho everyone! Triforce Knight here, if you've come to this profile by accident, then please proceed to the nearest toolbar offering home, or search. To those of you who came here looking for the mind behind the selected fanfic(s), welcome.

Let's see, about me . . . well, I joined this site five years ago, and have gained some knowledge. There will be pauses between story updates, because, like most people here, I'm a college student, and work/study comes before entertainment. I live on the West Coast of the United States, and like other authors here, I enjoy reading and writing.

Alright, now for some fun stuff. That's right, a few quotes.

The only thing active about me is my imagination. Garfield the cat

Goodbye everyone! I'll remember you all in therapy! Plankton, Spongebob Squarepants

Stories That Have Been Saved by Completion:

Don't Count on Updates:

A Fox Huntin' We Will Go - Face it, all the foxes died.

A Wandering Soul - Still love the fandom, but their are too many things that I want to change that I hate the story, and wish to burn it.

The Eyes of Dragons - I will admit, this story's just too confusing even for me.

The Mistake - My first fanfic and attempt at writing comedy. Let's just say that to save it, it needs a major overhaul, or maybe just a proper burial.

You're a Malfoy - I'm ashamed to admit that this was written in my pre-teen days, and as such, the main character is nothing more than a horribly written Mary Sue. However, it was not bad enough to be posted on godawful.net (best site ever might I add, not for the stories, but go for the forums) which I am thankful for.

There's Still Some Hope:

Breaking News - It should just be a one-shot, but I enjoy the concept, which might cause me to write more chapters.

Living in the Shadows - Yet another story that started and seemingly died in my pre-teen days, but after going over it recently, I found it to be a bit darker than most of the stories you see listed, so there is potential that I might start it up again, after some major revision.

And last Seasonal Stories:

Christmas Magic - A collection of one-shots, this one shows my evolution as a writer from the early years. Now, When the Last Snow Falls was written, it was originally going to be added to the collection here, but after looking at stats, I noticed that the hits for each chapter went down, so When the Last Snow Falls was posted seperately because I was proud of it, and I thought that it deserved the attention.

Twisted Christmas Carols: Harry Potter Style - These are fairly simple to write, and a lot of fun to set to music. I don't believe they need anymore explanation.

Alright, and now for the good news, I currently have three new stories in the writing process, two of them are for Harry Potter, while the third will be in a fandom that I love, but have not posted anything for it before. Every now and then I'll update the progress I'm making on them, and I think I'll give you their titles and current progress now:

Dragon Wings of Love - Outline partially writter; Chapter 1 started.

The Book of Prophets - Outline non-existant; Prologue started.

A Chance - Outline partially written; No chapters currently.

Well, I think that I've written enough, so if you have questions or believe that I should pull a story from the dead zone, feel free to send a private message, and I'll try my best to respond. TOODLES!
