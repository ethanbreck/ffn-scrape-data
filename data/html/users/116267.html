What insanity is this? I'm updating?

Well cleaning this up more like. This rambling text box reminded me of how badly I needed to chill the hell out in my early twenties. Yeah look at me all of twenty-eight and already shaking my cane at the world.

So I write

What do I write? Stuff

On this account, a lot of Naruto stuff as it happens, which is hilarious because I hate the anime and the manga but I genuinely like the characters

Or you could slide over to alt account at Valid Dreams where I have a lot of much more recent on-going projects focusing on CRPGs (Dragon Age, Skyrim, and Baldur's Gate if any of you recognize that dinosaur relic of a title)

Does this mean I am going to start updating? Yes, I hope so

Also, I will eventually launch a blog to begin hosting some of my original work and with it an ebeg account. It's not quite the dream of being published, but it IS hopefully a method of getting something back for my work.

It's important to note: nothing will be held for ransom. Once something is finished, it will be posted.
