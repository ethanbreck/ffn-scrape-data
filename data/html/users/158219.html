Well, I'm Kurt -- father, tech support guy, Grand High Poobah of the Lecterphiles yahoo group and importantly for here, fanfic author. I hail from Rochester, NY, where it is usually cold and Canada is right nearby.

In my fics you usually won't find too much moosh (sorry Steel) or your standard fare. I enjoy introducing new characters and new situations in my stories. Sometimes this ends up with the original characters being run out or killed off in the fic.

They say I'm the king of gore, having written some gory fics to start off with. Gore's all well and good, but I've been trying different stuff as well.
