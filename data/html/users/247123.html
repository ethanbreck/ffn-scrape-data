Ok, you know, I think it's about time I updated here. It has been, like, a year since I last did anything. So, thanks to some wonderful reviewers for Rhapsody Relived and a revived obession with Harry Potter I have decided to pick that..er.. keyboard back up again and start writing more stuff! Priority one will likely be the sequel to Impossible. Well, independent sequel since it won't really have much to do with the first one at all except some minor character similarities. Once that is well under way, and once I am home and able to use my own computer, I'll continue writing more of Rhapsody. I swear I did intend for another chapter to be here by now. It's just, with all those fun new games to play.. Kingdom Hearts.. Final Fantasy.. Harvest Moon! Well.. I got a little sidetracked. But, fear not! It will be on it's way in maybe two months tops!
