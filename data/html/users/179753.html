It has been too long.I will be adding stories every now and then,when my muse is gracious enough to inspire me.Some of the stories I have up now are from when I was younger, and as any writer knows, if you leave something unfinished for a long time you lose that spark for it. I will try to update the unfinished stories, if I can, and I will be adding some new stories.For now,I'll keep on reading Your stories,and hopefully You'll keep on reading Mine!

Nightwalker has become my main interest, though Gundam wing will always be one of my favorites. Marmalade boy is also working it's way into my heart. Expect to see more Nightwalker/Gundam fanfics in the near future.
