About Me

Hi, I'm David, and I like to write fanfiction. It's why I'm here at this crazy site, bravely facing the hordes of bad grammar and spelling errors.

Not only do I like to write fanfiction, I dabble in role-play as well. A little art, a little cosplay, a lot of video games, and an embarrassing otaku tendency that seems to flare up when I find something new and interesting to become fascinated with.

Not going to bore you with my life story, so I'll talk a bit about the kinds of stories I'm into. I mostly enjoy reading and writing romantic types of stories, either angsty or humorous. It's all good. I've read wonderful representations of both, and although my own writing tends to lean towards the angsty side, I've been known to write some pretty random things. I just need to post some of them, heh.

I consider myself pretty easygoing about my fandoms. I'll give any pairing a read, if you can make me believe it (i.e. well-written). Het, yaoi, yuri, it doesn't matter to me. I have my own favorites in all of those situations. Crack pairings can also be fun, as long as there's at least some loose tie to make me see them together. A well done PWP is especially delicious in some cases, as well.

Yes, I do like yaoi. Let it be known, however, I'm not some drooling teenage otaku that will throw any two hot boys together for the sake of fanservice. Or girls, for that matter. I'm actually rather picky about those types of pairings. I like smut as much as the next closet pervert, but please. Think of the plots. I do like it at least a little believable.
