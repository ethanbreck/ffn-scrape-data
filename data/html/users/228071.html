a b o u t . m e

Me? What about me? I love to read and write - the reason I'm on this site. Hopefully I'd like to become a published writer one day but until then I'm working steadily all the time. I have a few story ideas on the side that I plan on working on. I'm a very slow writer because I'm just so particular when it comes to some things but I fully intend on finishing all the fanfic/any other future stories I may post up on here. Con-crits welcome.

I have a writing journal where I post book reviews on books of all kinds though mostly focused on the YA variety, and the occasional update on what's going with my writing. Feel free to friend me or leave me some comments. I always enjoy hearing what others have to say.

If you want to keep abreast of what I'm working on right now or what have you, check out my journal and my twitter (my username is need_tea). I'm always available there.

Update (11.06.14):
The world is a busy place, constantly moving and changing, and I stand in the middle of it, wondering where I belong or where I shall go. These months have stretched into a year and my contact has become a hint of the past. There are so much pressures and deadlines, things I must do and things I want to do. Yet, I keep trying and keep hoping even though it's neither been good or positive. Up until now, there has been no desire, no urges to see anything written but finally, I've gotten that approval, that acceptance I've long been searching for.

It is not much and it may not happen, prolonging my empty stretch for yet still longer, but things have looked up and I may be free once more. Until that time comes, writing will not commence but I have not forgotten. Hazy and scattered are my memories but with only a little surface digging I'll be able to scrape them to the top once more. Please, for all that enjoy the words I write, hang in there once more because one day I shall rise again.
