My old 's Pen name was Ivy, but then they changed the system so every person can have their own seperate name, so I changed it to Aibi. I'm a Proud Chinese Canadian, and is a HUGE hockey fan. I think the Canadian culture has rubbed off on me :D My favourite hockey of course is Vancouver Canucks!

Lately, I've become obsessed with Ginny/Draco fics. nods Damn those people who abandoned their fic!

I love reading complete or near-complete fics, since I don't have the patients to wait for the fics. I would crave for them, especially when they are the REALLY REALLY good ones.

I used to read Buffy/Spike fics a whole lotta, but that was about a year ago. I haven't lost interested, but it's getting quite boring with the same plot line in a lot of fics.

I have this pet peeve of people having spelling errors. Since there's programs like Microsoft Word and such, shouldn't you spell check your story before uploading them? Another pet peeve of mine is when people write, Your instead of You're. My English isn't 100 since it's not my mother language, but it has improved over the years that I've been here. And I thought people learn grammar in school too!

November 9, 2004
I have no intention to finish any of my fics anymore. Hopefully I will have another fic that I will try to start in December because right now I am working on NaNoWriMo. See you then!
