Hello, folks - I go by the name "SpydaBass", but you can just call me Spyda. You may know me, you may not. I'm a psychotic, disturbed white male of 16 years of age. I've been an avid videogame nut since birth, with my favorite games being Mario, Zelda, Final Fantasy, Chrono Trigger/Cross, Sonic, Rockman/Rockman X, Metroid, and many many others which I don't really feel like listing at the time.

Fanfiction-wise, I'm mostly into Rockman/Rockman X stuff - I love reading the stuff, and I love writing it too. I've also been known to write some other things, mostly comedy, which is most definitely my strongest suit - I've been told that I'm a funny guy, since I have the uncanny ability to make people laugh. Then again, it's hard to tell if people laugh AT my stuff or WITH my stuff. Oh well. :p

Currently, I'm working on "Dissonant Dreamers", a new MMX story that serves as the sequel to the 'fic I finished recently, "Vile's Redemption." DD is still in its early stages, and VR is probably set for a bit of editing here and there, but any comments you could give on either of them would be greatly appreciated. Also, if you've got any questions, feel free to e-mail me.

That's all. :) Thanks for your time.
