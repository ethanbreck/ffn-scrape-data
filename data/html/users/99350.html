Author has written 2 stories for Magic Knight Rayearth/魔法騎士レイアース, and Sailor Moon X-overs.

The person behind this stuff is wingnut, sometimes also known as kunenk (with and without a capital). She is something of a dork. She currently mostly writes stuff for Kingdom Hearts, although she's also interested in Good Omens, Chrono Trigger, some of the Final Fantasy series, and a couple of other fandoms which she forgets the names of right now. She may even write a couple of stories for Harry Potter, although probably not any time soon, and she doesn't actually want to get involved in the fandom very much. She appreciates recs for good fics, if they're to be had, though (usually she goes favourite-hopping, although she doesn't think her own favourite list is really to be trusted right now. She'll fix it sometime).

Wingnut is also rather good at talking about herself in the third person. She's not quite sure what this says about her.
