Author has written 29 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Digimon, Harry Potter, Yu Yu Hakusho, Prince of Tennis, and Final Fantasy VII.

This writer is eighteen years of age at the moment, a fan of well-written stories. She is partial towards slash/shounen ai fics but demands good language and plot above all else. She will read gen fics and some het, provided they are pairings that don't make her cringe.

She reads in a variety of fandoms including but not limited to:
Bleach
Final Fantasy VII
FullMetal Alchemist
Gundam Wing
Harry Potter
Naruto
Prince of Tennis
Shaman King
Yuu Yuu Hakusho

She is also highly fond of crossovers, mostly of the Harry Potter/anime variety. That said, she does not enjoy crossovers involving more than two universes, or crossovers that are completely implausible. Or crossovers that are painful to read. She may like crossovers, but still maintains some sort of standard.

She posts only sporadically on ff.net now, being completely in the dark about the new changes that have been implemented and highly frustrated at the way some of her symbols are eaten. Much of her fiction can be found on her creative journal at soera.livejournal.com. Click on the homepage link to get to it.

She is willing to beta fics on a chapter-by-chapter basis. In other words, she will not commit to a full fic, but do feel free to contact her if you would like to ask her to look through a chapter before you post it. She warns you here that if you do so, she will not spare you and will put your grammar and spelling through the wringer.

She would like to warn all potential readers that far too many of the fics currently posted here are old fics which leave much to be desired. They remain up because they have sentimental value, and not because they're worth reading. Beware the old fics, and preferably stick only to the new ones.

Enjoy!
