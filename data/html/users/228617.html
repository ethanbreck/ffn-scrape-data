Author has written 9 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Skip Beat!, and Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir.

It's been a long time since I picked up a pen to write. Here's hoping I can still do this. Sometimes you gotta get the stories out so they can stop crowding your thoughts. Maybe you'll enjoy them too. Or maybe not. I don't know your life. But if you do, feel free to shoot me a review or message or something!

I think I first made this account in 2002 or 2003. You have no idea how badly I want to delete the old stories I wrote, but they're a part of me for good or for bad so here they'll stay. Okay, I actually only left one of them up because, wow, you have no idea how bad those other ones were. I mean, holy crap. Yea, you dodged a bullet there. Seriously. Feel relieved

You wanna know about my fandoms? Hint: look at my favorited stories.

After looking at my own list, I realized I apparently have a thing for broken men with unpleasant pasts who are looking for redemption in their new lives/identities. Except for maybe Trunks; I've had an irrational crush on him since I was a teen. Huh... I never imagined I actually had a type.

I read to enjoy and I write for others to enjoy. There is not much more that needs to be said.

UPDATE: I finally got around to activating my tumblr blog for any of you who are curious. You can find me under the same username. I really don't do much there except creep on my favorite fandoms but if you wanna stop by and say hello, feel free!
