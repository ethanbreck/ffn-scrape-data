UPDATE:

As of right now, my WiP are on hiatus. As much as I loathe leaving up unfinished work, these shall remain on FF.net for archival purposes. These may never be finished... or maybe they will. Your words are so encouraging. If I do finish any of these stories listed below, it will have been because of you.

Once again, many thanks, and God bless.
