Author has written 5 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Big O.

I'm the writer formerly known as Malevolent Angel. This name was fine and fitting when I was a moody teenager. Alas, I am no longer that. My writing style has changed and I feel encumbered by the name. I'm sorry to those of you I've confused, but I feel it necessary to be reborn in order to write again. I'm still going to leave my old stuff up, but I'd rather not be defined by it. So, if you're wondering, Poliahu is the Hawaiian goddess of snowy Mauna Kea and is a rival to Pele.

3-31-08

I can't believe it's been over four years since I've posted anything on here. It's been so long that I don't even identify with my pen name anymore. It's my own fault. I greatly underestimated how much of my time would be taken up by being a college athlete with a double major. A lot of things have changed, including my writing style. My influences are different and some new life experiences have given me the inspiration to tell new and different stories. Stories that people can hopefully connect with. I currently have some new ideas pooling for some different movies and anime.

There is some bad news, however. Some days must be dark and dreary. For those of you hoping to see more of "Sweet Uncertainty," I would recommend that you stop holding hope. It's been so long since I worked on it that I've lost my original outline and the three subsequent chapters that I had written. I don't feel comfortable with it anymore. I've also decided to move away from DBZ. I don't have anything new to give to the characters. There are so many other films and works that I want to pay tribute to. Since I can't make any promises about future works, I won't get your hopes up by telling you what movies or anime I want to work with.
