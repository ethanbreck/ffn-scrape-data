A thousand year ago the Silver Millennium was brought to an end by the Dark Kingdom, a tale only some remember. As to what brought the galaxy into the Silver Millennium, only one who witnessed those events remains alive.

If alive is what you can call Ephraim. For the last two thousand years he has cultivated his hatred against the woman who brought about the demise of his kingdom in the form of outlawed magic.

Meanwhile, the Sailor Scouts in present day struggle to adjust to their new, peaceful life. Within their "normal" lives a new set of Guardians awaken, bringing about more questions than answers regarding their past lives. With one Guardian's resentment of Serena overshadowing her actions, will facing off against Ephraim be what unites all Guardians to heal hearts that broke hundreds of years ago?
