9.02.17 - FINALLY, the story that got me writing again is published! Thanks to my betas for signing off on it, even though real life conspired against two of them being able to do the thorough job they wanted to do. I've been writing loads in the meantime, too, so more to come, hopefully in fewer than seven months this time.

~Neshomeh

2.23.17 - Wow, it's been ages since I last did anything here. But, Skyrim had temporarily taken over my brain and it's got me writing fanfic again, which feels pretty great. I'm a newbie to TES as of Skyrim, so I hope I don't screw up on the lore too badly. I'm wiki-walking like crazy and learning loads as I write, but I know it's not the same as having been there, so to speak. Pointers and corrections, as well as constructive critiques in general, are always appreciated.

Shout-out to SaxyGirl. I don't know her, but her series "Saving the Dragonborn" helped inspire me to go with the muse and just have fun. {= )

~Neshomeh
