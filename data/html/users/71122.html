Hi. I'm Ally. I'm a 25-year-old aspiring writer from Massachusetts. I read, and I write, and I'm generally a dork.

I'm warning you right now, I'm not the most prolific writer. I've been here for several years now, and the stories you see below are the only finished fan fiction I've written in that time. Hopefully, you'll all forgive me. It's more about quality than quantity for me, I suppose (though I've got at least fifty unfinished fics sitting on my computer at this very moment, most of which will never get finished). So, forgive me for the months between updates, and console yourself in knowing that I really do work hard, and perhaps if you bug me I'll finish writing quicker :)

My older stuff is pretty terrible. Don't worry, I recognize this. Believe me, I was just re-reading some of it the other day and wondering, "why the HELL don't I take this stuff down?" For posterity's sake, I guess. Plus, I'm a glutton for reviews.

I write a lot of romance, and a LOT of humor. I try very hard to write dramatic, serious things, but they ALWAYS come out as tongue-in-cheek humor. Why does this happen? Anyone got a clue?

As always, if you have any questions, comments, complaints, or requests, you can email me or leave a review and I'll get back to you when I can :) Thanks!

Update: As of now, I've been EXTREMELY BUSY with life in general (hooray for job searches!) and haven't had a chance to write or read anything in a long, long time! Sorry, guys (you know who I'm talking about)! Once the new year starts, I hope to have the time and the effort to work on the stories I've had on the back burner for the past few months and read my impressive backlog! Please badger me to write and read faster, it helps a lot!
