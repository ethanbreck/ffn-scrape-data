Hello, my name is Brynna and I'm 25 years old. I recently graduated from college for the second time and I now have very conflicting degrees in engineering and art. Strange, I know. I work as a graphic designer and GUI developer and I often work long hours. If there are ever lags in my posting, it is simply real life taking over.

I have been writing and reading fanfiction for quite sometime. I have a constantly changing list of fandoms that I love to read and write and a lot that have fallen by the wayside. Current favorites include: Stargate: Atlantis (Ronon/Teyla), Harry Potter (Ron/Hermione), Twilight (Esme/Carlisle, Jasper/Alice), Jericho (Jake/Heather), Lost Odyssey (Sarah/Kaim), Mass Effect (Female Shepard/Kaidan) and probably more that I can't think of.

Visit my newly resurrected journal for more ramblings.
