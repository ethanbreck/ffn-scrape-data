Well I'm updating this because what I'd written before isn't really relevant anymore.

All you really need to know is that I'm from England and that I used to write Harry Potter fanfics many years ago. Recently I got back into reading and writing them completely unexpectedly; I hadn't even thought about the fandom for years! Now I'm writing again I thought I might as well post the fruits of my efforts. I'd love to hear what you think of them.

I mostly write Draco/Harry is Draco/other female character fics. I know that's probably an odd combination, and I also know nobody really cares about the latter, but I hope someone reads and enjoys them because I certainly enjoyed writing them.
