Hello there.

I'm the online author English Savant, formerly Aqua Rhapsody. I've changed my pen name because the old one no longer suited me. I've come a long way since I began writing on Fanfiction.net, and I think that it is time that my pen name reflected the changes that I've undergone. So, for those of you who didn't know me before, I suppose now would be a good time to introduce myself.

I'm eighteen, and currently reside in a small rural community in Colorado. Well, it's small according to my standards, I grew up in San Diego.

I am a freshmen at a local community college, studying for my Associate of Arts degree. I plan to major in Education, specifically English. Which explains my new pen name. I'm just wrapping up my first semester, in fact, I sold back my textbooks earlier today.

I've put my writing at Fanfiction.net onsabbatical until I complete a story that I'm about halfway through on the Fictionpress site. It's entitled, "Our Lips Are Sealed," and it is full of tangled romance webs, typical high school crises, and lots of speaking without thinking. So if it appeals to you, please don't hesitate to drop by and give it a once over.

I'm a Pisces, symbolized by the two fish, born in March. We, (by that, I mean Pisces), are a highly creative bunch, who often think too far out of the box for it tobe at all effective or practical. We're known for our active imaginations, which often transfer over into frequent daydreaming in that class where the teacher drones on and on forever about the intrinsic properties of sublimated cadmium telluride. You know the type, even if you don't really know who I'm talking about.

I'm kind of minoring in law, in a program my school calls "Public Creed and Protocol." So if you insult me, I might just hunt you down, and take you to court. Then again, maybe not.

If there's anything I can do for you, drop me a line at my new email address.

Thanks!
