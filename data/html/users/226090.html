Hello Sunshine
hide bio
PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 06-08-02, id: 226090, Profile Updated: 09-05-12
Author has written 20 stories for CSI: New York, Digimon, Power Rangers, Bring it On, Harry Potter, Magic School Bus, Space Cases, and Sonic the Hedgehog.

I think it would be fairly obvious to the general population that I am a sporadic writer. I write when struck by inspiration and when I can find the mental energy to stay focused on a story. This is not very often, as a handful of other important duties (like school, and work...) tend to take up most of my time.

This would be why I mostly write one shots. I, for the life of me, cannot wrap my head around the concept of writing on one idea for an extended period of time. Eventually I hope to succeed in writing a multiple chapter story, but for the time being, I am not holding my breath.

I dabble in many fandoms, although I usually stray back to the same few. I'm not really picky. As long as I know the characters and the storyline, in a general sense anyways, I can read it. And I love reading it. I do more reading then I do writing and I put a lot of stock into other author's opinions. Criticism is meant to be constructive and I need all the help I can get. So feel free. It might hurt a little, but I am certain that I will get over it.

Any questions, drop me a line and I will (eventually) get back to you.
