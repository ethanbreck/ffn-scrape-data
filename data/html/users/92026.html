I'm 18, a girl, and live in Canada. I like to write all sorts of things, including fanfiction...doesn't mean I post everything I write _~ I like reading manga and watching anime. I read mostly shoujo manga so if you think that my stories are kind of sappy, well, there's my inspiration... yes, I'm a helpless romantic and my fanfics are full of fluff and sap, and happy endings! I am usually very busy and can't really find the time to write, but I try my best whenever I do! Please be patient with my updates!

Please check out my friends' (twilight knight, Aenoni maeus, and Moonlight Sunrise) stories! They are my REAL life friends and have written some neat stuff!
