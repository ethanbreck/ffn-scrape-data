Author has written 4 stories for Suikoden, Cowboy Bebop, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

My username indicates how little daylight there is to work on fanfiction. It's very difficult to find precious time. But I always look forward to sitting down and working on an idea, especially late at night, the Fanfic Witching Hour. I rewrite things over and over, until a paragraph turns into a few words. I also value 'showing' over 'telling.' I appreciate all reviews and readers and try to write with you in mind.
