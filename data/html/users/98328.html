Hello!

Finally decided to put something (possibly) worth reading on my bio!

About me:

I'm 21 years old, and I live in Mississippi. I am currently a college student, though my college is in Florida. I enjoy reading fan fiction more then writing it, and I (unfortunately) have a habit of starting out strong then tapering off. Since I have a hard time finishing stories, I'm trying to get into the habit of finishing a story before posting any of it. My readers should also know that, because of college, the only time I can post fan fiction is during summer and Christmas break. But, I'M GONNA BE A SENIOR THIS COMING YEAR!! YAY!! Of course, getting out of college and into the "work force" doesn't necessarily mean more time, but we'll see.

I'm a Bible-believing Christian.

Interests:

Reading (inherited family trait)

Writing

Photography (especially scenery)

Crafts (sewing, painting, etc.)

Laughing!! (lots of laughing)

Current Fan Fiction Interests:

Books

Lord of the Rings (complete Tolkien freak)

The Chronicles of Narnia (fairly recent development, so not really secure enough in the canon to venture into fan fiction)

Although I enjoyed watching the movies for both these sets of books, the books themselves are still 10 times better!

Stories In-Progress:

Mirkwood's Icy King - Lord of the Rings fan fiction that is on hold until further notice. There are several problems with it (yes, you flamers, I know) and it's something I want to correct rather then just tossing the story. It has potential, but I don't currently have the ideas or the drive to do it. Sorry people, I know it's annoying when stories go un-updated for a year or more. I'm trying!

The Quest of the Mary-Sue - Another Lord of the Rings fan fiction, this one meant to be humor. It's a Mary-Sue basher, so that makes a lot of people happy. I started writing it while on a chocolate-and-sugar induced high, which is when I'm (apparently) at my funniest. So, I do write on it ever now and then when I'm in the mood. Again, sorry for the slow updates.
