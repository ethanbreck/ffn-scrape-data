hi.

occasionally, i read fanfic. even less occasionally, i am compelled to write something outrageously pretentious. sorry everyone!

for what it's worth, everything written before 2005 i find terribly embarrassing now, and i suggest passing it over unless you want to die from boredom. everything written in 2005 or later is reasonably tolerable.

love is blind:

If you have written fic that you'd like to see added to the love is blind DOA community, or have a recommendation, or would like to help out as staff, or - well, anything having to do with the community, just drop me a PM or contact me via Dreamwidth. I may not reply quickly but I promise I'll respond when I can. The only requirement for submissions is that they include slash or femslash; everything else is fair game. ;)

OTHER STUFF

My participation in fanfic and fandom is sporadic (surprise!) but if you would like to get in touch I am slightly more active at the above sites than here.
