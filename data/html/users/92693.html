Hello everyone!

I am kumoriko1, procrastinator extraordinaire. I procrastinate at everything, and I think of excellent ideas for stories and such but then don't finish them. I'm a full-time college student and a full-time substitute teacher so those things take up a lot of my time.

My Fanfiction

Multi chapter. Incomplete. Holes.

Three girls and a gay guy go to Camp Green Lake to dig holes. Eventual romance. Eventual second chapter. I like the story, but I lost all of my notes on the plot and everything, and I have yet to rewrite them. I'm never going to finish it. It was written the summer after I finished ninth grade and is a blatant self insertion of me and my friends. I wrote it because I was missing them over the summer. Since entering college I've lost touch with everyone, but I'm keeping the story because it holds a piece of my past.
