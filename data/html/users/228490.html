On My Stories

(All Are Post-Hogwarts)

"Truth Is" (HG/BZ) - COMPLETE - This story that started as a great little plot bunny with Montague and Angelina Johnson then to DM and Hermione Granger finally came to a head at Blaise Zabini and Hermione Granger. Now it has finally began to weave it's tangled web and it's getting longer than I had actually anticipated. I finished it before I went back to college so look out I may have time for a sequel this school year, we'll see. This is probably my favorite out of everything that I've ever written. It shows my growth as a writer.

"It's Christmas" (Several Couples) - COMPLETE - This is my serious parody of one of my favorite movies, Love Actually. So it stars BZ/HG/D, GW/HP, R/PansyP, Montague/AJ, SusanB/AdrianPucey, and Dean Thomas. They all have their little plot lines which all culminated and wrap up together on Christmas Day. Its one of my favorites. FLUFF WARNING!

"This Christmas" (Several Couples) - COMPLETE - This is my SEQUEL to It's Christmas! I keep hoping for a sequel to Love Actually and it doesn't seem to be happening so I've come up with my own! It is really a series of One-shots, all taking place on Christmas Day some years after the ending of the original. This series will include: BZ/HG, GW/HP, RW/PP, Montague/AJ, SB/AP, and the much sought after endings of Dean Thomas, and last, but not least, Draco Malfoy.

"Ordinary Just Won't Do" (HG/DM) - COMPLETE - My very first novel length Harry Potter fan fiction. It's REALLY corny, it's my least favorite thing that I've ever written in this fandom but people seem to still love it.

"Hogwarts Class of 2026" (OC) - ON HOLD - The Sequel to OJWD. I hated the original so I it's no surprise to that I couldn't invest myself in this sequel. I loved my plot ideas and the children that I had created for this story but I couldn't find more of myself to put in this story. So I started "Truth Is" and I never looked back. More than likely will never be completed but we'll see.
