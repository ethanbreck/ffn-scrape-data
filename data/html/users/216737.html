Author has written 8 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter.

Hello! It has been an incredibly long time since I have been able to devote any time to fanfictions or my own fictional stories, but I am hoping to have some time to get back to them now! My time for stories such as these used to compete with school, my brother, choirs, performances, horses, competitions, planning a wedding, and so much more. However! I am finally able to say that I have graduated and I am married to an incredible Airman. I am in college, work a full time job, do still ride horses as much as possible and sing whenever I can, but the performances are down to a minimum, and my brother is doing pretty well without me (personal back story there, sorry if its vague here). This means I have at least a little more "Me time", and I hope to tackle some plot bunnies that have been building for quite awhile. Please note, a lot of these bunnies have been growing since before some of the recent books came out, but I'm going to put them out there anyway!

Feel free to e-mail me about anything, honestly! I love reviews, good and bad, so drop a line on the stories too! Be aware: most of these stories were written when I was in middle school, and I pretty much check conventions then post them...they aren't the greatest things in the world by any means, and most remain unfinished, but they're fun!

If you like fantasy, check out my story on fictionpress.com! I've been working on that for awhile too (seventh grade), and I hope to get the new (and VERY, VERY improved) version up really soon, but I confess I haven't touched either in quite a while. It's under the author name Silmaril, and the title is Magia Reale! Actually, what with my attachment to my first "novel" I'm having trouble finishing it, so I may be putting a new one up on fictionpress.com - watch for it - Fourth and Only

Thanks for reading and (hopefully) reviewing! Hope to hear from you! Take care and keep reading!
