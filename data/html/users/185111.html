A message from a fellow author die Seele ist immer frei:

I AM SICK OF THE FOLLOWING ON FF.NET:

People writing crappy summaries without proper punctuation or spelling. You could honestly have the best story in the world, but the summary drags it down and puts people off.

People posting a whole bunch of stories in one shot and pushing down other stories. Hate that. Hate it, hate it, hate it! Nobody has time to review, and the author who posts all of the stories in the first place doesn't even take the reviews they get into consideration because they're too busy posting more, more, more!

Stories that are blatant rip offs of other, better stories. I mean, COME ON! Get a mind. Seriously.

People posting unpunctuated, misspelt stories that don't have paragraphs and instead are one massive blob of unreadable text. It really makes you wonder why you bothered when you click onto that.

People who write, 'sorry if I suck, I'm only so-old'. You don't have to be a certain age to be a good writer. Sure, experience helps, but being a teenager doesn't make you a worse writer than some adults.

If you're sick of it, too, then join the crusade and pass the message on!

I joined the crusade, why don't you!
