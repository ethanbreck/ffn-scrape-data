Author has written 6 stories for Dungeons and Dragons, Morganville Vampires, and Supernatural.

I am an avid reader and role player. My ultimate goal in life is to do one of two things - either edit books or to read books on audio.

While I understand that neither of those will happen, I dream of the day that it does.

My favorite book series include the Rachel Morgan series, Rachel Caine's Morganville Vampires and her Weather Warden series, The Dresden Files, the Nightside series, and the Codex Alera. I have been known to delve into any number of other books series, and love to get reading suggestions. I ask that any of my stories you read you do so honestly, and if you have critiques of my work, please do send them my way. As an aspiring author (oh yeah! That's another ultimate goal in life!), I am constantly looking to improve myself. Thanks!

Feel free to email me, as well.
