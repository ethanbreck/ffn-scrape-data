Hello to my fellow readers and writers! My name is Shaina and currently I am 18-years-old. Over the years my writing style has changed, as expected. I began writing "full on" stories in 2000/2001 which was when I completed Love Can Come from a Child's Heart and it's sequel. You will notice those two stories are not long and are not written very well. I was just starting out with FanFiction so i really didn't know how to write it. I have definitely matured as a writer and my stories have become longer and better written. Currently I am writing Two Top Divas, One Huge Secret which involves an original character, Trish Stratus, Chris Jericho, and many other WWE superstars/characters. My other story, Accidental Love, will not be updated until I am done with Two Top Divas, One Huge Secret, but I promise that it will be updated and finished.

I thank everyone who reads my stories and reviews them. It definitely does inspire me to write more and if you have comments or ideas on what you'd like to see happen in my stories, email me. It really does make a difference. All I can truly say is Thankyou.

-Shaina, FanFictionWriter since 2000-
