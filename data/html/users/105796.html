-- First off my thoughts on this great anime and the accompanying fanfictions. The anime itself is just truly splendid, and leaves so many crucial decision points that the "What if" groups can be crazy with it. Why? Because either there are just way too many of them already out there that it becomes boring (Sakura/Syaoran). It has no basis at all and never even an inkling of a possibility or It just downright irks me (Syaoran/Eriol) (Sakura&Eriol) (Tomoyo/Eriol).
