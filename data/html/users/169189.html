Okay, hi. I hate bios. First I'll just say that my E-mail is lunalovegood713@gmail.com, so if you have questions, E-mail me! Or, if it's about a particular story, I'd prefer a review. Reviews encourage me.

Hm. What else might you want to know? I'd introduce you to my muse, but she has mysteriously dissapeared right now...I'll have to locate her. I hate when she does this -- as if I don't have enough of an issue with writer's block! Oh, I know, I know, pairings. Might as well let you all know what you're getting into before you start reading my stuff, eh? So, here we go, in no particular order.

X-Men Evolution:

Pairings I like:

Scott/Rogue (c'mon, someone give the Goth girl a chance!)

Kitty/Kurt (cute. what can I say?)

Pairings I dislike:

Scott/Jean (stereotype ftl!)

Harry Potter:

Pairings I like:

Harry/Luna (awesomeness incarnate)

Ron/Hermione (okay, I'll admit, it's cute)

Harry/Hermione (sometimes)

Pairings I dislike (AKA hate, when it comes to this):

Harry/Ginny (oh, god, don't get me started)

Draco/Harry (...wtf?)

Harry/Snape (...even more WTF!?)

Buffy:

Pairings I like:

Buffy/Willow (perfect for each other, and I think it SO would have happened if they weren't so scared to make their main character a lesbian)

Willow/Tara (classic... I can't help it... I have to love it)

Willow/Xander (pre-college only, and it has to be written right, but it can be so cute)

Buffy/Spike (I have a love/hate relationship with this relationship... I lose)

Pairings I dislike:

Willow/Kennedy (hate it. Hate Kennedy. Possibly because I loved Tara, possibly because I just want to see Will with Buffy, but whatever the reason... hate it)

Buffy/Xander (nothing against it... just... don't like it)

Buffy/Angel (same as above, this one just never hit it off with me)

Buffy/Spike (hate it, love it, god I fail with this pairing)

NCIS:

Pairings I like:

Gibbs/Abby (epic win, they're so adorable)

Tony/Kate (old school... should have happened)

Tony/Ziva (totally needs to happen)

Pairings I dislike:

Abby/McGee (She said herself, it would never work, get over it people)

Tony/Jean (I NEVER trusted that woman)

Ziva/McGee (are you kidding? She'd kill him, he's too dorky)

Abby/Tony (eh, they're too brother-sister for me to see this work)

Kim Possible:

Pairings I like:

Kim/Shego (the subtext... oh the glorious subtext!)

Bonny/Senor Senior Junior (...hilarious. I don't write it, but come on, so funny)

Wade/Monique (I don't know, it's just cute somehow)

Pairings I dislike:

Kim/Ron (used to like it... until they did it in the show and I realized 'Wow, this so does not work')

Shego/Drakken (...she'd kill him. seriously.)

Wicked:

Pairings I like:

Glinda/Elphaba (DUH! book, musical, doesn't matter, it's perfect... they're adorable, and I never use that word)

Boq/Nessarose (if he weren't such an idiot and would just get that Galinda doesn't like him, he could have had a great relationship... she wouldn't have gone nuts if he'd given her a chance)

Pairings I dislike:

Elphaba/Fiyero (No clue why. I just... do not like Fiyero.)

Glinda/Fiyero (same as above... Fiyero bugs me)

Boq/Glinda (she's just not interested! She's too taken with Elphie. ;) )

Well, that's all the pairings I can think of right now. If I come up with anything else interesting I'll be sure to add it. ...On that vein, expect a 'favourite quotes' list to appear soon... when I find the time to do it.
