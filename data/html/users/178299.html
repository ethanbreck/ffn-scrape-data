THE GENERAL-ISTICS:
Hi guys! My name is Kacie, I'm fifteen and I'm a sophmore in high school. ] I live in Aurora, Colorado, but I was born in El Paso, Texas. I'm about 5'7", blue eyes and I have dark cherry coloured hair. ^^ I'm a happy person, creative, bouncy person and I get along with lots of people, but I can be very shy. ^^; I want to be an author when I grow up, or maybe do something with history. OH. I like music a lot. I play clarinet, and have for 5 years. ] I'm considered an "emo kid". ^^ I have dark-blue, thick framed glasses.

THE LIKE-ISTICS:
I loooove music, both playing and listening, but I can't sing! And let's face it, I love to write.. Or I wouldn't be here. Angst is something I really, really get into.. And I love horror, but I can't write it. Shounen-ai is awesome. I love acting, and anything that involves the stage. I like anime, mostly Card Captor Sakura, though. I'm a very huge MeiLi [Meiling + Syaoran] and E+Sy [Eriol + Syaoran] fan.. ^^; Various video games [RPGs] make me very happy, especially FFXI! And some other stuff I like are: Psychology, photography, rain, French, cherry coke. ^^

THE HATE-ISTICS:
I'll tell you now, if you type like an idiot, you'll bother me beyond belief. I also don't like rap, or country for that matter.. Um.. I hate being cold, and I hate snow! I'm not a fan of Sakura [CCS] nor do I support S+S, or, for the most part, E+T.. But, yes, on rare occasions, I do write stories with these couples. I haaaaaate chick flicks.

MUSIC-ISTICS:
Taking Back Sunday. Brand New. Thursday. Bright Eyes. Death Cab For Cutie. Story of the Year. Three Days Grace. Hoobastank. Seether. Something Corporate. Yellowcard. Further Seems Forever. Yay, stuff like that!

CURRENT-ISTICS:
