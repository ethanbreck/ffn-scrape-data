Not sure what to write either, except that I'm just a shade past a certain landmark birthday (the one where you wish there were more numbers between thirty and thirty nine). As you might have guessed, though, I really don't believe in acting my age.

I live in England but am actually Scottish, which makes life rather interesting for certain sporting events.

I've been writing fanfic for more years than I can remember, and from the different series I've written for, you'll guess that I'm a bit of a TV nut too. Those who know me would just say nut - period.

I'm sure there's loads more I could say, but I think you've suffered enough just reading this. Thanks for dropping by...
