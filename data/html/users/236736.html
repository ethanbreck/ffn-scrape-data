Most of my fanfiction is for the cancelled show UC Undercover. I have also written a couple of Ardeth Bay stories as well. I'm an Oded Fehr fan and will watch him in anything. I also enjoy reading, watching videos and some TV.

I am currently rewriting my UC Undercover stories and I will try to get them posted soon. I will most likely post them under a new pen name of Lady Elaina.
