Author has written 19 stories for Harry Potter, and Card Captor Sakura.

It's incredible how much time has passed since I first published a story on FF. I was very young when I first tried writing something, and my changes in age, experience and viewpoint have had a huge influence on my writing since then. I still write now, taking the odd evening or afternoon to jot a few paragraphs down. My nearly exclusive fandom, HP, is still my first and primary love.

To everyone who's PMed or reviewed in the past few years, thank you. You're deserving of an update on statuses, so please see the below. Know that I now only publish stories that have been completed, but am always happy to share tidbits of completed chapters upon request. Just PM if you'd like a sneak-peak at any of the ones in progress. Also, prompts are wonderful, so if you have any requests, I always enjoy the challenge.

Story Update:

Those in progress/unpublished:

1: DAM Sequel: The sequel is still being written; I'm roughly a third through, largely due to a major re-writing of a significant plot point about a year back and the decision to include supplementary chapters. The sequel is an even mix of mystery thriller/romance and my first real attempt at a detective plot.

2: SBHG/RLHG: I think it's some kind of rule that if you write in the HP fanverse long enough, you must give the time-travel trope a try. I'm finally doing it, but sans the time-turner! I'm on the fourth chapter, and I've outlined sixteen plus an epilogue. It's set in the mid 1980's, and I'm trying my best to not make it too gloomy. It's hard, though, because I keep listening to the Interstellar soundtrack when writing, and that's just asking for sad things. I'd like to have this finished by Christmas and posted in early 2018. We'll see, though.

3: Teddy/Lily: A not-quite sequel to Missed, I nevertheless liked the AU I created there for Teddy that I felt the need to give him is own stand-alone. I'm working on this very slowly, so don't expect to see anything on this for quite a while.'

4: Teddy/Delphini: This is crazy, and it quite literally popped into my head while driving home. I had to pull over at an exit to jot down my thoughts, I was so worried I would forget by the time I made it home. SO looking forward to writing this out.

On Hiatus/Up for Adoption:

1: Unlikely Story: Poor story; I really felt for it at first, but once I started back on DAM 2, I lost all of my inspiration for it. I'd like to think I'll get back to it one day, but in the meantime, if anyone would like to adopt it-- please feel free. I'd love to read it when you're done, so please PM me!

2: TSU RAI KU: I think the only way I'll pick this up again is if I re-watch/read CCS; or if I get back into the fandom and read some of the newer fics out there. I do have an outline for the story, and it's fairly near it's completion, so if someone would like to pick it up, just PM me and I'll share what I have mapped out.
