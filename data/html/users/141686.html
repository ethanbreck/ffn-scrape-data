My excuses for not writing lately:

2. www.virtualhogwarts.net - It's pure genious. Look me up, I'm Morgan Madding, Gryffindor House Third year

Ok, So I want to say some things about my stories before you get all freaked out. Definitely some of them are better than others. The Better ones are in bold.

Fushigi Yugi
-The Freak Gasoline Fight Accident: Based on that movie...erm...What was it called?? Oh yeah, Zoolander. I'm never going to finish it, but it's a strange read. I was like 12 when I wrote it..so yeah.
-Tasuki's Date: So long ago I barely remember it...good golly. It's rather pointless and now that I go back and look at it, it sucked.
-The Evil Imposter: My very first fanfic ever. I was like ten. Okay, I was eleven. Don't read it, it sucked.
-Tasuki's Daughter: This is a well thought out story about Tasuki's Daughter Jielain. A reincanation story.

Inuyasha:
-On My Own: I intend to finish this some day, really. Honestly. When? No idea. Probably whenever I get my paws on more new episodes of Inuyasha and rediscover the actual story. I have the plot set out for what's gonna happen, but I've moved away from that particular anime at this point...

Other:
-Minako's Day: I wrote this in eight grade. I was like 12. It sucked.
-The Kiss: Teh=sucked. (I stole that Teh=suck thing from my friend Brock and probably screwed it up...)

Harry Potter:
-That Awful Boy: One of my favorite stories I've written, it's very close to my heart and very well researched. Probably my most popular story.
-Five Minutes: My most recent story, it's sad but touching. Also close to my heart as Neville is my favorite character. Future Neville stories are planned, especially now that I've discovered the possibility of a Nev/Luna ship...

I'm seventeen now, so that gives you an idea of how long I've been writing. (Since like...er...forever?) If I left you a mean review on one of your stories, don't take it personally, I'm just mean sometimes. No really, I try to use my wisdom to help other people improve their writing. Here's an apology in advance (or if I already did...a late one...) Feel free to critisize my stories as well, my ego could use some deflating...
