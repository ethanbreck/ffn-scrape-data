Oh, yeah, and maybe a sequel to "The Prince and the Princess..."

Now I'll list some of my favorite Japanese words: ^_^

-kusotare [me] (person made of crap, in the "nicest "form...)
-fuzakeru [na/yo] (stop kidding around, be serious)
-omae/temee/kisama (all pretty rude ways to say "you)
-saa? (meaning "who knows")
-betsu ni (nothing, not really)
-jigoku e ochiro (go to hell)
-kuso/chikusho/che (shit or damn[it])
-urusai/uruse/yakamashii (all pretty rude to say "shut up")
-sou ka na (I think this means "is that right?")
-mochiron (of course)
-joudan ja nai yo (you've got to be kidding, this isn't a joke, are you kidding)
-muda da (it's useless)
-zama miro (take that, you get what you deserve, kiss my ass!)
