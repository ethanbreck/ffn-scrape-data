December 18 2004

Well, HOLY CRAP. It's been...TOO DAMN LONG since I last touched ANY of my stories and, sadly enough, it doesn't look like that hiatus is coming to an end any time soon. *sighs* I am back from the move and have settled in again with internet, computer, all my things, etc etc. Actually, I've been settled for a while. *groans* I'm just at a COMPLETE lack of inspiration currently. I am not abandoning any of my fics and I will be working on getting a revised version of Sundial back out but until then... o; Gomen nasai.

I would like to thank the NINETY-FOUR people who see fit for me to be on their 'Favorite Authors List'. I am truly shocked at this. Thank you all SO much. I'll try not to disappoint in the future.

AN IMPORTANT NOTE, HOWEVER: As of late, my interest in the YuGiOh fandom has been...waning. I haven't watched the show in months, haven't read anything on it as of late other than the WONDERFUL work my onee-chan Moonchild DJ has produced and quite frankly I can't find any desire to further indulge int he series. Sad but unfortunately true.

Now, I'm not saying that I don't LIKE YuGiOh anymore, I do! I just... it might take a while to build up the desire to write anything for it again seeing how my interest has been swaying more towards the Yami no Matsuei fandom and, to a lesser extint, Fullmetal Alchemist. I apologize to all my still-loyal fans and promise to try and continue with the stories that I have but new ones might come first. Again, gomen nasai.

PS: My email address is jis_bubble@hotmail.com Do NOT email the emilu@wolf-web.com address!! DON'T!!!
