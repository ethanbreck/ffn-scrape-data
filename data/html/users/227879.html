NOTICE FOR READERS: I am currently involved in several original fiction writing projects as well as enrolled university. As such, I'm finding it difficult to maintain a regular update schedule. Most stories, therefore, have been put on hold although they may be updated from time to time as I find a chance to write. Some have been removed, however, as I've either lost interest or am no longer having fun in writing them. I'm sorry for all of you who are eagerly waiting next chapters to some fics. If you email me a poke or two I may make a particular fanfic a higher priority (this has already happened with Be There). I will be keeping all stories on my computer, in case I want to take them up again. You're welcome to bother me to read them, or to try and get me to repost ones that are down. Thanks for your patience and understanding.

Stories I'm Currently Writing:

Wind Garden:

A Class of the Titans fic. Sequel to Skin Deep. Just when things seemed to be wrapping up, Chronus pulls a trick out of his sleeve. Now Herry has to save the world by himself while the other six heroes try to figure out who is keeping them prisoner, and why. It doesn't exactly help that nearly everyone has forgotten them... including each other? (Active)

Symbiotic:

A House M.D. fic. Wilson is called away to a conference, leaving House to face the daily stupidity of the hospital alone. But because of this, House comes to realize just how much he gets out of their friendship, and wonders what Wilson could possibly get in return. Features a cardiologist and stuffed toy as Wilson stand-ins. (On Hold)

An Hour after Dawn:

A Fruits Basket fic. Kureno rises each day at dawn to watch the sun rise. As the sky grows lighter, with pinks and golds, he thinks of Akito and an earlier time, before darkness fell on the Sohma family. A story of beginnings, transformations and meetings, as each member of the zodiac grows and finds their place in an ever forward-moving world. (On Hold)

Hearts Change:

A Yu-Gi-Oh! fic. An impending war between the Shadow Realm and the real world. A sacred balance has been thrown off, affecting the monsters of the Shadow Realm. Consequently, the Change of Heart has assumed a lead role for the forces wish conquest of the human world. Ryou is thrust in the middle as the Change of Heart plans to use him to achieve its goal: opening a portal between the two worlds. As more pieces fall into place, the conflict becomes more than a simple wish for domination. As friendships are tested and feelings strained, can Ryou find enough strength to be saved? (On Hold)

Wonderland:

A Yu-Gi-Oh! fic. A/U. In a world of magic, prophesy, demons and heros,eight individuals find themselves walking a shared path of destiny. Not knowing one another, their greatest test seems merely to discover what lies ahead. But when each is hunted, can unity ever truly last? Join the journey of a mage, assassin, spirit cleric, half-demon, forest sprite, archer, pathfinder theif, and mercenary to find out what this Wonderland desires from them, and if they can survive such a demand... (On Hold)

Be There:

A Saint Seiya fic. The Knights of Athena have fought long and hard for the right to attack Sanctuary and stop the corrupt Master. However, Sienna feels they need a last challenge, to make sure their skills are honed. Her plans are cut short when a cell from Sanctuary learn of this 'vacation' and plot a few tests of their own. The Knights learn a few things about themselves, and a few things they never knew. But to believe it, you have to be there... (On Hold)

Completed Fics:

A Fairy Tale:

A Class of the Titans fic. Based on the story The Bird, The Mouse and The Sausage by the Brothers Grimm. Just because Chronus has faded into the background doesn't mean he isn't waiting for his chance to rid himself of seven pesky heroes. Jay, Odie and Neil learn this the hard way, and Theresa isn't there to warn them to watch their backs, don't open the door to strangers, and beware strange women.

Skin Deep:

A Class of the Titans fic. Chronus has tried many things to break up the chosen heroes and stop the prophecy from coming true. This time, he decides to prey on Neil's ancestor's weaknesses that have carried on into him, and he couldn't have picked a better time. Feeling a little unappreciated and left out, Neil's ready for someone to sweep him off his feet. He just has to remember that sometimes, beauty is only skin deep.

One Day:

A Fruits Basket fic. Hatsuharu Sohma has been travelling for several days, wandering as he usually does. When he passes a rather nice looking rock several times, he decides that a good sit is in order. Of course, as he pauses Haru's mind wanders. But where it goes is unexpected, and full of fluff. There's more to Hatsuharu than a split personality and a poor sense of direction.

Shadow Friend:

A Yu-Gi-Oh! fic. During the Duelist Kingdom, a new competitor arrives seeking the title of 'King of Games'. However, his purpose may not be so black and white. It would seem the power of the Millennium items is far more significant than anyone may have guessed before, so much that they link directly to their power source. Everything becomes a game..

Worth:

A Digimon fic. Daisuke isn't the happy-go-lucky soccer star he seems to be. Inside, he wonders if what he's done is significant, especially when he measures it up against the older Digidestened. When he fails to truly see his value, it's up to his friends to show him what life is really worth.

Fics that have been Removed:

Mortal Affairs - I've lost my direction with this, plus it's turned into a present giving monster that deals more with oc's than with cannon characters. I haven't even thought about it in probably a year, let alone tried to write something for it.

Points of a Circle - I made the mistake of showing this story to a club specializing in workshopping creative writing. Some people there are just too thick to get that not everything has to be totally original or written in the style of every other book in the world. Because of this, the story's died for me. I do have notes for the remaining chapters, but I'm not sure if I'll ever come back to it. This fic may be reposted.

I think everything else is pretty self-explanitory ; I love reviews, emails, anything of that sort that provides feedback. As I said, my dreams are to become an author someday, and every author starts somewhere. I just hope you readers (and reviewers ~) enjoy what I have to offer!
