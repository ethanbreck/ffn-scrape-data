Writer History

I started writing fanfiction shortly after discovering FF.net a LONG time ago. I was active for a while, writing AAML and the occasional H/Hr. Which are awful and I had purged from my profile. After an embarrassing OC story, I resolved to stay within canon and at the same time discovered BTVS/Angel thanks to a friend. Ironically, I love reading B/A and W/X but enjoy writing B/X much more. I don't mind NC-17 fic and have a soft spot for B/G. Don't flame me now, but the fics I have read have been realistic. I don't SHIP them per se, but the fics are good. Spuffy is mostly annoying with most writers turning the ship into a huge Mary Sue fest. I did write a B/X fic with the tiniest smidgen of B/S. That was a request for the people who got me into BTVS.
