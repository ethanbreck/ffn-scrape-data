Author has written 11 stories for Harry Potter.

I am a published fantasy writer and illustrator and love doing art and writing about Draco & Harry when I'm not working on original creations.

5/1/2016: I am pretty much retired from working on fandom creations and am now focused on doing original work. All my HP fanfic and fanart, including H/D art, is posted on AO3 (user Naadi there too) and I highly recommend finding me over there instead of here since the work there is the most recently edited versions - and I can't show my art on this site! I do still love to get reviews from this site, though I can rarely answer them now, and I do very much appreciate new readers here. You can also e-mail me directly at naadi_moonfeather@hotmail.com.

1/27/2010: A note on Checkmate translation requests: Currently I am getting several requests a month to translate Checkmate into many different languages and I deeply appreciate the offers and the interest in that story. However, for many reasons, I am NOT allowing any new translations of Checkmate to be started or for the story to be posted or archived anywhere other than where I have posted it myself. Thank you for respecting my wishes on this matter!

1/8/2007: Now that Checkmate is finished, I may not be posting as much here, especially since I've mostly been doing fanart, but I am very active with the HP community on LiveJournal. Thank you so much to all my readers here!!

6/15/2006: Wow. Checkmate has been finished for 2 months now and I can't say thank you enough to everyone for their incredible response, reviews and lovely words about the ending, or the story as a whole. Checkmate has over 319,000 hits and just passed 1500 reviews. THANK YOU ALL SOOO MUCH!

5/26/2004: Yay! Checkmate is two years old on this site today! I am completely thrilled and blown away by the response to this story. Thank you so much to everyone who has reviewed here! (And no, I'm not dead! But I just finished my Master's degree a couple of weeks ago, and due to the related work involved in that, haven't been able to work on fanfiction.) As of today - Checkmate has had over 26,000 hits since chapter 8 was posted, and has 697 reviews. Huge hugs to all of you for that!

5/26/2003: Checkmate is one year old! Love and hugs to all my ff.net readers - over 7750 hits since chapter 8 was posted. Many thanks and best wishes!
