I'm Chelsey, and I'm basically just a seventeen-year-old girl from Coon Rapids, Minnesota. I'm blonde. Blue-eyed. Easily annoyed and angered. Procrastinator to the extreme. Defensive. Controlling. Final Fantasy addict, not recovering and not seeking rehab. Pansexual. Slash/yaoi writer, obviously. Manipulative, if you fuck with me. Loves different cultures and languages. Independent. Loving friend. Nice, to an extent. Honest, in a biased sorta way. Huge goof. Amused easily and appreciates humor in many forms. Dominating. Tries to see the beauty in everyone, though it can be difficult. Agnostic.

Yeah, I know. I fucking rock.
