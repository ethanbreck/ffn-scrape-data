Author has written 2 stories for Stargate: SG-1.

My father recently introduced me to a science fiction show called Stargate SG-1, and I quickly became a big fan. I started reading fanfiction for it, and after a while decided to write one of my own. Or, more precisely, I got a story idea in my head and it wouldn't leave me alone until I wrote it. There is more on the way, as soon as I can somewhat cement some of the ideas I've currently got.

As far as my stories go, there's only a couple of things you should know:

1. The stories I most like reading are hurt/comfort/smarm stories with a bit of humor mixed in, and most of the stories I write are therefore likely to be along the same lines.

2. Unlike what appears to be the vast majority of the Stargate fandom, I actually like the character of Jonas, despite his unpleasant introduction and the horrible haircut he donned for the season 7 episode Fallout, and any stories I write that include him will therefore reflect that. So, if you're looking for anti-Jonas propaganda you've come to the wrong girl.

My Stories:

Safe Harbor: While this was originally intended to be a much longer story, I ended up stopping after the second installment, because I was having a hard time continuing and I realized that it had come to a nice conclusion all on its own.

The Past Reunited: Eventually this is going to be a kind of action/adventure story, though the first chapter mainly delves a little deeper into Daniel's past.
