I am currently a very busy postdoctoral researcher. Thanks for visiting my little page here; hope you get a chance to read my fics! My first fic ever is called DragonTek, a five-part crossover of Tekkaman Blade and Dragonball-based characters (i.e. the descendants of the characters we knew from DBZ). It even has a Rurouni Kenshin flavor to it. My second fic is Nightmare, which takes place over one year after the end of Tekkaman Blade II. This is the darkest fic I've written, and its a lot more complex than DragonTek. I'm close to finishing the story (at least I think so!). Check this one out too if you have time! And check out the fic 'Tekkaman Z', written by KajitaniEizan (who's listed in my Favorite Authors Section). It's the most hilarious Tekkaman fic I've ever read.
