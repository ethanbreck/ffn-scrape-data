Hi everyone I know and do not know out there!

My greatest hobby is writing stories of all kinds. My favourites to write in are Tekkaman Blade, Samurai Deeper Kyo and Lord of the Rings, with a few trips to other fandoms, like Star Wars, etc.

My other hobbies are collecting comicbooks such as Darkminds and Warlands. Pat Lee, you rock! Jo Chen, your drawings are awesome! But I also collect comics like the X Men.I'm a huge AD&D fan, which I play with five guys, who are my best friends as well.

I like manga a lot too and I love Ghost in the Shell, Street Fighter 2, Fushigi Yuugi, The Guyver, Tekkaman Blade, Dragonball Z, Gundam Wing, Escaflowne and Gensomaden Saiyuki. My newest manga obsession is Samurai Deeper Kyo, Inuyasha, S-CRY-ed, Fullmetal Alchemist. .My fave computer games are Baldur's Gate, Prince of Persia and the LOTR games.

One further note: Constructive critisism is always welcome, flames are not! If you can't say anything nice about something or someone, then you better say nothing at all!
