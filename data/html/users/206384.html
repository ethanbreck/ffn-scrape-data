Hello out there, my name's Konig15. Why? Cause there were 14 people before me "Konig" hotmail account. Since I think if ain't broke, don't fix it, I'm keeping it.

What do I write? Well, next to nothing, but I'm in a college dorm and I don't do something, my room will be overwhelmed by plot bunnies. O the horrors!
I guess I'll be writing in the Chrono Trigger section for a while (read: really, really, big story in production). In fact, I found this place looking for CT fanfiction.

When I write, I aim to please and do quality work. So if you review, be honest (erring on the side of cruel if need be) but be thorough, as it's the only way I'll learn. I'm a history major, as such, historical themes will be in my longer works. And I can be quite dark.

Here I should mention I HATE slash, again. It ruins everythin it touches. I'm less opposed to yuri, but not by much. Because I believe in fair play, I present the Konig15 Guarantee: I, Konig15, will never, ever, write an oncreen homosexual relationship. Ever read Invisible Man slash? I did, once. And frankly, besides being unnecessary, it got both men completely out of character. Interestinly enough, I've never seen Evil Dead slash before. I guess Ash is too macho to be gay. That and anyone who writes him as such is gonna have their ass kicked by the rest of us ED fans. I pity the fool who tries.
