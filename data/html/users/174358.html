I live in the UK but my home planet is very far away. I am unemployed and looking for work. For my ideal job, I want to be a level boss and work my way up to final boss and when I die I want to possess a black spiky suit of armour and haunt a mausoleum! Video games remind me of home so I love writing fanfiction about them. My favourite games are currently Dark Savior, Xenoblade Chronicles, Earthbound and NiGHTs.
