Odds are good that you're here because I've reviewed one of your stories. I fear that my own contributions to the literary environment are rather lacking, particularly recently. Not that I don't have fanfic ideas... it's just that A: they're often about series that I've never even seen/read, inspired by the skillfully written derivations of others. And B, most of my writing efforts have been channeled into original fiction for the past several years.

That said - please, tell me what does and doesn't work in my stories. I want to write well.

Please note that none of the stories posted before 2010 are being updated. In some cases, I've even forgotten where I was going to begin with. I tend to lean towards one-shots, anyway...

I may, however, write follow-up one-shots.
