Author Profile

Name : Teri LK
Gender : Female
Age : twentysomethings

It occurred to me the other day that I have been on this site for over 10 years, and while the writing and reviewing stopped for various reasons the reading hasn't yet. A small constant that is a large comfort, one I didn't realise I held on to.

So thank you for that, FFNet. For helping me stumble onto the words of those both equally and more passionate about the stories characters and universes we can't forget. -May 2012

Note: Yes I've deleted my first story and might possibly delete some of the others. No, it wasn't that difficult I was never going to finish it and it wasn't really going anywhere. It wasn't about growing up necessarily, I am at my core the same person I've always been even as the kid who started out on this site. However so much of the optimism and unbridled affection I wielded in my creations has been burnished, refined, hidden under a harder exterior I've adopted to endure and continue to endure this chaos we call life. I don't know if I'll reach that point again, I don't know how long it will take me to start creating again. But in my efforts to attempt to get back to that kid I'll simply leave you with a quote from Dak'kon, a character in Planescape: Torment.

"Endure. In enduring grow strong."
