Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, Hobbit, and Pacific Rim.

Um...mmm...

Right, well it's been years,literally, this i last attempted to write anything here... You should know it's nothing personally to everyone here at ff.net. I simply began to live a very hectic life, full of unfortunately, growing up. Which sucks. Does anyone else wish they could be like Peter Pan?

I live in dreary England, in a small village a few miles outside the town of Gloucester. All Harry Potter fans should know what our accent sounds like, after listening to a few sentences from Hagrid. Yes we do over pronounce our R's. Call it a local defect,we also add 'Mind' to the end of almost every sentence.

I'm also a huge Motorcycle racing fan, in which i have to say Nicky Hayden played a huge part in my enthusiasm for the sport. As i followed his and his brothers early racing careers in the AMA Series and later followed Nicky to Moto GP.

I'm also a Valentino Rossi fan, but hell who isn't if they follow the sport? The man's a god on a motorcycle.

Anyways i'm pretty much a fan of fantasy and sci-fi, when i write or read for that matter. I like to be able to bend the rules abit and escape from the real world every once in a while. I love the Harry Potter Books, though there were a few things i disliked and found too predictable in the later books. Anyone else miffed at the fact the elder Weasley Brother's seem to be missing from the films?

Big fan of Lord Of The Rings, books and films. Who isn't, right?

I'm actually in the mind set to take all three of these stories down and rewrite them. Ever read back over something a few years later and cringe out how awful it sounds? Yeah that's how i feel about these.

Visions Of Dust- I'm a big Harry Potter fan and i'm aiming to rewrite this chapter and keep updating regularly. This story will follow a rough time line starting from the end of BOOK 4 and will centre mainly around Bill Weasley and my OC. There will be some twists in the story as there are a few things in the JK Rowling's books i just didn't like and thought were slightly predictable... This story has now been removed for a complete rewrite!

The Call Of Home- *update 8/4/2015, taking a short break from this story for a few weeks, as i have a litter of puppies that need my full attention. I'm still writing, but now finding time in short supply and can't promise updates. Will return to Middle Earth as soon as i'm able. Remember patience is a virtue.* My new story, have several chapters in the pipeline which i will post regularly, if and only if i'm feeling brave and the story seems to be relatively well received here. Centre's around my own character Lili, with healthy doses of Thorin Oakenshield, Fili and Kili, because i happen the love these three characters in particular.

I've also been working on an Aliens story, which i might post depending on how brave i'm feeling...
