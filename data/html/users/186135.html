Author has written 18 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Silmarillion.

Considering that you have come here, you probably want to know something about me...

Proud geek-nerd-dork, as unladylike and unelvish as anyone can be. Female, twenty-something-ish, German. Rather irrevocably in love with Tolkien's creation and creatures, which never fail to inspire me.

I have not published anything here in quite a while, as should be easily obvious from the date stamps on the stories, and moved on to greener pastures, so to speak. All my more recent Tolkien fics can now be found at I like to think that I've come a long way.

To those here who reviewed, watch me or have added my stories to their favourites - thank you for the support. It means a lot.

-- Elleth
