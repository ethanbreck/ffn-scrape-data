I,Kerensky storyline:

Complete, the PDF version is available on my deviantArt account.

Zoids Storyline:

Off We Go Into The Wild Blue Yonder: a Zoids fanfic about a girl dreaming to fly for Helic Republic, but sucked into a political turmoil that threatened to destroy the Helic Republic from its very core. Completed in Zoids section.

The Exile Prince: Sequel to Off We Go... Five years later, Saskia found a life far away from the Helic - Guylos conflict. But she couldn't find peace. Another conflict, the Neo Zenebas and Guylos Empire special force Rottiger soon ravaged her world, piggybacked by opportunists that remorselessly took advantage of her misery. Complete in Zoids section.

Blazing Aces Chronicles: Lost Country: Third story in Saskia's storyline. While doing a simple SAR mission for the Backdraft Organization, Saskia and her beloved zoid got sucked into a wormhole, and ended up in an alien territory called The Inner Sphere. Here, without the Magnesser Field of Planet Zi, the Gilvader was castrated from its power. Saskia must find a way home to Planet Zi, 60,000 light years away at the opposite side of the Milky Way. Zoids X BattleTech crossover, currently in progress.

Blazing Aces storyline:

Blazing Aces Chronicles: The Early Years: a story of the descendants of Duke Gideon Braver Vandenberg as they rebuild the Blazing Aces. The story centers around Trystan and Caelia Vandenberg, the grandchildren of Duke Gideon, growing up from unassuming teens into tough mercenary commanders of the Blazing Aces. Rework of old stories. Complete in Mechwarrior/Battletech section.

Blazing Aces Chronicles: Lost Country: Second part in Blazing Aces storyline. Trystan Vandenberg was sold to a pirate band as a slave. All hopes were lost, until opportunities presented itself in a form of an alien girl with a battlemech nobody had ever seen before. Now armed with a powerful weapon, Trystan plunged into a game of vengeance to regain his honor, dignity, and the Vandenberg heirloom that was robbed from him. Zoids X BattleTech crossover, currently in progress.

Other stories:

Escape from Carver V: spinoff of I, Kerensky. It tells the story of Evee, Parker's love interest, as she desperately tried to raise an army to save her lover from the Jade Falcons. MechWarrior/BattleTech section.

We Are Cavaliers: spinoff of I, Ke2ensky. Being dumped by Parker, Evee led her unit the Ridinghood Cavaliers through thick and thin, greed, deception, political schemes, until in the end only the strong (and the loyal) survived. MechWarrior/BattleTech section.

Rubber Match Heisei Series: Jeremy's show as Solaris mechwarrior. It was 3067, and not only Jeremy had to fight mech jocks in the arenas, but also his own team mate, underground mafia, and - what else? - Word of Blake. Complete in MechWarrior/BattleTech section.

Road to Sedition: spinoff of I, Ke4ensky. Rocher, the young brash Davion mechwarrior, was thrown into a convoluted conflict in the world of Callone VI in the Outworld Alliance. Also a sequel to My Father's Friend by Ulquiorra9000. MechWarrior/BattleTech section.

Knights In FerroFibrous Armor: a compilation of short stories in MechWarrior/BattleTech section, ranging from Fourth Succession War, Clan Invasion, Invasion of Sarna March, up to Blake's Jihad. This is mainly my short stories from college, but other authors (Kat Wylder, SulliMike23, and Zmuh11) had kindly contributed to the pile, and I'm eager to see more contributions.

Eagle Screams and Sword Clashes: a collaborative effort with Kat Wylder in MechWarrior/BattleTech. It tells a story of two mercenary units, the Screaming Eagles and the Scimitars, as they went against each other in the world of Irian, Free World League.

I, Feddie: a dark insight of defected Federation soldiers as they fight a losing battle in Gundam Sentinel atmosphere.

There's No Mistletoe In Macross: a one-shot ficlet in Macross universe, a story of Hikaru and Misa as they spent a cold Christmas together.

Macross 2012: Insurrection: another try at Macross. This time, the heroes of Space War I were caught in the wake of conspiracy within UN Spacy. They had to use every resources they had to unveil the mystery that could destroy everything they had worked so hard to build. Robotech/Macross section.
