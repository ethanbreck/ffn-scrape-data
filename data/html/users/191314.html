Formerly Colibi

After a break of 11 years, I started writing again in 2015. Long gone was the college girl...

Although I now write in English, French is my first language. I have no plan to delete my French fics, but no plan to update them.

I am still a fan of Harry Potter, and over the years I discovered Torchwood, Doctor Who, Sherlock, and Twillight fics. I like reading crossovers, and looong stories.

Even my kids love Harry Potter and Doctor Who ( and LOTR, and The Hobbit, and SW) I am raising geeks! My 7yo already invents his own HP stories :)

I am not the big fan of Ginny and Hermione as I was before, but I still don't like Ron, and never really had. Luna is actually one of my favorite character, as well as Charlie, Bill, and the Twins.

I have a very busy life, but I like to write, when I have the time. I like writing Bellatrix, and Hermione too. When I write Ron, he is either a Jerk or I kill him, or both.

I don't really care about post Epilogue compliance, as most of my stories are AU after GOF. I have yet to write in other fandoms, the Potterverse is the one I am most comfortable with.

I also post on AoOO under the pen name Eriam. Most of my fics are over there too.
