Author has written 69 stories for Men In Black, Ninja Turtles, Planet of the Apes, A-Team, and Doctor Who.

If you've noticed that A-Team stories are disappearing, fear not. It's actually a good thing.

After a 5 year break, I have gone back to my beloved series to revamp it in light of all I have learned by working with publishers and professional editors. If you read it before, you will probably be amazed by how much it's changed (for the better). If you haven't, I invite you to start with Scars of War.

Regarding missing DW stories, they won't be back. They are in the process of being published and had to be taken down in their fanfiction version.

In light of Moffat's stamp on the fandom, and Chibnall now seeming to follow in his footsteps, I've officially retired from DW. In what I've already written, I consider myself a Classic writer who dabbles in New. I hold the Serials and Big Finish mainline as canon and pick and choose from the novels/comics/etc. My list is subject to change, but doesn't change much: 8,10,7,11,12,4,5,2,9,1,3,6. No comment on John Hurt, but I can give you an earful on my feelings about Moffat if you get me started. You'll see a lot of 8/Charley from me, as they provide the only canon "I love yous have been exchanged" relationship.

Feel free to contact me, esp TAT fans. I'm always looking for like-minded writers and readers for discussion and brainstorming.

-Shara
