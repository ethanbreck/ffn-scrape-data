12-6-08

I'm KASH, just KASH; always have been and always will be. I've had this account--and this pen name for that matter--for about as long as I've been on the internet, so to change it would really feel absurd to me.

I've abandoned this place off and on many, many times in the past, but as per nostalgic whimsy I'm trying to see about making a come-back. I cannot say if I will ever update the one remaining fic here that withstood the years of fanfiction purging whilest the site bounced back and forth from new staff and new rules, but I do hope to try posting some new content at the very least. I honestly DO miss writing and, equally so, meeting fellow writers and giving them interesting things to read.

Since my mind is a liter cesspool of strange and off-kilter ideas, I hope to provide interesting materials to add to this community again.

Cell's Breakout is an old mofo' of a fic, so before you try reading it, please keep in mind that it is a piece of crap. =D Kthnx.
