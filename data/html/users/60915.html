Wow, it's been a while since I last signed in. My enthusiasm for some of the more plotless animes(DBZ) has died down a bit, but I do still plan on writing Gemini, so don't worry. I've broadened my horizons to several different shows as of now. I'll always be dedicated to Gundam Wing, but now I'm more into Full Metal Panic, Gravitation, Yami no Matsuei, Scrapped Princess, DNAngel, and other such more obscure series. Some of the more well-known animes I am partial to are Rurouni Kenshin, Weiss Kreuz, Ranma and Inuyasha. In the world of literature, Harry Potter fics are the only kind that currently hold any interest for me, especially the Draco trilogy by the extraordinarily talented Cassandra Claire. My two stories have not been updated in over a year, but no, I have not discontinued them, but after careful consideration and much pain(writer's block, closed e-mail addresses, new ideas, etc.), I have decided to do rewrites. I also have a well-developed idea for an Inuyasha fic, so I'll keep you posted on that. If I do get any work done this year, it will be around long holidays because of heavy workload in school, and updates will be few and far-between. I will make an honest effort though. You can e-mail me with any questions and comments. Later!
