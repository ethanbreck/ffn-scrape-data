Author has written 3 stories for Dragon Ball Z/Sailor Moon, Andromeda, and Sailor Moon.

Well this little space will be used for my personal rants and some information that I wish to share about myself.

I am 23 and going part time at my job and planing to go back to college part time. I would like to get a degree in library sciences to keep heading toward my future career goal.

I want to become a published writer eventually and I have five or six original stories that I am working on. I love to write. It is one of my two greatest passions. The other being reading. I hope to one day become an editor for a fantasy publisher.

I currently am a Beta on the Harry Potter website FictionAlley.org. I hope to eventually branch out to other fandoms with my Beta-ing but for now, I am content. I am a slash/femslash/yaoi/yuri advocate. I absolutely love pairings that fall into these categories. That's not to say I don't like some Het pairings I just happen to like slash better. I only have two stories and one poem on fanfiction.net and the two stories haven't been updated in a very long time because my brain won't let me write. I also have quite a few original poems on fictionpress.com. Although those tend to be very 15-year-old goth girl type poems.

A couple of my favorite pairings are as follows:

Harry Potter: Harry/Draco Hermione/Severus Seamus/Dean Ginny/Neville Sirius/Remus Severus/Remus

Andromeda: Dylan/Rommie Beka/Rommie Rommie/Seamus Dylan/Seamus Trance/Seamus Tyr/Seamus

Stargate SG-1: Jack/Daniel Jack/Sam Sam/Teal'c Daniel/Janet

Stargate Atlantis: John/Rodney Elizabeth/Peter Elizabeth/Radek Elizabeth/Teyla Teyla/Aiden Bates/Kavanagh

Firefly: Mal/Simon Simon/Kaylee Wash/Zoe

At the moment I am experiencing what might be called a writers boulder. I really want to continue writing but I can't seem to find the inspiration.

I will soon have more time on my hands so look forward to the chance to pick up my writing again. (31July2008)
