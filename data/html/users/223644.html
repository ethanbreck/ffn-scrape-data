Author has written 7 stories for Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Lizzie McGuire, and American Horror Story.

A word to new readers: If you are going to be reading any of my old work, including the first LOTR story that was written by myself and friends, know this: I started writing on this website when I was 13 years old. I am now 25. My writing today does not equal back then by any means.

If you can get through the bad grammar of the first LOTR story I promise you the rest is just amazing. That story means SO much to me. This was one of my first fan fiction stories I have ever written and it's basically terrible. The grammar is terrible, yes I know. No need to leave multiple reviews on this. Please DO NOT tell me to take down or re-write the first story. It is not happening. As embarrassing as it is, I am proud of it. I am proud of how I went from a fan girl with no skill, to a woman with much experience. I am proud of how that story turned into something so special.

Thank you to everyone who has been so kind and thank you to those who have given me constructive criticism. We are continuing to write new chapters for our LOTR story, so if you are interested, please check it out.

Disclaimer: I do NOT own any of the characters in my Fanfiction stories besides the ones I introduce. All of the other characters are complete property of the original authors. I only wish to write Fanfiction based on others work and I do not take credit for any plots/characters that I expand upon. Thank you.
