Pharmakon: my main website, has all my fics and fic recs etc.
Dreamwidth: I'm on this pretty often - go look me up. :)

There's quite a bit more fiction posted on Pharmakon than is posted here, for various reasons - at last count, around 15 more. That count is definitely way out of date, though, so if you want to read more of what I've written, try my site!

Anyway, I hate writing stuff about myself, so if you want to know any of that check my DW. While I'm here, however, I'd like to take a moment to say a big thank you to everyone on FF.Net who's reviewed my fics, since you're a big part of what makes posting here enjoyable. There was a list here thanking everybody by name, but it was getting a bit long and then FF.Net stopped sending me review notifications at some point in 2007, so I'll just have to settle for saying THANK YOU! :)

May 2011 note on my long absence: The past year and a half have been among the worst in my life. I spent a long time very unwell, and just when I was recovering my Grandfather ended up in hospital for several months before he finally died. In between all of that a huge number of other changes happened, including getting forced out of my old job, moving house, getting a new job and having my car die, all at points where I really could do with not having any more stress. Things seem fairly stable at the moment, but I'm still waiting for the other shoe to drop! Anyway, this note is to explain why I've not posted anything since 2009. Some authors write more when stressed, and others stop writing at all. I belong to the latter group, it seems.
