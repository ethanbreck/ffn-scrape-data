Author has written 39 stories for Ranma, X-Men, X-Men: Evolution, Avatar: Last Airbender, Teen Titans, Batman, Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir, and Teen Titans.

I've been writing fan fiction on and off for well over fifteen years now (ack!). While I've drifted from fandom to fandom over those years, someway, somehow, I always come back to Ranma and Akane.

In addition to fan fiction, I've also sold short original fiction professionally. Two stories of my Dorian Valentine sword and sorcery series has been published in Lore. Go to www.lore-online.com for back issues of their excellent magazine, which publishes a variety of science fiction, fantasy and horror.

Update: I just learned tonight (10/12/14) that Lore is folding. I don't know how long back issues will be available, so if you haven't picked up an issue with one of my stories in it, please do so soon.

Feel free to PM me if you have any questions.
