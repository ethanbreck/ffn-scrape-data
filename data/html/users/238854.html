Author has written 5 stories for Fruits Basket, Inuyasha, and Naruto.

Hello everyone! Thank you for stopping by and reading my stories. Just a quick update.

I have deleted Absolution, the sequel to Don't Let Go. This story started in a very dark place of my mind while I was going through a very dark time in my life. I lost the spark and the vision I had while I wrote and edited Don't Let Go. Then I went on to write a sequel that basically destroyed all the bonds that I loved and adored in the prequel. It is not easy to explain without going into details, but after thinking about it for a long time and attempting to continue Absolution (writing and deleting and writing again), I decided that the need to use fictional characters to expunge my demons was not the right way to go this time. At least not on a fanfic that followed my favorite fanfic so far.

I know Absolution had fifteen followers awaiting for it to continue and I apologize for not doing so. Believe me when I say Absolution was going to kill the Don't Let Go.

Thank you all who have read my fanfics so far. You're all awesome. :)

-Shihori
