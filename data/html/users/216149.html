Hi, I'm Amphitrite and I've been into fanfiction ever since the first time I encountered it back in 1999. I've been writing original stories for as long as I can remember and started writing my own fanfiction when I was nine. Over a decade later, I'm the most active in the Transformers fandom (IDW all the way!), although I also keep up with Marvel Comics, DC Comics, LOTR, Steven Universe, Gravity Falls, Adventure Time, Regular Show, Pacific Rim, Avatar/Korra, TMNT, G.I. Joe, Sherlock, Merlin, House of Cards, and Star Trek.

I write for a variety of fandoms whenever the inspiration strikes. I like to write about exceptional people and their ordinary problems, as well as broken people finding their way to each other. Fanfiction is one of my most favorite things in the entire world, and there is nothing I love more than spending an entire day indulging in a well-written, well-plotted novella-length fic.

A lot of the stuff I have archived here was written quite a while back, like preteen/middle school back, so it's not the greatest. The more recent stuff is most definitely the better stuff. Be sure to check it out!
