In The Service Of The Queen : Sorry, I'm rather in love with this one. By far one of my favorite things I've ever written.

Portrait Of A Lady : I like hearing myself use pretty words, what can I say.

NotSoProverbial Wisdom : Actually based on a line that Harry had in the end of Ginny's Notebook that I felt pleaded to be made into it's own piece. It's simple, it's short, it's quiet, and sorry folks, but that's all she wrote. (Full fic, not a prologue.)

In A Sentimental Mood : I have gotten many comments that this story needs more details and discription. Now, usually, I am the discription queen, but this one can never be fluffed up with that junk. Because nothing else exists. You can't describe every thing else because it doesn't exist. Only their words and the most very basic emotions and gestures are that story. Think of how the characters are. Then think of the style. It's supposed to be that bare. Sort of Hemingway, ok?

Luck, Darling : Ain't Ginny a doll? Scary how characters become balanced and multi-dimensional when you aren't looking. I totally didn't intend for her emotions to become so. . . real.

Demons Of Routine : Can we say Purple, anyone?

Ginny's Notebook : A monstrosity. But a monstrosity people seem to really like. Ah, well.

Fior Ifreann : If you feel terribly cheated by the fact that this will never be updated or completed, mail me and I'll give you all the secrets. Or, the ones I had actually figured out and thought of.
