Author has written 6 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

Sorry for not updating - at university, and it's all got a little hectic... trying my best to update as soon as!

My stories:

Hope: A one-shot about Trunks' thoughts and feelings before he initially travels back in the past to warn the Z-Gang in the DBZ Series of the impending Android threat. I initially wrote this when I was 14, then reposted when I was 18 and it's still here... I don't think it's that good, but it's a nice reference (for me, anyway) to see how my style of writing has - hopefully - progressed. Thinking about making a three-part series with this one - I'll re-edit Hope then vibe on from there.

Coming of Age: My new baby. I'm trying really hard with this one. It's a Pan/Vegeta pairing... I know, I know... just try it, you might like it. I'm trying to adopt a more mature style of writing, and trying to get out of my horrendous habit of rhetorical question after rhetorical question. Hopefully I'll break the mould one day soon.

With An Admission Comes Consequences: A short one-shot written to try and shake the wee writer's block I was struck down with... A cheeky little romance, and my first attempt at anything slightly light-hearted and fluffy. Also my first attempt at a songfic.

Of Earth and Air: I'm trying a slightly different style with this one, for starters, it's an AU fic, which I haven't done before. I'm also trying to make it a little more 'deep', if you get me. My first attempt at a multi-chapter T/P as well... I think it's gonna end up a little angsty and dark, so except a few sanity-saving one-shots to appear as I get deeper into the story.

Waiting: A T/P of sorts. I thought I'd take an alternative approach to our 'ever-favourite' pairing. Also my first 1st POV story.

Forever: My first poem (kinda). It was written as a tribute a friend who I lost, but also can fit well as Chi-Chi or Bulma thinking about Goku.

If you read any of my stories, I would love to know what you think of them - even if you think it's the worst thing you've read all year - you learn from your mistakes, right?

Communities:

A Collection of P/V...: I've set this one up because I feel that there's a distinct lack of P/V fiction out there, and readers for the existing stories! I'm a massive T/P fan like many others, but I feel this couple can work just as well. There are some amazing stories involving this couple out there - initially I found I just had to 'get over' Pan being with anyone other than Trunks, but you'll love yourself for it in the end. If anyone would like to get involved as staff or suchforth, please just message me!

Thanks in advance, and if you ever want to chat, just message me through this!
