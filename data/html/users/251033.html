All right, folks, let's get down to business. The random little poem that once was my bio is now held at Fictionpress, if anyone cares. However, I have now decided to use this space for some much-needed announcements.
Several things have happened since I last updated my bio, one of which is the realisation of this little story that I am about to tell you.

Once upon a time, I had two muses: Common Sense and Writing Ability. When I turned twelve, they ran off and left me for the Bahamas. During the time span between when they left and when they came back, I wrote a little fanfic titled "Operation: PINK." It was a riot -- at least, at the time, when I had a screwy sense of everything.
While this insult to the writing community at large was in the making, Common Sense and Writing Ability fell in love, eloped, and had a child: Good Taste. As soon as he saw what I had done, he was beside himself; literally giving me "a fit of Good Taste." I have now decided that the fanfic (singular) that I have here is an embarrassment to myself and everyone who is associated with me. I am attempting to finish it as quickly as possible and rid it from our memories.
Unfortunately, due to my sudden loathing of this monster I have created, I am suffering from writer's block. It may be because Writing Ability has gone on maternity leave once more. I hope she names her child "Inspiration."

Meanwhile, I am still searching for a way to give this a quick and painless death. I believe it may involve the Pink Bunny wearing concrete shoes and being fed to the giant squid. (You'd have to read the fic in order to understand what I'm talking about.) If anyone else has any ideas on how to kill it (the story or the Pink Bunny), please either e-mail me, leave your idea(s) in a review, or drop me a line at my deviantART account (featured above).
Since that is the only fic I have on here, I am also reluctant to take it down until I have something to replace it with. There are a few I came up with, and I hope you won't mind taking a look at some of them.

Dracones Nunquam Titillandus, Ullo Modo (Never Tickle Dragons, At All): -Harry Potter- A "Draco Diary" fic, conceived as a well-needed apology to the way he is portrayed in "PINK." I know it's been done before, but I hope this will be much more in-character than in others (namely, "PINK"). This will accurately (I hope) cover the entire events of the Harry Potter series, only from Draco's point of view. No romance, at all.

(Untitled): -Yu-Gi-Oh- Yes, I realise that I've never published an anime fanfiction here before, but I've worked on many that never made it onto FF.net. A reformed (or so saith she) Mary Sue is sentenced by a group of angry beta readers to give up most of her powers and family history, and is stuck caring for a very unlikely client -- Ghost Kotsuzaka (Bonz/Bones). As usual, chaos -- and Chaos -- ensues.

I'm sure there was another one, but unfortunately, I forgot it. Maybe I should be hoping for twins: Inspiration and Short-Term Memory.
If anyone would like to see a sample of my REAL writing skills, please refer to my Fictionpressor deviantART account. I would recommend the essays.

Over and out,
~Kay Webb
