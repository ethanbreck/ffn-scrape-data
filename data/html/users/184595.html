just to let people know i am still here.
ok, so you guys have learned that i haven't been doing a good job in managing my time for writing. people have thought that my stories have been ended - they have not. i have a lot of things going on lately, and i just can't write a chapter. i'm really sorry if it bothers you, it leaves you hanging and waiting. i don't know how to fix this dilemma as of yet. hopefully i'll have better updates soon.

i would be deleting some of the fics, probably change it a bit and repost it in the future, i don't know, but there would be some that i won't be updating at all anymore so better get it off the list.

just to keep you glued to what's gonna happen, definite updates will be on queue for the following:
1. what's held within
2. not yet forgotten
3. while eating crunch

to the anime lovers, i'm planning on typing and posting my other funny (i hope) fic on YYH.

i'm sorry if you were mislead into thinking that i have left. i just wanted to redefine my way of writing, and widen my perspective a bit. i'm looking for some kind of change. i guess i haven't found that yet, hence the huge delays and false "rebirths". add to the fact that there are a lot of fanfics entering the HP system. that's pretty busy.

i guess that's about it. thanks to those who included me in their favorites, the ones who added me to their author alerts, i feel so guilty seeing your names on the list here and not doing anything about it. i hope you guys are still there.
