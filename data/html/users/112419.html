Author has written 7 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Mars.

Greetings! My name is Erin, and I am a college senior studying communications journalism. During the summers I work as a receptionist at an oceanography company. Some days, when it's really slow, I write stories and poems. I've taken advantage of my quiet office recently to update some of my Gundam Wing fanfics that I haven't looked at since high school (Shhh! Don't tell anyone!). Thank you to everyone who's been giving me such great feedback on the revised version of "Kismet or Chaos"!

I've spent the last few years studying and working on original stories, but I still have a soft spot for GW fan fiction and may eventually write more. The GW characters grew on me until they became part of the ebb and flow of my conscience. In my opinion, the five Gundam pilots and Relena are some of the most intricate and versatile characters ever created, and they fit so well into a variety of life situations.

Anyway, please enjoy what is on here; I'll continue polishing up my stories in my spare time. Some of my stories, you'll notice, have some serious formatting issues. That's because, back in the day when I uploaded them, the only word processer the site would accept was Microsoft WordPad, which made everything look ridiculously bad. I'm re-submitting everything in Word, so the stories will be much easier to read, and, best of all, aesthetically pleasing. :)

Also, I may publish some fanfics that I wrote years ago but never posted online, including one about Wufei... I also have a few more on Relena and Heero... We'll see what I feel like sharing. ;) If you have any questions, feel free to drop me an email.

Peace,

~Erin

UPDATE (2016):

If you happened to stumble upon this page, welcome! I eventually stopped using this profile and published Gundam Wing fanfics under other pen names. You can find my latest work under the name Relena for President.

Thanks for stopping by!
