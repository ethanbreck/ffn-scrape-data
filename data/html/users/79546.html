Author has written 27 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy VII, X-Men, X-Men: Evolution, South Park, Supernatural, and Star Wars.

I'm a 33 year old chick. My hobbies are writing, reading, playing games (video or tabletop), and failing to achieve postures in yoga.

I write the following fandoms:

Final Fantasy 8: My favorite pairing is SeiferxZell; that's why Seifer picks on Zell, you know. Cold Feet, Do Over, Quirky, and Zephyr are all that pairing. After that, I like Seifer and Squall, because of hot rivalry sex. Road Trip and Squall and the Beast contain them. Three of mine: Cybersex Chatroom of Balamb Boyness, Invasion of the Body Snatching Sniper, and Thanksgiving Grief involved Squall and Irvine romance, which I'm not even that big on, so I have no idea why three of them have that pairing. Irvine and Seifer fall in lust in Painting a Picture. The really random pairing of Seifer and Laguna shows up in Libido which was a giftfic. I would never normally stick those two together. :P

X-men: Nightcrawler is a dashing, indigo-furred, prehensile-tailed stud-muffin and he is my favorite X-men. In Cliched Revenge, he attempts to win over Shadowcat using a little strategic jealousy. This is in the Evolution fanverse. My story Manly Wager involves Wolverine and Beast. Those two were chosen because I thought it'd be incredibly hard to write while staying in character. Wolverine is SO damned straight. I was looking forward to the challenge. I really liked how it turned out and wrote the longer sequel, Caught Escaping.

South Park: Cartman is all kinds of rock. He and Kyle are madly in love in He Noticed and its sequel Tentatively Falling. This is part of a larger arc called Brother's Keeper, but the rest in its series have yet to be written, and may, in fact, stay cooped up in my brain and never make it to FF.net.

Supernatural: Supernatural, in true CW form, is bursting with attractive men which makes it ripe for my overactive imagination and underactive pen. I used to ship Destiel hard, but since the writers have done their damnedest to squash not only the inherent romantic bond, but the entire relationship between Castiel and Dean, I have lost interest. Frankly, the way Dean has acted since season 7, I think Cas can do much better. Still, I wrote a drabble called Warmth about the pairing. Since season 7, I have become much more interested in my pre-season fascination with Dean and Sam. R̶i̶g̶h̶t̶ Wrong and Wrong is an undeniably messed up version of Wincest. My fanfiction magnus opus is not yet on FF.net as it was written for the J2 Big Bang and is not allowed to be posted yet. When it is, it will have artwork and everything. Look for that in mid-July of 2015.
