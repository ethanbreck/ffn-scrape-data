Author has written 9 stories for Harry Potter, Gilmore Girls, Everwood, Dark Angel, Lord of the Rings, and X-Men: The Movie.

I love writing fanfic because it's a great way to delve into the "what ifs" out there. After watching a movie or an episode or reading a book, there are always all these hypotheticals swimming around in my head. That's how all my stories start. Someday I'll work up the courage to write an original fictional story, but until then, I'll work on completing the stories I have here.

I intend on completing With the Tide and The Bird Healer, although I know updates are few and far between given my hectic life. Redemption will remain incomplete as I have lost all inspiration with Gilmore Girls.
