Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.

Thanks for visiting my bio page. If you please look down below you see my creative works that have spawned out of my vast imagination, but I've deleted most of my works because they didn't make a lot of sense after reading it on my own. I don't typically use beta readers because most of them don't read the flow of my thoughts, but I'm working on that. I'm not a journalist by trade, but I do write for fun. I don't plan on making any money from it, nor will I ever seek any currency from someone else's creative works. As of right now, I'm not planning on rewriting Fueled Anger or making a sequel due to not having a lot of free time with everything going on these days. I'm thinking of taking the story down, but its still up in the air.

I do have an idea of a Harry Potter x Avengers crossover (3 parts) called "War of the Seidhr - Weapon". This will be completely AU and trying to stay away from the cliche's that I have been trapped into in the past. This story will take place during the Tri-Wizard tournament and will blend in with the Avengers. There will be no Master of Death nor will there be any bromance with Tony Stark like with other crossovers. It's completely original and hopefully this will work.

Thanks for reading my stories and I hope that you've enjoyed them. I will also let you know that by reviewing it provides me a critique on things that need to be corrected so what I publish will be works in progress.
