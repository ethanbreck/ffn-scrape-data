Joined 04-15-02, id: 200005, Profile Updated: 05-30-09
Author has written 24 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Wrestling, Dragon Ball Z, Legacy of Kain, Resident Evil, Metal Gear, Smallville, and Pirates of the Caribbean.

My stories largely fall between two categories; there's the older humour/parody works and the more recent, more serious ones. The only agendas I have with writing are to tell a (hopefully) good story and to stay true to the characters. Although there is almost always some romantic pairing involved, the pairing itself doesn't really matter to me that much so long as it makes sense and is right for the story.

My approach to writing can vary; sometimes most of the story is planned in advance; but sometimes it's fun just to drop the characters into a situation and let them get on with it. I always enjoy trying to surprise an audience, and it's fun when the outcome surprises me as well.

Outside of writing I'm a qualified engineer, but I've worked a variety of jobs. Hobbies include swimming, watching bad movies, fishing and playing poker. Feel free to look through the stories, pretend you've read them and leave feedback.
