Author has written 16 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Weiss Kreuz.
I love animes, drawing them, writing them, and looking at other people's doing so. Chances are, if it's an anime, I've at least heard of it, I've probably been to a webpage about it, and I might have actually seen it. However, I only write certain animes (they're easier to write if you've seen them and you know the characters). I also love feedback, so if you see any of my stuff, please tell me if you liked it or not and why. I generally write shonen ai, or shoujo ai. I *do* write the occasional straight couple, though generally only when begged to do so. The series that I write are Gundam, Weiss Kreuz, the occasional Sailor Moon, and DragonBallZ/GT. What I have posted at the moment? 4 DBZ/GT and 8 WK. Sad, I know, but I have to actually finish something before I post it!
