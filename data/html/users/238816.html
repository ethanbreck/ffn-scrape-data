Author has written 8 stories for Sailor Moon, and Final Fantasy VIII.

It seems that every year I come back here to rewrite my profile. I'm twenty years old. I'll be twenty one this coming December. I still watch some Anime, Sailormoon is included in that Anime. I'm attending Art School in Denver, Colorado. I used to live in Chicago, IL; but that changed after I moved here to be with my Boyfriend. Most of my fiction is set in one of these two cities, though I might not come right out and announce that. Huh... I really don't know what else to add here...

So, lately I've become a writing fiend. It seems that all I've been doing on me free time, which isn't much, is writing what I can. I've recently started three new stories; Undesired Arrangements, The Palmer House Suites, and Season Six: Sailormoon (O)mega. I've also been thinking about getting into a few other fandoms such as, but not limited to; Buffy The Vampire Slayer/Angel and Final Fantasy VIII. I just feel like I'm getting so much done when I write. I'm accomplishing something, you know? Some of my friends say I'm stretching myself a little too far, but I like having all of these different options to choose from. Also, it seems my ideas are just flowing out of me. I've never had such a healthy well of inspiration in my entire life. I think of something small; like my upcoming FFVIII story and it just sprouts right up. I hardly even have to think before new ideas come to me.

Recent Music Favorites: Right now? I'm listening to everything on my I-tunes player, which is really just too much to list. It's just repeat and shuffle. Just to give you an idea though; Eifel65, Regina Spektor, Vivaldi, The Rolling Stones...
