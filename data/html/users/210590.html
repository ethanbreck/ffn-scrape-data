Author has written 26 stories for Star Wars, Jedi Apprentice, Harry Potter, Chronicles of Narnia, and Doctor Who.

I started writing a lot back in high school and started posting here when I was a sophomore in college. Wow time flies! I am sure you can tell what I enjoy from my writing.

I have three degrees now, having recently received my teaching certification for my state of residence.

Thank you to everyone who read and commented in "Return to Narnia". The story is now completed.

I will be continuing "Return of the Unsung Hero" (Sequel to "The Unsung Hero) and will be adding a few other stories (including one that ties into Newsies). However, I will also be spending a lot of time working on an original novel. I will post here to share information once it is published.

Since they were published at different times and I have gotten questions, there is an order to reading the Unsung Hero Saga: Begin with "Beyond this Night", followed by "Desert Mishaps". Then "The Unsung Hero". There is a sequel "Past, Present and Future" which takes place just before the multi part sequel "Return of the Unsung Hero."
