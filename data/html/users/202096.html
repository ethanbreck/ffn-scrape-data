Author has written 12 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Lord of the Rings, and Criminal Minds.

I love reading :)

The only thing I might love as much as reading, is writing :D

I have been writing one story or another since I was about 10... I started writing in English when I was around 12 (and I like to think that I have improved since then).

I never post anything on fanfiction.net unless it is a completely finished story, because I hate when I start reading a story and then it is discontinued about halfway in. I have quite a few of those on my own computer (and some on paper), and it just would not feel right posting them. This is also why I have so few stories here :D

The one exception (or not really) is "Of Years Gone By" where in fact every chapter can be read a separate story. I do have the last three chapters here finished, so if I ever lose the muse for that timeline, I will post the last chapters and mark it as complete as well. (Although I would not promise I would never again post a chapter in it, just in case the muse would decide to strike again). So far, I have about 30 chapters of it finished, and am still working on it :)

Nowadays, I write most of my stories in first person, because I find it easier to delve into the mind of one person rather than many. For me, it also gives a slightly more structured approach, and makes it easier for me to identify with whomever I have assigned as the main character.

And a warning in general, for all my stories. They will probably (actually, most definitely) contain someone getting hurt. It is of course a less than original idea, but I just love making the characters suffer :) I am a sadist... sorry :)

Feel free to review. I try to take critical reviews that are aimed towards improving my writing to heart, but I will put no thought to outright flames. Also know that I will never stop posting a story due to a lack of reviews, as I'm mainly writing the stories for myself, and just want to share them with you on the off-chance that someone else will enjoy them as well :)

P.S. I am the worst at figuring out what genre my stories should be in.

Most usually, it's hurt & comfort, adventure, friendship and angst. If you squint you may find romance and some attempts towards humor.

I hope you will enjoy my stories.
