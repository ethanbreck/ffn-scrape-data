I live in Canada. I just graduated University with a BA in German and Russian studies. I took a year of creative writing, but it wasn't what I expected. I really love reading and writing, and I do write a lot of the time, but I can never seem to finish anything before I get a new idea and go with that one.

I read all the time, even for my school classes. My favourite books are Harry Potter, the His Dark Materials trilogy, The Mists of Avalon, Gemma Doyle trilogy, historical fiction books by Philippa Gregory and the Georgia Nicolson series.

My favourite tv shows are: Merlin, Skins, Robin Hood (on BBC), The Big Bang Theory, Bones, Make It or Break It, and Fringe

I do like anime/manga, but I don't think I've read/watched any in four years. My favourites are Vision of Escaflowne, X/1999, Sailormoon, Fruits Basket, Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicle and Cardcaptor Sakura.
