Author has written 4 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
NevermindDaria grew up in backwards, Republican Virginia with a deep-rooted cynicism and a keen ability to purge her angst through prose and bad singing. The produce of the former you will find here. A Buffy/Spike shipper to the core, her maniacally betaed Buffy the Vampire Slayer fanfiction tends to cling desperately to the Jossverse, rarely straying from canon or including plot elements that are outside the realm of the show.

Poor ND, now comfortable with her relatively new hobby, has realized through experience the need to take a hard line against flame reviews. Such reviews are best defined as hate mail to the author, railing against the author's opinions and biases, that seek to personally attack the writer more than offer suggestions that might improve said writer's future works. ND with a heavy heart will remove all anonymous flames, and will block users who post signed flames. No exceptions.
