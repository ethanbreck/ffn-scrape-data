Hi!

I've been on here forever, and if you look down at my list of stories you can see that I've drifted away from fanfiction over the past few years. I seem to have moved past my super angsty/emo melodrama writing phase. I've also seemed to have moved past slash. I'm not fond of all of the stories listed below, but I'll keep them up for posterity, and maybe I'll come back to the fold! I still love writing, but who has the time? : )

I work mostly with One-Shots, formerly a big slash writer. I still read it, but am less interested in writing it.

I'm at fictionpress under C.L. Rhodes. Short stories and poetry reside there.

We'll see what happens in the future...
