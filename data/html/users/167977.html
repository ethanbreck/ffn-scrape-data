Author has written 40 stories for Digimon, Avatar: Last Airbender, Kung Fu Panda, Harry Potter, and My Little Pony.

After a long, LONG, LOOOOOOOOONG hiatus, I'm finally back. This account will host all my non-NC-17 stuff.

About my fandoms of choice: I started out writing Digimon fics and also briefly wrote for Avatar (the Last Airbender) and occasionally Harry Potter. Then I watched Kung Fu Panda and instantly fell in love. A great movie like that deserves fanfics! Since then, I also stumbled over My Little Pony and Zootopia, so my muse is fired up, but I need to work on freeing up more time to write.

My fics are or will be mirrored on AO3 ("ChibiRenamon"), FIMFiction (MLP stuff only, "ChibiRenamon"), and most recently Tumblr ("chibirenamon").

A quick note: If a fic has a word count of 300 or less, it's a drabble (for the DigimonDrabbles or the AvatarContest communities on LJ), so it's short on purpose. :P
