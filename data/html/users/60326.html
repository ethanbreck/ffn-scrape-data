Author has written 11 stories for CSI: New York, Grey's Anatomy, Battlestar Galactica: 2003, Stargate: SG-1, Numb3rs, Stargate: Atlantis, and Rookie Blue.

No longer a poor university student. Got a job and a life. Still like to write.

I write to relieve stress and although I’m not very good at it nice reviews have a way of making my day so much better. It’s like when someone smiles at you and you get that warm and fuzzy feeling inside. I also like cliff-hangers it keeps everyone reading. I also apologize for my bad spelling and grammar I’ve always had real problems picking out my own mistakes. If I take too long to get my next chapters up and you feel like you need a sneak peak give me an email and I’ll give you a spoiler it’s the least I can do.
