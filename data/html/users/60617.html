I do writing the old fashioned way, by hand then type it up ^_^; So I might not be the fastest in getting stories up, but I take my time. The first fanfic I ever wrote was that of the Biker Mice from Mars: Remnants Saga, which I am currently rewriting. The original can be found on under By the fans--Fanfictions--Blaze Van Wham. :) Take a look and compare how much my writing has changed. I'm also an artist, so I usually have pictures to accompany parts of my fics. Hope you enjoy what you've read, and remember, i'm not the foremost on fanfic writing, I just do this for fun, so don't be too harsh when reviewing me.
I've had to rehaul a lot of my writings over the past while and I try to - since most of them haven't been touched for 2 or more years and my writing and style has definitely improved - please barewith some of the mary sues and bad stories I still have posted.
