Hello all. I guess you want to more about me. Well why else would you be checking out my bio page? For a good laugh you say, of course a good laugh. I love to make people laugh, cry, scared, happy... In otherwords I hope to envoke the emotions the characters in my stories are feeling. True I over exagerate some of the scenes, but that's the best way to make each and every one of us feel the emotions. Well now that you know why I like to write maybe you want to know more about me. Well... I'm a person. I have two eyes, two ears, two arms and two legs. I get up every morning and go to bed each night with a happy face. Thank God that I'm able to do the things I love. Everything I do is because He lets me have the courage to face this world. I have hair, which is generally found in one style, but if I'm in a quirky mood I do 'weird' stuff to it. I have my 'own' style of clothes and my 'own' stlye of doing things. I'm not like the rest, I behave in such a way that leaves the next guy in line wondering... 'what just happened?'. If you are getting that feeling from reading this bio then good, I have done my job. If not, I'll have to work harder in my stories.

Smile, Live, Laugh, Love and God Bless
