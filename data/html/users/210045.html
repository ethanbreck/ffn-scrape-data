About me:
I'm 16 years old...and I basically like reading one-shots. And writing them. However, if I'm really, really interested in a story plot when reading fics(which doesn't happen much), I usually follow the story because I'm curious about it of course. And I MIGHT write a multi-chaptered fic IF I'm really, very interested in finishing the story. Which I don't have many of, unfortunately for those long-story readers. Sometimes I can be a mushy writer, sometimes I can be humorous, sometimes I can be serious,but I can never do a fic with a solid plot line. So yeah. I know I have many favorite stories; some may be stories I've been impressed by once but don't want to read again. Those I'm going to clear out (sorry!) and I'm going to leave the ones I like to read over again, or in-progress ones that I'm keeping an eye on.
