Current Work: Returning Home and working on a new chapter for Mistresses of Burden.

How I come up with fanfic ideas: All my fanfiction is based on at least one song by a band I listen to or a song I like. I try to fit the idea to the lyrics of the song. So in essence all the stories could be classified as "Song Fics", but I like to think of it more as a joining of music and writing.
