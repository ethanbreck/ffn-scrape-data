Hey everyone!

I've been a long time reader, and just decided to try writing for the first time...never expected the first piece to turn out so long but it's been a fun adventure. Thank you to everyone whose been taking it with me! Though its been slow recently, I'm attempting to get back into it now that my life is a bit more settled once again :)

A little about me: I really appreciate psychological stories that are realistic in their timelines and watching a character overcome obstacles. I work in the healthcare field so I enjoy more of the happy ending fanfics as I get enough of the sad stuff in real life.

I love Coulson from Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D, which is also my current favorite show along with The Office. I know, on total different spectrums, but everyone needs some humor to go along with the angst :)

Some of my other top favorite shows: The Pretender, Doctor Who, DC/Marvel shows, How I Met Your Mother, Haven, Prison Break, and others that I can't think of right now.
