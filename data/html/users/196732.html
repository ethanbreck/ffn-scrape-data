Author has written 10 stories for Lord of the Rings, Valdemar universe, and Forgotten Realms.
BIG IMPORTANT NOTE: Just about everything I have currently posted is old. Old. Very old. And I am quite ashamed of much of it. Just so you know.

I am yet another crazy strange writer non-human. I do more original stuff than fanfiction, as I actually AM serious about writing, so my updates are slower than anything you can possibly imagine. It literally takes months for me to get around to updating. I've decided to finish all stories before posting anymore, so you'll have to wait for my Young Eowyn, Eomer, And Theodred story, and The Life And Times Of Clearbrook story, and my Mary Sue/fangirl harassment one (poor Legolas...shall I have him kill her in the end?) and the sequels to Stuff, and whatever else I feel like...

So, yeah, I write mostly Valdemar and Lord of the Rings fics, with a few ElfQuest ones tossed in there. I also like Tamora Pierce, Lynn Flewelling, Francesca Lia Block, the Bordertown books, Sara Ryan, R.A. Salvatore, Noughts and Crosses, Alanna Morland, Megan Whalen Turner, Simon R. Green, George R.R. Martin, David Eddings, Neil Gaiman, Alex Sanchez, Douglas Adams, Joanne Bertin, Michael Moore (even if he doesn't write fiction), George R.R. Martin...lots of people. A total book nerd, I am. Yay! Oh, and elves. Elves are just too grand.

Oh, what else...I attended the mad smooth your majesty Alpha Young Writer's Workshop last year ( Check it out, it's utterly grand), and plan to again this year, then I'm shooting for Odyssey and maybe even Clarion. Heh, maybe I should finish high school before that one...
