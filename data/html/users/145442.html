Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter, Card Captor Sakura, Tsubasa Chronicle, Sherlock, and Croods.

Well it's been years since I updated this! The last time I updated this I was saying that I had turned 19, yet I'm now 22 so maybe I should keep on top of this a bit better.

I've deleted the story Harry Potter: Film Star as I no longer had any interest in it at all and to be honest had completely forgotten about it. The only reason One Thing After Another is still on is because I intend to finish it even if it doesn't happen until I'm about 90 and no one even remembers who the bands I've used are any more. It will be slow, I need to work up my old love for the Harry Potter stories again. I've not even seen any of the films since the 4th one!

About me: As I've already said I'm in my early 20s. I live in Scotland in good old Britain (God Save the Queen or whatever) and have no taste in music. Trust me, my ipod is mostly full of the old bubblegum pop Britney Spears and Christina Aquilera used to pump out to keep their managers happy and Animaniacs songs. I also have some Linkin Park, Frank Sinatra, Imogen Heap and Andrea Bocelli but I suppose everyone has individual ideas of what's considered taste. I like anime and manga, yet rarely watch/read both forms of the same story (eg. Naruto and Inuyasha: I only watched the anime for Inuyasha yet only read the manga for Naruto) and mostly read girly ones (Cardcaptor Sakura being my first ever anime/manga that I watched/read).

I'm a slow updater, as you'll see from looking at my remaining multi-chapter story, so anything I do put up will either be written well in advance or be a one shot. Most likely the later. I also tend to overuse parenthesis (in case you hadn't noticed!).

Feel free to contact me, questions, quotations, find out about Scotland, random things or just to say hi. I like random messages and emails!

MY FICTIONPRESS FIC IS HERE: http://www.fictionpress.com/~purpleskies I've only got one story on it so far, and probably forever. And sadly it only has one review from my friend who I forced to review since she'd read it in English class and was sitting next to me at the computer. :(

Random Info about the stories!

For summeries, read the summeries given below. This bits for updates etc.

One Thing After Another

Not started the next chappie. Having a bit (ok, a lot) of writer's block. WILL continue and finish though or die trying! I'm NOT about to abandon it. If you read the A/N at the end of the last chapter, it asked for anyone that inerested in helping to write and/or give ideas (which is open to any reviewer! Of course). Just email or review about it if you so wish. You just need to be able to get me off my butt and force me to write (that's the only requirement)!

To The Furutre

A Cardcaptor Sakura oneshot. Just needed to get the idea out my head, and I know it's not all that great. But I just wanted to read another story about Sakura preparing for the next Cardcaptor, but not about the Cardcaptor themselves. I like those kinda stories, but find it really hard to find them!

Cupid Yuuko!

A Tsubasa Chronicle oneshot. Though the idea kinda came from the Cardcaptor Sakura second movie. Won't spoil anything, but the bit when Syaoran tells Sakura something after she got the Void card :) and I thought, surely that would go the same for Sakura in Tsubasa? (if she could at all recall her past for a while, of course) and we all know Yuuko loves Valentines day and wants her presents! Why not combine the two? Definetely my fav story of what I've written.

Brilliant

My only Sherlock story to date. A oneshot. After watching the season 2 finale I started thinking about what would happen in the next season (if there is one). I'd be more worried if I hand't wikipedia searched Sherlock Holmes and got some of the plot points from the original stories, which is sort of where this comes from. He's back, and John's furious and showing off what he learned from Sherlock.
