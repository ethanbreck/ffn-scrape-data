A Little About Me...

I'm Kitty, and the most important thing to know about me is that I am mad. The second most important thing is probably that I have a chronic illness which makes me behave like even more of a nutjob by having my brain malfunction at random. This is one reason why I have been christened the "Quenn fo Tpyos".

My main fandom is the Artemis Fowl fandom, although I do very occasionally dabble in others. Besides AF, my favourite authors and books are, in no particular order: Harry Potter, Lemony Snicket, Garth Nix, Confessions of Georgia Nicolson and Tamora Pierce. I am also into anime and manga (mainly CLAMP, because I love CardCaptor Sakura and Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicle - but also Azumanga Daioh and Sailor Moon) and movies (yay, Harry Potter and Lord of the Rings... and I like Keira Knightley too).

I only write fics that are PG-13 or less, sometimes squick at kissing and write all ships as though everyone were soulmates. This is probably how I got myself a reputation for being Innocent with a capital I. It also means that any ships in my fics can be taken as relationships or as friendships, and that any "slash" is... not really slash at all. (Although you can still flame me for it, if that is how you get your kicks.)

Please note that I generally only write one-shots. In the past I have had people ask me to "write more", which makes me wibble about whether the fic worked as a stand-alone piece or not. If I am going to continue a fic, I will say so, and I will take any requests for me to "write more" of a one-shot as a request for more of the same ship or genre.
