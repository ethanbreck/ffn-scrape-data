Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter, and Junjō Romantica.

I'm a 26 year old from Scotland. My greatest goal in life is to write a book and to see it published on the book shelves of my local bookstore. Its been a few years since I published anything; hopefully my style will have improved and the mistakes less frequent. I am working on a couple of Junjou romantica fanfiction which I am going to put up over the space of the next year; the first being In This Life. I hope to pen a few other stories including a Maiden Rose fiction and possibly a Haru Wo Daite Ita.

I am happy to consider requests from people. Please drop me a private message if you would like to suggest something. I have a story in the pipeline as a stand alone piece from the In This Life world focusing on Usami and Misaki, as requested by a reviewer of mine.

I am a big fan of manga and animes. My favourite mangas include Fake, Junjou Romantica, Haru Wo Daite Ita and Il Gatto Sul G. Anime-wise I love anything with a good plot: some examples include Kyou Kara Maoh, Gravitation, Junjou Romantica, Skip Beat, La Cord'ora.

Author's Note: Tomorrow, I am going to get my printer up and running, so I will be able to proof read the chapters I have recently written. They have been a little rushed due to the hectic circumstances in my life (I just moved house and started a new job). I hope to have them back to the normal standard in a couple of weeks for your reading pleasure. Thank you for your patience.
