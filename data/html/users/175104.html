Author has written 12 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Final Fantasy I-VI, Minesweeper, Final Fantasy VII, and Mass Effect.

His stabilizers useless, his fuel about to explode, our hero careens out of control over a strange, unexplored planet! Yes, it's just another typical day for the incredible Spaceman Spiff, bold and intrepid explorer extraordinaire!

So once again, I find myself returning from another hiatus, although this one was due more to some rather life-changing circumstances. I won't go into the details, but you might notice that my location has changed from USA to Australia. Let's just leave it at that.

I am really only dedicated to one story and that is Gnothi Se Auton. All of my other fics will bow to it, and be pleased with any scraps of my creativity that I have left to offer them. It will be an epic of truly absurd length if it is ever finished, but I've made a few edits and reorganized it a bit, in hopes that it will be a much easier read than before. I've also included chapter summaries if you do not wish to trudge through everything.

From time to time, when I'm feeling silly, I may update TL;DR - A FF4 Novle. I really have to be in the right mood for that one.

I'm always happy to chat about fanfics, writing, or anything so feel free to leave me a PM.

As always, thanks for reading.

Regards,

Spiff
