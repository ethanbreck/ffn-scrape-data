Author has written 5 stories for X-Men, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
Hello, all. I guess you'd like to know a little bit about me huh. Or maybe not, who knows. At any rate, I'm a college student, which pretty much equals POOR and can't afford to go do anything except read too much fanfiction (as if there's such a thing as too much! pshaw!).

I am a longtime fan of the X-Men comics, the character Rogue and Gambit in particular, and I started writing fanfic of them exclusively. But lately the writers up at Marvel Comics have been royally pissing me off with the way they've ruined my favorite comics, so I've kind of lost interest. Maybe after Chris Claremont retires or has a stroke I'll pick up on it again, but don't hold your breath or anything.

I am also most recently a Buffy the Vampire Slayer ADDICT. However, when I first saw the show, I thought it was just about the most retarded thing I'd ever seen. And David Boreanez-or-however-the-hell-you-spell-his-name just about annoyed the living shit out of me. But hey all you loyal Buffy fanatics out there, don't get your britches in a twist. I have come to know and love the show very much.
I started watching the show religiously when I caught the season 5 episode "Fool for Love" on reruns on FX, and I absolutely HAD to find out what happened after that, and after that, and after that, and the rest is history. Since then I've caught almost all the earlier shows in rerun, even the corny first and second seasons when I originally didn't like the show, and have come to at least appreciate them.
Now I have to tell you, I am a VERY loyal Spike fan. Even when I didn't like the show I liked that guy. I like Spike better than I like Buffy, although I am also a very loyal Spuffy fan. So yeah, for those of you who really hated that they hooked up last season or he has a soul now, then my stuff REALLY isn't for you. And if you flame me I'll...well I'll just delete it, but still. Don't bother.

Well I think that about covers it. Hope you enjoy my little stories.
Yapatchalata!
