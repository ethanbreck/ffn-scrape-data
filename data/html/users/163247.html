Author has written 18 stories for Farscape, Book of Amber, Firefly, and Dragon Age.

Old and dusty... me, and this account.

The first fanfic I wrote was for Farscape. Loved that show. Still do.

Wrote a lot of "cooperative forum fiction" (I don't know if there's a better name for it) comprising of Star Wars expanded universe adventures, then spent several years as part of a very active Farscape writing group (aka The Farscape Lounge). My primary character from those stories got to have part of his backstory made into a fic.

My pen-and-paper role-playing exploits in the universe(s) of Roger Zelazny's Amber led to writing another backstory fic for one of my characters.

Then Firefly (and so Serenity) made its appearance and I ended up writing some stuff for that. I never had a good idea for finishing "Bitter Spice", unfortunately.

And then... the wilderness years...

Now I seem to be writing small character fics again again, this time for Dragon Age Inquisition. Who knew?
