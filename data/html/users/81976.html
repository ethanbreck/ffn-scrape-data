Author has written 12 stories for Digimon, Sonic the Hedgehog, Angel Beats!/エンジェルビーツ, My Little Pony, and Zootopia.

Since you're here, I'm going to presume you're familiar with my work. It's not Shakespeare - it's not even Dickens. But it's mine, and I'm damned proud of it.

If you're not familiar with my stories, I urge you to read them. If you like them, drop me a review or a message. I'm always open to input and a constructive critique. Above all, though, I just want to know that what I write here is noticed, at least by a few. I write because I want it to be read, it's as simple as that.

I try to make a lot of my work revolve around the emotions of the characters, because for me that's the haulmark of a good story. If it doesn't grab you by the heart and leave you feeling some kind of way about it then it has been a complete waste of time.

If I manage that much in you, then I've achieved what I set out to do. And I thank you for dropping by.

I'm a big fan of anime, as you can no doubt tell from my stories and my favourites. It takes a special sort of anime to catch my imagination, and an exceptional one to make me want to write about it. I've been a fan of Digimon from the beginning, and I think it's going to be a life-long thing. And lately, as I've been rediscovering my passion for anime, I've come across works like Clannad, Rosario + Vampire, Angel Beats and so on.

Well, I've been itching to write something. And it was Angel Beats which started it.

The concept behind the series, the depth to the characters, and the open-ended possibilities just called out to me. So, the journey begins. And a new project is in the works.
