Author has written 6 stories for SWAT Kats, and Sonic the Hedgehog.

After finishing The Worst of Two Worlds and Timeless I took a very long break from writing fan fiction. So long that my writing style and quality has almost completely changed (hopefully for the better, but who knows).

My more "contemporary" stuff starts with Last Names, and if you've never read any of my stories before I'd recommend starting there. If you enjoy that, the continuity continues on through My Responsibility and then Ten Twenty-Four.

Also, if you like SWAT Kats fan fiction, make sure to check out the redesigned SWAT Kats Fan Fiction Archive at skfanfic.com. You can also find some of my older writings and co-authored stories there. And make sure to checkout The SWAT Kats Encyclopedia at www.swatkats.info.
