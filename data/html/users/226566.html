Wow...it's been ages since I logged in here...*fends off cobwebs with a broom*

What can I say about me...I've just turned 16 (July 19th as a matter of fact) and I live in Canada, though I was born in England and am currently writing from Brasil.

No, not confusing at all...

I love languages, learning new ones, perfecting old ones...my biggest peeve is when people don't take the time to spell, use proper grammar,or use short hand wrong.

I love to write, plain as that. I think one of these days I'm going to completely clear out my account and update with some fresh stuff. I currently have a huge Harry Potter fan fic in the works, but am not even going to think about posting it until I'm done, because otherwise I, uh...tend to neglect them. *shifty eyes dart to the scorch marks on her desk*

Stay tuned, and if you care, leave a review.
