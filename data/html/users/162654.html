Uh, I'm a firm believer that music and writing are both the two most therapeutic things in my life and I can safely say I don't know what I'd do without either of them. For a long while I was posting exclusively on Tumblr, but I'm back now.

I will also say that while I love writing, the inspiration doesn't strike me like it used to. Or rather, real life doesn't let me dedicate as much time to it when it does strike me. Which is sad, but also part of being an adult I suppose. Maybe some day that YA novel I'm writing will sell and be a huge hit and I can stop working (ahhh, gotta love the dream). But for now, all I can promise is to write when I can and to never abandon something one it's up here.

That being said, I'm notoriously known for my one shots.

Well, I guess that should do it! This is Silverflare07, over and out!
