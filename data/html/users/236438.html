My pen name Aurican. I'm not a great writer and for that I apologize- I'm mostly writing for my own entertainment and would like to share in the case that someone else might derive some fun from it as well. I write things with shonen/shojo- ai in it (ie. non-straight relationships) as if you hadn't noticed by now. If this bothers you leave now, otherwise I do not care and will take poorly to criticism on this front (and will cheerfully ridicule the person foolish enough to comment). Some of the characters are original so please DON'T TAKE THEM. The characters from other anime series are copyright their respective owners, even though I'd love to own them I don't. Don't sue me, you're only wasting your time as I'm a poor grad student, I do not claim they are mine- I'm just taking them out for a short jaunt. Hope you enjoy and PLEASE review? I greatly appreciate them. I'm sorry if I do not write a lot or often, as I said, I write for my own enjoyment when I have the time and inclination to. I deleted two of my old storylines because I felt they were crappy (even for something I wrote,) and one of the ones I left I left for sheer nostalgia and for some other reason I can't figure out. That one would be shelving the past. It is on a permanent hiatus. I have no need or inclination to complete it right now. I do intend to finish the one I'm currently working on, though. Wish me luck.
