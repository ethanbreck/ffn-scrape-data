About me.

Hey everyone, I'm Liquid, and I've been a member of this site since 2002. I do parodies, original stories, novelizations, spin-offs, you name it. Of course, if I had to choose which kind of story is my favorite, I'd have to choose novelizations. If you've got any requests for video games that you'd like to see novelized, send me a PM. I also appreciate everyone who views my stories, especially if they leave a review, so in advance, thank you for taking the time to read my stories.

I just want to put it out there that when I novelize a game, I am not writing a strategy guide. There are going to be changes to the order of events, the way dialogue and character interactions take place, and probably a lot of other things.
