Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter, and Pirates of the Caribbean.
I'm a college student who writes to give gruesome deaths to the plot bunnies that plague me. Death to the furry bastards. However, my muse is either a lazy sod or manic depressive, so anything I write is updated very sporatically.

My three multi-chapter HP fics have been discontinued: that means that I will not update "Out of the Shadows", "If you can't beat them", and "Better late than never" unless I have a dramatic idea where they're concerned. They were all bunnies that jumped on me during class and suffered very crappy writing in the process.

Anyway:
E-mail me with reviews, suggestions, reccomendations for other fics (Harry Potter, LotR, PotC), whatever. I welcome all forms of criticism, constructive or otherwise. Flames will be used to burn the old work so that a better version can rise from the ashes.
Have fun with my stuff. I try. I'm not professional. I write to get the ideas out of my head.
