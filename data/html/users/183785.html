I started writing Fan Fiction when I got into Star Trek:DS9. I wrote a few O/K stories and posted them on Usenet. I moved onto X-Files (M/S) stuff. Unfortunately I have lost all these works except for one.

UPDATE (2003) I am now into Buffy. I am working on a few fanfics, mainly B/X stuff. I hope to have it done by the end of October. I am now attempting to write Buffy and West Wing fics. Don't know if I will ever post again, but it's nice to see my work here.

UPDATE (2007) Still into Buffy, now that I have been reading the Season 8 comics. I am working on some Post Deathly Hallows Harry Potter. I did have a Xena in works, but I read a story very similar, but vastly superior, so that is no more. Thinking about continuing on with Star Trek:TNG, post Nemesis.

UPDATE (2014) Kinda fell off there. Just recently came back to the site after playing Last of Us for the first time. Wanted to see if there was anything on here. Was not disappointed. Working on about 3 of my own right now. I should probably get around to posting that Buffy and Potter stuff I wrote 7 years ago!
