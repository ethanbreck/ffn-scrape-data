Friday, Feb 4 2011

Sooo... it's been a while, just like it always is. Here are some stories, pretty juvenile and not very well-written, and which aside from Piano Sonata, will never be finished. I'm more of a reader than a writer, lately--a lurker and a lover of many stories, though rarely a reviewer. I'm really trying to change this... I know it's a horrible trait. But alas. I'm one of the shyest people I know. It's hard enough for me to leave my stories down there, knowing that I know how flawed they are. But there you go. Enjoy them if you dare, but more likely than not I'll enjoy yours first and give it a loving review.

"Anytime something is written against me, I not only share the sentiment but I feel I could do the job far better myself. Perhaps I should advise would-be enemies to send me their grievances beforehand, with full assurances they will receive my every aid and support. I have even secretly longed to write, under a pen name, a merciless tirade against myself." -Jorge Luis Borges
