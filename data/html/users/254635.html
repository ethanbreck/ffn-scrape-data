Recently I've been getting a lot of complaints about updating and any lack thereof. Let me explain something to those who say such things.

"Fanfiction", by definition, is a fanmade story based on an existing fictional work. I am not being paid to do this. While I do get satisfaction from reviewers who enjoy my works, the most it can really do is help my writing improve.

Writing fanfictions is entirely subject to my interest. Thus, when my interest wanes, there's little chance I'll continue with a specific fanfiction. I might finish it just to end it, but most likely, it'll just die.

Don't take this as me being a dick or anything like that. Just look at it positively -- this is your chance to come up with your own ending, if you liked the fanfic that much. Or maybe you could use the unfinished story as inspiration and come up with something of your own; I don't know. The world is your oyster, as they say!

So please, if you must say something regarding my negligent update status, say something like, "Please update. I want to know what happens next!" Saying things like, "Update now! Cause I said so!" will not make me want to get writing. I'm sorry, it just won't.
