Hey, my penname is Lady Red LipsMe (or as much as I'll let you know):

My name, or nickname rather, is Bri. I graduated May of 2008, moved from Florida to New York in June of 2008, and enrolled in college in January of 2009. I work at a convenient store/gas station known to the lower end of the Finger Lakes and northern Pennsylvania. I used to have so much on here, and then I left for a long, long time. To any reader that happens across this page, though I doubt many will, I will write again.

UPDATE

In late 2010, I moved into my own apartment in another part of New York. I've recently been bitten by the writing bug and hope to get back in the swing of things.

Status:

Everyone in the writing hell that has become fanfiction.net, could you tell me why Gundam Wing yaoi is so enticing? Why there is yet to be a decent fanfiction without yaoi?? Can anyone explain this to me? I understand the fangirlish nature that draws people to a yaoi story, but this is Gundam Wing. It is sacred. In my opinion, it is the best of all anime shows. Now, I have nothing against anything homosexual or anything of that nature, but why must you turn their relationships to more than what it was? Sure, it's an alternate universe created by you, but think about it. These strong boys who did something extraordinary are being used, by you, to submit to a shpincter-opening experience. They saved the world, the colonies, themselves, and millions of people throughout the series and then in the movie. If anyone comes to my page and expects to find a yaoi, they will be sorely disappointed. I would never make my Gundam Boys gay, they had solid friendship and care, though it was not entirely evident. If you see more in that, fine. But I won't do it. I won't read it. And I'll probably be the only one writing Gundam fanfiction without yaoi.
