Third update. I only seem to do this when I'm far away. I'm a 23 year old Ginny Weasley lookalike (red hair, brown eyes) with Harry's bad eyesight although I hope my taste in glasses frames is a bit better. I'm in London at the moment in graduate school doing a master's in bio-archaeology.

Interesting, my only consistent obsession has been Harry Potter. Then again, why not? Especially after the brilliance of OotP when Rowling made Harry a typical, annoying self-centered fifteen year old.

Ships supported
Harry/Ginny
Ron/Hermione (They disliked each other instantly and everyone knows what THAT means in the long run- Terry Pratchett)
Fred/Angelina
George/Alicia
Percy/Penelope
Draco/Pansy

I will write a scene or story for you if you ask. But I have to know a little about the fandom you're interested in first. These are the ones I know a bit about, Star Wars except not the prequels, the X-Files, Terry Pratchett, HP duh, Red Dwarf and Peter Pan. Any others, I need info. Still, I like challenges so don't hesitate to ask me.
