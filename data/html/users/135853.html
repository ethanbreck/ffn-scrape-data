About me...

For pretty regular updates on my life try my journal...

I'm 22 and have just graduated. I am now the proud owner of a Physics with Astrophysics degree. I'm from Scotland/England. From that you will probably guess that I'm more of a scientist than anything else. I write because the little fictitious people in my head force me too, so if you don't like it, blame them.

More...I love to read and book shopping is my one major vice. I drink, socialise, debauch...the usual stuff. I'm a hyper-social person sometimes. I love people and so have a large group of friends who I love to bring together as often as possible...hence my penchant for throwing wild parties. I've also just bought my first house so I'm feeling old, and yet enjoying it so much.

When I'm nervous I "chatter like cold teeth" and i love meeting new people to be friends with. I'm madly hedonistic and hopelessly gregarious.

My writing...
I write basically when I feel creative and I write what I like. I've fallen in love with slash (and gay men in general) so most of what I write concerns them. Some people think that's weird. *shrugs* I have been known to write some NC-17, but of course that is now banned so to read any of my more smutty stuff you'll have to email me.

I love Harry Potter even though I once vowed never to read them (I'm hate people who like things just because they're mainstream). I adore Severus Snape, Sirius Black and Remus Lupin, and recently my brain has been invaded by Bill Weasley of the Linenverse (see below)

I also love Trip/Reed so I write that too when I'm moved to do so.
