Hello, I'm Bronwyn - a twenty-something Australian who can usually be found on the weekend at the cinemas, the tennis court, the dance studio, the beach or out on the water in a boat, when I'm not chilling out reading the latest chapters of my favourite FanFics.

I'm not an author. I keep a FanFiction.net profile to keep track of all the FanFics I'm reading, so this is my "virtual" bookshelf more than anything else! My current favourite fandoms to read are Twilight & Transformers. I bat for Team Edward and I'm securely in the Autobot camp. :P

I am trying to improve my reviewing skills (which at the moment are sadly lacking). I hate leaving empty reviews, but I don't feel overly qualified to fully critique others just yet as I'm not brave enough to put my own writing out there for the masses like the awesome authors here do. I apologise for my severe slackness.

There are numerous better things to read than my bio on this site. So I'll leave you with a few of my favourite quotes that pretty much sum up my philosophy on life or just tickle my fancy.
