Hey everyone!

I'm am Janae. I am a wife and the mother of one. I am a California girl, but I live in the country of Belgium. I have been writing fan fiction since I was 12 or 13. I think my first fic on this site was when I was 14. Most of my stories are pretty old and I wrote them in high school. Most of those older fics are Harry Potter, but there are others (National Treasure, Gossip Girl, and Twilight). Currently I have been working on fics that center around CSI and Once Upon a Time.

My stories aren't majorly edited and I write on the fly (sorry for any typos). Writing is a passion that helps me relieve stress and it can get pretty stressful caring for a one year old and a husband while working and living in a foreign country.

I hope you all enjoy!

You can follow me on Twitter at MoonyAngel7 (just tweet me because my account is locked) and you can follow me on Instagram at NaturallyNayNay.
