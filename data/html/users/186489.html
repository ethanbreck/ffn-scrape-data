Hello, and welcome to my page on fanfiction.net! My name is Linda. I'm a Dutch girl, currently 26 years old and attenting university. As you can see from my stories, my main fandom is Harry Potter. I've been writing fanfiction in that fandom for nearly eight (wow O_o) years now.

My favourite character in the HP books is Remus Lupin, and as you can see I've written a series of stories about the books from hís point of view. I'm currently working on Half-Blood Prince as seen through Remus's and Tonks's eyes. Due to real life interfering, updates are sadly infrequent these days, but I DO intent to eventually get to the end of Book 7.

Feel free to check my stories out! I hope you'll enjoy them, and if you do please review them. :) (And of you don't, you may of course review too! I'm open for politely-worded critique and, if it's fair critique, I'll usually correct my mistakes or offer an explanation.)

Sincerely, Linda Lupos.
