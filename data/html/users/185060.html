Author has written 11 stories for Knight Rider, Beetlejuice, and Misc. Tv Shows.

Well, I do a little of everything. I read and write fanfics and other stories, maintain my Beetlejuice fan sites, and I also draw occasionally. I like to illustrate my own stories, but that doesn't mean others can't draw a pic from them too. (hint hint wink)

I'm big into cartoons and anime. I have a list a mile long of all the shows I like. And being signed up with Netflix, I am able to watch a ton of the things I've always wanted to, so I keep adding to the list of shows I like. But a small list of shows that have always stood out as all-time greats (and that I will forever love) are: Beetlejuice, Knight Rider, The Real Adventures of Jonny Quest, Darkwing Duck, Inspector Gadget, Stargate SG-1/Atlantis, and ReBoot. If I ever write anymore fan fictions, those would be the shows I'd write them for.

I have several original Beetlejuice characters that I'm quite into at the moment, which is why I'm writing more stories about them at this point in time. They are mainly: Endri Timbürland and Kyle Bennington. You can view more information about them at the following links: Endri, Kyle. If you already know about them, you might want to vote on your favorite in my poll near the top of this page.

A tad about me: I'm female, over 25, and live in Pennsylvania. I am also married and have one daughter.

Please enjoy my stories, and thank you to everyone who review my works!
