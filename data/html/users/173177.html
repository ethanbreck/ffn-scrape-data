I'm not a writer. Just a dedicated reader.

I found that I fell in love with certain characters and just couldn't part with them at the end of the novel, film, or tv show- which lead me to fanfic many, many years ago. The combination of being a hopeless romantic and usually devoid of a love life has lead me to be a shameless shipper. I'll take a good gen fic, but mostly it's all about the relationships for me. And maybe a little smut sprinkled on top.

I have miserable luck in shipping anything resembling canon- but a girl can dream right? What else is fanfic for? Hurray for unconventional ships!

If you are a writer- THANKS SO MUCH!! Keep it up! Also, don't ever feel like you have to apologize for writing a long chapter. We're here to read. The longer the better! (That's what she said- hehe)
