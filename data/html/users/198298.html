Hello! My name is Talking Hawk. I am Mormon, and this is the reason why I write exclusively K and K+ stories. I like writing and reading stories that promote high moral standards. If you have any questions for me, don't hesitate to ask.

I've been writing fanfiction for a number of years now. I've written a lot of Lord of the Rings fanfiction, and though I'm not interested in the series anymore, I will keep my stories up because I think they're wholesome. I'm now more interested in Bionicle, and especially in the relationship between Vakama and Nokama (I think they like each other). I hope you enjoy my stories, and please let me know what you think by submitting a review to the website. Thank you, and please enjoy.
