Idea.

It is a beautiful word; one of which has no clear definition. An idea can strike fear into the hearts of men. It can inspire, bring happiness, sadness, understanding, and confusion...but most of all, hope. A great french philosopher once said, "Nothing is more dangerous than an idea when it is the only one you have." It can be the greatest weapon. An idea is not a mortal man that fears death. It cannot think. It cannot hurt nor bleed. Or can it?

What would happen if an idea came to life? What would happen if that idea refused to die? Refused to be forgotten? How would that affect the world?

An idea can be a dangerous weapon, indeed.
