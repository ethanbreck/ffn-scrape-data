Author has written 1 story for Inuyasha.

Age: Well, since I was born in '82...

Hometown: Vancouver Island, Canada

Never fear! I haven't forgotten my baby. Life has been so busy over the last few years that I haven't had the time to sit down and properly write a new chapter. I *am* still planning to finish the story. It'll just take longer than I had originally thought.

I am a serious seamstress who has sewn several costumes, and is certified in making the clothes you see on tv, movies, and the stage. This has led to a greater interest in cosplay, especially over the last couple of years.

I love music, and in the last few years, I have come to appreciate the music in many of the anime I watch. I have all kinds of music from the InuYasha movies and show, as well as various tracks from other shows. My greatest musical discovery with anime music is the composer Kajiura Yuki. Her music is amazing.

I LOVE BOOKS! The majority of my collection consists of fantasy and science fiction. The ones that I can think of off the top of my head are Harry Potter, Tolkien, Star Wars (love Jedi Apprentice and Rogue Squadron!), Star Trek, CS Lewis, Jane Austin, Shakespeare, Sherlock Holmes, Edgar Allan Poe, Elizabeth Hayden, and some odds and ends.

The one thing that has been a constant in my life for quite a while is anime and manga. I absolutely LOVE Yu-Gi-Oh! (Seto Kaiba-hose me down!) and InuYasha (Sesshoumaru all the way, baby!). Others that I enjoy watching/reading are Witch Hunter Robin (Amon melts me into goo at sound of his voice), Card Captor Sakura (Yue drool), Ghost in the Shell, Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicles, Sword Art Online, and Sailor Moon. I am still discovering the wide amount of series that are available.

I have so many ideas that I want to share them with everyone. I am doing what I can to get them on paper, but because of life and my struggle to write them down, it may be a while before any major works actually do make it onto the site. I'm currently working on my one story, The Alternate Storyteller, which is going to be the size of a Greek epic from what I can see. I may do a crossover, but I have to see where I get with The Alternate Storyteller.

If you would like to see some of my original work, I have an account on fictionpress.net.

Thanks for taking the time to read about me and to explore my little corner of the Web.

Peace
