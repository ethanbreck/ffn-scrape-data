October 3, 2011

Thank you for visiting my profile page. I have been a member of Fanfiction.net since I was barely a teenager. Now, as an adult (That's terrifying to say), I have to admit that this page has a special place in my heart. Writing fanfiction is incredibly dorky, but I love it, and I hope you all do too! This page symbolizes the progress and growth of my writing skills. Not only has it matured, but so have I. So, thanks, and enjoy!

It's been a long time since I last uploaded anything. I have come to find that adulthood means giving up a lot of things I used to do as a teen in exchange for creating a lifestyle. I try my best to type out as much as I can. Smart phones are wonderful because I can write a chapter out while on the go, but fanfiction has really taken a backseat these days. I apologize to all of my fans. It is not fair seeing as how I have a fic in the process. I don't want to give up on it since I owe it to you to tell you how to story plays out.

Thank you for being so understanding.

xox Rain

Current Projects:

White Rabbit is currently in progress! Chapter Three is now posted! Honda visits his mother and gets terrible news. It's certainly worth checking out!

Oh, and reviewing it would be great. Even just to prove you read it. Feedback = faster updates.

Future Projects:

After looking back on Ramune, I think writing a side fic of Marik x Ryou and how that relationship panned out would be interesting. I'm not sure. You guys should let me know how you'd take that.

I also have a lot of unanswered questions in "It Means I Love You" and to be honest, it's because I gave up and wanted to finish it up. Looking back on it, I find that so unfair so I will be writing an epilogue. I feel as if I owe you that much.

Feel free to follow my twitter for nonsense and updates on my fics! www.twitter.com/RainTessman
