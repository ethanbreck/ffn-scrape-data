Well hello to the world. Waves to everyone.

So you wanna know about me do you. Well I’m currently a 17-year-old student at Westherts college in Watford. If you haven’t guessed it yet, I am female. Born and raised in London, England.

I absolutely love fan fiction. My love for it started off from about 10 years old or something. It started off from the Xena Warrior Princess (Lucy Lawless) and Ares God of War (Kevin Smith…. God bless his soul) fan fiction. I Am a HYYYUGE AresXena Shipper. I always loved the fan fiction. Reading the creativity people have. And though not quite of age yet have been know to stray onto the NC-17 Stories quite a bit…. What can I say I’m hooked.

Like I said it all started with Xena. But I have been watching BtVS for a while now. And it has grown on my quite a bit. And the moment Spike came into it in season 2 ‘School Hard’ I was hooked on it. I knew from that moment on Spike was going to be something unique. And he is. Weather you hate him or love him Spike has become the most talked about person from Buffy. I personally love him. He’s a great talent. Love that English accent………. Even though he isn’t even British. And now I have become an avid watcher. Loving the way Buffy and Spike’s characters have grown. SPUFFY SHIPPER OLA!!! I love their chemistry. And weather they are fighting, talking, arguing, or shaggin you know it’d be a good show.

My favo season has to be the whole of season 4. Why you ask? One word. SPIKE.
Yep he made season 4 wicked. He was funny, and rude, and just Spike. I LOVED Somthing Blue... and Hush. I have yet to see Fool for Love yet as i missed it. But i am hopng to buy the tape. So I'm sure that will be on my fav lists soon, as i hear its a real saucy one.

I’m into a lot of different things. Horror, Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Action, Smut, fluff, Cartoons. Just about anything, which is probably contributed to my imagination.

Although I am a lover of the Fan Fiction phenomenon, I have only recently got the courage to start writing my own stories. (I’m more into reading then writing stories) And I am quite proud of my first attempt. Since not only is it my first, but it also is my first NC –17 piece. And I think it has turned out better then I had expected. But I do plan to write more. So watch this space because I’ll be putting them up here.

Well that's it for now. Thank you
