Hey ya'll!!

Wow, I get this whole spiffy box to type in stuff all about ME? Oy, whoever gives me this box to type in is WAY generous. I get a WHOLE BOX!!!

Ok, so what's to say? I'm the typical teenager:

14 years old, gets SLIGHTLY below average grades (I'm no over achiever, but I'm not exactly stupid either...opposite to popular belief among my friends, teehee), am OBSESSED with music, and tries to be a rebel. Except, have you ever noticed that no one can really be a unique rebel becuase there's LOTS of people who try to be unique rebels? So it's like, there's the Unique Rebel Clique thing. Am I making sense? I don't think so.

I LOVE movies, and hope to work for the movie business someday...possibly screenplay writing or putting music to the backgrounds. My favorite flicks are HP 1, HP 2, Ice Age, Finding Nemo, Just Married, HOLES, and How To Lose A Guy In 10 Days.

My favorite show ON THIS EARTH is FRIENDS. I'm totally obsessed now!! Besides that, I love the WB stuff, like Smallville, EVERWOOD (hello, Gregory Smith is HOTTTT), Gilmore Girls, What I Like About You...the works.

How I never noticed how much stuff I like! Spiffy!

I listen to almost anything, but I hate R & B and hip hop stuff. I'm not saying it's ALL bad, because there ARE acceptable songs out there. But most of it's worth crap. I love Punk/Pop Rock best out of all rock. My sounds include Blink 182, Good Charlotte, Simple Plan, New Found Glory, Green Day, etc.

Did I mention that I LOVE reading? Probably it's pretty obvious since reading and writing almost go hand in hand. My favorite books are, of course, #1- Harry Potter series (the 5th, 3rd, 4th, 2nd and 1st tie), #2- Angus, Thongs and Full Frontal Snogging series (They're SOOOOOOOOOO funny!), #3- Someone Like You, almost anything by Sarah Dessen.

WOW I'm so self-centered I'll stop talking about me now.
