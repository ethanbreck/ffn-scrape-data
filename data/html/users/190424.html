I'm a female writer with bad eyesight and an odd pen-name. I'm currently stationed at college pursuing a career in writing, surviving off of Country Time lemonade mix and fandom. Reviews make my day, I'm grateful for each and every one of them, and I reply to all the ones I can.

About me? Lessee...well...

-- I never keep just one type of pasta on hand, and have an obsession with Town House Crackers. The original kind.
-- I adore summer and warm weather, but happen to live in a place where the winters last five months and the snow piles up to your thighs.
-- I'm the owner of a wonderful old dog named Oscar, and two betta fish named Crona and Kaschei.
-- I'm owned by a cat named Ofelia.
-- I can be painfully shy and reclusive, unless I know you well. Then you'll never shut me up. Speaking of which...
-- I'm prone to flapping my arms around when discussing something I'm passionate about. This can include anything from politics to cooking to fictional characters.
-- I listen to everything. My ipod is a giant anachronism.
-- I love listening to Disney songs in other languages, especially ones that fit the setting. If you haven't heard the Hunchback of Notre Dame soundtrack in french, you haven't lived.
-- I generally write for whatever characters/or fandoms peak my interest, but tend to come back to Kingdom Hearts and Blood+.

On the subject of my old stories, anything prior to the final chapter of "Love and Loss" is posted purely for nostalgia's sake. Eugh.
