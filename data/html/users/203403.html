Author has written 7 stories for Mass Effect, Dragon Age, and Diablo.

An opportunity to practice writing and immerse myself in the amazing worlds that talented individuals in the gaming community have brought to life.

I hone in on what interests me about a character, a pairing, a state of mind, or an event. I try to write one-shot pieces about individuals and what I perceive as their motivations, desires, or conflicts. I appreciate critique and insight from other writers.

Reviews are appreciated, and I will try to reciprocate.

Minor Update 5/19/12 - I am attempting my first longer-form piece, based around 'Dragon Age 2'. This a new world to explore and play in, and I hope you enjoy,

Update: 4/21/2013 - Still here, though not sure if I'll be posting anything in the future. If you have requests or like something I've done and would like to see more, by all means, feel free to PM, and I'll see if I can squeeze a few nuggets out of my brain. This has been known to happen.

Update: 9/13/14 - The yearly update, as it were. Still writing occasionally, though I tend to clear stories as quickly as I post them. These days I am more of a reader and reviewer than a writer, at least when it comes to fan-fiction. A few ideas are percolating here and there, but, like a good cup of coffee, they need some time to brew before they get poured into / onto (a) paper (cup).

Otherwise, I have been quite taken with a few new fandoms and some truly talented writers lately, and look forward to digging more into their respective works. For the Supernatural fandom, gaelicspirit has won me over, and for The Avengers fandom, harkpad is my go-to favorite. Do read what you can by these two lovely people, as they are both outstanding.
