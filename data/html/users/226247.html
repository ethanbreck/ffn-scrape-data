9-11-2009-That's right, she resurfaces. :) I will be updating soon, I've just had a rough couple of months. Thanks for sticking around!

1-30-2009- Hi, All! I just wanted to let everyone know that I will not be around much in February, so I will probably not be updating for awhile. Don't worry, I'll be back!

1-16-2009- Hello everyone and I hope the year has started out well. I have just finished Fire on Babylon and plan on devoting myself to Paradise Before My Eyes and Wrapped Up in You. That's right, I haven't abandoned either of them. For those of you still reading Wrapped Up In You, my problem is that I want to fix I Won't Walk Away first. I've been editing all the chapters and now I have to go back and replace the ones already on here. It's taking a bit more time than I would have liked, but I want to be at least partially satisfied with my work. Anyway, thanks to everyone who has read my fics, reviewed, or just decided to read this note. :)

I started writing fanfiction just after Goblet of Fire came out, so if you are reading my earlier works, don't be surprised if Sirius pops up or something just doesn't quite fit. I've thought about going back and making them fit, but after HBP came out, I figured it would mess up some of my story lines. As many of you might know and for those who don't, I am currently a sophomore in college and therefore my life has become crazed. While I love writing and am trying to BETA my fics I just don't always have the time. Unfortunatley, as we get older, real life seems to intrude more and more upon us and if I have any extended abscences from fandom, never fear, I will be back. I have also said that I will finish my stories and I am not backing down from that. I hate when I find fics that haven't been finished and I don't want the same fate to go to my readers. Thanks to everyone who has hung around, given me wonderful reviews, or just read one of my fics in passing.
