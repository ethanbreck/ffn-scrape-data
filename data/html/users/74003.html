I write both Harry Potter and Anime stories. I have written suspense/action with HP and Yaoi (boyxboy pairings). I really have only done Draco/Harry because anything else just doesn't interest me. The Animes I like are Gundam Wing (pairings Quatre/Trowa and Heero/Duo usually), Cardcaptor Sakura/Tsubasa Chronicles, Yu-Gi-Oh/Yu-Gi-Oh GX --have written a trilogy in Yu-Gi-Oh with Yuugi and Yami (and Ryou/Bakura) becoming a couple, Petite Princess Yucie, DN Angel, Kiddy Grade, Aquarion, Tactics among others.
