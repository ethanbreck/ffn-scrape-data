To Who It May Concern:

It has been a long time since I have updated anything on this account, even longer than some of my other ones...

Eventually I will removing everything on this account except for perhaps Dogstar, which had a fairly large (and really appreciated) fanbase. I thank you all for that even though I never did finish it. One day I promise that I will continue it and finally finish, especially with it being so close to the end. Although when I do continue on it I will be revamping and rewriting it. Now that I am a much more mature, and much better, writer I can have more description, more storyline, and more Sirius Black goodness. Because after everything that happened in the series (now complete) we kind of need our Padfoot. As for my other stories, everything I have ever written or wanted to write remains close to my heart, many of the details still very clear to me, however I am unsure which ones I will continue in addition to Dogstar. Unless you really wish to see my Pitch Black story complete I will most likely remove it and archive it on my own personal hard drive or my website/blog (who's link can be found in my new account's profile) in case you liked it and want to read it again. My Dragon Knights fic "Crystal Prison" was a very important story to me, and very fun to write, but it is along the same lines as Dogstar and I would love to rewrite it, or at least edit it and reread the manga once more to help get a better grip on things. I will try to update this section when I know for sure what I am doing or you can find my blog or keep watch on my new account. Please feel free to message me there or e-mail me if you have any questions.

Oh, and I know a few years ago someone e-mailed me and asked about Dogstar and I e-mailed back promising to update... I really meant to, and if you read this I apologize. I had a lot of personal and tragic occurences going on in my life and everything got out of control. I won't go into detail, but I regret that I did not continue it for you. I'm sorry. To everyone.

New Account: Shadow-Ocelot

Email: ocelots.shadow@gmail.com

I wish there was an easier way to merge my accounts, but I do not think there is.

Edit 11/28/2012: It is an interesting day for me to say that I have finally let go of my old writing. I have removed "Dark Adaptation" (Pitch Black), Ode to Vegeta (DBZ Poem), and Tangled Fate (Harry Potter) from my fanfiction list. If you, by nostalgia or whatever, still wish to have these stories available to read e-mail or PM me on my newest account and let me know and I will post it on my blog so it can be archived. I have grown so much since this first account and just couldn't bear to have them posted here any longer.
