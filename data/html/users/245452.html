2014/ August/ 8th — I decided to start writing in Archive of Our Own as well. AO3 is actually in some ways better. It supposes as a default that you are going to do crossovers. Probably, this is due to the fact that because the "Tumblr gen/thing" started and people ask question of what it would be like if the character was like this. I visited Son Christine's oeuvre and was surprised to see she was 2 years younger than me. She actually had a matured voice. More matured than most. When she wrote it was like someone 19 or 20 was writing but it was actually her as a 13 to 15 year old self and that was beautiful. Fanfiction has changed drammatically over the 10 years I have read it and read online stories that were somewhat part and parcel with Fanfiction though had originality either by its entirety or AU factors. I sometimes lament. I don't see writers like Son Christine or Sabam or even Yukmiya anymore. Perhaps I am not looking but the ecchi types of writing and ecchiness in anime has increased making people go on autotune on that. It is not a very good sign. People should not look at empty lusts (as ecchi) as the only pinnacle in writing because I think even writing for enjoyment has different dimensions in it. Us old fandom writers may survive but it may be a difficult run at times because people may not always like our work.

On a worldly scale, I hope that you support Palestine. Supporting Palestine is not really a Muslim thing. Nor is it anti-Judaism. Supporting Palestinians is supporting humanity because those people can't really fight back against Israel. Israel is just another nation that is bullying a nation that is not military-rich as it is. It could be any other nation doing that and our universal brotherhood demand that we rise up against it or any other people that do that. Israel needs to coexist with Palestine and that is humanly right and good and prosperous. I respect all religions so supporting Palestine is not going against Judaism; a religion is not determined by national boundaries so religion should never be used as an abuse against oneself and others. Support peace for both nations. Coexistence and Respect are the only things that humanity needs to survive and not fall into that dreary fettered chasm of narcissism.

I will say these things because we live in a more connected world so we should support each other and know each other as people as ourselves.


2012/ June/ 12th — I am an MA student now in English Literature. Thanks to Allah Almighty for that. I started this account 10 years ago in 2002 when I was only 14 years old and after a decade even FF.net changed its layout dramatically so kind of a celebration there as well. It is fun to revisit the tales that I wrote in my youth. Obviously, I didn't finish any one of them though I had certain mental calculations concerning them; it is both procrastination and busyness on my part that I did not do it but now I might finish most of them or at least try to. It'll be fun returning to the stories and writing them concerning that I am older now and I can see how my mind worked then and how it works now (ironically, not much has changed) and I can put in what I have learned in these past years into my stories s probably the stories will reshape themselves with probably more frolic or maturity (I doubt the routes will change much I prefer keeping the innocence of them thought of in those times intact as much as possible). I think I started seriously writing in FF.net and it was because that I was interested in X-Men: Evolution fandom that I first came across this site all those years ago even before Google had become a net celebrity and I used another search engine (most probably Yahoo) to find this site. I am forever grateful to Allah Almighty for making me find this site; it's been a great journey and thank you all my reviewers and friends who stayed with me.
