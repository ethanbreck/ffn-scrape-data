Author has written 3 stories for Sailor Moon.

My aim is to share with you my stories. I want to be able to make people happy with my stories. I want people to enjoy the getaway that stories offer. An adventure into another world and another universe.

Books have always been my safe haven. Coming from a non-English speaking background, coming to Australia was one of the hardest moments of my life. I had left behind my comfort in the Philippines to come to a country where I knew no one and spoke little of the language. Eight years old and no one to turn to, it was the wonder of the stories in the books that allowed me to come out of my shell. I still remember the very first book that I had read. As I grew up, I realised the passion that I had with the written word. And so, here I am sharing with you my stories and tales. Stay tuned for more.
