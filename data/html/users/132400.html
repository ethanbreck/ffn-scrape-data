Howdy, y'all!

Since I don't really do this anymore, I can give you a heads up as to where I've ended up. I write for keeps, now. That is to say, I get paid for it! The first book in the current series I'm working on can be found here. Dark fantasy, if you happen to be interested. If I could only carry over fans from here into things that aren't FF related, dang.

My name is Kris, and I was a fangirl.

My epic, Guilt's Cost, was written between 6/1998 to 4/2005. Ergo, it does not relate to Advent Children or Dirge of Cerberus, since it was conceived and written before those things. I guess now, with the knowledge of the above, I would have to say that it's an alternate universe fic. Dang, it seems hard to believe I started that one so long ago. I've raised a child to adulthood since then!

Jaras' Reclamation, the equally epic sequel to Guilt's Cost, has been posted here as well. If you don't like delving deep, deep into the darkness, this story might not be the one for you. It was sort of a random brain baby I had while going through some heavy times. Really heavy times. Things did get better, though, so yanno.

For the most part, unless otherwise noted, my stories have little to do with one another. I tend, however, to recycle my supporting characters time and again. Therefore, if you read through a few, don't be surprised if a few names and faces seem familiar from one tale to the next.

Oh, and for those of you who actually care on some sick level, my smut can still be found at Adultfanfiction Yaoi, straight, whatever, but I don't add new things to that anymore, either. It was fun to experiment with writing different things as I tested out various things.

I appreciate all of the reviews and faves I've received here over the years. You guys rock, and gave the confidence to move on with my writing beyond these simple beginnings. Several of you even became friends I still have to this day in RL. I think I have a story or two up on here and AdultFanfiction that remain unfinished. They never will be. For that, I apologize, but time has rolled on.
