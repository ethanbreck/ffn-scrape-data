Author has written 6 stories for Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon, Tekken, Teen Titans, D N Angel, Inuyasha, and Naruto.

Wow... I haven't been on FF.net in soooo long...! I really missed it :/ So life caught up with me, personal problems, college, nursing programs, homework, social life, boyfriend, yadda yadda right? No excuses, I know, but I guess after I lost my computer (but thankfully had saved some of my more important works) I just sort of put FF.net aside. I don't know what made me come back... I guess I was watching some old anime faves, started looking at some fanART which in turn led me to fanfiction all over again. Ah...! It was inevitable!

Yeah and all while I've been gone, I feel like I've changed soo much; especially as a writer :P I look at my old works and sort of... cringe, haha. Nonetheless I will keep them for memory, their long-gone fans (and some good laughs maybe, heh).

Anyway, I love to read of course, and writing is an outlet for things I have no control over, *hence the fanfiction* I'm also an artist at heart :) I don't think I have a favorite genre; I love comedies, dark fiction, drama and of course romantic works as well. I read more than I write and it takes me a long time to upload a new chapter, as most of you know by now :P It's a bad habit, but habits are hard to break, and it's not my only one, so I have to break those before I can try on this one, unfortunately. I'm not exactly a fan of canon pairings, of course that doesn't mean I hate them all; I have exceptions and I don't always dread the idea. I'm just saying. :)
