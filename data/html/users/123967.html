1/11/14:

I am alive, for what that's worth. My apologies to anyone who has messaged me and not received a reply. Most days it doesn't seem worth getting out of bed, let alone writing replies. For the past ten years or so I've been battling severe depression and anxiety, and unfortunately medication only does so much. Writing used to be an escape for me, but things have gotten so bad that I can't even do that anymore. I hope I can again someday, I really do.

UPDATE (4/2/2012):

My website is down for now. The "Severitus Challenge" will from now on be located in my LiveJournal at:

So, who am I? I'm an obsessed Harry Potter fan, who also nourishes a deep love for Final Fantasy VII and Phantom of the Opera. I fancy myself a writer and musician, though no guarantee if anyone else does.

Severus Snape is my all-time favorite character, idol, and inspiration. To me, he is a perfect model of all that a 'real' person is, including the good and the bad. I am constantly in awe of J.K. Rowling's masterful characterization in him. (I also refuse to believe he's dead...he just took the opportunity to slip away and start his life over :P ). The relationship dynamic between Severus and Harry is also fascinating to me, no matter what form it takes, and it is therefore the primary kind of fanfiction I write and read.

In regards to fanfiction, 'A Father's Sin,' no matter how crappy I think it is now, WILL be finished. I also have a strange fic arc I'll be posting soon, starting with a short Eileen/Tobias fic (with a dash of Petshop of Horrors) that leads to a SS/LE fic. I'll only say one word on it for now...Naga.
