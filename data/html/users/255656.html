Hello everyone,

I have recently been reminded just how much I used to love writing, so after a very, very long hiatus, I'm back on this site.

First order of business is going to be cleaning up "What a Night". I wrote it nearly ten years ago, and it /shows/. Frankly at first I was so ashamed of my juvenile writing that I thought to delete it all, but I made a promise when I first went on hiatus, and I'm keeping it. I'm hoping to eventually finish it, I still have all my notes, fortunately.

I stopped writing because I was asssaulted. I went to the police, and when the legal proceedings were over, 6 years of my life had gone by. It took me a very long time to put my life back together again. To everyone that reviewed, thank you. Even though I'd stopped writing, I still received your reactions in my mail, and eventually, that's what got me back into writing. Most of the reviews I received were incredibly supportive, and I thank you all for that.

I've also written a few slash oneshots that I'll post here eventually. I've posted them on Ficwad and AFF a while ago under a different penname, when this site was still much harder on people posting adult material. Most of the stories I read now are either slash or gen, there are only a few het pairings that I like, so I thought I should warn you now that after I've finished "What a Night", I most likely won't write het again.

I can't promise an exact date for updates, but I'll try and get to it as soon as possible.

Rosaera (formerly Polgara)
