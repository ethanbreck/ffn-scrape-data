Well, it's been a while since I was back here. I'm in university now, and I have a lot less time for ff.net. Because of this, I don't think I'll be able to continue writing ALLD. I still love the fandom, but I don't have the time, effort, and enthusiasm for this story anymore. It's been well over a year since I started it, and the gaps between updates were getting a little too large. I never should have attempted such a long, multi-chapter fic, since I've never finished one before. To those that were hoping for an update - I'm sorry, definitely not happing.

Furthermore, I've removed all my old YGO stories, simply because it physically pains me to see material so old and so terrible. I know that I still get occasional reviews from those who enjoyed them and requested more, but I'm not really involved with this fandom at all anymore, and the inspiration is not there. Sorry again.

It was a hell of a lot of fun while it lasted, thanks for all the wonderful reviews.
