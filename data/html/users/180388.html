Hi Everyone!

Wow, it's been a while since I updated this! Almost 7 years it would seem. Anyway, I'm a 27 year old languages graduate, originally from Manchester, England.

Anyone wanting to join my group, the address is

You can also find my fics at and

I realise it's been a REALLY long time since I updated, but I'm about to embark on the mammoth project that is rereading my Amulet of Time series. I've realised that it's been that long since I updated that I have to reread them to remind myself what they were actually about. Hopefully, this will help me summon the enthusiasm to finish writing the series. No promises, though. The series will remain AU after GoF, as that is how it started out and to change that now would mess with the whole story. I may, however, add some elements mentioned in the subsequent books as and when this becomes relevant. Also, as when I started the series JK Rowling hadn't yet stated that Ginny's name was Ginevra, and not Virginia as I had assumed, I'll leave it as it is now. I can't go changing every mention of her name throughout the whole 400k+ of writing. I will, however, be checking as I go for any grammar/spelling mistakes that may have slipped through the net.
