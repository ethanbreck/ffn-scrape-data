7/14/2013: Good morning followers, visitors, and avid readers…my name is Weasley, I’m a lady, and this is my collection of stories. It occurred to me that some organization might be in order so that you might know better what I do and what I’m all about, so…here goes.

MY ONGOING STORIES

“Lost and Found”—I’ve always wondered how Pete meets Georgina from Clockwork Orange, so this is the latest installment in something I call my ‘For the Love of Pete’ series. In this one, Pete has to do some time in the hospital after wandering around in a depression post-gang-dissolval, and a lot of angst/internal struggles abound.

“Frankenstein of Dystopia”—Nobody’s really written serious stuff for Billyboy until I came around, so…this is a look into what life might have been like for him as a four-year-old.

“Ahab of Dystopia”—I’ve also got this much longer story for Billyboy that details his life from the ill-fated battle with Alex (in the movie) to his life as a policeman (from the novel), and hopefully a lot of happenings in between/afterward. If there’s anyone looking for a break from De Large stuff, feel free to check it out!
