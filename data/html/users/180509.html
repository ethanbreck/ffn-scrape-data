Hey all!

I'm Jackson (and yes I'm a girl named Jackson).

Should I perhaps say something about myself other than a name... hmm.

Well I am 28 years old as of 2013 (maybe I'll update this more often than every 5 years). I am 5'3" with brown eyes and brown hair. I have a daughter who is incredibly sweet and thoughtful for her age. I was married but after many years of counseling it just seemed the marriage was in the way of us being friends, so I am now divorced but oddly enough I am very good friends with my ex-husband and father to my beautiful daughter.

I always wonder what my life might be like if I was born in a different place during a different time.

Buuuuuut... That's enough of that in my opinion.

New stories hopefully to come soon. I got a lot of ideas floatin' around in this noggin just gotta figure a way to get them out. :D

Favorite Pairings:

Kyo and Yuya (Kyo in his own body tho... not Kyoshiro's)

Sesshomaru and Rin (obviously when she's older)

Edge and Rydia

Manji and Rin (Blade of the Immortal anyone? No? Kinda? Maybe?)

Rakushun and Youko (I have much love for this sadly unwell known couple and series; 12 Kingdoms)

Kurosaki and Teru

Other things no one cares about...

I recently deleted some of my stories just because they were from SOOOOO long ago that I was actually rather embarrassed to have anyone read them, they were pretty bad, trust me you aren't missing out on anything. However, I haven't written in about 5 years and I have recently started reading stories again. I just moved back to the states (was living in Germany for the last few years), and I have a decent bit of time on my hands. I'm hoping that I haven't completely lost my ability to write, because I would like to start a new fanfiction.

So I am going to remove all but two of my fictions, and the two I am leaving aren't really worth reading, I guess I'm a sucker for the opinions of others and I don't want someone to think that all I do is read and make comments on other stories without making even a half-assed effort myself. So there is my 5year old effort for you all to curl your lip at, and trust me, I won't be offended, I don't even want to go back and read them for fear I might start crying at the horror of it all.
