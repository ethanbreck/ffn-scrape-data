In fact the movies just one big mind fuck but when you let some time pass then think about how the ones that commited suicide felt you realize what the movie was really telling you. That its not their fault at all, its YOUR fault. They dont want to feel the way they do. What they really want is to find a connection and not feel alone.

That by protending everything is okay and not actively trying to reach out to people you care about your making them feel alone. Humans always feel the worst when they think they're alone. Thats why they commit suicide. Sometimes I look at the world and everything thats wrong with it and just wonder why we do it to ourselves. Why theres so much hate for no good reason. Why we fill the need to take minoritys and tear them apart. But I know why...it makes us feel closer to each other. Thats all we really want. To hate anything we can with all that we are makes us feel closer to each other. To tear apart other smaller groups of people useing anything as an excuse makes us feel closer. When its a smaller group of people they feel close when they fight against the tide.

But when its one person picked on and tornapart by large group of others. That person feels alone and probably will for the rest of their lives if it starts young. Like me. The only time I dont feel totally alone is when I'm talking tomy closest friends, reading, or,listining to a good song. I cant be doing those all the time. I see my friends once a week and I try to read the rest of the time. Art is the only humancreation worth anything. To bad only humans canapperaite it.Writing, painting, building, you name it. Its all about reaching out and connecting to people and to be beutiful.

I totally rec the movie 'Suicide Circle' also known as 'Suicide Club'. Dont take the movie at face value.
