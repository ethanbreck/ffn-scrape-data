Umm... let's see... My mom was sitting on me one day, (I hatched from a Cadbury radioactive egg, mind you) and the egg cracked and the radioactive gunk that had caused the genetic mutation allowing me to have duck wings spilled all over her feet. Because of this, mummy dearest no longer has any feet. But it was a sacrifice I was willing to make. For a few years I ate weeds like other good duck-winged mutants, but then I discovered espresso, chocolate, Jolt, Krispy Kreme, gummie bears, and other wonderful human inventions. Of course, whenever I have a craving for any of these, I turn into Duckzilla and sit on, make nests of, and destroy any nearby towns until my hunger is appeased. My life is so much fun. =D
