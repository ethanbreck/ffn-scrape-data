Author has written 10 stories for Star Wars, Movie X-overs, and Supernatural.

I am a writer what more is there to say :) I know I sound full of it but if I don't say it no one else will. I had decided not to do anymore fanfiction. I was only going to concentrate on what could be published. But I wasn't happy. Here is where the fun stuff happens. This is where I get to play with my favorite characters from some of my favorite shows. Here I get to hone my craft of writing and enjoy reading stories by others. This is where I learn to write for an audience. Also here I can be the review junkie. :)

If you are interested in reading some of my work that is not fan fiction look here http://www.fictionpress.com/u/229152/
