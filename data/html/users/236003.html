Yes, I know hy pen name's Jakob der Ludner, which could actually be a very common name in Germany but my descent is Filipino (speak that language too) I live in America and I speak German and French (however scantly). Talk about international connections! In case you don't speak Deutsch, my name means Jacob the Liar in German. As you might guess, Jakob der Ludner doesn't even come close to my real name. I mean, come on, my first name's David for crying out loud.

I'm currently 13 and currently in 8th grade (die sixth graders!)

I'd probably be an aeronautical engineer in the future, though I'm seriously considering being either a novelist (duh) or professional musician (I play the clarinet^_^) in the future.

I just love writing stories. I've been writing stories since I was five (yes, I'm not kiddin'!). I think the first one was something about going to the Grand Canyon or something. Most of my own private (that is, out of school) ventures since then were fanfictions (Star Wars mostly. Though none of them on this site. I'm sure I can still find them somewhere in my computer files or maybe I could find a printout of them that I could scan and submit to ff,net). I find writng to be an extremely useful outlet for my *extremely* overactive imagination.

Most of the time, I like using my own characters in my fanfics so I can create entirely new personalities without getting any flames from hardcore fans about how their beloved characters are tainted by my writing. Plus, I like using my own personality in some of the characters.

I'm also the philosophical type. I like making quotations and if you've seen any of my stories, you'd know that. I also tend to look blankly into a wall and absorb myself in deep philosophical thought.

Also, I'm working on a novel type fiction that I might post under the originals section. Its a fantasy fic involving elves, sirens, dwarves, humans, little kids(whoa! where'd that one come from?), and people with wings. He he, it's a half-baked idea right now so don't go browsing through the originals yet!!! The prelude's already up top so you can survive on that for a while.
