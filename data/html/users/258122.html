I am Hope.

I love to write.

I recently recived this review:

"You love to write? Know what? You suck at writing. Get a beta reader, and graduate from elementary school before you start writing. Jeez. There are so many mistakes in the first few chapters alone -- it's disgusting. You suck at spelling, you suck at grammar, and you also suck at weaving a story together. It started poorly, continued poorly, and I'm pretty sure it's going to end poorly. For the sake off all mankind, stop writing. Please."

I have no intention of stoping wriitng. I love to write despite my dififucltys and I will keep on writing despite yoru disgust.

Harry Potter

The wails of a lion: chapter 11 is up.

The Legend Series: This series is both original and very typical. I took some typical story lines, 'Harry is kidnapped by voldemort' 'Harry is abused by the Dursleys' 'Harry is rescued from Dursleys by Sirius' 'Harry is dark' 'Harry is secretly a death eater working for voldemort' 'Snape and Harry actually get alone - well sort of' 'Harry is in some strange way related to Voldemort' and put them together in a reasonably original story. This story includes the four following stories, but there will eventually be a fifth one set after ‘No One’s Hero’ and the possibility of a prequel to ‘the Legend of Harry Potter’.
