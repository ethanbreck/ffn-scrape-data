Secret Series Idea:

I got the idea for this story the first night there would never be a new Ep of BtVS (May 27th 2003), and I originally wrote the first chapter of "These Secrets Kept" as a one-off. But! While I was away in Italy, I got the idea for how to not only finish the story, but write it into an entire series. The premise for the series is this:

(1) All stories in the series must have a first chapter that can be read as a one-off.

(2) All stories will be made up of three chapters and a Optional Epilogue (that means you can read any of the stories w/o the Epies and still understand the story).

(3) All stories must have at least one song in them, without turning the entire chapter or story into a single Song-Fic.

(4) The different stories in the Series must stand independent; the reader should be able to read them in any order without feeling lost or overwhelmed - - or feeling like they’re missing details.

~Vixen Raign~
