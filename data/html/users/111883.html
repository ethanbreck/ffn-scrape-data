well...

so much for me... which i oh-so doubt, anyone would want to know...

i became first addicted to anime because of time quest and super boink..

they were shown in a local channel, in channel 13...

oh yeah, i forgot, i'm here in the philippines, and i never had a chance to leave the philippines...

and then, there came patlabor and raijin-oh in channel 9...

and then there came thunder jet and ghostfighter which was still in channel 13.

so, you could probably conclude i am old...

hahaha. 23 and yet still an anime addict, well most friends of mine, under the same age, still do...

even at this age, i still prefer anime and manga (romance) and still in their senior year, mostly at 16 yrs old...

i also love anime and manga under fantasy...

the only one that i like which has a futuristic plot is gundam wing...

well, like is an understatement, i looove that anime!

i rarely like anime and manga that is much suitable for my age...

maybe because i'm still a kid at heart, and maybe in my own head...

i love to sleep but since i love to be awake at night, i barely sleep at all... this is ridiculous...

i really love night time...

which is why i was tagged as a vampire in my own home...

...bangkay ("walking corpse") by a close friend

...multo ("ghost") by another close friend...

i loooooove coffee... there are times i make drink this as if it's water (though it has water!)

i looooove sunset. i hate sunrise.

i love the computer, mainly because of anime...

i also love to drink cocktail especially the ones named bad trip and hell...

i love cross stitch as well... i usually watch anime and do cross stitch...

but then i find myself watching anime and doing my assignments...

i used to work in a call center, in makati and in shaw, under one company,

loves music and just got hooked up with classical instrumental music, especially those songs played with a violin

when i filed my resignation, i rested for several months,

i became a residential manager, nope, not someone who watches over an apartment or something,

i'm a bum at my very own home...

and now i'm studying again...

nope, no for undergrad, i'm taking my master's degree now...

wooooooah!

i never had dreamt of taking one in my entire life!

i'm currently taking masters in medical physics...

so much for being an anime addict...

this is the reason why i mostly can't update oh-so soon...

i'm currently hooked in my CLEMI fic in magic knight rayearth...

i can't get my head off of those two! argh!

and i'm still working on my la corda d'oro fics because i believe tsukimori is meant for hino, period.

i'm also currently reading mangas for vampire knights... and i want zero to end up with yuuki.

if not, i'll take him all by myself!

i'm also reading special a because i love the way hikari and kei act towards one another. hehe.

i also love gakuen alice. natsume is so darn cool!!

of course, i definitely love la corda d'oro. it was one of the best anime i've watched.

did i mention i like this kind of plot where there are a lot of guys hovering over one girl?

i also love the comedy of ouran high school host club. tamaki's cluelessness is one hell of a laugh.

wait, is there sucha word such as cluelessness? oh well...

definitely, gundam wing is one of my all time favorite... heero yuy forevah!

saiunkoku monogatari rocks! go girl power!

prince of tennis, echizen ryoma rules!!

random animes that i love: pretear, jyu-oh-sei, uh, blood trinity, let me think about the rest... i almost forgot

random mangas that i love: meru puri, beast master, wild ones, etc...

so there...
