I've got hardly the tip of the tip of the iceberg up on FF.net as far as fics are concerned. I have a 5 inch binder back home filled with fanfics and art, and that's hardly half of it. I'm selective about what I post, otherwise, I'd be stuck n the school counselor's office due to disturbing and sexual content.

Basically, I'll be pulling down stories for reconstruction (such as Flame's detestion for smokers in OZ: The Untold Story) and will do the corrections as fast as possible. I think that the changes will add to the new image and create a greater emphasis on GW's plot, not on my 'Look at me!' OC. Hopefully this will lead to less disgust on reveiwer/reader and hopefully more readers. Please let that be so.

Also,I will have personalized thank yous to each and every reviewer, whether I know them or not, at the end of each new chapter. Arigato.
