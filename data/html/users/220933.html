ATTENTION ALL! I HAVE A FANFICTION CHALLENGE!

Now, I know some of you must read this. So I pose a challenge to you. I have searched pretty much the entire web, and have not found one fanfiction of the following description.

And so I ask, someone PLEASE write me this fanfiction:

Series: Lupin the Third (Lupin III, Rupan)
Pairing: Goemon/Jigen shounen-ai or yaoi
Rating: Don't care
Other requirements: None

If anyone can write me this fic, I will be forever grateful! *hugs and chocolate chip cookies*
-Jen

Konnichiwa, minna-san! I've finally decided to make myself a profile. And I've been on fanfiction.net for at least over a year. Sad, ne?

Anywho, my name is Jen, and I'm 14. Currently I'm writing YYH fanfiction. Previously I was writing Harry Potter fanfiction. I do plan on finishing them, just not right away, don't worry.

My favorite books are the Harry Potter seriews (4th one in particular), and pretty much fantasy in general.

My favorite anime/mangas are Yuu Yuu Hakusho, InuYasha, Trigun, Silent Mobius, Utena (oh there are lots of inside jokes about Utena), Ranma, Tokyo Mew Mew, .hack//SIGN, Rurouni Kenshin, Yuugiou..geeze, the list goes on and on. I've also begun to watch Lupin the 3rd on cartoon network. A tad hentai-ish, but funny, nontheless.

My favorite pairing has got to be Hiei/Kurama from YYH. Although I also like Hiei/Yuusuke, Kurama/Yuusuke, Hiei/Kurama/Yuusuke, Hiei/Keiko and Kurama/Touya.

I haven't seen any Kurama/Touya ones out there, so I do plan on writing one, when I'm finished with my work in progress, Just Another Day.

I have a blog at rosewhip.pitas.com, and a site at distortedreality.8m.net. At that one I plan on having a writing archive, as well as an art gallery, so if anyone wants to submit something, write me an e-mail at Hi_no_Youkai@hotmail.com. Arigatou and ja ne!

Note: I HATE MUKURO! I HATE HER MORE THAN ANYTHING IN THE ENTIRE SERIES!...thank you.

Oh yeah, more hates: Mary-Sues. Bad grammar. Blatent self-insertions. OCs when made to be a main character and shoved into a relationship with another main character. (I've only seen a few exceptions to this one). OOCness that isn't apologized for. Incest (it's just wrong folks, just wrong). Yuri (*Shrug* just not fond of it)
Series specific hates:
YYH- All that interactive Dark Tournament stuff. Been done too many times. Hiei/Mukuro (I already said, I HATE MUKURO), Stories about the "new" spirit detective. Most of them are cliche, and self-insertion, Mary-Sue types. Kurama/Botan. Just don't like it. Hiei/Botan...are you kidding me?! Yukina/Hiei...again, the incest. *shudders* ...let's see, what else. I think I covered everything for now.

Oh, and don't take it personally if your subject is listed up there. It's just that I've read so many bad ones, that I don't want to read anymore. And I don't really flame people, at least I don't remember flaming people. So, there ya go.
