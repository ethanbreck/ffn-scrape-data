When imagination

comes to life

we are the watchers,

all unseen,

catching a glimpse

of other worlds.

It is easier to read than write, and easier to write when I'm into the genre it's established in. I admire the authors that can consistently update. Next time I start a new story, I want it to be all written out first.

Oh, and the picture is of my first pet, Eddie! Say hello to the cute little lap rat! (and by little I mean size-wize, as he's rather fat, heh)

Be warned, I am a major Hiko fan and especially like stories concerning him.
I am hooked on immortal or supernatural angles to things, like vampires, psychic powers, and spiritual ties between people. I prefer fantasy over scifi, and the swords over guns stories.
I've decided to keep most of my stories platonic, but I like reading a little romance every now and then.
Main projects are Still WIB and SIC.

I love my supporters.Thanks especially owed to Jason M. Lee, Firefury, Calger459, Colleen, and most of all Emiri-chan! Such enthusiasm makes it all worth it.
