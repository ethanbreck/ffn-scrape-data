Author has written 91 stories for Sailor Moon, Harry Potter, and Greek Mythology.

RL has made it a bit difficult to fic at the pace I used to. Ah well. I'm sure everyone will deal.

There's not much anyone needs to know about me aside from that. Minor character maven, picky canon and grammar bitch, a bit anal about characterisation, and once upon a time fairly active in fandom. I still write, just not as frequently, and take most things in stride.

The two biggest pet peeves are as follows:

Please don't tell me to update fics labeled "one-shot". It will never happen. In fact, the next person to do so can go eat a toilet plunger.

Also, please don't copy my characterisations of minor characters, including but not limited to backstories, personalities, or first names (if not given in canon). Should be self-explanatory, but imitation is not the sincerest form of flattery in this case, and I've been burned before. Will get snarky and vengeful if it occurs.

That's about it. Have fun.
