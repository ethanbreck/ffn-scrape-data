Date: September 9, 2008

A returning fanfic writer -- the last fanfic I've written was in 2002. Six years ago! Wow!

I've been writing fanfiction since 1998. I was pushed -- pleasantly -- into this lovely hobby as I read the works of Whitecat, Kuwabara no Miko, Talia Firedancer and many others whose pen-names escape me right now -- who write beautiful Gundam Wing, YuYu Hakusho and Rurouni Kenshin fics. Like them -- and just like many of the fanfiction.net writers -- we found some plotholes we could fill in, play god, and of course, be part of the action.

I'm not the type of author who aims for numbers. I don't have a hundred fics to write (due mostly to a lack of time). And I certainly hate publishing unfinished fics. To be honest, I'm the type of writer who makes sure the fic is actually finished (despite the number of chapters) before I publish them here. I am then left to my leisure as to when the fic will be posted. =) Of course, I don't rely on the number of reviews just to continue writing. As long as I have a story to tell, like it or leave it I say!

But of course, I would appreciate constructive criticism -- point out mistakes, points for improvement, etc.

I admire the creativity of people here. In fact, I like reading more than writing. Then again, as my Geography teacher once said, to be a good writer, one should be a good reader first.

I'm currently in a Mobile Suit Gundam 00 phase, so as long as I find the time and drive to write, you guys might see some treats from me.

Well, I guess that sums things up for me. I'll leave you now to your reading. Cheers!
