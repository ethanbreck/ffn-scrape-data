This is the first change to my page in over... dear lord... twelve years??

A lot has happened in that time, my first story published 12 years ago, but it was incomplete, and will remain so unless I get some inspiration for it. This was written during a very hard time in my life, the hospitalization of my mother due to cardiac arrest. The amazing thing, that even under 20 minutes, she had recovered within 4 months, though never to what she was. What we didn't see was that her life was forever changed. Several life changing operations later, we were told that she could not survive another one.

She passed away eight years ago in 2010.

After this I watched a bunch of different anime. It took all of this time to have another idea for a story.

Story Idea(s):

Legacy of Death (Naruto/Bleach crossover): A nine-year boy, Ichigo appears with his little sisters out of nowhere in a burning area of the forest, survivors of a disaster. They are found by Konoha ninja and brought back where they begin to show extraordinary abilities and start a friendship with five year old Naruto Uzumaki. This will be Ichigo-centric and takes place a number of years before the Naruto story line. Here, Naruto is the same age as Yuzu and Karin.

Current Story:

Strength of Fist and Heart (Naruto AU - DISCONTINUED...currently): My first fan-fiction which was written in 2004. Here Naruto is driven out of the village and thrown to the hands of the Legendary Sucker. If I do anything else with this story, it will first undergo a massive rewrite, but there are no plans for a rewrite in the near future. It was one of the first, if not THE first fan-fiction attempt of Tsunade being the one who raised Naruto.
