Author has written 12 stories for Roswell, CSI, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Without a Trace.
I'm actually called Georgia, but my friends call me me gij or geo or g or little g or... a lot of things, actually, but I like gij.

I've been writing fic since 2000. I've written Roswell, CSI, X-Men, Bring It On, Buffy, and now Without A Trace. I usually only write one fandom at a time, 'cause I don't have the tme or the obsessing capacity to be in more than one fandom at a time. So if you're hanging out hoping desperately that I will one day write more... you've got a long wait coming.

Only a very small fraction of my writing is up on this site, so if you want more, wander on over to "Hear What I Want To", my website. The link is just above this box of writing. I would love you to visit. I'd also love you to comment on any of my writing. Any whatsoever, although flames will be posted around the internet and openly mocked.

I no longer have the time or patience to attempt multi-part stories. The short story, the miniscule story, is my forte. So, no epics. Sorry. Although if you have a Pulitzer Prize winning story line that for some reason you don't want to use, drop me a line, yo?

I think that's all. If you wanna know more, review or email. That's all there is to it.
