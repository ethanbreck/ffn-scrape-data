About Me
I am Tobias Bluesummers. The name has stuck with me since I started watching Trigun. I am a male college graduate who enjoys writing. Many of my stories will be simple one shots, although I do like the idea of getting one or two multi chapter fics up and running. I enjoy hearing what others think of my stories. So please if you read my story let me know what you think, good or bad.

I have a LJ account that is used to write down my stories before they arrive here. Sometimes there are even stories there that aren't up here. So go have a look there if you want to see anything that is not here. t_bluesummers
