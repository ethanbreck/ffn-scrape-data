Author has written 10 stories for Sonic the Hedgehog, Harry Potter, Yu-Gi-Oh, and Twilight.

This is the first time since I think grade 11 that I've edited this.

I'm a second year university student, at a well rounded Canadian University. Currently, I'm enrolled in a honors double major history/philosophy. I have dyslexia, so I have problems sounding out words, and being able to see the difference in words that look a lot like. This means that when I edit, and yes, I honestly do edit, and I change words, I sometimes pick the wrong one in the auto-checker, and don't realize until much later. Like when wonderful readers, whom I love, point it out of me. Then I have to look back and read the thing out loud- that's normally when I catch it. Then I have train myself to spell the word, normally by repetition. The reason why I've told you this is because I honestly believe that you, the reader, desever to know why I make silly mistakes sometimes.
And sometimes it's just because I write at 5am with no sleep. Hahahaha.
