I'm crazy about most sci-fi. Especially The Invisible Man. I LOVE I-MAN!!!!!!! Darien, Hobbes, Claire, Eberts, and even the Official and Alex(though those mooks at the Sci-fi channel didn't give her character enough air time for me to make a decision about her). I enjoy watching them in new episodes, re-runs, episodes I've taped, as well as reading and looking at pictures online, going in fan sites and reading fan fictions in my free time.(every minute of it! hehe) I think I'm more addicted to the show then Darien will -or would have, at least- ever be addicted to counteragent. *hehe*

Another of my new favorites is the show Andromeda. I've taken a liking to Harper, and I think I might start writing for that show as well as The Invisible Man. It all depends on how much time i have. I just feel that Harper isn't appreciated or given enough screen time.

Other sci-fi that I enjoy(not in order): Charmed, Buffy, Angel, X-Files, Animorphs(though I *hate* the cliffhanger ending!), Big Wolf on Campus, So Weird(the ones with Fi in them), Lone Gunmen(their short-lived series), Sliders, and Tolkien's works, which I have recently discovered.

BTW, feel free to leave *lots* of Reviews! :) They're like candy! mmm...reviews. They get me motivated to finish writing unfinished stories, and to write new ones.

Constantly in mourning of all the shows I like that inevitably go down the drain :(
~Marcela86
