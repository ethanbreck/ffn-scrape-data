Hi. :)

I've left this account abandoned for quite a while now. I am back . . . again. I was about 13 when I first got this account, I think. So, my writing has kind of changed. I was deleting some of my older stories (which involved the Powerpuff Girls, et cetera =P), but decided against deleting the rest... just because. Maybe some people will find them amusing. I do. (Rather embarrassing, too... but. cough.)

Anyways. Hello, I'm Alcyone (here, at least). I'm either 2130 or somethingelse years old, I'm obviously a writer, and most importantly, I'm a Christian.

Previous names I used here were Cool Buttercup and Elf of Rivendell. You might recognize one of them, or maybe both if you were reading some of my stuff.

And yes, I love Tolkien's works.
