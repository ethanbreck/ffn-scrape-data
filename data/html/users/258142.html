Hello! I'm blinkie and I'm back! I took a nearly 2-year break from writing because I felt unappreciated, especially that two of my fics - "Salesmen Saiyuki" and "Untouchable Hakuryuu" - were removed by the admin because of "new rules" on form. Both were Saiyuki fics. Ah, whatever. They gave people their share of delight anyway.

4 years ago I started writing at fanfiction.net. And although I've been absent for long periods of time, they were never permanent :)

Fandoms I write for:

Gundam Seed/Destiny - Concentrated mostly on Yzak, Yzak/Dearka, and Talia/Gilbert. Fellow Talia/Gilbert fans, hit me up.

Gravitation - Most of my Gravi fics are humorous and more often than not involve the whole crazy bunch. Check out "Ryuichi Hosts..." (unfinished) and "Tohma's Mystery Lover" for crack.

Gensomaden Saiyuki - I love the pairing of Gojyo/Hakkai and Gojyo/Sanzo (yaoi) and Shien/Nataku (strictly platonic). I also love exploring the character of Shien. He's a very mysterious one. Sanzo is all-time smexy ;p

Some more of my favorite animes (that I haven't written for yet) are Yami no Matsuei, Samurai Champloo, Gankutsuou: The Count of Monte Cristo, Gantz, Great Teacher Onizuka, Getbackers, Tenjou Tenge, Sakura Wars, Clamp School Detectives, Ayashi no Ceres, and Tokyo Babylon.

My favorite pairings:

GUNDAM SEED/ DESTINY:

Yzak x Dearka, Yzak x Shiho, Yzak x Fllay, Talia x Gilbert (I think I'm the only fan of this pairing!)

GRAVITATION:

Taki x Ma-kun, Taki x Tohma, Taki x Ken-chan, Tohma x Ryuichi

SAIYUKI:

Gojyo x Sanzo, Shien x Nataku (platonic), Homura x Sanzo, Gojyo x Hakkai

TENJOU TENGE:

Shin x Maya, Bunshichi x Isuzu, Masataka x Maya

GANKUTSUOU: COUNT OF MONTE CRISTO:

Franz x Albert, Count x Albert

I'm an avid reader of stories featuring these pairings so if you have one, send me a message and I'll be reading and reviewing :) I hope you enjoy my stories! Reviews are always, always appreciated :) Thank you so much!
