Profile:

ABOUT ME:

- Name: Wouldn’t you like to know.

- Description: Of course, I could lie and pretend that I'm a worldly blonde reaching 6"1 with blue eyes and long legs that models for Christian Dior, but I'll just leave you to ponder the truth, which is a lot less glamorous.

- Other Hobbies: Meh. Reading. Nursing original stuff. The normal goings-on.

Pen Name: Dagonett

-Dagonet was one of King Arthur’s knights of the Round Table. Knighted by Arthur himself, he was also the court fool, or jester. More than a little dishonest, he was also very brave and loyal, loved by King Arthur. Though his skills at combat left something to be desired, Dagonet was a hero during the last battle, where he proved his loyalty and love to his king by being one of the last living knights to stand by him.

I chose “Dagonett” as my Pen Name because I adore the fellow to no end, and someone already has the name ‘Dagonet’. He’s a very minor character in Malory, and virtually untouched by following authors. *snatches Dagonet and runs away madly*

STORIES:
Very few.

- 'First Kiss' I wrote on my own, the idea was nagging at me for much too long to be ignored – I suppose you could say it’s a ‘new’ ship, Harry/Myrtle, but I don’t support it! ;)

- 'Career Advice' was inspired by a comment I saw on a Harry Potter forum: an offhand parenthetical thought that I think went as follows: "what I would have given to see Draco's career advice!" I saw it and went, "Holy Saint Smurf! Brilliant one shot idea!" Thus... 'Career Advice'.

- And finally I've posted 'Big Bad Draco Malfoy'!! I'm glad to see at least SOME people found it as amusing as I do... Check it out!!

WIPS:
I am one of those annoying people that doesn't like to post stuff until it's finished. MY reason is that I tend to feel pressured and such, and lose interest in the fic, then get guilty when people beg for more... *rethinks 'people' and decides plural is an exaggeration*

Waiting in the Wings:

1. Ten Ways to Get Draco Malfoy:
A novel-length, action/adventure fic that’s essentially d/g but really centers around good old Drakey more than Ginny. The ‘get’ in the title refers not to ‘get you as my boyfriend’ but get as in ‘make your life miserable’. It’s soooo loooong – I’ve been writing random scenes for about a year now, and it spans about 275 pages total. Good lord, that actually just registered.

2. I would also like to do another non-slash Draco/Snape fic (for some reason I just can't get into slash... dunno why). Someone suggested that I do a one-shot of the scene after the "You nabbed my father, Potter. Prepare to die, at the hands of my hulking and menacing and frankly rather stupid denizens!" Meh... I don't really know. I've always thought Draco might be the type to help Snape out after hours once or twice a week... slicing the beetles and putting away the dried newts and such. More for the pleasure of being able to really TALK to someone, though... I've been toying with the idea of a one shot of a scene like that. Dunno when I'll write it, though... *peers around shiftily*

3. I've had one fic waiting in the wings for a few months now... if anyone's ever heard of the book 'The Epicure's Lament' by Kate Christensen (it's an excellent and well-written book, essential reading to any Draco fan), it's basically going to be a fic modeled after that. I've written a few pages of it and I adore it, but I'm majorly stuck on the plot, reminding me why I normally avoid writing Adult!Draco fics... basically, the story will be written in journal format, from the POV of thirty-six year old Draco Malfoy, hermit and cigarette-addict. He's lived alone for eight years, but when his old Hogwarts cohort Blaies Zabini decides to move in, the past comes back to haunt him. D/G, humor/general/romance/maybe drama.

4. Currently started another one-shot... it will be D/G, of course, and longer than 'Big Bad Draco Malfoy'. I haven't quite figured out the plot yet, but I'm having visions of various things I'd like to throw together in one fic. Don't know when it will be out, but as I'm out of school and my days are free until July, I should be churning out stuff at an alarming rate.

NOTABLE HAPPENINGS:
Only two fairly noteworthy things.

- Saw HP 3 in theaters. Made a note in my journal afterwards. It read simply: 'Draco very spotty. Ginny very round-headed. Hermione very annoying. Ron very adorable. Harry very amusing. Dumbledore very vague. Had much fun. Would highly recommend.' As you can see, my dexterity with the English language extends far beyond the world of fandom.

- Doing a major plot overhaul on 'Ten Ways'. I'm beginning to drown in all the d/g-ness, and when I woke up at one thirty in the afternoon and I finally registered that it's summer and not just a gloriously extended weekend, I leapt at my chance to re-do what's bothering me. I really want to finish it, folks. It's overtaken my world -- I work on it every day. Why is the work not coming along, then, you ask? Good question. I'm not so sure myself.

SHIPS:
Draco/Ginny forever, man. I am as avid a Fire and Ice shipper as they come.

OTHER SHIPS:
I've only really got two other serious ships... Ron/Hermione, but I don’t think I’d ever write them as a feature couple, and the HMS Icicle, Lucius/Narcissa.

FAVORITE HP CHARACTERS:
Draco, Draco, Draco, Ginny, Girl or Boy! Blaise, Ron, and sometimes Harry. Did I mention Draco?

OTHER FANDOMS:
Are you kidding?! HP all the way! J.K. Rowling is a GOD!

THANKS AND OTHER NOTES:
To anybody who's actually taken the time to review, or even just read my stuff. Je te donne mon coeur (pas serieusement).

A reviewer named FieryAngelSprite suggested I do a fic on Draco's children's vocabularies after 'Big Bad Draco Malfoy'. *grins evilly* Well, a novel-length story that is in the very early stages of plot-dom right now (like a second or third sequel to 'Ten Ways') feautres his two sons, and, I suppose, THEIR vocabularies (one of them in praticular is going to have very extensive linguistic abilities). So, heyyy ya.

Also, you may notice I took off 'Ask Voldemort.' It'd been nagging at me for a while - let's just say it didn't come at ALL close to matching up to my other work, prob. because I wrote it two years ago while still new to the fandom.

Oh, and another thing: thanks to all the people that reviewed 'Big Bad Draco Malfoy'. I really appreciate it! (and The Breeze went on to review all my fics... and liked them. I was very impressed)

ET FIN:
That’s pretty much all I have to say. Thanks for reading, and again, please wait for 'Ten Ways'. I promise you won’t be disappointed.
