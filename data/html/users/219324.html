Hello! I'm a first year college student whose favorite pastime is, oh no you guessed it, writing! xD I don't know why, but I feel the more I write, the better I become as a person...odd, ne? o_o'' Yes, prehaps, but even I learn lessons from some of the stories I write. Why? Because they form a mind of their own. The characters I create or, just borrow (fanfiction) all develope in their own little way. Oh no! I'm rambling, again...

I'm a lazy writer, I will admit. However, once I start something, I'll be sure to finish, so no worries there. xD I don't like to leave people hanging (I know how it feels). But, I have an issue with getting stories finished quickly due to my inability to write a proper ending. Yes, I'm full of absolute fail when it comes to that. If I take too long to do an ending, just harass me back into work.

I've been feeling awfully dead on inspiration lately. So, if you have any request for me (ones that reside within DTB, or Katekyo Hitman Reborn) go ahead and pm me! I might use it. XD -is that desperate for help-

OH! And check out my deviantart account too. XD I'm still learning how to color and all that, but bleh...

To those who want to read my unfinished fanfics, I'm so sorry, but you'll have to wait a while. x_x Real life has been smacking me in the face with a fish lately, so you'll have to be patient. I'm so so sorry, but I'm still on my hiatus. Forgive me! ;A;

Anywho! All that aside...
