Author has written 1 story for Digimon.
Okay, firstly, I'm a person who loves Digimon very much. I make fics just for the fun of it and ALSO, make something that couldn't happen in the anime, happen. As my pen name indicates, I write story on Takari. Mostly, all my fic will be medieval and ALL the characters from Digimon (Adventures,Tamers, Frontier) will be in it (This is why I love fanfic).

My current story is 'Destiny'. A medieval fic that focuses on seven characters, but of course, as i said, others will be coming in as well. This fic will have Takari (Definitely), Kenyako, Taiora, Mimato, LeeRuki, TakaJuri, and maybe another couple from Frontier (Takuya/Izumi or maybe Kouji/Izumi). There will be other couples as well. Those above are confirmed ones.

I'm planning to make yet another medieval fic after this one (This one only have the Digimon Adventure 01 and Digimon Adventure 02 casts). Of course, I'm still plotting that one out. And it won't be post up until I finish 'Destiny'. And after more thinking, I've come up with another two more new fanfics. One of them is an interactive fic (The characters audition will be posted when I plan to start it at... probably next year). The other one is a crossover between Digimon Adventure 02 and Digimon Tamers. In any case though... my head is practically swimming with plots and strorylines! AHHH!!

Posted on the 17th of May, 2004 at 4.30am*

Surprise? It has been awhile hasn't it? I mean, with my story on hiatus for so long, and all of my fans waiting for the next chapter (Yea rite! ;p)... Well anyway, let's not waste precious space, and get straight to the point. First of all, I'll assure all readers that I've NOT forgotten Digimon, and I am definitely NOT stopping my ficcies. In fact, I've started to write again, aftre taking such a long break from it. I hope no one blames me for it... I mean, Digimon has been gone from the news for quite awhile now hasn't it?

But then again... this is what fanfics are for :) I just decided to dig up some past fanfics that I've collected and started reading them (such as Between these Walls, Caspian's stories, Time Lady's, Aphrael's Ground Zero, and alot more). Just when I come to TK Takaishi's Seihad, I felt my heart running once again (You people will know what I mean if you read his works :D). So here i am now, this early in the morning, trying to reach out to all the Digimon fans out there. Just remember to keep the Digimon alive in our heart, even though they are no longer here (Sounds familliar? :p).

Okay, I'll cut the crap-talk here then! I'll bid you all who stumble upon my page a good day ahead of you, and for my readers, don't lose hope in me :D

Well then, I'll see you all soon in the next Chapter of Destiny ~The Beginning of the End~
