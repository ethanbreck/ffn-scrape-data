01/09/2009

Wow! So I've recently realized I haven't updated my profile in more than 6 years!

Let's see. That baby I had just had when I last updated is now 6 1/2 years old and in first grade! Since then we've lived in NC, GA, TX adn now live in Okinawa, Japan, but will be moving back to Long Island this summer. I still love Rent and my 6 year old is a little Renthead in training. I haven't written anything in a long time, but I'm trying to get back into it. I really enjoy the Janet Evanovich books, and am trying to put together some new fics based on that. We'll see how it goes!

Here's my old profile from back in 2002! Hey, I started reading RENT fanfiction about two years ago, and actually started my own RENT fanfiction over a year ago, and am finally getting around to completing it and posting it here! I used to live in NJ about 20 minutes from the city, and miss it dearly. My husband and I now live in NC and just had a baby in mid July. He is the most wonderful, cutest thing! I am a big fan of RENT, but just love going to the city to see shows whenever we go home to visit.
