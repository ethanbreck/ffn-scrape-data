Stray Dogs Work Wonders (Tsubasa Chronicle)

Another Tsubasa one shot, on the completely opposite emotional spectrum. R!Syaoran in the main character in this one, and it focuses on his relationship with Sakura and his clone, as well as his feelings about both. I admit, it’s pretty emo, but I really like it. R!Syaoran is in need a of a long, long, hug.

Slight R!Syaoran/Sakura, but not really. One shot. Complete.

Never Spoken (Bleach)

Matsumoto Rangiku is doing the only thing she can think of to get her mind of Gin…meaning paperwork. By chance, she finds an old note in one of her desk drawers, which only throws her farther into memories and thoughts of silver-haired former 3rd division taicho.

This is a GinRan fic, a rather bittersweet one shot set after Aizen’s betrayal. This was my first attempt at a one shot, and I think I pulled it off. Gin and Rangiku are probably my favorite anime couple (even thought we don’t know if they are or ever actually were a couple…). I really like this, and I thoroughly enjoyed writing it.

GinRan. One shot. Complete.

Breaking the Surface (Pirates of the Carribean)

James Norrington makes his way back to the living world after he decides he isn’t quite ready to be dead. Once he’s back, James washes up onto the shore of Palma de Mallorca, Spain. There, James is given another chance at life, and love.

This story is my baby. It is my most favorited, most reviewed, most viewed… I am very proud of it. A lot has gone into it, and it is so gratifying to see that so many people like it.

James NorringtonOC. Complete.

Lilies, Everlasting (Bleach)

After the war with Aizen has been settled, Abarai Renji finds himself promoted to captain of the fifth division. After a wild and raucous party celebrating his promotion, Renji wakes up in bed and finds he is not alone… Enter Tsukinai Suiren, third seat of the ninth division. Not only has Renji had a drunken one-night stand with this alluring shinigami whom he’s never met before, but Suiren is soon promoted to fukutaicho of the fifth squad, which ensures much awkwardness for all involved parties.

If BtS is my baby, this story is my love child from a steamy affair. I promised myself I wouldn’t start anything new until I’d finished BtS, but alas, this story jumped out one day. I have to say, it’s coming along nicely. Renji is one of my favorite characters, and while I think he and Rukia did love each other (romantically) at one point, I think she and Ichigo belong together now. And so, that leaves poor Renji all alone. He is my favorite character in Bleach, so I couldn’t let that happen. Suiren is probably my favorite OC that I’ve ever created. I want her sword.

RenjiOC. Incomplete.

Reminiscence (Bleach)

A series of one-shots from Ichimaru Gin's POV while he is in Hueco Mundo. Each of the installments focuses an aspect of his relationship with Matsumoto Rangiku, or a memory of their shared pasts. This will grow slowly, as I find the chapters hard to do-- Gin is very hard to write. I enjoy them a lot, though I don't really know if Gin is all that in character.

Since the stories are all his thoughts and memories I guess it doesn't really matter, since we don't ever really know what he is thinking (does anyone? Does Kubo Tite even know...?). I've tried to add the factors that make him Gin into each installment, like his leaving all the time and his attraction to power, etc. They're sad (they'd have to be-- you can't tell me Gin's not a little bit sad), because I believe Gin does have some good in him (just not enough...), at least where Rangiku is concerned.

Parts of this collection were inspired and influenced by MatsuMama and her wonderful story 'Blood and Ashes'. So-- go read it!

GinRan. COMPLETE.

Reflection (Bleach)

A companion piece to 'Reminiscence', this time, from Rangiku's POV after Gin has left for Hueco Mundo.

I was dying to do this about halfway through 'Reminiscence', because I love this pair far too much to let them go. They are both incredibly complicated and hard to write, but I find them so fascinating and (in my opinion) so amazing together that I couldn't resist. I will probably have more to say about this as it progresses, but the description above for 'Reminiscence' covers things pretty well.

GinRan. INCOMPLETE.

MatsuMama and I have sort of a ByaOC challenge going, so we'll see what becomes of this one. But I'm freakin' PUMPED.

I had one half-way done, but then my hard-drive died...These may come up whenever I get them done, so keep your eyes peeled :3
