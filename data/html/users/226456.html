I caught the fanfic-reading flu very early on, and then it wasn't long before I started writing. I don't have the pretense of being an awesome writer, but I write what I like, and I'll work on fics until I'm fully satisfied before posting them. English isn't my first language, so even though I try my best with grammar and spelling, sorry if sometimes some sentences don't seem right.

I'm mainly a video games fan, but I'll dab in anything if the plot's good (or at least the characters are likeable) and that's really all there is to know about me...

I usually make it a point to reply to every review I receive, and review everything I read (unless I can't find a single good thing to say, because my policy in life has always been 'if you don't have anything nice to say then shut up').
