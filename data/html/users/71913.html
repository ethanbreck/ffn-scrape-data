Well, what is there to know about me?
I sometimes sign my reviews with the name Thryraci, usually only when I'm not signed in. I live for anime and belive in readable eye-candy. I have a good sense of humor and it shows up in all my stories, even if it is only as a sideways joke, funny phrase or "sight" gag in the tale. There will be a little dark language and humor as well, but it's all tastefully done. Need I say more?

For those unfamiliar with me I specialize in science fiction, humor, fantasy, and supernatural stories. I'm a serious fan of 'Original Characters' and probably every story of mine will have one (or two, or three...). That's what's great about being a Fanfic author, your world, your rules and you can have as many passing characters as you fancy.

About my surroundings:

I live where hurricanes and giant prehistoric mosquitoes are common and you *must* mow the lawn once a week or it turns into jungle.

My muse and I make a personal appearance in every story we work together on. And we work together for nearly all of them. Not pure self-insertion, that's a rather easy way in I think, but usually as a certain passerby or even a character that plays a part.

Here's a game: try to find us! 'Tis very fun actually.

I also check my spelling and grammar (A distressingly large amount of Fan Fic authors don't do that) Good thing I'm a grammar nut ;)
