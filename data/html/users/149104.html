Hi, I'm Tempest.

You will notice that I am not a very prolific writer, but I do love to read. Perhaps eventually I'll write more (goodness knows I have enough ideas), but for now I think I shall just make out a list of my ultimate favorite fanfics ever, by category, in no particular order.

Pirates of the Carribbean:
"The Measure of a Man" (author unknown)

Yes, unfortunately I'm starting my list with a story that is no longer available here on FFN, and hasn't been for years. I have no idea what happened to it, but I assume that the author got tired of it, as so often happens. It was epic though. I don't remember exactly what it was about (since I read it 6 or 7 years ago) but it involved an evil pirate with a ship with red sails, a poisoned dagger, and a ball at the governor's mansion. It was, and probably still is, the best written and best researched fanfic I have ever read and I am still hoping that it is someday re-posted. But since it's not currently available I'll have to go with...

"Revolutions of the Heart" by latebloomer04
'Elizabeth and Will were engaged but due to a cruel twist of fate, they were separated. 7 years later, amid the backdrop of the Revolutionary War, fate gives them another chance at love. But are they brave enough to take it?'
