Old Faces/Castles now has its own forum! Yay! Drop by and have a look around.

Think it’s about time I made my profile a little more interesting. Not that many people bother to read them anyway. If you’re like me, however, and you’re curious about complete strangers, read on!

Jagged Epiphany is…

…really named Alicia. Seriously. It’s on my birth certificate and everything.

…twenty years old and was born on February 23.

…proud to tell you she is from Sydney, Australia.

…a brunette with greenish eyes. I have no idea what my height or weight is. Let’s say average on all accounts.

…a recent university graduate. I have an Arts degree in English, which is quite useless when it comes to getting a job. For some reason employers don’t care if you can recite poetry and quote literature. Who’d have thought? Oh well. I loved learning it.

…determined to become a published author. I’m here doing fanfiction to improve my writing. It’s worked bloody well so far.

…a soccer player. Centre-mid, if you want to be specific.

…a huge rugby league fan. Go the Newcastle Knights and Andrew Johns!

…prone to go off on feminist rants. This is a by-product of some of my uni studies.

…awfully fond of tigers, frangipanis, the beach, her dictionary, literature, poetry, her reviewers, her friends, her (extensive) book collection, the work of Joseph Campbell, her notebook computer, her beautiful dog, her iTunes playlist, Australia, and life in general.

...frightened of spiders, snakes, eels, the Cat in the Hat, every horror movie ever made...and Tom Cruise, which goes without saying these days.

…picky about grammar and spelling. Note that typos are a different thing. I don’t expect everyone to be perfect, but I appreciate it more when people make an effort.

…quite old-school in regards to favourite films, music and television shows. Films: Casablanca, Breakfast at Tiffany’s, original Star Wars trilogy. Music: The Beatles (best ever!), Elvis, Queen. Television shows: MASH, Blue Heelers, Jeopardy.

…completely and utterly in love with book stores and just books in general.

…always happy to get emails and messages from people.

…having some of her stories translated into German by the wonderful Angel de la Luna. Amazing, no?

…the beta reader for Twisted Little Star and cowTXgirl. I’m also willing to be beta reader for anyone else writing George/Alicia.

…sure that you all stopped reading this a long time ago!

COMPLETED STORIES-
April's Wish: Everworld story. The first fanfiction I ever wrote. Did it ages ago. Complete now with two alternate endings. One with David and one with Christopher.

Yule Ball-take two: My first Harry Potter fic. Also my worst one. Don't read it. Then again, most of my reviewers liked it. I have better stuff anyway. It was just a sort of feeler story to get in to fanfic.

When an Angel Loses her Halo: Hehe. I love this story. Especially the title. Just popped into my head one day. My first (and only) foray into the Marauders Era. Pretty good considering it was my first time away from the twins. Though, all I really did was substitute one set of pranksters for another.

Star Light, Star Bright: It's sort of the sequel to When an Angel Loses her Halo. Obviously not all the characters will be there. I really like this story and I like Aurora (character I created for Sirius).

Old Faces, New Tricks: It feels wonderful to bump this baby up to the completed list. It's my favourite fic so far. It's also the best one, in my opinion. There's a sequel coming up. All fifty chapters follow Angelina, Alicia, Katie, Fred, George and Lee through their years at Hogwarts. Many highs and lows.

STORIES IN PROGRESS-

Castles In The Air: This is the sequel to OFNT. Basically, it's going to be the sequel to end all sequels. I'm throwing myself into this story completely. Even if you aren't familiar with the main characters and haven't OFNT, I'd recommend it. If anything, it's guaranteed to be a well-written story.

UPDATE: 30/01/06. Have started chapter 3. I promise that in this one you'll find out what happened between Alicia and Oliver. I'll be sure to duck the rotten fruit you all throw at me.

Slytherin Trilogy: 'When Planets Softly Collide' was meant to be a one-shot. I was meant to write it then walk away. Back to the safe familiar world of Gryffindor-only romances. I can't leave Warrington/Alicia alone now. I think I've been corrupted by everyone at Overcoming Rivalry. Damn them.

UPDATE: 30/01/06. There's won't be another sequel to 'Planets'. Instead I have a Marcus/Katie one-shot and a longer fic featuring M/K, A/W and Angelina/Montague. Blame HPfan000.

Various one-shots: 13/12/05. One-shots that are floating around will (separately) feature Ron/Hermione, Remus/Tonks and James/Lily.

Aren't Little Girls Made of Sugar and Spice?: Don't know where I got the title from. It's a Harry Potter/Alicia Spinnet story. No, that's not a typo. Harry/Alicia. You can thank my lovely shipmates at Chasing the Snitch for the inspiration.
UPDATE: 30/01/06. Have started the next chapter. No more than a page of progress so far. Better than nothing, though.

There you go! Information about me and my stories. If you're a fan of one or more of my stories then remember to check back here for the latest. Please read and review my stories anyway! And check out some of the stuff in my faves list.
Ciao!
