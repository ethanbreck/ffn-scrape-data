Hello! My name's Carol and I enjoy a good story weather it's books, anime, graphic novels, etc. Perhaps it's because of my wild imagination I've always had.

I do have some original story ideas, but as of now, I work on them when I can. I am not great at editing but I do try, though I cannot guarantee I will catch and correct everything and that it will be one hundred percent correct in everything. I can only promise to try. I do appreciate constructive critiques so long as they are constructive and helpful and not negavtive or derogatory.

Examples of appreciated comments:

I thought the story had promise but I noticed during your editing, you missed putting an e on the end of because in chapter 2, second paragraph down. (You can even copy and paste the section and point it out if you wish) I also noticed he instead of him or me/myself instead of I. While your stoy has great potential but I do not agree with how you portray Ryuichi Sakuma, Leon Orcot, etc.

Examples of Unappreciated comments:

You idiot, you had a lot of misspelled words, go correct them. Or, Hey stupid, what language do you speak? Your stories full of mistakes. Dude, there's no way this is a credible story, it's not even cannon. Do you even know what cannon is or have you even read the manga or seen the anime? Etc. etc.

Those type of comments will be erased. I will warn you now, that all my stories will have the Alternate Universe label because I am not the original creator, nor can I read her mind. Plus, with categories that have over the top slap stick such as Gravitation, I prefer something a little less on the incredulous side. Or in the case of Petshop of Horrors, I tend to pair D X Leon with D as the 'leader' (I'm really not big on the terms seme or 'top' because they don't really fit in my mind. If I remember correctly, 'top' tends toward a certain lifestyle that I honestly cannot credibly write in and Seme is japanese slang.) in the relationship. That does not mean he orders Leon around, simply he tends to take more initiative or instigate a situation that will enable Leon to take initiative. Alternate Universe leave me more room to play and as this is fan fiction, and not the real thing, nor am I publishing for money or as a 'career', I choose to write for fun.
