A Little Info About the Writer

A little about me well I’ve been writing here at Fanfiction for almost ten years. Most of its crap but I don’t write for the reviews (even if they do give me the warm fuzzies) I write to create something new out of another’s genius. I write mainly slash pairings if that bothers you don’t read my stories. There are thousands of stories out there go read one of those and leave me in peace.

One day I hope to write my own novel and win an Oscar or two. I love telling stories and talking (must be the Irish in me). I fell in love with Europe when I was 16 years old and can’t wait to go back. I also work part/full time at a job that I hate so I can get money to go to school to make money doing something I love.

So that’s me in a nutshell…
