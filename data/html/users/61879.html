About Me : Vital Stats: For anyone who cares, I'm a 15-year-old American female who loves Harry Potter, the Lord of the Rings (I'm in the process of reading the books after adoring the movies), Janet Evanovich, Catherine Coulter, and all things related to The Sims. This therefore means I'm drawn to reading, writing, acting, movie-going, long baths while reading, internet browsing, video-game (the Sims, that is) playing, and voicing my opinion whether or not it is called for rather loudly.

Love Tangles: For as long as I can remember I've loved Draco/Ginny (for the passion), but I've now been hooked into the Harry/Ginny ship (for the chemisty), and Ron/Hermione ship (for the hilarity and pure magnitism.) And of course if you can find me a well-written story full of chemistry about any other characters, I'm sure I'll love it too.

Contacting the Great and Mighty Me: Though this has proved hard to do, being that apparently my e-mail account was shut down sometime this past year without my knowledge (shows how much I keep track of my fanfiction life), I've renewed it at the address above and will be trying to log in as much as possible to keep up fanfiction life. I love to beta-read, and any comments, rants, or questions will henceforth be adored by me. : )

My Stories: Sad to say, but I probably won't be continuing any of my stories for a long while unless a plot whacks me upside the head whilst I'm driving along in my boat of a car, and if this happens chances are I'll be knocked unconscious and therefore unable to continue my stories in my comatose state. : ) But do feel free to e-mail me with ideas, because who knows what will unleash the slingshot of inspiration!
