Hey.. Using my sister's old and unused account. Decided that if I ever wrote fanfiction that wasn't anime it will go in this account. ; I really want to write a Roswell fanfiction that centers around Maria and Michael. I love them together! I actually never really got into Roswell until a few days ago ((Dec. 21)). Yes it's true. . I mean when Roswell came out on TV I watched a few espisodes here and there, but I never got into it. I thought it was okay..maybe it was because I was young and we only had an antenna and the WB never worked well. Anyway my older sister came back from basic training for the Army on Dec. 20, and while she was there she had bought the 1st and 3rd season boxsets of Roswell. o.o;; I had nothing to do and I thought to myself that maybe I can finally understand what happened in Roswell, so I watched the 1st season and...IT WAS FREAKIN' AWESOME! XD I immediately loved Max & Lix and Michael & Maria together. I couldn't believe that after all these years I never realized how great the show was. u.u; I actually stayed up for 22 hours straight watching the 1st season. hehe.. Since my sister didn't have the 2nd season I just watched the last espisode of the 3rd season and for some reason I thought the 3rd season was gonna be better than the 2nd because Tess wasn't really in it. ;; Was I wrong...I think a day or two later my sister bought the 2nd season. I was so excited! XD I watched it non-stop. haha And even though I despise Tess, it was very good, better than the 3rd season actually. So when I was done with that I planned on watching the 3rd season non-stop as well, but after watching a little bit of the 1st espisode I got a little bored and did other things. x.x I've only watched about 5 episodes of the 3rd season. I wish it was better.. T.T Other than that, Roswell's an awesome series and I've literally become a big fan over night. heh Let's see if I can get my friends into it.

Anyway, I have other fanfiction accounts..like 2 other ones. One I share with my sister ((in the beginning we worked together but now I've only been writing)), and the other is my original one where I put .hack//SIGN fics and any other anime fic that is only by me. I have 10 fics all together, and I haven't been updating them since last year. I try but I get distracted by other things and school.sigh I hope that one day I will get all of them ((or at least the ones I really like)) done because I hate it when authors don't update and look..here I am becoming one of those authors. T.T I guess that will be all for now...
