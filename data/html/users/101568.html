"Time is the most powerful force. It heals all wounds and reveals all truths, but no man will ever control it. We are all powerless in its currents."

Um, wow. Having not touched my fanfiction.net account in almost two years, I am extremely grateful to those of you who have reviewed since my period of gross inactivity. I do still write, yes. My interests have turned largely to original fiction, and most of these I archive on my own.
Not that I'm saying my fanfiction.net is dead and inresurrectible. Far from it. If there's anything I've learned in life it's that finality is never final.

So for those of you who adored The Rainbow Connection, After the Rain Has Fallen, The Long Road Home, Red Wine, Two Beds and a Coffee Machine, or Numbing the Pain...I thank you from the bottom of my heart. You finding joy in what I do gave me the strength and ambition to strive for higher standards and a drive to become better at something that not only makes me happy, but has the capacity to make others happy as well. In time, maybe, my pretty multi-chaptered Star Trek fics will be updated. If the masses wish it...who am I to stand in the way?

I used to think, on this great big website, that often I fell under the radar of the mass audience because I wasn't very good and it drove me insane. But now I see that I don't always fall under the radar...and there's a difference between "being good" and "being enjoyable" anyway.

Thank you all. :)

~Stella
