Author has written 3 stories for Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and Peter Pan.
Greetings! They call me Raisse, and so you may as well. I love to write, but I usually cannot find time to do so. I love to read - but I usually cannot find stories worth reading. That's why I'm glad Fanfiction.net exists. I find many wonderful, imaginative stories here. Stories full of love, beauty and drama. I am one for a good romance. And by 'romance' I don't mean an NC-17 sex-filled three pages of plotless nonsense. I crave a story where the hero and heroine truly love each other through all circumstances and all overcome great odds... that is the kind of story that I love.

I love relationships, the more unconventional the better, for those are the truly interesting stories, and those are usually the sort of stories I search out to read. Pairings I like to read about include: Harry/Ginny (Harry Potter), Draco/Hermione (Harry Potter), and Ryu/Chun-Li (Street Fighter), Sabé/Obi-Wan (Star Wars) and Legolas/Éowyn (Lord of the Rings).

I love to recieve reviews, and I also love to write reviews. Please read one of my stories, if one might catch your interest. Thank you, and until we meet again, Namarie.
