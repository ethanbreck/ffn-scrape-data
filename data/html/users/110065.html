Author has written 9 stories for Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, Vampire Princess Miyu, and Evangelion.

(April 12, 2015) Yes, I'm still alive - and still getting favorites on Masquerade! It's always nice to have one thing that is so well received as Masquerade has been. 14 years_and counting. I don't actually say it anywhere in Masquerade, but I know some people wanted a sequel. Well, there is one - Curtain! If you want a continuation/closure for Masquerade, that's the story that will give it.

I don't really have time to watch anime anymore (darn adult responsibilities!), so my fan fiction material has kind of dried up. I have an account at Fictionpress, and eventually I will get my Star Wars AU story fully onto paper/word doc and uploaded. Someday...

Anyway, that's it for me for now. I do still get a smile on my face to see a person reading (and enjoying) some of my old stuff. I hope you find it inspiration to take Vampire Miyu, Evangelion, and Hitchhiker's Guide writing to new, greater heights!

- Tux
