Author has written 6 stories for Pokémon, and Ninja Turtles.

Many things have changed in the last nearly fifteen years. I am still a huge fan of TMNT. Leonardo is still my favorite and I completely adore him, but I've also lately come to once again adore who is my favorite Pokémon, Mewtwo.

I did cross over for a time from the plains of Kanto of Pokemon to New York City's intricate sewers of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. TMNT and I go a long way back and several years ago when the 2k3 4Kids series was just starting out, I was pulled back into the obsession I knew as a kid in the 90's. Leonardo has been my favorite for 28 years strong and still going. Donatello and Raphael are close seconds.

However, Mewtwo is now once again in my life. While I love many things about Leonardo, it's Mewtwo who has always more closely resonated with me. I just regret losing those fifteen years of that love and passion I had for him. I will never let anyone steal my joy away from me again.

Update 1/8/05: To my Pokemon readers, Kikara has been resurrected, but not by my hands. I have "signed" the story rights over to my good friend and author ZeoViolet. I approached her with the idea and not only did she happily accept, she had eagerly wanted to continue it for herself for quite some time. Granted, as she does have her own plot and storyline for it, it will take a more different course of direction than its initiative. However, that doesn't mean it'll be no less wonderful than if had come from my own hands. She's a great writer and she will give Kikara the chance it never had. You can find the story here: http://www.fanfiction.net/s/2176282/1/

Update 6/9/19: Many of you probably don't remember me or have moved on from the fandom. In light of seeing Detective Pikachu, I was struck with the inspiration to recreate my story Between Two Worlds. It is titled Two Hearts, One Destiny-A Retelling of Between Two Worlds. I am writing this story for my pleasure, but you're welcome to come along for the ride. I have wanted to do this for a long time, but I had a mental block that prevented me. Please feel free to enjoy and don't hesitate to comment. I ignore trolling and flaming, so you're basically just wasting your time.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or just want to drop a message, you can e-mail me at the address above or send a PM.

Hope to hear from you.
