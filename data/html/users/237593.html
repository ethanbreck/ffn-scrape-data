Hi everyone, my name is Katie. I am female and I am 20. That is all you really need to know anyway...here is the important information

The Harry Potter Pairings I write and Usually read:

Lily/James Duh!

Sirius/OC

Remus/OC

Peter/OC

Harry/Luna! those two are made for each other! And the main reason why I put them together is because I still can't stand the idea of Harry going out with his best friends sister, it is just weird. I mean if I went out with one of my brothers friends...yuck...weird!

Ron/Hermione Of course!

My Stories:

The Chances We Take: This fan fiction is pretty much my baby, since I spend the most time with it. My pride and joy Lily and James story, and although Lily and James seem to always have a happy ending, nothing is perfect. UPDATE I have recently started updating this story again, I have abandoned fan fiction for too long and now I am back. I hope to have the chapters up every 2 to 3 weeks, but at least one up every month. Don't flame for that, I'm in college and have tons of work. But anyway, the writing in the beginning is a little crappy, but it gets better, so if you don't like the style in the beginning, keep reading, I promise it gets better. I"VE STARTED WRITING IT AGAIN, HURRAY FOR ME AND READINGS!!

Sirius the Secret Keeper: Although I am not happy with the way this story is written, it is a good idea but the writing sucks. I would fix it up and everything, but I want to finish my L/J story so I can get on with the writing of my own book. FINISHED

A Worthy Mistake: I would have to say this is my best written story/one-shot. It has great detail and I am personally very happy with it. Although it is under Harry/Luna, it really only shows Harry being nice to Luna. FINSIHED

Defying Fate: The Sirius Black Story: I really wish I could go back in time and write a full fan fiction just on Sirius' childhood. But since I really don't have time, I am stuck doing only a one-shot (though I did add some Sirius childhood stuff in my L/J fic). It is my idea of how James and Sirius became friends and how Sirius finally got the strength to not become the Slytherin he was supposed to be. FINSIHED

After I finish The Chances We Take I probably will not write any more fan fiction. I need to give my full attention to my own writings (that I hope to get published). But I will say that writing here has taught me so much and has helped improve my writing greatly. I have my reviewers to that for that.

Oh and I have acutally started writing again, since I have finally found the time. The first few chapters I start to write again will probably not be as good as I hope, but they will get back into the normal swing of things probably very soon.

Thank you,

Katie
