Hey, y'all. My name is Finn Mac Cool. Been a long, long time since I last visited the ol' FF.net. Not sure how long I'll still be hanging around here. Lately I've had a hard time getting myself to write. I'll have plenty of ideas, but the actual process of sitting down and writing just seems too much like effort. I'm going to try to fix that, though my goals are a bit more oriented towards producing original stories that I might be able to get published. Still, if I come up with a good fanfic idea, I'm not going to let it just fade away. I tend to write in a wide variety of genres and, when I do fanfiction, a wide variety of shows/books/movies/whatever, so, if you don't like one of my fics, that doesn't mean you won't find another that you do like.
