I'm an avid Digimon fan and love all the seasons of Digimon my favorite is Tamers because both Terriermon and Lopmon are in it and they're two of my favorite Digimon. I also really love Gaomon he's such a cute little doggy even if he's two legged and a boxer. I'm also an avid Anime fan and have seen a bunch of different animes by favorite at the moment is Bleach Tsubasa Chronicle and Naruto. I have a huge DVD collection of Anime and a huge Manga collection. I enjoy going to Borders and could live there if I wanted. During a good part of the year I'm a Preschool Teacher teaching the twos so I won't be able to update as much soon because I'll be going back to work soon and all my time will be spent doing Lesson Plans and what not. I might write a story for Naruto though it might not be good. Believe it or not I get most of my ideas from dreams I have weird as it may be but true nontheless. That's how I got the idea for the Digimon Story I'm working on now from dreams and playing Digimon Dawn and Dusk. Lunamon and Coronamon are awesome from that game and Digimon Championship is where Dracomon comes from. Don't have that game but will get it when it comes out.

Aka: Winry Rockbell

Techniques:Sharingan, Mange Sharingan, Divine Illumination Jutsu, Illusionary Underworld Moon, Fire Element; Grand Fireball, Fire Element; Phoenix Fire, Fire Element; Dragon Fire Technique, Double Headed Wolf, Double Wolf Fang, Piercing Fang, Beast Human Clone, Earth Element; Moving Land River, Chidori, Earth Element: Tracking Fang Skill, Water Element: Water Dragon Blast, Summoning Technique, Shadow Clones, Release, Wind Blade

Pets: Riku, Sunee, plus Ninken

Special Notes: Jounin

Family: Marisa (Mother Deceased), Hatori (Father Deceased), Kankuro (Boyfriend/Protector), Ieyesei (Son)

Short Bio: Talia is a ninja of Konhagakure but is currently settled in Sunagakure because of some unpleasent things. She is only 18 years old but she has already had a child her baby Ieyesei. No she did not wish to have a child so young but while away on a mission she was captured and brutually attacked. Tenten and Neji were the first to find her and brought her back to the village where she was sent to the hospital. Once the Hokage found out who jumped her she sent a message to Gaara and asked if she could be sent there to have and raise her child. Gaara agreed and so she was sent to Suna as protection against the child's father should he come back seeking his child once in Suna she visits with Gaara and meets the person that will protect her. She and Kankuro don't really see eye to eye at first the the more the two spend together the closer they become. She gives birth to a baby boy she names Ieyesei and together Kankuro and her begin to take care of their new charge.
