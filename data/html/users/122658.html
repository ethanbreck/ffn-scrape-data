Hello everyone. It's... Me...

I mean, I guess it is. Thing is, I've changed a bit over the years. And while I still do fanfiction, I've sort of grown a little red-faced about the things I did before. But, I won't just trash them. I'll leave them up, while I show off how I've grown as an artist.

I work in different fandoms, but I'm still the same old yuri fan. I even have a few Japanese issues of Yuri Hime to prove it. Err... Anyone out there know how to read this? Because I have no clue.
