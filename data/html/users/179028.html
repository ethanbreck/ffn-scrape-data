Author has written 137 stories for Pokémon, Harry Potter, Pirates of the Caribbean, Gravitation, Ai no Kusabi, Digimon, and Fullmetal Alchemist.

Fandom old. I jump from one fandom to another, one obsession to another, as if they were on fire. Currently writing for Fullmetal Alchemist, Digimon and Harry Potter (in that order.) Miraculous Ladybug is another fandom I'm absolutely into, and have considered writing for, but that's yet to happen. Keep an eye for that, if you're interested.

Fullmetal Alchemist is my great new love. My favorite character is absolutely all of them. Mama loves all her children, but I must say that Royai (and all of Team Mustang) have been waiting for me all this time. I am currently very active in the Tumblr fandom as mylieutenant, where you can read some work I haven't posted here yet.

Harry Potter was my main fandom for most of my life (yes, I mean it quite literally.) I'm a could-be-canonhead, except for the fact that I will pretend that The Cursed Child is a badfic until the day I die. In my Potter work, you'll find a lot of pairs ranging from canon to crack. Anthony Goldstein and Regulus Black are my boys. Regarding my Anthony-verse, recent events have led me to discontinue it. By "recent events" I mean that JKR/WB found the one obscure character I latched on to, and set to destroy one of the most crucial headcanons I have about him (ie. the Goldsteins being muggle) but it was worth it, because we got two badass Goldstein ladies and this more than makes up for it. Anthony-verse might get a rework to reflect these changes, but in the meantime, what I've posted will have to do.

As for Digimon, my oldest fandom, I'm an Adventureverse and Tamers canonhead with a particular love for the Digimon girls, both endgame Adventure ships, 02, the 02 gang, and the Inoue siblings. I may choose to ignore some events portrayed in Digimon Adventure tri. due to their decision of honoring fanon and fanservice more than proper characterization, coherence, and established canon facts. I work with Adventure and 02 characterizations, and may disregard much of the changes tri. chose to imply they went through, so consider my writing TWT (tri? what tri?) unless stated otherwise. I will say, however, that not all that's tri. is bad, and if you enjoyed Digimon as a child and want to feed your nostalgia, this is a somewhat decent movie series to follow. Just don't expect too much. Look at me - I expected nothing, and managed to be disappointed. I may be found on Tumblr as takerusfedora, but this blog is gathering cobwebs due to my feelings for tri.

Solía escribir un montón de fanfics en español, como está evidenciado. Nada que valga mucho la pena, la verdad (fuera de Fotos en la Pared o los trabajos traducidos, mas porque tuvo cierto impacto y menos porque este super orgullosa... que lo estoy, pero mas por el logro que por la trama.) Si escribo mas para el mundo anglo, culpen a las chicas del HPFC por hacer de mi estadía en el foro algo tan especial, y por seguir siendo fabulosas tras todo este tiempo.

Mi principal fandom es Fullmetal Alchemist, lo fue Harry Potter, y a ratos vuelvo a mi primer fandom - Digimon (culpen a tri.) Lo demás es una ventana hacia un pasado más inocente, pero espero que igualmente encuentren que es trabajo de cierta calidad. Espero. O no. Igual ahí están.
