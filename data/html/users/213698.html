eep. i'm not really much of a writer, but i'm trying! I'm a huge fan of Gundam Wing, so most of my stories (if not all ^_^;;) will be on that...Anywho..i would really really really love if you would review! It would mean so much to me!! Because then i could know whether or not to give up my futile attempts at authorism! (is that even a word?) Believe me, i'm not saying that i want nothing but glowing praise of my stories. i'm incredibly open to criticism, and even some flames if they're well deserved. So don't think that just because you didn't like my stories, you shouldn't say anything. But...yeah. REVIEW!! ^_^

Recently, it has come to my attention that one or two of my stories *coughRaindropscoughVioletWindows* are really actually pretty terrible in the amount of OOC Heero-ness. Granted, these are fanfics, but that doesn't mean that i should turn Hee-chan into a fluffy little angst muffin who will spout poetry or cry at the drop of a hat. The damage is already done for Raindrops, but i'm not sure what to do about Violet Windows. I'm either going to 1)revamp the first two chapters and try and make it workable, 2)leave the first two chapters alone, chalk them up to bad writing, and try to carry on making Hee-chan more bearable, or 3)abandon the fic. Please, let me know which of these you would prefer, either in a little review, or you can email me, or just let me decide for myself, but if you do let me decide, it's more likely that i'll go with either #1 or #3. But yeah. that's my little bit of realization. For all of you out there who thought that Raindrops was pure crap b/c of the OOCness, i am really truely sorry for that. Thanks for not torching me with flames like i deserved!*
