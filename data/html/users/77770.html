Hi there!

Welcome to my page...my favorite things to write are angst/comfort, drama, and friendship fics--often all at once. Recent things I like to write about include Death Note, Phoenix Wright, and Fruits Basket. I have dozens of others that I am fond of, though--you never know what I'll post. =) I'm extremely open-minded, so when I do write about romance, don't be surprised if the pairings are unconventional. It may be shounen-ai, shoujo-ai, straight--pairings are on a by-the-idea basis, so what works for one story might not for another. Whatever I write, though, I always try to make it believable and to give it a point.

Anyway, hope you have a nice stay, and have fun browsing my fics. One note--please don't give constructive critique on things that have been here for years and years--I leave them up because people seem to enjoy them still, even with all of the faults that I can now see in them. Thanks!

~Threshie
