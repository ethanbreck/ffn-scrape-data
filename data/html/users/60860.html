A little bit about yours truly:

I was lured into the world of Harry Potter and the tender age of ten, now six years later I still dwell within its depths. I particularly fancy the ancient house of Slytherin with all its shady individuals and the cunning abilities that they encompass. I am infatuated with no other than the smarmy boy we all love to hate: Draco Malfoy (the most enticing and sexiest character in fiction ever made). My music tastes include, soft rock and ethereal (singing in the shower too!). I love the theatre, drama (my whole life is one big soap opera), vampires (just think Lestat…drool) and reading any fantasy novel that I can get my grubby little hands on.

2 Hermione/Severus

FAVE Books: The Harry Potter Series (J.K.R), His Dark Materials Trilogy (Philip Pullman), The Sevenwaters Trilogy (Julliet Marillier), The Moon in the Water (Pamela Belle).

FAVE Movies: Harry Potter, the Lord of the Rings, When the Bough Breaks, Cruel Intentions, Peter Pan, Almost Famous, The Mummy, Interview with a Vampire, Queen of the Damned, IT, The Crow and Lethal Weapon (gotta love that movie).
