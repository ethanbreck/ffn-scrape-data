Hello!

So my pen is Little Mystery, but feel free to call me Chello (little less of a mouthful). I'm completely re-hauling my take on stories, profile, everything. Though I'm keeping my old stories on my account. As embarrassing as old stories can be, I find it both nostalgic and adorable how five years can change absolutely everything.

My old favorites are staying too because I don't have the heart to get rid of them. Feel free to judge everything based on this year going forward, however.

Anyway!

Going to try my hand at a couple ideas I have floating around. Some of my favorite authors plan out their stories meticulously, and I love it. But let's face it. I wing it with a faint overall plot-line fleshed out with chapters with what feels right.

Anyway! Hope you guys enjoy.

I'm also more then open to ideas and thoughts from others. :)
