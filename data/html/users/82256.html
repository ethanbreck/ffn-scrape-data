Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

Hello! I'm posting some fics that I've done over the past few years, hoping to get some feedback, and more ideas!

I always appreciate reviews and knowing what is wrong. But just saying eww isn't going to help you or stop me, unless you complain. Just as much, I love knowing what it is about a story you like, since I don't always like the story myself.

I'm currently focussed on Harry Potter pairings, however I read HP, Buffy, Charmed, True Blood, Stargate SG-1, LoTR and Star Trek fanfics, and crossovers within those fandoms.

I usually write Hermione-centric fics, though I have been asked to write other female-centric fics, and I am happy to try out a new character once in a while.

I pair Hermione with anyone once.

Please note that my stories, unless stated otherwise, will be M rated, for sex, language, and use of violence.
