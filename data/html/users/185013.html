Hey, I'm MaraJade, you can call me Mara. I'm a 23 year old corrections officer in Chattanooga, Tennessee, in the US. I'm a big fan of Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Stephanie Plum, and Firefly/Serenity. I've been unable to get on the internet for almost two years now, so I'm very excited to have a new-to-me computer and cable internet. I will be pulling my stuff down to re-edit eventually, but my first priority is to catch up on all the great fics I've missed.
