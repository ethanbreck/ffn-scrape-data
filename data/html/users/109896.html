31, recreation therapist, sci-fi and anime lover. Still on that quest for world domination.

You'll find quite a few abandoned fics here - I apologize for that. I have had this account since 2001 - most of them have been abandoned due to lack of time, lack of ideas, abandonment of the show/fandom, etc. I've been considering cleaning out some of the old stuff. There's some pretty poor writing from when I first got my sea legs under me (so to speak). I used to write a lot of song fic (some which have the most horrendous formatting, oh my word.)

I got my start as teen watching CSI and rooting for Catherine/Grissom. It was a truly amazing fandom experience and I don't think I have found that sort of bond in any other group since. However, as I aged, my interested shifted more into Sci-fi and I fell away from the writing scene and more into video.

After a long hiatus I am back and posting new fiction on a semi-regular basis. I mostly write one shots when I get the time/inspiration. Often the titles or inspiration still come from a piece of music, or quotes, which I will credit in notes. I also love drabbles (ficlets in the 100 word realm) which you will see a lot of in my repertoire.

I am hoping to branch into some new fandoms soon. Currently Patterson from Blindspot has most of my attention. I'm also dabbling a bit in Madam Secretary. I would love to write more for Joan Campbell from Covert Affairs but the fandom is pretty quiet.
