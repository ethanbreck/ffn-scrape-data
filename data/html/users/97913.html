Author has written 2 stories for Digimon, and Gravitation.

Past pen names include "El Hazardress", "Care Bear", and "El Hazardress the Care Bear".

Age: Older than I was when I first joined ff.net.

Once upon a time my most desired anime was uncut/original Japanese language/subtitled versions of certain anime shows that were heavily edited when they were brought to American children television. Thanks to Netflix, I have been able to start watching one of those anime.

Updated 03/02/2014: Going back and reading the stuff I wrote 12 years ago makes want to laugh and cry at the same time. Those, and facepalm until infinity. That's why most of my oh-so-many (aka 8) stories have been deleted. There is a 99.9% chance that I won't be adding anything new to this account (that .1% is if I ever have the desire to write crappy anime fiction). But I'm leaving it here (for now) as a reminder of how for I've come, both as a writer and a reader.
