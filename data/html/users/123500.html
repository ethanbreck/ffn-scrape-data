Hi!!! I'm a semi-experienced writer with tons of ideas. I write mainly DragonballZ fan fictions, with the occasional crossover. You'll find that most of my fics have to do with original characters. I like child and teenage Gohan, as well as Bulma, Future (Mirai) Trunks, Piccolo, Pan, and Bra in DragonballZ/GT. My least favorite DragonballZ character of all time is probably Yajirobe. You know, the fat guy who lives with Korin and has a sword? I have two fics, A Super Saiyan Story and Life and Death. I switch off between the two. Currently, I am working on neither. To anyone who reads my stories, please review. I need reviews. It makes me feel good to know that people like my stories enough to actually review. So, please review. I NEED IT! Enjoy my stories!

P.S. If you want to contact me, please do so by MSN instant messanger. My e-mail adress for that account is hypersaiyan@comcast.net. Thank you.
