Welcome. I suppose I should say welcome. My name, as far as you're gonna know, is Olhado and I'm a snot. Now that that's out of the way . . .

My main genre is X-Men Evolution, my main character is Forge, and la-ti-da, here's the fic information. Most of it is somewhere between humor and angst. If you're a romance shipper, I probably am not going to make you happy. Not that I would anyway, but.

Backdraft: Kurt-centric AU hack-fic (partly created from old material, in other words). Those who are already terribly offended by how Kurt looks in this, please take a careful look at the title and at chapter five again. Story will not get going until chapter six. Patience.

Falls the Shadow, Falls the Flame: The big crazy Forge epic of doom. Like, what actually happened in the Middleverse. That's really long. You know, like, over a hundred pages. That kind of long. Most people took kind of a sidelong glance at it and ran like bunnies. I don't blame them.

That aside, this is easily my best story on here. So there you are.

Friendly Warning: I've decided to discard the epilogues in my head, but you'll notice they haven't been deleted. I'm just saying that although this section of the story is finished, the final ending you get at the, well, end, is not the real ending. The story continues in So Happy Together and will be finished in the projected Feelin' Groovy.

Horde: Hack story -- i.e. the first two chapters are cannibalized from an earlier, non fanfiction work of mine. It's an Alternate Universe, Scott-centric, rather dark and demented, and I'm on the verge of rating it higher, for general violence and disturbed-ness. That, and it's over thirty pages RIGHT NOW and I'm not even realllly started, so not for the light reader, no.

Friendly Warning: Probably not going to be continued. I lost all patience with it in the re-reads.

This is a Deep and Significant Title: Snarky OC hack fic. As far as I'm concerned, the reasons to read it are chapter three, five, and six. Could take or leave the rest. Might re-write it when I have the energy, but, for now, it's as done as it's going to get.

A word on the ending: This is the spiritual prequel to FSFF, as in, Rafael is a study for Forge, but is otherwise unrelated.

The Confessions of a Kitty: On indefinite hiatus and I might not continue it at this point. I'm not sure it's necessary. Kitty POV, just for the heck of it, post DoR, without actually caring about proper continuity. Humor, mild angst.

The Walk on the Wild Side Spoof: Convinced just about everyone I was terribly bitter about this episode or something. I'll finish it one day, really!
