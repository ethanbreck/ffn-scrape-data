Author has written 24 stories for Harry Potter.

It all began in 2002, when I stumbled across my first ever Harry Potter fanfiction... then, in 2003/04 I believed to have outgrown it and moved on...

Now, several years later - at 30-something - I really should know better... but I figured; who wants to grow up, if there's so much to see (and read!)?

After more than five years of absence I found my way back to HP-fanfiction, discovering that I still rather enjoy sailing the SS/HG-ship. It would be tough on "us", I thought, with HBP and DH making it rather awkward to find a way... back. So I'm even more astonished (and rather happy) by all those gems I have found so far worth reading over and over again. Not all can be found here, sadly, as fanfiction-net does not allow adult stories nowadays anymore. But with the help of some old-timers on LiveJournal, I manged to track them down on WIKTT, Ashwinder etc.

Also, I've taken part in the 2009 Sorting Cat's 5th Drabblethon over on LiveJournal (organised by McTabby), and I enjoyed it so much I might even start writing my own stuff again. If in German or in English I don't know, but most likely it will be in German: even though the English audience is a lot bigger (albeit more critical), writing in German is just so much easier for me.

30+ und kein bisschen weiser...

Nach einigen Jahren habe ich meinen Weg zurück zu Fanfiction gefunden, und nach wie vor liegt mir SS/HG am Herzen. Obwohl JKR es "uns" mit dem 6. und 7. Buch nicht einfach gemacht hat, so gibt es dennoch wirklich gute und aktuelle SS/HG-Fanfictions zu lesen. Vielleicht werde auch ich erneut in naher oder ferner Zukunft die Schreibfeder schwingen, die Ideen sind immer noch da... und auch mein unfertiges Werk "der Klang der Stille" buhlt um Aufmerksamkeit, auch wenn die Geschichte den neusten Gegebenheiten (sprich der letzten zwei Bände) angepasst werden muss.

Es juckt mich in den Fingern...

~ Faris-Eirin ~
