Author has written 29 stories for Naruto, Black Cat, Final Fantasy XII, Firefly, Get Backers, Prince of Tennis, Ouran High School Host Club, Stargate: SG-1, Final Fantasy VII, Merlin, and Supernatural.

Name: Previously Wingless Night, Kyuubi-kun, and Kyuubi-chan. (There were a few before that but I don't even remember them.)

Likes: Mainly I like to hang out with friends, walk through graveyards, read and still have some quiet time to write or to just be by myself.

Dislikes: I don't really have that many dislikes, or none that I can think of. I'm the type of idiot that likes to live life and if I don't like something - I forget about it. No use dwelling on things I don't like, after all.

What I Write: Most of my older stories (and probably a few more to come) are Angst, H/C and Drama. If I can, I try to write a happy ending, although it may not come off that way. Recently, I have been very interested in writing Character Studies as well. I may or may not one day do a complete re-hauling of my older stories - because even I can admit that they suck. Really suck. Eh. Whatever.

A Little Bit More About Me: I've lived all my life for the written word: as a reader, an editor and more happily, a writer. That's what I want to end up as. I use FFN as my testing grounds - with my style of writing and see how people respond to it. The things I write here can be outlets, ideas, strange happenings or just some experimental work. While most of my time is devoted to work and all the fun times that are to be had there, I spend what little time I have to myself reading, watching movies or anime or cartoons, and occasionally, if the moon is in its proper position and my blood boils in just the right way and my brain finally decides to click the "On" switch, I write. And I don't stop writing until I collapse, gasping for air with nothing but letters and punctuation bleeding from my ears.

Announcements

6 March 2013 - So I published a new story today. It's for the Merlin category. If anyone's a fan, please check it out. I've been working on it on and off for nearly two years now and I'm curious to see what you all think about it.

24 February 2013 - Hi everyone (if anyone still even comes to my profile aside from me.) I just wanted to pop in and say that I am working on some new things, but I'm also working on a lot of original fiction and sadly, that takes precedent, especially since I'm trying to shape most of it up for publication. Sadly, publication for a short story writer is a pain in the butt. Seriously. I have been working on some of my fanfiction, as well as writing new ones. I feel insanely bad about leaving stories incomplete because ideas died or I moved on. For those looking for an update on my Naruto fanfiction, I apologise - but you have a long wait ahead. I have not been interested in Naruto in years. Currently, I've made myself promise to finish fanfiction before its even posted (even if it is a chaptered fic) if only because I do not want any more incomplete stories clogging up my profile and written list. But hey, good part is, I'm still alive! And I still write! Around work anyway. There's a lot of work. So hopefully you will see some things from me soon, and when they arrive, I hope you enjoy them.

Thank you.
