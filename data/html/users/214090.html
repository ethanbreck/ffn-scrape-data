Hi, I'm not telling you who I am and what I do, I can tell you I'm from the uk and I am deaf. This does affect my writing skills in some way but this shouldn't really interfer with your reading enjoyment at all apart from the odd spelling mistake or grammer error.

I decided to let it be known that this year (2008), I aim to update and finish a few of my more popular outstanding stories and these are...

Dont' You Fall - Lord of the Rings story following Legolas

To Fight by His Sword - Mulan story

Accusation - Mulan story

Bet that'll please a few people so bare with me while I do so throughout this year. I havent really got much of an excuse apart from changes in life and the fact that I lost inspiration and I didn't want to write crap chapters for the hell of it. I'd rather leave a piece of work in it's prime to come back and update it as I aim to.Thanks for the reviews and encouragement so far.

I hate going through Fanfic and start reading a really good story and finding it's unfinished. Hence why I deleted alot of my favourite authors and stories, I'd ratehr respect the ones who make an effort and has a great sense of writing. so if I do leave a review/make you my fav author/story - know it's because I hold you to a certain level of respect as a fellow author!

i currently love reading fics based on Full Metal Alchemist, Inuyasha, Rounin Kenshin, Final Fantasy 7 and by the time you finish reading this, i would love something else as well!
