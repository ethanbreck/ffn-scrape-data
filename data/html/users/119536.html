Update July 26, 2006

After much debate, I have decided to revamp my Harry Potter AUseries, formerly titled Family Trials. Now it is called Sins of the Fathers. I will begin posting once I have enough finished to justify it which should not be more than a few weeks in the future. I hope you all will enjoy the changes being made to this project and I hope you all stick around until it has reached completion.
