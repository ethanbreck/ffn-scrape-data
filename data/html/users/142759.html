Hey, I'm Branw3n and I'm a 15 year-old aspiring writer, like everyone else in FF.Net. Not much to tell about me, though.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION:

Lily, the Vampire Slayer

I am declaring this fic dead. *I* don't have the stomach to even read this, much less write it. I was twelve when I started and I might write something along the same vein and the title might be the same but the idea will be completely different. Lily comes off as some dumb slutty Mary-Sue-ish character in this one. I hate it, and I am going to stop writing this.

It originated from a deal I made with a friend and was a prequel to some Mary-Sue/OC story we were going to make, back when we had absolutely no taste.

I am not going to delete this but I will be ignoring it in hopes of getting over the humiliation of writing something as apalling as it. Hopefully, I will be writing another story about Lily being a Slayer but it will be completely different. Feedback would be appreciated concerning this epiphany and if one wants it, I will gladly type out a summary for you at your request.

Deadly Pursuits: Potter and Evans

Going way too slow for anyone's liking, everyone but me. Trying to make heads and tails of technical jargon and watching too much espionage movies that don't make sense and ALIAS eps to help me. Anyone know anything about electronic bombs, please e-mail me. I'd be very grateful.
