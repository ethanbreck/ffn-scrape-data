Author has written 6 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, and Mass Effect.

I'm a farmer, who has written since I could write a coherent sentence. Some of the stuff I write is about the choices that people make, and the long-acting ramifications of those choices. I started writing fanfiction in 2001, (my main fandom for most of those years was Final Fantasy VIII), and have since stopped churning out fanfiction so quickly. Mostly because I have grandiose plans for my fanfics, but the plans are for fifty chapters ahead of where I am with the actual work that I'm working on.

My main ships are Shenko (Mass Effect) Coustair (Dragon Age Origins) F!Hawke/Anders (Dragon Age II), Lady Trevallyn/Cullen. (Dragon Age: Inquisition). I will, however, read anything and everything if it's well-written, or very compelling.
