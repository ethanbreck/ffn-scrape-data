Author has written 21 stories for Sailor Moon, Harry Potter, Stargate: SG-1, Dungeons and Dragons, Misc. Books, Baldur's Gate, Mass Effect, Megami Tensei, Game X-overs, and Neverwinter Nights.

I'm the creator of the Crystal Kingdom Archives series of Sailor Moon fanfiction, as well as stories featuring Aribeth and Imoen (both separately and together), and random bits from a number of other worlds. Who knows? Maybe something will grab you.

Fair warning: Most of what I've written in chapters intended to follow what I've posted here has been lost in a terminal HD crash, and I've been working, off and on, on rewriting those newer chapters from scratch. Hopefully I'll have some of them in shape for posting sometime this millennium.

If you want to keep up with what I'm doing, I've finally gotten around to creating a forum here, where I'll do my best to keep you updated on what I'm doing and what's coming up next.
