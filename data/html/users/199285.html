I write in whims. I'm bad at losing inspiration and leaving large gaps in my story updates (years at a time). I'm aware of this; and I apologize.

I am always encouraged by reviews and comments. I thrive on them, and am grateful for any amount of time you may spend leaving me one. Do not be afraid to provide me constructive criticism. I am always interested in improving upon my writing, and will not be upset if you have something you would like to suggest or point out. I will do my best to reply to you, but know that I will often not have time to do so. I apologize in advance!

I also do my best to respond to PMs in a timely manner. If you have a question, feel free to drop me a line. (There was an issue with my receiving emails during this last year or so when I received PMs. I missed a lot of messages. I hope this does not happen again.)

And again, thank you to everyone who reads, reviews, follows or favorites my stories or myself. I greatly appreciate all the support you have given me over the years and am truly flattered.

And thank you to Fen, my wonderful Husband who willingly beta's my works despite not liking Fan Fiction. You rock!
