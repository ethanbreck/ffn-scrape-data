Fanfiction about games?! YES!

I'm a busy body of sorts and sometimes I'll write furiously for a few days...then leave it alone for a few months. Sometimes I write myself into a corner, and it takes some time to work out the kinks. Bear with me on those cases! Writing and music are the two things I could live my life for, if only they paid me well enough to do it. So for now, this will have to suffice.

I love BioWare. I'm a total Mass Effect and Dragon Age nerd. Oh, and I love me some Riddick too, as you'll see in my fics.

I just came back after a long hiatus. Lots of things have changed. I'm older, fatter, wiser, and adultier. It's both awesome and terrible at the same time.

If you'd like to know more or have suggestions/criticisms about my work, please feel free to send me a message.
