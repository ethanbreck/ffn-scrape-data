Author has written 19 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Angel.

Still love and thoroughly enjoy writing. Just not having the time to do much of it at the moment now that I'm working AND a mother to a teenager to boot. I know I have a couple stories hanging here and I promise I will finish them up as soon as I can. I am and will always be a spuffyholic. Spike seems to be my fave character to write about still though I might venture out with some others one of these days. Most of my stories are mushy, fluffy, spuffy stuff, so if you don't like that, sorry. I believe that's what I'm best at and why change a good thing. And possibly on down the road I will delve into something other. Now that I like The Dresden Files and the main character Harry, there might be some fanfic bubbling up from the deep recesses of my imagination very soon. Possibly! I've also decided to try my hand at writing fiction and posting at fictionpress.com under same pen name. If you want, check it out. At this time I have only two poems there but plan on writing a short mystery fic in the future. I would love to hear your comments, good or bad. All of you who read and/or leave me wonderful reviews make me feel like I'm doing something right and I thank you all from the bottom of my heart. The biggest thankyou goes to my best friend of 30+ years, scruffy66. Without her, I wouldn't be writing anything for you all to read. She is my cheering squad and helps me achieve and surpass my self-confidence level. I hope you enjoy what you read. Remember it's all for entertainment purposes and to make you feel all warm and gooey inside and, I hope, to at least make you feel happy. Just let me know what you really think about my stuff, but please play nice. My inner being bruises easily.8) ~Did you bruise the boy?~ Spike, Harsh Light of Day
