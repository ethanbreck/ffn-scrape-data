About Me
Writing is relaxing for me and I enjoy fanfic more than regular fiction. I write a lot of (non-slash) bromance; I don't know why I love it so much. I'm always looking to improve, so I love hearing any and all feedback. I'm trying to read more of everything, and more widely in general.
