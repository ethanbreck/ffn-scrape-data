Section 4: About my body of work here

I mainly write romance novels. I love the idea of developing relationships between characters, and tragic love is my favorite kind of romance. Every romance I've taken seriously on here has been very well accepted. Heaven: The Eye and the Flower, which I started writing years ago and recently tried to update, saw a fair ammount of popularity, but I dragged on too long in finishing it and lost the fanbase.

My UlquiHime stories... Wow! I had no idea when I started just how big they would get. "That Perches in the Soul" Has been finished for years now, and I still get reviews for it regularly. Reception has been overwhelmingly positive, which makes me happy. I just hope the community has as much fun reading it as I had writing it.

"For every dream shattered", while probably just piggybacking the first story in the series, is doing much better for where it's at in its progression. I broke the thousand mark quickly, and already have twenty reviews! Anyways, enough with my roundabout bragging, here are the stats on my stories:

That perches in the soul: Bleach. UlquiHime. complete at 8 chapters

For Every Dream Shattered: Bleach. Sequel to the above. Will run for 25 chapters. Though when I will pull it off hiatus is anyone's guess.

Heaven: The Eye and the Flower: Wolf's Rain. DarciaxHarmona. abandoned at seven chapters due to the loss of its fanbase.

Silver Devil: Tekken. Lee Chaolan. Abandoned due to lack of free time when I was trying to write it and minimal reception

Sanity is worth its weight in Yaoi: Yu Yu Hakusho. Comedy. I wouldn't mind finishing it, but I think it's been on hiatus too long.

The rift between Passion and Wrath: Inu Yasha. Only my friends ever actually saw it. it's a pity, because it's one of the few pieces I've ever written that I had minimal complaints about.

To lack feeling: Wolf's Rain. Darcia/angst. Complete one-shot.

Seventeen hearts beat in unison: Code Geass. poetic prose. will be compsed of seventeen sections. Abandoned for lack of time

Another Soul Summoned: A mixture of my two favorite series (Silent Hill and Bleach) but more of a concept than a finished idea, so I was never entirely sure of how I wanted to develop it.

Gary Stu's Sex Adventures in Seireitei: My parody piece, I may develop it, since it writes easier than drama. Time will tell. Time has told... I just never finish anything. XD

I got to write a Cezary piece with Nadine/Cezary highlights... made me happy! it was a one shot, so I couldn't help but finish!

Five parings I can't stand: (these are in no particular order. These are also just my opinions. if you like any of the pairings listed below I respect your right to feel that way.)

5.) Ichigo x Orihime - Romance only interests me when there's a challenge or an initial difficulty in the two characters associating with one another. One of the most annoying things I find in anime (especially Shonen titles) are female characters whose sould reason for being and whose only contribution to the plot is to serve as a romantic counterpart to the protagonist. I find this to be hideously boring.

4.) Hinata x Naruto- Pretty much for the same reason as above. Hinata's cute, don't get me wrong, but aside from her completely unfounded love of Naruto she doesn't serve any other purpose.

3.) Sakura x Sasuke- Why do girls fall for the guys who treat 'em like crap?

2.) there is no number two, really. merry christmas.

1.) Yusuke x Keiko from Yu Yu Hakusho- I disapprove of "Main Character x Childhood friend"

Final Section: Critical updates and current predicted chapter addition schedule

haha... I remember being young and naive enough to believe I could actually schedule my updates... sadly, they come whenever I have time, and only when there seems to be enough readership to justify them.

1-28-18 UPDATE: So, I'm sure you're familiar with the pain of rediscovering something from your teenage years, and through that rediscovery coming to realize that you were insufferably embarassing... such that you cringe just to look at yourself?

That's... an apt description of how it felt to re-read this profile. I'm happy I was passionate at one point in my life, though. I think I'll leave the profile intact as it is for now, so people can see me as I was. I've been using this profile since 2004... I was 16 years old. Half my life I've used this place, and I feel like the mix of different voices and levels of maturity I gave this profile as I updated it over the years deserves to remain as it was.
