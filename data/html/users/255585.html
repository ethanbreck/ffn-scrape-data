Hello. I'm lady thetis. I was writing DBZ fiction for a while, but I kind of stopped... I really like all the plot ideas and I still think about them sometimes, but I'm really lazy and I haven't done anything with them.

So I'm content to read Ginny/Draco fictions. Seriously... take a look at my favorite stories. They're mostly G/D stories. And they're all really good so if you like that ship, try any of those.

So I really like Harry Potter. I even got a Gryffidor shirt (at a garage sale, but hey... I take it where I can get it). I love fanfictions (they're way too addicting...) but I think that I read too man HP ones. I bet when the time comes for me to read Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, I'll be all confused and keep thinking, "Harry Potter is stupid! He's stealing all of Draco's glory!" and other stuff like that...

If you want a good blog site, go to www.mugglenet.com. Its excellent.

I have a question though... who is Blaise Zabini? I don't remember her being in the books, yet every freaking fanfiction has her in it! Her gender is even open to interpertation! Is there some unspoken rule that shes in all fics that need a Slytherin character?

Okay, but other than Harry Potter, I like some other authors and fantasy series:

- Juliet E. McKenna (The Thief's Gamble, etc)

- Starship Troopers

- Diana Wynne Jones (Christomanchi)
- L.E. Modesitt JR

And I love anime with a passion:

The Cowboy Bebop movie comes out soon... I can't wait for that. Or the third HP movie. or fifth HP book. Or the twenty books from the many different series that I am reading.

so. Thats me...
