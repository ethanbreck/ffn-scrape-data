Greetings.

Welcome to my biography. I'm a 21 year old college student.

My hobbies include: Chess, Jujitsu, and Reading.

I'm a speed reader, reaching speeds of up to 3000 words per minute.

My favorite anime is Ranma 1/2, and that's what my stories are based on. I've read EVERY single Ranma story on Fanfiction.net, Tass-Fanfic Archives, and the Penultimate Ranma Fanfic Index (scary, huh). Combined they take up 180 megabytes zipped. Here are those links.

www.fanfiction.net
fanfic.tass-anime.com
www.rakhal.com/ranmalst.html

Currently I am working on two stories. One is called Ranma Layer 1/2, which is a crossover between Ranma 1/2 and Angelic Layer. The other story is called Author's Resolution, in which Ranma meets the Author.

I'm not much of a writer, but after reading so many stories, I felt I should give it a try.

Also, my stories might possibly be in rough draft format, so watch out.

I chose 15 really good favorite stories on the fanfiction.net archives, so please check them out. The favorite authors are all good writers, and I recommend all their works.

Take it easy.
