LieutenantX stood on infinite blackness.
"Hello! Is anyone there?"
He wondered what had brought him here.
"Hello."
He looked. Another figure had emerged.
"Who are you?"
"Why, I'm you."
A pause.
"Face."
The Last Face bowed.
"What is it?"
"I need to ask you a favor."
"I won't help you degenerate the 'net. Your being exiled was one of the best things to happen to FF.net."
"Heh. I've whined about the ban enough to make even you refer to it as exile. No, I need you to do something I can't."
"What?"
"Write."
"You can write. You can write in a way I never could. That no one ever could."
"No, I need help. I need to write of love."
