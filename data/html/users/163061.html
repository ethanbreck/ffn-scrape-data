Favourite pairing:

The runner-ups:
NakagoxTamahome - FY (come on, you guys, you just gotta love 'em... ^_~)
Izumi&Koji - Zetsuai/Bronze (my all-time favvie manga ever...I'm kinda obsessed with it...)
Akira&Hikaru - Hikaru no Go (my latest obsession! ^_^)

Satoshi+Daisuke - DNAngel (WAI!!! ^_^)

In case someone hasn't figured it out already, I prefer shounen ai...If I'd have to pick anime het couple I actually like, I'd either go for Kagome and Inuyasha or Zechs and Noin...Yes, I actually like the 6x9 pairing, bacause I think they have a beautiful, mature relationship. :dodges all the GW-smutfans throwing empty milk cartons at her:

All in all, I like pairings that have actual basis in the series, not just two cute characters thrown together. Theoretically I'm a yaoi fan, but considering the usual quality of lemons I often find myself actually enjoying character pieces more...-_-;; Now here's the question: why do people who usually write otherwise decent fics suddenly decide to ditch all characterization once they get to a bed scene?

Maybe it's better I shut up before I start a rant on that one...
