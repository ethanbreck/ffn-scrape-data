Hello, you're at my profile. Not much to look at really.

If you're interested I have a Fictionpress account under the same username, a Mibba account under the name "Bunny-Mommy" and a deviantart account under the name bunnymommy16.

I like fanfiction that's grown up, original and interesting. I write the crazy idea's that come into my head and post the ones I finish.

On a side note, I have some incredibly old stories on here. Keep in mind I was born in 1988. If the story is exceedingly old it will not be re-written, corrected or otherwise screwed with. Acting like an entitled twat over this fact will just get you laughed at.

I write about whatever strikes me, this does not always translate to things that are rainbows and sunshines as character development does not often occur in the sun but in the deep, dark recesses of the mind and through events that turn ones world on it's side. If this is too much for you to handle, you are not obligated to read. I do try to post warnings at the start of stories if these things are present. Getting all offended and butthurt just makes me laugh.

You do not have permission to post my stories on any other sites. I have listed all the places my stories would likely pop up, all of which are my own accounts. If you find one of my stories on another site, please report it. Taking credit for my work, regardless of it's age or any other factors is a grave insult. Write your own ideas and stop stealing from other people.

I'm a nice person, I don't bite unless requested or pissed off, feel free to message me if you want regarding any of my stories.

I have been known to take on requests though they usually aren't very long unless my muse gets carried away with herself.
