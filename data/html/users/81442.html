Ok, I'm back. After a serious case of writers block I finally have something going. I think. Anyways hmmm something about my self. *Thinks* Ok, I am 17 years old, now finally have blue hair, well sort of it's blackish, bluish and brownish but it looks cool. I live somewhere up in Washington where I still attend High school. Ughh one more year, just one more year and I'll be out of that evil place. Oh Yeah I am a girl, most deffinently. I don't think a guy would write some of the things I am... never mind on that one. I'm into Anime (but of course), and I don't just mean the stuff on TV. No no, a lot of it is just crap. I mean the americanized version. The Japanese stuff is the best. My favorite animes must be Gundam Wing, Gundam X (The charecers in this one look like crosses between some of the boys of GW hehe), Inu-Yasha, X or X/1999 which ever you want to call it, Angel sanctuary and oh yeah Yami no Matsuei, a really nice anime to get into. I can go on forever about which anime's I like but a lot of them tie for the first 3 places. Well I guess this is it about me not much to say. Oh yeah there is one more thing! I'm like Duo Maxwell in girl from. I'm not kidding all I need is a pair of violet eyes and a 3 foot long braid which is in the process of growing. But my personality is the same as his, kinda scarry to see an anime guy act like you. And my boyfriend used to act like Heero Yuy before I got to him. even more freaker. But you know what they say, there are 2 people out there in the world who are just like you. So go find them. ^_~!
