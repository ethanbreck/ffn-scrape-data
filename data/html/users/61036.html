Author has written 31 stories for Buffy X-overs, Labyrinth, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, X-overs, Alice in Wonderland, 2010, and Harry Potter.

New profile. Yup. First off, I promise (Though it may be a while in the doing) that I have every intention of finishing all of my fics. Though alot of these stories are stand alones, so they actually are finished, unless I have a brilliant idea. Several are sort of only half way done though, and they will be finished! That said, fic I am currently jonesing for, Boromir (That is NOT slash, I'm sorry but there is noooooothing in Tolkein to indicate that with anyone), Spike and Buffy, of course, Labyrinth, as always. I am a Lindsey fan (Angel) Was Lindsey/Cordelia fan until she went and became a saint. Now a Dawn/Conner fan. Would love to see her on the show. You know, I really did mean that promise, and somehow they will get finished...really, they keep pecking away at my mind asking when they are going to be done. I've just been out of the fic loop, trying to deal with RL, grr.

So... RL still getting in the way. I still have fanfic loving binges. I started watching Stargate just so I could read the crossover fiction between it and Buffy. .

Wow, its been forever. I need to get back into writing, I write so many boring work emails on a daily basis...but I'd like to tap into some more colorful words and phrases again.
