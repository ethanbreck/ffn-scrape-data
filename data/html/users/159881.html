Author has written 8 stories for Lord of the Rings, Book X-overs, Edward Scissorhands, Harry Potter, and Doctor Who.

Hi, I'm a University grad with either too much, or too little time on my hands. Haven't decided yet. As this isn't my CV, I'll try to be truthful - I'm from London, I like kittens, Alam Moore, Pizza, going out and shouting enthusiastically at people, watching terrible movies in the hope to find one which beats "Plan Nine from Outer Space" for sheer horribleness ("Teenage Zombies" was close, a 50's film made in someone's Kitchen), hanging around and eating other people's food, being vaguely left wing and trying to read some Feminist theory, but never getting around to buying the books.

I tend to change a lot, so those probably won't stay static. I have a bad habit of "Method" writing meaning that I usually morph a little into my most interesting character to write. As my current project is "Apparate", I'm swinging between being suspicious and untrusting (Snape), and staying up all night working on things because I can't sleep and running into work with a manic grin on my face and jumping up and down getting excited(Moody).

I would also like to mention that whilst I may "method-write" this does not always mean what I write is good. It's a posher way of saying that I think too hard and like releases occasionally.

I am a massive fan of Phoenix Wright, Harry Potter, Most Alan Moore projects, Greek tragedy, the Aristotelian ideal, Hume's ideas on ethics and metaphysics (He wasn't fully right, but at least he was interesting and lost some base assumptions made at the time), dancing to loud J-Rock, dancing to loud indie Rock, dancing to Loud goth-pop, dancing to Electro disco, dancing to Loud glam rock and dressing up silly, whether it be running around a forrest hitting people with sticks, running around London in 5 inch heels raising the standard of dress or dressing up as characters from various cool things and showing off my 1337 sewing skills. I don't self identify to any particular "tribe", so I'm not going to say I'm any kind of person, but the closest you could get is probably indie-retro-rave-glitter-goth with strong helpings of weeboo and general geek. I guess an easier way of saying is that I like what I like, though it does tend to appear in collum "other."

I hope you enjoy what I've written - it's the accumulation of, I think, about six years writing, on and off. I think I've got better. I'm trying hard to be a better writer, so please give me your opinions. Even if you don't like it, as long as you can pinpoint why and you're not being unnecessarily mean (i.e random personal insults or just saying I suck and should die, or my interpretation is simply WRONG without backing this up), I really appreciate it.

Just a note

1/ I can’t spell, I'm Dyslexic. I've got a bit of paper to prove it!

2/ I do try my best with spelling and grammar, but my version of it is completely different to everyone else’s. I try to check my work, and spell check it twice, but lots of things slip through the net because I sometimes honestly can’t see what wrong in the same way just about everyone else does, until I read it when it’s up on the site. I guess everyone will tell me this if they bother to read this bio.

Thanks for reading, if you did, please R and R my work.
