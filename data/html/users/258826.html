Greetings fellow authors. I've decided that it was high time to redo this lovely biography of mine.

My 'name' is solarphoenix (notice sarcastic quote marks?), although in the darker world of fanfiction I go by a slightly more sinister persona, lunarphoenix.

What is there to know about me? Well, there really is no reason to trouble yourself with the finer details of my interesting life, is there?

I am a proud Canadian, if that interests you. Though my family seems to stretch to Ireland, France, and the United States of America, though to be frank, I haven't a clue what their names are as I have yet to meet them, and at this rate never will.

I have dabbled in the writing of Harry Potter fanfiction, though I must admit that all of that I post is not that good. My favorite piece that I have posted would most definately be 'Pride', with 'The Speech' following a close second.

I much prefer reading Harry Potter fanfiction than to writing it, but I never turn down a good plot bunny. I suggest you read some of my favorite stories or look up my favorite authors, brilliant reads, the lot of them.

I am a great fan of Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Angel, both created by the genius Joss Whedon (whom suggested that since Buffy is no longer on the air to write and read fanfiction).

My favorite fandom though, of course is Harry Potter, but Buffy and Angel are right up there as well.

Likes in the world of fiction:
-The entire Harry Potter series, my favorites being Order of the Phoenix, Prisoner of Azkaban and the last hundred pages of Goblet of Fire.
-Buffy the Vampire Slayer, my favorite season being the seventh.
-Angel, my favorite season. . .season 2 I think
- The Chronicles of Narnia, brilliant read. Favorite is definately 'The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe' though I rather enjoyed, 'The Silver Chair.'
- Star Trek, Voyager being my favorite though.
- The Matrix, brilliantly astounding
- Lord of the Rings, I have begun to dabble

Dislikes in the world of fiction:
- Nothing really, except for those impossible love triangle type television shows such as Dawson's Creek.

Likes in the world of fanfiction:
- Reviews!
- Plot
- Any ship as long as it's tastefully written and not out of character.

Dislikes in the world of fanfiction:
- Plotless stories
- Pointless angst
- Out of character
- Spelling and grammar mistakes
