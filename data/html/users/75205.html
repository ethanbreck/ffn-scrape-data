01-18-06: All right. I haven't updated in years. I am both wracked with guilt and strangely not by that. Life got in the way. I grew and changed--I lost interest. I'm sorry to say it, as these stories were, once, very dear to me. They still are, but not in the same way. Before I came to change and update my profile, I checked some of the authors who were also writing when I was, all of whom I had admired. It does not seem that any are writing anymore, something that both saddens me and gives me a sense of relief (because, well, then I know it's not just me). As of now, it would seem that all of my stories are discontinued. I apologize to those who still read them. There's a new set of writers out there--sadly, they are not the ones who inspired me and I do not recognize most of them--perhaps, now, none. I haven't ventured to these areas in quite a while. But if ever I should happen to fall back into the game, then we will see...

If that does not happen, then I suppose this is goodbye. I'll check back periodically because, well, in a way, these are still my babies, neglected though they may be.

08-26-05: Alright, I know I haven't updated in..well...probably about a year or so. I don't see many updates (if any) coming anytime soon, regretably. Between physical therapy, school, extracurricular, and all the joys (and I do use that term loosely) that comes with being a senior scurrying about with the impending weight of those college deadlines just looming ahead laughing at her..well, I don't think I'll be updating soon. However, there is always that chance that a fantastic muse will hit me...

It should be noted that all my stories are Sailor Moon, revolving around Usagi.
