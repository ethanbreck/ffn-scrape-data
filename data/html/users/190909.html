HEY EVERYONE!

I'm Dark, or Darkdragon depending on who you ask. I'm a 21 year old science fiction fanatic from Anchorage Alaska, known for my slapstick humor and my unusually annoying ability to remember the oddest and most awesome movie lines from movies I saw when Christ was a Child.

My favorites genres include Final Fantasy, Halo, Star Wars, Star Trek, and Ace Combat. I'm an avid writer in Star Wars based role playing games (online), which is where I got my interest in writing.

Most of my work carries a wide range of themes, from the aforementioned Slapstick comedy to horror, angst, drama and action...or a combination of those things. I have lost most of my work due to several computer failures, and unlike my early work (Captain Janeway Takes a Joint, Repression and Endgame parodies), my newer scifi and fiction is of a more serious nature and carries experience from real life situations. (And afew alcohol-induced conversations on Xbox Live.)

My current focus is on three different works: Captain Janeway Takes a Joint: Part Deuce (Star Trek), Slaving For the Soul (Halo), and Massacre (Original Horror/Supernatural).

I hope you enjoy the new work...Hopefully more should be up soon!!
