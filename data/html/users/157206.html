WARNING!

I will warn all of you ahead of time right now: I WRITE GIBBERISH. But if you're willing to tolerate this, then go ahead and read some of the stuff off of my laptop that I deem okay-ish. Oh, and also I rarely if ever finish my stories. Sorry. But when you jump from geekdom to geekdom as quickly as I do, it's hard to stay focused on one thing for very long. There you go. That's my disclaimer. Have a nice day.

A friend once told me, "You are what you aspire to be, and nothing more." Well, I aspire to be a great writer, and perhaps someday I will be. But for now, I am the humble servant of the general public. You read my work, you review it, and I will go to bed at night with the satisfaction of knowing that I am that much closer or even that much farther from my goal. As long as I know where I'm at. :) Thanks for reading.
