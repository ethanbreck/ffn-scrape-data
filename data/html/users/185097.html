Okay...I've said it in the poll I ran for 'Fade Away: That's Life.' : I AM depressed.

I'm a freaking lousy author who's got end year exams on timetable just two weeks from now... Need piles of good luck. Anyway, I had to update this bio page. The uh piles of name and favourite animations in this bio column kind of irks me. Don't know why. But hey, see the change when you get sarcastic and depressed.

Okay, enough of my wonderful blabbering and I'll start talking about my life.

Firstly: Real name, age, sex and birthday is NOT going to be disclosed. No point. Sometimes its better to remain anonymous.

However, that doesn't stop me from penning down my likes and dislikes:

Likes: Writing (currently: my genre has switched to angst and sarcastic humour, care to make a guess?, and recently, I've gotten crazy over RPGs, so if anyone's got an interesting one, please tell me. I've joined one where everyone got to be an assassin...quite cool.
I've also got a new obsession: GO. I love that game. And I really hate it when people get it all confused with Reversi or Othello for that matter. I'm going to make it clear: Go's no where like Reversi or Othello. I suppose it's the shit about the white and black stones. Ugh.

Dislikes: Too much smut in a fanfiction... I think it's just wasting space, but mush is fine. I also happen to dislike really rude people, especially the type you find in the yahoo games go room. I also equally dislike those that get points from cheating (in the yahoo game go room). Don't know what I'm talking about, that's fine. No one really knows what GO is nowadays save for the Japanese. Oh well.

Okay, I've disclosed as much as I can about my wonderful life, which is practically nothing, now that I scroll back and read it. Well, if anything's extra then this is: I'm a freaking school student. Not down to colledge yet and I rather not know what colledge life is like.

Okay, This is an apology: I will not be able to write the first chapter of 'Fade Away: That's Life' until my end years are over. For that matter, I won't be writing anything until then...so please bear with me and wait a little longer.

To anyone who actually bothers to look at this: Thank you.
