Author has written 4 stories for Degrassi, Harry Potter, and Twilight.

Hello. I am just another HP fanfiction writer. I have a story for DeGrassi that I wrote ages ago... and I'm not sure I will finish it. I can't remember where I was going with that one, and I feel guilty for not having completed it. If there is anyone who wants to take on that story, then by all means do so. Just e-mail me first. :)

I don't write consistently. My muse comes and goes, and I'm more likely to read and review stories than actually write them, anyway. I have several ideas for novel length stories that I will probably never end up writing. Short little one-shots is my thing. If you're interested in writing a novel length fanfiction and have no qualms with slash, then e-mail/message me and I'll give you the details of the ones I'll never do. ;P Otherwise, I hope you all enjoy my fanfiction. My grammar's not so hot (looking for a beta), but I put a lot of myself into these stories. I'm compassionate about everything I do (discounting 24 Hour Party which was written purely as a joke), and I hope that shows through in my writing.

Thanks for stopping by!

P.S. Please do check out my livejournal (homepage). It has an almost complete list of the fanfiction I have written (Harry Potter and Twilight), some of which you will need to friend me to see. Comments on here and on lj are always lovely... ;P
