A little about me:

I'm a mom who loves to write. I found my love for writing a little over a decade ago, and I've been trying to improve my skills since.

My current obsession is the smurfs. My fic Smurfs Forever is being written and has priority over anything else I may post here aside from my other obsession: Pokegirls.

I love to explore world building and concepts in my work, making some of my writing stuffed with information tidbits that can be missed. I may casually drop some racial truth or physical property of the world while a character is doing the dishes or taking a walk. It's just my style.

I write mature fiction mostly. As in there may be dirty jokes, sexual situations, actual sex, violence, gore, competent villains, real consequences, and people making mistakes. Consider this your warning.

Occasionally, I fall back on the old bad habit of abusing commas. I'm forever trying to fight this need to pause, and then start again, and then pause with no real reason.

I have a warped sense of humor. Feel free to ignore my horrible puns should I respond or post anything in reviews or author's notes. I also abuse smilies when talking to folks in chats or through email and such. :3

I'm Zikore over on Deviant Art too. I have crappy drawings and sprites. XD
