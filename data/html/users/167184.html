March 21st, 2003.
Wow. I have been so... NOT here. I'm sorry. I don't really think anyone cares, but still...maybe i'll try to write more.

-- Saturday, December 7, 2002 7:14 PM EST--
i just watched: the scary movies inside my eyelids.
i'm thinking about: holy CRAP it's been months since i've written a story...
randomness: i am going to die at the hands of the lighting crewhead. directly, or indirectly, it doesn't matter. he will kill me. i know he wants to. twitch
i'm having lighting nightmares. how sad is that? and no one outside of my hell hole of a high school knows what i'm talking about! ahh!
i need to write. it's gnawing at me, eating me up... i have nothing left except that. i need to write. and i will.
soon.
thank you guys for the extra reviews for "gold" and "black tears." i'm considering moving out of the rocky horror world for a while.. my fishnets are tearing. labyrinth, perhaps? moulin rogue? i've only been considering those forever... you know what's even better? casablanca. that movie rocks my socks.

-- Sunday, August 25, 2002 10:52 AM EST --
i just watched: his girl friday. mannequin. citizen kane. casablanca. breakfast at tiffany's. i went to the shore and we got TCM so i filled my eyes with old movies. i'm thinking about: school. eight days left. and the fact that i'm going to see RENT! finally! september 6!
randomness: it's been a hell of a long time since i updated. the last one was in april. i've been in and out of summer school (no, i'm not stupid, i was getting advanced credit) and to the shore and back and my boyfriend's been gone for a total of 6 weeks and now he's back and i'm not even sure that i wanna see him and i'm supposed to go to the franklin institute today but zac and caitlin haven't called me yet... yeah. i am confuzzled.
i doubt anyone's still reading my widdle updates. i'll have another story soon, like within two weeks, perhaps? i've been trying out a moulin rouge one, nini-based. maybe after i see rent i can write about angel and collins. columbia's tapdancing around my head again. i wonder if i should give her another spotlight.. which brings me to a giant thank you for everyone who reviewed "gold." i ditched two riff and magenta storylines because they sucked.
any suggestions for a story? i'd really appreciate them and i'd probably even use some. feed me. madteapartyy@yahoo.com.
