"Writing can be very much like painting a picture with words." I heard this statement once and it's never left my thoughts. I've never been much of a painter, but I work with what I got. And what I have are my thoughts and desires, some pictures and the odd memory or two all jumbled up into a muddled work of art that yearns to please and lives to entertain. With a stroke of the pen, I just might be able to give birth to an idea and all it needs is to be read.
