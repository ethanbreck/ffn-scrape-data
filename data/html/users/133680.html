To Those Who Get Flamed

First and foremost. Everyone, be proud of yourselves and your accomplishments, and don't be afraid to sing your own praises...but not until your head is swollen so big you could be used for a hot air advertisement. Be open to constructive criticism. Don't be so sensitive. Yeah, I was extremely sensitive at your ages too when I first started writing. What I wrote was my "baby," so to speak, and anyone who crossed a red line through my sentences was like drawing a dagger through my "baby's" flesh and drawing blood. Tears, tears, sobs, and more tears later...I realized somewhere along the road my teachers and editors were right and got the hell over myself.
The best writers in the world are the ones who actually listen to feedback. Not only listen, but adjust their writing style and learn from their mistakes. When someone tells you something is wrong with your story, they are courteously (a term I use loosely with this website) giving you their time, and offering golden advice. It might not seem so now, but as you grow older, hopefully your fanfictions will assist you as your talent develops with your journalism, novels, poems, what not and what have you.
The best way to get over sensitivity about your work is to develop a sense of humor. The best kind of humor is dark. :) That's just my preference. So when you get flamed, laugh about it.
Anything else that you want to know about good writing, refer to Born4Glory's website. Even though he's a tender college freshmen, and through all the sarcasm, his choices for writing guidelines are true.
My favorite fanfictions are anything that are controversial. My more favorite authors are:

Misty the...something. (no, I shall not call her Misty. A woman who knows her sensuality is a powerful thing indeed and one of the most beautiful things in the world (besides Vegeta...dabs delicately at the corner of her mouth with tissue). I will not refer to her as Misty. She is not pure. She is not meant to be. She is a fellow sister of the Dark Side, such as me. Bwah ha ha HA HA HA!!! *Thunder strikes* A-hem. 'Scuse me. I also think her fics are some of the funniest here.

Flirtatious Flamingo. This girl, at the tender age of 13 or 14, is going to have a good career in writing. Read her things. Enough said. But, GODDAMN IT CHILD, where the HELL did you GO???? You've disappeared!

Born4Glory. As much as you all might hate/love him, his commentary adds a spicy zest to this website. I know cross-overs of DBZ and SM are perhaps somewhere beneath you H3LL, but I want to see some more writing on your part, even if it is bashing. Why not use a hockey team as your new setting? Lord knows you love hockey, am I right? Of course I am. :)

Firefly: And There Was Light...A hell of a story. This is a good example of how to not write strictly romance. This is also a good example of back peddling of not knowing which direction the story is going to go, but doing a hell of a job with it. I know you said it's Vegeta/Usagi, but part of me still wants Rei, hon.

Larania: "A Special Training." That was a good one. And its complete too! If you want to talk about unusual pairings, read this one. And the explanations she gives for the loopholes are always neatly stitched up (You'll laugh your ass off at the explanation on Piccolo developing a penis...it's GOOD.)

Sporanox: The kid is 14. And he sounds like he's 30. If you're flamed by him, consider yourself LUCKY. Then at least you know someone care's about your pathetic little lives.

If I ever offend anyone, good. It's because I want to make sure you get my point. However, if I've unintentionally offended you (this, too, I do quite a bit) then write me and let me know, and you will get an explanation. Also, I always sign my reviews. Esp. my...um...more blunt ones. I don't hide my identity. If I can give it, I can take it. You should all be able to do that too. It'll build your callous to being critiqued in the real world. Trust me. Tenured professors in college can be real asses. Get used to it now.

Yeah, this is an angry post. I'm tired of idiots though.
