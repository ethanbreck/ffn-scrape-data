If you're reading this because you're bored... that's pathetic. Go read a book.
But hey, if you actually do want to know about me, read on...

I pretty much love to write, been doing it ever since I was itsy-bitsy-little. I don't really like writing fanfictions so much, more original stories, you know? But anyway, writing is just what I do, and if you don't like how or what I write, it won't stop me or frighten me away or anything. But, on the other hand, I really do want to know your opinion, for the sake of learning/improving. So if you totally hate my stories and think they're unoriginal or whatever... go ahead, tell me. I'll take your comments, and I probably won't take offense either. But just don't think that I'm writing for the comments, all right? These are my stories, my work. If you don't like them, you can write your own. Not trying to be offensive or in-your-face or anything... but that's just the way it is. (shrug)
