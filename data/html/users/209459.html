As anyone can tell from the dates above or from my stories last updates, I really don't write fanfics anymore. Not that I don't enjoy doing so, but more so along the lines that I just don't have the time nor inspiration.

Much has changed in my life. I moved out of my parents, found a great job, and got engaged! Lots of pluses in my life and I'm rather happy. I keep thinking that I should use that happiness to push me into writing a story, perhaps 'inspired' by my current lifestyle...but that just doesn't seem to be happening anytime soon ;

Anywhos, I'm still amazed and wonderfully shocked to find that from time to time I'll still get a comment or a story favorited. It really makes my day!

Maybe one of these days I'll write a random story and post it...but until then, Thanks Everyone! I hope my stories continue to entertain!
