Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.
Hi! I'm a big Harry Potter fan and I can't resist my urge to post stories here... I would like to borrow some characters from J.K. Rowling's book series. I don't own any characters, but I hope that you can all enjoy the story!

Elvish name: Enelya Inglorion
Horoscope: Scorpio
Element: Water
Flower: Chrysanthemum
Birthstone: Topaz
Favorite colour: Blue
Favorite character in HP: Harry Potter, Remus Lupin

The stories I write:
Diamond of Light - beta-read by Ashriel
"Diamond of Light" is the first fanfiction I've ever written. The chapters are fairly long, but I don't know whether I could manage writing such long chapters. May be the word-count would drop from three thousand something to a thousand words... I'm still writing that, very slowly actually. As book 5 has already been published, I think I need to rewrite or make some changes to the story. I've removed "Diamond of Light" from this site. If anyone who wants to read it, you can read it on fictionalley.org. I have a writing block... So I'd rather finish other two stories first.

Memory of a Wolf - beta-read by Hettie
"Memory of a Wolf"... The idea of this story came to me during the examination period. I've started writing it as soon as I finished the examination. I like writing stories about children. May be deep inside my heart I don't want to grow up... I've been observing children's behavior since I have started to write this story. So far I only meet some spoiled children... I wonder I could describe a child's antics nicely...

Friend or Foe - beta-read by Kayleigh
I always want to put Sirius and Severus together. It would be so funny! I decide it should be a humorous story. I like writing humorous stories. You don't need to think so much when writing this type of stories, do you? The characters in "Friend or Foe" have special personalities, they would not be the same as J. K. Rowling's descriptions. I've made a few changes to the characters' parentages as well. It is set in Harry's fifth year, and it has nothing related to the Order of the Phoenix.

Flying Free - beta-read by Crab Apple Fairy
It's nice to write something about animals. My favorite animals are birds, especially those that can talk. I like birds which have fluffy feathers, too. Oh, how I love to pet them and hold the tiny things in my hands...
So, "Flying Free" contains quite a lot of bird tactics. I always think about my previous pet bird when I'm writing drafts for this story. I just hope that it is still alive.
Harry was 16 in "Flying Free". I don't plan to include anything related to the Order of the Phoenix. The Harry in the story wasn't so emotional. On the contrary, he's much more optimistic.

I give my special thanks for my beta-readers.
Ashriel: Thanks for beta-reading my story, even though I haven't updated for a long time.
Hettie: You are doing a great job! Thank you very much! I'll work harder on "Memory of a Wolf".
Crab Apple Fairy: Thanks for beta-reading my story. You're an excellent beta-reader!
