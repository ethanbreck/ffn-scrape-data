Author has written 10 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Sonic the Hedgehog, Tales of Symphonia, Chronicles of Narnia, and Supernatural.

I'm 21 years young (Holy -- when did that happen?!), a busy unemployed former student and from the UK.

Of past, you would have found me attempting to write Yaoi and meaningful messages into fiction - now i aim for only meaningful messages :)

I'm currently working on Of Father and Son, which started off as a small idea and is now huge in my head. Hold on, I'll get there eventually :)

Progress:

Of Father and Son: Chapter 17 - in progress

If you're looking for some of my work, and it's no longer here - I'm sorry but I won't be reposting it. I have greatly appreciated every person who has followed and read my work, reviewed and added me to their communities and favourites. The truth is I'm entering my twilight (no not the novel) years and some of my early work is well... bad. Actually most of it was. Oh to be young again. Now i'm juggling life.
