Author has written 15 stories for Sherlock, Frozen, Sherlock Holmes, Into the Woods, and Winnie the Pooh.

Hello everyone and welcome to my little nook here on fanfiction.net! I have been actively writing fan fiction as far back as eight years old. I think my first fan fiction stories was a toss up between the Disney movie Susie Q and The Carpenters story. I wrote for a time many LOTR stories. I didn't write for many years due to the fact none of the things I was into included fan fiction. What got me back into it was BBC Sherlock. After not writing for many years, it feels GREAT to be writing again. I am constantly coming up with new ideas. I am very thankful to those that have liked my stories. As long as people keep reading, I'll keep writing.
