Just a 19 year old, broke college student right now, and even worse cause I'm majoring on Physics, so no much money on the future either.

~So since no one is interested about the facts of my life I'm going to stick to the fandoms I like. I have one long list of series I have been fan or I'm fan of:

The X Files being my very first one hardcore once upon a time. Not anymore though, me being a noromo and all, and with the turn the series took, it left me with no choice but to explore another paths of fandom.

Dragon Ball Z for a short while, before I discovered more anime series with a little more serious tone to them. I still enjoy some good old Vegeta/Bulma lemons and have lately dicovered a taste for Goku/Bulma fics.

Rurouni Kenshin is the one that totally turned me to anime way back on 99. I love that series and the manga even more. If you can get a hold of it somewhere I highly recomend you to get it, not just because of the plot line but of the drawing quality. I'm a K&K (Kenshin and Kaoru) fan, but I like some UC (unconventional couples) too, such as Kenshin/Tomoe, Enishi/Kaoru. I'm also a Aoshi/anyone but Misao fan. And I have found out that of the anime colony RK is the one that has the best fanfictions ever. If you can find Madam Hydra's fic That Which Lingers and Tim Mandigma's Farewell to Innocence read them now!!! They're the best of the best! others fics you might wanna catch that can be found here on ffn are on the Favorite Stories section.

Shinseiki Evangelion, better known as Neon Genesis Evangelion is another. On a much darker tones than the ones before I have to say this is the best anime serie I have ever come across. I can honestly say that it changed my perspective on the world. It really f*cked me up for a while there, and believe me, I'm not easily impressed, but let me tell its an awesome piece of work. The movie is even better than the series. End of Evangelion kicks a$$!!!

Roswell, yeah, I know, shame on me. Teeny bopper series ahead, but I just coulnd't help it. It's like a car crash, you know it's wrong, but you can't look away. I liked the first and second season, but the last one is just crap. I was never a dreamgirl (Max/Liz fans) but I was mostly ok with them when they were not looking at each others with moon cow eyes, but this season just turned me completly away from the series. I'm a candygirl (Michael/Maria fans) with a touch of stargazer (Alex/Isabel fans) but no matter how much I begged for the soulmate couple to leave my screen they just wouldn't budge, so I left. I read fics from time to time (cause some of the fics are better than the original series) but that's about it. To me Roswell no more.

And last but not least my latests obsession, Cowboy Bebop. I'm currently watching it on a animation channel (Locomotion) and what I've seen so far has got me very much exited and have confirmed one of the constants of my life. Men ARE real idiots. No offences, but it's just it ALWAYS goes the same way with them, they fool around with lots of girls, but then they meet the ONE, once they fall in love, they just won't let it go (and even worse if said ONE dumped them) and they get stuck in the same spot forever, never moving on for real, and even if they find a new significant other they are always going to be looking for someone resembling their lost love. It's just pathetic. But hey, that the way of life and damn it if I don't resent it!! Too bad I'm just too straight, otherwise I would have abandoned the male court a long time ago...

Other series I like are Ranma ½, Buffy The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Akira, X Men: Evolution, Salyers, Gunsmith Cats, and the list goes on and on...
