22/2/09

Hey guys, I only just realised now yesterday was my seventh fanfiction birthday whoo! I can't quite believe I've been a member here for so many years, its really strange as it doesn't seem to have been that long. I set this thing up just purely for a laugh when I was thirteen, I didn't really know the first thing about writing and somehow I've just kept at it so hopefully now I'm not as terrible a writer as I used to be. I hope! Eep! Seven years... I'm turning 21 in a few months, how scary that I still love writing final fantasy fanfics as much as I did back when I was a child. Hmm... yeah getting all nostalgic now.

Anywho, the real reason I'm writing on here is just to say I've had seriously bad writers block the last few months. Everytime I write something I just feel like my writing has gone downhill. I don't know if that is really the case but that's how I feel. I posted a one shot the other day, only 3000 words long and it took me over a month to write because all I seem to do is open up my laptop and sit and stare at the document, maybe write a paragraph if I am lucky, but all I seem to do is read and stare at it. Eugh, I hate it!

I decided for a while at least I'm going to try my hand at writing short stories, as I love writing them. My latest story 'A Battle of Land and Sea' I feel like I have completely hit a brick wall, so I'm having a break from it for a while. Personally, I'd like to take the thing down and completely change the storyline but oh well. I might get back to it one day, no promises though. I HATE giving up on stories, but seriously, this one is not working for me!

Hopefully though I will start writing with a little less blockage soon, and I will have a collection of stories up once I get my ideas all figured out - and I have lots of them, which is good!

Thanks for the reviews and support and stuff. Hopefully I'll have another happy seven years on here. Who knows?

~Sarah

A little background information... I am a 20 year old girl from the boring land of England, who still can't let go of my favourite childhood game. I love to write and be a geek. I love Final Fantasy, Kingdom Kingdom Hearts and Katamari. Although I don't always get as much time to be a geek as much as I used to. Damn life and working to pay my bills thing gets in the way of having as much time as I used to. I still can't believe that in February I would have been a member here for seven years.. that's just scary.

In the past I used to have a bad habit of never completing a story, but I've been good the last few years and tried to finish my stories and not give up on them. It takes me time to write... okay so I may go a rather long time without writing sometimes, but I do try! And I do always fall back to writing, people just need to be patient with me.

I also have a brand new editor, who is proving very useful and I feel they deserve a special mention. So thank you Emerald-Latias

I also have a brand spanking new blog: neon-sunsets.livejournal.com

And an email if anyone wishes to contact me at all: covetous_creature@hotmail.co.uk

Oh, and on another note. My brother used to share this account with me so some of the stories on here are from him. But he hasn't written since he was like..fifteen, and he's now nearly twenty-two. So I think we can safely say that he has given up on writing. Sorry to any of his old fans.

And that is the end of my ramblings. Happy reading!
