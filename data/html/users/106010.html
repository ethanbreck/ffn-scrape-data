Author has written 6 stories for Evangelion, and Witch Hunter Robin.

Alright, well basically I wrote all this stuff when I was young and kind of stupid, and now that I've evolved into a better writer and a more... er, I guess, nuanced individual, they all seem pretty stupid to me now. I mean, some are alright, but eh, really pretty mediocre all around. I've kept them up I suppose because some people might find them amusing. I guess I should be embarassed, but again, they're relics now, and don't really correspond to my writing anymore, so I'm not. But, if I left you a review and you're thinking, "Gee! That was a nice review, I wonder if this person's written anything interesting?", don't get your hopes up. Maybe I'll delete them, and actually write fanfics again. Eh, I don't know, I've been thinking about it for a while, so who knows. I really like the dynamic between Shinji and Asuka, but never wrote any story for them, so I might do that some day. But then, it's been done to hell and back, so part of me thinks, why bother? I could put my own little twist on things, but if the twist is big enough, why not just make it a new story altogether, with new characters, a new world? It's the dilemma of the fanfic writer, perhaps.

-- Supercat,

Dec. '08
