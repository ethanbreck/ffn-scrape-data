Hello, I am Neila Nuruodo, or Jonnakyn'eila'nuruodo (obviously a Chiss from the ruling family Nuruodo-I wish). My name is Beth and I live in the USA. I'm 30 and I am obsessed with the Chiss, and especially with Thrawn. I just graduated with a BA in English; now my goal is to become an editor (and maybe writer, who knows?)

Likes: Star Wars, Mass Effect, Les Misérables, Hellsing, Fullmetal Alchemist, Chrno Crusade, Bleach, other anime, sugar and other things that make me hyper, video games, music, funny or sweet fanfics (especially when they involve my favorite characters), funny jokes, food, vampires, Neopets, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings

Dislikes: almost everything on TV, annoying people, my family (usually), bugs, politics

Stories I'm currently working on-

All You Wanted: I'm currently working on the last chapter--my muse abandoned me when I lost the page I was working on.

Growing Up: I haven't given up on this one, but I've misplaced the notebook it lives in. As soon as I find that I'll wrap it up.

A Storm on the Horizon: An extremely AU story that will feature a version of Thrawn uncorrupted by Palpatine and a Leia not always on the run. (And yes, this story is all pureblood-slytherins fault)

Captive Heart: The Noghri succeed in capturing Leia on Kashyyyk and bring her to Grand Admiral Thrawn. He thought he was perfectly sanguine with handing her over to C'baoth, but something strange is going on aboard the Chimaera and Leia may be the key to unraveling it.

My Confession: What if the clone Luke and Mara found in the Hand of Thrawn had been fully finished and conscious? Repeatedly resetting the clone's release timer caused an unanticipated error in the cloning program, resulting in the clone being trapped, fully conscious, in the Spaarti cylinder. Needless to say, this causes some complications.

(As yet)Unnamed Mass Effect/Star Wars crossover: After the destruction of the Reapers, Shepard leads an expedition to a nearby galaxy. They land in the Unknown Regions and encounter the Empire of the Hand. I really needed to write something with lots of action after "Seal My Lips," and this was it!

Covenant of My Allegiance: Sort of a counterpart to "The Wish." A human woman finds herself unexpectedly trapped in the GFFA. Unlike "The Wish," where I made an effort not to change anything, this one will go vastly AU as a result of the heroine's choices and knowledge. Is any price too high to pay in order to save the galaxy from the Yuuzhan Vong?
