Author has written 12 stories for Harry Potter, Grey's Anatomy, Sailor Moon, and Hardy Boys/Nancy Drew.

I am a twenty something woman who does this to decompress from the stress of haveing three toddlers at home I am responsible for. (yes three though only one is mine, the other two belong to my sister who is stationed over seas and couldn't take her kids with her. So I have had them just short of two years. But their Mama comes back in April and then I think I will be a little lost without them).

My favorite things are Sailor Moon, Nancy Drew and Hardy Boys, NCIS, Grey's Anatomy, CSI, Fringe and many many more. Most of my stories from this point will be Sailor Moon and Sailor Moon cross overs, and some Nancy Drew and Hardy Boy stories.

I am a traditionlist, I like Shittenou/Senshi pairing and Mamoru/Usagi, Haruka/Michiru. I am ok with starlight fics if they are well done. I like cross over fics alot too, but not betrayl fics or Usagi decided she didn't love Mamoru and now loves whomever. I personally think Ronin Warriors/Youdin Samurai Troopers is the best show to cross over with, it just makes sense to me.

As far as Nancy Drew/Hardy Boys go I am an all out Nancy/Frank Fan. I have read the books since I was eight and I started with Hardy Boys and then read Nancy Drew books and like 4 years later I heard about the SuperMystery books and was so excited. That made sense! I never really liked Ned, thought he was boring and plain. I liked Callie ok but thought her and Frank seemed more like friends than anything else.

Thats about it for me! ~~~@
