Some free-association and a hole in her head...

I don't mind what you wanna call me, feel free to call me r3, Ree or even Crack if you like.

I've always been a writer of sorts. My first fanfiction (if you so wish to call it that) was when I was little and it is something too embarassing to mention...It involved something pink with yellow spots. My mother enjoys telling people of it though. Yet coupled with that, I've always been an artist since I could hold a crayon to my bedroom wall. So these two things are my passions and I do love them so.

Sometimes, being who I am is all I have left...

I'm twenty four, and a pure bred (lawlz) EnglishIdiot. Drawing and writing may be my main passions in life, but I adore many other things; like chocolate, music and simply being myself. I'm a little bit weird, in that blonde kind of way, but I don't really care. I can appear a little too crazy and hyper at times, but then work takes it toll on me, and drains all form of life to leave me as a mass of goo. Or an emo pillow on the pathetic bed of despair. I'm an emo pillow. Yes I am.

I'll tell you stories, bruised and blue...

Kingdom Hearts is currently my main love. I enjoy many different pairings, most of which contain shounen-ai themes. Love is love, suck it up. The current top list are: AkuRoku, SaiXem, CloudxLeon(Squall) and XigLux. There often tends to feature some Soiku(Soriku, SorRik, SoIku... x3 ) but I'm not a 'ohmigawd I need to write it in a story' type of person. I still love it all the same though (:

To contact me:

You can use my email, or you can use an instant messenger. I have the three main ones; YIM, AIM and MSN. I'm always signed on MSN, even if it says I'm offline. Send me a message any time of day, but don't be eager to expect a reply. My MSN is my email - arethie@hotmail.co.uk

For AIM, ReeBeanSoup.

My YIM isn't used as often, almost never signed on in fact. If you want to take a chance - blackvelve123

Notes: Things may take time to be updated since I'm a lazy-assed sod, so, don't expect there to always be a new installment. I'll try not to leave them too long though .

...In case you guys haven't noticed, I no longer update as much as I used to and a lot of my stuff is just sitting there. Sadly, when I went through all the annoyances of dealing with crap that everything I had to throw at me, not only did I loose the saved files, but I also lost the inspiration for many of my stories. I've tried working on them, but nothing feels right when I do. I could take them down, but then that'd completely wipe them from existence.

I've not given up writing though. I'm still working on Spanners when I have the time and the mindset to do so, and now and again I'm an anonnymous author over on the SRIFEHART meme on LJ.
