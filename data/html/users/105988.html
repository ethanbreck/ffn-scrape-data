ME

What can I say? Well, I'm a 23 year old Literature graduate and now journalistwho took an avid interest in books at an early age and has loved them ever since. For as long as I can remember, I have held onto the dream of being a writer (Quickly glossing over the short-lived notions of being an astronaut, princess or Formula 1 driver! heheh XD) There's little that can beat curling up in front of the fire with a hot chocolate and losing yourself in a favourite book - shame it's so hard to find time. I long for the day I can take time out to do just that and honestly say there's nothing else I should be doing ...

As for the sort of stuff I write ... whatever pops into my head really. I also write some fanfics which I can't post here as there isn't a category for them which is partly why I haven't been around much and I've been getting into writing original stuff too. I just love writing, period.

FAVES

Just some of my favourite things - a constantly changing list. What can I say? I'm fickle ... :p

Movies: The Lord Of The Rings Trilogy, Gladiator, Armageddon, anything by Hitchcock, Sixth Sense, A Knight's Tale, Phonebooth, Pirates Of The Caribbean and probably many, many more I'm overlooking.

TV shows: Lost, Angel, Buffy The Vampire Slayer, Tru calling, Eastenders, ER, Desperate Housewives, 24.

Music: Green Day, James Blunt, 3 Doors Down, U2, Matchbox 20, Lifehouseand loads more.

Writers: William Shakespeare (that's the three years of English at uni showing, lol!), J.R.R. Tolkien, Stephen King, Maggie Fury, Joss Whedon, Tony Jordan and too many more to name.
