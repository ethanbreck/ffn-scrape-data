As of January 24, 2004, I have received my fourth and last Perfect Score from my Solo Contest. It's not like anyone will read this, but I'm very proud of meself!
By the way, I'm currently working on two fanfics (which will not be posted until I have completed it); one is an Inu/Kag one-shot and a Ranma 1/2 fic. My IY fic "The Orphan" will remain as a one shot until I figure out how to continue it.

In very ancient news...
My obsession of Dragonball Z, the show of ultimate power and cuteness, the anime of proud princes and purple-headed hotties and naive look-alikes, the greatest thing that has ever come to me since 2001, is gone. I am no longer an avid fan. My interest went down the drain, but there's still about half of a 2-ton tank left of it left. I will still watch it from time to time, remembering the naivety, the humor, the action, the Veggie-torturing... ah the Veggie-torturing... Never will I come to hate this show completely, but I will have a humble liking of it. I decided that I will open my self to new things, otaku and non-otaku alike, and will discover more and more.
I might continue my stories, but I still have that damned writer's block.

Soo... ENTER, NEW FAVORITE ANIME: INU YASHA AND RANMA 1/2!!!!!

Favorite animes

Dragonball/Z/GT- I would say that I have been obsessed with this anime ever since the summer of 2001. I've known about it for many years, but I got into it more then. It gave a good introduction with have Kenshin show that a skinny-little man is more than meets the eye. I also have a thing for the 2nd spiky-haired guy with attitude I’ve seen. Sanosuke. He to me is half the image of Vegeta; stubborn, war-scarred (if there is such a thing), and has huge amount of strength. But the other half image of him is like Goku or Gohan; kind to children, never lets friends down, and is as cute as both of them (yea, I think the big oaf and the geek are adorable. Got a problem with that?). The anime also has good couple pairings.
Bad points:
I can’t figure out what the true plot of the story is. Is it just about the Batosai (?) repenting for all he’s done?
It’s like DBZ in a way, but the constant problems for RK appear too often. To me it’s more or less like Pokémon, just that the characters in RK are SO MUCH COOLER AND CUTER than those freaky Pokémon Trainers… especially Ash and Brock… :P

NEWER OPINION: I haven’t seen RK in ages! Probably because I keep on forgetting what time it starts on CN… Despite that, I still love my San-San!!
