~About Me~
I am currently in high school. I am a Capricorn, and I think that my blood type is O. My friends with interests in astrology tell me that I act like a Capricorn often, especially where stubbornness is concerned. My favorite color is green (obviously) and my favorite animals are dogs (and wolves).

I can read very quickly but write much less so. I have a problem concentrating for long periods of time, more often out of school than in school. I also enroll in difficult courses, so my free time at home is...somewhat limited (unless I do not feel like sleeping, but I love to sleep).

Mostly, I spend free time at GAIA Online (www.gaiaonline.com/index.php?referral=186). I am Chibigreen Tejinashi there. I joined Gaia the day after it began, when it was still a small forum, but now it is one of the biggest, fastest-growing forums on the Internet (with customizable avatars and tons of items to collect/buy/find!). I tend to spend my time collecting green items (128 unique and counting), roleplaying, and updating my price guide.
