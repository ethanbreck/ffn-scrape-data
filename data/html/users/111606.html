Lost in the Digital World mainly takes place in the digital world. The story revolves around Tai- he gets attacked by a digimon and slowly finds himself changing into one. With Sora's help, he is able to face his fears and learns to control his powers as he prepares to face the darkest threat that has ever come to the digital world. Taiora is the main couple with a small side dish of Takari, Ken x Yolie, and Davis and a character of my own. I currently don't have any plans for Mimi, but feel free to let me in on any ideas!
