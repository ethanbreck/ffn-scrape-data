I'm 30 from the USA. I've a passion for just about everything...it gets crazy in this mind of mine sometimes but that's the way I like it.

My problem is that I see all of my stories in my dreams and they play out as if I were watching a movie. Getting them out of my head is the hard part as I have something called expressive dyslexia.
