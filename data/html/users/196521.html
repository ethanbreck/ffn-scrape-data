Author has written 8 stories for StarTrek: Deep Space Nine, StarTrek: Other, Babylon 5, Starblazers, TV X-overs, and Battlestar Galactica: 2003.

Jason Bond is just my pen name, and it refers to my old college radio DJ personality.

Current projects in the works:

1) Rewrite of Phoenix Rising, working in some events that took place in the first four episodes of season three

2) Rewrite of Thirteen to add some events that took place in the BSG episode "Downloaded" as well as the movie "Razor"

3) Rewrite of Crossing Lines to add more character depth as well as a better explanation of the entire story in general

4) New Chapters of Star Trek Wildstar

5) Stargate Lost Colony: Colonel Derek Graham is assigned to a new team put together by General Jack O'Neill to investigate a new stargate found near the famed lost Roanoke Colony near Manteo, NC. When the enter the chevrons that are written on a piece of parchment paper found in the underground vault where the gate is found, it takes the team to a massive Ancient-built castle on a world in the Andromeda Galaxy. There they find the first clue as to what may have happened to the lost colonists, as well as the enemies that they encountered along the way.

6) Stargate Magellenic: A Star Blazers/Stargate Atlantis crossover. After Yamato fails to win the Battle of the Rainbow Star Cluster, the Star Force is destroyed, and Earth is without hope as the Gamilons prepare to take it over. That is until Queen Starsha of Iscandar finds a stargate and learns that humans have been to other worlds before, and sends the Cosmo DNA as well as plans for the wave motion gun and wave motion engine back in time to a perplexed Atlantis crew. With such immense power in their hands, the Stargate team must guard it from not only the Wraith and Replicators, but from forces on Earth itself. Takes place sometime during the fourth season of Stargate Atlantis.

7) The Thirteenth Chevron: Battlestar Galactica/Stargate SG-1 crossover. Still working on the rough outline of the story. Will take place during the beginning of the third season of BSG, but not sure yet when I want to have the story take place in the Stargate universe.
