'Bout lil' ol' me:

General:
I am a college student living in Hawaii. I am a band geek/dork/whatever. I play the clarinet and dabble in piano. Right now, I'm in love with Disney. The current actor-flame changes monthly and I don't update that often so he won't go here. I also twirl flags as a color guard during football season. I need to go down to the college and career center and tell them I've decided to major in English.

Favorites:

Anime: Sailor Moon, Card Captor Sakura, anything by Hayao Miyazaki, and Gundam Wing.

Manga: Sailor Moon, Card Captor Sakura, Gundam Wing, D.N.Angel, Chobits, and Marmalade Boy.

Books: the Harry Potter and Lord of the Rings series along with Meg Cabot's books. Julie Kenner is awesome. For older things, I really like Ray Bradbury and works by Poe.

Movies: Musicals. Any Alan Menken-scored/Miyazaki-directed film, The Lord of the Rings, the Harry Potter films, Sky High, Newsies, RENT, Labyrinth.

Fangirly Weaknesses: Haku (Spirited Away), Trowa Barton (Gundam Wing), Draco Malfoy (Harry Potter), Warren Peace (Sky High), Martin Allende (Shark)

Other Cool Characters I Want to Huggle: Pippin Took (LOTR), the entire cast of RENT, Mush, Blink, and Race (Newsies), Fred and George Weasley (HP), Dr. Spencer Reid (Criminal Minds), Sootballs! (Spirited Away), Skaara (Stargate)

Other:

Regarding Fanfiction: I love well-written original characters. I love alternate universes. I hate Mary-Sues with a passion. I hate not adhering to Canon without legitimate reasons. I hate bad spelling and grammar. I hate internet-speak being used in fanfiction unless some sort of internet chat is going on. If it's good work, no matter what ship, genre, or rating, I'll probably love it. I like to write songs and poetry for my works, even if I don't put them in. I love making up original characters, they're such a fun challenge.

After much deliberation, I've decided to leave up all the stories even though I wanted to delete everything non-Sky High because I believe they're no longer a correct representation of my writing style. I'm leaving them up because they've all, apparently, been favorited, for which I thank everyone. I know what it's like for a story I enjoyed disappears, even if it wasn't the greatest.

I'll try to start writing again, once I find some inspiration. :)
