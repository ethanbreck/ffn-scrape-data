Author has written 5 stories for Final Fantasy VII, and Final Fantasy XI.

I'm not writing because I think I am fantastic at it. I'm not writing for you or anyone else. I'm writing what I want to read, the plots I really enjoy and my OTPs. If you like my work then I am really happy that someone else does too! But I'm writing for me and me alone, I'm not here to please anyone but me. I appreciate the positive reviews and serious tips to improve my writing, I really do. But any flaming just isn't something I'm going to tolerate. If there's something I did wrong, tell me. If there's a plot hole, tell me. If I need to work on something, tell me. But don't flame me saying I'm terrible just because you didn't like the story or I'm not perfect. I encourage other authors to put this in their profile as well. Unless this is your actual profession then don't take writing too seriously and don't tolerate people telling you that you are trash because you are not. If you can sit down and pump out something you are proud of then I salute you! But don't let anyone take that feeling away from you.
