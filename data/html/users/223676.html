I figured that my bio was in need of a change, and a major one at that. I guess I should start out with a bit of info about me in general.

Name: I'm only willing to give my penname, which is Altia
Age: 19 (So old...)
Sex: I am a girl, in case anyone thought otherwise...
Location: California
Current plans: Getting ready for college in the fall.
Likes: Anime obviously, writing fanfics, video games, manga, writing/listening to music, drawing, roller blading (even though I haven't done it in almost 3 years...), collecting anything anime that I can, and playing DDR.
Dislikes: Flames (so don't abuse me, reviewers!), school, people who think they know everything, annoying people, and shopping for anything except anime and video game stuff.

Ok, now with that out of the way, I should probably have notes here about the status of my stories.

Who was that Skateboarding Fool?: By far my most popular fic that I've had on here, I will continue to write it as long as I can. I've really grown to love this fic, even though in it's early stages I've thought about discontinuing it. I hope to tie up all loose plot ends by the end of the fic, which hopefully won't be for a while. There's not much else to say about it.

The Day His Past Became His Future: I have discontinued posting on this fic for the soul fact that my friends and I never exactly finished it. We lost interest in it around Junior year of our high school career. Not only that, I think I've lost the notebook the story was written in...(sweatdrop) If I can find it, I may continue to post chapters up until the point the story stopped. Just be aware it will never actually finished.

CardCaptors Galore!: This fic is on, as of right now, permanent stand-by. I've lost my inspiration for this story at the moment, and may finish it once I've finished WwtSF?. It's a fun read though, especially if you compare it to my more current works. It's nice to see how my style evolved, considering I started this story when I was in the 7th or 8th grade.

Now, since all that is said and done, I should maybe state ideas for other storys that are floating in my head. I do plan, sometime in the future, to write an Inuyasha fic, but as of this moment, I can give no details to plot or setting. Mostly this is because I don't even know yet... (sweatdrop)

Well, I have nothing more to say, and for those who read my profile, I thank you for taking time out of your day to learn more about me.
