There are six main figures in our unit...three whom you will probably see commenting on a Review.

Teefa: Head of operations. She's half Woren (tigerperson) and half Winged clan (wings on her back) with long brown hair and eyes. Usually kind hearted, but if you mess with her you'll pay. Her favorite weapon, a huge mallet nicknamed Mallet-sama, has been known to crush the others if she's mad enough; specifically Skysplitter. Extremely obsessed with any hot guy that comes along from any series. However, she does get physically sick at the site of blood.
Fav Pairing Ark/Ivy (CIMA: The Enemy)

Lufia: Artist of the group. Full blooded Woren (tigerperson) with long purple hair and blue eyes. Like with Teefa, she's even tempered...though you might want to refrain from flirting with her husband. Heck, only one who has to worry about her is her husband if he does anything stupid. Her hobbies include any sort of art, including culinary. She also has a strange fascination with explosions.
Fav Pairing Lina/Gourry (Slayers)

Rutee: Musician and genius of the group. Half Woren (tigerperson) and half human with short black hair and violet eyes. Extremely cheerful and willing to give insight into anything she comes across. Her forte is songfics and songparodies. Though her inventions have been known to cause trouble at times; like when her Dimensional Portal broke down, trapping Prier of La Pucelle Tactics.
Fav Pairing Virginia/Jet (Wild ARMs 3)

Phoebe: The battle expert of the group. Full blooded Woren (tigerperson) with short blond hair and blue eyes. An explosive temperamental time bomb who cusses up a storm and always resorts to fighting her way out. One of Mallet-sama's frequent victims.
Fav Pairing Bit/Leena (ZOIDS NC/0)

Samantha: The romantic of the group. She's a human with dark skin, green eyes, and black hair. Polar opposite of Phoebe, she spends most of her time restraining the language to a PG level; well, before Teefa can use Mallet-sama.
Fav Pairing Alex/Luna (Lunar: TSSS Complete)

Burianne: The humorist of the group. She's a sprite which means that she's about the height of a Dwarf with elfin ears and fairy wings. Her hair is orange and her eyes are green. Besides humor, she loves food and hentai. Forever at Teefa to write a Lemon; though Teefa wanted no part in it long before they were restricted from FF.net
Fav Pairing Claude/Rena (Star Ocean The Second Story)

Animal Partners: Everyone has one Digimon and one Pokemon; with the exception of Rutee who has one Digimon and 24 Pokemon. Most comonly apperaring are Teefa's Toramon Skysplitter (the loudmouth), Lufia's Salamon Sunder, Rutee's Renamon Atwight, and Rutee's Vaporeon Vapor.
