All About Me!

I read a lot and write a lot. Someday I hope to actually make money off it, until then I write for free, isn't that nice? My favorites are Star Wars, Dune, Star Trek, Lord of the Rings, and most things sci-fi and fantasy. But hey, life is so much more interesting that way!

And yes, I know that I am one of those rare few who is a Trekkie and a Star Wars fan, but I have survived thus far!

Fan Fiction

In case you are checking up on me for the first time, I am going to finish all of these eventually, and I'm still updating, despite the fact I get a tad bit behind sometimes.

Yeah, I think it's time for a new fic, and yes I know I have many unfinished ones and I'm sorry but I just can't help it, if you have suggestions for new fics, just email me!
