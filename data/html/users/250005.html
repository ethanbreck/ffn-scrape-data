I absolutely LOVE fantasy! Harry Potter, Tamora Pierce, Patricia C. Wreede... It goes on and on. I also like others, but never non-fiction! ;) My fav Harry Potter is, of course, HP4. My fav Tammy P. series is ... well, I like the Song of the Lioness alot, but also The Immortals. I like Protector of the Small, but not as much. The Circle books are just not as much fun, though.

My favorite movies are ... Terminator, Anastasia (animated), The Matrix, and Robin Hood: Men in Tights. Of course, those are just the top ones. Sadly, I was slightly disappointed in the HP SS movie. They left out several of my favorite parts! (Hagrid actually YELLING at the Dursleys; the potions on the way to get the Sorceror's Stone...) Don't get me wrong; I liked it alot. I skipped school to see it the first day, first showing!!! I was just a bit sad... However, the second HP movie was just ... well, it just blew me away! I loved it!!!! I could go on for hours about how much I loved it! (Well, maybe not THAT long, but you get the gist...)

I love the Tamora Pierce and Harry Potter love/hate stories (where two people are fighting, and suddenly start kissing) It's so funny! "Hate is the closest thing to love." -My mom... ahem... I especially like Hermione/Draco romances... Also, now I am into just about everything with Hermione... Hermione/Severus, Sirius, Harry, Ron, Draco, umm... I don't particularly mind slash, but it's not my favorite thing. I really like humors... They are a wonderful pick-me-up on a bad day! So if you know of any good ones, please be so kind as to tell me of them! *puppy-dog eyes; cute pout*

I write HP fanfic, and poetry originals, original short-stories, original myths, and original very long stories. They are great fun!

Also, please go read Footprints on the Moon by Chavi. It's very good! (So is her Fighting with Words: The Last Resort, and Brilliance. Both are wonderful!)

Read Awakening of a Royal and you will never go wrong, and read The Past Didn't Go Anywhere and you'll have fun for hours. Also, try Points of No Return. It's a Hermione/Ron, and very nice. They're all wonderful!

I also have this ... obsession with Alan Rickman. He was so HOT as a young man, and now he's so ... sexy. As my friend put it, "Very very very very very (and a few more of those) nice." Yes, well. Ahem. In Robin Hood: Prince of Theives, he is sooo yummy! And then as Snape, I just couldn't contain myself:D
