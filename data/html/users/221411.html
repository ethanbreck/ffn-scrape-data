5/23/05

And she kept spinning, until her head was full of notions far and wide, and her feet were left dangling off the ground...and she smiled at him, knowing that he could never see her...

Good bye. I've tried to extend this, I've made promises that I cannot keep. I will not update "love your enemy."

Good luck to my fellow readers. May you find something that you are passionate in, and someone to share this passion with. Don't lose sight of what you want. Remember that fanfiction is just one more means of expression- we make our own lives by what we do. Fanfiction and fictionpress will always be a part of me, but it's time to move on. I will be starting college in the fall, and I believe that I've found a path for myself. This is the end of one journey, but the beginning of another. May you find your own journey.

Thank you for supporting me; I feel like I know many of you.
