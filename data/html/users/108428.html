In deepest, coldest, November, a Writer has The Block.

... well, it is not so much a block, more like I've-got-a-job-and-a-family-and-a-life-and-I-commute-four-and-a-half-hours-a-day block. takes a deep breath. I know what you are thinking. Why doesn't the Writer write on the train? The thing is the Call of the Suduku is still strong and there are so many free newspapers I get 9 to complete in one day! :D

Apart from that; I do have something that I am penning, but I have read so much fanfic that I'm no longer sure which is JKR fact and what is wishful thinking. So hence I am reading the books again.

(apart from the middle of GOF, naturally - don't look so shocked, I know you do too)

At least I am not rambling on like last time. What was I thinking?!

Toodles, until the next time.

- G

Later in the day of July 2008...

I didn't know whether or not to put anything up about myself here. I mean, is anyone truly interested? Maybe all you want to know could fit on a postcard. I dunno. It just seems like people seem to like doing that now. So, I guess the first thing about me is that I like to ramble.

I also like to not do what I should do. And do what I shouldn't do ;-)

(you might think that those two sentences say the same thing, but I think differently)

I also really really like brackets. And author's notes. And cookies... especially the ones with fruits like raspberries and strawberries, and I have a real desire to marry caffeine. If I dare say my age... I'm 24. It'll be interesting to find out if anyone believes I act my age. I think I act older than my age but think younger. I appear normal and quiet to most people but to a select few I am very very scary (Believe me; I have a whip and I'm not afraid to use it)!

Now, if I haven't scared you enough, my fics are below this rambles. I haven't written much on the daring side (once I'm away from uni, I will as I won't feel guilty all the time) but I have a few ideas. I love slash and femslash, a bit too much really. Scrap that, last bit - cannot love it enough... (hehe) I won't tell people if I am male or female... maybe you have it all figured out from the name, but those that get the reference of the name will understand it was the character that I was empathising (grrr!! Empathising doesn't have a "z" in it?! For goodness sake, I'm English!!) with and I am the sort of person that has gender rather ... neutralised.

(for fing bs!! Neutralised does not have a "z" either! Is there any way to get an English version of spell check on this site?)

(I'm not that angry)

(really)

I have forgotten what I was writing about.

Anyway, I hope that I have entertained you, just ever so slightly today. May your skies be purple, the drinking water pink and your pet sausage be tattooed with the number 887.

Gilaine.
