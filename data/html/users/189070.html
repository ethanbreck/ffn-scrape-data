I draw, I read, I write, I have no life.

yeah that's me.

I am also an avid Harry Potter, LOTR, and anime fan.

But I read and write Harry Potter Fan Fiction.

Which leads me to my pet peeves I must put here:

I don't completely love fanfics where Harry is in complete control and everyone knows it. Harry was never in control of his life to begin with!

and... Albus Dumbledore never shouts in surprise. The man is too calm, I think that is how J.K. Rowling made him that way for a purpose (see story below). For instance in the fourth book when he heard Voldemort came back, HE DID NOT SHOUT IN SURPRISE!!! He may be human but for instance if Voldemort himself was in front of him, HE WOULD STILL BE CALM!!!! This veiw will only change when J.K.Rowling says so.

another, the fifth book is in the year 1995 and the sixth in 1996! not 2000,2001,2002,2003! do the math, second book. all in there. (this normally has no relevance to the plot so i let it slide)

plus..(I sound like some old-bat don't I?) Please! Please! Please! Armor up your plot before sending it into the ring of nit-picky evil squirrels like me. I could be (and am)very cruel. If your plot is along the lines of this:

"Harry! qiuck! Let's tell Dumbledore!"

"You're right Ron! We should!"

~~~~~~~~~
[later in the Headmaster's office]

"Hello Harry and Ron what can I do for you?"

"Professor Dumbledore! Ron and I were in the Forest when Voldemort appeared!"

"Gad Smokes! We better take care of this!"

[the end of this stupid thing...]

leave town... run. hide. The sadistic squirrel will SHRED you!

plus while your at it... find me and slap me across the head for even writing that.

No, I am truly a nice person. really. I mean it you crack-brained ninny!

okay so I'm a tad spastic. Ask my friends (if you know them). They are nicer than I am and are the bestest friends a girl could have.
