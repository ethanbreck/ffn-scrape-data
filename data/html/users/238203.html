Author has written 6 stories for Tenchi Muyo, and Oh My Goddess!.

A fan of Tenchi Muyo, especially the OVA series (through 4 now,) though Universe also has a place. (Shin Tokyo isn't quite up there, but it's there nonetheless. Some of the ideas too.) I also own ALL current issues of Tenchi Manga (now all ten 'New' volumes, danke..) ..out in the States. Also have seen War on Geminar, and seen Ai TM, on YouTube. I'm also a tremendous fan of the Oh/Ah My Goddess! series (preferring the manga, but own the TV series too, which I own ALL of in the States out currently.) I hope my stories are enjoyed with the intent I created them for.

I like to play with the overreaching what if of they stories 'past' what is already published, and like my two OC toons I got going, so an easy way to not read what I got, and apologies if I seem like I OC the side of the stories a bit too much.

Feel free to email me if you have requests or just ideas I can use in my writing, and yes I know my grammar needs work. Been there, done that. Just don't tear my grammar apart, or writing style. I'm getting in my forties now, and my melon is rather getting pinned down in my decided writing style. I'll never be perfect anyway, so I don't formally try beyond what I am. My warped mind, is of additional warning.

Working on my Tenchi section of fics, no plans to stop any time soon here.

My OMG section will eventually be done, when I get to it, and probably rip down the stuff I have up and fix it somewhat before said finishing.
