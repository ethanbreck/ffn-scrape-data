What I'm known for: Wisdom that should never be learned, a listening ear without judgment, and the ability to understand.

It's been a very long time since I've actively written or done much other than read stories on FF.net. It feels good to come home. I really and truly left when my sister died six years ago, but I've finally mustered up the strength to come back.

And now that I look at it, most of my writing back then was CRAP. Good experience, trial and error, getting my feet wet, but ultimately CRAP. I'll leave them up as a reminder mainly to myself of what NOT to do in the future. I've been published, for goodness sake! What was I thinking back then?
