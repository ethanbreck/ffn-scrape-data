It has been several years since first I posted creative works to FF.net. In the time that has passed I have changed drastically both in style and in interest. I had several works that were posted for the public, however, now I feel it is time to delete that portion of my life and start fresh.

Perhaps my muse will come again and I can bring myself to rewrite the awful stories that I proudly thrust forward years ago. However, me thinks not.

As a college student with an active work schedule, a social life, and a wonderful boyfriend, I hope that you do not expect a regular update time from me. Still, fans, reviews, and any sort of interest encourage all.
