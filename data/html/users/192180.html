Author has written 4 stories for My Little Pony, and RWBY.

Truth be told, I've never been very sure what to put in profile pages. I have all sorts of various interests that range from casual gaming to music, to computers (though not very much), to reading and writing. I enjoy reading quite a bit and I don't limit myself to much in that I will try to read all sorts of fiction ranging from Star Wars / Star Trek, all the way to LOTR, and of course Fan Fictions. I find that sites like Fanfiction.net allow for many of us to have an outlet for a great amount of creativity that would normally be pushed down and locked away. I've seen all sorts of written works on the site ranging from a Troll fic, to a grand story woven carefully together with character development, action and romance to create a grand epic...And through all of these stories I notice one constant that stays the same from writer to writer, a passion or drive to create. I hope to someday find myself truly published, either in paper or e-print...and I hope all of you can find yourself in that position as well.

Sorry...I tend to ramble.

As for my writings, I do have one Character that I love, and it happens to share my pen name as well...but please rest assured that any of my writing that involves that character is not an attempt at self insertion. While that is a fun thing for some writers to do, it just never sat right in my heart so I try to avoid it when possible. The reason I enjoy this character so much is because I've been trying to develop him for years. I've scrapped it and recreated it so many times that I've honestly lost count...kind of a fun little project that works in the back of my mind from time to time.

I will say this right off the bat as well. I'm a Brony...and to be honest, a fan of good animation over all ranging from CG shows like Roughnecks: Starship Troopers, to Anime such as Gundam Wing, or Akira, and even Saturday Morning type shows such as the old Batman TAS. If you don't happen to agree in my opinions, so be it. That is your choice and I'm ok with that :). I cannot help but enjoy the fantastic stories that come out of Animation, not to mention the fun characters, action sequences, and various other items that come with it. I guess I can compare them to movies. I enjoy all sorts of films and escape into them due to the various stories, action sequences and everything in between including the changes in special effects, and wonderful music scores.

So...that's a little bit about me. Nothing special, just me. Hope you have a good day and enjoy some reading.
