Fandom has been a big part of who I am since I sat in front of my telly and got obsessed with Quantum Leap. Sure, this is not how people picture someone who writes Doctor Who fanfics. But the fact of the matter is, unlike the kids growing up now, Doctor Who just simply wasn't on my telly growing up. However that isn't to say that I didn't get obsessed with time travel and Sci Fi and Horror stuff through other means. When I was growing up I always sort of watched the things that weren't really popular. I was in to 'Are You Afraid of the Dark' and 'Goosebumps' a good bit as a kid and I still love the strange and unusual as an adult.

I am a huge fan of ,Doctor Who,10th Kingdom, Sherlock, Jekyll, Teen Wolf, Being Human UK, Big Wolf on Campus ,Angel and Randell & Hopkirk deceased 2000,Stargate Sg-1,Smallville and other series as well as the X-men,the first Hulk movie and Van Helsing. Reading is kind of a passion,Lord of the Rings,Harry Potter,The immortals quartet,White Wolf,Kelley Armstrong's 'Women of the Otherworld' series, Switchers trilogy and The Loop,Of Mice and Men,1985,Dracula,Edgar Allen Poe, Sherlock Holmes,Frankenstein. John Connolly writes the best scary stories I ever read. I especially love his volume of short stories called 'Nocturnes' and 'The Book of Lost Things'.

Unfortunately, I do admit, I get sidetracked with stuff
