Hello!
I have been with Fanfiction.net for about fourteen years now (wow, that makes me feel old). I live in the Sunshine State; that's Florida. I attend Family Worship Center, which, by the way, is an awesome church, but, of course, we serve an Awesome God. I now have a MA in Professional Counseling and have no idea what to do with it. Working as a TBOS Therapist now, Yay!

I currently enjoy a few fandoms: Transformers: Prime, Knight Rider, Sonic the Hedgehog.

Note: In Due Season was on hiatus, but is being worked on again. Hold Dear the Studious and Invariant is still on hiatus. In the meantime, I have been working on a few one-shots for other fandoms, so keep an eye out.

I also want to thank everyone who has read my works and taken the time to leave a review. Whether to praise or critique, it is always welcomed.

Name: For the sake of security and confidentiality, it is Dr. Cat.

Gender: Female

Likes: Music, movies, writing stories, music, drawing and erasing, playing piano (practicing guitar), running, music and a whole bunch of other stuff that would fill up a novel (and did I mention music).

Dislikes: Long lines, rainy days that continue on for rainy weeks, the bullying of others.

Loves: Father God, Jesus Christ and the Holy Spirit, family, friends, and, though it takes a lot of patience, co-workers.

Hates: Evil

Favorite characters: Sonic the Hedgehog, Amy Rose and Shadow the Hedgehog, yay hedgehogs. Lightning McQueen (number 95), Doctor Delbert Doppler, Captain Amelia, Cat and Dog (from CatDog), Darkwing Duck (let's get dangerous), Basil of Baker Street my good man, Theodore Tobey MacCallister the third and Emperor K-U-Z-C-O Kuzco, Kuzco, Kuzco! Gunther Breech (bat bladders, Jane), Sherlock Holmes (BBC and otherwise), the Knight Industries Two Thousand (Kitt, if you prefer) and Knock Out (Because I too am an automobile enthusiast).

P.S. Has anyone ever told you that God loves you and that He has a wonderful plan for your life? I have a real quick, but important question to ask you. If you were to die this very second, do you know for sure, beyond a shadow of a doubt, that you would go to Heaven?

Have you ever heard that the Holy Bible reads, "For all have sinned and come short of the glory of God" and "for the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord." The Bible also reads, "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved". And you're a 'whosoever' right? Of course, you are.

Here's a quick prayer for you: Lord, bless each person who reads this with long and healthy lives. Jesus, do a quick work in their heart make Yourself real to them. If they have never called upon Your name, I pray they will do so right now.

If you would like to receive the gift that God has for you today, say this after me with your heart and lips out loud.

Dear Lord Jesus, come into my heart. Forgive me of my sin. Wash me and cleanse me. Set me free. Jesus, thank You that You died for me. I believe that You are risen from the dead and that You're coming back again for me. Fill me with the Holy Spirit. Give me a passion for the lost, a hunger for the things of God and a holy boldness to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ. I'm saved; I'm born again, I'm forgiven and I'm on my way to Heaven because I have Jesus in my heart.

When you asked Jesus to come into your heart and to forgive you, He heard you and did so. All of your sins are now forgiven. Always remember to run to God and NOT from God because He loves you and has a great plan for your life. That's VERY good news.

Here's a website if you'd like more information: www.revival.com and I encourage you to find a local church to go to and tell someone about your new walk with God.
