Hello. ^^ I'm Yosomi, or 'Looking Away'. I'm 17, now, female and live in Northern Europe.

See that terrible timegap between my writing.

I wrote poetry at one point, but that needs inspiration which I havent had in quite a while. The reason is simple; almost all the poems are about Heero Yuy from Gundam Wing. I really liked the character for a long time, and 2x1 literally brought stars into my eyes. But, *sigh* not anymore. I still think those two are special and meant for eachother (as much as 4x3)in fanfiction, but.. now, I have a new favourite couple. :) Sorry guys.

Can't you just see that RanxKen is written in the stars?

Aargh, I read those old poems of mine. The horror that is typos. Can I not check my spelling? Apparently not. And I'm too lazy to correct them now. Actuailly, I don't even remember how it's done, and I can't bring myself to bother finding out. :3 Nyaha.
