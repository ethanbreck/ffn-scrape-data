Halloo!

My pen name in ffnet is right there in bold letters but it is a little awkward to call me by that so you can call me Citi.

I am half Spanish and Japanese. I grew up in a gypsy-like family because of my family's work. Some would say its hard but I got to call four different countries my home! Learned a lot of different cultures land language and made a lot of friends and the Internet was not even that accessible yet! I miss you hand written letters!

My hobbies are visiting museums and temples, playing video games, painting and reading.

I stole this account from mi hermana! (that was her version) back in 2008. It was basically to learn English--and what better way to learn that to ready and write constantly in that language! Lol! Imma getting good actually...not! Still a long way to

We have no religion but it does not mean I am an atheist!

I work in an advertising firm as a graphic designer (the fancy title, is an art director)much of my stress comes from there but many of my ideas and inspiration developed there too! It is both a blessing and a curse. I can't imagine a more exciting place than the Creatives Department especially when we declaim war over the budget of a project through "Quake" with the Accounts when the big boss is not around! LOL

I like almost all types of genre's. (BL) was the curse my sister brought upon my scarred childhood but alas, as they all said; once you get hooked you can never get out. O.o-- gaydars are installed for life--NoooooOOOooo

What I do not appreciate are (EMO) type of stories. I just can't empathize with those. The woe is me, the world is cruel and everybody hates me so I'm going to wear black make up and black clothes to look cool and push everyone away more! Might as well slash my wrist too because it hurts like hell but the pain I feel inside hurts more!... I mean seriously?

I'm like damn man! You are too focus on people who don't understand you but you ain't doing it to someone who needs it the more! You are wasting one precious lifetime! You could have use your crouching GOTH hidden EMO to find something more productive-- like volunteer for be a companions for abandoned children with cancer? Better yet, Help find a cure! I have seen how some of you torture kittens! I am hunting you!

Cough, cough

I got carried away there-- damn I need to stay away from these emotional newbies at work.

The person who I idolized the most is my grandma. She is the most open minded person that I knew. She was an environmentalist who traveled all over the place to teach Zero-Waste Management. She didn't care if she needed to travel half way across the world just to talk to one person as long as that person was willing to listen. Because if that person was inspired enough, he/she will go and inspire others as well!

Much of my storie's plot are inspired by her!

Love you Grandma! You are always missed!
