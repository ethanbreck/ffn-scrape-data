Hello, I'm A.J - Actually, that’s just my pen name because Amy is my favorite name. My real name is Jennifer, which I don’t think is that bad. I am currently working on many stories. WWE, Star Wars, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Pirates of the Carribean and originals.

“Amy’s Story” [original]was something I wrote for school, because we had to do mysteries in eighth grade. (I’m a 15 year old Sophmore.) It’s mainly about a girl who’s sister disappeared years ago, and suddenly her friends start disappearing too. She has to try and figure out the clues that have been left behind in time to save them, but unfortunately is too late for one of them. In the meantime, it comes out into the opening that her family has special powers, and that her sister isn’t all that she was made out to be…Guess you’ll have to read it to find out what happens!

“Crying” [original]Is just something I came up with in my head, when I was really upset one day. It’s supposed to be something to get people to open their eyes, and turned out to be a lot deeper than I had planned.

“Not Me” [fan] was only supposed to be a one poster, but it got such good reviews that I am continuing it. I just got a co-author, Monica_Skywalker, whom I adore with all my heart.

"To Love the Unlovable" [fan] was just a little piece that I wrote when I was bored, really not good at all...there will NOT be more.

"No One Should Be Alone At Christmas" [fan] this came about after reading a bunch of HG/SS stories, that I thought were really cute, but I wanted to keep this one on a more moral structure, so the relationship doesn't mature past Fave Teacher/ Fave Student.

"This Isn't How It's Supposed To Be." [fan] This was supposed to have a lot more. It's not going to. It's an incomplete one poster. THERE WILL NOT BE MORE.

"Completely." [fan] Just rantings. You might find it interesting. THERE WILL NOT BE MORE.

So, a little more about me…I draw a lot, most of the time Japanese anime to go with my stories, but I’ve been experimenting with other things as well. I also love volleyball, acting, music, computers, writing (duh!) soda, Midnight Milky Ways, and midnight itself. I’m definitely a night time person. I live with my dad, who is re-married, and my little sister lives with my mum, who is also re-married. My favorite place in the world is New York, and I’m going to move there after I graduate from Dartmouth (where I want to go to college) and become a Phsycologist and writer. I love responses, but really dislike flames because they aren’t very constructive. If you MUST flame me, I would appreciate it if you would not sign it, so I can remove it. Flames are very discouraging, and I tend to get down on myself very easily.
