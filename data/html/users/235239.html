Exceedingly eccentric, decidedly depressed (war and famine and WOE, oh my!), cautiously carefree, broadly bemused and alternately alliterate; Kayleigh is all of these things and more.

She is an English Language student at university, which proves she's a masochist. Shrewdly-schazenfraude would describe her sadistic side. Bisexual, even though boys have cooties, Kayleigh is single even when she isn't (because one never knows when Eliza Dushku or Mark Owen may pay a visit).

Kayleigh spends most of her time in her own world, Happy Gay Rainbow Land, where she can happily think up plots and the mansex is plentiful. She believes every fandom should have a well-written high school/college fic, and has set herself a mission to find them.

She does not have the internet and doubts she'll ever get it installed again. She is, however, very good at replying to emails.
