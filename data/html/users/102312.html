My name's Fiona, I'm sixteen and I live in Australia. Someday I want to get published. During the times when I am sick (which is a lot) I like to write and it's one of my favourite things to do - writing, perfecting my stories.

I almost keeled over because I found a review on my "Mrs Norris, Who Is She Really?" fic, just one review, so I'd really appreciate it when I upload that story I get a few reviews - hopefully more than thirty-six, this time. If any of you are lurking, please review. I'd very much appreciate it.
Thank-You!
