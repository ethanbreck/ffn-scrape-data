Story Updates

Dandelions: [2/11/13] In a desperate attempt to actually make some progress on this, I am working on Dandelions as my NaNoWriMo project this year. Fingers crossed it works!

Despite popular opinion, this story is not and will not be abandoned. I know, I know, I fail at a normal speed. This story, however, has been rewritten, rejigged, and altogether made a lot better. It will also be renamed and possibly re-uploaded under its new name. I'm not sure of time frames but it will happen. As of May 2013 I've been working hard on the new version of Dandelions. Once I've got a substantial amount completed then I'll start posting.

Ellipsis: [2/11/13] I loved Downton Abbey. Then I got my heart broken and decided to rewrite the universe. Hence, this fic was born. I'm trying to be really focused and good and not take 11 years to finish the damn thing so my current goal is a chapter a week... and have completely failed. I stopped writing this as after He Who Must Not Be Named (FELLOWES! *shakes fist*) completely ruined the show. I can't bear to read or write or even exist in a Universe where such beloved characters were completely ruined by their creator. I loved writing this though, so there is a possibility that I pick it up at some point.

Eventuality: [16/5/11] This story has now been revised and improved (with added juicy bits!).

Stupid Boys: [30/4/11] This story has now been edited- not too heavily, it's just been tidied up a bit.

Pretense: A Harry/Ginny fic that has long been abandoned. I wrote this when I was quite young so while it does hold a place in my heart, it won't be continued as my writing style has changed dramatically.
