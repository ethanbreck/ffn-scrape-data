Lover (Percy/Oliver slash)

I am a 25 year old, married woman who is currently in law school. Also, I happen to proudly be French Canadian (Woohoo!).

Having fallen in love with all things Harry Potter, I began writing some HP fanfiction earlier this year, and now, I'm completely addicted! Some of my most favourite Harry Potter things are, of course, the Ron/Hermione ship. *sigh* I just love those two together, and you'll notice that in all my fics, I have them paired up.

Also, I seem to have a soft spot for Perfect Prefect Percy (yum!), whom I always love when he's paired with Oliver. I've never written slash, and I don't know if I'll ever be talented enough to do it, but I just die for this pairing. I think they're perfect for each other. *again sigh*

Finally, I have a thing for our two favourite Slytherins, Draco and Severus (but not together. No slash for those two!) *fans herself wildly* If you like them as well, check out my fic, which I have co-authored with HarpinRed, called Post Tenebrae Lux. It's a spin off from my 'Cleverness has nothing to do with Love' fic. It's all DM/BZ, SS/NM, H/G.

http://www.fanfiction.net/read.php?storyid=789684

Check it out! (Shameless plug!)

Well, that's it for now. I hope you all like my stories. Don't hesitate to drop a line if you want to chat about HP!
