So here's the rundown:

I love fandoms. I love writing. That's why I'm here.

I'm about to be a Junior in college (I say this in August of 2006, knowing that this bio will probably stay up for another couple of years).

One of my majors is Creative Writing. (The other is Psychology, for those who are interested). I like to think I'm good - though by far not the best - but really, that's for you to decide. I just try.

I also love children.

I adore the connections between siblings, family, andclose friends...pointless downtime fluff and bonding makes me very, very happy so most of the stories you see here will feature it. If youare looking for action and bad@ness and fighting, hitthe 'back' button right now. I won't be offended. You will not find it here.

NOTE ABOUT "AFTERMATH"

Here's the situation: My one-shot, "Broken" was nominated in the 2007 Fanfic Comp. While I am pleasantly surprised, excited and flattered (profuse thanks to whoever nominated it), the news came within days of my finishing a heavily revised version of that story. The original didn't do what I had intended it to, so I revamped it and "Aftermath" was born.

But, for reasons of fairness/confusion, I don't feel right deleting "Broken" until after the Comp is over.

Another But: Fanfiction.net won't let me have both up at the same time.

So, "Aftermath" is going to come down until after the fanfiction comp is over. Then, I'll get rid of "Broken" and put "Aftermath" back up. Sound fair? That's the best thing I can think to do. If anyone has a better suggestion, I'm open to it.
