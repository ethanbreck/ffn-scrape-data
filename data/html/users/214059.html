My stories
Ratings: I will only write stories rating from G to R(maybe), no NC-17 . That is scary. Especially since I've only been writing stuff of Jhonen Vasquez' creations. Most of my Invader Zim fics will only be rated G or PG. Um, most of my Jhonen Vasquez fics will be rated PG-13, for obvious reasons.
Content: I will exclude from any of my fics, because, it doesn't fit the critera. I will use violence when nessicary, which, in JV's work, is quite nessicary. I don't use curse words for charcters in ZIM, because, they don't curse. I will not write ANY even implied Slash(Dib+Zim, Red+Purple) or ZAGR(Zim and Gaz Romance). Any SI(Self Insertion) or OC(original character) fics will not be ROMANTICALLY involved with Jhonen's characters(Alva is NOT romantically involved!!), or any character for that matter.
Reviews: I am one of the many people here who CRAVE reviews. Reviews make me feel better and think that people are actually reading my fics. Reviews also influence my work. Because people said they wanted more GIR in my fics, I dedicated an entire chapter to him in "Bubblegum Is Evil!", and because people are asking for a sequal for "When Comics and Cartoons Collide" I will surely write one. If you review one of my stories, I will probably review one of yours too.
Grammar: I am not a grammar or spelling queen, but I try. I use quotations and indentions, and if my grammar or spelling make it hard to read, please tell me so, so I can fix it.

When I Read Others' Stories
Ratings: In turn of not writing NC-17 fics, I also do not read them. I had someone ask me to read their fic, which was NC-17, and I read that one, but only because she promised it had a story. I don't like it when people don't rate their stories right, it makes me all sad inside.
Content: I don't like to read Slash. I hate it. I also don't like to read things that get too gorey. Gore is okay, but just not too extreme. I don't like it when people don't WARN other people of ual content. This has happened before, and I am now traumatized.
Reviews: I will review almost everything I read. If I didn't review, it's because I didn't read it. I will NEVER flame in reviews. Only on special occasions(such as that Slash fic that didn't warn that it was slash, that should have been rated R but was rated PG). If I liked a story, I will most likey go to that persons profile and look for more of their stories...and in JtHM777Vasquez' case, if I really hated it. That proved benifical since I liked one of her other fics. So one bad fic(or..three...) doesn't mean they are all bad.
Grammar: Like most people, I like grammar. So I can read the story efficiently. Spelling is also a good thing. But, some stories are REALLY bad grammar, I will read them anyway, but, I might not understand them. If the grammar and spelling is so bad I can't read it, I won't.
