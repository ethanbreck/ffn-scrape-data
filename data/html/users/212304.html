Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, House, M.D., Bonanza, and Sherlock.

I began writing Harry Potter fanfiction, with the majority of my stories centered around the character of Severus Snape (as anyone who likes anagrams might be able to guess from my pen name).

In April, 2008, I ventured back into fanfiction, with my first 'House, MD' story. In 2011, I went waaaaaaaaaaaaay back to my youth and began writing stories set in the 'Bonanza' world, with my particular interest that tall, sardonic, university-educated rancher, Adam. I guess I just have a 'thing' for tall brainy guys, be they wizards, doctors or westerners.

2013: Or detectives, like the wonderful Benedict Cumberbatch version of Sherlock Holmes. Guess brainy has always been equivalent with sexy to me!

Where have many of my older stories gone? Into the trash to comply with FFnet's policy of what is allowed on the site.
