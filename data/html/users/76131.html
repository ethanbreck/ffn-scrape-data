Hello everyone/anyone who is taking the time to read this. My writing is not great but I enjoy it, and enjoy the idea of other people enjoying it. I'm also working hard to get better.

Moving along, let's get to what you probably (hopefully) came to read. Cue shameless plugging...

Story Info:

Currently:
Cranes: A semi AU story set in a dark future. Naraku followed Kagome and Inuyasha to Kagome's time. They fought and lost. 200 years after the battle, they reawake under strange circumstances with fractured memories of the last of their time together. As they are swept into a new battle in a new world, can they put all the pieces together? Will they finally succeed where they have failed? In love and in battle?

Witnesses: Finished, but in need of much reworking for me to really be proud of it, even though it is my first finished Inuyasha story! (Yay me!)

Future:

Multiple things in the works here people:

Something to Sing About: Gone for now but not for good. When I have at least the next chapter written I'll re-post it!

Working on two other stories, maybe I'll put some better descriptions up later, One's an "after Romeo and Juliet story (IY and Kikyou were the star crossed lovers. Kagome's a girl who might help Inuyasha find a different fate).

The other is a high school story. It's the first day of (a yet unnamed) High School. Kagome is a kind hearted, popular, top student. Inuyasha is an outcast who doesn't want to fit in, even if the cost is his future. Miroku is a womanizer who isn't as cool as he thinks he is. Sango's a street smart tomboy with a fragile heart.(Yes, maybe it has been done, but it's still fun).

Well that's all for now. Please read, enjoy, and review my stories
