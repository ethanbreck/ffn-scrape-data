Ahoy. My name is Samara (Suh-m-are-uh; pronounce it properly, people =O) I'm seventeen, born on June 13th, 1990 =) I've got dark brown hair, green eyes, and an unhealthy obsession with books (favourite being Harry Potter, by far by far =)), anime (Full Moon wo Sagashite, Air, Fullmetal Alchemist, Noein), and all that is edible (mainly chinese food). My hobbies consist of singing, acting, reading and writing.

I rarely write fanfiction; usually once a year something strikes me, but I only write one-shots - not chapter fics - as I never have the inspiration or stamina to keep going. A lot of the stuff on here is terrible, as I wrote it maybe at age... thirteen. I took the stuff that I wrote at age eleven and twelve off, because that was more than just cringe-worthy. So far the only recent thing I have is a Harry Potter one-shot. Do read!
