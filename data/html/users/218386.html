I'm a seventeen year old girl from Denmark. Yes, Denmark, so if I've made some mistakes in spelling or grammar, then that's why. I love to read, draw and write. Exspecially something about Harry Potter. I can hardly speak ten minutes without saying something about one of the Weasley brothers. I also love to get in love with different guys. I have a thing for redheads.
Oh, and I love Friends, the TV-show. (My best friend is Monica. Really, she is. She's been cleaning our classroom!)
And, of course, Dawsons' Creek! Where do you think I've got my Pen Name from?

Some statistics:

Fave HP-book: PoA (I love Remus and Sirius, and the twist at the end was so amazing!)

Fave HP-character: Charlie. Call me weird, but Charlie it is.

Fave bands: Lemme have some BOYBANDS! Heh, sorry about that... a1 and Busted are my favorite, not to mention Lonestar (although I'm not sure about their boyband status...)

Fave song: I'll Be There For You (the theme song from FRIENDS, and also me and my best friend's theme song)

Fave actor: RUPERT GRINT!!!!! Not that I like the movies all that much, but Rupert is so RON!!! If it wasn't for him, Sean Biggerstaff and Robbie Coltrane, I wouldn't be watching those movies. (At least not in the cinema).

Fave actress: Katie Holmes (She plays me, for God's sake, how can I not love her?)

Fave fanfic overall: A Season of Change. (Go read it if you like R/S slash, it's amazing).

Ships I sail on: R/H, R/S, H/G, F/A, G/K, O/A, P/P, B/F, R/J (Figure out for yourself), and C/S. (Charlie and... someone I'll keep secret a little longer).

Good qoutes:

Nothing is impossible. The word itself says "I'm possible".

Those who danced where considered completely insane by those who couldn't hear the music.

Somewhere there's someone who dreams of your smile,
And finds in your presence that life is worthwhile.
So when you are lonely, remember it's true:
Somebody somewhere is thinking of you. (I used to think this ridiculous, until I learned that someone was feeling that way about me).

I cried today… not because I miss you… Or even because I wanted you, but because I finally realized I’m going to be all right without you.

No man is worth your tears, 'cause the one who is won't make you cry.

Lots of people want to ride with you in the limo, but what you want is someone who will take the bus with you when the limo breaks down. (I'll take the bus with you, Rupert, I will!)

The only nation you should have pride in is your imagination.

A kiss can be a comma, a question mark or an exclamation point. That's basic spelling that every woman ought to know.

And my two favorites:

There's a thin line between genius and insanity. I'm dancing on it. (Personally, I really think this describes me)

A friend isn't someone who'll jump off a bridge with you. A friend is someone who'll be at the bottom to catch you.

And last, I have a couple of persons to thank:
Charlie Weasley, for telling me so much about the life as a Weasley, and for being there when I need someone.
Sirius Black, for keeping me insane.
And everyone else, for giving me inspiration every day - thank you, everybody!
