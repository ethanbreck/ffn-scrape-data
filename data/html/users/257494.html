My, it has been a while, hasn't it..? I don't think I've come to FF.net in a long, long time. Not for posting my writings anyway... Well, I've grown older, and my writing has matured and improved (I like to think so anyway), and... I hopefully have become less prone to creating Mary-Sues. laughs slightly Any of you who were familiar with me before might not be aware of that, but yes, I was unfortunately very bad about that. Perhaps the only sense I actually did have was the sense not to post any of those stories.

That said, hello! I am Kage, and it's nice of you to waste your time reading this. chuckles Even if you are only doing so because you're bored. I'm fifteen years old and attend 10th grade at the local high school where I live in Alabama. I'm just your everyday, average teenage girl with a penchant for writing. I love anime, manga, and good fanfictions (no matter what sexual pairing - slash or heterosexual), and I am obsessed with Stargate SG-1. I like most music (except R&B and Rap) and prefer foreign music to American. I'm taking French III in school, and am teaching myself Japanese slowly but surely. What can I say? I like foreign languages.

Enough about myself though. Please read some of my stories (though the two at the bottom are old and not so good... I'm really not sure what I'm going to do with them... I might think of something one day, but if not, would anyone like to adopt them?) and I hope you enjoy them..?

~Kage
