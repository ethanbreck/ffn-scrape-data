Hey everybody!

12/8/03- Just wanted to give yall a little piece of news… I have another penname! It’s on my other AOL account. I’ve written a few things under it, all kinda fluffy, hehe! I’m not gonna tell yall what it is though… I wanna see if anyone can tell. If anyone does connect my two pennames, you will have the ‘high honor’ of… well I don’t really know yet, but I’ll think of something.

1/4/04- Hey everybody! Merry Christmas and Happy New Year! So much has happened in the past month: the release of The Return of the King, the holidays spent with my family, the last stressful days before vacation... I'd like to start off talking about RotK. I have one word for it: amazing. It was, without a doubt, the best movie I have ever seen. I laughed, I cried, I cheered. Never has a movie tugged at my emotions as much as this one did. I fell in love with Sam. I saw Legolas' blonde side (A diversion!). Bilbo creeped me out... I mean... he was just so gross... But, all in all, it was a simply breathtaking movie. With its wide scope and surprisingly intimate details, its enough to leave me senseless.
The holidays were nice. I got Sting the sword of Frodo if you didn't know that already, a cell phone, and a whole bunch of Lord of the Rings things. I spent a few days with my lake family, thoroughly enjoying myself, then headed home for the New Years bash. Let me inform you that my family is pretty redneck, so, of course, we all gathered at my Uncle Gene and Aunt Francis' house and played bluegrass. Then we brought the karaoke machine out my family's obsessed with karaoke too... and that lasted way into New Years day. Yesterday I spent the day at the truely awesome Natenate's house. We watched The Two Towers Extended Edition and CMT, life could not have been better. Back to school tomorrow though... My Natenate has a bball game, if yall would just wish him luck, I'm sure it would be greatly appreciated! Go #44!

1/24/04- Well... not much is happening in my life. Last night I went to NateNate's game. They won, so that was pretty cool. Today I'm going to the library... and well yea... I told you my life is dull. I guess I'll 'update' again when I have something else to say. 44

2/14/04- Happy Valentine's Day everyone! I'm going over to my boyfriends house to watch a, you guessed it, basketball game... Boys are SO dificult to shop for. Are you suposed to get boys stuffed animals? Because that's what I had to do. My Las Vegas trip has been pushed back to summer instead of Spring Break... We're going to spend more time out there and all... I'll probably update again sometime this weekend, I'm a little pressed for time right about now!

6/6/04- Long time, no update. I became the manager for the girl's soccer team from March through May which took all of my time right up. Now, however, it is summer. Expect me to be more involved in good old ffnet. In order to start me off with my writing, Sammy wrote me a short fic that I could work off of. Her's is Sweet Silence, a fic where Draco and Ron meet and Draco thinks back to the great battle. My continuence is Sweet Silence Once More where Draco and Ron reconcile.

Oh well... The boyfriend I talked about in months past is no longer my boyfriend (just thought you should know).
