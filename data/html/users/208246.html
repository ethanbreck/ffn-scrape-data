Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings, M*A*S*H, Stargate: SG-1, and Stargate: Atlantis.

I'm just an odd 23 year old girl. I'm from good old Idaho. Yes, I love the potatoes but I like the people better. I am a Christian and I am not ashamed of it nor should anyone else be afraid to talk about their spirituality whatever it may be, Christian, Jew, Muslim, Wiccan, Hindu, etc. My favorite vacation spot is Idaho City, though it's population is only 300. It's a nice little spot that doesn't have lots of crowds which is very nice in my mind. I also like snakes, mice, spiders, and lizards.

Some of my hobbies are:

1. Reading

2. Playing or watching Ice Hockey (GO Steelheads!)

3. Playing Lacrosse

4. Whitewater Rafting (better than a roller coaster)

5. Writing

6. Exploring forests

My Worst Fears are:

1. Crabs - I had a panic attack at Pirates Of The Caribbean 3. Oddly, my fave show on Discovery is The Deadliest Catch. Maybe because they are going to kill all those crabs. Hmmm...will get back to you on that.

2. Elephant Seals - Although this is more of just a thing I hate than am afraid of. I am very aware of what they can do to me as one did try to attack myself and some friends while we were with a park ranger. :-P

3. My cousin obsessed with explosives. Enough said.

4. Going psycho. I know what I am capable of.

5. Cafeteria food. I worked in my high school cafeteria and was really sickened.

My Worst Pet Peeves:

1. Whining dogs

2. Mispronouncing Boise: It is more of a local thing but does get annoying in the media.

3. Standing in lines that don't move.

4. Rude people - You know who you are

5. Being badgered when I am working
