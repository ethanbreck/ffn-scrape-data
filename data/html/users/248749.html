Author has written 78 stories for X-Files, CSI, Wire in the Blood, Law and Order: SVU, James Bond, Bad Girls, Rizzoli & Isles, and NCIS.

Wow, I've been at this a long time. Different fandoms, different characters, but they all have one thing in common- they all make me want to know more. What happens now? What happened then? What happened in between now and then? What happens next? My goal when answering these questions is to stay as close to canon as possible, so AU fic, while not entirely out of the question, most likely will not happen.

Some of the work I'm the most proud of is the stuff I've gotten the least amount of reviews- my Wire in the Blood stories. So the responses I'm getting for my Rizzoli & Isles fiction astounds me. However, truth be told, as much as I'm flattered by the feedback (who truly isn't?), I don't write for reviews. With that in mind, know I will never ask whether I should continue a story, will never hold a fic hostage for reviews, nor post a WIP that I don't already know the ending and/or have written at least 75% of it. I will never abandon a fic, which is probably why I write more one-shots than anything else! Unless otherwise noted, all my work is beta'd, because while in the end it's all for fun, I think approaching writing with a measure of seriousness can only help make you a better writer. I've been writing for over 12 years and I still strive to be better with every story. I hope you'll see that effort and I hope you'll enjoy taking that journey with me.

When I'm not writing, I'm running two websites: www.mandanafc.com and www.robsongreen.com.

Edited 12/31/14: Due to several issues, I've removed my Rizzoli & Isles stories and have posted them at archiveofourown(dot)com. I will be posting all future R&I stories there as well, so please bookmark the site if you're interested. Thanks for your continued support.
