Hi guys!! My name is Jessy!

I don't honestly know what to write on these things so I'll make it short.A lil' bit about me.

I have HORRIBLE A.D.D so I have a tendency to start a story, write a few chapter then get randomly inspired and start another story of a completely different topic. So it is likely I will be writing for more than one story but I do update them all as much as possible.

I love this website because it gives us the freedom to share our imagination with everyone. However, as much as I love reviews with constructive criticism, I will not tolerate negative and hurtful things posted as reviews. I won't even tolerate it if it's NOT one of my stories and I see it. I don't think anyone deserves to be bullied for trying to express themselves. In my opinion, if someone takes the time to review mean hurtful things it actually doesn't make you "Cool" or "Tough" it makes you an asshole that just took time out of their OH SO EXCITING life to post something pointless. It's freedom of speech and expression. Don't like something? Don't read it.

I love music. It's where I get inspiration from.

Norman Reedus in any role, Karl Urban, Zak Bagans, Alexander Skarsgard, Dan Feuerriegel (Agron on Spartacus) *SINGS* These are a few of my favorite things!!

Your/You're Their/They're Its/It's Then/Than...These are words that are almost ALWAYS messed up in my stories which is an issue I am working on. Not because I don't know the difference but because usual when I'm typing I'm going to fast to realize that I've done it (Does that make sense) trying to get better at proof reading.

I LOVE LOVE LOVE romance stories. Reading them and writing them. I guess I just LOVE LOVE lol.

Punctuation is my ENEMY LOL!

Again, I love reviews and I try to get back to people when they do post them. SO Please show me some love!!
