Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.

Ok guys, let's do it!

I am a girl who wishes to write the new great saga in the world. I am from Spain, and I think this is a good thing for me, as, let's check the facts: Harry Potter is British and Twilight is American... next teenager-saga MUST be from other country... so I got good chances! Of course it is easier for me to write in Spanish, but as I am almost bilingual with Shakespeare's language, I wanted to give it a shot. Sorry for possible spelling mistakes!

What the hell is in my AVATAR? Well, I am very proud of my collection of Harry Potter books in different languages! All started as a joke... a friend of mine went to Egypt and brought me the first Harry Potter book in Arabic (thanks Jara!). When I went to Poland, I bought the Polish one. And down the hill from then! I got almost 30 books of "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's stone" and ANY of them has been purchased by the Internet. Not at all! All of them bought in each country... European like French, German, Finnish, Norwegian, Danish... or Asiatic like Japanese, Chinese, etc. Some of them bought by me, as I love travelling, is my favorite pastime. Others, by my lovely friends or family in their trips, as they know very well that if they would have the harebrained idea of not bringing the book to me, they would hear my yells for weeks!

The master piece of my collection, the diamond of the crown is... the copy of the book in Latin! It took me more than 10 libraries in Rome, and some others in The Vatican, by finally, I got the probably last copy of the book in the whole Italy. It is in the front of the picture, I am very proud of it!

I am obessed right now with Twilight world... after having an almost decade of obsession of Harry Potter. I am not a traitor! I swear! I like the Harry Potter's books, some more than others, but I got completely hooked by the Twilight saga... by accident, really!

My favorite character is Carlisle, followed closely by Alice. Of course, I am madly in love with Edward Cullen. Someone doesn't? My other nickname in some forums is "Carlisla", but I like this one of Kalea, as it is the one I have been using for enjoying the Harry Potter world!

Just to let you know that another friend nicknamed Carlisla because I have bought the 1st book of "Twilight" for AT LEAST...ummm... 15 of my friends? They say I am good to make new vampires, as Carlisle... I don't succumb to the thirst! It is a good thing to do! I bought the book, and they got completely obssesed with the story, so I have plenty people to ramble on and on about the saga.

I will make a list of my favorite authors shortly. Thanks for reading this!

Kalea
