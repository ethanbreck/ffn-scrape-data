Author has written 6 stories for Lord of the Rings, Fanfiction Poetry, Harry Potter, and Outsiders.

Well, it is official. I'm am so out of the fanfiction.net loop that it is ridiculous. I feel so old whenever I log in.

My fandoms are numerous and expanding, my time in which to write fanfiction less so. I am reading, even though I am not reviewing nearly enough.

Hysterically enough, I do have an ending to my HP fic planned out, and I think I'm just going to put it up one of these days. Meanwhile, I post occasionally drabbly-type things on my livejournal every once in a while (eldarwannabe.livejournal.com) and I'll be hanging around.

I'm leaving my old stuff up for posterity, but I'll be honest, some of it is pretty bad. I lack the skill of a graceful endings, and I was known to write a Mary Sue or two in my day. (or four, but who's counting?)

I have a few things stirring in the back of my mind, but no promises!

Anyway, I've been away from this site for so long that EVERYTHING has changed. Forgive my updating mistakes, and my possible etiquette errors. Just know that I'm here, I'm aware, and I'm reading some fanfic.

And to anyone who has ever favorite-ed me, even if they know me, you all rock. Thank you.
