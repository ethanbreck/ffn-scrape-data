Hi! I'm Kami, the divine power that watches over fanfiction everywhere. I write the best stories you'll find on the net and I'd write them alot faster to if not for those pushy other gods (parents/siblings). Well it's been nice seeing you. Maybe so nice that I'll decide to do you a favour, but only if you read my fanfics. Never hurts to be friends with god does it. Well... Until my next update. Bye!!!

Okay then...

Bad news. After careful deliberation, and an exorbatant amount of writer's block, I have decided to delete a number of stories that I either believed would never be completed, were far below the standards that I expect from myself, or in one case, that of "Yajirobe the Legendary Saiyajin Slayer," I felt had simply become a rip off of another story. Anything that remains in my account is of significantly higher quality now, except for Herculean days -- a fic that's existence is hanging by an exceptionally thin piece of thread. Though the fic was somewhat original, it is well below the standards I have set for my writing; it was granted only marginal editing, leaving it in a less than satisfactory state. I prefer "Love Potion" over it, though I can't seem to write a decent ending for the next chapter (this has been a problem since mid-december).

Now that we've gotten the deppressing news out of the way, there is a plus side to all this. First, by taking a number of fics off my list of things to do, I will be able to focus more thouroughly on what is left; this means faster updates. In some cases the stories removed may not be dead as well. Many of the ideas are still interesting to explore, but I want to improve the writing itself. In addition to all this, another fine piece of work is... er... in the works. Though I already have its prologue written, I have not posted it yet, in order to have a few chapters ready when I post it. It's definetly a unique exploration, an epic story, sharing many features with the now temporarily dead "Damaio."

Well, that's all for now, so until I get off my lazy ass to update this again, Kami out.
