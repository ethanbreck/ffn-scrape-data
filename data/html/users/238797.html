Oh wow, i haven't been here for...quite some time.

I love writing. yes, that's right, i do. My works may not necessarily be the best, or even decent in some's perspectives at all, but i do spend a lot of time and effort into them.

Due to the limited categories on fanfiction dot net, i've stopped posting new stories here and instead, posted them on other sites.

Fanfiction.net fics:

At The End: Woots, my first E&T fic. A Story of a blossoming love between two people who couldn't be anymore alike...yet different at the same time. It started out as a serious fanfic with the whole 'dying kingdom' in the beginning, but then it turned into a comedy as time went on. I had SOOO much fun writing this fic, but then i kind of lost inspiration for it...and that's why it's currently on hiatus. I promise that i'll continue it though, maybe not now, but in the future, when my muse returns from her already-two-years-long vacation, this story will be back in action.

Feelings never Felt: Oh wow. My first fanfic. This story brings back memories of when i first started writing back in the 7th grade. Cliche plot, atrocious grammar, my first ever reply, my first ever flamer. Wow, how memorable. I still remember how mad i got when that one person flamed me for being immaginative and having bad grammar. Now that i think back, yeah, my grammar was ridiculously horrible. I guess the only thing that sparked fire in me after reading it was her person insult directed towards me-- How i was a disgrace to the ccs name for saying that the character's aura had color, and how random it was for me to write that Syaoran and Eriol were cousins. I guess it also bugged me that she was a hypocrite-- calling my grammar pitiful when she couldn't even spell "horrible" and "Disgrace" properly. Yeah, let's move on from this story.

Fanfics NOT on fanfic.net If you're interested in ANY of them, EMAIL ME AT TENSHIXD@GMAIL.COM for the link to them. _

Hope it's Not Too Late: Features two korean stars; Minwoo and Eugene. A Sweet promise during the Summer, A Promise to be TogetherMinwoo was devasted after his love's departure off to the states. As if things were not bad enough for him already, he gets into an accident and goes through something we call "Selective Forgetting" Yeah, it's a defense mechanism that makes you forget about somethign traumatic that you've gone through, but if that's the case, then why is it that the thing that Minwoo forgets...happens to be his fiancee who was half way around the world at the time? No Andy, ice cream cures all, but not in this case.

Help or DIE: Pedophiles, what fearful people to encounter...especially when you're alone at a trainstation. Luckily for Helen, her prince charming came just in time during her time in distress. First impressions are everything, and that's what Helen believed...which was also why she fell for her prince charming's best friend instead.

Defining Perfection: Brian Park is your every day, average, perfectionist-- infact, he makes a living by pointing out other's flaws and helping them strive to rid themselves of it. However, when the perfectionist meets the slob of his current century, how many lessons will it take to turn her into a lady?

Deceptive: What goes up, must come down is a famous proverb that proved to be more that just true for Stephanie Cheng, only, when things goes plummeting for her, they never go back up. life can be quite deceptive to some, and hers was just one of the many.

One...Two...Three.: Inspired by Kenny's TVB version of his song, Know First. Alluring, gorgeous, and tempting. She wasn't supposed to mean anything to him, not even a penny. He had a mission-- a task, and he intended to complete it. So why is it that he freed her instead?

Also with One...Two...Three. comes the PARODY version of it, featuring Bosco Wong and Leila Tong. Same link, same thread. You have a seemingly slow yet, supposedly really cold-hearted killer, and a loud mouth hostage. His task was to kill her, her goal was annoy the living hell out of him. So, how long will they last--no, make that how are they going to last at all!

The 'Best'- Take two wacky angels, throw in two soon-two-be lovers, a misthrown arrow and what do you get? A whole lot of chaos, cliche moments, and mushy moments.

Just Once- She only wanted to hear it once...just once and she would've been satisfied, but he didn't know-- he couldn't have. He was just THAT slow when it comes to these things...and that's why I'm in her place. Gillian Chung was a very average girl-- average grades, average looks, average everything! Yet, it was also because her life was so average that when mr.edison chen came, it turned her life unpside down. Don't be fooled by my quote, this story is actually a comedy. lolz.

Deleted Fics

Don't think that i was happy to delete my fics. It took me a lot of time to write them and i certainly didn't like seeing them deleted, but there comes a time where i just couldn't write the fic anymore, and i did not want to leave it in my account to give false hope to any readers i might still have for the story, so i deleted it. As for some of the other deleted ones, fanfic.net deleted it, not me. Why? I have no clue. lolz.

Jui Pa- A remake of a chinese comedy. Pork Chops are more than just the name of a dish in chinese, in fact, it's also used to describe ugly, unfit, hideous looking women. So, when the going gets rough, and the rough gets tougher, what do you do? You option for plastic surgery...at least that's what the four biggest pork chops of a certain company decided to do.

The 'Best'- The CCS version of my story. Deleted because i didn't want to change the name anymore

Eternal Happiness- A remake of a chinese series. Being forced to get married is one thing, being forced to marry someone you don't even know is another. Fed up with the old chinese traditions and thinking, Sakura runs away disguised as a guy. She meets her true love, new friends, and worst of all-- chaos, ALL to prove that a girl is just as good as any GUY.

Repeating Destinies- I have NO idea why this was deleted by fanfic.net. Apparently i violated some rule? I don't know what it was. I didn't plagerise, no cursing, and no lemon either. A comedy. Two lovers separated in their previous life must reunite in the new century to defeat the man who had killed them back then.
