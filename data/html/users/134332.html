Since I haven't edited my profile in 5 years, I guess it's due for an update.

I'm a boring old hausfrau from Oklahoma. I've been married for nearly 4 years, and have an adorable little blonde haired, blue eyed, 18 month old monster who would only eat cheese and bananas if I let her. I have a small farm where I raise chickens (I currently have 9 hens that my niece named after Disney princesses and a rooster named Rhodie), a Welsh Pembroke Corgi named Guinneviere, and a Great Pyrenees named Lancelot. If you can figure out why you get two bonus points.

Growing a garden I don't murder with my black thumb and getting Pygmy goats are my next real life projects. We're new to this farming thing. Chickens are fun though. Who knew?
