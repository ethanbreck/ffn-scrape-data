Update August 10, 2007

Fallen is finally finished, after four years of delays, starts and stops and writer's block. It's been a difficult ride, but it's done. I'm just not as into DBZ/GT anymore, but I want to finish them for those still interested. Are any of you still interested?

Thank you to everyone who has ever reviewed one of my stories. I appreciate it!
