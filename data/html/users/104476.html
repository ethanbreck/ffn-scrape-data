THERE IS AN IMPOSTER: Well, I can't say her name now because she changed the summary of her story which is still called "Winning Hermione" which I definitely don't mind, because it is a really nifty title, but she did have my summary up instead before that, which is unacceptable. SHe might be trying to steal my fanfic Winning Hermione. If you see this, please help me report her! I want to give special thanks to remuslives and and oOoJadedoOo for tipping me off. I owe yall big time! I didn't realize that stealing was such a big issue at this site. I really hope that the people in charge do something to fix it.

Wow, so I was 13 years old when I started writing most of my fanfictions! I'm 22 years old now and married with a baby boy named Ethan. Luckily, I'm just a big of a Harry Potter nerd as before, only now I have my own laptop with wireless! So I hope to provide some entertainment for others and myself by continueing my old fanfictions. You'll probably be able to see where the time gap was when writing, but I hope it won't bother you too much. I also have some plans for some new fanfictions as well, but we'll see where that goes. I have a semi-review policy. Anyone who reviews one of my stories, I'll usually review at least one of their stories that are Harry Potter fanfictions. Or at least try! That's a promise. :) So pick out a story, and enjoy!

Atrum Laus - Fondly known as Dark Glory. This is the most dark fanfiction I have ever written. But I love it for that very reason. Hermione explores her newfound wildness. She goes dancing and almost has sex with someone she hardly knows. Draco Malfoy saves her. She passes out and he takes her back to Malfoy Manor. There Hermione finds out just how dark Draco is. Will she ever believe that the light side might actually lose? Hermione returns to Hogwarts. There seems to be something between her and Harry, but that all might change when Ginny breaks up with Dean...

The Maiden - I've kindly twisted Harry Potter and The Little Mermaid into a fun filled, musically, challenging array of laughter and fun. Imagine Ron as Tritan, Hermione as Ariel, Harry as Sebestain (now that is hot), Ginny as Flounder, Draco as Prince Eric, and throw in Voldemort as Louis the Chef and you'll have yourself some fun. As of January 28, 2007 I have it updated to the part where Ron finds out that Hermione loves Draco...a bit of drama of course.

The Great Hall - This is a Harry Potter version of Moulin Rouge with Hermione as Satine and Ron as Christian. Also stars Draco as the unconscious Argentinian, Harry as Toulouse, Neville as Zidler, the twins, oh and Krum as the Duke. And many many other characters. I'm super excited about this fic because I really love Moulin Rouge, but it's a bit challenging to mush the two together. I'm also thinking of starting an alternate version of the story that would take place at the Burrow with Ginny as Satine and Harry as Christian. And Draco as the Duke. If anyone really wants me to pursue this sooner let me know and I'll get started on it. Oh, btw, this story is updated to the part where the bohemians decide that Ron will write the show and they come up with the plan to have Ron convince Hermione that he's a famous British wizard with amazing writing talents. Hermione has already done her intro number. She thinks Ron is the "rich one" that she has to convince to invest into the Great Hall so she can become a famous magical actress...

Deals - This has to be my favorite original fanfiction that I've written. I have it planned out with intricate details that I sometimes don't remember, but I do enjoy writing the chapters. The Malfoys and Weasleys have a strange connection. They make a deal that sets off a series of deals that affects everyone. Especially Ginny and Hermione who are stuck in the spiderwebs of the dark powerful men of their lives. Currently, Molly has dealed Lucius Ginny to Draco if she doesn't marry. Ginny deals with Molly to marry someone rich and powerful, kill them, and get their wealth. So far her two choices are Draco and Harry. Harry is her first choice, however Draco seems to have taken a hand in her choice. He deals with her to get her and Hermione to go to a dance club and dance with him. While there Draco deals with Ginny that she will belong to him and he will release her when Harry wants her. This deal takes place because Draco says he will distract Hermione away from Harry. Draco and Hermione have a bit of a snogging session that's rudely interupted by a death eater attack. Hermione is cursed and currently is in the hospital. Ginny saw the snogging and has her mind set to tell Harry and Ron. Hermione wakes up in the hospital wing. Hysterics occur. A prophecy. A suicide attempt?

Some of my favorite quotes from this story: Chapter 1 (Let the Deals Begin): Thus, a deal was made that set in motion a series of deals that would change forever the lives of the wizarding world.

Chapter 2 (Deals at Hogwarts): Harry answered darkly, "I'd rather see you kissing Malfoy than be on the receiving end of his killing curse out on the battlefield."

Chapter 3 (Draco's Investments): Before she was completely out, Hermione heard Ginny say, “Don’t think because you saved me I’m not going to tell Harry and Ron what you’ve done.”

Chapter 4 (Puppet No Longer?) Hermione laughed hysterically, "Harry, isn't this what you wanted? Now I'll never be on the recieving end of his killing curse, just on the recieving end of his fabulous kisses!"

Hogwart's Soap Opera - This is a story told through songfics. I enjoy writing it, because most of the songs are my favorites and they really inspire me. Chapter 1: Once Upon a December: Hermione has a dream. I loved this song since I was little. It was fun writing a dream story. Chapter 2: Blurry: I wrote this song in Draco's point of view and I loved the effect of it. It's kinda how he and Hermione get together. Fun times. Chapter 3: Tale as Old as Time: Yes, the song from Beauty and the Beast. It seems to fit Hermione's and Draco's circumstance. I love writing ball scenes! Chapter 4: Can you Feel the Love Tonight: Harry and Cho go on a date... Snape makes an appearance... only chaos can ensue! But the magic of the night is not lost, thankfully! Chapter 5: Dumb Girls: Lavendar Brown catches her boyfriend, Fred cheating on her with Katie Bell. Can Lavendar move on? Or will her heart stay behind? I have to say that as the chapters progress they get a bit more mature each time. WHich has to do with the fact that I get older and more mature everytime I update one of these chapters! But oh well. I love the beginning chapters, because they remind me how naive and innocent I thought love could be and I love the contrast with the older chapters which show the true nature of love. It's a battle.

Secrets of the Heart - Poor Hermione is all I have to say. Draco wants her. Harry wants to use her to find out more about Draco. Draco is making her toy with Ron's emotions. Ginny hates her because Harry is so worried about her. Lavendar hates her because she's stealing Ron from her. And Ron... well he's just confused. Anyways, this was my first fanfiction so a lot has changed, but the drama is still there! It's rather a light writing, so I like it. I mean, the plot is definitely there, but I find it easier to write than my others. Probably cause it was my first.

A Fire Inside - This is my newest fanfiction. It's a tribute to AFI. A collection of songfics really. It can be read as a story or you can just read your favorite song. Basically, Hermione joins the Death Eaters because she realizes that the darkness can give her everything that she can't have fighting against it, including someone to care and love. (Draco Malfoy) The plot will get more complicated with later chapters. Anyways, a lot of darkness, but always that small dark love that's a small light in the darkness. So far I've written Miseria Cantare (The Beginning) which is Hermione saying goodbye to Hermione, joining the Death Eaters, and establishing the feelings between her and Draco. Now I've written The Leaving Song Part 2 which I think is even better than the previous chapter! Lord Voldemort has a plan for Hermione. Will she be able to do it? Will Draco be able to let her?

The Burrow - This Moulin Rouge version of Harry Potter has Ginny as Satine, Harry as Christian, Draco as the Duke, Fred and George as Ziddler. Very different from the Great Hall though. This doesn't follow the script exactly. Actually not at all really. The most you'll recognize are some quotes and the songs. Everything else will be...well you'll see. hehehehe. It should be fun, interesting, and well spectacular! THe first chapter is sort of the background information you need to keep in mind while reading the story. I'm uploading the chapters how the movie is split up on the dvd. Main Ideas was suppose to the Main Credits or something, but since I have no credits, I just put the background info there.

Lily's Affairs - This is my first Lily/James fic! Well actually. It's not really Lily/James the entire time. Keep that in mind! The Mauraders are not the good guys. THey're actually Tom Riddle's followers. Lily is considered a mudblood by them. She's against everything they stand for. Remus Lupin feels something for her though so he offers his protection against them all. Lily secretly likes James even though he hates what she is. Will love prevail? What dangers and risks will Lily have to go through and survive to get to James Potter. The best weapon of love is jealousy and Lily plans to use it to the fullest.

Winning Hermione: I don't really write about Fred or George really, but this fanfic is a bit different. Hermione has lost her family because of the war with Voldemort. (he was destroyed though!) now the ministry of magic has assigned her "caregivers and providers" and they just happen to be Fred and George. Chaos ensues! Hermione's depressed and she thinks she's going crazy. (who wouldn't living with Fred and George!) But then Fred and George have a surprise for her! Then they decided that maybe if she had a boyfriend, she would get better. "Love heals all wounds" after all. They decided to have a contest to see who is worthy of Hermione's heart. However, the game doesn't seem to be working as Hermione finds herself farther from happiness. Who will rescue her? Who are you rooting for? I hope this turns out kind of funny, although I tend to write "dramas" so it might be dramaticallly funny? hehehe who knows!

Ta ta for now
