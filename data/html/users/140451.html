Author has written 24 stories for X-Men: Evolution, X-Men, Good Omens, Pirates of the Caribbean, Lord of the Rings, and Baby Sitters Club.

I'm a twenty-something person from Orange County, CA (yes, like that movie, and yes, like that show). For reasons even I don't understand, I resisted writing fanfiction for a long time; the stories had been in my head since childhood, but I could never see fit to write them down. That changed a few years ago, obviously, and here I am today. 'Tis a good thing...yes? I'd hate to think I'm wasting my time with this, but then again, how would that be different from the rest of my life anyway?

Don't answer that.

NEWS

Where in the world is MizzMarvel?

I greatly admire the fanfiction writers who manage to have real lives and are able to still consistantly produce many beautiful stories. Unfortunately, I am not one of these people.

But never fear, I'm still around, still writing, just on a less stable basis. My fic is not dead, just napping. New fics will come as I am able to write them. It just won't happen anywhere near rapidly. So, if you happen to be checking up on my profile for updates, mayhap you better email me (MizzMarvel@aol.com) or comment on my LJ, if you've got an account, to join my update mailing list.

Speaking of emails, reviews and kindly emails are the best motivation for me to make writing time. Subtle hint that was, no?
