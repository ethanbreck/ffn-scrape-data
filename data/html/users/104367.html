Author has written 15 stories for Law and Order: SVU, Beverly Hills 90210, Grey's Anatomy, General Hospital, and One Tree Hill.

I'm 22. I watch way more tv than I probably should. My current addiction is Grey's Anatomy. How could you not be addicted toa show with McDreamy in it? I'm currently working on about four stories. All of them are alternate universe. Why? Cause I like my universe better.

I love Meredith and Derek together, but I simply adore Addison. You really won't find me bashing her in anything I write. I like to write from her perspective because technically at the end of season two, she and Meredith are both the wronged women. Her husband has cheated on her with his ex, who Addison's arrival caused the demise of their relationship because she didn't know he was married. Derek has chosen obligation over love and now he's just making himself and everyone else miserable. Besides, I think Addison and McSteamy are hot together. She needs someone cocky and arrogant, but also totally in love with her. That's not Derek.

I haven't forgotten my 90210 fics either. They WILL get updated soon, but I don't know if I can say the same for my SVU. I lost interest during the seventh season. Last episode I really saw was 'Fault' which totally blew me away.
