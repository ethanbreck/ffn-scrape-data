Author has written 26 stories for Caitlin's Way, General Hospital, ER, Roswell, Alias, One Tree Hill, Gilmore Girls, All My Children, Eli Stone, Grey's Anatomy, and Vampire Diaries.

A real profile of me? Nah, too much work. But I did think that I should update this profile. I am still Asian, 3 years hasn't changed that fact. LOL. However, it has managed to see me add to my list of stories that I write. I think writing has been a way for me to both escape and realize the things I feel. I write things down that I want to happen, and in people's minds (you readers) it does. Also though, as I put pen to paper (or color to screen) I find my thoughts clarifying and I find myself focused on a goal and juxtiposed against the rest of my life which is indecisive and harry carry most of the time. I know what I want from my story and find satisfaction at seeing that come to life. I'm still working on that in real life. Anyways, enough with the deep stuff.
