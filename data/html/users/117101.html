Joined 10-03-01, id: 117101, Profile Updated: 02-01-12
Author has written 18 stories for Yu Yu Hakusho, Naruto, Saint Seiya, Bleach, and Mass Effect.

To sum myself up as quickly as I can: I'm a writer who just can't leave a good story alone without putting her own mark on it!

I have a few stories on here that I'm not totally proud of...but they are my creations. I think it's important to watch your craft evolve and grow. That is my goal in life: to just keep on writing, sharing, and hopefully entertaining those that read my work.

UPDATES:

2/1/12 - A HUGE thank you to everyone who has been enjoying my Mass Effect works! These drabbles just keep coming to me as the game progresses, although I'm not really sure how I'm going to end it. I kind of know how I want it to end, but I have to see if it can get there.

The reason why I say that is because characters very often have minds of their own. These drabbles were completely meant to be FemShep/Kaidan to the very last drop, but as I brought Zaeed in to give Shep some tough advice, things between them just...grew. I wrote one chapter and realized something was going on between them, and I've just been doing my best not to push it and to just let it evolve.

Anyway, I'm really glad everyone has been enjoying it! More is coming, so stay tuned!
