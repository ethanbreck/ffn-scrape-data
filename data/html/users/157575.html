Hi, I'm Herbie. I read Harry Potter mostly, but I also read Tamora Pierce and other various author's on occasion.

Ships that I support:
From Harry Potter:
Ron/Hermione (they're my favorite.)
Ginny/Draco
Harry/Draco

I'll also read Hermione/Snape, and even though I don't read Harry/Ginny, I know that's how the books will actually turn out.

I assure you that even though those are the ones listed here, its only because those are the ships I read with some regularity. I will read MANY other ships if I find a story that's interesting enough.

Pet Peeves:
1. People who deliberately have them graduating in 2003 (etc. etc.). In Chamber of Secrets, Nearly-Headless Nick clearly states that he was beheaded on October 31, 1492, and his 500th deathday party was that year. Clearly, Harry and Co's first year was 1991-1992. So please, for the sake of all that you consider holy, put them in the right year. They "graduate" from Hogwarts in the spring of 1998. You do the math.
2. People who don't really know what colors redheads look good in. I don't know about redheads with brown eyes, but MOST redheads don't look good in white, black, and especially red. Orange is alright, but yellow is iffy. Off-white/cream is best. Consult a redhead or a beauty-type person for other colors. Please, for the sake of my sanity!
3. Harry/Hermione shippers. They piss the hell out of me. The one Harry/Hermione story I wrote, I only wrote because the idea was too good to pass up, and worked best on the two of them. I'm rewriting it as a Ron/Hermione story sometime in the future. However, for evidence of the lack of a future Harry/Hermione romance please go here: Also, evidence that Ron likes Hermione can be found here: Please take some time to read the two of those.
4. Pardon the OotP spoiler, but people who think Sirius is coming back from the dead. You know, maybe if he hadn't fallen past the veil (depending on which curse he was hit with; it doesn't say), he would still be alive. However, the veil symbolizes DEATH and by falling past the veil you DIE! Why do you think people kept telling Harry to stay away from the veil?????
5. Authors that have no favorite stories posted in their profile. When I want something to read, I can usually look on someone's favorites list to find something. It becomes a bit of a problem, however, when an author doesn't have any favorite stories posted!

Also, I figured out the answer to the age-old Harry Potter question: Is Blaise Zambini a boy or a girl? Well, it turns out that Blaise is a BOY! Its a French name; I found out about it b/c of this guy, Blaise Pasqal (I think...) who was this mathematician who came up with some random pyramid thing. Anyway, Blaise Zambini is a boy with a French name. Could that be important?

Right...I think that's about all I have to say then
