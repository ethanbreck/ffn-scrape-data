Latest update 1/28/10: So I guess my run with fanfic has come to an end (not that there's much evidence of any run with so few stories up here, lol). I've been writing original pieces here and there and you can find those here: http://www.fictionpress.com/u/183733/ I'd love you to have a look and send me some comments or con crit. Thanks!

I promised to post more and I still haven't but I will I tell you. I've been working more on original fic than fanfic, but I'd like to get back in touch with fanfic-verses, since that's what really started my writing bug in middle/ high school. It's funny though, as I've gotten older, the movies that I think I normally would have written fic for seem too perfect and final to add more too. A lot of them have lead me to write my own pieces. I mean movies like Donnie Darko, The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, the Chronicles of Narnia...you know. I'm going to put myself to the challenge though for old time's sake and for fun.

So i guess it's fair to say that things have been pretty stagnant on this page as of late. I think an update is in order! The last thing I worked on was a joint fic you can find on The Brown-ie's page. It's a Harry Potter one shot about a relationship between a Muggle and a wizard with tragic results. Here's the link: http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3474864/1/

I'm 24 and from CT. I like listening to music!, singing, soccer!, the sun, playing capoeira, and chocolate. I'm a Latin American Studies major currently studying in RI. I like writing for fun.

So I'm currenly going over a lot of old stuff and finally working on some new stuff that I hope to post. I've always written things that I've never finished, but maybe putting them up will give me motivation.

I also write on fictionpress.com under GeckoPixie3. That site doesn't have very much up yet, but I'm working on some new things that I hope to be finished with in the next few months,

Reviews and con crit are always appreciated (even on the really old stuff if you feel so inclined). It'll help me improve.

Thanks!
