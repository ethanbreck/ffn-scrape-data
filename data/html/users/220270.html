I'm not all that good at talking about myself... I've dabbled in writing fanfics many times, but I can never really bring myself to finish them no matter how fantastically amazing I feel my ideas are. (I write all the ideas down anyway, you know, just in case.) I am, however, an avid reader. I'm not a fan of anything specific, but I enjoy any story that's well written. I read for the literary aspect, to see people's creativity, and to connect to my favorite characters when the story is over or in-between new chapters/episodes.

Pairings I Read the Most

Naruto: Kakashi x Sakura, Shino x Sakura, Sai x Sakura, Itachi x Sakura, Gaara x Sakura, Kabuto x Sakura, Sasuke x Sakura, Neji x Sakura (Yeah, I like Sakura, she's honestly a really good character.)

Skip Beat: Ren x Kyouko

Vampire Knight: Kaname x Yuuki, Zero x Yuuki

Inuyasha: Sesshoumaru x Rin

Soul Calibur: Kilik x Talim, Yun-seong x Talim

Fruits Basket: Yuki x Tohru, Momiji x Tohru, Kyo x Tohru

Gravitation: Eiri x Shuichi

Yu-Gi-Oh: Seto x Jounouchi, Yami x Yugi

Deathnote: Light x L, Light x Misa

Harry Potter: Draco x Ginny

Yu Yu Hakusho: Hiei x Kurama

Perfect Girl Evolution: Kyohei x Sunako

I also enjoy reading fics from Lord of the Rings, Trigun, Trinity Blood, Elfen Lied, Cowboy Bebop, Chobits, Samurai Champloo, Wolf's Rain, Initial D, RG VEDA, Record of Lodoss War, Ragnarok, Absolute Boyfriend, Gundam 00, and Negima. Unfortunately for me, this is not the limitations of my anime/book fandoms and because of it my wallet is very sad.

P.S. : I'm not a thief. Eventually I will be moving the stories I've written to here from AFF.net, where my penname is Weeble.
