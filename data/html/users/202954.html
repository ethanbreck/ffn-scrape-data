Author has written 6 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Full Metal Panic, and Harry Potter.

I changed my name to Gunso (obviously), since someone else apparently had Boogiepop.

(22 July, 2007)

Just posted the first chapter of Shining Through the Darkness, and hopefully it will do well enough. I thought it was a neat idea, anyway.

Now on to other things.

Just for the record, I have always loved writing, but I always had a
problem with loosing interest before I was finished. Zankoku was an
excellent example of this. I wrote around 80,000 words, then
tried to go back and revise before I finished, then a lot of stuff
happened in real life, and now I've lost almost everything I had
written. So unfortunately, Zankoku is officially dead and buried
(I'm sorry to all of you who were wanting more. I really did want
to finish that one).

As for other stories, Youth, When We Were Innocent and Free,
was meant to be a one shot and will probably not be revisited. I
had fun writing it though!

FMP: To Take Peace From the Earth is on semi-permanent haiatus.

Her Most Beautiful Smile is on hold for the time
being. It wasn't going to be a really long story anyway, but I just
sort of lost interest. I promise I will go back and finish it someday.

Something More is essentially finished, as I have wrapped up everything
that I wanted in those two chapters. If I think of something else, I
will add to it or begin a sequel. For now, however, it is finished.

Obvious Decapitation is a one shot, but it was fun to write, and I
still go back and read it every few months for fun. I think it's funny,
but who knows what you will think. (I'm currently thinking about doing
OD 2, though it wouldn't be in the CCS fandom like the first one)

Shining Through the Darkness is my first try at something non-anime. I
have a cool idea, but havn't really taken it anywhere.

That's all for now, thanks for reading my notes!
