12/07/18

I last updated this profile on 6/17/16 and yikes...

It's been a great while since I last been active, heck, I haven't written anything for nearly three years! Years that I've gone without typing anything remote to a story. Well, that has changed - I'm back! Sorta! In the sense that I'm trying to get back into the groove of things and what better way than telling a story through drabbles. Hopefully, once I'm comfortable, I can pick up my old stories again!

Things About My Writing You Ought to Know:

All of my story contain a certain amount of realism to them. I tend to write using dark, emotional themes. I'm not very good at humor or fluffy romance. I'm best at emotions. When writing, I aim to release whatever creativity I have that's brewing inside my head. At times, said creativity might not be everyone's cup of tea but that does not paint my state of mind either. So, if you happen to come across a story or scene that I have written that you did not like, don't call me mentally disturbed - This is only my creativity at work.
