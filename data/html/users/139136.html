UPDATE - APRIL 2018

It has been a long time since I've written anything. I'm still suffering from depression (about 14 years, now), but lately I've felt like writing. My 'new' story is a complete re-write of The Winds of Change, called "A Change of Season"

I've also got a new e-mail address - christinahilt@snakebite.com Feel free to say 'hello'.

My original story on this site was The Winds of Change (TWOC), which I removed but have now re-published here.

The sequel, The Ways of the Worlds, is unfinished, and it's possible that I will never finish it. The Daily Prophet is kind of an 'interlude' between the two books. All my Harry Potter stories fit into the TWOC universe.

Most of the characters, places etc. in my stories belong to others, but I take credit for the plots and the occasional place and character of my own.

I also have original stories and poetry published at http://www.fictionpress.com/u/139136/
