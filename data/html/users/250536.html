I have grown to dislike cluttering up my stories with lengthy author's notes and disclaimers, so here it is: I do not own any stories or characters except those of my own creation, and I make no profit from any of my stories. If I did, I wouldn't be working at the magical workplace I work at as a cashier even though I have a bachelor's degree, and just the fact that I'm always working on something or another and I never update even though I say I will soon renders any profit I would make...pretty much nonexistent. I apologize to my readers who are constantly left hanging...I truly do have the desire to WANT to write...I have great ideas that I love and that I would love to put down. I just never get around to doing it after everything else.

NEWS:

I am in the process of re-writing Too Much To Ask, and it will be posted as a new story, Too Much To Ask Redux. Updates will be as soon as I can...which are not very often but I'm trying to work on that. I'm sorry to all my readers! I'm a horrible updater.

ABOUT ME:

My name is Kristina. I'm 22 years old. I was born and live in Rhode Island currently. I recently graduated from the University of Tampa with a B.F.A. in Graphic Design, and in my spare time (when I'm not cashiering at my little slice of heaven) I try and do some work freelance to build my experience and portfolio. Words cannot describe how much I miss Florida. I hate everything about winter...the cold (and in extension, the heating bill), the snow (snow should only be on Christmas, and maybe a few times after, but after February...NO! And let's not get me started on shoveling and driving through it), the dead trees and general lack of greenness...besides the fact that in FL, I can wear flipflops and go to the beach...mostly year round, and there's no static electricity, dry hair, and cut cuticles. And let's not forget that my city is a stinking pile of crap...oh, we're from Woonsocket...like it's an excuse.

I am a devout Catholic by choice, not by birth. I loved my church down in Tampa, Sacred Heart...not so much the churches here in Woonsocket. It's just not the same. I was the President of my school's Catholic Student Organization. I was also involved with Intervarsity, a Christian group on campus, I was the secretary of our Anime Club, and a member of Circle K, a service group. I love reading, writing, art, art history, music, talking, nature, anime, and video games.
