Hey everyone! The name's Dan, and I'm a rookie fanfiction writer, but an avid reader. I have written a few works the past few months, none of which have been posted on the Internet.

Anime currently viewing: Naruto, Gundam Seed Destiny, My-HiME, Bleach, Rockman.EXE Axess/Stream. Currently suffering a serious case of writer's block.

Update: 8/11/2005

Yeah, I'm still alive. Enjoying the last legs of my summer vacation at home; just came back from the Philippines.

Even though I've had an account here for a while, I haven't bothered to look over my stories from high school. Well, I looked at them tonight and I feel kinda disgusted, to be honest. I know I can write better than that now, considering it's been roughly 3 years since I last touched 'em. I'm not positive on where I'm going to go with Chaos-Ken. I'm thinking that I'll trash the idea of it becoming a mega-crossover, which was my original idea in high school, and just restrict it to Ranma/DBZ. This also means that I'll have to change the outline. Basically this means I'll probably end up rewriting the story. It probably needs it. No set timeline on when this will happen.

Now, concerning my 1st fic, Lighting 'Em Up...it was originally intended to be a one-shot, but I saw how weakly I ended it. I think I ran out of ideas at the time. I doubt I'll have to rewrite this fic, since I kinda like the way I started it, but now that I've gotten some experience with Mage: The Ascension and Vampire: The Masquerade (yep, World of Darkness), I think I'll start implementing some elements from those two sources into this fic.I think it would be quite a ride, seeing as how I've read another good Ranma fic crossed over with Vampire: The Requiem. Oh yeah, I suggest you check it out. It's by GothicPhoto, and he's under my favorite authors. Anyway, once again, there is no set timetable for when I'll start writing again, but we can always hope. The couple of reviews I've gotten the past year have shown that some people actually somewhat enjoyed what I have written up to this point, and it's given me a bit of initiative. I'll do my best for ya'll!
