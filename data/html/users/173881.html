You have to let it all go, Neo. Fear, doubt, and disbelief. Free your mind.

I used to be Crystal Kitten, writer of crap stories beyond belief. Thankfully, I have progressed from the age where I thought Harry/Hermione was "okay" and introduced myself while abusing the Japanese language every chance I got.

Now I'm Nain, Matrix and HTML purist. I still give into the occasional preppy urge (the Hilary Duff CD is one example). I am a die-hard Ron/Hermione shipper, Lizzie/Gordo shipper and Trinity/Neo shipper. I haven't referred to anything Japanese in over a year. And I hate the Backstreet Boys.

So I would appreciate it if you would read my stories. I try to write quality fiction. And I'm my own worst critic. Look for stories on the joint account with my best friend (WingdCallisto), Nain and Calli. Thanks.
