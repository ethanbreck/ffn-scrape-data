Author has written 6 stories for Super Smash Brothers, Legend of Zelda, and Pokémon.

Welcome to my repository of Zelda parodies, original Nintendo fanfics, and imaginary head-canon adventures. Fanfiction.net was where my life as a writer really took flight. Now, I come back here now and then to write stories I get excited about, but that I can't publish in real life. Or when I have writer's block and need to write something really uninhibited to jar myself out of it!

I've got a few old stories--all unfinished--still up here so you can see what my writing was like back in the day (*Cough* Not that great. *Cough*). In an ideal world, someday I'll wrap them up! Readers here on FF.N have been so kind to me, and I would love to get to complete things for them--even if it's, like, a bazillion years late. (Love me some Ocarina of Time! P.S., have you guys played Breath of the Wild!? It is SO AWESOME!)

And I've got a couple newer fics out: Legend of Leen is a Pokemon fanfic (in college, I learned it was okay to be a grownup and still have nostalgia), and I've got it planned out from beginning to end, so I'm hoping for some top-notch story development. Check it out and see what you think so far! We'll see if I stick around long enough to get it finished. :)

And after reading some great feedback, I thought it would be good to write up the first story in the series, so I've started Pokemon: Red. Legend of Leen is late in the saga, and I originally started there because it was the most interesting to me. But I'm hoping this story will give some context for the character Jason's journey. In Pokemon: Red, Jason has already started his kid-superhero career, so it's not really the true beginning to the story--that comes in an original novel I'm hoping to finish soon! In this Pokemon side-series, Jason and his friends find a parallel world--the Pokemon World--, where they have adventures centered around the themes of Pokemon: the interplay between nature and technology, the ways loving bonds strengthen our resolve, navigating victory and defeat, and of course, the power of friendship. (Insert magic sparkles)

Christian themes manage to find their way into my stories by virtue of who I am; my faith has built my philosophy, and your philosophy pours out into your art, so that's just sort of how it turns out. I hope whatever your background or worldview, you'll have fun laughing at the funny stuff, thinking through the deep stuff, and enjoying worlds of story and imagination with me!
