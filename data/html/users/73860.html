Author has written 19 stories for Rocky Horror, and Silence of the Lambs/Hannibal.

As most of the true Lecterphiles, I see the end of the film as a very misguided interpretation of what was a fantastic novel. Therefore, most of my work revolves around fixing the movie to my liking.

This will likely not come as a surprise to anyone, as it's been about seven years since I updated anything...but I am retired from the Lecter fandom. I write Buffy the Vampire Slayer fic now, under my real name. If you're interested in reading porntastic Spuffy fic, feel free to visit my website, Paragon of Peace.
