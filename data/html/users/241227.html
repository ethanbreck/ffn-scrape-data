Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Transformers, and Transformers/Beast Wars.

Hm. I'm never quite sure what to put in these things. I've lurked and been involved in fandom for many, many years. My fandoms are numerous, though I tend to focus on them one at a time. My current love is Transformers. I'm pretty easy going and don't take myself too seriously. Fandom is meant to be fun and I treat it that way.

I guess the popular question is 'When are you going to update your fics?' closely followed by 'Have you abandoned your older fics?' I haven't abandoned anything, but I'm a very slow writer. It doesn't help that I'm more likely to read fic than write when I do have time to devote to fandom. (And one day, I will own a computer that is not shared with others so I can really write again.)

If, on the rare chance you want to write a fic or draw a picture based on any of my stories, go ahead. The more the merrier I always say. Just credit where you got the idea and drop me a link, cause I want to read it too :-D (This is not permission for plagarism, however.)

Otherwise, I hope you enjoy my fics and are maybe inspired to participate in fandom in some way.
