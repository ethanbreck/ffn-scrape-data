Homepage = my journal

Yes, I write mostly romances and stories that include romantic themes. I'm a lot like Christian in that sense... "Always this ridiculous obsession with love!" No, I don't write Mary-Sues. I have written them, but that was when I was young and stupid (some of them are still here-- for nostalgic purposes, I am compelled to hoard them). If the situations and/or characters my stories include seem Sue-ish to you, please find another writer to pick on, because I'm not changing unless it's of my own accord. Honestly, as a young adult, I have very little perspective on anything other than the lives and feelings of other young adults. I'm working on expanding my horizons, but not much of that stuff ends up here.

No hard feelings- just givin' everybody the facts.

:sings: Come on, people now! Smile on your brother! Everybody get together- try to love one another right now!

PS: Don't look for Creatures of the Night (PoTO) or Sanctuary (X-men). They were gross. Admit it. If they ever come back, it will be after some EXTREME revamping.
