The stories so far...

It’s taken what? Nearly two years for me to update this? Blame Life, burnout, lack of interest whathave you. In any case, I'm done with SaTW fan fiction. Why? I get all the country craziness I could want from work (which keeps me more busy than comfortable). I’ve written a lot for SatW and I’m ready to move on to other projects- mostly some original writing (which I strongly recommend checking out on my deviantart account also under ‘mekkabable’. I won’t ruin it for you, but I promise it’s an awesome fantasy story).

I know it's terrible practice to leave a story unfinished (as I have in the case of Iceland's Birthday Cake). For those of you who have been patiently waiting for something I have no intention to post, here's the final chapter that will never be: The others feel guilty about making Iceland feel sad and throw a weird party to show just how much they appreciate him.

I have faith the rest of you will continue to write for this fandom though. It’s given me a lot of joy and entertainment and hope it does the same for you.
