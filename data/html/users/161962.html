I'm Erana. I've written fanfic for a TON of different fandoms, but only a small few are actually posted here. One day I'll stop being lazy and post the rest. The odds of my finishing "The Memory Remains" are very, very slim, I'm sorry to say. But it's not completely impossible - I may well be re-inspired one day after re-watching the show. Just don't get your hopes up.

My current obsession is "Doctor Who". I've learned from my Profiler story, however, that I shouldn't post stories that are incomplete. I certainly hate it when I find a story I love that's been abandoned, and I don't want to do that again to other people. So even though I do have a WIP for Doctor Who, I won't be posting it until it's finished.
