23 June 2004

Wow. It's been a while since I've logged in. It took me a couple of tries to remember my password, but here I am.

I'm hopefully going to find another place for my fiction soon and then delete this fanfiction.net account. To tell the truth, I've been planning on doing this since fanfiction.net took down all of the NC-17 stories, but have been too busy working on other projects. I've also been considering starting a live journal.

In other news, I just got back from PortConMaine a few days ago. Man, it was awesome! I met so many wonderful people and bought a lot of nice anime posters and CDs. I'm particularly proud of a Kakashi and Iruka shitajiki [Naruto] I found.

As far as fanfics go, I have a number of Yami no Matsuei fics in the planning stage. Most of them are short Hisoka x Tsuzuki one-shots, but I do have a lengthy semi-crossover with Angel Sanctuary that I'm very excited about working on again.

I guess that's all for now.

~Armadei
