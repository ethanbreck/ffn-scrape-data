Ah. Hello, all! A few words of warning, if you will. A lot of what is posted here, under my name, is from when I first started writting. It's not exactly good(okay, not good at all, and I was an incredible bitch to my readers during Aftermath) but seeing as it's when this madness all started, I just had to keep it posted as a tribute to that insane day when Allison and I were bored during band (as we often were).

The only fics I view with pride are Cry, Every One Unique, and The Little Things That Matter. Cry because it has a deeper meaning for me, and because it's something other than all this mad, mad fluff. And Every One Unique and The Little Things That Matter simply because they were my first HP fics. Except for no one really seems to want to read The Little Things That Matter (or at least they don't review), which is a shame because I put so much work into it and was quite proud. Also, Ciel De Soiree, which I wrote with my friend Dani quite a long while ago.

Anyways, enjoy! And always remember that we don't know what you think or if you're even reading if you don't review. Even if it's restating the same thing someone else said, or [constructive] criticism, every review counts.
