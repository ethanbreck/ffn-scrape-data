I started writing way back when the fifth season of Stargate SG-1 was still to be announced and now it has completed ten seasons, has two spin offs, one of which has completed five seasons - so, yeah, that was a long time ago. I like to make a connection with people; I try to respond to every review I get as it is more fulfilling as a writer to discuss with the reader why it is they like/dislike my fics. Fandom has given me many great friends and I thank them heartily for their support throughout the years.

I deal in angst. Lots of it. It makes me happy to make our characters sad and gloaming. I'm like that. Because life isn't made of happy endings and fairy tales and when we do get that happy ending, it's generally because we've had to fight so damn hard to get it. I try to write my characters as close to canon as possible and when canon doesn't give the characters a happy ending, I find it difficult to give them happy endings, too. Part of my angst is multi shipping a lot of my characters because... Well, I'm cruel.

I lack an ability to write in linear time, which is why there aren't many multi-chaptered fics on my profile. One-shots suit my style of writing but I hope to change that over the course of my writing career, lol. I'm experimenting with different forms in a Roswell fic called "He Lays in the Reins", so I will hopefully transfer some new styles into my other works soon, too. I wrote my first slash fic on a train on my way to a Stargate convention and I have to say, the only slash I can stomach is Holmes/Watson, so don't worry faithful hets - I shan't be slashing Sheppard/McKay anytime in the future, lol.
