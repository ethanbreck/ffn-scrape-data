Hello to all!

I'm sixteen yrs old, and now have my Learner's Licence, so if you live in Australia, or are visiting STAY OFF THE ROADS.

I live in Australia. And no, we do not ride to school on kangaroos. I live in a community outside Canberra, which is, contrary to poular beleif, the capital of Australia.

I do not consider myself a 'writer', so therefore do not suffer from writer-related problems, such as writer's block, artistic writer's depression, writer's foot etc.

I am hardly ever satisfied with what i write. That's probably because most of it is a load of crap. Don't call it 'putting myself down', it's the truth, which i am accepting as true and constant at the moment. I get very sick of people calling my crap good. If they honestly beleive it, well that's their own lookout.

I like Hitch Hiker's Guide to the Galaxy; LOTR, Two Tower's being my favourite; Harry Potter, the series, not the kid; Catch-22, except when it applies to me. I also like Gershwin, Saint-Saens, Tchaik, Bizet, anything people would put in the 'boring classical' catagory.

I HATE all out, no apologies slash, though implied is not too bad. For this reason I do not write all out no apologies slash. I basically don't write what i wouldn't like to read.

I like reading fics, but don't like sifting through 100s of them, so if you want me to read your fic, or know of an awesome fic, tell me.

Don't get distraught when i don't put up new chapters, they'll come eventually.

And feel free to email me.

That's about all i have to say.

Beware of the Grim (the Grim being the car i drive)
