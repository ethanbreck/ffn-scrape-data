Author has written 35 stories for JAG, Friends, Psych, Once Upon a Time, Grimm, and Numb3rs.

I started posting fanfiction a long time ago. Some of you have been faithfully waiting since 2005 for the completion of one story in the JAG fandom and others waiting for completion of a story in the Friends fandom. I keep telling myself I'll finish both, but in both cases it's' been almost 10 years, although the friends one, I really had something humming there, so hoping a phoenix might rise from the ashes from that one. At this point, though, I don't want to get anyone's hopes up. Generally I'm so busy with work that I barely have time to write more than a sentence or two, but I try to scratch the muse when and for as long as it itches.

You're pretty much guaranteed to have something spark creatively if there is a couple that shouldn't or can't be together, but they clearly should. I'm a sucker for any plotline featuring unrequited love, or two people who can't stand each other that somehow find love together trope (let's call it the Bennett/Darcy effect).

For some reason, Grimm has kickstarted the muse in a major way. Don't know why (although see above on the love trope; also there weren't nearly enough Nadalind fics, sooo...I tried to fix that). I've posted more stories about this fandom than shows I've watched for ten years, but whatever, we're going with it.
