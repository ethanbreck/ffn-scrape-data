A note regarding A Human Saiyajin:

I have been asked numerous times if I am ever planning on continuing A Human Saiyajin. The truth is, I really would like to see this story finished, and put to rest. However, I must admit, my knowledge of Dragonball is rather limited, and I don't really like it enough to actually read it. The story has always been a two man project, each of us having contrary view points on where the story should go. Every chapter, there was a fight or argument on the artistic integrity of each of it's creators, and because of that, it has tempered the story to what it is now.

I do not think I can achieve it's full potential alone, which is one of the reasons I have not finished it myself. I have not passed it along because that would relinquish whatever creative input that I hold.

Ideally, I would rather continue this story with Mike. If not, then I would be open to finding another co-author to finish this story, and perhaps put it to rest.

Sincerely, Kazblah
