Author has written 14 stories for Harry Potter, Book X-overs, and DC Superheroes.

rabbit is a children's librarian who's been writing fanfic off and on since before high school. She is writing, honestly, just slower than before. She tells stories to small children, occasionally does magic tricks, and has been known to attempt to pull herself out of a hat... ~v~Jinx~v~ is a mysterious black-garbed figure who occasionally is mistaken for a Miserable Penitent Reformed Death Eater... which is nonsense. She is, of course, a charter member of the Rogues Gallery. And she's reformed. No, really. Yes way! Would I kid you?

If you want to get a response, try the LJ. I don't guarantee anything, but it's a lot easier to answer there than here. rabbit

JUST A NOTE (October 2011): I recently peeked at reviews here and found one (anon) accusing me of flaming people. Just so you know, I haven't actually used this alias for anything at all for several years. If you got an anon review purporting to be from me (or jinx) and it was more recent than the last story posted here, it was a faker. Sorry about that. Judging from the vitriol aimed at me the faker must be a real toad.
