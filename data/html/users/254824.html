Author's Note

I'll be honest with you here - the stories dated from 2002-2004 is subject to dubious grammar, incoherent formatting and screwed up plot, to say nothing of an embarrassing lack of control over plot and characters. Meaning the Saiyuki pieces, the Disney-esque stories, and almost all the stuff within the Mulan section, with the exception of about two. Maybe one.

But, if you would STILL like to read them, you are welcome (although I don't really understand why). Just be warned that the writer was, back in the day, proud and happy, young and foolish. Thus I committed some of the worst no-nos in fanfiction. Be told.

For those of you who revisit this page and remember reading my earlier works when I was just starting to write, I thank you still, from the bottom of my heart. You gave me the support I needed to grow. And I appreciate that immensely. I couldn't have practiced and/or improved without any of you. Thank you once again. (bows)

Other than that, I hope you enjoy reading and have a nice day. :)
