Chapter 8 of Devotion has finally shaped up into something presentable. Sorry for the long wait.

About me: I am just about the biggest bookworm I know: I read pretty much everything, and so what I write is equally eclectic. I have tons and tons of original stories, all in a binder that's almost overflowing. Maybe I'll put some of them up one day: www.fictionpress.net/profile.php?userid=207570.

But, for the moment, what you'll get here is fanfic.

As far as reading fanfics goes, I like Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Labyrinth, the Princess Bride, Firefly, Stargate: SG-1, stuff based on the White Wolf games, and generally anything mushy and romantic or sci-fi/fantasy. Actually, anything that's well-written is definitely worth a peek.

Oh, and if you review one of my stories and you don't have an ff.net profile, please at least leave me your email address so I can thank you, and let you know when there are updates (as they are infrequent, unfortunately).

Thank you all for your patience!

~Sierra
