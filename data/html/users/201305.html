Name: Lyris Malachi

Age: 29

Location: USA

History:I've been writing for as long as I can remember. I'm working on a couple novels and possibly a poetry compiliation. I do know a thing or two about writing, so rest assured I'm not just another squealy fangirl that fantasizes herself in the plot. I basically only write fanfic when I'm stressing, need a break or have writer's block. I don't update regularly if I write longer fics, but if it's been longer than a month, feel free to e-mail me and demand an update (assuming I ever have a story that popular).

I welcome all reviews, even the OMG, TEH KEWL!1oneeleventyone but if you speak in netspeak often, we probably won't be friends. I prefer constructive criticism, the kind that actually improves my writing, so if you see something wrong with canon or grammar/spelling (gasp), please call me on it. I will be very grateful. And please, please don't touch my dialogue unless it's missing a verb or is otherwise incoherent. Dialogue is written how a person really speaks. Don't. Touch.

Hobbies/Interests: hockey, gaming (table top, video, you name it), Flogging Molly and Korpiklaani, writing, reading, mythology/religion, languages

Favorite Fandoms: Stargate SG-1, Fullmetal Alchemist, Firefly, Cowboy Bebop, Sandman

Disclaimer: I got sick of putting disclaimers on every fic, so I'm putting one here. I don't own the created characters, plots or fandoms I write it.
