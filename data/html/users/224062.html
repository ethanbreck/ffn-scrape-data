"Procrastinators unite... tomorrow!"

Hello. My name is Kacie, and I'm just your average thirty-two-year-old fan. I honestly don't have much of a background.

I'm for starters the youngest in a family of four from Texas, and I have recently gone from working part-time to officially full-time as a receptionist at an assisted living facility. I prefer (black) tea over coffee, and my favorite color will always be green. I have two pets, thus far: a black and white domestic short-hair cat (Oreo) and a black Labrador Retriever (Scout). As a side note, my astrological sign is Pisces, and my Chinese Zodiac is the Rabbit.

I'm a college student aiming for a Bachelor of Fine Arts, in the hopes of going into the business of writing. Though, I'm starting to wonder if I should have changed my degree into an English major, by now. Oh well. Although college has unfortunately been on the back burner for me for some time, at some point I plan to finish it when I can.

As an all-time nerd, my life is dedicated to anime, cartoons, and video games. I have quite a few nicknames accumulated on the internet and in person, but you can go by my real name or any part of my account name. My hobbies include drawing, reading, roleplaying, and writing (but mostly roleplaying and writing).

I once hoped to go into a profession towards art, but my own personal challenges made it get complicated and I realized perhaps an artistic profession wasn't meant for me to achieve. Through discovery in my English teacher back in early high school, I realized I had a knack with writing. I think that deep down, I've felt a bit of something while I wrote; a spark if you will. ;) I eventually realized that I quite enjoyed it, especially coming up with enjoyable storytelling and weaving plots.

I hope to one day see original stories of my own published and on the shelves. Some people seem to think I have the potential, so I can only hope. But first comes effort, and as any writer knows, coming up with something from scratch that's original and not a fanwork takes much more time, especially if you want it to be right. Then again, I'm OCD and I know for a fact it has foiled me on a number of occasions. All the same, I consider fanfiction to be a good medium for honing my skills as a writer.

Along with being a beginner in cosplay (when I'm not broke that is), I'm a bit of a shipper. Though who isn't, these days? Writing fanfiction on pairings is a great passion of mine and I have many pairings I'm in love with from all forms of entertainment.

I hope to post some fanfiction on here, and I also hope you will all enjoy what I have to offer.
