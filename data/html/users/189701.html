27 January, 2007

Well then. It's 3 days short of 2 years since I've updated my profile, and all promises I made beforehand have not been fulfilled. But there are now new promises - although I doubt they mean much - and they're currently in progress.

First of all, I doubt I am ever going to finish my old fanfics. They're long past their due date, and I think it'd be best to leave them there. Maybe sometime in the future I may pick them up again, although that is extremely doubtful. I just hate to finalise things like that, so I'll leave my options open for them. But like I said, extremely doubtful. I was thinking of taking them down, but I'm extremely sentimental and can't bear to do such a thing, so for now, they stay.

Right now, I'm at a point in my life where I love writing but I just wish I had more time for it. I'm heading to university in a month, so anytime before then will be filled with as much writing as possible. However, I'm in a place where I'm trying to improve my original fiction, not fanfiction. Fanfiction is helping me back on my feet, and getting me in touch with the fandom I love so much, but if there's not much fanfiction coming from me, it's because there's so much more of the original stuff.

Current projects?
I'm currently in the middle of writing for that famous challenge, the 100 themes challenge. I'm also in a challenge to finish it over 3 - 4 days. Currently on the second day... and I think I'm going pretty well! Look out for new stuff sometime soon.

Lots of love,
Christina

(Formerly known as MoonDiamonds & Queen Dia).
