Author has written 17 stories for Harry Potter, and Crystal Cave.

When I first began reading/writing fanfiction, I swore that I would NEVER become one of those annoying writers who post a few chapters and then make her readers wait forever for updates. Never say, "Never," my friends. I'm afraid the "Choosing the Head Boy" and "Cave Canem" are on permanent hiatus.

If you like to read Remus/Sirius slash, go to my other pen name, http://www.fanfiction.net/~mysid My other stories, both here and under the pen name "mysid" are complete. So, if you don't like cruel writers who leave you hanging, please give them a try.

This quotation always reminds me of MWPP: Now this is the law of the Jungle-- as old and as true as the sky; And the wolf that shall keep it may prosper, but the wolf that shall break it must die. As the creeper that borders the tree-trunk, the law runneth forward and back-- For the strength of the pack is the wolf, and the strength of the wolf is the pack. -Rudyard Kipling "The Jungle Book"
