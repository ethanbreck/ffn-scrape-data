Current Series:

Captain N for modern times. Kevin Keene is dragged into Videoland, the video game multiverse with his mother to become the Game Master. The seal on the warps has collapsed and Power Palace is in danger.

1 - Power Bloc Unification - Explorers of Videoland - Chaos Control - Demon War
The N Team is formed after their first encounters with the worlds of Videoland. They help Sonic and Tails retrieve the scattered Chaos Emeralds. Eventually, they find Outland, where a Burning Legion attack cuts them off from their warp. The Legion invades Power Palace and other worlds, before the N Team stops them, leading to the creation of the Power Bloc.

2 - Six Worlds - Open World - Rival Teams - World Compression
The warps are now open, leaving Power Palace exposed. The Power Bloc continues to grow, but its ascent begins to attract attention. One of these groups is the Cross World, which dispatches agents Reiji Arisu and Xiaomu to build its own Cross Team. Another is the UEG, which is blessed by the arrival of Mike Vincent and his girlfriend, Mandy Summers.

Demons of the Dungeon (Devil Survivor & DanMachi)
Someone is summoning demons in Orario, which is causing the world to become connected to the Expanse. Once demons start going in, Abel and friends discover the openings. To complete the investigation, Abel goes undercover as an adventurer, learning about the world of Orario.
