The not-so-mysterious entity known as Maggoctopus. I'm more commonly known around the internet as "Iatro," but I prefer to use my muse's name for writing.

Once upon a time, Maggoctopus (affectionately referred to as 'maggotoctochick' by a select few) only wrote Metal Gear fanfiction. Projects are 'If It Weren't For Bad Luck...' but that was pushed aside for 'La Tanière du Serpent,' and there was the occasional addition to 'Stupidity Abounds'. All MGS projects have since been abandoned and left to rot.
Also wrote the now infamous 'Russian Teacakes'.

Current original writing projects can be found at the website link provided; that points you to Maggoctopus' creative writing journal.

Rumors circulate that Maggoctopus may return to the realm of fanfiction writing.
