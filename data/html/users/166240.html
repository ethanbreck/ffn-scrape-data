I like the Kelloggs box because it has a great big ELLO in the middle.

So ello ello. My name is of little consequence but my nick is Kat. I live in the cold and freeze my butt off and dream of the warmth. I am of the class of 007 (for high school). And yes I do love trying to talk about myself and remain vague enough that whoever the freaky stalker people are out there, they won't come knockin' on my door.

I love Inuyasha. I only write Inu/Kag pairings other than some OC characters. I am cuurently working on Lady of the Blade which I hope will go ever well. As far as my other fic, do not read it. It's not exactly my best piece of work, it is old, and pretty much the only people who will appreciate it are my old friends.

Oh and I love to read other peoples fics so if you have anything you want me to read, let me know. And I'm wonderful with the English language so if you need a Beta, let me know about that too.
