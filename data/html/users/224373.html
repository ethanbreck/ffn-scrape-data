Threads of Fate (ZnT):

Hey Everyone. For those of you that are still awaiting the next chapter (those precious few of you) I've got GOOD NEW! Kinda. I talked with my editors, we discussed what was supposed to be coming up and what will be coming up and I decided I couldn't handle several plot points. In essence, I couldn't reconcile Louise's existence with what would happen in the story. It isn't a case of I couldn't write her in but rather... she seemed to just tag along for the ride. Sure, she had several cool scenes, but she feels like that fifth wheel that never belongs. I couldn't find a place for her that wasn't filled by another canon character. As such, since the year long hiatus, I have actually re-written ToF. Without Louise.

Yes, much tears were shed upon this but I believe it is for the better. To those that wish to continue reading, the current version (and newest chapters) is up on Beast's Lair under the same name until I believe there's enough new content to resubmit the story onto fanfic (dot) net.

Regards,

Vigilantia
