UPDATE:

2018.Nov.13

I am still working in the Education field but now work in the Registrar's Office, reviewing documents on a computer all day long. I had an idea for an Ita/Saku pairing for Naruto and used a 50 Themes set for inspiration. It is complete but I will be uploading it in groups because I'm always going back and tweaking it. 2014.Oct.15

I was promoted to the Corporate Office about a year ago and have been doing over-time when allowed. I stare at transcripts and transcribe them to the computer all day long so I rarely feel like doing anything more than catch up on my fav tv shows when I get home each night. Occasionally the itch to write something emerges and I start writing it down on paper but I lose the gumption to type them up once they are out of my head.

Currently, Transformers owns my soul and I continually have little plot-bunnies running around that involve the characters. Also, my dad got into Transformers Prime and so we both were watching the series recently (he for the first time and me for the second). My dad is pretty cool like that. :)

Also, I've been working on rewriting one of my Gundam Wing stories but it is slow going--I have an idea of where the plot should go but getting there is taking a bit longer than I expected.
