Author has written 18 stories for Weiss Kreuz, Final Fantasy VII, Matrix, Saiyuki, and Prince of Tennis.
I enjoy writing when the inspiration hits me or when I fall in love with characters that just need written in what my minds eye sees. Writing is just a release of the tension most of the time, something that gets whats in my mind out, and why not have people enjoy it while I'm at it.

I don't care about flames, so try all you want you won't get much of a reaction. Though constructive criticism is always welcome. I love to improve.

If you want to archive anything please just tell me. I don't mind but I like to know where my stuff is so I can send people to the sites to check it out. Also if you see characters that you really like and want me to write something just e-mail me and maybe we can make something work.

Have a good read.
