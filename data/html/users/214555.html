Hi!

I'm a Buffy/Firefly/Roswell fan who doesn't write because I don't have the drive to continue on one story very long, and I fear the wrath of impatient readers, a group of which I am a part.

Thanks to Alsike for yelling at me when I don't write, and when I switch stories constantly. Someday I'll finish something and post it, but by then I may be well on my way to senile (if I'm not already).

If I seem really impatient for more in my reviews, I'm not trying to be rude, it just means that I love the story and want as much as possible. I don't send compliments I don't wholeheartedly mean.
