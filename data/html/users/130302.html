Author has written 22 stories for A-Team, Stargate: SG-1, and Thunderbirds.

Jack-of-all-trades, master of few, fingers in too many pies, an imagination faster than lightspeed with fingers struggling to catch up.

Incalcuable thanks to my Drew and not-so-wee-Hamish for being sounding boards, both willing and unwilling. And to Avatar2--we've been writing together for a long time, my friend.

1 August 2007

What was it someone once said? "Life is what happens when you've made other plans." Life has not yet finished with me and my family. This past year has been one of constant "whump", as if making up for ignoring me for so long.

Katie Breanna, you are missed SO much. I sincerely regret writing "White Night", for now I truly know how Josie and Lydia feel.
