If there's one thing you need to know about me, it's that I'm a fandom nomad. I will be utterly smitten by one series for one week, then fall for another the next. That's not to say that the old flames are ditched entirely - I still enjoy them, but I find I can only properly devote my attention to one at a time. Some I've outgrown (ie. realized they weren't half good as I initially thought them to be, and I'll refrain from mentioning which), some have become mainstays that I carry with me despite my flavour of the week.

That said, my current fandoms are:

Akira
Megaman Zero and Legends
Hellblazer (the 'Constantine' movie's going to SUCK, by the by)
Sandman
