So, a little background, huh?

Well, I am a very eccentric person and don't have just one focused fan-base in my mind. I see something that I like, I will more than likely become obsessed with it for a time and write a fanfiction or two on it. Some obsessions stay for a few weeks, others last for years. It just depends. So that being said I write on any show or movie that does or can have a couple. I write very little OCs, and I mean VERY little to couple up with main characters. I have been known to do so in the past, but I am trying to stick with main characters now. Trying... I don't always succeed.

I am working for the Walt Disney company in the resorts and hope to one day move toward the Burbank side of things. I have a bachelors in Criminology and am now in process of my second degree. This time I am going after Animation. I will tell you all when I get to Burbank.
