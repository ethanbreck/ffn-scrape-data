Not much interesting stuff about me to put here. I'm 21, about to be certified for dog grooming, and I love to write, sing, play golf, travel, and role play online. I have a Papillon named Kujo who's my best friend in the whole world- sorry, internet friends. You come in second to my puppy!

A LOT of the stories on this account are VERY old. Some of them are very bad. I'm leaving them up for the people who DID like them, and it's nice to look back and see my improvement over the years I've written here.

Also, if I haven't updated the story in a few months, don't bother reviewing JUST to demand that I update it- I'm not going to. With very rare exceptions, if a story dries up on me, it's not going to go anywhere. If you want to, you can poke me on IM and ask me what my plans are/were for the story- I don't mind being questioned about it, but when demands are thrown at me, I'm MORE inclined to leave the story dead in the water.

I have lots of interests, but one of the main ones is anime and manga. I'm into Bleach, Saiyuki, Naruto, Legal Drug, Rorouni Kenshin, and Death Note. I also LOVE Final Fantasy and Kingdom Hearts. As far as movies go, I love Constantine, Jackie Chan movies, Newsies, musicals, Jurassic Park (all three!), and I find the Pokemon movies strangely fascinating. (Yes, I'm one of THOSE. A girl in her twenties who still likes the stress relief provided by Pokemon games.)

I'm very friendly, so if you have a question or just want to chat, here is my IM information! Don't be afraid to poke me! If you need a player in your rp, don't hesitate to ask. I'm always interested in new rps to check out and possibly join.
