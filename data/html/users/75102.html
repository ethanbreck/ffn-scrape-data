Ah, screw it. Let's face it, I'll probably never finish Fire, Flower, and Warrior. I realized in re-reading it that.. well... it stunk and needed to be completely re-written. Everyone begged me to finish it first, and I really did try - I have half a chapeter sitting unfinished on my computer - but I just don't have any desire to work on it anymore. I'm dreadfully sorry, but my heart is yearning away from fanfiction to my own writings. That doesn't mean I won't post the occasional fanfic, but I wouldn't expect anymore epics. If you actually enjoyed my reading - I won't blame you if you didn't - look me up on Fiction Press, same screen name. I apologize to the few but faithful readers of FFW, but I fear it will be a very long time before it is finished, if it ever is. If you simply *must* know what happens, you can email me and I will give you a summary of events. I apologize again.
