Mercury Blue is a twenty-year-old woman from BC, Canada. She's been writing Sailor Moon fanfiction for six years, but only recently began posting at Fanfiction.net. Her favorite characters to work with are Amy and Raye, and she prefers to use dub names, although she is quite familiar with the original Japanese ones.

Mercury Blue enjoys writing and reading fanfiction. Though she only writes Sailor Moon fanfiction, she likes to read stories based on Final Fantasy X and The X-Files, as well as various other types.

In addition to being a writer, MB likes to draw and sketch. She has an extensive collection of Sailor Moon fanart, which can be seen at her homepage, Mercury Blue Online.

MB loves romantic fanfiction. Her favorite couples are (from Sailor Moon) Raye x Chad, (From Final Fantasy X) Lulu x Wakka, and (From The X-Files) Mulder x Scully. She also favors any stories having to do with Ami/Amy/Sailor Mercury.
