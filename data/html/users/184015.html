Hey all, I'm an avid fanfic reader. I collect Favorite Stories. Check out my list! I only add stories to my favorites list that I have read and really consider good. I am most peeved by malapropisms, implausibility, bad characterizations, and bad grammar (in that general order), so my favorites are usually clear of those. I tend to like more serious stories that are character driven, so most of my favorites are in the genres of Romance, Drama, and Angst and not so much in the genres of Humor/Parody, Mystery, or Horror.

I've mostly given up on my attempt at writing. Maybe some day when I have more motivation (more like discipline).
