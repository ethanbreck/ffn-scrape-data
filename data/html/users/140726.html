About Mundungus42

Mundungus42 was raised in a log cabin in the woods. She spent her childhood falling out of trees, catching toads, and building up an immunity to poison ivy. When she was of age, she set out to seek her fortune in the big city, where she currently resides. Ms. 42 was introduced to fanfiction in 1998, when one of her summer students asked her to beta-read an X-Files story. A few archive searches later, she was hooked. She began writing fanfiction in 2002 and has never looked back.
