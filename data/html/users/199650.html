Author has written 65 stories for Neopets, Harry Potter, Chrono Trigger, Inverloch, Warcraft, Kuroshitsuji, Warhammer, Borderlands, Assassin's Creed, and Dishonored.

I started writing back in DOS Edit and saved my stories to a 5" floppy disk. I wrote fanfiction back before I knew it was called that. It's probably a good thing that the internet wasn't really accessible back then, as my first stories were pretty darn bad. Like, shapeshifting rebellious dragon princess bad (look, I couldn't decide if I wanted to be a dragon or a cheetah with magical powers, okay?) But after lots of practice, I think I can toss around a fairly decent character and a plot that actually makes sense.

I enjoy writing original characters into fandoms and it seems to be working so far. I like strong female characters and anti-heroes.

Some of these stories are quite old, so check the dates before you form an opinion on my writing to make sure you're not reading something ten years old or something.

My philosophy on reviews is that we're all at different levels of our writing and I'll try and review you at where you're at. If I just don't like your piece at all, I won't leave a review. I'll only comment if there's something in it I like. I try to limit what I suggest to improve on because I feel it's a bit much to ask someone to magically become an awesome writer all at once. It takes practice. LOTS of practice. Don't get discouraged. Just keep writing and if something doesn't turn out so well, discard it and write something else.

I tend to only read short stories. I don't have much free time to commit to much more, sorry.

If you want a really long-winded version of how I feel about critiques, take a look at this.

You can also find me at deviantArt as fainting-goat and my book is Mortal Gods by Bonnie Quinn on Amazon.
