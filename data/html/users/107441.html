Author has written 9 stories for Stargate: SG-1, NCIS, Criminal Minds, Numb3rs, and Stargate: Atlantis.

I cannot tell you how I became addicted to fanfic except that it occurred during high school (which is ages ago for me) and I started out reading Star Trek: Voyager fic first. I found FF.net when I was in college. That is when I discovered most of my favorite shows and fandoms.

I am still heavily against bad fic. I still hate those who cannot and will not use proper grammar, spelling, punctuation, sentence structure, hell, everything the epitome of an English professor is obsessed with. And Yes, I did end that sentence with a preposition. And cannot stand those who refuse to research the fandom they are writing or writing without any regard to canon or even civil decency.

I will not write slash or romance. I consider slash wrong, so morally wrong, and will refuse to even consider it, let along write it or read it.

Hopefully, I will get my act together and write more fic but I am great at ideas but not so much as to follow through beyond a certain point.
