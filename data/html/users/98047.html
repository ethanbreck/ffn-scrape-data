Author has written 47 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Yami no Matsuei, Tiger & Bunny/タイガー＆バニー, and Harry Potter.

I'm a sluggish updater, but I'm determined to finish my stories. My thanks to all you readers, especially those who come back for more! I have fun doing this and you make it even better than it already is! Thank you for all your patience. Feel free to drop a line via email or my journals if you have comments, questions or concerns. I do tend to disappear from the internet for weeks at a time. This is usually due to work or travel so don't be alarmed if you don't hear back right away. I can usually manage to at least read your comments and they are all appreciated. I can also be found on AO3 and I post occasional flash fic to Tumblr and Dreamwidth as well, if anyone feels like looking.

If by some chance you want to translate one of my stories to another language, that's usually fine, just ask (and make sure you leave some way for me to get back to you). ;D Check out my favorites list for some good reads that frankly blow my humble efforts out of the water. There'll be all different types and preferences, but the fanfic universe is big enough for all of us. :D

Current Project: Revamping and completing 'Path of Seduction'. Stay tuned!
