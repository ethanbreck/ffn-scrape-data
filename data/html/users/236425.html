Well here I am, back again after a second years-long hiatus. I'm inspired by news of an upcoming miniseries of actual new Gilmore Girls episodes. I believe it will air on Netflix and that something was going down in the summer of 2016, but whether that something would be filming or airing I don't know. I'm hoping to actually finish some of the stories I've started, prior to the airing of the miniseries as I live in fear that the new miniseries will dash my hopes and dreams for our favourite couple - or even worse the fear that it will inspire me to start yet another story destined never to be finished! Aak!

There's just something about Rory and Jess! They draw me back in every time!
