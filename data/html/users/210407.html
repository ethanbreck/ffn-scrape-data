Author has written 19 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter.

Hello there. My name is Kathryn. Some call me Kat. I'm a college student, and I have no idea what I want to do with my life. I've changed my major from History, to Theatre, to General Studies with a minor in Anthropology, to Criminal Justice. So as you see, I'm quite clueless about my future. I live in south central Louisiana. Now you're thinking, "Ew, icky Southerner and their lawnmower of a car, and alligator of a pet." No. That doesn't happen. The Waterboy was so untrue. Uh... anyway.

I'm a big Lord of the Rings fan. I saw the Fellowship of the Ring in the theater eight times, Two Towers three times, and Return of the King five times. (Crazy, huh?) I've also read the trilogy three times, and the Hobbit twice. I took a stab at the Silmarillion, but my brain exploded at the complexity of it. As you can see, I used to write lots of LotR fanfics when I had time on my hands. (You know, scribble story ideas down in class and stuff. Fun times.) I love the hobbits to pieces. If I had the power, I'd go to Middle-earth and find myself a nice little hobbit burrow and just live there forever. I stopped writing once I got to college and found myself a neato boyfriend. That tends to take up most of my time right there.

In 2007, I became a big Harry Potter fan. My boyfriend is really into it, and convinced me to start reading the books. So I quickly read the first four before the Goblet of Fire hit theaters. I finished reading all seven books for the first time. Yay! Go me! I've come to realize that Ron is probably my favorite character. He's just a silly funny little guy, so there.

In case yall (OMG I say 'yall' cuz I'm from the South!) cared, I do write Mary Sues (mostly LotR but now a little HP). I generally don't share them with the public. They are kept in my Word Documents. And if you do see a Mary Sue story that I've written, it's for humor purposes only. I would never EVER post a true Mary Sue story on fanfiction.net. That's just silly.

I also just finished reading the Twilight series, and I love it so. But I really don't think I'll be writing fanfictions about it. Sorry folks.

So now you're probably thinking, "Wow, what a strange girl. Isn't she a bit old for this?" Yeah, you're probably right. But you know what? Oh well! Who cares? So I'm just going to mosey on off to do whatever I please.
