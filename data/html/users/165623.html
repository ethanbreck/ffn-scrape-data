Name's Kimmy, and I'm a Junior in High school, studying to major in Theatre Fine Arts. I write for a hobby, and don't claim to be the best writer, cause I'm not. -Eh-

I'm an active RPGer, going on 3 years now and I do have a life, but it's school and my drama night life.

Let's see here... ships I support? Ginny/Dean has gotten me since OotP came along - - I've always been a Harry/Hermione freak. It's just perfect - - Remus/Hermione, when written right is also quite the sinker.

My writing is mostly short stuffs, based off various songs I hear and get stuck in my mind. It works, I suppose. Some of it is plot-line inspired stuff from my RPGs, those are fun sometimes too.
