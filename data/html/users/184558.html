Greetings!

I've been writing fanfiction for years, but for some reason I never got the urge to post any until I got bit by Kingdom Hearts bug.

As a child of 80's I grew up on Disney cartoons, in fact I never laughed at cartoons until I saw Donald Duck, and he's been my favorite Disney character ever since. Unfortunatly, when they came out with "101 Dalmations 2" I lost the faith.

So I turned to the more promising world of anime, and I 've never regretted it. In no particular order, my top five anime series include:

Tenchi Muyo Ryo-ohki!

The Slayers

Trigun

Utena

Anything by Rumiko Takahashi (I guess that's cheating a bit ;)

Also, I'm a huge fan of Terry Pratchett (as if my pen name didn't make that glaringly obvious). I haven't written any Disworld fanfiction, because I'll never do it justice. Hey you don't mess with perfection.

If you haven't read any Pratchetts, GO READ ONE NOW! I mean it! He came in second in the vote for Britain's best fiction author (I don't think I have to tell you who came in first) and quite frankly he was cheated. I don't care what anyone says.

Go read Soul Music. Trust me, you'll love it.
