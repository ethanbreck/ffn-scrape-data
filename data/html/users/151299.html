Author has written 5 stories for G. I. Joe, and Misc. Anime/Manga.

See the avatar? She's Belamis (Beramisu ベラミス（SF西遊記スタージンガー）, from Starzinger, an anime I used to watch during the 80's. Thanks to You Tube, I had the opportunity to watch all the episodes after so many years.

I love this character. She's a great cyborg warrior, just as good at Jan Kugo (the main character in the anime).

There are a few fics about her, most of them in Japanese. That's the reason I wrote in English, because, I thought that if those fic were in English I could read them. :(

Another character that I love is Zarana, from GI JOE (80's cartoon). In reality, the whole family Zartan, Zarana and Zandar, fascinates me.

La chica de la imagen es la Capitana Balamia, de la serie El Galáctico. ¡Me encanta este personaje! En los 80's pude ver algunos episodios, pero nunca transmitieron toda la serie. Ahora, gracias a You Tube, pude ver lo que ocurrió con Balamia.

Balamia es un personaje extraordinario y creo que sobresalía de la Princesa Aurora.

No hay mucha información de Balamia en la web, pero hay un blog dedicado a ella que le rinde tributo .

Tengo también un blog de Balamia en tumblr.
