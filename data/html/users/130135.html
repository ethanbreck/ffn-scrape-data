What I have to say right now: AUGH! ff.net added new rules to their terms and agreement rules and that just bothers me. You can't post dialog based stories? So no play format, or IM conversation stories, which sometimes work better than anything else with a particular plot. No parodies, lists or bloopers, which when I was really into Harry Potter, were my favourite things. I'm entirely pro political correctness, to a point. I think we should all just act like decent human beings to each other, but when it comes to literary expression, no one is tying you down and forcing you to read anything. There are tons of things I've stumbled across on the net that have pissed me off to no end, but I've either delt with it or replied. I figure even if I disagree, even if I find what they have to say sickening, it is their right to say it and I have the ability to leave the page if I want to. It should not be ff.net's responsibility to keep kids from reading inappropriate material either. Sure, they should be able to ban people who post M rated things under K, but really, if someone underage is looking for adult material they're going to find it. Anyway, I just wanted to say how much I appreciate FF.net's slogan "Unleash your imagination" unless of course it actually involves leashes, whips, chains, ect.
