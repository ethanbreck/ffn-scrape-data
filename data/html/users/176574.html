I've been a lurker on this site for many years and I have finally decided to try my hand at a few plot bunnies that won't let go. My concepts are not new, but I like the twists that have been added and I think they offer a fresh story.

My favorite ships by far is HG/SS. As individual characters I find Hermione Granger & Severus Snape to be complex, unique, and full of life. As much as I loved JKR's story telling, I believe Hermione and Severus's stories were in no way told and I am very happy to be part of a fandom that has taken up the reins and continues to write in their world. I believe a connection between these two characters is quite natural and I love to read and discover how other authors bring these two together.

My Ships:

HG/SS - Hermione Granger / Severus Snape (HP)

HG/DM - Hermione Granger / Draco Malfoy (HP)
