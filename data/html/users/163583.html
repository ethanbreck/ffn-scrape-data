Hello dark past :D I haven't written in a while, and reading through my old works makes me gag for some reason. Please excuse my poor grammar and writing style--they were written during my young, tender years :D I'll try to edit/fix some of them if and when I can, and while I'm at it, update some ongoing fics as well. I can't promise anything though, and I'm not sure if anyone is still following my works here so..

Anyway, I'm hoping to write again soon, if I could squeeze some time from my busy, busy adult life. :D But I have missed this place, and it feels great to have some old part of me, documented and immortalized somewhere, no matter how messy and inexperienced I was back then.

So, hello. And see you around. :)
