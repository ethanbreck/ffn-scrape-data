My name is Kayla and I am 18 years old. I am a freshman in college and I really enjoy writing fanfiction. I am currently writing many stories for a few sections, so if I update slow, I'm sorry. I'm working as fast as I can. I am writing stories for the following series:

Sailor Moon stories:

1) Falling Rose Petals
Kayla McGinnis and Destiny Alumni are the new Senshi in town, who are seeking answers about their pasts and trying to find the Earth Senshi. Their mission is a hard one (find the Moon Princess, Earth Princess, Earth Prince, the Inner Senshi, and the Earth Senshi), but with the help of their new friends, who Kayla just happens to find out are Sailor Moon and Tuxedo Mask, they just might get the answers they need. And who are all these new Senshi?
I hope to get Chapter 6: Looking for Answers out soon. So, look for it and if you haven't read this story yet, please read it. It is really a good story, even if the main characters are not the Inner Senshi.

2) The Moon Princess
Princess Serenity and Prince Endymion are being set up by their parents, in hopes of marriage. They meet, but it is NOT love at first sight. Later when they are older, they slowly start to fall in love, but something, or rather someone is trying to keep them apart. Who is it and why has Serenity gone missing? Based on the cartoon movie, The Swan Princess!
I hope to write Chapter 3: They Meet, but I am not sure when I will get it out. It turns out that The Moon Princess is a popular story, so I'll try my best to get it out as soon as possible!

3) Sailor Moon Another Story
The Sailor Senshi are facing their toughest fight yet. An evil force from the future of Tokyo, Crystal Tokyo, is trying to change Destiny. In aneffort to change Destiny, she brought back all the Senshi's old enemies...Beryl...Diamond and the NegaMoon Family...the Heart Snatchers...Fiore...Why is this happening and can the Sailor Senshi stop it before it is too late? Based on the video game, Sailor Moon Another Story RPG!
I hope to get Chapter 2: Her Destiny written and posted. This story is going basicly by the video game, so bear with me on this one. Lots of different things are being put in it, so that it is NOT the video game put to paper.

4) Sex, Lies, and Videotape
Serena and Darien are very happy together and have a secret that no one, not even the Senshi, know. They are married, but only as Sailor Moon and Tuxedo Mask, but still married! As the 30th century draws near, Sailor Moon and the Senshi have started talking to the World Leaders about support and help to protect the world before they automatically get the help in the 30th century. As all of this is happening, Serena and Darien have a happy and romantic weekend planned. But when a reporter out to hurt the Senshi catches Sailor Moon and Darien in an passionate embrace, it is up to the Senshi, Serena, and Darien to sort out the mess before the World Peace talks end very badly and Sailor Moon is thrown in jail for adultry! Based on the episode, Sex, Lies, and Videotape, from the series, Lois and Clark: The New Adventures of Superman!
Sex, Lies, and Videotape has turned into a popular story and I couldn't be happier! Chapter 2: The Senshi meet the Leaders will be up before the year ends!

5) Reflection
Usagi has tried everything to figure out who she really is. Is she Sailor Moon? Princess Serenity? Or just plain Usagi? She lost Mamoru, the love of her life, to Queen Beryl and he became Prince Endymion, Crown Prince of the NegaVerse. The Senshi are spending more and more time trying to protect her, as they are destinied to do, than trying to be her friends. Why is her life so...boring and lifeless? Is she doomed to live the Silver Millennium fate over again? Well, she just may just get her answers when she goes out one night to stare at her reflection in the lake and Prince Endymion just happens to be there...Song is Reflection, sung by Christina Aguilera from the cartoon movie, Mulan!
This was just going to be a one-parter, but one of my reviewers (Thank you for your kind words) has asked for another chapter or another story to go with Reflection!

6) Can't Fight the Moonlight
Peace has come to the Earth after the final battle with Beryl. Mamoru is back at college, Minako is Sailor V again, Rei is strictly in priestess mood again, Ami is back to being the lonely bookworm, Makoto is back to being to lonely girl who gets into fights, and Usagi watches all this from afar.She alone has her memories and thanks to her final wish after the battle, the Senshi and Mamoru have their normal lives back. Now she is fighting alone, without Tuxedo Kamen or Senshi or Luna and Artemis (because they are on the Moon investigating some strange activity) and this new enemy is getting stronger. Even with a new power up "Moon Crystal Power, Make-Up!", she is losing and losing fast.Can Usagi survive without the Senshi and her Prince, or will she fall and the Earth become part of the Doom Tree, or can the Senshi and the Prince stop fighting the Moonlight and remember their past lives in order to save their Princess? Sequel to Reflection! Song by LeAnn Rimes!
I hope this story explains alittle more on Reflection and Ireally hope all the readers like it! Chapter 1: Meeting the Girls Againwill be out soon!

Buffy the Vampire Slayer stories:

Buffy Summers thought that life couldn't get any worse after her parents split before she and her mother moved to Sunnydale. She was wrong. Sailor Moon stories:

1) Serena, Girl of the 21st Century
Serena Tsukino is a cool girl. She lives on the Space Station, Selene, and has the best friends in the world. But she is also a big fan of The Prince Band and a mischief maker. When one of her great ideas gets her landed her aunt on Earth, Serena has to find a way back to the Station to see the Prince Band concert and to save her family and friends before the owner, Diamond Wyndom, blows it up and why is his son, Demando, following her? And just why has the hottest guy in Juuban and college, Darien Shields, taken such a liking to her and is she falling in love? Based on the Disney Channel Movie, Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century!

2) The Locket of Crossroads Jr. High School
Serena and the Senshi are on a mission. Miss Haruna's boyfriend has gone missing and he was lastseen on the school grounds. They are fixing to try a Nancy Drew move and solve this mystery themselves and still fight the NegaVerse! But when Darien asks Serena out, things start to get a little hecktic and the Senshi find a very vaulable clue that could find Miss H's boyfriend and save his life!

Serena and the Senshi have graduated high school and are attending college. One day, Serena gets a call from a friend of her Aunt Marie's, who just happens to be the big time soap opera star, Alicia Terran! She heard from Marie about Serena and her friends solving the now world famous case, nicknamed The Locket of Crossroads Jr. High School, and asked Serena to come and help solve another case. This time, the case is Alicia's co-star has been getting death threats and Alicia is scared for him. Can Serena go undercover, by herself, and solve this mystery before something tradgic happens on the set of the hottest soap opera in the world, Love's Moments? Based on the computer game, Nancy Drew: Stay Tuned for Danger!
