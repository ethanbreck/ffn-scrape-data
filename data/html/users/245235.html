Author has written 12 stories for Digimon, Pokémon, Duel Masters, Sailor Moon, X-Men, and Royal Tutor/王室教師ハイネ.

I am, in fact, ALIVE! =) That's a good place to start I suppose =P

My Stories:

1) Anything pre-2007 is painful to read, so unless you are sincerely wanting to either a) have a good laugh at disjointed plot lines and Mary-Sueish characters, or b) you're terribly bored and are happy to waste several minutes of your time, I would advise you not to read!
I keep them there for personal reference. At least I can see how far I've come re: writing, and for all you budding writers, there is hope! You do improve over time (yay) haha.

2) My deepest apologies, however the fictions currently on indefinite hiatus are Far From Home, the X Men fic and Celestial Kingdom, the Sailor Moon fic.

3) I have realised that some people are just not destined to write long fics - I am shamelessly one of them. And I have learned my lesson not to stray too far from one, two or three shot stories

My sincerest thanks to those who continue to read my fiction, especially those who take the time to review.

Me:

I am a 90's kid walking my path hand in hand with the One from whom all life flows, and from whose nail-scarred limbs show me my worth despite the opinions of others, especially myself. I love nature, being in awe of the little miracles that make up our individual days and journeys.
Our world is far from perfect, but in its brokenness we see glimmers of hope and beauty shine in humanity through love, compassion, forgiveness, mercy... this is not theory, this is the reality I walk in - one person, action, and word at a time. It isn't easy, but it's always worth it =)

Alofaaga (with love)
