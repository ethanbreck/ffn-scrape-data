Author has written 17 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Weiss Kreuz, Gilmore Girls, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Naruto.

First of all, I was the first to write Tsunade dropping her youthful jutsu. Let me say that. It's mine!!! Well... maybe not, but I wish it were. Somehow, I think Kishimoto Masashi will have Tsunade do this in a future chapter of Naruto. Still waiting for that.

Clicking on my homepage link will take you to my new site where all my fanfiction is kept. Yes... even the dreaded NC17-rated ones...

Questions, comments, reviews? Email me, feedback is cool!

I'm sure people have realized that I have not been here for over a year. That entails me having abandoned all unfinished fanfics. I will not be writing sequels to the completed stories either. I've jumped 'ships since anime and I've landed comfortably in the Harry Potter fandom, although I have not written anything HP-related. Yet. I'm working out ideas and trying to rearranged all these loose sheets of paper before I commit myself to something.
