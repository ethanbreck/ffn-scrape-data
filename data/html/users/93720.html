Right... so it's been quite a few years since I've visited this place... dusts things off

My life has gotten really busy over the years, and I'm finding I don't have time to do everything I want to anymore. Still, every now and then an idea for a fanfic enters my head and sooner or later I'll put it down in text. Thus the reason my FF.net account is still active, for those just in case moments.

Anyway, so I'm 21 years old now and going through college; I just finished my first year of like... 8. I'm still very much into anime and manga, but I don't always have the money to fill that obsession. Lucky for me, my family gets cable that offers anime on demand on two different channels. :3

Yes, I still live with my parents and younger sister. There's nothing wrong with taking it easy on the financial responsibilities while one is in school, and if the parents say it's okay, then why not?

I don't really have anything else to add... so I'll just end here. Please read my stories if they interest you and leave a review! - It's always good to see how I'm being recieved as a writer.
