Updates

December 14, 2005 // Deleted Fate Has It's Ways and replaced it with Sacrosanct, which you can consider to be the revised version of the previous story. It's an altogether different plotline folks, you won't recognize it anymore. I hope that you'd still like it though. It's a retelling of Escaflowne, which I hope will be to people's liking. I've manipulated characters and their relationships with one another, and will be trying to fill in some gaps I found in the original series, plus other twists and changes here and there to make it the Van and Hitomi romance it should be. We all could use a better ending than the one in the series. winks Do email me or leave a review for any comments you might have. :D

May 15, 2005 // I am terribly sorry for having left all my stories hanging for the past three years. To tell you the truth (and a pathetic one at that), I had actually forgotten about this account and the stories I've written ever since our family purchased a new computer and the original drafts of the four stories disappeared along with the old CPU. Logging into this account after three years makes me want to continue some of the stories I had already written while some, I'll soon be deleting. You can check back on this page from time to time to check my progress on continuing these stories, or email me at my new address: starskyvenus@yahoo.com. (Please don't spam!) The websites no longer work, or if they still do, they're static, and I'm no longer updating them.

This time I'll be focusing much more on the plot. Having been through high school (I'm now on my first year at college) and a myriad of experiences, I've got more ideas for plotlines and a much better grasp of English grammar and composition writing, so hopefully the revision of the stories will render better versions of those that you've come to read and appreciate them. On a not-so-unrelated note, I'm no longer into pop/r&b music. In fact, I abhor them most of the time, with J and K-pop being the only exceptions. My musical influences include mostly rock, electronica and classical with occassional (slash exceptional) pop and R&B. One thing hasn't changed though: I still can't stomach most rap, especially gangsta rap, although I do enjoy Will Smith and Eminem once in a while.

I'm currently rereading and reevaluating - and soon, revamping -"Fate Has It's Ways", my Escaflowne fanfic, seeing that it has received the most reviews from ff.net visitors/users. I'm still studying how it can be improved and in what direction I'll be taking this story. I can't promise a specific date though, lest I break my promise. I do have this bad tendency to resist deadlines I set for myself...Also, if you've got some ideas or opinions you'd like me to know about any of my stories, please don't hesitate to email them to me at the address above. I'd appreciate all the help I can get.

Before I end, I would also like to apologize to all my pre-readers for not having sent word at all regarding the discontinuation of the story. If you're still willing (which I think is highly improbable after what I've done previously) I'll be more than happy to send you the drafts of the chapters I'll be writing/editing before I post them on ff.net and make the appropriate corrections based on your feedback.
