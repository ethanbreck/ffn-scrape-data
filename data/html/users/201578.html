About me:

In case anybody is still looking at this:

I am no longer an active writer - simply because I'm now old enough to (1) know I'm not good at it and (2) don't have enough time to both read fanfiction and write them as well while working 40hours a week.

The only reason why I'm not deleting all my old stuff (which I wrote somewhere between my 15th and 19th birthday just in case anyone is curious - wow, that's sooo long ago) even though I know all of it is preatty bad is because of nostlagia (;). It's a good way to laugh about your own younger and naive self.

I am a big yaoi fan (or Shounen-ai, depending on your understanding of the term) which is somewhat obvious in most of my fiction but definitly not in all of them.

However, I haven't written anything since 2003, so most of my fiction here is rather old and childish, and so is my style. I apologies for that.

There is also something I'd like to say about my penname:
I am not a fan of inuyasha and the name is not supposed to refere to Kikyou. I chose the name due to it's meaning (吉凶 'kikkyô' means fortune and misfortune) and had no idea a character with a similar name even existed at that time.
