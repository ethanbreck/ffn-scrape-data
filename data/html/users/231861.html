Author has written 11 stories for Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon, Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing/AC, Tenchi Muyo, and Anime X-overs.
I started my otaku obsession when I first saw anime on the sci-fi channel, around '94. I am a 23 year old male, have been a college student and am trying college again soon. I am also a big fan of old school heavy metal and harder edged new rock. I also occasionally enjoy The Doors, Pink Floyd, some classical (mainly classical guitar), and 80's new wave.
My favorite anime are Slayers and Rurouni Kenshin, though Sailor Moon and Gundam Wing are really fun to write about too. I occasionally read non-manga such as X-men (my favorite non-manga character of all time is Shadowcat, but I don't like the cartoon version of her that listens to country). When I refer to my LoveAntelope I am talking about my girlfriend who I find is integral to my writing process, as she proofreads, offers ideas, bounces ideas, and makes my heart smile. Every story I've written owes something to her, and I feel most of them are written by us in equal parts. Love to us all, Peace out.
