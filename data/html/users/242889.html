Bye, Yours Phil

So, what is there to say about me that *might* keep the rest of you interested ...

Name: Hm ... let's stick to Philomel, shall we?

Age: 19 ( God, I feel so horribly old ^.~ )

Personality: Well, it would probably take a heck of a long time to fill this one, but I can tell you that all the people I met said that I was different. You can take this as a good or a bad thing; it's up to you. Besides this I'm rather vivid and energetic, but I can also be very secluded and shy ( especially around people I don't share a good relationship with ). I'm rather stubborn and tend to stick to my principles and opinions quite strongly, however, I can also be convinced once people give valuable arguments. I've been called good hearted and open a few times before and I like to believe that, too ^^ ( sounds so nice ) Other than this, I'm a little complicated and even I have a tough time, figuring me out ( I'm still not finished o.O )

Favourite Authors: Shakespeare, Austen, Tolkien, Isau, W/ H Hohlbein, Doyle and many more ( you see, I don't have a specific genre )

Favourite Animes: The Vision of Escaflowne, Inu Yasha, Detective Conan, One Piece, Yu Gi Oh!, Ranma 1/2, DB/ Z

Favourite Mangas: W. Juliet, Fruits Basket, One Piece, Inu Yasha, Hikaru no Go, Naruto, Shamanking, Prussian Blue, Kare Kano

Why do I write fanfiction? I started writing fanfiction about six years ago, simply for the fun of doing it. However, I've been writing for fifteen years now.

Why Inu Yasha? I've always loved Inu Yasha. Rumiko Takahashi did an amazing job on creating this manga. The idea of a love triangle, the different characters' problems, which are all unique, but horrible and the beautifully constructed character's design have been developed so skilfully, that you can do nothing but love it ^^
I hope to see from it as much as possible, even though she tends to drag something into length sometimes ...

The Amazing Adventures of Go!Five *ON-GOING* *FIRST CHAPTER IS UP* [ AU [ Humour/General ] A group of adolescent, aspiring detectives, chosen to rid the world of all evil, makes their way to fight off all the obstacles put in their way ... as if school, puberty and parents aren't enough problems to deal with already!

I loved these 'kids detective stories' ( some group of children solving mysteries in all kinds of places ), when I was young. So, I thought why not 'using' Inu Yasha & Co. for this ^.~ ( Poor Inu ^^ ). These is not 'novel-like' like IAON and D-V. It is more like a 'series'. There are going to be different 'cases', which should be somewhere between five and ten chapters big. They don't necessarily need to be connected and all. So, there is plenty for me to experiment with and I already have a few ideas in store ^^

Case I - Medieval Pandemonium or How Go!Five came about I got the idea for this case by wandering about a REAL medieval fair. It was so much fun, you wouldn't believe it ^^ It's not exactly like the one I present in TAAoG!F, but the two are very much alike. Since this is also their first case, it is more like an introduction. They haven't formed the group yet, but will do so at the end ( of Case I, not of the whole story ^.~ ).
By the way - how did I come up with that name?! I really don't know. 'Five' - obviously, because there are five members in the group and 'Go', well, you can guess why ^.~ There is also a little numerology enigma hidden in it and I'm going to spill it at the beginning of Case II.
