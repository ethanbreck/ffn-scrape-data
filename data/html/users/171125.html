Hi, uh, basically I'm a frustrated, aspiring writer. I take my writing very seriously, so any and all comments, good or bad, are welcomed and appreciated.

For the first three years of my college career I was a creative writing major (yes, you can actually major in that) with a minor in medieval history. Basically that means that I took a lot of English classes and participated in a lot of writing workshops. But eventually, I realized that I actually did want to be employable some day, so I changed universities and majors. I ended up graduating with a B.A. in Journalism.

I currently work in a government research library.

I have four dogs (all Newfoundlands) and two cats.

Um, well, I guess that's really all I have to say. Thank you for your support. :D
