Hey, it's me, Damien, some of you may remember me as Haitani Masayuki, I've been thinking about using my real name for quite some time. I was hesitant to actually use it, after all, Haitani's been my pen name for the better part of four years and people keep telling me about releasing personal information on the web. I've discussed the change with some of my friends, the opinions were mixed, some said that I shouldn't change anything, since I've already created a reputation bearing the name, Haitani. Others on the other hand supported the change completely, saying, that my real name sounded a lot cooler, after weighing the opinions carefully I've made my choice and you can see the results quite clearly.

I know that I've been saying that I'll upload stuff soon, for the last two years, and I will, but just bear with me for a little longer, once I get over this breakup I'll be back online. Most likely I'll be posting something new in the next two to three weeks, maybe even earlier if things go well on my side of the story.

By the way, I've been reading a lot of Gothboy lately, some of you guys might like it... if you ignore the whole Mo & Ivy thing...

Oh, and one last thought, if you're fortunate (or desperate) enough to catch me online, my YM is grey_dystopian@yahoo.com
