Author has written 4 stories for Dragon Ball Z.
I've been into anime for about 6 years, starting with Sailor Moon. Now I've moved on to stuff like Record of Lodoss War, Slayers, DBZ, and Gundam Wing. Most of my stories focus on either Trunks or Pan from DBZ (two of my favorite characters, the others being Vejeta, Bra, and Gohan), but I have several originals. One of my trademarks, especially in my DBZ stories, is creating words in another language. I've been drawing anime for about 3 years and I'm planning to create my own web-site one of these days with my art and stories on it. My absolutely favorite kinds of stories are angst, for some reason. Therefore, all of my stories have at least SOME angst in them, some more than others. Anywho, enough about me... I have entirely too many stories to put down here, so here's what I'm gonna do. I'll put down the first chapter and you guys tell me whether you like it or not. If a lot of people like it, I'll continue! Deal? *passes out cyber candy* Thankies! ^_^
