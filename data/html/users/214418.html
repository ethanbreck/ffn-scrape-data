Hello, I’m Anna and my chief ambition in life is to be queen, not fussy about which country, so if any heads of state happen to be reading this and are looking for a successor/wife, e-mail me at the above address. I like to keep a nice, well-rounded, healthy balance to my writing and therefore my fics range from Buffy-Spike ones to Spike-Buffy ones to Buffy-Spike ones. I know, I know. You never know, I might branch out one day…but the chemistry, children, the chemistry. And SUCH a way of delivering a line the man has. Mmmm. I have many obsessions (and a Spike mouse mat, did I mention that?) including sunny days and old musicals and long black coats. Again mmmm. I write first and foremost for my own amusement but love all feedback, and I do hope you get as much pleasure out of my fics as I do writing them.
