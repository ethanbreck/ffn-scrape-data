Hello, everyone! The name's Shukumei; not really, but for the sake of the internet, it is.

As for anime/manga, I really like the romance/drama ones with all the fun comedy stuff that anime has to offer. I like to read fantasy novels mostly; if it's realistic, I'm not too interested.

Status of Fanfics as of September 25, 2015:

My interests change over time, and with it, my muse in writing for certain series. Currently, I'm obsessed with Supernatural (TV Show) with occasional dabbling in Dragon Age (Inquisition) and a little bit of focus in Mass Effect.

I'm afraid it means that my Kyou Kara Maou fics, "But I Don't Like Boys?" and "Demon-filled Valentine's Candy", will remain incomplete. I still like the series, but it doesn't hold my attention like it used to. I may one day try to hash out DfVC since it's so close to the end, but BIDLB is far too early in my original plans for me to continue writing it and make any progress. :(
