Author has written 6 stories for Lethal Weapon, A-Team, and Harry Potter.

Writing is the one thing I can see doing and enjoying, and still call a career.

I am the author of a m/m book published by Dreamspinner Press called Mortal Enemies and it is published under my pen name - J.L. Farnsworth. It is the start of a series called the Tyler Michaels Chronicles. If you're interested, I'd love for you to read it!

Dreamspinner Press

(also found on Amazon, Barnes and Noble, and many other booksellers. In both print and e-book.)

Writing allows me to escape, to be anyone I want to be, go anywhere I want to go, and to write the world from my point of view. In my stories, I can make whatever I want to happen, reality.

My hope as a writer, is to take you, the reader, on a journey so far from your everyday life that you're actually there, in the world that I have written. And I hope that maybe, just maybe, when you are done with my story, you'll take a piece of it with you, and perhaps it'll brighten your day in some way.

For that, is what a good book - or story - or fanfiction piece - is all about.

I am a wife and a mother of two beautiful girls, ages 20 and 12, and a 5-year old son. They (and their daddy)are the inspiration to all my dreams. The goal worth working toward. Without them, I am incomplete.
