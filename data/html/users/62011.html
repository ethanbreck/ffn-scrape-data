September 19 2019:

It's freaking done. Wisdom of Snow is finally done. It's been a long...long hard road. Now I need to look over and make some intended changes but it should be released in the next week or two, if not before. I simply want to make sure continuity is there and the last 10,000 words I just wrote over the last several weeks match with what I want the story to be. If anyone visits that used to read before, thank you for coming back and I hope that any new readers enjoys my work.

October 12, 2019:

So Part 1 has been extensively edited now. I've added bits that foreshadow events in part two better, changed the basis of my Magical system and re-wrote Yuki's meeting with Motoko in chapter 4 pretty extensively as well as switched the order of some parts. Hopefully it makes a bit more sense but I am an amateur at this still. I'll look forward to the feedback once everything is out. I've pushed back the release date to likely early November as I now move to editing Part 2. I'll release all new parts at once, all 8 chapters will come out the same day.

Upcoming Release Date: The Wisdom of Snow, Part 2, Chapter 3 and 4: Friday, November 1, 2019
