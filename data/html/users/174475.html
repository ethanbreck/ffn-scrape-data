If anyone actually reads this, i guess i'll tell you about myself. I'm 24 and live in Texas.

I have 2 horses, a 3 year old paint named Dakota, and a minature pony named Penelope, a cat named Midget that i found on the side of the freeway,a cat named Sherlock Holmes, and a dog named Riley, commonly known by my friends and coworkers as Goober (he's the sweetest thing in the world, just not all that smart at times, but i love him all the same!)

I love to read just about everything, but my favorite books are the Harry Potter series and the Lord of the Rings series.

I enjoy reading ff and sometimes writing it, and i'm very greatful for all the reviews that i get, thank you all so much! But enough of that! I hope if you do read my stories that you enjoy them and I am open to any suggestions you might have to make them better, i appreciate feedback in any form.

My currents wip's are:

Reestablishing Trust-my favorite right now. It is a/u after GOF and basically, Harry didn't make it out of the graveyard, an imposter did and Harry had been kept a prisoner of Voldemorts for five years while the imposter proceeded to very publicly lead the death eaters. When the imposter dies, Azkaban is raided and the real Harry is found, but not everyone believes the story about what happened. Lots of angst and flashbacks to his time as a prisoner as well as new horrors await. Read it and let me know what you think!

Too Good to be True-No secrets will be given away here! All I'll saw is that it is the sequel to If All Hope Were Lost and involves a plot to turn Harry and Ginny's children away from all they have grown up with. Read to find out more!

I try to update as often as possible, but if it has been a while, don't worry, i will get around to posting a new chapter eventually! Thanks for reading and please let me know what you think :) Tismy
