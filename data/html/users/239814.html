Author has written 52 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Silmarillion.
I have been writing for a very long time, since I was 12..and it was fan fiction (The Man From U.N.C.L.E.)! I actually have been paid for my writing (long ago, in a galaxy far far from here!) And then, the Northridge earthquake threw my complacency out the window and I moved (after being born and raised in LA) taking my adorable adoptive daughter with me to where I am now (Phoenix).
LOTR has really inspired me to write...and I have been having the best time residing in Tolkiens universe, especially with the elves..and tho Legolas is indeed a hottie, I have to go for ol Elrond...you know, the older man type, dark and mysterious and subtle.
I was thrilled to find a site like this where my fan fiction could be displayed for all, as my friends, though fond of LOTR are not quite the geek I am I guess (I am flying to LA to hear the LOTR concert at the Hollywood Bowl, will even dress up, she admits sheepishly).
So, here's hoping you folks read my stories, enjoy and don't hesitate to comment!
