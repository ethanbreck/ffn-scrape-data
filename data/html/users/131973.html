Author has written 2 stories for Sailor Moon.

Hello! My name is Linka and I have been and continue to be a devoted Sailor Moon fan. My other major interests include Arthuriana, Outlander, Sharon Kay Penman, Nancy McKenzie, Loreena Mckennitt, Tool, and many others.

I've been writing since I was 13, when English was still a pretty new language for me (I moved to the States from Romania when I was 10). Love and Desperation was my first attempt at a fanfic. Looking back on it now, the writing is terrible, but I am proud of it either way! I started writing My Lady Queen when I was 14, so it's pretty safe to say that I've been majorly slacking on the updates.

P.S. I'd like to say that I'm very upset about ff.net giving out my original name Moon Sparkle. I don't know how it happened, but it did. And I am sad and feel betrayed. Therefore, I am going to hit ya'll with some truth and say that I am the QUEEN of Moon Sparkles. : kthx
