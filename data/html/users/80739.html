Too long ago, I tried my hand at writting a story- 'Queen of Hearts' was the result, and two years ago I thought it to be a wonderful creation- and like all creators, I could not see the flaws.

Now, two years behind me, I understand why I received the critisism as well as the praise, and I am sorry to say I will no longer write 'Queen of Hearts.' Perhaps one day, when I feel my writing talents have grown to where I can salvage this project, I shall. But I cannot do that now.

Thank you all for the support, hate and love.
