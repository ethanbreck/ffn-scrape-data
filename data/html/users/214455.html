Hello, this is your friendly neighbour, well, annoying git of a fanfiction writer, Naquiel.

Hiya, please, feel free to read any one of my poems, stories or theories.

Okay, now to avoid any confusion. I am also Lord Hexian Angelus Good God what was I smoking?.
A short while ago I switched from btinternet to tiscali and I have only just made a new 'slot' on fanfiction.net. I will be placing my stories as Lord Hexian Angelus on this site. Also I changed my pen name because I felt that it was ever-so-slightly naff.
I hope that no-one is confused by this.

I will use this place to store my fanfiction, all up-to-date! I have a good spectrum of ideas from Dragonball (Z mostly) to Ranma 1/2 to Buffy the Vampire Slayer to Naruto to Yu-gi-oh, the list goes on.

Warnings: I am a recent and adamant convert to Akane-bashing, I am passively against Buffy/Spike relationships, more vemenantly against Gohan/Videl relationships as well. Other than that I am relativly open-minded.

Also, on the topic of my pre-sent fanfics, to prevent breaking the rules of Fanfiction.net, I have altered the titles of my already posted fanfictions under the other penname. The advantage is that I may be updating these in the future.

I am very grateful to those who send constructive comments / criticisms, on my fanfiction. I am new to the world of fanfic writing and I require all the advice I can get. ^_^; However, flames will be meet with contempt and will not be appreciated in the slightest. I believe in the old adage: If you don't have something good to say, don't say anything at all.

To conclude, I hope you enjoy my fanfictions as much as I have by writing them.

Oh, and I have a few other fanfics on the drawing board, expect to see them soon. I am really sorry that I haven't updated in so long but I have been majorly into reading other people's work for a while, but I am setting myself up to write more, or at least rewrite or revise my stories.

Any flames or offensive reviews, will result in either blocking or/and complete contempt.

More chapters of Wild Sand is coming... eventually... any time now, yeah... Alright, I'm on hiatus. I admit it, I have writer's block and need to focus on school. I know lots of you want me to continue writing, and I do to, but for now it's not practical. I'm sorry to keep you guys in suspense. You don't deserve that, and I apologise. However, I do have a number of mostly-done first chapters of some stories and rewrites coming right after my hiatus is over.
