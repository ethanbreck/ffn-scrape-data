Ok, here's a quick note for all reviewers to read. Please keep this in mind when you review:
If you have come with the intent of flaming my work, I encourage you to email me. As a warning, if i get an email saying "Your stuff sucks." I shall in turn go and review your stuff telling you what a load of crap you just wrote. I'm sorry if this offends you, but that's the way I am. Also, if you are cowardly enough to review my work anonymously and leave me no email or way to contact you, I'll still hunt you down. Someone that cowardly does not deserve to live in our society, we have enough of them running around stealing our air. If someome wants to flame me, then I have no qualms about emailing them back and being rude. If you don't like the fic, then don't read it. That's all there is to it. I may be desperate for reviews, but I'm not desperate enough to take flames. You have been warned.

I try to only update one story at a time, otherwise I start loosing interest in a plot and leave stories unfinished. So if you're waiting for an update on a story, make sure I'm not working on something else first.
