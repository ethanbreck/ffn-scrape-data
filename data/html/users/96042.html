My Goodness, but haven't we grown? I do admit, it has taken me a while to update my stories... one of them I had to resort to deleting, but that's okay... that was something that I have never found it more difficult to write about. I am still a KITHfan. (Long live Buddy Cole!) I still love The Late Big Wolf on Campus, I have become a new HarryPotterFicFan, and I am still a die-hard M*A*S*Hfan. I have, however become somewhat more... shall we say, edgy. Easier to provoke... lucky readers. :-) That will eventually spill over into my writing, so watch for it, kiddies. Much love to everyone writing anything that has to do with Dave Foley.

~*Long Live Buddy Cole*~

One of them being as follows:

My name: Carla

Home State: Oregon

Likes/Dislikes: I have found myself to be a Harry Potter ficcie fan from the second I read my first fic, entitled:

Will the REAL Draco Malfoy Please Stand Up.

I admit to having an awkward attachment to the character Draco... most likely because I'm a bit of a sadist on the inside @_o. I used to be really attached to the M*A*S*Hfics, but the very first one I ever wrote, I Need A Hero, I can't for the life of me figure out how to end. And so I am somewhat miffed with that catagory...
