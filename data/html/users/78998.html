Author has written 28 stories for Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Redwall, M*A*S*H, and Billy Elliot.

Once upon a time, I wrote (or attempted to write) a lot of fanfiction. Then I barely wrote anything for years and years.

Today I write mostly Remus/Sirius fanfiction in the Harry Potter fandom, but I also write other pairings -- almost always featuring Remus Lupin. I dabble in the MASH fandom as well (but only very rarely).

I'm currently going through and editing old fics -- mostly minor edits. Harry Potter fics are getting Brit-picked a bit. Problem areas are being smoothed out. As of right now, I'm primarily posting new work to my LiveJournal, but I have started posting here again as well.

There is only one WiP fic archived at this account. It's my policy not to post a WiP until the whole fic has been drafted, so, apart from "End of an Era", if you ever do see a WiP here, I can assure you that the ending will have already been written and will be posted. I hate coming across abandoned fics and don't intend to leave any. Despite the nearly 5 year hiatus, several further chapters of "End of an Era" have been written and may eventually be posted.

A Victory in Dark Times
Love Is Such a Strong Word
End of an Era

All other fics have been edited already.
