I like to make friends but I do come across as a bit strange and intimidating sometimes. I like having fun and hanging out with people, maybe cause a bit of trouble here and there but I also know when I need to stop and think. Sadly my mouth works faster then my brain so if I accidentally offend then I am very sorry.. I’m hyper for the most part but not annoyingly so, I can be very sober and caring too. I listen to what people have to say and try help where I can. I have a very big and more then often strange/mean sense of humour which is more then likely because I’m a very blunt person xD So basically.. come talk to me and I’ll be cool with it =3 I’m dyslexic which is why my profile is riddled with errors XD
