Author has written 44 stories for West Wing, Due South, Mummy, Gilmore Girls, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Harry Potter, Monk, House, M.D., Stargate: Atlantis, Tin Man, Spaced, Big Bang Theory, Inception, and Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D..

Fanfiction is marvelous. It really is. It's also sanity-saving, because without it I would be stuck with millions of "what-if" questions, floating around in my head. As it is, I get to write them out, thus saving myself from insomnia or talking in my sleep. I mainly write humor or fluff, because I don't like making myself cry. Romance is good, too, and my favorite couples of all time are (currently, at least): Josh/Donna (West Wing), Ron/Hermione (Harry Potter), Sam/Jack (Stargate SG-1), Elizabeth/Rodney (Stargate: Atlantis), Luke/Lorelai (Gilmore Girls), Willow/Spike (Buffy), Booth/Bones (Bones), DG/Cain (Tin Man), Sheldon/Penny (the Big Bang Theory)...I think those are the main ones...

In real life, I am a full-time teacher, a part-time writer, and a quarter-time undercover Belgian spy.

Aren't I just about the most interesting person you've met today?

That's what I thought.
