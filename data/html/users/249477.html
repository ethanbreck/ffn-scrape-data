Hey there. Thanks for stumbling upon my profile. The name's Trynyti, or Tryn if you prefer.

As you can see, I don't do a whole lot of writing. Lately, my life on FF.net has been all about R&R-ing - in hopes to inspire some of the awesome storylines in my head to work their way down my arm and into my pencil/pen/keyboard/(insert other media here) through my fingertips.
(Wow, that's probably the most creative thing I've written lately!)

I'd love to do a collaboration on a story with someone. Interested? Here are some of the series I'd like to work on:
~Sailor Moon
~ Fire Emblem
~ Poke'mon
~ Final Fantasy (X or X-2)
~ Animorphs
If interested, please contact me! I'm completely serious about this. Seeing how this is my grad year, I'd love to do something.

WHAT I'M CURRENTLY WORKING ON:

1. A Poke'mon story that desperately needs a title. . . it's really cool though, and I have a lot of the later parts already written out. Let's just say it'd be my personal experience if I after got magically transported to Pallet Town! Might even be in the form of a Nuzlocke.

2. Oodles of different Fire Emblem pieces, mostly oneshots (Jill/Haar, Tethys/Ephraim, Eirika/Lyon) - this started off as a onesentence challenge using different pairings, but I quickly realized this was pointless given my tendency to use run-on sentences.

3. A onesentence challenge for the pairing of Lenne/Shuyin, for the community on LJ.

4. Power Rangers Dino Thunder songfic - Kira/Connor, unrequited Kira/Tommy

WHAT I HAVE/HAD IDEAS FOR . . . (but for some reason remains unfinished . . . grrrr!):
(read: in other words, it'll probably never get finished . . . unless someone works on it with me tee hee)
1. Spirited Away ficcy that got pretty much dusted. It was all about Haku being so miserable that eventually Granny gets so annoyed that she basically sends him off again to see Chihiro. One problem though - she's aged about 4 years (time suddenly passes uber-quickly), and doesn't remember him at all! Feel free to use this idea as your own. I scrapped it long ago.
