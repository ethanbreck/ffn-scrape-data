About Me:

Honestly, I don't write fanfiction much, but Bioware has me enthralled once more. My Hellsing stuff is probably never going to be updated. My style has changed since then and I just don't see it working out. So right now it's Mass Effect and Dragon Age and maybe some good old KOTOR when I have the time.

I am a bad reviewer these days.

I want to write Mass Effect crack pairings. Love cracktastic pairings, seriously. Willing to do a trade or two. Of course, I dare not plan too much because once I commit I'll lose interest or something.

I. Sine Qua Non- An essential element or condition

III. Esse Quam Videri- To be, rather than seem

IV. Domine, Dirige Nos- Lord, direct us. (It's the Motto of London.)

V. In Limine- On the threshold

VI.Quæ Nocent Docent- The things that hurt, teach.
