Ahem...about me...hmm...

Name: Rumer (What, you don't believe me?)
Age: I refuse to say on the basis that most people laugh loudly and say something along the lines of "No, seriously...how old are you?" People have mistaken me for a college graduate before...which is, suffice to say, pretty pathetic. ^^;
Weight: Considering I'm a girl, I won't say. We females are very self-conscious about that sort of thing. Though I will say that I'm not fat, but not anorexic either. I'm on the blessed middle ground. *grin*
Hair: Long, wavy and blackish...
Eye color: Bright aquamarine. The first thing people notice about me, actually. "Hey, did you know you have really pretty eyes?" ¬_¬

I've graduated High School! Go me! Off to college in the fall! Exciting.

Anyway, I not only write, but draw. Ooh. For a taste of my art, check out the weblink above. Like my art? Let me know. My ego could always use a boost from time to time.

If ye wish to contact me, log onto AIM and look for me. RaptorCross is my SN on AIM, as stated above. Anyway, I could always use a second (or third...or fourth...or more...) opinion. And possibly a beta reader for AR2...You never know. However, I do, and that's what's so fun about it. ^_~

Pax!
