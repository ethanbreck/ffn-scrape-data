UPDATE (June 18, 2015): Wow, it has been so long since I last updated. I've had several excuses, most of them connected to being an adult. I eventually graduated from PhD studies in 2012 but I still remain a game developer (now a studio owner) and a college professor, which takes up a lot of my time. Some of the fanfics here, however, have begun to have a life of their own in my games. So I know that I probably won't be finishing The Houshi Chronicles in written form, but if you are a gamer (or RPG fan), you will be able to experience the world I created in the War Guardian Chronicles (see senshi.ph) hack-and-slash RPG series.

I believe many of the fanfics I started here will go on that route: converted into a game. Maybe someday, some of my readers will be able to create fanfiction out of my works as well.
