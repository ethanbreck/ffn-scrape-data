Seeing as all my profile information makes me sound like a dinosaur, let's try this again.

I joined FF.net when I was 14, and have, since then gone through phases of massive activity and massive inactivity. I started writing epic, sprawling DragonBall Z fanfiction which I have sinced purged purely on an embarassment standpoint.

I finally managed to wander back and stay for Kingdom Hearts fanfiction, which has captured my imagination for a long while now, and continues to do so to this day. I have one story I hope to finish sometime between now and the apocolypse, because I haven't given up on it yet even if I haven't updated in forever.

I am a twenty four year old Fashion Design student in Ontario Canada. I am engaged to my best friend of fourteen years and partner of six and besides writing, I love photography and sewing (quite obviously)

I also love to read and I draw.
