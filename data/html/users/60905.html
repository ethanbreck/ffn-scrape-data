Author has written 4 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Suikoden, Resident Evil, and Final Fantasy XII.

Hello to anyone who has/ is taking the time to read this!

I've loved FF8 since I was 14, I'm 21 this year and and it doesn't look like I'll be over it anytime soon. I looove Squall/Rinoa pairings, eat, breathe etc hehe, and generally only read and write about them. But... after playing Suikoden III love anything and everything about Chris (mainly) and pairings with her and Borus/Percival (mainly Borus, but I don't mind Percival).

Out of the two squinoa's that I've written, only Spellcasted Amnesia is currently being updated at intervals. The Great Attractor was something I wrote during my last year at high school, during finals... so that story is indefinately being put on hold, until the day I'm really bored and got too much time on my hands. I can hardly remember the storyline that I had intended for it.

I've finally updated Breath of Starlight! Chapter 3 and 4 are up now, have a read and don't forget to review!

I've started a new fic on Resident Evil... my new obsession. And I'm very into JillChris. Funny how when I started out reading RE fanfiction that I was looking into LeonAshley, but I'm over that now, I still read them from time to time though.

Besides Final Fantasy and Suikoden - video games in general, I love reading fantasy: Raymond E. Feist, Jacqueline Carey and Juliet Marillier are foremost among my favourites. But for some reason I cannot fathom, I've been multiplying the amounts of romance material on my bookshelves lately, hmm... mainly Jude Deveraux and Judith McNaught.

I love anything and everything to do with Robotech - a life long passion. Any fellow fan feel free to drop me a line!
