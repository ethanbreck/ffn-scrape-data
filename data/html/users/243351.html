Update: As of April 25, 2011, I am an ESL Teacher with specialization in creative writing, essay writing and non-fiction writing. Hmmm.

Update: As of March 25, 2011, I am on official college graduate with a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts with a major in Creative Writing. Oh yeah!

With a new view on life and the fragility of it all, I’m hoping to take my writing even more seriously than I used to. During the time I spent away from the world of fan fiction, I’ve realized how much I really do love it and intend to stick with it. Hopefully this means no more five month gaps in between updates. Right now, I do intend to focus on writing and completing “Summer Snowflake” before taking any other projects on.

I am, primarily, an Original Character writer. Hopefully I can deliver characters that are dynamic and far from being classified as Mary Sues. For those of you who are interested, I am a Beta Reader and do accept many of the stories presented to me as long I have knowledge about the stories/movies that they are based on and the author is committed to their work. If you wish to contact me for beta reading purposes, feel free to PM me or e-mail me.
