Author has written 3 stories for Wrestling, and Harry Potter.
Prior to this day in history, I was a revered writer in the drug dealing part of this site (if there ever was one). Now that it's gone, I have become a mere shadow of my former self, my former glory. But I don't let that get me down! Since the Music section is gone, I still have the books.
I love Harry Potter. I could read HP fan fiction all day if I didn't have to go to school and work. Some people think I'm sick (perverted) when I say this, but Hermione and Snape together makes me giddy. With excitement. Yeah.

Future dreams? Become another American teen idol and sing my ass off. Okay, not really. I would rather be a writer, if not something like J.K. Rowling or Stephen King then maybe more like the lesser known authors. My goal in life is to get at least one book published. I'm easily satisfied, I know.

So if you would like to read some of my work and see if I have what it takes, please feel free. Flame me if you so desire as well. I have no problem with those.

As of this moment I'm reading the book "Dune" by Frank Herbert. Jonathan Davis said it was his favorite book, so why shouldn't it be good?

Now if you don't mind, I've got writing to do.
