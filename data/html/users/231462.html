Basic Personal Info:

Gender- Male

Age- 27

Political Affiliation- Right Leaning Moderate

General Info:

I've never been much of an artist or a writer. The closest thing I had ever come to has been the creation of several 'codex' / 'encyclopedia' / etc. I greatly enjoy fleshing out a fictional universe, creating or expanding the background. Prior to just recently I had never completed a story, I often will wind up with dozens of pages of background information but no actual story.

With that said, I'm currently working on a story for Mass Effect and I've actually got several pages written. Unfortunately it has stalled at the moment and is unlikely to be finished.

I discovered Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood. Being a huge fan of FMA, particularly the Manga, I was overjoyed to see a 'reboot' of the Anime. This time following the Manga much closer. So this is great news... except its kinda derailed my attempt to actually finish my mass effect fic... /sigh

While FMA has derailed my Mass Effect fic, it has spurred me to write and FINISH a FMA fic... so I guess in the end they balance out... equivalent exchange one could say... /cough
