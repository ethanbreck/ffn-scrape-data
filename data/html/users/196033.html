Author has written 5 stories for Phantom of the Opera, Doctor Who, and Merlin.

-Wondering if anyone will ever read this... oh well...-

Hmm, what to say... I have loved good books, series and films ever since I was little. I read or watch everything, as long as it has a good plot/script and good performances. What I especially love is the psychological aspect of everything, character and emotional analysis. Give me a story or film with palpable characters and I'm hooked. :)

What got me to fanfiction.net was the "Lord Of The Rings" trilogy, the films actually. Most of my favorite fanfiction stories are from LotR, I even beta-read for a wonderful author, Nebride, for a long time.

I have had many favourite fandoms and written a few one-shots myself.

Due to my total dislike/disappointment of/over series 5 of "Merlin", I am over this show. It's over itself anyway. My story "Moments of Transition" is not going to be continued, I have no will or heart for it now. My apologies to those who have been waiting. Thank you for your support.

Favorite words: "Be gentle with yourself. You are a child of the universe no less than the trees and the stars; you have a right to be here. And whether or not it is clear to you, no doubt the universe is unfolding as it should." Max Ehrmann

Health, Happiness and Peace!
