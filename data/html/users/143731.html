Author has written 4 stories for Card Captor Sakura, and Fairly OddParents.

I don't know how I came up with the pen-name, Alo. I don't use it on any other websites but I am fond of it ;] My love for fanfiction is powered by my ever changing interests in random shows and movies. I am tentatively thinking about writing something for the first time in what seems like a decade, but am waiting for the right motivations and influences to peak. My interests for fanfiction are not known among my many, I'm a bit eccentric in my ways outside the realsm of the interwebs but I still am able to conceal my identity of a closet fangirl surprisingly well.. .;;

The only factual evidence you will get out of me includes the following:

I have a boyfriend, going on 3 years now.

I am working towards a degree in education.

I am 20 years of age. ;D

I love reading, and books can tend to get expensive and time consuming to read when I'm trying to juggle school work, my pain in the butt part time job, and enjoying what little social time I can get. So this is my main outlet for getting my dosage of literature. If I were my own ideal author, I would definitely get a martini of influence from ﻿Chuck Palahniuk's sort of sarcastic dark humor mixed in with Sherman Alexie's light heartedness. That would be pretty bad ass, in my opinion.

I needs moar books.

Anyway, in conclusion, if and when I do decide to start writing something that is actually provacative and empathetic enough to read, it would most likely be: Doctor Who (soon as I catch up on the latest season!), Left for Dead, and Fushigi Yuugi. Or all three combined. I'm a sucker for WTF crossovers...Or I might try to finish up any of the crap that I started writing back before I even passed an eighth grade english class without almost failing. I'll keep ya posted ;]
