Just a little tid bit about me...

Name: Joy

Age:22, Libra

Location: Florida, USA

Likes: Cheese, ponies, the Sims (sometimes, it's an off again/on again obsession), Yoda from Star Wars, my computer when it's actually working, reading, drawing, and the list goes on...

Dislikes: Onions (ewwww), old people driving, my computer when it's not working, yeah...

Fav. animes: Fullmetal Alchemist, Inu Yasha, Fushigi Yugi, Princess Mononoke, Escaflowne (I prefer the series over the movie, but both are good), Trigun. There's probably more, just can't think of them at the moment.

I'm going to school for digital production. Mostly I like the whole scriptwriting side of things, ya know, coming up with the ideas. I use to be a computer animation student, but decided to switch majors. This way I know about both film and animation. I'm also learning some audio stuff. I figure this way I'll be more well rounded when I get out into the working world. I'd like to do script writing for both film and animation. I'm currently thinking about making my own independant film, just don't have much time inbetween classes and work to do much right now though.

As far as updates go, there probably won't be any in the near future. I just don't have the time. I've tried to sit down so many times and write on some of my current fics, but the time I do have to do such things is just so little. Sorry to those of you who have been reading them, I'd love to work on them but ya know how it goes. So yeah, don't expect much any time soon. The only one I'll probably work on at all is Reflection of Time. As far as Learning to Live, yeah, I'm taking it down. My apologize to those of you who like it, but I can't even remember where I was going with it now. Well, I think that's about it. Toodaloo.
