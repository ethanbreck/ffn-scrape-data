Hi everyone. It has been months since I posted anything here due to my computer's internet crashing. So while I bum off other computers to give you this update, I have some things to say:
* The works you see here are from the stone age. Most were written in the beginning of my sophomore year in highschool and a few are from my junior days. So please excuse the...umm...crappiness. *cough*

But the sad thing is, I will probably never get to post anything on here ever again. * So! I came here to apologize. :-( Red Cinnamon is gonna be gone for a while. But I'm hoping to start my own website once I get settled into a dorm, and maybe then I can regain some of my dignity! Wheee!

All right everyone, Take care!
