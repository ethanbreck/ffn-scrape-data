Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter, and Maximum Ride.

Follow me on Twitter @LilRedSpitFire for potterful tweets. It's a parody/rp Ginny Weasley account.

I love Harry Potter, Ink Heart, A Great and Terrible Beauty and His Dark Materials. though i only write Harry Potter and Maximum Ride fanfics. I love many other books as well, but will not bore you with all their titles.

if you have anymore questions about me, send me a PM. i may or may not answer, depending on the content of the question.

Falling- i'm not sure about the title yet. but anyways, it's my Maximum Ride fanfic. Max, Fang, and the flock are trying to get away from the Erasers who are trying to terminate them. or at least that's what Max thinks. There is a new experiment that the White Coats made to "replace" Max, her name is Experiment 256. She is suppose to be able to save the world, and be stronger and more deadly than either Max or Fang. And of course, how can you have a fanfic without Ari trying to kidnap Max and have her for himself. this story will contain lots of FAXNESS!

The Price of War- It's mainly in Ginny's POV. there will be H/G, and some H/R. it's rated M because of later chapters. It takes place right after the sixth book is over. But instead of following Harry, Ron, and Hermione, it follows Ginny and the terrible place she gets held captive in. Don't fret though, this will end happily. i like things that end happily, so i try to make all my stories end happily in one way or another.

The Darkness Within- My semi newer work. It changes POV. They're are some POV of the antagonist as well as Ron, Harry, Hermione, and my OC. It takes place three years after there last year. so they're 20 now. Voldemort's dead, but another powerful dark wizard comes into play, and he wants to wipe out the muggle-borns.

so, go and have fun! Don't forget to review!

I also write on Fictionpress.com, so check that out!

http://www.fictionpress.com/u/196200/ They're mainly supernatural. i have a thing for vampires and shapeshifters and the like. but i have a couple that are sci-fi, general, anti-government and young adult.

March 14, 2013- I am continuing The Price of War. I'm not continuing the Maximum Ride one. I may look back into The Darkness Within, but I'm not sure yet.
