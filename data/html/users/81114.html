If you own a hardcover copy of HP7, you may get the next reference. If not... you'll have to wait!

"Does it make a difference, being muggle born?" "No. It doesn't make any difference."

Now, who said that, I wonder?

Ohoh, and I have an idea for a sci-fi story... Not sure if I should make it a fanfic or an original though. Many key elements of the sci-fi ish ness will be largely drawn off of other's works, with slight mods, but absolutely none of the characters will be the same as any book-they'll all be mine. (Ahh, liberty to actually do life changing things to them!) I'll probably write some chapters of it, then post it over at fictionpress (under the same penname, Peremptory Decisions, although the account number is not nearly as cool... 81114 is greater than 435218 in my little world, but... hee) More info will be posted over there as the time comes.

Be prepared, however. 'Scout' will be COMPLETELY, ABSOLUTELY, NOT Charms! So don't expect it. Well, except for the update delays. (I should make my excuse be that I live light years away and really I wrote Charms before I knew JK Rowling even existed, however, that would be false.)
