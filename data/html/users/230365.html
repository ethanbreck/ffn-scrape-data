Author has written 31 stories for X-Files, Ed, Office, Bones, Party Down, and Parks and Recreation.

I started writing fan fiction in 2001 as a high school student honing my fiction writing skills. So, I apologize in advance (or perhaps it is too late) for most of my earlier work. Please remember, I was 15. I keep them up here because from time to time people read them, but I am really quite embarrassed by pretty much everything I wrote before 2012. My older work is mostly Friends and X-files fic and as a grad school/college student wrote a few fics for Ed, the Office, Bones, and Parks and Rec.

Recently, I've been back writing X-files fic, having rediscovered the characters. I like to think that I "get" them in a way I didn't back then, so PLEASE if you like anything I'm writing now, DONT go back and read the archive...
