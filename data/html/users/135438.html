December 2006 as of me updating this. I don't do that or post stuff here very often :) Planning for that to change, though. A couples years ago I re-posted my Genetic Eclipse epilogue entitled "Splintered Hearts". GE is a crossover from a fanfic group called Marvel Omega from way back in 2000 (when it was known as Marvel-X)... the site's now at , the whole crossover is available there (and a lot more). We currently have over a dozen ongoing series encompassing the whole Marvel Universe, including my own Uncanny X-Men, so check them out! For my own series, I have posted several issues through #24 here, but I passed the #50 milestone last year on the main MOsite.

So I'm planning to post lots of my issues from Marvel Omega here on FF.net... hopefully broaden my reader base a little and promote the site and the other authors as well. Group fanfic isn't bad, really! We can live in peace with archivists!
