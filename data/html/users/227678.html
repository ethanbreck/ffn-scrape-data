LOOKING FOR A PATIENT BETA-READER who can handle my sporadic writing tendency. If you can be brutal, but effective, please email me. (yoda at mail2alien.com) Answers to reviews:
starphoenix23 for commenting on "The Last Chance Space Stop"--
Reader: "Is the continuation to your earlier stories about Kayel and Annbri?"
Me: Yeah. I didn't mean for it to be, but I just can't get off the subject of them. This one appears to be in the middle of the third book in the Bounty Hunter's Legacy Trilogy (of which The Nondiran Legacy is the first, and Daddy's Girl is the third) I really need to just sit down and write them one at a time, but I keep getting so many IDEAS! Glad you like 'em!"

Starknight for commenting on "They Call Me SnowWalker"
Reader: Hm, the flow was good and it was interesting to see someone doing a good job with first person, but what's wrong with dwarves? Gimli was my favorite character in the movie! They seem like villians in this fic. Also, it's strage that someone who was raised as family just turns around and kills them. I thought you did a very good job with the emotional parts and the metaphores, but I just don't like the dwarf killing. shurgs But that's just my opinion, the writing was still good.
Me: Oh good, I usually have problems with flow. This fic was an accident. I started musing about Legolas, and elves, and over analyzing dwarven culture after reading a particularly disturbing slashfic about them, and I started writing. I know it must seem weird that this elf never realized she wasn't a dwarf, then accepts it so readily. And then kills the dwarves...yeah, I wasn't quite comfortable with the continuity there either. What I was trying to show, was that these particular dwarves were villains, and that this elf was estranged from her family because of the obvious differences, for which she had been basically ostracized. Remember, Legolas says his "friend, Gimli" was a dwarf. Not all dwarves are bad. You need villains to have a story, though, and these dwarves were bad. I think it ended a little quickly, though, and I should try to fix that. Thanks for taking the time to review!
