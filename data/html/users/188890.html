Flamingteen here with a bit of unfortunate news. I have completely lost interest in Higher Ground. Oops. _ ; My stories are on quite a hiatus, if not stopped altogether. I might start to write again, but for now I am completely engrossed in reading Harry Potter fanfic... Sorry. XD;

Unless they're about Severus, they just don't interest me...
