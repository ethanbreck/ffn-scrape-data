My name is... Meh, that really doesn't matter. You can all just call me Descendent, or D for short.

I like all marvel comics, Samurai culture, mythology... the basics.

My primary works are a pair of fictions called Brotherhood Evolution and the newest, The Brotherhood Chronicles. Check them out. I hear they're both pretty good. Or people are lying to my face. Either way's fine by me.

Also, check out the Fic's "A green eyed Devil" by Bad-boy X and, "Omega Attack" by L1701E. Both of these fic's use my creation Omega in them, and quite well in fact, so I gotta give the prop's to them. Also gotta give Props to Red Witch and Todd Fan, two of my biggest supporters. Red Witchs "Misfit-verse" as well as Todd Fan's "Sidney Chronicles" have been a huge inspiration to me. And there damn fine fics, so check'em out.

Also, I found a really cool picture that a fan named Wriggle did. It features the Charicters of Brotherhood Evolution, pre X-23. HEre's the website. I highly recomend you check her and her art out. It's awsome.

Here's an acurate cronicle of my trilogy, and all the side fics tied to it, which I'm calling "Evolving Brotherhood"

Brotherhood Evolution: Part one in the trilogy. The fic that started it all. Introduces my creation Omega, as well as makes X-23 and Psylocke members of the Brotherhood.

A Very Brotherhoody Evoulutionary Christmas: The Brotherhood celbrate Christmas.

Brotherhood Evolution, Journey of the Lost: My creation Omega goes on a soul searching trip after the death of his girlfriend Psylocke. Takes place during Brotherhood Evolution.

Generation X Evolution: One of my favorite comics, Evolutionized. Ties in with Brotherhood Evolution

Brotherhood Evolution, The Early Years: Takes place during Brotherhood Evolution, chapters 4 - 5. The summer hilarity of the 'Hood.

The Brotherhood Chronicles: Part two in my Trilogy. The Brotherhood continues to grow and cause mischief.

Honeymoon Hi-Jinxs: Written with L1701e and Dragon of the Eternal Flame. Omega and Psylockes honeymoon horrors.

NYX Evolution: Comming soon. Takes place after The Brotherhood Chroinicles.

Finally, I live by a simple philosphy. I really don't now what it is, but when I figure it out I'll let you all now. Until then, ya'll have fun reading my stuff and laughing at my stolen jokes. And plots.. And everything else in life.

Peace out,
