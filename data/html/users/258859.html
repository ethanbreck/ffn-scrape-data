Konnichiwa! ^^

Oh, look! I finally put something in my bio….thing! . So now you can all share the love as you read this info you didn't know before. Or did you? *eyes people warily mumbling about stalkers* :D

Well, my name is actually *whisperwhisper* Katrina, but I prefer Aleu. Gotta keep my identity a secret. *looks around suspiciously* I'm in junior high. I honestly feel like I've been there forever and a friggin' year MAN!. But really I'm only going into 8th grade. Oy, I can't even stand 3 years of JH, I'm gonna fry in High School. ;.;

And, I'm a red head. I hate being a red head cuz people always stare and I stand out in a crowd like I'm the Grim Reaper or something. Peoples at my school got some major staring issues. Oh, oh! And I play clarinet in band-- Whoo-hoo-hoo-hoo! ^.^ Anyway, enough about me. Let's talk about my muses.

drags out her 3 main muses* Ta-da! *they all grumble and start to fall asleep* Don't they all look so excited? *smiles nervously* Let me introduce them individually. :D

pokes a guy with black hair wearing a hat* Ahem. This is Alucard. Complete moron, but my best friend. He sits on my shoulder and talks to me when all my other muses abandon me. ^^ Mostly there for the 'stuff-that-would-normally-under-any-circumstances-never-happen' moments, he likes action and is easily excited. Poor guy has to have Velcro to sit on my shoulder without falling when he thinks! *shakes head* Moving along….

bonks a blue haired guy on the head, rudely awakening him* Now this piece of work is Legato Bluesummers. Not a moron, really, just…. Overly romantic. ~.~ Any mushy moment is brought to you by: "Legato- Hopeless Romantic Without A Cause!" Unfortunately, he is barely ever around to help me actually write any romance. He's a terrible womanizer and he's always traipsing around. Another extremely odd muse. Last but not least….

smiles triumphantly, pointing to her muse Misaki Suzuharu* This beautiful little number is Misaki Suzuharu. -Misaki: Wouldn't they know that cuz you just said "her muse Misaki Suzuharu"?-
Yes, yes… I see it now. Hrm…. Anyway, she works on all the intelligent parts of the story. She may not look it, but by golly she's got some brains. Actually, wouldn't you find it interesting to know she is the weirdest one of all? She was almost nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize. She a friggin' non-visible -Misaki: That's Invisible!- *glares* invisible muse. I mean, c'mon. *sighs* Well, that's enough of that I think. Next thing….

Ahem. Moving along…. I write for anime 99.9999999 *10 minutes later* 9999 percent of the time and I have some that I should be posting soon. I also have works in progress for some original fics to put on FP.net, and for some RP videogames. *clutches Kuja and Vincent plushies possessively* Of course, if the urge to write another type of fan-fic imposes itself upon me, I'll surely put it up. ^.^
