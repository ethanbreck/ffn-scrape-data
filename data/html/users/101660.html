I'm a Canadian, currently residing in Tennessee, writing from the giant hutch of plotbunnies in my basement. A little gen, some het, and a whole lot of slash dominate that hutch. I heart Harry Potter, specifically Harry/Draco, Sirius/Remus, and Ginny/Tonks currently, but you never know what you'll find down there. I write mostly one-shots, but am working on a slightly longer fic to be published once it's completed and polished.

I've been on ff.net for at least eight years now, and my work has gradually evolved. Please, don't judge me by my 12-year-old self's writing skills. ;) Intelligent, well-thought-out constructive criticism makes you my favourite person. I also take suggestions & requests for stories, although I can't promise they'll be any good.
