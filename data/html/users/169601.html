Someone who writes a lot of fanfic, but rarely takes it to this level. My passions lie with Phantom of the Opera, Jane Austen, Harry Potter (Severus Snape rules!), Vampire Hunter D, Inuyasha, Miyazaki, Legend of Zelda, Jak and Daxter/Jak II, Final Fantasy 8 (Squall is my muse), Lord of the Rings, Broadway in almost any shape or form...Alan Rickman, Colin Firth, Anthony Warlow, Michael Crawford...I love to sing and act, whenever I get the chance, and to write, although I'm terribly nervous about letting people read my writings, so please be kind if you review my work! Hello and love to all my friends. Love you all!

My favourite kinds of fanfictions to read are romance, and I, of course, have my favourite pairings. Some are up for debate, but some just were not meant to be toyed with! I listed them (and categorized them ) below.
