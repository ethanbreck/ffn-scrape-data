I wrote this back in 2004 and it's still the same.

Hello, allow me to introduce myself. I'm a geek... or so a friend says. I'm called a geek 'cause apparently I can't just watch a television program, say like, Xena, Star Trek Voyager, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, All My Children, Crossing Jordan, and now The L Word, without getting into the show. I have to find websites, read fiction, then... OMG... start writing. So I'm a geek. I'm in great company.

2013: Of course I need to add the following shows/movies I've written for since then.

Let's see there's Hex, Lost, Lost Girl, Law and Order: Criminal Intent, NCIS, Criminal Minds, The Devil Wears Prada - which I haven't seen, nor have I read the book. I just read LOTS of fan fic. Ugly Betty, Anna and the King - Movie with Jodie Foster and Chow Yun Fat - excellent film and Yentl - also another movie. Directed and staring Barbara Steissand, Amy Irving and Mandy Patkin. I also added The Walking Dead to the list two years ago.

I think that's it...

ETA: As of September 4th, 2015 I can now add "Rizzoli and Isles," to that list. However, I do believe the story I've written will be the only entry in that genre.
