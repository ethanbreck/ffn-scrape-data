Almost Infamous

Publicly Known As: Samantha Bridges

Also Known As: Kelly, Kells, Her Sociopathic Majesty, Frigging Redheaded &!

Age: 28-ish

Location: That wretched Den of Villany known as... Colorado

Hobbies: Writing (obviously), webdesign, baking, reading, photography, autocross

Fanfiction I Write: Primarily my efforts can be found in the Lecter-esque communities (Hannibal is a hobby). Other dabbles include Boondock Saints, Nash Bridges, and most recently- House, MD. Generally I aim for the drama, criminal, and angsty. I have no idea where the damned fluffy House stuff is coming from.

Pet Peeve: People who are rude, ignorant, stupid, and inconsiderate. There are times when I am tempted. Fortunately for the world, I am only violent to spiders and in my writing. Oh, and bad fic- the kind that people write w/o realizing it sucks gopher nuts and they get tons of reviews on it. Why, people, why?! And slash... I have nothing against it, really, and I like some very appropriate well written slash, but just because something contains two guys does not mean they have to do each other! And fangirls/fanboys...

(wow... I sound like a crotchety old woman today, don't I?)

Why are you almost infamous? Because. Actually, I happened to write a fic that involved a novel, and now notorious, use of a cheesegrater. Between that and springy heads, I've been branded for life. That, and a notorious killing off of an OC that I've never been forgiven for.
