About Me:

PLEASE READ!!!!!!

I am currently just a reader. My muse long ago left me. Or maybe it's that I simply do not have the time to write anymore. Someday, I wish to write again, but I doubt it will be on any of my old stories, sadly.

Most of my stories are being removed- anything unfinished is going away, simply because as a reader, I hate finding stories unfinished from so long ago.

If any remaining stories lead to a dead website, I apologize- I used to host my stories off FF for anything not fit for FF.net, but long ago ceased hosting them.

I'm a 40-something mother of 3, all of whom are currently eating me out of house and home.

My youngest has Autism, and I spend my spare time leading her Girl Scout troop, and volunteering as a coach on her Special Olympics swim team.
