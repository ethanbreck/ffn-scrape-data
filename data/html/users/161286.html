Jack Ichijouji is a writer who can best be defined by the Google game whose name I can't recall, but for the sake of finishing this sentence, we will refer to it as "Name is." For those of you who don't know how to play, it's simple. Go to Google, type in "Your Name is" in quotes, then see what happens.

Jack is a ribald, picaresque trip through an 1840s New Orleans saturated with sex, drugs, death, and corruption.

Jack is a pet therapy dog, he visits nursing homes and hospice, he makes everyone he meets happier.

Jack is 'best of all time'.

Jack is the best at what he does.

Jack is clean and free of dirt.

He's also a writer, but all that stuff just makes me look fantastic.
