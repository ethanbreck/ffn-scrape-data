Hey ya'll! Not too much to say about me . . .nope. I got nada. Below you see the fanfics. Read and review, feel free to criticize, but remember this is a hate-free zone, ladies and gents. So, by all means, be critical, but don't send me something that says, "You bleepity bleep! Your story bleeping sucks!" Thanks!

The link up above goes to my livejournal which, being only newly started, has practically nothing on it. Ah, well.

In one month's time (July 2, 2007) I will be changing my penname to safire_griffon. Just want to give plenty of warning to those who may want to revisit fics...

Works:

For Light Without Darkness Is Nothing: This one is my baby. The first multi-chapter fic that I've ever completed. Started plenty before, but never finished 'em, eventually deleted them. I think I deserve a commemorative plaque.

Legend: I'm going to try to update twice a month. At the moment, it's just started, but it should be about 25 chapters long by the end.

Intimacy: The oneshot that wouldn't end! XD Seriously, I only meant to write this one 353 just to see if I could do it, since I loved reading them so much. But it has now grown to include two other stories; "Fooled Me," is a prequel, and "Aftershocks" is a sequel, leaving "Intimacy" smack dab in the middle. Nevermind that the whole reason "Intimacy" was a oneshot was that I didn't want to have to elaborate on "befores" and "afters." Ah, well. :)

Fire and Elemental: These two stories can be read alone (since "Fire" does not have any yaoi or shonen-ai content) but they are meant to be read together. It just makes "Elemental" pack a much larger punch if you read "Fire" first.

All other oneshots do stand alone. Really. They do. Seriously.
