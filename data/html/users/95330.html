Author has written 21 stories for Dark Angel, Star Wars, Crossing Jordan, Stargate: SG-1, Smallville, Chuck, House, M.D., Avengers, Spider-Man, and Avengers.

My current fan fiction obsession seems to be the MCU particularly Spiderman and I really enjoy the Spider mom and son between Natasha and Peter. I also like the interaction between Carol Danvers whos (made up name) is Captain Marvel and Peter.

There is only one thing in life worse than being talked about, and that is not being talked about. Oscar Wilde
I thought I was past that, until I started writing again after a several year hiatus now, I find myself seeing if others have read my story, have they reviewed... I really should stop that. do I like when people review... hell ya, but its not the only reason I do it.

I love to read, and write, cause duh otherwise wouldn't really need a profile on here.

I love TV shows, Movies, books both graphic novel (comics for those that don’t know) and regular hard and paperback, and Music (soothes the savage beast) anything that lets the hyperactive portion of my brain that most normal people call their imagination run buck wild, that coupled with my thinking that’s part science part philosophical (which if you think about it boiled down are really the same thing, they both ask ‘Why’) makes for some wild, weird, sometimes even good stories…

House and given my hidden romantic side Huddy (House and Cuddy) Plus I think I might be just a smidge in love with Lisa Edelstein

I have a couple of stories the Crossing Jordan’s come to mind that are finished but not to my liking. I plan to one day go back and clean them up like I want to and re-publish them. But for now they are on hold

When I write a story, I try not to but I can’t help but make one character usually the main character a little like me…or rather I put bits of myself in them. I write them as I think they are thinking, usually comes off as a little OOC but I say that unless your talking about Star Wars or another story that has telepathic people or beings you don’t know what they are thinking, and like most people I know you act differently around people as you do when you are alone and your thought process are different. Also, I always get a kick out of reading reviews and forums like for example I was reading a forum for the show House I read one thing one writer saw one thing the other saw another, I know this is because we all have preconceived notions in us, it is because of things like: what we believe, how we where raised, even something like hearing one word and it means something else. But I digress take 5 people tell them something and they could and most likely would hear 5 different things which is why we have mottos like ‘Can’t please everyone, all the time’

I love reviews both Good and Bad (but i prefer you have a reason for it being bad... otherwise I can't grow as a Writer) I also promish to start reviewing more stories. I have read sooo many stories I really love but never took the time to let the Author know.
