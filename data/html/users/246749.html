Well Im a girl who lives in small..ity bity town America. I mean really small..like 'cant see your neibors' small. Trust me..it explains alot.

In case its not apparent enough in my author notes, im a bit insane. So, basically, try not to judge me,or my stories, on them (they tend to be lengthy accounts of unimportant events in my life).

Usually my stores are Lord of the Rings of every kind (I avoid slash, but occasionally write it...tastefully. Sometimes I'll get a Harry Potter in there, but not always. One thing you may notice is that I'm always re-writeing and adding/takeing down stories. Im a perfectionist, and sometimes my own work disgusts me so much that I wont leave it up for long. The only stuff that stays a while is stuff I've re-written to death or stuff thats still in the works.

Reviews..ok..im not one of those people who go 'oh reveiw review so it makes my story look better!' personally..i refuse to reveiw stories like that. I usually only ask for reviews on stories that I dont really like yet and am looking for some help. So if you see me asking for a review..help a perfectionist out!
One last comment..I, personally, love to write song fics, they are so much fun...weird, twisted plot bunnies are fun too. If ya would like to request a song or want to challenge me with a plot bunny, let me know, and i'll give it a shot.

wow..that was a babble and a half alrighty..signing out..

~~Tinnuelenath~~
