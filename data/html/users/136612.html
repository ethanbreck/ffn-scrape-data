Words of Wisdom (or Randomness)

I just want to thank everyone whose reviewed any of my stories. You all truly enrich my life with your kind words.

To all those who have just begun writing, I hope it's not too arrogant to say I've been where you are and it can be frustrating. Keep at it. You'll find your voice, just like I found mine (just look at A Summer Only for Me and then Rain, big difference). I grew up on this site and I know the cycle. Bad writers become okay writers, and okay writers become good writers, and good writers become great writers. With age comes practice and you will be amazed how well you can write in two, three, even four years.

And don't let anyone bring you down. Fanfiction is an elite group. We're the next generations of staff writers and novelists and poets. As in all things in life, be proud, but humble. They're just jealous you have a muse.

I love you all!
