The Reasoning behind The Insanity Corner

I know I'm not the only one who's come up against road blocks when writing. But my road blocks were bouncing between having to work a lot and not having the inspiration to write when I had a little free time (not to mention no internet access). It got to the point where I was about to throw in the towel and remove myself completely from the world of fanfiction.

However, one day when I was at home looking over some of the fanfics I have stashed away (this was after I got a new job that 'allowed' more time for inspiration to strike), my mind decided to come up with a new fanfic idea. Out of that idea was born The Insanity Corner, the 'talk show' fanfic where I interview the various characters that populate the many fanfics I have stashed away. That, in turn, help jump start some ideas I had for some of the old buried fics I had up a long time ago (Saiyan Animorph Rise of the Gatekeepers being two such fanfics).

Another idea I had for The Insanity Corner came from various fanfics (mostly Naruto Harry Potter) where they mentioned a fic or two in their author notes. I took that idea and took it further than that. Instead of it being relegated to the author notes, I'm actually 'talking' about those fics in the sections between guests (except for Chapter 1, where Superman talks a little bit about some of the Smallville crossovers with Buffy the Vampire Slayer). So, if you're looking for more things to read, make sure you read The Insanity Corner to get fanfic recommendations.

So now, with pen paper in hand (along with a tablet PC access to free WiFi), I'm back in the game... hopefully to stay. Look below for a 'sneak peek' to see who'll make a stop by The Insanity Corner.
