Author has written 13 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Lord of the Rings, Dark is Rising Sequence, Chronicles of Narnia, His Dark Materials, Dead Poets Society, Pirates of the Caribbean, Gone with the Wind, Greek Mythology, and Liberty's Kids.
Am seventeen and a senior, currently residing in a suburb of Portland, OR. Erm.

I tend to be more canon-based than other ficcers I know. When I read a book, and notice something that really ought to have more time devoted to it, I write about it. Sometimes the occasional random experimental idea will pop into my head, but on the whole I gravitate toward solid canon grounding. This means that most of my fics are both set within the canon timeline and hypothetically possible.

I write a lot more fic than I will ever publish here. This is because a lot of my fic is self-masturbatory drek, to be saved on my hard drive and giggled over on late-night AIM sessions.

...That's about all. I read more often than I write, and write more often than I post. And of course I love feedback--all of it, even (gasp) constructive criticism! So don't be shy, please, even if it's just a short "I read it and liked it" (or "I read it and it was donkey shit," if it was).
