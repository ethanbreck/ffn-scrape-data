Author has written 9 stories for Smallville, American Dreams, Teen Titans, and General Hospital.
Okay, I’ve been at the fan fiction for over a year and a half now, and I thought it was time to add a little more to my profile than just the one line I had. I still like comic book related fiction on most DC characters, but as you can tell from my list of stories, I am a big fan of Smallville the TV show (Base on a DC comic character, go figure.) and now American Dreams.

I like anguish and stories with a little mystery and humor on the side, so it is no wonder that Clark Kent and JJ Pryor would be two of my favorite subjects. The fact that Fan Fiction dot net is also a great little community of people who share common interest is very appealing to me, and I have made some real good on line friends through my associating with this sight. I like how we can all band together and encourage each other and help each other along the way, so I hope to be a part of this sight for a very long time to come.

If you want to know a little more about me, you could always drop me an email, but I don’t actually like to talk about myself in such a public forum, so I hope you will all understand. If you want to know what I believe, then just read a few of my stories, and I am sure you will have a clear picture.

What I will tell you is that I am proud born again Christian with a strong commitment to my God, religion and beliefs. These will all show up in my stories, so be warned. I respect any and everyone freedom to write and believe what they choose, but know that I also hold firm to that same level a freedom in my own life. So please respect me as I respect you.

Thank you for reading.

My best wishes and God’s blessings on all who read my stories.
