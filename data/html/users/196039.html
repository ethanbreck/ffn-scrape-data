hmm, well finally, after almost a year and a half, I've decided to change my bio. I've been meaning to do it for months, but having to change my pen name becasue of ff.nets new rules was what finally tipped it (the other two Lynxes that were out there had only written one fic each and they hadn't been heard from since early '00. I could rant all day about ff.net and their rules, but I'll just say this; any new authors that try and gain prestige by copying a pen name are quickly found out and 'sorted' by the other authors, so 'intervening' with the new rules is stupid and unnessicary.) personally, I thought my last bio was rather good; it was to the point, but I was told that it wasn't very /inviting/. anyway, whilst I detest people that put down everything including what they had for breakfast in their bios, obviously you're only reading this to know a bit about me, so here goes. age changes every year, but I was born in '83, so that should give you an idea. I'm a fanatic fan of Transformers, particularly G1. if I were an insecticon I'd be Kickback, and if I were a Constructicon I'd be Hook. this just about sums me up; I'm the meticulous one that no one knows anything about. I've also been liked to Shockwave by my friends, although I don' know if I'm 'that' calculating :p I'm currently at University studying Mechatronics (robotic engineering in laymans terms). I love all things robotic, especially the 'reverse Metropolis' aspect where the robots are the oppressed (everyone should be forced to watch Armitage III). I'm a fairly avid anime fan. I'm not particular about what I watch so long as it's anime, but I would prefer it if our fledgling club would show more mecha :p whenever I get the chance I'm on IM (Yahoo). if you're looking for a chat, drop by some time. anyway, that should do my bio for another 16 months at least. Lynx. older bio: what, someone actually wants to know something about me??? well, other than that I'm a 19yr old uni student from Australia who loves fanfiction (Transformers, Beast wars) then there isn't much to say.
