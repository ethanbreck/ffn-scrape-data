Ok. I don't know why this happens to me but, I'll get an idea for a fic but I won't get the plot. So I have decided to hold off my fics until I can come up with a plot for them. I might even rewrite them. I don't know. Hopefully I'll get a plot to one of my fics soon. The ones I have taken down may come up again. I don't know, it all depends on if I get a plot for them. I'm just not a very good writer. Of course after looking at the ones I have finished, it looks like I'm only good at writing short stuff. Oh well.

FINISHED WORKS:

The Armor that the Ancient Built: Thought up this one while I was in the bathroom one day.

The Gundam that Dr. J Built: Thought up this one after the other one.

WORKS IN PROGRESS:

Into Organization 13: This about me getting kidnapped by Org. 13. That's pretty much it. But surprise, surprise, I actually have a plot for this one. So I'm happy.

Humanimals: Taken down b/c I just could never come up with a plot. I thought of this fic a while ago and wanted to make so that the SM characters had animal qualities and all Humanimals were slaves to regular humans. And the SM characters would be slaves to the GW characters but I just couldn't come up with a plot for this story so I just decided to take it down.

Wishing: On hiatus until further notice. This story was going to be about the reader. I was not going to give any specifics so the reader could really feel like they were the main character. And this story was about the characters life with all the pilots. Each pilot was going to have their own story, but again, couldn't come up with a plot. :sighs:

Ink and Paint:Taken down b/c Ijust could nevercome up with a plot. This was about the SMpeople being "real" and the GW people being the "cartoons" and they would interact with each other and even fall in love. No plot came from this so I finally took it down.
