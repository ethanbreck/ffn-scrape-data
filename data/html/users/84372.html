Author has written 7 stories for Harry Potter, and Hey Arnold.

As someone who has been apart of fanfiction over seven years, I would just like to say that the majority of fanfiction on this page is NOT worth reading. I started writing as Sunkist is seventh grade - angsty, dramatic, painfully insecure and naive about a great deal of life. I look back on the majority of these unable to get past the first few paragraphs of dialogue - however - they will remain on here as I rewrite them. Young and inexperienced as I was, some of the plots deem worthy of being rewritten. As the stories re-emerge, told by my adult self, these relics will be deleted and hopefully never seen again. :) The story I'm working on now is a fairly large one. One that has recently been consuming hours and days of my life with revision and research. That being stated, "Number My Days" is my new piece, one that I'm considerably proud of thus far.

As for me, I love all things art, music and literary. I have two Labrador retrievers that keep me in shape and an unhealthy love for coffee and wine. I laugh more than most, usually at the expense of myself and try not to take anything too seriously. I hope you enjoy what you stumble across here and forgive the earlier, younger works. We all have to grow up a little. :) Love, me.
