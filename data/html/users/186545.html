There was a time that I had some fanfics posted on this here site, looking back they were all horrible pieces of trash written before I started highschool.

Now that I'm studying at University, have matured somewhat (only somewhat) and have read fanfiction for over 10 years do I feel like I want to properly get into it. All my stories go through a planning phase, some setup and I want to complete a story and put it through a Beta or two before I start posting.

Fanfiction in the works.

Kim Possible: The Scars Stories

Three stories centered on three different times in the Kim Possible timeline all with one basic principle: Kim and Ron don't go through their adventures uninjured, in fact they just might get hurt a lot. This series deals with their wounds, pain and how shared scars create a bond that can't be broken. The stories are a mix of sad and happy moments with a significant focus on different elements of Hurt/Comfort.

- These Scars We Bear

Planned for 3 chapters, currently in the writing.

- The Scars That Make Us (But Do Not Break Us)

Planned for 3-5 chapters, currently being outlined.

- Made of Scars

Planned for 3-5 chapter, currently being outlined.

Beta will soon-ish be needed.

Harry Potter: Martyrdom

A story of perspectives in a world where things go differently. Explaining things would spoil too much.

Planned for 5-7 chapters, currently being outlined.

Anyone out there that still remembers any of my earlier amateurish garbage, know that it has found it's proper place in the trash.
