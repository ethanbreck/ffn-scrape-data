Author has written 10 stories for Legend of Zelda, Tales of Phantasia, Mario, Earthbound, Sonic the Hedgehog, and Animorphs.
I am 17 years old and more or less retired from writing. I still write occasionally (My most recent fic, "Sorrow," is hot off the presses.) However, I've found that I have a problem when I write, which is I'll lose sight of where it's going and eventually give up (case in point: Star Bright, The Parents of Dhaos, Hotel Mobotropolis, and basically any fic that isn't a one-shot deal).

BTW, if you're interested in adopting any of these dead fics and continuing my work, then feel free to contact me.

Anyway, I am an avid gamer (and perv, though you wouldn't tell by talking with me ^^") and a major bookworm as well. I am also a bit of a recluse, and am VERY annoyed by advertisements and spam, and am rather cynical and skeptical about things that seem out in left field.

My tastes towards humor goes towards the more nonsensical and impractical (such as 8-Bit Theatre, The Demented Cartoon Movie) and I'm also a fan of improv (like Whose Line Is It Anyway?).

Well, I've gone and layed myself out like a book for you to read. Happy now? =P
