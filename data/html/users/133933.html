A very super special thanks to everyone who's taken the time to read and review my story Never Think. I appreciate all your kind words! And believe me, I read every single review.

About Me:

I'm sorry, my life really isn't that interesting! I'm fairly young, I'm a writer, and surprisingly for some of you, I'm a mom. What little time I have left when not with my son is spent on writing.

I'm in the process of piecing together a book. I don't know if it'll get published when it's done... if it does, you'll be the first to know. Hah. I really should spend more time on my own stuff, but it's much more fun with Twilight and Harry Potter. I live in a fantasy world some days.

I love music, and use it as a base of inspiration for whatever it is I'm writing. I can put a mood or a thought or words from my mouth or a character's, based on the music I'm listening to. As you can probably tell with Never Think, Gutterflower, and Garbage.

I will not be able to finish Garbage (though I'm sure not many of you care, it's been so long), and a few others. I don't think I'll do much more for Long Road To Ruin, I just don't have the inspiration anymore. Sorry about that.

I have a tendancy to start things and never finish them. It's a curse, I tell you.

Anyway.

I'm head over heels in love with Edward and Emmett Cullen. I'm a TwilightMom (scary!!) and I'm equally obsessed with Harry Potter. I live each day for the magic.
