Author has written 25 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Harry Potter, Firefly, Smallville, Spider-Man, Superman, Batman, Ultimate Marvel, and Batman Begins/Dark Knight.

Welcome to Summersfan's stuff.

When I was in college, I wrote several novel-length fan-fictions. It was a good hobby for college. Kept me sane.

Then I graduated and joined the real world. I turned my eyes towards writing real fiction. I got a job. I started volunteering to save lives. In short, I had no time left for it.

But still, the characters called out to me.

Enjoy my old stuff, first and foremost. Because new stuff? Comes at a glacial pace. I'll update when I can, where I can, if I can. I've tried to clearly label the ones that are done. I especially like Deconstructing Hell and The Summer. Both of them are Spike-centric. I'm a bit of a fan. (not much of a shipper, though) Long, interesting character arcs are the very best.

You'll see that play out through most of the stuff I've written here.
