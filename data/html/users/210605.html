Greetings, fanfictioners.

Wow. It's kind of a delight to return to fan fiction after a 7 year hiatus and find that the term "mary sue" still means the same thing, and Skittery and Snitch, Spot and Race, and Mush and Blink are still together.

Anyway, I'm back, upon realizing that writing had become a chore and lost a lot of the fun that I had writing fanfiction that eventually led me to major in fiction writing at Columbia College in Chicago. I've been graduated for a couple of years now, married a sailor, and quit my job to write, and I find that I can't effing do it. I sit here and stare at blank screens for hours a day and torture myself with guilt. So here is my return to writing for pleasure. Which is what it should ALWAYS be.

Peoples.

Glimmer Conlon O'Leary is my cousin and best friend, she's also an exceptional writer, you should check her out. And she named her son (my godson!) Conlon. So. You know she's dedicated.

I don't know many other fanfiction writers who are still active, but I am excited about getting back into the community! Feel free to message me anytime!

Writings.

I will be writing mainly Newsies and Harry Potter fanfiction: they are the only two universes I know quite well enough to do it properly.I welcome all feedback, good, bad, and ugly. I realize that sometimes I post prematurely and have niggling little errors, feel free to throw them in my face. My favorite thing in reviews is QUESTIONS! Such as, "Why did Mush single out Skittery at the party?" or "How did Skittery get his car back? I thought they left it at the police station." These help me see things I've missed. For god sakes, if you see a plot hole, bring it to my attention so no one trips and hurts them selves okay? Liability, you know.

I am also considering setting up fiction press to post original things. If you like me, and I do, I'll keep you posted.

Contact.

Check me out on Youtube.
