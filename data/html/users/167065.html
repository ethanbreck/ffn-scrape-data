Welcome to my bio page! I am a female anime fan from the US, and probably one of the older ones in CCS fandom. I'm also a TV addict, and I used to read real books until I got hooked on manga. My goal still is to be MSTied. Please enjoy my stories and leave feedback, positive or negative, because that's how I learn to be a better writer.

Mostly I write for Card Captor Sakura, but lately I've started writing for Tales of the Abyss. Until I'm more comfortable writing for ToA, most of my fics will be posted to LJ instead of here.

I'm always up for a good discussion about characters, fics, and fandom, so please feel free to drop me a review, PM, email, or swing by my LJ (mellowcandle.livejournal.com) if you have anything you'd like to talk about.

I am not currently working on any of my CCS multi-chaptered stories. I'm sorry to leave them unfinished, but please do not expect or ask for updates.

If you're a fan/author of CCS fics, please check out tsukimineshrine.livejournal.com.

More of my work can now be found at lchanfics.livejournal.com.

An explicit version of the Wedding Night scene from Chapter 17 of How Far can be found at lchanfics.livejournal.com/34427.html.
