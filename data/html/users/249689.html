Author has written 1 story for X-Men.

Yes, I'm alive.

Anyone who was a fan of the X-Calibre stories, take heart. It's in revisions right now, and the ENTIRE TRILOGY will be posted once I've got it finished.

Please note, though, that I have no definitive date on when this will be. I'm in the middle of several aspects of the revision which I hope you'll agree are worth the wait:

1. Historiograpical research to create as realistic a time line as is possible;

2. Broader character development;

3. Richer descriptions of scenes and settings;

4. "Fill-in" chapters to balance out all the narration Miriam gives for the first half-dozen or so chapters.

In the meantime, I've left Part One up in case anyone wants to re-read it. Part Two was taken down because I wrote myself into a corner and I don't expect any of what was published out of it to survive the revision anyhow.

I thank you all in advance for your understanding and patience, and offer many times over my thanks for those who offered their honest criticisms of the draft currently available.

If anyone would like to drop me a line, please do. In the meantime, rock on!

Sincerely,
