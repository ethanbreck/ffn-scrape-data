Whew! This thing needed an update!

I've been a member on this site for so long it's hard to imagine how old all my favorite stories are! (Really go look at some of those dates!) Around 2005 I lost most of my interest in fanfiction and only recently has it captured my attention again. My interests range far and wide, but when it comes to fanfiction Inuyasha will always be my favorite. I also have a serious fondness for Digimon, Yu Yu Hakusho, Card Captor Sakura, Escaflowne, Trigun, Cowboy Bebop, Case Closed, & Ruroni Kenshin. As far as anime goes. I couldn't even begin to list the books/movies I love.. we'll just say it's a ton.

I stopped writing fanfiction years ago, deleted most of my old work, and figured I was done with it all... But my gosh - those damnable plot bunnies are running a muck in my head! So you may see some new stuff pop up from me in the near future. (You may not. Depends on the determination of those plot bunnies.)

I also write random one-shots (total smut I warn you) http://www.fictionpress.com/u/99341/!

I welcome any and all messages if you want to talk to me.
