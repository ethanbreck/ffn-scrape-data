Well, time to update my profile. It was looking pretty dusty *coughs a bit*. I previously listed obsessions with BtVS and The Lost World. While I'm still a fan, my obsessions have shifted and changed over time to include Stargate: Atlantis, Law and Order: Criminal Intent, Life on Mars, Wire in the Blood, Ashes to Ashes and most recently Primeval. I'd love to write something as stories are constantly floating around in my head, but they never seem to want to finish themselves off properly. So, if I get really brave I might put pen to paper as it were and write something for Primeval. Need some more Nick/Claudia/Jenny over there! :) Meanwhile I'll enjoy trawling the depths of the fiction over here and pretend that episode 3.3 ended differently. Darn that Douglas Henshall wanting to make movies!
