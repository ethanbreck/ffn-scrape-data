Hello!

I'm Himitsu-no-Paradise! I am so blessed to have this writing talent that God has given me and I want to use it as much as possible! My goal someday is to be a novelist, but I feel fanfiction is a medium Christ has blessed me with to practice my craft, share what I love and also share Him! (which is why you'll find all of my fics end with Bible verses :D)

My current OTPs are:
Blackfrost (LokixNatasha; Marvel movieverse)
Namiku (RikuxNamine; Kingdom Hearts)
Kacchako/co (BakugouxUraraka; My Hero Academia)
Mercykill (Gabriel ReyesxAngela Ziegler (ReaperxMercy); Overwatch)

But for the most part I have and love a lot of ships but these are the ones that get the creative juices flowing.

I just want to thank all of my reviewers for their lovely, kind, excited reviews! Your words are such a beautiful blessing to me and you make all the hard work I put into these fanfictions soworthwhile! I love you all and God bless!!!!!!!!
