astral-angel was named as such by a fangirling 11-year-old, so don't hold the name against me. Seriously. I'm now 21, almost 22, and if I didn't use it for everything, I'd change it if I could. And I'm not such a fangirl. I'm also from New Zealand and no, it's not part of Australia.

I wiffle back and forth between fandoms, and I procrastinate to epic proportions, so don't expect relatively quick updates. I read books, watch t.v, listen to music, hang with friends and am past my epic teenage angst years. I play sports that don't involve me throwing things. I have a livejournal and all my fiction, graphics, icons and such are archived at my community, adherent addict. Note, some of my more smexy fics are likely to be archived there and not here.

Note, that all my Charmed, most of my WWE and early HP fics are abandoned. They will not be completed.

For those of you who are interested, my Hermione/Charlie fic 'Wrong' has been translated into French, by Bel-o-kiu-kiuni. The fic can be found here.
