Well, hmm... I guess if I'm putting my age into this profile thingy I should update it every year...

My real name is Megan, and I'm 19 now, going into my second year of university and having the time of my life. My hometown is a small town in Saskatchewan, though I do live on a farm outside it, and now my University is in a small town in New Brunswick (CANADA!) I know what you may be thinking... Farm girl from Saskatchewan goes to university in New Brunswick? WTH? But its great fun, and there's nothing wrong with exploring this great nation we live in.

My stories normally come from weird dreams I have and somehow manage to remember enough of to put them down on 'paper' but just so you know I haven't done anything with these stories for a while b/c I've gotten no inspiration... so if you want more stories from me... tell me.

LATES!

PS: heres some silliness for your enjoyment:

In the ancient war torn days of the Land of Nod, the leaders of the great realm of the fearsome archers, and their well known army the Panters, they learned to themselves a language to befuddle their enemies, which was not unlike to Krythstal and the Watery realm's slurs. 'This' was a carrot, 'that' was I's pen, 'I' was the female counter part of 'we', 'Me' being the male,(twere the rulers) together with another companion 'You', they formed the allianace known as 'Us'. Now myself and my counterpart him, were We's body gaurds, and were quite proficient, unfortunately the rebellion took hold and the Archers overthrown. Now the land has peace under the firm rule of Queen Janna's heir King Dust.
