OOOH MY GOSH!! I JUST FOUND OUT AFTER MONTHS OF IT POSTED THAT WHEN MY CHAPTERS WERE UPLOADED I HAD A TON OF MISSING WORDS!! Sentences were incompleat and made me sound like a compleat BUTHEAD!! I SWEAR WHEN I WROTE IT, IT WAS CORRECT!! I DONT KNOW WHAT HAPPENED!! I AM TRYING TO FIX IT NOW!! BEAR WITH ME!

Iwas inspired to write and complete my first story scars and fire, from a few different influences. I think the strongest inspiration came from listening to a lot of Breaking Benjamin. I took a lot of the supernatural powers of Jania from World of Warcraft. World of Warcraft is a staple in my family's life, so it felt natural to pull some of my inspiration from some of its complex characters. As far as the religious aspect of it, I was trying to show in my story, how hard it is to truly know good from evil. How difficult it can be to tell the difference. As the cold ones originally started out as a good thing, it all went bad, as soon as they too fell into sin, making what was once a soldier for good, in to a nightmare for man kind. I was inspired by movies that had a similar message. Like Constantine and Boondock saints. I know that my story wasn't the easiest to follow, but it was my first. I was very proud to finish it, I was very proud to write it. It almost didn't get finished. I had a time of uncertainty. It was because i would find other stories, by new authors, that were six chapters long with over 300 reviews and here was mine over 30 chapters and only 52. It was frustrating. My best friend told me to write it for me, what was what i originally set out to do. To write the perfect story for me in my eyes, and make Jasper the unstable hero bad boy with an attitude that i so desperately wanted to read about, but couldn't find a story that had all those elements. So yay for me. I wont be writing a sequel, but i will be pairing up with a chick, who has a hell of a lot more reviews then me, and we will be writing a story together. Ive been preparing by watching a lot of bleach and listening to more Breaking Benjamin, LOL so I'm sure ill be inspired again to write some kind of good vs evil themed story, So look for that one in the future.

I would also like to thank from the bottom of my heart all the awesome people who took the time to review. You are all so wonderful, and if it wasn't for you there was no way i would have been able to finish it.
