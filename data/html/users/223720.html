Howdy. The name's tishy. I'm a 25 year-old-female, a professional day-dreamer who's still waiting for my MIA pay checks. Current college student, I don't get much time anymore to write. I will be try to find some free time in the near future though. I enjoy many different things, at the moment, though, my thoughts seem to revolve around Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter or X-Men. Things can get pretty chaotic around here. I'll start one story, then get a different idea and have to put the first one on hold.

About my fan fiction. I'll give y'all a forewarning, most of it ends up being my dreams/fantasies pertaining to whatever tickles me at the moment. Also, some of it ends up being Mary Sueish, because I love writing myself into the stories. Either get used to it or, here's an idea, DON'T READ IT. Idiots.

Read and review at your leisure. I enjoy all kinds of reviews... except those that contain the words "eew" and "blech," as I've come to find I don't like those words very much. In other words, constructive criticism is welcomed, but don't be mean for mean's sake.

That's it. Enjoy.

UPDATE - 3/09

Hey. I'm not dead. Sorry about taking so long for updating stories and starting new ones. I'm working on things between classes. College is a little more then I bargained for and it's eating up a lot of my time. To those of you who have added my stories as favourites or left reviews recently, THANKS. I mean it, seriously. Getting the messages in my e-mail that people are still interested in my stuff really means a lot. It's what keeps me going on some of these things.

UPDATE - 5/10

Still alive! Still writing! Still in college! Still trying to find time to finish up my stories! Still loving the messages I get from you guys 'fan'ing my stuff! Thanks!
