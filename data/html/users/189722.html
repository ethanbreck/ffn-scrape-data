Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter.

Hello! My actual name is Anna Marie, as I made this account way back while I was in middle school and wasn't talented in the creativity department XD! The initials "CG" stand for "Cover Girl"- but it's not what you think! I like writing cover stories or stories that originate from movies or scenes from movies such as Moulin Rouge or Disney's Atlantis; You know, like those cover bands who play songs that have already been released by someone else XD!

I LOVE Harry Potter! My favorite characters are Remus Lupin and Sirius Black (and occasionally fall in love with Harry all over again XD). I'm into a variety of subjects, from Chronicles of Narnia to Gundam Wing and Sailor Moon crossovers XD. My favorite GW boys are Quatre and Trowa, and I will always go for my girl in blue- Sailor Mercury/Ami Mizuno XD!

So far I've only had enough time to write romance fics for Harry Potter. But even with my life now, I barely have enough time for it. Most likely I'll write maybe one or if I'm lucky two chapters until I go on hiatus for another three years, lol! I unfortunately had to take down "The Power of Love from Within" because1) it sucks, 2) I don't remember how i planned on ending the story since I started it way back in middle school and now I'm in college (yeah I know, BIG time gap XD), and 3) I tried to start re-writing it and eventually finish it, but I gave up because...well, because I felt I was never going to finish it. XD Sorry!

I only write romance stories! XD I'm a sappy hopeless romantic, and I'm proud of it!! However, I have read a whole bunch of stories that weren't of the romance genre and found them actually really incredible! LOL XD I tend to find really well written stories, if I do say so myself, so take a gander at my favorites section. You'll really like the stories, trust me. XD

UPDATE:

If you haven't already caught it from my author's notes and from the above paragraph, I started this up back in middle school, and now that I graduated college and doing bigger and greater things, I've decided to come back and try my hand in this again. Now, it's just a fun thing for me to do on the side. So please don't be angry if I take too long to update! XD

I'd like to thank from the bottom of my heart all of my reviewers and all my fellow fanfiction authors out there who have waited literally years for me to return! XD I lovers you!

Current Projects:

To Where You Are: Harry Potter fanfiction, slightly A/U, Romance, Pairing: Sirius Black/OC; Plot: Grieving over the death of her more-than-favorite Harry Potter character (and admitting to herself for being more than a little crazy for doing so), Rose Wright gets the shock of a lifetime when she finds a mysterious injured man lying in her backyard, who later claims to be her late Sirius Black! What crazy magic is this?! XD; Status: tentatively ongoing! I've re-written the first two chapters, and am currently working on the newest chapters! I don't want to update too often, but then again the point of writing a story is to finish it, right? XD Hopefully I'll finish it without putting years in between chapters, haha!

In Her Eyes: Harry Potter fanfiction, Romance/Drama/Angst, Pairing: Severus Snape/Lily Potter; Plot: This is a one-shot fic I wrote after reading the last book. I found the chapters with Snape's death, followed by his memories were just so heart-wrenching sad and yet so good, that I felt that Snape wasn't given enough justice. I found myself wondering, What were Snape's last thoughts before he died? Did he know he was going to die? What did he see exactly when he made Harry look at him during his last moments? And so, this story was born! Status: Complete! Hope you like it!
