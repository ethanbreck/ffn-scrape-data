I am not dead. Simply just busy with whatever life throws at me. But I promise you, I do have things in store.

I will be doing a revamp in the future. A couple of the stories here will be deleted and stored on a separate site but since Yahoo! deleted their GeoCities I'm not really sure I will be doing that anymore.

Hello I'm Ayuki and for the Digimon Frontier people, formerly Minamoto Chanel. Just your average student who likes to write every now and then. Lazy as hell, I find sitting around and doing nothing very entertaining. I read anything that catches my eye. Lately I realized I like crack/fanon pairings rather than canon. A few of my favorite series: Hot Gimmick, Digimon (Adventure, 02 and Frontier), Victorian Romance Emma, Full Metal Alchemist, Fruits Basket, Pokemon (Kanto and Sinnoh mostly), among many others.

I know I take a century to complete any fic I start, I'm trying to get out of that habit and I apologize if I keep you waiting too much. I don't review very often with some exceptions for others, but I do read other's fanfictions as to return the favor since we would like others to read ours as well. I am not a flamer (though I'm surprised so many people on this site don't know how to differentiate between that and constructional criticism) since there is the concept called respect which everyone deserves-- in most cases. Whatever I post is for the reader to enjoy and are fully entitled to their own opinions of which I respect and could care less whether it is good or bad. If you like it, thank you and enjoy. If you don't it's completely fine with me and thanks for taking the time to read it anyway. I am open to constructional criticism... if with proper reasoning and analysis, not mindless insult profanity just because you don't like the pairing.

I am eternally grateful to know her and if you love Yu-Gi-Oh or Yami no Matsuei, check out her stories. Also Maia's Pen, who beta-read Snow's Requiem for me. She's a flawless egoshipping writer; joy to read ANY of her stories... all I can say =)

Lastly, the reviewers who patiently wait for me to publish a new chapter and critique it to show what you think I can do to be a better writer while also taking the time to read what I write. Thank you all for your support.
