Well, well... where to begin... Let's see. Ah! I know.

I'm your average anime/Harry Potter/ god only knows fan freak as well as scifi and old westerns. I'm talking Bonanaza, the Duke, and Clint Eastwood. I'm 100% danish, lived in the US of A, but I'm back to living in Denmark, the land of hit and run weather. Oh! And red cows, can't forget those.

A quick warning about me, I have a habit of making Mary-Sues for series (I've always lived myself into stories, crazy imagination I know) but I try my best not to make them annoying. If anything I think I'm a down right bitch towards them and often hurt them more then the original characters of a series.

I love:

I'm sorry, I know it's popular, but the series seriously makes me think of constipation adverts. Never the less, each to their own.

Book series: Harry Potter, Vampire Chronicles, Almost Night, Southern Vampire Mysteries, Aurora Teagarden, Jurassic Park, Twilight (I'm a vampire nerd people. I know it's not a challenging read, but it makes the 12 year old in me squeal), the Millenium Saga by Stieg Larson, Molly Harper etc.

Movies:Labyrinth, Lost Boys, M. Hulot's Holiday, and any horror flic that isn't Friday the 13th, or Chucky related, or so cheesy that it's funny like 'Strays'. The Saw series (though I'm forever disappoint by saw 3) Highlander, Mindhunter, Hiyaoi Miyasaki movies. Massive fan of the original Cube movie as well as Puppet Master.

Oh and just for laughs, I'm get sick from peanut butter and I'm a role player too. Can I be more of a nerd?
