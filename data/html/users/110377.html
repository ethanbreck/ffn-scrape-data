Author has written 7 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Suikoden, and Warhammer.

I've been writing since I first learned how to form letters with a pencil, fiction, fanfiction and poetry. I started posting it on the web some years before the turn of the millenium. Lately, as I am a member of the GW fanfiction writing community Imperial-Literature, I've mainly written Warhammer 40,000 fiction, and some for WFB. It is a wonderful world to explore, and I do my best to write pieces out of what you may ordinarily get to read. A few unfinished pieces of other fanfiction rests, waiting to be finished and put here. I am very happy for the reviews I have received, and I hope to receive more in the future! I enjoy playing with words and language, although English is not my first language.

I also have an account at Fictionpress.com, where one can find my non-fanfiction stuff.
