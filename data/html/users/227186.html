ok ummmmmm... what to say...

I GOT IT!!

Hi everyone!! My name's Lauren, but i go by many assorted nicknames a few include Blondie, Jessica (cause I have MAJOR blonde moments at times) Lor, H-Town, and my last name which I won't state for safety reasons. I love food...dumplings, french fries, burgers, ramen, chips, steak, chicken, (not fish though and pork is a rare thing for me to eat unless its bacon) ice cream, chocolate...so many things- except veggies. It's like I tell my parents and friends, I did not claw my way to the top of the food chain to eat vegtables...or as I so "fondly" refer to it- rabbit food. But despite my poor health choices (I'll be the first one to admit it) I manage to stay at a healthy weight. At 5'5 ...I'm happy, and in shape. I've played so many sports including basketball, gymnastics, ice skating, rock climbing, ballet, tap, track, softball, jujitsu, and now Tang So Doo. I love being active but I also just love to veg.

I love being a girly girl but when I can I'm the girl playing football or Halo with the guys. I'm comfortable in my skin and I'm girly, tomboyish, geekey, shophisticated, sloppy, rude, considerate lovable, bitchy, nice...all rolled in to one package known as me. And with all my different traits and characteristics, i make friends really easily.

My friends and family are my life. I don't know what I would do with out them. I go to college, and every half-day or day off (unless I'm sick) is spent hanging with my friends and/or family. unless of course I have to work. I was a lifeguard for the longest time, but hey, summer's over, so for the time being I'm toughing it out at a fast food joint, and in October, I work at a Halloween store.

I'm A GREAT Beta reader, but don't meet this site's qualifications because I haven't published 5 stories or 6000 words of data. But I'm still good at it. Just let me know if you need me. College english classes help a lot

LATER! --LO
