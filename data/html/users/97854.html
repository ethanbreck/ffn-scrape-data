Author has written 17 stories for Sailor Moon, Magic Knight Rayearth/魔法騎士レイアース, and Full Moon wo Sagashite.

I've spent a long time on fanfiction.net and I'm pretty sure I've never once updated this thing, but I guessed it was time for a change. I have the unfortunate pleasure of being an extremely busy high school student who barely has enough time to get in a few meals and a couple hours of sleep in between work and school let alone practice her craft. That has been making my updates scarce recently, and for that I'm supremely sorry, but I unfortunately have to force even my writing to take a backseat to my schoolwork, but now that it's summer maybe I'll have some extra time to squeeze in the last few chapters of my stories. . As for all the other stuff, it's inconsequential so long as I'm still producing half-decent, at least tolerable fanfiction right? I do write my own stuff of course, I'm currently working on four manuscripts, (yes all at the same time) which would point out my second weakness at writing. I have a lot of trouble staying on one project. I will finish them all eventually though, so don't lose heart! It just means that updates are sometimes long in coming, but I won't disappoint you, I'll always come back to them at one roadblock or another. If there's writer's block in one story I'll generally have enough luck to find a second story with a free road ready for me to travel on it. I write romance/action which all generally end happily enough, but I'm a sucker for angst, so the way that the romance achieved is very rarely easy. Oh, and most importantly, I'm totally addicted to reviews, all sorts of reviews, good, bad, ideas, questions, everything. I like getting emails too . Especially now that my email is working again, so I encourage anyone to send me mail. I'll offer to beta read/give advice, but sometimes that's as long in coming as my stories, there just isn't enough time in the day sighs. Well I think that's about all there is to my simple mind, and this is quite long enough as it is. Read and be a responsible reader and make sure to review!

Lots of love

~Umi-chan
