Hey Readers!

(August 27, 2008) Wow, yet again, another long span of time before I even remember to look at my profile and update my stories. Thanks again to everyone who is still supporting me through my extensive writer's block. If anyone has any ideas or suggestions on what direction Dude Looks Like A Lady should continue in, please, feel free to suggest!!

(June 16, 2009) Wow...I can't believe how long it's been...again! I apologize for the wait, and I also apologize for the fact that it will be an even longer wait until I update. I am in the process of revamping Dude Looks Like A Lady (and there might even be a title change) as I have over the past 2 years, gained a little more life experience and I want my writing to reflect that. The fic will probably be a little more mature, and will continue along in a similar vein (that's the plan at least) as it already is. I have mapped out a basic outline for what I want to accomplish with the fic. I can't promise more than that, as school and my job take away from the time I have to update and write. So, a hearty and most appreciative thank you to all of my readers and the people who have put up with my general lack of dedication to my readers. As most know, life just get's in the way sometimes.
