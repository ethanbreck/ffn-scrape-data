March 2008

Because updated bio's are nice things.

Life is pleasantly settling down for me, leaving me more free time to write and not worry about when things will get done.

Stranger at Redwall: currently on hiatus, quite possibly for ever. I don't know. My muse for this went for a coffee break a while go and has yet to return. If found, please send it back.

No, You Can't: See above.

Wayfaring Stranger: In progress, being worked on actively, almost done, a few chapters to go, and a sequel to follow. Please be patient with me. My muse for this one is finicky. Very.(Still picky. doesn't like my wraith muses, who refuse to give place without a fight.)IN PROGRESS

You Do Not Know: COMPLETE!!! Prequels now in progress, a one shot up, one sequel done and several more sequels to follow, one in progress. COMPLETE

A Promise Kept: A one shot sequel to "You Do Not Know"; John's thoughts afterwards about what happened and what he learned... COMPLETE

Shadow of Death: Cullough(wraith from Common Ground) needs John to understand somethings about the wraith and about him. Carson helps John get through. Friendship, no slash. COMPLETE.

A Mother's Love : The thoughts of Searlaid, Cullough's wife. Takes place About 18000 years ago, before the queens came to power. Chronicles what she was thinking as a person, a mother, a wife, and a spiritual leader during the final year or so of her life. PLEASE REVIEW

A Father's Love: In response to requests for more from Cullough's POV, I have created this parallel story to "A Mother's Love". Chronicles Cullough's thoughts during the same time period. PLEASE REVIEW!!!

Orphan No More--the continuance of my "You Do Not Know" alternate timeline/AU. Cullough was able to hide his infant daughter, Mairghread, from the wraith, and Atlantis, lots of trouble and discussions later, decide to adopt her. The problems of adopting the child of your worst enemy, both for you and the child. COMPLETE

Vengeance is Mine--Set after "Orphan No More", Part 1 of 3?. An alternate "Submersion", where Mairghread discovers the wraith queen who was responsible for the death of her family and that she was not the only one to survive...but maybe she's the only one who survived intact. IN PROGRESS

COMING SOON: Learning to Live: Part 2 of "Vengeance is Mine". Maighread's brother Gilleasbachan has been rescued from slavery, but can 10000 years of torture and hurt be undone? And can a family long seperated be reunited in this life? A story of healing. Also, a marriage! You are cordially invited to celebrate the union of Teyla Emmagen and Ronon Dex.

Also, I have created a forum for discussion about the wraith. Deò abh an Wraith. (Life of the Wraith in gaelic). Please, drop in! There is currently a discussion concerning the nature of evil and how it pertains to the Wraith. We would love to hear your thoughts, but PLEASE proof read. The discussion can't proceed if we can't understand what you are saying!
