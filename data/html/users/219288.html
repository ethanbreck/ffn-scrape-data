Author has written 8 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and D.Gray-Man.

I don't write because I WANT to... I write because I HAVE to...

I have been writing for a long long time, with a few breaks here and there. I'm very proud to be able to provide stories that I enjoy re-reading, and hopefully I provide stories that others enjoy reading as well.

I will never beg for reviews or withhold chapters on any of my stories. I do enjoy them but I write for myself just as much as I do for others. :) However I'll take the time here to thank everyone who has ever reviewed any of my stories! THANK YOU! You always bring a smile to my face.

Important Story Info:
Last Two Standing - is going through a revamp right now. The story has always been in need of some substance and a meanings as to why the plot is occurring. The old chapters will be removed since I am now putting up the new work. IF you want the old chapter, please message me. I do have them all.

Seven Heavens - I understand a religious story might be a little harder for people to pick up, but give it a chance. I promise it will be worth it :) Action starts in the second chapter. I'm not really religious, but angels fascinate me, so I'm doing some research to bring correct info into the story. Let me know what you think at my attempt.

Haven Series - A collection of stories set in a Fantasy story line. All occur in the same world, but none related to the others. In total there will be five stories. One for each race of the world.

Moonlight in a Martini Glass - This was not a story I planned on continuing this soon, but it pushed its way through :)

D. Gray Man - Shorts for now. I never planned to branch out to other genre's but man does that story hit you and ask for more to be written about it.

General Note:
No matter what it might look like. I tend to keep all romance out of my stories. I just don't believe a good story 'needs' romance. Though I'm not against it at all :) I just don't write it into my own stories. Everything you read is strictly friendship, close brotherhood/sisterhood.
