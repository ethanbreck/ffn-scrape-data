Author has written 2 stories for Buffy X-overs, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
I read mostly BTVS and/or Batman fiction. I write BTVS with a strong Xander bias. To me, he has the most potential of any BTVS character, which is sadly wasted by the writers.

Finally, I got so frustrated that I decided to develop him myself.

I have begun to create a storyverse around Xander with some X-overs from somewhat new and hopefully interesting places.

The first story is Romeo Lost(currently a WIP), which begins in AU S3. It has Intrigue, conspiracies, hidden agendas. It will be somewhat dark(not Season Sux dark), intense, yet balanced. Lemme know what you think so far?
