I guess it's time...although I don't know why I'm bothering after all this time. shrug Hate to say it peeps, but this account is officially dead, though I'm sure no one is too heartbroken to hear that. It was crap. Pure, unadultered crap. So I'm moving on, in hopes of a brighter future somewhere else.(anyone besides me see fields and fields of golden corn?) I've done some growing up during this prolonged absence, and I can say with confidence that so has my writing; It was nice getting response from readers, and I'm happy that anything I came up with (no matter how stupid, idiotic, & moronic they were) has served as entertainment to all you good people out there.
