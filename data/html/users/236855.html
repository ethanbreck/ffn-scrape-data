Alright is not a word. All right, however, is an expression. It's two words, not one. All right?

Alot is also not a word. A lot of people seem to think it is.

Anyways, that's my biggest pet peeve. (Anyways is not a word if you didn't pick up on that one since it wasn't exactly obvious.) Anyway is not a noun, it cannot be made plural nor possesive. It can, however, be split in to two words and become any_ ways. I don't think there are any exceptions to this, but let me know if you find any ways, all right?
