Author has written 15 stories for Trigun, and Haibane Renmei.

Do you know what the best, and worst, thing about writing is? Being unable get the stories out of one's head, unless one writes them down. I've looked; they're still there.

I'm sorry I've left everyone waiting--or not, as the case may be--for me to finish my stories. The only excuse I will plead is that for the last three years I have endured the most difficult intellectual and spiritual challenge of my life...and emerged victorious. That being said, I have about a year to finish everything up, and set down some new stories I've been thinking about. I'm not sure if I'll be able to write much fanfiction after that, though I'm sure the stories will still clamor to be told.

So, here it comes: the year I finish everything--and then some!
