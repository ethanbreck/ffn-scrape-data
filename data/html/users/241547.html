My stories:

I am planning on finishing my other stories since I'm relapsing into my NaruHina craze. I can't take it anymore--my countless 'i'll do it later' attitude has finally bit me in the arse in real life. After cleaning THAT mess up, I've realized I have a few loose ends to tie in my free-time life where I doodle fanfiction. Countless reviews and even personal emails has convinced me that up and leaving a fic that I myself am still pretty fond of isn't really fair. Unfortunately, though, the story My Favorite Night will be at this point in time--on hiatus indefinitely as far as I'm concerned. I've been struggling with the climax of the story for a while, and don't know how to write it. And as you all know--I'm really sucky at delivering endings, so I'd like to concentrate on the stories that I still enjoy.
