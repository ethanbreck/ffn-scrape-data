Author has written 5 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Final Fantasy VIII, Chrono Trigger, and Yu-Gi-Oh GX.

Not much to say at this moment; I am an aspiring author who mainly only writes recreationally instead of professionally. I do hope to be published and have my works sold eventually, but for now I'm just going to perfect my art here with fun projects. So far, I am dedicated to completing a story I have entitled Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Shuffle, a third series that takes place in Duel Academy, and the projected length is amazing. Despite already having a huge project ahead of me, I am also thinking of another large, but not quite as imposing, project. I am talking about redoing my oldest fan fiction entitled Cry Not Seifer. I wrote it when I was fifteen, and it shows. I still love the story of it, and my projected storyline is still fresh in my head. If I do remake it, it will take the place of the original, and will be slightly different, since I couldn't bear to make a direct remake. I hope that Cry Not Seifer 2.0 would fix some of the little problems the original had if I do indeed do it. Only time will tell if I can find the time. Don't bet the farm on it though...

Interests: Duel Monsters, Dungeons and Dragons, many console RPGs, writing both original and fan fiction stories, and just having random fun (like most everyone).

Remember: fair play, fair duel.
