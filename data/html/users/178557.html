So, about me then. I figured we would have both some normal and some utterly useless information about me.

1) I'm from the UK. Therefore if you are American or have learnt American English then my spellings may be different to yours. However, I was born in The States and therefore have two passports, as the US won't let me in without an American one. It's bizarre.

2) I am probably one of the few people on this site who God did not intend to be a writer, and I haven't been to an English lesson for over four years. This is not because I bunk: I just dropped English, because I was bad at it. Also I prefer science and foreign languages, except my french teacher hated me because I spent three years drawing (manga) and never did any work, so I took spanish for A-level instead.

3) I used to live in Alaska, although not for long enough for me to learn to ski like a pro, sadly. I can ski well enough to avoid hitting a tree, touch wood (sorry couldn't resist) which I suppose is some achievement.

4) I can't tie a tie.

5) I never throw anything away. This makes for interesting clear outs. In my wardrobe I recently discovered a very tacky five year old paper bag from Monsoon (a shop) saying SALE on it, and a dress I wore when I was eight. And that was just in the first five minutes.

6) I'm really quite lazy with my writing. I also have a relatively busy non-writing life.

Well, OK I should probably talk about my stories. Um...I want criticism (see point number two).
