These are my stories, listed with a much better description than Fanfiction.net allows

Completed:

Dark Force- Not that different from many others, but I think that it is okay, not great, and I know this, so please don’t flame. I had to write this story for school two years ago. A young teenager finds herself over taken by an ancient power that wants to take over the world. Will anyone be able to stop her (or him depending on the way you look at it)? I made explanations for a couple of events (true or mythical) from the middle ages. I know my spelling sucks in this story.

The Emerald- Yet another story I had to write for school. Really short, only three pages typed. This story had a couple of criteria points that I had to draw into my story. It had to be fewer than five pages long and it had to involve a dream. A young mother is devastated when she finds her five-year-old daughter dead one morning. An autopsy reveals nothing about the little girl’s death, and the mother is left wondering about her daughter, and if she is really dead, or only gone.

In the Works:

Amorola- Will is lord of Amorola, and is set to marry the girl of his dreams. He is rich and good-looking. But what happens when his life long friend King Richard sets his eye on Will’s fiancé? Will is beat until nearly the point death, thrown out of the capital and is said to be dead. In fact Will is cling to life in a small cottage just a few miles from the cities walls. Does the newborn-orphaned baby have anything to do with Will’s future? Will he ever get his life back? What about his only love? Loosely based on Robin Hood and The Count of Monte Cristo.

Unnatural Powers- Mercy is definitely not princess material. Awkward and a bit eccentric she is an outcast in her own home. Her mother had powers, ones that are not accepted in her society. At the same time there is another living in the same city with similar powers. Together their powers are unnaturally strong. But after the King’s death, Mercy knows that her life is in danger (sort of like Queen Elizabeth the first after her brother died) and she will do anything to save her life. I started this story over a year ago, but I will work on it if people think that it is okay.

Ideas (I will write this stories, but if you really want me to start on one please tell me):

Big Machine- Ever heard the song “Big Machine” by the Goo Goo Dolls? Do you know the line “ecstasy is all you need, living in a big machine”? Well this song inspired this story. Not completely certain about what will happen, but I am certain that it will involve a rave of sorts called The Big Machine, which bounces from major city to major city all around the world. The police are it there wits end trying to figure out how to stop this giant part. No characters yet, but I have ideas. Not a song fic!!
