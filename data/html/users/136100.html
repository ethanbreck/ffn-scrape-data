Hey everyone!

So, once again it has been 2 years since I update anything on this account, lol.

But, I've recently decided, even if I don't think I can, I should try and update anyway, because I like writing! And the best way to get better is to practice, right? XD

So, I'm back! Sort of. I haven't posted anything new yet. But hopefully I will, soon.

Thank you to everyone who read it and left such awesome reviews. Maybe someday I'll pick it up again, or maybe someone out there would like to finish it for me!

I'm going to be continuing my 30 kisses fic, however! I have been trying to think of plot for it, but I think it might be better suited to a series of one-shots.

Thanks everyone for your patience!
