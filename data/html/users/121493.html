Alright...I haven't updated this in awhile...

It's been two years since I've changed this, so I am now fifteen (and driving...I've only hit one car) and in high school, LaGrange High School to be exact, and it sux let me tell you. I have two best friends in the world, their Latin nicknames being Fidelus and Acreaea, and without them the world would be a living hell. Oh, and speaking of that wonderful, dead language Latin, I'm attempting to teach myself, whether it's working or not...I've yet to truly find out...

Anyways...enough of this crap on to my stories...

Oh, and by the way, I started writing on here when I was about eleven, soI would not suggest reading that Catasra story, it's horrible...Most of the others are pretty sloppy works as well, really and truly I'm pretty sure I can write much better than what is shown on this site, but, ah well...what can you do? Read and formulate your own opinions I suppose...but then again, who reads these bios anyways?

Beauty Full's Archive of Song Ficlets: This is just a collection of little songfics that I've done, they aren't really that good, but they were fun to write.

From the Bizzare Minds of Beauty Full and Skit Tles: Yeah, this was definitely one of the weirdest fics I've ever written, and it is also the only one I've written with another person (Chelsea on a sugar high). Read it...it's weird.

Welcome Back To: the Bizzare Minds: Yep, this one's weird too...it's not really a sequal or anything like that, you'd have to read the first one, and then this one and you'll see what I mean...

Of Presumptions and Absentness: This is the story I'm working on now, most of the time at least. It's the first L/J fic I've ever written besides all the song fics (yeah, those are ALL L/J). I'm trying, that wouldmost definitely be the key word in thissentence,to make it really good so it takes a while for me to update it, but I have discovered a plot for it. It's going to take place over the last three years of the Marauders and Lily's schooling. Despite my best efforts, it's turned into crap...but I suppose I will be obliged to finish it, just so that I can say I have.
