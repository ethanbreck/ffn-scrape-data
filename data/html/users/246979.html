T h e • B A S I C S

I've deleted several old, old stories from this account that I just can't stomach the horrible writing on anymore. Works from your teen years are a bitch, ain't they?

I'm in the middle of a seemingly never-ending quest to re-write and move forward with my DBZ T/P fanfiction, A Place In Time. I started back in 2004 and have been updating it about once a year since then. I apologize to those who have followed the story all this time, for my life has always seemed to get in the way of finishing at a faster pace. I'm always purposing myself to try and update it and I do promise to keep going with it no matter how long it takes! It's one of those that seems to constantly grab my interest back again and again, so I'm sure it'll be finished one day.

In the meantime, I'm always working on new one-shots and longer story ideas that come to me. I've got a few Star Wars EU things rattling around in my head and I've got another couple stories going that are connected to my A Place In Time canon.
