Author has written 18 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Man From UNCLE.
My magnum opus on ff.net (or anywhere else in fandom for that matter) is Recaptured. This story is currently in two parts. The first is incongruously titled Recaptured II. This is because the original Recaptured was deleted for being labelled NC-17, although that was just to be on the safe side as there was nothing NC-17 about it really. Nevertheless, it has been reloaded and is still as innocuous but just as long. Also, so they are not lost to posterity, I have copied in all the original reviews to the newly posted version and if sometimes they don't seem to make sense, that will be, not because the reviewers are insane (although sometimes that can be true)but because a chapter got rewritten, not to remove smut, but to remove crappy writing.

It continues to grow in Recaptured Continued. (Catchy title eh?) and I'm not sure when it will finish - probably when the reviewers get bored or I run out of words whichever happens first.

In the meantime, if anyone would like to talk to me or find out more about what I think I can be found in my Live Journal - here:
Please come by and say "hello", have a cup of tea and a piece of lardy cake, make me your friend.

Here you will find the links to the Golden Mushroom Awards

Thank you to all who kindly for Recaptured and for Moria's Revenge, which I co-write with Kookaburra, in these fun Awards. It was nice to see these "just for a laugh - let's poke fun at ourselves" awards, which don't say any one fic is better than another. I was delighted to get noticed, especially in such esteemed company as the other amazing writers who were nominated.
