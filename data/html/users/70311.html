I. Joe, Pirates of Dark Water, and Captain America.

I'm Firestar9mm. I used to be Serena4. I love writing, and reading. For me, writing is like putting on makeup--even if I'm not going anywhere, I still like to do it, just for me.

I love canon. So much it hurts. I don't have any interest in reading about beautiful Sues who lobotomize the canon characters and turn a story on its ear. However, people are entitled to write these things. I just won't read them. I'll defend their right to do so, but not the integrity of their craft. Free speech is like any other right--really amazing when people don't abuse it.

Sometimes people ask me to read over their work and tell them what I think. I'll happily do that when asked, regardless of subject matter. I will always be honest about it, but also polite, because courtesy doesn't cost us anything.

I love getting reviews, but ever since my early stories here I've had one rule: please be constructive. No unreasonable ranting. (It's right there on the beginning Big O stories. Go ahead and check if you like; I'll wait.)

The stories are under this profile. If you'd like to read them, you are most welcome. If you don't, I'm sure there's something on YouTube you can watch, or some farm that needs tending on Facebook. Thanks for stopping by, either way.
