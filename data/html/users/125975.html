17, huge geek. I love writing angsty, horribly depressing stuff. Or stuff that's really strange and completely fucked.
Name: Jenni, but my pen-name is Kelvena.. I've had it for as long as I can remember. It comes from a really old Playstation game called "Xenogears", she was a very minor character, but she was really cool-looking. (Blue hair, and she's blind.. and I thought she was interesting, even though she doesn't have my personality at all.)
Location: NEW JERSEY. And no, I don't have that stupid accent! And yes, our governor is gay.. GO HIM!!!
Appearance: Eh.. I'm okay-looking.. black hair, spiked in the back, long in the front. Extremely dark, chocolate-brown eyes. People describe me as 'cute'. I dress like a gothy nerd. I'm actually into fashion, I like to make a statement, but I don't really think I stand out as stylish.
Favorite bands: If there's one thing in my life I'm BIG into, it's music. If you want to know anything about music, I'm the person to talk to. I'm really into a lot of sounds that people don't know of. I like industrial, which is basically a very muddy and grungy mutation of metal and techno, wrapped into a disgustingly beautiful musical genre. Think bands like Nine Inch Nails and Skinny Puppy. I also like hardcore, which is basically punk except a lot better and lots of screaming. Atreyu, As I Lay Dying, Bleedingthrough, Zao, Killswitch Engage, Unearth. Then there's just tons of random bands I like, like Bauhaus, Joy Division, Franz Ferdinand, Modest Mouse, The Smiths, Thursday, Finch.. yeah, I'M OBSESSED WITH MUSIC!! CAN YOU TELL!?
Job: I'm a slave to a women's clothing store. And it's not cool or anything, think tacky vintage floral-print shit. There you go. That sums up "Fashion Bug", a store that doesn't have much "fashion"-sense whatsoever.
Marital Status: Uh.. as most people know, I really love my ex-boyfriend.. and I get very angsty and sentimental when I think about it, so I'm not gonna write a million paragraphs trying to definecomplex feelings. He's one of the coolest people I have ever met, he's attractive, and he's just...great..
Interests: I love to play video games!!! Also, hanging out with my friends, watching movies, going to the movies, writing, drawing and painting, being a nerd.. Favorite subjects to write about: -Johnny The Homicidal Maniac -Devil May Cry -Final Fantasy VII -Cowboy Bebop
