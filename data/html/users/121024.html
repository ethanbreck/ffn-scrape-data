This is the profile of the non-prolific, oft-distracted Aimaina Hikari. Who does get a lot of plot-bunnies, but they tend to die on her before she actually gets them written. Don't bother her about not writing/updating, because she writes for herself and no other. Feedback obtained from reviews is welcome, but more of a fringe benefit at this point. She won't talk about what she's currently writing, since they tend to commit suicide after that. So it goes.

Oh, and her current fandoms have shifted to Naruto and Full Metal Alchemist. And sometimes Witch Hunter Robin. So she'll be writing for those, if at all. That bad Digimon fic she has up currently really needs to be taken down soon. But it probably won't, so you can go and laugh at what is arguably bad writing. Or cringe. She would.

She wants plot-fics and in-character fics. And fics about Roy Mustang or Haruno Sakura, where Sakura is actually useful and not helpless. That's like asking for world peace. And she'd really like more intelligent Naruto-fic, even the anime/manga is *really* like "Character X was pwned by Character Y but then Character Z comes in and pwns Character Y". Oh, and she likes some *really* odd pairings. There's probably something wrong with her. She would also like to say that her taste in fanfiction is excellent...but that's probably a lie.

She likes kakasaku, sasusaku, and sasusakunaru. And royxhawkeye and amonxrobin. Write a fic using any other pairing well enough though, and you'll probably have a convert. She's slightly allergic to most shounen and shoujo ai, though.

And one last thing - you will never ever see her write like this: "OmG THeiR LUV iz LIEK so OTP!111!". Nor will she read fiction written that way. Not even if it's the greatest thing since sliced bread. It gives her headaches. Not to mention those annoying eyetwitches.

...this has been Aimaina Hikari.
