FAQ

Will you write a sequel to Bound?

- I know that a lot of new people are getting into the DBZ fandom with the new versions and reboot, but no. The DBZ bug has not bitten me.

Will Behind Closed Doors ever be finished?

- Short answer: No. To much work, not enough time. Long answer: I would love too and the rewrite I've been working on is insane, and fun and everything I think the story should be, but it's already twice as long as the original and isn't even half done. I'll finish it one day... Everyone needs goals for the future.

Are you still writing? You haven't published in a while.

- I am! But time constraints mean I don't often have the ability to work on things consistently and I don't want to post up anything I can't finish.

I love your stories! Who do you recommend?

-Thanks! I don't get much time the read fanfiction these days, but I'll always recommend my sister-author, Justice-hime. She is currently in a KakaIru (Naruto) frame of mind and her stories get better every time she writes a new one.
