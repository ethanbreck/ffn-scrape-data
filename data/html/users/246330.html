Author has written 3 stories for Dragon Ball Z.
Before light, darkness, even space...the concept of time was created. In order to balance the past, present, and future, three celestial beings were created. First was Fingo, Fate of the Past. She is the Keeper of Knowledge, Wisdom, and Wit. Then there is Rachiela, Fate of the Present. She is the Keeper of Love, Romance, and Beauty. Lastly, there is Tetris Xienya, Fate of the Future. She is the Keeper of War, Spirit, and Fighting. Together they they are The Three Fates, Keepers of Destiny.

Just to give you a little background on us...
We are actually seperate writers on FF.net. OUr names will be in the Fave auth. list. We tend to write B/V fanfics, but we're also expanding. What we do is a little strange. We (as the three fates) believe that, when an author creates a story, he/she has created an alternate universe. What we do is change one thing in a story/show/movie/book and try to keep history from screwing up. Anyways, on to personality, looks, yada.

~~Fates of Destiny~~*

Fingo: Fingo is the tallest of us all. She's got light green hair with two white streaks framing her face. She has dark green eyes and her celestial clothing is that of a rennasaince girl. (dark brown bodice, maroon skirt, knee high leather boots..(old style))
Fingo is somewhat of a know-it-all. She's totally super smart and kinda goofy. She tends to be the more serious of us all, :if you wanna call ANY of us 'serious'.: To her everything is either black or white. No grey. She has one major quirk (as all of us do): Bacause she is the fate of the past, whenever something screws up, she gets this SERIOUS headache. Most of the Time it is Rachiela that is deleting things on the Fate Files, so any clicking noises tend to make her left eye twitch. Fingo is book smart, as well as common sense smart. She understands the scientific reasons for love/lust as well as the romantic aspects.

Rachiela: Rachy is taller than Tetris, but shorter than Fingo. She has Long lavender hair, and her eyes are a light blue. Her celestial clothing is more modern than Fingo's. (Thigh high boots, black leather miniskirt, Dark gray and Purple shirt.)
Rachiela is a total hopeless romantic. She's a little airheaded (not much) and always believes that true love will always prevail. She's knowledgeable about anything mushy or whatnot. For example... Say Vegeta was badmouthing Bulma and they were yelling and screaming...well, she would go all analytical and explain that their anger is actually masking their deep respect and passion for the other. The other two would actually just sit there... anyways. Rachiela's 'quirk' is that she cannot stand the thought of love not prevailing over death/pain...If she even hears the Mention, of Romeo and Juliet, she bursts into tears. It's quite amusing for the rest of us.

Tetris Xienya: Tex is the shortest of the group, standing at a mere 5'1/2. She's really sensitive about her height. She had navy blue hair with gold ringed blue eyes. Her celestial clothing is a black and grey/blue bodysuit. Her bodysuit kind of covers her feet, so...no shoes.
Tex is more hotheaded than the others, her answer to everything is "Kick _'s ass" Even though she's a hopeless romantic, she is clueless about love. She's a little sarcastic (little being a major understatement) but her intentions are usually good. Out of the three, she tends to be the most trouble, (besides Rachy, who is always deleting past events). Her quirk? Beign the Keeper of War, Tex cannot stand heartell of peace. Any mention of it, usually sends her into one of her 'moods'...meaning, she needs to go and beat the crap out of someone.

That's it for now...
Until then

With your destiny in our hands:
Fingo-Fate of the past
Rachiela- Fate of the present
Tetris Xienya- Fate of the Future

"Free Will? We beg to differ..."
