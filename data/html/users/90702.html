If you felt the need to leave a review on about how my grammar need to be improved. Please, don't say "your grammar is lacking" or "your grammar sucks". That's not constructive and I will not learn from this. Instead, point out one or two example in my story that need improving and give me a feedback on how it need to be improved. I'm more than happy to open up a PM and have a chat.
