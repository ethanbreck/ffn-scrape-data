May 21, 2006:

It saddens me to say that this account, and all the fanfics in it, are officially dead. This penname was started at a much earlier time of my youth and no longer contains the kind of works that I feel represent me. I'm sorry that any uncompleted fanfics will remain so, but I no longer have the drive and inspiration to see them through. I will leave this account inactive so that the stories will remain I'm still writing, just under a different name and probably a different style.

Thank you so much to those who have taken the time to comment on my work. Reviews have really been the lifeblood of my years on FF.net, and I truly appreciate the gesture.

Carpedium.

~Seeress
