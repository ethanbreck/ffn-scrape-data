Entei-rah here. Hey, how dare you all run away. Anyway, if you're wondering, I'm in the North of the UK, and I love Watership Down. Based on that, can you guess what all of my fanfic currently falls under. I did have another series on here called Sixth Voyager, a parody of Star Trek Voyager, but I have now removed that.

What remains is something called 3rd Water. 3rd Water is what I call my 'Virtual Alternative Watership Down Series 3' - long title, hence why I call it 3rd Water instead. It's a continuation of the Watership Down TV Series, from episode 26 (The Homecoming). It ignores the existence of the official series 3, as that was never released in the UK. Currently I'm only experimenting uploading the episodes here, as I fear the massive public backlash from the majority of 'book-lover, series haters' out there! =;)

I'm also working on 4th Water away from here at www.thankustars.co.uk/4thwater. Once I finish it, I will upload them all here too. =:)

~ Entei-rah (18/8/2003)
