'The Bill' current status:

Things I'd like to write (And will one day):

15 odd scenes: that featured on the Bill I'd like to add to and make stories out of.

Story idea - Being a cop and dating: I have no idea where this idea (Ha ha) came from, just jumped up and grabbed me one night, they're probably the best ones. Newly divorced Neil struggles on the dating scene, by chance he and Andrea come to discuss their collective failures in their personal lives. While both on seperate dates they run into each other and through a strange co-incidence end up together. My first attempt at something humourous (for this pairing), nothing written yet just two pages of notes, no title or working title, posting date unknown.

Story idea - An accidental pregnancy: No title, no working title, nothing written yet but a few pages of notes and a rough plan, posting date very unknown. Andrea is enjoying her new relationship with Neil but is shocked (or maybe not so) to find herself pregnant within the first few months of seeing him. Faced with a decision to make Neil decides he can't at this point opt out of his marriage and can't support Andrea in an open way, despite this Andrea decides to keep his child. In the ensuing months she finds herself attached to CID when life on the street gets too dangerous for her, often times uncomfortably for Neil. She does respect his decision, understands it and tries to keep the baby stuff subtle but CID are caught up in baby fever and with Neil's lack of presence in her life and the baby's, Andrea finds it uncomfortable to defend and apologise for him to his colleagues. Through a series of events Neil finds himself at the birth and subsequent first's for his new child...will he realise he's made the wrong decision?

Story idea - Twice in less than a month: No title, no working title, nothing written yet but a half a page of notes, posting date very unknown. Based on the events of episode 235. On a dangerous job with disillusioned Kerry Young, Andrea bravely walks into an armed situation. When the assailant pulls the trigger only for the gun to not fire, after the bomb Andrea is shaken by her second close call in less than a month. When Neil arrives as part of CID he berates her for her actions and Andrea breaks down only to have Neil confess a few truths of his own about her.

Story idea - The downfall of Gabriel Kent: Clutching her evidence against Gabriel Kent in the corridors of Sun Hill after everyone has found out about her undercover journalism job, Andrea is done feeling threatend by him. She slips what evidence she has into Neil's mail slot and it's the last anyone sees of her till her body is found. In the depths of his grief Neil pushes aside the information to deal with his crumbling career and life, when he's come to terms with Andrea's death he picks it up again, passing it onto MIT when he realises Andrea was onto something. As he gets deeper and deeper into the investigation he finds things are not as they seem. No title, no working title, nothing written yet just a few scribbled notes, posting date very unknown.
