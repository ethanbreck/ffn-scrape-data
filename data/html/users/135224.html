Author has written 52 stories for Supernatural, Merlin, and Glee.

Hi. I'm a mom of two girls who squeezes in TV whenever the DVR or Netflix will allow. I write for a living but not creatively. Reading fanfiction has been an off and on again pleasure depending on whatever show I'm obsessing over. Nothing however prepared me for Supernatural. This underrated but incredible little show has led to my writing some fanfiction of my own. I'm not the fastest writer so I don't know how many or how long or where this will lead.

For those who have stumbled on these tales, welcome. They are from the heart. Please review so I know what works and keep feeling inspired to create more tales.
