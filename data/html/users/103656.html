P.S. I have one published story, and I plan to edit it at some point (i.e. whenever I decide I care enough); I wrote it in high school, and it reflects that. Four years and many, many research papers and creative works later, I've learned quite a bit. In any case, my writing on this site does not necessarily "represent me."

Several of the stories on my 'Favorites' list are from a defunct television series called Witchblade. I must have been going through an action movie phase.

I am a devotee of several fandoms, including, but not limited to: Pirates of the Caribbean (Jack/Liz), Harry Potter (AU Lucius/Hermione), Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and especially The Office (Jam, Dwangela, and R.Kelly).

Though I sometimes use my 'Favorite Stories' list as a means to save fics of possible interest, in general the stories from my list are of a certain standard. I keep a short list of the jewels on hand, for my own benefit and for the benefit of those people who want to read The Best from certain fandoms on a site that's prone to a lot of fic of really variable qualities.

Please also feel free to recommend stories to me for inclusion on the list!
