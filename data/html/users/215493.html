story. status.

I'm...back? Or at least, I'm going to try to revitalize this account. I've been on ff.net since I was 12 and have written something over 40 stories (to varying degrees of completion). I've been absent for going on five years but here's my plan to dust off the cobwebs and get going:

A Day in Paradise: Will receive edits and final chapters. Should be complete by Aug. 1, 2016.

Intoxicated: Will receive heavy edits.

Widowed: Will receive edits and final chapters. Should be complete by Aug. 1, 2016.

I'm Not Your Mother: Will receive edits.

Final Act Acknowledged: Will receive edits and final chapters. I'm looking for someone to take over for this story, or else I will just choose to make cuts to the plot.

author's. commentary.

I try to write stories that are firmly founded in the universe of my chosen fandom but that hopefully add depth to the characters that I love. With few exceptions I write to cannon, and most of those exceptions are for head cannon :D This is the criteria that I wish my stories to be judged upon, although any other comments are deeply appreciated.

disclaimer.

Any suggestions for how I should improve my writing, please review or PM me. I promise I don't bite.

~illegiblewriter
