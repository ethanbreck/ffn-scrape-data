Author has written 12 stories for Dragonlance, and Lord of the Rings.

I am eighteen years old, a college student and I love to read and write. I warn you now that a good many of my fics, "Mistake" among others, have been going on for a time and may or may not come to an end. Usually, I start posting before I have any clear idea of where the story is going and I find that those are the best sort of tales, for they take on a life of their own and anything can happen. However, more of my fics consist of short tales, like "For Middle-earth," "Possession," and "There Were Four." These I write really for no particular reason other that to write about those I love. They are short fics and usually not intended to continue or be anything more than they are. However, if enough people insist or I take a liking to it then I might take it to a different level. I guess it just depends on my mood. Also, most my fics are Lord of the Ring (I think I have one Dragonlance fic) but I am open to others and may write such. Anyway, that's a little about me and my stories. ChildofChild

UPDATE*

Well, I'm certainly no longer 18. Neither am I a college student. But I have neither the heart nor inclination to remove my previous profile. Many things change in 10 years. However, it's good to remember..
