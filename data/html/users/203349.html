May 5, 2007:

Yeah... I think it's safe to say that it's not likely I will be finishing "Hope." I'd like to... but I don't think my brain is in the right mindset for it.

I've just finished my second year of college... and college ain't no joke.

Plus, I'm trying to hone my... uh... "regular" fiction skills at this point in time (which means, if you really like my writing, I've got stuff on fictionpress you might want to check out). Not to mention, I started that story a long time ago, when my life was 5000 times different, and I was a lot younger, not so much chronologically, but psychologically.

I'm not really giving up on the whole fanfiction thing just yet. "Hope" will always be in my thoughts. I still know where I want to go with it.

But my heart's not in it like it used to be.

If anyone's still interested, that message was for you.

The One and Only,
The Angry Black Woman
