Name: Yami Megami

I am a teen girl in High School. I love fan fiction, particularly dark ones. I am a melancholic thinker, seeing the most innocent things and finding in them something that could be dark. I am fascinated with the basic concepts of light and dark. It's at the core of most my work. I also have a twisted and sometimes ((ok, alot of the time)) bad sense of humor.

Hence my pen name and nick name, Yami megami, Goddess of Shadows. I used to be Goddess of Light ((Dea Luminos, a spin off the latin words for goddess and light)) But I find myself more on the other side of the spectrum now. In all of my work I have a piece of me. So please be easy ;;

Fave fic: Disastrous behavior ((FF8)) and Division (Naruto))

Greateste Work: I Need You ((Jimmy Neutron))

Quote: "The closer you get to the lighter, the greater your shadow becomes..." - Kingdom Hearts.
