May 21, 2005:
Well, no update now, either. None of my stories are really terribly ready for posting. The farthest ones along are in the 2nd rough draft stage at best. When I finally do post after all these years, it'll probably be a few stories with a few back chapters ready. But, for anyone who, by some freak insult to nature, are actually waiting for me to get something out, here's a taste:

Ranma approached slowly, not really knowing what to do, but unable to just leave. As he neared, he recognized the girl. Her dark brown hair was done up in a high, short ponytail that bobbed as she took in sharp breaths between her tears. She was curled up with her knees to her chest as she sat. Her face was buried in her arms, which were folded atop her knees. Cautious of how the girl might react, he just quietly sat next to her, not too close and a bit a ways behind her, and placed his hand on her shoulder.

—Excerpt from A Girl's Feelings

I hope this proves that I'm not always sitting on my ass instead of writing. He he.
