I just need a Publisher and friend who does watercolors better then me. (Wish me luck on that one!)

I'm sure I've forgotten a hundred or so more things to say about myself, but lets not talk about me all day long, check out my "ORIGINAL works of art" at fictionpress.com under the name 'Cassie Parris.' I'm truely desperate for a review once in a blue moon, so PLEASE check it out!

Another Ramble

Note: What I've typed up of my Original Anime peice is not complete and jumps around like a kid on speed. Thats because I'm trying to translate my scribbles off of over 30 spiral-ring notebooks, as well as, trying to make it a "novel" version of the manga i've been illustrating. Its a backward-assed story with bizarre, disturbing, dark humor tinged, dramatics in it. Romance is sometimes thrown randomly in there because the book is trying to explain so many things, it must jumps from past, present, to future in order for you to understand it. Except for three, all the characters are based off someone I know,or have known, so that breathing life into them is fun, lovely, and entertaining for me. I'm not trying to say its the best story out there.However, when it's finally complete, I truely hope my story istwisted with good doses of horror, action, adventure, bittersweet, heartwarming, romance, angst, friendship growing, drama in it. I hope it is as brutally fantastic as the Fushigi Yuugi, FY:Genbu Kaiden, Ayashi no Ceres, Alice 9th, Hana no Yume (aka: Furuba/FruitsBasket), Elfen Lied, Yami no Matsuei, Angel Sanctuary, FullMoon wo Sagashite Mangas are. Hell, I'll hope I'm half as good as Yuu Watase or Arina Tanemura are. Please, just leave a review. As long as it's constructive, I don't care what you say.
