Stranger Inside
What can I say about SI? It started as a one-shot songfic and then continued, and I had the whole thing running through my head... But wow, it was rather boring to me! It's the 4th book re-written with lots of torture for my poor OC. I won't be taking this down, but unless I ever get inspired for it again... I just don't know.

All my other stories were written years and years ago and I no longer have any interest in them. WT is full of Mary-Sue's waiting to happen, I have no idea what went on what that AU IY fanfic and DYFD, while fun - just wasn't.
