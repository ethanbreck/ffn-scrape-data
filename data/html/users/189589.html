Updated 12/2014:

Too much real life, not enough play time.

As things currently stand, all stories that were not one-shots or completed should be considered ABANDONED. It has been years since I actively posted anything new or have been able to work on any of the unfinished stories here, so looking at the last upload dates I would really like to think that this isn't much of a surprise. While I've always appreciated reviews & reviewers, as well as the platform offered here in which to be able to share what I wrote with an audience larger than I could imagine, I was still writing for my own enjoyment if I look back at why I wrote what I did & if I were to start writing properly again.

Life is what it is, & it isn't always what or as easy as you expect it to turn out to be - as I've learned personally. There's still too much real life to have to deal with & not enough play time.
