Well, hello to everyone! I blew the dust off my muses, changed my pen name and ready to write again, after 6 years of hiatus.

During these 6 years, some of my stories were removed to FictionPress (original fiction/poetry archive), and some were removed completely because of violating their authors' rights (yup, not all authors grant permission for fanfiction stories based on their books). They're all on hiatus now, until I decide what to do with them.

I've got a lovely 8-months-old son, so you must understand, that I actually will be updating, but as soon as possible with a little child interfering with everything you do :)

And the last for now... I'm 23, and all my stories will be rated M, for if I decide to describe sex or violence or another disturbing part of human life, I'll desribe it as it is, as I see it. Feel free to read or not to read, to review or not. I appreciate every sign of attention :)
