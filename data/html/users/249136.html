I am admittedly a terrible poster of stories. I write a lot and it takes me years before I get around to posting them. I'm so sorry.

I like to write but I'm not really as prolific as I'd like. Too many distractions with online games usually pull me away from completing stories so reminders do help in encouraging me to stop being lazy and post.

I have a new fandom. 07-Ghost. Hope the interest is sustained but its a very quiet fandom.

I used to be a heavy Clamp fanfiction writer. I do have a Fruits Basket story and a Yami no Matsuei one too. But a lot of it was too "adult" so I never could post here at FF.net. Most of my new stuff is all in the FF VII fandom.
