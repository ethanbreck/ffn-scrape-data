Hello! My penname is Keyanna, and I'm a grad student. I'm a serial lurker with a notable problem finishing stuff of my own, so unfortunately you're not likely to find much here aside from my favorite stories/authors. Hopefully one day that'll change.

I love fantasy books (most books in general, truly), humor, slash, and the "Hey!" song that they play at baseball games. I've got no problem with flames as long as they address specific problems in my writing and take the time to spell correctly. Criticism, when it's well thought-out, is a good thing; it helps you to revise your work into a better story.

I'm currently spending most of my time lurking around SGA fandom, but my various loves include Gundam Wing, Sailor Moon, Slayers, Firefly, House, M.D., Lord of the Rings, Good Omens, Smallville, Harry Potter, the League of Extraordinary Gentlemen movie, and many more.

Favorite type of amphibian: salamander

Favorite flower: lilacs, followed by mock orange blossoms and grape hyacinths. Then crocuses.

Favorite insult that makes no sense, even in context: "You're an allegory!"

Favorite garden in France: Giverney

Favorite 1980s PBS miniseries hosted by James Earl Jones: Long Ago & Far Away

Favorite ride at Universal Studios' Islands of Adventure: Jurassic Park River Adventure

Favorite amusement park: Cedar Point (in Sandusky, Ohio)

Favorite all-purpose solution: burning down Russell Crowe's house Ah, good times.
