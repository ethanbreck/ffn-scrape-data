Well hello! I don't know why, but I haven't logged on or updated this page in an age and a half. So I will now update it.

Thank you so much to all you lovely people who have continued adding me to your author watch/favorite stories over the years that I've been inactive in this community. At some point my fanfiction writing bug left me, and I've never been very good at multi-chapter stories. In the last year or two I've realized the value of creating fan works, and have been drawing almost nothing but fanart for a little while now (check out my Deviant Art page!)

I also maintain a Tumblr where I've been posting tons of art and silly things (A lot of ponies. So many ponies.) and I've started writing fanfiction again. However, it is BATMAN FANFICTION BECAUSE I LOVE THE BATFAMILY. So much. (Except for Bruce. He's kind of a dick, haha.)

I still love anime and Harry Potter and all kinds of other nerdy dorky things, but I haven't watched anime as avidly as I used to in a long while. It's kind of just become part of my overall love of comics, which is as it should be, really.

So unfortunately I'm not sure how much more I'll ever write on some of those Naruto/CCS fics, but I hope to add at least a little to those of you who have waited patiently for me to write anything.

-- Briget
