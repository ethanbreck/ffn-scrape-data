Hiya

Just a little about me! I'm just about to be 18 which i can't wait for and live in Jersey which is a mini Island in the middle of the English Channel (between France and England for those of u who aren't so excellent at geoggers). I live at home with my parents and my 2 little sisters and our own little zoo of small animals.

I love to read most things! At the minute i'm working through the UK's list of 100 best books! Normally though i have a thing for all the rry Potter's, LOTRs, Nightworld books and most teen fictions like a series of books called Confessions of Georgia Nicolson, which is poractically my life written down!

At the minute i'm studying for A-levels, but i'm only doing two. English Language and Art. I'm really into my art and it's very imprtant to me. I want to become a teacher for younger childern so once i've finished at the school i'm at now i'll be going to college to get a degree in childcare and then it's off to university for me!

Other interests i have include (other than writing) singing, playing my guitar, art, reading, dancing, shopping, lifeguarding (im a lifeguard), teaching swimming (i'm also a swimming teacher!) and playing on the sims... LOTS lol My taste in music is a little messed up though! I love Greenday, Blink and then i love pop bands from the UK like Busted and McFLY. Music is also very important to me as i've been brought up by a very creative family!

I mostly write Buffy and Harry Potter fanfics but i've dipped my pen into the ink pot that is Final Fantasy. One hue problem with writing for me is i end up with writers block! I prefer to write my own original stuff which i can keep writing for ever! I'm in the process of writing a story called Callie Can which i'm planning to make into a different series of stories!

Please r&r my stories and leave feedback, i don't mind if it's good or bad, i lie to get it! Aslso i'e made a oule of friends on here alreay through email and would always appreciate more!
