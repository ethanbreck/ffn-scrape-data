ABOUT THE BABYVERSE

The Babyverse (also called The Pride and the Scourge) is a BtVS/AtS alternate universe. It splits to a new timeline the night Jenny Calendar was supposed to be killed by Angelus in Season 2 (1998). Baby's appearance stopped that from happening, as she had knowledge of all events up through Season 6, which she shared with Spike in a accidental telepathic transfer. Some of the regular BtVS/AtS characters are slightly different because of this different history. To date, the Babyverse covers approximately 20 years, beginning in BtVS Season 2 (1998).

Here are a few basic differences between the Babyverse and the canon BtVS/AtS universes:

Buffy & Spike never got together at all. Angel, Cordelia, and Wesley still ended up in L.A.

Instead of disappearing when Darla left, Drusilla went to live as a recluse with Angel and the A.I. gang, where she met Wesley. They hook up in 2002. Faith was released to Angel's custody and works with them in L.A. Connor was still kidnapped by Wesley, but not taken through the portal by Holtz.

Buffy never died. Glory was dispatched by Spike before "The Gift."

Giles & Jenny married and had children. Xander & Anya did not. Joyce did not die. Dawn battles evil with Buffy.

Baby is married/consort to Spike. Spike calls her Baby, that is not her real name. Spike has established himself as Master of New Orleans. His children were made from crime victims they rescued who were dying and could not be saved. They hunt and feed from criminals, thus they have live, fresh blood, but they also do a service to the community. Their oldest children, Jean, Claudia, and Rene, are masters in their own right. Baby was age 57 (year 2014) before she was turned. Angel and Spike both are her sires (it's complicated). Baby is Wesley's sire (At age 50, year 2016).

Spike has a gem of Amarra implanted in his body, neither sunlight nor stakes will kill him. Decapitation will.
