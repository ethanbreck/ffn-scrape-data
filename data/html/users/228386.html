Hiya!

Welcome to my profile page!

There isn't much about me other then i enjoy Anime/Manga and Video Games! I haven't been too on the ball with Anime or Manga recently... My focus has slowly going towards Cartoons, Comics and TV shows!

As for Fanfiction... i wouldn't be here if i didn't like it now would i?

I have recently been reading Harem stories. I know, they don't make sense but i find myself enjoying them nonetheless. This could be connected to my fascination to odd pairings. Mainly just MY favorite character hooking up with any female in the story, even if it goes against their character.

I can't stand romance stories with characters instantly liking each other... I want character development dammit!

Here are some story ideas that i have thought of. I am planning on creating them sometime in the future. I have written stories in the past... but they were just horrible. I don't know what i was thinking posting those. I am so glad this site started beta system with other authors, now i might actually produce a decent story!
