Slowly you walk through a strange forest, wondering how da monkies did ya ended up there, in the first place! Just when you think you've gotten yerself totally lost, you come across a huge clearing with a big castle and very scary looking green dragon fast asleep in front of it.

Suddenly without any warning, the dragon yawns and huge flames shoot out of it's mouth, narrowly missing the top of yer head. Yer trying to decide whether to stay and find out who lives in the castle, or just run away screaming blue murder back into the forest, when a short girl wearing a pair of black cargo pants and a long sleeved t-shirt that reads "Tha dragoness is in da house" across the front, comes stomping around the corner of the castle, nudges the dragon awake and starts scolding it.

"Spot!!" she says crossly, wagging her finger at the dragon, "How many times have I told you?! No chilly dogs before nap time!!!" The girl is just about to walk away from the dragon who's now hanging it's head like a remorseful puppy dog, when she sees you standing there. "Oh I'm sorry" she says apologetically, giving you a charming smile, "I didn't notice ya there. Don't let Spot scare you. He's a big softy really. By the way, what's yer name?" You take the charming smile and put it in yer pocket, then tell her yer name.

"Cool name" the girl says, giving you another charming smile (which ya put in yer pocket along with the first one), "My names are Pandie and Suki" "Actually," she adds, "Pandie is my name at my other home. I'm just called Suki, here" "You live here??" you ask her, pointing puzzled at the castle. "What? There?" Suki laughs, nodding her head in the direction of the castle, "Nah, I don't live there. That's Spot's kennel. We live in the palace, behind the castle."

You're wondering why she said "We", when another girl walks around the corner of the castle (ie Spot's kennel). This girl's taller than Suki, and wearing a similar outfit, except her t-shirt reads "I'm a sassy tigeress, a nutter, and my friend is insane! Ya wanna make something of it??" on the front. "I saw flames" she says to Suki, "Idiot reviewers or chilly dogs?" "Chilly dogs" Suki answers her flatly. "Figures" the other girl mutters, before noticing you standing there. "Cool" she says, with a big grin on her face, "A new reader" "New reader??" you think puzzled to yerself, before the new girl introduces herself.

The new girl smiles at you. "Hey! I'm Hazel... well... I'm a lot of other things too, but you'd get confused so let's not get into that. Just stick with Hazel. How ya' doin'?... Good? Awsome. Yeah, well feel free to look around, don't worry about Spot, he won't hurt you unless you leave flames in the review box. He insist's that's his job." This Hazel person shook her head at the dragon. "And if you see any crazy guys running around just ignore them. Unless they start flirting with you, then drop by and tell us so we can kill them. I swear we can't do anything with Tauren or Loki." She crossed her arms and gave you a smile that makes you wonder what secret she's keeping.

"Alrighty!" Suki says, as soon as Hazel finished introducing herself, "Now we all know who we are, please stay, glance around and read the stories." "No flames, please" Hazel adds with a smile on her face, "or Spot'll be making butt toasties." They both grin at you and at the same time exclaim; "Thank you!"
