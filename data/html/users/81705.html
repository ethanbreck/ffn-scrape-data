After a VERY long hiatus from the fanfiction world, Alaura Fairfield is back, bay-bee!

Although I write for a couple of different fandoms, I'm currently active only in the Phantom of the Opera community. This might change in the near future, but for the time being, I'm focusing only on my Phantom fics (particularly my in-development story, Warmth Beyond the Winter).

Here are some likes and dislikes of mine regarding phanfiction (both what I'll read and what I'll write):

Likes

Carefully considered characterization of all principals, including (but not limited to) Christine, Erik, Raoul, Mme. Giry, and Meg.

-Proper grammar and correct spelling. These are ALWAYS appreciated.

- Phics with an engaging storyline that extends beyond fluffy romance.

- The occasional well-written lemon.

- Anything involving Meg Giry, so long as it's well-executed.

- Philippe fiction! Keep 'em coming!

Dislikes

- Evil/homicidal/abusive Raoul. I mean, come on. I don't care how much Erik/Christine makes sense to some people--that's no reason to turn Raoul into the spawn of Hell.

- Story synopses with improper spelling. If you can't get it right for the two-sentence SYNOPSIS, there's little hope for the rest of the story, and I for one won't even bother to read it.

- Erik/Other Woman fics. I know there are some very nicely written ones out there, but the idea of Erik finding a love beyond Christine seems just a tad unlikely (in my opinion, anyway).

- Pointless, poorly-written smut. Really, I dislike pointless, poorly-written stories in general, but bad smut bothers me especially.

- Mary-Sues of any sort. Unless you're writing a parody, I'm not interested.

Cheers, all!
