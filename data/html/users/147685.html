Author has written 26 stories for Harry Potter, StarTrek: Voyager, StarTrek: The Next Generation, Buffy X-overs, and West Wing.

Update 2015: I am now a mom to 3 kids. Writing doesn't much happen these days. Everything on this site will remain as is, with no planned continuations. I do still read your reviews, though! Thank you for continuing to visit these bits of my past.

I'm a grad student in a large Midwestern City in the USofA pursuing a Masters in Middle Eastern Studies. As such, I have a lot less time to write than I did in college. All projects are on hold until the summer months (and, possibly, until after HP6, in the case of Fallen).

My Passion is Buffy: the Vampire Slayer, though it does extend to most science fiction and fantasy (and some mystery) books, along with Harry Potter and The West Wing. I love writing, and have since I was introduced to the creative sort in 4th grade. I've got some very old Star Trek pieces and an ever-growing number of Harry Potter bits, along with crossovers and the errant West Wing short.

~Indarae~
