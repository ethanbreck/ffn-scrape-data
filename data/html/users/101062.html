Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Baby Sitters Club, Transformers, and Kuroshitsuji.

I never thought I'd be posting here again, but Kuroshitsuji drew me back. There's just not enough of it on AO3! If you're looking for more of my work, a complete catalog of my stories and podfic can be found at AO3 under the same username. You can also find me as Piscaria on Dreamwidth, Tumblr, and Livejournal, and as Piscaria_Sum on Twitter.

I give blanket permission for all transformative uses of my fic, including remixes, sequels/prequels, fan art, translations, podfic, etc. You do not need to ask permission, but please do link back to my work. I would also appreciate a heads up if you decide to post something based on one of my stories. That always makes my day! Podficcers, I know that written work does not always translate precisely into audio, so if you need to make minor changes to the text (such as adding dialogue tags), feel free. If you want to make more extensive changes, please consult with me first. Repods of stories I've already podficced are also fair game, as long as it's okay with the author.
