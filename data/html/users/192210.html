Okay...FirstlyI should let CTY readers know that it is on temporary hiatus.The reason for this being, as you know it had quite a long break in between a few chapters (okay over a year to be exact). And in that long time that I didn't really write a lot, I sort of lost the gist of what kind of characters I was trying to portray and how I was supposed to convey the story as a whole. So until I can sit down and figure it all out again and get back into the swing of things (hopefully it doesn't take too uber long), CTY will be on hold. I'm really sorry, but in the mean time I'll be hopefully working on a new ff8 fic as well as my GSD one.Thanks for all your support! ..4thMarch 2oo6..

So! If you just happen to be reading this, then it means you've stumbled across a fic of mine. Just a little about myself...I am an Australian uni student so if my updates are a bit delayed, you can safely assume that uni work is the main reason of that. As you've probably noticed I love Final Fantasy, especially FFVIII and FFX and my favourite pairings are Squall&Rinoa, Seifer&Quistis and Yuna&Tidus. I also love Gundam Seed not so much Destiny and my favourite pairing would have to be Athrun&Cagalli. Apart from what I like visually, I love music - my list of favourites, however, would simply be too long to list here so I won't.
