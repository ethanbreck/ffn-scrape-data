I love anime and writing! I really like writing crossovers. My favorite animes are DBZ, Magic Knights, Ronin Warriors, Sailor Moon (especially the Sailor Stars Season), Card Captors, Inuyasha, Yu Yu Hashuko, and Street Fighter. I'm also kind of a love ballad freak and I've written two B/V romance songfics. I love the mushy stuff. When I'm not doing that I'm taking dance classes (been doing that for 4 years now) and going to school. FAVORITE ANIME CHARACTERS: Gohan (he's so cute!), Mirari Trunks, Chun Li, and Usagi (its so weird cause I use to hate her character the most, but after seeing Sailor Stars she's just too damn hilarious to not like!) STATUS OF MY STORIES: "Stillness of Amethyst Eyes" to be finished by fellow FF.net author Mayuka625 "Crossover to Save Time and Space": My very first fanfiction! Put out a very brief chapter 32 after a very kind message received from a reader. After some careful thought I've decided to go ahead and continue this fic. So once again it is a progress in work. A very slow progress in work since I want to go back and revise and change some things. Have patience, though. I've added a prologue and already revised chapter 1. Only 31 more to go! (ducks to keep from being clobbered by flying objects) "World Tournament: Junior Division - With a Twist": COMPLETE with a total of 14 chapters. I think I let myself down with the ending though. Just didn't know what to do. "Sorrow of Sacrifice" Actually, I'm a bit proud of this little fic. I think its one of my better stories. Not to mention the better of the three B/V fics I've written. An over emotional piece of fluff, but I can admit that with pride. I've been thinking of starting a Rurouni Kenshin and Magic Knight crossover, but I still need to do research. I think what I have in mind is going to be pretty cool. NEWEST WORK: "The Daughters of Z Saga" - An author by the name of NishaNisha has offered to take the fic off my hands and transform it into her own creation. Which is fine by me. Check on her if you at all were even following that story. MY FAVORITE AUTHORS & STORIES: The authors and stories that I mention next are the best of the best in my personal opinion. These of course are authors not located here at FF.net, but if you read their stories you'll understand why I say this. 1. "Twist of Fate" by Miranda Stoddard. She's not an author here at FF.net but so what. This story is awesome! Superbly written. I totally bow to this author. This is absolutely one of the BEST DBZ fics I have ever read, by far. I don't exaggerate when speak of the work by this author, also known as Takira, especially considering that there are thousands of DBZ fics in existence. Check this story out. You will not disappointed. 2. "Thinking of You" by ChiChi. Also another author not located here at FF.net, but like i said before who cares. This is also another awesome fic. A lot of action and a great storyline as well. This is a Chi Chi fic but it has all the DBZ characters. Oh and last, but by far not least "Boy meets Girls" by Julian Soullard. This is one of the BEST DBZ/SM crossovers I've ever read. One of personal favorites! It has action, loads of humor, and romance. The perfect fic as far I'm concern. It features Mirari Trunks (one of my favorite DBZ characters besides Gohan) Oh yeah and he also has a sequel to this called "Past, present, and Uncertainty". READ THIS!
