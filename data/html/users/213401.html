If you are interested in keeping track of my progress or reading weird ramblings from my brain, I have a LJ set up

I am a huge sci-fi/fantasy fan. I read a ton of books, always have. Favorite professional authors include: Frank Herbert, Robert Jordan, Tad Williams, David and Leigh Eddings and Terry Goodkind. On a whim I decided to try my hand a writing. My old roommate kinda gave me the idea, she occasionally contemplates trying to go pro.

Got hooked on Farscape when SFC re-ran the series over the winter. Trying to get my husband hooked, he doesn't understand the obsession. Of course he's already big time into the Star Fleet Battles game and their BB.

I'm known on most Farscape boards as LT Garrix.

Just a little update. I'm a rather late comer to the world of creativity as far as Harry Potter goes. I'm generally more interested in fic that looks at the adult side of the HP universe. Probably has to do with the fact I'm adult. I think there is a lot more creativity since there is really so little known about the adults. I like to try to explore those characters. Severus is my favorite because there is so much more to him than we think and I like to see the tidbits we get. I've recently become pretty sympathetic towards Lupin. But that doesn't change my preference for the Slytherins. There's just something about them that calls out to me.

My first HP fic, mostly Severus, but Lupin has a good supporting role is done, going through beta. I've got a Lupin fic in the works. My fics are posted elsewhere under phoenix or phoenix316. (Paying homage to X-Men)
