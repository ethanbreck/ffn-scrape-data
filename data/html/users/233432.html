Hey All! I'm elinox, and was previously known here under the name lostworldlady.

I've written fanfiction for the following fandoms: Farscape, Castle, Charmed, Frasier, Monster's Inc. and Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's The Lost World.

I'm a huge fan of The 10th Kingdom; both the book by Kathryn Wesley and the movie versions. I also a fan of anything by Brandon Sanderson, but especially The Stormlight Archives and his Mistborn series. I love The Dresden Files and The Codex Alera by Jim Butcher and The InCryptid series by Seanan McGuire.

I am not particularly verbose and I don't tend to write in flowery metaphors or beautifully concocted similes. I write in a very straightforward manner; up front and simple. I've been told that I tend to write just like how I talk: down-to-earth and easily approachable.

I am:
- 37 years old
- female
- a writer
- a wife, daughter and sister
- creative
- easy going and laidback
- funny
- so many other things...
