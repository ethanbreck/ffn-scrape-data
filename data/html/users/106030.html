Greetings minna. Well, I have finally decided to post some of my works on FF.net, and here they are. For anyone familiar with my writings, you know that I generally write to a selected group of anime series; Here is Greenwood and Gundam Wing the main ones, and Yoroiden Samurai Troopers (_not_ Ronin Warriors, shudder) under the name of Shin-Chan. I am playing around with some Slayers fics, but they are still a while from being finished. I have even moved to writing Harry Potter fics, which is a big change, considering that for the past four years I have devoted myself to anime fan fics.
I write both yaoi and hetro stories, Greenwood and Harry Potter have a mix of both, Gundam Wing shounen ai and Samurai Troopers and Slayer hetro.
I shall be posting most all of my stories here as soon as I finish revising them, so it may take quite a while for all my works to show, considering how much I have written (since 1987). This also means that new chapters shall be coming few and far between. My pairings that I enjoy writing about are as follows:
Gundam Wing - 1x2, 3x4.

Here is Greenwood - Hetro - Mitsuru/Misako, Kazuya/Miya
Yaoi - Shinobu/Mitsuru

Samurai Troopers - All are new characters, so I won't list them here.
Slayers - Lina/Zelgadiss, Gourry/Sylphiel.

Harry Potter - Harry/Draco, Ron/Hermione.
Current works in progress, To Have and Protect, chapter five; The Gundam Mummy, chapter three.
