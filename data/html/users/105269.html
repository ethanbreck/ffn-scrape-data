Author has written 18 stories for Magnificent Seven, Forever Knight, and Vampire Diaries.

I've been writing on and off (mostly off) for the past 17 years or so, and I still don't have the hang of it. More practice would help, I'm sure. :) On the very rare occasion, an idea hits me, and I attempt to 'put it to paper', no matter how terrible it may end up being. I'd appreciate some feedback. Whether you think a story is okay or simply awful, I'd like to know why. Constructive suggestions are always welcome. So please take a minute to review.

I've been, and will always be, a "Forever Knight" fan. Nicholas Knight (for me at least) was one of the best angsty vampires trying to make it in the mortal world. I'm biased I know, because he was my first. :) I loved watching his struggle.

I'm a big, big Chris and Mary fan. I wish Mag7 was still on the air. It ended much too soon.

I used to be a big fan of TVD, but after the huge train wrecks that were seasons 4 & 5, and the major disaster that season 6 is shaping up to be, I've lost any enthusiasm I once had for the show. Plec And Dries have pretty much distroyed a once very good show. Soooo disappointed in them for completely assassinating Elena's character. I wish I could walk away from the fandom completely, but Stefan/Paul make it REALLY hard. UGH!!

Oh well. It is what it is. Hope you enjoy one or two of my writing attempts.

Oh...and those are 2 of my kitties in my avatar...Calvin & Hobbes when they were itty bitty.
