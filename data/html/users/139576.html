UPDATE: 2011 (Holy crap, the world is still here..?)

My (almost) 23 year old self scoffs at my 20 year old self. I mean, seriously, "An 20 year old"? Pshaw.

By the by, I still don't drink, past self. Aren't you proud of me? God this place is like an archeological site. Full of dusty old stuff.

I can't even figure out how this site *works* anymore. It's been what, 3 years? Jesus. For all you... weird... weird~ folk who read my stories: What are you doing? They suck. Go find a real book. :P

How does this site even work anymore? XD

~-~ The Past Me (June 5 2008)~-~

A revamp is in progress. It's been a while since I've been here. Years, in fact, and I'm sort of disappointed that fans of the things I like aren't as extraordinary in their talents as they used to be. The stories I have up at the moment are complete crap from years and years ago, when I was a noob. Now, I'm probably the oldest Cell fan here. oO

I'm an 20 year old college girl; a maverick, a rather rude and inaccurate soothsayer, a lover of dragons and science, a woman of contrasting ideals and qualities. Be forewarned, I don't care for pretty lies. I give straight, harsh, truthful answers with what I really think. I've taken six years of Japanese, so watch out, because I suck at it.

-Updated June 08- I'm loud, proud, shy, I cry, I love, I bleed, I do all those little human things, and sometimes in passing I bang my soul on the edge of my desk. I have a restless spirit which means I stay up late trying to suck the last bits of existence out of the day. And sometimes everything I've just told you about myself is probably completely untrue. Because sometimes I'm so scared of how worthless I must be in other's eyes. But hey, this is a prof, not a blog.

Have you ever felt like you couldn't find the words to describe something because it was so changing? I guess you could say I'm like the wind. It's a little troublesome.
