Howdy. I'm Kris. I'm 30, a student majoring in English at Brigham Young University (Utah), and entirely (and perhaps unhealthily) obsessed with the following:

Star Wars (most especially the prequels, and even more especially pre-prequels. The Jedi Temple fascinates me and I hate that it was destroyed. Grr.), Star Trek: The Next Generation, Lilo and Stitch, Avatar: The Last Airbender and Legend of Korra, Timeless, Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde, Firefly, Mercedes Lackey's Valdemar series, Tamora Pierce's Tortall books, X-Men, Phantom of the Opera, Love Never Dies, Les Miserables, and the obligatory Harry Potter.

And there's always more.

I'm a wife and a mom nowadays. When I first opened my account here, I was a teenager. I had a lot of spare time in which to write and much more confidence in my writing skills back then. I have very little time or confidence in my writing skills these days, but I'm willing to try to write again for old times' sake. Also I hate that I left my most popular fic unfinished.

Yep, so that's me. Feel free to drop me a line, either by reviewing one of my stories or just plain email. I'll usually respond after I've crawled out of my hole. Thanks for stopping by.
