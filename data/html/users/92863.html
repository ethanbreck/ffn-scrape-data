About Me InDepth:

I'm a veteran writer here- please respect that I have been here a long time, so I've pretty much seen and read every plot imaginable. That being said, I usually read the UNIQUE stories that pop up. I do enjoy romance fics, but I usually like the ones that contain a lot of action/adventure as well. I'm open to pretty much ANYTHING, and while I like some pairings more than others, I will read them ALL.

I do NOT read anything dealing with Original Character's(OC's). Sorry, but nearly all OC's are godawful Mary Sue's, and therefore they SUCK. I WILL read stories where OC'S are NOT the primary character/love interest. I usually love to see OC bad guys- they usually turn out to be the only believable form of OC's.

We need more sinister stories out there! Come on guys, happy endings don't happen in real life to often, so make that true to your stories! Kill off the love interest or best friend; make the bad guy win; create a plague that destroys the world. Too many stories write about happy-go-lucky characters that get the girl and that's it. MAKE YOUR STORY WORTH READING! Make it unpredictable and interesting by adding EVIL and DILEMMAS.

I believe in constructive criticism. Does my spelling suck? Tell me. Was my character OOC? Tell me. Did my plot make no sense? God, tell me. No flames, tho. Nothing like, "OMZG THAT WUZ THE WORRST SHTTTT I HAVE EVA READ!" Say, "Your plot was GREAT, but make sure you spell check!" Show respect to your fellow writers, guys. They'll return the favor:)
