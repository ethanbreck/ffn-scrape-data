Ola, mi compadres! It be's me! Xeaeros! With stuff irrelevant to this website! Woohoooooooo! Don't ask me why I'm updating. I was bored and needed something to do. Oh well. Who's complaining. It gives me another chance to spread my love of Christ. Yeah. All you got to do is ask him for forgiveness of your sins. Come on. You know you want to. Just do it. For me? Pleeeeaaaasssseeee? Okay. So maybe you still have questions or doubts. Why should I give my life up for something that may not be real? He is real. I know it. Where's the proof?Look around you. Have you noticed how perfect everything is for human inhabitation? That wasn't a coincidence. There's too much of an error margin for that to be a coincidence. If our planet was a foot closer to the sun, it would be too hot. A foot farther, too cold. See what I mean. What happens if you don't admit you are a sinner? Well, either you die and don't get into heaven (That's putting it politely.) and you'll suffer eternal torture, or you'll be left behind after the Rapture.
What is the rapture? It's when Christ comes and takes all of his followers up to heaven with him and all who have not asked for his forgiveness are left behind to face twenty-one terrible judgements, each on worst than the last, over a time period of seven years after a treaty was made with Israel and the antichrist. What is the antichrist. The antichrist is a person or thing that can perform miracles, brainwash people, and will say that they are God. Many people will be fooled by them. When will the rapture happen? The bible says that the date and the hour are unknown. The only one who does know is God. But I have a strong suspision that it will happen soon. What type of judgements are in store for those of us who are left behind? Well, there are things such as a worldwide earthquake, the seas and rivers turning to blood, complete and utter darkness, and boils on the unbelivers. The only way to be spared most of this is to become a believer. If you want to find out more, just drop me or Trenton an email. You can reach me at dehmen@ccrtc.com
or Trenton at trenton@ccrtc.com. Or if you can get a hold of a bible with the new testament, read the book of revelations. That's all for now. See you guys later.
