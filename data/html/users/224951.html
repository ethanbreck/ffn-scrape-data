Author has written 27 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Roswell, Twilight, My So Called Life, Queer as Folk, and True Blood.

I am crazyevildru. I start writing fanfiction in 2000 and have been writing ever since. It started with Buffy fanfic and spread through Angel, Roswell, Eminem, Degrassi, Latter Days, Pirates of the Caribbean, Queer as Folk, Twilght and, most recently, True Blood!

I have well over 20,000 pages of fanfic on the internet, most is NC17. I've written various pairings including both het and slash.

My fanfic.net account only holds a few of my fictions as they are too numerous to attempt to upload.

I wrote Buffy for nearly 4 years of my fic writing career. There are still a few stories pending that will probably never be finished. In addition, I co-wrote stories with EvilWillow, an amazing author. All my Buffy stories (or most of them anyway) are archived here at fanfiction.net or at my yahoogroup. Futher updates to any open Buffy fictions will happen via my LiveJournal. I already posted a story that had been started but never completed or posted. It's called Back to the Future and features Angelus, Angel, William and Spike.

I then became infatuated with Michael and Maria from Roswell. Unfortunately, my Roswell writing career was short lived. Most of my one-shots are archived here.

Next, I became infatuated with Eminem and his lyric style and started writing real person fiction. Capture the Rage is my second largest archive of fiction and includes several Eminem/Kim fictions as well as an Eminem/Britney Spears story. You'll also find tons of Eminem/original character fics and a few Nate Mathers fictions as well. All is NC17. During my Eminem stint, I started co-writing with my closest friend Diamond. Together, we wrote thousands of pages of Eminem stories. I've created a list of these fictions because I was archiving them all at my website but we started writing too quickly and I couldn't keep up. So half of them are found at my website and half are available for download at our yahoogroup.

As Diamond and I began having difficulty thinking of new pairings for Eminem, she got hooked on a canadian television show called Degrassi. I became enamoured with Marco and Dylan and wrote a few fictions about them. We also co-wrote a few fictions as well.

It was summer of 2007 when I signed up for netflix and discovered the movie Latter Days, thus inspiring a fiction called Now and Forever. While reading reviews given to me, I noticed two gentleman in a rather saucy position in someone's icon. When I asked who they were, I was told it was Brian and Justin from Queer as Folk. I immediately put the show on my list and have become obsessed once more. I've written several fictions and all are hosted on my livejournal.

Toward the end of the summer and into September of 2007, my friend Diamond told me how obsessed she was with Pirates of the Caribbean and I became enamored with Jack Sparrow as well. We immediately began co-writing again. One of our fictions, Fumbling Toward Ecstasy, can be found on my livejournal. Any other future updates will be done through livejournal.

Then, after I was told I had to read Twilight by Stephenie Meyer. I was infatuated with Alice and Jasper and wrote a few one-shots for them as well as Rosalie and Emmett. They are here at fanfiction.net.

Most recently, I have discovered the beauty that is ERIC NORTHMAN of True Blood. So that's what I'm doing now, archived at livejournal and here!

Throughout all my writing, I've always written under the name crazyevildru. I originally started writing under the name dru but was continuously told that I was crazy and evil for my horrible cliffhangers and ability to drive people mad. This is a name I use proudly and have never changed, chosing to stay true in ALL genres to the fandom that brought me to the wonderful world of fanfiction.

crazyevildru is who I am.

Writing is a socially acceptable form of schizophrenia. - E. L. Doctorow
