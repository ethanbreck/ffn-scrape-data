Author has written 16 stories for Lost World, StarTrek: Enterprise, Remington Steele, Tales of the South Seas, Stargate: Atlantis, CSI: New York, Eureka, JAG, and Once Upon a Time.

First and foremost, I am a shipper. And proud of it. :) At the moment, I am concentrating on being a Robin/Regina (or Outlaw Queen for those in the know :D) shipper for "Once Upon a Time" and a Jane/Lisbon shipper for "The Mentalist." I also love John/Elizabeth from "Stargate: Atlantis" and Brennan/Booth from "Bones." I also love all things scifi. I am also working on actually finishing a multi-chapter story BEFORE posting it, so that I don't have the long lags that my last few multi-chapter stories have had to suffer through. It's a character-building thing. :) Anyway, hope you all read and especially review, and enjoy!
