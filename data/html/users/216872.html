Stupid mistake in 'Wrath of Sorcerers'

Some of you may point to chapter 10 - where kero made a mess of the kitchen and morphed into keroberus – as proof of the staff’s knowledge of Li family’s association with sorcery.

Admittedly, this was an oversight on my part. I, at chapter 10, forgot that the staff were supposed to be ignorant of it [ I remembered to do so in chapter 4 when Xiao Lang shooed the kitchen staff out b4 exterminating Voldermort’s rat (forgot his name) ]. This means that the kitchen staff would probably have fainted at the sight of keroberus, or even kero’s floating form.

I can only propose 3 dumb explanations to cover up this stupid mistake that I believe, nobody even noticed (lol).

1) Sakura informed Xiao Yu of the Li family’s association with sorcery before Kero appeared in front of the manor staff for the first time (and went on his kitchen rampage)

-While this explanation is feasible, it makes a joke out of this side story. Xiao Yu is all so happy that she gets included into the Li family’s secret when Kero pops out three weeks later and promptly informs the entire manor staff of the existence of sorcery. This also means that Sakura will be harshly reprimanded by the elders – pretty stupid don’t you think?

2) The kitchen staff were told to keep their mouths shut over the incident - dumb explanation

3) In CCS world, rearing a tiger with wings is deemed to be within the Li family capabilities [chimera experimentation?, much less a harmless floating softoy - beyond dumb explanation

So I basically dug my grave in chapter 10. As of now, I am unable to remedy this loophole. Maybe I should just erase this section in a week’s time and readers won’t notice anything. (I’m unethical :D)
