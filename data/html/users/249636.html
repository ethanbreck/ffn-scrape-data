~Hola, Konnichi-wa, Bonjour, Hallo, Hi!

Greetings, I'm Maggie, a not-so-typical college student type person that likes to write fanficton based on anime and magna, mostly. I'm a huge anime fan, and since I'll read most anything fun I can get my hands on, manga's cool, too. I also really like the Final Fantasy series and Harry Potter.

As for favorites, well, in anime it's... :deep breath: Yu Yu Hakusho, G Gundam, Rurouni Kenshin, Full Metal Panic (and Fumofuu!) Princess Nine, Bleach, Fruits Basket, Full Metal Alchemist, and Kare Kano (His and Her Circumstances). Fandoms come and fandoms go, but I've liked all of those obsessively at some point during my (10+) years as a part of various fandoms through the good olde intarwebz.

I'm watching Fairy Tail at the moment. I also like Glee (as far as live action TV goes), and Final Fantasy IX. I guess those are my big fandoms at present? :P

On the manga side of things, I'm favoring Kare Kano, Kodocha, and Shonen Jump. (Shaman King, YYH, and Hikaru no Go are favored from there.) Also Paradise Kiss, Nodame Cantible, Ouran High School Host Club, and Hana Yori Dango (Boys over Flowers). I can't help it, shoujo is such a dysfunctional relationship trainwreck that I can't seem to pull myself away from. And Shounen is just odd. :P

Favorite anime/manga characters include: Kuchiki Rukia (Bleach), Satan Videl (DBZ), Yukino (Kare Kano), Maho (Kare Kano), Sana (Kodocha), Sango (IY), Kuwabara (YYH), and Rinku (YYH). Special mention to Rory (Glimore Girls) and Tina (Glee) in the live action favorite characters department.

Final Fantasy IX is my favorite Final Fantasy, followed closely by Final Fantasy X (and X-2, reluctantly). However, I do have a solid appreciation for Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy IV, Final Fantasy V, and Final Fantasy VI. Final Fantasy VII, and its sequel, Advent Children, also rocks in all sorts of ways. Um, I like most of the series, really!

Hermione Granger and Remus Lupin are my favorite characters from the Harry Potter series. (Prisoner of Askaban is my favorite book.)

I like to 'ship characters. I like canon parings for the most part. I like secondary characters. I like characters! :P

I have three "muses", Azuma, a fushica-haired, pigtailed, anime-style ten-year old, and Airella, a Jigglypuff I was "given" for Christmas by an online pal when I was 11. The third one is Misaka, my Gaia Online avatar, more or less. The muses like to squabble, are kind of lazy, but have an erstwhile obsession with NaNoWriMo. (Five time winner, heyhey!)

I think I've told you more than you ever wanted to know about me, so why don't you go read my stories? Also, feel free to e-mail me at the above address, I promise I won't bite! At least, I don't think I will?

By the way, if you click on the homepage link above you'll be taken to my Live Journal, which I update fairly frequently with my general musings and obersvations about RL, fandom, and things in general. I also have an account on FictionPress: It's Miss Maggie over there too. (I haven't updated it in ages though!)

I have a joint-account with the amazingly awesome Hecate's Rath. We write Artie/Tina fanfic. It's epic. eleven.gemini--check us out, lovelies. Please?
