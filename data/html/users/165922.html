About Me: A wandering Brit who traveled across the pond for love and currently lives somewhere in mid-Tennessee. I am currently in my mid twenties, surrounded by seven cats, two dogs and coexist in sci-fi themed madness with my American fiance. I have far too much time on my hands.

About My Writing: My love for fan-fiction hails back to when I first started scribbling Sailor Moon stories in 7th grade. Although I spend most of my time writing my own stuff these days, fanfic is the old friend who I always come back to.

About My Fanfiction: After many years of not writing fanfiction, I became inspired to write in the world of my absolute favorite franchise: Stargate. I am currently writing "Stargate: Delta Site", a story about a remote scientific outpost in a far branch of the Milky Way that becomes cut off from Earth and plunged into the middle of a war spanning multiple realities and races. Although primarily populated by original characters, I have used a few minor characters from the original series and given them a home here. All of the multiple-reality worlds and cultures are of my own invention.
