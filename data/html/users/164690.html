I'm 22 years old (female), from Cape Town South Africa. Iwas at the Waterfront Theatre School where I studied acting, singing, dancing, musical theatre and theory of drama/theatre/acting.I love to write and am currently doing a BA in creative writing. I'm writing an orignal series, two plays, a fantasy novel and some other odds and ends. I like to experiment with original characters in my fictions and find it fun to play with plot ideas.Music is my inspiration, my heart-beat. Ken is my favourite charater, purely because I can see myself in his situation and was quite a dark person back in school. I relate to him. But acting, writing and writing song (rock) lyrics helps me battle my dark side.
I should add, I love Impmon and Beelzemon! (I love all underdogs, don't you?)

Fics I'm currently working on:
'Cruel Kindness' which is a detailed story of how a bad element can change our kind Ken into the cruel Emperor without him knowing it.
'Gone' a story centered around Ken having schizophrenia and thinking the Digital world is real when it is really his fantasy...or is it? (Based on the Buffy the Vampire Slayer episode 'Normal Again')
'Sins of the Father' (seqeul to Doppelgangland).
Like with all my fics, I will only start posting them when the whole thing is finished, so please be patient. (The only exception is Cruel Kindness.)

I have recently started a Live Journal, where I chat about life and my progress (or lack there of) of the stories I am currently working on. Check for updates on how far along a story is, or why I haven't posted anything in ages! (The World I Know).

Some rules for reviewing my stuff: Please give me critique on my style, my characters, my writing all that stuff. I'm doing this for practice and need to know where my weaknesses are. Please don't ask me to read your fics in a review, I am very fussy on what I read. If the summary pulls me in, then I'm half way hooked, then I'll read the first three lines and if I can't stop reading, it's good, if I am bored, I'll leave it. I mainly read Ken centered fics but if I read something else, then it must be very good 'cause like I said, I don't hook easily. If you want me to read something of yours, e-mail me with the name of the story and what it is about and who the main characters are.
Don't bother to flame me. I will laugh and then delete it. If you have a strong opinion on something to do with my story or writing, then tell me in a civilized manner what you don't like and why.

A big thank you goes out to my friend Shmeilia Rockie, who got me writting fics in the first place, you really should read her stuff, it's stunning! A HUGE thank you goes to Akino Ame who is an incredible writer and a huge inspiration.
Thank you to all those people who review my stuff. At first it didn't matter to me if I got reviews or not, but surprisingly, they really inspire me, so thank you people. I wouldn't do this without you. Blessed Be!
