k, first thing's first, I am the same person as the 'Elvea', but I lost my other account somehow, and needed to create another one. The first fic posted here is a continuation of one on my other account. (which I can no longer access).

hmm...I suppose you won't get too bored if I say a little more...

I'm a 16 year old English Secondary school (high school) student who has just finished taking her GCSE (the English eqivilant to SATs, I believe) mocks and will soon be doing the real thing and so has very little time on her hands.
Currently, I only have a couple of humour fics up at the moment, but I do have plans for a much more serious fic (actually, several bits of several plans) waiting in the wings and it may find it's way here if I ever manage to finish some of it to an acceptable level.
As for the fics (if you can call them that) I have up here now, much credit for them must go to one of my best friends, Hnk (www.nedrichards.com/hnk). Her...um...unusual sense of humour has been much utilised and as is said, the one fic was entirely collaboration between me and her. In an English lesson, no less. Despite her many predjudices against elves (probably mostly my fault, I make a bad impression), she's an invaluable muse. And she's mine. No one steals my muse! She's MINE, I TELL YOU!! *mutters* even if I have to tie her in a sack to keep her from escaping...
Besides that, I think it should be fairly obvious to anyone I meet that I am a Lord of the Rings/Tolkien nut, and any upcoming stories shall probably be based around that. I also intend to stay away from romance, as I think it takes talent that I do not have to write it well, as well as any explicit stuff, being without any experience to draw from. That said, I do not have anything against well-written romance, however rare that may be, or against slash. But I do feel that slash does tend to be written for the sake of slash, and it pains me to read something like that. So I fear I have been somewhat scared away from it.
Anyway, if you've continued to read on into my random blatherings, you must be really bored, so I suggest you read on...read the funny things (or so I've been told they are)! Enjoy!
*bounces off*
