Woo! More changes to profile. Well, nothing much has changed, except that I have become steadily more annoyed with this site. I had a fanfic removed for a title that wasn't rated G, but I got over that. It's in the rules, and I just completely forgot. Fair enough, I put it back up under another title. But, lately, for some unknown reason, this site has been removing punctuation. Not random punctuation, by accident or anything of the like, but punctuation in a specific fanfiction of mine which happens to occur before a backslash.

Those of you reading Mine will know that whenever the native language is spoken, it is marked with backslashes. What I didn't realise was that almost all punctuation, especially ? and ! marks that occur before the final / is being removed for some reason that I am not aware of. Therefore, I recommend, and beg of you, that you go elsewhere to read my fanfiction. I would completely remove myself from ff.net if it weren't for the fact that it is still the most-used of the four websites where my fanfics are hosted. Furthermore, I would simply put links to the chapters at the other websites, to remedy that problem, but ff.net will also not allow URLs in fanfiction pages.

Anyway, I'm the KittyMeowMaxwell at all three of the following places:

Go there, and stay there, and, if you can manage it, never read my fanfiction here... But always review! ;)
