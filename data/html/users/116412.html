Author has written 20 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Escaflowne, Sailor Moon, Lord of the Rings, Final Fantasy X, and Final Fantasy X-2.

I'm nothing special, just a girl who likes to write a lot. I'm a fan of Final Fantasy VII, VIII, X and X-2, And I absolutely love Quiefer/Seiftis pairing. I'm also a big fan of Escaflowne (especially Folken) and Fushigi Yugi to name a few. Please read my fics!

I've recently realized that I really love song fics. They are short and get a song to go with them. I believe that music is a big part of life and always use songs to describe the way I'm feeling so don't be surprised if you see a lot of song fics spring up on here soon.
