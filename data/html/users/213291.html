Author has written 3 stories for Mega Man, Powerpuff Girls, and Dragon Ball Z.

Me: California native, 19 year old male, Bla blah... nobody cares anyways. I often have a hard time picking favorite characters in anime (or anything, really) simply from lack of identifiable characters or characters whom I like all aspects of. Fortunately I hardly ever DISlike characters either, so it balances out. Every once in a while I'll find a gem though.

I'm also terribly lazy and a procrastinator, despite my many talents. (cough Riiiight...)

And I get bored easy, so don't expect me to not disappear for half a year or more at a time, only to show up again randomly.

Status:

Okay, I lied, I hadn't started writing again. Sue me.

(actually don't please)

Anyways... I'm probably going to start writing again again, for real this time (maybe), on all three stories. Thanks to the people that have reviewed over the forever that this stuff has been up... without you I may have never come back. (okay, that's probably a lie too, I get to bored easily to never come back at all... but you keep me interested, and I DO appreciate it.) I'll probably a Yu Yu Hakusho fic soon also... simply because my most recent anime binge involved watching episodes 1-112 almost straight through, and Kuwabara is a highly underappreciated character.

(And I'm not certain why people like Jin so much... Chu is way cooler.)

If I don't do a Kuwabara starred fic, it will either be about Zeru or Chu or about Chu hooking up with Yusuke's mom. Bwa ha... I find the thought hilarious for some reason... anywho...

As for my other ones: I'll possibly be re-writing them from the beginning. Simply because I actually HAD gotten somewhere in my Megaman one, only to have lost the pages. My PPG-universe one... needs work. And I'm seriously rethinking where I want to take the DBZ one. Plus I'm dissatissfied with my formatting and writing in general. Actually check that, I'm reevaluating where I'm taking ANY of them, but don't worry... not much major is going to change, and not really anything that anybody's read yet anyways, so really my only reason to tell you, dear readers (I'm ignoring the not so dear readers, or the people that haven't even read my stuff) is to taunt your curiosity... mwa ha ha...

That is, if anyone's left... oh well...

Anyways, that's haps yo. Catch y'all later. Word.
