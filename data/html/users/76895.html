25-August-2011 I'm not dead!

...Okay, fine, in terms of writing fanfiction, I'm pretty dead. I have been for a while. Sorry, y'all. D: I'm just going to quietly remove outdated information from here.

For those wondering about Reclaiming Honor, I hate to say it, but it's probably discontinued. I have about half of the next chapter written, and I'm... not satisfied in the least with the direction it was going in. I'm so sorry! I would like to say that it'll be finished someday, but "someday" looks pretty far away at this point. That's not to say it's impossible, just... very improbable. (Not to mention, completing it would require a boatload of original characters, and I don't really want them to take the spotlight off of the main cast.)

That said, I'm trying to get back into writing again, so we'll see what happens!

I'll more than likely be posting old drabbles, just to have them archived somewhere other than Livejournal.

If you're looking for me on Tumblr, you can find me here.
