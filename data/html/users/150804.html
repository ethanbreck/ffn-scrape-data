Updates

November 19, 2018 - Hi guys! So, the past few months have been a crap-load of bad, but things are finally starting to look up a bit. I'm on a new medication that's been really helping me fight back my depression, and though I still have equal amount of bad days as good days, on the good days I feel like me again, so I race to write as much as possible before my mood inevitably tanks. On so-so days I edit old works, so that you guys can have a reading experience that is free from odd grammar and spelling errors.

With regards to my stories, my current goal is to finish An Inch of Gold by the end of the year. There are only a few chapters left, so with luck and a few up days, I'll get that done. Then I'll decide which project to tackle next.

As always, your support means the world to me, whatever form that takes, and thanks so much for reading my fics!

XOXO
