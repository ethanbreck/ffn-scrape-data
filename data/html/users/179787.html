Author has written 4 stories for Lord of the Rings, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Sweeney Todd.

I've been a fanfic writer on hiatus for too many years. I've written in Lord of the Rings (here) and in the printed past, UNCLE, Alias Smith and Jones, Adderly, Airwolf, and many crossovers. Crossovers include Night Court/Beauty and the Beast, Adderly/Stingray, Stingray/The Shadow, Airwolf/Dangermouse and more. Yes, I like unique crossovers that others wouldn't think of.

Current fandoms include: Fullmetal Alchemist, Doc Savage/The Shadow, Gargoyles, Sweeney Todd, Lord of the Rings

After having writers block for too many years, it's finally been broken by a razor wielded by Sweeney Todd. I love the stage versions and the movie (two different creatures, admittedly), and my favorite Sweeney so far is George Hearn (check out Sweeney Todd in Concert for a fantastic rendition) - I got the chance to meet him in NY when he was in the play Dracula! Although Johnny Depp does look nice as Sweeney... And Len Cariou still has a fantastic voice (I saw him in Canada last year).

Stories include:

Lord of the Rings:

Beachside
Merry, Pippin, Frodo and Sam, on the beach in Dol Amroth, reflect on the War of the Rings and their return home...

Remembering the Prankster
Years after the War of the Ring, Aragorn, Sam, Merry, and Pippin remember Middle-Earth's best prankster... Frodo Baggins.

Sweeney Todd

Dead Man's Razor
Sweeney Todd finds himself in a place that he'd never expected he would go - the supernatural ship of the dead, the Flying Dutchman.

The Other Anthony
Sweeney Todd had his Epiphany, and his barber chair was nearly finished. He was ready to be a Demon Barber... and then someone else with issues against Judge Turpin showed up in his shop.

My writing partner and collaborator on the Other Anthony is DorisTheYounger, who writes wonderful LOTR stories. I've even gotten her to write a few Sweeney Todd stories on her own (heh, heh). Check out her stuff!
