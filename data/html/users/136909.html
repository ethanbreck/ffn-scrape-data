Author has written 3 stories for Song of the Lioness, Recess, and Harry Potter.
Has anyone else noticed that ff.net seems to be scrounging up more and more reasons for people to move their stories elsewhere? If I had the time (or the effort), I would do so, but since I don't, here my stories remain. First of all, I would like to apologize for the disgusting delay of updating by yours truly, but when real life takes over, well...it just takes over.

Stories:

ANYTHING BUT ORDINARY
Part One of a Trilogy
T.J., Spinelli, Gus, Gretchen, Mikey, and Vince are all in their 6th and final year at 3rd Street Elementary, and when three boys show up in their small town, disaster ensues. Friendships are torn, trust is broken, and the future of playground fun is in danger. *I swear I'll update this soon. Really.*

MIDSUMMER'S DANCE
An AU fic based on the Protector of the Small series by Tamora Pierce. Keladry and Cleon find themselves in a bit of a pickle, and have to sort out their feelings and the mess they've made before the end of summer. *Slowly working my way through the next chapter.*

WASH IT ALL AWAY:
This might just be a one chapter story. Ginny is visited in the night from a man she loves, but cannot be with. Influenced by the lyrics of Evanescence, it's pretty dark and depressing, but I tried to make it as sensual as I could.

Stories currently brewing:

LIZZIE MCGUIRE:
Untitled, as of yet, and only the basic outline of the first two chapters has been semi-completed. It's a continuation of the movie, since I can't seem to find one that fits quite right. All the other stories out there are either too far fetched, not written well enough, or are entirely OOC (out of character, for all you non-internet-literate individuals). I'm hoping for this story to be my best, but time will tell...

HARRY POTTER:
A friend and I are starting a crazy, twisted, totally awesome Harry Potter story in which...no, I'm not going to tell you. But it's gonna be great. Draco is a beast. A sex-god beast.
