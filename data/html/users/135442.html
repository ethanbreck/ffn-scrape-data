Follow . Favorite
Joined 12-06-01, id: 135442
Author has written 5 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Harry Potter.
Suffice to say, I was born in the sixties but really don't remember them. I have one husband, three children, a dog and an addiction to the written word. In an effort to cut down on the book bill, I turned to fanfiction, and developed my first real addiction. (After all, chocolate doesn't really count.)

And so I began to write. Given my fascination for vampires and all things dark, Buffy the Vampire Slayer was my first metier, but I have recently forayed into the world of Harry Potter and am enjoying it immensely. So many of the themes are the same, after all.

QUOTE:

"Why do writers write? Because it isn't there."
- Thomas Berger
