January 19, 2014

Hello again, if anyone is still out there. Judging by the sporadic e-mails of stories added to favorites and watch list I'm guessing I must still have a few readers out there in orbit. You probably don't care much about my personal life but I have graduated nursing school and am recently working as an RN. In short, I have time on my hands occasionally now that I haven't while I've been in college and studying for my license exam. I'm in the process of applying to get my Bachelor's degree, so that free time may be short-lived.

My goal for 2014 is to pick up the fragments of stories I have in progress and finally complete them. I have many active ideas to continue some of my plots. I think The Secret Language will be the hardest for me, but I aim to try. Call it my New Year's resolution.

As always, I love hearing from you guys! My homepage is my facebook. Due to some recent issues, mostly familial, my profile is now private but I can still receive messages and friend requests from everyone. I'm also open for e-mails and private messages if you want to prod me on updating one of my stories, even by giving some suggestions. :p

Thank you for the support!
