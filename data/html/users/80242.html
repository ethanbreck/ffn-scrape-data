Wheeee!! I finally decided to update my bio.

Hi! I'm Laguna's Twin sister... because... I am Laguna Loire's twin sister!! haha. (I wish. -sigh- ... the woes of an only child...) Besides having the same birthday, I think we also share a lot of personality traits. But mind you, we have differences too. Mwahahaha. (I think I may be smarter... but then... that's just me. That might not be true...)

Nothing much changed. I still think it's my glorious mission to forever write about minor/seemingly invisible/very neglected characters... though now i think i know why. It's because it's fun to try to explore their character/or even try to figure out how to make them "deeper" and more dynamic.

Note to self:write about important (not that the minor guys aren't important) people...

But then, I'm also having a hard time writing about cannon pairings. Well... I suppose it's because I don't like to go with the flow most of the time.

My friends tell me I have only have two formulas for writing mushy fics (mushy fics are only fics I can semi-successfully pull off btw...)

1) Painfully and excruciatingly mushy story of an unrequited love that bloomed

2) an unlikely couple suddenly ending up together.

Someday, I would like to broaden my horizons and actually write using another mushy fic formula. Someday, I also wish i could learn to write faster! God. It takes me years to finish my fics. I'm really sorry for that.

Anyway...

-sigh- too much to do, too little time.

Hahaha! Please enjoy my fics. And if they suck, please tell me. I love to hear from you. I'm STILL an amateur (don't think i'll ever be good enough...) and well... suggestions and criticisms help me and stuff. So there. thanks:)
