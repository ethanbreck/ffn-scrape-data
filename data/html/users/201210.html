Author has written 17 stories for Lord of the Rings, A-Team, Master and Commander, King Arthur, and Starsky and Hutch.

Hello all,

I am back to writing, though I cannot commit to finishing old work I will say there will be some new pieces. I've grown a lot in the last decade and I want to put some of that into my fanfic and this has been my number one means of escape for years!

I'm married now and have a daughter of two months :) I imagine this will shape my writing style and of course slow me down sometimes.

I will most likely be writing mostly Tolkien fic. I admire him greatly and so hope he would not mind me using his beautiful creations to amuse my soul. His writings have been an obsession of mine for over 10 years. My dearest friend and I were Tolkien fanatics. We lived and breathed his work. She recently passed in a car accident. If you read any of my Sam and Frodo pieces, they are all based on the level of intimate friendship that existed between us. We embodied those two characters. Losing her has been my greatest trial in life.

Now if only to satisfy my own heart, I will write to free my mind of my burdens and to release joy in word.

For anyone from my past or anyone interested in older work for any reason here is my old LJ, who knows when they will delete lol!
