Author has written 17 stories for Lord of the Rings, Inuyasha, and Pokémon.

"Et sic itur ad astra - reach for the stars."

Alternative name: Antarel (may be used in conjunction with Nefertili)

A melancholic. Facial expression may not show what lies within. History addict. Has eaten frogs. Proudly bilingual. Normally a sideline watcher, but could apply Sun Tzu's "Art of War" if provoked. A survivor of the white plague. Low recruit rate, but has the Nontraitor and Bodyguard IQs right off the bat (think Pokémon Mystery Dungeon here). May be sweet. May be vengeful. Take your pick.

Finished Collaborations:

A Lengthening of Shadows (Pokémon, collab with Chaos Githzerai): Shadows do not lengthen without a reason. In the Reverse, there was an era when the Renegade did not exist alone.
