Author has written 12 stories for Harry Potter, and Lord of the Rings.
As you can tell by my pen name a have a soft spot for a certain potions professor. However, I'm a Gryffindor at heart. Actually, if I were sorted, I would probably end up in Ravenclaw because I'm too lazy for Hufflepuff, to honest for Slytherin, and too chicken for Gryffindor. But whateva. I'm a 17 year old American from FLA. Besides writing, I love music. I play the flute in band.

I love LOTR and Harry Potter. My fave HP character are Snape, Ron, Sirius and a sudden strange obsession with Draco (he's totally hot). My fave LOTR characters are Elrond, Frodo and Eowyn. :D

My partner in crime, WaNdA and I have started a fanfiction site at the the URL in this bio. Come check it out and submit some of your work. :D

I would like to say that since there are over 20000 stories poems and such on this site that I could not possibly have read them all. If I steal your idea I am very sorry and did not mean to. I will be very careful not to do this.

Also, my stories are very precious to me. It hurts when someone is exceptionally mean to them. If you have nothing kind or constructive to say, please don't. I'll show you that same respect. Thanks so much :o)

If you'd like to contact me or ask a question, just send me mail at serioussirius@yahoo.com, and I'll get back to you.
Thanks all, and happy reading and writing

~The Potions Mistress~*
