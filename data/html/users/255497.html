Hello. My name is Keba. Since you are reading my profile, you probably want to know something about me and the fics I write. Well, I won't disappoint you, then. Here we go.

Let's kick things off with some quick stats!
Name: Keba Si Rota (You think I'd give my birth name? Heck no!)
Sex: Female
Age: I know I shouldn't give my exact age on the Net, but let's just say I'm a legal adult according to USA law and I've been so for a while.
Height: 5ft 3in
Weight: Weighty (In other words, I should lose a few pounds.)
Hair: Brown and curly
Eyes: My left eye is blue and my right eye is hazel. Freaky, huh?
Family: I still live with my parents. I also have a brother who's away at college.
Location: Just north of Chicago, IL

Information on my fan fics (in order of when I posted them):
Science Fun with Lanky Kong! is my first fic on this site. It's based on the characters of Donkey Kong 64, even though it's in the Donkey Kong Country section (it was the closest they had to DK64). I originally wrote the story in script form when I was 12 or 13. I then typed it out in story form and posted it.

Pokemon Red Series was based on a bunch of stories that I had my Pokemon toys act out. I then wrote a few of them down and I eventually turned the first story (the first chapter) into a manga that I wrote when I was 14 and finished when I was 16. Years later, I thought I'd turn it into a story in the hopes that people would enjoy it, but it was not meant to be. My first review was from a stupid kid who flamed me. I didn't take it well, and I didn't post any new material on this site for a year in the fear of getting other flames. I know the story isn't complete, but it probably won't ever be. I ran out of good ideas, and ever since I got flamed, I've lost my passion for writing this story.

NINJA BATTLE (of the Bands) is my first Naruto fic ever. It's a story about Naruto starting up a rock band. So far, it's my most popular story. It's complete, and I'm very proud of it.

Here are some random things about me!
I like anime, but I wouldn't consider myself an anime junkie. I mostly just focus on Naruto. I've been watching the original version almost religiously. And, I have this adorable plushie of Naruto as a kitsune.

I used to really be into video games, but I'm not so much anymore. The only one I still like to play is Super Smash Bros. Melee. In fact, I am a member of the SSBECF (Super Smash Bros. Escapee Control Faction). To find out what that is, go to my Favorite Stories page and read Smashing Days the Super Smash Bros. Way. The author, Cali IX, was really kind to write me into the story (along with about ten other people).

I would like to take this moment to talk about my favorite thing in the whole world. That would be rock music. I've considered myself a rocker for about four years, now. The bands I like range from pretty current to over 20 years old (who still manage to stay relevant after all these years). I won't name any names (at least, not yet) because you are going to think, "Keba likes THEM? How old is she? Five? She should listen to death metal and grunge." (For the record, those are the sub-genres of rock that I'm not too keen on.) Still, I'll try to wiggle in references to some of the bands I like in that Naruto fic, but I'll make references to bands and songs that I'm not really into as well.

Other things I like:
1. Singing. I want to be a singer. I'm in a band, well, it's just me and a guitarist. I'm trying to look for more members. I've been looking for almost four years now, and I haven't found any.
2. Writing songs. Hey, if you're in a band, you gotta write your own songs to have credibility. Too bad I'm not good at writing parts for instruments. But I do have a way with lyrics.
3. Drawing. I always had a talent for drawing. I'm not the best, but I've improved a lot this year. I draw for fun. I don't really think I'm going to make a career out of it, though.
4. Making websites. I have two websites and three blogs. The sites feature my art, mostly. My first site is called KAAMP (Keba's Anime and Manga Place), but I haven't done much to that one recently. The other site deals with my obsession for my favorite band-- with an anime twist. My blogs are just about my random ramblings and opinions, but hey, isn't that what blogs are for? I hate to say it, but I don't have many other interests.

Thank you for reading all this. If your eyes haven't started bleeding from reading my profile, please read my stories. However, if they did bleed, you might want to seek medical attention. After that, THEN you can read my stories.
