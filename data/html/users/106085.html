Well, I'm back.

I just want to send a shout out to the producers of JAG for being AWESOME! I saw the episode where they basically sent their encouragement to facfiction writers everywhere. I was jumping around doing the caldroun going "Uh huh, Yeah, They know it, Oh yeah." It's been no secret that they've view OUR fanfics for ideas for the show. And that's cool. They know what viewers want to see that way. I think any of us would be proud to have our ideas used. We're not just nerds with too much time. We're writers.

It's this exact reason that I want to tell JK Rowlings that she can KISS MY LILLY WHITE ASS! We know that you use our ideas. Then you turn around and bitch that we're writing them. It's not our fault that you're washed up. It's not our fault that cash was more important then quality. It's not our problem that your writing alone lacks substance and luster. If you're going to use our ideas, don't bitch about us writing them. Otherwise you are just STEALING them. We write disclaimers on every fic so YOU don't get offended. BACK OFF!

Peace Out!

~Ria~
