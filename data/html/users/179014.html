Where my inspiration comes from: my brain. duh! lol. I also get alot of inspiration from the way M. Night Shyamalan directs his movies (the guy who did Sixth Sense, Unbreakable, Signs, and The Village). His movies always feel like real life just with a supernatural twist. I like that. -My stories-

I suppose I am an angst writer, who loves having unhappy endings. My stories usually are a tad more dark and emotional compared to the Norm, but occasionally can end on a happy bit. Lately I have been into one-shots and have just completed three: “Under the Crescent Moon,” which my inspiration was from an episode from Futurma, “Bitter Hot Chocolate,” which is my New Year’s Eve fic, and "Heart Beats," a story based off the poem “Sleeper in the Valley” by Arthur Rimbaud and the the line “pride will tear us both apart”, from the song "Ordinary World" by Duran Duran. An idea i have had for a long time would possible feature Trowa and Usagi. This one would be a spin off of “Porphyria's Lover” by Browning.

Dark Playground and TIWCL have been put on hold until i actually have the time and creative energy to add a chapter. school is killing me. I assure you that DP will be continued because I myself have no clue where that story will be going next, and that is what keeps me interested. As for TIWCL… I am not as sure because I have already thought about every single chapter months ago. If anyone is still interested in that story, just to let you know, I only have 4 more chapters to write and then post my long-ago written conclusion and the story will be done. On December 12, 2003, and also on October 15, 2004, i removed my three earliest works- "For Away So Close," "Safire," and the "Bunny in the Shadows." Reason being is that i never completed those stories, and have grown as an author since then.

Last updated: October 15, 2004
