I started writing NSYNC fanfiction back in freshman year of college (and every day, I try not to be ashamed of that, lol). Probably because I like to have my story completely written before posting anything, and so there are like 5 incomplete stories just sitting in my documents folder, lol. Well evidently my babbling quota is up for today, so I'll stop here :)

I am currently uploading a General Hospital fic that I completed in 2005 and outlining a Twilight story for the future as a way of distracting myself from the original novels I should be working on...

Some things you should know about me: In my LnL2 fics, Lucky is always Greg Vaughan. I have a weakness for Jacob Black, Emmett Cullen, and Jasper Whitlock (wait, is that basically everyone but Edward Cullen? It is, isn't it?), and I tend to root for the underdog. I'm disappointed a lot in novels and TV, so I end up needing to "fix" them by writing fiction. You've been warned. :)
