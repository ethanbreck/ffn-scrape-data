Hello there-

I've been a member of FF.net since 2002 and I've been writing for Harry Potter for awhile... I just don't like to put my work out there unless I feel is doesn't completely suck. Almost every story I've ever written has been DM/HG but I'm in the middle of a story collection with all kinds of fun ships... other than the Lord of the Rings stuff I dabbled in, but none of that was any good. I just started reading 100 fanfiction... and I'm a little obsessed, so I wanted to try an idea I had.

I enjoy reading everything I can get my hands on... and when I run out of hard and paperback books I run to the realms of fanfiction. I just love the imagination that people can utilize!

As of now I have a couple of stories that I have and am working on...

1)The One I'm Waiting For
This is a one-shot about Ron Weasley and Pansy Parkinson. Honestly I'm not a big Ronald fan... but I was listening to Relient K's song and this entire story line just came to me, and when inspiration knocks... I don't ignore it. The actual story is about Ron's rejection by Hermione and Pansy's desperate want to be rid of Blaise. In a hurried decision she uses Ronald as a way to escape... and in the end, well you'll have to read it now won't you!

2)My Story: Hermione Granger's Side
This is my only story that is written in the point of view of the main character. It is currently a that is under some severe editing so be forewarned. This story is probably as dark as I've ever written and it has it's moments of greatness and also some moments of shame. It is a story written from Hermione's point of view as she dictates to someone who is writing the book My Story: Hermione Granger's Side in a futuristic world where Voldemort rules. Throughout the story Hermione explains every action she can think of and everything that was ever done to her by Voldemort.

3)Starting Over
Modern AU. Clarke's mother just moved her 2000 miles across the country from Phoenix to a backwater Virginia town. She needs to forget so she uses a fake I.D. to drink away her problems.

I hope you all love it!
