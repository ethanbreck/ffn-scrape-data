July 13th, 2015
I really, really suck at writing. Mostly only when the thought strikes me. Strangely it struck me twice this year. This little Inuyasha drabble and I hope another NCIS one as well. As everyone can tell I do short bursts of humor. Enjoy the ride and thanks for the reviews.

November 20, 2009
Unemployment sucks especially so close to the holidays. I am getting sidetracked by real life and working on refreshing my SQL and some programming to make myself more presentable. Ick, I hate programming. I don't want to work on getting certified with Cisco because I really hate Network Engineering (and as a lil' girl I can't fathom lifting one of those Cisco routers much less one of those huge Sun Servers) but I know some of it working at my last job sigh. I hate the telecommunications industry. Oh well, the NCIS/Dark Angel story is still in progress and I'm stuck. I had to create a quick one-shot for two of my favorite shows Bones and NCIS, enjoy and hope that tides some of you over, lol.

Update I renamed the NCIS/Dark Angel story Reflections, if anyone disagrees with the name change or can think of a better onejust shoot me a message.

September 9, 2009
Sorry for the delay it's been a hectic month. Well it's final, at the end of this month I will be without a job. Constantly looking and worrying so I can't write. If anything I will have lots of time to write next month. I got the story partially done. Chapter 3 is now at 40 but I won't be able to work on it until after the end of this month.

August 20, 2009
I had intended to have chapter 3 out by today but I am having a tough time with some of the finer details and the story is stuck at 30 complete. Oddly chapter two came rushing out which was surprising. I am off on Monday and hopefully my brain can complete the chapter with a bit of rattling and shaking.

August 16, 2009
Wow it's been a long time since I have written anything. This one came to me like my last one for InuYasha, it hit me from above and it kinda hurt. I am having a tough time with chapter three but four and five are coming in nicely. Recommendations are welcome. I don't think I am a good writer just a passable one so good critiques are helpful.

December 15, 2008
New story up, to satisfy my love of the cutest pair of Inu Ears.

March 21, 2008
This Author lurks more than she writes. Unfortunately real life interferes too much with having fun and stories are on hiatus. I will try to update and write more, but it's kinda hard. Thanks for all those who enjoy my stories. I wish I could continue with more.
