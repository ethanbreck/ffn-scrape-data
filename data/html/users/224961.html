anyways.. i've started my first fic EVER! very exciting, well to me anyways.
It's gonna take me a looooong time to write. For two reasons:
firstly the altered timeline I've thought up goes onto the majin buu saga, and at the moment I'm only writing at the beginning of the androids saga... so like i said, this could take awhile.
secondly... i had this idea during a particularly boring Latin mock exam, back in January, and i've only just got the prologue into the sorta shape i'll let other people read!

I'm a perfectionist, so sue me! But i warn u, all i've got is a failed latin exam and some fluff from my pockets, so u won't exactly be winning a lot!

anyways, i really should get back to writing my story! If u have a chance, take a look at some of my favourite stories and authors. There are some really talented people in that list and i can whole-heartedly recommend them to anyone looking for some slash-tasticness!

Ja ne!

WOW! it's been ages since i updated this thing! I kinda like living...

Anyways, i've finally started my first fic, am waiting to upload the second chapter and have got 90 percent of the third chapter finished... meaning it should be up around September i suppose:Ducks Sporks: er... heh -

My little brain is teeming with weird and wonderful ideas of how to get our boys together... there's one involving early 19th century asian pirates, one about bounty hunters, one where Goku's a vicar, One where he's a fireman (okay, these two don't have much in the way of plot... i just wanted to see the big lug in a dog's collar or in the fireman's calendar hentai grin) and loads more!
