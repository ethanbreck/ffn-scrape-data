Author has written 34 stories for Mighty Ducks, Scooby Doo, Mechwarrior/Battletech, Neopets, Harry Potter, One Must Fall, National Treasure, Blue's Clues, and Voltron.

Profiling? No, no profiling. Profiling is illegal.

Oh, wait... nevermind.

Aqua has all kinds of ADD, no ability to write summaries, even less ability to write profiles, and a creative writing degree. Oh, and a tendency to refer to herself in third person when she's feeling awkward. Hopefully the stories are interesting enough to make up for the fact that the person writing them isn't all that interesting.

I don't ship, much, though romance bunnies will occasionally bite me. Pidge/Larmina is my OTP. (I think I may have been the first one there. Someone had to run with that beautiful beautiful ship!) Also Velma/Joe. Yes, Velma from Scooby Doo. Yes, Joe from Blue's Clues. Don't judge me.

Proud to wield the Green Pen of the Snarktron Force! Mangle canon, and I will bury you in details. Mangle the English language, and I will NAIL YOU TO A WALL.
...No, I'm really not that scary, honest.
