Vampire's Family definitions: ('cuz I promised them)(The new ones are marked)

Vampire King: The one who rules over the vampires.
Humans: Normal race that is often weaker than the vampires. They maintain a non-hostile relationship with the vampires and some live together, be it through Hosting or under the watch of the Defense Team.
Family: Members that follow a specific vampire (often called ‘Captain’), trusting their lives to the vampire and vice versa. There can be different reasons as to why someone becomes family. Family is needed by the Candidates who want to be the successor.
Candidates: Vampires who joined the competition to become the successor of the Vampire King.
Competition: An actual competition between one vampire’s family and the King’s family. Only the one who wins against the King’s family can be the successor to the throne. Sometimes referred to as the Race for the Crown.
Family Candidates: Humans that can become family members.
Defense Team: A team made up by special individuals that reside in almost every town, composed of students from a school or normal residents. They make sure that local or visiting vampires are kept in check. All visiting vampires must check in with the Defense Team so they can be accepted in the town.
Japanese: Vampires normally talk in a street accent, but those of nobility will often use higher status words when referring to themselves. In Luffy’s case, he reverts back to it when he’s surprised or excited.
