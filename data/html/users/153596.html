Let's see, I'm relatively new to the realm of writing fanfiction... However, I'm not very new to reading it. I'll probably have one up one of these days if I ever get enough time to actually sit down and write one... or if something is just that inspiring.

I come on here mainly for shoujo-ai pairings such as: Rei/Usagi, Rei/Minako (PGSM), Mirelle/Kirika, Yoruichi/Soi Fong, Sakura/Tomoyo, Fate/Nanoha, Lightning/Fang... though I do read straight pairings depending on the anime.

I like to play lots of video games primarily on my XBox360, (former) Nintendo DS, or play Beatmania IIDX. I also watch random anime and read random manga. By random, I mean that I've been picking up series from years ago up to following some of the current season's anime/manga episode by episode or chapter by chapter.

Sure, there's more about me than what meets the eye on this profile, but the rest of it would be better chatted in person. Drop an e-mail if you'd like to say hello. :)
