Next Update: Not a clue...

Here's the deal. I hardly write anymore. Well... that's not entirely accurate. I write lots of poetry and original stuff; I don't write much fanfiction at this point. I will try to finish what I have. I really will. Of course, I don't foresee that happening in the near future, but that's okay.

Another point. I don't really write slash anymore, although that's what I started out with. I'm going to finish my stories and leave them up for an indefinite time. Then they will be gone. Gone gone gone.

I've recently gotten back into RPG fiction, so I might write something for Final Fantasy 8, X, or X-2. Watch for that if you've a mind to.

Oh yes. One final note. Please don't flame me. I don't like flames. I find them highly distasteful and more embarrassing for the flamer than the one being flamed. Use constructive criticism and more people will be happy. ;)
