My name is Anthony and I'm still a fan of video games and fanfics, along with certain movies. Too many guest reviews are just scumbags, hiding behind the veil of anonymity. Racist, sexist, judgmental, homophobic trash. And even the ones who aren't that bad are generally jack@$*es, too difficult to deal with. :-/ If you really want to comment, please take the few minutes to sign up for a free account and comment with that. Really, it's the least you could do. :-) If you don't like that, then take action. Tell Fanfiction.net to kick out the jack@$*es and trolls from here. Tell them you'll leave unless the option to disable anonymous reviews is restored. Do something. Anything! Even constructive criticism isn't worthy to leave anymore in an anonymous review, because it's often twisted into the harshest of criticisms, most of the time having nothing to do with the story. :-/ Sorry, I sympathize, because I've left a few anonymous reviews myself. I know what it's like to have the veil of anonymity behind which you have the freedom to speak your mind, without pressure or going through the trouble of joining a new site. This is the way it has to be. Unfortunately, far too many people have misused this gift. :( I hope they all get lost.
