I am obsessed with so many things that I'm just going to list them, and hope I remember them all. X-Men, Star Wars, Elfquest, Pern, fantasy (of all kinds), dinosaurs, Dinotopia, Nightworld, Gargoyles, Tamora Pierce, Lord of the Rings, comics/superheros in general (ASTRO CITY RULES!!), Animal Planet (the channel), Mercedes Lackey (wonderful author, read), sci-fi, reading, writing, reading fanfics, and *writing* fanfics! In fact, I like writing fanfics so much I'm writing one on a subject I don't know anything about - heh . . . ANYWAY!

-My fav X-Men character is Nightcrawler, Kurt Wagner, as you can probably tell by my pen name! I am an ENSA (elfaholic-NOT-so-anonmyous), and proud of it!

I'm also into showing things from the important but oft-left-out characters POV, so some of my stories ought to prove interesting. For example, I wrote the first gryphon-based fic in the Mercedes Lackey section, and proud of THAT, too! I also wrote Neville in as a MC in my HP fic NIGHTMARES.

Speaking of Nightmares - it's going to be right where it is for quite a while. I'm working on a few too many things, and if I try to write any more now, it'll be really bad. It will be finished (I already wrote the end! I just don't know how to get there . . .) I might just post the end and let you people figure out how it got there.

For SW people out there - Rally with me, my fellows! I'm boycotting every New Jedi Order book until they bring Anakin back! Star By Star was a culmination of everything Star Wars DOESN'T stand for! When did they start killing the best characters, anyway? I know that people kill characters to keep a series alive, but with the death of Anakin Solo, they've gone and killed it. Anyway. Done ranting (but not done fuming)

For any and everyone who reads my stuff . . . A thousand apologies for my months-long silence. Unfortunately, I am not planning on doing much of anything in the fanfic community anytime soon. Ideas still come, but I’m mostly writing original stuff now . . . If I ever actually get my novel published, I’ll write it in big letters in my bio. Other than that, I resign all my unfinished fics. If anyone feels like continuing them, e-mail me and ask. Sorry, and my apologies to anyone who still cares. Fanfic rocks, but writing it regularly takes too much time away from MY writing. For those still out there writing - best of luck, and I’ll read anything good I can get my hands on!
-Righto, slight amendment to that - I'm writing SOME things. Just not the pile I WAS writing. And you never can tell what I'll update . . .

Also: If anyone knows a site where I can post plot-bunnies for other people to write, TELL ME! I would love to see some of my ideas written, even if it’s not by me.

Note for those who care: the homepage listed above is a bit of a half-truth. I have fanfic archived there, and I like it b/c it's a freer version of ffn. Check it out! Sign up! Spread the word!

Please review, and do check up on my fav stories/authors! We can always use the encouragement!
