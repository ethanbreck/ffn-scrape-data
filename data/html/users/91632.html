Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter.

Lavender is a twenty-year-old procrastinator by nature. Because of this, she often is notorious for capturing plot bunnies, but never showing them off. She vows to try to do better at that.

She is a List Mum (along with Zetta) at the Lazy MWPP Writer's Unite Yahoo Group (click the link above. The list is great, she swears, but says that there is often little activity because of the procrastinating nature of members).

She is obsessed with Scotland, Young Adult fiction, The Office (best TV show EVER), ghosts, occasionally vampires (when they come in the form of Stephenie Meyer's Twilight Series), and music - of which her tastes seem to vary on either extreme: Progressive/Industrial Metal (Nine Inch Nails is her favorite) or Neo-Celtic (i.e. Loreena McKennitt, and a band she discovered mere days ago Omnia). She will occasionally listen to Emo and Punk, but she will never admit it. Don't tell anyone.

When she is not at her day job, she is busy writing down plot bunnies for both original stories and fanfics (and then being too lazy to expand them), reading, or desperately trying to convince her husband to move to Scotland (she hasn't had any luck yet).

If Lavender ever gets around to posting anything new, please be nice to her. She's a bit rusty, and Sirius is still not talking to her for abandoning him with the way things were left in Falling Into Grace. Remus is being nice, though, and trying to convince Sirius that if he talks to Lavender again, she just may be able to redeem him...

Happy reading to all!

(November 3, 2006)
