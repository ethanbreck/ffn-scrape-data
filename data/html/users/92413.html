I hate my fics and they hate me too. Then I love them and they love me back.

If you have to flame, go for the whole thing! I won't flame (unless you write crap with mary sues and deserve to be flamed).

I like football. I support Liverpool FC. I wonder if people do football fanfiction...ergh. Oh gee it must be coincidence that I also happen to love talking. I would like to do as much of it as i can before I die. I can talk to anything as long as it doesn't move away. Just don't put me next to a dead person.

Oh yes, my earlier fics make me cringe. I do not dare to read them again.

Cheers!
