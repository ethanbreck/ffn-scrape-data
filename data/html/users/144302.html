... time for a change ...

had always loved fanfiction ... had always been an ardent fanfiction reader ... yes, just as a reader and nothing else ... first ficcie that i fell in love with? *These Changing Times by Sakka-chan* of course! It was back in year 2000 when i was browsing the rk reader's choice awards ... and i was totally enthralled by the way she writes ...

best ficcie ever read till date? *Overture by Yui-Seta* i am no rukawa-fan ... at least not until i finished reading that fic ... it was great! the characterization ... the plot ... the storyline ... the way the story started ... the way the story ended ... the cherry tree ... the wheelchair ... just PERFECT ... i have never read a story as beautiful as that ... SUGOI!
