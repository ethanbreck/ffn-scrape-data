EDIT: This is a random update after years of nothing. I am not writing fan fiction to this account anymore, or even posting anything at all. I just don't have enough time to write full stories to post. What I do write nowadays are just random plotsand ideas that pop into my head, so nothing long enough to be afull story. Thank you to those who actually read the ones here. If you did happen to write an MST of, or wish to MST, one of my stories, use my new email address to either ask me permission to write the MST, or better yet, just write it and send me the fic! You'll make my day. :)

Anyone who has sent me reviews, or questions, by email in the past two or three years, I haven't used that accountin that time, and haven't seen your email. Please resend it to the new address if you wish.

Maybe in the future I will post new stories to a different account. I won't take down the stories posted here. Even though I do not like them that much anymore, (except Maxi Predacon), they are a part of my past history as a fan fiction writer, and will remain up. Especially because some people might still enjoy reading them, and there have been many times where an author takes down a story that he/she does not like anymore, and I still do.

I still enjoy reading fan fiction, so I'll be around. Justindefinately not posting. So long, and thanks for all the fish. :)
