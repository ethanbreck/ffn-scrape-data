Hi! I'm Tam.

I'm a mother, and a novelist. I play around with fanfic for fun, to get my creative juices flowing. I love feedback from my readers...and I kinda crave the attention. I missed it while I was gone.

Please be kind, and be patient. If I'm not updating a fanfic you are itching to devour, I might be busy writing something original future publication.

I hope you enjoy what I write. Sometimes I write just for fun, and sometimes I write in hopes of making people think. In any case, if you don't like something I've written, you won't hurt my feelings if you close the story and don't come back. I understand. It might be me, it might be just that story, but don't ever feel obligated to stick around longer than your enjoyment lasts. If you tell me, politely, why you had to leave, I'll even see if I can use what you've said to improve my writing skill.

Thank you for your interest in my favorite playground. Words.

p.s.

If you are looking for the missing part of Not Human, or anything else of "adult" nature, it is posted on AO3 now. (July of '18)

p.p.s.
