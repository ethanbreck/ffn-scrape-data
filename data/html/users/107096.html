5/30/2011

So apparently soon is five months? But the next two chapters are up. They have been read through, but I don't have a beta. So I want to declare posting for Soul Separation officially on Hiatus. This does not mean that i am not writing on it. I just know how frustrating a work in progress is, so I'm going to finish writing it, no matter how long it takes, and then post. So thank you for any reviews you see fit to leave, they always make my day better, and i will be back.

12/28/10

So, in the last few months I've had major failures in both my computer and my external hard drive where I had my chapters for Soul Separation stored. I've lost everything but the chapters posted to here and my live journal. I've been considering just abandoning the story since so much of my work is now gone, but I have decided not to do that. I'm going to take this to mean that this story needs a re-vamp and am going to make some changes to be beginning chapters before going on to write the next few chapters. I will upload the next chapter as I write it to give you all something new to read since it has been so long, but then I am going to finish writing the story before I post another chapter. It might be a month or more before you see the next chapter and then months after that before I seriously begin posting again. I just wanted to let you know what's been going on with my writing and why I've been silent so long.
