I am the Maiden of Mayhem...yea pretty dorky name, I know. It's from middle school, as is this account. So here's a little bit about me, since you'll probably be seeing me a bit more...

Name: Hannah

Birthday: August 27

Age: 20

Profession: College student with a double major in English and Secondary Education. So, in reality...I'm poor and without a profession at all.

Hobbies: Reading, writing, sketching, architecture, photography, archery, horse back riding, firearms, and gum chewing. I am passably good at most of these and proficient only at the last.

Fanfiction: I constantly read Harry Potter fanfiction, but I also adore Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Inuyasha, Sailormoon, Howl's Moving Castle, Spirited Away, Lord of the Rings, and Twilight.
