I stopped writing fanfiction many, many years ago, and will in all likelihood never ever finish anything left incomplete here. Nevertheless, thanks for looking and I hope you derive some joy from the stuff I used to write as a teenager.

I can be found on Twitter these days as @gerjomarty.

Fanfics I Will Never Finish

Dark Light
Digimon. Post-02. The Digidestined are once again called to the Digital World to deal with the forces of darkness. This time, a huge area of the Digital World has been claimed by evil digimon and is protected by a huge forcefield. This time however, the only person who can defeat this threat is Kari. How? By using the power she fears the most - the power of darkness.

Strange World
Digimon. A strange fog descends over New York one night, and has a very strange mutating effect of the teenagers of the city. They become Digimorphs - humans with digimon attributes and features. The fic follows Tara and Liam, two seemingly ordinary teens who have been afflicted by the fog. But do these two have a hidden past? How are the adolescents of New York going to cope with having digimon powers? And just how are the ordinary humans going to take to their new neighbours?

The Unwritten Rules
Digimon. This short fic follows the feelings and emotions of a digimon who has just evolved. Ever wondered what it would feel like? How you would react? Or what about loading another digimon's data? Is it really as fulfilling as it seems to be? This is my take on what both reactions would feel like.

Home Away From Home?
Digimon. Picks up directly after Tai was sent home in the rift after the battle with Etemon. With the loss of their leader, the digidestined are a split group. Some are mourning the apparent 'loss' of Tai, while some have other issues on their mind. What would be going on inside the digidestined's minds at their lowest ebb in the DigiWorld?
