I'm wild again, beguiled again,
a whimpering, simpering child again.
Bewitched, bothered, and bewildered am I.
~Kurt Vonnegut.

Writing is art. Art is expression. Expression is the one thing we must never let go.
Live, love, laugh. It's the best you can hope for. Try to make a difference. Not in the grand scheme of things, but small scale.
Bake a cake for a friend. Take a walk, leave the car at home. Recycle.
We've reached a stage where we simply don't care any longer. We all know life's not short enough. Why do you think Western civilization has tried mass suicide not once, but *twice*. We failed in our first world war, and our second.
They always say the third time's a charm.

We are on Earth to fart around. Don't let anyone tell you different.

A pessimist? Of course I am. I never asked to be born in the first place.
I'm just doing my best, with what little I have.
Maybe it'll make you smile. Or laugh. Or think.

That's what I hope for.
