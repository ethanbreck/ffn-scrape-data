An Announcement 11/14:

After long consideration, I have decided to completely stop posting to ff.net. This is in reaction to the NC-17 ban they instituted, which, while completely within their rights, has some very upsetting implications. Though I do not write NC-17 material I see this issue as something that affects all fanfiction writers. And while I will not remove my works from this site, they will not be updated, either.

PoL will be updated regularly at my website, Schnoogle, and the PSA. The same goes for my shorter pieces (website, Fiction Alley, and the PSA). Eventually I will be posting at Snitchfiction.

In addition, my livejournal is the place to check for announcements on PoL, ficlet postings (sometimes exclusive-to-lj), and more.

Thanks for reading

~Ishuca
