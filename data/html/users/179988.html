Author has written 60 stories for Law and Order, Princess Diaries, Harry Potter, 24, Taggart, Downton Abbey, StarTrek: Voyager, and Game of Thrones.

Hello to all!

I've written stories since I can remember and I don't think I'm going to stop anytime soon. I'm an amateur and I do it because it brings me joy, simple as that.

At the moment I'm loving the Chelsie pairing in Downton Abbey and writing for them is a joy.

I've written for 24 (Bill/Karen) The Princess Diaries (Joe/Clarisse) Harry Potter (Dumbledore/McGonagall) and Taggart (Robbie/Jackie).

I'm a long-time fan of ST:VOY - Janeway/Chakotay were my first fanfic love and I spent most of my A-Level period writing them and reading others' work.

Thanks for reading and I hope you enjoy what you look at! xx Rhonda
