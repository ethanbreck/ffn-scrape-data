+Update+
Whoa... Look at FF.net'sscrewed up-ness. I hate "Easy Updating for the Critically Challenged". Learn some basic HTML people. It's honestly not that hard.

Alright, with that said, it's been forever since I've been here. The last time I actually updated a story was May 2003. All I have to say is, "I'm sorry". I know I have some faithful fans that want my stories to be updated (Esp my HP fic and Remorseful Eternity) but due to college life I just haven't been inspired to write, unlike my fellow writer friends; clearshadow and sharpsnout (Damn you two!). I don't have any inspiration for new stories or to even finish the ones that I have started. Every time I go to write, it comes out "blah". I'm hoping that my creative side has not complete lost me. I'd be so sad and will then realize in full that I have changed since HS. In HS I could whip out ideas in a moment! Nothing big or good but at least it was an idea. Now, nothing. Absoultly nothing.

Though, I still want to write, and I really want to get out the new fic out if I ever get it typed (Clothes Come Off?). It's a stupid fluff comedy fic but at least the Miroku enjoys it_~

I hope my inspiration returns to me. Possibly this summer since I'll actually have a computer in my room where I can actually type the random ideas when the flock to my brain instead of trying to hold on to them until morning (because it wasn't worth trying to boot up the slow old laptop). Well, that's it!

3Love to all! 3
