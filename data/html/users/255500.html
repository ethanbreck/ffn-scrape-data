Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter, Prison Break, Alias, and CSI: New York.

I've just started getting into writing again, so please be patient with me. While perusing some original works of fiction I'm also trying my hand at fanfiction again, mostly for fun and for practice. I've kept my older posts up, but look at them more as frozen moments in time, and hopefully not a reflection of how I write now. I couldn't bring myself to delete the older entries, no matter how painfully bad, because it felt too much like burning a time-capsule. For better or worse I wrote them and published them, where they will remain on fanfiction.net forever more.
