Author has written 10 stories for Final Fantasy VII.

It's been a while since I've had a chance to write. Life happened and the writing muse just took off for parts unknown. Currently I'm trying my best to coax her to come back home, and so am working my way up with little dribbles and drabbles and an occasional tiny ficlet here and there.

My main fandom is Gundam Wing, and I do have some GW stuff that I will be posting in the future. Recently, I've also discovered the world of Final Fantasy VII - I have finally succumbed to my obsession and bought not only a PSP (only with Crisis Core, of course), but also a PS3 (so I can play DoC and FFVII). I still tend to play most of my games on the PC, but these are a nice change.

I like the possibilities in both fandoms, because they have such great and varied characters and the stories leave so many questions open that might be answered by writing about them.

English is my second language, and most of these are not betaed, so if it sometimes sounds a bit strange to your ears, please be lenient. :)
