Hello! For anyone who remembers my work or is new to my stuff I want to let you know that I am writing and will be posting again soon. Its been a looooong time so my style is much different now then it was the, but I hope you will enjoy it. I will be posting two stories simultaneously and switching between them as fancy strikes. One will be a Lucky Star Fanfic and the other a Harry Potter fanfic. I do have several ideas in regards to my Waking Ami series but I need to know if people want it continued, convoluted plot holes, multiple crossovers, dated writing and all, or should I rewrite what I have into a shorter more coherent story and continue from there?

I Never-- A game of I Never leads Kagami and Misao to find that all of their friends have left them behind in the romance department and decide to catch up together. This is a Kagami/Misao/Hurt and Darkish Konato love triangle.

This will be a short story under 40K words I imagine. I finished the first quarter of it and have an outline for the rest.

Lunatic Lovegood Vs. The Basilisk!-- The wizarding world is rocked by the death of Harry Potter one year after his return to magic. Voldemort's horcrux leaves Harry unable to pass on to the afterlife. Luna Lovegood is about to start her first year at Hogwarts, never did she expect to do it with her soul merged with that of Harry Potter and the Dark Lord! This is a first year story so there will not be a romance but Luna Potter Marvolo Lovegood will have Sapphic leanings.

I have come to love the Potter Universe and this will be my epic works spanning hopefully six books. They will follow the adventures of Luna Lovegood and her quest to rule the wizarding world. I plan on Luna being morally ambiguous in this story, those of you familiar with D&D she would be Chaotic Neutral. Luna will not be fully Luna as her personality and power will be influenced both by Harry and Lord Voldemort.

I Really would like to find someone to Beta these stories and bounce ideas off of. Let me know if you are interested.
