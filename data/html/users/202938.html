Note on the Vignettes Series

These stories are all collections of vignettes from several different canons all centered around the same topic. Some have the possibility of being never-ending, while others like Bonnie's Eyes are most likely complete unless I get a huge burst of inspiration. I have written out plans for nearly 20 different themes to be covered, so they probably won't stop anytime soon. Hope you like them!
