Greetings, to all curious and intrigued readers and lovers of fanfiction who have managed to stumble upon my humble directory page. I am James Beston, greatly obsessed reader of Harry Potter (both the series and fanfiction). The best out of all seven books is either Azkaban or Deathly Hallows.

I have been reading the series nonstop ever since I started reading the first book, roughly four months after it came out. I give my cousin, whose name I shall leave anonymous for privacy, the ultimate thanks, for introducing me to the greatest series EVER! I spend every free minute at school, and some at home, on FF.Net reading and soon, writing fanfiction. The ONLY pairings I believe in, or will willingly read are in order of rightness:

Harry/Hermione

James/Lily

Ron/Luna

I suppose - Harry/Luna

And if written well - Harry/Ginny

Likewise - Ron/Hermione

Thank you my esteemed readers and fans. Any and all reviews are greatly appreciated. Don't feel obligated to review though if you don't want to.

Take care,

James
