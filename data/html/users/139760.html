Currently a lurking reader (not a writer), I'm still majorly into vampires - especially Twilight and True Blood, with some Vampire Diaries thrown in. It's a good time for vampire fans! However, it must be said: Buffy forever.

The following was written back in 2002 when I was ickle! I don't want to delete it. Funny how some things change yet some things stay the same...heh!

8

My name is...( dunh-dunh-DUNH! ) Jessie. It really is, and it's not short for Jessica either. I am also Buffy Anne Summers. I am fifteen in September... yes, I suppose I'm a bit young, but everyone always mistakes me for a college student the first time they meet me. I have green-blue hazel eyes and dark brown hair with highlights. I also live in sunny southern CA, like our favorite slayer. I was a cheerleader for 3 years... I've been acting, singing,and dancing since I was born... I have a blue belt (8/10) in Tae Kwon Do...I'm thinking of taking up Flamenco and Salsa dancing...I like Britney Spears, I really do, even though I understand the teasing. Her personal life is her own business - icluding her virginal status to her outfits. I say, hey, if you got it, flaunt it (if you want to,that is :)...I love music. I'm learning guitar, and I sing. SMG rocks the world - she and Freddie are SO cute!... HP CoS movie is gonna be so kick-ass, I can't wait... Buffy the Vampire Slayer has been my favorite show ever since it came out and I saw the very first episode! It's the bestest. Big BA shipper; BS is sweet, but I miss Angel and Buffy together. Angel's kind of a jerk right now, so I don't know how the heck that would work!! How could he like Cordy?! I write Buffy fanfic, but I Love Harry Potter too - my fave couple is Hr / Dr. I dunno why, maybe it's the whole badboy thing. I just think it's cool. I am also leaning towards H /Hr. ~ My webpage is not finished yet, by any means. My fiction there will be up soon, as well as the links to my sections. If anyone would like their fiction on it, e-mail me and we'll talk! :) I love living in Los Angeles. I really do. Anyways...I am writing a HP / Buffy crossover!! It's up, and I will continue it. READ! and Enjoy!! I have been superbly busy. I apologize.
