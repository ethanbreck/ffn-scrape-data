Update as of August 19, 2006
If you want to email me (at burninglight@gamebox.net), PLEASE DO! Any form of contact would help to spur on my writings. Keep reminding me that these things exist, and I'll definitely feel obligated to update sometime soon! Go ahead and tell me what you like/dislike about the fics, or ask me to work on one you like. I'll get back to you.

I've gotten quite a few new reviews on Born of a Black Sea, so I'm considering putting in the required effort to update it. The problem is that chapter 2 (which was 80 finished) is now so far out of continuity with the actual series as to require a complete rewrite. I'll definitely give it a go, (I've spent an hour or two, and my writing has definitely improved in recent years), but I don't know if anything will happen. Consider that to be the only active fic, as of this moment, although more reviews could get my other fics moving again too. I plan to do a bulk update in the near future- say, by the end of the month, I'll have two new chapters up. Chapter 3 of 'Pluto' is still nearly finished, and can probably be expected sometime before winter. That's the best I'm promising anyone.

Also, encouragements would be good... I write better with them.
