Kia ora. I'm just a strange little rambling Kiwi chick with a fixation on "Shoujo Kakumei Utena," which is why most of what I bother to write fanfiction-wise is all about Mikage. I always do love a bit of feedback, especially if it gets me into a conversation about Professor Nemuro!

In other news, I'm supposed to be concentrating on original works, but oh well. I'll take it as it comes.
