NOTE 7/2/05: Since the release of OotP I've been kind of out of the loop here. I haven't read the stories I said I would read and I haven't written the stories I said I would write(coughPiratescoughHarryPottercoughcough), but I'm trying to tie down my life again and will get to these things eventually, I promise. The truth is, I have some really great story ideas (mainly in Pirates of the Caribbean and Harry Potter, and some originals as well) that I simply haven't had the time or taken the time to write out, which I will! As I get more review alerts for my The Da Vinci Code Epilogue, and as more random people put me on their favorites list after reading Out of the Night (which is just crying for a revision) I get guiltier and guiltier for sort of leaving this world behind for the time being. I won't leave it behind forever, though, and though it might take a little while, my reviews will begin to reappear and hopefully my stories will, as well.

I am the writer formerly known as "Rainbow" and "Rainbow-Star in the sea"

CAUTION: This is a fairly long profile, some of it has a point, much of it does not. If you're interested in me or any of my stories, look around, you're probably find whatever you're looking for...if you don't, email me! Or just email me anyway to chat about fanfiction or whatever...

Hi! Well...my name is Emily, and I'm 17. I live in New York, and I'm going to be a senior in high school. For anyone who wants to learn a little more about me: I plan on becoming a professional actress. I love reading and writing. I've been singing since I was a little kid. I'm addicted to the Internet. I'm dancing now, too, woohoo, I've never really done dance before and I'm already getting really good at it! I want to travel so much it's almost ridiculous. I have two cats and a younger brother. I love animals. I was forced to stop taking French at school and I don't want to forget it, so someone speak to me in French! I'm also addicted to various ethnic foods that I am constantly craving. Wanna know more, just ask!

~Just Three of My Favorite Fandoms (There Are More)~

Harry Potter: I know that I sort of disappeared from the HP world after I finished Out of the Night...I went away for the rest of that August, then school started and I sort of stumbled into other fandoms...I know I promised stories, and they will appear, I promise. Just not in the near future. I'll be back to Harry Potter, I guarantee it, just not right now. I am pretty proud of Out of the Night though, even if it DOES desperately need a revision...ugh...

Star Wars: Haven't been to this fandom in a while either...those two stories I have up were pretty random, but fun, and I must add them to my list of stories needing revisions. EEEEEE! I'll be back here too also, but not for a bit...probably not for months...

Pirates of the Caribbean: Just tons of fun. Lots, lots, lots of fun to play around in... A while ago I started planning out a story called To Sail the Seas, kind of a prequel to the movie, but with a twist...that'll pop up eventually, but alas, not for a while either.

HUGE thank you to everyone who has me on their favorites list! You know who you are! (Especially since I only recognize about 4 of you--1 is my friend in real life, and about 3 from reviewing Out of the Night.)
