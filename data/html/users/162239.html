my goodness me. it's been ages and ages since i've done anything here, hasn't it? i believe my last update was in 03... yes, mid 03. ah the days of high school.

i'm out of college now and i've gotten much, much less hyper. reading back through some of my old fics, i can honestly say i still rather like some of them... and others, not so much. but i have come to a conclusion.

i will try to finish each of the unfinished fics.

no matter how old, how terrible, or how lame the story's premise was/is, i will try to at least bring each one in turn to its projected ending.

and who knows? maybe i shall have more writing goodies for you of a more updated type soon.
