Author has written 7 stories for Suikoden, Evangelion, Chrono Trigger, and Fire Emblem.
I thought it nigh time to be updating this. I haven't been 16 for two years, anyhow.

My real name is...something that Westerners like most of you (hopefully) will probably have some trouble pronouncing, since it's in Thai, and Thai is reputed to be a remarkably tortuous languge to pronounce if you're not born into it. Heck, I'm a native and I admit to having severe troubles in several instances. So, well, that's the long way of saying my real name's likely to remain secret for a while. Until my family gets rid of its internet phobia. Yeah.

I'm currently 18, studying some sort of weird biology-related science in college, and hate physics like the plague. I enjoy gaming (of course), anime/manga, reading, drawing, music and, when the blocks loosen their stranglehold, writing. Hailstones of inspiration hit my solar plexus from time to time, and the end result is what you're seeing in the 'Stories Authored' section, which admittedly have not been edited as much as my internal QC would've liked. After this change of bio, though, I've made a resolution to thoroughly edit before sending. My word. *gets hit by a brick*

I'm also a drooling fangirl of many a bishounen. Current and longest active stalking victim? Finn, from Fire Emblem : Seisen no Keifu. I think I'll probably settle down with stalking just him for quite sometime to come.

That's about all there is to say about me...I think...
