"Ello."

"Excuse me, did you say Hello?"

"No, I said 'Ello', but that's close enough."

I have recently relocated to a new penname. I'll give you a million dollars and a cookie if you can find me.

I may redo Normal, I may not. It really is all about motivation and inspiration with me. I usually get the most inspired when I have something else I have to do like work or class or a paper or something and when I actually have free time it's all ooh, look at the pretty TV/fanfiction.

Normal: I know I have said time and again that this story will be updated, and those of you who have read my old bio page and the final chapters of Normal are probably sick of hearing it, so I won't say it anymore. Normal is officially dead. Sort of. Let me explain.

When I started Normal I was a junior in high school. It was almost four years ago that I began that story. Since then I have matured greatly in my writing style and can barely look at it or think about it without feeling imense shame and embarasement. I don't know why, but I'm like that with most of the things I wrote in high school, not just fanfiction.

But, there is a silver lining to this cloud. I am thinking about redoing Normal from the beginning. I will leave the old story posted because it is the first real story I put at ff.net and I'm a little nostalgic, but I am almost positive that I will try taking on Normal once again and actually finishing it this time. It may be a while, so don't hold your breath, but it is in the works.

Angel: An early ficlet I wrote to test the waters at ff.net. It's about Sirius going to the Potter's right after Voldemort's attack in 1981, and what followed. This is a rare bit of writing from me that I did a long time ago and can still stand to look at. Songfic to "Angel Standing By" by Jewel.

Wintered: My first and possibly only Lord of the Rings fanfic. It's about Frodo after he destroyed the ring and what would have happened if he hadn't recovered as he had in the book. I'm quite proud of this story, that I wrote after being inspired after reading a story in my Lit in English class about one and a half years ago.

Song of Innocence:Fifth year AU; takes place at the beginning of OOTP. Sirius has been a bad, bad boy. “He could feel the gasps for air and the hurt and betrayal and sadness and whatever else you might feel when you’re dying by the hand of someone you loved.”

I have been working on this story literally forever. The book Times Arrow by Martin Amis inspired me, a book that I read in the summer of '04. I just couldn't get this fic to flow right, then it just...did. I am very proud of this (as I am with most things I've posted.)

This fic might be difficult to follow, as it flows backwards in time. I originally wasn't going to put times before each paragraph, but then decided it would be to confusing without it, so there they are. Hope you enjoy.

More fics to come!
