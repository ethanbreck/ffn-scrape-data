MOST RECENTLY UPDATED: June 30, 2007, 6:20 AM EST

Well. I've been gone for a little more than four and a half years. Suppose my best bet is to blame writer's block, but in reality, I just really didn't like the quality of my work. Since then I've been through a couple years of college and a lot of life experience. I've dated a few girls, developed a slight affinity for the occasional alcoholic beverage, gone to Marine Corps boot camp and gotten separated (long story short--medical issue), gone from a fervent Christian to a jaded agnostic, and developed relationships with people that have outlasted any of my friendships I had when I was last here (aged 16). I've travelled a little more on my own, although never really straying from the East Coast, I've studied a lot of subjects, and I've made some life decisions that will set the course for the rest of my existence on this planet.

Through this all, my love for writing never diminished. Thanks to some very dedicated English teachers and some very quirky Communications teachers, I've improved my ability sufficiently to a point where I feel comfortable making a foray into the world of fanfiction once again. My primary focus used to be on Dragon Ball Z, I strayed from that to various other animes before settling on Harry Potter as my primary fanfiction focus. Hope you enjoy my work, and please, feel free to leave constructive criticism. Even the most widely-acclaimed authors could use it sometimes, and I'm far from their level of expertise.
