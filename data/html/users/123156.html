Basic Information

Name: Boaz

DOB: August 11th, 1986

Sex: Male

Nationality: American-Israeli dual citizen

Religion: Deist Jew

Political Affiliation: Libertarianism

Skype: firelordlionheart

Occupation: Photographer

Words to Describe Me: Short, a bit lazy, slightly overweight, easy going, creative, laid back, intelligent, insightful, friendly, shy, nice guy, very opinionated, cynical, introverted, bluntly honest.

Works in Progress

Light from the Netherworlds: A novelization of Xenogears. Huge project. This one scares me. I'm trying to put as much originality in it as possible without altering the plot.

Harry Potter and the Crystals of Atlantis: Semi-AU second war fic.

The Journey of Yun: A new Avatar story about Korra's successor. This is big and intense, but I shall prevail.

Justice in Agnishima: A story about the trial of the Fire Nation war criminals, including Ozai.

Works Completed

Hymn of the Reunited Kingdom: Not a fic, but the National Anthem of Gondor and Arnor based on the old Soviet Anthem.

A Freedom Fighter to the End: A one-shot Avatar fic about Jet contemplating on his life just as he's about to die.

The Final Testament of Phoenix King Ozai: A short fanfic about Ozai writing a memoir before his execution.

Works Deleted

Harry Potter and the Old Alliance: A post-OOTP 7th year fic.I wrote it in 03/04. Yes many people did like it, but after reading through it, I felt my stomach turn at the poor quality of the work. I portrayed the characters terribly. What could've been a great plot turned into utter trite, I believe. Last time I ever write a Hogwarts fic.

Harry Potter and The Fortress of Shadows: My first Harry Potter fic. It was also 7th year. Epically bad.

A Lesson in Life: One of those 11 walker LOTR "fics". I thought it was ok, but I didn't put enough if any original detail into it and the writing was a bit immature. I was afraid of the readers' opinions at the time so I got rid of it instead of revising it. I regret that.

Above Mahanon: A series of events before the invasion of Mahanon dealing with what each of the characters had done the night before the battle. Poorly written and utter trite.

Favorite Fanfiction.net Authors

Peptuck: Wrote a Final Fantasy VIII novelization which is worth reading even if you didn't play the game. He's now working on a number of other novelizations and fanfics. This man has the potential to be a great science fiction/fantasy author one day. In short: his stories kick all ass.

Peter J Marcroft: Wrote an incredible Final Fantasy VII novelization. This man know what a novelization should be. Read Curse of Memory! Ultor commands you!

Griselda Banks: Wrote an amazing Final Fantasy X novelization

DJNS: Wrote kickass fanfics of Avatar. Start with the Avatar and the Firelord, then read all the one shots, and then read Smoke and Mirrors. Read them and enjoy.

thejealousone: When it comes to Harry Potter fanfic writers, this guy is certainly in the top five. In the Words of Ginevra Molly Potter really delves into Ginny's mind and her role in the series. Now he's doing the same kind of fic with Draco Malfoy. You'll need a lot of time to read his work, so do it on a holiday or something. Also, there's nothing better than having a nice cup of tea, or a bottle of beer after a hard day's work, sitting at the comfort of your desk reading his stuff.
