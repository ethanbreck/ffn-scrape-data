I joined FFN more than 10 years ago, like most people here, I've observed. I wrote some Slam Dunk (I've taken them all down though); stopped for a few years, then took up YYH fanfiction sometime in 2007 (another account). Man, time flies. I'm here again, but I'm afraid it's only until my YYH throwback mania lasts. I plan to post my old YYH works here once I take then down from the other account and cleaned them up.

FFN has gotten fancier and easier to use in the past ten years, but for some reason I'm glad they kept the minimalist homepage. So nostalgic.

Read a bunch of awesome YYH fics in the past month. I'll try to update my favorite stories so you guys can also check them out.
