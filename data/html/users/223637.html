Hi, I'm 17 years old (as of July 2nd). I live in Georgia in a town where everybody is somebody, but nobody cares. Right now it's summer so I'm really inbetween grades, but let's just say I'm in 11th grade now.

I like reading, writing, watching movies (action, horror, comedy you name it), hanging with my friends and so on. I plan on attending either UGA or Georgia Tech, but most likely Tech because I want to be an architecht and they have a better program.

I mainly write about Charmed and General Hospital, but not so much Charmed anymore. Most of my stories now will revolve around Skye and Jax of General Hospital because they have such a great story of love and betrayl that it's easy to write for them. I hope you'll enjoy my stories, because I enjoy writting them and getting feedback, especially the good.

My bestest friend in the whole wide world is a young girl by the name of Tori (more affectionately known as Gomer). She's the bestest! LOL! Anyway I've known her since were were freshman and it's never been the same since. In my opinion, I like to believe that she's changed my life a bit, if not completely. We've been through the good times and the bad together and we've never fought (which is more than I can say for me and my past bestfriends). She recently lost her mother (well not really recently, it'll be a year at the end of this month, but it seems like she were here just yesterday) and I know it's been hard for her, but I think she's doing well. Anyway, she's great. She helps me with my stories and listens to my problems (though she does complain). I couldn't ask for a better best friend. She's also an author on FanFiction.net as well and has written 2 stories and a couple of poems. I recommend any of you who are reading to check them out and give her feedback because she's a very insecure writter, though in my personal opinion she's very talented.

My other bestest friend is Ashley Griffin. She's my sister (not really but we could pull it off looks wise). I've known her since I was in 4th grade and she was in 3rd (so basically since I moved to my current house). We've got an odd relaitonship. We don't talk all that much and I think she's spent the night at my house 2, maybe 3 times whereas I've never spent the night at hers. Despite the fact that we don't spend all that much time together, we're really close. I love Ashley!!! hehehehe

About my looks. I'm tall about 5'7" last time I checked. I weigh 130 (or there abouts) and have really long auburn hair in which I plan on donating to a cancer society in a few months when it's long enough because if I get it cut now then I'll look really bad (Me and short hair just don't go together). I have really dark brown eyes, so dark that people often think they're black (I have a feeling that's why people always think I'm pissed off -black eyes and pissed off people just go together, don't ya' think?). Anyway I've got really white skin (think a sheet of paper) and lots and lots of freckles (guess that's where I get my nickname 'Freck' from).

As for my personality. I'm a speratic goofball with odd tendencies to say weird things out of the blue. People often think I'm high, though I don't do drugs (I'm too afraid to die -I value my life as pathetic as it is) it's because I'm hyper I guess. I believe people think I'm odd because I don't really have a problem voicing my opinion to people I don't know or like, though when it comes to my friends I'm very careful of their feelings. I have the classic redhead temper, yet I am careful with it. I don't loose it very often, but when I do it's sudden and not very pretty (believe me i'm not nice -just ask the guy who gave Tori a bad review once). Also I'm very friendly (I was voted Friendliest in my Aerobics class this year along with Most likey to be rich, To have the most kids, and to own the biggest house).

I'm smart (or atleast I like to think so). I get mostly A's in school, sometimes a B. My best subject is Math which is good for the whole architect thingy. I'm also pretty good at anything Social Studies and fairly good at Language Arts (best at writting -who knew?). My worst and most hated subject is anything having to do with Science (thank God I only have to take three years of it in High School -God knows I'm not takin' more than I have too), though I am reffered to in my Chemisty class as the smart girl whom everyone copies (no lie).

Well that's all I can tell you for now because I'm tired. I think you guys know more about me than my own family and friends do, lol. Well please read my stories and enjoy them! Thanks for reading my strange and disturbing bio, though I'm sure no one got this far into it.
