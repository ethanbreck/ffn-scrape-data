UPDATE (11/3/09): If you're also interested in FictionPress and original fiction, check out Let the Words Flow! This is a new blog featuring current and former FictionPress authors who either are published already or are seeking publication. I'm one of the contributors, along with Sarah J. Maas (S.J. Maas), Mandy Hubbard (MandyHubbard), Savannah J. Foley (Svonnah-la-fay), June Hur (J.E. Wyatt), Renee Carter (Pen-Ink), Alexandra Shostak (frozeninmotion), and Rachel Simon (bringmayflowers). We're hoping it's going to really open up a dialogue about how you go from FP to published author and ought to be a lot of fun. We'll be updating regularly, so please take and look and let us know what you think!

I'm a 20-something journalist who loves writing fan-fics about characters I know and love. I write a lot about Star Trek: The Next Generation (a.k.a. my all-time favorite TV show) and the Star Wars backstory because I grew up with those fictional people and feel I know them well. But, my interests are diverse and I love doing comedic stuff too. I've got all kinds of stories in my head, so as soon as I get a chance to get them on the computer, I'll post them. I also try and read and review as many stories as I can. I know how much I like getting that e-mail saying your story has a new review! However, since FictionPress has been up and running, I've been focusing on my original fiction, mostly in the historical section. At the moment, I'm not planning to write any fan fiction stories... but you never know when I'll get inspired.
