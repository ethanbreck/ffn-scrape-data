Hey, I'm WolverGrim! I'm 27 and an unemployed college graduate. I love writing and films, and so have a great time incorporating both into my fanfictions.

I've gone through a lot of interests over the past few years but my most current are in the TV shows Supernatural, Teen Wolf, Psych, Sherlock, Merlin, Doctor Who, Burn Notice, Royal Pains, The Vampire Diaries and Game of Thrones. I'm also a fan of anime and have been for quite a while. My top five favourites are Vision of Escaflowne, Sailor Moon, Fruit Basket, Fairy Tail and Gakuen Alice.

I write both Gen and pairing fics (mostly slash but I do on occasion write het). I also read both. I guess you could say I swing both ways in fandom depending on which show/book it is. And speaking of books! My favourite author is Jim Butcher and my favourite book is one he wrote: Blood Rites. My second favourite book is Dark of the Moon by PC Hodgell.

My most favourite genre is Urban Fantasy (I hardly ever venture outside the box). I enjoy writing original pieces, though I have yet to complete anything. I hope to one day, but in the mean time I will practice with fanfiction.

And now for the obligatory reminder that really should be tacked on to every piece I write, but sometimes I forget!
