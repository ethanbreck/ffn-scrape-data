Hi Everybody,

Well it’s a new year and I figured it was about time I wrote a real bio, instead of using this as my personal ranting space.

A little about me:
Let’s see I’m a 20 year old student in my second year of college. I’m incurably addicted to manga and fanfiction, and fanfiction is free so here I am. I just started writing fics a little over a year ago. (A late bloomer, I know) I’ve never been big on writing, except for my lame poetry and probably wouldn’t be writing now if not for my dear friend Guru~sama, who came up with the concept of the “Word of Yaoi” with me. He said I should try to write that and now here I am working on my eighth fic (which isn’t much considering I write mostly one-shots but still).
I don’t write because it’s fun (hell no, do you know how many times I proof read things @_@) nor because I’m talented (I wish), but merely because I love to see how far I can run with the crazy ideas in my head. ^_^
