Konnichiwa,

I'm Starkitty. I discovered and cultivated my love of fantasy and fanfiction with Anime, Harry Potter, and Lord of the Rings. Since then, I have been living in my own little fictional world. Its okay, they know me here. Maybe one day I will get around to putting all of my ideas around on paper, but for now, my collection of finished stories is limited. Even for ongoing stories, if I lose my muse, I lose my interest and some stories may go years without ever being updated. For that I apologize. I shall try my best to keep interested long enough to finish my stories.

As for my stories, I have few favorite characters. I tend to like them all. My fanfiction will tend to reflect a wide range of characters. However there are some that I completely hate. I rarely stick with canon romantic pairings. These characters are meant to be played with, and play I will. There are some canon pairing that I completely hate, and rarely will I use it, if ever.

By the way, I am in the process of doing some major renovations with my archive so I am changing a few things. I wrote many of these stories years and years ago when I was still young and inexperienced. Now that I am more mature, my writing has matured and I aim to reflect that in my works by updating those that need to be.

I hope you enjoy my stories!
