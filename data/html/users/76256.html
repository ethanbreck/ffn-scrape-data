Author has written 3 stories for Digimon, and Gundam Wing/AC.

I am a wannabe writer that has constant writers block, which prevents me from putting my ideas to print so I come here to push the boundaries of my imagination.

My Top Ten FanFiction stories; Subject to change without notice.

Number Ten is a sweeping Gundam Wing AC fic called 'Tragedy's Consequence' written by ‘Gerald Tarrant’ and ‘Quicksilver’. This story completely takes out ‘Endless Waltz’and replaces it with a confused and conflicted world that is thrust into yet another battle for supremacy over the earth sphere, with the boy’s again being one of the major pieces in this epic conflict. The nearly complete story can be found on the website www.midnightrevolution.org/gundam. Be warned there is a major character death!

Number Nine is probably the most interesting take on ‘Pokemon’ I have ever seen. 'The Omega Mutation' written by ‘Joshua Falken’ centers on Ash’s mom of all people as a dark and tortured soul, a victim of unheard of genetic manipulation, who is searching for peace and to love her son.

Number Eight is an action adventure story with a little romance thrown in on the side. 'To Hell With Destiny' written by ‘Boombubble’ combines the first series of Digimon with the second series flawlessly. This story goes on a tangent about the digidestiend’s true purpose in the grand scheme of the battle between Good and Evil. Even though it only has seven chapters I still consider this story to be an epic read.

Number Seven is one of the more interesting crossovers I have come across. ‘Till Eternity's End’ by ‘The Sielent Muse’ takes one of our favorite ninjas, one Naruto Uzamaki, and puts him in the Final Fantasy universe as a SOLDIER for Shinra under the tutelage of Sepiroth to become one of the most powerful beings ever to be seen when he returns to his home dimension.

Number Six features in the Naruto-verse once again. ‘Soul of the Seal: The Ninja Adventures’ by ‘MrDust’ takes an interesting storyline twist with the great and mighty Kyuubi becoming the parental figure for this ninja of destiny.

Number Five is a very well written story that crosses the Harry Potter-verse with the Stargate-verse. ‘Harry Potter: An Ancient’s Journey’ by ‘keiranhalcyon2010’ is an epic read that blends two completely different series together flawlessly.

Number Four is written by ‘3D Master’. 'Buffy Z' takes one Xander Harris, gives him Say-jin heritage, and thrusts him from the DBZ-verse to the Buffy-verse where he will proceed to upset every plan the corrupt PTB’s have for the future.

Number Three follows Naruto Uzamaki as he is put into the care of one of the best originations in Kohana the ANBU black-ops. ‘ANBU BRAT’ by ‘Nauto-pwns-Kyuubi’ is a complex and well-written story that covers the fights and ninja know-how with stunning accuracy.

Number Two is a classic romance and adventure story from 'Dragon Ball Z' between Son Gohan and Videl Satan written by ‘FateChia’. This story as well as most other G/V stories centers on the couple’s search for a reason why they love each other, with a bit of heavy violence and the fate of the universe hanging in the balance that we all come to expect from DBZ. All in all 'Love's Trials' is a definite must read for anyone who's a fan of the Dragonball Z series.

Number One is the collection of original stories called 'Chronicles of The Wanderer' and can be found on the website www.wandererverse.com I suggest one read the collection of 'Calling Out The Clan' fist to get a handle on this multi-verse that revolves around 'Buffy the Vampire Slayer' and 'Highlander' the series. It cannot be explained it must be read! The series also contains a number of pairing including Buffy/OC, Willow/Oz, Xander/Cordy, Giles/Jenny, Amy/OC. A warning, some material is for mature readers only!
