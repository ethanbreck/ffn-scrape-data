As of right now, I'm back and beginning to update fanfics. I'll start posting new chaps slowly. Watch out for them ^_^ It's great to be back!

I thought it would be a good time to update my profile. Im Kasumi Yawa. Also known as Sara. I love Japanese Anime. I love reading fanfics, especially Pokemon, Sailor Moon and my new fave Dragonball Z! Veggie-chan is soooo cute! Ok before you ask, I'm obsessed with him, and if you knew me well enough, I've got him locked in my closet ~_^ Another love of mine is Ash from Pokemon. He's soo cute! Yes, I am sick minded, but hey I dun care so :P With the help of my good friend Carley, I've called my obsession with Ash, Sashshipping. I love writing fanfiction too. So please read my fics and review. I'd really appreciated it.

Some things about myself: Im a poor high school student who loves to write fanfiction, so please don't sue me.

I also admire the books written by Nora Roberts. She is a fantastic author and she's a great inspiration to all romance writers! I've started my own project for this summer. I've gotten a list of ALL the books she's written and have vowed to read all her books! Doing pretty good so far! ^_^

I wanna give a big, big shout out to my new friend Violet Emeralds. You guys have gotta read her fics, they're awesome! Hey Violet, great job with ABO. May I get a peek on the next one like last time?

My friend Satokasu Suki writes some good fanfiction, so read her fics. Mostly AAML fics. Hi Tor-chan!

Also a big hello to A_M Destination Love! Hey gurl! You know I love ya! A_M Destination Love and I started a fanfic called "Loving Embrace." It's goin slow, but I'd recommend that you read it. Not your typical AAML.

Carla Powers, my movie buddy and my best friend!

A big shoutout to Sailor Epyon aka Wild Suicine and Sapphire Espeon! Love chattin with u! Wild Suicine, Trunks is cute rite? ~_^ LOL.

Hope you enjoy my fics! ^_^

New works in progress:

The Heart of a Prince:
My very first B&V romance fanfic. A bit of a fairy tale fic, inspired by the movie "Ever After." I'm pleased with it so far. ^_^

Ash's Heart:
A sequel to Ash's Heart. A look inside the Pokemon Master's life as a husband and a father. Check that out as well!

I'm also beginning to brainstorm on new projects. We'll see what I come up with. That's all for now! Bye!
