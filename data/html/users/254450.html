Author has written 6 stories for Lord of the Rings.

In my story list you'll find only Lord of the Rings fic. I'll admit to being drawn into the Tolkien world by the movies, and I have Peter Jackson to thank for introducing me to such an incredible world. But while the movies captured my imagination, the books have captured my heart.

I will forever thank Thundera Tiger for providing the nudges, prods, and resulting beta work above and beyond what one should expect; her encouragement brought me to the point where posting a story was not so terrifying and where writing the stories in my head have become nearly mandatory. In most stories, you're likely to see a concentration on bookverse, with a smattering or nod to movieverse. My imagination is most often sparked by the friendships between Legolas and Gimli or Legolas and Aragorn, though Sam and Frodo often do the trick too, as do the Fellowship as a whole. But let's see where these characters take me, shall we?

I would like to add a special thanks to any anonymous reviewers. Your comments are greatly appreciated, but if you post anonymously, I cannot send you an email to tell you so. Any way you'd like to comment is wonderful, and since I cannot tell you otherwise, here it is: thank you!

I'd like to add a tremendous thank you to decembersonnet, who recently wrote such a wonderful review, but does not accept private messages. Your detailed review deserves a large helping of gratitude, and I couldn't bear not sending you a thank you for taking the time to write with such thoughtfulness. You really understood all the implications in the story, as well as the consequences that were realized.

With Hope and Without Hope was the last story I published. It won several awards, something I never imagined. Don't know if there will be a next one, I'm afraid. I began a new story, but I found a major plot hole rather late in the game. It needs reworking, and I don't know if I have that in me.

In the meantime, I'm working on original fiction, and that's taking up most of my time, certainly all my writing time. I would like to finish that last LotR story one day, but I can't promise anything. What I have here may be all there ever is. Hope you enjoy what you find.

One last thank you to all my wonderful reviewers--you make all the hard work worth it!
