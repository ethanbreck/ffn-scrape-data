Author has written 16 stories for StarTrek: Voyager.

I love J/C! I love J/C fanfic! Constructive reviews are always welcome - good and bad, but I really don't see the point in just, "This sucked!" reviews. If you feel that way, tell me why you thought it sucked and I can take a look at it again and see if I can fix it.

Pet Likes : J/C wet-fic stories (see my archive at ), Chakotay introspectiveJ/C stories , Janeway introspective J/C stories, J/C humour/banter stories.

My bad: My Muse runs off with handsome Chakotay look-alike men at the drop of a hat, so let me apologise here and now for any unfinished stories. I do try to while away the time waiting for my Muse to turn up by making J/C or J and C pics and wallpapers though :)

J/C is so entertaining and thought provoking, so a huge THANK YOU! to everyone who keeps writing and reviewing and discussing. :) TPTB obviously just didn't realise that Fate had dropped a chunk of gold into their laps when Mulgrew & Beltran started arcing electricity back n forth right there on the screen, but we did -right? Yeah, darn straight! TPTB dropped the ball and we've picked it up and run with it, and even now over 10years after the show ended we're still running with it. That says a lot about how affecting and uniting and thought provoking J/C is ; it is so much more than two good-looking people in positions of authority and there was so much of their complex 'relationship' that wasn't explored on screen, but we can explore it in fan-fiction and in artworks etc. I've made so many good friends in so many countries because of my quiet Janeway/Chakotay obsession, and we just keep challenging one another to ask, "What if...?" don't we.
