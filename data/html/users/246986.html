I love writing, and have wanted to be a professional author since the tender age of twelve. I'm still not published, but that will come in time. All things come in time. My favorite world to write fanfiction in is Harry Potter. It's just such a wonderful baseline. Severus Snape is my favorite character, and not evil. He's not allowed to be evil! You can read my thoughts on the matter here and see whether or not you agree. Savvy?

And guess what. I was right.

The Classic DBZ OC Project:
This is a personal project of mine, which will consist of DBZ fanfics featuring OCs of a variety of poular sorts - OCs from our world, Saiyans who were not killed when the planet was destroyed, etc. The root of this project is Pacifist, a story which started as a spoof on all those girls who fall through wells, or the TV, or whatever, and come to DBZ-land, where they promptly find the love of their life in Trunks. But it became more than a spoof. In fact, it became good. And so, the Classic DBZ OC project is born: take those Mary Sues and make something of them!
