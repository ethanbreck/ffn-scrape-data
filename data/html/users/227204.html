I started writing a while ago and just stopped for about two years, I've come back to it and will only be doing one story at a time now. I mostly enjoy HG/SS pairings for Harry Potter. I love getting reviews and hope that my writing will improve greatly as I try this whole thing again.

I generally listen to certain music when writing, usually set the music to the pace of what I'm writing. I mostly write in M, it follows my life and what I know better seeing as how I am in my twenties.
