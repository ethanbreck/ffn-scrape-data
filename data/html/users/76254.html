WATCH OUT FOR MY SONGFIC "STILL LOVING YOU"! Inspired by the Scorpions' song of that title. But take note that I didn't know there was a song with SLY title when I wrote the multi-chaptered SLY fic! Just a bit of fact! ^_^

May 29, 2003

~Ongoing Fics~

1. Still Loving You (Fushigi Yuugi)
- I just can't put my ideas down to writing for this one. Too many conflicting ideas... no, scratch that! JUST too many ideas (sequencing, the twist, the list goes on) that I kept changing the middle parts. Thankfully, I got the ending chapter down. ^_^

2. Wasn't That Love (Rurouni Kenshin)
- Stuck in a mental block... but mostly because I can't quite characterize Sayo Amakusa. I need more background information about this girl!

3. Stay Forever (Rurouni Kenshin)
- Only one chapter to go... that's why I want it to come out great! ^0^ And it's a bit limey sooo... I want to make a good lasting impression!

4. Within Distant Reach (Rurouni Kenshin)
- A definite Sano and Megumi pairing but I want to portray Christian unlike any other men in Megumi's life. I don't want him to be like the secretly possessive or the psycho kind of guy. I want him to be LIKE the perfect husband for Megumi so as to add to the drama (or the angst!).

5. UNBELOVED (Final Fantasy VIII)
- I like this one. Supposed to be just a short fic but I got carried away with the plots and all... and Selphie having an amnesia (I love stories plotting around that subject ^^)... I will continue AND finish this but not now! Gotta revive my FF8 touch!

6. The Greatest Thing (Rurouni Kenshin)
- My first ever Aoshi and Misao fic! Just for the record, I was once an Aoshi and Misao fan. Though short-lived, I still like their pairing... but not as much as that of the rooster and the fox! I say, this is yet to be the hardest to make coz I want to make it somewhat historical (though it's not obvious with the current chapters). The kind of fic that doesn't just revolve around the love and all that usual stuff I write about. Expect some action, I dare say! n_n

7. I Love You (Rurouni Kenshin)
- Inspired by the very song sang by Celine Dion. Only one last chapter to go too... I want it to be short but poignant in its brevity! Short and sweet (but not WAFFy, I hope!).

8. The Myth and the Magic (Fushigi Yuugi)
- My Tamahome and Miaka fic. Aside from not being a H&M pairing, it differs from SLV in a way that I want to start it light and build the intensity along the way. Sort of fun moments first then the real story starts...

~One-Shot Fics~

1. Just Like That (Rurouni Kenshin)
- My pathetic poetry-woven story. It's a WAFF (well, for me it is!). Ah! Just go read! ^_^

2. If I Never Knew You (Rurouni Kenshin)
- For some reason, this is one fic I'm proud about. My first ever Tomoe-Kenshin-Kaoru fic and the readers like it! Whew! Well, considering that I don't read any Tomoe, Kenshin and Kaoru centered fic (except Beauty & the Battousai!) and this pairing/triangle is really the least of my interest, I got this one right! So... whoooppeee-doo! Don't get me wrong, though! I don't hate the pairing/triangle... just that there's just too much of it out there already and... they don't have that love-hate relationship I always like about! ^_^v
