Hey There!!

Sorry but I really won't be able to continue any of these since I approach my senior year in high school and college applications take priority. Maybe once all the craziness is done with I'll get back to the fanfics but until then, read Her Story (my personal favorite and not too difficult to continue) and either of the other two!

I've been reviewing like crazy lately since I love to support my fellow writers!

My favorites fics are Final Fantasy X/X-2, Pirates of the Caribbean, Harry Potter (but it's so hard to get through all the fics on here so I have be really determined to go through them), and Gone With the Wind (best book ever written).

I really hate it when Paine is paired up with anyone other than Nooj. Why do people insist on Baralai? She's obviously in love with Nooj and Leblanc is just the cover for their mutual love! I also don't think Tidus and Yuna should be separated since they really are the best couple in the world.

Concerning Gone With the Wind, Rhett Butler is my favorite character in literature. He is just so damn intelligent :-D I hate Scarlett though since I see too much of myself in her sometimes. Wade is awesome, too. I actually started a fanfic on him but I haven't gotten back to it in a long time. Hmm, maybe I will soon. . .

So check out my stories that are here and encourage me to continue them since I need that positive feedback to update.

Flames are accepted though too since they will only improve my writing.
