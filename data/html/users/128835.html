I just started writing fan fiction recently. (Oct 2001)Right now CSI is my favorite show, so that's what I wind up writing about. I'm a big Grissom fan, so all my stories, so far, have centered on him.

Sept 15,2002
Okay, I must admit that I flew off the handle the other day when I read the announcement about the removal of NC17 fics. I got pissy and thought to myself that if they don't want all my fics, they can't have any of them. And I removed all of them.

Then I recalled that there were many stories that I have read here at FF.N that I don't remeber seeing anywhere else, so I thought I would read them again before they are gone.

Of course then I realized that I had deprived other reader of the opportunity to do the same with my stories. There might be thousands (hundreds? dozens? a handful?) of people out there that are wondering what happened to my stories.

So I have reposted one of my most successful stories. And I will be reloading some of my other lower rated fics. This gives people the opportunity to get to my bio page, where I have listed the URL for my webpage, where you can read all my fiction, including my adult fics.

I do have a couple gen fics and a couple with het pairings, but I write mostly slash and quite a few of my fics are NC17.

I'm up to seven fandoms and I get tempted everytime I turn on the TV, so that will probably change. So far my fandoms are
CSI
Invisible Man
Nash Bridges
Now and Again
Sentinel
Simon & Simon
Starsky & Hutch

Stop by my Website and take a look.
