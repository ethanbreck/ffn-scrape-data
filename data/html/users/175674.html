I lean toward genfic; if I don't explicitly say something's romantic or smutty, please don't expect too much please let me know about typos, unmarked spoilers, (unwarned) problematic and triggering things, and so on I welcome messages and try to reply to every one, so feel welcome to drop comments or messages! :D
