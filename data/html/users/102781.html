Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

This is the part where I give you insight into myself.

I write almost exclusively Harry Potter fanfiction, specifically centered around Hermione one-shots (thought I have some unposted non-Hermione ones that I may decide to post in the future). Most of my stories are inspired and influenced by music, but they are not really songfics. I'm working on a number of stories right now, which may or may not be posted in the near future. It depends on how I like the finished products, and I can be a very harsh critic of my work.

I love getting reviews (what writer doesn't?). I prefer the positive ones, of course, but constructive criticism is always appreciated. How else am I supposed to get better?

Thanks for reading!

Oh, as a P.S: some people were requesting follow ups to my stories, especially "The Dance". I won't be doing a follow-up because I feel like the story was finished where I left it.The way i see it, the point of one-shots (my preferred stories to write) is to create a situation and then allow the reader to draw some conclusions. It doesn't matter why he left, it just matters that he is gone. If you are still not understanding, please contact me (I believe my email is available. If not, please write it in a review)
