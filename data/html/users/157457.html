ssp47's Corner I actually got that name from an old email. I used to be a huge Sailor Moon fan back in the day. Pluto was my favorite and for many years my old email used to be super_sailorpluto 47 at hotmail.com. During that time I hung out on the message boards dealing with Ronin Warriors. Mostly it was on Studio Bent, but that was recently taken down. Everyone called me ssp47 and the nickname stuck. Realize that this has been YEARS since I placed myself under this profile. I have developed alot over the years and what you see here under my name is a shadow of what I once was. College has come up and a millions other things, besides my writing has gone over to another side of life. However I decided to write a fic for Twilight after reading Breaking Dawn. I got a great idea for a suspense thriller and as you can see I couldn't help myself.

Anime Like I said I was in love with Sailor Moon and Ronin Warriors for years. I hardly watch any TV now adays. Every now and then I'll catch myself watching old eps.

Manga This is what I mainly do now. I'm collecting Boys Over Flowers, now that I have completed the Mars series. Crimson Hero and Vampire Knight are my new favorites in manga.

Games Legend of Dragoon will be forever and ever my video game. It's been my fav over countless others. No I don't "own" any rights to it but the love of a loyal fan. Partly this is why Legend of Fire and Rose were compelted so quickly. I've been playing the game again and again.

Stories Possession is my new obsession at the moment. I decided to write one last fic and it happened to be my first for the Twilight saga. Lately I've been writing my own stuff but I thought it'd be nice to deal with someone's characters in a new twist for awhile. At any rate I'm excited. It's different and pulling at me daily. I've never written a suspense or a thiller before so I figured this might be a good exercise.

As always I love and appreciate any reviews I get for any of my stories.

~ssp47
