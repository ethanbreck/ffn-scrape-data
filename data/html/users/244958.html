Hello everyone...Well I'm not very good at talking about myself so I'm warning you it's going to be short.

I've been a writer since sixth grade but my stories back there are really horrible. I've spent most of my summers since I started high school re-doing those stories.

I have a lot of fanfics but not too many that are actually good. I have two fanfics that I really liked but that are horribly written and I'm trying to re-write them.

Update: 1/28/2016

I'm not dead everyone! I'm still struggling to get something published. My novel first and foremost, but I've tried sending short stories to contest. Didn't win any of them. Oh well. Off to the next attempt. I will try to write a new fanfiction this there. Hopefully it would be longer than a oneshot.

Anyway I decided I wanted to make a wish list or bucket list. No I'm not dying. Well not that I know of. You never know what tomorrow brings. I just wanted to share with you some of my dreams.

1. Publish my novel, or short story, anything at this point preferably my novel. I know it's a hard thing to do and I'm struggling, but maybe someday it will happen or I might run into someone that could help. It does take knowing the right people to make it easier. Unfortunately as you notice, I'm not a big talker.

2. One on one time with Vic Mignogna. Yes, I'm a fangirl. I admire his passion. He's someone to live up to. I did get to meet him once, but there were so many people around that also wanted to talk to him and I managed to stutter one sentence out before becoming too shy. LOL Better than nothing right?

3. To own a home that doesn't look like a run down horror story house. Or to remodel my poor house. LOL. I would love to have a house of my dreams. Big kitchen, nice bathroom. Let's not to mention instillation so I don't freeze in the winter and melt in the summer. Well maybe I'll hit the lottery and find money to make repairs. LOL.

Those are my dreams! Not to many and not very special to most people. But they would bring me happiness. Maybe some day I'll scratch one thing off.
