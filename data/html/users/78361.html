Author has written 15 stories for Dark Angel, Harry Potter, Star Wars, and Stargate: Atlantis.

Whoo, it's been a long time since I've updated this thing. About me: I'm a college student living in the eastern US and an avid fan of sci-fi. My favorite TV shows include Stargate Atlantis (my current fanfiction addiction), Stargate SG-1 (I'm really, really happy that they brought Vala back), Firefly/Serenity, and Dark Angel (the first season, not so much the weird second one). I also occasionally dip into StarTrek Enterprise, Star Wars, X-Men, Alias, ER, and other realms. And I should probably mention I was raised watching Monty Python, so I have a kinda weird sense of humor...

As for books...well, I read constantly and eclectically, but when I'm not reading academic stuff for school I tend to gravitate towards sci-fi and history. Some of my many favorite authors/poets: Orson Scott Card, Terry Pratchett, Diane Duane, Ernest Gaines, Steinbeck, Zadie Smith, J. K. Rowling, Carl Sandburg, Octavia Butler, Tennyson, Yeats, and T.S. Eliot (see, I told you it was eclectic).

In addition to fanfiction and the occasional sci-fi story, I write poetry. Lots of it. I've been writing poems since I was probably 10 years old. You can find some of my older (i.e. worse) poems on fictionpress.com. I also do spoken word poetry sometimes.

If you're interested in archiving any of my fics, you're welcome to them--just please let me know first!
