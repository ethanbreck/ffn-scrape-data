Author has written 13 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Rurouni Kenshin, Witch Hunter Robin, Fullmetal Alchemist, and Full Metal Panic.

I haven't up dated this thing in ages so perhaps it's about time I did, though bios aren't my thing. I'm an animal mad Australian who can't spell for a toffee never mind any thing else. In the dark ages we use to breed Collies, and at present I share my life with one very opinionated representative of the breed, and a little one who has yet to learn her power! The big girl takes great pleasure in interupting my writing with demands of various kinds. It seems the Collies control my writing, as now I have two again the muses are back!

My writing is a fickle beast which often doesn't get the time it deserves.

Just so you know since I'm an Aussie I do use Australian/UK spelling.

Out of hells fire is on hold until I get around to rewriting some of the chapters.
