Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, and Walking Dead.

Hello! I am really too old to be writing Harry Potter fiction, but writing is my addiction and Harry Potter is my crack!

I haven't written anything Harry for eight years, since 'Jude' (yeesh! I think it will forever remain incomplete...so tired of her!) but the idea of Tom Riddle at the circus would not leave me alone. So now I am writing my second feature, 'The Illusionist's Daughter.' I hope you all enjoy reading it as much as I am enjoying writing it.

Inspirations for 'Illusionist':

'The Night Circus' by Erin Morgenstern

'Water For Elephants' by Sara Gruen

Music from 'The Hush Sound', 'The Civil Wars', 'Mumford & Sons', and 'Forest Sun."

Old photos and sideshow banners I can't help but pin like crazy on Pinterest.com

Happy Fanfic-ing!

Sara
