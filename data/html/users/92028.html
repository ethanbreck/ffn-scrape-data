Thanks for Visiting & Welcome!

I hope my characters are portrayed truthfully. I love reviews and am appreciative of the time it takes to leave one after reading. They also make me smile :)

My style of writing is primarily filled with character interactions. I don't normally plan out stories ahead of time and simply begin to write as it comes to me. Kind of like "McGee's free writing," for you NCIS fans. I love leaving cliffhangers, so you may want to invest in some climbing equipment before you start reading.

You may also want to bring some tissues when you pull up for one of my stories. I write angst more than anything. I do the occasional humor piece and love to delve into the friendships of the characters. I ship Gibbs/Abby firstly, with Kate/Gibbs coming in a close second. I will do the occasional het romance (but no slash). I write for your enjoyment as well as my own. My goal is to make you laugh and cry, and if I can, both at the same time. All the while enjoying the depth of the characters that we love so much!

So by all means...Enjoy...
