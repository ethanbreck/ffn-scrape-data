I love to write, and I'm really enjoying writing fan fiction. Write reviews for me please!!! Disclaimer: These characters aren't mine, but they're fun to take out and play with once in a while!

I've taken down a few of my old stories. Perhaps sometime I will clean them up and finish them so that they can be reposted, but in the meantime, I think it's better if I take them down. The Gargoyles story, The Strangeness of Family, is still there and will be getting updated sometime. I have plans for it and hopefully there will be much shorter intervals between updates now so that it might actually be completed sometime. I'm sorry to everyone who has been patiently waiting on that one. I promise that I haven't forgotten you!

I have started "Morning" stories in various different fandoms. They are sort of always in the works as I write one or two of them almost every day. I will keep adding more people to them whenever the mood strikes me. Unless otherwise stated, all the "chapters" in any one story are the same day. They are often experimental in nature and I rarely know where they will take me, but some of them have turned into quite interesting stories told in an unusual way. If there is a specific character or fandom that I have not done that you would like to see, feel free to request it. I make no promises, but I'm always open to suggestions!
