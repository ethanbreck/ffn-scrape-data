Ok, lets be honest. The WIP fics will never be completed. I'm sorry. As much as I love Lost World, and always will, I got really irritated with the fact that they ended the last season with a HUGE cliff hanger and then never finished the series. So that kind of killed my entusiasim. And I don't feel as if I could write true to the characters anymore. It's just been too long since I've watched it. Although I've been debating doing a Marguerite/Veronica fic. I think that would be cute. And fun to write. We'll see. But it's just been too long.

The odds of me posting to this account again are slim to none. You can find my most recent fics at: I have a few Warehouse 13 and Grey's Anatomy fics posted there.

You can find my fanvids at https://www.youtube.com/user/CrimsonCat21

Other than that, it's business as usual. Trying to write whatever I can whenever I have the time. Poetry, short stories, journal entires... Completely obsessed with music and the written word.
