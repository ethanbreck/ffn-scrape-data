Dear readers:

I know. I suck. I'm a slave to laziness. I'll be gone until the writing bug decides to hit me again, and that might be a very, very long time away. A big part of it has to do with the fact that, like many other people out there, I've outgrown the style I've established for myself on FF.net, though I'm not the type to go around purging my profile of old stories, no matter how bad and embarrassing they are. Maybe someday I'll have the drive to rework everything properly from the beginning, but it would require so much effort that I'm not sure I'd ever build up the resolve to take on the job. Best case scenario, I'll try working from what's here, but it's unlikely for most of my stories in categories I'm no longer interested in. I don't want to depress anyone, or make you lose any hope that you'll be able to see the end of what began, but I just want to be clear on my feelings about all this. You've been great readers, and I still get incredibly happy when I still see reviews popping into my inbox from time to time. I'm sorry, and I don't deserve you. Hey, chances are all the guilt will pile up and force me to update again, someday.

With love,

Lanti
