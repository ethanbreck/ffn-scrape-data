ABOUT ME

Wife and mother of three. I write stories in the Harry Potter, Yu-Gi-Oh, and Warcraft universes, as well as some original works. My works can also be found on AO3 and FictionPress.

I have a tumblr blog and a Discord server! If you want to chit-chat with me on a regular basis, ask me questions, chat about the newest chapters, or even just want to have a place to connect with other people in the Harry Potter, Warcraft, or Yu-Gi-Oh fandoms, send me a PM here, on Discord (Taedae#7683), on tumblr or ask about it in your comments/reviews.
