Last Updated: September 2016

I'm baaack! After a twelve-year hiatus (wow, has it really been that long?), I've finally gotten back into fanfiction. This is thanks to discovering several incredible authors in the Rise of the Guardians, Legend of Zelda, and Tales of the Abyss fandoms, which led to reading all of their work, and realizing that fanfiction isn't the crutch I once thought it was, but a genuine art form that can help an aspiring writer like myself learn and gain confidence. It did take some time to find my old account and get everything in working condition again, but that was to be expected.

To those of you who read my stories before I left, I'm sorry. Despite the fact that I had said I was leaving ff.net to work on my own stories, I never actually did. I did start working on my own video game along with my brothers, but real life got in the way and that hasn't gotten very far either. I'm not currently planning to update any of my old fics, but I'll leave them up here for now (if only just to see how bad I was back then -_-). The only thing I'll be updating in those is the author notes.

I have finally started writing fanfiction again, though, and my first project is a continuation of one of my favorite games of all time, Tales of the Abyss. As much as I love the game, I can't help but be dissatisfied with the ending - they didn't explain how it happened very well, and there's just so much more they could have done with those characters. Maybe they did that in the hopes that they could make a sequel, but it's been so long now I doubt they ever will. So I'll just write my own!

Reviews are very much appreciated - even if all you say is which part you liked/didn't like - but if you don't have time, that's fine. I'm writing this for my own enjoyment and to develop my writing skills, but I do hope others like it as well. However, if you do leave a review, I will try to read & review one of your stories in return, as long as it's from a fandom I like, so please drop me a line if you can!

I don't really have a set update schedule - I'll try to post something new at least once a month, but since I'm now working 36 hours a week and also taking two college classes at the same time, there's a good chance that things may be delayed. Unfortunately (or fortunately? Depends on how you look at it) I am an incurable perfectionist, so I tend to spend a lot of time editing my chapters before posting. I do intend to finish this Tales story, at least, so please try to be patient. ;)
