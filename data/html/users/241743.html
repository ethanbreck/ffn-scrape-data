Smashed Sunshine

PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 07-26-02, id: 241743
Author has written 13 stories for Harry Potter, X-Files, and Pirates of the Caribbean.

Gosh it's been a long time since I last updated my profile. It occurs to me that such alot has changed since my first ever entry to FF.net. First of all there's the fact that I now have a better grasp of grammar, punctuation, spelling and so forth. I look back at Tomorrows Yesterday and shudder at my lack of skill! For starters I'm pretty sure there should be an apostrophe in the title. Eek. So for all new readers I would like to say please don't judge me. Some of these stories were written over three years ago and I was but a wee child then. Obviously I'm still pretty young but a little better at this writing thing.

Okay. So I've added a new story to my workload. I know what you're thinking; will she ever finish what she started? Well next year I'm off to university, so let us hope that I can pull it off before then. After all, what else is a gap year for? I don't like to break a promise, so I won't make one. All I'll say is that I won't give up. Yet.

Current Stories:

Inclination - Sequel to 'Tomorrows Yesterday'. Draco has a secret life. Plauged by nightmares and visions, he is forced to replace Snape as Hogwarts Potion's master. Hermione Granger has a new life ahead of her and is trying to put behind her the past. That is until the past comes back to bite her...Will Draco let her slip through his fingers again?

Paper Roses - There are some things in this world that were never meant to be. Ginny feels she has found one of these things and is trying hard to fight back the nearing fate. Draco is confused by a single paper rose that offers him things he doesn't understand...This is quite dark and is about the relationship hate and obsession can bring around.

Dictionary - A story written by myself and Clara Lou (ID:142468) revolving round the lives of the now grown up Hogwarts students. Chapter by chapter, fate unravells for each seperate character as they try to deal with the struggles of work and love. This one's an R due to sexual referances and cursing - don't be fooled though, it is quite funny! (At least I think so!)Involves various ships including a Draco/Ginny/Blaise love triangle.

Keep It Unclean - Not as perverse as it sounds! Ginny is trying to write a novel and failing miserably. Her publisher hates the characterisation, the overblown romance and the unrealistic feeling. Will Ginny be able to pull her act together and pay the bills? Will she be forced to accept that the search for perfection is futile? Maybe imperfection is just around the corner...

Completed Stories:

Tomorrows Yesterday - My first dip into the world that is Harry Potter. This story circles around Hermione and Draco, and the forbidden fruit of their love. (awwww!) It's now complete and after popular demand I have started a sequel.

Untamed Adoration - Sometimes all thats left in a world full of perceptions are words. For Draco Malfoy this is true. In an attempt to portray his feelings of regret and love to Ginny, he writes her a love letter. Can he ever be forgiven? Is redemtion out of reach? A one off, sentimental letter.

With Or Without You - Based around the lyrics of U2's 'With Or Without You', this is a story of love and pride in a blaze of war. Can you survive when nothing is ever fair in love and war? Or will the ghosts be the only thing to keep Draco alive when there is nothing left? Very dark, so not for the light hearted.

Acid/Alkali - This one approaches the relationships held between friends and the effect it can have if the link is severed between the two. Two weeks has passed since that fateful night - Remus is scared that the door will open and it won't be his friend. Acceptance seems out of reach and he is alone with the memories that haunt him. Can Tonks help the pain?

The Musings of Luna Lovegood - Luna would tell Ron how she feels, but he can be ever so dense!

Craze - This one's an X-Files fanfiction! I am verging on obsession towards this programme and felt that this would be the best place to start on my expansion plan. It looks into the idea of soul mates and whether or not we are settling for second best when it comes to love. A Mulder and Scully shipping of course!

Simply Dreaming Nirvana: Pirates of the Caribbean story here, involving Will Turner and Elizabeth Swan.It's been six months since she saw him. Six months since she ran out in a fit of anger. Will it still be the same now that uncertainty has lived between them for such a long time? Or is the dream over?

Coeur Corrompu - French for 'Corrupt Heart'. Sorry for all you Hr/D fans out there, but this is a G/D fic. I am still faithful to both ships though. Ginny is working as an Auror's researcher when she is given a tricky case to handle. Why does love have to be so complicated?

I'd like to thank all those who have reviewed my stories. You know who you are! Thats all for now, Holly xx
