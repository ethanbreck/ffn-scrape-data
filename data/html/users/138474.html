My path to writing serious fanfiction was a long and winding one. Well no, it wasn't really, it just isn't where I started.
Being cursed as being a 20 year old O-Town fan who had way too much spare time on her hands last summer in between my university years, I decided to put my time to good use and...write parodies of O-Town songs. I know, I know, a job might have been a better way to pass the time, but there we go. So I wrote 14 parodies (a test of endurance if ever there was one) and then branched out into comical and light-hearted O-Town stories, mostly revolving around Ashley taking showers and having long talks with Erik (don't ask, it'd take me ages to explain but as many people who frequent the message board at the band's official UK website will know...it's all Ashley's fault!)
So after writing over 20 short funny fanfics, I decided it was time to bite the bullet and start writing serious stories, and ended up creating the 5 part series about Ashley and his "soul mate" Laura. I'm currently re-writing the stories because I realised that actually, I never re-read them after I wrote them! They're all published here and if you'd like to take a look at them, I'd love to know what people think!
I'm currently expanding my fanfic horizons and writing about...shock horror!...somebody other than Ashley! Trevor actually says more than a few words in my latest endeavour, but I have to say, I can't write quite as easily about him as I could Ashley. That says something about me, doesn't it?!
