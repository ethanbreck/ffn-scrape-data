Author has written 17 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Yu-Gi-Oh, Chrono Cross, and Beyblade.

I believe it's safe to say that I no longer touch this account, mostly because I'm a little embarrassed of my past writings, well more than a little embarrassed actually. Back then, I wrote because it was fun, it was a way to kill time, and I basically had the "grammar, what's that?" kind of mindset. Uh, anyways... my ff.net account should be considered dead, not that I don't peek around anymore... I still find some gems. *coughcough*readAbyssus*coughcough*

Uh, but that's also not to say I don't write at all anymore, just life has caught up to me, and I had been battling a writing block that can only be described as too-friggin'-epic. I'm not even sure if it was mentioned before, because it's been a really long time since I touched this account, but does hold a fair amount of my newer writings (tags and all?), good and bad. And having said that, I think the bad outweighs the good by quite a bit; but that is only because I don't really hold much confidence in my writings these days... which might be a little weird because I learned so much about writing in general.

But anyways... that's about it. Erm, and kids, just keep writing, take chances and make mistakes, and all that jazz; you'll improve, no worries. Just ignore all the naysayers. Yep. Ciao now.

Oh yeah, and favorite pairings? Still the rare and few ones that not many people seem to like o_o FF13's Snow/Hope is my current OTP though =3

Edit: Well, actually... who ever knows what might happen? Inspiration might strike. Right? Right. Nyah, kinda doubt it, but it's nice to assume every now and then.
