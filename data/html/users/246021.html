Hey everyone. If you've read my story then you know that I really like to write. I pour my heart and soul into my stories, wait, I only have one out. Well you get the point!

Why don't I tell you a little bit about myself.

Name: Sarah Emily Perkins
(Ya know what? I don't care about putting my whole name down cuz if you try and find out where and live and stuff, then you must be one demented mother fucker.)

D.O.B 11/18/87
(For those of you who are trying to figure out when that is, it's NOVEMBER 18th 1987...Got it?)
And If your trying to figure out how old I am...it's 16.

My favorite things in the whole entire world are:

Dragonball/Z/GT...Amazing anime series and very well done. Though I just started watching a couple years ago, I've feel in love with the series. I'm at the Kid Buu saga right now though I have already been to sites that have told me what happened. I haven't seen the GT series yet, but I've read alot about it. I just ordered all 64 episodes, english subtitled, for $29.00...Not to shabby eh?

Harry Potter: Ah no words can express how much I love this kid. The books are outstanding. I know this sounds gay and everything (Your probably yawning) but it's really brought me up when I've went through hard times. I can't wait for the next book to come out.

Lord of the Rings: I had the pleasure of being introduced the books before the movie came out. So I finished them and waited patiently to see if all that I had imagined things to look like were the same as in the movie. I was pretty close. I'm glad I had read the books before the movie so I could appreciate it for what it really was in it's fullness.

Left Behind Series: As I sit here writing this Bio thingy, I'm watching Tribulation Force. It's the second movie based on the adult series. I haven't read all the adult series. I find the kids series to be much more apealing. I've only read up to book 18. #1. Because I didn't see the others at the library (Curse those fools for taking my reading material!) #2. Cause I have an over due and damage fee of $39.00. I swear I wasn't the one who damaged the Harry Potter listening tapes. They were already like that! *What? I can't listen to them while I'm sleeping? I guess your right, it does show how obsessed I am with it.* Back to Left Behind. I do encourage all of you to at least try to read the first couple of books in the kids series.

Ah last, but not least:

Star Wars: Good old Star Wars. Now this is something everyone can like. At least you know you can strick up a conversation with you dad about Star Wars when the subject of Harry Potter gets to kiddish for him. I love Star Wars. Right now as I type I'm looking at my Anikan Skywalker poster. Damn he is a fine mother fucker. I mean seriously, Hayden Christiensen is the hottest guy ever. Ya you know what I'm talking about girls.

Ok thats just a few things of what I like. I just want to tell you all that the most amazing story on this site is Dark Angel by Camaro. If you haven't read it I urge you to click out of my biography and go read it...NOW...

I told you to go read it!!!

Well ok, if you wanna hear more about me I can understand. Who wouldn't?

Ok people...My favorite song in the whole entire world (at the moment) is Gollum's Song by Emiliana Torrini. It's from the Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers soundtrack. Please listen to it!

There really isn't much more I can say about myself. In closing I would like to say that when you write a story, the most rewarding thing is when someone reviews. It may be bad or good. Just to know that someone is actually listening to what you are saying, I mean reading what you are typeing, can make the sun shine on a cloudy day. I know, that was so Barney.

Thanks to all that have stuck with me through my horrible writing. God damn you people must really like me if your still reading this.

Love to all!
Panny
