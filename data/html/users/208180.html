NEWS:
! Seeing as how none of you know me personally (and those of you who do know me and know of this site never read my work although you claim to - I know, damnit, I KNOW), I have absolutely no shame in saying that I've been feeling sick for two weeks and have had my Prozac dosage increased just a bit. Since then, I've found my usual happiness in writing at night return to me. I hope to kick some of these stories in the pants and unleash a pile of ideas I've been storing in my head.
! Not only that, but I have the excuse of having enjoyed Europe during the first little bit of the summer. I love France and I love Italy. I miss my jellyfish of the Mediterranean. We swam well together, my pretties.
! As for the stories...well, they'll just spew out and you'll have to see whether or not you like them, as usual. I've moved the location of "Survivor 112" from the French Catacombs (I'm saving that for no. "113"; I think it's more fitting) to the Roman Forum because I was there a month ago and therefore feel the need to make the interesting cast of "Survivor 112" endure each other at the Forum instead. I think that, maybe, it will be easier to select my winner than it had been while writing "Survivor 111." Expect developments on the "The Headless Walrusman."
