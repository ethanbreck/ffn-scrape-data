Author has written 14 stories for Pong, Parodies and Spoofs, Lunar: Silver Star, Wild Arms, Lunar: Eternal Blue, Naruto, and Breath of Fire.

I have been a member of this fanfiction community since the days before it had things like alerts, and before fictionpress dot net split from it. While I have been out of the fanfiction game for a while I do still keep up to date with a few of the ones that were not finished when I left. I have, and still am, a long time poster in what was once the message board of the Shrine to Ghaleon. I have a large group of fandoms that I am a part of ranging from video games, to anime, to manga.

Personal stuff:

I currently own 27 game systems (including handhelds and computer)

I am a rabid fan of Working Designs games (Lunar, Arc the Lad, Growlanser, etc.), and most of my collection is signed by their former president.

I have every season of NCIS on DVD, as well as an NCIS hat.

I used to work for Gamestop before leaving to try to start a gaming convention (it never materialized)

I am going back to school for a computer science degree
