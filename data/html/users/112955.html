April 19th 2009

Sorry I fear that I have drifted away from the desire to write fan fics. Now I wish to focus on writing my own original pieces and perhaps giving the chance to be published a try.

Maybe once in a while I will pop back up with something or another. But I must thank everyone who has reviewed to me both the good and the bad. Really writing for FanFiction has helped me along in spelling and in grammer far better than the classes I attended in school. Perhaps those who are reading this are laughing and comparing my work thinking to yourselves that I don't stand a chance at anything that I write being published. But then again with some of the things that I have seen published recently I believe that anyone stands a chance.

Once more thank you one and all. And I apologize greatly and a thousand times over for not completing some of the stories I have already started.

~Lady Bern
