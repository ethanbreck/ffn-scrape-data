Author has written 6 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Legend of Zelda, and Final Fantasy X.

My name is Ash, I am 17, I enjoy fantasy/sci-fi mashup video games, I rarely anymore watch anime, but if the art is good and the story and characterization are impeccable I'll follow it (a favorite is Elfen Lied), yet I am a rabid lover of pokemon. If you dislike literature with high studies of human emotion and large use of metaphors and other poetic devices, intelligent speech, original characters, or infrequent updates, you might as well click the back button. Want me to update? Harrass me publicly, regularly, and in groups? Doesn't work. Type me up a thoughtful comment on a chapter or two and you'll have me eating out the palm of your hands. My definition of intelligent meaning letting me know what you thought, why, who you relate to, telling me about memories or feelings it reminded you of, or even trying to guess what my next page will be about.

In Progress: Yes, I know, my FFVII series is screwed up 6 ways to Sunday, but bear with me on this one until I can sort them out again.

A Wretch like me- (FFVII) Prequel to Pandora's Paradox. How, exactly, would any woman find herself with Sephiroth's child? Who was she, what kind of mother might be fitting for Sephiroth to impregnate? He was, after all, a man before a beast.

The Legend of Zelda- The Siren's Song- (Zelda) Basically a story that could pretty much be a Zelda game. Another pretty lady, another curse, another chance for Ganondorf to try to screw up Hyrule.

Pandora's Paradox- (FFVII; OC based) Sequel to A Wretch Like Me. Sephiroth has had a daughter, and somehow with Cetra blood. Cetra and Jenova contradict each other directly, and even the slightest upsetting of the delicate balance can end up in mayhem.

It Didn't Use to Be this Way- (FFX; OC based as well as Seymour focused) Action occurs in and out of game, most of Seymour's life follows what is told in the game. Fanpairing, OCxSeymour. Will make some attempt to not affect FFX's actual storyline with OC's interactions with Seymour in-game.
