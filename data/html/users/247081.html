You want to know about me? First of all.. I obsess over Rurouni Kenshin, and Inuyasha. I; as well as some friends, am convinced I am Kenshins reincarnation. He was a real person, after all...

Oh yes...I like to be myself, I honestly couldn't care less what people think. I really don't see the point in a realtion-ship yet, considering everyone I've known who's had one has been hurt. However. I do look foreward to one, eventually. Right now I am in the point of my life where I am trying to discover myself. Meaning I don't know what I plan to do in the future.

I'm a bit paranoid, a tiny bit anti social, and I am female. Oh, I have many different sides, but am a generally nice person.

I fear spiders, and change doesn't much affect me. My sister moved, but I haven't yet begun to miss her, I have never missed anyone before, saddly. I'm 14, with terrible spelling, and a worse memory at times.

I like the colors silver, purple and black the most. Yes, I consider black a color.

I wrote poems...Maybe me will post...Many dark poems, I have trouble writing nice, happy things...
