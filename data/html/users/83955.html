Katronette? She's boring. But she likes to talk about herself, so here she goes.

She's sixteen and American, to her intense dissatisfaction. She is a junior in high school. She's very petite. She likes British comedy, musicals, opera, and drama. She's an actress. People have compared her to a particularly pedantic branch of the Ankh-Morpork Poets' Guild, which she takes as something of a compliment. Her favorite author is William Shakespeare. Her favorite living author is David Sedaris. She quite likes Greek mythology. She's a pagan. She is a vegetarian. She fangirls Mercutio of Romeo and Juliet.

She likes to read pretty well everything as long as it's well-written.She does not mind either slash or het, as long as they're both well-written. Her fandoms are Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Shakespeare. She realizes that bad angst and bad romance are diseases. She thinks that anyone who writes them needs help. She would like other people to realize that constructive criticism is not the same thing as flaming. She likes to concrit. She's usually very thorough and polite. (She will not beta for you if you get mad at her for her review.) She will flame sometimes, but only if you really really deserve it.

Quote: "The most adventurous of our sex will hear within herself a voice that says,'Be beautiful if you can, virtuous if you wish; but be discreet, you must!'" --Beaumarchais, The Marriage of Figaro or One Mad Day.
