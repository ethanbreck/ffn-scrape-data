Ok, I know I said I would never post on ff.net anymore because they deleted one of my fics. (Damn that NC-17 ban). But I changed my mind. Let's face it, this is the best fanfiction domain out there even with the NC-17 ban so I'm back. (sigh) What can I say? I'm weak and I know it. So I'm back and I'm here to stay, at least for the mean time.

Here's a list of the fics that I will update soon:

1. Tainted Glass (Inuyasha)

I haven't given up on this fic. I've just hit a writer's block, but don't worry I will finish this fic. I'm already working on the next chapter.

2. History of Vegeta (DBZ)

Working on it.

3. Deadly Seduction (SM)

My new fic so of course I'm finishing it. For those of you who liked "Silver Rose" you'll love this one and if you're into general/senshi pairings, then this is the fic for you.

Those fics I won't be finishing (at least not any time soon)

1. Sailor Moon Bridges and Goddess

I'm not really into the whole senshi bit anymore. I might be sometime in the future but for right now, i'm into SM AU's.

2. In Destiny's Hands

I want to finish HOV first and I don't know how long that will take and since this fic has similar themes, it would be best for me to work on it last.

3. A Light in the Darkness

I'm not ready to do another fantasy fic right now. When I finish my other fics though I might pic this one back up. It was a good idea and I want to see where I can take it, but I can't focus on it right now.

I more than likely won't be starting any more series fics until I finish the above 3 or at least until I'm half way through.

To all you Tainted Glass fans, I'm sorry for the delay but I promise I will finish this fic. It's becoming my baby so I'm going to complete it, don't worry.

To all interested in Passion and Possession:

I've gotten some reviews and emails recently from people wondering when I would update this fic. I'm sorry everyone but P&P was only meant to be a one-shotter or short story. I won't be adding another chapter and I'm flattered by all the reviews I've gotten for it. I'm glad you guys like it. If anyone wants to take up where I left off, go for it. Don't forget to let me know though, I'd love to read it. Who knows, maybe when I finish all my other series fics I might consider making this one a series too. It would be a long time coming though.
