Hi, I'm Carrie a.k.a boxers-or-briefs ;). I've been on this site since I was barely a teenager, or before, I don't even remember it's been so long. I've been writing here on and off for years. I'm now 28 years old and run my own business. Whenever I feel the need to write, I come back here to my comfort zone. I've written solely for 7th Heaven. I used to love the show and watched it constantly. I don't really watch it anymore, but I still feel a connection to the characters, so I use them for my stories.

I love the atmosphere here at FanFiction and greatly enjoy the support from those that take the time to read what I've written and give me feedback - so, THANK YOU!! :)

My life these days is really busy so it might take some time for me to update. I have a little boy, and 2 girls now and I run a jewelry business on Etsy. If you want to check it out to see what I've been up to for the past year, you can head over there and search Pilboxx, and you'll find my little shop. :)

Anyway, I'll probably be in and out over here. I know I still have stories from 2008 that have gone unfinished, but maybe I'll get around to completing them one of these days. Or just posting random new stories, who knows. :) Whatever I get the inspiration to do.

Thanks for reading this little rambling. I hope you enjoyed learning a little bit about me. :)

Carrie
