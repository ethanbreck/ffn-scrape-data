Author has written 2 stories for Sailor Moon, and Sailor Moon X-overs.

Please only constructive critics.

So after a very long time away from writing, I am trying to get back into the habit of writing every day. I am a proud teacher of 7th grade ELA (English Language Arts) and that means I encourage 11, 12, and 13 year-old students to write and share that writing with me. I have been writing for them for the last 7 years and, while they have achieved much success in their STAAR scores, it has been all academic writing for me. With this new school year, I have set a goal of writing for myself and sharing my fiction writing with my students. I hope this will inspire them to write creatively as well and I can pass along my love of language and the written word in a new form (maybe not have it seem like all writing is homework and boring).

I can't believe the stories I have on this site started when I was 11 years old. It inspires me that maybe my students have this kind of writing inside them and hopefully I can mentor a future author. We'll just have to see where the written road takes us.
