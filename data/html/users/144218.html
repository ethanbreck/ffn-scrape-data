Hello there to everyone who is taking the time to read my little bio!

My name is Rachel. I'm an art history major, world history minor undergrad graduate, and am currently an assistant buyer for my retail job. I haven't really frequented FF.net very often these days, but I occasionally stop by.

I run two domains - is home to Sinister Attraction - the Spuffy Zone for the BtVS Writer's Guild, and home to over 1300 stories, as well as Poetic Attraction - the only William and Buffy site, with fan fiction, artwork, and other extras. The sites suffered some major down time and slight loss of data, but the archive is in tact and has been untouched for several years. Please note that SA is now a .net address instead of a .org address like it was for so many years.

And my newer website is which is one of the largest non-livejournal fanfiction sites for Sam/Dean and Jared/Jensen from Supernatural. Lots of great stories there by some wonderful writers.

Updated 3/6/2011 - Believe it or not, I still have people e-mailing me asking about Defying Reason and Time for Love and if it will ever be finished. Truthfully? I have no clue. I want to say "no," but then a part of me says to not tell you guys that and try to get off my butt and finish the last quarter or so of the story. I have the majority of the last chapter and epilogue finished, but there are probably anywhere from 3-6? chapters before I feel like I could conclude the story and achieve all the plot points I had originally planned out. I guess for any readers who are interested in the epilogue, feel free to contact me and I can send you a copy of the unedited chapter. Never say never, right? I may come back to this story. Thanks guys!
