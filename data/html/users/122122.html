Professional life sucks. I don't have time to write anymore...but sometimes my drunken harlot of a muse comes back and makes a mess of my life, and I can't function correctly as a human being until I shut that stupid pixie up.

Don't expect any masterpieces here. I'm too focused on getting rid of the stories to bother giving them more than a once or twice over before posting them. But I do, generally, promise decent plots, occasional bouts of brilliant humor, and generally one very long story per fandom.
