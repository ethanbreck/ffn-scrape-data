Current Obsession

One Piece

I've fallen head over heels in love with this series. So much so that for the first time in... a long, long time, I've felt the urge to really write again. And that, trust me, says a lot since I've become a greater fan of reading fanfiction than writing it.

Oh, One Piece. *swoons, flails and...* Marry me till death do we still never part?

Pre-2005 Fics

In short, all pre-2005 fics have been discontinued.

I really apologize for that and to anyone who has been waiting forever-and-a-half for an update (shows that I should never publish a fic without writing at least ninety percent of it first *sweatdrop*), but I no longer have any interest in them.

And while reading them over now is actually nauseating (especially the pre-2004 ones), I've decided to leave the fics up anyway for that one-in-a-hundred-million person that still actually wants to read any one of my fics.
