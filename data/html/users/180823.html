~~UPDATE~~
1/20/06

I know there's no excuse for why I haven't updated...heh...but I promise I'm trying to get some writing done.

Oh, one more thing. JK Rowling gave a great interview with Mugglenet and The Leaky Cauldron. If you haven't checked that out, you need to. She cleared up a few things about the relationships, too. One of my favorite quotes of her's being...

"Yes, we do now know that it's Ron and Hermione. I do feel that I have dropped heavy hints. ANVIL-sized, actually, hints, prior to this point."

Of course I already knew that it would be Ron and Hermione. Everyone should have known, after she said this in a interview in 1999...

"Harry and Hermione are very platonic nonsexual friends. But I won't answer for anyone else. Nudge, nudge, wink, wink."

So now, people have been writing hate mail to JK because she chose to write it R/HR instead of H/HR. I'm sorry, but anyone who ignoredthe fact that she already clearedthis situation up, and then got their feelings hurt...well, who's fault is it really?

"The problem with America is stupidity. Now I'm not saying there should be capital punishment for stupidity, but why don't we just take the warning labels off everything and let the problem solve itself?"
