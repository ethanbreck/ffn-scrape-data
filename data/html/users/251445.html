Current Projects:

Diamonds and Steel: NOT DEAD, just an undefined hiatus. Extremely uninspired. I've hated what they've done with D/C in the second, and now the third season, and it's sucked all the inspiration to write them out of me. But I do know everything that's going to happen with this story, so I certainly won't be entirely abandoning it.

Evil Incorporated: My current love, chapters forthcoming. With all the excitement of the new movies coming out, I'm really into the HP universe, which is getting funneled into this story. As usual, chapters will take a while, but this story's turned from a side project to my little baby.

Mousehunt: Still a side project, but getting written slowly but surely. Would really love to turn this into a comic, but I can't seem to find a style that lets me express the way I write visually. I've given up on reproducing something manga style, and am currently looking through the lens of American-style comics, which seem to include a lot more narration.

Shattered Time: Ummm...dead, as much as I hate to admit to abandoning a story. I started it as a redo of One Summer Day (now that I've improved immensely as a writer), and it's just too big for me. Gundam Wing and Sailor Moon are both oldies for me now, and it's hard to get back into the groove of the characters. I may write another Rei/Wufei someday, since they're my two favorite characters, but for now I'm concentrating on my other two stories.
