Starting Bio

Name: Little Blossom (AKA Blossom)

Sex: (No thanks) Female

Species: Zombie (A University Zombie)

Habitat: Library

Behaviours: She appears sporadically and disappears in the same manner. She writes and updates on misty whims and likes candy with an unhealthy obsession. Her writing style changes with the tides and her sleep patterns resemble a directionally challenged line as insomnia drives her batty. She is quite friendly though, just a little ADD, and is probably a little too materialistic for her own good.

Likes: Sweets. Blood. Manga. Books. Video games. Miscellaneous Things. No particular interests. She follows her brain (Mmm brains).

Dislikes: Very few things. She is mostly neutral. She does not tolerate stupidity directed towards her though, or attacks on those she's fond of.

Rituals: Baking

End Bio

But on a more serious note about myself, I do take forever to update. I have a very involved life outside of writing on this site, or even in general. Everything not finished, I've known how I was going to end them for a while, I just don't have the time. Some stories will be written quickly on the spot, some I put care into the message I want to get across. I'm very finicky. I currently want to go back and redo everything, but I keep everything to see if I progress anywhere.

I fancy and write many different things. And while it appears that I write and like only slash (boy love specifically), I do enjoy het and girl love. It's just that most of the series I like are shounen series and they tend to seriously lack in the estrogen department.

I have many series I adore. Many I don't write for. Many I keep meaning to.

Oh, and I don't tend to reply to reviews. For I am a lazy :nods: and often sleep deprived like woez orz.

Fandom Stats:

For PoT fans. Um ... I'm kind of not in the Thrill Pair frame of mind of late. I am still very fond of the pairing, I'm just not really into it the way I used to be. I still might come up with a few more things and finish older ones. But no promises.

D.Gray-Man: There is this fic I've been working on for years. I will finish it one day even if it kills me. It is Lena/Lavi/Kanda/Allen because I think their polygamous relationship will be all kinds of dysfunctional (but functional) adorable.

Kuroko no Basket: Still writing. Just working a lot. Studying a lot. Writing is slow, but I'm still playing with the fandom.

A lot of fics I write are gift fics though, so who really knows what fandom it'll be. I have too many one night stands with a lot of series.
