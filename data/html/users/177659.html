Why must I be Omelette
hide bio
Follow . Favorite
Joined 02-28-02, id: 177659
Author has written 17 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Pokémon, Bible, and Harry Potter.
Hello! There's not much about me that's noteworthy; I'm a fanfiction writer who hates 99% of fanfiction... I can't stand slash (not because I'm homophobic-- because it's flurking OOC!), fluff, slut!Draco, unrealistic angst, Mary Sue (that includes GinnySue and HermioneSue), or badly written claptrap in general. Wait... with that in mind, let's make that 99.9% of fanfiction that I hate. ^_^
I took off my fanfiction about Lily, James, and Sirius... Although I thought it was good, Severus's pensieve has ruined it for me. I wish every fic author would do the same, and take off their fics that have been rendered untrue by book five, but the day that happens, Hell will freeze over.

I read Harry Potter fanfiction. Most of it on this site is complete rubbish, but recently, I've found a few good ones. Behold, in alphabetical by author:
Shades of Grey by Aleph Null
Halfway to Hell by Aleph Null
Ludlow Fair by Angel of the North
A Bridge by crimsonthread
Fractured Triangle by Fyrie
Their Mingled Tears by lakemonster

If anyone can recommend a GOOD HP fanfic site or mailing list, I will kiss their dragon-hide boots.

Updates:
I added three ficlets; they will not be sequeled or chaptered (why does everyone want that? I love ficlets as opposed to multi-chaptered stories; am I the only one?). One is Blaise-y, one is Ron-ish, and the other one is full of Flamel goodness. None are slash, none except the Ron one could be considered to have an eveninkling of romance, and all are guarenteed OC free.
Since I have support services, you can add me to your author alert. It's a very nifty little service, so if you like my stuff, take full advantage of the fact that I pay $5 a month for it.
