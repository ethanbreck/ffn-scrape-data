I thought maybe this would be a good time to update this. And maybe make it make any sense at all. I am not confident in my abilities to do the latter, but I shall strive to.

Me: I'm Poison Ivory, or Jess. I go to Barnard College, where I pretend to be studying while actually reading fanfic. Vanilla lattes are the essence of my soul.

What I Write: I used to write an obscene amount of Hey Arnold!fic, until the show got cancelled and my Hey Arnold!muse apparently retreated into its bedroom to sulk, teenager-like, until The Jungle Movie is released. So who knows when my next HA!fic will be out. That said, I have not abandoned my unfinished ones, and am determined to continue with The Queen's Treasue, Always, Home For Christmas, and Before Woman. Also, eventually I'll probably tweak the end of Crazy For You. Also, eventually I'll finish the one-shots sitting gathering dust on my hard drive. And write the OTHER long saga-type stories I've planned. And make my website. *le sigh*

In the meantime, I mainly write Newsies slash. Because JackandDavidtheirloveissoobvious. Yes. The odd other fanfic may pop up from time to time (Down, Princess Bride plot bunnies! Down!) but you can pretty much count on Newsies and Hey Arnold from me. And that's that.

Exit music, please.

-PI
