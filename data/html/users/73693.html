Author has written 11 stories for Lunar: Silver Star, Card Captor Sakura, Ranma, Lunar: Eternal Blue, Rosario + Vampire, and Naruto.

Writing isn't just about putting words into a story, it's about creating life where none exists. A writer can tell a story, it takes an author to give it depth and make the characters seem real. And on that note, I'm so damned sick of all the self-insertions, Mary Sues, Slash, Out of Character, poorly written, and overly short chapters with a synopsis longer then the entire story for a single chapter. Too damned many fanfic authors have absolutely no writing skill and need to realise that. Fanfiction is something that could be very beautiful, and fun, but too many poor ones are giving us all a bad reputation. As for me, I'm an avid gamer and roleplayer. I'm currently schooling to be a radio DJ, and I love writing. I'm looking for a good artist though, who can draw a storyboard. I wanna write for a webcomic, but I lack artistic skill other then with words. contact me if you want to co-op on a webcomic
