Author has written 14 stories for Harry Potter.

Update: August 30th, 2005. There are a -lot- of people waiting on Silent Moon and the Truth About Werewolves, and I am -so- sorry that I haven't updated. Unfortunately, Silent Moon is currently on a computer that... doesn't work. My brother claims he can fix it, but who knows when that will happen? Other than that, school... school, lots and lots. I really hope I can get to work on these two stories, though. I'm sorry to leave everyone without updates! Feel free to nudge me with e-mails and reviews. It may help!

I'm a 18 year old female Leo. I am also a very dedicated Harry Potter fan. I live in Pennsylvania, USA, way out in the middle of nowhere. I'm going to Clarion University, studying Library Science. I love to write, but I don't have much experience at it, and I can't spell for the life of me. When I write Harry Potter, it's american Harry Potter, because I have never been to England, nor do I know anyone who has.

What Am I Writing?

The Truth About Werewolves - A different look at Remus Lupin, and all werewolves. Suicidal themes, with a little bit of language, as well.

The Wolf and His Boy - Not actually posted yet. I plan on finishing this one before I start to post it. Will feature Remus and Harry in an AU world. Will probably be put off until after book 6 is released.

Lost - Based on books 1-5, for the exception of that tiny little detail of Sirius being dead. Didn't know he died? What are you doing here, then? I'm glad I ruined it for you. You should have known by now. On the other hand, he's not dead in this story. Hurrah for denial!

Poetry - Any particular scene that gives me a strong emotion will get a poem. So far, I've only written one based on the fifth book, so expect more soon.

Silent Moon - Will be taken down on the next update of The Truth About Werewolves.

What Have I Written?

My Name Is Sirius - Angsty, and written for no particular reason. I was just wondering why he had that name.

Fight Between Friends - I didn't write it, but it's posted here with permission. Read it, it'll cheer you up!

Why? - A poem about the death in the fifth book. If he hadn't been showing off...

Rising Shadows - Based off 1-4. After reading book five, I noticed I was closer to the truth than I thought, but still far enough off to stop me from writing a sequel. Sorry about that.

A Simple Child - A poem based on Remus Lupin and his monthly "adventure."

The Light Shudders - Originally, this was supposed to be about a suicide, and it still is, but I also notice that it seems a lot like someone in Azkaban, so I posted it here. It's also an expansion of Prisoner...

Prisoner - This wasn't intended to be about Azkaban, either. It's the first poem I wrote, and it was written for an english assignement. We had to use a lot of adjectives, even though I didn't like them. I'm not really fond of this one.

That's all about me. Feel free to contact me: about my stories or someone else's stories or whatever you want. I love contact! Please?

And finally...

"I believe the road to Hell is paved with Adverbs, and I will shout it from the rooftops!"

-Stephen King, On Writing
