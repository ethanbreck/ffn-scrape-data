Hello, it is I - George!

Who am I, you ask?

Well, I'm a college student from Minnesota who loves the Harry Potter books. Oh, and I'm female. I've been told that this is something that needs to be stated as my name is generally reserved for boys. Damn boys!

Now don't bite my head off if you're a boy, there's nothing wrong with boys per say. In fact many of my best friends are boys, and surprisingly enough I actaully do love my little brother a lot. It's just that some boys are poop-heads and most boys that I've met are evil in some way (an accusation that they readily agree with).

Now the only other important thing about me is that I tend to rant quite a bit, which I'm sure that you've noticed already. If you don't like it, it doesn't matter because I'm not going to change (trust me, several people have already tried to change me and it doesn't work ;)

On that note I will say farewell for now!

Oh, and always remember - Do not anger a dragon, for you are crunchy and go well with brie! :)
