Hi, I'm a 22-year-old student from the UK doing an MA at Exeter University. Most of my fanfiction is slash, and the majority of that is Draco/Harry, though I like other slash pairings too!

Hello everyone. This is just an update to answer several questions you may have, so you don't have to bother emailing me.

1) I am not writing any fanfiction at the moment. I am in the middle of a Masters at University and really haveno time. In fact, I kind of think I've grown out of writing it, especially Harry Potter fanfiction. BUT I will never say never. You just don't know when the inspiration will strike. Rest assured, if I do write any it will be posted here, but first at my LJ (link at the top of this page). I really do hope I'll have some ideas soon.

2) The Lightning Letters Universe is finished. I have no plans to write anything else here.

3) I have no plans to write a sequel to SIS. I did have at one point, but I'm afraid that plot bunny is no more! Sorry.

4) Courtship, Wooing... was only ever intended as a one-shot. I am not planning a sequel.

5) I am not going to finish In Love and War. The main reason is that JK completely copied me when she came up with Dolores Umbridge ;)! Also, two books have come out since I started writing it and now I find it's too AU. I'm leaving it up, however, just in case anyone wants to reread it.

6) I have taken down The Journey. I intend to finish it in the summer. I feel I should, really. I won't put it back up on a public forum, but if anyone wants a copy please email me in July or so and I'll send you a word document containing the whole thing. If I manage to finish it before then I'll put a notice up here. Sorry if this pisses anyone off!

It never ceases to amaze me how many kind people take the time to review.It always makes me feel fuzzy inside. Soif you read my fics, I'd love a review.They really do make my day.

Thanks a lot, everyone.

ARI MUNAMI. XXXX
