6/22/2017 Hello all, I'm back. After six long years, I have returned. A lot older, a lot poorer, and a lot more wiser about the justice system. They get rich while I got poorer. But I'm done with all of that s*. I've learned so much about myself, and you certainly find out who your true friends are. So, on we go with the Harry Potter's Ninja Wizard. Things certainly are getting worse for our hero.

9/6/2018

I'm still here. Kind of lost my enthusiasm for writing so that's why the new chapter is late. My apologies. I am intending to merge books six and seven together as I felt there wasn't enough action going. To me all the characters did was to sit around and listen to Dumbledore expound on Riddle's past and watch memories he obtained once a week. Harry let his obsession over Malfoy keep him from doing what it was he needed to do and that was to work very hard at learning spells and battle techniques. So having said that, I am going to depart a bit from the last two books and write out what I thought should have happened. That I think is one of the greatest advantages to writing fan fiction and posting the stories to fanfiction.net, they allow us to create what we believe would be great stories in how we believe the characters of any given genre should act. So I am going to do the ending to Harry Potter a bit differently, and I hope all of you will like it.

To all of those out there who have given up on your stories, please don't. You created good starts, please keep it going. There are those of us out there who truly love reading your imaginations printed out on a page for us to read.

David
