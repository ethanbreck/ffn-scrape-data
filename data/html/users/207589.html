I'm just a poor 21-year-old journalism student who is WAY too busy to be writing and posting (not to mention reading) fanfiction. But I do anyway. So there.

Anyway, what follows is a rant about things that annoy me in fanfiction. Take it for what it is, or ignore it. Either works for me.

1. affect: to produce an effect upon

effect: to bring about, power to bring about a result

Same applies for affective/effective, affecting/effecting. They are NOT interchangeable.

2. Will you ACCEPT this as a token of my thanks? Everyone EXCEPT you may come with me.

3. Anyway, backward, forward, toward, afterward do NOT end with an 's.'

4. It's vs its. "Its" is the possessive form.

5. Plural forms of words do NOT have an 's. Possessive forms do. "The BOOKS are on the shelf." "The BOOK'S pages are torn."

6. "She spoke ALOUD." "He was ALLOWED to go."

7. "That is YOUR book." "YOU'RE not going anywhere."

Anyway, that's all I can think of for now.
