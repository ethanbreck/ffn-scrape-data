So I was going through some stuff on my computer and I discovered a bunch of my Newsie fic and decided to put it back up here because...why not? I don't know... Pretty much I was bored one day. So, I went through a bunch of them and semi-editted them which was fun and finished up a couple so that was exciting...and kind of fun to read them since I hadn't for quite a few years. So, hopefully you all enjoy them...I very much doubt I will be doing any more with any of this though cause I kind of got out of the whole fanfiction scene but whatever it's cool.

Most of the stories are just completley random. I do have one series and just so you know the order goes: 1) Unexpected Friendship 2) Wrongfully Attracted 3) The Passed is Past 4) Sounds Like a Plan I kind of redid those stories a bit and retitled them, so if you happen to have read them before yes they are pretty much the same exact stories with a couple little things added and some of the spelling errors fixed :)

Also a lot of the original characters aren't mine...most of them were random people from the NML so if you notice one of the characters in my stories are remarkably like one of yours...it probably is one of yours lol
