Heh. Isn't my nickname cute? I'm not saying I'm JKR, that'd've been really terrible, cause then you'd get very bad Potter stories, but yeah, it's just an edited play on my name by one of my good friends. I used to be barbequed, as in, that was my nickname, so yeah.

I usually don't write, I just read.

I
Like: R/H, McG/AD, McG/SS... Stories that have gone through thorough grammar and spelling checks, stories without a lot of Voldemort, but just romance...Haha... Hermione with bushy hair and her full name... Squin's, Ozma's, Arabella's and etc.'s stories : )... everything else in my favourite lists : )

Dislike: Slash. R/Lavender, H/G, H/H... Hermione getting nicknames... Hermione without bushy hair...Stories which everyone basically do not like, blah.

Dum de dum. I think it's kinda weird to announce what are your likes and dislikes. I mean, who cares? No one's going to write a story along those guidelines, are they? But hey, it's fun to write them down!
