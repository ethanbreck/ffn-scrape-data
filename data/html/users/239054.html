Author has written 25 stories for Yu Yu Hakusho, Fullmetal Alchemist, Stargate: SG-1, Final Fantasy X, and Star Fox.

Well hello there, dearest Reader! I bid you welcome to this humble page which I call another home. Here you will find an interesting collection of fan fiction, both canon and non-canon, most pieces being especially the latter.

The majority of my works here are short pieces; samples from my challenge with the 100_women community over at Live Journal, that I completed at the end of January. I deal with primarily the YYH ladies and an occasional drift into another fandom, but YYH is my base and I'm pretty set in it.

Additionally, I want to clear something up. You guys may jump into a story that looks like an excerpt from a longer one, and I'm sorry to say that unfortunately, that is an incorrect assumption. These stories are only scenes born of the prompts I've been challenged with. If I really had the mental energy to devote to these prompts (while I deal with life at the same time), I would gladly write longer stories to incorporate with the rest of what you're reading. However, this a reality I live in and I can't give such lavish attention to my work like I used to.


With The Earring finally complete (albeit after seven years!), I will now devote my creativity to new ideas and will try to go in another direction. I will continue to update my little Yusuke and Yukina series with the pieces I have already completed, and in the meantime I hope to conjure something new and intriguing. Not sure what that'll be yet, but stay tuned!

Why am I rambling so much? XD Thank you for your time and I hope you find what you're looking for!
