Update Section:

3/1/11

It has been a long time since I posted or did anything regarding stories. I've been roleplaying more than writing and it doesn't look as though that will change anytime soon. I'm going to attempt to do more writing but eh, in college and most of my attention and time is going toward that over anything else. I've also started to pull away from the canon world of Naruto and focus on my OCs instead so there is the whole lack of interest in writing stories that are canon-based.

About Me:

I never really used this to introduce myself but here goes. I'm Star, a twenty-eight year old Southerner. My three addictions are: music, reading and anime. Not in that order but music does rate very high for me. I always have my nose in a book (or at least 95 of the time reading something even if it's a fanfiction) and been known to ignore everything while reading to the point I wouldn't notice if there was an earthquake happening. As someone with her head in the clouds a majority of the time, I tend to be boring. XD At least I think I am. My friends seem to find me entertaining, especially when I just had a boatload of sugar and/or caffeine. (Think Shikamaru on speed.)
