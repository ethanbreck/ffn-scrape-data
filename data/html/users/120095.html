Hi, I'm back.

I've sort of been in self induced exile for the past couple years. It's not that I haven't been reading fan fiction, I just haven't had much motivation or time really to write any. I still don't really have a lot of time to work on it but I seemed to have rediscovered that itch to write again so here I am.

Hmm, let's see. I write primarily Cruel Intentions stories, namely cause I love the character Kathryn and Sebastian but also because I love the whole debauched world. There's a certain way they talk and act, that's kind of not like any other fandom and while I enjoy other fandom's it's the only one I feel completely comfortable writing in. I love the movie (and the many other Dangerous Liaisons interpretations) but was always deeply unsatisfied by the ending. I think it left a lot to be explored in fan fiction.

I've written about 30 CI stories, 5 of which aren't featured on this sitenas they were written back in the ‘olden' days when you could post NC17 stories on this ff.net. However you can find them on my CI website (which yes, hasn't been updated in ages)

They are:

Something Different, Turn Around, Summer in the Country, Homecoming, The Missing Scene

I believe I posted my first story here back in December of ‘01. I tend to cringe when I read a lot of my stuff, especially the older stories. Most of them are badly in need of a beta but I rarely use them. I think I've gotten slightly better with my grammar and such as the years have gone on but not tons. Sorry kids, that's just not my forte.

Okay, here's some updated info on my current projects:

Cruel Intentions: The Serial: Sweet Lord. I published the first chapter of this in November of ‘03 and here we are 5 years later and I'm still not done with it. Well I can tell you there will be exactly 20 more chapters and everything, down to the chapter titles, is already plotted and planned it's just a matter of me writing it. A few notes about this story:

While I promise Kathryn & Sebastian will eventually hook up, they will not, I repeat NOT, fall in love. This isn't that story. There's nothing sentimental or romantic about this fic. I'm just throwing that out there in case you weren't tipped off by Sebastian blackmailing Kathryn into sleeping with the chess team against her will or her making her stepbrother think he slept with a dude. No love, but they are fond of each other...

Originally I intended to make a sequel to this. Sort of K&S adventures during their first year of college. I don't know if I'm still gonna do it, thinking it over.

All This History We've Written: So when I start a story I don't write the first chapter until I have everything planned far in advance. I know the middle, the end, the chapter count, etc. When I wrote the first chapter of this story 2 years ago I kind of winged it. Oh I had an idea of what I wanted to do, knew I wanted to do a Kathryn/Sebastian/Annette triangle and such but I didn't really plan it out. So when I started writing again I kind of scratched any initial plans I had for the story and started out fresh, hence a title change (it was originally called Killer Queen as it was then going to focus more squarely on Kathryn) and it now features the world's longest prologue. Anyway given I have little time to write these days, this story will have many chapters but they will be closer in length to CI: The Serial. Little of what's to come:

This will be the EPIC Kathryn/Sebastian story. Seriously. We go from childhood all the way to adulthood and everything in between. It will be long/angst-y/romantic/sexy, the whole bit. At this point I just pray I'll finish it as I've spent a great deal of time plotting the fucker out. Wish me luck and I hope you like it for it may be my final CI outing. Not sure yet.

Dangerous Liaisons: I'll be honest, I kind of planned on letting this one hang as I was kinda over the whole CI mystery murder angle I was going for in the second half of this story. However, I recently stumbled across (literally) all my old notes for this story and I kind of got inspired. So I'm hoping to get this updated once ATHWW is up and running a bit. As I've mentioned Time is an issue and this is a story with long, involved chapters so it might be awhile but I will do my best to get it updated. I can tell you there is 8 more chapters left and yes, we do find out who shot Steven.

Hiatus:

Let the Games Begin: This is likely done for, indefinitely. For the life of me I can't recall what I intended to do with it. Thanks to everyone who's shown an interest in it.

Second Generation: I was planning to put this on full hiatus like LTGB but I don't know, I just reread it and I forgot how much I like the characters of Sydney and Garret (named of course after the characters from Swans Crossing or as I like to call them, baby Kathryn and Sebastian). However my notes for this one are long gone and I vaguely remember what I wanted to do with this Romeo&Juliet on crack story. Hmm. We'll see.

Okay I'll end this by pimping out my website (again) featuring all sorts of fic by different authors, some of which isn't on this site. Also my yahoo group:

Thanks again to everyone who reads and reviews my stuff and your support all these years. Without you I would have stopped writing a long time ago. bye bye
