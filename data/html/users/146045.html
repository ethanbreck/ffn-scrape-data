Hey, I'm Meagfen,

Twenty-something UK born teacher currently living the high life in Egypt with my idiot of a golden retriever. I'm a bit of a lurker with a tendency to flit between fandoms. An occasional writer, and an even more infrequent vidder...

Could accurately be described as a musically talented(ish), football (soccer) crazy tv, film and game enthusiast. Always full of ideas, but never seem to have to time to spend on them. Curse the need to work full time!

I seem to ship something in every fandom I like, even if it's not a particular popular pairing. I am constantly plotting out multi-chaptered epics, writing several one shots (normally all at once) and I frequently panic about the quality of the work I'm producing... such is life!

Currently writing for:

The 100 (Kabby - Abby/Marcus) Doctor Who (Whouffaldi - Twelve/Clara) The Avengers (Bruce/ Natasha, Wanda/Vision) The Blacklist (Lizzington - Red/Lizzie) The Last Ship (Rachel/ Tom, Rachel/ Tex)

P.S. I'm on tumblr too under the same username

The Mentalist WIPs:
I know that some people are waiting for some long overdue updates for these two stories (Together We Stand and Blood on the Scales) but at the moment I literally have no motivation to write in the Mentalist fandom, which sucks immensely. I'm behind on S7 as well, so maybe when I've caught up with everything I'll get a bit of inspiration. Until then, I guess those two stories are on semi-permanent hiatus... I am so so sorry to anyone that is following them; I promise that I will finish them one day, I just can't guarantee when :(
