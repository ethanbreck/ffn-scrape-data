Now that I have a few minutes to type...

My name is Amber. I'm a rabid Final Fantasy VII fan in my twenties. Writing is just a hobby to pass the time. I enjoy reading fanfiction and am always lurking around, but rarely finish anything I start writing myself. Of course, I'm working to change that...

Currently I am: Working on three stories at once. What am I doing?

Have a lovely day.

08/29/13
I've started a livejournal as a place to keep some of my random scribbles that won't make it all the way to story form. If you're looking for more of my writing, that's where it will be.

7/23/13

Every couple of years I use fanfiction to get past writer's block in original work. I really need to sit down and finish some half done stories I have and post them here. For now, a humorous little story about Vincent being turned into a frog.

5/22/11
A new Yuffietine up for your enjoyment... if you enjoy that kind of thing. Maybe I'll do more. We'll see. :)

3/16/08
Not too much exciting, I guess. I finally have an avatar. It was done up by C-D... () Amazing artist! I'm also up now as a Beta Reader. If you're looking for someone, I'm more than willing.

Due to the shocking response to If Only, I'm finding myself more and more inclined to work on it. I'm even thinking of things to do in advance. Apologies to those who might be upset by the slightly adult turn of things. As always with this story, I'm writing the stuff that seems funny in my head in the middle of the night.
