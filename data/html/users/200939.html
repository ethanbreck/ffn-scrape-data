Author has written 11 stories for Harry Potter, Glee, and Pirates of the Caribbean.

Ever since NaNoWriMo '05, creative writing has been incredibly difficult for me (which is why Picture A Pirate took about four years to get its final chapter). I honestly think I used up most of my creativity for a while. Now and then I read my stories and wonder what the heck I was thinking when I wrote that; the writing that I do get done is in a very different style now. And there's a reason most of my stuff is oneshots; I tend to like the scenes I write better than a whole story, especially when I don't have a clear goal for the story. Even though I'm not thrilled with all of them, people seem to like them, so I'm leaving them up. Picture A Pirate will probably get a big revision at one point (to fix typos, if nothing else), and Eight Summer's Time could probably use it too...

Anyway--enjoy and have fun reading, whether its my stories or another author's stories!
