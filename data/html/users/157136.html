Hi,

I'm a 23 year-old French girl in her sixth year of medical studies. That means lots of stress, lots of work and little free time. But well only one year left to go before the internship/residency.

I apology in advance for the poor grammar, spelling mistakes. English is my second language and my high-school lessons are now quite far. I'm mostly learning through reading and trying o improve by writing. However I'll try to do my best.

I love writing and reading, though I'm having less and less time for it. My favorite kinds of books are SF, Fantasy and detective novels as well as mythology or history related stories, more particularly concerning the Antiquity era up to the Middle Ages.

As for my hobbies, I play the piano during what little free time I still have, practice contemporary dancing and and do some horse-riding.
