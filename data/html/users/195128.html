Ok, something about me. . .

Well, I live in the southern parts of the US, TN to be exact. I was born in CA and moved to TN when I was just 2 or 3 years old. I've lived in TN since then and I mostly like it here. I'm a 16 years old Chinese girl who loves animes and books. I don't have a lot of friends and I don't mind. I also love animals and love to read anything about them, except for bugs and insects. I'm kinda overweight [135 pounds] and taller than most Asians [5 feet something]. I've never had a boyfriend and not interested in looking for one. But I am always on the look out for 'Mr. Right'. Although I seriously don't think I'll be finding him any time soon or ever really. I do support gays and such. I believe that love knows no boundry and you can not tell anyone who they have to love. I get annoyed when people are closed minded and think that there is only one way of doing things. *sighs* I'm very liberal and think that if people want to do something, who are you to say they can't? But that doesn't mean that I support drug abusers. I just think that they have the right to destroy their own bodies if they want to.

As for my reading materials. . .

I read anything. :) I believe that reading is the closest you can get to complete freedom. Because in real life there is always rules and boundrys that can not be crossed, but in books anything goes.

Animes - just some of my favorites.

Sailor Moon was my first big love. I still read Sailor Moon fics now and then. I think I was about 12 when I first found Anime in the form of Sailor Moon. Couples for this fic are Serena x Darien.

Yu Yu Hakusho is one of my top favorites. I've stop watching the Anime but I still read their fics. My favorite character is Hiei. Couple for this fic is Hiei x Kurama. (Yaoi)

Naruto is now one of my top top favorites. I'm still watching the Anime and reading the mangas. I also like to read their fics. My favorite character is Naruto and second favorite would have to be Garaa. I admire Naruto a lot. He is so determined and it makes me feel. . . something when I watch him fight. Couple for this fic would be Naruto x Sasuke.(Yaoi) I like Garaa but I can't really see him with Naruto. Now Sasuke on the other hand is perfect for Naruto, him or Hinta.

Hunter X Hunter is also one of my top favorites. I am currently watching the Anime. I haven't seen enough to set couples and read their fics yet.

InuYasha is one of my favorite reads. The fics for this Anime is wonderful. I don't watch the Anime anymore, I just read the fics now. Couples for this fics is Kagome x InuYasha, Kagome x Sess., Kagome x Mir. As you can see my favorite character is Kagome.

GetBackers is a wonderful Anime that doesn't have a lot of fics yet. Couple for this fic would have to be Ban x Ginji.

Candidate for Goddess is also a good Anime. I'm watching it now but haven't start to read their fics yet.

Fruits Basket is a wonderful Anime. I like the Anime a lot but I haven't read their fics yet.
