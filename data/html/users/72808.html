Author has written 3 stories for Phantom of the Opera, Alice, 2009, and Supernatural.

So I have not written in a long time. My life has been rather derailed by a disease many don't realize that can affect everyday life of a person. I suffer from Chronic Migraine and Occipital Neuralgia. I have painful headaches and migraine pain pretty much 24/7. Unfortunately that makes it difficult for me to focus on more than one thing or really anything. I am very lucky though, I am now married one year to a wonderful man who understands and takes great care of me. I don't have to work full time anymore so I can focus on my health. Writing is helping me with my depression from the constant everyday pain that I experience.

There is no easy way to put it but there have been days where I have wished I was dead rather than deal with the pain of my migraines. I am not ashamed to say I am on medication and seeing someone for this. Too long we as a society and pushed mental illness asides as a problem that should be looked at as something we should be shamed of. Depression is a real thing and it can kill. My depression is due to the fact that I have pain all the time, but others may have different reasons. No matter the reason, don't put someone down because you don't understand what they are going through. And when you can give a stranger a smile, you never know how much that can truly brighten up someones day. I know it helps me on my bad pain days.

So there you have it. That's why I haven't been around in a while. First I had to finish college, then I got married and now I am battling a disease that affects over 1.5 million Americans everyday and there is no cure. Nothing stops Chronic Migraine, you can only treat the symptoms.

Now for the fun stuff. I have a new obsession. SUPERNATURAL (Thank you Michelle). And of course I have a giant Character Crush on Dean Winchester, I mean who doesn't? But I don't like Desitel. I will try to finish Phantom as soon as my mind stops racing with the ideas for the Supernatural fanfic but in the meantime I appreciate you reviews.

Thank you for taking the time to read my stories.

Kristen B
