All righty, then.

So...it's been what? A few years, I think. About four if I'm completely correct, maybe three. Well, anyway, to put that aside, I have come back to finish previously unfinished business. I really just want to thank EVERYONE that has continued to review over the years. I have received AND heard your voice. However, I must inform you that my writing style has changed greatly.

I'm a junior is college...I'm going for my biology degree...and then heading off to med school. I am a busy person. I have two jobs, one full time and the other part time. I have a full time boyfriend/fiance. I just want to stress this because I know some of you might be incredibly happy that I am coming back to finish what I started and, might I say it, possibly start NEW stories. But, like I said, my writing style has changed and my vocabulary has increased. I still love all the same animes and now EVEN MORE animes.

I'm still laid back and relaxed...I tend to not be too grim. I still hate flames, but absolutely love constructive criticism no matter how hard it is. I've, also, hit a few milestones in my life and tend to be a tad bit more mature than I used to be. Don't be confused, however. I'm still just the same, old, immature me...as sad as that is to admit. XD

Anyway, please feel free to e-mail, IM, or even just review about my stories. I will be revamping 'The Christmas Spirit' to better suit my new writing style. You may find some ideas change within the story and maybe some different happenings change as well, but they will general be LONGER chapters with better language. That is my current project. I don't plan on putting out anything new until I've finished it. So, bear with me while I do this so I can finish the story for you all.

3,

KaBunny.
