Hi y'all. I'm Star, and I have a thing for sarcasm and wit drier than the Sahara. Not that I'm good at it, just that I find it funny- also a thing for rambling, and babbling. Anyway!

I started writing when I was old enough to type, though I'm not gonna mention *what* I wrote, because scribbling on walls your name, over and over generally isn't conisdered genius. But hey! Whenever people go on about Mozart composing at five, they never say *what* he composed! I like to think of it as signs of early genius. *Looks important* That then faded.

My knowledge of Buffy/Angel trivia is highly overdeveloped, thanks to a British friend, ( thanks *SO* much ,Jo,) and yes, I *do* watch both shows. Yes, I am pathetic.I love all seven seasons of one, and all four of the other.

Also overdeveloped is my knowledge of Disney songs. No, I'm not addicted to Disney, probably haven't watched it unless it's Hercules since 1998, but one of my best friends is, and he's always singing it, so presently I have 'I'll make a man outta you', from Mulan stuck in my head. Anyone weirder than me, hands up.

If you've got thus far, you've discovered another delightful part of me. I'm part of a fanfic team, Souless_Wonders,with my bestest friend in the world (Oz) Insane1 aka Anita, and at present we're writing a FANTASTIC fic, (mainly her) called Wheel of Fortuna in the HP section. So check it out.

Anyways, me stuff. Um, i'm also on here under firefly, ((good luck finding it, I always seem to choose cliched names)) and some of my best stuff is there, including a story that I'm gonna upload here, so good luck with that to me. Anyway, I'm gonna go write. Back soon!
