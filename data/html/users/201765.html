Hi, I'm a big fan of anime and a few TV shows. My main obsession and favorite anime is Gundam Wing, and then, not in favorite order, Card Captors Sakura, the Gundam Series, etc. I write mostly Gundam Wing and I'll probably have a few others. And he has friends, such as an ex-fighter for hire, (*Dreamy sigh*) an assistant master for the Kamiya Kashin style dojo, a samurai in training, etc.
7)Trigun- Wow, where to start? This anime is very complex but awesome. Set to a futuristic Western World, a lot of guns are seen, amogn other weapons. I have not finished watching the series, but it is good so far. (My friend Sonyaabsolute has. *Sigh* Heh.) The main character fights for love, peace, etc. But he also has a sad and dark past which I have yet to discover fully.(Oh and this is a very funny anime as well, especially in the beginning.)
8)Sorcerer Hunters-(Watch out little kids. I have not seen this all out, only like 6 episodes) But I like it.(Also very funny) This is, shortly put, an anime about 5 sorcerer hunters (Evil sorcerers that is), two of who get, *ahem* costume changes.(Enough said heh.)
9)Silent Mobius-(First of all, VERY SAD anime. Don't get me wrong, it's a great anime, but it's very sad. I cried a couple of times through it.(I know, I know.) Very good though.) I think it's set in 2023, somewhere around there, where Lucifer Hawks attack Tokyo. There is a police squad who fights them, which are called the AMP(Alien Mystification Police, I think. I'm probably wrong, but still. It's somewhere along those lines.) This is alos quite a complex anime, because inside the police squad, there are many secrets to be unfolded.
10) Weiss Kruez AKA Knight Hunters-(First note: For all you ladies out there, if you like hot anime guys, this is a highly recommended one.) This is about 4 young (Ages, around 17, 19, 20, 21 or 22) assassins who work to serve(Uh oh, I forgot. This is also a sad anime, so watch out. Not as bad as Silent Mobius though...I think.)
