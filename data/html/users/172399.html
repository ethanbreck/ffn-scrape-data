Author has written 14 stories for Yu Yu Hakusho, Digimon, Hikaru no Go, Kyo kara Maoh!/今日からマ王！, Naruto, Gundam Wing/AC, and Kamen Rider.

Woot! Out of college, finally! For the record, UCLA is evil. But hah, the working world leaves me with even less time. Lameness. Stories are also sporadic from me 'cause I have a bad habit of over-analyzing my stuff and then never getting to the end. And a bad habit of starting things and making them epic .

Randomly: I major in Japanese (and Linguistics, most useless major evar!) so I have a tendency to throw in some bits of the language and whatnot in the stories, though I generally go back and remove it unless it's simple enough to be understood, I think. Hmm.. yeah, don't have much to say. Thankies muchly for taking the time to read my story and even more luffles on those who stop to review! You people make an author's day.

er.. yes... Oh look! Shinies!

Note on For the love of the Hat! - It is finished and will not be continued. I'm very sorry for those who want more, but it was a gift for doragon and she's happy how it is. If I were to continue it for the sake of a lemon the thing would become epic, and probably would stop making sense at some point. No quick rolls in the hay, I'm afraid. As doragon says "We like logic, kaythanks..."

04/28/09: On another note.. I'm sorry, I've been trying to work on Lunatic Carnivale and Apocryphal Reality, but life is kicking my ass this year... I really can't wait for this year to be over. I promise I haven't given up and will try to update as soon as I can. Thank you for your patience.

11/22/09: ...I really do intend to finish my stories. Lunatic Carnivale is being especially difficult. I've actually had to rewrite the next chapter three times already. While I still adore the GaaNaru pairing, my ever-growing dislike of the series with almost every new chapter is making it much harder to find motivation to write about it.

06/03/10: So I got back in the groove for a bit with Lunatic Carnivale, but despite my ever-growing dislike of the Naruto series, I must admit that I'm happy that Kishimoto is actually focusing on the demons aspect again. For that reason, because even though my story is by necessity an AU I like to keep it as close to canon as possible, I'm going to hold off and see where he takes this so I can adjust my story accordingly, if necessary, to be closer to the canon. At least as far as the Jinchuuriki and Demons are concerned. Because honestly, retarded stuff like Batsuke and Madara's face-sucking powers and plots involving eyes on the moon... I really would rather not touch..

On a side note, I have become amazingly obsessed with Kamen Rider recently. Enough that I wouldn't be surprised if I start tossing out a few KR ficlets here and there while I continue to slowly churn out LC.
