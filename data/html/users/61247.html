As of September 01, 2014

I'd like to thank everyone who has supported my stories since I started here in 2001. Although I have retired from writing fiction stories for Bulma and Vegeta full time, my love for them has never waned, even in the years since I stopped writing about them.

I have appreciated the constructive criticism, all of it. It has made me the writer I am today, and I am humbled by the experience.

Currently I am a tired late 20-something with the world on my shoulders, but my dream stays in my heart. Additionally to becoming a famous New York Times Best Selling Fiction Author, I'd also love to become a voice actress, commercial and dubbing, and to be an editorial/fashion model.

I hope to have the first part of my fiction novel published sometime in 2015/2016. It is not associated with Dragonball Z. Once I have finalized the draft I will provide more information on it here.

As I am older now and have more responsibility, I am forewarning that I do not check this page often, so if you message me, I will not see it in a reasonable fashion and apologize for that.

Keep reading my stories, if it's a source of inspiration, please don't feel deterred by negative thoughts. The best of you comes from your beginnings, whether small, or riddled with spelling errors :P

If I had to recommend a fiction romance series that I believe all would love : The Dark Hunter Series, GO! :P

PS: 75 percent of all the lemons I've written were during my virgin years, so they will suck, but thank you for the praise thus far.

Have fun, Love Peace and Chicken Grease! (b'o')b
