Generally Useless Trivia

1. If you give a greengirlblue a cookie, she will probably ask for a glass of milk.

2. greengirlblue is both singular and plural, for example: There is one greengirlblue; there are many greengirlblue. The collective noun for greengirlblue is a dance party. Though it is a proper noun, greengirlblue is never capitalized, as seen here. greengirlblue is abbreviated to ggb, as seen here.

3. How many greengirlblue can dance on the head of a pin?
a. One.
b. Two.
c. The real question is this: Do greengirlblue dance?

4. greengirlblue are generally friendly and do not steal bubblegum, pencils or kisses, unless the kisses are chocolate, in which case, a greengirlblue will probably announce that she is stealing it, and do so, or not.

5. Do not kick greengirlblue. Do not call greengirlblue mean names. Do not give greengirlblue your credit card number.

6. Do take greengirlblue to fun places. Do give greengirlblue compliments and constructive criticism. Do give greengirlblue cookies (and milk if asked).
