Author has written 6 stories for Sherlock Holmes, and Harry Potter.

Hm... what to write? I haven't much to say, as far as things go.

My first obsession in life is Sherlock Holmes. I started my Sherlock Holmes series when I was but eleven... and now I'm twenty. There are four stories in the series, and it's now complete. I am very fond of my characters, but seeing that I was very young when I wrote those stories, they are deeply flawed. Perhaps someday I will take up my pen again and write new Sherlock Holmes adventures, but for now, I am content with lurking in the Sherlock Holmes section and reading what other, better writers are writing about my hero.

My other obsession is Harry Potter. Not Harry Potter himself, since I hate the brat, but the older generation. My two favorite characters are Remus Lupin and Severus Snape. I do not write Harry Potter (my HP story, The Moon Wolf, is still up but discontinued), but rather track down some of the best RL and SS stories I can find. I will read a great variety of HP fanfiction, however, and am always on the look out for intelligent, well written stories.

I don't write fanfiction so much anymore. As a college student, I don't have a great deal of time. But I leave my stories up because I know people still read them, occasionally, and they were very important to me when I was eleven to thirteen. Thank you kindly to all my reviewers- I always appreciate the comments.
