Hello, my name is FalconWind and I live in British Columbia, Canada.

I'm a patriotic college student.
I'm a 'light' Trekker (I don't own a costume or anything like that). I'm a Star Wars fan, though I don't care for TFM and AOTC. I also like anime, like REAL anime, not Pokemon and stuff like that, though Digimon is OK.

I like to think that I'm a good writer, but I'm notorious for not finishing stories I've started. Other than that negative, I get nothing but praise for my stories that I write in my english classes.

Some people say I'm egotistical, but I know that I'm above that sort of thing. ;D

I try to use the 3-Q Rule: Quickness, Quantity, Quality. Pick 2 of the 3.
Quickness & Quantity = Quick mediocre novel
Quickness & Quality - Good Short Story
Quantity & Quality = Good novel
