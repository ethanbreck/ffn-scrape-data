"If you break enough glass / And there's no one to hear / And your heart's full of hate /'Cause your mind's full of fear / Let it go..."

"So Far Away" ~ the Goo Goo Dolls

12/26/06 Well, I figured that it's about time that I updated this thing. First of all, I just want to apologize to those of you who have been following "Midnight Song" and waiting for an update. I know that my last update was in early August, so that makes it almost five months without anything new. The truth is, so much has happened in my life that the world of FF8 just doesn't seem all that important to me anymore. I know that's a horrible thing to say, and believe me, I never thought you'd hear those words coming out of my mouth. That's not to say that my love for the game is gone, because that couldn't be further from the truth. Even now, as I am playing Final Fantasy XII I just can't seem to get into it because it is so different from VIII. The thing that's changed is my inspiration. To put it simply, I just can't seem to get my head back into the FF8 world in order to sufficiently write about it and the characters that are so near and dear to my heart. I'm not going to say that I will never go back and revisit fanficition, and I would really love to finish "Midnight Song" some day because I used to be very passionate about it. The other problem I have is that I just don't have the time to write fanfiction anymore. I'm in college now, and between juggling classes, homework, and a social life, that really doesn't leave much time for writing. If it's meant to happen, though, it will one day. For the time being, I'm sorry to say that I can't offer you anything new. The minute I do write something new, trust that I'll have it posted here right away. You all have been so good to me over the years, and you have allowed me to grow as a writer in so many different ways, so thank you for that. Finally, I just want to say that I used to hate it when a fic I was reading would just cease without any explanation, so I once again apologize for doing the same exact thing. Although I won't be writing any more fanfiction for the time being, please feel free to e-mail me or friend me on Livejournal. I would definitely love to hear from you about anything at all, and no matter how busy I get, I always find time to reply to e-mail or comments that people leave me _ So once again, thank you all SOOOOOOOO much for the support over the years and constant interest in fics. You guys are amazing!

~Thanks to everyone who has taken the time to read and review my fics. It really means a lot to me.~
