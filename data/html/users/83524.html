Works Completed:

I'll Be Coming Around--Schraeger is carried off to Switzerland to keep her safe from a serial killer she put away. She is not the most cooperative of material witnesses. She wants to put him away herself.

The Way to a Fae's Stomach is Through His Heart--What happens when Bo gets so involved with Dr. Hot Pants she and her other fae friends start ignoring the Great KenziI. Which considering her trouble magnet status, that's a bad thing. So she makes a new friend who is even a worse trouble magnet. Yikes! They both have made an enemy of the Morrigan. What happens when her friend is more than he appears and things turn deadly.

You Had to be There--This is a sequel to I'll Be Coming Around. Walsh and Schraeger are getting engaged. YES! Whatcould possibly go wrong? Hint: Federal Marshalls, clowns and the FBI are involved. Need I say more?

The Wolf, The Sword, and The Goth--The second in my fae series. Kenzi is becoming a fae and according to the prophecy is destined to become Shifter Queen. What prophecy oh there always has to be a prophecy! Recover relics, reunite friends and lover, overcome obstacles, fight battles, and oh yeah try to avoid being killed by both sides of the fae who are trying to kill her. Easy right?

Dimensional Travel Guide for Avengers--Purgatory--While the Avengers are fighting dragons and a wizard. Hawkeye and Natasha get pushed into another universe. How they get out is the adventure.

Delinquent Hearts--What would have happened if we had written the Delinquents episode.Background scenes and my own little twists and turns. Jommy26 requested this and gave me the storyline, credit to her. Bo doesn't play with Dyson anymore so why shouldn't Kenzi be able to play with him. She is conflicted with wanting her besties ex though.

The Incredible Hulk Meets a Green Woman--Third in the Lost Girl series, that includes The Way to A Fae's Stomach is through his Heart, The Wolf, the Sword, and The Goth. What happens when the Avengers answer an assemble request from SHIELD and find a whole new set of enemies and allies. What happens when Dr. Bruce Banner meets the woman of his dreams and the Hulk meets his match as well?

It Doesn't Matter--A one shot for the Unusuals. When a former partner is killed in the line of duty and then vilified for his life choices. Jason gets angry. An anonymous author inspires him to make a stand. Implied homosexual relationship of an OC.

To Be Or Nogitsune--The Nogitsune has returned and Stiles is scared until he gets a message from the Dark Fox telling him that foxes aren't usually Pack animals but they can be very protective of their families. He puts it down to a nightmare until he wakes up running through the woods to save his dad from a rogue Alpha.

Works in Progress:

The Spark series: A Teen Wolf series: Stiles centric with eventual Sterek. Stiles goes to the Nemeton to have a night of drinking. He meets an older woman there who tells him the Nemeton likes him. Shades of White--An Avengers story with fierymenace. (On hiatus)

Also working on an independent piece just not sure where or if I may post it.
