Once upon a time, I wrote fanfiction, and I was good at it, I think. The occasional reviews I continue to receive seem to verify that, but anyway. When I was thirteen and fourteen years old, writing fanfiction was one of my favorite things to do. I'd come home from school and just write away, non-stop. Just as the slogan of this site says to do, I relished in unleashing my imagination. But eventually, I got busy, I moved on to other things, and I stopped spending my afternoons on ff.net. I miss those days. It's been a long time since I last wrote a story -- seven or eight years, I think. My life is a lot different now, far different from how I dreamed or expected it to be. But in recent months, I've come back to this website and have found incredible inspiration from some incredible writers. It reminded me that you don't have to be a published, paid author to write one hell of a story, and I want to be that kind of writer again. This summer is full of possibilities and I hope that in time I may be able to produce something worthwhile of reading. But til then, thank you to those who still read my stories, thank you to those who will read my stories, and thanks most of all to those writers whose imaginations have reawakened my own. :)
