Across the Universe

I've been writing Across the Universe for a few years, and I've finally decided that 8/1/14 is an appropriate date is start sharing it. It has a much more layered and ambitious plot than the Perhaps stories, so I'm both nervous and excited to see how it will turn out.

It won't become clear for a while, but Defining Our Universe actually comes from the same timeline.

Perhaps Universe

Mysterious forces drew me back into the Digimon fandom. As a Takari shipper, I was always really disappointed that the epilogue didn't show them together, which in my pessimistic mind meant that they didn't make it as a couple. Eventually, I created my Perhaps Universe, where I would use my powers as an author to reconcile Takari and the epilogue canon.

I wrote three versions - pessimistic, optimistic, and objective. Please take a read and let me know what you think, whether it is in the form of reviews or PMs.

Once Upon a Fairy Tale was the easiest to write of the three as well as my favorite. It's by no means perfect, but I am quite pleased with the end result.

Solastice, one of my favorite readers, was kind enough to commission fanart for this fic. Please enjoy the artist's talent here!

Perhaps Love was actually written before Once Upon a Fairy Tale, but I lost the original files due to a computer virus. It's finally been rewritten and completely posted, and will probably see a minor revision once I have more time.

[Version 3] probably will never be posted. It was actually the story that created the whole concept behind the Perhaps Universe, so it has by far the most complicated plot. I've been working on it, on and off, for a very long time, and I'm doubtful that I will finish it.

Tale of the Other Woman does take place within its timeline and incorporates a sliver of the plot. Gray, which is WIP, expands on the plot a bit more. I'm not sure at the moment whether I'll have additional stories.

Other fandoms

The Good -

Rainbow is inspired by a song that reminded me of Ecruteak City. It's pretty different from most of the Pokemon fics out there so I don't expect a big audience. Nevertheless I hope you like it!

In the Time Capsule is the product of playing my old games, and possibly spending too much time on Bulbapedia.

Everything He Does is a parody of the Ash/Misty/May triangle. It was so much fun to write!

...and the Bad -

[Removed] When the Night Conquers is old. It will not be updated. It was my first attempt at writing a good OT Pokemon fic. Needless to say, it did not work out.

Silent Wishes and Night of the Werewolf are two of my earliest stories, written for the Harry Potter fandom. I was thirteen when I wrote them...and you can definitely tell! The writing is nothing spectacular, but I kept them archived for sentimental reasons.

Please PM me if you'd like to contact me for any reason. Enjoy!
