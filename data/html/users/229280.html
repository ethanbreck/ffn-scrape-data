Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

Ok, what shall I tell you? I'm an soon to be former university student who loves to read good fanfictions. I write fictions sometimes, too, but they are so bad that I want nobody to read them ;)

Well, as you can see, I actually posted some of my stories but writing really is an on and off thing for me. I write to dream myself away but it's mostly a spur of the moment thing so chapter stories and larger fics really aren't my thing. I also tend to be a bit hard to please with what I've written which is kinda infuriating 'cause it keeps me from writing longer stories...

I'm not quite sure of I will ever finish Night of Change or any of my unpublished chapter stories and I'm sorry for that. Real life just didn't let me get in the right mood and I doubt that will change soon.

I'm also easily obsessed with something - at the moment it's still Blaise Zabini (male) from Harry Potter.

My favorits are BtVS, LotR, HP, Highlander, but I read others, too.

Favourite pairings: Buffy/Spike, Hermione/Malfoy, Methos/Cassandra, Max/White and Sesshoumaru/Kagome... yeah, I know they are all not very likely (exept for Buffy/Spike) but what can I tell you, this is fanfiction and I'm a person with a very twisted mind :p ... but I'm not the only one there, am I?

So, that was all that comes to my mind right now ;)
