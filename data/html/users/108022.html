Hello and good day. I am in the process of uploading a few new stories. Keep a look out--edits and continuation of old stories to come.

~Fandomes I enjoy~
(you know, if you care. XD)

Naruto - I quite like this fandome. It has so many interesting stories, and doesn't have to follow a set amount of rules. Anything can happen! I like to read about Iruka, Naruto, Hinata, Kiba and Lee. My favorite couples are Naruto/Hinata, Iruka/Kakashi. Currently I'm absolutely loving any stories about fatherly/brotherly Iruka comforting/helping Naruto out.

Kingdom Hearts - I've been a huge fan since it has come out! And I started reading fanfics about this game as soon as I finished the first game. I love to read about Sora, Riku and Roxas. Don't have a favourite couple right now, so as long as it is not an OC it's awesome. I currently love reading anything about Sora fighting Heartless - and if it is crossed with different anime's/shows then it is even awesomer. (Crossed any of my interests I LOVE :D)

Tales of Symphonia - I love this game, and I'm rather excited that there is another ToS coming out! More Lloyd and Zelos! Whooooo! I like reading about Zelos, Lloyd and Genis. My favorite couple is Zelos with anyone, but I'm rather partial to Zelos/Raine, Zelos/Lloyd and Zelos/Genis. Love anything with angsty-ness in it.

Harry Potter - I've been a fan since my father bought me the first four books for Christmas one year. Ah! The memories... I like to read about Harry, Draco, Remus, Voldermort, Neville, Ron, Percy and the Twins. My favorite couples are Neville/Ginny, Remus/Sirius and Percy/Voldie.

Tsubasa - Oh gosh... I love the manga and the anime! I'm almost obsessed with it right now... okay, I am obsessed right now. But that is only because it is awesome! I enjoy reading about everyone, but I like reading about Fai and Kurogane the most. And yes, my favorite couple is Fai/Kurogane.

Pokemon - I know... I love pokemon... and I proudly admit it! I've loved it since Red and Blue came out, and I don't think that is going to change any time soon. I like to read about Ash, Gary, the Cerulean Sisters and the odd Original character. I don't have any favorite couples. (I would love to read a Pokemone/Kingdom Hearts cross over, so if anyone knows of any... please message me!)

Digimon - I still read the odd Digimon fiction piece. I usually stick to the original kids, mostly because I know the most about them. I do read the odd story about the 'next generation'. I like to read about Matt, T.K., Kouichi and Kouji the most. No favorite couples.

Cardcaptors - I've only read the odd one every once and a while, but I still do enjoy them. I like reading about Yukito, Yue and To-ya. My favorite couple is Yukito/To-ya.

Ouran High School Host Club - If you don't know about this anime/manga, shame on you! It's the awesome-est, most excellent thing EVER. Look! It's so good it's making my sentences look horrible! I love reading about all of the characters, though I'm leaning towards Mori the most. My favorite couple is Mori/Haruhi and Mori/Hunny.

~Fiction I'm Currently Reading~

Lost In Translation by Ontogenesis

This is a great Tsubasa fiction, especially if you're a fan of the Kuro/Fai coupling. It's set during the Shurano arc, and focuses a lot around the language barrier between Fai and Kurogane. Not complete. Ontogenesis has quite a few fics that are quite good, and I particularly find 'What came through a wormhole: A Crossover' quite interesting. It's a Tsubasa/DS9 crossover. Check it out!

An awesome Naruto fiction which sends Iruka on a mission with Kakashi, Ibiki and Asuma. Bouncy cat has stated that it's going to be Iruka/Kakashi... but even if you're not into that, it still is a good read. Not complete.

Another Naruto fiction! This piece sends Naruto down a different path then he was originally intended for. A great piece if you're looking for a Hinata/Naruto relationship! The story doesn't center around the relationship, but about the normal ninja-type-things. Quite a nice story. Not complete. This author has quite a few Tales of Symphonia shorts is anyone is interested.

Burning Deadfall by Samurai101

A Naruto fiction focused on Iruka! Naruto's body has been taken over by the Kyuubi, and Iruka will give up everything in order to save the young Genin. The problem is... the Kyuubi has been terrorizing Konoha. How far will Iruka go to save his student? Great story, and has an update schedule! Not Complete.
