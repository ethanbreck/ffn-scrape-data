When I first created this fanfiction.net account, I'd only recently discovered the world of Fan-Fiction online. That was seven years ago; now, I'm approaching my twenty-fifth birthday and I have a range of Fan-Fiction that spans several fandoms spread all over the internet (though, mostly these days, you can find the majority of my more adult-themed Fic at aff.net). I'm also in the process of revamping my own site, so that I can have a safe place to store all of my stories, be they original pieces of fiction, or that which is inspired by my favorite books, t.v shows and films.

I do not buy into the whole 'Twilight' thing. I think Stephanie Meyer's characters are one dimensional and that her general attitudes towards women, terrifying. Girls are supposed to be empowered, not simpering twits.
