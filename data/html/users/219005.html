Welcome to my bio, v 4.2! I'm a 30-something, female technical writer, who wastes way too much time writing fanfiction. (Yeah, it doesn't make much sense to me, either. o.O) I can also be found on LiveJournal as Kantayra:

My little affair with fanfiction started way back in grade school, when I would create cute stories - 'plotting' I called it - about the cartoons I watched. These evolved into long, contorted sagas, most of which never got written down. I've watched roughly a bajillion sci-fi/fantasy/hardcore nerd shows over the years, so they all provide a contorted background from which I pull my huge, verbose sagas.

Fandoms I have written in to date include: Alias, Angel, Buffy, Doctor Who, the Dresden Files, Heroes, House, Lost, Naruto, Night at the Museum, Pirates of the Caribbean, Real Person Slash, Ronin Warriors, Smallville, Sherlock Holmes, Supernatural, Tanya Huff, Transformers, and Veronica Mars. For all of those not listed in this profile (which is, incidentally, pretty much all of them)? Check out the links above.

I'm currently here for the Naruto fandom. I just got hopelessly addicted to the show, and have gobbled up every episode and chapter that I could. (Yes, I even suffered through all the filler. In one month. Now, that's dedication!) My top ten characters are: Shikamaru, Sasuke, Deidara, Sasori, Kakashi, Tenten, Naruto, Neji, Temari, and Tsunade. My top ships are Naruto/Sasuke and Sasori/Deidara. But, in the end, I'm really just in it for the laughs.

Anyway, I hope you enjoy my work. I love all feedback/reviews. Valete!
