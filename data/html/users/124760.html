Author has written 29 stories for StarTrek: Enterprise, StarTrek: The Original Series, StarTrek: Voyager, and StarTrek: The Next Generation.

I left Trek fandom in 2003 and wanting a fresh start, I chose the handle "Harmony Bites." Those stories, almost all Harry Potter fics to date, can be found here. There is one Trek story there, "Akin"--I can't cross-post it on Rabble Rouser because it's against the TOS--a story can be posted only once on FanFictionNet on the entire site. That story, inspired by the Star Trek Reboot of 2009 and published August of that year is actually my most recent Trek story. The stories posted in in December of 2013 and January of 2014 on this site are actually stories several years old but only archived here now. It's unlikely I'll write or post more Trek stories--although I would never say never--I still love these characters.
