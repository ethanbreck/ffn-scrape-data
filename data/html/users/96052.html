Hi wonderful reader!

If you are taking the time to look at my profile than I am grateful! I was talking to one of my friends the other day and she said, you never write anymore. It made me sad because she is right! I have been all consumed by writing case studies and term papers that I just never have time to write anymore!

So starting soon I'll be writing more. Probably not long chapter stories, although those are my favorite to write. Just little one-shots.

So please forgive me as I try to dabble in the world of fan fiction again.

Now, for authors and readers here are my main areas of interest: Angel (Fred based, because in my world she is still alive), So Weird, Twilight, Law & Order: Criminal Intent (Alex and Bobby only), Gossip Girl, The 10th Kingdom, and Buffy.

Do I read other stuff? Yes. Will I comment on it. Yes

If you are a reader who is a writer and would like me to read your stuff then please comment. I am, as a rule, nosy; so more likely than not I will journey on over to your profile and see what you have written.

If you are an author, please take a minute to read an comment if you see anything that you like in my story list.

If you really, really, really want me to finish one of my long chapter stories, well please send me a message and I'll do my best to comply with your request. Or I might just be kind and give you some spoilers if I do not intend to finish the story.

FYI to all you authors out there. Be careful! I just found out that Liz Braswell, an "author", basically copied my idea from Evelyn Rose Giles and turned it into a book. I am not looking for money or anything like that but I put a lot of work an time into researching all the information I did for Yellow and for another "author" to just take all of that research and turn into a story without even saying, "Oh I loved your idea", well it sucks! I don't think there is anything you can really do when something like this happens but it does suck and it does hurt so I just wanted to caution you all that it could happen.
