Jamie:

I don't own a dress.
My hair is always a mess.
If you catch me stealing, I won't confess.
I'm beautiful.
I never compromise.
Love babies and surprises.
Wear hi-heels when I exercise.

Daddy wrestles alligators.
Mama works on carburetors.
My brother is a fine mediator for the President.

I only drink coffee at Midnight, when the moment is not right.
My timing is quite - unusual.
You see, my confidence is tragic, but my intuition magic.
And the shape of my body - unusual.

Meet...ME.

Yuffie x Vincent

Hermione x Draco

Doesn't change a thing.

There may be a few drabbles of some other things, or a smattering of some random characters thrown together, but those ARE the exception.

Hope you enjoy!
