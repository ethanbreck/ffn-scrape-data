Hi, I'm Kemi. I've been lurking here and there, writing stories for this and that. I enjoy video games on my PS4, anime, badminton, movies, books etc. Some of my favourite books that I proudly display on my shelf are the Dune Chronicles by Frank Herbert, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six and Without Remorse, The Girl With the Dragon Tattoo trilogy by Stieg Larsson, The Earth's Children Series by Jean M. Auel, The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy trilogy in five parts and The Long Dark Tea-time of the Soul by Douglas Adams, The Zombie Survival Guide by Max Brooks, The Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling, Lord of the Rings by JRR Tolkien, The Twilight Saga by Stephenie Meyer, The Vampire Hunter D novels by Hideyuki Kikuchi, several shelves of manga, comics, and anime art books. A modest collection of DVDs start with Doctor Who (10th Doctor, 2nd - 4th Series) Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Angel, Dexter, Moonlight, anime, Elfquest volumes, Learning Japanese language books, Final Fantasy toys, Lego collectibles, and various figures.

As for my love of video games, I play a fairly broad range of games. Among them are Red Dead Redemption, The Legend of Zelda, Horizon, Street Fighter, Grand Theft Auto, Gran Turismo 6, RPGs, Battlefield COD, The Last of Us, Final Fantasy…etc. One of my regrets: having failed to preserve my Atari 2600 and Nintendo 8bit system from total destruction. xD

When it comes to fan-doms, I totally love the Tenth Regeneration of Doctor Who (and I keep a room in the TARDIS next to Jack Harkness xD); devouring stories regarding FrostIron (Loki x Tony Stark) and BlackFrost (Loki x Black Widow); mad about the pairing of Ban x Akabane from the anime, the GetBackers; forever loyal to the Japanese version of Dragonball Z; wish I could have Crying Freeman's or Vash the Stampede's mad skills; will forever trek the starry quadrants of space with Data aboard the Enterprise; fond of the characters of the Twilight novels (Dr. Carlisle Cullen and Jasper are my movie crushes); can never ever get enough of Vampire Hunter D's lovely face and incredibly long, black hair; add Adrien Tepes from Netflix’s Castlevania; and looking forward to every new Star Trek movie released in the theatres.

Damaged characters are the most interesting to write about and I tend to gravitate towards them like a magnet. xD Love action and adventure? Me too! First and foremost, I prefer to delve into complex character studies and interactions, and although romance isn't an integral part of my writing, it makes it no less fundamental. I encourage readers of all ages, creeds and genders to try them out!

Sadly, my story updates are largely dependent on inspiration and the time to write, so I wish to extend a thousand heartfelt apologies for my erratically sluggish behavior. However, I will endeavour to try to finish my stories and will never (hopefully) abandon them. I extend my sincerest gratitude to everyone's continued support of my humble fan-fiction !

We all enjoy a good story, right? So sit back, kick up your feet and delve into the worlds of my sometimes twisted (mega-happy fun-time) imagination!
