My name is Lisa, but I'm better known as Jamus. The stories here on ff.net will probably not be updated again because I am a lazy, lazy person and I... well, I ran out of fic ideas, plot bunnies... Whatever you call them, I ran out of them.

There is a story I'm working on now, but it is very, very long and is no where near completion. Or, actually, I intend it to be a long fic, but I'm only writing out the plot for it now. I seem to always run into a plot-barrier so the jotting ideas down in a notebook is working for me. I plan to write the chapters before I post them here, so it's likely you'll see the story sometime late 2006, early 2007. Most likely the fic will be finished before I graduate highschool in 2008. Ideas come to me slowly, but it is getting there. I have lost my muse, you can say.

Sometimes I come on fanfiction.net, but not really anymore because I find that most of the high quality fic I'm looking for is elsewhere. As in portkey.org, the haven for all Harry/Hermione shippers. I admit I do see a good amount of good fics, but portkey is the best for me.

If for any reason you wish to contact me (why would you, I'm so boring :P), you can do so by my livejournal

Hugs and kisses,

~Just Jamus
