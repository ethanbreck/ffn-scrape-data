Well i am a 19 year old female, who wishes she was still 12. i go to college full time, i work full time, and i drink (which accounts the long breaks between updates). i go to a lot of concerts and i love rock music. i am a native Texan and i currently live in east Texas, and no i am not a cowgirl, but i do love nature, space, and peace and quiet (god i sound like my grandma').

i have always been trapped within my own imagination. when i was 4 i started to sit outside with my grandpa' in the afternoons and tell him all about my adventures in another world where i was a queen and had all sorts of magnificent pets like tigers and lions who could turn into golden statues, and well strange adventures like that never seemed to stop happening to me.

i mostly write Digimon fanfiction. why Digimon? Well, because i love the show, i am familiar with the plot and the characters, and because the creator of Digimon left a lot of plot holes that are fun to fill. i like Digimon Adventure the bestest! But 02 wasn't too bad, although i like to pretend the ending never happened. Tamers was cool, but i was not impressed with Frontier. i didn't like the fact that the kids turned into Digimon, it eliminated the partner concept, which is essential in all the other Digimon realities.

This is how i rank Digimon.
