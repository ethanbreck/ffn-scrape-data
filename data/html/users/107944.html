Bio:

Let's see, who is CT? Well, I'm a twenty-one year old guy who isn't afraid to admit he likes Digimon(preferably non-dubbed). I am a huge Mugen Dramon, Millenniumon, Fladramon and Renamon fan. I've watched all of Digimon Tamers and I personally think it is better than 02, but I enjoy 02 as well. I've watched some of Frontier, but it just doesn't appeal to me as much as the previous seasons. I have started to get into Pokémon fics through Digimon but have yet to see a single episode of the anime. My forté is adult oriented Romance and Drama but I do humor as well.
