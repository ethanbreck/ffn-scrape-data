Hi everyone! I figured I ought to update my bio a little.
I'm still just a girl with a healthy amount of imagination and a head full of stories. I never dared to write anything down until a year ago or so.
I love anime and basically anything that has to do with fantasy and science fiction. I guess these stories are due to my passion for reading. I'd write more if I had more time, but since I'm attending school right now, you all probably know how busy that can get. I try to update my stories as fast as I can so please be patient with me.
Currently writing chapters for Wait for Me and working up ideas for two new stories. And if I find time, I might do some revision on Wait for Me. I took out Unexpected Destiny, I will not be working on it, 'cause no matter how hard I try the plot just won't work with me.

Here's a rundown of my works:
Completed:
1. Lessons in the Life of a Demigoddess (Fushigi Yugi)
2. Immortal Fate (Highlander)
3. Return to Me (Gundam Wing)
4. Angel of Music (Shamanic Princess)

In progress:
1. Wait for Me (Gundam Wing)

In the works:
1. The Coming of Triad (Fushigi Yugi) An attempt at a Seiryu reincarnation fic
2. Stay with Me (Gundam Wing) Last installment of my "Me" trilogy
