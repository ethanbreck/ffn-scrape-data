Author has written 9 stories for Fairy Tales, Harry Potter, Forgotten Realms, V for Vendetta, Titanic, 28 Days Later, Ironman, Doctor Who, Frozen, and Red Eye.

Stories are a beautiful beautiful thing.

Married with an amazing baby girl. American. Love, love, love Joss Whedon. He could create anything and I would become a follower.

Other interests:

Marvel Movies- They have really raised the bar on these and I am SO happy for it!

Red Eye- The chemistry between Cillian Murphy and Rachel McAdams was perfect.

V for Vendetta! Amazing, passionate film that hits my every fancy. Also, Watchmen (by the same creator Alan Moore, although he will have nothing to do with the movies) was amazing!

Sweeney Todd- I love the music from the Broadway, but in love with the film even more! Following on that line, if you haven't seen Repo! The Genetic Opera! watch it...such a well done story, fun, and amazing music!

Pirate movies of any sort! Captain Blood, The Black Swan, The Buccaneer, Muppet Treasure Island, Pirates of the Caribbean...

Wicked, Les Miserables (broadway), Disney movies (Frozen!)

The Scarlet Pimpernel, any Jane Austin books/flicks, Elizabeth Gaskell's North and South, any Juliet Marillier novels, Outlander, Game of Thrones (book and show)

Music: Can't say enough enough about good old classic rock. The music score for Iron Man was incredible, other than that, whatever suits my mood for the moment.

PC games are nice...I'm such a sucker for them.

Don't ask yourself what the world needs; ask yourself what makes you come alive. And then go and do that. Because what the world needs is people who have come alive.
