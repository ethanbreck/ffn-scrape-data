Kunoichi of Literary Intent

Name: Theresa

Age: 26

Location: Ohio, United States

I live in with my fiancée Jared who I love with all of my heart. Hobby wise I enjoy Writing, Reading, Video Games, Art, Movies, Music chatting with friends I wrote my first story when I was fourteen and I have been writing pretty much ever since.

As far as stories go I believe people should be able to write whatever they please. If someone doesn’t like the pairing in a story or the general plot they don't have to read it.I understand constructive criticism but I see no need for pointlessly flaming people just because their pairing isn't the one you prefer. In short there is a small button at the top of everyone’s screen its marked “back” if you don’t like something feel free to use it.

Literary Intentions:

With the release of DOA 4 I have gotten the information I needed to further work on an idea I have had for sometime and refine it into a more workable fashion as well as get new ideas and revamp some others accordingly. It’s all for the better be assured of that. (Smile)
