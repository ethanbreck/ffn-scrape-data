I am a lover of fanfiction. I will forever dedicate my being here to my all time favorite pairing Jason/Trini (Power Rangers). A pairing I truly had wished I could have seen happen in the show.

I've been alone for awhile now! Come back!

Other pairings I love:

Harry/Hermione (Harry Potter) I don't care what the rest of the world says or especially what J.K. Rowling wrote. They belong together. HHR shippers, I know you're with me on this.

Alex/Tina (Ghostwriter) Who can't love the kiss in the back of the garbage truck. This is my childhood pairing and I don't think I'll ever grow out of it. Anyone want to join me in this small lonely community GW fans?

Naruto/Sakura (Naruto) Believe me I've gone back and forth of why Hinata should be the one because of her undying respect for him since day one. Yet I cannot get over the fact that Sakura has seen Naruto grow in the ninja he has become and how much they've gone through.

Neji/Tenten (Naruto) Tell me not, by the end of this manga/anime they should be end up together.

Ichigo/Rukia (Bleach) Lol. I don't know why but I can't imagine Ichigo with anyone else and Renji just isn't good enough.
