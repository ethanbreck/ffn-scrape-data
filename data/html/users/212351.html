I have been writing nonsense for 8 years now, on and off. I write for a ton of fandoms, which seem to get more and more embarrassing the older I get. But dammit, if I'm gonna be a loser, then I'm going to be a loser in style.

I like rarepairs and villains, basically.

Fun fact! As you go down the page, the stories get exponentially worse.

WHO THE FRIG I AM

I'm a giant nerd who has giant nerd pasttimes.

I am a female, feminist, foodie college student in Arizona. In January of 2011, I'm going to be a student at the Culinary Institute of America in St. Helena, California. I am attempting to become fluent in Spanish. I have a weird sense of humor. I like things that are inherently outrageous or over-the-top, such as pointing dramatically and The Decemberists. I fall in love too easily with a lot of things. I am a yaoi fangirl by nature, because subtext rules and context drools. I am an attention whore who knows better than to actually act like one.

My stories have two settings: absurd humor and maudlin drama. Occasionally, introspection! I used to be really good at things like descriptions but then I wrote 4 years worth of theatrical writing and now everything I write is dialogue-heavy and overloaded with doorknob symbolism - a term which refers to symbolism that does not enhance or contribute to the meaning of any given story. Example: calling any given movie character a Jesus parallel. Oh, and also! I have a really weird tendency to accidentally write lots of Marxist theory undertones into my stories. I didn't even realise I did this at one point, but it's true. To my knowledge, it is the weirdest writing problem ever.

I have been a contributing member to a bazillion fandoms since I was like 11. No joke. Now I am 18 and I have like, no grasp on reality at all. FANFICTIOOOOON (shakes fist)

I like slash, long plot-heavy fanfics, tiny well-written drabbles, badass villains that do not cry all the time, vampires, and thiefshipping. And Regina Spektor. Oh, Regina. You are the world's Mary Sue. Please come back to Arizona.

I read Twilight for the lulz. Google Cleolinda Jones and you'll understand.

Fun fact: I will spell Malik/Marik's name however I feel like at any given time. Also, I use dub names and original names interchangeably. Not like, within the same story or anything, because that would be SO confusing. But it isn't uncommon for me to call the blonde guy "Joey" in one fic and then "Jounouchi" in another. Why do I do this? Because I CAN.

IN THE FUTUUUUURE

LOL UPDATE SRS BZNS: I seem to be taking another extended break from writing! But fear not, I will always come back in the end. All the stuff I talked about writing still exists, I'm just dealing with the dreaded Real Life Stuff right now. Once I move early next year, I predict another outpouring of writing. Hey, who knows? I might even upload the prologue chapter to the multichaptered nonsense I was talking about below some time in the near future.

I'm working on a few one-shots - namely xenoshipping and bounceshipping, because for some reason I find writing those couples really enjoyable. I scrapped the prostitutes AU fic I mentioned last time, because it wasn't going anywhere and it was turning out to be more original flavor than You-Gay-Hoe related. So now I'm working on something completely different: a supernatural multi-chaptered spiritshipping after-canon fic called Soul Release. It's very different from anything that I've ever written before, in that it actually has a coherent plot. I haven't ironed out all the fine details yet, but the first chapter is almost done, so that'll likely be the next thing I upload.
