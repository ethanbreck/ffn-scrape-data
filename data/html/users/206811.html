Just an average Joe who likes to write, and has for a long time. He's not very good at it, yet, but he tries, and enjoys himself. I try my hardest to be a nice guy, but I CAN be enormously antisocial and because of this, a lot of people think I'm a prick. It's just not so! I'm just really shy and nervous-natured! Honest! I'm a huge fan of the Star Wars universe as well as the (former) MTV glory known as Daria. Sandi Griffin is the Anti-Christ. Long live the Quinn...hey! As of late, I'm also becoming quite addicted to the Harry Potter universe. A wise man once said, "Harry Potter books are like popcorn for the brain," but there's more to it than that! J.K. Rowling is the Roald Dahl of the new millenium, period!
