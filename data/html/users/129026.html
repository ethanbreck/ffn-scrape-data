Note to all you Authors out there:

While I would run away screaming from any Mary Sue, über-fluffy fics or meaningless sex/torture/loony orgies, I can stomach almost everything as long as it is well-written and in character.
Still got that 'fic that made you fear your own imagination because was so dark, odd or both? Yeah, that one. Post it, I am really curious about it.
You wrote a story in the POV of a character everyone despises with a passion? I'll review fair, even if I hate the character myself.
I can do nothing else but encourage you to write as you like.
Go on, kill every character I admire, write that sarcastic remark, mock all I hold dear! I may curse and mourn and flinch while reading it, but as long as the story fascinates me, you will receive only praise.
Good work deserves to be honored.
