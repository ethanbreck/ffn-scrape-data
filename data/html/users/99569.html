Author has written 3 stories for Escaflowne, Vampire Hunter D, and Weiss Kreuz.

I've been writing anime/game-based fanfiction for the past five or six years. Some of my favorite fandoms include: Saiyuki, Weiss Kreuz, Shaman King, G.I. Joe, Resident Evil, Witch Hunter Robin, Guilty Gear, Mortal Kombat, Trinity Blood, Voltron, Skeleton Warriors, Winx, King Of Fighters, Street Fighter, Soul Calibur, Devil May Cry, Bleach, ExoSquad, Mass Effect, Dynasty Warriors, and plenty more.

One of my fics, Moth to the Flame (a Vampire Hunter D: Bloodlust-based story) won three awards in the 2002 MediaMiner.org FanFiction contest for Best Romance, Best Male Romantic Character (Meier Link) and Best Dramatic Author.

Under the joint pen name of Raven_Ladies, I've partnered with another author, LadyRavenlocke, for a series of G.I. Joe/Resident Evil crossover fics (Unsung Heroes) featuring the pairing of Lifeline (Edwin Steen) and Billy Coen. The current finished fics are posted here on ff.net under the cartoon/G.I. Joe category. We've also branched out into the Trinity Blood universe with a fic titled Blood Sacrifice. Please check them out and let us know what you think as we're quite proud of what we've done. http://www.fanfiction.net/ravenladies

Please note: all stories are rated M for Mature for violence, gore, strong/harsh language, and slash (male/male sexual encounters).

UPDATE: Unsung Heroes: Mission Zero was awarded two first place honors in the 2006 MediaMiner.org Maximum Challenge FanFiction Contest! Best Action/Adventure fic and Fielder's Choice!

ANOTHER UPDATE: Unsung Heroes: Mission Zero was named as an Honorable Selection of the 2006 Dogs Of The Military FanFiction contest!
