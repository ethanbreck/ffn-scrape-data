My name is DevilWithTheAngelWingsOn. I'm 21. I live in Florida half the time, the rest of the time I'm in my own world. I am completely and totally in love with Pitch Black, Dark Fury, and The Chronicles Of Riddick. I adore Vin Diesel and think he's absolutely genius. I write fanfiction. I have poetry too but my real love lies with Pitch Black fanfiction, namely with the characters Jack and Riddick. (And that is Jack not Kyra, I think the plot for TCOR is fine but I enjoy Jack better. Nothing personal.) I heart reviews, good or bad. They make me happy. I started a myspace for my stories and I hope to add other people's stories as well. I'd really love to start a website where we can all showcase our talents, wouldn't that be amazing? Whose in it with me?

I am striken with ideas in the most random places and I am shameless with songfics. I like! I'd love to get to know every writer on here so you ever want to talk I'm up for it. Email me or catch me on myspace. Best of luck to everyone in their stories and may you never get writer's block!

"Leave Out All The Rest" by Linkin Park

"What I've Done" by Linkin Park

"Dancing" by Elisa

"Shackled" by Vertical Horizon
