Author has written 20 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Harry Potter, and Greek Mythology.

Erm, I really don't like these profile things. I never know what to write...

OK. So I've been writing crappy fan fiction since forever... well, actually probably since I was 14. I remember my first one, it starred Mary Sue me and Lance Bass from NSYNC, definitely cringe worthy considering I'm 19 now. Before that I did wrote my own little descriptive pieces of prose that shifted tenses too much. Past, present, future... I fused it all together.

I've had stages where I wrote fan fiction on different subjects. I had an account where I wrote only NSYNC fiction. Thank god all first person fics have been deleted! Next I started this account and I evidently my phases can be seen quite clearly. Dragonball Z was a secret addiction, which was strange as it was supposed to be the boys who liked that stuff. I made it all soppy though... I sucked at doing fight scenes. Anyhow, the next thing I discovered was Harry Potter. I still love it! I RP regularly and I give RP'ing credit for how I write now. Hopefully I've improved, I don't see how I couldn't have.

Also, since I did a university paper on Greek Mythology, I have a bit of a fascination for that too.

Well, thanks for reading. Comment on my livejournal if you have any questions or want to prod me for a story update. I respond to prodding but I'm so forgetful when it comes to updating!

Love you all,

Maz
