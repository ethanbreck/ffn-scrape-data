Author has written 11 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Tsubasa Chronicle.

Back in the good old days, I churned out a whopping 52 Gundam Wing fanfics, a GW/YnM crossover, and small Weiss Kreuz oneshot. Three summers ago, I took down my fanfic site and burned all the hard copies in a great big bonfire in my backyard, and it was good. They exist only on a single CD buried deep within a dank, terror-ridden cave called "my basement." They will never see the light of day again. What's here are the first fanfics I've written in about 5 years, since I graduated high school and discovered Tsubasa Chronicle.

I've got bigger aspirations, and fanfiction has been relegated to the position of sometime-hobby when a plot bunny happens along that refuses to go away. Otherwise, I concentrate on my original fiction and getting published within the next few years.

Enjoy.

I finally got around to getting an AFF account (same username, radioactivealchemist) so you can find some "dirty" versions of my fics there. So far, that's only Staying Warm.

UPDATE 3/11/14

Oh holy shit I am working on a new TRC fic. Please try to restrain yourselves while I churn out the first chapter.

UPDATE 5/14/14

Okay that may have been a little premature but I am getting there I promise. So many ideas, so little time.
