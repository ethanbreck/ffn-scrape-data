Author has written 6 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Vampire Knight, and Count Cain: God Child.

I am a full time college student, so life can get hectic pretty quick. But I love writing, ever since I was small. There are a ton of series I read and watch, but I've only written on three. X_X Which I am trying to do more of them, but I don't always have time. My Cain and Jill Saga is my current writing. Brave my high school era (02-04) if you dare. Sanity wasn't a strong suit back then. X_X

As I would like to thank everyone who reads my stories, and everyone that comments on them. Thank you!

4 Dec 2013

Due to an awakening in my life I will no longer continue The Cain and Jill Saga. I'd like to thank everyone who has read, commented, and supported me all these years in my works. I wish everyone well and bid adieu to my Cain fans. Thank you and God Bless!

Lots of love!
