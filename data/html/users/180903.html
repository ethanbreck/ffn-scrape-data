I love everything about LOTR. I have been declared mildly obsessed with LOTR in generaly and majorly obsessed with Legolas by my friends. By the way Queenie, Legolas is mine, all mine. He is my precioussssssss. (inside joke)
If you haven't read any AJ Matthews fanfics read them, they are awesome.
Anyway, enjoy my meager attempts at fanfics and review please!
Oh, yeah, feel free to aim me...i'm usually online.

I'm going to make a few comments about my stories here: For starters, The revised version of Escape to Life is better written, though if you're looking for a quick and easy read, just to get the gist of the story, read the original version. Another point: I have had many many inquiries about the relationship between Anna Maria and Legolas...*grins* Everything that happens in my stories happens for a reason. Believe me, I have a reason for Anna and Legolas being so "close". Hehehe. It will come into play in the sequel. As for the sequel: Keep the fact in mind that I am a college student. So if I'm not updating on a regular basis, there are probably a variety of reasons for it. I apologize in advance for any cliffhangers that I leave for extended periods of time! I plan on taking the sequel all the way through ROTK, merging the film and book storylines to make it work the best. Hence, obviously, it will be at least next Dec before Anna even reaches ROTK. ermmmm i think i just gave some of my plans away so i'll shut up now. As always Read and Review and Enjoy!!!

If you are looking to read a slash story, you are in the wrong place. I cannot and will not write any explicit slash in my stories. If you want slash scenes in my stories, e-mail me a sample scene and i will consider including it. Oh yeah, btw, slash is being eliminated outa my stories in a very angsty fashion that will have alot to do w/ the correct order of Aragorn marrying Arwen in the Tolkien world. Hmm that causes probs for Legolas doesn't it? ;)

If you want awesome fanfics, read stories by AJ Matthews. As for me, all I ask is that you review. Give me ideas and criticize me...Thank YOU!
