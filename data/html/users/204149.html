Guide to my WoWiverse

I'm no longer writing WoW-fanfiction, but the collection of fics I wrote are all in the same continuity. All of the stories from Relief to Crossroads to Ravines and Diplomacy take place in the middle of the Burning Crusade to some time before Wrath of the Lich King, timeline-wise. There is a what-if short story in the first chapter of A Slice of Life which takes place during WotLK, but that's the only one.

I stuck with the canon lore as much as possible, however there were some deviations. Diplomacy (Thrall/Jaina), which is heavily what-if-ish to start with, is sort of the sun which the other fics orbit.

My OCs (and if they ever get Mary Suish, feel free to roast marshmallows on me) pretty much start off in two groups, then come to interact with each other.

Dor'ash, an orc shaman, and his Forsaken mage ("pet zombie") friend Sarah first appear in Wail, Baby, Wail. Note that this is a humor fic and doesn't fit in very well with the later stories (it's the Pluto of this fanfic solar system). However it introduces Dor'ash and Sarah, as well as the troll hunter Rohdjinn and Sarah's undead boyfriend Jonathan.

Thomas Southstone, human paladin, and his friend Collins Reed, a rogue, meet and maim the raptor trainer troll Vo'don, and just meet the blood elf mage Shana in Shades of Grey (oh yeah, really innovative title there). Collins went through a whole lot of character development through me roleplaying him, and I meant to bring that out but never did. Maybe I'll return to it someday.
