Krinaia on...

krinaia

law student. which pretty much takes up all my time.

fanfics and writing

I don't get to write frequently anymore. I keep old stories up here though even though the plot lines are flawed and the grammar is bad for posterity's sake. I am guilty of very annoying writer habits such as long author's notes and confusing my 'your' and 'you're'. I also didn't know you had to end sentences with quotation marks with periods. In my defense I was 13. :D I like to think my writing has matured along with me. I don't claim to be the perfect writer, but really none of us are. So for people getting flamed, don't throw your writing dreams away just yet :P Though I've pretty much traded in "author" for "lawyer".

I just realize I've been in the fandom for almost ten years now! Wow. Back when Cassandra Claire's Draco Dormiens was THE epic fic and I lived and breathed for Lori's Paradigm of Uncertainty.

I feel old.

fandoms

The first fandom I fell in love with was Harry Potter. I've moved around a lot, occasionally dabbling in Gilmore Girls and other shows. That's what I love about books, tv, and movies. To loosely paraphrase Anne of Green Gables, there's so much 'scope for the imagination'.

Fandoms and everything to do with them - the fics, the challenges, the plot bunnies, the movies - they're my favorite form of escapism. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who's helped build each of my fandoms, you've helped me through a lot of troubling times.
