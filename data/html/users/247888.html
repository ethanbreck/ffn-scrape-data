There's really nothing much to say...I'm a huge Anne Rice fan, and, if I could, I would gladly write fanfiction with it. I role play it enough. Ah yes, another one of my little obsessions, role playing, can't get enough of it ^^;

I write alot, I wish I'd put up my fanfics more though...I have a billion. I wish I'd get more reviews too x_x and this from the girl who normally never reviews...

Favorite anime characters? In no particular order: Juri, Ruka, Shiori and Utena from Utena, Dee from FAKE, D from Vampire Hunter D, Michiru and Haruka from Sailor Moon, Black Lady and Mistress Nine from Sailor Moon, Chihaya and Messiah from Earthian, Shampoo, Ranma, and Ukyou from Ranma 1/2, and lots more.

Favorite Movie characters? Once more, in no particular order: Doc Holiday from Tombstone, Eric Draven from The Crow, Aragorn from The Lord of the Rings, Poison Ivy and Catwoman from the new Batman movies, Satine and Christian from Moulin Rouge, Obi Wan and Qui Gon from Star Wars, Curt, Brian, and Arthur from Velvet Goldmine, Tyler and Marla from Fight Club, Louis and Phillipe from The Man in the Iron Mask, and.. lots more.

Requests on fanfics? Sure! I love a good challenge, so just email me what you want, or leave it in a review and I'll gladly get around to doing it for you! I'm more open to writing slash and yuri pairings, but I'll right hetero too. However, if it's a pairing I don't like, I might not write it. ^^;

Much thanks to those that have always inspired me and been there for me:

Jen
Meg
Himemiya

You guys keep me writing.
