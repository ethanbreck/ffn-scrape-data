Previously IceAngel6

After a very long hiatus, I am back and set on finishing all of my stories. First, I will be finishing Trials and Tribulations, then working on both Draconis Sanguen and Where do I belong? similtaniously. I hope to finish TTBR soon, although this may be slightly longer as I will have to reread the story to finish it. I am also trying to write the remainder of the story out completely before posting - there are not that many chapters remaining. Never the less, my plan is to complete all of my stories and then, hopefully, start a new one. Thanks to all those who have continued to send me messages and reviews asking for updates. Just wait a little longer, and I hope to be able to fulfill those requests! (8/1/10)

I have actually tried working on all of my stories, but I read them and then second guess many parts of the stories...it is painful. It has been so long since I have written, my style of writing has changed and I find so much of my work flawed. I plan to finish these, but it is taking me some time. I am interested in a beta for all of these projects, so if interested please let me know and you will be instrumental in moving these stories forward. I just ask that my beta be open minded and willing to work with me (not against me, it kills my writing buzz if all I hear are the bad - can we look for some of the good?) Thanks!
