Author has written 8 stories for Harry Potter, Zorro, 21 Jump Street, and Lord of the Rings.

First of all I am not a writer - I am a musician. Music has been and will always be my first and only love and I think it's quite save to say that after 25 years of loving, learning, living, and breathing music.

However, much like music does there are a few books, movies, tv-shows out there that can capture my heart equally. And sometimes, not very often, it so happens that an idea for a story pops up in my head and I can't get it out. My first fanfictions I wrote for a tv-show called "The New Adventures of Robin Hood", starring Matthew Porretta way back in the 90's. They cannot be found here, though. Shortly after that I was introduced to J.R.R. Tolkien's genius and so I wrote "A time to heal" for I was quite unsatisfied with the ending of the Lord of the Rings for Frodo. Obviously I wasn't the only one thinking that way. There are a lot of stories out there concerning Frodo's healing after all that he had to go through.

Even though I don't think that my writing is particularly good, I do try to achieve at least some sort of read-ability (if that is a word - it probably isn't). I seriously lack confidence when it comes to my writing and I'm always dying a small death whenever I post a new chapter.

To my great dismay I once started a Harry Potter fanfic and never finished it. Well, I don't think that I ever will. The Harry Potter universe has lost its magic for me so to speak. It was nice while it lasted.

So if there's anyone out there who thinks that "Dreading the morning" is worth continuing, feel free to pm me if you have any ideas for this story. Maybe someone even wants to finish what I started because I find myself unable to do so.

That's it for now. Can't think of anything else worth telling about me. ;-)

My favorite authors are J.R.R. Tolkien, J.K. Rowling, Noah Gordon, William Shakespeare and Friedrich von Schiller.

I do like dark and angsty fics and I certainly do not hesitate to kill some of the characters in my stories. So, be warned.

However, there is one thing I greatly despise, especially in Harry Potter and Lord of the Rings fanfiction: SLASH. Cannot stand it, won't read it and certainly never write it. Other than that, I'm pretty much open to everything.

Well, I don't think there's anything else worth telling about me.
