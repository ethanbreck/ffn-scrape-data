HEY THERE.

I am one of those once active writers who sort of vanished off the face of the earth for like, what, five years or something? Eek. I'm really sorry to everyone who was waiting for installments for my fics, and greatly appreciate all the new readers who somehow stumble upon this.

Do not hold your breath for my updates. I learned a long time ago that my life is meant to be a stressed out crazy mess at all times, and my writing suffers from that greatly. I try to write when I can, and encouragement is always appreciated! But I'll never be one of those 'once a week update' kind of writers. Sorry!
So I've noticed that TIMEBOMB! got flagged and taken down. So obviously we'll just cut our loses on that one. I've received a couple of PMs from fans, and to answer those questions, no I am super too lazy to have uploaded Timebomb anywhere else on the internet. That was a story I always meant to go back and fix up, reading it over now makes me wince.

However, if I get enough requests I can either shoot you an email with the chapters I had previously written for your own personal enjoyment, or I might be coerced into uploading it again.
