Author has written 23 stories for Ranma, Evangelion, Sailor Moon, and Harry Potter.

So how is everyone out there in Fanfiction land? I know, I know, I'm a son-of-a-bitch and tough to get in touch with but the world just keeps on turning and my life and work have been turned upside-down in recent years. My god, has it really been so long since I've logged on to this site?

Unfortunate news is that I'm trying desperately to be a really author of original fiction but possess nowhere near the nerve or focus to finish. As it stands very little is really accomplished other than volumes of brainstorming materials and little to no actual writing. On that subject I realize that I haven't updated anything recently (or for years as the case may be) but looking through the old material I see all the mistakes I made or how I wrote myself into a corner and it brings me to the inevitable conclusion that everything needs serious revision or rewriting - something I feel that I unfortunately cannot spend time enough on to please even myself. And so I make the following announcement:

ALL MY STORIES ARE UP FOR ADOPTION! If anyone has the imagination or wherewithal to take on any of my stories shoot me a message (I promise I'll actually read them this time) and I'll tell you the plans I had for each story.

In other news I've started reading a heavy amount of Harry Potter fanfiction lately (the original novel I'm working on is a tween fantasy) and have written half a drabble. I'll post it here but please don't expect anything since my track record on finishing any of my stories is abysmal.
