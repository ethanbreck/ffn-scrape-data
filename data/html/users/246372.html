Author has written 3 stories for Clock Tower, and Gravitation.

I'm a reviewer, not a writer.

Well, I DO write on occasion. Although the whole lot of THREE stories I have here are all rather incomplete. (One claims to be, but only because I got lazy and didn't write the next chapter which said the previous chapter's end didn't really happen. Guess which one. Go ahead. Make my day.) (Oh, and one that technically is finished, I just never uploaded the final chapter due to content guidelines)

Anyways, here'sa better description of the three:

Clock Tower: A novelization of the SNES game. I'm no Stephen King, but I tried to put a bit of the horror into this. Took a shortcut with a bad ending, mostly because the next part of the game has no real plot to it, and I couldn't write it well.

Gravi Tower: Updated after a two year break. Combine the irreverence of Gravitation with the basic plot and setting of Clock Tower, and throw in my unique sense of humour for a fresh, rather funny piece, if I do say so myself. Chock full of some pretty good one-liners.

Grasping: Someone told me to write a TatsuhaxSuguru lemon. So I did. And uploaded all but the lemon chapter. It exists, somewhere on the vast internet-dealy-thing. At one point I intended to continue the story, and move it to HiroxSuguru, but I just changed my mind, mostly because the way I was going to deal with it was something I don't like reading in other fics, so why should I write it?

So, that's it then.

Enjoy.
