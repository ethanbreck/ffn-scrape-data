Author has written 2 stories for Lord of the Rings.

I am a copy editor/amateur writer living on the East Coast of the United States. I stumbled across fan fiction several years ago, and since then have spent many hours playing in other authors' worlds instead of creating my own.

Most of my writings -- the ones that are worth reading, anyway -- are set in the "Lord of the Rings" fandom. I recently discovered a few that I never started/finished writing that will eventually be posted here. In addition, I'm in the process of branching out to the "Pirates of the Caribbean" fandom. Those works -- when I finally stop planning them and actually start writing -- will be posted here as well.

If you happen to read one of my stories, please take a few minutes to let me know what you think of it -- I always appreciate comments and constructive criticism.
