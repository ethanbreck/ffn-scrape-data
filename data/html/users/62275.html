Hello all! I am the writer known as Xasonnex, but if you've bothered to read this profile, you may call me Cason. I am a 30-something living in Louisville KY. My favorite series for fanfiction is Ranma 1/2, and it may be the only one I ever write for. My favorite anime/manga series of all time (so far) is Maison Ikkoku.

If you have not read any of my stories yet, I warn you: I get writer's block AND am a procrastinator.

I now try to write under the following philosophy: The characters I write are not mine, and are not quite their owner's either. In many series (especially Ranma) the characters take on a life of their own (In the case of Ranma, I've heard that Takahashi never intended for Ranma to be the egomaniac he became). I don't have to write them, only transcribe what they would do in the situations I put them in. That's all I try to do, shape their fate by molding their environment. They do the rest. Unless I'm reading or writing a clearly stated OOC fic, then I consider any molding of a character's personality to the author's whims a perversion of said character. If I or another author take a personality beyond the confines of their existing personality, there better be a good reason.

That being said, I love it here at fanfiction.net! Read my series, please!

BTW: I'm going to work on revising the first 3 chapters of both Video Reminder and Overtime so that they are easier to read. For the record, Overtime's first 3 chapters were written in outline form in '98. If you see one of your own plot ideas in it consider it either flattery (if your fic is older) or a case of great minds thinking alike (if your series is newer). I try to be original.
