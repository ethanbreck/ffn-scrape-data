Detailed Summary for We Are One: It has been two years since Rei had left her miko training and disappeared without a trace. Minako is worried and one day decides to go to the church where she and Rei first met. What she didn't count on was that this was where she would reunite with the person she secretly loved. After Rei agreed to move in with Minako she finally got the courage to admit her feelings to the ex miko. However her plan comes crashing down once Rei admits that she know about her feelings and that she can't return her love... as she gave her heart to another. Minako is obviously shocked by this especially when she realises that the events Rei has shared with her current lover are just too similar from Rei's relationship with Minako in the past life. Minako locks the pain she is feeling away as she realises that Rei needs her at the moment... not as a lover but as a friend. Minako takes this on a challenge until an event happens which breaks the dam she has been holding her feelings in for so long. This causes Rei to runaway again... straight into the arms of the newest emeny. Minako and the rest of the senshi desperately try to save her, with the help of new ones as well. There is one new senshi. Not to mention Ari who just happens to be Rei's lover- has made an appearance despite being dead. Putting her feelings aside again as it is up to the two people who are closest to Rei's heart to save her from the evil. But Minako can't help but wonder after Rei is saved who will she choose Ari or her...

Detailed Summary for If I Were You: Have you ever wondered what it would be like if Rei was the one who had a sickness, not Minako? Would their lives be any different? Would they have still met for the first time in the church? Would Minako still pretend to be the Princess and be obsessed with the past life? Would Rei still be the first Senshi to awaken? Would Minako still isolate herself from the others or would Rei. Most importantly would Minako react the same way to Rei's death like she grieved for Minako? And would their relationship work?
