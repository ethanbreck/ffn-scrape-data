hey. so you want to know more about me, huh? duh, you're reading this, right?

okay, i live in calfornia, usa. i love television, music, basketball, and tennis. l.a. lakers are the best! derek fisher is my fav. NBA player. i'm also a big fan of andy roddick.

right now, i only write dawson's creek, dark angel, and glory days fiction but i'm hoping to branch off into other things, like gilmore girls and the o.c. fics.

i'm a big fan of adam brody, orlando bloom, josh jackson, josh hartnett, bsb, LINKIN PARK, JOHN MAYER, maroon 5, eminem, lifehouse, boysIImen, and nelly. i have an open mind when it comes to music. i listen and like almost anything and everything. from rap to r&b to alternative to pop to rock. i like to incorporate song lyrics to my fics.

i like any story that's written well but somehow, i'm drawn to powerful, tragic fics. i like the happily-ever-afters that make you feel all warm and fuzzy inside, but tragic fics are entertaining for me, maybe that's why i write mostly tragic fics.

couples i support: pacey and joey (dawson's creek), max and alec (dark angel), tristan and rory (gilmore girls), jess and rory (gilmore girls), cordy and angel (angel), fred and gunn (angel), buffy and spike (buffy), vaughn and sydney (alias), nathan and haley (one tree hill), seth and marissa (the o.c.), seth and summer (the o.c.), and sam and zane (glory days).

characters i support: alec, original cindy (dark angel); pacey, jen, audrey, charlie (dawson's creek); tristan, jess, lorelei, luke, sookie (gilmore girls); haley, lucas, nathan (one tree hill); seth, marissa, ryan, sandy (the o.c.); vaughn, sydney, weiss, jack, and marshal (alias); and zane, rudy, hazel (glory days).

that's all for now.
