Author has written 6 stories for Saiyuki, Harry Potter, Star Wars, and One Piece.

I have not logged into this account since... years and years ago. Out of the blue, I suddenly remembered my user name and password, literally, it came to me in a dream. Its so strange, I logged in and got so overwrought about it... it was like taking a big step back into my past. I read all my old work, and all the lovely reviews I've been left in my absence, and I felt inspired to write for the first time in oh so long.

So if anyone is still following this defunct old thing, please look forward to some new work from me.

Fandoms I'm following include but are never limited to Bleach, Naruto, and One Piece.

Warning- I ship very few het pairings, so be wary if you're not into that.

Does any of my writing seem familiar to you? I'm also known as Kingyo_Hanabi on livejournal. ;)
