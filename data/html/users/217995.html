Author has written 12 stories for Angel Sanctuary, Gundam Wing/AC, Rocky Horror, and Harry Potter.

I don't write fanfiction very much anymore. I'm a postgraduate student, so I really only use fanfiction as an exercise, and because I think it's fun. All of my Gundam Wing / Angel Sanctuary fics are very old, even if they were posted recently, so please do forgive them.

Here's some useless personal info:

I think Flannery O'Connor is the best American writer of the 20th century. I'm really into artsy-fartsy stuff. I'm addicted to fashion-based reality TV shows (Top Model, Project Runway, etc.).

Favorite books include: A Clockwork Orange (Burgess), A Good Man Is Hard To Find (O'Connor), A Man in Full (Wolfe), An American Tragedy (Dreiser), Bag of Bones (King), The Bonfire of the Vanities (Wolfe), The Count of Monte Cristo (Dumas), Cry to Heaven (Rice), The Dark Tower Series (King), Dubliners (Joyce), Everything That Rises Must Converge (O'Connor), Gone With the Wind (Mitchell), Harry Potter (Rowling), Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell (Clarke), Leaves of Grass (Whitman), Les Miserables (Hugo), The Lord of the Rings (Tolkien), The Mists of Avalon (Bradley), Mystery and Manners: Occasional Prose (O'Connor), Nine Horses (Collins), Picnic, Lightning (Collins), Rose Madder (King), The Stand (King), The Violent Bear It Away (O'Connor), Violin (Rice), The White Hotel (Thomas), Wise Blood (O'Connor). Favorite writers include: Flannery O'Connor (again), Joyce Carol Oates, Tom Wolfe, Theodore Dreiser, Stephen King, Alexandre Dumas, Stephen King, James Joyce, Billy Collins, JRR Tolkien, Raymond Carver, Zora Neale Hurston, Victor Hugo, T.S. Eliot, William Carlos Williams, and other assorted people who are/were friggin' geniuses.

In terms of fanfiction, I go through phases of fandom. I have two very old Gundam Wing novel-length stories and a few one-shots, as well some really old Angel Sanctuary stories. My more recent phases have been Tolkien and Harry Potter. In Tolkien I tend to work with the principal characters in Rohan and some of the less conventional characters (Ungoliant, Aredhel, Grima Wormtongue, etc). As for Potter - I'm a big fan of Lupin but I've never written a Lupin fic; I really find the Death Eaters interesting, so I like to work with them more. I also currently have a fascination with Merope Gaunt (Riddle).

And since those lovely little "ships" are so important in fanfiction, it seems, you can usually expect something along these lines from me:
Gundam Wing: Zechs/Noin, Treize/Zechs, Treize/Lady Une, Trowa/Lady Une, Treize/Relena (but I warn you- I hate Relena), Odin Lowe/OC (Sakura- Ballad of War, Remnants), Heero/OC (Aphrodite-Ballad of War, Remnants, though I never actually went through with this pairing).
Angel Sanctuary: Rosiel/Katan, Setsuna/Sarah, Lucifer/Alexiel. And I use the American spellings and go from the English translation; I'm aware that some people have a problem with that.
Tolkien: Eowyn/Faramir, Eowyn/Grima Wormtongue, Eol/Aredhel Ar-Feiniel
Harry Potter: Bellatrix/Voldemort, Lucius/Narcissa, Snape/Lily, Merope/Tom Riddle Sr, Albus/Gellert, Lupin/Tonks, Andromeda/Ted. Winter 2007 is seeing my Harry Potter phase, so expect most recent fics to be in this fandom.

Current Projects: Posting the rest of a Gundam Wing fanfic I wrote years ago as I format it for my current computer, a Bella/Voldemort story, and my next big fanfic project MIGHT be a chapter-by-chapter reworking of Deathly Hallows from the Death Eaters' point-of-view. I've also just started a multi-chapter fic exploring the beginning of Andromeda's relationship with Ted and the end of her relationship with her family.

2011 UPDATE for "CONSTELLATION": Hasn't been updated in a couple of weeks because I only keep it on my computer and my computer is in the shop. The power button broke. More will be posted once my computer is back.
