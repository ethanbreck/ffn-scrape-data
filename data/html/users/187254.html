Author has written 22 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Harry Potter, and Pitch Black / Riddick.

I'm updating yet again!! Okay, so I'm a little wired most of the time, and I do get random moments. These days I find myself reading more fanfiction than writing it. Though...if you guys could see the seventy odd notebooks full of fanfiction I wrote last year, all stored in a box...sad stuff that.

If i get the chance, I will be loading a crossover of DBZ with Chronicles of Riddick. Mostly because, take one hot psychopathic killer and put him in with another hot, prideful prince, and you get warm goodness coming from everywhere!! It makes my heart sing thinking about it!!

Here recently I've even tried my hand at Naruto...it's a work in progress.

I'm now a mother of three children, working on a divorce still!

Oh yes, I am really bad about getting writer's block, and it can prevent me from updating as often as i'd like, just ask any of my DBZ fanfiction Fans...they'd definitely warn you i can take a loooooong time to update. But I do try to do so.

I love reading and writing everything, oh, and drawing it as well. If i had a scanner i would have a lot of artwork up for every story i write. I think having artwork is great, it allows you to have a visual of what the writer sees when they're putting a story together. Mind you, I also believe that a reader's own imagination is the best asset they can have, since it is what helps them to continue reading a story.

I normally don't get a lot of reviews, so I cherish the one's that I do get, yes, even the flames. I had a great flame once that was both an insult and a compliment at the same time. Made me feel all quivery and burny eyes and mushy inside. In other words, i was beyond angry and at the same time i was unbelievably happy.

What else should I say...hmmm...Hell, I'm a huge anime fan and have probably read, watched, or looked up, more types of anime than some of the people I know. :grins: Right now I'm working on owning complete collections of:

Saiyuki

Trigun

Chrono Crusade (i'm a little fuzzy about this one right now since i'm tired and it's very early in the morning for me)

Maharomatic (That is a funny anime!)

Full metal alchemist

X (I've only had 6 for several years now, just need more and I'll be great!)

Go Nagai's Devil Lady, a very interesting series.

That's all i can remember off of the top of my head, i'll have to go through my manga and dvd collections to finish finding them.

I'm also a fan of JK Rowling and dozens of other authors, such as Anne McCaffrey, Piers Anthony, Eve Forward, Mercedes Lackey, Andre Norton, Terry Pratchett,and many many others. I don't stick with any one genre of reading either, as much as I enjoy Fantasy, I enjoy some sci-fi, alot of romance, thrillers, Horror...oh how I enjoy reading the Horror stories. Steven King is one of the best writers I have scared myself with. But not to be outdone, I have also been scared or disgusted for that matter by Douglas Preston/Lincoln Child books, and a guy who goes by Edward Lee..I think. Maybe it was Lee Edward...hmmm... :Note to self, find proper name:

There probably isn't a vampire book out that i have not heard about or picked up and read, I had the misfortune of living in the same house as my brother while he worked at walden books. Not that i didn't take advantage of the 30 discount perks but, he also bought wayyyyy to many books.

Back on track now, I am currently writing two stories under the Harry Potter Genre.

It Comes- A story where Harry's dreams take a turn for the worse and the only person who can help him is the one person he'd rather not have any contact with. (Judging by the reviewer vote for this fic, it'll end up being a Snarry)

Until Now- Harry doesn't have anything planned for anything anymore and Draco has decided to drag him away on a series of adventures. Unfortunately he doesn't count on several things occuring, such as getting attacked by a twenty odd foot long snake, meeting up with an ancient god that no one even talks about anymore, and...the last one's are surprises.

What Nighttime Brought- this is a crossover between Dragon ball z and chronicles of Riddick. Mostly it takes place after chronicles of riddick, and wellllll after dragon ball z.
