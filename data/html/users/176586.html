Author has written 18 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Parodies and Spoofs.

Hello everyone. I've recently drifted back into DBZ fandom, and though I haven't written anything yet, I am feeling increasingly guilty about my single unfinished story. I have decided that Bring Your Father to School Day WILL be finished. Eventually.

It's been so long since I wrote anything for this story, that I need to re-read the whole thing, and figure out where to take it. I'm not going to cop out and slam it to a close in one chapter, so there will likely be at least 3-4 chapters more, before it is finished.

When it will be updated/finished, depends a lot on what happens in the next few months. I've applied to join the Peace Corps, and should know soon when and if I'm leaving. If I'm leaving soon, the updates will probably be held off until I'm overseas and have adjusted, and even then will depend on the availability of internet access =)

So, while this story WILL be finished, it's all a matter of when/how long/where. So, anyone out there who had consigned this story to the void of never-to-be-finished-fics, NYAH NYAH! My fastidious nature got the better of me.

If I decide to write anymore fics, I do not know if I will be posting them on fanfiction.net. The original reason I stopped writing/posting here, was my indignation over the NC-17 ban. I don't actually WRITE NC-17, but many authors whose work I respect, did. Most of them are gone now.

I have problems with authority. When a site that makes its living off of fanfiction, which is not quite plagarism, but certainly not wholly disassociated from the dubious word, starts regulating said activity, it irks me. You can't save everyone, and IMHO it is the job of parents to regulate, not websites. Anyhoo, I've said my piece. Hope all out there are well, peace!
