I rarely add favorite stories or authors. The reason for this is that I have read thousands of fan fic stories in multiple genres over the years, and I really don't want to have to go back and have to weed through them and figure out what qualifies as a favorite. So if I don't favorite you, don't worry about it.

The one exception is that if I write a story that links with someone else's, I will favorite them so it is easier to locate.

I also suck at reviewing. As in, I rarely do it. I am a horrible fan, I acknowledge this. Sorry.
