“Hold back your dog.” ~traditional Mongolian greeting

24, January, 2005 Well, long time no writing. Sorry bout that. I'm going into revision mode as soon as i have time, not much new work is being produced on this end--INSANELY large amounts of school work and the like. On a wonderful note, I have obtained and watched the entire series, Firefly, and I LOVE IT! I have a few of those fics in progress, but they also are on delay until I have time to write for me instead of for class. That's about all that's new on this front, happy readings!* *9 July, 2004
I have finally started work on one of my stories that's kind of almost based on Carrie Shepard's death...I'll explain farther down

I'm in the middle of exploring all angles of Ivanova's death right now...I've been in a really cryptic mood lately. I've got Marcus's pre-death thoughts up, and am working on a multi-chapter fic now exploring Susan's flashbacks and hallucinations while she drifts in and out of consciousness. I may also add Marcus's thought while she halluscinates if I feel the need to. It'll be fun. Also, if I get more urging, I may start writing five stroies exploring all of the different outcomes I saw happening at the end of 'Taken by the Light,' but that would be a trmendously overwhelming project that I don't think I'm ready for yet.

I got the greatest Bab 5 parody ideas on the way home from work today! Not many people know about the collection of poems called God wennt to Beauty School, but it is one of the funniest poetry books ever! So I am re-writing the poems with Bab 5 characters instead of God like Morden went to Beauty School, and Kosh wrote a book and maybe even Lennier bought a couch, and if I should be so inspired, I may write more than one person for each poem...I'm also thinking of doing parodies of Simon & Garfunkel songs with, say, Marcus instead of Mrs. Robinson, because that would be just cool!

So, I have started the wonderful summer job at the family company...yay. That means I won't get as much written this summer as I'd hoped, but I'll still write more than I do during the school year!

However, I have decided that my second summer job, working at the Bristol Renaissance Faire, is the single coolest job on the face of the earth. note, I didn't say best. reason being that we are paid very little and have to buy so much!Despite the hundreds of butterflies I have appeared to swallow, it still can kick any other job's butt!

I've got a few Harry Potter fanfic ideas, most of them dealing with Sirius and Remus (they were such good friends, there was so much potential that was exploited and killed in the movie which was really EW!!) My friend and I are also trying to re-write the entire fifth book based on the fact that in our version Harry has a brain, and also write alternate endings. We haven't quite worked it out yet...but when we do, we'll post it!

The fic I was working on for the Marauder's first full moon has chapter one up. I haven't thought of the rest of the chapters yet, so it'll be a while.

After reading the trilogy about the passing of the techno-mages (B5/Crusade) I've been so inspired. One fic I'm planning will involve alternate lives for Galen and Isabelle, two or three before Crusade, and one, possibly two after, tracing the progression of their souls through the different lifetimes as well as following the patterns and transformations they go through on their way to transcendence.

After reading the Psi-Corps trilogy, I have about six different alternate endings planned out for Bester which I'll write up when I have time. After reading that, I'm even more into telepaths than I was before!

Um, I guess that's all for now--I'll hopefull have some work up soon, so stay tuned!*
