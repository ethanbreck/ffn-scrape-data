Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, Gundam Wing/AC, and Warcraft.

I'm a small-town girl living in the city now, and it's a strange adventure, but I'm with the love of my life, so things are great. I like country and rock/alt.music, but I listen to lots of things. Yes, I can shoot a gun better than you, but that's only because you probably can't shoot a gun or have never shot one in your entire life.

Yes, my husband can shoot better than me (but he's the one that taught me!), and yes, we're completely happily married for 5 years now. And yes, when I first started this account, many years ago, I was a lot younger, naive, and peppy. I'm much better at adulting now (lol), level headed, and mature compared to the me from 10 years ago.

I've had a huge love of writing my entire life. Recently, though, I haven't been able to make the words come out of my brain and onto the paper. I've been trying though, and when I have something, I post it.

Just a few random tidbits: I'm the mother of 2 lovely children, I don't eat when I get depressed (quite the opposite of most other people, I'm sure) and I still write better when I'm sad than when I'm happy, though that's slowly changed over the years.
