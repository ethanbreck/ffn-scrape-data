An Introduction
Hello and welcome to my little insignificant corner of Fanfiction.net. I'm female and 19 years old, and currently studying English at Uni, but you don't really need to know that so lets get onto the interesting bits ^-^

At the moment, the main thing you need to know is that I'm Pokemon obsessed, and so have written quite a few fics based on it. My longest one, and the one I suggest you read, is Story of a Dreamer, which is about Pikachu's life before he met Ash. My favourite Pokemon is Jigglypuff, and my first story dedicated to her is called Summertime in Paradise. My favourite pairing is Misty/James, and I have one story based on this, called Everything Changes. I also like Meowth/Jigglypuff, Ash/Gary and Jigglypuff/Poliwhirl.

My other favourite TV show is Yu-Gi-Oh, and although I haven't written anything on it at the moment, if you look below at my future plans, you will find one there that should be beginning when one of my others is finished. My favoutite characters are Ryou Bakura and Joey Wheeler, and I think that those two should be a couple [OK, so I'm a Yaoi fan!]

I will also be soon publishing my first Final Fantasy Nine fic, as I have recently developed an obsession for the game. The main character in this story will be Vivi, as I think he is completely cute!!

I also write a lot of fanfiction on The Tribe, as this was what introduced me to fanfiction in the first place. I am curently writing a series titled "Leaving the Past Behind", and it so far involves six stories, two of which are complete, three uncomplete and one planned.

Updates
March 17th 2003 - I finally have a new fic up, and it's my first Yu-Gi-Oh one, based on the story of Arkana in the Battle City series. There's only the first chapter up so far but more is coming very soon.

A Request
I'm looking for some stories to beta-read. I'm always correcting and helping people with their stories, and decided it would help me both in my writing an in my Uni course, if I could get some more experience doing so. I'd like fan-fiction on Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, maybe Sailor Moon, The Tribe, and a few others. If your fic isn't based on one of these, then I will still correct grammer and spelling, so feel free to send them. I will also accept original fiction. Please email me your stories, along with any particular problems you may have, to and I will get back to you as soon as I can.

Future Plans
1. "Loner" - Another part of my Tribal "Leaving The Past Behind" series, following the life of Vincent before and after the virus hit.
2. "The Power Within" - What will be my first Yu-Gi-Oh fic. Joey longs to own a Millenium item, even though he thinks it is impossible. But he's about to find out that his dream is closer than he thinks.
3. "The Dream Team" - A Jigglypuff/Meowth story, showing an alternate follow-on from their plans of making their own 'team'.
4. "Untitled as Yet" - A Final Fantasy 9 story. What would have happened if Vivi had remained in the Black Mage Village?
