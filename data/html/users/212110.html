The writer known as "Dark Day For Anime" began his life as a genetic mutant born in the radioactive wastelands of Adelaide, Australia. The name he was originally known by was Mark Andrew Page, but it has been many millennia since he has heard anyone describe him as such.

Having discovered anime and a wordprocessor, he has thus been subjecting the poor and unfortunate to his ham-fisted attempts at writing fanfiction, also known as bashing peoples' collective cerebellums with a thick, half-broken beer bottle.

The rest of the time he succumbs to the vagaries of a bipolar disorder and wonders why he likes to pay out angst in any form, as being depressed and sulky is so addictive.
