Hullo. Welcome to the twisted mind of ME!

You wish to know more? There isn't too much... I'm a sophomore in high school, in the suburbs of Columbus, Ohio (U.S.) My interest include band, band, and every so often I indulge in a bit of BAND! I am an avid Harry Potter fan and have been hiding in the shadows of fanfiction for a LONG time now, reading anything and everything, which my favorite's list will reflect. I'm a lazy bum, who, after a pitiful attempt at a fanfic, has resolved to continue my enjoyment of other people's hard work.

My favorite quote: I refuse to have a battle of wits with an unarmed person.

My favorite RUDE quote, graciously provided by my EVIL Latin teacher: Every day at least one of you screws up. (I'm touched)

My favorite instrument: Flutes rock, as I am forced to constantly remind my trombone-playing director.

My favorite character: Ummmmm, so many to chose from... I LOVE Gred and Forge... but Dumblydore is also so cool... and I like Snape (don't ask me why)... can't stand Malfoy... and of course I love our dear Boy-who-lived!

Ummmm... guess that's about it... TTFN
