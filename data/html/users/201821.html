AND HERE IN THE NIGHT

AS I FEEL THE INFERNO

I STARE IN THE DARK, THINKING

WHAT IS ETERNAL?

7/25/06

I'm not sure what prompted this sudden update, but here I am, almost three years later! There is a lot to update on, and I realize that everyone who still reads this might not want to have to read extensive paragraphs. I'll put all the important points in bold.

- I don't know if Getting Over It will be completed or not. This is largely due to a lack of interest in the story. The stories were based upon events occuring in my life at the time, so much of the story line that I had planned escapes my memory. Not to mention, Adamska borrowed the planning book with the outlines in it and I have no idea where it is now. Neither does he. I am taking this as a cosmic sign from the God of Writing That Isn't So Great from...wherever...that perhaps the entire series is left unfinished. I have not yet made the decision as to whether remove it entire or not, however.

- I have written at least 25 Metal Gear Solid fanfics since I last visited here. However, I am unable to post any of them. Why? Well fanfiction.net has this little policy about R+ rated stories now. I was going to explain how they were intended to be a gifts for Adamska, but then I realized how that still wouldn't help me look like any less, well...never mind. They will not be posted. Ever. Don't ask me about it.

- OK, so it looks like I've done nothing. But I'm still writing! I swear! Granted, most of it is incomplete, but I am actually working on two extensive pieces of original fiction, and those are taking up most of my time. The two pieces are Harmony of the Wolf and A Grand Unification, the latter of which is based on true life events. I've actually stopped writing it several times because the latter bares strong resemblance to an amime I used to watch all the time--so much so that it was actually frightening to continue the story because they were so close! It, of course, was unintentional, but it's frightening when real-life events are so closely tied to something you already know. Not to worry! All will be completed! (...eventually.)

- Yes, it's true. Kenshin and I are once again the Indubidable Duo. It took a year, but we got over it. Neither of us are dead. Go us!

- Yes, it's true. Kenshin and Takkun got together. ...OK, so this is where things stop making sense. Just go beat your head against a wall until it makes sense. It will start to after the first few hours of brain damage, I swear.

- Sashenka and Adamska got together, and then disbanded. Well, we're not technically "together" anymore. In fact, we make hobbies out of pestering the other. But hey, I am glad that my Comrade is still here. He's just... irreconcilable at the moment. But that's okay, too. He still serves at the inspiration for many of my stories and fanfics. Basically, for all intents and purposes, David = my muse. As for your questions: Yes, we're still best friends. Yes, I still love him very much. No, I'm not going to regain my health. And for that reason, no, we're not getting back together. Which brings me to another point.

- Yes, it's true. I have Tuberculosis. ...? Do I really have to write details on this one? I don't think there's really any way to explain how that effects everything! Don't ask me to be in my will, either (you greedy, greedy little bastards!). My stocks, my electronics, my money, my car, and pretty much everything else I own go to David in the event of an emergency. Except the motorcycle. That goes to Matej.

- No, seriously! I wrote stuff! I just have nothing to upload at the moment. I'm working on a gift fic! It will be done soon, I promise!
