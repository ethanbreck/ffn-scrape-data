Ok... what should I tell you other then that I love to read all types of B/S fanfics. I'm a really big fan of BTVS! I hate Riley (who doesn't) and I love Spike (duh!)! I like Angel as long as he gets his butt kicked by Spike and if the great poof plays nice he is ok too! I don't write fanfic myself... yet, 'cause my English isn't good enough for a propper story.

Well, time to update... I do not only read BTVS fiction now but also delve into Stargate SG-1 and my newest addiction is LOTR. I'm very glad so many authors keep on writing stories about BTVS even so the series is long over. I still do love Spike dearly. Thanks for all the great stories out there. Maybe some day I will try my hand on writing one myself. Though I still think my English needs a bit more improvement.

I also added a few of my favorite storys and authors in this nice little section down there! Enjoy!

December 6, 2006: I wrote such a nice update yesterday, but it didn't save the changes. growls Anyway, time to update this little section. A lot has changed over the past few years. For example, I don't read Buffy anymore, mostly LOTR. I love stories with Elrond and Glorfindel in them, but also read stories about Eomer or Haldir. There is one author I very much want to recommend. She is one of the best LOTR authors on ff.net: Nili. Please take a few minutes to look her up. She is an amazing writer and all her stories are well worth your time.

If I'm really really bored and can't find anything good in the LOTR-section I have taken to read NCIS and Gilmore Girls. I'm a huge fan of NCIS and there are some nice stories here, not many though mind, but enough to keep me satisfied.

I have also begun to write my own LOTR-stories, but I'm too chicken to put them online. A friend of mine told me that they are good, but still, there are always some parts I'm not totally happy about and until those haven't been changed I won't give them out to read here. Soon maybe, but no promises in that regard.

Once again I want to direct your attention to Nili, who has just began to post a new story, which already promises to become a fantastic as her other ones. Look her up and enjoy her wonderful stories!
