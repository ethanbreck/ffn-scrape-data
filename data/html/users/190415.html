Okay, i know you guys are really pissed at me. I know I havent updated in four months (Has it really been that long?) Anyway, here's the deal.

Anyway, I got so frustrated cause I had been on a writers block and that chap had been so hard to write that I just said F*K IT!!!

Then, for Im With You, I had a bunch of chap written, got a betareader who made me make a bunch of changes, and now have to rewrite ALL of those previously written chaps. So, theres that.

As for Highschool Years, I just haven't gotten to it. It's that simple.

SO! What all this boiles down to, is that I will TRY and have some new chaps up within a few weeks. I'm gonna stock up on Dr.Peppers, park myself infront of the computer screen, and HAVE AT IT! IF I can pull myself away from my Em fics...

PEACE OUT, YALL!
