GUESS WHAT?! MY BROTHER IS GETTING MARRIED!!! HE HAS HAD THIS GIRLFREIND FOR SO LONG, AND NOW, THEY’RE GETTIN' HITCHED!!! YEY!!!! I REALLY LIKE HER TO!!! NOW, GO ABOUT AND YELL YAY!! FOR THEM, THANKS!!!

Hi!!! I luv Lord of the Rings! Elrond is my fav. char.! I think that he rulz! He is also cool in the Matrix! I luv that movie! I also like some anime and things like that. DBZ is cool...so is Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, Card Capter Sakura(sp?), Monster Rancher, and Outlaw Star. I luv all of these things...but not the dubed version...I don't like those...they stink! X_x I also like the cartoon Mighty Ducks...it plays on cartoon network. I also like Star Wars...I like the old version, but the new version is cool, too. I'm also a fan of slash and Yoai. I don't care if you don't like it. I think that it’s very cool although I like "regular" relationships also.

I have some cool friends, and some of them I am closer to then others. Two of them have names at FF.net, which are Eowyn of Ithilien and Queen of the Fairys. If you tire of my stories, go read theirs.

I mostly keep to myself, and only let my true self come through my writing, and I, more times than not, end up revealing things that I had wanted to keep hidden in them. I am a true teenager, and I try my best not to be stereotypical whenever I can. I usually wear baggie clothes. I try to surprise people with my attitude. I have a carefree happy-go-lucky kind of dimmer, but I usually only use it as a mask. I think about things that I shouldn't be thinking of at my age.

I like writing, and I enjoy it when people get joy out of my writing. I hope to publish a book before I turn 14. I listen to many kinds of music, but mostly Pop Rock. I highly dislike pop, but I can stand it. Papa Roach, New Found Glory, Box Car Racer, Queen, Reel Big Fish, Alien Ant Farm (I don't think I spelt that right...hehehehe?), and Blink 182 are some of Fav.'s.

I have 5 pets (4, if you count that my cat, Moonlight, lives at my grandmas now) 3 cats, whose names are weird, but they fit them very well. Their names are: Survivor, Psycho, and Whiskey. My last pet is a dog and his name is Squealer...which also fits him. My family and I aren’t the best at naming things. I once wanted to name everything male Tom, and everything female Sally...I had a weird child-hood... ^,^!!! That’s about it...

One more thing, I have an imaginary pet ferret named Bob who is very entertaining to my friends and me. Although, one of my closest friends does NOT get along with him. (You know who you are...) They have a long-standing grudge about cheese. Bob also doesn’t like my friends’ imaginary punker (whose name, for some long forgotten reason, remains a mystery...dun dun dun...)

...bye!!!
