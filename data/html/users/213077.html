Hey! Thanks for liking my stuff enough to want to know who I am! My name is Sunny, and I am 23 years old. Nobody likes you when you're 23!! I'm getting old. I live in Idaho, and, NO, I do not grow potatoes. Believe it or not, I live in the capital and I'm not a hill billy. :D People are always surprised at how big my city is. -rolls eyes- Oh, if you want to see me as I am in all my non-funny glory, ;), this be my youtube account, complete with vids: http://www.youtube.com/user/JacintaSeabrook

I am a short girl more fitting in looks of a hobbit, even tho I desperately believe myself to be more elf-like in inner self. Usually I just settle for Jedi, since there is definitely not a height limit on that. Look at Yoda. Now I just have to show them I have the force. -glares at keyboard, willing it to type from the force of her will-

I used to want to be a writer, but I am now considering that to be a side thing, because it has become very hard in the last while, and more of a burden than a joy. Hence why I am not writing as much. I am trying to infuse the joy back into it. When I do, I WILL be finishing my stories, and knowing me, starting more. -sigh- I would like to be an American Sign Language translator, and I fianlly got approved for an accelerated course that gets you at least passingly fluent in 3 or 4 months and is free!! I'm on my way, YAY!

I am a rabid fan girl who, along with her scores of plot bunnies and hormones, follows pictures of men all over the internet and wastes away hours watching movies with hot guys. When I am not doing that, I listen to music, do temp work when I can get it, try to write, do some volunteer work, and read way too much fan fic for my own good. LONG LIVE FAN FIC CHARACTER TORTURERS! You guys are the wind beneath my wings.

I am finally settled in. I moved back with my parents, but it worked out since I got laid off soon after, and they have been great about helping me during this time. I have one cat, one dog -finally!!- and too many gerbils.

Favorite Actors: Hot AND Talented- Cillian Murphy, Karl Urban, Ewan McGregor, Colin Firth, Cary Elwes (when he was younger), Jeremy Brett (when he was younger: AKA, Freddy from My Fair Lady) Keanu Reeves (congrats, boy, you made it! -Lake House did it for him-) Jonathan Rhys Meyers, and of course Johnny Depp, tho I am not as big a fan as others. I just respect him and think he is hot. Also, I'm adding Michael Rosenbaum. I thought long and hard about it, wondering where to put him on my list, but in the end, any guy who can play a cross-dressing humorous role and then turn around and be evil Lex Luthor has to get some attention. :P Hot actors, but not necessarily amazing in acting- Tom Welling yes, he IS a good actor. He is really good in Smallville, but I can't judge on one good performance. I need a few different roles to see their versatility before they make my talented list. Orlando Bloom...Updates to follow...Keep working at it Orli, I'm waiting for the breakthrough performance! Jensen Ackles and Justin Hartley are going here for the moment. They're insanely hot, but I need to watch some more of their stuff before I can pass a verdict. Actors who are amazingly talented but not necessarily hot- Liam Neeson and Sean Bean (amazing men, and their voices make me weak in the knees), Hugo Weaving (mmmm, V), Jimmy Stewart (cutie), Gary Oldman, Danny Kaye, Peter Sarsgaard (amazing actor,but not really handsome to my tastes) and, no matter what ANYONE says, Hayden Christensen. I don't think he is handsome, and I was unimpressed with Anakin, but Shattered Glass and Life as a House were stunning performances. I'm adding Leonardo Dicaprio. I don't care that he's not handsome to me and I hated Titanic, he's been amazing in everything else I've ever seen, so he's going on here.

Fav Actresses: Cate Blanchett, Liv Tyler, Natalie Portman -most beautiful woman I know of, and totally everything I would like to be. :)-, Meg Ryan, Emma Thompson, Kelly MacDonald (she makes me laugh), Hilary Swank, Francis O'Connor, Julia Ormond, Renee Zellweger, Amy Adams, and I don't know who else. I find less female actresses impress me. And I usually can't think of the ones who do. Sorry all you women!

Fav music artists: Tokio Hotel, Jimmy Eat World, Oasis, O-Zone, Weird Al Yankovic, Loreena McKennitt, Enya, John Williams' soundtracks, Vertical Horizon, Metallica, Switchfoot, Creed,Third Eye Blind, Three Doors Down, Supreme Beings of Leisure, Nirvana, and just about anything not country, out and out pop, and rap. -shudders-

Likes: Italian food, especially Timballo, music, reading, singing, acting -lettered in high school, putting on own stuff,- hot actors, good movies, computer games, Strong Bad, Invader Zim, chocolate, root beer, Scotland, Ireland, New Zealand, Ender's Game series, LotR, Star Wars, PotC, Pendragon series, Smallville, Batman Begins and Batman Forever, wine, Smirnoff, and my friends.

Dislikes: MEAT! -I am a vegetarian- school, bad acting, bad writing, bad teachers, sweet potatoes, mushrooms, beer -see? Not a hobbit,- boy bands, female pop artists, zucchini, cheerleaders, bullies, and guys who are full of themselves. Oh, and Lana Lang. Death to all characters that have no depth but only serve the purpose of bringing angst into a story. And death to the people who are trying to turn Smallville into a freaking soap opera.

Favorite saying, currently: Before you critize someone, walk a mile in their shoes. Then you're a mile away and you have their shoes!
