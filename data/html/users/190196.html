Anime and manga are my life. I love them so much. I doubt that I can live without them. :p

I've watched too many anime and manga for me to count. Some of my favorite anime and manga are: Gundam Wing, Weiß Kreuz, Fushigi Yuugi, Vision of Escaflowne, Detective Conan, Slayers Series, Card Captor Sakura, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Ranma 1/2, Slam Dunk, Kindaichi Shounen no Jikenbo, Digimon Series, Tenchi Muyo! Series, DragonBall Series, Yuu Yuu Hakusho, etc. Though my fics are mostly going to center on GW.

My No.1 anime character is Heero (for GW). He is just so cute. Cutie Hee-chan. *hugs my Hee-chan plushie* ^^;; My favorite pairing for the GW is 2x1. My fics are going to be about yaoi, but some are going to stay neutral...

I also have a big obsession to the Japanese seiyuu (voice actors). Some of my favorite seiyuu are: Midorikawa Hikaru, Seki Tomokazu, Okiayu Ryoutarou, Koyasu Takehito, Kanna Nobutoshi, Takayama Minami, Hayashibara Megumi, Sakamoto Chika, Iizuka Mayumi, Sakamoto Maaya, Ogata Megumi, etc.

I've only learned English for a few years. So if you find that my fics are sucks, please do tell me. I appreciate any comment and critic. Feel free to let me know how do you think about my fics.

Thank you for those of you who actually read this. ^_^;;
