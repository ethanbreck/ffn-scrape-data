Author has written 4 stories for Veronica Mars, and Criminal Minds.

So here's my chance to say something very profound, if i think of something fitting i'll put it here.

I've always been a very dedicated fan fiction reader marvelling at the creativity of some authors, i've dabbled in it occaisionally writing a chapter here or there of rather appalling excuses for stories then deleted them in shame. That was a few years ago.

Unfortunately of late all these ideas keep swirling in my head and refuse to rest unless i put them to use, maybe it's because i'm far away from uni and civilisation stuck in a country mining town. Boredom and isolation does funny things.
