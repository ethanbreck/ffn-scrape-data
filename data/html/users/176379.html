So, what can i say about myself?

The reason why i write romance fanfic is probably the fact that somewhere along the line, i've stopped believing in love, the kind we read about all the time. I have yet to change my mind. *grin*

I'm gilmore obessed. (and suffering from a lack of gilmore magic.. season 3 hasn't reached us yet.)

Well, trory obessed first, then gilmore obessed. What can i say? That boy is soo hot. Plus, that whole roguish smile, love-me-hate-me thing appeals to me.

As for my favourite books, there are quite a few. Lurlene McDaniel never fails to make me cry. i'm a sucker for the star crossed lovers thing, though i suck at writing 'em.

The other books i read are mostly romances, stuff from Karen Robards and all. Of course, i love sci-fi and murder books, but romance is still my favourite genre. *grin*

P.S. feel free to add me to your msn list or whatever or even send me emails. I love the chance to make friends.
