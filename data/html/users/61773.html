FICS IN PROGRESS

The Flip Side: Harry Potter, set during CoS. Marcus Flint/OC with teammates Pucey, Higgs and Bletchley vs Weasley Twins, Wood, Spinnet, Johnson & Bell. Mainly from the POV of the Slytherin 6th years, the majority of the Quidditch team. From Chapter 14 on, includes the character of Holly Hocks, on loan from fellow site member badly drawn girl featured in her fic "Goodbye Sky Harbor," a really nice little tale that I really wish she would find the inspiration to finish. Harass her if you feel the same.

Some Kind of Monster and Master of Puppets: TNA wrestling. First two parts of a trilogy involving Chris Sabin, Alex Shelley and Raven, primarily. Set during the Shelley/Devine/Nash Paparazzi Productions era. I fully intend to finish the third part.

Crossed Paths: Lord of the Rings, set during the WR. Occasional lines of dialogue taken from the movies to fit with the action, but otherwise is is generally based primarily on the books. At long last - chapter 13 is finally up.

It's All Relative: X-Men, set after last ep of 90s series. Revised and Complete.

The Memory Remains: X-Men, set after It's All Relative. Under heavy construction, plot needs tightening so I don't suggest beginning to read because it will change.

Fateful Reunion: Andromeda, set during season one between "It Makes a Lovely Light" and "Its Hour Come 'Round at Last." Was originally set during season two but will also be undergoing construction to fix the plot.

Other than that, I just need to send a shoutout to my pal Frootkake Productions, whose knack for nailing the characterization in Buffy and Angel stories never ceases to amaze me. Dude, you rule. And to badly drawn girl, who kept me inspired to write The Flip Side, and loaned me one of her characters (Holly Hocks) to boot!
