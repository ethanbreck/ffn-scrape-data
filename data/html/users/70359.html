In the three years it has taken me to change this profile, I have come to realize many things. One, I cannot and probably will never retain the ability to write a fic. Alas, that doesn't stop me from trying. And for that my deepest apologies for inflicting such torture upon you. Second, I may grow out of certain things but I will never grow out of obsession. It is my middle name and the code by which I live. So, I've once again decided to take my pen into hand and chronicle the adventures of a peculiar man named Sherlock Holmes. We'll see what happens after that.

I'm a university student too ignorant and too lazy to actually produce anything worthwhile. Don't sue me, I only have a J-drama collection that I don't even completely own the rights to. You'll only be compensated with lint balls and a few spiders.

Currently obsessed with:

Nodame Cantabile (drama), SH22, Johnny's Jimusho, Tennis no Ohjisama, Bleach, Gokusen, HYD, Avatar: The Last Airbender, Spectacular Spider-man
