UPDATE!! (AS OF 25 OCT 2019)

With The Final Sacrifice done and rounding of the DoW series, I'm taking a break from writing fic for a while, so I can concentrate on other things in my life for the time. I'm not going to write HoO from Annabeth's, or anyone else's perspective, for reasons I listed in my note in the last chapter of TFS. But if any readers who've followed DoW should be inspired to take Annabeth's story forward, though, I would be most pleased. And if you would like to speak to me about doing such a project, I would be happy to advise and share my experience of doing DoW, and help where I can.

At the moment, I've just got The Tyrant's Tomb out of my e-library, so I'm going to kick start that my read-through and reaction sharing on the old discussion forum I started for TBM last year.

Hi! I'm shiiki and you've found my fanfiction.net page. I write primarily for the Percy Jackson fandom these days, although you'll probably find some of my old stuff from other fandoms kicking around here, too.

The stuff I post here is mostly what I hope readers here will enjoy. If you're interested in reading more, a full list of every fanfic I've ever posted is here.

I can also be found on LiveJournal (shiikifics is where I archive my writing and recommendations for stories I've enjoyed; shiiki is my primary blog); tumblr, and Ao3.

My main fandom project in PJO has been the Daughter of Wisdom series, an alternate PoV piece following Annabeth Chase through the Percy Jackson & the Olympians book series. You can find the relevant parts here: Daughter of Wisdom, The Golden Fleece, The Necklace of Harmonia, The Impossible Maze, and The Final Sacrifice. I also have a kind-of future fic written for the 2017 Big Bang: The Curse of Lethe. You can find other assorted short stories on my author's page.

I love to engage with my readers, find out what they liked or what they didn't, and constructive criticism is always appreciated! To me, sharing fic is like starting a conversation ... don't leave me hanging, okay? I want to know your views on the characters in our beloved fandoms as well! So don't be shy about leaving a comment. I try to respond, even to anonymous reviews via my discussion forum.

If you want to discuss plot ideas, talk about characters, writing, fiction, or anything at all, send me a PM, check out my forum, or come find me at my blogs (shiiki or dotshiiki)--you don't have to have an account to talk to me on either of those, so you can always keep your anonymity if you like.

Thank you, and enjoy reading!
