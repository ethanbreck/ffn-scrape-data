Dear FF.net Profile (or readers),

I have only recently been coming back to this account. It has come to my attention that some of my stories are rather cheesy. I might point out that the good majority of these fics were written several years ago. I was young and naive and new not the rules and etiquette of fan fiction. Forgive me. However I will not remove them. They are my history, my beginnings, and however embarassing they may be, I'm hoping that they might atleast be a learning experience for those just entering the fandom. I am also, against my better judgement, going to continue the sequel to Trials of Life, Love, and Mischief. I know that it is horribly Mary-Sue. But please know that it's mainly written for my own sick amusement. It's my baby, my first LONG story (or second, as it is the sequel). And despite it's tendancy to embarass the hell out of me, I love it. I will continue to post it for the very few of you who read it (and I want to thank you, especially, for putting up with bad grammar, those KILLER LINES (remember those? Still gives me a headache), and the sheer cheesiness of it all.

But I will make you a promise. No more Mary-Sue fic. Ever. EDIT: Except for that time today when I posted the last chapter of the unfinished Mary Sue sequel. ANDthenwroteanentirethirdstorytocomesoon. Sorry.

I hope you all can take me seriously in future endeavors.
