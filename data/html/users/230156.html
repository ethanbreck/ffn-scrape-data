30-MAR-2015

I'm nearly finished with my pride and joy, a story for Gundam Wing, AU fantasy setting, where Lord Heero, looking for a challenge, gets himself a slave nicknamed Shinigami. Meanwhile prince Quatre sets his slave free. This story will consist of two parts.

Part I is as good as finished, just needs some tweaking and can be seen as a finished story in itself. It just has enough loose ends for a follow up. This part is twenty chapters and 100.000 words. I've found two new beta readers for this story, so applying for the job is, at this point, no longer necessary.

I have an outline written for part II of this story, but I'm not certain I'll ever get around to writing the full story. If I do, you'll be sure to see it appear on fanfiction.net eventually! And if I never come around to writing it, don't worry, Part I has, in my opinion, a satisfying ending.
