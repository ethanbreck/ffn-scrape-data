What's there to say? I'm a girl. I like rock and hip-hop. I go to a cheap school. I like to write about whatever I'm into at the time. I read way too much. And am the biggest Paul fan you'll ever see! Your name's Paul, I LOVE ya! It's that simple...or is it? Mwahahaha
I have lots of names.

I have also gone by:
Kar-chan(my most common)
music nimf(obviously)
Nobody Important
Katipiller
Grassy

At least that's what I said, give or take, three years ago. I no longer go to a cheap school (quite the opposite in fact...so bloody expensive...). I have also seemed to have gotten over my obsession with the name Paul (One of my children WILL be named that in the future though *grins shamelessly*). Music Nimf and Kar-chan are pretty much the only names I use now. I like all music and polka dancing is the shiznits. I also, unfortunately have a life now and can't afford much time writing fics, but I might, and I have at least a dozen unfinished fics on my computer that I could always start up again.We'll see. I do miss the music section terribly, though. Oh well, the past is the past.

I WILL write a story one of these days...yeah, one of these days -_- ...

Or not. Now a whole hell of a lot of time has passed since I first wrote this bio. That was like 6 years ago. Now I pretty much just read and review. I pretty much use this account to keep track of my reviews. So yeah. I suppose that there is a chance that I could write another story, but don't hold your breath.

So doesn't look like I'm going to be writing any stories any time soon. I haven't even been reading fanfiction as much as I used to. I go to an expensive school again (yet more inexpensive than the last) because it's college. Yep, this girl's growing up. I should be doing my math homework right now and I'm sure that I'm annoying my roommate with my typing since she's trying to sleep (it's 3:50am). And that's that I guess. Oh, and his name will be Paul Isaiah [insert father's last name here].

Well I'll be, I wrote a story. Actually I wrote many, but they're neither posted here nor do I think they ever will be. I'm just sayin'... Also, I've taken to calling people Deary.

It's been over two years since my last edit. oh shi-! I just got called out on the Paul thing. Still a lovely name btw. I thought about making my profile less... chronological and more respectful, but I don't think I will.

So as of two days ago, I have had this account for ten years. I don't even know what to do with that information. For heaven sakes, I was a kid when I made this account (to write in the long since removed Linkin Park section of all things). Now I've graduated from college and I'm not even in the same country right now. Oh these times, they are a-changin', yeah? Well thanks to everyone who has read my stories some time in the last ten years. I do truly enjoy fanfiction.

Hi, it's 2014. Some of you weren't even born when I made this account. Hello kiddos.

2016. I was an embarrassing child.
