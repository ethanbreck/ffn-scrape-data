My name is Sirena Nachelle Robinson, I am 25 and a lawyer. I am married, have one daughter, and I am working on my Master's Degree in Social Work. I write pretty much whatever strikes me. Harry Potter, Buffy, Angel, Dark Angel, Twilight. Those are the ones I've done, or have in progress right now. I live off of reviews, and I tend to lose interest in fan fics if I don't get feedback. I feel like I'm writing for nothing. And I have original novels in progress that I could devote the time to. So drop me a line, let me know what you think of my stuff. I am currently only writing Twilight fiction, and while I doubt I'll go back to any previous worlds, I do still occasionally enjoy visiting them through the works of others.
