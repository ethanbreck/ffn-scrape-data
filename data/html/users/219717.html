Well I finally did it! I registered on Fanfition.net! Yay for me! ^_^

Now for the real test to see how well I do with posting the stuff I write. I write a lot but I'll say this right up front I AM A FICKLE WRITER!!! I have the very bad habit of being very into a story for a few days then dropping it completely for whatever new muse strikes my fancy.

On a personal note the only thing I like better than writing is reading! My favorite books include Laurell K. Hamilton's Anita Blake series and her Elven Princess series, Lynn Flewellings books, Mercedes Lackey's Valdamar series, Janet Evanovich's Plum series, (are we seeing a patern here? ^_^) and a lot of other books I can't think of at the moment.

I'm also a big fan of anime. Some of my favorites are Ranma1/2, X, Gundam Wing, Please Save My Earth, Bastard!, and many, many more. In fact I like most anime.

Well that's all I've got to say for now. I really REALLY hope you like my stories.

Buh-Bye!
