Okay. So...

(Here's the Earth)

I'm back from a long haitus! I haven't updated for over two years, and re-reading my shiat, I see that I'm in desperate need of a rewrite.

As of May 18th, 2005, I am in a process of rewriting my stories. Because, well, I'm not too pleased with them. More on this as it occurs!

Status: 4/5 Fics Rewritten. See stories for details on what has changed. (Which so far isn't too much, I'm starting with the minor edits to the fics I was originally pretty happy with)

Allright, HUGE APOLOGIES to the people who originally reviewed Demon Tooth... ff.net was being glitchy and made me delete it... :grumblegrumble: SORRY :Cries:

After ff.nets desiding to be retarded and make me delete my stories before I can edit them, I've stopped editting and have moved on to writing new chappies!

The reason I haven't updated in so long is, basically, after starting an account at fictionpress and getting nothing but flames for the one essay I'd poured my soul into, I figured I just wasn't a good writer. I gave up for a long time, and now have gotten back into it just because I'm bored, and I think that maybe the reason for the flames was because of blantant homophobia on fictionpress, and not necessarily my skills at writing. But I'll let the reviews do the talking, and if I get good ones I'll be encouraged to write more, and if I get sour ones, I'll probably stop writing again... I want some cheese...

once I find it I'll post the link to the offending essay here and let the ff.net readers judge for themselves...

This project is suspended, who knows? Maybe I'll rewrite it after the Seventh book, or just stick an AU label on it later on.
