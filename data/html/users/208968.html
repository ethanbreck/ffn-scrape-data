Yes, I'm aware that I update ultra slow. But! I do update! And when I do, most likely my audience has changed entirely, but that's okay. I'm alright with that. After all, I write because I want to, not because I have people to please... which may be the cause of my weird pairings... I mean, c'mon! Lily Evans and Voldemort? What am I ON?

Anyhoo, I do love reviews, they make my day a little brighter and gives me reason to update sooner, but no reviews means no pressure of updating soon. So make my day. I dare ya... squinty eyes

I am currently focusing my attention on
1)Finishing these last 2 years of school to get my masters in math/math ed. (I'm going to be a teacher, can you believe that? LoL)

2) Homework, homework, homework

3) (the part you're concerned about) Working Model, At First Sight, and last, but certainly not least, Starcrossed Lovers. I hope to finish before I finish school (that is, within the next two years), but RL comes first.
