Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, Stargate: SG-1, Star Wars, Hana Yori Dango, and Tin Man.

i love reading and this place provides me with stories about characters that I have loved and that I wished something different might have been done with their stories... To me reading and writing are ways in which I can visit places and things within my mind that I can't do at other times. It is a relaxing passtime. My little sister enjoys my poetry and she is very honest, so she says very honestly when it sucks!!

Actually, it was her, who convinced me to post my very first fanfiction, named True Spells. She said that she wanted to read something that I had written. She keeps asking when i am going to complete it, but things just got in the way and somehow i got stuck.

Currently my favorite fandoms are: Harry Potter, Iron Man, Hana Yori Dango, Stargate-SG1, Tin Man, Ugly Betty, Firefly and Star Wars.
