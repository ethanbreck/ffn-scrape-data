Hey, My name is Kara. I'm 16-years-old and I'm from Ohio in the U.S.A! I'm finally writing my first fanfic! I plan to write more if this goes well. These are a few things you should look for in my writing:

Harry Potter in action! ;) Now, the whole romance thing is alright, given the quality of the writing. Of course, Harry will be 15 years old in the next book, and he's gonna be into girls and all. But no use obsessing about it. :D I like to read Harry Potter action fics. On occasion I'll read romance. Most likely Harry/Ginny. But I settle for Ron/Hermione. And the only slash I like is pg-rated Sirius/Remus. Though I don't really expect a lot of H/G or R/Hm in the books, and certainly no S/R!!!

2) My favorite Characters are Harry, Sirius, and Dumbledore. I mean, you gotta love Harry! The average type of kid who turns out to be a great wizard in the making...we can all relate to that! Well, uh...we can't relate to the wizard part...but the average part, we...er *cough* Anyway, Sirius is also one of those type of people you gotta love. I mean, the guy is in complete Hell for 12 years, never having any hope left in him. All of a sudden, Harry's in danger and thats enough to make him risk more then his life, he risks his very soul! I will miss Sirius very much in the last two canon books. And Dumbledore, well, Dumbledore is the type of person you can trust no matter what. That is pretty rare in this world, that's something you gotta respect. But being as how Dumbledore, Sirius, and Harry are completely fictional, and I'm talking about them like they're real people...don't be frightened...I'm not usually this strange. I also like Ron, Hermione, Hagrid,and Lupin!

Favorite qoute: Nobody's perfect. I'm a Nobody. Therefor, I am perfect. ;)

Other things about me: hmmm...I hate it when you're reading a really good story...and the author is almost to an end...but it takesmonths to write the next chapter.

I know, I have done that a few times with my "Harry Potter and Voldemort's Revenge" fanfic. But at least I've never abondoned it entirely.

If someone dies that didn't have a point, I usually stop reading the fanfic. Too much melo-drama for me!

sighs* Of course, Sirius died in "Order of the Phoenix" for no apparent reason. And I won't stop stop reading the canon. *crosses fingers and hopes that J.K.R brings Sirius back*

Also, if something unrealistic happens (you gotta think, HP is pure fantasy, but anyway) or its *really* corny (Harry bringing back James and Lily to life...not gonna happen in the HP books, so why in a fanfic?

I go for realistic AU that is fairly true to the canon. Though unrealistic fanfics that are well written are okay by me. :)

Or a NC-17 het/slash paring...it's a kid's book folks! I also hate how authors introduce characters you've never heard of and make them the star of the fanfic(ex. the author herself, and/or the author's best friends).

Mary-Sue's and Barry-drew's are not cool and can ruin well developed fanfic's entirely. Careful not to make any character too perfect.*winks*

I'm a catcher in Softball (my 8th year playing)! I'm probably in the top 10% of my class, but I assure you...I'm not a loser. :)~ I'll be a sophmore in highschool.

Anyway, I used to like the BSB (hence my screen name) but am not a fan of the band (though used to be...did you know Nick's middle name is Gene? *blushes*) I just created this screen name before i realized how dumb it was. I'm currently a little obssesed with Harry Potter. Just a little. My favorite authors include: The Black Unicorn, Lilly Potter, Lin-Z, Prongs, Dreamer4, Essence of Magic, and Amadeus!

Just a little bit on me. I hope you like my first fanfic. Please R/R! Be nice though! Hope you read and like it! E-mail me when ever you feel like it! I'll respond! I always like to chat!

Kara*

UPDATE: I'm not nearly as frequent the writer as I was when I started with "...Voldemort's Revenge". But obviously if you look at the difference between then and my latest (though long-awaited) work, you can appriciate the difference in techinque! Above all, I still hope you enjoy my fanfics. And though I've long since stopped resorted to begging for reviews, I still love it when I get one occasionally. So...enjoy!"
