Author has written 21 stories for Fushigi Yuugi, Lord of the Rings, X-Men: Evolution, Final Fantasy I-VI, Chrono Trigger, Shadow Hearts, Fairy Tail, and Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney.

Jesus H Christ. It's been nearly 4 years since I last updated my profile. Holy Hell.

Well, you can see from my library, I haven't done much these past years. Unfinished rewrites of my high school stories remain on the back burner. I wish I could finish them. I regret not finishing Red Wings of Fate but...I just lost inspiration for the original and the rewrite. Honestly...I started completely rerwiting the back stories for some of the celestial warriors and that got more interesting than the actual story. I don't know what it says that I lost interest in the 2 characters that were supposed to be the main protagonists but yeah. I totally did.

Anyway...Fairy Tail got a lot of my attention. But one shots were about all I could manage.

I played Ace Attorney: Dual Destinies and was hit with all my Phoenix feels again. So, while I had this miracle 11K word coherent thought, I stared writing a continuation. It's already like...3-4K words I think. Also thinking about some companion pieces to Let It Snow.

PS - I don't log onto FF.net that much any more. If you really want to make sure I see something you want me to see, bug me on tumblr. Username: missazurerose
