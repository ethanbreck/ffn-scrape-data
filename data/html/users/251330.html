Hi.

I love to read! I absolutely love it. I have a very wild and extremely imaginative imagination. (redundant, i know) Every time I read something or see a movie that I enjoy I put myself in the main charcter's place and turn the story over and over in my head. I make things different and expand on things.

My favorite books are the Harry Potter books of course, The Hollow Kingdom Trilogy by Clare B. Dunkle, and The Guardians of Time Trilogy by Marianne Curley. I also enjoy Crown Duel Duet by Sherwood Smith, as well as the Wind on Fire Trilogy by William Nicholson, along with many others. I stongly suggest all of them.

My favorite movies to date are probably A Knight's tale, the Pirates of the Caribbean movies, Legally Blonde 1 and 2, all X-Men movies,and Chasing Liberty, oh yeah and the Harry Potter movies (minus the 3rd one), Employee of the Month, and The Prestige.

I have recently posted a story about Sarah, a transfer student from Australia, and Draco. I am a huge DM/HG shipper but just for fanfic... I'm not naive enough to think that it would ever happen in the books...

Anyway I hope you enjoy what I write... please review... i wanna hear what you think...

Love,
