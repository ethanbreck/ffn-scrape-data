Author has written 9 stories for Harvest Moon, and Tales of Graces.

What can I say here... I don't have that much to tell really.

I'm twenty-three years old, female and Australian. I'm also an animation student now, so if you don't see me update for a while, it's because of school

I kind of have a love hate relationship with writing - I enjoy it, but I easily get discouraged and distracted. (I'm much more of an artist really, check out my deviantArt page! /shameless self promotion). I mean, I think I can write, but I'm not like wonderful at it or anything haha. Well, I try my best anyhow. Romance is my favourite genre to write, be it something dramatic and serious or some light-hearted fun. Be warned, however, I like my stories to have an unexpected twist. ;)

My favourite fandoms include Sakura Taisen (Wars), Ace Attorney, Harvest Moon, Pokemon, Golden Sun, Ghost Trick and the Persona series. Also, Dexter is the most amazing TV show ever. And Breaking Bad is pretty amazing too.

When it comes to Harvest Moon, I've been obsessed with the Tree of Tranquility/Animal Parade universe for a while now. My most favourite bachelor ever is Calvin, with Chase coming in a very close second! I also like Cliff, Marlin, Carl, Will, Pierre, Angelo, Cam, Hiro and I'm pretty sure Neil will be the next one to join my harem (and I hope to write a fic about each one of them, if I haven't done so already lol).

Pairing wise I'll read almost anything, as long as it's well written, that is.
