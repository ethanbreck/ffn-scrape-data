I'm mostly a stalker with a very vivid imagination. In well written stories, I tend to lose myself and it starts to live in my mind. I have a constant whirlwind of stories, dreams and fantasies swirling around in my mind at any given time, but unfortunately for me, I have neither the talent nor the patience to actually write them down in a way that can be appreciated by others. Therefore, I have the utmost respect for all of you writers out there. I know how HARD it is. That's why this account is mainly to keep tabs of favourite stories of mine, instead of me publishing stuff.

I study Japanese at Leiden University, the Netherlands and am 19 years old. Favourite genres (currently): Lord of the Rings (YES, never ceases to stop being awesome), Harry Potter, Naruto, X-men. I also really like crossovers between the above categories and I love the 'girl falls into ME kind of story' I really dislike Mary Sues, although I can actually read such stories with a rather morbid fascination. Tee hee. It amazes even me somtimes. So, if you know of any good ones, don't hesitate to let me know!

Also, I'm artist. If you want me to draw a picture of a character of yours, send me a message stating why, along with a description and reference pics, if you have any and I'll see what I can do. _ I'm very busy though, so I can't guarantee anything.

So yeah, I'm always up for a chat, sofeel free to send me a message or (especially if I don't respond) an email.

Oh, and the story authored was written by me when I was 12/13-ish . Naturally, at the time I had no idea what the hell a Mary Sue was. If you do decide to read it, please don't take it seriously, it merely amuses me to look back at it. ;D
