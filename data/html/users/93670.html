Author has written 25 stories for Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Forgotten Realms, and Pirates of the Caribbean.

Where has the time gone? Yes, I am still alive and have the use of both hands. I am hanging my head in shame as I have still not completed any of my incomplete stories. My hubby and I have spent the last two years working toward retiring early in Mexico and are now here. My sons (yes, both of them) keep reminding me that it is evil to just leave the stories hang. I need, in their opinion, to poop or get off the pot (finish or pull the stories), so starting Monday I will dedicate a specific amount of time to writing and see if I can't let these characters get on with their lives. Thanks to everyone who still looks in on my world. Hope you are all doing well. K
