Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and Stargate: SG-1.

Hey everybody my name is Ember. How's it hanging? I love Harry Potter and Lord Of the Rings. I've read both series several times and OF COURSE I've seen the movies. So anyway here's a list of fics I'm working on and some I'd like to start.

The Power- I've gotten kind of hung up on this fic and I think the problem is that, like most of my stories, NOBODY EVER REVIEWS! So anyway, it's about a half Valar/half Elf girl that has to lead the Fellowship to safety.

The Meca- It's about a Hunter (to find out what that is read it...) who attends Hogwarts and is called back into "action" to save her native world. And of course as you know, it's a Snape/OFC fic. I don't plan on putting up another chapter until I get at least ONE review. Pretty please to any takers...

Andromeda- This story is my baby. Yeah... Andromeda is an Evlon and, by circumstance a Venamia as well. She and her Animungo, Tal, are trying to stop the vampyre uprising that is overtaking both the Muggle and Wizarding worlds. Unlike the Muggles, the magical world is very aware of the threat to their survival and asks for help, and who should answer but our two ORIGINAL main characters. Confused about all those capitolized and seemingly important words? Well, all the lore behind them will eventually be in the story. So read to find out. :) Not a story for kiddies, as there will also be some violence.

Children of the Gods- This is a crossover fanfic between the tv show Stargate: SG-1 and the book Shade's Children by Garth Nix. If you haven't read Shade's Children, don't worry about it, I didn't really use any of the characters from Shade's Children except Shade himself. It's also a definite AU, so you can read it and learn about their world with SG-1... just treat it like a new world they're traveling to that you've never seen before. : )

I would like to write a Sirius/OFC fic, but I need an idea to get me started. If you hadn't noticed, I'm kind of hung up on strong female character types. If you have any ideas or suggestions, feel free to e-mail me, and I'll probably try to write it. With that having been said, I hope you truly enjoy my stories, as that is the idea, and look forward to getting feedback.

Kisses,

Ember
