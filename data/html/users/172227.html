Profile last updated: 2-11-15

I love to read. Mostly I read fantasy or science fiction books. Some of my favorites books and book series are Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Dragonriders of Pern, Chronicles of Narnia, Valdemar, and Darkover. Some of my favorite authors are Anne McCaffrey, Tamora Pierce, J.R.R. Tolkien, Marion Zimmer Bradley, Mercedes Lackey, and Mary Stewart.

I also love anime and manga, though I feel like I haven't seen or read very many. My favorite anime are Sailor Moon, Sailor Moon Crystal, Samurai X and Rurouni Kenshin, Naruto, Fruits Basket, Str.A.In., and Baccano. Favorite manga includes Sailor Moon, Fruits Basket, Rurouni Kenshin, Negima, Kaze Hikaru, Library Wars, Cardcaptor Sakura, Ouran High School Host Club, and anything I've read by Arina Tanemura. I also enjoy many of Shojo Beat's other manga.
