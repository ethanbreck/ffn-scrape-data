Hello and welcome! Not sure why you're here, as I've only posted the one story, and that was YEARS ago. Over 10 years ago...which is really weird to think about.

Anyways, "The Takeover" is a quick little story I wrote YEARS AGO based on a role-play session that occurred between myself and members of the guild I was in at the time (the guild-leader (King), and several other guild-mates). I was only there for the part that I was immediate involved in, but I talked with the others about what happened during their parts, and was able to piece it all together. The guild-master approved of what I wrote, so I suppose I did a fairly good job getting the point across. It was going to be an ongoing rp, but, as you know, things happen, so this is as far as it has gotten. If anyone wants to adopt it, I'm fine with it, just let me know so I can read it!!

None of the names have been changed, though most of the participants aren't playing WoW anymore. I still am, though, on the Moon Guard server...and I know what you're thinking, and, no, I don't go to Goldshire at all :D

If you ever pay a visit to the Moon Guard server, feel free to say hi! I still go by Luneia, as she's my main, and by Adyshia, who's my fail tank. I participate in a nightly RP event that happens at 9pm Server Time at the Lion's Rest called the Gnightly Gnews at Gnine, so you can definitely find me there.

Cheers!!
