Four words: I like to write. A few more words: I tend to get extreme Writer's Block when I'm half way through one of my best ideas, thus causing there to be no story and lots of angry readers. But have no fear, I WILL finish those stories... It's just a matter of when...

January 5, 2003-

Under certain circumstances, I have been unable to complete my unfinished fanfiction. Of course, since 'Midnight Fox' has been such a big hit, and because I've only got a bit more before it's done, I've decided to complete it when I get the chance and then post all of it at the same time to make up for my absence.

Very sorry to any fans of 'Pride and Love.' I've suddenly developed a great dislike for AU fics and 'Pride and Love' falls in that category. I won't be finishing this particular fic and can only ask for the readers' forgiveness of my sudden decision.

Aside from that, when I do begin writing again, they will most likely be original fiction because I've lost interest in Digimon and many other animes.

Once again, my sincere apologies to the fans.
