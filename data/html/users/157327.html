It sort of hurt me to erase what I had previously written here...eight freakin years ago!

...Hi. My name is Carolanne. And...I'm old now. Honestly, I have not been on fanfiction.net for almost six years. It was my search for L.J. Smith 'The Secret Circle' fanfiction that brought me back here. And then...curiosity...because at one point in my life I wrote a lot of fanfic. And I read a lot of fanfic. Being back on here brings me back ten years. I have grown up a lot and I am not even sure that I am capable of writing fanfiction anymore. But...I did. I wrote Chapter 8 (for the Dating Game) as of 2/22/2010. ...Heh...Yeah...what has it been...six or seven years since I updated THAT one? For those who have e-mailed me over the years about 'The Dusty Magical Closet' I DID get those e-mails. Life...just got in the way. But I have always wanted to continue it - and the sequels ARE actually finished and in a notebook somewhere. But I'm sure as I wrote them many years ago they suck. So I want to rewrite them. So let us pretend I never wrote the sequels. After I reread the Dating Game tonight I figured it was time to stop leaving things unfinished. Even if I cannot deliver the perfect product in the end...at least I finished it. So that is my plan. I want to finish 'The Dating Game' and the sequels of 'The Dusty Magical Closet' series. For those out there who still think of my stories...thank you. That honestly shocks me. But you are the reason I came back to finish.

UPDATE:

1/15/11

Currently in the middle of writing Chapter 17 of "The Dating Game" - I am very pleased with where the plot is going - but I'm freaking out due to my crap writing! Lol. I felt so confident with my last chapter - but now all of my writing seems to lack any eloquence. Time for a BETA I think...

-Carolanne
