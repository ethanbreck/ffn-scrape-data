Hello everyone,

I'm a 18-year-old girl from Germany and a huge Harry Potter fan! I think the HP books are the best! My favourite characters are Harry Potter, Remus Lupin and Severus Snape. (That last one snuck up on me!)

So far, I have uploaded two stories:

1. Harry Potter and the Unexpected Powers (Status: In Progress!)

After two terrible weeks at the Dursleys', someone comes to rescue Harry and takes him to Hogwarts. But Harry is not in for relaxing summer holidays: A strange, bodiless being haunts his steps- and only Harry's. Who is this being, and why is he after our favourite Gryffindor? To add to the complications, Voldemort is on the rise and Harry is on the top of his to-kill list. The Dark Lord won't rest until he has got what he wants most of all... the life of one Boy-Who-Lived. Between this, Quidditch, the upcoming threat of the Ordinary Wizarding Levels, new friends and strange prophecies, Harry is in for an exciting school year! This story will continue throughout Harry's fifth year and will eventually lead up to the final battle between the light and the dark forces in the magical world!

2. Unicorn Child (Status: In Progress!)

This story is an Alternate Universe. Harry didn't defeat Voldemort at the age of one, though his parents were killed in the attack. Harry has to live with his horrible relatives, the Dursleys, for three years. But Voldemort has not forgotten little Harry, and so, when Harry is just four years old, he attacks again. This time Harry defeats the Dark Lord, but the Dursleys die and Harry is left alone and scared and runs away into the nearby forest. There, he meets a group of unicorns who accept the little boy into their 'family'. Will Harry ever learn to trust humans and return to them again? Or will the magical world search in vain for their little hero? Read and find out!

I hope that you like my stories! Since I'm neither British nor American or in any other way a native speaker, please excuse any mistakes you may find.
