Time for a change in the bio, eh? It's a bit outdated.

I love to write, read, draw, breathe Harry Potter. My room is like a friggin' museum...all those unopened boxes of HP dolls, and I have an entire half-shelf devoted to magazines/newspapers/whatever that had the words "Harry Potter" used. Including ads in the newspapers for the movie/book. Every one. EXCEPT THE STUPID SECOND ENTERTAINMENT WEEKLY TO FEATURE HARRY! I'm SO MAD I don't have that. Grr...

I'm a shipper. That may be sad, but I am. And I sail the Red, Orange, Purple and Green ships. For those of you sad, sad souls who do not sail, I suggest that you GET. WITH. THE. PROGRAM... And the ships I sail are...Ron/Luna (Red Ship), Harry/Hermione (Orange Ship), Draco/Ginny (Purple Ship) and Lily/James (Green Ship), though I won't say no to anything with Snape in it. Why?

Because I am a DIE HARD Snape fan...GET IT? If you don't, you're a HUGE loser.

I have seen nearly every Alan Rickman movie, and the great part, is that he just...gets...freakier with every movie. Seen Rasputin? You'll see what I mean. And he won THREE FRIGGIN' AWARDS! "How can he?" is all I can ask.

I also love Lord of the Rings. New obsession...well, sort of. I love, love, love David Wenham. He's Faramir. And looks SO MUCH like Alan Rickman. I think it's the beard. A Hans Fucking Gruber look.

And side note: All time favourite Alan Rickman Movie (means he looks the best): Sense and Sensibility. Ah...

I also love manga. In fact, I like to draw CLAMP style. If you don't know who CLAMP is, you are an even bigger loser.

Love, Kary.
PS: I have a quote! My senior quote!
"My life is somebody's idea of a cruel, sick joke."
