You've stumbled across my little page here. Hey! How's it going? I guess since you're here, I might as well tell you a little bit about myself.

Well, I'm Morgan. I'm eighteen, and I'm a freshman in college. My current majors are vocal music education and vocal preformance. I love being on stage, but still freak out when I have to use the phone! Weird, I know.

I have a bad habit of starting stories and never finishing them. Totally not cool. Like, in the extreme. So, if you've noticed, more recent stories from me are one shots, or continuations of fics I didn't finish. I have to be honest, my fear is that once people start liking a story, they'll turn on me if I don't continue in the same direction that they're picturing it going in. I'm getting over that though!

Please, feel free to drop me an email. I love making new friends!
