Author has written 20 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh.

Well, you all probably expected this considering I haven't posted an update in AGES, but I figured I may as well say something now: I'm no longer returning to FF.net. I'm rather tired of sifting through the excessive amounts of crap to get to a decent fic, or getting exceedingly retarded "feedback" (if it can even be called that) in my mail.

I've disabled anonymous comments, and I'm going to turn off all alerts except for the rare few fics I have my eye on for updates, so don't bother trying to contact me.

As for the YGO/HP crossover... well, I know I swore up and down that I would finish it, but that's just not happening. I'm not even all that in to Yuugiou anymore, and the way Harry Potter ended leaves much to be desired. I'm sorry to everyone that was looking forward to reading the rest of it, but... well, it happens.

I'll still be writing; the determined and those that know me will be able to find just where, but that's it for me here.

~Skyla Doragono~
