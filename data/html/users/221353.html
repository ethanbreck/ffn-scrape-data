About Me:

My hobbies include writing, reading, playing WoW, and watching tv shows and movies with my friends.

I have too many favourite books to even try and list them all, but recent favourites include: the Demon Child series by Jennifer Fallon, Patrick Rothfuss's Kingkiller Chronicles and Dresden Files. Other long-time favourite authors include Robin Hobb, Katharine Kerr, Tamora Pierce and Robert Jordan (cannot give Brandon Sanderson enough praise for the continued quality of the series since he took over).

Harry Potter is a no-brainer though, like many, I was a little disappointed by the way the series ended.

My favourite TV shows are, in no particular order: Castle, the Mentalist, Buffy, Bones and Perceptions.

I've been posting stories here for many years now, though my focus has moved from fanfiction to original fiction. When reading any of my completed works (ie anything besides Ties of Blood), please be aware of the published and updated dates - a lot of things were revealed in later books or interviews that were not known at the time these stories were written, and I have no interest in going back and revising them to suit.

9 June 2013

I continue to write in the original fiction realms, and anyone looking to connect with me these days is best off looking under 'wolfmoon1' at the NaNoWriMo forums, which also includes excerpts from my original works. I am actively looking for some beta readers to give some constructive criticism of those works.

As yet, there have been no offers to take over Ties of Blood - if that ever changes, be assured that I will post a new chapter advising of the change, and will also post about it in my profile.
