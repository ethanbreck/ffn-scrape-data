Two women walk out in front of you. The woman on your right is dressed simply enough, with normal looking jeans and a black shirt. She is of medium height, with brown hair streaked with auburn and brown eyes. The woman on your left is dressed much more differently. She is dressed in flowing black pants and a red tank top. On her belt are two daggers; both are intrecetly detailed, although the hilt of the dagger on her right holds an Alexandrite stone, while the other holds a Moonstone. Her black and white toned hair is braided loosely down her back and her stormy blue grey eyes stare entently ahead. The woman to your right speaks.

Well, is has been some time since I was last here. My name is Liz, or London to some of my friends. On ffn, I am known as FawesnFlame and Moony. And this she points to the woman next to her is Aislin, my muse and my character I RP with friends.

I am a writer of many things, though they mainly consist of writing fantasy stories. At least that's the case as of late. I am mainly a writer of Harry Potter fanfiction, and am an avid Harmonian. Most of my stories are either angst ridden, fluffy, or funny. At least that's all I've been writing when I do actually drabble in fanfiction.

I am becoming an avid reader of Hellsing fanfiction, although my friend Max doesn't seem to keen on having me read that kind of stuff. He'll just have to live with it now won't he?

I am horrible at posting stories, unless I feel really good about how it turned out. I am mainly a reader.

To all my previous readers, I swear that I will post a new one shot or another chapter of Reflections at some point. I swear. I've got another chapter of it in the works.

If you wish to talk to me, feel free to drop me and IM, an email, or a comment.

9.18.07
I am letting everyone know who has been following my works that I will no longer be writing HP fanfiction. I have been working on my novel and even now, it's been hell to write. Nothing has been coming to me... possibly due to the fact that I've begin my Senior year of college, so that's taking a lot out of me. I've also been offered the most amazing job: working with horses and teaching lessons. So that has kind of put a damper on my writing as well. I still continue to try and read everything, but I don't have time to review. DO know that I love most, if not all of what I read.

I thank you all for the criticism, and even flames, over the past 5 years here. It has helped my writing in unimaginable ways, even with my essay writings. Thank you to one and all and I wish you the best on your endeavors in life.

Sincerely,
