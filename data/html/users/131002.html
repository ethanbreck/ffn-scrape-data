Hey guys. Nice of you to drop by, enjoy your stay. Read this profile and hopefully, it'll relieve you of the stresses you may face in everyday living (or add to it, I dunno).

I've been sporadically writing fanfics for almost SIX years now. Good Lord, I've never realized how long it's been since my first fanfic, a story set in the Sakura Wars universe. Can you believe that? It's sorta amazing, but it's disappointing since out of the ten stories I've published here in Fanfiction.net, only six stories are considered complete. Two are in limbo, and the other two are still in progress.

Still, inspiration and ideas come every once in a while, and once I determine that there's a story to write, there's no stopping the flow...unless something vile like a writer's block comes along. I'll probably keep writing fanfiction for Lord knows how long, but it's fun, and a great time-killer until the next big game or anime.
