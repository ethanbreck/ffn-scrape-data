Author has written 1 story for Lord of the Rings.

You know, I was looking at this thing the other day, and I saw how completely boring it was to read. I actually can't stand writing these things...

I'm in college, and am having serious wafflings over what I want to do with the rest of my life. Biology? Journalism? Culinary Arts? Woe!!

Both of my Harry Potter stories were terribly cliché, so they were removed. Permanently. ...I should've been able to write something so much better. I'm ashamed, actually. I am currently working on a Lord of the Rings fic, but that's turning out highly overdone as well. You may read it if you dare.

Right now, I love anything to do with science, and have a weakness for musicals.

I've also recently discovered House and Stargate Atlantis. They both grow on you, like a fantabulous symbiotic fungus.

And Swing Kids.

If anyone would like a beta-reader for the following fandoms, I'd be glad to help!

Just send me an email or leave a comment on my blog, which is what you get when you click on the 'homepage' link above. :)
