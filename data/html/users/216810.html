A fan asked me earlier why I write. It was a good question, with a simple answer: I write the story I want to read. It's not about pleasing masses when it gets that way my writing struggles and I go blank. It's about pleasing me. It's flawed. It's sometimes organic. It grows as I grow. It's not perfect.

But what story is? Life is imperfect. Writing is imperfect. So I write what I love. That's all any writer can do. You'll never hear me refer to myself as an author. An author creates their own universe. They make their own world. I? I just play in someone else's. I'm a writer.

I know where my flaws are as a writer. I'm aware. I don't need it hammered at me. I don't even consider them flaws. I think of them as quirks. Nobody writes perfectly every time. Again, if you can tell it better, do it. I encourage you. I support you. A good story needs a pen to craft it. I will give you honest feedback and help you grow. Please do the same for me. I encourage you. But do it constructively, to help me, not to tear me down.

For my unabridged works, go to Archive of Our Own and look up The Lady Frost pen name on there

Demonleon3D is a good friend of mine. Check out his fabulous art on DeviantArt. SamBKS (we're far distance companions in cyberspace), Lonewolf117 and YegiHCH and CyberBrian360 have made beautiful art for me as well or allowed me to use theirs. You will not be disappointed you stopped in to look. Frank3nstein fixes my stuff for me when old fashion paint fails. And I often catch myself cruising around SKstalker and MistFighter lately. If you want your heartstrings tugged try Suffertears or Taitiii.

In fair warning, I'm what one might call a fan girl. I'm a huge devotee to a series that's pretty dead (or undead) save for the die hard fans that still hold out hope for a rebirth. I'm a Kennedy fan girl at heart. My 20 year love/hate with him continues well after the rest have seen the backside of his perfect hair fade out into the distance. I've been writing for him for so long, I feel like he's probably fused himself to my creative soul in a way that makes him inextricable. That being said - I enjoy all versions of him. I will never flame you, fault you, or feel the need to tell you your interpretation of him is wrong. I love reading him as much as writing him. Need a buddy to kick ideas off of? Toss that idea at me and let's see what we can cook up.

Ok. I'm going to make one remark, only, on this subject:

Guest Reviews: If you leave them challenging me or calling me names, flaming my work, or generally throwing shade of any kind...you won't get an answer. I will happily debate you all day long if you have an active account and you review my work with a tone of constructive criticism. If you think putting: What a piece of shit! Counts as constructive criticism, you would be sadly mistaken. You're entitled to your opinion. But not to my time spent arguing with you about sheer hateful slander. It won't happen. But feel free to waste your time putting it up there. By all means, go for it.

A fair warning to what I write - it's not the dark and tragic world that brings some readers to the table. I don't like that world. That world is depressing. It makes me feel bad to read it let alone write it. I don't want that world. So I write the world I want to see. Want to see it? Read my world and come join me. It's uneven. Sometimes it's unsteady. Sometimes it's unbelievable. It's always, always, always unequaled.
