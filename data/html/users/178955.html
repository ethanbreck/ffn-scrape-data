I'd like to give a huge thank you to everyone who is still reading my work.

I so fondly remember writing these and I'm glad people out there are still enjoying them. It's actually been many years since I've written anything new, but I can still remember the days and nights of giggly fan-girl writing. Most of these stories are almost embarrassing to go back to as an adult now, I hope you'll excuse the mistakes and general obvious "youngness" to the writing lol.

Currently, the only copy of my fanfiction which still exist is here on ff.net (as far as I know). It has changed very much since I joined and I hadn't been paying much attention since I wasn't writing anything over the years. That's why my mature stories were edited to exclude the sexual scenes and haven't been changed.

If possible, I would like also to post the unedited versions that were supposed to be available. Sorry to those who have requested. It's been almost 20 years since these were written, so much has happened. If anyone has versions of these stories saved after all these years, especially the unedited ones, I would be extremely happy if you would be so kind to pass them on.

For now, I humbly offer up what is left here and thank you for reading.

With hugs

Aqua

PS - Since I've been lazy about fixing the spelling, if anyone would like to do so and send me the fixed chapters, I'll credit you with thanks!

PPS - Spellcheck didn't exist yet when I wrote many of these... Yes, I'm aging myself here, but each chapter was lovingly beta'ed by myself before I put them up which, unfortunately, doesn't fix my own mistakes. This is what I receive the most heat for so I hope you'll take this into consideration when you're reading.
