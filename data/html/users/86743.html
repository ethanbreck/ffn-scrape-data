To all my readers,

Or at any case, to all the people that have taken the time to read my stories, only for that I'm eternally grateful. At the age of 16 I started writting and publishing my work in this website, I know that it's wasnt much but it did help me in my learning process of writting that hasnt end yet. It will never end because the day that we stop learning something new everyday it will probably be the day we die.And now today, at the age of 19 I finish my journey. This jouney.

Im taking these lines to inform all the ppl that have follow my stories that due health problems I will no longer keep writting, nor continue my stories. For that, I'm very sorry. I know that I have left a enormous amount of cliffhangers and for that all I can is say that I am sorry.

However, my stories do not end where you last read. In the next couple of weeks I'll update a small summary of what I had in mind to end all the stories that I had written so far.

All that is left to say is THANK YOU, to all those who took the time to review (good and bad ones) since I learned a lot with both, for those who read and for those who waited patiently for almost two years... for those readers Im specially grateful. You ppl made my day!

See you around,

Sakura Angel.-
