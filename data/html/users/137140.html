Author has written 1 story for King of Fighters.

I know my stuff may be a bit weird for some of your tastes, but hey.

I'm a passionate Andy/Mai supporter and can't stand stories that pair Mai with someone else and/or kill Andy. And for the record, my favorite characters besides Andy and Mai are Terry, Blue Mary, Iori, Mature, Chizuru, Chris, and Shingo. Other pairings I like are Terry/Mary and Iori/Mature.

Another note: I've been a KoF fan since '95, but I really don't like where that and the Fatal Fury series have been going as of '99 ("Real Bout 2" in Fatal Fury's case). Therefore, as far as I and my stories are concerned, the KoF series ended with '98, Fatal Fury with its third installment (though I actually like "Real Bout" and "Real Bout Special").

I've also gotten into "Harry Potter", though, like KoF, it also has passed it's prime ("Order of the Phoenix" in HP's case). Thus, only the first four books "count" as far as I'm concerned. My pairings there are Harry/Ginny, Ron/Hermione, and Remus/Sirius, all of which I'm as passionate about as Andy/Mai.

I do still love the Andy/Mai pairing and characters, but I've mostly fallen out of the KoF/Fatal Fury fandom and am actually trying to move past being simply "that Andy/Mai fangirl". KoF isn't even really about the older characters, Andy and Mai included, anymore, which doesn't help. "Big Bucks and Then Some" will remain up no matter what, but don't expect any more KoF stories of any kind from me. A very different fighting game currently has my heart and got me interested in writing anything again after all these years: Super Smash Brothers.

I've been a long-time fan of Nintendo in general and have played the SSB series since the N64 and unlike HP and KoF I am pleased with its most recent offering (well, save for one character, but lucky for me that character is only optional DLC that I don't have to purchase, so no biggie). My pairings there, many of which include characters that aren't actually in any SSB games but are in the characters' series of origin or other games in which the character appeared, are: Mario/Peach, Luigi/Daisy, Wario/Mona, Link/Zelda, Samus/Captain Falcon, Ness/Paula, Marth/Caeda, Roy/Lilina, Ike/Soren, Female!Robin/Chrom, Male!Robin/Lucina, and Shulk/Fiora. No, for pairing purposes I don't consider Zelda and Sheik, Dr Mario and Mario, Link and Toon Link (or Young Link for that matter), or Samus and Zero Suit Samus separate characters.

And yes, I like 80s TV and 80s music- and the occasional police procedural. I do also like some Disney movies.
