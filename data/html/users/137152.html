Joined 12-09-01, id: 137152
Author has written 9 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Zoids, Harry Potter, Final Fantasy I-VI, and Naruto.

There's not much to do here except change the profile. Name's The B.A.T. As far as my likes, it's anime, manga, video games, books, writing, and food (though I'm quite the picky eater).

Favorite types of video games are RPGs and Fighters. I also have a place in my heart for RTS (real-time strategy), puzzles, and FPS (first-person shooter) games. Action-Adventure/Platform games are okay, but it's been a long while since anything has really caught my eye. That having been said, I love the Final Fantasy series, Suikoden series, Zelda series, Tekken 5, Naruto: Gekitou Ninja Taisen 3, Ico, and Halo series. GTA bores me, no matter how "shocking" they try to make it, because that's all it is: shock value for the sake of shock value. I don't care too much for most sports games, but I've been known to play PGA Tour; I'd actually rather be playing most sports in real life.

As far as anime goes, I'm currently fiending over One Piece and Yakitate Japan. I've sadly found that pirates and afro-sporting bakers do, in fact, beat out ninjas, but that's not to say that I dislike Naruto now. I still love it, although I fear Kishimoto as of present is going the way of Toriyama (story-wise, so I'm referring to the direction the manga's taking in its second part; I've given up on the anime, since the animators don't seem to understand/recall what it was that made the series so great). All the same, I can't abandon all-time favs like Shikamaru and Temari, who, surprisingly, make quite the couple. I'm also really enjoying Bleach, which shows no sign of letting up, as well as Tsubasa Chronicle and Monster. Basilisk is also really good; I mean, REALLY, if you can get past all the gore and stuff.Going back into anime past, I've come to love the Sekai no Senki/Monshou series and I treat it as an all-time fav. Love Hina was great for laughs, and there's a Chibified Guardian out there who would probably appreciate that I've been catching up on Oh! My Goddess, which I'm loving every bit as much as he does. GTO is also addictive, as is Cardcaptor Sakura. So, I suppose my favorite anime (period!) would have to be Sekai no Senki/Monshou; that being a definite, there are many things that really deserve to be up here, but I'm still second-guessing myself on them. I'm REALLY hoping Yakitate and One Piece will join it up there, as I've no doubt about how good they are and only hope they continue to get better.

Manga-wise, One Piece and Yakitate are dominant here as well, as is Bleach. Ichigo 100 is a drug for me here, and while I was waiting for new chapters, I checked out Lilim Kiss by the same author. Not bad, though a little short. I do hope to find more of Sekai, since most of it is either in novel form or manga form. I'm currently awaiting new chaps of Gekkoh (kiddies, stay away) and Boku no Futatsu no Tsubasa (My Two Wings). I'm thinking of starting on Death Note (and Suzuka, if and when I find it). I don't have the patience to read the manga versions of Fullmetal Alchemist or GTO. I'll get around to it eventually. Also, it may not be an actual manga, but Megatokyo is giving me the time of my life.

Book-wise, I don't get to enjoy very much since I'm currently in college. Sadly, I'm the type who can't read leisurely when not in leisure, so most of my reading is put on hiatus. I still find time for the Harry Potter series and for Orson Scott Card's books about the Battle School students, and ocasionally, class makes me run headlong into a decent book as well, so in those cases, my problem sort of takes care of itself. Since I love to write, I probably regret most my inability to read more. But hey, it would do no good for me to learn everything I can from previous writers, wouldn't it? I should make my own way in that regard, or else I'm just a carbon copy composite of my predecessors; well, that's how I feel, at least, and I am quite certain there are many who will disagree with me.

I'm a sucker for romance. In fanfiction, I like writing it, though I probably don't like reading it. In anime and manga, I can't get enough of it, because well... pairings make the world go round. In actual novels, I don't have a particular interest; I just want a good story, and more often than not, romance novels tend to, well... suck. A lot. Too much. So a good story is key, no matter what I read, and if I even see the slightest hint of a possible pairing (between people I actually like, of course), expect me to bite. I can't help it, you see.

For those of you who bothered to read this far without finding some reason to hate me, I have some more tedious information for ye. I'm a Muslim, and I'm quite proud of it (ducks all anti-terror missiles, stands, and nods). Quite. As in, don't expect me to change my religion any time this millenium. As a reader, I don't dislike shonen ai/yaoi and shoujo ai/yuri; just don't expect me to read it. More often than not, OOCness is the issue, and constant flooding of pages with OOCness is what gives the genres such a bad name, though the problem is mostly with yaoi (yuri is quite rare, and even rarer is the OOCness in it). Oh and yeah, the fact that I'm a straight guy also turns me off to boy-boy pairings. I don't believe there's a need to apologize, but because there are people out there who just want a reason to be offended by someone, I'll apologize anyway in advance. I want to write. I have no idea what "tangible" profession I want to pursue when I leave college. I'm... balding... I think. twitch It doesn't really matter, though, since my hair is usually covered; I didn't even really think about my hair much until I noticed my hairline was receding; I still shrug, though I must admit that for this to happen to someone who's barely 20, it sucks. Oh, yeah! I just turned twenty a couple months back. Good for me. I still can't drink. Not that I would though (refer back to "I'm a Muslim").

Favorite characters (from anything):

Jinto (Sekai no Senki/Monshou), Naoko (Golden Boy), Admiral Spoor (Sekai no Senki/Monshou), Bean (Ender Series), Luna Lovegood (Harry Potter series), Hermione Granger (Harry Potter series), Kentaro Oe (Golden Boy), Urumi (GTO), Shikamaru (Naruto), Temari (Naruto), Usopp (One Piece), Hitsugaya (Bleach), Chad (Bleach), Rangiku (Bleach), Hinamori (Bleach), Yoruichi (Bleach), Alai (Ender Series), Kai (Yakitate), Manager (Yakitate), Pierrot (Yakitate), Jim (Outlaw Star), Ed (Cowboy Bebop), Asuka (Evangelion), Natsuki (Mai HiME), Nishino (Ichigo 100), Toujou (Ichigo 100), Mutsumi (Love Hina), Tomoyo (Cardcaptor Sakura), Link (Zelda), Jacques (Suikoden III), Aila (Suikoden III), Jeanne (Suikoden series), Yamazaki (Cardcaptor Sakura).

That having been said, I hope you enjoy my works, which seem to have taken a hiatus for quite awhile. For those who are new here, and for those who have been so very patient with me, I shall point out what is still being worked on.

Works Still In Progress

Love Letters Unsent: Naturally, I'm far from done with this. Currently, the next chapter for this is being worked on, so please wait just a little longer.

Blow Them Away: What was meant to be my first "signature work" since "Two Birds, One Arrow." I'm finally starting to see where I want to take this piece, so I especially beg those who were awaiting a new chapter to hold on just a bit longer. I promise it will not be in vain.

Two Birds, One Arrow: Believe it or not, this one was close to completion ages ago, but since it suffered from what the above are suffering from, it REALLY has been awhile since I touched it. But I promised I would finish it, whether anyone still remembers it... or not. It's more juvenile in style than how I write now, so I'm kind of embarrassed about mentioning it here. Eventually, I will revise it, because I want it to be as good as possible, but that's going to take even longer than me updating, so don't hold your breath. Man, I'm horrible.

Works That May Come Out of Hibernation

The Dark Elements: After having finally seen the entire series in Japanese, I REALLY want to relaunch this story. I will need to reread what I wrote, but someday, I want to write a fic for the CCS series as good as the one I read ages ago (that, alas, was never completed... much like my current works, only it was infinitely better than anything I've yet written), Starlight, Starbright. You'll still find this fic on ff.net if you search hard enough, and I highly recommend it, if you are, of course, a CCS fan.

Hogwarts: A History- Yet another tale that I hoped would be epic. I need to revise the first two chapters. The thing about this is, I wrote it after having read book four. I drew many conclusions back then, but since then, two more books have come out. I'd like to continue this someday, but that depends on whether or not the story is salvageable.

Works That Are Forever Embedded in Obscurity

Bahamut's Legacy: Whatever was I thinking when I wrote this? What the HELL was I thinking? Not that it was bad, or anything, but I just know I was on a Final Fantasy high when I wrote it. Someday, maybe I will want to continue it, but I just look back and think... this is how I was only four years ago. Not that I've changed much... but, hopefully, you get my idea. I don't believe in deleting anything I write and submit, so it will stay here for the time being. If you get the urge to read it, go right ahead. Just... don't let me know, okay? -_-U

Well, there you have it, me in a nutshell. Signing off,

The B.A.T.
