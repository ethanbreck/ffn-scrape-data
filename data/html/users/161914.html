Hello hello, I have been writing fanfiction for years, I recently changed my username so if you see stories under this name that seem familiar it's probably because you read it under my old handle. I hope you enjoy my stories!

With all that said I would like to explain something. I want you all to know why I don't take anonymous reviews. I have experienced in the past (not on this site, but on other forums) that it is always the anonymous reviewers that make the most ugliest and snide remarks. They don't offer any helpful criticism, just hateful babble. Now I find that to be quite uncalled for and cowardly. There is something humbling about having to sign your name to a review, rather it be a pen name or not. I don't mind getting useless, non helpful drabble, but if you are really going to waste your time writing flames at least own up to them.

With much love,

-Atticus Hill
