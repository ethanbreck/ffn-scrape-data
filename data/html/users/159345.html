Hi! I'm a college student who spends her time doodling, writing, and, uh, listening to music. My likes range from books like Harry Potter, Twilight, LoTR, to mangas such as Naruto, Evangelion, and Death Note, to rooting for delusional pairings, and cherising random stuff like the smell of rain. I also tend to "fall in love" with fictional male characters that invariably end up dying, for some strange reason, save for a few fortunate exceptions. Oh well... Moving on. Wouldn't want to start ranting.

Pairings which I like:

H/Hr (my OTP)

R/Luna

Hr/D

Hr/SB (This one sort of grew on me. And I love it with a passion)

NON HP RELATED:

Edward/Bella

Asuka/ Shinji

Akane/ Ranma

Misty/ Ash (yeah, so I used to like Pokemon. Nnyeh.)

Sasuke/Sakura

Pairings which I dislike: in the potterverse, anything that involves Harry and it's not Harmony. NaruSaku, GTFO.

Characters I dislike: Ginny (Since the second book... Voldemort should have killed her and fed her to the Basilisk!), Ron (Since the fourth book...), Eclipse!Jacob, Light Yagami, and many others that are in no way connected to the abovementioned fandoms I skulk in.

Books: The Little Prince, Angels & Demons, Damian, Equus, Pride and Prejudice, Twilight, HP, LoTR, Speak, Wuthering Heights; and various picks from here and there. I proudly announce that I'm a bookworm... And I think it's the greatest thing!

All in all, I'll make it simple: I'm a die hard H/Hr fan, a heated D/Hr follower, and passionate Hr/S reader. I guess that I'm just as delusional as it gets. I might also add obsessive SasuSaku fan, and drooling Sasuke and L fangirl.

Well, I think that's all. I hope you like my stories!
