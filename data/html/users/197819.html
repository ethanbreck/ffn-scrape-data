Author has written 3 stories for Escaflowne, and Stargate: SG-1.

OK after four years I think that this is well past an update. I am a 24 year old university student, hence the rather long gaps between updates. Writing and art happen to be my passion in life and I do both to relax from my studies.
My favorite shows to watch are Stargate SG-1 (way sad at seeing them hit their final season), Firefly (so should have gone longer than the 14 episodes they got), CSI, and Criminal Minds.
My first story on here is an Escaflowne piece and rather crudely done at that. My second attempt was a Stargate SG-1 story it has been completed and is half way decent, although it had no beta. My third and in progress attempt is another SG-1 story and is a crossover with Dune. This piece is slowly coming along mainly due to a large writer's block and then school. I did have a rather good beta for the first half although I have since lost touch with her and the second half is done with the best of my ability and the rather doubtful attempts with a 5 year old computer and a sketchy old spell check.
