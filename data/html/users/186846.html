I WRITE MARY SUE STORIES! LEAVE MY ARCHIVE NOW IF YOU WISH TO FLAME ME!

Greetings and salutations! My name is Lisa, I'm 33 years old, and I enjoy writing very much (I wouldn't be here if I didn't). I'm warning you right now: My stories are almost entirely Mary Sue. If you're one of those people who look down on those, then please don't read my stories. Although I'd encourage you to read at least one.

Back to business. Let's see. I'm an accounts payable clerk at a scrap metal company in Jersey. I'm married and a mother of the most beautiful girl in the universe. I also love science fiction and fantasy.

The one character you will see is my story alter ego, Alien Lisa (original name, eh?). She comes from the planet Alnilam (which is where a few of my stories are set). Yes, she's powerful, but no, the other characters don't look up to her (unless you count the unicorns) and no, she doesn't have all the answers. Yes, I make the people I have crushes on fall in love with her and no, I don't care what other people think. I write what I write. Again, if certain readers don't like it, they don't have to read my stories.

Shameless plugs: Go check out the links in my "Favorite Stories" and "Favorite Authors" sections.

That's it. Enjoy my stories!

Updates: 12/4/16: One small tweak to "The One" because I decided having Jamie and AL separated by the Time Lords didn't sit well with me.

Flaming Trails is my Inspirational Fairy. XD
