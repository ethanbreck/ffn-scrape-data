Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.
I got interested in Harry Potter when my youngest son, an autistic child, became very interested in the saga. I read the books myself, and here I am.

This is the third universe that I've written for. I started writing Dragonriders of Pern FanFic first, then (unsuccessfully) started a universe of my own, and now I'm writing Harry Potter FanFic.

I'm a retired programmer (due to a stroke I suffered back in the October 2004) and I live in Lansing, MI. I enjoy computers, music, watching auto racing, and being with Jon-Jon, my youngest.

I would currently like to thank the following people for my start.

My wife, Deborah, who helped me get started with writing. We've talked recently of starting writing Pern FanFic again.

My friends in the Pern FanFic community, who have been extremely supportive of my efforts.

A growing list of Authors here at FanFiction.Net who have inspired me to no end.

Finally, high praise to J.K.Rowling, who's complex and fascinating universe is an inspiration to all authors.
