Hi. I'm Elizabeth.
I, uh, I don't really like writing about myself, so pardon any bad writings upon this tablet. Like that there.

I'm almost sixteen, I like ska (go Less Than Jake! Whoohoo!), which no one else in my family does, I like to pretend I'm a British schoolgirl, and I like Harry Potter. The books, not particularly Harry himself. He's too much of a goody-goody. I like Hermione though, and definitely Draco. He just sounds super sexy, to be like my mother about Donny Osmond. And that's about it. That's why I don't particularly like any Harry slashes. But there is nothing wrong with a good Draco/Ron, or those other assorted characters.

I'm a budding writer, so feel free to point out anything that I could change to improve the story.

Also, I only work on one story at a time, because otherwise all the plotlines get messed and fused in my head, and we would all wind up with random plot lines. "Oh, Peter! What is that blondhaired elven archer with a zigzag scar on his forehead doing here? We're supposed to be building a machine from an architectural plan aliens sent us on radio waves!" Wendy Darling cried.

Can't you just imagine a story like that? Odd.

I have multiple nicknames, included among them are the Flourescent Muskrat (and yes, I do know fluorescent is misspelled. That is just the way that I like to have it spelled.) and the Queen of Norwegia. To all you Norwegians out there reading this, bow to me. Please? I just think that inhabitants of Norway should be Norwayan or Norwain or something like that. Or Narway should change it's name to Norwegia.

And if any of you go to EPCOT, go to Norway and ride the Norwegian ride! Whoohoo! ...okay.

Oh, and the homepage is my deadjournal. ...okay.
