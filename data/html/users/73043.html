Well, I've begun to write again, but only for my X-men Evo fic, Best of You. I'm looking at adding new stories eventually, and likely at some point going back to my HP stories too.

I also have posted some stories over on AdultfanfictionDOTnet under the same username if anyone is interested in reading them. I love reviews, so please leave me one if you feel I deserve it!

As far as Best of You goes, I'm debating some things there. The story has changed tons since I first started it from the original plot, and I'm still trying to decide if I'm happy about that. So for anyone who has a thought on this, I'm debating between thing following:

1) Rewrite Best of You as it was meant to be (which would be time consuming and erase a story some seem to enjoy).

2) Write a new story that goes the way Best of You was going to be, which was a Rogue-centric Epic with no set pairings and keep the current Best of You to work on as well.

I'm leaning towards number 2, but I'm still trying to decide. If you have thoughts, you can leave them in a review for Best of You or PM me. Thanks!

My favorite Fan-doms are Harry Potter, X-men (any universe), and Glee, tho I never wrote anything for Glee. LOL. Music is a HUGE part of my life and stories will likely reflect that, just as they will be affected by the fact that I am now a wife and mother. Tho the latter mostly will affect how often I can update more than anything else!

As a note for Harry Potter fics, I am a HUGE Snape fan as well. I always have been, and him being played by Alan Rickman in the movies didn't hurt! God that voice...*shiver* Therefore you will NEVER see him be "evil" in my stories. Its rather the same with Ron. I don't want him with Hermione, but Harry NEEDS a male friend besides just Neville or Draco in fan fics. I will READ Ron bashing, but I won't do it myself. There is a reason JKR gave him to us to play with (however unknowingly at the time), and I intend to explore his flaws and strengths.

I am a shipper, of course. Prefered pairings?
