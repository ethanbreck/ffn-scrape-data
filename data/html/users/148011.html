Hello and Welcome!

My name is Jennifer, and am best known for writing fan fiction for the daytime show General Hospital.

Lately I've been going in different directions, but also sticking with my roots and finishing all the fan fiction I've started or left behind.

I've gone through different name changes and the reason for the name change, I wanted to do something different. The character of Lianna Keru was brought to life when I was about fifteen years old. A year before I wrote my first story.

My writing style has changed since then, as you can see from my writings.

I am now writing for other shows like, Supernatural, Stargate Atlantis, and now Doctor Who, and maybe even Torchwood.

The stories down below are those of WIP (Works In Progress), however some are completed. I am also working on completing two colloboration works with my pal Michelle (Shellbell822) and you can find the two stories under the name JenShell

Enjoy what is here, there is so much more to come!
