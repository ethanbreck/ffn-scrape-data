A Few notes on the stories:

ALL OUR STORIES CAN BE READ INDEPENDENTLY

Due to the publication of 'Order Of The Phoenix', our stories no longer fit in with JK's events. We will however endeavour to finish them. I will not mention how they differ as this may spoil things for those of you yet to finish reading.
All events in our stories follow those of the first four HP Books.

'Before They Were Famous' (BTWF) comes before all of them. It's all about when the marauders etc. were at school. It's also the start of Severus and Florence's relationship.

'Wrongs Darker than Death or Night' comes next. This is the sequal to BTWF, although you don't need to have read that.

'Over the Moon' probably starts around chapter 19 of WDTDON, but u can read that on its own too!

'The Wrong Kind of Man' starts around chapter 23 of WDTDON and ties in quite tightly, but again you can read them separately.

'After they were famous' is new - it starts off being mainly about Florence & Severus but will grow to involve Remus and April as well as Branson Legget and his trials in love!

We love to think people are reading and appreciate ALL comments - so please leave a review if you stop by to read!!

You can now follow Florence on Twitter: http://twitter.com/FlorenceFindal

We would like to find a fan artist to draw a pic of Florence for her twitter profile - get in touch if you can help - we will pay you for your pencil lead. Thanks!
