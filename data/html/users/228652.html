I never know what I'm supposed to put in these profile thingies.

Let's see, I'm in my forties, and a single female from Ontario, Canada. I was working full time up until about 2009 but then I ran into the collapsing economy and since then I've been trying to find another full-time job rather unsuccessfully. So I've been working both long and short-term temp jobs which is why my story posting has been rather hit and miss. I have since decided to go back to school full time this coming September 2011, which should hopefully make it a little easier to get back to writing and posting stories (I make no promises though) although the bunnies do seem to be reappearing now.

Also as some of you know, I did have a bit of a falling out with my writing partner Isawien a while back. That has since been mended but we do have a slight issue where her computer/internet is concerned so that will make writing with her a little difficult. But it does mean I can re-post some of our old stories again, which I am now in the process of doing. I am currently re-posting Can True Love Really Be Timeless, which is in the process of being finished off. I also have plans to re-post Can A Mistake Ever Truly Make Right.

I am also going to repost all of our other Harry Potter Stories, including The Seelie; The Nobles; The Dark Four Series (Heir of Slytherin, Changing the Past, Not Really A Weasley, Remembering the Past, Past Remembered, End of the Beginning); To Love A Malfoy; To Love A Malfoy - Alternate Version; The Darkness Within and As The Queen Decrees.

Along with our Harry Potter stories, I plan to post all of our CSI and CSI/NCIS crossover stories. These stories include Night Stalker, Dream Stalker; A Return to Faith, A Return to Avalon; Returning Home; For Now, Forever and When the President Calls.

I have a request to make of our CSI readers. There were a number of you who requested a copy of our stories before they were removed from the site. If any of you out there have a copy of Night Stalker, Dream Stalker, could you please contact either me or Isawien via PM here on the site. Between the time the stories were removed from the site and now, my old computer died a rather painful death and it seems to have corrupted the end of my copy of the story and it zapped the final chapter off the story. I am missing the epilogue and I would really appreciate it if someone could contact me and let me know if you have a copy of the story so that I can get a copy of the last chapter off of you so that I can get it up on the site!

Thanks!

Cindy a.k.a. Jamie a.k.a. Jyn
