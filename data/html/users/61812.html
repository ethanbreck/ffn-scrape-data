Hey,

I'm a twenty-something Canadian librarian. I've been a member of ffnet since before some of the newer members have been alive, and have been reading fanfiction since I was 10-ish. I took a crack at a fanfic right around then. It wasn't good (actually it was terrible) and is gone now. Forever, hopefully. I have an overly-complicated method of sorting my favourite fics, and I dislike reading WIPs generally, so I tend to only put finished fics into my favourites list.

This account exists for the sole purpose of leaving signed reviews. Since I probably read over a thousand pages a week between the texts I have to read for class (yay Master's degree!), the original fiction I follow, and fanfics, if you've found this page, your story is really very special, and worth reading.

Happy writing!
