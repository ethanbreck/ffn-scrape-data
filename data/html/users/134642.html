My full pen name is Lord Zeddicus Zu'l Raul. I got the name from Terry Goodkind's book series "The Sword Of Truth", and I got his powers from "Slayers".

Some of the anime shows I like watching are Digimon, Dragon Ball Z, Cardcaptors, Gundam Wing, and Yugi-oh. Some of my muses are TK, Renamon, Sakura, Keroberos, Duo, and Yugi. I get my Digimon info from Meg-chan's Digimon Sakai.

I have two fics in progress:
1) a detailed description of my fan creations
2) a 3 story long saga crossover between Digimon 02 and Tamers

Praise is blessed, help is welcomed, and flames are ignored, or, if deemed worthy, posted here and ridiculed.

If you wish to use my fan-creations or MST my stories, please ask me first. People who can do this will be posted here. Ultimanium has been given permission to MST my works.
