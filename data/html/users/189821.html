In Depth:

I like to think of myself as a chipper happy person. I can be outgoing, strong willed and nutty all in one. I am considered a high-context person according to my speech book for my class, which means I'm all touchy feely. Haha... I am also, in my eyes, a decent artist and heavy influenced by the style of Anime and Manga. I am also an avid gamer. That about sums it up.

Welcome to 2015 everyone!

So wow, it's been a while since I've blown the dust off my profile page. Some of your have seen my recent activity I'm sure. Anywho, let me start of this New Year by giving a humble and happy "Thank You!" to all the people who have ever reviewed, favorited, and followed my stories. I do truly appreciate all the words of praise, critizisms, help, and the general feeling of enjoyment I've received from all of you. While I do mostly write these stories for my own amusement there is always that little tickle in the back of my mind that says 'Maybe someone else out there will enjoy this with me too!'.

Moving on. Now that a new year has started and everyone's made their own resolutions about what they want to do or be or have done, I am going to share with you one of mine. One of my resolutions for this year was to finish what I start. Sound advice for anything, anyone and anytime, right? Well I'm going a little more out there with this. Basically what I'm saying is that I am going to work hard to finish a lot of the stories I've already started that many of you have been waiting months (if not years) to update. This will require a lot of work, so I'm going to start small for now. I'll be doing this by doing batches of three. Now, for this first batch, it'll technically be like four, since right now I'm in a zone and geared for working on my newest addition. I'll also be giving myself a month of dedication to each chapter for each story. This is because I need to go back and re-read my own work; restructure my thought process and figure out again exactly where I was going with each story. Some will be more easily to remap than others. With that said, I'm going to put up a list of the stories I plan to work on first, and in which month I aim to complete said story's next chapter.

*Please note as of right now my Bob's Burger's related stories take priority and will update/publish sooner than previous stories. Gotta follow my muse and my heart.
