Basics

30 Female Massachusetts, USA

I am still alive, I swear. I graduated with a BA in Equine Business Management and worked myself literally into the ground for an unappreciative human being. That job left me no time, energy or desire to pursue any of my creative outlets-- such as writing. I was in a very toxic lifestyle at that time-- although I loved working with horses and am going to continue a career in that industry, I have since retired from horses for the time being.

I'm working for a company that sells equine products as an inventory control person and have picked up helping a local woman with her few horses. I am slowly getting back into writing, but the fandoms have changed. I'm currently working on:

Dark Horse: Harry Potter/Full Metal Alchemist Cross-Over-- not your average 'edward goes to hogwarts' story. This is a fic featuring Sirius and Edward working together to fight Voldemort along with the Ministry. Much angst for Sirius... much discovery for Edward. Good times.

GotG Fic- Rocket centric because I absolutely love him

Avengers- Clint- centric, without the wife and the farm and the kids. Honestly, wtf Marvel?

BBC Musketeers- Aramis-centric

Naturally, these are all being slowly worked on between living my newfound life and freedom, so please don't hold your breath! It's safe to say, that the stories I have left unfinished are most likely not going to be finished-- as those ideas and fandom interests have fallen to the wayside.
