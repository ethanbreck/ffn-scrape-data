Man, my profile feels like an update, AGAIN! After three years of leaving this thing alone...

Hi, for anyone that's seen my profile. I'm Rexy and I've written fanfics since 2000, though I didn't really start coming until about a year or two after I started. I'm a University graduate from England and I have a strong passion for all things arts and stuff, whether it be doing fanart, fanwork or the occasional remix of a music track from various games and whatnot.

My character likings mainly go around the field of the adventure genre. Mostly my tastes relate to PlayStation based characters like Crash, Spyro, Jak, Ratchet, Sly Cooper, maybe Rayman as well (though he didn't start on the PS to be honest), but I can be able to adapt to just about anything that I can get my hands on (don't quote me on that!!).

That's possibly as much as I can write right now. I mainly hang out on the Sierra forums so much these days if you want to get in contact with me. Thanks for your time.
