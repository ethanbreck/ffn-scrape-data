If you don't know what i'm talking about, PLEASE go read anything by Tamora Pierce. NOW.

etc..?
I'm a cross-country runner for my school; my PR for the 5K is 20:30. I'm not really happy with my times, but.. oh well. I guess I should be happy that I'm on the varsity seven on a team of eighty girls, but.. yeah.. You know what? Can I live in Tortall?

Speaking of Tortall, I like to think that I would have chosen to disguise myself and train to be a knight, like Alanna. Just because at eleven I could not have seen myself going to the convent. Even now, {15} I would not enjoy being there. Even though my girly tendencies are increasing (mascara lust)? *laughs* I would LOVE to be a "lady knight." I enjoy being athletic, and I would hate to be all frilly twenty-five hours a day, even if I enjoy wearing plaid miniskirts and doing up my hair.

Right, anyway... I'm strange.
