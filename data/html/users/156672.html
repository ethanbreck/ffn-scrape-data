For those that have read my work, keep in mind that most of the stories on my profile I wrote when I was just getting out of 8th grade, and I think that the writing shows that. If you follow the stories in chronological order by my publish dates, you will see a progression in my writing corresponding to my growing skills throughout high school and early college.
