Author has written 5 stories for Ai no Kusabi, Gravitation, Diana Wynne Jones, and V for Vendetta.

Oh I just love these Biography things :p Anywho, I live in Upstate NY, working full time to pay off my college loans. However, I have been writing since I was little, and it's one of my favorite hobbies. I also started writing fanfic before I ever knew there was a term for it (I started my first back in 1991. A "Secret Garden" fanfic. Ah, memories! Sorry, it won't be seeing the light of day.). My other current loves include anime (mostly older, shoujo series like "Mask of Glass" and "Patarillo!", although I do watch and enjoy newer series as well. Give me anime, I'll watch it. ), reading, and cross-stitch. My favorite authors at the current time are Louise Cooper, Laurie R. King, and J.K. Rowling.

As you can see, there was a little lull between my last writing and the current. Life has been incredibly hectic in the interim.

Now.. if only you could pay the government back for your education in fanfic... ;)

03/36/07 - I am so, so sorry for making everybody worry! I did not intend to let this go for so long. Let's just say that a combination of burnout (I think trying to work on seven short stories and two novels at once will do that to you! sheepish grin) and fighting with my health, does not make for much in the way of writing. However, I have been able to at least get a few things down of late! I have lost one HMC "kiss" story that I wrote longhand... I hope it's just stuck in a drawer somewhere. sigh But I may have something up my sleeve. playful grin
