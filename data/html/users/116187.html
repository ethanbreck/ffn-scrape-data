Wow, an update.

For those wondering where I've been long story short: life. It seems if one thing finishes up another pops up and it had gotten at one point where things were chaotic but now everything is in working order. Hopefully.

As far as fandoms I think I can safely say while I will continue to write for the Gundam Seed fandom I have to admit my interest in the series has dropped considerably from when I first wrote for it. Not to say that I won't write anymore for this fandom that I have written for a better part of three years for but I just wanted to let my readers know I've moved on to other interests such as Harry Potter (which I don't know if I will ever write in considering my love for many ships like Harry/Luna, Harry/Ginny, Harry/Hermione, Ron/Hermione, and so on), Powerpuff Girls (yays for Season 1 dvd!), and Cardcaptor Sakura (I will always love this series) so fics from me for Seed may be few and far between. Though to be quite honest I am watching Seed again and it might just give my muse the kick it needs to start writing. Or something. I hope.

And on that note rest assured that the next chapter for Kismet (my quasi attempt at a chaptered story with Athrun/Cagalli) is in progress and should be posted by the end of this month...well, at the earliest. :p It's just like previously mentioned I have had so many things come up with school, work, and any other misc. things that I can't really guarantee anything.

Umm, yeah. That's it. Thanks for anyone who has read, reviewed, added me to their favorites whether by stories or author, and all that good stuff. It makes an author's day.
