hi hi hi !!!

yup, i finally updated my bio after like 3 or 4 months ! since i'll probably be changing this bio sometime waaaay in the future, i figured, might as well make this one long ...

first of all, for those who actually read by biography ( this ), i'm totally done with entrance exams and and that jazz !!! i'm proud to say, i'm finally a college freshman ! WOOHOO! ... my course is AB - Communications ! ( my first choice, in the college i wanted !!!! ) *jumps, skips, and dances around like an insane maniac*

as for the art and piano lessons ... well, i just got too lazy to go for them ... instead, i got a summer job and i took up the violin ! ... anyway, now that school has started, i've really busy ... aaaargh ... actually, my schedule is pretty good since i usually finish my classes a bit after noon everyday ! ... and yes, there are tons, fine fine - SOME uber cute guys in my university ... hehehehe ... there's the tennis guy, the artist/painter guy, aaah, the list goes on and on ... sigh, i sound like a kid let loose in a candy store ^_^;

anyway, here's stuff about ME ( not that you'd want to know anything about me ):

age : i'm old enough to drink and stuff

birtday : may

likes : dogs/cats, anime, drawing/painting, baking, playing the piano ( nope, not the violin ), starbucks' new coffee jelly frappe, shopping, reading british romance novels, mars ( my favorite manga at the moment ), table tennis, mint chocolates (any brand, from andes, junior mints, etc)
... just for the heck of it, i want to mention that one of my closest friends ( who by the way is in another university ) visited me and gifted me with a slice of cake, which, based on the menu, is called "BETTER THAN SEX" ... hahaha, it was good, the cake i mean.

dislikes : math, filipino class/lessons, itchy worms, flamers with no good reason or explanation, bare feet, ampalaya aka bitter melon, liars/racists, erm - and one of my blockmates

anime that i'm currently obsessed with or watching :
prince of tennis, naruto, slam dunk, hellsing, witch hunter robin, gravitation

personality wise:

from a teacher's point of view - i look really mean / b*tchy ( yup, a teacher actually told me that, and another affirmed what she said )

new people i meet - i look antisocial/quiet

relatives - i'm insane and abnormally loud ... a cousing told me that i just can't shut up ... hehehe ^_^

friends - i'm sweet, kind, nice, and all the good adjectives you can put in ...

... hmmm, seems like a have some sort of split personality ... does that mean i'm a shizophrenic? ( did i even spell that word correctly ? probably not *shrug* ) ... or does that mean i've been watching too much anime? ... or does that just mean i'm a deranged fanfic writer like many of my lovely and amazing contemporaries? ... WHATEVER ... i'm too confused to think anymore !

... anyway, love y'all for reading my fics, and thanks bunches and bunches for the reviews you guys ( hmmm, to be politically correct, let's say " you peeps " ) for the reviews you have written, and will hopefully write ... they really do help !
