I love to read a good story that develops the characters of my favorite TV shows (and their relationships, of course!).

Emotion . . . that is what I try to show in all of my stories, and that is what I look for in a good story.
“He liked to observe emotions; they were like red lanterns strung along the dark unknown of another's personality, marking vulnerable points.”
This is a quote by Ayn Rand, a Russian-American novelist.

Each person is an individual - different things affect them and they react in a certain way. That is what I look for in a TV show and also in a story. I want to see the character vulnerable and I want to see them pull through. I like to know what makes a person tick. What is it that gets them through those hard times. What was it in their past that makes them act/react the way they do.

I think that this is why I have had such a hard time finishing any of my stories! LOL. I get too involved in the emotions that I can't focus on the plot. So, I have decided that this is the year that I will finish a rough draft of a story!

I also love to work on FanArt for my favorite TV shows. Check it out at: ClassicCouples' Fan Art
