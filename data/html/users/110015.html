And now, TWO LISTS! Top 5 series I got into this year (not necessarily released this year) and Top 5 moments...that I cried at. Seriously.

TOP 5 SHOWS CHRISSY GOT INTO THIS YEAR

1) Akazukin Chacha
2) Yami To Boushi Hon No Tabibito
3) Gunslinger Girl
4) To Heart
5) Sensei No Ojikan (could rise as unfinished...)

TOP 5 MOMENTS WHERE CHRIS CRIED LIKE A BABY...

1) Full Moon wo Sagashite (ep 52) - Oh god. Too trock full of spoilers, and after tossing between this moment and number 2, I changed my mind and went with this one. I think I cried more...I won't say much, but various events up to it involving Mitsuki, Eichi, Takato...and then the wings on an angel...Meroko...god be with you...
2) Princess Nine (ep 26) - Sad music, a defeated dream, rivals hugging - it was all too much for my all time favourite series. It would be one, but Full Moon edged it on generally more sadness.
3) Sister Princess (ep 25) Crying scenes always make me a little sad, but when my two favourite characters of the series Haruka and Aria are doing it...Haruka breaks down hugging the girl apologises...and of course, it gets Aria-chan all worked up as well...heartbreaking.
4) Angelic Layer (ep 26) - Misaki and her mother. That's all there is to it - Hatoko's reaction didn't help much either...
5) Gunslinger Girl (ep 13) - I've mentioned this moment as well before in cute, the sadness is with Angelica...and how poetic this scene is. Just magnificent.
