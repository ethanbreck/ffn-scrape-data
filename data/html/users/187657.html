I am a 25 year old North Carolinan, though I was born in CT. I've been writing short stories that are hidden from public view since I was in fourth grade inspired by my wonderful teacher Ms. Witzel. In NC you have to write a brief paper in order to pass the grade. Each year you are given a different subject to write about. This begins in Fourth Grade and continues until you enter High School. Ms. Witzel was an artist and would bring in her paintings for the students to describe. My papers always got high marks for good use of words and such. During a parent teacher meeting she confided in my mom that she believed I was destined for something great in the creative writing field.

Currently I am working on a story I hope to get published but you hope to be career writers know what that means. RESEARCH. Researching before you write to get published is a must for those that don't know. If you write that the Mohawks were found in Alaska you'd be wrong and just may insult someone. Research is crucile to a writer and can be their best friend. Even know when I e-mail rpg I research about the thing I am going to have in my post to make sure that I don't get what I am trying to say across.

In college, yes I work and go to school, I am studying to get my Liberal Arts Degree and hope to transfer to NYU or UNCW (Unversity of North Carolina in Wilimington) which offers both English and Anthorpology. In my senior year of HS I got interested in Anthropology when I had to write a research paper on Margert Mead. Someday I'd like to be a writer (fictional and non fictional) and a Physical Anthoropologist who travels around the world studying bones and finding connections between us and chimpanizees whom we are 95.9% related too. I would like to write for National Geographic as well and be a professor during the school year.

I got into Anime in my junior year of HS when my best friend compared me to Ami. Apparently since I strived for good grades and ALWAYS had a book in my hand that made me Ami. Ah well he compared another friend of ours to Minako. Since then she and I refer to one another As Minako-chan and Ami-chan. My online friends call me Kit, this was started by my best online friend whom I dubbed Logan. Since we are both X-Men fans and I like Kitty and he likes Logan he started calling me Kit since that is what Kitty is called by Logan. The only friends besides Minako-chan that doesn't call me Kit are Ororo-mama who calls me Kitten (that is what Ororo calls Kitty) and Midlight21 who addresses me by my real name. Two of my online friends; Tomoyo (Cyn) and Meilin (Kat) dubbed me Sakura as a joke. Most of my real life friends call me my real name or shortening it just a bit which is odd since there are only four letters to my name. Hey I am not gonna tell you what it is! :)

Fanficing started with Atamus (the one who started the Ami-chan nickname) when we wrote a bad one called Senshi Gone Bad. That is found on my website but I have choosen not to submit it here because it is my ealier work and badly written. Next came Family Secrets which there are 15 chapters to but I started it when I was still sharing a computer with my parents so it is hand written. I have other stories that follow Family Secrets as well. Family Secrets started as a story that I was going to write just on it's own but then it ended up a triology. Any how the idea for Family Secrets came when I was watching Batman and Robin. Runaway came next and I am right now dabbling in writing a Tamora Pierce fanfic.

Well that is me! Thank you for reading this bio.
