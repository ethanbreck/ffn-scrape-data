My name is Amanda, I am 28 years old and I am a born and bred Texas girl who loves reading, writing and fangirling. I've been writing fanfiction on and off for fifteen years now and I have some original stories in the works as well. Writing is my passion, and I love to dabble in worlds others have created.

As for my ships--I can be a little fickle but generally I will have my OTPs (One True Pairings) for certain characters. (See: Spike/Buffy, Buffy the Vampire Slayer; Luke/Lorelai, Gilmore Girls, Robin/Marian; Robin Hood, Eric/Sookie; Southern Vampire Mysteries/True Blood). Very rarely I will have a couple who I don't want to see with anyone but each other (see: Luke/Lorelai once more).

And one final note--I am very sorry for the inconvenience, but my One Life to Live stories are on hiatus, where they most likely will remain. I don't believe in ever saying I will never go back to something, but I have been out of the fandom for several years and I don't think the odds of me going back to these stories from years ago are very good. I am terribly, terribly sorry!

I promise that I am still working on my Robin Hood stories--I didn't have a computer for a long time, but now I have two and the Internet so I will get back to work as soon as possible! In the meantime...

My muse tends to enjoy feedback (it makes her stick around longer). And don't feel like you need to log in to comment--but please keep all criticism constructive and please please please check the ratings of the stories before you read (and stay away from M if you are underage!). I hope you enjoy what you find here!
