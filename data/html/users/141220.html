Author has written 3 stories for Sailor Moon.

Rose Asali here.

Sailor Moon was my entree into anime and, by extension, the Internet. I'm a huge fan of fantasy, magic, romance, and strong female characters. Therefore, most of the fanfiction I write is set in (at least loosely) that universe -- I'm so inspired by it.

Generally I write romance fanfiction with fantasy, magic, and magical girls elements. I also love reading fanfiction with romance, magic, and fantasy elements. I can get into a lot of different kinds of fanfiction if the piece is well-written, though - I like steampunk, science fiction, and slice of life stories, too.

Besides writing fanfiction, my biggest hobby is probably reading. I will read in a lot of genres, but I like historical fiction, romance (especially historical romances, and particularly Regency romances), young adult dystopian literature, fantasy (surprise), and science fiction. I love to read works by women and authors of color. I also really love playing video games, board games, running, hiking, and sailing.

Anime I love: Sailor Moon (both the original and Sailor Moon Crystal!), Attack on Titan, Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra (I know they aren't technically anime but...), Death Note, Sword Art Online, Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood, Cowboy Bebop, Gundam 00, Fushigi Yuugi, Kare Kano, Code Geass.

Currently watching: My Hero Academia.
