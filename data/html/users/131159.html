Author has written 17 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Lord of the Rings, and Wheel of Time.

Time for a new profile! Last one was made EXACTLY three years ago (or, it was edited three years ago on the dot), and a lot of things has happened since. But not much has changed.

I haven't posted anything for... three years? But that doesn't mean I haven't been active lurking around fandoms. Because that's what I do best. Lurk. I could probably do it professionally.

But here's the deal: I made a New Years resolution this year that I (very non-originally) named "Just do it", and it involves doing things that may be a bit out of my comfort-zone but that I'm pretty sure I'll regret not doing. Hence, updating all my fanfiction-accounts (there's three of them, but I have conveniently forgotten the passwords, so that's ok. And the website...) and really get into writing again. The desire is there, it's the discipline that's sorely lacking. So I'll "just do it". =o)

I've always been part of "Final Fantasy 8" fandom, and that's not likely to change anytime soon. It's still the fandom where I know the characters like the scars on the back of my hands.
I've branched out into "Lord of the Rings" but that didn't stick for the long haul.
"Wheel of Time" is still very much active, but unfortunately it's too daunting to dare write anything in. Too much fact, too much fiction.
"Supernatural" was the rage for a while, but while I still enjoy venturing into it immensely, it's not a priority anymore. Despite my obsession in the past, it only spawned one fic, that never got posted.
Now a-days, it's a lot of Adam Lambert and "Generation Kill" (and aren't I late for THAT party?!) and a bit of dabbling in "Hawaii Five-0", although I've never written a word in the latter. The former two, though... Yeah, that could be some of the fics I could post...

Hope to see you soon!

Hugs, Zierra
