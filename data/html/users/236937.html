"L'homme n'est rien, l'oeuvre c'est tout." —Gustave Flaubert (The man is nothing, the work is all.)

Me:
I am somewhat conversant in the following fandoms: Akuma na Eros, Aladdin, Angel Sanctuary, Batman Begins, Count Cain, Death Note, Demon Diary, Fushigi Yuugi, Hunchback of Notre Dame, Lord of the Rings, Peter Pan, Petshop of Horrors, Phantom of the Opera, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Sherlock Holmes--

but my primary fandoms are Harry Potter, Yu Yu Hakusho, and Hellsing.

I will read most any pairing in Harry Potter, although I favor those with Snape, Harry, or Hermione. Karasu/Kurama is my OTP for Yu Yu Hakusho, and I wrote an essay on it for ship_manifesto on LiveJournal, which can be found here: I also like Hiei/Mukuro and Kuwabara/Yukina, and Karasu/Bui definitely has potential. My Hellsing OTP is Alucard/Integral, but I'm open to most permutations involving them, Seras, and Walter.

Story Policy:
I vastly prefer one-shots to chaptered stories. For a long time now, I also haven't believed in WIPs. All of the chaptered fics that I post (with the exception of "Dear Diary," which I have abandoned) are more than halfway done.

Other:
Feel free to stop by my LJ or drop me an email!
