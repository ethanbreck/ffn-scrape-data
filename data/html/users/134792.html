I have been gone for quite some time, I notice. Recently, I have given myself over to my role as GM for the 7thSea and Rokugan roleplaying games, and have been getting my writing urges out that way. I don't promise to be more active than I have been, but sometimes Minako or one of my other favorite heroes/heroines is going to inspire my fingers again. As to other sites I'm active on, I run a roleplaying board and hang around Pony Island. I have no chat, I have no facebook, and I have no other social media.

Please R&R my stories, and all the usual blah-ness that people say around here, including: I don't own very many of the characters in my work here. Gundam Wing, Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball Z, Mai the Psychic Girl, Witch Hunter Robin, Fire Emblem, and Crystal Cave aren't mine either. Since there was a reference, I don't own Persona 3 or 4. There. A master disclaimer. My home page is the page that links to my GW and SM fan-sites. If you have the time (if you even CARE, which I doubt), please check them out. Asagao/Asago, Leila, and Itelia ARE mine, though.

I might be culling through some of my earlier works. They were written more than 10 years ago in some cases, and I HOPE my writing has matured since then.
