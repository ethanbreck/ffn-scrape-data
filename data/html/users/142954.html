Hello fellow fanfictians! Momochan here, letting you guys know a little bit about myself. Hmm, let see. Well my hobbies consist of anime, eating, video games, cooking, listening to music (I like bout everything), reading books and manga (I prefer things wit pictures, lol), eating, sleeping, and did i mention eating?

I'm currently STILL a student, lol. Although, I think I'm still somewhat of an asset to society, since I do work, in retail, lol. I am in the Air force reserves too, but I just consider myself a weekend warrior. Gaaah, one of these days I'll proly end up deploying. However, my main concern is getting done with school and getting a real job. I've been in school WAAAY too long. Anywho, enough about my hum drum life.


Favorite animes: Codegeass, Fullmetal Alchemist, Baccano, Host Club, Cowboy Bebop, Trigun, Slayers, Kenshin, Gurren Lagann, Deathnote, Mushishi, Soul Eater, Melancholy of Haruhi Suzamiya, Evangelion, X, and Bakemonogatari.

There are alot of other series I like, but these are just my favs. I guess you can tell, I'm a big fan of Shonen stuff. Although I still like a lil Shojou here and there. I'm a big fan of Clamp, but usually prefer to read Shojou and watch Shonen.


Must see animes I recommend: Codegeass, Fullmetal Alchemist, Baccano, and Hostclub.

I think any anime fan could learn to love these series. They have a little bit of everything, well Hostclub is mostly silly stuff, but I know plenty of fangirls and fanboys who love it.

Favorite Manga/Manhwa: Gentleman's Alliance Cross, Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicles, Papillon, Your and My Secret, Meru Peri, Vampire Knight, Black Sun Silver Moon, Now, 07 ghost, Kill Me Kiss Me, XXXHolic, Junjou Romantica, Gamerz Heaven, and Tactics.

As you can see, I prefer Shojou manga.

Hmm, I'm not too much into Yaoi or Yuri anymore. I use to be a huge Yaoi fanatic, lol. I still don't mind it though. The idea of two hot guys together, oh yes yes. As far as Yuri...I tried to have an open mind, but it ended up being to mushy for me lol. I do own one Yuri though. Kannazuki no Miko, it wasn't too bad, lol, but I ended up routing for the hot guy to get the girl...so I think I missed the point, lol. Yesh, I lurv hot bishos.

As you can see though, the fanfiction I usually write is Yugioh stuff. Yeah, for some reason that's all I really like to write. Honestly, I'm not a huge fan of the series, but I love the characters. It's hard to say who's my favorite. My favorite pairing though is definitely SetoxJonouchi, follwed by a close SetoxYami. I love putting people together who will fight and bicker and try everything to be against it. Then again, as far as Yaoi pairings, I think I have read it all, lol. I'm pretty open-minded too. If you're a good enough writer you can make any pairing seem good. As far as the actual series, I like JonouchixMai and YuugixAnzu.

Well, I know I've been on a writing hiatus for the last 4 or 5 years. Well, honestly, I probably won't be writing fanfiction anymore. I just lost my motivation and my touch. I know there are a few stories I haven't finished, and I probably won't. Honestly, my memory is so terrible I forgot where I was going with them. Maybe when I finally get a real job and settle down, I'll get my writing muse back, but until then, this is it. So I thank all you guys who have supported me up till now, and I hope you guys enjoy my fics. I know I will continue to enjoy all of yours!
