T_T

Random Thought of the Week: If I were as sexy as Axel, I wouldn't be surprised if I got gangbanged...

More Random crap: I turned 189 in 2007, I like painting with acrylics and watercolors, I'm fond of casting in bronze, I like spinning fire, welding, and blacksmithing, and I once glued my friend's hand to a table. My half of the dorm room is a mess, and there's a string of blue quartz crystals sitting next to my ball-joint doll on my desk. I'm attending Humboldt State as a marine biology major, and I have a passion for learning languages. I speak fluent Fringlanese, decent French, crappy Japanese, and a handful of random words in Hawaiian, Italian, Irish, and ASL. Not to mention I know how to write and read Atlantean, Yautja, Draconic, Theban, and backwards.

Yes, I am Chaotic Neutral. I play D&D, and I have a pesky Gaia Online addiction that I can't quite get under control. I'm in college, I own a pair of knee-high leather moccasins which I love dearly, and I like drawing on my hand with a Sharpie. Much to my delight and awe and wonder, I am only four degrees of separation away from Jhonen Vasquez. A friend of mine works with a 'hot, intelligent, nerdy Goth boy' who is good buddies with JV. Man, I'd settle for the Goth boy alone. n.n I can happily say that I have heard Quinton Flynn say 'Roxas, put your pants back on.' That made my week.

These are the important things, you know. I could tell you how tall I am, and what color my hair and eyes are, but you wouldn't really know me, would you. Besides, I wear colored contacts, one blue and one green, so they change a bit. My hair is mostly my own dark blonde, and semi-short, and right up in the front there is a SHOCKING...uh, shock...of bright reddish orange. The orange is not natural. Nor was the bright blue, dark blue, purple, green, black, yellow, pink, or auburn hair I've had in the past.

Hee. Let's see...favorites. Everyone else has them.

Favorite Games: KINGDOM HEARTS. And also, Kingdom Hearts II. "Where...is my heart...?" Oh Saix, just run up the stairs like I have to every day, and you'll find it easily enough. It will be trying to LEAP OUT OF YOUR THROAT. I play the Sims 2, as well, where I make everyone a gay man and a pick pocket.

Favorite Books: Just about anything with a sci-fi or fantasy plot, and a lot of things in plain general fiction. I also enjoy Le Petit Prince (The Little Prince) immensely. You should read it, and educate yourself. Also Terry Pratchett, John Ringo, and Diane Duane books. ("Motherfisker...!")

Favorite Shows: I was a religious zealot in the Church of Ninja Turtles when I was four, and I still love them. I also enjoy a decent number of childrens' cartoons, and...stand up comedy. Also CSI and ghost related series, although I haven't watched a show dutifully in agess.

Favorite Movies: Anthing with dragons in it, as well as Jim Carrey movies, and the Predator movies.. The seccond one sucked, but I very much love the first one, and wasn't TOO disappointed with AvP. Leave me a message if you want to argue the intricacies of plot twists. Also anime! Currently on a Yami no Matsuei kick...lots of yaoi series. :snicker: I'm a sucker for AMVs made with Kingdom Hearts people...And Axel.

Favorite Comics: Stuff by Jhonen Vasquez, Inhumans, assorted manga, and the Predator comics. One of my best friends gave me #s 1-4 of the Predator comics; first printing. X3 I heart her dearly for it. And manga, can't forget the manga. I own-- Yami no Matsuei, Loveless, X(/1999,) Negima, Eerie Queerie, Yuu Yuu Hakusho, GTO, and...some other stuff I can't remember and am too lazy to get up and check.

Dunno if I'm going to start writing again...I really want to, but I'm so impatient; the words get stuck in my head and eek onto the paper like something coming out of a clogged drain. Give me support, and I will give you stories! I really prefer to draw, lately, though. It seems I'm too much focused on what things look like...if there's anyone out there who still reads my stuff, fine, please drop me a line and bother me about my art. I love showing off, and frankly, I think I'm pretty good. I have proportion and shading down, anyway, which is more than can be said for a lot of people who think they're 'pretty good.'

Ya know, throughout this entire bio, I don't think I've mentioned that I'm female. I guess I was hoping you could figure that out. But if you can't, well, I'd hope you knew by now.

And if you still don't know...take me to your special little place inside your head. It must be nice there.
