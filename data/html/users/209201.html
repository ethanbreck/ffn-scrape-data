Notice:

It was in this little corner of cyberspace that I was first inspired to place hand to keys and allow my own soul its piece of freedom. The years I have spent writing, reading and reviewing on this site have been some of the best of my life.

Alas, such things can never last and as the site has grown in girth, it has also grown in rigidity, with no concern for the quality of writing that once made it one of the most highly regarded sites on the web. As more and more epic and emotional storiesand authors are exiled to comply with fanfiction.net's technicalities, it is now difficult to find quality stories. As my own stories rely on lyrics and quotes to build anatmosphere and set the piece to its own rhythm, I can no longer obey the administration. My stories will be archived at freedom of speech fanfiction, where I can communicate with reviewers and write as I please.

I am sorry that this amazing community of authors was effectively destroyed by the enforcement of unjust rules and abuse reports. I thank all of those who have deigned to read my own works and those who have written the stories that have affected my life.

Fare thee well,
