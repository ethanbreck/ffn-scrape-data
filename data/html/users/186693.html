Due to changes at ff.net, I will no longer be putting stories up here, even those rated below NC17. I don't support sites that censor. Of course they have a right to say what goes on their site, but I also have the right to thumb my nose at them. For those reading The End, please check out my site listed above and feel free to join my update list.

Well, that was about four, five years ago and I'm going to give it another shot...

Not really Laure Alexander very much any more, and not really in the Buffy fandom. My shiny, new fandom is DC Comics, primarily Batfic. The search for more fic to read brought me back here, and now I'll try sharing some of my own work. Gotta figure out the rating system, though, and see how mature I can go...

If you enjoy my tamer stories but want something a bit naughtier, check out my website listed here, or my livejournal, ladyoneill, well, as long as LJ doesn't ban me.:P

As for me, I'm not a kid. I'm not even young. I've been writing since I was thirteen. I've been writing smut since I was thirteen--poorly written, oh so poorly written. I started writing fanfic nine years ago and once upon a time was known in the Buffy fandom, but I like being obscure, and I love my new fandom. I never thought anything would replace Buffy, but DC has.

Also, icon by magnolia_simms over on LJ.

So, though I've left the name as Laure Alexander, I'm writing under my real name, Lara.

Enjoy!
