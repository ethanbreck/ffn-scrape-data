+ EXTENDED SUMMARIES +

Tsuki no Namida
Alternate Reality - Silver Millennium PG-13

When a woman of darkness went to the Terrans with an offer to give them the chance to wipe out the Lunarian race, there was no hesitation. And so on one night the woman was to cast a spell to bind the Lunarians from their powers and the Terrans were to attack and slaughter the silver-haired people. It was a night they called Tsuki no namida.The children were imprisoned in slavery, powers bound by special collars. As the years pass, one child grows into a young woman under the shelter of a couple. Her life is simple and secluded, always hiding her silver hair and crescant moon when she goes into public. But one day she meets a Terran who steals her heart... What happens when he finds her identity? A prophecy is fulfilled.

War of the Roses
Alternate Reality. PG-13

Serenity Blake is excited to attend Whittenfield Academy in England--until she arrives there. The rules are different, but when she learns them, it's an emotional decision whether to confirm. There's a thin line of friend and enemy. Torn by the need to belong and frustrated by their ways, she finds herself perilously caught in a century old feud with Whittenfield Academy and their rival, Bainbridge Academy. Not to mention between Darien Bainbridge, the one who seems to hate her and yet against the odds finds herself falling for, and Seiya Whittenfield, the deceptively charming one whose school would obey anything he tells them--even if it meant taking rivalry too far.

Lunar Magic
Sailor Moon(A.R.) / Harry Potter Crossover PG-13

Serenity Moonsquire is the last of her kind. She has the Lunar bloodline, the blood that gives her an affinity with magic far above that of the ordinary witch. She held a wand since age four, tamed and befriended and outcast dragon, and became an animagus by age eight. With a spirit rivaling any magical creature, she was caged, forced into a quiet life by her guardians, Luna Meltrivon and Artemis Filch. However, one day, it all changes. Her guardians drag her across the country on the run, unwilling to answer her questions of her pursuer. You see... there are things she doesn't know--many things--her past, her heritage, and a prophecy linking her with the most famous young wizard, and consequently, a vital role in the final battle against a terror returned.
