Notice to readers and those I beta for (1/15/13): I'm finally back! It feels nice to have the internet full-time again, and also to be able to get back to work on updation and beta'd pieces. If I'm currently beta-ing for you, I will start sending things back this week. Updating my current works will continue as scheduled from now on.

So, I realized I haven't visited this thing in over 6 years. (Six years, you say! Were you living under a rock?) Not really, I didn't write a whole lot of fanfiction during that time, it was mostly original works and a little freelance stuff for e-zines, as in, the stuff that helps pay for the light bill. That said, this year's NaNo threw me for a loop, and I ended up writing (and winning) it with a Fallout: New Vegas piece called 'We are Dust and Shadow'. This made me realize I missed fanfiction more than a little, and I had some orphaned things over here and in my hard drive that needed a little love and revision since I've grown, both as a writer and a person, in the interim. So, you get updation! Aren't you thrilled, assorted kiddies and fan-stalkers?!

I'll also be throwing some of the stuff from my lj and various memes over here...because hey, why not have it all in one place? So no, I'm not stealing it from some random lj, it happens to be mine, don't freak out if you've seen me post it over there. Additionally, I write for myself most of all, and share because I think someone else might get a good laugh or something out of it. If you like it, that's cool, let's party. If you don't, that's not my fault, is it?

Any questions, you know where the mail button is (I don't bite, promise). Any flames, you know where the round file is.

*Additionally, on the off-chance that I forget a disclaimer on one of the stories below, this is it. I will never, and have never, owned any of the material used for the fanfictions presented below. I might slightly own the occasional OC, but they tend to run off and cause enough trouble without me claiming them. Any material below is owned solely by the current copyright holder/s. All fics below are done for the purpose of parody, and are allowed under current United States Copyright Law.*

Enjoy! (Or don't, I'm not making you.)
