When I started writing, it was my stress relief for stuff that happened in RL. I won't say I'm a great writer nor will I say I'm a bad writer. I tend NOT to write canon/fanon pairings. Blame it all on Gundam Wing fanfics.

I like a ton of otome games like Kichiku Megane, STEAL!, Omerta, Sweet Scandal, Office Lover, Contract Marriage, Forbidden Love, It's Our Secret, and a TON of Voltage Inc. games as well (too many to list here, but ie., Seduced in the Sleepless City, Be My Princess, In Your Arms Tonight, Kiss by the Baddest Bidder, My Sweet Bodyguard, Metro PD: Close to you, Office Secrets, etc...).
