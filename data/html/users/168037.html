Questions:

Q. Will there be a sequel to Unnatural?
A. Probably not, unless someone else writes it.

Q. Where is "Unnatural"?
A. On my own website and adultfanfiction.com

Q. Are you going to write more SB/RL?
A. Possibly. Eventually.

Q. When are you going to finish "Through a Glass"?
A. Probably never. Posting a story in parts when the end wasn't properly drafted was not a successful experiment and I won't be doing it again.

Q. You sometimes use odd lines from other shows or books, shouldn't you reference them separately?
A. If you're widely read (or have seen enough TV) to identify where a quote has come from I rejoice with you, but as it's one line here and there (and I can't guarantee that all of it's intentional) no, I'm not going to give a reference for my sources. These are odd lines in stories, I am not being paid for the stories (and even if I were, have you ever seen a fiction book which references quotes?) and nobody is going to give them marks out of twenty. Live with it.

Q. Isn't that (OMG!) plagiarism?
A. No. As I said, have you ever seen a fiction book which referenced quotes? Neither have I. I expect you to be well-read enough to realise when I'm using a quote.

Q. Can I write a sequel to your story? Or a remix?
A. Yes, sequel or remix until your eyes bubble. Just do three things for me: let me know on the address above so that I can read it, finish it before you post it, and get it beta-read. Oh, and let the archivist where you submit it know it's a sequel to a story of mine - I don't really care, but for some reason archivists tend to get bent out of shape if you don't.

Further note: I accept anonymous reviews. On the other hand, political rantings (however well meant) will be removed as soon as I become aware of them - which will be pretty quickly as reviews are emailed to me.
