OCs as main characters (see OOCs rant). 'Sides, far too often you're just putting yourself in anways and you're inevitably a Sue.

Script format. It sucks, learn to write paragraphs.
Present tense.
Copying canon. If i wanted to read the dialogue/exact events of the canon, I'd order the frickin' transcripts. It's called fanfiction, not stenographer 101. Stop bogarting other people's work, and start being creative.
