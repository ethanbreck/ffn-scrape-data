Author has written 4 stories for Moulin Rouge, StarTrek: The Next Generation, and General Hospital.

I love TV, and I watch a lot of shows, some of which inspire me to write. Currently my fanfic inspiration is Sexis from General Hospital.

Oh, and if you have not already met Adrian Mole and his diaries you should! Written by Sue Townsend, Adrian's diaries start at age 13 and 3/4 and with increased success the series consists of 6 books as of today. Adrian is around 40 right now. The books are hilarious, especially the last two (Adrian Mole: The Capuccino Years and Adrian Mole and the Weapons of Mass Destruction). I am not a very humorous person, and it takes me forever to get jokes, and I do not laugh out loud much, but Adrian made me laugh sooooo hard! BTW, Adrian lives in England.
