Author has written 10 stories for Myst/Riven, and Casper.

Finally! I'm back at ff.net, and this time I've deleted all my old crappy stories to make way for new, much better ones. So keep coming back, because I have an odd assortment of things coming this way. I have the tendency to write one-shots, either really emotional ones like "Just Kat" and "Reflections" or quirky, funny ones like "Questions and Answers", but I'm also paranoid. It takes me forever to decide to put a fic up, simply because I don't want anything there that isn't ready to be there.

Other than that, there's nothing else really to say. Read my stories (there are only three at the moment, but that'll change). Be happy. Constructive criticism is very, very welcome, although I do get a good kick out of the ones of "U sux!11!1 go to hel!1!1". Quite funny, if you ask me. :D

I also think (and this is my own opinion, mind you) that if you're going to post stories in a place where thousands of people can read them, that you should use SPELL CHECK. Just a simple click of a button. It's easy! And for the love of everything that is good in the world, space your stories out! I already have eye problems as it is, so I don't need to be reading stories that are one paragraph--but several pages--long.

Rant over. Who wants twinkies?
