Amused By: Currently, people who presume to tell me that my writing is based on illogical premises. I'm going to write what I want to write, when I want to write it. That's the beauty of writing. I can take whatever the hell I want and create something out of it. ^_^ So I won't be horribly offended if someone tells me that I dare pair two characters that they could never see together, etc etc, because I don't give a flying fart in space. ^~ Oh, and closeminded idiots who go, "Eeew! Gross! Yaoi is yucky and ewww!" Because that's just funny. @_@

Currently obsessed with: Harry Potter slash! @_@ Harry/Severus and Harry/Draco, in that order too, mostly. ^_~ Also Bill/Harry and Bill/Severus, if I ever get around to writing it...

Randomness: Oh, as for all my Sonic the Hedgehog fandom pieces, there is no world setting, unless I specify it. Just consider them alternate world fics with the characters thrown in. Because I don't really care about specifics or this or that, unless it pertains to my plot. ^^ As far as being told that characters won't work in this setting or that, well, my view on people attempting to tell me what I can write and whether it will work or not is stated above. I have very small tolerance for people who try to tell me that my writing is illogical or unfounded.

But, y'know, this doesn't mean I'm a bitch. ^_^ I'm a perfectly amiable person. And I loooove getting comments. @_@ I about died because I actually got comments on my fics right after I posted them. I was thrilled beyond words--and I'm still basking in the afterglow.

I don't mind when people criticize my work and tell me what I could do better, because I try to do that when I review other people's. Not that I think I'm an expert or anything, God no, just helpful hints. So I'd appreciate if people would go ahead and say those things constructively in a comment. ^_^ And if someone just outright insults me or gives me what I see as pointless comments, I'll just laugh. Because I don't care what people say. If something is illogical to you, or you don't like a pairing, it's as simple as choosing not to read the fic. And then to take the time to review it and throw it in my face? I'm flattered. It shows you care. ^_~

Anyway, time to stop rambling. Talk to me!
