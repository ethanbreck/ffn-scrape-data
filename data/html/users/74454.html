Stories I'm currently working on:

The Collection - The third installment in my modern Sherlock Holmes series, featuring my original characters, Ethan Rhodes and Nadia Bridges. A cross-over of sorts, in which Rhodes discovers he has an older brother in New York who has gone missing, and the pair must try to locate him, with the help of a certain eccentric FBI agent. Written from Bridges's point-of-view, with some third-person present-tense.

Requiem for an Angel - A wicked long Final Fantasy IX adventure story in which Kuja, big surprise, escaped death at the Iifa Tree and is attempting to live a secluded life, until he meets Claire Giovannucci, a young Earthling who was transported against her will to Gaia. With the help of Alleyway Jack, a crass, four-armed thief, and Felina Flynn, a Cockney cardsharp, Kuja and Claire must get back to Earth before it is destroyed by a madman - and before time runs out for Kuja. Third-person omniscient.

And I suppose that's about it. I'll leave you with my favorite quote:

"There are always some lunatics about. It would be a dull world without them." - Sherlock Holmes
