About me, hmm... I love reading, writing, dancing, music, cartoons, and men. In no particular order.
As a writer, I have a bad habit of starting too many stories at once, and having to put one or more on hold until I finish the ones I started with. I also go through spells where my writing tends to be long and, well, boring. If you're ever reviewing my stories as they are being written and see this is happening, please tell me! I appreciate useful feedback a great deal, how else is the writing supposed to become improved? Never too proud for help. Pointless criticism is not appreciated, though. If you can't explain why something's not to your liking, don't bother mentioning it at all...

Just a heads up for my readers: when I say 18 and up for my stories I mean it.
