Author has written 2 stories for Twilight.

I got out of posting stories on here for such a long time, because I was running into reading a lot of juvenile pieces...not that what I do sometimes isn't just as silly, but that's not my point. When people use bad grammar, it kills me... I'm a writer...and because I have a love for writing, I sometimes just write whatever is on my mind, which often happens to be something from a book that I've read or a movie that I've seen. :) It just happens that way. The Twilight series was so incredible, and I wanted the love scene between Bella and Edward to be about them...and I was left wanting when there was no detail. (Yes, I realize that there are younger girls who read the books.) I just felt like it needed more. So...I wrote it, and am fairly satisfied. It will probably be the only story I post for awhile.

Other than that...I'm just a poor college student like most everyone on here, bored in my apartment if I'm not working at the coffeehouse. Writing is one of my biggest inspirations, yet my creations don't always get finished...I guess it happens.

It's been so long...years, in fact, since I've posted anything. Breaking Dawn seemed like a good excuse to write again...so, tada. :)

And...I love the reviews. The feedback I've been getting from all of you is amazing. :) It makes me want to keep writing. So, thanks again. You're my people. You rock.
