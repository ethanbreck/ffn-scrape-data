Hello folks! I am glad you stopped by to check out my works. I do appreciate everyone who reads my stories. And I would like to thank everyone who has ever written a review on my stories! Thank you for your continued support!

Well, let's get through some of these formalities, shall we? I have three pennames that I use here on ff.n. They are Arldetta, Knight MysAri and shikigami-kun. Outside of ff.n, I have posted to a number of websites including: boards.theforce.net, mediaminer.org and others.

Well I have a few sites set up; yahoo groups is there but I haven't posted to that in a LONG time, I have posted some things through my livejournal but that is a bit sporadic too, and lastly my own website at arldetta.com is currently down and being re-designed - I will update here when it is finally up and running again.

Real life and writing have not been good bedfellows for a while now. Although I have been trying to work on my stories, it is very slow in coming. I have promised myself a few things this year and am determined to see them through.

At this point the game plan is simple. With limited time to write, I will only be able to work what my muse is interested in. So this is the status:

"Skin Deep" in Supernatural is complete! Yeah, so happy. :) No Sequel planned still at this time.

Well, after the last post to "Reliving Shattered Dreams" my muse got discouraged, so I am not sure when the next post will be again.

On top of that, my daughter dragged me kicking and screaming into Teen Wolf, so my muse has decided to frolic under the moon, which wasn't too far from where she enjoys spending most of her time anyway. So yeah, I've posted a few things there now too.

Anyway, if my muse is kind, I still hope to get back to "Mask of Duty," also Star Wars, and then work through "Conquering the Realm" in Merlin. Although I have been working on a few other fics as well as an original story, so I can't promise anything on these. Either way, keep those fingers crossed!

All remaining works will be on Hiatus unless my muse is struck by some powerfully creative lightning and I can juggle more that one fic at a time again. If only, if only. -sigh-

Any way, Thank you everyone for your infinite patience! I cannot express how much I appreciate it! I will keep you all updated.
