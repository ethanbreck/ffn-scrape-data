About me:

Wow, Well I guess I should tell you a little about me. I first joined Fanfiction.net in 2001. It is now 2010. LOL So I was...14 when I started writing Fanfiction. I'm now 23 years old and a college graduate. I studied Psychology. I am going to Nursing School and then into a masters program. I've been writing since I was in the 7th grade, and I enjoy it very much. I recently went on a 4 year haitus.(2003-2007) Mostly to go to Japan as an exchange student in 2003, and again in 2005. Then I graduated from High school and attended my first year at the state university. So I didn't really have a lot of time to write in between doing all of that. I have recently found some free time and am happy to announce that I will be around for a while working on some stories.

I think you will find that my writing has grown as I have. So please check dates. Many of my stories are very old. And some have been resubmitted. My current project is working on a Silent Hill fanfiction.

I'm also a member of 2+2 productions which publishes AMV's on animemusicvideos.org, So if you are looking for some fun AMV's check out our productions there.

I recently started a Deviant Art Account, and you can find me under the name LizLadyNinja. :) So please go take a look at a bit of my fan art. It's just getting off the ground but I'm happy with some of the work I've done. (2009 update)

Catagories I write under:

I use to write a lot under the Anime section, but in the last few years I have strayed from my anime roots. So if you are a fan of any of my old anime works, please do not expect updates. I'm not working on them anymore. They should all be finished, but if I over looked one then I am sorry, but I will no longer be working on them. After I review the content that we can post I might re-add Assault, and Kitten. Assault will be the last anime piece that I work on. :(

I also write under the video game section. I'm a pretty serious Silent Hill, Fatal Frame gamer. I will play just about any survival horror game. I love the Resident Evil books by S.D. Perry. So if you play any survival horror games look for my publications. I think you will enjoy them. I'll write just about anything with a horror element. I enjoy many horror movies and novels. I'm sick of the generic horror idea. "A group of kids, or a lone female is being stalked by a crazy psycho killer." Don't expect that from me. I also am not a big fan of the generic "Amityville Horror" type story. Its old, over done and annoying. I try really hard to stay away from slashers and the generic horror story.
