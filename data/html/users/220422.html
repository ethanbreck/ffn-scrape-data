Hi guys!

I'm the Ponytail Goddess! Welcome to my little corner of fanfiction.net! I'm 22 years old and a general music teacher in Illinois. When I'm not making music, however, writing has always been one of my favorite ways to express myself, whether it be through writing songs and poems, journaling, or writing fanfics. I hope you enjoy my works-I know I enjoy many of yours!

I'd like to just say what a wonderful website fanfiction.net is. I've had an account on here since 2002 and have been reading here for even longer. This site works wonders for writers! Anyone who looks at my writing can tell that I have drastically improved since 2002. I know a lot of people don't like to write because in school, they aren't allowed to write about things that interest them. Here, they are able to do just that. Fanfiction.net encourges people to practice writing and encourages overall literacy, which is a problem in our country today. I've learned in my teaching classes that many people are conditioned to think reading is boring because they don't get to read interesting things in school. It doesn't matter what someone reads, as long as they are reading. There's something for everyone on this site; it's truly a great advocate of literacy.

"We read to know we are not alone."-C.S. Lewis
