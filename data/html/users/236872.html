Breezy

Just a simple request.

I will NOT, nor have I ever played the 'hold a story hostage for reviews' game. I absolutely despise that kind of garbage as the lowest of the low. If I have a story, I will post it, whether it's complete or as I go along (note that I tend to try to finish before I post when I can as it keeps the flow going) BUT reviews 'feed' the muse - I can see footie-prints - a lot of them actually- but so few people have taken the time to leave me a cookie, letting me know if they've enjoyed the story. If I've passed a little time for you, and you've enjoyed the tale, please do let me know... :)

Some ‘Author’s notes’ for those who are new to my little Galaxy.

First, this is a blanket “I don’t own the rights to Star Wars, or any of the officially licensed characters, and I’m only playing. Like all fan fiction authors, the only ‘payment’ I receive is reviews and comments.

In this Universe, Luke’s wife is Aubé Canaille, a character of my own creation, as is the entire ‘family Canaille’, including Schurke, Kampher (nee Lutador), Aubé (now Skywalker), and Kay’leb, as well as many ancillary and main characters such as Toa, Pemberian Skywalker, Auryn Skywalker, and this version of ‘Ben’ Skywalker.

With few exceptions (aka the ‘AU of the Breezyverse’) all the stories tie into each other a chronological list will follow, with the ‘Alternative Universe’ (of my AU!) following after. You can get a general idea about each story by reading the summery link on each one.

I’ve been writing stories for Star Wars for over 10 years now – scary thought! Some of my earliest work is painful, I admit it, but as I said, I leave them unmolested as a testament to the fact that if you want to be a good writer, or even a mediocre one, you must keep writing. With practice, comes confidence and strength as a storyteller, and even if I’m not a ‘great writer’, I like to think of myself as a pretty OK storyteller.

My works ultimately span over four generations… something that frankly blows me away, I never thought it would come to that. As you can see, I primarily focus on the ‘post OT’, post ROTJ era. As a rule, I don’t follow the official EU, so with very rare exceptions and the odd referance, you won’t find it here.

I also feature characters that are partnered with the same gender. I don’t believe love or physical attraction is cast in the same mold for everyone, and while it’s never ‘the story’, it is a part of who some of these people are. Per LFL request, relationships of this nature only applies to OC’s.

In later works, there are terms used quite frequently –

“Ma-mere” for mother, “Pay-pa” for father, ‘Yay-so’ for ‘bond mate’ etc.

“Vitty” is short for ‘Shavit’, a well known Star Wars swear, and is the name of the stuffed bantha that once belonged to Pem, and was commandeered by my ‘Ben’.

Other authors have ‘used’ my characters (especially Aubé) such as the very talented Golden Jedi. They have done so with my blessing and permission, because I trust them to do ‘right’ by them. I do allow ‘borrowing’, but please play nice and ask me…
