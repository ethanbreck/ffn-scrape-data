Wow. Alright, I haven't "graced" ff.net with a story for several years. I'm sure that the general public has heaved a large sigh of relief. The things that were on here were general junk, and I'm pleased to have just deleted most of them. The two that remain are Across the Sea, Where the Idiots Run Wild - which was never meant to be a shining beacon of good writing in the first place, as it is a humor fic - and Because - which was far too short to have bombed like my other stories. I honestly appreciate that you liked my stories. I really do. I just hate them myself. : So perhaps you'll see something better from here in the future.
