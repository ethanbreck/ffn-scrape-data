Author has written 20 stories for Enchanted Forest, Zeta Project, Harry Potter, Jack of All Trades, Discworld, Big O, and Persona Series.

Beware ye who wander here. This is the page of a demented old fart who tends to inject large amounts of crack into everything she touches. Has a background in European History, Museum Education, and Folklore, and will use them liberally to disastrous effect.

Generally more inspired in the art variety, fanfiction is easy to start but hard to finish. I draw. I doodle. I scribble. I scrawl. I gesture. Writing happens when I can't spend that much time fleshing out a story- save Yin Yang, the webcomic I've been drawing since February of 2001. Many of my fanfiction is either inspired a doodle, or illustrated.

Currently, most of my artwork can be found at jesidres @ tumblr and deviantart. I also post to AO3.

I own nothing but my imagination and a drawing utensil. You have been warned.
