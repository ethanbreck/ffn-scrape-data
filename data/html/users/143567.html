Author has written 10 stories for Star Wars, Anime X-overs, Dragon Ball Z, Sailor Moon, Ranma, Harry Potter, Captain America, X-overs, Superman, and Misc. Comics.

There isn't too much for me to say really. I love to write. I love expressing myself in a way that is powerful, and provocative. Words are one of the most powerful things on Earth, for it can change minds, create emotion, and cause harm. I write to let my imagination run freely, and for others to enjoy. I try to get all angles, and to get as much detail as possible without choking a person. Although, I must admit that I'm not always successful at that.

I also do some artwork, which you can see at my DeviantART site

As to the name, I'm a former M1A2 Abrams tank crewman (or tanker).

May you enjoy my stories.
