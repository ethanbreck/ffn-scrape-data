What to say about myself? There's not much to say. Guess I'll start with the basics. I am 26 years old and a woman. I graduated from HS in 1998 and got a four-year college degree in 2002.I'm a fan of DBZ and Buffy, but for the life of me I can't write fanfics using those characters. I only write original works. Most of my writings are fantasy, though I do dabble in Science Fiction. I also enjoy reading anything and everything, but fantasy and sci-fi are my favorites. I also enjoy writing poetry. My poems normally have a fantasy setting and tell a story. I prefer poems that tell stories because they have a more solid sense of realism.I also enjoy editing works by other people. I'm fairly good at this considering I have a degree in English. If you want me to edit your work send me an email. I love getting emails.
