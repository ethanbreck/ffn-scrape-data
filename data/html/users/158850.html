Hi, I'm Rhi aka TheOriginalRhi. You may have also known me as labelbasher, Pirate Rhi, or back in the very old days when I first joined up to ff.n, RJ.

All about Me

I solemnly swear never to add a story to my alerts without leaving at least one review! I think it's a rule everyone should go by. Authors spend a lot of time and effort creating stories and it's the best reward ever to have people like their stories and to leave reviews. It's what keeps a lot of us writing. If you like a story enough to add it to your alerts, then you can spare a minute of your time to tell the author that you liked it. However, I will never hold a chapter hostage for reviews, I will post no matter how many reviews I get. Although in saying that, lots of really nice reviews do make me more inclined to write and post quicker, especially for those who are really enjoying my stories.

Hates: Bad drivers, snobby people and windscreen wipers (I can never get them to go at the right speed!), ppl hu spl lyk diz, and authors that hold chapters hostage for reviews.
