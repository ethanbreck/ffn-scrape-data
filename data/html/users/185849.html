Author has written 19 stories for Ranma, Gundam Seed, and Blue Submarine No. 6.

I've been writing fanfics since later 1998 at a slow and occasionally steady pace. So far most of my fics have been Ranma 1/2 or crossovers with that series. If you don't like serious stories in the Ranma setting, you won't like my fics. You should also tear the Hiryu Shoten Ha, Musk Dynasty, Ryugenzawa, Ryu Kumon, and Phoenix Mountain stories (among others) out of your manga and write to Rumiko Takahashi complaining about putting drama in your comedy. If you can't imagine anyone but the Temperamental Fiancee ending up with Ranma, you won't like my fics, either. Constructive C&C is appreciated. Flaming is not. Personal attacks masquerading as reviews will be deleted.

I'd be interested in adding some pre-readers.
