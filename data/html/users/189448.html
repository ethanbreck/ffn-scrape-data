Author has written 47 stories for Charmed, Strong Medicine, Harry Potter, TV X-overs, For the People, General Hospital, Gilmore Girls, and Joan Of Arcadia.
I am the kiwi.
If you read my fics, please review them. Constructive criticism is more than welcome. I want to get better at writing, but I can't do that unless I know what I need work on, so if you don't like my stories, tell me why. Just don't say "uR sToRiE sUxXx!!111" or something along those lines, because that makes you look stupid.
I love listening to music. A lot of my fics are inspired by songs I hear on the radio, so if a title or a line or just the basic theme of one of my stories seems familiar, that's probably why.
I tend to get sidetracked while working on fics. If you like something I wrote, and I haven't updated in a while, don't be afraid to get on my case about it. Review, e-mail, whatever, go ahead, it'll keep me motivated :)
Oh, note about my Charmed fics? Except for one or two, I generally try to forget that season four ever happened. Do not attempt to save me from my denial.

About specific fics:
I wasn't going to do a sequel for "Now That You're Gone", but since I've gotten such an incredible response, I've reconsidered. The sequel is called "All You Thought You Left Behind", and it's up now, so please read & review.
I was planning on doing a sequel to "Etre Aux Anges", but I haven't had time because I've been working on other fics. If you want to see the next part to that one, let me know and I'll start.
