The Author is BACK TO BEING SPORADIC

So it says. I'm an average person with outlandish dreams and a loud-mouthed muse that refuses to shut up and leave me in peace. As such, I'm constantly scribbling about story settings and characters, histories and futures. Is absent-minded, occasionally long-winded, open to many different points of view, and tries his hardest to be honest with others. Also tends to ramble on in the third-person if given the chance. More importantly, knows that every little story written can help sharpen your skill, and that you never quit learning how to do it better.

(8.5.18)
OH MY GOD IT'S ALMOST MY TEN-YEAR BEING A LAZY PIECE OF @#$% ANNIVERSARY!!

For real, though, I somehow completed The Past Concluded after all this time. It's a disjointed mess, no doubt, but I felt like I owed it to everyone to finish it somehow. I felt like I couldn't move on to other stories without putting it behind me, and now it is done. After three revisions and ten long years, it is finally done.

I posted it as a new story named The Past RE-Concluded, and hopefully FF.net won't make an issue of it. I'll be updating it weekly until it's all here.

Thanks for reading, everyone.

(10.31.08)
The only thing really scary about this particular Halloween is how long it's been since I last updated. But, as people have said since time immemorial: Life Intervenes. So! With that said, there is little more to mention regarding the status of affairs surrounding my writing. I've puttered about with several story ideas, even got a few off the ground and into text that may or may not go ignored. Sooner or later The Past Concluded will be finished, and something's gotta become the new king of the hill. It will likely be my Tactics novel and my first step in that fandom. A few one-shots could see life here as well, provided I can get them to make sense. Yeah. So. That's about all there is, then. We'll stop here.
