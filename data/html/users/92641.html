Author has written 3 stories for Card Captor Sakura, and Naruto.

Oh wow, look at this account. I wrote my first fanfiction back in 2002. You know what I was doing in 2002? I was like, a Sophomore in high school I'm in college now, currently with an AA degree and working on a BA, and well, needless to say since 2002 I have had LOTS of writing classes since, and the very idea of even TRYING to read these fanfiction I wrote causing a giant, epic facepalm.

For fans of these fics, I will tell you now, I am sorry. I very likely will NEVER write a sequel to Cherry Blossoms and Magic. I don't even want to look at it ever again, but since people keep faving it, I'm not deleting it. I'm alright with the Small Meets Big fic, but I don't know if I'll ever be able to continue it. Maybe if one day after I get ahold of the CCS anime and rewatch it all so I refresh my memory, and MAYBE if I have a co-author and I rewrite everything that has been written so far will I continue it. That's a lot of IF's. If you like my CCS Small Meets Big enough, and want to be a co-author of sorts and help me through it, send me a message and I'll look at your fics and reply if I like your style. If not, don't feel too hurt if I don't reply back.

I'm now seriously trying my hand at writing a fic for the first time in over 5 years. This is for various reasons, mainly because I have an idea with the Naruto world I wanna try out, mainly because I need to stretch my writing muscles, and mainly because I'm curious as to if I can write Sauske and Sakura, one of my fave manga pairings.

If you like my story though, please comment! They're really great motivators, and might cause me to persue other plot bunnies that hop across my mind.
