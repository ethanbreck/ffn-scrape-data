Roadtrip the Pickpocket
hide bio
PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 08-21-02, id: 256532, Profile Updated: 03-25-09
Author has written 9 stories for Newsies.

Basically, I’ve decided to give my profile and my stories a reboot. I am 21 years old and my birthday is in October. I’m the type of person who basically has a giant shell around them and I’m extremely private. This is pretty much my way of trying to crack the shell and open it a little. Hopefully I don’t become like Humpty Dumpty.

Anyways, my taste in music, movies, tv shows, and books is pretty much random. For movies I like anything from action movies like Die Hard and Lethal Weapon to romantic comedies like Fools Rush In. I also like pretty much anything horror, mystery, or supernatural related. I am also a huge fan of musicals like Jesus Christ Superstar, Rocky Horror Picture Show, Shock Treatment, and Mamma Mia!, among others. One of my new favorite musicals is Repo! The Genetic Opera. Of course, there’s also Newsies, which I’ve liked since pretty much the first time I watched it.

The television shows I watch are just as random. Though not usually my type of show, I enjoy watching Gossip Girl for some reason. For other shows still on the air, I watch Saving Grace, Heroes, Fringe, Reaper, Lost, Supernatural, Dollhouse, Eureka, Sanctuary, and Psych. Some of the cancelled shows that I still love to watch are Angel, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Charmed, Joan of Arcadia, Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s The Lost World, Invasion, Moonlight, and Dark Angel. I don’t watch many reality shows but I do enjoy American Idol and Big Brother.

Right now I’m on something of a superhero kick as I’ve been watching Heroes, Dr. Horrible’s Sing-along Blog, and have just bought a book with a collection of superhero stories that aren’t exactly expected. I'm also thinking of toying with the reality show idea again. As I said before, I’m planning on playing around with the stories that I have on here, rewriting them and hopefully finishing them and making them better. I’m planning on leaving the stories that are one-shots (Last Call Casualty and The Best Independence Day) and I might add more to Roadtrip’s Revenge. Some stories will be deleted and some will just be reworked. If anyone would like to talk or give me feedback or anything send me a private message or an e-mail. I check my e-mail pretty much daily so I will try to send something back as soon as possible.
