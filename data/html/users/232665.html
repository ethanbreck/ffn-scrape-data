Hello, Bonjour, Guten Tag!
I am a 15 year old German female living in England. I write Lord of the Rings fiction, and my favourite characters are Legolas and Gimli (especially Gimli) so nearly all my stories will have lots of them in it.
I also need to point out that I write as much in-canon as I can (I have not yet read Unfinished Tales or the Silmarillion).
I do not like slash- LotR is about friendship. Full stop. Although you people who like it I don't mind.
Please give me constructive reviews that help me. Suggestions, comments about my spelling and grammar (always striving to improve it- I want an A-A* i my English GCSE. If I do 'll give u guys half the credit!) and a few compliments maybe.
My reading list includes J.R.R. Tolien's works of literature (all of them), Terry Pratchett's Discworld series, Douglas Adams and the Hitchhikers Guide To The Universe, and J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter books.
Danke and please remember English is not my first language!
