Bio:

My name is Lisa Gomez, and I'm a very mature 15 year old. I love writing fanfics and stories, and am an aspiring best-selling writer. My absolute best dream is to become a famous singer. My other more known pen name is VegetaGokuLover.

Favorite Anime:

Besides DragonBallZ, Yu Yu Hakusho is the best and my most favorite Anime. I watch the show everyday and even buy some of the DVD's. My gallery of pictures continue to grow, and my love for this show continues to grow as well. I really love all the characters, there all so complex. I don't have one least favorite character, as in DBZ my least fav is Gohan. Finally an anime where I think everyone rocks.

Favorite Character:

My most favorite character in YYH is Kuwabara. I love him so much! He has a very massive ego, a lot of pride, and he distributes a lot of humor to the anime. His ever-growing hatred of Hiei is great. I love watching them argue! I know that, whatever anyone says, Kuwabara and Yusuke are best friends. I bet they really care about eachother. Their rivalry is what caught my eye the first time I watched this show, and I would love to see them fight one day! All in all, I think Kuwabara's the most unique character in this show.
