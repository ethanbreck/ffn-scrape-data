Hey, my name is Laura, and I'm from South Carolina. I am in the 9th grade and am currently involved in marching band, academic team, and tsa(I'm an officer!)In the fall, I play soccer(football for you non-Americans :)! Anyways, I know many of you have noticed the serious lack of updates lately and I am terribly sorry about that! Band is taking over my life(Mon,Tues,Thurs,Fri,and Sat)!

In my off hours(not that there are any), I write fanfiction and host a web page(or try!) My former pename was Queencaps, non HP fics can be found there now. I know I am insane I can't finish one fic before starting another! My muses tend to come and go as they please.

I am so sorry I haven't had time to update anything lately! This week I have exams too.. But just know that I am going to try and update Midnight Rendezvous on Saturday! Thanks so much for bearing with me through all of this!

Please be sure and check out my latest fic, The Perfect Crime At the beginning somebody's(not going ot tell you!) parents die. Will Hermione with the help of a Deatheaters son be able to convict the most unlikely suspect of them all!?

I have made a new message board for my web page; it has several helpful features you might want to look into: such as a place to have your fic beta read and discussion.

All of my stories are still being written. None of them are completed(yet!). So please do review :)I want my writing to improve and if you see anything wrong feel free to comment! Flames are welcome as long as they are critisism and not just "your story sucks". But anyhoo, Puh-leez review :)
