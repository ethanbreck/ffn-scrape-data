Author has written 18 stories for X-Men, and X-Men: Evolution.

Long gone are the days when fan fiction was a do-able past time for me. Yet, I enjoy and, yes, still entertain the thought, the notion that my understanding of my favorite characters actually matters. To bring them to life, albeit, in a not-so-grandiose way has been a pleasure. Alas, I am no Hemmingway, no Toni Morrison, no Higgins-Clark.

I maintain a website that is dedicated to my favorite X-Character, Storm. It consumes some of my time, but not most of it. Sounds Like a Storm's Comin -- for Storm fans who can't get enough of the Windrider. ;)
