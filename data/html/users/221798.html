Author has written 7 stories for Harry Potter, and Phantom of the Opera.

Newly married, almost 25 year old, scribbling odd ramblings about beloved characters. Yep, my birthday is January 30th and yes, I do have green eyes. I win. Easy halloween costumes for me. Especially seeing what my husband looks like. It's rather weird. Tall (6'-1"), dark-ish, skinny, hunch shouldered (too much time on the computer) with a beaky nose and glasses . . . best of both worlds and yep, he's all mine. You should see the costume I made him! We won a Tri-wizard Cup lamp at a Harry Potter costume contest. All he had to do was stride around and glare while I passed out business cards. Even made a few small children cry.
