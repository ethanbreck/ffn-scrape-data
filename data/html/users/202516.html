Author has written 11 stories for G-Gundam, Saiyuki, Hana Yori Dango, Yu Yu Hakusho, and Dark Angel.

So, obviously, from the favorite stories list, the anime obsession is back - blame the RK live action movies.

I'm going a hundred miles per minute with Rurouni Kenshin (I swear, Sato Takeru is the perfect Himura Kenshin; so perfect that I think I'm a little bit more than obsessed. I named my labrador/chow/spitz/husky mix after him). I still hate the OVAs, and still for the same reasons: I thought it was demeaning Kaoru, and her love for Kenshin, and Kenshin married Kaoru just because she was there. And I hate, hate, hate it, because it wasn't at all the message of the manga and even the anime.

Following close behind is Gundam Wing (still Heero Yuy and Relena Peacecraft for me, after all these years. And by all these years, I mean, all these past SEVENTEEN years). Having rewatched some of the episodes in YT, it amazed me how all the political issues went right above my head back then. I blame Heero's eyes and arms.

I'm also still in love with Sesshomaru being in love with Kagome, because I am obstinate like that. This one is just because I don't like how Kagome got hurt all the time because of Inuyasha's carelessness with her feelings, and him being reckless all the effing time. But I do love Inuyasha as a character - he has so much room for growth.

I do hope to get my muse back - I've been loading up on fics just so I can get inspiration to write, but the drive just isn't there yet.

And this is it for me for now.
