Writing is my heart and soul. This is the only activity that I have commited myself to for a period longer than two years (well, I was on a swim team for six years, but that's another story). I got into Fanfiction writing during the winter of 2000, when I started Gundam Wing fics under a number of pennames.
My writing interests include Harry Potter, Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon fiction and I think Dragon Ball Z is growing on me. So, please, read, review and feel free to send me an email at the above mentioned addy. I look forward to hearing from you.
