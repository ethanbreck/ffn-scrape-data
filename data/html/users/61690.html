About Me

To be honest, I'm not quite sure what to put here since - I think - most of what you might want to know about me can be extrapolated from the list of stories I have published so far.

But I suppose a bit of redundancy can't hurt: I have been into fanfiction for a number of years now (a little over a decade, on and off), and am active in a number of fandoms, mainly Star Wars, One Piece and Final Fantasy. I do, however, follow many more than those, having recently rekindled my love for The X-Files (a few one shots may follow).

While I don't think that my writing style is anything to be proud of (I am not a native English speaker, after all), I especially enjoy well written fiction. Too many grammatical and orthographical mistakes can easily turn me off, just like protagonists acting too out of character.

Last but not least: I'm a compulsive writer, which means that I do not follow a strict writing schedule but rather sit down when I feel like it. Then, however, I tend to finish what I start, often resulting in 10-14 hour writing marathons. What you receive as an update then is mostly what I publish at the end of that night, so the occasional mistake or two may make it in there. To add to that: I'm an educator by trade (an M.A. in English/English Lit and Linguistics being the basis for my professional life) and have precious little free time, much less than I would like. Hence, I am usually quite busy during the semester and often have a hard time squeezing in writing hours.

As most people on here, I write because I feel the urge to, because I'd like to see my thoughts in writing and want to find out how they will develop once I free them onto the digital page. That is, of course, not to say that I don't care for reviews: like all writers, I am quite flattered and encouraged by any feedback I am given. So, please, always feel free to let me know what you think.
