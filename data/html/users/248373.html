These are potential idea that I may or may not be working on in the future. They are open for adoption to those who feel like writing something by themselves. XD To be honest, I want to read them more than to write them.

1. Pieces of Heart (M) - Naruto Fandom, AU, SasuSaku, Multichaptered

Uchiha Sasuke (24) vowed to become a prestigious psychiatrist so he can save his brother from being locked in the asylum for the rest of his life. What he did not expect, however, is to meet Sakura during his internship in Konoha Mental Hospital who quickly captivates his attention to the point of annoyance. But who'd have thought that Sakura, the dangerously skinny, long pink-haired, and uberly moody young woman with striking green eyes and angelic face is a highly monitored patient. Now Sasuke has to deal with the fact that the woman he possibly love... has at least three different personalities.

2. Tempered (M) - Naruto Fandom, AU, SasuSaku NaruHina, Multichaptered

This place is The Facility, where all those genetically modified killing machines are made and trained. This is where failure means death and running away is not an option. Well... let see how a pink haired undead, a dark haired demon, a blonde kyuubi, and a x-ray visioned girl could managed to break all those rules.

3. Shinigami (T/M) - Naruto Fandom, AU, SasuSaku, Miniseries

Inspired by "The World Ends With You". The rules are simple: survive for 7 days and your wish will be granted, those who fail will be erased. When Sasuke woke up with no memories, he was in for the death game. The only way out is to trust a pinknette who claimed they are both already dead.

4. Blacklisted (M) - Naruto Fandom, AU, SasuSaku, ShikaIno

Alternate Universe. Based on true story. Sakura, Ino, and Shikamaru are the inseparable trio in Konoha University, but that perfect world is about to change. Shikamaru was found dead on the ground after jumping from the 4th Floor balcony of the University. Worse, the media accused him of stabbing a professor before he committed the 'suicide'. Ino was devastated, but Sakura believe none of it. She was sure that Shikamaru was the victim. There is a dark and twisted conspiracy going on the University that rooted deep into the goverment. Angry and vengeful, Sakura realized her only chance to clear her bestfriend's name is by making a deal with the powerful Uchiha family, while being completely aware that the family has their own hidden agenda. That's how she met Uchiha Sasuke, and that's how her peaceful life turned into a life-threatening situation. The situation got worse when the people behind Shikamaru's death had started to target her back...

5. Bloodlines (M) - Naruto Fandom. Semi-AU, SasuSaku, Multichapterd

An underground organization collects the possesors of bloodline limits in order to create the ultimate ninja army through genetic engineering. When Hinata Hyuuga went missing, Konoha-gakure could no longer turn blind eye to the matter. Sakura, a newly promoted ANBU medic-nin, is sent on a mission to infiltrate the organization and work as a double agent. But to enter the circle, she needs to bring an offering. And what would be a better sacrifice than the missing-nin Uchiha Sasuke, her long-lost ex-teammate?
