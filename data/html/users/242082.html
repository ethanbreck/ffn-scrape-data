My name's Katie. Full time college student, part time secretary and babysitter. I love most of what I do.

I'm a huge TV and movie junkie. I go for the couples that are destined from the beginning. And usually the couples that take the hardest/most agonizing path.

One Tree Hill used to be it for me, but now I probably hate it more than anything. I feel bitter and betrayed, and that's all I have to say about that. My fics will get finished, I absolutely promise that, but how fast... that, I have no idea. You all are amazing for the continual support, and I owe it to each and every one of you. I know how it feels to have an author quit on you.

I will always have love for Lucas and Peyton, and they will always hold a special piece of my heart... but it's not the same. It'll never be again. Their ending was beautiful, though, and it was everything I ever wanted for them.

But now I'm head over heels in love with Glee. Will and Emma hold my heart, I'm uber obsessed, but I like every character so much, and a few other couplings as well. Lost is my favorite show, but with it winding down, and mindfcking me every chance it gets, I feel I would ruin Jack and Kate - and they are sacred in my heart.
