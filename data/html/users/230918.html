Author has written 14 stories for Rurouni Kenshin, Phantom of the Opera, Gundam Wing/AC, Avatar: Last Airbender, and Harry Potter.

Writing is a release. It's where I put my stress or how I share what amuses me. I've realized that I like to read stories with happy endings and satisfying resolutions, but my writing tends to be a bit more melancholy. Canon is always my inspiration. Sometimes it inspires me to explore something more deeply. More often inspires me to fix something that I found to be broken.

While my writing is therapeutic, I do always try to finish projects. At the moment I feel satisfied with what I've done.
