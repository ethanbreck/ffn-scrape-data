Author has written 5 stories for Slayers, Silent Hill, Tenchi Muyo, Love Hina, and Naruto.

The story Lucky Numbers was originally published by an alternate pseudonym of mine, The Drones. So while you may have seen it once up under a different name, I can assure you that I and him are the same person.

Meanwhile, in October 2008...

Anyway. So my laptop died a while back, taking with it the majority of my writing. No huge loss. In the off-chance that anyone is still being strung along and waiting (im)patiently for the next chapter of WBTCD, you can stop now. It's not coming. Nevermind that it was eaten, but I haven't even so much as looked at the Naruto manga or watched a single episode since like Christmas circa 2006. I'm mostly going to file that story in the, "What the heck was I thinking when I wrote this?" dossier, where it can nest snug and fat alongside just about every other fanfic I've written.

The other folder is titled, "I know what I was thinking and yet I still went and wrote it anyway because I am an imbecile," for the curious. So yeah. Thanks again for coming out, and we'll see you again next year when I don't have anything else for you to read again. Drive safely.
