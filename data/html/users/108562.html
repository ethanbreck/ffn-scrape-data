Author Note:

So, I know that I haven't updated in a long time.

No, I'm not dead.

However...

In all honesty, writing is not coming to me very easily right now. Almost every time I try to write, it’s just so forced. I want more than anything to be able to post chapters and finish everything I’ve started so that you readers can gain from it. However, right now I can’t feel good about my writing and when that happens I can only feel bad about myself. That’s why I’ve decided to put everything on hold for now and possibly for good.

Don’t worry; I am trying to finish up what I do have written so that I can post it and I don’t think that I’m done as a writer for good. I’ve still got so many ideas; I just can’t handle it right now. I know how frustrating it is to never know the ending of a story and if it’s really a priority, feel free to contact me and I’ll be happy to explain all the details of the rest of any story I’ve written as much as I can.

What I've Got Written And/Or Am Trying to Finish Right Now

Gundam Wing: I've got a chapter or two of Climbing Steep, Falling Hard and I started a new story but may not finish it.

Digimon: I'm trying desperately to finish Discovering College and I have most of the Taito chapter for Everyone Has a Poker Face.

I must extend my greatest apologies to any readers I have left; I truly feel that I have failed but I must do what’s best for me.
