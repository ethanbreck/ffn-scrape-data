Updated: February 17, 2008

Hello all! Hm, about me. Well, I live in the Dallas, Texas area and am a shipping manager for a very high-end retail clothing store. (Trust me, high-end just means overpriced.) I'm married and have a gorgeous three year old daughter.

My current anime obsession is Naruto. My current fanfic obsession is the Kakashi/Older Sakura pairing. If that happened in the manga/anime I would squeal like an idiot. But that's probably just wishful thinking.

Sorry to those who were reading my Rurouni Kenshin fanfics. I'll finish them eventually, but unfortunately I just don't have that much interest anymore. Most stories will probably wrap up in one or two more chapters. After that, I'll just work on Naruto fics.

The Last Story Kay-san will write:

Solace - Kakashi was supposed to accompany her, but due to the flu she's now on this mission with Gai. Is he really just a loud mouth taijutsu user or is there more to him? (Gai/Sakura, side story to 'Sex Therapist'.)

After 'Solace' I'm just going to finish up my unfinished things. I do have another penname that I'll keep writing under. I don't know what it is, but my stories have always been sort of wholesome, anyone can read sort of things. And dang it, I finally have the courage to pump out some more adult things and I want to do it! I just fret about it so much more when it's posted under this penname. Hell, I almost hyperventilated after posting 'Sex Therapist.' So, if there was a synopses on here that you wanted to see written, just PM me and I'll tell you what penname to look under for it.

Well, that's it from me! Hope to see you in reviews! Hugs & Kisses!
