Author has written 7 stories for Angel, Brittas Empire, NCIS, Man From UNCLE, and Thunderbirds.

I'm a 30ish fanfic author from the UK with dubious health, well I say author but normally I get too distracted by reading other people's wonderful fics :) I read far to many fandoms to be listed here but write in only a few though I'm trying to expand.
If there are any mistakes in my fic please tell me, I am constantly editing things as I go and sometimes I change something and forget a previous reference to it making me contradict myself.

I started off with using this account for Slash fic and had an alternate ID for some Gen fic at http://www.fanfiction.net/twistedimagination However I soon got far too lazy to keep things separate and there are some fics that counts as Gen listed on this ID for various reasons that everyone would find very boring.

Mini guide to my fanfiction on both profiles in written order.

Second Chances: Slash. My first ever fanfic and never completed *hangs head in shame*. Scheduled for removal August 2018. Removed March 2019

When Darkness Falls: Slash. My first LOTR fic very AU and never finished. Scheduled for removal in August 2018. Removed March 2019

Middle Earth Adventure: Mary & Marty Sue parody that has been cancelled for good. Written as Twisted Imagination. Removed August 2018

Alternate Beginnings: Gen. Complete. An AU based around the first episode of the series SeaQuest DSV. Written as Twisted Imagination.

The Angel series: Both Gen. Both Complete. There are currently two of them there was originally supposed a third that would be slash but now long abandoned.

Betrayed: Slash and Het. Complete. First Atlantis fic. Scheduled for removal August 2018. Removed March 2019

The Brittas Empire story: Complete. Been watching too many episodes on DVD recently. Missing scene from an episode with Tim/Gavin. Might write some others.

Echoes of the Past: Gen or maybe slightly pre-slash if you really squint at it. Complete. First NCIS story I've tried to write, I think it needs a bit more work but I need to take a break from it atm. There is, I suppose, room for a sequel when my brain recharges and inspiration strikes.

Past Lives, New Beginnings: Very mild slash. Complete. This is a first in two ways. My first fic using Man from U.N.C.L.E. and my first crossover fic. It is a "What if?" story that crosses NCIS and Man and from UNCLE by using the line from the NCIS Meat Puzzle episode "What did Ducky look like when he was younger?" "Illya Kuryakin."

Splintered Facets of the Mind: Gen. Complete. First Thunderbirds fic, which is something I never thought I'd be saying.. the after effects of a traumatic event have far unforeseen and far reaching consequences.

In the Still of the Night: Gen/Het. Complete. Another Thunderbirds fic. Set long before the series begins.
