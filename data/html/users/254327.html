UPDATE 3/15/07 I'm terribly sorry to all that have read my Unsettled Emotions fic and been disappointed because of its incompletion. I thank all of those who enjoyed and reviewed this story, and humbly apologize as well. UPDATE 10-14-09 I apologize to those who once read my fan fiction. I had planned on finishing Unsettled Emotions, but my life since entering college, back in 2005, never seems to allow for it. I honestly rarely read more than textbooks these days, let alone pleasure reading or writing fan fiction. Unsettled Emotions will probably never be finished. If I was to finish it, it would have to be entirely revamped since my writing style has changed, and I would like to think become more grammatically correct. To those of you who were fans of it: thank you so much for your support.
