Dear Readers,

I have to say, I very nearly forgot I had fics on FF.N until I recieved a few reviews, and hadn't realized that the last 9 chapters of "What I Need" had somehow gotten lost between all the reformats (thank god for cd backups, yeah?) and re-uploads -- as it got booted when FF.N removed everything that was rated R and asked us to repost -- and that people were actually waiting for me to finish it. Bit of a shock, that, as I've never considered myself much of a writer, just had some plot bunnies that needed getting rid of, hence, the fic. So for you all who've been waiting, for those of you that have read my stuff and not run in fear . . . The last 9 chapters of "What I Need" are up, in the same form I wrote them in four years ago. Or...something that extremely long ago. 3 maybe. Who knows what the future will bring, although I have to admit that it's likely I won't be writing more fanfic, as my job as a graphic artist has gotten me working too much to write. I do have a few ideas though, and if I get time, I'll post them up.

Thanks again!

:Ana:
