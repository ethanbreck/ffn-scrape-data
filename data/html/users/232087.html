Stories

Well after a long stint of not writing, I've finally gotten back into my old most loved hobby. Most of my older stories however are not likely to be continued as

1. I lost the original drafts and layouts of how I wanted them to go when my computer crashed.

2. Most of my muse unfortunately fled with the crash of my computer. I can't write what I don't feel a passion for.

Granted that's not to say I will NEVER continue them. Some of them I might pull down and just start from scratch and re-write, though that won't be for a long while seeing as Olicity at the moment has my complete attention (yes, they even beat out my love for Twilight *gasp*). So for the moment I only have two stories going on.

Sooner or Later - Olicity drabbles and one-shots. This will be randomly updated seeing as they'll come up as I get inspiration. I am however accepting prompts if anyone wants to see how I lay out a specific situation. Just leave me a PM, and I'll let you know if I think it's totally doable!

A Whisper of Hope - A more straight forward story, however at the moment I am having conflicting choices of just HOW I want the story to entirely layout. I have several branches that I could go down. Plus while I love what I have so far, I'm not entirely pleased with the writing style I have going for it. The next few chapters could see a difference of writing once I decide what's going down when and where.
