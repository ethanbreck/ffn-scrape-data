For the moment, I exist, so I live for the moment. ... Wow. That's pretentious, isn't it? In my defence, this is an old account. Not that that makes it any better. I can't believe I wrote that... It's not even funny!

I've only just recently started writing again, because I spend a lot of my time just reading instead. And sadly, a lot of the time I don't review, so I won't complain about any lack there. Though I won't deny that I do like my ego stroked. Who doesn't?
