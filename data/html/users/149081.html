Hello! My pen name is John Smith, and thirteen years after (apparently) setting it to that, I have no idea why.

I write because I enjoy writing. It's one of my favorite hobbies and very little feels better than producing something with your own hands and intellect and then being able to share it with the world. It's for this reason that I don't believe in flaming, ever. I don't even believe in critiquing art unless people directly and seriously ask for critique. If it's a hobby and not a job, we should be doing it because we love it and not for others. /soapbox

I'm a very friendly person and love to chat with people. Feel free to contact me for any reason, even if it's just to vent about something in your life to an absolute stranger. That's coo. I don't think I'd be good at collaboration because I tend to cycle out of fandoms too quickly to finish my more long term fic ideas (most of which are not posted ahaha). But I guess I could try.
