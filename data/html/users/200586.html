Hmmm...where to start. Well, I HUGE anime fan for quite a while now. I started out writing Ranma 1/2 fics, which i used to have a whole webpage dedicated tto. I'm a pro Ranma and Akane kind of person, so most of my fics are about them. However, I simply love Gundam Wing and out of everyone in the series I love Duo. I'm fairly new to writing Gundam stuff, but I love to do it. You might notice that most of my stories are 10 years plus old. I took a really long hiatus. I am hoping my current work is much better then my old stuff. I do have a grammar issue and sometimes my mind goes faster then how fast i could type. From the time i started to write to now I got married moved 5 or so times once from NJ to NC and had a kid. Life can get in the way bbutI found myself reading fan fics again and the ideas started to flow. I will eventually go back and revisit some mt other stories and edit then finish them up. That is just a little about me. Enjoy!
