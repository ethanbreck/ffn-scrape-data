November 20th, 2018

Some of you are aware that my mother died in May. I have spent two years dealing with everything leading up to her death and there is still residual things having to be dealt with from all of that. I am trying to move on, take my baby steps, and learn the new normal now.

However, it seems that just when I have developed a little bit of healing on that great, festering, wound known as grief, it all got ripped off for me again on the night of the 18th with the death of my dearly, beloved, elderly cat.

I'll be frank. I am not doing well. I am exhausted. I feel like a pinball in a very bad machine. I am taking a longer break. So I apologize to the readers of The Healer. I am sorry to leave it hanging. Forgive me for that. I have to deal with this monster called grief.
