Author has written 10 stories for Tenchi Muyo, Love Hina, Full Metal Panic, Detective Conan/Case Closed, Karin, Tsukihime, Haruhi Suzumiya series, School Rumble, and Higurashi/Umineko series.

Hello there everyone. Whether you may be a fan of my work or just visiting to see who I am or such, I thank you for dropping by.

I am a very big fan of anime, manga and video games. However, my inspirational moments for writing aren't that plentiful and I end up leaving stories hanging. Many apologies to all my fans for stories that have been neglected or abandoned.

Currently I am working on three titles, "If For But A Single Day..." "Behind the Mask" "Broken Vows". These are stories for the School Rumble anime, Karin manga, and Tsukihime game, respectively. Apologies, I have left "Behind the Mask" and "Broken Vows" hanging, I will get back to those in time.

I don't have any idea what I want to do currently. I don't especially care to finish schooling, but I may at some point go back. I am halfway done with college and stopped. Throughout my schooling, however, I have taken a number of English classes including a few Creative Writing courses. I found those to be amongst my favorite of all my classes I've ever taken.

My philosophy on life is 'Enjoy life to its fullest. Live in the moment.' Basically, don't worry about what has happened or what's to come. Those things don't matter, what matters is what is happening now and how you can have fun to your fullest.

Again, thanks to all my fans and readers alike who have come by. Look for updates to the stories I am writing in due time. I'll get to finishing them someday. Thanks for all the comments, reviews, and everything else. It is from these that true refined works of art may we find.
