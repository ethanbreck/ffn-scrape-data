Hi! I'm Anime Leo and I'm from CA. I'm pretty busy these days (though I know that's not a good enough excuse) so I won't be updating anything much. Even if I do, it won't be very periodical. VERY VERY sorry about that! I hate it when other people do that, but since I do it too, I forgive them. Lol.

So...on to a little about my anime preferences...

I like (in alphabetical order)...

Ayashi no Ceres
Shuro was cool. And Yuuhi's steel chopsticks...those were fun.

Bleach
I used to like this one a lot, but now it's all fight fight fight with little character development. I mean, Ichigo just always somehow happens to get more strength when fighting a tougher opponent. O well, still good to read. I stopped watching. It's too behind...

CCS
I like CLAMP's style (as in CCS and Chobits and such...I'm not really into X/1999 style...)

Code Geass
I've only just started season one recently, but it's a pretty interesting concept.

Digimon
Seasons 1 and 2 rule! and though I'm not crazy about it (partially because they no longer show it on TV), I'm crazy about some of the couples...hehehe... And I like Yamato. Haha. Except I hate him for the whole Sora thing. GRRRRRRRRR.

Fullmetal Alchemist
SOOOOOOOOO COOOOOL. I love the characters in it. The more I think about it, the better I think the manga is compared to the anime. You haven't seen anything if you've only watched it. Go read it!!

Fushigi Yuugi
No da!

Marmalade Boy
Short and sweet.

NARUTO!
Love this anime. Though I think it's hard to write fanfics for it. But it's awesome! I could start rambling on about it for hours... Everyone in Konoha is so great. And you just gotta love Kakashi. If you've never tried it, go read/watch it! Except I hate fillers...which is why I read instead of watch!

Ouran High School Host Club
What more is there to say but cute and hilarious!

Tsubasa
Cuz there's Sakura and Syaoran in it. And it's CLAMP.

Zettai Kareshi (Absolute Boyfriend)
It was amusing and cute and sweet. Except for the ending... But good none-the-less.

Now on to pairings...

Syaoran + Sakura (CCS)
Cuz they're cute together.

Eriol + Tomoyo (CCS)
Cuz they're also cute together. And their personalities match.

Taito/Yamachi (Digimon)
Cuz it's PERFECTION! And it's my other obsession (besides Naruto). There's just not enough out there. Sigh. And I really have no preference between Taito and Yamachi... Both are great, as long as they're together (haha).

TK + Kari (Digimon)
Dunno...there's just something about the Yagami & Ishida/Takaishi blood...

I also love video games. Big Nintendo fan, hehe! Super Smash Bros. series is the my favorite! I also like Tekken 5, Kingdom Hearts, CTR (Crash Bandicoot games are fun), DDR, Guitar Hero, Prince of Persia, God of War, Zelda: Twilight Princess...the list goes on. :P

So that's a bit about me. Now you can go read my fics! Hope you like them! And if you do read them, please leave a review! Thanks!
