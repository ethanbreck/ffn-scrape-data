~5/28/08~

Wow. That's all I can say right now. I've been a member since 2001 and my last "update" was in 2004? That's pretty sad. As you can see (and as my last update pointed out), I've stopped writing fan fiction. However, I haven't completely stopped writing because it's definitely a passion of mine (although I've had some pretty major writer's block for the past year or so). I haven't visted ff.net for some time now, but when I finally did I realized how much I missed it. It was nice reminiscing (visiting my favorite authors' pages and rereading old stories) and even reading over my old stories (I had a lot more posted when I first joined ff.net, but now all that remains are three DBZ fics I wrote when I was 12 or 13, lol. Imagine that), and through doing so I've realized that I've lost something (in my writing) over the years. I don't quite know what it is, but I'm determined to find out because I think it'll help get my creative juices flowing (hopefully). Even though I wrote those fics so long ago, there was something about them that made me stop after I read them and think, "Wow. I had a lot more potential than I thought. This really isn't half bad." Like I said, I wrote that stuff when I was 12 or 13... I'm 19, almost 20 now, and it amazes me how consistent and passionate I was about writing back then. I have improved immensely over the years, but sadly I don't have the same reaction to my writing now (probably because all I write anymore is essays for school! Bleh...).

Anyway, I doubt anyone's reading this right now, but maybe, just maybe, I'll happen to get inspiration for something, anything (either for ff.net or fictionpress.com). I'm not into anime/manga as much as I used to be, but from time to time I'll still watch or read something (DBZ is WAAAY out of the picture now... but I still have some favorites--mainly Fruits Basket). The last idea I had for a fan fic was a Kisa and Haru one shot, but that was back in my junior year of high school, lol XD (I'm a sophomore in college now... Weird). I think it would have turned out fairly well if I had fully developed the idea, but who knows if the story will ever get on paper or to the computer.

Enough of my rambling! I'll do my best to put forth something worthy enough to post, but really my aim is just to start writing again. Sure, it makes me happy when people review my work, but what I need most is constructive criticism. That's all for now, I guess. Whether I update in a month, a year, a few years, or even never XD... I've enjoyed being a part of the ff.net community (or at least what it used to be). Sayonara!

Krazy Brunette

~2/17/04~

Hey, hey! It's been... a long time! XD Lol. Sorry to inform all of you, but I'm not here for updates (Boo hoo, sob sob). Like I've said so many times before, school has taken over my life x_x. Also, my liking for writing fan fiction is slowly diminishing... due to the fact that I'm using my creativeness for my ideas to write original stories. I've removed all of my writings except for three, just for the sake of sparing myself the embarassment of looking at my past stories and noticing how horrid they were. If I ever get the chance or have the time, I'll try to finish "Someone to Love" though I have no intentions of continuing it. Who knows... maybe I'll come out with something new before or during summer vacation! GASP! Until then...
