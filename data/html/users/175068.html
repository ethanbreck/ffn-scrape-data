Location: Fairborn, Ohio

.: NEWS :.

I am still with my amazing man, John. We recently celebrated our four year anniversary back in May.

In June, John graduated with his Bachelors degree in Geography.

Currently I am student teaching at a middle school and I will be graduating with my Masters degree in November.

.: Physical Profile :.

Still a shorty at 5'4". I am currently concentrating on growing my hair back out now. I'm still going with the layered look and I have not colored my hair since November of 2004 and I'm happy with it. My eyes change shades of brown depending on the mood I am in. I've also lost about 5 pounds since I graduated from college the first time.

Stress is a big part of my life, but you would never know. I'm the one who hops up and down even when I'm really angry. I'm that teacher that parents wish they would have had when they were middle schoolers. I love to smile as much as possible. I also get upset and quiet though, too.
