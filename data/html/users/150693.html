I don't write slash.

I don't write Mary Sues.

I write in my free time simply because I enjoy it, and hopefully some others will enjoy what I write as well.

I mostly write wrestling fic, though other genres may show up as well- Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Angel, Smallville, Batman, Harry Potter, Supernatural, and others. I like writing crossovers.

Favorite pairings: Eve/Seth Rollins, Eve/Dean Ambrose, Eve/CM Punk, Lita/Randy Orton. Kaitlyn's been popping up in a lot of fic lately, and my top pairings for her are Roman Reigns, Dolph Ziggler, or Dean Ambrose. I also have a soft spot for Lita/Chris Jericho.

I also enjoy Chloe-centric Smallville fic (and Chloe/Bruce Wayne crossovers) as well as Hermione-centric Harry Potter fic. (I enjoy multiple pairings, including crossovers, all of them non-canon).
