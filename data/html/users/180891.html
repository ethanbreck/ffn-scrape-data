Hello everyone. No, I'm not dead. (though I could see how you may have considered it due to the fact that I haven't posted anything in, literally, a year) Well, I decided that I would hold of on posting all my fics. The problem was that I couldn't update them as often as I wanted so I just stopped all together. I may begin posting soon, I may not. I have not made a decision yet. In any case, feel free to contact me (via the above information). I love input.

I have recently changed my pen name from DeamonKatte to Azzelya.

Some might argue that I have too much free time. How wrong they are. I never have enough time. Whether it's fanfics or what not. But I still try to keep my fanfic reading/writing/posting going. (although most of you probably know that I'm not too good about posting)

I have seen numerous anime including Gundam Wing, Trigun, Escaflowne, Ranma 1/2, Outlaw Star, Rurouni Kenshin, Fushigi Yuugi, Slayers, Ayashi no Ceres, Oh My Goddess, and Inu Yasha. I also love all the Final Fantasy games though I have not yet played X.

Well that's about it for now. Enjoy!~

~Azzelya
