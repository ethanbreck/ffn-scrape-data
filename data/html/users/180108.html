Hello, I'm FEARMEfrancis.

I'm new to writing, and have a VERY short attention span, so my updates will be somewhat sporadic. I'll apologize now.

I normally stick to betaing. If you see a grammar or punctuation mishap, PLEASE DON'T JUST TELL ME THERE IS A MISTAKE, SEND ME THE LINE! This also goes for any stories that I beta. That way I can try to fix it.

I beta for: Barrel of Monkeys, Ireadtomuch, and Emisck currently.

I have a HUGE hatred for OC's. It's an issue.The way I see it, is that none of the canon characters are good enough to be the lead in your story. Usually they are self inserts, over powerful, everything is just too convenient in the way that they meet the other characters, and they are loved by everyone and perfect. While I understand that this site is for FANfiction, I won't read them. I think that the point of fanfiction is to take the ready made characters and work off that. I think that OC's CAN be used, but as side characters, never the main. It's kinda rude to write a story and say "it was good, but I think my character is better then all of yours combined, so I'm just going to use my own." Yes, that's how I see it. If I see a story with OC in the summary, I will NEVER read it.
