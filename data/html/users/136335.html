Author has written 64 stories for Pearl Harbor, Misc. Movies, Mummy, Homefront, Misc. Tv Shows, Smallville, Criminal Minds, and General Hospital.

My main focus when I first started posting to this site was Pearl Harbor, but I have since branched out into other genres.

Danny and Ann: The Next Generation, is complete. I was going to have them return in a sequel to finish their story, but at this point in time I have no plans to do that.

In addition to my PH stories, I also have three other movie fics that are posted and completed; The Mummy, Backdraft and Anna and the King.

The Homefront story that picks up at the end of the series and focuses on Jeff and Ginger is now complete while a sequel to The Magic of Ordinary Days has been suspended and may not be completed.

Jonathan's Gambit, set in the 'Noir' universe of Season 6 Smallville is complete and Bundle of Joy, the sequel to Jonathan's Gambit, is in progress.

The Trilogy Series set in an AU Season 8 of Smallville is complete. There are twenty-seven chapters and are grouped into nine trilogies: 1. Do Not Disturb, Guardian Angel and On the Outside Looking In; 2. Cupid Strikes, Cupid Strikes Again and Cupid's Arrows; 3. Taking a Chance, Leap of Faith and Trust Your Heart; 4. Hands Off, Countdown and Going Home; 5. To Tell the Truth, Double Whammy and Hearth and Home; 6. Seeing Red, Flower Girl and A New Identity; 7. Giving Thanks, Family Time and Pointing Out the Obvious; 8. Finishing Touches, The Question and Engagement; 9. Slice of Heaven, Homecoming and Vows.

The Honeymooners, set in an AU Season 9 is another Smallville story that is now complete.

For those of you who have left all of your wonderful reviews, thank you.
