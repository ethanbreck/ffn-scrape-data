Author has written 11 stories for Code Geass.

Hello. I am a twenty-something who writes things as an escape from the stress of real-life work.

I used to be a huge fangirl of Code Geass, to a ridiculous extent. This obsession, particularly with canon, has faded some, and if you'll notice a lot of my more recent writings tend to veer either towards AUs and mashups, or more experimental themes. I realize I've kind of left several older works of mine hanging, which has me kicking myself whenever I remember it. While I'd never discount the possibility of those fics eventually being finished -- (I'm sitting on half a chapter of Bird's Eye View, almost all of High Tide [2, and a complete outline for Geass Noir) -- it would be fair to say that it won't be for quite a long time, especially since I now tend to write mostly 'when the spirit moves me'.
