Current stories: SideQuest

SideQuest is a story that asks "What if Bakura won in season 5?" Tired of the world and having achieved his revenge, Bakura tells Zorc that he just wants it to end and moves on to the afterlife. The gods, however, have decided that since Bakura was responsible for unleashing Zorc that he must go back and set things right by defeating him. Bakura refuses at first but, upon realizing that sitting in a waiting room for eternity isn't much fun, he agrees to return to earth.

He is tasked to gathering together all of the Yamis, Hikaris, Millenium Items (of which the puzzle is in pieces and scattered), and the two who control Obelisk and Osiris (Marik controls Ra).

And on top of everything he has to answer to Bast, who is along for the journey and keeps in contact with Bakura via a bell collar.

Can Zorc be defeated before the world crumbles completely into darkness?
