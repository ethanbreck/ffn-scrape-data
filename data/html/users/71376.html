8/12/05

I'm not dead, I promise. Moved out of my parents house, and my computer, unfortunately, was one of the last things to be put into the new house. It was far too tempting to just sit down and write and surf the net non stop than it was to clean the house and make it mine. If anyone ever tells you living on your own is easy, tell them they're full of sht. Very frustrating and time consuming.

And damn, it's been a long time since I've posted anything, hasn't it? Well, that's changing, like right now.

I've been writing, on and off,but nothing seemed post worthy, until now. Started my next epic, and want to share it, so I'm going to. Have a few other things I might post, but have to re-read them and make sure they're ready/worthy of it. As always, reviews, comments, questions, etc are appreciated.

For anyone who's review my other (that should read "my ancient") fics, thank you. Your thoughts mean a lot to me. And those of you wondering about a sequel to "Everything Changes"... it is possible, and very probable, but not right now. When EC was written and finished, I was involved with someone who helped make it what it is. We have since stopped speaking, and I'm not quite sure the idea for the sequel is still do-able, being as she was the one who inspired it, and created two of the main characters for it.I have to rethink the sequel, and re-read EC, and I'll take it from there.

Anyway, off to write some more, and get something posted, so check back for updates soon.

~Teek
