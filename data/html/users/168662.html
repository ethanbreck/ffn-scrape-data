Author has written 5 stories for White Wolf, Quantum Leap, DC Elseworlds, and StarTrek: The Original Series.

Hiya. When I first created this account, I was happy to say I write for a living despite mild aphasia and dyslexia. I have a real job now with health benefits and everything.

I've been published, fiction wise, in comics and in small press magazines. My main interests are transformational stories, where one turns into something else; Vampire:The Masquerade; and Allegorical (furry) fiction. Please review my stories whenever you have the chance, that's the only payment I need for my fiction (although a movie deal would be nice). Be on the look out for Black Petals Magazine's Halloween issue. My story, The Flick of a Switch, will appear there. My "third" sale in the past year! I was nominated for the 2001 Ursa Major Award for my story THE GOOD SPORT! I did not win, but it was an honour to be nominated by my furry peers. Next time, I stuff the ballot box! :-)

In the very near future, my "The Good Sport" will be reprinted in a slightly revised version in a Fred Patten edited anthology named An Anthropomorphic Century. Now, available for pre-order from FURPLANET.

I will also have a brand new, never before published, and very adult story published in the 2015 Rainfurrest Adult Anthology NSFW, entitled "Steed and Steel." It's a Metamor Keep tale doesn't take place in the Keep. There's a talking toad, a dream walking dragon, and human on human rape... pretty much what you expect in one of my stories. Also, available from FURPLANET.

I have a third story that is now available in the soon to be released book Inhuman Acts, available from FurPlanet, "Brooklyn Blackie and the Unappetizing Menu" A sample of that story can be found in my FictionPress account. But maybe you should just order the book. You'll like the other 12 writers, too.

I am returning to Fanfiction and Fiction Press so that I can get ready for the next November writing event.
