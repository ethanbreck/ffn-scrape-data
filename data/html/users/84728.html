Author has written 3 stories for Disney, and Hey Arnold.
I'm just a crazy 28-year-old guy somewhere in Manila, Phils. It is about in 2001 that I started story writing. I wanted to do this because it's something that I've never tried before in my entire life. I'm an adventurer who's LOVES trying things out! I learn more about myself this way.

I would say I'm pretty creative but I wanted to know how far could my creativity go. To me, there's only one way to find out... story writing! As for me, it's something completely out of my league. How is story writing competely out of my league, you ask? Well, irl, I'm a C/C+ IT guy with a business educational background, with knowledge in corporate finance, stock/derivatives trading, and the financial markets. Top that? Lol. :)

By the way, I love the "Hey Arnold", "Pepper Ann", and "As Told by Ginger" cartoon series! I love 'em because I've never seen any cartoons that are closer to real-life and more down-to-earth. Pretty realistic for being animated series and all.

I also like Starcraft (where the author's name came from), strategy games (Warcraft 3 including the Defense of the Ancients DOTA, Command and Conquer Generals, etc.), RPG games (Baldur's Gate, Neverwinter Nights, Final Fantasy 7 & 8), and a lot of other PC gaming stuff! Currently playing Hattrick, an online football/soccer manager strategy game (www.hattrick.org).

As far as my stories are concerned, though they may be part of a fandom, all of my stories below are SELF-CONTAINED meaning, even if you don't know anything about the animated series where the story is based, you'll still understand it and enjoy it. Hey! Not all of you are fans of what I like but it doesn't mean you'll be denied of a great story, right?

You guys can email me anytime, have a talk, a chitchat, maybe even a cup of coffee, or tea. No problem! Friends and friends-to-be are always welcome:)

See 'ya!

- tst : )

My Latest Fanfiction Ramblings

August 28, 2006, Monday

Wow! I've just posted my "Lead us Not into Temptation" chapter 7 again. After three long years of being absent, I'm back here at ff.net! I can't believe that HA is still one of the top fanfic categories on the site! Amazing!

Have to fix up my profile, stuff, and things. I'm even reformatting the first 6 chapters, so it looks easier to read by everyone.

I wonder how many people are still reading this fic after all these years? Nevertheless, Chapter 8 is surely the real biggie when it comes out, hehehe. :)

September 14, 2006, Thursday

Wow! There are several Filipino HA fanfic writers now! There's even a HA fanfic that is written in Filipino here, that was posted in 2005! I don't believe it! Honestly, I wouldn't have noticed if it weren't for Hellerick Ferlibay (which I will thank here). I'll give all of them a signed review in their fics next week! The last time I've checked in 2003, the only Filipino HA writers that I was aware of were Jedy and myself. And even until now, Jedy's "Changed" is still my #1 favorite Arnold/Helga fic.

If only Jedy finished her "Sometimes Love Just Isn't Enough" fic...

...and if I could only finish this current Arnold/Helga fic of mine...

September 16, 2006, Saturday

As I've continued on my "Hey Arnold" fanfiction trail again, and after 3 years being away... just finishing MBA (thesis is the only thing left), I have seen the real world once more.

I've heard of Howie Mandel's "Deal or No Deal" game show which is a big hit in the US, and a local network here in my country have a local version of the same gameshow (the network paid the rights for it). Different host, different banker, different cash values, but SAME gameplay (and also 26 cases to open). Even if it's the local version, I liked the gameshow anyway and it is also a local hit. But as I see it, learning corporate finance in business school, the overall strategy to make the most money of the "Deals" or "No Deals" there is to simply rely on good ol' statistical probability and risk management. If there is only one big value left (among the top 10 cash values), I would likely choose "Deal" and simply stop there! I can't assume that the original suitcase in my hand would be the one with the biggest value. Too many contestants get burned by saying "No Deal" too much.

As I write my next chapter of my "Hey Arnold" fic, I wonder... supposing I was the "Deal or No Deal" host, and my contestant was Arnold? Yes, I'm talking about goody-two-shoes Arnold, who is so dense, that he doesn't know that Helga secretly loved him so deeply. If only Arnold knew her true nature and true feelings, he would really enjoy hanging out with her.

I could actually see myself saying to the football-headed one, "I have the banker's offer, Arnold." I would continue on by saying, "The banker's offer is simply... eternal happines with Helga, the nicest girl you've ever met. Arnold, just kiss the girl, and she's yours... so is it a DEAL or NO DEAL?"

As I said before... too many contestants get burned by saying "No Deal" too much!

September 21, 2006, Thursday

At last! I am finally going to post chapter 8 of my "Hey Arnold" fanfic! I am so excited, because I really, really love this chapter. I can't get over its unforgettable romantic and dramatic impact, even if I already know the secret twists of this chapter! This is a biggie... a REAL biggie! In fact, I would rather read this chapter alone a hundred times, than the previous 7 chapters COMBINED! To be honest, after reading so many Arnold/Helga fics, I would rather read THIS chapter alone than many of the non-ending chapters (i.e. all except the final chapters) of other Arnold/Helga fics (this even includes those listed in my Favorite stories!). It is partly because the first 7 chapters are actually my setup chapters for this chapter 8 and beyond.

On another note, I want to give thanks to Hellerick Ferlibay for just giving me a copy of "Dark at Heart", the HA fanfic record-holder for the greatest number of reviews. I will see why so many people are so crazy about it.

But as for now, this is it...this is it... the moment of truth! The match of the season! Patay kung patay ("Dead if we're dead")! Sink or swim! Chapter 8 is the "make-or-break" chapter. Will everybody feel its wonderful impact just as much as I did? Or shall it simply fail to make an unforgettable impression on its future readers, putting my future chapter plans in my HA fic, into a potential tailspin? The reviews in this chapter alone will ultimately decide it.

My hands are so cold... as I am now about to click the "Add Chapter" button... okay... here goes everything...

October 13, 2006, Friday

Well, I'm almost finished with chapter 9 of my fic which is going to be another pretty interesting "Arnold and Helga" moment. But unlike all the previous chapters, this will be a happy "Arnold and Helga" moment. I shall post this pretty soon. I am glad that I'm getting wonderful reviews on chapter 8. With the future chapters, now centered on the "Arnold and Helga" relationship, this story will get a lot more interesting as future chapters unfold. As of this writing, I got 38 reviews and 10 people declaring this fic as their favorite (even if it is not yet finished!). Pretty good so far, but the romantic part of this story hasn't happened yet, so let's see if we can rack up the numbers, shall we?

At the same time, I've so far read the first 9 chapters (out of 23 chapters) of that "Dark at Heart" fic. No offense to the fans of that story, but to be honest, I haven't felt much impact yet. In fact, from what I could see, the first nine chapters could be compressed into one single chapter summary and the story would still be the same. Sure the first 9 chapters were sad, but to be honest, anyone who has read the first 8 chapters of my current fic would know that it is a lot sadder.

Of course, my current HA fic will have the happiest of endings involving Arnold and Helga. How will that happen? Everyone will find out soon enough.

October 15, 2006, Sunday

So far, I have read the first 18 chapters (out of 23) of "Dark at Heart." But it's soooooo weird! There is not much impact on me yet! Am I losing my touch as a HA fan? I know that Arnold would slowly tend to fall in love with Helga as he's beginning to understand her in this fic (and will predict that they will be together in the end, I'm sure, like most A/H fics, as well as this current fic of mine), but I still don't really understand why this fic would still stand out as a fic with 400+ reviews?

Maybe the last 5 chapters have something to do with it? I'll read the rest tomorrow.

As for chapter 9 of my current fic, it's almost done. Also, I have to make one final strategic deliberation tomorrow if there are any changes still to be made.

October 17, 2006, Tuesday

I have finally finished reading that "Dark at Heart" fic and I would say that it is...okay... pretty good. Absolutely no offense to the fans again, but to tell the truth, it just simply falls short of my expectations. In fact, for some bizarre reason, I felt the romance between Gerald and Phoebe more than between Arnold and Helga. But even that impact was not as much, I'm afraid. Though some parts of it is okay, including when Helga had taken a sleeping pill, and the poem at the end, among others. The last two ending scenes reminded me of a certain movie starring Whitney Houston. I won't disclose the movie here as I wouldn't want to give the ending away. So, can somebody tell me why this fic got 400+ reviews, while most other A/H fics didn't?

But anyway, I have just deployed chapter 9 of my current fic. Hope the "reverse-umbella" moment between Arnold and Helga in this chapter will please the HA fans (crossing my fingers).
