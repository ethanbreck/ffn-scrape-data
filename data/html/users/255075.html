How I write a fan fic:

1. Turn MuchMusic on in background.
2. Sit in front of laptop. Open word processor.
3. Stare blankly for undetermined amount of time.
4. Close word processor. Check friends' away messages.
5. Open word processor. Stare blankly.
6. Close word processor. Open notepad. Maybe less intimidating?
7. Close notepad. Open wordpad. Notepad sucks.
8. Close wordpad. Check friends' away messages. Decide to join friend at gym.
9. Go to gym, forgetting to turn off MuchMusic.
10. Find inspiration on treadmill.
11. Return from gym. Shower.
12. Sit down in front of laptop. Open word processor.
13. Stare blankly. Inspiration has fled.
14. Curse creatively. Close word processor.
15. Check e-mail. Ohh, look. Friend from high school sent e-card!
16. Write 2000 word response to 10 word e-card.
17. Open word processor. Type tentative introduction.
18. Delete tenative introduction. It sucked.
19. Retype tentative introduction. It wasn't so bad.
20. Reread introduction. Hmmm...
21. Be shanghaied by inspiration. Lose all sense of self-awareness.
22. Awaken hours later to finished fan fic. Gape.
23. Reread fic for errors. Tweak weak spots.
24. Save fic. Horde for two weeks or more. It probably sucks...
25. Two weeks later... realize you don't care if it sucks or not. Post it on ff.net and hope for best.
