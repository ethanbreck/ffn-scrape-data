STORIES: Just one for now

Xibalba:Set four years after the Holy-Lifestream negation of Meteor. Cloud has been wandering the Planet in search of an answer to a question he doesn't understand. Returning to mercenarial tasks, he takes a job attempting to ascertain the reasons behind the actions of the Cult of Xibalba, purportedly committed to the revival of "The Great One." Reuniting with the surviving members of AVALANCHE, he once again must travel the world and his own psyche to stop the Cult from ripping apart the very fabric of reality. Last update: chapter ten on 03/18/2007.

I guess this story could be considered a bit of an alternate universe now that the Compilation of FF7 (Advent Children, Crisis Core, Dirge of Cerberus) has entered canon. When going into this, just assume that the events depicted in those works didn't happen.
