There are two of us (at times, three or more), the one called Rutsu doesn't want to write a bio. I (the other half) thought it would be fun.

The world of anime is an interesting one. Anyway...let's move on, shall we? Now, if you look on the right, you'll notice a lovely painting of a beach, covered with seagulls. On your left is actually the emergency exit. Please keep that in mind in case of...well...emergencies. In the right corner we have an antique vase from the 17th century. Please don't touch that! If we look closely you could see...hey, where did everyone go?

We like reading fics about:

D N Angel, Pirates of the Caribbean, The O.C, Criminal Minds, Death Note etc etc

We read more than we write, which is a shame. We should write more often. Reading is fun too, you know.Yes, you probably would know...what else would you be doing here?

So don't let me stop you in your search of fan fiction. Go on, read. Good luck. Don't forget to drink, otherwise you might get dehydrated.
