O_O Energetic 14 yr old? I WAS YOUNG WHEN I FIRST STARTED WRITING! O_O Well here's an update, i'm not 14, ; Heh in fact i'm 16!! Everyone cheer! i'm much much much older now... let's see, I haven't changed much... I'm probably a bit more mature, but still energetic. I love writing, can't you tell? I've started on my own novel, starting to kind of lean away from fanfiction now, i've got my own style of writing, boy that took forever to learn. I'm waaaaay into books, Roleplaying which is always fun, and I'm really into dancing, playing neopets, hanging with friends both online and off. _ If you ever want to question me about my writings feel free to email me at my email: hinotetenshi87@yahoo.com or IM me on AIM or MSN! My msn addy is: tenshihinote@hotmail.com Hope to hear from everyone, and if you take the time to bother to read either Love's Sacrifice or Untilted both of them, you'll notice how very fast I became off track. Hopefully i've grown out of that, but if it ever happens please do inform me! I WELCOME FLAMES! Contrary to many writers, i take the flames, in fact I welcome them! I try my best to meet the tastes of as many readers as possible, by flaming me you all help to improve my writing! _ Keep the flames coming! LOL KEEP THE REVIEWS COMING TOO!! THANKS ALL! Update: Whoot I'm actually changing this again! heh as you've noticed I haven't been reading many fics lately, only because I feel that many of the older, learned writers have moved on to creating their own novels (GO YOU ALL!) Well I gtg. ' HEh i'm in school doing this @_@ go figure. Anyways Transforming the Ditz has really gone underway but I don't think i'm getting as many reviews. .' Maybe I should just cancel production... hmm or change the rating!

Fastforward nearly over 10 years later. It's like reading from a time capsule. The Fanfics were HORRIBLE. My little auto-blurb was JUST as horrible. It's incredible how time changes the attitude and beliefs of a single person. I'm glad Fanfiction.net isn't going anywhere. This little time capsule on the web is refreshing to revisit time and time again. *--Yasha Starrider 8/19/2015--*
