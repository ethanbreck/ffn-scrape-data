These stories were created from my overwhelming adoration of J.R.R. Tolkien and his characters within The Lord of the Rings. I've never been good at describing my own work... so I think I'll let some of the reviews left among the stories do to work for me below...

I would just like to say, this is one of the most poignant fics I have ever read on this subject. Your plot is original and the topic is handled amazingly well. There are a lot of fics out there which are kind of an indulgence rather than something that really stirs your heart, but this is one of the latter. As a Frodo and Sam fan I am especially happy that you treated their characters with the respect they deserve. Keep writing, you do it so well! -- tearsofnienna

What a lovely, gentle story. Your love for these people just shines through in your writing, and your tale flows with such a smooth, peacefulness. -- shirebound

This is beautiful. You've expanded beautifully on this small, wonderful moment in the book. Sam is just incredible. -- shirebound

I love all your work that I have read so far, so you are now officially added to my favourite authors list. I just adore the heart and soul you put into your work. -- Michelle Frodo

I was very very impressed by this. Many people have attempted to write the events of Cirith Ungol from Frodo's point of view, and have completely changed it. But you stayed true to the book. Loved it! -- Michelle Frodo

What a marvellous story! Your Sam is just perfect. -- Obelia Medusa

This is just the kind of fic I've been wanting to read of late. Please keep on writing as we need more authors like you around. -- Isi

Very, very nice - a little sad but that only added to the wonderful emotion and sensitivity. Well done - I enjoyed it very much! -- Jules
