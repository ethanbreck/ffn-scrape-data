About Me

You're probably wondering who this "Rain in the Morning" person is. Someone with too much time on her hands, or more accurately, someone who loves to procrastinate by writing fanfic. I write all the time, and the stuff posted here is just a fraction of the crap that spews out of my pen and invades my drawer full of notebooks. I welcome questions and criticism, and am more than willing to discuss my stories with readers; I reply to all signed reviews. I've also offered feedback to authors who've asked me to read their work, given advice to authors asking for writing tips, and betaed a few times. Feel free to send a PM!
