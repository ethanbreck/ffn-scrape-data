What you see before you is what I have contributed to fandom. Every so often, I contribute a bit more. To be perfectly honest, I'm very focused on writing a (rather expansive) original novel series right now. I know, isn't everyone? But I have high hopes of getting published. So, for the most part, this account won't be updated much, if at all.

However, that doesn't mean I've forgotten it. These fics have helped me along and, I hope, developed my writing style slowly into something enjoyable. And, of course, I hope that these stories give some people out there a good read. I can't promise they will, since a lot of this is old and thus not all that great but still, I leave it up.

As for me, I'm a 19 year old female going through college with hopes of being a published author and a sociologist on the side. Wish me luck, ne~? Feel free to contact me.
