Short Description of Myself
I love books and movies that don't go overboard with the sex, violence, and gore. I like to write and when I have the time, I try to. I have three boys, a girl, and a husband, so that really doesn't leave very much time for me to pursue writing. But I try whenever my muse decides to hit me over the head with inspiration. :)

I love to scrapbook and create crafts, do a bit of baking and cooking, and I'm currently into Asian entertainment (music, movies, dramas, etc.). My sister got me interested in K-Pop (Super Junior, Shinhwa, DBSK, SNSD, Kara, etc.) music, and I've always liked anime/magna, including Death Note and Kuroshitsuji. I'm always on the lookout for interesting manga.

I'm not too picky when it comes to reading, whether it's fanfiction or not. All I ask is that it be well-written. There's nothing more disappointing than getting hyped up over a plot, only to drop the story because it's writen so poorly that it's hard to understand and/or follow.

And, just to get it out of the way: no, I do not like the Twilight series, even though I've read the books twice. I only put this here because I've had readers drop me from their favorites and alerts because I'm not a fan. So, if that's going to be a factor, I'm telling you right now I DO NOT LIKE TWILIGHT.

Okay? Okay, great.
