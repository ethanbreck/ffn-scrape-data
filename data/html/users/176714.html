Author has written 14 stories for StarTrek: The Next Generation, StarTrek: Deep Space Nine, Babylon 5, Rizzoli & Isles, Lord of the Rings, and Doctor Who.

Space operas! Time travel! Alien anthropology! Romance! Danger! Really complex plots and memorable characters!

Or, at least, that's what I like to read and watch, anyway. :)

I write a little here and there, mostly small moments in the aforesaid complex space opera universes. Babylon 5 and Doctor Who and Star Trek, Lord of the Rings and CJ Cherryh's sci-fi novels, random bits of sweetness or angst or humour in various fandoms--any of these are likely to make an appearance.

I'll read any pairing and genre you can come up with (femslash, slash, het, poly, gen, etc), if you can convince me of it for the duration of the story. I'm bisexual and so are most of my characters, but most of what I write is slash or femslash, except Delenn/Lennier, which hits all my loyalty kink buttons hard.

You can find me (and a slightly different selection of my fanfic (read: more porny)) at An Archive Of Our Own as nenya_kanadka. FFN user NotBlinkingMan has also translated my Star Trek: DS9 stories "A Hard Landing" and "Security Blanket" into Russian over at FicBook.net.
