Hey, I'm Winchester Mythology, formerly Fiery Charizard, and welcome to my page!

I've been writing on and off for about 15 years but more off for the last 5, having lost inspiration and the will to write. However, thanks to the likes of Supernatural and the SPN Family, I've regained my inspiration.

Unfortunately I don't have as much time as I used to, so please be patient with me and I'll try my hardest not to disappoint!

Here's to another 15 years!

You'll find them on MJ Ellsworth's page (she's in my favourite authors link). I hope those of you who loved it stick with me on this journey into Angsty-land!
