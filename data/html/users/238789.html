Hello and welcome to my profile.
I've always wanted to write something big and profound.
So let's see...

My name is Chasyn. Duh, right? I'm nearing 30 and that's scary. But I'm still pretty much a 15 year old boy at heart. Video games and dicks and vaginas and all that junk. Yadda, yadda, yadda.

Current Obsessions:
Avengers: Mostly Tony/Steve. A bit of Thor/Loki because it's weird.
Walking Dead: Rick/Daryl
Star Trek 2009: Kirk/Spock.

Lost Interest:
It happens. Sometimes, you lose interest in things you used to love. And no matter how much you loved it once, you just can't seem to bring yourself to love it in the same way anymore. I always used to say that if I started a story, I would finish it. But it seems that I'm a liar. Because I'm no longer updating any of my Kingdom Hearts stories. And I apologize. But really. They're all train wrecks.

Strong musical influences: “Isn’t that story/chapter title a song title/lyric?” Yes. I love music. Pretty much have earphones glued in my ears. And I sing. Horribly. Off key. All the time. And I hate coming up with titles. So usually I just use something from whatever I'm listening to at the time.

Time management problems: I work a full time, tiring job. I spend the remainder of my non-working time on a website where I do art and game hosting. So my time for writing is non-existent. Honestly, if I manage to write one line a day, I consider it a productive day. But I’m not one of those writer’s that just vanish and never finish their stories. (Unless I lost interest in the fandom completely.) Everything will be finished. (Unless I lost interest. Like I said.) When I have time. I'm also really bad about working on too many things at once. But I get really excited about an idea and BAM CAN'T STOP!!!

Porn: Yes, I enjoy porn. But I don’t write porn. Contradiction much? Maybe. But graphic sex scenes are actually not allowed on this website. A lot of writers are able to get away with it and that’s awesome for them. But I’ve had friends on here who have had their stuff removed and I don’t want that happening to me. So if you’re reading my shit for porn, please stop.

I don’t speak Japanese: (I can’t even spell it. Spell check is awesome.) When I first started writing stories on here, back in 2001ish, I used the term “gay” to describe my stories. I had a few people tell me this was wrong. My stories weren’t “gay,” they were “yaoi” and that shoe-something-ai word. One persistent user pissed me off so much I took down all my stories and stopped going on this site for a couple years. I speak English. The Japanese (as awesome as you are because your game design skills beat anything America has made in my opinion) didn’t invent being gay. Or writing gay characters. And honestly, most yaoi I’ve read/seen seems more like rape anyways. Some beefed out, dark haired guy pounding some tiny, girly blond.

Uke vs. Seme: This pisses me off too. Someone once said to me, “(Character Name) is too much of an ass to take it up the ass.” Contradiction much? Totally. If you want to write that way, fine. But it’s not something I follow.

MPREG: Is something I’ve always been highly interested in. From an early age. (Not just in males, but female pregnancies that involve two females and no male DNA at all.) Now I do prefer some science/reason behind it, even if it’s something magical. Baby can’t just naturally appear there so give us a good reason. But to take a point from the Uke vs, Seme argument from above, if one boy in the relationship can have a baby, why not the other? When I first started writing my first MPREG, In Life and Death, I talked about the idea with someone I thought was a good friend on this website. I said, “I want to write where Sora becomes pregnant from a genetic experiment.” And the user was appalled, said that was horrible/unnatural/couldn’t happen/bad idea/a lot of other things. But eventually they said, “Just as long as Sora’s the mother. He’s practically a girl. But Riku can’t get pregnant.” Why the hell not? Because he’s taller? More muscular? A jerk (in his endearing way)? So I created a storyline in which Riku is the one to get pregnant. But never got around to working on it because I can’t finish anything and start too many things.

I actually hate babies: This is true. Not just babies, kids too. Probably stems from my years of being tricked into playing nanny. Started with one baby. Ended up with 4 babies by the end. But gotta keep the species going on and whatnot. And despite my hang-ups, a lot of my favorite characters I think make great parents. Or at least fun parents.

My point to all the above arguments? Don’t tell me what I can and can’t write. And I’ll do the same to you.

Thank you.
