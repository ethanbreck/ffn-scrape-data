Hey people! My name actually is Jash, hmmm, aren't I intellegent? It's pronounced J-A-SH (like the a in chase). Sorry, I just hate when people mispronouce it...Yes, I AM a girl. I'm 20, and I love to read fanfic when I have nothing better to do. Hmmm, which happens to be most of the time. -_-

Anyway, I love this site, and have decided to try my hand at writing, so here goes. Scary, I know...but don't give up on me yet.

My favorite shows are Dragon Ball Z (my fav. characters are: Vegeta and Mirai Trunks, *grin* I just can't get enough of royalty ^_^), any of the Highlander movies or tv shows & Dark Angel, though I also love poetry.
