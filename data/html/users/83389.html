Right. Eleven and a half years of writing nothing more than papers for college (which I was done with six years ago) and plot concepts or scene ideas scribbled in margins and random notebooks and finally the Labyrinth has dragged me back. With a vengeance. Let's hope it lasts.

In the pipeline are several Labyrinth fics ( not quite sure how many stories the ideas in my head are going to split or condense into) a Triun fic and something from Skip Beat. There is an Inuyasha concept playing tag in my brain as well, but we'll see.

Also my keyboard at work is terrible.

The sentances below make me feel old. This was from back in High School. I do hope I have improved since then. Though only one of the four fics I'd had sitting up here since 2003 was embarrassing to take down, there is some truly mortifying content in my review history.

Well this is who I am. You will probably be able to get a pretty darned good Idea of who I am from my writing. Which will, by the way, eventually have an entry in almost every genre, I believe
