Author has written 3 stories for Legend of Zelda.

I started a Zelda OneShot fanfiction community a while ago; please visit it if you like! When sorting through the Zelda fanfiction, you only get the option of choosing fics that are longer than a certain amount of words; not shorter than, so I figured it would be a good community to create. It's for the those of us with little free time, or very short attention spans o.O lol. But mostly, I love the witty humor that comes with a oneshot, or the sweetness of the romance oneshots, that can become too drawn out or redundant in regular fanfiction, and it really does take a great author to make a good oneshot. They really do deserve their own category!

If you'd like to me a Staff Member, please PM me!

I no longer write stories.
