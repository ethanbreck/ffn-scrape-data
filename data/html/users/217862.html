Author has written 4 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Sorcerer Hunters, and Dragon Ball Z.

I guess from the fact that someone is reading this, they either really like my work, or they have found some grammatical errors. Suggestions are always helpful! For those wondering my titles and my pen name are in Polish (I only know a little Polish but I am trying to learn). My pen name means "Dangerous Moon" I thought it sounded cool...back in the day...

I haven't written anything in a while because college and life in general has been keeping me busy. I graduated with an environmental studies major and minors in biology, French, and political science. I haven't posted anything in so long, and I would like to think that my writing style has changed and matured...maybe.. So perhaps I shall try to sit down and write something. Any suggestions? Seriously...I have no ideas...

I love watching cartoons (Cartoon Network is almost always on in the room), and anime. My favorites include: Dragonball Z, Gundam Wing, Naruto, InuYasha, and Samurai Champloo. I also love CSI (all three of them) -- it goes along with the whole science nerd thing I think.

If you like my work, please go to www.geocities.com/finaltriumph (it's a site for anime fics)! This site has fics that are exclusively written for THAT and only that site (there's a Zechs/Noin fic for my fellow 6x9 fans)! Plus I am one of the webmistresses _ If you own a site, and are interested in posting some of my fics, please contact me at the e-mail address above! Thanks again and e-mail me if you want to ask me any questions etc...I promise to be nice. If you need anything translated, or wanna have random discussions about biology (you know you are a biology nerd when someone says the word 'cosmopolitian' and you immediately think of a pattern of species distribution) and what not do not hesistate to e-mail or IM me, and I would be more than happy to do so.

Before I forget, I just want to say a quick thanks to everyone who has given my suggestions, reviewed my work etc... you guys kept me going and are my heroes! Also, it takes a lot of guts to post your work online, so I just want to say thanks to all of the authors who have posted their stories for our enjoyment!
