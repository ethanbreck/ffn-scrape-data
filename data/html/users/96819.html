If you'll please step into my office...

...Good, now that you're here I can share a few things I've been meaning to discuss with you.

I'm just a bored lonely 17 year old girl with an entirely too large amount of time on her hands. Below are listed some of the products of that time. Others include my journal (listed up there under homepage), my various "creative" art projects such as my closet doors and my dresser, the collection of bruises on my legs from giong to the river with my friends, the stack of finished books on my other dresser (not to mention the one on the floor), and the empty pocky wrappers on my bedroom floor. I also like to waste my time with music, (punk, rock, emo, what pop I can stand, and a little ska), horseback riding, chatting with my friends online, swimming, and watching a very little amount of tv.

Currently most of my friends have decided they have lives without me (losers), and no one is usually online for me to talk to (hint hint, add me to your aim, or msn). Though I must say a few of them stick around.

Some of the stranger things I like are the weird movies that make no sense and leave you with a feeling of what-the-hell-just-happened, and movies that bore other people to death. I hate children, I hate overly obnoxious, shallow people, I'm not real big on mustard either. I was very excited when I learnt that my mother liked my punk music, I danced around the room singing, "my mommy likes punk." 'Twas all very amusing. My favorite band is a punk band from Sacramento called National Opposition. I met one of the band members a couple weeks ago while I was doing fireworks with my old best friend at some girl's house. He was a friend of her brother's. I like dead animals, the clean kind, not the bloody, gutsy kind. Gina and I are slowly making a dead animal photo album. Dead sheep are most definitely the best, but I really have this whole sheep obsession anyways, so no surprises there. Llamas have got to be the coolest animals in the world. I'm thinking about buying one. Or at least calling and harassing the idiot who owns a llama farm. (1-530-878-1040 from USA or Canada, don't know about country codes and all that shit for other places.) I want a brown one. And I plan to ride it. Everywhere.

Lessee. I have a thing for fun haircolors, though last night I found out I may only be doing it to piss off my father. But I'm not sure. It's severl shades of blonde right now, at any rate. I believe I'm a dirty blonde-ish color, naturally, but only heaven knows. I was born with black hair, which lightened pretty quickly to bright bright blonde, which I kept for most of my early childhood. It kind of got the diry nasty brown mixed into it some time around when I moved to this God-forsaken town in the middle of Northern California. I finally managed to die (sic) it a nice bright shade of blonde when I was a freshman, 'round 14 or 15. In November of '01 (junior, 16) I died to brown. I liked that, but it kept fading too fast. So I died it blue in May (junior, 17). But I struck a deal with my parents that I'll die (sic) it brown for senior pix (decided I don't like me blonde, too many jokes) if I got to die (sic) it hot hot pink for school. Which I was planning on doing whether or not they agreed. Not sure they have, for that matter... Point being, I died (sic) it brown for pics, but it still had some blue/brown in it, and then I went black for school (senior, 17), and the other day I bleached it all, which has had an interesting effect, leaving my roots almost white, and the rest of my hair a mix or dirty-blonde, black, green and normal blonde.

I'm sure you don't care about my hair. Really. I have nifty eyes too, but we won't go there, okay? Neat.

I own a six-eyed monster named Koby, but his nickname is BillBoyJoeHermanFred. Neat, eh?

Oh, damn. Wasn't supposed to talk about him. Whatever.

Enjoy my stuff, and read all my favorite crap, authors, stories, all that stuff. They're cool.

Now, get the hell out of my office. I have work to do.
