Hmm well what to right here? If you clicked here then your probably either looking for other fics by me (which is more than great), or your just being inquisitive. Either way the bio isn't going to matter much- you hardly going to want to know about me, only fanfiction orientated stuff.

Well as you've probably found out I'm an angst/romance/drama type of writer (well I try to be). I much prefer darkness and depression to puppy love any day). I'm also a major fan of the concept of forbidden love, the type that will probably never exsist outside the world of fanfiction- (For me) In Resident Evil that'd be Claire/Wesker, in Harry Potter that'd be Hermione/Draco or Hermione/Snape.

Well at the moment I've written fics in both RE and HP, more RE fics though- although I've become a bit disillusioned with the RE world and am leaning more into HP.

As for the sexuality of pairings when writing I really don't mind - i.e. slash doesn't bother me, but neither does writing hentai or 'straight' pairings.

Well if there's anything else at all that you want to know you can email or IM me on the appropriate addresses above.
