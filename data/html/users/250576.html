Author has written 16 stories for Lord of the Rings, Wheel of Time, and Frozen.

The pen name's Dagorloth.
But don't call me that.

You can call me Rai (pronounced 'Ray' or 'Rye', it really does not matter because both work for me, though I personally pronounce it 'Ray').

And if you are reading this, then you are bored.

If I happen to write a new story, it's because of plot bunnies that needed killing in order to satisfy my own unnatural curiosity for all that makes sense in my little world of canon. Either way, I've come to decide that unless a story is FULLY fleshed out to my personal standards of what constitutes a 'good story' (and is not simply a 100-word writing challenge as a means to draw pageviews), it's not finding its way onto FFN.

So the gap between stories may be long and somewhat obnoxious.

Oh... and if you also happen to read any of my stories, expect Canadian spelling. I could never understand the point of American English.
