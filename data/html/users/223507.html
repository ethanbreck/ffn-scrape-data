My Spark Notes

My name is The_Cavity.

I am a regular at Cheever's Used Book Store, My local Cofee Shop complete with an excess of chai lattes, and the Public Library.

My favorite writers are: Elizabeth Wurtzel, Chuck Palahniuk, Slyvia Plath, JD Salinger, JK Rowling, VC Andrews, Mary Higgins Clark, George Orwell and Many Many more.

I harass one of my best friends by sending her dity novels in the mail...In return she also sends me dirty novels...Now we have each have a small collection of dirty novels (because you can't return a gift!)...What she doesn't know is I've started reading some of my collection--and they aren't that bad.

My Achilles heel is that I can't read in the car without throwing up.

I think my dad is a spy.

I just graduated from college and am jobless, so if you have a job for me PLEASE LET ME KNOW!

I live a simple life.

I'm in a personal relationship with the russian language and my cell-phone. Every day it does something I never new it could do before. AMAZING!
