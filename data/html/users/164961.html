My name is Roselyne Marot,

I'm a former engineer in Biochemistry & Chemistry IT developer, now cameraman, director, video editor, graphic motion designer, musician, singer, scenarist, and since the beginning of 2013: wrestler in the BCWF federation.

AND I have a huge passion for drawing comics and writing (original stories, and fanfics).

Warning:I've been writing fan-fictions since 1997 as a way to develop my skills in English (I'm a French speaker), but also to "keep the magic goin' on". Fanfics keep a story alive, by adding new plots, as-ifs, developing characters’ psychologies, etc… It offers usually a new points of view on a story/anime/movie. Sometimes humor, sometimes drama. It leads sometimes to funny parodies ;-)

!!! BUT !!!

Recently, I've observed that the readers have changed. No more interested - it seems - to well developed thriller/drama plots, but mostly focused on "Plot-What-Plot" porn fanfics. I have no idea of the reading stats those fanfics make, but I based myself on the amount of respective reviews.

Since it's apparently too much to ask 30 seconds of your time to leave feedback/reviews on one of my stories, but you take the time to leave a note on the PWP porn ones, the conclusion is easy: readers are no more interested into my style.

Hence, I'm going to stop my fanfics, one by one, using a rather abrupt ending, not necessarily what I had planned when I started writing it.

Update: April 16th 2018.

I didn't realize that YEARS had gone by... OO; I'm sorry for all those who've waited for upcoming chapters. There will be update. Things are about to change in my life in the upcoming months, which will leave me more time to write. But not just fanfics. I intend to start what a teacher at university had advised me to do: writing REAL stories :) With the Kindle publication on Amazon, things are easier too. :) Let's cross fingers _
