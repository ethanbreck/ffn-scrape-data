12/18/05: Hmm... I figured that I owed anyone who's read any of my stories and still holds some hope for them, well, something. That, and I haven't updated anything for over a year.
Anyway, first of all, I am terribly sorry to anyone who has enjoyed any of my work and has stayed true to me even when I lost hope. I appreciate it more than you could ever know and then some.
It's just, well, I can't seem to find my muse anymore. I have all of these great ideas, but... I just can't get them down in a way that I like, or, in many cases, at all. I'd really like to finish all of these stories some day, as I do get quite a bit of pleasure out of writing them and I'd love to see how they end as much as anyone else; I've long-since forgotten the original directions in which they were meant to go.
Currently, I've been attempting to begin a long-time writingproject that I've been planning for over three years. Rather, that's how long the storyline and characters have been forming in my mind. Perhaps after I finish that and learn to stop procrastinating I'll take up the fine art of fanfiction again. But, until then, I leave you with my most sincere apologies and my word that I will someday finish.

Despite all that I've said, there is still one way that my stories can live on sooner than... however long I'll take to start writing them again. If you'd like to use any of my ideas as a base for a fanfiction of your own, I'd be more than happy to share it. Just please, if you do, let me know and give my some credit. That's all I ask.

Thank you again for being such great people. It's been an honour entertaining you.

The Almighty Disclaimer(which will be referred to instead of re-writing it in the fics): Unless I state otherwise, or it's incrediably obvious that the character is original, I do not own any of the characters mentioned in any of these stories, nor do I own the songs used in the parody(ies). I do, however, own most of the plot ideas and some of the settings. I will state within the story anything that I have not said here. If there is any confusion towards this disclaimer saying that I don't own anything, I think you really need to gain some better reading comprehension skills. Thank-you. ~RG
