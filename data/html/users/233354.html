Well, about me, ne?

Quickie and FUN analysis: I'm exactly like Jim Hawkins from Outlaw Star and Rubette (LaLette!) from Gokudo

Not much can be said about me, I guess, despite that I work a lot and for no apparent reason. It could be because I'm a Capricorn, Type A (beware my random craziness!), and zodiac animal of Ox (or cow, whichever you prefer of giant hooven creatures that graze). Hmm...what else?

Well, I am currently a college student! Yay me! I plan to double major in psychology and human communications...I might even choose a minor. Yes, I know I'm crazy. Moving on.

I also love to swim! OH YEAH! Swim team for Ashland here I come!

Ho hum... I guess I should list favorites here then, yes? If you've noticed, I love Kirby and the color purple (that's also why Ashland University will make me rich; it's school colors are purple and gold).

So...my favorite musical artist? I'll only say this a thousand times:

Masami Okui

She SO owns Megumi in everything. I mean, yeah, sure, Megumi's great, but Masami owns her in everything, and if you WANNA fight me on this topic, be warned, I have a compliation essay of points and arguements on this, especially since I thought Megumi was the stuff before Masami came into my life (thank you for showing me the way Makkun)!

So...my fave songs? My god, you shouldn't have asked me that. Well, definitely "Mambo No. 5" by Lou Bega, ANYTHING by Masami Okui, but my three faves are "Shuffle," "Rondo(or 'Rinbu')Revolution," and "Jama wa Sasenai." I could list tones of faves for you, but I'm afraid I'd keep you here all day listing them. '

How about favorite anime characters, eh?

At the top of my male category is Vegeta from DBZ, and topping my female faves is Sango from Inuyasha (too very drastically different characters, I know), but I dunno, Rukia from Bleach is really great too...I can't decide there.

Favorite va's:
Brian Drummond (the GOD of dub voice acting!)
Crispan Freeman (XD, he goes from Zelgadis to Alucard!)
Veronica Taylor (her range is aMAZing!)
Ted Lewis (He owns Yugi's voice!)
Dan Green (how can you NOT like it! Or at least laugh in he gets all "high and mighty")
Eric Stuart (Two words: Seto Kaiba)
Rachel Lillis (Martina and Utena!)
...and heaps of others I can't remember.

Anyway, I'm sure most of you are here to see if my anime/manga preferences are similar to yours... Well, let's see:

Top anime I love (and in no preferential order):

Yu-Gi-Oh! (Beastly characterization and the most and sweetest plot twists I've ever seen)
Fruits Basket (MORE beastly characterization)
Yoroiden Samurai Troopers (popularly known as Ronin Warriors) (I've been infatuated with this show since I was in third grade; that should count for something)
Yu Yu Hakusho (Sweet characters, an "anti-hero" of sorts, and excellent plots and back stories)
Inuyasha (It's Rumiko Takahashi at her finest)
Slayers (This show hardly needs an explanation; it has everything from music to plot to comedy, and everything inbetween!)
DBZ (...and my only reason is Vegeta/Vejita/Bejiita/etc... especially the Brian Drummond version. Although, I do love all his va's, but B. Drum is the best, IMO)
Dragonhalf (Because how else are you gonna get 4 episodes of greatness?)
Gokudo (Because he's the coolest anti-hero out there!)
Revolutionary Girl Utena (Because I like surrealism in show form)
Outlaw Star (I love character plot-driven shows!)
Escaflowne (EVERYTHING! HAS EVERYTHING! Even B. Drum_)

Top Manga I love:

Bleach (Because we finally have a GOOD Capricorn in anime: Rukia!)
Yu-Gi-Oh! (You already know...)
Full Metal Alchemist (Roy Mustang, Maes Hughes, Havoc, and Hawkeye! It's all that needs to be said!)
Slayers (...and the novels!)
