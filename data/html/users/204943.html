Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, and Lion King.

About me: I'm a Mexican woman living in the Netherlands since 2001. I enjoy writing spoofs, parodies and fanfiction in my spare time. I also like singing and playing the piano, but I spend most of my time behind this computer developing RSI. ;)

Keep in mind that English is not my native language, so please forgive me for any misspellings and grammar mistakes that you might find in my writings. Let me know if I can change something.

Some facts:

- I was born in 1978. You do the math if you want to know my current age. ;)

- Married to a Dutch man since 2001 (the main reason why I moved from Mexico to Holland)

- No kids, just two adorable cats.

- Currently unemployed, but doing voluntary work at the local animal shelter, tending to the homeless cats.

- Yes, I LOVE cats.

- I'm terribly allergic to cats, go figure! *snrrrffff*

- I speak Spanish, English, Dutch, and some French. (I used to know some Portuguese too, but I've forgotten most of it due to the lack of practice :( )

- I suffer from Globophobia (What can I say... There are some weird phobias out there, and I just happen to have one!)

Future projects:

- I got an account at DeviantArt! Feel free to pop by at nichers . deviantart . com (remove the spaces!) I've made character sheets so that you can see what my lions look like, and I've been trying my hand at learning to draw. Hopefully I can illustrate my own novels some time.

- As of May 2013: I had started writing Ndani's tale a while ago, but I hit a major block so I've focused on another character of mine instead. As such, the Lioness Tale #3, "Jiwe's Freedom", is almost complete! :D Stay tuned to learn about the rough life of a lioness addicted to forbidden herbs and plants!

- May 10: Jiwe's tale is complete! Chapter 1 is online now, and the rest of the story will follow every two days or so. The whole tale is already complete, so you won't get an unfinished story! :D
