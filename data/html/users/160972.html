April 2019: Still here, reading & reviewing... writing much less, though, what with new TV addictions -- and especially
the obligations of RL.

May 2015: BACK-- reading and reviewing, at least.

-- Laughing at the updates ... has that much time really passed?
Was it really nearly a dozen years ago that I posted my first fan fic-- for The Pretender? Wow. I'm trying to update,
add some new stuff. Family stuff (my circus=my monkeys...) and breadwinning have been taking precedence.

-- Note 6 8/05: I changed my e-mail address and fixed a few typos and annoying redundancies in Worst Case Scenario.
I also uploaded my first "drabble" in response to a challenge about socks. I couldn't resist. I've always been a big
fan of "red socks." ;-)

-- Note 5 10/03: "The Haunted" (Pretender) is my first fanfic. For some reason, when I expanded it, the new word count
didn't take. Believe me, it's more than 500 words long!
-- Note 4 8/03: Uploaded Tracker fic "Dreamtracks." Wish I could see the end of the sole season to find out what
happened. (2014: Finally saw it, in 14 min increments, on YouTube. Kind of a pffft ending, but also rather sweet.)
-- Note 3 5/22/03: Uploaded a new story ("Worst Case Scenario"). It's shamelessly sentimental MP/J, but I guess I was
in the mood. I'll try to pace myself on that sort of thing.
-- Note 2 10/21/02: I made three tiny little changes and fixed a couple of typos on 'One Step.' I emphasized a
connection a little more tangibly. As a result, a certain 'prop" appears in a later story (see above). Watch for it.
It's shiny. :)
-- Note 1 10/12/02: I've revised & expanded "The Haunted" and I'm still working on those other stories.

Heartfelt thanks again to all who have left encouraging reviews for my stories. It means more than you realize.
