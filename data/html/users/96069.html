Welcome to my page! Wow, I just realized how long it's been since I last updated this... My name's Kitty, I'm 30, and I'm a full time stay-at-home wife and homemaker/writer. I've loved creative writing since probably late middle school/early high school, and my writing skills have definitely improved and evolved through the years, haha. My earliest works were crap (I'll readily admit that), but I have kept those stories on here, in part to remind myself of where I was then, and where I am now, and that I can only go forward if I continue to push on. I'm highly considering going back and revising a lot of my stories on here, as looking back on it now, I feel like it could be a LOT better... I'm not going to make this updated profile as long as it was, but I will just say this: you guys, my readers, are my inspiration. I've loved reading every single one of your reviews -- the good, the bad, and even the ugly -- and it has helped me strive to try to reach my potential, by adjusting my writing appropriately in a positive way. So, thank you to all of ya'll out there -- you guys are awesome, rock on!
