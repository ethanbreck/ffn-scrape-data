helloooooo. i'm aspookah. i'm guessing you're here because you've read some work of mine. i must warn you, i rarely finish anything i write. i have just returned here after, oh i don't even know how long, honestly. not so much into writing the Mummy stuff anymore. i got bored with the stories and don't much feel like coming up with new ones. sorries! i think i will be back to doing some harry potter stuff tho, marauders era of course haha.

i am a huge dork. w00t for me. but anyhewwww. i am a college student without much time to write during the year, i'm in the midst of switching schools and concentrations yet again :(.

so yes, i have trouble finishing stories i start. i am so fond of Simple Flame of a Candle, though. i really am. i'd like to rework some stuff in it and continue the story. i think it could be quite interesting. obviously now it will sort of be AU, with Sirius's death in OotP and Snape's killing of Dumbledore in HBP. maybe i could actually work the HBP thing in though. and somehow explain through Sirius's curtain fall. i dunno. oh the creative juices are flowing. bloody lovely.

Let's see, anything else about me? Hmmmm, I'm based out of Buffalo, NY, but will be moving to the U.K. in the not too distant future to be with my fiance. he's a military guy and i am missing him so terribly. I'd do anything for him.

I am an artist, i've been in university for three years, starting in art, then switching to something else, and now i'm back to art concentrating on graphic design. i would love to continue on with my writings a bit more than i do.
