Author has written 3 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter.

WELL. I haven't been here in nearly ten years.

Fan fiction was my first love, though it started out involving bands (Hanson and the Moffatts; yes, I'll own up to it. I still dig the music). It grew into my own original fiction, and writing was my passion for years. Something's happened recently, though. The writer's block has built up and up until a fairly sturdy wall has been placed between me and my creativity. I'm breaking it down, brick by brick, and I'm hoping going back to basics will help with that. IE: gonna start writin' the fan fiction again.

I've been interested in Harry Potter fan fics for awhile (my favorite is probably "A Year Like None Other" by Aspen In The Sunlight), even though I'm fully aware I'm not the largest Potterphile out there (and as such, I'm researching things I don't know, so please don't hate on me! I'm learning!). I'm rather fond of Dramione, but have decided to start out with George Weasley/OC. Probably safer than stepping all over two beloved characters.

(eh, more to come on this later)
