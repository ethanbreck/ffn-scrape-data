Well, after awhile of writing fanfic after fanfic for longer than I've been a member here, I regret to say that I will no longer be writing any more. There is a chance that a new one may turn up...but that is highly unlikely. I've moved on into original novels and writing on FP.Com and don't intend to look back. The fanfiction that I have neglected to update, and I apologize for, are officially dead. If anyone wishes to adopt the story as their own email me about it and I'll take care of the rest. FF.Net is the best place to begin writing, and the best place to start a future career in writing. So...tank you EVERYONE that supported me here and everyone that helped me improve on my stories. I hope that you'll read my original stories on FP.Com too! (I put a LOT more time and effort into them.) With that said, I bid you all farewell.

- Raiju
