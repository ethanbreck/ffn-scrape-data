I screwed up on my poem "I Walk Amongst Heroes". I forgot to add a disclaimer. Stupid Kat! So, here it is. I don't own anyone. If I owned them, would i really be writing fanfic? Or would i be out producing my own shows?

Yes, yes, ladies and germs, I am not dead. Not yet, anyway. The Resurrection Dept. head would pitch an absolute fit if she ever had to bring me back. Anyway, I hope to be more consistent with my updates, but no promises are being made. I often have a lot of homework, and many other things that require my attention.

And, if you ever want to find me, you can hit me up on my Tumblr or on my Twitter. That's where I'm most often hanging out. Just send me an ask. Or a tweet. Just let me know if you want the URLs, since they apparently won't work here.
