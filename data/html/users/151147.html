About Me

I am a writer with an avid interest in psychology, physics, history, and economics. Writing has long been a passion, and I hope to someday make a career of it. Apart from writing, I have worked in education as a lecturer in one of the aforementioned subjects.

I also believe that writing is a wonderful thing and that good writing and the techniques required to develop it should be shared with as many people as possible. In that regard, I'm always open to hearing from anyone who was read my work. If you want to contact me (perhaps to praise or perhaps to put your proverbial stake and pitchfork to work), then by all means leave a review, send me a PM, or drop by my blog/deviantART to let me know how you feel.

I've normally got quite a few stories going at any given time, so there can be big breaks between updates. But rest assured that I am working on my stories. If you want to know where I'm up to on a particular story, you can always message me or a leave question in a review.

Apart from fan fiction, I also write original fiction. If you're interested in my original fiction,you can find me on Amazon as L. G. Estrella.

My Fan Fiction

I tend to have multiple stories going on at once. This means that there is almost always an update or two each week. However, this also means that it can be a little while between updates for a particular story (I try to keep it to less than a month). Some of my stories are also highly episodic in nature (e.g., Ordinary Heroes) and thus tend to have more sporadic updates than the stories that follow a more traditional structure (e.g., The Vestige). Here is a rundown on what you can expect from some of my stories.

The Ordinary Heroes Universe

Ordinary Heroes started off as an attempt to answer a question that I thought might have an amusing answer: what would it be like if Lightning and Fang were married and had kids? What started off as a quick chapter or two mutated into something that now spans several stories and hundreds of thousands of words. At present, the Ordinary Heroes universe is made up of several stories:

Ordinary Heroes. This is where it all began. Lightning and Fang might have conquered the fal'Cie, but can they conquer parenthood? Diana, Averia, and Claire make their first appearances here, and later chapters offer a glimpse into how the kids might be in the future. Their parents, of course, remain certified badasses. Features things like: Halloween, bath time, pets, snowball fights, and much, much more! Tell Me A Story. Technically speaking, this was actually the first story featuring the kids, but it wasn't supposed to be that way. Each chapter features one of our favourite former l'Cie telling a story to the kids. The themes and stories may vary, but there's always a lesson to be learned and some fun to be had, especially when the former l'Cie start putting themselves into the fairly tales they tell! The Cookie Jar. Rather than focusing on big, episodic chapters, each chapter of this story contains a number of small parts that all belong to the same theme. But it's still a story about the former l'Cie and their families. Life is all about the little things, and that's what you'll find in this story. The children of Vanille and Hope make their first real appearances in this story, along with Claire's younger siblings, and Dajh's son. Final Fantasy XIII Omake Theatre The chapters of this story are all bits and pieces that are either independent ideas that might one day become their own story or are too short to stand on their own. Several of these chapters involve the characters from Ordinary Heroes. Lightning and Fang Sitting in a Tree. These chapters are all prompts from Fangrai Forever. Some of them are set in the Ordinary Heroes universe.

Lightning and Fang Sitting in a Tree

This story is made up of a collection of prompts from the Fangrai Forever prompt list. Each chapter can stand on its own, although a few of them are slightly connected. You can expect something different from each chapter: some chapters are humours and others are full of angst; some are playful and others are racy; some are highly romantic and some tiptoe on the very edge of parody. There's something for everyone in this story, and there's a whole lot of Lightning/Fang.

A Dose of RWBY

This story is a collection of snippets based on RWBY. They are mostly fluffy in nature and range from slice of life chapters to alternate universe chapters. The main pairings are Ruby/Weiss and Yang/Blake although you will see others too (e.g., Rena/Nora and Pyrrha/Jaune).

Asami the Korrabender

This is a collection of Legend of Korra snippets, ranging from fluff to angst. Some of the chapters are AUs whereas others are simply set within the normal Legend of Korra universe.

Blue Fire and Polar Bear Dogs

A story in which Azula was Korra's firebending master because, really, who doesn't think that would be awesome?
