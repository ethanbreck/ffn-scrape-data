In the begging, there was nothing. Then, created from pure darkness, an almighty beast arose from the darkest reaches of the underworld. His name was Banisher. He dedicated his life to make miserable all the souls of the pathetic humans. His reign was absolute; he reigned with an irion fist, and he showed NO mercy. All until that fateful day...when his mommy found out and grounded him for the rest of his rotten life. Peace once again returned to the land, but Banisher swore revenge.
