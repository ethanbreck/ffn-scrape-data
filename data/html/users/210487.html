My main purpose here is to write SW, CSI Las Vegas, CSI:NY, House, and Quantun Leap fics!

I'll explain...

I write Jedi Apprentice stories, as Jedi Amni with my friend Jedi Jenna and a young Obi-Wan... They're usually very comical and feel good... ocassionally angsty... I am also a supporter of Qui/Obi love! I write Fab 4 (Wedge Antilles, Tycho Celchu, Wes Janson, and Hobbie Klivian) stories as Tahl (Amni and Obi-Wan's daughter) and she's usually paired with Wedge, whereas, Jenna's daughter, Jelena, is paired with Wes, and Tahl's cousin Tae is paired with Hobbie... (we let Winter keep Ty, though I enjoy him too and may ocassionally write a Tahl/Ty fic that is AU)! I've now gone to the great third level of Star Wars ridiculousness and have already started my third generation of Star Wars fanfiction series focusing on Wedge and Tahl's two children, but I'm trying to go back and start with Tahl and Wedge's first meeting before I post their angsty forced departure and Tahl's lonely pregnancy.

I write CSI stories as Alex Guillory and she is a CSI from NY that goes to LV to work with Nick after the stalker incident and never leaves. She ends up with Nick, though the relationship hits a rough patch that sends her back to NY and Mac's arms! I'm also working on a few CSI:NY backstories for her where she's involved with Danny. I am all for Nick/Greg, GSR W00T CANON, and WCR for CSI:LV! Of course, I still enjoy the ocassional Snickers and GregSara pairing! I also have recently become a fan of Hodges/Wendy:-)

For CSI:NY I support Mac/Danny, Flack/Danny, Flack/Mac, Flack/Mac/Danny, Stella/Mac, and I realy love the idea of Flack/Stella like whoa!

For House, I'm all for House/Wilson. I started out a Cameron/House shipper before I realized Wilson is House's soulmate ;-) And if I could have my way, I'd be dating both House and Wilson. hehe! I'm so greedy! I also enjoy Huddy and Chameron. Plus, I love Honey the nutritionist because I love Piper Perabo. And House+Wilson+Piano=OT3! ;-)

For Quantum Leap I have recently become interested in the Sam/Al ship. They are the quintessential best friend relationship in my eyes and I have just started to dapple in the slash!fic arena with them.

As for other fandoms, I love quite a few movies and I'm sure I will dapple with other fandoms in the future. These are just my main fandoms for the moment!

So right...I own Tahl, Tae, Amni, Alex, and thr future children of Tahl and Wedge...Jenna and Jelena are Jedi Jenna's...CSI, CSI:NY, HouseMD and SW are not mine at all and, alas, never will be! So that's it! Hope you enjoy my stuff ;-)

I also write some nonfiction under the same name at fictionpress.net :-)
