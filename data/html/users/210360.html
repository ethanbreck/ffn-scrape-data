Author has written 43 stories for Sailor Moon, and Yu-Gi-Oh.

Hi! My name is Samantha. I'm 25 and dangerously obsessed with Bishoujo Senshi Sailormoon and Yugioh. In high school I mostly wrote Haruka/Michiru romance fics, then upon entering college I took a year or two off from writing (unfortunately). I tried to get back into it focusing on Yugioh characters, but that didn't last very long :\ I only have one short story so far, and was planning a huge action/adventure story which never made it past the first chapter. I haven't written anything in years, but here's my archive of stories back when I was obsessed with writing fanfiction :)

Most of my fanfics are what I like to call 'short and sweet.' They're random one-shot ideas that popped into my head that I couldn't fit into a longer story, so they may not have a definite beginning or ending, but they're fun nonetheless! If the story isn't designated as a certain chapter, it's not going to be continued, sorry. Let your imagination run wild :)

All the characters in all my stories are portrayed the way they are meant to be: Japanese. No dub names or personalities here :)
