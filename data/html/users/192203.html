Author has written 19 stories for JAG, CSI, X-Files, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Once and Again, Gilmore Girls, and Leverage.

I'm Melissa...24...Philly-girl through and through even though I have strayed from time to time...I work in a body shop and love cars...I hang out primary with guys and suffer from sports-obsession...Left Catholicism when it began feeling like a shoe that no longer quite fit...Meditating on Buddhism at the moment...Yeah, that would be me in a nutshell.

I haven't really written in awhile...No real excuse because I'm constantly full of ideas that I let run their course through my brain without putting them to paper (er, screen). I read a lot though, both actual books and fanfic. Lately it's been Leverage and publishings of His Holiness the Dalai Lama (can you say opposite ends of the spectrum). I went though a 2 week obsession with Jake Gyllenhaal that coincided with the release of Prince of Persia. And I have wasted countless hours reading various ways of bringing Sirius Black back to life (or not killing him at all) in the Harry Potter category.

I suffer from obsessive thoughts, so I tend to get fixated on something until I accomplish whatever it is I need to accomplish to put my mind at ease...And then I get bored. I try to avoid multi-chapter fics because of this. I have enough unfinished work on my hard-drive.

If there's anything else you would like to know about me, feel from to PM or email. I'm also on Windows Live Messenger whenever I'm on the computer. LissaMarieFic@hotmail.com
