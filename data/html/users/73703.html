Bored with profile and decided to re-do it.

I'm an Australian high school student who yearns for greater things (eg. Being among the finest scholars and University lecturers...you know, being an Academic genius). I'm ambitious though reserved, outgoing but quiet. I'm also an incurable romantic and bohemian-type and am known to speak v. crude (in the grammatical sense) Latin.

I specialize in HP fanfic mainly, but I like reading other types too. As of late, I've grown badly addicted to Moulin Rouge fanfic and I have a few unfinished fics that I might upload if I can get around to finishing 'em.

Ummm, what else...I'm in love with the following men: Alan Rickman, Ian Hart, David Hyde Pierce, Alan Cumming, Richard E. Grant, John Hannah and the beloved boys of LoTR (elves! hobbits! woof woof! *cough*). And then there's my latest unhealthy obsession with Baz Luhrmann (you can thank Severa for that one).

Oh yes, my friends here. Sevvie, Iphigenia (author of Odi Et Amo and Greek and Roman academic-type. Go her!) and Threeoranges. Methinks we need a nickname for our clique...

Quotes of the moment (suspicious number of these are from Moulin Rouge, movie of the moment):

'Veni, vidi, nates calce concidi' (Latin for 'I came, I saw, I kicked ass)- Henry Beard
'Free the Tiger!'- Satine, Moulin Rouge (my current catchphrase)
'My Mum. A strange creature from when the gherkin was considered the height of sophistication.'- Bridget Jones, Bridget Jones' Diary
'I like him! The boy has talent! Nothing funny...I just like talent'- The Argentinean, Moulin Rouge
'Everything's going so well!'- Zidler, Moulin Rouge
