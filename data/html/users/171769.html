College is over! Finally!

About my writing: I write stuff that amuses me. Personally, I'd rather read than write, but when I can't seem to find anything good, I take matters into my own hands. Clearly this does not happen often. I've come to really appreciate oneshots. Probably because I don't have to update them...anyway, I'm still planning on finishing "Battles Won, Battles Lost". Just haven't gotten to it yet. And I haven't quite decided if I want to leave "Moving Forward" as it is or continue it. I think I ended it in a good spot, and continuing it would...take effort.

I love talking about anime, so feel free to drop me a note. If anyone ever wants to buddy up on a story, let me know...maybe we can work something out.

That's all for now. Happy reading!
