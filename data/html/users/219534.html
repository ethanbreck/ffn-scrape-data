Hi. My name is Jacy. I am an avid Potterfan, and probably always will be. I haven't written in ages, though I still read it a moderate amount. Unfortunately, most of my favorites (and almost all of my stories) have fallen behind the times and sort of lost track of canon. It's a terrible shame, really, though we do now have the comfort of knowing it will never happen again. And as much as I like to read things that gave up on canon a book or two ago, I just can't bring myself to write them anymore.

I fandomhop occasionally (I read Twific even though it's almost all dreadful, and occasionally I browse others). I am a sucker for clever innuendo, brave and realistic heroes, and funny-awkward sex scenes. I have cried at fic before, though I only admit the specifics to the author.

Someday I'll write again. I'm letting myself be lazy for now, but I'll come back eventually.
