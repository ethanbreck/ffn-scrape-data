I like to write all sorts of fiction genres so expect my work to appear almost anywhere. I'm a big Legacy of Kain fanatic too, hence my nickname. But I also enjoy crossovers of all sorts, X-men Evolution being my guilty pleasure, and I also appreciate reviews, the knowledge that my works are at least read is greatly appreciated.

UPDATE* Recently I became interested in the Titan AE franchise and have created a 14 chapter crossover fanfic, my largest and most ambitious story yet. (See Kilo Two Zero for that) I also rediscovered one of my old favorite cartoons, Where on Earth is Carmen Sandiego. Expect a fic or two to appear in that department. I write a lot about young soldiers marching off to war as well, because I believe it is at the same time the most noble and also the most frightening thing a youth can do. I tend to sympathize with the guy who loses the woman he loves. I dunno, I guess I'm a romantic at heart. I also sympathize with the guy who loves a woman but she doesn't even know he's alive. Hence the reason I sympathize with Josha from Where on Earth is Carmen Sandiego because I too have lived through that sort of rejection. At least that was until I read Morning Blue.
