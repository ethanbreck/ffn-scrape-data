Well, I assure you, in the five or so years since I posted them, I've gotten better. Or... so I like to think. Eeeh, I'll probably take them down some time, but for now, I'll leave them up. Partly because I know some of you will be absolutely crushed if I remove those two godawful suefics, partly because I have problems with throwing out my old things, partly to serve as a comparison or some smart-sounding excuse, and mostly because I'm lazy. Go minor characters and faceless minions!
