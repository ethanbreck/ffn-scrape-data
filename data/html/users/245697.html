Author has written 30 stories for Harry Potter, Newsies, Angel, and CSI: Miami.

So this is me: Bella7. I used to be just "Bella" but then they slapped a number on the end of it without my permission. Aside from that, I'm a fairly normal word addict with a ferocious beast of a cat named Radcliffe (after Daniel, of course) and an apartment which should probably be condemned. Such is life.

I like kisses, TV on DVD, Latino men, and British accents (real or fake, it makes no difference) and intend on being a published writer one day. I had just about weaned myself off of this coke-addiction-like fanfic high when my charming baby sister introduced me to a little something called CSI: Miami. It was there that I fell in love with a little pairing known as Eric and Calleigh. It's more or less taken over my life.

I love collaborations and have been called a proof-reading Nazi. If I can help in any way, don't be afraid to shoot me an e-mail or a private message

Also, I'm a narcissist, so reviews help.

Happy reading!
