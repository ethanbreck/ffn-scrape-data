I'm sixteen. A hopeless romantic. Daydreamer. A little sarcastic. Okay, really sarcastic. Japan is my passion. I love pineapple and dancing in the rain. I'm an artist. A little odd at times. I love my family and friends more than anything and would do anything for them. Spiders and heights are scary. I absolutely love the color blue. Writing is my forte. I'm a very organized person but I love spontaneity. I'm a sucker for boys who ride motorcycles and cute nerds (but not together haha). Swimming is pretty much the only sport I'm good at. My imagination runs wild. I'm just me.

"This feather may seem worthless but it comes from afar and carries with it all my good intentions." -Amy Tan, The Joy Luck Club

And considering I've finished it, I can update it really quick :) Sorry for the lack of updates.

Fragmented Wishes- I actually have the third chapter completely done, I just need to edit it.

All my other fics that aren't finished, which I think is only one...are on HIATUS now, yeah sorry, until I review what I wrote and figure out what I'm going to write, no updates on those for quite a while. A couple of new stories will probably be hitting the CCS fic page by yours truly and one of them based on a chapter of the The Joy Luck Club that I absolutely adored. Actually I loved the whole book. READ IT. It's AMAZING.

Anyways, hopefully I finish all this before school starts again which by then I'll be busier than ever.
