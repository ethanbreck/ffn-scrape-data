Hi. As you can probably guess, I'm Cat (though I do answer to a variety of names) and I hail from the beautiful city of rainy Glasgow in Scotland.

I don't write as much fan fiction as I used to but I read a fair bit and may update here from time to time. When it comes to my fandoms I have diverse tastes. Primarily I’m a slasher but I do enjoy a little bit of het now and again. My favourite fandoms currently include Harry Potter, Jossverse, X-Men, Lord of the Rings, you know, the usual ones, but I also like a bit of Doctor Who (the recent stuuf, not familiar with the older stuff), Stargate SG-1 (Daniel is my soulmate but...I like Jonas too, dammit.), Enterprise (yummy Trip) and even the odd spot of WWE (but not very often). There are many many more that I read but I can’t think of them at the moment because my brain is full of mashed potatoes.. I have lots of favourite characters and ships but I won’t list them, it’ll take too long.

So, uhm, yeah. Enjoy!
