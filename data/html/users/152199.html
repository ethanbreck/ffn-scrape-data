A little about me:

I exist. I enjoy reading, writing, and drawing. I currently work in insurance claims and am trying to put together a comic book series. Um...yea, that's about all I've got at this point.

Concerning FanFiction:

Well, if you couldn't already tell based on my list of favorites, I am a fanfiction addict for stories featuring Nightwing, Supernatural, The Walking Dead, and Lord of the Rings. I enjoy stories that send chills up my spine; the scarier the better, both physical and emotional trauma (including non-con). Please message me if you've got a story you think I'd enjoy!

My very first introduction to fanfiction was through a Lord of the Rings series known as The Mellon Chronicles by Cassia and Siobhan. Their stories will always have a special place in my heart, and I'm confident there are many others who feel the same. Most of the websites where the series had been posted have disappeared. Up until this year, I have only been able to find a few of their stories still floating in the online realm and some that I had saved as word files over a decade ago. Upon moving some of my own fanfiction to a new site called "Archive of Our Own", I discovered that you can find the full series there.

Completed 12/22/18: Fatal Attractions (Supernatural). A simple hunt comes back to haunt the boys in ways they never imagined. Settings at the end of season 2 and the beginning of 3. Although slightly AU, I am trying to keep as close with the series timeline as possible. The only main OC is the villain, any others will be side/minor.

In Progress: The Krampus Before Christmas (Supernatural). Sequel to "A Krampus Christmas Carol". Three years after the first onfrontation with the Winchesters, this fiendish holiday horror is back and out for revenge on our guys. Will a Christmas miracle save them this time? Includes kidnapped!hurt! Sam and protective!angry! Dean. ON HOLD.

In Progress: The Imminent Storm (The Walking Dead). Putting down your rabid brother-turned-walker can really take an emotional toll. Daryl needs a moment to grieve before recomposing himself and returning to the prison. Unbeknownst to him, the Governor and his men are still lurking in the area, looking for revenge and a little leverage. “Tell you what Rick, we’re gonna make this choice a helluva lot easier: eye for an eye, savage for a savage.” Alternate ending to ‘This Sorrowful Life.’ ON HOLD.

Happy Writing and Reading!
