Author has written 4 stories for Ranma, Rurouni Kenshin, and Inuyasha.

Ok, so I have no time for myself ever. But I have decided, that I cannot deny myself the simple pleasures that I once lived for.

I am no longer a teenager, with a carefree life.

I have a family, and children.

But I have gone through a lot of stress lately, and I have had a hard time with my depression. I have been trying to rationalize things that I don't think can be rationalized, such as my love for art, and writing. I have denied myself these things, letting them fall into the category of unimportance. But I think that I am at a time in my life where I am realizing, that those things cannot stay boggled up, that not doing them only makes life harder, and more depressing.

I have decided that I will try to find time to write some. I know, I never update anymore. But I still have the stories in my head, I just don't have the time to put them on paper. Plus I always end up writing myself into a corner with my stories.

I am moving to a place that gets no internet. Gaming has been my current love, and now I am having to let that go. It might be better for fanfiction though. I have started another story...

I know. I start so many...

Cant promise finishing anything...

But I can say that I will be having a ton more time on my hands... and nothing else to do...

Lets hope together.
