Author has written 16 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, StarTrek: The Next Generation, and Supernatural.

Charli lives in the UK with a large collection of action figures (including four Fox Mulder figures) and an Angel lifesize cutout. She currently has 6 points on her driving licence for speeding (another 3 have recently dropped off), and is the third cousin once removed of Robert Duncan who used to play Gus Hedges in the Channel 4 sitcom "Drop the Dead Donkey".

Charli is infamous in certain circles for having been stalked by Dominic Monaghan before he found success with Lost. He used to steal her rooftiles, now they just trade solicitor's letters about defamation of character.

Charli was recently bitten by the Supernatural plot bunny from hell and has been unable to write anything else since. She hopes this is just a temporary wound since a tiny little bunny called Smallville has recently started nipping at her ankles. Watch this space.

Before she dies Charli would like to have relations with Matthew Fox or Jensen Ackles. Or both if she really has to.
