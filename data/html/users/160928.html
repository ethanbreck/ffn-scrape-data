Hello! Yes. I am alive again.

I am the sole creator of the Lord of the Rings Fracas sisters Cliff and Ash, and their legacy will always be remembered as my developing writing prowess. Their story has been improved, revamped and recreated, but not entirely for this site. Hopefully if the project is rekindled the other versions featuring Cliff's new name "Clae" would be published here. But for the meantime, these time capsules would remain as they are until inspiration does come.

ASH: LEMME OUT OF HERE I AM SO GONNA KILL YOU -

CLIFF: -stuffs her back in-

Hehe, ASH STAY IN THAT TIME CAPSULE. Forgive me for that intrusion. -sweatdrops-

Their insanity can still be meddled with mine so they tend to escape my brain once in a while. Only once in a while.

Currently, I am a Bleach fan who is very fond of the Ichigo/Rukia pairing, especially Rukia. I have written a few prologues in order to introduce an original fiction intertwined with Bleach Fanfiction as well as Death Note Fanfiction. However that is also in the works.

I am also a Mabinogi NA player. You will find a real-life and Mabinogi-inspired fan/fiction which you could classify in the game's archives because of the footages and scenes mentioned that is part of the game. I rarely make any Mabinogi fanfiction, but please enjoy this short drama called "Never Online".

That is all for my profile.

Please enjoy!

~ Cliffhanger
