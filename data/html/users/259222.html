A Little About Myself
I'm retired, guys.
It's official.
I haven't written fan fiction in quite a while. I may someday update with a short fic here or there but that'll be it. I don't have the time for it anymore. I'm working a lot and fanfiction was really just a great way to pass the time when I was in high school but I have lots of big girl issues now and unfortunately, don't have for it. I do enjoy writing but my passion is not in fan fiction anymore. I'm done.
Someday, maybe I will write more Bonanza but I do not forsee anything else that's new coming from me.

It was fun!
Happy reading!
