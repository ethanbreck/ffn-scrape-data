Blinded by the Light is also due for a revamp, and most probably a new title as well. A New Beginning has been taken down, because it has been done a million times before, and I had another FY reincarnation type fic in the works that I enjoy a lot more.

Along with the old I hope to write some Final Fantasy XII fanfiction, as well as Pirates of the Caribbean because I love both dearly 3

Also debating on a new pen name, possibly LunarBasket, but I haven't decided if I should just make a new account for that. Only time will tell.
