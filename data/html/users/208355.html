Update, 10th June 2012: Since the safety of some fan fictions are not assured on fanfiction.net at present, and I don't happen to agree with what's been happening here as of late, I've decided to relocate my stories to AO3. If any of my stories disappear from this site, you can find them over here (in freshly edited form):

Note: I've removed a couple of my older stories that were written for the Buffy fandom. If you still want to find them, those are archived on the kitten board.

My current projects:

Blood Letting - Even though I said I'd never make a sequel for Halloween Special, I have anyway. There was plenty more story for Natsuki and Shizuru so I thought I'd tell it. Again, it's based around the Underworld setting but sticking with IC versions of the Mai HiME characters. Handles the effects of Shizuru and Natsuki's actions in Halloween Special.

Destiny Awaits - Based on the Mai HiME Destiny novel. For all those that aren't aware what this is... it's the next incarnation of the HiME universe, much as Mai Otome was, except it's only in novel form and a one-shot manga, I believe. I saw lots of potential for this and especially for ShizNatness so I went ahead and started writing a multi-chapter story.

Eternity's Last Embrace - A Mass Effect story, based on my FemShep, Commander Kristen Shepard. It is a Liara-centric story, set several centuries in the future where she reminisces about her time with Shepard. Somewhat melancholic but including all the things I'm known for.

The Art of War: An Introduction to Teasing - First of the requested shorts from my blog. This was requested by ifuritka. Just a short, fluffy piece focused around Shizuru and Natsuki, set shortly after Giving In. Second half of the story will be up soon.

The Chains That Bind - My updates have been getting fewer and further in between but I do intend to complete the story.

Completed:

Halloween Special - An AU Mai HiME fan fiction. Again, set around Shizuru and Natsuki, it takes aspects of Underworld and creates a modern vampire/werewolf setting for the characters. I had only planned on this story being in two parts.

Losing Grace - Mai Otome ShizNat, lemon. Shizuru returns after months away from Garderobe. After discovering her love is wounded, Natsuki wishes only to take all of it away. For mature audiences only, you have been warned.

Giving In - Mai HiME ShizNat, lemon. A four chapter exploration of Shizuru and Natsuki's emotional development after the events of The Carnival. Oh yes, and there's smut!

Discontinued:

Ascension - This project has been dropped; but Leebot has continued it on his own, so check out his profile to read it.

Sacrifice - This project has also been dropped.

All others - Long dropped into the annals of time unfortunately, though I still get harassed from time to time to continue with Awakening the Phoenix. Perhaps I'll take it up again just so I can say I finished it. I also feel rather guilty about leaving Four Seasons there as I had some grand plans for it. We'll see what the future brings eh. :)
