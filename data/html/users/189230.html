Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Bible.

23 years old and still hopelessly obsessed with LOTR :) I've just come back to fanfiction.net after a long sabbatical (3 years) and find I still enjoy reading the stories that people's minds can weave about my favourite characters - notably, the stories of Dragon Confused and Frodo of Bag End :)

I try to pay homage to Mr. Tolkien by writing fanfiction about his characters. I have a special affinity for Elladan and Elrohir, my Mirror-elves, but I also am partial to sick, baby, or "growing up" stories involving Frodo, Bilbo, Legolas, Elrond, Estel/Aragorn/Elessar, Arwen, Celebrian, Gandalf, Pippin and Merry. I pale in comparison to most of the writers on this site, but I'm happy that they have made their wonderful stories public so I can read them and improve :) Thank you all for your tales that keep me so happy :)
