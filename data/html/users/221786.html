Author has written 1 story for Gilmore Girls.

I'm new at this writing fanfiction thing. Though I've been reading them for ages. I have been published once before in a Chicken Soup book back when they were popular, however that little piece of work took 4 years to be published. I only sent it in at a friend's insistance. I'm a University student so I'll probably only be able to update a few times a week. I admire those that can update every two days. I know how much they write, and how little space the pages seem to fill online, and I also know those two days to wait for the next update seem like eons.

Sorry Trory Fans...don't have a heart attack...my story isn't over yet. I've had a few nice polite emails asking me when the next update will be...all I can say is hopefully right before Christmas. I've got 8 christmas finals at school, I've written four and I've got another four to go before Dec 21st when I finish. Thanks for reading and supporting my writing and I'll update soon. I've got the ideas in my head I just can't find the time to sit down for 2-3 hours and let the chapters come to life.
