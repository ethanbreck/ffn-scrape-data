Author has written 6 stories for Naruto, Dragon Age, Evangelion, and Sword Art Online/ソードアート・オンライン.

Not much to say about myself, I've been writing since I was twelve and I hope to one day make it my profession. I'm no one really special, just an average dude who works an average job (well, below average really) and wants to entertain people with my writing. I figure if I can make someone's day better with my writing, or inspire someone, and if I may be so bold, make a story so grand it's one they'll always remember, then I can ask for no more.

I love my fans, and I always try to respond to any PM's I get. I also am addicted to getting feedback and hearing what everyone thinks of my story, good and bad. Don't feel shy about wanting to ask me questions, or even sending requests in, I listen to everything.

Please enjoy my works, and may they bring you joy!
