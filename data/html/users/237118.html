Notice: As of January 2006, I am no longer going to be posting here. I've found I have very little free time, and because of it I no longer feel the drive to write about worlds and characters that have already been created. I'm an author, and always will be. Just not for fanfiction.

On the off-chance that I do write something new, anything (including previous works) can be found on any of the forums listed below (Shattered Glasses, Astral Light, Passionate Fire, ect)and on the site listed as my homepage.

Thanks to everyone who has supported me the past few years. I'll miss my dedicated readers. If you wish to contact me, all information is at the bottom of my profile.

Well, I’m an eighteen year old girl who wants to make a living off her writing. I have two novels in the works, one of which will definitely be published within the next ten years. When I’m not writing, you’ll usually find me reading. My grandmother is a librarian, so I’ve been around books my entire life.

My favorite authors include Kim Harrison, Tolkien, Tamora Pierce, Gregory Maguire, and Tad Williams, though if I were to write out the entire list it would take up more space than I have room for. Anyone reading this needs to go out and buy/check out a book by one of those authors and read it...you won't be disappointed.

I love to draw, and usually go with the same genre as my books. Fantasy and sci-fi. I've been drawing since I could hold a crayon, but nowadays I'm big into the art program at my school, currently in a Drawing/Painting II class, after having taken three other courses. Next semester I'll be taking AP Studio Drawing. I love the Art Club dearly and have worked on set design for the school musicals/plays, as well as helping put together decorations and such for the Special Olympics in our city every year.

As well as being a writer and artist, I’m also a musician. I play various instruments, from bamboo flute to piano. My instrument of choice, though, is the clarinet. I’ve been playing for more than eight years, and am pretty good. I’m part of my high school’s marching band, in my last year of participation before I graduate. Said marching band recently placed 2nd at Grand Nationals in 2004. I have been in one of the top two concert ensembles four years running.

My favorite actress is Carrie-Anne Moss, so it’s kinda obvious that my favorite movies include the Matrix, Memento, Chocolat, New Blood, Red Planet, Suspect Zero, and The Crew. Plus anything and everything else she’s been in. I have an autographed picture of her up on my wall right next to a Matrix poster given to me by the wonderful Trialia. If you read my stuff you’ll know I mostly write about her characters.

I write fanfiction for many fandoms, my longest running obsession being for The Matrix. About half of my stuff is written for that movie. One of my new obsessions is Fruits Basket, namely Arisa Uotani and Kyo Sohma. I also enjoy writing about various characters from the world of Tolkien, and then a few random bits from random books or movies. Also anything that has anything to do with Carrie-Anne Moss. :)
