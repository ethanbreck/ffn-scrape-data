Okay, what to say? I'm a huge fan of stories in general, but I go for humor, romance (sad, I know...), and some adventure. My favorite is Sci-Fi, though. :) I made the mistake one day of watching an anime on Toonami so now I'm hooked to DBZ and Gundam Wing (So I have no life? Is that such a bad thing? I'll develop one later...After I publish a book or something. :)

My friend and I trade books at school (I don't know if our school actually allows that, but then if they don't see us I don't have a problem). She is the one who gave me the Harry Potter books (You just HAD to do that didn't you? *glares at her friend*). And I (of course) go for the 'just this side of good' character. (Oh, I know, but I tend to do that. It's a habit I developed after watching too much Star Wars) Professor Snape was the most interesting character in the book. But that's the way things go for me right? The guy most likely to get killed off before the series ends and I have to like him. *sighs* Well, after I went through the entire series (less than a week and I had school to deal with too...) I found myself saying, "THAT'S IT??! SHE LEAVES IT THERE? NOW WHAT? WAIT FOR THE NEXT ONE?" So I looked around for fan fiction, and here I am. After reading through most of the REALLY good stuff in here I decided, 'What the heck. This place is my favorite website so I might as well register. ' So here I am, writing this stupid thing that almost no one will read, and wondering, 'Do I have too much time on my hands or what?'

Well, let me know if you like my stories. (You know, when I get around to writing them. Then putting them on...that stuff I tend to put off until the death threats finally make me do something about it. :) But seriously, any stories I put up I'd love any reviews. And if you decide to flame me...well I'm not THAT creative so I'll just do what this nice Gundam Wing author did. POST THEM~!!! *grins* Which reminds me...I wanted to see her most recent flames...*clicks the link*
