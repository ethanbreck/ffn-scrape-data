Time for some introduction I guess. There's not that much to be said about me, really. ;) My pen name is Nikoru Hagane (the japanese version of my real name), female, aged 20 and something (no, I'm not telling you... never ask a lady for her age, remember?) and I'm passionate writer. Whenever I can find the time and have the right inspiration I start typing away on my laptop.
My style is mainly focusing on three major genres: romance, drama and angst. Also, I'm a girl to play with words so when you read any of my works you'll find that out sooner or later. I like to be quite descriptive in my stories so that I can catch the proper scenery and maybe even the overall feeling. Oh, and dialogues are quite rare compared to other authors.
Most stories are first published on my livejournal before they end up here, so if you want to be the first to read the first versions of my latest works visit the following link and request to be added to my friends list.
And for those interested in the original stories I've written, please visit my fictionpress.com account.
