Author has written 8 stories for Saiyuki, Naruto, Final Fantasy VII, Lord of the Rings, and Get Backers.

I have been a long time fan of slash fan fiction since before the internet. I used to play with Man from UNCLE, Lord of the Rings (way before the movies), Starsky & Hutch and Miami Vice.

Then came anime in the form of Weiss Kreuz (released as Knight Hunters in the U.S.), Saiyuki, Gravitation and Yoroiden Samurai Troopers (Ronin Warriors).

When I received my first computer and found that there was a world out there full of slash and yaoi writers, I found my new home. :)

I am a fan of fangirl Japanese and Sindarin and Quenyan. I feel that these, when used within the flow of a story and explained in an appendix or glossary, can add to the enjoyment and the verisimilitude of the story. As much as I love these little flourishes in a story, you will certainly find them in mine.

I am a Mom of 5, grandmother of 10. I work in the Financial Sector and am currently going through chemotherapy for Breast Cancer. I use writing as a way to keep my mind off of serious issues, which apparently hasn't worked so well since I am also active politically and environmentally. I believe in equal rights for all and a clear separation of Church and State.

I love video games, with my favorites being Dragon Age I & II (and hopefully III when it is released this October), Harvest Moon (Tree of Tranquility), Rune Factory Frontier, Bravely Default and Final Fantasy VII & VIII.

My favorite movies are any and all of the Peter Jackson Lord of the Rings, Pirates of the Caribbean, The Avengers, Thor, Captain America and Iron Man.

My favorite books are The Hobbit, The Lord of the Rings, (J.R.R. Tolkien); I, Robot (Isaac Asimov); The Dresden Files series (Jim Butcher); The Scarlet Wolf series (Kirby Crow); Blood Ties series (Tanya Huff); The Nightrunner series (Lynn Flewelling) and The Heralds of Valdemar (Mercedes Lackey).

Aside from these specific books/series, I have a slew of favorite authors, too numerous to tell.

I read both fiction and non-fiction with a heavy leaning towards math, philosophy and physics.

My more mature works can be found on mediaminer.

I am in the process of recovering my fan fiction so I can re-upload those that meet the criteria of Fanfiction.net.

Please bear with me as I re-upload some of the old content.

Thank-you!
