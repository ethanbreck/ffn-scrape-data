Author has written 10 stories for Digimon, Flame of Recca, and Gilmore Girls.

A frequent lurker, an out-of-practice writer.

One-shots are my thing, okay? I just can't commit my time to anything more than 5,000 words... yet, here we are.

Favorite fandoms: Rekka no Honoo, Digimon Adventure and Gilmore Girls (the list used to be longer, I swear, but life keeps getting in the way)
Favorite character from a fandom: Mikagami Tokiya and Ishida Yamato because bisshies are best served cold
OTP of all time: been shipping TokFuu before knowing what shipping was, Takari was a close second
Favorite author: Jack effin' Kerouac
Favorite poet: Pablo Neruda
Favorite book: The Subterraneans

The secret to writing according to Kerouac:

  1. Scribbled secret notebooks, and wild typewritten pages, for yr own joy
  2. Submissive to everything, open, listening
  3. Be in love with yr life
  4. Something that you feel will find its own form
  5. Write what you want bottomless from bottom of the mind
  6. Remove literary, grammatical and syntactical inhibition
  7. Accept loss forever
  8. Believe in the holy contour of life
  9. No fear or shame in the dignity of yr experience, language & knowledge
  10. Write for the world to read and see yr exact pictures of it
  11. Composing wild, undisciplined, pure, coming in from under, crazier the better
