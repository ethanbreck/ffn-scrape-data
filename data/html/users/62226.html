Hi! I'm L. Emmist. Hmm ... what else? Ah, I know. I'm an eighteen year old guy living in a college dorm somewhere in America. To my right is a list of homework I have yet to do. Scattered around my desk are lots of Animorphs books. I frequently ignore the former to attend to the latter.

If you decide to try my stuff, you should know that The Wheel comes before The Heretic, and that The Heretic has major spoilers for The Wheel.

I love reviews. Reviews are my salary and my inspiration. Asking the right question could utterly alter the direction of my story. Just ask Oedipal Kat about that. Three cheers for my prime reviewers. You guys know who you are.

As for my stories, I only ask the following: Read. Think. Review. Enjoy!
