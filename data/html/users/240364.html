I love Lord of the Rings and Harry Potter. The worlds of Tolkien and Rowling are two of my favorite to visit in my mind. The enchantment, the danger, the beauty, the adventure, the love, and the magic make both worlds brilliant and beautiful. Please trust that when I write fanfiction for these worlds, it is not meant with disrespect, or frivolousness. I have the utmost respect for Tolkien and Rowling (and CS Lewis, as well, let's not forget the mastermind behind the Chronicles of Narnia) and would never insult them intentionally. I do my very, very best to keep my non-parody fics free of cliches and mary-sues. I do everything in my power to make sure that what I write fits with what I know of their respective worlds (hence why one of my fics is on hiatus since Harry Potter 7 came out - I'm still trying to fix the plotted-out part so that I keep it in the storylines of the book, with only few changes) and I do lots of research. Especially in the incredibly complex, unique world of Middle Earth.

Luineyende is my biggest effort in this. I am trying very hard to keep it non-sue, and in cannon. Please have patience with me - each chapter, once posted, will probably be revised at least six or seven times. As for my tales involving fairies... I'm using the English definition of fairy (Tolkien was English) and using The Hobbit as my reference guide. If you want an example of fairy-type creatures in his other works, you'll find Tom Bombadil and Goldberry, as well as Old Man River, Goldberry's father, the Ents, and other such.

Thank you for reading my profile. I hope this has kept you from writing me off as a deranged fangirl. I'm trying to slay the Dark Side of my Soul for the purposes of good fanfiction. Thank you.
