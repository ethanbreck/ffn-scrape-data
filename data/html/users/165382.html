Update as of August 2019: Hello, and welcome to my personal profile. I've been publishing on FF.Net since about 2001 and created a new profile from my original one because I felt embarrassed writing about more adult-themed topics and plots. Over the years, my story publishing as slowed down and I have started to rewrite old ideas that seemed rushed, less developed, and not enough character development. I decided to take a break from fanfiction to work on my own original works but honestly, it's hard to stay away from the comforts of something you know. I plan to continue working on both fanfics and original ideas while I finish up what I have here since it has been years since they were updated.

I want to personally thank you all, followers, reviewers, and readers, for the wonderful years of writing; helping me morph and develop my style into what it is today. My inbox will always be open and I will never forget this community.

About The Author
I am an aspiring accounting student, mother to a beautiful toddler daughter, wife, and American living in a foreign country. I enjoy a hot cup of coffee in the morning (maybe two), long walks on the beach, exploring my new location, eating street food, and enjoy showing my daughter what a real farmer's market should look like (even though she thinks the octopus and squids are gross). This is my first time out of the United States and I want to explore as much of this peninsula as possible since I don't know when we'll be moving elsewhere. I'm glad I'm living close to where the characters in my fanfics are since I can have a better understanding of the climate, weather, fashion, and culture of the area. I hope to be able to incorporate these new-found pieces into my future works.
