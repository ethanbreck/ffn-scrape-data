OMGHI!! ﻿I'm gonna' try to keep this brief.

I graduated from college with my BA in Japanese Language and Culture and a minor in Religious Studies in May of '06. I now have a full-time office job with the university which granted me my degree, and I've started classes on a second degree in Informatics. I'm an average person of moderate intelligence who has no particular skills or talents. I still live in the same town in which I was born and raised, and I generally suck at life. I'm incredibly and cripplingly shy, which is why I'm a chronic lurker. I may lurk on a particular site for years before I finally work up the guts to actually post anything, even if the posting is anonymous. In person I avoid physical contact, and even eye contact, unless I know someone very well because people (strangers in particular) make me nervous. Online, when I do interact, I think I come on a bit strong. I probably come off as an annoying sycophant, but honestly I wouldn't bother to say anything unless I meant it. Otherwise it's just easier to say nothing.

I'm also a chronic reader-non-reviewer. I know that's a major breech of fanfiction etiquette and I am really sorry for not telling all the great fanfic writers out there how much I appreciate their work. I promise I really am trying to change my ways, though. ._. Additionally, to anyone who's ever noted me or posted a review and never gotten a response; I want to apologize for that, too. It isn't because I don't appreciate the feedback, be it compliments, questions or criticisms. I really do appreciate when people take the time to let me know what they think. I just get so nervous that it's like pulling teeth for me to get past my anxiety and send a reply. I'm working on it, I swear I'm trying to do better. ;_;
