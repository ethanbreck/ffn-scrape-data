Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter.

Hi my names Nikki. Instead of giving you a bio of my life, I just wrote what I like to read and the stories I've written and their full summaries! I usually write Harry Potter fanfics. ENJOY!

MY STORIES:

1.) "Harry Potter, Time Traveler" -- Completed -- Set in Harry's fifth year. Harry opens a book in the Restricted Section that sends him twenty-one years into the future, where he meets the future Ron and Hermione, and their son, Eddie. But, Harry also meets Lily Potter, Harry's spunky, firey, future teenage daughter. Harry wants to know what the future has in store for him, but he thinks that Ron, Hermione, Lily, and Eddie are keeping secrets from him. Why is the future so important that secrets have to be kept from Harry?

Harry Potter, Time Traveler is also written in French; translated by Pheneatis: http://www.fanfiction.net/read.php?storyid=989798

Harry Potter, Time Traveler is also written in Spanish; translated by Amon Black: http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3553751/1

2.) "Lily Potter and the Change of Time" --Completed -- Sequel to "Harry Potter, Time Traveler" -- Thanks to the "Book of Time" and Harry's little vacation into the future, something changed. Something dramatic. Something that will effect Harry and Lily's life and destinies. But, you never want to mess with fate, it's not always for the best.

3.) "Lily Potter and the Final Prophecy" -- Completed --"Sequel to Lily Potter and the Change of Time" -- In her final year, Lily must face many obstacles; boys, relationships, rivalries, new teachers, her NEWTS, and not to mention the threat of Voldemort and the newly written prophecy. Now, Lily has found the ultimate weapon to destroy Voldemort and she doesn’t even know it! Also, with the help of her friends, Lily is on the verge of finding out who her true biological mother is. Now, Lily must overcome these obstacles to fight the final battle against Voldemort. It it the final battle between good and evil and only one can prevail.

4.) "Lily Potter and the Stone of Merlin" -- Completed -- "Sequel to Lily Potter and the Final Prophecy" -- It has been eight years since Lily defeated Voldemort and she has lived a very successful life as an Auror and has become Mistress of Magic. Now, after not seeing or hearing from him after all of these years, Lily has taken on her former friend's case and he insists on accompanying her. Now Lily and Eddie must use their ultimate powers to learn of the truth behind the kidnap and the Stone...before it is too late for both of them.

5.) "A Walk To Remember" -- Completed --Based on the movie and book "A Walk To Remember" starring Lily Evans and James Potter. Lily Evans was a unpopular, shy, quiet, and unfashionable girl who didn't care what others thought. James Potter was outgoing, popular, and loved by everyone. But, when their worlds clash, they will learn more about love than they ever dreamed possible, despite the fact that Lily is in serious trouble.

6.) "Harry Potter and the Heir's Revenge" -- Completed-- Set twelve years after Harry's seventh year. Harry has defeated Lord Voldemort, but his followers and Death Eaters will stop at nothing to seek revenge against Harry and try to bring their Master back to life. Because of this, Harry must find a way to protect his twin daughters, Lily and Jaime, from Voldemort's followers, and possibly, from each other.

COMING SOON:

1.) A possible Ginny/Harry fic: the entire series through Ginny's POV.

2.) Another possible fanfic involving Harry's future.
