Just a shameless GW yaoi fanatic. I absolutely crave 13x5,but I also love 3x4, 1x2/2x1,1x4, 6x5, 1x5, 1x2x5, 6x1... I just can't get enough. I've recently begun to dabble in other fandoms as well, such as Harutoki, Prince of Tennis and Sorcerer Hunters. If the muses are kind, you will also find stories for these fandoms here.

After a long hiatus, I've returned to Fanfiction.net. Since most of my stories are above the rating allowed, many will not be posted but whenever I do have something softer and citrus free, I've decided to go ahead and post it here. For all my fics, take a peek over at my web site at www.sweetromance.org. Or come say hello at my journal at michalyn.livejournal.com

Thank you for reading, and I hope you enjoy my stories,
