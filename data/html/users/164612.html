Author has written 25 stories for UC: UnderCover, Mummy, and Troy.

I have been writing since the age of eleven. It has been a much beloved hobby most of my life, but I wouldn't mind turning it into a career. However, I doubt that will happen any time soon, sooooooo...;) Anyway, most of my fanfiction will primarily focus on characters from The Mummy, The Mummy Returns, and UC: Undercover. I am a fan of many actors and musicians. Those who inspire my work are Oded Fehr, Eric Bana, Matthew McConaughey, and Johnny Depp. To those interested, I have original fiction published at fanfic's sister site, FictionPress.Com. Read and review, if you please. Constructive criticism is always welcome. Thanks!
