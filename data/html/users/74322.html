Joined 05-29-01, id: 74322, Profile Updated: 09-19-14
Author has written 33 stories for Silence of the Lambs/Hannibal, Labyrinth, Phantom of the Opera, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, X-Men: The Movie, Heroes, and True Blood.

I joined fanfiction.net in 2001, and became an avid reader and eventually found my niche in writing one-shots. However, I think I reached my acme of fanfiction writing some years ago, and defaulted back into become an appreciative reader and reviewer. However, every now and again, my muse comes around to bite me in the ass or I will read an old fanfiction of mine that is filled with great ideas but so appallingly flawed in many other ways that I simply MUST fix it.

Regardless, I had a rather large collection of Buffy: the Vampire Slayer fanfiction all stored under one story. With no warning, the entire fiction was deleted because I accidentally put a swear word in the summary. I was given no chance to correct my error, but rather was told that I was lucky my account was not erased all together.

This saddens me as Little Bits was home to just about all of my Spike/Dawn one-shots and now I have no records of any changes I may have made between the drafts I saved and the version of the story I eventually published. In the light of this, I have decided to start a project with no end date: to update all of my old one-shots. This is any anything-goes update-- meaning I could end up changing the path of the story all together or simply end up fixing the grammar.

I have also written some fiction for some more "contemporary" fandoms, but I am more interested in keeping my older OTPs alive because there aren't enough fictions written for non-traditional pairings as far as I'm concerned.

Hopefully you will enjoy reading my work as much as I enjoy writing it.

Best,

Claire
