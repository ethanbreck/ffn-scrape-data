Author has written 12 stories for 7th Heaven, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
Although I have been a "fan" of many shows throughout the years, the majority of fanfic I have written is for 7th Heaven. This is largely due to the influence of my favorite website, televisionwithoutpity.com, where a wonderful community of people gathers regularly to discuss the show's many shortcomings. I enjoy reading well-written 7H fanfic much more than I enjoy watching the actual show, and enjoy attempting to add to the canon. Additionally, I feel confident in my ability to produce a better product than what the WB serves us on a weekly basis. (Trust me, if you've been watching the show lately you'll know that's not a cocky statement.)

Also, I finally ventured outside the CamVerse and wrote a post-"Chosen" Buffy fic that I'm fairly proud of.

Overall I tend to enjoy writing and reading dark to semi-dark stories, although I try to lighten mine up with bits of humor here and there. In addition to my work on ff, I have written a pair of NC-17 fics which -- since they can no longer be posted here -- are posted at the homepage link above. "Rape, Lies, and Camdens" and its sequel "Beyond Redemption" were very experimental, and they ended up dragging in some places and going over-the-top in the others, but I'm still fairly proud of "To Be a Boy Again". It's short but sweet.

Enough rambling from me. I hope you enjoy my work. Feel free to review it and let me know any praise, constructive criticism, or gripes you may have!
