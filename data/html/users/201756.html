Christmas 2005

Uh...hi? Remember me? I'm the one who keeps saying she'll come back to her fics and hasn't yet. I'm here to tell you there's a very good reason for that: I'm still at university. After finishing my Bachelor's degreeI went on todo aMasters and now I truly know the meaning of the word'deadline.' I have three of them next week so I'm currently burning the candle at both ends to meet them.

A veritable barrage of reviews from Saline the Salt Lake Queen made me take a look at my fics for the first time in years and I have to apologise for my appalling lack of punctuation. I remember thinkingeverything was perfect,but now I know better. To repeat myself I really do want to come back to fanfiction but I don't know when it'll happen. I'll try to bash out a chapter in the near future but my Gundam Wing muse is stronger than my Cardcaptor Sakura one at the moment.

To everyone still reading and reviewing, what can I say?

Answer: "I'll update next week."

Well, I doubt that. But I haven't forgotten about you or my fics, and how could I possibly forget about Yue and Touya and Yuki and Fujitaka and Clow and...

Yes, well, you get the picture...

So here's wishing you a VERY Merry Christmas.

Ja ne!

Clow'd9
