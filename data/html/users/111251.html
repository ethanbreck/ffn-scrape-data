Things you might possibly want to know about me:

I am Hecate, goddess of the dark side of the moon and black magic and all things fluffy and shiny. If y'all couldn't tell, I'm a Texas girl (and by the way there are hardly any "cowboys," and we do say y'all because it's easier than saying "you all") I went to Washington DC this spring break and I got asked some of the stupidest questions: "Do y'all say y'all?" "Do you ride a horse to school?" "Are you a cowboy?" and several more that floated around that. ANYWAY I play the E flat Alto Saxophone and I specialize in writing stupid humour. My current anime obsession is TRIGUN! My current game obsessions are PACMAN and MORROWIND! I am working on a manga project (untitled) with one of my friends and also one by myself. I like shiny things. and pointy things. AND BLOODY THINGS! MUHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHHA! *AHEM* Pardon my french. I know Tai-Kwan-Doh and about three other chinese words.

Things I'm pretty sure you don't want to know:

I'M A PYRO! LOL j/k but I'm a VAMPIRE! j/k too. I'm actually a mighty and powerful goddess. Your best bet would be to call me a liar. I hate leprechauns and I am emotionally disturbed by things such as coloured cheese and peanut butter and jelly in the same container and jell-o in a tube. I like to spell words the Brittish way and I don't like tea. Anything else you don't want to know?

What are you doing reading my stupid descriptions of myself? Aren't you going to R&R?
