I'm an author in my own mind... unfortunately I cannot bring myself to hurt my characters (unfortunately I fall very deeply in love with anyone I create, unless it's a short-story. Those characters can be demolished), so all my stories turn into happy-go-lucky love fests that even make me want to vomit.

So I'm going to mess with characters that I like (but aren't mine) in hopes of leaving this tendency in the dust.

I love BtVS (huge spuffy fan), and I did love Angel for a couple of seasons (then it went seriously off track and I didn't find it very interesting until Spike turned up). At one point in time I was once an X-Files and Roswell junkie, but I probably couldn't remember their timelines very well anymore. I also adore Gilmore Girls (Jess and Rory belong together. It's a fact of life).

So wish me luck! Hopefully I'll start posting soon.
