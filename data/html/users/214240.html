Greetings. I just like to say a few important things on my behave of my absence:

1) I have been out looking for a job because well.. it says something about you when you're a 19 year-old teenager who still hasn't been given a job to take care of herself.

2) I have been contemplating on my future with FF.net. On the plus side, I get to hear what others think about my stories and imagination, however, I for one feel that there are way too many rules on FF.net that prevents me (at least) from getting my wild side out (unleash your imagination and free your sould my ass...) and it's really annoying wondering if your fic(s) is gonna get removed for some stupid reason the higher ups think up so I figure 'why bother'.

This has gotten me thinking 'why should I try to express myself if I have limits?'. Don't get me wrong, I will be reading fics by those who do stay and continue to send fics here, I just might not be one of them.

I just thought I'd give you all a heads up in case I do decide to leave so you don't send e-mails later asking 'Hey, why aren't you updating (Like I get alot of interviews anyway... well, maybe for a couple of my stories).

For Beast Wars Fans, my fics will be at lexicon.tf and bwint.net. Fro DBZ fans, I haven't found a location yet but I will try to find one soon.

Thanks for all your suport.
