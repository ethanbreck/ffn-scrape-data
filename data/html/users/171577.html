~ TRUTHS ~

I am a Gemini and AB+.

I like patterns and orderly chaos.

If I actually manage to write anything, I don’t often write about main characters or well-known characters – consequently characters such as Terry Boot, or Violet Strange might appear. Therefore, I like to ‘borrow worlds’ from their authors temporarily. (In such stories, usually the main characters of the canon stories are barely mentioned.)

I like reading stories about... whatever has recently struck my fancy.

I am picky - if there are problems concerning spelling, grammar or canonical inaccuracies that aren’t part of particular aspects of the plot or story, I will point them out to you - as gently as possible. I hope for the same from everyone else towards my "stuff".

I believe that everyone should have the right to express themselves: all I ask is that please try to do it tactfully, thoughtfully, and eloquently.

I really appreciate well written stories and interesting ideas.

I do not believe that characters have to die to a) prove a point, b) provide motivation for a character, or c) create catharsis for the reader.

I make mistakes. I am very sorry.

Treat other readers and writers as you wish to be treated - what goes around comes around.
