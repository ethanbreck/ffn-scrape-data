Update: This is a notice for anyone who's been to my site before or noticed that it's been down. It's back up again on a new server and I've changed the link. Unfortunately, I can't changed the home page of the site on the old server to link it to the new one, so I'll just be informing everyone through places like this.

Well, I go by several names online. The most common ones are "Muffles", "Meliachu" and "Muffin Squisher" (Wow, look at all thos M's! I guess it's no surprise if you know what my real name is... _;

I'm 16 years old. My favorite anime would be Slayers, followed by Yu Yu Hakusho, Rurouni Kenshin and Fruits Basket. Writing fics isn't really my thing. I tend to be more of reader, but hopefully I'll be able to build up my confidence about
writing with this new project of mine...

I'm a trad fan all the way. Along with subs. Alternate pairing can annoy me, but mostly if there is OOCness or character bashing involved. (One of my greatest fears is actually writing something OOC myself...) On a kind of related subject, I enjoy debating. x3

Most of the time my fics are parodies, or are humorous in some way. As for the parodies... Don't read them if you don't have a sense of humor, I suppose. I think my weak point when it comes to writing is romance, but like I said earlier, hopefully I'll be able to build up my confidence on it with this new project of mine.

Other than that I enjoy making AMVs (Anime Music Videos). If you want to watch any of my AMVs they can be found at animemusicvideos.org if you search for the Member "Muffin Squisher." I also enjoy web designing and icon making. I suppose it would be kind of pointless to say that I enjoy anime and manga?

Current projects...

The Thirty Kisses Series:

1. Theme #19, red, "Freebie"
2. Theme #18, "Say Ahh", "Sleep Rituals"
3. Theme #23, candy, "Fluff Day"
4. Theme #6, The space between dream and reality, "Walk Through the Flowers"
5. Theme #30, kiss, "Unconventional First Kiss"
6. Theme #20, The road home, "Vision"
7. Theme #5, "Hey, you know...", "Hay Ride"
8. Theme #10, ten, "Geezer"

The "Thirty Kiss Series" will be a series of short, usually unrelated ficlets featuring Amelia and Zelgadis from Slayers. They're written for a community in which you chose a pairing from any series, and then you must write or draw thirty things featuring that pairing, one of the thirty themes, and a kiss. The kiss doesn't have to be a real kiss, it can be metaphorical or between two other people, just mentioned... etc You can interpret the themes how ever you like. I'll be doing the themes out of order and I'll add more to the list as I complete each theme. For now, I've just gotten started... This is also the little 'project' I was referring to.

Update: The "Thirty Kisses" series will be translated into Spanish by Amelia F. Hook. x3
