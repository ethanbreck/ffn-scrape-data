January 16, 2005

sigh* Things are going much more slowly than planned. I've been incredibly busy with studying that I haven't had time to sit down and think of what I wish to right. Add that together with loads of family issues, and you get hell. I'm not going to make any promises of when the final chapter will arrive, because I would surely break that promise.

The future, which I still thought of as far away a year ago, has come rushing up on me. I'm studying hard so that I may graduate high school 2 years early, and so I can get a job without having to worry about studies.

After I graduate, which I hope to do by June, I need to get into shape, as I wish to become a restocker. I'll be doing that during this time of study and during the summer months. I hope to start a job by September, though part-time, as this will be my first job.

I'll be saving up so that I can go to college part-time to get a 4 year degree, but I hope to graduate in less than 4 years by challenging classes. I'm not sure what I'll be studying for, so that's another thing for me to do.

After I graduate, I plan on starting my career. My biggest dream is to become a manga artist, but I'm not sure if I'll get that far. So I'll probably settle for becoming a commercial artist or owning my own anime/manga store.

As you can see, I'm unavoidably busy. I'd love to finish this story, which plagues my mind, but without the time or the proper inspiration, I don't know if I can. To try and get the proper inspiration, I plan on searching for a new muse (as my last left long ago) and possibly revamping the entire story. My writing level has improved a little bit since I wrote that story, but has also darkened considerably. Life has taken its toll on me, as I'm sure it has on many of you, and I've come to love the darkness more than the light airyness that is a normal childhood.

Again, I apologise for taking so damned long. But I hope that all of you can understand now. If anyone wishes to contact me, I'm no longer with Juno. My new address is: Fluffy_Seph @ yahoo . com. Without the spaces, obviously. I've heard that certain spammers search solely for complete addresses, so this is a precaution.

Allena
