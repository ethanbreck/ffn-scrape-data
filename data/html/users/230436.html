Just another Final Fantasy VII fanfic writer...

Anyway, I've been writing fic for about five years now (and have slowed down quite a bit, though I've no plans on stopping). Most of the fics in this account are comedy, though I've started posting some of my more serious works here (none of the epics yet?, though). If you'd like to read any of my other fics now, please check out my site.

In general, constructive criticism of my works is discouraged; you can leave it, but don't expect me to follow it. I am a perfectionist by nature; as such, my fics generally go through three or four drafts before they are posted to my site, and even after that, are prone to revision. If anything shows up here, it's usually something I'm happy with, or otherwise have no wish to go back to. The only concrit I'll pay attention to are spelling and/or grammatical corrections, and any tips I can use in the future. If you don't like my plots, characterizations, and/or pairings, please move on and read something else. I won't mind :)

If you do like my work, please drop by my site when you have a chance for even more. I am no longer updating this account, but I'm still writing fanfiction.
