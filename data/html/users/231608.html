Well, this is my fourth or so change of name. This might or might not be final.I'll tell you a bit about myself. My name is of no consequence, I am young and idealist, and reside in the United States after living 16 years in another country which I still love. I enjoy staying up late with friends, talking about subjects ranging from politics to abortion to religion, anything controversial. I consider myself a romantic, the Quixote of my generation, and hope to become a statesman or some sort of leader someday. I follow the Roman Catholic religion, not to the letter, of course (we all need change). Politically, I swing for the left, and don't advocate any particular political creed, other than a Moralistic Democracy, or Democratic Socialism.I have no opinion on the current election(two old,rich, white guys...not a lot of diversity), but I sincerely hope for the Democrats to win. There is nothing i fear most than ignorance in charge of nuclear missiles. I listen to rock, death-pop (Orgy, Deadsy, HIM), and old-school music (J.A.J, Molotov, etc.), and dance (Paul Van Dyk, Oakenfold, Infected Mushroom). I'm just trying to carve a niche in the world, using guitar and claw, sword and pen.
