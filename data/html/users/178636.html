March 2007

This would be the first time in 5 years that I'm finally updating my bio lol so here goes:

I'm a twenty-something year old female of mixed parentage, and people have often confused my appearance as being anything from Russian to Greek to Japanese to Middle Eastern to British.

Anyways, I speak 3 different languages.

I'm currently stuck in college studying design, or to be specific majoring in being stressed and broke when I would much rather be whoring myself to some big corporation and earning money.

My daily routine usually consists of breathing, eating, sleeping, and dragging myself reluctantly to class on some days. Most of the time, I'm online. Occasionally, I read. If I want to, I draw. I love drawing manga-styled characters!

My specialty? Chomping down a Big Mac in 15 seconds.

I was once able to down a whole bottle of grappa without throwing up afterwards.

Oh, and I eat from Hello Kitty plastic utensils.

That's right, I'm a wuss hahaha...

I used to be obsessed with the Harry Potter series a few years back. I've toned down a bit for now though, but the love shall forever remain :)

Anyways, I've been reading HP fanfics since 2001, so some of my all-time recommendations would include but are not limited to:

Cassandra Claire's Draco Trilogy - the one that started it all → Draco in leather
Roman Holiday - 1st part of a trilogy, complex and brilliantly written
Sometimes the Dragon Wins - absolutely hilarious!
Underwater Light - one of the best Harry/Draco fics I've read so far
Irresistable Poison - fantastic storytelling
Once Upon A Thyme - well developed plot
Hermione Malfoy - witty like hell... I like! haha
Valentine Encounter - couldn't help doing a marathon with this one
Customer Service - brilliant & witty to the core!
The God of the Lost - D/Hr adventure galore
Harry Potter and the Nightmares of Futures Past - the HUGE number of reviews speak for themselves

I absolutely adore Draco/Hermione ships. Harry/Draco and Sasu/Naru pairings are top of the list as well.

As for Naruto ficcies, some of the best I've read would go to these:

I Fell in Love with a Boy - absolutely one of the most BRILLIANT & HILARIOUS pieces of writing I've come across
Walking Corpse - extremely well written & touching, was in tears with this one
I Think I Better Leave Right Now - read this while listening to Gackt's Last Song piano acoustic and bawled my eyes out haha...
Team 8 - also from the talented author of Nightmares of Futures Past

I've got some serious issues with stories being posted up without having been beta-d, so feel free to utilize my OCD (re: perfectionist) tendencies.

Cheers~!
