Thirsty for Your Love has won several awards on various platforms and I'm so very grateful for everyone's consideration. I'm touched and thrilled that this story I wrote on a whim has resonated with so many. Thank you all for reading - you make my day :)

I know fanfiction places us all in the same creative sandbox, but I do request should anyone wish to use my original characters/plots/ideas or should you wish to translate my stories to another language, then please PM me beforehand. I'm certain we can work something out - truly, I can't think of a bigger compliment than someone wanting to use something I've created in their own work. There's no need to take my story and paraphrase it as your own - though you may have changed some words, that is still stealing and absolutely uncalled for... And it always get found out for the plagiarism it is.
