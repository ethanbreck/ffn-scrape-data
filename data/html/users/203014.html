Hey all! Just wanted to keep you guys updated about moi and of course, my stories. First of all, lo and behold, I am not dead ^^, I'm very much alive and well!! But...hehe...Listen,
to those who read and like (if anyone does -_-;;) my stories, I'm sorry to say that i have decided to DISCONTINUE WRITING ALL OF MY UNFINISHED STORIES...

WHY?! Well, for one thing, my reason aint that "I'm too busy to update" crap. I think I'll use the "I'm stuck in writer's block" line?...hmm...that's a bit true. But most of all,i think i'll just sit back and read other people's work. Not only does that entertain me, but i also learn how to write better. I recently looked over my stories and let's just say, I'm not very satistfied with it. The plots are cliched, okay grammar, but certain areas need to be more broad and descriptive...Basically, needs improvement.

However, I'll keep them up for those of you who want to keep reading them over and over again (which i don't think anyone will do...:P) or get ideas from them. I apologize for those few who waited and waited for so long for me to update.

Thank you to all those who reviewed. I really appreaciated all of them very much. :) They motivated me to add chapters to my stories.
Don't worry, I'll still be around reading other people's work. I'm not promising anything, but if I get bitten by the inspiration bug, I *might* either update on my unfinished works or write new stories...Have Hope!!!:)

Ta Ta!! Oh, and...Sailor Jupiter rulz!!
