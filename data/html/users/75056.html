Author has written 30 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Fushigi Yuugi.

Fanfiction is my life. I love reading it, I love writing it. I'm a huge yaoi fan and really, that's all I ever write anymore, though not all I read.

Due to the recent policy changes at ff.net, I have removed all fics that once contained an NC-17 rating. I am in total disagreement with these changes. I know ff.net has the right to make these changes, but it doesn't make it RIGHT. But I'm done with my little protest now. I have removed the following fics:

"Openings"
"Distraction"
"All This Time..."
"Sí Beag, Sí Mór"
"Lather. Rinse. Repeat."
"Sakura Trilogy"
"Cherished"
"The Equipment Room"
"The Headmaster's Office"
"Afterimage"
"Houseboat Haven"
"Final Admonition"
"Absolution"
"Act of Contrition"
"A Mote in God's Eye"

Note: "Shall We Dance?" is now revised and repost, sans lemon of course.

These fics can still be found at A Little Piece of Gundam Wing ( ), or at Shinigami and Wing ( ). Thanks to everyone who left lots of kind and lovely feedback for these fics. My only regret in deleting them is losing those comments. Thanks again, and please visit my fic site for any future updates on stories.
