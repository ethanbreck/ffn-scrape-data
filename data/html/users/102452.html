I am frooit, lil_neko, it. With the old breed, yo. Or something like that. Don't be afraid to contact me through PM. I'll answer questions, consider requests, or suggestions, or just say hello back and ramble on about the weather, and my favourite character, or whatever. I don't bite. And when I do? It's not very hard. Unless you deserve it. Then I'll probably leave a good mark. Because that's just how it works, right? That's just how it works...

Current fandoms: ff7 (final fantasy vii) and sometimes random stuff...

Typical staples: UST (unresolved sexual tension), male/male relationships, sudden smut, character studies, the series that never ends DID end (almost), character maiming, "why is this happening?", copious amounts of cursing, general unkindness to characters, unusual prose, probable purple prose, floppy tenses, too much wordplay, drama, drama, drama, pain, tragedy, and everything else I'm certainly forgetting.

Remember, kids: this is for fun. I am a simple fan. I make no monies. I want no monies. All blood, sweat, and tears (and induced rage) are on the house.
