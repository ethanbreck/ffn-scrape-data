It's been a while, but goodness, I AM BACK!! \o/

I'm still an avid Buffy fan. Period. The end. Forever, and ever, and ever.

My latest obsession are books by Charlaine Harris, more specifically, her Southern Vampire Series. I love those books to pieces (as well as the oh-so-sexy Viking of a vampire, Eric Northman). And grown from my addiction to the books is my obsessiveness with True Blood (of course, would you expect any less?).

I've rekindled my interest in LJ Smith, author of The Vampire Diaries series since she just recently (finally) came out with a sequel to those books... It's been 15 years since I read the last book in her series, and I am so glad there's a new one out! Though... upon reading the new installment, I find that I like the original series that I read back in 1994 so much better.

I've always dabbled in anime/manga, but just recently I found myself majorly addicted to it. Among my favorites are: Fullmetal Alchemist, Trinity Blood, Fruits Basket, and Ouran High School Host Club (I am such a Kyoya fangirl, it's almost embarrassing...).

I finally have found the time to start writing again, so keep and eye out for some new work from me.

Works in progress:

An Eric/Sookie collection of fluff/smut ficlets. (I've been adding little drabbles day after day, but I've a lot to edit. It will be up hopefully in the next coming months!)

MOAR OURAN. Yes, this series has probably been the sole reason why I've started writing so much again.

Speaking of which, I'm slowly working on getting more chapters of Devoid up. Please be patient!

I'm always up for requests. 'Tis why I've been spending so much time on the LJ Ouran Kink Meme (which is the bestest place for any Ouran smut/fluff/and whatever you wish for) lately...

BUT I'm also up for any requests from any of these fandoms:

- The Vampire Diaries
- True Blood/Southern Vampire Mysteries
- Buffy: The Vampire Slayer
- Fullmetal Alchemist

Happy reading!
