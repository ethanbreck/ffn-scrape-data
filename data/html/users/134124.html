UPDATE

Um. Mass deletion of all my old fics. I hate doing this, as I know a lot of these are favorites of some people. But I can't handle having some of this stuff online anymore...4 years since my last update of this profile, reading through all these fics, and...just. Gah. The badness. Ick. I'm keeping up my Power Ranger fics, and maybe one or two Harry Potter. But most BtVS fics are gone. Out the window.

That said, I'll be populating my ff.net account with my stories from LJ. So that's something.

ABOUT ME

Twenty year old university student in NJ. Loves anything Whedonesque and Power Rangers. Needless to say, am a HUGE HUGE HUGE dork. But I'm in good company here :) I write primarily on LJ, so my list of fics up to the minute can be found here: and that includes NC-17 and RPS. So hit it up, yo.

Archival of my fics is fine, as LONG AS YOU ASK and send me linkies. Same for noms, which I am always grateful for. Feedback is what I live for here, so it's MUCH appreciated. Other than that, I love meeting people, so drop me a line on AIM, please.
