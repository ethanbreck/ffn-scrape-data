Author has written 37 stories for ER, Star Wars, Stargate: SG-1, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Stargate: Atlantis, Downton Abbey, Hobbit, Being Human, Sleepy Hollow, Sherlock, and Lucifer.

Welcome!

I'm a novelist and screenwriter who should be pouring all of her creative energy into her publishable works but can't resist the joys of writing fanfiction, for fandom is such a warm, welcoming place full of friends both old and yet to be made!

I write professionally under my initials K.M. Rice. You can learn more at kmrice dot com. As of 2016, I have three published books with a new series readying to launch called Afterworld. All are available on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, and a handful of physical bookstores.

And as some of you already know... I am Kili from the webseries Happy Hobbit.

If you enjoy my stories, then you are sure to love those of my sister, Alex Took, as well! You can find her under my favorites. Though I am older, she is the Fili to my Kili!

Much love to all!
