Author has written 36 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Lois and Clark.
It all started one night, when I got an e-mail from my friend, Danielle (Who writes great Highlander fics) talking to me about Fanfiction.net. I already knew about the place, but didn't really get into actually posting up fics here.

I thought: Wow, a big ol' place where I can put my fics up and more people will read them. Before this, I just stuck around with yahoo groups and a couple other places. But now...well, I'm sure that no one really cares about stuff like that.

Anyway, I LOVE Angel and Buffy! B/A Forever you guys! (Damn Joss...we've ALL been Jossed way 2 many times) I write B/A fics- mostly pure fluff, since I have been in denial (and living in 2nd season) ever since the summer after 3rd.

My site I run is devoted to Angel, the show. Stop by there, and you can see ALL the fics that I have, and read some others. I hope you all enjoy my fics that I have written.

~*Rachel*~ A.K.A. Soul4Angel
