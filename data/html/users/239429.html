Finally getting around to posting some stuff. Most of these I wrote a while ago; I just haven't felt like posting. x3

I'm not as much of a canon freak as I used to be. What can I say, I'm a rabid slasher now. I have fallen to the dark side. At least it's yummy here. 8D

I'm a lurker, so my posts are few and far apart. I'll mostly be posting one-shots, because for the life of me I cannot stay focused long enough to post a chaptered fic. Ehehehe?

I am the Amalthea of the We Looked Like Giants equation.

My dæmon is settled as a palm cockatoo.
