Author has written 3 stories for Shakespeare, Song Fics, and Harry Potter.

So, my best friend and I decided to post things on here way back...and just so you know, we're not completely insane. Our screen name actually comes from one of our high school summer reading books. I suggested we use the book "Of Mice and Men" just to be funny because I knew we'd never think of a good name from it. However, my friend then came up with our screen name because (if you haven't read the book) there's a character who hallucinates this crazy giant rabbit at the end of the stories who talks to him. Anyway, we did our crazy Romiet and Juleo (which, by the way, I can never say the right way now) story but eventually she stopped reading stories on here and I kinda took over. So now it's just me and I've got tons of ideas for my "Hogs, Warts, Deaths, and Eaters" and I hope to post new chapters more often. So keep reading! Thanks!
