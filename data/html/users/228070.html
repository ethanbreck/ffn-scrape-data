*this is a work in progress*

i love harry potter. i guess that i just get caught up very easily in many things... besides harry, there are my obsessions over buffy, angel, star wars, roswell, alice in wonderland... we'll leave it there, shall we???
anyway, i first read the harry books in my first year of university (2000). i was seriously depressed and having a hard time dealing with change and a lack of support. my little brother had gotten completely caught up in the potter-verse, and he lent his books to me. my fondest memory of my first year at uni? sitting in the library devouring the words of the brilliant j.k. rowling. without harry, ron and hermione, i would not have survived that year. and now, two years later, i'm still caught up in ms rowling's world!!!
ummm... i guess because this is the "bio" section that i should offer some info about the wonder that is moi... so, here goes...
my name is rach, i'm (almost) 21, i live in melbourne, australia. at the moment i'm in my third year of a double degree at uni - teaching and arts. i'm majoring in history and literature, and have just been offered a spot in the honours program for history!!!! yay!!!! i have a natural affinity with children, and i guess that my obsession with harry potter (the books and the film) reflects this. on my last lot of teaching rounds, i spent far too much time conversing with my grade 5/6 students about the world of magic. i amazed them - they had never encountered a so-called *adult* who can get so completely caught up in something *cool*. yay! go me!!!
want more? well, my favourite character is hermione (she reminds me of myself... not always in a good way), and my favourite pairings are ron and hermione, and ron and harry. i love slash fiction, but only if it's done right!!!
i haven't written fanfic since my hanson-obsessed days (somewhat far behind me now - thankfully) but i've got ideas running around my (already far-too-crowded-and-overactive) mind... so watch this space!
email me! i love chatting harry!
~rach
