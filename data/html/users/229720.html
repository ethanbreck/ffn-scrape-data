Author has written 15 stories for Warhammer, Doctor Who, Dungeons and Dragons, and Elder Scroll series.

I'm a 47 year old English teacher with a love of good sci-fi and fantasy. I've been writing for pleasure since I was about seven when my first fanfictions were based on 'Doctor Who'. What do I enjoy in fiction? Interesting characters, involving, multi-layered plots. And that moment when the hair on the back of your neck stands up or a shiver runs down your spine. I try to incorporate those elements in my own stuff with, if I'm being strictly honest, varied results.
