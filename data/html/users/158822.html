Well, hello there!

I've started writing a long time ago and I've always posted my stories here, so you will find lot of old ones in my portfolio alongside with the more recent stuff. I admit some of them, mainly the early pieces, are pretty cringe worthy, at least in my opinion, if you enjoy them nevertheless, I'm glad. I thought about deleting them, I even did with one of the stories I couldn't stand reading myself, but I think I'll leave things the way they are. Looking back on the time and place I wrote some of those stories, it helps me remember how I felt back then, what sort of person this younger me was, a little bit more enthusiastic and idealistic. So, if you look at it that way those stories sort of document my growth as a writer and person. Like a diary (those usually tend to be a bit embarrassing as well when you read them again later). So that's why I keep them.

Still, I'd recommend the more recent stories...
