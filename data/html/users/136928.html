Updated Wednesday, November 20, 2002, 10:10PM EST I have noticed that I have gotten (and still get) a lot of reviews telling me that when I say 'Tarx' in my FF7 fanfiction I mean 'Turks'. No. I mean 'Tarx'. This is the original Japanese translation, and even in the game, you can see that the translations are faulty. Reno often tells Elena that she is a 'Turks' and shouldn't be weak. 'Turk' means 'foriegner'. They are not foriegners, they are a group of assasins. They are Tarx. I will not use 'Turks' in my fanfiction, so be warned that if you flame me for it, I'll just ignore it, and bring up the fact that in Sector 5 when you help the guy in the pipe, Aerith (Yes, Aerith, another original translation) says 'This man are sick.' .. Faulty translation is all over the game, so I'm going to stick with what I know of the original Japanese.
