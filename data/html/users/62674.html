Author has written 94 stories for Harry Potter, Sailor Moon, Chronicles of Narnia, Lucifer, and Sherlock.

Profile last updated on June 8, 2018

I have started writing for two new fandoms recently. I am a big fan of the TV series 'Lucifer' and am a bona-fide Deckerstar shipper. Also a huge fan of BBC's 'Sherlock' and am a bona-fide Sherlolly shipper.

I NEVER doubted Severus Snape's loyalties. R.I.P.

Caveat lector...I don't usually deal with traditional couples. If you are not partial to Severus/Hermione stories, there will be precious little for you to read. I do occasionally dabble with the more canon couples.

I have not abandoned 'Price of Redemption' and am committed to finishing this story at some point in my life. It is entirely plotted out. My real life (I'm a married mom of three children and I work full-time) is quite full and I haven't been able to write as much as I would like. All other stories are to be considered COMPLETE.

LIKES

Well-written, original fanfiction that places effort on good writing, decent plot & character development.

DISLIKES

Plagiarists (contrary to the old saying, imitation is NOT the sincerest form of flattery)

IMPORTANT NOTE REGARDING ACCURACY OF STORIES

Please take a moment to look at the dates when stories were written or started. Many were written when "Goblet of Fire" was as far as we'd gotten and so must be considered AU and OOC at this point.

And, regardless of publication date, any story that features any kind of relationship/love/friendship between Hermione and Severus (or basically any ship other than the canon ones) must also be considered non-canon, AU and OOC...for those of you who care about that sort of thing.

AND FINALLY...

Special thanks to my family, my friends and all my readers!!
