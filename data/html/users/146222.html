Author has written 24 stories for Chrono Cross, Dragon Ball Z/Sailor Moon, Final Fantasy VIII, Sailor Moon, Sailor Moon X-overs, Legend of Dragoon, Labyrinth, Vampire Princess Miyu, Inuyasha, Final Fantasy VII, and Harry Potter.

So, it's been seven years since I've updated anything (nine in some cases). After stumbling across the old e-mail this account was linked to, and reading more-or-less recent reviews for some of my stories-- curiosity got the best of me.

I'll be updating sporadically as practice/a hobby. I don't have a ton of free time, but I figure I need to get over this creative writer's block that took hold around the time that I stopped writing.

I can't promise that I'll update frequently, but usually I start from the bottom and work my way up-- so stories that have been abandoned the longest get attention first. (I will probably also be doing some re-writing of old chapters as I update-- mostly just grammatical corrections and some smoothing out.)

I've done away with the banter between the heteronyms of: Thor, Vale, Vince, Tomoe, and Fantasy Girl.

Unless you all enjoyed the muses and their discussions; if you did, perhaps they can come back.

Old readers, thanks for sticking around! New readers, welcome!

-Saturn Angels
