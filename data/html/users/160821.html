Why am I updating this? Why, I'm NOT procrastinating, how can you tell? ^^;;;

Ahem. Since someone actually asked how old I was because of the 48 years thing I placed here ealier (you know who you are and you know I'm totally teasing. ^_~), I decided to actually put something about me. So, here.

NAME: qkslvr (yes, it's on my birth cert)
AGE: I'm ancient. 20. NOT 48. --;;; (sorry, can't let that slip *giggle*)
BIRTHDAY: December. If you really wanna know, that means you're gonna give me a gift. If that's the case, my email's up there. ^_^

Okay, I can't think of anything else to put there. So, I'll just insert an 'author's survey' here. From the lovely people in LJ:

1. What's the name of your current project?

Learning to Breathe. And that new Haru/Kyou thing I'm planning on writing.

2. What kind of work is it (novel, play, short fiction, etc.)?

Fanfic. Multi-chapter. Yep. That's it.

3. How long have you been writing it for?

Since June 6. At least that's when I first posted it. *shrug*

4. How far have you got in that time?

Not that far. ^^;;; Just a prologue (that's soooooo not related to the chapters after it) and two chapters. Third chapter in the making. For the last three weeks. --;;;

5. Have you planned out the rest of the work?

smiles sheepishly* ...that's actually the reason why I can't continue it. I did the whole thing on impulse and now I have no idea where it's supposed to go. Which is why I planned to making another one. That's to take away one part of the plot because the original plot was sooooo damn complicated. --;;;

6. Is it going to be a standalone, or could there be sequels?

Read above. It's not really a sequel, per se. Because I plan on working on the 'sequel' first because I know the plot to that compared to the other one. So, it's more like an...extension. Hmm...I can make it a sequel...maybe not. One complication's enough, thanks. ^_^

7. Have you written anything else with the same characters?

Erm...attempted, yeah.

8. Would you consider writing an erotic story based on the work or the character in it?

laugh* You HAD to ask, dintcha? I would, but I don't have the skill. ^_^ But my imagination's amazing. ^_~

9. What would you say are your main influences?

Ehh? WTF? The show itself. And my imagination that won't leave me alone. *beats imagination with a stick*

10. If you could do a crossover story with another famous author who could it be?

Hmm...I have a lot in mind right now...but I ain't telling...

11. Do you have friends who write as well?

Hai. Mostly online ones. But my RL friends prefer to write stuff of a different genre.

12. Have you ever done a crossover with your work and theirs?

Iya.

13. Ok! So your work's been a massive hit and a big movie company wants to make a movie of it. Whom do you cast?

After having everyone in that movie company's heads checked, I'd stick to the original ones. Seiyuu, that is.

14. Would you say there are any particular themes/issues that run through your work?

laugh* They're all in progress. Save for those one-shot things.

After 48 years and a half, I finally have a homepage. Even if it's just a blog. Oh, and I removed my mile-long bio. It's outdated, it's annoying. And it's long. ^^;;;
