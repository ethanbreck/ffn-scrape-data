Author has written 19 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, Star Wars, and CSI.

I am a mother of two children, three if you count their father.

A lot of you may have noticed that I have been MIA for a few years. I haven't been writing stories and my fan fiction archives were taken down. This was because "real life" got very crazy on me. My job became crazy and I was always too tired to write or maintain the archives because of it and other real life issues. Plus I was taking some online classes in the little free time that I did have.

I have recently had a lot of changes going on with my real life. I have a different job now, which is less stressful and less tiresome, which therefore gives me time to write again. Sometimes I can even do a little writing while at work. I plan on updating some of my unfinished stories, although some may never be finished do to lack of interest in the fandoms and co-authors that I don't write with anymore and rarely even talk to anymore. Another hindrance to finishing some of the unfinished stories is that I lost my flashdrive that had all of my stories on it (I'm still trying to find it, but it's been months since its disappearance and I'm not very optimistic.), I was able to retrieve some stories that were written in notebooks or saved to old flashdrives and or CDRWs, but they were most of my older stories. I'm missing a lot of my more recent stories like my CSI stories. I had the Double Life story complete and a sequel started, but just hadn't gotten to put the new chapters online those chapters are now gone and since I haven't been writing or talking to the co-author I doubt they will be re-written. Another CSI story April Fools Nightmare, had a chapter or two finished that were not put online because they needed to be edited first and those chapters are gone now too, however, I found a notebook with a missing chapter for that story and I have a couple ideas of what I want to do to finish that story, so that one may be updated (I can't make any promises though).

In addition to writing again, I decided to make two new fan fiction archives. A Journey Through the Las Vegas Crime Lab is a CSI: Crime Scene Investigation fan fiction archive. And A Journey Through a Galaxy Far Far Away is a Star Wars fan fiction archive. If you want to check either archive out send me a PM or email message psychoschultz@yahoo.com and I will give it to you. Adding a URL doesn't work on this site.
