Fiat has four cats and far too many computers. She gardens obsessively. She is hard at work on Out of the Blue, which story has basically taken over her brain. When she's not writing, working, or out in the garden, she co-owns The Floo Network, a 6th year Harry Potter RPG.

Given the current state of FFnet and the fact that things get removed without review or warning, I'd like to invite readers to click the "Homepage" link in my profile, which will lead you to my author profile on a more reasonable archive (not that I don't think FFnet has a right to say what's on their system, but deleting things without checking them and without warning the author so that they can have a chance to save and delete their own work is doubleplusungood.) If you're going to review my stories, I would really prefer you did it there, as the other archive has a policy of allowing 14 days for the author to comply with their TOS after a violation. Much better.
