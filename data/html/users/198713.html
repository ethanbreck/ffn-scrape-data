28/9/06 - As of yesterday, Chain Reaction is back in full swing. I've one new chapter up, and all the other chapters have been redone.

However, I am also currently working on What Dreams May Come, my Syl centered fanfic, and that means chapters for both may take a little while to get out. Especially since I've lost half of my ideas relating to Chain Reaction (this is what happens when you ignore a fanfic for years), and have to spend a lot of time trying to dig thoughts out of my memory.

I hope you guys continue to enjoy my stories.

9/9/06 - Last night, I did something I've been meaning to for about a month - I finally read over Chain Reaction for the first time in years (my gosh, I really didn't realise it had been years since I'd updated it), to try and fix up a number of little mistakes I knew were in it. Mostly little grammatical things, but also some really odd things I never really liked. I'll be uploading the redone chapters in about a week, when I'm back from holidays.

I also intend to write some more for this story. Partly because I'm still getting reviews for it, and clearly people want to see it finished. Also because I found two future chapters tucked away in an old folder that I really want people to see. It will, however, take me awhile to write things up. So while I'm going to write more - it won't be quickly.

21/8/05 - If you have a problem with the fact that the majority of my stories are one-shots, and have a tendency to finish just when things appear to be getting good, don't complain in reviews. Email me if you have a problem with it. Otherwise, I will ignore your review. When I say one-shot, I mean one-shot. I doubt I will ever write more to any of the one-shots, because I like them as they are, and I simply can't work on long-term projects at the moment even if I did want to make them longer.
