SSSSSSSS

Hey, I just want to let everyone know that, even though I haven't updated my stories in a while, they are NOT abandoned. I simply have not had the time to update them. I do not currently have a computer at home, and the only day I have time to get to the library is on Saturdays. I spend at least two hours on Saturdays trying to catch up on things I can't get done during the week on my phone. I promise I am thinking about the stories, and I will try to update when I can. Like I said, I know it's been a long time. I have not even been able to keep up with watching the show.

I thank the people who are still following my stories!

SSSSSSSS

Reading and reviewing of my Blogs and other writing is as appreciated as reading and reviewing of my stories!

My story 'Ninety Seconds' (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/5959803/ninety_seconds/ ) came in second in 'the-vampire-act's (http://www.fanfiction.net/u/1648586/the-vampire-act ) fan fiction contest. Thank you to all those who voted for my story!

I posted my first ‘Criminal Minds’ fanfic on (03/05/2010). That was actually the first story of any kind I had put out there for anyone to read in at least three years. I have so many unfinished fan fiction stories, and so many stories and ideas I have failed to follow through to fruition. With getting this one little story out there, maybe I'll actually use some of my Creative Writing classes from ASU and my many ideas to get something going again! I'm keeping my pen name from a few years ago when I decided to try and write some 'Lost' fanfic. That worked out well...

Disclaimer: I don’t own ‘Criminal Minds’, and I don’t own any of the characters or storylines from the show. I only own the plots and characters I have created myself.
