Well when I saw you there

It was the first time

And you just walked on by

I was catching the sparks that flew form your heels

Trying to catch your eye...

Date: 1st Of November, 2006

I think I'm getting a bit old for this now...

Hello everybody, am back updating again- just when I was starting to think I'd never be writing fanfiction again!

If you didnt know already, I'm Jammie-Bro, an unbelievably enthusiastic 17 year old writer (when I want to be!) I'm going to 18 soon so am really starting to re-evaluate everything I write (coz you can't get away with writing cack when you're technically an adult...)

Am currently meant to be revising for my A levels, but am using fanfiction to help revise English- yeah, it's cop out tosh, I know. So that hefty revision schedule may be the reason for my irratic updating and general laziness in... well, everything computer- related, really. Not my fault. AlsoI apologise about my awful titles and summaries, I'm totally cack at them, but maybe the actual stories are better. Please read them, and review!!!!

I love Hockey, Squash, Reading, Writing, And drawing. As of fanfiction writing, I'm finally out of being stuck in that inescapable'writing Harry Potter mushy R/H fluff' rut. My new projects are generally of the Doctor Who vein, but every so often I get a 'OOH KH THEORY' moment and jot it down. But I'm that changeable, anything could be on the way, so be on your toes! laughs

To be honest though, I'm getting very open minded when it comes to shipping; which is a bit rich saying that i was the most staunch reader when I first came to fanfiction. It was like 'R/H or nothing at all!' But now I'll read anything within reason as long as it's well written. I also don't get the concept of how most readers love endless-chapter stories, while one-shots get about one review each, if you're lucky. I hate that- as i've decided that some of my favorite works (of my own) are 'Unwilling: Puppet on a string.' this got about 2 reviews but i still think its my best peice, as i used it for my English coursework. Well, I like it-even if no-one else did.

My most recent piece, and by far one of the best, is my general Harry Potter one-shot, 'The Recluse.' It's rather open to which characters or ship are used, but I wrote it with the usual suspects in mind; so I don't know how it comes across. Reviewers loved it though... all 0 of them!

As for 'A little out of the ordinary'... I hated almost all of it. Really. Looking back- ugh! But everyone seemed to love it... strange. Maybe I should do more chapter stories? hopefully with a better plotline? give me your views- Jammie_Bro@Hotmail.com (please title the email 'fanfiction' or something- i get sooo much junk its untrue!

And by the way, b4 i 4get, none of the books, anime, games, etc, i write about belong to me. No profit is being made by me- although i do accept bribes and donations!!!!!

Keep reading, writing, and dreaming- thank you for all your comments and thoughts. I'll leave you with a wonderful quote from one of the greatest (and longest) stories I've ever had the pleasure (and misfortune) to read.
Jammie-Bro -x x x x
