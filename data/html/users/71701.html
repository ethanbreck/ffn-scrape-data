- Reviews - - -
I'll try my best to review your story. If you ask me to read a story, I most likely will. I'm also not gonna sugar coat anything I say. (Recently I noticed that I've been kinda critical on what I read.) I'm not trying to be mean or a bitch. (Even though it may seem like it) But I try not to be rude when I review either. If you leave an E-mail address in your review, I will e-mail you.

- Flames- - -
Sure, go ahead. I'm confident (some might be bold enough to call it cocky) enough about my writing that they bounce right off. Hell, I'll even laugh. I write to piss people off and shake stuff up and change things and open up new ideas to people. Whether you flame or not, I've obviously done something to get a reaction out of you and that was my goal in the first place. Thanks. :Cocky smirk:

-I really don't care what you say about any of my stories as long as it's the truth. And don't just write 'This Sucks' Tell me why.
