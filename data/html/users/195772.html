Minibio: My name is Sinead Rivka, and I stared writing fanfiction in the Beast Wars series back when I was 11. And yes, some of the stuff on here dates close to that age. I don't take it down because I'm sentimental like that. Thank God for early beginnings because otherwise, I wouldn't have learned how to write properly! If you don't like my stories, well, that's your opinion, and you're certainly entitled to it. I don't claim to put all my best work on here, and I certainly don't claim to be the best writer. I write for fun, plain and simple, but I am happy that readers enjoy my work.

Whenever I'm not writing or doing artsy stuff, I can be found either in a cube farm or performing with my band, The Penniless Jacks.
