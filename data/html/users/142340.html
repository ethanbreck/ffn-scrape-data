I'm basically redoing everything I ever considered doing, fanfiction-wise. I have a lot of ideas, all of which I consider pretty darn good, but executing them is another matter entirely. A lot of the things I was going to write I'm not even considering writing anymore, and when I finally have something worth posting for the public eye, I will likely remove the "Omake, Omake" ripoff I had up here as well. It'll probably be a while though. I still think it's kinda strange: I basically know everything I want to do, with many holes that patch themselves up nicely on an almost daily basis. It's almost like I'm seeing the stories I want to write as a bunch of sorta live-action series(es,) skipping back and forth through different seasons of each "series" constantly. When I finally get one whipped into shape, or at least the beginning of it, I send it here. As I'm pretty much a, so far at least, fairly abysmal but unfortunately overcreative (when I'm not actually writing for some odd reason...) novice writer, It'll probably be a while, so, whatever. - 10/16/2004

Something of an update, mainly because I couldn't stand sitting around looking at this the way it was for too much longer. I'm going to be writing now, posting stuff when I get the chance. As you can see, the "Omake, Omake" ripoff has been removed, but more because it was so awful then any other reason. Most of my posts at this point (when I actually start posting again anywho,) will be short stories, written so that I can try to get into the heads of the characters of the series I'm trying to write about. Before you say 'so what?' I mean that every time I'm trying to write a big story I'll be writing a lot of awful little onesin order to understand what's going on in the heads of those characters. As an added precaution to prevent major screwups & such with copyright bull & whathaveyous, I'll be writing stories based only on series(s) that have already ended, & then only with series where none of the people with legal control oversaid series in question has ever givenany troubleto honest fanfic writers, period. However, it may take a while for me get even that far. Feh. My grasp of the English language itself (which is my primary language, the only one I know enough of to be considered to fluent in, & the one I'll primarily be writing in, so big duh there,) both with spelling & grammar, is actually pretty good, compared to people at about the same level of education as me, but as for everything else... Also, my writing skills seem best when I'm in an extreme mood, so... Yeah, can't call up an 'extreme mood' whenever I want, & it probably wouldn't exactly be healthy for me if I could. So, like I said, might be awhile. I'll be working, primarily at least, with one of two series, if not both - Buffy The Vampire Slayer & Bubblegum Crisis. There are a few other ones I could use, but those'll be the ones I'll be trying to stick to primarily, for now anyway. More when I've actually got something, so laters! - 8/20/2005
