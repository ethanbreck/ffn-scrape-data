I will definitely try to get some updates in this summer. I'm feeling quite a bit of shame about my lack of updates, having 200-odd people keeping my on author alert... oops.

If I get suspended again, check for new stories at instead of here. Yes, I know it's currently unupdated to match what's here, but next time I have a free weekend I'll try to work on it.

I'm Adrienne Wolter. I write slash, usually in the Potter fandom, though I've ventured into a few other fandoms. I'm a fifteen-year-old female aspiring writer; what I write is usually either very short original fiction stories, fan fiction one-shots, and poetry. However, I am rewriting a novel that I'm hoping to finish and rewrite (yeah, again, my writing style keeps improving) by the end of 2005. I've also been known to start very long, chaptered fan fictions, and as of yet, haven't finished any. The first was almost 200 pages long and featured a Mary Sue, and I eventually got bored; I stopped writing it in the fall of 2002, about the same time I really got into HPSS.

I've got two HPSS stories going now, and I will finish them both if it kills me. I won't die on Switched, even though sometimes it feels like a chore to continue it - I really do love the storyline, but my writing style seems to change every chapter. Side Effects is a newer story and hopefully won't take so much time to complete; I'd like to keep my chaptered stories fast-paced from now on.

Besides HPSS, I like venturing into unusual pairings or scenarios (examples of unusual scenarios can be found in the two stories mentioned above, lol), and my other favorite slash pairings include PWNL, PWHP, and RWSS. However, I am a very loyal HPSS-slasher and very rarely read any other pairings. (I don't have time to anymore, either. :( )

When I finish Switched (I will NOT be posting it before finishing, or I'll kill Switched with disinterest...) will come another wonderfully complex chaptered story, featuring the pairing RWSS. Woot.

My life isn't all about fan fiction though, and other than being an aspiring writer, I am a website designer (soon to be located at fogbutton.com, but nothing's there yet) and am learning ColdFusion and hopefully will eventually learn Flash. I play the cello in my high school's orchestra, and I also sing in the choir. I'm a part of the gifted program, and most all of my classes are honors classes. (Go me!) My best friends tend to be ones through the internet, but I am finally developing an in-person social life. Life is an adventure, and I'm determined to enjoy me, because life is short and I want to make a difference in the world, somehow.
