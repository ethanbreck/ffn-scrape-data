Author has written 9 stories for Ranma, Daria, Wild Arms, and NCIS.

Important note: Most of my stories are incomplete and likely to remain that way.

A lot of my stuff hasn't been worked on in a long time, and in some cases the uploaded version is only copy of my story I have left.

I'm a lot better at summaries than the actual stories, but I am going to give it a try.

I do most of my writing at work, so my writing speed depends on how busy work is . . . and how many of my favorite authors published that week.

I'm a big fan of anime and reading, so naturally that got me into fanfiction.

I like a good story in practically any category, but my some of my favorites are: Ranma 1/2, Daria, Harry Potter, Sailor Moon, Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha, Ouran High School Host Club, and almost anything involving Sci-Fi other than Gundam.

I love stories where the underdog comes from behind or shows surprising depths, humor stories, and a evil/bad conspiracy/plan foiled by something the bad-guy overlooked. Crossovers are also nice. I especially like when the crossover is odd, but the author makes it work anyway, and when an old crossover is handled in a new way.
