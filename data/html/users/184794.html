Author has written 29 stories for Hawaii Five-O, Kung Fu: The Legend Continues, and Law and Order: CI.

I used to write fan fiction for Kung Fu: The Legend Continues, and I will always love that show, and the complex and somewhat tragic relationship between Kwai Chang Caine and his son, Peter Caine.

But I've moved on, and am currently obsessed with Vincent D'Onofrio, all his work, Law and Order: Criminal Intent and everything about Robert Goren. He is a complex and fascinating character. I think that I'll find plenty to write about, hopefully for a long time.

Thanks for visiting, I hope you find something you like. I love criticism, I have a very thick skin so let me know what you think! I put my work out here and love to know, good or bad, exactly what you think!
