I'm a small town girl from a small town world, with my eyes set on accomplishing everything. Je parle français et/y yo hablo español. I hope to soon add Italian to that repertoire, but I make no guarantees. I've been known to use my Frapanglish (techincal term, no lie) in my stories, but it's cool. They're romantic languages, and I like to write romantic things ;)
I have a slight southern accent but talk as though I'm from Norcal, or England...sometimes a little of both. I have no doubt my stories reflect that as well.

Also, you may notice I use a few words more often than others, namely 'intense' and 'countenance'. I can't explain intense, but countenance is a part of my favorite benediction:
"May the Lord bless you & keep you; may the Lord make his face to shine upon you, and be gracious unto you. May the Lord lift up his countenance upon you, and give you peace."
