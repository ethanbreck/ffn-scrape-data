Hello everyone! =D My name is Flying Heart, well, that’s not my real name of course, but I also go as Miyu-chan on the online world. I’m a freshman in a high school in San Jose. I hope to become a writer or illustrator for children’s books when I grow up, but for now, all I write is fan fiction really. ^_^; I just came back to the US after spending 5 years in a Chinese-speaking foreign country, but I still managed to remember my English. (Isn’t that a miracle?!)

I mostly love anything exotic, like Chinese or Japanese stuff. =3 I also like to watch Anime, but that can be expensive at most times. So I mostly read Chinese-translated Manga. (They are like 1/3 of the American price, and they translate it faster.) I also love reading Children novels, though teen novels aren’t bad either. ;D I also have a passion for really bad and long series books.

I’m 100% Chinese-American. (AzN pride!!! XDD) I’m 15 years old, turning 16 in December. I’m pretty short for my age, but there are shorter. I’m pretty shy in real life, but I’m classified as out-going online, so don’t judge a person by their silence. ;D I can be pretty caring, optimistic, stubborn, and lazy at sometimes. (Isn’t that a good excuse for the un-updated fan fictions?) =_=“

I hope ya’ll enjoy my stories, and I, like any other author adore feedback, so click on the “review” button, won’t ya? ;D I love reviews~~ *puppy dog eyes*
