Joined 05-23-02, id: 218047, Profile Updated: 07-27-10
Author has written 9 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Charmed, and Glee.

Pen name origins: I got the Spitfire idea from what Xena used to do when she drank wine and then spit it on the torch she was holding and burning the face of whoever was standing in front of her. That was always really cool to me. Ummm the Maguire comes from Tobey Maguire, SPIDEY! hehehe I'm addicted to that movie.

I'm a college grad and have been out in the working world for a few years. I wrote lots of fanfiction (mainly Buffy and DragonBallZ) in high school, picked it back up in college, and then let it drop again. With the coming of Glee, I started reading fanfiction again which got me back into writing it.

I 'ship Puckleberry all the way, but I love developing unique friendships as well such as with my current story "Three's Company" (featuring a Rachel/Kurt/Mike friendship). That will be a trademark of future stories, if I get around to writing them.

Story status:

Three's Company: Just got through a killer filler chapter and am glad it's updated. Now I get on to the stuff that's been revolving in my head for awhile which, in my opinion, will make the story great.

When the Elements Are Called: I've posted chapter 9. I'm so sorry it took so long to get out. But I have absolutely committed myself to finishing this story. I've got some great ideas and a few more cliffhangers to pull out of my bag, and I will try to deliver an ending befitting what I think is a wonderful saga. Thank you all for your patience and support.

New note: Sorry for the long wait! I lost my notes on chapter 10 but have recently found them and will resume writing ASAP! Thank you so much for your patience and encouragement!

Dog Days: Transitioning to different perspectives and the things I want revealed and/or discovered and how. I will try to update this as soon as possible, though I might need to go watch another 30 hours of Animal Planet to get the right inspiration again.

Oh Baby: Okay, so I wrote the prologue for this one and never quite got around to the other chapters. Since I've gotten a lot of great reviews around this story too, I will try to develop it to its fullest potential and give you a great story of love, laughter, and of course, babies! Your continued patience is appreciated.
