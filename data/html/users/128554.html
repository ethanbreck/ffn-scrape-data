I reached that four decades of life mark in 2017... yet I can only be counted as a fanfic reader for just over twenty-two years, and an author for twenty, even if I didn't put anything here until 2001.

I've got so many fandoms I'm interested in, television, books and movies being the main genres. But I've only written ST: VOY and Buffy, so far (and if pushed I can be inspired to give ideas to my friends in other fandoms.) Though, I did add the Hawaii Five-0 2010 Revamp to my repertoire with a crack fic comedy at the end of October 2010.

I think the penguins are stealing my sanity one by one... and I know my siblings are helping them!

Finally, on a personal note... I have diverse tastes in fanfic and my Fav Authors and Stories reflect that. I strongly caution you to pay attention to the ratings on the stories. They're not just there to look pretty. If something on my list of favorites (or that I write myself) is rated M it SHOULD NOT be read by those not prepared for the content therein.
