Author has written 27 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Beyblade, Fruits Basket, Yu Yu Hakusho, Hana-Kimi, Loveless, and D.Gray-Man.

Regarding Name:

Shi(n) death + (g)Kami God

Shinigami's Voice literally means God of Death's Voice.

It combines both my liking for preternatural themes and Japanese culture.

It is also a reminder that Shinigami's are high on my list of admired beings, since Gundam Pilot 002 Duo Maxwell was the first character to strongly introduce me to the world of reading and writing fiction. For that, he has my eternal gratitude.

Regarding Personal Stories:

I am determined to do a whole sweep-edit-maybe-repost cleaning in this ff.net account. My first stories were a rather poor but valiant attempt to write in English -not my first language but very much cherished- and were also unbeta'ed.

My latest stories, and I dare draw a line and say that from the fic "Beyond Life and Death", are much more solid and more careful in the art of getting your grammar right. Or at least die trying.

I have some which are my personal favorites:

- No esperes, voy a ti (spanish version) and its English version Wait no More. Fandom: Yu Yu Hakusho. Completed

- Make a Wish. Fandom: Fruits Basket. COMPLETE!!

- Beyond Thirteen. Fandom: D.Gray-Man. Complete with a sequel on the making

I have other stories, which belong mostly to the D.Gray-man fandom, but those are hidden over my LiveJournal account. Either because I kind of forgot to post them here or because they belong to the Kink-memes and thus the rating is rather...controversial.

Regarding Other Authors and Stories:

Well, it is rather hard to come up with a list of favorite stories and authors, but I do have some names that have stuck to my mind as if fire-branded, and which I am honored to recognize as very influential writers for me:

- Dyna Dee. Fandom: Gundam Wing my first multichaptered author and first writer to introduce me to Duo Maxwell and his true background story

- Sunhawk. Fandom: Gundam Wing the best psychological twists and turns that I have ever read come from this lady, as well as the longest and most intricated arch-lenght fiction I have had the pleasure to find

- Mikkeneko. Fandom: Gundam Wing abundance of heartwrenching endings as well as exciting cliffhangers, this writer has a mastery to push the right buttoms to make the readers cry, at least in her angst stories

- Fabula Rasa. Fandom: Harry Potter an incredible skill to write sensual and dark stories, centered around opposite but ensnaring characters such as Severus Snape and Sirius Black, a master in writing genious dialogue

- Vara Shamus. Fandom: Harry Potter beautiful drama and angst insights with amazing references to old and rare legends as well as a fixation for rather dark characters, especially Severus Snape

- R Junkie. Fandom: Fruits Basket my introduction to FB fiction and to the darker side of the story, with focus on Souma Yuki

- Nerva al'Thor. Fandom: D.Gray-Man. She is my inspiration for AU versions of my favorite DGM character Kanda Yuu, and thanks to her fanfics I have regained the will to write. I am really very grateful to her and this fandom very much.

Regarding miscellaneous matters:

Finished my carrer in September 2008.

I am already formally working with International Cooperation Agency of Japan.

Been to Japan 3 times as of June of 2009.

My work and life rythm would scare anyone alive. Because I suppose for the undeads and other preternatural creatures, the extreme lack of sleep is rather common.

I have a LiveJournal account filled with mundane things like my love for J-rock music and artists like Hyde and L'arc-en-ciel and generally for other ramblings and fics, with the username of may_unleashed, over here:

And that is about all, I would think.

Shinigami's Voice signing off.
