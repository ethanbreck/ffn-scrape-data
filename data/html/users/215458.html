Constructive critisism. Very good. It helps me become a better writer (wait, wait, can you consider my crappy works writing?), so review as much as possible, good or bad. I would love for you to review, even if it is to tell me how much you hate my story. Hey! That's ok. If you like it, that's awesome. I'm totally psyched that you do! If you don't, I respect that. One thing I ask: if you are going to criticize me, do it in a mature, orederly fashion. Don't just cuss me out and tell me how much you hate me, even if that's what's on your mind. I would much rather you tell me some things that I could improve on, because, let's face it:
no one is perfect.
No one will be perfect.
No one will be close to perfect.
All we little human beings down here on earth can do is strive to be better in everything we do, and that is my goal, to become a better writer, not to be told i suck and to go to hell. No, I don't think that will help me too much (maybe I'm wrong? Does someone draw inspiration from that kind of stuff...?).
