Author has written 10 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Tekken, and Guild Wars.

I write for myself, if anyone else likes it it's just icing on the cake.

I used to write really bad fan fiction for Final Fantasy VIII years ago, I've deleted most of it because it was mostly unfinished projects. I left the one that was completed but it's so bad it may induce eye bleeding, so be warned. I'm currently working on finishing a Tekken project that I started around the time Tekken 5 came out, it was yet another unfinished project so I decided to delete from the site, finish it, edit it and repost it. It turns out that I'm rewriting the whole thing and breaking it up into two separate fics, I'm posting new chapters as I finish them, the story is mostly done, it just needs a lot of fleshing out. I'm having a lot of fun writing it and am pretty happy with it so far.

I want to thank everyone who has taken the time to read my stories. I used to have a passion for writing (though I was admittedly very bad at it) and suddenly stopped, I didn't write anything for years, not a paragraph not a journal entry, nothing. Then suddenly a few months ago I was struck by my muse and took it up again. I've found that it's a very important part of my life, I'm so, so happy that I have this platform to share it and I sincerely want to thank you for reviewing, putting me on your follow and favorite lists or simply for just reading. It means a lot to me.

I'm always looking to improve, so if you see some way that I can do that please let me know in a PM or a review, whatever.

Current Projects:

"The Hardest Truth"- It's taking much longer to complete than I thought because I found most of what I had inadequate and am basically using the old version of the story as an extended outline.

"Holding out for a Hero(ine)"- My first attempt at an AU and a story that is light-hearted and dare I say, often humorous? Please give it a try, if you're tired of the boys taking all the glory this might be for you. I'm working on it, I swear.

Red- Anna Williams origin story! (Not a happy story.) Why are she and Nina always trying to kill each other? Namco doesn't give me answers, so I make up my own.

Just a quick PSA, I recently became aware that part of my story, "The Hardest Truth" was stolen by someone and passed off as their own. Fellow writers, don't forget to Google your stories every once in a while to make sure no one has taken credit for them or posted them on other sites without your consent. Fellow readers, if you come across a story that you know has been plagiarized, please, if possible, let the original author know.

I post my stories on this site as well as AO3 under the username Liquid_Sky. I also have two of my one-shots (The Will to Live and As Old as Time) posted on Inkitt under the username LiquidSky.
