Works in Progress (including more in-depth summaries):

SSBM: The Endowment of Exigency: BASED ON THE SUPER SMASH BROTHERS MELEE! (not brawl!!) Three years after the defeat of the Master Hand and the Crazy Hand, the twenty six smashers take turns in groups guarding the Final Destination to make sure that the powerful Hands do not come back. It was a completely normal day for Fox, Luigi, Samus, Captain Falcon, and Jigglypuff, when suddenly after an hour they are forced to embark on a quest to hunt down the Ring of Darkness, a group of villains that have obtained the Endowment of Exigency. Apparently, this particular power gives more power than even the Master Hand and Crazy Hand combined! Even worse, these familiar villains have teamed up under one master to rule over the worlds and destroy their rivals. It's up to these five smashers to defeat them and save the universe! Read as they travel across the various planets, meet both new and familiar Nintendo characters from new and familiar planets, unfurl the true secrets behind the creation and use of the Endowment of Exigency, and ultimately grow more powerful as they fight on in this epic adventure! Of course, their adventure isn't just "Kill this guy and go to the next guy." No, problems within the team of smashers itself arise, forcing them to fix them while reluctantly fighting on. I do not own any rights to any Nintendo-owned characters/games/etc. Some multicrossovers with other Nintendo games, including Golden Sun, Pikmin, and Kid Icarus. DEFINITE SPOILERS FOR MANY NINTENDO GAMES!! READ AT YOUR OWN RISK!!

Tales of Smashing! A Parody!: Simply put, the smashers are acting out a parody for the legendary game: Tales of Symphonia! Many characters from several Nintendo series will make appearances, every smasher will have a part (varying in importance, though) and feel the EMOTION! THE DRAMA! And most of all... THE HUMOR!! Obvious spoilers of Tales of Symphonia, and possibly for various Nintendo games because of character appearances. If you love parodies and love Tales of Symphonia, this is a must-read! I do not own any rights to any Nintendo-owned characters/games/etc nor anything from Tales of Symphonia. On Hiatus until Endowment of Exigency is done

SSBB: The Three-Way Survivor Game: Multi-crossover. You might have thought there were too many survivor games out there... Here's another one! The 48 smashers split into three teams of 16 with adjusted rules to fight in a game of survivor! 48 Participants are playing, but only one will win the grand prize, which is worth over a million dollars in American currency! Who will win? Who will get owned? If you enjoy humor or survivor games, this story is definitely for you! Possible spoilers for various nintendo games!! Read at your own risk!

Future Works:

No Title Yet: A prequel to the Endowment of Exigency. The various worlds of the Nintendo Universe are slowly deteriorating, and what seems to be simple "Rescue Peach" missions turn into an epic adventure of twenty six smashers, who hardly met each other, to save the universe from the two evil hands.

Keep in mind that none of these fics are related to each other (unless otherwise stated), as in each fic will be typed as if the others didn't exist. Get what I'm saying? So the events in Golden Sun: Hidden Secrets never happened and never will happen in SSBM: The Endowment of Exigency, and vice versa.
