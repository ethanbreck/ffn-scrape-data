Since I haven't updated this profile in a while, so here goes. I'm a huge Sailor Moon fan, and I love all types of fanfiction. Ironically I don't write a lot of fanfiction. Haha. I have a lot more original fiction on fictionpress. More on my Sailor Moon fandom: It's one of the first influential anime/manga's in my life. Don't expect me to update a lot though because I write in pieces over weeks and maybe even months. I also go back frequently and edit past chapters. So please be patient with me, I'm a working girl and I try hard.
