Let's see...I have no idea how to explain myself. I've been writing most of my life, but in recent years I've suffered a major writing block. At last, after four years I think I've broken it. I THINK .

I love fan fiction as a whole. I love the fact that you can take characters and set them in situations and...positions...that they normally wouldn't be in. That's the beauty of it. It's not like the norm and that's why it's fan fic. If it were right on spot with the whole thing it would be the whole thing. Now getting plainly out of character is something all together. Having chars get involved in a situation that's not probable is different from making the meek mouse into a pervert. See what I'm saying?

I'm a big fan of the following:

Naruto

Resident Evil

Hellsing

Legacy of Kain

There are more I'm sure I just can't think of any right now. :)
