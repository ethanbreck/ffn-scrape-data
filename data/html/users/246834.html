Another profile update. Because, heh, it's time.

I'm a hip little chickie who likes to write fanfiction every once in a while. I'll write about anything I like as long as I can make a decent fanfiction from it. I've written mainly Utena and Harry Potter fics, and I've been immersed in the RENT world. I've deleted a few stories because they were old and I didn't like them anymore. However, since it was so popular back in the day, I've kept StarCrossed Lovers up. (Honestly, compared to what I write now, I really don't like it. But since people seem to fancy it, I'll keep it up.) I might write the sequel one day; until that day, it's on hiatus.

I've written one Van Helsing fic (no, it's not based off the movie). Er, yeah. I liked it, but people don't seem to. Since I'm stubborn, it's staying up.

I write mostly RENT now, because that's the fandom I'm currently obsessing about. Actually, I didn't want to write any RENT fanfiction at all before...but I've started, and I can't stop. Really. There are so many, it's insane.

Fanfiction.net tore off my three Utena fan fictions (which I'm still quite heated about) because they were song-fics, but I'll put them up again one of these days. I really did like those fics; I was so angry when they got torn off. Gah. Anyway.

I love reviews, so if you do read my stories, please try and review. Of course, constructive critism is liked, and feel free to e-mail me if...I don't know, you have a question about one of the stories or something crazy like that. (I just noticed this new reply thing they have going now, so maybe that'll happen in the future.)

And I'm a total slasher. It ranges from total and utter fluff to hot and heavy smex. Bwaha.
