UPDATE THAT AFFECTS ALL CATEGORY FICTION THAT IS UNFINISHED: Since I can't just email people I am leaving this in my profile. Several things in my life have changed, but for the better. It has raised everything from my sense of purpose to my daughter's confidence. It has done wonders for my family, except it has one drawback. It is very hard to write as much as I used to, or to find a specific time to get this done. This has affected me a lot, personally, since I often write on many stories.

If your fic is affected, please wait or find me. (You have my user name and names of stories, you can find me out there on the net.)

My place: All unfinished fics are going to my place except three exceptions. (See exceptions below here.)

All the unfinished fiction (except the exceptions) will be removed from this site.

They will only be in progress from my place but not here or other places.

They will come back when I have everything written out and done on my own computer. They will be uploaded pretty quick, probably a few chapters per day, or if I have a free day, all of it at once. While this does make it less likely it will gather the same audience numbers, I will just be happy that I can continue to write and share.:)

EXCEPTIONS:

Life Finds A Way: This fic is almost done, it has a few chapters to go. I will be focusing on it almost completely throughout the Thanksgiving break so it will be finished by Thanksgiving or a little after.

Dead Shadow: This fic isn't almost done, but I will be concentrating on it after Life Finds A Way. It makes no sense to pull it down. It will be done sometime after Christmas (maybe before) but definitely before Valentine's Day.

Revenge of The Lost: This is a big story, and not something I want to put on a burner to finish in a set amount of time. It's got five main characters and plenty of subplot so it will be a huge story. It's best if I leave this up and instead try to add a chapter at least once a month instead.

This does not affect any fic that I finished. This only pertains to fics in progress. Chameleon Protection, and Inner Sense Tattoos won't be affected by this change.

All Unfinished fics will eventually come back, but because I will also be working to the end, they might be extra edited, new parts added, old parts cut, I don't know. I just know they will be complete and meet with my approval of being shared.
