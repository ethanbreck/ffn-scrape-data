Be: Honest, especially with yourself

And so here is a piece of honesty about me - I am terrible at getting out there to read other stories! I have to apologize for this because it isn't that I don't want to, it's just that I simply put my own writing in the first room and as I have serious issues with time lacking I simply put off the reading of other author's brilliant work until I feel I can commit to that task.

Bottom line, my darling reader - should you choose to be one - is that I am officially a writer, and not a reader. Love my work, hate my work, but please don't read my work simply to get read yourself. With this, I mean please do not hastily read one chapter and then wait for me to come and do the same thing for you! I want you to read my work because you want to read it! Goodness gracious, I hope I'm not sounding obnoxious here!

I have the best readers in the world, and I respect the fact that a lot of them also are writers -but I know my readers do not read my work on basis of me reading theirs, because sadly if they did I'm afraid I wouldn't have any readers left.

Anyways, I don't wish to sound selfish - it's just the plain truth.

Thanks a lot to all the people who always have supported me throughout! You guys are unbeatable, unbreakable and I love you!

AllMyLove,

Annie.
