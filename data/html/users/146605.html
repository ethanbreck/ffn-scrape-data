July 13, 2006:

From Within:

Chapter Five: I actually finished writing this chapter back in June but the death of my great- grandfather on June 10th overtook my concerns at that time. But finally the chapter has been submitted and up for viewing. I think one more chapter, maybe two are left before From Within is finally complete.

I am a slow writer. I write stories the old fashion way. I write the first draft on paper and then rewrite a chapter or story again for improvements and the deletion of any mistakes or unneccesary passages (on paper). It is after that step that I then type the new story or chapter on computer where I can better check for mistakes in grammar or spelling. After that, I submit the new chapter or story on fanfiction.net

The reason I take forever in updating is because I want to write a good chapter or story that is worthy of being read on this site. The writing of a good chapter or story takes time for me because I like things to be well thought out and written in good detail so that the characters come to life in a good way. I hope that potential readers understand what I am trying to say so that they can better enjoy reading what I submit.

Special note: I give thanks to everyone of my readers who take the time to review, it means a lot to me.
