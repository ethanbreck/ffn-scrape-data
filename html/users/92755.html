Hello,

I LOVE to write stories (original ones which I don't publish and so called fanfictions which I think I can risk to share with other people :) ). I just love to invent new plots, to explore (and exploid, hehehe) the characters, to add what is missing to an anime and to answer the questions that were still open for me after the last episode (or the movie, for that matter).

My most favourite topics are friendship, hope and love (that sounds quite romantic, huh? Believe me, that's not always the case!). And for me it doesn't matter if the people in love are male&female, female&female (yuri) or male&male (shonen-ai). Sometimes it's clearly hinted in the animes, sometimes I see it in the characters and sometimes it simply slips into the stories (hey, I'm only the writer!). You can see that as a warning, although I don't quite see why I should warn someone, because I write about love. As long as it's true love, it doesn't matter who loves each other. At least that's my opinion. Disagree? Don't read.

Disclaimers for all stories: I don't own the characters nor the animes. However, I do own the plots and some of the minor characters in there. If you want to post the stories on your own website, feel free to do so, but please inform me and give me the adress of your site. Thanks!

I'm a native speaker of German, therefore you can find German stories on this site, as well as on my website, next to my English ones.

My German stories are from different fandoms:

- Detective Conan

- DoReMi

- Hikaru no Go

- Matantei Loki Ragnarok

- Neon Genesis Evangelion

- Saber Rider and the Star Sheriffs

- Shaman King

- Zelda (Nintendo)

However, my passion are my English stories in the fandom Sailor Moon, especially about the pairing Tenô Haruka & Kaioh Michiru. I've started to write my stories in 2000 and I still write them (whenever I find the time :) ). I've really tried to reread and correct my old stories, but I simply don't have the time to do so. I know that there are still tons of errors in there (typing, spelling, grammar). Please bear with me, because I'm not a native speaker of English, but I love the English language and it's much more fun to write my Sailor Moon fanfictions in English.

Therefore, I'll just upload the old stories, even though there are still mistakes in there. It would be great if you could point them out to me (or even become my beta reader).

I did already correct some of my old stories:

- Wanted Child

- Break the Silence

The rest is the way I wrote them between 2000 and 2004.

But rejoyce, I did have some time while I studied in England this spring. I used it well and wrote a brand new, almost 300 pages story. It's called "Tenshi" and it's another Haruka&Michiru story. It's already completed and uploaded here. Let me know what you think about it :).

I've finally managed to reread and upload the complete story of "Love is a drug". And I've found a small oneshot on my computer called "Ticking". I wrote it in 2001 and it's my darkest fanfic.
