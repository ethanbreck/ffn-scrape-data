A Bit About Me!:

Name: I'm not going to tell you. On ff, call me Fleur.

Age: 15 (my last profile update said i was 13, wtf! :))

Location: California, United States. However, I plan to move to Britain the second I'm an adult. I'm completely obsessed w/ England!!!

Biggest Obsession: Harry Potter, of course...and Sean Biggerstaff :)

Favorite HP Book: Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban; it's by far the best.

Favorite HP Character: I'd say Oliver Wood, and NOT because I am in love with the actor who plays him in the film but because I have *always* loved him. He's slightly odd, yet very passionate, and overall just really cool. Besides him, I love Fleur Delacour. She totally kicks butt!!!

Least Favorite HP Character: Viktor Krum *really* gets on my nerves (Hermione deserves better!). And I'm really not all that fond of Hagrid.

Hogwarts House: Three different online quizzes proclaimed me to be in Slytherin, and I accept this. "Slytherin" is NOT just a nice way of saying "Death Eater," you know, but on the other hand, the Malfoys really suck.

Rating of the Film: I must say I have a couple quirks with it, but it was well-written, and *EXTREMELY* well-casted.

My biggest story is caled "Heart of Lies," formerly titled "Lies." I don't think it's all that good, but lots of people seem to like it, and that's why I continue. It's a naughty, somewhat explicit Lily/Sirius fic, in which Harry's real father turns out to be Sirius Black. Actually, I've written it so that it could virtually be a prequel to the HP series (not that it WOULD EVER, that's just how it's written!). This affects nothing in the actual series, unless, of course, you count Harry ever finding out.

Besides this, I have lots of weird, quirky humor, of which include "What If HP Was American?" and the infamous "Harry Potter and Friends Visit Fanfiction."

Ok...I have decided to take on a far more mature view to my whole writing-fanfiction-thing. First off: I'm cutting out the stupid humor and will begin to write nice, pleasant, quality fics. I've decided I'd rather enjoy long-term projects as opposed to short, one-page, write-in-five-minutes 'ficcies' that mean nothing. This means more POVs, more poems, more epic dramas.

And I'll be fulfilling my long-delayed dream of writing romance. My 13 years of age have not yet found love, but I do love to fantasize and carry it out through writing.

I've adopted a new ship, as well: O/Hr, or Oliver/Hermione (tell me, should I put W/Hr, for Wood/Hermione? But then it seems more correct to put Wood/Granger for use of surnames. What do you think...?) I'll continue with my L/S thing, and try to *actually* do some of my favorite, R/Hr, if time permits.

See ya all! And review, please!
