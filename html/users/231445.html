Hi there,

My name is Lindsay, I'm a 30 year old Canadian who usually prefers the written word to social interaction. This is what makes my chosen path in life all the more interesting - I am a Library Tech with a certificate in American Sign Language. I fell in love with reading and writing at the age of 7 and have never looked back.

If and when all else fails, I will always have my books and my writings and thats the greatest comfort I will ever have.

Reading is one of the greatest joys in my life. It's my escape when I want it and it grounds me when I need it.

I'm bad with punctuation and have trouble with tenses so if you see something out of place, I'm sorry.
