Hello,

I am 32 years old, Birthday is Dec. 5/85 and I am a photographer and videotographer who loves writing on the side.

Sorry I haven't updated my profile here in a while...

I am a huge fan of Supergirl, The 100, Criminal Minds, Stargate SG1, Stargate Atlantis, Harry Potter, 3 Ninjas, Vampire Diaries, Castle, Modern Family, Charmed, Alcatraz, etc. I am not much of a romantic, so when I write them... please give me your thoughts on what I can do or how to improve. As a writer, I accept critizum, I want to become a better writer after all and the only way to do that is to listen to my reader's ideas, suggestions and what not.

I have completed Journalism in College learned a lot about writing from it, however if you compare the journalism writing style to that of fan fics, they are far from being the same.

Most of my stories will be in the Suspence/Drama as I love to write how the character feels when going through something and then those close to that character, how their reaction is. I love to write fight scene's, sure, they're not easy but usually the end result is rewarding in itself.

I read a lot of fanfics here on this site, and if I like a story I'll try and give the best review I can. To talk about the chapter, what happened, what I think will happen next and ask questions. I give advise where it is needed, one thing to always keep in mind is that everyone has their own writing style. That is key. One thing I have gotten into with my fanfics is when I am doing something particular, I'll do research, I'll look things up and put it into the story because it is nice to know actual facts if there are any, if not, do my best to give a background and explaining.

I am a huge JJ fan for Criminal Minds and a huge fan of Samantha Carter on Stargate SG1 and Atlantis. Don't ask me why, but for some strange reason, I like to put my favourite characters through pain and then to have them recover.

My central website is , My photography facebook www.facebook.com/nikkisphotographyandvideos I love to chat to other fans of those listed above, I also enjoy Resident Evil, Fatal Frame, Hitman: Blood Money, Gundam Wing, and many others. So if your a fan of any of those and want to chat, send me a PM or an email, I check my email regularily.

I know... I have to write Secret Santa 2, I have been meaning to start up on it but can't get at it yet. Working on an SG1 story that I hope will become a big story.

Sorry again that it has taken me so long to update my profile, I get caught up in writing stories and things going on in my life, like I am taking a night school grade 12 College English two nights a week and I'm going to be going to Durham College for Journalism Print this fall to upgrade my skills ever more in the writing field.

Have any questions? Give me a shout.
