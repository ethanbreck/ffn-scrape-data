Author has written 5 stories for Labyrinth.

Genesis is sort of on hiatus. It's been a while since I've written anything since my brain is still recovering from various issues. I really want to find out how this story goes, but it may take a back burner in favor of the novel I intend to publish. I'm not going to put this story on ACTUAL hiatus, however. I know at some point I'll get the sudden urge to work on it (I've been feeling an itch to write over the past couple of weeks). So hopefully I'll be able to update before I got back to school.

I hold myself accountable for the few other stories posted on here. Two were posted when I was only sixteen, and the other perhaps a year and a half to two years later. I like to think I've improved since then. Please judge my writing for what it is now and will continue to become instead of what it used to be. :)
