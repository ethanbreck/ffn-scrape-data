Author has written 2 stories for Poltergeist: the Legacy, Warcraft, and Power Rangers.

Nothing much to say here other than I am more of a reader than a writer. My first story on this site was a co-written silly little thing that was more my friend's writing than mine.

My current story, 'A Taste of Iron', is my first real attempt at writing anything on my own. I don't know how to write, it's going to be obvious, but I'm trying to learn as I go. The story is being posted on another site first and I will be updating it here after I find all the errors I miss the first time around. I also have to figure out how to edit out the explicit bits without losing any story content before posting here.

I currently have 108 pages of rough, rough draft written in OpenOffice to work from. The plot is 90% complete so I do know where it's going.

Current interests: Mad Max: Fury Road, Vikings, the Warcraft movie, and World of Warcraft.

Current looping soundtracks while I write: Rogue One, Mad Max: Fury Road, World of Warcraft Legion soundtrack.
