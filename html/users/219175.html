Author has written 47 stories for Legend of Zelda, Avatar: Last Airbender, Legend of Korra, and A song of Ice and Fire.

I haven't updated this thing in forever. I can't believe I've been on this site for so long. It's unbelievable. Anyways, if you're reading this I'm just going to give you some basic info. I'm a female who lives in the United States, and I'm over eighteen years old. I write mostly Legend of Zelda and Avatar: the Last Airbender fanfic. Most of my stuff will be romance, though I do try to stay away from outright fluff, with occasional slices of humor and horror as I see fit. I'm going to go ahead and tell you that I do not tend to write canon pairings, and some of my stuff will be very AU.

Because I've been flamed in the past for the pairings that I write about I'm just going to go ahead and post the ones that I support and write about

Legend of Zelda pairings:

Ganondorf and Zelda-If you don't like it, don't read it. It's as simple as that. I know it's not a popular pairing, but it is probably my favorite. I guess you could consider it my OTP for this game.

Malon and Link-This is about as close to canon as I'll ever get. I love the thought of the simple farm girl falling in love with the fairy boy from the forest. Pretty much anytime I hear an old Irish love song I think of this pairing.

Malon and Sheik- Another very weird one, and yes, I mean the male Sheik. I support both male and female Sheik, so I'm not hatin' on either one. I don't know why, but I love the bittersweet feeling of this pairing, and if you could point me in the direction of any fanfic for it I would be grateful to you ;).

Midna and Link-They're a sexy pairing, enough said.

There are various others I support like DarkLinkxMalon and DarkLinkxZelda, but those are the main ones.

Avatar the Last Airbender (oh God the shipping wars):

Zutara (Zuko and Katara)-What can I say? I just think that they fit together so well it's ridiculous, and they just have such great chemistry together that I couldn't resist.

Taang (Toph and Aang)-An adorable and vastly underappreciated pairing that very quickly endeared itself to me.

Jetzula (Jet and Azula)-I read an interesting fanfiction one time that put them together, and I thought it was just brilliant. They just seem to complement each other's crazy so well. If those two had gotten together, they would have ruled the world.

Jai (Jet and Mai)-Another crackship that I find really intriguing. I know they've never met, but I can't help but feel like they would have had their own Zutara-like dynamic because they were so opposite. I guess I just like the idea of them together so much because I feel like Jet would have really brought out Mai's character and personality more than anyone else.

Urzai(Ursa and Ozai)-I ship this one strictly for the mystery it provides. We never truly found out the condition of their relationship. Were they ever in love or was it arranged? Does Zuko's father still care for her? I like to think that they were and that he does, but then again, I'm a reluctant romantic.

Other pairings I mildly ship in Avatar: ZukoxJin, ZukoxYue, JetxKatara, TophxSokka, and I'm sure there are others in there that I'm forgetting about.

There you have it! All the pairings that I support for my fandoms. If you got to the end this I'm honored that you cared enough to stick around this long.

11/29/2013
