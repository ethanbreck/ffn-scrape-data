I'm a poorly-employed loser in his mid-thirties with dreams of voice acting, reviewing, or making it as an artist or writer, or webcomicing. I sound like every sitcom's sarcastic next-door neighbor.

I also happen to be of an age where I'm not supposed to be writing fanfiction for cartoons. I don't care. But, still I'm so, so damn old. Like, you don't even know, man. It would blow your mind.

If I inadvertently rip your story off, I'm sorry. I've actually been working on most of my stories for years now. One of the problems I've had is that I spend so long writing that I find other authors making use of ideas I wish to use. It's pretty frustrating, but it's my own fault.

My likes are varied and many, my dislikes are as well, and my taste in fiction tends toward the melodramatic and funny.

I'm a sap for a good love story, or a good "mutual dislike turns to passion" story, whatever. As for the stories I write (should I ever actually finish one), let's just say I love love stories, and I believe in happy endings...but I don't believe in making things easy for anyone.

As of February 2018, I've been given a diagnosis of stage 4 cancer and may be dead by the time anyone reads this.
