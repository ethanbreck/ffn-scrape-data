author's drabble:

hello all! after a long and semi-painful couple of years, i'm back to writing again. though i don't exactly attract all the reviews per say, my musues haven't given up on me yet. i have more ideas...and am in many cases yet to finish the ideas i've already started. my computer is repaired for the most part, my health is better, and i feel like i've finally found a style that works to covey how i feel now. needless to say, i am very happy to be writing again!

please help feed my muses and review! i would appriciate it so much to know that people are enjoying the hard work i put into my stories.

DISCONTUNED: the used is discontinued...this is in part because my entire harddrive was deleted but also in part because of a lack of reviews. when i recover it, (as well as the final chapters of ichidou) i will post the rest.

updates:

NEW:

"prelude to the lost" - RK fanfic, still in progress. set in 1920's new york city, kaoru and kenshin meet one another at a prestigeous japanese-american college. well...it's full of waff, but kaoru doesn't realize that kenshin is an assassin and kenshin doesn't realize that kaoru is bent on revenge for the murder of her parents only a few years prior.

"the burden" - SM fanfic, just started this past week. definately au, with a tinge of angst. as a boy, mamoru is savagely bitten by a vampire and left for dead. somehow he survives that snowy night and lives on, unaware of what he has become. fifteen years pass, and our favorite pair of lovers are fighting it out again on the streets of tokyo. out comes the rebirth of the ginzuishou...and not long after the birth of something worse as the monster within mamoru emerges. will mamoru control the beast within as he tries to protect usagi from both himself and the enemy?

...and several others in the near future.

ja ne all,

-cloudiko
