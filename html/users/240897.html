Hello to all of you slightly insane, generally dysfunctional people out there. Cheers to us. We have more fun anyway.

Who am I you ask? I've been trying to figure that out for sometime now. It hasn't been working though. I blame it all on my conflicting personalities.

As for this site... I found it one day and never left. I'm addicted to the Harry Potter universe and have decided to start posting some of my poetry here as well.

Oh yes, back to me. If you really want to know... I've warned you. I'm either going to make you laugh, make you think I'm insane (which I don't deny), or scare you off. But I don't bite. Usually. Here are some basic facts as found on my homepage:

I love emo, indie, punk, rock, metal music.

I read way too much.

I don't believe in God. Yes! I am that naughty A word! Atheist. Shhh... Don't tell your mommies. (If you're a religious person and this offends you... I really don't care. Debate with me if you'd like about religious issues. I'll win.)

I'm a complete introvert. This means I don't like YOU. Then again, my being an introvert can't be blamed for my anti-social tendancies, right?

I like cats. I have three.

My family is insane. Don't laugh. I'm serious.

I like serial killers.

Then again, I'm sorta out there too.

I'm too empathetic and caring.

I love to color my nails with black permanent marker.

For now I'd like to pursue criminal psychology as a career.

I don't watch T.V. Only movies.

The one guy in The Shining, One Flew Over The Cuckoo's Nest, etc. scares me. Jack something.

Duckies go moo and cows go quack.

There is me! More or less at least. I'm going to copy down some (ok, a lot) poems, lyrics, and quotes that I like too. I have no life. It's a sad, sad thing.
