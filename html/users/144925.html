Author has written 14 stories for Stargate: Atlantis.

I've never written a profile before but I guess after 9 stories it's time to do so. I'm a Brit but I've lived in Germany for years. I've always loved science fiction even at a very young age. My introduction to fan fiction came as early as the original Star Trek. I remember buying a book that had published some fan stories and they were great. I really got into it myself with Star Wars when I wrote a few Han Solo stories that were published in Fanzines. Nothing else inspired me to write until Stargate Atlantis came along and with it the very whumpable John Sheppard.
