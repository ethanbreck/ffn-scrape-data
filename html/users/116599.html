Most of my stories are R or PG 13 - There is no Slash - and the stories are interconnected, characters from one show may crossover into another's. Fan Fiction writing is a hobby for me.If the dates in the stories or the location is wrong, please let me know, nicely please. The characters in my stories have grown, and I will give a reason in my stories if something doesn't match with your approval , but I will not change a bad character into a good one even though the actor who plays that character is very handsome,etc. Enough said? I give critiques, and suggestions, so if you want me to pat you on the back and say, "oh you're so wonderful" forget it. Of course, if you write the story well, and you show the characters well as well as the settings, I will let you know. However, I cannot review all stories, even those I like. By the way, if you are begging for reviews and there are over 25 reviewers, you are just fishing for approval. . I cannot understand why the character does something, I'll ask. If the character starts back with a bad habit, I would like to know why even though I cannot understand how a character went back to a bad habit after four years of not indulging in it. If the writer has an excellent imagination, and a way of putting things, that's a plus in my estimation.My writing home page is at http:suspensewriter.nutzworld.netNo critiquing of my web site is allowed. If you are really serious about being a writer, you can join the allcrits yahoo group. It's private.
