:CHALLENGE REQUEST:

For those interested in the VHD/Hellsing crossover universe: "Dinner Etiquette" came from a meme challenge ("Give me a character or a pairing and a promt and I'll write a few sentences about it." And so the request goes out to all of you. Any character in Hellsing or VHD goes, any promt goes. Note, that the pairings in this universe include: AlucardxIntegra (duh), SerasxPip, DxDoris, DxSeras. Although I will read other pairings, they don't fit in this universe, so I won't write them. Unmentioned pairings will be played with. No yaoi please. Will attempt yuri if someone really wants me to. Any promt goes. Send requests to the_prophet_talia@yahoo.com

I started this account while I was in high school (now about 6 years ago, holy crap...) I am now 25 and working in the banking industry (hur hur). I have shifted to doing more art than writing lately, some of which can be found on my deviant art account, which is saved as my homepage. Currently I am working with Vampire Hunter D and Hellsing, these works can also be found on my mediaminer and adultfanfiction.net accounts (both are either The Prophet Talia or Talia of Eragon, I can't think of which right now). I am intrigued by the idea that D could be the son of Alucard and Integral Hellsing, so I'm exploring the concepts there. As of now, stories written or planned in this universe include:

Ties that Bind: posted 12,000+ AD: D reworks Alucard's wards in order to keep him imprizoned.

Breaking Point: posted 1999: Integra reaches a breaking point with both herself and her monster after the Millennium battle. (Contains graphic sexual content, can be found at mediaminer.org and adultfanfiction.net)

Dhampir: posted 1999/2000: Integra discovers she is pregnant by Alucard--a study of the emotional reaction to an unwanted and seemingly impossible pregnancy.

Doctor Trevilian title needed: 1999: Dr. Trevilian's POV, Integra requests prenatal care (and threatens our poor doctor a little)

An Intimate Moment: posted 1999: A short scene between a protective Alucard and a pregnant Integra.

The Glorified Babysitter title needed: 2000: There are issues trying to deal with a newborn dhampir, especially when trying to keep his breeding under wraps.

Sol Solis Morbus: 2005: Two words: sunlight syndrome.

The Origin of the Symbiot title needed: 2012: A small faction of Millennium scientists escaped destruction in 1999 and have continued research into the field of parasidic/symbiotic mind control. They are getting close to their goal: their symbiots function properly with one small problem: they kill their human hosts. Likewise, when placed in a midian, the midian destroys the symbiot. Rumors surface that a child in England is a cross between a midian and a human, and so they work a plan in order to capture the child for experimentation. Too bad the poor bastards don't realize whose child it is... (I haven't really started this yet)

The Seals: 2014: Integral tells Damien about her views of Alucard's sanity and more importantly how strong the wards really are and warns him that if the time comes Damien will either have to control his father or destroy him. sniff

Interlude--After the Talk: 2014: Alucard knows that Integra finally talked to Damien about the seals and confronts her. A brief battle of wills that ends in a draw.

The Fall of Hellsing tentative title: 2015: The bombing of London, the destruction of Hellsing, the death of Integral and the beginning of Alucard's maddness.

The Rise of the Vampire Hunter tentative title: 2015: Alucard has gone on a rampage after Integral's death. Damien and Seras take the necessary steps to reign him in...or worse. (Ends with Damien beginning to go by 'D'.)

A Momentary Lapse: posted: 12,000+ AD: After the battle with Count Lee, D is weakend and must recoup with vampiric sleep. Doris Lang comes a little too close during his recouperation. contains graphic sexual content, can be found on mediaminer.org and adultfanfiction.net

Reminiscence: 12,000+ AD: D's reflections of Doris Lang.

The Gilded Cage: 12,000+ AD: Alucard's reflections on Integral and his self-made prison.

Into the Lion's Den: 12,000+AD: Four frontier bandits take shelter in an abandoned castle only to awaken a sleeping noble. Things don't turn out so well for them... contains graphic violence and sexual content... may or may not be posted here...

An Unexpected Encounter: 12,000+ AD: D and Seras unexpectedly run into each other in a frontier town. Contains graphic sexual content, probably won't be posted here

That's about it for now.
