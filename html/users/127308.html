I think it's pretty obvious that I love CLAMP. Most of what I write has something to do with them after all. :-) Including the stuff that I don't have posted here. I've decided though, that if I don't have something close to finished, or finished already, I probably won't post. (This in regards and apology to the fans of some of my posted and unfinished works.) Thanks to all those that read my work, and kudos to you if you like it and review. I'm sorry if it isn't liked as well. I hope that I will do better in the future.

At the moment I'm on a Transformers kick. Really, it was the last thing I ever saw myself getting into. But in truth, I really do love it. Maybe because it's so different in so many ways. Either way! I decided to doodle a picture for my running series Light of the Sun. I drew it for a scene at the end of chapter nine. Mind you I'm not that great of an artist. XD But here it is:

Unlikely Support

This section is for the two series that I never finished and my reasoning as to why they are incomplete.

In regards to "For a Smile" -- I don't think I'll ever finish it. O.o I
have no inspiration for it. But I may go back one of these days and
take what I have to devise a simpler ending. And if inspiration ever
hits in the future, I'll write a sequel with the plot that I started
originally. I'm sorry if this upsets anyone...

As for "Tears of the Moon" -- Oh, this one I love and adore. But because
I worship Tolkien like nothing else... I fear a loss in my ability to
live up to his standards. I WILL when inspiration returns, finish it.
In truth, this installment is only a chapter or two from completion...
So, with hope, I look forward to finishing it.

Otherwise! Thanks again everyone. :-)
