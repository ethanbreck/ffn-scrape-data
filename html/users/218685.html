Hiya. It's Rei Rei here.

I'm don't feel like wasting space here with pointless trivia about myself, so I'll be quick and get down to basics.

I write. I'm not perfect. If I have something to say to you, I'll say it. It might hurt your feelings, but rest assured I'm only bothering because I think you have potential. If you choose to take offence or 'have a sad', that's okay. Send abusive things to my inbox, I can take it. Personally though, I feel you'd be much better off taking the advice to heart. There are so many writers here that are stuck on the 'Let's go to the park!" level, because their fans are too scared to hurt their feelings by leaving any reviews that may cause even the slightest offence, even though fair critisicm is a most useful tool.

Having said that, if I feel I that I have been unjust in my comments - I will apologize. I'm not pretending to be perfect, after all. If you feel you deserve an apology and you haven't recieved one, feel free to drop me an email or a private message. Usually you will discover that if you actually READ what I have said, without taking offence at the slightest thing, I am actually complimenting you in the fact that I feel you are worth improving.

Once I got a comment on one of my 'babies' that tore me up, but I took the advice to heart (and without TOO much offence... :p) and I can say that it definitely helped me do even better than before.

Enjoy.
