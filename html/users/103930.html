Author has written 4 stories for I Dream of Jeannie, and H2O: Just Add Water.

I think I need to get this up front and center:

I do not write romances.

I do write adventures, with a hint of romance, though I prefer to make them into friendships.

Now on to the rest of the profile (which needs a major re-write!)

I am a big fan of Djinni (genie) stories, and like to write "new" I Dream of Jeannie "episodes" in addition to stories that borrow from the series. Although I have recently dipped into the waters of "H2O: Just Add Water" recently. I wonder if I could combine Jeannie and H20...

Factlet #1: I tend to write long chapters.

Normally when I write, I don't even put the chapters in, preferring to just go with the flow, and leave it at that. But, as we all know, on the Internet, attention spans are minuscule (that is, exceptionally tiny.) I do my best at making chapters that are shorter while keeping them focused on that particular scene or set of related scenes, yet I do understand they can go longer (3000 words or so.)

Now, if my writing style is compelling to you (that is, you don't want to stop reading) then you may not even notice that the chapters are longer than what most people post. Other than with shorts stories, though I have managed to give them chapters, too.

Of course, my sincere hope is that y'all don't notice and are not only reading, but planning on posting some critiques of my work here. Because, as any writer or other creative person knows, reviews and constructive criticism help make the artist produce that much more. Otherwise, they stop producing altogether, being absent from such sites for several years. Suddenly, they return, with massive stories to post, and then complain when they see site stats depicting people looking, and then leaving, without so much as a phantom footprint in evidence (hint hint, y'all...) ;)

So, please, do read, and review, and enjoy what I have to offer. Because I'd like to offer so much more, with a little incentive :-)

Addendum: Recently I posted a guide on the powers of the djinn for IDoJ, the cartoon-Jeannie, and related realities. Some might think it's just a copy of the various "super hero powers" out on the Internet. However, my version was always with the intent that it was strictly for djinn who blink their magic (or use their ponytail, as with the cartoon-Jeannie.) Nothing about my version has anything to do with super heroes other than coincidence :)

Another aspect to what I determined on the magic-powers is a kind of "Djinni-Persian" that their race developed separate from Farsi. Thus, I have come up with words semi-based on real Farsi words and names to create my own words for each type of "jin" power ("jin" means "magic" to them in Djinni-Persian.) I haven't written any fanfic using the words, but am considering it.
