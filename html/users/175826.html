Greets!

Hey there, thanks for checking out my profile. Here's a little bit about me, as well as a link to check out more of my work. (The stuff on FF.net is, by necessity, only a small sample of what I've done.)

I'm a long-time writer that's recently gotten back into the scene in a big way. I began writing erotica all the way back during the time of the Alt.Games.Final.Fantasy.Hentai Newsgroup about fifteen years ago, give or take. (A crazy long time ago, it seems.) I used to specialize in strictly F/F romance stories with an erotic slant, but since then I've expanded my horizons some.

I've gone by some of the following names in my past doing Fanfiction online: La Parka Girl, Take Me New, and Angel Empress among them. As you can tell, I had a bit of an identity issue back then, in more ways than one. I did a lot of stupid things back then, and chief among them is giving up on my writing because I didn't think it was going anywhere. Sure, it still might not, but that's a poor excuse to give up on something you love. So I'm back to give it my all, and I'm having a heck of a lot of fun doing it.

More Writing:

Most of my stuff is found on Hentai Foundry, since a lot of it violates the ratings structure of FF.net. There's also a fair amount of original fiction there, specifically my two signature stories; Detention for Kim and Rise of the Battlebitch. One's a simple tale about a girl and her teacher, while the other's a comedy-based futa-filled fantasy romp.

Follow me on tumblr for the most up to date stuff! dracedomino tumblr com (Just add a few dots.)
