Major Phantom Characters' Descriptions that I usually write:

Erik: My Erik mostly, if not, always will be based on Leroux version. I read the book first, and Leroux Erik is by far my favorite version. He will be dark and tormented almost to the point of insanity. He will not be insane as such; rather, he will go through periods of insanity and not. Usually, I write him as a murderer, but one that does not take perverse pleasure from murdering. I.E. He is not a serial killer but has murdered. His past will usually remain a mystery that will slowly unfold in the story's progession. He will always LOVE CHRISTINE, and I do not write E/OC! Erik will only love Christine. His signature look will be an all black tuxedo, black mask, gold glaring eyes, and the deformity of Leroux's description. I try to keep his age from 30s to 40s. Leroux never specified how old Erik truly was but he is always, however, older than Christine. I try not to stray far from that.

Christine: I use various versions of her unlike Erik, who stays consistent to Leroux's version. She can range from quiet and timid to bold and strong-willed. One thing, however, she will not be is ignorant. I will always refrain from making her into a mindless and stupid twit. Although, I do not support her character's actions in the novel, I respect her as a ROUND character. She will NEVER be flat/static. As far as physical appearance, she will remain blonde-haired and blue-eyed. She will be torn with her feelings between Erik and Raoul. She will be frightened of him at first, but usually will grow to understand him. She will feel a loyalty or duty to Erik, which will only add to her confusion. Her age will usually remain in early teens (16) to early adulthood (20s).

Raoul: At times, I did write him as the bad guy; however, I have found I am more comfortable with him as the genuine man in love. In the novel, I was turned off by his childish reactions, but I also acknowledge that he did genuinely and truly love Christine. He will always be of wealthy background and have a significantly older brother who is his mentor. His physical appearance does not alter from blonde hair and blue-eyed. He may or may not have the moustache that Leroux bestowed on him. His reactions/actions will seem a bit too impulsive, but his motives are always pure. He is a noble character, and I wish to represent him as such in my writing. His age will usually range from 20-25.

Meg/Madame Giry: Ah, the Girys! Meg will usually be timid as described in Gaston's version, with dark brunette hair and brown eyes. She will help Christine by defending her or guiding her. Or she may not be included in the story if not needed. Madame Giry is probably the only character that I try not to remain solely dependant on Leroux's description. She will usually have dark hair in a bun, and piercing beady eyes. She will have known Erik for years, and tried to be his mentor. She will feel devotion and a need to protect him no matter his insane acts of violence. She WILL have teeth, unlike the description in Leroux's novel where she had few in her head. Her age is not important; however she will be older than Erik. I make her and Nadir around the same age.

Nadir/The Persian: I have always loved this man's character. Using a bit of Kay's and Leroux's mixture, I combined the elemnts into one character. He will always have the title daroga (police chief). He has a bit of mystery like Erik, and is the only one Erik considers a friend. Although, the man can be presumptuous, he tries to help Erik. His looks will be based off Middle Eastern men since he was from Persia (i.e. Iran). He will know most of Erik's secrets, and will call Erik out on his misbehaviors. He usually is first introduced as the mysterious Persian man with the Afghan hat like in Gaston's novel. He takes it upon himself to protect Erik while also protecting other people from the masked man. When Erik's bouts of insanity or anger flare, Nadir is one who tries to reason or bring him back to reality. Without the Persian's tale, we would never know what happened to Raoul, Christine, Erik, and himself the night of the scorpion and grasshopper. He is usually older than Erik but may at times be the same age.

ABOUT ME:

What can I say about me? Well, I am a twenty-one year old who enjoys a myriad of activities such as writing, playing piano, singing (not very well), reading books. Writing since third grade, I have always had a knack for creating stories and reading them as well. I am a complex person that knows the value of humor and the love of God. Yes, I am a Christian...However, I realize that not all people share my beliefs so I do not press them on anyone. I absolutely loathe it when people judge others...probably explains the connection I feel for our dear masked man. Although, I appreciate constructive criticism; there is a difference between tearing down someone and building them up with beneficial advice. Therefore, I do not support flamers in any shape or fashion, for it is not necessary to 'bash' or 'de-value' another being. If you cannot see it, I have an eclectic style and mindset. I plan on attending college and getting a Bachelor's degree, which I will use to further myself in the law enforcement field. I plan to rise and work for law enforcement on a Federal level. Because of my interest and future in this field, much of my writing will entail a police/interrogative/criminal theme. Much of the crime that I write has been observed in movies, media, or serial killers that I am familiar. I realize the value of reviews, so if you are to leave a review, I will kindly return the favor. I pray that you enjoy my writing, for I love to write for your pleasure. Do not be afraid to leave me a note, detailing what you liked or disliked...or even what you might want to see happen. I am a very vocal person cyberspace-wise but in person, I am very introverted. I do not speak about myself, but I enjoy helping others. Stories that I read usually entail a good plot, round characters (some static ones are fine), decent to excellent grammar, beautifully developed syntax, and creative phrases/descriptions. If you would like to know more about me, feel free to ask! Enjoy my works!
