Glad you noticed. A soft smile, a quick glance into your eyes and you are in awe at the creature before you. A woman like no other you have ever seen. Dark auburn hair with bright green eyes that are almost electric. She stares at you pulling you into her gaze, controlling every thought you have. This woman, in her long black vinyl gown is smiling at you. Her fangs are showing slightly, they give her beauty such a devilish quality. This lovely vampyre. Stepping forward quickly she places a hand on your sholder then begins to whisper to you softly. Her voice is so smooth and enticing that you wrap an arm around her waist pulling her closer. The black leather ball gown she is wearing is cool to the touch, showing off every curve of her body. You smile to yourself as you walk with her knowing that an interesting evening is ahead.
