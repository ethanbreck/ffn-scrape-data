Author has written 23 stories for Trigun, Star Wars, Harry Potter, and Twilight.

Basics: I write a lot of stuff, but I upload very little of it. Probably what you'll see on here is just my song parodies. You might expect to find one from any genre, but seeing as I'm a nerdy girl, it will most likely be Star Wars, Harry Potter, Twilight, Psych, Pirates of the Caribbean, Scrubs, and who knows what else?!

Not that I would think anyone ever would, but I suppose I could do requests, if people have them. After all, I'm a procrastinating college student so just shoot me a message if you have an idea or something you like to see.

I love reviews, because that's how I get better. So give me your open and honest opinions! If you feel I am in need of constructive criticism but don't want to leave it on the reviews page, by all means, send me a message. I love hearing people's suggestions!

That's it for my writing. As far as me personally, like I said, I'm a college student with a terrible case of senioritis. I'm studying Theater and Electronic Media and I want to work on movies. Eventually I want to direct my own, but for the time being I just want to work on the set and in post production. I'm an Irish dancer, and no that does not mean clogging or 'riverdancing' (whatever that's supposed to mean). I wear a curly wig and a pretty dress and I love it. Dance is pretty much my life except for when I'm being a creative nerd.

I love movies, and there are actually a lot of TV show out there that I enjoy, but I just don't have the time for them! But that's why I want to go into the filmmaking business, is my love for the art. Putting something real in a space where it can be captured and then shared with the world. That's just awesome. (Told you I'm nerdy about this stuff!) I also LOVE music. Many types too!

I get bored easily, so I'd love to chat. just shoot me a message anytime!
