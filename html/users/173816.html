Low's Blog (Updated 3/14/2016)

Hey! How're you guys doing? I've been... busy. With life. I actually haven't done much writing in recent years due to other things distracting me or interesting me or things like that. And inspiration just wasn't happening for a long time, and any writer will tell you that if you're not inspired then writing just doesn't happen. So I was in kind of a funk for a long time. I've watched a lot of different things in that time but none of them - not even Gravity Falls which was awesome - really sparked any creativity for me.

And then I started playing a game very recently that has changed all of that.

So yeah, a new story is coming from me soon! About that game. Which game? Well you'll just have to come back and see when I start posting! I ain't spoiling anything.

Seriously, check back here in the near future. There's something new coming from me and I'm very excited for this story idea. I think some of you who're still following me will like it. At least I hope so. See you all soon!

Continuity Check - My Phineas and Ferb Stories

It occurs to me that I should make this clear, but my Phineas & Ferb fics are all intended to be within the same continuity.

In chronological order the stories and their events go like this:

1) A Day in the Life (except for the epilogue)
2) Royal Rumbling Hearts (happens not quite a year later)
3) A Day in the Life epilogue (happens roughly four years after RRH)
4) Love the One You're With (happens roughly two months later)
5) When I Come Around (happens roughly two months later)
6) Summer of Love (happens the summer after the gang's Freshman year of high school)

I will update this list as I add stories to the timeline. Also, if I write any P&F fics that are outside of this continuity, I will denote them as such.
