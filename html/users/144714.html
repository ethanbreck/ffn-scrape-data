i love the way thoughts seem to stick in my head.

sugah, dragonflie, sunfreak, nine, allekto, rae, aphrael, el juno, faded jae, herongale etc are daiken/kensuke authors. all of them are devastatingly talented, and (and nine is really really fabulous, but has a takedai tendency which i cannot get used to ;;;)

in my head, they belong to the daiken fandom. they are some of the best there is, and in a way, they define the pairing. make any sense? don't worry, it doesn't make any sense to me, either.

debra, jheya ono, angel of peace, natsu, clearskies, splash, n-chan, zara and many many more are taito authors, and are amazing.

these are the people who can make me laugh, make me cry, make me squeal and make me want to glomp and dedicate a shrine to them. everytime they update, i get so happy it scares me. how is it that my happiness lies in the hands of a bunch of fanficcers?

oh, fanfiction. i must be crazy...

17, female.

avid taito and daiken fan, and author of many fluffy, sappy and very much cliched fics. most of them aren't good at all, but it'd be kind of you to check them out and perhaps, leave a review?

and i have a tendency to neglect fics i should work on, and start new, pointless fics. it's a bad habit, really... so if you really wanna see something updated, please mail me, else most of what's down there:points to fics: will be left to rot.

save my fics! thank you. _

WARNING: you should probably avoid the earlier fics... :winces: i will not be held responsible if anyone chokes on the fluff and/or bad writing.
