Hello all :)

For those who know me, I need no explanation. My reputation precedes me. As does my ego.

For those who don't know me, I'm awesome. No, really. I'm just frickin awesome. I'm also a total hottie, and own an ego the size of a small country. It's name is Spike. You've been warned.

I love writing, movies, music, video games, good stories/books, anime/manga, and so many other things. I love horses, and ride competitively. My device is a feather, although it can be many other things. I'm Wiccan. I have the bestest friends ever.

I congratulate all of you that have put your writing on ff.net, or sites like it. It takes courage to post your writings for others' scrutiny, and I applaud you for taking that step. For those who haven't, I encourage you to give it a try. You learn so many valuble things, and grow as a writer.

Thanks to my buds and loyal readers ;) you guys know who you are. Without you I wouldn't have the motivation to write like I do. Thanks for all the times, both good and bad. You're all crispy, and I love ya to bits. Huzzah!
