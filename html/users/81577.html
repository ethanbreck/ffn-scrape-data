Snafu's Challenges:

Two On Two:This one is simple enough. Named after the cutscene in Resident Evil 5 in which Al Wesker and Evil Jill beat down Chris and Sheva inside the Monarch Room.

The character doing Wesker's role must use the following dialogue: 'You haven't changed,' the family reunion line, 'So slow to catch on,' and of course, the 'two on two' line. The character doing Jill's role has to be masked or wearing a cloak of some kind when the big reveal is made. You can mix it up, use good characters for the evil roles and vice-versa.

The Exiles: A Naruto challenge, inspired by Perfect Lionheart and from my own Naruto fanfic, Naruto: Consequences. Konoha has finally fallen, and has burned to the ground. Half of the Konoha 11 survived the onslaught, along with several of their senseis.

Naruto and Hinata, having long since been banished on trumped up charges, has found refuge in either Spring, Wave, Water or Wind Country, and wants nothing to do with them. Sasuke is either dead or still with Orochimaru. Tsunade and Jiraiya are dead. The surviving members of the Rookie 11 now must make a new life for themselves...either as shinobi of another village, or as missing ninja.
