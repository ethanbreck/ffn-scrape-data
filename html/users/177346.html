Author has written 27 stories for Xena: Warrior Princess, Wrestling, Dark Angel, A song of Ice and Fire, and Vampire Diaries.

I used to write stories, back in the day when my English wasn't even that good. Then I stopped writing. Oh I could blame Life, I could blame a lot of things but the truth is, I got lazy. Yup. L-a-z-y. And then I kept switching shows and fandoms and started reading way too much fanfic and decided I liked reading better than writing. (Because there is way way too much talent on this site, it's very intimidating lol)

But... Some stories never die. Some characters just won't shut up. And to this day, I am still carrying a notebook in my purse with ideas and notes about future or even old stories. There might be hope still. We shall see.

*Update: Well, I have started posting again, 2 Dark Angel fics for now, a one-shot and a bigger project I'm quite fond of.

As for new stories, I have so many ideas, Firefly, Dragon Age, Veronica Mars, Dark Angel... So many possibilities... We shall see what happens next...

*Update 2: The world of A Song of Ice and Fire (Aka Game of Thrones) has claimed me. I have been religiously watching the show and reading the books for a while, but somehow today I started writing about it. One-shot short fics for now, maybe more later...

*Update 3: I started working on a Vampire Diaries project called : The Witch and the Vampire Chronicles. It's a series of scenes and one-shots about Damon and Bonnie. Will be posted soon.

Please remember to review, I accept even 2 words-reviews such as: Reading it. Like it. Hate it. Good job. It sucks. Keep going. Kill it. Or you can even tell me that I am wasting valuable internet space with my ramblings. Anything, just let me know someone is reading ;)

About my old stories, I will not be re-posting them, as horrible as some of them are, especially my first "Xena" stories, with the bad English. I am fond of them still, even though slightly embarrassed. We are start somewhere and this where I did, they are great memories. My Wrestling stories are a bit better, as I was very focused on learning English by then, but I still had a lot to learn, both in languages and writing as well. Might be I'll even finish one or 2 of them... But warning to all the readers (Can you believe I'm still getting hits for 10 years-old stories??) Please don't judge me on my early years, we get better as we go, right? :)

Enjoy!

If you'd like to read my ventings and travel experiences *
