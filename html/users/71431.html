Author has written 15 stories for Harry Potter.

MY FANFICS:

Look to the Stars-Another Rose and Scorpius story, this time focusing on their relationship, or lack thereof, post-Hogwarts.

A Way With Words-This story focuses on James Sirius Potter, Fred Weasley II, and their BFF and OC, Alice Longbottom as they navigate the very competitive world of Quidditch in their seventh year at Hogwarts. Completed as of October 3, 2019.

A Different Kind of Love Story-This will me a multi-chapter story set in Rose, Scorpius, & Albus' seventh year. I enjoyed trying my hand at something new. I posted this a week before HP and the Cursed Child came out and before that play, little was known about the Next Generation. So it was nice having free reign over the characters and the plot.

Goodbyes on the Balcony-This is the sequel to Tears on the Balcony and Kisses on the Balcony. It's going to be the final story of the three-part series. It's the gang three years after Hogwarts dealing with so much turmoil mainly due to the war. They are no longer teenagers and are finding that concept somewhat hard to grasp. Completed as of May 4, 2016.

Blame-This is just a one-shot on the day of Fred's funeral. It's about how all of the Weasleys and the people who love them cope with the aftermath of the unfortunate death of one of their own. I really enjoyed writing this. It's one of the first stories I wrote that wasn't set during the Marauder era and I was surprised to find that I enjoyed writing a short scene to coincide with post-Deathly Hallows. I felt like a lot more research was involved, not only with specific details but in learning about each of the characters and their mannerisms.

Kisses on the Balcony-This is the sequel to Tears on the Balcony so you should read that story before reading this one. Completed as of March 2010.

Tears on the Balcony-Another LJ fic. It's a little bit AU as it was started before the 6th book came out. Completed as of February 4, 2007. I wrote it when I was only 16 years old so bear with the amateur writing.

Memories- This story is an AU fic since it most definitely does not follow the fifth book. It's so far from the truth but I don't care. This is my first L/J fic that takes place after Hogwarts so it was an interesting experience for me. This one is completed as of April 5, 2004. I wrote it when I was only 13 years old so bear with the amateur writing.

Someday-This was just supposed to be a tiny fanfic based off of James' thoughts about Lily. I wasn't planning on writing a sequel or prequel to it because I like it the way it is. It was inspired by my best friend's thoughts about his crush and it led to this.

Late Night Kiss-I have received some strange e-mails and reviews about this. This wasn't supposed to be preferably realistic. It was just supposed to be fun and cute. Everyone receives a broken heart at one point in their life and this is what had happened to Lily and what led to it.

Written in the Stars-This is a one-chapter story about the relationships of Lily's past through the eyes of Remus which I had a good time writing. I like the character of Remus and it was fun to get inside his head.

Elaborate Lives-This is a story of how Sirius had found his true identity by running away from home written through his own eyes, which I also enjoyed writing since he's my FAVORITE character. This story coincides with WRITTEN IN THE STARS, but you don't have to read one to understand the other.

My Valentine-Naturally, this is also LJ story, just a one-shot, but it's mostly just about Lily and her thoughts on Valentine's Day.

Hogwarts: Beginning to End-Just something I wrote when I was bored one day

Unconditional Love-This is just a one-shot on Sirius' thoughts on Lily and James' wedding day.
