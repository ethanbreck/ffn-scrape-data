Author has written 25 stories for Misc. Plays/Musicals, Weiss Kreuz, Prince of Tennis, and Saiyuki.

I'm a fairly lazy author, mostly retired from posting things out in the big world. I've bopped around from fandom to fandom over the past bazillion years that this account has been active. Currently I'm not in any particular fandom, but what I've done over the past few years can be found over on my livejournal.

Here you'll mostly find what I thought was fun at one time or another. Some of you, who've been with me for a while, may notice a few things missing. I decided to delete them because I'm never going to finish them. I thought it was for the best. If you really have an itch for something you once read that is now gone, just drop me a line and I can point you to where to find it. Everything else that's here is finished with the exception of one piece. I couldn't bring myself to delete it, though. Too many good memories come with it.

So yes. Here are my stories. Who knows if or when there will be more. It was fun, though.

- Whisper 16-10-2010
