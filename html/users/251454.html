Hey, Gryffindor Heiress here. I'm updating once again since this bio thing just seemed too crowded.

Real Name: Alina (but feel free to call me Li, Lina, Lala, Nina, Lilo, Leo, etc...I pretty much answer to anything. Yup, one of my friends actually calls me "Child" and I answer to it!)

Pen Name: Gryffindor Heiress, and that's that. I'm not changing it anymore, or getting a new one. I love it the way it is.

Age: fifteen (as of January 27! Even though the whole world just about forgot my birthday, I'm coping just fine. I'm not exactly on that bridge between preteen and teendom anymore. It's great being JUST an adolescent!)

Other Works of Fiction (Written By Me): These aren't posted at fanfiction.net, or anywhere else for that matter. They're just stuff that popped into my head when I had writers' block while writing BOTC, and other random things that just come to me during the strangest times. Anyways, they are- "The Early Days" (Justin Timberlake / MMC fic), "New Beginnings" (sequel to TED; *NSYNC / Justin Timberlake fic), "Together Again" (third installment of the TED saga; *NSYNC / Justin Timberlake fic), "Happily Ever After" (last installment of the TED saga; contains alternate endings), "Overseas" (okay, I lied, this fic is up at *NSYNC Dreamworks, although I'm not sure if Dreamworks is still up; *NSYNC / JC Chasez fic), "Over Troubled Waters" ("Overseas" sequel), "Hard to Get" (a love triangle fic; *NSYNC / JC Chasez / Lance Bass fic), "Until You Came Along" (Justin Timberlake fic), "Choosing Between the Ones You Love" (Justin Timberlake / Nick Carter fic), "Ain't No Mountain High Enough" (a "Remember the Titans" fic revolving around actor Ryan Gosling's character, Alan Bosley), "We Still Got It" (*NSYNC / Lance Bass fic), "If I'm Not the One" (sequel to BOTC), "Parallel Universe" (prequel to BOTC, working title), "The Marauders Meet the Raiders" (working title; the MWPP finally meet their match), "Just Another Slytherin" (spinoff fic), "Living in the Shadows" (spinoff fic), "Save the Last Dance for Me" (Draco / Hermione fic), "Fallen From Heaven" (Harry Potter/Greek Mythology crossover fic); "Keep Digging" (Holes fic). And that's it for now. I might think of something else eventually, and that's end up taking all my time out of homework, and I'll suffer the consequences greatly.

Hobbies (other than writing): singing (I sang the National Anthem at a baseball game), dancing (I've been dancing with Westlake School for the Perofmring Arts for nine years now), acting (I just finished my run as an extra in the ARHS production of Joe Mantegna's Bleacher Bums, I just finished up the ARHS spring musical production of Little Shop of Horrors, and I act with John Robert Powers during the summer), boys (what to say about boys other than that they're great and handsome things, but they're the center of all evil? Actually, that's my friend Max's theory on girls, but I decided to switch it around to make it my theory on boys), reading (HP is the best!), listening to music, Rent (NO DAY BUT TODAY!)

To Those Reading This Profile at FictionPress: I am the author of "...Or Forever Hold Your Peace", which is just about the corniest pice of fiction ever! Anywho, I know that some might want a sequel, and I'm trying really hard to make that happen. I've been working on a sequel for about two years now! So, let's cross our fingers and hope that works out fine.

P.S.: Be on the look out for a possible new work of fiction from me, "Keep Digging", my Holes sequel fic! I've really been itching to get it posted, so be on the look out. The production of it is going quite smoothly!

Much Love,
Gryffindor Heiress
