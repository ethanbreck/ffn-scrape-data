About me as a writer:

I'm a stickler for grammar and spelling. If you see a mistake in my writing, please let me know so I can fix it. It bugs me to no end if I get something out there and someone notices a mistake. I know when I'm reading a fic and see a mistake, it pulls me out of my imagination long enough to where it's hard for me to get back into reading and I don't want that to happen to any of my readers.

I've tried writing fics without OCs but I just can't do it yet. My writing depends on being immersed in the world I'm creating and I can't seem to do that if I'm having to think as an already established character. But I'm working on that. I hope to write a fic soon between two characters in a show (probably a Hak/Yona from Akatsuki no Yona) but for now most of my work is that of the female Original Character variety. You'll notice that most of my OC's are from Texas. Yes, I'm from Texas and I just find a character I can relate with easier to write. I am in no way like my characters I create. I'm not tough, smart, and/or charismatic so maybe I make them into what I wish I could be. Hopefully they aren't Mary Sue's. I try to make some glaring character flaws but I am prone to failure in that department.

When I start a fic, it usually turns out to be a multi-chapter, 40k plus words fic. It's like I get started and have these ideas but I've only ever finished one. However, I wouldn't mind writing a one shot or two. I have a bad habit of moving between obsessions and interests often and if I'm immersed in the world then it's hard for me to stick with a fic.

Writing is an escape for me and it also gives me control over something when I find I have little control over everything else. I love creating characters and putting them into these fictional worlds.

List of shows/movies/anime I've started writing on, all with OCs:

The Walking Dead - Jess/Daryl: My favorite show and the show I've written the most about over the past several months. I've almost completed one fic (50k words) and started a sequel (30k so far). Daryl is just too awesome NOT to write about. Walking the Line is finished and I've picked up the sequel again!

Leverage - Jennifer/Eliot: I got pretty far into this fic before I lost the inspiration. It was during this fic I realized I constantly abuse my OCs. They usually take the most damage, mentally an physically, within the first couple of chapters. It's a reoccurring theme I use to show that my characters are strong, but still human and capable of injury, even death. I have about 60k words so far on the main fic and a sequel for Leverage with my OC. Don't expect I'll be finishing this one, though. Not unless I suddenly get inspired again.

Marvel - Sarah/Steve: I've started writing an OC into Captain America's life. She's a sniper on a mercenary team for good. There's more to her than that but that's the basics. I've got about the first 5 chapters written, which cover their meeting up to the part in WS where (SPOILER) Fury "dies". I've got plans for her between WS then AoU up through AoU though she won't have a big part then another bigger but not impacting part in CW (WHICH WAS AMAZING, minus the Sharon kiss. Not enough substance in that relationship to warrant that even though we know they're a thing in the comics). Anyway, it's going to be a super slow burn for my OC and Cap. This fic will take me quite a long time to finish, if ever so don't expect to see it up here anytime soon.

Star Trek - Kassandra/Kirk I actually created a character for the 2009 reboot that I wrote along into the movie. I'm literally just trying to finish the fic. I have maybe one or two chapters left and it's done but I'm so stuck on what to do next I keep moving on to write something else. It's probably one of my favorites I've written so far. Expect to see this one start getting put up on FF.net soon.

Lord of the Rings - Gilriel/Legolas: I made another female probably more of a Mary Sue Elf for LotR. I have an outline for everything through the Battle at Pelennor Fields. Just got to finish up the outline through the Black Gate and the end. I doubt I'll ever do this one but it was fun to make a character and work an outline. Maybe I'll offer it up for someone else to write if they want to. I kind of quit working on this as my favorite character isn't really Legolas anymore. It's Eomer after reading about him from the books but he has Lothiriel and I just can't see him with anyone else.

Fairy Tail - Callista/Rogue: I was super proud of the backstory on this character until they release the recent chapters of the manga. It practically blows my plans up. I'd hoped to someday publish this one but it doesn't look like it will see the light of day. Fairy Tail has some great history so I had fun writing it and it gave me some practice at incorporating a back story into cannon. This is probably the most OP character I've created next to Gilriel from LotR. Though Gilriel is more of a Mary Sue, Callista has a huge Achilles heel. Well, two really. It really helps balance out her powers. She's probably the most well rounded character I've created.

Naruto - Momoko/Kakashi This is one of those fics that I started strong with, like I felt it was the best writing I've ever done then kind of let it fall to the wayside. Momoko is probably my weakest character to date as far as strength. She has her own style of fighting that makes her valuable but she's not physically strong at all. She's one of my favorites I've ever written because of her interaction as a sibling with another canon character. It was so fun to write their banter. Also, writing Kakashi was interesting in itself. He's serious most of the time but the few jokes and teasing he does were fun to throw in with the banter of the siblings. It's also fun to see him fall for my OC before she falls for him. It's been the other way around for all my fics.
