So...this profile page used to be about 8 billion pages long and contain everything there was to know about my reading habits, or at least everything there was to know three years ago, so I suppose it's time for an update.

My name is Tyrror, or a least that's what I go by almost exclusively on the internet, and I answer readily to it. I love to write and tend to get swept into fandoms which I will write exclusively for over a period of time before finding myself swept on to the next one. I'd go through my past trends, but it's just as easy for you to skim through my old works and judge for yourself. I don't believe in deleting old works unless you are replacing them with a revamped and reworked version so you will find a lot of old (really old, for all you know it may be older than you) stuff in my archives most of which is crap but some of which seems to have garnered me a small following that hope I'll eventually get around to completing something (I do hope to get there some day however right now I'm a bit crunched for time so...we'll see).

I've finally made it into the medical field as a student doctor thus my time is rather pressed and what I'm doing tends to depend less on what I choose to do and more on what I'm told to do and who's attempting to die at that very moment. One day I hope to rewrite many of my old stories and complete my more recent ones as well as write a lot of what I haven't even put on paper yet, but we can only hope as of now. So, please be kind, leave me your comments and reviews (I do actually read them all) and perhaps you'll find something you like along the way.
