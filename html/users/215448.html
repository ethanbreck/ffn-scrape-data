Hey, this is Sea-Tiger170 here! (Obviously. If you didn't know that, then why would you be at my profile page, hmmm? Yeah, that's what I thought. Uhuh. Right.)

Anyway, I'm 16 and a girl, as if you couldn't tell. I'm a born-and-bred American, and proud of it.

That's basically all you need to know about me, other than the fact that I hardly ever (if ever) write anything, and that I'm notorious for my illegible fan-girl-freak-out reviews.

I put only the best stories in my favorites, so check them out!

Here's a run-down on some of my fav's of the fav's:

~A Man of Stature by TrudiRose: A quaint Beauty and the Beast story, with a great moral and narration that never gets boring. I could reread this again and again, and that means something because I usually hate rereading stories. An OC is present there, but she's more of a cannon character than anything else. TrudiRose is a master of prose, dialogue, and everything else a fanfiction (or original fiction) author needs to craft a perfect final product that readers will always adore. If you're looking to read this one, check up on its prequel Just Rewards first, and then chow down on this bit of meat. It's one of my ultimate favorites. The second fiction I've ever read on Beauty and the Beast, really (the first being Just Rewards). Even if this usually isn't your cup of tea, I encourage you to check it out. It's the greatest of the greatest from an author that doesn't accept any less. Read it, and you'll love it. I promise from the depths of my heart. My all-time favorite slice of fanfiction out there. What's more, it's based on Gaston. Who doesn't love Gaston (coughcough)? That's what I thought. What happens when Gaston gets afflicted by the enchantress' curse? Is he, by his vanity and utter disregard for anyone but himself, destined to stay cursed forever? Can he never break free of his old habits? Let's just say, once a Gaston, always a Gaston, but that doesn't mean people's insides can't change with a little magic push, and a whole lot of tears. Am I ranting too much for you? Sorry, but I've got to convince you to read this somehow. It really makes a serious impact on you, and might even change your outlook on life. It did mine. I can only hope that you can find it in your time-schedule to give this fic a good read. Because that's exactly what you'll get from it, a fabulously good read.

~The Other Trainer by Writer's Block: While I haven't read it in the longest time, I still have this sweet want-to-cry-but-can't feeling in my gut whenever I think of it. Pokemon fiction can get repeated over and over again, with meaningless OCs, horrible Mary Sues, freakish Gary Stues-- you name it, you've got it. But The Other Trainer has none of these classic fanfiction pitfalls. It's one of the most endearing tales out there: one of true friendship and what it means to truly work for what you get. You're cheering for Stephan the whole way, regardless of whether you normally like the Pokemon genre or not. Who exactly is Stephan, you may ask? Another horribly done OC that makes true writers want to cry? Not likely. He's exactly what the title says, one of the "other trainers". Yeah, that's right, you've heard of them if you've ever watched the first few Pokemon episodes. I bet you've never even given them a second thought, hmm? Well, neither did the world, but despite that, this rounded-out character pulls through heartache and heartbreak to make something of himself. A few words of caution, however: the narrative might seem a bit silly at first, but that only fits Stephan's style. It's in first person, so what can you expect? Regardless, it's one of the best out there. I highly encourage you to sit down and chow down.

~The Neglected by Cerse Liminara: This author's contributions to the fanfiction scene are truly memorable and thought-provoking. The Neglected starts a short trilogy of one-shots based on the three aspects of the Derris Karlan emblem in Tales of Symphonia. Since they're so short, there's not much to say, but...they're good. Try them out and you won't regret it.

~To Fight The Culinary Forces of Evil by Jadax: A cute, (adorable) piece of fiction about the Wonder Chef from Tales of Symphonia. What ToS fan doesn't enjoy the Wonder Chef? Certainly not I, that's for sure. Check it out, you'll like it, really.

Anyway, those are some of the best of the best. Who knows, maybe if you e-mail me, I'll check out one of your fics!

A final goodbye from the rabid-reviewer, and a "ta-ta for now", just to keep things edgy.

Remember: if you want to read story but you can't find one that suits what you want, write it yourself! Just...please make sure it's of good quality, okay? Thanks.
