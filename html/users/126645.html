Then I am the graceful and mystic elvin princess ingvanya or swashbuckling raina, queen of the pirates.

But I like moonlit walks on the beach...ha just kidding. More like archery, stories, freedom, leadership possibilities, horses, swords, being different. Stuff like that.

My story faves are vision of escaflone, spirited away and Samurai Champloo (aka: Fuugen). I hate slash (okay, not all slash, but certain slash like frodo/sam. RAWR), but love fluff... I'm also very appreciative of not overdone comedy.

Apparently I'm comedian level funny when I bitch.

My writing level has improved/matured since I last wrote in 2006.

After that point (2006) I took a long Hiatus. Now I'm back. But I'm very busy. I will do my best to get my chapters out in good time.
