Author has written 17 stories for Tenchi Muyo, Misc. Anime/Manga, Card Captor Sakura, Evangelion, StarTrek: The Original Series, Outlaw Star, Yu-Gi-Oh, Stargate: SG-1, Dragon Age, and Mass Effect.

Amazingly Still Alive. Somehow. thirty-something, Occasional Storyteller. media-editor, professional Wizard and per-viewer of random things. Fan of fantasy (Game of Thrones, Lord of the Rings, Shinia Chronicles, Dragon Age) , Science Fiction (Killjoys, Honor Harrington, Darhak, Star Trek, Star Wars, Doctor Who, Mass Effect), Political tech thrillers (Anything Tom Clancy), all things military and general comic geekyness (Marvel more than DC, but Loving the new DCTVU). I do not write happy stories. in terms of 'shipping' because of how my muse works I tend to not ship the canon couple for a show, game or series there are exceptions obviously and sometimes the canon couple do hit just the right spot, but instead I tend to make instinctual connections which often means some strange uncommon pairings come out of my head. Generally I won't write a story for a pairing unless I can see the connections for myself. If I can't convince myself there is something there worth writing about; I'm never going to convince anyone else of a relationship dynamic. which unfortunately makes me terrible at fulfilling prompts. feel free to send 'em to me, I will read them all but I can't give you solid time I'll get around to writing it unless its something that catches my eye. So hope you enjoy my Writings, random babbling and personal brand of crazy. If you spot any really, really obvious errors Fanfiction Quick Edit has almost certainly done something new and terrible during parsing after I've uploaded - I always check before I publish live but I'm only human mistakes happen.

Announcement - Stories inbound, relatively soon. The muse is with me for the first time in ages after the 'Great Mass Effect 3 burnout'. Stories for The Flash, Supergirl, Legends of Tomorrow, Doctor Who, and Dragon Age (That Fic) will be incoming, not necessarily in that order.
