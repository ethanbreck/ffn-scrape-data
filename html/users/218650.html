Hey I'm SilverDragonChild aka the KeijoGirl:

I've been writing since I was in the 5th grade and during the 11th grade I took a massive break from writing in general. I keep vowing to make my return, but after millions and millions of setbacks - I finally think it's time. The characters are becomming more and more developed day by day and are no doubt ready to be placed in their respective stories. _~

The first pair to debut will be "Nephilim" and "Playing the Hero"
"Playing the Hero" - Originally titled "Deku meets Dante" was a story I started a long time ago back when I was still in highschool and it is my first real submission to FF.NET. I stopped work on it because Deku sucked as a character, she was a God-Moding Mary Sue and a brat. So, I've fixed her and the other characters as well and now, after much Role Play (RP) to to develop them, and they're ready to pick up where their old selves left off.

"Nephilim" - Was spawned from the character Damian in "Deku meets Dante". The story's main character, Nathaniel, is the directly polar opposite of Damian. I decided to start this story because at the time there weren't enough Vergil centered fics. Now they're kind of everywhere, but no one ever made Dante out to be the bad guy and I thought of it as a good idea, took it with a slight bit of creative liscence and ran with it. After RPing as Nathy for a while, I grew to love him a lot and I'm pretty happy to do this story using him.
