Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter, and Lord of the Rings.

I am now 31. I started writing stories on for this site over 15 years ago, I honestly don't know where the time's gone!

I'm not going to promise regular updates anymore because let's face it, I never keep them (yes, I'm one of those type of writers, I really am very sorry).

I am still writing though, in dribs and drabs, so keep checking back, you never know one day soon you might get the pleasant surprise of seeing a new chapter up!

GENERAL UPDATE 24th January 2019: Right I'm not sure what happened. I've blinked and lost three years of my life. I was also supposed to be working on Green Eyes but a new story plot has smacked me in the face and I can't stop writing it. Really not what I wanted as I do want to finish my other stories but I'm running with it for now.

But What If - I am halfway through Chapter 22. I know it's slower than my normal updates every 2 weeks sorry but I'm finding these latest chapters emotionally difficult to write (which if you're reading you will totally get) added to that I'm still renovating my house and working full time so I can't be as quick as normal. Hopful of updating next week *fingers crossed* (19.10.19)

Green Eyes - 19th April 2015 - righty next chapter up, time constraints mean it's shorter than usual. Might not be another update for a couple of weeks just so you know. (P.S. my couple of weeks clearly means two years per week - sorry - 24.01.19)

Dreamers - drafts are ready for the next couple of chapters, will be writing Green Eyes stuff first though sorry.

Light in my Life - - chapter 11 is not going so well, but I'm working at it bit by bit.

That's it.

Over and Out.

Yavanna
