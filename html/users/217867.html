Author has written 5 stories for Tribe, Harry Potter, and High School Musical.

NOTE: I've done a bit of clean up with my stories. Mainly deleting the crap ones. I am currently working on non fan but totally fictional novels and am a Beta reader on this site.

"Me" is a very fluid concept. "Me" can be whichever cultures I am studying or have studied all rolled into one. "Me" can be British, German, Spanish, French, German, Deaf, Hearing, Goth, 'Southern', Post-Apocalyptic. Yet, "Me" is ALWAYS integrity, love, studious, determined, caring, friendly, open-minded, and a Christian.

Things I read/watch: Pretty much any fiction that is written by a talented author and I am starting to read more non-fiction (i.e. In the Wake of the Plague, Letters of Mrs. Adams, Letters of John Adams, The Irish Penny Journal, Philosophy and Theology books). I like most books made into/from movies. For my TV viewing pleasure, I enjoy scifi and investigation shows, rarely teen dramas (i.e. Degrassi, 90210, Gossip Girl, etc); pretty much anything on USA, BBC America, and SciFi as well as New Girl, Downton Abbey, Bones, Castle, and Switched at Birth (one of the few teen dramas I watch, but mostly for the educational value). I used to watch Gilmore Girls, but that was canceled and Rory said no to Logan, like a muppet. I also used to watch gLee but it has gotten boring and the singing isn't as good anymore. I watch some anime. Movies are across the board, though I tend to gravitate toward SciFi, Action, Western types. Everyone loves a good RomCom and pure Comedy.

Things I won't read/watch: SLASH, Twilight, Wizards of Waverly Place, Star Trek, 90210, most dramas on the telly recently, Unedited fics (i.e. many misspelled words, horrible grammar), Fics with OOC characters (at least warn me in the summary or A/N).

Caution: I am brutally honest when I write reviews. If I love it, I will tell you why. If I don't, I will tell you why. Don't be offended, it's called constructive criticism. Please reciprocate by being honest in your reviews to me as well. No one learns if they do not find out what they did wrong.
