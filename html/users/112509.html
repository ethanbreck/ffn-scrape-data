ABOUT THE FANFICS

As a fanfiction author, my overall intention is to explore and expand upon the canons I write for, and to fill in plot loopholes that were not addressed in the original game / manga / anime. In order to not disrupt canon, the worlds and events of each universe I write for are viewed from the eyes of original characters (more about them below). If you are looking for a story about your favorite character pairing, you will unfortunately not find it here (at least, not in my numbered "Acts").

I've broken down my overall intention into three main aims.

1) To stay as true as possible to the source material. My stories are meant to weave around and/or incorporate the dates and events of each universe's canon, complimenting them and adding more layers to what their creators have already established.

2) My stories are connected only by a pair original characters, primarily Kenneth Feng and occasionally Craig Crankurt - a pair of adopted brothers. Each fanfic is written to stand alone in their own right but if read in order, each serves as a chapter in their lives as children in Act 0 to an adult men with their own families in Act 10.

3) Collaboration with fellow authors. Though I've had the pleasure of borrowing other authors' original characters, some of the stories I've written were in deep collaboration with fellow authors Vammy for my Street Fighter fanfics ( http://www.fanfiction.net/u/256998/ ) and Hyperactive Hamster of Doom for my early Resident Evil fanfics ( http://www.fanfiction.net/u/363838/ ). Readers of both myself and either of these authors will notice many ties linking our stories together.

And I'm sure this needs no mentioning but these stories have been written over a 14 year time-span, and not in chronological order (wow, I'm old). Readers will no doubt notice a vast difference in writing quality between stories.
