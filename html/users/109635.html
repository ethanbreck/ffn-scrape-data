The Sesshoumaru/Rin Controversy

If you looked at my list for pairings, you may have noticed that I did not type Sess/ (older) Rin as many shippers of this pair do. The reason I do not feel the need to do so is that I have no problem with Sesshoumaru in a romantic relationship with Rin at age 13 or older. I do not support pedophilia but in their case, I do not see it as a problem. If the fanfiction is set in the Sengoku Period then technically speaking there is no taboo with the age difference. By age 12 or 13 Rin should be into puberty and ready for marriage and motherhood by that period's standards. However, if the fanfiction is set in present times, then yes, I do feel a little uncomfortable having a very young Rin with Sesshoumaru.

Many argue that their relationship is similar to a father/daughter, but I honestly never saw that even before I became a fan of this pair. I cannot see Rin looking at Sesshoumaru as a father figure because she seems to be a total fangirl of his. She does everything he tells her to do without question, how many people actually listen to their fathers in that manner? In addition, if she was considered his daughter then I doubt she would fear her place in Sesshoumaru's life once he builds his empire.

Call me idealistic, but I am a firm believer that Sesshoumaru will end up exactly like his father, but he is presently fighting it. He drops everything to protect her, he provides for her in some way (Rin seems to get her own food), yet he refuses to admit that he cares for her and Jaken as seen in the third Inuyasha movie. However, in one of the later episodes Rin asks Sesshoumaru if he will please promise to remember her after she dies. Sesshoumaru blank expression slips and he lets out an audible gasp at her question. Personally, I believe he reacted in such a way because it never occurred to him that he would lose her one day. Things like this lead me to believe that his feelings for Rin are similar to Inuyasha's feelings toward Kagome.

Another moment that leads me to believe Sesshoumaru and Rin will have a romantic relationship in their future also occurs in the third movie. Throughout the series, Sesshoumaru has expressed and attempted his desire to kill his little brother but has always held back. However, when Inuyasha attempted to kill Rin (and Jaken when he put himself in front of her) I believe he actually would have killed Inuyasha had it not been for Kagome. Kagome herself must have seen the look in Sesshoumaru's eyes her second sit command was screamed in terror and she pulled him down. I must say it is the third movie that leads me to believe that the Sess/Rin pairing is not far fetched.

Finally, I truly believe Sesshoumaru's father always planned on his son eventually loving a human woman because of Tenseiga. As he viewed Rin's dead body, Tenseiga for the first time reacted, as if telling Sesshoumaru to use it on her. His meeting Rin does not seem to be an accident.
