Joined 06-07-01, id: 77935, Profile Updated: 09-22-15
Author has written 11 stories for Lord of the Rings, Pirates of the Caribbean, Troy, Scarlet Pimpernel, Once Upon a Time, and Pride and Prejudice.

I have a great many unfinished stories just lounging about on here, the red-headed step children (I say this as a mostly-redhead myself) of my literary ADD. I'm slowly working on resuming The True Meaning of Humanity, and have plans to (slowly but surely) do the same for my other stories. The process is simply slow, between work, volunteering, and life.

A little on me: I'm a late-twenties-something from the Midwest. I work for a child advocacy non-profit, and I'm a bit of a workaholic, but I'm working on being a bit more of a well-rounded person.

A little on my fandoms: My current obsession is definitely Once Upon a Time, though the PotC franchise will always be close to my heart (it's just hard to keep up when your favorite character gets killed. Boo). Belle being my favorite princess (at least, Disney-wise) and having a soft spot for bad guys with complex histories/soft sides, Belle and Rumplestitskin are hands down my favorite characters, though I'm pretty fond of young Henry, and I like where they seem to be taking Aurora's character.

Just a note: I love hear from people! I'm very social and chatty by nature. Feel free to send a PM! =D

I would say, "Happy reading!" but that seems a little odd, considering I wouldn't recommend reading any of mine (who knows if they'll ever be finished?). Oh, hell, I'll say it anyway:

Happy reading! Just don't read mine. =D
