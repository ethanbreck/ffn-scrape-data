Hey all, I decided to update this because I hadn't for a while and a lot has changed since my senior year of high school. I really want to be a novelist or to work in book publishing in some way, so I guess this is like practice for me. I'm in my sophomore year of college and an RA in the residence hall that I live in. It's great, a lot of responsiblity but fun. I am an English major Creative Writing minor, obviously, and don't really have time for anything else. I love writing though, it's the one passion that I've had ever since I was in the third grade and that really stuck with me. But breaking into it professionaly is really ambitious so I guess we'll just have to wait and see what happens in that situation. My tastes in a lot of things have changed and writing new things is definitely broading my horizons and opening my eyes to new things, so it looks like I'm finally growing up, oh no...
