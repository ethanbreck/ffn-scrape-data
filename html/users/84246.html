Author has written 15 stories for Harry Potter, Misc. Plays/Musicals, Les Miserables, and Pitch Perfect.

In 2001-2002, I wrote one-shots and so-called poems for Cats and Les Miserables. More than a decade ago, I already had this thing for ensemble musicals.

In 2005, I began a still-unfinished story exploring the relationship between Legolas and Eowyn in The Lord of the Rings. The first two chapters are published here on FF.net under the name irishbrogue. In that universe, they're still my non-canonical OTP. I still have my argument on why they can form a deep, powerful, and pivotal bond, albeit not a romantic one. I also still might continue that story, one of these days, if the upcoming Hobbit movies rekindle my interest in it.

It's 2013. I thought I had long retired from the crazy, stress-relieving world of fanfiction. Thanks to Pitch Perfect--an ensemble musical in which any two characters look like they can at any moment form a deep, powerful, and pivotal bond (which is just as likely to be a romantic one)--I guess I was wrong.
