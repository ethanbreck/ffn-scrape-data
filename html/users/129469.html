THE GAME

How you play: You forget about it. You forget about it for as long as you can. As long as you have forgotten about it, you are winning. If you at any point in time are reminded of The Game for any reason, you must say aloud, or type out as the case may be, "I lost The Game." Now, if you are around someone else who already plays, they too will lose with you. If someone who does not play asks you about The Game, you must tell them about it. They are now thinking of The Game, and as a player, they must say that they have lost. The Game then restarts once you forget about it again and continues until you remember again, thus restarting the cycle. It ends when you die. Good luck~_~
