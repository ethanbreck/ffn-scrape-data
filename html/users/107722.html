Summery of myself: I am currently twenty-three years old, and I work at a local WalMart. I am currently really into mostly Prince of Tennis(PoT to those who know the fandom) and Air Gear. I write mostly original fiction. In the sci-fi/fantasy genre. Rarely do I delve into the cesspit that is fanfiction. That's mostly because I fear barging into someone's territory, and the fact that there is always someone more knowledgeable about the fandom than myself.

I will refrain from putting up pairings that I love to read. Having stated that, I will say that I prefer to read yaoi, slash, and shounen-ai, but I don't look away, though, from nicely written yuri/shoujou-ai and het. I do like to read pretty much anything good, as long as it isn’t too unbelievable.
