Author has written 32 stories for Friends, Tribe, Gilmore Girls, Firefly, Wonderfalls, Stargate: Atlantis, Beverly Hills 90210, Private Practice, and Castle.

Previously known as GMB loves Angel (yes, I was a teen once, try not to judge), but now more commonly known as Misskitten (it was sadly taken when I tried changing it, so this was the closest I could get to it).

Stories prior to 2008 use hyphens instead of quotation marks, but all new stories have been written with quotation marks. Yes, I converted, I sold out *hangs head in shame*

I'm a 29 year old Norwegian girl, but no worries; I've been learning English since the tender age of nine, so I master it well (well I certainly think so, lol). I'm a shipper at heart, so most of my stories are centered around pairings. I love a lot of pairings, but while I write several, some I will probably just stick to reading.

Doctor Who - Doctor/River
Firefly - Simon/Kaylee
Castle - Castle/Beckett
Bones - Booth/Brennan
Gilmore Girls - Luke/Lorelai
Private Practice - Violet/Pete
Beverly Hills 90210 - Brandon/Andrea
to name a few...

I can also be found here:
FireflyFans.net (Firefly fics only)
FanNation (my own fanfic archive)
YouTube (yes, I'm a vidder, too)
Dream Universe (my personal website)
Tumblr (warning, primarily Doctor Who oriented)

I live for reviews, the longer and more detailed the better (doesn't matter if it's praise or critisism - but flames are ignored), I'm not a fan of empty one-liners (at least give me an opinion... put some thought into it). I like replying to reviews whenever possible, so please be signed in and have PM's turned on :)

Story status
I have not written for Gilmore Girls or Private Practice for a good while, and I can't promise I will write more (will never say never, but it's unlikely). I do wish to one day complete Comfort Food, but I am not going to make any promises I can't keep. Fandoms I am more likely to post stories for at this moment are either Doctor Who, Castle or Firefly.

To Daria (wish you had an account so that I could tell you this directly instead of hoping you will see this on my profile): Yes, I do read every piece of feedback I get, regardless how old the story is, and I thank you for taking the time to give me such a long and honest review. I agree with you on every bit of critisism you had for me, and I think if I ever do manage to re-do/continue CF, I will absolutely be more selective in what ways I tell the story, like bring more focus to the important parts, cut away some of the filler material. I was going through a phase of wanting to do the ripple effect, meaning the changes would be small and easy to miss in the beginning and get larger over time. I'm very much over that particular phase now, but the story is still my precious baby that I hope to one day see through :)
