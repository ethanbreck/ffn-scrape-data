Accusations: STATUS-COMPLETED, another Jr High fic. This was actually my first fan fic that I wrote on the way to and from school. A total of about 45 minutes. It's poorly written and has tons of grammar mistakes that I will fix someday but it's what started me. Loser of all: STATUS-COMPLETED. Oh man do I have to do some major editing on this one. I didn't even notice until just now how it is all jumbled into about two major paragraphs. I will take care of that. Um another Jr High fic. Wasnt even really planning on writting a fic for this one. That little chant at the end was what started it all. I came up with that then I decided to write a fic around it. Ick. Moving on: STATUS-COMPLETED. Ah I sense an improvement in my writing. This one was my first one in High School. You remember what I did with Tears Gone Dry? Well this one was my first assignment of my freshman English I year. I pulled the same stunt as I did before. With all intentions of this being a fanfic. And I even got an A on it.:D Losing myself: STATUS-HIATUS. I began this one towards the beginning of my freshman year and was doing good on writing it but then I just stopped. I have another chapter written I just have to decide if I want to type it up and continue the story. No. I will finish the story. Even if its for closure. I just don't feel the urge for it to draw me back to this one anymore. Hopefully that will change. Please let me know if you do want me to continue it. Evolutions of the heart: STATUS-WRITING. This story scares me. I've planned this story. I've never planned. I always went with the flow. I know exactly how many chapters it will have and I know what each chapter will have in it. This is my current baby. I began it a little over a year ago and is one of the few stories I've posted I look forward to working on. I know the updates come slowly but I write when I'm the most relaxed. Hopefully I will have the next chapter up soon. Confusion(working title): STATUS-NON POSTED/WRITING. Now I am finishing it if it kills me. Thank you for your time and I hope you keep reading my stories and watching me improve.
