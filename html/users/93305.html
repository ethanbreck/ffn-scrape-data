Status: Under a new name.

That's right folks, I now write under a new alias - DJ Quicksilver! I now write and post stories on this new account rather than my older one because, to be quite honest, my writing style has changes so much over time that whenever I revisit the stories on this old account I kinda cringe a bit at how... 'bland', I guess, the stories here are. However, the stories on these old accounts DO represent my first forays into the world of fanfiction, and thus, I leave them here for historical purposes, both for my own benefit, as well as for those who want to better see just how I've evolved from where I began to where I am now as a writer. (Also, since this account has all my favorite stories in the Fav Stories tab and I REALLY don't wanna move all those to the new account, well...)

That's not to say that you might not see some of the concepts in these stories used ever again. Quite the opposite, I actually hope to reboot some of these works, under heavy modifications to suit my new style of writing.

For those of you who wish to read my new stories, you can find them here: https://www.fanfiction.net/u/1685106/DJ-Quicksilver

Know that updates are sporadic at best with my life being as hectic as it is. Keep an eye on my profile page for updates on whatever story/stories I might be working on at the time. Thank you for your interest
