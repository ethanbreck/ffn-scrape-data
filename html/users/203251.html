I've decided to begin a formal review list of stories I have read on FF.net. The review list begins with "Clash" and will be updated with my newest reviews at the top (newest to oldest). The grading system is as follows:

1. 1 point for spelling.
2. 1 point for overall correctness (times, places, events, etc.).
3. 2 points for effort.
4. 2 points for staying in character (doesn't necessarily apply to AUs).
5. 4 points for entertainment (slight chance of 5 for a possible totoal of 11).

Other characters make appearances but so far the 2 main couplings are Yamachi and Takouji. I'll update once the story is finished.
