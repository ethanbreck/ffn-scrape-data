Author has written 3 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Ranma.

Hi, I'm Rob, but I go by Ricnan Penderyn on here.

I've been a struggling writer of both fanfiction and original fiction since high school, but after being in a creative rut for the last few years I'm finally starting to feel like I can write again.

Besides my interest in writing, I enjoy listening to music, reading novels and graphic novels, playing board games, tabletop roleplaying and video games. I watch a variety of different anime and live action shows, and like going to the cinema with friends. I also occasionally dabble with tarot cards as a means of inspiration for roleplaying characters, and for writing projects.

As far as fanfiction goes, I've mostly been reading Evangelion or She-Ra (2018) fanfiction recently. I'm going to be getting back into writing fanfiction soon, as I already have a She-Ra story in development. I've also got a Doctor Who/Evangelion crossover fic that's been stuck in development hell for a while, but I'm hoping to get that to a place where I'm ready to publish sometime in the foreseeable future.

These days, I'm trying to get in the habit of outlining stories before I write them, so hopefully that will help me actually finish longer projects, since I have a plan for what's going to happen by the time I actually write the stories themselves.

Current Fandoms: Elder Scrolls, Elite Dangerous, The Expanse (books and TV), Fate (Type Moon), Marvel (MCU and Netflix), Mobile Suit Gundam, My Hero Academia, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Persona 5: The Animation, She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, Stranger Things, Travelers, and Voltron: Legendary Defender.

Works in Progress

Fractured Soul (She-Ra and the Princesses of Power)

Although Etheria has returned to normal, Catra came back wrong after the portal was sealed, and now there are two versions of her running around. One is the Catra that broke the world out of spite, still driven by ambition and hatred. The other is the part that was closed off after Adora defected from the horde: the Catra who still cares for those around her. Now, the 'good' version of Catra goes on a quest to right her wrongs before her existence fades, while her darker counterpart works to secure her place in the Horde and restore their strength following their recent defeat.

Progress: Working on outline, but already completed first drafts for two opening chapters.

Neon Genesis Whovangelion (working title, crossover: Doctor Who, post-Angels Take Manhattan / Evangelion, post EoE)

After the end of life as they knew it, Shinji Ikari and Asuka Langley Soryu encounter the mysterious Doctor as he crashes into their lives from a parallel universe. Though reluctant at first, the Doctor is persuaded to bring the two troubled teens aboard the TARDIS on his return to his own universe, rather than leave them to long and lonely lives on their desolate homeworld. However, the Doctor soon discovers that his adoption of the two former Eva pilots has unforeseen consequences...

Progress: First 'episode' completed, but it's way too long and needs revision.

Projects Currently On Ice

Dragonslayers of Sunnydale (Buffy, post-S5)

Buffy Summers is dead, leaving the Scooby Gang to defend Sunnydale in her stead. But when the town is terrorised by a dragon that came through Glory's portal, they soon find themselves in way over their heads.

(Note: I may revisit this eventually. It was the first fanfic I tried to write so I would like to get some closure on it, and I love the idea of the Scoobies trying to take on something as powerful as a dragon without a Slayer on their side. I just need to sit down one of these days and start re-outlining it from scratch.)
