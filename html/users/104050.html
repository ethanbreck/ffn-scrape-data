Author has written 13 stories for Chrono Trigger, Final Fantasy VII, Slayers, Final Fantasy I-VI, and Kingdom Hearts.

Let's stick to the basics, shall we?

I'm Shaded Mazoku. Sha for short. I write. I write a lot, in fact, though a lot of it never sees the light of the internet. It's probably for the best.

I write mainly yaoi, with forays into gen fic on occasions. I can and do occasionally write het, but I generally don't care much for female characters. Not because they're female, but because so many female characters are written as simpering and helpless. Certain series have changed my mind on this, mainly Bleach, so maybe I'll write het eventually. Or just go straight to the yuri.

Mostly, I write FFVI. Actually, mostly I write Kefka, with a little bit of Terra and Leo added in. I'm working on broadening my horizons a little. I also write quite a bit of Chrono Trigger, and I play with Slayers, Versus, Batman, Final Fantasy VII and IX, Bleach and a number of other fandoms. I sadly have the attention span of an ADD patient on a sugar kick (which is incidentally what I am), so I have a habit of starting fics and then going "...Oh, bugger this."

A lot of my writings are sadly not FF.net appropriate. They may be found on my web site, or, since I'm too lazy to update said page open, at my writing LJ at Madness Infused or the IJ mirror of the same name. The IJ is a WIP.

I have accounts in quite a few other places, but many of those are abandoned and the fics they hold are no longer updated, unlike this one.

I happily accept constructive criticism, and suggestions. Flames... ...Well, flames get printed out, laughed at and burned in the fireplace during a heathen ceremony. If you are going to flame me, at least make an effort to use proper English? It's not my first language, you see, and it would be a shame if you worked hard on a flame and I couldn't understand it.

Updated 25th of February 2009: FFVI-fic spam, go! Uploaded three stories formerly only on LJ/IJ. Wasn't sure if I should add Beneath It, but it's one of my favourites, so up it goes.
