First, let me say I love Video Game fanfic. I have been writing it ever since I was a kid (But none of early work is good enough for the web). Parodies are my best catigory to write about. Probably what drew me to fan fiction was this picture I saw when I was a kid that turned my idea of video games forever (i'm not going to say what the picture was, but it involved two major video game franchises). From then on I came up with some freaky ideas for what could happen in a video game that got down right weird and sadistic.

Right now I've launched some mellow fan fic, but some more bizzar one's are coming soon...
