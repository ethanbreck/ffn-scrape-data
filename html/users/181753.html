Hi Everyone,

If you are reading this, thank you for taking time to get to know me and my stories. I have decided to change my name as you can see to FallenTears84.

About me personally, I am a full time student and employee so I often have alot on my plate but I love to write and I wanted to put my story out there and see how it goes.

I love Dragonball Z and love all of the main characters: (Goku, Vegeta, Gohan, Chi-Chi, Bulma, Videl, Trunks (future and present) and Goten). Any and all stories I write about this anime will be based on these characters.

Most of my stories will be A/U because I love the idea of expanding an idea and running with it.

I accept good reviews, bad reviews but please not hard-core flames because I want to improve. I take all reviews and use it to improve myself so please let me know how you feel about my stories. I appreciate your feedback.

I will be changing this profile as I go forward so look out for updates.

Sincerely,

FallenTears84

Summary of Stories

Diamond in the Rough: A A/U story following the lives of our favorites saiyan's if Vegeta had taken over his father's planet. Includes Goku/Chi-Chi, Vegeta/Bulma Gohan/Videl. I love this story and I have a lot of plans for it. I hope other people can enjoy it as well and watch the direction it goes in.

Ego Trip: Ever since watching Vegeta let Cell absorb Android 18 I've questioned what would happen had he destroyed Cell. This story will focus on Vegeta and M.Trunks and will include some bonding on there part, (as only Vegeta can bond with someone). I have plans for this story and hope you all enjoy it.
