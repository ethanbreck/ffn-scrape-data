ME
:==:==:

Alright then, I'm nothing special, but apparently, I'm special enough to have an account on FF.Net and a little bio thingy ^_^ Right off the bat you can tell that I'm very easy to please.

Currently, I'm a fifteen year old girl, tenth-grader trying to live through highschool and cope with her wacky family with a Harry Potter obsession. That's right, my family has officially told me that I'm obsessed and that I should lay off. But I don't listen to them, because there's just so much to look forward to in the HP world that I can't help but be excited 24/7. My favourite ships are H/Hr and L/J...though I admit that I have a small spot for D/Hr (due mainly to CassandraClaire's Draco Trilogy). I sail, known as happy, on the HMS Pumpkin Pie and the SS Thorn and Buck on FictionAlley.

But don't think that this chick is all HP, because I have other obsessions *evil grin* I love The Simpsons and can practically find quotes from the show that is fitting for any situation in life. My favourite characters are Ralph (for he has the best sayings in the universe) and Homer, because he's just plain stupid it cracks me up. I also love punk music! blink-182, Green Day, Sum 41, No Doubt, Good Charlotte, Simple Plan, Coldplay...are just a few of the bands that rock my radiowaves.

I'm really into music and acting. I can currently play (not exceptionally well though) the piano, the saxophone and the guitar. I really want to learn the bass, because it just seems cool.

Welps, that's about all about me. But you know, if you're still interested in li'l ol' me, just drop me a line and I'll get back to ya!

100% pinay-canadian ;)

THE END
