If you wanna know anything bout me read on...if not don't read on but read my fics if you're interested or read more if you've read one already.

Well I'm from the UK. I have been writing fanfiction for over a decade now. It started with Dragon Ball Z, but progressed onto Beyblade, then Escaflowne and now Final Fantasy too. I had a writer's block for years as my health took a nosedive after the birth of my daughter. But in recent years I have got better and continue to keep well, so I started writing again. I have quite a few stories that need finishing and I will be working on them when I get time. So, watch this space! Diesty-chan is making a come back!

I mainly write Beyblade and Escaflowne stories and have recently got into writing Final Fantasy fics too, so far in that category its X and X-2, but I have written an VIII and I have a Cloud and Tifa fic forming in my head! With Beyblade its mainly Kai/Rei fics but I occasionally write Yuuri/Boris Beyblade fics mainly for Ladyfiction and you can always find the pair in most of my Kai/Rei fics causing mischief for Rei and Kai. Hehe! I'm a huge shipper for that pairing. And yes I'm a shounen-ai fan! Damn proud too! Escaflowne characters I love together (now please don't beat me with sticks) is Hitomi/Dilandau, Hitomi/Van and Folken/Sora from the movie! I love Sora, she is so amazingly awesome! As for Final Fantasy its gotta be Yuna/Tidus, Squall/Rinoa, Cloud/Tifa, Zack/ Aerith and Serah/Noel (again, please don't beat with sticks).

I don't like TyKa fics! I just find Takao really annoying and I love making Kai have constant digs at him and stuff. And Re/Ma...what are you people thinking! YOU are breaking all the rules writing him with her! I love Mao bashing! And above all that I hate Mary-Sue's! They need to be shot! I will not and definitely refuse to read them! Boy-boy love is the only way! And that's that for dislikes in Beyblade! I don't really have any dislikes with Escaflowne or Final Fantasy except Hitomi shouldn't be with Allen or Armano. And that's all for all my dislikes.

I use to draw mainly Beyblade and some Escaflowne plus my own character Diesty-chan and some random characters. I'm drawing again as I have joined an art group and learning more art techniques. This a link to my work on deviantart, if you wanna chack it out *Shameless Plug

I'm a major fan of Doctor Who, seriously crazy on it/him - when David Tennant was the Doctor. So I'm partial to reading them aswell though I doubt I'll ever write for that category.

A big THANK YOU to LADYFICTION, my wonderul Imotou-san! She has listened to me whine and inspired me, made me laugh, act crazy all through the years I have known her, which is now nearly reaching a decade! 0.o

And just Thank You to anyone who has ever read my fics and left reviews...very much appreciate it. Free cookies to all!

I have also joined fictionpress now so check out my non-fanfiction fics there.

I have also started to write the next installment of Gay Bar if anyone can remember that. It's in it's first stage and hopefully I'll shall be posting it this year.

And that wraps it up for now.
