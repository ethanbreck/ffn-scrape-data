Author has written 11 stories for Ghost Busters (Real/Extreme), Evil Dead/Army of Darkness, TV X-overs, Sailor Moon, and Back to the Future.
Well, it has been a while since I updated this crappy profile of mine. Time for change, do ya think?

Anyhow, i can see that most of you are wondering, "Who is this nut?" Well, I'm overall a Ghostbusters fan, as seen from my collection of fiction here, but i have started a new fiction series, called "TimeWise."

I have might as well explain my treasure trove of stories in some detail so you can actually READ IT! I'll start with the stories which are all associated within time frame, onto totally alternate reality.

Welcome Aboard: Starting in June of 1995, the Ghostbusters run short on hands, so it was about time they hired a new man. Dan Shannon (your truely ;) ) is hired and goes through training for Ghostbusting.

A Trainee's Run: On a training bust, Dan has tagged along of a call with the Ghostbusters, but is turned down to assist with the capturing of the ghost. When the very ghost that the others tried to bust makes them disappear, Dan is the only one left to stop it, but he needs help from a mysteryous girl, which has to do with not just the guys' disappearence, but other disappearences as well.

Getting The Idea: When Dan and Ray start a conversation on other methods of transport from a Proton Pack, Egon takes the idea further, and creates a full prototype jet propelled partical thrower.

Dark Side of the Moon: A Real Ghostbusters/Sailor Moon crossover. Back in 1992, Sailor Moon, Sailor Mercury, and Sailor Mars defeated the Negaverse General Jedite under Queen Bryal. After failing to kill the Sailor Scouts at the time, Bryal sentenced Jedite to Eternal Sleep. 3 years later, Ghostbuster Dan Shannon starts having strange dreams about a strange girl, as does Lita, aka Sailor Jupiter, about a strange man. After the Ghostbusters are called into Japan to investigate disappearences in the park, Dan and Lita make contact, and grow for one another. After so, the ghost of Jedite returns, all hell breaks loose. Can the Ghostbusters and the Sailor Scouts find common ground, and fight Jedite together? what did Dan and Lita's dreams mean? And who is this mysteryous Jupiter Knight? Read on!

Of the Evil: Evil Dead/Sailor Moon crossover. *Note* Follows "Dark Side" but does not include the Ghostbusters. After the encounter with the ghost of Jedite, the Scouts need quiet time away from the duties as Sailor Scouts, and go to a remote cabin the woods of Tennessee, in the US. But when Raye aka Sailor Mars finds the Necronomicon Ex Mortis in the surrounding woods, she reads the passages to the book and brings about the dead of the Evil kind. When all hope is to be lost, a certain man with a chainsaw, a shotgun and a serious attitude/insanity problem arrives, the fight finds its was into the Scouts favor, with a slight surprise at the end. *Note* Might not get finished for a while.

Highschool Screamheart: When Dan's old High School in his hometown of Tinley Park, Illinois comes under a ghost attack, the boys head out to the school, but when an old lost love of Dan's comes forth, it shakes things. When her and her boyfriend, who hates Dan woth a passion, are going to the Prom in the school the night the ghost is going to attack, the stress builds. Can Dan capture the ghost, confess his love for the girl, and stand up to her boyfriend in the same night without dying from either event?

Running Out of Time: Extreme Ghostbusters/Back to the Future crossover. When the DeLorean Time Machine was destroyed by the train in 1985, the very parts that made it were left nyglected on the tracks in Hill Valley. After discovery by the California State Police, the FBI come into play, and send the parts to Silicon Valley for reconstruction. 15 years later, at GBHQ, a large paranormal-affected expariment arrives. When Ghostbusters Dan Shannon and Eduardo Rivera go to test it, and figure out what is causing it to function unusually, but discover it is in truth the DeLorean Time Machine. When they cannot reset the time circuits to their own time, they must find Dr. Emmett Brown with the assistance of his friend Marty McFly and his girlfriend Jennifer Parker. But as all good BttF styled fics/plots go, it's just not complete without Biff stealing Doc's time train and kidnapping Eduardo, Marty and Jennifer. Now, Dan and Doc must fix the DeLorean, go though the hellish version of Hill Valley, stop young Biff from finding the lost Sports Almanac, retreve the stolen time train, get Eduardo, Marty and Jennifer, and get back to the future.

TimeWise: *Note* Not a part of my Ghostbusters series. This is a totally different story. When Dan Shannon, (Do you really have to guess?) a talented and eccentric Electronic Enginner, who owns his own company called Paratech Industries, creates a replica of the DeLorean Time Machine from Back to the Future. What he did was actually make into a real working Time Machine. Not just so, it also has the ability to travel to dimensions where TV shows and Movies exist on there own timestream. Both Dan and his friend, Ed Ulatoski, (You-La-Ta-Ow-Ski) venture into the worlds of imagination. Join the TimeWise Dimensional Assistance Unit as they stop Randal Paulson and Rob Drayman of Darkworld Technologies' YearGear Parallel Havoc Elite from destroying not only the worlds of movies and TV, but their own world as well!

Flightsuits Comin': When you have time, you just wonder what would happen if you put different words to a song. Obviously, I put Ghostbusters oriented words into a Men in Black song. Hope you like it.

*Whew!* That was a mighty task. Now, for coming attractions!

Screwed in Jersey: Extreme Ghostbusters/Clerks crossover. After a young girl is killed in Red Bank, New Jersey, a Ghostbusters business card is found on top of her body. During a Devils/Blackhawks hockey game, Ghostbuster Dan Shannon meets up with a convenence store clerk, Dante Hicks. On there way leaving the stadium, Dan is almost placed under arrest, when him and Dante escaped. When both of them discover that they both have been framed for the murder of Veronica Loughrun, Dante's ex-girlfriend, they must find a way to GBHQ with the help of Jay and Silent Bob, as well as other... Biblical... helpers to stop an unknown demon out to destroy the Ghostbusters, and all others who stand in its way.

Paranormal Evolution: Extreme Ghostbusters/Evolution crossover. It's Spring Break, 2002. Business is slow, and most of the residence of the firehouse have taken leave. Dr. Egon Spengler, Janine Melnitz and Slimer in Tucson, Arizona for a paranormal convention, Roland Jackson on a trip with his family to southern Florida, Garrett Miller playing the Wheelchair Basketball circuit in WInsconsin, and Kylie Griffen in California meeting up with old friends. Only ones occupying the HQ is Dan Shannon and Eduardo Rivera, holding down the fort while the others are off. It was also an off week for Glen Canyon, Arizona firefighter Wayne Gray, who decided to take a trip to New Yor City. Wyane meets up with both Dan and Eduardo, and decide to hang with them for the time being, when a chunk of the meteorite from last years "Alien Invasion" drops out of the sky, and into 5th ave., it doesnt take long for the Ghostbusters and Wayne to figure out what's to happen. The only things standing in their way is an old enemy of the Ghostbusters... Walter Peck.

Swap: After an accidental Proton Pack firing with two ghosts, Dan Shannon and Roland Jackson have found themselves as ghosts, and the ghosts that were involved with the accident... are now in their bodies! Can they switch back before the ghosts take vengence on the Ghostbusters without them seeing it coming?

So, that's all i have for now. Check back to see if i am really getting some progress down on these, and please remember...
