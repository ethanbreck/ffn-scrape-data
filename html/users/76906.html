Fri 02/09/2007

If you don't like it, don't read it. I'm not writing for you.

I have been writing fanfiction back in Ungraduate College, off and on, since 1992/1993, and making up stories about TV shows and books I have read for a much longer time. Starting first with Sci-Fi/Fantasy TV Shows and novels, weaving them into a collective whole (which is all worked under the heading of _Adventures of Zaxxon Hunter_) and rewriting scenes. Then, in 1994 or so, I was learned of the fascinating world of Anime and Manga. Favorite Series are, to name a few: Hokuto Shin Ken (Fist of the North Star), Bubblegum Crisis, Angelic Layer, Samurai Troopers: Legends of the Mystic Armor (Ronin Warriors), Magic Knight Rayearth, and several others. In the Summer of 2003, I have "fallen" into enjoying Harry Potter as well.

I have found I enjoy fanfics of Ranma 1/2 and Sailormoon more then canon, which is why neither is listed previously. I also enjoy thought out fanfics where relationships are shown and established/worked on, rather then just assumed. No, I do not see, canonly, Ranma+Akane, Harry+Ginny, or other just assumed relationships.

I enjoy:
Ranma+Nabiki, Ranma+Setsuna
Harry+Hermione, Harry+Tonks, Harry+Bella(trix)+Narcissa

My view of Ranma Saotome is that he strives to be a modern-day Samurai, who was raised by a Ronin father. I disagree with other fanfiction writers that protrait Ranma as nothing more then a dumb jock. I do see most of the Ranma 1/2 cast as submissive in nature, save Nodoka, Cologne, the late Mrs Tendo, and Ranma. Akane Tendo is, as far as I am concern, Rumiko Takahashi's SI avatar, and I will write her as such.

My view of Sailor Moon Universe is that it is misandric in nature, and my fanfictions reflect my view as such.

Hokuto No Ken is a classic martial arts manga/anime, and needs to watched/read in the original Japanese, not the dumb-down, beat-them-up version that Viz put out.

You are wasting both our times by sending out reviews that my portrayal of any character is Out Of Character. Every character is how I view them, or have been adjusted for the purpose of the fanfiction I am writing.

Don't just say there's grammar mistake(s) in my fanfiction, without quoting the mistake in question, and what the problem(s) is. Otherwise, you are just blowing smoke and wasting my time. Nor can I learn from such.

My website has all my fanfictions, definitions of words I use either in Japanese or in my fanfictions themselves.
