I am a female member of the species living a little South of the North Pole and much further North of the South Pole. I am old enough to know better but young enough to get away with it.

I got back onto FanFiction.net after a nice long break but I'm back now, dragging my slashy mind (and also a little of my dramatic mind)along with me. Stargate still dominates my work, though I have written a few poems that are up on FictionPress.net.

In life I wouldn't hurt a fly. They make a mess when you squash them and it's just horrible to clean it up. But seriously, I'm the nicest person you could meet. At least in life.

In the world of FanFiction I'm a complete and total sadist, especially for poor Daniel Jackson. He really deserves better, but it's just so hard to be nice to him. I try my best every so often, but then I give into temptation and give Danny a good old fashioned whumping. Always fun :)

In the last couple of days I've written a couple of drabbles and posted the first chapter of my new slash fic. If anyone reads and reviews it I may post more... it's not looking too good right now.

I've also started a new AU Stargate fic (my fist AU) that may be up here in a few days or a couple of weeks... really depends if I can get my arse in gear and get writing on it...

E-Mail me if you like. Always fun to hear from people I don't know. E-Mail me if you like a story of mine... review me too! Reviews make me smile and make me want to write more. I'll stop rambling on now and go do something destructive...
