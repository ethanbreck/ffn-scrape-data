UPCOMING

My Father's Son: (Catscratch/Earthworm Jim crossover) It's Mr. Blik's birthday, the day when he gets anything and everything he wants--but why isn't he excited about it? In a private conversation with Gordon, Blik admits that he has a feeling that something bad is going to happen on this day, and sure enough, it does. Discovering that their mother mated with a tom by the name of Evil the Cat, and that Blik was their offspring, Evil comes to claim his son and take him to Heck, teaching him the ways of the universe and how he is the token to destroying it once and for all. It's up to Waffle and Gordon, along with Jim and Peter, to travel across the galaxy and stop Evil before he carries out what could be his most disastrous plot of all time.

The Plague: (Krypto the Superdog) After being injured by a Kryptonite monster, Krypto awakens to find that the entire city of Metropolis is under some strange disease--a deadly disease that causes them to walk, even when dead. The only ones that seemingly haven't been affected are the pets, who have taken on some kind of immunity. Alone now in the midst of the crisis, Krypto and the others must bond together to save themselves--and the city--from this menacing impact.

Beyond Tambelon: (My Little Pony) Grogar has returned to Ponyland to claim it for his own--and even earlier than the Ponies expected. Discovering that Grogar has obtained a new source of power, the Ponies try and find a solution--only to end up being injured and taken prisoner. However, when Ribbon uncovers a secret from Grogar's past, she finds that he is not the one responsible for Ponyland's invasion this time. If they work together, could it be possible to send Tambelon back into the Realm of Darkness and put a stop to the doings of Rem, Grogar's evil father? Betrayal, war, and romance await the group as they struggle to undo what has already been done...and Rem is not going down without a good fight.

Lord of the Flyers: (Land Before Time) Shortly after his expulsion from the Great Valley, Pterano, now entirely alone, is attacked by a group of Flyers--and is saved by another. Finding himself in the midst of a tribal civilization of Flyers, he realizes that he is in a world that is everything he could have ever hoped for--and more. After becoming a member of the tribe and reconciling with Yaari, one of the warrior Flyers, Pterano discovers that the Flyer tribe has an enemy beyond their world--a pack of vicious creatures called the Night Flyers. Things go from bad to worse as Pterano discovers that Sierra, his former henchman, is one of them--and Sierra has not forgotten the events of the past. When Sierra makes one of the most threatening claims of all time, Pterano must abandon his former ego-centric life and fight...even if it ends up killing him.

Angel of Darkness: (Xiaolin Showdown) It's Jack Spicer's 13th birthday, at long last--but he is far from pleased, and for a good reason, too. Around this time, Master Fung also announces that the fifth elemental Xiaolin Dragon--the dragon of the Heavens and Earth--will reveal his-or-herself. Omi is very excited until a plague of darkness appears to spread across the land like wildfire--brought on by a being named Malus--who is more familiar than one would assume. As Jack slowly succumbs to the darkness, everyone around him is pulled into a plague of suffering, and the only hope left for the world is for Jack to look deep within himself...and his friends and foes alike.
