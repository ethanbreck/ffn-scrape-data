Myself: My name's Gillian, and I prefer to use that name rather than my penname. To me, bravo_6, is just a screen name. I'm 18 (19 in a few days), go to university, and just love reading and writing. I read all the time, and though I may not be able to write my own original stories (although that day may come!) I adore fan fiction. I also love to frequently visit Chapters, even if I don't buy books everytime, I just love it there. I love 'ships. I can't help it. And I 'ship Ron/Hermione to the death.

Fandoms: Mainly Harry Potter, but I will occasionally delve into the Star Trek, JAG, and Jane Austen fan fic areas. I've only ever written Harry Potter fics but I hope to write some Inheritance Trilogy fics, especially with the release of Eldest, the second book.

Favourite books: Eragon and Eldest (Inheritance Trilogy)-Christopher Paolini, Pride and Prejudice-Jane Austen, Harry Potter-J.K. Rowling, Random Passage/Waiting for Time-Bernice Morgan, Jane Eyre-Charlotte Bronte.

In the Works:
2 new fics, both post HBP. The first is untiled at the moment and is being beta'd. It's R/Hr, taking place at Fleur and Bill's wedding. The other is chaptered and is in Hermione's POV, as if Ginny had never got angry at Ron making him run to Lavender. That one is still a WIP.
