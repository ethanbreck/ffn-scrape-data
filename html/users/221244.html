About me

What to write? Hmm... I'm a girl from Canada who loves writing, drawing, singing, cosplaying and cats.

I used not to write a lot, because I usually have trouble finding ideas that I find are worth putting down on paper (or the keyboard, whatever), so I hadn't written anything in over five years until a certain fandom brought me all this inspiration. Thank you, Phoenix Wright :) It also made me realize that what caused my author's block was that I was trying too hard to be serious. I seriously fail at it XD So now I bring you my silly humor and hope it can create some smiles :)

I don't read as many fics as I used to cause I don't have a lot of free time, but when I do, I like to leave reviews and feedback. I think it helps people get better and motivates them to write more.
