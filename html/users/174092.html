¡Hola, amigas y amigos!

Every once in a while I feel the urge to redo this little profile, then I get depressed because I can't think of anything interesting to say. I'm a medical student, a writer, a reader, a smartass, a Wikipedia addict, quiet when you meet me, loud when you get to know me, an involuntary insomniac, and probably far too immature for my own good.

The Fandoms: Sherlock Holmes, Laurie R. King, LoTR, HP, Star Wars, Star Trek, Cowboy Bebop, all things Joss Whedon, Mercedes Lackey, Terry Pratchett, numerous other authors who may be found at your local library and sci-fi and fantasy in general.

If you like the stuff, I’ve got down here, I’ve also got some stuff over on fictionpress.net. (hint,hint) feel free to go take a look.

Update!

I think it's pretty obvious that I don't update over here anymore. Sorry if you were hoping for a sequel for anything I've already written, but it probably won't happen in the forseeable future. I'm glad so many people have enjoyed what I already wrote.

Please review! This account represents about 10 years of my writing output, and I often come back to see what I did right and what I did wrong. I can see you guys out there, with this crazy shiny hits tracker. I do want to hear your feedback, even if it's negative, as long as it's constructive.

Thanks and love,

Kerowyn
