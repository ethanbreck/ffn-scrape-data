Name: Denise =)
Age: Old enough. :P (I feel ancient now.)

So... what about me? I've been writing fanfiction for ten (!) years now, and I've been a member of fanfiction.net for six of those. I began writing fics for soap operas but have since expanded to write for many things! (I used to have a lot in Misc, until FFN took down the Sports and Music Groups section (:). So now... I've got five in TV Shows: two in Passions... a soap opera, naturally... two others in General Hospital... yes, another soap opera =D, and one in Heroes; four Digimon ones, three Harry Potter fics, and one Baby-Sitters Club one.)

A summary of my stories can be found here, which often elaborate from the summary posted with it on the listing. I appreciate reviews if you enjoy my work; they help me become a better writer, so please leave one, even if you find something I can work on! I thank you for taking the time to read my stories. Honestly, I write for other people just as much as I write for myself. :)

My fandoms, pairings, and stories (completed, unless noted) include:
