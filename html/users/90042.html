Author has written 17 stories for Punisher, Equalizer, Luke Cage, Black Lagoon, Person of Interest, Jack Reacher series, Deathstroke the Terminator, and StarTrek: Deep Space Nine.

For anyone who cares, here are a few things about the man known as Blakkstone.

English is my second language. French is my first. I'm in my 40s.

I like all forms of storytelling (TV series, cartoons, movies, novels and comic books) and my "writing" is inspired by all of them.

Some notes about the stories:

-I wrote a LUKE CAGE ON DEEP SPACE NINE fanfic. Most of my stories feature the Punisher, this one is very different in tone and style. I enjoy STAR TREK movies and shows, my favorite being the Dominion War era of STAR TREK: DEEP SPACE NINE. I am a fan of super-heroes as well and have been for most of my life.

-THE PUNISHER: DEEP WATERS, THE PUNISHER: HOME FOR CHRISTMAS and THE PUNISHER: A CROSS TO BEAR are part of my take on a MAX-like universe, where there are no superheroes and no super-tech.

-HUNTERS AND KILLERS, ATTACK OF THE VAMPIRE NINJA OF THE YAKUZA and FULL CIRCLE form THE GANG WAR TRILOGY.

-THE EQUALIZER MEETS THE PUNISHER (this is based on the 80s TV show NOT the 2014 DENZEL movie), PUNISHER OF INTEREST and FOR DAMN SURE are a trilogy of loosely connected Punisher crossover stories in which Frank Castle runs across characters from TV shows and crime novels.

-DEATHSTROKE and THE PUNISHER: AMBUSH. A CROSSOVER WITH NO CONNECTION TO ANY OF MY OTHER STORIES.

WARNING: THERE ARE SPOILERS IN THE REVIEWS. REVIEWERS: PLEASE, TRY NOT TO SPOIL MY STORIES. THANK YOU.

THANK YOU TO ALL THAT ''FAVORITE'' MY STORIES! IT'S APPRECIATED!

That's it. Read and hopefully enjoy my stories. Whether you do enjoy them or not, reviews are appreciated.

PS: I have a fictionpress account as well. If you like my PUNISHER stories, check out my ''original character'', Luther Jones, THE SENTENCER. Yes, it's inspired by The Punisher, but different. There is also Charles Rochenoire and his friends from the GUARD, private military contractors.

http://www.fictionpress.com/u/90042/BLAKKSTONE

PPS: Some people may disagree with the portrayal of certain characters. Remember one thing: it's FAN FICTION. It used to bother me as well the way certain writers wrote some characters. I realized that it didn't matter at all. I stopped caring. If there is nothing wrong with the story itself, in my view, I don't care if events or people are out of continuity or out of character. I write the stories I choose to write HOW I choose to write them. You should too, in «canon» or not.

PPPS.: Special thanks to rdc88 for his proofreading and feedback!

Check out his action packed stories here:

http://www.fictionpress.com/u/772272/rdc88

Take care!
