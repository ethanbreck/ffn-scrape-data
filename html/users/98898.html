Update 5 August 2006

Why do I only seem to update this thing on a yearly basis? -_- Well, anyway, I thought I might as well change the notice here, since the news from 11 months ago is a bit old.

Really old stuff (ie. Digimon fic) is deleted from here, if you still really want to see it, you can find it over at mediaminer.org under the penname Sora Ishida (which would be name here too, but the rules changed and I lost it a few years ago -_-).

Frankly, I don't update or keep much here because I don't like the interface, I don't like not having my usual penname, and I don't like the content restrictions. I also haven't written a whole lot lately, though the past few months have seen me get eaten by a project that is probably far too big and ambitious for my slackness and meagre talents. But I'm going to at least take a crack at it...and I managed to get some of my friends hooked on it enough to prod me, so here's hoping!

Currently writing for: Kyou Kara Maou fandom

ALL my KKM fics (currently 10 works) are archived here. Check it out, it's a very nice archive!

Most of my creative talent these days goes into the interactive fanfiction that is Roleplaying...if you're into crack and fancy some interesting reading, head on over to OMG Maou () and check out the community friends page for some good not-so-wholesome KKM fun. I play IC Murata and OOC Julia there (though poor Julia needs some work, whoops...)

I'm not easily squicked, most pairings will entertain me as long as they're written well. You won't find me flaming people for liking certain pairings, or character bashing in my fics. I'm only mean to the characters I like, anyway...because angst is a beautiful thing!

~ Sora Ishida
