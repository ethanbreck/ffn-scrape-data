Hi there. My name is Ovo, which you no doubt know since you're sitting here on my profile, reading these words. I've been reading and publishing works here for over a decade.

If you're wondering when and whether I'll update a particular fic, I can't tell you. I try to keep up, but the muse can be a fickle mistress. Story alerts are your best bet here: I do try to finish what I've started.

If you've left a review and are wondering why I never got back to you, I apologize. I do try to respond to direct questions, but I can be very shy when I don't know what to say, and don't always know what to say. This isn't to say I don't appreciate your feedback, however: I keep every review I get and appreciate it very much. Thank you.

If you're here because you enjoyed my work and are looking for more, it may interest you to know that I also have an AO3 account: FireEye. I have a lot unpublished here, and am trying to upload it all in one place.
