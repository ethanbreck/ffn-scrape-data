My name is DarkCitadel I’ve been writing Fanfiction off and on since 1997. I decided to create an account here as a backup for my regular website. I’ve had an account at fanfiction.net for quite sometime, but never bothered to upload anything until now.

I have found that with the popularity of Fanfiction.net my main referring source of hits for my web page (Anipike.com) has completely dried up.

It is hoped that by placing some of the stories we have written (I have a coauthor, Daemon Ross for some of the stories) that I can broaden the audience for our works and hopefully get some good feed back.

My account here for the time being, will hold only the most recently corrected stories from our website.

A lot of our old stuff needs a lot of work, until I can manage some time to rewrite these old stories I will not posted them here. I do not wish inflict the horror that is the giant unformatted blocks of text, bad grammar, and spell, that marked our beginnings into fanfiction.

This place is already too crowed with such crap, no need to add more. However, if you are masochistically inclined feel free to read these old stories on my website.

About the website, any updates I make will be there first. Eventually they will make their way here.

Anyway Enjoy
