Hmm, what to say, what to say. . . well, let’s start at the beginning. I’m 18 years old, and I’m a first year undergraduate at Simon Fraser University, near Vancouver. Yep, you guessed it, that means I’m Canadian. And yes, that means I’m one of those people on the other side of the border, up in “snowy Canada”. . . not that it ever even snows up here, it didn’t even snow for Christmas. But hey, Canada is pretty cool. . . free healthcare!! And on top of that, we can download music for free. . . AND ITS COMPLETELY LEGAL!!!

Anyways, enough about Canada and me. Let's talk about the greatest show ever to air on network television - Dark Angel. You know what I miss most about that show? Alec. Alec with his hotness. Not to mention his loving, caring nature. . however deep down and repressed it may be.

Make sure toscroll down and read my fic ‘A Bad Day’. I wrote it like 3 years ago, so I would have been 14 or 15 at the time. If you’re one of those people that was waiting for me to update the story, well you are in luck! Currently, I am revising and rewriting the story, because like most people, I’m never happy with what I write. As soon as I’m finished revising, I’ll add another chapter, and continue adding chapters until the story is complete.

By the way, you may all be wondering why my fic is M/L,but I can't seem to stop talking about Alec. Well, it's all really quite simple. I began writing that fic in the first season, and at that time, there was no one else to use as a boytoy, before Alec had graced us with his presence. But now, I have seen the light, and that Max and Alec are destined to live happily ever after.

And thanks to all the reviewers out there, you really made all of this possible. Without the reviews, I probably would have abandoned the story!

Make sure you type in proper English, for we have grammar Nazis in our midst.

I am one of them.

~ Jessica
