(10/4/05)

I apologize for the screeching halt. I'm trying hard to apply myself to actual, publishable (s'that a word?o_O) fiction, and for that, sadly, my fanfiction pays the price. I've had a few fleeting fanfic ideas, but I imagine there's going to be a large slump before all's said and done. It may be some time, but I'm pushing myself to straighten out three or four original fictions that have real potential. My mom has a publishing agent, and she's encouraging me to take a stab at it.

I'm mulling over a few light fanfics in the meantime, one-shots most likely. I don't know why I kill myself over multi-chapter ones. I've always been happier writing shorter stuff.

It's tough work, but exciting. I hope someday something comes of this, and that the people out there who read my fanfiction can read my original fiction, too.
