Author has written 30 stories for Slam Dunk, Prince of Tennis, and Naruto.

Having been on hiatus for three years, I sincerely apologise to those who had left reviews for the stories, but I had never got the chance to revert personally. I'm truly sorry and I certainly feel horrible for not being able to do that. Life caught up with me big time and in view of the things that I needed to deal with in real life, I hardly had time for FFnet.

But I still love writing, and I'm back here, hopefully for a while, to finish posting some of those stories I started on so long ago, but never got around to posting or completing it. Do rest assured though, that once I start on a story, I will make it a point to see it through completion.

I won't list out what I have in plans, lest I disappoint. But right now in the pipelines would be the sequel for Second Chance. And I might just continue the one I had started some two to three years ago for Drifting Affections. Although it had been so long since those requests were made for a sequel, but I think I still do need to honour those wonderful reviews, encouragements and certainly my promises to have one. I'm planning another sequel of sorts for one of the other stories too. A couple more new stories are in the works, though I can't say when I will have them up, if ever.

I still write yaoi mostly, and the occasional friendship fic. And mainly on my favourite couple, KakaIru. While I do love other pairings in the Naruto universe, I haven't really the time to explore writing about them. Anyway, if you aren't into BL or man x man love, then my fics probably aren't what you are looking for.

Once again, thank you to everyone who reviewed the last three years. I've read all the reviews, and you have my heartfelt thanks for all the encouragements you have given. If ever a chance our paths cross, I would like to thank you personally.

To everyone waiting for a new chapter to Finding the Right Way, I apologise for taking so long. My laptop crashed, my notes for the next chapter gone. I've just gotten my repaired laptop back and putting all my notes (or my memories of them) back together. It may take a while, but I promise to have it up soon. I'm so sorry!!
