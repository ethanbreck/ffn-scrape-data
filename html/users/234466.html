Talemio
If you've read Lost In Dreams, you'll know my name. I thought of it one day while trying to sleep. I've become fond of the name. The name represents many different characters, and not just the one who most of you know.

Me
I'm was born in Illinois and am currently living in Texas. I love writing, especially in journals. I started fanfiction in early 2003 to exercise that fun little creative bug with the aim that, once I improved, I would begin an original story. Honestly, I was absolutely horrible in the beginning. After a bunch of school journals and writings, essays, projects, and blog entries, I got a general idea of how to write the way I like it--and, I loved it.

Etc.
I design layouts for websites, mostly journals. I used to have pre-made templates up on AET (azn elf templates), but some idiot hacked onto the site and deleted every file. Now, I'm working on something new--Iridescent Presentations. That's all I'm saying about that. ;-) I love fantasy. Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Dragonlance...all of it. I'm your typical arts/literature nerd. My most favorite class in high school was humanities. I've read The Lord of the Rings, The Hobbit, The Silmarillion, The Lost Tales (I and II), and the Unfinished Tales. Yes, I'm all for canon. I'm a die-hard Tolkien fan. Bite me.
I'm the Headmistress of Plano Hogwarts ( an online community for Harry Potter fans who would like to experience what Hogwarts might be like. You will find an RP club, the DA, classes, Quidditch, contests, discussion of the movies and the books, and many other things for Harry Potter fans everywhere. As of November 2005, Plano Hogwarts has over 800 members. If you're a fan of Harry Potter, you're more than welcome to join. =)

Updates - May 12, 2006
Everything Is So Blurry: This story will not be continued/finished. I might have said that I would continue it, but after rereading this story many times, I've decided that it's impossible for me to write a story like this. To be honest, every time I read it, I flinch. Even now, I still can't think of anything that could work with what's been written and what I want. So, end of story. To all those who reviewed--thank you for those comments! I'm glad you liked the story. I'm sorry that I won't be continuing it for you.
A cookie to the curious reader who happened to stumble upon this story and even review it! Thank you!

Lost In Dreams: As I've said before, writing over the span of three years changes a lot, especially your style. My view of this story changes so often that it's become more and more difficult to update. However, I've grabbed time, a lot of influence, and a good deal of creativity, and I have now begun the long, tedious, yet interesting process of rewriting this story. Don't worry, most everything will be the same. However, the writing style will probably change quite a bit, and many, many more things will be added to the story itself to make it more complete, to ensure that there aren't any holes. A few unnecessary bits of plot will be removed. They're nothing important--only things written at one point, posted, and later found to be horribly inconvenient and wrong, resulting in a chapter or two of horribly quality.
Old readers: If you've come back to read this story, you'll find a bit of a surprise. I will be deleting all the chapters of the story and posting them once again, to avoid any confusion. I am going to rewrite all the chapters before posting any, and then begin a more systematic method of posting. Every two weeks, I'll try to get a chapter up. This will probably slow down after the first 13 chapters, but I hope to keep that up as long as possible so that, during those two weeks between chapters, I can write and not worry about posting a new chapter.

I thank each and every one of you for reading this story. It's amazing how people still stumble upon this after all the dust it has gathered. I love the reviews you all write. I will be so ecstatic if I still have readers when that next chapter is up!
