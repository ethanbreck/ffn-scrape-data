I am a 36 year old married female with a 4 year old daughter and a 2 year old son. Currently I'm a stay-at-home mother. Prior to Mamahood I have been in the military, a financial auditor for the mortgage section of Wells Fargo bank and a residential property manager. That is me in a nuthshell.

I've been a big fan of fanfiction for quite some time as I always found myself wondering "What happened next?" after the credits rolled or I had turned the last page. I've been a lurker here for years, reading but never posting anything. I'm a non fiction writer most of the time however I've finally decided to start posting on this site. We'll see how it goes.

I have recently started a forum website with the writer Essathetwerp. It's called The Word Garden and it's a community to talk freely about writing and reading. Also to post if your work if you want to. We have spots for original fiction, poetry, non-fiction and fanfiction. Along with book reviews, inspiration and other things. So come on by if you're interested.
