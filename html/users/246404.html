Hey all! My name's Jezzy. I love anime, writing (of course), drawing, reading, and singing a lot. I get most of my ideas when I'm taking walks while listening to music. I specialize in OC fics, since it's something I've done since the times of old-school Power Rangers (I wrote a picture book about the green ranger saving me from one of the bad guys. Gimmie a break, I was 4). Music is really what creates a story for me. Take any one of my fics and there's most likely a song that inspired it. I also put a substantial amount of research into my fics to make them more enjoyable. Any drawings I do concerning my fics can be found on my deviantart account. Don't be afraid to drop me a line if you have any questions or just want to chat. I love meeting new people!

Here's why my writing takes so long: I need to write in a notebook before typing it up. For some reason, my juices flower better that way. So not only do I have to finish a draft, I have to then type it up.
