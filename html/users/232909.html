Well, anyone who was reading my fanfics must have noticed that I haven't updated in...many months, maybe a year? wow...it's been so long. A lot has been going on in my life, though, and I just don't have time for fanfiction anymore. I have to contentrate on original pieces that would be suitable for publication, find a job, maybe go to college...so you see, fanfics were fun but as of now I'm ready to declare all of them EXCEPT my HP/FF crossover OFFICIALY DISCONTINUED. I MAY continue my HP/FF crossover because of all of them it has the biggest place in my heart, plus I've been tremendously inspired by the newest Harry Potter book and movie and want to continue it...but we'll see how things go and if I can possibly do it...I thank all of the people who read my fics, and if YOU want to continue any of the ones I have now discontinued then by all means, go for it. :) Another thing, my life has been so hectic lately, a real nightmare, I haven't gotten a chance to read the fanfics of authors I love in many months, so for that I am really really sorry. I can't even remember half of the ones that I was addicted to at one time, but I will try to find them again and leave reviews. Anyways, this is a sort of quasi-farewell, I may still hang around and maybe even update my Harry Potter/Final Fantasy fiction again...anyway, its been fun.
