Author has written 33 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, and Smallville.

BtVS fic writer since '01. I'm pretty much all about Spike/Buffy, though I dabble in other pairings/fandoms. If you like your fanfic fast and fun, with heart, humor, happy endings* and a hearty helping of smut, you may enjoy what you find here. Escapism, yay!

(*some less happy than others, but no one dies or anything.)

Go to NautiBitz.com for continuations of anything that's unfinished here, as well as reader favorites — and dare I say it, 'classics' of the Spuffy fandom — In Heat (my first, which was considered super-risqué at the time and launched a hundred fanfic clichés), Crash and Burn (a breezy teen AU beach read for which I also composed and recorded an original song; this one got about 5000 hits a day as I was posting it in 2003) and Older (a taboo AU Lolita-like love story that inspired several fanvids, tons of fanart and its very own fansite). These 3 works have won a combined total of more than 80 awards. (Disclaimer: this may come across as braggy, but so be it. It's all true, and I'm proud of my meager fandom accomplishments! :)

At my website, you will also find my personal favorite, the recent Don't Stop (a raunchy season 2 sex-brawl with banter galore), and links to my latest in-progress (but nearly finished) AU, Crave. (It's the first story I'm adapting into an original novel, so I'm especially protective of it. Therefore I keep it in a locked community.)

(I recently republished most of my M-rated work here when I realized FF.net was accepting it again, but I won't be adding the above stories -- they're too long and/or too sexually explicit. My site remains my most complete archive.)

Want to know when I write a new fic or update an old one? Sign up for my mailing list (over 4000 members strong!) or send an email to NautiBitz_Newz-subscribe@yahoogroups.com. (No spam, just update notices.)

NO RE-DISTRIBUTION. Links only. Please don't re-post any of my stories. Please don't post translations. Thank you!

A NOTE ABOUT PLAGIARISM: Don't do it. Call me crazy, but I don't like finding my hard work pasted into other people's stories. If I find out you've plagiarized me or any other author, I will make sure everyone knows it. If you're not clear on what 'plagiarism' means, the definition is here.

Fanfiction may be based on someone else's characters, but our words are our own. Don't be lazy. Use your brain. Be inventive. You'll see that no amount of empty praise will equal the feeling you get when you write something wonderful all by yourself. :)
