I'm Jen, and welcome to my page! It looks a little different, but I promise it's just the dust settling down. Things will come back to normal eventually.

Due to various reasons, I will not be posting some fics of mine to this site (usually because of R-18 or extreme AU reasons). Or, conversely, I'll be posting censored versions of said fics. If you want to read those, just come on here: touchofspringficblog.tumblr.com

Out of everything you should know about me as a writer, there is one thing you should know in particular:

Almost everything I write is AU. Not so much High School AUs (though I have written a grand total of...one), but more alternate reality. This goes for every fandom I'll ever write for: If I've written in it or I have an interest in it, chances are that I have some weird and wacky (and yet completely thought out in a way that probably shouldn't be healthy) Alternate Universe in which one small detail (if not a few) in series canon is altered, changing the course of the entire story as a whole. It's sort of a Jen Staple, and I don't mind being criticized for it; writing the canon timeline tends to be boring for me--though I have made exceptions to this rule before.

Warning: I have done heavy cleanup of a lot of my old fics! So stuff that I'm not satisfied with from, say, years ago are likely gone. This includes almost all of my Naruto stuff and some of my Sonic stuff. I've been wanting to do this for a long time now, but never had the courage to do so until now.

Also, I do sometimes write slash (same gender pairings). If this bugs you, please check the descriptions before you click! I'm also an enthusiast of strange / 'crack' pairings.

Current note: My hard drive ended up getting corrupted, so I've lost a lot of data that I'm slowly trying to get back. That includes fic chapters for both Mine and God Save the Children. I may have to rewrite these chapters, which can send these stories' schedules back even further than they already are. Sorry for the inconvenience :(

And please note that I write in not just English, but since I'm trying to perfect my Japanese, I write in Japanese as well...and maybe French when I get the courage to.

So those of you who understand Japanese and see my Japanese stories on my list, please don't hesitate to read them!
