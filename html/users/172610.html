Author has written 17 stories for Stargate: SG-1.

waves* Hi! Welcome to my Stargate fanfiction! I like to write early SG-1 team fics and enjoy placing Sam or Jack (or both!) into angsty situations, dripping with loads of hurt/comfort. I love trying to get into their heads and placing one or the other in danger is a great vehicle to do so! YI am an unabashed shipper, but I do try to keep it cannon. Except for that one time... when I totally fell off the wagon. *ahem*

I also love Daniel, and dedicated an entire 175k series to the guy (Barista Series). It was massively fun revisiting pivotal episodes through the eyes of a Barista and although I never actually finished the sequel Assistant series, I seriously enjoyed having my original character work at the SGC. She didn't seem so out of place after watching her with the gang over the last 5 years. Really! The Barista Series were kinda popular at the time, having won a few fanfic awards, including Best Gen Series, Best Original Character and Best Quirky Story (I swear, they made that up for me). The Seven Days story won best Hurt/Comfort (Ship). It was my very first story and while I'd kinda like to re-write parts of it (the punctuation is not good!), it's still an enjoyable read. :)

Let's see, what can I tell you about me? I discovered Stargate in 2001 and SG-1 fanfiction shortly after that. I started posting my own stories the following year, hitting my stride a few years later while I was in grad school. But I'm afraid those heady days of simply working, studying, drinking and writing had to end sometime. In 2006 I bought a house which unintentionally resulted in a major remodel in 2007 (while pregnant!). The kid came along in mid-2008 and writing and reading fanfiction I'm afraid fell by the wayside.

So now it's 8 years later and I have rediscovered the joy of writing again. Yeah! Not sure if I'll be able to write much, but I've sure missed this community and will do everything I can to post when able. My dream of course is to write my own original stories one day, but for the moment, I'm quite happy to borrow existing characters. :-)

I hope you enjoy these stories of mine and thank you to each and everyone who has taken time to review over the years. I can't tell you how much that encourages me to write!

My Website (which hasn't been updated since 2007, 'cause I've lost the ISP info.D'oh!)
