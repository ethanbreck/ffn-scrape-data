Hobbies: Anime, RPG's, Reading, Marching

Charateristics: Although his grammar skills quite literally stink. Robert understands many mechanismsthat go into thewriting process itself. He's also a 6 foot 2 ape decendent. He has brown hair and eyes.

Bio: Robert is the son of a retired Navy Officer so he spent much of his childhood moving around. Due to this lack of permanent friendships Robert grew a big interest both the video game and literary fields very young. Growing older this only intensified causing his love for reading and a good story to be a big thing for him.

At the age of 21 he enlisted into the Military for his residence in Albuquerque, New Mexico wanting to serve his country. He graduated from Basic Training in July going to his "A" School for StoreKeeping. Upon Graduating from that he proceeded to Basic Enlisted Submarine School where he graduated from in December of 2004.

Although he is in the Military Robert doesn't plan to let his hobbies slip . . . and despite the hardships he does keep up on them as best as he can.

That does include his writing and reading of fanfics. Despite being known for some of the harsher reviews here on FF.net Robert's reviews do not overly insult the person who did the original writing usually trying to just point out places where improvement would be more sufficient and make the story better from a readers perspective of the story then from the authors.
