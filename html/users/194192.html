WORKS IN PROGRESS - CURRENTLY

Obsession: Cuts like a Knife – Newsies
From the author: Obsession is the author's rewrite of CLAK and is currently entering its second revisal. In preparation of a historical novel I plan to write, I decided to delve back into the Soul Mates Series and rework this fanfic. It's been eleven years (eleven years this April in fact!) since I first published this fic and, as I've grown as a writer, these characters have grown with me. I'm excited to take a sort of twist to the Rip/Jess/Spindle dynamic while, at the heart of the piece, keeping it as familiar as it ever was. New characters will replace some of the old ones and we will eventually get back to Manhattan, but I want to really bring some of my OCs to life in this before introducing the movie characters. Update: Check out Don't Trust Me by Jessica Lynch, an original piece of fictions starring contemporary versions of these characters!
awards: 1st place best original character fic, 1st place best protagonist Jessa (NML FF Awards 2006), 2nd place best original character fic, 1st place best villian Rip (NML FF Awards 2007)

Fireflies in the Morning – Newsies
From the author: Originally conceived for and written during Camp NaNoWriMo 2011 (the July session), Fireflies is my attempt to give a realistic spin to the old cliche that gives a Newsies character a long-lost sibling. In this story, Jack Kelly is tired of running from his past as Francis Sullivan when, all of a sudden, we are introduced to Emma -- Emma, Jack's thirteen year old sister who has run away from her boarding school in Boston in an attempt to reunite their family.
