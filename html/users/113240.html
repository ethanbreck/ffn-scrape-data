Author has written 10 stories for Digimon, X-overs, Futurama, and South Park.

I joined this site back in eighth grade. As you can see, my writing needed improvement, and I didn't exactly fit the description of "emotionally mature." I also loved Digimon, which explains the profile pic. My writing, personality, and interests have changed immensely since then. Nonetheless, I've decided to keep all of my writing here for posterity and for the entertainment of anyone who happens to stumble upon it.

In case anyone is wondering "what happened to this person and why isn't she responding to my comments?", I am now pursuing a career in gynecological oncology (otherwise known as Gyn/Onc), and my writing is limited to patient notes and research papers. I have also gone on several medical mission trips to serve communities in Central and South America, and I am also an avid public speaker, artist, and photographer.

Take care of yourselves and be peaceful to one another, dear readers.

"Athena"
