Hello there. I've been writing fan fiction since I was 12. I am much older than that now, and I still like doing it. I think it's like playing with dolls but more acceptable for an adult.

I'm kind of off-and-on the radar with this stuff. Generally, when my life just gets too busy, I don't write anymore. It's a shame, but so it goes.

I also, unfortunately, have a bad habit of not finishing stories. Anyhow, this happens so much because I generally have ideas for long stories, then I get another idea, and work on that, and before I know it, two years has passed and I don't really care much for the fandom anymore. I'm really, really sorry about this. I mean it.

For the time present, I am only writing Pride and Prejudice fiction.
