I've been writing fanfiction (not always on ff.net and obviosly not all Harry Potter) since 1997 and I've been reading fanfiction since 1996.

These days I only do Harry Potter fanfiction, since there is so much room to play in this fandom. I have a preference for stories with good battle scenes and really new and interesting ideas. I like graphic violence in fics but only when its tied to an actually interesting plot. I really don't like suicide fics - I can't see Harry doing that.

I have no favorite pairings, it doesn't really matter. I really hate pairings of Harry with any of the female characters so far in the books. None of them seem to match him well enough. I prefer stories with Harry being more powerful, smarter (lets face it - Rowling made him into an idiot) and much more intelligent and agressive.

HOWEVER

I have started writing fanfiction again. (Yeah! HP 6 actually inspired me enough to make me start writing again.)
