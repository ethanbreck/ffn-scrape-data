Author has written 24 stories for Valdemar universe.

Find me at: https://www.facebook.com/Shaelesand

I am on there regularly. I'll post the covers I make for the stories, some of Cherry's recipes, Valdemar related sites, and preview chapters. If I can figure out how to upload documents I will be putting epubs on there.

Despite not uploading for AGES, I have continued writing. University just took over everything. Now that I have graduated (with honours YAY!) I am going back, polishing what I wrote and posting stories. I am only posting those that have endings but it may take a while for me to get them fully posted because I will focus on those stories that have the most interest. The stories stretch from the founding of Valdemar to the decades after the Storms with a conclusion for Birds of a Feather.

Adolie knows the first Heralds of Valdemar and even King Valdemar himself. Cherry is a dessert cook who is searching for something she knows she will never find. Icestone and Firesong are more like a pair of cats in a bag than siblings. Fyn has a massive dose of wanderlust. Brenna and Accolon are facing treason of the worst kind and everyone who knows the roots of the treason is dead.

Now a little background...I am female (Which is why most of my main characters are female) and Canadian (which explains some of my odd spelling). I studied Archaeology and Computers at university and I use these to colour my stories, especially the archaeology. A few things have bothered me about the Valdemar universe, especially the lack of development in the culture. Vanyel's time is not that different from Elspeth's, so that is one thing I have tried to address in some of the stories without mangling the canon.

I, like most other writers, read what I thought I wrote and will often skip over mistakes and I am grateful for any feedback I get. Feel free to e-mail me. If you see any place that I have made a mistake about the worlds of these most honoured authors please tell me.

Please read, enjoy, and review! The more reviews I get the faster I post. I welcome positive and negative feedback.
