Hi there. You must be pretty bored to actually be reading this, right? XD (So what does that say for me actually taking the time to write it?)

Anywho. I'm a writer, first and foremost. I focus mostly on fantasy and horror. I'm also a learning artist.

I feed myself by working for an answering service. This means I answer the phones for hundreds of different companies whenever they aren't there to answer. This also means I know the extent of human stupidity. Fav choice caller question is: (after having had an interactive conversation with the caller already) "Am I speaking to a person or a machine?"

...XD Like I said. I know the extent of human stupidity.

As for ficcage, I've not written much, but I seem to have gotten the bug lately. Kind of a relief, in a way, because I've been suffering from a henious writer's block, and at least right now I'm writing.

My current obsession is with Demon Diary, which for some reason spawns bouncy little plot bunnies like nothing else I've ever read or seen. For me, at least. I have several DD fics in the works, as well as one (very abnormal) Yami no Matsuei fic.

Oh, and did I mention the smut factor? ^-^; You're not likely to see me write anything fan-related that isn't at least a little bit smutty. That's just my bag, baby. So if you don't like that sort of thing, don't read my fics.

But if you do, please, read, and give me REVIEWS! Reviews give this author motivation ^-^

Did you actually read this far? Whoa. 0.0 You must be REALLY bored.
