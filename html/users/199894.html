Author has written 9 stories for Transformers/Beast Wars, Legion of Super Heroes, Legion of Super Heroes, and Ninja Turtles.

If things get quiet in here and i don't post new chapters or stories in a few weeks or for many months, then don't worry, it does not mean that the account is deserted.

I'm a scandinavian lady with many interests, some of them changing, some of them still the same. Ranging from biology to mythology, reading and writing stories, web design and science fiction. Deviantart and FF net are my main creative stomping grounds and i also have a webpage but the latter hasn't been updated in a long while and will probably undergo a major revamp it can't seem to keep up with my ever changing plans, no?

Other usernames/handles i use a lot: Ravenspook on DeviantArt, Snakeguru Lass in the LOSH fandom.

My main fandom interests, or some of them anyway: Transformers/BW, TMNT (mainly the 2003 series) Farscape, Gargoyles, Teen Titans, Justice League, X-men Evolution, Storm Hawks, the animated Legion of Superheroes (or used to be anyway now it's a minor interest) Star Wars Rebels and Terry Pratchett's Discworld.

post the latest events in my Deviantart journal since I'm more active there but lately eve that one has been quiet.

As a writer i'm not perfect and even though i speak and write english fluently, i make mistakes just like everybody else.
As a reviewer i tend to be inquisitive, i lean towards constructive critisism, but if something makes
me a bit puzzled i will usually comment about it. I'm not perfect though, if someone writes something
i find enormously disturbing and i have nothing positive to say at all i usually don't review at all.
You will never see me write things like "This sucks". Why? because that's not helping anyone and
it's a rude thing to say. It's a trollish thing to say.

The Legion stories i've written is divided into two main story arcs, one begins with "Catching up" and the other
with"Sentry Duty and Revelations". Why? Because some events would go notably apart from the other.
All LOSH stories are on a long break some fandom focus was lost along the way but this hiatus isn't permanent for all
the stories, the christmas story will most likely get finished one day. I can't promise anything.

I support various pairings in various fandoms as long as they are well done and written in a believable
manner. Alternative pairings at times.

I returned to two of my older fandom interests with a lot of enthusiasm and inspiration and i'm still fond of them.

TMNT 2003 and also the Gargoyles series have well written characters and a wonderfully darker yet still action packed and
fun feel to them and that really appeals to me. My fav Turtle used to be Raphael from the movies because he
stood out the most. But now Donatello is my 1# favourite with Raphael as very close runner up along with Mikey and
in fourth place comes Leonardo. In fifth place then? Master Splinter, i really like how he is portrayed in the 2003 series.
Pairings i like: April/Casey, i also like April/Don implied stories, Leo/Karai, Raph/Joy and Don/Quarry (aka Sydney the blue
haired girl from "Notes from the Underground and Return to the underground) i even started to like Don/Renet at one point.

So I started to a write my first Ninja Turtles story "Reflections" and it's set pre-2007 movie but it follows the events of the 2003 TMNT
cartoon series, not the 3 previous Turtles movies. In this, after the events in "Good genes", season 4 Leo went away on his trip
to Central America and then one year and a few months later he's still there and has stopped writing letters home.
There have been many stories set around the movie events and even alternate versions of it. I had the same thoughts as many
others about the family's reaction to all that so it's hardly original. I just hope to add a few surprises that are more unique, such
as Donatello doing some solo crime-fighting - Ninja style - and nobody knows except April. TBC!

Then came a TMNT Drabble after that plus a second story set completely in the TMNT 2003 cartoon continuity.
The second story introduces another family of mutants. But on their way to New York, a tragedy
happens and three mutant teenagers: Heather, Candace and Sebastian must fend for themselves.
It was no co-incidence that brought them to New York, a certain urban myth worth investigating did.

At that point I also started to get a few ideas for X-men evolution stories starring some of the new mutants/recruits and Brotherhood members in larger roles but i had to cancel them due to the writing block i had for quite a while.

Thank yous and kudos

Beta Reader kudos! A big thank you goes to Funkyfish for beta reading a few of my LOSH story chapters.

I'd like to say that i greatly appreciate reviews on what i write so that i may know what people think,
what things and scenes they liked best and so on. It isn't a requirement but it's a nice gesture if you like
a story to do that.
