Author has written 19 stories for Harry Potter, Invader Zim, Naruto, and Detective Conan/Case Closed.

July 09, 3:32AM

I'm still writing. Unfortunately, I found that not having deadlines (self-imposed as they are) and not having critics riding my ass about everything helps my creativity. I'll upload things in chunks, I think. But I AM still writing is the important part. Not going to keep super updated here. And I'm sorry about slacking on replying to formspring questions and to emails. I rarely check emails, and that's where the formspring alerts are. I'll try and set aside a day sometime soon to get around to all of those. I may not reply to them all, but I will read them all.

I'm a few chapters past where the uploaded versions are at. That's all I'll say for now. I'll try and throw some stuff up before month's end. Real-life and laziness permitting. Sorry for the extended, unexpected delays, everyone. I hate when authors I follow do it, and I knew it'd happen to me eventually. But forcing myself to try and write when I don't want to just makes me churn out subpar stuff, even for my usual garbage. I'm not good, but I'd at least like to write the stories as they are in my head, rather than just sitting down and writing whatever spills out of it. That throws me off of where I need to be, forces me to rewrite changes, and slows things down considerably.

I'm not dead and I've not stopped working on the two ongoing stories. I just got down churning out another third of a chapter of Changing Fate, actually. I'm on Twitter. Not hard to find me, though I rarely check replies there, either.

If you want to ask me anything about my stories, feel free to ask me a question there. I'll try and check it every few days or so, but given how lazy I am, it may be a once-a-week thing. I WILL answer questions that might spoil the story, so ask and read at your own risk, alright? No one gets to say I didn't warn you!

Off to write!
