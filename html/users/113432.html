Joined 09-21-01, id: 113432, Profile Updated: 06-30-10
Author has written 12 stories for Spider-Man, One Tree Hill, Harry Potter, Titanic, and Gossip Girl.

I am a college student who writes fanfiction to let off steam. My whole world revolves around story-telling, whether I'm on the stage, behind the scenes, at the movies, reading a book, or writing something of my own. And yes, I am one of those dreaded masses, the coffee-hoarding, late-night working, half-carpenter half-light technician breed from the seedy underbelly of society. A drama major. Oftentimes people who hear this disgraceful truth are quick to tut and shake their heads in sympathy. "Can't do anything else?" they ask, feigning kindness but doing an altogether poor job of it. I love creating and visiting fantastic worlds, because who said real life was such a picnic anyway? I update my livejournal with lots of little one-shots and in-progress works that never make it on here, so drop by there and friend me if you're interested.

I love canon ships (Lily/James, Chuck/Blair, Spike/Buffy, Logan/Veronica, etc etc) and non-canon ships (Draco/Ginny, Harry/Hermione, Remus/Sirius, etc etc) and crack!ships (Draco/Blair being the one that currently holds my heart). Obviously I'm big on Gossip Girl and Harry Potter, with Buffy and Veronica Mars thrown in there for good measure. I like a lot of TV shows and read a lot of books, but only participate in a few fandoms. Harry Potter is something I've been involved with for years, while Gossip Girl is a relatively new obsession (three years isn't that long when you're a HP fan).

xoxo CB
