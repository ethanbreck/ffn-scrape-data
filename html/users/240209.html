About Me

I've been part of this site for a very very very long time.

My main fandom is Harry Potter, though I dabble in anime from time to time. Usually my works consist of some type of romance, generally always Hermione-centric.

I’m kinda obsessed with the Hermione/Draco pairing and the Hermione/Theo pairing. Although when I write Theo he’s tall and handsome, not like in the books or movie. My new obsession is Thorfinn/Hermione after reading A Promise Unspoken by Kittenshift17 which is hands down my favorite story on this site.

I’ll probably never do another full length fic again, just one-shots or shorts. I love getting requests, but will only fufill them if they meet my interests.

My biggest pet peeve is Guest reviewers who have questions or leave critiques, but no way for me to message them in response. I don’t like keyboard warriors. I will end them.
