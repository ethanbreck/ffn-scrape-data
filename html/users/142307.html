Author has written 7 stories for Bleach, Naruto, and Mega Man.

Hoo-boy. Never did I expect that I would end up not writing for many, many years. It's been long enough, anyway - not as if that means that I'll be posting like mad from now on, but hopefully I'll at least be posting from time to time. I am horribly rusty though, so please forgive me if some of my recent stuff is pretty crappy.

Fandoms I love/am currently involved in include:

Ichigo/Renji (BLEACH)
Shinji/Kaworu (Neon Genesis Evangelion)
X/Zero (Rockman X)
Touya/Yukito (Cardcaptor Sakura)
Tidus/Auron (Final Fantasy X)

Most of my stuff here has been Ichigo/Renji so far, but hopefully there'll be some other material on here in the future, once I get around to rediscovering some of my old fandoms (e.g, it's been almost fifteen years since I touched anything Rockman-related. WHY)
I do also have a few ideas floating around in my head, but who knows how long it'll be before I get them out.

UPDATES

121019: Hello again everyone! Fandom really does have a strange way of coming back when you least expect it. After I completed the last chapter for "inu to tsuki", I decided to open an account with AO3 and port my older works there. As I was doing so, I stumbled upon some Rockman/Megaman fanfics, and reading them rekindled my love for X/Zero, so I decided to try my hand at writing something about them. And behold, "Dearest", my newest piece. Unlike the RenIchi fics I've been upping so far though, this one is completely new, written over the last few months. I actually came up with a few other ideas while brainstorming for themes to write about, so who knows, there may be more new fics coming?

140219: And as promised, the last and final chapter, Chapter 10, for "inu to tsuki", is finally out! Thank you to all my readers for not killing being so patient with me while I intentionally drew out the release of the last four vignettes. The theme is a little cheesy, admittedly, but that's good in small doses from time to time. Besides, we all know these two dorks love their chocolate *cough*.
So this may be it, for now... I hope all of you out there enjoyed reading these as much I did writing them!

040219: And Chapter 9 for "inu to tsuki", a little more than half a month after the previous. The last one will be out on Valentine's Day - and after that, who knows what'll happen? As much as I'd like to be writing more frequently like I did back when I was in university, it's a little hard to as a Working Adult now, sadly. (That, and I'm still mulling ideas for new pieces.) But we'll see what comes along, though it would be a shame to lose this momentum.
The new piece is a rewrite of "seventeen" that I originally put up nearly two years ago - it was originally intended to be a short piece for this collection, but it just kept growing and growing, so I decided to keep that as its own piece and edited it to make a shortened version like I was originally planning to.

190119: And as mentioned in my previous update, the next piece, Chapter 8, for "inu to tsuki", half a month after the last chapter. Another Renji-centric piece, though the next two (also the last two for this work) will feature them both as leads!
Trying to think of new ideas for more drabbles for "inu to tsuki" - I'd like to end at twelve instead of ten (a dozen feels better to me for some reason), so if anyone has any ideas, feel free to PM me!

050119: Happy New Year! And guess who's baaaaaaack. (cough)
Reading all these Captain America/Iron Man fanfics kinda got me inspired in a way (though I'm not sure if what's turning me on is Captain America/Iron Man, or Chris Evans/Robert Downey Jr) - in the least, I'll finish up "inu to tsuki" for good this time, seeing as how all ten of the original stories I had planned for it have been written since 2017, so it's just a matter of posting them up. That said, Chapter 7 is out - Chapter 10, the last of what I currently have, will be out on Valentine's Day, so the next update will be half a month later? Gotta space them out to keep y'all coming back, y'know. (Assuming anyone still reads this)
