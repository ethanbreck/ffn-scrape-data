Hey guys,

S'been forever and a few lifetimes, but I'm back. Back?Yes. BACK. Where I've been and what I've done and seen is best not talked about, but yes. Take my word for it, I was an ff.net-er back in the DAY when they allowed NC-17 fics on this site. My, how times have changed.

If any of you are from "back in the day," you may remember that I'm the author with that epic... Hanami? and it's never-completed Never-Never-Land sequel? Well, don't hold your breath. I've been away so long, and written so much in the meantime, that I just can't get back into those stories. Not as they are... I'm actually re-vamping them, ironing out the cliches, rearranging the plot, adding some common sense and some continuity... and basically, you'll never recognize the stories anymore, but you will recognize the characters. So yeah. That's the far and distant goal. I'm not posting any of that until the WHOLE thing is finished. I promised my edittor.

In the meantime, my fairytale fics are worth a chuckle or two. I'm collaborating on a really dark and terrifying peice with Meira-Bates under a joint-penname: "PeasnCarrots." I'm also sporatically throwing out funny one-shots and reviews that are prolly too damn long.

But yes. I'm back. I look forward to meeting everyone who's showed up since I left, and HOPEFULLY bumping into old friends.

--kya
