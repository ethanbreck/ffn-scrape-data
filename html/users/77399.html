Guess its time for an update after 6 years. No.1, I am proud to declare that I'm the beloved of God...He loves me to BITS and His been so amazing in my life...transforming me from glory to glory. Jesus makes everything about life so fun, including schoolwork! I can't imagine life without Him.

No. 2 I still love X-men, Tamora Pierce, Elizabeth Peters...but now there's Raymond Feist, Frank Peretti and Francine Rivers to add to the list. I love writing...and I wish there's a course that can teach me the techniques and mechanics of writing and creating characters (that's why i write fan fiction coz i think its very hard to create a well established character).

No. 3 I love classicalish music ALOT...Josh Groban, Phantom of the Opera soundtrack, Holst's Jupitar, Beethoven's Appassionata and the like...not so fond of pop and rock.

No.4 I'm very into science too...einstein relativity, quantum, parallel universes, black holes, astronomy...as long as there is no mathematics. Its something i cannot process properly.

No. 5 Well I guess that's about it...if you want to know more about it you hafta get to know me personally. heh. Oh yes, philosophy rocks too. love making my brain think in strange directions.
