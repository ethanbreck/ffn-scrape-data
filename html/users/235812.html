Wotcher all.

My name is AyeAvast and i'll be your tour guide today.

Let's start with a spiffy introduction of myself!

My name is AyeAvast and i'm a 20 year old, Taurean woman with bright pink-red hair. (Yes, i'm being serious.)

I'm stubborn and persistant, but decadent and take a long time to think things over.

I LOVE romance.

If it's fluffy and cute and full of kisses and adorableness i'll melt.

But I also like real romance and always strive to make me couples have not only problems, but a lot of passion too.

I'm currently attending college so my updating is rather SLOW. I have to be inspired to write and when that happens, i'll update like no one's business. Unless i'm studying... .

I love emoticons. Don't know why, but I use them alot. Tee hee~

I'm a sucker for music. Especially if its catchy or metal. Both are great. Especially if it's got a good female vocalists. I'm really picky about my guy vocalists too. They have to be pretty good. (Patrick Stump will always be the best in my book. He's got range.)

I read alot of books so sometimes my writing tends to be a little wordy.

I like constructive criticism. I want to be a writer one day (i'm going to major in writing) so I need all the help I can get right now!

I'm a real lover of fashion. That's why all my characters have such detailed outfits. Sorry boys.

I'm just a generally happy-go-lucky (albeit clumsy) and optimistic person!

As stated above, romance is love. (Wait, no... Rococo is love! Gothic lolita is love! GAH!) So most of my stories will have lots of mushy love. That's probably why they're rated so high because I find love needs to be expressed in all its forms.

My bestest buddies are on Fanfiction. Check them out~
Mellybrooke is amazing. She's even writing a story about me!

Well! That's the end of our tour. I hope you've all had a nice trip. Please watch your heads and limbs as you exit the compartment. Have a wonderful and I hope to see you again very soon! Buh bye now!

What i'm currently working on...

Oh... You were expecting something here, eh? Oh dear that's not very good. Alright. I'll see what I can scrounge up for you.

Remus/Tonks Lovelyness: Ha ha. Let's see how many people I can get on THIS bandwagon!
Hey! Us Remus/Tonks shippers didn't get our fair shair of their goodness in the HP series, yeah? Well what really happened? What was their relationship really like? Did they cuddle happily next to a fire? Was having sex with a werewolf difficult? And what about the wedding? And Teddy? So many unanswered questions! Your dear AyeAvast will do her best to answers all these questions and more as she explores what she thinks really happened behind closed doors between these two very different people.

Aurikku Goodness: I can't have HP love without returning to my original older man/ younger girl- serious guy/ chipper girl ship!
Rikku's got to figure out someway to get Auron back. She loves him! And Yuna got Tidus back, so why not Rikku? But that entails perilous missions, saving massive amounts of people and heartbreaking flashbacks of this tumultous and forbidden love.

Wizards of Waverly Place AKA Justin/Juliet Fluff: Oh god, I got a case of the fangirlitus bad. I seriously love Justin and Juliet and think there isn't enough love of them around! I will do my best to think of an engaging, angst filled story that show cases their strange love. Obviously there will be lots of fluff but also lots of raging hormones. C'mon, they're teenagers! As of yet I don't have a plot but alls I know is there will be lots of making out, a few drops of blood here and there, maybe a crash course in sex-ed with a vamp (yikes!) and definitly a lot of laughs.
