From the top

I am me...

Indecisive. Unstable. Hyper. Dreamer. Hopeless Romantic. Coffee-addict. Music-obsessed. Chocoholic. Bookworm. Fan-girl. Wallflower.

The FACTS:

As a writer, if I even have the right to call myself that - I'm carry a "my pace" sort of attitude, and for that, I ask forgiveness.

Works in Progress:

March 14, 2008
Radiance

The eighth and ninth chapter have been posted, virtually back to back. I am just as surprised (laughs) This can be called my White Day Present? Eriol and Tomoyo have been running circles around my head for the last week - being at my desk with practically all this time on my hands (aka: hima) But now with the addition of Sakura and Syaoran stepping up in the story, my brain is dizzy. Ahhh the signs of insanity.

September 19, 2007
Radiance

I have uploaded the third and the fourth chapter in the last four days. For me, that is something of the miraculous degree. I am beginning to find that Eriol is not as gentle as he seems, he is fiercely protective and a very passionate soul, but that doesn't mean that Tomoyo will pale in comparison. She is my heroine, so naturally, she will shine just as brightly.

August 11, 2007
Radiance

This is the new re-write of This Time Around. Presently, the prologue and the first chapter has been uploaded, as well as the Author's note. I hope that you guys read the author's note .

Works Dropped:

This Time Around

I have stopped writing this story in order to move on and re-write it in the form of the new story Radiance , but will not take the story down for about a year or so. Thank you all for reading this story, and my humble apologies and regret that I was not able to finish it.

Future Works:
(I know, the nerve of me to put something up when it has taken me so long to get Radiance where it is now. orz)

March 14, 2008
Timeless

The second story of the duology. The first, of course, being Radiance. This shall take place either right after, or a few years after. Yeah, I'm surprised I've managed to think this far in advance as well. This will be Sakura and Syaoran's story.

On Reviewing:

Before I used to be all about reviews, but have come to realize that it's okay without, I do love reading what you have to write as reviews, nothing makes my day brighter more than reading what you have to say about my writing, I kid you not! Some of them surprisingly and pleasantly long, and I enjoy the criticisms as well. However, if you truly feel neutral about the chapter and/or have nothing constructively criticizing to say (as in, you simply want to flame flame flame) or simply cannot find the words, you don't have to review, it's okay. Is it selfish of me to want reviews with content rather than nothing at all? Maybe, but I hope that you, as fellow writers, will understand my stand in this.
