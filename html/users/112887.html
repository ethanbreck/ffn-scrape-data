Author has written 15 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Buffy X-overs, Crow, Sandman, Harry Potter, and Book X-overs.

Been writing fan fiction for several years, I also RP and been more of that on LJ of late. Even before I knew of the term. (First was a fanfic based on Tolkien's "The Hobbit," the story being Bilbo's journal during his adventure. Alright, yeah it was for a HS English project, but it was me idea.) Anyway, have written several stories and poems, some of which is published on other sites. Mostly Buffy the Vampire Slayer (Got one on Anya's Slayer Fanfic Archive called the Slayer Files), but also, X-Files (Gone Away by Dragonkat on the Gossamer archive's mine, was going under another handle then), Babylon 5, The Crow, Hellblazer, Sandman, some anime, and others. There's also some on Kielle's Subreality archive Yes, I'm American, but don't hold that against me. ;) . And despite what you might think from my handle, yes, I'm a woman.

Further update: Above works still in progress, now with Second Star Skerry (tentative title) a Peter Pan/Books of Magic/Sandman crossover, To Be A Soft Place, a Dead Zone/Sandman crossover, and Reflected Kingdoms, a Buffy/Tenth Kingdom crossover. (And there might be a possible Buffy/Devil Hunter Yohko crossover in the works at some point as well. Can't believe there's none out there with all the common ground between the two.) And yes, seems like all I can think of lately are crossovers. Doh!

Another update: Got into Supernatural and Glee so got some ideas percolating in that direction. Might try to hammer out some of my old WIPs but making no promises. But hope to have some new stories again soon.
