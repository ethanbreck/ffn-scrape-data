Author has written 5 stories for Ranma.

I'm a college student. I like reading and writing. I write fanfiction mostly for practice, partly for a little boost of confidence when I feel like I'm slipping. I enjoy writing in someone else's universe -- it's always fun to try and think the way someone else does.

My favorite animes are Ranma 1/2, Paranoia Agent, Dragon Ball Z [in all its horrifying glory, and pretty much anything by Miyazaki [durrr]. I'm also a huge Avatar fan.

I love constructive reviews! I'm not just a sucker for positives, I'm also a sucker for negatives -- I'm very serious about my writing and I want to improve, and the only way to do that is to not be sensitive about things when someone critiques my stories. Please feel free to leave "negative" comments, I just ask that you're as polite as you can manage over the internet =D

I have another Fanfiction account over at Mediaminer.org, under TheBatCat. That's where I post my "saucy" stories!
