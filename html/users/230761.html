Hiya! This is Flaming Trails here. Here are three things you ought to know about me and my fanfic:

--I'm a girl, born December 27th, 1986.

--My favorite fandoms are the Back To The Future universe (primarily the movie trilogy and the Telltale Game), Corpse Bride, and the "Alice" games by American McGee.

--I love crossovers -- crack and serious -- and AUs, and I like fics where people get turned into occult-style monsters (like vampires) or gain superpowers. Not to say that I don't like canon-related fiction too -- I just like imagining things as they could be.

Fan Fiction Universes (no overlap unless noted):

BTTF: Vampire Doc Chronicles: Features Doc as a vampire and an OC girlfriend. Canon: Part I minus ending.

BTTF: NonTrilogy: Features a version of Doc who went to a different date in the future than the Trilogy version, and thus ended up fixing things a different way. Things proceed from the end of Part I. Canon: Part I minus ending

BTTF: Trilogy: Features the Doc we all know and love from the movie trilogy. Canon: Parts I, II, III

BTTF: Night and Day: Features Doc as a vampire from the Biffhorrific timeline in Part II, and his OC girlfriend. Canon: The Biffhorrific timeline from Part II for the mains; Part I and alternate versions of Parts II and III for the secondaries (there's a lot of weirdness in this verse)

BTTF: PreTime/1955: Stories that involve both the time period before the fateful weekend in Part I and Marty's original week in 1955, which are pretty much common to all universes. Canon: Usually Part I's Nerdy George timeline for PreTime; the week in 1955 from Part I for 1955

BTTF: Crossovers: Stories crossing BTTF with other universes, like the Ock-Doc-umentary. Canon: Varies from story to story; example: Ock-Doc has Part I and alternate versions of Parts II and III

BTTF: Humorverse: Humor stories, including "The Adventures of J.C. Jones," my alter ego. Canon: Parts I, II, III, some TAS inspiration

CB: General: Stories exploring the characters in Corpse Bride. Canon: Not really applicable, as so far they all happen before the movie

AMA: General: Stories exploring the characters in the "Alice" games. Canon: "American McGee's Alice," "Alice: Madness Returns," Alice Storybook app

CB/AMA: Forgotten Vows Verse: A Victor/Alice crossover, where Victor ends up at Houndsditch and goes through the events of A:MR with Alice. Canon: Corpse Bride, "American McGee's Alice," Alice Storybook app, an alternate version of "Alice: Madness Returns"

Other: Anything else that doesn't fit into the above categories

My Websites:

Doc And Victor's Girl's Site: My NaNoWriMo site, containing the three stories I've done for the challenge (two complete, one hopefully to be completed this year)

LiveJournal -- Doc's Girl Prompt Tables, Butterfly Boy's Fanfic Roundup: My two LJs where I post lots of fanfic. Doc's Girl Prompt Tables is for short fic based on prompt tables (majority Victor/Alice, but there's a BTTF table too); Butterfly Boy's Fanfic Roundup is for fic based around the Secundus-Verse seen on my NaNo site

DocFangirl1 -- Victor/Alice, Marty In Wonderland: My devART account, which has fanart and, as linked, extra Victor/Alice fics and a weird semi-BTTF: TAS-based crossover

Cover images are from: Gamekult, Grande Caps, dj43 on LJ, the Alice Wiki, velldt on tumblr

See you in the Future! --FT
