Hey there! I see you've somehow stumbled upon my humble bio. Let me start off by saying welcome, and follow up by extending my apologies for my rather empty and boring bio.

As it now stands, I am a third-year undergrad student of English at UC Berkeley. My way-too-numerous interests include literature, poetry, movies, music, art, writing, reading, drawing, and singing in the shower (when no one is around, of course).

In the fascinating universe of fandoms, you could say I'm an avid fan of Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and a few choice anime as well, such as Bleach, Cardcaptor Sakura, and Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicle. As for my genre preferences, I generally consider myself a reader of almost anything, though I do prefer fics that include action/adventure, romance (within a realistic perspective, of course; no sappy harlequins, thanks), humor, and sometimes angst, should I be craving it at the moment. And I tend to stay away from smut and slash. As for favorite pairings, I have quite a number, though my ultimate OTPs will always be Faramir/Eowyn (LotR), and Eriol/Tomoyo (CCS).

I would say "Go read my fics or else!" and brandish my Mechanical Pencil of Doom most fiercely at you, but alas, I have no fics to call my own as of yet. Nonetheless, I wish you happy reading and (to all you writers out there) happy writing!
