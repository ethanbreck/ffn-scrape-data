August 7, 2006

It's been AGES since I updated this account and the stories contained in it. I'm very sorry about that. It's just that I've lost interest in the YYH and RK fandom, writing fanfic about those characters anyways. Sometimes I still read other people's fanfic about YYH and RK, more on RK really, but I'm just not that interested anymore.

I still love anime and manga, titles I like to read fanfic about are Gokusen, Sailormoon, Gensoumaden Saiyuki, Naruto, Inuyasha, Fruits Basket, Gals, and Fushigi Yuugi. Pairings I luike from those mentioned titles include KakashiXSakura or ItachiXSakura (only if Sakura's of age, mind you!), UsagiXMamoru and InnersXGenerals, ShinXKumiko, NarakuXKagome (I know... it's a bit improbable but I'm not the only one who supports this pairing and at least I don't write it), ShigureXTohru, TasukiXMiaka, andKotobukiRanXOtohataRei (coz I despise Aya). Non-anime pairings I like include DracoXHermione of Harry Potter, and Jack Sparrow X Elizabeth Swann of PotC.

So about my stories... I won't be updating them anymore. I decided to focus on my original work (when I have spare time) and stop writing fanfiction altogether. I might change my mind and actually post the rest of the chapters available for the stories listed below... but I'm not very much inclined to do so right now.

Got nothing to do so I'm typing up something for this bio: "Feifu" is my RL nickname given to me by my highschool friends. As far as I know, it's a Vietnamese name; there's actually a place in Vietnam with that name, I checked ; But no, I'm not Vietnamese. Although I do come from Asia I like writing stories (obviously...); both fanfiction and original. My original fics are still ongoing; they're works in progress and quite long. Haven't posted any of them at fictionpress.net though. I'm still thinking if I should. There would be copies of them at my site so if you're interested just go to my website. I LOVE anime. Among my favorites are Yuyu Hakusho, Gundam Wing, Lupin III, Nausicaa, Whispers of the Heart, Please Save My Earth, Fruits Basket, Fushigi Yuugi, Ayashi no Ceres, Rurouni Kenshin, and a whole lot more titles. What else? Hmmm... can't thinkof anything else to type. Maybe I'll add something again next time I'm bored hehehhe
