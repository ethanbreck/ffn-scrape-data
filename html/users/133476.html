Hullo and welcome, to Obviously Oblivious' Bio page. This Bio page is brought to you by the CAPS LOCK KEY and A Bit Of General Insanity.

Scared yet? Believe me, by the end of reading this you shall be. Because it hath beene sayde she whoe ith withe sugah shalle be scarye asth. Translation? I'm weird and have read Good Omens far too many times and find it rather amusing to type in pathetically bad Olde English.

Okay so down to business. My name is Katie, despite popular opinion, which informs me that it is Katherine. Do not listen to these people. I'm also 18, which infers that I should be writing this with a much greater deal of maturity that I have or ever shall display. I'm currently in Australia, though most of the time I wish I were in Ireland, or at the least England. This is not because I believe Australia is a horrible horrible place and should rot in hell, but more so that I'd prefer to be elsewhere. And Ireland is a *much* better place. And the accents :DIES: I rather like to *think* I'm in Ireland as well, and make traditional Irish food and then totally wreck the effect of Irish-ness because when I speak, this British accent comes out and it's all OMG ACK er and stuff. (Yes, I'm 100% Australian and have a British accent. This is not my fault. It came naturally. And as a result I was teased extensively for my inability to say 'Aussie' or 'mate' as normal Australian's do. Er).

Right-o onto the topic of FANFICTION. I write for various fandoms, predominately Harry Potter but only MWPP because...I don't know just because. I've never had an any inclination to write anything else er. Mainly because Remus/Sirius = OTP. Oh yes, and I'm a supporter of SLASH. If you don't like it, it's not really my fault so...for the sake of sanity, go elsewhere? I haven't any of my slash fic's on this server yet but MER they are to come.
Another major fandom for me is Good Omens. This is because Crowley and Aziraphale's love issoCOMPLETELYobviousandstaringthemintheface. However I haven't written any GO fanfiction yet, mainly because I am quite okay with just reading other people's masterpieces for now.
My third fandom is Hornblower because Horatio/Archie issocanon. In the A&E versions of course. It's Horatio/Bush in the books. Yes.

Music. I like music. So much so as that I torture myself by learning Theory Of Music, which is just about as exciting as I'm assuming History of Magic would be but as I have now progressed to grade 6 (only three more exams to go!!11!!!!1), we are FINALLY learning how to write PROPER SONGS. NO MORE SCALES actually I haven't done those since grade 3 ER but anyway.
Along the listening to music vein I prefer:
The Dandy Warhols, Placebo, Queen, The Clash, The Jam, Spineshank, Autumn to Ashes, Thursday, Poison the Well, Sunk Loto, Maroon 5, Bright Eyes, Desaparencidos, Commander Venus, Cursive, Something for Kate, etc.

And now, I do believe I have babbled on quite enough to certify ye alle as bored so I shalle departe.
