I'm old enough to think of fandom as my little dirty secret. I found the Lord of the Rings books in a dusty attic and they were my gateway drug to sci fi/fantasy. Then I saw the Lord of the Rings movies and they were my gateway drug to fanfiction.

I'm on tumblr occasionally at lurea(.)tumblr(.)net. Mostly Supernatural squeeing there, but please feel free to send me a message. Click on the 'ask me anything' on the right side of the page.

I read just about anything that catches my eye. I used to not post fanfiction as I wrote a lot for kink memes or closed fic communities, but I'm trying to bring more of it over and to update more frequently. I write Lord of the Rings, Fallout, Supernatural and sometimes other shiny--SQUIRREL!!
