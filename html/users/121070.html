Hello!

First of all I must say that I am an avid Star Trek fan (DS9 is my absolute favorite). I like the new Enterprise show too but it really hasn't caught my complete interest...yet. I do have some Enterprise fiction but a few of the stories are incomplete. It's not because I don't want to finish them it's because I haven't had the time to sit down and write. Anyhow...My favorite characters from Deep Space Nine are: Major Kira, Tora Ziyal, Gul Dukat, Garak, Damar, Legate Ghemor (remember him...such a sweet cardassian man). And Iliana Ghemor (even though we never saw her on the show, I always was curious on what she'd be like, so I'm writing a fic about her,which will include a lot of cardassian characters from the show. Okay now you all know that I love cardassians :) Yes they were horrible but they just looked so awesome and Marc Alaimo's (Gul Dukat) voice was so soooo nice to listen to. For books the best Deep Space Nine book I read is A Stitch in Time by Andrew J. Robinson (Garak). It's so good in fact, I'm reading it again :)

I hope you enjoy my stories, drop me a line any time!!!

Ziyal
