Well, I've finally come back to FF.net. I haven't posted anything on here since like... 2002. Yikes, that was 15 years ago now, I feel old. I'd like to think that I've grown as a writer since then, but it turns out I have significantly less time to read and write now that I'm married career woman with a home to look after. Who knew adulting would be so time consuming? As such, my creative writing has become a hobby that as dwindled in usage and is sorely out of practice, but I'm greatly enjoying delving back into it in my free time. While I have written plenty of original short stories and fiction in my time, FanFiction is the easiest for me to write, as it allows me to practice with style, content and building scenes without all the effort of creating the characters, universe, locations and backstories. I would imagine that's a huge part of why any of us who love to write use FanFiction as an outlet.

Also... sometimes that one scene you WISHED SO HARD they had included with your favorite characters never happened, and you just HAVE to write it yourself. :P
