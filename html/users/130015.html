Hello everyone. I am Relena Peace. I'm also known as BubblesRelena, but with my fiction and poetry I like to keep my pen name as Relena Peace. I honestly haven't been on this site since high school...three years ago. I haven't written anything in ages either. But the other day I got a wild hair and wanted to read some of The Black Rose's stuff (great, GREAT writer! She's on my fave list, you must check her stuff out!) and I happened to look at my stuff too. I re-read some of my stories (and couldn't believe how horrible I was at spelling and grammer) and thought, "You know what. I used to LOVE writing. I really want to start doing it again."

So, of course, I stayed up still 6 am reading stuff from The Black Rose (omg...I am a total fan. LoL.) Anywhoo..and when I went to bed my story "The Vampire's Kiss" kept playing in my mind. I think I am inspired, and I really want to try to keep going with "The Vampire's Kiss." I know, it's been three years. But some of you out there STILL have me on your fave authors, and even have one, or more of my stuff on your favorite stories list! I was so touched I almost cried. And then to see how many hits my story "Medieval Scandal" got...I was shocked.

So, please bare with me as I again pick up a pen and continue a story that has not been touched in three years. And thank you, for everyone who continued to read my stuff. It really makes me dream again...and that is where all my inspiration comes from.

Thanks.

: huggles :

~Relena Peace
