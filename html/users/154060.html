Hi, I'm J.T13, Rally4ever or just Plain Jamie... for those of you who knew me on the- "N"etwork's site... I am muy, mondo excited to be here!

Well, I've been "around"... auser since 1/13/2002... It's just, I've never posted my own work... But I've been reviewing and encouraging other writers behind the scenes (J.T.) ...

Anyhow, I plan big things with my writing, and when it comes time for it... I try my best to give it all I've got. Yes, I miss RFR extraordinarily... This helps me cope. But, WOW... WHAT AN ENDING! I have about three fics up now... I hope to create more in tribute to a GREAT SHOW!

Well, I also have other fics in the works ... I have multiple obessions... PTO (Phantom of the Opera), and my classic 'HEY ARNOLD' fic, I've been holding onto for a while now... It's been one of my fondest wishes to post it... IT WAS MY FIRST FANFIC EVER! WEE, IT's like all my dreams are coming true!

Anyhow,

I'm one of those people who have a lot to say... IF NOT... I'll find something to say..or write about. Writing is FREEING... WRITING is EXPRESION!

It's just a part of ajourney for me on my path to "self-discovery." I'm not saying there won't be any "bumps" in the road... In fact, I guarentee it! All I'm saying is I'm not perfect, neither is my writing... I STILL HAVE A LOT TO LEARN! And I'm so glad I do,otherwise I'd be so bored with life...

PEACE OUT!

WRITE LONG and PROSPER!
