Author has written 8 stories for Ranma, Anime X-overs, and Naruto.

Yes, I'm still writing, but I've decided to change how I write. Instead of winging it, writing myself into corners that keep me blocked so long my update schedule takes years between chapter releases, I'm now outlining everything out, beginning to end, and writing out all the chapters before I release anything. So the next time you see a story update will be after I have completely rewritten it and finished it, allowing me to release regular updates until it is completed.

This does mean frontloading all the work, resulting in a long drought followed by a bunch of updates, but considering how long it usually takes me to update anyway, I doubt anyone will really notice a difference.

If you want to adopt one of my stories, contact me for more information. If I feel you have the ability to continue my work, I'll give you everything I have so far on those projects in terms of plot outlines, future plans, intended themes, and in rare cases, unpublished chapters in progress.
