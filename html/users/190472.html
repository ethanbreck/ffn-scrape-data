Guess it's time to update a one-liner I made back in '09 to something a bit more substantial for the no one that reads this page. If anything I guess it makes for a nice time machine. I can reread it and go oh...I wrote that about me. Strange.

About Me:

I live in Seattle so I'm the stereotypical Seattlelite portrayed: I like my Starbucks (I try to drink it every other day, but I become a super grouch in those in-between days), I'm a loud twelve (SEA-HAWKS!), I wear shorts when it's forecasted to be between forty-five to fifty-five degrees out with 90 percent chance of rain and 10 percent chance of measly sun breaks because I do like my sunshine, but I love rain more. We have bad drivers here. They drive me mad (pun intended (I never said I was good with them)). I've been to the Space Needle once when I was eight and I just came over from the Philippines. I don't do any of the touristy stuff (don't even know most of them) which probably solidifies the fact that I live here. I've been writing since I was in fifth grade: a Sailor Moon fanfic back before I even knew what a fanfic was. I kept at it and now it's this full blown hobby that I enjoy.

My Current works:

Completed Works:

All Sailor Moon and Haruka and Michiru based, so if they're your pairing, then give it a shot. I think the synopsis provided per link is already good enough.

I don't write for other fandoms, but it doesn't mean that I don't read or are not interested in them. Right now, I'm into: Arashi (their music in general), Bechloe (Pitch Perfect), Shiznat (Mai-Hime), Harry Potter (I'm one of those crazy HHr shippers that got all emo when they didn't get together in the books), Game of Thrones, Walking Dead (I'm always so sad when there's a gap and these two shows aren't running like right now...), Orange is the New Black, Bones (don't spoil anything I'm only on season 6), and I love movies. Seriously though Beca...how can you not like movies?

I'm usually quite introverted but if you message me, I'll certainly message you back. I don't bite, I see myself as being quite nice actually, so if you have any questions, don't hesitate to PM me.
