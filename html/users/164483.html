Hey there! I’m Glora, or Artificial, whichever you want to call me. If you’re here than I suppose you’ll want to know SOMETHING about me, so here you go: I’m twenty-six, I live in Texas, I want to be a writer (like half of the other people here) and I’m a college student who works in retail off and on.

Contact Me?

Email - Missglorasun@gmail.com

Also, please check out my original works at FictionPress, under the pen name Glorapants. Any reviews would be much appreciated!

Please note that I can't figure out how to change my homepage, and the 'Isis' website is dead. Instead, please check out my fanfiction blog (or artificialximagination.tumblr.com), where there will be bonus material that include playlists, recipes and images, as well as some fanfiction recommendations.

Writing a book is a horrible, exhausting struggle, like a long bout of some painful illness. One would never undertake such a thing if one were not driven on by some demon whom one can neither resist nor understand. George Orwell

A lot of the stories I have here are crap. I'm the first to admit it. But I'm leaving them there in hopes that one day I'll actually go back and rewrite them!

Here are some of my better stories:

ISIS, for Mummy fans who can take a bit of Mary-Sueness. Alex O'Connell's friend Paige has come to visit, and while in Egypt she accidentally raises Imhotep. Strangely, he seems to know her...

STRANGE WORLD, for Raoul/Christine fans, though I would hope E/Cers would read it, too. It's very E/C friendly. It is the third anniversary of Erik's death, and Christine reflects on him and how things have changed since she last saw him. I wrote it hoping that hardcore E/Cers would read it and see that Raoul fans can love Erik, too. SADLY, the formatting seems to be off now, and since it's a songfic I'm scared to go in and fix it for fear it'll be removed.

THE MAIDEN, for Phantom fans who enjoy a bit of a crossover now and then. Erik spends his last evening on a stroll in a graveyard, and meets an extraordinary woman, and then witnesses her unusual death. Unfortunately, there are a few errors in here that I didn't catch the hundred times I read it before I posted it. Honestly – how could I not catch Ursula major?! Curse you, spellcheck!

INSANITY, for hardcore Erik fangirls who hate every other character. Just 'cause you need to learn a little somethin'. (ON SEMI-PERMANENT HIATUS. But the first two chapters are more or less self-contained.)

CINDERELLA AND PERSEPHONE, for Tom/Ginny fans. After the last battle, Ginny looks for a quiet place to be alone as she reflects on her memories of Tom Riddle. Memories that seem to haunt her.

SHADOWS, for Tom/Ginny fans who would prefer a longer story. No matter where she goes or what she does, she always hears his voice. Out of the corner of her eye, she can see his shadow. He can see and hear her, too. And more than that...it seems that Tom Riddle wants complete control of Ginny Weasely.

THE FLOWER GIRL, for Erik/OC fans who would like a more realistic story for once. Looking for shelter from the rain, Erik ducks into a flower shop. It's there he meets a girl of no unusual beauty or talent. The girl he would one day marry.

INNOCENCE, for Phantom fans who enjoy a bit of mystery and drama. Erik is accused of killing Christine Daae, but Erik insists that she is still alive.
