Author has written 12 stories for That '70s Show, Fast and the Furious, Harry Potter, Glee, and Misfits, 2009.

My name is Amber and I'm 27 years old. I am currently in California and have a love/hate relationship with this State.

I write occasionally. My first works of fanfiction were for the show Roswell (and you will never ever see those stories because they were just awful.) I started a story (was supposed to be just a companion piece) as a sister piece to a friends story. It's for The Fast and The Furious and is now known as the 'never ending fic' because it's been in progress for 9 years.

I have written stories for That 70's Show, mainly Jackie and Hyde. As much as I want to delete those stories because of how horrible they are, I can't bring myself to do it. Simply because I am proud of those stories and it was a lot of fun writing them.

I am a huge Harry Potter fan and have a soft spot for Draco/Hermione fics. Writers for that fandom are amazing. I love that they are able to take these 2 characters who hate each other so much and make it so that they enter this relationship, all the while making it completely plausible circumstances.

I write while listening to music, it's my motivation. I'm a huge angst person who sometimes can't resist those happy endings.

I'm on tumblr, so if you want to follow I usually follow back. My username is seeyouintee.
