Author has written 4 stories for Chrono Trigger.

Well, seeing as how I haven't updated my bio since 2002, it seems a renovation is in order. About myself, I've managed to live through 20-something years of life thus far and am currently in the employ of the United Stated Navy. Residing in Japan till April of 2007 from whence I will whisk away to San Deigo to finish the remainder of my contract out. Due to my most-of-the-time hectic life updates may not appear with any kind of set frequency so please bear with me.

I'm a fan of almost all videogame genres though my playtime with games has shortened drastically over the years (damn you real life lol). I'm also a large fan of any books in Forgotten Realms or Eberron settings in D & D. Used to roleplay as well but I don't at the current moment in time. Hmm what else...I like to write, read, code, ride bikes, long walks on the beach, etc, etc ad nauseum.

Anyways, my top 5 games / series are as follows:

1: Chrono Trigger

2: Final Fantasy Tactics

3: Zelda Series (or else my the rest of the entires would be Zelda games)

4: Shadows of the Colossus

5: Grandia I / II

As you may have noticed, if you posses even the dullest of perceptions, my favorite series to write about is Chrono Trigger. It may be since I can mess with time, and dimensions, in any way my evil heart desires. Too many possibilities, so little time. My ongoing series, The Observers, is my only work in progress at the moment. The series will break down into three sections as follows:

Starting at the end of Chrono Trigger:

1: Observers: Aftermath

2: Observers: The Black Wind

3: Observers: Reverse Chronology (Perspective Title)

At first I disavowed all connection of my fics to Chrono Cross (seeing as I wasn't a huge fan of the game) but I end up borrowing alot of story elements from the game. So instead of saying it isn't connected at all, I'll simply say that they might loosely tie-in with one another. It may not be obvious how, but I assure you it works out somewhere in my head (if that is any kind of assurance anyway). In between the 3 I might release one-shot short stories of various Observers, even some about Janus once in a while, and their missions trying to keep time in line. I think it would enhance the overall picture of the world I'm trying to build and let me focus on writing short stories instead of large epic things.

I have some other, previous, fics that you can check out too that aren't exactly things I will boast about. Some people appeared to like them so :shrug: maybe they are worth a read after all. They aren't tied in with the Observers though so any coincidental likenesses are just that, coincidental. Anyways, hope you enjoy reading some of my stories and please leave reviews (like or dislike, don't matter to me!).
