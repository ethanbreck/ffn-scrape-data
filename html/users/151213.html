One day,

We'll look back at today,

And say,

"ick"

... Ha ha.

Too late.

Naheka is a 14 year old who keeps getting her foot caught in the lines that seperate reality from fantasy. She likes to draw anime stuff and write fiction, as well as listen to that loud annoying music that her mom can't stand (the last CD she got was 'American Idiot'). She likes to draw on oekaki, too.

Joe is the duo secretary/supervisor that helps Naheka make sure that she doesn't actually breakthe line between fantasy and reality. Though he is a parodic version of one of J.R.R. Tolkien's undead men of Numenor and therefore a fictional character, he is based on several of Naheka's close friends and family.

Current Fanfiction Projects
I've got a lot of things that are due for reconstruction - changing NahekaOC's name, tossing my Legomances, and so on and so forth. However, I realize that even keeping up with my recent fanfiction has been a great trouble. I will concentrate on my late works, but looking at the overall schedule, it's not very likely that any reconstruction will begin any time soon.

I most frequently update my homepage. You may not find any fanfiction there, but I'm always drawing my characters, so if you want to hop over sometime, go ahead.

I may start working on another site to store information about my fanfiction. That project's fate is quite unknown as well.
