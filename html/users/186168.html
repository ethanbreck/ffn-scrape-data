After seven years on here, I felt a need to update my hideously-teenaged username and profile. (I was previously known as Cass of the Netherworlds - don't ask.) I haven't updated my Favourites lists on here in most of that time, either, but I obviously wanted to remember those fics when I saved them so I'm sure they're all good reads.

I should apologise at this point for being a very infrequent reviewer; anything I do review, it's because I was utterly struck by it, but that's not to say that I don't enjoy the stories I don't review.

I doubt anyone's even looking at this page, but I would like to thank all the people who spend their time writing stories for people like me to read. I have been creating fanfiction in my head all my life, and reading it for the last seven years or so, and it has given me countless hours of pleasure.
