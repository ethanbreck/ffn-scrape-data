Twenty now, most of my work is from years ago though. Anime/fantasy/sci-fi fan with an itch to write, and a serious bookworm. I have a thing for Bleach and Tsubasa Chronicle manga/series at the moment. My current animes of choice are Naruto, the two from before, Inuyasha, and YuYu Hakusho. Escaflowne has always been an obsession as my first anime, I also read/have read Chobits, Fushigi Yugi, Dragon Knights, Demon Diary, Immortal Rain. Mermaid Saga, .hack//Legend of the Twilight bracelet, Escaflowne's shonen manga,Angelic Layer, Alice 19th, Inuyasha, Saint Tail, Ceres, Mars, xXxHolic, Crescent Moon, Shaolin Sisters, Wolf's Rain, Sailor Moon, and Rayearth. There are several others, but I can't think of ALL of the them!

As for books...I have read my fair share. My favorite books include the Dark Jewels Trilogy, works from Patricia C. Wrede and Tamora Pierce(though they are gettinga bit young for me and I haven't read any ofPierce's Trickster's Choice series), the Pern Chronicles, and...more.

In the gaming arena, I've got mostly RPG's of the popular sort relating to Final Fantasy with a few others that I'm working on. My favorite game is probably FinalFantasy 9, but none of my fics have been posted for that bcs they're horrible. I am working on a Legend of Dragoon fic, but it seems to have stalled and may not get posted either. Have played FF 8, 10, and 10-2, got about half way through seven and dabbled in FF 4. It is safe to assume that I have played the first two .hack games and completed them, but I'll probably be working on the third forever.

If you would like to have me write a specific kind of fic (or continue one I've started, specifically RETURN or HIDDEN SNOW) send me an e-mail subject: Fanfic or something related to that. My inspiration for those got killed I'm afraid, and I'm now writing an Inuyasha fic I may or may not post. All stories placed on hold may be revived in January/February when I return to classes from working full time. I have published an ADULT fanfiction piece on AdultFanFictiondotNet recently, which may yet see more light if my laptop (which holds the next chapter) is ever fixed to the point where my literature may be safely transferred to a more stable system.

I have been doing a lot of beta work and if you would like, send me an email or an IM to see if I'm available.

Enjoy FFN!
