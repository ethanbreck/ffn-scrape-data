NEWS Nov 2019:

Trying to get back in my groove after work swallowed me whole, chewed me up, and spat me out again for a product release.

Look me up @vermaxen on Tumblr. It's just romance reviews, fic announcements, and yet another Enishi/Kaoru shrine. Because we all love a hopeless cause.

Everything is cross posted to AO3 under AngelOfDeath10. All explicit material is also there as FFN doesn't believe in sex.

I write fluff. There's very little content, and a lot of the kind of junk you'd see in any romance novel. I know this. You now know this. So long as you're not coming into this expecting Joyce and getting Harlequin, then we'll get along just fine. Yes, I'm well read. No, my writing does not generally reflect this. I want to have fun. I hope you do too. Humor is a big part of what brings me back to writing romance, because I think a lot of romance is inherently humorous.

Fanfiction was/is an experiment, then a writing exercise, then a way to pass the time for me. I hope things have improved, but sometimes I read my past work and the mortification reverberates so hard it probably knocks birds from the sky. I don't delete old/badfic only because I value keeping myself humble.
