Pename

If you are looking for ElfPilot, you are in the right place. My name is no longer ElfPilot, but I am still the same person :) My new name is CrimsonSnapDragon. If you're wondering why it changed, I have 'outgrown' the old one :)

Fics

PLEASE NOTE: The Odd Couple, Just Friends, Secrets, and Lady of the Rings have all been deleted. I know that many of you are going to be angry with me, but I simply couldn't keep up with the demand that they imposed. Half of them lacked direction, as well; I had no idea where to take those stories, and I simply didn't have the time or motivation to find out. If anyone is truly desperate, I do, of course, have all of the old chapters saved, so let me know if you want to read those. Otherwise, I have three fics left, and I'm not sure if I can even keep up with those.

As the Wheel Turns: I am not sure, yet, what direction this story is headed in, but you can rest assured that it will not be abandoned.

Of Past and Present: This is another fic that presents a challenge to me. It is certainly a struggle to keep the Lord of the Rings charcters in the foreground here while still helping Ondraya to find her path. I am also fighting tooth and nail with her so that she does not become a Mary Sue, LoL ;) My final battle with this fic is revealing her past slowly (so as not to give it all away at once) while still keeping you lovely readers intrigued, LoL :) Perhaps because of all of the challenge that it presents, it is high on my list of priorities. Also, I am contemplating removing the Lord of the Rings characters and adding this story to FictionPress.com as an original, while leaving the LOTR version here. Thoughts?

To Stay or Leave?: My "runaway horse" fic (again, no pun intended), as it started in one direction and then completely spiraled away from me, LoL. I am very happy at all of the great reviews and remarks made on this fic and it is very close to being finished. I will be alternating between this one and Of Past and Present for my next few updates. Please continue with the wonderful feedback :)

Please continue to e-mail, review, praise, constructively criticise, etc... Positive reviews always inspire another update :)
