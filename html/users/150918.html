Author has written 8 stories for StarTrek: Voyager.

Kira's a Voyager writer whose delusions of grandeur and disgust at C/7 led her to rewrite the end of the series in "The Long Road Home" trilogy. The rest, as they say, is history. She writes almost exclusively J/C, but enjoys writing the rest of the cast as well.

When she's not indulging her overactive imagination, she's working on her Masters in microbiology. Aside from Trek, she's also a fan of Veronica Mars, Battlestar Galactica, Lost, and reads far too much Jane Austen for her own good.

From the Slowly But Surely Department: "Lead Me Through the Fire," chapter 6, is currently in progress.
