I'm fated to write.

Admittedly, I don't consider myself a profound writer. I've given in to stupid entertainment too many times to count, and I can only say that I'm proud of one (maybe two?) of my works. Neither are "great," but I stamp my name on them with satisfaction. But hey, writing is my pastime, and as long as I keep breathing, I'll keep on writing. And perhaps one day, I'll conquer the unimaginable and contrive something worthy of that "great" achievement.
