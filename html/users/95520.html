Hi!

I'm not good at these things, but I'm a nurse from Sweden that started reading fics in 2000. Back then it was Backstreet Boys, SeaQuest and Roswell. It evolved to Harry Potter, Lord of the Ring and Queer Eye for the Straight Guy (Fab 5).
