Here are some things you maybe should know about me:

– I am also a chronically obsessed fangirl. I’m a daughter of geeks and possess an anal-retentive, OCD-esque personality. I have ‘Depressing Story-Writing Disorder’ and place Tissue Box Warnings on most of what I write.

– I’ve been on the Pit since 2001. I was eleven at the time, and this is what I must say about the Pit: I value it. It has a lot of problems, and its moniker is well-earned, but I grew up on this site - literally and figuratively. I grew up as a writer here, interacting with other writers to fine-tune my craft. I still have a long way to go, but I am eternally grateful for the help I've received here. For that, I will always respect the Pit.

– You can also find me on deviantART and LiveJournal under variations of the same name. For more frequent updates on my life/fandom insanity, I'm verfound on Tumblr.

– I am still writing. I find not as often as I used to, and sometimes it'll be months between postings. Usually I'm a one-shot, challenge-type writer, but sometimes I post chapter fics.
