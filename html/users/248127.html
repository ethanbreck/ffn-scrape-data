Hey guys! I'm British Chicky, your typical opressed teenage brat who finds pleasure in getting my feelings out through my writing. Oh, and I've recently discovered that I'm a huge over user of "..." I love Harry Potter, Les Miserables, and of course, my cartoons ^_^. Oh, and I'm a hopeless romantic. *sigh* Well, I hope you guys like what I've got here, I'll love you forever if you could take a minute to read and review! Keep it real guys! Oh, and drop me a line sometime, I love talking to people!

Here's a summary on my story progressings, for anyone who cares...

Okay, despite my intentions to go on a bit of a hiatus, I've decided to continue the ATBG story that I've been working on... I can't believe y'all like it! You guys are too nice to me... I want to get a really mean flame that I can just growl at for awhile *grin*... but yeah, fourth chapter coming soon!

I'm in the middle of writing a sequal for "The Lady Doth Protesteth Too Much", due to popular demand (Oooh, I love saying that!) but I'm having trouble thinking of a decent way to tie it all up together... it'll happen someday.

Um, I put up one new ATBG story which was basically an attempt to clear my extreme writer's block, so it's short 'n' sweet, (the best way to write 'em at 11:00 at night with the flu..)

NOTE - All of my original work has been moved to fictionpress.net - I won't express my feelings on this *rolls eyes, but whatever works better, I guess... so if you guys want to see some of my other stuff, that's the place to go... love y'all!
