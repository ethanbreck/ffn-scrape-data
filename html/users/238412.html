Fandoms:

I'm not currently watching any anime, really, except for Naruto. Though I have a lot of series I need to catch up with and finish. Right now I'm really into Avatar: The Last Airbender. I still read the Inuyasha manga and mostly keep up with Naruto.

Pairings:

I do tend to like canon pairings, but sometimes they just...bore me, even if I like them. So while I definitely support Inuyasha/Kagome, and Harry/Ginny, I tend to gravitate towards "forbidden" type pairings because they're more fun for me to read, like Sesshoumaru/Kagome and Ginny/Draco.

It should be noted that A:tLA seems to be an exception to the canon pairing rule. I don't like Zuko/Mai and I don't like Katara/Aang (for some reason Katara/Aang REALLY squicks me out). I don't know why this is. Oh well. :)
