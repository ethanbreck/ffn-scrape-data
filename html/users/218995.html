Author has written 12 stories for Misc. Movies, A Knight's Tale, Lord of the Rings, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Pirates of the Caribbean, Pitch Black / Riddick, Fringe, NCIS, Alice in Wonderland, 2010, and Once Upon a Time in Wonderland.

I'm mostly a reader, but I do like to write when I can stay inspired long enough. I like several fandoms and tend to jump from one to the other simply based on what's new/what I've been obsessed with at the time. Tend to write one-shots/short stories because I'm a procrastinator.

Current obsessions include: Sherlock Holmes, Fringe, Firefly, and House, M.D. I'm also very fond of Pirates of the Caribbean but I can't seem to get the story I've been working on completed. And, I'll pretty much read everything if it's well written and generally stays close to the character's first role. (I've made exceptions to that rule, but they are rare enough that it stays a rule.)

Oh, and if anyone has ever reviewed me... I love you. I may not reply back because I'm lazy, but I love hearing from you readers. Thank you so very much.
