Well, hello to everyone in Fanfiction Land! I have been absent for AGES! (Today's date, by the by, is February 24, 2010!). I JUST logged in tonight and, after reading how many reviews and traffic I've gotten on the "Time Enough For Tears" and the "Intimacy" stories, I've decided to undertake their completion! I'm working on some professional projects right now, but seeing how much everyone liked them goaded me into action.

I also have another Storm/Nightcrawler (RoKu, KuRoRo, whatever you like to call it!) in progress. This one is much longer and will be titled "Faith And Fury."

So please stay tuned!

Thank you for sticking with me all these years at Fanfiction.net, and may the muse gift you often and generously! (If it doesn't, shove some chocolate or caffeine in its face... it will be so spazzed out you'll have to write SOMEthing!)

Cheers!

--Rhi
