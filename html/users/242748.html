Wow it's been a while since I had anything up here. I finally have something up, one poem based on trigun, which is still one of my favorite animes. If you guys like it I'll write more like that, that one was to Knives, though maybe the next one will be to Vash. Enjoy. Anyway I'll give you a brief bio of the princess (who is my original character, do not use her or her land without my permission):

Name: N/A, was forgotten 6000 years
Alias: The princess of donut land, The Angel of Shadow
Past positions: Was a Death in numerous worlds, guided the dead that were in denial of there own death. Acquired the title "angel of shadows" as a teaser from other Angels 3000 years ago when she turned out to be the most ignored Angel. keeps it as a sign of status for reasons unknown. Retired 1000 years ago, still guides souls sometimes.
Current position: The Princess of Donut Land, a planet she created after retiring, is still working on it.
Strengths: Most of her power has been locked due to retiring (she can only access them with permission). Is still stronger than most angels. Has three (now black) wings and a three bladed sword (only she can weild it).
Weaknesess: Cannot interfere much without permission from the big guy.
Domein: Donut Land,a land made comepletely out of Food which does not rot and replaces itself. The Queen is a giant donut, made out of all the flavors in the world. The caste is made out of donuts. The garden is candy. There is a jello-lake (which changes flavors and collors daily), Ice cream Mountains with fudgecycle and popcycle caves. Rivers of milk, lakes of juice and oceans of Soda (none of which are connected). There is also a Hot Dog Park, with all kinds of junk food in it. More will be added soon.
