I am a writer of mainly NC-17 HP fanfiction. I've removed my Weasley Sex God Series from this site, as they are (obviously) meant for a higher rating.

My Weasley Sex God Series so far:

"The Taking of Hermione" - A Bill/Hermione story of non-consent.

"One More Chance" - Ron needed some work on his bedroom techniques. Will Hermione give him another chance?

"Relieving the Tension" - Hermione needs flying lessons to pass her O.W.Ls. Who better to show her how to put a leg over than Fred Weasley?

"A Night in Paris" - Hermione and George Weasley meet up in Paris and sparks fly. Will that night turn into something more? (WiP)

"Percy's Return" - Percy left England after his girlfriend's death. He's returned a different man, one that medical student Hermione Granger wants to know better. (WiP)

I sail on nearly every ship in the HP fandom, but my favorites are D/G, R/H and SS/HG.

Most of my fics are now archived at the GryffindorGroupies Yahoo!group so if you are of age, you may want to check it out. There are many other authors there as well.

Please feel free to drop me a line if you would like to know when I've updated my unfinished stories ('A Night in Paris' and 'Percy's Return') and I will send them to you.

Special thanks to Mabel Weasley, whose stories started my HP writing, and to Taigirl1325, for whom I wrote 'A Night in Paris'.
