Author has written 6 stories for Pride and Prejudice, Robin Hood, Persuasion, Harry Potter, and Mansfield Park.

Second chapters are always harder to write than the first ones.

Challenge results:

Thanks to everyone who gave me ideas of where to go with my next projects. I think that whatever I do will be shorter than a full story, but the two ideas I like the best are:

Hermione is Sorted into Slytherin

Several of Jane Austen's characters together (it will not be what you're expecting, and will probably be very short)

The first chapter of Ambition has just been posted, so I hope you enjoy it. The Jane Austen thing will take a little longer to get going.

A note on Hermione in Slytherin:

I am aware that this is completely against everything Hermione stands for in the series. I'm not looking to rewrite history, but I thought the challenge was interesting, because I love Hermione and I would never want her to change.

Aaaaaaand we're back: Still absolutely (slowly) writing my other active stories, but I had a brainwave and this is it. Mansfield Park is my least favorite Austen novel, but I thought I'd give it the ol' (post) college try. Any suggestions are more than welcome.
