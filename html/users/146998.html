Author has written 8 stories for Hey Arnold, and Secret Show.

My name is Megan, and I'm a 20-something who has loved to read and write her whole life. Writing is an outlet, a stress reliever if you will. If I can't write, I get depressed (or go mad). Even if I tried to stop, I don't know if I could. I also seem to mainly focus on romance stories, even though, if you met me in person, you would never guess me the type to do so. Just a warning ;)

Current Projects:

Brick and Mortar - Arnold's Grandpa Phil dies in accordance to the family curse, leaving himself and his Grandmother to deal with the leftover financial mess. In terrible danger of losing the boarding house to the bank (or worse, Helga's dad), Helga steps in to lend a hand. ON HIATUS Sorry guys! The Writers Block is too big to deal with right now.

FUZZball - Anita Knight is an employee at a simple kids clothing store, but she dreams of bigger things and making a difference. One day she meets a rather oddball man named Victor, and in doing so, throws herself into a life of bizarre characters and sheer chaos. ON HIATUS

Strawberry Seeds - A series of short stories (or "drabbles" or "ficlets" as what seems to be popular at the moment) focusing on Helga, Arnold, and other lovable characters. Added to when inspiration hits, to keep me from getting rusty.

Under Consideration:

All's Faire (Jimmy Neutron) - Jimmy is commissioned to build a life-size working dragon for the Retroville Renaissance Faire. But when Carl and Sheen get their hands on it, will they turn real life into a terrifying fairytale of yore?

Untitled (The Secret Show) Set 20 years after the end of the show. Victor now occupies Changed Daily's old job, and Anita is always a step above. But when the career resurrection of a certain evil villain threatens the world, it's time to dust off the jumpsuits.

Mid-Evil (The Secret Show) What if Doctor Doctor had simply left everyone trapped 500 years in the past? Society has adjusted and life has moved on, but when U.Z.Z. relocates to a castle from that era, things just get weirder.

Untitled (Ducktales) Webby and the triplets aren't kids anymore, and Scrooge is no spring duck himself. But there's still one adventure left in him, and one more legend to profit from.

Please remember that I always appreciate feedback. All writers need it to see what they've done right, and what they need to change. If you've read and enjoyed any of my stories, I thank you very much for taking the time to find them among the thousands of others.

Winner for "Best Rookie Author" and Second Place for "Best One Shot" of the 2005 Hey Arnold Fanfiction Awards - Much love and thanks to those who nominated and voted for me.
