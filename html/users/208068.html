1s = Oneshot. Only one part, and therefore compete.
C = A complete multi-part story. If it doesn't say this, it isn't.
MP = Multi-part story. May not be updated for long stretches at a time.
AU = Alternate universe. Much abusing of characters and/or situations.
OC = Original Character. I swear on my love for my computer that I shall not entertain Mary Sues.

The 'Coversation' pieces are not at all linked to one another, except in that they all follow the format of a dialogue, with little or no description. But they span different series and entirely different genres, from light-hearted to metaphysical, so don't think you have to read them together.
