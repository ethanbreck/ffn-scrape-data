Author has written 12 stories for Invader Zim, Gravitation, Sonic the Hedgehog, Ninja Turtles, Trigun, Metalocalypse, A Bugs Life, and Pokémon.

As you can probably tell from looking at some of my submissions, I do write some male/male romance stories. I get that it's not everyone's cup of tea, but I would like to kindly ask that if you're not into this kind of writing, please don't leave me comments telling me how wrong/disgusting/etc. the pairing in question is. Remember: to each their own. :)

Also, as a small aside, I intend to eventually start re-writing all my older stories here. As of February 2017, I re-wrote and re-uploaded "Benevolent Blizzard," now known as "Truce in a Tempest." By far, that one is my most popular story and I was so happy to see that it was so well-received by new and past readers alike. I don't know when I'll re-write my stories since it's going to be particularly time-consuming for my longer ones, but it is something I'd like to try and do because, compared to now, I was an awful writer back then.
