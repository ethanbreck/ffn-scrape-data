Hey Ya Folks!

I'm T.Q.O, otherwise known as "The Quiet Observer" My Pen Name kinda gives you an idea of my personality.
I'm a long time resident of the sunny south. (USA) When I'm not working (which isn't often) I like to relax with a good Fic.
Eventually I may actually post some of my modest attempts at fan fiction. My main interests are drama and sci-fi. Some current fav's are: NCIS, Harry Potter, SG1 , Atlantis, Buffy, Angel, and any Trek series.
I know how Author's love feedback, so I try to send out the occasional review. If you get one from me, please note that it is meant as just that, a REVIEW or COMMENT. I do not Flame, but I may put in my 2 cents!
Until Next Time:
