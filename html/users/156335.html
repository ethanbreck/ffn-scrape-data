Greetings Shiny Person Who Is Reading This,

Currently I'm writing mostly Doctor Who & Hunger Games fics and Transformers for my friends. The latter all at the Botosphere. You can find me on the Jedi Council Boards under SpiritofEowyn as well. Fratricide is completed over on those forums.

I follow and write the following 'ships: Doctor/Rose, Eowyn/Faramir Piper/Leo, Mary-Lynnette/Ash Minako/Kunzite Helga/Arnold and most recently Peeta/Katniss.

I hate slash,yaoi, and anything else that takes characters into gross situations and makes them do things that their characters would never do. (If the relationship is canon then I don't consider it in these categories. I'm talking about the absurd 'ships of changing the sexuality of characters or making moral characters immoral with no explanation of what great trauma changed them.) I hate explicit stuff too, so you won't find me writing any of that either. Sorry. I consider romance to be two characters caring about each others well being and attraction, not just lust. I love the Back to the Future trilogy and time travel in general. I tolerate the Twilight series. Bella annoys me and I think Edward is controlling. I saw Catching Fire three times in a few weeks and read the trilogy in a week while sharing the book with someone else reading it and working full time.

I love reviews whether they are good or bad, as long as they can help me improve my writing so I can become the best author I can be someday when I try to get published. My icon is a self portrait I did years ago, so that is what I look like.

Friend Promotion

If you like Harry Potter fics I have to recommend my best friend Kateydidnt. She rocks. She's also apparently really popular. Her stories are translated into about 4 different languages. I also recommend Eowyn77 and DarthIshtar my other writing buddies who write Transformers, HP, and Star Wars.

If you like Transformers: head to the Botosphere where my lovely best friends and I collab.

Sincerely,

Spirit of Eowyn
