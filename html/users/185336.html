Least Favorite Ship:

Lukewarm Ship:

I have Malfoy men syndrome. :)

However, my thoughts took a wicked turn when I read a review somewhere along the lines: ‘Move over Harry Potter….’ Ahhh Hell hath no fury than a HP Lover scorned. I thought that was a greatest insult comparing the writings of Meyer to JKR. Pfft.
