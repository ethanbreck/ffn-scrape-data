I haven't posted a story in quite some time.

I was planning to about a year ago, except I wanted to have it finished before the posting began.

Then my jump drive disappeared.

If I can do something as good as I had... then it may yet appear on this site.

My muse and I have been on the outs with one another since its loss.

I hope to make something worthy one day soon.

Update 2012: I think I will have a Roswell marathon in May/June. If I do then I am planning on fixing and finishing 'Don't Have One at the Moment'. I have looked at it and will fix what I have, but need to re-invest myself into the series (since BSG, Twilight, and Harry Potter have consumed me since Roswell). This way I can write the characters correctly and whatever plot line I was thinking of ten years ago may resurface or who knows, maybe I'll think of a better one. Quite frankly I think what I have up now is horrible, but considering I was 14/15 when I did that, I will excuse myself temporarily. Either I will fix it/finish it or it will just be removed. Only time will tell.
