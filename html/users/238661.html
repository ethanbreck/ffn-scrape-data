Heya! I'm a 16 year old girlie from London, who loves fanfiction!

I love writing...even though I'm not very good!!!

I'm a cartoon freak who hasn't really grown up. I love Fairly Odd Parents and Invader Zim, quite pathetic really. I'm a bit of a quirky character who takes her weirdness out in her writing (hence my oddball stories).

My fanfiction is all FOP because I'm not as madly into any other show or movie, but that's just for now. I might have a sudden character flip and start writing about Clifford The Big Red Dog.

It's not likely, but you never know.

To those of you who take the time out to review my stories, oh how I love you. If I knew you in reality I would give you doughnuts and lemonade.
