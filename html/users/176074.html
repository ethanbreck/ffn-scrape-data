Author has written 14 stories for Harry Potter, Newsies, and Whisper of the Heart.

"I'm a writer, that's what I do." I can not help but write. I write everything. From poems about romance to fan fictions to random science fiction stuff about another world that an old friend of mine made up. Yay! Writing is fun!

I love Pirates, Fiddler on the Roof, Harry Potter, Newsies, Young Hercules, as well as more I'm sure!

My real name is Jenny but I also go by Ace.

2008 was such a long time ago! Holy Cow it has been a long time. I have been really busy but have not stopped writing! I have managed to find some free time (I'm not really sure how!?) lately and have been putting the finishing touches on a chapter for the Fred and Angelina story. I've got a chapter or two in my notebook for Whisper of the Heart too. I've also come up with an awesome idea that I really, really want to begin writing but told myself that I had to work on Bits and Pieces first. I hope that you will take a look at my old stuff in the mean time and I hope to have something new up very soon. So fear not faithful readers, I am still out there and alive and sort of well at the moment. I will post things as soon as I finish them.

Got any ideas? Feel free to send them my way! I always have a few running around in the way back of my head but more is always appreciated!

So read my stories, give me comments, good or bad, and enjoy reading what I have posted. Thank you for reading my profile/story(ies)! Have fun reading and/or writing!

Updated: April 28, 2011

PS. There is always a method to my madness. Although sometimes I'm not sure quite what it is.
