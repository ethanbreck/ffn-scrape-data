My name is Elena, obviously. I discovered fanfiction around around the turn of the century. Fifteen years later, and I feel really old. I originally wrote Dragon Ball Z and Star Trek fanfiction, before realizing that it was crap and deleting it. I was then sucked into the Star Wars fandom, and never quite recovered. Thankfully, however, my new stories are a lot better than my old stories.

At least, that's what people tell me. Could they be lying? Could it all be untruthful flattery? I don't think I want to know...

Anyway, I write primarily Star Wars Expanded Universe (SWEU) fanfic. That's mostly the books set after RotJ and the pre-AotC games KotOR and KotOR II. At least, I did. Since the end of the New Jedi Order (NJO), the Dark Nest Trilogy (DN3), and the Legacy of the Force (LotF) nine-book cycle, I have been less than impressed with the EU's post-modernist take. I read and write Star Wars as harmless escapism. At this current point in time, I am searching for an escape from my escapism.

No, the irony does not escape me.

Now I write - when I write at all - anything from Naruto to Bleach to Fairy Tail. I really depends on what inspires me at the time.

Not much else to say about me, other than the fact that I have no discernable schedule. Sorry, but my updates are sporadic at best, and that's saying something.
