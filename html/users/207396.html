well, I'm currently working on an internet-site (through Tripod) for this fan fic of mine, Rude Awakenings. maybe I'll make one for my other fics too, but I'm not sure about that. At the moment I have two galleries, one on side7 ( I think, but don't quote me on that either!) and deviantart ( I also have four neopets! (spreading the love!) and, as you can read in my fics, an overactive imagination. but that's not bad! Hell no! that's a good thing! Why be dull, right! right? anyone? hello?

okay, then, about me... ehm...I'm currently not going to college. got my Havo-diploma ('s means I have finished high school) and am momentarily recovering from making the wrong choice by going to art school. shouldn't have done that, though I think it was clarifying something to me. mainly that drawing should be a hobby for me, and not a profession. am now concidering psychology, or going abroad for a year. maybe work in a hotel for a year or so. that would look good in my curriculum vitae (silly word, innit?). well, I'm not sure about that yet. what I am sure about, though, is that Rude Awakenings is a project thatI'm going to finish, even if Hell freezes over and the sky falls down! ( I'm usually a bit sloppy, with a lot of work that I will probably never finish, and I don't really like that about myself)

Ow, and another thing. call me crazy, but I'm actually working on a complete Alan Rickman video/DVD collection (yes, I know, not enough hobbies!), which I'm hoping to complete one day. call me crazy, but once I started, I just couldn't stop. I only have about eleven of his movies so far, but I'll get more over time. after all, I'm only eightteen currently, so I have all the time I need.

Well, that was it for now! damn I talk too much! LOL! anywayz, leave reviews! make me feel... joyful! yeah, that's it, joyful! gleeful even! happiness is mine! (insane? me? nope. lies, all lies!)

P.S. Am currently working on a series with Denethor in it. Uncanon, yes, but fun! please tell me what you think.
