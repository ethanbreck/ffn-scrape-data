Author has written 13 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Yu-Gi-Oh.

September 12, 2015

Man, it has been so long since I kept up with Fanfiction.net. It's crazy! So many of the writers that were active when I started have all but deleted their accounts; Yu-Gi-Oh! has five seasons and multiple iterations (when I started, there was about a season and a half total); I was in middle school. Now, I'm going to graduate from law school in a few months.

So of course I decide to finally finish The Game of Seduction. I've written about 2,000 words into the final chapter, but I can tell there's a long way to go. If anyone reads this and would be interested in acting as a beta reader, PM me. It's been awhile, and I want the story to end with a bang, not a fizzle!

Otherwise, feel free to check out some excerpts and random bits of fluff at my Tumblr. Just search for Jack V. Briefs.

And, it feels good to be back. :)

Ja ne for now!

JVB
