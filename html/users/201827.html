What's up? I'm T.M.R. otherwise known as Sword and T.J. Infinat! Anyways I have things I have to do so let's get down to buisiness.

BIO (HAZARD) Name: T.J. Infinat / Sword / T.M.R.

Age: I'm a teenager. I'll tell you that much.

Gender: Depends on what time, life, and situation you find me in!

Personalities: 5 for now. . .

Interests: Anything! I'm flexible, and I'm not afraid to speakup, so if you suggest something, I'll try it, unless I have good reason not to.

Favs.: A lot of things, no personal favorites.

Talents: Artistic, literature, athletic, ability to read emotions (Can tell if your happy or not.) Good anger surpresser, quick learner, instrumental. Now, for my intro fic.

T.J. sits on a bench in a park one day, typing fanfiction on a lap top. The lap top suddenly desides to save and shutdown his fic and shut itself down. "WHY!" T.J. yells into the sky as he shakes his hands and allows his glasses to fall off. He looks down as he is about to turn his laptop back on and is suddenly pulled up into a graceful hug. "What?" T.J. asked. He looks up to find none other than. . . .Chelsea! "What are you doing here?" T.J. asked. "You supposed to be in my mind like my other personalities!"

"Chill!" Chelsea exclaimed as she looked into the rocker's eyes. "We've come to give you a new life. The life of a Fanfic writer! You've got the resources." She pointed to the laptop. "Now, lets give you a look." 5 minutes later. . . . T.J. has pointy dreadlocks to the small of his back, is wearing headphones that wrap around the back of his head, and some glasses that are thiner than the ones he had before. "Yes!" Chelsea exclaimed as she jumped in the air. 'She has big knockers.' T.J. thought. After that, he mentally slapped himself.

He could hear the other 4 personalities laughing at him. "Now, to give you the thing that all authors have!" "A phony Narrorator?" T.J. aked his female counterpart. "No, the powers!" Cherlsea then gave him a list of powers." "1." T.J. read. "The Plot Hole. . . The ability to pull objects out of seemingly no where." T.J. looked up a Chelsea, confused. Chelsea then proceeded to demonstrate. She pulled out a big boken, some gasaline, and a match.

She then proceeded to light the sword on fire. She gave it to T.J. "2. The ability to do anything, Action wise, to contridict physics, to delay times. . . . . You know, you are Neo from the Matrix" she stared down on T. J. as he felt the power course through him.

"Feels good," she whispered. "Doesn't it?" T. J. stepped to the side. "To write," he stated, right before he pulled out a Dell(TM) Laptop . . . .
