I'm nineteen and I go to college at the University of Oregon, but Reno is my real home! I miss it there, and yet I can't help but love it here. I love BtVS, Lord of the Rings, Stargate SG-1, and - obviously - Harry Potter. I'm experimenting/working on fics for all of them. If you know me at all, though, you know that nothing will be done for a very long time. Lily-bug lives right across the hall, and Comedia is still in New York. I hate them both. Nah, I'm joking. I love them both and desperately wish they would get lives because they are whores. Just like me! Heeheehee...

I have two guardians (aka muses). A tall, dark, and mysterious man named Gabriel who's melancholy a lot and is an artist in the truest sense of the word, and a rambunctious British girl named Molly who seems to be in control of most of my irrational outbursts (meaning nearly my entire personality).

Here's to laughter in the shadows. Slytherins Unite!

~chrestomanci
