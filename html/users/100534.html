Bio literally means life. How am I supposed to fit my entire life in this small space I am given on the net?

This hasn't been updated in forever and I feel I have a duty to finish it.

My name is Heather, I'm an art student studying Digital Art and concept art. I'm 24 years old, and I write a lot of fic for video game fandoms! I used to be really into anime and the like, but I've mostly fallen out of that. (I still love One Piece.) I love theatre and musicals, and consider myself a pretty good source for movie trivia. I live in a nice apartment with my fiancee and my Pembroke Corgi/Shiba Inu mix, Leo. In case you couldn't tell, I'm also a total geek. I write a lot of original fiction, none of which I'll be posting here (one, because of the lack of relevancy, and two, because if it's posted online I may have trouble publishing it later).

I also love to RP and do most of my writing there. I very rarely write on my own any more, which is why I feel like there's hardly anything here. Not a day goes by where I don't write something -- but unfortunately since it's all collaborative, I can't post it here.

My all-time favorite character from anything ever is a toss-up between Drebin from Metal Gear Solid 4 and Albert Wesker from Resident Evil. Duke it out, you two. (Oh, and then there's Portgas D. Ace. He'll just light them both on fire and be done with it. Apparently Wesker's pretty weak to that strategy, anyway.)

I'm real easy to talk to and like to make new friends, so if you find me interesting, drop me a line!

ALSO, if you're going to favorite my work, PLEASE COMMENT ON IT. It makes me really sad to get favorites and no comments. ):
