Hello, hello. Kathleen here, on extended vacation to the realms of hockey goodness. You may know me as a person amazingly fond of cliffhangers, irregular in my posting, and prone to obsessions that swing weekly, monthly, even daily.

If you know me as that last, obsessed person, you might be sad to know that nothing I am obsessed with now can or will be posted at fanfiction.net. I have married into the ranks of those thrown into disarray by the sport fiction ban that this... darling site has arranged. I am now, first and foremost, a hockey writer. Sorry all.

Eventually, I'm sure, these fics will be updated. I'm slowly working on them, but my passion is in pretty boys with accents, curls, and rosy cheeks. Who's name is... Ales. Hemsky. God, the prettyness.

"First you take an Ales and then you take a Jiri and you put them both together in a pot.

Add a package of Kraft Dinner and a pot of paint thinner, but you need no fire 'cause damn these boys are hot.

Then you cover them in chocolate and wonder where the plot is and the prettiness starts driving you insane.

And since this is a teaser I won't give you any more.

The end!"

For anyone who found the contents in the above interesting, you might want to check out my site. *g* I wouldn't suggest it if you only plan to flame me for being sick. I'll only laugh my ass off at you. My wife, Robyn, is sick. Frala is sick. Mae is sick. We're all sick, and it's a damn good life.

Thanks for your time, see you on the other side.
