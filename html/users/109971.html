Author has written 7 stories for Dark Angel, Star Wars, and Twilight.

The pen name says it all. I am the female version of Walter Mitty. My imagination never stops, and my ties to reality aren't always that strong. :) I seriously spend my day daydreaming my way through work, and on my way back from bathroom breaks, I have been known to peek around corners while running the Mission Impossible theme silently through my head, lol. (The hard part is making sure that no one catches me doing that because they just wouldn't understand. They're used to me being me, but I'm sure that sometimes they want to have me committed.)

At present, it appears that my fanfic writing muse has deserted me, but you never know when that might change unexpectedly . . .
