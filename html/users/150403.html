So, I'm Summer, I'm 20, and I'm a junior at NAU. Trying to get my BA in English (I will end up in the same boat as Princeton from Avenue Q in two years. I know it.) Thinking about being a librarian. Working for a publisher. Don't really know. All I really want to do is write, but I don't think I can make a living doing that.

Things I love: The Office, Avenue Q, Ben Folds, Scrubs,Wicked, Jimmy Eat World, Motion City Soundtrack, books by Tiffanie DeBartolo (you should look her up!), reading, writing, crocheting, knitting...all very interesting I know.

My favorite canon couples (for the Office) are Ryan/Kelly (they WILL end up married with six children. I just know it.) and Dwangela. Because those two are great. I love Jim and Pam on the show, but I don't really actively look up Jam stuff. I dunno, now that they're happy and dating and all that, it just feels like there's not a lot left to do with them fanfic-wise. Also, there's a million Jam fics, and I tend to like the underrated ones.

Non-canon for TO: Kelly/Toby, Dwight/Jim, (i've read some really really awesome fics for both that just made me love the idea of them. I will find links. Promise.)

For some reason I don't like Karen/Ryan or Pam/Ryan. I'll take Pam/Karen though. I don't know why.

Guilty Pleasures: Fanfiction, Smoking (I really should quit), Chocolate ice cream with brownies (so bad, and yet so good), good Chinese food, wasting time on Youtube. Facebook applications. And Solitaire.

I seriously hadn't written fanfiction in like four years until I started Wednesday's Child. Which is sad. I've been working on my regular fiction, however, so that's why I haven't been around.

To be honest the fics I have still in progress are probably going to stay that way, sorry. When I have something new to post, you'll be the first to know. :D
