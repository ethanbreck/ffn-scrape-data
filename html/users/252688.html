The reason I chose this name was due to the fact that a conglomerate of people wished to publish a story we had been working on. That's dead now, but I'll keep the name since it isn't a stock name others have a lot of connection too.

I have a full-time job now as a speech pathologist at the local hospital, so it does make it difficult to continue to write. I promise, I am adding at least one paragraph a day/minimum of three paragraphs a week. I also am talking with CharMoonRaven consistently online to work on further Saiyuki/Gundam Wing/Yu Yu Hakusho stories.

I have a question for my readers too...do you think the language involved in some of these stories is a little...complex?

Also, if you want to use any of my ideas for stories, please EMAIL ME and ASK FIRST!! As long as you give me credit, I (or my co-authors) tend to not care. HOWEVER, these scenes are UNIQUE and something WE WORKED HARD TO CREATE. Please be considerate.
