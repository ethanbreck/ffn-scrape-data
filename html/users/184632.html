Inuyasha

Just for fun I'll list all the stories/poems that are posted:

Fanfiction:

1. I'm With You- Well...it's written by me. It's another Spirited Away fic about, what else, Haku coming back... this time its nine years later and Chihiro can't quite remember everything, until she sees him again...It's a song fic based on the song 'I'm With You' by Avril Lavigne. It's a one-shot.

2. The Other Side of FF9- not under my pen name...my strange little brother's...THEMISCHIEVOUSBOY... Well it's about...the other side of FF9. The authors (My brother, sister and I) are taking the readers on tour through Gaia and are finding out, well, many, many strange things about the characters.

3. Nah- Another song-fic, This time it's about Inuyasha and Kagome. It's one-shot and AU. Inuyasha breaks up with Kagome...

4. YYH Rewritten: Kurama- DELETED E-Mail me if you really want me to try again.

6. I'm Not That Girl- Kagome has a huge crush on Inuyasha... secretly (or maybe not so secret) he likes her too, but he's seeing Kikyou. What happens when...? A song-fic based on the song, 'I'm Not That Girl' from the broadway musical, 'Wicked' One-Shot

Original fiction (FictionPress)

1. SummerTime- (My brother and Sister helped on this one too) Basically, five kids (four girls and a boy) travel through time...and end up in the future, and find, well, chaos. Currently: They just went on vacation...

2. What About THEM?- A poem that I wrote a couple years ago after I saw a picture of a starving little boy... It's about how no one seems to notice the people that really need our help, becaused we're too concerned about ourselves...It asks everyone to help out those less fortunate...

3. Gen's Story- It's a short story that was written for a contest (it wasn't finished in time to enter it though)...The main character, Gen, is telling her daughter, Ashley, about her life... her parents demise, her husbands comforting help...It's kinda sad though...how well do you think it would've done? (honesty is greatly appreciated, just tell me why you don't like it, if you don't)

4. Right Now, At This Moment- Another poem. This one is about a disabled sailor, who is at sea for the last time. It has a sad ending, but I think it's pretty good!

5. Abandon (Working Title)- It's about a werewolf, in the 19th Century, who, while fighting his greatest enemy is spelled into a sleep that lasts until the 21st century (2003, to be exact). It's only co-written by me.

Not Posted Yet:

1. Fate and Destiny (Working Title)- The Bentley twins, Amanda and Charolette, have a special job... they are Fate and Destiny... it's told from Amanda's (Fate's) POV.

2. Kyriin and Lymaan's Tale (Working Title)- Kyriin and Lymaan Lyaac are twins and they are out traveling for the adventure of their lives.

3. Rock Me to Sleep (Mother)- Another song-fic. Featuringthree anime (Rurouni Kenshin~ featuring Kenji and his mother; Inuyasha~ kinda obvious it's Inuyasha and his mother; and Yu Yu Hakukusho~ featuring Kurama and Shiori), it will be multiple chapters to feature each character and their mother. RK, will take place shortly after 'Reflections, it will contain a majorspoiler from it, so if you don't know, I would suggest not reading that part of the story, unless you like a spoiler. IY will simply be Inuyasha visiting his mother's grave once more and reminiscing. YYH will be more future-like, Shiori died at a good old age, but Kurama is still sad about it.

Well there's my stuff... so far... Tell me what you think!

I'll try to update this everytime I update or add something too!
