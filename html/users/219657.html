Mnemosyne's Lock Storylines
Lately I've been writing a lot of stories regarding people with memory loss; hence I’ve decided to create this particular corner to accommodate them. Due to any particular sort of accident (and it will always be an accident) whatever character be it male or female will suffer partial or memory loss. If you don’t know Mnemosyne was the personification of memories in Greek mythology and the mother of the nine muses. The above title, shameless as I am, was stolen from a writer (VR Trakowski forgive me!!) in the Iron Man fandom (this story is extremely good so I recommend you go read it if you like Iron Man fics, Pepperony in particular).
