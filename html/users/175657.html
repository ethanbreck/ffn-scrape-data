EDIT: July 2nd 2015

I haven't been active on here in a long time (like, several years. I'm 28 now), but I wanted to thank all of you for reading. The fact that these fics are fairly popular even after all this time means a lot to me. I might someday come back to these, but for now I'm just going to leave them up for you to enjoy (or hate!).

If you would like more recent activities from me, follow me on tumblr under "cuttleflesh"

I love you, I love you, I love you and whatever you do in life, even writing shitty fics like me, you MATTER. Your writing matters. And never ever stop doing it.

Have the best day ever,

-Sevlow

So... Currently I'm 23/f/California, but any of those are bound to change without notice.

I enjoy reading, writing, and visual art. I suck at all three, but enjoy them nonetheless.

I have no pride to shatter.

I tend to list toward the darker side of fanfiction, so please note that your skin is bound to crawl at one point or another if you choose to read my stuff. I rather enjoy gore and angst. I'm currently obsessed with the Fullmetal Alchemist fandom and I don't see any end in sight.

Roy Angst = love.
