One Last Note...

Since I joined ff-net in 2002, I've always loved the humor genre, so the majority of my stories are humor or it's a minor theme. When I was younger that's all I would write--every time I tried writing something serious it turned out awkward, or I'd want to put a funny scene in it. But as I've gotten older I've thought of a few more serious plotlines; I like action and adventure too so nowadays I'm trying to incorporate that. Though of course because of my sparkling personality I can't help it if there's a dash of light-heartedness too :)

Chapter-wise, I'm one of those writers that tend to write a lot. So my chapters do tend to be long. But I try keeping it interesting! I just have a lot I want to express and mention... ;; I've been told that my writing style is more like watching film with words rather than reading a book. That if someone were to animate my stories it would barely need to be edited for the storyboard because the actions are already there and explicit and the dialogue well-written for speech, like a movie, which I think has to do with starting out writing in script-form.

But anyway, I've always written for myself and for the love of writing, both as an outlet and to bring alive the various shades of my soul (cheesy). But really it makes me so happy and proud to see pages and pages of words and paragraphs and dialogue on the screen, and knowing that my lil' brain came up with it! So it's a major awesome bonus & blessing if other people read and like my stuff too.

So like I said on March 15, 2002 at the end of We're In Hyrule, stupid!'s first chapter:

"I hope you all love it!! I DON'T CARE IF YOU HATE IT!! I WILL WRITE MORE AND MORE AND MORE AND MORE AND MORE, well, you get the picture."

LOL so thank you in advance to all those that read my fics. I really, truly appreciate every single review and favorite. Thank you!
