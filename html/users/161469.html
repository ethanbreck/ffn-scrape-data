hide bio
PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 01-27-02, id: 161469, Profile Updated: 09-27-11

Who am I? I wish I could answer properly. I am a person just like any other on the planet. One that has many dreams and high aspirations but sadly because of the way she thinks; they'll never be realized. One of the few things that I enjoy about myself is that I can be creative in different ways. Whether I pick up a simple pencil to draw on a white piece of paper or sit down at the computer to type out worlds of fiction - I never once have not found a way to express what I think or feel. I realize often that there are people out there that can do and achieve more than I could ever hope. But over time I have come to terms that what I do in itself is pretty amazing. So in a way I make myself happy. I'm a dreamer. A romantic. A young girl at heart and definitely in soul. I'm also an actor - I can put on different faces for any person. Whatever it is that I think that you'll want to see - I'll find a way and show it to you. Very few know who I truly am. Rarely, have I shown the real me to someone. I also love to write and photo manipulate. One of my biggest dreams is to have one of my fanworks published somewhere. Do you know how wonderful that would be for me? To be able to see my name printed on a book cover or my artwork showcased in a magazine? Only. . .that's what I wish could happen. A something that will probably never happen. So who in reality is Lady-sama? Me. A part of me. One of me. A name. A random face. No one special.
