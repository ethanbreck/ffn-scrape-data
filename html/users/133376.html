Currently in the works (the following are going to be posted on FictionPress since FanFiction no longer posts original fiction, but I think I'll probably still think up stories that aren't original fiction. Maybe a Tikku (Tidus/Rikku) since the idea's been nagging at me):

Summary: He’s running from his past. She’s trying to avoid her future. What happens when the two meet? The present may seem normal, but hidden beneath the everyday lives of the human race, chaos rules and we are powerless to stop it. Then again… who said we were all human?

-- Of Sunsets and Sunrises (Original Fic): I started it a while back, but never really got around to finishing it. And when the idea for "Apparitions of Infinity" came to me, this was sort of left at the back of my head. However, I do intend on completing the story.
Summary:
“I can help you find Hikari…” .

“What…?” Jin asked. His eyes widened for a moment, before closing shut tightly as he shook his head once more. ‘First she looks exactly like my dead mom and now she says she can find my missing sister… great’ he added mentally. “… You can’t. She’s been missing for over 3 years. No one’s seen her since then”

“That’s because you’ve all been looking in the wrong places. She’s not here.”

“What are you talking about? Of course she’s not here, she’s dead. There were at least hundreds of rescue workers looking for her; they never even found a trace”

His mother-look-alike crossed her arms and let out an exasperated sigh. “I mean she’s not here, in this world. She’s in another time, an ancient world known only as Endiness. And Hikari didn’t just disappear, she was kidnapped…”

... A young man sets off on a journey to find his little sister, in a world where past, present, and future collide. Cold and solitary by nature, Tetsuya Jun-Jin is sure the key to survival is on his side. But what will he do when he meets his exact opposite, Akemi Oteyo? And what business does she have in Endiness?
