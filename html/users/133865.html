Author has written 1 story for Detective Conan/Case Closed, and Persona Series.

Call me Mi-chan or Miaka. I'm currently obsessed with Meitantei Conan (Detective Conan/Case Closed) and stories of that universe. Satou-keiji and Takagi-keiji are my FAVORITE characters. There are also a few other series or games I adore, but DC is the one you'll most likely see me writing for. I am hoping to have one or two shorter stories up sometime soon, and I have a much longer fic that I'm trying to get written. It's taking forever, though, especially as I keep deciding that this or that piece needs to be reworked...

The only story I'm working on at the moment is The Case of the Midnight Channel. It's going to be pretty long, as I've got a good 7 weeks or more of plotted-out timeline. It's a pretty major work-in-progress, actually. Research and writing, and coming up with the various personal Shadows for the DC/MK characters...

I do have a couple other stories that I have plans for. 'In Pursuit of Justice' is a DC story without crossovers, somewhat focused on Ayumi-chan. 'Tokyo Investigations: The Vanished Detective' is effectively the reverse of CotMC, as it involves dumping Naoto into Teitan High to figure out what happened to Shinichi. Both of those are on hold until I figure out how to write cases like you'd find in an episode of Conan.

A little more about me:

I am a happily married (though sadly childless) 30-something female. My husband is a much better writer than I am, though he doesn't seem to actually believe that.

I love pretty much all things cute, especially bears and kittens.

I enjoy reading, gaming, and a number of craft-based hobbies: baking, crochet, drawing, and sewing.

Sometimes I have some odd headcanon. Like that Ran is a fan of Cardcaptor Sakura... which oddly enough the manga doesn't contradict, and might even support...

I suppose that's all I can really think of to say at the moment.
