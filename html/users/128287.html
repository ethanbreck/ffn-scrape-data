Well what can i say, i love writing. I have a thousand different ideas running around in my little brain. Being a 22 year old stay at home mom to five dogs and three cats gives me a lot of time to write, and read, and watch Firefly...

Any way, my main kick is harry potter, though i am pretty well rounded... except when the muse kills off one idea, it is off for a while. I am currently starting up a Firefly fic... No name as of yet, but lots of shiny work to come. My msn name is Syndaly@hotmail.com if you want to talk, feel free to message me.

My favorite quote from Laurell K Hamilton. it's in her book obsidian Butterfly and it is from Anita Blake's point of view.

I shook my head hard enough that my hair lashed my face. i literally threw my hands in the air. "Fk it, for now. i didn't get on a plane at a moments notice to stand in the heat and discuss your love life. don't we have a crime to solve or something?"

"We could discuss your love life," he said. "Werewolf or vampire, which are you Fking now?" there was something close to bitterness in this voice. It wasn't jealousy, but utter disapproval. you killed monsters. you did not date them.

That was one of Edward's rules, and used to be one of mine. Just another example of my moral down fall.

he he, if you want more, read the series. but beware, crime scenes in detail and sex is abound in these books. and you can not write fanfiction on them either. that sucks the most. but i respect it.

one final thought i leave you with is: if you find my kitchen a mess and brownies on the stove uneaten late at night, then don't come to my room... oh, and i wouldn't eat the brownies either.
