03/15/2008

Name: Elainor
Age: 19
Residence: United States, in a house, still with mom and dad! I swear I'm not a loser who lives in the basement. We don't have a basement.

Okay. Another three years have gone by, and I'm now 19. I am finally ready to delete my worst work on this site. [Risk, December 8 2002, about the "persecution" of Christians. I did not delete it before because I thought it would be more mature to admit to having done something silly, instead of trying to cover it up. But it needs to come down now. As I re-read that poem tonight, I literally cringed over its sickeningly false self-righteousness and attitude of not only unnecessary defensiveness, but an uncalled-for attack. Thank you to those who commented in a critical way. You were right.

I love being nineteen. I love coming back to ff.net, looking at my stupid 13-year-old self, and being glad I've gotten older. When I'm 22, I'll undoubtedly be back, with a harsh criticism of my mid and late teenage years as well.

[C 2005

Oh dear.

My profile, after nearly three years, truly needs an update. Therefore, I have deleted all the crp concerning the personal details of my thirteen-year-old self and her life. I am no longer thirteen. That should be obvious.
