4/5/2012

So I see it's been ten years since I've updated my profile, and I have to admit, I never thought I'd be back writing on this site since my teenage Harry Potter years! I definitely read fanfic during that lapse, but I haven't written in a long time, as you can see by the dates. The Harry Potter stuff was 2002 and beforehand.

Fun stuff: I'm in my 20s, and about to finish grad school. I hadn't written fanfic in a decade, but Skyword Sword's potential with Ghirahim kept me up with plot bunnies, the first of which I finally exorcised.

Confession: There are two fictional characters that have turned me into a raging fangirl. The first was Severus Snape, even before Alan Rickman. That hit at the appropriate age of 14.

Then there was the Demon Lord Ghirahim... Sexy, sexy Ghirahim. And so here I am!

I got asked once by a reviewer how I could ship GhiraLink and GhiraFi at the same time, and I was a little surprised by the question, but once I thought about it, I realized I took a lot of my thoughts on the two ships for granted.

I see GhiraFi and GhiraLink as two completely different types of ships. My GhiraLink preferences feature stories that are darker, with the passion being based on obsession and lust, with some underlying BDSM themes. I don't see Ghirahim capable of being “in love” with Link, really, or them having any kind of traditionally healthy relationship.

In contrast, I see GhiraFi as having potential for something a bit more pure. I think Ghirahim could easily see Fi as the closest thing to an equal, an entity he can admire, both for her own attributes, as well as for the qualities in himself that she mirrors by virtue of being a sword spirit who inhabits a beautiful, powerful sword. I see a lot of his potential affection for her as an almost Narcissus-like type of fondness. I think he could be capable of approaching something like love with her.

Favorite Zelda Ships: GhiraFi, GhiraLink, Sheik/Malon, MaLink, and I love good Sheik and Scrapper stories (mutually exclusive Sheik and Scrapper stories).

I guess that's all for now. (Reviews fill my heart with rainbows!)
