About My Stories:

Love Thy Enemy
I have to admit, my favorite type of fic is Hermione/Draco fics. I wanted to write one, but I didn't want to be cliche or unrealistic- I wanted mine to be as close to what would really happen as possible. I've used a few events from my own life to try and create the situation.

I Loved Lord Voldemort
After reading Half Blood Prince, I noticed that Tom Riddle was many times referred to as handsome. If Dumbledore thought he was handsome, maybe there was a girl that felt the same. I also began to see certain parallels between Tom and a boy I know (which is kind of scary in a way), and I was inspired to write this story. I've been working really hard on it, and it's my pride and joy right now.

My C2:

Ulitmate Harry Potter FanFiction
I wanted a place where I felt like I could personally honor all the really amazing HP fics out there, whether they have thousands of reviews or none. I get sick of weeding through all the really bad fics out there, so I wanted to create a place where there were only good fics. I accept all sorts of fics- romances, mysteries, parodies, and all pairings, just as long as it's well written. If you know of a good fic, yours or someone else's, let me know and I'll take a look at it. Also, please feel free to subscribe to it- I'd love your support!

About Me:

Hi! I'm 17 years old and I live in New York. I'm completely obsessed with Harry Potter, and that's what kind of fanfiction I write. Besides that, I like to sing, dance, act, and listen to music like Billy Gilman, Clay Aiken, Will Young, and broadway. I once wrote something called "Tips to Creating A Successful Harry Potter FanFiction." However, I seem to have lost it over the years. If I find it or decide to write another one I will try to let people know, so I love to give advice, so if you need any help writing or with your stories, or even with a problem in real life, feel free to email me at broadwaystar32@yahoo.com. Just make the subject something about fanfiction or Harry Potter so I know what it's about. You can also check out my Harry Potter fansite at www.geocities.com/harrypottermagic32.
