Well, I'm Koosh. You're either reading this because you've just found this small archive of my fanfics and are curious about the author behind them, or you're an old fan of mine, and want to know where the hell I've been this last year.

Well, I used to be a big fanfiction writer, with hopes of one day being published. I still have hopes of being published, but don't write a heck of a lot any more, fanfiction or original work. I don't know why this is, but my writing kinda dried up when I moved out of home and to the city.

What little writing I have done includes a few original short stories starring Izzy Parker (yes, that Izzy Parker), a few paragraphs here and there, and the first chapter and a half of an original story which kind of died almost instantly. And, most recently, my first attempt at a slash fanfiction, which you'll be able to find below.

Uh, and I suppose a little more personal information about me, since you're here, and possibly wondering.

Names: Koosh, Kooshball, Kooshie, Ghost.

Age: Currently 19.

Birthday: June 3rd.

Lives: Australia.

Type of Fanfiction I write: I usually stick to MASH, and I rarely delve into slash or pairings, though I have on occassion written both. Klinger is my favourite character, so don't be surprised that he plays a major role in most of my fics. There are only three (all relatively short) that don't have him in them.

Since I started my writing career with Pokemon Fanfiction, I have a soft spot for it, and because there's so much that can be done with it, I'll still occassionally write it. Posting it, however, is another matter.

Other: There's not really a lot to say about me, really. I don't have any of the problems that 95 of the rest of the internet population seems to have. I don't have abusive parents or partner, I'm not in a community that doesn't respect me because of my sexuality (on that note, I seem to be one of the few people online these days that's straight, and not bi or gay), I'm not on medication because of a mental condition, I don't have a terminal illness.

I don't mean to be rude (and those who know me know I'm rarely rude), but it just seems to me everyone who's on the internet has a huge problem they feel like sharing with the rest of us, just to feel special. I'm just letting you guys know, I don't have that problem.

Other, other: I was actually co-writing something with another girl on FFN, though it's probably been long forgotten by everyone who had an interest in it. It was just for crazy fun, and can be found here.
