Author has written 3 stories for Love Hina, Rurouni Kenshin, and Ever After.

A little bit about me: I love history, languages, video games, movies, and art. In stories involving historic settings, I strive to be as accurate with details as possible. I wish to improve my creative writing skills especially concerning tenses, plot development, and characterization, because someday I would love to publish a historical fiction novel (my pipe dream). I enjoy stories that are descriptively written and deal with characters and relationships. I LOVE reading, it is an escape from reality for me. I am a huge sucker for a great romance story, especially in historic settings in Scotland.

I have an eclectic taste in music. Some of my favorite artists include Green Day, Taylor Swift, Maroon 5, Sugarland, and Dropkick Murphys.

Some of my favorite movies include Ever After, Monty Python and the Holy Grail, Hunger Games, Gladiator, The Count of Monte Cristo, The Princess Bride, and Tangled.

I'm a Netflix addict for TV shows: Once Upon a Time, The Vampire Diaries, Reign, BBC's Merlin, BBC's Robin Hood, Downton Abbey, Outlander, Marco Polo, The Tudors, and Game of Thrones.

My list of favorite books could go on for miles, but some of my top faves are: Harry Potter, Pride & Prejudice, The Other Boleyn Girl, Jane Eyre, The Hunger Games trilogy (Catching Fire is my fave), The Fault in Our Stars, Highland Velvet, and anything by Philippa Gregory.

I am also on FictionPress under the Penname Lady Marian, where all my original work will be.

(1.) My one and only Ever After fanfic, The Privilege of Love: Chapter 17 has been added. Working on the next chapter. Also going through all previous chapters for editing. Making them read more fluidly and filling in some gaps of detail and plot.

Thank you to everyone who has reviewed, favorited, and followed my stories! Your support makes me smile when I read your reviews and gives me encouragement. Merci beaucoup!!
