Welcome to my bio, which I finally decided to update after a long while of inactivity.
I'm an art major, English minor, musician, dance lover, poet, daughter, sister, friend.

I love to draw, paint, write, sing, learn.

Five things I couldn't live without: My fingers, though if you count both hands then that'd be ten things I couldn't live without, now wouldn't it?

The first anime I can recall seeing was Voltron, when I was about six years old. It blew me away and at that point I decided that I wanted to be a superhero who rode around in a giant robot lion. That dream didn't last long, alas.

I became obsessed with Sailor Moon when I was in sixth grade, and started to draw anime. I met one of my best friends to date and we started writing fanfiction together. Sweet Lord. Let's not talk about that, m'kay? Suffice to say, we got better. What's funny is that we've known each other for almost nine years and have yet to complete one of the many stories we've started.

When I was going to go to high school, I wanted to join the drama program. But when it came to auditioning, I realized that I was too afraid to do them. So I joined the art program instead, and found the love of my life. Which would be art, and not some guy. Keh. Funny how that happens, eh?

Oh yes. I'll beta read your story for you if you'd like. Within reason, of course. I'm an English minor and am willing to dish out constructive criticism if you're willing to take it. Fandoms that I'm pretty familiar with (in no particular order):

- Lord of The Rings / The Hobbit / The Silmarillion
- Star Wars (The original trilogy)
- Bleach
- Inuyasha
- Shoujo Kakumei Utena
- Magic Knight Rayearth
- Cowboy Bebop
- Sailor Moon
- Naruto

And assorted others. Of course, if you just need help with grammar and punctuation, I can give that too. :)

If you'd like to catch me on AIM, my current SN is InNeedOfBandaids. Use it, don't abuse it.

Also, check out my new joint fanfiction account:
http://www.fanfiction.net/~inutachi

My friend and I are writing an Inuyasha story together, and that's where you can find it. :)
