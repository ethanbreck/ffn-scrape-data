In a nit-pick, I am:

A teenager who is currently hating school for all the headaches it has managed to give her. She wishes she had more time and talent to write, thus the current hiatus on her vampire fic. She will review once in a while, mostly on fics concerning Rekka no Honou and Harry Potter. She doesn't like flamers who don't have an ounce of intelligence and seeing overused plots. She hates the defamation of characters. Example: turning Harry/Ron into a jerk so that Hermione will run to Draco. (Thumbs down!)

Another thing is, she would not waste her time reading fics that have rape scenes wherein the act is described as sensual. IT IS NOT. Rape is a horrible and traumatizing act and she has no idea why people enjoy using it. (If it's a well written angst fic, she might change her mind.)

She doesn't see how student-teacher (sexual) relationships are possible. A romance will do, but for pete's sake, does is have to be Snape and Hermione? ¬_¬

She likes nicely plotted Lily and James fics. She's currently enjoying Penseive by Oy Angelina.

Her HP ship: D/Hr, L/J

((In my opinion, Harry doesn't belong to anyone. I'll need a very good fic to be able to convice me to change my stand. So far, only Lissanne's Love is a Battlefield has managed to make me enjoy H/Hr))

When it comes to anime, her all time favorite pairings are Tokiya/Fuuko (all hail tofuu) and Touya/Yukito. She's a big fan of fuukonomiko, aka one of the greatest FoR authors ever, and is currently reading 'A Cup of Tea' by ms-word. She cannot bear poorly constructed lemons (most especially when it comes to yaoi), and has only read 1 very good one.

End of third person description.

Feel free to email me with questions or comments on my stories. If one doesn't really mind the things I don't like (mentioned above), I'm a pretty easy and agreeable person to talk to.
