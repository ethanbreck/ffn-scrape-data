A Little About Meh

I'm nuthin more than a 18-year old, 239lb, 5'10" African-American Male who lives his life for tha moment. Right now I'm attending J.Sargeant Reynolds, a community college, to raise my GP and to get basic classes out of the way for my major. In life, these ae the goals I've set so far.

1. Become A Pararescueman- For those not gifted in Military Terms, I'm going into the Air Force soon, having to leave and go to Texas this Summer actually for basic training. I'll be down there for at least 2 months to graduate and become a wingman, and then I'll be headin back to boring Richmond to finish off at least 2 years of Junior College. As for what a Pararescueman is, think of a Paratrooper and then think of that Paratrooper being trained in the field of EMT-P(Emergency Medical Technician).

2. Become a Game/Simulation Programmer, or Graphics Designer- Yes, this may seem strange, but I'm very interested in the field of gaming. This is the reason I'm getting myself on delayed entry in the Air Force so I can get two years of Junior College. See, in order to begin my journey down this field I need to get at least three more classes completed before I'm able to get into the School of Engineering in a college. After my two years, I'll go full-time into the Air Force and continue my college training to become a Game Programmer. Hopefully when I'm finished I'll be able to throw together a good enough portfollio to get myself hired by a great gaming company. Hopefully Activision, Black Lion Studios, Ubisoft of Valve. Or maybe I'll just find some guys who are willingly to take a chance and try to come together and create a new gaming enterprise, with dreams taking the world by storm!

Interests- Ahh, my interests...damn what can I say? I guess I can tell ya how I like to play MMO's, RPGs, FPS, Sim, God Games, RTS and others. I'm a fan of almost all kinds of music like Latin, Dance, R&B, Rap, Techo, Alternative Rock, Heavy Metal, J-Pop and J-Rock. I like ta play Football, BasketballEven though my dribblin suck right now _;; , Soccer, Tennis and others. ANd I guess I can say that I'm an avid writer of fanfiction. Though I gotta say I never am satisfied with it as I feel that I can never do any type of emotion right. And describing battles isn't as easy as most may think.
