Author has written 20 stories for Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon, Inuyasha, Power Rangers, Angel, Gundam Wing/AC, Star Wars, Roswell, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

I began writing fan fiction when I was in elementary school. Sixteen years later, I'm teaching elementary school and my interests are, understandably, not what they used to be. When I was bitten by the nostalgia bug this summer, I decided to revisit some of my works — both those displayed on this page and not — and found a good portion of them to be rather mortifying. Although there were a few bright spots in that sea of fic shame that I found myself wishing I'd seen through to the end, I know that chances of me actually doing so are slim to none for a variety of reasons.

What can you look forward to? Possible edits and rewrites, but not necessarily new chapters. I haven't given up on fan fiction; in fact, I would love to start writing again! However, it's unlikely that anything I wrote before 2010 will ever be updated again, and for that, I apologize.
