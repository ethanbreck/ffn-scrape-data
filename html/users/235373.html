My name is Liarra. I'm currently 21 years old (as of 2013), although I started virtually all of the stories here on fanfiction when I was between 10 and 13 years old. I would delete them, but I have a thing about history and keeping memories of the past. I'm currently a university student, so I don't get much time to write during the school year, but I do whenever I get the chance.
