There's not much to say about me I'm afraid. My tastes are all over the place. I LOVE stories that take the path less followed, and I adore alternate pairings. I'm not a fan of too much drama (especially of the high school variety)- I like a well thought out story with realistic expectations. Don't rush it!

~oOo~oOo~

Current Favorite Subject: Harry Potter

Current favorite pairing: Hermione x (Anyone not Ron or Harry)

Favorite site: deviantart.com ( -- LadyAlexial)

Fanfiction Pet Peeves:

Changing the name of the main character (unless they're using a new name purposely with the understanding that they're character name is originally still the same)

Too much drama - maybe it's just because I'm older now, but "pointless" drama really just kills it for me.

Rushed relationships - I like a slow burn, feeling like emotions are rushed and unrealistic bugs me.

Too many convenient situations - Sometimes I like to kick back and read a good fic where all of the bad things from the original story get fixed and the main character is a bit OP, BUT only to a certain extent. Especially if it's a fic that's meant to be taken seriously (Crack fics, and just for fun fics obviously don't apply here)...conflict and character development are important.

Things I love to see in a story:

Get creative with the universe and really expand on the topic matter. This is something I particularly love about HP because the universe has so much potential for creativity. Give me insights into old magic, show me a mix of magic and science, create new spells, etc...

Reasonable/mature characters - Obviously this can't always be because, let's face it, people aren't always reasonable in real life BUT I love a mature main character. To me this means someone who thinks things through before taking action, is willing to face their own flaws, handles their emotions well for the most part, etc...

Favorite fanfic genre: Romance w/e (preferably not a tragedy) =P -- I'm a sucker for alternate pairings
