Author has written 15 stories for Harry Potter, Pitch Black / Riddick, Aliens/Predator, and Warcraft.

Real name: Lace

Age: Somewhere between 19 and 30.

Location: Contained within an apartment, complete with mood-setting music, incense, and cold milk. yrmmmmm!

Inspiration: Spider Solitaire, night-time, and my special music mix. Oh, and for some reason, my best idea's come to me when I am in the shower, with a pen nowhere to be found.

About: Okay, so I'm Firesblood, and I write Harry Potter fanfiction, mostly those that contain Severus/Hermione pairings. I'm just beginning to write Pitch Black/Riddick fanfiction as well. I have quite a few one-shot fictions up. Those of you who have been following my multi-chaptered fics may notice that they have disapeared into thin air! Let me assure you that they are not gone forever; I am merely finishing them offline and will post them when I am done. I know how much I hate it when writers take forever to finish their stories or even, gods forbid, decide to not finish them -horrified gasp- So, in order to save my readers much suffering and such, I am going to make sure they have the comfort that the next chapter is indeed coming soon. Keep in mind that I have a 2 year old daughter, and another one that will be turning 1 in july. For those of you who know about children and raising them, you know that sometimes time is elusive. There is, however, that magical time after they go to sleep and before you go to bed.

Works in Progress:

Cracks in the Ice: SS/HG- Hermione and Severus form a bond and are thrust into events where nothing is as it seems, even themselves.

Prisoner of Time: GW/TR- Ginny goes to 1943 to retrieve a medicinal plant that will cure Hogwarts of a sickness, and meets a young Tom Riddle.

Under a Year: SS/HG- Over the summer, Hermione finds out that she has a rare disease for which no cure exists. She leaves school and decides to go abroad, with a not-so-happy Potions Master at her tail.

Kindness: DM/HG- Voldemort has won the war and Hermione Granger has come into his possession. Will Draco make good on the unspoken life favor between himself and Harry Potter, or will he decide that Hermione is better suited in other ways?

Evolution: SS/HG- After accepting a position as the Charms Professer, Hermione Granger returns to Hogwarts, but not before she reads a scathing article about her by Rita Skeeter and decides to make some rather cliche changes to her appearence.

Just Like Me: Riddick/Jack(Kyra)- Riddick goes to Crematoria to get Jack, and discovers that he might have done more damage in leaving than he would have in staying. Together, Riddick and Kyra must find a way to stop the Necromongers and accept the past in all it's forms, along with the monster it has turned them into.

The Tracker: No Pairing- Nix Grant, tracker and 'Domino' Bounty Hunter is hired by Weyland-Yutani to help find and capture one of the Yautja, and one of the Xenemorphs. However, after coming face to face with a Yautja, Nix discovers the complexity of such a species and yearns to learn more. But first, she must prove herself worthy.

Updates are to come soon!
