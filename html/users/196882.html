So, lazy bum that I am, I finally got around to updating Spinning. :ducks to avoid the flying pigs: The story's almost over! Maybe I can finish this summer... if
- driving school
- job at A.C. Moore
- summer reading
- vacation

don't get in the way. Yup, I'm going to be a lot busier this summer than I originally anticipated. But I'll try my hardest to write write write!

YGO's over! T_T But island isn't... if I can get Spinning taken care of (I really want to finish it soon) I'll try to work on my YGO fic. No promises though.

And if you're curious as to what's up with me, you can always check out my LJ. It's usually just frou-frou stuff... but it's somewhat interesting (perhaps... maybe... in my dreams...).

fyi

Sir Mocha is a high-school girl in the US who takes a masochistic approach to school, fritters away valuable time online doing nothing, and is known for being just a bit too quirky for her health and the health of those around her. And yes, "Sir" Mocha is, in fact, a female. Gender confusion all the way!
