~*~Ring bearer~*~

Boromir: *has just been shot by the first arrow* Nice shot mate! Bit off target though, the script says I'm menna die.

Orc: Well, die then!

Boromir: I can't, this one isn't in the right place. Do it again!

Orc: So bloody demanding! I could have got a job as an alien in MIB II, but no, I just had to go for the big epic tale, didn't I...*mutters to self*

Boromir: Well, come on then! I don't want to die with my robes all bloody! *Fighter's stance*

Orc: I don't know why I bother, I really don't! *Strings an arrow* All you do is complain, complain, complain. Without me, you wouldn't have that shield. Made out of pure orc bone, but do you care, oh no...

Boromir: Stop blabbering and shoot me dammit!

Orc: *Shoots*

Boromir: Oh, for Ellar's sake, you missed me again! Legolas, this Orc needs coaching, he's a naff shot!

Legolas: *Sashays in, with a top hat circled by a purple ribbon* what was that, Boringmir? I was busy fucking

Boromir: *Unfazed by the elf's strange appearance* this bloody Orc couldn't shoot a hippo. Can you show him, my robes are going to be awfully stained when I go over the falls!

Legolas: *Sighs* If I must. Give that bow here, you amateur! *Bends down, and chucks the Orc under the chin, giving a little wink, before standing back up in a firing stance* This is how you shoot.

Orc: *Raptly staring at the elf's very defined hind quarters*

Legolas: *Pulls back bow string, and lets arrow loose. Hits Boromir dead on* That’s how you fire an arrow.

Boromir: Well done Legolas, that was...ow...crap that hurts! *Slides down a tree* Ow...*Coughs*

Orc: Uh...I was supposed to shoot him...now the whole thing has to be done again!

Legolas: *Shrugs, and leaves, twirling his top hat*

Boromir: *Weakly* I don't think I can do that take again...*gargle* just dub it.

Orc: OK, but I don't think Peter J will be happy with this.

Aragorn: *Stumbles in* Boromir! My darling Boromir! *Runs over and hugs him*

Legolas: *Has come back in elf garb* what, so we all have to nearly die to get some affection? Geez *walks off in elven huff*

Aragorn: I love you, you can't die on me! *Kisses Boromir's forehead*

Boromir: Perfect...timing. How am I menna fuck you if I'm dead?

Aragorn: I will see you in Ellar's house when my time comes. Now, I must carry on the quest.

Legolas: My ass you will! *Shoots Aragorn* Ah...now no competition for the ring bearer. All mine, the little cutie! *Clicks heels together, and disappears, leaving behind a pair of red, sparkly heels*

Aragorn and Boromir: That was a little strange *slump into each other’s arms, dead*
