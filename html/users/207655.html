I am still bored so I thought I would re-write my bio. Yeah, whatever.

Things to say...So much to talk about so little space.

RAVEN IS MINE AND I AM THE CENTER OF THE UNIVERSE!!

Whey is he mine you might ask. Well, I wrote the first Raven fic. Okay, I posted the same day as Kala, so I am tied with her. But it's all good.

I am still 17 years old, and an okay writer, I guess. I like lots of things like Raven, Shadow, Raven, chicken, Raven, and money. Blame the money on my greedy side.

But I must say that Raven is by far my fav. You see Raven could steal the money that I could use to buy the chicken. And where ever Raven is, Shadow is.

I like Zoids G.F. the best. Mainly because of the evil people:
Raven& Shadow!
Hiltz& Ambient (Yes, I know he tried to kill Shadow, but he is cool and good- looking)
Prozen (he is okay, but NOOOO Raven!)

Van and the rest are okay, except Ryss! She is a...gardening tool! And I bet you can't figure out which one. She is creepy and is way to old for my Raven. Did you happen to catch the age of her last boyfriend? His zoid is a rock! Nope, she is no good for Raven.

Some other Zoids characters I like are:
Vega (he reminds me of someone...?)
Jack Sisco
Van (he is okay, but he should leave Raven
alone)
Thomas Schublatz

I don't consider the Backdraft group evil b/c they don't really do NEthing bad, save "don't play by the rules" ohh so scary. Now Raven and Hiltz are REAL "bad guys".

Ahhh. Raven, my beloved. Now that is a cartoon man. He just has some anger management problems. Van is EVIL. In reality, Ravin will defeat Van and rule the world. Bwahahahah. Hiltz is cool, too. He as a little to much ambition.

I think my life is kinda sad, if I am daydreaming about anime guys:(. But what can I say, those Japenese sure can draw em. I must admit I often find myself drawn to the "bad" guys in these cartoons. I mean Ravin, some Seto Kiab(?) from YuGiOh, and etc. I also like that Dick guy from Batman which is weird because he is actually a good guy.

My personal goal for life is to be amused with it. And you can't be amused without money. I think that Vogue and Cosmo are hilarious. I can't imagine NEone thinking that much about_.

Some of my other hobbies include talking about myself and typing randomly. I am in the band and newspaper. I am strange.

I can't spell w/o word and grammar check. My stories have a plot somewhere(somewhere being the key word). I hate high school.

a word to ppl who read this- I am sure you are wise, witty, and wonderful, but you shouldn't rot your brain out by reading this.*
