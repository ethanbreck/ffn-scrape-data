Hi! Long time, no see, yeah? I was just reading through part of Yumemiru, and I must take the time to apologize for all the typos and misuse of the Japanese language. I was 14/15 when I wrote that story and had no real knowledge of the language. My apologies!

A huge thank you goes out to everyone who has ever read the story--especially those who reviewed it and/or added it to his/her favourites! I still get really giddy whenever I see a new notice. (Another huge thank you goes to those who checked out my other two story attempts!)

I'm sorry to admit that I have not been steadily writing fiction since I disappeared from ff.net. I've recently been trying to pick things back up though, and I finally came up with a new idea last week that I'm running with. It will be original fiction, as opposed to BSSM fanfiction. I'll post it my fictionpress i.d. if that ever happens.
