Author has written 21 stories for Charmed, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Sweep, Mutant X, CSI, NCIS, and Dark-Hunter series.

Greetings, anyone who has stumbled upon my little corner of the site. It's mostly unoccupied, I peek in occasionally however, and periodically get bitten by the writing bug, though it seems less and less regularly these days. I'm Nic, I'm currently 22 years old, and I've been a reader and writer on FFN since I was 13 - though those early works are hidden in the depths of hardrives and shall never see the light of day again, as they were quite awful (as I think most would admit their early works were!). I make no claims to being a decent writer, but sometimes it's not possible to find what you seek in a story without plucking it from the depths of your own brain.

If you have found your way here through some chance of fate, please spend some time, read and review anything that catches your fancy. I love to hear from people who enjoy anything I write, or who have constructive criticism for me - I'm sure you all know how to get in touch with someone on here, so feel free!

~~~My 'Ships~~~

NCIS - I don't have any matches that I won't write for NCIS, but my favourites are Zabby (despite the fact I'm generally NOT a slash writer, I just love those two together) and Jibbs. Also if we're taking waltzes back into the early seasons, I 'ship McAbby and Tate.

Buffy - Strictly a Spuffy girl, I don't think I've written anything B/A.

CSI - Strangely, I prefer to read Grissom/Sara, but write Grissom/Catherine - possibly because I'm just terrible at shipping Grissom/Sara. Also, Warrick/Catherine on occasion, and Greg/Sara is my favourite of favourite ship from this show. Poor Nicky doesn't get any action from me! That and I haven't seen later seasons, so this fandom's on the backburner anyway.

Charmed - Piper/Leo all the way. Other than that, I tend to have OCs in the other 'shippy roles.

Glee - I haven't posted anything Glee yet, but it's niggling at my brain so I think there'll be something coming. I think I'm a Puckleberry fan for the moment, if only because that is one of the most awesome 'ship nicks I've heard!
