Thankyou, everyone who read any of my stories and especially thankyou for those who tirelessly put up with my crap and reviewed every chapter. I'm sorry I've been such an ass to you all, and for taking nearly a year off.

I know how painful it is to have someone stop right in the middle of a story and I'm not going to become one of those people again. It is going to take a lot of time but I promise you I WILL finish this!
Thankyou.
