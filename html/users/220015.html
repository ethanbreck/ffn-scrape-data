Hmm...what about me? Well, I'm an obsessed WOT reader as well as Mercedes Lackey and Tamora Pierce, though generally I only write WOT fanfiction; occasionally Tamora Pierce. And they're all parodies. I can never seem to write a serious story for some reason.

12/4/06: For some reason Fanfiction has decided that the majority of my stories had to be taken down. I don't understand why because I wasn't violating any of their rules. So, I'm no longer going to post stories here if they're just going to be deleted. Goodbye.

1/10/05: Hm. Well. The time has gone by rather quickly, hasn't it?Apologies for the lack of stories and all that but school seems to get the wayof everything.I've come to the realization that much of my knowledge of WOT has leaked out of my brain and so consequently, don't expect ANYTHING in the way of WOT parodies. But I've become interested in Mercedes Lackey again so perhaps I shall do something around that.

1/3/05: Aha! While re-reading some of my favorite Tamora Pierce books, I had a sudden flash of inspiration (I knew it would come eventually). Anyway, expect a Numair related story at some point.

8/30/04 First Update in about 2 years: Errr...so yeah. I don't really write stories all that often anymore. I'm mainly using this account to keep track of my favorite stories/authors and to comment on stories. But who knows...I may be inspired to write something someday. fades back into the shadows
