Name is JasmineUnicorn. Known as Medli across the web. My favourite anime is Inuyasha. Don't know why. Probably because it never has a dull moment.

Currently have a domain of my own with all my collectives including two shrines dedicated to the anime. One is just dedicated to the anime itself. The other is dedicated to my favourite anime couple, Inuyasha and Kagome.

I enjoy listening to music. My favourite singer is Evanessence. I enjoy writing from fiction stories to fanfiction to even poems. Sayings are what I write the most. I post them on my site that allows me to express who I am.

I enjoy other animes such as YuGiOh and Witch Hunter Robin. I enjoy reading mangas such as Ceres, Inuyasha, Fushigi Yuugi, Alice 19th, and more.

My collection consists of mythology from unicorns to fairies to dragons to magic. More about me is located at my online journal, Tranquility.

Favourite Pairings: Inuyasha and Kagome / Inuyasha : Yami Yugi and Tea / YuGiOh : Amon and Robin / Witch Hunter Robin
