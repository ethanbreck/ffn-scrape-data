CONSTRUCTIVE CRITICISM IS LIKE CANDY TO ME. PLEASE, R&R. (:

More than likely, if I've been lurking around the site, I'll do the same for something of yours.

There isn't much about me that you need to know; I'm really not that interesting.

I'm nearly a twenty-four-year old girl that has an unhealthy obsession with Harry Potter and a need to express this obsession through the occasional fanfiction because she struggles with developing her own characters.

I love to write—when I'm in the mood to, it makes me ridiculously happy, verging on hyperactive. I have a strange obsession with em dashes and use them often, and no doubt quite frequently incorrectly, in my stories. I think it would be wonderful to become an author but I have little faith that it will happen because most days, I'm perfectly content writing in another person's universe.

I'm my own worst critic and am well aware that I am absolutely horrid at finishing stories, which is why most of my recent stuff is written in a one-shot format. I've had a major plot bunny for the past four or five years, however, so maybe my one-shot streak will turn around soon.

No, I haven't really considered going through and deleting my old stories, even though I have no real plans of continuing them. I kind of like the fact that they're on here. They show how I've grown over the years and how my writing style has grown with me. When I started writing fanfiction here I was but fourteen or fifteen and I'm very aware that it shows. What ever flames are thrown their way will be compiled into a bonfire for s'mores.

If you'd like to get to know me, the girl behind the pen-name, feel free to PM me.

PLEASE?

I have a few requests. Well, sort of, anyway.

As of 8/30/2012.

I want you to challenge me. If you'd like to see something written, feel free to run the idea by me and, chances are, I'll give it a go. In other words, please, please challenge me! Challenges are my writer's block medicine. If you'd like to give me a challenge, there are only two rules; it must be in a book category I've already written under and it cannot be novel-length. If you're interested in doing this, email me!

Moving on.

I am almost always looking for new roleplay partners. Unfortunately, as of now, I only roleplay in the Harry Potter fandom, but I've several originals and canons. My canons are as follow and are listed from most developed to least developed.

• Draco Malfoy
• Harry Potter
• Theodore Nott
• Severus Snape

These characters are all slash but I may be persuaded into playing them straight. If you're interested in roleplaying with me, please shoot me a PM!

Feel free to check out my tumblr!
