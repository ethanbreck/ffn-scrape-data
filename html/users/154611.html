I'm a fan of King of Fighters and most Nintendo games (Super Smash Brothers Melee and WarioWare Inc. in particular) and love to read mostly romance and/or humor stories about them (regardless of the rating). I also like Animal Crossing, but I really don't see that game as fic-worthy, so I don't read that section. The pairings I like are Peach/Mario, Daisy/Luigi, Zelda/Link, Samus/Captain Falcon, Mona/Wario, Mai/Andy, Mary/Terry, King/Ryo, Yuki/Kyo, and Shermie/Yashiro. And as for those whose pontential pairings seem to know no bounds, I like Iori/Leona and Iori/Chizuru (I've yet to find a good story where Marth has a female partner I don't already prefer with someone else, though I want to think that he had a girlfriend in "Fire Emblem", same with Roy). I may read and like the occasional story involving the pairing of any one of them with someone else (so long as it's not yaoi/yuri), but that's very rare and I wouldn't write anything like that myself.

Sad that a lot of stories currently on my fave list are Nintendo stories. If only the KoF/Fatal Fury fandoms had more good stories. Too bad at least 7 out of 10 KoF fics are Kyo/Iori if not that freak with the one-letter name/that girl freak unworthy of her gem surname (or anything stupid passed off as "humor"). And that Fatal Fury has so few fics to begin with (and even fewer that are really worthwhile). And SSMB doesn't have many worthy fics in its category (most are Marth/Zelda, Marth/Link,or Marth/Roy).

And if you're wondering why I don't review a lot of stories, even the ones on my favorites list, that's because I'm not always sure exactly what to say (you know the feeling).
