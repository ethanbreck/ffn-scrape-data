Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter, Digimon, and Card Captor Sakura.

Hmm... I guess I should update this once and awhile... It's been awhile since I've been able to write.

I don't know what to say, I know my strength in writing, I need music, there is something about setting up the atmosphere of the story. I also know that the story has a life of it's own, so no matter what I do I can't force it out. Lately I've been able to write again, I don't know I found a new stockpile of music that works for the story. I hope that I will be able to finish the stories I've started finally.

Well I hope you enjoy the stories that I tell, until then I guess...

I'm a big reader, I love reading and now I enjoy writing for the most part.

Thank-you to all my reviewers, it seems a bit vain but I do enjoy reading all your reviews because it lifts my spirit when I'm working till one in the morning trying to make the fic sound better.

Special Thank-yous goes out to all those authors and writers over the years... Most of them are on my favourite author and favourite stories list but few has also disappeared. You inspired me and aided me, I hope to see your works in the future!
