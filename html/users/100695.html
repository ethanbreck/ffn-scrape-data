UPDATED: IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR THOSE WAITING FOR UPDATES

Allrighty, due to many reviews I still seem to be receiving for "Helping Hands", and to the many questions--and rather pointedly worded comments--that have appeared, I decided I will have to clarify for people that this story is NOT going tofinished any time in the near future. In fact, I have serious doubtsit will EVER be finished. Feel free to blame me entirely for this abrupt ending of the story (though I have to admit that, after re-reading the last chapter some time ago, it could very well serve as a inconclusive ending in a sense).

As for reasons as to why I am no longer writing, I have to say that it was a few things--the chapter that I had promised was coming along so nicely in my last posted chapter was lost, as was the rest of my fics in progress, when my computer got a virus. I have also lost interest in anime--and Inuyasha--a long time ago. Thirdly, I have a very busy life right now balancing family, school, and the various other problems that surface as each day passes.

I wish I had inspiration to finish "Helping Hands", I truly do, and I know that leaving a story unfinished is a waste, but sometimes things don't work out the way you hope. This isn't meant to offend anyone, and I am so grateful for everyone's support, but please understand that I am no longer writing, and as such, my stories will have to remain the way they are now.

Thank you for everything,

~Windigo~
