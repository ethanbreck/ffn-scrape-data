(Formerly known as Angel Lawlor)

Can't believe my old account still exists! I haven't been here in forever! And now I use it read Supernatural fan fiction.

I am a Jane-Come-Lately to Supernatural, started watching at the end of July 2010, and it amazes me how quickly I took to it. What made me watch? I went to the San Diego Comic Con, and I decided to sit in on the Supernatural panel while waiting for the Glee panel, which was happening in the same room. And Mr. Kripke and Co. totally won me over. And I figured, if the panel is this entertaining, then the show must be, right? So, I came back, watched the episode "Dead Me Don't Wear Plain", and haven't looked back since.

Maybe, someday, I will publish some fiction of my own, but don't whole your breath. Right now, I'm here simply to enjoy the work of others. I've come to learn that Destiel is my perferred pairing. The thought of Wincest creeps me out, cause I can't get past the fact that, you know, they're brothers. I've developed kind of a soft spot for Sam/Gabriel, which is an odd pairing to support, but it has to be written well and the characters have to feel true to their potrayal on the show for me to go there.

Here's to reading some pretty awesome stories!

And feel free to pass on to me recommendations for some stories :-) !
