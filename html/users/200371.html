I am fourteen, and the top of my class in writing. I've written, as you can probably see, far, far too many stories. I believe Heirs of the King, despite its lack of reviews, is my best work. I love Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Pirates of the Carribean, Labyrinth, Knight's Tale, Willow, The Tenth Kingdom, X-Men, The Matrix, A Series of Unfortunate Events, The Gunslinger, Star Wars (original series, not I and II so much), and Star Trek, especially Voyager and Next Generation...and so, so many other things.

A Veiled Sun - Harry Potter - a series of very short drabbles from a post-Hogwarts reality. OotP AU.

Aftermath - Harry Potter - OotP AU, after a battle at Hogwarts. Not among my best works.

Always - Harry Potter - during OotP, spoilers, letters between Ginny and Percy. So-so, not fabulous.

Bittersweet - HP - MWPP era, some OotP spoilers, a conversation between Sirius and his cousin Andromeda.

Black Night - HP - FINISHED! MWPP era, OotP spoilers, R-rated. One of my best. If you want a lesser rating, skip the first two chapters, you can figure out what's been going on really easily. Sirius' cousin goes missing and an unlikely group of people band together to find her.*

Black's Story - HP - no OotP spoilers, between third and fourth book, what Sirius was doing. Done as far as it's going to get done, so-so.

Brothers Forgiven - HP - no OotP spoilers, after the prank, Remus has to come to terms with what Sirius has done to him.

And this is just the beginning...I'll summarize the rest later, okay?

I'll probably be doing a few TTT in-movie-POV pieces, due to the recent release of the video.
