This is so strange to re-write after all this time... I found fanfiction when I was 13 and I am now 25. I don't write so much anymore and had considered deleting this account but I couldn't bear to part with it.

I have an M.A., I work with old and pretty things, I can't stand the feel of cotton wool and I've been vegetarian for twelve years. Fanfiction is, and always has been, my secret life, something familiar to return to amidst turmoil. It's a love/hate thing.
