Hello world.

Recently, I've been dabbling (or rather, have allowed myself to be entirely pulled) in a fandom I'd sworn I'd never write for, Kingdom Hearts. I suppose I can take a modicum of comfort in knowing that I'm not writing based on the game's continuity. I would like to get back into Harry Potter (if only so I can write third gen fics and screw with canon by writing gratuitous Harry/Luna) and am very interested in writing something for xxxHOLiC (I do have a WIP piece on the backburner) and South Park (I've got a little project in the works for the kids). Additionally, I've been stewing on an idea for Bully/Canis Canem Edit, but I may not post it on this account; I have a separate account for pieces I'm not sure about and this one is one I'm REALLY not sure about.

I adore reading well-written fiction, but I've become incredibly hard-to-please when in-character portrayals are concerned. It's hit me hardest in the South Park fandom, so I'm rather picky about what I'll favourite.

Lately I've been delving into the dark recesses of the Psychonauts category (and by lately I mean the last two years...), rediscovering Bully, introducing myself to South Park with much enthusiasm, and wishing still that someone with amazing writing skills would join The Seventh Tower's tiny don't-blink-or-you'll-miss-it fandom.

As you can see, I'm largely a gen fic writer and I'm not entirely sure how that happened, seeing as I'm fond of 'shipping; it's happened nonetheless. What's more is that I'm sure you'll have noticed that there's a good bit of angst/drama going on in my writings as well... I'd like to think that my angst style has matured over the years. I'm attempting to write more humour, friendship, and general fluff, but I must say that angst comes easily. Gotta step out of your comfort zone eventually, I guess, yes?

So yeah, this is me... Growing more restless by the minute.


My Current Projects:

In progress.
