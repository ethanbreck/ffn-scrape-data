Author has written 14 stories for Card Captor Sakura, GALS!, Star Ocean, Suikoden, Naruto, and Eureka Seven.

Na is a hypochondriac who loves fruits.

She believes in sasusaku, princelyon, erioltomoyo, domiane, faytsophia, zeroyuuki and a million other more pairings. The authors from which these fandoms come from delight in breaking her heart so she has decided to write happy endings for her pairings.

It takes a really long time for her to churn out stories. So no more multi-chaptered fics in the forseeable future anymore.

Notes on unfinished stories:

Stigmatized and Next Time Around are both on permanent hiatus. There is more hope of me writing more on NTA though (because I misplaced the draft for Stigmatized and am too tired to rewrite it).
