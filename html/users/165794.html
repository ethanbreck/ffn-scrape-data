I am a person with many obsessions! My fandoms include the following:

-Final Fantasy - this is the big one. I've played through III, IV, VI, VII, VIII, IX, and X. Of these, X is my favorite, but IV and VI are both really close behind. VII has a special place in my heart too, but thanks to the DS, I recently rediscovered the old-school FF games and realized just how awesome they were. I'm on an FFIV kick right now, and can't get enough of my favorite character, Kain.

-Transformers (G1 and the 2007 movie)

-Harry Potter

-Prince of Tennis (the username is a reference from it)

-Lord of the Rings

-Doctor Who

...and whatever else I find myself interested in at the moment!

I like angst...lots of it. My favorite characters are usually the ones with tortured pasts who are overcoming all sorts of internal demons (Kain from FFIV, Celes from FFVI, Sephiroth from FFVII, Auron from FFX). But I also love reading parodies and general hilarity. I'll read pretty much any genre.

I'm much more of a reader than a writer, but maybe I'll throw some stuff up here if I'm feeling inspired and brave.

Credit for my icon/avatar goes to greenangel at LiveJournal!
