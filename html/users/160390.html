Author has written 30 stories for Harry Potter, Cats, and Into the Woods.

I am proud to say that I have been a CATS fanatic since 2004. I think it's the greatest thing to ever happen to stage... or the world for that matter. Okay, so maybe I'm going a little too far... Just a little, though.

I'm 20 years old and am currently enrolled in Sir Wilfred Grenfell's (Memorial's Corner Brook Campus) Theater Arts course. I'm in my third year, as of right now. I love theater. Especially musicals. Though 'A hatful of Rain' and Shakespeare will always hold a very special place in my heart; as will all the hundreds of great authors that have graced the stage with their images and ideas.

My favorites from CATS are: Mungojerrie and Rumpelteazer (like you didn't know); Munkustrap; Rum Tum Tugger; Mistofelees; Alonzo; Etcetera; Jemima; and so on and so forth... I like them all. Let's leave it at that. But I personally believe that Mungojerrie and Rumpelteazer are mates. If you think they're siblings - that's great. Write a story about it so I can read it. I think that they're the cutest...

I am Super Poet Gurl's official beta (and cookie timer -but that's another story). If she ever updates, it'll be a miracle. And Ashy4 (not that she's here) is now my muse for my Into the Woods fic. All hail the "4"s!

And I don't own Cats... yadda yadda yadda... end disclaimer
