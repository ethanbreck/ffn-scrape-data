Author has written 11 stories for Fullmetal Alchemist, Naruto, and Kingdom Hearts.

For those of you keeping tabs, I'm a language and musical theater enthusiast and I spend all my free time working at Disneyland.

For real time updates on fanfics and RP logs, please check my homepage~ It leads to my writing journal on LJ, so please feel free to watch it. Any time I finish any piece of writing, it will be there, so it's a good way to keep up with fics I may not post here. (And there is quite a lot that doesn't get posted here.)

At the moment: I'm writing a bunch of Kingdom Hearts 2 stuff. I'm so in love with AxelRoxas, for serious. I don't think these muses will ever leave, which is cool, I think.

About my stories: The older is it, the less I want you to read it. I really want to delete some, but I can't quite bring myself to do it. But seriously, just because you like my writing now, doesn't mean I was good at writing my whole life. So don't read my old crap. And don't make me say "I told you so."

Also, I do this weird thing where, if a fic is more than a month old, I won't leave a review for it. I'm not asking for reviews, because I don't do that crap, But (for me at least) if you read something here that's, I don't know, a year old or something, or more, whatev, and you liked it enough to warrant comments; Knock yourself out. Go hog wild. Have at it. I promise, I remember writing it, so reviews on old things wont be scoffed at.

And I suck at replying to reviews because I sort of fail. But... If I ever write another multi-chapter (which I supposedly am) I'm going to put in the effort. That is, if you all care about that sort of thing. We'll find out.
