Hi, My name is Nellanie and I'm a huge 3 ninjas fan, I usually like to write fantasy or horror, thriller stories but I do comedy and romance too. The other stories "final fantasy" are from a friend of mine and I asked to post them for him, he said 'go on then' so I did.

If you're getting alerts from old stuff I'm just fixing mistakes and things, but there might be a few changes here and there, but nothing major.

Final Fantasy Readers: My friend, whom I've been putting stories in for, has got an account here now. So if you're looking for a somewhat part 3 to the 2 stories than you can go read Uprising: The Beast Wars under the user Resting Madness. He said he'd revamp the other two if they get moved to that account. Also I've added that fic into my favorites so you can find it faster there. There's also the conclusion fic to the saga called The Final Distance. please do enjoy them.

My plans: I might still do a few Rocky one-shots or just include them into Colt's story on the side, I haven't decided yet, but thank you guys for reading and reviewing if you do!

Big Time Battle of the Boy Bands: Gustavo is so nice, taking the boys on a ski trip and allowing them to stay in his fancy lodge and all... if they only knew that the real reason is that they're going to be competing in a battle of the boy bands going on at the ski resort just over the hill. Vacation somewhat deterred, the boys make the best of it anyway as Carlos and Kendall learn how to ski, Logan tries renewing his relationship with Camille, and James tries to model when spotting models and a camera crew, and Kendall and James; with their relationship out in the open, use any opportunity they can find to be alone.

Small Hours: Is the last of my Silent Voices saga. For right now its on hold. I'm just not in a serious story mood. I will most likely start a new story, but for now this one is on hiatus.

My Tum Tum stories will come after that, and they're heavy enough while being humorous enough. I don't like to take Tum too seriously because then he wouldn't be him, would he? But those will be good. And after my Tum serious I'll just be doing scattered unlinked to each other fics. Thank you for reading. Adios.
