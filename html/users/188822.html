November 15th 2010

It's been nearly three years since my last finished story. My goodness, has it really been that long? Time certainly flies! :O For those wondering (if anyone's actually reading this after three years!) no, I haven't given up on my stories. I will finish them/write new ones. I just can't promise you when. It seems as if stories just aren't coming naturally to me anymore. The ideas do, but as soon as I sit down to write...nothing. Well, I'm holding my fingers crossed. If I'm lucky, it'll get better. I did get a story out just recently, and that can't be bad thing, right?
