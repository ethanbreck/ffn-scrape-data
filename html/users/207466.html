I'm sixteen. I gather I should either defend this fact or apologize for it. I'll do neither.
I read like I eat (which is to say not as often as I'd like).
I don't participate in fandom half as much as I ought...
... but when I do I both read and write slash and femslash. (And no, I'm not going to put specific warnings up for slash pairings. I'll warn for graphic sex, language, or violence, but the double standard BS shall be left to the government.)
I like unusual pairings.
I think canon is a good thing, but purists are not.
I think flattery is good so long as you don't inhale, so send me positive feedback. I think constructive criticism is good so long as it is in fact constructive, so send me critiques. I think that outright flames are good so long as they're sufficiently amusing, so send me those too.
And I think the majority of stuff I've posted here pre-2003 is utter shite, but people seem to get a kick out of it, so I'll keep it up.
