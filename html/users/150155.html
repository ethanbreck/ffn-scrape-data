Author has written 43 stories for Harry Potter, Fushigi Yuugi, Ayashi no Ceres, Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy VII, GTO, Kamikaze Kaitou Jeanne, Dynasty Warriors, Pet Shop of Horrors, Misc. Anime/Manga, Battle Royale, Get Backers, Fullmetal Alchemist, Misc. Books, and Darker than BLACK.

I'm a college student and a fan of anime, manga and video games. I also read a lot, the "Harry Potter" series being some of my favorite books. One-shots are my forte, especially angsty and introspective ones. Recently, though, I've been focusing on writing romantic one-shots. As I've stated over and over again in my fics, I greatly appreciate constructive criticism. Trust me, it helps an aspiring writer like me improve.

I used to give writing multi-chaptered fics a try. But in time I found out that I had the tendency to lose interest and/or commitment to them, so now I'm sticking to writing one shots. Perhaps when I feel more comfortable (and more patient) writing multi-chaptered works, I will write some of my own. Also, if you're looking for a good read, perhaps you might want to read my more recent works. The ones I wrote when I was younger aren't that polished. After going over my older fics, I deleted a few, but still I'm leaving some here for you to assess whether I've improved much... or if I haven't improved at all. :)

By the way, just so you know... I have to admit that I'm often busy with school and/or real life, and when I'm not, I am a bit lazy when it comes to reviewing and responding. That's why I don't get to reply to reviewers. Rest assured that I appreciate every review I get (except maybe for flames); they make my day. Perhaps one day I'll overcome my laziness and get around to reviewing others' works, and replying to reviewers. I also admit that I'm not the most eloquent reviewer. I hope people don't get the impression that I'm snobbish for not interacting much with other authors.

Oh, and one more thing. I'm looking for two of my songfics: "She Will Be Loved" and "Knife", both written for the "Dynasty Warriors" fandom. When FF.Net began purging songfics, they took mine down before I even got a chance to do so myself. Thanks to a virus, my backups were erased, and I've been trying to find these fics all over the Internet, to no avail. I doubt that I can reproduce what I once wrote, so if anyone can find them for me, I would be truly grateful.
