Author has written 5 stories for Sentinel.

I am not very comfortable talking about me but I'll give it a try. I started writing fan fic back in high school, many, many years ago. But I did not know it was called fan fic, I just thought that I was borrowing someone else's characters. I have hundreds of notebooks filled with stories. Most of my stories were about wrestling with a MAJOR Mary Sue...gee I wonder who that could be. Then I switched my focus to television shows and I started writing Kung Fu, The Legend Continues before moving onto Diagnosis Murder and The Sentinel. I stopped writing for a while because I had lost my enthusiasm for writing.

One of these days I will tackle typing my handwritten stories into my computer and maybe upload them after a LITTLE tweaking...ya know taking out Mary Sue.

When I finally got online, oh my god let me tell you what an amazing world I discovered. Not only did I find fan sites dedicated to MY favorites actors and characters, I discovered fan fiction. A light bulb went on and it was amazing. My first foray was Star Wars, and then I found FanFiction.net and I was hooked.

I read mostly The Sentinel, NCIS and Numb3rs although I do dabble in Due South, Northern Exposure, Criminal Minds and a few others.

My Sentinel stories are a few years old and many were written for a Dues list that I was a part of. Please enjoy.
