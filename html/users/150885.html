Author has written 16 stories for Card Captor Sakura.

I felt that I should explain myself to those of you still reading my stories. CCS and its characters will always be very close to my heart. But I am at a point in my life where it had become difficult to keep up with this piece of my childhood. However, please be rest assured that I never abandon things close to my heart or to the hearts of other people, for that matter. These stories will get finished. I'm waiting for inspiration to come to me, maybe hit me like a bus. And I'm waiting for my own heart to feel once again like it had when I was younger, lighter and less jaded and more carefree. And I'm sorry you have to wait with me. I'm doing my best, I promise.

Please do feel free to add me as a friend at corposant.livejournal.com
