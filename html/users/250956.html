Author has written 11 stories for Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Dynasty Warriors, and Sherlock.

For any of you that might be out there...

I joined FF.net back when I was a tiny junior high student...and now, I'm headed to college! How time flies!

I'm an avid fan of good writing in general. If I had to name my favorite author (by style, not story), it would be F. Scott Fitzgerald - his style is magnificent.

Creative writing is also my favorite hobby. I do all kinds of styles and genres, though recently I've been writing less and less fanfiction.

Other favorite hobbies include: playing the piano, drawing and painting, reading, cooking, watching movies, and photography. I only post what I think is good enough to be read.

My scrutiny over quality of work will ensure that my stories aren't just a waste of your time. Constructive criticism is always welcome in my review column, and you can e-mail me or read my works at your whim.

Thanks for reading,
