Right... so obviously I haven't been updating in a (long) while. Um... technically, I just write or update when I feel like it (like when an inspiration comes) and just ditch it for about a (few) month(s) or so before writing again. So yes, I'm very sporadic so don't expect frequent updates since even I wouldn't know when THAT will be.

But,
rest assured, when I've set out to do something, I intend to see it
done. So, even with my unpredictable appearances, I will (somehow)
finish these stories and have a conclusion to at least a part of my
messed up life. =D Dedicated, aren't I?

Though you can't count on

and look up other authors' creation which I admire and gawk. So take a
time to browse through them (a lot of them actually) and hope you all
have a nice reading!
