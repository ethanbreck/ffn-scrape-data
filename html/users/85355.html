Author has written 85 stories for StarTrek: The Next Generation, StarTrek: Voyager, TV X-overs, and Stargate: SG-1.

Recipient of the 2006 Alara Rogers ASC Award for Best Author Overall; 2004, 2005, and 2006 ASC Award for Best TNG Author, Second place in the 2005 ASC Voyager Author Award.

Our home page is Whoa Nellie's Star Trek Fan Fiction Stories at

http://sites.google.com/site/whoanelliestartrekfanfiction/

This site contains Whoa Nellie's entire collection of Romance Star Trek Fan Fiction stories including their signature Picard/Vash stories, Crusher/Chakotay stories and the Voyager Twist of Fate series. Most stories are NC-17 for romance novel-style scenes.

Whoa Nellie is a writing team of two women with Psychology degrees who have been writing together for over a decade. We write romance-novel styled TNG and Voyager stories and most of our stories are NC-17 for romance novel-style love scenes. As we began writing our first work, 'Double Entendre,' when either of us went too far the other one would call out "Whoa Nellie!" while hitting the delete key. This practice stuck. When we decided to post our stories to the internet, 'Whoa Nellie' was a natural choice for us as a pen-name. In short, the Whoa Nellie stories are what happens when you give two women degrees in psychology, good chocolate, and way too much time on their hands. Star Trek Bodice-Rippers - Gotta Love that!

The stories posted here are edited down to R-rating or milder as a sampling of the romance fan fiction that can be found at our website.
