Hey, I'm Libra and I have been writing Harvest Moon fan fiction off and on for many years. My longest running fic Over the Harvest Moon I have been working on since 2001. I am determined to finish it!

Chapter Stories:

Over the Harvest Moon: A Back to Nature fan fiction about a man named Jack who takes over an old friend's farm after he passes away. Jack is given 3 years to make it successful and he often wonders if this is possible. This story concentrates more on Jack's relationships with the townsfolk. They are his key to success. In the end the ultimate question will be answered: Who was the little girl Jack made a promise to so long ago? In progress

Finding Success: A More Friends of Mineral Town fan fiction about a girl named Clara who is trying to escape an arranged marriage. She tries to prove she can take on life on her own without the help of a wealthy man. To do this she'll take over a run down farm. Who is the man she is supposed to marry? How do you define success? On hold

Short-Short stories: These are told in chapters, but with a shorter story within each chapter

So I Married a Goddess!: A Friends of Mineral Town fan fiction about a man named Jack who has found himself completely smitten by the Harvest Goddess. Each chapter documents a certain part of his relationship with the Goddess and is all told in the first person perspective. This story pokes fun at the way the game has you court the Goddess and how your life is after marrying her. In progress

Possible Future Work: These stories are in the intial planning stage and may or may not be written

Socialite and the Beast: A Harvest Moon DS Cute fan fiction. The story will revolve around a spoiled socialite who was never punished for anything in her life. After going to court for several crimes she is sentenced to an indefinite stay on a farm where she must work until she has paid off her debt to society. Who is the beast? Will her experience make her a better person?

Growing Up Harvest: A Wonderful Life fan fiction. This will be a short collection of essays written by Jason, the son of Muffy and Jack. He is grown up now, and has been required to submit a writing portfolio for his college English class. Each week he wrote about different topics that were about his life and how he got to where he is today. At the end of the semester Jason finally understands who he is and where he is going with his life. Each chapter will be a different essay.

Anything else listed on here is from when I was in high school and is complete crap. Just ignore it:)
