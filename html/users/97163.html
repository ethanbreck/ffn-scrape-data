Author has written 7 stories for Digimon, and Harry Potter.

I guess you can consider me back, considering it has been over three years since I last updated my profile and updated any of my stories. Honestly, it has not felt like it was that long, but I have been heavily focusing on my education and finding a job. I feel bad for not updating my stories, especially when get e-mails about people who favorite my stories or leave me a review. Not to mention that it personally bugs me when I get into a story and I find out that the story has not been updated in years, and the author seems to be missing in action in regards to their account.

So, the next step here is that I am going to revise and rewrite some of my newer stories, but not sure about my older stories. I don't have the heart to change something that I wrote when I was in middle school and early high school. They may not be the best, but they are something I wrote as well as proof as how far I have come as a person and a writer.

Regarding Right Now; At one point or another I had the next chapter and a half written, but I am not sure if i still have it on my laptop. Oops. I do plan on continuing this fanfic, but I need to plan where I want the story to potentially go as well as find someone who is willing to proofread my story. As for Closed, I do not want to say this on hiatus, but I am not sure where I want to go with this story. The idea behind the story is something that has been done plenty of times on this website, but sometimes it is not the idea but how you present it that makes it interesting, creative.

Anyway, except updates this summer as I will have some free time.
