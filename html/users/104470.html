Thought I’ll see how this posting on fanfic.net works out. What to say about me? I love to write, read, surf the net (of course!), update my 7 webpages, movies and music. I’ve written fan fic in many fandoms including X-men (300 something), Lord Of The Rings related (8 plus 22 something), Stargate: SG-1 (20 something), Buffy: TVS(1), Angel: TS(around 6), Star Trek: TOS(5), Roswell(1), original poems (300 something) and stories (7 something).
My stories varies from a G to a NC-17 rating but they are often dark, angsty and not completely sane. But to quote my best friend, the infamous Sorcieré: "hey – sane is boring" and I wholeheartedly agree.
Warning: I’m a free and open-minded person; my work reflects this. If you’re not then you shouldn’t read many of these stories. That way you’ll save us both a lot of time; you on cursing me to Hell’s fires (or something along those lines) and me trying to explain why I think it’s always wrong to hate blindly and that we all have (or rather should have) the same rights no matter skin colour, religion or sexuality.
Enjoy!
