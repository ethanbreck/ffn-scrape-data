Author has written 3 stories for Life is Strange.

As someone who believes in tradition, I'll start with an introduction.

I am primarily a writer of drama/adventure stories with some dabbling in romance/horror/comedy. I have plenty of ideas, but, unfortunately, less time to write them than I would like. Currently, my main fandom of choice is the video game, Life is Strange. I do have some desire to get into original fiction, but, hopefully, I'll stumble onto another fandom I'll love just as much and expand my repertoire.

FanFiction.Net (FFNET) is what I consider my primary writing account and where I'll post as many of my stories as possible. I also created an account on Archive Of Our Own (AO3) after hearing an exuberant paneler at an anime con spoke of it and I went to check it out myself. Finally, I also have an account on FictionPress.com (FPCOM) with the intention to upload my original stories there -- frankly, I would have been a lot happier if FFNET and FPCOM was one website and I get the inkling a lot of writers feel the same.

For those interested, here is my profile on each website:
FanFiction.Net: Demon King Piccolo
FictionPress.Com: CharacterFiction
Archive Of Our Own: CharacterFiction

The FFNET is my oldest account and, as I said, my main account. From the pseudonym alone, you can tell which fandom got me into fanfiction, eh? Anyway, I'll try to keep my AO3 account updated with the same stories as the ones in my FFNET account, so there will be no need to peruse the other if you prefer one. The FPCOM will be available with original writings.
