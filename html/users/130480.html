Hello there!

Moonchild DJ, aka Dee , here! Thank you for taking the time out to read my profile! am so proud and happy to be posting to FF.Net, and that people have been so nice with my modest lil' fics. I put a lot of time and thought into them-so please be nice...remember, though, I'm not perfect, so I can't please all of you, though I do try. I LOVE writing fanfiction, so be prepared for a lot. Yugioh, Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing, X-Men(especially with Jubilee), anime, crossovers...you name it, I generally write it! I hope you like 'em as they come along(barring writer's blocks), and do tell me what you think-that's what the reviews and the e-mail address are there for! I love hearing what you think, good or bad, it keeps the ideas a'flowing. I promise I won't bite. Thanks so much for checking out my fics, and take care, all!

True Bio(For Once!)

Name: Dee, but I mostly go by Moonchild DJ in the writing circles

Age: Old enough to know better, but still too young to care!

Place: USA--country girl, baby!

Favorite--

Movie: Lord of the Rings, naturally (glomps Aragorn and Legolas)X-Men, Batman, Star Trek, Brokeback Mountain, Memoirs of a Geisha, and many, many MANY others, too many to list!

Book: Star Trek movie novel by Alan Dean Foster, Phoenix Rising by Kimberly Gardner, Memoirs of a Geisha, Lord of the Rings trilogy, anything by Mercedes Lackey or Anne McCaffey, Supernatural novel series

Anime: Yugioh, Inuyasha, Gravitation, Haru wo Deitaita, Fullmetal Alchemist, Code Geass, Death Note, Naruto, Bleach, practically anything and everything of the yaoi nature, and many, many others too numerous to list!

Manga: Gravitation, Fullmetal Alchemist, Code Geass, Bleach, Naruto, etc, etc, etc. XD.

Pairings: I like a bit of everything! Although..I do not much care for Mary Sue/Gary Stu fics, be they het or yaoi, so...XD Mostly yaoi pairings in many of the titles I've already listed, but Seto/Jou still remains close and dear to my heart!

Hobby: Role-playing! I got into it about seven, eight years ago, and I've done it ever since. It keeps the creative juices flowing, and it's sure helped to bop away those writers' blocks! And also writing, reading, drawing, etc. =p Hm..I think that's all! Hope you learned a little more about me. =D

--Update as of September 28th, 2009--

I am terribly sorry to all of my readers for my lame lack of updates to my stories! Real life has gotten in the way, and I had writer's blocks up the wazoo. XP I am currently working on fixing the stories that have been the most demanded, and will try to get them all updated, for the most part! Please bear with me! I haven't forgotten about my stories! I rarely write het, even in RPs...please keep in mind that most, if not all of my works are yaoi-minded! Don't like, don't look. XD
