Hey everybody! I'm just here to tell you that I write for myself and what I feel. I won't change or alter anything in them to make more people like them. That's not what I'm here for. I write because I love to write not to make people feel better, or to have people 'like' me. All my Reviews are read and very appreciated, but if you don't like my story, please keep your opinion to yourself. But please feel free to contact me directly if you would like to discuss any of my ideas or stories.

I'm in search of a roleplaying partner. Mainly Harry Potter, but will branch out if it is interesting enough. Past, Present, or future, I don't care. I haven't had a decent roleplay in some time. I have AIM so if you're interested IM me at Psychoelfofchaos, you if you are on neopets, neomail me at starburstxrevolution. Thanks!

I only really write poetry and Harry Potter fics. Sometimes, a desire comes over me to write Sailor Moon, and SM/GW crossover fics. But I haven't posted them, I might if I ever feel the need to.

I have my own views as do all writers on here, and I also have my preferences on character coupling's. The most common that you'll see me do are: Hermione/Draco, Sirius/Rachel(My character), Usagi/Mamoru, Minako/Heero, Minako/Duo. I've also read some Luna/Remus and am trying to figure out an interesting story to go along it it.
