Author has written 10 stories for Mediator, Misc. Books, Secret Circle series, In The Forests of the Night, Song of the Lioness, and Ella Enchanted.
I am a thirteen-year-old North American freak. I love reading, writing (obviously), mythology and early civilizations. I've started a lot of stories - most of them have only one chapter up now. I update frequently - well, usually. I am obsessed with perfect spelling - it can be a very bothersome obsession sometimes. I love Meg Cabot (Jenny Carroll), Tamora Pierce, Vivian Vande Velde, Patrica C. Wrede, Phillip Pullman, Lloyd Alexander, John Bellairs, and James M. Barrie. I also love Relic Hunter and (I'm ashamed to admit this - even to myself) Brothers Garcia. I love the characters George Cooper and Damon Salvatore.
