Author has written 7 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
wintercreek is a 20 year old college student. She has apparently picked up the interesting and mildly annoying habit of referring to herself in the third person. Hmm.

wintercreek is currently on extended sabatical from this particular fandom. In plain English: her Buffy muse ran off to Canada. How inconsiderate! At any rate, please enjoy what's here but don't be surprised if significant time passes before anything is added.

Time now for a *shameless plug* for wintercreek's alter-ego, Alkalphiel (http://www.fanfiction.net/profile.php?userid=324819). Here there be Lord of the Rings fic, more specifically Legolas fic.

wintercreek's goal in writing fanfiction is to produce in the reader a moment of perfect stillness. It may be filled with a wave of grief, a fond smile, a sense of peace or many other things. The important part is that a hush falls, like snow on a frozen creek in winter. Listen.
