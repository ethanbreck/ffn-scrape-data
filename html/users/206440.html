Author has written 26 stories for Lord of the Rings.
I am a 29-year-old woman living in the eastern United States. I have a degree in English literature which I am utterly wasting by working as an executive assistant and writing fanfiction in my spare time.

I first read J.R.R. Tolkien’s “The Hobbit” when I was eight years old, and was delighted. When I was 10, I read “The Lord of the Rings” trilogy, and immediately fell in love with Middle Earth, and most of all, with the Shire. I have continued to love these characters and stories.

LOTR is the only fandom I write for. However, I have read and enjoyed The X-Files fanfiction for years, and have strolled through the fanfiction in a number of other fandoms. I also am a proud Ravenclaw.
