Well now, let's see if I can do this in a relatively mature way.

I'm a Diet Coke addict (just for the fun of it!) who's got an obsession with writing forgotten people's stories. My minimal writing skills and I wander the world, searching for ignored people/things to write about.

Constructive criticism is welcomed with open arms, as I’m trying my very best to improve. At the moment I’m kinda looking for a beta reader, or at least someone who wouldn’t mind helping me smooth out some of my stories’ rougher edges every now and then. I tend not to be a very speedy writer, so it wouldn’t be an awfully big job or anything.

Anyway, if you wouldn’t mind occasionally helping out a poor stupid kid like me, email me! It would positively make my day!

Tolkien's work has been my obsession for about three years now, although I've only started writing fanfics centering around Middle-earth since the movie came out and I managed to stumble across fanfiction.net. While I love The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit, my real favorite was The Silmarillion, with Maedhros the Tall (and one-handed…) as my favorite character. Nope, he ain’t perfect. But he’s awesome just the same!

Oh, and if you wanna read a really great story… read the story of Túrin. Best part in the book. Why? You’ll see. Wha ha ha. ^-^
