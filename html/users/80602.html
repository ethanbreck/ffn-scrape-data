Why is it that I never bother updating this thing for a few years, and then suddenly I get drawn back and do?

Anyway. Hi, I'm Varis. I'm 20, going to be 21 in 11 days. I don't write nearly as much as I used to because I've been distracted by Role-play, but I'm attempting to get back into the habit of it. The stories here were written when I was in my mid teens, and are all very...um, bad. So please do not judge my abilities as a writer by what you see here, because it's dismal at best. If you want a better grasp of how I write these days, turn your attention to To The Black my writing journal.

Otherwise, I enjoy a wide variety of fandoms, from Harry Potter (although I rarely read fiction or write for it), to Resident Evil (which I have written for, just not at length), and curving around to Legends of Zelda (Which I am currently working on.)

Hopefully I can make a touch of a comeback. Guess we'll see.
