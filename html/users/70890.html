Author has written 17 stories for Harry Potter, Bleach, and Gintama.

I like to read fanfiction! I'm very old, and so feel a little ashamed to be reading it at my age, but I try not to judge myself too harshly. I have been in and out of fandom for about 14 years now.

I've written fic in Gintama, Bleach, Sengoku Basara, and Harry Potter, which I may post some day (some are already posted on ff.net).

I'm not really active in any fandom anymore, but I still like to read.

Things that I love:

Friendship fic, subtle, drawn out romance/relationship, secondary character rivalry, main character angst, close to canon to start with, comedy duos, plot rich one shots, suspenseful punchlines, crackfic, unusual pairings or circumstances that shouldn't work but do because of the good writing and premise.

Gintama - Kondou, Hijikata, Shinsengumi, Katsura & Elizabeth, Otae, Kagura, Shou-chan, Hasagawa, Kondou/Katsura/Zurako, Kondou/Saachan, Kondou/Anyone, Gintoki/Tsukuyo, Kagura/Okita

Bleach - Grimmjow, Rukia, Ichigo, Renji, Uryuu, 11th Division, Byakuya and Rukia, Isshin and Ryuuken, Yumichika/Ikkaku, Byakuya/Renji, Uryuu/Renji, Byakuya/Kenpachi, Rukia/Ichigo

Naruto - Orochimaru, Kakashi, Gai, Jiraiya, Tsunade, Anko, Temari, Itachi, Kisame, Haku, Zabuza, Team Orochimaru, Team Gai, Team 10, Team Sannin, Sand nins, Gai/Kakashi, Temari/Shikamaru, Lee/Neji/Tenten, Sasuke & Naruto... orz

One Piece - Nami, Zoro, Buggy the Clown, Ace, Nami/Zoro, Shanks/Buggy

Harry Potter - Snape, Lucius Malfoy, Dumbledore, Percy Weasley, Slytherins, Hufflepuffs, Ron/Hermione, Ron/Draco

Things that I dislike (but am probably also guilty of):

OC main characters, obvious in-fic character bashing, women bashing in slash fic, unexplained OOC except in certain contexts, too quickly progressing relationships/resolutions, first person (most of the time), POV switching mid section, gratuitous unintentional in-world anachronisms

Takasugi/Katsura, Harry/Draco, Harry/Hermione, Kakashi/Iruka

Yamazaki.

Things that I've written (which may or may not be posted here):

I have fic that hasn't been updated since 2003. There have been a few hard disk crashes and external drive meltdowns. I have a lot of "WiP"s, so just so you know beforehand... updating is probably never going to happen for those old fic. Sorry about that. Not too big a fan of most of the old stuff anyway. My favorites are It's Karma, Baby, What Goes Around, and Towering Inferno because they're fun. Wish I'd finished those.
