Hello hello! Thanks for visiting my profile. I hope you're enjoying my work. Can you believe I've been a member of this site and penning fanfiction for the past 17 years? I have to laugh at my writing when I first began; I was such a silly teenager fangirl. I'm still a fangirl (woman), just with a bit more skill (I hope). I know my stuff isn't on par with a lot of authors out there (especially in the HP fandom) but I hope you still enjoy it.

My favorite activities are reading, writing, singing and playing video games. I guess I'm kinda boring! I am a Hufflepuff married to a Slytherin though, so that should speak volumes ;) I always like to hear from new people. You can also visit my tumblr (emerald-sparrow), I'd love to get to know ya!

You can also find me on Archive of Our Own and Ashwinder under emerald_sparrow.

I do enjoy romance, which one may have noticed. It's my favorite genre and I'm pretty loyal to it. The awesome thing is, you can add so many other elements to romance. My favorite other genres to mix with romance are humor and angst, just because that's how life tends to go, eh? But I like a little bit of everything... I just can't write it all!

The ships I'm sailing on:

Severus Snape/Hermione Granger (My OTP y'all, get out) Hades/Persephone Jareth/Sarah The Phantom/Christine Ares/Xena Drogo/Daenerys Imhotep/Anck-su-namun

Some of my favorite heroes I usually pair with OC are: Captain Jack Sparrow, Ulfric Stormcloak (love/hate relationship with this arsehole), Brynjolf, Uldren Sov

That's all I've got, so just remember folks - especially those of you who put a little too much emphasis on the criticism in "constructive criticism": It’s hard to reach for the stars when you have your head up your arse.
