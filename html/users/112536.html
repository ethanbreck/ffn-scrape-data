7th October 2007
As the last time I updated this account was all the way back in August 2004, I imagine that by now most people have (correctly) assumed that the chances of a new chapter are extremely slim at best.

However, a slim chance is not no chance. Before anyone gets their hopes up (if indeed, anyone even remembers me and is bothering to read this):
- Yes, the ol' brain cells have been stirring a little restlessly, and have reminded me that LNR was sadly left hanging in the middle. And yes, I feel it was a shame that I never managed to complete it.
- However, at the same time: Life continues unabated. At the moment I simply don't have the time to gather all the disjointed threads of a story that I've forgotten the future of (there are other more immediate/important concerns to devote that energy to).

Plus...well, I've kinda lost all my notes, though I'm sure that they're languishing in a box somewhere. :p

Basically, though I've not forgotten about the story, please don't expect anything to materialise - I fully realise that time may yet kill the fic completely. I'd far rather surprise people who've all but given up on this than disappoint those that keep on hoping to no avail.
~Jheya
