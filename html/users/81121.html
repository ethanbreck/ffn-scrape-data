Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter.

When I was younger (so much younger than today...), I had this silly delusion that I would become the Next Big Thing in HP fandom. Quite obviously that didn't happen, but I'll keep this profile for laughs and self-indulgence. Enjoy my middle school attempts at writing fic, if you dare. You're lucky the Newsies stuff never saw the light of day.

I'm not the diehard Potter fangirl I once was (though I still Believe in Trees) and currently only lurk on the favorites lists of a few key authors, but I'm currently loving The Office, Fruits Basket, Firefly, and Ouran High School Host Club fic. And I can never get enough of the Will/Bran in The Dark Is Rising.

Who am I? A left-handed theatre freak; that says it all.
