Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter.

I got into fanfic through Harry Potter and an undying love for Severus Snape, which quickly morphed into Severus/Hermione obsession, though I have been known to indulge in the occasional Severus/Remus, Severus/Lucius, as well as Lucius/Hermione.

So, when's the next update of The Wedding, you ask? I'm sad to say I don't know. The part of me responsible for fanfic writing died with The Deathly Hallows, and while other SS/HG writers have been able to move on, alas, I cannot. Not many writers understand the difficulties of keeping such a complex being as Severus Snape in character; on that note, please check my Favorite Authors list for the writers that do manage to keep him in form while spinning a fantastic tale as well.

My stories

All Secrets Sleep in Winter Clothes, an action/drama/romance between Sev and Hermione, set five years after graduation. Sev has been uncovered as a spy and Hermione is forced back to Hogwarts to protect him. In one year, they will have to survive Death Eaters, a mystery potion, Christmas at the Burrow, a rousing game of quidditch, Ron Weasley's lack of love life, Draco Malfoy sneaking about, and Harry Potter, who is up to no good as well.

Subterranean Homesick Potions Master: has been removed due to pushing the R boundary. Don't want to risk being kicked off ff.net before I can tame it some.

The Best Laid Plans of Cats and Men! Severus Snape is going to try out his new love potion at the Valentine's Dance, but things go horribly awry when Minerva McGonagall falls for ... Argus Filch?! It's all fun and games until someone falls for Filch...

Severus Snape and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day, my entry for the LiveJournal ficathon. Just when his day couldn't get any worse, it does. A funny, cute, short one-shot, HG and SS of course!
