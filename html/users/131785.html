Author has written 24 stories for X-Men: The Movie, ThunderCats, Slayers, X-Men: Evolution, StarTrek: The Next Generation, X-Men, Smallville, Fanfiction Poetry, and Criminal Minds.

I love writing fan fiction. What-ifs fascinate me. Some of my work may contain only cannon characters re-imagined or also have original characters as well. I am interested in a wide range of TV series, movies, manga, comics, animation, novels, and anime. I am also not afraid to tackle cross-overs and find was to make different universes work together. When possible, I uploaded a character illustration as the cover art as I am a professional artist in my own right. I hope you enjoy my work and if you would like to see some more of the illustrations that go with a few of these stories, feel free to head on over to my website www.glamourcat.deviantart.com!
