Ok, I think it's time I updated my bio. I'm a Harry Potter freak from somewhere around Chicago, IL. (I'm still trying to figure out where. It's so big around here).

When not reading Harry Potter, (or writing it for that matter) I'm working on my original fic, Millenium Ninjas.

The OotP fic? I think everyone knows what that's about. Harry Potter gets chosen for the Order of the Phoenix and strange things begin to happen around Hogwarts. Of course, now with the release of OotP, I realize once again that it sux, but I still have hope for my other fic, Revenge of the Muggles.

RotM is set in the future after Voldemort has been defeated. You really have to read it to understand it so I suggest reading it because it is much better than my other fic.

I will read most Harry Potter fics, though I try to refrain from reading H/Hr fics or other fics with the exact words "Harry had been noticing how pretty she had gotten lately," or "Harry knew that his best friend liked his other best friend." If you can't be at least a little bit discreet in your stories about relationships, please don't write it. Thank you very much.
