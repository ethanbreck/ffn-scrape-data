I havn't updated this in a LONG time. My name is Gyousano Kon, or just Kon if you're going to talk to me on one of my IMers. I like to read things that are dark and make me really question everything I know...and I draw, play piano and sing a lot. All plans for Unjust Seduction have come to a screeching halt. The story was meant to be the second story in the series of stories...But my plans of writing a five-part series were doused when my partner quit...sad look He was too busy, so I won't bug him about it though. An endless night is running on autopilot right now, and it's not taking it very far. I had a plan and everything, but it sort of leaked away when my maid trashed the paper my plot was on. And so, for forgiveness from all you wonderful reveiwers, I have started another fic. It's going to be super dark; so be warned! "Desire's Deciet" The title is actually going to be connected to the plot, and I am determined to finish it sometime...unlike my other floating stories, which have yet to be updated in months, if not years. The plotline for it was trashed too, but luckily I wrote everything I needed down on my trusty palm pilot first, so I HAVE the storyline this time. Huggles palm pilot I have several people on my favorite's list if you need something more 'thrilling' than the stuff I've written so far, and I have some others I'd like to point out. 1101 by Lord Chaos is posted on Moonromance.net and Dance of Death and Caress of the Night both by Lunar Rose are also posted on Moonromance.net Any other great stories I've found over the years are in my favorites section. I might be missing a few though...so I'll go check it. (and still probably miss a few...) Almost anything written by LunarRose, Lord Chaos, Tap-chan, Sailor Perfect, Kimra, and Meara will turn out to be a wonderful fanfic.

UPDATE

I really don't feel like reading through all that I typed above just to find what I need to change and delete...all that junk. So I'll just update here. I am sad to inform you that the worst possible message an author can deliver you is about to be delivered by moi. T.T Due to outside circumstances, my stories will be on hold until I can take care of real life matters. Sorry to be informing you of this so late...but it can't be put off any longer. Someday the stories will be finished, but until then, don't expect updates for a LONG while. Sorry...again. If you are just DYING to know--right now, how any of them were planned to end, I am willing to spoil it for you if you email me or something. XD Sorry again...
