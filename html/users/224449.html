Wow, It's been quite some time since I've wondered about my FanFiction account and stories. Actually, up until about 3 days ago I didn't even remember that I had an account. I guess that's the real tragedy of MySpace, itn't it? I get a MySpace and suddenly I forget that there's an entire internet out there for me to explore...

But I'll try not to get pulled in to a rant about MySpace.

I have decided to give this whole "writing as a hobby" thing a second go. I might go back to Harry Potter fanfics or I might take a swing at some stand-alone fiction. Whatever I do, I'm really psyched about writing again. I'm more than a little out of practice, but thanks to the wonders of MSN messenger I do type a lot faster than once upon a time.

Hopefully I'll have something new posted soon!

- Cheshire Bat (Mary)
