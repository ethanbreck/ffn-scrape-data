Yuri loves Lilies. Hence the name. Maxwell is to honor the great Shinigami that Yuri adores.

Yuri would like to apologize for not updating. Yuri's fics get written when they want to (they have a mind of their own). Not to mention, that her plot bunnies are always hiding from her. They love to hibernate for long periods of time regardless of time, season or reason.

But Yuri is back. And she hopes to get something written very soon. Well, at least as soon as she finds he old notes. ~ _ ~;;

Anyway, feel free to email Yuri comments, suggestions or whatever. She likes having people write to her.
