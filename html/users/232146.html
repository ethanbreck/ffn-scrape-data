Why Hello There!

I am a 23 year old graduate student attempting a PH.D. in cell biology and continuously reading fanfiction to procrastinate. Feel free to send me a message, maybe a link to your story! I'll read most anything once at this point.

After a decade of silence, I have rediscovered FF.net, surprisingly unchanged from how I left it back in 2004. Since then, I have survived much, acquired a few degrees, moved on my own, and now delve myself into life as a biology graduate student at the ripe age matching that of my current fandom's own Arthur Kirkland.

I wanted to update this profile to show my current state of mind - how drastically it has morphed, evolved, bloomed, or even begun to mold - but I could not bring myself to remove the ramblings of a girl still bursting with glee from 2004.

Below, I have chosen to preserve what remained of my profile upon stumbling back down the rabbit hole. Enjoy her rambles as I once did.
