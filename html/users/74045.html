Author has written 2 stories for Rurouni Kenshin, Lord of the Rings, and Anime X-overs.

Although I hope that you enjoy my fics, as I release them, I write primarily for my own amusement, and as such, I write to no schedule. Sometimes I'll churn out 30 pages in a day, sometimes I'll go for weeks, even months without writing anything. I'm currently writing one epic length fic, with detailed outlines for another seven fics, detailed ideas for ten more fics, and vague ideas for an additional three fics. All of which will be in some massive crossover continuity, all of which will be a MINIMUM of a hundred typed pages. Minimum. So, you may see something here in the near future, or you may not--my muse is a fickle, fickle thing.

About me: I like fanfiction, particularly crossover anime fanfiction. My favorite anime, in no particular order, are Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing, Card Captor Sakura, Yu Yu Hakusho, Inuyasha, Ranma 1/2, Magic Knights Rayearth, Flame of Recca, Rurouni Kenshin, Slayers, Dragonball Z, and Yu-Gi-Oh. I also like Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Harry Potter, Star Wars, various incarnations of Final Fantasy, various DC comics, and Lord of the Rings. For some of my favorite fics and authors, see both my favorites lists and my C2 group, which is basically just all the stories I like that are rated PG-13 or below. Of my favorite professional authors, Mercedes Lackey tops the list, followed by Patricia C. Wrede, Anne McCaffrey, Tanya Huff, Robin McKinley, Diane Duane, Tamora Pierce, Katherine Kurtz, David Eddings, Piers Anthony, L.E. Modesitt Jr., J.R.R. Tolkien, J.K. Rowling, and Roald Dahl. Plus George Carlin and Jon Stewart. ;)

I'd like to thank everyone who has reviewed and encouraged me with Fear No Darkness. I assure you it has not been abandoned; I have merely hit a bit of writer's block regarding the best way to phrase something in the middle of chapter 3, which is already 7 pages long and hasn't even hit the half-way point. Also, I may be posting both the story to date and my fragments of chapter 3 at my new livejournal if I can't get past this snag in the very near future, and possibly even if I can.

6/30/09: Well, I've finally managed to find a fic idea that has transitioned into a final product quickly enough to be posted! This is something of an event, as my ideas tend to be for epic length crossovers that get bogged down at the outline stage. Anyway, once a day over the month of July, I will be posting drabbles featuring various anime characters as part of the Fellowship of the Ring. There will be a character for every letter of the alphabet. I've already finished the first five, and three other non-sequential ones, so I'm hopeful I can pull it off. See you tomorrow with Never Joined the Fellowship! (Warning: Pure Crack Ahead!)
