FROM ME

Okay i'm a terrible terrible terrible person. I haven't written a story in an extremely long amount of time. Or posted one in even longer. My excuse is that i've been swept up with school and homework and assignments but truthfully i'm not that motivated anymore. Ichanged schools at the beginning of 2004 and I think my inspiration stayed at my old school with all my old friends.

I'm currently in the middle of a few stories with friends. The Chosen Two. Middle Earth Academy. To the Tip of Mount Caules. But i'm finding it very difficult to find the patience to write much of them. Most of the stories I write are very fictional comedy. "Legolas are you sane?" is completely nonsensical. My main prizes are my poems. Short little things with simple rhymes that barely make any sense. You should read my poems about the different members of the fellowship. I'm very proud of them. The seem to get fairly good reviews.

I am supportive of:
