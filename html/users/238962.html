Hello all...

I tend to write lengthy tales. I can do short stories of course but I like to develop thick plots about characters. I do not normally write fanfic unless I am really moved to by a story that suddenly comes to my mind for whoever the fanfic is for. I write both original and fanfiction based works, but often with new plot lines that are either AU or continuations. I've written more than my share but rarely publish as these are often for my own twisted pleasure in writing. I go through chapters fast bc I live in fear that I may stop and not finish the story, leaving in the purgatory that is the In-Progress pile.

'Of Tea and Chess' began as a two chapter deal where I was going to have smut on a tea table. It has gone beyond that and absorbed my writing that I normally reserve for work and articles. It became a novel. It spawned two sequels and essentially became what I've taken to calling the "Games of Wonderland" series. The first sequel, Wild Cards & Gambits, started as one story but had to be broken up into two, with the third novel in the series being Illusions & Destinies. It gave me a chance to bring everything about full circle and continue developing the mythology I've had to develop. Thank you all for reading/still reading this series. It's been a rewarding thing to write.

my LJ is serskye.livejournal.com. feel free to friend me. You can twitter friend me @tea_chess and tumblr me (but be gentle) at teaandchess but be aware, insanity may follow.

Feel free to shoot me an email as well!
