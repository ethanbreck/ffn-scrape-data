Author has written 9 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Deltora Quest, Harry Potter, and Dragon Ball Z.

Quick Info: I have been on ff.net since 2002 and still have aspirations of writing. I have had many hiatuses and dry spells, but have never given up entirely on finishing my stories.

All my pieces are either one shots or unfinished novel length fics. I am currently rewriting 'Lovefools' under the name 'Lovefools Part 1', and intend to update it once a fortnight where possible. The original story of Lovefools is still on my profile, but it is now discontinued. I mostly keep it on the net to remind me how far I've come.

'History Repeats' is a work in progress, though it will take much longer than Lovefools.

'What Became of Tira' is postponed indefinitely.

I also have a Lion King idea in the works, but will likely not be posted until Lovefools is finished.

Reviews keep me going, so if you like my story even a little bit, or have any contructive criticism to offer, please comment, as I really do appreciate it.

UPDATE:

Just to let people know I'm still alive, yes I know it has been a very long time since I have posted. Lovefools Part 2 was postponed a lot whilst I dealt with other life things and worried about how to make the story I wanted to tell cohesive. It is back in progress now, and yes I WILL be finishing this story.

I thank everyone very much for their encouragement and constructive criticism offered in Part 1 - it has helped a great deal.
