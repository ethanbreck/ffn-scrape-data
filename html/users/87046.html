I'm Ninetalesuk! And I've been on FFNet for 6 whole years with my style of writing evolving along the way.

My favourite shows are Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, Pokemon and Sonic X, along with shows from the UK that you never heard of before... Top Gear, Have I Got News For You, Only Fools and Horses, etc.

My favourite Pokemon includes Vulpix, Ninetales, Eevee, Espeon, Glaceon, Arcanine, Lugia, Articuno, Lucario, Mightyena and Suicune.

My favourite Digimon includes Alphamon, ExVeemon, ShineGreymon, MirageGaogamon, Imperialdramon Fighter Mode, Susanoomon, Gallantmon and Imperialdramon Paladin Mode.

I'm also interested in Duel Monsters, the infamous game from Yu-Gi-Oh. Mostly the Elemental Heroes, Destiny Heroes, Crystal Beasts and Dragon-type monsters. My favourite ones are Elemental Hero Shining Flare Wingman, Elemental Hero Darkbright, Elemental Hero Plasma Vice, Elemental Hero Neos, Destiny Hero - Plasma, Red-Eyes Darkness Dragon and Armed Dragon LV10.

Some other things like favourite Sonic the Hedgehog characters (Sonic the Hedgehog, Miles "Tails" Prower, Knuckles the Echidna, Shadow the Hedgehog and Blaze the Cat), All Dogs go to Heaven (Charlie, Sasha, Itchy, Annabelle and Flo), Lady and the Tramp (Lady, Tramp, Scamp and Angel) and The Lion King (Simba, Nala, Kiara, Sarabi, Kovu, Sarafina). There are other favourites, but it'll take too long... heh

Favourite video games include Sonic Adventure DX, Pokemon Pearl/Diamond, Digimon World Dawn/Dusk, Tales of Symphonia, Skies of Arcadia Legends, Grandia 2, Digimon Rumble Arena 2, Pokemon XD; Gales of Darkness, Kingdom Hearts 2 and many more!

The type of writing I use is comedy, action and Duel Monsters! But, I can come up with emotional, serious and dramatic scenes amongst others to make my stories interesting.
