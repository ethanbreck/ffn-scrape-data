I'm 19, second year student at the University of Victoria. Don't know how much I'll be writing, but I was just reading back on some of my old stuff I wrote and never posted, and forgot how much I missed it.

I just finished "The Soldiering Life", as a one-part. I may be writing a piece about Spike and Drusilla in South America, tying in "Condolences" and "The Soldiering Life", as if it all happened within the same sort of Universe. Will probably write another 2 pieces then with it too. Who knows?

I'll be working on "Grief, Subject to Change" BEFORE ANYTHING ELSE - Hopefully. Hopefully a chapter up in the next week or so. May be trashing chapter 7 and starting it again. My creativity hops all over the place, I never know what I'm gonna write about until I do.

Would love to hear from other writers, especially some criticism. I'm a writing major, I have a thick skin to people slamming things they don't like about my work. It makes the good comments all the more heartfelt.
