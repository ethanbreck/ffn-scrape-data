Oi! Lady AngelFiren here.

Thought I'd tell you a little about myself. I'm 19, I live in Ottawa, Canada, and I love to write/draw/read.

Anime is my main focus. Gumdam Wing is the topic for most of my fiction, but lately I am more into Dragonball Z... As many of you must alrady know!! Thanks to everyone who's taken time to review 'Being Vegito' !!! You've all encouraged me greatly, and I've Got plenty of ideas cooking upstairs, so i do believe that more DBZ fics are on the way.

NEKO-HEERO IS FINISHED!! OVER! KAPUT! COMPLETED!!!!!!!!!!!! Bet you all thought I'd never do it, huh? well, I've proved you all wrong, you're all encouraged to re-read the whole thing, as it will really make the story much more satisfying to read. Please tell me what you think, and THANKS TO ALL OF YOU for your support and encouragement with this strange and stranger tale. I LOVE YOU!!!! EVERY LAST ONE OF YOU!!!!

Well after a very long absence i have returned to the site to give you more of my work. My biggest project is SCARS right now. I went back through the fic and read the whole thing (i do so love to read my own work) and i am working of fixing chapters 28 and 29, because i think they suck, i noticed some bad mistakes in grammar and spelling and sentence structure that made reading those chapters a pain in the ass. I have already fixed Chapter 30, and there is more on the way, i just need some time to make sure i wrote exactly what i wanted to write before i post it.


I'm always looking for good fics, but I tend to hate browsing endless webpages just to find old junk or plotless lemons. If anyone knows any good DBZ fics (mainly I like anything centered around the saiyajins and Bulma(yaoi/non-yaoi)), then give me a shout. I'm a fan of novel-sized stories, so the longer the better. ^_^

Oh! And for the readers of my unfinished Gundam Wing Fiction, here is a breakdown of what i have or have not been working on of late. Firstly, the fic Essence, as proud as i am of it thus far, will probably never be finished. Its a great read, but hella annoying when you come to the part where I stopped and realize that there's NO MORE TO READ!!! However, I have brighter predictions for The Vampire Heero. I have gone backwards through the fic and re-read almost the entire thing in order to re-familiarize myself with Duo's changeable characteristics as i had originally portrayed them. I intend to finish The Vampire Heero if it takes me the rest of my damn life. I am immensely proud of my work on it, and I promise all of you not to let it go to waste! Although...we are not near the end yet, and i refuse to sacrifice quality just to get a fic over with.
