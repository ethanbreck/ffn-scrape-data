Personal Opinion

Alternate universe = good.

Time travel = better.

Crossovers = best.

I don't think Malik has a yami/omote/mou hitori no boku.

Japanese words can't be pluralized, people.

If you're going to call Willow and Tara Wiccan, then do some damn research first. Hello. It's a religion, not a magic system.

Swearing? It's a word. Get over it.

I have nothing against het. I just like slash better because I love conflict. For example: threesomes? I'm there. Plus, a lot of canon het just doesn't seem like it has real chemistry to me. I love role defiance.

When Kenshin says 'sessha,' he does not speak in third person as we think of it. Japanese people always technically speak in third person, and the dubbers were just trying to show that his use of 'sessha,' '-dono' and 'de gozaru' instead of 'desu' sounded very strange to their ears. Not bizarre, crazy, and just plain stupid, which is what they got instead.

If you have absolutely horrid grammar, learn better grammar before you post a story. Honestly, if you do like writing, it should be a labor of love. And it's horrible to see people write things when they can't even put quotations in the right way and they're posting it for public consumption! Usually I promote people posting what they write and getting feedback, but it makes me cringe occasionally, and you do have to have genuinely despicable grammar. Spelling doesn't bother me as much, though www.dictionary.com is there for a reason, you know.
