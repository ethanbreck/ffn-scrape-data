Alias, Angel, Babylon 5, Battlestar Galactica (1978), Battlestar Galactica (2003), Bones, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Castle, Caprica, CSI, CSI Miami, CSI NY, Crusade, Firefly, Galactica 1980, Grey's Anatomy, JAG, Jeremiah, Jericho, NCIS, NCIS Los Angeles, Private Practice, Sports Night, Star Trek: DS9, Star Trek Enterprise, Star Trek: TAS, Star Trek: TOS, Star Trek: Voyager, Stargate Atlantis, Stargate Infinity, Stargate SG-1, Stargate Universe, The Finder, The Lone Gunmen, The Newsroom, The West Wing, and The X-Files. In addition to TV and reading, in my spare time I enjoy tinkering with and building computers, hanging out with friends, listening to music, cooking, playing video games (World of Warcraft and Pogo most recently).
