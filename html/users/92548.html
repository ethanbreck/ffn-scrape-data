I had several unfinished stories posted here at one point but I retired from posting fanfiction many moons ago. To give you some idea: since I last posted, I graduated from college, began and completed graduate school, got married, and moved from the mountains to the piedmont of North Carolina.

I write off and on: for a few years I have been seventy-five single-spaced pages in to a Labyrinth piece, and forty into a Snape-Granger story. But I can never seem to end them right, and don't want to post anything till I know I won't abandon it, since there is nothing I hate more than abandoned stories! (Well, except for poor grammar, "I don't own this" disclaimers, and smut written by virgins; no offense ...)

I enjoy reading well written post-Half-Blood Prince Snape-centered works, and I ship Jareth / Sarah (10+ years post-Labyrinth), Granger / Snape (10+ years post-DH), and Sheldon / Amy (since "The Lunar Excitation," you guys). Back in the day Mulder / Scully was my OTP, but I haven't thought much about them in recent years. In general, I really and truly enjoy well written lemony goodness.

I hold a Master's degree in English and work in book publishing. When I'm not working (or reading fanfic), I'm most often working on my fitness, spending time with my husband and our dog, shooting portraits and weddings, participating in community get togethers and events, eating delicious vegetarian dishes, and drinking craft beer. Not all at once. Although, actually, sometimes most at once.
