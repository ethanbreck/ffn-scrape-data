Err... Well, I'm not sure my private life interests anyone!! So if you're not interested, don't read...

I'm a 20 yo girl... and of course I like writing, that's why I'm posting stories, isn't it? The fics I post here are all about Harry Potter; other stuff of mine isn't on the site because it's not using anybody's characters or universe but mine, and so it hasn't its place on ff.net! I'm still wondering what makes us all so keen on writing about HP... I mean, the original books are a wonderful piece of fantasy, with a whole fantastic (in both significations) universe and lots of terrific characters, but there's loads of great books out there, and... just look at the list of the "books" category on ff.net, and the number of fics for each... But I suppose the fact that there's still books to come in the serie keep us waiting, and fantasizing about it... disproportionly! (Does that word exist?)

Anyway, actually, there's also loads of other things I'm interest in, from history and philo to maths and physic, and also literature and languages (ancient and foreign), etc, etc, etc... which can explain that I'm usually totally overwhelmed by really harassing studies in lots of subjects at the same time (I couldn't choose only one subject at the Uni)... Apart from that I'm a musician (violin player). I also like horse-riding very much, but I've haven't got any more time to practice often, and have had to stopped for some years becase of an accident in which my ankle got hurt enough that it handicaped me for 1 year and a half ! That's about it about my usual hobbies...

Otherwise, I live alone in Paris. I have friends, and a family, but well... especially friends, except if you consider my little sister, which is now 13 - and 13 is not a comfortable age ! What else? People that know me often say that my until-now-not-so-easy life made me strong, though perhaps (certainly ?) it's just a gentle way to explain how stubborn I am... Like having character perhaps means not being very sufferable !! Anyway two things I learnt so far are that

judging is silly, understanding is the only available path,

and

everything ever goes forward, including... err, including everything !

But we're getting quite private, here; Read my fics, that's more interesting !
