Hi. My name is Nina. I’m 14 years old, and have just recently discovered to blessed gift of fanfiction. The angel who came a night in my dream and told me about FF.net where I could be together with other humans of my specie, was Zille.
( Notice the religious metaphors I’m using aren’t because I’m any kind of religious fanatic, actually I’m an atheist.. )

Anyway, let’s get to the point: I love X-files. Actually that’s al you need to know about me, but it’s much more fun to tell a lot about yourself witch no one really wanna bother read anyway.

I also like Harry Potter. I know some people say it’s pretty childish to read those kind of books, like a person from my school once said to me when I asked her if she read Harry Potter. “I´ve read some of the first book, I think it was boring. It’s probely fun, if you’re ten.”
Okay, thank you! I´ve same humour as if I was ten! So stay away all people from the girl whose mind always will remain like a ten-year old. Keep distance or you might be infectedwith the disease! And don’t read any of the things she writes, you might come to like Harry Potter then!
I, despite the girl from my school, think Harry Potter is fantastic! I don’t know where JKR get her imagines from, but I sure would be glad to borrow it, just for a day maybe? Please?
I’m always trying to find a plot to a new fic, but it’s just gone! All the ideas I had when I first entered the FF.net are gone!
I´ve begun to write an X-files fanfic ( “Flenched” ) but I have no idea what should happen now. Absolutely now ideas! So therefor I would be very glad if anybody would please read and review and tell me if they liked it, what I should rewrite and what should happen next. ( Hint, hint ;-)
I think I’m that sort of person, who actualy like to not end the things you start.
When I’ve written one chapter of a story I am sure will be really good, I get some kind of writers bloc. But it always first happen after I've posted it.
So maybe I should stop that?
No way! ‘Cause I love all the reviews I get, and I can’t live without some kind of response to my stories.

Back to HP:
The books are fiction ( great fiction! ), which is why I think they are so good.
Couse seriously, who would ever wanna read realistic books! I mean you live in the realistic world every day, why also wanna read about it?

I know my spelling sucks, but I’m excused! I live in Denmark, and have therefor only had English since 4th great, that makes 4 and ½ year! Wich is not very much! ( Okay, it actually is...) So, please excuse me for every mistake I might make.

As you just have heard, I live in Denmark, so the X-files ( the actually show.. ) is only at the beginning at season 9. And it sucks. Big time. Of course I then have some episodes to look forward to, but I would so have liked to see the final episode, last Sunday.
I was so depressed, it ended! It didn’t make it better to read all the X-files fanfics, written after the final, ‘cause they were all like “Chris Carter can kiss my ass!” and “Does Mulder care about Scullys feelings at all!” … It doesn’t sound nice…

Anyway, please don’t tell me what happens after “Trust no 1”.

Hi to Helena from my class, if she decides to drop by, and thank you so much, Zille, for all those references you have to me and my fics in the beginning of your great fics! And always remember: Long live the Basilisk!

If you read my work I would really like to get some response, so I can improve my writing.
Thank you!

X-Nina ( ninahn@sol.dk )
