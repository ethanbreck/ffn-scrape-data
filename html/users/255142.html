Well, my name is Chris, pennane Godfather, and I'm back on fanfiction.net for the first time in. . .a while. I have a lot of fanfictions here from high school days, and I may update them, but my focus right now is on my Boondocks fic, as that has become my favorite show by far.

I like to write and I love to draw, so if you can, check me out at I've definitely got some Boondocks stuff in the works there as well (and I do take requests)

Fanfiction. net has changed a lot in my absence (half of these features I don't even recognize), but I'm glad to see that at its core, its the same writers' haven it was four years ago. I thrive on constructive criticism, and to me the thoughtful, mixed reviews are worth ten of the "Wow, keep it up" reviews that tend to offer little qualitatively about my writing. By all means, if something strikes you as odd (rampant grammatical errors, poor characterization, insufficient detail) point it out. I've long since come to terms with the fact that I'm not a perfect writer, and the only way to sharpen my edge is to recieve good, unbiased feedback from others. In return, when I review fics I try my best to be encouraging and yet still my best assessment of the strengths AND weaknesses within.

Well, thats all i can think of to say for now. Feel free to drop a comment, and I look forward to interacting with the fanfic community once again.
