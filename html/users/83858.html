Hi! You may have noticed I've deleted the old insane sugar-rush bio and have written something a bit less hyperactive. Warning, I did say a bit.

Name: Crazed Zarbon Otaku. My real name is none of your business.

Gender: Female, in case you haven't guessed yet.

Country of residence: Australia.

Age: 14.

Likes: Anime, especially DBZ, The Irresponsible Captain Tylor, Tenchi Muyo, Van Dredd and Fushigi Yugi. I also love to read, especially fantasy books like Harry Potter and some sci-fi like Animorphs. I do try my hand at video games a bit, and adore all of the Legend of Zelda series.

Dislikes: THE BIG O, otherwise known as EFAATSNBN (excuse for an anime that shall not be named), and Pokemon, EFAATSNBN2. I hate peppermint, bubblegum and soft drink. I have absolutely no idea why, but I can't eat or drink any of them. Vegetables are not on my menu if I can help it, but I'll put up with them.

Characters I like: Zarbon (or Zarbie, as my friend Time Soldier and I like to call him), Vegeta, Bardock, Tylor, the Empress, Mr. Alien (I love calling him that), Tenchi, Washu (her hair is cool... OK, that's a lame reason, but I can't help it. Cool hair is very good. ^_~), Kafei, Nayru, Link, Saria, Hotohori, Tamahome, Marco, Ax, Cassie, Sirius, Lily, Remus, Ron.

Hated Characters: WANG! I despise that guy. Pettigrew, every character in EFAATSNBN, and EFAATSNBN2. Malon too. I CANNOT STAND HER!

Favourite couples: Tylor/Empress (they're too cute), Hotohori/Miaka (So I'm weird? I happen to think they're cool.), Vegeta/Bulma (they make me laugh), Zarbon/Fasia (So she's my character? I still like them.), Saria/Mido, Link/Zelda, Ax/Estrid, Deiter/Mr. Alien. (So sorry, habit.)
Least favourite couples: Uh... Bra/17. I hate that couple... Link/Malon, all couples in the EFAATSNBN and EFAATSNBN2. I don;t hate yaoi, and I think the authors can do anything they like, because I never read it anyway.

I hope that, by reading that weirdness, you got an insight into my mind... probably not, but don't worry.

And if anyone has any Tylor or Zarbon voice clips, images or mangas in the case of Zarbon, send them to me, they're greatly appreciated.

If you managed to suvive the insanity, continue on!
