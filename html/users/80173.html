Author has written 29 stories for Digimon, Card Captor Sakura, Zoids, Haruhi Suzumiya series, Death Note, Gurren Lagann, Harry Potter, Doctor Who, Torchwood, and Glee.

Perhaps it's time to post some proper profile content here.

About me: I live in Melbourne, Australia, and I've been writing fanfiction since who-knows-when. I'm mainly involved in anime fandoms although I dabble in sci fi and fantasy as well. I'm also a keen convention-goer (again, mostly anime) and I'm a voluntary organiser for the Melbourne Anime Festival.

Fandom history: Digimon was my first big fandom used to be my happy place from about 2000 onwards, though it was also a hotbed of drama, and though I dabbled with other things during my Digimon phase (Card Captor Sakura especially) it took me ages to move on. Zoids was my next big fandom, from 2003 to about 2006-7. I wrote some fic for Zoids but most of my fandom involvement was reading and beta-reading, and I eventually lost interest when Zoids Genesis turned out to be a bit of a disappointment. Since then I've floated around with interest in Doctor Who and Harry Potter before finding inspiration in The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya, which is now where I do most of my writing. I still mess around with Who, HP and other anime, but Nishinomiya is where my heart is right now.

The fandoms I write for aren't always necessarily the ones I think are the best. I read and watch reasonably widely, at least as far as anime and manga go. My all time favourite anime are Neon Genesis Evangelion, Revolutionary Girl Utena, The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya and Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann. One of those things is not like the others, huh? Given that Haruhi was on that list I thought I'd never find fanfic ideas for it, but somehow I don't stop getting ideas for more.

Recent writings: I've almost finished posting all of The Melancholy of Shinigami Ryuk and as much fun as it's been to write, I'm going to be very glad when it's over. And I'll probably move on to some other lengthy work that will have me bashing my head on a keyboard wondering why I got myself into this.

Specifically, I'm working on a Haruhi Suzumiya/Ouran High School Host Club crossover, a Haruhi/Doctor Who crossover and a lengthy future fic with Kyon as a teacher at North High. Yeah.

I don't know whether I'll ever finish The Last Enemy. I love it, and I find myself scribbling little Jack/Albus ideas every now and again, but I can't seem to find a neat ending for that particular fic.

And in case anyone honestly wants to know, all the fandoms I care about in any significant way, in no paricular order: Digimon (Tamers was my favourite series although Adventure inspired the most fic) Card Captor Sakura, XXXholic, Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicle (while we're on a CLAMP roll) Bleach (just for Yoruichi/Soi Fon; sadly I lost interest in the manga ages ago) Mahou Sensei Negima, Naruto (also stopped reading the manga ages ago but I love Sakura/Ino and Gai/Kakashi) Cowboy Bebop, Zoids (the friendliest fandom I've ever been in) Death Note, Dramacon (DRAMACON!) Evangelion, Utena, Fruits Basket, Gurren Lagann, Gravitation, Harry Potter, Hanazakari no Kimitachi e (J-drama) Stand Up!! (also J-Drama) Honey & Clover, Welcome to the NHK, His and Her Circumstances, Karin, Midori Days, Ouran High School Host Club, Doctor Who, Torchwood, Robin Hood, Merlin, The West Wing, and probably a bunch of other things I've forgotten. Plus I like RPF for Top Gear and Australian Comedy.

Nice to meet you.
