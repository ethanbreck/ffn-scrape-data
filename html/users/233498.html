News: July 23, 2017

I really am not sure who here still looks at my profile. Back in the day, it was quite busy when I actually wrote with the goal of publishing. Now, I use my profile to save favorites and follow stories as I have little time to write myself. I have a full-time teaching job and two children, so things are definitely a lot different time-wise than when I began writing around 15 years ago. The fandom I wrote for was Super Smash Bros. I wanted to venture into Legend of Zelda and Fullmetal Alchemist but my adult life called and I stopped having as much time.

My favorite fandoms are now Legend of Zelda, Fullmetal Alchemist, DreamWorks Trolls and Disney/Pixar Cars. I think the last two are because I have young children and I get very invested in their interests. It's not a bad place to be, really. I may publish some of the one-shots I have written in these fandoms, but I can't promise anything. I mostly enjoy Fanfiction.net on the "lurker" setting now. ;)

Current Projects:

I have several one-shots written in various fandoms. I have waltzed outside the world of Nintendo and joined DreamWorks and Disney Pixar. I blame the fact that I now have children. ;)
