Author has written 10 stories for Secret/Legend of Mana, and Final Fantasy XI.

I'm a spiffy guy. Trust me on this. I'll get back to you, okay?

I've stopped writing for Legend of Mana and Final Fantasy XI, but may occasionally toss up new short stories as I stumble across them on old hard drive backups and message boards. Apologies if they don't make 100 percent sense in and of themselves; they refer to characters and events that took place in a continuously evolving role playing storyline shaped by the various members of the Canta Per Me linkshell on the Lakshmi server. These stories are short, standalone vignettes that explain or expand situations in an ongoing plot with many contributers. As such, there will likely never be sequels to any of these; I try to avoid writing about events that involve other people's characters out of paranoid fear of portraying them wrong. Unfortunately, Canta Per Me is now defunct and we've moved to the Final Fantasy XIV RP server, but don't let that stop you from enjoying a yarn.

Oh, and please be brutally honest in your reviews. If I don't know what does and doesn't work, I won't be able to make future works better.
