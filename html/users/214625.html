"Those who dream by day are cognizant of many things which escape those who only dream by night." (Edgar Allen Poe)

I must say that Writer’s Block is by far the worst possible homicide of an individual who thrives off their imagination. In saying this, I speak of myself. My imagination remains but a crumpled ruin after two years of the worst case of writer’s block known to all mankind. I, quite honestly, have not written a single thing in two years time. Nothing, and my imagination is hurling rocks at me for this very reason. I have all these original ideas swimming in the ocean that makes up my imagination, but nothing is spilling out. The dam will not break and let them flow to my fingertips where these thoughts, images, and ideas could become something amazing. As such, I return to Fanfiction like a dog with its tail between its legs. I am hoping that, in writing fanfiction again, I might be able to get the juices flowing once more and finally break through that indestructible dam. Thank you for joining me on this journey (if you are indeed reading this, that is). Although, this does strictly depend on whether I can come up with a feasible idea for a story of some kind. This will take a great deal of thought...
~-~

Random Bio Bits:

For anyone remotely interested in the writer behind the pseudonym, I am usually known as Riley Barton (though for obvious reasons that is not my real name). I am nineteen years old and on the brink of starting my sophomore year of college. I am an English major, and I have been writing stories since I was a little kid. I am a bookworm and a film addict, as my large library of books and movies can prove. I love to travel, and I am very interested in interior design.

Should you want to contact me, I am afraid you will merely have to rely on reviews for the time being. Unless I decide to create an e-mail strictly for fanfiction.net, then there will be no other means to contact me. Sorry about that, but I do believe in such a thing called privacy.
