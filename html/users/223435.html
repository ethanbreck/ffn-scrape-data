What I Prefer In Fics

I like many action/adventure fics that has a slight mingle of romance in it. Pure action/adventure kind of loses my interest after a few chapters. I like many with original ships(Just as long as there not nasty original),plot ,and settings. I sometimes like Mary-Sue, as long as the character has a good amount of limits. Not where the new character is all knowing and has complete control of every situations where the actual characters are played as pawns. I also like most stories with Harry as powerful yet with limitation(Example: Harry is good looking, fit, and has strong magic. Yet not like; Harry can destroy the world with a thought, or Harry is much more powerful then Dumbledore and Tom Riddle combined. I mean sometimes I'll like the story with Harry being much more powerful, but it has got to have a very good plot. I like a sense of realism that fits with the actual era of the story{Like magic is a sense of realism though}. However if Harry has god like powers for no reason, na-uh. That's overdoing it even for me.) Also I like fics that have a kinda childish plot, yet I am now okay with the a more depthful story. I mean I don't like fics where there is a world conspiricy involving the whole wizarding world. Where the "Dark" forces have everything figured out having Harry and the "Good" forces being played as pawns by Voldie'. Try "Harry Potter and The Psychic Serpent" and it's sequels. Then you would know what I mean. The story is good just too rich for my taste. No offense writer!
