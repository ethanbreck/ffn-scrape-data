Author has written 5 stories for Wrestling.

I'm a huge Triple H and Stephanie fan. I have been since I was a teen and still am to this day! I love them as a couple and I have since Triple H interrupted Test and Stephanie's wedding but I think my favorite moment with these two was when Stephanie turned against Vince at Armageddon. I've only ever written stories about Triple H and Stephanie and they will probably be the ones I do write about. At least for now, maybe one day I'll write about something else but for now I'm sticking with my two favorite people :)

I used to read and write on some Triple H and Stephanie boards but then I got busy and didn't have time to watch or write anymore. When I did finally start watching and writing again the boards were dead so I came over here to start reading stories and start my writing again. I'm pretty rusty but I hope the more I write, the better I will become. So if you enjoy reading my stories don't be afraid to leave a review or even pm me because it keeps me motivated to keep writing!
