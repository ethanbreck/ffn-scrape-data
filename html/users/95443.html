ABOUT ME:

I am just your normal fanfic writer. Siriusly... all except using stupid puns like I did before and trying to learn British Slang for my senior trip when the time comes. I am just a mindless teenager who hopes later her book will entertain others when the time comes, and that doesn't happen, taking a leaf out of her grandfather's book will help...

But I am still a mindless teenager who has favorites:

-HOBBIES: Writing, Reading, Dancing, Acting, Drawing, Photography, hanging with my friends, horsebacking riding, debating Harry Potter, Tennis
-THINGS I BELONG TO: Debate/Forensics, Against Animal Cruelity, Harry Potter Mental Institution. (Oliva Frost and I kind of escaped the bounds of HPMI... and now the Knight Bus is after us.)

-BOOKS: I read a lot and so far my standings are like this: "Speak" (Laurie Halse Anderson) "Harry Potter 1-5" (JKRowling) "His Dark Materials" (Phillip Pullman)

-T.V. SHOWS: I don't watch much television. Just too much time is taken watching it...but when I do I usually search for Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Charmed, Boy Meets World, and Thats So Raven.
-MOVIES: Don't Say a Word, White Oleandor,

-MUSIC:Oh, boy. Here's when I go all out. I use music to help me get into the mood of writing a certain scene, so I have bunches of favorites. "Because You Loved Me" (Celine Dion) "Travelin' Soldier" (Dixie
Chicks) "Miss Independent" "A Moment Like This" "You Thought Wrong" (Kelly Clarkson) "AM to PM" (Christina Milian) "A Thousand Miles" (Vanessa Carlton) "Roxy" (From Chicago)

-HP CHARACTERS: Ron, Hermione, Luna, Cookshanks, Tonks, Remus, Dobby, Ginny
-HP SHIPS: This one usually gets my friends and I starting to debate, because they have a different views as to who Hermione belongs with. I am I BIG BIG PUMPKIN PIE EATER, WHICH IS H/H. I am also a Ron/Luna, Neville/Ginny, Lily/James. However, one of my desires is Ginny/Draco which won't happen.

As for my writing. I write almost everyday, whether for my original or my fanfic. Below are the stats and full summaries for each of my stories:

-INSANE COMPLEXITY: *WORK IN PROGRESS*
Hermione recieved a letter that created her the sole bearer of information that could destroy the world if used rightly. She must hide and she does at St. Mungos as a ward. Eight years later, her cover is blown and she must help fight with her knowledge. Meanwhile, Voldemort takes another move in his terror... based loosely off of "Don't Say a Word"
INCLUDES: A trial, Death Eaters using arrows, concerned parents, magic with nature.

-MAKE NO PROMISES: *ON HATIUS*
Seven years ago, a backing out at the last moment event broke up the trio. Sadly, they went their own ways... only to be brought back together on Harry's charges for killing a person. He is sentenced to Akzaban. However, Hermione suddenly believes he was framed and there's something lurking out there worse than Voldemort...
INCLUDES: DrunkJurk!Harry, Rape, House-elves island, and a scene in Azkaban.

-A WITCH AND THE BROOMSTICK, CRICKETS, FLOO POWDER: *FINISHED*
A challeged by Practical Magic and is based off of The Princess and the Pea. I wrote this during the last chapters of "The Looking Glass", and surprise! I went over and tried to catch all of the mistakes. This is an AU, and Harry's parents are alive, trying to make sure Harry gets the right girl. Very short.

-THE LOOKING GLASS: *FINISHED*
When Voldemort and a ghostly-like man named Simon try to find a weapon to help them take over the world, Harry and Hermione are called to fulfil their destinies. However, Hermione has been through a lot. Over the summer, she had visited Krum's... when she came back, she wouldn't tell Ron and Harry what happened. In the scheme of things Hermione learns the truth about her past, which includes a mysterious Looking Glass.
INCLUDES: Death, Betrayal, Love at the last moment, a trip to Germany, and what's that, Sirius singing a long to the radio.
* Paige's Note: OK, I wrote this two years ago, so there's going to be a lot of mistakes. I was a horrid proofreader two years ago, but somehow got better over the years. I hope to revise this story in the near future.*
