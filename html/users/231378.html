Hail and well met, traveller! Welcome to my humble corner of FFN. I call myself Majorkami in cyberspace, and I hope you find something here that appeals to you.

A few stats about me, for those of you who are curious:

DOB: Feb 1984

Gender: Female, last I checked.

Place of Residence: Toronto, Ontario, Canada

Romantic Status: Happily married to my best friend on 16 Sept 2007.

After a three year hiatus I found myself drawn back to fanfiction by the movie 9. For the critics among you I know its not perfect, but I'm hooked on stitchpunks and their world. We get such a short time to meet them in the movie before characters start dying, so I love stories that explore the characters of 9 in more detail. Obviously I have no problem with slash, but it must remain true to the characters for me to enjoy it.

And now, a few pictures on deviant art I gained inspiration from. Check out the artists; give them love! I wish I could draw...

Thanks go out to Elaine B for drawing my stitchpunk alter-ego (27)

If any of you fine people choose to sketch something from one of my stories I will love you for always :)

Well you didn't come here for me to blather on, so enjoy your journey!
