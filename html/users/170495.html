Author has written 8 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

I like DBZ, and I like to write. I'm also a closet-case romantic, and this is my outlet for it. I'd say I write every genre, but they're all masquerading around as love stories. Each fic is self-contained (more or less), even in my series, so you don't necessarily have to read one to understand the other. Lots of fun fanart on my site. Go see!
MY SERIES
1. PICCOLO AND CHI CHI
2. GOKU AND BULMA
3. (spinoff #1)HUMPTY DUMPTY - A Vegeta Story
4. GOTEN AND MARRON
My R-rated fics are only rated thusly for brief scenes of standard anime violence. That's all. I don't write lemons. I made the decision before it made me, and I believe my writing is better off for it.

Check out the Thunk bio for Inuyasha and Trigun fanfiction.
