As you might have guessed from the username, I'm called Anita and I'm from Scotland - to be more specific, Glasgow. I'm currently obsessed with Power Rangers (MMPR, NS, DT and SPD), and have been obsessed with Lord of the Rings in the past, while my obsession for Harry Potter has never really gone away.

I used to write under several different usernames, to seperate out my different fandoms, but it was getting too much effort. So if you recognise a story here from under a different name - don't worry, I promise I didn't steal it, just decided to put them all in the one place. By now I'm kind of embarrassed by my LOTR fiction, and I think my HP fic - though it's so long ago I wrote it, I really can't remember.
