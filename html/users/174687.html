Hi! I read LotR after the movie. I think the movie was better. I came to fanfiction.net looking for Aragorn fics, because he's my favourite character. Since then, I have become totally addicted to all the stuff you guys write! I love A/L slash, when written well, and I'm beginning to love Legolas, too! (Although Aragorn is still the best.)

General Update (7/4/04)

How bad is that... I haven't updated in a year! I'm surpised and a bit flattered that I still get the occassional email and review every now and then, asking me to continue with my fics. So, what's happened? Partly, real life has caught up with me and partly that I've kind of lost interest, and moved on to another fandom - Viggorli, anyone? *g*

However, I've been thinking that I might pick up some of these stories again during my break in June, especially "Crossing The Line", which a few people have emailed me about. If I do continue "Crossing The Line", it will be mostly thanks to Red Minerva's constant encouragement!

Many apologies for the lack of updates! Hope you're all doing well and having more success in your writing that I am! ;o)
