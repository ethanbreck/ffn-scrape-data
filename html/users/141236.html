Hi...

I'm ills... nice to meet 'cha.

I have to say I'm a manga type girl and I'm heavily influenced by that in my writing.

Shojo manga and Pocky are my treasures.

I love to day dream and that’s how I started writing. I started writing Fan Fiction when I was eleven; it was a short story on how Usagi one day magically grew wings. It was dumb as hell but it actually started me on my crazy path.

Two of my first Fan fictions were Death is only the Beginning and Dark Side of the Moon. I got to two hundred pages on each and then suddenly decided to take them down between 01-02. The reason being was they were horribly written. My English wasn't quite as developed as it is today so I had a lot of grammar mistakes and spelling issues. I promised the readers of it that I would someday return and have them re-written. So, um, come six years later I’ve come to do that very thing. Lol, well.. took me quite a while but I’m back.

Well anyways, since I started re-writing those two stories I suddenly have a whole bunch of ideas popping up for different fan fictions. I'm thinking of starting some Sailor Moon fiction but for right now I am staying in the crossover section writing Dragon Ball Z/Sailor Moon fics.

I mostly write Usagi/Vegeta and Usagi/Trunks pairings.

So be on the lookout for my new fiction, also I have a whole bunch of original fiction that I’m working on over at fictionpress. If you want to check it out click on my homepage. I usually write a bunch of horror fics and romance over there.. be sure to leave me a review.

I usually have a tendency, depending on reviews though, to update every week. If I get overly excited, aka I have a lot of reviews on a particular story, then I do end up updating twice a week. So please keep my muse alive by leaving me support and love.

Love, ills
