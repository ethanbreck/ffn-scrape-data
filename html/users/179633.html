Claire's Profile:
I was adopted into a humanoid family in February 1989 AD because my home planet, Reido, had been infiltrated by the hostile forces of Planet Cool and my parents had sent me off in a small space capsule. The capsule landed on Earth, and my *adopted parents* decided that I looked enough like a humanoid to pass as one. They had been earlier informed of my arrival, so it was no surprise.
I quickly adapted to humanoid lifestyles, but one thing I will NEVER get used to is the presence of an annoying little pest, the humanoid named Andrew. His life's goal seems to be to annoy me, and me PERSONALLY. I have seen this similiar goal in many other humanoid boys, such as Michael (a non-related humanoid who lives in Rach's house) and Cameron (a humanoid with accentuated annoying-ness). I hope to one day travel into space and find the remnants of my lost planet, to take back to Earth to remind me of what used to be. ~
