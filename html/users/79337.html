My name is Arthur Hansen and I'm a single, white and living on my own (for the first time at 44 years old. Yay.)

I seem to really be able to only write fanfictions focused on cross-overs. They are sort of an exercise of the mind. What would happen if... blah blah. I also do artwork, but I'm kinda sporadic about that, too. I used to be big into Bubblegum Crisis and Ranma, though now my tastes are a bit wider. Witch Hunter Robin was very good!

Looks like people decided that my RPG-OC stories might be worth reading. Just so people know, most of these are based on Scion:Hero to Scion: God (which is useful, as the character can scale from pulp-hero all the way to goddess!)

Most of them are about a young Scion of Athena named Sheila. I've started having a meta-plot so all the 'Sheila-verse' Scion stories are related, but not necessarily in a way that you would normally think in a story. So a meta-underlying-plot. I've completed the 'original' story-arc of Sheila, daughter of Athena!

For new Readers, I would suggest starting with: Scion of Athena (which is the original 'timeline' so to speak) and then probably either STARGATE SCION or ANGELS AND GODDESS. They are the two 'most complete' spinoffs.

I started a forum, so drop on by if you want to send a more public chat about some of my stories. http://www.fanfiction.net/myforums/Arthur_Hansen/79337/

had a fun MST'ing of Nightfall Wars, in case anyone was interested. (Seems to be down now. I saved it as a second chapter of Nightfall Wars (BGC/VtM))

(Note) I disabled anonymous reviews. It's pretty hard to respond to you otherwise, as I won't bad my stories with unneeded replies. If you have a cool review, I'll probably reply to your account.

Fanart for Stargate Scion.

Cover art for Sheila-verse.

Just so people know, I tend to write a lot that never gets published. It took me three times to get a decent Harry Potter / Scion story. So if you don't see anything new for a while, it doesn't mean I'm not writing, it just means I don't have anything new to publish. Though I do admit, I sometimes write myself in a corner and get stuck. But I can sometimes figure out what isn't work.

(I still haven't figured out a good way to make Battlestar Galactica's mythos work with Scion, as the "angels" are so nebulous.)
