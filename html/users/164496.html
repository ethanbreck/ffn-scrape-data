Stories I'm currently working on or getting around to...

The Prototype System
It's a three parts series. Sort of a self-insert (would it still be called this even if I wasn't using myself?) with a bigger plot.
No one knows his real name. He only goes by the codename, Genesis. This man has been sending out strange console devices out to those that he had picked. Using the strange devices he gathers people into their favorite games. It is unclear what his motives are or how long he has been doing it.

Press Start to Begin
Time-wise it is the first in the series, but I'm going to be writing this one 2nd. Takes place in FFVIII and it will reveal just what Genesis wants when he sends out the consoles.

A New Way to Play
In progress takes place in FFX. It's the second in the series. Follows a teenaged boy by the name of Steve. Through a freaky set of circumstance he gains possession of the prototype system, and just like others before him he is sucked into the game. He gets more than he bargains for when he steps into a fight between Genesis and a girl who dares to fight back. Will he discover just what is going on as he tries to survive the world of Spira.

New Game +
Continues on from where A New Way to Play left off. Takes place in FFX-2. I don't think Steve will survive all the magical girl transformations.

The Leon Cub (working title)
FFVII AU. What if Laguna raised Squall in Esthar? How would that effect the events that happened in FFVIII?

Cub of the Pride
What happened if Laguna went with Ellone to Winhill and found his wife giving birth to their son. A series of one-shots showing Squall being raised as the President's son.

Leaving the Pride (Also a working title)
The game of FFVIII in this AU. How will things go if Squall wasn't the loner SeeD we all know?
