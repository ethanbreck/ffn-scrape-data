Hey everyone! Soar and Stealth is a writing duo. We are actually authors TigerLily and greeneyes. We're best friends, and even tho we live a thousand miles away, we write fanfic together! The thousand miles does cause a bit of delay in writing, tho. So we hope you'll understand. We are both completely obsessive about the reading and writing of HP fanfic...it's quite sad really. But hey, we have nothing better to do. We are in awe of JK Rowling! And also the Queens of H/G at gryffindortower.net! We are quite in awe of them, and their bouncing quarters, stolen socks, and tartan boxers! We are compelte H/G, and R/Hr shippers. We also love J/L. We will not, under any circumstances, read and H/H, altho we read the occasional odd pairing. We're just weird. Hehehe! So, go ahead and read our stories! And then, go read Glimpses of Truth by TigerLily and Memory Lane by greeneyes! We'll love you for it!

We hope to repost chappie one very soon. As for chappie two, well no promises at the moment. Let's just say we are doing a very good impersonation of JK Rowling at the moment. But YAY for coming appearance of book five. That's all for now.~Stealth, who is actually talking to Soar on the phone at the moment.
