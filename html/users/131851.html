Hi! I'm Tish aka Jade. I'm a graduate of Political Science and Classical Civilizations but work in accounting (I hate politics and no one cares about history). I'm also too old too read and/or write fic but I don't let that stop me :-)

My favorite (current) shows include Supernatural, CSI: OR, Big Bang Theory, and (sadly, and happily) iCarly. Gone but not forgotten are Buffy, Third Watch, Angel, ER, Off the Map, Whose Line, and many many more. And my good god holy obsession ever since I first saw it is Sailor Moon. Don't ask me why cause not even I know, but iCarly is quickly following in its footsteps. I have a thing for tween shows lol

When I'm not working my hobbies (other than watching TV) are reading and writing poetry and fic. I will read just about anything as long as it is well-written; I've written fic for GH, Sailor Moon and iCarly. I haven't posted a fic since I was a teenager - self-consciousness grows with age. Maybe eventually I'll throw insecurity to the wind and allow a fic out for public scrutiny... Or not :P

And I spend WAY too much time in the heads of fictional characters...
