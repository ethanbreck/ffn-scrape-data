Author has written 23 stories for Star Wars.

I adore Luke and Vader and love driving my characters crazy / to the dark side as well as using other forms of mental or emotional torture, and I'm just generally evil. ;) I have a morbid fascination with the dark side ... but I'll probably get over it eventually. (Though I haven't in eight years or so, so that may be debatable.)

Even though I adore Luke and Vader, I'll write just about anything that pops into my head ('cept, perhaps, the Solo children). I tend to ship Luke/Mara, though I've also developed a distinct fondness for the Han/Leia romance. I've written Anakin/Padme, I havea few Vader fics, and the rest ... well, see for yourself - there's a fairly good variety. ;)

Be warned that I write a lot of angst - it gives me a chance to go all out on prose, something that I can't really do with humor. But have fun. :D

I'm currently going through some sort of Dark!Luke phase at the moment. o.O You can kind of see the results below, and there are quite a few more in my brain that are just clamoring to be released upon an unsuspecting public. grin Yeah, quite a few more ... expect a few dark L/M stories in the future, something in which Luke interacts with Leia, and perhaps a follow up on "Everyone Drinks in Hell".

Anyway - enjoy. ;D
