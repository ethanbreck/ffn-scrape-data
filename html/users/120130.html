Couples I ship: ...

Couples I hate: ...

Above list isn't due for an update. There's no point in listing ships because to me, it's more about how you write, and less about what you write.

Drama, Action, Romance, Comedy, Horror, Het, Interracial, Age Gaps, Incest, Yuri, Yaoi...if it's well written, then that's what counts.

Reviews: I don't write for them and don't believe they're essential to writing a good story. An understanding of what makes a good story work, the willingness to put our all in our work and the neverending desire to improve are most important. I would consider a good harsh Beta willing to tell us where we can improve and a layer of thick skin almost essential as well, but at the end of the day, we are our own best critics.
