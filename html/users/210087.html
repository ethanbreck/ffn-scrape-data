Hi!

I am a twenty-three year old college grad who works at a regional theater in the frigid North. :-P

I really love Star Trek, House MD, Robin Hood BBC and many other shows. I am currently finding myself with so much free time that I don't know what to do.

Most of these stories I wrote and posted when I was much younger, so be aware that some of the less recent stories might be a little lacking in technique (to say it nicely). But I keep them here because I loved them so much when I wrote them that it seems like I would be betraying myself if I deleted this record of who I once was.

You can pop me an e-mail if you like what you read. I am a pretty friendly person!

Thanks!!
