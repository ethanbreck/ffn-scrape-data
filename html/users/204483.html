Author has written 1 story for Final Fantasy X.
I'm fifteen, a sophomore at high school, and I have been writing before I ever had the skills to write a good story. It was only three years ago that I really started taking writing seriously and to my amazement, I found that I had a deep talent and fierce passion for it. It was only last year that I began looking into writing as a possible career choice. Since 1999, I have composed twenty-six stories - nine of them are novels which I define as stories that are over 100 pages - and over three hundred poems. Feel free to e-mail me, however understand that though I respect everyone's opinions, I would like it if they respect mine as well. I want feedback on my stories so that I can improve them because I hope to become a published author. Flames are not generally well-liked, but if they offer constructive criticism, bring it on! I am glad that I am finally able to post the vampire-fiction stories that I have poured my life, soul, and heart into.

I would also like to mention that posting up my story/ies on a website is a gigantic step for me. I am always concerned about plagiarizers and I am certain that many can understand my fears. I have had my share of plagiarists. I would just like to point out that all the characters in my story/ies belong to me. I created them and I know them better than anyone else in this universe, I do not mean to brag about it. I would also like to point out that all original works created after April 1, 1989 have a copyright, whether it receives notice or not. All my stories were created sometime after 1995, give or take a year or two. Thanks for understanding my concerns and I hope everyone who reads this and also writes will feel security knowing that little copyright rule. It is unfortunate, although, that not everyone follows the rules. Please understand that it is not fair for anyone's original work to be "ripped" off and no one should have to go through that.

Some might be wondering why I pulled off my first story, IMMORTAL BLOODLUST: RETRIBUTION. First of all, I want to thank everyone for reviewing my story and everyone who has contributed their thoughts and opinions. I am eternally grateful. The reason for the termination of the story is because yes, to answer anyone who has asked me this question, I *AM* GETTING PUBLISHED. The book is, of course, IMMORTAL BLOODLUST: RETRIBUTION. You will find the book under my real name and it should be coming out sometime between October 2002 and December 2002. I hope you pick it up. A sneak peek of the cover of RETRIBUTION can be found on my website. I will notify, on this page, when the photo has been put up into the site. And along with the photo, I will also have my website address on this page so you can visit it if you want. Any questions and/or comments can be sent to my e-mail. Thanks! You guys are the best! :)

Love and farewell from the darkness,
~SHAMARA AMBROSE~
