Author has written 18 stories for Buffy X-overs, Charmed, Secret Circle series, and Alias.

It's been ages since I've posted a story here... really sorry about that! I usually get inspired to write when I'm obsessed with a particular fandom but after Season 4 of Charmed and what they did to Cole, I lost my main source of inspiration and have been in "obsession limbo" ever since...

Looking back at some of stories, I am inspired to write again - it will be extremely out of canon though and I'm not sure about my ability to continue my stories from where I originally left off but I'll definitely try. I don't like leaving stuff unfinished and reading all the lovely comments have been really encouraging... So as soon as I've finished with my law exams, I promise I'll get back on to writing LoC and Convergence...

So what am I currently doing? I'm now a 2nd year law student at the London School of Economics i.e. perpetually bogged down by tons of readings where the library is my second, possibly first, home. I enjoy reading, listening to music and watching a good movie... Yes, I know, a nice quiet life. :p

No sports. Never.

Hm... Perhaps it's not a good idea to write this at 3 am in the morning... I'm starting to ramble. Anyway, thanks for reading!
