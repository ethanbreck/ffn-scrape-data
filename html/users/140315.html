Hi, I'm Priscilla.

After a few years of being inactive on ff.net, I've finally realized how much I miss this place (or what it was). I decided to rejoin the community at the beginning of this past summer (2006) and start writing again!

Looking back at all these stories that I've written, I only have to say that I'm just embarassed. But hey, I was young and immature when I started this business. I'm glad to say that I've grown up a lot more, and gotten out of a lot of weird writing habits. (I.E. Randomly commenting in on my own writing, and inserting A/N's throughout the text.)

A huge thanks to all of you who have supported me and been patient with me. You have no idea how much you have inspired me to keep writing, and keep thinking of new ideas.

Works:

An original story inspired by everyday things, and I actually really like how it's coming along so far. I started this around a year ago, and it was sort of on hiatus, but now I'm back and updating :) Please take a look if you have a chance!

This is my shot at redeeming myself on this site, haha, because it's safe to say I've improved a great deal than from when I wrote my other fics back in like '03, and my writing style has changed a lot.
