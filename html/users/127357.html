Em hotep! (Hi.)

So, if you're new here I suppose it calls for an introduction. I don't much care for introductions, they always seem incredibly awkward and oddly composed.

All you really need to know is that I'm (obviously) an author with too much time on her hands spend writing fanfiction while she could be doing something useful like feeding the poor or ending prejudice in her hometown or helping smelly babies learn how to spell but no, I write fan stories instead. If I had better artistic talent, I'd probably be doing fanart as well, but my skills are mediocre at best. Ah, well. Such is life.
