I've dabbled with writing from time to time, but never seem to be able to get it right. However, I am an avid reader and have over a thousand stories I subscribe to on FF.net and several hundred elsewhere. I love Ranma, Naruto, and Harry Potter fanfics, especially ones where they're not paired with the typical characters (i.e. Ranma & Akane, Naruto & Hinata, Harry & Ginny respectively). I don't like Yaoi. I have nothing against it personally, it's just not my thing. One of my biggest pet peeves is when a writer starts a story and the suddenly decides he doesn't like it anymore. If you're not sure about a story, DON'T START IT! Don't get a person hooked on a story just to leave them hanging. That's why I subscribe to over a thousand stories. Most of them are eternally dead due to the writers loosing interest.
