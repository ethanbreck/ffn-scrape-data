A writer can only truly live within their words...

Konnichi wa, minna-san. Watashi no name wa Ishida Katsuki desu. Iie, I'm not some obssessed fangirl wanting in love with Yamato Ishida.. that's just wrong. Yamato, he's only my cousin and the best cousin ever. Even though I'm older by a couple of months, I like to call him Yama-niichan..I was born with the name Katsuki but when my parents moved from Odaiba to America, they decided to give me an American name, Kristin...but my sister had a hard time saying my name at first so I was stuck with Krissy.. I don't mind.. It's shorter and simliar to my Japanese name. Then my sister and I had to move back to Japan without our parents. We had to stay with Yama-niichan. I didn't mind. Later we learned we were digidestineds...and I met a kawaii little red head computer wiz by the name of Izumi Koushirou. I like him a lot but we are only best friends..I hope in the future that we could be something more..

Anyway.. that's my character Krissy Ishida in a nutshell.. I use her for my FDD fanfics but I haven't posted any of them here before.
