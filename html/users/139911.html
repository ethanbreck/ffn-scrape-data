I love collecting dolls, skateboarding, roller skating/blading, singing karaoke, tea parties, ceramic objects, perfumes, seahorses, goofing off, stunts, extreme sports, and playing video games.

I tend to procrastinate a bit too much for my own good. Not sure what I'll write after I'm finished with Cry Die. I will finish it. I've been quite busy over the years, but Kamikaze Kaitou Jeanne is still a very precious story to me.
