It's one of my passions in life. I have several fics in the works. One is continuing the remake of 'A Place To Rest' (took down the old version in prep for the new version). After seeing the 2k14/2k16 version of Ninja Turtles (I don't care what anyone says, I loved them! Particularly the 2k16.)
