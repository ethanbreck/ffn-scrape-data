Author has written 6 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Supernatural.

I am a married mother of two boys. My husband is a fire-fighter instructor who graciously tolerates my over-active imagination and my spur-of-the-moment forays into the world of fan-fiction writing. My first experience writing fan fiction began when I was a junior in high school. That was a LONG time ago, and I have not written a new story in quite some time. My latest obsession is with the show Supernatural. I love exploring the amazing and sometimes heartbreaking story of the brothers Winchester. I will admit I tend to lean toward Dean a bit more, but I really love both brothers.

I hope to one day hone my writing skills enough to be able to write and publish my own works of fiction. I love reading, listening to music, riding horses, and of course...writing. I also love reviews. :) They feed my muse...
