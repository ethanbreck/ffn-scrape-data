Chronicles of the Protectorate (Series)

Note the name change - it's not "of the Protector" anymore, but "of the Protectorate" - there's a plot reason for that

Who cares, though - I've been calling it just "Chronicles" for years. You suggest something better (other than just Chronicles)

For right now, there's only one story in progress as far as uploading chapters goes (and I'm reposting it from scratch because I've done a lot of work on it), and that's the first "book" in the series. I came up with the idea which eventually turned into Chronicles near the end of middle school (around 1996, I suppose). I have played all the games in the FF series up to XIV (I currently don't own a PS3 so no FFXIII yet), the Chrono games, the Dragon Warrior/Quest games, the Kingdom Hearts games, etc. I know quite a bit about storyline and such and everything that makes up the realm of Square-Enix RPGs. I always got asked questions about Final Fantasy games, but the one I had never been able to answer was "How can each game take place on Gaia?" It is a good question, and one I decided to add a character to tie them all together. This character would later become Karrin (Karrin...yeah, pronounced just like Karen, and meant to be a pun on the word Caring), who starts out as a nerdish character with the same problems as everyone else on Earth, and gets taken to Gaia by an extreme accident...and from there the choices he makes due to his strong sense of justice eventually lead to him being recognized as the Protector.

I've got maybe a few hundred pages of story arcs and how Karrin flows in and out of the games. And the great thing? I cover pretty much everything. FF, Chrono, DW/DQ, KH, etc. At times there were plans to expand into other series that perhaps are story driven but aren't JRPGs (Japanese RPGs, which are the basic descriptor for the early FF style). And instead of just being straight novelizations of games on a different world, we see it through Karrin's eyes, and I even go so far as turning many of the games' storylines upside-down and back onto themselves. Hope you'll stayed tuned for another exciting chapter in the world of the Protector.
