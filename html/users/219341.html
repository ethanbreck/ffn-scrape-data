Author has written 9 stories for Rurouni Kenshin, Inuyasha, Phantom of the Opera, and Dresden Files.

Very on-again, off-again, citizen of ff.net for about twelve years now. I'm a 25yo, 5' tall, blonde located in Denver. I act on a small scale in indie films and as part of a Dresden Files Fan Filming group. I'm on Tumblr as tiny-fierce and on twitter as @_tinyfierce.

I've been posting stories here since I was a kid so, seriously, please take a peek at the publish date before you hate on me too hard for making childish writing mistakes. Because I was thirteen when I joined here. I worked hard on the stories I have up so I'm not going to take them down (high five littler me) but I'm not updating any of them. Any that I may want to update/change/finish I'll probably fully rewrite.

I'm sporadic. The more recent fics I've tried to start have kind of lost my interest as fast as they've gained it (I'm sorry!) but I'm trying to get better about it. I all but quit writing for about three years so I'm still very hesitant to put anything new up or update anything I've started. Just so you're prepared. I do more writing on my Tumblr than I do actually publishing anything so feel free to join me over there and follow my roleplays if you are so inclined.
