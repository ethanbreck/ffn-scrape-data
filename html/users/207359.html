English

I'm pretty new to English fandoms, to be honest. I started to seriously write fanfics in English last year, even if I've been around for almost 16 years. Damn, that's more than half of my life now. I guess I should say it's my number 1 hobby...
Anyway, I've been into a lot of fandoms over the years: Evangelion, Gundam Wing, Naruto, Mushishi, Pokémon and District 9 were the main ones, I'm currently into Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Mass Effect. But that's the short list because I actually wrote a lot of prompts and shorts on more than 40 fandoms, and a few originals too. I actually have several original universes of my own but I'm mostly into Mass Effect those days.

Hmmm, what can I say? I like "serious" stories, either in published books or fanfictions. Not necessarily stories without fun, I loved The Hitchhiker Guide to the Galaxy or the Discworld, but serious in the "seriously built" way. As long as the universe is believable, I'll usually jump in.
I've always been into serious stories. It started with Evangelion when I was around 13, and it influenced my work a lot ever since, I think. I like plots and subplots, with secrets and dark corners, clues left for the readers and all of that. I also play with romance but I see it as a complication device to the plot, not a plot itself.
I read a lot when I was a kid but my first writing "lesson" was Lord of the Rings. Then, Zola's Germinal. And, more recently, Game of Thrones. So, basically, I'm not afraid to kill my characters, ha ha ha.
My favorite book is French, though, and it deals with psychopaths, serial killers and scifi (Les Racines du Mal, Maurice G. Dantec - I don't think it has been translated and it's a shame, really, even if it starts to be a bit outdated).

I write by phases so I might disappear for months at a time, especially during winter. If I run out of motivation, I also tend to abandon everything. It's kind of a big problem. For example, I was really into TMNT last year and even started a long fanfic on it, Neververse, but some fandom bullshit made me back off from the fandom, therefore from the fic. Well, that's not a problem to me per se because I already know the story, but I would have liked to write that story. I still could but I don't feel like it. I like TMNT, I hate the fandom, why bother sharing my work?
So I switched to Mass Effect, and I'm avoiding the fandom as much as possible, especially on tumblr. I don't want some fandom drama to interfere with my motivation to write Semper Fi.

Speaking of, I've came to start this fanfic after reading a lot of the same things. Either it was a retelling of the story of the game, often abandoned after the events of ME1, or it was a fanfic set after the Destroy end, both with fast romances. I've also read a bunch of AU focused on femShep/Garrus romances and they didn't convince me at all. But I'd searched for them so, my fault.
A few ideas naturally popped in my head while playing and reading, and I thought a serious AU could be fun to develop, so I started making lists of characters, with what I knew about them, and finding new links between them. For once, I didn't keep the plot in my head, I actually wrote it, and it's massive. Like, 70,000 words massive. I started drifting from it in chapter 3 already, like I always do, but I have a good base anyway, and I know what the main arcs of the story will be. SF is also a good excuse to write down all the headcanons I have about all the ME species and cultures. Me likey.
Don't hesitate to send me a review once in a while, it's always welcome, and it keeps my motivation running, to be honest. I write mostly for myself but it doesn't hurt to know the readers appreciate the story too.

And that's pretty much everything I had to say.

Français

Je ne répondrai plus à aucun MP à propos des reviews laissées entre 2007 et 2009 sur le fandom Naruto francophone. On est en 2015 les gens, passez à autre chose.

Je sais que je n'ai pas terminé Till Kingdom Come (Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles) et je n'en ai pas vraiment l'intention. J'aime beaucoup cette fanfic mais le fandom TMNT me donne des aigreurs d'estomac à cause d'idiots sur tumblr. C'est moche mais c'est comme ça. Et puis l'histoire était devenue un peu hors de contrôle. Il faudrait que je m'y remette sérieusement pour la finir mais je n'ai ni le temps ni l'envie de m'y plonger. C'est dommage, j'étais proche de la fin. M'enfin, c'est comme ça.

D'ailleurs, je doute écrire à nouveau en français, du moins pour un bout de temps. Les fandoms anglophones sont plus actifs. Et puis ça me fait pratiquer l'anglais. Je savais déjà me débrouiller en anglais avant mais, comme beaucoup de Français ayant appris cette langue à l'école, je n'osais pas l'utiliser. Ecrire des fanfics m'a donné plus de confiance en mes capacités. Et ça me fait trop plaisir qu'on me confonde avec un natif pour que j'arrête, honnêtement.

Donc voilà, si vous voulez lire mes fanfics, il va falloir vous mettre à l'anglais. Et à Mass Effect.
