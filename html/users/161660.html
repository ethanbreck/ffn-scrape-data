Alrighty, time for a new profile. First, I must begin with a message to you all.

WORMTONGUE ROCKS.

Anyone who is bold enough to disagree shall be locked away in Isengard, inside. You won't be as lucky as Gandalf. n_n

If you don't know what I'm talking about, go see "The Two Towers." I will drag you into the theatre myself!!

Right. I would also like to announce, to the extreme disappointment of many of you, that I have given up anime. I have returned most of my books, and my videos are currently being transferred to the bottom of a drawer. As for if I'm completing my in-progress anime fics, I'm not sure right now. I haven't updated any of my fics in a... long time.

But if you're new and you like Ranma 1/2, I would recommend reading "Twelve Days of Kuno." It's not my best work, but I think it's a pretty good fic.

Right now, it's early January, and I have 2 months off until I get back to school (yes, you read correctly). I've been neglecting my writing stuff for too long. It's time to write until my hand falls off now!!
