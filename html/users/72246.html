You hum a catchy tune as you skip down a trail in Termina. Recently, after the heroic Link saved Termina, it was named "New Termina." An inhabitant of Termina, who helped Link on his quest, was elected leader of the country due to his valiant efforts. However, he had some thinking to do, and disappeared from Termina for a while. Suddenly, a large flying ship (which resembles Lawrence III's in Pokemon the Movie 2000) zooms overhead. Questioning its presence, you follow it to New Clock Town. It hovers for a moment over the large castle in the center, and then zooms to Snow Head. As you approach the Southern Entrance to town, you spot a banner flying overhead. Suddenly, your eyes fly open as you read it. You dash inside, and see swarms of people crowded around someone on a stage. The person spots you, waves, and begins to speak to you. Prince Jay Dralacon has returned!
