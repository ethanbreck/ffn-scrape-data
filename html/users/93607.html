Hello!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. My name is Kylie and I am a 31 year old woman from Melbourne, Australia. I have been writing fanfiction on and off for 15 years.

Its been a long time since I've had enough free time in my life to devote to my writing, but I do now, and I'm really enjoying getting back into this little hobby of mine.

The good news though, is that I never really lost my passion for writing OR my beloved Krillin and 18 coupling, it was always there, simmering away in the back of my mind.

I do feel as though I should put a general disclaimer here for all of my work going forward so as not to catch anyone off guard. I am an adult who has lived an adult life, my writing comes tinged with those experiences. It contains swear words (it's the Aussie in me, we can't help it!) where I believe they are appropriate. While I don't necessarily write stories about sex, I don't shy away from sex as a part of my characters lives. At the moment, I am really enjoying exploring the darker side of Krillins character, particularly, so while I do believe that what I write is overall happy, it often comes wrapped in a dark and gritty outer layer.

Finally, I would just like to say thank you to each and every person who has ever read, reviewed or recommended any of my stories. Even today, having not published anything for 10 years, I still receive so much love from this wonderful community. It is so humbling to know that even in absence, I have been able to bring a smile to people's faces, I hope to be able to continue that now.

I always encourage reviews, not just on my own work, but on every story you read, even if it's just a simple 'Good Job'. People put hours of hard work into their stories and and it makes people's days to know that others enjoyed it, or if you didn't, constructive feedback is important to all of us to help us hone our craft.

Looking forward to seeing everyone around the place! I am happy to receive PMs if anyone has any questions for me about any of my work past or present, or if you would just like to chat!

Very best wishes

Deadly Beauty
