Hello all!

This is Inazuma Akai (formerly Lady Saturn and Fiery Dove...yes, I've had plenty of pen names -sigh-). I'm coming back to the fanfiction community after a very long hiatus. I was working on my Bachelors and Masters degrees. Now, I've started on my Doctorate. It may be difficult, but I would truly like to throw myself back into my writing at this point. As they say, can't live with it, can't live without it. : )

I'm working on original fiction and fanfiction, so updates won't be as consistent as I'd like them to be. All of my fanfiction will be hosted on the lovely Fanfiction.net. If you follow this link, it will take you to my online tutoring and editing website -insert shameless plug- I've realized that professional tutoring and editing is the perfect profession for a serious writer. I can't get down with the whole starving artist deal (I want to eat!) So I started my own business. I'm a certified teacher and tutor. If you (or anyone you know) are in need of my assistance, follow the link!
