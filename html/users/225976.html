I'm an 20 year old female, a full-time college student, a part-time worker, and an obsessive writer. I'm glad to have joined fanfiction.net because my absolute passion is to write. (I do, however, have problems with ff.net at home, I'm working on fixing it). Despite that, I'm an avid Alias fan, and at the moment, my work here is primarily ALIAS-related. In the future, I plan to post up originals as well, but due to a newfound interest in ALIAS fan fiction writing, I'm making that my current focus.

I do hope you enjoy the first ALIAS fanfic series I've created, entitled "Storage Room Secrets". SRS centers on the Sydney-Vaughn relationship, and the mess that ensues following their initial kiss. Although, I'd like to say it's primarily a romance, I've decided to make it a dramatic romance (therefore, it's listed under "DRAMA", not romance) due to certain core elements. Please read and review, and email me to tell me what you think - regardless of whether your comments are good or bad, I welcome feedback.

I do have aspirations to one day become an author. Whether or not that dream is realized, I don't know. The least I can do is try. Which is what I'm doing here, honing my skills as best I can, with the help of others who review and/or beta my work. In the meantime, I plan to become a high school English teacher.

In my spare time, I enjoy writing (of course), reading romance novels (Nora Roberts is number one), watching tv (Alias rocks), and eating junk food (fork over the chocolate, baby). Despite that, I try to counter my negative eating habits by balancing some salad in there and working out. My friends are primarily in the age range of 23-33, and mainly male. I totally love them! I love my female friends as well, but sometimes... they're just too "girly" in my opinion. (I grew up around guys, so I'm more comfortable around them).

I feel I should point out that this name is not my real name. It is (as lame as the pun is) an ALIAS. In actuality, I stole the name from a good friend of mine, who's in the army. (Hi Matt, you know I love you). It is his middle and last name which I use, and he is aware of it (he laughed at me for it!).

Again, I hope you enjoy my work, and please leave me a review or a note in my inbox! I promise I answer my email. Thanks again and happy reading!
