Well look at this. I've updated my bio again. Too bad no one looks at these things. :P

I've gotten that better job now. And I'm still going to school. Thank goodness I'm almost done, I hate school. Maybe then I'll be able to concentrate on writing again and finish my original novel.

I'm still participating in those two small RPG's very slowly. Feel free to take a look.

One can be found at www .livejournal .com /community /angels_of_chaos and is rife with insanity and DBZ shtuff and quite NC-17. grins

I'm revisiting Fall of the Mighty. It has been on my mind a lot lately and I am still trying to work back into it. I really am!! I want to get back to it so much!! I AM updating it, but slowly.

Also another fic that I'm slowly working on is Young Man's Heart. That one is more likely to be finished before FOTM.

Let's see.

Personality - If you want to know who I am, just read my works. You can find everything that I am in the words that I write. I hide nothing of myself with my stories, in fact I lay myself bare as an author. Everything that I am I freely give to anyone who wishes to sift through my stories to learn it.

I love to tell stories and I love for you to tell me stories in return. I love my fantasy realm. I wish that I could delve into it and never resurface. But as long as I must exist in reality, I will just dabble. One day, my fantasy will become my reality and I'll be paid to share it with the world. Till then, I'm content to play at storytelling.

Anyway

Colors - Dark ones.
Black, dark greens, dark blues, grays, a tiny bit of red here and there, some tan. And bright Orange - I don't know what's wrong with me, but I love it.

If I could have any three wishes come true they would be...:drum roll:
1. To have a billion dollars - that about covers everything.
2. For myself and my two best friends to be in perfect physical condition. I.E. - all old battle wounds would vanish, faster healing, lean sleek muscles, etc. Think Wolverine. WOOOO!
3. To be able to morph myself into any shape, being, or sex at will. To be anything that I ever wanted to be at a whim.

I go for action and angst. No fluff. A little siliness is good but it's gotta be well done. I need no muse! It all comes from the black depths of my soul!! Perfect from the start!
