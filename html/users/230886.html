Author has written 11 stories for Harry Potter.
I am a graduate of the History program, University of Florida (go gators). As is evidenced by my involoved series of stories, I have way too much time on my hands. I love to research and I thought I'd try my hand at writing for pleasure instead of grades. (Probably heading toward a career in fiction).

A lot of my stories are influenced by my talented sister's tales of an unfortunate witch named Jude.
Check out her stories on ff.net under the pin soupofthedaysara. They are well worth the read and choc-full on suspense and intrigue.

I am a huge fan of "Blue Eyes" (he's in my stories everywhere.) What can I say? Frank Sinatra is the coolest!
I will never understand those crazies out there that don't like James Dean. (Random...but worth mentioning still).
Also some of my ideas and characters are inspired by authors that I hold dear for example; C. Dicken's 'Tale of Two Cities', V. Hugo's 'Les Miserables', and the great Jane Austin's 'Pride and Predjudice'. More modern influences include T. Harris, M. Crichton, J. Grisham, P. Pullman and many others as I expand my knowledge of literature.

I love to hear from my readers so please review. I'm open to any and all comments that you would like to share regarding my fiction. Please do not hesitate to write to me if there is anything you would like me to clear up.

These stories are written for and dedicated to you fine Harry Potter fans and of course to my faithful editor and braintrust Sara.

Read lots. Read often.
