Hello everyone!

I thought that it was about time that I updated this profile as I've now turned 21, well that was actually a month ago but hey better late than never.

Anyway as you can see from my story subjects I'm a loyal shipper of Obidala. My opinion is that the potential of this couple was wasted in the films and that we've missed out on some great story telling because of it. I don't think that there should have been a full on romance between them in the films but I do think that maybe some hint of an attraction between them would have been good for the overall story. For the life of me I've never been able to understand why Padme would fall for Anakin when there was a much better example of a Jedi and a man in Obi-Wan, I don't care what George thinks, Padme (and most other women) would fall for the MAN not the BOY.

Ok enough of that. I have a long running Obidala series which starts off with 'Come away with me' then continues with 'Wonderful tonight' and then 'How do I live?' and now with the latest addition of 'Those sweet words'. I'm by no means finished with this series yet, so have no fear there will be more stories to come, maybe not for a while but there will be some.

I also havethe stand alone stories of 'Feel the rush', 'Insatiable' and my long running and still not finished 'An Indecent Proposal'.

I'm a huge fan of Ewan Mcgregor and have beenever since I first saw 'A life less ordinary' 8 years ago. In my humble opinion there are few actors on this earth that have more depth and spirit than him, if any. I have him to thank for kick starting my love of film and for that I'll be eternally grateful. If you're old enough and open minded enough then I would strongly reccommend that you check out most of his films as they really are a joy to watch.

Have fun reading!
