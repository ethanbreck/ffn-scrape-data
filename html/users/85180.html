I have liked watching anime for a long time. After reading many of the fan fics, I decided to give it a try of my own. My stories may be a bit over romantic, but I just ask people to bear with me. My favorite character is Sailor Saturn, my all time favorite show is Escaflowne, and my favorite manga (right now) are Hot Gimmick and Basara. My favorite stories to read are Van&Hitomi, Rin&Sesshoumaru, and Kenshin&Kaoru (among various others ^^). I also help run a Sailor Moon site with my friend, so please go visit it and tell me what you think (My fanart is on the front page!).
