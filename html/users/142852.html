Author has written 8 stories for Star Wars, Lion King, Doctor Who, and Hercules.

I'm a 30-year-old female from Syston in the United Kingdom. I have a foundation degree in Animal Studies from the Nottingham-Trent University and love working and training dogs. In my leisure time I love to let my imagination run wild and write. I'm also an artist, love horse riding and enjoy reading.

I have many fandoms and interests, including the first series of the new "Doctor Who", "Mighty Morphin Power Rangers", Disney's "Hercules" and "Hunchback of Notre Dame", the "Pirates of the Carribean" movies, the "Star Wars" films, the "Discworld" books (especially the Vetinari ones!), and much more. I adore history, especially that of King Henry VII and Sir Francis Walsingham. I'm also told that I have a "thing" for mature, scheming men (generally with beards) but we shan't go into that. ;)
