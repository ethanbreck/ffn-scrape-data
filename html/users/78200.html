Author has written 4 stories for Young Hercules, Star Wars, and Supernatural.

Hmm, let see. 20-something female. Married. Brand new Flight Data Analyst (wow my education actually got me somewhere! only took 2 years after graduation! ;)) I'm still slightly giddy about that...forgive me. :)

Likes: I'm currently terribly addicted to the WB show Supernatural for reasons I can't really explain. I will gladly enter into inane conversations on the mental state of Sam and Dean, which has earned me many a strange look and a few threats of visits from the men in white from my real life friends. :)

I dabbled in poetry for years, though my creative juices seem to have dried up lately. I am currently working on a role playing script for a computer gaming company that my husband and several university friends have started.

I love the outdoors and anything relating to the outdoors. Biking, hiking, swimming, skiing, basketball, running, roadtrips, camping, picnics, you name it.

Obviously, due to my presence on this site I have a minor (read: all consuming) addiction to fanfiction. (For those of you over on LJ my pen name there is Astiraa.)+Of late my plot bunnies have become rather viscious so hopefully that means I'll be posting more stories in the near future. I'm a firm beliver in constructive criticism so I appreciate any and all feedback on my stories. (Yes, I know my Young Herc fic has been incomplete for ages and I do apologise, I really have no excuse except a terrible bout of writer's block and a loss of interest in a fandom I have no access to anymore. :( I'm still holding out hope that I might someday finish it, but I've lost my feel for the characters and I don't feel confident in the new chapters I've written.)

Dislikes: There aren't many really. Eggplant, mosquitoes and on some days the Edmonton Oilers. (What can I say, born and raised in Calgary. ;))
