Author has written 16 stories for Labyrinth, Naruto, Fullmetal Alchemist, Harry Potter, Wolf's Rain, One Piece, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

All throughout grade school, I was that shy kid at the back of the class, nose stuck in a book and mind miles away. Now, I am still that shy person, albeit grown a bit. Actually, I am 4 ft 11, the same height as Edward Elric, so scratch that last bit.

I like reading, writing, animes, mangas, and any storytelling medium. My favorite part of writing is playing around with characters and seeing what they would do in certain situations.

I hate character-bashing with a bloody vengeance. No character, no matter how annoying or useless they seem to you, deserves such hatred. I'm looking at you, Kagome and Sakura-bashers!

Ahem. I am currently in search of a Beta for my stories as I'm not exactly pleased with my writing and would appreciate help. If anyone's interested, please email me.

Other than that, please enjoy my writing and do occasionally leave a review. I like reviews. They make me feel all tingly inside.
