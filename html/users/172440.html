Most of what I write hovers somewhere between PG and R (or, in the new rating system, K+ and M). I have been known to crank out a G (K) rated piece from time to time, but usually you'll find at least one swear word, dirty joke, or "adult situation" in my work.

There are a few things that show up quite frequently in my writing and in the fiction I read, and I'd like to address them.

Homosexuality is the biggest. I'm a fan of yaoi and yuri, as well as het. I don't carewhat gender my characters are, as long as their pairing is believable to me.Please don't flameme if you don't like this. I post warnings in the summarry of every fic I put up, so it's not as if I throw "surprise gay" at you out of nowhere. I trust that if you're reading my work, it's because you knew what you were getting into and decided to proceed anyway.

Incest is another hot-button topic. To date, I have not written any incest fic, but I'm sure it's merely a matter of time. Let me stress that, while I have no problem with incest in fiction, I realize that, in real life, it's a traumatic and scarring subject. Again, if and when I write fiction featuring an incestuous pairing, I will most certainly post a warning in the summarry. If you're offended or grossed out by it, don't read it.

Finally, language. If the language I hear used on the street and in school every day is any indication, there aren't many people left who are offended by cursing and slaty language. However, for safety's sake, take it as a given that anything I write is fair game for a bit of colorful expression. I'm not dropping profanity randomly in the text, but if it's in-character for a character to curse in a certain situation, then they are going to curse.
