No, I don't know anything about stalking Joss Whedon... and thank you very much, I was at home yesterday night. Yes, you can give me Spike in any way, shape, or form. :)
I'm a girl, obviously, and... I have hair. Lots o' hair. Eyes, too, and a nose. I'm young, younger than you would probably think (anyone who can guess my real age in one shot, will have my next story dedicated to them for sure). Uh, what else... ah, i dunno. If your still reading after this long, I'm quite shocked. I am very chatty, so anyone that likes to speak BTVS, LotR, or James Marsters, I'm here. That's about it. The End!
