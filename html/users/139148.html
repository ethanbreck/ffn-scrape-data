and now i'm gonna tell you about the origains of my pen name: Grey Wolf

Grey Wolf was a wizard from Xanthus out of an old cartoon i watched when i was 8. he was a good wizard and had a brother and a sister, Sasha and Misha. Then this snake guy came and turned them into wolves. Grey Wolf them joined up with Conan the Barberian (that was the series' name) and tried to find a licanfor flowerto return his sibs to human form and distroy the snake people that was inslaving earth. And that's Grey Wolf.

Now about me, I'm a 19 year old girl that lives in South Africa and loves writing, reading, music, writing, chating, answering emails, writing, watching movies and you guest it: writing. I'm also trying to study film making, trying the opretive word ;p

I love Final fantasy 7 (not such a big fan of the others), Harry Potter, Highlander, Vampire Chronicles(but I can't post here), Gravitation, Yami no Mtsuei, Gundam Wing, Digimon and Lord of the rings
