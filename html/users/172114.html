Hey!

So you want a bio, eh? Well, I might as well make this long and boring. Here goes..

Pen Name: Rin Adhara Black. Or just Rin if you prefer.

Pen Name Meaning: Rin means "park". Adhara means "maiden". Hmmm...Park Maiden. I think I am going to change my pen name. But, Rin Adhara does sound cool, though.

Real Name: Irena

Real Name Meaning: Serene, Peaceful

Birthday: May 19

Zodiac Sign: Taurus

Personality: A lot lie Draco or Snape, a real asshole most of the time. But I have a nice side too...somewhere. A surefire way to get on my bad side is to insult me or say I'm an idiot. It's true sometimes, but I DO NOT need people pointing it out.

Pets: I have one cat, named Soxy. He's a gray tabby cat with black stripes and white feet. He is, according to all my friends, evil. My friend Risha calls him the "Cat of Satan" because he constantly hisses at her. I think it's pretty funny because she won't even walk accross a room in my house if my cat is there. She wants to give him cancer.

Favorite Things: My favorite colors are pink and silver. My lucky number is 7 (yes, I know, how original). My favorite animal is any kind of cat (big or small). My favorite class at skool is drama, I guess, because you barely have to do anything and the guys in there are funny (in a perverted way). I love junk food, shopping, going online, and most importantly, JOSH HARTNETT!

Can't Stand:ANIMAL ABUSE!, people who think they are pretty/funny/skinny/popular but in reality they aren't, people who giggle incessantly, when my hair is messed up, people with no sense of humor, my Spanish teacher, having a cold, and terrorism.

Favorite HP Characters: SIRIUS (duh!), Lupin, Harry, Draco :), and Snape.

A Typical Day In The Life Of Rin:

5:15 a.m.: My alarm clock rings. I take it in my hand in smash it into the ground. I pick up the alarm clock and check for any damage.

5:20 a.m.: I walk down to my bathroom and plug in my straightening iron. I go back to my room and try to find some clothes that look halfway normal.

5:40 a.m.: I finish getting dressed. I walk downstairs and shove a handful of Cinnamon Toast Crunch into my mouth; ahhh...breakfast. Sometimes I even have a glass of milk.

5:41 a.m.: After hearing my cat's pitiful meowing, I decide to be nice today and let him out of the basement, where my parents so evilly locked him the night before. Sometimes I even go as far as feeding him.

5:43 a.m.: I go upstairs and begin to straighten my hair.

6:12 a.m.: I finish straightening my hair. I proceed to wash my face and out on makeup.

6:22 a.m.: I finish with the makeup and brush my teeth.

6:27 a.m.: I go downstairs; done with my daily ritual. Sometimes I watch Rocket Power on Nickelodeon. Or do homework that I forgot to do before.

6:50 a.m.: I grab whatever books/work/weapons I amy need for skool that day.

6:55 a.m.: I yell at whoever is taking me to skool to hurry the hell up so I won't be late.

7:02 a.m.: We depart for skool.

7:05 a.m.: We arrive at skool.

7:06 a.m.: I stop at my locker, which is not allowed before skool starts. A teacher walks by. I tell them my backpack is too heavy. They believe me and walk away; unsuspecting..

7:07 a.m.: I go to the table in the cafeteria where my friends sit. I listen to whoever's there complain about stuff.

7:25 a.m.: Risha gets here. We talk about people; evilly.

7:28 a.m.: Drea comes. I listen to her talk about...Joe!

7:35 a.m.: We go to our lockers. I cram the stuff in mine, unaware of the fact that it is overflowing with crap.

7:35 and 48 seconds (a.m.): I enter my homeroom and ignore all the mindless idiots there. Risha comes in and we talk. Sometimes she stays and helps me with stuff.

7:45 a.m.: The bell rings. Risha leaves. The "skool news" comes on. Nobody listens. I sit; bored; listening to my classmates talk about their idiotic, meaningless lives.

8:05 a.m.: Bell rings. I go to first period science. Sit down next to Risha. We proceed to talk about people; evilly. Or we pay attention. Well, Risha pays attention. I sit there, stupidly trying to figure out what we're doing.
