Author has written 1 story for Golden Sun.

So, two years later . . .

Here I am, nearly finished with my junior year of college, and I really haven't written any fan fiction since I started my undergrad. Partially, this is due to the fact that one of my majors is English Composition, so I've been doing my own plots, but it's also laziness. As per usual. Since all the rest of it is really quite terrible, I've taken down everything except GS, SM. I've got copies on my computer for posterity; nobody else really needs them.

You can contact me over MSN, AIM (Super Emili), or email if for some reason you feel like conversing with the pathetically lazy me.

The best place to watch for updates is my deviantArt page. Even if I don't actually post anything here or on Fictionpress, there's a fairly regular update of doodles and such over there, as well as the occasional link to whatever I might be working on in literature.

Story info:
Golden Sun, Silver Moon. I still like this story, really. If I work on any fanfiction, it'll be this. I'm not sure whether we'll be visiting Alex and Rakka next, or be checking out what the heck is up with Issac.

This story is a bit of an experiment for me. Besides being an opportunity to expand on a game series of which I'm quite fond, it gives me a chance to test out writing from the points of view of several very different characters. I intend to cycle through the points of view of all the major characters, plus a few extras.
