I am a kindergarten teacher that just has a true passion for writing. I have two kids, I am currently on maternity leave I just had a baby boy. My time is about to start disappearing once I return to my classroom. Between a Mommy and writing curriculum to prove that I'm way to valuable to let go in these economic crisis times, updates are slow going, coupled with the fact that I a recovering from a crippling case of writer's block. Hopefully reading all these fics as cured me for good...though honestly I doubt it.

My Likes: Naruto, X-men, Harry Potter, Circle of Magic, Teen Titans, Jonas and Ben 10: Alien Force (what can I say my students get me curious than get me addicted)

I must admit that I am not the best poster in the world and I've been questioning my inspirations. Currently my x-men fic have fizzled and I have lost all passion for them so feel free to request to take them off my hands. I have however found my spark in Naruto. My newest story Underneath The Underneath is just so tangible to me. I feel the need to write it as if it is bursting through my fingers hopefully I do not fizzle that out.
