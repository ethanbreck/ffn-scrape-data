My name is Elyssa. I am a terrible writer, but I'm a decent judge of good writing :D I mainly read Harry Potter, mostly the ships Harry/Draco... and Draco/Harry. Haha. I dabble in a little Sirius/Remus as well, and I'll give any really good general story a chance, too. I've also started dabbling in Twilight fanfics as well (BellaxEdward only!). If you have a story you would like me to read, or beta, I would love to, but I'm pretty terrible at just writing stuff off the top of my head.

Um... more about me...

I study film and video right now at college. I love all things magic. I would much rather take a walk through snowy streets than lounge outside on a hot summer's day. I hate sitting around in my house by myself, but since I'm an only child, I'm forced to do it a lot. That's basically why I read fanfiction so much- fictional characters are much better company than my own mind.

I love happy endings. I live for happy endings. I can't possibly imagine why people love tragic endings so much- I read and watch fiction to escape reality. Why would I escape to a world without a happy ending?

Love is probably the most beautiful thing in existence.
