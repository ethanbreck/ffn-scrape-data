A woman living in the UK, I did my degree in creative writing and I've been doing fanfiction since I was 13 (I'm now in my early thirties). At the moment I am working through my unfinished fanfiction stories, trying to finish them or at the very least re-editing what I do have so it's cleaner and easier to read.

One tip I've picked up and I'd like to share is if you tend to write your stories in a passive voice rather than an active one then there is a simple test you can apply to check your sentences as you write.

If you can place the words 'by zombies' after the sentence and it makes sense then you are writing in the passive tense, if not then it's active. Generally speaking, one should avoid using the passive voice all the time as it doesn't create a sense of urgency or drama in the mind of your reader.
