Fave Color: green, black, and silver. They've been like that since before I read Harry Potter so no, it's not just because of Slytherin colors.

I like having Gryffindors play like idiots and having the Slytherins be good (most of them anyway) because I think that the Slytherins in JKR's mind are all too stereotyped. Really, there has to have been at one point a Muggleborn with ambition put into that house. Voldemort and Death Eaters haven't always existed you know.
