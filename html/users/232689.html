Author has written 9 stories for Stargate: SG-1, and Supernatural.

I'm a student at Mount Allison University and within the past few years, I have just started writing fanfiction. I wouldn't say that I'm new to the art, but I'm definitely no pro at it.

Just so everybody knows, I am widely open to constructive criticism about my work. If you see something that has the potential to be improved, tell me and I'll take your words into account. Anything to make me a better writer is welcome.

None of my work is beta-read, I just read it over and edit it countless times on my own and then post. I haven't gotten any complaints, so I'm guessing that that's a good sign.

Like any writer, I love to read what people have to say about my stories, it really is inspiring to get feedback from those who like the work. It also lets me know who's reading.

Happy reading and writing!
