Okay, time I think to update it is. Sorry, channeling Yoda again. It happens sometimes.

For those brave few who actually read the author pages of stories you come across- WELCOME! Most call me Drace but some of the old timers might say Drac. I live in the United States in the lovely landlocked state of Iowa. (Iowa...not Idaho!) Sorry, had a few people from other countries confuse the two. Where in Iowa you might ask? I live about a hours drive from Des Moines, an hours drive or so from Iowa City about 2 hours drive from the Missouri border and about 150 miles from Omaha, Nebraska. Hehe, if you can figure it out from that, email me and I'll send you a cinnamon-vanilla cappu-latte with extra cream and nutmeg. I'm 24 years old until the beginning of next month when I'll be 25. Yee gods and goddesses, a quarter of a century...where did it all go? What am I forgetting...oh yes, I am female if you haven't already figured it out.

Since my last profile update ah...much has happened. I switched majors and actually graduated from college. Now I am looking for the ultimate of horrors- a job! So my attention for fanfiction is still strong but with everything going on in my life I have no idea when either of my fics will be updated. Also, I kinda lost the original copy - the only copy- of my Phimonis story. Considering however that I wrote that thing in...8th...9th? grade its kinda old and problably has quite a bit of typos and other errors. Whenever I find a job and actually go have to go through all of my lovely boxes I'll probably find it. Until then its on hold.

As for interests-my tastes are eclectic by some standards and just plain strange by others. I like general fics as well as slash but detest femslash (blegh). While you wait for me to add to my fics though please check out those great fics and authors that have caught my attention long enough and seem grand enough to put onto my favorite stories and authors.

Chow 4 Now,
