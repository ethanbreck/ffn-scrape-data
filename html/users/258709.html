Hi there! This is Song of Amazon from Tower of Time: Otaku Senshi or SoA from TheForce.net.

I primarily write Sailor Moon and Star Wars (Knights of the Old Republic Era) fanfiction, with a focus on otaku senshi and other original characters. Lately I have also been branching out into Dragon Age as well. For more art galleries, profiles, and other goodies related to my Sailor Moon fanfiction, feel free to check out my Sailor Moon web site, the Tower of Time.

With the exception of my brief stint in TOR era fanfiction, all of my Star Wars fanfics tie into a single, moderately AU fan universe. The primary focus of this is the Redemption Quartet:
Book 1: Under the Shadow of the Builders (complete)
Book 2: The Road to Rediscovery (complete)
Book 3: Echoes of War (in progress)
Book 4: War of Shadows (in planning stages)

"The Exile of Bastila Shan" breaks off from "The Road to Rediscovery" and runs in parallel, rejoining with the main story part way through "Echoes of War." All other shorts are meant to act as background for the various characters of the main story.

I primarily write fanfiction during NaNoWriMo and the two summer Camp NaNoWriMo, while the rest of the year is spent working on my original fiction and editing the fanficion written during NaNo, so you may see large gaps between the posting of chapters from me. I appreciate your readership and your patience.
