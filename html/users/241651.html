Author has written 5 stories for Power Rangers, Four Brothers, Dragon Ball Z, Harry Potter, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

Update May 15: Am finally writing again. For those following the Four Brothers fic expect an update soon.

Update June 8: I finally caved and set up a livejournal. I also posted some original fiction at fictionpress if anyone is curious. http://www.fictionpress.com/u/580138/

Hello! Welcome to my lil space on the web. I am a writer, didn't choose to be it just happened. Sometimes it's wonderful and sometimes it's downright aggravating. I am 26 and work for a living so updates can be sporadic, but I try. Right now I'm really into my Four Brothers fic. Eventually I will add to my Power Rangers one, but it's not in the near future.

Completed Fics

What Might Have Been is a Power Rangers fic about Tommy and Kimberly. A rather overdone subject, but one I had to add my two sense to. Basically, Kim and Tommy find their way to each other without Kim becoming a Power Ranger again. It's a little choppy and unrealistic, but I'm rather proud of it.

Still Frame is my little foray into Harry Potter. I've been toying with story lines and characters from that story line for years and finally decided to post a piece. It is drabble based so not much, but a start.

Elemental is a little adventure into the world of TLA.

In Progress Fics

Seamless is a Four Brothers fic about Bobby Mercer. In the synopsis I do write pre, during, and post movie. To be honest it won't have much during the movie because, well, if you wanna know rent the movie. I'm sure not gonna pretend like I can do better then the peeps who made the movie. Bobby is such a complex character and I try my best to put it on 'paper' and, while there are some original characters, I like to think they don't crowd the story. Anyways, check it out. She's my pride and joy right now and I'm really excited about where the story is going.

Dancing On Water is a Dragon Ball Z fanfic, because the plot line wouldn't leave me alone. This is one that I work on when Seamless frustrates the hell out of me. It's a Goku daughter fic that I hope won't turn into a cliche. Mainly features Goku's daughter, Vegeta, and Bulma as of now.
