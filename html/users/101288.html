Atashi, chotto okashii wa ne~?

If you all haven't noticed, I've changed my Pen name here on FF.net, I'm still technically writing under Hana-chan but I really didn't want to be Hana-chan1 so now you all shall know me as Yanagawa Hanako

Well of coarse I am, I'm a high school student from Hickville, Iowa!

I love writing, it's my major passion, and it gets me through boring boring high school.

But this year I've discovered, that sadly, school actually requires work. so less writing from me T_T.

Plus I finally got a PlayStation 2, so I've kinda been playing Final Fantasy X a little too much, I can't help it! Tidus is really hot! Plus, do you have any idea how long it takes to beat Seymour? Plus, there's the ego blow from getting killed by someone named Seymour.
Eh... lo siento, tend to babble, I guess I'll tell you aboout my favorite things, even though no one cares.

Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers and FFVIII as well.

Book: Lord of The Rings

Alternate Personality: Personality #7, definetly Personality #7, she's a cool chick.

Wierd things to do: Practice modling my Japanese speech patterns after Nuriko, but then I have to make very sure NOT to use them in Japanese class. (Yatta wa ne? Atashi, nuigurumi no kami da wa!)

So now you know a little about me... Ah, here's some updates on my Stories:

Deleated Scenes: Need to watch the series to get ideas, and that would require me using my tv for something other than FFX and The Daily Show (With Jon Stewart!)
Everything else: Writers block

Bleh, sorry for the major major lack of updates minna, I've been having a severe year+ long bout of writers block, I think I just realized how extremely mediocre my writing was before, I don't know If I'll be ficcing anymore in the near future, but I don't think I've completely given up all hope.
