Age: 22. God I'm so old.

I've been writing since I was about 3. Of course, not all of it was good. :D I have had my fics down from ff.net for about 5 years now, ever since my major writers block hit. Thankfully, I have some new interests which inspire me.

I would like to thank my two fabulous beta's for helping me with "The World Has Turned"--Silver Wolf Pups and Ladyhawk89. You guys are great and I hope you know it.

Some of my fiction cannot be posted on here due to the fact that it's of a mature nature. If you are interested in it, go see my website, a majority of it is posted up there.

I appreciate every single review I get, even if it is criticism, because it helps me grow and know what I am doing wrong. However, I write for myself because I enjoy doing it. If you read the story and like it or have a problem with it, let me know. But it's most certainly not required to review. Although, even if you don't, I hope you enjoy the stories.
