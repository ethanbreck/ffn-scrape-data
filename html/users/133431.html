Hi! I'm just another girl who's taking a stab at writing.

Some say I look Japanese. Others Chinese. Still others Korean. They're all wrong. I'm actually Filipino and I'm quite proud to be one!

I LOVE Final Fantasy (FFVII being my most favorite, FFX-2 being my least), Resident Evil, Tekken, Japanese street fashion, Chinese movies, Meiji candies, almond jelly, surfing, and geeking around.

I also love hearing from you guys! I really appreciate all of your sweet reviews and e-mails. Thank you so much!

I'm currently working on Sunsets at Wutai. It's another Cloud x Yuffie fic. But unlike Hearts Desire, this isn't light or funny. It's actually filled with angst. The writing style's kinda different too. I'm hoping to finish it over the weekend.

FF COUPLE SEARCH - - I pity the FF fan who hasn't been here.

I'm obviously bored. I rarely write long bios. Press the END button quick.

Aaanyway, here are my writings so far:

FFVII

Hearts Desire
Cutesy Cloud x Yuffie fic. Don't let the pairing scare you. You might just like it.

FFVIII

Rinny's Tress Stress
A stupid (and supposedly funny) fic I wrote back when I hated Rinoa.

Walking After You
My personal favorite. Quistis x Seifer.

Infatuated Souls
My Quistis x Seifer multi-chaptered fic. Don't really like this one that much but it's the most popular among my writings.

Sweet Charade
An angsty Squall/Quistis/Seifer. It features as single story told in the three souls' POVs. I'm still hoping to finish this one.

Dulled Hyperion
Yet another Quistis x Seifer. This won best poetry in the first Seiftis awards. I'm quite proud of it.

Angel's Masquerade
Another angsty poem this time in Seifer's point of view. Written while waiting for ideas for Infatuated Souls.

Stained
Squall x Quistis this time. Think suicide and hidden feelings finally expressed.

FFX

Unexpected
My very first fanfic. Very very crappy. I just wanted to be the first person to write a Rikku angsting over Tidus fic.

Stigmatized
My sappiest fic. Tidus x Rikku angst once again.

FFX-2

Waiting for the Sun to Shine
Rikku angsting over Tidus + Gippel = Gipryu! The first English GxR fic.

Pyreflies x Petals
Another Gippel x Rikku. My take on FFX-2.
