Author has written 40 stories for CSI, Lost, Prison Break, Supernatural, Mentalist, Veronica Mars, Psych, and Harry Potter.

Well, I'm in my twenties. I graduated with a BA in English and Creative Writing, which I don't regret despite it being about as practical and as useful to my everyday life as toe socks. As far as fanfiction goes, I'm a huge h/c fan. I'm all about angsty childhood memories and concussion stories and panic attacks . . . because I am, at heart, a very mean person, and I like to torture my favorite characters, either physically or psychologically. But then they get to talk about feelings and calm down and heal just a very little. See? Happy endings! (Mostly, anyway.)

What you are less (that is, not at all) likely to find here are mpreg stories or incest fic. I try not to judge, but it's just not my thing.

My Mentalist story "Only That Which Is Written In Blood" won for Gen Best Drama in the 2012 Paint It Red Awards. This was kind of awesome.

Hope you enjoy!
