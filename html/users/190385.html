Wow, so I've finally reached the age of 23, and I still find myself writing :P You'd think I would have grown out of fanfiction by now, but no lol

My homepage is my Tumblr. Almost everything I write now-a-days is written on there first, then cross posted to FF.net. So if you want to be one of the first to see it, Tumblr is the place to be. It is also where I will post goings on, and when I may plan to write more.

My obsessions come and go with the wind. One minute I'll be completely ga-ga for one pairing, and begin writing a fic. Then I start watching, playing, or see art from another coupling, and I start a story on them. Though I do this frequently, I always shift back to the other pairing some time or another. So just bare with me

Reviews are like my guilty pleasure. I have a lil squeal when I actually get one, and each one makes me want to write more on the fic

Ps: I love everyone who reads my fics and enjoys them... you guys rock

I enjoy all kinds of feedback on my stories. Even flames will spur me to write more. Why? Cuz then it makes me want to continue just to spite them :D So feel free to leave me comments of any kind. Also feel free to email me, or get a hold of me on facebook if you have questions, or comments. I check my emails everyday (unless my internet is down)
