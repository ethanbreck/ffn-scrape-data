Hello there! Well, you want to find out a little more about me, eh?

First off, I'm a huge anime fan. My favorites include Sailor Moon, Slayers, Utena, Weiss Kreuz, and Yu Yu Hakusho. I have many favorite characters and couples, that would take far too long to list here. I am the Self-Proclaimed Xelloss no Miko, the official (at least, as official as you can get on the internet) Co-Crawford no Miko, and Keeper of Brad's Evil Glasses. I also am the Self-Proclaimed Keeper of a Flood of Chibi-Farfies (do not be afraid, we have blenders!).
I'm also an avid Doctor Who fan. I have a website (geocities.com/sddalek)for my Whovian interests (shameless plug!). If I had to choose, Sylvester McCoy would be my favorite Doctor, and Ace one of my favorite companions. I'm a softie for Daleks, especially black-and-silver ones called Harvey. I love the UNIT men as well. Yeah Sgt. Benton! He's my favorite!
Miscelanneous Schtuff: I'm a rabid fan of the Sera Myu musicals, and think Hikari Ono is a goddess of the stage. The Utena Musical is another of my guilty pleasures. Saito Rei as Touga...sooooooooooooo pretty! Lots of yummy eye-candy for Lufia! Dance, Touga! Dance!

I haven't updated in a very, very long time, but I am planning to soon. I've been working on revisions to "Reunion," which is finished and has been for ages, but I haven't gotten round to putting it up. Once my beta's finished with it, though, the entire story will be up. I've also got a Doctor Who novella I've had for years, and a short story, and my Sailor Moon Nanowrimo effort coming down the pipeline. It's all a matter of transferring them from one computer to the other for ease of uploading.
