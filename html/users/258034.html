8/28/2013 - Okay, so, been gone a while. But, I want to start coming back, so many stories itching under my fingers. First off, I'm going to start posting Abscission of Light up over here. Finally. I was startled when I came back and realized I had posted a link for the trailer/music video, but hadn't started adding the story! Depending on how quick I am in finishing it up (I'm finishing up chapter ten), I'll try to post new chapters at least a couple times a week. I'd forgotten about Night Wind! So, it's on the back burner, along with the Angel of Darkness thing. I'm going to focus on finishing up Abscission of Light, Hold Me Close, One Last Time, and Twilight of the Ballerina. In the interim, I may also post some one-shots I've done, just so I don't go without updating here.

2/15/2012 - EAB went down at the end of last year, but it's been brought back again! The new site is It's Always as Easy as Breathing, and now encompasses several fandoms across the spectrum - anime, movies, books, tv shows, and stars.

12/4/2011 - The last of If has been added! Currently mostly at work on a story called Abscission of Light, I'm going to start adding it here soon. Again, the 18 version can be found on itsalwayseasyasbreathing.ning.com. Hold Me Close, One Last Time is still upcoming, it's just taking me a little longer. Night Wind and If: Hidden Moon are still planned. The original concept of Angel of Darkness has been scrapped - it was a Jacob/Renesmee, and I can't bring myself to write it. Instead, the song has inspired me to finally start on a Leah/Sam fic.

I'm still trying to fix any errors in If since I lost my beta but wanted to continue updates. So if you see any mistakes and feel up to shooting me a private message about any of them that you catch before I do, I would appreciate it!
