Author has written 36 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Captain Planet, and Dragon Age.

Hi - I'm Kate, aka, Pervinca, katydid, brachan90 or WonderBra (depending on the site).

I have been a FF.net user for over 10 years (kind of sad when I realised that. I feel so old!). I started out writing DBZ stories, but later moved onto Lord of the Rings (which make up the majority of my stories). I have written one random Harry Potter story, and a couple of Captain Planet (since there was a bit of a lack of these!).

It's been a while since I've written, but my mind has some Dragon Age Stories in the works, which I hope to make a start on soon :)

I've also been a beta reader in the past, so more than happy to assist people. I like to think I have very good spelling and grammar, and depending on the fandom, a pretty good grasp of canons. Send me a PM if you want some help :)
