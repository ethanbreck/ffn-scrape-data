Why 'RainbowCubes87'?

The username I'm using comes from a conversation held more than 5 years ago while I was still 18. It was on the way to my great uncles funeral. My uncle (whom is only 7 years older than me) was getting a lift from my Mum and the brief discussion was something along the lines of:

(Someone talking about sugar cubes)

"or rainbow cubes" Mark (my Uncle)

"What's a rainbow cube?" Me

"It's like a sugar cube, only it's got rainbows in it" Mark (my uncle)

He's not quite normal, but then who is. Proven by the fact that on the way back from said funeral he, without notifying anyone, stowed away in the boot/trunk of his father's (my grandfather, R.I.P) car and played on his phone. My Grandfather had no idea where the beeping noise was coming from until we arrived at the house and my Uncle got out of his boot/trunk.

Wow, I just noticed the year I joined. Jeez, I've been a member for 13 years? Holy crap, was I really only 13 when I joined up?
