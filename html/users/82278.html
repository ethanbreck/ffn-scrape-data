Author has written 32 stories for That '70s Show, General Hospital, Stargate: Atlantis, Battlestar Galactica: 2003, Charmed, Doctor Who, Torchwood, Instant Star, Queer as Folk, Vampire Diaries, and CSI.

I started telling stories when I was a baby and once I learned to write I started to write them. When I started to get favorite shows I began to write small stories in a notebook. It wasn't until I found this site that I learned that other people did it as well.
Now I do have an Archive where I will be putting these stories as well. I also post my banner, icons, and music videos. My Blood Ties stories are only on my archive because they are rated NC-17.

I don't mind constructive critismism, but please don't be rude! Telling me my story sucks, and not telling me why doesn't help me improve.
Thank you to everyone who has reviewed my stories, I always love feedback. Also I only get so much time to write; I am a full time college student, supervisor at my job, and I help raise my two nieces and nephew.

I deleted all of my stories (over 20 of them) that were written/posted between 2001-2003.
When I get the chance I will be rewriting some of them. This will take awhile because as I said before many of the stories were rushed and had so many errors.
