Hello fellow writers and readers. My name is Frannie. I've been writing fanfiction for a while now, since I was twelve years old. I have a fairly eclectic variety here at Fanfiction.net for you to enjoy (among them Final Fantasy X, Full Metal Alchemist, Master and Commander, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and most recently, Hunger Games). I'm currently shipping Peeta and Katniss, so there will be more of those stories to come, if that's your ship. There might be some other things up here as well.

If you are a fan of Mystery Science Theater 3000 or RiffTrax, check our the site that I run with my sister Aurora West, MSTs Are Fun. It's no longer allowed on Fanfiction.net for obvious reasons, so you will find many of our MSTed stories on our website. Check it out at https://sites.google.com/site/mstsarefun/. If you like Harry Potter characters, you'll like our MSTs.

So, enjoy my stories, and leave a review if you like.
