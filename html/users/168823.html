My name is Caroline and I like: Buses. Sleeping in. Sappy romance movies. Kissing in the rain. That feeling you get during happy endings. Dancing down the aisle. Drawing hearts on my notes. Driving a bus. Having a crush. Falling in love. Singing in the shower. Listening to loud music. Day dreaming. Best friends. Sleep overs. Hair cuts. Baking cookies. The 5 second rule. Puppy kisses. Sunshine on a cloudy day. And Tom, I like Tom a lot.

Mostly I read Harry Potter fics and some Hunger Games fics.

I used to be a huge Romione fan, but now I pretty much only read Dramione!!!

Other pairings I enjoy are: Katniss/Peeta, Rose/Scorpius and Drarry!
