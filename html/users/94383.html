Other then that I'm the mother of two and I love to read plain and simple. My grandmother writes books and has been published as long as I've been alive and she inspires me to write. I also write original stories, but I tend to keep those to myself at the moment. I'm also going to eventually put together a real home page instead of just having a link to my Neopets. give me time I'll chalk it all up with pictures fic's and more! Whoo!
