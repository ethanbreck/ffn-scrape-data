I'm Lucky Seven, and yes, my handle used to be Konstantine. But hey, the world's an ever changing ocean of... blah blah blah. Sorry, my writer's sensibilities are failing me at the moment.
I've been writing fics since the fourth grade. It started as a hobby that whenever I would get a new obsession with some facet of pop culture I would write fics about it. Now that I haven't been obsessed with anything for a good couple of months, I write about whatever moves me to pick up a pencil.
If you want to know more, I live in Chicago, I'm in high school... ummmm... I have a dog...

Parting Quote:
"Dreams are our rehersal for adversity. They are our self-education, a way for us face our fears before we have anything to fear. We do not have to have degrees in psycology to know that it is our dreams that keep us sane. And literature - storytelling - is our collective dream."
-Elliot S. Maggin, Kingdom Come
(Kingdom Come is the novel adaption of the DC graphic novel/comic book epic of the same name. I think that's appropriate, considering what it's about and where I'm using it.)
