Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter.

I started this account nearly a decade ago, and have been very sporadic in my time writing fanfiction. I spent much of the past five years writing original short stories in university, and took many hiatuses based on devoting my time to things like theatrical productions and operas, getting married, graduating first high school and then college, finding a job in this wretched economy with an English degree (probably my proudest feat to date), battling leukemia, getting a bone marrow transplant, and just getting distracted by various things in life.

I have added two stories I wrote in 2006 and 2007, respectively, that until now have just been posted at Phoenixsong. Nothing on my profile is DH-compliant yet - I'll be working on updating some of my unfinished stuff, but there's no reason to rewrite the story I wrote as a dare or the pre-OOTP songfic that both deal with how Voldemort dies... Because I'm updating my stories, though, you'll be able to figure out what's old and what's new just based on how the prose comes across. Sound like I'm 15? Probably was.
