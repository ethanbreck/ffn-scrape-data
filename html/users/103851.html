Author has written 20 stories for X-Files, Sound of Music, Picket Fences, X-Men: The Movie, Gilmore Girls, JAG, CSI, and Army Wives.

I've been a fanfiction writer for about two and a half years and have recently branched out into new universes. My first fic ever was for Boston Public. I have published fiction for that show, the X-files, Sound of Music, X-men, Picket Fences, JAG, Gilmore Girls, CSI, and the Commish. My ships are:

Boston Public: Scott/Lauren, Kevin/Marilyn, Harry/Dana

X-files: Scully/Skinner or Scully/Mulder w/ Skinner/Mrs. Scully

Sound of Music: The one and only ship, Captain and Maria

X-men: Wolverine/Rogue

Picket Fences: Maxine/Kenny

JAG: Harm/Mac

Gilmore Girls: Lorelai/Luke and Rory/Jess

CSI: Grissom/Sara

The Commish: Casefile, and Tony/Cyd Madison (AU of course.)

I am a realistic writer. I write fiction that mirrors real life experiences. I do not believe in fast endings or in miracle endings i.e. a victim of a car crash with no seatbelt in a come for a year waking up to the sound of his or her true loves voice and leaving the hospital in perfect health the next day. I have lived a hard life and I am stronger for that and more so my the relationships that have lasted through the hard times are stronger than any others I've ever had. It is that joy, the joy of a constant rock through sorrow I bring to my characters. I thank everyone for reading.
