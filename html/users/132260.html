Author has written 10 stories for Pokémon, Digimon, MÄR, and Hayate the Combat Butler.

If you can't tell, I'm a very musical person, even my stories tend to have at least one song in them/based on a song lyric/etc.

If you notice, I'm slow about writing things. There's more in my head, both fanfiction-wise and non-fanfiction, but there's a disconnect somewhere between my brains and my fingers. Maybe eventually I'll get it cleared and have more written. The muses are distracted from helping me with that too.. maybe once they deal with their issues we'll all be able to get back to writing, though everything probably won't be dealt with for quite a while.

Unless someone kicks me about finishing, this story is at an end, even though I've written a last chapter for it, there are more pieces to the story before it makes sense.

MAR Anime Music Writings: Don't Wanna Miss A Thing is by Aerosmith, My Little Girl is by Tim McGraw (inspired by friends and (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rnQMcQiuj7o) song), Itsy Bitsy Teenie Wienie is by Bryan Hyland, Hello Muddah, hello Fadda is from Dr. Demento and performed by another group (having trouble locating the song and the actual artist), My Sister is by Reba McEntire (http://youtube.com/watch?v=-LI42Z0cM7c) and the characters are from MAR.

Six Months: The story of MAR isn't mine, this story is just my attempt to fill in the holes left by the first venture into the Shuuren no Mon using the episodes (9 and 10?) and manga volume 3. Taking suggestions for more plotlines for future chapters.

The main story is for Ginta and Snow and Merilo. I'll also probably make a side-story to fill in the gaps for Jack and Dorothy and Bumoru

Nekomimi: Finally got back into writing, first thing I managed to finish was a short piece for Hayate no Gotoku, entirely written for humor.

Hurt Me: Something else for HnG, but still not in the course of the main fic I want to write, in fact, farther from it. It's an AU from that plan (and hopefully Hata's as well) The song isn't mine, and the story it takes from is Kenjiro Hata's. I'm just a fan of both. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZ7A9CZfyXU). And I decided to add to the story to show that I don't hate Athena, and the pairings for the Baka Trio are taken from plans for the primary fic-line.

Have other ideas for stories, but the muse is being slow to let me type them together for upload.

Wanting to write a lengthy piece of HnG, and have many of the plot points planned (though most are just notes). Oddly enough, the work I'm furthest along with is the sequal short, followed by the 'last chapter' piece. So essentially I'm writing it backwards O.o

Favorite characters for it are Hinagiku and Nagi, but I'll be trying to give all the characters their time in the spotlight.

I'll try to post it as it becomes viable to, so try to coax my muse into helping with that. Not sure what her name is now, so you'll just have to try things.
