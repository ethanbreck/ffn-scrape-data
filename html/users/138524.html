My LiveJournal

About Me:

I'm 33, married, and the mama of a very naughty giant schnauzer. (If you know this breed, you know that 'very naughty' is code for smarter than most people, with freakishly human reasoning skills, and the ability to get into trouble in less than 10 seconds).

As some of you may have guessed from my pen name, I'm a health care professional, with long and sometimes odd hours. TV, movies, and reading (fanfics and books) are my escape from the seriousness of work.

My Obsessions:

Someone else on this site wrote in their profile that they 'collect obsessions'. I'm beginning to think I do the same, aided and abetted by the internet and the 24-hr access to anything I'm interested in.

My number one interest(obsession) is the tv show 'Bones', and anything related to it. This extends to basically anything containing the smokin' hot David Boreanaz, so I am currently working my way through the first season of 'Angel'. I'm beginning to understand the rabid fan base for that show, based solely upon the observation he is shirtless at some point in almost every episode. (No complaint here.)

I'm also totally addicted to Harry Potter fanfiction, for the simple fact that I just can't say goodbye to these characters.

Oddly enough, I'm also pretty fixated on Johnny Depp as Capt. Jack Sparrow in POTC. Something about those kohl-rimmed eyes ...

Fan Fiction:

I love to read and I love tv so fan fiction is a dream come true. I also love to write, but I concede to my mediocrity in that area. I feel like I write well enough for me to enjoy what I've written, and that's enough for me.

I love for people to recommend good stories, because it's hard to have to time to search. I usually base what I read off the recommendations of others and then also there are some authors I always read. I won't read self-insert stories, but anything else I'm pretty open.

If you read this much of my profile - thanks. I hope my ramblings didn't bore you to tears :)
