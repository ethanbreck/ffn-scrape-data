STILL NOT DEAD. I'M LIKE RASPUTIN BUT I SMELL BETTER

Elsewhere on the Web I go by Tensaihime, so if that should happen to pop up here someday don't be alarmed. But I like this handle, so it probably won't change any time soon.

I love funny stories and exciting stories. I come here for escapism and I'm not ashamed to say so. Most of what I write is going to be "aww" (the friendship/bro sort) or "tee-hee" (watch for 12-year-old humor). To quote Family Guy's Stewie: I love crack. I'm absolutely cuckoo for crack! And I am alllllll about a good parody. :D If i wanna be sad/gritty, I can just switch on the news or pick up a paper. But I'll read a little bit of serious work too. I guess it's a case-by-case sort of thing.

An important (well, sort of) word: Sometimes I might leave a review saying that a story or chapter depressed me. THIS IS NOT A BAD THING. This means that you are a good enough writer to reach through the screen, into my chest, grab my heart and proclaim "WHEN I SAY GET SAD YOU WILL GET SAD. CRY, DOG, CRY FOR STEWIE." It means you've got skills and I salute you.

I still like my old faves like Pokemon, DBZ, LoTR, Sailor Moon, etc., but now I've joined the most rabid group of Kirby fans EVAR (with some Mario on the side) and am having so much fun with 'em. And then came Rockman. I don't think I'll ever quit having new favorites. I don't even know, man.

Not to mention the site's crackdown on stuff in the script format -we lost a lot of crap that way, but we lost a lot of good fics too. I have mixed feelings about that. So either way some of my stuff had to go. Although some of it was actually pretty lousy... Sorry to torment you with that, fellow fans. Also watch out for the occasional typo, bad Japanese grammar or OOC moment. I try to minimize such things, but nobody's perfect; I'm trying to improve and if you feel the need to point something out, go right ahead. It's good of you to care.

Soooo... you could read the stuff I do have. Or, since I have a lot of the awful old stuff saved, if you really wanted to see it I guess you could ask me. I don't bite. If I don't respond right away it's nothing personal; I'm randomly on and off of here.

Stuff In Progress:

Darn near everything, in a way. My beloved purple thumb drive crashed for no reason, taking a lot of my fan-work and a crap-ton of chemistry work with it. Darn near broke my heart... But I'm a stubborn broad.

No, I have NOT given up on Tsurukame! -it's too much fun to quit, and that's why I've finally finished Chapter 7. Yaaay!

Although Fellowship of the Metroid is probably gone for good, if I'm really bored I might re-do it someday...

I think I'm going to write a story about the Koopalings. They're just the coolest and cutest group of evil kids. (What I'll actually write it about is a whole other story.)

And I'm working hard on the Kirby's Adventure musical. :D Currently I'm reconstructing Butter Building, due to the crash. If the first Kirby musical disappears, it's only because I reconstructed it to not have the script format (I'm paranoid like that sometimes), and it'll be back up sure as (stupid Kirby-related pun of yours here).
