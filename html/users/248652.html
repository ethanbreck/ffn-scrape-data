Hiya, (1st July 2005)

I'm Rebecca and I've been a bit of a fanfic nut for a good while, LOL, I'm 23 and have a baby girl so she is the reason I don't update much...but there was always some excuse right:) Before it was probably my degree course or something...anyway...forgive me if this is a bit garbled, it's 1:30 in the morning and I really ought to be in bed, me thinks this is the reason I am knackered all the time!

I live in fair old England,it's a beautiful place in general and I love it.

I love loads of fandoms...Alias, Buffy, Angel, Labyrinth, Smallville and the new Doctor Who which is my current madness, will probably try to write some Doctor Who fanfic soon but am gonna try to finish the Buffy ones I have going at the mo. Though have to add that 'Back to Reality' is a serious work-in-progress, if you read it you will realise why! There's loads to do on it, though I do have some clue about what will happen. I do have the ending of 'Fate takes a hand' in my head, so just as long as I can find the time (that isn't some stupid hour in the middle of the night!) it shouldn't be too too long before I've finished it! Here's hoping!

I've written numerous fics I haven't posted, mostly as they aren't finished or because they are pants and need some serious re-writing or probably deleting in some cases!

(12th August 2005)

Okay, not updated as soon as I thought on my fics (whoops sorry!), mostly because I'm co-writing a Doctor Who fanfic with Smego Baggins, called 'Will We Ever Be Again' (posted under her user) and I'm totally caught up in it! I am co-writing from chapter 6 for those interested.

I suppose it's easier to write Doctor Who fic at the mo because I LOVE it, and while I still like Buffy it's hard to like it as much as I used to when it's not current and if I don't watch the dvds religiously or something, and therefore more difficult to write Buffy fic as I'm not as into it as I used to be. That said I will try and keep writing the other fics, even if no ones reading, think I need to finish them, I like making sure I finish something I start!
