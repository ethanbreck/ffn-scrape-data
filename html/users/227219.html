Hello True Believers! Okay today brings an new dawn for my writing career as I finally attempt a group story, normally I really wouldn't branch out any further than a single main character for any of my stories but after getting really into the Teen Titans I decided that I wanted to do my own version. I'm not going to pretend its better, but I just decided there were a few things I wanted to change and to do that I had to start from the ground up. I'm going to be doing my favourite storylines from the titans so unfortunately if you're a huge Titans fan and know everything about the series then you may know whats going to happen, but I have changed a few things even at the beginning so they may not turn out as you expect...

Second order of business is to plug my work over at fictionpress.com where I write under the pen name of Ture Believer1 because some arsehole stole my original pen-name. This person, whoever they may be, has become my first hated fanfiction writer as now everyone will put my work together with theirs and it'll be all wrong. I'd also like to plug my work at Another World (my website whose link is on this page) as there's stuff on there that used to be convered by ff.net but was purged with all the other original stuff. It also has the exclusive story of the saga of Captain Britain, an award-winning story! Also my anicient story, Newcomers, has been nominated for a Barefoot award, although I have yet to recieve any news on this.

BIO: Hi everyone my name is Sam, I live in England, am 18 and have just finished school so I have lots of time to write. This is obviously good news for you, but less for me if I ever want to make any money through regular work. I welcom all criticism and whatever as I'm seriously considering becoming a writer as a career, particularly a superhero comics writer. If you want to contact me the best way is to e-mail me, but if you do not recieve a reply just give me a review on one of my stories with your comment as I may not have updated my e-mail address on this page.
