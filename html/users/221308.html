So I'm a 20-something medical student who lives two streets over from the beach but doesn't see it all that much because she spends her entire life being suffocated by a forest worth of notes and lectures. I love vodka lemon lime and bitters, reading, writing and am a self-proclaimed bibliophile. I am still trying to edit the book that I began at 14 and have just started being paid money by the government.

I have just uploaded the first chapter of Mindlessly Senseless - a reworking of my story 'Mindless Snogging' which I began four years ago during my undergraduate degree. I intend to finish this story and it will be going in exactly the same direction as Mindless Snogging, only it will be neater, more grammatically-correct, and hopefully a little more understandable. I hope you all like it as much as Mindless Snogging.
