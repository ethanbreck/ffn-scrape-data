12-27-06

Whoa, HRD. Way to procrastinate. I joined FF.net when I was 13, when I loved to write and wanted to be a writer. But now, as I'm pushing 18, I realize that creative writing isn't my passion--literary criticism is more my forte. But I won't abandon this story. This is the one piece of writing that's stuck with me through all the career and college uncertainty, the research papers, the reading logs, my two jobs. It's that one piece of music that I know how to play. As 2006 comes to a close, I'm making a resolution. I want to finish this story by December 31, 2007. One year. Over the next four days, I will be feverishly revising Ritus Luna (it won't be much, just some minor age/date adjustments, as well as the deletion of scenes I abhorr). On January 1, 2007, I will be deleting Rara Avis and Ritus Luna and reposting Chapter 1 of Ritus Luna. I will update at least once a week (seeing as how the story's already finished, I don't see how this will be much of a problem). By the time I return to school on January 8th, I hope to have at least half of Rara Avis finished. Rara Avis WILL NOT be reposted until the repost of Ritus Luna is complete.

December 31, 2007. I can do it.

Thanks to all you fans who have stuck by me and the story.

It'll be worth it, I promise.

12-29-06 ADDENDUM:

So yeah, I started going over Ritus Luna and realized that it was C-R-A-P. I mean, it's terrible. So I'm not doing a minor rewrite. I'm revamping it completely. Don't worry, the storyline is still the same but the godawful dialogue is getting a rewrite. And I made a MAJOR personality change to one of the characters, one that will be more consistent throughout both stories. And I modified the relationship between two characters. I like them so much better now! So here goes the plan: On January 1, 2007, I will still be deleting both stories. But I will not be re-posting Ritus Luna at that time. This story deserves my best effort, and that is what I intend to give it. I'm still keeping to my promise that Rara Avis will be finished by New Year's Eve of 2007. I just need to work a little harder, that's all.

When he returns, he finds that an evil King has taken the throne, and his beloved, Zelda, is dead. He begins to build his life once again, but he soon discovers that an ancient ritual could threaten to take away everything he holds dear.

Child of the Desert Rose- I love this story. It was the first I ever wrote and I love my OC’s in it. However, once I reread it, I realized how bad the writing is. I really want to rewrite it, but I just don't have the time. HOWEVER...if the muses decide not to be kind with Rara Avis, there is a SMALL chance I might take to rewriting this story, just until the Rara Avis inspiration kicks back into gear. But don't count on it.
