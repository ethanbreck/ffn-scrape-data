I love fan fiction and have finally decided to take a stab at it. I hope to post a story eventually. I have a few unfinished fics sitting on my hard drive that I hope to finish and post. I'd post what I have so far, but I have no idea if I'll get my muse back so that I can finish them. And nothing is more annoying than an unfinished fic!!

I plan to write mostly Pitch Black fic featuring Riddick/Jack and X-Men fic featuring Logan/Marie. Maybe some Draco/Ginny from Harry Potter as well. But I'm not ruling out writing other fandoms and couples. I've grown quite fond of Scott/Kitty from X-Men as well.

I've also started to get into Harry Potter fiction. Draco/Ginny are my favorite. But I also like Draco/Hermione, Ron/Hermione, Ron/Lavender, Harry/Pansy (don't know why, but it's just so off base it makes it so interesting). I also like to read Sirius/OFC fics. So if any of you out there come across some good fics, or have some of your own you'd like me to read, email me. I love to read, and I always review!!!

I'm also a big fan of smut fics (just in case you didn't figure that out already by my name). I love a good PWP as much as the next woman. Before NC-17 fics were banned, I had an excellent selection of smutfics on my Favorites list. I'm trying to find some good ones to put on there now, so any suggestions would be appreciated. There's some great stories by some great authors, it's just that there's no way to read every story on FF.net and recommend the best. I like to think of myself as the Fairy Smut Sister. I'm always recommending good smut fic to friends.

All right, I'll go now. I'm just bored on a Sunday and thought I'd add a bunch of useless crap on my bio. ;-)
