"We must never permit the voice of humanity within us to be silenced. It is man's sympathy with all creatures that first makes him truly a man.”

“Change happens by listening and then starting a dialogue with the people who are doing something you don't believe is right.”

I'm a nomad who adheres to the old adage that not all those who wander are lost. I strive to seize the day and take risks because you're not really living if you choose to stay hidden in the shadows. Stagnancy enslaves. I love to travel and do it as often as I can. Lago di Como and Firenze (aka Lake Como and Florence) have been my favourite places I've seen thus far, but the Nu'uanu Pali Lookout holds a special place in my heart because it's my own version of Van Gogh's "The Starry Night.". There's beauty in simplicity--Visions of Paradise--and I search for perfect moments and find them hiking through the woods and watching the sunset from a summit. Ever heard the sound of the waves while smelling the salty-sweet scent of the ocean and feeling the crunch of the sand beneath your feet? It is only then that you can truly say time stands still and you're holding forever in your hands.

I'll start my 1L at law school in the Fall of 2008. Although I majored in Government and am politically-active, my life's goal is to work in the field of language and cultural preservation of the Native Peoples. I believe that the extinction of a People's language and traditions are the most effective forms of genocide; by ridding a People of both, you are essentially taking away their soul and their strength. It's a genocide that not only impacts the generations to come, but also erases the history of those who have come to pass.
