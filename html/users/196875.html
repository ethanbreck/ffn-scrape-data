Author has written 4 stories for Les Miserables, Misc. Movies, and Scarlet Pimpernel.

I have been writing fan fiction since I was a teenager. I started with Dr. Who and Blake's Seven. Yeah, none of that is posted. They were written and typed without electronic assistance and I can't face them to type them in. I also write original fiction (most sf and fantasy), but I don't put much of that on the net. I started Les Mis fan fiction back in 1996 with the first chapters of Beyond the Barricade. I'm sorry that one is so infamous for not being finished. I think it's done. My fan fiction passion lately has been for The Scarlet Pimpernel.

For various reasons, I am taking down a story or two, but there will be new ones someday as well.

I won't be posting any new work here. I won't take down what is here already though. The only unfinished story will be finished on A03.
