I fell into reading fanfiction as a teenager, a gangly girl perching on my father's desk chair with a bowl of ice-cream in hand. Consequently I've spent many hours dreaming within the worlds of Sailor Moon, my original fandom, and Harry Potter, my most long-lived obsession.

Now twenty-three, I read few new stories but retain a three-inch binder filled with my favorites. Now and again I flip through the printed pages, newly appreciative of the plot devices, narrative techniques and literary elements for which the teenage reader did not know to look.

My favorite stories -- many containing strong, complex writing and other more valuable for their role in my emotional journey or their ability to make me laugh -- can be found here.
