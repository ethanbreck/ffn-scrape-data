For all my loyal followers of the story FNG:

I have a current a folder in my documents entitled W13. In that folder is 11 stories for FNG. Two of those 11 are the same story but I'm in the process right of revising it, making changes to it. I keep the original as reference material which I then delete when I'm done with that section.

That story is called Breach. It is the story of the Warehouse being stormed by heavily armed mercenaries being led by a mysterious woman. Riley and Claudia find themselves in the crosshairs and attempt to retake the Warehouse. It is now at current 3,802 words, the revised edition.

I'm also working on the double dating chapter as well and I'm sorry to report that is only at 1,567 words right. It is called Disaster Date.

My biggest document in that file is a collection of stories I started writing and it is where the first chapter spawned from. It's called FNG. And it stands at a mighty 20,372 words coming down from 33,000.

The next one down the list is a new addition called Future Perfect? I can't go into details about what happens in terms of plot but it will be a good one and it is something that the show has NEVER touched on. However, it's only 1,185 words.

After that and this is in NO order in terms of chapter structure, is Game Changer. Riley and Claudia are dispatched to search for some missing Regent personnel and find instead an artifact of mass destruction. It's 2,447 words at the moment.

And now a story that I have finished but yet to post is called Home. Riley returns home to London after his commanding officer is murdered with an artifact. This will be a big chapter in terms of Riley's history. It's 10,080 words right.

The next one down is called Love's Battlefield. This will be another AU story except it will be set during World War 2. No artifacts will be present in this one. 1,596 words.

This next one I'm particularly proud of. It's called Remember Me Not. Claudia gets her hands on an artifact that allows her to travel back in time (No, not Artie's pocketwatch!) and attempt to right a wrong but when she returns, things have taken a severe turn. 1,002 words.

A story that I just started writing called Regret. When Claudia learns of a secret Riley has, she's prepared to find it out, no matter the cost. Even if it costs her relationship with Riley.

And the last file, is called The Coming Storm. And it will be the FINAL chapter in FNG. The new character from Breach is getting ready to invade the Warehouse and the team must stop her at all costs while they relocate the Warehouse. 1,013 words.

I thought I should at least give you guys a heads up in terms of how the story is progressing.
