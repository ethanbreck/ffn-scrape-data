Hey everyone! My name is Misha (you can also call me Inigo?? Okay.). and I'm twenty-nine years old. I'm an all-around silly kind of person, but that's almost never reflected in my writing. My current obsessions are, but aren't limited to: SVTFOE, Borderlands, Fire Emblem (Awakening!), Persona (3 and 4), Otomen, Final Fantasy (VII, VII, X, XIII), Gurren Lagann, Catherine, Ace Attorney, Trauma Center, Resident Evil, Pokemon, Austin and Ally and Young Justice.
