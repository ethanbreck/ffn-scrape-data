Hello. Names Digiprotector. Anyone who has been around here for a while (at least two years or so) may know of me. I've been writing my fics for about 4+ years now, sadly they have died out due to college and work. I am trying to start up my writing again with a new fic, as well as work on finishing my older fics. I hope you will enjoy my fics and that you guys will check out some of my old ones

I am in my last year of college, so I get chapters out and posted when time allows. I am finishing up Universal Ranger and hope to get it completed and posted by the end of the year. It is looking as UR will take the place of Wish for Power as my first long running series.

First Fic: Tales of the Digiprotectors (Series)

Digiprotectors is the first fic I ever worked on and "published" to the web. It was fairly popular in its time. I actually won an award from Envision.net (Online Digmimon Fan Fic Award). It is broken up onto 3 book, with the first currently posted here. Being my first fic, its not that well written. Sadly, Digiprotectors has fallen to the wayside as I have moved onto bigger projects. I hope to rework the fic and repost it someday.

Newest Fic: Universal Rangers

Any Power Rangers fan should take a look at this. I am not going to say anything about it so I don’t ruin all my hard work, but lets just say this may not be going where you guys are thinking… or maybe it is. ;)

Universal Rangers is finally finished!!! I have finished editting all the chapters and only have two more to post. I will be spacing them out so the last is posted the week before Christmas. PLEASE take a look at it as this is one of the few complete stories I gotten posted here. If I can get enough people interested in it, I plan on expanding it into a full-blown series!!
