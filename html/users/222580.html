In short, I am 25 years old, and have been an avid reader of fanfiction for over half my life (eep!). Obviously my tastes have changed somewhat over the years ... I'm a Ph.D. candidate in art history and have a part-time job while also interning at a NYC museum, all of which leaves me quite busy, but I'm always in the mood for a good yarn! My favorite fandoms include but are not limited to Jane Austen, Harry Potter, C.S. Lewis, Batman Begins/The Dark Knight, Jane Eyre, Tin Man, Tolkien, the Mummy, Sharpe, MI-5/Spooks, Jane Eyre, Battlestar Galactica (the new version!) and Star Wars...in no particular order. Among my favorite authors are those mentioned before, as well as Virginia Woolf, A.S. Byatt, Anya Seton, Nancy Mitford...and many more. I'm currently working my way through the letters of Lady Mary Wortley Montagu. And I'm thoroughly addicted to chocolate. And Gossip Girl (shh!).

Please check out my website (link above!) for some recommendations of great fics to read! It's been a long time since I've updated but there are still lots of lovely stories to be read.
