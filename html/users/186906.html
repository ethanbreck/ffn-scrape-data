I think it's high time I edited out all the old crap in this profile and came clean with everyone here.

I'm no longer writing fanfiction. My focus is on other things now, including college and a part-time job. I still write, and in fact I still write from the perspective of characters owned by other people (including Sonic and Zelda characters), but it's not fanfiction -- it's Role-Playing. I do it as a hobby on various forums and it gives me all the writing practice I need right now.

If you're interested in RPing with me on any of the forums I attend, shoot me an email. Don't bother asking if I'll continue my stories -- I'll finish them eventually, but that time period is way far in the future right now. I've got a lot of stuff to take care of first.

Thanks for your time and understanding, guys.
