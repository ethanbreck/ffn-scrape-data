Hiyas everyone!

My names Queen Bamfie and I'm a writer (yes a real live one that does this for money as well as fun!)
I write everything and anything X-Men!

God, what do you tell people in bios? Right maybe I should start with my stories.

Stories: (Formal, yes?) I tend to focus a lot on characterisation rather than anything else. I prefer to delve into my characters minds and find out what makes them tick (God, I sound like a regular Charles Xavier, huh?). Most of all I try to make my stories as loyal to the actual characters as possible.
The reason I only have a few stories up right now is probably mainly due to the fact that I have one pasterpiece and that is Demon Eyes. I love my story and I have about 10 or so chapters to go still plus a planned sequel!
Also I have a few other half-finished first chapters here and there and also a few stories on the back burner.
I'm trying to involve the X-Men into Sept. 11th and I want to do it as tastefully and as true to the events as possible. Thus, it needs time and care. I also have two totally new stories called 'Deception' and 'Zorro X'!

My Boys: I absolutely love Wolverine, Nightcrawler and Gambit. Almost all (in fact, yes, all...) of the stories I write are about them and their realtionships the X-Men and one another. I DO write slash. I believe in writing slash that sounds plausable, however, and hardly ever do PWP's because of this. 90% of my stories involving just these three characters go past the 10 chapters stage and the three that are up just now (plus coming soon: The sequel to Demon Eyes) will be almost 20 chapters in length.

Next: (Not so formal, no?) Check out my faves list! There is sooo many good writers out there and there is just a few of them on the list. Read, read. You will enjoy.

Also: (Getting worse...) I will co-write a story with anyone that asks nicely (trust me it does not take much persuading!). I like helping out where I can but I don't usually do Interactive fics for the simple reason that I don't understand why other people do them in the first place, lol. So, to that, no.

Oh and why so many unfinished stories? Well, see above, people, see above...

Going now because bored, headache and more writing to do...

Luvs, Bamfie xx
