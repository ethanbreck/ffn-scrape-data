Wow... it's been over 6 years since I updated this. I guess at one point I had moved my stories to www.stories.com, but now I can't remember my user name I had there. So I may have lost everything there. At least I have my first chapters, outlines, and plenty of new story ideas. Too bad most of my new stories are originals.

Anyway, when reading, I hate coming across bad grammar or missed typos. I am seriously considering asking a few authors to allow me a week or so with their work so I may revise and submit it to them. When I print out fanfiction, out of habit I grab a red pen and mark as I go. I would really enjoy being able to Beta for people. Throughout college I helped my room mates with their papers, and I have a few creative writing classes. I've had experience with a great variety of work so far, from good to perfect to plain, out right trash.

Note: I have decided to work on the fanfiction I have posted so far (Eos story first) as I work on my Twilight fanfic, while doing some work on some unpublished original stuff on the side. If anyone has some plot twist ideas they'd liked worked into a Twilight fic, let me know. Mine is going to have the Bella x Edward pairing, starting off where Edward leaves Bella. I am going to have Bella be a witch or some sort of magic wielder, but Edward does NOT know about it.

FYI: The avatar is my personal drawing. I don't see why anyone would, but please don't gank it. Thanks!

Anyway, if you want to know anything, let me know.
