If you're still waiting for something to be updated, chances are I won't ever do it, though I'm pleased people care enough to keep asking.

I (occasionally) write Doctor Who fanfic over at my Livejournal: aralias.

The Original

once again i stumbled through the horror of reading my own biography and decided to change it. i would say "it doesn't matter because nobody ever reads these anyway" but this is obviously a lie because i always do so: here we go.

i'm 18, female and live somewhere vaguely in the region of south east england but even i'm not too sure where and i mainly write harry potter, marauder centric alright alright...remus/sirius fluff fics.

i don't really like AU fics.
and i really don't like remus/james, hermione/snape etc fics because they're wrong yes i know i write remus/sirius... look, the point is james loved lily not remus, got it?

on the other hand i do like: remus/sirius, james/lily, ron/hermione and i'm undecided about ginny so either harry/ginny or draco/ginny. either way plays well and we don't have enough evidence in canon to support one over the other.
i also like peter pettigrew sadly not in any romantic entanglements... poor old wormtail. i think he's fascinating and must have been a really good person because a) he got entrusted with the potters lives and b) he's a gryffindor: they just don't make them evil.
speaking of evil people... i like them too which doesn't say much for my liking peter case

please read my stories and leave reviews because it will make me extatically and stupidly happy. if you really want you can head over to my livejournal where i've got a couple of other hp/marauder related stuff kicking around that doesn't get to go here because its lame too short. equally getting emails from people i dont know makes my day so feel free to leave a note.

thanks for getting to this point.

"I had come to write about Truth, Beauty, Freedom and that which I believed in above all things: Love ... There was only one problem: I'd never been in love."
