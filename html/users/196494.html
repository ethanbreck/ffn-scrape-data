Author has written 6 stories for Sailor Moon, and Harry Potter.

I am a big anime fan. My fav animes are Fushigi Yuugi, Rurouni Kenshin, Sailor Moon and Vision of Escaflowne. Currently, I'm writing Sailor Moon fics because I'm a mushy romantic at heart. My other interests include: comic books, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, science fiction (including Star Trek) and fantasy. My penname comes from combining my two favorite princesses: Princess Amethyst of Gemworld (DC comics) and Princess Serenity of Sailor Moon. Thanks to AngelicMoonDJ for getting me to post.

2/21/07

Yes, I have not died! And I have posted the start of a new fic. I blame my hiatus on lack of inspiration, my shameful laziness and an insane obsession with reading fanfiction (for which I probably need intervention...). I now consume Harry Potter, Smallville and Buffy crossovers like starving men inhale McDonald burgers. Plus, my buddy s0hma_m0miji did not help me by drawing me ever deeper into the world of slash. While I'm no longer devoted to Sailor Moon, I do intend to finish my fics at some point. Currently, I am dragging my feet on the last scene of Farewell. XD

2/14/08

Sorry to all who follow my stories. I have not been writing much because I've been overworked and now have health issues. Luckily, I might be freer to write now that I am recovering.
