My name is Krystal, and I am a full-time college student with a Theater Arts major who still manages to spend too much time on the computer, often seeking out quality fan fiction stories to read. I live in Oregon in the grand ole' USA, and I am very content here for the time being. I have many hobbies, some including writing, acting (Ahh, theater!), singing, snow and water skiing, playing my guitar and/or violin, and reading of course. I also enjoy spending time with my family and chasing my dog.

I used to only post favorite stories of mine from the Lord of the Rings genre under this screen name (to keep things consistent), but I read from so many different fandoms that soon I am going to be posting favorite fanfiction.net stories of all different genres. I also have a few ideas of my own for some stories that I may or may not be putting up, depending on how busy I am and how much I end up liking my own work.

If you're looking for fan fiction and you love Lord of the Rings and/or the Silmarillion as I do, I highly recommend the website Henneth Annûn at I in no way lay claim to having created it, but it has some of the best stories that you'll ever read in the Tolkien fandom.

Look at me, typing up my life story when there isn't even any reason for you to be here yet unless you want to look through my favorites. Well, I apologize if you are this bored (as I obviously am). ;)

To make a long story short:
I have no stories posted (yet), but under favorites you will find some stories by other people that in my opinion are *darn* good reads.

Thanks for reading, and God Bless. :)
