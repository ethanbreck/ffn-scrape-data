Author has written 13 stories for Sailor Moon.

I've been writing Sailor Moon fics (some better than others, I'm afraid) since high school...and here I am, ten years later and still obsessed. Like any self-respecting lady in her late twenties, OBVIOUSLY. A quick look at my stories will reveal that I have a particular devotion to Senshi/Shitennou pairings, and especially to Rei/Jadeite fics. What can I say, bitchy brunettes and golden boys are my weakness.

My writing has been on a rather extended hiatus since 2005 - college, grad school, real life, you know the drill - but I'm happy to be posting fics again.

There's earlier work on here (pretty much anything published before 2011, i.e. Twilight Bastille, Seduction, etc.) that I have to admit is probably both massively cheesy and mildly embarrassing, but I'd like to keep everything uploaded nonetheless so you can all laugh at my high school endeavors :P

Love reviews, comments, criticisms, whatever. Make my day - and enjoy :D
