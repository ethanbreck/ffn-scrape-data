Author has written 2 stories for Animorphs, and Sonic the Hedgehog.

Welp! While I like reading, I apparently forgot that I even made a story back ... 12 years ago almost! An here I thought I hadn't written one. That tells you about my memory. I do enjoy writing but I was always afraid that my lack of grammar, point of view and such were too terrible to even consider posting anything. I am trying to remedy that and improve by submitting myself to the butchers bloc... I mean the viewers and readers here!

I am starting from scratch as I am clueless of what I am doing here. So any pointers would be greatly appreciated!

I do have E-mail, Aim and Skype, So if you ever want to chat just send a email first and I'll get back to you.

E-mail frostdrgn@gmail.com

Alittle addition about myself. Some of the Fanfictions I enjoy are; Sonic the Hedgehog, Ushio to Tora, X-Men, SwatKats and others.

Some of my favorite characters include; Tora, a tiger demon from the Manga Ushio to Tora. Sonic, Scourge, Knuckles and Shadow, Nightcrawler, Sabertooth, Razor, Rapheal, the list goes on.

Now granted with Archies little thing with a previous writer. I became uncertain as to whether or not I should continue using characters that are no longer with the series. Finally I decided. Welp why not. I'm not taking credit for them, not being paid anything, and if there is ever a problem just take it down. So here we go!

I do love talking and meeting new people, ESPECIALLY if they are fellow writers or just people that share the same interests. So feel free to hit me up.
