Welcome...

I've been writing fics for quite some time, because I felt that the movie/cartoon/anime I saw did not satisfy me or I just wanted to add a little something to it. Brace yourself for romance, angst, and those rare moments of humor when my funny bone gets inspired. One day, I'd like to come up with someone on my own but until then I'll just do what my muses tell me to do.

I hope that you all enjoy what I've got out there and please pardon the grammatical and spelling errors. My fingers aren't fast enough for my brain and proofreading is a skill I haven't yet mastered.

Since I didn't put disclaimers on anything: I own none of these characters. I simply enjoy playing puppetmaster once and a while. If you see roman numerals or some other form of listing next to fics in the same series, please read them in that order. You won't regret it! (I think)
