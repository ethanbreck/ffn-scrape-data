Author has written 11 stories for Warcraft, Final Fantasy X, StarCraft, and Final Fantasy VII.

Thought I should write something creative and insightful in my bio, but ironically that also happens to be why it’s been left blank for years.

But meh, what the heck. My name is Jen. I write. I want to publish my books and my paintings. And I want to become an editor.

For those who know me, I’ve been away from FFN for quite a while due to real life concerns. Now I’m back and the crowd goes wild. Yeah, urm, anyway, for those who don't know me, hello to you.

Some of the stuff that has my maniacal love:

George R.R. Martin; Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, Warcraft, Starcraft, Final Fantasy VII, Crisis Core: FFVII, Final Fantasy XII, Anne Rice, and Professor Snape in the Harry Potter series.

Other things that you should know about my fics—first of all, I write fanfiction, which means I have the right to diverge from canon. And yeah, that actually means I'm not sticking to canon down to the last letter.

If you read more than one of my stories, you’ll probably notice they're generally depressive...a bit, yeah. That doesn't mean I am a depressed person. I'm actually pretty darn funny in real life (at least I pretend to be).

I avoid IM like the plague but I have an email: jenconcept@yahoo.ca (wow, at least she's not from the Stone Age). Feel free to chat.

As for the frequently asked questions of when Blood Leprechaun or Listen will ever be finished...let's just say, sorry, I honestly don't know.

Short-term goal is to get my Crisis Core fic posted.

Long-term goal would be to get my two KotOR fics editted and posted as well. And of course, to write a witty author's bio.
