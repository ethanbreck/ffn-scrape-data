Update Again.

I am having issues updating so it seems. @_@. I guess having a breakdown does that. Anyways, I have two parodies planned at the moment. One involves zombies and the other involves every Harrison Ford movie ever made. Yes, you read those right.

So I have finally completed my history degree and now working on a secondary education degree. Thus my plan to corrupt the youth continues...

Well, a list of favorites/recommendations:

Manga: Saiyuki (everyone must read it), Gravitation, Angel Sanctuary, Yami No Matsuei, Suikoden III, Weiss.

Books/Literature: The Three Kingdoms (do read the unabridged), Lord of the Rings (and anything by Tolkien), The Vampire Chronicals by Anne Rice, Dante's The Divine Comedy, Bram Stoker's Dracula, Mary Shelly's Frankenstein, Alexander Dumas' The Three Musketeers and The Count of Monte Cristo, and Edgar Allen Poe's short stories (they are really all good), and lastly (for now, I suppose) Mark Twain's Huck Finn and Tom Sawyer.

TV Series/Anime/Movies: Saiyuki, Weiss Kreuz, Gravitation, Kyoto Arc of Rurouni Kenshin, Vision of Escaflowne, Trigun, LOTR, Gundam Wing, Kill Bill Vol 1 ( I didn't like two that much), Revolutionary Girl Utena, Gankutsuou, Alien Resurrection (I have no idea why), Lethal Weapons, Die Hard movies.

Games: Suikoden series, Final Fantasy VII, Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy X and X-2, Lunar series, Breath of Fire IV, Chrono Cross and Trigger, Zone of the Enders series, Kingdom Hearts I and II. Guilty Gear should be here. There are probably more, but I can't think right now.

One of my most favorite moments in all of literature:

"Since I cannot be your wife in this world," she said, "I want to arrange to meet you in the next."

"If I cannot have you as my wife in this world," answered Lu Bu, "Then I am no hero worthy of the name."
