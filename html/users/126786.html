I am a Mowi. That is all you ever need know.

..Well, *need* and *want* are two different things, although why anyone'd want to know about me at this point is beyond me. But in any event, here's a lil bio:

I am now eighteen, and will remain thus till next August. I'm a hermit, a recluse, and an extremely opinionated, open-minded, obsessive, sometimes depressive, candy-cane-loving, strong-willed, stubborn, exceedingly brilliant (and oh-so-modest) writer who draws on the side, has trouble with run-on sentances, and has sudden bursts of creative genius that astound, amaze, enthrall, and captivate people only to dry up and wither a moment later leaving only the ashes wrought by a temporary brilliance so bright and overzealous that it momentarily outshone the sun and all at once spontainiously combusted.

..Aaah, shit, I did it again.

You probably won't see many stories up here -- Although I love fanfiction, I sometimes have trouble getting over the feeling that I shouldn't be writing it. The characters aren't mine to write about, and therefore I think it's impossible to truly do them justice. Inspiration sometimes overcomes this feeling, however, aided by the fact that once something gets in my head, it won't leave me alone until I write it down..

Probably the thing I'd love most is to be able to write consistantly. And I said "consistantly", not "better" or "well" -- I don't doubt my ability to write. I simply am well aware of the fact that I currently lack the skills to think up elaborate storylines, and I definately lack the attention span to write about them. x_X

Such is why my Inuyasha story is on hold, and my LotR story, um.. sucks. Considering just taking it down.. but, I do apologize for the Inuyasha story. Maybe someday I'll finish it.
