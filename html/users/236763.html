Author has written 14 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and Angel.

I found the BtVS late in the series but casual interest quickly grew to regular viewership. In the fall of 2001, obsession erupted when Sarah Michelle Gellar sang her first line in "Once More with Feeling" 6x07. ( I'm a sucker for a showtune! And the fact that "the curtain closed on a kiss..." :sighs:)

Following the season 6 finale, desperate for some new connection to the Buffyverse, I discovered fanfiction. That summer I penned my first series.

Through fanfiction, I found discussion groups. Life hasn't been the same since!
