Author has written 8 stories for Matrix, X-Files, Star Wars, Batman Begins/Dark Knight, Bourne series, and Terminator: Sarah Connor Chronicles.

I tend to like epics, but mostly for the details. I know that sounds contradictory, but I also tend to like paradoxes.

As a writer, I live by the immortal words of G.K. Chesterton: "A good novel tells us the truth about its hero, but a bad novel tells us the truth about its author."

My favorite male character in the known universe is Malcolm Reynolds (Firefly), and my favorite female character in the known universe is Aeryn Sun (Farscape).

Fandoms: Terminator (1, 2, and SCC), Star Wars, The X-Files, Batman Begins/The Dark Knight, The Matrix Trilogy, Firefly/Serenity, Uncharted, The Bourne Trilogy, Daredevil (comicverse), Farscape, Buffy, Dark Angel, Prince of Persia – and those are just the heavy-hitters.
