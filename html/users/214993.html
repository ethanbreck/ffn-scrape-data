Hi, I'm Sarah. Pretty much all you need to know about me is that I'm 19 and a student studying BA Creative Writing with Film Studies at University.

I have a fictionpress account of the same username, and I'm an admin at FictionNet along with several other lovely writers. The forum is primarily based around the Harry Potter fandom, although we offer a varied range of writing and blending challenges which allow original fiction and alternative fandoms to be entered in challenges. If you look at my site link, you'll find the address for FictionNet.

On the subject of fandoms, I write:

Harry Potter

X-Men: The Movie

Dead Like Me

Casanova (the BBC adaptation, starring David Tennant and Peter O'Toole as Casanova)

Tekken

and am considering branching into several others, such as Buffy, Angel, Firefly/Serenity (can't you just tell I love Joss Whedon?) and Doctor Who.

And in terms of ships... well, I love slash and I love het. So I'll read anything, but my favourites are:

Harry Potter: Remus/Sirius; Remus/Tonks; Draco/Ginny; Draco/Hermione; Tom/Ginny although I'm pretty easy-going with any ship as long as Ron isn't involved. My soul feels that ickle Ronniekins should be asexual, regardless of canon and fandom alike.

X-Men: Pyro/Iceman; Pyro/Magneto; Pyro/Logan; Pyro/Rogue; Pyro/Mystique;Logan/Jean; Storm/Nightcrawler. And yes, I am rather fond of Pyro.

Dead Like Me: Mason/George; Mason/Daisy; but both Mason and Roxie are delicious characters.

Casanova: Casanova/Henriette; Casanova/Rocco

Tekken: Jin/Xiaoyu; Jin/Julia; Jin/Hwoarang; Hwoarang/Julia. But Lee also floats my Tekken inflatable boat.

Buffy: Willow/Oz; Willow/Tara; Andrew/Xander; Xander/Anya; Spike/Buffy

Angel: Angel/Spike; Wesley/Fred; Wesley/Illyria; Illyria/Spike

Firefly: Zoe/Wash; Mal/Inara; Kaylee/Simon

Doctor Who (New Series): Doctor/Rose; Rose/Jack; Doctor/Jack/Rose

I'll read anything that sounds interesting from another fandom though, and I don't flame. Any criticism I offer is purely constructive.
