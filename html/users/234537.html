Some random stuff about me:

Favorite movies: Lord of the Rings, Good Will Hunting, RENT, Spider-man, The Butterfly Effect, Secret Window, Happy Feet, The Faculty. (most of the stuff with my favorite people in it, I've seen)

Favorite Bands: The Fray, Dashboard Confessional, Taking Back Sunday, Goo Goo Dolls, Switchfoot, Oasis, Snow Patrol, The All-American Rejects, and Eminem.

Favorite Books: Harry Potter, The Kite Runner, The Outsiders, Running With Scissors, Speak, My Sister's Keeper, Slaughterhouse-Five.

General people I just adore: Elijah Wood, Johnny Depp, Tobey Maguire, Dominic Monaghan, Robin Williams, Orlando Bloom, James Franco, Patrick Swayze.

Fictional Characters that are amazing: Harry Potter, Frodo Baggins, Ponyboy Curtis, Spider-man, Sirius Black, Roger Davis, Shawn Hunter, Joey Tribbiani, Casey Connor, Sam Gamgee, Will Hunting, Harry Osborn, Captain Jack Sparrow, Johnny Cade, Mark Cohen, Norrington, Sean Maguire.

I love guys. Guys in stories. Almost exclusively. It's weird, I know. But I hate Snape fics. I hate love/hate relationships. (Funny, that) I don't like Malfoy. I can only stand slash when the main point of the story is the awkward tension leading up to it. I love not-quite-slash-but-really-eerily-close-to-it. I don't like grammar or spelling mistakes but I make a lot of them. Reading my own stuff makes me nauseous. Reviews are nice but sometimes bitter-sweet when it comes to old fics. My favorite stories are the least popular ones. I cringe when I think about the popularity that The Price of Love got. I want to branch out into Rent fanfiction but I'm afraid of starting another story I don't plan to finish. The same goes for The Outsiders.

As far as old stories are concerned, they are just that. Old. Don't start any of them except Loss unless you want to be disapointed. And Loss sucks. I toy with the idea of redoing it. I've gotten the first two and a half chapters written.

If anyone knows any good fics in the areas of Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Rent, or The Outsiders give me a shout, cause I'm really searching hard. I love male bonding fics. Those are my favorites. You can kinda get the gist from my favorites list. And check out Sailoranime's fics, cause she's my best friend!
