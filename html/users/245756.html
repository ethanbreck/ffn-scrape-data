Author has written 24 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Lord of the Rings, Naruto, Death Note, and Legend of Zelda.

Most of my stories are just single chapters; 'one-shots', if you will. That's because whenever I try to write something longer it gets well into the story - where cliffhangers would kill people - and then I get tired of writing it. Also, the stories are little snippets of how I'm feeling that day, or sudden inspirations; those can't really be drawn out into more than single chapters. So don't expect second chapters to most of these, especially if they say 'completed', but if I get enough reviews on something requesting a sequel or some such nonsense I might try for it.

Most of these stories can also be found on my DeviantArt account( ), as well as art by myself or friends of mine. I'll try to put links to the pictures in the story.

I moved Storm from here to my recently-created AdultFanFiction account( ). My only regrets in doing so are losing two AWESOME reviews, one for and one against the story. Both were very well-written and had good points.

also posted another story on my AFF account; some wonderfully cracktastic KakaGaiLee smut.
