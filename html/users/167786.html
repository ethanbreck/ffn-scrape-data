"Insane people are always sure that they are fine. It is only the sane people who are willing to admit that they are crazy."

-- Nora Ephron (screenwriter and director)

Hello, and welcome to my little abode. I'm a recent return to the world of fanfiction, as you might be able to tell from my semi-rusty stories. They flake at the corners and it may cover you as you leave.

Just a warning.

My name is Emma and I'm a 21 year old student (who still lives at home with her parents, whee). I'm in my second year of studying primary teaching -cough-the ways of corrupting young children-cough-. -wide grin- I kid, I kid. A little. Maybe. Perhaps. We shall have to see. I live next to down under - a little country called New Zealand in a city called Christchurch. Apparently it's the biggest city on the South Island, but I don't think it's the best one. I like small towns. :)

I have a thing for many, many things. I like collecting socks, I love penguins -- we have this place in Christchurch called the Antarctic Centre and I was able to go backstage to be less than a foot away from one-two-three of them. I squee'd so much and so loudly that I think I frightened the poor buggers. I speak English and German and am really keen to learn other languages. I have a brother and a sister; both of whom are younger (and taller -weeps-) than me. I enjoy writing (obviously), good movies and books, catching up with friends, dancing in the rain, laying in my bed when it's raining really hard outside... and many, many more things.

My current TV obsessions are Glee, Grey's Anatomy, Gossip Girl, One Tree Hill and The Big Bang Theory.

My favourite authors include Tamora Pierce (obviously), Trudi Canavan and Garth Nix.
