This blog used to be owned by five people: Dallas, Nate, Jake, Celeste, and me, Rachel. Now, it's down to just me. Everyone just decided that they weren't cut out for writting, and dropped out, dumping everything on me. Brilliant. Anyhow. To give credit, "Priceless treasures" was written by Jake with a bit of help from me, and "Rubies and Opals" was originally co-written between all of us as a Round Robin, but it turned into me writing it all. Yes, I do sense a conspiracy. Anyway. This account's come a long way since I first started writing... in March '02. So I'm going to keep it up.

edit: Okay. The last time I posted a fic here was when I was fourteen. That was in '03. (You can do the Math.) My writing's changed and matured by a lot over the years and because I'm rather embarrassed of these older ones I decided to move accounts. You can find my more recent fanfiction over here.
