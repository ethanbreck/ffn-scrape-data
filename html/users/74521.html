Author has written 14 stories for Ever After, Harry Potter, and Doctor Who.

Dry humour, satire, sarcasm and moments of irony in a story make my day.

My favourite characters are often the underdog, or the servant, or the misunderstood-villain-with-a-kind-heart. So, books, movies and tv shows with intriguing 'minor' characters, ironic humour and fantasy/sf settings really appeal to me. And I prefer fanfiction that explores unconventional pairings or unusual situations or takes a different approach to the characters.

Most of the stories I write could be classed as fluffy sarcastic comedy. Hopefully there's something in them to make you smile.

cheers
Kazza

P.S. I also like any stories that suggest or hint or say that certain events did not occur in the shrieking shack in "Deathly Hallows". You'll find my favourite "Post DH Snape Fics" in my 'C2' Community

P.P.S. I used British/Australian spelling, so you may find a few words that look a bit 'wrong' to USA readers like humour, favour, jewellery, etc. They aren't wrong, just spelt as per a different dictionary

About some of my fics

Wizard's Honour - Comedy romance. Severus Snape and Aurora Sinistra find themselves facing an arranged marriage thanks to a medieval wizarding law and two elderly relatives. The ghosts of Hogwarts decide to take sides, some helping and some trying to stop the wedding.
This fic was written before 'The Half-Blood Prince' was published and before JKR gave us Sinistra's first name. So it's now very alternative universe.

Crystal - Fluffy drama. Percy Weasley has been estranged from his family for a few years when he returns in the middle of the night, bringing someone called Crystal with him.
This fic was also written before 'The Half-Blood Prince' was published. Very AU

GA Princely Plan - Dramatic comedy. Medusa fromWizard's Honour returns as Severus's great-granny and only surviving relative. What happens when a slightly senile lady decides to rescue her great-grandson and takes him to her retirement village? Follows canon, but ignores some parts of the DH epilogue.
