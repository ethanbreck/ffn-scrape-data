Hi. I'm me. Who are you? Are you you? It's good if you are you. I like yous who are yous, because I'm a me. Have I confused you yet? Good.

I have crazy friends which is good because I'm crazy too. Random thoughts:
Ben has a giraffe under his bed named Roger.
Terri loves Talon.
Boatman is "far far" too good.
SHA lives on! Wow, that last one was from a long time ago. I'm rambling. Trust me, I'm not dangerous. I'm not, really.

I'm always writing but not everything I write is worth posting. I just finished my CSI story and am considering writing a follow-up, but it took me 12 years to write the first one and I don't want to commit until I'm sure I can complete it within a more reasonable time line. I try not to post anything I'm not happy with, and I won't force an idea just to finish a story. Anyway, I'm trying to be a bit more disciplined with my writing so hopefully I'll have something new posted soon. In the meantime, I hope you enjoy what's here.
