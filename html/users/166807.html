I'm from the UK, currently studying games design, 3rd Year. My ultimate goal is to one day work for Nintendo, but it'll probably be an unrealised dream. But to be a games designer to any length is a great goal too! Its a fun industry. I'm a sufferer of Multiple Sclerosis (Go gooooogle) but I'm not letting that stop me do things. I just tend to run around with a walking stick.

In my sections of interest, I mainly float around the EarthBound section, because you can get a lot of deep and meaningful stories from it, because so much lies under the surface in each game, and unlike so many of the other sections, you're floating in gems rather than fighting to find one.
Ouran's just funny. Its not your common romance, and it's no Love Hina. Its better. Made so much more so as the heroine isn't even paying attention half the time. Kyouya is scary! The twins are great.
Naruto... Well I'm actually curious to see if anyone will actually make 'fics for the neglected characters like Udon. ;) That should be fun. I've been keeping up with the manga series. Shikamaru's proved amazing, but things are going a bit... annoying at the moment, as far as I'm concerned.
When it comes to Mario and Zelda, I really have to be in the mood to 'fic for them, or think of any such creations for them. As of October 07 I've clicked into Mario mode, so we'll see where that takes me. ;p Still strong halfway through November, so something's going right.
