Story Updates

Vagrants Rhapsody - (November 24, 10:10 PM) Hello! Wow! I didn't think anybody'd be checking this (assuming your actually reading these words.) Well, I bet you're wondering what the heck is going on with VR. Don't worry, I haven't given up on it. But my interest has trailed onto another project--one that's demanding most of my artistic energy. If you click on my homepage link, you'll be sent to my online web novel, which is another shoujo-ai type story I'm working on. It's an original work, and if you guys liked VR I'm sure many of you will also enjoy "Eikasia". I don't know when I'll update VR again, but my mind does travel to it now and again, so don't lose hope. Sometimes this just happens.

Fandoms I'm Eyeing/Possible Future Stories:

Final Fantasy X-2: Yes! I've been meaning to write a Paine/Rikku story even before Vagrants Rhapsody! It doesn't have a title yet, but I started outlining it ages ago. I have yet to come up with a resolution for it, but I think the main thing keeping me from writing this is the fact that I'm sure the idea the plot is centered around has been done to death...

Neon Genesis Evangelion: I'm still looking to do this one. A Shinji/Asuka story that is meant to 'answer' at least ONE of the many questions left by Hideaki Anno's film, "The End of Evangelion." My outline has a solid beginning, and I've churned out a few pages all ready...but two things hold me back: first, the lack of any sort of conclusion to the story (which I always at least have an idea of when I start writing,) and second, the suffocating angst...Evangelion was a sad story with a plethora of tragic characters, the saddest pair being the focal point of my intended fanfiction. I'm not sure I want to deal with something so depressing at the moment--thus, my wariness.

Strawberry Panic!: I have recently finished watching this amazing anime. I wasn't aware that there was another shoujo-ai as good (if not, better,) than Maria-sama ga Miteru. I wouldn't mind doing some short stories or one shots for this show. I think Tamao-chan, at any rate, deserves some SERIOUS lovin'. Poor girl...Nagisa was so DENSE!

Bleach: I don't care how much the manga and show are falling apart, I STILL love the characters. That's why I hope to do some humorous one-shots between them. Tatsuki, Orihime, Keigo, Ichigo, Hitsugaya, Soi Fong, and Yoruichi look to be the characters that I'll probably end up tormenting in the near-future. (moo-ha-ha-ha)

If you wanna see what else is distracting me from my writing, check my xanga at the link above.
