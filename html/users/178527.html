Aloha!

I'm Jeff, better known here on Fanfiction.net as the writer ProDG. I love to write stories, especially in the Lilo and Stitch section, but don't get a lot of time to do much writing anymore. My fics so far have included a Rocko's Modern Life one-shot and a Lilo and Stitch story that I WILL finish. I've also become a fan of the "Stitch!" anime series and have written a one shot based on the show

I like to try to add characters to my stories based off of my real life friends, or places from or around my hometown or events that have actually happened.

I come from Eugene, Oregon, a little town in the pacific northwest.

Besides writing I also like drawing and music and going to any and/or all local concerts I can. I listen to hip hop, rock, classic rock, 90's alternative and some punk.

Inspiration for my work comes from Chris Sanders, of course, who along with Dean DeBlois helped to create the Lilo and Stitch universe. Also the voice over artists who brought life to the unforgettable characters of said film, and Elvis Presley who's classic tunes can be heard on the soundtrack.

Other inspiration I can credit to the music/songs that I listen to, family and friends, Neon Genesis Evangelion series and any other anime with dark and disturbing subject matter, ADD/ADHD, Franz Kafka, the Silent Hill game series, ATHF, Metalocalypse, Dean Koontz, Brian K. Vaughan's Marvel Runaways comics, my fellow fanfiction.net authors MisGelRcy, Avatarjk137, Alaer Kino, and Kenjaje, Kihei Maui, DeviantArt.com and some of my favorite artists from there ShaloneSK, creator of the very awesome Spyro comic "The Legend of Spyro: Zonoya's Revenge, Culpeo Fox, Isis Masshiro, and others.
