BASICS

I first discovered FanFiction way back in the day when the site allowed Real Person Fics and I stumbled onto the U2 page. I was 14 and completely weirded-out by the NSFW content and incredibly confused by slash. That didn't last long. FanFiction thoroughly and irrevocably corrupted me in no time at all.

My first story was a fun, romance-free romp in Las Vegas with U2. Unfortunately, the entire music section of the site was deleted almost immediately after I finished it, so my 9th grade writing skills are lost to the abyss. In 10th grade, I wrote a story for Pirates of the Caribbean. Though I was beginning to find myself drawn to slash fics, I was not yet ready to pen my own. However, in retrospect, the nature of that story was most certainly a precursor of kinks to come.
