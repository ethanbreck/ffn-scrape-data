Author has written 8 stories for Pitch Black / Riddick, and Ninja Turtles.

Apparently I'm not dead or anything, and I haven't given up on writing. I've just been... busy? Gonna go with busy. Anyway, I am back and ready to crank out some new material. I will need to get back into the swing of things, so excuse me while I muddle through a few awful pieces in an effort to get my groove back. Love y'all for tolerating me!!

Updates: I'm gonna post some Black Panther stuff and get back into TMNT. The new series (Rise of the TMNT) has me feeling some kind of way anytime I watch it, so I am going to just pretend that it does not exist. Please don't ask me about writing anything for it.

Discontinued (for now, maybe):

'Obsession'... There's a screw or two loose in Raphael's head. Splinter is all too aware that Raph's unraveling and doesn't know how to help his son. Enter April O'Neil. When April comes across Raph's sai after her mugging, Raph is bound and determined to get it back. Unfortunately Raph's obsession with regaining his sai turns into an obsession with April that Raphael doesn't know how to handle. His thoughts get darker, more twisted. His desires become more apparent, and he struggles to maintain the carefully crafted facade of sanity he shows in front of his brothers. When Raphael begins to suspect that Donatello realizes what he's actually capable of, his facade finally cracks, endangering not only the well-being of April but of his family as well. This is rated M for a reason. Contains disturbing commentary, swearing, violence, voyeurism, fondling of an unconscious female, masturbation, rape, and character death (eventually).

'You Know Who'... I've always wondered how in the world a big goon like Casey could end up with someone as sharp as April (I know, I know, she doesn't seem so sharp when she's needing to be rescued every five mins in the '87 cartoon series, but still). So, I decided Casey had to go. But it was which Turtle that would take his place that interested me. Raphael is the obvious choice given his fawning over April in the films. Donatello's over zealous crush on a young Miss O'Neil in the '12 cartoon series puts him in an interesting position as well. And Leonardo... oh Leonardo, ever so stuffy and uptight might just need a little distraction from his Fearless Leader shtick. And I just thought it'd be fun to have Mikey playing detective because I could get away with goofy detective cliches coming out of Mikey's mouth. Either way, this one will be finished before summer hits!

'Conquest'... I know exactly where I'm going with this. I haven't given up. I've just not had the time in oh, seven years or so, to get back to this. Lame excuse. But this one, you can expect to see more of... And Indiana Jones meets Star Wars really was the best way to sum this up. Wonder if I could get George Lucas on board for the sequel...?

You want to know about me? Ha! Brace yourself, people, this could get ugly...

I be's a college graduate... I is a college graduate... I'll get that grammar thing down one day.

On a serious note, I double majored in college in Mass Communications and English, though the fics that I have up circa 2005 really do not show that I understand the English language in slightest bit (and the fics I upload in the future may not either). I have spent most of my early twenties traveling the world, and further familiarizing myself with other cultures and foods. Food makes me nearly as happy as writing, though I can take a long break from writing and not be as severely affected. Also, I am planning to obtain a doctorate's degree sometime before I reach my 40's, cause after that what am I really supposed to do with it?

I'm in the process of writing my first book. Anyone interested in beta'ing would be appreciated because, throughout all of this, I'm still aware that my writing is highly flawed and often times a big pile of poo. I need someone to sort through the poo for me. To really get down in there, squish it through their fingers and find that little gem that I swallowed a few days.
