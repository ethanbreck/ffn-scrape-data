Author has written 9 stories for DC Superheroes, Supergirl, and Marvel.

Creator of the Original Otherverse and webmaster for the Continuum Worlds and creator of the Continuum Worlds Universe: Multiverse.

It should be noted that the Otherverse and its current incarnation, the Continuum Worlds, are the playgrounds for other writers as well, each having our own characters and story lines. When posting here on FF.net, I will only be posting stories I personally have written or that were written as part of my story lines by guest writers. I will not be posting stories written by the other writers of the Otherverse/Continuum Worlds. Some of these stories may have impacted my story lines or be referenced in my stories. You will be able to read these stories by visiting the Continuum Worlds .

For the past ten years, I have slowed down writing Fan-fiction and am concentrating a bit more on Original fiction. I will post Original Fiction and continue to post Fan-Fiction on my new site: Clay Clearbrook's Voyages of Imagination.
