Voices are heard and she stops, listening for familality when it comes, she ducks under a grove of bushes. Two men ride in on war horses; although you dont know them, recagnzation is plastered all over the face of the young girl. Questions run over her dealicate features. The two men start talking and even though your far away you catch glimpse of phrases "...died trying to protect..." "...caught the others..." "...missing..." "...afraid caught in one of the raids..." The girl smiles, and you relieze that its her their looking for. "...princesses...captured..." "...protecting crystal..." You dont understand whats going on, or what their talking about but suddenly theres a burst of light; tree branches scattering bright rays of pearly white. When the light disappeared you look to see that the girl disappeared. Spotting her on the side of a tree you see her looking into the night sky. Fallowing her gaze, you notice that the men on the ground are also looking up into the sky, you see a large white dot the size of the moon (...think about how big the moon is lookin up 2 it at night...) a smaller white dot was right under it and you see that this is where the light is coming from; both of them floating in the night sky. There was another burst of light and the two specks disappeared. Murmers come from the clearing where the men and soilders wait. You look over to the girl in the tree and you notice a golden shape on her forehead that wasnt there before and a dim light hovering above her hand...
