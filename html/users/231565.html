Hello everyone!

I'm back! :)

Life is always interesting and surprising. When I first joined the FanFiction site, I was a young teenager driven by a passion for writing. I would lose myself in the characters and the stories.

Now, I am in my young adult life, married and with a lot of new decisions and endeavors in my future. I'd like to think I found that Jadeite whom I always longed for. The reality is so much better than my fantasy. :)

I was also inspired by the many wonderful stories and talented writers on this site. Below in my "Favorites Section," you'll find many great stories and authors whom I return to year after year. Some stories have sadly been left incomplete, allowing the reader to envision his or her version of the end. Others have continued on with fantastic style and the "wow" factor. I don't think any will disappoint a curious reader.

Also- I recently discovered (for myself) the cool feature that allows you to see where in the world your readership comes from. I was pleasantly surprised to see that readers in countries like Spain (I dream of going there someday) and Pakistan have accessed my story. What a wonderful world it is when you can interact with so many different people!

Anyway, please feel free to drop me a line. I try to answer review questions in the author's notes (located in story chapters), so if you asked a question, look for the answer there.

As always,

Happy Reading!
