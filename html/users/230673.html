Author has written 13 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Tales of Symphonia, Ronin Warriors, Final Fantasy VII, Wolf's Rain, StarCraft, .hack/SIGN, Zootopia, Armello, Tales of Vesperia, and Sword Art Online/ソードアート・オンライン.

Born in 1986, to an American military family in Madrid, Spain.

Gifted with incredible amounts of raw creative energy but with no talent for sketching or painting, I took to the only art-form that I could pursue, writing. Admittedly, my writing skills have improved over the years but they still need refinement. I am in no way a master of my craft but it is a craft that I believe myself to be talented in and one I am passionate about. My style of writing can be a bit lazy or rushed at times, please don't be afraid to give constructive criticism. I take all constructive criticism seriously on my never-ending quest to better myself in my art-form. Take note though, I write largely on impulse and my stories take their directions from however my gut tells me they should go.

Upon making requests or suggestions to me, bear in mind, writing and gaming are my escapes from reality. How I tone out and forget about the stresses of my day. I will therefor not be taking up any political stances, nor will I be identifying my political alignments here. If you're still here, I do not write gore or horror and due to sour experience with a couple games from my early teenage years, I tend to avoid character deaths.
