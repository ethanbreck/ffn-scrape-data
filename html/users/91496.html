Author has written 13 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, Sliders, Remember the Titans, StarTrek: Enterprise, Firefly, Harry Potter, Boston Legal, Sherlock, and Castle.

"Anything is possible in an infinite universe even survival. Strange but true." - A quote from Restaurant at the End of The Universe by Douglas Adams which I live by.

What can I say, I am a newlywed (relatively), so not much free time to write, etc. Most of what little free time I have is spent working on our house, watching TV, reading (both fiction and non-fiction), trying out a new computer program, or working out. For at least part of the year I'm involved with the German American Society, which get's me out amongst the living on a semi-regular basis, and has taught me how to party well into the wee hours of the night (well sorta ...). I've also made some really great friends there. I have a very broad field of interests, none of which are very stereo-typical for my age (or any other social group really).

I love stories, pure and simple. I love the tale they tell and the people you meet (both literally and metaphorically). I don't get to write as often as I'd like, and rarely get to finish something before life gets in the way. But I will always enjoy becoming entangled in stories, whether they are my own or a kindred spirit's.
