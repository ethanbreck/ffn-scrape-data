My former screen name is Cid62, and I've just changed it to the one I use on AO3; hence, you will hear me call my fics the "Cid62-niverse," which still applies. Otherwise, I'm still here. I've been on and off of this site since 2002.

IN-PROGRESS:

The Ludovico Technique makes reference to events in "Love For Tender" but is in a different part of the cid62-niverse and a Hermione/Dolohov pairing. The title is from "A Clockwork Orange."

COMPLETE:

Godless takes its title and chapter headings from the Dandy Warhols song of the same name and was originally a one-shot but now...isn't. That happens. It's a Yaxmione. (COMPLETE)

Love for Tender is a Marriage Law fic because I find the premise amusing (I recall reading the WIKTT group back in the day). In my version of the Law, she is ultimately paired with Draco and Corban Yaxley, so if that isn't your bag, Run Away! (COMPLETE)

Nothing Else Matters was begun due to locating some awesome folks on FB (the Death Eater Express). Readers of my original trilogy will recognize some of the characters in this long, convoluted ride through postwar Wizarding Britain; it's a Hermione/Walden Macnair pairing with a bit of Lucius on the side. (COMPLETE)

Dark Redemption (trilogy) was formerly an AU story arc, but I've updated it to be compliant with the entire book series, and it is now complete. (Apologies to those who I kept waiting for a couple of years, but that pesky Real Life intervened.) It is a Slyther/DE-centric story arc, featuring Severus Snape, Lucius Malfoy, Walden Macnair and a host of OCs from Salem, Massachusetts. Lots of other HP characters have cameos. The story order is: Dark Redemption, Date with an Executioner, Spell-Less in Seattle. And of course, reviews are very, very welcome.

(If you read the original trilogy all those years ago, you will note that there is a bit of content missing from "Dark Redemption"--I like to call it the "50 Shades of Severus" sections. It's still around. If you want to read it, I can arrange that via email.)

ON HIATUS:

The Lucius/Hermione story, The Road Less Travelled, is on a semi-permanent hiatus, which means I may or may not get back to it someday. (Note: it does have a bit of continuity with the above-listed trilogy.)

Marked for Life, the Macnair/Carrow story is also on hiatus at the moment until I become re-inspired. I tend to prefer to write about redeemed DEs.
