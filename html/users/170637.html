Author has written 40 stories for Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, and X-overs.
Ivory Tower is 26, loves horror movies (B-rated included), and has been writing since age 14. I would tell you my favorite Harry Potter character, but after reading a few of my stories you will be able to figure that out on your own.

Ivory Tower is quite fond of parodies and humor, and has little patience for those who are easily offended and hop on their little pedestals to whine about the unfairness of the world. Ivory Tower has had more than her share of bad luck, health, living circumstances, etc., and is even able to mock her own ill fortune and issues in her stories.

Ivory Tower would like to thank those who appreciate warped humor and disturbing concepts.

For a good laugh, Ivory Tower recommends the following movies:

"Plan 9 From Outer Space"; "Flesh Gordon"; "The Evil Dead" trilogy; "Time Bandits"; "Fear and Loathing In Los Vegas"; any movie with Kathy Ireland.
