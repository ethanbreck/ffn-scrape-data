And as I stare at this blank white space to work out what else to type, I trust that you'll entertain yourselves. :oP

I'm just a simple gal from Sydney Australia. A teeny bit reclusive, I find my greatest joys in writing (solitary), listening to music (mostly solitary) and playing computer/Xbox/NDS games of any sort.

I am trying to get back into writing, but you know how it is... life, and stuff.

I'm also looking at starting my own original works in the months to come, and would appreciate some soundboards. If you like my stuff, keep checking my profile here or my blog above for updates.

Keep leaving comments - makes me feel all warm and fuzzy when i read them. If you want to find me, I'm on facebook: http://www.facebook.com/aimeemaree.scott - I welcome random friend requests. :o)

You can read my blog, The Daily Moan, via the above link. Warning: the blog can be a bit misery-laden at times.

Thanks for reading!
