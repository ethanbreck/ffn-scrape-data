To the people who still look at my updates - I do really plan on continuing Like Nicotine, and I also have a definite ending planned for the Harmony collection. Life has been rough for the past years, for many, many reasons, and most of my writing tends to be one-shots these days. On top of that, I no longer keep up with Naruto like I used to, and feel almost out of touch with the characters. I really haven't meant to leave you waiting, if you still are. I appreciate everyone who has told me they still are interested. I hope that sometime soon I can give you something to make the wait worthwhile.
And to those of you who've given up holding out for a new chapter - if you see this - I'm sorry. I've never experienced so many people being excited about something I was writing and I really did appreciate the comments and support. Harmony alone reached over 30,000 hits and became my most popular story. At a time when I used my writing as something to make me feel more complete, you guys really helped me get along.

Thank you.
