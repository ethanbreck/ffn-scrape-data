Welcome to my profile! Decided this thing needed a bit of an update, so:

I'm currently studying English and Creative Writing at Aberystwyth university (in Wales, land of rain and sheep and rained-on sheep.) Which is incredibly hectic and rather stressful but still very, very fun.

Thank you to everyone who's ever reviewed my stories for giving me the confidence to go for this! I would never have got here without the support of all of you lovely people.

Yes, Macbeth is finished. I want to go over it when I actually have the time and do some minor edits - scrap a scene that really annoys me, change a few minor but glaring inconsistences, things like that - but fanfiction time is severely limited when I'm mean to be concentrating on original things for university! This means Fallen is still on hiatus for the forseeable future, I'm afraid; I don't have as much time to spare for fanfiction anymore.

For those of you who know of NaNoWriMo, however (www.nanowrimo.org) - I took it on for the first time this year and won! Though of course, the story still isn't finished. When it is, however, I'm going to put it up on the internet somewhere and provide a link.

To quote my wonderful beta, who wrote the previous version of my profile, "Fics Cyropi has written include the weird, the wonderful, and the ones I’ve never quite got the guts up to read." I think that sums up everything, but I reccomend reading the more recent fics, as they have a much better quality of writing. The older ones are mainly still here for sentimental value. My personal favourite fic is Macbeth.
