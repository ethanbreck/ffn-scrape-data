Do you really want to know who I am...where I come from? Tough, because I haven't yet figured that out for myself.

I was born in the year of the Monkey, under the Metal element, on a stormy night.

I am just a silly Harry Potter fan who likes to write and hones his skills, writing stories about his favourite fictional character.

My advice to fellow fanfic writers is to be a patient writer, and ponder the actions of your characters, before you actualy write them down. Never rush! And if possible, find a competent fool who is willing beta-read your story.

May the muses inspire you.
