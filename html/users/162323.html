About Me

I am a bookworm, a dreamer, shy around people I don't know very well and possessing of a fine disdain for the usual teenage stuff. This includes your traditional girlish expectations. For one, I don't like makeup and always feel rather sad when I meet someone who thinks they must put it up every day to be presentable.

My greatest joy in life is writing.

Recently graduated from high school, I am trying to figure out this independence thing. I'd rather be off to Neverland. Never never grow up.

Music: Enya before anyone. Tori Amos, Loreena McKennit, Dashboard Confessional. Others ... Sixpence None the Richer, Jaci Velasquez_. I listen to Oldies, Classical, Christian Contemporary & Alternative, and hit and miss songs from other genres. I am a great fan of foreign folk music and some musicals, like Les Miserables. I like music best, however, when I can write something to it.

Authours & Books: Robin Mckinley, Patricia Mckillip, the Sevenwaters Trilogy, Lloyd Alexander, Louisa May Alcott, poet Emily Dickinson, Margaret Mahy, Patrice Kindl(sighs with rapture and hearty admiration), many, many others. (be still, my heart 3)
