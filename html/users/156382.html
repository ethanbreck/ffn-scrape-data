Okay time to change the bio again! Okay I moved to Arizona in '04 and now I am moving back to California in '08. I have been writing for as long as I can remember but however fanfiction is my passion, because I wish I could be some of these characters, j/k lol.

I started writing the fanfiction when they allowed the celebrities like JT and Nsync and them to be a part of the site but after that changed I had to change my fanfiction which should explain why I have such a hard time updating...that and I am super busy.

Anyone looking at the pic should see my adorable niece Isabella born on April 27. She is almost 4 years old already! Time flies.

Obviously I am obsessed with wrestling, especially john cena and jeff hardy. I love Triple H as well but with him having his second kid I decided I must turn my obsession elsewhere, :Sighs: sad right?

My fav shows are MISSING, NYPD blue, cops, Walker Texas Ranger, Wrestling, Picket Fences and Saved By The Bell... CSI is up there to LOL. And most recently I started watching NCIS and Cold Case, NCIS Los Angeles, Supernatural, and so many other shows that are just interesting to me.

The recent loss of my Great Grandmother has hit me really hard. I used to spend my summers with her all the time as I grew up, she shaped the person I am. I know I am a sensitive person, so please, be nice when you review. If you have something mean to say, keep it to yourself! This isn't about being a rude mean person, this is about giving constructive critiscm and helping someone become a better author, folks. Thanks everyone for reading my stories and reviewing, nicely.

As always if you read the stories please review the stories... and be nice!
