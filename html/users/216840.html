Author has written 8 stories for Wicked, and Sea Patrol.

I love to write, and there barely goes by a day when I am not doing that. I've been writing for over a decade now, both original works and fanfiction. Sometimes the ideas are personal and for my own enjoyment, and then on the odd occasion, I venture into the cyber world to share the pieces I have created. On these few rare occasions, they will appear here. Since starting a particular story that got put on hold for too many years due to real-life, I now no longer publish anything until it is finished, so you can rest assured that any new publications that are serial, will have full, complete endings!

If there is one thing I ask of you today, that is to REVIEW. And not just my work, but that of anyone else that you happen to read today. There is nothing more frustrating than watching the 'view figure' creep up, but having no responses to gauge whether or not your work was liked! Constructive criticism is always welcomed, although flaming is not. Be kind to an author today, and take two seconds to type a few words of encouragement and appreciation.

Favourite TV shows/musicals (pairings) to write for: Sea Patrol (Mike/Kate), Rescue Special Ops (Dean/Lara), X-Files (Mulder/Scully), Wicked (Elphaba/Fiyero) and We Will Rock You (Brit/Meat).
