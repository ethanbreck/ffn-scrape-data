Greetings! I am a 27 year old princess in disguise, known around these parts (and most everywhere else) as Sapphira. I started writing fan-fiction 10 years ago at the tender age of 17. I like movies and watching tv, though I'm not always up to date on what's new. I participate in voice acting online and also like to sing and write music.
