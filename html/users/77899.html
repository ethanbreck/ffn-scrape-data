Yep. Hi. So you're reading my profile. Great! Tho I have no idea why you would be...

I graduated with a BAs in Computer Science and English last year with an intent to go into editing/publishing but ended up working as a legal secretary instead. In the meantime at least...

Writing Influences and favorites: Mercedes Lackey, Orson Scott Card, Microserfs by Douglas Coupland, The Wheel of Time Series by Robert Jordan, Stephanie Plum Series by Janet Evanovich, Anita Blake and Meridith Gentry Series by Laurell K. Hamilton, Rachel Morgan Series by Kim Harrison, Various things by Anne McCaffrey, Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams, Starship Troopers by Robert Heinlein, and probably many others i dont recall at the moment.

Anime: Bleach, Fullemetal Alchemist, Naruto, Eureka Seven, Evangelion, Beck, Love Hina, Melancholy of Suzumiya Haruhi, DNAngel.

Manga: Megatokyo, Mars, Only the Ring Finger Knows, Othello, The Wallflower. I'm a sucker for Sport/Competition stories (Nothing related to cards tho), Shoujo-anything, gender swap (Girl shows she can be just as good as the boys something or pretends to be a boy), Mecha, and the occasional well written yaoi (it MUST have a good story and not just be all smut).

Writing wise, i prefer to keep things light and humorous, but as i recently discovered...lol, i can be dark and disturbing too. Muahahaha!

I am currently working on a a whole bunch of stories that I can't quite finish. The stories keep appearing in my head and i can hash out an outline and bits of scenes, but I never have time to actually write it all out. ARGH.

I have a whole mess of Bleach fics, ranging from Yachiru related fics to an "omg ichigo died?!" story. There's a Digimon spin off fic that's gotten so off the beaten road that its probably closer to being an original story with digimon influences than a fanfic. A few naruto ones i cant quite start...The list goes on...and on... I havent forgotten about Chain of Being...Its in the que I assure you.

I am STILL astounded by the reaction people have had to Hard Definitions. Gee. It easily tripled all the hits and reviews than all my other fics combined. You guys trying to tell me something here? lol. I do have an idea for a related story...but again...Real Life keeps interfering. Thanks the encouraging reviews everyone. _

Dunno if you read all that, but congrats if you did. Here's a cookie and a random bit of origami.
