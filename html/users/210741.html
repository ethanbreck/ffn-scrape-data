Okay, so here's the place where I'm supposed to write a little bit about myself, right? Okay, where to start?

I'm 23, female, live in New Zealand, and I'm a fanfic writer. Ooo, geez that came a little AA sounding didn't it? NTM... That's the facts, leave it as is.

I write for a few different fandoms, not many. I've tried writing for various ones, but they don't always come out very well. So I've chosen to stick to a few well chosen ones, where I think I do a fairly good job of accurately portraying the characters, and where the writing just seems to flow. At the moment all my stories are on hiatus due to the fact that I am now working full time at a video store as the store manager and am about to start a business management course part time.

BHH - Strange Day's at Blake Holsey High, otherwise known and Black Hole High, depending on where in the world you are situated. I love writing for this fandom. Totally fun! My favourite characters are Josie, Z, Vaughn and Lucas... Shipdom - JV, completely. Love the father-daughter relationship between Z and Josie... I just really like Josie's character, very much like myself I guess.

Roswell - Who doesn't love this show? lol. I just love everything... okay, almost everything... about this show. Fav couples are Candy, Stargazer and Rebounder, with quite a few AU ships which include GZ, and Groupie among others. I write all fandoms virtually, with every character. Currently am writing a SG fic, an Antar based fic, and am posting two fics here.

Grey's Anatomy - Love it, love it, love it. Totally awesome show. Ships are Junkie Love (Isobel, Denny), BGL (Isobel, George). Have written one main fic, which was pretty much the first ever full length GA fic ever written, or at least posted anywhere. Am currently writing several others.

Supernatural - Got into Supernatural last year when Season 1 premiered here in New Zealand, and just fell in love with it. Hot guys, hot car, guns, monsters. It's the perfect show. Am currently working on a SPN fic, which will eventually get posted when I can find the time to get it finished. For now I'm content reading SPN fics in my very very very spare time. Am a Dean girl at heart, something about that boy just makes me so hot, and him and Starla together? PERFECT! Love Sammy, but Dean's my man. Out of the recurring girls of the show, Bella was defintely my fave, I just cant stand Ruby. Starla was my main favourite and I really wish they'd bring her back, she was perfect for Dean.

One Tree Hill - Discovered this amazing show this year courtesy of the wonderfully talented Sophia Bush (as in I saw her in several movies then quickly ran out and bought the show, not because she personally introduced me to it). Favourite characters are Brooke, Nathan, Haley, Skills and Mouth, and I absolutely would love to see Brooke and Lucas get together, despite the fact that I think he's a royal ass. I have a few fic idea's rattling around, but I'm just gonna see how it all plays out before I do anything with them, so keep your eyes open!

So as you can see, I'm writing a lot... I have something like 7 fics in the works, with at least 20 more idea's running around in my brain. I blame Brian for setting the plot bunnies lose in my backyard!

Tahra xoxo
