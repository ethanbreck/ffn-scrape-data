7/29/09--Okay, so it's been over four years since I last updated this page and pretty much just as long since I was writing. Real life interfered in a major way.

I'm not really writing in the same fandoms anymore. What drove me back to writing was the Star Trek XI kink meme, so that's the fandom I'm currently writing in. All my ST stories are posted at my lj (except a few drabbles on the kink meme I decided not to repost), which is now up as my homepage since my last site is defunct. I'm working on posting all my stories of a certain length both here and there . I might be taking down the WIPs that have no hope of being finished, since I am trying very hard not to start something without finishing it anymore, and I feel bad that I posted them and then abandoned them.

Note: I don't like posting on this site as it completely ignores all my html coding, no matter what I do. If you want to see the original html, including my section breaks (grrr, a pox on you, ff.net!), check out my livejournal.
