What can I say about myself...

Well... I have an obsessive personality.

I obsess.

I consume fandoms like a locust and when I've completed the feasting I move on to new ones. Same goes for arts and crafts, jobs, and housework. I'll get good at something and until, or unless something brings my attention back to a topic or activity, I will likely ignore it for the rest of time. (the housework one infuriates my mother)

I have poor mental health on a good day. A lot of the stuff I write or play is mostly distractions so I don't think about that. Fantasy is so much more preferable sometimes. I like to come up with the most off the wall shit that no one had previously considered or published and I'm a big fan of "Hold My Beer." moments, because growing up as the oldest child gave me a very... Alpha personality (My housemate diagnosed it as "Always having the biggest pair of balls in the room.") and a perfectionist streak.

I'm a fan of writing low key relationship stuff in my stories, partly because I'm shit at writing smut unless it's for myself, partly because I do understand that people sometimes don't like what I like (mind you, outside of fiction, adding other people to an equation gets a big ole NOPE from my brain). I've had positive feedback in the past about my stories and their pairings being tolerable even though it was something the reader didn't like (e.g. slashfics) and I hope to continue along that line. XD Sides, everyone knows that the romance stuff works better on readers than porn without pictures. XD
