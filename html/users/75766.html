New Stuff:

19th July 2005

No longer to be updated. For all my new fics, of which there are many, please refer to my Livejournal. Most recent fandoms are Naruto and Star Wars. It's been fun.

13th January 2005

Um. Well. Yes.

I can't say I've been writing much lately. As is obvious from the six month silence. I've uploaded two Harry Potter drabbles I wrote some time ago. I wonder if my writing style has changed much...?

Shackles is pretty much on indefinite hiatus. The PotC AU I'm writing on gets about five lines added every few months. All my old unfinished Digimon stuff is officially dead. I won't delete them, but they won't be finished unless I go crazy (or Digimon starts showing again and I am shockingly inspired...). Newest fandom is Fullmetal Alchemist, and I have literally half an hour ago started writing some random FMA thing. Expect no epic Ed-and-Al journeys from me. It's Roy!angst all the way, thankyouverymuch.

I do update my livejournal fairly regularly. Also my art site got deleted, but you can see my stuff at Deviantart if you're interested. I update that not-too-slowly as well. Oh, since the site was deleted, that means my few LotRips fics are gone, and I can't upload them here. I'm sure no-one will miss those anyway.

Well. Perhaps this is the start of something new. I hope I'll start neglecting this account a little less now...But no promises I'm afraid.

Meanwhile I hope you all had a marvellous Christmas, and wish you all a most prosperous new year. Hugs all round.
