Hello and God Bless you ALL!

I'm a Christian rapper born and raised in Brooklyn New York and I specialize in writing Christian/Biblically Based fiction, specifically, movie scripts. I'm an executive vice president of an independent Christian rap record label named "Most High Inc." Our website is listed above. The "Most High Inc" website also has a radio station that brings you the best in independent and underground Christian Rap and Christian RB.

I'm a Puerto Rican male who specializes in mixing the elements of inner city, fantasy, horror, historical, sci-fi and action thiller genres into one big creation. The whole point to my writing and why I do it is so one day I can get discovered and get paid for it. I like writing my original stuff more than fanfiction. As a matter of fact, any fanfiction I post on this site was originally intended to be submitted to the show but either got rejected or couldn't be sent because the show ended. So I post them on here as to not waste my work. So far, the stories I have posted are non-Christian stories.

I NEVER change Biblical facts and contents to fit my stories but I always make my stories fall in-line with Biblical facts and contents. The scripts, stories, and poetry I display on this site are my attempts at making seperate pieces of fiction in an effort to be seperate from the music and tv/movie industries who aren't very interested in producing Christian entertainment. This is one of the reasons why I'm with an independant record label.

My stories aren't meant to re-tell the context of the Bible but to make non-Christians understand the Bible and fundemental Christianity in a suspensefully entertaining way. No malice is intended toward Catholic Christians, Eastern Orthadox Christians, Mormons, Christian Scientists, Christian Kabbalists, Unification Church, Church of the Living Word, Children of God/of Love, Church of Scientology, and the newly founded Jehova's Witness Christians.

My stories reflect a "Protestant-Christian" point of veiw and aspect because Protestant Christians hold the Bible and its context to be true without questioning its credibility or commands. Protestants believe in the Bible as the very word of God and have complete faith in the Bible as being the only legitimate source to know how to function in God's will, to know what God's character is like, and to know how to live the way God wants us to. The only other legitimate source Protestants are willing to accept is the Holy Spirit and that's it.

With this in mind, I ask that you keep an open mind when reading my stories so that you can see that I'm not writing these works of fiction to try and convert anybody, but to let people understand the Bible and New Testament Christianity more clearly through interesting plot twists and fantastic story telling. So enjoy and look for my Christian rap album "Da Mark of the Anti-Thug" to come out on the internet next year through "Most High Inc." EnJoY!

Make sure to take a look at my favorite authors section and favorite stories section to see the kind of stuff I'm into and where I get my creative inspiration. Thanks for reading and don't forget to leave a review, whether good or bad, for any of my stories. For all other inquries, don't hesitae to e-mail me.
