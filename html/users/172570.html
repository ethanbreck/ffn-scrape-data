Author has written 40 stories for Heroes, Once Upon a Time, and Star Wars.

I am a RN and I am a compulsive reader.

I started reading fic (good god actual) decades ago, and writing has been an ongoing endeavor of improvement. This is for the second stage of editing, as I have an AO3 for a little more polish.

I have also an active Tumblr, and there may be hints about where my muse is at or where I am in fic writing land.

My sideblog is all about hosting the Gremma Appreciation! Come stop by and find all things involving Graham, Emma, or Gremma!!

If you are looking for Gremma fics, including hard-to-find ones, I suggest you look at my Recc List on tumblr. It is sortable by filters!

Major Multichapters in Progress:

Redefining Home: Prompted from Tumblr, an AU 3b in which Graham appeared in NY with Emma and Henry. These are one-shots in non-chronological order and is a prompt-based fic. Send as many as you want! Alternate banner here. Some chapters have gif accompaniments, so I suggest looking through this tag on tumblr for those!

Wilding: Part of the I Carry Your Heart series. It began with (I Carry it in My Heart) and has 2 one-shot collections. I am trying to mitigate some of the ending right now, which is why my focus is on one-shots and RH currently. This story is not gone! The hiatus is long-lived but hopefully not permanent.

Like My Mirror Years Ago: The Prompt That Won't Be Written. This fic will be a long-haul fic, and is slowly being chipped at. I'm trying to write chunks at a time, but it's tough-going. Slow burn is my downfall, guys.

Hope in All Its Forms: Underworld AU. Another that was a prompt at first and has now become more. Another slowly updated one.

Between the Shadow and the Soul: Basically, I took one look at lovely Wishworld Emma and I thought "you are too awesome for this mess." This is Princess Emma growing up loved but sheltered, but staying true to that fire and determination in her. Not having to suffer means she can grow in ways our Emma could not; it doesns't mean that she was weakened.

Stories with Links from Other People:

Once Upon a Dream: Inspired by this fanvid. 3B finale rewrite.

I have offered up my services for beta-ing, though I am only beta-ing Gremma and Swan Believer stories right now.

I do have other ships and fandoms, but gremma is where my heart lies right now.
