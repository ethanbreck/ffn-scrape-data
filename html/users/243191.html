Well, I figured I should update this thing. Not that anyone reads it. Ever. But we can pretend that people read it, and are interested, and blah blah blah.

Hi! I'm Hawke! I'm 18, slightly psychotic, and full of energy. I'm also a member of the SCA, a group which recreates the middle ages. It's fun, and in my case involves picking up a rapier, putting on fencing gear, and kicking the butts of people who think girls can't fight.

I'm an angst fan ( or pathos, however you phrase it), and rather enjoy watching my favorite characters get beat up, kicked around, mentally and physically tortured, emotionally wrung out, and then put back together by my second-favorite character. Not a bit fan of slash, but I do enjoy the brotherly-love thing.

Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, and Buffy seem to make up my fan fiction reading list, though I also read a bit of X-men and Sentinel. I like nice, looooong fiction; novel length or better, complicated plots, and funny characterisations. My blog page has lots of fun quotes, and can be found at

- Hawke
