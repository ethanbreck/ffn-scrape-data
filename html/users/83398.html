RL Update: So can we just delete this last year? Because, seriously, it was crap. Well, hopefully not for any of you, but sadly, it was for me. I don't like to talk about crap online, but needless to say this year I've written...oh...nothing. I'm moving today, setting new goals, and 'planning' on working hard to get all my mental blocks back in order. It's just that lately those blocks have been too close and when one goes down the rest go down like dominos. Bleh.

Cairis Rin's actual Bio:

I don't usually put up much in my bio, thinking my writing can speak for itself, but I've decided it's worth putting out a bit more of the real me.

Wanderer.

That's me in a nutshell. It's another name for planets, astronomy being one of my major passions. It's also a good description of my habits. I like to dabble, learn a bit of this, a bit of that, explore, travel (although I have yet to leave North America), and I have a real hard time dedicating myself to just one passion at a time. Likely why I keep ending up with these big pauses between updates. I'm distracted far too easily. But I'll throw myself whole heartedly into the deep end of any passion without bothering to see how deep the water really is.

As for the mundane stuff. Well, let's see. I'm a Canadian currently living in the States, the desert of Arizona at the moment. As summer quickly approaches (it's Febuary as I'm writing this. You all have snow or rain and I've already got the AC on in my car) I'm sure I'll start reconsidering staying here...again. I'm single, 31, have two cats, and am extremely religious. Ha! Bet you didn't know that, did you? But don't worry, I'm a firm believer in keeping fic to character. Although if a character swears a lot, you're more likely to find me writing 'He let out a string of expletives' then the actual dictation of his speech. But, eh, if you haven't noticed that by now, you're not going to.

I'm the perfect example of a starving artist who fixes houses for her rent, lives off the charity of others, and is working on the whole 'lets try to be a real author' gig. Not the easiest of career changes. Certainly not the smartest, but eh, why not? As a backup plan, because one always needs a backup plan, I've gone back to school to get an Engineering degree. Mechanical or Chemical? What do you all think?

Umm...I guess if you want to know more, you'll have to check out my blog...not that I blog much. I did finally update my website, but I update stories here, first. I do have some scary older stuff on my website, but, well...read at your own risk. I've really got to learn how to be more internet social, don't I?

So enough about me. Go read some fics you cool and crazy people!

Que sera, sera.
