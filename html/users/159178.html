Notes:

27.6.11 First, I would like to thank the reviewers. All of you, with both positive and negative points. I took all of your advice to heart, and am working on revising ALL my stories. Both Ice Queen and One Time Warrior are particularly difficult because they are based on rather poor plot design. I'm proud to say that I've grown as an author since I first wrote these, and have learned to take criticism in the spirit it was written. I am very willing to admit that my first few fictions were rather trite and ill conceived and mostly an escape tactic. I've taken to writing characters that are more true to life.

Second, I would like you all to know that, while the revisions will be fairly quick, anything past that point will take much longer. I'm having to re-write many plot points, and even though the stories are very overdone and poorly conceived I am determined to finish what I started. It will be more difficult than just churning out the same fic that has been in circulation since fanfiction first started, because I want to make this my own. Yes, the basic plot is overused, but I am trying to put my own spin on it. It can be done, I've seen it before. If I were interested in being wholly unique, I'd only write original fiction. I don't expect this to be my best effort, I didn't give myself the best to work with. I appreciate that it seemed to be popular thought that I had a fairly good grasp on the technicalities of writing by the time I was thirteen when I first started these fics.

Finally, anyone interested in the fics I wrote between when I first started writing for this site and now can check out my other profile at www.fanfiction.net/~nanashitsurunaji. Any constructive critiques are completely welcome. Thank my husband for teaching me that I don't have to be so defensive about everything I do and say.

23.6.11 No, I'm not dead, no matter how much you may wish I were. Life happens, and I'm back for at least a year. Since I last posted anything on this account, I've gotten married and have (hopefully) become a better writer. Not by much, though, in my opinion. Please bash the hell out of my newest work, because I know I couldn't get this beta'd if I wanted. I'm not a big fan of Twilight anyway, but beggars can't be choosers, and I can't help where the inspiration strikes from.

14.7.08 Having recently returned from my mental trip to Japan, I am ready for more studious fanfics. If you are curious about my anime/manga kick, e-mail me. I will gladly give you free reign to bash those fics as well. Having recently grown up, I feel that my older fanfictions need to be completely re-hauled, and my newer works should be beta'd first. Before being released to feast on the brains of the general public.
