Hello, Sierra here.

Anything published before February 2015 that is incomplete will remain incomplete. I just don't have the interest to rewrite those stories into something that's decent. Just assume they have a happily ever after--that's what I intended for those stories.

Because I can't help myself, I will likely post more fanfics in the future, though without any regularity at all.

Thanks,

Sierra

Note:

If you didn't notice, ALL of my Harry Potter stories are written before the series finished. Therefore, a lot of things we know now weren't canon back then. I wrote many of these before the fifth book where we have the first real introduction to Lily, so PLEASE stop correcting me on things I "did wrong" because it wasn't wrong at the time. Also, for some reason, those old stories with Lily Evans were replaced with Lily Luna as a main character after the 7th book came out. I did try to change it back, but the next week they all said Lily Luna again. It was a big pain in my butt, so I'm leaving it the way it is!
