Basic stuff about me:

I'm over three decades old

I am a college professor

I have been writing fanfic online since I was 15, and was writing fiction (including fanfiction) before I knew what it was starting around the age of 5. The first series I remember ficcing for is Star Trek: The Original Series. These fics will never, ever see the light of day. I am a lesbian-leaning asexual who writes m/m slash

I have no tolerance for homophobia

Series I enjoy and fic for:

Yu Yu Hakusho - I mostly read KuraHi, but I'm very picky about characterization and I require actual plot. I sometimes read YuuHi.

Slayers - Big favorite anime. My favorite pairing is Xellos/Zelgadis. I can also tolerate Lina/Zelgadis, Lina/Gourry, and Lina/Amelia. I've read the first 8 original novels, a goodly portion of the manga, and all of the series (Slayers, Next, Try, Revolution, Evo-R, the OVAs, all the movies).

Pandora Hearts - This series was such a mindtwist, and I love it. I typically write Liam/Break, but I generally RPed more than I wrote fic.

Miraculous Ladybug - This series grabbed me hard and brought me back into fandom for the first time in 5 years. So many ships, including polyships.

I'm back to try to finish Detour.
