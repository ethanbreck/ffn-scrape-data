WORDS OF MY OWN

"The only day I'm beaten is the day I die."

"If one day, there are no such things as masculine and feminine, gay and straight, normal and abnormal, religous and athiestic. . .if we just were beings, with no stereotype or rulebook of how things should be, we'd be at peace. And I don't think anyone could handle it."

"LIFE, LIBERTY AND THE PURSUIT OF HAPPINESS. . .

. . .is all I really want."

What else is there to say? You want my name? Adia, Jessica, Nishey, Mistress, Hedwig. . .call me whatever. You want my age? It's nothing but a number, though some people consider that to be how wise you are; really, what you've been through defines that. You want to know where I live? On Earth, like everybody else. You want to know what I look like? Beauty is in the eye of the beholder, my dear. ;)

My muse is flighty. I can never focus on the same thing for a long period of time, so I tend to have several stories running, or I write poetry or ficlets when especially impatient. My writing tends to be what people would consider contreversial; I just consider it a breath of fresh air. Writing is, in a total cliche, my release from reality. My work is no one's but mine, and I can (usually) control what happens. Anyway, hope you enjoy my writing, and feedback is always appreciated!
