UPDATE 26/06/2015 - Updating my profile and latest news on my future plans.

Current/Active Works/Future Projects

I'm more active on AoS right now (using different name) so some of the stories I've listed here are only posted over there. I will bring them to FF.Net but only when completed. (And not enough room in my profile there to update on current status.)

Please note that all of these are not guaranteed to be written to completion. Those that I decided not to follow through will likely be posted in whatever complete form into a singular Story Entity.

AoS stories:

Rare Love: Bunch of interconnecting drabbles in different fandoms. Not any definitive path just an as and when thing.

Apple Core: Yes this was another miserable attempt at posting something I should have held off. It was meant to be a way for me to just drive on through with a story without spending too much time worrying over endless details and mistakes. But unlike the discontinued stories this one still has just enough life for me to probably pick it back up again.

Howl at the Moon: Standalone story at the time but I have been debating with a sequel. Not suitable here due to FF.Net restrictions.

Letters from the Blue: Like Howl at the Moon it is standalone but I have left plenty of room for expansion. I even have two "letters" written already for it to further the plot. It's also one of only three stories where I have actually laid out a course of future chapters.

FF.Net Stories:

Ghost Roads: Been debating on a sequel for years. Never managed to get a word of it down but it keeps poking me.

All Stories:

Untitled or It's all Fun and Games until... : (One Piece) This is actually written. The only problem is the idea was more amusing then the end result and I'm not happy with it. It's a one shot and I think I will publish it at some point but will need to do some tweaking first. Major tweaking.

Impel Down: (One Piece) A what if story. What if Kuma only rescued Luffy, and the other SHs got captured and sent to Impel Down? I've even got four pages of writing down. There is slight Zoro focus but it is intended as a crew piece. It would also be a very dialogue light story.

"I'm Sorry": (One Piece) My currently most active story I'm working on. Promised myself at least a paragraph a day and I've exceeded that. It is also a what if. What if Usopp did get left behind at Water 7? Obviously Usopp focused and goes down a very dark route. It is also one of those three stories I have actually laid out a full plan on. I just wish I was a better writer to do the theme I'm aiming for with more justice. It's already over the 3k word mark.

Church of the Moon: (The Hobbit) Set in an AU where Smaug never attacked Erebor, but nothing to do with how that changes things. Instead this is a Dori, Nori, Ori driven story. It's a take on the complexities and dissidence of religion, poverty, duty and brotherly bonds. This one is at 5k words.

Death Match, working title: (One Piece) Since catching up on 15 years of OP I've had tons of ideas for stories so yes it's another OP fiction in works. This one is more of an action romp with Luffy facing an enemy determined to win one way of another. It's going to be my attempt at writing action.

Big Brother Usopp: (One Piece) Usopp is of course my favourite SH. In this story I hope to write a cutesy little story developing a less tackled aspect of the character in fanfiction. I noticed especially in early OP that Usopp gets along well with children and I thought it was cute enough to work with.

Rope, working title: (One Piece) So I've got stories planned in the OP fandom that have either Angst, action, comedy, cuteness or what ifs. This one is about relationships. It's not even a simple romance either. I'm hoping to try and get readers intrigued by lesser delved possibilities in the fandom. A sort of but not Love triangle. Friends with benefits. Questions on sexuality. And rare pairings. (And naturally the only stuff I have written for it is porny. When in reality that's only a very minor part of it.)

Untitled: (Original Fiction) A young journalist decides to write a piece on a pair of notorious serial killing Siblings. Set in an Original Universe rather then in modern times. Can't help but feel the journalist might end up being very self-inserting. But she's only really there as a foil for the brothers and wouldn't have any presence at all if I took it further then the initial premise here.

Untitled: (Original Fiction) My take on a variation of werewolves and pack dynamics. It's more of a self indulgence piece. Probably end up in snippet form. The Alpha/Beta stuff is vastly different to the one people are most familiar with in fanfiction.

List of OP AU: One Piece) As stated I just writ a list of AU's I would like to read or write but don't have anything beyond concepts. Native American AU. Western AU. Vietnam War AU. Demon AU. Strawhats as Marines AU. Magic powers AU. Victorian Era AU. Space Pirates AU. Carnivale AU.

On Hold/Discontinued

Elementals: My oldest work and one that will stand as is. Too many years now and lost interest in the fandom.

We Are: I won't be adding anymore to this now. Thankfully it stands alone just fine.

Legion: Done. I can't even remember what the end goal was.

Bunraku: It fizzled :( I still would like to finish it one day maybe, but I'm not sure it ever will. I might just post some sort of layout of what could have been had the plot continued.

Raven: Sirius Corvus: Like Bunraku, I doubt this will be properly finished. I think at some point I'll add one more chapter and skip to the end. Give it some sort of conclusion.

My Fanfiction Pet Hates

Mary-Sues, OOC, Character Bashing, Japanese abuse, Shipping/Slash Madness, Script-style stories, Rape = Love, Purple prose , Freaks of nature, Myself.
