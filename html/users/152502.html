Lordy this thing needed updating oo;;;; *feels like she neglected the account*

Anyways, it's a fanfiction.net acount, so this should be about the fanfiction side fo me. Here we go! (yes, I was in a weird mood when I wrote this, sue me)

Name: Isabel (and variations thereof. call me Isabelle or Isabella and I'll hate you deeply)

D.O.B: 13th of Mai, 1986

Looks: Brown hair, generally kept at shoulderlength(shorter at the back ^^), brown eyes, indoor west-european(no mom, I'm not pale, damnit.). Wears glasses and dark or transparent nailpolish. Mostly wears black or other dark colors, but also bounches around in light-brown baggy pants an anime-shirts.

Years of writing: you want me to put a number to this?! Okay, I've been writing pretty much since I learned it. Since I was 6 or so.

Years of making up stories: Do you believe me if I can't remember a time without makign up stories? I mean, seriously, I was a kid with a lot of imagination and free time on my hands.

Years of making up Fanfics: dunno for sure. It started somewhere when Bikermice from Mars aired in Holland and got me hooked. Back then I also had a friend who shared the imagination and the fandom, and we made up some stories together... *snaps back from memory* My first real 'fanfic' idea was after the Lion King, I was so weird that I already prevered the baddies back then and had this idea that Scar had a mate somewhere that Disney forgot. Imagine the look on my face when the Lion King 2 came out *makes a face* Turns out I wasn't the only one too, but I can't prove anything untill they discover the world-wide telepathic spoinage network that's hidden deep within the Disney Studios. *scowls and blows bubbles through a pipe*

Anyways, I tried my best to write some BMFM fanfics, but they always ended up as the histories of my own characters (which popped up like frikkin' rabbits) so I eventually sortha gave up on that one... Things went pretty calm from there on,untill I encountered Dragonball Z

Now, I know that Dragonball Z isn't THE ultimate anime around, but back then it was the best I could get and I liked it a lot. I liked it even more when I encountered Juunanagou (#17) What followed was horrible, but it helped me a LOT. Sit back and kick your feet up, this is the first time I'm telling this.
First of all, Mary Sues...
I have a hidious talent for Mary Sues and I can't deny it, so I won't. Anyways, my first realy Mary Sues were dragonball fandom. First was Kohlrabi(yes, I have a twisted sense of humor) who is a saiyain(Oo spelling...) but had gray fur instead of brown. She made an entrance in a very short and lame humor-fic I wrote (it was completely insane, but I enjoyed writing it)
The Second was a young lady of whom I forgot the name (I think I'm happy) She's by far the worst of the two. All I'll say is that she's a cyborg and managed to run into Juunana in an AU. Thank the gods I never kept working on that one =_=;;;

Now you are probably thinking "How the HELL did that help you?"
Very simple. When I looked back on those I realized a couple of things about fanfic writing:
- Characters sharing a lot of resemblance to yourself (Mary Sues) SUCK
- Readablility of the story an sig is important
- storylines are nice at times.

I think I spend a big time afterter that just reading fanfics, but still writing Origional Fic. Then (after the closure of a rather large project) my best friend and me got bored and launched of a second big project. By that time we were both on a bit of a Sailormoon hype, and had our original senshi hopping around. First we wanted to introude them to the Sailor Moon Univerxse, but that just would get messy, so it because an (unfonished) 11 chapter fic that was sailormoon based...

ehm, that didn't have crap to do with this. ahwell. I started writing fanfics again. Most of those didn't make it beyond my computer due to OC with MarySue tendencies or crappy storylines.

Right now whatever fanfiction-talent I have lies within short fanfics with litle or no original characters and simple storylines. "Yume" is my personal fav. right now with "Photos" running second, these are examples of my fanfics at their best. the others are nice, but I'm not really proud of them...

As for bigger works: I try. I have a big Slayers story stored in my head for well over a year, which is unlikely to come out anytime soon, and a Harry Potter one is slowly building (also has a snowball's chances in hell fo comming out)
My main focus right now when it comes to fanfics is a big Drakan Fanfic I'm working on right now, though I don't know if I'll finish it (then again, it kicked itself beyond anywhere the other fanfics came...)

so... that's pretty much me I guess... *looks up* I know, it's long... gomen.
