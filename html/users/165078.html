September 16th, 2015: Chloe Grace, my niece.

Completed Project:

Soulbound: My most ambitious and exciting project that my years of fic writing has led to: a pseudo-novelization of RWBY Volume 1. I love novelizations when done right with my favorite one of all time being Starcraft: Liberty's Crusade. Started off with ideas of rewriting The Stray and Black and White only for my brain to cut in with "Hey, people have done that so let's novelize the entire volume!". Am throwing in my own ideas, popular fan theories, and pairings (majorly Bumblebee) in the mix and it won't necessarily end as it did in the show once we do get to Black and White. Hoping to get this done before the start of Volume 2 but there is the possibility of me being one or two chapters short of the deadline.

(Holy crap I got this done a day before Volume 2. And for the fun of it, I'm going to list which theories and ideas I had hold water in the RWBY canon for Volume 2 as each episode comes out, even the smallest tidbits.)

Proven/Disproven Theories of Soulbound:

Disproven: Second semester starts during Volume 2, not before.
Proven: X-Ray and Vav comics exist.
Proven: Beacon does have an entrance exam which Blake completed without going to combat school.
Proven: Schnee Dust is headquartered in Atlas.
Disproven: Weiss isn't an only child - she has a sister.
Proven: Penny is an android (Brain: Who didn't see that one coming?)
Proven: White Fang chose to wear their masks as symbols to the monsters that humanity see them as.
Proven: Roman supplying weapons to the White Fang.
Proven: Dust can be woven into clothing.
Disproven: Ruby and Yang were raised on Patch, not in Vale.
Disproven: No safe landings. RPLs - rocket-propelled lockers - do come in hard and fast.
