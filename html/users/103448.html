I solemnly swear that I am up to no good!

Alright, just kidding people. Well, maybe...

Anywho, what is there to really say about me? I'm 14, female, 5'2(all my friends say I'm short and make fun of me for being vertically challenged)and I live in the lovely Los Angeles, CA. I go to music Academy, and love it. I'm one of those Drama Geeks but at my school, it's cool to be into musicals and stuff like that, so it's heaven on earth for me.
My Fav's since I'm bored and not creative enough to think of anything else currently:
Color:
-Green and Silver, and no no because they are Slytherin's colors...I swear I liked them both before that.
Books and authors:
-Harry Potter series
-Gossip Girl Series(as cheesy as it is I love them...they're addicting!)
- Enchanted Forest Series
-I love poetry by Robert Frost, Fire and Ice being my favorite poem of all time. I don't know why...it just compels me for some strange reason. I also love the lockless door by him too.
Food: Sushi, Ice Cream, Caramel, Coffee
Random ramblings: Well now that I've run out of favorite's and put you all through that, which I apologize for. See that is what comes of my boredom. Another thing I love to do when I'm bored is read, write, and act. The reason why is because it's a way to escape. Don't get me wrong, I love life, and I love my friends, but life can just be rough and jagged and pointy when it wants to be, and those are my ways to escape. The world can be beautiful, but it can be just as cruel. I know it, and I love it anyway. But come on, what could be cooler than magic, and sword fights, and princes in disguise? Through stories and acting, those things are real...that's why people spend so much damn time at the movies. ;) Anyway now that we know WHY I write, hopefully you won't hate WHAT I write. :D Give me some feedback. I love talking to new people, so please don't be put off by my late night ramblings. I swear I am more sane than I seem.

Mischief Managed...
