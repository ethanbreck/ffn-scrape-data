Uh... RiaRE. Yes. I got cold feet, and now I'm making sure the timelines all fit together before I post anything more. And hey, I put pen to paper for the first time in roughly a year (I'm so glad I kept notes on this stuff). I discovered this crazy little thing called the org and got horribly distracted by the videos contained therein, and after adding a couple of my own to the catalogue I feel in need of a break from the whole thing. I feel bored already when looking at the New Releases forum. : /

NON- PERMANENT NOTES:

Okay, here we go. Author's note to the (not-so) recently-posted Reflections In A Ruby Eye.

The major questions you might or might not have regarding Kuja's Aryan appearance will be answered much, much later, as will the question of the understated big revelation just after Kuja hijacks the guard's memory. If all you have to ask is "Why, God, why?", then I'm afraid I can't help you much there. Just rest assured that if I do break with explicit canon at any time during the fic, it's because of unintentional oversight. Implicit canon, on the other hand, is my unwilling slave and will be whipped mercilessly until it learns to behave at all times. :evil:

The 'entry into Memoria' section was originally planned to come in at less than a thousand words, so there's a disproportionate amount of present tense until Kuja reaches Memoria. The meat of the story will take place in memory, with first person past being used for one character and third person past for the other. It's less confusing when you read it... I hope.

Any questions? Go ahead and ask. All author's notes will be kept here (well, unless Xing passes a rule saying what profiles can and can't be used for), so I can answer questions without having to wait until I post another chapter.
