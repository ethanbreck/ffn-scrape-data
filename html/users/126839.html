Author has written 4 stories for Percy Jackson and the Olympians.

I believe writing is very much like love. You can not truly love another until you truly love yourself. It needs to be your 100% and your significant others 100%. I apply this to writing in that, I believe a person must write for themselves first and their audience second. As an example of this, you will find that VESTA is 75-85% for myself, it's a bit more polished and thought out than my other fic. It is written for myself first, then polished up for my fellow readers. Though, I feel the more recent chapters have been for me and less polished, which is why I'm taking more time to update, so I can polish the forthcoming chapter up a bit. My other currently posted fic, HANDS is more like...95-97% for me...it needs a great deal of polish, but at the moment it is my pure nearly unadulterated creative release from the creative structure necessary for my other works. Eventually, if deserving, it will get the polish it requires. In the mean time, as stated earlier, feel free to join me in the crazy, if you so choose :-D
