Updated July 2010

Still reading like a madwoman. I have branched out into so many new fandoms. Right now Twilight that evil beast has held me captive for over a year. I've nosed into NCIS and Sookie stories, tore all through Gokusen, Ouran High Host Club, and Fruits Basket. Still have warm fuzzies for Daria, Teen Titans, certain HP fics and Labry... *jareth* rowr. When burned out I'll step back into Gilmore Girls my original love and cuddle a Witchblade fic or two to my chest like favorite friends.

Due to all the changes and lifestyle alterations the writing well has been bone dry. I haven't given up hope just haven't had the inspiration.
