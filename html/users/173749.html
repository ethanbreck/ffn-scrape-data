I’m a fifteen year old girl that is currently in a state of shock that someone is actually bothering to read this. I am amazed and I thank you.

I read fanfics far more than I write them, but when I am writing, I’m writing in my Cool Green Hand Chair of Inspiration. More often than not this is in the dead of the night, on a day when I have to be up early.

You’ve no idea how much I adore post-Hogwarts fics. If you know of any good ones that aren’t curretnly on my favourites lists, PLEASE email them to me! I would love you forever AND bake you a muffin on your birthday.

The other day I watched Dragonfly on TV, and the entire time I was thinking, ‘Chh, Jenny is so much better.’ I honestly think it could be the best fic ever written.
