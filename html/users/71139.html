UPDATE: 1/18/08

I have FINALLY updated the Art of Redemption. I apologize for the wait, BUT I'm gonna keep on truckin' because I've already started chapter four. Woo hoo! Keep your fingers crossed that it'll come sooner than chapter three!

I'm here for two reasons: to read and to write. I do more reading than writing, but I do love to write. Like any author, I experience writer's block from time to time. But I don't like to call it writer's block. I like to call it...writer's constipation. I have all of the story in my head; it's just a matter of channeling it through the keyboard and onto the monitor. It's just like wanting to take a dump, but you can't. I have all of this CRAP that I want to write...it just won't come out.

I guess after that wonderful analogy, I should probably just leave it at that. :-)
