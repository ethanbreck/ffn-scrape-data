My Name is Angel and I'm 20 years old. I'm a big anime fan and love to share my stories with others. Some of the shows I have fallen in love with are as followed, Sailor Moon, Ranma 1/2, Dragonballz, Inu Yasha, Night Walkers, Tenchi, and Candy Candy. Other than anime I write sorties about sci-fi. I one of those people that love Werewolves, Witches, Vampiers and so on. Not much scraces me, but I love trying to put fear in people's heart. I'm currently working on a novel call First Moons Rising. Hopfully I will have it finished and editied by the end of this year.

But in the mean time my muse has taken me to write more fanfiction. With this I hope to get more veiws of what other people think of my writing, and in the end to help me improve my writing style.
