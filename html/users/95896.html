Author has written 14 stories for Harry Potter, His Dark Materials, Echo Bazaar, Jonathan Strange & Mr. Norrell, Doctor Who, and Undertale.

I write fics!

Mostly they're a little odd.

I use a lot of OCs and obscure characters in my writing, and a lot of ridiculous made-up worldbuilding.

I write under the username Kaesa at the Archive of Our Own, where there is more fic by me. I have a Dreamwidth and a Livejournal, both under the username kaesa.

Since people ask me every now and then what the endgame ships are for my Founders stories, I will answer here:

Rowena and Godric are going to end up together. However, they will both have relationships (and/or brief flings) with other people over the course of the series. This is partly because I enjoy long, drawn-out UST, partly because I like angst, and partly because my characters always take forever to get together.

(The ships in that series other than Godric/Rowena and Helena Ravenclaw/The Bloody Baron will pretty much all be a canon character with an OC.)
