Archer of Mirkwood
hide bio
Follow . Favorite
Joined 07-14-02, id: 233477, Profile Updated: 02-27-08
Author has written 2 stories for Lord of the Rings.

Well, for a while I had a very simple profile, but I decided to give you loyal readers more than a snippet of who I am.

My name is Leah. I am a 19-year-old college student. My major is English, and that will never change.

Writing is not just an escape for me -- it is what I dream of doing for the rest of my life. Unfortunately for me, I cannot publish my fanfiction. Instead, I have to come up with my own world and develop my own characters. As dedicated as I am to writing, that task is a very difficult one for me to face. I actually began writing my own story that involved Elves living in a world of my own creation, but I have since discarded the idea entirely. My problem is that I will never live up to those I strive to become: my two favorite authors (and probably the two most successful, influential, and talented fantasy writers of our time), John Ronald Reuel Tolkien and Joanne Kathleen Rowling. I expect the same of myself: to create an entire world out of my head with its own unique races and languages. However, I am unable to do so (maybe the ability will come with age, but I seem stuck on their ideas rather than developing my own).

Having said that, I did in fact read the books before seeing the movies, and even then favored Legolas as a character; and yes, I admit I think Orlando Bloom did a stunning job portraying Legolas (not to mention he's incredibly sexy with a blonde wig and blue eyes). However, the Legolas is my stories (and yes, he is my character of choice) is not from the movies and he will never have the blue eyes that he sported in them, because to me he was always a brown-eyed Elf.

To all you wonderful readers out there who put up with my constant writers' block, chronic fear of actually finishing a story, or general laziness: thank you. Thank you for reading (whether or not you review), because knowing that someone, anyone out there enjoys my writing gives me the hope I need to pursue a future as a novelist. Maybe one day you'll be buying my book, huh? We'll see. For now, I'll finish my story.

FanFictions:

Maybe Then - Post-LOTR. An Elf named Tialyn from Imladris stumbles upon Legolas, the Prince of Mirkwood at Aragorn and Arwen's wedding. At first, the very sight of Legolas disgusts Tialyn, but with time, can the emotions between them change? Can they defeat the new dark forces threatening the balance of Middle-earth?

Father Time Tells All - Pre-, during, and Post-LOTR. Not a tenth walker. Legolas, Laina, and Malian have been friends since birth. When Laina's family decides to relocate to Imladris, she is left without much word from the best friends she grew up with. Five hundred years later, Laina returns with a certain Ranger at her side. Legolas is engaged, and Malian is acting very strangely. The years pass, and things change. Suddenly, year 3017 of the Third Age comes and passes, and with that year begins the fight for Middle-earth. With the dark forces rising in the East and the return to power of the Dark Lord, can the Elves of Mirkwood survive?
