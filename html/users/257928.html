Author has written 5 stories for Alias.

I place the blame for my life-long fangirl obsessions squarely at the feet of Mr. Spock who beamed into my tweener imagination and refused to leave.

It's been a very long time since I wrote any fanfiction. What there is, is archived here. These stories were written during the heady first and early second season of Alias and they are about Jack Bristow. With the benefit of hindsight, I see now that my interpretation of him was a highly eccentric take on this mysterious character. Sorry about that Jack purists! It felt right at the time. If you choose to read the stories please take this into consideration. I dropped out of Alias-love after the second season and haven't felt the fanfiction muse too often since then. I admit the muse stirred during the early seasons of House MD and now a bit with The Librarians but we'll see if anything comes of it.

I live in Boston, MA, USA with my husband and son and work for a publisher: reading manuscripts, assisting editors, and getting answers for frustrated authors. I love TV and my obsessive viewing includes my current favorite shows, Justified, The Librarians, Backstrom, Banshee, Once Upon A Time, Sherlock, Strike Back, and, always, Dr. Who. Past loves include: Buffy, Firefly, Burn Notice, Dexter, Leverage, The Dresden Files, Life (with Damian Lewis), Space: Above and Beyond, Battlestar Galactica, Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles, House MD, Psych, Criminal Minds, and, of course, Alias.

Thanks so much for dropping by! :)
