Author has written 5 stories for Sonic the Hedgehog, Transformers/Beast Wars, Trinity Blood, and Pokémon.

Welcome to my little space here on FanFiction.net. I have been a member since the summer of 2001. I previously wrote under the names of Strawberry Girl, Wingweaver, Garnet Princess and Maxanda.

I like to write fan fiction to keep myself busy as well as to get rid of ideas that have been floating around in my head for a while. I also use fan fiction as an outlet with which to increase my skills in reading and writing, as well as reviewing. I also occasionally post some of my original fiction at FictionPress.com (under the same username and id). I hope you enjoy reading my literary works. All constructive criticism is encouraged and greatly appreciated. I accept anonymous reviews, so you can still comment on my stories even if you don't have a FF.net account (or can't log in for whatever reason ie. work). If you have any questions about my stories or if you want to become a beta reader for any of my stories, please feel free to PM me. :)

Unofficial Fan Continuities

Sometimes when I get too many ideas for my stories to exist as part of an existing official continuity, I create full-blown continuities to accommodate them. For each of these fan-created continuities, I create a forum in which I post information about the universe as I write, allow my readers to discuss the stories and even to ask questions. The link for each forum will be provided.

Transformers: Absolution
http://www.fanfiction.net/forum/Transformers_Absolution/112067/
This universe falls within the G1 Continuity family. Most characters and concepts are pulled from G1, with a few additions from the live-action films, Unicron trilogy and Transformers: Prime. All stories from this continuity typically have the word "Absolution" in front to differentiate then from other Transformers stories I write.
