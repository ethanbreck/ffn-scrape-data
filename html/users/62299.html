3/4/11

When my computer crashed a year ago, I lost all the chaptered work I had for my stories. Since I haven't really written fanfics since high school (Chem and nursing majors leave you with very little time), I decided to remove them. I might get the urge sometime this summer to rework them up to my current writing standards...or not. It just seems the Sailor Moon, Ranma, and Harry Potter genres are not only getting to be unoriginal, but most of the work is TERRIBLE. Not like back when some of the fanfic greats were writing (even though some still are, albeit sloooooowly).

Sorry guys, unless I get the itch to write something that's most likely already been done before, and poorly at that, I probably won't be putting anything back up.

Cheers.
