Now how do I introduce myself?

I'm long under tree. How d'you do. The first sentence is already bad grammar. I'm still a kid I'd expect (well, half of the people I know [whom call themselves adults] call me that; the other half call me old with a mind that's... not quite there :p) but that's really up to one's own imagination ;)
I have other nicknames but the list is too long and it is likely to crash the entire system. Which is not advisable.

Currently I'm both a reviewer and a writer, more of a reviewer, because I'm only working on the fanfic, "Memories" and it's only the seventh chapter (argh. stupid stupid school :p)
Oh, and I just posted the fic entitled "Broken".

I ought to say also that I originally liked the character Legolas in the first place (from the bk), not just because of Orlando Bloom :) and Pippin also, though I admire Billy Boyd :)

Anyhow, if one chances upon this Bio, read my fanfics and review thanks :)

But I must state here that as much as I love writing, Time is not the kindest entity I know of and will very likely not allow me to update my fic regularly, but I'll try my best to post as often as I can. :)

And if you would like to convert me, or recommend me stuff... These are the stories and their fanfic I love:
1. The Real Ghostbusters (esp. Sheila Paulson! awesome stories...)
2. Lord of the Rings (obviously. my nick and first fanfic comes from there)
3. X-Men (but so far I haven't seen much good ones so it'd be good if someone convinced me)
4. Dragonlance (so far i haven't got down to reading the fanfic though)
5. Cardcaptor Sakura (haven't seen any fanfic yet)
6. X-Files (good grief. the only ones I've chanced upon are not quite suitable for the underaged. :P)
7. Record of the Lodoss War (the one fanfic I saw was great. I haven't got to see the rest yet.)
8. Pirates of the Carribean (jack!! :) some good fics out there)
9. Harry Potter (well, some are good :P)

There. Quite a long bio considering I hadn't had much to write at first.
Ciao :)
