Hey, my name's Lauren, I'm sixteen years old, and I live in Washington State. I am a sophomore at Hockinson High School and ended freshman year with a 3.8 GPA (whoo hoo!). I also got on the Honor Roll! Aren't you proud of me? I lettered in band also, though I don't have a jakcet yet. Oh, and I won "Most Improved in Spanish" in my school. lol, yay for me. In my spare time, I enjoy watching TV shows such as The Simpsons, CSI: Las Vegas, and any of the Law & Orders. I love hanging out with my friends and just messing around.

Making movies is a big dream of mine, and I hope to someday be a director in Hollywood. Well, rather I'd like to change Hollywood, then be a part of it. I love watching movies too; my favorites include (but are not limited to) My Cousin Vinny, Chicago, Sleeping Beauty, Spiderman, Secret Window, School of Rock, The Natural, The Butterfly Effect, Phantom of The Operaand the Lord of The Rings series. Speaking of Lord of The Rings, I love to read, and my favorite books would have to be the Harry Potter and LOTR series, of course. I also enjoy reading classics such as Edgar Allen Poe's short stories, Dr. Jekyll & Mr. Hyde, Phantom of The Opera, The Scarlet Letter, etc! Oh, I love Disney movies too; the oldies. When I was a kid I had a crush on Aladdin, *rolls eyes* I'm in love with other Disney movies such as The Jungle Book, The Little Mermaid, The Rescuers Downunder, Cinderella, Snow White, Beauty & The Beast and of course, Sleeping Beauty.

I love to laugh, and I love making people laugh. Sometimes I act so weird that people who don't know me well think I'm insane, (which I am, *wink*). That's fine with me though, because my friends seem to enjoy my personality. Ah, I'm also obsessed with Australia. I don't know why; I've never been and I don't know anyone personally who's from there. *shrugs* My favorite Australian candy is Violet Crumble bars! Wow! Heaven. Plus it tasted pretty good after those darn Musk Lifesavers...

Anyway, I hope you enjoy my writing. Usually I am unhappy with the results, but people tell me they like it. So I don't know. Also feel free to e-mail me, either at MintyF007@aol.com or TayHan1117@aol.com and please visit my website!! I love you guys!

Love, Lauren
