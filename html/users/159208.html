My Story

I am a member of the House Tyrell who is a Divergent Amity and Dauntless, proud Ravenclaw and living in Narnia. I used to be a forger for a guy name Nate and his team while simultaneously working the night shift as a ballistics technician for the Las Vegas CSI. They sent me to study archaeology with Dr. Henry Jones, Jr and provided me with a room in 221B Baker Street. He introduced me to the Librarians, who took me to track down the Milennium Falcon, the Enterprise and a yellow Camaro that can transform into an alien robot, all of which were hidden in Austen's England.

I've fought orcs in Middle Earth with a pair of Hunter brothers, compete to the death in televised reality games for children with Tintin as my district partner and somehow managed to learn Earthbending in the middle of a duel with the Juppongatana while travelling across all of time and space with a Time Lord. Currently I am serving as a lady knight of Camelot while battling Hollows alongside the Ninja Turtles.
