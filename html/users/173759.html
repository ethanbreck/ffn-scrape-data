Updated May 11th, 2006.

Attention: I might be a little slower at posting stuff for the month of May, as this will be my final month of high school, and I need to concentrate on not screwing up.

I'm eighteen years old, asian, and finishing up my senior year before going off to college. I'm heading to UC Riverside, majoring in English or Creative Writing.

A lot of my stories pre-2006 are kind of bad, but I don't have the heart to delete them. They're a reminder, I suppose, of how much one can improve in four years.

As you see, I've grown fond of one word titles.

These days I don't like including author's notes; rather, I prefer having the work speak for itself.

I appreciate all reviews I get, so don't be afraid to point out a mistake or inconsistency. Any review is fine, really.

I tend to put up a story only to add little details here and there later on if I spot a mistake or something.

Look here for any new information, okay? Good.
