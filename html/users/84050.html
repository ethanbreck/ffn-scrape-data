A pleasant hello to all. *waves merrily* *grins and glomps you*

I am 15, yet I ask that you do not hold my (presumably young) age against me.

I like reading and writing fantasy and fanfic, my ultimate fave in the latter being LOTR (I'm a Tolkien Purist). I love the Harry Potter books, (I'm Snape's bitch... well okay, in my dreams I am) and completely detest the movies. I also enjoy Redwall, HDM, and PotC. Recently I've been getting into L&O and CSI fics as well, but I could never write a fic about them because I frankly don't have the talent. (I do watch the shows religiously.)

In fanfics, I only enjoy plausible pairing; that is, pairing that is as true to canon as possible. As a rule I generally don't enjoy romances, as most of them are self-inserts, MS's, or slash (I have nothing against the concept of slash except for the fact that usually it's not plausible in the circumstances). I do like some romance fanfic. But for me to like it, it MUST BE WELL-WRITTEN. I dislike most soppy romance in general, but I will not tolerate poorly-written soppy romance.

I have FIVE stories, one of which is dead, three of which are finished, and the last one is in a comatose. Please don't stalk me and harass me about updates, as I will update as soon as my muse strikes me. At the moment, my muse is being very fickle however.

I used to flame a lot, it was great fun, but then I realized (maturity showing once again!) that it was quite frankly hurtful and unhelpful. So now I've resorted to CC, with varying amounts of sarcasm.

Hm. I want y'all to meet Schelob, my mini-Balrog. Isn't she pretty? *proud smile* Many thanks to Miss Cam!

Lastly, it goes without saying that I also like people to review my stories *coughs pointedly and glares at everyone* But, I can't blame all of my loyal fans, because right now, well, I don't really have any. So read my stories, and review them, and enjoy yourselves! (And feel free to email me!)

-LeoD, aka Agent BriRi

P.S. HALDIR LIVES!!!!!!! (in case you didn't know)
