Author has written 4 stories for X-Files, and Bones.

Well, let's see, I hate doing these things.

I am fan of a lot of TV shows and books and have been writing fan fic since before I knew the hobby had a name and other people did it. I guess I started writing when I was 12 or 13. I would say my first fan fics were for Dempsey and Makepeace, a British crime drama that aired in the US in the mid 80's.

I love to watch Bones, The X-Files, Harry Potter, and all the true crime shows on the Discovery Channel. I used to read constantly, but I now work full time in a nursing home as a SRNA and now I spend most of my time catching up on housework and other things I hate.

In the past, I have written fan fic for X-Files, Silk Stalkings, Janet Evanovich's Stephanie Plum, and Patricia Cornwell's Scarpetta. Sadly, most of these stories were lost in one of my computer crashes.

As far as personal stuff goes, I am a widow and have a 17 year old daughter who was diagnosed with autism at the age of 7.
