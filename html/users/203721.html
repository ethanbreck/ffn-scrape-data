New! Gasp! New! *
Because I'm in a romantic-y mood at the moment, I feel like making a small section for a pairing from a fandom that I'd like to promote. ^-^ Hopefully, I'll get around to changing this every once in a while. This time, the pairing is Tom/Ginny from Harry Potter!
Why you should go read a Tom/Ginny fic: Now, everyone knows, in every fandom, that there are good fics and there are bad fics. It's unavoidable. No matter which fandom you run loose in, no matter which ship you sail, they're out there. But in some areas, these bad fics are a little less common. I think I've found this while looking around for Tom/Ginny fics. I'm currently a bit obsessed with the pairing, so I've been scouting around for fanfics, of course. And I realized that this is a really difficult pairing to write. You have two characters that are more minor than anything. Sure, we see a lot more of Ginny than we do of Tom, but it's not a whole lot to be able to really know Ginny, like you would know Harry. Whether AU, during CoS, after CoS, whenever it's written, Tom/Ginny is a very difficult pairing to write. The two personalities are very separate. Tom is scary, and well, Ginny is not. Quite the opposite. Tom is manipulative, and Ginny is far from that. Making a successful romance fic out of this ship is difficult. Their relationship is bound to be intense. I can't really explain it, but I have so much respect for T/G authors. There are such great Tom/Ginny fics out there. I can't really convey what I mean here, but when you read a good T/G fic, you'll understand what I mean. This pairing is so awesome, now go read a fic! The Eagle commands you! O.o;;;
