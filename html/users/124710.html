Author has written 7 stories for Song of the Lioness, and Harry Potter.
I'm a 15 year old girl who is obsessed with reading, Lord of the Rings, and Johnny Depp.

I have every Tamora Pierce book except for Shatterglass. I read these a lot; my favorite pairs are Alanna/George, Sandry/Briar, Kel/Neal or Dom, and Daine/Numair (obviously).

I have the Harry Potter series too, and my favorite couples in those are Hermione/Ron and Harry/Ginny. I'm a huge Ginny fan.

I love The Dark Is Rising series. I don't do fanfics on those, because they have a very final conclusion. I do think that there should be more Bran/Jane fanfics out there.

Lord of the Rings is the best movie(s) ever made.

I also do a few duo fanfics with MagicMagelet; she comes up with the idea, I write it down.

Mostly I go for romantic, sweet fanfics, but I've got one or two angsty ones out there. Those I write when I've had a terrible day at school and have way too much time on my hands.
