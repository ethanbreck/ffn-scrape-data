Glasses/contacts/NA: N/A

Powers- Terra-kinetic Body Enhancement: He absorbs earth and stone to augment his body.

Notes on Powers-

Masato possesses the ability to absorb solid matter, such as earth-related structures, substances and materials into his body, augmenting his physical strength and making his skin harder than steel. When using his ability, fully, his skin turns completely black, his hair becomes sharp as needles, and his nails become sharp as tiger claws. At his maximum he can lift a M1 Abrams Tank (67.6 short tons) and his skin can't be pierced by Caliber .50, Armor-Piercing Round at point blank range. He's also immune to heat up to 1538 C (melting point of Iron)

Weakness: His power is partially active at all times, making it hard to control his strength and suffers from Congenital insensitivity to pain (CIP). He’s unable to swim and he weights eight times more than he should for a person his size. While he can jump long distances due to his immense strength, he also creates craters in the ground and holes in roofs. He’s also more vulnerable to magnetic fields than Wolverine’s metal-laced skeleton.

Quote "The average human has a density of 1.01 g/cm3, but my weight indicates a density of 7.874 g/cm3. I am like an Iron statue."

Age they got powers- Partial: Age 2 Full: 15

Personality- He a cold, logical person interesting in reading and gaining knowledge. He’s a Bibliophile - an obssessive lover of books. He loves Soccer, Reading and Chocolate. He likes Music, Science, Art, and MONEY. He’s ruthless, cunning, and almost amoral. He does possess a greedy side, which shows up occasionally. He will protect his friends, because they are HIS.

Family life- Where they were born/Where they grew up-

Masato’s grandfather, Masahiko Kimura (Age: 59 Born: Sapporo), is one of few sword smiths licenced to make Shinken, a Japanese term used to describe a Japanese sword that is sharp and used for real cutting or combat, as opposed to those that are blunted for other types of practice. He’s also a master of Shinkendo and Iaido. He’s a cold, commanding teacher whose presence demands respect. He also taught Masato for several years to learn discipline.

Masato’s father, Masaru Kimura (Age: 40 Born: Sapporo), is a geologist, metallurgist, and worked in the resource investigation department of his company. He’s a cold, logical man dedicated to his work.

Masato’s mother, Shion Kimura (Age: 36 Born: Sapporo), is a government employee dealing with foreign relations. She is half-Russian, which is how they explained Masato’s whitening hair during his childhood. She’s kind hearted to Masato, but ruthless during business.

Masato’s little sister, Masami Kimura (Age 13 Born: Sapporo) is a hyper-active emotional tomboy. She’s proven incapable of learning cooking, flower arrangement, or anything tradition talent of a woman. Instead, she into violent sports, manga, and shopping. What scares Masato is she might also be a latent mutant.

Clothing Preference-

Beach/swim wear- White shirt with Japanese flag on front and black shorts.

Sleep wear- Black shirt, shorts, and sandals. Occasionally, wears large bath robe.

Athletic wear- When training, he wears a white headband with kanji for strength, black shirt, black pants, black sneakers with special ankle weights, and fingerless black gloves with special wrist weights.

Winter Wear- Black shirt, black pants, and black snow boots. He also wears his usual Consadole Sapporo jacket.

Uniform - Black shirt, black pants, black steel-tipped boots with buckles, and black fingerless gloves. He also wears a black belt with a yellow Mitsudomoe (three joined tomoe) buckle and a black jacket with Consadole Sapporo (a Japanese professional football (soccer) club) symbol on the back.

Pet Peeves- He hates Prejudice, Stupidity, and Politics. He also hates Cigarettes and Alcohol. Not an easy think to hate since Sapporo is famous for it’s alcohol.

Sexuality/preference- Heterosexual - Masato likes women.

Relationship History- Follows 1-2-7 Rule so will only date between 15-18. He’s only been on a couple of dates with the daughters of his dad’s co-workers. He wasn’t really interested in a committed relationship.

The one thing that makes them happy- Masato loves to Read. He’s a Bibliophile and would love to spend his days reading books.

Who they have a crush on (optional)- N/A

How they relate to other people- He’s not very social, but once a person becomes his friend, he with defend them to his death and Masato is very possessive of his friends.

Is good at- He reads speaks Japanese, Russian, Mandarin Chinese, and English fluently. He's currently learning to speak Spanish (Castilian)..He’s good at research, math, and science. He’s good at tutoring others and has skills in martial arts - such as kenjutsu, jujutsu, and Russian Sambo.

Loves to- Read, Run, Mountain Climb, and Free Running

Fears- He’s scared of deep water due to his inability to swim. He had Aquaphobia (severe fear of water) when he was younger. He’s embarrassed by his severe Bibliophilia (love of books) and is very protective of his personal collection.

Favorite;

Music artist- JAM Project and Yuki Kajiura. He hates American Pop.

Clothing- He likes dark clothing and his Consadole Sapporo black jacket is very important to him. It’s important enough that he had it specially treated. (So it doesn’t get destroyed like Superboy’s jacket always did.)

Possession- His most important possession other than his Consadole Sapporo jacket is a collection of rare books including some books which cost more than an average car.

Person- Tony Stark - American Industrialist

Movie- Gojira/Godzilla

Food- Miso Ramen (miso-based ramen noodles). Sapporo is known as the birthplace of miso ramen.

Drink- Flavored Milk.

Place- Library

Hobbies- Hiking, and Running.

Whats there job in the group- He’s a strong man and defender. He wont charge to attack an enemy, but he will prevent an enemy from escaping. He’s there when a teammate needs a shield or powerful backup.

Extras- Alignment: Lawful- Neutral. He doesn’t believe in Xavier’s human/mutant utopia dream or Magneto’s human/mutant domination war. He believes some mutants should be registered due to the danger they pose to others, but it should be mutants doing the registering since the majority of the Government can’t be trusted. He will fight to defend his friends and family, but not to be a Hero.
