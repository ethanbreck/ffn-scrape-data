Title: Lord Nosferatu

Former Alias: Wolfey

Age: 19

Location: Houston, TX

Hello everyone. If you're reading this, then that means you either clicked me by accident, you know me, or you actually like my stories, for some odd reason. I am perhaps one of the most unreliable authors on Fanfiction.net, simply because I spend more of my time reading and planning stories than I do actually writing. I'm extremely proficient at grammar, syntax, and diction, as well as familiar with various writing styles for different genres and age groups. As such, I am available as a Beta Reader for stories that fit at least one of the following guidelines:

1. Authored or Co-Created by a personal friend of mine.

2. In the Universe of Harry Potter, Naruto, Sonic The Hedgehog, NiGHTS into Dreams, Watchmen, Batman, Spider-Man, Mega Man, Devil May Cry, Kingdom Hearts, Legend of Zelda, Pokemon, Outlaw Star, Final Fantasy VIII, Dragonball Z, Air Gear, or History's Strongest Disciple Kenichi.

3. Under 10,000 Words or Over 100,000 Words.

I am working on several writing projects right now to be posted. However, because I am a perfectionist, I keep going back and changing them, retconning my own ideas and plot developments. This is the true reason why there have been no updates to any of my stories.

My Current Fanfics Are:

RedRose (NiGHTS into Dreams) (Started 2000)

Alternate Universe where NiGHTS is a male agent. He and his sister act to protect their city by using using equipment and weaponry supplied by the Twin Towers Association, whilst traveling to different zones that resemble video game level cliches. This was supposed to be a comedy/action fic, that was neither well thought out nor developed. This is definitely in Development Hell, and will most likely be the last story that I finish.

Chronos (Chrono Cross) (Started 2000)

An unofficial sequel to Chrono Cross and a continuation of the Chrono Trigger series. Cast is largely original creations, but several of the original main characters in Chrono Cross will return in various forms throughout the story, including Serge, Kid, Glenn, Norris, Orlha, and Harle. The setting is a several centuries after the events of Chrono Cross, in a developed El Nido Archipelago. Human and Demi-Human interactions are strained, with the majority Full-Humans exacting political and social discrimination against Demi-Humans in a more modern and apparent fashion. Beneath the shallow surface lies to powerful organizations battling against one another throughout various landscapes: The Syndicate- a malicious organization that uses deception, theft and violence to influence media, and politicians to exact control over the newly democratized Zeal Republic and to subjugate the Demi-Human Subspecies into slavery and eventual extinction. The C.O.R.D. - The Corps of Radical Dreamers are a benign militaristic group created in opposition to The Syndicate outlawing of Pro-Demi organizations. They are outside of the law, yet have their allies in various industries and locations. CORD recruits young demi-humans and full human allies through their schools and community outreach programs. Their existence is quite well known throughout the land as opposed to the almost ghostly presence of the Syndicate. Two members of CORD, Kage and Xang get caught up in the Syndicate's greatest plot: to fill the gap made by FATE's destruction. By gathering the six keys that unlock the Gate of Time, the Syndicate will be able to retrieve the Frozen Flame, with the power to devolve all Demis into Full-Humans. In order for Kage to stop them, he will have to gather a team of heroes from all the elements, and steal the keys from their resting places before the Syndicate does. But the Syndicate already has their own team ready and trained, led by two masters of the rare new elemental innates: Glass and Steel.

This is my masterpiece work, and I've already dedicated almost a decade to it's making. Extensive planning, editing, and revising have been done, and I've posted the first seven chapters on Fanfiction.net, as a teaser. Those chapters however are rifled with problems and are completely unedited. The complete story will have significant changes, as must be due to my writing ability's maturation. The challenge for myself in this work is to keep all the elements of the Chrono Cross video game, even leveling up, but to redefine them into a more believable and explainable form for a novel. How the story of Serge and the original cast fit into this new world will be played out through flashbacks, therefore there are two stories being told simultaneously. There are plenty of new ideas added to the universe including: New Boss Monsters, New spells, New Special Techniques, New Weapons, New Fighting Styles, New Monsters, New Areas, New Characters, New Technology, New Religions. As far as story rating goes. it will be rated M for Graphic Violence, Sexual Trauma, Swearing, Intimacy, Abuse, Torture, and Death. There will also be elements of Yaoi, Yuri, Perversion, Taboos, Racism, Discrimination, Slavery, Misogyny, Misanthropy, Cross-Dressing, Masturbation, Drug Abuse, Infidelity, and more. I push the boundaries in this one as I believe that in order to make a believable world, you have to have even the things that society likes to turn away from. There are no Gary Stus and Mary Sues in this tale. The Good Guys aren't perfect, and the Bad Guys aren't complete evil. Even Serge and the original characters will have various character flaws in order to make them more believable beings. People will be hurt, the team will suffer, there will be casualties, and main characters can and will die. I will not change the story in any way unless I feel the desire to do so, though I will read any and all reviews and will respond to them if requested. To put it succintly, I write stories for me, because the stories I want to read, haven't been written, so I have to do ti myself. This is a Fanfiction, for the fan who is writing it. Everyone else, comes second.

When six people banded together to infiltrate the Department of Mysteries, everything changed. The world learned of Voldemort's return. The Ministry began to act. People began to panic. However, it's all too obvious that Voldemort would never stop attacking Harry and his friends, never give them time to grow stronger. He'd hunt them until he knew they were all dead. So Dumbledore hatches a plan to do just that. This idea was born after reading literally hundreds of Harry Potter Fanfics, cursing J.K. Rowling to hell for the bullshit final two books, and hating with all my heart the combat in all of the Harry Potter video games. I dislike the idea that there is no institution of higher learning in the Wizarding World, because there is such an infinite amount of things to learn and do with magic. Why is the combat so bland, with only a handful of spells available for dueling? This story tries to rectify the flaws of the Harry Potter Universe, while expanding it's scope, and making it much more mature and action-infused. I have created over seventy new spells, a scientific system for spell casting, twenty-six university courses for the team to learn from during the two years they'll be training, Harry and his friends aren't going to be fighting in every little battle, they're specifically after Voldemort and his inner circle. So, I had to actually detail the Law Enforcement branch of the ministry, Patrollers are wizard police, whilst Hit Wizards are the equivalent to SWAT. Aurors are the Military Counter-Terrorists, with Unspeakables acting as Ministry Agents akin to FBI/CIA/MI6. This story will also finally detail the International Confederation of Wizards, various magical schools around the world, Dueling Competitions, and classes of wizards such as: warlocks, sorcerers, magicians, mages, conjurors, etc. Each character will learn to use and specialize in various skills including all of the following: Wandless Magic, Silent Magic, Elemental Magic, Healing Magic, Dark Curses, Apparition, Runes, Alchemy, Potions, Animagus, Transfiguration, Beastspeaking, Telekinesis, Telepathy, Psychometry, Legilimancy, Occlumency, Rituals, Channeling, Physical Assault, Combinations, Traps, Stealth.

Ideya (Nights Into Dreams)

Dawn Wizeman is one of the heirs to the Wizeman Corporation, famously created and run by her grandfather Methuselah Wizeman. Wizeman Corp. is famous for its Intrinsic Dream Exploration software, which gave psychiatrists the power to treat patients with psychosis stemming from mental disorders and illnesses by projecting themselves into their dreams and correcting the mental flaws from within. This 'corrective' procedure became so popular amongst patients and criminals that the Wizeman Corporation is planning to mass market the software so that common people may deal with minor woes and experience lifelike fantasies through dream manipulation. Every person who has the procedure has the blueprint of their mind copied into an I.D.E.Y.A. or Intrinsic Dream Exploration Yearly Assessment, which allows specialist to repair any damage that they cause whilst performing the procedure. They are color coded according to general occupations and character models. Both Dawn and her brother, Dysmas, are considered Reds, which beyond being the rarest of all color codes, is unusual for those slated to be in command of a service industry. Dawn, who after the age of 10 never applied for another I.D.E.Y.A. to be performed is fine with this, as she plans on joining state law enforcement and hav been training for such with the Wizeman Corporation's own elite security: The Nightmares. They have been working with local law enforcement in an experimental program that uses I.D.E.Y.A.s to cross reference cases with potential suspects that fit a psychological profile, and use such knowledge to anticipate criminal activity. Things go sour during such a case, involving several missing people between the ages of 10-28. The attack are happening too quickly, and both regular law enforcement and the Nightmares seem completely ineffective. The Wizeman Corporation begins lobbying the state executives to make the I.D.E.Y.A. mandatory for all citizens, in order to provide more complete background information to be used with crime investigations. Dawn, who finds this fundamentally against the Right to Privacy speaks up, but finds in disturbing how many people within the Wizeman Corporation are for this legislation. After sneaking into the IDEYA records, she finds that every authority lobbying for this legislation were all previous patients. Her brother Dysmas, the vice president of the company, explains to her that he and their grandfather Methuselah plan to use the hidden mental persuasion aspect of the IDEYAs to gather greater power and control the vote. Passing of the legislation would mean that the entire state would soon be under Wizeman's control, allowing them to rule as they see fit. She threatens to expose them, but they call in the Nightmares to indoctrinate her using the IDEYAs. Their leader Jackal, rapes and tortures her physically, before continuing the assault in her mind using the technology, however she escapes using her fresh traumatic memory, and manages to mentally harm him. When scientist Eliot Edwards helps her escape and brings her back to his house, she notices a book called 1001 Arabian NiGHTS, with a purple robed court jester on the front. Her 10 year old self emerges from her mind and imitates this character. Dawn takes on the name NiGHTS and vows to stop her family, and the Nightmares from taking over the state.

All characters from the first NiGHTS into Dreams game will appear in this novella. It will probably be a short novel, nowhere near epic. There will be graphic depictions of rape, murder, child abuse, sodomy, incest, and might have some politics thrown into it. This is not a kid story, it is a hopefully realistic depiction of the Nights Universe in our world.
