Author has written 5 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Legend of Zelda, and Final Fantasy X-2.

I have written a few fics throughout the years, most of them pretty average, and then in the middle of my high school career I made a major move and lost interest in many things due to a mild bout with depression. Unfortunately one of my lost passions was the art of fanfiction writing. But do not despair for I have returned to the living and breathing world fanfiction and plan on writing to my heart's (as well as yours) content.

The only fics I will be posting right now are the ones which I completed. Please keep in mind that I was in the eighth-tenth grade when I wrote them. My most recent project, Redemption for a Weary Soldier, is my current work-progress.

At the moment my current preference of fanfiction lies in the Inuyasha category, but I haven't watch enough of the series just yet to feel confident enough to write a competent piece on the matter. I have only recently got over an obssession of the game Final Fantasy X2, and having clocked in well over 150+ hours on the game, I feel confident enough to pull off a story about it.
