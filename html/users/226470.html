Well, this is where you can find a little bit about me. It's not much, but it's something...

Name: Darien
Location: Michigan
Gender: Male

I'm rather happy at the fact that "Stormbringer" has taken off quite well. It's not as easy as one might think as far as combining these two universes.

You may wonder why someone would be doing this? Well, I have a lot of free time, not much else to do. Plus I want to help entertain you, the people. Also, many of you have noticed I keep making slight changes to what I've already completed. I can answer that easily... Through research & some math, I begin to get a clue or two as to what's, what... Am I tired of fixing it, yeah & I'm hoping I'm done for the time being. Unless the writers of the manga stuff starts to change the names of places & people. Instead of Edward & Alphonse, they change them to Bob & Steve...

After doing some surfing, I found a website that has 3D animated transformers & their transformations, Megatron to Optimus Prime & even Thunderwing from the Stormbringer series. So, I'm going to do a little selfless promoting for someone I don't know, enjoy.
