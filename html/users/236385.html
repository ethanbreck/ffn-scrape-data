Welcome all to the forbidden lair of Anne (MaboroshiTsuki) and (Ironi) Numair, it rhymes! We live near each other, by a road. Can you guess where we live??? We've known each other since we were really young, lived across the street from each other. We have interesting senses of humor and are pretty anti-social, but hey, that's okay!

Anime and manga are a high source of entertainment for us. ^_^ We love all sorts of different series (for example: Escaflowne (obviously); Generator Gawl; Orphen; Inu Yasha; Saiyuki; etc etc). Anne enjoys girl oriented manga/anime while Numair tends to enjoy the guy oriented stuff (so you see a wide variety in our fics).

Our Escaflowne Fics:
-A Little Ryuu-jin: A parody of 'A Little Princess' with Escaflowne characters. (Ch. 3 started)
-The Pyro of Notre Dame: A parody of 'The Hunchback of Notre Dame' with Esca characters. (Ch. 7 posted)
-A Guy in Gaea: Humor fic, Hitomi and Van have a son named Ban. (Ch. 2 started)
-Mary Sue Saves Gaea With Her Pinky!: One with no plot, another statement of ours. (Ch. 9 posted)
-Numair in Love: Numair goes to Gaea and does "normal" people reaction to it. (Ch. 2 posted)
-The Even Odder Couple: Allen, the Duke, and Chid... sitcom style! (Ch. 1 posted)
-Escalator: The Esca. movie and series mixed together, takes place in a mall! (Ch. 4 posted)
-Bitter Much?: Our attempt at Van's POV for each episode (Ch. 2 posted).
-Compact Disk: One shot. Where DO our lost CDs go?
-The Vanpire: Parody of vampires. Van, the vampire, seeks virgin blood. Blah!

Our Dragonlance Fics:
-A Night of Hot Steamy Passion!: A humor fic where we do yaoi pairings. (Ch. 3 posted).
-All My Magi: We felt cynical, par usual. (Intro. posted)

Currently: Despite lack of updates and rumor we're gone (dashing the hopes of millions) we're around and writing in secret... :p Life has been really hectic for both of us but we *do* have updates on the computer, waiting... hoping... for the day they'll be posted. ^_~

We love to joke about life, thus we write parodies. :) We have lots of ideas but do not plan on writing most of them until we get some of the above finished. ^_^; We have this monster project for the Escaflowne section that we are devoting endless research to and such, AND it's not a parody! It's a, gulp, series fic! O_o Our main goal with our fics is to amuse ourselves and hopefully make people laugh in the process. We realize that our humor can be a little "out there", but hey, people need to be able to laugh at themselves. Mary Sue probably got a lot of people mad at us but like we said, just laugh at us! We do it all the time. :)

We both in school thus don't have that much time... plus we're busy being our weird selves half the time. :p But yes... read, enjoy, and... er... prosper?
