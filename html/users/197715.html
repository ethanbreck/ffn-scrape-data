Undergoing updates.

I'm a Transformers 2007 fan:
Favorite Autobots: Optimus and Ratchet
Favorite Decepticons: Starscream and Barricade

I've put all my other stories on hold to work on my very first Transformers Arc. The title of the arc is "Duex Mondes", which
means "Two Worlds" in French. I originally only planned for four stories for this arc, but recent developments in the storyline
helped be decide to add another three stories to the arc. This arc is constantly evolving, so check back often to see what
new developments are happening.

Credits: I got the idea for my profile from Blackwing.Rose, whose insight and constructive critisism have helped me
out alot since I started writing in the Transformers universe. Her profile is my inspiration for doing this.

In-Progress:

Autobot in Shining Armor
Summery: After the events of Mission City everything returned to some form of normalcy. Maggie's role in
everything is all but forgotten by the world. Then her apartment is broken into and her world is violently shifted
to accommodate a new guardian, Optimus Prime
Pairing(s): Optimus/Maggie (centric), Sam/Mikaela
Notes: First story in the Deux Mondes storyline.
UPDATE: Chapter 11 is now up!

To Protect and Serve
Summery: After a near fatal encounter with the Autobots, Barricade is left with wounds that could result in his death.
Unable to transform, closing in on critical system failure, something saves him. He comes back online to find his injuries
completely healed with no idea as to how it happened. As he searches for the answer to this he learns the one thing that
he never thought he'd find himself learning: the true meaning of To Protect and Serve.
Pairing(s): Barricade/OC (centric), Optimus/Maggie, Sam/Mikaela
Notes: Second story in the series. Takes place in the same frame of time as AiSA, starting from the events of Chapter Seven.

Duex Mondes is actually broken down into two parts.

Autobot in Shining Armor (in-progress)

Healer (planning stages)
