Author has written 6 stories for Ranma, and Rurouni Kenshin.

I love Rurouni Kenshin, and can't get enough of reading fanfics. I haven't really been able to get into much other anime other than Fushigi Yuugi, they just don't make em like they used to I guess. I've written four Kenshin stories, and one for Ranma (which is discontinued).

I really do apologize to everyone for leaving some of these stories unfinished for so long, but I really want to start cranking out the chapters like once a week or two.

Progress Report:

Battousai: 40% complete. I am writing chapters for this story about once every three weeks or so.

Reality: 90% complete. I have a little bit of writers block with this story, so it might be a while before I write a new chapter. I have to make sure the ending is good, I don't want to disappoint anyone. Actually, I just started re-reading this, and I think the writing is definitely not up to par with what I want. So, I am in the middle of a re-write for this story. I think I am going to change the title too because it doesn't really seem to fit with the story. (Hey, this was my first fic that I started writing in 2001 when I was a junior in high school, so lol I guess my writing style has changed, but that's to be expected.)

If I believe: 30% complete. I am currently writing chapters for this story.

Making Lemonade: 10% complete. I am currently writing chapters for this story. This story is loosely based on things that have happened to me in my life, and I am actually surprised that people seem to be very interested in it (jumps for joy). So, thanks and happy reading. :)

Thank you to everyone who has taken the time to view my profile and review my stories. Readers really have no idea how much it means to the author to get that little message in their inbox saying they got a review. It can really make your day :)
