BIO:
The name's Lily, I'm 16 and I'm an American living in Germany. It's okay here, but somebody needs to open a 24 hour 7/11 before I die. I love writing and right about now fanfiction is the only thing I can do. So I do it. I also play soccer, about the only other thing I do.

I've been here for a while, since I was 13 or 14, and people don't see that reviewing and even flaming help an author out. I reread all that I write and it's amazing how much being here and having people read your stuff brings out in your writing. Style and everything that go with writing are brought out, so don't be mad at people who are young and are a little rusty around the edges. Help them out. Telling them to suck your dick because their taking up space with their prepuberty trash doesn't help, no matter how desperate you are to get something. So review, the button's there for a reason.
