Name: I have no name.

Age: Unknown

Gender: Guess

Most common words people have discribed me with: Mysterious and Sophisticated.

What I want people to think of my stories: The stories I write, I wish to make a deep impact on people from what little moral I twist into them. The most valuable reviews I treasure are when people realize how stories can be good even though they don't follow the usual trend, have graphic sex, excess language, or yaoi, etc...

What I wish my stories would do for people: I hope it would inspire them in some way to do good for other people and the world. I also hope that they'll allow readers to discover a different point of view other then what they've been seeing.

The sad thing: My writings are not that good, so I really doubt the last two things I put on my bio would be into effect.

The Helpful Criticism I Get The Most: Well, it's definitely on getting my tenses right about switching from present to the past. I am an US Citizen but I wasn't born in the United States, so I'm trying my best. It would be really helpful to tell me exactly how I should prevent that instead of just pointing it out. (Because sometimes I have no idea how to revise it...)

Last thing I said before I updated my bio: How do you upload a new story?

Anyway: I am a Christian and will not write stories over PG, shounen-ai, yaoi, lemon, or graphic sex scenes and language. Most of the stories I put up will be completed. And by the way, if anyone is wondering, I always like to put a summery or prologue in the beganning of the story of what the story is going to about because my stories are usually long. Hope all you writers and readers out there would enjoy my stories. And if you have any flames, please e-mail me instead of putting it on the reviews. Thanks!
