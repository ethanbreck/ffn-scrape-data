A little about me if you're interested

My name is Yume (on the internet.) 30-something, USA, she/her. I believe in God and also science. I feign under-confidence to hide my arrogance, but underneath that is some actual under-confidence. (Like pie?) My favorite color is red. I use too many parentheses. These facts are in no particular order.

Here you'll find mostly Sailor Moon fics (manga version!) with one ridiculous Zelda epic in the works. I have a bad habit of making plots way too complex, and then losing track of them. Time-travel angst is my favorite genre. I'm learning to walk the line between not enforcing/reinforcing oppressive dynamics in fiction and sugarcoating to the point where any serious discussion becomes impossible. Please let me know if you think something needs to be tagged.

Reviews: Love! (Who doesn't?)
Critique: Very much love!
Fic recs: Also love!
Requests: Sorry, not now, I've got a backlog a mile long.

Hope you find something you like!

Love to read

Fantasy epics, fluffy one-shots, and romance as long as it's somewhat realistic and not overly sappy. Well-written villain redemption. Canon-based, or at least canon-compliant. Regency or Victorian era settings because Pride and Prejudice. Also because Merry Faerie's Of Good Ton and its sequel The Dearest Rose.

Sailor Moon: Neo/chibi senshi stories, even though I've seen a lot of really bad ones. Currently reading Crystalmoon.Silverlight's My Shitennou which has an interesting premise and lots of heartwarmingly adorable OC kids. Hino Rei family angst--if you like this too, look up Heavenly Pearl's stories. You won't regret it. Genderfluid or feminine Three Lights.

Legend of Zelda: OoT setting, Twilight Princess setting, most games in the "Downfall" timeline. (I'm a timeline believer!) Mildly angsty Link. Ganon origin stories, Ganondorf/OC pairings, Ganondorf in general. Nabooru. Anything with a lot of world-building. Stories where Zelda is the main character and goes on a quest. Yunobo and Teba, the most underappreciated champions. Zelda and Malon friendship.

Pretear: Platonic Himeno/Mayune or Himeno/Mawata, but I can't find any.

OTPs because I'm a bit of a pairing snob. Please forgive me.

Sailor Moon: Usagi/Mamoru, Haruka/Michiru, Chibiusa/Helios... I'm so conventional. One-sided Seiya/Usagi. Luna/Artemis... does anybody actually write Luna/Artemis? Guardian senshi/Shitennou (including the rarely-sighted Ami/female!Zoisite), Star Fighter/Kakyuu, Hotaru/Perle. Yes, that last one makes no sense.

Legend of Zelda: Link/Zelda, forever and ever, even in Twilight Princess. Nabooru/Impa. Occasionally enjoy Shad/Ashei. I will admit to seeing the charm in Malink and Midlink... but I've been a Zelinker since the 90's and it's a hard habit to break, so please excuse me (princess.)

Evangelion: Shinji/Rei Asuka/Rei. I don't change my mind often but the fandom won me over.

Pretear: Hayate/Himeno, Sasame/Takako

How I Met Your Mother: Robin/Barney (check out up to the moon by moeten!)

Princess Tutu: Ahiru/Fakir, Rue/Mytho

Don't really like to read

"Harem" stories, "character's friends betray them for no reason" stories, Mid to late 20th century settings, stories where everybody dies at the end. Most crossovers.

Sailor Moon: Male Three Lights, especially when used to make Usagi/Seiya a straight couple. PGSM-verse, I don't know why.

Legend of Zelda: Modern AU, stories where Sheik is not actually Zelda, Ilia hate fics (please stop), ultra-angsty Link, Tingle, Majora's Mask for some reason. Stories where Midna is able to come back from the Twilight Realm, even though I love Midna, because that renders her sacrifice kind of meaningless. Ganondorf/Nabooru oddly enough, feel like I really should like that pairing. Link/OC. Midna/Zant.

...but I'll read the first chapter of almost anything, because you never know.

Current projects

Completely rewriting Fragile Flower so it doesn't suck Or rather, writing a completely different story that happens to include a few OCs and subplots vaguely resembling ones in Fragile Flower. Mostly outlined, a few chapters posted. Stealing most of my attention at the moment.

Finishing Crystal Generation. Halfway there. I am really bad about updating this but I'm going to finish it!

A fanfic-of-fanfic based on the gorgeous doujinshi Future Reunion by mangakachan. (I'm so late on this! Sorry!)

Continuing the Gens Una Sumus series?

Or What You Will, a SM fic based on Twelfth Night. Outlined, shelved for now due to lack of time.
