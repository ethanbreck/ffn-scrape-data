Hi everyone!
Wow, I doubt someone will ever read this, but oh well...

I am a 22 year old girl from Germany who is obviously unable to update stories quickly. This is due to lotsa work and a constant lack of ideas. Besides that, in the last two years, I started writing books in German in my free time, meaning not much activity here anymore.

While writing lots of stupid OOC-stories in my early years of writing, I am now mainly writing parodies and humor fanfics. If I am in the mood, I also write Darkfics.

I always try to write proper English (including grammar), but I don't have English lessons anymore. I proofread three or four times before posting a fanfic, but a few grammatical mistakes are most likely bound to occur. Sorry if this bothers someone.

Every writer puts lots of work into each fanfic he or she creates and therefore loves reviews. That's why I encourage everyone to spend a few minutes to write a review for a story on which the author might have spend hours or even days - not only for my stories, but for other fanfics, too. Constructive criticism is always welcome, as well as praise. Please don't flame.

Thank you for reading!

~Sheba

Special Note: For those who are reading my older stories (those with the terrible English) you might notice that I have changed my Username from Faria to Sheba. At the end of some stories, I also put an email-adress. Some of those still work, some don't. If you want me to get a mail, please use the one from this profile. Sorry for the inconvenience.
Another Special Note: I've already gotten a few mails that people don't understand my Slayers-fanfictions which aren't parodies. If you are one of them: If there is a mysterious person and you can't figure out who it is: (This is your last chance not to spoiler yourself...) Without knowing you, I can nearly assure you that it's either Shabranigdo or Cephied. There is just one exception, in all other Slayers-fanfiction, you will find a god somewhere hidden in the story. That's because I'm obsessed with them. Go Shabby! Go Cephied!
