Greatings to everyone that might decide to visit me. I am called DarkLight, The Guardian of Hope and Light. Surprisingly, there is another that also uses the title of Guardian of Hope and Light, he is a fantastic Takari fanfic writer by the name of Ivan Ishara. Needless to say, I am an unwavering fan of TAKARI (Takeru and Hikari).

I've done it, I have completed the first chapter of my subfic: Digital MechWarriors. It is a fic using digimon characters based in the world of BattleTech. Of course there IS going to be Takari. Also Mimato, Taiora, and Kenlie.

About Me:
Age: 20
Gender: Male
Location: Indiana, USA
Personality: Shy (to the point of almost anti-social), I try to be as unbias as possible when making decissions, relatively cheerful, Loyal (almost to a fault it would seem)

Stories:
Digital Mechwarriors: A story about a meeting between 2 enemies and a story of forbidden love. Takari!! Based in the world of Mechwarrior with multiple twists in timeline and facts. I'd just like to say that my focus is on the Digimon characters, and since this is really just a side fic and it would take years longer to study up on Battletech, I will not focus on as much detail or "factual" information concerning the Battletech timeline or information. But please believe me when I say it really will be a great story...so long as people tell me how to improve my writing = ).

(Main fic, currently in development): Digimon N-D: A story that I have been formulating in my head since i saw episode 1 of season 2 back when it aired in the USA a few years ago. It deals with many of the issues I felt were incomplete in the series ex: Daemon, the dark ocean, Kari's powers, etc. But with many many twists developed by my demented mind. Wars between multiple dimensional plains. Unknown adversaries, soon to be awakened sleeping powers in all of the digidestined and their digimon. Links between all 4 seasons of digimon. Multiple wars and skirmishes. Death, love, faith, betrail. I hope it to be one of the greatest stories ever. Takari of course! Infact, UBBER TAKARI!!!! With many other pairings. I plan for it to be a multiple book fic, and with the way my outlines are currently looking it will be atleast 5 books.

Alright, Digital Mechwarriors is back up, and better than ever. Project Vortex has done some major editing work on chapter 1 and now I'm finally satisfied with it...sorry about it being down for so long.

On a side note: I've decided that I'm going to start keeping progress information for all of the stories I'm currently working on, as well as the expected completion date. I plan on updating it at least once every 1-2 weeks; if you notice something not progressing at a rate you would like, then please feel free to send me a threatening email and I'll see if I can kick my butt in gear. = )

I have a confession to make. I had just about lost faith in the Takari authors of fanfiction.net...I feared that they were just about all dead. However when I started skimming through fics today after a long, long, loooong leave of absence, I was greated by soo many new fics, big fics, huge fics, GREAT FICS, and even a new chapter to Kale's Once and For All book which I never saw before! BTW, if by chance Eowyn the Fair is reading this, I would like to thank you for posting the writings of the GREAT KALE!! Your the Best!
