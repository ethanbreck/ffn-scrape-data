Author has written 77 stories for Gundam Wing/AC.

Yes, I'm still around, though just barely. Real life has me hopping over hurdles, so there doesn't seem to be much time for writing. My beta reminded me that it's been ten years since she's started correcting my stories. Thanks Swordy for hanging in there with me. I've been typing out stories since before then, which adds up to a lot of time on Gundam Wing fanfiction. I'm still dabbling at it, when time allows, and one of my goals is to finish the Duo's Friendship Arch with the non-yaoi ending, which I've put off for far too long. The fandom has diminished greatly, so there's probably very few who've read that series. Still, I don't want to leave it unfinished. I'm also working on finishing up several long stories in my files, but at the rate I'm going it's going to be a long time before they're done. I've enjoyed writing these stories and reading comments by the readers, almost all of which have been positive. Thanks to all who have supported me one way or another. You've inspired me and helped me in ways you'll never know.
