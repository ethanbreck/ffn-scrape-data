Hi! My name is Melissa. I'm a 15-year-old girl who lives in the United States. I'm in 10th grade, and I'm homeschooled. I love creative writing, and I love The Lord of the Rings, so that's why I'm here. Lets see... what can I tell you about me? Not that you -care- or anything... ;)

Hobbies: Reading/writing/obsessing about LOTR, watching Spongebob Squarepants, playing piano and guitar, and soccer, listening to my music of course!

Fav. LOTR characters: All of them! Frodo, Sam, Merry, Pippin, Aragorn, Boromir, Faramir, Legolas, Éowyn, and definately Gollum! But if I must choose...Aragorn, because he's awesome in both the books and the movie :)

How did I come up with my pen name?
I USED to be Melissa Kat, but that was only cuz I couldn't think of anything more creative at the moment. Now my pen name is Chai Lattè, because I love chai lattès! Chai is a sweet, spicy tea that people drink in India and Africa and places like that, and it's the yummiest drink on the face of the earth! Go get a chai at Starbucks! ;)

My friends and I have a "fan club". (Yeah, it sounds kinda lame calling ourselves a fan club...we're trying to come up with a name!) We all loooooove fan fiction and a few of us are registered here. (Elessariel's and EowynThePyro's stories...and uh, gosh my other friend changes her pen name so much I don't know what to call her! Uh, they're all on my favorite authors list anyway)

I make sure that my stories have good spelling and grammar...all that fun stuff. I find well written stories more enjoyable, and unlike some authors, I care about the quality of my writing. =)

I am not like some people who are able to update their stories every 20 minutes, and I do take quite a while to update my stories. A long while. Weeks. Months. Uh, actually That one story it's even been a year since I updated...heh heh...oops.

Please keep in mind that I do NOT write slash, and try to avoid reading it at all costs. (It would be helpful if people who DO write slash would warn us! Don't you just hate it when you begin to read a story that you think is good, and you find out is slash? I do!) I fail to see why people find slash even remotely entertaining, because it's not. Homosexuality is wrong, period. End of discussion.

Another type of story that I don't write: Mary-Sues. Yuck. Some are pretty good, but it gets old after Mary-Sue #29856938, if you know what I mean. And some of them are just...ugh.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
If you're bored, check out my favorites list. Have fun!

Melissa
