Yes, I'm still alive, just taking a break from FF.net for a while. I'm currently writing a novel, which I must finish by the end of March, and it's kind of squeezing all my writing juices dry. I'm trying to still write short stories and poetry, but it's difficult. So yes, I'm still here. Don't despair. I know how to update. :)

Oh yes, and please visit my site: www.skyra.tk. If at least 30 people don't visit in the next 90 days, they'll take my precious domain away from me. My precious ... there's not much there, since it's new, but all you have to do is click on the link, and you'll be doing me a huge favor. I'll love you forever, promise.

On a side note, I love LotR, even to the point of obsession ... especially when it comes to Frodo! ^_^ I'm a sophomore in high school who wants to be an author, has a thing for dragons, and absolutely loves life to death.

Things I want to write:

- The Journey of Runes
- My new novel, the Rune of Kirindar
- Optimistic poetry
- More short stories
- A LotR fanfic (but there's so many out there!)

That's all for now ... I love you all!
