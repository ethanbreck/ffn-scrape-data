Okay, about me. My name is Katy. Destiny is an original character I wrote in my first fanfiction penned with my best friend Ivydraken. The "hazy" is addition by me as I'm not really certain, at this time, where life is taking me. I'm just living one day at a time. I'm 31 years old, and I live in Holly Springs, NC. I am, however, a born and raised Jersey girl, and am looking for a job that will take me back up north. I graduated from St. Andrews Presbyterian College in 2009 with a major in Religious Studies and a minor in psychology. That alone should tell you I really love messing with my characters' heads! One day I hope to go to seminary and go into Youth Ministry - but for now I'm just attempting to get a job (which is a 24/7 job in itself)! I haven't written in a long time, besides jotting down things in my head. With moving from college into the real world, it's been crazy. However, now I'm finding the time to get back to the things that I love.

My passion is the Power Rangers. My favorite realms like in the original time line - MMPR through Space, but I also love Time Force and DinoThunder. I'm believe in love despite the gender, if I see two souls that connect I will put them together. That means you will come across some same sex couples, mainly Jason/Tommy and Wes/Eric - two couples who I believe are truly perfect together. However, my slash stories are labeled, so if you're uncomfortable with males expressing love to people who don't look as great in pink as Kimberly and Jen, don't read them!!

Thank you all so much for your support and infinite patience through the years. But for now - I'm back (don't know where life is gonna throw me, but I'm gonna be working hard on at least finishing if not posting more)!! Keep checking up on me!!
