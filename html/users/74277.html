Hi, I'm Alex (or Alexandra, if you like). I am a New Yorker with a passion for writing and reading Harry Potter fanfics. I like Harry/Ginny, Ron/Hermione, and I boo or am indifferent to most everything else, unless the fic is really really good writing, 'cuz then the pairing matters a bit less. Warning: The Stupid Story of Stupidity is NOT to be read by people who will take it seriously. It's not anything like my other fics, it's just that one day I thought so hard about a good plot for a skillfully written story that would earn me lots of good reviews that I just gave up and went insane. And this fic is my insane spawn. Jessi-Thank you so much for pushing, shoving, forcing, threatening me to continue Alternate Identity. You make me proud of it. Updates with me can be slow, but now that I'm on vacation, maybe they'll come more frequently.
