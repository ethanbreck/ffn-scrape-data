Welcome to the Bio. I can warn you it will not be an interesting one because the girl it belongs to is not all that interesting.

She is a little (physically and perhaps emotionally) girl with a heart of diamond (hard, unbreakable, but always reflects different colors of light). She is overly poetic half the time. It really is just to creep people out, and it usually works.

She's a Harry Potter, Les Miserables, Fushigi Yuugi, Marmalade Boy, Phantom of the Opera, Inu Yasha, Ruroni Kenshin, Witch Hunter Robin, Lord of the Rings, and Kare Kano fan and reads fanfiction thereof. She is a chicklit, LM Montgomery, Betsy-Tacy, TS Eliot, and romance novel gobbler. A rabid fangirl and Broadway geek forever, don't you dare tell her Sir Percy Blakeney, Raoul de Chagny, Marius Pontmercy, Barney Snaith, Joe Willard, and Faramir do not exsist. They do. It's been proven.

She is highly picky, and really just an attention-whore and loves to love and be loved by people. Look around her fics, read a few, give her a review or two and she will love you forever.
