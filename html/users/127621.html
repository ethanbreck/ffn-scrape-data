Hello, I'm a perfectly abnormal 16-year-old human worm baby... I love to write fanficion, especially Invader Zim, and hope to become a profesional science fiction author someday.

Currently, my favorite topics to perseverate about are: commercialism and how it continues to ruin society, psychology (autism in particular), neurodiversity, the dangers of overly-prescribed psychiatric medications, physics, parapsychology, writing, drawing, reading, the stupidity of politics, the stupidity of people in general, existential questions, UFOs, synchronicity (I heart Carl Jung!), chemistry, mathematics (Thank you, Sir Isaac Newton; calculus is my friend), Johnny the Homicidal Maniac (if you don't know about Jhonen Vasquez--and I'm sure that everyone who reads my fics are familiar with him in SOME capacity...I hope--then go buy his comics and/or the Zim DVDs and action figures), and, what a surprise: INVADER ZIM!

I am a high-functioning autistic, possibly epileptic, and have at least atouch of just about any DSM-IV-TR psychiatric diagnosis you could name.

My fav shows are the following: INVADER ZIM, SIMPSONS, Twilight Zone, Futurama, South Park, Family Guy, Outer Limits, X-files, I love lucy

My favorite anime are: Evangelion, Marmalade Boy, Magic Knight Rayearth, Sailor Moon (NOT the dubbed version), Sorcerer Hunters ...and some other stuff.

My fav channels include: Sci-fi, Discovery, History, Also, did I mention I'm a huge fan of science fiction?

I recommend, of MY stories: I Have No Purpose, The Red Balloon ...and, some other stuff...maybe.

Right now I'm working on a finishing a couple of fics that have been in the good ol' OLD FILES FILE FOLDER for FAAAR too long and are FINALLY going to get finished...sometime. One is about Dib becoming psychotic and suicidal, called Solitary Problems. Another fic I'm working on is called Chemical Lobotomy, about Dib's struggles being put into psych. hospital and being force-fed
medications and suffering addiction to what he's. Yet another one called Epilepticcenters on Dib finding out he's epileptic and his struggles with no one caring about potential injuries he might incur and instead laugh at him while he has seizures (and that's TOTALLY not based onme AT ALL; ) ). I don't believe I've ever read a story about Dib--or any of the IZ characters--developing epilepsy, so hopefully it will get people's attention. I just love to torment my favorite two-dimensional obsession, don't I? Ah, well, maybe these stories will sensitize more fans to his plight.
