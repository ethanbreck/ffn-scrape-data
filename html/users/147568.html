AUTHOR UPDATE!*

Okay, thanks to one of my very close friends, Alonia Everclear, I have decided that it's time to get off my ass and actually write more in The Walls Between Us. The story has gone untouched since January 2003 and for that I am deeply sorry. I just lost my will to write it. But now...well now I'm leaving the first chapter up and rewriting the rest. My writing style has changed quite a bit, and there are A LOT of changes I want made. Don't worry for all you long time readers, it'll still be the same basic story that I started with, only...with a few more plot enhancements. ;) I'm excited about it, and I WILL finish this story. and when I do, it's on to Never Is A Promise. Yay! Thanks guys! --Braze

UPDATE January 10, 2005
Well...I said I was going to rewrite TWBU, and I will. But not today, nor tomorrow, nor next week. Believe it or not I have three chapters fully written. But it's not nearly as much as I want to have before I post it for reviews.

I'm not nearly as interested in my writing as I used to be. Most of my stuff has gone into my roleplaying, which takes quite a bit of my time. But I'm going to try and I promise I will write my epic Seiftis that I always wanted to have since I was 15. 15 years old...seems like such a long time ago. I'm really sorry to everyone waiting for me to write. But I have to do what I need to do for myself. I will get to my story one of these days. Once again...just not today. Best of luck to everyone in their writing. I'll be seeing you.
