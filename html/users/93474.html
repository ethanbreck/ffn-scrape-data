About Mintaka
Mintaka is an avid creator in many, many fields - from writing, to web & graphics, to art. Her muses simply do not let up. While she's been on a bit of a 'hiatus' from writing here at ff.net and in general, she hasn't stopped creating and thinking - and gertainly not gaming either. Her love for RPGs and Strat-RPGs knows few ends, and she's often plotting and scheming her way into a new game... even if she STILL has more to finish. whistles innocently Sometimes called 'muse' she's good for inspiring the worst ideas out of people without trying, and sparking twists unforseen. You can find her floating around the internet a lot - oftentimes on Gaia Online under the same name as here. (For those who know the site, 99 of her time is lost in Breedables/Changeable pets.)

She's re-gearing up to write again, after having simply lost her muse of focus on fics for awhile and had to suffer a string of life-incidents incluing seperation from the Navy (after a knee injury thatw as mistreated and now must deal with the results of that), and assorted random pitches of ill luck since then. But now she's rekindled the spark fully, and wishes to resume.
