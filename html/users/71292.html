Nothing Is Fair In Love And War: This is a Gundam Wing fanfiction that I started in 8th grade, but hadn't really typed it until I was a freshman in high school. I finished the first part, but now I want to redo the whole thing, so bear with me. It's a story about a ninja, named Kasen (in Japanese it means Fire Arrow), who arrives with a gundam. Right off the bat it becomes obvious that she has a deep connection with Heero, but no one knows really what it is. However, Kasen isn't the only ninja in town. Taki, her arch rival, shows up, out to get revenge. There is also Mokurei (in Japanese it means Silent Bow Mm..bow...arrow. Coincidence?), who despises Kasen for leaving the temple and betraying him. To make things worse, the Gundam pilots and Kasen keep being attacked by mysterious machines called Komotos. Who is Taki and Mokurei really working for? Why does Kasen care so much about Heero? And what will become of the intrigued stare between Kasen and Duo? Find out in this tangled story of war, revenge, and love.

These other stories are ones that are posted at fictionpress.com

Heaven's Earth Book 1: The Legend: This one is about a sorceress named Sophita, who one day, loses control of her power and destroys a whole city! A collective of clerics decide that as punishment she was to be encased in an icy glass prison. A century later, a dark prince named Lucifer, (that's right I named him after satan. What can I say? It is an awesome name!) discovers her and wishes to set her free so she can aid him in his conquest for global domination. But when Sophita is freed, she remembers nothing of her evil deed, and has no desire to take over the world. The rest of the story ia about her adventures as she tries to find out what really happened to her town and if she is really the one to blame. This one will probably end up being rated R for heavy violence and sexual situations, but it will still have its cute moments. I plan to spend a lot of time on it because I really feel like this could be the best story I have ever written!

Wow. I typed a lot more than I planned to. Sorry about that. Well I would like to leave you with this one thought. "Just remember that in the end, everything tastes like chicken, and chicken...tastes good." - (Adult Swim).
