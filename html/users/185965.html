Author has written 22 stories for Shakespeare, Dungeons and Dragons, Dragonlance, Secret Circle series, Harry Potter, Farscape, Invader Zim, Misc. Books, Song Fics, Superman, Everworld, and Matrix.
A note to my readers... Here's the deal. A lot of you are commenting, asking me to update. Honestly, I don't know if I will continue any of my stories, and I apologize.

I've decided that writing is what I want to do with my life. Fanfiction is good practice, and a lot of fun, but there is no future in doing it. I am trying to get my original works in print. I have a lot of ideas, ideas that don't fit in the boxes other authors have already made. I need to make my own worlds, and fanfiction doesn't let me do that. Throw in the fact that publishers don't want anything that goes up onto this website, because uploading it here is equivalent to publishing it.

In short, I've reached a place in my writing where I don't have the time to write work that won't get me anywhere. I've reached the point, at seventeen, where I am committed to my writing, and to my future.

With that in mind... you haven't heard the last of me. Please message me on AOL, check out my webpage (basically an online writing portfolio). My good stuff is all up there now, lol.

I am willing to beta read. If you are a serious writer with a story you think I would like, email me at tressymfamiliar@yahoo.com (NOT lylia) or message me on AIM (through the given sn). I'll get back to you with a reply. Don't send me the story, send me your idea, the basic plot. If it has any glaring spoilers to a book or movie, let me know. I'll tell you if I want to read the story itself.

Gods bless you all, good luck with your own writings.

PS: A universal disclaimer, in case I forget one. IF YOU RECOGNIZE IT FROM SOMEWHERE ELSE, IT AIN'T MINE! IF YOU THINK YOU RECOGNIZE IT, EMAIL OR REVIEW AND ASK ME IF I GOT IT FROM SOMEWHERE. I'm honest, I'll admit if I don't own something! :)
