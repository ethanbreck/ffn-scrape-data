Hello. If you've stumbled across this site, I'm sorry to say but it's likely going to be empty for quite sometime. Though I started out writing (really bad) fanfiction, I mostly write (passably good) original fiction. Despite having both this account and a fictionpress one (and, you know, just the one way back when), all of my stuff is on my dA account. It's the only one I keep excessively updated, the second being a toss-up between AFF and my lj. So if you think you like my style, give those a whirl.
I've been writing for about seven years now, and while I definitely have improved in that time (or so I hope), improvement is always the goal. So when you read anything of mine, if you took the time out to give me a crit that would be fantastic.

Contact me if you wish.
Sets, out!
