Update! (11th November 2004)

I am getting far too lazy to update this thing. O_o First off, Never Forgotten is on an indefinite hiatus, in other words, I have no idea when I'll start working on it again. It's been ages since I updated, and frankly, I have pretty much forgotten what I was going to write for it. O_o Maybe I'll rewrite as a oneshot.

Next up: Perceptions. Right now, I'll try to upload a new chapter once a day or two, but next week, starting from the 16th to the 21st, I will be off to a camp, and it should be fairly obvious that I won't be able to update the story. : P In addition, starting from the 2nd of December to the 21st, I will be in Australia for a science exchange programme, and once again I probably won't be able to update unless I have internet access at the school, and I find enough time to write. XD

Oh, and Perceptions will NOT have any Yaoi pairings. I don't particularly like them, and I feel that it's been overdone. Half of the Naruto fanfics here are all SasuNaru. O_o Not that the pairings I'll be choosing are any more original, but they're sure as heck a lot less common than SasuNaru.

And no, I won't be revealing the pairings just yet. : P

With that said, I'd like to thank the people who read and review Perceptions, and in particular, people who have given me ideas as to how to write future chapters. Thank you. :D It had initially started out as a joke between my friend and myself, and then when we came up with more and more ideas, we decided to write a story. Also, once December comes to an end, I definitely won't be able to write a new chapter once every few days anymore (and if the story is still going at that point), because school will have restarted for me. And it's going to be one really busy year. XD
