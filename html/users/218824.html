Here I am. Formerly known as Tim the Enchantress (poor reference to Monty Python), I'm a simple college girl who hails from North Dakota nine months out of the year, and Minnesota the rest of the time. My notebooks are littered with snippets of stories that never quite get finished (mostly in the Harry Potter fandom, though some are more Outsiders, and even some original). Perhaps someday I'll put in some work on them, but first I need to finish my Outsiders story. It's fast becoming a real labor of love.

If for some reason you want to contact me, feel free to email me, AIM me (screen name: tln8727), or leave a comment on my livejournal.
