Just to clarify. Jurian Stallion is an old un-finished piece that was sitting on my HD for a long time. What has been posted so far is the quick and dirty start of it. It is un-edited and practically raw.

To those of you who have submitted reviews to Jurian Stallion, thanky you for the comments and much needed criticism. Hopefully I can meet all your expectations when the re-write is finished.

Update

12th Sept 2007

Just to let those that want to read the full Jurian Stallion that the work is still ongoing and will be completed. Hopefull before the new year but more likely early spring. Sorry for the long wait.

Another Update

15th Dec 2007

I'm well on the way to being roughly about approaching nearly-ish close to arriving towards the area of a third of the way through getting this done. If that was in any way understandable then I truly pity your poor sanity (what's left of it) and might get a few of those reviewers I've had over tha past 18 months or so to proof read this in the springtime. For those that posted the constructive critisism I thank you for the help. I've never been much of a writer more of an ideas man but it helps and I'll try to meet your expectations.

Final Update

6th June 2012

Wow nearly five years. Bad news first, I suffered terminal computer failure at around the halfway mark and lost everything. I had in fact already lost my muse by that point anyway and was not going to continue without one but alas she never came back. Good news, although I am not likely to continue this (although if my muse returns I will) I am happy to let someone else take over as I'm well aware that there is a good premise in this fic and a solid beggining so feel free to PM me as regards taking over.
