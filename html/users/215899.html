RANT 1: NaruXHinataXHarem

So this is my first rant in this, and I couldn't help but make a comment about it. I, for one, hate NaruXHina Harem's. I've grown to completely detest them, even if Hinata is the main focus in it. I just don't see it being believable at all, and the fact that EVERYONE in the story is literally OK with everything going on, even in some doujin's there's a little tension in between the girls. But, I'm not trying to sound like a NaruHina fantard, and also not to sound hypocritical but there's some harem's I can deal with, and other's I can not. And Naruto harem's involving the Naruto X Hinata, is just one of them.

Let's talk about why. In these harems, Naruto is the basis. Why? Because he's practically being shit on his entire life. I get that, and it's understandable, so some authors feel the need that he should get more than just one. I, for one, don't. Since I support NaruHina in its fullest as one solid, unhindered pairing. I believe that Hinata can give him more than what he wants/needs. Look at Hinata's character in general, the girl is Ga-Ga for the guy. She alone has admired him from afar, believed in him when no one else did or would, reassured him when he needed it most, and just in general, held him in high regard. Do you honestly think that she would just allow someone to passionately have their way with him? I don't think so. Hinata's a well-reserved character but all in all, she would defend what she had strived so hard to earn, which was his acknowledgement, but most prominantly, his love. I can't believe she would so openly share that with anyone. Why would she? Another thing is that LOVE, in retrospect, is a selfish feeling. When you're in the love, you don't want to share that person with anyone else. You want them to yourself. Think about it. Would you honestly want to share your girlfriend/boyfriend/husband/wife with anyone else? Could you honestly stand there and watch someone you openly fell in love with turn their attention to someone else? I don't. I couldn't. And I'm pretty sure a lot of you couldn't neither. Perhaps I'm just being to realistic about it, but that right there is what steers me away from it all, because above all, it's just not believable. While in doujins, you see it frequently, one guy lays a bunch of 'hot' girls. I got that, and I'm guilty of some of that too, but it's a read and forget type of thing, but at least even in there, the girls literally fight over the main guy. Which, to me, makes it a bit more likeable. They all vie for his affection more than the other.

I also understand, Naruto sets himself up for a lot of this, especially with the Shadow Clone Jutsu, which makes way towards another that I'm sure the lot of you can guess. And you know what, I think it's a bit creative to use them in such a way, it's even funny at times, but I still don't think that necessarily makes to where he is to make himself subjective to it all. And while I can't predict how Naruto will actually respond to all of that, I can say Hinata wouldn't respond very kindly to it. That I'm sure of.

I guess in short, I like my NaruHina stories, just the way they are. It's just my opinion, and if you like NaruxHarems, good for you.

Don't even get me started on SasuHina, let alone SasuXHarem's. BLECH!
