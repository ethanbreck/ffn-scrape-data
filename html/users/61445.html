Since you're here, allow me to introduce myself! My name's Freedom Fighter, and I've been a fanfic writer since 1999! I've written stories for many shows over the years, and I'm still dedicated to give you the best works I can. There's plenty to read here. And if you don't like what you see here... there's even more available at my website, Freedom Fighter's Library (), including exclusives like the famed "Roomies" reality series, which put anime characters into a mostly original reality show, and "The Adventures Series," the fanfic that brought about "The Year of Rocket's Revenge," which is a sequel/spin-off to the original work! Currently I'm dabbling mostly in fanfics based on Phineas and Ferb and the Total Drama series, and stepping out into others on occasion in the form of reality series-style crossovers.

Don't forget to check out and follow me on Twitter (@freefightwriter)!
