Deep thought of the moment: Religion, everyone believes in their own religion, I'm not dissing anyone by saying that only this religion is right or 'that religion is wrong'. I believe, being a Neo-pagan, that when we die, we see only what we want to see. Let's say I believed that I'd go to a world of lollipops and candy, I most likely would, because I created the image. While others that believe they will go to heaven, will indeed go to their image of heaven.

I'm not saying that the Creator (I'm saying creator 'cause we really don't know if it's a girl or a boy, which I think a true god is genderless) doens't exist, it does, but it gives us the choice of our actions, and has spirits also show us the door, but we have to open the door and walk though it. So if we have been bad and fear going to HFIL, we will have created a image of our suffering and believe it to be real... And for those who believe they will be reborn, they will be. After all, our soul is mere engery, na na na! Don't get mad at me! It is TRUE! Our whole body is run by eletricity, and when we die, that engery leaves, therefor i could be our soul, but then again I could wrong, that is for you to judge after reading this.

So, to sum it all up, When we die, we pass into the dimension of what we believe, and no other belief can be wrong, because we cannot prove it wrong. So, in the end, we all win, and we all are right.

But remember, be careful what you believe in, for it may be true.
