Super Mega Update Powahz, GO!

Ok, so I haven't touched ff.net in like... 2 years or something? Well I ended up working part time and going to school part time, so I had NO time to write at all. It was a sad, sad time indeed. Anyways... early in 2008, I stopped working to focus on school for a while, so that first semester was a little cooldown from work. I had some extra time in class to hand write some stuff on my fics, but I didn't ever really get around to posting it. Then in the summer (I kid you not on this) I took 4 classes in the span of 2 months. Then after a very small 2 week break, I took on a full semester of classes. Good news: I've almost got a BS. Bad news: STILL NO FICS. So now that I'm taking a well deserved semester off from school while I look for a job, I decided it was time to get some work done! Hopefully in the near future, I'll get around to actually posting something here. :)
