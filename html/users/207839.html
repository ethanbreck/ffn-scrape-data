Author has written 11 stories for Hunter X Hunter, X/1999, Slam Dunk, Kodomo No Omocha, Gundam Wing/AC, Gundam Seed, Nodame Cantabile, Monochrome Factor, and K.

I figured a major revamp of my profile was in order, seeing as I'm all fired up to write again after god-knows-how-many-years.

Right now I'm writing Counterpoint, a Monochrome Factor fic, and obviously slash.

For anyone who even bothers to read this, I'd like to say that the writing from way-back-when and now is very, very different, so I hope that you don't even attempt to compare them. XD My writing style hasn't changed that much, fundamentally, but I'd like to think that it has improved and my thoughts are much more coherent now.

Pairings I am in love with: Monochrome Factor's AkiraxShirogane, RyuukoxShirogane, LuluxAya (yes, I think it actually works), Durarara's ShizuoxIzaya, Tiger & Bunny's BarnabyxKotetsu (or the other way around? I'm actually not that picky about this one), No. 6's NezumixShion (because it's already pretty much canon!), weirdly, but finally gotten into HxH LeorioxKurapika and KilluaxGon (again, other way around may be possible for these two), HisokaxIllumi (because their tag team is just too damn hot)...

Surprise pairing: Got converted to the HP HarryxMalfoy crowd, somehow. There's just something about rivals getting together, I guess.

Classic pairing: Gundam Wing's HeeroxDuo. That was a given from the get-go.

Um, so yes, I've officially become a slash addict. That's not to say I don't appreciate good het pairings, though. I just find the darker side more appealing, lol.

... As of 11/20/2012...

And holy cow, it's been a year since I actually wrote anything. Well, K has a really good storyline, so this time I've decided to try my hand at some Fushimi/Yata. Hindsight should be a three-parter now, if I calculated it correctly last night, hahaha, and I should hopefully get the next part out in a couple of days.

I'm crossing my fingers and hoping the fandom for K just keeps growing, and of course that more people try out writing Fushimi. He is a delight to write, honestly, and I can sort of understand the way he thinks about things (although mostly Misaki, haha), which makes me a little creepy too, I guess.

Long live Fushimi/Yata! :D

...12/12/12...

Seems like a lucky day, hahaha, with all the number lining up. Finally got around to writing Hindsight's second chapter, so I guess just one chapter left to go. :) Who knows, after the season's new anime are over, I might end up picking up another project.

...1/15/13...

Wow, I'm actually being semi-consistent here?!? Anyway, posted second chapter of my new fic, Half. It's my coping mechanism after K ended, hahaha. Anyway, the fic is based on a 'what-if' scenario, and the primary pairing is Suoh/Munakata, because no one could watch the last few episodes without getting sucked into this couple's UST.
