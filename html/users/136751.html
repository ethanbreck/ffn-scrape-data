Words are my playthings.

Well, I finally have written some fanfiction. About time too.

I do beta for other writers. Feel free to email me or contact me on AIM under Starfrog32 if you feel you need help. There are certain catagories that I know well, and thus beta very well, but I am willing to look at any catagory of fanfic.

I love to beta, though I wish FanFiction.Net would offer a listing and review service for betas.

I read a lot of stories here. I will go through a catagory and bookmark all the stories that catch my attention. This means I have a huge list of stories waiting to be read. Your story(ies) may be bookmarked if you've given a good summery or if I've read another of your stories and enjoyed it. I read fast, but not fast enough to keep up with all the interesting stories here on FanFiction.Net

I do not review every story I read. I know every review is nice, but sometimes all there is to say is "I liked your story" I do review stories that I think have a lot of potential, but may need some help.

About Me:

I am a college student, currently working on an undergrad in English. That's right, I wanna be a EDITOR when I grow up!

My most current project is training a large, fluffy, meowlee two year-old feline to not to bite everyone, jump on everything and not to dump his food dish over. Yeah, it's a score of Cat:10, Owner:1. On the plus side, he has good litterbox habits.

I've reached an age where the young are getting younger, and think it was impossible that I was ever that young.
