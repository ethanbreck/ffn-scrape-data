Soooo, it's been a good long while since I've been active here at all. Just updating to inform any of you who might by some strange chance happen to stumble across this site that I beg of you to please NOT judge me by my past mistakes (aka, the fanfic you see below you -_-; ). Seriously, it's so sickeningly Mary Sue and Piccolo is so disgustingly out of character that the only reason that I do not delete it immediately is because when I do happen to find my way back to this site, reading it never ceases to put me into a fit of giggles. xD No, really--it's that pathetic.

Honestly though, check the date folks. I posted that thing back in 01/02 but had most of the original concepts developed in 2000. That was seventh grade for me(I'm in college now). But trust me, the story that you see below you is nothing more than the result of the illusioned whimsies of a pre-teen with a crush. Totally lame, I know. Thankfully, I never really finished the first 'installment' of the five part series (yes, you heard me right, series. Frightening, huh?) and so could not in good conscience post the later bits; on which I had invested a considerable amount of time in. I believe that the second part/fic was up to chapter 13-ish or something. e_e; Anyway, point being read them if you wish (though I wouldn't advise it unless you're looking to change your opinion of me for the worse) but, if at all possible, take only a chuckle away with you and please save my few remaining shreds of dignity. e_e

Anyway, I recently rediscovered this place and am currently reading and reviewing some people who have actual talent--that's the only reason I'm here, I promise. I have no intentions of posting anything new here, ever. e_e;
