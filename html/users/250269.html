I'm a hopeless romantic. That being said, I make my stories in the extremes: either it is heartbreaking-ly sad, or it's sugary fluff that I like to call Cotton Candy. Although I don't have many stories published on here, I've been around for years. I've written for Chronicles of Riddick, Harry Potter, Smallville, Phantom of the Opera... there's more, I know it! I just can't recall them. Why did I remove them? Well, because I felt that I grew out of them, so to speak. They needed editing, because the writing style wasn't mine anymore, or because it was just juvenile. I've been creatively writing for over ten years, and I spend way too much of my life on here. :)

I absolutely love reading other's work, especially if it's well written and thus, I try very hard to have my fictions well written. I'm open to any criticisms you may have, but please remember to make them constructive criticisms. I don't appreciate flames.

Other than that, read away, please review and feel free to add any to your favorites.
