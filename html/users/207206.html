Hi, I'm not new anymore, but I still only have one story so far(if I'm lying, send me an e-mail to yell at me for not updating).

Thursday, March 6, 2003 3:44 A.M.

Watched the episode of G Gundam where Domone (is that how it’s spelled?) is forced to kill his brother. This was so heartrending that I found myself crying right along with all the characters. You can make fun of me all you want, but the director, writers, etc. did such a great job that I had to write it down before I forgot about it. I may even make an entirely new fic revolving around what happened there (or not) anyway, the point is that it’s enough to make me want to cry even now that the show’s been over for like 9 hours or something.

Thursday, March 6, 2003, 12:46 P.M.

Oh, and not to dig at sore wounds or anything, but I've noticed that in response to the columns here @ FFN, slash writers seem to react just as badly to bad press as any other group, even though a lot of them seem to(emphasis on seem) think of themselves as, I don't know, more sophisticated? More talented? Better writers?

Anyway, the point is this: whether you're a reader/writer of slash or of some other genre, don't put you and your category of stories on some Almighty Pedestal of Most Supreme High Quality work, and for God's Sake, don't call me a bigot. I have nothing against you reading/writing whatever the heck you want. Calling me a bigot tells me that

1)you haven't paid attention to anything I've tried to say

2)you're not only a bigot yourself, but a hypocrite, as well

3)you don't consider my opinion valid

and/or

4)you're as close-minded as people who think gays shouldn't have any rights (I DID NOT SAY THEY DIDN'T, I SAID THAT YOU ARE JUST AS BAD AS PEOPLE WHO DO)

I expect flames and such all the way across the board, but even so, I'm going to ask that before you send me an e-mail saying either "YOU'RE AN ASSHOLE AND A BIGOT AND A MILLION OTHER MEAN, NASTY, HURTFUL THINGS" or on the the flip side of the coin "YEAH BABY, DEATH TO FAGS, THEY SUCK, ETC," you take a careful look at what I wrote and at least PRETEND to see that I'm not trying to say anything bad against slash or homosexuals(is that capitalized?) or any other group. While I don't especialy care for slash, I find it fills as important a niche as any other type of Fiction. and you're wecome to read it/write it to your hearts content.

thank you and good-bye.
