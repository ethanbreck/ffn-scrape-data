Finished Works/WIP - A little behind the scenes look at what I've done so far. Scroll down to actually read them.

Growing Up ("Lullaby" Nominated for Most Original FanFic at RBSU)
A group of one shots revolving around the married life of Garfield and Raven. Titled "Growing Up" because all stories held within will involve in some way the character of Rebecca Logan. This is going to work like Slaughterhouse Five, each chapter is going to take place at a different time in Becca's life. I think it'll be neat.

Tradition
Ah yes, the New Years quarters. I actually do this, it's something that my friend shared with me and the others in our group. His family has all kinds of strange little traditions, including a Christmas Shoe. So when I saw that RBSU was having a contest about New Years, this idea jumped right into my head! I hope you folks enjoy.

Test
My first story made just for the 100 word challenge (I snuck the other two in the list to make me look good). The word for this one was "67 percent". Which is equal to 80/120 when rounded, and is also a 'C'. I know most people depict Beast Boy as a little dumber than a 'C' or 'B' student... but come on. He's not all that stupid. The guy's hacked mainframes for crying out loud... Give him a break. At least he's always trying his best.

Fool (Winner of the Best Comedy FanFic Award at RBSU)
This story line isn't original, so please don't think it is. It's been done many times in shows ranging from MASH to Foster's Home for Imaginary Friends. I really liked the idea of Beast Boy delving into paranoia, and especially liked that Raven was the cause of his discomfort without even trying. I'm happy that I wrote it, and although there are a few pacing problems, I am mostly content with it. I can't believe all those mistakes at the end snuck in though. Both me and my Beta-Reading sister thought it was clean. Clearly we were both drunk. I will improve in the future I hope

Down Came the Rain
First multi-word title! Huzzah! Ummm, I don't know why it went to chess. Maybe because I like chess? I have no other explenation. However, I do like this. A few of the jokes I wanted to make weren't made though and that makes me sad. This was entered as April's RBSU contest. The theme was April Showers.

Shaken
I dunno what made me write this or why. Thought came to me, and it worked out well for the 100 word challenge. The word was along the lines of Death. But I'm not about to kill off anyone, so a near death experience is the best I can muster.

The Devil and Garfield Logan
Based off of a handful of literary classics. Beast Boy sells his soul, and needs help from Raven in refuting the contract. Can the two of them beat the devil herself? Please, please give feedback on this one... I've been wanting to write it for so long.

Ideas - No promises, but these are thoughts in my head.

1. Sea of Strangers
Sometimes, stuff happens, people have to deal with it and who're you gonna call? Yep, that's right! It's a Ghostbusters tribute using the Titans! Prologue and first chapters are written, but I'm building a backlog before I start posting. Story will go up once I'm a ways through Chapter Three.

2. Songfic
That's the title of the story, not the type of story. You see, I HATE songfics... but I love music references just as much as TV and movie references. So, here's the gimmick. If I write this story, every line of dialogue will be a lyric from a different song. No promises on this. At all. If I do write it, it'll be a long time coming.

That's about all for this update I do believe.

Later Days;

Shawn 'the Unfunny Joke' Wheeler
