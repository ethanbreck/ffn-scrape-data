A pre-physical therapy student, diagnosed with ADD whom has a hatred for essays but finds enjoyment in writing fiction.

2-17-07

As it turns out, I am still alive. A shocker, I know. I honestly thought I left the world of "Fan Fiction" years ago, but that is obviously not the case. Inspiration has struck and I have submitted myself to the fact that I do want to continue writing light-hearted fiction based from stories and characters I love. In fact, it has come to my attention that TDOS lacks the level of maturity that I have developed over that past 4 years. I am not saying that the humor will be gone, I'm just saying the humor and writing style in general will be in better taste than it was when I wrote the story at fifteen years of age.

Do not expect speedy updates because I openly admit that I am slow and a perfectionist. Beggars and whiners, I'm sorry but you will not have any effect whatsoever as to the speed of my chapter updates. Also, the plot I have in mind for TDOS is much different than the original; a.k.a. A heck of a lot better. Look out for a rewritten chapter one within the next month!
