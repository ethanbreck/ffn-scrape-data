Hello all! I'm just a city boy, born and raised in south Detroit...

...no, wait. Sorry. That's not right.

Actually I'm more of a science fiction fan who loves, just about as much as anything on Earth, a good Crossover. My favorite stories are the ones that combine two fandoms while managing to stay true to each of them, and so of course that's what I try to do myself. I don't always succeed.

Right now I'm working on a longer series of stories featuring the Tenth Doctor and Quinn Fabray from Glee as his companion.
