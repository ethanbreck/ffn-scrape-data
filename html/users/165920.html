Hello there!

After many, many years in hibernation, I have recently discovered a new found, fresh love for fanfiction. A good few years ago, during my High School years, I had somewhat of a healthy obsession with reading and writing fanfiction. In more recent years, studying, work and personal changes have seemed to erase some of that passion.

However, I find that everytime I re-read some of my favourite fanfiction, a spark somewhere re-ignites itself.

This is going to be my new, whole hearted attempt, to re-create a love and fire for something that I once had so strongly.

The first fic that I intend to re-write was one that I started writing a long time ago, for an author called yaoifantasy, entitled 'Noire'. I am re-naming this fanfic 'Oscuro Profundo', to reflect my current image and life choices.

Considering how my style is expected to have developed, I am re-writing this fic from scratch based on what I originally wrote. This holds the intention of the fic being better than ever, and eventually properly finished, as I was never able to do in the first place. Yaoifantasy, if you're still out there, I hope this does you justice!

Kat/24/England ;) Pleased to meet you!
