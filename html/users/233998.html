Name: Stephanie 'Stevie' W.

Age: 21

Stevie's gorgeous and has a wonderful singing voice and flair for the dramatic. She has a strange sense of humor, kind of random and sarcastic and morbid depending on her mood. It's easy for me to write about her because I know her really well. So she is almost a permanent charachter in my stories. She is happy being bi-sexual and a sex addict as well as liking alcohol.
