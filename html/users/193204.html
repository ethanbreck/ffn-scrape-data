Author has written 6 stories for Tenchi Muyo, and StarTrek: Voyager.

The Blue-Haired Goddess was born in 1980 and has been a member of FF.Net since the early 2000s. I have previously written a few fanfictions for Tenchi Muyo, which was my first love when it comes to Anime. Mostly, I've been a lurker, but even after 15~ years on and off of lurking on this site, I still am drawn to the awesomeness of writing, fanfiction, and stories in general. Originally, my focus was anime, but I have since moved on. Currently, my focus is science fiction in general, and Star Trek specifically. I might write a few things, but mostly I lurk.
