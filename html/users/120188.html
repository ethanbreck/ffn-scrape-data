I'm migrating some of my LOTR/Silm stories over here in view of the imminent closure of HASA. So even though a number of stories are at these sites, I'm still moving a goodly number of them over here for purposes of keeping them all in one place (more or less).

Transformers fic is still all here, and BSG fic is still all on my LJ or on different BSG sites.
