I figured it was due time that I actually decided to change my profile. That thing was left over from when I was in eighth grade. Come to think of it, so are many of the stories as well. It is always interesting to see how one's style changes with the passage of time. I would like to think I've matured. Still, I can not help but to fall in ove all over again with Rurouni Kenshin every time I start to reread the fiction that people produce. The people, the giants among us in this field, keep changing. It is interesting to be in the periphery and watch as the change progresses, recalling those authors who first made you love the characters. I am getting nostalgic here.
