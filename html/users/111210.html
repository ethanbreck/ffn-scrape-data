Bio:

Hello, my name is Ryiena, but you can call me Ryi, Ry, or just R as some do. I am 24 years old and live in Charleston, South Carolina. Over the years I've seen lots of Anime, but only certain ones have inspired me to write fanfiction. Currently the only fanfic I am working on is The Hunt Continues, while the others will probably be deleted.

My passion is drawing and writing, even though I still struggle with both of them. I am not a professional writer, nor do I claim to be one. Keep that in mind when you read my fics and feel the urge to tear my writing apart. :P For almost 11 years I've been writing fanfiction, even before I knew that fanfiction was called "fanfiction." XD Never have I had anything published, but my mother has and my grandfather was a sensational writer. Someday my family hopes to get his book published. Currently I am working on my book DragonScars, which will hopefully be published sometime in the next 20 years, cause i r slow typer. o.O

I encourage those of you that would like to see my other works to visit my web site. It serves as a site to hoard all of my writings, fan and original. Sometimes I may update there before anywhere else.
