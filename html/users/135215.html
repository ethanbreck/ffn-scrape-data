Update: 7/05/09

5 months later... dun dun duun.

At this point, until further notice, Delicious Revenge is discontinued. I always hated when this happened to the stories that I looked forward to reading, but now that I'm in this position where I can say that I've "grown up" (or really, just realized my many numerous faults) this story seriously disturbs me. XD It was, truthfully, never meant to be written seriously, nor treated seriously, but after nearly 5 years, I think I can say that there has to be a limit in how ridiculous a story can be before I completely cringe in horror. I was thinking about revamping it, and who knows, maybe one day I will. But at that point, it will be a nearly entirely different story and it would make much more sense to just post it as a new story. But I would hate to lose all my beautiful reviews (even one of the latest one that basically told me how horrible I was, lol) and I think it'll serve as a good reminder of how not to write a story anymore.

Besides, there were some things I liked, no matter what anybody says.

I'm basically updating this because I have plans of writing a new story- or rather, get back to work on a story that I've drawn up around the time DR was being written. And I'm sure there's still a few people out there willing to frown at me for discontinuing DR and writing another story instead... -cough- Anyway, it'll be another cliched, roll-your-eyes at the plot SasuNaru story. But there's a couple of original ideas speckled here and there, enough that I still want to write it. It'll be better organized than DR, lol, but just as laugh-your-heart out. And totally AU considering everything that's going on in Naruto right now...
