Hey, y'all. It's nice to know someone cares enough about me to read this. I'm just sitting here, otherwise, chewing on a matchstick and arguing with my computer.
I'd be honored if you read my works, and most appreciative if you reviewed them, too, even if it's criticism.
If you'd like, AIM me at S Memento Mori. I'd love to talk with you, no matter who you are. I've got no one to talk to if you don't! Feedback, emails and messages are ALWAYS appreciated. Or, you could head on over to my livejournal, username SMememtnoMori and comment on one of my inane ramblings. I have no friends there as of yet, so I wouldn't say no to one or two. Especially if I didn't know them personally.
I've also got some of my work posted at Fictionalley.org, and if you can find me there, I have more respect for you than you can imagine. I cannot for the life of me figure out that site. You can also find random assorted snippets at my livejournal:
Kudos to all,
-MM-

Who am I? Difficult question, that.
I write because I can, and because my muse tells me I should.
I post because I can, and because I need to know what others think of me.
I read because I can, and because I would miss so much otherwise.
I love because I can, and because I would be bitter if I didn't.
I live because I can, and because I never refuse a gift.

Always remember, in the words of a great man: Don't panic.
