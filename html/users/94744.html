Recent Rants

Monday, 7 March, 2005, 6:23pm
Is is just me or has the quality of writing slightly deteriorated since I last decided to read fanfics? Not to say I am the best writer on this site, which I know I'm not. It's just that we once had fantastic stories such as Into The Hands Of Fate by Sea Chelle, which was a fantastic story about the Marauder's Era. The moment I read that story I fell in love with it. Recently though, every story seems to use the same plot line once again. Sure, sometimes people can pull off the repeated story line, but that's because they make it their own. Many of the writer's recently have been writing Harry Potter fanfiction as regonalistic stories. For all of you who know nothing of regionalism, it involves stock characters involved in similar situations and are often not written too well. You have to embrace the characters people! Write them as if it's all being played out in your mind! You have the ability, use it! I know it's hard not to pull out a chapter a day, but even if it takes over a day to produce a chapter, take that time and use it wisely. If writer's block strikes, let it work itself out! Don't shove junk out calling it a fic. This also applies to throwing something on the site just because you're bored. Write because you enjoy it. Post because you love it. Don't write for the reviewers, write for yourself!
