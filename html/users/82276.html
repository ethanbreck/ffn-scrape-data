Author has written 68 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, Andromeda, Stargate: SG-1, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Star Wars, Stargate: Atlantis, Doctor Who, Scarecrow and Mrs. King, and NCIS.

I began my writing career at the tender age of 11. While my imagination helped keep me out of trouble, I'm sure my English teachers cursed every time they received a completed assignment from me. Writing fiction has always come easy to me, but I had problems completing college papers of more than 1,000 words.

I began writing fandom before I even knew there was such a thing. At the time, I had just moved to the United States and wasn't working. I didn't have a computer so I watched a lot of TV, including Northern Exposure reruns. In my head, I created an entire scenario based on Joel returning to Cicely although I never wrote it down. Ten months later, I went online to look for Star Wars related websites and discovered Han/Leia fanfiction. My course was set.

I've written for numerous fandoms, including Star Trek Voyager and Stargate SG-1. The majority of my works are relationship 'ship' fics, with only a small amount of plot. A complete list of fandoms in which I write is at the bottom of this profile.

People have asked why I use the pen name Matt. I began writing using my own name, Sally, and then a nickname given to me in a music fan group community, Matteabrit since I like the singer Kathy Mattea and I'm British. Someone in the Voyager fandom called me Matt one day and I liked it. I used it first in the Andromeda community, but it really stuck when I began writing Sam/Jack in SG-1 fandom. There were already a number of Sallys in the community and I wanted to be different! Even today, people still call me Matt when we meet in person.

Most of my pieces are only one chapter long. I try only to post completed works. I've only ever posted a Work In Process once, and it still haunts me. I keep swearing I'll complete it, even though it's been four years since I first began writing it.

I have a Live Journal with the same username. A lot of my fic gets posted there before it comes here. You'll also find the drabbles that I write as I don't post those here.

I've noticed a few folks looking for new chapters to a completed fic. Just to make it clear, TBC means I plan on posting more and Finis means it's done, it's complete, no more to come. Yeah, I'm a bit weird like that. Never could do anything the simple way.

My Standard Disclaimer for all My Fanfic

Most of the characters in my fics do not belong to me. The exception is any character that is not identifiable from any television show/book/movie/professionally published work. If there is a crossover between works, this crossover will always be identified. Any resemblance of a fictional character to anyone else, alive or dead, is purely coincidental. Historial characters may or may not be portrayed according to real life and their use will be noted accordingly in each individual work. No money is made in this venture. No offense is intended towards the registered owners of the characters and professional works.
