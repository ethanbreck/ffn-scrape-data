A Note About the Regularity of Story Updates

Fanfiction is an escape from my original writing, but my original writing always takes precedence over my fan fiction stories, so if I haven't updated for a while, chances are its because I'm working on an original piece. There are times when I disappear for years because I've got a piece of writing that I'm working on that is under contract to a publisher. But then, lo and behold, I pop up.

Reviews

I consider each review a small gift. I don't believe in holding readers hostage for reviews in any way, shape or form; you are free to enjoy the story and leave feedback, and you are also free to enjoy the story without giving feedback. I also don't believe in dictating to readers what kind of reviews to leave - that defeats the entire purpose. Don't feel bad about adding a story of mine to your favorites or your story alerts without reviewing! For me, this is all in fun and I'm very glad if my little scribblings in any way add any sort of joy to your day.
