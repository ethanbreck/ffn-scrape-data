Heya! I'm Kris Yamaki, AKA Efad (sometimes Eferad, by a certain Miranda Crystal-Bearer type person), Dafe (Efad backwards), Saira, Rice Girl (long story), George, and Genius Efad. I was also one of the few people at my church who passed Miranda Crystal-Bearer's class on properly shoving items up people's noses. I have a lisence, if anyone would like to see...

I'm a 16-yr-old Christian girl who enjoys football, Christian Rock music, sketching, writing, and anything revolving around electronics! *grins*

I'm a big Gundam Wing, DBZ, the Japanese version of Sailor Moon, Legend of Zelda and Lord of the Rings fan. I'm also starting to be into Harry Potter, but I claim to only be a closet fan. And I'm not a rabid fangirl. I like most couplings, but I despise all Yaoi, Yuri, Hentai and homosexual couplings. It's disgusting in my opinion. That's just me, though...

I also like using songs in my fics, so usually I get a character to sing. So beware, all ye people who hate musicals! RUN IN TERROR! GWA-HA-HA-HA!

You'll never find any profanity, sexuality, homosexuality, adult themes or anything similar to aforementioned topics in my fics. I stick to my morals, thank you very much...

If you e-mail/IM me, I WILL e-mail/IM you back! I promise, and I accept all praise, suggestions and criticism you have...'cept flames.
