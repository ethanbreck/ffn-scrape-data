About Rispah Meron
She is a 400 yr. old Dhe'nar Dark Elf rogue. This means she's a professional thief... although she's yet to be particularly good at it. Still, she's gotten to be a slightly decent hunter... but then, let's face it, the Dhe'nar are like saiya-jins: they are full of themselves, thinking they are the best fighters... the best race in general, actually... in the universe. At least the vast majority are - Rispah is... atypical, having had encounters with many members of the various other races over the years. These dealings and friendships have made certain of the other Dhe'nar believe she has become soft. Rispah's trying to balance these relationships and Dhe'nari philosophy, plus her feelings of loneliness and not quite fitting in anywhere, tear at her sanity and she has taken Zelia, Mistress of the Moons and Madness, as her patron.

About Xiralo Wezen
She is a 30 yr. old Aelotoi of the Vaer'sah Clan. She is a professional bard, however, she's still learning. The Aelotoi were former slaves, and Xiralo would perform for them and their masters, the Kiramon (whom they called the Overseers), until the rebellion. She occasionally wrote protest songs for the rebellion, and still keeps a small collection with her. When she came to Elanthia, she learned that the Elven Houses were the ones responsible for the Kiramon invasion so long ago. Most of the Aelotoi forgave the elves, for they had no idea where their magic sent the Kiramon, but Xiralo still holds a terrible grudge...
