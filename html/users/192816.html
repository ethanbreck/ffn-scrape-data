Hi,
I feel a need to update this profile for a variety of reasons, not just to point out that I have another pen name now - rumflavouredkisses.

For over a year now I have been writing in the wrestling fandom, under the above pen name, but I have found my interest has become almost non-existent anymore. This means that my writing has also become rare.

I am no more enamoured with the amount of review hungry drivel that is being posted in the fandom. The first wrestling Mary Sue/Be in my story venture was interesting, orgional and made for a rather interesting concept. However months later the sheer volume of them makes them all seem samey, uninteresting and plain tacky. I could go on about this for hours but I won't, suffice to say though, that I despair at the state.

As I no longer write in the fandom, and barely ever read except for the work of gifted authors with whom I am already accquianted (see shout outs), I have ventured into other fandoms which is why I have got a new pen name.

This will remain for me to use under wrestling, but expect it to be highly inactive.

Much Love Me
