Last time I updated this I claimed to be 27...

Whoa, those were the days.

So, to make it short: I'm an 367yo from Germany, full-time working mommy of a 6yo girl and a 3yo boy who I adore like nothing else on this planet... probably beyond. Which, of course, doesn't help with my free-time. Haven't been writing for a while and only recently started dipping my toes into the Forever and Star Trek 2009 Fandom. I try to write as fast as possible but I do have my priorities.

Most of what you can find here is Supernatural, some Buffy and Harry Potter. Only the first in English, by the way. Two of my recent passions are Forever, Star Trek (Well, it's admittedly a very VERY old passion) and Musketeers. It's not the ideas I'm missing, you see? So, if anyone invents either a cloning or a time-machine I'm a willing consumer.
