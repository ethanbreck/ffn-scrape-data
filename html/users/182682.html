I am a frustrated fiction writer. I was a newspaper reporter for 9 years, but suffered severe burnout. (How many city council meetings can you attend?) My main line of work now is computer operations. There is nothing creative about my job, so I am glad I stumbled across fanfiction. I read a wonderful story written by plasticChevy and became inspired through my emails with Claire. Besides writing this fanfic, I take care of my registered quarterhorse, Dylan (named after Dylan Thomas), and my red-and-white Boston Terrier, Carlie. I haven't written anything for a long time. I hope to soon change that. Please note that I have changed my Pen Name to Adara of Middle Earth. I used to write under the name BoromirFANATIC.
