Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter.
East of Eden - was my first attempt at fanfic ever. I went back and did some serious editing later.

Fortuna's Bitter Smile - came later and is supposed to be some sort of short story triplet.

Unfamiliar Roads - was originally planned as a sequel to East of Eden. It gathered momentum and
is now my primary fic. It more and more drifts away from being the conclusion of East of Eden to become a stand-alone piece I'm very fond of.

Embers - is something I don't know where it came from. My first and last attempt at bittersweet, I guess.

Zaubern mit Links - is a test if Harry Potter holds the same appeal for me when I write in my native language.

I appreciate all comments and reviews since German my first language -kind of makes it difficult for me to judge my own writing. Thank you.
