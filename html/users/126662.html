Author has written 115 stories for Passions, General Hospital, Walking Dead, and Stranger Things, 2016.

Most of my older fics are Passions fics with a definite Sheridan and Luis slant, but they usually involve the entire cast to varying degrees.

I've fallen completely in love with Carol and Daryl on The Walking Dead, so much so I've even jumped into the deep end and started a couple of stories about them.

Thanks to the everybody in that talented fandom that has made me feel so welcomed.

I'm enjoying the resurgence of creativity and feeling of community, and I hope these good feelings are the kick in the pants that I need to finish up some of my (much) older WIPs.

Fingers crossed.

Recs for any of my fandoms are welcomed. Reading and writing in my free time makes me happy, and well-written stories are love.

If you want to make my day, drop me a note sometime. I love feedback as much as the next FF writer.

Or, you could just point me in the direction of more good stories; a girl can never get enough of those.

There are a lot of shows, pairings, and characters that I love right now and from the past. If you don't see a character or pairing, it doesn't necessarily mean I don't like that character. I may like them but not feel the need to seek out fanfiction for them (Maggie/Glenn, for instance, or Michonne--love me some Michonne). Conversely, characters you see listed might not be characters I necessarily love on the show, but I find fascinating to read and explore in fanfiction. Oh, and just because I list them as a pairing, it doesn't necessarily mean I mean romantically. It just means the idea of them sharing screen-time or the written page as it were is too tantalizing for me to pass up, lol.

To narrow it down some, I'll just list the top ones.

The Walking Dead: Carol/Daryl, Carol, Merle, Lori (I know...unpopular opinion), Lori/Rick, Lori/Daryl, Tara
The X-Files: Scully/Mulder
Battlestar Galactica, 2003: Roslin/Adama the elder, Roslin
Hunger Games: Effie/Haymitch
Lost: Juliet/Sawyer (James), Penny/Desmond, Miles
Nashville: Rayna/Deacon, Juliet/Avery, Layla

Feel free to browse my favorites page if we happen to share a fandom in common, and you're looking for something to read. I've personally discovered more than a few favorites that way, and I like to think I'm a pretty good judge of a well-written and/or entertaining story.
