Update March 2011

It has been close to a year since I announced the rewrite. Still haven't finished the first chapter... Sorry I am easily distracted i guess. I am however working on it now, and as soon as FF.net lets me edit my story (some unknown bug is preventing this at the moment) I will be taking down all but the first chapter. It will then be shortly replaced. I was going to replace them all at once to save the reviews, but I would rather have new ones.

Also the Sailor Moon manga is being published in the US again starting in September! I am simply ecstatic. I will finally be able to start replacing the worn copies that I already have, and since it is also being retranslated I get a whole new understanding of the story (frankly the other versions are poorly translated).

UPDATE April 2010

So I am a soon to be graduating college student who hasn't updated anything since, well, since I was a senior in High School. Sorry. Good news is I am back. And Destiny of the Moonlight is getting a complete rewrite. So if you want to save a copy of it as it is now. Do so soon. If you miss your chance, then send me a message I guess, though I am not sure why anyone would want the old version as I was a horrible writer. It scares me how bad it is. Now when I say rewrite I don't mean a bit here and a bit there. I mean I am writing it again practically from scratch. Some things that I had set up already with be changed. Others will be moved. Much will be added.

I do have someone reading it before I update. Also my writing has changed a great deal. So there should be less errors.

Harry Potter and the Tsuki no Hime will not be updated. Most will not think this is a bad thing. It kinda reeks.

So look forward to my return. Well I hope you do.
