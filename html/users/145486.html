Author has written 23 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Tenchi Muyo, Trigun, Rurouni Kenshin, Fullmetal Alchemist, and A Goofy Movie.
I like comics, I like anime. I like putting different mediums together.
From youth I had a wild imagination in crossovers. For example, I pondered much on an "Inspector Gadget/Count Duckula" X-over. However, I never wrote anything down. It was a good thing I learned about fanfics a few years back. After I got really interested in Tenchi Muyo (which really started my love for Anime), I decided to finally write a fanfic X-over with Marvel's THE NEW MUTANTS (Readers of my work know what became of that!). Fanfiction has truly helped in the realization of my dreams, and I thank anyone for reading them.
Right now I'm working on THE KAKARROTTO SAGA, MUTANTS IN GUNSMOKE, SAIYA-MAN & THE LEGION OF SUPER-HEROES (I must admit this story is something of a disappointment, as I don't feel I've spent enough time on the Legionnaires, having devoted more on the DBZ cast. This 'X-over imbalance' is something I've hopefully remedied in my other stories. Fortunately, dear S&LSH fans, I'm gonna continue writing chapters to this fic, as I still haven't written a certain time-travel battle which was the major reason I made this X-over in the first place) while writing and planning others:

RESCUE IN LIMBO- While Tenchi and the New Mutants are hopping through dimensions, what is Ryoko and co. doing?

TWILIGHT- A climatic storyline that will bring the sagas of KAKARROTTO and RESCUE together for one big conclusion.

Presently untitled- A Rurouni Kenshin story concerning the Boxer Rebellion.

Presently untitled- RK, Inu Yasha, and the Charmed Ones...in the same story?!

Now I will admit that some stories may never be finished (or started). I also apologize for the slow pace it takes for my stories to get published. I write long, and am very, very sensitive about perfection. I hope you can bear your crosses of impatience for my sake.
