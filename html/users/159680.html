May 28, 2009

Okay... I know what I said about the planning. But I got distracted before I could get anywhere there. My problem was I had a general story in mind when I started but that... twisted as I started working on it and I didn't recognize my original goal in the new work. Also I mostly disappeared from the digital world when I was dealing with a stalker around Christmas time.

So, what you'd all like to hear! I've sat down and started writing DF again! There'll be a new opening chapter but things should stay the same after that through the Diagon Ally visit with minor changes.

Thanks for reading and special thanks to the review that reminded me to get off my arse and start working again!

Novemeber 13, 2008

I bet you all want to know how the stories are coming, don't ya?

...well... cringe I haven't been writing. Anytime I would even try I'd sit down and then be utterly incapable of getting anything out that I would be willing to post. Adding to that, I've moved several times since I've been writing and have managed to misplace my books... So I will be writing off of memory and using the wonderful HP Lexicon (Which can be found here: ).

If it's on my page... it's probably on hiatus. Life is way to hectic and I've gone through too many computers to try and get everything together. Anything besides Darkness Falls that I might have is barely accessible on my old laptop that sometimes will run in safe mode but only if you hold the power cord in a certain way. Fun. -_-;

Darkness Falls is going through an edit and I'm spending most of my time planning instead of writing right now. It shall not be abandoned! And the key to this is planning. Usually I just write where the story takes me. But with DF I'm planning out what's going to be happening through all seven years and maybe beyond. I'm also trying out British terms that me, as an American born and taught writer wouldn't particularly know. I've got a quick list of random words that can be found at . I'll also provide the definition for some of those obscure ones (like cloakroom. Really. I thought it was a closet. Turns out its more of a water closet) at the end of the relevant chapter.

Thanks for reading!
