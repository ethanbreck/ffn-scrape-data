Author has written 101 stories for CSI, StarTrek: Enterprise, Harry Potter, Firefly, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Battlestar Galactica: 2003, and Walking Dead.

Since my bio is once again hopelessly outdated, I thought I'd better fix it. I am:

- married

- mother

- owner of pets

- employed full-time

- currently too busy to write anything

I do appreciate the reviews though, and am amazed at how many people still read and comment on my stories, even the ones I wrote when I first started lo! these many years ago. I'd like to think my writing has improved somewhat.

For those of you wondering what's up with the fact that I include songs, etc. for a lot of my stories, it's mainly because I create soundtracks for them in my head. Sometimes the songs provide clues to what's happening, sometimes not. But -- for me -- they are always songs that fit the stories themselve. If you don't like music, sorry -- but you can chose not to read the lyrics if it bugs you. For those of you who do like music and know what I mean when I say I create soundtracks for almost everything in my life -- we could possibly be star-crossed friends, destined to only ever meet thorough our love of fanfiction.
