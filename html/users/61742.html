(Updated 3/27/15) Due to personal situations I have been offline for years, but I am back online now and officially coming out of hiding. I know I left a lot of people and fic hanging. I apologize profusely for that. I appreciate the reviews and encouragement to finish the fics still left unfinished more than any of you know. The good news is that even though I've been offline, I have been writing. I have a lot to type up and start posting. Once I get it all done, I promise never to post a work in progress again. Everything will be finished before I start posting it, even if I don't post it all once. This way everyone knows it will always get finished.

The following warning still applies: I'm a dedicated shipper. I have at least one for almost every show I watch. I review everything I read. So if I haven't reviewed, I haven't read it. The most likely reason is because I haven't gotten to it or there is a ship involved I don't want to read. I just felt like I needed to clarify here. I want people to know that I'm just one of those people who reads things and doesn't review. I promise I do.
