Well, given my pen-name here it should be "Let Me Entertain You" but life has been stressful and I love all the great writers here so "Let YOU Entertain ME". I need the distraction. I only read E/O, Amaro/O, Fin/O, Cassidy/O SVU, hopeless hetero that I AM BUT no offense to anyone otherwise, just doesn't float "my particular sea-going vessel". ;)

I'm an angst-whore -n-- love the very well-written violent, sexy, angsty, case-driven story. Stories of abuse/rape do not scare me off. I generally won't read same-sex simply because I personally don't get much out of it. Mindless fluff, "the gang as kids", E/Octomom (excepting the occasional well-written oNE KID born to the two or conceived thru rape and he or esp. Cassidy or Amaro help take over). Really Fin -- I love in the brotherly over-protective bad-ass w/ a heart of gold role rather than romantic. Elliot my fave pairing no doubt. The "suits" she's been with turn me off (tho her actual husband's a hottie) - I mean, Bill Pullman? Please. Even Harry Connick -- just too tame. I want a HARD male edge for Liv's love interest and overall El fits that bill better than anybody. Donnelly (Judith Light) or Sonja (C. Lahti) would be interesting in that type of role as well - but if it turns romantic, just doesn't do it for me in particular (tho I knowthose that write it that way have PLENTY of fans).
