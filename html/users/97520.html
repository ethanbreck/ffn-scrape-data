Author has written 9 stories for Digimon, and Attack on Titan/進撃の巨人.

A major overhaul of my profile was long overdue. For those of you just joining us, I'm Gaia. I've been a member of FFN for over a decade, mostly reading fics but writing a few of my own. (Mostly in the Digimon category; I've just recently dabbled in Attack on Titan.) I have 2 muses: Gwen, my grammar check, and G, my...uh, everything else. (G: I prefer a-hole extraordinaire.) Oh yeah, Gwen also doubles as a censor and won't let me cuss in anything I write. That might change eventually, who knows.

I prefer watching subtitled anime, but for the sake of consistency from when I wrote based on the dubs, my latest updates for TNRFTW have stuck with dub names and dialogue.

Writing's always been a hobby of mine, so don't expect fast or frequent updates unless I'm feeling super inspired. More often than not, it's taken a backseat to everything from fansubbing (retired from that now, btw), WoW, school, my job, and my family (I'm married and have 1 kid.) Feel free to drop me a line about anything concerning my writing except for status updates. If you want to know when [insert story]'s next chapter's going to be posted, just bugger off. It'll be out when it's ready.
