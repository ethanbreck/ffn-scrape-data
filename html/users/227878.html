despite my ramblings, I will be trying extra hard to write out anything that comes to my mind. You, my readers and reviewers (espepecially the latter) are my life when it comes to my stories. It's a spectacular feeling and I'll hope you'll do me the honor of letting me get it with one of your spectacular reviews.
