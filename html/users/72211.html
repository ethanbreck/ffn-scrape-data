I'm ashamed to be back in "business" but I'm currently working on a NaruSasu fanfic (from Naruto, fyi). "Home of the Sun" will be the ONLY fic I'm working on now because I really do not have time. I'm getting ready to apply to Grad school and the only reason I'm even working on this story is because my plot bunnies are just way too hyper. I'm hoping to finish the entire story (8-10 chapters) before summer ends, but I know that's impossible especially since I'm also preparing for the GRE in september. So let's say end of the year.

About my unfinished/planned Rei/Jadeite (sailor moon) fics... sorry but I probably will not work on them in the foreseeable future. Maybe in 6 years after I finish grad school? lol, we'll see.

About my planned Taito (digimon) fics, I pretty much don't remember my original plots anymore but I would like to write "Ruby Falls" if inspiration hits.

About my unfinished Ranma/Akane (ranma 1/2) fics. I really like "A Different Life," "The Phoenix and the Dragon Trilogy," and "Angel of Vengeance" so I will probably re-write what I already have and work on more in the future. When? Depends. If I can get my applications out of the way early, I'll start working on re-writes next Feb-March. Let's keep our fingers acrossed.
