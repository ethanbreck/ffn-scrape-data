Author has written 1 story for Inuyasha.

From the time when I was still young, I could recall watching anime shows from a time when they were still called "Japanese animation". I remember my some of my favorite shows were Astroboy, Macross, Robotech, and Candy Candy. So I was an Otaku even before I knew what an Otaku was.

It's always amazes me to see how much the Anime community has grown over the years, and it truly warms my heart that society has finally accepted this wonderful artform into it's culture.

Some of my favorite titles right now are:

Inuyasha, Fruits Basket, Peace Maker Kurogane, Escaflowne, Slayers, Kare Kano, Azumanga Daioh, Charcoal Feathers, Spirited Away, and Here Comes Greenwood.

I'm always looking for new titles to watch, so if you guys have any recommendations, please feel free to email me at: Oneechan25@yahoo.com .
