Hello, everyone. Yes, I rewrote this. O.o I figured that it's been almost two years, so I need a new bio description; lots of stuff happens in two years, after all. I'm currently 17, a junior in high school. Ficwriting is a sort of a hobby of mine, but a rather neglected one, I'm afraid. My inspiration doesn't come very often right now. I have, however, been making an effort to at least update my two most popular fics at a somewhat regular interval. ...okay, so it's not regular at all. --;

My hobbies are listening to music, RPing online via IMs or chat rooms, and, most importantly, drawing. I have a website that is linked to my bio. It's under construction but is accessible. O.o just don't yell at me about the layout. I don't know what happened to it and am too lazy to fix it. Oh, the site's for an RP world I came up with, but there's other stuff in there as well.

Eh, any suggestions or comments about any and all of my stories are more than welcome, just E-mail me. I don't bite. Who knows? You suggestion may become a part of the story. ^^; Don't be surprised if it does.

~`~`~`~`~
Slayers is my favorite series right now, so here's basically what to expect in its fics:

I try to write in-character, especially if the situation is feasible. Frozen Summer is my serious fic. I am trying to write the mazoku in-character, but I add some human characteristics to them so they are easier to relate to. I'm going to end up bending some rules, though. Now, about my pairing preferences and favorite characters:

Hellmaster Phibrizzo: he's my favorite; I don't remember when I first started obsessing. Sometime this past year, I suppose. Who doesn't like kawaii homocidal maniacs?

Dynast Grausherra: Also my favorite, tied with Phibby.

I like the mazoku so much better than the humans.

Pairings: (in no particular order. I support some and not others. Read my comments to figure out which is which.)

Dynast/Sherra: I know mazoku can't love, but it is called fanfiction for a reason.

Lina/Gourry: I have no objections to this. Besides, they're paired up in the anime and manga...

Amelia/Zelgadis: I have no objections to this, either.

Zelgadis/ original char.: It really irks me when authors have Zelgadis see a girl and then presto! He's in love! NOT GONNA HAPPEN. He isn't trusting. At all. He won't fall in love at first sight.

Xelloss/Filia: It can't happen. People will be angry for my saying this, but it's not possible. I don't have any objections to reading fics with this pairing, as long as it's not overdone, but, for the record, it's NOT POSSIBLE.
These two hate each other. Genuinely.

hmm...those are my fave/"trad." pairings, and I'm not going into the other possibilities.

There is one other thing that bothers me, though. The fanfic Xelloss. For those of you who have never seen the anime or read the manga (I doubt they've dubbed the manga that far yet; all I have is stuff with just Lina and Gourry. And Zelgadis!!) Xelloss isn't a pervert. He doesn't flirt with Lina that much at all. He doesn't flirt with Filia at all, from what I have seen. (I've seen all of Try, all of Next, and about half of the first season) He angers her because he feeds on anger. Although he is a masochist, I have never seen him comment on how "good" it feels. He's more subtle than that. Oh, and he CAN'T leave Beastmaster's service; she owns him. Even if he was half human (which he isn't) she would still own him. Nor can he disobey her. It's as impossible for him to disobey her as it is for fish to breathe air.

Ok, make that two things: Fics where the dark lords consider each other their "enemy." The dark lords under Shabby (sans Garv)get along fine. Shabby wouldn't have made them so they'd fight.That would be a waste of time and energy on Shabby's part. It would be like Sherra and Grau fighting over territory. The only reason Garv was able to turn against Shabby was because his mazoku soul merged with the human one. (This wouldn't be true with half-human servants for reasons regarding the circumstances around their creation.) Lei Magnus Shabranigdo -is- conscious in his inprisonment,and I KNOW that Shabby is in control of the mind and body. Lei Magnus Shabranigdo informed Phibby of Lina's ability of casting the Giga Slave. So it's not like the Dark Lords have no leader, either.

Okay, I'll bore you with my rant no longer.
