Dude, I completely spaced out and missed my three-year anniversary on FF.n. Which was like, a month ago.I first joined this community on March 23, 2002. 'Tis a momentous occasion, but I don't have any fics to update to celebrate it. Hm...

So I used to have one of those huge long bios containing my entire life story, but then I decided that being mysterious is cool, so I shortened it. =P I am a teenage girl living in the USA. I've been writing fanfiction for two years. I love writing songs, stories, poems, fanfics; anything, really. I play guitar and accordion and love acting. I want to be a film/theater director when I grow up.

In my spare time I enjoy kicking tractors, angering Republicans, yodelling, summoning naked demons of unsurpassable beauty, and playing with cats. I write mostly LotR fics because LotR is my favorite fandom. I don't always stick to canon, though. I'm more of a humor writer, though I hope to write some serious fics in the future. (Fat chance...)

Oh, and I also have to my sisterly duty... If you like the Matrix and/or Final Fantasy, go check out my bro Yar Kramer's fics. He could do with some reviews. ;)

There. Now you know absolutely nothing about me. =D
