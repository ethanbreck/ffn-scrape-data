My stories:

All my Potter fics I wrote in the years 2001-2005 when I was young like teenage young. So don't judge too harshly. :) They aren't as refined as they should be nevertheless they we're written with love. Mad snogging obsessed love.

The Path of The Grey I wrote after falling back in love with Star Wars because of The Force Awakens. I have always had a special place in my heart for the galaxy far far away. My first crush ( aside from Peter Pan) was Darth Vader. Yup I loved that wheezing butthead even at seven years of age. The story involves small aspects of EU and both animated series. The aspect I focus most on, not from the movies, is the Nightsister who I don't believe get enough love outside of Clone Wars.

All my Gilmore Girls fics were spawned because of A Year in the Life. There were parts that were brilliant and some that weren't. But, I was highly inspired by GG in my writing. So, I had to take a crack at the quippy, pop culture soaked dialogue.
