Writing Details -
I've written in a number of fandoms, but right now my focuses are largely in Dark Angel, Supernatural, and True Blood/Sookie Stackhouse. I am still planning to work some more on my remaining Veronica Mars WIP, Only Hope.

I post all of my finished work at LiveJournal - also listed as my website above in my profile. I try to keep things updated here as best I can, but occasionally, I do fall behind. I love feedback of any nature, so long as its constructive, so please feel free to leave me your thoughts about what I've written. I'll be more than happy to hear from you and respond to you as soon as I'm able.
