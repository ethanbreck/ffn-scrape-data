Author has written 6 stories for Lord of the Rings, Faculty, and Misc. Movies.
I am a 21 yr old writer/artist/singer who is obsessed with anything from the 1960s. My favorite band is the Monkees. I have my own website dedicated to the group. Check it out.

I also love the CLASSIC Star Wars trilogy. Do not mention any prequals in my presence.

I have been a fan of Lord of the Rings for about 2 years now. I play Frodo Baggins and the other hobbits in many role-plays. I hope to write simple fun fictions for all to enjoy, and I hope you all enjoy them as well.

I have many other stories in the process of creation, including a more adult Lord of the Rings/Hobbit story. Every now and then, a writer must experiment outside their normal boundries in order to grow. We shall see how I do.

Special Thank yous to Lenna (legolas little angel) and Tulip/Iris (Tulip Proudfoot) for helping me by either proofreading and giving me story suggestions. Please check their stories out. They are listed under my fave authors section.

and lastly. I am co-writting a story with a role-play friend of mine. Kalista. It is an AU pre war LotR story where Frodo meets Kalista and the friendship that evolves from the meeting. As I said, it is AU. Kalista is actually Sam's older sister. This story is based on the role-plays that we are both in together and we loved the chemestry between the two that we had to write their story down. please check it out. the story is listed in the fave story section as "when the Dawn Comes."

thank you!

~ Frodo Baggins1982 ~
