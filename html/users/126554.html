Author has written 1 story for Blue's Clues.

If you remember Buffy: Unit G, it was leading up to the revelation that Dracula was really Sauron from LOTR reincarnated, and he was bringing forth horror and fantasy's most powerful wizards to resurrect Morgoth. Eventually, he would have succeeded, and Morgoth would have destroyed the Zoalords with the insane amount of power he was given in his resurrection. He would have possessed Archanfel and sent out a mental command to kill every Zoanoid on Earth. Eventually, the army of heroes would have destroyed him and his army. That one was getting too big too fast and I decided to move on to bigger, better things.

I moved onto screnplays. I have three now. Combined together they would equal one complete work, but right now- I have a ways to go and I alternate between the three of them. But I'm still not above taking a break and writing down an amusing fanfic idea. Fanfic, however, doesn't push me as hard as real writing does. Now matter what, I always rush fanfics a lot faster than my regular work. While it may sound like I'm covering my butt in case you read my work and say "Pshh! That idiot can't even write and he wants to be a screenwriter?" I merely point this out so you know where the majority of my writing time goes.

That said, I still want to entertain you, and all I ask is constructive criticism.
