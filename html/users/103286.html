Author has written 15 stories for Sailor Moon, Magic Knight Rayearth/魔法騎士レイアース, Final Fantasy X, Card Captor Sakura, and Harry Potter.

My biggest pet peeves: bad grammar and spelling (I can excuse them if you're an ESL speaker, but it's still frustrating). I also get frustrated by fangirl Japanese (I admit I'm guilty of it... but come on... it's a huge problem for continuity... write in all one language unless you've got multilingual people (e.g. Heero/Hiiro screaming "Duo no baka!" makes sense; Quatre saying the same thing is absolutely ridiculous). And if you're going to use it... at least be CORRECT. I'm not claiming to have perfect Japanese. But I like proper grammar, usage, syntax and spelling in ANY language.

I'm a huge sucker for fluff. I love yaoi. I like writing angst but hate reading it.

Favorite pairings: GW 1x2x1, 3x4x3, 5x6x5; HP H/D and H/SS; SM H/M and U/M; FFX Y/T; CCS S/S and S/T; MKR E/L; MTT TakeoxAburatsubo, AburatsuboxNanaka; Earthian C/K and C/M

I live in the eastern US. I love math and foreign language. I don't write as much as I used to. And I'm very grateful if you're even reading this.
