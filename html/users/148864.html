This profile definitely needs updating. Ok, I'm (almost) a junior in highschool and granted I'm not the greatest writer (or not even one of the not ok writers) but I try (though I have a tendency to fail). If you haven't already noticed from the first couple of sentences, I'm a pretty depressed individual (which sometimes fuels some of my deeper stories). But I do like to write comedies because they lift my spirits and I've had some compliments on how amusing they are. I do have this horrible tendency to start a story and never complete it but I'm very proud of how far I've gotten with Disney Demons and I actually know who I'm going to end it, so...yay!
I'm a huge anime fan and my favorite series are:
Kamikaze Kaitou Jeanne
Inu Yasha
Ayashi no Ceres
Cowboy Bebop
Tokyo Babylon
Gravitation
...and much more that I can't recall at the moment.
I also am a movie fan (have the greatest idea for a Mummy III and X-3) and television adict. I am completely obsessed with Gilmore Girls and currently am hooked on Dawson's Creek (I'm becoming too sad for my own good). I like most other shows on television as long as they aren't so stupid that they are not funny.
I also write poetry and have a short story on Fictionpress.net so try and visit those. Well, I hope you enjoy my writing and remember, reviews are the sorce of my happiness...which is probably why I'm depressed. Toodles!
