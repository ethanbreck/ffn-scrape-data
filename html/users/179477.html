Hi! I'm Kristen, but I am more commonly know as Mirai Noah in some circles, most circles... Gundam Wing was one of my favorite anime series, (hence the pen name; I'd love to change it, since it's a little too long but I keep it anyway). I'm a strange young woman whose main hobbies are watching TV/movies/anime, reading books and manga, listening to music, surfing the internet, learning new languages, and cosplay. I am basically a geek/nerd/dork, with a Bachelors of Art degree in Japanese and Spanish, and I have a Masters in Library and Information, with a concentration is archival studies.

My all-time favorite anime is Gundam, and I have seen every series. I love the original Mobile Suit Gundam as well as Gundam SEED. I love all most every Gundam series (yes, that includes both UC and AU series). My top favorite characters are Murrue and Mwu and Mirai and Bright. My favorite non-Gundam anime series is probably Legend of the Galactic Heroes. My main fandom nowadays is Downton Abbey (I am a big Anna x Bates shipper). I also love The X-Files, Harry Potter, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Avengers, Avatar/Korra etc. I'm really into J-Pop, especially Hello!Project, and also the Eurovision Song Contest.

I hardly write original stuff, but you can go to http://www.fictionpress.com/ladyunechanakamirai to read what original stuff I have.

I'm normally a fan of canon couples. I'm like some slash, and I'm tolerant. For example, I just simply wouldn't read or a review fanfic about pairing I don't like. Even if I hate a pairing, I'm not going to read a fanfic solely to bash it. You can like whatever you like, and I can like whatever I like, and we can all live in harmony by being tolerant of each other. I take the internet seriously; be kind online, be kind in real life.

Most of my fanfiction is pretty bad (i.e. horrible). Please note that most of the fanfiction published here was written in middle and high school. I also, well, suck at writing in general. I know that I haven't posted anything in awhile, and that's because everything I write sucks. But that doesn't mean I don't read fanfiction. I love it death, and check this site dozens of times a day.

FF.net once said to shorten our bios, so we can "showcase" our work. Well, my work sucks, so I don't care. The link that you want to click is "Favorite Stories". That's where all the good writing is.
