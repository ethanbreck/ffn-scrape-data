Author has written 6 stories for Parodies and Spoofs, and Harry Potter.

I'm seventeen, a freshman at UC Santa Cruz. Writing is only one of my hobbies, and though I like to think I'm good at it, it's unfortunately not something I spend a ton of my time on. Especially recently, my main efforts are focused on college, which means basically going to classes, surviving dining hall food and bus strikes, meeting people, partying out, occasionally doing homework, and playing insane amounts of Ultimate Frisbee. I doubt I will post anything new on this account for a very long time, but that's mostly because I'm moving on from fanfiction. If you like my writing, you can find me at fictionpress.com (name still Chunky Chihuahua).

I currently have six stories posted on this account. Some will never be finished; others already are. Check here for updates on how writing is going on them!

The Stupid Kid is a one-shot and was never intended to be continued. Therefore it is finished.

The Fourth Animagus is quite old, and I no longer like it. Therefore I refuse to finish writing it. It was posted on "pottersues" (livejournal.com), which makes me laugh because I'm one of her many minions. I'm not offended, but take it as more proof as to whythat storydoesn't deserve to be finished ;).

Tears of Blood is roughly two years old, and oh man has my writing style and interest changed since then! I have finally finished it! THANK BOB. It was taking me way too long, and I'm afraid the ending leaves much to be desired, but at least there is an ending. Thanks to my reviewers for forcing me to finish that.

Life at Burnside is quite recent, but I've had an epiphany about it (namely, it isn't going anywhere) and I'm rewriting it. I want to take it down because I'm going to COMPLETELY rewrite it, but a lot of the ideas in the first four chapters will remain the same, so really that will be confusing... But I hate taking things down and I'm not going to. So I ask that you don't read that one unless you REALLY want to, because a better version will be posted later. Much later. Like when I'm already accepted into colleges and have nothing to worry about except graduating and AP tests. Maybe even after that...

Oops is a one-shot that I wrote in response to a challenge in 30minutefics (livejournal.com). It was just a fun thing that's not like what I usually write, but reviews are very welcome :). No, I'm never going to expand on it, so please don't ask me to.

The Happiest Person in the World is another one-shot written for a challenge in the same LJ. It's just something a little different that I thought might be interesting.

I like reviews. Everyone does. Please review (or contact me at jodragonfire@hotmail.com, or leave a comment on my LJ--name is yipeeskipp). That would please me greatly, and I'd probably be so pleased with a helpful review that I'd go leave helpful reviews for you. It's a beautiful world like that :D

"Sarah lives with Bradley now, I guess that is how she wants her shit / He needed a place to stay, so it's of mutual benefit" -- Wheatus (Love is a Mutt from Hell)

Actually, I much prefer my originals works to my fanfics anyways, so if you at all like my style, PLEASE go to that account! It doesn't get nearly enough hits, cause fictionpress just isn't that popular.

There, I wrote a bio.
