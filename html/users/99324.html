A fan of all things sci fi and fantasy I was working on a little project called The Daughters of the Silver Millennium but is quickly lost steam. It was fun at first and there elements within it that were recognisable I picked bits and pieces from all my favourite sci fi and fantasy shows, chopped them all up and put them in stew, stirred it around and called It "The Daughters of the Silver Millennium."

A long time ago my first love was a fan fiction series called The Adventures of Sinbad. Check out the website and look for the episodes "The Phoenix," To Rise from the Ashes" part 1 and 2 and finally "Call of Destiny."

Also check out Eve: The Virtual Series. My baby for the last decade.
