About Me*

I'm Kirjava-chan, and I'm a 16-year-old girl. I absolutely loooooooooove words, and anything connected to them, whether its reading, writing or talking. I'm a horrible typist, too. Ha...

Looks? Well... think Natalie Portman meets Brittany Murphy before her morning coffee meets CCS's Rika. Yeah... something like that, though more Natalie Portman than anything. And I'm willing to bet I'm taller than any of them. (I must add, albeit reluctantly, that the popular opinion of late has been that I bear a certain resemblance to one Eliza Thornberry--though I'm not a readhead, I don't wear braces or glasses, I don't have freckles, my overbite was (painfully) corrected a long time ago, I haven't had braids in my hair since 1996, most animals despise me on sight, my voice is way deeper, I'm about 20 meters taller than her, my nose is sharp enough to cut glass with, and my head is rather...round-er looking.)

(I'm still finishing all those fics, though, as soon as I have a minute) I absolutely love Amelia At-Water Rhodes' books (especialy Demon in my View), His Dark Materials, Artemis Fowl, and A Great and Terrible Beauty.

Movies? Pretty much all; I adore cinematography. My current faves, however, are Legendes of the Fall, Pirates of the Caribbean, Van Helsing, and MOULIN ROUGE. Also, anything Johnny Depp, because he rules all. (so there!)

Harry Potter? Like the books, hate the movies, LOOOOOOVE Order of the Phoenix. Oh, and I worship Alfonso Cuarón! He totally saved the Harry Potter movie series with his oh-so-wonderful Harry Potter and the Prisioner of Azkaban.

Writting? A book. A bunch of them, actually, so I hardly ever have a chance to work on fics. Sorry. I WILL finish them, though. (I know I said that a year ago, (when I last updated anything) but I really WILL finish them soon enough. Summer's coming!)
