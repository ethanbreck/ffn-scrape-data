Author has written 26 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Veronica Mars, Dragon Ball Z, Wrestling, Office, and American Horror Story.

I'm a 30-year-old lady with two kids, a dog, a husband, and deep love for fandoms and fanfiction. It has been a long time since I wrote anything, but you can never really stay away, can you?

I used to write fanfiction back when I was in middle school and early high school. Most of that's since been removed from this page because I absolutely died from embarassment when I reread them recently.

My favorite things to write are character studies and one-shots - especially with rare characters and couplings - but I really admire someone who can craft a lengthy, well put-together, epic fanfic. I think what attracts me to writing fanfiction the most is the whole "getting into their heads" aspect of writing for characters. I really dig seeing things from different perspectives, trying to fit the right dialogue in a scene or imagining the mannerisms that would go into each interaction. I intensely value great characterization and keeping a story canon.
