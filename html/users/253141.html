Ok, my dull and uninteresting space to occupy

Name: For all intents and purposes here I am Seraya

Age: Probably too old to enjoy Tamora Pierce but the woman is timeless! You can't grow out of her!

Fav Books: Beside all the Tamora Pierce books, her Tortall series being my favourite (still haven't read Terrier yet, sniff) and the Song of the Lioness being my very very favourite. Go Alanna, she's awesome. Anyway, I also have a penchant for Russian literature, Dostoyevsky being my favourite, I also love the playwrite Chekov, the poet Pushkin, and Tolstoy isn't too bad, fairly dramatic though. Also Paulo Coelho, he's a Brazillian writter and he is my idol!!!! Harry Potter are also a fab series in my mind.

Music: Rock, punk and alternative indie stuff. Fav bands at the moment are Karnivool and Behind Crimson Eyes, my long term fav band are the Distillers.

My Stories

I haven't written anything in...ohhhhhhhh, a good 4 years on this site. Not too sure why. I lost touch with it somehow. So my last story I wrote 'Do You Believe in Second Chances?' Was suddenly ended abruptly, so I appologise. I have been flirting with the idea of revamping it (I'm a lot older and wiser now...cracks into hysterical laughter). But that may never happen.

The others that I wrote way back when will never be elaborated on, lengthened, revamped or otherwise tampered with. They're long dead.

My new story 'Walking Within Dreams' is the first fanfic I've written in a helluva long time, so I'll just see where that one goes.
