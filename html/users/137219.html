Hi -- Welcome to my profile page. The last time I updated this was ages ago... I guess I'm still 22, right? Lol, not quite, but not as old as you might think. I'm in kind of a long-term hiatus from fanfiction, HP especially, considering the result of HP VII. Not a Ron/Hermione fan, by far, which I might be able to tolerate, if it weren't for the train wreck that is the 2D Harry/Ginny. I just ... it fizzled, guys. I still love writing, and have hope to continue my stories someday, but I have an incredibly bitter taste in my mouth right now. I have worked in other genres. Gone with the Wind, The Hunger Games, and Doctor Who, off and on. Right now, I'm on a Bones kick, because, heck ... David Boreanaz' Booth really does it for me. Good Lord.

Drop me a note to say Hi! I would love to hear from you!

Steph
