Wow, it's late 2005, and I forgot that I once lived here. Fan fiction isn't really my thing any more, I'm more of a tech-head/podcaster, but I guess I could write one last story. I'll probably do a crossover spoof or something.

History of writing:

When I first signed up at FFN, it was as nothing more than a reviewer. However, when I posted my first fic (The Death of Tracy, since lost to time.) I started to love the writing more and more. I dabbled in Pokemon, until I grew and came to my sense, at which point I traded in Pokeballs for Rings... Lord of the Rings, that is. As of now, I have few plans for posting anything; I'm currently at FictionPress.com, working on poetry and such.

Don't despair, though. I shall be back someday! I'll update The Gimli Show, Sauron: Under The Microscope, The Silm for Morons... just not now!

'til next time...
