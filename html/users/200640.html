Hi, I'm Michiru! I'm a huge fan of Star Wars, FRIENDS, Crossing Jordan and Anime/Manga. I also love to laugh. I will take a good comedy over drama any day! I'm one of those geeks that record stand-up comedy on Friday Nights just so I can laugh whenever I'm bored. And aside from those, I like to read and write stories. But my #1 passion is singing. Currently, I'm in school - looking towards Law School and business, so I've been really busy with school and work. I also work in a department that has a lot of account testing and paper work up the wazoo! So yeah - back to my point - BUSY! I'm also taking two language classes: Japanese and Korean. Can you tell I love languages?

Currently, I'm heavily addicted to dramas (as if I don't have enough in my life). It's got me to bond with my family, and they're just so interesting. To name a few I liked: It Started with a Kiss, Love Contract, Mars, My Name is Kim Sam Soon, Sassy Girl Chun Hyang, My Girl, Full House, Sweet 18, Wonderful Life, Hana Yori Dango, Pride, Long Vacation, Beautiful Life, Nobuta wo Produce, Kurosagi, Mukodono, Gokusen, and Kimi wa Petto.

Current Favorite Books (coz I'm tied): Memoirs of a Geisha (I don't know why half the movie cast is Chinese...I hate Asian type-casting...), Pride and Prejudice, Marrying Mozart, Wind-Up Bird Chronicles (a confusing and risque Japanese novel but awesome), Angels & Demons prequal to another fave The Da Vinci Code, Lovely Bones, and Birth of Venus. (I don't have a lot of free time, but when I can, I love to read. I'm also one of those people that see a book on display and have to have it.)

Favorite Play (Thus far - hands down!): The Crucible ("She's a whore!")

Favorite Music artists: I love a lot, but I especially love Yoko Kanno's work. She's unbelievable!

Animes I like (that I can think of off-hand): Rurouni Kenshin, Cowboy Bebop, Tsubasa Chronicles, Cardcaptor Sakura, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Macross Plus, Tokyo Babylon, X, Bubblegum Crisis 2040, Full Metal Alchemist, Excel Saga, Big O, Magic Knight Rayearth, Inuyasha, Kare Kano, Sailor Moon (Gateway anime), Noir, Witch Hunter Robin, Vampire Princess Miyu, Outlaw Star, Record of Lodoss War, Escaflowne, Fruits Basket, Chobits, Trigun, Samurai Champloo, Fushigi Yuugi, and Gundam Wing.

Fave Anime Movies: Anything Miyazaki made (ex: Princess Mononoke, Spirited Away, Howl's Moving Castle)

Favorite Mangas: CLAMP series, Itazurana Kiss, MARS, Marmalade Boy, INVU, Full House, Now

Fave TV Show: FRIENDS and Crossing Jordan

Like I said before, I love to sing, but I like to write and read books. Not a big fan of romance novels, or "pocket books" as my cousins like to call it, unless they are really one of the better fiction. I don't think I'm an especially good writer, but it's still something I enjoy and would like to share with others. So I hope a lot of people read and enjoy.

I'm sorry for keeping people on hold! I'm working many hours and dealing with college. Plus, I have a small-yet existant-life. I've tried to cut down on activities because it's so stressing. I will post my stories as soon as I think they are post worthy. Thank you for your patience.

Stories I'm Working on that are Posted

- Bound to Vast Expectations - Ex Parte (CB) - Currently on hiatus
- Dangerous Beauty (RK) - Currently on hiatus

Unfortunately, if you haven't noticed, all my stories are currently on hiatus because of problems with my schedule - it has no holes in it! It's just work, school, friends and family. If I want to get to Waseda University, I've got to work hard, y'all!

Also, one of my fave stories not on the list is "Cruel Fate" by BunnyRena and another story that was about Anakin and Amidala that's not on fanfiction.net anymore. It was the only one I liked, too. I'm not a fan of Star Wars fanfiction for some reason... No offense.

My special thanks go to Karisma and my onee-chan, Zeero Yuy (Pulse) Read their stuff! They're very talented. Thanx for reading and giving me pointers when I was starting out.

And thanks to those who read and review my stories!
