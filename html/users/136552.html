Torina "Tori" Pegasus:
Story: Shining Friendship (Yu-Gi-Oh!)
Tori Pegasus lost her parents in an accident when she was five and ended up living with her uncle, Maximillion Pegasus. At 15, she got the guts to run away... and life has never been better.

Gokea Li:
Story: Heart of the Tiger (Dragonball Z)
Gokea is a 23-year-old saiyaness in a 16-year-old's body, thanks to the Time Warp Chamber she spent ten years training in... in order to beat the man that killed her mother.

Sarah Kaiba:
Story: Change of Heart, I Know Everything, Bring Me To Life (Yu-Gi-Oh) and some of Princess Ria's Yu-Gi-Oh! Fics
Separated from her family at birth, Sarah lived in America most of her life with abusive step parents and little hope for the future. Scared of just about everything, she has been called a "yowamiso," or weakling... but when her step parents died, she took her hope and went to Japan in search of her twin brother, Seto and younger brother, Mokuba. Slowly, she breaks away from the "weakling" she once was and should transform into a powerful Sorceress... but she died...

Koujo, age 5:
Story: Daddy's Little Girl (Yu-Gi-Oh!)
Yami's only daughter, Princess Koujo is a mischevious six-year-old who loves games as much as her father, her favorites being Tag and Hide and Seek. Her sweet innocence can bring a smile to almost any face... except for the ones that want her dead.

Koujo, age 16:
Story: Asayake (Yu-Gi-Oh!)
The sweet and innocent child has grown into a teenager... unfortunately she gets to be sixteen forever, but that's a different story. Anyway, 5000 years after her death, she is the guardian of a young girl who looks and acts like her... boy, does she have her hands full.

Saya:
Story: Asayake (Yu-Gi-Oh!)
After being dropped in an orphanage by her grandfather, Koujo Tora became a gaming princess who idolizes Seto Kaiba and hopes to becomes just like him.
