LAST UPDATE: Thursday, July 26th, 2012

Hi, thanks for reading my profile. I am a 26 year old otaku. I live in the state of Oregon, in a small coastal town called Florence.

My interests include Anime, Manga, Video Games, Computers, Books, The Internet, Music, and much much more.

I have a lot of favorite anime and manga. The one I love most is Hokuto no Ken (Fist of The North Star). I also really like Naruto, One Piece, Bleach, Hajime no Ippo, Fullmetal Alchemist and many many more.

When it comes to video games, I love racing games and rpgs. My favorite racing games are the Need For Speed and Burnout series', and for RPGs, I like the Final Fantasy series, the .hack games, the Kingdom Hearts series, and many more. I also enjoy the occasional FPS, and enjoy music games such as Rock Band.

Music is a great passion of mine, and my favorite bands are Metallica and Dream Theater. I also really enjoy classic rock such as Queen and Led Zeppelin.

I havn't written anything in quite awhile, and mostly read these days. However, I have recently taken up writing a full fledged novel with a friend of mine. Creating a world from the ground up is a much harder task than I thought it would be. Hopefully, someday, I accomplish this task. If you ever see a novel with Fanfiction.net in its acknowledgements someday, then perhaps it might be my novel. :D
