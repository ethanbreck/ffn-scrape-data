Author has written 22 stories for Harry Potter.

I'm a 30-year-old woman, married and living in the UK. I've been reading and writing fanfiction for around 18 years, and my favourite pairings in the HP fandom are HP/SS, RW/DM, RW/SS, HP/DM DM/RL and SB/RL. Having said that, I'm open to new pairings that I stumble across, although I tend to read and write exclusively slash.

I have a degree in English and American Literature with Creative Writing and I work in publishing. I also write a lot of my own original fiction and screenplays, and I'm currently undertaking the screenplay for a fantasy TV show.

If there's anything you'd like to know about either me or my fics, then please feel free to drop me a message, I always love speaking to people who've read my stuff and want to discuss it. If you're looking for a beta, then check out my beta profile and see if I'd suit you. I'm not always marked as 'active', but send me a message anyway and if I'm not too busy with annoying life stuff, I might be able to take a look for you.

Follow me on Tumblr @willmoanbutdonothingtochange. I don't blog a whole lot, but I re-blog a lot of HP stuff and it's also a really good medium to communicate. I'd love to answer any asks you might have, either about me or my writing.

Also, as many of you know, I suffer from several chronic illnesses. If anyone would like any information regarding this then please feel free to contact me. I'm very open about my conditions and I'd like to spread a little knowledge or support those of you who are also struggling.

Artwork

People have expressed wishes to produce artwork for A Brave New World many times over the years, and I'd like to take this opportunity to give my unequivocal permission to anyone who'd like to to create art for any of my stories, all I ask is that you credit me and the story. I'd really love to see something for An Honourable Man, I think that could be quite special.

SnowKissYuki created a piece a number of years ago called 'Dancing Lessons', based on a scene from A Brave New World. It's so stunning and she really took the description I gave her and worked it into the drawing, it's just how I had the boys pictured in my mind - kireiyuki. deviantart. com / art/ Dancing - Lessons - 165943021 (just remove the spaces between each word as you can't post web pages on this site)

Also, a few years ago, TwilightxXxChild created a piece that went with A Brave New World, but these days I can't find it anywhere. If anyone has a copy or a way of getting to it, please let me know. It depicted Severus, Harry and Daniel together and was absolutely beautiful. I'd love to see it again, and to share it with those who haven't seen it.
