Greetings and Salutations Dear Reader.

I am, at best, a hack writer who enjoys what she does. I write when I feel like it and I write what I feel like. The majority of what I write is yaoi or has the overtones. If I write a threesome...trust me...the guys are goin' at it too.That being said Dear Reader, should this offend you do the SMART thing; hit the back button on your browser and ignore my stories. I won't be hurt if you do, for I shall never know, shall I?

I also tend to write only drabbles or very short pieces; I don't have the stamina or creativity to do long pieces. Long projects also inevitably require dialogue and I REALLY detest writing dialogue. So what you see here IS the "finished project"; anything that is up here will likely remain in the state it was posted in. That being said, I also go for long periods of time where I don't post anything. Its the way I work.

And a small request of your Dear Reader, if I may. Leave a worth while review for me please?

Loved the story? Don't just tell me you did, tell me WHY you did. Have something you didn't like about the story? Leave me a
good concrit. Flamers will be snacked upon with a good strong cup of Earl Grey tea to wash it all down. If you'd like to contact me outside of FF.net you can find various ways to do so on the blogs that are linked here.
