What I am Writing

Due to an overwhelming case of writer's block and an unsettling dissatisfaction with my current fandoms of choice, I am writing less and less fan fiction these days. Occasionally, I will write a one-shot and I may attempt a multi-chapter fic in the future. I also write original fiction which can be found over at fictionpress.com under the same penname.

What I am Reading

If you write Bleach, Final Fantasy VII, Fairy Tail, Samurai Deeper Kyo or Angel fanfic then you are probably on my reading list. I am however, very discriminate in my reading - I expect a story, no matter how long, to be well-written and engaging. Proper use of the English language is a must. I also have a tendency to like crack!ships.

06.15

Hello dear readers! I know it has been six months without an update, and I've touched on the reasons on Twitter (follow me, yo) but in case anyone is checking here as well, I haven't updated Love Be Yours for the following: 1. I suffer a chronic illness that fluctuates in severity, and in the late winter and early spring this year, it was quite severe. 2. I've had computer issues, and didn't have access to large chunks of my writing for nearly two months, and 3. I have begun writing an original non-fiction with the intent of publication, so that takes most of my writing time. However, do not despair, for I will have another chapter by the end of June! Because I am continuing the story, I am looking for a beta-reader. What I need is someone who is:

Very familiar with Fairy Tail and the characters. Ideally familiar with similar-style NaLu AUs and can warn me if my story starts to go anywhere that has already been covered (to a point). Good at finding punctuation errors.

I'm not worried about prior writing/beta-ing experience, so just drop me a PM and tell me why you'd be good for the job! Add any blog/profile/social media links, too, because I'd like to get to know you!

In the case of Taken Back: I don't know, man. It's looking pretty hopeless at this point. To continue the story would mean I needed to re-watch much of Season 5 of Angel and I just don't think I can do that. My heart can't take it. Well, it isn't a lost cause quite yet but I can't promise anything.

oOo

Thanks for taking the time to both read my fiction (and review, I hope) and look up my profile! If you liked my work, read and review my favorite authors, they are all highly deserving!

Thanks and Happy Reading!
