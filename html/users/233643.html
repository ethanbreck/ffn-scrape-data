Bits and Pieces: 19, female, been on this site since the tender age of 12, located in California/Rhode Island, aspiring writer (like everyone else)

Status, as of 8/13/09: WRITING MANY MANY DEAD-END ORIGINAL PIECES. Haha, but it's fun. Both fiction and creative nonfiction (the latter of which is now my potential major, thanks to an amazing intro class.) Officially no longer updating Bittersweet; sorry for everyone who's on alert because of that. I'm kinda-sorta-not-really back here...fanfiction was fun and certainly polished my skills, but it doesn't do as much for me anymore. Bittersweet in particular is a sort of shippy, fluffy, run-of-the-mill thing that doesn't hold my interest. If anything pops out of my head, it's sure to be along the lines of character-introspective one-shots, not lengthy plot-heavy clunkers. Stay tuned if that interests you :)

:D Reading, writing, doodlin', Kikyou/Inuyasha, Viaresu, sappy unrequited love stories, Nintendo, Zelda, katsudon (mmmmmm), shopping, scanlations, Yelp, buffets, BROWN UNIVERSITY, teddy bears, chocolate

D: Ignorant homophobes, math, committees, professors who all plan their tests on the same day, Rath, too much free time (yes, it does exist!), the postal system (sigh), OOC characters

Contact: My email-checking is almost as sporadic as my writing, but feel free to try. :3 I’m a pretty nice person. Email is somewhere on this page, I think.

Sites:
My DeviantArt - Art stuffs, occasional fic illustration
Utsukushii Youkai - Viaresu (Bierrez) shrine

Stories: I feel liberated. HURRAH for one-shots! o_o Currently churning out bits and pieces in spurts. Original fiction coming out of the whazoo.

And: Someone tried to steal my story. Legend, to be precise. This is a very, very scary thing. The sprawl of writing within Fanfiction.net is staggering; there's no way one author, alone, can possibly keep an eye out for plagiarism. And to be honest, it never, ever occurred to me that it might happen. But it did. Luckily, it appears that one of my readers (THANK YOU THANK YOU, WHOEVER YOU ARE) called out the author in question, who then contacted me with the most feeble of excuses ("I must have subsconsciously remembered..."). BEE ESS. Anyways, it's removed, it's terrifying, it's eye-opening. Just wanted to say.
