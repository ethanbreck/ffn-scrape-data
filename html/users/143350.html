3/29/2007- @_@ 3 months short of 2 years since the last update. I was surprised a few months ago when I recieved a review for my SKU fic. When I have time, I hope to go back, and revise GW and SKU chapters. After that, I hope to write more chapters (during the summer). Funny enough, I have been rewatching GW and Ronin Warroirs/YST.

I hope to watch all of Gundam Wing in Japanese so I can get a better feel for the characters. You loose stuff no matter how good a dub is. Even though I do have a small yaoi fangirl streak, I still don't think Duo and Heero are gay. I can see why some people hate Relena though. I still like her though. She she matures rapidly throughout the series. Also, while rewatching the series, I came to love it again, but this time I noticed how psychological the series really was. Also, I really can't tell if it was love between Heero & Relena. The creator only said that he was bad at working with love. It could be argued either way. I hope to change and reflect this in the fic.

With Utena, I keep starting and stopping the anime. I do own the manga though. I need to change stuff. I know what happens at the end, but I should just finish it straight through.

As for Ronin/YST, I'm still not sure if I'll be continuing the fic. If I do, it'll need MAJOR revisions.
