Author has written 3 stories for Farscape, and Law and Order: CI.

I've been an ardent reader of scifi/fantasy for years, ever since I first picked up the Dragonriders of Pern by Anne Mcaffrey.

Just recently found out about fanfic (don't ask me where I've been all this time, but it was dark), and was instantly fascinated.

I had been writing my own fanfic since I was 12, starting with Voltron of all things. Kept it up with Star Trek, Bab5, Farscape, etc... Realized that there were other people who were willing to read my stuff, so here I am!

Don't blame me for what comes out; sometimes I have to write this stuff down or it will keep me awake at night.
