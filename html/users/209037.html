I'm an American ex-pat that lives in Beijing, China. And you probably won't believe me but I'm the lead singer of a big band, voted the best band in Beijing 2016. Stevie Mac & The Mac Daddies, look us up!

I'm a geek. I love science fiction. Star Trek, Star Wars, Robotech, Firefly, and much more.

When I'm not doing my music thing, I'm writing fan fiction! Yep, really love to write!

My bread and butter is writing for play-by-email RPGs, which is a collabrative effort by several writers in creating a story that is grand in scale.
