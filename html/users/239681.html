~*Legacy Draft Outline*~

"What if" Just as the Jedi purge is beginning, the kaminoan prime minister realizes the error of his/her peoples ways. With Palpatine gaining power, the Kaminoans frantically try to get genetic samples of all The Jedi, and republic senators and representatives they can get a hold of in hopes of someday being able to rebuild the republic to its glory by cloning its greatest heroes. The Kaminoans, while still producing clone troopers, secretly produce a "back up republic" deep in the secret cloning chambers of a hidden city underneath kamino prime [their capital]. While developing the clones of the samples they could retrieve, Kamino is left desolated by a battle between the new empire and a newly formed rebellion. While this battle is a loss for the rebels. The empire is forced to rely on recruiting new solders, as their chief producer of clones has now been reduce to ruins. The Kaminoans are left to fend on their own, without cloning to build up their economy they are left in poverty. But Tuan We, the last prime-ministess of kamino. her final act before her people fall to shambles, is to lock up the secret cloning chambers fro the "back up republic" in hopes that no one will disrupt the embryos inside, the new hope for the galaxy.

2000 years later, the great Yoda Skywalker decedent of the great Luke Skywalker, and master Jedi, head of the new Jedi council on New Corascaunt. Is sent on a mission, to investigate a possible resurgence of the dark side on a distant planet in the outer rim. When his ship is damaged by espionage, he is forced to land on an uncharted water world [kamino]. Yoda feels a vergence in the force. And is compelled to delve deeper into the ruins of this forgotten planet. Along with his fellow Jedi investigators, they discover a recording by Taun We, describing the error of their ways and why they created the "back up Republic". Yoda and his Jedi followers find the genetic samples and the preserved embryos, Containing Old Jedi masters, knights and Padawans like, Mace Windu, Obi-wan Kenobi, Eeth koth, Plo koon, Adi Galli, Shaak ti, Aayla Secura and more including republic Senator Padme Amidala. Yoda believes the force brought him there for a reason. And so he decides to take the embryo’s [all 4, thousand or so of them] to New Corascaunt. Where the Jedi embryos will be hatched and raised in the teachings of the force but as for the embryo's of the senator's senator they to will be hatched and let free to lead normal life.

[About twenty years later] Aled Oden a powerful Jedi knight. Is sent on a mission into the unknown territory. To combat the new sith threat along with his child hood friends and fellow Jedi knights Mace Windu and Obi-wan Kenobi.

As you can tell all the clones assume their former's name, Aled Oden is a buffer character who bridges that gap of the full born Jedi and the cloned Jedi. He was born the same year as the embryo's Hatching so they all grew up together and was raised in the Jedi temple.

Aled was once sent on a mission as a Padawan, with his master Yoda Skywalker. To protect the young ambassador Padme Amidala. [She has taken on her former’s career as a politician.]And a small romance between her and Aled.but was put on hold when their paths went different ways some 5 years before. But now Aled most protect Padme, along his friends Obi-wan and Mace as they enter the unknown territory.
