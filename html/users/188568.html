Author has written 15 stories for Lord of the Rings, Stargate: SG-1, Saiyuki, Torchwood, Criminal Minds, Primeval, Alphas, and Teen Wolf.

I'm probably not really interesting enough that I need a bio, but I guess I'll throw a few things about me up here.

I'm 26, female, I live in Wisconsin, and I work at a group home taking care of disabled people. My penname is a reference to the fact that I like coffee a lot. Probably too much.

I don't write a lot any more but every once in a while something strikes my fancy and if I like what I've written I post it.

I prefer to write character-driven stories, and I really like hurt/comfort and angst genre things. I do not write too much in the way of romance. I won't write slash unless the characters are actually gay, or it is a very believable leap. I will never write m-preg cuz I find it rediculous. I prefer to keep things in-cannon.

I'm not a review junky. I will never abandon something just because I don't get enough reviews or if it gets bad reviews, and I will never require a certain amount of reviews before I post more of a story. Personally it always irks me when I see a writer doing that. (Sidenote- If your writing is fueled by reviews and nothing else, you are writing for the wrong reasons!) But I do really appreciate getting feedback, so if you're reading something of mine, I would love it if you have time to let me know what you think. I can handle critisism, and actually enjoy it, so don't be afraid to let me know if you don't like something so that I can know what to do to make myself a better writer. That being said (and I hope this doesn't turn you off to reading anything I've written) I do not find myself to be a very good writer. Not saying I suck or anything, pretty much everything I have up right now I like to some degree, but most of what I write isn't what I would consider high quality. Sidenote: I have deleated several of my older stories so if you came here looking for them I apoligize, but they are gone 'cause I didn't like 'em.
