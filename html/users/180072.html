Author has written 4 stories for Lord of the Rings, Warcraft, and BioShock.

The writer formally known as SeskiLexi.

Greetings and salutations. I am Lexi, Keeper of Memories, weaver of words, teller of tales. Currently, I write fanfiction for just about everything under the sun. I can not even hope to resist the siren song of fanfiction... I can only hope to become good at it. Someday. My stories, and my fics, have a tendancy to write themselves. This is because, more often than not, somehow one or more of the characters has found their way into my head and speak to me. Yes, I am crazy. No, I'm not joking. Currently, I've got quite a few people trying to take over my skull. Many, many people. Characters. Whichever. Also, some of my fics are written via RP with friends. And most of my fics wind up unfinished on my hard drive because I wind up distracted by a shiny new idea. I'm trying to finish. I'll post a warning for any of those I post. Anyhozzle, please, relax, read, and enjoy. And do comment... I like getting feedback of all kinds.

Also, I tend to lurk a bit. I do a lot of reading on this site for favorite fandoms (of which there are legion) but I don't generally comment on things. I am trying to improve on this, because I know how much comments mean to me, as well as honest critiques of my work. (You are free to dislike anything I write, just tell me why, so I can improve or give a bit of an explanation where needed.) Unfortunately, sometimes I switch back into literary editor mode (yes, I was an editor at one point) and can be a little... Vicious. I don't like being mean, so I try to reign that in and not get harsh when I do critique.

I am wishing quite sincerely I never deleted some of my old works. I miss them, and the files are long gone. Granted, I deleted them because I thought they were absolute dung, but still. We learn as we grow, and I should have kept the reminders of what my work used to be so I could compare it to now. I apologize that some stories are no longer around.

I also have a profiled on AdultFanFiction.net There, you will find works of mine that do not exactly meet FanFiction.Net's rating approval. Some will be/are entire stories that cannot be posted here, some are the stories posted here, with certain bits no longer omitted. There is a link below for anyone who would like to take a look, but please, do not feel pressured to do so.

Also, many thanks to the person who came up with the website www.750words.com and to my sister in law for telling me about it. That site is keeping me on track with my writing now, so I don't spend more time on other things. I can make sure to get my daily words in! For writers, check it out. It's a good way to help keep track, in my opinion.

Worth Her Weight in ADAM is complete. A one shot bit of a drabble I wrote after playing through BioShock and BioShock 2. I felt bad for the little sisters, and the splicers. Loosely based off of personal childhood experiences.

A Forest Full of Jade is complete. It is a bit of fluff I decided to work on after playing the Jade Witch questline in the Jade Forest. I wondered what happened to the rest of the cubs who came to life in her yard. This is what I came up with. Rated for violence, and death.

The Metamorphosis of a Quel'dorei Rose is a work in progress. It is the back story for one of my World of Warcraft characters, who plays a large part in several works in progress I am working on. It will follow the life of Talathiel Dawnbreeze and her family through the terrible siege of Quel'Thalas and destruction of Silvermoon, and the trying times that follow. Rated primarily for later chapters, and I doubt it will be for the faint of heart.

The Foundling is on a tentative hold. I was halfway through with the entire story when my computer ate the file and wouldn't regurgitate it, even under threat of percussive maintenance. I didn't even have my notes by that point, they got lost in a move, so I threw my hands up in despair and said "Frack it. I'm done.". I'll try to revisit it soon, but there are other stories that have been brewing. Update: Honestly, the only reason I haven't removed this by this point is because some people like it (yay! Thank you friends!) and because it is good for me to see past works. I found some scribbled notes, but I doubt this will be making a comeback any time soon.
