Hi! Mystical Angel’s the name! I've had a few pen names over the years.

My first pen name was: Poké Girl LOL
Mystical Angel's been with me since 2003. I used to have a 5 beside my name but very recently I discovered that I could finally drop the 5 so now I'm just "Mystical Angel".

I've been on this site for several years (2001), but I never published anything. I did in the past but I took them down because I wasn't really happy with them...

So, a little bit about me. I joined this site ages ago, at the tender age of twelve when I was obsessed with Pokémon. I'd been obsessed with Pokémon since I was ten/eleven. I got over it and recently I've gotten back into it! LOL

I've always believed that Ash & Misty are meant for each other. I stopped watching the show in grade 7, when the WB took a hiatus and kept showing repeats of the Johoto league. It took forever for them to reach Goldenrod City!! After about 4 months of waiting and life getting in the way I gave up. I then popped back a few years later, when the Advanced Generation started and I was like, "Where's MISTY?!" "Who's this other chick?!" T_T
By then I was a junior in high school so I had exams and stuff so I never got back into it.

Needless to say, since January of this year I was curious about Pokémon again, so I went to check out if Misty came back. I was saddened to hear that she still hasn't...:'(
But, I will hold out hope, that she will come back one day and she will finally tell Ash that she loves him!!

With Harry Potter, I've always loved it. In 2003 I discovered the world of Harry Potter shipping! At first I was an avid Draco/Hermione shipper. Then I switched teams and joined the Harry/Hermione ship and I haven’t looked back since. Needless to say, I was really crushed when HBP came out and my beloved ship had sunk...:( But thankfully, that’s what fanfiction’s for!! XD

With Naruto, I got into it around 2002-2003. Used to read it religiously but I find it’s not what it used to be...lol
I enjoy reading Naruto/Sakura fanfics. I think they're absolutely adorable!

And finally for Fullmetal Alchemist: A friend introduced me to it in 2005. I was hooked. Instantly! XD I had my wisdom teeth pulled out one week and this friend lent me all the episodes of the original anime and I finished them all within 4 days!!!! lol I also had a huge crush on Edward Elric, but I’ve gotten over him! XD

It kind of makes me sad inside that the manga is finished. I used to have something to look forward too every month. *emo tear* I love how it ended but I am so sad we didn't get an EdWin kiss!!!! ARG!!!! :@

For FMA, any pairing goes. (As long as it's not reallllllllllllllly weird! XD) Lol I even like yaoi pairings! XD This friend who introduced me to FMA is OBSESSED with yaoi and she’s a great artist.

So some stats about me,

Age: 21

Likes: Writing, Shopping, food( seriously I love it!), chocolate, school (Yes, I like school!!XD (But I hate exams. Who does?!))

Education: I just finished my first year of university. I’m majoring in Accounting. I’m not sure if that’s what I want to do for the rest of my life. But, we’ll see!!

Favourites: Pokémon, Harry Potter, Fullmetal Alchemist, Cardcaptors & Naruto

Pairings:
- Pokémon -- Pokeshipping all the way baby!
- Harry Potter -- Harry/Hermione
- Fullmetal Alchemist -- Royai, EdWin, AlWin
- Naruto -- NaruSaku

Uhhh...anyways, that's it! Enjoy my stories! :)
