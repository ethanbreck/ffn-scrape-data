Author has written 9 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Harry Potter, Yu-Gi-Oh, Lord of the Rings, and Fruits Basket.

I have one pet peeve:

Comparing one thing to something different and saying that they are 'exactly' the same.

For example, in my story New Life I talk about ‘talking about missions, weird trainers and looks to be working in/for a military or law enforcement organization of some kind’. First about the ‘weird trainers’ as you put it. There is only one guy; there is no plural in there at all. And nowhere does it say that this one guy is a ‘weird trainer’ or even a trainer at all. All this guy is is a person that raised Kris. As for the military/law enforcement thing, I just really wanted my Kris to arrest and beat up Harry’s uncle. Though that is not the only reason, but those I am not going to say for now. And the missions, well that will be explained later.

Now for the blond guy, though you know he has a name and it is Edward Conner, or Ed as he likes to be called. For your information, Edward is not the rich one, though he does say “the other houses we own” but that is mainly because they all live with each other and consider each other as family. And families tend to share things. Kris and Kai are the ones who ones most of the homes anyway. So you can’t really say that the blond is extremely rich.

Last we have the ‘Someone that seems to be REALLY good with knives as well as a fair hand at cooking’. Let me remind you that Kai is in a military organization. Of course there is someone who is very good at knives. Though one of the characters that has not appeared yet is better then Kai at that department. Anyway, if he was ‘REALLY good’ as you put it, then Touya should not have been able to dodge the knife. Also, there are many people with a ‘fair hand at cooking’, though I was sort of thinking of one of my friends when I wrote that so you really couldn’t say that I got that from Gundam Wing.

Along with this you can same that Sailor Moon and Tokyo Mew Mew are exactly the same, if you go of what this one person, Dreamweaver, told me about my stories. Since both of them have magic girls that transform to fight 'bad guys' from outer space. With these points we could say that they are indeed exactly the same.

So please, if you are going to review one of my stories, do not tell me that it is exactly like something else. Unless it has to do with someone else's story, then I would be happy to know that either someone else is using my stuff or I happen to be using theirs.
