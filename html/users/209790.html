Wow, about time I update this thing.

I am now married for almost four years and have a 10 month old son. I used to write Harry Potter fics, Ginny/Draco mostly, and finally realizing that JK wasn't going to make Ginny and Draco a romantic item in the stories, I decided to put away those fantasies and write real stories on fictionpress.com. However, Pirates of the Carribean has been such a great series of movies I decided to write some of those fics. First is the Rising of Davy Jones and when I'm done with that I'll start on another based around Will, the Captain of the Flying Dutchman in todays world.

Please review the stories you read so that I might get some feedback: what's good, what's not, etc...

That's all for now, bye!
