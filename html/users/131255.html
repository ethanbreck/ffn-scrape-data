Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, Song of the Lioness, and Star Wars.

A brief 'about me' session here: I'm 21 and a college junior/senior (who's keeping track?) from northeastern Ohio with too much schoolwork and not enough free time on my hands. I go to school at Whittier College in LA. I'm the sort of nerd you don't want to mix with coffee.

I don't think I need to comment on my stories. Not many of them have plots anyway, and consequently aren't waiting for updates. I'm back to actively writing Of Healing and Love, much to my distaste, but I just couldn't let it languish any more. It was a choice of completing it or deleting it, and I'm too much of a weiny for the latter.

Some of my stories may be missing a disclaimer. We all know the drill by now: anything you recognize in any of my work isn't mine; it belongs to whoever made it up in the first place, and I'm just having fun with it. Meesa making no mula.

Not much else for me to say. Happy Writing, all!
