Yep.

... So, I'm still alive, just not writing. It's been years, so I've decided to leave my stories up for anyone interested in reading them, but chances are, Unforgiveable Sinner and Dark Souls will never be updated. It's been way too long and I have no drive to continue writing them, nor do I even have their plots or outlines anymore. So if anyone wants to take them off my hands, let me know.

I look back at my writing from years ago and just cringe (with some exceptions... I do still have a soft spot for Abandoned, because it's prettiful...) so unless I get back into the writing game (which is harder now that I'm no longer a teenager; I have two kids and a husband), don't expect anything new to be published.

I have started reading Twilight!fic (DON'T LAUGH AT ME!11!!) so if I do write anything, it'll be from that fandom. HP fandom ain't so interesting now that the books are finished and the wank naps a lot more.

Still able to be reached via the old email address, though I haven't checked it in, like, months. Drop me a line there if anywhere.
