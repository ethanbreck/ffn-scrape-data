As to why I labeled most of my Enterprise slash fic R? I was asked to by a mother who allows her children to read everything up to the level of PG-13, and she doesn't want them to stumble over slash. Sure, I'm just one author, but hey, one can make a difference. I honor those wishes and even if a slash fic is actually PG-13, I rated it R.
