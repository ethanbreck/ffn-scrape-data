Author has written 8 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Maid Sama!, and Twilight.

"Who am i? I'm nothing and everything. I'm your best friend and your even better enemy. I'm the darkest before dawn. I'm the pale moon that wanes. I'm not the star that shines brightest, but i'm the Polaris that always points you north. I'm not truly evil, I know that. I'm not truly good either, I know that better. I'm not as smart as you think I am, I'm far smarter than that. I take limited chances and I take calculated risks, but I'll always be striving for a better me. So, what else do you want to know about me?"

That was done on a night when thoughts were unforgiving.

I have written fan fiction sporadically in the last few years or so. I like playing with reality and even it's alternative. I only write things that I enjoy reading. I'm not a grammarian, but I dislike bad grammar. I do not crucify, though. I'm past the age of angst. I have unlimited interests. The world simply has too many fascinating things in it. A whole lot of my works are doodled on notebooks. Someday, I may just find the time to create soft copies. That's a blue sky dream, though.

Interests I have dabbled on:

- Rourouni Kenshin

- Gundam Wing

- Card Captor Sakura

- Tsubasa Reservoir Chonicles

- Hana Yori Dango

- Kaichou wa Maid-Sama!

- Twilight Saga

I honestly haven't written anything creative in a while. So, this skill probably needs a whole lot of polishing. I'm currently trying to work on that by offering my services as a beta. I try to write for the fandoms above, but I don't really want to post anything until I'm positive I can finish it. For now, I'll work on shorts/one-shots and see where will that find me.

- Domyouji Tsukasa, Hana Yori Dango Returns
