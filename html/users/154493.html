Author has written 13 stories for Harry Potter, Twilight, and Sookie Stackhouse/Southern Vampire Mysteries.

Seven years of writing fanfiction!

I love the paranormal and I especially love paranormal romance. I'm in the process of writing my own novel but I come here for a little motivation and support. Writing fanfics is a lot easier and when I get good reviews it boosts my self-esteem therefor making it easier for me to believe in my novel. So please read my fanfics and leave some good reviews. I'll put up the first chapter to my novel soon enough and some of my best readers will get to read the whole book before I get it published so keep in touch.

I also sell Avon products so if you are interested check out my website at www.youravon.com/cberens

The books I love the most are Southern Vampire Mysteries, Twilight, The Host, Harry Potter and the Chronicles of Narnia. If you have a suggestion for a fanfic you would like to see written and want me to do it just ask.

Sorry to all of you readers that have been waiting for me to update some of my older fanfics. I haven't been very inspired in a while so I haven't kept up with my fanfics. I will try my best to at least get another chapter up on my most popular fanfics and if you want me to get a chapter of a specific story up just ask and I'll try to get it.

Thank you all for your support and kindness. I look forward to getting your reviews soon.
