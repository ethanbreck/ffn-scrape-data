Author has written 4 stories for Phantom of the Opera, and Doctor Who.

I'm from Germany and happy to find lots of great stories around here.

At the moment I'm quite obsessed with "Doctor Who" ... it started with the new series 2005, but I'm interested in the old stories, too.

I know a character like the Doctor can't really get a happy ending and thus he and Rose just had to be seperated, but I still love nice, happy reunion-fanfictions about them ;)

Although I never felt like writing any fanfiction myself, "The Phantom of the Opera" changed this and I acutally started writing "Blind Mask". I haven't finished it, because I'm a bit stuck, but I hope I'll continue it one day.

For the moment I have plans for some Who-fanfictions and I'm curious to see if they'll get finished.

All my stories will be in english, although its not my mother-language. So feel free to point out what I'm doing wrong or how I could write better.

I would be very happy if you read and review any stories of me. I'll even accept flames, but of course I'd prefer to get rather useful and positive comments ;)
