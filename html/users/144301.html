NOTICE

I am no longer updating.

I'm sorry y'all but it's just completely impossible. One, I lost my dedication to some of my fandoms (namely LotR) and it feels weird forcing myself to write about characters I can't understand anymore. Two, I've also lost my drive to write fanfiction. At all.

I'm sorry. I'm really, really sorry. I've always wanted to finish Hail. I had (and still have) a whole notebook full of outlines, timelines and plot lines. It just never happened. Hell, I started that story back in 2003. I still know exactly where I was taking that story and how I was going to get the characters there. I know I vowed I would finish no matter what but that seems silly now. It would be wrong to force myself to finish the story. The connection isn't there anymore. It hurts to admit but it's true. LotR just isn't what it used to be for me.

I am still amazed that I had readers. I had people email me and beg to update. I feel horrible that I'm letting them down but I can't keep pretending.

Thank you for everything.

p.s. I do still read fanfiction. Lol. So you may see me around.
