Sapientia Stats (the joys of alliteration): *I am sixteen *I am female *I am insane

I enjoy reading Harry Potter, Discworld, Hatrack River, etcetera. As far as fanfiction is concerned, I read only that which strikes me as well-written. It can be cliche, as long as it interests me.

HP: I'll read just about any pairing in the HP fandom, slash or het.

The few pairings I cannot, for various reasons, tolerate, are: Ron/AnyMale, Harry/MarySue, and Hermione/Severus. I dislike Harry/Hermione fiction, but I'll read it if I find the style of writing particularly compelling or the plot interesting.

The pairings I enjoy reading- and writing- are: Harry/Severus, Harry/Draco, Harry/Ginny, James/Lily, Lily/Severus, Remus/Sirius, and, strangely, Harry/Susan.

Plots: Right, I said I'll take cliche. I meant to say I've got a fetish for cliched fics. Time Travel? I adore it. Snape-Meets-Dursleys? Yum. Severitus Challenge Fics? My fave. Harry-Goes-To-AU-Where-Parents-Are-Alive? I live for them. That's all I need to be happy and healthy. I'm a sad, pitiful little girl. Sigh.

Stargate: I just can't see any Slash pairings work in this fandom. I like Sam/Daniel or Sam/Jack, but I'm mostly a fan of real team stories, without romance. I always enjoy a good Torture-All-The-Characters fic, especially if either Sam or Jack gets possessed by a Goa'uld.

Discworld: No one can match the Master, Terry Pratchett. But I'll read anything with Death, Vimes, or Susan.

Tortall: Alanna/George, Jon/Thayet, Kel/Joran, Kel/Dom, Daine/Numair

Favorite HP Fanfic Quote: "To our first years, I welcome you to Hogwarts. As always, I should like to remind you all that the Forbidden Forest has, in the past year, devoured eight-and-a-half of our graduating students, as well as several underclassmen, and is strictly off-limits. Hence the name Forbidden Forest. Believe me, if it were completely safe we would have long ago changed the name to the Come-And-Go-As-You-Damned-Well-Please Forest." --Harry Potter and the Song of Night by QuizzicalSphinx
