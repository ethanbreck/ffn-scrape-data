Author has written 3 stories for X-Men, and Chuck.

I am not much of a writer. I will be the first to admit that it is not exactly a priority. On a daily basis, I am more of a visual artist (a gallery of mine can be found here). Either that or I prefer to waste my time doing what gamers do. However, I do have ideas on occasion...

Current Interest(s): X-Men, Avengers, Chuck

Past Interest(s): Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, Farscape, Fullmetal Alchemist, Naruto

Update: 2/17/11 - I haven't written anything in a good, long while. But, I've uploaded a new Chuck drabble to help get the wheels turning a bit before I tackle something a bit more meaty. (And still very Chuck-ish.)

Update: 4/15/11 - First chapter of "After El Bucho" has been posted. I hope to make this a more long-term, serious effort. The basic idea is that after a brutal attack on the CIA by The Ring, Sarah and Casey have to leave Chuck behind while they undergo a long-term mission to take The Ring out. As they're out fighting the good fight, Sarah starts to learn what it really means to achieve her dream of being "a real person again" - meanwhile, those left alone back home struggle with the knowledge that the world is a far more dangerous place than they ever realized.
