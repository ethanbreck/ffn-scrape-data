Update Okay, it really has been a long time. Again, I ran out of steam for Pokemon and my other stories. When I post again, it will be much shorter content. Consider that Dilandau, Hiero, Val, and all the characters got their happily, and bloodily ever afters. I pushed it too long trying to get everyone in relationships, and it just cannot be forced to work in a story. So, sadly, no more additions to most sequels and they will remain as they are, except Escaflowne which will be given an end because it needs that. Others will be removed.

And again, to all those readers and reviewers who have been so courteous and given me constructive criticisms, Thank You, but still and sadly the "No Anonymous Reviews" feature is permanent. Some of you who have been flamed, harassed, and had horrible things put in the comments section for no other reason than that person can do so... you know how ridiculous and annoying deleting that tedious stuff is.

I am sorry for this inconvenience.
