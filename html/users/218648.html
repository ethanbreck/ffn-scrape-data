Hmmm. What to say, what to say.

I'm a typical anime fan, although I've been less into anime lately and more into manga. I'm absolutelycrazy about Japanese rock, though. I've been reading fan fiction for a long, long time. In fact, I think I joined Fan Fiction about four years ago. Even though I've been writing stories for just as long, I've never posted a story up until now, though, for one reason or another, although I've been bopping about reading a bunch. There are a lot of good authors on here with some fantastic stories. I blame you good authors for making me take so long, because I've had to tweak my stories for years in order to get them good enough to be noticed on this site. Or something like that.

As for my stories, I really only write about Weiss Kreuz. Yes, I am a fan girl. But I only write about an anime thatis basically terrible. Why? Because the really good stuff doesn't need my feeble fan stories to help it along, whereas anime like WK needs some help. No, I do like WK, but (excuse the rant)what could have been a really awesome series was destroyed by lack of funding and too many cliches. Thus, I write it how I want it to be, and how I think the characters would really act based on their truly shallow development in the anime.

Currently I've got only one story up: Traust Faellan, which is a Gravitation fic. Gravitation isn't bad at all; it's fine how it is, but I couldn't help but write about Yuki and Shuichi anyway, simply because I'm fascinated by Yuki Eiri and wanted to explore him some more. So Traust Faellan is the one exception. I'll probably neverwrite another Gravitation story, so TF will have to standas my solitary attempt at delving intoYuki and Shu more.

Well, that was a really boring bio. Hopefully my stories are a bit more interesting to you. Please read them and enjoy.
