Author has written 8 stories for DarkWing Duck, and DuckTales.

Hi! I'm a 28 year old animation fanatic who does adolescent and family therapy. Fun, huh? :P

The only fandom I've ever been into enough to have the gall to write fanfic for is Darkwing Duck. Everything you'll find on this site from me is for that show. I even have a website dedicated to it, but there's also other things there like my original artwork and stories. Eventually there'll be a comic, too, if I ever get off my lazy butt and work on it.

I have two fancharacters, Ariana McCawber and Jacob Mallard. I've had Ariana since I was 9 (since the show aired in '91). Jacob is a recent addition to my character creations. Both of their origin stories are currently on this site and my own site. I hope you all enjoy reading about them as much as I enjoy writing about them.
