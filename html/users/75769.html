Oh I hate writing in these things... here goes... So you want to know a little about me eh?
Well let's see... I'm a sixteen year old highschool student, currently working her way through her second, and might I add, LAST semester of Grade 11 (it's Grad next year baby!!!). I love to read and write,and do various other teen-related activities (going out, phones, study, listen to music, cause overall mischief in the lives of others... uh strike that last one) but I haven't posted anything or updated any of my on-going stories in what seems to be almost a year now.

But that's changed!

I have made it a mission to finish my latest work, "The Lonely One" for I've grown to become quite fond of it and would hate to see it go on unfinished. So, for anyone who's still following the story or that still is interested even after all this time, you can expect to see more updates for this one in the future. I've finally resolved how to wrap up the storyline, so the creative juices are flowing quite well in this little-ol-head of mine!

Thanks for your understanding for such a late delay, and happy reading!
