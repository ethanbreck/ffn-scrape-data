I happen to be rather terrible when it comes to saying something about myself, so bear with me while I struggle through making some sort of introduction.

Off the computer, I am a just-recently-turned twenty year old something who refuses to admit my age in person just due to the fact that it tends to remind me of how old I happen to be getting. I'm a big fan of anime and manga, the type of individual who cannot live without more than two consoles systems in the house (not to mention a variety of games), and an avid reader of Harry Potter. My parents firmly believe that my way of life is just an excuse which allows me to waste my money, but it is a strange personal goal of mine to eventually prove them wrong. I also cosplay, because I'm a weirdo.

For those of you who may actually be curious enough to ask, my favorite anime and manga is X, followed closely by Yami No Matsuei and Get Backers. My favorite video game happens to be Final Fantasy X.
