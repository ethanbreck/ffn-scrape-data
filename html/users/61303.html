Look, I don't usually have too much to say when I fill out these things, but here goes. I am 21 years old. I have been writing fanfiction since I was 15 years old. I don't know why. Call me crazy, but I think it's a wonderful outlet for the imagination. I am a writer at heart and always will be and I love feedback on my stories. It lets me know how and what to improve upon and whether I should keep going. How can I serve my audience if I don't know what they think about my work?

Feedback is like a drug. Yyou get addicted to it. I am a HUGE Star Wars fan as you can see from recent posts. So sue me for being a part of a large marketing trap. Star Wars has made me more creative in the last few years than I thought I could ever be. Anything else you want to know about me? Don't hesitate to email me. I love email. I live for correspondance.

I've recently developed a thing for Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Buffy/Giles ship, so don't flame me for that!
