Crap. Here we are, days away from 2010 and I have no new work on my WIP's to give you. I apologize. But I'd rather not give you crap, which is all I seem to be writing on those fics. Instead, I've been slowly working through my writer's block on LiveJournal, writing mainly senshi/shitennou ficlets. I'll eventually get around to editing them and posting them here, but I seem to spend most of my time either working, or being exhausted. Anyways, as usual, I participated in this year's ficathon. I overreached and produced four fics, that, surprisingly, I'm pretty happy with. The theme this year was Song Fic, so the fics posted here are the edited versions.

To find the unedited versions, you need to travel to my livejournal page, which, conveniently, is listed as my home page (look up on my profile here). Or, you can just go to livejournal and search me out. I'm listed as li_yingfa.

I hope you like the fics! Don't forget to read and review! I haven't given up on my WIP's!!

Broken Melody-Just updated B & S and now working on the next chapter of this. I have a chunk of it written but I'm not too happy with it and will most likely be revising it in the coming days.
