xi fantasy - let it be known
finally.. time for an update!

last update: february 14, 2007

i am chelsea ann, a fellow fanfic lover and anime // game obsessor. currently, i am seventeen, licensed to kill. filipino, i am, though i don't know tagalog. attending high school as a senior, heading towards william paterson university as a nursing major. in ways, i wish i lived anywhere but where i do live now. japan would be nice, but then again, so would anywhere else but here. i don't like change, but change is inevitable and we can't help but do it. i am always reminded of my 7th grade self, when i first discovered ff.net. i got sucked in to fanfics, and i shall start that up again. obsessions are my escape from reality. it all started on december 21, 2001. ccs, tsubasa: reservoir chronicles& kingdom hearts will forever be my number one obsessions. writing expresses my emotions, but, i never get a chance to fanfic. so don't expect many fanfics. once in awhile, i shall write. but for now, i only review.

in-love with:
animes: Card Captor Sakura, Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicles, Chobits, Escaflowne, Angelic Layer, Fruits Basket, Furi Kuri (FLCL), Inuyasha, DiGi Charat, Fushigi Yuugi, Sailor Moon, Serial Experiments: Lain, Samurai Champloo, Himiko-den, Prince of Tennis, Rurouni Kenshin, DNAngel, Pita Ten, Spirited Away, Princess Mononoke, Ah My Goddess, Ai Yori Aoshi, Kimi ga Nozomu Eien, etc. (more shall be announced at a later date)

games: Final Fantasy VII, Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy IX, Final Fantasy X, Kingdom Hearts, Dance Dance Revolution, Soul Calibur II, etc. (more shall be announced when i think of the games)

and to end this, i will say.. syaoran will forever be my husband! he's just that sexy!
