Hey, I'm redoing my bio yet again in light of OotP. And yes, I know I'm really late, but I had to work some stuff out before I could do this.
So, here's stuff about me.
I'm a teenage girl in the US. I love writing, reading, fanfiction, computers, horses, musicals, and various other things. I've been described as: smart, confusing, cynical, pessimistic, a bookworm, a crazy gramatical nut, and an introvert.
Here are various projects I'm thinking of doing:
1. A musical slash page, because I love musicals and there is no musical slash anywhere, and Danny and Kenickie from Grease belong together.
2. I now have a second idea! A web page to hold my stories and various other stuff. It's actually already started and the url is: but bare in mind that this is just the bare beginnings of the sight.
Also, I'm participating in the Percy/Oliver Fic Carnival ( so at some point I'll post the story I'm doing for the first day of that, once it's finished. Also, if you want to do it, it sounds really fun! Check it out!
Here's stuff about my stories:
*Apathy*
It's my new post-OotP story! So far there's only the prologue (which I'm uploading in a manner of minutes), but I intend to finish this one! I Do! Of course, this doesn't mean I will, but I'm trying.
*Understand Me*
In light of OotP, this probably will not be finished (there's a chance, but it isn't a big one). It breaks my heart to say this because I have such wonderful reviewers and I've had so much wonderful encouragement about it, but it goes against some canon I don't want to have to work around and I hate writing AUs. It's a personal thing. It's in MWPP era, but a future story in the series may take place in Harry's generation. So far, I have at least three others stories in the series. More updates on them when I have a clearer idea about the sequels. Chapter eight is in the works, but I’m not really rushing to finish it because I’ve got a lot of other things to do at the moment.
*Angelina*
On hold at the moment (take out “at the moment” and put in “forever”). I have horrible writers block, and it's probably not going to get finished. However, thanks to the wonderful (three) reviewers. If anyone really, *really, wants me to finish it, I might.
*Fire and Ice*
See above. I may write more on this, but inspiration isn't happening at all. Sorry!

One-Shots*
I don't write them a lot, but I have a few here and there as the muse strikes me. So far, my only one-shots have been H/D, but that doesn't mean they always will be. I’m having a lot of fun with some one-shots at the moment, so you may see more of them in the future.

My Poems in general*
*Note: My poetry is now on FicionPress.Net, under the same Username as I have here. You can find it there.* Poetry is something that I love to do. Most of it is free-form, with a bit of rhyming stuck in here and there. It's not something I do seriously, but I do enjoy it and a lot of it ends up here. I always enjoy comments on it as well, because I'm constantly trying to improve myself as a poet, and a writer in general.

Stories I'm thinking of doing/have already done part of*
I am constantly getting rabid plot bunnies, but a lot of them will never get done because I don't have the motivation. Here are some of the ones I am seriously considering doing. In light of OotP, a lot of the stories have been nixed, but also I have a lot more new ideas. Also, I've recently managed to watch seasons one, two, three and four of Buffy the Vampire Slayer and season one of Angel (and season two's out on DVD today, so I'll be watching it fairly soon!) and I'm hooked, line and sinker. I love the show. And I might be doing some fanfiction from it some time in the future.
*The Jewel's Calling*
I’m kind of debating upon whether to do this one or not. It probably won’t get done for a while, in any case, because I’m busy at the moment and inspiration isn’t coming. This is a Tamora Pierce fic. She is my favorite author in the world, and probably always will be. This story, if I finish it, will follow Miache and Zefrem as they steal the Jewel from Galla and take it to Tyra (I believe that's the country, but I might be wrong). It's outlined in Lioness Rampant, but only outlined.
*Story from the Heart*
This story probably will get done, eventually. I could still do it with OotP and I'm seriously considering it. A story about Trisania Weasley, the daughter of Ron and Hermione, and the rest of her generation. When she begins her fourth year at Hogwarts, she doesn't know that it will change her views on her life forever. For one thing, she meets Luke Malfoy- supremely cool, sixth year Slytherin, only son of Draco Malfoy, and apparently head over heals in love with her. For another, she and her best friend, Jackson Potter, dig up something in Jackson's attic which may change all of their lives, Luke's more than anyone. And to top it off, she has Quidditch practice! (Will contain slash as well as het).
*Life in Knockturn Alley*
Used to be called "Life Down the Alley". I've had to change Luna's name to Libra because of the Luna in OotP. Other than that, it could still work and might. Ginny Weasley runs away from home, ends up in Knockturn Alley, and meets Libra. Soon she has moved in with the spunky and vivacious Libra and her group of pick-pockets and thieves. Ginny has no thoughts of going back to the shelter of the Burrow, and Libra assures her she won't have to. But Libra has her own life that may come looking for her. Slightly based on the lovely book, "The Thief Lord," but only slightly.

Original Stories I'm thinking of*
*Note: Any story I happen to post here will be found on my FictionPress.Net profile.* This is another thing that I've recently decided should be up here. I want to be an author someday, and I want to write stuff that's not just fanfiction. Probably I won't put any of it up here, but who knows.
*Unnamed Robin Hood Story*
I love Robin Hood. Seriously, Robin Hood stories are some of my favorites in the world. I want to contribute one a bit different to the world of Robin Hood, but I don't have much on it yet except that it will have Original Characters, and plenty of Maid Marian.

Also, I am a fairly reliable and eager beta-reader. If your looking for someone who can do general punctuation, spelling, plot and characterization and don't mind if it takes a few weeks, you're welcome to email me. I'd prefer if the stories aren't too long as I'm a bit busy, but long is fine too, it'll just take me longer to finish it.

Recs*
Here are some recommendations for really good sites/individual stories that I like.

www.fictionalley.org - If you haven't been here, you're missing out. It's the center of the fandom, with all the best fanfiction and really wonderful message boards.
- A wonderful Joey/Chandler slash site.
- If you like Rhysenn's stuff, this is her site. If you haven't read anything by her, what are you waiting for?
- Debbie (aka Plumeria) is a wonderful writer, and one of my all time favorites.
- Into Rasberry Swirl is one of the very few femmeslash arcives on the web. It's lovely.
- Lasair and Maya are two of my favorite writers. They both write lovely stuff.
- A lovely H/D site with lots of quality fics. I work there recommending fics, so if there's one you don't think is worthy, I'm to blame.
- A Lord of the Rings slash fanfiction archive with lots of very good stuff.

Well, I think that's all for now! If you've got any questions, comments, suggestions, critisism, or just want to chat, feel free to email me. I like getting emails. Just make sure you title it something like "A Fanfiction.Net person" or some such so I don't delete it thinking it's spam.
