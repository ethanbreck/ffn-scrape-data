Author Notes for Chapter 7

Hi, remember me? That's okay, no hard feelings if you don't.

I've been staring at this chapter for a year now, sometimes muttering, sometimes shouting at it loud enough to worry my roommate.

"Why!" I cried. "Why won't you do what I want you to do! There's an outline, the whole thing is plotted out meticulously. There are plot twists that need to happen and you people want to go to the river. That wasn't in the plan! Come on Rei, help me out. I got you drunk, doesn't that make you happy?"

(Which by the way is why Rei is somewhat silent in the chapter.)

Anyway, this chapter didn't go the way I planned. It was supposed to end in tears and misunderstandings and ANGST. The good news is that I'm interested in this story again beyond the dread of being one of those authors who tortures people with unfinished stories.

The deleted river scene will chapter eight. Chapter nine will be out...sometime. I don't suppose it'd be wise of me to give a release date, look at how well the last turned out. Anyway, it'll be out. No worries.

I guess author notes will be put here and deleted until I figure why I'm having spacing issues with this site.
