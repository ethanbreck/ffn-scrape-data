Author has written 29 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Dragon Age, Harry Potter, Thor, Avengers, and Mass Effect.

I write bits of fiction that tend to languish in scattered notebooks. But on occasion the bug bites hard enough that I consider posting. Dragon Age bit hard, though it's all headcanon stuff. Dragon Age II for the moment. Maybe widening circles, later.

Hope you enjoy.

5/13/13

Steal Away Home is in progress, the continuing tale of Sebastian Vael and Aeryn Hawke and what happens after they and the companions fled Kirkwall at the end of Shelter. To the Free Marches, and Starkhaven! (Finally!)

Tales from the Shelterverse is where I'm placing various point of view stories written around the canon of Shelter. Things that don't fit in the story or that enhance certain aspects. These do not follow any particular order and are written in different styles depending on the story, listed as chapters because that's the set up on this site. The latest, Blessed Satinalia, is simply a little holiday story. A bit of Aeryn and Sebastian's love story.

mille libri was my joyous and talented beta for the first half of Steal Away Home and several of the stories in Tales from the Shelterverse. My thanks to her, she was wonderful.

jillyfae is now betaing for me! *squee*

fruit_sexual, aka Elyssa Wecera drew the beautiful Aeryn Hawke that I use as my avatar and on the cover of Tales from the Shelterverse. Look at all the endless daggers! So cool!
