Hi! I'm Danielle, aka Gooniegirl3333. I'm pretty much everywhere on the net if you take the time to find me, =).

So, you wanna know the real me or the internet me? Let's think: I'm a dancer (Bachelors in Arts), and an actor (Associates in Fine Arts) and am about to start a Crime Scene Certification Program to become an investigator. I'm completely obsessed with certain things making my family and friends to believe that I'm obsessive compulsive. Well, I say that closely following your favorite tv shows is not obsessive, it's dedication! (Although, there is that thing I have with colors...) Anyway, I'm completely in love with The Goonies (hence the name) and even went to the 25th & 30th Anniversaries in Astoria, OR, although I would never dare write a fanfic for it. If you couldn't tell by now, my favorite television show is CSI:Miami. I'm just so bewildered by this show, I sometimes forget who I am.
