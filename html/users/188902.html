"I am the lord and sovereign of the applemonkies."~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

Name: Only my friends know that piece of information. And some of them don't even realize that they're calling me by my middle name.

Age: 21

Location: In a winter wasteland known as White River Junction, Vermont. I'd rather be somewhere else though, Kansas City perhaps.

Alternate personalities that possess me from time to time: Bardiel, LTG-666, the Applemonkey King , Jigimi Kamui, Shigami, Fear Ghoul, Hurei and Blackheart.

Hobbies: watching anime (I'll give anything a chance), playing video games, reading and writing fanfics (like that's a surprise), and working to support my habit that will ruin me someday (my mother's words, not mine).

Favorite anime/manga: Love Hina, Maison Ikkoku, GTO, Kimagure Orange Road, Fruits Basket, Mars, Marmalade Boy, Kare Kano, The Vision of Escaflowne, Berserk, and whole bunch more.

Types of music I enjoy: Pretty much anything and everything, with the exception of Country. Punk, Rock, Opera, Classical, Jazz, it's all good.

I've seen both the dark and light side of humanity during my life. Most of those twenty-one years were spent wallowing in a sea of darkness and self-loathing. But at the moment, I feel nothing but joy and an overwhelming sense of happiness. Whee, it looks like the one-winged angel has found someone to love. And that sensation is all that matters, well, except for her happiness, that is.

* Note *
The person stated in the above possesses a wing identical to my own, and with our matching set we shall soar into the heavens and live our lives together. That's the reason why her happiness is more important than my own. If she is happy, then I am happy.~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

Currently watching: I My Me Strawberry Eggs! Weird little show, but damn funny. Still need to watch the last nine episodes though.

Currently playing: Final Fantasy V, Final Fantasy VI, Final Fantasy X (is it really over a year old), and Lufia 2: The Rise of the Sinistrals. Then there's the numerous roms on the computer, like Breath of Fire 2 and Bahamut Lagoon. Me love Roms, ook!

Currently reading: Mars (by Fuyumi Soryu), Marmalade Boy (by Wataru Yoshizumi), Love Hina (by Ken Akamatsu), Chobits (by Clamp), Maison Ikkoku (by Rumiko Takahashi), Kodocha: Sana's Stage (by Miho Abana), and a whole bunch more.

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~Current projects:

The Story of a Guarantor = the third arc in my story based in the world of Final Fantasy X. It's a side story to the game.

Poetry = I used to be a dark poet, but you know what? There's just so many times that a person can write about his death and make it sound original. Just something I discovered recently. But whenever I get an idea for a poem, I'll write it down and post it.

Macalania Lagoon: The truth behind the music = One of those crazy ideas mentioned below. Take two gamers, sheer boredom at work and this is the result, utter insanity.

And possibly something completely random that begs to be unleashed from my warped little mind. You've been forewarned.

I may even start on another project soon entilted Blood on the Snow, based on Final Fantasy X. But that should be a while from now, I need to finish up a story or two before I begin.
