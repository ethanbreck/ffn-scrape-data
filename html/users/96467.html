And now, let's play everyone's favorite game: Will I Ever Update It?

This is where I tell you whether I'll be updating currently unfinished fics, and why or why not. I know many of these seem stalled; but take heart. Not all of them are. Even when I get out of a fandom, sometimes resurgances do hit me. Sometimes I even flashback to old fandoms that I haven't touched in months or even years. Of course, the more I see a series/movie/whatever, the more likely I am to update, but not current doesn't always mean dead forever. Let's go through the list.

Avenues of Interest: Um... not likely. Even if I ever do come back to this fandom, and it would take watching the series again to get me to do so, I really never had anything planned beyond the listed chapters, so I don't know what should go next. ; Don't hold your breath for this one.

Coming Home: Surprisingly enough, I do still plan to continue this one, even though I'm not sure I'll ever finish it. I could use a jolt, but I do still have at least one vivid scene still planned out that I'd like to write.

Intoxicated: Not listed as complete not because there will be more chapters (there won't) but because I plan to do a rehaul. When I uploaded it, ff.net removed all my spacing between scenes. So I have to go back and fix that in addition to a lot of small bits of editing.

Keeper Secrets: 4 Allegiances: Yes, I do plan to finish this! I wish I could still play DK on my comp, because not having it is really slowing me down, but I actually do have the entire end of this fic (mostly) planned out, and I do expect to finish it eventually.

Long Lost: Probably not, unfortunately. I really liked this idea a lot (and I totally called Bootsrap still being alive, by the way - go me :PotC2spoiler:), but unfortunately, I don't really know where I wanted to go from here, other than getting Jack and Will sr. together. So... yeah. Probably this is all I got.

Pick Up the Pieces: Definitely yes. All the chapters are already written, just waiting to be posted.

Revelations: ...Mmmmmmmmaybe. I would reallyreallyreally like to finish this one, and I have an idea about another chapter or two and then sort of a vague notion beyond that. I've kind of gotten out of the fandom, but I want this one to be done very badly, so it might happen, especially if I stick to my New Year's Resolution to write more of the stuff I've been meaning to.

The Danger of Trying New Things: Unfortunately, much as I love this fic dearly, this will probably stay unfinished unless some MAJOR inspiration hits me. I do have one scene from later on burned into my head, but have no idea how to get there. Mostly I just have no reason for the witches to be in the city. -_- I want to have a plot, not just have them be sight-seeing or something, so unless that plot comes to me, this one's stuck.

So there you go! For all my fans, thank you for your dedication; it's the good reviews that really make me want to finish fics. I love each and every one of you, and if I had a million dollars, I would totally buy you all cars and chocolates.
