SENA'S BLAH BLAH BLAH:

In regards to my update schedule: I don't have one. I really should try to set one up, but I've attempted it multiple times in the past and I can never stick to it, no matter how determined I am to start off with. Some fics may sit for two or three months in between updates, while others may be updated three times in a matter of two weeks. It really just depends on my muses and my writing drive.

For those of you who are wondering about fics that have sat forever and not been updated: These fics are not necessarily abandoned. To check and see if I have discontinued working on a particular story, check the section of my profile that says "Discontinued Projects", and anything I have given up on will be listed there. So long as a fic stays off of that list, it's still one I plan to work on. Currently the only stories I have discontinued are Devil, Mine and Down Came A Blackbird (which isn't even hosted here at all anymore.)

That said, fics that have been relegated to the Discontinued Projects list may come back off if I'm struck with the inspiration. This is very unlikely, though.
