Author has written 14 stories for Bubblegum Crisis.

Hiii! I'm Amanda Stair-Duran, and for those of you who know me, I get my pen name from the main character in my Bubblegum Crisis fanfics, Yumeko (who is, uh, well, the purple Knight Saber =D). Read of her adventures in my fanfics "The Next Generation," "A Farewell to Arms," "Half Moon," "A Deviant's Days," and the newest, "One Degree of Separation," and to know the background behind them, go read "Innocent Guilt."

6/18/14: Long time no update! Looks like the scene breakers in FTA magically got restored with one of FFN's more recent updates (I admit I haven't checked recently). Also, I do have another story idea churning around in my head. It'd be more along the lines of "Silver Tears" and would focus on Priss and Sylvie's relationship during Moonlight Rambler. I guess I just need some encouragement from my fans to get started on it. Let me hear it; is it something you'd be interesting in seeing?

If you haven't visited my site and read Next Gen or the others, please do so! And while you're at it, leave some feedback too! I love hearing what people think!
