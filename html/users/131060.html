Author has written 3 stories for StarTrek: Enterprise, and Stargate: Atlantis.

I love fan fiction! I have been a member for a long time and still enjoy it. I grew up reading fan fiction in elementary and middle and high school, college and grad school and now as a "real person" with a job. There is such talent here.

My favorite fandoms are: LotR, Star Trek (all series), Doctor Who, SGA, and occasionally Harry Potter. I prefer gen fic, relationships seem to take over most stories and you loose the adventure, plot and quality.

I wrote my posted stories when I was in middle and high school, they are not superb but have their place here and a place in my heart.

I liver near Wedgewood Ave. and like the name.

I still love fan fiction and am a very active reader but have not written lately. I am no longer active on this site but can be found on AO3 under the same pen name.
