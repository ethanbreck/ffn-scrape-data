Hello there.

I'm mibi chan, and I'm a crazy DBZ fan with a B/V obsession and an unnatural crush on Vegeta and his smirky psychosis. I am a huge fan of most DBZ characters though, and have some plot bunny plans in mind for non-B/V one-shots. *gasp!!!

I do not own any of the characters/places that my work is based on, but I do write about them and I profess to loving said characters/places. The beautiful picture in my avatar was rendered on e-shuushuu(dot)net by the artist Arisu K. Several other images by this artist can be found there. Wonderful work!

I can also be found on the beta site, An Archive of Our Own (AO3) as mibi_chan, and on Mediaminer as Saiya-jin44.

If you feel inspired, please leave me some reviews to let me know what you liked about my work, or what you think needs more work, or what I should do more with - I would love that!
