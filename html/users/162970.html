Sometime in 2003

I'm bored. Hella bored. Oh, yes. I'm never coming back here. Occasionally, I will come by and read some fics but that's it. No more updating. Blame it on FF.net. It kept on deleting "Frivolous," Yot's and my masterpiece. We're sick of FF.net. We are boycotting its existence by not coming here anymore. Yes, "Frivolous" still lives. Please check it out at the Dynasty Warrior Fangirls forum. =) Have a nice life, everyone. And remember: BOYCOTT FF.NET!!

09/01/2008

I chortled reading the above. That was me a couple of years ago. I think I haven't logged onto this account for a good five years. I don't know why, but I randomly decided to come back and redo some things.

My ID was Chunsa back in the day. I had two fanfics: "Frivolous" (the one mentioned above that was co-written by Yot) and "One Inch of Love is an Inch of Ashes." The former was taken down multiple times by FanFiction.net while the latter was left incomplete, collecting dust for five years until I removed it from the site last week. It was actually the reason why I came back; I thought of it a few weeks ago and had an urge to finish what I had started. Rereading it, I was unsatisfied and decided to start all over from scratch. So now the only story I have up is "An Inch of Ashes." Hopefully I finish the story this time around. :3

I left this site at 13 and I returned at 18. Time flies, eh?
