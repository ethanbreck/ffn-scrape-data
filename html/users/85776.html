So, I basically deleted all of my stories because I felt they were shit. I'm only on this website now to read what few GOOD stories there are in the Fanfiction universe. By good stories, I mean the ones that stay fairly consistent with their story universe, make few typos, and keep consistent characters. Everything else on this website is shit, and don't let a few good reviews tell you otherwise. Be a better judge of what's good writing and what isn't, people. Maybe I'll post something on here again someday...'til then, it's just reading the quality writing and critiquing the crappy ones for me. Cheers.
