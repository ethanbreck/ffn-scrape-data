Hello all! Dee/Bmv no Miko here. So obviously, I haven't written much in the way of fanfiction. But I do enjoy writing when I have the time, and really wish I wrote more. I'm

currently living in Osaka, Japan for the second time and loving it, despite the fact that my company's gone totally down the tubes. Even so, Japan is awesome! I love anime and

manga, but these days I'm often too busy to really keep up with it. Even so, it's a fun diversion, and I've been a fan for...wow, over ten years now. So, on to the basics:

Fandoms: Final Fantasy (all of'em, but especially 6-7-9), Gundam Seed (alas that it's over) and Transformers (which is currently pwning my free time).

Fave Charas: Final Fantasy--The black mages! Anywhere they should appear I also like Cyan, Sephiroth, and Reno.

Gundam Seed--Yzak and Dearka. They are my OTP. I love them more than I'd care to admit. But I also love Athrun and Nicol too.

Transformers--Um, I like everyone. Seriously. But if I had to push it down to the wire, I'd say...Bumblebee, Ratchet, Jazz, the twins, Bluestreak, and Wheeljack. G1 forever!

Oh wait. On the Decepticon side, I heart the Seekers--Starscream, Thundercracker, and Skywarp. Hzzot jet planes!

Age: Mid-twenties

Inspiring music: Kokia, Rurutia, Kajiura Yuki, Hirasawa Susumu, L'Arc en Ciel, Sarah Brightman, Tomoyasu Hotei...and a lot of others.

Good anime: Shoujo Kakumei Utena, Haibane Renmei, Mahou Shoujo Tai Arusu, Gankutsuou, Uninhabited Planet Survive

Good manga: Full Moon wo Sagashite, Kaze no Tani no Nausicaa, Fruits Basket

What creeps me out: Scary movies (no way in hell), scorpions, and mayonnaise (eeew)

And that's about all I can think of for now. Reviews on my one story are always appreciated, and perhaps someday I'll be able to convince myself to write something else!
