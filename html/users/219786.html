That's all for my actual biography. Now, just a brief note on my opinion of certain things. I hate, I just HATE, to do oc's for stories, or taking stories written by others and taking them in directions where they could never actually go. To me, it's like tresspassing and not respecting the rules of that world or whatever it is. So I never do it. I'm not actually criticizing, but that's my view. It will be based on something that actually happened in that world but isn't shown in the book. *double checks list of stuff she's written* Yea, I think that's right.

Boromir is way under-appreciated. He sacrificed his bloody LIFE for a quest he didn't even believe should happen... And he doesn't even get onto a single, bloody poster! Orcs do, but not Boromir. GRRRRR. If I'm mistaken, please tell me where I CAN get a poster of him. My room needs one. Anyhow, I would go on to tell you all the reasons why he is the best, but it is too long. I really do have a list, though...

YAY! Time for disclaimer!! All of Tolkien's chars are not mine, and I don't even know why I'm saying this as I never do OC's! Whatever... Yea, and neither are Lynn Flewelling's chars, and neither are... Wait, have I done any other fics? But the fic I did of MY book is my chars... :) heehee!

I think that's all I want to say here. If you want to know more, go read my journal!!!
