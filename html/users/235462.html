I'm Autobot Princess Arcee, but you may call me Arcee. As I'm sure you can assume, I love Transformers. I'm an Autobot girl but I tend to get horrible naughty crushes on those horribly naughty Decepticons! Shockwave, Barricade, Soundwave, the seekers. Even Megatron has a certain... allure. Of course, I find Autobots attractive too! Prowl, Jazz, Drift, Ultra Magnus. Really there's just too many to name!

That being said, I love reading good TF fanfics. Usually romances because I'm a girly-girl like that. Be it bot/human or bot/bot, if it's well written and interesting, I'll give it a chance and read it. Feel free to recommend something to me! I plan on uploading some of my stories. Y'know, once I stop being lazy and write them up!

Feel free to add me on xbox at AutobotPrincess or on skype as Arcee.Medicbot and I can also be found on tumblr at ArceeMedicbot.tumblr.com
