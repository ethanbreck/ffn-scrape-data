Whew! New year, new profile (even if it's March...)

I'm a sophmore in the I.B. program, which essentially means that I have evolved into a creature that requires neither sleep nor a social life outside of people at school. Just coffee. Luckily for me I hang out with a bunch of people who are equally obsessive about things as I am!

1 1/2 years ago I began the petition at my high school to start an after school anime club. We started out as 20 kids crammed into the very small green room of our library, with watching an hour of anime being our only activity. This year we have 45 members, the spacious and awesomely decorated stastics classroom, a forum, an official website, and coming soon a web comic! We've grown past just watching 2 episodes of a random anime to having fanart contests, our own set of holidays, (including samurai day, medieval day, and good vs evil day) cosplay competitions, storyboarding meetings (for the comic), trivia games with plushie prizes, overnight marathons, and banquets at our local steak house (with our usual chef, whom we love with all of our hearts. He has been nicknamed Yuhi in honor of his awesomeness). Currently I hold one of the two manga-librarian posts in club, and hope to run for secretary in my junior year, and president in my senior year.
