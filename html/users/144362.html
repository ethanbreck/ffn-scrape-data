Most recent news

I'm currently writing my first RWBY fic called "Stay Afloat". I noticed there are barely any Pyrrha x Nora ship fics, so I decided to help fix that. It focuses on friendship, drama, comfort and romance. Feel free to read it. If you like it, please follow, favorite, and leave reviews.

The Review Game

Are you tired of people who leave only short reviews like "Good job! Keep it up! Can't wait for next chapter!" You want longer and more detailed ones? I can help you with that. When I leave reviews, I talk in detail about your story, characters, dialogue, etc. I say what I like, but also what I don't like. I always leave constructive criticism when I talk about things you can improve. The length of my reviews depend on how long your fic is.

What I want in return is that you review something for me too. I'm currently looking for reviewers for mainly Stay Afloat, but also The Desires Of Metal and Flesh, Listen To Your Heart, and A Secret Hidden In Darkness.

Message me if you're interested.
