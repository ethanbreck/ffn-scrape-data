Author has written 20 stories for Angel, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Gilmore Girls, Enid Blyton, Terminator: Sarah Connor Chronicles, Dexter, Xena: Warrior Princess, and Glee.

I'm Australian. I love good TV. I don't write often, or much. Just when the mood strikes me. Hope you find something you can enjoy!

If you're here because of the Brittana stories, it pretty much just occured to me that I've been writing a series without realising it, about pre, during, and post-out Santana. I've basically covered everybody's voices and perspectives except for Santana's own, which will be the last story I write.

They've been written in all different orders, but for a chronological read, go like this:

1. I'm With The Band (3rd party POV, pre-outing, pre-relationship - complete)

2. Front Row, Baby (Rachel POV, pre-outing, post-relationship - complete)

3. Everyone's Gonna Know Now (All Glee kids POVs, except Santana, pre-and post outing - in progress)

4. Primary Colour (Brittany POV, post-outing - complete)

5. Not yet written, Santana POV, pre, during and post-outing
