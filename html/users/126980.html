Author has written 3 stories for Criminal Minds, and Pretty Little Liars.

I am at best a sporadic and undisciplined fan fiction-writer, thus not much of what I do produce tends to show up here. I very rarely finish anything, so I tend to wanna keep'em short and sweet - I love to read well-written, canon-compliant stories, and bravely strive to produce my own (not that I don't enjoy the occasional AU jaunt).

I prefer to inject some element of slash (male-on-male relations) into my texts, though I maintain a principle not to swing straight characters the "wrong" way. Consequently the gay guys are original characters lusting for the straight guys, in turn injecting some element of unrequited love and often non-con (which I love to write, aware though I am that it is a big bucket of suck in real life). Overall I enjoy exploring the dark and depraved. Sunshine and lollipops just piss me off, you know?
