Author has written 10 stories for Hardy Boys/Nancy Drew, X-Men, X-Men: Evolution, and X-Men: The Movie.
And I'm actually changing this. Weeee!

Still like Shakespeare, still like fantasy. Moving on a bit to more recent British authors. Seeing as I can no longer financially support my reading habits, I've taken to hounding the library to get the books I want to read. They love me there. Really.

Hmmm...I'm webmistress of four sites. I'm a huge Scott/Wanda fan, and a huge Piotr/Kitty fan, but since he's dead I've moved on to comic Kurtty. And that's my life, ladies and gents. Sad, isn't it?

Okay, about the kind of ficcies I'll write. No lemons. No limes. (Well, no EXTREME limes.) Citrus free, kay?

Ugh...okay, here's how I feel about slash. Most slash is smut. I hate smut. But if a story ISN'T smut, and it's good, the fact that it's slash comes secondary. Will I write any? I don't know. If, like, Marvel decides Beast really is gay, yeah, I might do a story with that. I wrote one about Jean-Paul, but I didn't give him a lover because I wasn't addressing that part of his life. But I won't write something just for the sake of writing slash. Sound fair?

Loved LXG, don't know why so many critics hated it. Desperately hoping they still make the sequel.
