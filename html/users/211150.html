Author has written 29 stories for Stargate: SG-1, Yu-Gi-Oh, and CSI.

Updated June 2010:

Just a gal who likes to write once in a while. I have loads of story ideas in my head...just need to find time to write them down!

So you wanna know what I like to watch and read? Here goes! Fave T.V Shows: CSI, Bones, 24, Drop Dead Diva, Simpsons, Stargate SG-1, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Alias (or any other show where my fellow females kick ass), Grey's Anatomy, Criminal Minds, Mythbusters and that's just the start! And no, I don't spend my life in front of the tv...just my weekends...:-)

Fave Books/Authors: The Lion by Joesph Kessel (all time fave), Phantom by Susan Kay, Gabriel Knight by Jane Jenson, Terry Pratchett, David Eddings, Katherine Kerr, Anne McCafferey, Guy Gavriel Kay, Patricia Cornwell, Trudi Canavan and if any more come to mind I'll add them. I do try to read a wide variety of books so at any one time I might be reading an archeology book alongside a sci-fi/fantasy book with a true crime book waiting in the wings!

Fave other things to do: Cook, write, knit, graphic design, playing games on computer (right now it's the Dragon Age: Origins), paint model figurines and basically doing anything creative!

Interests: Archeology, Archeo-astronomy, space flight and exploration, travel, Ancient Egypt, true crime, Mt. Everest, animals animals and more animals and anything else that takes my fancy. If I am interested in it, I'll read all the books I can about it.

A note about my CSI fics: You can take them all to be within the same 'universe' that is...mine. Follow the Evidence is the start, one-shots may appear from time to time, but consider them follow-ons to the main story(ies).

I am listed as a beta-reader too.
