Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

First off, my penname is what my real name would be if I spoke gaelic and lived in Ireland (I've got a mom with red hair and my dad's soda bread is the best -need you ask if I'm Irish? oh, and my dad grew up with 10 kids, my mom with five - of course I'm the stereotypic Catholic Irish child ) I could go on about my family and heritage forever, however on to the bio

Did you know that I wrote my first biography when I was eleven years old? As if there was something interesting to tell... Actually I consider my life intriquing - but this is not a place for a life story, it's a place for a biography! hmmm... why does that sentance not work? I've written many half finished bios since then, none much good. But the essential details? I have been trying to work those out now for sometime. I am a girl. I love writing, music, dance and art (at least I like looking at that) I like history and heritage. I was introduced to HP when I was 12 years old (before it was a huge craze...) I love the characters; my favorites being Ginny, Harry (though I make him evil in one of my fics -which will be redone at some point in time) Dumbledore, Ginny, Molly, Charlie, Cho (c'mon people she's 16 who's not emotionally messed up at time?) and of course Voldermort. My favorite theory is Voldermort is Dumbledore! my brother and I have an elaborate argument for this theroy. I like being on this site next to incredibly talented authors. Now, most good authors I know tend to have slightly scary bios with random randomness thrown in - unfortunatly I can't give you that.

I will tell you that music teachers are crazy, though, and to stay away from them! et finalement, "les enfants ont plus besoin de modeles que de critiques" J. Joubert (the children need models more then they need critics) have you ever held a baby? try one and understand they are everything. oh! and learn another language it's good for your soul:) thanks and have fun reading
