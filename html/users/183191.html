29/12/2006

I'm very sorry for all the ppl who were enjoying my story. Writer's block is a very difficult to overcome, but I believe that it is beginning to fade. This story was started nearly 5 years ago, and the ideas (along with my writing style) have changed a little. But, I will get this finished, even if it takes much longer since I attend a school that is very demanding and requires most of my time.

Looking back at the chapters posted, I do feel a bit weary of having an extra character I created for the purpose of the story. If I still receive encouragement, I'll take it as a good sign and keep writing.

For the past couple of years, I have been writing other stories and creating other characters on paper. I may also start posting another GW story with HY+RDP, QW+DC, and the other Pilots paired with OCs. I know ppl may not like to read that kind of thing, but I enjoy writing them.

Anyways, those who have been fans of the first story are free to suggest some ideas for it and I'll consider them.

Thanx for the patience and support!!!
