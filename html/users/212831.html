Saru no Megami no how-to-review

Now don't get me wrong; If the only thing you possibly have to say is "thats good write" go right ahead. But if you care about me enough to read my bio *g* then please, *please, give me more than that. Specifically, any one of (or even... all?) of the following:
-What you liked (a favorite line, joke, scene)
-What you didn't like (made no sense, ooc in a serious story, etc.)
-Anything that didn't make sense
-Anything you'd like to see in future chapters

Note that some of this doesn't apply to my humor stories. I *like* ooc in humor stories, and most of them are random and out of left field. But still, you can give constructive reviews! Please!

So everyone else has these mile-long bios; I figured I'd slip into conformist mode and write one too.

I am a freshman at a normal university, which, ironically, is neither.

I write mostly comedy, because that's what I enjoy. I write really good angst, having gone through some myself, but I don't like to, because, well, it's a sucky feeling. The only way I write angst is of things turn out well in the end. That'll probably give away the endings of some of my fics, but eh, c'est la vie.

I am a Spanish Education major, but I'm going to change it to Industrial Computer Systems, which is sort of a mix of all the computer sciences (networking, programming, hardware, etc.).

My current favorite anime are pretty much all the shows on Adult swim (Yeah, I know it's lame, but I have no money with which to buy anime). Manga series I'm currently buying include Fruits Basket and Wedding Peach (the latter being mega-kawaii ^_^). My favorite game, if you can't guess, is Final Fantasy 9, and my favorite character is, you guessed it... Bratty Marin! No, really, if you can't figure it out, you probably have stumbled across this site by typing random words on a keyboard (and knock it off, that's *my* racket!) My favorite websites, speaking of which, are currently Somethingawful.com, homestarrunner.com, and maddox.xmission.com.
