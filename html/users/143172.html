Story Synopses:
-Love on a Leaf - There was a man who believed in love...There was a man who did not believe in love...Then there were the love stories. A story that teaches you to never give up on your love, even if there are so many obstacles in your path, and even if you are not loved in return. Read and find out why some love stories live forever.

-Brave Hearts - a story about love, sacrifice, and strength. Kari has gotten close with Davis, and TK is crestfallen. TK must learn how to deal with his unrequited love. Also, Devimon has returned, and is out for blood, but he's got some new tricks. It looks like TK and Patamon will have to defeat him again, but can they manage? Will TK and his digimon have enough strength (both emotionally and physically) to subdue the demon when his loved ones' lives are at stake? Read and find out as this Takari story unfolds!

-Across the Web - my first attempt at something close to angst. This ReBoot story came completely out of the blue for me. Part of the idea for this came from a dream of mine (no fooling). It is an AU story done in Dot's POV, and set after "My Two Bobs". Megabyte has succeeded in carrying out his vengeance. He has destroyed Mainframe, and has taken over the Supercomputer. Bob, Dot, and Enzo are the only survivors, but they have been cast out into the web. This short story is done three days from the fiasco (which of course is a long time for them) about Dot's dismal recollection of these horrific events.

-Love Comes as a Shock - a Digimon season four short story (contains spoilers). Orimoto Izumi recounts her time in the Digital World, but she stumbles on something that surprises her - she believes that she fallen in love with the pudgy Shibayama Junpei, keeper of the spirit of thunder!

-A World Without Goggles - an angst story done in Davis' point of view. Davis has always thought that nothing went his way, and that he wasn't anything special. He feels that he is of little use to everyone, and what's worse, Kari loves TK, not him. In his sorrowful state, he wishes that he never existed. Something shocking happens and Davis is tossed into such a scenario. However, he may be surprised at what he sees. This story uses similar concepts to "It's a Wonderful Life". Read on and see if Davis finds his place in the world.
