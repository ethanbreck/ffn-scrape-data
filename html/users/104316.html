Bio: My name is Jocelyn and I am an Australian-Chinese girl who lives in Melbourne. I love writing and love reading. But who here on ffn doesn't! lol...

I love Tamora Pierce fics - especially about Daine and Numair (hence my biggest project on here is a D/N fic) but also enjoy Alanna/George or Alanna/Jonathon (can never decide which pairing I like best!).

I am also interested in Sailor Moon fics. I started watching the series when I was in year 8 and love it still. Childish but who cares! I've heard that the manga is much less childish and more mature. I want to buy the novels. I am loving those fantastic fic writers here on ffn. There are really some fantastic ones out there!

I shall try my best to finish them some time in the future. At this time, I barely have enough time to finish my 18,000 word thesis! So perhaps during the big summer break!

In the meantime, feel free to message me and look at me fics and review.
