Author has written 50 stories for Practice, Daughters of the Moon, Harry Potter, Charmed, Ally McBeal, Misc. Books, Alias, Pirates of the Caribbean, Once and Again, Instant Star, One Tree Hill, Gilmore Girls, House, M.D., Lost, Greek, Grey's Anatomy, FlashForward, Parenthood, and Bones.

Ah, well, I've been writing fanfiction as a way to ease my writer's block since I was about eleven. All I know is writing and I love to dabble in fanfiction (recently House, Greek, and Lost) now and again.

I'm an English major at Grand Canyon University, and I'm going for my bachelor's currently and eventually my MFA in Creative Writing. (I figure, there are no jobs out there right now, so I might as well keep going to school, eh?) I love to read and I also love to edit just about anything; papers, stories, articles, etc. So if you need anything looked over, feel free to pm me!

I just recently became a beta-reader and I love it, so if you're looking for a beta, let me know.

I also write on livejournal, but I'm not very involved there at the moment. My site's mainly just a database of all of my fanfics, but if you're on lj, feel free to add me.

Lastly, please forgive me for any of my earlier stories...I was eleven-fourteen when I wrote most of the older ones (for the Practice, Harry Potter, Ally McBeal, Charmed), but I cannot find it within me to delete them. So there they sit.

And about me personally? Well, I'm 22 years old, I work too much, make too little, love my friends dearly, and have learned to find irony in humor in all of the catastrophies thrown my way. I currently write for House (House/Cameron, Wilson/Cameron, and if I'm up for a challenge Cuddy), Lost (Kate/Sawyer, Claire/Charlie, Kate/Jack, Juliet/Sawyer, Juliet/Jack), Greek (Casey/Cappie and sometimes Rebecca), Gilmore Girls (Rory/Jess plain and simple), Once and Again (Grace/Mr. Dimitri...I know, I'm disturbed...), and recently I've gotten back into Harry Potter and began writing Severus/Lily. But overall, I write in canon...I like to write behind the scenes pieces and I tend to fall for doomed pairings. Ah, well, more challenging that way, I suppose.
