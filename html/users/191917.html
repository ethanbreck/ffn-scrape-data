Author has written 3 stories for Cowboy Bebop, and Harry Potter.

I shall use this space to cover my own ass. Any intellectual property that I may use is property of their respective owners and I am making no claims in any way shape or form as to the ownership of these characters. This is purely for entertainment purposes and I am making no profit from these stories in any way, shape, or form.

I pretty much fell out of writing for quite some time (obviously) and still haven't started back up. Only recently got back into fanfiction after playing through the Mass Effect trilogy and wanting to re-experience it without having to play it again. This eventually lead me to re-read a bunch of my favorites and now I'm hooked again. There's been an idea floating around in my head for quite some time for a semi-gritty, post Deathly Hallows story centered around Harry as an Auror still dealing with the fallout from the war. We shall see if it ever manages to make it to paper.

Let Me Be [Complete/One-Shot] quick and dirty take on what I consider my favorite pairing the HP universe. Tonks is pretty much the coolest character you can find in the series and I still think killing her off to keep Arthur around in book 5 was a weak move.

[Abandoned] Fallen Seraphim is mainly my bid into the world of alternate ending Bebop fics because, face it, none of us wanted that kinda ending. I will be one of the first to state that it fit the series, but, to put it in the words of Agent Orange, "I wasn't quite done with these guys yet and damnit, that's what fanfiction is for."

[Abandoned] Shadows of War is a fic based on Harry Potter set after the events of Order of the Phoenix, but prior to The Half-Blood Prince. It follows a series of events that take place from mid-summer sixth year until the end of the war against Voldemort. For those of you who are into short fics that take a few minutes to read, I'd recommend you turn back now since based upon my concept for the story this will be a rather long endeavour.
