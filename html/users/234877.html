What can I say? I love Harry Potter! It's what I live for!

Alright, so, what do you wanna know about me? I COULD tell you I'm a five-dollar streetwalker who likes to party...but I'd be lying. ;) or am i? Hehe, just kidding. Anyway, on with my life: I'm super hyper, anyone who knows me could tell ya that, and I love it! I like eating waffles on Saturday mornings, playing DDR and reading novels. I hate closed-up roses, dark chocolate and I can't live without rice. Got rice, bitch? I also like wearing a tiara and pretending I'm a princess. My shoe size is 7, and I have two cavities. I like pineapple on my pizza and italian dressing on my salad. The best video game ever is Super Mario Bros. on NES. I'm a camera whore and I love to color. My favorite lotion is Warm Vanilla Sugar from Bath and Body Works and my favorite animal is the Giant Panda. I love Classical Disney movies and Broadway Musicals. I like singing in the shower and dancing along to the radio. Purple? Yeah, that's my favorite color of all time and that will never change. I'd like to have Dane Cook's babies, but I wouldn't say no to Aladdin either. ;) I like curling up and reading a good book with a cup of tea beside me. I love romance and happy endings. I want to travel the world and experience true love. I'm a hopeless romantic who loves happy endings... I guess that's a little peek at my messed-up mind
