Author has written 5 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Fire Emblem, Star Wars, and Fruits Basket.

Ok, first off...ANDRAYA IS JUST A NAME! If you see that name in multiple stories within the same genre...it's a coincidence! I just like the name and the spelling like that. It's rarely the same person between fics. And if it was, I would say so. So the Andraya Saino in Mirror is NOT the same person in OLTC, and the Andraya in FND is yet another person entirely. I just like the name. So curse my lack of creativity in the name department if you like, but now you have no excuse to be confused as to how Andraya got from the FE universe to the SW universe.

Moving on...

I'm back! I know, I know, I haven't published or updated anything in forever. Yeah, I know. But my life has changed a lot in that time, and I'm pretty much just now getting settled into any sort of routine.

So...updates? Yes! But only one story for now, sorry guys. Obsession Leads to Coruscant, the stupid title I gave to my favorite idea for a Star Wars fanfic ever, is my current project, and I'm going to work on it until I finish it. It's rattling around in my head, pretty much complete, so I need to get it written so I can move on to something else.

Maybe after I'm done with it, I'll go back to my Fire Emblem story. I recently got into playing the games again, and I remembered the fic. Whoops. Been how many years now? Sorry...but still no updates yet. I doubt anyone still cares, but if anyone does...maybe someday. Sorry, but that's all I can give you for now. :(

And if anyone ever comes up with a better title for OLTC, please let me know. I just needed something so I could post it here, but I hate that stupid title more each day. It focuses too much on the part of the story in the beginning that I didn't even write. D'oh.

Also, I changed my e-mail address recently, and completely forgot to change it here. Whoops. So if anyone sent me anything recently, sorry... :( Try again, though. I don't bite, really.

Also, if you like my stuff, make sure to check out Obsession at my fictionpress account of the same name. It's my pet :)

That's it for now. I'm a Star Wars and video game nerd that likes to write but never can seem to finish a story. Me in a nutshell! Oh, and I go to an art university, so no one's allowed to bug me for daily updates. :)
