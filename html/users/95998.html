To whatever fans I have left, I owe you an apology.

In my last story update, which seems like forever ago now, I joked that only my death would prevent me from finishing my stories.

Well, that almost happened.

A few months ago I suffered a house fire. I've lost my cat of twelve years, over 1,000 material items, and temporarily been displaced from my home. In the month that followed the fire, I also lost some friends, one of whom I'd known for about a decade. As you can imagine, I grew terribly depressed. It got to a point where I was, once again, suicidal. I'm currently looking into therapy options for myself.

I'm sorry to my fans. I'm sorry that I've become one of those writers who never updates. I hate that just as much as you all do. Right now though I obviously have to focus on getting better first and my free fan fiction writing second. I hope you all understand. Some days I still struggle to get out of bed, let alone write anything, let alone write anything good. Please be patient with me while I recover. I'll try my best to make it worth the wait.

Thanks for your continued support of my work.
