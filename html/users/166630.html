Updated 11.8.15

NaNoWriMo was my first attempt at Steampunk this year. I'm going to try and finish it and see if it will go to a publisher or just up on the internet somewhere.

For those wondering, I am going to finish AWTaF. I've been listening to the audio book of HMC obsessively in preparation for getting back into it. Sadly this summer witnessed the theft of a computer and external hard drive containing all of my writing for the last ten years, which I think includes the starts for my next chapters in AWTaF. Here's hoping they're still rattling around my brain somewhere. And a pox on all burglars. That is all.

Thanks to everyone for reading. Your story favourites and reviews mean a lot.
