It's been a while since I updated this...

Since my last update I have found gainful employment - that is to say I have a 'real' job (along the lines of what I went to school for). Every now and again real life eats my brain. This means that any and all updates will be sporadic.

Anyway I am on this site because I love stories. I am one of those people who will speculate for hours, days, week, months (I think you get the picture) on end about what can, should, or would be cool to happen. This is of course where most little ideas come from.

I'll apologize right now to anyone who may have been reading the one fic I have posted here. It is on hiatus indefinitely. If nothing else it needs a serious overhaul.
