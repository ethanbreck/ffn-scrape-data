Hi,

I would like to apologize for the quality of the fics that are posted on this website. I wrote most of these when I was in high school and was just starting out, when I'd decided that I wanted to be a writer, but didn't understand what went into writing a story, how to create believable characters, manage a plot or otherwise. I understand that most of the fics on this board contain "Mary Sues". I am DEEPLY SORRY for their existence. It's been around twelve years since the creation of most of these stories, and in that time, I've gone to college and graduated with a bachelors degree in Literature and actually know now how to write a story and how to make believable characters and not write CRAP, because all of these stories are CRAP. The only reason that I haven't taken them down yet, is because I feel they are a learning experience for me. I may leave them up for a few more days, but most of them are getting deleted. The only fandoms that I write in anymore are Tolkien fandoms.

If you're interested in reading any of those, you can find them on my tumblr: chronicles-of-tirion.tumblr.com

Thanks.
