For those of you who knew me once or elsewhere as casper12 or McSplunge - name change alert!

What is below is currently not the full extent of my work. I had two other 'fics' which were removed by ff.net for being lists. One was '404 Ways to Annoy...Severus Snape' and another was it's dark lord counterpart '101 Ways to Annoy ... Lord Voldemort'. I have numerous requests to re-post these. Obviously, I cannot do that here.

The lists are now, however, currently (and very generously) being hosted in their original format at Lady Claudia's Snape Website 'Why Snape?' at: . Whether you could care less about the lists or not, I would urge any fan of Snape's or anyone even interested in the character to visit this site, it is well worth it. Lady Claudia, who has been kind enough to offer food and shelter to my outlaw lists, gives us an informative and entertaining selection of Snape related writings and paraphernalia.

I also have numerous requests from ff writers who wish to base a fic on something out of those lists. They have my blessing, I ask only that they credit the original work. Some of those same writers have asked for copies of the lists to work off - I am happy to email them out as I have done, just write and ask. But since now it would probably by easier to copy/paste them
from Lady Claudia's page, I merely ask that you email me and let me know that
you plan to write something. Mail me a link to wherever you post it too, I love to read about what people draw from it, how they imagine reactions etc. It's all good.

As for what remains - two poems. I hope to extend myself into actual stories sometime soon, but in the meantime - do read what little I have posted so far. The reviews seem to indicate it isn't as bad as I thought and hey, it can't take more than two minutes of your time, can it?

I am 19 years of age and female. I live in Australia and see surprisingly little of the world outside my bedroom. 'Strange', 'Wierd' and 'Creepy' are terms which have been applied to me that I do not like. 'Eccentric' is one that I begrudgingly accept.

Besides the obvious predilection for Harry Potter (and the ff pairing therein, HG/SS) I am also a huge fan of Mervyn Laurence Peake's 'Titus Books' (otherwise known as the 'Gormenghast trilogy') of Monty Python's Flying Circus and it's various related multimedia, of the bands The Whitlams, The Who & well, many others. Art-wise, I like Goya, Van Gogh and Gaugin. Terry Gilliam & Anthony Minghella a favourite film director of mine. If you happen to share in any of these interests in any way (or just want to make contact for any reason, really) you can feel free to email or ICQ me, I don't mind, rather, I welcome it. Am I supposed to be saying all this? If I think of anything else I'll add it later. Well, I say that now...
