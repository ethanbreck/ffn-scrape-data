So allow me to introduce myself. I'm Kelly. Yes, my userID says otherwise, but I always had a love for the name Kimberly, so I used it here. It also allowed me some anonymity.

I'm an 80s geek. If I didn't hold such love for today's computers, I would be fully content wearing oversized sweaters, teasing my hair, jamming to the latest 80s band, and watching shows like "Family Ties" and "The Facts of Life."

Also, Disney shows can be pure love. I was a big "Lizzie McGuire" fan a few years ago and even helped in the creation of the now-on-hiatus website, Digital Bean Smoothie. I also continue to update an Adam Lamberg blog entitled Life After Gordo.

"Life With Derek" is my newest fandom, and I'm a strong supporter of Dasey. The fandom is addicting and I find myself spending much of my free time on YouTube, LiveJournal, and an array of different websites that involve the fandom.

I'm brand new to writing fanfiction. To be honest, I'm more of a graphic artist. I have a strong love for Photoshop (I am a Graphic Design major), and I enjoy editing videos regardless of the program. For my YouTube contributions, check out my account.
