AUTHOR IS MIA! DOESN'T INTEND TO UPDATE EVER!!

So about 3 years ago I logged into my account because I was getting periodically sent reviews regarding some of my stories and I made a promise that I had every intention of keeping, and well I'm sorry.

I don't honestly think that anyone is likely to be reading this after all this time, but I feel like it should be said. I said I would update, and I didn't, and to be honest I don't intend to. While I'm writting this, I am thinking about posting some of the stories that I have written for my friends in the past years, and possibly reposting the stories that were lost in the Great Fanfic.net Fire (I don't know what happened to them...) but as much as I enjoy reading fanfic, the part of my life where writting it was something that I did is gone. Which isn't to say that I'll never be writting or posting on this site again, but it's not something that I see doing regularly in the forseeable future.

Thanks again to everyone who read and enjoyed my stories over the years. Sharing them with you, well it meant the world to me, to know that there was something that people liked that I did. I know a lot of us here come from that background and I think it means the world to us, to know that the world is there listening... Or reading as it were :D

If there are any fans of my stories posted here who wish to adopt or continue them; I would like nothing more. I'll even pass along my blueprints assuming I can still find them :D
There would be no greater joy for me than to know that my stories have made someone feel so strongly that they want to put their own pen on them.
That being said, I now consider my original style of writting to be slightly painful to read, so as much as I appreciate suggestions and "oh! there's a spelling mistake here", well I can't honestly say that I'm going to pay attention at this point.
I mean it's like reading my diary from back then... I've got like 6 spellings for banana on one page.

I will (now that I know how) be listing my favourite authors and stories here. If you liked my ideas, I hope that you look through them and find someone else to read as well. I mean isn't that what you're here for?

Signing Off,
GhostJ
