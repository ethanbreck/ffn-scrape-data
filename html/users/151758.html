As you all can see, I am a total reviewer now, and I have decided -- until I can finish my degree in Japanese, and STILL remember my mother tongue of English, I will not longer write fanfiction --Plus, I cant remember why I wrote half the stuff I did.

My desired field of writing or reading is in anime/Books/Games, also crossovers...sooooooometimes!!Only if its an interesting mix!!

Sailormoon, Digimon, Card Captors Sakura, Inuyasha, Gundam Wing, Final Fantasy X and X-2, Final Fantasy VIII, Naruto, BubbleGum crisis, Harry Potter, Animorphs (don't ask), Discworld, Bleach, Cowboy bebop, etc very old school in taste -_-

New story updates: I have finally decided after ..ermm 8 years, to take down my old fics and slowly work on the old concepts. I have to re-read all the crap that I wrote when I was 14!

Anyho, all my new stuff will probably come out next year ... or I can wait till 2012 and release my fics for their 10year anniversary...

Until the sun sets, and I fight no more...
