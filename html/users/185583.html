Hi!

First off, thank you for taking the time to read my fanfiction. As you can probably tell, I'm a fan of The West Wing. Most of my stories are based on the show, and dedicated mainly to the character of Toby Ziegler.

So, some personal information: Well, I'm a Travel Agent from Boise Idaho. I've been an agent for almost 2 years, and I love it. I continue to write fanfiction in my spare time, which is limited as of late. I do enjoy feedback, good or bad. I understand that I may not be the best writer out there, and I don't claim to be. I write for fun, and most of the time my stories are silly and are written just to get a laugh.

I hope to write more in the future. And I hope to write some on my other favorite character, Niles, from The Nanny. If anyone has any comments, please feel free to email me. Thanks again for reading.

Take care,

Doc
