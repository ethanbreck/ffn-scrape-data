The 411

I'm Silence. I write, I read, and I indulge in too much sugar. I like shiny objects and am easily distracted. My fan fic resume across the internet consists mostly of Buffy the Vampire Slayer fics. Buffy crossovers, mass crossovers and the wonderful world of fics that make no sense what so ever. I don't use beta readers, simply because I don't like to be a bother about it to others. I'm also the permanent victim of the Typo Beast, because I speak Typo flawlessly.

I'm a chaos fic junkie. I love chaos in fan fiction but only if it makes sense. Yeah, that's kind of confusing, isn't it. I'm a hardcore Buffy/crossover fan. In fact most of the fics I read these days have to be crossovers of some sort just to hold my interest.
I like "Mary Sues". They're usually fun and most characters called a Mary-Sue really aren't, as it has become a term used for any female character someone doesn't like. Know the difference, people! There is one, trust me.
I even write Mary Sues on purpose for, you know, reasons.
