Dislikes: Stupid people, ignorance, cruelty, Jennifer Lopez, brutality, stuck up people.

To be perfectly honest, I've been out of the fanfiction writing scene for a while now for I've been dedicating all of my free time to my novels, which I hope to have at least one manuscript by the end of '07. And I really don't know if I'll ever be back in the fanfic swing. Why I'm updating this? I really don't know. I just got the feeling that this would be a good thing to do. And being the intutive person I am, I decided to roll with the whim. Chances are fairly good that no one will ever read this and I am perfectly okay with that. Actually, its kind of liberating to write about myself like this. I never write about myself, truth be told.

As you may or may not have noticed, dear reader, I am a bit older than most on this fine establishment. In my defense, I must say I've been a member for a good...oh...three or four years. I'd have to check in with a friend of mine to be sure. A friend who I happened to meet on this very site. Isn't that funny? I thought it was. She remains one of my dearest friends to this very day, close to five years later. Funny old world we live in, eh?

I suppose now would be a good time to put about the types of animes, etc, etc that I am fond of? Very well.

My favorite all of time would have to be Yu-Gi-Oh! It's such an entertaining, strange, and utterly interesting tale - it doesn't hurt that the guys are smoking hot either - not to mention it centers around Eighteenth Dynasty Ancient Egypt (a time in which I, myself, am very fascinated with...and I'm working on several novels about it).

So. We have Yu-Gi-Oh! Next would have to be...
Chrono Crusade.
Ouran High School Host Club.
Ghost Stories.
DN Angel.
Bleach.
Gundam Wing.
Inuyasha.
Zoids.
Tenchi.

I like other animes but...those are the ones I can think of at the moment. To be perfectly honest, I love anything with Dan Green in it. Ever seen that foreign film, Cutie Honey? Yeah...I loved it. Why? No, it wasn't because of the stupid sexual humor (although that didn't hurt none) it was because one of the badies was voiced by none other than the ultra sexy Dan Green. purrs ...Well, his voice is sexy anything. Its the whole reason I started watching Yu-Gi-Oh!...so I could fantasize about his voice...but that's besides the point...
