Author has written 8 stories for Avatar: Last Airbender.

THE "THREE DRINKS" SERIES

A Girl's Drink, A Man's Drink, and A Royal Drink are all Avatar: The Last Airbender stories, and are meant to be read in that order. I began them when I believed Iroh to be dead, between Season 2 and 3. They are something of an AU, now, but I still like them very much. They are told from Zuko's perspective.

THE "THREE CHORES" SERIES

Mending, Cooking and Gathering are the three chores in the Three Chores Series, and are meant to be read in that order. I wrote them after Season 3 began, specifically after "The Western Air Temple" aired. They are told from Katara's perspective.

OZAI'S VENGEANCE

This is a complete Avatar post-series story. Season 3 has yet to end, but many other authors have done post-series stories, especially about Zuko and Katara. I wanted to do something a little different from the standard capture or arranged marriage story. This story takes place twelve years after the final battle, when an illness begins sweeping the Fire Nation. Zuko summons Katara to help heal the victims with bloodbending. But all is not as it seems, and along the way both of them learn what they want from life after the war.

STORMBENDERS

This is a Season 3 AU set after "The Firebending Masters." It features a group of rogue water ninja living in the Fire Nation jungle who ask Zuko and Katara to go on a secret mission to the Fire Nation capitol. It is told from Katara's perspective, and involves plenty of bending battles, tea, innuendo, and chase scenes.
