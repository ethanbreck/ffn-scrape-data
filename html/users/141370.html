Author has written 6 stories for Movie X-overs, Miracles, Dragonriders of Pern series, and Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.

I started writing as a kid. I think my first ever story was a short story that was written about my favorite stuffed toy (remember the Wrinkles puppets from the 80s?) and the obsession with writing has just continued since then :)

During High School I stumbled upon the Dragonriders of Pern series and fell in love. I spent most of my time day dreaming about what it would be like to be a dragonrider, and eventually began working on a story, that, of course, involved all of my friends (because that's what you did back then) It has since been rewritten several times and I'm now in the process of posting it here under the title Renegade Weyr. (Currently both Small Miracles and In Stars are on hiatus)

I recently self published my first novel Revenants by Christina L Olson. It's the first book in The Undying Ones series about a cursed castle where the dead don't stay dead. If you're a fan of Game of Thrones and The Walking Dead, you'll probably love it. You can find it here mybook.to/Revenants
