Yinza.com

Now accepting artwork commissions! I host my artwork and original fiction on my site. You'll also find the complete Final Fantasy VII script, recorded specifically for everyone's fanfiction reference needs.

Please let me know if you find typos or instances of FF.net eating things, and I'll do my best to fix them!

Break Me -- An AU take on FF7, if Aeris had lived. Originally written between 2003 and 2004, and edited over 2009 and 2010. It is, in my opinion, much better written, but has a very different mood from Fallen; don't go looking here for WAFF. A 3-chapter epilogue is available on my website.

Fallen -- Rewrite of "Like Fallen Angels." It's a lot more balanced both style-wise and in how it presents the characters and story. Prologue 45 chapters (though with a significantly lower word count than the original).

Something Beautiful -- AeriSeph oneshot, written in response to a fanfic challenge.

Not So Different -- AeriSeph oneshot, written in response to a fanfic challenge.

Like Fallen Angels -- The first major FF7 fic I ever wrote, begun in January 2001 and completed in February 2003. In other words, this fic is OLD, initially had no idea where it was going, and frankly, the early chapters make me cringe. "Fallen," the rewrite, is much better. I've only left this version up for comparison purposes, and also because the reviews make me happy. =P
