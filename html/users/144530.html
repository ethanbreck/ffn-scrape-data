Author has written 8 stories for CSI: Miami, and CSI: New York.

I'm a youth librarian (hense the bookwrangler), old enough to know better (I think there's a song in there).

I work full-time, have a stay-at-home husband (no, he doesn't do housework, sorry) and three boys. I also have a few other hobbies and obligations. This is just a warning. Often life interferes w/ my fanfic writing. Every year is a little rougher. I now have to fight four other folks for a computer. And the one that was supposedly mine keeps being stolen by my DH. I'll try not to let things slide too long. God willing and the creek don't rise.

I love to write fanfic, in varying degrees of obsession. All my stuff is pretty much AU, I've never been one to hold strictly to Canon of any sort. I've been involved with Due South (might start posting it here. Maybe if I post, I'll finally finish the series.), have written but not published some HP and Doctor Who related. I've done a little CSI (not posted here.) My big stories involve CSI: Miami and CSI: NY. What else I might get into depends on Perdita, my muse and the plot bunnies. She has a little problem with getting distracted by the cute little bunnies.

Send me reviews, send me PMs. Good, bad or whatever. I'd love to hear from you. I might even write back. Who knows?

Bored waiting for me to update (I know it's been forever!!!)? Check out my favs. I don't chose favorites mildly. There are some awesome writers out there. Show them your love.
