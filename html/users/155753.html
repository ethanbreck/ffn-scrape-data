although i am a Sagittarius, i may shoot first and ride down your corpse without asking questions; i consider it an Art.

i admit, i'm somewhat crusty on the surface; and Cognitive Dissonance is the meme of my life. for example*i* want to break some capitalization or grammatical rules, but reserve the right get pissy when careless spelling or language makes a story unreadable. that's Chaos for you! anyway, i believe there's as great a difference between carelessness & creativity as between stupidity & wile, disorder & disguise.

i like slash, but don't like the obligatory hollywood sex scenes stuffed into so many modern novels & films. go figure! yaoi rox, G/L/Bi/Het, it's all great if you're supposed to be reading a romance or sex fic.

i'm also really into ANGST. hit me with your best unhappy wounded hero shot! take them to the breaking point! w00t! -just don't send them bouncing and frisking back the next bl00dy morning, as though nothing had happened, please. scars don't disappear overnight, even if you are an elf. raped char's don't act skittish for two days, then eagerly become horndogs.

Personal Quirks:
-- if your medieval char's are speaking self-help, new-age, or 12 step psychobabble to one another, i will print your fic so that i can burn it. in a bag of poo on your doorstep.

-- if your medieval, fully-grown, male char's are speaking like teeny-boppers and have the petulance of 14 year old girls, i will print your fic so that i can burn it and jump around on the ashes. in your living room.

-- if your fic's preface insists on reviews, demands reader acknowledgment, begs for flattery or fawning attention, and implies that you will withhold your gift if your ego is not sufficiently stroked you will never, ever hear so much as a single *mweep* from me. looking for direction or advice is one thing, looking for egoboo is pathetic. grrr! suffer my silent disdain.

-- if i absolutely love your work you will get some kind of pathetic, brief, inadequate, illiterate, spontaneous fan e-mail that you will dismiss as the work of a middle-school crank. ooops. sorry. it means your work has been well and deeply truly appreciated. no, really. i'm lame.
