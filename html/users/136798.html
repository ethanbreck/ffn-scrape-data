September 12th, 2017

I always keep finding myself drawn to Fanfiction.net. Though my love of fictional couples keeps changing, I keep coming to this site to fall in love with author's writing styles and to get lost in endless plots.

I've gone back and started re-reading a lot of old Bulma/Vegeta fanfics that I had loved. This has also made me realize that I wanted to finally go back and edit some of my old stories. I've been spending hours upon hours working on "A New Beginning". This was originally just going to go and draw out some of the details and keep the plot, but my writing was so cringe worthy I've ended up changing so much more than I anticipated. I'm scrapping a chapter or two and re-writing in new plot lines in. It's actually been a lot more fun than I thought it would be. So that will be posted once it's all done (hopefully within the next few weeks!).

As for "A New Life, A New Purpose", I've forgotten where I was going with that story so currently I'd say it's permanently on hiatus.

Please do check out the stories of amazing writers on my lists! They have written (from what I've read) truly wonderful stories across many wonderful fictional worlds.

If you'd like to contact me you can reach me at sapphire-angel@hotmail.com.
