Hey everyone,

It has been a very long time ago that I subscribed myself to fanfiction.net, in a time where I was less oppressed to write alot and had the inspiration to do so. I wrote a number of stories, with many of them ending into writer's blocks and before finishing I would find a new interest to write about. The only one I ever fully finished and was happy to do so, was the Lord of the Rings fanfiction "Loss of the Light", it truly carries a theme that I adore using in my stories. I recently started having some inspiration again to start writing again and the first task I put myself on was rewriting that story, hope you will enjoy the expanded version of the story, whether you have read the original or not.

On a personal note, I'm from Holland and I spend most my time talking, reading and writing english. That said, I am nowhere near perfect in the language, as I tend to write a bit too fast, especially in my younger years. Now, 12 years after first signing up to Fanfiction.net I have come back and hope to hold on to my inspiration to continue writing, if not here on fanfiction.net with fanfics, then on fictionpress with original stories.

In any case, thank you all for reading this and perhaps also my fanfiction, reviews as always, are welcome. Flaming rather not, constructive criticism much more appreciated. In the past reviews were always my fuel to continue, so if you are reading one of my fics, please :)

And for the Music Video I made to "A Thousand Years" between Cullen & Shayenna: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FEOMXz7yht8

And then the obvious disclaimer to the characters and storylines I took from the various games, books and movies I have written fanfiction about, I do not own any of them... unfortunatly ;)

Thanks and happy reading here on Fanfiction.net,

Jane
