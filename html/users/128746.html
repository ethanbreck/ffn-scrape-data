Hello, guys! I'm twenty years old... female (we think), and I'm a complete and utter anime addict. So, fanfiction, huh? All of my fanfiction takes place IN-UNIVERSE. I never write AU stuff, and you'll very, very rarely find anything out of character in my stories (except when we're talking parodies!) I know that most of my fanfiction is for Inuyasha, but I do write and read other fandoms... like, say, Hana-Kimi, FullMoon wo Sagashite, Fake, Slayers, etc. I do write alternate pairings, but I have to say that you will never see a Sesshoumaru/Kagome coming from me. Ew.

I do take story suggestions, and I really appreciate feedback on fiction. So, if you have the time, drop me a note!
