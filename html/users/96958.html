Greetings. Welcome to my head. It's a scary place, but once you get used to it, you'll find that it's...well, just as scary as it was in the beginning. But you'll adapt. I am one of the few, the proud. No, I am not in the marines. I am a Collins/Angel writer here on ff.net, and have been for the past 5 years. A rare find on this site, because everyone is too busy writing about Mark and Roger to care about the REAL male/male pairing. They get no love, but they're my babies, so I try to give them some. I conquer with Angel of the First Degree, Arch of Wand, and Shelby mainly, although BohemianCane04 and Nata, along with a few select others, help us along as well. We're not at all elitist. Want to join our cult:-) My older stuff? It's bad. REALLY bad. I wrote it 5 years ago, when was still new to this site, which can explain that. BUT, if you're in the mood to laugh at the badness, by all means, go right ahead. My newer stuff is much better. 08/14/2004: TO ALL MY READERS: I regret to inform you all that my days of posting on ff.net have come to an end. For those of you not in the know, after the posting of my latest story "As Long As You're Mine," the entire piece was deleted, all my reviews removed, and I was put on probation and banned from posting for a week, having been told that I violated the guidelines of the site by posting a story too high in sexual content/adult material for the rating I specified. I was not informed until afterwards, when nothing could be done. I can understand their argument, but I am extremely disappointed in ff.net's policy of deleting an author's work without even so much as a simple warning prior to doing so. I was not at all aware/informed that I'd violated any such guidelines, and I wasn't even given a small grace period to rectify the situation, or up the rating to R. Instead, they just deleted my story and anything that had to do with it. If it's their policy that they have the right to do so, I understand, but I strongly disagree and cannot continue to publish works on a site that is, in my opinion, unreasonable in their punishments of unintentional infringements. I will be setting up a new site shortly on which I will re-post all my works from this site, and also "As Long As You're Mine." I will be sure to inform you all of the new link, and you will be more than welcome to read or re-read my work (along with a few other authors who will be posting with me) over there. Hopefully, we'll be able to find a way to let you review as well. If anyone has any questions, feel free to email me. I would love to hear from you. Thank you to ff.net and to all my readers for 4 great years. I hope to see you on my new page. :-) Peace.

UPDATE 11/05: Hello again readers! No, I am not back for more ff.net writing. I still refuse to post on this site. However, I did want to give you this link to anyone who was interested in reading "As Long As You're Mine." FINALLY, at long last, I've gotten the darn thing up on a webpage!

Enjoy! You can send all feedback to my email. Happy reading!
