Author has written 5 stories for Digimon, Lunar: Silver Star, and Yami no Matsuei.

So I make these people and I be them instead, and I write them and dream them and while I walk around or drive or work or dance it's these people looking out of my eyes. I've gone on vacation, hung up a sign and vowed to let them play until the tide goes out again.

First things first, my writing comes sparingly, because my muse has proven himself exceedingly fickle. He works when he wants to. He's also currently in the guise of Adult Detective Ken, and moody. Very moody.

Secondly, most of my work will be anime.. I adore Digimon lately, it's a guarantee that if you story has the word "Kensuke" anywhere near that summary, I be readin' it. Same for any mention of Hisoka in a Yami no Matsuei fic. I also do original pieces, but with two jobs, classes, and trips to Japan it's kind of difficult to find the time to type.

But, we persevere..

I'm alllll about angst. Angst and .. ahem.. restraint. If you know what I mean. Show me a good fic involving Ken, Daisuke, and some good romantic angst, and I'll be your best friend forever.

*Currently working on*
Lots of Schism work lately, allowing me to break my "half a year between chapter" trend that I was not pleased with at all. I should be able to post a chapter tonight, and then I'll do my best to get a few more out before summer really hits and my summer blood deprives me of time and blood. As for other things I should work on, there are a few YnM ideas floating in the vast abyss of my head, one longer sort of comedic piece and a few drabbles. Someday I'd like to start on the sequel(s) to When to Bicker, and aside from that my NaNoWriMo piece needs to be finished and then completely overhauled. XD friggin hell. Someone make me a time machine...
