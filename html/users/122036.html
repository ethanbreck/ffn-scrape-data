Behind a Name:
Maron Kanzaki came to be my pen-name after role playing such character in a few Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG's on "Yahoo! Clubs" (before the merge to "Groups"). The name Maron originated from Maron Kusakabe from Kamekaze Kaitou Jeanne; the surname Kanzaki came from Hibiki Kanzaki from Macross 2: Lovers Again.
