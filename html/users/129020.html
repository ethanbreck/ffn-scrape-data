Hello!

I'm a 23 year old woman, who resides in Arizona. Let's see here, I enjoy reading, writing, drawing, anime, sewing, cosplaying, conventions, naughty stuff, and a whole lot more. I fell out of writing for a long while, with a horrible case of writer's block. Perhaps getting older has made me a hell of a lot more self conscious about my writing. Either way I'm trying to get back into it. I've recently began writing out the back story of my newest Harry Potter OC. (Newest being I've had her in my head for the past few years) Once I get her background worked out(I'm even going by the timeline of the HP world.) I'll work on getting a story with her started. I can almost feel the creative juices flowing. Though when I do get around to the story it'll most likely be up on my AFF.net account

Anyway! A lot of my work on here is very very very old, and it makes me cringe when I read it. I was probably around 16 or 17 when I last posted anything. My views on things have changed a lot, so yeah. I might be sprucing up some of my older work, making them a bit more mature. So if and when I do they'll be up on AFF.net

I'm at a loss for words. So...TATA!
