Hey everyone, Lady Potter here. I guess since this space is meant for my personal bio, that means I should put one down. Well, I am 18 years old and live in Pgh, Pa. I am about to be a High School Senior and am currently enrolled in a program for me to associate with a college professor about my writing for a class. I think I will have to post my work once it is graded. On with the discription then shall we? I have short curly brown hair and grey eyes with flecks of crystal blue. I'm only about 5'4". I love to write stories, especially about HP.
I have an odd sense of writing. My stories involve the characters themselves from the books, but they don't follow the story line. Most of the time my characters act in character, but a lot of times they don't. Also, the stories I write aren't about myself. Meaning that they don't involve me as a character, that also means that I'm not disguised as one. However, I've take some real life experiances of my own and worked them into the plot, changing them to fit my story line. Personal experiance makes a writer. All I ask is that you keep the flames to a dull inferno. It really puts a writer down when you completely put them down, suggestive opinions are good. That lets the writer know what they could do better! Well, I'm outtie! Peace!

~Lady P.~*
