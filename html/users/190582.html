Author has written 17 stories for Flushed Away, Reboot, Harry Potter, Nightmare Before Christmas, Doctor Who, Red Dwarf, Megamind, Hobbit, and Maleficent.

Hi, I'm MoonliteShdow (named after one of my all time favourite songs by Mike Oldfield and Maggie Reilly). I'm no stranger to fanfiction.net, having posted my first story on here back in 2002. However, I've been reading fanfiction since 1999, so I really am a dinosaur...

I do occasionally still post on here if I get into something good. Unfortunately, now I understand a little more about the process of writing I can spot many errors in my stories, especially the older ones, but I continue to leave them here for the enjoyment of others.

I collect movies, and have a big collection of DVD's and blu-rays. I also enjoy watching TV shows.

My other big hobby (since I was about fourteen) is collecting music - it will always be my first love, and since I have a couple of friends who are as music mad as myself (one, a DJ) we often seem to know random stuff about past charts and acts. I have quite an eclectic taste in music - basically if I like it, I buy it, whatever genre or decade.
