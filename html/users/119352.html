A little about me: I am 23 yr old recent college graduate, working in graphic design. College sucked up the last 2 years of my life (in a good way), but I unfortunately lost track of some of my favorite authors. However, I'm back now! Anyway, I live in Dallas, TX, but I am originally from Lousiana. I love all things fantasy/sci-fi/classic comics, including anime. My current anime obsessions are: Wolf's Rain and Inu Yasha. I also like Cowboy Bebop, Chobits, Jubei-chan, Escaflowne and a few others (I used to love DBZ, but I grew out of it obviously). I also love a lot of movies, so I am a big fan of the Star Wars universe obviously. I'm also a big fan of the most recent Superman movie, the LOTR, and Howl's Moving Castle (random, I know).

My favorite couple for a while now has been Sesshomaru and Rin (grown up, not as a child). I think it has an interesting fairytale dynamic to it, and I just can't get enough of the idea. I also enjoy the father/daughter fics that are just too cute! (BTW, if you have not read the recent manga, the current chapters 470+ have some great Sesshomaru character developments in them, involving Rin! and can we say Sesshomaru's mom!).

I also enjoy good Kagome and InuYasha fics. I've also been keeping up with the recent onslaught of Clark/Lois fics and have found a few favs I'm keeping up with.
