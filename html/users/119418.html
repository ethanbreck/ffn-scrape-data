Hi to everyone out there!

Pen name: cquill13

Definition of pen name:
'c' stands for the first letter of my name.
'quill' because that's what they used to write with in the olden times.
Also, 'cquill' sounds like sequel, which is a type of story.
'13' just because it's my favourite number.

Age: Some number between 1 and 100.

Gender: FEMALE

Country:
I live in Malta. (Readers: WHERE?) I know, I know, you've probably never heard of it. Actually, it's a small island in Europe, in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea. I know how to speak English because I've been learning it since I was about five or six years old, and NOT because it's our first language or anything. (waves at Bella)
