Author has written 21 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Clamp, Scryed, Pirates of the Caribbean, Spiral, Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, Alichino, Fruits Basket, House, M.D., Final Fantasy VII, and Time Machine.

Yes, I've been on hiatus for four hundred years. It will probably continue. I'm not dead though, and I still write (I've improved!) though nothing completed enough to post, or it's original fiction that I'm convinced no one will be interested in. Maybe one day, far into the mists of time, something will get finished, though it will probably be a Doctor Who fic.

If you really want, you can message me about a story and I'll give you my notes for it so you can know how it ends even if I haven't finished yet. Some stories, though, were started on a whim and were going to be written on the fly, or I didn't write notes and have forgotten how it was supposed to end. Such is life, and that happens during a crazy workschool hiatus. Still, I like getting messages. =3
