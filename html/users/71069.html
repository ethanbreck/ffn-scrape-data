Hello, thanks for looking
Fifth Voyager is the name of the AU fanfiction series based on Star Trek Voyager. It stars the original main cast along with several new others.

Final Update - November 2013

I've finally deleted every story on my profile. I quit this site years ago but I didn't quit writing. The series is still alive and well on its own website ( ) if you're interested in checking it out. I just don't care about getting reviews, views, favourites etc... anymore, none of those things are the reason I write anyway. Just having it on my own website makes things so much easier for me as well, I don't have as much time as I used to.

So yeah, that's that.

x Marill x

PS. I was surprised that during my deleting spree, the first story Aggressions was very recently favourited. If that was you, I apologise for the inconvenience. It still exists as its original form and in a rewrite on my site ;)
