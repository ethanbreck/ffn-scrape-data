I'm your average 15 year old girl. I have amibitions, dreams, hopes, fears, just like everyone else. Sometimes it feels like I'm pulled in 2 directions by what I think has become a split personality.

Sometimes I find myself being kind, sweet, and flirtacious. I'm a little charmer, and a talented student. But other times I find myself being stubborn, bitchy, and moody. This side of me has become known as Alexandra, whereas my normal side is affectionately called Alexis. They are as different as night and day.

I appreciate Reviews more than you will ever know, as an aspiring writer I love to know what my readers think about my story. So Please! Don't be shy. If you hate it, lemme know, and if you love it, Lemme know Twice!!

This comes from Alexandra and Alexis, the two souls in one body, with only one thing in common:

A Love for Harry Potter.
