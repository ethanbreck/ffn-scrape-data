Greetings...

My name is Dawn (aka Dragonsoul) and...it's been a while since I've updated my bio. I have had a renewed interest in my writing, specifically fanfiction, so you will probably see a slew of it coming in the next while. My life in general has vastly changed...yet again.

I have moved once more, to a nice little condo south of the city. I work as a Director's Assistant now (much more pay, wootness!) for a large energy company. And...I am dating again. Yes, again. To a wonderful man of integerity, honesty, kindness and humility. He's a really great guy who is my equal and my mate in every single way.

I love him.

More has happened in my life than a simple bio could say but suffice to say one thing has not, nor will ever change: my interest in writing. Here it is for you to enjoy...

Hobbies: Writing, video game playing, reading, watching TV, movies, attending cons, occasional parties and bar hopping, spending time with friends and family
