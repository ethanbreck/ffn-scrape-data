June 2014

Well, I'm still writing! I'm currently working on an original piece of fiction which I hope to publish next year. I am also playing around with a new fanfic, which I'm having loads of fun with.

Part of the reason for my absence is that I just had a baby (yea!). He is the most adorable little man in the whole wide world and I love him to bits, but he does make it hard to find time to write. However, in a world filled with an endless sea of bottles and poopy diapers, writing is turning into one of the only things that keeps me sane.

So, I can't make any promises about chapters going up every few days like I used to, but I'm sure you can understand.

My latest story is a LOTR's fic, which is kind of exciting, as I haven't "travelled" to Middle Earth in a very long time. It is called, "Postcards From Middle Earth". Please check it out and review.

Lizbit :)
