It's the end of January 2017, and I was looking through my various account options, (I wasn't sent a review email and was trying to figure out where the setting was to make sure that was on); and I found my list of signed reviews. I considered it, remembering fondly the stories I used to read and the entertainment there...and saw something that shocked me. I'd submitted a signed review in October of 2002. I've had an account on this site for at least 15 years; that's bizarre lol. I don't read as much as I used to, and I only recently started posting stories again, but that still makes me laugh to see.

Anyway, if you like my stories, you can find my original works here: amazon.com/author/lisasarter
