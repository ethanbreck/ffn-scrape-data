Author has written 115 stories for Mutant X, Stargate: SG-1, Desperate Housewives, Doctor Who, Coronation Street, EastEnders, Holby City, and X-Files.

I've been writing on and off for a rather long time (so I hope that there's at least some variation in quality as I've gotten better). I've pretty much had a couple of years off and I'm just trying to get back into the swing of things once more.

I'm the queen of unfinished fic (A lot of ideas and a very short attention span do not mix) but I do intend to go back and complete things especially my Corrie stories.

My thing generally is a lot of angst/darkness and detail but with a certain amount of hope. I don't write too much fluff and I don't go near kid or pregnancy in fic.

I normally write

Corrie: Carla with nearly anyone but especially Liam, Tony, Paul, Peter and Michelle

Eastenders: anything Ronnie centred

Holby City: Jac with anyone she has chemistry with but especially Joseph, Zosia, Michael, Connie or Oliver (I just hated Jonny)

I read in way more fandoms than I write such as X Files, Battlestar Galactica, Stargate SG1, Sanctuary, Warehouse 13, Person of Interest, Veronica Mars and probably a lot more
