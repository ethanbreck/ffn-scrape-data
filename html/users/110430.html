If you want to know about me this won't help much.

I am young, but old.
Silly yet serious.
Wise and a fool.
Odd and delirious.

I use too many words
As a poet should.
I'll not get it right
Like I wish I could.

I'm a cat and book freak and I like books. My favorite books would be Tolkien's works (before I heard of the movie, so there all you movie converts :-)), Jane Austen's works, WoT, Terry Pratchatt's Discworld, Valdemar, Dark is Rising, Redwall, Wrinkle in Time trilogy, Blade of the Immortal, Mara Daughter of the Nile, Sherwood Ring, Perilous Gard, and many many others (told you I was a book freak).

I know I'm not a good writer, I try though.
Please do not judge my writing on two of my poems: Ode to my Garbage Can and The Horror of My Room, they were both written on a sugar high.
Most of what I write in humour is not at all near what I normally write.
