Okay, this is for all the people who leave reviews asking/bitching at/whining at me about my bold text: I LIKE BOLD TEXT. Yeah, that's right, I LIKE IT. It makes things easier for me to read and so that is why I use it.

Apparently, that is not the case for everyone.

When I update things, I simply enter the story into the the magic story uploader and more or less go from there. I don't mess with it because the less steps there are, the less likely it is for my computer to eat my updates. This means things update in bold because that's how the magic story uploader does things.

NOW YOU KNOW. Please stop leaving reviews that say things like 'why is this in bold text? I hate bold text!' or 'bold text is annoying!' or screaming about your precious little eyeballs or anything like that. Comments like that do not help me become a better writer or motivate me to write more. All they do is annoy me and make me less likely to write.

Thank you.
