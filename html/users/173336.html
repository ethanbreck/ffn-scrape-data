Hi, I'm Emmu. 4218935943 years ago I began a lot of fics, and my life revolved around them. And then... I disappeared. What happened? I don't actually remember, I think I got caught up in a lot of things. Role-playing, for one. And my boyfriend (he wasn't at the time, but that's a minor detail) went off to Maine for the summer. I was afraid that, because all I thought about was him, all I'd write about was him. And I didn't want to mess up any stories, particularly not HPATGITSJ, so I didn't write anything. Then I finally began reading the Lord of the Rings series, as I'd promised myself long ago, and lost a lot of HP information that was lodged in my brain. I just saw HP2 on opening night, hopefully that'll refresh my memory. There may be a new chapter yet. Feel free to hit me repeatedly for abandoning FF.net.
