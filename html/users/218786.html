Author has written 6 stories for Pokémon, David Eddings, Warcraft, and Dota 2.

This account has been inactive for two entire years, and finally I've completed a story I'd started working on two years back. Hooray!

Well I'm just a typical, run-of-the-mill kind of guy who lives in a tiny island nation called Singapore. I started writing with "The Search of the 251st Pokémon". Yes, it's a corny title with corny contents, I know. But forgive me, I was young then. I'm only leaving that story up there as a souvenir of sorts. I even attempted at a sequel to that story, but gave up on it not long after it took off.

I've tried my hand at a David Eddings fanfiction, but that didn't really seem to work out. I couldn't think of ways to continue the plot, and I still can't think of any now.

After years of inactivity I suddenly got the urge to write all over again, and came up with "Old Loves, Old Hatreds", which was inspired by another fiction with a similar storyline. It was pretty badly done with terrible English, and I thought it a pity for such an interesting plot to be wasted like that. I cannot remember who the author of the fiction was nor what title the fiction had; after all, I did read it about two years ago. So I took the idea up and wrote a short fanfiction on it, spanning nine chapters long.

Well, now that "Old Loves, Old Hatreds" is over and done with, I might or might not write more fanfiction. I need inspiration, and although I've already had a storyline vaguely spinning around in my head it's too vague to put into any form of writing yet.

Oh, and if any of you ever deign to review my fanfiction, can you please be a little more comprehensive about it? "This is interesting, write more!" doesn't really tell me anything about what I did right and what I did wrong. A few sentences will only cost you about thirty seconds of your life, and you won't die from typing those few sentences will you? Thanks in advance then. :)

If ever you feel the need to contact me, my e-mail should be displayed in my profile, or you can visit my blog by clicking on the homepage link at the top to get me. I check back on my blog pretty often, same goes for my inbox. Just don't flame me on my tagboard or send me hate/chain mail; I hate it when people do that.

Adios then, people.

~Blitzed off by Wakka02~
