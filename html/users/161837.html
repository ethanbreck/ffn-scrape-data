I'm an on again, off again writer. Had several stories written here before but they were lost. A real pity since they were several pieces that I was rather fond of. Perhaps, soon-ish, I'll re-write them, and re-post. Although to be honest, I'm not really looking forward to re-writing them. Re-writes are never really the same as its original. We'll see what happens.

I enjoy writing. That doesn't mean that my pieces are any good. I prefer writing fanfics for the Anime/Manga genre but I've taken a liking to the Harry Potter realm. I've read more than a few good stories and wanted to try my hand at it. My favorite so far are the Dramione fics. I've read so many now that I can't help diverging from the normal path of love and happy endings. There's so many now, why add on to that? So yes, my stories will be on the other end of the spectrum. If you really don't like my stories, tell me why and we'll debate about it.

I'm currently working on several plot bunnies involving the Harry Potter world, but I did finish the one-shot (Cliche) and I admit it didn't quite end up the way I wanted to. *shrugs* I thought it was pretty morbid and satirical to the point of hate. My other work (Mr. Brightside) was also supposed to be a pretty ironic, especially since Draco Malfoy is anything but. But I was curious to see whether or not I could make it work.

So without further stalling on my part, I bid you a good day/night and a good reading.
