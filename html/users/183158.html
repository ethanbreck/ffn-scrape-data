*wave wave wave* Konnichiwa! I'm Seshiri! well uh O.o i like to write fanfics.. obviously... and uh i LOVE anime, totally obsessed with fy ^.^;; and er O.o yeah...

OK a few things explained about my fanfics.. i have TONS of personal jokes in there and i'll explain them here:

1) PB & J, why is there so much mention of them? ok.. @ school my principal said "Now at dances i don't want you all dancing sandwiched together like PB & Js OK?!" so since that i HAVE to have that somewhere in my fic ^.^;;;

2) Golden ball of light, pond with flowers, whats up with that? in 5th grade our science teacher made us meditate before class... she would actually say "envision the golden ball of light" and all that other stuff

3) In the JS+FY Seshiri says "Oh pickle" well THAT is because our "beloved" principal said that the cussing in our school was getting out of hand.. so she said: "Replace those cuss words with things like: Oh PICKLE, and Fiddlesticks!" so we all make fun of her by saying "OH PICKLE" heh, so yeah...

4) Fuddrucker? wtf is FUDDRUCKER? okay, well, in NYC nanuri and i shared a room. we saw a comercial for a burger place called "fuddruckers" we found this hysterically funny (because everything is at 2:30 am!!!!) and we were laughing and i fell in the crack between the bed and the wall... i began to scream fuddrucker. i finally got out and some girl came to our door and was like "helloo you're door is open!" so i didn't hear what she said.. i MEANT to say what, but instead i was all "fuddrucker?? fuddrucker??" as you can imagine that scared HER away...

5) "walk this way" mel brooks movies

6) blue soup, bridget jones's diary

7) "don't call me down for dinner" bye bye birdie!

8) "wannabe" okay, this is hard to explain. Nanuri and i were talking about a cartoon that is like an anime but not really. for an HOUR i kept saying it was a wannabe anime, but she denied it. i was like "well back then people didn't know anime that well, so it wasn't known as wannabe anime. but right when it enters now now things change to now now terms instead of THEN now terms. so it's wannabe anime because it's NOT anime but has anime elements" so now we crack up whenever we hear the word wannabe

9) "wannabe joe" joe was an x bf of gurase's who was like a stalker wannabe

10) the teddy bear tasuki gets seshiri.. I HAVE ONE AN I NAMED IT TASUKIIII! mainly cuz i could sleep with it at night and truthfully say "i sleep with tasuki every night!" (although it was originally nanuri's idea)

11) the whole 'woke me up by pounding on the keyboard' 'no i was typing QUIETLY' well that actually happened! i was sleeping over at nanuri's house, and then i was still asleep when she woke up so she decided to go on the computer. the keyboard was REALLY crappy, so whenever she typed the keys hit REALLY REALLY REALLY loudly, and it woke me up. i claimed she was pounding on the keyboards while she said "nooo i was typing quietly" it was funny...
