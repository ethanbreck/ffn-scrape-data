Wow, it's hard to believe that the sadness of real life has brought me to the place I've been for a bit.

I have gone through so much and this site has brought me much joy and many tears from well created works of literature from fan to fans.

I keep saying I will be coming back and then more foundation shaking things happen in my personal life to where I cannot touch a single letter on my keyboard for my rampant emotions or lack of time to work on things.

At least I finished college with an AA in Applied Science in Pre-nursing and I have a wonderful job which is taking me away from my main muse: Writing.

I hope that you, my fellow readers, have no lost faith in me. I know some of my stories will be in the process of being rewritten since I really need the calming effect that writing and sewing give me.

So please, when you think of me, drop me a line for possible one-shot requests or just little words of encouragement and comradeship.

With much Love,
