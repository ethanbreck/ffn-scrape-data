10/5/2015 Wow I haven't posted or written anything since I was in Highschool! That was 11 years ago! Man I'm rusty.

Okay so my writing style has probably changed a lot... I like to take an introspective look at things now, discover why characters act the way they do, their motivation and what not, and since it has been forever since I have even attempted to write any fanfiction you are going to have to go easy on me if my style seems inconsistent, I'm going to be experimenting. I try to stay true to character and I appreciate constructive criticism as well as suggestions and input. So if you want to say something about something I've written, have questions or comments that might be too long for the review section, feel free to PM me through this site and I will reply ASAP!

'Cuz... You know, I have no life. (T_T)

Anyway, my current obsess- er, "fandom" is TMNT. So while I had a lot of DBZ in the past, the turtles are going to be my new focus.

I promise a mix of thoughtful character analysis, humor, fluff, possibly some angst and maybe even some romance.

I also promise NO OC's! Unless it is a bad guy or minor background character needed as a plot device to fill in gaps or move the story forward. Any original characters, if any, will have small roles, they will never be love interests of the main characters and I doubt they will feature in any story long enough for you to even remember their names. I like to use as many characters from the actual series as possible, including lesser known ones from the comics, so if you hate Main Character falls in love with Mary Sue OC stories as much as I do, you will find relief here.

Also know that possible future stories may contain elements of things people find offensive, such as same sex or "gay" romances, trans-gendered issues, feminism, equal rights (for both men and women 'cuz guys are victims of sexism too ya' know!) so if any of this stuff leaves you butt-hurt, save yourself the aggravation and walk away now. If you are a homophobe, just know that every time you "flame" me or post hate comments on my work, it inspires me to create kinky homosexual scenes which I will continue to post on the internet until everyone has been corrupted BUWAHAHAHAHAHAAAA! *ahem* Thank you.

Also a warning to all you bible-thumpers out there who can't stand any thought or opinion that is different than your own. I am an atheist, I do not believe in Christianity, Catholicism, Judaism, Islam, Mormonism, or any other "isms" However, since Splinter is Japanese, he is most likely Buddhist with hints of Shinto, I also may incorporate other asian concepts such as Hinduism in my stories.

If your religion is so overwhelming in your personality that a fictional character who does not share your faith is offensive to you, again, please move along.

As for possible pairings... While I did like Apritello, I also know it's never going to happen because Eastman and Laird don't approve of turtle/human romances and they are pretty dead set on CaseyxApril. However, several artists who have worked for the creators of TMNT have hinted that they are not opposed to turtlecest. So I do spew out a turtle romance story, it will be my otps- Raph/Don and Leo/Mikey- with focus on Raph/Don. Don't like? Don't read. Everyone wins.

~Peace out y'all!
