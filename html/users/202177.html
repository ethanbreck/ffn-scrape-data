Some other important stuff you should maybe know:

An unnecessarily long title for a few simple ideas I would like to assert.

Mostly in relation to Kyou Kara Maou (though can be applied to other random fic sections)... In gay relationships, why must one of them always be a 'female' of sorts? Specifically, calling Wolfram the 'wife' or 'queen'. Honestly, in a society wherein homosexual relationships are freely and openly supported, do you really suppose they would demean a man's gender? In a sense, though these writers may be 'supporting' gay relationships, they are still being intolerant of the idea. Still slightly prejudiced. Many gay relationships have two very masculine men who would be insulted to be related to a female. If a man marries a man, they are husbands. If a boy marries a king, he is most likely going to be called the consort or prince consort or perhaps just prince. He may even be given a specific title, Duke or Earl or what have you. Now, you may argue, "But Wolfram and/or Yuuri is so GIRLY!" I say to you, Yuuri is a blundering, inexperinced fool, albeit endearing, and is definately not the I'm-going-to-dominate-you-sexually-and-domestically-because-I-can type. Wolfram, despite the pink nightie we all so love, is so manly I think he doesn't even know what to do with himself sometimes (and if you don't believe me, read the novels or manga. Wow. It makes you that much hotter, Wolfram. Really!). So there.

On another, though often related, topic. ... Mpreg. Oh, wow. Oh, WOW. What female doesn't wish such a thing existed! But here's the thing... It doesn't. So why must every fic in every section have it? Yes, that was hyperbole, but you understand. If you are going to write Mpreg, could you at LEAST incorporate Mmenstruation? Make it scientifically accurate? If you give me a VERY believable background and argument and evidence, I'll go with it. But otherwise, it's ridiculous.

Marysues. I don't think I need to explain this one, so I won't. Just ewwww...

The colour orange. I think it's ugly. Like bile or something. Just a little point of interest for those of you still reading. :)

This has gone on for much too long! So just enjoy!
