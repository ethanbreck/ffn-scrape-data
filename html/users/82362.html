Hello everyone, Angel here.

Well, I've finally buckled down and tried my hand at Fanfics again. I've been a loyal follower of Fanfiction (as you can probably tell from my favorites lists.) but I've always had a hard time writing my own. Not that I'm not able to. I'm told by a number of people that I'm quite talented with writing. My problem is I tend to loose interest and only write a story part way before jumping onto something else. Its a flaw of mine, one I'm working to correct.

But after many joyous years of reading over other peoples stories, I've decided to start some of my own.

A bit of an introduction of me.

I'm Canadian born and raised.

I like anime and games and my taste in fanfics shifts a lot, depending on how I'm feeling at the time.

I'm in my early 30's and I love cats.
