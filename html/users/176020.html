Seeing as it's been well over a year since I last updated this, I figured I'd better give it another rewrite.

Hey. :) I'm female and I live in the United States. I used to write for a bunch of genres on here but I deleted most of my fanfiction, leaving mostly marching band fiction left. All I can say is -- I love marching band. I've graduated high school but those years weren't enough, and I am continuing it into college as a music major (flute!). Band was and still is my life, I can't deny it.. actually I'm very proud of that fact. Who I am today is completely shaped by the music in my life. With that in mind, writing is something of a rarity for me nowadays. All of my free time is taken up by music-related activities, but I still find time to get something on here.

Really, feel free to review my stuff with comments/criticism/whatever. I take into consideration everything you all say. Thank you for visiting this page, because I'm assuming you're here only because you've read something of mine. :P
