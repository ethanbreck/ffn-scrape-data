This is me! Back from the dead once again! I will revive once every few years to bring my strangeness to cyberspace!

My co-writer for my Phoenix Wright fanfiction lost all the data, and we've both had too many other things on our minds to continue, so you will never see it finished. Ever.

My Bionicle fanfiction got screwed over the longer and longer that Bionicle went...because if you're a fan, you know how that ended. (Who the heck gives children's books such complicated plots? Greg Farshtey does.) So that's done.

About my four-oldest, potentially multi-chapter fanfictions--they're all discontinued. It's been so long I forgot what I was planning to do with them! Lo siento! Ano...there's not much to say, except that my fandom net has increased exponentially, which means I have more stories on hold than ever! At least I don't have a lot of fans to disappoint...heh-heh, things are easier when you keep everyone else's expectations low! To whatever fans I have left--don't wait up on me.
