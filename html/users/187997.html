Author has written 12 stories for Sailor Moon.

Let's see, what to say ...

I really enjoy reading and writing, not to mention chatting with my friends on the computer. I also enjoy rping, though I'm not currently involved in any.

I really like various different anime, though my favorite will always be Sailor Moon. To name a few others that I'm fond of would be Black Butler, Inuyasha, Detective Conan, Gundam Seed, Tokyo Mew Mew, Cowboy Bebop, Ranma 1/2, Eureka Seven, Ouran High School Host Club, Witch Hunter Robin, Esclaflowne, Wolf's Rain, and Card Captor Sakura. There probably are others, but I can't think of anything else right now.

Despite the fact that I like so many various anime, I've only ever written for Sailor Moon and that probably won't ever change. Well, I have attempted a Sailor Moon/Black Butler crossover but it isn't anywhere near ready to share and it might never be. I enjoy writing for Sailor Moon though, especially Usagi and Mamoru.

I can't really think of anything else to say right now ... I'll probably be around to edit this again later. Oh keep in mind that I am an extremely slow writer, but I do intend to finish my stories. If they are still up here for you to enjoy then I still intend to write for it. If there is no hope for me ever continuing something then I would either remove it or put up a notice saying so. Even when it might seem as if I've disappeared for long long periods of time, I will eventually always come back. I just suffer through horrible bouts of writer's block sometimes that is difficult to cure.

Update 1-29-2019: I apologize to everyone that is reading my stories. I know that it has been forever since I've updated my stories, but I haven't stopped writing them. I am actually working on my stories, its just I'm doing so very very slowly. I'm suffering a serious case of writers block for one thing, but I promise I haven't forgotten. I hope that you all are able to wait for me and will still enjoy those stories when I do get them out. I enjoy all of your reviews, but more then that I enjoy the fact that I was able to entertain you. Anyway I will be back as soon as I can. Please be patient with me. Thank you.

Also, I would like to invite you all to come visit a wonderful site. Destiny's Gateways. It has many many romantic stories with many great authors from many different categories and ratings. You can find it at destinysgateway.com . Please come join us.
