15/08/12: Someone is coming... a figure Darker than BLACK.

AutoBio: I've been away from FFN for a long time due to a lack of motivation and a general loss of inspiration. My writing hit a snag while writing Porta Interpono's 11th chapter and I have been trying to get it back. It hasn't been because I forgot about it. Believe me, it's been a nagging sensation in the back of my mind ever since I had to put it away. Right now I am debating on whether I should expand my Shingeki no Kyojin fic to be more than a one shot and just how I'm going to get to where I want Porta to be.

As for an autobio... I have a lot of fandoms that have no limitation in range. Since there are so many, I obviously, do not write for them all. Mostly this is because I have nothing to add in the way of fanfics to them. Most times, my motivation is to simply contribute when I think a fandom I adore doesn't have enough fics to support it on here or I can't find what I personally need in canon and there hasn't been anything by way of fanfiction either.

The goal is to write something with substance. In an age where ANYone can typed out a few words and hit the post button, I feel that writing has hit a decline as an artform. Having a writing program does not make you a writer, like simply having a camera does not automatically make you a photographer. It doesn't have to be poetry but words need to be written with a certain fluency that I feel is lacking in what I read now (either published works or posted ones).This is what I keep in mind when I write and it's probably why I haven't been able to finish my poor crossover fic! XD

Side note: It absolutely tickles me that Thaman still gets some traffic. I still believe in the story and the messages in it, it's just the ... ack! I feel bad for the readers who have to read how horribly written it is. I can't even promise that I'll rewrite it because it would be a complete retouching that I do not have time for.

Regarding LJ users: If you would like to add me to your Friends List, please let me know. If I don't know who you are, I'll most likely not add you to my friend's list. Just let me know who you are and I will have a separate Group List made up that will be visible to you.

Character names in earlier FFXV (formerly known as FFvsXIII):I would just like to note that though we have names for the three friends now, I will NOT be changing the names within my fics. The reason for this is because they were the characters as I had seen them and not what they actually are. They were merely interpretations.

Fic Translations There have been several instances where readers have asked me whether I would give them permission to translate any of my fics. I would like to post it here on My Profile, that I will decline any of these requests. There were some heavy issues that arose when Noctum was being translated into Chinese and I will not have that repeated. It is very humbling and touching to have people offer to translate your work and I am very thankful that there are those who would volunteer to do such an endeavor, but I cannot accept.

For Sentio Omnium: As mentioned in the Epilogue, I have posted up the complete list of every prompt that involves The Lost, The Undead, The Dragon Knight and The Distortion series in story chron order, at my LJ. \O/

30/10/16: An update at last! I wish it were longer but i just couldn't get it to be longer. The chapter would have dragged otherwise. When I saw that eye exchange in the chapter I just HAD to write something about both of their inner turmoil over what was happening. That it was even in canon is something. Why does Isayama keep giving these two such intense eye exchanges?! I toyed with the idea of writing a chapter in Rivaille's prospective but reasoned that this is a "T" rated fic and the language that he tends to use isn't "T" rated so I'll keep it with Mikasa's perspective.

27/02/2019: I honestly do not mean to update annually but somehow time just flies by me while it seems to also crawl along. The latest chapter of ATB has finally been posted and words cannot express how much I agonized over it. It's been written in my notebook for almost a year since I started almost immediately after I posted Amends. As per usual, I had my layout ready in my head but I wasn't quite there with the ending. The fic could very well end at this point but I feel like I have at least two more chapters left in me. We shall see.

Side Note: I totally heard their last exchange in Japanese. Her saying, "Hai" at the end, but it didn't seem right to write it out like that.
