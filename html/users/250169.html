How I first got into yaoi, well I never really thought about the sex part of things until I was about 12, but I've always thought that two boys together was better then having a love interest. When I was around 8 I noticed I tended to like shows where boys were hanging around other boys, such as Swat Cats, Thundercats, Ronin Warriors, DBZ, Johnny Quest, etc... I always thought it was better without female interference as I put it then. To this day I still have a gravitation towards male characters, but now a days I try and give the female characters a chance.

Fanfiction has been a part of my life for as long as I can recall reading online, lol. Fanfiction.net is actually very dear to me, because it was one of the first sites I found people like me. Girls interested in yaoi. It made me feel normal, like I wasn't just some weird kid who thought about "what could be" in television or books. I wasn't the only one who wanted there two characters to have a relationship!

Oh and please don't hate me, but I don't really like Twilight, but wanted to post my Lycan story there because I was using wolves similar to hers. I watched the movies and I like Jacob! I just don't really like Bella... cause she's a bad role model for woman. Edward, I'm more neutral about. Also... Vampires are not shiny. They are scary, and like to bite.
