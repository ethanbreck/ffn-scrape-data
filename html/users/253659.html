About My Writing:

I'm a mediocre writer, I mostly like making stories and often struggle with articulating things with words. I'd rather express them all in images if I could. Honestly, it's really been a long time since I've written anything of substance even though the stories my mind gets flooded with haven't stopped. It's been a little more than 4 years since I last updated anything here. But I've returned because the latest obsessions have grabbed hold of me, giving me a slew of new ideas and I hope to get rid of them by writing them down. Will I continue to write afterwards? Who really knows? I'm just going with the flow.
