GUESS WHO'S BACK?!

Hello. Name's HungrySano. I write stuff. I started writing fan fiction many years ago. It's been about 8 years since I've touched any of my stories here. Life happened, too me to hell and back and now I'm realizing that writing is a love I should have never left behind. So, let's just say me and writing have kissed and made up and I plan to be back for the long haul.

So, I'm working on some original stuff that I plan to novelize and publish, but in order to be successful, I want to brush up on my writing chops a bit. What's that mean? Well, for you guys, it means finally getting more "In Vice Versa" and "To Find Inner Strength", the two most popular stories on my account. Also, out of personal preference, I want to tackle "Truth, Lies, and Gunsmoke" because I adore Rebecca Thompson and her crazy adventures.

I apologize to everyone who has been waiting literally YEARS for me to get back to this and I plan on finally making good on these promises, as I now have the time and opportunity to dedicate to writing again. You guys have been awesome as I did notice that even during my turbulent hiatus, I still received email notifications of reviews, favorites and following for "In Vice Versa" and "To Find Inner Strength. Also, for fans of Mary Sue Hunters, I haven't forgotten you! I can't give out exact dates yet, but that will be resumed as well.

To newcomers, I warmly welcome you! I hope you enjoy reading my stories as my as I enjoy creating them.

All I ask from you guys is patience and constructive reviews. In the end, I just want to improve my writing ability for my original works. Just think of it as a Goku-like ambition. I want to get better and better, break my limits and improve some more. Something that I have lost and found again over my eight years away.
