...When the hell was my last bio written? Before I became me, that's for sure. Though of course, the hypocrite I am, in three months I'll be saying how I was such a loser for writing this one.

I hate people. Have for about 6-7 months now. I mean, when you really look at people, you realize how miserable we people truly are. We are so weak and pathetic. We never have the power to restrain ourselves, hate others instinctively, can never get rid of an emotion once it has been felt, we hurt others constantly, we're sadistic, uncaring, stupid, etc. I am a very sick, twisted mentally deranged person, so I'm TOLD. But who is to tell me that because of some mental 'disease' I'm SICK? Does that mean there is something WRONG with me? No. I may have a different perspective than the majority of people, or ALL people really seeing as how no two are alike, but that does not mean I have an illness.

People who say that should be killed. Just like fashion magazines, television shows, advertising, brand names, and all of us people. I do not think I am better than you - I hate myself too. Really, I do NOT want to be a mindless zombie, but these things affect even me by forcing me to rebel against them. I wish I could just support myself and my girlfriend, nobody else. Screw all of you.

And because I hate all of you for being people, a lot of you will no doubt isntinctively hate me. But do you disagree with me? When you have spent months in some of the severest depression and confusion EVER, or you don't even need that much, you realize how screwed up life really is. All these memories may be lies, as are our lives. We're all stereotypes, too. I'm the classic 'I hate stereotypes, hate myself, want to die, f*ck you guys, I'll go and shoot myself in the head' guy. I could really go on for hours on this subject. Anybody want to criticize me so I can bitch at you just cos I hate everything, my AIM name is MiKeZTrailMix, EMail is Offspringrulz86@aol.com, and my MSN Messenger name is ShadowJake07@hotmail.com

Moving onto another subject, and altho it has a lot of emotion to me, I will not depress yall. Music. Music is such a RELEASE from this planet. Without music I would die. Anything but nu-metal, rap, 'garage', and mainstream pop just takes me away to a whole other place. If anybody likes music as much as I do (I doubt it, honestly), send me an E-Mail. I have a couple of hundred fave bands, like all genres except for the ones mentioned above, and can really connect to people thru music. My 6 favorite bands are Offspring (I find them like a mix between the Clash (Conspiracy of One reminded me of Sandista!) and thrash metal (their instruments kick ass), plus there's all that emotion, badass vocals, and just lots fo energy. My second fave band is Nirvana because all the hate and depression is there, and tho the music is sometimes repititive it had a good beat to it. Third fave goes back a lot further, all the way to Lynyrd Skynyrd. Skynyrd had the most outrageous guitars in them and those guitars are the main reason I love the band so much. I didn't like the vocals too much tho. Fourth fave is Queen, but this is due to change. Queen have the greatest vocals in rock history, that much is known. But their instruments bore the hell out of me! They're alright, but GOD, I jes need some good music!! Fifth favorite is the Clash. No matter how many people hated Sandista! I loved it. It was a totally great mix of punk and experimental rock. I also bought Clash on Broadway, so I have 5 CDs by them. They have great melody, cool beats, absolute crap vocals, and everything. The only 'true' punk band I really like. Number 6 is the Kinks. I just love them so much, the greatest pop-rock band you could ever hope to hear.

Okay, now I'll move away from music to what this site is all about. I have TONS of ideas for stories - original AND fics. But the fact is, I get no reviews, and the idea of this place is for people to read them and criticize the writing so it can be better. I ENCOURAGE you people to flame me! But no. I know it seems bitchy of me, but the only reason I put stuff on here is so that I can become a better author so I can then provide you with better reading material. Yet it all goes on ignored. And nobody will read this either, cos nobody reads any of my stuff. So it's all pointless, isn't it?

If anybody is interested in my writing, please tell me so. One true fan of at least one of my works is all I ask for. If you like it and wish to see more of it, I will write. So long as I get somebody to criticize my writing and say what bits are good, I will continue writing.

Peace, love, empathy
