Bit of my background: I was born in Washington State, raised in Idaho, have lived in Oregon, Boston, MA. & Colorado. I've traveled quite a bit, seen much. Yellowstone National Park & the Black Hills area in South Dakota, are two of my favorite places. Anyone who reads my stories will find quite a bit of personal experience in them. As my writing teachers kept saying, write what you know. As the pros say, write what you're passionate about.

I'm divorced. I'm owned by a very pretty Russian Blue kitty girl named Adara and an active young Ocicat boy called Taj.

My chief hobby has over run my working space, but I keep at it. It is dollhouses and it shouldn't be surprising they all have stories about the people and pets that live in them.

I'm a filker, Science Fiction Folk singer and meet with our local group monthly.

Grew up reading SF with such writers as Andre Norton, Anne McCaffrey, Isaac Asimov, and others from the '50s, '60s and '70s. Worked in a bookstore during the 1980s and was very familiar with many of the big names. In more recent history I attend SF conventions, I know many of the pro, semi-pro and working to get published local writers, several of which are my friends. I also know many of the local artists, including a number who are nationally known. It's like that in fandom.
