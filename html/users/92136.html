Hello to all who may be reading this. Here is a quick summary of my favorite animes.

-I love escaflowne, as you can probably tell ,and Dilandau Sama is my fav. character. (He is my 'fire'.)
-Naruto is my current obsession and Gaara is my favorite male character ('earth'), Hinata my fav. female character and I like them as a couple as well. Naruto / Hinata is my favorite pairing though.
-Inuyasha is another one that I really enjoy. Sesshomaru Sama is my fav. character in that one ('ice') Favorite coupling is Sessh/Rin.
-Fullmetal Alchemist is up there as well. Winry is my fav. character ('metal'?) and fav coupling is Winry/Ed.

For some reason I like to relate my favorite characters to the elements. Dont ask me why. Allowing Sesshomaru Sama's 'ice' to fill in for 'water' I need to find one more favorite that can be 'wind'. Let me know if you guys can think of any character in any anime that might fit the description.

At any rate I've just finished writing my very first Naruto fanfic and I am fairly pleased with how it turned out. I do plan to continue with my Escaflowne stories for those of you who are still interested in them. I am trying to keep my Naruto fics as one shots as I have a bad habit of losing interest in stories and not updating leaving unhappy readers. I hate noy knowing how a story ends too so I feel your pain. I'll do my best not to let you guys down. ;) So please continue to R&R.

Lots of Love,
Karmen
