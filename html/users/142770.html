"Holy cricket! Are you really?"- Emma Watson as Hermione Granger in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone the film. (What is with that saying?)

"That was bloody brilliant!"- Rupert Grint as Ron Weasley in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone the film. (I'm sorry, but Ron would NEVER say that to Professor McGonagall's face- not without getting detention anyway.)

"But Hagrid, how am I to pay for all this? I haven't any money." Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter in... (yeah yeah, I'm not writing the name again). This is far too posh for Harry, the film-makers exagerated far too much. What Harry REALLY says is :Ahem: "Um- Hagrid? I haven't got any money." YES THERE IS A DIFFERENCE. If you are English you can really tell because you know about the differences in the tone of voice, accent etc., and you will understand me. Or think I'm crazy. Whatever...
