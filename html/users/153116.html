Author has written 3 stories for Naruto, StarTrek: The Original Series, and Inuyasha.

Updated as of 12-13-2018:

I've done an excellent job of forgetting I have this account since 2011 and before. I've been posting fanfiction to my Archive of Our Own site (PM me if you want the link for that, but what you'll see are my entries for the SPN-J2 BBs from previous years), but I've also not been really active in fanfiction in general for a long time. I'm starting to slowly get the itch to write again, so I'll be updating, posting, and writing reviews sometime soon (as of this update).

Still love fanfiction, writing, reading, and various other things. The stories that were here prior to this update are complete, and I won't be adding other chapters to them (though I may flesh out their universes, as the mood strikes me). I don't have a specific fandom that I'm really hardcore about (these days), and my interests in that area are pretty varied! What I'm interested in is digging into the psychology of some stories, and writing fanfic to explore their themes. Ranma 1/2, for example, is an amazing story, but definitely dated in regard to LGBTQ issues, and exploring the concept of sexuality in general.

Fanfiction, on top of being amazing, is also therapeutic for me, to read and to write. I hope you like my stories, and if something I've written piques your interest, I hope you'll share your thoughts with me.
