11.) Get my camera out and film myself doing dumb and possibly dangerous things.
12.) Hold a conversation about Dust Bunnies and their importance in our economy.
13.) Slam my head into a solid wall for an hour. (Don't worry, after the first twenty times, my head goes numb so I don't feel a thing!)
