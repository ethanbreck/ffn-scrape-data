01/29/15

So...Not dead? Almost dead? Haven't written in about three years kind of almost dead? It's been a weird few years, mostly because at one point I couldn't imagine going a day without writing and some how went years without a word on paper and no explanation as to why. Other than stagnation. I'm slowly getting better and easing my way back into writing.

That said, Connections is not completely abandoned. It will not be update anytime soon, however. If/when I get back into writing it, I'm going to need to finish several chapters before I'm willing to start posting again. It is one of my favorite things I've worked on and I hope to find my way back to it.

I've been writing since I started high school. That was a while ago. I won't say how long. I mostly write original fiction, and have even finished my first novel (yay!) which is posted on FictionPress under Sophie. In my original fiction, I tend to hint at slash but it's usually in the background.

With fanfic, I apparently love crossovers. It's what gets me motivated to write. I read and follow a lot of different fandoms, so there's a lot to choose from. I don't often use pairings in my fanfiction, though I may allude to some het, slash or both.

"All are lunatics, but he who can analyze his delusions is called a philosopher." - Ambrose Bierce
