How do I explain myself?

The basic facts? ...rather dull.
Me female. Me college student. Me Canadian.

My aspirations? ...I guess that works.
I want to do something in the medical field while writing professionally part time.

Or should I just blab? ...why not?

My name is Laura. I won't give you my last name because I am, of course, paranoid like ninety percent of my generation. I'm a newbie college student who hopes to make a tiny difference in the world, do some action on stage or infront of the camera and hopefully publish a work of mine or two. I like drawing and making ceramics too. To put it bluntly, I'm a Jill-of-All-Trades. Unfortunately, I'm also lazy, and often pursue these trades when someone gives me a kick in the pants.

Once again I will not say too much, since I am, of course, paranoid. This therefore means nothing. The truth is I just said that because it sounds midly amusing and it gives me an excuse to write a somewhat entertaining bio for your viewing pleasure (I said I was lazy after all).

Good day.
