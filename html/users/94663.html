Author has written 30 stories for Fushigi Yuugi, Anime X-overs, Ranma, Invader Zim, Angelique, Inuyasha, Sonic the Hedgehog, Naruto, and Yu-Gi-Oh.

So, are that interested in who I am? Don't get too excited, I'm just your average perverted fangirl with an exceptional grasp of syntax. Need more information that that? Currently I'm in the process of getting back into the writing game after a prolonged 7 year absence.

Here's a bit of fun trivia: My name originally came from playing D&D. I was a female monk. Can you guess who I designed my character after?

ALL my unfinished fics have been put on hiatus indefinitely due to switching fandoms, as such things do happen over time. I do apologize to the fans who have made me a known name in certain fandoms, although if you want to know how some of them were planned to have ended, then please feel free to contact me and I'll go into great detail how these stories would have wrapped up. In the meantime, look forward to some forthcoming projects as I sort out fresh ideas.

Stay tuned for updates.

Special thanks to my fans who've supported me as long as I've been writing and to the people who've put me on their favorites list. Hopefully you'll like where I'm going.

If you want more details, then check out my tumblr or facebook.
