Author has written 40 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, StarTrek: The Next Generation, Rocky Horror, X-Files, Alice in Wonderland, 2010, Dresden Files, Harry Potter, Sherlock, Sherlock Holmes, and Night Stalker.

What can I say about myself? First off, I'm an incurable J/Qer, after seeing them share the stage for the first time I knew they deserved each other! They're my first real ship and I still have a special place in my heart for them after all these years. I tend to favor crackships to the "normal ones" simply because they're more fun. I've dabbled in each as the mood takes me.

Thank you to all of my readers who keep me going. Your enjoyment of my stories means a lot to me. I know I veer off the beaten path oftentimes, but I'm always happy to take some down the road with me.

Special thanks goes to my loyal beta, SV, for all her help in keeping my stories and tenses straight. I shudder to think what I've put her through in the last year alone. Thanks for all you do :)

I tend to be more enthusiastic than talented, please keep that in mind if reading my fics! I'm just doing this for fun :-)
