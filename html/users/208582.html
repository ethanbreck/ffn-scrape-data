I am an elf.
STOP LAUGHING!!!! My parents admitted (after a severe Chinese Water Torture session) that they found me in the woods when I was a baby and had my ears clipped to prevent me from finding out my true identity.
So :P to all you elf-wannabes!
;)

I don't write much - I basically got this account so I could keep track of my favorite stories and authors (oh, btw - go read To Have and To Hold by Lady Rheena - its pretty long, but its THE best fic I've read on this site {and trust me, I read a LOT of fics, even though most of them are crap nowadays - all frigging mary sues or stoopid tired old humor fics}). So the stories I have on my favorites list are probably THE cream of the crop on this site - I am a veeeerry picky reader.

I don't know why i'm even writing this, because since I have like one fic (and thats not even mine, its just a compilation of humor that i've picked up here and there), nobody will ever read my profile. So if you ARE reading my profile, you either A) have no life (;) don't worry - I don't have one either! Lets start a no - life club!) or B) are a skeery stalker person *coughANTHONYcoughcough*. In which case STAY AWAY. Do NOT contact me through email, IM, snail mail, or send cheesy poofs through fed ex (okee, I made that last one up - too much South Park for me! :P)

Okay, so I'm rambling - what else is new?
And just because someone asked me this - I don't write because it would probably turn out to be a self - insertion (aka Mary Sue) fic, no matter what kind of fic I tried to write. And some may ask, "Who cares if its a Mary Sue?". Well go read Key's "One Moment, Untainted" (and if you have time, its sequel too, The Haven, or something like that). Its kind of explained in there. Its a short read (one chapter), so it will take 5 minutes of your time, tops! (and if you can't find it, its on my "favorite stories" list.
I don't write humor because there are SO MANY frigging humor fics on this list - all with the same theme and with the authors saying stupid things like "A/N: Skittles/pixie sticks/cheesypoofs and dr. pepper/sprite/nestea are BAD if you want to keep your sanity!" and "Why? I don't know - but my skittles/pixie sticks/cheesypoofs and dr. pepper/sprite/nestea do!" and crap like that.

Oh, and I DO like Star Wars and stuff like that, but I am mostly in the LOTR department. I very rarely go elsewhere, but when I do its always Spiderman or Moulin Rouge or His Dark Materials or Star Wars. I am just a hopeless case of LOTR obsession, one in a sea of many.
Oh well! :P
