(Monday, August 14, 2006)

Hello, my name's Andiavas but you may also call me Cherry.

For a while I have been writing at a very young age and I guess, as fast as my childhood has left me, so did my passion for writing. I've actually settled to read brilliant stories once in a while, but I am very caught up in my life right now. Maybe when I do find my leisure, I could continue my stories.. for I had so many wonderful plans for them, I'm sad to see them forgotten. It's like picking up an old photograph, dusting off the pictures, admiring them, and overall laughing at the sillyness of it and feeling slightly embarrassed for old behavior and memories.

But here are my two stories that I decided to keep. = )

You can decide to read but I must warn you, the grammar content is unbearable but I still kept it because the stories gave off such an innocent vibe.

I don't know anyone who would read this actually.

Toodles~
