Author has written 9 stories for 7th Heaven, Pirates of the Caribbean, Gilmore Girls, and 21 Jump Street.

"I reject your reality and subsitute my own"-Adam Savage

I love pirates. With the exception of a few other movies/tv shows, the majority of my fanfiction stories are about Pirates of the Caribbean because I just love the movies so much. The POTC trilogy is like my Star Wars. I love collecting stuff from it and love anything pirate related, anything with a skull and crossbones, skull and crossed swords, or skull and crossed torches. You get the idea lol.

Apart from my living vicariously through my characters when I type, I have 3 boys and am married to a guy who tolerates my fascination with pirates as long as I tolerate his Ah! My Goddess obsession and we usually enable each other's obsessions. Admittedly I like that series too, but not NEAR much as he does. Rather like how he likes pirates but nowhere NEAR as much as I do.

As far as my stories go, I try to portray Jack as the loopily sunny good man that he is, and not a devious, selfish womanizer. I won't write Sparrabeth because I think she and Will do belong together. Jack and Elizabeth simply have a sort of platonic love between them, as he did have Will stab the heart because he knew he couldn't live with himself if she lost Will forever and in "Winter" they are good friends.

Update as of 6/5/09: I've decided to revamp "Winter". LOL, yeah yeah, I know. Perfectionist struck again when I read the story over again. I appreciate all the glowing reviews that everyone has written for this story and I'm flattered that many of you like it the way it is.

Just wanting to say that I LOVED On Stranger Tides for the way that it portrayed Jack! Which is to say, just as I always thought him to be! :) A truly good man with his own moral compass, with a big heart that's not as black as he might want people to think it is, just sweet, naughty and trying to survive in a rough world while keeping his legend alive! :)

And he himself proved just what I always knew to be true, that he would make a WONDERFUL father. :) I always knew he would be a responsible father and would do the right thing by a child he sired, and this movie proved me right!! :):)

Who, me? Smug? Well...yeah!! LMAO!
