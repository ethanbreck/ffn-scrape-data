30 APRIL 2013

Wow... I suck at life right now, guys and girls. I do apologize, but this pilot thing is a lot of work. Anyway, I have been working on 'The American Ashikabi' chapters to follow, working through some writer's block to churn out a good one. I will update no later than the end of May, I promise. But expect it before then.

Thank you all for your continued support and reviews. I won't let you down anymore than I already have.

What up everyone? So, I decided to amend my last profile update. I read 'Taming the Beast Within' before wanting to take it down, and found out that I did some good work on it. So, while I'm still cranking out chapters for 'The American Ashikabi' I'll try to work in some new stuff for 'Taming' as well. I didn't realize what a gem it was...

19 MAY 2012

Holy shit, deployment sucks. Anyway...thank you to those who put 'American Ashikabi' as a favorite/update alert. That means a great deal to me, and for that, I am working diligently (in between my quals) to ensure that you aren't disappointed when I update again. I'm nearing completion of the next chapter, so bear with me. Also, I can't update until I pull into port...which rarely happens. Keep watching, it'll be there.

Thanks again to all of you who read my stuff, leave reviews, etc. It really means a lot to me, and I thank you from the bottom of my heart.

-StrkHwk
