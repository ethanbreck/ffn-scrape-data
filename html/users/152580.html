11/13/2013 -Over a year?! Seriously??

It's been awhile, and a lot has happened in my life, both good and bad. I've stayed out of the fandoms long enough. I've just finished playing the newest Phoenix Wright game and started a new story, Turnabout Strangers and Freaks. Ripoff of GTAV, but will change the title in due time. Since I've logged in for the first time in a year, I've found letters and emails from people requesting to repost my previous stories. I may do that eventually. Apparently someone took my previous penname Hunty, so I'm using my secondary penname Huntydamus Belmont.

Unfortunately, I can no longer own blogs at the moment due to a problem of mine that never goes away - Stalking. This one I actually had to call the authorities and I was ordered that until this issue was handled, I can't for any reason use them. So, updates may be posted between here and my personal facebook for the time being.

Favorite Music - Anything except Rap. ;.; it depresses me, believe it or not.

Personality - Quiet and Anti-social. Has had a long history of being backstabbed and being taken for an idiot, so I'm very hard to be friends with. Can easily sniff out a bad person. If you come to me the right way, I can be very loyal,caring and protective to friends. I'm stubborn, quite clumsy and it's hard for me to pay attention to most things.
