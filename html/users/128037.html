"All I have is a red trenchcoat, hairspray, and a fuse"

Okay, so the trenchcoat's black, and the fuse is actually a barbecue lighter, but you get the idea.

Yeah, I changed my pen-name. I figured that since I leave most of my unsigned reviews under 'Sage of Hairspray' I should be consistent. If you're curious, my pen-name used to be 'The Amber Dragon'.

If you've gotten bitched at by me in a review: I really don't mind flames (I like regular e-mails better, though). My e-mail is right there. *points* Knock yourself out.

Hm. Well, for starters, I'm a teenage girl living in the western United States. I love almost all anime that I come in contact with, the DragonBall series being a notable exception. My absolute favorite anime is Ronin Warriors, but I also like Gundam Wing. (I'm also adamantly against Sage/Mia parings. Grr.) As for my other obsessions... I love The Legend of Zelda video games, and I'm a closet Ghostbusters freak. Now that I've disclosed all of this information, you'll go review my story, right? ~_^
