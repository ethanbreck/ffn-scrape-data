Hello, my dears! I thought it would be nice to update my profile page since it has been nearly 7 years...what about me?

Well...I enjoy writing romance and fluffy topics such as friendship and family as you can probably tell from my huge list of fanfiction (I know there are many but I had a lot of time in between studying and eating and sleeping back in 2002). I like a bit of angst and hurt/comfort thrown in provided that there's some definite conclusion at the end on a bittersweet or sweet note. Like my drawing and painting, they give me an escape and calm me from my stress.

I love manga and anime even though I don't watch or read as much as I used to. My top favorites include Slam Dunk, Pokemon, Yu Yu Hakusho, Yumeiro Patissiere and Gensomaden Saiyuki (I have more but they'll take up too much room). I'm also into Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings and many cartoons in the 80s and 90s. Oh I forgot to mention that FAKE, Naruto and Hetalia are included in my collection of fave anime and manga now.

When I don't write fanfiction, I enjoy drawing and painting, writing some original children's stories, reading good books and manga, listening to my music and browse random clips on YouTube, browse the Internet and hang out with my family, friends and looking forward to weekends with my wonderful boyfriend who's been with me since 2005. I also like shopping for books, CDs and DVDs and play videogames on the PS3, my IPhone and IPad, my Nintendo 3DS and most recently, the Wii U. Dynasty Warriors, Samurai Warriors, Radiata Stories and the Kingdom Hearts and Yakuza series are my all time favorite games (among many many others). I occasionally like clothes shopping, provided they're casual yet pretty and feminine enough to wear on any occasion. I also like to watch DVDs; comedy, fantasy, some book-based, Disney classics, Dreamworks and Pixar and anything to do with animated features..

I also have to switch between my creative hobbies from time to time. One time I would be focused on writing for a few months and the next day, I would be busy drawing my anime art or go back to my embroidery. So don't be surprised if I suddenly stopped writing but be assured I will do all I can to complete my stories so long I have the motivation and inspiration to do them.

To readers: Some of my stories are related to shounen-ai and yaoi (as in male/male relationships). You're not obligated to read them if this is not your cup of tea and I usually put up warnings in the summary and/or my author's notes. Just click the back button and read something else that tickles your fancy. It's that simple.

Another thing: Although I have said that I only accept feedback and constructive criticism, it appears that my works, no matter how much I try to take care in my spelling and grammar and what not, are not immune to insensitive flamers...although I have accepted that this is a part of the fanfiction world, FLAMES are not appreciated in my world. Thousands of writers, including myself, write for enjoyment and the last thing we need is for a person to write a thoughtless sentence jabbing at how and what we write just to incite spite and to stir up a useless argument. And after being on Fanfiction.net for over 10 years with submissions done in between, I feel there is a huge definition difference between harsh constructive criticism and a flamer. So please...use your heads and just...turn back if you don't like what I write.

But enough doom and gloom, I have an account on the Deviantart website. Just look up my username vulpixfairy and you'll be able to find it. I post my anime art there with some other miscellaneous stuffs.

Anyway, I would like to thank every visitor that has read my old and new stories. It makes my heart happy that they are well read and is always a pleasant surprise to be given a review here and there and a favorite or a story or author alert. I know that I'm not writing as often as I liked to but when I do, I pour my soul and creativity to make it enjoyable for you guys to read :)

Kudos to you all, my dears!

Signed by the vulpixfairy
