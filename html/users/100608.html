Author has written 9 stories for Frasier, and Glee.

I began my fanfiction "career" writing stories from "Frasier", namely Niles and Daphne. While they continue to be somewhat of a muse...I have found myself hooked on the world of Wemma from "Glee" and now find myself enjoying writing for them. This is my secret passion for the whole world to see meaning those close to me know nothing of my talent for writing or my passion for it and yet I share it with the world!

A few things about me, I'm just 11 days older than my idol, Jayma Mays. I've spent the last 8 years working at the Vanderburgh Humane Society where I am the Public Relations/Event Coordinator. I share my home with my rescued balls of fur: Bindi & Mowgli (my dogs), Rupert, Bubba, Maddie, Stormy & Ophelia (my cats), and Lucky (my parrot). Our new feathered friend Elvis will be joining us this summer.

I love feedback and even ideas tossed my way. Writing does wonders for the soul
