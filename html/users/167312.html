Welcome to my bio. My name is Amanda. I write in my spare time, which I don't always have a lot of.

I've loved watching NCIS since about season 5, then I had to get caught up so thank God for USA marathons of it, and DVD boxed sets. I truly believe they made a mistake killing Jenny Shepard off.

In Your Arms is on Hiatus. I hope to continue it again soon, but I don't want to write crap, just to post a chapter.

*Update 8-6-17* Currently working on updates for Accidentally In Love. Between real life and other things, writing has been on the back burner for awhile now.

In Progress:

NCIS FICS

In Your Arms: This story takes place after the Season 9 finale, so if you have not yet seen it, there are some spoilers inside. Jenny Shepard did not die at the end of Season 5, so she is still the director here. What will happen after the explosion in the Navy Yard? Will Gibbs ever get to tell Jenny how he really feels about her? There will be plenty of Tiva and McAbby along with Jibbs of course, for everyone.

Accidentally In Love: Jenny Shepard is the probie on Agent Gibbs' team. What happens when his 12 year old daughter Kelly, and the new forensic scientist Abby try and push them together? Will sparks fly? Or are they just destined to not be together? Starts out before Paris.

In Your Arms the Deleted Scenes: Exactly what the title means.They will be one-shots that will be centered on flashbacks from chapters in the story, as well as anything significant that is mentioned--before the story actually begins and even some after the story ends.

Before Your Love: A lead up to the beginning of Accidentally In Love. This is just a collection of one-shots from the time Gibbs first learns about the accident that killed Shannon, leading up to the arrival of Jenny on his team. Looking to be about 5 chapters long.

NCIS Completed:

Surviving Judgment Day: When Tony answered Jenny's phone that day in the diner, Gibbs' world was turned upside down again. Just my take on Judgment Day with what I would have liked to seen happen.

NCIS One-Shots:

Chimera: What happens after Jenny sees the Chimera hit by a rocket? She thinks that Team Gibbs is still on board the vessel. *Tag to Season 5 episode Chimera*

Who You'd Be Today: What happens when Gibbs and Maddie open the 'time capsule'? Story inspired by season 5 episode Requiem and Kenny Chesney's Who You'd Be Today.

Homecoming Surprise: Gibbs had been recalled to active duty and sent over to Kabul, Afghanistan. Will he be able to make it home to see Kelly graduate from high school?

Getting Gibbs: Kelly and Jenny play a belated April Fools joke on Gibbs. *Tag to AIL*

*On Hiatus*

If Paris Never Happened: Jethro Gibbs and Jenny Shepard have had their fair share of tragedies. They meet each other in Mexico a year after Gibbs kills Pedro Hernandez. Neither one realizes the ramifications of a drunken night together until Gibbs is set to marry Diane. *Still to be written in any way shape or form. Looking to start it mid to late summer '15.*

Currently Untitled: Gibbs goes to visit Shannon and Kelly’s graves when he comes home from Kuwait, but is surprised to not see one for Kelly. He had been told he had lost both his girls. What if Kelly survived the crash and was staying with Special Agent Fornell of the FBI? Will Gibbs find out the truth, or will he be led to believe that his daughter is dead.

Coming Home: AU Gibbs and the team are sent to secure a Navy/Marine Veteran's Day celebration. Madison Thompson is a 16 year old high school junior, performing with her high school choir at the celebration. What happens when her path crosses with Gibbs? If this story happens it will be after a lot has ended.

Stand By You: What if Michael Mower got out of jail, and was once again stalking our favorite forensic scientist? How far would McGee go to protect her? Will Abby be able to escape him again? *Probably never going to happen*

One-shots in the works:

Return of Madam Director: When Jenny returns as Director of NCIS after the effects of Judgment Day, will her and Gibbs' relationship be able to survive? Or will it all come crashing down again.

Ex-File Tag: Story is currently untitled. What happens after the 'poof' when Gibbs catches Hollis listening to his tape of Shannon and Kelly? Will Jenny be able to provide him any comfort?

Time of My Life: Kelly is a senior in high school. The school's talent show includes a dance rendition of the song I've Had the Time of My Life, from Dirty Dancing. At Lo Levad: When Eli David is killed, Tony tries to be there for Ziva. He is haunted by her cries for her father as she saw him in Vance’s dining room. Will she let him be there for her? Or will she succeed in pushing him out of her life?
