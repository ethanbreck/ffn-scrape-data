I've removed all my stories and no longer write (though I do collaborate on occasion). This decision is due to a variety of reasons; the two major ones are because of plagiarism (many of my ideas were getting stolen and I was just tired of it all) and because of added responsibilities in my life.

who am I?
Laney-- older than twenty-five but younger than thirty-five -- probably old enough to be the mother of many of the people on this site

fandom:
When it comes to fandom I'm a non-slasher. I prefer my guys to share a familial relationship/like bond; be it brother/brother or father/son. Relationships like that are much more interesting and emotional to read about than cliche, out of character, slash relationships that have been done to death. Plus, I think making every relationship purely about sex cheapens relationships. People can love each other deeply, without being *in* love with them. People don't understand the various forms of love...
