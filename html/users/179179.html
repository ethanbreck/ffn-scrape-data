About...

My Writing:

Graded Absolutism will be updated as often as non-online life allows, sorry it can't be more often!

All of my fic is mirrored at AO3, if you prefer to read there.

I do have a Tumblr account, listed under the same username. Feel free to follow (and unfollow, I'm not everyone's cuppa!) as you like. Expect a fair amount of silliness.

Me:

I don't generally beta-read, sorry. I'm terribly subjective and tend to project. (Also, have you seen the technical errors in my work? Good gravy. I shouldn't be beta-ing a shopping list!)

I don't read much fanfiction, either. While there are some amazing writers out there, the possibility of inadvertently borrowing phrasing or imagery and recycling it into my own work makes me very cautious about indulging.

The old fic:

Although I was fairly active in the Farscape fandom around 2001-ish, I posted very little of my work in a public venue. When I left that community and closed my website, all of my fanfiction went, too.

I've had this account since '02, but finally (mid-2011) uploaded some of the things I wrote during my Farscape obsession.

Yes. I realize it is riddled with head-popping POV shifts, as well as other errors.

PS... Teri... if you ever get here... we love and miss you.
