Hey, my name's Chris. Now lets get down to the fiction.

I have wasted a lot of time trying to write sequels to stories because reviewers told me to or I felt like I should. This has interrupted my creativity. So from now on I will promise no sequels and give no "previews" of upcoming stories. If I finish a story I'll post it and hope someone enjoys reading it as much as I enjoyed writing it. But that's the great thing about fanfiction you don't have to write for anybody but yourself.
