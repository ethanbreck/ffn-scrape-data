Author has written 6 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, and True Blood.

I used to write Buffy fanfic and had a nice little handful of Spike/Buffy stories up before they took all the mature rated things down a few years go. I recently put a couple of them back on the site, but I have no plans to update in the BTVS fandom at this point.

These days, my fandom of choice is True Blood/SVM and I'm All. About. Eric. :-)

Thanks to everyone who was so great to me about my first E/S fic, She's Not There, a (mostly) canon story set between seasons 3 and 4, focusing on what was going on with Eric while Sookie was in fairyland. I also wrote a sequel, He's Not There, which took the story from the end of SNT to the end of the situation with the Shreveport witches. It was mostly canon up through a certain point and then I diverged from what we saw in Season 4. I am now working on a third story in the series that picks up at the end of HNT and will result (despite some angst along the way) in an S/E HEA girl. I know it's a weird mix of strategies, but I started writing within canon in order to get "warmed up" to writing fic at all again after a ten year hiatus, and it seems to be working! I'm feeling more comfortable branching out into my own imagination after doing that exercise.

I am also a PAINFULLY slow updater because I work full-time and have family responsibilities. My updating frequency is even worse when True Blood spoiler season is upon us since I run an Eric and Sookie fan blog. If you want to know more about my geekgrrl side, you can check it out at It's basically the dumping ground for any and all thoughts about the Eric/Sookie ship, from spoiler speculation to fanfic recs. If you are spoiler adverse, please tread carefully, because I am a TOTAL spoiler whore and post spoilers and speculation on the blog constantly. I try to hide spoilery details from the front page, but I don't want people to stumble on things accidentally. Consider yourself warned!

If my stories ever disappear unexpectedly due to FF.net policies, I do post on my blog and will also be posting on Archive of Our Own. Also check out my listing at Random Fandom!

Besides True Blood, I've also become a K drama addict, especially romantic comedies like Lie to Me and My Princess. I haven't written any fics based on Kdrama yet, but you may detect some K drama influences in my style.

Please note, reviews left by people who are not logged in (i.e. "Guests") are set to be moderated. If you are logged in as a Guest, a review may not show up automatically. I'd actually prefer that unnamed Guests not be allowed to review (since a lot of people tend to abuse the privilege of anonymity, but, hey, that is not the way ff.net is set up, alas.
