From All The Pretty Little Horses - Chapter 9

Ginny felt herself slipping away again as the black haired boy stared into her eyes. He traced a fingernail down her arm, drawing a thin line of blood. Ginny felt a sudden chill of cold fill her entire body as the room came into clear focus. Her ears buzzed and she didn't know what was happening.

"I own you," he repeated his mantra.

She didn't blink and just stared into his eyes.

He pierced her gaze with his own onyx stare, "Now show your master what you feel for him."
