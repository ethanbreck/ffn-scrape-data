WHAT SHALL I WRITE?

Words on paper

Now what do I write?

Shall it something sad

Or bring delight?

Words that are many

Or may they be few?

An idea old

Or entirely new?

A story, a poem?

What shall it be?

The answers to these

Are known only to me!

03/26/13

Mary Edna Ryan
