Author has written 16 stories for Tenchi Muyo, Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball Z, Escaflowne, Edward Scissorhands, Beetlejuice, Twilight, and Final Fantasy VII.

The name is Emochan.

I used to write a lot more when I was younger, but have been recently more and more inspired. So I've been writing when I have free time. It's not any kind of steady rhythm, but I try my best. I'm also branching away from doing anime-based fanfiction. Hope you enjoy my stories!

Ignore the spelling/grammatical errors in my stories. I usually try to catch them, but I tend to write in frenzies so I don't notice most of the mistakes (Word doesn't help often, either). I also will write at school on computers that are pretty lousy, so mistakes often get missed during transference. I also get really busy with school, and since I can't really publish these stories, it's not the highest priority on my list at the moment. I'm not perfect, and neither are my stories. xD I do plan on fixing them up in the future when have the time, so bear with me! Thanks to everyone for understanding!

Also, I obviously don't own any of the series I write these fanfics for. They are copyrighted and belong to their respective owners.

I only own my representations of the characters, and I love each and every one of them. Hope you will, too! :)

Go to my name on Fictionpress.com for my original works.
