"Here I sit upside-down on the surface of the planet earth,
held by gravity, scribbling a story
and yet
I know there's not even need for silence
but there's an aching mystery --

why else should we live but to discuss
(at least)
the horror and terror of all this life"

-excerpted from "Desolation Angels", Jack Kerouac

In time I'll be writing a number of Neon Genesis Evangelion fan fiction stories as part of a greater series. However, I write fan fiction as a hobby and do so only as a break from my original stories. This means that it will take a great deal of time to complete them. Luckily, I'm not the type to give up on things easily. I'm rather certain that I will eventually finish these stories; some of which are in there primordial planning stages, while others are already being reviewed by preliminary readers.

Until then, a note to any interested parties: Thank you for your support. I am merely on hiatus, and will reactivate this account with new stories eventually.
