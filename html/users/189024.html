Author has written 19 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Kamikaze Kaitou Jeanne, Escaflowne, Teen Titans, Incredibles, Bleach, Powerpuff Girls, El Cazador de la Bruja, Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha, Frozen, and Tangled.

About me: male, 31 years old, Austrian

I like fanfics which try to answer questions that aren't answered in canon-storylines, and I try to write mine that way too (mostly quite verbosely, you may already have noticed). So mostly my fanfics are sequels. And I have to admit that I like heartache-romances, so many of my pairings won't be happy at the beginning (yeah, I know, I need a girlfriend). Kinda strange liking though when I look at my favourite anime, but I don't like writing simple action - that maybe works on TV, but hardly in a fanfic. Aside from that I began making AMVs some time ago. If you want to, you can visit me at amv.org.

Favourite manga/anime: Yakitate Japan, Hellsing Ultimate, One piece

Favourite books: at the moment Terry Pratchett's "Monstrous Regiment" and Scott Lynchs "The lies of Locke Lamora"

Favourite film: at the moment "V for Vendetta", "The Avengers", "Rise of the Guardians" and Disney's "Frozen"

I haven't written anything in a long time... I'm working now and pen&paper RPGs and Anime eat my spare time for breakfast. But well, it's all about motivation.
