Ohayo gozaimasu, konnichiwa, or konbonwa. Gomen...I'm ranting on in Nihongo (Japanese). Well, this is my pitiful little profile. Anta suki? Uhh...there I go again. I'll try again...you like? Anyway...I'm a huge anime/manga otaku. My favorites are (but are not limited to) Dragonball/Z/GT (no really!), Yu Yu Hakusho, Yu-Gi-Oh, Ayashi no Ceres, Magic Knights Rayearth, Wish, Inu Yasha, Gundam Wing, X/1999, and Card Captor Sakura (NOT Cardcaptors! That has to be the WORST dub I have EVER seen!) And...like any good female otaku, have at least one bishonen from every one of them. :grins: I also love Tolkien's works (a.k.a.- Lord of the Rings, The Silmarillion, Lost Tales I and II...). The movies aren't half bad either! ^_^ My favorites are Legolas and Frodo. (Hehe...) What else? Oh, yes. Oddly enough, I've become a sort of Matrix fan. I sincerely doubt I'll ever write fanfiction for it though.

I suppose you would want to know what I look like. :sighs: Eeto...I have blonde hair that's slightly longer than shoulder length and grey-blue eyes (that oddly seem like they change colors...). Woohoo. You're boring average white chick, ne? Think again! I personally dispise describing myself, so forgive me if it sucks. As for my personality...I'd say that it pretty much speaks for itself in my writing. Even this bio could tell you a little bit about me! (i.e.- my insanity!)

I don't really specialize in any particular kind of fic. I write what I feel like writing, basically. That could be action/adventure, romance, angst, humor, combos of those, and more. You name it and I might write it. Anywho, A big thank you and a load of pixy sticks and kudos to those who have reviewed! Thanks so much! I just love getting feedback, even if it is flames. Be warned though, I use them to cook noodles and rice. :) Tabemono! Now that's a good way to put flames to use!

I realize there are TONS of errors in all my fics. I won't make very many excuses, as most of the time it's my fault, but there are some that don't get corrected or are overlooked. If you see one or more of those, feel free to point them out in a review or an email. Thanks! Oh, for anyone who care, yes I am crazy and a pixy stick addict. Maybe they should lock me up in the nut house...hey! Come to think of it, I wouldn't be that bad...as long as they give me a life-time supply of pixy sticks and my computer. It would be nice to have a life-time supply of chocolate too...okay that's it. I've lost it! MMWHAHAHA!! Pixy sticks! Come and get me Funny Farm! *a bunch of people in white coats run in and say something to Kakarrina* No computer?! NANI?! SHI'NE!! *whips out a window pole (the short kind you use to keep your window shut...it's wooden and kinda looks like a dowl) and bashes them all on the head* HAHAHA! Take that! :)

Three stupid side notes...
1. I've have massive amounts of homework and I can only really work on the weekends. Even then I don't have as much time as I wish I did. Kudasai...don't hold me responsible for the actions of my teachers. Again...gomen nasai! *bows* While I'm at it...a big "domo arigato gozaimasu" to all my reviewers! *bows again*
2. I'm begining to intigrate more and more Japanese into my fics, since that's the foreign language I'm taking. I'm not THAT great yet, so if you speak Nihongo very well and notice a grammatical error and/or the use of rude sentance structuring (unless intended), please notify me! Again, domo arigato gozaimasu.
3. If you need the translations for any the the words in my fics, I'll be happy to send them to you. Just don't send me 10,000 emails if I don't reply for a few days. I can't get on my computer every day and thus, can't check my email every day. Send me ONE email and if TWO weeks has gone past and I haven't answered, send me another one. Domo!
4. Ahh...I'm sure you all are thinking something along the lines of "Kakarrina-san wa jaamaa desu" (Kakarrina's annoying) or "Urusai!" (Shut up!/You're noisy!), but just in case you're wondering, about 50% of my work is on FictionPress.net, so if you're curioius, head over there and look me up! It's mostly old poems, but whatever. I'll stop ranting now. Jaa!
