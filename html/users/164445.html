Speaking of delightful pictures, Bridgie has drawn a really lovely illustration for Chapter Seven of "Squib Apprentice." It can be seen here:

Notes on my fiction: My kids are the ones who really got me obsessed with the glorious world of Harry Potter and I will always be grateful! (I try to remember how grateful I am when they are making messes in the house and refusing to do their homework... ;-)) I have a menial job at a school, which explains my fondness for a certain grumpy old guy with a cat. My stories are told from Filch's point of view. Snape is another favorite character of mine, who often plays a prominent role. Minerva McGonagall, Dumbledore, Hagrid, Draco Malfoy, Neville, Ginny, Harry, Ron and Hermione appear as well.
