Hey guys. I'm just updating this profile to say that nothing in here will ever be finished, barring a miracle, and that I'm moving my two favorite stories that were complete to my other profile, jacob,

So that's basically that. Maybe I'll come back here, most likely I won't. Sorry guys, but I'm a bit past Harry Potter now, and frankly the last couple books really made me more upset than anything else. I heavily disagree with how Rowlings ended and wrote the ending, so for now, no more HP. Thank you to everyone in this fandom who has ever reviewed, read, or written something, you guys are all wonderful people, and you know who you are.

I've noticed that FF.net has become rather swamped by people who cannot read or write, but think they can. This is dismaying to me, as back in the day, folks like that were regularly discouraged from not-improving their writing. And lots of people have this really stupid cut and paste thing they put in their profile. So here's mine:

Ninety-five percent of the kids out there are unconcerned by the dismal state of their education, or the fact that their fanfiction is raping the English language. If you're part of the five percent of fanfiction writers who do care about such things, cut and paste this, and then leave reviews for those poor souls who know not what they do.
