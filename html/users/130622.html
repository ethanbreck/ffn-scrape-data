ABOUT ME:
I have a writing addiction/passion (have been doing it since I was 4!) and if I could would do it 24/7 (damn RL job)! That's all the personal stuff you're going to get!

MY NAME:
So many have wondered about where AIP came from...long story very short. I have held back on saying this but feel I can now talk a bit more about it. The 'Princess' part of my name comes from my beloved who was lost when the towers fell on 9/11. He used to call me his Princess and we both loved Sci-fi (more so at the time than I do now) and Ice skating was a fave pastime. *sigh* miss him so much. I had thought of changing it over the past years since I've been on this site to something more writing related but just can't. Miss you - "always"!

MY WRITING STYLE:
I'm a story teller and love to entertain with stories. I write for escapism and entertainment; fast paced action; peril; some hot romance; heated conflict, emotional or tension filled angst or whatever else my overactive imagination (Alice my muse) can throw into the mix. I am here to write for fun and the sheer joy of channeling Alice's incessant ramblings (and trust me she never sleeps! eek) for you all to enjoy. No death fics! It's not entertaining when a fave character dies! Situational peril and emotional or mental angst are my true writer's voice.

I like to get into both sexes heads; to explore thoughts, fears, feelings, emotions, etc etc. I get tired of reading stories where women are the victims (or one male is whumped over/over/over again) nor a fan of DID stories (damsel in distress) *yawn* to all of those. And my stance on female OC's is this...I don't like or read them as a love interest or main protagonist BUT if she's a very well rounded, multi-dimensional character who is preferably the baddie you got my attention, but if you are writing a mary-sueish female OC based on yourself opposite the handsome male lead - I will pass, thank you.

So thank you for visiting and please enjoy my stories and REVIEW!! AiP
