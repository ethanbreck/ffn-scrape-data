De Moi

Salut, tout le monde! Ca va? J'mappelle Sarcastic...et Je suis 17. J'habite le Michigan et j'adore lire fanfiction de Harry Potter! Pourquoi Je me sens comme je remplis une annonce personnelle en français ? Je ne suis pas français. (Ok, time to stop the French, lol. I just like speaking what little French I do know...to gloat...lol.)

I don't know where all of that came from. Just had a French moment. Anyways, if you didn't catch any of that, I'm 17 and I'm from Michigan and I LOVE, LOVE, LOVE reading Harry Potter fanfiction almost as much as I love reading the actual books. Until recently (the beginning of last December) I had totally forgotten all about any types of fanfiction. I began reading stories on mugglenet.com when I remembered this site-where I'd initially signed up because I was a HUGE wrestling fanatic, in love with Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson. (So, if you read that story, don't hold it against me. If you read in the summary, it was done in a few moments of absolute boredom. It's HORRID, I know, don't tell me) I am trying to write my own HP fic, but since my days of writers' block with DJ, I still can't get all of my many ideas down right on paper to even think to type a story up to present to everyone! I consider myself a much better writer since 7th/8th grade and I expect nothing but the best from myself, so forgive me if I don't have a story up for a loooong time. Ok, I'm done boring you all.

A tout a l'heure,
Moi :-p

4/26/15 UPDATE:

It's hilarious to be able to go back and read what my 17 year old self said. I hadn't been on ff.net in a long while, but after suffering some set backs in life, and not having any new books to read at home, I came back to something I used to really be passionate about and found out that people still were writing stories. I don't know why I would think they wouldn't have, but I guess I just thought maybe the site wouldn't be running anymore. I'm glad it is. I was surprised I remembered the password still, lol. I've fallen in love all over again with the Harry Potter series through the eyes of several different people. I haven't actually been able to get the muse in me, though. The one story I have/had on here, I can't even bring myself to read...it's that awful. But I was...probably 14 then. If I hadn't grown since then, that'd be an awful, crying shame.

By the way, I ended up dropping French and going with Mandarin.
