Okay, it's pretty obvious that I haven't updated anything in a LONG time. My job hours make it hard to find time to sit down and write anymore. But I refuse to leave all of my fanfictions unfinished and it wouldn't be fair to my readers either. If I still have any out there lol.

My creative juices have finally started to flow from my brain to my pencil and I am now writing again. But here's the deal. I have gained the philosophy that I should wait until I finish a story before I start posting it. So right now, all of my stories are on hold until I finish them. And many of them will probably get some rewrite on existing chapters. Right now I'm focusing my attention on Return to Me. I'll start posting it when I complete it.

This may take a while so please, I beg of you to bear with me. I will be back one of these days with new chapters. I hope to see you then.
