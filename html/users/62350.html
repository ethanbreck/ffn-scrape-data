Homepage:

All Star Trek Stories posted in December of 2011 are backlogged stories that I didn't post back during the period 2002-2011. They mostly have not been touched since their original posting on the alt.startrek.creative groups.

Review and posting policy: If your review is unsigned and just says "post more" then I see no reason to approve it. If it's signed, you've added another day before I post that story. I generally do not post new parts closer than a week apart unless the whole work is done, in which case a posting schedule will be listed. I consider post more soon posts to be useless for readers, and harmful to writers. It tends to cause some authors to increase their writing speed to the point that it harms their writing due to less checking before posting. As a longtime archivist and awards coordinator, I will not encourage this.
