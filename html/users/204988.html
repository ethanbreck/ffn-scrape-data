I am The Golden Dragon. Also known as Taruriel. I love to read, especially J.R.R. Tolkien, Mercedes Lackey, Orson Scott Card, L.E. Modesitt Jr. or DragonLance. I like co-writing stories (currently I'm co-writing with Cami Drace and Dalamar the Dark) and writing original stories. I like getting reviews and I like reading funny stuff.
