Primarily a fangirl, I tend to read and review fics far more often than I get around to updating my own. Nonetheless, once I start a fic, I refuse to abandon it. Once it is posted on ff.net, it will get finished. Eventually.

The URL above points to the Wing & Two Fangirls website, currently serving mostly as a repository of information about WTF Productions anime music videos (AMVs) made by Starherd and myself. Eventually it will expand to include a fanfiction archive featuring our works.

If you're curious and feel like stalking me, you can read my livejournal:

I keep much more regular fic updates in it.

I read and write primarily Gundam Wing (Quatre-centric), Weiss Kreuz (Omi-centric), and Yami no Matsuei (Hisoka-centric) fics. I'm a big fan of bizarre pairings written believably, particularly 13x4 and SchuOmi.
