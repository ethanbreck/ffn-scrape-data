Formerly: moonlit rainbow

My name is Alyssa, but I go by Aly on the internet. I'm Canadian and I'm in the eleventh grade. I have two American Cocker Spaniels, Sassy and Farley. I have a huge, borderline unhealthy obsession with Grey's Anatomy, Gilmore Girls, and Bones, and while I have tons of favorite movies, one of my top ones is definitely National Treasure (both the amazing first movie and the even more amazing sequel!). If you want to know more about me, feel free to ask.

I have an account on fictionpress under the same pen name, which has a lot more of my work on it. I tend to avoid writing fanfics these days because I'm always worried I'm not going to be able to do the characters justice, especially TV ones, so I'm more of a fanfic reader now. I mainly read National Treasure fanfics and Bones fanfics, and I am a huge fan of the fluff!

Stressed About School: This isn't my favorite story, but it's the first fanfic I actually ever finished. I wrote this like three years ago, so it's not an accurate example of what I currently write like, just for the record. (I say this because I personally don't find it that good...)

Tagalong: So here's the thing. Part of me finds this story pretty good (if I do say so myself) and I want to continue it, but, as ashamed as I am to admit this because it is a clear example of how scatterbrained I am, I cannot remember where I was going with this. I, being the loser I am, forgot to make an outline or at least jot down my ideas before I started this, and then school kind of took over my life for a while, and all of a sudden I rediscover this on here, find a bunch of nice comments from some awesome people, and don't remember for the life of me what I was going to do with this story! So, even though I don't want to because it'll be like the fiftieth time I've done this, I may have to scrap this story. It drives me insane that I have a decent beginning and don't know what to do with it. If anyone has any suggestions, I'd be glad to hear them!
