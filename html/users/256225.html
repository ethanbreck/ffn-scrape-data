Hello, I am the Englishman who went up the hill and came down with all the bananas. Um, not really...

I'm a band nerd/freak/book nerd, if we're going to be all cliquey about it. My sex is actually female, and not "unknown" as I told the people at Trillian. *evil sniggering*

My obsessions include SOUE, LOTR, VeggieTales, LOTR, Steve Taylor, Newsboys, The Red Green Show, Are You Being Served?, Les Miserables, LOTR, and a few dozen other things.

My main things in life are fighting the forces of evil (mostly seagulls, nutcrackers, and Furbies), conquering the forces of stupidity, and...giving noogies to my little brother. Oh, and also staying up late every night, chronicling (spelling?) the Nutcracker Conspiracy thingy, and reading. In fact, reading is what ruined my eyes. Maybe that's why I like Steve Taylor and hang out with Unknown (a dude I met on the bus in high school, okay?). Don't ask me to explain my affinity to Skippy, however. Too involved.

If you really want to see my good writing, please look at my account on fictionpress... http://www.fictionpress.com/profile.php?userid=256225
