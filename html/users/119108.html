UPDATE - 10/15/2017

2:15PM

Wow. It's been...a while. Despite what you might have assumed from the disappearing act I pulled, I am indeed still around.

However, I spend my time reading fanfic these days, not writing it.

Being an adult in the Real Word drains me of the energy needed to write. I miss it, and I mourn the loss. I still find myself jotting down ideas or dreaming up new plotlines with each new fandom I fall into...but so far I've been unable to translate anything into an actual story.

My heartfelt apologies to my readers (old and new) who have been holding out for an update to Love Affair or What You Can't Fix. I like to think that someday my creative spark will come back, but I won't promise you anything. I feel terrible leaving you hanging, and I hope you can understand and forgive me.
