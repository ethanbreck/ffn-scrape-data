Hm, oddities - humour's my first and only love, but weirdly enough I've been writing nothing but angsty romance and drama lately. My name's Kathlene (But you can call me Kat... why not? Everyone else does) I'm 14 and living in Ireland.

If you know me, you'll know I'm usually the one in the corner of the classroom passing notes, doodling over her Geography book or staring at the ceiling with an amazingly stupid look on her face (The latter is usually when I've got a fic idea in my head)

Characters I Work With: Well; if you're familiar with my insane little writings, again you'll know that I adore toying with the Marauders ^^;; They're so fun to write. Good chemistry, too. Tom Riddle makes a few appearances, though that's more in the mad little ficlets I write for the HPN. I have no OCs *sob* as I've got a phobia of Mary-sues.

If you read "Romance, Sugar Quills and Irish Stew." (Abandoned because I got past chapter 2 and hadn't a clue how to continue) - well, Juliana isn't mine. She's also not normally a spoilt brat. She's Kris (Nagini)'s character and she's usually less toddler shaped!

Stories

Goodbye, Cinderella This was a One Shot, intended completely for fluff purposes. Draco's bizarrely OOC; he's only nearly sarcastic once! And I'm warning you, character death. The grammar leaves much to be desired (Run on sentences... at least there are commas in there) since by the time I got to proofreading my head was wrecked. But go on! Give it a try! I dare you!

Make Me a Fighter JK never went into detail on how Peter framed Sirius. We were only given the bare details, but not a scene... so, suffering from writer's block, Kat went and wrote it. Chock full of a few characters that we will NEVER meet again and are only mentioned in passing(Much like the Quidditch World Cup)This fic is my baby... so be NICE.

Looking Past the Obvious This was the first fic I ever finished and is probably lucky to ever make an appearance on ff.net! I mean, the poor baby's rather forlorn, chock with spelling mistakes and a little senseless (a few plot holes) but the main threaded story isn't ALL that bad (Who am I kidding?) ... I'd recommend you to steer clear. The only thing that's stopping me from deleting it is sentimentality.

Ask the Marauders Co authored from chapter 3 with Adele, this is utterly nonsensical... one of these days I'll get the 20 something reviews left unanswered and write another chapter... let's just hope it doesn't get deleted.
