Author has written 15 stories for Newsies, Harry Potter, Angel, Smallville, Star Wars, Heroes, True Blood, Dexter, and Watchmen.

"Listen - some things are worth worrying about. Impending doom is not one of them." - Fraggle Rock

I get obsessed easily and analyze everything, but I don't think that's ever an excuse to brood. I carry a book with me everywhere and forever write notes on the backs of my hands. Joseph Conrad, Annie Dillard, Mary Stewart, Tony Kushner, Fyodor Dostoevsky, Pablo Neruda, Carl Sandburg, Neil Gaiman, Margaret Atwood and countless other authors and poets never cease to inspire me. I like to pretend you always have a choice.
