First Name : Anna Last Name : Plurabelle Age : 21 Location : New York, Baby! Occupation : College Student...the poorest of the poor. Hobbies : Writing the great American novel or Scottish depends on the mood. Watching Fellini, Bergman and Lynch films so that I can say I love pretentious (foreign) films. Reading Joyce and Camus and Nietzsche, again just to be pretentious. Listening to indie rock bands, wondering whatever happened to music. Doing whatever anyone dares me to do. And reading and writing Days of Our Lives, General Hospital, Buffy, Angel, Harry Potter, and Star Wars fan fiction! Personal Bio : I was born in Iceland and immigrated to the United States (New York) at the tender age of two. At which point I began my illustrious career as a child as well as a would be dictator (believe me, it will happen). Upon puberty I began to show signs of extraordinary strength and intelligence, the cause of which has never yet to be revealed. Now a college student by day, and super hero by night, I pass the hours away plotting world dominance and the perfect story line for my next work of fiction.
