Wow. Long time no see!

I wandered back here on a whim and somehow remembered my login. Right now, I just plan to be writing fics as kind of a writing exercise, nothing too serious. Anything published about a decade ago I won't be updating. I was almost tempted to take those stories down, but it's nice to compare them with current material and see how things have changed. Or not, in case of by comma-abuse and tendency to delve into abstractions and overly-descriptive prose. Seriously guys, if you're reading my stuff and find yourself thinking "it's pretty...but I have no idea what it's about" let me know! I'm trying to work on that.
