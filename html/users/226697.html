Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter.

I've been absent for a nearly two years now. Why? First, there was university, where I committed myself to doing too much. By the time I learned the life lesson of knowing how much I can handle, a health crisis sprang up. There was months of dealing with side-effects from medication (doctors gave me too much) and recovery from surgery and the medication that I was placed on in order to deal with the side-effects of the previous medication. I then spent the rest of the year playing catch-up with organic chemistry among other courses. Now I'm studying abroad while managing my (finally regained) state of good health and facing the scary prospect of finishing university.

I plan on finishing Melange and maybe Maruading Lily (if I can get around to writing that epic... it is an epic based on the notes I had written). I realize it will take me even longer now to continue because it has indeed been a long time since I last looked at the stories. I've also changed. Who wouldn't change in two year? Let alone not change when life philosophies were challenged by health problem? I thank the readers who have been patient, and the new readers that Melange have picked up. Please bear with me.

Thanks again,

yours truly. (7 Dec. 2007)

I write more my own self-improvement. The art of communication is one of those over-looked skills in basic education, and I'm hoping practice will make up for the years of instruction lost.
