Hi, I'm Kaerra. I've finally decided to try my hand at a few Inuyasha fanfics (as opposed to college creative writing assignments). IY is so much more fun. ^_^ Feel free to drop me a line to ask a question about one of my stories or just to chat. Be warned though, I will probably blab your ear off. That said, thanks for checking out my stories! ^_^ Anyway, the webpage was originally an mp3 and anime cel gallery site, but it's in dire need of updating. Visit it if you like, but there's really not much to see.

8/1/04

Wow, it's been nearly a year since I last updated Much Ado. Man... time sure flies, even when you aren't having fun. So anyway, I have dabbled in the Harry Potter fandom for most of the past 6 months or so. But I promise, I won't discontinue Much Ado. I'm sort of getting back into Inuyasha again. But more than that, I don't want to let this story die. It's so much fun to write! ^_^ So anyway, thanks to all the reviewers who have stuck with me all this time. I swear, I won't disappoint you. Hopefully the next chapter will be finished sometime this week. It's the least I can do for you guys. ^_^

I suppose I need to list an update thingy like most authors do about the status of my fics... very well, here we go:

Active stories:

I'll post that information here when it happens. IMO, it will be well worth taking a look at for any serious writer looking to improve their fiction in general. : P
