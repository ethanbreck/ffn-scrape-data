Hmm...been a while but I figured I'd update this in the hopes that I will get motivated to write some more fiction--fan or otherwise--on this site. So, let's see...I'm an engineer (read: geek) whose Mind's Eye was struck blind about many years ago when I got busy in college. I'd like to attempt to start writing again and see if I can resurrect any imagination I might have left in me, lest I admit that undergraduate and graduate school killed it off completely.

Well, that was dramatic, wasn't it? :)

Despite my age I've found I have a great appreciation for a lot of present-day animation, games, and whatnot; mainly because the only thing worth watching on my TV is 'Law and Order' or the Cartoon Network (Reality TV must die!). So, a few things I tend to like:

Final Fantasy IV, VI, and X
Harry Potter
Inuyasha
Slayers

With regard to reviews of my work: please, if you can, leave me a way to contact you. I am the harshest critic of my own work so please don't think I will bite your head off if you have something negative (but constructive) to say about my writing...worst-case scenario is you end up with a 'thank you' when I revise the work in question:)

That's probably enough for now ;) In addition to all this, I am an avid fan of Role Playing games. Being old, I enjoy Dungeons and Dragons but I haven't played for quite a long time. :P

That's all for now! If you enjoy my work, please let me know.
