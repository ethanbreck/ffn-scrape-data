Author has written 10 stories for West Wing, A-Team, SeaQuest, Highlander, and StarTrek: The Next Generation.

Uh...I'm a 30 year old college graduate trying to figure out what to do with the rest of my life. I read any number of fandoms but the muse to write rarely stays long enough to finish. Hopefully this too shall pass

In case anyone is wondering I'm actually working on three epics. I've got over 20,000 words written on two and about 5,000 on the third. Don't know if I'll ever complete them but I do work on them when the muse and my computer are in the same place at once. Unfortunately that's rather seldom thanks to work and twenty other things vying for my attention.

One is a Stargate, New BSG crossover. It's probably the most fleshed out.

The second is a Highlander/A-Team crossover that takes place in an alternate universe version of the 5th season of the A-Team. It takes place sometime after the Highlander movie and before, during, and after the Series as we go although I'll change somethings.

The third and maybe my favorite is a Star Wars story. An AU of the Emprire strikes back. Han and Vader know one another and Han knows something about Vader that may well change his life.

So no I haven't given up writing I just refuse to post till they're finished cause I hate to leave people hanging, begging for more that I haven't written and don't know when it will be.
