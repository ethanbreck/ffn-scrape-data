f a n f i c t i o n

I've started writing fanfiction back in 2000. I've always loved to write, but I only started with fanfiction at that time because I was completely obsessed over Card Captor Sakura. I'm loyal to my fandoms and I never abandon them - but I tend to get a bit too obsessive over one or another and forget about the rest for a while.

Unfortunately, I haven't been active anymore - partly due to roleplaying, partly due to real life. I have no idea when or if I'm ever going to update any of my ongoing stories and for that, I apologise. I don't know how many of you were waiting for new chapters, but I just have no idea when I'll resume them. I still have ideas and I haven't given up - but I can't give anyone a date or a time limit to do so. But rest assured that, if I ever write anything new or update old stories, I'll post them here. Thank you for your support.
