ZephyrSamba, /ZEF-er-SOM-buh/, (n.):

A nocturnal, shortsighted creature of the Nerdis gooberius Family, characterized by highly-developed 'blathering' skills, a deep-rooted aversion to holes, and an inordinate fondness for insects. Favored activities include walking in the rain, reading the dictionary, and acquiring useless facts and tacky toys for her various collections. Known to trip over objects that aren't really there. A native Florida species, thrives in British environments though more commonly located in linguistics classrooms in the southeastern region of the United States.
