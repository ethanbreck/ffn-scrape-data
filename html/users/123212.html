Hello!

First... in case anyone wanted to know.. lol.. and I dont know why.. but Im not done with Take a Chance on Me.. yes.. I know I havent written on it for a while.. but I was feeling uninspired when the new episodes came out. Is it me, or did Chad get weird... he wasnt the same fun guy I loved in the first season. Maybe its his dorky haircut... So.. I will go back to this.. I plan to.. I just wasnt feeling it for a while... and besides.. it kinda makes me laugh a little.. kinda a departure from what I normaly write about.

Second..Fragari Vesca... I have an almost complete understanding of where Im taking Rukia and Ichigo in this fic. I really wanted to do something diffrent, but at the same time true to them.. does that make sence... hummms... I love writing about this pairing.. and this is the first fic I decited to post of all the one's ive written. Its been fun so far.. but I have changed a major plot point.. well.. Ive kinda changed it... I have two endings in mind.. I guess it will depend on how I feel when I get there? There is a lot of set up that I dont think ANYONE gets... which doesnt surprise me..but I kinda wish somone would.. or maybe someone does.. they just dont comment. Thats fine.. but can you pick out some of the plot? The obivious one is Ichigo and Rukia... duh.. this is about them first and formost.. but there is lots of subplots!! Well... maybe not lots.. but a few..

Third.. okay, so I know its NOT posted on FF.net.. but if anyone happens to be reading Alternatives from the other place.. and got linked to here.. hello! Yes.. I know.. Fragari Vesca kinda took over for awhile but I will be getting back to Alternatives soon! Hey! It's so insanly long now that I have to figure out how to break it up! Id say I didnt mean to write a fic with over a thousand chapters... but that would be a lie.. Alternatives is completly timelined and chaptered out! I knew from the first post that there was a 1000+ chapters... its my mini epic...

I say mini because the truth is its just good pratice for the actual story Ive written... Im still working out things on it.. but Ive had editors look at it.. and maybe someday I'll be a real published author. Its sooo much hard work and I love fan fic for giving my mind a break when my own characters demand things and I dont know quite how to work it out.. Alternatives has been great pratice for writing long plots and drawing characters out and still having the back bone of fan fic to do so.. since my real story is souly my responsiblity and doesnt have the backbone of being someone elses to help readers understand the characters.. I guess the same can be said for all of my fan fic.. I love writing them.. and I love what they teach me.
