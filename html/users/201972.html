Sundial ~

"We're fools whether we dance or not, so we might as well dance."

What the heck happened to fanfiction.net? Whats with this sudden abhoration (is that a word? At 4 AM it is!) of brackets. How do they expect us to seperate our stories!? No lines, no stars, no dots!? SERIOUSLY, when reading something I get SO freakin' CONFUSED when one scene suddenly jumps to another - and its not the authors fault. Its this site!

Oh, btw, my laptop is slowly dying and for some reason doesn't allow me to use any word document program aside from notepad ... which I have to fudge around and open in a really weird way (internet explorer - right click - view source). So spell check it out of the question, and I'm not a very good proof reader. Lazy~. Good thing I don't have to write any reports for school.

I'm an animation major! A bad one! Real lazy!

I play Final Fantasy 11 (obessively) and World of Warcraft (when I need a break from Final Fantasy 11).
