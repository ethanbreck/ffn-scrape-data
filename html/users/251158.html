Hi everyone!
If you're reading this I guess that you're about to read my story, or stories. That all depends how I'm feeling and how much I want to write in the future.
Let me tell you some things about myself. I'll start off with how old I am. I'm 16 (turning 17 in November). I'm a Scorpio (I don't take astrology seriously enough to say that I believe in it, but I do read my horoscope everyday to see what it says). I'm a HUGE lover of music. All kinds of music...Opera, Classical, Blues, R&B, Hip-hop, Pop, Punk, Rock, Heavy Metal - EVERYTHING! But I do like this one group a bit more than all the others and that group is...O-Town. I love their new sound, and them.
I don't really have a favourite member of the group, but I do favour Trevor a bit more than anybody else. I adore them almost equally. I live in Ontario, Canada (in the nation's capital...Ottawa!!). I love Canada, but I can't wait to see the rest of the world. I plan on someday seeing the most exotic, and interesting places in the world (ex: Japan, Africa, France, London, Scotland, Ireland, Mexico, Cuba, Dominion of Republic, Bahamas, Virgin Islands, etc...).
In the future I plan on opening up my own Bed & Breakfast or something on that line of the B&B and being an author of short stories that would be published or something like that. I love serving people, making people happy, cooking, managing, and cleaning. I know that sounds a bit weird to you, but I enjoy it. Maybe its because I've grown up with a mother who was a chamber maid, but I do, so deal with it. j/k. In my future stories I will add some of things that happened in my life, and things that I would like to happen to me. I'll tell you what those things are on the way...or will I?
I love meeting new people, making new and interesting friends. I love traveling (as you read before).
There's one another thing that I haven't told you about myself. I have a half-sister. I don't know where she is, or if she's still alive (pray to God that she is). I plan on meeting her in the future and whatever family she has. All my life, all I've wanted is to be an aunt to my brother or sister, but I don't have any siblings so that is nearly impossible for me to have. You might see that in my stories as well, because its very dear to my heart.
Well, I think I've bored you with enough details about me, I'll let you get on with my stories. Enjoy them, and remember...R&R.

Oh yeah, for those who have MSN Messenger you can add me if you want. Just don't do it so you can harass me or tell me that I suck, or worse that my stories SUCK! I wouldn't do that to you, so please, don't do that to me. Thanks, take care
