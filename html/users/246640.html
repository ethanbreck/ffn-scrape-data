Author has written 10 stories for West Wing, Bring it On, and Arrow.

I've been in various fandoms now for over 15 years, and all of my stories are scatted across the web under various names. I have fic here, at Ao3 (also redtoes), on a couple of different LJ accounts and in various Buffy archives most of which have gone the way of the dodo. I'm mostly found these days on tumblr but I don't post fic there.

I am an aspiring novelist, and one of these days there will be a link here to ebooks, but not yet. Someday soon, though.

I was at one point a semi-professional screenwriting but that's several years in the past. I'm still a movie geek though, through and through.
