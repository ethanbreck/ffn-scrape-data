I've been reading FanFiction for about 20 years. Early on I thought I'd write some stories of my own, but I've found that I lack both the inspiration and the inclination to follow through. Never say never I guess, but for now and the foreseeable future, I am content to continue simply as a reader and consumer and appreciate the amazing works that talented and dedicated fanfic authors put out. I may stray from reading avidly, but I always come back to FanFiction. I'm a terrible reader, in that I haven't written nearly enough reviews, but I never feel I will be able to articulate exactly how I feel about a particular story, and I worry that I'll sound dumb, or pretentious, or both. I'm working on amending that because writers deserve to know just how much they can move a reader.
