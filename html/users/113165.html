The Wolves of Jorrvaskr:

Now and then someone asks me, 'Is this story linked at all to The Amulet/The Rescue/The Wolf/etc.?' The answer to that is, yes.

The Wolves of Jorrvaskr is an ongoing series of Skyrim stories I've been working on, that can be read separately as stand-alone pieces, or if you wish, together to create a larger piece of fiction with an overarching storyline.

Focusing on The Companions, Vilkas, Farkas and newly appointed Harbinger, the stories explore life at Jorrvaskr, their ongoing ties to Hircine, and the developing relationship between The Harbinger and Vilkas.

If you wish to read the stories in order, they go as follows:

The Amulet.

The Rescue.

The Wolf.

Thank you to everyone who has left comments, liked, favourited, critiqued. Your excitement and curiosity regarding these stories help fuel me :)

Follow me on tumblr for story snippets, updates and other things.

28th May 2015: I recently gave birth to my little one and therefore stories are on hold for a moment whilst I get past being deprived of sleep ;) Thank you for your patience and sticking with me :)
