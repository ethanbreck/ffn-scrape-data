Hello All!

This is just a bit of info about myself. My name's Sara. I'm 17 and I'm a senior in a rural high school somewhere near Pittsburgh, PA.

I absolutely love anything having to do with horror, sci-fi, the supernatural, and comedy. Slash fics aren't really anything I'd like to read. There's something just wrong with that kind of stuff.

I'm a huge fan of The Witchblade, Friday the 13th (old TV series), Charmed, Harry Potter, Stephen King, Dean Koontz, Thomas Harris, Michael Crichton, Dick Francis, Lilian Jackson Braun, Edgar Allen Poe, Anne Rice, and Frank Peretti. Hannibal (T.Harris) is probably my favorite book. Jeepers Creepers could be my favorite horror movie, but I find it too amusing.

Anywho, you're welcome to read my story. It's probably not that great because it's just my little hobby. Just so you know, I skip around a lot, add/take away multiple characters, and add a lot of detail. Oh, and I know I haven't updated in a long while (I've got around 20 chapters already done), but I've given up on posting for now because of too few reviews. And the rating is for language, violence, and gore. Although, I might have to up it to R. Not quite sure. Any insight on the subject? I'm always open to constructive criticism. On the other hand, I can't take flaming, so please don't do it. It's really annoying.

If anyone wants to talk to me, my email address and AIM name are there. I talk to almost everyone.

Many Thanks,

~Moony Smith
