12.21.04 - notice of voluntary account freeze

Important Notice! Due to repeated questionable archiving practices, I will no longer be updating this account. Please see my literary journal for the latest archiving information (and most recent fics).

All right, on with the rest of the bio. ^_^ I've been writing for . . . a lot of years, now. Most of that will probably never see the light of day, for reasons as varied as the stories themselves. Such is life. Current obsessions: Stargate SG-1, GetBackers, and Fruits Basket . . . though I have been known to dabble in other fandoms as well. Sometimes too many others. _ You can keep tabs on the latest in my fandom obsessions and writings through my literary journal, Monochromatic.

Okay, now this next bit is Really IMPORTANT. So much so that I'm putting it here AND in the author's notes of the next several chapters I upload AND on Monochromatic. I love feedback, I'm thrilled to hear someone loved something I wrote and wants to see more. If I've screwed with continuity without tagging it as AU, let me know, please. If you think I'm an uncultured hack ... well, actually, you can keep that bit to yourself. But what I cannot stand, what I really abhor, are whiney bitchings about how I don't update Fic X anymore or haven't written for Fandom Y in weeks/months. All such whinings push your beloved-but-"abandoned" project down to the bottom of the queue. Gaurenteed. I publish on my schedule, not yours. I do solicit feedback, I do want to know what people like, but in the end, the arrival of any promised fic listed below is COMPLETELY at MY WHIM. Thank you.

* Story Key:
* In Storyboards: Means there's just a bare idea and a kernal of a plot
* Projected: Plotted out, but no formal writing; may have scenes scribbled
* Pre-Composite: Some scenes written, but on hiatus; only applies to compilation-type projects
* In Progress: Started and active
* Pending/Stalled: Started, but deactivated, pending space in rotation or other inspiration (fics are shifted to Pending first, then Stalled when out of rotation for more than a month)
* Stasis: Drafted, but waiting for rewrites
* Redraft: Everything from premise to writing is being completely overhauled
* Mothballed: Deactivated; will not be re-upped until status changed back to "stalled," so please don't inquire after these.
