Author has written 15 stories for Star Wars, Pokémon, Signs, Xena: Warrior Princess, Lord of the Rings, Power Rangers, Degrassi, Troy, and Robin Hood BBC.

I strongly believe that a story never ends. In the case of fan fiction, the story lives on in the hearts and minds of the fans whom it touches. It's up to us to continue the work of the artists who gave us these things to believe in. Let us not not ruin their work, but improve upon it by fulfilling our duty to expand on the story and make it our own. I wish all my fellow writers a warm welcome to my simple profile. The names Andre. That's me right there on the left. My pen name was once aznneggroepnoi, but I have changed it to Crimson Knight as a way to indicate my growth as a writer. I am a man of little utterance, but armed with a pen, I have within my grasp, an arsenal of vocabulary that is sure to stir emotion to those with pure imagination. Come join me in my crusade. I promise you, it will be worthwhile. The time has come for you to unleash your imagination!
