Been a long time since I updated this, so here it goes.

Hi. I'm an author. I write stories.

... That's pretty much all you really need to know about me, isn't it? Wait, I should put information about my stories here?

Okay, okay, fine.

My current project a Digimon Savers AU called Broken Dreams, taking place ten years after Digimon Savers, and is in fact an all seasons "Crossover". I say that with quotes because all the other season cast members are just "Savers-verse" versions of themselves, not the ones from thier actual series. It also focuses on the younger cast members of Savers, and it's main protagonists are extremely minor characters in canon.

Seriously, they're Daimon Chika, Relena Norstein, and Noguchi Yuka. I couldn't have found a more "minor-in-canon" cast to expand if I tried.

Everything written before Broken Dreams is either a oneshot or a Dead fic.

You guys can stop favoriting and begging for updates to "Harry Potter and the Digital Plane" now. It's not going to happen.

I have a few other fic ideas in the works, but you're probably not going to see most of them for a long time. Anything that's not as massive as Broken Dreams isn't going to be posted until I have the whole fic done, and the one as massive as Broken Dreams or longer is going to be at least 25 chapters in before I even considering starting to post.

But hey, at least you know that the next new story I upload you'll actually get to see the end of it.
