On Hiatus

All my fics will stay up, but they will not change. I still welcome any reviews, good, bad, and both, and I will strive to reply, but I won't be updating or posting new fics for awhile. Thanks for reading!

xxxx

Hello.

I love "The Outsiders" by S.E. Hinton, love writing, love this site...in fact I love you all! I dislike spelling errosr. I'm a crazy and "tormented" writer, but what can you expect from an artiste?

If you're not annoying somebody, you're not really alive.

- Margaret Atwood

This is the creed by which I live, and also, sometimes, by which I write. Still, I appreciate constructive criticisms on all my stuffs, including harsh criticism. I also appreciate rampant praise.

xxx

I just decided I needed a break from patroling the Outsiders' section of this site. I decided to browse some writer's forums (completely outside of fanfiction) for some plot ideas. The first feed I click into about a plot idea involves Dallas Winston. Yeah. The second, a gang fic involving Tim Shepard. Outside of Hinton, outside of fanfiction. The Outsiders is everywhere - it has infiltrated our collective literary consciousness! I'm still hyperventillating.

- Friday April 20th, 07 at 10 pm

That's enough of my ramblings. Thanks for the public therapy guys.
