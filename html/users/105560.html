Thunder crashes and fire rages in front of cliff that drops down into the bleak darkness of an endless void. Suddenly a tall, lean figure steps from the flames. She is clad in gold and red cloth of the finest quality. Her chestnut hair flips in the wind and appears red when touched by the light. The flowing cascade does nothing to hide her delicatly pointed ears as she slowly opens her violet eyes and gazes at you with wisdom beyond what her young appearence suggests.

"I am Phoenix Storms, Empress of the Phoenix fire birds. All who look upon this void tremble with the fear of Death. Look now, mortals, mayhap you are the ones who will close this rift in time. The endless blackness you see is not ment to be, look to it now and use your hearts to change the cruel nothing into its true form!"

She raises her golden spear and holds it to the sky. As you look into the nothingness of the void you feel Death creeping toward you. Fear begins to rise in your chest, but at the same time, you feel the power of the immortal fire birds and their Empress impowering your courage. You see the void with new eyes. A wonderous forest of green trees and blue flowing rivers streaches out before you as far as the eye can see. Phoenix fly through the sky with herds of Pegasi, and below you, centaurs and unicorn prance in meadows and dash past great trees. You turn back to the Empress of immortality. She smiles at you, a smile that gives light to even the blackest of hearts.

"Well done mortals. Welcome to the land of Fantasy. My domain."
