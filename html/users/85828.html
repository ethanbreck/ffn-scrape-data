Author has written 7 stories for Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Harry Potter, and Digimon.

I've been a member of tihs site for 15 years. That makes my head spin. I lied and said I was 13 (I was 12) so I could sign up. I had no idea I'd still be on here after becoming an adult...but dude. Fanfiction is legit. I love it.

I've written for Digimon, Harry Potter, and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. Digimon's my baby, though; I've been writing stories for Digimon since I started. Alas, those stories do not exist anymore...they were lost on a floppy disk in 2002. I'd like to think my writing's improved a lot since then, but since they don't exist anymore, I can't even be sure...

I like all of you. You guys are great. This has become an amazing community, and I am so proud to be a part of it. This place got me to start writing. I owe so much to it.

Please read review my stories. I like them...maybe you will, too?
