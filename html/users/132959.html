Greetings All!

Let's see, I've been reading and writing fanfic for 10+ years, mostly Gundam Wing but lately a bit of Dragonball Z as well.

General notes on my work: I like to think I have a mix of angst and humor, good fics and bad. Though hopefully the more recent fics are all good :)

In fandoms this old, I've more or less given up on pairing wars, though there are some pairings I like more than others (1x2, 5xR, Gt/T in particular). You won't hear me bash on a character or pairing- if I don't like it I just won't write it.

I write mostly slash, some het, and plenty of gen, and post here very infrequently. For a more complete collection of my recent fics (including the Sorting Hat Arc) check out writing journal.

Also, they pretty much assume you've read Harry Potter, or you're familiar with it, so there will be spoilers and unexplained things and re-written scenes.
