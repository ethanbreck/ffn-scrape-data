Author has written 12 stories for Smallville, Babylon 5, Batman Begins/Dark Knight, and Glee.

I've been writing since I can remember - my first fanfic was Star Wars and I was 7 years old at the time and it included white tigers and some chick with platinum hair! LOL Most of what I write is never published on the web unless I'm really proud of it, and currently the universe I am most proud of writing in is 'Smallville'.

My AU series, 'Downfall', wherein I have changed the mythology by writing that Kal-El was in fact born as Kaela-El, the last surviving daughter of Krypton. I'm fascinated by the changes this creates in the Smallville universe, especially the dynamics between Hudson Clark Kent and Lex Luthor. Could their future animosity be caused by a lover's quarrel? After all, th eopposite of love is hate, and it's easy to see how it could come down to it between these two. Not to mention, it's just really fun to write a female teenager with all of the usual teenage crisis', including being an alien. :)

Hope you enjoy!

~Ae
