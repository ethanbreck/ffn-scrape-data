Author has written 7 stories for Newsies, and Harry Potter.

My haitus has been too long, somewhere between junior year bullshit and senior year stress I forgot all about my most precious fandom. However my love could never die and a fateful night of making TARDIS shaped cakes and deciding to dig out an old, once upon a time beloved movie has rekindled the flames of that love when I thought they were little more than coals gone cold in the grill.

Newsies has been my farthest reaching fandom, spaning the phases of my life and helping me form some of the best friendships I've ever had. I will never forget my first adventures in the Newsies Fandom. How could I? Who could forget Morgan Jacobs or B's amazing MSTs? From the first time Shade Mayer debuted in a newsie fic, running away from her orphanage and dressing up like a boy to join the newsies, I knew I could never really be free again.

Here's to Captain Shorts and the Kezinator. To Duck Hunt and renting space in Hell. To blush, sprace, sputchy, jot, javid and skumlets which sounds kind of contagious.

Dude, Jack totally caught the skumlets. Gross.

It's good to be back.
