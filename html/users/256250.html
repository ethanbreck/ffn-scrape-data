Spelling of Names some people like to spell things differently so I added this just so that people can get used to this one. Yohji, Schuldig, Schwarz, Weiß. That is how I always spell them. Black in German is spelled 'Schwarz' not shwarz or schwartz, and I like to make sure they're right. Same with Weiß although alternate spelling 'Weiss' is ok for those who can't type the 'ß' and Schuldig 'guilty'. As for Yohji, I just like his name that way. 'Youji' would mean it's pronounced wrong, and 'Yoji' just looks weird to me.
