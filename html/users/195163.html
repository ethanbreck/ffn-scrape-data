My favorite TV shows include but are not limited to: The Pretender, Stargate SG-1, Charmed, Angel, Torchwood, Doctor Who, and Battlestar Galatica. I have numerous ships but the ones I write about the most are Jarod/Miss Parker (The Pretender), Obi-Wan/Padme (Star Wars), and Harry/Ginny (Harry Potter)

My favorite books are The Chronicles of Narnia by C.S. Lewis, The Last Vampire by Christopher Pike, Mists of Avalon by Marion Zimmer Bradley, The Firebrand by Marion Zimmer Bradley, The Werecat Series by Rachel Vincet, and The Twilight Saga by Stephanie Meyers.

If you like any of my fan fiction, you can check out some of my original pieces at fictionpress.com at this address: http://www.fictionpress.com/~LadySarahJane. There's isn't much there now and I don't know if that's going to improve any.

Most of my fan fiction is now posted at LiveJournal. My epics (which are few and far between) will be posted here but for shorter pieces and drabbles, please see my livejournal
