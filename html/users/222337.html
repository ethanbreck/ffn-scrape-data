Don't wish upon a falling star. You fell so far. You should have been a star.

(from "Falling Star" by Jet--my current Artemis Fowl/Holly Short theme song)

What "Caravelight"is.

My old pen name was Luna Project. However, I think the name was overused. So I changed it. "Caravelight" is a combination of the words "caravelle" and "light". The boat of light, that is.

Who Am I.

I'm a girl, Aquarian, 27 years old. I'm a freelance translator, starting my post-graduate study. An all-time dreamer and a stubborn real-life fighter.

Why I Write.

Simple. I love it. And I have no better things to do in my spare time...No. Just joking. I've told you, I'm a Harry Potter addict. I have been writing fanfics since I read the Harry Potter novels. I guess it's because I see many dimensions in stories I read (well, not necessarily Harry Potter) that I can dig more and explore (the characters' point of views, the possibilities of their "lives" in the future, and so on). It will be interesting to have our fanon minds transformed into written words.

Who Prompted Me To Publish My First Fanfic

Weasleylover1, a fanfic writer with whom I corresponded back in 2003. Thank you very much, Jenny.

What I Want to Say About My Writings

DO NOT filch my fanfics. APPRECIATE OTHERS' HARDWORKS. Simple though my fanfics are, they came out from my brain.

Concerning My Favorites

Harry Potter Characters: Snape

Harry Potter Pairings: Harry/Ginny, Ron/Hermione

Harry Potter Book: The Deathly Hallows. Three thumbs up for Madam Rowling!

Harry Potter Movie: Prisoner of Azkaban.

Lord of the Rings Characters: The Four Hobbits and The Gondorian Brothers.

Lord of the Rings Book: The Return of the King.

Lord of the Rings Movie: The Fellowship of the Ring. Cate Blanchett was enthralling as Galadriel. And Boromir was there.

Lord of the Rings Pairing: Eowyn/Faramir.

Inheritance Cycle Pairing: Eragon/Arya, Murtagh/Nasuada

Artemis Fowl Book: The Opal Deception.

Artemis Fowl Pairing: Artemis/Minerva, Artemis/Holly.

Fanfics: Angst, drama, humor, ocassionaly fluff. I HATE slash.

Concerning My Fanfics

~A Lone Voice of Truth~

"A Lone..." is a very simple poem based on Harry's ordeals "Harry Potter and The Order of the Phoenix". I think the lines are not well-written, but it was my first attempt at any fics.

~Icy~

"Icy"...I wrote it because I like Mortal Kombat, especially the early parts of it. The most intriguing character in the story (for me) is Sub-Zero. I'm not a gamer, so I was afraid to publish it. But I like thecharacter. My mind is a confusing machine, I must admit.

~Nothing But A Mirror~

Well, the mirror is something! Rowling said so. Ha ha ha... But I didn't know it by the time I wrote the poem, hence the title. I want to know what the mirror might "say" when Harry threw it down his trunk in "The Order of the Phoenix". The idea was very amusing for me, so I wrote the poem.

~Rotten in The City~

I was a great fan of Detective Conan. And this minor character, Junpei Matsuda, appears briefly in one of the manga volumes as a tough loner who thinks that his life is hell--"Rotten in the City". And sadly--plus, ironically--he ends up dead when he tries to save the city he lives in. The inspiration is from Red Hot Chili Peppers'"Under the Bridge".

~Sinking~

Sirius Black's death shocked Harry, as well as Harry Potter fans. I was one of them. "Sinking" depicts Harry's loneliness when he stared at the lake and thought of how Sirius was once there, trying to fend off a hundred Dementors. People waved at him merrilybut allHarry did was grieving.

~A Time To Reconcile~

The main character in "A Time..." is Percy Weasley, and this story is about how he seeks apology from his family and come to 12 Grimmauld Place. Percy is scared of how his family will react.I like the cliffhanger. However, it turns to be an AU after the sixth book in the Harry Potter series is released.

~Decision: To Rebel~

I wrote "Decision..." based on Weasleylover1's fanfic trilogy. I like how she portrays Draco Malfoy there. In this poem, Draco rebels against his father's tiranny--after he finds very foul things that Lucius has been concealing for years. It is highly AU now.

~Let the Threads be Intertwined~

This is how I portray a certain event that might take place before the calamitous Spinner's End. I like the Black sisters (well, Narcissa and Bellatrix), despite their cockiness...ha ha. The title contains the words "threads" and "intertwined" because I relate the story with the word "spinner"--which for mesynonymous with"spider". I have a feeling that "spinner" here might be Bellatrix instead of Snape, because Bellatrix is a spider species, and because Bellatrix star is part of a spider-shaped constellation.

~Divergence~

This poem was inspired by a certain thread in a forum I join. There was a Peruvian guy named Coki who brought up the topic about Draco and Harry's similarities and differences based on Peruvian mythology. And there was an editorial by Daniela about mirrors in Potterverse, and by Chris about Draco Malfoy's personality. I think they're right: Draco and Harry are different but are the same (like two sides of a coin.)

~The Rubicon: An Adagio~

The title is too grand, but well... I love it. My first long fic (umh... only three chapters long, in fact). It's about Draco Malfoy, crossing the rubicon (hence the title). I want him to grow up as an independent wizard, no longer counting on his family name, his admired teacher and all that. In short, I want to see him as a redeemed young man, but still retains his characteristics... not fading into the woodwork behind Harry & co.
