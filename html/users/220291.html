2014 edit*

I'm leaving the 2012 update on here (below this) because most of that still holds true--with the exception of one story so far. Alternating Current (Fairy Tail) is "in-progress". I can make no promises on the speed of chapters, except for that there will be a couple more. I was kicking around on the Fairy Tail page and found that the kind of stories I like to read really haven't been posted there much yet, at least not that I had noticed (it would not surprise me if I simply missed them.) So instead of sitting around waiting for contribution, I thought I would contribute.

2012 edit*

I figured I'd stop back by and edit this old profile page! Thank you to everyone for reading what I've got on this account. I'm having that horrible problem of I look back at what I've written (especially the things from very early on) and hate it, but I think everyone does that at some point. At this point, I will probably not write anything more on here (unless the muse strikes as it sometimes still does.) EDIT: She struck, read the 2014 edit above for more details!

There's a certain feeling of insanity when you look back and realize all the growth that's happened within yourself and writing style in the last 10 years or so! There are some things that I absolutely know are stupid that I've written, but they will stay--for a self reminder to never ever do that again and just incase someone likes sugar fueled brain-storms (good for you, I applaud your sense of humor. I wish I still enjoyed everything like that!)

As for any sort of personal details, I'm a college horn player now. (When I started this account I could hardly play! Lots of things happen in 10 years...) I like to read and review stories more than write now because that's what I enjoy more at the moment. If anything pops up on here, it'll most likely be over the northern hemisphere summer months because that's when I have more free time to be writing creative instead of playing gigs creative.

So once again I thank you, nameless readers, and stay safe out there.
