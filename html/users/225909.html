I recently regained access to this account (having lost all email and login information ages ago), and I fully intend on completing Lacuna. Chapter 15 is nearly ready to go. Thank you to everybody who has continued to review my stories, particularly the Darkness trilogy! 12/17/2012

My stories:
Juxtapose: This is an AU post-Hogwarts fic. Pairings include Harry/Draco, James/Sirius, James/Lily, Lucius/Narcissa, Draco/OC, Narcissa/OC. Rating will be R for a reason, possibly NC-17 at a later date. Any smuttier parts of the fic will be posted to my fics LiveJournal. This story is currently on hold. My beta for the Darkness trilogy has expressed interest in working with me on this one, and I have to say that that sounds fantabulous as she is a great beta. Sooo, I will wait until we are done with Lacuna before I inundate her with yet another fic.

Bound and Broken: This is a WIP. Seamus/Harry/Draco I'll work on it as I feel like. The prologue and first chapter are up right now. I don't know when the next one will be up.

The Wrong Moment - Harry/Draco humor fic. I haven't worked on this one in ages, because I've been concentrating on my trilogy. I hope to get another chapter posted someday, though I can never say when I will. Sorry! But at the moment, I don't think I'll ever just dump this story.

Let the Darkness Take You - First fic in Darkness Trilogy. COMPLETED. Summary: Harry returns to his sixth year at Hogwarts to find that darkness is very quickly permeating the school. It seems as though the only person to realise this, is Harry. When all seems lost, two people come to him and offer their help. Severus Snape and Draco Malfoy. As the three work together to unravel the mystery that is hovering over them, they each have to fight their own battles and overcome the darkness that is threatening to take them.

Remember You - Sequel to Let the Darkness Take You. COMPLETED. Harry/Draco Summary: Harry is given a mysterious gift for his birthday that gives him new happiness. But if it is so great, then why does Dumbledore feel he needs to hide everything that it is from Harry?

Lacuna: Sequel to Remember You, the third and final fic to the Darkness Trilogy. IN PROGRESS - TO BE FINISHED EARLY 2013. Harry/Draco. Summary:When Harry finds that those closest to him are hiding something, he begins a search for his own answers and unwittingly throws himself into a criminal investigation. Only then does Harry come to understand what happenedfour years ago.

For more info concerning this particular fic, please feel free to join my Yahoo! group. The link is provided above. Otherwise, I do update sporadically in my LiveJournal about my fic. However, I tend to friends-lock most of my posts as they concern real life as well. So, if you have a LiveJournal, you may friend me! The link to my LiveJournal is also posted above as my Homepage.

IMPORTANT: I do not email readers when I am updating any of my fics! Why? Because it would take FOREVER and my sanity would be at stake if I did email everybody! (-: That is what the Yahoo! group is for.

Changing Face - I hate this story now. I acquired a beta for it, who is going to beta the first chapters of it... but now I'm not so sure I want to put that much effort into it. I don't like writing het anymore. Argh. I'm going to try and finish it. I just don't know when. CF is no longer a priority.
