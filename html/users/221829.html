Hi, my name's Diana. Sometimes I write fanfic.

As you can see, I've been on this site for a good long while! I'm afraid I've taken down a lot of things that I felt were either too derivative or just not worthy of publication from those early days, so if you came here looking for something you read years ago, I apologize. The remaining stories from my first couple years on this website are mostly still here due to nostalgia and shouldn't really be used to judge my skill as a writer. Unfortunately, everything from before 2012 should be considered abandoned, though I'm making a very real effort to not leave anything unfinished from here on out!

Currently, most of my energy is going to the Brother's Blood 'verse, my ongoing collaboration with Thaelia15. If I ever end up finishing anything else, it'll most likely be some form of Star Wars fanfic...
