Grim: In case you were all wondering, I'm Grim the Duskull. ShadowedTactician's new muse. This is currently the new pen-name that my... er... owner has adopted, while he's drafting, writing (and will hopefully be completing in the future) his Fire Emblem (VII) crossover fanfiction. So, in the meantime, here he is with his explanation behind the story...

When I started out writing fanfiction, one of my firsts was a crossover between Samurai Jack and Pokemon which I eventually gave up writing due to its abysmal quality and lack of ideas. During the time I had worked on it, however, I began to have thoughts about creating Pokemon cards for Jack, similar to those in the Gym Heroes and Gym Challenge expansion sets in the Pokemon Trading Card Game. Later, the idea soon gave way to plans for projects, where I would create Pokemon cards for characters in cartoons or games I liked, assigning them suitable monsters that fitted their background, character and so on. The Fire Emblem Expansion Series is my third project featuring all 43 usable characters and some major bosses - and it's so big, I've had to divide it into five expansion sets. It's gonna take me years to do. Call me a maniac with an Elfing load of time, but hey - it's just a hobby.

Above, by the way, is a link to the official website of my second card project, Kardz Next Door (expansion set for -you guessed it - Codename: Kids Next Door). Construction for theFire Emblem Expansion Series will begin as soon as I finish completing the Trainer cards... and some niggling homework assignments.
