Hello, random people and surfers and others...

Name's Miss Anne Thropy and I'm a fanfic writer. I tend to stick to one fandom and then move on, so you can say that I'm sort of a serial monogamist. I've written for Fruits Basket, Supernatural, X-men, Harry Potter, LOTR, Marvel Universe, The Hobbit, Kingsman: The Secret Service, and The Mighty Boosh. I write RPS also, but that's somewhere else.

Anyways, that's the fanfiction CV...real life CV is as follows in the point form format. Simply because I can! HAH!

-30 something professional, have been working all over the world and am currently in Northern Quebec.

-Living in Quebec for the first time since 2010. It's still the same.

-I have been pretty prolific in a whole lot of fandoms and have many things in AO3.

-Hockey is my passion and I love the Edmonton Oilers, Buffalo Sabres, Montreal Canadiens, The Pittsburgh Penguins and the Washington Capitals.

-I also love collecting action figures and am currently collecting Funko figurines of my favourite series.

-I love reading historical romances and novels and also history books. I love fiction and historical facts.

-I'm picky about my music, but I've been discovering that if I keep an open mind, I can discover little gems that make me smile, that's for certain.

-I draw what I would like to see (Thx Manics for that great line) and write what I'm feeling, what I see and what I know, which is a good way to do it.

-Live life as well as you can, but also live it for yourself and know what your limits are before you burn out and cannot give anything to anyone.

-One day at a time is all you can handle in life sometimes.

-Did I mention Iker Casillas,Jonas Hiller, Henrik Lundqvist, Simeon Varlanov, Marc-Andre Fleury, and Carey Price are my fav goalies ever? No, now you have heard of them.

And that's all. Enjoy the randomness of my scribbles here and there shall be more coming.
