I'm 20. I'm in the 2's! I think the blend of encroaching adulthood, hormones and cynicism is starting to make me mad as a hatter.

Ithaca, NY. Long Island, NY. Don't know which is worse, stuck in the rural pit of neverneverland or the glacial deposit of malls, movie theatres, and Blockbusters. Luckily, I'm in England now. Thank karma.

Let's see, minor little things i think are important to know about me...

Favourite Artist: art-wise...van gogh, though i'm partial to 'the scream' by munch. it's so...primal? music-wise, i'm quite eclectic: rock, alternative, folk, jazz, techno...no rap though. Always in my CD player: Fiona Apple, Garbage, Sarah McLachlan, Tori Amos, or Bjork.

Something i'm proud of: have never broken a bone. doesn't sound like much i know, but if you only knew how many times i've fallen down stairs or gotten hit by cars. one of the many reasons why i'm scared of heights and refuse to learn how to drive.
