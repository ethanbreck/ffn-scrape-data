Author has written 5 stories for Slam Dunk, and Card Captor Sakura.

I'm already 21 and I feel old. I know a lot of the writers here are younger than me. I miss the days when I was part of the younger ones, 'cause it kinda gave me the excuse to suck at my writing. But now, I find much younger writers who are so so great at what they are doing. (Dearies, 14 year olds shouldn't know that much about love. haha! Though at that age, I remember I felt so grown-up too, I was even writing lemons at a younger age even. Teehee)

I have far too many favorite animes, but my current addiction is Nodame Cantabile. (Tomoko-sensei, please update the manga already! Your maternity leave is long enough. September was too long ago!)

I have the biggest crush on Chiaki. And because of his character, I simply fell in love with Hiroshi Tamaki too. (live-action actor). It's even a bigger plus that Seki Tomokazu is one of my favorite seiyuus. Because of Chiaki, I've realized that my taste in men purely consists of "socially-inadequate" guys: those who are often misunderstood, those who are sometimes hated for their attitude but are only poor at expressing their feelings, and those who have a perpetual frown on their faces. It makes their smiles so much sweeter. And it makes it more special if you get through them. (I feel I was brain-washed when I fell in love with Fujimiya Aya/Ran at a tender age)

My favorite pairings are:

Eriol x Tomoyo (Card Captor Sakura)

Sakuragi x Rukawa (Slam Dunk)

Tokiya x Fuuko (Flame of Recca)

Tatsuha x Ryuichi (Gravitation)

Eiri x Shuichi (Gravitation)

Touya x Yukito (Card Captor Sakura)

Athrun x Cagalli (Gundam Seed)

Roy x Riza (Full Metal Alchemist)

Mwu x Murrue (Gundam Seed)

I have too many other favorites but they are the only pairings I mostly read here in ff.net. They are either non-canon or I feel too frustrated on how they ended up in their respective series. (In Eiri and Shuichi's case, the manga was just so much better for me, Yuki was a lot sweeter gush) When I'm too fond of the pairing and I love how they turned out in the anime, I feel satisfied enough and I want to preserve the memory that I don't read much about them.

And I do love Stewart Gilligan Griffin of Family Guy that's why he's my avatar. But I am a she, by the way...
