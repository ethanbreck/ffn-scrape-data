Author has written 9 stories for Harry Potter, Matrix, Doctor Who, and Glee.

Hi! I’m PurpleStar1985, previously Angel Of Darkness 4585. I'm 27, living in England.

I have been writing fanfiction for a number of years now, and have been a member of this site for 10 years. The first fanfictions I wrote were Friends fics, and were mostly in script format - I never posted them.

The first slash fanfic I read was a Harry Potter one, the pairing Harry/Draco. I don't remember the name of it (it involved a bath if I am not mistaken) but I was hooked from the outset. As time has gone on, I have found myself slowly reading other slash pairings. My 2 OTPs are Drarry and Snarry.

I then found myself getting into the Matrix films and fanfiction, and started writing that too. OTP has always been and will always be Neo/Trinity.

I love the Tennant era of Doctor Who - OTP is Ten/Donna.

I have a love/hate relationship with Glee, but decided to try writing some fic. OTP is Kurt/Blaine.

I also wrote some RPF for a band I was once into, some smutty, and a couple with myself shamelessly inserted - such a Mary Sue.

The fic I am most proud of is 'Don't Leave Me', and I am currently trying to write the prequel/sequel, but it has been ongoing for some time, then got scrapped completely and I began again a few months back. I'm also pleased with how my 2 Matrix fics turned out, and my fluffy Doctor Who fic as well.

Not so happy with a couple of my earlier HP pieces, but will always keep them published, cringeworthy as I find them!

Well, I think that's me covered - feel free to read and review my work (always gratefully received) and check out my favourite authors and stories.

Thanks for reading!
