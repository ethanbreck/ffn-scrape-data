Hi! I'm redfblued.

There are a few things you should know about me:

The first thing is: I don't start to post long stories until they are COMPLETELY written out. Why? I have a tendency to stop writing and abandon stories. I've done it several times. It's really bad. So any multiple chapter stories (excluding any short story collections) that I post on this site are already finished from beginning to end. If it is taking me a little to update a story, I like to draw out emotions from fans and give people cliffhangers so that they're psyched for the next chapter of the story. If you hate it when storytellers do that...sorry! The farthest I'll stall for excitement is two weeks but even then I'm going to be antsy about getting it out to readers so I won't do it very often.

Second: I write slowly. Sometimes it takes me SEVERAL months to finish even one chapter due to life, college, and that cursed being known as writer's block. I've actually been writing an original story for several years now since I put it down and pick it up constantly. So any fanfiction I post onto here could have taken me months or even over a year to complete. Please be kind to them.

Third: I write what I'm passionate about (hence why I keep putting down my original story all the time) and my passions tend to change so at this point in time I have an obsession over DC Comics speedsters. I. Love. Speedsters. Right now, I'm working on a few stories and have a few story ideas that all deal with speedsters (The Flash, Kid Flash, Impulse, The Tornado Twins, etc.) in some way or fashion. Favorite speedster: Wally West (1st Kid Flash, 3rd Flash). I grew up only knowing Wally as the Flash; not Barry or Jay (gives you a hint on how old I may be eh?). Second favorite speedster: Bart Allen (1st Impulse, 2nd Kid Flash, 4th Flash). I just think he's adorable.

Fourth: Beware! I love tugging at emotions. If I can get a reader to squeeze a few tears out (whether it be out of laughing, happiness, or sadness) or feel a powerful emotion for a character or situation, I've done my job. I will be ruthless when it comes to characters in order to get any type of emotion out of a reader. This is your one and only warning!

Fifth: I do NOT do any slash (guy/guy, girl/girl). If you like those pairings, then you like those pairings and that's fine with me. I just don't write any of it.

Abbreviations that I use:

OOC = out of character (it happens)

A/N = Author Note

AU = Alternate Universe

Fufic = This fanfiction is based in the future not the present time
