07/14/19

Oh, this is hilarious. I actually forgot how to log in on this site since it's been so long. So, Before a Live Studio Audience is on hiatus. As you can see. So, I'm trying to write again! I just uploaded a short smut fic for FF8 because I will always be able to write smut for that fandom. I have a ton that just too short to be considered fics at all. And so, I say, "Be gone, fics! Be gone!" I am starting to upload these fics, give them some air, if you will, because they have been stinking up my hard drive for YEARS. I hope you enjoy them, or at least get a laugh out of some of them. I mean, some of these are, literally, about ten years old and it shows. That's hilarious. Fics that are part of the Fic Amnesty are labeled with "Fic Amnesty" before the actual title. If you enjoy any of them or have a comment, question, or suggestion, please drop me a message or send a review. I would love to hear what people have to say about the skeletons in my closet. (_)
