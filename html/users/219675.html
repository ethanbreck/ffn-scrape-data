“The average life is full of near misses and absolute hits, of great loves and small disasters. It’s made up of banana milkshakes, loft insulation and random shoes. It’s dead ordinary, and truly, truly amazing.” - Torchwood, Random Shoes.

So Hi *waves nervously*

It's really nice to meet you, thanks for stopping by.

So...this is awkward isn't it. I suppose I should tell you a little bit about me.

I love to write as you can tell, almost as much as I love to read.

My main OTP is Chloe/Ollie of Smallville but I'll also pair Chloe with pretty much anyone I can make believable.

I've also just recently gotten interested in Puckleberry (Rachel/Puck) of Glee and hope to write much more for that fandom.

Um...I really really try to respond to reviews. I know you guys took the time out to let me know how much you liked what I wrote so I try to give you the same courtesy to let you know I've seen and appreciate your praise but I'm not always so good with it. Just don't think I don't care, I do, you guys are the reason why I write so if you don't like it, then I've got no reason to post it and even if it's just a few words it really brightens my day!

So...feel free to drop me a line if you have any questions or just want to say Hi!

Much love to everyone who reads my stories!
