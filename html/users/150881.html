I'm not sure who will read this, but I think I should make a few announcements ...

First of all, I apologize. I wrote the majority of these stories a long time ago, and I am unable to continue a lot of them because I am unsatisfied with their quality. That is why you see several of my stories have been revised, and some of them have earlier chapters that do not correspond with later ones -- because I began but did not finish revising them.

Unfortunately, I have neither the persistence nor the time to continue either writing or revising.

What this means for you, the reader, is that my older stories, revised or otherwise, have for the most part come to a standstill. Between my life and the new story ideas I get all the time (all stories I never finish, I assure you, as I have learned from past mistakes and no longer publish anything unfinished).

The length of time between starting these stories and now also means that a lot of my unfinished stories have become invalidated by canon, and need to be revised accordingly.

I have marked all my older stories either "Complete," "Discontinued," or "Hiatus." I am still working on revising a number of my complete stories, but that may well be a fool's errand, and I do not at present plan to post or update anything until it has been completed. As my progress is averaging one word per year per story, and that is putting it generously, it is simply most accurate to state that I will not be updating anything for an indeterminate amount of time (read: forever, minus a few days).

If you are indeed interested, any writing I deem publish-able or that I want to get out is generally posted at my livejournal, linked under my homepage. If you search the writing tag, you should be able to avoid my babble about real life. I still use this account to read, review, and add other author's stories to my favorite and subscriptions, but don't expect much from me by way of updates. If you have any questions, I'm sure you can email me or PM me, and if real life does not get in the way, I will be sure to respond.

Thank you for your time.
