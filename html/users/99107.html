The name? Alyssa.

Sixteen, sophomore in high school.

I've been writing fic for aroud three and a half years. As of now, I'm on a writing hiatus...instead, focusing myself on saving the Mighty Ducks fandom...as well as preventing the SoR fandom from becoming destroyed as well.

Yeah, I'm no Shakespeare, and I don't claim to be perfect, but if I see a problem with your fic, I'll sure as hell tell you about it so you can fix it. And I'd love for somebody to do the same for me. I'm a perfectionist. I want my work to be something that I could hand in to school (not that I would) and not be covered in red marks by my English teacher.

So don't review my stuff in anger when I try and constructively criticize you. It's immature. Something my nine year old brother would do (who, coincidentally, has better spelling, grammatical, AND writing skills than 75 of the people who write here).

So no, I'm not 80 years old. And I don't think I have a stick up my ass. Think I do? Read the Livejournal. It'll change your mind, fast.

When I end my hiatus, I'll probably write for School of Rock, Mighty Ducks (I have a fic in progress there), Harry Potter (H/A all the way!), and POSSIBLY Pearl Harbor. I might have some Meg Cabot thrown in there.

Most of my fics are about two years old, written when I was 12-13. So be forewarned. They're awful and kind of embarassing, but I don't feel like re-writing them...I'm actually quasi proud of them...I've deleted all the early shit that I've done. Crap.

Well. Done with that rant.
