POOF!

For all intents and purposes this account is currently well and truly dead. All stories will be deleted and new ones might or might not replace them. I am only recently recovering from two years of writer's block and my muses are not always the most helpful of creatures when it comes to writing. Sorry to disappoint anyone hoping that I would update anything here. I will eventually be going through and deleting all of the old stories I have posted here, as they are painful to look at and my writing style is drastically different, considering that most of these were written when I was in middle school and I am now twenty-years-old...

And for anyone wondering no you may not take the plots of my stories and add your own ideas to them. I have actually gotten e-mails asking permission to which I must decline. The plots might be dead and the characters revamped but they are still mine. Use your own ideas and come up with your own characters.
