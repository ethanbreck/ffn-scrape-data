hello people! I'm revising my profile. im on the verge of finishing a ton of fanfics that are in the final writing stages. i have about 3 almost done. the first one is a wrestling fic based on the Undertaker from wwe. the second is my first POTC fanfic that i wrote while i was at camp this summer. and the third one is a Disney fanfic. yeah i know, i never thought i would be doing a disney fic either.

oh well!

The Final Ultimate Showdown:

A story about imagination and reality. its disney based, but i assure you it will have a lot of stuff in it that will cover a lot of categories. it can be considered as the ultimate crossover! the main topics i will cover are Disney, Warner Brothers, Universal, new line cinema, 20th century fox, & discovery channel networks, and pretty much anything that i think will make a good plot.

Barbossa's familia:

a simple fanfic based on POTC. what if Barbossa had a wife?

"Darkside's Angel" and "Forgotten Memories":

two fanfics based on WWE's Undertaker.

"Darkside's Angel" is a fic that starts from Taker's childhood after the fire and what really happened at the neighboring funeral home. it then breaks from there to flash forward to just before the ministry era of the WWF/E.

"Forgotten Memories" is a reality spin-off of this based on the people involved behind the gimmicks of "Darkside's Angel" rather than the gimmicks. it involves Deadliest Catch, Dog the Bounty Hunter, Miami/LA/NYC Ink, and American Chopper.

"Old Habits Die Hard": (deadliest catch) The State of Alaska's department of commercial fishing decides to mandate that ALL fishing vessels in the Bering Sea must employ a Registered Nurse Practitioner in order to stay in business. not many captains are happy with the decision, especially the captains of the "Deadliest Catch" boats. The story covers the five main boats: Northwestern, Cornelia Marie, Maverick, Wizard, and Time Bandit. The problem? The nurses that have been chosen are all women. Most of the story will center around the Cornelia Marie. (this idea was spun off by another author WITHOUT MY KNOWLEDGE! so this one will not be posted here unfortunately. details soon to follow.)
