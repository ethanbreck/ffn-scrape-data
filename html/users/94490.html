( 2008.03.01 )

Fics are written whenever -- I'm not exactly the most prolific writer around. This account has been in existence since mid-2001, at which time I was only thirteen. Most (fan)fics I wrote at that time has since been taken down. Mostly because they embarrass me now. I have personal copies though, and, maybe, if I find the time to edit and rewrite them, I'll put them back up.

I started writing again around 2005, thanks in HUGE part to Shikamaru and Temari of Naruto. I have since abandoned that particular fandom, but if inspiration hits, I'm not averse to writing more Shika/Tem. My favorite couple to write for, now, is Shin and Yankumi of Gokusen.

I've also started writing ARASHI RPF lately, but those are not allowed here, so you migth as well check out these other links:

13papernotes.livejournal.com; writing LJ (better to read my fic there, as LJ keeps screwing up my formatting, not to mention everything is posted there first, more or less).
