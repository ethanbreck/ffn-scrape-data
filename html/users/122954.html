Author has written 22 stories for Mighty Ducks, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Power Rangers, Dragon Ball Z, Biker Mice From Mars, Star Wars, Hey Arnold, Misc. Cartoons, So Weird, Digimon, Yu Yu Hakusho, and AcceleRacers.

I've gone by quite a few names and the latest is Wings of Speed and it'll probably be the one to stay, at least for a little while.

My interests come and go and once they go they usually don't come back so if a story hasn't been updated in quite a while (2 years or more) you can pretty much count it being out of the writing loop. I will once in a while revert back to an earlier interest and in which case an uncompleted story may have new life breathed into it, but don't count this to happen to many times.

At the moment, my main interest seems to lie in AcceleRacers, I can thank my little boy for this interest. I love fast cars to begin with and the whole idea behind Hot Wheels AcceleRacers seems to fit into my limits of interests, at least for the moment. That's always subject to change.

Stories that are still in the loop for the moment are as follows...

AcceleRacers: The Truth That Lies Beyond The Lies

Stories that have a chance of being updated at some point...

Power Rangers: Remembrance Is Just A Touch Away (this story is usually updated on the 9/11 anniversary, but didn't make the cut for 2005)

Yu Yu Hakusho: Chaos of the Past

Star Wars New Jedi Order: I'll Be Missing You (I'm seriously going to try and get this story moving once more)

So Weird: All Is Not As It Seems

The rest of the stories are on the back burner and if I ever get back to them is unknown. Maybe. Maybe not. If there's something you'd definitely like to see updated, drop me a line and let me know, I'll get back to you and let you know if it's at all possible or not.

24, March 2008: I was just hit by the worst type of plot bunny, but am currently debating on whether or not to run with it. If I take it and run, I will be completely rewriting the Star Wars Universe as it is known...but that may be for the better as they have decided to kill off one of my favorite characters of all time: Mara Jade Skywalker. Who is to say, that in an alternate universe, that which we know in our universe will transpire? One single event can change things completely and one mistake can completely alter that which we know. What if Anakin Skywalker were not the Chosen One? What if he was not the one foretold of in the prophecy, what if there was another? What if the Chosen One was born light years in the future? What for 'The Chosen One?' coming to websites near you in Summer of 2008, lol.
