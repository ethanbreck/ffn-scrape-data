Author has written 2 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter.

Welcome to my little place in the world to publish my stories.

I am a college student about to graduate next summer (finally!) so updates are always sporadic. I am a history and literature double major so I have alot of reading and writing to do but I will try to update my stories as I get the bug. So let me update my readers on the progress of my two stories which I have just realized I haven't updated since 2003. Oh my!

I am working on both as well as many others. Do not fear. I have not abandoned either.

A Sister's Love is currently being written long hand because I like doing it that way. A couple of chapters have been written with many, many more to come. I have plans to continue the story of Haldir, Eiowiodth, and the others through at least the War of the Rings, and possibly through Aragorn's reign. However, it may be broken up into sequels...in fact I think that is exactly what I am going to do. There is just too much I want to include to do it in one story.

For Facing His Past, I am currently revamping the story. Some things will be changed but the general plot will remain the same. I am hopefully making the chapters longer and more substantial and better in general.

I promise to update soon. I want to thank all my readers and reviewers. Your words make me feel that my stories are worth continuing. Lots of love to all of you.
