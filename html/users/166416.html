Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

I am a rabid Harry Potter fan. One of my friends looks like him. Aren't you jealous? :) Favorite characters are Neville, Ginny, Sirius, the twins and Draco. Least favorite are Lockhart, Voldemort, and Fudge (ha. who likes them anyway?). I also don't like Ron much, in canon anyway. I like a lot of shipping variety in HP fanfiction. Don't read much slash or incest (RonGinny being an exception where I can find it). But I am fiercely loyal to my first and longest ship, DracoGinny. But I am also fairly big on GinnyLee, NevilleGinny, and RonCho. I have indulged in a few DracoHermione, RonHermione, and HarryHermione, because personally, I can see her with any of those three. Just for kicks, I ship MyrtlePeeves. I also immensely liked SiriusFleur until JKR killed off my Sirius! Hmph. *sulks* So yeah. Read, and stuff.
