Wow. It's been a while since I upgraded this, hasn't it? Here's how my summer Vay-K went: I sent the first month of June sitting in my house freezing to death. July comes, and so does band practice (what fun that is) August is red hot as usual. And speaking of "red" Mars is a-coming! I just hope it doesn't get cloudy like it usualy does whenever there's a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see something. And along with Mars (so to speak), Inu-Yasha is back on Adult Swim (12:00 AM) with new episodes. Surprise surprise. You know what? I've just gotten into Ranma 1/2 and I'm so ANXIOUS to see the anime, though it's probably gonna be a disappointment when it comes to voices because I just heard a voice clip of Ranma, and he sounds like a fricken 11-year-old! That drives me crazy! I can't stand it when people screw up perfectly good anime characters' voices! No wonder I like the subtitle version better! So maybe I'll just stick to the manga. But however, I'm on book #4 and no bookstore near my home has it! I'm dying to know what happens! And Borders better get it in soon before somebody buys the rest of the series so that I have to spend all of my money by ordering those damnable manga books that I'm only gonna read at LEAST six times! MAN! People! I swear! You know what? I'm gonna create a bug spray, that'll wipe out ALL of the stupid people on the planet! But then there's the extinction of all human beings to worry about. Hell, what am I worried about? I'd probably do the earth good! Okay, I'm done speaking about what's on my mind. Back to being bored: bored bored bored bored bored bored...
