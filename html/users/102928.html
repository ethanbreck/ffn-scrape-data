Author has written 12 stories for Harry Potter.

Long time writer.

I have been creating HP fanfic since 1998, but I am a notoriously slow writer.

As of this update (July '05) I'm still working on Fate's Manipulation, Destiny's Dilemma, Matter of Relativity, and My Angel...

WIPS that are on the back burner are Pure Magic and The Authoritarian. If you want to help me brainstorm these, please email me at potterblacklupin @ gmail dot com

Finished stories and one shots are Dark Days Ahead, Hello Again, Inspiration,Shared Sadness,Strong Enough and Toys.

Coming soon is Sins of the Fathers, a sequel to Shared Sadness. I hope to post the first chapter of this by August 1.

On a personal level, I'm married to a great guy who is starting to give HP fanfic a try, and between us we have SIX totally insane felines. He wouldn't let me name ANY of them after any of the HP characters. Then again, I'd probably name one MWPP and totally embarrass him!

I'm one of the older HP authors...a thirtysomething I write in a few different fandoms and originally joined FF.net in the early days!

As for reading? I read anything especially long sweeping sagas such as Carpe Diem,stories that make me forget about everything except turning pages. I'm also very drawn to mature James/Lily fics and well crafted 'Harry's parents come back' stories. I'll sail any ship (Het or slash) that is well written :) Email me if you want to check out my stuff in other fandoms. I'll be glad provide my other screen name.

I tend to write somewhat alternate history/reality fics. Check out my first effort Pure Magic which deals with Harry's life as a Muggle. You can find my fics at FictionAlley.org or here, of course :)

Just some random thoughts: I can't get the idea of Pierce Brosnan as James Potter out of my head, though the idea of Russell Crowe has certain appeal as well. I keep seeing Nicole Kidman as Lily. And the even delectable Bryan Ferry as Snape. Is my mind twisted or do you see these too? If anyone wants their story betaed, just drop me a line, I'm glad to help out!

Update:2011

I haven't written in the HP realm for a long time. Unfortunately, Jo lost me around book 5 and never recaptured me. I do still write, both professionally and fanfiction. If you're interested in my current work, please drop me an email! I do hope to finish these WIPS...someday!
