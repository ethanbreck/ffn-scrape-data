To the Readers;

For those of you who haven't already guessed from the extreme lack of updates, Black Legacies is on a semi-perminant hiatus until further notice. In addition to a new job that's escalating to feed me 45+ hours a week, impending loom of the start of design courses, and life in general, I've lost a lot of the will and mindset to work on the story of late. And so, I'm having to set it to the back burner. I could go on about the bulk of reasons behind this, but what it boils down to is this: for all that I'd like to do with this story, I feel like I'm losing a fight with a bull elephant. There's too many mistakes, gaps, and othernonsuch to patch up, as well as the remainder of the story of itself, but I feel like I'm not enjoying it any more.

I'm really, truly sorry. Please do not poke me, because there's not much beyond this to say. Again, thanks for all of the support.

...bah, I sound emo.
