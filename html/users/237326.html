About Me: My name is Christa and I am a full-time student/musician. I love writing but it's hard to get into the right mood. He is an author of a greater caliber than I am, and his motivation in continuing his own works is amazing.

Animals: I have one. His name is Jimmy and he's a mini pincher/terrier who is about 15 years old. He's my snaggle toothed puppy.

Music: I love all kinds; except for screamo. Not my cup of tea. Radiohead and KT Tunstall are my muses.

My newest hobby: I am a trained violinist and I have taken it upon myself to learn the guitar. I began in August and I really enjoy it. I learn something new every day and it's quite a challenge.
