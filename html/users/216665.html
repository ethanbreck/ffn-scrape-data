I READ FANFICTION!

Reading often serves as my stress reliever. Because of this fact, I crave the moments I stumble across the very best quality writers. I relish being a critic and reviewer for their golden fics. Truly, you people are marvelously imaginative! And brillant with your plots, might I add. Granted sometimes I do have a raging urge to smash something when encountering those nasty cliffhangers. Nevertheless, a million thank you's to you all!

Feel free to peruse my favorites. There'll be no stories of my own until my life has reached the point where I can devote enough time to writing again. I loathe leaving stories unfinished, so until I have something of substance to post, I will not do so.

Till then, please, move along. Nothing to see here.

Bye.
