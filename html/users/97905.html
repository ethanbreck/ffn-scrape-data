Author has written 82 stories for Legend of Dragoon, Final Fantasy I-VI, Final Fantasy IX, Final Fantasy X, Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy VII, Final Fantasy XII, Final Fantasy: Tactics, Magna Carta, Lost Odyssey, Persona Series, and Final Fantasy Tactics A2: Grimoire of the Rift.

A bit about myself: I have a bachelor's degree in history, and am obsessed with literature. I like strong women, honourable men, and pulling the rug out from beneath my favourite characters just to see what they will do. I tend to prefer dark themes and painful situations, because nobody ever grew as a person by being happy. Well, very few people, anyway.

My currently active fandoms are Final Fantasy IV, Final Fantasy VI, Final Fantasy Tactics, and Final Fantasy XII.

I dabble in Magna Carta: Tears of Blood, Lost Odyssey, and Shin Megami Tensei. Periodically I also write for Final Fantasy VII, VIII, IX, and X/X-2.

My largest current on-going project is an arc centered around Kain Highwind of Final Fantasy IV, called "Lucis Ante Terminum." The fics which go in that arc are as follows, in chronological order of when they happen in the arc:

In His Footsteps
The Good Fight
Aufer Calorem Noxium
Broken Wings
Every Light Casts A Shadow

There are additional fics in the arc which do not meet the content restrictions imposed by this archive. Those, as well as all of my drabbles and other short works, may be found at my writing journal, either at or at .

I absolutely love constructive criticism, or feedback of any kind (save, of course, for flames.) If you see an error, or if something does not work for you, please do tell me! I'm always looking to improve.
