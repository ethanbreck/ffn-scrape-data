Hey! I'm Sola. Until recently I showed up on here as Pooglepoogle; I used that name when I first created this account, but now I'm beginning to slide back into actually writing 'fic I figured it was time to update my FF.Net account to better correspond with my other usernames.

I happen to be a Honors II Latin student--if anyone needs translations, I'm always willing to help, considering that I love Latin with a passion.

I used to write incredibly bad Neopets fanfiction (well, mostly poetry) but I took it down. My writing ability has since improved (I hope!), but I really don't have the time to seriously take up writing. I'm willing to beta fics, and I'm always willing to give concrit if I think a fic's got promise, but that's about the extent of it for now. Eventually, I might start writing again, but what with my insane courseload, I doubt it's going to be any time soon.

I'm currently obsessed with Harry Potter and Danny Phantom.
