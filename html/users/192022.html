Hey, my name is Alyssa.

I have been a member of the FanFiction community for a while now. Well, an unactive member. But recently I have come back to put a nice tidy package on my works, because I believe that for me not to would be an action of the upmost disregard for my readers.

My first project is Empress Mage. I am re-writing it as well as finishing the story off. I just edited the first chapter, and am currently re-reading the rest of the story so that I may write more for it.

I am not sure how I will go about dealing with my other stories yet.

So everyone knows, I also am a fiction writer and am currently working on my first book, entitled On Swift Wings.

If anyone is interested in giving it a shot- very little of it is up right now and criticism from a general reader would be fantastic. You can search for it on Fictionpress.net, my user name is Alyssa.Luttenberger. I'll have the link up shortly.

Thank you for your time, anyone who read this.
