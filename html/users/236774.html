Author has written 3 stories for Lord of the Rings.

At first, I created this account to post signed reviews, I never thought I'll write a story here one day. My latest work is 'Strange land', hope you like it. I am also working on a longer story but I may not post it for a long while yet. Please be patience. Thanks for all my reviewers! I can keep writing because of your encouragement.

To me, a good story must have a good plot, good flow and be reasonable. Good stories can be both short and long. I love reading action/adventure stories, and a bit of angst/suspense/tragedy elements certainly suit me. So, don't read all my favourite stories non-stop or you'll certainly find yourself in utter despair. My favourite story categories are:

- LotR: focus on Elladan (more towards brothers, like twins and Estel; father/son stories, like twins and Elrond; and friends stories, like twins and Legolas).

- Sherlock Holmes: focus on John Watson (like colleagues, like brothers)

- Batman: focus on Dick as Robin

- X-men: focus on Scott/Cyclops

If you're still reading, below is some background of why I love these fanfict categories:

LotR: I have never heard of LotR before the movie came up (too bad!), I then realised it's a classic tale and started reading the book. Though the movie trilogy had ended, this site here can fill up my desire. I love Tolkien's Elves (not to mention the new Hobbit movie certainly enhances my love of elves). Legolas is one of my favourite characters but I would now read and search for stories that're Elladan-centric.

I've learned Quenya and have given an elvish name to myself. I have an elvish pen name also. Those who understand Quenya should know which month I was born :)

Sherlock Homes: certainly because of the movie and subsequently the BBC TV Sherlock in modern times. I like both Jude Law as well as Martin Freeman as John Watson! Benedict's Sherlock is wonderful too. I'm eager to watch season 3 of both!

Robin/Nightwing: I watched the Batman 60's TV version when I was a child. I like Robin very much, however I couldn't get the comic books here and inevitably, I've lost their storyline soon. I watched all the Batman movies and some animation series. While reading through fanficts, I realise the Robin I like is called Dick Grayson, and he became Nightwing when he grew up. Anyway, I still prefer to read Batman stories with Dick as Robin.

X-men: again, I just saw the movie only (I'm a movie goer!) and I like Scott - being a good leader, serious (not talkative) and maybe the good husband type :P Though I'm not happy there're not many scenes with Scott in X-2 and he even died in X-3!
