About the Author

Most of my personal details are irrelevant. Mid-twenties. Married. Scorpio. Et cetera.

About the Fanfics

I have a few things that I'm a fan of, but now my main fandom used to be Final Fantasy X/X-2. I love Aki/Nao, but I pretty much ship Nao with everyone else too, at least in some small way. Genfic is also love.

I'm totally in love with Shinobu Kaitani's Liar Game right now. I'd do anything for a fandom.

Not all of my fics are on FFN. My home page leads to my fanfic community on Live Journal. Yes, there's smut there. The Archive of Our Own link up top is the most complete collection of my fanfic, and is the home of the 'finalized' versions of everything.

Yeah, I have seen the Liar Game drama, but barring any sort of fourth-wall breaking exercise in which the manga characters react to the drama, all of my fic is based on the manga. For those of you unfamiliar with that it means: Akiyama is even more emotionally repressed, Nao has about 30-40 more IQ points, Fukunaga's awesomeness rises in proportion to her gender ambiguity, and Yokoya has a mouse fetish.

I do one shots because I have hard time committing to series. When I do series, they are purposely kept limited in scope.

My editing skills are lacking. Please forgive them.

I don't beg for reviews, but reviewing, commenting, and favoriting let me know that my work is appreciated. Also when others write more Liar Game fic. Both of those encourage me to write and post more stuff. =P
