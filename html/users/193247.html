Author has written 7 stories for Digimon, Zoids, Inuyasha, Spirited Away, and Diana Wynne Jones.

Wow.

I started writing fanfiction when I was in middle school or so, probably before that but that was also before I realized what fanfiction was. I started college this last fall. I haven't forgotten about any of my fics or about fanfic (I admit I still read it sometimes). I just don't write it like I use to.

Looking back on what I've posted here I'm embarressed and proud of my past writing attempts. I don't think that they're all much good. I hope that I'm a better writer now, but I don't count on it.

I really want to address what's going to happen in Kohaku's Tale because it was my most sucessful fanfic and looking back I am shocked at the number of people who cared so much about it. I'm still surprised when I get an email from ffnet telling me someone reviewed or added it to their favorites. I'm also very flattered.

I want to finish Kohaku's Tale because I think that all of the people who read the story and put up with my writing deserve it. Yes, I haven't updated that story for three years. No, I don't have anything written past the last chapter posted. But I'm going to try and write the end before I turn 20 in October. After that I might post an add-on chapter with a basic outline of what I thought might happen in the sequels. I'll also be happy to answer any questions anyone still has at the end via email.

I have loved being a part of the ffnet community. I think that fanfic does more for aspiring writers than anything else I've found on the internet. I cannot thank the readers and the reviewers of my stories enough. Without all of you I probably would have stopped writing as a hobby and given up one of the great joys in my life. So thank you.
