Iz speshul

My sense of humor is an acquired taste. Before reading any of my stories, be sure you leave your brain and all sense of reasoning at the door. You'll enjoy them better, trust me. If not, I apologize. Why do I write the stories the way I do? I don't know, really. I guess you could say I have a disability that prevents me from taking things too seriously. I'm a clown and I'm not afraid to throw punches. I've been on this site for 8 years and I've seen a lot of changes since then. But that doesn't mean I'm gonna change my style of writing. If I offend anyone with anything I've written, don't expect an apology. You won't get it. I don't mean to upset anyone on purpose (most of the time) but if it happens, it happens. End of story.

Other than that, I guess you could say I'm a sort of laid back kind of geezer. Not that I'm calling myself an actual geezer, mind you. I'm in my 20s, after all. Let's see, what else? I live in Texas, I enjoy music and movies and when I'm not writing fan fiction, I'm goofing around on YouTube.

Oh, and for those of you who don't know and can't tell by now... I'm a guy! A dude! A Y-front! Surprised? No? Then I'll shut up.
