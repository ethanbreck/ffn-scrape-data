My fan-theories

1. Seekers are primarily Vossian (from Vos) I heard it in a fic somewhere, and it stuck.

2. Praxians are distant cousins of seekers, I read somewhere in anchient times a trine of seekers settled in Praxxus, and so doorwinged mechs are distantly (quite!) related to them.

3. Prowl, Bluestreak, and Smokescreen are brothers. I think Prowl is the eldest, with Smokescreen in the middle, while Bluestreak is the youngest of the three.

4. I wanted to have Bumblebee, Hubcap, and Wasp (who never really appeared in G1, but I wanted to throw him into my fiction for the fun of it) related, but I'm not sure .

5. Soundwave's cassettes are either his creations or, well, I know they allready share a gestalt-like bond. I just don't know if it would be right for one person to keep another cooped up in casette "storage" mode all the time... I kind if like the idea the cassetes are his creations. The same with Blaster.

Also, I would like to point out that a lot of these pairing-ships and relations-ships were contrived (thought up) somewhat recently. So some of my older fiction might not have certain people portrayed as related. I'm sorry if anyone gets confused or squicked by the confusion.

I live for reviews. If a story hasn't been updated for a while and you want to see it updated, leave a review and tell me so. If it's a finished story, I probably won't write a sequel. Those tend to sneak up on me without warning. :-)

If there's a fanfic you don't see here and want to read, drop me a line. It might be on my website, or I can send it to you.
