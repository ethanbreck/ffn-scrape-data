About me:

I am a college graduate and a certified veterinary technician. My life's passion has always been to work with animals because I think pets bring out the best of us. When I'm not taking care of animals, I spend my time being a goof ball with friends in real life or online.

I began writing on FanFiction in 2003 when I became a LotR fan. Circumstances and my luck with technology forced me to stop posting the one story I had begun, and I later forgot about it entirely. Fast-forward five years and I dusted off my FF account. I tried to finish my previous story, Take to Flight, but the story has lost its direction and I don't have the time to fully relearn the world that was Tolkein's that I can be true to his writing for a tale of my own creation. The story remains on the site as a memento and to prove to me writing only gets better with practice. I have since picked up the pen again to write about the world of Aion, a Korean MMORPG that my husband and I started playing together. It is a beautiful game that allows for stories of many kinds.

My friends deserve the credit for why I continue to write little stories here and there. They are all bookworms, so it was only fitting I be one as well. Books I have enjoyed are The Forgotten Garden, His Dark Materials trilogy, Lord of the Rings trilogy, Harry Potter series, Hunger Games series, Inheritance series, Warriors, Song of Ice and Fire series, From These Ashes, books by Dan Brown and Terry Pratchet, and The Host. Anime has also been a major influence to some of my writing as well as enjoyable in those stories I've seen. My favorites are Stein's Gate, Wolf's Rain, Kiba, and Princess Tutu. My friends have been encouraging of all the stories I dream up, original or not, and they keep sparking my own imagination whenever they share their stories or ideas.

I am a fairly reasonable person, and I pride myself on three qualities: Open-mindedness, Unending loyalty to my friends, and Always giving the benefit of the doubt. I am open to constructive criticism since that is how writers progress. If you would like, feel free to ask me to read your stories. Any story is worth a try, and advice is always offered.

-Avatar is not my artwork. I purchased it at an anime convention and I sadly do not know the artists name-
