pet peeves: Making the character look like a total twit , Legolas does not carry a mirror and hairspray around all the time , Duo is not a total idiot , he did after all hack the Doc's security system at like age 9. To many times I have gone for what looked like an interesting story only to find the author had neglected to mention it was more out of charater then in. Wow , that is a big pet peeve I guess L

Anything else: Well , just e-mail me and ask , I'm usually great to talk to and no , I'm not always bitchy , just wish people would put it in the summary when it's going to be silly , even just a warning ... LMAO
