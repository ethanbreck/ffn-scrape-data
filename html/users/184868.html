Umm Hi!
Not a posted author as of yet. I have a lot of ideas started but none put up here yet. I'm really busy so I don't want to leave anyone waiting for new chapters. Once I finish my first fic, I'll post it in its entirety all in one shot.
I'm a big Lord of the Rings fan (or freak.. depends on who you talk to) and have recently come to enjoy Harry Potter fanfiction. There isn't really much LOTR fanfic that I haven't read, and all of it seems to be becoming more and more cliched and Mary Sue-ish. Hopefully the premier of the new movie will inspire some new ideas.
If you check out my favorite stories you'll find mostly Harry Potter stuff. I'm still reading a lot of that. There's all different pairings in there from Hermione/Snape, Hermione/Draco, and Hermione/Fred. I am a firm believer that Hermione and Ron will end up together in the books so I read many of those stories but I don't put them on my favorite list because I only use that for incomplete stories. You will also find one LOTR fic that I absolutely LOVE. Updates on it are a little slow and the plot may seem cliched at times but the commentary is hilarious and well written.

I have a million favorite quotes from LOTR, but I don't have time to type them up right now, seeing as they're all from the books.

My all time favorite quote from a Harry Potter fanfic has got to be :
"...you know how easily we Weasleys procreate." - Ron to Hermione from Stranded! by Alcamenes
It's a great fic. You should really check it out. Ron and Hermione stranded in a cabin and Snape carry around contraceptives.. it doesn't get much better than that! lol
