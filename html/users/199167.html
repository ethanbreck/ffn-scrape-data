Hiya readers!

Wow! Someone actually wants to know about me. Where to begin? I’m weird. That’s a start. I like to write, if you haven’t noticed, and of course I love to read as well.

Interests, you ask? Erm...going to the park, worshipping the letter H, socializing with my imaginary friends, cooking (I’m a vegetarian), movies from the 80’s, David Bowie, looking up big words in the dictionary, books, Duct Tape, going to the library, making fun of popular people...yeah. I don’t watch much TV, but I do adore “Mister Rogers’ Neighborhood,” may he rest in peace. As for music, my tastes include an ecclectic mix of practically everything. From Enya to Evanescence, Bowie to the Beatles, Weird Al, They Might Be Giants, 80’s music, and everything in between, I love music. The only thing I don’t like is pop music. *shiver*

I LOVE BOOKS! They’re really swell, and anyone who doesn’t like to read should perish in the fiery pits of the Underworld...erm...never mind. Some of my favorite authors include Amelia Atwater-Rhodes, Terry Pratchett, Charlotte MacLeod, Tamora Pierce, Louis Sachar, Torey Hayden, and Edgar Allen Poe. Books on tape/CD are nice, too, because I’m a loser.

ALL GOOD THINGS COME FROM THE 80’s!!! Hahaha. Movies, music, mullets... If you hadn’t noticed, I’m quite obsessed. Seriously though, some of the best movies came from the 80’s: Labyrinth, Stand By Me, Goonies, The Dark Crystal, Legend, Ferris Bueller’s Day Off, Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory, Ghostbusters...you get the point. For an extended list, see PossessedByTheSquirrels’s bio.

Here are some quotes that I absolutely love, and have nowhere to put so I’m placing them in my bio for right now:

”The worst joke God can play is to make someone an artist, but only a mediocre artist.” -David Bowie

”If shorts are not evil, then I am Lewis and Clark!” -My friend, PossessedByTheSquirrels (don’t ask...)

”...So the odds against him were higher than a vanload of hippies...” -Terry Pratchett, excerpt from “Good Omens”

”There was no light at the end of the tunnel--or if there was, it was an oncoming train.” -Terry Pratchett, excerpt from “Good Omens”

Let’s see, I wanna say hi to all my friends who might be reading this right now, YOU KNOW WHO YOU ARE! *waves like a maniac* LONG LIVE THE LETTER H!!! Yeah, that’s pretty much it. Until next time, this is CrystalDreamer620, signing off.
