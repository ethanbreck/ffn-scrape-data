Author has written 6 stories for Final Fantasy VII.
It seemed like time to update my lookup, since it's been a while since I've changed it, and it really should be more up to date. As can be seen by my works below, I write Final Fantasy VII fanfiction, focusing mostly on the Turks. Currently, my main project is Ouroboros, which is very Reno-centric. I have a few other ideas I'm planning, although I think I will keep them quiet for the moment. Must finish Ouroboros first, before I start anything else. As may be obvious, Reno is a favourite character, as are the other Turks, although I also enjoy reading about Yuffie, Vincent, and Reeve. Favourite pairings include Reno/Yuffie, Vincent/Yuffie, Reno/Tifa, Vincent/Tifa, Cloud/Yuffie (very rare, but definitely kinda cute), and Reno/Reeve (generally not into yaoi, but again, kinda cute).
One thing I cannot abide is anyone/OC. I really don't like 'Own Characters' when they're used in a romance. As a character in the story, they're nice to have around for variety, so long as they're not a Mary-Sue, and not the main character of the story. Hence why I wrote Terror Incognito... if you use OCs, I would suggest you don't read that one...

Anyway, outside of writing stories here, I'm a fairly active roleplayer, although less so now that my roleplaying buddies spend more time poking me to write more Ouroboros. Still, in the roleplaying group Kalm Storm is my version of an angsty Reno. Semi-angsty anyway. Another future project of mine is going to have an angsty Reno, just to prove that I can.

And now I seem to have given away a handful of my future plans, despite saying I wasn't going to. I think I should shut up now. My AIM s/n is available above, as is my email, so I welcome contact with other authors, if reading this hasn't proven too strange and terrifying...
