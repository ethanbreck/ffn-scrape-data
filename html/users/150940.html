All right, what can I say? I'm twenty-something gal from Western MD. I've been reading since I was old enough to pick up a book, and writing stories almost as long. My literary tastes are wide-ranging, and I have a keen interest in mythology, folklore, and world religions.

I am highly opinionated and tend to be emotionally driven. I can be a loner, sometimes existing in my "own little world." Most people think I am a bit odd (though I suppose that isn't a problem here).

I'm a liberal Christian by definition; my beliefs are important to me, and you will probably see them reflected in some ways in my writing (provided I actually get around to writing something here). I enjoy photography, yoga, concert-going, movies, serious discussions, and long walks. I also like to research urban folklore in my spare time.

I despise hypocrisy, double standards, politics, and fanaticism of any kind.

My favorite TV shows currently include: Ashes to Ashes, Doctor Who, Pawn Stars, Project Runway

My favorite movies currently include: Doubt, District 9, The Prestige, Finding Neverland, Session 9

My favorite book is Mary Shelley's Frankenstein--and I don't trust people who don't like Harry Potter. :)

Look for me. Sometime soon, I hope.
