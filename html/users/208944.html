Hey everyone, I have been a proud member of this site going on ten years now. Wow that's a long time ten years, anyway I have read many fic's over the years and have found several favorites as seen by my list. I have also seen many wonderful examples of good writing, good background, and perfect plots. However sadly a good portion of these were either not finished, seem to be abandoned without notice, or simply ruined in my opinion. This was due to either having the whole fic sounding like a Quentin Tarantino film, with the Characters using profanity every other word. By having a interesting storyline with a family friendly rating, turn into a hardcore Lemon fic in the next chapter while keeping the same rating with no warning. Another example is having a seemingly innocent fic, turn to either the before mentioned type or a lime by having two characters of the same gender throwing themselves at each other again without warning. I know most people would say if don't like, don't read!, and I fully support that but a warnings either in the title description or in the disclaimer would be nice. Before everyone starts up their flame throwers, after reading my final example I would like to say some of my closest friends over the years couldn't speak English clearly, due to it not being their first language. I went to an international collage several years ago, and had friend's from several counties so I am very understanding when it come to that. I just wanted to state that for the record so no one can accuse me of being racist or hating k? Which brings me to my favorite and final example, I know most have run across one or two of these type's during their time on here.

Fic's that are horribly misspelled, looking like either a text message, or l33t speak and finally sometimes like a drunk monkey wrote them. I understand nobody is perfect, I would be the first to admit that do to my own creations over the years and as I said for some English is their second language. But sadly a good portion of these are usually the same people who whine and complain if someone gives a review somewhat critical of their work. And by forcing signed reviews they retaliate by either repeatedly reporting one of the complainers fic's with some lame reason that has nothing to do it at all. Thus getting it pulled by the Admin staff here, or simply if they don't like a pairing they pull the same stunt and get it pulled. We are all authors who use this site for creative writing of some sort, and if we ALL could act our age not our shoe size in some cases everyone experience would be good. I know we all have our own beliefs and Opinion's, I personally would never write anything with anything that could be considered porn or have profanity in it. That my readers already know for a fact. I just finished a fic by JackPotr and I believe they said it best, "Something about me is that I will never write smut or horror. I just don't feel comfortable with writing those topics. I will also try to keep the language fairly clean. I am not putting down anyone else and I mean that sincerely but to me, droppin the 'f' bomb all the time does not add to the story. In fact in some cases, I just move on and stop reading it and that is sad because there are some truly great stories out there that do just that." What he said was so true, in the last week or so I had to stop reading close to five differant fics due to that same issue. They had such promise, and could have done without the said content but oh well once again "Don't Like , Don't Read". I would like to thank anyone who is still reading this after my mini rant there for reading my work, or at least this on my profile.
