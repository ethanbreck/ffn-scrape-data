Hoorah. Here I am. I promised myself never to do fics again. But here I am. I even told a reviewer that fics took up too much of my time. But here I am. Fics aren't my specialty. But here I am.

Yeah, well, if you AIM me, half of the time, it might not even be me. Don't ask :P.

Self intro, self intro... hmm... Well first off, I'm not a good writer! Ha ha. Nah, I'm decent you could say. I try to be a little too creative in word choice so it's kind of screwy.
In my spare time, my roommate drives me crazy with her femmy boyfriends... at least she doesn't come home with some macho guy who puts his feet on my table. Heh, I guess life could get worse.
I'm into anime, ha ha. Who isn't around here? Characters have their own lives, personalities, and limits. Every anime fan knows that the people in them can be deeper than one thinks... no matter how shallow it may seem.

I think that's enough of the self-intro... don't wanna include info of me getting fired around a month ago, spending all day with sketches, etc. Ha ha, some of it's true... ^_^

Anyway, hi there. Talk to me if you like!

One thing that I've learned to treasure while writing in FF.net is reviews. I have found that I would continue a story forever if at least one person asked me to in a review. That's the most rewarding thing I think. ^.^
