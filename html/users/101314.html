Hello there, I am Kaz. I joined ff.net in 2001 and I used to be really active at writing and updating. That fell apart in 2005 when my interest kinda died. After 5 years of hiatus I had an impulse to update Tempting Fate. I still read fanfiction a lot mainly Naruto and Beyblade though. Anyway, if you read my fics then please leave a review, I love to know if anyone is still reading them and it motivates me to update! :)

My older fiction is really, well the only way to describe it is 'badly written'. I did edit them for some changes ago but really, I re-read through them all and cringed. However, they all mean too much to be to be deleted and are up for memories and value. Please keep that in mind if you choose to read them.

Mainly now, my interest lies in Naruto. I love (love) Naruto/Hinata and Sasuke/Sakura. I do like reading alternative pairings as well (itaSaku/KibaIno). Neji is my life.

My older fics are unlikely to be finished, sorry but I have lost interest in continuing any of them
