Brief warning: Many of my earlier writings (i.e, Rebellion, Reconstruction, Live) were completed when I was about 12/13 and while I have some amount of pride in them (mostly in the fact that so many people actually liked them), they are in my honest opinion...bad. There are many mistakes and even though I stand by the plot and my intentions, it is definitely the work of a young person. I must therefore force a separation between myself and my "younger" writings.

Reconstruction Abandoned fic, A sequel to Rebellion, After the events of the rebellion, the remaining members of the group must somehow try to rebuild their lives. Without technology or weaponry, earth and its members must somehow stop the complete annihilation of the human race.

To be absolutely honest, I will NOT be continuing this. If any one cares enough to take it and run with it, be my guest, just let me know ahead of time.
