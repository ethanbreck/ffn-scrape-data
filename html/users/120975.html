RE: The Person Behind the Weird Penname
I started writing when I wasn't even a teenager. I read more now than I have ever written and the fact I actually am writing again after five odd years is a miracle. I have another account on Portkey.org under Dark Illusion (I have an extra D/G fic up there) but love hanging out at DracoandGinny.com under infiltrate the enemy fat kid.

RE: My Draco/Ginny fics
I'm so pleased they're all one shots because I still fervently love this pairing and *I* left canon station with sheer determination once JKR (even thought she blessed us with SO much) gave us that questionable epilogue. Fire and Ice FTW.

RE: My Lily/James fics
All up I wrote 'Inner Power', 'Live Forever', 'These Three Small Words', 'Secrets' and 'Hiding From the Past' (4/5 have been deleted).
I was barely a teenager when I wrote theseMy main reason for keeping HFTP is just to keep a token of what was and never will be. Lily/James fics were my first loves. Their beautiful canon love will forever be just that and never again be tortured by the hands of my long lost plot bunnies. Let's just imagine that they all ended the way you wanted and their only begotten son saved the world.

RE: Fic Abandonment
Fic authors give up all the time. I want to tell you why I did. I know that there are some that still flock to Hiding from the Past because of the 300 reviews expecting much more than you deserve. You need to know now that there is no chance I will update/complete any of my Lily/James fics. When I wrote these I was so incredibly young that my writing skills were improving much faster than my ability to publish new chapters. What happened in the end was new chapters were being scrapped and accidently replaced with old ones and it was one hot mess. So much so that I was always editing to the point where I was pushing around unsalvageable rubbish in a Word Document. And then without meaning to, the path was lost and my fic was left for dead.
