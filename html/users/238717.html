Konnichiwa, Minna-san!

Rakuengaki here and I'd just like to say thank you to absolutely everyone for reading my fics despite my absolutely horrific updating (or lack thereof). I'm afraid that I have a bit of a problem with writing in that I get to a point and the project and I get into a staring contest that lasts for a while until I'm able to write something. If anyone has any ideas please drop me a line and it will be greatly appreciated!

I'd also like to give a quick thanks to those that have reviewed The Good Doctor's Plan! Without your support and responses I doubt I would have the motivation to keep writing. And ofcourse I need to thank my Koi for being my beta reader and helping me come up with ideas for GDP! She's actually writing the prequel to it called Falls the Shadow which details Zack and Sephiroth's "fun" with Hojo after the Nibelheim incident. If you haven't checked it out yet then shame on you! Go read it!
