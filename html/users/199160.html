Hi guys, to make things short,I'm a normal girl who loves anime, any
and all saturday morning cartoons (except for Woody Woodpecker, I hate
that bird) ;-) I can write just about anything, and I assure you won't
be crap (I didn't receive top of my English class for nothing) I am
heavily invovled in the Sea Cadets, Bosn's da bomb!!!! I like music
from Linkin park. I'm a bit of an oddball, a hermit, and I have been
called a freak many times. If you haven't noticed from my first story,
I like sharp, pointy things. You'll see lots more of those things!! I
really like the idea for my orginal character in Warrior for the
Fallen. Xeexru's her name, and swearing and beating up things is her
game. I know that right now she's like a supreme tom boy, but really,
when you think about it, what girl (in her right mind) would have long
hair if she was into fighting? I have short hair, but that's because
I'm into the Sea cadets, and it's too much to try and manage long hair
for 8 weeks on end. No...I'm not a dyke... I love Dragonball Z, they're
butchering the story with DBGT, but I'll watch it anyways.
Trigun... Oh god! I am absolutely obsessed with Trigun! For
all of you who don't watch it and like anime, this is definitly for
you. It is absolutely hilarious! Vash is so freaking
awesome, and Knives is one sexy beast!! Nick...hmmm...You'll
have to be the judge of him yourself, but I love Nicky!! I'm
actually writing a little romance story about Trigun right now.
It's got my own OC in it (Don't worry girls, Nick and Vash get the
women their meant to, just like in the show. I probably won't be
posting it until it's mostly finished. Damn I wish we had it here
in Canada. (Inuyasha
is great too!). If any of you youngsters can remember Beast Wars (or
Beasties up here in frigid ol'Canada), I love that show, I plan on
making a story about it once I finish Warrior for the Fallen about the
show, mainly based around DepthCharge, Rampage/X, and my own original
character. I don't like my female characters to be weak, it just
bothers me when people write up these girls that cannot defend
themselves. I'm writing it so that Xeexru is one badass, powerful chick
who ends up winning the heart of everyone's favourite time travelin'
sword slashin' DBZ character (uhh...that's Trunks in case anyone
didn't know). Story is kind of dull right now, cause i'm still trying
to develope her and Sularon's characters. But hopefully in a few more
chapters, I'll be able to start on the real climax of the story.
(Spoilers!!!! Xeexru must return to Tomakacara because something is
attacking the planet, and even the Tomakas can't defeat it. She
swallows her pride for a brief moment and asks Trunks for help. So
together, they take off with Sularon towards Tomakacara, to face this
unknown enemy, with one massive problem along the way. An even bigger
problem and one massive secret reveal themselves once they arrive.) Uh
oh, did I just give the whole story away? No ;) I didn't, there's gonna
be lots of surprises along the way. Anyways, I've got some writing and
school work to do...yuck...unversity kinda sux...Go MSVU!!!! Until
later days, no_name_writer
