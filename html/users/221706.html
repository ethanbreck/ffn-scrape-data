My name is TheResidentBitch, but you can call me whatever you please.

I am a girl. I have three roommates, all boys. I live with my boyfriend and our dog, Baxter, in our hometown in Southern California. I work as a cake decorator for weddings and events, as well as doing other work in a bakery. I sing opera, perform semi-professionally in musical theater, tap dance, and have worked as a makeup artist and costume designer. I write fanfiction for a variety of topics, and I also take requests for storylines and personalized works. I write for a series of blogs, some multi-authored and some entirely my own work, and I spend a lot of time doing crafts and baking at my home.

If you are interested in either a personalized fanfiction, or any work of literature in a genre of your choosing, please feel free to email me at burningquestionsyo@gmail.com and I will respond to you as quickly as I am able. I do not charge for this service and I will write almost any genre/pairing you would like to see. I do not, unless the circumstances are special, generally write mature or NC-17 works for people I do not know unless they can prove to me that they are of age to receive such material but it is not entirely out of the question.
