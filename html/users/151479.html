I feel really bad about neglecting everything here for so long, but I've been in a creative rut for a while and extremely, insanely busy. My sincerest apologies.

Other points of interest. What stories I haven't finished (and there are a shameful amount) probably won't be updated again. There is light at the end of the tunnel though, since I'm out of the creative rut for some reason and now have some rather brill ideas for some King Arthur, Horatio Hornblower, Master & Commander, and traditional Sherlock Holmes fics. I'm also working on an original story over at FP.com and have sworn off working on more than one story at once. I've had several good original ideas in this new creative flurry, so bear with me as I attempt to get thoughts in head into word processor.

So, while I can't say exactly when, I will be active over here somewhere in the near future.
