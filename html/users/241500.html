I'm a Freshman in College.
...Summer will be better, I promise! Unless life changes its name to "Stupid Apple working 10 hours overtime at a new job"

I graduated last sunday... (being 05/04/08)

Updates of anything published before 2008 should not be expected. I've lost data of the plots, and even though I've reread one or two stories, I can't remember where I'm going with the story except the main plot point. And my writing style annoys me anymore.

7/31/11

Anticipate is probably dead in the water. The idea still intrigues me, but for the life of me I can't find a proper motive to kick Harry's butt into gear.

More stories might start cropping up 'cause I'm stagnent. I need a new job. A higher paying job. A job with benefits and regular hours, and not talking to stupid people who refuse to read and co-workers who are teenagers.
I really need a job without teenager-co-workers.

I've got a new story up. It's Sirius and Remus pairing. The concept of the story is awesome, if I do say so myself. Remus and Sirius decided to help me out. Two different songs, a conversation, and no original words between them. Edit to cut out repeating lines and modify pronouns, add description so it doesn't sound ridiculous, and voila! Dancing with Feeling.

The hardest part of that fic? Finding two songs that meshed well together.

9/14/12

I am announcing my intention to fall off of the face of the earth. Well, fanfiction.net, at least.

I haven't stopped writing. And I haven't stopped writing fanfiction. I just write differently now, well, still words on the reading surface, but you catch my drift.

I'd like to think they are more mature since I started. I haven't taken off any of my stories since I first started publishing them except for the horrid Sailor Moon fic I did. I actually only originally loaded that one to figure out how to load stories. Heh.

Who knows, maybe I'll resurface with a new story since I decided to let this account fade into obscurity. I'll post something here if I actually end up published.

I'm off to take over the world. Will you support me when I become monarch?
