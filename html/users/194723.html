Holy...yeah. I haven't been on here in a while.

The first thing I'd like to say, if you like my fanfictions, thank you. I have matured much as a writer in the past year or so. I am currently working on a completly revised edition of "Sing for Me", entitled "Sing Me Home". Its sequels are "One Tear Spent" and "The Rise of Hope". This will take a while to do, however, and i dont think i will post a story until it is done. I hope to fix everything that people got mad about, and make the story more true to the books/movie. I am a deeper Tolkein fan now.

Sam's interview will be left mostly as it is, besides a few minor alterations.

I will finish "Lost Rose" (you hear me? FINISH!) as not knowing the ending myself has been torturing me.

I am also writing a completly new fiction, entitled "A Hidden Evil", but I wont reveal the plot just yet.

Anyway, thank you to those few loyal, patient souls. I have returned.
