Hello everyone!

Currently it's 2018. I can hardly believe I've been on this site since 2002. I was 13 when I first fell in love with the idea of fanfiction. Now here I am at 29, and I'm still an avid fan.

I've always enjoyed writing. It's always been one of my favorite hobbies.

For the longest time, I had trouble coming up with my own concepts. So you will notice some of my work I had another story line to go off of to help me get through a story ark in order to finish it (I.E Secret Garden and It Started With a Kiss). I feel like on my last fanfic, ISWAK, I was able to mesh the story line of the anime as well as put my own CCS spin towards it. However, after completing that story I felt like I really wanted to finally come up with my own concept. So that is how Love and Lyrics came about.

Love and Lyrics is a completed story. I will be uploading it one chapter at a time. I'm so proud of it, and I hope everyone enjoys reading it as much as I had joy writing it.
