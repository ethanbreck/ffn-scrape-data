Just a little background for you nosy people;). I'm 17 years old and I live in CT in the USA. I've at one point in time played pratically every sport you could possibly imagine and then some, but I am not currently playing anything. I'm thinking about softball again. :)I did play lacrosse for a while though. In other words, I'm violent by nature. So, if you notice this coming out in the crap I call writing, well, now you know why.

I have a few favorite authors, but not many. I absolutely LOVE Mercedes Lackey, and I adore J.K. Rowling...but I think I'll attempt Misty's fics; there are FAR too many Harry Potter fics and far to FEW of Misty's. :)
I'm rather new to the whole writing thing, but I try and I guess that merits at least one or two brownie points. I don't think about what I write, I just write it. And I cannot re-read any of it because I'll end up changing everything and killing it, so if you see something horribly wrong, then let me know, please. :)

Also. As far as my "Herald of Ice" story goes; I own nothing except the people and personalities. Mercedes Lackey owns Valdemar, Haven, Companions and Heralds. So, if you're looking for someone to sue, find another target, because I'm pretty darn broke.

And if you feel the need to flame me, at least have the balls to log in first. I have no respect for people who can yell at others from behind closed doors, but not to their faces. Thanks :)
