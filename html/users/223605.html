And now the reasons why I really dont like Ashley Tisdale...
-Even before High School Musical was ever done, I really didnt care for her acting. On the Suite Life, she seems so forced in her acting. At least Brenda Song makes up for it by London's stupidity.
-Her singing in HSM was absolutely horrible. Her singing in general is just so bad. She sounds like one of the rejected kids that audition for American Idol that truly thinks that she can sing, but for her life she cant.
-The only reason I believe that she got the part in HSM was strictly because she is a Disney Channel star. DCOMs are notorious for using actors from their series in their movies, whether they are in the series first or a series comes shortly after the movie, i.e., Ricky Ullman in Pixel Perfect and then Phil of the Future. Since she is the only one with the established career (if you can call bad acting a career) on the Disney Channel, therefore, she has a part in a DCOM.
