Hello to Everyone!

My Name is Lizzi but online i go be a variety of names, Arisu being one of them.

I wan to thank everyone who ever visited my stories.

About Me:

I started wrting in highschool when i first got attached to anime. Back then, it was the basics, you know, what ever was being played in america on the TV. hence the limited story lines in my writings. SM was my favorite back then.

Now, years later, im a 5th year college student. And i still looove the anime. lol, though now i definately watch more types.

Unfortunately, my writing days are behind me. While i still do have all my work, i havnt looked or touched it in years.

I still picture stories in my head. I wish to write them, but I just cant seem to get the ideas in my head to words on paper.

Maybe someday I will return...

Thank you again to all those who read my work. who continue to read my work.

Did you know... years after i stopped writing you wonderful people still add my un-completed stories to your favorite's lists? You're all amazing!

Lizzi
