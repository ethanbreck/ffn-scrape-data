(this profile has been rewritten because I realized what a total goontard it makes me out to be.)

Hello you few who dare to care! My name means Angel (Angel Royal Apple if you want to be technical) so I tied it into my pen name. My writing started in 6th grade when a boy in my class asked me why there were no girl Saiya-jins (DBZ) and, of course, I tore him a new one on the spot for his stupidityand smacked him upside the head when the teacher wasn't looking. I started doodling on my notes and drew a girl in a very DBZ style and colored her with my highlighters, the only things with me. Thus, Dashi Toku, my first original character, was born.

School was boring so I spent most of my time drawing and working on my stories. "Lavendar and Pink" was my first fanfic and you can tell by the writing sytle and plot progression that it was the work of a middle schooler - in my opinion at least. It was a couple of years until I got the nerve to post it online. It was, in essence, my Missouri story. I worked on it basicly the whole time I was there and posted the first chapter of the sequel once I moved again.

Sorry for the rambling, I tend to do that alot. I also find gore and death not as repulsive as I should and tend to throw it in the blender that is my writing every-so-often. Lovey-dovey scenes are hard for me, but I get by. It can't all be exposed bone and blood-written messages, now can it?

I'm a big anime fan. Just about all of my work so far has been anime related but I am trying new things. Pick a story, taking consideration of the year it was made please, and let me know what you think.
