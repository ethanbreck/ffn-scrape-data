My fics

Evil Minds: After getting their asses beaten by all of the spiky-haired protoganists of their respective games, the Final Fantasy villains are all now in hell...arguing with one other. A complete one-shot.

Never Ever in a Million Years: My first (and most popular) fic, this one is quirky little (ok, RATHER BIG) LuMa which started out trying to be angsty, failed miserably, and ended up kind of a -er- romantic comedy thingie. Check it out if you like LuMas--and be subjected to the style of writing that I had two years ago. It's also up on mediaminer with some slight changes.

Rooming with Kakkarott: Ahem--forgive the horrific spelling.Vegeta is cramped in a small space (namely, in a cheap hotel room) with Goku for several days in the middle of the biggest blizzard in history. What happens? Explosions, constant fighting, cheesy TV shows, and plenty of embarrassing moments! The sequel, when they are trapped on a senior citizen cruise together, is in the process of being written.

Another Twist of Fate: Another LuMa. Not as good as my other one, though... Post-Lavos. Magus, after a foiled attempt in getting his sister back, somehow ends up unconscious in Lucca's backyard. Besides trying to play nursemaid with him (obviously), she is plagued with the problems of a possible invasion of Guardia, the unnatural dreams of her charge Kid, and a strange client that calls himself Lynx. UNFINISHED.

Shattered Time: Mirai AU. After the battle with Cell, Trunks goes back to his own time and goes on a journey to Namek in order to collect the Dragonballs and to wish the Z-senshi back. However, this doesn't bring the happy ending that he expected... UNFINISHED (possibly never)
