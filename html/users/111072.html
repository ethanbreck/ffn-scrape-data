From the time I was in elementary school, I knew that I wanted to write. It all started in first grade, when I got this horrible little 'story' published in my school literary magazine. Of course, at the time, I thought it was the best thing in the world. I've written stories my entire life. It's as natural to me as breathing...so much so that I left the state of Pennsylvania to come to the foreign world that is Florida in order to attend college.

Now that I've graduated, I can move on up in the world (AKA: Now I have free time to edit my novel and hunt for an agent! )(Especially since my writing muse has finally come back into my life.)

...Eventually, I'd also like to find a few old stories that were posted on the SMRFF Mailing List and never made their way here...I'd like to have everything in one spot...
