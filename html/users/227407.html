Hi. I'm Sol. These stories are all mostly terrible because I wrote them as a teenager.

Everything written in the early 2000's are from my teenage years. They are not great but we all have to start somewhere. I have been writing now for close to twenty years so I do hope I've grown. Forgive the formatting of many of them, I was young and did not understand many things, just wanted to write. I'm leaving these up as a sort of time capsule and nostalgia thing, but you are more than welcome to go back and read them. Many are bad, but some are still pretty good to this day.

As for me, I am Sol. No that is not my real name. I write a lot of sex, horror, and fantasy, many with queer themes because that is my wheelhouse. Many of my stories will be mature so I ask that you be mature as well. I will be attempting to write again and updating here accordingly. I hope I can entertain you again after so many years.

Find more work and original fiction on my tumblr (solsethegreat.tumblr.com)

As of now, I have opened an account on Ao3 and will be primarily sticking to there. Feel free to give me a follow because I think my updates here will be sparse from now on.
