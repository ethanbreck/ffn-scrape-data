Well, after having an account here for nearly 4 years without posting anything of value I thought I should finally start posting what little I currently write.

My current interests are (in order): Avatar, Teen Titans, Danny Phantom, Kids Next Door, Xanth (novel), Dragonlance (novel), and Oracle for Hire (a webcomic). I have many other interests, but those are the main ones I read here.

My favorite relationships (I refuse to call them ships) are: Toph/Sokka, Terra/Beast Boy, Jinx/Kid Flash, Aang/Katara, Zuko/Mai, Robin/Starfire, Danny/Sam, Jimmy/Cindy, Dominic/Luna.

I will read just about any relationship though, as long as it is well written and convincing.

My favorite current characters are: Terra (TT), Jinx (TT), Raven (TT), Toph (Avatar), Sokka (Avatar), Danielle Fenton (DP), Rabi~en~Rose (Di.GI.Charat), Cindy Vortex (JN), Electra (Xanth), Raistlin (Dragonlance), and Luna (DD). I am also getting closer to Argent, but thats mainly because of my fanfiction.

My favorite older characters are: Nuriko (FY), Yaten Kou (SM), Michiru Kaioh (SM), Ami Mizuno (SM), Tomoyo (CCS), Numbuh 0 (C:KND), Rei Ayanami (Eva), Hazuki Fujiwara (Doremi), Misty (Pokemon), Xellos (Slayers), Sakura Kasugano (Street Fighter), Urd (AMG), Jubilee (X-Men)

I practically worship Megumi Hayashibara (or at least I did), just so you know.
I consider myself a renaissance geek (aka a geek of many colors) and have a pretty good working knowledge of just about every animated show out there, I just don't search out fanfiction for all of them. I'm also a self-style computer geek, roleplaying geek, and I date a band and music geek. I am frightened of what we might spawn...

Anyone who is interested in chatting about any of these interests, my fanfic, your fanfic, or is just bored feel free to IM me.
