Author has written 51 stories for Harry Potter, Boondock Saints, NCIS, Stargate: Atlantis, and Stargate: SG-1.

I live in Philadelphia, and I'm a teacher. I'm also in graduate school, which keeps me from writing as much as I'd like - well, I'm still writing, but nothing that I'm going to post here. I adore crime drama (like NCIS and Bones) and science fiction (Stargate: Atlantis is my drug of choice, but I enjoy the other Stargates, Star Trek, and a few others as well). I used to write for several other fandoms, but have since pretty much ceased to do so. I took down a lot of my very early work. Don't be sad, it was terrible, trust me.

Reviews are a writer's lifeblood. If you like something I do, please review and tell me. If you hate something I do, please review and tell me. I honestly base part of what I write off of reviews, and I read each and every one that I get... please let me know what you like or don't like about my stories, because I can't progress as a writer if I don't know what my readers think of my work.

For anyone who's reading Always Searching... it will get finished, I swear it. I know where it's going and where it ends, I just have to get it from my head to a Word doc to you. I will do it. I promise. My beta will kill me if I don't. Seriously, she's a scary woman.

If you read fic on livejournal and think that some of this seems familiar, well, it's probably not stolen. My account over there is somehowunbroken and I post there as well as in several communities across the board, mostly in Stargate. On the other hand, if you see something of mine that's posted on LJ under someone else's name, well, please do let me know. Also, some of my more adult fic is only posted there, because it goes above and beyond fanfiction.net's squick factors. There's not much, but if you're interested, head over there.

And if you have an idea for a story, and you think I might be interested... let me know and I'll see :)

-Kay
