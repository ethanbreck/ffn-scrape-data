Author has written 6 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

It's your friendly neighborhood on again off again DBZ fanfic writer! It's not unheard of for me to randomly leave a site and come back months later. I am always lurking! I come by and read once in a while and its not until recently that I decided to come back and write. It seems 'Metamorphosis' still gets attention, and too be honest its the one I'm most proud of, aside from my one comedy. I have however decided to revamp it since there are things I am unhappy with, and let's face it, I'm much older now and therefore my writing has changed since then.

I will consider working on my other stories as well if it seems people want to see more of them. I'd also accept challenges if they are for the DBZ universe, or a crossover with it. While I may read just about anything, I generally only write for DBZ. However if its a great idea and it gets the creative juices going, I'll be more than happy to give it a go.
