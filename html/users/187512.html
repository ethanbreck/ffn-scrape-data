My due date is February 24th, and is fast approaching. The last ultrasound determined that this little baby is a little girl. My laptop jumps on my belly as she wiggles around. Despite the swollen feet and hands, the false-labor contractions, and the difficulties of sleep (getting comfortable and always having to pee), I have enjoyed being pregnant. I look at the assembled Pack 'N Play in the corner of our hotel room, and wonder if I will like being a mom as much as I like being pregnant. Even this close to my due date, the idea of being a mom seems abstract.
