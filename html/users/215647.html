Update June 19th 2007- My writing is atrocious lately and I don't have the courage to post anything up quite yet. I'm starting to become a real perfectionist and nothing is up to my standards. I'm going to be taking a lot of English classes this coming fall semester to boost my confidence up. It's a shame that I don't feel like I grasp the English language well when it is the only language I speak!

I'm sorry to anyone who is really hoping for that Selphie fic that I keep promising! I will post it as soon as I get everything organized and polished.

I plan to rewrite and edit Gippal's Ghost. It's been two years since I've posted it and I reread it a few weeks ago and saw a lot of mistakes. It's also a little bland, isn't it? Heh.

If you haven't noticed, I stick mainly to the Final Fantasy fandom.

Also, if you are here because you received a review you felt was unwarranted feel free to PM me and you can yell at me or insult me all you want. However, I would like to point out I certainly didn't flame you, I simply told you what I liked and/or what I didn't like about your story.

I freely admit to not liking most original characters, but I'd love to find one that I could adore as much as canon characters!

Please, don’t change your POV in the middle of your story! Changing your POV often is very distracting and takes your reader away from the experience of reading your story.

I’m not a big fan of people screaming about his or her OTP being canon, it rubs me the wrong way I will probably say thing about it. Your OTP doesn’t have to be canon for you to love it! I’m a huge SquallxSelphie fan and they are definitely not canon. I guess it’s really just the FFXII fandom that’s been bothering me lately. _ In my opinion, BalthierxFran is not canon, nor is AshexBasch. AshexBalthier is, also, not canon. You really won’t get me to change my mind about that. Sorry, that's my Final Fantasy rant of the day. Haha.
