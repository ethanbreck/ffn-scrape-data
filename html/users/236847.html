Author has written 9 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

Hi everyone! I originally joined fanfiction.net on July 18, 2002 when I was only 12 years old. I was inspired to write after reading many fanfic stories from other talented authors. When I started one of my stories "We Meet Again" back in 2003, my intentions were to finish it, but that didn't happen. I just stopped writing after awhile. After many years, I finally finished the story in 2009. I guess you can say that it took me 6 years to finally give my story an ending. This was also the year I changed my pen name to what it is today: xSapphireAngelx.

The change in my name is just to give me a new beginning. Since it has been such a long time since I've written, it only made sense to give myself a brand new start. I know that stories I will be writing in the future will be different than what I would've written when I was 12 and I wanted a slightly new pen name to reflect that change.

I hope to find more inspiration as I continue to write more stories and I thank all those fans who have supported me throughout the years.

I want to thank everyone for the kind reviews throughout the years. You guys are the best...so thanks for the support.
