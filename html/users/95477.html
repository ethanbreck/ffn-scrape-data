What is there to say about me? I like sugar, caffeine, and the sand siblings. I spend a lot more time reading than writing anything, because I always have at least one project that I should be working on for college. It’s true; I’m a horrible procrastinator. I vowed I’d work on my procrastination for New Year’s, but that was actually a few years ago. No progress yet.

I have certain animes and pairings that I favor (cough Temari/Shikamaru!! cough ), but it’s been my experience that a well written story can make me enjoy almost any pairing. There are many amazingly talented authors on here, so yay!

My muses are on crack. The plot bunnies they send after me are often rabid. That’s your warning. Take everything I write with a grain of salt.
