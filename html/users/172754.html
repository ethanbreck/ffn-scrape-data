Joined 02-19-02, id: 172754, Profile Updated: 04-22-13
Author has written 10 stories for Slayers, Stargate: Atlantis, Doctor Who, Alice, 2009, Haven, and Covert Affairs.

I love everythin sci-fi/fantasy. Magic, logic, and science are all fair game as far as I'm concerned. Anime will always have a special place in my heart, but I'm not really writing any more Slayers stories, or really any anime-based fics. I've lost much of my passion for that genre.

Currently, my obsessions include Doctor Who (New gen and old, I love anything and everything Doctor, and that will never, EVER change. I've loved the Doctor since I was 15... and not to give away my age, but that's now been more than half my life.), Sherlock, Firefly/Serenity, Haven, and Being Human. Older fandoms, which I still stop by and visit, include Stargate: SG1 and Atlantis (Universe was kinda meh), Star Trek, Covert Affairs, Primeval, Tin Man, Alice, NCIS, X-Files, and lots of others that flicker off and on with varying intensities. I also have a bunch of shows that I like, but I'm not really in the FANDOM of, like Castle, Fringe, Suits, White Collar, Eureka, and a whole mess more.
