I am a professional fangirl. No, really--I actually write and publish fanfic for a living. I've written three novels based on Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen--you can find out more about those on my website.

Regarding the unfinished Doctor Who stories:

Overall plot disclaimer for canon-verse Doctor/Rose stories:

My headcanon is 100% Bad Wolf!Rose. Sometimes I can work that into the narrative of the story, sometimes I can't. But even if I don't say it, know that in my mind, they get forever together.

On a slightly less important note--and this is more of a trick I use that I'm offering to you--whenever I'm reading or writing fic set pre-Doomsday, I do so with the thought that "Because this happens, Doomsday doesn't." Like I said, that's more one reader offering a tip to another than anything I can really say as a writer.
