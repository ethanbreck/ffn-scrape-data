Author has written 11 stories for Pokémon, and Kim Possible.

My interest in writing started when I wrote several short stories and a few poems while I was in sixth grade, never really considering the fact that maybe I would be a writer someday. I like to write because of my appreciation of music. I have learned so much through music, such as the importance of good story telling, rhythm, passion for the subject being discussed, and the need to have a voice in the world. Without music, I don't think I would have ever started writing. I write because it makes me feel safe when I'm doing it. When I'm writing, I can become lost in whatever it is I'm writing about, and forget about the world around me. I can get lost in the characters I'm writing about, or the essay I'm writing, or anything else that has to do with writing. As a talent and a hobby, I am free to be me, to love, and to be everything that I am. Writing gives me a comfort like no other and is so much a part of me that I can't imagine life without it.

My Main Characters
Name: Angelina Murakami
Age: 17 (or three years older than Ash)
Height: 5'4"
Eyes: Hazel
Hair: strawberry-blonde; mid-back-length, curly/wavy
Hometown: Pallet Town, Kanto

She is the daughter of Giovanni and Delia Ketchum; a secret-agent of the criminal organization Team Rocket, but is very kindhearted and treats all Pokemon with love and respect. She was raised by Clarissa Swarovski-Murakami for seven years, whom she thought was her mother for six years until her birth certificate reveals her biological mother to be Delia Ketchum and her involvement with Giovanni up until Ash was born. She didn't participate in the Johto League Silver Conference when Clarissa died while giving birth to her step-sister Christina. She has a childhood past with James Morgan (current member of Team Rocket), and became good friends with Danielle Emery while living on Crystal Island, before moving away to Pallet Town to be closer to her step-relatives. She then became friends with Misaki Oak (Professor Oak's granddaughter) and later acquainted with Gary, Ash, and Amber Trainum (another OC of mine). She was also introduced to Professor Oak, who gave her a Charmander when starting her first Pokemon journey in the Johto Region. She gained a long-time best friend and traveling partner in Jeremy Cretonne (whom is dating Danielle) and long-time rival in Seamus Nichols, whom she is now dating throughout the Battle Frontier.

While Angelina traveled the Johto Region for the second time, she and Jeremy stopped at the Goldenrod City Radio Tower where their good friend Marcus Trainum was a DJ for the Poke-Music Channel and introduced them to his producer George Nichols (whom is Seamus's father). George agreed to be Angelina's agent after listening to a song that she made and brought her into the world of sensational singing as Crystalline. She had two concert appearances in Johto, her first CD release, many magazine pictures and videos, and countless of other concerts/CD signings in Hoenn.

Somewhat feisty and stubborn, Angelina can be sensitive and mature at times, very protective of people she cares about most, loves to sing. She gives her Pokemon nicknames someday wants to become a Pokemon Master. She doesn't stay in one spot when traveling, and is always there for her friends. Willing to learn new things, has met famous people and rare Pokemon, sometimes naive and forgetful, knows that battling isn't about winning or losing and can battle very well. She knows her types and what advantages they have over other Pokemon. When it comes to her feelings, she falls head-over-heels in love, but that depends on the guy she knows and likes best. This involves her current relationship with her long-time rival Seamus Nichols, although before dating she was mildly crushing on Brock, yet she is doubtful that he will even acknowledge her more than a friend when he attempts (but fails) to go after other women. Her determination has earned her many badges, and her hard work got her placed into the hall-of-fame in the Orange League, third in the Indigo League Tournament, and second place in the Hoenn League's Tournament. She also shows interest in the Pokemon contests; winning four ribbons herself, but mostly enjoys watching May and Drew compete in the Grand Festivals. Although she became the Champion of Johto/Kanto, her defeat in Hoenn's Elite Four has taught her that winning isn't everything as long as her Pokemon have done their very best. Angelina is currently traveling the Battle Frontier upon invitation by Scott and can't wait to start her new adventures into the Sinnoh Region.
