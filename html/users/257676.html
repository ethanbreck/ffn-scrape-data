I'm hungry.

Give me waffles.

If you're reading this, chances are you're pissed at me for trashing one of your godawful stories. Well, I'm sorry, but you deserved it. I only comment on stories that are exceptionally good or mind-numbingly awful, and 9 out of 10 stories published on Fanfiction.net fall in the latter category.

It's your own fault for publishing that horrid piece of garbage, really.

To be frank, you should be honored to have received an honest review from me since you were probably being inundated with baseless and undeserved accolades written by people with English abilities rivaling those of illegal immigrants, botched lobotomy survivors, and... well... you.

You're very much welcome.

Don't misunderstand: I don't enjoy acting like an Internet Tough Guy (though it sure does feel good at times). I just think a little well-intentioned vitriol is necessary to balance out the deluge of pink fluffy rainbow idiocy that gets jammed oh so tightly in this website's bloody craw on a regular basis. Wouldn't you agree?
