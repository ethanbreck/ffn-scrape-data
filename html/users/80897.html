Perpetual college student. Semi-professional writer. Enjoys screaming at the walls and slashing Palmer and Heidegger. Don't be scared. I don't bite...hard ;-p.

My name is Aoi. That is not my real name but it might as well be somedays. I am old enough to drink, drive and swear, but I can't run for Congress. Yet. You do the math :). I am proud to be female and have never wanted to change that fact. Being male does not automatically make a person happy, sane or special in any way. And anyone who says otherwise needs a reality check. I like yuri, het and yaoi and not always in that order. My dream is to make enough money writing that I can retire and live in a comfortable apartment in a big city with a lot of cats and enough herbal tea to survive the Apocalypse. I may sound cynical, egotistical and catankerous. Aside from being so, I am also a very nice and tolerant person who loves constructive feedback and making new friends... provided they are housebroken ;).

Update: 12/22/03

You may have noticed the absence of a few stories these past few days. Some are well-known, some are not. They have been removed from my account by my own decision until further notice for a variety of reasons. Some of them ("Hana the Next Door Neighbor", for example) I have removed because I feel they are examples of poor writing, things I do not wish to share publicaly having grown as a writer in the past two years.

Other fics (mainly my League and Lilo and Stitch stories) have been removed for personal reasons. I sincerely and humbly apologize to readers who enjoyed these stories. And I thank you all in advance for respecting my decision to remove them.

Best Holiday Wishes.

Please join if you have the burning desire to know when I update my fan fiction. It'd be cool if you did :).
