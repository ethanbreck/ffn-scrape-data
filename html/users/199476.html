Force Strength: Unknown

Light saber color: blue (primary), green (secondary)

Family: none

Pandora was born on Korbin. Having no knowledge of her parents, she assumes them dead, and considers herself an orphan. At a young age, she was found and adopted into a gang known as Calypso. She was given the name of Magnet, because things ‘seemed to stick to her’. One afternoon, as she was out on one of her ‘jobs’, she spotted Zizziana. As things go on Korbin, any Jedi Knight without any traces of the dark side sticks out like Darth Vader in a field of storm troopers. Later that day, Pan literally ran into her future Jedi Master. Zizziana believed that Pan had the potential to become a Jedi Knight, and Pan was more than happy to accept the offer of training, as well as a dry place to sleep and regular meals. After, a meeting with the Council, Pandora was allowed to stay, and Zizziana allowed to teach her.

Hair: Blonde

Weight: 110 lbs.

Force Rank: Master

Force Strength: Combat

Light saber color: purple (primary), green (secondary)

Family: 2 siblings

Alec “White Menace” Nightstar

Leia Nightstar

Zizzy was born to Terain Nightstar and Princess Katrina of Corellia. The marriage of their parents was forbidden. One Sith lord in an attempt to turn Terain murdered Katrina in front of him. His fall was complete and the Jedi hid the younger child Leia on one planet and then split the twins (Zizzy and Alec) up. While Alec was raised by Ewoks (Ewok name “White Menace”), Zizzy was taken to Tatooine and raised by a Jedi Master. Zizzy had no idea of her father being Darth Trepidus. Eventually the swoop bike riding Jedi crossed paths with Alec whom she had an immediate connection with. When Darth Trepidus went after his son, Zizzy went to save him. Trepidus revealed he was Alec’s father and after Zizzy rescued him, Trepidus killed her master. A conversation with her dead master revealed her connection to Alec and their younger sister.
