It's that time again. Time for a new profile! YAY!

Ok, well let us think. My name is Emily (or Riley, depending on who you are. And yes, Criminal Minds fans, Emily really IS my name! LOL!) and I'm a 25 year old TV/movie fanatic that's currently jobless. I have no money so if anyone should feel compelled to sue me for writing my fanfics, I'd advise against it as it will get you nothing. I trained at the New York Film Academy as an actor, but I'm now studying Pre-Wildlife Biology/Zoology in Wyoming.

I started out writing Stargate SG-1 fics which have since grown into a multitude of other things (and by multitude, I mean like...5 other things). I was big into Mutant X when it was on the air, and still like it, but have generally lost interest it writing fics about it anymore. It pains me to say that as I haven't finished half of those stories. Who knows? Maybe a bolt of inspiration will attack me one day and I'll finish everything. I sure hope so!

As for my other fics, I'm still working on my CSI: Miami ones (trying to get inspiration for them), my random Van Helsing story that just keeps going and going and going, my Criminal Minds ones that are going to get me murdered by fans if I don't finish them (wouldn't that be fitting?), and my Tin Man-ness. Oh, and now I've started working on Warehouse 13 ones, too!

And I now feel the need to make lists of my favorite things. Yes.
