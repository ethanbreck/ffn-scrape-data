Author has written 31 stories for Gilmore Girls, Friends, and Harry Potter.

I'm 20, I go to an art school 1000 miles from home, I'm a TV major with a writing/production concentration.

My friends call me the Swami--not only because it's a take-off of my eight grade nickname, but also because I have swami-like qualities. Not really.

My passion for TV has actually been slowing down lately, but I have my days I watch. Monday and Thursday are chock-full, and I watch one show each on Wednesday and Friday (Pushing Daisies and Friday Night Lights, respectively).

I write fics for Gilmore Girls, I think because I just always have. I've also written one Friends fic, and I've recently started dabbling in Harry Potter, though nothing is posted yet.

I love music, and I watch a lot of movies. If I didn't have Netflix and an iPod, I don't know what I'd do.

The link to my homepage is a link to my story journal, where one-shots in the "Comfort-verse" are posted. The Comfort-verse is the universe including Too Close for Comfort, Comfortably Close, and anything that comes after. The story journal now includes other stories, which so far is one HP fic. Hopefully more will come.
