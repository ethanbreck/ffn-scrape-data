Progress and updates on "The Chance of Change" - 12/November/2018

Just last week I realized that TCOC has turned 20 this year. Oh dear me. Talk about unfinished business! What's nice is that I still feel so strongly about it, about the characters. Anyway, I keep updating this section, as a signal that yes, I'm still alive, and yes, still writing. And I only do this because for some mysterious reason, people continue to read this story and encourage me to write more of it. It always warms my heart when a readers tells me they can totally relate with the characters, or that the writing can evoke strong emotions in them. It's a good motivator. I won't ramble about the difficulties of real life, because everyone knows what that's like. Just let it be said that 2017-18 haven't given me much time outside my responsibilities. I've somehow managed to finish chapter 9, though. It was rough going. And I find that I'm a bit drained after that, and making very little progress with the two remaining chapters. Nevertheless, I keep at it. I had a mind to post the whole think once it was finished. Now I'm thinking I might just dish out chapter 9 little by little. Anyhow, thank you very much for reading!

I'll continue to post cut-versions here due to rating restrictions. But the whole thing can be found at Archive of our Own, under penname piasan. THANK YOU for your unbelievable patience.

Thanks for visiting!

About “The Chance of Change”:

I've been told this story doesn't really start to get its thickness until chapter 5, more or less. Around chapter 7 it gets the right consistency :) Hopefully, you won't be discouraged. Initial chapters, although rather lame and cliched (in my opinion), are not long at all, so you won't have to read long to get hooked, and by then, you'll be happy about the word count!

This story is an alternative universe romance, sprinkled with drama, mixed in with heavy doses of realism. This doesn't mean its not fluffy at times (at lots of times, really). It just means that I've slice-of-life'd the Ranma 1/2 crazy. I feel the amazing characters from the series could reach further depths, only the nature of the show (not so much the manga, actually) doesn't allow for it to happen. This is as it should be, in any case. There's a reason why we love the series. But I was always wondering about the quiet side of things, and what would happen if these people were, well, normal. At least in some ways.

When I was a teenager, I had a few interesting things happen to me (not many, mind you) that got me inspired. And I started writing “The Chance of Change” during my Junior year in high school. I mapped it all out fairly quickly. It started as a short story that would have 6 chapters, tops. But with time, it became a sort of reflection on life, and a way for me to deal with my own issues through the characters. So, it takes me long to write. It seems I need to mature and change, so that I can transfer those emotions onto the characters. The story is slow to start, although later chapters move a bit more quickly, even if almost nothing ever happens, I'll have to admit. Almost everything is in the character's heads. My posting is even slower, I’m afraid. One chapter a year. Two, if I’m being very productive! But because the characters and their feelings are very dear to me, this is a story I have no doubt I’ll finish writing. If you’re here for the first time, I’m sure readers that have stuck around the story since it started can attest to this. Slowly but surely, the story will be finished, some day. I beg for your patience.
