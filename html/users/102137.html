Author has written 12 stories for Sailor Moon, Inuyasha, Full Metal Panic, Itazura na Kiss, and Tangled.

Sooo...

I realized that it has been four years since I have written a large portion of my fiction, many of those left unfinished. I also realized that I have grown tremendously as a writer since then and I hate some of the things I have written (not the stories, just the way I wrote them). So much of the fiction I have, mainly the Sailor Moon fiction, desperately needs to be re-vamped. I also have other stories floating around in my head that I recently feel the need to write. So there will be changes.

I am in college full time now as well as seriously writing a novel that I intend to publish, these will take precedent over my fictions and will cause time to be an issue. But I do intend to finish and improve these fictions.

I thank all of you for your reviews, encouragement and understanding.
