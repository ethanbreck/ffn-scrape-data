Hey! I'm Lani, a redhead from NZ.

Well I've 'offically' discontinued and deleted "And the Road holds no Answers" due to reaching a landmark in my life. When writing it, I felt that I was struggling with Faith to battle the challenges that life had thrown at me. I completely intended on continuing this fanfiction but I don't think I can for the simple reason that I can't identify myself with Faith any longer and thus am unable to continue writing from her point of view. I've been through several nightmares over the last year or so and I'm moving on. Sometime during the last year I set up a new account almost symbolising my 'moving on'. You may see me around as "ScarsOnAScribbler'sHeart". I used it as a new identity, almost. I'm sorry for the confusion that this may have caused.

I spend more of my time writing original pieces now, published at http://www.fictionpress.com/profile.php?userid=320451 where I have three poems to date. I'd really appreciate your comments on them if you'd like to read more of my work.

Maybe I'll come back to writing Fanfiction again, but until then, I'll stick around Fanfiction.net (most likely as "Scars"), and read the odd story, review now and then. Thanks for everything.

Love
Lani
