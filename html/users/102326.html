Welcome!

I used to write stories here many moons ago, some of which were a lot larger than I could manage; now I tend to read more than I produce. My style has changed over the years, so whatever you may find on this account likely represents a different writer and media follower.

My interests vary: as a sample, I consider myself a fan of My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic, Frozen, Pretty Soldier Sailor Moon, Adventure Time, Star Trek, the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Mystery Science Theater 3000, and The Matrix. If you're ever interested in more detail for whatever reason, feel free to ask!

My background in fiction writing is limited to fan fiction; I do, however, have a master's degree, giving me a sense, I hope, of how best to string together some interesting sentences. I can even turn a phrase on occasion. ;)

I may not always reply promptly to messages, but I do read them. I hate to think someone might be ignored.

Thanks for stopping by!
