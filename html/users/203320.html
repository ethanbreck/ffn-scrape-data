Slow Dance With Boba Fett

Look beneath my body armour.
Can't you see there's more inside?
More than just a bounty hunter?
I've got feelings so strong I can't hide.

Ignore my rocket launchers.
They've been known to choke the children.
Run away with me from Mos Eisley.
We could start all over again.

I love you.
I won't kill you,
unless I descover that you're worth more when you're dead.
I love you.
I won't shoot you.
For once I'll expose myself and show you who I am.

And I'll put down my weapons,
and I'll take off my helmet,
and I'll take off my chestplate,
and my combat boot and gloves,
and the rest of my weapons.
Here I am!
I'm ecstasy!
Most truely, Love Boba Fett.

Love Boba Fett, baby!
