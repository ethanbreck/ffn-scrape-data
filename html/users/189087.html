i'm elodie. i used to like rent, not really anymore. but i liked the fics i wrote, so i'll leave them up.

i sail on ships. fo'realz. my HP OTP is draco/ginny, but i also sail harry/luna and lily/james. my x-men OTP is marie/logan. i like bobby, but he is just not man enough. besides, his relationship with rogue isn't nearly as interesting as logan's. also, he does not have sideburns. i also ship sean/ellie for degrassi tng. my ships tend to be a motley crue

let's see. i like music. big surprise. i enjoy neutral milk hotel, the magnetic fields, iggy pop, broken social scene, leonard cohen, the velvet underground, the fiery furnaces, brahms, mozart and copeland. seriously, aaron copeland was a genius. you've all heard rodeo. now go look up the tender land, or appalachian spring. mozart's requiem in d minor is my favorite requiem, and brahms liebeslieder waltzes is my favorite song cycle. there is something so complex in mozart's simplicity and something so simple in brahms' complexity.

i won't be finishing the smiling game. i started writing it when my love for rent began to wane. but i'll leave it up. if anyone wants to take over it, i don't care. just drop me a message.

i'm trying to get back into writing. i haven't been able to write anything much since i finished wisp. seriously. it was the only story i ever finished. i've changed a lot since then.
