ABOUT ME:
Let's see...

Pen Name: Endless Moonlight.
I've gone through several in the past, but I'm actually quite attached to this one. It has no real meaning aside from that I thought it was a beautiful phrase. I tried to pick something that evoked the same feeling as cellar door, if you know what I mean.

Real Name: secret ^^

Age: 22 (so old!)

Sex/gender: female

Location: somewhere in the US

Life in general: I used to be all about this site back in middle/high school, and recently rediscovered it. I'm in a clinical psychology doctoral program which takes up most of my time, but I've found this is a nice stress release from all that. Other than that, I can't think of anything about myself that is especially unique or interesting that anyone would care to read about.

Writing: I started writing again mostly because I discovered this wonderful anime/manga/jdrama called wallflower and it inspired me. (You should really check it out, it's one of the best I've seen!) I've also found writing to be a nice break in between the stress of writing papers and exams. Sadly I'm rarely inspired enough to write anything truly original of m own so I stick to borrowing others ideas and building upon them(yay fanfiction!). That in mind, I try to stay as true to the characters as possible. I also openly welcome any criticism! I think I'm a pretty good writer for the most part, but I always enjoy feedback that would help me improve my skills!

Current works:

Made for Each Other - this is like my Wallflower baby. I'm quite fond of it, and it is the fic that started me writing again. I would really like to finish it, but finding time to sit down and write it doesn't come as often as I'd like. I update it as often as possible! (ps, I'm a review-whore so reviewing will get you faster updates)

Fairytale - I've had part 1 written for a few months now, and finally polished it up so that I was satisfied enough to post it. Part 2 is 0% complete so far, but the story is just supposed to be short and sweet. There's not really much substance there, so when I get around to it, it shouldn't take long to finish.

Others - I have several other ideas cooking up in my little brain right now, but no time to write them. I don't feel like mentioning them since I don't want them stolen, but they may start popping up on the site eventually. :)

Oh, and if you're here because I left a review that offended you or something, I apologize. On occasion I do leave more critical reviews, but it is always with the goal of helping the writer, not to flame or upset you. (I tend just not to review things that I find truly awful or unreadable due to grammar mistakes)

That's really it. I'd love it if you read my stuff, but keep in mind most of it was written a long time ago! :) pm me if you are inspired to.
