Author has written 24 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, Stargate: Universe, Warcraft, and Once Upon a Time.

Hi, my pen name ist Zottelchen, Janola or Kathryn. Depends on the websites you're surfing.

Since Voyager started in 1995 I'm a huge J/C Fan. I think after a year with the show running on tv I worte my first fanfiction - followed by many others.

When I was writing "Tauschgeschäfte" and got to the part where I had to bring Voyager back out of that nebula - I had absolutely no idea how to do that. It took 6 years (no joke) until I came up with a solution and I managed to finish that story. I don't know why, but in there years I wasn't able to write another story or come up with new ideas. Maybe it was a "Voyager-burn-out".

But when I finished the story, suddenly my head was full of new ideas for many many storys and I started to write again. I literally bursted out with new storys and ideas and I felt a new Voyager-fever burning my cells (love that).

To all german-spreaking J/C fans - I really hope you enjoy some of my storys.

You can visit my homepage for some (nice) pics in a few storys.

For the Stargate Fans - absolutely LOVE SGU! Of course Robert Carlyle as Dr. Rush :) He's my inspiration for storys right now and so there are more SGU storys coming.

regards

Kathryn (my pen name on my homepage)
