Hi. I'm a 19 year old college student. I'm majoring in Exercise Science and minoring in Psychology. Exercise Science is going to open up the world of Physical Therapy to me (yay!) and I'm considering becoming a pediatric physical therapist. I love to read all sorts of things, mainly the classics. For example, "Jane Eyre", anything by Shakespeare, "Frankenstein", "The Picture of Dorian Grey", "Dracula", Tennyson and Wordsworth are two of my most favorite poets, etc. Face it. I'm a nerd to the umpteenth power.

Iteach children how to swim - I have been for the last six years - so most of the children you see in my future stories will be based on past pupils. I quit my job as a lifeguard/swim instructor in January 2006. Sad news, eh? I miss it, but I felt that I needed a change in my life (plus it was hard commuting from the dorms all the time without a car).

I have AIM, however I am not using it right now due to some technical difficulties with my computer, the Hal 5000. See, I've dropped him one too many times and he decided to crack his screen. It's going to take a lot of money to get him fixed up. I'm actually considering getting a new one...anywho. I use my Dad's computer now, so that means no AIM, but you can feel free to add me anyway. Once my boat anchor...err...computer...is in fighting form again, I'll go back to beingan AIM addict. My screennames are Nyheinz57 and PsucheAthanatos. I use PsucheAthanatos more often than not. It's Greek for "the soul undying" Nifty, huh?

Once Hal is up and running or I have a replacement for the little shit, I can continue my work.

Amazingly Cool News: I got a part-time job for the summer (2006) at a pediatric physical therapy clinic, and I will also be doing another part-time stint as a swimming instructor at my beloved YMCA. Yay money!
