"It was only a middle of the night thing..." - Ariella Chant (ain't it always a middle of the night thing?)

Dhasku (DHAS-koo): female mage (From Tamora Pierce's book Shatterglass) [it's a title in a county called Tharios, sort of like Miss or Lady, just for someone that's a mage]

Alidath (AL-ee-dahth): small winged one (Latin) [it was actually Alida which means "small winged one" but I made up the name Alidath with plans to use it for a fire-lizard, but I decided it would be more fun to use it as a pen name]

Here's the low-down on me, Dhasku Alidath. I am an 18 year old girl trapped in senior year of high school. I currently reside in Texas, though I used to live in Missouri. In what little spare time I have, since I'm busy with school and such, I write fan fiction and I draw. I'm a rabid Harry Potter fan, so be careful I don't bite you. I have read the books several times over and am considered the Harry Potter guru among my friends. They all think I'm crazy. Good. I'm also an avid RPGer. You'll see me around Ezboard under the usernames Azurial, Berlioz Callaghan, and Lady Alastraina.

Basically, I'm a crazy little psycopath, as my friends have dubbed me, as well as a morbid sadist. *grins evily* And I love it. *cackles insanly as she rubs her disks* My precious... *hack hack cough cough* Yeah... that was my Gollum impression. Not only do I love Harry Potter, I also love Lord of the Rings and Smallville. *smiles* I AM a pervy hobbit fancier, with prefrence to Pippin.

I'm really big on following the canon, so you will not be seeing any AU stories from me. Except for one, and only one. Also, I don't do slash, or any other relationships many find squiky, simply because I don't feel it's supported by canon. Canon Canon Canon. It's not just a brand of camera, it's a way of life.

Please Sign Your Reviews. I may want to reply, so PLEASE sign them. Flames will be used to make s'mores. Thank You.

[No, your eyes do not deceive you. I'm the same person that went by Wand*Light not long ago. I'm now working on a new fic and felt that name wasn't really fitting anymore. So now I am Dhasku Alidath.]
