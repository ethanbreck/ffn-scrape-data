Just when you think I'm dead, I come back. ;-) Chapter 20 is posted and I am working on 21 (the beginning of a new arc)! The website is temporarily down while I move domain hosting, but it should be back up within a few weeks.

Bio: My name is Kathy and I'm 26 years old. I have been writing on and off in my spare time since I was in seventh grade. Moon Senshi was originally conceived as a horrible, amateur fanfiction in my youth, and later as a much better RPG that I ran along with several of my friends; after a successful run of a year and a half, the RPG ended (the only RPG I have played to completion) and I decided to create a fanfiction based on the events of the RPG called Moon Senshi: Children of Destiny. I also enjoy Pen and Paper RPGs, Anime and Manga, and Video Games. I have a keen interest in "greening up" as well.

Favorite Anime: "Twelve Kingdoms", "Escaflowne", "Hime-chan no Ribbon", "Saint Tail", "Wedding Peach," "Princess Tutu," "Kaleido Star."

Favorite Novels: The Bartimaeus Trilogy, Jane Eyre, Tamsin, The Last Unicorn, Howl's Moving Castle, Twelve Kingdoms, Tithe, the Spiderwick Chronicles.

Favorite Manga: Dream Saga Takamagahara, Basara, Please Save My Earth, Here is Greenwood, Planet Ladder, Hana Yori Dango, Swan.

Fanfic Info

Moon Senshi: Unmei no Kodomo is my long-running fanfic and where you will see the most updates and effort. Yes, there are fan senshi, but there's also a great deal of Chibi-UsaxHelios, and later on in the second arc when the Moon Senshi's characters are established, there's also a good deal of the Sailor Quartet and the Planetary Senshi.

Occasionally I write drabbles for the Sailor Moon fans community on LJ (there's a bi-monthly prompt.) I usually post the drabbles on here afterwards. If the drabble is related to Chibi-UsaxHelios and it takes place in the future, I'll usually try to write it as a prequel to Moon Senshi (e.g., I will make it about the events occurring before the Moon Senshi's appearance in Crystal Tokyo.) In those cases, I will shelve it under Young Love, for the benefit of of many people who really enjoyed Helios' Return and requested that I write more Helios 'fic. That way, all my Helios fic is conveniently located in one place, in the chronological order that the events occur.
