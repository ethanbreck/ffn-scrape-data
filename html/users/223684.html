About Me: I'm a 19 year old from the Garden State. My dream is to be a Broadway actress and maybe do film as well. I have 5 dogs (papillons) and a twin sister who you can find here under the pen name Ryoko-sama. I don't think I'm a very good writer but I've been visiting this site for so long that I thought I'd contribute. I love Cardcaptor Sakura and you could bank on the fact that if Syaoran Li were real I would marry him ;) I also really enjoy the anime Tsubasa Chronicle, though I haven't read the manga.

Whats Up? Finally! I was able to write a very little today, about two and a quarter pages for Nadeshiko's Eyes. I also did some more editing on He Loves Me... He Loves Me Not. This is huge, seeing as I've had the drive of a potato all summer. I tried working on Hope and Faith, but it's just so boring... I really want to have a good finished piece that I can be proud of, before I go to Florida. College begins two days after I get back. I think I may be the only person on Earth who doesn't care about early classes. I woke up early for 13 years. A few more won't kill me.

Click Here! for my writing journal so you can stay updated on whats going on with my fan fics! You can also check out my reviewer replies. Every time I post a new chapter I'll leave replies to my reviewers that had any questions to be answered or comments that I feel the need to respond to. Be sure to check it out!
