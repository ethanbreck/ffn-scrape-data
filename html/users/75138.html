Author has written 22 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Reboot, Gundam Wing/AC, Banjo-Kazooie, Cowboy Bebop, Harry Potter, and Final Fantasy X.

Find me here under my new name, ZacksFlowerGirl

If anybody even keeps up with this -- most of these stories were written a good five to seven years ago. A lot happens in seven years, and I suppose that writing fell to the backburner (especially that of fanfiction -- most of these works were fueled by the warped mind of a pubescent teenage fangirl!)

About me: I'm 21, going on 22, getting married soon (yay!) and living with my wonderful fiance (who we'll charmingly refer to as Zack!) in a Chicago condo. Lately my focus has been on studying biology/chemistry and pursuing a career in scientific research and development (ironic for a woman who identifies herself with Aerith, no?), along with much work in the area of animal welfare, fostering and rehabilitation--more specifically, with that of small animals such as rabbits and guinea pigs. It's been a busy past few years, but after playing Crisis Core recently I've found that that little spark of fangirl-ism still lives within me, and I may just have to indulge in it once more in the form of awful fanfiction writing.

Though it can officially be said now, I am definitely an Aerith/Zack supporter now. ;)

I appreciate and love all the support I've gotten from readers over the years. Unfortunately, I doubt I could ever continue any of these particular stories. I do have my reasons. I will be writing anew, I hope, under my new account.
