Hmmm. theres so much how are you to know where to begin?

Well Im 17, I live in Wales, UK, and since about a year ago I have been obsessed with Lord of The Rings, and basically everything Tolkien. The story is so real to me that I find it hard to believe that Middle Earth never existed.

I haven't always wanted to be a writer, though when I read Tolkien, and a few other books as well, I decided to write. I didn't find out about fanfiction until a few months later, and since then I have been a member of this site.

I am bonkers. Completely. My favourite quote is: I am so gorgeous that there is a seven month waiting list for birds to suddenly appear every time I am near - The Cat Red Dwarf. (that may give you a rough idea!!)

I have a massive internet addiction, I think its because I prefer typing what I'm saying :S. It just goes to show you dont have to be a cannibal to be fed up of people.

Anyway - this is Gilraen, signing off - thankyou for wasting 3 minutes of your time to read this!!

Loving always

Gilraen
