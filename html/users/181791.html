Yello'. You wish to know about me? I be human, if that be you looking for. I be a guy, with 19 years of age under me record. And these be my stories:

"When History Repeats Itself" - Resident Evil
Umbrella has been experimenting in Lilypad City, and the zombifying virus is loose again. This once peaceful town is ravaged by undead and monsters. Only a handful of teenagers are alive at the high school, one of them a young man named Falco Alfred. Falco is determined to find his stepsister, and what happened to lead to this. Yet the odds are so much against survival...
Contains original characters, and RE characters in future chapters, original and new monsters, and similar aspects to the RE games (puzzles in everyday buildings).
Warning - Contains extreme non-stop violence, harsh language, rape, sex, and other issues one might find offensive. Aka NOT for the children.

"Mr. Game & Watch - Stories of a Comedian Warrior" - Super Smash Brothers Melee
The 2-D man of Flat Zone and his extremely intelligent friend Pichu are living in the SSBM mansion with all the other Smashers. Yet many bizzare things happen to them. The other Smashers try to kick G&W out of the league, they play Dungeons & Dragons, giant spiders try to take over the mansion and the world, and yet another really stupid "Survivor" spin-off. Where the madness starts, who knows?
Contains all SSBM characters, including Daisy, and several cameos. G&W and Pichu are main characters.
Warning - Contains language, comic violence (including lots of giant spider squishing), and sexual innuendos.

"Angel Bird" - Super Smash Brothers Melee
Fox met Zelda when the second year at SSB started. He fell in love with her, regardless of the fact a relationship between the two seemed impossible. Then Fox's friend Falco was murdered. A trail of emotional suffering for Fox and more murders follows shortly. Someone is out to get him, and his friends want to help him, before it's too late...
Contains all SSBM characters, and adds characters from other games and TV shows into the fray. A Fox/Zelda/Link story, also with other pairings.
Warning - Contains language and violence. Some find Fox's attraction to Zelda to be wrong, but whether you do or not is up to you.

"The Adventures of Tyler and Magmar" - Pokémon
Tyler receives Magmar as his first Pokémon, but the fiery Pokémon is not your typical Pokémon. He is a talking, rebellious, wise-cracking Pokémon who loves to give his trainer (and others) a hard time. Among Tyler's other Pokémon are a coffee-addicted Pinsir, a warrior woman Jolteon, and a fraidy-cat Nidoking. Here is a lighthearted spoof everyone's favorite Pocket Monster game!
Contains original characters and all sorts of Pokémon.
Warning - Contains comic violence, sexual innuendos and mild language.

"Yoshimitsu, Glad To Meet You" - Soul Calibur
The masked, one-armed ninja known as Yoshimitsu has always been somewhat of an enigma with little fanfiction around him. So I gave him his own story! Now he's a wise-cracking smartass with an equally sarcastic, talking sword! Another silly story.
Contains SC2 characters, main character Yoshimitsu.
Warning - Contains comic violence, language, and sexual innuendos.

Now you know 'bout me. Read them stories! Good night.
