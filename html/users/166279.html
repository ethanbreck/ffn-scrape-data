Author has written 9 stories for Dark Angel, Lord of the Flies, and Harry Potter.
I'm a South African Dark Angel fan and a devoted M/A shipper. I've also recently discovered the wonderfulness of the Dr/Hr ship of Harry Potter.

OK folks, listen up.
No more fanfiction will be coming from me ever again.
I was 14/15 when I started and now I'm legal.
I can safely say that my life's direction has signicantly altered since then. Whatever limited writing ability I once possessed has now practically vanished. I'm sorry I left so many incomplete stories but if the words refuse to flow, I can't wring them out.

Thanks to those who supported and encouraged me: Your goodwill was highly appreciated and I wish you all the very best.

In addition to the stories listed below, Little Fella and I had begun writing "Big Brother:Dark Angel" under the pen name Little Goddess:
http://www.fanfiction.net/read.php?storyid=852511
Though not the most original of titles, it's a manifestation of our hatred of that pathetic reality show, while portraying our beloved Dark Angel characters in a less-than-flattering manner. I really regret that we were unable to complete this one. Sigh... writing with a touch of wickedness is so liberating! Though it escapes me as to how - after bashing Logan horrendously - we got so many reviews asking us if Max and Logan would get together.

Cheers
