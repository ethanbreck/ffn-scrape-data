Hey ho all. It really is a long time since I last updated this but then I havn't been posting much stuff in a while. At the moment I have six stories on line one of which has been recently published.

Update 30/01/07 Right I am sorry I have been so long to update The Ultimate Pokemon Master- quite far indeed from my own pronouncement of weekly indeed. I should have the third chater of this up soon. I might also post up a new story soon which would mark more of a return to my normal style of writing.

Completed Stories

The Androids Perspective- (Dragonball Z)- My only completed story. I suppose this story is one that I am most proud of though it is in urgent need of a re-write as not only are there some grammar and spelling issues but my idea's for my main character shifted as I wrote and there are a number of plot holes and pointless sidetracks from the main narritive. However if you like an AU saga with plenty of action go on ahead.

Likely to finish

Do Mews make good fighters?- (Pokemon)- My first ever story posted here. I blame youth for the fact that the first couple of chapters are a bit Mary Sueish but (at least in my mind) it does get better and other than a bit of needless overcomplication in the science bits I feel it is okay and at least in my eyes fairly origonal. Also in need of extensive re-write as is anything which is five years old. Again fairly action based with appearences in the first half from Ash and co. Good possibility of finishing one day- I know how the rest of the story goes- I just have to decide if a re-write is more important or not.

The Ultimate Pokemon Master- (Pokemon)- Something which had been buzzing about my mind for a while and I eventually decided to put finger to keyboard. Probably least likely of any of my stories to contain errors and the most paragraph friendly. This story marks a bit of change in my writing as it is not an extended saga of epic proportions and it is likely to be finshed in a couple of chapters time. Probably will be updated on a weekly basis.

Unlikely ever to get back to

The legand of the Sayain Neophalie- (Dragonball Z) The prequel to "The Androids Perspective" and as such one that does not include any of the Z fighters though Freezia will probably make an appearence. I think this is as much as I will probably do and only someone who is interested in "The Androids Perspective" would probably enjoy this. Again mainly action based.

Wars of the Ancient Sayain- (Dragonball Z) As it is based a couple hundred years before Dragonball Z it is unlikely to contain any of its characters. In my mind it isn't a bad first chapter but due to my own lack of interest in this project (mirrored by the fanfiction community) it is unlikely to be ever finished. An action based first chapter.

The ninth child- but not a Digi-destined!- (Digimon) Written about the same time as "The Androids Perspective" and "Do Mews make good fighters?" I feel that it might actually have been better written than the opening chapters of either but I am aware now it might fit into the bill of girl from a bad background style of Mary sue. Readers may also notice striking similarities between her and two of my other characters (but as no one reader has ever read more than one of my stories this has luckily never been commented on). I can only say I was experimenting my a particular character in my mind and each of the three stories are interesting at least to me as they mark the different ways she could have developed. Any way due to the contraints of the Digimon universe which I lost interest in I was unable to continue this and likely never will.

Anyroad if interested please take a look.
