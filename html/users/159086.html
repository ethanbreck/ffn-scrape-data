Profile
Greetings. My name is Dave and I'm pushing 30 at the moment. I haven't been posting stuff in a while and it's been mostly due to a severe case of writer's block. In the meantime, I write a blog, post YouTube stuff, and do other stuff to entertainment, like read books and play games...

Continuity Within the Ranger fics:
Generally, most of the Power Ranger fics I wrote share a continuity among them, so what might happen in one fic could be referenced or affect the events of another. This is especially evident in a trilogy of fics that I wrote between 2003 and 2005. Despite the existence of a shared "fanon" among the written works, it shouldn't be cause for concern as each of these stories could be read and enjoyed individually without having read anything prior.

Current Status: June 21, 2012
Noticed that FF.Net changed their layout and even allows for story covers. Uploaded a couple old fic covers for the sole trilogy. Meanwhile, things have kept me busy, but I'm slowly working on updating all the old stuff... and who knows? Maybe something new will pop up... that's a very huge maybe.
