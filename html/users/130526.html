What's next, ship lists? I'll spare you the agony of spouting that off for every fandom, and just hit the ones that I ship strange things in.
HP: Any of the Trio-era Slytherins, any combination; particularly fond of Crabbe/Millicent. Viktor/anyone, though I must say Viktor/Fleur tickles my fancy even if I can't ever find any. As for the adults, Lockhart/Skeeter trumps all. Tell me it's not the perfect match made in hell. Also, Peter/anyone, because he was a Marauder too.
YGO: Pegasus/anyone. No lie. (I'm the one-person army for Pegasus/Pandora. YES, YOU READ THAT RIGHT.) Also have a soft spot for Shadi/Isis, Honda/anyone (but particularly Honda/Jou), most minor character ships, and any combination taken from Yugi/Anzu/Seto/Ryou. Thank TechnicolorNina for that last one.
Cats: I know, it'd be kind of pointless to list stuff here, but I just wanted to ask - am I the only one who ships Bustopher/Jenny? I've noticed that Skimble/Jenny seems to be regarded as canon, and furthermore that it's a bad thing to ship them with anyone but each other. I'm curious.
Batman: ...I am seriously not going to make that list. I ship almost anything. I say 'almost' because Joker is really hard to ship at all. But seriously, it'd take forever. Riddler/Two-Face is among them, though I'd like to see it done outside of Schumacher-verse, and that's all I'll say. Although can I put out a friendship? Because after reading Gotham Underground, I'd like to see Riddler/Penguin friendship. Also, this is one of those fandoms where I'm more okay than most with OCs and thus OC/canon pairings. It's a great way to explore the canons, really, in this instance! (And, uh, in other news, Gotham could use more ladies.)
House: I'll get this out of the way - I like House/Cameron. And House/Wilson. And Wilson/Cameron. And, you know what, House/Wilson/Cameron. Also, Thirteen/anyone, Lucas/anyone, and - blast my love of tragic ships - Taub/Kutner. (Though I shipped it before 5x20, mind you. I just have awful luck with these things.)
Teen Titans: Well, I've written Starfire/Red Star, so I mean, that's a thing. Been a thing since I was 15, let's be real. Also I have 99 feelings and Speedy/Aqualad is at fault for most of them. And I will read Cyborg or Bumblebee with just about anyone because holy crap they need love.
G Gundam: I am so grateful that I found a fandom where my otp is canon, and just about everyone is on board. So, bless you, Argo/Nastasha. This makes up for the thing that may get me skewered, i.e. you will take Chibodee's big dumb crush on Domon from my cold dead fists.
