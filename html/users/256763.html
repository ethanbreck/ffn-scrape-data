-- about me --
I am an internet junkie. I am aged 13 and I am a sardonic perfectionist. I am talkative. I sound and talk like my cousin Ashley, which is a bit weird. I am also an artist, at least, I consider myself one. I am a writer as well, but I draw more than I write. I love literature, and I aspire to work in the field of Law when I am older. But anyway, I am also a self-claimed insane anime and manga lover, with a taste for a wide-range of music. Yes, I enjoy classical. No, I do not play an instrument, but just so you know, I played piano when I was about nine... then I quit after two years and now I lost everything I've learned.

The quote by my father -- "When you get older, you get worse," is true for me. I used to be fluent in Cantonese, but now I barely know about my mother language anymore. I am quite advanced in English however, and I have a wide vocabulary. Not that I'm bragging (FYI, again, I tend to brag, however). Sometimes I can be annoying. The way I laugh can be annoying, and the way I wax poetic can be annoying too. I am violent, no doubt about it... I usually kick my male friends every single day. And it gains them the usual bruise or so. I have an aggressive nature. And no, I'm not angelic either. Inside, I'm a badass, like it or not. I talk back, I use curses way too often for my age, and I have a very short temper. I slam the door, throw expensive objects at my wall, and I stomp my foot. Otherwise, I am a child. But I can be mature by will.
