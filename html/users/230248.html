Greetings to all! Welcome to my fanfiction Author page, version 4.0. My name is Angel and I've had this profile since I was 12. For years, I've adored the Artemis Fowl, Harry Potter, Terry Brooks, and Young Wizards series. Most of my fanfiction was written when I was 13-16 years old and is therefore not very good! If you're in the mood to read some fairly simple things with fun (I hope) concepts, then by all means! Please feel free!

Although constructive criticism is ALWAYS appreciated, I would ask that you reserve it for anything I put up after 2017 simply because my writing has improved quite a bit in fifteen years! It is, of course, completely up to you. (Psst. Compliments give me warm fuzzies, too!)

Someday, I may upload stories again, but for now this remains up as a vaguely embarrassing but fun homage to the person I was in my early teenage years.

That's all for now! Thanks for reading and I hope you have a great life.
