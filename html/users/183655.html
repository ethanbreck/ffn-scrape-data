Hey! :)!

Sorry, loves, I've been sorta in a slump for the last...couple...months. Yeah, and I apologize. I guess you could say I've put myself on hiatus, because I've been busy doing a whole lot of nothing these days, and don't have the strength nor to conviction to continue writing.

I sincerely hope that no one is too disappointed to my scheduled disappearance, but not to worry, loves, I have not yet lost faith in Lolly and therefore you should not either. Truthfully, there a lot of other great fics out there waiting to be read, and moping over one that is temporarily discontinued is, (though I am guilty of having done exactly this so many times so I am in no place to judge) a waste of your energy, and I do not want that.

There. Enough with the mush. Bleuarghh...

I do have most of the next chapter for Genie ready, but it's supposed to be this humorous situation, and I can't seem to make it work the way I want to...sorry...

Other stuff, like revisions and new chapters, will...I dunno...I guess we'll have to wait and see. No, I'm NOT depressed and doing self-mutilation.

I suppose you can blame this, in a way, to the publication of the fifth HP book, yes that one, to which I lost lots of inspiration. Don't ask me why or how, but currently I am obsessed with Harry Potter and these fics are on the cupboard, gathering dust.

The featherduster is coming soon, don't worry loves, I just have to drop by the shop first..

Much love and patience and apologies and comfort hugs,
-SPL

P.S. Go treat yourself to some ice cream and tv for listening to my inane ramblings. With Lolly's blessing.

Oh, and g'luck with the new school year!! (As if y'all need it, heh heh)
