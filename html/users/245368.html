Hi, everyone. As I'm sure you've noticed, I haven't updated any of my stories in quite a while. Most of them I wrote when I was thirteen or fourteen and as I've matured my interests, tastes and writing style have changed. I still enjoy reading Harry Potter but I no longer have the time or inclination to finish stories that I began years ago. Everytime I glance through them I cringe at the terrible writing style and simplistic plotlines that I used, but I've decided not to delete them because some people do enjoy reading them.

Thankyou very much to all those people who reviewed my stories, or had me on their Author Alert and Favourite lists. I'm glad my writings entertained you and that you took the time to say so.

-Dizzy Flower
