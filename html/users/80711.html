Hi fam!!! Long time no see!

After the second--er, third--make that fourth--(dare I say, "fifth"?) longest hiatus known to man, "Chronologically Speaking..." is finally nearing its end. I can't believe it! Around that time I was writing the conclusion, I started dabbling in "Temperature" which started taking on a life of its own. I still would like to exercise my description muscle going forward since I feel that's what I'm primarily lacking even though all of the teachers of good writing insist that "less is more." Still, I would like to thank every single person who reads any of my written work and even takes the time to give me feedback (esp. the blind fandom ones! whoa!). It is sincerely appreciated.

In the future, there will be updates to improve the flow, grammar and literacy of most of my published and unpublished fic on FF.net. I'm starting to look into the world of beta-ing because thanks to @Hoverbike Girl, I enjoy getting feedback to help me articulate! As a married teacher, you can imagine that my life has been quite busy as of late...so I will try to complete as many of my cliffhangers as I possibly can over the course of the next couple of years. They've been stagnant for far too long!

So, yes. In a nutshell, I am a nerdy long-time SonAmy dork who writes for fun (even though she struggles a lot with gettin' it just right), and loves to read the works and impressions of others for a variety of fandom as you may tell from my favs and fics below. Yay.

Respectfully yours,
Kazuhira "Kaz" M.
