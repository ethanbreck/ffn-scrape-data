02/2013 Update:

I started writing FanFiction when I was about 13. My stories are around 10 years old now and unfortunately incomplete. As I look back at them, I realized how much I've grown from 11 years ago as a writer and as a person. My stories I posted as a "kid" are not the greatest (Oh, to be a little teenager again with terrible grammar! I say this sarcastically.), but I'm hoping to start back up again with better structure and storylines.

I ship:

Trish/Jeff, Stacy/Randy, and Torrie/John. I've been getting more into the "Jorrie" and "Rancy" stories lately. I miss the good ol' days of wrestling. I honestly have not kept up with the shows since around 2006. Though, I still love reading new stories with these couplings. It's just a great way to bring back that feeling of nostalgia. The more realistic, the better.

Thank you to the other authors and readers out there for your kind words and encouragement in the past. Some of you may no longer be members, but if you happen to randomly stumble upon my profile again, thank you.
