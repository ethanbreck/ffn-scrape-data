Pull it's tail out, claws out and" she's making the noise of a squeaking door. Staying true to her ff.net username: Psychosane. She's a bit Psycho after she's had a ton of sugar and not a lot of sleep and sane when she's caught up on sleep and off sugar high. One of my oft-used saying is in use here, "Moony, you worry me sometimes." To which she laughs and than says "Thank you." Somehow, when she acts like that, and makes comment's like that, it's a sign for me to take away the sugar. (screams in the background, "It's my sugar. No. Mine, all mine. NO!) Don't mind that. This is completely normal. Just give her a few hours and she'll be back to normal. That and give her a cup of coffee with milk, with the sugar and creamer where she can't reach it. How she associated a shower scrubber on a stick with a cat on a stick is beyond me. Except that she owns one very crazy cat named Pikachu or Chu for short and I assure you that cat is NUTS. I've met a ton of cats and Chu is by far the craziest. Make that the only crazy cat. The rest are pretty normal by cat standards.

I'm back. Reads above "Prongs?" "Yes, Moony?" "What happened? What did I do?" "Nothing, Moony. Nothing yet. Here's some coffee with milk." "Mel, what happened to the sugar?"
