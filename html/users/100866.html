Author has written 12 stories for Harry Potter.

Hello! My name is Felicite, I'm returning to writing fanfiction after a 10 year or so hiatus. Most of the stories here were written when I was a teenager, so they are quite different from what I write now.

All stories written in 2014 or later are epilogue compliant and take place within the same universe. Stories written before that are compliant through Order of the Phoenix or so, but for the most part didn't end up diverging too much from canon (I do not have a wild imagination).

A brief chronology:

Reparations: Takes place from February 2003 - to June 2004, epilogue in July 2007

The Ring: January - June 2005

Incendio: July - August 2015

Forever: June 2018 (it is the future, so everyone is on a hoverboard broomstick)

Please enjoy, and either way, let me know what you think!
