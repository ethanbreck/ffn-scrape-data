Frequently Asked Questions:
Q) Why do you write only Usagi Centered Fanfiction? There are other senshi out there too, you know.
A) She's my prefered character.

Q) Usagi and Mamoru belong together, why do you constantly have them apart? Don't you know it's destiny to have them together?
A) I don't know if you think that I only saw one episode of Sailor Moon, but why would you bring that up? Of course I know who she is supposed to be with. I have nothing against those two as a couple. I think their relationship is beautiful and deeply spiritual thing, but I have a right to do as I please with who ever I want in my own story. I'm not trying to write a Sailor Moon Story Arc or a new season. I think that the show spelled out their romance beautifully; they don't need me to do it again. I could never do their relationship justice therefore I content myself with focusing on the 'might have been' instead of the 'should have been' or 'what is'.

Q) Why do you take so long to update! I'm going mad waiting for you to update!
A) I have no excuse now other than pure lazyness. Or the fact that I often have sever writers block.

Feel free to pm me with any questions or comments that you may have.
