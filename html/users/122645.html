Author has written 5 stories for Superman, Avengers, Blue Bloods, Captain America, and James Bond.

Still trying to figure out this writing thing.

I love fan fic, hate 90% of authors notes, and still believe if you write long enough you'll get a feel for it.

If you want to reach out, feel free to kik me at the same username as on here.

Current Series:

Sunday Dinner Partners [Blue Bloods] - We always see the partners of Jamie and Danny refer to the in/famous Reagan Sunday Dinners, but surely they've all gotten dragged to the table at least once, right? Well I've always wanted to see that, so I guess I'm gonna have to write it.

Beacon - My original inspiration for Beacon was the excitement for Man of Steel as it was coming out, and several of the trailers in particular, which is why I think my version and MoS both have Lois's trek to discover Supes before Texas he big reveal. However, I also wanted to give a Superman story where Lois was not the damsel in distress, but Clark's anchor in the world as he faces the growing threats while learning to be Superman.

The Apex of Vengeance [James Bond] - A female James Bond? Because why not? Of course I start to write this, and then I see Atomic Blonde ads and Dr. Who is a woman now as well. Oh well, something different. James is sent out on a first mission, and it goes wrong. -OR- How James got her 007.

Completed Series:

Black Widow: Widow Bite - So after months of thinking about this, and days of plot charting and procrastinating, I have begun writing Black Widow: Widow Bite. "But Ta', you lazy fuck, you still need to finish Beacon!" you say. You're right! (See below). Anyways, I've felt that Natasha doesn't get enough of her own stories, and while Iron Man 2, Cap2, and Avengers 1 could be loosely retitled as BW movies, she still needs her own billing. Plus, I like origin stories, and while the MCU has given lots of hints about her past, and the comics give more, they don't mesh well in my opinion. So this is my attempt to give both a story where Nat is top billed, and gives more of an origin story for the MCU version.
