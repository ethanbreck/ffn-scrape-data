Author has written 56 stories for Doctor Who.

Hello, everyone! I've been writing fanfiction for a long time and I can honestly say that over the years my abilities as a writer have gotten better each year. No small part of that growth has been the support of my amazing readers. As a writer, I can best be described as a plot person. I plan stories and series out far in advance in order to build all the little aspects into the story that lead to a big payoff.

Right now I am focused on the Guardians of the Universe series which is a major project which will take years to complete. This series follows the creation of an alternate reality rather than just dropping into it which is a bit different for Doctor Who fanfiction. This series is focused on Rose as she is the epicenter of the changes made to the timeline and the being causing this change are the Black and White Guardians from Classic Who. I am currently working on the eighth story and fifth full season of thirteen episodes. If you are a fan of Classic Who then I'll recommend my own series to you as there are many Classic Who elements and characters throughout the series.

If you enjoy my fanfiction then I ask you to consider picking up my original fiction. You can find my current series: The Iron Soul series by J.M. Briggs online or order it through major retailers.

Series Guide

Guardians of the Universe: The Blonde Girl (first), Defender of the Earth (second), The Companion Connection (third), Time and Trickster (fourth), The Tyler Factor (fifth), Under the Mistletoe Moon (Sixth), Child of Earth (Seventh), and Travels in Time and Space (Eighth). Additional material in The Bits in Between and Glimpses of the Future.

Wolf Pact: Wolf Signal (first) and Wolf Pact (second)

Legacy of the Bad Wolf: Turn Your Back, Look Away and Blink (first) and Survivors of Gallifrey (second)

Timey Wimey... Stuff: To the Past From the Future (first), Extreme Time Twist (second), Building Blocks (third), Sugar Rush (fourth), Twin Time (fifth), Human Holidays (sixth) and Since We're Here (seventh) This is a fluff series with plenty of Doctor/Rose and kids, but a lot of complicated time travel issues.
