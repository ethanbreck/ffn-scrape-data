Happy New Year!

Posted a story named One Shot (it's actually a repost with a different name). 11 chapters in all. Most of the chapters are shorter than I'm used to posting, with two chapters longer than most but that's the way it worked out.

Been working extensively on editing processes since I last updated this profile; One Shot is the test to see if I was successful. My next bit will be working on the novel (promise!).

A little about Me

32 year old Receiver working in retail. Because it's cheaper I still live at home with my mother and my sister. We each have a cat to call our own: mine is a 13 year old big baby by the name of Rascal. Lazy as fuck but a sweetheart nonetheless. I've been on this site since I was 16 years old, so about half my life now that I think about it.

I used to write Dragon Ball Z fiction but now I focus mostly on Final Fantasy VIII.

Contact me

My email address is queen.triplezero@gmail.com. Feel free to drop me a line, be it about the progress of a story you’ve been reading, or to just say hi.
