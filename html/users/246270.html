Quick Note on The Adventures of Emma Davis and Emma Davis herself: Emma is a chicken. She's perfectly content with living her life normally and however she enjoys it and she's not looking to change that. As such, she doesn't go looking for a lot of information (accepting that it happened and she knows she probably doesn't want to know how or why). I'm saying this because I feel it's going to come up as the story goes on. Since the series is from Emma's point of view, you miss out a lot on information - particularly because Emma feels no need to know. To her, Dean brings a lot of problems (some she hasn't even realised yet). While she'll do whatever needs to be done if she feels there's no other choice, but often she'd really just prefer to stick her head in the ground, plug her ears and sing "la la la la can't hear you..."

Hope you enjoy!
