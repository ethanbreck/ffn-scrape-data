An Excuse

Most of what little fans and friends I had have probably given up on me. But just in case anybody is reading this, I will give the explanation for my sudden and prolonged disappearance.

Linda Heidel, my grandmother (who you would know as mommawolf, my biggest fan) died in January of the year 2005. I had a very deep connection with my grandma, and her death hurt me greatly. Once it happened, I began to pull away from my friends, my hobbies, my interests. I was trying to make a new life for myself, both offline and on.

I quit MSN, I quit IRC, I quit all of my usual online haunts. Most of them are now gone. Servers have gone down, buddy lists have been wiped due to inactivity.
