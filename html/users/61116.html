Howdy all! You've reached the ff.net profile of Marie Kenobi. Sorry I can't come to the keyboard at the moment, but drop me a line and I'll hit you back first chance I get. :-)

A little about me:

I'm 23 years old and I love horses. I own a 25 year old bay Thoroughbred mare who raced in New York. She's retired now for the most part. I also adopted another ex-racer in 2006. She's a 10 year old chestnut Thoroughbred mare in retraining as a hunter/pleasure mount.

I recently graduated from the University of Georgia with a degree in Linguistics. I love languages and have studied Japanese, ASL, and Spanish. Someday I want to be published and I would be thrilled to see my characters come to life on the big screen.

Favorite TV shows are: BBC Robin Hood, Horatio Hornblower, Charmed, Will & Grace, Buffy, Dirty Jobs, Ghost Hunters/TAPS, and American Pickers.

Favorite animes are: Ronin Warriors, Escaflowne, Rurouni Kenshin, Cardcaptor Sakura, and Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicles.

I enjoy delving into the creative worlds of Peter Pan and anything Robin Hood. I have read the Twilight series and I enjoyed it. My childhood loves were Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and My Little Pony.

Favorite movies are: Lord of the Rings, Star Wars, Seabiscuit, Memoirs of a Geisha, The Chronicles of Narnia, King Arthur, Gladiator, Troy, Batman Forever, The Dark Knight, The Covenant, Speed Racer, Tuck Everlasting, Transformers, Avatar, A Knight's Tale, and CRASH to name just a tiny few.

Fictional characters you will most likely find me reading about: Obi-Wan Kenobi, Legolas, Aragorn, Ryo, Rowen/Touma, Van (Escaflowne), Sakura and Syaoran, Kenshin, Arthur, Lancelot, Peter (Pan), Robin (Hood), and Speed.

Favorite actors: Ewan McGregor, Orlando Bloom, Ioan Gruffund, Eric Bana, Heath Ledger, Robin Williams, Will Smith (and that's all I can think of at the moment).

Favorite comedian: Jeff Dunham "Achmed: How do you spell that?" "Ah, well let's see. A. C. ...Phlegm."

Greatest dislike about ff.net or fanfiction writers: When they TELL you how many reviews they expect to get for a chapter. Nothing turns me off a story quite so quickly as that.
