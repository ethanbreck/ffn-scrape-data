~Stories I've Written~

Predictability

Lily Evans, in her seventh year at Hogwarts, comes to terms with life, love, and herself, deals with hardships with her own special brand of humor and sarcasm, and learns a few valuable lessons along the way. James Potter, in his senior year at Hogwarts as well, slowly begins to understand life and it's expectations, the way the world spins, and the weight of the heavy decisions on his shoulders. The two share laughter, tears, hopes, friendships, and obstacles thrown in their paths as their lives become imperceptively entwined together. View life from their eyes, their best moments, their not-so-great moments, and somewhere inbetween, you'll find the subtle charm in the... predictability of it all. My personal favorite, so why don't you give it a look?

Perfect Prefects... Or Not During their sixth year at Hogwarts, Lily and James work together as Prefects on a project involving business skills in the muggle world. Conflicting views of theirs both bring business up and down... Perhaps focusing on something else will help? Like, for instance, the bizarre powers Lily is capable of? Mmm. Not my best work, I can tell you that much.

A Christmas Carol An interesting rewrite of Dickens's Christmas classic. The stingy, lonely, twenty-one year old James Potter is visited by three spirits on Christmas Eve, in hopes to redeem his soul. He is forced to view himself as a child, always unwanted at home, when Lily left him on Christmas Eve just two years ago, how his secretary spends her Christmas, and the inevitable death he will meet, to name a few things. And then, on Christmas morning, what sort of lasting effect will this lay upon him? Mmmmmmmmmmffffff... I don't know about this one. Not bad, I suppose. People have said that I combined J.K. Rowling and Charles Dickens's styles perfectly. Decide for yourself.

~A Little Ditty About Wonderful, Magical Me~

Er... hello. I'm a rather boring person, now that I think of it, who discovered her true calling to the world of writing somewhere during a phase of some reallyhorrid poetry... Looking back at some of my other fics, there's probably some really cruddy writing in general, besides just the utter cringe-worthiness of some of them. Besides that, I'm just your average struggling writer (yes, I'm struggling...but perhaps not in the classic Dickens sense... more like struggling to survive getting to theage of twenty, what with the pratfalls of the teenage years and all that jazz...) and am constantly writing random ficlets of L/J fluff that I don't commonly put up here as I just write them when I suffer from Predictability writer's block... Like that doesn't happen very often. ;) So... Excluding all extraneous/personal information, whatever you managed to gather from that rambling, run-on sentence is pretty much all there is to know. Oh, and I support the following ships: Harry/Ginny, Draco/Ginny, Ron/Hermione, Lily/James, and Snape/Sinistra, though I'm willing to read pretty much anything as long as it's well written.

Anyway, lots of love always,
