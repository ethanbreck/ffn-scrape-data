It has come to our attention that the English language is lacking in an important category: differentiation between types of love. Greek's totally got us beat on that one. To rectify this, we decided we needed a word to mean "the love of a reader for an author, inspired by their writing." So that when you walk up to someone at a con and say "I (word) you", they'll be flattered instead of creeped out. Lightbulby came up with "ent". See your favorite fanfic author at a convention? You can run up and say "I ent you!" It evokes love of Tolkien, and it translates well to chatspeak. Someone on LJ comes off with something you adore, you can now just replay with "ZOMG IN2!" or, less urgently, "I am filled with enty goodness just for you." or just "ENT." Pass it on. :-D
