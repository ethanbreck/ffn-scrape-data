Hmmm...what to write, what to write...well, lets start with the basics. I'm fifteen...but I have been mistaken for a 25 year old at a casino(long story)! Um, well, I play the saxaphone, love to draw and paint and read and write. I love anything that has to do with Harry Potter (my mom threatned to ground me for reading so many Harry Potter fanfics on the web!), Shakespeare(I use Shadespearean in my fic! He proves that love is over-rated), Phillip Pullman, and just about any other books I can fit into my busy day to read.

I don't really like the whole Ron/Hermione or Harry/Hermione thing, but anything else is cool, like Draco/Hermione or George/Hermione. Oh, and after seeing the HP movie, I decided to nick-name Hermione 'Mya'...you know because they say Her-my-o-knee, and when they say the middle part I thought it sounded like 'Mya'...so there you go! I also think it makes more sence than 'Herm' or 'Mione'...they look good, but no...not in my story...and I really think that Ginny/anyone is stupid and pointless. She's proved that she's a flat character and has no depth what-so-ever.

My first story is THE ENEMY...OR THE FRIEND?, a Draco/Hermione fic. It's the same Head Boy/Girl scene with a little bit of my own crazyness thrown into it. Hehehe. My second story is BEFORE SHE LEARNED TO TRUST, and it's about Hermione (another Draco/Hermione ficlet) and Draco and a ghost-Lady Grainne-and a spell that involved Lady Graninne and someone else...you'll just have to read it. Hehehe.

*Lol, I should probably put up some sort of excuse as to why I haven't been writing lately...well, since April, really. If you saw my Friend/Enemy update, it basiclly said that I just moved to another state about 1,000 miles away and have tons of school work and marching band going on. But now that that's over and I'm about as settled in as you can get after 3 or 4 months, than I will try and get another chapter out ASAP!!! Not to worry, I haven't abondned my stories, or you for that matter!
Much mahalo as always for your support!
