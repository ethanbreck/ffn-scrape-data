Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter.

Just for the record, I'm completely addicted to Harry Potter Fan fiction. I love anything that is well written, but have found that the more obscure pairings of HG/DM and HG/SS have attracted some wonderful writers. To me, its not about believability, because a good writer can make you believe anything is natural and possible.

I have only two stories of my own - mainly for my own education and entertainment. The first, Christmas Kisses, came about slowly, picking up an idea here or there, and I can't say I am very proud of it, though I don't have the heart to take it down either. After that one dismal attempt, I read fanfiction religiously for a few years until one day an idea entered my head.. and as some ideas do, it wouldn't leave me alone.. so I put fingers to keyboard and attempted my second story, which would later become Clash of her worlds. The story started out with no point, not a lot of plot, and a really strange idea that Hermione was really trapped between two worlds. The idea grew on me the further along I went, until it seemed to me that everyone else must have been blind not to see how the poor thing suffered as neither a fully recognised witch, nor a full muggle. Writing has never been one of my strongest skills, as an oh-so-kind english teacher pointed out to me at one stage, though I love reading. This story has given me the opportunity to explore writing and hopefully improve my skills a little, though I can't say I'm very good at it! Hopefully, for those poor souls who are courageously battling through my epic story, I am improving slowly!
