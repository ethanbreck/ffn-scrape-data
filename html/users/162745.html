~May 13th, 2008~ Wow. It's sad when I need to post a year behind my date. I haven't updated in...ages... To be honest, fanfiction has been kind of dead to me this past year. I've been so preoccupied with college that it just hasn't been my top priority, and I apoligize sincerely for it. It can be maddening when people like me write stories..then just abandon them. :p My last final of this semester is tomorrow and it is driving me up the wall, so of course I needed something to occupy myself with other than biology. I'm excited to get writing again, even if it's just for the summer. Hope to get writing as soon as Friday. We'll see what happens from there. :)
