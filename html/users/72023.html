I love the Harry Potter, so I therefore love any author who writes HP fanfics.

Do you want to know about me? Well, I'm 23 years old, and I go to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. No exciting news as of yet. I spent some time in England, went to Cambridge for a while and did some work in English and Neuroscience. Yeah, I admit, I'm sort of a dork, but don't let that get out. It might hurt my reputation. (: I'm a history major now - a bit of a change, I know, but I enjoy it. Just don't ask me what I'm going to do with my major, I have no clue. Alright, this has been fun. Feel free to say hi anytime. I love emails too!
