About the Crazy Author Type

My name's Jen, formerly known as Jennu. I'm 19, going to be a sophomore at NYU this year as an English major and not much direction in life. Yes, I want to be an author, but something has to pay the bills in the meantime. So naturally, I take English classes at a prestigious private school. Only reasonable thing to do, amirite?

Um. I liek writing, reading, video games, pie, and cats. And my boyfriend, who is just a bit more high-maintenance than cats. Damnit.

I don't really write fanfics anymore, I'm usually found skulking around on my Fictionpress. Which is not updated frequently either, but I run more to write poems than fics nowadays. Fanfics are fun, just not really getting a writing career / the rest of my freaking life anywhere.

Oh, I like music a lot too. My favorite bands are, in somewhat particular order (though dwindling as we get further down the list): Tool, Agalloch, Muse, Rammstein, VNV Nation, Murder by Death, Opeth, Woods of Ypres, Isis, Katatonia, Dredg, A Perfect Circle, Radiohead, Nine Inch Nails, Explosions in the Sky, Red Sparowes, Sigur Ros, Eluvium, Claire Voyant, Jesu, Porcupine Tree, Mesh, Coheed & Cambria, Stabbing Westward ... god, the list goes on. You get the idea.

All my contact info is at my Fictionpress site, which I encourage you to visit to see what my most recent writing is like outside of fandom. I like it and you should too:3
