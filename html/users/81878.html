Short version: Hi, I'm a medical school student who writes fanfiction in her free time (which is supposed to be 'none') but in-between trying to learn how to save lives and trying to digest as much knowledge from books and algorithms, I do get the occasional plot bunny that refuses to leave. Thus, I try to ressurect myself in this cyber world of a million and one fandoms by writing. Hello!

lllll

I ended up not deleting most of my old stories. I tried, and I was able to delete I think three or four, but as I went through them again and realized I liked them lingering there, laughing at me and reminding me what a dorky teenager I was, writing fanfics with a lot of ellipses during my free time. Of course, at twenty-five, I am still dorky and I still write the occasional fanfic, though this time with lesser ellipses. I think that this, at least, is a testament to my improvement.

I'm still debating whether I should delete some of the "author's notes" in my older fics, because a tad of them are even more embarrassing than the many ellipses, but I'm not sure if my younger self would approve. I do feel, however, that it adds to the nostalgic (though admittedly humiliating) charm. Either way, if you read anything 2006 or older, consider yourself warned.

lllll

Recently, I do not know where to place myself in this larger internet world with a million and one fandoms.

I admit that Zuko/Katara from Avatar: The Last Airbender has been my recent guilty pleasure, however. And oh, Taichi/Chihaya from Chihayafuru. I'm also very much into Detective Conan and Magic Kaitou.

As you can see, I have a penchant for pairings that don't end up together. It happens all the time. (I know Chihayafuru isn't finished yet, but the expectation is deep-set in my bones. If Taichi ends up with Chihaya, my entire life as the woman who ships pairings that never end up together will have been a lie. Of course, I would rather have that than them not ending up together!)

lllll

The hardest part of this all is that since I am practicing to write again, I feel like my old style has abandoned me. So until I grasp that stage where I'm comfortable with with what I write, please bear with whatever I release on this site.

lllll
