Hello everyone! I'm an 18-year-old female who is still in love with Yuugiou as much as ever despite the fact that it ended...(sniffle, pout). I'm a yaoi AND yuri fangirl, and puzzleshipping is my OTP! n_n I think I'm a rare breed of Yuugiou Fanatics, because my favorite character is Yuugi whereas everyone else seems to like Atemu or someone else more. XD (Atemu's still my second favvie, of course!)

There are also plenty of other pairings I like, though. Jounouchi and Yuugi make a cute couple, because I feel (at least, in most fanfics/artwork) that Kaiba treats Jounouchi like crap. And he's one of my favorite characters, he deserves better than that!

And...oh my gosh...I LIKE ANZU! She's not my favorite character, but she's still pretty cool. I think anyone would be lucky to have such a loyal friend like her! And before you start complaining about her friendship speeches (which I blame entirely on 4Kids), take a look at Yami no Yuugi's speeches and then I'll listen to anything you have to say against hers!

I doubt I'll be writing many more fanfics, if any. As much as I'd love to be a writer I just don't feel that's my talent area...I'm a much better musician. And anything I do write will probably just be a silly little one-shot I got a quick idea for.

If you do happen to read any of my old works, I think my fics follow the rule "better with experience" or some such thing. My earliest fics were the typical omg-I-want-to-smex-Yuugi-up-and-then-some, and I was tempted to take them down at one point recently, but I find it amusing to look back on them and see how much I've changed (hopefully for the better XD) now! I mean, Yuugiou's gotta be a pretty kick-ass show if it's held my extreme-bordering-on-obsessional interest for...what, going on 6 years now? n_n
