I am a female fanfiction author who's been writing fanfiction for over half of my life. Despite life and all the joys and obstacles that come along with it, I've somehow managed to keep up writing as my way of expressing the stories in my imagination. I was not quite fourteen when I joined this site in 2001. You do the math to figure out my age. :)

The fandom I write in is primarily Digimon Adventure (both series), though from my penname it's fairly obvious I started out as a Pokemon fanfic writer, and I still do write for this fandom... subtly. My efforts in writing in serious stories aim to keep it as close to canon as possible, though I have a habit of stretching pieces here and there to fit around what is set in stone. I do occasionally dabble in other fandoms, but Digimon is my main focus.

Other fandom interests include Star Wars, Final Fantasy series, Gundam Wing, Chrono Trigger/Cross, Legend of Zelda, Naruto, Fire Emblem, Bleach, Legend of Mana, Ouran High School Host Club, Fullmetal Alchemist (manga/Brotherhood), Silent Hill series, Tales series, The Elder Scrolls series, Fallout 3, Kingdom Hearts, Avatar: The Last Airbender, Wolf's Rain, Assassin's Creed series, Harry Potter, Dragon Age, Mass Effect trilogy, Borderlands, Puella Magi Madoka Magica, and How to Train your Dragon. I probably missed a couple in there.

I also count gaming, anime/manga, martial arts, and playing music as hobbies.

I have a fanfiction-themed blog! It contains such things as my current project thoughts, writing process, commentary on fanfiction in general, omakes, and occasional scenes, as well as thoughts on gaming, anime, and music. It's also updated relatively frequently (especially in comparison to my stories!) It's at mewgiaotaku.wordpress.com.
