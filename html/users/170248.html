Hello, my name is Syn. I love fanfiction (when I can find the time to write it). I mostly write for Angel and BtVS, but I've dabble in Firefly, Charlie's Angels (movies), Harry Potter and Dead Like Me.

Most of my work is shipper fic and I love UC couples. If its strange as hell, I'll be drawn to it like a fly on shit. Its a proven fact.

I love feedback by the by. So drop me a line if you like what I've done. Constructive criticism is always welcome and appreciated. I don't, however, like it when people try to tell me what to write. I'll write what I please, when I please, got it? If you don't like it, don't read it.

You know who you are.

NOTE: I don't always post stories on ff.net, either because of the rating restriction or because I don't think there's an audience for it. I post these stories on my personal site, (Bleeding in Prose) which is home to over a hundred stories. Drop by and see me some time.
