~waves~ Hello everyone, and welcome to my profile. My name is Eve, but here I go by my old online alias of Kolibri. I've recently started writing again after disappearing from the world of fiction for 9 years, and it feels good indeed to be back. My current muse and inspiration is Fenris, a character from the fantasy RPG Dragon Age 2. He seems to have stuck in my head for some reason and now I can't get him out, but I guess that is just as well because without him I wouldn't be writing. :)

I hope you're all currently enjoying my story Wolf Unchained, which seems to be growing at a rate that even I find alarming! I'd kindly suggest to steer clear of my earlier fiction, because those were stories written in a time of innocence and foolishness, along with bad grammar. :P

Thank you for stopping by!

- Eve aka Kolibri.
