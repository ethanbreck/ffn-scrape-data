For those of who actually wish to know about me, I'm flattered, and you may read on and satiate your curiosity. For those yawning already, I will not be offended if you stop know

I'm an average teenaged girl who loves anything Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, etc. but also anything literary. I'm not to great about posting things but I try. Really. The ideas just get quite comfortable in my head and don't want to come out. That, and the fact that "Enchanted Love" without my consent explored a bottle of superglue. Anyway, thank you so much for taking the time to read this, and any of my stories, if you do.

And now for some plugs...

If you want to read some really fantastic LOTR or POTC stories, check out the wonderful Gwyn (ID 87781). I confess I haven't read any of her LOTR stuff, but that's because I haven't even gotten around to the books yet, and that is the canon she uses. Her POTC, however, "To Watch Through Other Eyes," is truly splendiferous and feels just like a continuation of the film. Read it, it's well worth the effort.

For truly superb Harry Potter fanfiction, you must check out the extremely talented Katrina Littlebird (ID 270540), most especially "Bride of a Dragon" (which she is NOT allowed to take down) and "Magic of Love." She's written more, but I can't read them because I have yet to read the fifth book; I'm scared it will ruin any chance of the best ship ever, H/Hr. And no, nothing will convert me. And yes, I do love reading; at this point you must be thinking that I have an antipathy for book, but I'm just reading other things.

Okay, you're bored. I'm done rambling.

~ Violet Princess

P.S. I should add that I am a proud member of the all-exclusive Starkissed Writing Syndicate! : )
