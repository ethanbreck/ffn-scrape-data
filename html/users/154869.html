About me:

I am 26, and a graduate student with too much stuff to do and too little free time, so please forgive my infrequent updates. I've been a fan since 1998 and have been writing fan fiction since 1999 - there is a ER fic somewhere out there that I can't think about without wanting to hide... ER and Stargate Atlantis have been my most prolific fandoms so far, but I also occasionally write in other fandoms. I am a fannish butterfly, being a fan of ER, The X-Files, Due South, Starsky & Hutch, Wiseguy, Emergency, Pretender, From Eroica With Love, House, Bones, Sharpe (movies and novels), Torchwood, Lord of the Rings, Scrubs, MASH, Dexter, 21 Jump Street, Life on Mars, MacGyver, Homicide: Life on the Street, Smallville, Firefly, Horatio Hornblower, James Bond, Angel, Buffy, Stargate Atlantis, Stargate SG-1, Farscape, Jeeves & Wooster, Doctor Who, NCIS, Quantum Leap, Nash Bridges, CHiPs, the Seafort Saga novels, the Bolitho novels, Master & Commander, Miami Vice, Millennium, Professionals, Twin Peaks, Third Watch, The Sentinel, CSI, I Dream of Jeannie, Weißkreuz, Law and Order, Law and Order: SVU, Law and Order: CI, Police Rescue, Boy Meets Boy, etc... etc...

My previous pen names on ff.net were VjeraNadaLjubav and Zmija.

Want to talk with me about something? Check out my forums. I am happy to talk about my fics, and am ready to help you out with the foreign languages I know, or beta for you.
