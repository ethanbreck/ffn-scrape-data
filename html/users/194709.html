Author has written 6 stories for X-Men: The Movie, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Harry Potter, and Firefly.

As many of you who read and review my stories may have noticed, I haven't posted any new chapters for quite some time. I'm on an indefinite hiatus because my muse has up and left me. I don't think the good folks at ff.net will boot me off for having a long author name (29 letters) but if they do while I'm gone I'll have to sign up and post it all again. Thank you all for your support and reviews. I hope you'll all still be interested in my work when my muse comes back to me.

Sometimes I get really bored, usually late at night, and plot bunnies attacked and beat me over the head with their evil little bunny feet. Then I write things, things that tend to be a little weird. I like pairings that would most likely never happen in canon, like Rouge with Pyro (Xmovieverse) or Faith and Xander (Buffyverse). Also I lean more towards writing that falls into the M category on this site, but I do occasionally write kidfic (because it's fun and cute).
