Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter, Fullmetal Alchemist, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

Ok...so...I'm just kinda lazy...and yet so busy at the same time...hm...In a Doctor Who fix right now. Why did I not know about this before? It's amazing! Fantastic, even. I'm totally busy with my first AmeriManga, hoping to hit you up on the internet sometime this year. Waiting for my artist to finish it and then being hounded by said artist to finish the next script. Can't release anything more than that, I'm afraid, but I'll let you all know as soon as it's done. Also trying to finish anything I've ever written, which is much harder than it sounds, especially when you're just not motivated to do anything.

There's this thing called Wizard Rock and if you don't know what it is, look up The Remus Lupins or the Ministry of Magic on Myspace, they will blow your mind. They are bands that sing songs about Harry Potter and they are amazing. I had the honor to meet The Remus Lupins (funny story-I have no shame, so I asked them to go to dinner with me and Moonlit Eyes and now I'm totally in love with the lead singer)

So my best buddy and roomie, Moonlit Eyes has totally turned me on to Guns N' Handcuffs (That's Draco and Harry in case you didn't know) so I have some stories starting for that, plus I'm working on my ultimate fanfic for SakuraCon. As for my other stories...I don't know...I stare at the screen like this -- @.@ willing my mind to move and it...doesn't. It just kind of flops to the side and says "No, I don't think I'll work right now. I feel like having an attention disorder. What? Food? Yes!" or something like that.

On any sort of upside, I am now awesomely medicated which makes Matchbox very happy. And I thought that maybe you would like to know a bit about me, so here it is-

I like Harry Potter, reading, writing (and not just fanfics), I really really love Matchbox Twenty, but I think that's just a given with my name. I like dressing up for no good reason. I recently got into Doctor Who ( as stated before) and it's just about one of the most wonderful things on the face of the planet. I also love Pushing Daisies, and spawning from that ( Thanks to Lee Pace) my favorite movie is 'The Fall'. It's very wonderful and I highly suggest you check it out. I also love getting tattoos.

Anyway, feel free to hit me up with an e-mail at matchboxofmusic@yahoo.com . That would be fantastic.
