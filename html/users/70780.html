I am 17 years of age and I read and write slash. I am an obsessive Harry Potter, Stargate SG-1, and Smallville reader and I have found that I serious deprivation when I have not read a FanFiction on one of those seriesin any more than a few days. I also love to write poetry. It is the one thing that I have faithfully done for the last 6 years. Anything else tends to be an obsession for a few weeks, maybe a year or two, but so far nothing has lasted as long as my poetry writing. I feel I must warn you, though. Most of the poetry tends to be a bit dark.

As I said, I am an obsessive slash reader. My favorite Stargate SG-1 couple is Jack/Daniel and but I can't seem to decide on a favorite for Harry Potter. It seems to be a tie between Harry/Draco, Remus/Sirius, and Percy/Neville. I also have a newfound obssesion with Smallville. I absolutely love Clex (Clark/Lex).

I can't say that I never read Het, though, because I also have a rather wierd obsession with Hermione/Severus from the Harry Potter fandom and Buffy/Spike from the Buffy: The Vampire Slayer fandom. I am also know to read the ocassional Chlark or Chlex (Chloe/Clark or Chloe/Lex) from the Smallville fandom.
