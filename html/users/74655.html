Author has written 22 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Final Fantasy VII, Final Fantasy VIII, Final Fantasy IX, Final Fantasy X-2, and Star Ocean.

I'm Sorceress Myst! I'm also known around the net as Paine the Wicked and atecwriter. I'm a ten-year veteran of fanfic writing and have improved over the years. Like all new writers, I've written god-awful plots, Mary Sues, and turned characters into OOC hyperactive mental patients on crack. But, I kept writing and realized that I could do better. Now I don't have OCs running around as the hero and the few that are there are not perfect or hated. My plots are better, if not captivating (it's up to you to judge though :D) and I try to keep the characters IC based on what I've observed or read from the game/anime/novel. Go ahead and take a look, the newer the fic, the better written it is.

One thing I dislike most in fanfics are Alternate Universes (mostly High School fics) and I did try to write one myself to better understand the appeal of writing one. Even though I had a few chuckles with it, I still don't like High School fics and I don't think 'Aquios High' is one of my better works. But I'm glad others liked it. As I promised, I turned that fanfiction into an original novel called 'Euri High' and it has the true ending to the fanfiction. If you want to buy a copy (there are three volumes) you can find it here: my lulu.com page. LonelyCitadel has drawn the cover art for the last book.

However, I do love Alternate Realities. The difference between AUs and ARs is that in an AR, it's still the same universe, timeline, and characters, but there is something different about the original storyline. I'm more likely to write an AR or stand alone one-shots to experiment with a character's personality. Another thing about me is that I'm not against any pairings. I tend to like some canon pairings and sometimes I like crack pairings. I'm not against yaoi/yuri either, but it will be only implied in my stories.

Hopefully I didn't bore you with all that information :D
