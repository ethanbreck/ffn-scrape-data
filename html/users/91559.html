UPDATES:

5/26/2018: So when I began most of these projects, I was a functional human being who used to do rather too many things at once as a matter of preference. Now I'm chronically ill, permanently disabled, financially unstable, and a widow in my 30s, the latter as of this past March. I really do intend to keep writing, but I've been demoralized hardcore by just about everything, and I've also gotten next to no feedback in so long that I figure no one is reading my stuff anymore anyway.

Basically my life went to shit and now I've been away too long to feel like a writer anymore. But I'll keep trying. If you genuinely want to see more of any unfinished project, posted or listed below, please tell me directly; the morale boost would be amazing and immensely helpful.

PERSONAL INFO:

-I'm a lesbian English major/gamer/poet/former roller derby player, who parents a herd of seven highly adorable house-cats and one rather Machiavellian box turtle. I make custom character sheets and RPG charts for relaxation and enjoy making housing mods for PC Skyrim.

-I write (and read) het and slash both, though I'm usually in it for character development. I also adore femmeslash, but it's generally much harder to find it well-written. I don't ever write explicit stories, due to having no feel whatsoever for articulating sex scenes (they come out clinical and wooden, and are consequently very bad). I'm more of a UST/fade-to-black kind of girl.

-I cherish concrit. It is, in fact, immensely important for my writing process. Praise is nice, but please be harsh when it's warranted; I avow that while I'm not above making excuses and/or sadface in my less proud moments, I always give weight to criticism and try to use it to improve. When I ask for something, I actually do want it.

-It's a goal of mine to include all proper warnings so as to avoid triggering anyone—I am always grateful to be helped in this area.
