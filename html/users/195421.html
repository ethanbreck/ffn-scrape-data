I totally have no money though, and haven't bought any new tankoubans in ages. Woe is me.

I like most everything, and I enjoy how creatively free FFNet can be. Constructive criticism is most appreciated! How else do you expect to grow as a writer? I do not enjoy, however, the following here:
1. Flames
Seriously. Would you be that rude to someone in person? Flames do not do anyone any good, and all they really serve is to show how uneducated and close-minded the flamer is. Flames are obnoxious and useless and if you're really going to be that terrible, let me know and we can discuss in person. Let's see how brave you are once the anonymity of the internet no longer protects you.

2. Poor grammar and spelling
Not that I'm a grammar-nazi, and heaven knows I've committed errors before, but come on. Some things are stylistically intentional, and I understand that. Some posters are not native English speakers, and I definitely understand that. I'm talking about the basics here. Please understand, a good story can be totally undermined by awful grammar and misspellings. The flow of the story is bogged down when people aren't sure what you meant. Shoot, if you want a beta-reader, just let me know. I'd be more than happy to do it.

3. Mary-Sues
But hey, I just choose not to read them. If you like those kinds of stories, then go for it! I probably won't be writing any of those at any time...ever...
