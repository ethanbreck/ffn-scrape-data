Author has written 3 stories for Inuyasha, and Dragon Ball Z.

Real Name: Liz

Age: born in 1988. Easier than updating my age every year.

Interests: Writing, singing, acting, knitting, crocheting, sewing, swing dancing, reading, cosplaying

I've been reading but not doing much reviewing. Not much writing either. Trying to start up again, but being an adult is hard sometimes.

I like to read DBZ and Inuyasha fics. My favorite pairings are Bulma/Vegeta, Gohan/Videl, and Inuyasha/Kagome. If you make Videl part alien, I will not read your story. If you put the DBZ characters into high school, I will not read your story. Certain stories with Inuyasha characters in high school are okay. And if your spelling is atrocious, I will not read your story. The only ratings I read are T, and sometimes M. Another note on spelling, if you are old enough to write a lemon into your story, you are certainly old enough to know how to spell. Just putting that out there.

Apparently, you have to mention a fandom in your profile to actually be interested in it, so I would like to point out that I LOVE Hunger Games fanfiction! I pretty much only read the ones filling in the huge gap between Katniss going back to 12 and her and Peeta having babies. Suzanne Collins definitely left much to be desired there. I also read some alternate endings, like if the rebellion hadn't happened, but only Katniss/Peeta. Gale can go suck a nut because he blew up Prim. Inadvertently, but still.

I honestly have nothing better to do most of the time, so if you need feedback on a story pertaining to DBZ, or Inuyasha (the only animes I've seen EVERY episode of (read all the manga as well)) feel free to ask. I like reading and giving people tips. Though don't ask if you don't expect some criticism, but I promise I won't just say "Your fic is a horrendous piece of crap, burn in hell". Unless it happens to BE a horrendous piece of crap and you SHOULD burn in hell. But I doubt that will be the case :) This does not mean I can be a beta reader right now. I don't have the energy to put forth all that effort right now, I've got a lot going on and I'm just reading for pleasure. I will gladly take a look at your story and give you some thoughts, but don't expect an in-depth critique of every sentence
