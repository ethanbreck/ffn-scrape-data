"The time has come," the Walrus said. "To talk of many things. Of shoes and ships and sealing wax, of cabbages and kings. And why the sea is boiling hot, and whether pigs have wings."

-Lewis Carroll, "Through the Looking Glass"

For as long as I can remember, I have always had an interest in reading/writing. I've managed to churn out a few short stories in the past, but now I think I'm ready to kick it up a notch by writing fanfiction. I write my own works of course, but I have always been a big fan of comic books, science-fiction, anime, and classic works.

I majored in English and minored in Film Studies, and I have put those disciplines to good use through college and into the "real world." I am still young, and I hope to someday make writing my ideal career and livelihood.

Favorite Books:"Alice in Wonderland," "Through the Looking Glass" (as evidenced by my username), "Animal Farm,"1984,""20,000 Leagues Under the Sea," "Prey," "Frankenstein," "The Time Machine," "Lord of the Flies," etc.

Favorite Movies: I've seen so many I don't think I could possibly name a favorite, but I have strong feelings for: "Batman (1989)," "Arachnophobia," "Iron Man," the X-Men series, Spider-Man series, "Double Indemnity," "Miracle on 34th Street," "Alice in Wonderland (1951)," etc.

TV Shows:Batman: the Animated Series, The New Batman Adventures, Batman Beyond, Married...With Children, Spider-Man: the Animated Series, Kitchen Nightmares, Hell's Kitchen, Law & Order (with Jerry Orbach, may he rest in peace), Monk, The Muppet Show, World's Most Amazing Videos, When Animals Attack, etc.

I am presently working on a Batman Beyond script, as well as an original X-Men script based on the fan creation over at the IMDB in a thread I began about one year ago.

I always appreciate reviews, but please, be civil.
