This was originally something that I told to a very good friend of mine when she was feeling a little depressed about how her family didn’t seem to understand her wanting to write all the time, and since then, it has kind of become my motto for writing in general, and provides an outlook that I believe that most writers (struggling or not) need to find within themselves to believe in…

Story telling goes back within history since the beginning of time. It weaves dreams. Creates fantasies, and all in all, makes reality one hell of a better place to live in. Most people don't comprehend (not truly) that without this gift, the world would be one boring and extremely sad plane of existence.

Many would tell you that it doesn't matter whether or not you're talented, because there are so many people out there just as talented, so why bother joining their ranks, when your chances of coming out on top are near non-existent?

But yet it only takes that one person to ignore the pressure of those negative sentiments to create something that will change the fabric of a generation... And as a result, the entire world.

All good stories are precious, and provide so much for this planet. Whether it's shows, movies, books, or fan fic (or music) in all honesty we can't live without them. It doesn't matter whether or not your art touches millions, or just a dozen... Point is, it touched SOMEBODY, and in the end, isn’t that really all that matters?

To touch one life and make them look around at their surrounding world just a little bit differently... isn't that why we write in the first place?

Well, I guess I'll take a moment to say a little bit about myself... This is, after all, a bio page...

First off, I'm 20 years old, and I run pretty much single-handedly a fairly good sized breeding operation of a very special (and endangered) line of horses that are my first passion, and the pride of my life! I also manage a small aviary, in which I handle the breeding of exotic parrots. At one time, I can be found caring for around 60 animals of various species, as well as a 100 acre farm which is a little piece of heaven in my eyes!

I've been a fan fic fan for about 6 years now. It originally started with fanfic based on the Labyrinth, and it just kinda grew from there. I'm a huge Laby fan, but sadly, I've never written for the movie, simply because there was never a need to do so. Some of the best fanfic stories that have been written have been written for the Labyrinth, and frankly, I'd find the pressure to be a little too much! So, I have instead taken my talents elsewhere...
