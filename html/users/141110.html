Wow, been a long time since I've updated this.

I'm now 29 years old. I'm poor. I'm 5' 11" naturally dark blond

DA Account:

I love cosplay.

I've cosplayed from Petshop of Horrors, Fullmetal Alchemist, Doctor Who, Hellsing, Rurouni Kenshin, Sailor Moon, Bleach, LotR, Final Fantasy VII, and X/1999 characters. All fairly badly. I'm fat.

I had given up on writing fics, but I just sat down and started writing without really thinking about it. Too many ideas in my brain. Now I'm writing a Pet Shop of Horrors fic...which is probably going to become a crazy epic, spanning hundreds of chapters. ALMOST FINISHED WITH MY LOTR ONE!

I DO like yaoi. I DO NOT like M-preg. I'm not Completely ANTI m-preg, but I usually find it to be gratuitous. I'll give it a chance if it makes sense, and is well done.

Pairings I believe in, with all my heart, in no particular order: (OTPs simply mark a higher love than others at this particular moment)

1) Leon Orcot and Count D (Pet Shop of Horrors) *OTP*

2) Rumpelstiltskin/Belle (Once Upon a Time) *OTP*

3) Captain America and Iron Man (Marvel Comics)

4) Julian Bashir and Elim Garak (Star Trek: Deep Space 9)

5) Hajime Saitou and Sanosuke Sagara (Rurouni Kenshin)

6) Sailor Venus and Sailor Mars (Pretty Guardian Sailor Moon versions only)

7) Tenth Doctor and Rose Tyler (Doctor Who) *OTP*

8) Eleventh Doctor and River Song (Doctor Who)

9) Cho and Kamatari (Rurouni Kenshin)

10) Rorschach and Nite Owl (Watchmen)

11) Gimli and Legolas (Lord of the Rings) *OTP*

As you can see, I'm not exclusive to any type of pairing. I generally don't go 'looking' for pairings. I usually have strong evidence/reasoning behind my ships. Just because two boys are pretty, DOESN'T mean I'm going to pair them together. I actually get a little annoyed at that practice. Don't get me wrong. I think people should be allowed to ship characters to their hearts content, but, don't shove a crack pairing down my throat like it's canon. Most of the above pairings are either canon, or ALMOST canon. And I fully admit that my RuroKen pairings need a little more backup at times, but I think they still work.

A Note about my stories:

My Sailor Moon story is awful. I started it in high school and never finished it. Don't read it.

My Saitou/Sano one is better. Still unfinished.

My Doctor Who one was going to be longer, but I turned it into a one shot. I'm proud of it.

My Pet Shop one is my new baby. Even if I don't finish, it's going to be a long and fun ride.

Might start a one-shot Cho/Kamatari one soon. I have a nice idea in my head, of which I want to make a short doujinshi, but I might write it up as a one-shot instead/also.

Read my LotR fic, Closer. It's decent.
