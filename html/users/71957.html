My story-in-progress "The Ideal" has hit a very extended lull... but I certainly don't want to abandon it! I know-- I'm a freakin bum. I've sunk to the ultimate low of fanfic writers... . Hopefully enough encouragement and INSPIRATION (where the hell are you?!) will help give me a little push towards its completion-- which truly isn't that far off (In fact, I did make some definite progress with the upcoming chapter!). My first year of University is done, and while I'm contemplating what I want to do with my life, I may get the chance to work on this baby. I know there's at least a couple of people out there (other than me) who want to see it finished. _
