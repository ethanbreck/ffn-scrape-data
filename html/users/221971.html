Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

I like to write, but i do have to say my passion is in stories. Writing is just a way of getting them out.

I have now read Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, and while commenting on anything would obviously spoil it, all I will say is that I am crushed at the thought of this great adventure being over. However, fanfiction will be my crutch in these dificult times, and I encurage any one who still has a story going, to keep it going, no matter how AU. I want to read the end of all the wonderful fanfics out there.

Having said that, I obviously can't be a hyporict. So I am going to write my fan fiction Ghost Story. It will take some time though, before anything happens with it. As I said in the summary, I mainly posted it that firday morning (around four) to get it out before the book. I needs some reathinking now, as while I won't be using the Deathly Hallows plot, I will be using information that we learned from it. Hopefully when it dose get posted there will still be people around to read it.

As a general warning; My short story No Longer You, is not a Ron Hermione ship story, even thought it is filed underneath those two. It is filed under Ron and Hermione because that is who the story is about.

Snape is still my favorite character, and I am only disapointed with the amount of time spent with him in this last book. (wont say if it was to much or too little, read it and find out.)

Another warning:

Flying Without Wings, a faniction by yours truly that is posted here is total crap. I have no idea why I haven´t deleeted it. (Probably to sentimental about it. I have a tendancy to hang on to usless stuff.) If any one wants to show some realy awfull Mary Sue, do direct them here. (the first part might seem bad, but it is nothign compeared what was comming. To put it short she was going to save the world from all evil, not just Voldemort, she died twice and came back both times. Need I go on?)
