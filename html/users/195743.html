Hello, and welcome to my small 'claim to fame' on ff.net. Since Sangwaelen is a mouthful, you may call me Sangwa.

SHAMELESS SELF PROMOTION: I got mentioned in one of ff.net's columns http://www.fanfiction.net/column.php?columnissueid=554

General Info: If you really must know, I'm a 16 year old female without a driver's license who has become quite enamored with Lord of the Rings, and thus discovered fanfiction. I also dabble a bit in the Moulin Rouge area. I do not write slash, and will never write slash. Partly because of my faith, and partly because I don't think anyone in LOTR was gay, so nyah!
I happen to be very hobbit like in stature (well, a tall, small footed hobbit like stature), and I have a penchant for elves and hobbits, and seedcake. I like my hobbit boys to be wide-eyed, as well.

I sometimes write controversial, twisty, and just plain weird stories, and I guess some may be considered flame worthy, but flame away. I write for myself, and only myself, and I don't need everyone's approval.

Also, unlike most fanfiction writers, I crave constructive crit more than reviews that just say "that was good!"
