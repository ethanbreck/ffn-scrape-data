june 9th, 2003

for those who have asked, i am NOT dead.

i thought when i graduated i'd have all this extra time to write.. boy, was i wrong. working full-time is just that- full-time. especially since i want to have a life, too. ;)

but. i do have lots of ideas, still, including a long-running fic called "Ranma the... Gentleman?!" which hopefully i will have posted soon.

i've also been combining "A Moment of Darkness" and "Hedgehog's Dilemma" into one fic, and changing some of the weirder passages. As for "Ryuugenzawa," i have no current plans to continue it. it was started so long ago (my first fic) that i have since forgotten where i was headed with it.

if anyone has suggestions, though, i would be more than happy to entertain them. especially on titles. i kind of suck at those.
