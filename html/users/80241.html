Well, after over two years of being a resident at this site, I think it's time I updated my profile. I'm a 19 year old anime geek. However, I'm not into it as heavily as I used to be. I've become more interested in MMORPGs and other RPGs as of late, and thus, my passions for anime have been on the decline.

I still visit this site from time to time, and I still read my reviews. Thanks guys. Well, and to answer some . . . as it stands, I don't think I'll be writing anymore stories. My last two projects were lost, and each of them were over 100 pages long in length. Although this happened last March . . . I've still lost my inspiration to write.

However, that's not to say I'll never be inspired to write again, but as it stands, don't expect anything new from me for quite a while. ^_^ However, I still appreciate all you knew readers out their and your comments. I do read them still. ^^

And as it is almost Christmas time, I'd like to wish everyone a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year. *smiles* Take care everyone.
