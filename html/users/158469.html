NOTICE

I am not a writer any more. I'll read and review and leave the stories that i have up here... but i just cant write. I mean i can... believe you me... I have a notebook right now that i'm trying to fill up with thoughts of this culture and a history and stuff for them. But that's for my own piece of mind. Maybe i'll type it all up and post it on Fiction Press or rather right here... because really its a bunch of stuff that i've put together from stories and stuff that i myself love... along with my own ideas and plot. So i guess it is a bit of fanfiction along with original fiction. Any ways... um... I'll do that when i finally finish this sort of history book to my liking. And i mean i have to like it. I have to like it enough to want people to read it.

Any ways. I think the reason for this is because of my art. I love art. I'm an artist.. i love to draw and paint and do portraits of people and do character designs for people that randomly pop into my head. I've even begun to do CG stuff. But i am not a writer. I am unable to come up with a stable plot, and i cant stick to any idea for a long period of time. It's not fair to my brain, and not to the readers. When i read something, i want something that i know for sure can be finished and the writer won't give up on it. I dont give up... i just forget about it.

It's easier for me to come up with thousands of pages of backstory, because i dont have to come up with a plot. Backstory is simply that, a history of something or a group of people that gives people better insight on what they're reading. It's easier for me to do that for my own piece of mind. I can create something that needed to be done, and then leave it sitting there with out a second though, but it will always be there with out needing to be changed when i'm happy with it. Because it doesnt need all that much detail. Stories ask for too much from me that i cannot give.

That was longer then inteded, but i felt like i needed to say it, not for you but i guess more so for myself. Sorry that you had to endure that.

~Kim
