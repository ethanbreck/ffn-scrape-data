Author has written 19 stories for Hana Yori Dango, Inuyasha, Harry Potter, Naruto, Hikaru no Go, NANA, Ao Haru Ride/アオハライド, Blue Exorcist/青の祓魔師, and Kuroko no Basuke/黒子のバスケ.

I often try to trick myself into believing I can write. Sometimes I succeed and a story of some sort pops out.

I'm currently not following (nor am I able to follow) any sort of specific update schedule. I write when I can and when I have something to write - or at least that's the attitude I'm trying to adopt in order to stop fretting so much.

I also post at AO3. There are a couple of things that are only archived there or here, but I'm generally consistent with posting at both places eventually. If you can't find something, my DW fic archive probably has it, or at least a mention of the status or where it went.

I do the micro fic for art, casual writing, and headcanon things over at my fic Tumblr, though I also do have a regular Tumblr, mainly fangirling. Feel free to say hi wherever!

Fic things:

• The Feelings Within: This has been deleted for good, sorry. D:

On comments and reviews:

I always welcome feedback on my stories, whether positive or negative, short or long, and new or old. :)

And because I feel quite strongly about this, I'd like to also add that I don't hold updates hostage. While reviews may sometimes encourage or discourage me to write more or less of a story, you will never hear me threat readers about not updating unless I get them.

Disclaimer & Warnings:

This is my blanket disclaimer for the fics I've posted on this site. Other than any applicable OCs, I don't own (or claim to own) the series, the worlds, the characters, the fandom, or whatever else related to all that. No money is being made from any of the writing I do on this site either.
