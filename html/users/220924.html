Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, and Smallville.

Originally started on here with Harry Potter fics. After being absent from fanfiction for a few years, I'm back and needing my daily dose of Smallville fanfiction now. I'm really excited about getting back into writing!

Smallville is definitely my interest right now. I have very firm ideas about the pairing that should occur whenever a Clark Kent/Superman exists, namely, it's all Clark and Lois Lane all the way. No exceptions. Lana turned me off from Smallville for a while, but I rediscovered the show with Season 8 and immediately gourged myself on seasons 4-present. Starting with Lois and dealing the least with Clark and Lana and their totally too long relationship. I'm ready for Clois to come into full force now. Enough of this Clana crap! Season 9 is Clois time!!

Lois Lane and Clark Kent always wind up together. They are soulmates. It's a fact of life. Clois is endgame

Anyone interested in a Smallville RPG?
