My fics:

used to be fics with an s, but now, I've taken them both down (The Shang Lady and Jewelled Dance). I'll explain:

The Shang Lady:

1) I've reread it again. Not just once, but, like, 5 times. And I've hated the fact that I could write that badly. And there was me trying to give advice to people. Could I be more hypocritical? Possibly not.

2) A very nice reviewer has brought to my attention the fact of reviews. They said that barely 100 or so of my reviews were remotely interesting and good. The rest were just half-reviews since they were mini-spams of Dom! and Neal! and so on. And I was thinking, they are so right. It's not that I don't appreciate the fact that so many of you have read and reviewed, it's just that, if I were to have half a decent brain, I would notice that they don't really count, as such.

3) At age 13, when I first wrote this story, and after the whole I'm-going-to-take-it-down then I'm-not-now, I've realised how rather pathetic I was. I should have taken it down before it became a mini-obsession for me to get reviews. I've grown up, now, (or so I hope) and I don't really care.

4) The winner of the Neal/Dom thing, if you even care, is Neal due to obsessive spam reviewers.

Jewelled Dance:

Yes, it's going down too. Been up there too long and with too little interest from you readers. Written before TSL, when I was 12, it was not particularly good (at least, you didn't seem to think so), and so down it goes. I'm going to spend more time working on my GCSES (which I'm supposed to be doing in the summer, help!) and on reading a different type of fanfiction: Harry Potter.

Well, here's a little about me (not too much!)

- I prefer to read than to write.

- I live in England, although am not English.

- My favourite colour is grey-blue (as if you really care)

- Ships I support (i.e. fics I read): Lily/James Harry/Ginny Ron/Hermione

I have been working a lot on grammar, etc, and, having decided writing for me is hopeless, but helping others who have a gift for it isn't, to give nice, long, detailed, thorough reviews on fics that deserve them. Yes, I critise (if needed!) but, actually, if I make it past the first three chapters and leave a review (which I try to do everytime), your story will go directly onto my favourites list. (i.e. a good story)

Which is a good time to say that all the fics on my Favourites List are VERY worthwhile to read.

Have a nice day.
