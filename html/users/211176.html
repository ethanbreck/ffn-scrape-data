Author has written 29 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Weiss Kreuz, Yu-Gi-Oh, FAKE, and South Park.

I don't read much anymore, but when I do, I'm a sucker for complex character motivation, heartfelt interaction, and entertaining realism. I have a weakness for AU or post-canon fics, and high school or college AUs are my guilty pleasure - as long as they're not OOC. Bonus if the story fits my own head canon!

Confession: I also occasionally indulge in fic that's so bad it's good.

Other GW stuff that I've written (including explicit versions of some of the fics here) can be found at GWAddiction and Deb's Gundam Wing Diaries site.

When I have time, I sometimes I lurk on LJ as mookiegatto, AO3 as Mookie, and tumblr as gotsomeapples.
