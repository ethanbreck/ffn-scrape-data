A little about what I write:

I stepped away from anime several years ago and have recently come around to love it again. Gundam Wing, mostly, but I have a new passion and I'm broadening my anime horizon to include Inuyasha, Death Note and some others. You'll probably only see Gundan Wing works here, but you never know.
I love the characters of Heero, Duo, Trowa and Wufei. I used to have a very hard time relating to and portraying Quatre, but I'm slowly starting to get a handle on this boy.
