I am known as Mortania Riddle. I am a HUGE Harry Potter Fan. So much that in most of the stories myself, and Sasha, my darling stepsister write, I am Lord Voldemort's daughter...so...you'll also notice that Sasha and myself have a huge variety of brothers and sisters...well, they're step brothers and sisters to her...except for 2...our sisters, Coralynn, and Mortisha.

I love Snape, he's the best, and so's Alan Rickman. So power to all the Snape and Rickman fans...Haha!

Well, I have to be taking my leave, or as Dumbledore puts it, because we don't see eye to eye, "The Parting Of the ways." GOF...

~¤ Mortania Riddle ¤~

Oh, and by the way, I am really a 14 year old 8th grader, my real name is Autumn. ANd I owe all my writing abilities to Mr. Philip D. Deckard, my old 3rd grade teacher.Mrs. C. Roxanne Glassco my old 5th grade teacher, she taught us that by writing, we free our imagination, and soul, all on a piece of paper. Mrs. Kimberly Wheeler, my old 6th grade teacher, for telling us we could do whatever we wanted to in life, as long as we believe in ourselves, and look beyond the negitatives to see the positives...or look beyond the rain to see the sun. Ms. Pamela Horner, the english teacher that I had from 6th to 7th grade, who touched everyone's lives whether or not they realize it, they miss her, and they have to at least have a sense of pride with every quote she used, she taught me that your imagination is something that you may forget you have, but if you don't, use it forever, along with your heart, those two elements combined, will give you all you need. Just take a deep breath and lest not forget that you are winning everyday, by getting up out of bed.
