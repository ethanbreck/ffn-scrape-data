Age: Unknown (Let's just say I was older than a teenager when this site was founded)

Home Location: Somewhere in the Northern Hemisphere (see Identity above)

Hero/Villain Designation: Hero

First started reading and reviewing Fanfiction anonymously in 1998.

Weapons of choice: Merr-Sonn missile launcher, Stouker Concussion rifle, Double-bladed lightsaber, Imperial repeater with concussion launcher, Class-A Thermal Detonators, Leichenfaust 44 (from Wolfenstein 2009), just to name a few, all heavily modified.

Ground vehicle of choice: 1987 Buick Regal GNX; two modes of operation: 1) authentic mode - same as it was when Buick sent it to ASC McLaren in 1987. 2) Battle mode - heavily modified, can out-accelerate a top-fuel dragster. Carries dual laser cannons, dual gatling cannons, missiles, and other offensive/defensive weaponry. Can fight its way into heavily fortified locations and carry out search-and-rescue, sabotage, hit-and-run, or recon operations. Think of both Knight Rider and Airwolf combined, on Steroids, and you'll get the idea.

Personal Starfighter: XM-1 Missileboat, appropriated from Emperor Palpatine's treasure house on Wayland - Most dangerous starfighter in known galaxy - "Following in the traditions set down by Han Solo and the Millenium Falcon, this ship has been heavily modified from its original stock specs." - quote from The Pilot. This ship is also the blueprint for a new experimental ship that I'm working on - The XM-5 Super Missileboat - I just need to have someone actually draw it.

Starfighter design: Created by Grand Admiral Thrawn; produced by Cygnus Spaceworks; modified and upgraded by The Pilot

"Hello, everyone. I'm a guy who enjoys Star Wars, Legend of Zelda, reading, and reading/reviewing Fanfiction.

09/09/2012 - FINAL ENTRY - *sheds a tear, before turning to the camera* Well, we all know this day had to come along at some point. I'm sorry to say it's today. It's been a real blast, these 14 years of fanfiction reading and reviewing, but there comes a time in every person's life when things beyond their control don't allow them to do everything like they used to. That said, I'll miss all of you, especially those authors whose fine stories gave me many a laugh, tons of shock and surprise, and even the occasional tear. And for all of the young, and up-and-coming authors that I was able to influence and aid along the hard road of fiction writing, as well as anyone else who finds this page, my last words of advice are: Never give up, because you never know what lies just around that next turn in the road!

Pauses to grab a walking stick, hat, and backpack, before opening the door of his fanfiction base, walking outside, and closing it, leaving the key in the doorknob, and a sign on the door, before starting to walk down the path to the road beyond, singing the words of a very familiar J.R.R. Tolkien song as he goes swiftly, but surely*

TP:

The Road goes ever on and on, Down from the door where it began. Now far ahead the Road has gone, And I must follow, if I can, Pursuing it with eager feet, Until it joins some larger way, Where many paths and errands meet. And whither then? I cannot say...*as he continues to walk, slowly fading away into the sunset.
