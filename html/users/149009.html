I'm a very nice girl from Sweden, so you'll probably notice that english really isn't my first language but I'm working on it and I feel my vocabulary build up more and more each day. Grammar mistakes are a little common, but just tell me if I mess up. I won't be mad at all. I'll take whatever help I can get. The same goes for beta-readers since I've never really had one.

My name is Anna and I'm 21 years old. I've always loved to write and I wrote my first fanfiction for the Star Trek Voyager fandom a lot of years ago. Nowadays however I'm more into Harry Potter, Veronica Mars and X-men. I also like to read and write original slash stories. There's not much of my work around here, but I bet that will change in time. Read and review, tell me if I should keep writing the way I do or if I should change something.

Enjoy your stay.

I would love to find some new friends who write for the same fandoms as me, so if you feel this itching need to bitch about a story idea that turned out wrong or just blab about how gorgeous Logan Echolls is, drop me a line at: eyeliner_@spray.se or just add me to your MSN list.
