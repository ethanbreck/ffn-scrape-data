Awright! Changed my sn again--I'm now The Sweetheart -- Kay-chan!! I like change, and I didn't like the apostrophe in my previous sn. You may know me as Goten's Gurl, and I still love that title, but now I want to proove that my writing style has improved greatly.

Here's a bit about myself. And just a bit, mind you. I have lots of interests now. The top anime shows that I find myself watching at the moment are Rurouni Kenshin, YuYu Hakusho, Digimon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, .hack//sign, Dragonball Z and GT, and Inuyasha. I have other favorites as well, but these five are my current obsessions. For video games, my definate favorites are Kingdom Hearts, Threads of Fate(DewPrism), the Legend of Zelda, Skies of Arcadia, and Sonic the Hedgehog ((preferably Sonic Adventure 2 Battle for the Nintendo Gamecube)). I love Bust a Move/Groove and Dance Dance Revolution as well. I love to draw and I tend to create characters of my own for different anime shows and video games. And when I create them, I use them in roleplays with my friends, and then they pop up as voices in my head. Currently I have about twelve of my own created character voices and about eighteen if you include Takuya and Kouji(Digimon Frontier), Sonic the Hedgehog, Shadow the Hedgehog, and Knuckles the Echidna (Sonic Adventure 2), and Seto Kaiba (Yu-Gi-Oh!), ((GAH!! CAN'T COUNT!!)) who all popped up when I was roleplaying them. Others come and go... but I'm not going to scare you any more than I have already.

Most of the stories that I will write in the future will probably be my own character backgrounds and stuff like that, but will include the anime characters that you know and love. Anyways, it may depend on my mood, but don't expect updates too often, I have too much going on. If you have any questions or comments, please, e-mail me and ask or tell.
