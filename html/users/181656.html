Someone's reading this... you actually want to know about me? Wow. I feel so appreciated. Does my hair look all right? No, no it doesn't. I'm a guy. We get away with that.

If you want to know how I think, just check out my selection of quotes down there. Otherwise, know that I be a second-year university student, intending to major in economics despite a dream of becoming a novelist, with a definite thing for RPGs, fantasy novels (not the general schlock, just the good ones), Star Wars, Star Trek, all things Timothy Zahn, the Japanese language and most of its culture, and this one really amazing girl... ahem. Ye didnae hear that. Oh, and pirates. Pirates and Mongols improve almost any situation. Arr.

My personal recommendations, as far as my stories go, are Unleash the Fury of the Djinn, Heartstrings, Shining in the Darkness (no relation to Sega), A Wok of Infinite Light, and Awakening. If I can say this without reaching critical ego mass, Awakening rules.

I feel like I should find something valuable to say here, so that there'll actually be a purpose to reading this far. So here goes: a few sentences that represent a lot of my personal philosophy.

"If you really want to be happy, no one can stop you."
-Mary Tricky

"It's time to start slapping people."
-George Carlin

"You must be the change you wish to see in the world."
-Mahatma Gandhi

"Life is like chess: the women are all-powerful."
-very likely someone, but I don't know 'em

"Time spent laughing is time spent with the gods."
-Japanese proverb

"Sometimes it's better to light a flamethrower than curse the darkness."
-Terry Pratchett (and I could list plenty more of his)

"For all its sham, drudgery, and broken dreams, it is still a beautiful world. Strive to be happy."
-from the Desiderata

"When I hear someone say 'life is hard', I'm tempted to ask 'compared to what?'"
-Sydney J Harris

"When you're going through Hell, keep going."
-Winston Churchill

That'll do for now. Maybe I'll put up a different batch next time, but don't bet on it. While you wait, read any stories below, plus everything by Vilya 'n' Company, Midnight C, and Griffinkhan.
