Hi and welcome to my profile, I'm Crystal and at present a University student :)

Ive revamped my image a little on here, my name used to be nessawen and I wrote a few small stories, that never saw completion and in my recent return have decided to remove. Since then I have co-worked with my sister Gummadoon Fanatic on a number of her stories and seen them through to completion. I've not used this account since first beginning Comprehensive School and plan to read & review under this account, I may be tempted to write again some time in the future under my categories of interest, ideas already swim around in my head and plague me lol, but I will certainly try in the future to have written my story to near if not full completion before posting to avoid disappointment.

I enjoy reading - J.R.R.Tolkien, C.S Lewis, Stephenie Meyer, Margaret Weis & Tracy Hickman are some of my favourite authors to date.

Some of my fav things are LOTR, Harry Potter, Dragonlance, Twilight Saga, TMNT, DS9 and a passing interest in Card Captor Sakura and DBZ.

Latest obsession: AVATAR - simply an amazing film, seen it 3 times in cinema now, in 2D and 3D and it is still just as great as the 1st time! _

I like experimenting with all kinds of ships in each area of interest and try to keep and open mind, some of my fav's so far have been - (Harry Potter): HG/LM, HG/SS, HG/SB. (LOTR): Haldir/OC, Legolas/OC, Elladan/OC, Elrohir/OC. (Dragonlance): Raistlin/OC, Tas. (Twilight): B/C (TMNT): all the guys/OC, L/R, L/D, R/D. (Avatar) J/N & J/Tsu (recently converted to this pairing, still gives me the creeps occasionally but it's growing on me) :p

I'm also quite a sporty person when I get going and like to swim, play badminton and basketball.
