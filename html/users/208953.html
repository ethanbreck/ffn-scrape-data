Author has written 13 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, Beyblade, and D N Angel.

April 28, 2010

I think it's safe to say that I will not be writing much anymore. I know I said that I would finish them eventually, but with my RL schedule I don't see that happening. However, fanfiction and those stories hold an odd little place in my heart, and I feel weird not providing some form of closure for them. Since I still have the original outlines for most of the remaining chapters, my plan is to write up summaries of what would have happened for my unfinished stories and post those instead. I'd like to think this will happen in the summer, but I can't be sure about anything anymore D=

In any case, thanks for all the support over the years! Writing those stories and making connections with other writers and reviewers was one of the best experiences I ever had. PMs are welcome, as always.

Information on the Sheep:

...What about yaoi: Yes, although I am a sucker for certain het. canon pairings, this is what I prefer to write about. The stories that contain yaoi are marked by the pairing in the summary (if you know the anime, you know if the names are male or not). Please take the time to check before reviewing with complaints about the content. Thank you.

Story status:

Spirits: Hiatus

...If I remember anything at all, it is to never take full credit for anything I do, because all of my work is influenced by someone one way or another. Everyone contributes to it, and for that, I am truly grateful.
