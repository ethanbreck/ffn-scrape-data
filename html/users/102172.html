VERY IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:

I am currently working on several different fanfictions that I will hopefully post soon. Before that happens, I'd like to post a general, all-purpose disclaimer here on my profile rather than putting it in each chapter of each story.

I think it should be obvious that I am writing fanfiction. It should also be obvious that I do not own any of the stories or animes that I use for my fanfictions. I do not own the characters or settings, I just play with them. In most cases, I could claim that I own the plots and OCs that I use, but I for one do not care if other fanfiction writers use similar plots and characters - it's all fanfiction to me. Also, other fanfiction writers may recognize plot elements that they've come up with. If I can remember specific sources of inspiration, I will credit them. If I do not credit someone and you recognize something from your own or someone else's fanfiction, I apologise. I have read literally thousands of fanfictions over more than a decade and a half. They have a tendency to somewhat blur together in my mind while certain ideas, details, characters, whatever stick in my head or come back to me at the oddest times. Let me know if I've used something of yours and I'll credit you for it.

In addition, I do not make any money from writing fanfiction. I write fanfiction for pleasure, and for practice writing. I do write original fiction too, and I hope to one day make money from it. I also hope that enough people will like my work that someone will fanfiction based on it. As I mentioned above, I hope to one day have a website that will have ads on it that I get paid for. That money is for the ads, not the fanfiction.
