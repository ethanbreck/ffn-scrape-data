Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

Well, I'm a college Freshman at Good Ole Illinois State University. Still getting used to the college life, though, with the exception of classes, it's going all right. But hey, if any of you guys are Chemistry geniuses, go ahead and feel free to transfer that knowledge into me...

Lesse..about me...

I'm a Bio major (unless all these Chem classes make me quit), planning on becoming a high school teacher (oh, the horror). I am constantly looking for instances of magic, and I can't wait until I go with the Marching Band to IRELAND next year because that means I will be a little closer to Scotland and Hogwarts.

Don't feel I'm a strong writer, but I try anyway. I have thousands of plot bunnies running in my head, but I'm not confidant to write them out, and when I do, they turn out horribly wrong and I quit after a few paragraphs.

I'm currently trying to re-vamp Professor Binns (it'll be done in a couple of years, I promise!), so I would recommend you not reading that. Don't. It's bad.

Little Cupboard boy is my favorite, though it's corny and short. I still like it anyways.

The Task is my newest... As I just wrote it about 10 minutes ago (3:30 am) I'm not sure how I feel about it yet. Maybe after a good night of sleep, I'll re-read it and then spend the next few hours trying to figure out what the heck I was thinking.

Enjoy!
