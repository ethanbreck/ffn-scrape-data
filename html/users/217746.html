A little about me:

Writing fanfiction has been a side hobby of mine since middle school (I've been a member of FFN since 2002- holy moley!), and recently, I've become a college graduate (yay, go me). If you want to know how I got addicted to fanfiction, I can sum it up by saying: Pass the Snog (see my favorites list).

I'm huge on various anime/manga, book, and movie fanfiction. Like any fangirl I enjoy romance fiction immensely - but of course, every story is ten times better with a plot or intention, no matter how short it is. Adventure/fantasy is fun to read, and on occasion I will crave a suspense/horror/mystery fiction. Fluff only gets me sometimes.

News about my fics:

Black and White (A Song of Ice and Fire): One-shot, added on 3/24/11 (Complete), now AU since A Dance with Dragons came out

Standing on Opposite Shores (Spirited Away): Removed.
