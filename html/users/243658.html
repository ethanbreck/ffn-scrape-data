About Me:

It's always been my philosophy that if you really want to see or read something that doesn't exist, then you should do it yourself. Hence, I greatly enjoy drawing fanart (as evident by my art account) since my favorite pairings usually aren't canon and therefore I will never officially see or read them. One day I would like to be able to draw realistically, or semi-realistically, but for now I'm content with my anime/manga style. Lately I've also been greatly enjoying writing fanfiction as well as my own fiction (I have a writing account as well as this one). Again, this has to do with it doesn't exist and may well never exist.

Though I love many fandoms, my primary focus is on Naruto and Twilight. Hence, a lot of my novels and ideas revolve around those two universe's characters (though I don't think I'd ever seriously cross them). My favorite genre is probably romance, so many of my stories include romantic elements. My next favorite genre (which is just only slightly second to romance) is family, so I tend to focus on family a lot as well. Humor is a great genre as well, but I've found that humorous stories aren't usually well-written, unfortunately. Otherwise, I don't really care about the genre as long as the story is well-written.

Stories I'm Working On:

I'm a college student in her final years before earning her degree, so although I often write when I can, sometimes I just don't have the time. I also sometimes have a problem of not feeling motivated, which severally hinders the writing process. And finally, I don't like publishing stories unless they're finished because I can't guarantee they'll ever get finished otherwise. So, while these are all stories I'm working on, don't necessarily expect to see them any time soon. However, if you pester me enough (nicely, please), they might get finished faster. ;)

Twilight

- Untitled: With a bit of tweaking to the original timeline and the lack of vampirism, this story focuses on how the Cullen family is formed, starting with just Carlisle and Esme and ending with Bella. Many of the details in the novel are retained but altered to fit the modern timeline and to be humanly possible. For example, Edward is saved from death by Carlisle (like in the novels), but rather than dying from the Spanish Influenza, he is instead dying from a case of untreated pneumonia. Many of the main parts have been written (such as how Carlisle first meets Edward, Rosalie, and Emmett), but the filling still needs to be done. Total chapters: 9-12 (I lose count every time I try). Total word count: 30,905.

- Untitled: The prequel to the above story, this story focuses on how Carlisle and Esme meet and begin a relationship. I have two versions at the moment: one in Carlisle's point of view and one in Esme's point of view, but I think I will continue working on Esme's point of view since hers is much more interesting. There's not much yet to say about this, other than it focuses on Carlisle healing most of Esme's wounds, such as the ones caused by the loss of her son and Charles Evenson. I'm thinking of doing Esme's point of view simply because she has a lot of emotional baggage from being both physically and sexually abused, making it difficult for her to trust Carlisle initially. Total chapters: 2. Total word count: 2831.

- Red-Eyed Monster: Another one-shot for the 100 Themes Challenge (see "87 Days"). This story focuses on a time when Esme loses self-control and feeds on a human, changing her eyes immediately from the murky butterscotch to a brilliant red. She falls into a depression of sorts when she realizes that a human family somewhere will be missing their son, and she remembers the pain of losing her son. As fate would have it, Edward hears her thoughts and immediately finds Carlisle, who in turn reassures Esme that she is not a monster. Total word count: 0 (I will start soon, I promise).

- Untitled: Renesmee is cute and all as a "toddler", but I think she would be much more interesting once she is fully grown. This story focuses on her perspective after she is 8 years old and is finally allowed to attend high school with her parents and her aunts and uncles (she couldn't previously because the family didn't know how much she would age in a school year). It explores Renesmee's unique half-vampire traits, her personality (influenced by many in her family), her relationship with her family, and, of course, her relationship with Jacob. Total chapters: 4. Total word count: 6264.

Naruto

- Untitled: A prize for one of the members on my ItaSaku group (Itachi-Sakura). She wanted an ItaSaku one-shot based on the movie Mr. and Mrs. Smith. I haven't actually seen the movie (I'm going to watch it in a moment), but I did have an idea. The two are married (Itachi is 26 and Sakura is 21) but due to the war, they can't live together, and are sent to kill one another. This story focuses on the night before they are expected to confront each other where they discuss the situation they are in before copulating. Non-lemon. Total word count: 1389.
