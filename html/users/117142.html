Hola kids! I've never been one for making profiles but here we go...

I'm 22, with a degree in painting and drawing, and a ridiculous obsession with fanfiction. I had previously only put one story up on the internet, way back in 2002. Not sure if it's going to stay, but it's a nice reminder of how much I've grown if nothing else I guess. I've dallied with other fandoms in the years sense, but never enough to actually complete another fanfiction. I might pic one of my old ones back up that I started a year ago for Weiss Kreuz. That is the fandom that really defined my preferences in fanfiction. I started reading fanfiction back in 7th grade. I actually attribute my enjoyment of reading to fanfiction, and then Anne Bishop won me over the rest of the way.

I'm a slash-a-holic. Guilty as charged. I do love hetero stuff, but generally slash just... entertains me more. The main reason I am here today, back on ffn, is Star Trek 2009. Oh my holy... is that movie amazing! Spock and Kirk forever. Hell, just give me Spock and I will die a happy woman. I thought I was a trekkie, growing up with Next Gen and Voyager, but I had no idea how much I was going to love the original. Lack of opportunity sadly prevented earlier corruption. But I have recently acquired the first season of TOS and am quickly remedying the problem. Expect more fics to abound!
