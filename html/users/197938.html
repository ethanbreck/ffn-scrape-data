Author has written 3 stories for Naruto, and Tales of the Abyss.

I also have a few stories under the pen name Ashakko, a joint fanfiction.net account with a friend of mine, ChiByakko. Any stories where we have both contributed to the writing (one writing, the other editing) go there. The stories I have written by myself (and are usually un-beta'd) are here.

As for what I write about... In general terms, I like romance, monogamy, and happy endings. I'm not fond of death fics, and I hate rape. I am fascinated by fairy tales, and find stories featuring gender confusion (boys dressing as/mistaken for girls, and vice versa) funny and entertaining. I like working with cliche ideas, and seeing what I can do with them, and am very rarely original.

About myself... well, I'm obsessed with anime, manga, and video games (especially RPG's). I read, write, and draw. I like all types of pairings - het, yuri, yaoi - and write for all of them. ...That said, I tend to focus most on yaoi pairings.

My writing is VERY sporatic. I'll write something, and then not put up another fic for two or three years. If I start a story, I will eventually finish it... but it may literally be 3 years between updates.

On a side note, any stories which I have listed in my favorites section are COMPLETE. I refuse to list a story, no matter how good or entertaining, if it's not complete. So even if, for some reason, the story doesn't actually say "Complete", the story really is done/finished/complete.
