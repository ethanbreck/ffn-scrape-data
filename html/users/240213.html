Books, Percy Jackson and the Olympians, Arrow, Lucifer, and It.

I'm not a native speaker of English but have consumed most of my fandoms in English, plus, I actively work with the language every day. So the majority of my stories is in English, too. Naturally, my English is way better now (as of 2017) than when I first started posting here (in 2003), so you'll probably see the progress if you read and old and a more recent story of mine back to back.

Things I love ... above all, my friends and family, but also: reading, movies, singing, musicals and opera, lots and lots of TV series, and writing. Most of my stories involve some romance and some angst (I'm not really good at comedies). Have a look for yourself :)

Another note on my stories... I'm a 'shipper, I admit it. Whenever I see a spark, I take up on it. That's one reason why I don't like OC pairings. And that's also the reason why most of my stories turn out 'shippy in the end (depending, of course, on the type of story). Usually you will find my 'shipping ideas to be canon, and if a relationship remains unresolved in the original, I might take that into consideration, too. "Special unit 2" is the exception rather than the rule, but hell, those two were just made for each other, only their show was canceled too soon!

So you'll see a lot of fluffy one-shots on my stories list, but a couple of "feature-length" stories, too.

One more thing: Unlike many FF authors, I don't post works in progress unless they are unrelated chapters/series of one-shots. But if I have a story with a plot and storyline, I will not leave you hanging. NO UNFINISHED STORIES from me, promise!

I love reviews. I really do. And that doesn't mean I only want praise. I just like the idea that people read my stories and feel they're worth thinking about and therefore send me a couple of lines saying how they liked it. So please, even if you didn't like the story, share your thoughts with me. Criticism is welcome as long as it's constructive. Nobody likes flames, and neither do I.

That said, go ahead and enjoy :)
