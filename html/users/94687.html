Fanfic Thoughts & Preferences

Lately become fascinated in reading fanfics on Sakumo what if or history perspective pieces. Open to recommendations.

I'm not into character bashing, especially of fathers. And some my favorite "what if" story are "Team 8" by S'TarKan. Also, a cool Time Travel with Obito I'd recommend to check out is "New Identity in the Past" by Dreamyin. Dreaming of Sunshine by Silver Queen is an amazing self insert that is so uncliche. There's even a lightsaber it in! Even though I'm not into reincarnation its still one of the best stories I've read. I'm in awe of her ability to stay in the first person perspective.

A fan of well written Iruka fanfics. Particularly for Iruka & Kakashi platonic friendship fics. Such as "True Confessions of a Paperwork Ninja" by Goldenkizamu and the "Not Over Til The Paperwork Is In" series by WhyMustIWrite.

I've done a LOT of reading of almost any Iruka Fan fiction and feel pretty confident, excited & happy about this story. Though I must admit I was quite inspired by the Paperwork series, (see above for title & authors). Yes, in Kokoro's story, our poor Chunin will be deluged by some bureaucratic duties. Hopefully to delight you with unique twists.

I believe in free speech, but personally not interested in reading the "Icha Icha" genres in Fanfiction. Wish this website made it easier to exclude these from my searches. If anyone has attempted a Iruka & Kakashi straight friendship search you know

what I'm talking about. And no, excluding the M rating doesn't put that much of a dent into the results.

So instead of wading through it all, check out & subscribe to the "Iruka - Underneath the Underneath" Community. If you have suggestions, feel free to pm them to me.
