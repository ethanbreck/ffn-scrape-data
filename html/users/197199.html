Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.
Ok well my REAL name is Kate, I am 19 and live in London when I'm not away at Uni studying (read: partying and refusing to do any work). I have been reading fanfiction for YEARS and have finally decided to try writing some... eep! I am a die hard R/H fan, and in some ways that’s annoying coz I find it extremely difficult to read anything else, but hey, what can I do! I also love R/S fics (that’s Remus/Sirius for all those who are unfamiliar with that pairing! Oh and I mainly like the pre-Azkaban ones, I just can't take sad fics!) but I doubt I can write them yet - being a Mathematician at heart, I find writing a few coherent sentences about as hard as not reading fics for weeks, so I am worried I'll be one of those annoying authors who NEVER updates :( Having said that, once I get writing it can be difficult to stop me (as anyone who has ever received a review from me will know! They tend to be... hmmm, how to put it... long and rambling, a bit like this bio!). So, as I said I'm not really a writer so forgive me if my stories leave much to be desired (!) but I am trying, so any feedback (via review, e-mail, whatever) would be MUCH appreciated - I just want some idea of how I am doing! Oh, just one more thing, spelling will no doubt be appalling, I do use spell-check but it's not infallible! I apologise in advance and hope it's not TOO annoying!

Ooh, for anyone looking for good R/H fics and a selection of other good HP writing, go to its fantastic! I am not a member yet (too scared to try having had no feedback here yet!) but I would put almost all of the authors there on my favourites list if I could, I just don't know how they do it! I bow down in admiration!

What else? If anyone cares I love listening to music including Skunk Anansie, Metallica (don't run away, they're not nearly as shit/heavy metal as most people think!), Muse, System of a Down and a random mix of old Irish folk and classical (well, you can't deny your heritage can you?). I read far too much, any fantasy book I can get my hands on, I accept quite a lot of SciFi, but not much else (the real world is so dull!). I love playing Final Fantasy games, but Tetris often stands in when I don't have the time (it's addictive I tell you!).

Having thoroughly converted all my school friends to Harry Potter, I am now starting on my Uni friends, who still seem to think it’s sad (poor souls, they have so much to learn!) by leaving the books in their rooms at all possible opportunities and giving them to them when they’re bored or upset, hee hee, I’ve got to two of them already, who are now helping to spread the word ;)

Ok, now that I’ve rambled (and you’re all rightly dreading the day I decide to leave you a review!) I’ll go, but I have to just apologise for how long it’s been since I updated – real life has taken over for quite a while, and as I’ve mentioned, drawing stories out of me is like getting blood out of a stone. Anyway, reviews help, so if you’re desperate (HA, who am I kidding!), keep pestering me and I’ll give up some sleeping time to write a bit more to tide you over to the summer when I’ll be blissfully free :D
