Hi, I'm Beatrix. Okay, so that's just middle name, but I don't trust you all enough with my given name. I love to watch anime! My absolute favorite anime is Slayers, but I also like Princess Nine, Ceres, Mysterious Play (Fushigi Yugi), Sailor Moon, Kodomo No Omocha (Child's Toy), Rayearth, Gate Keepers, and Record of Lodoss Wars (Chronicles of the Heroic Knight). Aside from watching animes, I'm also addicted to the Final Fantasy RPG series for Playstation (2).

Favorite Guys:

Slayers: Zelgadis. Isn’t he everyone’s???
Princess Nine: Coach. ^_^ I still laugh at the episode where he tells the girls to emotionally seduce the Rinkai High boys in the baseball game.
Ceres: Yuhi. He’s just an over-sexed teenager.
Mysterious Play: Tamahome. He might be a greedy a$$ hole at times, but I just love him!
Sailor Moon: Yaten. He’s the cutest of the Three Lights in my opinion!
Kodomo No Omocha: Hayama. The way he’s laid back, yet highly over-protected of Sana is just adorable. Ahhh…sixth grade love is so cute :)
Magic Knight Rayearth: Lantis. If I said why, it might spoil the ending of the first season. ^_^
Gate Keepers: Shun. But only because I didn’t like the others.
Record of Lodoss Wars: Grievus. He’s so calm and wise about battle.

Final Fantasy 7: Cloud. He cares a lot about Aeris and Tifa.
Final Fantasy 8: Not really sure. Most of the guys in there were total dorks. I guess Seifer is the man for this one.
Final Fantasy 9: Once again, the guys were total losers in this game, but Zidane won by process of elimination.

Favorite Girls:

Slayers: Lina. She never shows any fear, and I envy that.
Princess Nine: Izumi. I know EXACTLY what it feels like to be at the wrong point of a love triangle.
Ceres: Chidori. She just never gives up on Yuhi! :)
Mysterious Play: Well, out of Miaka and Yui, I pick Yui. But I really hated both of them.
Sailor Moon: Trista / Setsuna / Sailor Pluto. She’s just so wise.
Kodomo No Omocha: Sana-chan, of course! It’s a good thing she was around to set Hayama straight.
Magic Knight Rayearth: Caldina. I love how she’s just down with the gambling!
Gate Keepers: Yukino. Very mysterious, and she uses big words.
Record of Lodoss Wars: Leaf. She’s always so sure of herself.

Final Fantasy 7: Yuffie! I don't care what other people say about her. I think she's awesome.
Final Fantasy 8: Quistis. She's very level-headed.
Final Fantasy 9: Eiko. It amazes me how aware she is about the Facts of Life at the age of six!

Favorite Couples:

Slayers: Amelia / Zelgadis.
Princess Nine: Ryo / Hiroki.
Ceres: Aya / Toya.
Mysterious Play: Miaka / Tamahome.
Sailor Moon: Serena / Darien.
Kodomo No Omocha: Sana-chan / Hayama-kun
Magic Knight Rayearth: Hikaru / Lantis.
Gate Keepers: Ruriko / Shun.
Record of Lodoss Wars: Deedlit / Parn.

Final Fantasy 7: Tifa / Cloud, or Aeris / Cloud.
Final Fantasy 8: Selphie / Irvine.
Final Fantasy 9: Dagger (Garnet) / Zidane. Even if I hated both of them.

Best Couple Overall: Sana-chan / Hayama-kun! I LOVE these two together! Sana, the extremely genki child actress, and Hayama, the calm and collected head bully, who doesn’t let anyone else come between him and Sana. Even though he can’t just come right out and say that he loves her, he’ll claim that any other boy who likes Sana is his rival. It’s just adorable!
