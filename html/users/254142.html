Hey! My name's Elise and I live in a big purple house. I own three miniature horses that I love dearly. Loretta, Sophie, Fancy, I love you girls!!! I couldn't live without music. I play the violin, piano, flute, percussion, and I sing. Here's some stuff about me:
Music: Enya, the Lord of the Rings soundtracks, spiderman soundtrack, the Beatles, Sting(and the Police), Puddle of Mudd, U2, Creed, Shonen Knife, Weird Al, John Williams, Train, and just about anything else under the sun other than rap and country.(and i LOVE to play the Indiana Jones them from the Raiders of the Lost Ark on my violin)

Favorite Actors/Actresses: Orlando Bloom, Elijah Wood, Liv Tyler, Hugo Weaving, Keanu Reeves, Sandra Bullock, David Duchovney, Cate Blanchett, Gillian Anderson, Richard McKinze(who's probably my absolute favorite, because he's so cool to talk to and has such great stories, and is a GREAT COOK!)

Favorite Movies: Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, The Matrix, Ice Age, Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon

Favorite Sports: Horseback riding, soccer, and skiing

Books: Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, The Hobbit, To Race A Dream, Wild Magic: the Immortals (Pierce), Holes, and manymany others...

Annie Oakley is my hero. She will always be. She out-shot, out-did all the men out there. She became famous, but that's not what she cared about. She melted her medals and gave the gold and silver to charity. That's what we should all do. Do our very best, but not all the time for ourselves.
