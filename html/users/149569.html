Author has written 19 stories for Dark Angel, and NCIS.

I am a legal secretary from Australia, and a full time fan of Dark Angel, NCIS and Supernatural.

I hope that I'm giving you some enjoyment, and, if in some way I tweak an emotional chord, then I have succeeded in what I'm trying to do here...

Please review - that's what keeps me going, and also tells me where I'm going wrong. I can take criticism, so feel free.

All of my stories are also posted to kasman dot dreamwidth dot com and kasman dot livejournal dot com.

Also, please check out the Virtual Seasons 3 and 4 at dot livejournal dot com/ virtualda/ and my favourite playground at http:// postpulse dot conforums3 dot com.

I've also recently done a Supernatural Drive-verse adaptation of roqueclasique's Weird Scary External Fixators, which can be found here on my live journal: http:// kasman dot livejournal dot com/1191.html. And if you stop by for my re-write, please visit Roque's wonderful original story and drop her a line.

"Fan fiction is a way of the culture repairing the damage done in a system where contemporary myths are owned by corporations instead of owned by the folk." -Henry Jenkins, Textual Poachers (Thanks to my friend Green Eve for the quote - couldn't resist it).

"...someday I should post an essay about why the world is better off because of fan fiction." - Doris Egan in the Tightrope Walker's Journal http:// tightropegirl. livejournal. com/

Please do not archive any of my published stories or post links to them without first asking for permission. Generally speaking, if you ask, the answer is going to be yes.
