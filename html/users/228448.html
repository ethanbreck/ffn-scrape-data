Oh, gosh ... about me. I'm a single mom living in Oregon with my beautiful daughter. I am a practice manager for a small animal veterinary clinic. I enjoy writing, film editing and photography. I have been writing since I could pick up a pen and enjoy all kinds of fiction, not just fan fiction. I do however, think it's a real compliment to writers to have have their characters so profound that others would borrow them from time to time. I don't claim to be an amazing writer, but it keeps me sane. If others can enjoy it as well, it makes me happy.
