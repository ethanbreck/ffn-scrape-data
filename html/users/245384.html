Author has written 11 stories for Harry Potter, RENT, and Trigun.

I write predominately Harry Potter fan fiction. It flows the easiest. Everything that got deleted on here (because of lyrics. lame.) is now on www.astronomytower.org, so feel free to go and check it out. My name is briagabrielle on there. It includes Those Three Small Words Were Too Late...Right?, Kiss the Girl, an edited Down With Love, and I'm working on recovering Without You and putting it up. I just forget which computer I wrote it on. I hope it's not my old one or else I have to write it from scratch. I'm also moving My Best Friend's Wedding because it's only a matter of time.

Also, I like the Marauder era best. I don't know, there's something about the "Golden Trio" that just sits with me funny. Possibly because I cannot stand Harry and Hermione. :\

I can write fan fiction about other subjects. Obviously, I've done it. I think they're pretty decent. Nobody Needs to Know is backed up upon backups upon backups. It will not be lost forever. Most people seem to like that one the best.

A lot of the stuff on here is old. When I have free time (and once I get my own computer) I'll be editing it and reworking it to make it better, unless it was already rewritten for the Astronomy Tower or Nobody Needs to Know. In which case, it's done.
