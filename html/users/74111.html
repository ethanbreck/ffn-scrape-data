Author has written 13 stories for Gargoyles, Xena: Warrior Princess, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Final Fantasy VIII, Highlander, White Wolf, and Warcraft.

Ashti is one of the many names I go by. I'm a 20+ college student who has troubles finishing things. Including her education.Cue sweatdrop.I'm trying, but discipline and me, man, we don't really hang out.

I'm slightly talkative and can be quite tactile if I like you and think you'll let me. That doesn't keep me from being distant and unreachable at times. Heh.

Reading and writing are slightly to the top of the humongous list of things I enjoy. I used to be quite an accomplished draw.. er.. someone who is good at drawing, but inactivity as a result of a hand injury kind of put me out of the loop. I've tentatively picked up my pencils again, but as of now, I kind of suck, hehe.

I enjoy action and adventure stories, but I don't seem to be too capable at writing them myself. I'm trying to change that, and for that purpose I've put up new stories. Geared is my Mage story, and perhaps I should've been more descriptive in the summary, but since all I have up now is the teaser anyway ( strikeI'm greedy and hope to get reviews for that too XD /strike) but will put up chapter 1 shortly. I'm just looking at changing one sentence, or a word or so, so that's no problem.

To be added: I'm prodding World of Warcraft, and even though a NE Rogue is probably one of the most common things in the entire world, I hope that my story is at least entertaining enough that people will want to know what happens enough to prod me to effin´ update already (not so subtle hint. ;)

The sequel to Sin will not be added until I figure out a way to put it up in a way that resembles Sin. I am annoyingly stubborn about certain inconsequential things and lay-out is one of them. Ffnet also eats my tilde. What the heck? It's not as if it's tasty, or anything. Give it back. XD

Oh, and check out my favourite authors and stories! They're pretty varied, and also rad :D

Hopeful note: I'm pondering some Lord of the Rings fanfiction as well as some Dukes of Hazzard stuff, but I know better than to promise. Cross your fingers, lads 'n lasses. :)
