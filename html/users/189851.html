I'm a 31-year old graphic designer.

I'm a huge fan of Stargate Atlantis and to a lesser extent SG1. I used to ship Daniel/Janet but yeah since they killed her off that has wained a little. I hardly write any SG1 stories anymore and am fully concentrating on SGA. I know I have some unfinished fics in SG1, which I hope to one day be able to conclude. I'll read anything as long as these three ladies are included in the story.
