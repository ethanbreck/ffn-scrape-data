Fan of Buffy the Vampire Slayer since Welcome to the Hellmouth. My favorite characters are/were Spike, Tara, Faith, Willow, and Veruca. I can be a little shippy, though I usually go the way of the show. I was once a B/Aer, and I am a bit of a B/Ser (all hail Nekkid!Spike!). I'm just in it for the Nekkid!Spike, I'll admit. What? I'm supposed to have unseen depth? Pshah! I've also suddenly and irrantionally become a Dawn/Connor shipper. I don't know where it comes from. I don't like S6 Dawn. At all. But there it is. Am in desperate search of D/C fics.

I'm slowly being dragged into the murky depth of fanfic reading. Ewwww. I blame all my friends who write fanfic and make me read it. Yes, that is right, I blame YOU!

You can usually spot me at the Buffy Cross and Stake Spoiler Board or the Buffyverse Spoiler Board. I'm also a lurker on a variety of yahoo groups and message boards, from B/S, B/X (I say "Ewww!" with the deepest respect to my B/X friends), B/A, W/T, W/X, to just general BtVS, minus the posty-ness. Actually, you can also find me in some actual fanfiction. Check out my Favorite Stories section, with an eye toward Shell in Her Shell's fic and ThePeacemaker13746 fic.

I love humor and parodies, and am beginning to considering writing some BtVS fanfic in that vein... oh the horror!
