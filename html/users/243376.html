Author has written 2 stories for Fushigi Yuugi, and Harry Potter.

AAfter about 4 years of hiatus. I'm sad to say that I might never come back to writing. I stress the word might. Even though I may be much better at phrasing and my work will probably be less immature now. While I'm glad I had this experience of feedback and writing which helped me grow as a person, I just don't have the time to put down plot bunnies and the like. I'm pursuing a dentistry degree now and when I do have free time, I like to kick back, relax and perhaps read other people's wonderful work.

Thus, a short but unnecessary bio of moi.

Name: Jamie
Age: currently 20 years of age in the year 2008
Nick Names: Fiery-chan, Icey-chan in this fandom.

My fave characters are Tasuki(Ultimate fave) and Chichiri from FY, Rukawa and Fujima from SD, Tsuzuki from YnM, Touya from AnC, Kikumaru Eiji, Echizen Ryouma and Tezuka Kunimitsu from Prince of Tennis and blah blah blah i am sure you would be asleep if i do go on.

Finished works

FY- Where Do I Belong? - It's my baby and i'm proud of it even if i would like the change the first few chaps but am too lazy to.

Harry Potter - Silent Expression- My first slash one shot. Not too bad if i do say so myself! grins

Reasons for transition from obssessed reader/lurker to authoress:

Hmnn i guess its because of inspiration from ALL authors, encouragement from my author friends and a few other minor reasons. Still feel really insecure and low confidence level on my writing. But right now i am doing it for the treasured readers i have now and for my own pleasure.

However, I am now going back to lurker/readerdom.
