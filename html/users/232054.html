While I don't write much fanfiction anymore, I still have plenty of ideas rolling around in my head and I still love to read other people's work. Every now and again I'll do a little more work on an old project, but it's outright requests by fans that make me deliver.

This is still my number-one go-to site to read and write fanfiction, Livejournal will never replace it for me. Fanfiction.net has given so many people the encouraging outlet and excellent editing environment they need to start writing their own work. It started me on my path - I can't wait to see who else it touches.

~ Maya
