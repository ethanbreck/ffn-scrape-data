Pet Peeve: When characters recover from a concussion or serious injury in a day/couple hours. You can always tell the authors who either haven't done their research or haven't had a nasty blow to the head/broken bone. (Not that I recommend breaking a bone or smashing your face into a brick wall all in the name of research) but...Those that have know how horribly debilitating those types of injuries are. Even "mild" concussions will leave you whammied for days...and broken foot bones are torture to walk on...believe me, I know. So, seriously, unless the character has some super healing ablilities or the story realistically calls for it...don't insult the reader's intelligence, or in my case, memories of how long it can take to recover from these types of things. thanks.
