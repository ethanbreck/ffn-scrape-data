My Life, The Abbreviated Version

I just turned 25 years old, graduated from college a couple years ago and have lived in Iowa since I was born. I'm not necessarily against Iowa, but I would love to be able to travel more and see new, interesting places. I don't really wanna stay in one place for the rest of my life.

I am finally down to working only 1 job - thank God. I have more free time now, but less money - give & take, right?! I sell & service insurance in my hometown and the surrounding area. I've been in the insurance field for almost 5 years now.

When I do have some time to myself, I am either out of town at a concert or trying to write. I've loved writing ever since I was a child, but unfortunately I have a serious case of ADHD when I try to sit down and get into a story so they never turn out the way I want them to. (Which is why it took over a year to complete LTLA.)

I'm just your average twenty-something. I'm paying off my car and my student loans while trying to get some fun out of life and experiencing new things. I'm very independent and sarcastic. I don't take a lot of crap from people, because I don't feel that it's necessary. I think that everyone has a right to their own decisions minus the judgment of others. I believe highly in honesty and faithfulness.

I love movies, music, celebrity gossip, fashion, interior design, tattoos and mellow people. I have a really ecclectic taste in movies and music. Here are some of my faves.
