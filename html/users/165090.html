Hey, I'm finally back on Fanfiction.net. After the last few crashes, my previous account and stories were eaten by the Internet, so I'm starting fresh. ^_~

I am currently working on a manga series of my own, though the artwork is coming along a little slowly. ^_^' I'll be posting the scripts here as well as some of the fanfiction-versions of the scripts.
Anyway, my stories tend to be either really silly or really depressing, but sometimes both.

I'm completely open to all critique and commentary, even flames and death threats. My only request is that flames not be used in the reviews section. Flamers tend to use all CAPS, foul language, and bad grammar, all of which clutter the review page and disrupt the flow of text. If you want to send unintelligable, poorly formatted blather, please use my email so I can delete it with ease. ^_~
