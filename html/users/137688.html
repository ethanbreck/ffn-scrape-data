Update for 2018

GOOOOOOOOOOD MOOOOOORNING FAAAAAANFICTION.NET!

Got your attention yet me SHIPPING hearties? Hope so as I've got some updates to throw your way! ;)

Let me begin by saying that after a hella bad, long year and half I am finally on the road to feeling better. Though I still have more issues to deal with, the pain has eased up considerably, I have more energy then I did before, and I have thought up lots of new plot bunnies that I hope to turn into fics/shorts/etc before long. This update I won't bore you all with a drawn out menagerie of what's been going on nor will I keep apologising for my AWOLness as I've been doing. Nope ... none of tha maudlin stuff this time! Instead I'll simply move things along by giving you some of the plot bun-buns I've been shuffling about in my over-wild imagination, along with some other stuffs.

For starters - I will be going through my WIPs and weed out the ones I am likely not going to work on/update anytime soon. I know right off that I intend to redo "First Impressions" so will be pulling it shortly, along with "Remember Me Tomorrow." It's not that I dislike the fics mind, I just want to take things in different directions is all.

Seconds - I am going to be returning to work on my original novels/etc as well so I will do my best to update my fics as often as I can. If anyone is interested in taking a peek at my original work (including my 1st solo novel Blackwood Manor that I've been attempting to finish for about 15 yrs now lol), please drop me a shout and I'll be happy to get with you.

Thirds - Although I have an extremely vivid and wild imagination on my own merit, I am in need for an 'Idea Bouncer' for my HP fics and my original works. It never hurts to talk things out with others to see what might/won't/etc work best. There is NO set times/dates/whatnot for helping me out, whenever you're available that works for me. I admit that as my fingers aren't always up to snuff I would prefer to talk via a chat system if possible (if not it's fine, I have AIM, FB, etc for PMing instead). I have a private Vent channel I use for my WoW guild, or I can have my girls help me set up Discord or any other chat systems you'd prefer, I'm happy to oblige! So yeah, if you're interested in helping me out please let me know via FFN PM or email me as to a good day/time to catch up and also whether you'd be interested in fic, original, or both bounces ok?

Fourths - I know that some of my finished fics (Change of Seasons I know for sure has this) have some chapter ends cut off. I am going through each fic to fix this as quickly as I can so bear with me. Back then I used a demo programme that I didn't realise had a word count limit so I apologise for the issue. If anyone comes across this problem (you'll know it when you see it trust me) please contact me via PM and let me know which story/chapter(s) you found them so I can fix them right away. Much appreciated!

And Lastly - My fluffy bun-buns, being the over-active tykes they are, have a ... errr ... not so small list of what they'd like me to write lol ... so if you mates can maybe help me narrow a few ideas down I'd be forever grateful! Here goes a couple of my favourites so far --

1. My ever enjoyable "Time Travel" fics with our fav pairings. Places for TT could be:

Ancient Egypt (Middle Kingdom Era so Seti I to Tut's timelines) Ancient Roman Era (particularly Pompeii's ending time of 79 CE, or the arrival of Rome in Britian in 43 CE) 1800s Eras (perhaps Civil War era or the 'Golden Age of Artistians' (late 1800s)) World War II Era

2. Current Times of H/D still in Hogwarts (so 17 or 18ish ages) and how they (and everyone else) are dealing with the threats from Voldie.

3. Not far after H/D leave Hogwarts/death of Voldie and how everyone is dealing with picking up the pieces and going on with their lives.

4. A mainly Sev/Rem fic centred in either MWPP, current/post-H/D War, or the TT Eras (see above).

5. A Sev/OCM fic centred fic set in any of the #4 choices. Sev and/or OCM can be a vampire or other mythological creature (NOT just an animagus form though)

6. Normal pairings set in any times but someone is/becomes a vampire or vampeal (not Remus obviously :P) or another type of mythical creature (NO animagus forms).

7. A Fic based on a book, play, and/or film (I have already started fics based on: Pride & Prejudice, Groundhog Day, and 50 First Dates so either I can continue on them or move onto something else - either works for me).

I have plenty more (naturally, wouldn't be me if I didn't right?!), but for now we'll leave it at the above options. As for my personal favourites ... hmm ... TT is always at the top for me as I have so much fun with them! But ... a Sev/OCM combined with #6 sounds fun and something I haven't written in a long time ... and of course lately I'm partial to doing #7 things ... ugh! Honestly it's so hard to choose meh! That's why any help on narrowing the choices down would be lovely, and remember you can combine the ideas as well so a Time Travelling Sev/Rem or Sev/OCM where someone's a vampire/vampeal is just peachy, or something where they're travelling through various Times could be fun. Let's see where your imaginations run off to mates! :)

And to remind you all of my writing styles/etc so no one flames me that I did this, didn't do that, wrote someone to OOC, etc:

  1. First and foremost remember that I am an AU writer. I will not stick solely to canon, so don't ask me to because I won't. Far as I'm concerned if I wanted to read a canon fic then hell - I'd go re-read the books. The entire point of writing (and reading) FAN BASED FICTION is because you weren't or aren't 100% satisfied on the 'canon' that was written. So yeah ... if you can't take reading AU then I'm not the writer for you! :P
  2. I adore writing dark, angst-filled, plot-driven fics with the (usually) very happy endings, so expect that there will be twists and turns whenever possible.
  3. I DO NOT write 'sexy, smutty fics.' Doing hard-core sex scenes when writing on an 'adult RP group' is one thing (something I have done in the past and do enjoy when the time calls for it), but writing sex/smut for the public eye is totally another cauldron. I do not want to be known as a 'smut writer' be it in my fics or in my original works - I know smut sells better but ... it's just not me ... no see I prefer tastefully written love scenes for my public audience. Heck, it took me 3 days just to write the scene between Sev and Remus in 'An Experiment Gone Wrong,' and that is as hard-core as I'm likely to ever get again so do not expect more from me.
  4. Pairings will usually adhere to the ones I normally write (though I'm fond of throwing in odd pairings now and again so don't be surprised if you find some). For those that may not be wholly familiar with my work my 'normal' pairings are: Sev/Remus (I was the 1st person to officially create, launch and Captain the "Master and Wolf" SHIP on FictionAlley, as well as have spent all these years bringing many a non-believer over to the joys of this wonderful pairing ... and I hope to continue bringing folks onto my adored SHIP for years to come!), Sev/Remy/Siri, Harry/Draco, Siri/Peter (AU/Marauder days/TT/pre-betrayal times ONLY), and smatterings of Canon/OCM and Canon/OCFs on occasion.
  5. MPREG - personally I have no issues with it in other fics, but for my own part I will not write a full fic based around it. I have no problem indicating that a male is, hopes to be or has been pregnant, but it is almost always as a result of a spell/potion/or some other viable (or even wonky) situation that got them there. I also will not write any details involving giving birth/etc, if anything it is written as an 'off-screen' assumption it happened and not how/etc (basically no full-on details).
