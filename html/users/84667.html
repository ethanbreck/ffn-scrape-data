Ok. Time for a profile update.

I'm 17 and freshman in college. I love writing. I always have. And if you asked me why, I wouldn't be able to answer you. Maybe it's because of the feeling of power I get when I force the hand of each of the characters I write. Maybe it's because I can sympathize for all the characters I write for. Maybe it's because I'm just that passionate. Who knows.

Anyway, my current fic writing obsession thing is the Spider-Man movie. So you'll probably see a lot more of that in the future. Well, that is, until I get a new writing obsession. :grins:

And last, but not least, a couple of my stories feature the creative genius of Katrina Makani and Death's Angel. They're amazing. Both of them. Go check out their stuff. I'm sure you'll be pleasantly surprised.

Later!
