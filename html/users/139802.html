Hi! I'm 36 years old, and a wanna-be writer. But until an idea strikes me turning me into the next JK Rowling or Julie Garwood, I'll have to stick with showing my adoration of other authors works. I have several favorite authors, and read all kinds of books. For childrens books, it's J.K. Rowling and Lemony Snickett. For Romance Novels, it would be Julie Garwood, followed closely by Johanna Lindsey. For Young Adult books, L.J. Smith is at the top of my list. For fanfiction authors, I would have to say GinnyPotter387 and Agivega! These two could be (and should be) published authors in my opinion!
