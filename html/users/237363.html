My fanfiction career is effectively dead, as I have moved on to short stories and novel-length fiction that I plan on publishing as soon as they're in a publishable state. Still, I hate the fact that I have stories left unfinished, so when the mood strikes me, I try to work on them for as long as the mood lasts. If you enjoy my unfinished fanfics but you run up against the impenetrable wall of my writer's block, don't despair. The block occasionally moves a little bit, and I can squeeze a chapter or two through the crack. Just keep your expectations nonexistent (which is just as applicable elsewhere as it is here), and keep me on your alerts in case I miraculously update.
