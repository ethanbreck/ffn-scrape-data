Author has written 8 stories for Prince of Tennis.

The current fandom I am obsessed about is Prince of Tennis. My OTP is Tezuka/Ryoma, for the simple reasons that it is canon, it is beautiful and their tennis speaks volumes. I am fairly tolerant of all other slashy couplings in the fandom, except for Tezuka/Fuji. It might be because I adore Ryoma, it might be because I really can't see the two of them happening, but it's mostly because I read a wonderfully written Tezuka/Fuji fic and felt nauseous halfway through chapter one. I'm pretty sure that's telling me something.

Because of PoT, I am also obsessed with the Tenimyus, and ship Shirota/Yanagi. It's not just because it is Tezuka/Ryoma translated into 3D characters (that's just a plus!), but I fell in love with them after Hyoteimyu where Yanagi mauls Shirota and shoves Kazuki. Best. Scene. Ever. Also, they are obviously in love with each other--Shirotan has not picked up anyone as many times as he has Yanagi. It is absolutely adorable. (I also have a special place for Yuuya/Yanagi because their friendship is heartwarming and they're attached at the hip.)

Other series I like but do not really fic for are: Air Gear, Black Cat, Bleach, Count Cain, D. Grayman, Death Note, Demon Diary, Escaflowne, Eyeshield 21, Full Metal Alchemist, Furuba, Hana Yori Dango, Hana-Kimi, Hikaru no Go, I'll (Generation Basket), Inuyasha, Nana, Naruto, Ouran Host Club, Rurouni Kenshin, Saiyuki, Slam Dunk, and Yami no Matsuei.

This is mostly because a third of them have too many characters to remember and another third have plots I don't remember. The last third is series I want to fic for but have yet to get plotbunnies. I've ficced for Card Captor Sakura because it was my first true love, and I occasionally read Sailor Moon because it's just classic. I like CLAMP's works, Yuu Watase's works and various shonen-ai series.

I read Harry Potter, too, but then again, who doesn't?

Outside of fandom, I am a stressed-out teenager without enough time nor enough sleep. I detest standardized testing--I hate all forms of testing, actually. I like to hang out with my friends but my parents are stingy and lazy and refuse to take me anywhere. I have no life. I spend too much time on the computer.

I have far too many bad habits. My friends tell me I need to stop slashing everything. I don't do my summer homework in June and regret it by the end of August.
