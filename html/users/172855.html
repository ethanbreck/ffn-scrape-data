Once upon a time, I think I could safely say I was the only [srevan] on the internets, but that doesn't seem to be the case any more. Other once-upon-a-times include being a teenager, being a student, and writing lots of fanfiction. Not so much any more. In other words, this place is more like an archive than an active site to me.

Given that, I have to treat it like an archive, which means keeping things I would rather pretend never existed. I was not born writing at the level I am now, so unless you'd like examples of typical mid-to-late teen wish-fulfillment, poor attention to characterization, and beginner's writing, I'd stay away from... eh. Pretty much anything before 2005. The rest of it is okay, and some pieces I still really like.

Do not expect responses or updates from me. If a story on here isn't complete, then it's abandoned.
