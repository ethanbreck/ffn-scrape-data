Updates:

gaps An update?

Yes, you are reading that correctly. I have in fact updated Rising to Destiny. I know it has been a long time coming and I apologize for the long delay. I was truly pushing myself to having something posted by Sept. 17 and for a long time, it appeared I would not make it. Thankfully that was not the case due to last minute inspiration.

Hopefully, I will not fall into another funk like this last time. I am actually having a lot of fun writing chapter 16, where many new elements and twists will be revealed. I am not saying this chapter will done anytime soon (I have to do some traveling soon), but I am confident it will not take 2 years either.

Thank you for being patient with me.

Two websites for everyone to check out:

Artastica- my friend's Sparky's website, with her art, comics, and more (I'm even a character in her pokemon one!)

For The Love of Writer's Block- A message board create by sparky16, that I help run. It was designed for her story, For The Love of Lugia, but is a great place to ask questions about writing, get tips on your writing, plus any art (fan, realistic, comics, anime etc) that you do to. -coughs- plus there is a section to leave threats to the authors to update faster -coughs- again but you didn't hear that from me.

Sparky and I have a lot of fun on the site, talking with fellow authors and learning new tricks about writing, overcoming writer's block, and just talking about general things. Check it out, just be aware of Sparky's and I's randomness.
