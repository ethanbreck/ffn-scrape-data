Hiyas!

I've always enjoyed creative writing, simply because my attention span is rather short and I'm always having to be doing something or I get bored out of my rather empty skull. LOL But to be truly honest, I'm not particularly good at writing. LOL I much prefer to read what other people have written because it's more interesting to me. On the other hand, I love making up my own stories, though they tend to taper off sometimes. LOL I’m addicted to reading, fanfics specifically, and books in general. I have no idea why I became addicted to them, but I think it’s because they give me something interesting and entertaining to do while I waste my time on the computer. LOL It actually started off as a way to pass the time while I downloaded information for my assignments and stuff and it sort of blew out of proportion very quickly. LOL

I’m a great fan of yaoi/shounen ai/slash, but I’m not adverse to reading heterosexual, or non paired, fics as well. I also read some yuri/shoujo ai/femslash, depending on the fandom (mostly Read or Die). I love action and comedy, but I quite like romance and drama as well. I’m quite fond of dark fics because they tend to be better written (in most cases).
