Author has written 17 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Sailor Moon, Inuyasha, and Witch Hunter Robin.

I am a 3rd year university student now. I started dabbling into fanfiction in 2001, so it's been 7 years! Looking back at my work, my strengths lie in imagery, symbolism and wild concepts. However, my weaknesses lie in clarity, precision (or delivery) and punctuation. Hopefully, my writing has matured and improved as much as I have! After a 3 year hiatus to clear my head, I am back now to revive all my work. Thank you for all of your continual support, criticism and encouragement. Keep them coming ;)

-- Currently, I'm working on Epoch Augmented. I'm in the process of working out some kinks, so please bear with me. --
-- Also, all 50 chapters of The Black Dragon have been recovered and are in the process of being edited. --

Please visit my site, to get the latest updates on all of my work. I prematurely post things and re-edit them later, so if you want the fastest glimpse of my work, go to my website (It is updated frequently). Thank you.
