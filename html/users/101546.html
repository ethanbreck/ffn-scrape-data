Author has written 8 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, StarTrek: Deep Space Nine, Harry Potter, and Sailor Moon.

Feel free to PM me if you're looking for a beta-reader!

Fandoms and types of stories for which I'm particularly interested in beta-reading are as follows:

  • Twin Peaks, including: Fire Walk With Me and Twin Peaks The Return (I have a soft spot for Coop/Audrey but ANY Peaks fic is more than welcome, especially if it involves the overarching mythology - both in terms of Laura Palmer, the lodges and their denizens.)
  • Love Live! Sunshine!! (And more specifically, Kanan/Mari focused fics.)
  • Evangelion
  • Star Trek: Deep Space 9 (though anything in the franchise is fair game)
  • Game of Thrones
  • DBZ/Super (Really only interested in stories focus on Vegeta, whether they're mainly shipping/romance/pwp or something else entirely as long as they're heavy on the Vegeta. But of course I do love a good VegeBul or KakaVeg, ha.)
  • Life is Strange LiS: Before the Storm
  • Sailor Moon (Manga, the classic anime, Crystal, even the musicals and the live action PGSM I'm game for. Especially looking for yuri pairings, Venus/Mars is a favorite, for instance, but I'm open to all. Shittenou/Senshi pairings are also nice, though I equally adore Naru/Nephrite.)
  • You can still ask me to beta if your story doesn't fit on this list! I love editing.

    • I also enjoy simply discussing good fics (and getting recs featuring odd pairings, or pairings that I've also written about) as well as general fandom chat.

    I find myself obsessed with odd pairings. Unrequited love, age differences, forbidden romance: Most of my fics focus on such things. Het/slash/femmeslash are all fair game, though most of my stuff tends to be het. I can be fickle with fandoms. However, the following comprise the old guard of my preferred series and have remained constant favorites - Sailor Moon, Harry Potter, Buffy, Evangelion, and Star Trek.

    I've deleted all the fics that were originally here (10 years makes most of my stuff pretty cringe-worthy in retrospect) but in time I may rework some of those older stories, and every so often I upload a piece that is a bit more recent. Speaking of recent stuff, most of my writing energy goes toward RP these days. I have quite a few works in progress, but finding the time/motivation/inspiration to complete them has been an issue... hence being lazy and getting my writing fix from editing the stories of others and doing RP, haha.

    I LOVE constructive criticism. It is one of the most important ways to improve as a writer. I would absolutely adore more comments or PMs offering constructive criticism, so please don't hold back!
