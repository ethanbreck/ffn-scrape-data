The Baka Mushroom is here. gasp world ends

I love anime. I love games. I love jdramas/kdramas. I love music. I love a lot of stuff. Yay for me. .

I watch more anime and jdramas/kdramas than I eat rice. And that's saying a lot. I've actually watched or read every single series on the anime page on fanfiction.net. Whole lot more, actually. For some reason...I like to stick with the classic anime series when writing fics. Right, they're not really classic (except maybe sailor moon), but classic for me = my past favorites. XD

Tired of losing to the last boss in that final fantasy game? Sick of visiting your insert number friends on myspace? Not another episode of Oprah? Well, I'm here to relieve you of your pain. coughyeahrightcough.

Ahem, actually I love FF games, and Oprah's not that bad of a show either. I hate myspace though. Anyway...

I'll write for whatever couple I love most in a series...whether it be a het, shounen-ai, or shoujo-ai. ~whoosh.
