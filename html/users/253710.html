General Profile:
My name is Cherri. I am an intermediate fanfiction writer. I am a female and live on the sunny island of Guam. My birthday is on 1/18. I am a student and like art, photography and writing. I take many interests in roleplaying, web designing and just being with my good friends. I like anime, manga, vintage things, sweet foods and chocolate.

Anime & Manga...: I like inhales Cardcaptor Sakura, Ranma 1/2, Bleach, Samurai Champloo, Inuyasha, Kareshi Kanojo no Jijyo, Cowboy Bebop, Chobits, One Piece, Shaman King, Fruits Basket... and loads of other ones. =D

... and their couples: Sakura x Syaoran, Eriol x Tomoyo, Yamazaki x Chiharu, Ranma x Akane, Renji x Rukia x Ichigo (Sorry, Abarai-kun.), Fuu x Mugen (don't ask why.. please), Arima x Miyazawa, (Eck. Yaoi?) Arima x Asapin, Luffy x Nami, Yoh x Anna, Kyou x Tohru, and yes.. continue.

Books: I am merely a hopeless romantic... so I like romance novels by Nicholas Sparks, like The Guardian, The Notebook, Message In A Bottle, The Rescue, and others by him. I also like the Harry Potter series and the Artemis Fowl series. And I did enjoy The Da Vinci Code.

Sites:
Ara... ehm... Peace Of Mind is my personal site with loads of stuff about me, weird comments on the world and a bit of ranting. Mon Cherri is just my (kinda) daily blog... with stuff about my life as I track it down. I do have more, but they are mainly fanlistings. =D
