Author has written 5 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Legend of Zelda, and Inuyasha.

Oh fanfiction, my love, how I've missed you dearly. But I'm back!

I started writing fanfiction as freshman in highschool (terrible though it was), and now I'm a twenty something girl, with a major in fiction. We've come a long way, baby.

"Let Sleeping Dogs Lie" will forever be my baby, and will continue to be updated. But I won't lie, I cringe when I read the beginning chapters. If you give the story a chance, and read it through, I'd say you can definitely tell how the writing improves as I age. The later chapters just keep getting better and better. For everyone who's been with me all this time, thanks for your support! You've inspired me to try and make it as a real writer, haha.

"Simple Things" is my newest story, and coincidentally the best to date. I hope to have a chapter out per week, if not more! Please enjoy it. :bows:

If you couldn't tell already, I seem to have a thing for the dog motif. :Shrugs:

"Protect us from Ourselves" is temporarily stuck in almost oneshot form. I'm too distracted by "Simple Things" at the moment, and I'd rather not have the plots/vibes get intermixed. Check back in the future for updates on that one, but in the mean time, check out "Simple Things"! (hmm... too much hinting, me thinks.)

To all the fantastic reviewers: You are the reason I write! I love hearing what you have to say, and I'll try and respond to each and every review I get. I've been gone so long that this "reply to the review" feature is new to me! - But I'm excited about it.
