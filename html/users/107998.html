Emma

Hi! My name is Emma and I'm a 20-year-old teacher student living in the north of Sweden in a small community called Vindeln, where I have livedmy whole life (I still live with my parents to save money while I go to University). So far I have studied English for three terms, andlast fall I spent six weeks in Leeds, England. I will also study Swedish as well as some teacher training courses before I'm done, which will be in about three years..
Here in Sweden we begin our studies of English when we are fairly young. I began when I was about nine or ten, but now children start even earlier. I do want to make a point of something. This does not make me an expert in English. Believe me!After spending six weeks in England I noticed that I make many mistakes and I probably make even more that I don't even know about.I reallt hope that you won't mind some of my spelling, and grammar mistakes and I alsowill have some problems with the names of places and locations in the US, and most definitly with the military stuff. this has already been pointed out to me.

I believe that all feedback is good feedback, as long as it's constructive. I don't want people to just tell me that my stories are bad, even if that is what they think. Please give me a reason why you don't like it. I also don't want people telling me that my story lines are wrong. When I write a story, it's my story and I will decide what will happen. If you don't like it - write your own story!

I began watching "JAG" when I was about 13 years old and the first episode I watched was a re-run of "Full Engagement", followed by the beginning of season four and "Gypsy Eyes". I was hooked from the start, and now nothing can keep me from watching this wonderful show.
I started reading fanfiction when I was about 17 (mostly JAG, Diagnosis Murder and Kung Fu: The Legend Continues) and now not a day goes by without me checking my favourite sites at least twice a day. I'm obsessed, I know!

I am a hopeless romantic and for a long time now fanfiction has made my dreams come true, bringing Harm and Mac together. Now that the show has ended and I have gotten my happy ending, I hope that all of you great writers out there will continue writing your stories. I will definitely keep reading. I don't think I wille ver feel about any other show the way I feel about JAG. After hearing about DJE's leaving the show I hoped it would end, and that it would end with the same class it was brought to us for ten years. I got my wish.

As you can see, I have written a few stories, but I would rather call myself a 'reader' than a 'writer'. I'll leave that part to my dear friend Rabea who has written some great stories and poems (no fanfiction, only original work). You can find them here: http:www.fictionpress.com/profile.php?userid=436328

This is me! If you have any questions, please let send me an e-mail. I'm always interested in making new friends.

Thank You!

One of my other obsessions is "The West Wing", but this is not something I like to read or write about (with the exception of Aerogirl's story "Clearing Skies").
