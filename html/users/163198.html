"I like to write stories. Realy weird ones you have been warned. Most of them are about animals. When I get older I want to be a novelist of a Journalist. My favorite colour is red. My favorite food is Spaghetti. I like to listen to music and read too."

Obviously this was writen along time ago...four years ago to be exact. From reading the above paragraph it is apparent that its time to update my profile.

So here it is:

I am working on my way to becoming a journalist or novelist. In fact I recently graduated from college in 2005. As of now I am currently enrolled in university in Australia.

I still like to read and my favourite colour is red. Like that really matters anyways and its true, my stories tend to be a touch weird but I can write serious ones. Lately a lot of my stories have been inspired by my friends in real life. Also when I write I tend to switch from past tense to present tense or present tense to past tense. Its something I am working on.
