Hello and welcome to my Fanfiction.net author profile.

I really don't know what to say. I haven't written fanfiction in a long, long time, at least nothing worth sharing. Nowadays I just wander back here occasionally to re-read old favorites and discover a few new favorites. My old work may still disappear at some point, but for now it's useful to keep it so that I know where to find it.

Here's a list of genres I enjoy reading:

Wheel of Time: I used to love the random humor fics, but nowadays I prefer more serious fics. I especially like fics that fill in little gaps in the canon. I love anything involving Mat and Tuon, and anything regarding the Forsaken, but other characters will catch my interest too if the plot is good and at least semi-believable.
Daria: I love Daria and Trent fanfics. No shame there. But I prefer that the characters are kept fairly well in character.
Pokemon: The anime is usually too "happy sunshine rainbows" for my taste, so I find myself drawn to well-written, creative OT fics.
Digimon: Hmmm... I can't say I like anything in particular here. It's just whatever story happens to catch my eye. I tend to look for fanfics featuring Kari (Hikari), TK (Takeru), Gatomon (Tailmon), or Patamon. Of course it has to be well-written and have a decent plot.
Inuyasha: I'm not too particular here. Just whatever story whatever catches my eye as long as it stays fairly true to the characters.
Enchanted Forest: It's hard to find good fanfics here, but I continue to try.
Descendants: I've just started digging into this fandom because I loved the film and the books.

I love Harry Potter, but most of the fics out there are so far off-canon that I can't stomach them.

Highly Recommended:

Wheel of Time
"Omens" by Millia
"Moonlit Walks" by EvilChani
"Forsaken Tales" by Asmodean

Daria

Pokemon

Legend of Dragoon

Thanks for visiting. Happy reading.
