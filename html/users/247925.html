10-15-05

I just checked an old decrepit email account and discovered the minor dismay over me abondoning my fic. Well. I do feel bad- I hate it when authors do that. I'll sum up my reasons in one word: calculus.

I may or may not try writing it later- you have to realize, I certainly don't have any of the pages of notes I had relating to my story, having abandoned it 1-2 years ago. I've also seen a few other stories pick up a similar plot line, which kind of dulls the fun of writing it.

Perhaps a new fanfiction. We shall see.
