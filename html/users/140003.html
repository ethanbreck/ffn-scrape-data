And when she feels like this she: Trys to remember what her homework is...

What she said just then: Great, can't believe I forgot my maths textbook again...

I wanna have a summer break -.- but I can’t, because I’m in the southern hemisphere (Good'o New Zealand). While the people of the north get a summer holiday I get to write a prose on some old fella.

Do I want to move to USA right now? Yes, yes I do. But I’ve been learning the metric system for a decade. And I’m not switching to miles and gallons on the basis that I’m lazy.

I have a whole load of mind lint I need to clear up right now. If you'd like to read it, scroll down the page.

What do I like right now…

X-men Evolution
HALO
DoA
Soul Caliber II (when it comes out in New Zealand)
Zoids and a little bit of Cardcaptor Sakura

I used to like Cardcaptors a lot more before the mary sues took over most of fandom. Now all I see everywhere is pairings, oh lord the irrational pairings.

My fav characters of X-men are… actually I really don’t have a favorite. I like Lance (because I like earthquakes), I also don’t mind Pietro (Because he can… er… run fast?) and I occasionally talk to St. John Allerdyce (Pyro for all you new-age fangirls) because it’s nice to share your maniac comments sometimes.

I like laughing insanely. I like writing about things that don’t make sense. I like the new generation of plasma weapons. I like irony, sarcasm and insanity in general. I also like humor/parody.

I don’t like celery.

And right now I’m eating a milky bar (yum yum)
