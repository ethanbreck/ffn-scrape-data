Author has written 11 stories for Final Fantasy VIII.

February 2007 - Almost four years have passed since I last posted any fanfiction. I'm afraid I lack the inspiration to even write original fiction, save for the occasionally awful bit of poetry. I have bits and drabbles scattered throughout various notebooks, but I will not hold my breath in hopes of posting new writing any time soon. However, I won't be discouraged. College is a hectic time, and I am in the transition of becoming an independent adult, which can be stressful and exciting all at the same time.

Thank you to anyone who has reviewed my old writings. Your kind and constructive words are much appreciated, as it is great to know that people are still occasionally reading them. My only hope is that if I ever do post a new bit of fanfiction, the reader will be able to see a great difference in style from my days as a high school sophomore to my role now as a college junior/senior.

Currently, I am a college junior (technically third year senior, but I still feel like a junior) who is majoring in music theatre and minoring in French. I hope to attend graduate school for costume design and make up, and eventually work as an actor and costume designer in various mediums. I am a self labeled geek, and I take pride in being one. My fandoms include:

Final Fantasy VIII (which I believe all of my fanfiction is set in this universe)

Fullmetal Alchemist (both anime and manga versions)

Beauty and the Beast

Escaflowne

Treasure Planet

Kingdom Hearts

Gilmore Girls

Besides fandoms, I enjoy knitting, singing, reading manga, art, history, and music. I am also a seriously devoted Goo Goo Dolls fan, and I take insults against my taste in music personally. I spend way too much time on Wikipedia, learning useless facts that no one else really cares about. I'm messy and disorganized and it is amazing that my room mate does not try to kill me for it. I like crayons and coloring books and My Little Ponies and Disney movies. I like trying to tap into my inner child every so often. I may not look like a girly girl, but I enjoy fashion and make up and ankle breaking stilettos just as much as the next Cosmo reader.
