Author has written 4 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Lord of the Rings.

Wow, it really has been a long time since I've updated my profile or even visited ff.net, but I suppose I should offer up some explanation before the wind blows me away again for the next few years or so.

I am 25 year-old aspiring filmmaker (read: starving artist) and have been involved in fandom - beginning with my first love, Gundam Wing - since the age of 13. From GW I moved along to Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, CSI, Young Guns, and a few short flings with other fandoms. I still write fanfiction today, although I can admit to reading it a lot more than I tend to write, much to the chagrin of a few of my friends (and most loyal readers). Most of my writing is done for myself or with a close friend, and no intention to publish.

Despite my love for fandom, I do not plan to post more fanfiction here. I still use this profile to review others' stories, so hello! if you're here after I've reviewed your story. It probably meant I liked it a hell of a lot! I will continue to keep this profile here with my two poems and two short stories as a remembrance - mostly for myself. I am not expecting anyone else to read or review my stories as they were written so long ago. This will remain here to remind myself I of who I was and the abilities I had (which have since positively developed in great abundance).

And so, I bid you all adieu, merci, and arigato for all the memories.
