8/4/2008

Oh My God. I have not updated anything in so long. I'm so very sorry!

School has taken over my life, and then my job decided to add to the problem.

I do plan to finish all of my stories, even if I have to tie the muses down and force the issue. I will be working on Endless Night until I get a chapter up. I have to do a little more research into the Justice League characters, no reason for this to be half-@ed.

I am a perfectionist without a Beta to harass me into working on my stories, so if anyone would like to help me, either through harassment or as a Beta, or both (I'm not picky) I would be great full.

Update Plans: I will not offer dates at this point, I would disappoint all of us. I will work on Endless Night first, with plans to finish that before I work on One of Many. Little short, one page, standalones may show up in different fandoms when they have pecked at my brain to hard for me to work on anything else. I will not start any other long stories until I have finished what I have started and not finished...sounds a little better then ignored, right?

Thank you all for your patience I will work hard to prove it has been worth it!
