HEEEEEELLLLLOOOOOOOOO, EVERYONE!!!!!!!!

Definitely thought it would be a good idea to finally update my profile here...just for informational purposes. I should probably remember to update this thing at least once a year or something. Let's see what's new with me.

Time of writing this: January 2005 the start of yet another year where the worlddidn't blow up or something...yet anyway...I know, depressing thought...CHANGE OF SUBJECT! ^_^

Last time I udpated I just graduated from the Local College. Now...been in the working world for well over a year at my current job. Gets mea good pay check.

I'm a Canadian born and raised. My reading tastes are still basically the same; mainly drama, some action/adventure, some comedy and even a little romace every now and then.

Got several fandoms I report to on a regular basis. Gargoyles, Kim Possible, He-man mainly, but I occasionally check out X-men: Evolution, Teen Titans (cartoon, not comic book), couple of movies, lots of animes, and just anything that catches my eye, which can be quite diverse.

I'm still a kid at heart, always have been (andif I have anything to sayabout it) and probably always will be.

SORRY, SO SORRY about my lack of updating to my few select fans, but procrastination has always been one of my faults that I can't seem to shake. Getting OFF my butt and MAKING the time to update is about my biggest personal fault...that I know of.

SCI-FI still rules in my book. Oh yeah your Star Treks, your Star Wars, B5, and just about ANY sci-fi that's ANIME, etc etc etc, you know. But hey...Adventure, comedy and even Drama are fun too.

Like to give a shout out to some of the great pals I've met here:

FairDrea; Romance/Drama writer extrordinaire: has works in several fandoms, and is the first great author I read here. Also a girl after my own heart at times. She's been a good buddy for some time. Congradulate her, she's getting hitched to her boyfriend...when, I don't know. And just to clarify for those who might not have guessed already...no the boyfriend ain't me. She's been dating that guy for years, way before she heard of me and I'm like 2000 miles away...do the math people... I hope this has been enlightening ^_^

(I know...I'm being stupid...but not as stupid as SOME of the stuff I've seen here)

Classic Cowboy; Action adventure, drama, and angst man supreme: He calls several fandoms home... everything from Pokemon, to He-man, but his best stuff is in Kim Possible. He's also got more fic IDEAS and some on hiatus than you can shake a 10ft stick at. But he'll get around to them... I think/hope. =^P Keep on writing Bro!

Geor-sama; met him just recently, but he has some Good stories in Kim Possible, and we're getting along just fine: not much to say here. Read one of his stories... reviewed it... review got his attention... we chatted bout some stuff... we chatted some more...that's about it. Not much else to say so far.

Also like to pay respect to authors like Wyrd Sister, Nikki Dee, Christine Morgan, Storyseeker, Spacebabie (if she's here anywhere), Jerizanna, Darwin, Meya Orion, Kay3 and so many others that their names escape me right now. I don't talk to them as often as the others, but they all have good stories you guys/girls might wanna check out. Go ahead and check my favorite authors and stories pages... have some choice cuts there from all over the place. And they're in there for one reason or another.

Finally to those who might want to see my adventure fic update: I'm afraid at the moment I'm at a stand still... meaning I gotta get off my butt and make the time for it. Don't know when that'll be. But we'll just have to wait and see what the future holds. =^)
