Lucy, 19, now sadly only an occasional writer, due to time and inclination.

To check the quality of my work - check its published date. Chances are, the newer stuff will be better.

Key to my summaries:
oneshot - this piece is a one-off. It will not be continued.
unfinished - this piece is, for now, on hiatus. It is still visible because one day I intend to continue it. That day will not come quicker the more you badger me.
complete - this is a complete, chaptered work.
work in progress - this is an incomplete, chaptered story that is currently being written and posted.
drabble - a short, standalone, completed piece of one hundred words only.

My main fandoms are Friends, Will & Grace, Holby City, Harry Potter, and Desperate Housewives. Within them, I ship (and occasionally write) Phoebe/Rachel, Monica/Chandler, Grace/Karen, Ric/Diane, Tom/Anita, Sirius/Remus, and Bree/Lynette. As you can tell from that, I sometimes write femmeslash or slash. Don't like? Don't read. I don't care for your flames about my opinions or lifestyle.

I welcome constructive criticism.
