Wow, it has been a long time since I updated... Sorry anyone who actually reads them. I am trying to write more, but I personally think I'm a mediocre writer, and I feel that the readers of ff.net don't need another mediocre writer. I wrote another little piece that I have had the inspiration to get done, and I hope that means that I might be able to write more... Oh well.

I like all kinds of fics... I greatly enjoy Yaoi, but I can't write it... I guess I'm too much of a girl... :sigh: Anyway, my favorite characters to read and write about are:

Severus Snape (HP)
Faith (BtVS)
Draco Malfoy (HP)
Luna Lovegood (HP)
Lily Evans (HP)
Spike (BtVS)

Yeah... those are the ones I can really think of now, but I really do like fics that aren't HP and/or BtVS. If you look at my favorite stories list I believe I do have one that is not from those! :hangs head: I am a hopeless fanatic...

Recently I have fallen out of my 'must read Spaith' phase, and moved into my 'aww, Draco/Luna, Chou Kawaii!' phase. I think that I shall never recover from my "po' Sevvie needs his Lily" addiction. Ah well... I still search for a good Faith/Andrew and a good Severus/Faith... I know... weird ships but... I think F/A would be sooooooo cute, and Sev/F would just rule... I had a good idea for a possible fic for that but... again I am mediocre and I can never finish anything...

I think that is all of it. If you read my fics and get really interested, sorry, but I am trying to update. Try reading one of my favorite stories.

I recently got on a "joining" kick, and joined like... 10 C2 groups... I am such a dweeb...
