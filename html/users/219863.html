Author has written 27 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, StarTrek: Voyager, Criminal Minds, and Call the Midwife.

Recent convert to Call the Midwife - due in large part to the way they have developed Patsy and Delia's storyline so sensitively. It was when Heidi Thomas said that CtM was not a soap so personal lives would only get small glimpses as part of the main story that my interest was piqued to fill in the blanks. It does help that Emerald Fennell and Kate Lamb appear to have such a wonderful on-screen chemistry.

I have previously written Criminal Minds Fic, Buffy the Vampire Fic and Star Trek Voyager - so I do like a bit of variety.
