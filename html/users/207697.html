Greetings to all of you who somehow found their way over here! I am known by some as Alectai, the Whimsical Wanderer, and due to several incidents that happened in a short period of time, I took to writing once in a while to amusemyself, as well as develop my own skills when the day comes that I attempt to write professionally.

NEWS: I have begun production on the "Twist of Fate" series, beginning with a shot at Magic Knight Rayearth. Unlike the Self Insertion Project though, these stories are only loosely connected by one common theme. "How Poking Fate With A Stick actually DOES make a slight change... Which eventually snowballs into an entire Alternate Universe"

Taking suggestions as for other series to do a Twist of Fate with, keep in mind though, I prefer fantasy/sci-fi series, and would infinitely prefer it to be a reasonable size soI can do my research on it.

In other news, I will be inconvienently missing for the entire summer it seems, on the plus-side though, by the time I return, I should be able to go on a proverbial blitzkrieg on my Projects though!

Current Projects:

"What am I supposed to be doing here?": (Non Mary-Sue, SI-MegaCrossover) The second part of the saga begun in "Because it seemed like a good idea at the time". Having had the support of the local Arch-Demon Louis removed at a rather inconvenient time, Sei has been banished from the Ranma/Sailor Moon-Crossoververse, and sent to a rather dark, dreary place, and the only way to get out would be to support the efforts of the local ruler in gathering a few oddly shaped artifacts...

STATUS: About a thousand words, but fairly blocked from going much further
ETA: When Hell Freezes Over

"Rayearth: Another Tale": (The first of possibly many "Twist of Fate" Stories) Though not quite a whim, as I've been mulling the idea in my head for about a year now after reading the Manga, my recent "Illicit Acquirement" of the animated series has allowed me to actually begin writing this.

It's essentially one of the many attempts at slipping another character into the dynamic of the Magic Knights. As is my wont though, I intend to take the concept and make it respectable for a change. For instance, Kazuki (Said New Character) Wasn't actually CALLED, he just happened to proverbially "Poke Fate With a Stick" at the same time the girls were called to Cephiro, because of this, he accidentally got caught in the portal as well. He's also NOT a Magic Knight for a change:)

STATUS: First chapter uploaded, second in production
ETA: Two Days

Halted Projects:

"Because it seemed like a good idea at the time": A Self-Insertion Experiment, this is for the dual-purpose of improving my skills with an easy concept, and to see if it's within my power to write one of the highly rare, "Good" SI Fics.

Reason: It's just not coming to me I'm afraid, no other reason then that _

Dropped Projects:

"Another Side-NARUTO": Dropped, though sometime in the future, I might do something else with that universe...
