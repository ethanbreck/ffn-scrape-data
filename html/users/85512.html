My name's Erica. I am a 28 yo pharmacist.

Depending on moods and current obsessions, I read fiction for fandoms listed below. I like angsty, romantic stuff that ends happily, and I'm not much of a writer. I read. The... persistent... obsessions are 2, 3, and 5.

Favorite Couples (In no order):
1) Hairspray - Link Larkin & Tracy Turnblad
2) The Phantom of the Opera - Raoul de Chagny & Christine Daae (Because if I were her, I'd want him.)
3) Sailor Moon - Serena/Usagi & Darien/Mamoru
4) Peter Pan - Wendy Darling & Peter Pan
5) Smallville - Clark Kent & Chloe Sullivan (In a world where Chloe Sullivan exists, it must be her.)
6) Chronicles of Narnia (movieverse) - Caspian X & Susan Pevensie (If they didn't want the ship, they should've recast!)
7) CSI: Crime Scene Investigation - Gil Grissom & Sara Sidle

Fanfiction Pet-Peeves:
1) Incomplete fics. I always check if a fic is finished before starting. If incomplete or a WIP, there's a 90% chance I'll skip. Why? Because I understand inspiration is fickle, but I'm not going to put myself through the wait or uncertainty.
2) For the love of everything and anything good and holy: Formatting. Grammar. Spelling.
3) Avatars and marysues.
4) Songfics and poetry. People just tend to... copy and comment. Plus, unless I know the song, how will I know the melody?
5) Censoring of adult situations. It can be very tastefully executed, and besides, smut is fun!

I do not have favorite authors. I do, however, have favorite stories, which are set to high standards and must be complete. This doesn't mean that I don't recognize talent; I'd just never read it again. And if I'm absolutely loving a fic and it never gets finished, well, I'd rather just never be reminded of it. The stories in my favorites are those I've read and will read again.
