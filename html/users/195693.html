14 Novemember 2004: Wow! When was the last time I went here? It's been over a year and it's unbelievable what has happened and what has changed! I'm slightly less Harry Potter obsessed and I haven't written any fanfiction in a long while. I'm currently working on writing my own novel set in a world very much like ours and it's a dark romance. I've sorted the general plot, characters and setting.

HARRY POTTER:

Character: Draco Malfoy! Actually, I think the obsession is more of Tom Felton (the actor that plays Draco in the films) than the actual, Draco Malfoy! Hermione Granger, I like Hermione Granger because she is Hermione Granger, not, an obsession with the actress (God, I hope not). I don’t like Harry or Ron, much really. Funny, isn’t it?

Book: Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. I think, so now, anyway. It was Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets. So, Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, then, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, then Harry Potter and Prisoner of Azkaban, then the first book. I think the first book is the worst out of them all; it’s the most boring one. Does anyone agree?

I’ll let you into a secret - *whispers* There is a lot more of Draco Malfoy on screen in the second film! Whopee!

Actor: Tom Felton, duh! Of course! He is well cute!

I don’t know why, but I don’t really like Ginny.

Shipper: ONLY Hermione/Draco. I don’t write anything else! I will rarely read Draco/Ginny but very rarely, I’ve read some Hermione/Oliver before. And, I’m reading some Harry/Hermione at the moment. Even though, I think Ron/Hermione would be together in the books. I just don’t like Ron!
