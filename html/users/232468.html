Actually got around to doing a bio for the fanfiction site, since I got tired of seeing the same one on here and my fictionpress account. All in all, people seem to want to think I’m a lot more interesting than I think I am. But, hey, I’m not going to kill their delusions, since they favor me. Laughs

Now, things about me that seem to amuse people.
I am utterly marvelous in the area of acting like a moron to cheer other people up; I get a lot of practice.
I can go from uberhappy to morbid in about ten seconds flat.
I live on caffeine and yogurt, because what else do you really need in life, right?
I’m twentyone and I still live at home, and I blame that on the fact I am trying to become a better artist.
Eh, I figure it will work for a few more months at least. Laughs
I’m loud and shy at the same time, and I’m somewhere between bisexual and pansexual by nature.
I’m horribly addicted to role play chats and tend to forget who I am.
People call me a multitude of names, most of which are the names of my rp characters, depending on my moods.

Otherwise I’m normal, I think.

Things that I like:
Cartoons, never too old for them.
Music, I love all sorts of different music.
Drawing, such great mental therapy.
People, such fun.
Pyschoanalyzing myself.

To read: Slash/yaoi, not the smut though, usually just the spiffy sad stuff.

HondaxJoey

HondaxOtogi

WolfwoodxVash

AngelxSpike

just about any LotR slash.

Sad stories in general, I like the depressing stuff.
Original works, I love people’s own work.
I have a serious addiction to LotR fiction of all sorts, I spend hours reading it when I should be doing anything else.
Faramir however, rates high above all others in my opinion, followed closely by Merry.

To write myself:
Original stuff, too many voice in my head to keep them all under wraps.
Sappy love stories, gotta love those tear jerking moments.
Analytical points of view stories, love getting into a character’s head.

Things I can’t stand:
People with intolerance issues.
The color yellow.
People that tell me I need more sleep, I do not.
Breaking my pencil in the middle of a sketch.
Mirrors.

And that’s me, eh, for the most part. Though if you have some desire to talk to me about anything, stories, life in general, whatever, feel free to AIM me, YamiJessi
Or use my main email: Yami_Jessi@hotmail.com to contact me about archives of my works, fiction requests, or any questions/comments about anything here. Or if you just want to say hello that would be great too. :)

Things in the works

Current project is a lengthy and involved HondaxSeto storyline.
