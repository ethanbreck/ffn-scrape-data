Some things will move faster than others and I have every intention of finishing even the slower ones.

Currently plotting a sequel to those four Tomb Raider fics, as well as some other misc stories and fandoms, and dealing with some writer's block which has effectively put a hold on the Star Wars: TFA saga I was posting.
