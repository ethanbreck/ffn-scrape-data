Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, and Initial D.
DrMM (aka Michelle) is 28 years old and recently returned to college after she realized that her English and History degrees were useless when it came to finding a job in a field she actually enjoyed. She currently is going to school to be a Radiologic Technologist and plans on getting a degree in Medical Imaging. At one point in time, she was both a certified EMT and lifeguard, although both of those certifications expired when she moved to Japan for two years to (pretend to) teach English. She loves anime and manga as well as reading and writing.

DrMM's current or past obsessions include One Piece (Zoro), Hikaru no Go (Sai), Shamen King, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine (Odo/Kira!) Highlander (Methos = fascinating), Zorro (the 1990's Family Channel version) and Severus Snape from Harry Potter. She loves Harry Potter doujinshi and has posted about them on Addiction: A (mostly) Harry Potter Doujinshi Website.
