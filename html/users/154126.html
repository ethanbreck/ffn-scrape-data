A 26 year old who enjoys reading and/or writing in various fandoms, to include Fushigi Yuugi, Weiss Kreuz, Yoroiden Samurai Troopers, X, Stargate SG-1, Tokyo Babylon, and Harry Potter. I love cannon and AU plot-driven gen stories, but relationship fics are also good. I often enjoy well-written slash/yaoi pairings as well as unusual 'ships that are not well-represented.

I used to keep my Harry Potter fic under a different penname, but I'm in the process of consolidating it under this name. Expect many drabbles, as that seems to be all I can consistently produce. I do have a few epic WIPs sitting on my hard drive, but past experience has taught me it's much better for me to finish a story before I try to post it.
