Author has written 7 stories for Final Fantasy VII, and Fairy Tales.

Helloes! I write a lot, but I have a hard time ending stories, so I don't post anything new that often. I'll probably stay mostly in the Final Fantasy realm, because I have good luck with those characters, but don't be surprised to see a Pirates of the Caribbean story pop up someday (I think I have one around here somewhere...), and I'm always open to new things. Hmm...what else? I read a lot of Terry Pratchett (too much, probably), so don't be at all surprised if random Pratchett phrases pop up in my stories. I'm far too easily amused. On that note, have a warning: I make fun of fangirls. I realize I am one, but we are pretty funny sometimes.

To be specific, I'm rather partial to the Final Fantasy VII universe, especially anything regarding Vincent or Sephiroth. I am of the (fairly large, it seems) group of people who believe that Seph was insane, not evil, so don't expect any manic laughter from anyone other than Hojo. Oh, how I hate Hojo. But that's another rant altogether. I also...well, hate is a bit strong. I don't like Yuffietine. Period. It makes me swear, for reasons others have pointed out, on top of the fact that I just flat don't like Yuffie. I also, despite my favorite character choices, don't tolerate emo that well. Or, in fact, pretty much at all. If I see myself heading down the emo path, I smack myself, trust me. If someone else sees it and I miss it, please smack me. Vincent and Seph are not emo. Cloud has his moments (lots of them), but we loves him anyway. Sometimes. When he's not being mean to Tifa, who deserves better.

One more thing: You may notice that Reeve ends up in most of my stories, in a more or less prominent role, or at least a positively influencing one. This is, in fact, deliberate. The poor man gets no love most of the time. I'm not saying that as an accusation, but I personally want to give him as much coverage as possible.

Err... where do I get my inspiration? From other stories, the games, other people, random conversations, even more random thoughts, and insomnia.

I take requests, if I think I can do them justice, and I will often become inspired by someone else's work. If I am, you will get mentioned, of course, and thankee for helping me continue the madness...in a good way, of course.

Holy crap I'm writing again! Woo!

Hey, just a quick update! The computer that had the word processor on it has died. Luckily, I backed up the story files, but I have nothing to edit them with right now. I'm working on it, but it may be awhile. Sorry, everyone! :(

UPDATE to the update: The laptop still works, sorta, but it's VERY slow, and the only way to move things is by e-mail or SD card. As soon as we get a word processor for this beastie, though, I will hop to work doing just that. Thank you for the patience!
