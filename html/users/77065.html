Greetings to all who find me here.

Years ago here, I maintained an identity of Sonic Whammy, as I did most everywhere else. But then, after some trying times, I abandoned that identity and the lone fanfiction you saw here back then (even though I hadn't touched it for several years by that point). Now, I'm back, and though I may not be actually writing fanfiction anymore, I will gladly share with you on this page my favorites from all of you.

I am a fan of Sonic, Street Fighter and Pokémon, among a scattering of other realms when the mood strikes me.

Personal favorite ships: Sonic/Sally, Ash/Misty, Ash/Dawn and Ryu/Sakura. But if the story is written really well that I can believe it, I'll back you if you have a different idea.

My true passion, however, is game shows. To that end, I host game shows on the anime convention scene in the northeast U.S. as part of Lost Silver Productions, and from time to time, you will be seeing me post questions to some of you here via polls, or perhaps even hear from me directly if you're a fan of a particular show that I'm researching.

Other than that, that's pretty much it. I hope to speak with a lot of you and hear some great stories.
