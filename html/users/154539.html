About me:

I am a fan of countless TV shows and movies; so many, that it is hard to list them all.

I write fanfiction because I love to put my favourite characters in situations that you won't see in canon.

I tend to write erotica or mature stories since often my stories have a romantic slant to them. But occasionally I do write general or teen oriented fic.

I love feedback and constructive criticism is welcome so don't be afraid to give either. Flames though will be used to roast marshmallows and weenies.
