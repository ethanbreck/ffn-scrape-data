Me:

I'm Camille, pen name cmar. I'm in the running for oldest Power Rangers fan in existence. (No, I won't say how old.) I've been a comic book fan since the age of 12, a science fiction fan for almost as long. After getting a degree in psychology, naturally I became a computer programmer. Location: New York City. Profession: computer programming and security. If you have nothing better to do, check out my LiveJournal: for assorted thoughts and general complaints about life, and some plans and story fragments.

What I've done so far:

I've removed my first fic, 'A Year of Time', which was basically a slightly AU, more science-fiction rewrite of the Time Force TV series and is background for my main series of TF stories. If anyone is interested in reading it, it's posted on my website. I will warn you that as the first fiction I ever wrote, it's not exactly Shakespeare. (Translation: get out your suck-o-meter.)

Everything is listed below, of course, but I'm heavily into writing series and this lists those series out. In addition, stories that are two-parters or trilogies have slashes between them.

My 'Year of Time' Wes/Jen series: The Other Side / The Second Time / Time Over, Sins of the Fathers, The Time Tunnel, Connections, Unreal Life / Violet, Valentine's Day, Crossroads / Reversals of Fortune / Tapestry. There are also a bunch of stories set in the same 'universe' but sidetracks to the main series: Night Time, Double Time, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Fourth of July, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Christmas Past Present and Future, New Year's Eve, and April Love.

My Wes/Eric slash series, set in the canon universe: Red Fire, Fall From Grace, Boxed In, What Dreams May Come, The Secret of BioSynth, Turns in the Road, and All the Wrong Places.

And last but not least, an AU 'what if' Batman and Nightwing series: Birds of a Feather, A Woman Scorned, Duplicity, Silent Night, Chilled, and Chiroptophobia.

Others: After the Fall and The Price are a two-parter.

Co-written stories:

I've been lucky enough to work with two of the best writers in the PR fandom, MsDany and Dagmar Buse.

MzDany and I have done a crossover between my Time Force slash Wes/Eric series and her Ninja Storm slash Cam/Hunter series. It's posted under a combo name of Dany Cmar.

Dagmar Buse and I have written a crossover between my Wes/Eric slash series and her Tommy/Jason slash story/series More Than Friends and Brothers. It's posted under the name of Cmar and Dagmar Buse.
