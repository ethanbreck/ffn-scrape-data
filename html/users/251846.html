Dearest Readers,

I still receive e-mails that some of you are reviewing my stories and adding a few of my stories to your favorites. For that, I am very thankful and very blessed. I keep all of my fanfiction here for memories and for laughs. I know that sometimes I think of an old fanfic I read in my youth and want to read it again to see if I harbored the same reactions and am quite upset to never find the story again. I don't want any of you to feel that way, so I leave my works here, untouched.

Thank you, once again, for reading.

(Kitzaku) 2008
