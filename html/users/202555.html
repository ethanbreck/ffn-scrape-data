He fellow Fanficers (is that even a word)

So ill try and make this as interesting as possible.

So my name is Natalie Elizabeth, obviously.

I live in South Australia, Australia, Yep that’s a state in the southern part of Australia, haa haa. Well one of them.

I am on Twitter as Natalie @ NattieLizzie. Although it still confuses me a little so I am not on their much.

I am currently writing Castle fanfiction but I have written for Lois and Clark and Buffy. Some of my work is ten years old and even I’ll admit a bit corny, out of character and in need of major editing. At one point I did think of deleting them but then I thought nah it shows how I have grown as an author.

Some of my Castle work needs to be reedited and I am in the process of getting that done. In my line of work I don’t do much writing or use a computer. I was amazed at how bad my skills had gotten over the years. So bare with it if you can and simply enjoy my work.

I am always looking for new story ideas. So if you have an idea for a story but don’t think you can write it and think I can, please let me know. I enjoy discussing all things Castle and love it when peoples ideas come to life.

I also like helping new authors. I am terrible at grammar so I would be no good at being a Beta. I am really honest though, at times too honest. If your after advice on a story you are writing or thinking of writing I am happy to help. Often other authors tell me there ideas and then my brain goes crazy coming up with ideas for them on where they can take their story. At times this leads to another story being written. Just PM me and we will talk .

So I have a variety of stories to read. Most are romance and not case related. Some are just silly. Some are, pft yeah right no way that happens. And some are just well, the way it should have gone. I hope you enjoy reading my work and let me know by reviewing.

Taking a little hiatus as I am currently studying eek

Big thank you to Castle Fanfic writers guild for hosting the awards. The judges for chosing my story Win a date with Richard Castle. And Angie Smartypants (@dtrekker) for creating the award/picture

OMG OMG OMG I was in the Season 6 Castle promo. I was one of the fans AHHHHH. If you Youtube the promo I am the second fan vid you see. The girl on her own

Nattie.
