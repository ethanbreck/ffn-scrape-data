Author has written 3 stories for Gundam Wing/AC.
Formerly under the penname Rebel Storm, I'm a primarily Gundam Wing fanfic writer with emphasis on Lucrezia Noin (geez, I'm making this sound like a college application!). Ok, well, Noin is amazing. She's the only Gundam Wing chara who could comfortably be with any other chara in the series and not lose any of her personality traits. I especially think her and Zechs are cute, and her and Trowa, with Heero and Wufei coming in a close second. However, I'm starting to fall in love with Bleach and Shaman King (I'm already in love with Spirited Away), so we'll see. On a non-manga note, my favorite movie is Edward Scissorhands, favorite book is The Only Alien on the Planet by Kirsten D. Randal with Harry Potter 5 and the Kushiel's Dart series by Jacquiline Carey running a close 2nd and 3rd, with Ender's game an honorable mention. I am also addicted to Star Trek...shameful, I know...but I do so love Dr's Spock and Bashir...
