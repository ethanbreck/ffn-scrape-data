Author has written 12 stories for Harry Potter, Teen Titans, and Portal.

My earliest fics were written in 2002. I feel (I certainly hope) my ability as a writer has progressed since then. Hopefully, you will enjoy all my fics but will find my newer stories of a higher quality than my older ones.

Notes on my fics, alphabetical by fandom:

Harry Potter - all of my stories for this fandom (with the sole exception of "The Consequences of Love") were written in 2002 and 2003, after the publication of Goblet of Fire but before the release of Order of the Phoenix. Because of that, many of my stories now blatantly contradict canon and certain characters (in particular Sirius Black) are OOC. I am leaving them up for nostalgia and because many people have told me that they still enjoy them.

The Russian translations provided on my profile are the work of Saint-Olga.

Transformers Animated - Re-Sparked is the first installment in a trilogy. It features multiple pairings and is an AU as I've opted to ignore Season Three of the series.
