I want to spread the manga love! So I want to pay to get it to people who will enjoy it. For this reason I am going to put forth challenges. I am going to post five now and anyone who wants to enter for the chance to earn a book can. Use the E-mail that I have posted and in the subject line put “LOOK! Will their future have a happy ending? (This story line can ignore the sixth book completely or it can find a way around it.)

Challenge #2: Hermione finds herself in a very odd situation one day when she notices that a certain someone is sending her puppy love eyes (or passionate eyes). Hermione can’t stop from blushing and feeling warm all over when this girl brushes by her whispering things to her that she had never imagined. But just as Hermione begins to think about a relationship with this girl who other but a certain red head confesses his feelings! What’s a girl to do?

Challenge #3: Must be SS/HP, must include one of Harry’s fantasies, an extremely erotic fantasy that involves car/bus/scooter?! Harry must be 18 or older and Severus must be mostly in character. Bonus if they are in a moving vehicle!

Challenge #4: Incest is best… between Ron and Percy or Twins and Percy. Percy is on the job doing some field work = you chose. (the new minister of magic seems to have it out for poor Percy giving him all types of odd jobs) This job just so happens to coincide with his brothers work and they find themselves breaking some of the most well known rules in any profession. _~

Challenge #5: There is not nearly enough Draco/Ron out there nor Severus/Harry or Hermione/Cho or Percy/Ron but this challenge has more to do with pairings like … Hagrid/GiantSquid… this challenge is a rare pairings challenge so you can pair up anyone and everything. As long as at least one is human~ish.

If I get kicked off fanfiction for this I will have to move the contest to another site if I can find one.
Thanks if you participate! This is going to be fun _
