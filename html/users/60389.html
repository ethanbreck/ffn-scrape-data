*bows* Hello, my name is Chibimegan. I'm your average fourteen-year-old-anime-and-fantasy-addict-who-likes-to-be-creative-by-writing-fanfics.

I have four muses, two of which are currently in a cruise in the Bahamas. But here are my other two:

Lady Dragon: She's my major anime muse but also dabbles in angst and evil plots. She's also my biggest critic, sadistic and has a major crush on Yami Bakura. Poor soul...

Lady Dragon - *scowls*

Saria Silverbranch: She's my fantasy muse, an elf who was kicked out of Lothlorien for setting fire to Celeborn's throne. She's an annoying bubbly thing, hates angst and unhappy endings, has a tendency to eat all my candy and steal the bishonen I like. I can never seem to get rid of her...

Saria Silverbranch - *oblivious to what's being said about her*waves*

Anywho, for anybody who's here because of Changing the Past, I'm NOT planning on continuing it anytime soon. But I do have a fic in the works, a Harry Potter fic, and the first chapter should be coming out hopefully sometime soon. I won't give anything away, but I hope it will be good, sort of my legacy on this site.

Please feel free to e-mail me at any time. I love getting feedback on things I write. My e-mail is queen_of_games99@smileyface.com.

Goodbye now, thanks for listening and please read and review my stories.

-February 6, 2003-
I'd just like to say that I have decided to open a new account on FF.N under the name of Lady Dragon. All later stories will be posted under that name, including a forthcoming Harry Potter fic (coming REALLY soon) and the rewritten version of Changing the Past, which will start sometime this month. I'd just like to say thanks to Angel Abi, who, by putting me on her faves. list, helped me get out of my writer's slump. Thankies!
