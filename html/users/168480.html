You actually clicked on my name? Thank my lucky stars, come on ... don't be shy!

makes visitor comfortable on a cozy pink chair*

Well then, I suppose my Bio should tell you something about me, even a little bit, ne?

smiles*

All right! I'm Fehrocious, Fehr for short and Fehr-chan for my close friends. Well, they call me that here anyway. For some obscure reason only known to me, in reality, my other friends think any name-chan is funny as hell. *lifts up nose in the air* Hmph! They don't have diversity and are often stiflingly (?) close minded. *sniffs disdainfully*

Oooh, we can't blame them if they're just...*narrows an eye*...well...close-minded.

grumbles*

forgot the visitor*

Whoops, I let my darker side run away with me just now. Sorry, have some milk and cookies! *smiles politely*

What? You don't like milk and cookies?

Okay...fine. Then I won't serve you any. Go bother someone else bucko!

What? The cookies tastes great? Aww shucks, I only took them from my next door neighbors...they make great cookies. Speaking of that, excuse me while I snatch the blueberry pie cooling on the window. Don't worry it won't take long. I'm prepared at times like these...

returns with a big smile on her face*

Fresh from the oven! Mmmm...

Here, have some while I rattle of. Let's see, I'm Bruneian and a proud member of the Inori forum ( Where all Brunei Otakus meet! and go crazy! ) ... I'm an avid writer and imaginer...fantasizer, whatever. =)
