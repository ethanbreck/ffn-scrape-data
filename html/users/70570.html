Author has written 11 stories for Legend of Zelda, Star Fox, and Super Smash Brothers.

Hi, everyone... Hmm. Where do I start? I'm an 21-year-old slacker of an artist from the UK. As my stories might hint, I'm a video game nutter. Current faves are Zelda, Metroid and Halo (particularly that side of it known as Red Vs Blue). I'm a big bookworm, too, and my favourites are the Discworld series. Like Harry Potter, too. Am currently getting into the Ender's Game books. Fave thing on TV is the Simpsons, and I'm a recovered Trekkie/er so I've never lost the taste for spaceship. I totally love anime, but you can't find that much of the good stuff where I live in the UK. They keep taking it off the air. Sob. Musical tastes? Very varied. Currently very much into techno and industrial (VNV Nation, Assemblage 23, etc), but I'll listen to almost anything.
Well, ciao!

I'm sorry to announce that Generations has had to be cancelled. I'm leaving it up here, but don't expect updates. The remaining unused material is posted on my Livejournal.

If you're looking for In The End or Tears of Pearls, those have been moved to my LJ, too, due to the songfic crackdown-
