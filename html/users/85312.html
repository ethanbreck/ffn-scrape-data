Hello, all! Welcome to my home on FanFiction.net!

I've been around this place on-and-off since 2000, but felt it was best to remove my earlier work as I was just a high school boy starting out then. Since then I earned my BA, an MA, and am considering Ph.D. work in the near future. I spend my days as a college lecturer and freelance editor and writer, while also getting in some gaming time when I can.

I write stories based on my favorite video games, which include Resident Evil, Final Fantasy, and Mass Effect. I am currently working on a few crossover pieces that I hope to have up soon.

Thank you for visiting and please come back soon.
