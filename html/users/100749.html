Ok, about time I updated this thing, it's been the same for years. So, if you're reading this thing, I bet you're trying to find out a little about me after reading one of my stories.

First off, the pen name. It's not something I made up! It's what you call light-skinned Filipino girls like myself in Tagalog. If you must know, my real name is Noelle, as if you couldn't figure it out from my AIM name (SN: noelpinay, by the way). Yeah, my old pen name used to be my real name before they turned me into "Noelle2".

If you like having intelligent conversations about good fanfiction, chances are I'd like to talk to ya. Any suggestions for future stories you'd like me to pen or even having me act as your beta-reader (I'll have to agree first) are totally welcome.

And yes, it's an understatement that I like Final Fantasy. A really really BIG understatement.

The older CCS stuff down there? It's just there for nostalgia. I swear I can write better now (at least I hope so)!
