Author has written 6 stories for Night World series, and Harry Potter.

After a year in absentia I have returned to write once again. I'm currently writing White Revenge, a story written in the aftermath of the Half Blood Prince. I last wrote over a year ago the story Dream of the Dragon and though the stories are quite different, I dare say the writing style is still the same.

My stories usually have the characteristics of frequent character death. Total death counts in stories can range between a mere five to around a thousand. I do not often display sex in my stories but violence and swearing are frequent. I use a moderatly descriptive style withan occasional lack of grammer in sentences which is often not poor beta reading but instead just a feature of my writing.

There will be no sequel to Dream of the Dragon. Raine and several other reviewers helped me see that the story was best left with the ending it has.

I like Harry Potter, Star Wars & Lord of the Rings. I am likely to read in all the fandoms. I sail the good ships HG/DM and (even occasionally TRHG) in Harry Potter whilst I'm Ani/Ami in SW and don't really care for LotR.

I'm English, 18 and an atheist. My favourite movie is Silence of the Lambs. I admire Johnny Depp for his performances in Benny and Joon, Pirates, Sleepy Hollow and Edward Sissorhands and Anthony Hopkins for his performance in Silence of the Lambs. I'm spiffy. I'm a Film and English student as well as Geography and Sociology.

Summer 2006 Update:

Hi everybody! I'm still resolved to not write any more but whether that will be forever is still in doubt. I'm just here to keep it up to date and check up on everything. I have no abandoned FFnet. Still here, still loving it. I'd like to thank everyone who has read my two stories from HP, and those who are coming from my far older LJS fanfiction archive. My first storyarchived here dates back to 2002, when I was very young, so thank you very much. I'm still in touch with the fandomsand open to be contacted should youwish to.

I'd like to thank the person who nominated me for www.freewebs.com/ajarofpensaward site for various awards. Always a pleasure to get these things.

Winter 2006 Update:

I am still knocking about but I'm not writing any more. The reviews are always read and welcome, thanks for all your comments and I hope you're all doing well!
