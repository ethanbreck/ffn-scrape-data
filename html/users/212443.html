Info you may or may not wanna know

I just turned 17 hehehe…. I’m a junior in high school, and I generally dislike it strongly with a passion. (I may use these sayings rather than hate because hate is a powerful emotion and shouldn’t be wasted on things like school and stupid people.) I’m taking honors english, chemistry, Japanese 200 and many other not very fun classes like trigonometry…

I have only submitted stories on ff.net about Zoids though I do have interests in ton of other animes. My friend and I together have seen 108 animes, and that’s not including the ones we forgot or didn’t finish.

I personally don’t like Hentai, Yaoi, or Yuri, writings, and have only written one rather terrible lemon since I found couldn’t write it without be sick with myself. I have nothing against the above but the fact that I just don’t like them… kind of like I don’t like beets.

If I come off snobby I don’t intend to so just tell me kk?
