Author has written 3 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Star Wars, and Harry Potter.

My name's Leonie, I'm an 23 year old female from the Netherlands. I'm quite the web addict, have been for almost seven years now. It all started when I searched for Buffy sites out of curiousity. I stumbled upon the whole Whedonverse community and I was hooked. I mostly love to read: Harry/Draco, FootballSlash, Blair/Chuck, Sookie/Eric, Arthur/Merlin, Richard/Kahlan, Mick/Beth, Clex, Buffy/Angel and Padme/Anakin.

I've written some crappy B/A stories, and only dared to post one of them on here, since I've written them when I was 13/14 years old ;) I'm not really a writer, stuff sounds great in my head but when I actually try to put it into words, I fail miserably. I've got more unfinished works than finished ones. Writer's Block is evil and I constantly seem to have it.
