Hello fellow readers! It has been a long time since I have been on here. I've been a part of this site for...well...near 10 years now. I started writting on this site with Dragonball Z fanfics because that was my favorite anime at the time. I even started writing some for "Full Metal Alchemist" (way before 'Brotherhood' came out) but due to my inactivity I have pretty much deleted all the stories I had on here previously except for one, and I only keep it for nostalgia sake. XD I started on this site when I entered high school and now I'm a high school graduate and have been working ever since (class of 2006 woot!). Guess I'll put some info about myself. I am 24 years old and female. I draw anime (though I have been in a huge rut with it) and I work all the time. I did a few years of community college but never got anywhere with it and I've been to a few anime cons. I've met: Vic Mignogna, Jason David Frank, Chuck Huber, and Jason Charles Miller. The animes I'm really into are: Full Metal Alchemist (original and remake), Hetalia, Naruto (original and the time skip, Shippuden), Bleach, and Soul Eater. I like A LOT of others but that would take up this whole page.

A lot of my characters that I draw (and eventually write stories about on fictionpress) got their start here on fanfiction.net as fanfiction characters for "Dragonball Z", "Inuyasha", and some other animes. Here are the ones that got started on this site: Kat, Tigra, Tengu, Risika, and Koneko.
