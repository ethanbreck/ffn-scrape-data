Author has written 2 stories for Kiddy Grade, and Kingdom Hearts.

Hi. I'm Museless. I'm bookworm and I enjoy doing research. I like to learn. My nose is almost always buried in a book and my glasses are almost always trying to escape my nose. I write more often than I post, but when I do write it's generally a drabble. My inspiration comes from random events. It comes from heartfelt moments with friends,and from the "I can't believe you really just said that" moments from anyone. I'm a geek. I'm friendly and bubbly, but I can be quite quick to anger. I've been turned to completely random fan-pairings because of one particularly well-written story. I'll read just about anything, as long as it's written well. I enjoy a plotline. Completely pointless fluff/smut/humor is enjoyable too though. Just in small doses. I don't like character-bashings, in any series. It's puerile. You don't have to write every character in a perfect light. But stories written just to bash a character are lame.

I accept requests. I don't promise that I'll ever get around to writing and/or posting them. But I'll happily accept, and consider doing them. I'm randomly inspired, so something as simple as " I just really like this pairing and you don't see much of it" might be enough to get me writing. I write drabbles more than anything and one shots more than anything else. _

It's nice to meet you.
