It's been a hell of a long time since I updated this, and I aplogize for that. I'm 26 years old, a college graduate, and still currently reside in Upstate NY. I'm into film, Japanese shows, games, and of course writing. I love all kinds of action, science fiction, and even certain types of horror. As you can see from my current works, I love to play around with these genres, and see what my mind can come up with. If I find an idea to be especially gripping, I usually think my readers will find it equally so.

Some of you might find my ideas to be a bit...unusual. But that's just the way I like to be. _
