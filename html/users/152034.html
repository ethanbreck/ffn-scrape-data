Well as you may have noticed, I took Perfum N 18 down completely. After rereading it a time or two I saw that it just sucked all around and I really didn't like the turns the story took. I couldn't think of a decent ending that would somehow make the whole thing worthwhile, so I scrapped it. I'm so so sooooo sorry to those of you that really did like it, but there was just so much wrong with it that I just couldn't leave it up.

In other news, I'm just starting school (yay college!) and a new job. Things are crazy busy, and I'm staring at another six months living way too far away from the love of my life (it's been seven months already). I hope to find time to write in the coming months, because I really do enjoy it. However, I'm just not going to make promises because I don't want to disappoint you folks more. If there even are anymore of you out there reading this...not likely.
