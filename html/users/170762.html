Author has written 84 stories for StarTrek: Voyager, and StarTrek: The Next Generation.

I have been a fanfic writer for many years, starting with TNG in the 1990s (back with the print fanzines). However, my interest was caught by Voyager, and especially the command team.

I enjoy exploring the relationship between Voyager's captain and first officer. However, when I realized about fifth season that the only place J/C would ever happen was the Alpha Quadrant, I began to wonder just how it could happen. Many of my stories do just that--look at the different ways those two might finally get together.

Since Endgame, I have also been fascinated by the Admiral Janeway who returns for her ship and crew, trying to understand this troubled woman who so willingly tosses aside the temporal prime directive.

I love constructive feedback, so please don't hesitate to read and review.

Thanks, Trek Core, for the avatar.

Note added 10/2019: Yes, I still receive and read reviews, so please let me know what you think of my stories. If you sign your review, I will send you a reply here. I am not currently writing fanfiction, but ”tomorrow is another day,” as they say! Maybe Star Trek Picard will inspire me! Thanks!
