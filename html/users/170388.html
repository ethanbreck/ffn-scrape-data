My life is unique, I won't go into details, but someday may write parts of it down. Problem is, no one will belive it. But, right now I am, 48, divorced, raised my youngest sister, have a Dalmation that rules my life with her "looks".(My mother should have had such looks!) I have 5 younger sisters, I am proud of all of them. I love all things and tolerate many rude people. I have lost 80 pounds, and plan on losing another 150. Yes, I am fat. Probably why my husband was glad to divorce me, but we are friends now, there is no need for hate. Well that's me. I cook, preserve, and dry foods, collect recipes, and do all hand arts, quilting, knitting, ect. I like to help people if I can, and work two jobs. My real name is not what you see, but its close.
