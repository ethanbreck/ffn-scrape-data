Author has written 27 stories for Harry Potter.

I like writing. I like reading. I do both slowly. Everything posted will be finished. Eventually. Some of it will be rewritten; I'm not proud of my early works, and honestly have no clue as to why people like them at all, particularly 'Blood, Torture, and Witty Comebacks'. I started to write it when I was twelve or thirteen and like to think I've improved drastically since. I like my one-shots better, just because I can sustain a small plot-bunny and not have it collapse on itself, whereas with the multi-chapters, my writing tends to fall apart. I think my favorites would have to be 'A Different Kind of Insomnia', 'Making Faces', and 'Don't Step'. I have great plans for 'Desperately Seeking Harry'; and in the works I have a murder-mystery set post-War that is Ron-centric, though all characters make appearances. If anyone feels like being my beta, please e-mail me and we'll have a trial go.
