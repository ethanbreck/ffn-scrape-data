Author has written 31 stories for Harry Potter.

I'm a huge fan of the Harry Potter series, as reflected in my works. Other than that, I read a whole lot of other books. Lord of the Rings, Princess Diaries and Chronicles of Narnia are the book series I've read. But my ultimate, all-time favorite book would have to be A Time to Kill by John Grisham. I've also read a lot of his books.

I've been writing "creatively" (meaning not for school) for about eight years now. I started with short poetry when I was in the fourth grade. In the fifth grade, I began writing fan fiction based on the Harry Potter series, and I've been working on that fandom ever since. My OTP is H/D (has been for about four years!), but I also appreciate H/Hr, D/Hr and R/Hr pairings. I've tried to work on other fandoms, but I haven't found one that I feel as passionately about as Harry Potter.

Aside from reading and writing, I also do music. I sing, mainly. Next in line is guitar-playing. I've dabbled on piano and drums as well.

I also watch TV. (Who doesn't? :p) I'm a big fan of the One Tree Hill saga. My OTP is totally Leyton. Someday I hope to be able to write a fan fic on them, or at least something One Tree Hill-related.
