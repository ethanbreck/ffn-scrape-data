Hello. I rather like writing fanfic, but I'm a slow writer. Oh, well. I also like reading it and discussing it, if that helps, and I do beta-read (or would if offered).

I've bene writing fanfic for a while now, and this has a lot of my old stories, as well as some newer ones, once I get around to updating this site. My older work isn't as good -- I like to think I've improved since I wrote them -- so keep that in mind. Also, I've got a couple of unfinished stories on here, that I really should just delete, because I probably won't work on them again, but I remain hopeful I'll change my mind.

If you do see any of my older work that got mangled by ff.net's War on Punctuation, please give me a heads-up via message and I'll try to fix it. As of now, I'm mostly fixing my newer work, as that is read more.

Please, be perfectly willing to be honest in your reviews. I LOVE concrit, and that include negative concrit. I do know the difference between negative concrit and a flame, and know how to react accordingly. I give the reviews I would like to get myself. Take that into account.
