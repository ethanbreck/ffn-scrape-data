Greets!

Cognitive Dissonance is the meme of my life. for example*i* want to break some capitalization or grammatical rules, but reserve the right get pissy when careless spelling or language makes a writers work unreadable. well, that's Chaos for you!

anyway, there's as great a difference between carelessness & creativity, as between stupidity & wile, disorder & disguise.

i like slash, but don't like the obligatory hollywood sex scenes stuffed into so many modern novels & films. go figure! yaoi rox, G/L/Bi/Het, it's all great if you're *supposed* to be reading a romance or sex fic. again, i object to Hollywood-ization or the thing done halfway, for effect but without depth.

i'm also really into ANGST. hit me with your best unhappy wounded hero shot! take them to the breaking point! w00t! -just don't send them bouncing and frisking back the next bl00dy morning, as though nothing had happened, please.
