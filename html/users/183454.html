Hey there. Since you're on this page, I guess you're interested in knowing more about me! How flattering!

Since one of the first things people usually ask is "Where are you from?" I suppose I should try to answer that as simply as possible. I'm Italian/American, born in Rome, but I've also lived in the States(CA) and Austria for some time. At the moment I'm going to college in Georgia, studying Sequential Art. (AKA comics and storyboarding. =D) I enjoy forum roleplaing a lot. I used to write stupid comedy fics in my spare time, but now I rarely write at all outside of these roleplays. I also enjoy illustrating comics and character designs.

I speak Italian, English, conversational French, pretty good German, plus a little Spanish and Japanese to boot. I'm interested in animation and comics, especially anime and manga.

My favorite anime are: Shoujo Kakumei Utena, Mai Hime (Otome is a steaming pile of shit!), FLCL, Neon Genisis Evangelion, Card Captor Sakura, Ojamajo Doremi, and yes... I was once obsessed with Sailor Moon.

My favorite comics are: V for Vendetta, Lost Girls, and... argh I can't think of any more specifics right now. I'll read anything if I have the time.
