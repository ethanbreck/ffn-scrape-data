Author has written 4 stories for Veronica Mars, Hart of Dixie, and Haven.

I hereby fully acknowledge my slight obsessiveness with certain TV shows/characters. So much so that when they don't do what I want them to do, scenes run through my head, and sometimes I can't think until i get them out...

As of right now, the only shows/books I see myself doing a little writing for are Veronica Mars (LoVe), Harry Potter (H/G), and Hart of Dixie (Zade). Maybe down the road a little Eureka (Jo/Zane), or Haven (Audrey/Nathan) ???, but we'll see. Those are pretty much the only stuff I read anyway.

And I'll admit I'm kind of a fic-snob...I don't pretend to be a super amazing writer, (I of course appreciate the con-crit, and I know everyone has to start somewhere!) but bad writing just annoys me... It also annoys me when my fav characters are written completely uncharacteristically without good reason. But i'll pretty much give anything a shot!

I'm always up for something new, so I love fic recs too!
