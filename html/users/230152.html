I'm presently fourteen years old. I have brown, curly hair that usually really annoys me because, well, it just does! I'm 5'5 (I think), but I'm still growing.

I was born in Australia, though I am Eurasian. My father is Irish, but was born in England before coming to Australia when he was only 5, and my mother is from the Phillipines.

I enjoy playing tennis, dancing, music and writing. If I didn't enjoy writing, I wouldn't write fanfics!

My favorite animes are DB/Z (duh!), Inu-Yasha, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Orphen, Trigun, Yu-Gi-Oh, Tenchi Muyo, Cowboy Bepop, Bubblegum Crisis Tokyo 2040, Gasaraki, Marmalade Boy, Gundam Wing, Ranma 1/2, Cardcaptor Sakura, Fruits Basket, Hamtaro, Sailor Moon and Yu Yu Hakusho.

Oh yeah, can't forget Manga! My favorite manga include Inu-Yasha, Mars, Marmalade Boy, Ranma 1/2 and Magic Knights Rayearth!

I also love Gohan, and think that he's the best character in DBZ. I hate GT for what they did to Gohan, plus it wasn't even actually created by Toriyama-sama. Trunks and Goten also suffered quite a deal, if you ask me...

I also love learning different languages! I'm learning Indonesian at school, Tagalog (my mum's native language, although she's starting to forget it! ^-^') at home and Japanese at home, via the internet! I've found this really cool site, and I'm learning a lot of new words from it!

Well, enough about me, cya! Also, Gohan is mine, so lay off!
