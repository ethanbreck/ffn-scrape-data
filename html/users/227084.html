Heya~ I'm mostly known as Mikamika or simply Mika nowadays, and I live in Montreal, Canada. I'm not much of a writer; being French, I only have a general grasp of english litterature. I'm more of an artist, actually. Drawing is what I do most with my spare time.

It doesn't mean that I cannot try to write something somewhat readable, though. Keyword being try... ¬_¬;;

I'm an anime/doujin/manga/ff whore, as I've always been. I doubt that will ever change, anyway~ Also, I am quite a lot into yaoi, but not ~all the time~. As I am, first of all, French, I suppose there will be both English and French works on this account. :3

What else... Hm, yes! I am mostly interested into SamCham, Naruto, Hellsing, FMA, Death Note, Bleach and Harry Potter when it comes to writing fanfics. Oh, and FF7 too, which is... my longest addiction. I'm currently working on a french Samurai Champloo fanfic, actually.

I'm a pretty busy person, what with my studies and all, but... I hope I'll get to find enough time to write, as it's probably the best way for me to improve my English.

Well~~~ I'll try my best, anyway.
