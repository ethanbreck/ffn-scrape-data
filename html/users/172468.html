Well hello again.

To anybody that actually remembers me and my stories, it's been quite some time since I've been in the fanfiction world. However the old writing bug is back, and I'm hoping I can crank out at least a few more.

I love writing and my favorite subjects are evident by what I write about. My fantasy is of course to become a "real-life" author, but that could be well in the future.
