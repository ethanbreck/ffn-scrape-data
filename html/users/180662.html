Hello, Nina here. I write stories. As you can tell, I write a lot of stories. I like to write Smoochy (Chris Jericho/Stephanie McMahon) stories, and recently have started a bunch of Zigglee (Dolph Ziggler/AJ Lee) stories. I also include a lot of other couples. But basically, I like Smoochy, and I like Zigglee, and I want to share that with all of you, so explore, read, and hopefully enjoy this peek into my madness.

And for those of you who review, thank you so much, you have no idea how much it means to me that you took the time out of your day to review one of my stories. If you just read my stories, and you don't review, well, I'm just going to assume that since lots of people keep reading my stuff that you're enjoying it, and that makes me happy. I've been doing this a long time now, and it's still amazing and humbling to see how many people have read, reviewed, followed, or favorited my stories and me. I keep doing this because you keep coming back, and sometimes I'm not even sure why, but I'm really, really thankful that you do. So thank you for sticking around, and for being so loyal and amazing.

Now go read some stories!
