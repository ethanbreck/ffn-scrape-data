-Welcome to my Realm.-

Name: Court-izzle. Er... Courtney.

Nickname: Smart-izzle. Bah! Smartass.

Age: 17, baby!

Location: A dreary little town in northern New York.

Looks: Fair skinned. Black hair that falls to my mid-back. Blueish-green eyes. Average build. ((What, were you expecting a Polaroid?))

Personalty: Check out the nickname, yo. It says it all.

Hobbies: Gee... writing? shrugs At least, it used to be. I've been in a rut for a while... Been sticking to reading more than writing. Rolplaying! My biggest hobby. Hey, quit yer snickerin'. I'm a member of the best Newsies lodging house out there; the Second Heaven Lodging House. Oh yeah, it rocks. I bet you're just dyin' to be a member, eh? Aaaand, I'm a member of the other best Newsies lodging house out there; the St. Thomas More Lodging House. Update The newest lodging house I'm a part of! The Hoboken Lodging House. That's a great one, too. La dee da... I'm a swimmer, too. I love to swim. Heh. I love to read... My favorite books are "The Dastard" by Piers Anthony, "Peter Pan" by James Barrie and "Watership Down" by Richard Adams. What else... I like to draw. People, mostly. I'm not very good at mouths, though. Oh, and I like to sleep.

Stories: Well... These will all get done. Eventually. I promise! I just need to wait for the urge to finish them to hit me. Then I'll finish them. Heh.
