Author has written 15 stories for Adventures of Sinbad, Action Man, Inuyasha, Angel, Zoids, Cowboy Bebop, and Hellsing.

I'm an amateur script writer and portrait artist who loves British import television and the fantasy genre. Despite a mild form of dyslexia, I am a voracious reader. I used to channel all the ideas that I desperately wanted to read about into new stories, but you may notice my stories are (all) from a decade or so ago. Due to the growing popularity of fan fiction on the internet, those stories that I wanted to read are now just a click away and my former 'NEED to write' has dwindled to a 'want to write' which has further lapsed into "I'd rather be reading." However, I do still jot down scenes every now and then in the hopes I'll eventually flush them into complete stories I can share.

Whether you're a new or old reader of my works, I love feedback (comments and criticism) to tell me where I've gone wrong and what I'm doing right. A comment such as "Great job," is admittedly nice to come across, but doesn't tell me what I did right so that I can continue to do a great job. Since I write without a beta or proofreader, despite going over the work numerous times myself, I often use feedback to correct the mistakes in a work until it is at its best.

P.S. - If you'd like to post one of my stories on your website, I don't mind...but would really love for you to notify me with the site address, please. I'd like to know where my stories end up, thank you very much.

P.P.S. - Nov 2018 - I continue to lurk daily, fiction helps me stay sane and I love the expanded worlds fan fiction allows. I don't know if or when I'll get around to posting another story as I've been working on tabletop game systems and multimedia story-telling platforms most recently.

Enough about me, onto the stories!
