ATTENTION TO ANYONE WHO READ MY FICS!

Good morning/afternoon/evening to anybody who read the first few chapters of my story/ies! I appreciate it sooooo much, everytime I read the reviews I smile! I would just like to let you all know I have not abandoned the stories or anime in general. I was busy with keeping up in school and depressing jobs, so I kinda lost my inspiration. But now that summer is here, I have oodles more of free time so I can update this story as well as others like CRAZY. I should have chap 4 of White Night up this week and I'll be working on Dying To Live and hopefully some more fics of the Chrno Crusade genre... Thank you soooo much for your patience, love you all!

Hi everyone! The name's Spritz, and I LIVE for anime and manga! My faves:
Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing, Dragonball Z, Fushigi Yuugi, Chobits, Paradise Kiss, Ayashi no Ceres, Peach Girl, Inuyasha, Ranma 1/2, Kodomo no Omocha, Cardcaptor Sakura, Magic Knight Rayearth, Fruits Basket, MARS, Hot Gimmick, Kare: First Love, Imadoki, Vampire Game, Chrno Crusade, Hellsing...

I go through mini-obsessions with my fave anime-manga... I become obsessed with one or two at a time, not disliking others, just focusing on that one or two for a period of time. Lately it's been Inuyasha, but I am starting to get back into DBZ and SM again...

Yeah, I've kinda not updated lately... I can't think of many authors who haven't done it at least ONCE... I believe I'm back to stay this time, though I have midterm exams coming up next week... After that I'm gonna have much fun writing fanfiction again... :)

About the non-anime me: Age: 17... Sign: Cancer...Interests: reading, writing, IMing, dancing, DDR, videogames (I'm waaaay into Kingdom Hearts right now_), singing (not much talent in that field, however), acting, languages, roller coasters!

I love warm weather, therefore I do not like being cold. Unfortunately I am cold a lot of the time. I love eating, so I exercise to make up for it. I love yummy boys too... Though there is only one I have found who is not a celebrity...I have a tendency to become very energetic and hyper when I have consumed a large amount of sugar... Especially after having none at all for about a month... hehehe...
