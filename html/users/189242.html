My name is TyrantFlame. I've been a writer for quite some time now and digimon stories arewhat I'm most prominent at. I watch a LOT of anime, as you'll see from all the stories that I've written.

I create a lot of OC's to support my stories and I usually put myself in a situation where the stories I write are AU. I rarely stick to the anime or manga, unlike the S-CRY-ed fic that I'm currently writing. As for digimon, it's natural to have TONS of OC in it.

Just to enlighten you all, here's a list of my OC's. At least, the ones I remember...

MY OC'S

S-CRY-ed

-Aconite-

An old friend of Cougar's who's had feelings for him for the longest time. She' an Alter User like he is, but her Alter's a reare case; it has a 'life' of its own.They come in the form of a pair of wings, but the two colors of her wings hide a lot of secrets... She calls it 'Oblivion', since she passes out after each usage.She's a short-tempered girl who's very sensitive and cares for other people a lot more than she appears to neglect them.Outgoing and a bit shy, she's a money-crazed HOLY member.

-Black Oblivion-

She ishalf of Aconite's Alter. Cannot live without her other half.She comes in the form of Aconite herself as she doesn't have a proper 'soul'. In this form, she loses her swords' ability to fight, but has full control over her wings, which are very strong and completely black as her name implies. She's sharp-tongued and very impulsive, rarely listens to what people have to say but is actually quite nice, though she never admits it. Is the twin of White Oblivion.

-White Oblivion-

She is the other half of Aconite's Alter. Cannot livee without her other half. She comes in the for of Aconite herself as she doesn't have a proper 'soul'. In this form, she loses her ability to fly as her wings are smaller and weaker compared to Black Oblivion's. But on the contrary, she has full control over her swords and makes a very powerful opponent in spite of her being mute and not being able to fly very far distances. She is very kind, unlike Black Oblivion, very caring and has the ability to communicate with Aconite via telepathy. Is the twin of Black Oblivion.

Full Metal Alchemist

-Freesia (the 8th sin)-

As her 'title' implies, she is the 8th sin, or is known as the 'Ultimate Sin' by Dante. She was created as a 'harness' just in case Dante lost control over the first 7 homunculi. Freesia is a homunculus herself but has no memory of her role or even her birth into the world. She has no idea what she is capable of doing as she is basically all the other 7 sins rolled into one and including her own natural powers. She has feelings for Greed, but it may not be what she thinks it is.She was, in fact Dante's daughter from one of her many husbands, once named Lilith. But when'Lilith' died unexpectedly, Dante recreated her as a homunculus.The name Dante has given her was... Life.

-Aizawa Rin-

He is a State Alchemist, like Roy Mustang. Known as the Seraphic Fury Alchemist, he is capable of using alchemy to raise his physical abilities and partially is capable of distorting space in a small range. A quiet person who doesn't speak easily and is mainly unresponsive to the rest of the world, he is close friends with Freesia before he realises that she was a Homunculus. After knowing her dark secret, he starts being distant, not knowing whether he can trust her or not. He was born of a chimera father and a human mother, which gave him the black wings that he hated very much but the very ones that saved his life on more than one occasion. With Freesia's help, he can transform himself into a dragon for an hour via alchemy. He lost his mortality to the door, which makes him kind of immortal. He has to choose one person to 'give' his soul to and if this person lives, he lives. If this person dies, he dies...

-Melody Exhall-

She is a State Alchemist like Aizawa Rin.Known as the Half-Breed Alchemist, she is capable of using alchemy to fuse a person with an animal, or an animal with another animal, creating a chimera. She's only as tall as Edward,even though she's nineteen, or maybe she's smaller thanEdward...She is also capable of transmuting numerous objects like Edward and is also able to perform alchemy with and without an alchemic cirlce. Her father died after attempting to stop her from trying to transmute a human body for research purposes and in her attempt to save her father, tried to lengthen his life with the help of an animal. She failed and when she attempted to bring her father back, she lost her lungs and her intestines to the door. Has a cheery disposition and is a very kind character, she has a younger brother who nearly died from an accident. She saved him by combining her brother with his own pet chimpanzee. Later in the story, she falls for Envy, one of the Seven Deadly Sins.

That's all I have for now...

Hope you all read and review my fics. :)
