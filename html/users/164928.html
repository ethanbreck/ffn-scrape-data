Okay guys. If anybody actually reads this, HUGE apologies for not updating ANYTHING. I have been EXTREMELY busy lately, but I'm about to take a year off from school so things will hopefully be getting better. I have ideas floating up in my head, just hardly any time to think about them, let alone write them down. I'm hoping to update "Spring is Here" and "A Day in HFIL" soon. Be patient with me! I WILL get back to this! Thanks!!!

And now on with the bio:

I'm a 20 year old woman from the Seattle area. I enjoy, reading, writing, music, and anything that has to do with the theater. DBZ and fanfiction are my guilty pleasures. In fact, no one knows that I write fanfics. Right now my current obsessions are Saiyuki, Yu Yu Hakusho, FAKE, and Gravitation. I'm hoping to write and post fanfiction about these series soon. Other favorites include Wolf's Rain, Alice 19th, Ceres, and Inuyasha.

Music is my life. I love everything about it. Sadly, I cannot read (well maybe a little), write, or play music (as in an instrument). However, I can (and frequently do) sing. So you will probably see a lot of songfics. Especially Hootie and the Blowfish. Not only are they a good band, but a lot of their songs tell stories, which makes them ideal for songfics. I also like songs that really explore a person's psyche.

You will probably mostly see short, one-shot fics here. I have a lot of ideas for longer ones, but since I am short on time and I don't like to post without having the story finished, those will probably be very rare, but updated regularly. Ummm...That's all I can think of for now. I'd like to go more in depth, but as I've stated before, I am low on time.

Note: I have decided to make "A Day In HFIL" more than one chapter. I have a bunch of little ideas floating in my head, I just need to organize them and write them. So bear with me folks! Also I am still working on chapter 3 for "Spring Is Here".

I would also like to state that I will write whatever pairings come to mind. I'm very open minded. So there.

I love to receive e-mails and make contact with new people. So feel free to write to me anytime!!
