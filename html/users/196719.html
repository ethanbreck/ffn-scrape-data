Fictions and sites may be submitted for review:

Rules of Submission:

1. Fiction MUST be posted to a personal website or an archive site. A link must be provided with the submission. Please refrain from attaching text files for review. They will not be accepted.

2. All submissions must be COMPLETED works. Submissions will be considered for review, but a posted review is not guaranteed.

3. All genres and ratings are accepted. All sites should have ratings and disclaimers posted.

4. Please remember if you submit a request for a review, you are SOLICITING a review of your work which reflects the reviewers opinion, positive or negative.

Submissions, comments, and questions should be sent to meanderings@aol.com

Rating System: It is pretty simple. The reviewers each have a singular "Thumbs Up" rating. Whether it might be Five "Powerbombs" or Five "Shooting Star Presses," the highest rating is always Five.
