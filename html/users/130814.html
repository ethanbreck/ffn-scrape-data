My Insignificant Others
Once in a while, you meet a certain someone. A stranger. A stranger you'll forget after a day or two. Or three. Or years. Whether it be on the street, in a cafe, on the train, anywhere. You're not friends with each other; heck, you don't even know each other. Yet, there's something about this person that captures you, makes you think and ponder about him or her in that 1/1000th of a second.

A collection of short stories focusing on a barmaid named Tifa Lockhart and the different people who had left a teeny tiny mark on her life. Slightly AU and OOC. Each story would focus on a different person with Tifa. No specific pairings. An experimental story of mine.
