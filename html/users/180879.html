Alright, everyone! Spyridon has uploaded "Harry Potter and the Four Realms" up until chapter 10! She will be uploading the next chapter soon, and as I have already gotten a chance to read it...I can tell you it's AWESOME! She has really done justice to the story without taking away from the original plotline. I hope that everyone reads the rest of the story, and that you enjoy it as much as I am. Here is the link for anyone who wants to finish it up!

http://www.fanfiction.net/s/6486980/1/Harry_Potter_and_the_Four_Realms

~*~Daphne Li~*~

*PLEASE READ THIS ALL THE WAY THROUGH! IT IS VERY IMPORTANT!*

To my most loyal readers:

I thank you all for hanging in with me all these years. And I know that all of you are hoping that I will continue with this story.

Unfortunately, life has a way of sneaking up on you and overtaking you. I have been attending University for a while now, and my degree has been consuming all of my free time. Because of this, my unfinished stories have been pushed onto the backburner—actually, I haven’t even touched them in almost a year or two.

But hope is not lost!

A little while back, a writer named Spyridon approached me about possibly finishing “Harry Potter and the Four Realms”. This writer has agreed to keep all my chapters as they are—up until the last chapter I uploaded. Then they will take the story from there.

I know this will disappoint many (if not all of you), but Spyridon is an amazing writer and I feel confident that I am leaving my story in safe hands. So please be supportive and as soon as Spyridon uploads the story, please continue to read it!

This will be my last post on this story, and probably my last post ever. I thank all of you for your support and encouragement over the years.

I love you all!

~*~Daphne Li~*~
