Author has written 3 stories for Sailor Moon, Rurouni Kenshin, and Labyrinth.

I've recently started reconstructing my old fanfic, The Princess of the Moon's Quest, and hope that it will be successful. I love dramas and am trying to incorporate almost all of the Sailor Moon cast into this story. I'm not for sure how long its going to be, but don't worry it'll be worth your time.

All Thanks to You is my second priority. I've changed it so there is no past marriage between Tomoe & Kenshin, so Tomoe never died. This is a drama/angst and shows a different side to Kaoru's personality.

Without You is a Labyrinth fic and a love story between Sarah and Jareth. Its coming along, but w/the lack of reviews, its on the backburner.

I love reading anime. My favorite anime is Rurouni Kenshin and I'm struggling to find out what happens in Season 4. I know that he fights Enishi, but I can't locate all of the manga to read it. Anyway, my favorite couple is Kaoru and Kenshin. I also like Inuyasha, Escaflowne, Gundam Wing, Fruit Basket, Sailor Moon, the Labyrinth, Angel Sanctuary, and etc.

I'm hoping to become a great writer and post many more stories. So just be patient with me because I'm a huge procrastinator! I appreciate any reviews I get and any constructive criticism.
