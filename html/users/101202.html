Hello and how do you do. I'm Copper Wise, a lover of geology, video games, books and more. While I'm rather quiet on the internet I enjoy it far too often. I love all sorts of things such as the Legend of Zelda, Mass Effect, Star Wars, the Lord of the Rings, well you get the picture. I'm lost in my daydreams far too often yet strangely have never really taken the time to get them down on paper, er, computer screen.

Until I wrote These Broken Wax Wings. This helped me through one of the toughest moments in my life and felt really good to do. While I don't have something as horrendis going on I find myself leaning towards the itch to write again since my love of drawing is slipping away.

This is my decloration that I will be writing a Legend of Zelda fanfiction before the end of the year. This one following Princess Zelda, heroine of the story.

Hyrule has been thrown into chaos once more. It's greatest general, Ganondorf Dragmire, has suddenly defected from the Hyrulean Army. He now leads the very enemies he swore to defend the kingdom from and no one knows why. While the rulers of Hyrule scramble to muster a defense the princess Zelda knows that something is amiss. A general doesn't just up and leave his country without leaving clues behind.

Against all warnings Princess Zelda decides to take on the task of finding out what happened but she isn't alone in this endevour. Soon-to-be knights and her personal guardsmen Link and Groose hurry in the princess' wake and her personal protector Impa won't let her leave her sight. And of course the wise if annoying words of their only faery Navi tags along to remind them how silly this all is. This rag tag little band plunges deep behind enemy lines to figure out this mystery before Hyrule is over run.

But things aren't always what they seem. Things are not always as black and white as they seem and the Princess Zelda will come to find that destiny will find a way to force it's will upon you. But maybe, just maybe, there is a way to change everything she has ever heard of the old legends of time.
