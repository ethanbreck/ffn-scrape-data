I'll probably update this with some decent info some time in the future, but for I'll just leave this message.

Well here I am back again. I've been on this site for quite some time, and have read a lot of stuff. I even tried my hand at writing, but a lot of my writing has come off as fairly juvenile, nonsensical, haphazard, and all around terrible. However, with age comes wisdom...and hopefully better writing, so I'm going to throw my hat in the ring and tell a story that I've really wanted to tell. So hopefully you'll be intrigued and entertained tales I have for you. Most of all I hope that I will be able to adequately convey a few themes that, while they may be common, are things that people should think about/strive for...anyway I rambled long enough. Enjoy.

Update - 04-13-2015

I just wanted to drop a quick update. I'm sorry, I haven't been updating recently. The last few months have been a real mess with how busy I've been. The next few weeks don't look that promising about letting up on the workload. However, I still have the next two chapters scoped out. The fifth one will be about average length for my stories while the sixth one will be a bit shorter. I'll try to get them out by the end of the month, and I'll be hard at work with the seventh one.

On a semi-related note, I've been brainstorming some stuff for Jojo's Bizarre Adventure set in the SBR Universe around the same time as Jojolion. BUT I won't work on that until Persona: Avalon is done. I want to stick to one project at a time.

Anyway. Sorry for the delay, but look forward to two chapters at the end of the month.
