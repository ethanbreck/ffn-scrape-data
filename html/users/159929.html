Author has written 27 stories for Shakespeare, Earth: Final Conflict, Charmed, Andromeda, Farscape, Twilight, and StarTrek: Enterprise.

I'm a 33-something female geek in Ontario, Canada. I'm a full-time archaeologist by week, part-time book-seller by weekend. I love reading, writing, and long walks off the beach with a scuba tank on my back (actually, I prefer cavern diving, but reefs are awesome, too). My writing is admittedly sporadic and my notebooks are littered with WIPs in various stages of completion, but I do get some stuff down and posted on occasion.

I'm also a vidder and have created vids in 10 fandoms thus far: Farscape (13), Stargate SG-1 (12), Stargate Atlantis (6), Earth: Final Conflict (4), Supernatural (4), Star Trek: Enterprise (1), Battlestar: Galactica (1), Heroes (1), Andromeda (1), and Charmed (1). If you're so inclined, you can check the vids out at The Cardboard Experience. The latest additions:

"Cut the Cord" (Supernatural fandom, Castiel, August 2017)

"Confident" (Supernatural fandom, Crowley, August 2016)

"Radioactive" (Enterprise fandom, Trip/T'Pol, March 2013)

I'm always looking to improve my writing and vidding, so any and all feedback is greatly appreciated :-)
