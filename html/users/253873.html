A New Year means an updated profile.

Hey and welcome to my new bio/profile. The other one was way out of date so i made a new one. Well here's some useless info. about me.

Age: 23

Sex: Male

Country: The USA
State: Maryland (Boring as hell over here)

CURRENTLY LOOKING FOR AN ARTIST!

This is actually something I've wanted to do for a while now. I'm currently looking for an artist to do some illustrations for a couple of my fanfics. Nothing major just an image I could use for the cover of the story so I don't have to search Google and steal someone's else image. And maybe a scene or too from the story. But we can work out the details later.

If you're interested send me a PM and we'll discuss it.

So here's the run down of all my fics and their status.

Serena's Kiss: Discontinued. Wrote it when I was just starting out, no real desire to get back it.

Gallio Saga: FINISHED. One of the few stories that I've actually written from beginning to end.

Salga Saga: ON ICE. Started off good but the plot line and everything just wasn't coming together. Really couldn't get into it as much as I did Gallio. Hovering on just discontinuing it.

Partners: Discontinued. Good idea at the beginning but plot-wise, it just didn't work.

Student of the Battosai: FINISHED. Other than Sesshomaru's Return, don't expect any follows however.

Student of the Battosai: Sesshomaru's Return: FINISHED. But like I said, no follow up is planned. The whole thing kinda got derailed when my friend insisted on shoehorning his own character in, then I just lost all interest in it.

A Saiyan Wizard: Discontinued. Really no plot. And besides how many more "anime character goes to Hogwarts" stories do we need anyways?

A New Life: Discontinued. Sorry. Great idea, no lasting plot.

Endless Memories: Discontinued. Same deal here. Thinking about just combining the two ideas into one story. But for now they're both done.

Digimon: The Rantomon Chronicles: ON ICE. Readers really seem to like it, me...not so much. Reading through it and to me the story as whole just wasn't working. It really had no lasting power. I may continue simply because people really like it, but I'm not sure.

Samurai Jack: A New Adventure: Discontinued. Really didn't see it going anywhere.

Seven Deadly Jewels: ALIVE but AGED. This one I have every intention of finishing but the sheer scope of the story and the continuity just keeps getting bigger and bigger. So big that I can never just sit down and write. I have look over every word to make sure it all fits with everything that has been established in 80 chapters. So because of that expect very slow and deliberate updates.

50 Years Too Early: ALIVE and WELL. Everything is on course for this one. Nothing to worry about here.

Superman: Retribution: ALIVE but INJURED. Didn't really get much of response for this story. I have plenty of ideas for it and a ton of different sequels in mind but the demand for it just isn't there it seems.

Late Nights: ON ICE. This one got halted when I started my other Mass Effect story. But it's just one shots anyway so no real rush to get back to it.

I'll Find You: ALIVE and WELL. No real hiccups for this story.

Padawan Kakarot: RECOVERING. Stopped this story at first but recently found my muse for it again. Currently working on an update. Expect it by the end of January at the latest.

So that's the deal. Hopefully this will cut down on PMs asking me to update stories I really have intention of continuing. Sorry if your favorite story of mine is discontinued but I really don't know what else to do with them. Any discontinued story is up for grabs. Just PM me if you want them and tell me your ideas for it and if I like it I'll give the story to you to continue. Anyways thank you all for continuing to read and I hope you keep reading in the near future. Happy New Year!
