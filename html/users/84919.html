Hey Everyone! Welcome to my humble profile. I'm Bridget, nice to meet y'all.

Fandoms: I only write in three fandoms currently. Boston Legal, NCIS and NCIS Los Angeles. I may choose to write more, but I don't watch a whole lot of TV anymore, so it depends on what my muse wants me to write for.

Pairings: My pairings tend to lean toward het these days. Not because I dislike it but because I don't really find anyone that would make a good slash pairing right now. Denny and Alan from Boston Legal are my only slash pairing currently. Their marriage IS canon, sooo...I take it a step further and have them fall in love. They're really adorable, I can't help it! Although I really did like Tara and Alan together too. It's a shame she left.

On NCIS Los Angeles, I really like Eric and Nell together. They're so cute. Yes, in a lot of ways, they are alike but Nell is a bit more tougher than Eric and more aggressive. I think they balance each other out well. I also really like Kensi and Deeks. Their chemistry is off the charts. But aside from that, Deeks shows in a lot of ways that he cares for Kensi, and I think Kensi is starting to see that more and more.

On NCIS, I'm a really big TIVA (Tony/Ziva) fan. I wish they'd just get together already! My new favorite pairing in NCIS though, is Gibbs and Samantha Ryan. Dr. Ryan is the one who will tear down those walls Gibbs has up.

Of course I write more than just romance. Drama, suspense and friendship are also favorite genres for me to write. I really like to get into characters heads and let the reader know what they're thinking and the motivations behind their actions. I am detailed as I can but I know there is always room for improvement on my part.

Please be kind when you review. Constructive criticism is appreciated and encouraged. I'm always trying to improve. I hope you all enjoy my stories. :)
