"Chance Travelers" Hobbity Fiction My Goal As A Tolkien Fanfic Writer:
To protray all characters in question as complex emotional beings. To accurately have this interact with their body language, manner of speaking, and attachments to other characters. To also understand their cultures, and how this effects masking various feelings and insecurities. And to enjoy weaving stories, explanations and diaglouge between some of the most beautifully complex people, elves, hobbits, dwarves, and even wargs, ever created. My Goal As A Writer:
To progress with dialouge progression and body language significance. To focus on the characters, not the setting and to let the reaction to the setting paint the picture for the reader. And to have fun! :) Why I write Angst:
Now if you haven't noticed, almost everything I've written is either Angst or Drama. This is not because I'm a dramaqueen, emotionally disturbed, or depressed. Frankly, I don't know why I write angst. It's exciting to me, full of body language and caresses. :) As a teenage girl, my emotional stability is always in question, and writing angst helps get some of those extra feelings, out, and thus makes me a happier, more well rounded person!
Yea, that's a good reason I think! Why I love Hobbits:
I just do. End of story. :P Concerning My Reviewers:
Thank you so much. All of you. And all the people who have read my stuff and not reviewed, thank you for reading it! Just knowing that my work is somewhere where other readers and Lord of the Rings lovers can enjoy it, amazes me. Reviews keep me going, give me the confidence to continue with something I might be a little shaky with. They're fun to read, flatter, help, and most of all encourage. Thank you! :)

Lots of Love and Joy for the new year. May it be filled with Plot Bunnies, reviews and most of all...Hobbits!-Melanie
