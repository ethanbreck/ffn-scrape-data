Author has written 5 stories for Harry Potter.

Hi!

All my current fics are set in the Harry Potter Universe. They are canon up through book 4, but do not coincide with books 5, 6, or 7. I was writing most of them before book 5 even came out so any of the plot lines did not include any of the information or storyline that progressed through those books.

My current goal is to finish Into The Flames. I have the plot outlined and think I should be able to get through it, hopefully without a five year hiatus between chapters again. Chapter 21: Remorse has been published as of 07/26/09. There will probably be some discrepancies between the characterizations and my writing style, but I tried as best as I could to keep it in line with all of Second Chances and the first 20 chapters of Into The Flames. I also realized that I have to do a bunch of grammatical editing in what I had already written.

Take Me Away will continue to be on hold until I can find my notes for that fic, which I believe are tucked away in a bin in my basement. The computerized notes somehow got corrupted and that story was no where near being completed when I stopped working on it.

About Me: I am short, blonde, and wear glasses. I absolutely adore tea, kitties, and driving in my car with the windows rolled down while blarring music that I can sing along with. English and I do not get along too well, but I still enjoy writing. My BA is in Mathematics and Politics. I am currently 23 and am happily engaged to a wonderful guy who I actually started dating when I was first writing these fics.
