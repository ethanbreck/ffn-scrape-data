Hello:waves:

Well, I guess if you're taking the time to read my profile, you must be very bored and have nothing better to do, or you do have something better to do, but you don't feel like doing it... either way...

I don't know what I could write that would be interesting, so I'll just loquisiously go on and on...

My favorite things include flutes, pianos, piccolos, the tenor saxaphone, colorguard (especially spinning weapons), anything with words or numbers on it (books, chemical formulas, equations, you name it), dance, deep discussions, and socially deficient people (myself included)

Umm... I'm a vegetarian, I listen to Josh groban, Sarah Brightman, show-tunes, and scatted classic rock... My master-pieces include a play about the American revolution, a 315 page YA novel that I haven't been able to "finish" because everytime I read it, I change something, and a bunch of random poems...I'm a brazen liberal with communist"associations" and... I want to be either an astrophysics or a nuclear chemistry major (I haven't decided yet) and I aspire to speak french fleuntly.My favorite writers are Franz Kafka, J.R.R. Tolkien, and Emily Dickinson. I'm very talented in that I can write a lot about really boring subjects (IE- myself)

The only active piece I have right now is "Snowing on the Beach" which I just started. If you're a numb3rs fan, I'd love to get feedback on it!
