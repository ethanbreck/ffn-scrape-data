I am 19 yrs. old and (like my name says) I love to read. My favorite characters are Harry Potter and Severus Snape from the Harry Potter books, Duo from Gundam Wing, N'SYNC, and the Backstreet Boys.

I currently have two in progress works Hate Transformed and Coercion. Due to some real life problems HT has been on hiatus for almost 2 years. However I have been bit by the writing bug once again and I have the feeling a new chapter will be out soon. Not that anyone probably remembers it:)

Coercion on the other hand is a story that I have just begun and I have a feeling it will be quite lengthy. The rating will be increased eventually but not for a LONG time. Plot development takes time you know! I do hope to update this once every two weeks at the most but I am not promising anything. School has just started for the fall and I have a feeling I will be loaded up with papers quite soon. Save me!

I would also like to thank everyone who has taken the time to read these crazy ideas I put out and I hope that you have enjoyed reading them as much as I have writing them. I allways love hearing from readers, so feel free to email me anytime with anything. If you think I am absolutely horrible, please tell me! And why you feel that way as well. I aim to improve!
