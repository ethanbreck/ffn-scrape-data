Hello there!

A lot of things had happened in the past few years and that is one reason why I haven't updated any of my fics. I am sorry about the long wait.

After graduating with a Master Degree in Cultural Studies (minor in Film Studies),and becoming a primary school English teacher (elementary school teacher for you Americans out there) in a Christian boys' school while finishing my second Master Degree in English Studies, I have since moved on to teaching English in a community college in Hong Kong.

Please read and review my fics, even if you have no comments. Just let me know that there is someone out there reading my fics.

THANK YOU SO MUCH!

6th February 2014

My God! I can't believe that it's almost 10 years since I first started. Anyway, good news and bad news. Good news is I am back and is now taking offers as a beta. Bad news is I am not sure when my stories will be continued as I need to revamp them all. Also I will be going back to studying part-time again, so beta-ing is the most I can do for the time being.

15th April 2008

I am now sort of back, and I hope to update Half the World Away and Big Big World by the end of May.

27th October 2005

After a searching for over 5 months, I had to come back to ff.net because I had lost my beta yet again, and nowhere else seemed to accept Half the World Away in its current state. Anyway, Chapter 1 of Half the World Away is now back on FF.net. The rest of the chapters would be reloaded shortly.

14th May 2005

I don't know how it happened, but somehow ff.net decided that because two of my fics' prologue violated their bloody rules, and deleted not just those 2 fics, but my most popular fic Half A World Away as well. So now I am going to look for a new home for all my fics. I'll update as soon as I have found a new place.

Thank you for you attention.

Calbee
