GENERAL DISCLAIMER: I don't own anything in the fandoms I write for unless specified. If I did, you can bet I'd be a lot richer.

Hey folks. I'm still alive, just busy at the moment, which I guess is better than being not busy. I haven't forgotten about 'Lessons', and am working on outlining Mikey's chapter. Hopefully it'll be satisfactory soon and I can post it. Entertaining the idea of expanding for April and Casey as well, but no promises. Also getting distracted by other fandoms. O be still, my fickle heart.

Anyway, I'm Jones. I write and post to have a fun outlet, but also for help in becoming a more well rounded and altogether better author. I wasn't a creative writing major, so feedback is GREATLY appreciated. I will make and send you cookies. They'll be the pre-made dough kind, but hey, at least you won't die from my cooking.

Don't be surprised or put off if I PM you asking for general clarification regarding editing/criticism. I am systematic and thrive on details. The more I know, the more can improve and all that rot.

A Note: There is one thing I have no tolerance for: disrespect. People who are scathing and rude for their own sakes. Criticism is appreciated; cruelty is abhorrent. I don't mean to speak to how others should live their lives, but please. If you wish to comment, be respectful. Mainly speaking because a friend had a bad experience, and I found it very nasty and sad.
