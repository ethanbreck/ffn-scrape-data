Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, Power Rangers, CSI, and Bleach.

Stink at Bios. Love Reader Response. Started writing fanfiction in the old harrypotter.com board days (’02). Uses Flames to BBQ. Sherlockian since '93, Potterhead, CSI nerd, Whovian, devoted Ghostbusters fan (movies, cartoons and recently IDW Comics). Socially awkward, clumsy, a little shy, but friendly enough. I don't run with scissors anymore, at least. Not after that unfortunate accident...

I write here because I own nothing. Writing is a great escape from real life-- I've lived a thousand lives through my favorite characters, usually with a cat (or four) in my lap. There is a singular challenge of working in established parameters. For me, writing fan fiction comes with great responsibility. I strive to stay true to canons and characters, while taking liberties and making expansions upon plots (or filing holes). I just stay a little closer to home than some wild Crack! and AUs (not that they are bad). Some of my OLD stories (esp. Substitute) are rough, grammatically speaking. We all start somewhere. As they are not likely to be updated/ altered at this point, do what I do: Shake your head as you find comma splices, extraneous words and semi-colons, and be grateful I don't write in txt. Unless otherwise stated, my stories are not Betaed. :(

Despite infrequent posts, I am here lurking all the time! Fanfic is a favorite diversion. I read Doctor Who (9, 10, others with Rose tagging along), Sherlock, Bleach, CSI (GSR), HP, Holmes, Ghostbusters (Egon/Janine =OTP), Labyrinth, and PRDT. Occasionally I wander off... kinda like my brain.

Please R/R the fics, Email Me, IM me, Whatever... Just don't stalk me. That right is reserved for the plot bunnies.
