I've been reading fanfiction for years- probably four or five years by now. Regretfully, I haven't written anything new or worthwhile in a long time (probably three or four years ago). I love writing, and looking back on my horrid stories from my early teenage years, I realize how much my reading and writing has grown. My short story from a year or two is posted on fictionpress, but Harry Potter has virtually rendered any plot bunnies useless. Fanfiction and the books around me, shaped who I became. I will probably continue reading, but my writing career has ended years ago. I do not long to write Harry Potter fanfiction anymore, for not only have I read great stories and am ashamed to post my thoughts, but life does get in the way. I continue to journal and blog, and change through my life experiences. I will continue to read fanfiction, but retrospectively, fanfiction has been a huge part of my life and almost a marker of how much I've grown.

I applaud any current or future fanfiction authors for having the guts to publish anything, since there is a huge legacy left behind from some legendary authors.. Some have left or like me, have moved on to different aspects of their lives. No matter what, fanfiction brings back so many memories.. and reading and reflecting on this huge part of my life is very nostalgic... I miss rushing to scribble on a post-it or a scrap of paper about a little plot bunny. I still have quite a few fanfiction plotlines scattered in my old binders and notes. The memories of it all is overwhelming, and I'm sure most of us have been affected greatly.

Good luck to everyone with their fanfiction and writing.. and life.
