Author has written 8 stories for Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon, Sailor Moon X-overs, and Fanfiction Poetry.

Hi everyone! I'm a college graduate who spends most pf her days in her favorite hobby...anime! That is when I'm not at work...40 hours a week is enough to drive anyone bonkers. I also enjoy reading manga and listening to music. I like all kinds, but my favorite is probably oriental pop, primarily JPOP and anime stuff. Though I also listen to Irish, classical, oldies (occasionaly...but I do love the Beach Boys), American pop/rock music .

My favorite SM character is Makoto/Lita, so any story I write with SM included will probably feature her, but not necessarily. I have a lot of other favorite characters too, considering I've seen part or all of about um...lost track of the number of series/movies, but the list would be so huge, I can't put them all here. Hehe.

Projects in the works currently: WK/SM crossover based on a legend I created, GW/SM crossover, a 3 way crossing Burn Up-W!/SM/GW...and I think that's all at the moment. I'm sorry about my slow progress on the WK/SM story Souls of a Nation. I've had a bit of writers block on it and I've been focusing more on You've Got Mail. In the future I hope to do an X/1999 and SM story and also Dragonball Z/SM. (The second is a definete, but if I told you the plot now somebody might steal it!) A little update here for ya' all. No, I haven't abandoned my stories! I've just had some major writers block and I changed jobs recently so my schedule is really varied. Some days I have to be at work at 5 am and other times I don't get off til 1 am so finding time to write hasn't been easy. But, I have found a place to go with You've Got Mail, so look for a new chapter up, hopefully, soon. _

Lots of love, and enjoy the stories!
