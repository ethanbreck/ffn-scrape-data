About Me: I'm a 2012 college graduate (with a major in journalism and a minor in psychology), who is probably too old to still be writing fanfiction. Oneshots--especially sickfics--are my calling, mainly because chapter fics require too much of my time and an actual plot. I write for fun and to relieve stress. Reading fanfiction will always be my guilty pleasure. I do not accept OCs, so please do not ask. I'm currently working to publish my first novel.

Pet peeves: I cannot stand people who do not separate/indent their paragraphs. Regardless of your actual writing potential or however awesome you may think your story is, if I see that you write chapters as one big blob, then I will not read it. Period. Do not ask, email, review, or PM me and ask me to read your friend's story (or any other story that is not your own). Sometimes, I will read a story as a thank you for reviewing, but this is not an advertising firm. Please do not treat it as such. I am also the standard punctuation, spelling, and grammar nazi. Anyone who says 'grammer' should expect to be hunted down.

Current Manga: Ao No Exorcist/Blue Exorcist, Arata Kangatari/Arata: The Legend, Bleach, D.Gray-man, Fairy Tail, Heart no Kuni no Alice/Alice in the Country of Hearts, Hetalia: Axis Powers, Katekyo Hitman Reborn, Naruto, Soul Eater, Ouran High School Host Club, Pandora Hearts, Vampire Knight

Current Authors: Kristin Cashore, Karen Miller, James Patterson, Rick Riordan, J.K. Rowling

Fanfiction Status: Retired as of 4/22/09. I have slowly begun to ease back into the writing scene since May 2011.

Continued Works: Fields of Innocence; Absolute; Dude, You Suck and I Like the Other Dude. My updating priority of these are set the way they are listed. However, I may occasionally write a oneshot. Including the aforementioned stories, I also regularly collaborate ideas with Mei-chan4's "Baby Universe" stories/series.

Update Schedule: None. I've tried to stick to a schedule, but I hate deadlines, so I'll update when I can.

Regarding My Avatar: DO NOT GET ME STARTED ON THE FLAWLESSNESS THAT IS ROYAI.

Now, go read...or hit the back button on your browser. ;)
