Author has written 5 stories for Legacy of Kain, Castlevania, and Transformers/Beast Wars.

Name: Christine Burke (It's easy enough to find on my site.)
Age: 29
Writing Style: Shameless Procrastinator

It has been some time since I have written anything worth sharing with others. Usually I'm more of an illustrator/photographer. You can see my work on the site listed above. It takes me an incredibly long time to write anything. However, I do seem to follow some trends. One year I draw with intensity, or photograph everything I can find through a camera lens. The following year I desire to write.

This year we'll just have to wait and see. (2009)

Last year was a dud. (2008)

My technical notes regarding Cybertronian reproduction can be found here:
On the Reproductive Habits of Cybertronians
This will play a large part in my Transformers stories.

I am currently working on:

"Prowl: Origin" - Transformers: Dreamwave & IDW Comics - Prowl's life and how he became the mech he is today. Warning: Characters may seem out of character at first.

Untitled Series - Transformers: G1 - Just a bunch of little blurbs and shorts that have no real relevance to anything. Most of them are Prowl-centric because he has been and probably always will be my favorite Transformer. I do have to warn that original characters show up in this.

"Sympathy for the Devil" - Castlevania: Curse of Darkness - Pretty much a sequel because I really hate how Isaac got shafted in the end. Warning: Original characters. (This story is on the back-burner because I pretty much got caught up in Transformers.)

Pieces I have completed:

"Minutiae" - Transformers: G1 - Basically I've concluded the movie sucked and wanted to take out the parts I found trite and overdone. That turned out to be basically everything. Read the Author Notes in the story if you're interested. The story then goes on to save the shuttle crew and explores a lot of psychology and beliefs.

"Of and Pertaining to a Certain Reluctant Leader" - Transformers: G1 - Basically a story following an important decision that has to be made by Hot Rod after the Return of Optimus Prime. (I do not acknowledge Season 4 and all that Headmasters crap. I barely even follow the "Hate Plague" crap but it takes place in this story because it lends itself to an explanation of Hot Rod's character development. Halfway through Season 3 it really felt like the writers were getting desperate, even when I was a kid.)

So here's the deal:

I write because I feel like it and to prove I still can. It relieves stress and boredom. It also allows me to further explore my favorite fandoms through my own mind. If you have a dislike of anything I write, please just don't read it. If you have constructive criticism, by all means, share. Don't ever expect to see finished projects. If you like a project I've worked on but haven't continued, feel free to contact me if you have ideas about continuing it yourself. I jealously guard my photographs and illustrations. I'm a little more open to sharing with my writing. Just give me credit for my stuff.
