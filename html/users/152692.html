A little about me: I love anime and my favorite anime is...I don't know,there are too many! I am mostly a fanfiction reader not a writer. I write not-so-bad songfics though. For the record I really don't like Kikyo and Kagura/Koga fics, I am morally agianst them.

I love this site and I love anime!
