Author has written 4 stories for Final Fantasy VII, and Kingdom Hearts.

I don't update often, as I go back and forth between school, laziness, attempting to find a job, and sometimes coming up with a decent fanfic idea. I also procrastinate, which means updating is even less likely to occur. However, I will do my best to finish the fics that I start (IBaW readers, say nothing!). Most of them are going to be one-shots anyway.

I welcome reviews and critiques, both positive and negative. What I don't welcome is flames. If you don't like the fanfic, tell me what you don't like about it (grammar, characterization, plot, plausibility, etc) and why, instead of lapsing into infantile screaming and insults. Otherwise I will laugh at you. Also, while I appreciate positive comments, if you can be a little more specific with what you liked about the fic, that would help me out a lot -- I like knowing what works and what I need to fix.
