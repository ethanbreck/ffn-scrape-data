Inertia:
A simple law of physics: An object once put in motion will stay in motion until a strong force acts upon it. But what happens when there's no stronger force and things get out of control? Falling deeper and deeper in over his head, he finds that he's loosing control and the people he thought he knew, may not be who they seem at all.

Pairings: Bakura/Malik, Yami/Yugi, Bakura/Ryou and implied Malik/Ishtar
