Author has written 4 stories for Matrix, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Phantom of the Opera.

I figured it was about time I got a new one of these. My old one, written in the middle of my junior year of high school, quite frankly sucked. Now, I'm a college girl and I figure I need a more mature profile.

Anyway, I write mainly as a hobby on the side. I am currently a Nursing major, leaving me little time to write fanfiction. However, my current story, The Same Old Songs we Used to Love, will be updated, if sporadically. I'm also thinking of taking on another project, called Fugue State for the Cowboy Bebop fandom, so keep your eyes out for that one!

Story Progress:

An Angel's Reassurance (A The Matrix fanfiction)- Was intended to be a stand-alone, despite my stubborn attempts at trying to write a second chapter/sequel. I believe it's better as a stand alone.

Who Wants to Live Forever (A Pirates of the Caribbean fanfiction)- Also a stand-alone. A sequel/second chapter was so hard to write!

Haunted (Another POTC fanfiction)- Sad to say, my one chance at horror was not only eaten by my home computer when I was transferring files from that computer to my laptop, but the outline was accidentally thrown away when I was purging my room before going to college. So, this story is officially DEAD until further notice.

The Same Old Songs we Used to Love (A Phantom of the Opera fanfiction)- My current project. However, school comes first, so updates will be few and far between. UPDATE: Sorry, to you loyal lovers of the phic, but Chapter 4 isn't coming along as well as I would like, so once school and life are less hectic, I might be able to finish.
