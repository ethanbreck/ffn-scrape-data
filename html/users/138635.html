October 19, 2011

Can't believe that it's been nearly 10 years since I've started posting on this name!

In that time, I have graduated both high and tech school, and decided I didn't get enough! Currently I am working on getting my degree in Business Technology, and am planning on opening up an Etsy site in which to sell some wears.

I'm also currently working on re-plotting several of my stories that are housed here, as well as my older stories from my previous pen name. I have hopes to have them re-written in a timely manner, but everyone knows how that goes!

As for the stories that are here, they will remain posted as-is. I know quite a few people prefer to read the old in addition to the new, and I also want to show how I've grown as a writer instead of hiding what I was.

Don't expect any updates for a while; I want to get the bulk of a story complete before starting to update.

Current Projects

Too many to really count, but rest assured I am planning on finishing all of my current WIPs!
