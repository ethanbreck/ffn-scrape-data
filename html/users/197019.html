Author has written 4 stories for Dark Angel, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.
I'm 22 and female, living in east-central Illinois. I'm mostly a sci-fi/fantasy fan (especially Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Dark Angel, and Star Trek), but I also write the occasional short story, and a LOT of poetry.
I'm in love with this website, mostly because of how interactive it is, between author and audience. You don't have to WONDER whether something "works" or how it might be received--you get to KNOW!
Which is why I try to review just about every story I read (as long as I can find something good to say about it--cuz negative reviews are no fun, for the reviewer or the reviewee).
Just FYI--you can find my one and only Star Wars fic under the author name "Adi Gallia". :)
So...read it all! I hope you enjoy it, but either way, let me know!!!!! ;)
