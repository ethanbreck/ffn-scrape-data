NOTICE: Transformers: Nexus Point has been cancelled, and the story has been taken down. My apologies to anyone who was a fan of the story, but I wasn't satisfied with how it was turning out, and the final decision was to remove it.

Special Notice: Most of my SW stories (anything written before "A Midsummer Night's Chaos," basically) were written before ROTS. There are now inaccuracies in these stories, I am aware. But after giving it some thought, I have decided to leave these stories in their original state. After all, Timothy Zahn, Alan Dean Foster, and all the other EU authors aren't being required to re-write their novels to fit the new movies...

Special Notice II: I tend to avoid the Expanded Universe novels, so my SW stories may draw on the movies and supplementary books, but very little on the novels. So expect discrepancies between my fics and, say, Shadows of the Empire or the Thrawn trilogy...
