Let's see. My name is Chelsea, but on the 'net I'm better known as Whiskers, Wishkres, and a million other annoying names that you don't really care about. Call me whatever you want... or just don't call me. *pokes her phone*

I like to read fanfics (duh), but I'm not much of a writer -- don't expect too much from me, okay? I do write as Else N.S on fictionpress.com, if you care, check them out!

Currently I'm interested in Harry Potter, Early Edition, Quantum Leap, The Lion King, Buffy, Angel and a little bit of X-Files fanfiction, so if you have any around I'd love to read it. I also love crossovers, they are really helpful for me to get introduced into new fandoms... Seeing that I've only read books 1-3 of Harry Potter (but I know most of the facts from the other books, yay for spoilers!), seen one episode of Buffy , half an episode of Angel (I've read episode transcripts though), and no X-Files. Last time I saw Quantum Leap was 5 years ago, and it's pretty much the same for Early Edition -- but I do watch it now that it's on PAX!

The point in me telling you this? Well, if I left a review and you are interested in constructive criticism, feel free to email me; I'll respond if I have time. I don't like to leave that sort of thing in my reviews unless the author okays it. But in any case, if I don't feel confident enough of the facts in the fandom don't expect me to comment on that, only on your writing style, grammar, overall plot, et cetera. I think I'm okay with Early Edition, Quantum Leap, Buffy, and Angel; Harry Potter I think I'm pretty well-versed in, especially MWPP stuff, but I know next to nothing about X-Files. Just warning you...

And for your information, I am a fifteen-year-old girl (American, if you care) with absolutely no life whose only goal in life is sit on the computer, become a crazy cat lady, and die. Cats rule, you see. My pets? I have a GORGEOUS tortoiseshell longhair named Ember and a shorthaired calico (actually she's my sister's, and very special herself... but I still have to favor my little Emmy!) named... *coughs* Sweetie Angel by my sister. Please note that my sister is 4 years older than me... enough said...

My next birthday is on a Thurday. My last birthday was on a Tuesday. I believe that my birthday before that was a Monday... Gifts, anyone?

I also have an absolutely pointless web site, feel free to check it out if you are bored -- whis-kers.org.

"This is the profile that doesn't end... And it goes on and on my friends..."

...That is all.
