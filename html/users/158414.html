Author has written 1 story for Dragon Ball Z.

Hello! I go by the pen name dragonzmoon and thought up the name because of my love for dragons and the moon. I have read and reviewed on ffn since around 1999, but not much for the last four years. I’m just recently getting back to reading fanfiction. When I first started I read every day and anonymously reviewed under the name Prorunga1220 or dragonzmoon.

I’m open to all kind of pairings in the Dragonball fandom. My favorite pairings are Goku/ChiChi, Bulma/Vegeta, Trunks/Pan, Trunks/Marron, Turles/Pan, Raditz/Bra, Vegeta/Pan, Bardock/Barb. I’m fine with “Mary Sues,” and over look small spelling and grammer mistakes. Some of my favorite fanfictions are OC’s paired with our favorite Saiya-jin men. I’m open to yaoi but don’t really read it.

I only read and write DBZ fanfiction and have been writing since about 2004, although Erotic was started in longhand in 2000. I also started a discarded fanfic called, “Fight for the Ultimate,” but I got so angry at myself one day that I ripped it up and threw it away…something I really regret.

Shattered Moonlight is my baby, I’m up to chapter 20 but not all chapters are finished. I’ve also written a nine chapter Goku/Bra fanfiction called “Office Indiscretion.”

I only write lemons. (I miss ffn before the NC-17 ban!)

I also have only posted Shattered Moonlight at one other site- deviantArt.

I’ve started several fanfictions in the dragonball kingdom:

1. Erotic – Two chapters. B/V, G/CC

2. Behind Blue Eyes – Two chapters; it’s a B/V

3. Once upon a Christmas Cheer – One chapter; B/V (Bardock’s in it.)

4. Obsession – One chapter; Pan/Turles, Bardock comes back to life.

5. Weird Science- ½ chapter; Goku/OC

I also have three other fanfiction ideas that I haven’t started yet...and one written out in long hand called, The Way of the Son. (One chapter)

Please review Shattered Moonlight and tell me what you think. I've got more hits/visits than reviews and would really like to know what you think. : D

Thanks!
