Me huh? Well I'll be quite surprised if you're actually interested. But here it goes anyway.

I'm a 24 year old grad of Gettysburg College with dual degrees in English and Theatre Arts - as that may or may not tell you, I'm obsessed with Shakespeare. That being said, I'm also and absolute Harry Potter nut. I've been a memeber of FFN for quite a while now, though I took a bit of a hiatus for the last two years, and I'm only now getting back into fanfics. I'm an actor and a musician in my soul (and in practice), but I have a love of writing that just won't die (though most of it goes into my lyrics and poetry, I enjoy writing the occasional story, and I'm trying to branch out into more lengthy pieces).

That being said - I love editing other people's work. Language has always been, and will always be, my true passion. I still edit my college age friend's papers for them just for fun (I know, crazy - but true!). Once I'm up to FFN's standards I look forward to being a beta for those in need!

Since I've already mentioned I'm an HP nut, I should mention I'm a huge Dramonie fan. However out of cannon it may seem, I really think that they're perfect for eachother! Ron is sweet and all... but he just doesn't have the brainpower to keep up with Hermione. Outside of Dramonie I'm just a huge fan of the Slytherines - I find Snape absolutely facinating. And Hermione is hands down my favorite HP character.

As far as my own writing goes, The stories I have up right now are all a bit old and outdated... Though I'll admit, Coffee and Cream and Tuesday still hold special places in my heart (for very different reasons - which is probably apparent if you read them). I hope to start writing a little fan fic again - so keep your eyes peeled for something new in the near future!

I really appreciate any feedback people can give me, constructive criticism is always welcome.

Oh.. and my works (though I'll be very impressed if you're still reading at this point!):

Waves of Steel - SS- One shot. This was my first ever fic, so while its not perfect, I do have a soft spot for it which is why I leave it up.

Under the Wing of a Dragon - HG/DM - discontinued? I like parts of it... have more of it written than is actually up, but I've sorta backed myself into a corner. We'll see... I may steal from it for another fic at some point.

Coffee and Cream - HG/BZ - One shot. bit of fluff... I don't do fluff, I know, but I couldn't resist

Tuesday - HG/DM - One shot. quite dark... you've been warned.

Alright... Now I'm done!
