o hai thar!

Welcome to my profile! I'm a 24 years old (26 in korean age), and currently living in South Korea. I'm from Ontario, Canada. I'm teaching ESL English to korean kids between 5 and 12, in a bunch of different classes.

Most people like to list all of their favorite movies, songs, bands, shows, movies et excetera, and I have those too. But I've decided to only list my favorite podcasts, because these people do it because they enjoy it and they rely on fan donations to keep running. So if you are ever looking for something to listen to, jump over to these casts and take a listen, you'll enjoy what you hear.

Escape Pod: A science fiction short story podcasted magazine. Really great stuff in here. Bolt fans should really listen to this story, right now! Just click the play button to listen from your browser.

The Metamor City Podcast: A Sci-Fi/Urban Fantasy Podcast, hands down some of the best story telling I've ever heard. Start here

Antithesis, Book I: Predestination and Other Games of Chance: A space Sci-Fi/Spy suspense. A really good, adult themed story.

anything from Mur Lafferty: This Godness of podcasting has produced tons of really great stories, the quality is utterly amazing. Start with Playing for Keeps

7th Sun: This is an excellent, completed trilogy of Sci-Fi novels about beta clones. I highly recommend it.

Drabblecast: This is another short story podcast magazine, they focus on strange flash fiction, about 15 minutes per story. These stories are often really really strange, very entertaining and quite thought provocing. I highly recommend it, find it here.

Billibub Baddings and the Case of the Singing Sword: An excellent, completed podcast novel about a Dwarf in the 1920s trying to solve the case of a fantastic object fallen into our world. Really great.

Chasing the Bard: A fantasy story about William Shakespear and the Fey. I Highly Recommend it.

Any of these podcasts can be found with a quick google search, I can't link them as ff.net doesn't allow links in profiles.
