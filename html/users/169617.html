UPDATE: September 10, 2003

Yes, an update, but don’t take it the wrong way. I have not decided to start posting here again, but I just thought I should share with everyone this lovely little flame that was sent my way.

"From: Blaze()

You stink. This site is doing you a huge favor by posting your crappy little stories yet all you can do is bitch and moan, you pathetic, stupid teenybopper. FOAD."

I don’t take flames lightly. I believe that if someone publicly flames me, then I have every right to publicly respond to it. So here goes.

First of all, FF.net is NOT doing me a “huge favor.” If they really wanted to do me a favor they would have READ my fic before they deleted it, and if anything, they’ve shown me how careless they can be by censoring a fic that DID NOT NEED to be censored. I have every right to complain about this incompetence on their part. They are NOT the only fanfic site on the web and should not be treated like some sort of online gods who deserve our undying gratitude. It’s just too bad more people don’t realize that.

SECOND, I would advise you to be careful calling someone you don’t even know a “teenybopper.” I am not a teenager and I haven’t been for several years. So if you’re going to flame someone, why don’t you go to where the REAL teenyboppers are and leave your little love notes to the Mary Sue writers?

Third, let me point out to everyone that this is coming from someone who doesn’t even have the guts to leave an email address or a signed review. That tells me you don’t have anything better to do with your time than insult people, so the next time you feel like getting on my case why don’t you show some backbone and email me directly?

- Shiva

IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT: May 10, 2003

Okay. I never thought I would live to see the day when I could get any more pissed at the FF.net administration. I was quite surprised, and upset, when I got a notice saying that they had removed my latest fic, “Where I’ll Return.” The reason they gave me? “Rating: explicit content or adult content above current rating.” I wonder, do they even take the time to LOOK at stories before they decide to delete them? NONE of the chapters that I posted so far had ANY “explicit adult content.” I wrote a kissing scene that was PG-13 at best! Okay, so it was yaoi, and the scene involved two boys being naked in a bathtub, but ALL they did was kiss and IT WAS NOT GRAPHIC, and definitely NOT more than an R rating! (I am sure many of my readers will back me on this.) I also stated very clearly in the first part of the fic that I would not post the actual lemon content here, but that I would put up links to where it could be found! Um... HELLO?

I am very, VERY pissed off by this. It strikes me as odd that they didn’t delete my other fic, “Midnight Rain” that is even more graphic than “Where I’ll Return.” The only reason I can come up with is that somebody might have complained about this fic specifically, and the FF.net admin took their word and didn’t bother to read my author notes OR the actual content of the fic. But whatever. The point now is, FF.net has finally pushed me over my limits. I accepted it when they banned NC-17 fics, and I worked around it the best way I could, but this... THIS I cannot accept. When the NC-17 ban was first initiated, I stopped posting here, but I came back because I told myself that I could live with posting a majority of my chapters here, but post the lemons elsewhere. Apparently, though, that’s not good enough.

So what am I going to do now? What will become of my fics, and any future fics I may write? Well, they won’t end up at FF.net, that’s for sure. From now on, you can find my stories at the following places:

My website:

Fandomination

The Riku x Sora Mailing List

The Kingdom Hearts Yaoi Mailing List

To all my readers: I love you all dearly and I am very sorry that this happened. I am saddened because I got so many wonderful reviews, and now FF.net has deleted them. As pissed as I feel right now, I am also saddened that I have once and for all decided to sever all ties with FF.net. I’ve been around a long time, and made many wonderful friends through this site, but what they’ve done is beyond unacceptable. Any positive feelings I have left for this site are now pretty much gone. If you would like to give me any feedback, please email me at ice_blue_shiva@yahoo.com or contact me on AIM (AeonShiva10).

Well... that’s all I have to say for now. A warning to all: Beware of what you post. Before we know it, FF.net will probably start banning Yaoi and R-rated fics too. At the rate things are going, it wouldn’t surprise me.

Adieu, FanFiction.net. Parting is such sweet sorrow.

Love,
Shiva
May 10, 2003
