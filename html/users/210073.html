YAY! I finally figured out my login info! I'm baack! I admit, I've been roleplaying a lot in the past decade, which is another reason why my presence here has been lacking. But I've been re-infected by the writer-bug. I'm regrowing my plotbunny farm, and am up to writing real stories again!

This is a general disclaimer for all of the fanfics (of mine) you read here or any other ones you find on other sites that I have authored:

None of the recognisable characters belong to me. They belong to their respective creators. Any characters, besides the few OCs that I have in my possession, are the property of those who originally came up with them. No plagurism is intended. All the plots, however poor (or well written) they may be, are my own. I may on occation use another's writings as inspiration, and in which case I will cite them as such. If anyone finds inspiration in any of my stories, please feel free to write them, just be sure to cite me as your source.

Yay! now that that's over with, I don't have to worry about going back into my fics and making sure that they have disclaimers.

Reviews are of course welcome, both good and bad, as long as they're constructive. None of my stories are betaed, so all errors can be attributed to my own folly, and if you'd like to review to critique my grammar, don't bother, I'm working on that now. If you want to critique my plot, character personalities, storyline, etc, feel free. One never learns unless one knows what their mistakes are.

Now, for a bit of an update:

I'm going to go back now and edit all of my stuff now. I realise that while my stories are wonderful, they're also riddled with novice errors and so many typos I cringe just reading them. I'm getting to that now, promise.

For my stories, A Night to Remember and Spatial Unit 2 are on hiatus until further notice. SU2's 'future' was supposed to be a crossover, but the series changed too dramatically (and has long since ended) so that's out. I need to think of a new verse, and/or create one for it. That'll take time. ANtR will be a while for me to get back into the right mindset.

All my other incomplete fics, I'm hoping to (now that I have a working computer) finish. Hopefully before I start anything new.

If you have any ideas you want me to write, I'll definitely do my best.

Just send me a review through one of my stories or send me an e-mail at:

tpol12st@yahoo.com, seymour-smoothies@hotmail.com, sir_integra_f_w_hellsing@yahoo.com.au or Anamaria_Sparrow@yahoo.com.au

Hope you enjoy my stories. Review them, tell me what you did or didn't like about them. I promise that I'll listen as long as it's not a blatant flame.

Thank you for reading my stories...

Peace... err... War (Sorry Ares. Don't Smite me! I still love you!)
