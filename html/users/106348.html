I'm a crazy hip gal - cool if you will, cool if you please, cool if you want.I'm 17 years old, i live in a dodgey little town near london, which is the proud owner of 4 funeral homes (yep folks thats how fun it is).

i used to be drucilla malfoy and then pApErBaCk WrItEr but i got bored with those names so now am BlueJello86 - dedicated to our fav. SG1 gal.

My favorite things are... my puppy,stargate sg1 (shipper ahoy!), hitchhikers guide to the galaxy, star wars, harry potter, monty python, red dwarf (craig charles used to live down my road (my claim to fame)),um... my favorite food is hobnobs with cheese.

My worst writing habbits are, writing 'avec' instead of 'with' and bracketting everything oh and my spelling is apaulling.

I started writing fanfic when i was 6 years old (no really) when i wrote my first Famous Five fic - it was amazing let me tell you. From there i went onto Starwars (ages 8-11ish)
First published some Harry Potter stuff on here (aged 14-i think)but they've since gone missing
Then came the days of storytime666, which hold many fond memories for me - they were 'original stories'
Now i write Stargate SG1 - which is the best TV show EVER. My fics are either humor or Sam/Jack fluff.

I'm on live journal and am always looking for like-minded people to friend so if your on there then drop me a line!:)
