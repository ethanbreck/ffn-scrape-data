Hmmm in the writing mood so might as well tell you a little about myself. I currently live with my boyfriend and my two cats Tempest and Apollo. I love to write and color...yes color, you know, the thing you did when you were little and you never were able to stay in the lines? I used to be really big into anime and manga, though I've kinda grown out of it, but not completely. I've been writing original stories and fanfics for years but I've never liked anything enough to post anything. I'm NOTORIOUS for not finishing things, BUT with enough reviews I feel more pressured to post chapters because I hate to let people down so REVIEW REVIEW REVIEW! I'm a really open person and would probably love to get to know you. So if you ever have any questions about things don't worry that you're bothering me. I'd love to hear from you.

I'm an avid supporter of all kinds of fanfiction and write whatever I feel flows. That includes slash, yaoi and yuri. I'll always warn you in the summery if the stories fall into these categories so if you don't like those types of stories don't read them. Flames will be ignored and deleted so don't waste my or your time. No one else but me will ever see them and I'll completely ignore them once I've noticed what they are.

The one fic that is currently posted will probably never be finished unless I'm suddenly struck with the urge to write more. It's sad but true. I really liked where it was going and had the whole thing planned out in my head, so you never know. I haven't really written anything in a long time, but I'm going to try to give it another shot.
