Dedicated dreamer, whose schemes almost never work, I fell into writing by accident (well all that day dreaming at school had to have a purpose!). And that sums up my life really... A series of fortuitous accidents!

Female of the species, writer, publisher, social media marketing puke and eccentric party animal, my life revolves around these things in real life, my pets and my growing collection of ink. My fantasy life revolves around the charming, the funny and the downright ridiculous.

I guess I'm wired a little differently than most folk, my ink is part of who I am. Every tattoo has meaning for me. Beyond their meaning they serve a wider purpose. They represent a liberation in my life too. Besides, I'm a size 22 in a size zero world, there really is nothing left to fear, proud of the fact that all things naughty apparently begin "Since meeting Sj..."

I suppose I'm naturally a bit of an outsider, because riding with the tide and going with the flow are things that I like to avoid. I don't do psychology, you have one life...live it, as the saying goes. Dance, Make Stuff, Try Not To Be A Dick! Live life without trampling on others' lives. Well I'm trying to, it's just perhaps that my perception of realities is kinda different from most folks. Sometimes my deep hot dark soul is more than a little odd. But I don't bite. Honest!
