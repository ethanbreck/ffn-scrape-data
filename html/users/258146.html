The Blog From Previous News

May 2, 2012:

I've been gone for quite some time, and of course, during that time a lot of shows have passed. Still, after all that time, things are finally looking up. Now that my current preoccupations are finally clearing up for good, I have truly free time in the longest time. I have a lot of discipline issues, and a lot of hypocrisies. A desire for attention, yet a lack of effort for gaining what I wanted. Regardless, it's now time to start up again, and regain what I lost. Now that I have all the time in the world, it's time to do what I claim I want to do.
