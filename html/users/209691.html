Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.
I'm just your normal average teenage girl, obsessed with books, dragons, swords, medieval costumes, drawing, and writing fantasy stories... well, ok, so I'm not that normal. And you know what? I don't want to be.

Harry Potter books are wonderful. I think that's pretty self-explanatory, considering the only fanfiction I have is HP. I tend to go for H/G and R/H ships, but I like romances with Draco as well. I like reformed evil. There's something intriguing about it. And his silver eyes remind me of Vanyel Ashkevron, a favorite character from Mercedes Lackey's Valdemar. But enough about that.

I really really really love reviews, so if you're reading my story, pretty please drop me a review and tell me what you think! I'll love you forever if you do!
