I'm not very good at these things... you know, updating information about me... updating in general. I have this horrible habit of leaving stories half finished since I have so many things going on in my life. I have a few fics in the works that I have decided are worth saving, and therefore I will throw myself back into them. I'll hopefully get the chance to do that sometime soon, but there are several other responsibilities that call my attention at this point in time.

Ah, Naruto. So much can be said about it, right? I've been trying to think of an original fic to start on, but I haven't found one yet. Because of that, I have spend a great deal of time finding many authors and stories that are well worth your time if you are interested. I have a sort of bias as to which couples I like, two to be specific in this manga. Try not to let it offend you, both tend to be the source of a lot of debates. Love is love to me.

Thanks for all the support!
