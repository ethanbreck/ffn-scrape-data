Hm. What's there to say? I'm 18 years old, I'm obsessive about new fandoms, and I'm rather shy about posting my own stuff. That said, I also don't have the will to finish the big ideas my brain generally churns out - I'm more of a plot bunny breeder than a fanfiction writer, at this point.

My favored fandom at the moment is Naruto, though I've also enjoyed Knights of the Old Republic and Inuyasha - I still dabble with KotOR ideas, even.

I love a good epic and a good romance, but I dislike slash. I also dislike writers who randomly interject entire Japanese phrases for no particular reason - using titles (Kakashi-sensei!), the original Japanese words for attacks or skills (Kage Bunshin no Jutsu, as opposed to "Art of the Shadow Doppleganger") I don't mind... but switching dialogue between Japanese and English is aggravating. That said, I read stories that do this anyway, like the hypocrite I am.

I do like original characters (so long as they aren't hideously Mary Sue), and crossovers - they can provide a much-needed twist if done properly.

I'm generally happy to beta people, or serve as a springboard for any ideas they may have. Drop me an IM (preferable) or an email if you'd like to speak with me about either.
