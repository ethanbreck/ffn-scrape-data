Last Update: 16th January 2012

I'm Australian. I think that's the only thing you need to know about me. That's like the only reason why I ever check these profiles.

In case you're an actual fan of my writing, I do have a FictionPress account with my series of stories about young adults living in the fictional town of East Peaks on the New South Wales central coast.

Cobbled Together: A little choose your own adventure style story that I've started. Feel free to join in the fun by creating your own scenarios to add to the story.

My DeviantArt Account has some pictures from my stories in it. At this time my versions of Harry, Tom and Voldemort from "Time and Again"

Random gripe: Why is it that sometimes my best reviews are from anonymous accounts? Why can't you leave an email address so that I can tell you that I think you're awesome? Why can't you let me explain myself to you?

Story Notes:

Next up is a Supernatural/Fallen mini-series crossover. I secretly started posting it on my poor excuse for a LiveJournal.

In progress is another Gilmore Girls story. It's Jess-centric, longer than an epic and unlikely to be finished for years since it follows the series plot from season 4 to the series finale before Jess and Rory get together. So far I'm through season four, the summer between four and five, and about eight episodes into season five. The going has slowed on this one.

Dawn Came Again - My first Supernatural fic. Technically the first in a series. But since I doubt I'll ever actually write the rest of it, it's alone. If you ask nicely, I'll tell you what happens next.

Heat Induced - My first Dark Angel fic. Probably the only Max/Alec fic I will ever write. Not that I'm a Max/Logan fan or anything. Now complete.

The Spare Wand - A story I wrote for my tenth anniversary of being on this site. It's not my greatest, but I think it's an okay H/D fic.

Time and Again - Writing this story is kind of crazy nostalgic for me. It's so similar to my earlier fanfics, like GAAM, ROTF and WSTSS, and yet, not. No idea when the next couple of chapters will be done. I'm vaguely thinking of sticking to a once a month schedule but as always, don't hold your breath.

The Book - A short Gilmore Girls story. It was supposed to be about 5k but turned into closer to 15k. Still a one-shot, though. It's in Rory's POV and I've finished writing it from Jess' POV but haven't gotten around to typing and posting yet. I might have to finish T&A before that.

Of Memory and Memories - I'm thinking of rewriting this. The way season ten left off fits easily into this story so I might as well make it as canon as possible... if I ever become a less lazy person.

Gods atop a mountain - I've decided against continuing this story. Which is probably devastating for some of you. I just can't bring myself to write a story that's an AU from so far back anymore.

Everything else is either a one-shot or died. I can't say that I ever will finish any of the stories that are Harry Potter at Hogwarts stories but you're welcome to ask.
