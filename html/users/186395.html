Author has written 5 stories for Lord of the Rings.

I don't like talking about myself, other then to say that none of my stuff is slash. I have nothing against anyone who writes or reads it, but my stories were written with friendship in mind. You should also note that my muse like to take long vacations and often leaves me without inspiration for long periods of time. Therefore, I always finish a story before I begin posting so that I don't leave people hanging for months or even a year, but often stories are few and far between. Anyway, instead of wasting anymore of this space on me, I thought I would use it to recommend one of my favourite series. The Nameless One by Elf Eye is a collection of witty and humourous stories revolving around my favouite Mirkwood elf. This series is AU and covers Legolas (Anomen) from the time he leaves Mirkwood as an elfling up until he returns to Rivendell for the Council of Elrond. They can be found at http://www.fanfiction.net/u/442271/
If you do end up reading this series, may I personally recommend "Got Milk?" which has the official designation of 'my story' =)

One more thing, if you enjoy "Little Legolas" stories, I have a C2 community filled with wonderful stories of his adventures as a child. Simple follow this link, or click on the C2 community button below and enjoy. http://www.fanfiction.net/c2/6296/3/0/1/

NEW! I have very little time for reading fan fiction at the moment, and even less for searching for new stories, so if you think that you know some good qualityones that fit with my community and wish to be a staff member, simply drop me a line and let me know.
