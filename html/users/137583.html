Author has written 8 stories for Lord of the Rings, Wrestling, Dark Tower series, Labyrinth, X-Men: The Movie, and Harry Potter.

What can I say? This stuff is old. Way old. Back when I was a very bad writer old. It's embarrassing to have it still posted. However, I have been convinced to leave it up, as it is seriously depressing when fic you like disappears from the internet - something that's happened to me more than once. So here it will stay, in all its embarrassing badness. There is no chance in hell any of this will be continued, just so you know. Still, if there's something you fall in love with, feel free to beg for more. You never know what might happen if you beg enough.
