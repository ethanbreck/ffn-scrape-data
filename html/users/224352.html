Hi, I'm Meghan Heath and I'm a fan of Digimon, Harry Potter, and Star Wars. I'm also a spontaneous writer, meaning that if an idea pops into my head, I'll write it down, and work on the details along the way. I know it sounds ill-prepared, but it helps me get my thoughts down on paper. I also read each review, and take each suggestion seriously. So if you have any suggestions on how to improve stories, I take them. I ignore flamers, that have no suggestions, and just critcism. I'm 19 years old and like horseback riding, photography and reading. This is my series: "The Fifth Guardian" "Deceptions and Lies" "The Final Leg" "The Struggle" "Dangerous Mistakes" "The Past Remembered"

I'm back! Miss me? I'm going to be back for at least a while writing stories. REVIEW EVERYONE!
