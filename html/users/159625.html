Author has written 18 stories for ER, Smallville, Wrestling, Higher Ground, Alias, West Wing, Numb3rs, and RENT.

Make no mistake. I am the writer formerly known as CrimsonRose1. I decided I didn't like that screen name anymore (far too corny, in my opinion, but hey, I was 12), so I've changed it to Sassafrass86, based on two (though not necessarily my favorite) Green Day songs.

I haven't been very active in writing fan fiction lately, but I'm trying my best to update and write new stories. My writing style seems to have greatly improved since I was a pre-teen, so that's a plus. If you read my fics, you're either going to be an old-school reader or a new-school reader. Old-school readers will be reading my fics (and I'm pretty sure that most, if not all, of them are incomplete) about ER, Alias,and The West Wing. Will I be updating those fics at all? Probably not, as I have stopped watching the latest seasons of all three programs and have no muse to keep the fics going. New-school readers will most likely be reading my fics about Numb3rs and, possibly, CSI. So either way, I hope you enjoy what I write, as I really do enjoy writing myself. Below I've listed some of my favorite fandoms and what not, just so you get a feel of who I am. And if you're feeling lucky, you can always e-mail me and request a fan fic about one of the fandoms. I provide no guarantees, but if I'm in a muse-y kind of mood, I'll try to write you something nice. Or, you can just e-mail me, and we can have a little chat-chit, maybe become friends, have a cup of tea...

The End
