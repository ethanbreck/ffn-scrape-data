Author has written 9 stories for Lord of the Rings.

If I have made you rethink the current fanon assumptions about Elladan, Elrohir and Aragorn, then I have succeeded.

I recommend reading the stories in chronological order, as each one builds on the previous ones:

1. Touch

2. To Give Hope

3. Answers and Questions

4. Day and Night

5. I Will be Your Memory

6. A Yearling Shoot

7. Into the Wild

The Sacrifices of Friendship is my attempt at a bit of a humourous aside and is not part of the series. It should be considered AU, very AU, at least when compared to Tolkien’s canon. Unfortunately, though, I fear it is not so terribly AU in comparison with the majority of Legolas / Aragorn fanfiction that is out there.
