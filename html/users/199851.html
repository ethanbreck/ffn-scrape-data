Author has written 11 stories for X-Men: Evolution, Harry Potter, Final Fantasy VII, Yu-Gi-Oh, Escaflowne, and Beyblade.
Many millenia ago,seven powerful treasures known as the Millenium Items were created.They were the Puzzle,the Ring,the Rod,the Scales,the Key,the Eye, and the Necklace. Together,these seven powerful items,each with their own power,were sealed within a tomb inside a temple.

The legend was wrong.

There were in fact eight items.

The eighth item was the Millenium Temple,where the other seven Items were sealed.Created solely for the purpose of containing the Millenium Items safely,the Temple was locked up,and forgotten.Only three of the original Millenium Items were thought to hold trapped souls-the Puzzle,which held captive the young Prince of the Amenhotep Dyansty; the Ring,which bound the dark soul of a tomb-robber, and the Rod,who also held within it the imprisoned soul of a young man. These trapped souls became known as 'Yamis'.

Legend was wrong again.

The Temple itself held an imprisoned soul;a soul of great power.

A soul of the Shadow Realms.

This soul was bound within the Temple itself,to guard the sacred relics within until the rightful owners came to claim them. Only when all seven Items were returned would the Yami from the realm of shadows be allowed to roam freely.

Now at last,the Shadow Yami is free.

And she's ready to play with some destinies...
