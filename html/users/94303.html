August 9th, 2019

I think it's fair to say that this account has been on hiatus for quite awhile (the last time I updated this profile, for instance, was back in early 2013) and that there's little chance of much being updated here in the future. Much as I enjoyed my time in my various anime fandoms (Dragonball Z and Naruto, in particular, were my special loves), it was time for me to move on. Real life was beckoning me and I just didn't have the time to devote to fanart or fanfiction any longer.

I've kept busy, however. At the time of this writing, I've been married for eight years (together with my husband for eighteen altogether) and have two beautiful children, a girl and a boy. My sweet girl, Penny, is three and full of rambunctious energy. You can see her coming with all that curly red hair, but you likely still won't have time to jump out of the way! My baby boy, Alex, has just turned ten months old, gotten his first tooth and is contemplating his first steps. I've been lucky enough to stay home with them, which has really put a damper on participating in all my old hobbies (such as writing), but I wouldn't give them up for the world. They have been my biggest and best project.

I might come back to this profile one day and finish up some things, but I can't promise. Besides, considering how long Naruto has been going on and the new episodes of Dragonball Z (which I never thought I would see) that I have yet to find the time to watch, everything here is already horribly outdated. New readers, please take pity upon me and remember that mommies can't watch much anime (though I've seen a lot of Peppa Pig and Toy Story these past few years).

With much affection,
