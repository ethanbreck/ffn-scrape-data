UPDATE 07.07.11

I can't believe it's been ten years and two weeks since I joined this sight. It's been one heck of a journey, and I like to think it's been a sign of progress. I hope everyone's enjoyed the works I've posted here, and I hope that whatever I post in the future makes readers happy as well.

UPDATE 12.09.12


My goodness it has been a while since I've done anything here. Spent too much time on my phone and, as I noted over a year ago, working on other projects. Ideas are still churning, still, but I'm not sure when I'll be able to get them out. Just wanted to say thank you to all of my readers and reviewers. It's nice to know that after a decade, people are still reading Sailor Moon fics, and that they're still being enjoyed. Much love to everyone out there.
