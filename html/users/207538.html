Hey everyone! My name is Marita. I'm eighteen years old and have been writing fan fic since I was 12, though it wasn't always good, and some may argue it still isn't.

Mainly I write CSI nowadays, but you'll also find the random movie fic or something of the Frasier variety in there, too. I write at random intervals when the muse moves me, and lately that hasn't been often. I've got a few unfinished fics, which I feel terrible about, but not terrible enough to actually finish them.

I've been writing Warrick/Catherine (CSI) for a long time, and will continue to do so. However, don't look here for any new Grissom/Sara fics unless you're really hopeful and optimistic - that ship's pretty much done for me, at least when it comes to writing.

Feel free to post any kind of review, bad or good, I appreciate the attention either way. :)
