Hey, I'm Satanic Nocturne, your not-so-average girl living in a not-so-popular area in New York. I love reading and just listening to music. These things are my life.

(Besides, how much of a life can a teenager have?)

My Favorite Anime Shows,Games, and Couples are-

Inuyasha:

1.Kagome/Sesshomaru (Ah, a classic. Don't we all "love" Kagome? To tell you the truth, I miss the concept of this pairing completely. It's always nice to read one every once in a while but a "couple" always follow the same story line!)

2.Sango/Miroku (I love Miroku, he's a great character.)

3.Kikyo/Sesshomaru (I am in the process of writing one, it's really hard when you don't have any others to look upon. These two characters are not developed thoroughly in the show, so they might be OOC. Sesshoumaru is hard enough, but Kikyo too? I'm gonna kill myself! One person said that Kikyo was OOC, but I gotta remember that she has a soft spot for children and Sesshoumaru, well he seems different in the USA Anime than how all the other authors portray him. I watched my tapes over and over.)

4.Kikyo/Inuyasha (I have no idea why people hate Kikyo so much, I think that she is pretty cool. She also has better lines in the anime than Kagome:) )

5.Sango/Sesshoumaru (this is one kick-ass couple. I love Soledad by Thunk, it's the only fic on FF.net that I can read over and over again and never get tired of!)

6.Kagome/Naraku (Not many of them have been writen, but that's all right, I like "The Meaning of Truth" by ForestKarma")

7.Kikyo/Naraku (How could I forget? This one is classic! (I'm not being sarcastic here) I still have yet to find a good fic with this coupling, in any case, if you're reading my bio, write one!)

8.Sango/Naraku (YEAH!This couple has been on my mind for the longest! Finally ,someone had the guts to write one! I might write one someday, that's how much I like it!)

9.Kagura/Sesshoumaru (Now this is one couple that I love and can never get tired of, cause there aren't enough out for me to do that anyway!! I love the few stories that are out on the couple, and I hope that more are created!!)

Final Fantasy VII

1.Tifa/Vincent
2.Tifa/Sephiroth (I oughta write one someday, Sephiroth is as hot as hell.)
3.Tifa/Rufus (I can't beleive that I forgot to write this pairing before! The best T/R I ever read was Sanguine Depths, I think it was taken of FF.net, but it was so good.)
