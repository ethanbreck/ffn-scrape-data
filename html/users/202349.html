Hi everyone and welcome to my Fanfiction Archive! It's pretty small now, but hopefully it will grow with time.

I am a big anime/manga/cartoons/reading/RPG fan, but I don't have as much time for it anymore as I used to (crazy work schedules that mess with your circadian rhythm do that to you!). I'm also a big toy collector, with my current favorites being Masters of the Universe Classics, Winx Club, Monster High, the new Jem and the Holograms from Integrity, the occasional DC or Marvel figure, and the upcoming Ever After High.

I love 90s cartoons (since I grew up on them), so for the moment that's where my fanfiction efforts will lie. Also, even though so many series are close to my heart, I want to focus on a few series that don't have a whole lot added to them already (Series like Dragon Flyz and Princess Gwenevere/Starla and the Jewel Riders). If you're interested in trading reviews, just email me!

Update 12/30/13 - My NaNo-ing was a success! Heart Stone is now finished, and I am currently in the process of doing some (very) light editing and cleaning up. It's strange that I'm finally finished with this story after more than five years of working on it! But I'm delighted to share the completed work with everyone who has waited so long. :)

I'm hopefully going to also finish my one-off Jewel Riders fic and get that posted up sometime soon as well.

Cheers all!
--Ry
