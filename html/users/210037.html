Hello, hello!

Wednesday, March 18, 2015:

I am writing again! I needed a creative outlet and this was the only form of artistic expression that didn't require supplies. I'm very rusty, and I need practice. Fidelity is being continued as I write this. I'm working on Chapter 12 now, although there isn't much of a following for Crescent Moon anymore. I love the story, so I'm going to keep writing and hopefully finish it. I'm also thinking of revamping the Escaflowne fic, Tsuki no Hime. I had great ideas for that story and I didn't execute them properly. I need to write the prequel first, I think.

Well, I hope you enjoy my musings below.
