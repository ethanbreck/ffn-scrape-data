Name's Tenshi no Yami. Somehow or another, the name got shortened to Tami. I really do answer to it, so feel free to call me that. lol

I'm 18. Graduated high school. Taking a break before college. I work at the Starbucks in my local Safeway. I've become a total Starbucks junkie since working there. It's insane.

I'm a Christian and proud of it. Doing my best to live for God. Failing miserably most of the time, but He's patient with me and merciful. Yay!

I'm not going to do an update schedule, cuz it'll happen as it happens.

If ya'll have any questions for me, my e-mail is OkamiYoukaiHime43@hotmail.com. That's my preferred way of communication, really. Feel free to e-mail me. I promise I don't bite. ;-)
