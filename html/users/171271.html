To my great annoyance, the latest round of code sweeps has eliminated all of the section breaks in my stories. It will take quite a bit of time to fix this, especially since I'll have to fix quite a lot of old code that ffnet has progressively disabled over the past six years. Those URLs are now listed on my homepage under 'Tolkien Sources'. (Updated 04/22/2007 - all links are in working order.)
