Seems to have left...

Hello, curious peeps. If you are reading this, then you have managed to stumble onto my profile. Sorry, but I don't keep anything fancy on this. I kind of left the site as fanfiction doesn't really interest me that much anymore, plus this site has changed a LOT since I first signed up here. I've mostly been writing about my own stories and plotlines and characters instead of going back to the good old days.

I'm sorry, but Shelter from the Rain and The Realms of Desolation will probably never get finished. I wrote myself into a brick wall with those two stories, and to be frank, I have no idea HOW to save them. =C

I will still keep my stories up here, but don't expect me to write anything new, I'm afraid.

But yeah. I might come back here...someday. For now, au revoir!

And I'm not dead from the entire Internet.
