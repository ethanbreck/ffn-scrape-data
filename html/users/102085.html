Oh my gosh I haven't updated this in quite some time. I am no longer twenty-four, but twenty-seven (twenty-eight later this year) but I still haven't found the courage to post one of my own fanfics. I still think "A Fathers Sin" is an amazing fanfic but there are some others that really stand out in my mind ("Little Green Leaf" comes to mind). I'm not reading as much Harry Potter fanfic as I used to but instead find myself reading Naruto fanfic the most. I'm still canadian but no longer in the time of my life that involves full-time post secondary education.

Updated April 2005

This is me :)My favorite stories are ones that have made me giggle out loud, tears fall from my eyes, or just too good to stop reading (usually more than once).
Yes they are all Harry Potter ones currently but that is not the only kind of fanfiction I read. I read a lot of anime and original ones too but as of yet I haven't found any that really "ring my bell" so to speak.
Otherwise I am a twenty-four year old canadian currently involved in my post-secondary education. I do write fanfiction myself but I have yet to write a fanfic that I really feel comfortable putting on here for all to see, maybe one day.
If you do find yourself reading this I do recomend the stories listed in my favorite stories (especially A fathers Sin by Severtius and A Little of Your Time by Serpentina)
