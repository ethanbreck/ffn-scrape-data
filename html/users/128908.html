Author has written 8 stories for Labyrinth, Harry Potter, Star Wars, Robert Langdon series, and Sherlock Holmes.

I am 46 and nearly all of this fiction was written at least 10 years or more ago, some of it nearly 20 years ago. I have been a Labyrinth fan since the movie originally came out and a Harry Potter fan since book 3 came out. I became a Star Wars fan when Revenge of the Sith came out, but I'm not that impressed with the latest trilogy. I am not a huge fan of the Da Vinci Code but I wrote in this fandom because I wanted to debunk some of the rubbish in the book and I thought Silas deserved another chance. I have been a fan of the Sherlock Holmes stories for many years and particularly love the BBC's adaptation with Jeremy Brett.

Please note, the HP stories were written before book 5 came out. The 'Luna' is an original character, not the Luna Lovegood in OotP. The Star Wars fiction was written after the release of Episode III but is AU.
