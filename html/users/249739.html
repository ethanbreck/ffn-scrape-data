Hello, and thanks for stopping by my profile!

I would love to write more fanfiction than I currently do, but thanks to time constrictions, so far I've only worked on DN Angel. My current project is Phantom Destiny, but if you have other ideas I'd be more than open to hearing them. In fact, I'd be profusely flattered. Phantom Destiny is also the first fanfiction I've ever written. Most of my normal writing is listed HERE.

I'm not the type to nag for reviews, because I appreciate silent readers as much as vocal ones, but it sure does motivate me to know how people are reacting to my writing. That's why, if you have an interest in one of my stories, you'd be making this author's day by leaving even a small comment to let me know you were here!
