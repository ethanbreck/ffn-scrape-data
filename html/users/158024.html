Hmmm...what can I say about myself? Would you even care? But I guess that's what bios are for, though, so...

I'm a college student attending a small private school located in the middle of nowhere, USA. The campus is known for it's squirrel infestation, and the town it's a part of is so small that its technically classified as a village. Which is a nice way of saying that there is jack squat to do around here. Thus, the reason for my contribution to the world of Harry Potter fan fiction.

I will warn all of you now - my favortie character is Professor Snape, and a good deal of my stories will deal with him. I know what most of you are thinking, but we Snapefans know that deep down inside he's really a good guy - even if his personality can be worse than a raging hippogriff. And it helps that he's played by Alan Rickman in the movies. :)

Aside from HP, I also like the Lord of the Rings, Watership Down, and anything by Frank Peretti - especially "The Oath" That book rocks!

The series I am currently working on is entitled "When Worlds Collide." Eventually it should be about five "books" long, but at the moment I have just gotten started with "The Phoenix Reborn." Reviews encourage faster writing, hint, hint! *-^

Anyway, feel free to email me if you ever want to chat or ask a question, or whatever. Flames will be laughed at, and used to heat my abominably cold room. Please read and enjoy!
