Alright...people want to know about me???

I am one of the biggest Harry Potter fans that ever was. I know pretty much everything there is to know about it and surprisingly enough, my favourite couple from it are Hermione and Draco. Even though we all know it will never happen really, we have to hold onto hope right? I also like, as characters, Sirius, Remus, Lucius and Snape, but Draco is my favourite. I don't even know why I like Hermione and Draco as a couple, because as a character I am not that fond of Hermione. But meh. What are you going to do?

I also enjoy Lord of the Rings, which shouldn't surprise anyone because I have found out that most people that like Harry Potter usually like lord of the Rings. Don't ask me why, maybe they are sort of similar in a very different way. ;)

Um, I am 18 and have just finished school...I am hoping that my marks were good enough to get into Uni, and I am going to major in Graphic Art and Design. I am 100 Aussie, but hope one day to travel the world. Living in the one place is sort of boring.

Um, thats all I can think of at the moment. Feel free to read any or all of my stories...some are better than other but they all eventually go on. Sorry to everyone about not updating my stories earlier. Those of you that are going through school at the moment will understand how hard it is the update fanfiction and pass school at the same time. But now thatI am finished I hope to update my stories much more regularly.

Love to you all,

Samba, aka. Malicious Malfoy
