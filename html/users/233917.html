Hi. It was nice of you to come here. I wish I had more to offer you, but I'm a very private person. I write when I have time, or when the mood strikes me. Or when I can think straight. Notice I only have two incomplete stories up...

There are some very talented people here. I'm sure some of them will make it going pro. I once thought that I would be one of those people when I was younger. I now realize that I will not. I lack the structure and clarity necessary to be a professional, published author. So I won't lead you on with my delusional aspirations.

Anyway, I really enjoy helping people, with practically anything, including money. My mother says it's because I'm a virgo. I'm not too sure about that though, because my younger brother is also a virgo, and he couldn't care less about other people...

Anyway, as you can tell, I'm not too good at this profile thing, so I'll quit while I'm only marginally lacking in worthy information.

Good luck, and may happiness cling to you like duct tape to a cat's paw.
