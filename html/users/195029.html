Author has written 38 stories for Stargate: SG-1, Dawson's Creek, Harry Potter, Eureka, Stargate: Atlantis, Arrow, Game of Thrones, and Walking Dead.

I write really rather sporadically, and can normally spell words, so I have huge periods of not writing anything and then a few months of posting loads. Apologies if you find my fics when I'm in a "down" period.

I love every review I receive and try to reply to them and want to thank everyone who adds me or a story as their favourites :-) Thank you! If you message me, I will reply and I like to hear from people :) I like to make new friends xx. Check out my website or twitter for the latest news on what I'm working on. Check out my polls whilst you're here, I'd love to hear from people :)

I don't like to post WIP, or at least not complete ones and I tend to always know where a story is heading to. I used to mainly write short, one shot scenes, but now I seem to be going more for the longer, chaptered plot filled stories. Because I seem to be working on quite a few things all at once at the moment, I've listed beneath what I'm working on. Although, I'm hoping to finish some things soon! For two chapters of More Than Mini that are not on here, please see here.

Update November 2017 - Well, now I've been back on the writing train for more than a year, maybe even 18 months. My muse hasn't been this cooperative in years! I'm still working away on the More Than Series - suckered more in to the Atlantis spin off than the original Stargate Sam/Jack pairing that inspired it all. I have the next More Than ready to edit, I have one of the Atlantis More Than's ready to edit, maybe 2 and have started writing the third. I have plans for two other Beth/Daryl Walking Dead fics along with a short rewrite of Beth's death that is ready to post. However, I am on pause from fan fiction as I have decided to do NaNo with my own original creation! Wish me luck! Hoping I'll be back on the fandom stuff in December.

After writing, I love to travel and visit cultural, historical sites. My honeymoon to the Nile is the inspiration to a HP idea that I *will* get around to writing. Greece is my next hopeful holiday. People describe me as slightly crazy, especially as I can randomly start scribbling down bits of dialogue or a random description of something. None of my friends or family have ever read anything I've written, but I have made some very good friends through fanfiction. Dukester, Daniela, Annie - love you xx.

My favourites: Which of my own stories are my favourites? My absolute favourite is Stargate: Yellow Roses. It is very close to my heart and I still love it almost ten years later. I wrote it when I hadn't quite gotten over things I mention in it, but I have now just as I wrote. Even if you're not a Stargate fan, please read it. And my current favourite HP fic is "First Tears" because I'm a bit odd and like it when I write something that makes me cry! I chose to write this one day at work during my breaks and realised it was a bit of a mistake because I was all teary and blotchy all day. I also quite like the beginning of HP: Stars in The Black Night Sky because it is generic and the end of MT5, which came to me on a very long flight! I will also add in a favourite of my first posted Arrow fic, Two Words, as it is very personal to me and explains some of my inactive period.
