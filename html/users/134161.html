The story if me:

Once upon a time there was a girl who loved to write. She wished desperately to write a story based on Harry Potter, but she was afraid. "I didn't come up with the characters! That's like copying!" she cried.

And then she met FanFiction.net and saw that everyone else was doing it. She's scheduled to jump off a bridge next tuesday at 3.

After writing a few fics she began to read and review others' as well. She came across a song parody and thought: "I could do that!" So she tried it. Baby Got Beard was born! And what is this? 10 reviews in one night?? Requests for another song? What magic is this! What draws so many LotR fans to parodies? She soon let this thought be, and only spent her time writing more.

A note from this strange girl called, "Me":

I love LotR, Harry Potter, Xena, Star Trek, Star Wars, and a whole bunch of other things that I can't think of right now. So if you think my parodies are OK, email me with a request! I'm always open to ideas. Just no NC-17 stuff. Well, MAYBE... only if it's a really good idea. But I won't post it.

Even if you don't want to request a song, email me! I love gabbing with other authors/scifi & fantasy fans. Or even just people in general! When you email, be sure to put "FanFiction" or "ff.net" or "Hi Katarina Freeman!" in the subject, or I may think it's junkmail and delete it.
