Greetings my fallen angels, wandering souls and the rare, black tainted wandering heart.

This is the court jester and scribe of the Halls of Something-or-Other in the Slash Wing of the Harry Potter Fandom Complex. I may be unseen, but I am put on a place that overlooks the fandom.

The gods are wanky. They amuse me and steer my writing down the path of sarcasm and snark.

I am doomed to remain single and stay in my Educational Tutoring forever, giving me a little bit more time for writing. It is a gift I treasure and find the burden to share with others.

Here I lay my works on the altar of the gods. Not the bitching ones.
