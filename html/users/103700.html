Okay. A more in-depth explanation for my extended hiatus...

Tl;dr, I'm not able to keep up with my fandoms anymore. I'm sorry to those disappointed by my starting up something only to abandon it, but it doesn't make sense for me to continually invest energy into projects that I am no longer passionate about. As a full time college student with financial issues, my free time needs to be devoted to things other than my writing.

If you're wondering if any of my stuff will be picked up in the future, the best I can do is give you a dissatisfying "Uh, maybe?". I'm too partial to my Jacob story, for example, to be okay with leaving it where it is. But I make no promises to when updates are going to start appearing again. In the mean time all I can do thank you guys for your support and understanding, truly. I've made some great friends through fandom, I love you all, and take care now. :)
