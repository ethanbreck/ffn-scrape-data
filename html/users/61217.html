I am a writer who has stories long overdue. Balancing college and life in general is difficult, but that's what makes the gift of writing, escaping into another world, all the more precious.

My fics mainly have a romantic/comedic focus, but I dabble in other genres as well. Gundam Wing is the mainstream content of my stories for now. I try to write and upload whenever I can and suggestions in reviews are welcome. Enjoy the fics!

November 12, 2007

Hello, to anyone who still visits me in hope that my fanfiction gets updated. ;-)

I've been busy since the beginning of this year; graduation, summer job, applying for full-time work, etc. But, I have been writing , here and there, when I can. It's been somewhat difficult to get back "into the groove", since some of my fanfics are over 4 years old. I've lost story notes for some, which means I'll have to do major revisions and brainstorming to get back my intended plots... @_@

Aside from that, I also have some other fics I've started randomly over the years, so I may put them up as well. Depending on what I can finish first. I'm really working myself on this, but I think it's worth it. Sort of a present for me, and my readers, to finish what I started. :-)

Edit: Dec. 18: Let me know what you think! Take the poll above=)
