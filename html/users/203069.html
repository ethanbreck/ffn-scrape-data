Author has written 10 stories for X-Men: Evolution, Yu Yu Hakusho, Gundam Wing/AC, and Yami no Matsuei.

Hey, everyone! I'm Cece, a married woman with a full-time job. When I'm not busy with married life, I love writing fanfiction, watching anime, and reading manga.

My fav anime/TV characters:

Gundam Wing - Quatre (sighs), Duo (wolf whistles), and Trowa (shrugs)

Yu Yu Hakusho - Kurama hands down b/c he's so cute! (hugs him)

Inuyasha - Koga (mainly), Inuyasha, and Miroku

X-Men: Evolution - Nightcrawler (Kurt's so cute! And he's blue!)

Transformers: Energon: Jetfire (I'm a Scott McNeil fan, okay?)

Yu-Gi-Oh! - Bakura (naturally, since he's so cute!)

Yami no Matsuei (Descendants of Darkness): Tsuzuki and Hisoka. Just because. :P

Fanfics I Am Working on and Want to Finish:

  1. Uprising - This is my first crossover with Yami no Matsuei and Yu Yu Hakusho. Both sides have to work together to stop the Seven Deadly Sins from destroying humanity. Will they succeed or lose it all? It's primarily a Supernatural/Adventure/Romance/Comedy/Psychological. Rated M for language, violence, and some sexual situations later on in the fic. Chapter 10/? up. Please give me a review. Mini-site up now!
  2. Regret - This is my VERY first from the Doctor Who franchise. I wrote 50K during NaNo 2013, and while I'm quite proud of it, I want to ask if you guys think I should post it. I know it's around the time of the Eleventh Doctor (Matt Smith) leaving, but "Regret" is about the Tenth Doctor and in his POV. I'm thinking of posting it after the holidays, since the fans need time to process Eleven's goodbyes.
  3. Gundam Wing: Gemini - I'm starting on a Gundam Wing kick, so I figure this would be the first one. A singer from the colonies arrives on Earth and stumbles onto two Gundam pilots. But what secrets does she hold? What does the future have in store? Chapters 5/?

Here are a few that I had been working on, yet I have stopped:

  1. When You Least Expect It -Chaos ensues within the Xavier Institute. Next chapter! On Hiatus due to working on other projects.
  2. Trial of Halves - the trilogy. After the attack by Karasu, Andrea thought it was all over... but it isn't. Will the truth come out, and how will it affect Kurama and Andrea? On hiatus

These are the ones I've finished:

  1. Anything, Anytime, Anywhere - During the Dark Tournament, Karasu wants to hurt Kurama... through a girl he cares about most. Kurama/OC pairing.
  2. Titanic and Tantei Don't Mix - A one-shot about what if the Tantei were to watch Titanic.
  3. The Trials of Life - (sequel of "Anything, Anytime, Anywhere.") After a year of being apart, both Andrea and Kurama have to make a decision. What will it be? Will she stay in Orlando or will she go back to the one she loves?
  4. Thank You, Mother - A one-shot about Kurama's POV of his mother. Sweet Mother's Day story.

Fanfics I Have Discontinued:

  1. Keikendan no Tantei - I decided to discontinue this because of school and other projects I am working in.
  2. Under the Crashing Waves - Discontinued as the plot didn't make sense for me anymore. So sorry for the people who read this. :(

Well, I've got to get back on the FF train here, so please RR my stories. I promise you I'll make mine worth your time.
