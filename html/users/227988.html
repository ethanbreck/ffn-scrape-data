//Biography//

Hi! You can call me Blu. You can consider me... young... hehe. Obviously I'm not very mature so you can't really count me as someone over 25 but hopefully I don't come off as 12 so let's just say I sit comfortably in the the middle.

I reside in the beautiful Southern California and I must, unfortunately, confess to the SoCal girl cliche minus the surfing,(Though I DO want to learn how to do so) and the perfectly fake blonde blue-eyed look. (Ha! Sorry!) But I'm not American. I just sortah live here. I was born on a bunch of tropical islands on the Pacific Ocean and arrived in the States when I was about eight-years-old.

Love to read and write fanfics... (As can be seen on my list of stories.)
Genres include:
-Doctor Who
-Orlando Bloom
-Star Wars
-Pirates of the Caribbean
-Troy

I love:
-reviews
-Bistro Shrimp Pasta from Cheesecake Factory
-my beloved friends
-the internet connected computer
-every genre of music save for techno... techno just makes me angry

ANYWAYS... Have ANY of you read this? Have I bored EVERYONE away? darn... ahh, well... Just remember:

There are several types of people in this world. One type would look at the glass and see it as half-empty, a sad pessimist's perspective. Another would look at the glass and see it as full, some happy optimistic outlook that would actually make the world a better place, but there's one other type of persepective. The person who has this perspective would look at the glass and go: "THIS is MY glass? This is NOT my glass! My glass was FULL and it was much hell of a lot bigger..."

To all readers past:

I know that my long absence is hardly tolerable but reality really had to take charge of life for a long time. I've received a few email about when I would ever return to the world of fanfiction and I want to answer that I do mean to return. I'm meaning to return right now. It's just immensely difficult when you can't write when you want to. (and trust me in this, I'm trying to write at this very moment. I'm reading my old stuff and I'm wondering how I managed to lift this damn writer's monolith...)

But I will return... I just need to get my plot bunnies going again.
