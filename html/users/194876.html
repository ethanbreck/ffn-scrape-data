"I prefer being call a treasure hunter." Hehehe...that is from Locke from Final Fantasy III.

Hmmm...how can I start this off. First of all, I will like to say that "Revelation of Destiny: Goddess War" will be put on hold right now. I want to finish "Humanity" first. Second of all, I will not be writing a continuation of "A Heart That is Cold." I like Miyuand Larva but I cannot come up with another story that include the both of them. Until then, I will have to say that is the end of that story. Thank you for enjoying that story as much as I have fun writing that story.

I will finish all the story I have started. I just need time. I am on the verge of finally getting my credential and starting my career. I have been busy for these past few years trying to get all of my things in order and getting a good grade in all of my classes. Thiscoming semester (Spring 2006) is especially crucial to me. So...I can't gurantee there will be an update in between now to the late June. I am sorry for the delay, but I hope you understand my condition. I have made it my goal to devote at least one hour of my time each day to my story. So...it might be up there sooner than I expect.

As for review, I made it not an obligation to ask for review. I do not want to force my readers to leave a review if they do not want to. It is already an honor to me that people are actually reading mystory. Lol. I welcome constructive criticism...but I don't welcome criticism that contains cussing or badmouthing without any supporting evidence that relates to my writing. Constructive criticism helps me become a better writer, and I know there are a lot of you that are helping me by pointing out that I need to improve on my grammar (lol).

Not to be long-winded but I treat review as a way for me to know what questions you have about the story (so I can cover those questions and explain them clearly) and what you thinks I need to improve. They also works as a medium in which I can communicate with my readers (besides email and pm) and know what you enjoy and what you don't like. I thank everyone for reading my story and taking time to leave a review. Thank you.

Finally, please feel free to email me at digi_03@yahoo.com(actually, thiswill be an even quicker way to reach meif you have any questionsrather than leaving your questions in the review).I thank everyone for taking the time to read my profile, and thanks for reading my stories. Take care.
