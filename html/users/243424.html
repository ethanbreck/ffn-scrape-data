All of my stuff is unbated, and I post as soon as I'm done writing. There will be typos, misspellings, and possibly wrong words. Unless you're offering to beta for me (which, honestly, I would love. I know I need one), please try and ignore them. I'll get around to editing eventually. I think.

For reference: I write lots of OCs, and I break canon for the fun of it. I don't care if you don't like it, because let's be honest, I'm not writing this for you (unless I'm writing a giftfic, but that's different.). I'm writing it because I enjoy writing it, and I'm posting it because someone else might like it, or might be inspired by it. I don't depend on reviews, favorites, or follows for my personal well-being. Believe me, I'm quite used to screaming into the void.

Rule of thumb for reviews: Is it constructive? Is it polite? Does it need to be said by me? Did the author express an interest in con-crit? If the answers are no, and you did not actually enjoy the fic? There's a back button at the upper left, and an x in the upper right corner of your browser.

Also, a blanket thanks to everyone who DOES review thoughtfully/fave/follow. You're awesome, and I'm glad you're having fun with my nutty worlds.
