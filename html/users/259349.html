Name is Do Re Me 123

Salazar's Encore Update:

It's been quite a few years since I've updated any of my fics! Life just got in the way, a lot has changed for me but honestly these stories often popped up in my head over the years! And I've actually tired a few times to sit down and write new chapters. It was too difficult though. However this winter I've had more time than ever to consider what I need to do to get back into writing. I'm starting from the bottom up! I'm rereading my stories and editing along the way (man, grammar is annoying!) It's helping me get acquainted with my own characters and to remind myself what I was going for when I first started writing Corvus's story.

So hopefully in the next couple of days I can get some new material posted for everyone who has been waiting! I love these characters and want to finish what I started!

Current Projects:

A friend is taken from him after she falls from grace in the pureblood society, it turns out that she is a half-blood. Regulus has to struggle between doing what is expected from him and doing what his heart desires. And there's his brother, who's always messing up his day... RB/oc/SB

Salazar's Salesmen: The Slytherin, Corvus Dante Blackstone runs a lucrative business smuggling in and selling contraband within Hogwarts. For him and his friends it's about making money and having fun. Then the Chamber of Secret is opened, and his group's on the verge of collapsing onto itself. But Slytherin's Heir is nothing compared to what Corvus has to deal with when the infamous Sirius Black escapes Azkaban... (Some Things Are Regrettable is the prequel to this story)

Salazar's Encore: Sequel to Salazar's Salesmen. After a turbulent fourth year of scandalous revelations, Corvus Black returns to Hogwarts. Running the infamous group Salazar's Salesmen and avoiding Potter like the plague, he hardly notices something very sinister developing around him... set in GoF-OotP.

Future Projects:
