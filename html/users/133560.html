What is beauty?
It is a mile range of bloodred roses
A solitude beneath the sunset
The love between two lovers

But beauty is sometimes
Not what you see, but what is inside you
One may not know,
but even a bullfrog has a certain beauty

A woman,
Admiring herself in a mirror
Though the image is not what she seeks
She is beautiful

A man,
Who is above all aesthetes
Appreciates not only a woman's soft curve
but the artistic ability she has within her mind

But what is beauty?
Beauty is a child
Blooming through their years
Adapting in new places
Creations the world is proud of

Beauty is a child
Watching a balloon float through gravity
Defying its odds
Retiring into the stars

Beauty is what makes the universe
Though outside beauty fools many
Inner beauty fools all.
