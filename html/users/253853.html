Well, at least the good thing is that I don't have that many fans, haha. ahem But I will be gettingA Sorta Fairytale finished. It's all in my head. It's just a matter of getting it out of there. I've officially given up on that. I can't even remember where I was originally planning to go with that. If it ever comes back to me, and if I find the time, then I'll continue it.
