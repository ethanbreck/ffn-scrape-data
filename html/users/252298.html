Do people even read these things? I mean what could i possibly write in here that will enrich all of your lives and make me feel like a better person?

I guess I could give you a rough idea of who sits behind this screen but does anyone even care? oh well I'll do it anyway just cause i can and there's nothing you can do about it... hehe...

I belong to the better gender meaning I'm female which means I get all the perks that come with that little fact like natural intuition, I can fold road maps, my place doesn't smell like old cheese and cat piss... I could go on but I can't be bothered stating all the lil facts which makes us better. Which is unusual. Lol... I'm joking relax boys, I appreciate your presence... most of the time.

I was born in Italy and lived there the greater part of my life but I'm currently studying in NZ otherwise lovingly known among me and my friends as the forgotten land. That's because 1 out of 3 maps don't even acknowledge it's presence.

Hmm.. what else.. I have a homicidal cat that has something against my ankles and is convinced she's a panther and queen of the jungle. I have a dog which pops the cat's bubble and destroys her dignity by licking her from head to toe. She doesn't mind when there's birds chasing her however.

I also have a healthy obsession with Dark Angel which goes beyond psychiatric help and i love making people laugh.. unfortunately for me usually at the expense of my dignity. But i don't mind.. anything for laughter.. brings people together and makes the world go round.
