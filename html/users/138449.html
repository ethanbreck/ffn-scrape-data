Oh. First of all, I have to say this:
I am in LOOOOVE with Sean Biggerstaff ;) But I like Daniel Radcliffe more. Hehz.

Back to Sean. I'm a big fan of his. I became a big fan after I watched the Harry Potter movie though. Sean as Oliver Wood was just soo...wonderful. Hah. *Obsessed* Hehz. Anyway. I've started to like Oliver Wood alot more after that, and my fics would usually be about him. If not, it would be about Ron, Harry and Hermione, you know, the usual characters. My favourite pairings are Oliver Wood + Cho Chang and Ron Weasley + Hermione Granger. I believe that Draco belongs to Harry, but Harry does not belong to Draco. Hm. Did that make sense?

I do dabble with other pairs like Draco/Hermione and Harry/Cho, but the latter one will be less because I don't really approve of that pair ^^;

Well. Apart from Harry Potter, I am also crazy about the Lord of the Rings. AND Elijah Wood. AND Orlando Bloom. *Swoons* I can't really write Lord Of the Rings fics because Tolkien writes so well, it would be hard to imitate his style.

My favourite authors list is carefully chosen, and I believe...REALLY believe that they are the greatest authors in fanfiction history. They're the best. And yes, Cassandra Claire rules too. I tell you, she's as good as, if not better than, JK Rowling herself. Cassie and Rhysenn, you have gotten yourself a new fan.

Hehz. Hope you enjoy my fics though ;)
