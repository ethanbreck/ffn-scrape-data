About the Author (ME!):

My real name is... well... maybe THAT can be a secret. I am 21 years old though. When I'm not busy being a single mom, I'm reading or writing. I've been obsessed with fanfictions since I was in the 2nd grade. And no, I'm not kidding. My friend and I used to write Zelda fanfictions before we even knew what fanfictions were. Of course, they sucked, so that's why they're not posted. lol. Now that I'm older, and have survived a number of English-Grammer classes, I can take pride in some things I write. :-)

I own a 1986 Jeep Wagoneer, an australian shephard named Sydney, and my very own boyfriend. Yes... everyone "oo" and "ah".

I drink Coke... LOTS of it. You might say I'm a Coke-addict... but don't. Because that could get me in trouble. lol.

My Stories

I finally went against my better judgement and started writing a Harry Potter fanfiction. I was perfectly content with Rowling's work until the very end. She left it wide open for her fans to continue the story how they felt it should. So, I'm filling in the blank. I wanted to center this story on Harry's friends rather than on him completly. I thought the poor guy needed a break. :-) Don't worry though, he will be in it. ( UPDATE: Sorry for not posting. Inspiration was lost on me. Perhaps it will be picked up again when I see the new film. I sure as hell hope so. Maybe I need to read the last book again. Eh...)

My Pirates story is now (finally) complete. I doubt there will be a sequal. I am, however, considering writing more Pirates stories. Maybe oneshots? I don't know, I have too many ideas, and none of them seem good or fun enough to write. Maybe if you all have ideas, you can let me know? Cool.

My X-men stories are kind of dead at the moment. I got faced with a bunch of drama and...well...life. SO I dropped out of my X-men obssession. Hopefully, I'll find the time to pick back up in that area and finish my story, or start over with it or something?

I am now working on a Bleach fanfiction. I never thought I would, but then this whole Ulquiorra/Orihime thing started happening. I was totally cool with her and Ichigo, but I saw a fanart of the couple that was BEAUTIFUL and I started thinking... yeah... that could work. Above is the status of my new chapter for my story. Sometimes I say I'll have a chapter out and it isn't out... if you want to know where it is in progress, just check on here. So no more stressing. :)
