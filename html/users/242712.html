Yugi-game, play, enjoy

Ou-king

Boku-I (to refer to ones self) male only

Watashi- I (to refer to ones self) all gender polite (you wouldn't hear Yami or Bakura use this word)

Anata, kimi, kare- you(polite)

Kisama, anta, omae-you(impolite)

Hitori- one(another) person

Aibou-partner

Bonkotsu-common person(very rood) Kaiba often refers to Jounochi with this word

Mou hitori no boku-the other me

Mou hitori no Yugi- the other Yugi (usually what Yugi's friends call Yami by the way NO ONE on the show calls him YAMI Kaiba calls him Yugi and Anzu, Jounochi, Honda, ect. call him Mou hitori no Yugi or just Yugi i know its kind confusing.)

Onisan- brother depending on the end of the word can change the value of the word for exsample Mokuba WORSHIPS Kaiba so he changes the word to Onisama with adds great respect. On Dragon Ball Z Goten would call Gohan onichan which is a cuter childish way to say brother with more of a cute love towards an older brother. Onisan is just a formal way of saying it. It is like the difference between mother mom and mommy almost

Okasan-mother, same as brother the value can be changed with the use of sama or chan in place of san
