18.08.04 - 12:01

I have been gone for a looong time. And I realized tonight (this morning) that I really do miss it here. sigh I've been slacking a lot in the fanfiction department, but unfortunately the past few years haven't been very inspirational for me as a whole. Then suddenly, I fell in love, suddenly realized how hot a lot of people around me really are, understood what best friendship was really about, and experienced the satisfaction in becoming the master of Amplitude. The latter really has no relevance, but damn that gave is addictive and feels damn sweet to beat. Anyway, when this all happened, I was filled with the familiar warmth of creativity again. My Muse came back from her long vacation and I can suddenly write again. I'm finally in touch with her range, and my many months of writing half assed fluff are over. sigh A huge thanks to you all that are still with me, I love you, and I apologize for letting you down. But I assure you I'm alive and still kicking. My first fanfiction for my new inspiration, Criers, is my kickoff. All my love, xaphanea P.S. If you'd like to see what I'd taken solace in when I couldn't write anything worth shit, visit my gallery. I'm quite proud of it, and I intend on keeping it updated as well.

There are only three things that matter in this world: Cars, sex, and the wonderful world of fiction.
