I began my career in fanfiction writing in anime fandoms in Ranma 1/2, many years ago. I later moved to Gundam Wing, and discovered yaoi fanfic there; after several years in that fandom I drifted away, and spent years in the Fullmetal Alchemist fandom before moving into Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicle. Following that was spent several years in the Marvel Cinematic Universe fandom, and lately I've been moving into Dragon Age.

I am a yaoi author primarily; my writing fanfics are primarily Heero/Duo, Roy/Ed, Kurogane/Fai and Hawke/Anders, although I have written for many, many different pairings, including het and femmeslash pairings. Most of my pairing-centric work will be severely edited due to content restrictions and some will not be displayed on this site at all. Fortunately, I have also done a large body of non-pairing work, in genres ranging from humor to horror to adventure. Hopefully, you will be able to find something on this page that you will enjoy.
