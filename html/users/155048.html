So anyway, Now I am 'Neata Skeeter'. (I'd prefer to be a Snape mmmm Sevvy or Malfoy mmmm Lucius mind you...but that doesn't have the same ring to it)

Now to the basics of me: I'm an Aussie gal.

When it comes to reading I'm addicted to a)- Fanfiction! (dah!) and b)- Harry Potter. Of course I analyse the books way too much, and have rather fallen in love with the bad guys. NOT a fan of the dream team (the three goodie goodies that manage to do all sorts of shit and get away with it) one bit. With regards to mroe 'traditional' texts, I quite enjoyed Homer's 'The Odyssey', Chaucer's 'The Canterbury Tales', Bram Stoker's 'Dracula' and I LUUUUURVE Shakespeare. Well, almost everything that Shakespeare has written. I DESPISE 'Romeo and Juliet'.

On tv I loved watching AtS, Btvs, WLIIA (both the old and new series) and all sorts of stuff. My favourite show of the minute is Little Britain. Those boys are brilliant.

My hobbies include all sorts of things...ummm... shopping (yeah i know, it's not really a hobby, but to me it is), reading, playing basketball and soccer (not at the same time...but u knew that _), spending time with my friends and family and writing (and getting feedback...'cos that's the best part).

If you read my stuff please tell me what u think. You wanna know more? Ummm... well...i'm not really that interesting. I'm a little insane but that's to be expected.

I actually have another account here on FFN, as I had dramas trying to log into this one, and it's taken me over 2 years to get it back. I'm just so glad my fics are still here. LOL. So, if you want to read my Harry Potter works (which are far more recent), please go find Molvanian Queen_In_Exile. I've actually added myself as one of my favourite authors to make the link easier to find. I am not vain, it's just far easier to access this way.

And now, I'll shut up. Grins
