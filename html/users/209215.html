this profile was updated sept 2008!

hahaha i don't write. i should but i'm not creative or knowledgable enough. i personally believe fanfics should be believable. even if facts are changed. they should be adjusted properly. following the bleach and naruto mangas closely as long as some others.. but i'm a naruto fanfic reader! hehe. i love sakura and kakashi pairings b/c they're the most believable, in all honestly. and least dramatic. i've found that people who write kakashi and sakura fics take more effort to making a believable story. i hate when they flame a character though. like all the fics taht make sasuke a jerk. .:sigh:. those are too obviously biased in my opinion. when reading sasuke and sakura pairings, there's so much strife its not worth it. and plus, the good fanfics should be when they're older and more mature. i'm dying to find a good naruto and sakura fic. lately been reading some itachi and sakura fics. they're interesting! i dont like the naruto and hinata pairings though. they're not interesting!!

also a big cardcaptors sakura fanfic fan. eriol and tomoyo pairings rock!!
