Okay, I'm redoing my bio because it was stupid and boring. I'm going to make it more bio-ish.

So, yeah. I'm 15 and I live in the Untied States. But don't worry, it's not for long. I'm moving to France as soon as I get out of school. Why France, you ask? Because it's cool. So neyah. I live in what is most assuredly the most boring state. Which happens to be . . . (drumroll please) . . . solid! (Get it, state, solid, you know, solid liquid gas, ya know? Okay sorry.) No, although I am quite solid (thankfully) I live in Michigan. I know what you're thinking - "Who lives in Michigan?" Yeah, pretty much me and the other 2 people in my city. Can you see why I want to leave?

1.) Harry Potter

That just goes to show how dangerous it can be when you leave healthy people in places like Michigan for too long. And for a full listing of authors and stories I worship, see my favorites. I love to stump people with that one.

Just so you all know, the main purpose of this account is so that I have a list of stories that I can check to see if any of my favorites update. So don't expect me to update often. I only do so when A. I'm inspired (yeah, like that happens all the time) or B. Someone leaves a kick-ass review and I feel bad whhile looking at the date I last posted (not that B's very common either).

1.) Buffy the Vampire Slayer (I don't dislike her, but I don't like her. Just thought you'd want to know.)
2.) People named Arwen

That's pretty much it. I'm not to picky in general (hey, I live in Michigan! You can't be picky and live in Michigan!)(Okay, enough with the Michigan bashing for any of you fellow Michiganders out there.)
