Looking at the date I joined, I realize that the only thing I've done for more years than write fanfiction in my life is study. It feels a little bit amazing, actually.

I'm almost BL exclusive when it comes to fanfiction, so I don't write anything romantic that isn't BL. Het and GL are fine and dandy, but not my area of interest, so you might get side pairings/ships of either variety, but relationships between men are always going to be the main focus of my work.

While I'm more prolific lately than I used to be, I'm still often very busy, so I still don't have as much time as I'd like to write. Every year, I tell myself, "This is the year I do NaNoWriMo," but then November comes, and I'm swamped. As such, please be patient with me when it comes to updates. I try to update once a month, but due to writer's block or too much work, this doesn't always work out.

Reviews are easily the most effective way to get a timely update out of me because they remind me that someone is out there waiting for me to write something, and it is extremely encouraging that someone would take the time and effort to either praise or criticize any aspect of my work. Whether you like or dislike some part of what I've written, please let me know! We might not agree, but I'd love to hear your thoughts, and I truly appreciate honesty.

I'm also open to discussing the choices I make about my writing and the reasoning behind them with regards to either the work it is based on or the story itself. So if you have some burning question about something in a fic, leave a signed review. If a review sounds like it warrants a response, it will be responded to.

About abandoned stories (I know, I'm sorry), there are 2 reasons why I would ever stop writing a story:

1) I have REALLY lost touch with the fandom and its characters. You probably wouldn't like anything I wrote in that state anyway.
2) Upon reconsideration of the story, it's better off ending where it does, and adding to it only detracts from the quality of the existing work.

Reason 1 = Discontinued indefinitely. Once in a blue moon, I revisit an old fandom, and then maybe I pick up an old fic. This is incredibly RARE.
Reason 2 = Complete. I'll change the status label, so you don't have to keep waiting.
