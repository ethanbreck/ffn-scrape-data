Author has written 15 stories for Red Dwarf, Enid Blyton, Press Gang, Life on Mars, Bridget Jones' Diary, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.

I started writing fan fiction as a kid back in the dim dark days before the Internet, based on shows like the Famous Five and Press Gang.

My fan fic philosophy is to try and write something as the original authors would have. So you'll find no random slash, X-rated canon or language stronger than the original here.

I like to try and capture the original tone of the dialogue so let me know if you think I'm doing it to your satisfaction. I also like to use correct spelling and punctuation and for others to do the same.

One day, I'd like to write a well-received fic for something with a huge rabid fandom base. I've played it safe so far in fairly niche areas so to attack something like Doctor Who will be my biggest challenge. One day.
