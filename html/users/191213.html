And now back to your previously scheduled bio (now with more filling!).

I've got lots of ideas swimming around; far too many, in fact. I am constantly thinking about ways I could expand on my favorite fiction universes, particularly when I am watching, reading or playing them. Sometimes, my fanfiction daydreams even distract me from actually paying attention to the story, to my annoyance. And to my further annoyance, I can't bring myself to actually sit down and write most of these ideas.

Why is that? Well, sometimes it's because the idea in question sucks, but mostly it's because I'm afraid of ruining the ones I like. I want to be slow and careful; I practically have a phobia of starting a project without proper backstory. One-shots scare me (when I write them, anyway.)

And when I'm that determined to do something right, I usually end up not doing it at all. :P It's pathetic, really.

--
So, anyway, read my one story at your own risk; it's old, and a one-shot, and a prime example of why I'm dangerous when I don't plan ahead. :P And sorry about the "I'm no good at summaries" stuff...I was ignorant and lazy then. Er, moreso than now. Really.
