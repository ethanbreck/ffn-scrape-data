Author has written 16 stories for My Little Pony, Charmed, Harry Potter, X-overs, House, M.D., and Sixth Sense.

I've been writing stories since about grade one or two. I've still got a few of them and they're cute to look back at. I wrote my first series in grade three about some kind of slug/alien thing. I think I've somewhat improved my writing skills since then though. I enjoy writing FanFictions, but I hope to have some original ideas of my own one day. I'd love to be the author of the next Harry Potter or Twilight, but I'm still waiting for inspiration to strike. For now, I'm enjoying taking characters that have already been developed by others and putting them into my own stories. I try to update my stories as often as possible and I always finish them. I hope you enjoy reading them and I appreciate any feedback or constructive criticism that will help me to improve my writing skills. Happy reading!
