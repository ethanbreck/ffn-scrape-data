Author has written 24 stories for Witchblade, and Tribe.

I used to like to make these "deep" and "eccentric" but that is not me, but rather a 14 year old trying to look deep and eccentric. I'm much more of an odd and snarky kind of person. The kind that drives you crazy at the movies because while the comments I make are funny, you would also prefer to watch the movie without noise. I'm all about the noise.

I like science fiction, post-apocalyptic stuff. I love "1984" and "Brave New World". I also love fantasy and mythology, the idea of the magic in the corners of our mundane world. You can see this in the comic books I read, stuff like "Fables", "Grimm Fairy Tales", and Gaiman's Sandman and its offsets.

I like writing characters who make me laugh and who become so real I can argue their plot points with them. I haven't updated in forever but will hopefully be changing that soon.
