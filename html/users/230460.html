About Me

I've been around this site for a loooong time. I left for a few years then came back again to read my old favorite stories and to filter through the new ones so I can have a new collection of Favorite Stories. :)

Even though I know I'm super busy with school now and should be studying, this site is so addicting that it's hard just to stop. Coming back reminded me just how much I loved Cardcaptor Sakura and, of course, the main characters. They're all so adorable and funny and just so hard not to love.

I don't write. It's too hard for me lol. Even if I tried to write something, I already know that it's going to be really cliche and horrible! Haha. I'm just happy that people on FF.N can write, can come up with something original, and can make wonderful stories for their readers to enjoy. I do know what I like and dislike when I read, so I try to leave reviews to support the story and the author.

I definitely recommend reading the stories in my Favorite Stories. They're awesome!
