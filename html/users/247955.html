Other Crap I Know People Don't Really Read

Peeves: People who have the capacity to be smart but just are not. Racists. Homophobes. Lack of common sense. Shopping for clothes.

Likes: Character design. Sarcasm. Hanging with my friends. Sleeping. Incubus (you'll notice a lot of their lyrics in my future fics).

Persona: Generally cool and laid back, but I can be a biyotch at times (mostly when dealing with stupid people, thank god I'm outta high school). Perverted, sorry folks. Sarcastic, in my nature too. Extrovert and a talker. I realize a lot of my faults, but it's ingrain, I can't help it sometimes.

My Writings

Right, in case you haven't noticed I'm an AU and Songficwriter. I like to create worlds for myself (AU) and I personally think everything in the world has a song to go with it, like a realistic musical (Songfics). A lot of the happenings in my fics are based off of conversations I've had with my friends (or minions), so if it's funny it either happened in my life or I discussed the possibility of it happening. Don't like my style, get bent and read something you enjoy. If you do like my style, don't get bent and enjoy. Leave a review as well, I like nice comments or creative critics.

Anon. Reviewers

As much as I love hearing from everyone, I hate people who decide to flame and use their anonymity to do so. I only allow signed reviews now. I know it will cut down my review responses, but that is a risk I am willing to take because then I can report anyone abusing me.

Stories

Sorry, but for now I'm taking a rather long hiatus in my writing. School has to come first for me. I might, or might not finish my WIPs. I'm a visual artist before I'm a writer.

I'll keep my completed works up, but I will be removing my WIPs. Thank you for the support throughout the years, but I have real life priorities like a career and future.
