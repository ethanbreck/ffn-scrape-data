Author has written 10 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Harry Potter, and Weiss Kreuz.
I'm a 26-year-old Welsh American living in Portland, Oregon and I manage a crisis-respite facility for the mentally ill (say that three times fast!) I'm also married to two wonderful men (no it's not legal, and no, it's not an open marriage), who think it's just great that their wife is a terrible pervert.

The two fandoms I write in regularly are DBZ and Harry Potter. I also have one Weiss Kreuz story. Currently, I'm writing only slash fiction, though I have plans to do some non-slash focused stuff. I'm a terrible romantic at heart, but I still can't bring myself to write many sweet fics in which people are not either damaged goods or screwing like minks.

For the Harry Potter universe, I'm a Remus/Severus shipper, and my main body of work is the Obsession series, which is, chronologically: Obsession, Restless Spirits, Murder of Love, What Would I Give for Just One of Your Smiles, and Wolfsbane. Another story in the series, Uncommon Perversions, is pretty hardcore and not really appropriate for this site. It is about Snape's time as a Deatheater and can be found here, if you're so inclined:

The other HP stories are: We Need to Talk..., a humorous bit of fluff written for the Snape Fuh-Q Fest ( and Temptation, which is a bit of Neville/Snape also written for the Fest. Neither of these take place in the Obsession universe. The last is The Wager, which is sorta AU, and also not a part of the Obsession series, but still Remus/Severus.

Confusion is a WIP as is Wolfsbane. Lovers in Flames may be, I don't know yet.

Lastly, I'd like to thank the kind folks who have left reviews and encouragement, both here and in e-mail. That, along with some of the great stuff here at ff.net, has focused me more on my writing, and on cranking more stuff out when I snatch the time.
