Joined 12-02-01, id: 133915, Profile Updated: 11-23-17
Author has written 13 stories for Pokémon, Invader Zim, Movie X-overs, Space Ghost, Freefonix, and Redakai: Conquer the Kairu.

I've always loved writing stories and although I don't have so much time any more, since I've gone into drawing alot more, I've written two published novels and I'm working on my third one and two long fan fictions. My first two fanfics I wrote a while ago are Danté's Dark Side, a Mewtwo fic and The Fist Conquers All (unfinished) a Zim fan fic. Don't look for any new chapter coming however though. I read it a while ago and just realised how good it was and how I should finish it. But I doubt I will, since I've lost interest. I have written two books and so have no time to write fan fics at the moment since a sequel is in the works!

My books are called Danger in the Darkness and The Search for the SilvaGryphie and you can buy them on Amazon, Lulu and other online bookstores!

I used to be known here as AvistA, the name of one of my characters (she appears in three stories here but I'd like it to be known that Avvy's Night of Action, Road Trip and Ship Wrecked have a different version of her, MY own personal version which I made up myself - NOT a Mewtwo!) but now I'm known as Silvolf, this is my net name, I'm sure you'll have seen me round the net on deviantART or something.

I have an IZ short, Zimmy Pigs. It's really funny so check it out. There aren't enough stories with guinea pigs in them! There is also a Gorillaz fanfic I've written, its a short one but funny and freaky at the same time. Gorillaz are one of my favourite bands. I've also put up half of my personal fanfic about Zorak from Space Ghost Coast to Coast (among other shows). Only the first half though, the second has content which as far as I'm aware, isn't allowed here any more. Besides which I wrote it for myself and it would be embarrassing for others to read it. The main reason I uploaded it is because there aren't enough Zorak fics out there, the few that there are are so old and the best of them hasn't been finished. I've also uploaded a short 5 page fic about Zorak and Lokar's final fight. Lokar fans probably won't like it, but its a good read anyway.
Road Trip is another Zorak/AvistA fic. I felt randomly inspired for it so I wrote it. It's 16 pages long, one of my longer short fics.
Ship Wrecked is the sequel to Road Trip, another ZovistA one. There is room for a third fic since I left it open to carry on, I don't know if I'll continue it though.
Metal Works is a Freefonix fanfic. Freefonix is a little known show that's been on TV in the UK, Australia and France. It focuses on my fan character for the show, Razz, and how she fits into the story. I'm very proud of that fic. Its up there with my fave fanfics I've written.
Another fan character focused story I wrote for fun is Spirit of Radikor (I have no idea why I called it that actually) and is a short backstory for my OC, Silv. The actual fic is 3 pages longer than what is posted here. It's been edited down because of content. Not much of an epic storyline and you'll probably only understand what's going on if you're a fan of the show.

I'm working on an X-Men: Evolution fanfic as well but currently the progress is slow since it's very involved and I need to be in the right frame of mind to write it. Those who have read it so far say it's good though!

I'm also working on an X-Men/Guardians of the Galaxy crossover fic that I may or may not post here (probably not, there is a lot of sex in it) about my fan character, Formshifter/Sasha in the Guardians universe living on Yondu's ship as his co-captain and lover. This fic is pretty long, the longest fanfiction I've written to date and I'll probably get a copy printed for my own personal use once it's done. My husband and I did a TON of notes on it and I figure it would be a shame to waste all those ideas which is why I'm working on it in between publishable projects.

I write fanfics as and when I choose and most are short now, since I'm trying to get my own stories up and running with my own characters it seems pointless to put all my energy into writing long ones about other people's characters. Short fics are fun and refreshing though!
I hope you enjoy reading my fics, I'm proud of all of them!

Constructive comments on my fics are always welcome.

Also, check out these great fics that people have written featuring my OCs! I'm truly honoured that my OCs are worthy enough to be written into other fanfics and that people like them enough to keep the pairings of them with the canon characters.

A War for Kairu by SoupSofia has my character Silv in later chapters.

Freefonix Full Band by Song of Hope features Razz in a slightly alternative universe where she is evil and powerful.
