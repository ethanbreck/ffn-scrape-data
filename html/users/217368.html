Author has written 1 story for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.

I decided to write a fanfiction about Buffy because:

1) I'm a die-hard Spuffy fan and I wished that the two of them had gotten a better ending

2) I want to write an original story someday, but I want to build myself up to that by practicing on characters that everyone already loves.

I really enjoy writing. I try my hardest to be detailed and give unique perspectives and creative twists to the stories I create. Right now, my biggest challenge as a writer is improving my proofreading skills. I hope to gradually improve on my own, but for the sake of my current readers, if anyone would be willing to beta, I'd love to have your help.
