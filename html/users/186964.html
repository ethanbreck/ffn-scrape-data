Ladies and gentleman, boys and girls, welcome, welcome, to the Kingdom of AnnaMel.

She is a Brazilian girl who is much more of a reader than a writer. Her favourite fandom is Harry Potter, closely followed by Twilight. Harry/Ginny, Ron/Hermione, Remus/Tonks and Teddy/Victoire are her ships in the HP fandom, and Edward/Bella, Jasper/Alice, Carlisle/Esme and Emmett/Rosalie in the Twilight fandom. She is a bit of a canon-nazi. She loves Lost, but doesn't read fanfics about it. She is a bit weird, but she likes being like that. She is stubborn. She is currently in love with Robert Pattinson. She dreams about meeting JK Rowling, her favourite author. She loves talking about herself in the third person.

Oh, and by the way, Teddy Lupin is a Hufflepuff, just like her, thank you for asking! XD
