Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter.

About me: Male, married, live in Florida. Anything else you want to know about me that isn't here, send me a PM and ask respectfully.

What I read and like: I like stories with lots of details and plot. I'll read almost any pairing if the author can make it believable.

I don't like graphic smut and/or depictions of violence. There's quite enough of it in the real world, and I don't choose to read it for entertainment.

I strongly prefer stories with proper English (American OR British) spelling and grammar. I'm not fluent enough in any other languages to criticize. I'll overlook the mechanics if the story is good enough.

My current pet peeve is lack of internal consistency. If you say that Harry is really a pureblood in chapter one, and that purebloods can't be forced to testify under veritaserum in chapter two, you better not have Harry being forced to testify under veritaserum in chapter twelve unless you've set up a good reason for the exception to the law.

With regards to my own stories:

1 - Invictus is complete in 12 chapters. I may eventually write / post a sequel, but that's not high on my priority list.

2- Catspaws is (finally) complete in 20 chapters. I'm not planning a sequel for this one.

3 - If you want to translate any of my stories, please feel free. I do ask that you send me a link to the translation once you post it.

4 - Most of my HP fanfic is unashamedly AU. I didn't like the last three books in the HP series, and tend to ignore them.

5 - No idea what I'll work on next. Send me a PM and let me know what you'd like.
