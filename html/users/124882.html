Its seems I have gotten an urgent plea to update my one story here. First off, my apologies for leaving it fallow for so very long. I did try to continue once I finished the second chapter, but I simply could not come up with anything. In the end, various other creative desires and stuff to do (they are legion, but unfortunately the ones I finish are not :( ) pushed it to the side.

However, I do hate to leave things unfinished, and I would like to continue this, but there is yet a legion of things to do (first and foremost, my duties and tasks in pursuit of my masters degree.) I really don't have time to struggle with making a plot work. To be honest, I struggled for so very long to find a unique angle in the already excellent body of work of K/18 fanfiction until I had, well, an epiphany. I quite frankly will need another, a story that just HAS TO GET OUT, in order to be able to continue. It's been so long since I've been a DBZ fan, the embers that were once hot have grown rather cold, but I will give my old work another read, let it rattle in my head. But in order to make something work, I think I'll need all of your help.

So, I ask this of all of you who traverse here. If you enjoy my story, and want to see it done(or at least expanded). Please pray that an idea strikes and I am inspired to write, so I can finish something you enjoy.

God Bless

PegasusJF
