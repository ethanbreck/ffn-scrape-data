Author has written 8 stories for X-Men: The Movie.

Once upon a time, Jenny11 was a college student. She was a voracious reader of X-Men comic books, a dedicated fan of the X-Men animated series (all of them), and a scrupulous critic of the X-Men movies. Jenny11’s passion for X-Men grew and grew until it exploded from her imagination in a flurry of fan fictions. She is pleased to say that she continues to receive positive reinforcement from these fan fictions even today (nearly 10 years later!).

After graduation college, Jenny11 underwent a dramatic transformation. She traded in her beloved comic book mutants for vampire, werewolves, dark fey, vukodlaks, lisitsas, and skinwalkers. The fan fiction author Jenny11 had become . . . Jennifer Blackstream, author of paranormal erotica and paranormal romance.

I realize that most of you are here on fanfiction.net because of the fandoms, not the authors themselves. However, for those of you would like to read more of my writing even though it is not X-men-related, I encourage you to go to www.jenniferblackstream.com. If you use the contact form to join my mailing list, just mention that you know me from fanfiction.net and I’ll send you a free copy of whichever of my ebooks catch your fancy :)

- Jennifer Blackstream (aka Jenny11)
