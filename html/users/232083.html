UPDATED: June 8th 2016 - Wow, it's been years since I've done anything one here. Years that have included multiple moves, growing in my marriage, three beautiful children, and now a tiny farm with pigs, goats, chickens, rabbits, and one nutty dog. I haven't written much of anything in so long that I feel rusty. Maybe it's time to dig out the old files and attempt some sort of updates...

I am just a loyal fan to several shows who wants to write things the way they SHOULD happen. I am unhappy with some of the character interactions, relationships (or lack thereof), and storylines. These are my stories of love, romance, humor, teasing, and just plain utter stupidness and silliness. I also sometimes co-author with my friend (pen name: RoughIslandSunrise) under the pen name BelovedSunrise. Thank you for reading my stories, and please let me know what you think of them! Also -- my favorite shows (so far) are Stargate: SG-1, Stargate: Atantis, NCIS, JAG, House, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Scrubs, Law and Order: Special Victims Unit, Walker Texas Ranger, The X-Files, Witchblade, Star Trek: Voyager, Star Trek: The Next Generation, and Touched By An Angel! I also like ER! I've also been known to catch the occasional episode of Charmed or MASH. In addition, my favorite books include the "Harry Potter" series, the "Twilight" series, the "Circle of Magic/Circle Opens" series by Tamora Pierce, the "Clan of the Cave Bear" series, nearly anything Peg Kehret, and most things magic, vampire, werewolf, or werecat.
