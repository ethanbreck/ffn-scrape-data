Once-upon-a-time I wrote fanfiction, but now I enjoy reading what other and more talented writers come up with. I write frequently, but I'm the only one who ever sees what I come up with.

I also realized it's been like eight years since I updated this bio, so how about a refresh?

I am a happily married Pinterest addict who became a mom for the first time two Christmases ago when my husband and I had our rainbow twins. My son and my daughter are pretty much my world now, and I'm very fortunate to be able to stay home with them full time. Even thought my days are busier than ever, being home and watching these two little people grow and develop has very much reenergized my love of writing, and I'm working on my original works for the first time in literally years. And I blame the recent Beauty and the Beast movie for re-introducing me to the fan fiction world. I've been a long time reader and occasional dabbler- Harry Potter was my gateway almost two decades ago (and wow, I've never felt as old as I do typing that) and I've been an addict ever since, but with how crazy my life has been with my marriage and this last pregnancy, I'd forgotten how much fun it is to read about favorite characters in new situations. It feels good to be back.

Feel free to PM me- new friends are always welcome!
