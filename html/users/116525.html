Ah, nostalgia. It has been so long since I've been able to get back to this. Technical difficulties. I'm sorry to say that I probabaly won't be continuing any of these stories. It depends really. I have decided to take my own personal storywriting to the next level. Meaning, I'm working on a book. Several, actually. And I have sooooo much material to work with. I hope that what I write is good enough for everyone out there. I wasn't all that popular here, but I didn't really get that far though. I pray it is different with this new endeavor of mine. Again, I'm sorry to any of you out there who have read my stories her, but I thank you as well. Your encouragement has meant a lot to me.

-Link

Hey. How are you? Decided to update this. Sorry to any readers waiting for me to update my stories. Life is getting in the way at the moment. I just got Interenet at my home recently so hopefully updates will increase.

"If life hands you lemons, throw them back and say 'I don't want your stupid lemon's!'"

~Stories~

Dead Letters~ This is really a collection of letters written by different characters from various animes. So, you'll just have to read.

Forgotten Serenade~Part One of three separate stories. ~Usagi is reincarnated as Link, the so-called daughter of Sauron. After abandoning her life of destruction, for she had wanted to take revenge on all of Middle-earth for her father, she meets Gandalf the Grey and establishes a grandfather/granddaughter relationship with him. But she is lured toward the Shire unknowingly by the Ring, and in an effort to cast away her unhappy past, she embarks on a journey to destroy the One Ring with Frodo. Will she be able to resist the Ring's call? What are Myoko's real reasons for joining the Fellowship?And what of the hauntingly familiar visions she keeps having of women with abilities to control the elements?~

Divided Soul~Part Two of three separate stories.(Work in progress. Will be posted when Part One is finished.) ~Link/Usagi is divided just as the Fellowship is. She has separated herslef into three beings: Herself with the entity of the elf, Interia, inside of her, Alexandria the Wanderer, and Oranya the Lost Child, but Link's dark past has come back to haunt her in the form of the Black Monster, the shadow of her destructive self controlling Oranya. She is caught in a struggle for the survival of Rohan and a struggle to know her feelings for her fellow comrades. Will she survive the battle of Helm's Deep? Will she and Alexandria be able to drive the Black Monster out of Oranya? What are the new visions she's been having of herself, Myoko, and the Riddermark horseman, Madhwind?

A Father's Love~Part three of three separate stories.(Will be posted when Part Two is finished.) ~Link/Usagi is in love with Aragorn, son of Arathorn, Isildur's Heir, but will their love last? Having gotten no answers from Saruman, she prepares for the fight for Middle-earth. She begins to dwell more and more on her so-called father, Sauron. When the Ring is destroyed, Sauron will die, but did she really want that? Why is she afraid of Frodo reaching Mount Doom? Did she even want Sauron to die?

My Only Wish~SailorMoon/Harry Potter-FirstYear crossover with a few characters from various other things, and a few of my own characters. ~Serena Usagi Tsukino is no longer a Sailor Scout. After a year-long disappearance, she has returned as the person she was born to be. She is the daughter of the Hero of Time: Link Venus de Milo Serenity. She has taken residence with her birth mother, the mad scientist, Washu. Her only obligation is to protect and uphold her father's legacy, but that can be difficult when you have your younger self from a different dimension living with you. Link(Older.)and Li's(Younger.) histories are similar: they lost their namesake father in a terrible war, they recieved similar scars over their right eyes from Dark Link, and both have recieved the Master Sword. Link has enough trouble with her own four children: her adopted son and daughter, Goten and Hotaru, and her twins, Tralex and Trinity, but Li has left her alternate dimension, due to her dimension being destroyed(a consequence of time travel), and has brought along her friends as well. Link is now surrounded by the younger versions of herself and her friends, but worst of all: all of them(Consisting of Li, Myoko, Incar, Naboora, Carrie, Terry, Gerdi, Goten, Hotaru, Tralex and Trinity.)have been accepted into Hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry. What's a former Sailor Scout turned Hero of Time to do? Let them go, of course!(Focuses mainly on Hogwarts activities with Li and her crew, but Link is still a vital part.)~

That's all for now. Ja ne!
