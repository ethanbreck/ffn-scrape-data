UPDATED NEWS! 1/26/10

Wow, just wow...after nearly three years since my last update on this profile (for those who even bother to read this), I am here to say...yeah everything is pretty much dead. Looking back at when I first started writing fanfiction, it's just so odd to look at this page. It amazes me that I surprisingly get a few hits and comments every now and then which really makes me go O_o wtf are you serious...especially when they are just so bad! Dude, one thing for sure...at the age of 13 couldn't write crap. Even now, I'm not sure I can even write, but thinks I guess I am really disturbed at my lurkdom around here.

In all honesty, I am active on this page...just check out the reviews section...still...the same problem with my DA page. I'm there, just not doing anything, and I suppose I've had enough of that. So, why am I rambling here? Well, I suppose I am trying to make a goal. I am going to make time for writing and my artwork. However, I realize it's futile to even try writing for a fandom I've lost interest in. So, basically I'll be writing for the fandom I am currently obsessed with, and that is Eyeshield 21.
