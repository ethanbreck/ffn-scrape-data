Author has written 7 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, and Supernatural.

I'm 44. I'm a night person which is when I read and write Buffy fics, one amongst too many of my hobbies. I live in Georgia with my hubby. One of my stories is posted at a site called inamorati - this site is another favorite of mine. It can be reached at www.geocities.com/inamorati_sb.

Hello again. I decided to leave my old profile with a few updates for nostalgia, but in truth it's been a long while since I've posted any new writing here, and I have missed it. Real life got in the way of my inspiration for the past few years, but I am hopefully back. I am also going to try to make sure I finish any new stories I begin. I have at least one new Buffy fanfic in the works, but I won't start publishing that one until I get more of it done.

In the meantime, I've decide to go into some new territory and try my hand at Supernatural fanfic. I am not sure the Supernatural fans will be quite ready, because truthfully my stories are, by and large, unusual, and dabbling in new territory won't mean that's going to change. You've been warned.

Most of these stories will be multi-chaptered and somewhat long. The first chapter or two are sometimes slow and a little wordy as the stories get their bearings, but good (hopefully) - and generally unusual - stuff often follows. I apologize that my older stories have now lost their scene breaks. I will eventually try to fix this, but it will take time.

I like feedback of all kinds and it's never too late to get it. My first story was entirely rewritten based on feedback I received here, and for that I am grateful, because the story now posted is much better than the original. Feedback via e-mail is also good, but I will warn in advance that I don't check my e-mail often, and if you ask questions, you might get a wordy response in return. It's one of my quirks, and try as I might, it doesn't go away.

Okay, enough of my babbling... on to the fanfiction...
