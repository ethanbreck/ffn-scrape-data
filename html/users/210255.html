x bio x

Right, so I'm Chesra, and I am fangirl, so beware. :D

I'm insane. Most of the time. Sometimes I lapse into fangirl incoherent squee, but I do check my grammar and spelling and all that. I make icons and I write fanfics, but on both accounts I fail. There is no genius, only a little girl who is obsessed with the internet.

x obsessions x

Games: Final Fantasy VII-XII, Kingdom Hearts
Anime/Manga: Death Note, Bleach, Inuyasha, xxxholic, Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicle, X/1999, Ouran High School Host Club, Katekyo Hitman Reborn, D.Gray-Man, Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya,

Books & Authors: Good Omens (Neil Gaiman & Terry Pratchett), Harry Potter (JK Rowling), Chronicles of Chrestomanci (Diana Wynne Jones), Jane Austen books

OTP(s): Axel x Roxas, Roxas x Namine, Ichigo x Rukia, Momoshiro x Ryoma, Fuuma x Kamui, Kurogane x Fye, L x Raito, Yasuchika x Satoshi (PEOPLE IN THE FANDOM DO NOT APPRECIATE THIS PAIRING . ), Yamamoto x Gokudera, Squalo x Dino, Xanxus x Squalo, Matt x Mello, Crowley x Aziraphale

Note:
I move from one fandom to another like a disease. I have an attention span of a flea. XD I can never stick to one. I mostly write whenever I'm bored and there's a pen and paper about.

x reading list x

I'm open-minded, despite my slashy tendencies. Het, when nicely written, is great. Slash, when plotless and pure porn, is a little mind-numbing and predictable. Basically, I'm up for romantic comedies (though I always end up writing buckets of angst myself) and crack fics, because nothing cheers me up like funny things. Sexual innuendo is good enough for me, because sometimes the actual thing can get a little too descriptive for my taste.
