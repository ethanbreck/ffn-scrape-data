Oi. Where to start?

Well, I'm StarryEyedChild, but usually I respond to either "Aly" or "Alyson." I'm an 19 year old girl from Nova Scotia who's just starting to renew her love of fanfiction writing.

My favorite series to write/read are:

-Cardcaptor Sakura (NOT Cardcaptors. Eugh)
-Harry Potter
-Digimon
-Neon Genesis Evangelion
-Lizzie Maguire
-Radio Free Roscoe
-Friends
-Queer As Folk
-Kingdom Hearts
- others... that I can't think of at the moment. Hee.

Things I can't STAND in fanfiction:

-poor spelling/grammar. Spellcheck is your friiiiiiiiend.
-net talk like "lol" "omg" or "rotfl"
-chat fics/script fics
-wRitIng LiKE THis.
-characters that are horribly (horribly,horribly, HORRIBLY) out of character.
-Mary Sue's/Gary Stu's.

Also,
-Sakura is not a brainless ditz/slut.
-Syaoran is not a player/sex fiend.
-Touya is not a heartless bastard.
-Tomoyo is a bit obsessed, but not a stalker.
-Meiling is actually a sweet, understanding person. NOT a bitch.
-Syaoran's mother isn't a bitch either.
-Meling and Syaoran we never really engaged. No, really. They were, what, eight years old? Syaoran just agreed to do it to shut her up.He didn't take her seriously.It wasn't an arranged marriage by their parents. It was just Meling being a bit obsessive because she had a crush on him, ok? Yeesh.
-Shinji is a scared and frustrated young man, not a pansy. Quit picking on him.
-Rei does have feelings and emotions, she just has a hard time expressing them. She's confused, not a robot. (ok, maybe Rei 3 is a robot. Rei 2 was pretty cool in my books. Whoops. Spoiler)
-Rachel is a little bit spoiled, but she's not a heartless bitch.

Got it? Also... Will you stop with the CCS gang fics! Original ideas, people! You. Box. Outside. THINK!

I'm not a cold, grouchy, fanfiction author, I swear! I'm not saying I'm "Super Author" or anything. I just get really picky and frustrated sometimes. I actually am rather mild tempered and sweet. Honestly! Just drop me a line, ok?

Wanna know more about me? Send me an IM or read my journal (http:www.livejournal.com/users/secretagentpata) for my daily rambles. Ciao.

~Aly
