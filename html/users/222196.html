Oh, hey! Welcome to my lame profile. So, let's get this shenanigan over with:D

If you must know...

I've grown up a little bit... been here xxxa whilexxx forever, and it's sad to say I've only written a paltry few fics in the xxx5 or soxxx million years I've belonged to ff.n. I'm xxx20xxx 25 now. xxxI go to collegexxx I went to college for 7 years and am finally about to graduate. It's good times. My dad asks what the hell I'll do with a double major in Anthropology and English. It was my little joke (and a possible reality!) that what you do with a B.A. in English is go backpacking in Europe. Now that I've added the Anthropology, I could go backpacking AND study some cultural anomalies. Har... har...

But yeah. I've decided to stop putzing around and try to dig my heels in on doing some real writing. It's this strange relationship with me and my writing (and art), that causes me to be terrified of it. I enjoy it, and I love it when others enjoy it, but somehow I'm afraid of building up this awesome idea and then having it do the whoopee cushion thing. -deeeeflaaateeddd- But, I will try to suck it up and do justice to the wonderful and lofty things that live inside my head. I really appreciate those who, after all this time, come by and throw me a review or two. It's what gets me back on that untamed horse. You guys have all my warmest and most sincere feelings pouring out to ya.

UPDATES:Hooolyyyy hell, I take long breaks from this site. I feel horrible, penitent and guilty every time I come crawling back, because I feel like I've let people hang. Which I have - it's a wonder someone hasn't mailed me a rotten tomato to tell me I'm annoying because I never update. I owe it to those people who have been so lovely to me, to continue sharing the stories I write. If you like, mail me that tomato; I need a reasonably sized blow to my ego every now and again.
