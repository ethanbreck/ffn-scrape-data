Hey, my name is Jess, I'm21 and I live in Mass. and I'm now attendingcollege in Ohio. I'm a Classical Humanities major. It's been a long time since I've been on fanfiction, (at least 2 years)but I've begun to miss it.

I believe languages are very important, I am multilingual: english, spanish, latin, ancient greek, and some sign language. I think its fun to study languages.

My penname comes from my own name, not Jessica Simpson and Nick Lachey (although I was sad that they broke up).

Don't forget to read the stories under my favorites and my favorite authors! Reviews of any kind are greatly appreciated. If they are flames, it just helps me to learn from my mistakes and keeps me interested in this process!
