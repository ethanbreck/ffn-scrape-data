Schehezerade
hide bio
PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 12-26-01, id: 144289, Profile Updated: 09-29-09

The Schehezerade is a nocturnal creature of the upper central United States. It thrives in thunderstorms, used book stores, and the manga section of the local comic book store. It belongs on one of the lowest rungs of the food chain, hunting only the weakest members of the wild herds of Diet Dr. Pepper and grazing for grilled onions and mushrooms only when the sun is at its lowest ebb. It is a solitary creature, highly territorial, and prone to hibernation during the humid summer months. Glimpses of this pale, red-haired creature are few and far between, and it is speculated that the Schehezerade will soon be included on the endangered species lists.
