Nothing will be updated ever again. Including One Moment. I have moved away from all of the fandoms I have posted fic for here. Bleach is actually me revisting an old fandom and getting bitten really badly by a plot bunny I can't resist. It was supposed to be a fling fic for me to write while I ponder on my original fiction at the back of my mind, but now it's an epic with no sign of ending and I am spending all of my (sparse) spare time writing it. Go figure.

I also have ideas for another epic fic, this time for Naruto (yes, another old fandom; I've been catching up on my Jump.) Whether or not that ends up being written depends to be seen. My to-do list is enormous.

I have other fics that I don't post here out of a) laziness, b) inappropriateness. They are either on Archiveofourown as evocates or on own my Livejournal, also as evocates. If you would like to see the porn I write, they can be found on those links. There is some for almost every fandom I have posted here.

Anyway, I've had this account for almost half of my life. It is literally a chronology of how my writing has grown and changed as I grew up. I started posting fic on this account when I was fifteen years old; now I'm in my mid-twenties. This means that the quality of the writing gets shittier the further down you scroll. I recommend that you don't go further than 'Allegorical'. I'm tempted to remove every single fic from below that one, but 1) I like having a chronology, 2) my standards =/= everyone's standards, and 3) I'm not mean enough to remove fics that people might like. So here they stay.

But, seriously, don't read the Gundam Wing fics or the Hellsing/Harry Potter crossover. I cringe away from the writing so hard that I can't even think about mentioning the titles. Just ugh. Ew. /shivers

Things I like to write:

Character/relationship development and exploration Expansion of plot points in canon. Otherwise known as 'some lines that interest me and which I elope with for a Vegas marriage'. Not contradicting canon, whether about characterisation or plot points. I'd rather make my ideas work within the constraints. The only exception is when one of my assumptions is contradicted by canon after I wrote it. Research. I am a nerd. I also don't like to be wrong about something. Sometimes crack. Honestly, I don't write crack often, but when I do, it is really, really cracky because I have denied the crack for so long.
