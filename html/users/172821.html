Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, Dragon Ball Z, and Series Of Unfortunate Events.

Firstly, I hope you didn't read my stories. They are a mess. I don't think I'll ever update them. I have bits and pieces written, but I don't think I'll ever connect the dots. I only read fan fics now because I'm just too lazy to write fan fiction even though I get ideas...I just never write them. Maybe someday I'll fix that, but who knows. I'm just a big pile of lazy.

Actually, I'm surprised my stores still get hits! Wowza! Seriously, I wrote them yeeeeeeeeeears and years ago. Ridonkulous. They're only up because they are on people's favorites list and I wouldn't want to take them away from someone. I've lost a lot of favorite fics that way.

My favorite fandoms are: How I Met Your Mother, DOCTOR WHO, Pride and Prejudice (the book and 1995 movie), Harry Potter, THE OFFICE, LABYRINTH, TORCHWOOD, and ALIAS.

I'm in college and I'm majoring in E-Business. Basically website design, website programming and marketing. It's a barrel full of fun! Especially staying in the school computer labs until 9:30 at night working on homework! Oh what I would give for a new Macintosh and the Adobe Creative Package.

Woah! I'm 21 now! Party timez! Just kidding. I seem to spend all my time at work or at school...or doing fandom things.

I also may or not have a so called "unhealthy" obsession with David Bowie. I need to run more scientific tests of staring at his pictures, watching his movies, and listening to his music for days on end in order to come up with conclusive results.

More random trivia to come...never because I update this profile about once every two years.
