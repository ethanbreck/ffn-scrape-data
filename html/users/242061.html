Author has written 4 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Lord of the Rings, and Inuyasha.

Hello there! I write as a hobby, and lately have been tending entirely to original works. As a result, this place has suffered, but I am astonished and flattered that people are still finding these old fics and enjoying them (especially as they are terrible! *hides*). Thank you! If one day I ever get around to writing fanfiction again, they will definitely find their way here.

Note on my fics: I lean heavily towards canon (you might call me a canon-purist), so in the cases where there are multiple versions of the same story (i.e. - book/manga-verse vs. movie/anime-verse), I will almost certainly be writing for the original version, whatever that may be. Any exceptions to this rule will be noted in the stories themselves.
