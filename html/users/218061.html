Hello. I'm Yvonne. I'm in my 40's and I live in Michigan (USA), but I dream of living in New Zealand at least part time someday.

I have a few links I'd like to share:

Yvonne's Fan Fiction
This was my first website I created for my writing. The two stories that are posted at fanfiction.net are featured at this site. It makes for a good Web Site Companion piece to the stories, especially JP New Beginnings. There are character profiles, maps and other things there to check out.

JP Dawn of Retribution
This is where my most recent JP story resides. I wrote it with one other person, so it's not all my work and that's why it's not listed at fanfiction.net. There are character profiles and a few other things as well, so please check it out!

Alan & Ellie Fanfic Archive
This is a site devoted to the JP characters, Alan Grant and Ellie Sattler/Degler. It was first started by someone else, and now I've been maintaining the site for about the last 2 years. There are quite a few good stories here and they all involve ... you guessed it ... Alan and Ellie!

So far, I have only submitted Jurassic Park fanfiction here at the site, but I hope to post other types of stories in the future, so please keep a look-out for them!

Thanks for reading my work! Muchly appreciated! And if you have any Jurassic Park-type fiction you want me to read and review, please email me with requests.

-yvonne
