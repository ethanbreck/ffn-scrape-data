Me? Most people would describe me as disturbed,though that's not ENTIRELY true. Hehe. The lucky ones might think I'm completely and uterly taciturn. Think whatever you want, I really don't care. I am 13, and in eighth grade.
I love to write. (duh, why would I be on here if I didn't?) Last year I was on my school's newspaper, and this year I am going to be co-editor. I'm excited, but this means I'll have even LESS time to work on my fanfics. This summer I'm working on an original story, somewhat darker than my usual writing. The tentative title is "Waker". I'm considering posting it on fictionpress when I'm finished.
I play the piano, right now I'm obsessed with Vanessa Carlton's music. And I like to tap dance. Most people think that the tap dance part is kind of weird, but it's a lot of fun. (*evil grin* you get to make a lot of noise). I'm currently choreagraphing a dance to Evanescence's "Bring Me To Life". I have a new obsession every few weeks. Currently it's the TV show Birds of Prey. Even though it was cancelled. I'M LOVE LOTR! My new years resolution was to learn how to speak elvish. So much for that. It lasted about 5 months. Anyone who speaks elvish knows that my name (Eleriel) means "elf-girl".
So far I only have Redwall fics, BoP fics, and a few poems(Black Rose claims they made her cry!Horray, maybe I CAN write poetry!)Just one thing more, I HATE TO TYPE! So even if I have 5 finished stories, chances are that I'll have typed 2 of them. Please be patient.
