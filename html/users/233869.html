Author has written 42 stories for Harry Potter, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Angel, Skins, Primeval, and Doctor Who.

Firstly, the basics; I’m Heather, I live in the West Midlands in the UK, I’m 27 and I'm a Ron Weasley fangirl, one simple fact that will never change.

I’ve been writing since I could string a sentence together, but I didn’t discover the world of fan fiction until I was 16. Shortly after, I started writing it myself. My earlier works are rubbish; I know it, you know it, and some of it is highly embarrassing. I won't be deleting them though, in order to write well, we must learn to write badly, too.

As the song goes, things can only get better!

It began with Buffy and Angel, followed shortly by Harry Potter; I then dabbled in the world of band fiction for a while, before finally settling back to Harry Potter last year. I also took to writing stories for the television series, Skins, at the beginning of 2007, which I have finally posted up on here (14/02/08 to be exact, I didn't just suddenly get a ridiculous bout of inspiration, that's a years worth of fanfic there lol).

HP loves: Ron/Hermione, Draco/Ginny, Harry/Ginny, Harry/Ron, Ron Weasley, Luna Lovegood and Colin Creevey.

Skins loves: Tony/Maxxie, Maxxie/Chris, Chris/Jal, Naomi/Emily, Maxxie Oliver, Tony Stonem, Chris Miles and Emily Fitch.

Time After Time: I know I only just started it, but this fic is on hiatus as of 22/03/08. Apologies, I'm suffering a bit of a Skins writers block and I'm currently writing a bunch of Rupert Grint/Emma Watson fic instead. If you fancy a read, pop on over to my Livejournal (link's at the top of the page).

Update (10/04/09): Still writing RPF, might get something else up on here one day lol.

.x.
