10-24-12:

Up-date:

To Bare Witness has finally been up-dated! Working on Wolf Wind and Untouchable and so many stories at once that I may just go crazy. Apparently the writing bug took a healthy bite out of me, because all I want to do is write!

I also have some Rurouni Kenshin one-shot ideas I hope to get out over time, so I hope they are well-received. .

Thanks for reading!

My passion is, and shall always continue to be writing. I love fantasy and sci-fi mostly, though enjoy a good historical novel or modern day telling now and again. I've graduated from University about three years ago, and am attempting to write original fiction as well as getting back in to fanfiction so please be patient and courteous with me. I seemingly always have too many story ideas than I know what to do with, but I hope re-writing old stories and posting new will enable me to get back into the swing of truly writing--which I have not had the time to do and am finally making time for now--along with also re-gaining some old and new readers.

Thank you all for your support and understanding. I hope I don't leave too many stories unattended.

May there always be new ideas and stories to be told.
