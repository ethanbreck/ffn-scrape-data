Author has written 12 stories for Baldur's Gate, Forgotten Realms, Neverwinter Nights, and Star Wars.

Was born and subsequently raised. I am no Writer and have no hopes of becoming one. But I am an avid reader. Biggest ambition: to write a simple, cohesive novelette with structured plot and sufficient characterization.

Favorite books of all times:

GRR Martin: The Storm of Swords

G.G.Kay: The Lions of Al Rassan

F.M. Dostoyevskiy: The Idiot, Brothers Karamazoffs

W.S. Maugham: The Theater

JRR Tolkien: Silmarillion

R. Aldington: Death of a Hero

K. Capek: Short Stories

Things that I tell everyone, and nobody listens (even myself, apparently). Do not hurry. Find a beta-reader. Finish the first draft of the full story and make sure that it is internaly consistent/makes sense before starting to post it. Adrenalin/few seconds/cool and other every-day expressions trip the eye when used in a medieval-ish setting; hence - substitute. Recently enamored of KOTOR-SWTOR verse.

I really need to post a link to Yvolgar's picture. He's cute!
