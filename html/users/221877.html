Konnichiwa minna-san! I've been an avid anime fan for over ten years, so much so that I usually refuse to watch any anime in dubbed format (unless it's for the first time for exposure's sake.) My all-time favorite animes are Sailor Moon, Rurouni Kenshin, and Gundam Wing AC (of course, I have every episode and movie for those three. ;) ) I lost count long ago of how many anime I've actually watched up to this point...
