You know, I freaked myself out when I realised when the last time I updated this bio and wrote an FFVII story was. It’s been 19 months since I updated this. Ouch.

Anyway I’m Lindz, or Lindsey whichever one you prefer. I’m 21, female, employed full-time (it’s actually a part-time contract but I work nearly full-time hours most of the time) and single (still and at this rate will forever be).

I never realised how much I missed FFVII fanfiction still I stopped reading them then started again a few weeks ago. I really did miss it.

I’ve removed the three stories that I haven’t updated for months and I’m sorry to say have no intention of finishing. When I get time I’m gonna start writing again, hopefully. I have the urge to write I just need a storyline; I’ll work on it, promise. It’ll probably be something about the Turks coz I missed the guys so much hehe.
