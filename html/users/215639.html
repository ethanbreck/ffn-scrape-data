Update! April 2011

I live! Barely. At the moment I have one hell of a horrible cold, what a way to spend my vacation! This was actually the week I was to spend in Japan, but because of the Earthquake, Tsunami and the horrible aftermath, my trip has been postponed. My prayers are with Japan.

I've been thinking about Ken and Ran a lot lately, I think its time to crack those fingers and set to writing and typing the conclusions to their stories. Maybe add a few others. I graduated with a MFA recently, and with no foreseeable "grown up" job, I have a little free time apart from work. And being sicker than a dog surrounded my mountains of tissues. My head honestly feels like a bowling ball right now. So! It's back to the writers corner, where hopefully my muse will whisper words of a great story to me!

I'm in the process at the moment of re-reading where I've left off then it's to the keyboard!

10,671 hits for BLIND! Thanks everyone!

Review! Reviews make me one happy girl!

Chapters are in the works for both stories! Check back for updates.

Unless noted, stories have not been discontinued or forgotten for that matter.

Your Goodbye Chapter I

Blind IX Uncertainty

Everything's like an open book, continue to read and you'll always find what your looking for.

Most of my stories are shounen-ai/yaoi centered. You'll be hard pressed to find stories from me that are not, though there are a few. I'm an avid Weiss Kreuz fan, and a die hard RanKen fan; most of my stories are too. I also drabble a bit in KaiRei, Bakuten shoot stories, another one of my favorite pairings. Of the other pairings I love, KuroFai of Tsubasa Reservoir Chronicles and Yuki and Shuichi of Gravitation, and Havoc and Roy of Full Metal Alchemist. One of the first pairings I ever wrote was Mimato, from digimon - one of the first series I got into. Though I really dont write anything on them anymore, I really would like to continue (and revise the hell out of) the stories I did write on them.

Care to know more about me, catch up with me on my Livejournal. Stories and chapters can occasionally be found there a few days ahead of postings here. Have questions, send them here.
