A Knight's Work is Never Done: Chapter 19 is on hold while i go back and see if anything needs any serious editing.

Never Leave Jorowyn Alone: New chapter coming soon. May even lead to a new chapter of Silver Alliance being published by Lynnwood. How exciting!
