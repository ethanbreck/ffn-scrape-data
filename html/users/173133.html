Hello everyone, I figured it was about time that I updated my profile. I am a 21-years-old graphic design student and love what I do. However, reading and history are tied on the top of my favorite things' list. I would read almost anything I can get my hands on (literally!), and I love to learn and do all kinds of new things. I find Tolkien's work fascinating in all respects; there is a little in it to satisfy all claims: history, philosophy, geography, poetry, adventure, mythology, even romance!

I am currently working on several new ideas that are still in the rough state, but are stories that have kept me very excited. I will be posting a Silmarillion Fic, THE ARRIVAL OF THE HUNTSMAN. Very soon I will also post an update to RANGER OF ITHILIEN.

My favorite elves would have to be Finwe and Finrod, and most surprisingly Feanor. My favorite man is Faramir, I think I might be a bit obsessed about him. My favorite hobbit is Merry! (I am a little annoyed by Sam, but still love him) I like the tales of troubled families within the Lord of the Rings, but I think you know that by now (Denethor-Faramir-Boromir, Finwe-Indis-Miriel, Aldarion-Erendis, that kind of thing) I love to research all the little details. If you see any inaccuracies in my writing, please let me know and I will fix them. I appreciate feedback and any kind of ideas you can give about how to improve. I'd love to hear your opinions and thoughts about Tolkien or anything else. What is your fave Tolkien work? What is your favorite Numenorean tale? What do you think is the fate of the dwarves after they die? What do you think happened when Finwe took Indis to wife?
If you wish to mail, write, read or review, feel free to do so. I love to get e-mail and meet new people. Have a great day and enjoy!
