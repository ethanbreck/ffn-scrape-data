02~08~07

Ooookay, long time no updates. I'm not dead. Heh, if we only had a quarter for everytime that appeared in one of these profiles. But seriously though, still alive, still kicking, imagination's still in overdrive. I just haven't had the time and lasting inspiration to keep at it til a chapter's actually finished.

I've also been having technical difficulties since the middle of last year. Technical as in my work was stolen (along with my computer) and I didn't have back-ups of everything. Real smart on my part, I know. -_-' And now my replacement comp's in the shop. That's technology for ya.

Lately I've been debating taking some of these fics down (at least until I get around to rewriting them). I dunno though. I may leave 'em up, if for nothing else than as a reminder that I have actually improved as a writer. Speaking of which, I'm more into writing my own stuff these days. May even post some of it online within the next year or so. Check my dA page for art and whatnot if you'd like.
