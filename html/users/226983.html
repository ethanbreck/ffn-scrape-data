Sup.

As of right now I am 30 years old, and been writing fanfiction since I was 16! I try and update as quickly as possible. You know...distractions. Whenever I'm not busy at work I type a chapter for one of my stories. I try to do two stories at a time, but sometimes I start writing a new story and well...updates take forever.

The one story that will take a couple of years to complete is Low Mans Lyrics. It was quite popular back in the day, but people reported it because of "mature content." At the time, it was rated NC-17. What can you do?

I recently read some of my old stories. I apologize for the confusion, the poor grammar, bad everything really. Sometimes I look at the notes, and roll my eyes at my younger self. Young me was very immature, I said as I updated my bio in my Batman fottie pajamas.

Most of my stories revolve around Gohan. If you haven't realized it by now he is my favorite character on the show. I came up with the name CrazyGohanGurl because I am crazy fan of Gohan, and the gurl happened because spelling 'girl' like 'gurl' was the thing. Yep, I thought I was being cool. My name used to be sweetlilgirl, and I do not know how the hell I came up with that name. I'm pretty sure Pedrobear would approve that name, especially coming from a 16 year old.

When I have free time it's usually wasted on the internet, or television. I sometimes think 'Hey, I should update fanfiction.' Instead I'm on the internet killing my brain cells one at a time. Herp derp. Update: I have a boyfriend now. Most of my time is with him, as like any new couple.

I do want to thank you guys for reading my stories. I know not many people read fanfiction anymore, especially if writers like me never update.
