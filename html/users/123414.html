I have a tumblr (newsstandrock) but I don't really know how to use it. My LJ (newsstandrock) has my old writing as well, some that didn't make it here

I'm almost 30, haven't written in almost a decade and am trying to come back. I've lived around the world, Australia, England... I'm half Hawai'ian, half Canadian. I've made some mistakes in my life, and am trying to make up for them. I'm back in school for working on getting a bachelors in Addiction in Underserved Populations to get my CAC (Certified Addiction Counselor) III, then hoping to get a masters degree in clinical social work so I can get my LAC (Licensed Addiction Counselor) the highest you can go without an MD. I'm doing this in Denver but am a mountain girl at heart and either want to move there or to Hawai'i to work with young women battling addiction.

I'm looking for inspiration to write, so throw little prompts and plot bunnies and other inspiration. I want to go back to my story about Charlie Weasley and completely scratch it and start new at the drawing board, updating it with DH canon, making the themes more mature...

2007

I'm a Canadian girl from Colorado who, when and if I grow up, wants to be a ski bum. So logically I go to university in Rhode Island and in the mean time I've been working at pizza places and doing internships out of Washington DC.

I find pleasure in the little things in life... The chalk board at the local coffee shop, sprinkles, playing frisbee in the rain, making tie-dye, successfully climbing a 5.11 route, skiing fresh powder, playing pool with friends, baking eight layer cakes, the first lap around a freshly zambonied ice rink, the original Oregon Trail computer game, American Sign Language and other things like that.

A quick rundown of some of the fandoms I enjoy, pairings and plots a like (a common theme is my love of angst and pairings that aren't able to manifest whether they be forbidden or unrequited) and my writing style and stories...

Music

blink-182: I started writing blink fics about six years ago. I'm an avid Mark/Tom shipper and really enjoy one shots and angst. I'm mostly a slash supporter, but if they guys are paired with their wives, that's okay as well. So long as the closeted M/T is acknowledged.

TV Shows

Law and Order: SVU: This has been my favourite TV show for quite some time and I just recently got involved with fan fics. I really hate stories that put a relationship with Elliot and Olivia in the forefront. They are far too professional to ever run off together and have twenty children and that also takes away from the point of Law and Order. I acknowledge the sexual tension that exists, but I like to leave it at that. Two best friends and partners, who work well together, and over the years something deeper may have developed, but that's that. As far as other pairings, I'm not a big fan of putting anyone in the 1-6 together. I'll read the occasional Liv/Alex or Liv/Casey if it's well done. In my opinion SVU fics shouldn't be romance-centric, they should focus on cases and character exploration.

Movies

The Mighty Ducks Triology: Yep the only and one Disney favourite. I love realistic stories with the Ducks, especially ones that explore their transition into college and later life. I refuse to read fics where there is a new girl on the team. Mary Sues ruined the FF.net fandom and continues to do so to this day. I especially hate Mary Sue Ducks, where some super girl comes in as an 'enforcer' (that's not even a position). It's pathetic. Bash Slash is always okay and so is Conway/Adam. Especially unrequited attraction between Conway and Adam. Any other pairing is pretty much acceptable except for Charlie/Connie, and that includes femslash with Julie and Connie. I'm a Banks angst whore. I also like well developed OCs in this category because it's always been unrealistic to think the Ducks only hang out with one another at school. I'm an ice hockey player and can't stand it when a writer has no clue about hockey.

Books

Harry Potter: Another fandom that I was involved in years ago and am coming back to. Charlie Weasley is by far my favourite character, and I love a good Weasley centric fic. Bill's a close second, and I love the twins and Ginny. I've recently developed a taste for dark Ginny stories. Acceptable common pairings are Harry/Ginny, Draco/Ginny, Draco/Hermione. I hate Ron or any other Weasley/Hermione. The odder the pairing the better. It is essential that it's well developed. OCs are acceptable in the outside magic world but not within the context of Hogwarts. There are so many students mentioned that could be developed well. I'm also a fan of any of the Gryffindor Quidditch Team members. Fics about Wood, Katie, the twins, Alicia, etc. and any pairing there after are always good.

That's a basic run down.

Most of my fics are four or so years old M(EDIT IN 2017- make that over 10 years old now), back from when I was in high school. My writing style has changed as of late. If something was written in 2002 or 2003, keep in mind how much time has passed. Also I like one shots better than novel-length fics, and even my chaptered stories aren't lengthy.

For anything else, feel free to contact me.
