Osu!

How are you doing?

I'm a big girl now, going to Michigan State University. Gonna be a pharmacist and give people pills.

Still love my manga, anime, and now, I have regained my love for wrestling.

I'm a gamer, and you can find me on XBOX Live on both systems.

I will probably delete the works I have now. They were old, experimental, and not my best

Of course, I learned from the mistakes, and soon, I will have something up more...suitable to show what little literary talent I have.

I love reading other stories with originality.

I am not a stranger to slash or yaoi and I have no problem with it...most likely because I'm a woman. Eh.

Romance isn't the only thing to write about, though. I learned that over the years, and I happy to say that I have found authors who utilize the other genres for fanfiction.

Everyone loves a good laugh. And I'm part of 'everyone.'

Currernt works in progress:

Genre: Games (Street Fighter)

Stay With Me (Unlikely)- named after the Celldweller song that I was listening to on loop for five hours before I typed out the first thing that came to mind. Evil Ryu has abandoned his friends and morals in his pursuit to be stronger, but his friends are not ready to let go of him yet, especially Sakura. She has a bone to pick with him, and she'll have to speak with her fists to make her point stand.

Notes on SWM(U)- I may have been a fan of Ryu/Sakura before there was even a real fandom for it, and no matter what valid points people may have that it isn't possible, I still stand by it. I've noticed that Sakura has usually been portrayed as a schoolgirl with no respect for others, and is extremely annoying. I want to break out of that mold and give Sakura that warrior edge and womanhood that she's been well-due for. Not sure how long this sucker will be, or how frequent the update may be, but I plan on finishing it...eventually. I'm already surprised and honored by the reviews, and that is inspiration enough for me to continue. The fightscenes in this will be something of a crowning achievement for me. Incorporating the moves that the characters use into words is hard, but after seeing a superb flash animation of Street Fighter vs MK, I was inspired to give Ryu an unconventional way of fighting. If you would like the link to the animation, please feel free to IM on instant messenger.

Spanish Eyes- named after yet another song listened to on repeat, this time the Ricky Martin song. I am deleting "Just One of those Nights" to substitute it with this. Sean is the headliner, once again, because the poor man needs some loving too. Sean is visiting Rio de Janeiro, and one summer night will be quite memorable for the Shotokan trainee.

Notes on SE- Spanish Eyes is going to be a one shot, and hopefully a decent one. Sean has so much potential as a fighter and a lover, but no one really remembers the dear. He will be paired with an original character, but not the same one from before. I've grown as a writer, and am above putting myself in stories, unless its for pure humiliation. The song 'Spanish Eyes' sang by Ricky Martin is incredibly romantic, and I know I would have passed out if I either danced to the song, or if it was sang to me.

NEW!!

Genre: Miscellaneous (Wrestling)

In'turns': An unknown entity has an evil agenda against Vince McMahon and the WWE, and they viciously attack three fans with no ties to the company other than their fandom to the wrestling world. In an unforseen fall of events, the three will be given the chance to live a life anew, and work for the company to be kept safe from harm's way. They will become Inturns.

Notes on Inturns- Like the little play on words? Interns. Inturns. Cause...they get turned into vampires. Hah...yeah, horrible, I know. But, I hope the story itself doesn' flop. I will do my best not to have a Mary Sue/Gary Stu syndrome with this. I will probably end up making fun of the whole idea in a future chapter. But, I will be throwing in wrestler after wrestler into this, hopefully catching their personas in check.
