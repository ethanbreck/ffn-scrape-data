Since I hadn't updated this thing since... well... like, July 2002, I thought I should update it. I still have all my fanfiction on here, excluding the original fiction which has been moved, per the change in the site. But yeah. I don't really write for Cartoons anymore, or anything else, really. The only reason I even use Fanfiction.net is for the General Hospital stories that I am doing, or have done. That's not to say that I won't ever use it for anything else again, but at this point in my writing career, the entirety of my creative energy, at least in the fanfiction sense, is devoted to General Hospital. Why is this? I don't know, because I'm weird I guess. Not many complaints from the people over on the reader side. Not that there aren't complaints... people just don't express them, I guess. I wish they would.
