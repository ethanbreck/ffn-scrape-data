Author has written 27 stories for Lord of the Rings, Animorphs, Swing Kids, Motocrossed, Lizzie McGuire, Tuck Everlasting, Kingdom Hearts, and Misc. Movies.

About my stories: I had a lot of fun with the IYR stories. I think they're some of my best. If you're not an Animorphs fan, you should probably stay away from my stories. Everything else I have up here was written a long time ago, so they're not very good.

I wrote "More" as a real epic when I was a freshman in high school. It's over 70 chapters. I'm posting it just because I'd like this site to be a good reflection of my works, but I don't expect people to read it as it's not very good, and it's a beast. But if you do feel like reading it and reviewing, please do.

Original poetry on Fictionpress.com, my penname is the same.

I appreciate constructive criticism and usually cater to what the reviewer suggests, so please, give suggestions! I also offer such things when I can. Don't take it as a flame; I'm just here to help.

About me in general: I'm a Christian, but I have a few "different" beliefs than mainstream Christianity because of some experiences I've had. I have been writing for as long as I can remember; I think I started about in 4th grade. Right now I'm just your average college student. I speak English (obviously) and Spanish, and I am working on Japanese.

NEVER hesitate to ask me a question about any of my stories (confused by something? Curious? Have a suggestion?) I ALWAYS respond to questions.
