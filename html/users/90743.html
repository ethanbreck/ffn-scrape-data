Hello! I started writing fanfiction YEARS ago when I was 15 . . . I am now 20 and look back at my old work and laugh at a few, but appreciate the effort that I made. Since dabbling into the realms of fanfiction, mostly of the angst and romance genres of Dark Angel and the O.C. I have continued writing. Currently I am a journalist for The Wheel, a campus newspaper, and have a plethora of original fiction, many poems, and a few short stories on fictionpress.net and a few poems and shorts published in literary magazines. In the past I have also worked as a columnist for a paper and an editor for a literary magazine.

I am currently in college and studying English literature and writing as well as Social Work. I hope to one day publish a book. I have been gathering matreials for a book for years on my daily webblog so that maybe I can use it some day for some sort of memoir or piece of fiction based on life experiences.
