I can't believe I'm here again. I've been Charlotte W. Ross for ... years ... but I decided to change my name. Wanna fight about it? I'll meet you in the parking lot. 3pm.

Mmmk, hi -- what's up? I go by many names, but I'm the same person. I'm a Jersey girl. I read and write like I breathe. It comes naturally. Comm major, English minor. Wannabe actress. I like Broadway. I love music and movies. I could probably kick your ass at the movie buff game. I drive a Ford Focus. I type on a Dell Inspiron 1501 notebook. My dogs are cooler than yours. Cut open my veins -- what's inside? Coffee. Yeah, that's right, coffee. It's the writer's crack-cocaine. If I could mainline it, I would.

Favorite pasttimes include watching movies you've never seen, listening to bands you've never heard of, and making Gaz spazz.

In the past, I've written for Dark Angel and CSI. I did one attempt at Full Metal Alchemist but failed miserably. Once upon a time, I threatened to finish all my unfinished fics and, well, uh ... yeah, that never happened. Moving onto Rent now, hoping I'll succeed in that.

I don't tell my characters what to do. I just take dictation.

I like to hear from people. Wanna talk? Send me a PM. I'll read it. I might even respond to it. If I like you enough, I'll let you know what my screen name is. But that's a pretty big if.

Steph, AKA Mark's Maureen, is the best little sister in the world!

By the way, despite the fact some of you were pissed about the end of "Sara's Place", special thanks goes out to the 30 people who added it to their favorites list.

If you want to check out my original work, by all means:
http://www.fictionpress.com/~bellawinterwhite

Hey, I'm only doing what the voices in my head tell me to do.

OK, je t'aime, au revoir, au revoir!
