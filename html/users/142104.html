Unfortunately, I am neither an author nor a poet. My talent only takes me so far; maybe I will have the guts to write something one day, but for now I will leave that task to more qualified individuals. I am 21, une femme, and I am an alumni of Cypress Falls High School as of June 1st of 2002. My 'Pen Name' used to be Mickey, but there are just too many of those at fanfictionNet and that defeats the purpose of being original. I am a romantic, a hedonist (only to a certain level), a cynic, a pessimist and I can be all of the above combined. Sounds contradictory doesn't it? I love fantasy, anything romantic, dark gothic tales, and sometimes heartbreaking dramas. You can find out more about me (as if you want to) by going to my favorite stories and my favorite author sections; they can tell you so many things about me. And if you have time, review those stories! They are written so wonderfully, and deserves a great deal of praise. And if you have time, please recommend me some good readings (email me at fae_crazed@hotmail.com ). Normally I do not criticize authors - I'd leave comments about their writing, yes, but I do not flame the author - it seems a bit discorteous, uncivlized and hypocratic since I have not written anything myself. But I'm vexed at some of the authors here at FFNet and must voice my thoughts. Some would begin writing a wonderful, magical, and sensible story and decide that they would not write again or update in a long while, leaving me and a number of other loyal readers hanging to an incomplete story. What a waste of time. The reader's time and the author's time as well. So please, if you are an author, set a goal to finish your work once you've started it. (This doesn't apply to all authors, of course, and sorry if I've offended anyone). Here is a random (and very cliche) list of my favorite people/movies/things... I find this... well pathetic. LOL. This is another addition to my contradictory personality.
