I have bad grammar so sue me. Damn, it's not like anyone writes perfectly. We all have our own style and mine just happens to include the butchery of the English language. LOL.

Now that that's out of the way go read one of my stories and flame about something else.

I'm 19 going on 20 this month and I've spend most of my free time (which isn't much because I go to a 24 hr college) working on my writing and reading others fanfiction. I know, its sad that my life has been reduced to that. But its so much fun!

My publisher wants me to work on a 5 book series but I don't know if I feel up to it.

If theres one thing I've learned its that your writing is better when you write about something you know.
