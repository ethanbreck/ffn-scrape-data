BIGGER NOTE: I'VE STILL NOT QUIT YET!

(08/06/2013) Its been a while since I've updated this. Now is as good a time as any. :) Anyone who thinks writing is easy isn't enjoying it. If it were easy, it wouldn't be nearly as rewarding. I have little time to write. My life has been very busy, but I'm making room for it where and when I can. I hope that people can enjoy what I offer, and that they'll let me know what they think about what I've written.

A bit about me:

Just a guy (A little crazy, perhaps) who thinks that he just _might_ have something to contribute to society. Perhaps not. We will see, I guess.

I have been writing since college. Started out with some Babylon 5, and then I got side-tracked with life and making a living. Now I have a giant incomplete folder with about thirty incomplete fanfics taking up almost 11 mb of drive space. Lately I've become a fan of Evangelion - and that will likely be one of the first stories I post.

I'm a big fan of: Farscape, Babylon 5, Star Trek, Star Wars, Evangelion, Robotech... Pretty much all that is Sci-Fi.
