Summaries:
'Valerious', FINALLY FINISHED...YAAAAAAY:

The main OC in this story is Christine Aveire, the descendent of Charlotte and Augustus Aveire, a family who Dracula has been out to get for so many years. Christine basically goes back to Romania for a ball, only to be discovered by Dracula, who knows she is a descendent of this family line. He basically wants to kill her, lol. Short and sweet!

A bit of history on the Aveire family: They are all of Spanish descent. Charlotte was an old flame of Dracula, one of the only women he ever became infatuated with. He basically falls in love with her and resents the fact that she is already married to another man. (There is a suggestion of an illicit affair between the two, but nothing confirmed, heheheheh!)

While trying to avoid Dracula at all costs, Christine ends up falling in love with Velkan Valerious. Dracula by this time has basically become obsessed with making Christine suffer the slings and arrows of unrequited love, but that doesn't end up happening when Velkan Valerious admits he returns her love...to be continued. .

If anything, it will answer some questions you might have had as to the storyline...with a little bit of my own creative plot twists...heheheheheheheheee...please read and let me know what you guys think, lol.
