Author has written 28 stories for CSI, Without a Trace, Dark Angel, Law and Order: SVU, NCIS, and Fringe.

I have become a semi-retired fic writer for what has blossomed into several shows/pairings: CSI (Sara/Grissom - my first pairing), NCIS (Gibbs/Kate and Tony/Ziva), Without a Trace (Sam/Jack) and Dark Angel (Max/Alec - ok, this was just the one, but I was pretty proud of it). I am contemplating a new ship or two, the part of my brain that used to be brimming with fic ideas is starting to bug me again.

I am in the US and probably a little older for a fic writer (mid-30s), but its what I love to do.

One day I might actually make this profile page interesting since it seems people still look at it! And read my fics. I couldnt beleive I was still getting hits for stuff I wrote YEARS ago. Gives me the warm fuzzies inside. :-)

See ya on the flip side.
