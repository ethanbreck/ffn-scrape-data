Author has written 9 stories for Digimon, The Santa Clause, Misc. Tv Shows, Gilligan's Island, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Tin Man, and Wizards of Waverly Place.

Hello, my name is Sheila. I graduated from the Kansas City Art Institute in 2006 with a BFA in Photography. I work a seasonal job at a yearbook company, and in the off season I write. Unfortunately, I have given up fanfiction. I have moved on to original fiction. As of Dec. 19, 2008 I am two chapters away from finishing the rough draft of my very first novel, a coming of age story about two teens dealing with tragedy and the consequences of their own bad decisions. If you are interested, I would be honored if you would check out my writing journal.

I would like to thank everyone who has reviewed my fanfics over the years. Your positive reactions and the occasional spot of constructive criticism has inspired me to forge ahead with my novel. So, thank you all. Please do continue to enjoy my fics, even though I most likely will never finish them.
