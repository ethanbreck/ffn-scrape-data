Hi, I'm Sheen. Just a humble university student living in Melbourne Australia at the moment. I love writing fics but when writers block hits me, oh boy does it really hit me. Anyway i've decided to continue writing my fan fics here as well as posting them up on livejournal. But i think i might update my livejournal section more just because its more of a blog for me. So if you'd like to know the inner workings of my warped and wacky mind, you're more than welcome to add me as a friend and be appalled by my weirdness. I'll not only be posting up my story chapters but i'll also be posting up movie and book reviews that i've watched and read. Well thats about it. I'll post up a link to my livejournal on my homepage. Thanks all...HUGGIES!

Sheen

Suki Yuki-It's a Soujirou and Yuki fan fiction and i'll be updating this fairly often.

The family Man-Don't worry i haven't abandoned this story yet, i've just been suffering from a long, long writer's block that i can't seem to get over but slowly i've been getting chapter 9 ready. Bit by bit. It's an Oliver/Chloe fic with their children.

Meeting the son in law-I haven't abandoned this story either, i've just been focusing more on my other stories but when the time is right i'll come back to this one. It's a Scorpius Malfoy and Lily Potter fan fic. I wanted to play around with this idea a little.

All i need-Another Yuki/Soujirou fic that i'll be updating soon.

That's all i can give u an update on at the moment. When i update the other stories i will let you know about them. I hope you like them. All this is updated as off 6th November. Please read them and review!! flames welcomed. Also anyone wishing to beta read please e-mail me at constantvigilancehp@hotmail.com.
