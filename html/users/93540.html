Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, and Card Captor Sakura.

Oh my. It's been years -- YEARS -- since I did anything with my account or stories. Except read fanfics and add favorites. I recently just read my old fanfic, The Fourth Potter, and O.M.G. I apparently loved throwing around big words when I was 14, without understanding the proper use of them. And it's unbelievable the number of inconsistencies there were. I cringed, oh yes I did. As popular as that story become, I honestly am sorry to those who had to endure such juvenile and atrocious writing. You all definitely have my gratitude as well as my sincere apologies. :)

I suppose I could give a little update to my profile. I'll keep the previous updates listed below for kicks and giggles. :)

I am 25, a registered nurse, and a poet that is still kicking strong after 13 years. Give or take. I started on this lovely little site when I had just turned 14 years old and was newly introduced to the wonderful world of Harry Potter. There were roughly 13,000 fanfics when I first came to the site. Oh how that fandom has grown tremendously, to 600K! As many people of my generation, I grew up with Harry Potter. And when the series ended in 2007, I felt like it was the final closing chapter to my teenage years. It was time to grow up just as Harry and Co. did. I have my moments where I feel like I want to get back into the groove of fanfic writing, but my muse has been MIA since apparently 2004. Still no sign of it. Nope.

I hope this site will stay around for another decade or so. It is inspiring to see so many people putting their creative juices to work and producing fantastic stories. You can escape to a whole different world, experience new adventures, and discover the characters that will never leave you. The love for reading is a beautiful thing. Let's keep this love alive. :)

I'm just your average teenager (uh, turned adult, age 19) with an endless creative mind. Putting it bluntly, I am absolutely in love with the Harry Potter series, and have a passion for reading Hermione/Severus and Hermione/Tom pairings. After all, great minds think alike. Okay, the end.

8.15.04
The Fourth Potter has been officially discontinued. Like I said, no inspiration. Plus, I have other issues to deal with for this year. You know how they always say that your senior year is supposed to be fun and care-free? Yeah... LIES=P College applications, SAT/ACT, college essays, recommendations, clubs, senior dues. Yeah... Get my point? Anyways, so until furthur notice, everything has been discontinued. Gomen.

4.8.04
Wow.. it's been a long time. Last time I updated The Fourth Potter was five months... GOMENASAI! I'm really sorry, but I think I might be discontinuing this fanfic. I don't have any inspiration for it anymore. I don't have a will to continue to write more for it. I don't understand why.. but it just is. I've been sort of focusing my poetry, short stories, and a --ahem-- pitiful novel. I'm sorry once again. I will try and update.. one day.. but it's not likely. SORRY! And for those that faithfully (haha) reviewed and stuck by me over the past couple of years with that story.. YOU GUYS ARE THE BEST. Without y'all, I don't think I would've ever gotten this far (50,000 words!). I might write a tiny side story (very tiny) to The Fourth Potter that involves the same characters from TFP, which will also be the outcome of the story. AND it will be dedicated you, the reviewers_ My apologies once more.
