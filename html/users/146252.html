About the Author

Okay, so where to begin? I'm a college student from Sacramento, CA who is absolutely inlove with the persona of Draco Malfoy. What can I say... icy eyes, intellegence, manipulation, confused soul, witty, and he smirks! I wish I went to Hogwarts, if only for a day. But don't we all? Anyway, so I've been having difficulty writing my stories. I mean I think I have excellent plots and storylines. At least my frends say so. However I am a conscise writer, so I smash what should be a hundred pages of jucy writing into ten pages of "to the point" narrations. So I hate myself for that. But alas, I will try. So please be patient with me darlings. And if anyone wishes to beta for me, e-mail me. And if anyone wants an idea on something to write about, e-mail me too.

About the Stories

The Avengers (Hr/D): Harry Potter barely escaped with his life after killing the Dark Lord in his seventh year, but is now both Junior Assistant of the Aurors and a diplomatic agent with the Muggle world. Ronald Weasley has become Keeper for the Cannons. Hermione Granger was recently promoted Head of the Muggle Relations Department, a large accomplishment since she was also finishing school in Muggle England as well as Magical America. And what more, Hermione was getting married. Until the Avengers caught her. Now her only hope is her worst enemy.
