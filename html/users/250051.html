Here lies The Crimson Comet (2002-2004). An author of twenty-seven, poorly written, fictions. She was your usual kind of writer: misunderstood, under the age of fifteen, and with too much time on her hands. She was a loyal follower of the Zoids fandom, her OTP being Brad & Naomi (later switching to Raven & Reese). She does not know why she is speaking in third person and will now cut it out.

Okay, guys. Hi. Yes, I took the stories down, it was time to let them go. But to those of you who read my fics, thank you. Thank you, thank you, thank you! I had a lot of fun.

If, for some unknown reason that I cannot even imagine, you absolutely must must have a copy of one of my fics, hit me up at CrimsonComet_FFN@yahoo.com and I will email you a transcript.

Once again, thank you for reading (and enduring) the products of my teenage angst ;)
