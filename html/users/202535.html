Author has written 7 stories for Dark Angel.

I don't think it's necessary to know much about me but since I almost always check a writer's profile before embarking on one of their stories, I figure I shouldn't be hypocritical about this. I'm going into my final year as a law student in the UK, scheduled to graduate in 2007.

My primary fandom is Dark Angel. Let me not get started on its more than premature demise. I'm an M/A shipper, if ever there was one born with the introduction of Jensen Ackles to the show, but do not dislike Logan. I recognise his role on the show as being more than peripheral and certainly more than warranting a quick death just to get him out of a story. Lately I've been warming to the idea of Alec/Sam, if only to explore the possibility of a relationship every bit as complex as that of M/A but for different reasons. Failing that, to reassert the incompatibility of every ship that isn't M/A. I can't really see M/B or M/Z happening because I can't get past the brother business.

Writing status:

Foreseen is still in progress. Thank you to everyone who's still following the story despite my sporadic updates, for your patience. I would like to offer my other writings to you as bribery but they just aren't written in the same style. Except maybe Choices.

Undeserved is also still in progress. This one isn't nearly as complicated as Foreseen in terms of plot etc., but is more so in that it deals with emotions and issues such as betrayal and forgiveness and the effects on relationships. It's semi-autobiographical, but has also been quite fun to write. It doesn't take much thinking in terms of making sure all the plot devices fit together since I've kept it quite simple. I have thrown a few more twists into it than I planned to initially; rest assured a major surprise awaits you all. I anticipate an end soon; though soon does have the potential to stretch!

All the challenge response stories will be clumped together eitheras the rather unimaginatively titled 'Challenge' series, or the more suitably named 'Conversations' series, since they all seem to be. They don't necessarily follow but I feel a need to tie them under one if only to make myself feel like there's more substance to them than they actually turned out with.

Other show pairings:

BtVS - B/A

OTH - Brucas, Naley, Jeyton

The OC - R/M, S/S
