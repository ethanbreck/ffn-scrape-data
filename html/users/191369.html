Hi everyone! I'm Maggie! It's my Newsies name from back in the day. I think most of you have noticed I haven't posted a story in years! I tried writing again, but I'm no writer. I do enjoy a good fic though!

I started writing Newsies fics when I was in high school. I was dealing with a passing of a friend and I found the writing helped me deal with the loss in a positive way. Once I moved on, I kind of forgot about writing. It was for a season in my life. BUT if YOU have a story you want me to read...let me know! I mostly like reading Twilight, Vampire Diaries and Newsies fics, but if you have a story you think I might like...I will try reading it and I will always leave a review! :)
