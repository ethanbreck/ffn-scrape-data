I want to apologize to some of my readers. I've recently deleted the following fics:

1. All You Wanted - Final Fantasy 7 fic. CloTi

2. Ironic - Card Captor Sakura fic. Sakura/Eriol

3. Love by Mistake - Rurouni Kenshin fic. KenKao

4. Love for Sale - Rurouni Kenshin fic. KenKao

I will be revising/re-writing them since reading my old works make my eye itch terribly. Seriously, I gag just trying to finish one chapter. Ugh…

Those stories just simply didn't stand the test of time (at least for me). I just think that...if you like something that's pretty old or vintage...you don't leave it as it is. You check up on it, improve it, give it a new shine, so that it will live longer and be appreciated not only by the old generation but also by the new and younger ones. But of course, I fully intend to leave the heart and soul of the story as it is.

Again, I'm really sorry for those who have invested time and emotion on those aforementioned fics, but I do promise to put them back up in much better condition some time in the future. But in case you want a back-up copy, hit me up and I'll send them right over to you. Hope that's satisfactory for everyone.
