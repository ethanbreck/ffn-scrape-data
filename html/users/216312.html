Author has written 33 stories for Yu-Gi-Oh, Spirited Away, Shaman King, and One Piece.

I'm not particularly interesting--just weird--so I'll just say a bit about my stories.

What to expect from my stories: (also the basic criteria to be on my favorites list)
-Absolutely no shounen/shoujo-ai/yaoi/yuri (despite what some people interpret it as. And my usage of the word “queer” goes by the traditional definition of “strange.” Always.)
-Little or no language (I use none) -Relatively good spelling and grammar
-Originality (I try. I really do.)
-Generally clean all-around (although I’m more lenient on the violence) Also, as this is fanfiction, I'm really lenient about people archiving and such. Go ahead and save to your computer if you feel so inclined, but I generally don't delete fics, even if I don't like them anymore (because I've had a few that I loved disappear and it made me sad) so they should always be there. If, by chance, I should happen to write something good enough to go on a site, just let me know where it is, but you don't really have to ask if you may put it up; I'll always say yes. Drop me a line through a review or a pm.

For the many who asked, the word "Chichiue" (from Memories Too Dark)is Japanese for "father." It's highly respectful and pretty much archaic, so you'll mostly only see it in old samurai movies and stuff. There is one exception on my favorite authors list who has a single shounen-ai story, but the others write purely straight. I do not hate people who write shounen-ai, I just don't write or read it.
