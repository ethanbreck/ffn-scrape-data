I'm a single, 38 year old female who is absolutely obsessed with Labyrinth. I am a huge fan of anything by Jim Henson Productions or the Walt Disney Company. Other than that, I am devoted Christian who wishes I was doing more to lead people to Christ.

My original stories (although only the one-shot is complete) can be found at: http://www.fictionpress.com/alorindanya

Here is my favorite discovery of Laby wittiness by the greatest writer of Labyrinth fan fiction ever, K L Morgan (and if you haven't checked out her Labfics, shame on you):

Give me the fanfic!
Through crashes untold, and aborted page loadings unnumbered,
I have fought my way here, beyond the Movies listing,
To the the Laby fanfiction that's recently updated!
For my download capability is as strong as yours,
And my internet connection as great -
You have no power over my reading choices!

Some of you may know that back in September 2005 I moved to Florida for a job and in February 2007 I purchased my first home, which is why I haven't posted as many chapters as I used to. I love my job (LOOOOVVVEE IT!!) and have been having a lot of fun (who wouldn't, I'm at Walt Disney World every day). I am in no way abandoning TKS; I guess you could say I am trying to make sure that everything that I post is consistent and complete. I'm not one for posting just to please others; if I'm not happy with it, I shouldn't expect others to be.

Thank you to Imanginarium for creating a wonderful sketch based on my story Linda's Wish. She has given me permission to use this picture as the cover picture for the story.

Please visit my pages at and at http://www.youtube.com/profile?user=alorindanya to see things about me if you are interested.
