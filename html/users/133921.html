hello everyone...well, i suppose i should tell you a little bit about myself, eh? HA. The above was written in 2002 I believe, and my how things are changed. I'm still trying to figure out where some of the people have gone, and catching up on angles that I missed so I can understand what some of you are talking about, heh.

So I guess...welcome back to me.
