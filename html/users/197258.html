Author has written 15 stories for Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Battlestar Galactica: 2003, Stargate: SG-1, TV X-overs, and Dollhouse.

I am from Northern Australia (Townsville, QLD) and currently like to work on BSG 2003 Fanfiction. I like to think that I have a knack for realism and for making multiple-crossover stories work and/or mesh together various universes in some kind of logical way. I always try to incorporate things that I haven't seen done anywhere before into my stories and try not to do a loose clone.

I work as an Environmental Scientist and tend to work my knowledge of the environment into my stories somehow. However when doing a space-based mythos (such as BSG) this is sometimes difficult.

The current project I'm working on is Origins of Humanity. I have big plans for that story and hope to continue updating it.
