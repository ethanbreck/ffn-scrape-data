It's been a long while, folks. For reasons unknown to me, some of you seem to not have forgotten about me entirely, despite my unforgivable lack of updates.

I won't bore you guys to death with the story of exactly why I didn't update for such a long time - it would be a nasty story anyways. Alas, I'm still alive, still around. still a pervert and still in love with Dragonball Z though Kyou Kara Maou did capture my interests in between for a while. (if you don't know the series, check it out - I heartily recommend it for all shonen-ai or yaoi-fans out there).

Other than that.. well, there's not really any big news considering my fictions. To be completely honest, I don't know if I'll ever finish them. To those of you who know "In'lar-Anth" - which is sadly not listed on this site anymore - I have bad news - I don't think it'll ever be finished. It was a good story and I'm sad to see the thousands of pages go to waste that existed in raw form - but working on it just seems impossible, since the person I wrote it with and for is long gone and will (hopefully) never be back in my life.

Maybe I will return to it on some later occasion but I highly doubt it.

As for "Schattenseele" (The Darkness Within) and "Kriegsmond", my german fictions - I do intend to hopefully finish Schattenseele at some point - since it was so close to completition. However, it will need a complete revamp beforehand - I get the shivers whenever I look at my poor attempts to write fiction back then.

Other than that - I consider writing a english HP-slash fic (don't lynch me, please!) and maybe retuning to my own, original fantasy world, but if I write someting there, sadly it will be in german.

I don't know wether to ask for emails anymore or not - too many of them have been left unanswered, sadly, due to my personal anxieties. However.. I think I can use a little encouragement to continue Schattenseele aka The Darkness Within and writing in general so.. feel free to write me a few words. And a big sorry to those of you who did and never got an answer. I did however read every single of your mails and treasure them, be assured.

Yours truly half fallen angel

Dark Serapha
