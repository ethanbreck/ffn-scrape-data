Welcome to my fanfiction.net profile page. I hope you'll enjoy the fantiction I am archiving here. It may be old and a little dated, but I hope it brings you pleasure anyway.

Let me say a few things given this opportunity.

First off my name; yes it's unique and that was the most important point of it. Yamcha's Big Pants, has however nothing really do to with Yamcha's underwear, beyond the fact that he's the only reason I would watch DB/DBZ/DBGT.

Secondly, I do appreciate critical responses; i.e I would love for you to review my fics. However I'd like you to keep a few details in mind. I'm Scottish, therefore I write according to Proper English spelling and grammar (when I'm skilled enough or when I'm not making mistakes) Therefore please do not send me rather pompous reviews pointing out mistakes when you write in Americanish. (Sorry to get anyone's back up, but I've had some frustratingly arrogant reviews of this nature.)

Anyway, with that said, I hope you enjoy these fanfics and please bear in mind that I claim no rights over the characters, names, places, or material belonging to the artists/companies of the shows/books/films featured. I do not make any money, profit or otherwise from these stories, they are merely for my own entertainment and that of others.

Update - 19/1/09

Okay, I have a lot of work to do here huh? I haven't updated some of these fics in a long long time. However, I have made it my job to finish "Black Balloon" before the end of the year, which I feel is a realistic target. I want to apologise to everyone for the long haitus on that. I may possibly have lost a few readers in the two years that it wasn't updated. I'm very sorry, because they were loyal and big RxR fans, I really feel as though I've let them down. For those of you who are still bumbling about the ToS archives, thank you. I hope renewed interest in the sequel of the game will draw new people to read this fic. From what I hear there are a few little RxR moments in the game which might entice people to read some fanfiction. Here's hoping.

Thanks
