Author has written 5 stories for Sailor Moon, and Twilight.

Writing has been a dream of mine for many, many years. It started with a co-authorship of a story in a story forum over ten years ago. It is safe to say that writing fan fiction has taught me a few things... particularly that the English language is complex, and people should treat it with respect. That being said, one can only learn if they practice. That's what I feel fan fiction does - it exercises the mind through the creative process and utilizes the writing rules of the Golden Grammar Gods. I do not regret writing fan fiction, but I do realize that it is only a stepping stone. The true test is to actually write something original. But, it is nice to practice with someone else's characters from time to time.

As with all good things, I've moved on with life. Thanks for the memories ff.net!
