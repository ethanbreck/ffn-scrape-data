- ON HIATUS -
to be entirely honest.. i don't have the time for this. i really wish i did though! i'm not one of those people who totally reject this place now that i'm getting older and have different priorities. i definitely wish i could keep my creative juices going alongside school and sports, but it's just not happening. not to mention, i wasn't getting enough reviews when i would update to keep my spirits up and give me the boost to write more often. so, for now i must depart. i'll try and get back on here and continue and possibly finish some of my stories in the summer but who knows what my situation will be like then either. so this is goodbye for the time being. thank you to all who have read and reviewed my stories! you've been great :) until next time..
-brooklynbridge
