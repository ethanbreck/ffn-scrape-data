April 09: I know, I'm terrible. I've been getting a lot of reviews and pm's lately and I just wanted to put this up in case anyone actually remembered me. I really am busy almost all 24/7; I'm finishing my first year on my second career, Fashion Design, and that plus being on a dance company takes most of my time. It's almost impossible for me to sit and write, the urges do come but I find myself with designs to complete or homework left to do, so the words just are locked up in my head...

About two weeks ago I sat down and read all of my work and thanks to that I reminded myself how much I actually miss writing, so, because of that, I vowed to at least write either one shots or continue with my drabbles. I can't promise I'll continue the multichaptered stories, simply because I really don't have the time. Thank you ALL so much for reading, and I especially want to thank the always great Simply Sarah and .aprimavista. cause they ROCK in my mind, they're totally the best writers here, and I still consider them my friends even with all the years and miles that puts us apart.

Love always,

Kath
