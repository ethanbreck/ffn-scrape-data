Author has written 15 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, Slam Dunk, Lovely Complex, Wallflower, Kimi ni Todoke/君に届け, and Kuroko no Basuke/黒子のバスケ.

"There is no such thing as right or wrong side...
Just two sides holding different views."

--Squall Leonhart (Final Fantasy VIII)

I am also writing for Ao3 under the name VanillaDaydreams.

Birthday: 75 years 2 months and 12 days after the Titanic sank
Occupation: big-time sleeper

How time flies. I've grown a lot taller and perhaps wiser since I first started writing for this website. I'm still fond of video games and anime, although now, I am losing friends who share the same interests as mine. I missed writing, I would love to write again but I can't seem to get a firm hold of the idea that I want to pursue. Perhaps, it is because I wasn't trying hard enough and because I've got so much to do that I don't get to straighten my focus on the task at hand. Anyway, I'd like to thank you for taking the time to read my works.

Tools of the trade: black ball pen, pc, hive mind, bed, sappiness, incompetence, broken english, old notebooks and lots of courage.

Interests: books, writing, fanfictions, newspapers, movies, internet, video games, and ah, bishounens!

My favorite pairs:

Squall/Rinoa (Final Fantasy VIII)- My first love. Always my first love. (FFVIII is the only game that I've REALLY played and the only game that I've really paid attention to.) I've been loving and breathing this pair for 7 years and up to this day, I don't feel like stopping. The only ironic thing is that, I haven't written a fic for them. I've contemplated a plot or two but then, I always lose the intention to write it all down. Probably because the game ended blissfully for them.

Seifer/Quistis (Final Fantasy VIII)- Another pair in FFVIII oozing with chemistry. They're quite lovable, this two, and there's just a lot of potential and anything could become possible given the kind of relationship they have for each other.

Seifer/Rinoa (Final Fantasy VIII)- a bit twisted pair, yes, but come to think of it... Life would have been equally fantastic if they got the chance to be together.

Sendoh/Rukawa (Slam Dunk)- Bishounen for an equally promising bishounen. They're polar opposites, but then, they can be quite similar in countless ways. The pairing that started my love for shounen-ai.

Yuuri/Wolfram (Kyou Kara Maou!)- Ah, they're simply very cute and adorable. I wish I have more splendid moments with this pair in the original series.

Risa/Otani (Lovely Complex)- and so I thought I'm not gonna be able to write fanfics again after my desire for SenRu has sort of mellowed. Then I stumbled on this anime series and my love for fic writing was revived! I so adore this series very much for so many reasons. One maybe because I am just like Risa. I'm tall, practically boyfriendless since birth, and I have fallen in love for guys shorter than myself. Anyway, I just love Otani's character. And the plot gave me a hope of finding true love.. =)

Kyohei/Sunako (Perfect Girl Evolution/Wallflower) - I looove them!! Though I was kinda disappointed when the anime ended up as dry as that.. I mean, how come they never get together when it was just so obvious that THEY SHOULD be together?? Arggh! Plus, I really have a thing for Kyohei, he's sooo droolworthy!!

There's just a lot of wonderful pairings out there and I do not mind going astray sometimes...

'Every motive is disarmingly beautiful... if viewed in the right perspective.'
