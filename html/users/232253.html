Author has written 6 stories for Star Wars, and Jedi Apprentice.

I'm a Star Wars fan by trade, a reader by frustration and a fanfiction writer out of love. In other words, I love the idea of Star Wars, but frustration with the latest embodiments of the series led to me writing my own versions. Maybe someday I'll branch off into some other fandoms, but for now I'm happy writing my first love :)

ETA: Wow, it's hard to believe it's been many, many years since I first wrote this profile! In the passing years, I've moved away from Star Wars (and moved through several states here in the U.S.) and have started some work in other fandoms. While I am still working on REDACTION, I find myself cringing whenever I even think of going back and reading some of my earlier stories. I guess we all have to grow up someday- and maybe someday I'll try to go back and make them a bit less... overdone.

Stories:

Price of Sacrifice: A monster story that was my first ever.

Why Live Life from Dream to Dream: A story that is equal parts angst and sorrow.

One Day More: A chance to do things over.

Obi-Wan Kenobi and the Big Bad Bug: Who doesn't hate creepy crawly things?

The Revenge of Obi-Wan: Qui-Gon and his pathetic life forms becomes a bit too much for an over-wrought apprentice.

Star Wars Redaction: Take a right turn at Geonosis and this is where you end up. This story is NOT COMPLETE

A quick note: I love reviews, even if I don't always get the chance to answer. Please leave me an email or someway to contact you, as I'd like to express appreciation for your thoughts!
