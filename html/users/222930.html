Hey! My name's Sarah and I love writing(obviously the reason I'm here). I'm 23 and have been writing short stories since I was a kid. Everyone told me my stories were pretty good...so here I am, hopefully I can make something I can be proud of and is enjoyable. A little bit about my home life...I live with a big family, we're poor and whatnot, so I make a lot of characters based on my own experiences, and not having the luxury of the 'higher' classes is a major contributor. In general I'm a very shy person, though online my 'true self' is better portrayed. This is also a characteristic several of my characters will pertain.

Besides writing, I do read and draw. Most of it being anime, but recently I've been drawing more realistic things. But this is besides the point, and has no relation to writing. I just like to brag ;)

Like I said I''ve been writing stories since I was a kid, and poems the odd time. They were based mostly on horror since I loved those R.L Stine books. Another series of books I read were animorphs, which catapulted me into fantasy related books.The last half decade or so I've been reading fantasy - Dragonlance, Forgotten Realms, etc., which has influenced my preference in writing stories, more often than not that are planned to be fantasy related.

Though nothing's up yet by yours truly, I swear I'll get there soon. I have a ton of stories in mind, I just need to get them straight. Don't expect anything too special however, this is, afterall, a fanfiction site. So it will most likely turn out I'll be swaying towards writing stories for Forgotten Realms and Dragonlance, with my own original characters instead of the beloved characters of well-known authors. I don't like using others characters, it's just something that's a big no-no for me. In no way am I insulting others using already created profiles, in fact I enjoy reading some great fanfics here, but it's a choice I've made.

Hopefully I haven't bored you to death, or at the very least caused a half vacant, open-mouthed stare at the absolute nonsense I manage to come up with. If anyone's interested in my writing (if I ever write something), or has questions about anything to do with such, you can find my email somewhere around here...
