I'm nearly in high school, and I've been writing for less than a year (So yes, my fics need looots of polishing and CC is greatly needed!) I love anime/manga and reading. When I'm not doing homework or projects I chat on the Internet, read fics, write, learn Elvish and Japenese and mix them together with Spanish and English(fuun!) and play my flute or piano ^_^ My favorite anime/mangas include Inu Yasha, Gundam Wing, Rayearth, Fushigi Yugi, and Ranma 1/2.
