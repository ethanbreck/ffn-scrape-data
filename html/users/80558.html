My stories-
Harry Potter and the Founder's Legacy - Sorry guys, my muse has left for greener pastures.

The Tale of Five- See above.

I do a lot of Ron stuff, for three reasons. One, he is by far my favorite character, and I really do not believe he will switch over like Pettigrew. If anything, it will be like I said, Harry Potter will need a Secret Keeper, no one will want Ron to do it cause they remembered Pettigrew, but Ron does it anyway and ends up saving Harry. My second reason for doing Ron stuff is that I can slip into his psyche a lot easier than any other characters. Sirius Black is the second easiest. Prongs and Lupin are virtually impossible and Hermione, forget it. She's the type I've been trying to prevent myself from turning into my whole life, and I won't start now. Ron is almost exactly like me, mentally. That's my third reason.

"Falling didn't sound so bad when there was someone there to catch me"

-Me

I hate to say it, but I have had no interest in writing anymore, though I still love to read, everything and anything. I've got stories in my head, but I can't usually get them down on paper right, except for emotional/angsty short stuff. Most of my stuff is pre-OOTP, when we had no clue what sort of background Sirius had. OOTP didn't really inspire me much except of course for Sirius's death, which I think has been waaaay overdone at this point. SO, after HBP comes out, we'll see what I've got up my sleeve.

In the meantime, I'm a freshman in college now and haveso much chemistry/biology/english that I am finishing about one book every four weeks, instead of my previous four books in one week. (No wonder my muse has given up.)
