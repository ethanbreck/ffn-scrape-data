Hello, everyone! Been here since I was only 11!!! (Over 10 years ago!!) :o) Hope you enjoy my stories... they're probably all in the fantasy genre... it's my specialty ;o)

I love witches and magic and am always looking to find the strongest and most intriguing ones. My absolute favorite witch is Constance Hardbroom, whom I believe does everything properly--the double finger spell casting, side saddle broomstick riding, straight crisp hat, etc.

If you enjoy a story, please let me know by commenting!! I enjoy critique as well, but only if it's well-founded.

If you like what you see, please tell your friends to read as well! I love sharing my work with others who appreciate it!
