Updated 01/02/2006
Right. So it seems I haven't updated this in just over a year, and before that, I hadn't updated it in a year and a half. Oh well, I'm here now at least. So what's changed in the past year? Not much. The basics about me are, I'm from the UK, I turn 19 in April, and I'm working on getting through university (who'da thought I'd ever've got through college =P). My BTTF fic, Time Watch, is dead. Aaages ago, I was going to update it, but really, honestly, it's crap. I still like the general premise, but it requires a TOTAL rewrite. Which, having long ago excised the plot bunny, I'm totally uninspired to do. So it'll probably stay dead, not that anyone reading this probably cares at this point.

Since my last update however, I have written a small Stargate blurb. A missing scene of sorts for something that always bugged me about Season 3's Point of View. That was ages ago though, and in recent news, I've just spat out a Smallville alternate scene too. I really hated the way they did the 100th ep, Reckoning. I understand why they did it, it was a means to an end, but I don't like the ends they're after. So, I rewrote one key scene the way I believe it should've gone. I doubt I'll follow it up with anything, but hey, the muse can strike at any time right? So, you never know.

Right now I'm on my first break from Uni that doesn't come with any deadlines attached juust on the other side, so I'm free and clear for now. Hence the new ficlet, and my catching up with the stories that've been growing while I've been away. And hence my updating this profile, apparently, because this is just about the time of year I updated it last time too, give or take a week.

I guess that's it, anyway. Have a good one,

Stoko.
