Okay, so obviously my writing has slowed down a lot since I first started posting here. I know I've already lost a lot of readers, but this is a note for those of you who are still with me. A Legacy Left Behind: The next chapter is in progress though not nearly going so well as I would hope. I'm having a bit of trouble hammering out all the little bits of the plot that worked their way in without presenting a resolution to all the problems they caused.

Against the Odds: As soon as I find a song to fit the next chapter, this'll be continued.

At the Heart of it All: Ummm... I promise I'm trying!!!! I have an outline for where I want it to go and everything, but... I dunno. It's being difficult right now. I have the first part of the next chap. written but can't get any further than that. /sighs/ We'll see...

Lightning Strikes Twice: Same basic problem as AtHoiA. I have the whole story outlined (with a sequel!), but it doesn't move from the part I'm currently working on.

Lights Go Out: Just finished Ch. 9 (obviously. /grins/). It's actually starting to wrap up a bit, even if it doesn't look like it right now. Ch.10 is the ever-important connection chapter that just seems to be evading me right now. I know what happens beyond that but just need to get to it first.

(Untitled for now): I have a Gravitation fic! /dances/ The first chapter is done and every following chapter planned. There'll only be 5 total, for anyone who wants to know. It's the basic premise of one of them leaves the other and they both pine away for a while before realizing that they actually belong together. Overdone, yes, but still fun. I want to make a bit more progress on it before I start posting it, but it will be coming soon. Promise!

Okay, and that's all of them. Hopefully all of you reading this will stick with me, have patience with me. I may be a bit slow, but I don't give up! /laughs/ 'Til next time! /waves/
