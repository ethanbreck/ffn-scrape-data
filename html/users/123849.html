Also, apologies for the Red Dwarf reference that slipped in at the end. I'd just finished watching "Only the Good" and there we go-- it just fit with what I was writing. I'm not terribly fond of the title, but it's the one that worked best. Do I think this is possible? Yes. Unlike much of the other stuff I write, I think this could actually have happened. Well... 50/50.
