12/1/06

Wow. This thing still exists. o_O;

Um...yeah. Don't shoot me. Not that anyone cares about my stuff anymore anyway.

So. It's been...four years since I put anything here. I haven't written any fanfics between now and then. Ideas have come and gone, but none of them could be brought to life. Plus, I was getting rather discouraged about the fact that I could always start a story, get a couple chapters in, but I could never finish it. It's disappointing. I've thought about just seeing if I could write a whole story so that I would have something to post when people ask for more. But I just get so happy when I finish a chapter that I want to share it with everyone.

I'm a sophomore in college now. Last time I posted I was...a sophomore in high school? Nice. XD

Anyway. I just read Twilight and New Moon by Stephenie Meyer, and it's just begging me to write a fanfic. There's an idea forming in my head, but I don't have a specific plot yet. I think I might just sit down and write whatever comes to mind, instead of laboring over making an elaborate plot and trying to stick to it. That might work better.

Haha. I thought that when I came back here I'd be totally embarassed by my older writings. I'm slightly embarassed, but not mortified like I expected. I have a desire to delete all my old stuff and get a fresh start, but... I dunno. If at least two people enjoyed them enough to leave reviews, then I don't have as much reluctance to leave it up here. There was some stuff I just couldn't bear to leave up though. It truly was embarassing. XD Cell Phone of Time... I ain't so sure about that either. That may go soon, but I'll save it to my computer so I can upload it again someday... Yeah, right. I probably never will. XD;

That's that. If you wanna know more about me and what I've been up to in this crazy-long downtime, check out my journal linked above.
