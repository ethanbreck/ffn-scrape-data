~~ Friday, March 23, 2007 ~~

Hey,

Wow, it seems like it has been forever since I’ve updated my bio page. Two years may not be forever, but I guess it could be considered pretty close.

Well, even though I’ve been pretty lacking in updating this page, I swear I have not been a total slacker in regards to my stories. And, although everything is probably a few years in the making, I have also started working on my first book. It will be a young-adult fantasy, and before everything is said and done the story will be a trilogy.

In regards to my fanfiction, I have already started working on editing A Weekend to Remember. The revision of chapter one was posted back in October of 2006, but FFN did not show it as an update since I only replaced a chapter instead of adding-a-new chapter. Yes, technicalities are a nuisance. So, hardly anyone knows that A Weekend to Remember was updated. The revision of chapter two is actually complete. It just needs a few final touches before sending it off to my BETA. Chapter three is about 50 percent complete.

I recently completed a Trunks/Pan one-shot. It just needs to be re-sent to my BETA, since she accidentally deleted it when she went to save it to her computer. I hope that’s not a bad sign.

Chapter five of To Snag a Saiyan is still in the works, and I still have another Trunks/Pan fic, The Ties that Bind that I began a couple summers ago while I was still in school that I’ve never completed or posted because it is turning out to be longer than I originally intended. Le sigh.

And, for any Ranma ½ fans out there, I have the prologue to a Ranma/Akane story also in the works. But, it is still going to be a while before that one sees the light of day.

Well, that’s where I am with all my stories. I will probably my updating this page more often now, since FFN seems unable to show when my stories are being updated. So, start checking my bio for updates and notices.

Bye,
Carebear-chan
