A little about me:

I'm a 19-year-old university student from the Philippines, currently enrolled in B.S. Management of Applied Chemistry. It's tough but it's fun as well, although I really wanted to take up Journalism or Communications. Of course my Chinese family would have to disagree with pursuing my dreams in favor of picking out something that would ensure a future filled with cash, but I'm not complaining. Life is too short to complain about anything, so I'm just enjoying it.

For my readers:

Thank you for the reviews, messages, comments. I would appreciate it further if you could give me some constructive criticisms. This is because I have not written anything creative in a long time, and I am still working on rekindling whatever’s left of my creative writing skills through fan fiction. Ideas don’t flow as easily as they used to, and I feel very, very old and rusty.

Basically, I'm back because I dug up the old notebooks I wrote stories in and read the fan fiction I wrote way back in grade school. I felt a little nostalgic and they have triggered my passion for writing which had been lying in dormancy for years.
