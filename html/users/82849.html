Well, enough rambling... Maybe I should actually do what this space wants me to do...Ok, I'm into werewolves although I'm not currently writing anything about them. My current obsession is Rodney Skinner from the League of Extraordinary Gentlemen movie... He is soooooo awesome! I went through the movie and captured a whole bunch of screenshots of him since I wasn't all that pleased with the lack of those I found online. For any of you other Skinner fans out there, feel free to visit my photobucket:

Otherwise, I like Extreme Ghostbuster fics, especially about Eduardo. Eduardo/Kylie, though not TOO prominent, are always the best. I'm into Big Wolf on Campus. MERTON IS SO COOL! As you can see below, I wrote a fic about the episode "Everybody Fang Chung Tonight". It will probably be the only totally romance fic I ever write. I just felt really really sorry that Merton couldn't get the girl. I'm currently into Kim Possible. Ron RULES! I like K/R fics every once and a while but the really Out of Character mushyness is really bad. Put them in character for crying out loud! I like the Animorphs though there are so many stories posted that it would take me forever to find ones I like. It's a little embarasing but I have to admit that I like The Mummy: Secrets of the Medji TV series. I keep getting ideas for a story staring Jonathan (my fave character). I'm planning on writing a fic but I don't know whether to post it under The Mummy Movie catagory or Misc Cartoons... Oh well, I'll worry about that when I actually write it.

I am sorry to discontinue my Everworld fic. I started it thinking that I had a really cool idea on how they communicated their stories to the old world through K.A. Applegate but I just didn't have the time. Maybe just for the hey of it I'll continue it but it won't have the same stamina as it started out with. On a side note, the catagory is starting to die. IF YOU LIKE EVERWORLD START THINKING ABOUT WRITING A FIC!

Overall, one of my favorite story plots is when the character is changed into something: body-switching, shape-shifting(werewolves, vampires), mutations, spell-casting, being turned into something period!
