5/26/10 - I live.

Hello! I stumbled upon this site very long ago. I've been lurking around ever since, and have just gotten the nerve to start posting the stories I've written. I got two up, check em out if you like.

Feel free to ever IM me on AIM, My screenname is Mellowpenelo. I love random conversation.

By the way, I'm a complete dork.

Here's the original summery for it (way more than 255 characters, gah).

Comfort in Surreality

“After a series of chance encounters, and one of Kisame’s more capricious decisions, a pink haired girl is the newest tool at the Akatsuki compound. With Hidan performing bloody rituals in the backyard, Itachi being… well, Itachi, Zetsu giving mixed messages and Sasori commenting that she’d make a great doll, Kisame is finally considered normal. Unfortunately, he’s also getting attached to the normality … or is it the girl?”

This fic is more light hearted than Ways and Means, and should have more humor.

Also, it's semi A/U. I started the story back when the manga revealed very little about Akatsuki, so it won't be following the canon on that. Not completely, anyway. I've twisted canon facts to suit the plot I have in mind, and thrown in my own hooha.

The first few chapters go in segments and then go into a steady time line. They also deal with Sakura more than Kisame. Her growth and development and all that. Anyway, I really love writing Kisame's character (the way I envision him, anyway).

By the way, the romance is going to take awhile. Sakura's still a kid (Fourteen) right now. It's not that kind of fic.

Chapter 2 is up - This chapter mostly focused on Sakura, on her growth. You know how Sakura vows to change herself, and doesn’t actually do it till the time skip (even then Yamato is always pulling her out of danger)? Well, in this fic she commits to changing herself after her little epiphany in the Forest of Death. Awesome opossum?

I just intend for her to be more mature and be more capable than Kishimoto makes her out to be. I’m not making her super women, but making her utilize the abilities Kishimoto himself has given her (he frustrates me to no end, giving Sakura strength and then having her get knocked out or watch from the sidelines). So there you go.

Lots of reviewers for this fic, Thanks so much!

Ways and Means


"The technological advances of her planet were renowned throughout the universe, Frieza himself was their greatest ally. For those reasons, a five year war with the Saiyans was not so arduous. So now, how exactly did Bulma end up married to their Prince?"

This fic is way to intense for it's own good. Geez, why do Bulma and Vegeta have to be so damn explosive? Well, I'm addicted to love/hate relationships. However, I really have to get myself in this certain mindset to write for those emotional scenes. I have a bunch of plot, twists and turns set up for this, so I refuse to EVER abandon this. Got a notebook chalk full of everything that will happen to these two kiddos. Though maybe for my first fic I should of just posted a oneshot... Well, hope you people enjoy the product of my toils. ;p

By the way, I'm sorry for any confusion on the plot so far. Everyone just has to remember that Vegeta and Bulma have very different perspectives on the war, and the past in general. Fear not! All will be revealed in due time. The lovin' is also on it's way. Just stay with me, people.

I really love all the reviewers for this fic. They give me meaty reviews, nice paragraph ones. Gah, I live off those, so a big thank you to all them.
