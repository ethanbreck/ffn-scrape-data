Time to update this mutha! I think I actually have spent more time over the years (ick, years!) ignoring this account than anything. And now I'm about to enter the real world and...yeah. But I'm back, baby, and so is my muse. (hugs muse) I have to say that I am BEYOND thrilled with the responses I've been getting on my newest string of fiction (if you're scratching your head in confusion, it's because I've deleted a lot of old stuff from this account a while back). But, OMFG, you guys are awesome for reviewing so much! It really makes me feel happy and spurs me to want to continue my writing. I've found a niche! (niche! niche!) It makes me so happy. And COMEDY of all things - who knew? (Well, my past roommates, but I always figured they were damn liars). So, yeah, for right now, I am mainly a LazyTown ficcer. Oh, so much love do I have for that show. My sense of humor is a little "adult", so to speak, so my fics will probably stay in the "T" range. Normally, I could do worse than that with my jokes, but I'm not sure if I'd feel comfortable doing that for a NickJr show. (blushes) For those of you that wanted to know, I have a list of fandoms I suppose that I belong to (I'm a fangirl of a bunch more than this). I don't read/write fics in all of them, but I'm a geek about each one of them online: LazyTown Harry Potter I used to be UBER SailorMoon, so I think it still counts Rayearth (same as above) The 10th Kingdom The Secret Garden Peter Pan (2003 film) Lord of the Rings Serenity (George Lucas who?) Fairy Tales (original grisleyness) Phantom of the Opera etc etc etc

UPDATE: So, I'm all graduated from university and am currently in transition mode. I haven't been able to catch the new episodes of LazyTown yet but I plan to. I heard those writers took some inspiration from me. ;) Once I get around to seeing some episodes again I promise I will have a new episode of my own written for you all. Not just because you write me such wonderful reviews but because I get as much of a kick out of writing them as you get from reading them. Stay healthy, LazyTowners!
