3/18/11 o.o wow been a while. So... yeah last time I uploaded anything I think I was 17... I'm 24 now. I still write from time to time but its almost all original stuff now. I draw more now than I write. Anyone who had been wondering about me: I'm an adult now with a full time job and trying to balance my time with art and spending time with my girlfriend. For those who had ever wondered ^^; I'm actually a guy. Over the summer I'm going to endeavor to re-write some of the stuff I had posted. Back in the day I just had too many ideas in my warped little brain to ever get down a completed story. But I still stand that I don't want anyone else finishing something I started. I write in spurts. If you're interested in my art I go by icemagepathfinder on dA.

I think most people who had read my stuff kept in contact with me when I vanished. Like I said, I'm usually around on dA when I'm not busy staffing anime cons or working on cosplay... or getting mauled by cats. I also collect dollfies now. My go-to anime sadly is no longer Ronin Warriors (though I still love it); it is now One Piece.
