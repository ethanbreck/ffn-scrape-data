Author has written 19 stories for Greek Mythology, Star Wars, Dragon Age, Mass Effect, Hellboy, Misc. Movies, Elder Scroll series, and Star Wars.

-I've been writing since the very beginning. In fact, my very first story was Star Wars fan fiction. I wrote myself into the story and married Luke Skywalker. So basically I haven't changed since I was 5.

-I do write original fiction (in fact I'm sitting on a novel if anyone's interested...) but fan fiction is just too much fun.

-I'm not old enough to be your mother, but I am probably old enough to be your cool aunt who always gets sauced at family weddings and argues with your racist great-uncle.

Note: My Mass Effect 2 stories should be read in this order--"Dosed", "Probed", and "Dated". Then, "Tonight and Tomorrow", "Tender Prey", "The Sea", and "Shadows".

Concerning Shadows: I started writing Shadows pretty immediately following the release of Mass Effect 2, before I was even finished with my first play-through. (Incidentally, this is why people like Kasumi are missing: She hadn't come out yet!) Of course, now that ME 3 has been released, my series of stories has become, uh, AU, I guess. This wasn't my intention, but hey, it happens. Please read my ME2 stories, including Shadows, with the understanding that the events of ME3 don't occur in this timeline (thankfully).

UPDATE!

For some awful reason, the site has made most of my line-breaks disappear. Like, in all 15 fics. So if you are noticing an annoying lack of breaks between passages...don't worry, I'm working on it. Or rather, I will be working on it, once I find about 8 hours of time to save and re-upload literally everything.

Currently Working On:

Dynamics and Diversions- In which I explore the psychlogical consequences of the, uh, arrangement between the Inquisitor and the Iron Bull. Don't worry about judging me, I'm already judging myself.

The Great Cold Distance- A follow up to The Force Awakens. Heavy on the Kylofeels. Lotsa angst and Jedi stuff.
