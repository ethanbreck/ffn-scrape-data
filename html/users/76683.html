A new name - a new beginning. uh...yeah. No, its just that I was doing asearch and noticed that five other people have the same name as me. And that's no way to be on this website.
Hesper means "evening star" in Greek, and as I am a quarter greek i thought it would be appropriate. The other three quarters, in case you wer wondering is Polish. And as I live in Poland I do not have access I would have had (like tv shows or books) had I stayed in the USA instead of moving when I was four. Now I'm sixteen, you do the math. Needless to say I have lived in Poland for a very long time.
I like X-men (evolution, mostly), Harry Potter, Animorphs and whatever else happens to be invading my mind at the time. You should also know that I am a sappy romantic at haeart and I have a tendedncy to latch on to a particular couple and become a die-hard fan of them. So my fics are really for all you romantic obsessors out there. Another you should know is my laziness...if you want another chapter, you have to bug me for it, there's no other way to get it.
