Current Fics and Status Updates:

MASS EFFECT:

Aconcagua Dawn- The Series: What was meant to be twelve episodes might just end at two. I began this series after the release of the first game, full of crazy ideas and reckless, wild abandon. Some seventy-thousand words and five years later and I'm just now putting the finishing touches on Episode Two. Too many deployments, too much time having passed, too many striking differences between what I wrote and what the canon became; whatever the reason, these last chapters of Episode Two have gotten zero response, and frankly, I think that's fair. If I close up shop on this, though, I'll start looking into different stories I'd like to tell in the ME Universe.
