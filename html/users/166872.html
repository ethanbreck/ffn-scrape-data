Writing Info

Hello all! I finally decided to update this bio...

Well, anyways, I just seem to be writing one-shots nowadays. Quite a few will be inspired by challenges on the temps_mort community, for those of you familiar with LiveJounal. I've also been expanding my fandoms, so you can expect a lot of random fandoms from me. Some include:

- Weiss Kreuz (original, Gluhen, and Side B universes)
- Gravitation
- X
- Final Fantasy (VII, VIII, and IX (finally bought 9!))
- Harry Potter (possible, but not likely)
- Gundam:SEED
- Cardcaptor Sakura

Well, I think that's it. Let it be known that I'm sometimes random... I haven't written random insanity in awhile though, I hope I haven't lost my touch 0_0.

And the unfinished fics that are up here? Consider them as on hiatus until I find them ^^;;; We moved awhile ago, and I haven't gone through my papers to find the written out versions or the plot notes yet. This is also true for Hyne's Beginning, the first real serious fic that I wrote. That one is not up anymore, taken down months ago to get rewritten. But I thought I'd let you know ^_^

And one final thing. I am a fan of yaoi/yuri pairings. I write them, and I read them. But basically, if a fic is well written, I enjoy it, regardless the subject matter. I do still write "straight" pairings at times, and support them. The fact of the matter is that I am also very supportive of the "Love knows no gender" idea. I understand if you don't like that, but please don't flame me for something you don't like.

Well, I think that's it. Don't really know though, it's kinda late to be thinking...
