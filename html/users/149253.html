I study a lot. However, I also procrastinate a lot. I love to write, I love to read, and I especially love FanFiction. There is just something nice about finishing your favorite episode and logging on here to see what someone else would like to see. Fan fiction also allows us all to extend unfinished series, cancelled tv shows and give sequels to movies. Sometimes, the happy ending isn't all you wanted, and at least you can rely on FF to help pave way to the ending you wanted. Also... who ever said literacy was lost in America, hasn't been on here. We might not read traditional books, but we read damn it! WE READ!

I was once a very diligent reviewer, and I was prized of my efforts to be thorough, but I have also been in school for the last couple years trying to be an "educated" individual and so I have been slacking on the reading, the reviewing and the whole Life Having thing, but I am trying to be better rounded and be less stressed, which means I will be here more! Reading! Yeh!
