If you have a chance, I'd love to hear what you think. Thanks!

UPDATES (as of 09/29/12):

So I decided to post the current status for my incomplete stories. Please feel free to drop my a message if you have comments or questions.

Bitter Ashes (Star Trek 2009) - *sigh* Best laid plans. At the moment, I've completely lost my Star Trek muse. I'm not giving up on this one, but it's been agonizingly slow going.

Burgeoning Affections/ Restless Soul (Batman/Ironman) - My muses all decided to run off without me, apparently. I have gotten this one back, at least, but whatever road trip they were on darkened her outlook a bit, and the new chapters I've written don't fit in with the original. I'm in the process of rewriting this sucker.

Cold of Winter (TMNT) - I'm still playing around with this one, but only really in bits and pieces. There is still a good chance that this will eventually be completed, though.

Spectacular Ignorance (Avengers) - I know this is technically listed as complete, but I'm actually working on expanding it a bit. We'll see how it goes. I'm working on a big bang story at the moment for November, so probably nothing on this before December at the earliest.

Though the World Perish (Naruto) - Eh. I like my premise on this one, and already have an ending semi-written, but it would take a while to get there from where I am. I'm not going to say it will never happen, but I wouldn't bother holding my breath.

Words of the Day (Various) - At the moment, there's too many other things on my plate to even think of these. Still, you may see some pop up here and there if I'm needing to play around/experiment with a character or thought.

Fade to Black (Harry Potter) - Similar status as "Though the World Perish".

Spirits of the Past (Inuyasha) - It is supremely unlikely that this will be picked up again.

Lords of Darkness (WWE) - This one isn't happening. I lost my WWE muse years ago, and I just don't see it coming back.
