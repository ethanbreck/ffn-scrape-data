This Riks

PM . Follow . Favorite
Joined 04-01-02, id: 193140

The name's Riks. 'This Riks' was inspired by Lord Sesshomaru from Inuyasha who sometimes referred to himself as 'kono Sesshomaru' or 'this Sesshomaru.' If Sess-sama says 'this,' Riks says 'this.'

Riks' computer now works...yay. Tenka Hadou no Ken comes out in less than a month! Riks is ecstatic beyond words.

I watched Guren no Houraijima after I downloaded the torrent. Lovely, but no Tenka Hadou no Ken. Though, I loved seeing Sesshomaru pwn Kyoura with "Souryuuha!" Twice. Oh yes, Riks was happy. Riks was squeeing with joy.

I'm still feeling uninspired. I'm forcing myself to accept Sesshomaru/Kagura as canon, and I'm okay with that. However, it doesn't help that she dies...thus any possible relationship between the two is made impossible. What I don't accept is Sesshomaru/Rin. She's a child. Even when she does age, I don't see the possibility of romance between the two. Personally, I hope Takahashi-san never, never decides to go in that direction. If she evers finishes Inuyasha...

I've dropped the Sesshomaru!daughter idea. In the end, my heart wasn't in it. So, goodbye Serouyuu.

I need to brainstorm plot ideas. Right now, I've got nothing. I don't want to stray from canon, and that's what makes it so hard to write a Sesshomaru story that isn't a filler Inuyasha episode. All the good story ideas have been taken. Not fair. -_-

If you ever come across a criticism of mine, be warned. I don't care if you respond to it in an intelligent manner. But please, please, don't start up the "I luv Sesshomaru mor thn u!111" statements, because frankly, I don't care. To be candid, I don't believe that. I have yet to find anyone who obsesses over him as much as I do.

And for the crazy-ass Inuyasha fanbrats: Tessaiga is Inuyasha's sword. Inu no Taisho is a title, not Inu-Papa's name. Sesshomaru does not, for the love of my sanity, DOES NOT have his tail around his right shoulder! That is Mokomoko-sama, the piece of loose fur over his right shoulder in his true form. He has a tail. It's attached to his butt.

The Inuyasha fandom is insane. The majority of it, or rather, 99 percent of it, are idiotic thirteen-year-olds who watch only the English dub. No offense to thirteen-year-olds, because honestly, I've talked with numerous younger people, and they're some of the brightest, most pleasant internet-goers I know. But the Inu fandom is crazy. The fangirls need to be slapped. Many, many times. You morons give the intelligent Inu-lovers a bad name. I can't go anywhere on Neopets without having to defend myself because I like Inuyasha. Do us and yourselves a favor: stop writing insulting fanfiction. It breaks my brain, it irks me to no end, and I'm tired of defending the fandom. Why don't you remove yourselves from the fandom?

As for any Sesshomaru fanfictions in existance I have been too lazy to snark: for his lordship's sake, stop writing him ooc. I cannot stand this. If you can't write Sesshomaru, and you're too lazy to study the canon, don't write him. Good lord, if I were Rumiko Takahashi, I'd be incredibly offended at how Sesshomaru is canon-raped in nearly every story. Leave him alone. He didn't do anything to you. Leave Jaken, Rin, and Ah-Un alone as well. Do not touch the Sesshomaru-gumi. Take your Sesshomaru-loving Sues, and get the heck out of the Sengoku Jidai!
