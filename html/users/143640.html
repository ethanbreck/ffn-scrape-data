Author has written 8 stories for Harry Potter.

I thought a change of name and approach were in order. I will be going with my strengths, and writing one-shotters. They are my favourites, they inspire more people to write more, and they are the most fun. Very few of them fit together (though I am working on something that's *almost* a sequel to "Try not To Hate Him"), but taken on their own, they make Hogwarts that little bit more *edgy*. I love my one shots dearly, and that will be most, if not all, of what I write now.My longer fictions remain, sadly unfinished, as pieces of nostalgia, and also in the hope that I will one day fulfill my promise and finish them, because the're not that bad, and they deserve conclusion. But not now.

My stories are rarely explicit, never slash (just not my bag, baby!), always dark and usually tragedy-related, because the inevitable consequences of the Magical civil war that is approaching Hogwarts make for better reading than "Oh look, Harry floored 45 Deatheaters with a single jellylegs curse, and now he's off to marry Hermione!" style things. I dearly love to write, and yes, I am an UTTER REVIEW WHORE, so please leave feedback. Remeber, all my reviewers will form a new aristocracy of privelidged people when I become dictator of the earth! And you just can't afford to take that chance, can you?But seriously- One shot stories get very little exposure because they aren't updated, so reviews are very gratefully recieved, and I'm happy to return the favour.

Also, please feel free to e-mail me about anything and everything to dow ith my work on Fanfiction.net- I'm always happy to help, answer questions, challenges etc. Most importantly, please read and enjoy these pretty little ditties.

Love

Laura
