Hello!

I hate talking about myself and will therefore be brief. I am far more complicated than I appear and less pretentious than I sound. I reel, writhe and attempt to faint, although at present I have only reached the point of scratching.

I write as the muse commands, and my muse is intermittent. I just finished NaNoWriMo and had a whale of a time. Now I'm done with that, there will probably be some sequels to The Experiment - which is complete! Hooray! And it's short, so you should totally read it. And there will be some silly crossover fic, because the voices in my head like to chat.

The X-Men fic is pretty much defunct, but might be revived if I get short of writing material.

I also write poetry, which can be found here: . Go on. Humour me and read my poems.
