Author has written 9 stories for Star Wars, Moulin Rouge, and Harry Potter.

Pen name: Mjade

Location: A galaxy far, far, away...okay not that far.

Fave Star Wars Character: Yoda. What can I say apart from the fact that he's got the Force!
Fave Star Wars Episode: Episode VI Return of the Jedi.
Although people disagree with me and keep saying Empire Strikes Back is the best, I felt more excitement in Return since there was no boring part from start to finish! And the ending of Return of the Jedi was such a heart-lightener. It just sort of made me feel warm and fuzzy inside...I know I'm awkward, you'll just have to deal with it!

Fave Harry Potter Character: Remus Lupin.What a fascinating character!
Fave Harry Potter Book: Book 3 Harry Potter and The Prisoner of Azkaban.
In my opinion, the finest and most well-written of the first six books of the HP series.For once, Voldemort was not the immediate threat and it went into a more emotional concept with the characters involved.Prisoner of Azkaban was not insanely long and I really enjoyed the book. Do I need to mention how much I love Remus Lupin?

Intriguing Pairings:

Star Wars
Obi/Ami- You can tell it's an AU pairing. Though most people think the relationship is not going to work just sit and think about it for a while. You'll find that the possibility is actually quite intriguing.

Harry Potter:
Draco/Hermione, Fred/Hermione (on the side)

Approved pairings:
H/Hr- Though they remind of Luke and Leia in Star Wars, I don't think the pairing is bad. It's just too...sweet? There's no real conflict between the two.
George/Hr- You're probaby thinking why I favor Fred over George. They're twins after all, right? I don't know the reason either. I just find Fred a more favorable character. However, this pairing doesn't really bother me either.
Ron/Luna- This is one of those light-hearted parings. Absolutely adorable characters! Ron does need some "dottiness" in his life after all.

Annoying pairings:
Snape/Hr- Okay, I have nothing against Snape. In fact, I love the character and I understand why he is the way he is, more or less. The pairing just doesn't do anything for me. It may work but no matter how good the story is, Snape's character tends to fall apart a lot.You cannot write Snape without him being relatively mean and upleasant. Also, there is the fact that Hermione is his student. I've read some good ones, but their pairing still hasn't failed to creep me out.
R/Hr- TOO PREDICTABLE! Next please!
D/Ginny- Connection? What connection? There is nothing and I repeat NOTHING going on between the two!
