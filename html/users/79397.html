Author has written 22 stories for Digimon, Outlaw Star, StarTrek: The Original Series, StarTrek: Voyager, Power Rangers, Gundam Wing/AC, Lord of the Rings, Fullmetal Alchemist, Fables, Hogan's Heroes, and DC Superheroes.

"If you have a point to make don't try to be subtle or clever. Use a pile driver."-Winston Churchill. The whole point of writing is to make a statement. The famous, remembered novelists, satirists, and fiction writers did not write because they wanted property, prestige, or people to fawn over them, but because they had an important message that they felt imperative to impart to humanity. If the pen is mighter then the sword, then I agree with Frank Lloyd Wright: "I'm all in favor of keeping dangerous weapons out of the hands of fools. Let's start with typerwriters."

If I can approach accomplishing that, I will be content. Additionally, I can only hope that I can live up to this quote by the greatest leader in modern history, Winston Churchill: "I'm ready to meet my Maker. Whether my Maker is prepared for the great ordeal of meeting me is another matter."
