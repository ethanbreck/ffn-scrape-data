Hey, I'm Star.

I mostly write in the Mighty Ducks fandom, though I absolutely detest the original Queertet series, so if you ask me if it will be updated, I probably won't respond.

I've been told that sometimes Queertet.net is down so, if you must read the series, the correct order goes: Smoke, Snow, Breeze, Sun. Smoke and Breeze are under my name, Snow and Sun are under Victory Thru Tears' name.

Any fic I have written outside of the Ducks fandom can be found at Starlight-Tales.com - and I have written far more than I bother to upload to here. You can also find me at Sweet Valley.Online.

I have co-written with various people, such as Vic, Charisma, Carla and Meme. I'm open to co-writing again.

The fandoms I'm still active/interested in are: Sweet Valley (I lean towards Twins, rather than High or anything after it) Mighty Ducks; Boy Meets World (focus on the bullies, not the lead characters); Girl Meets World; and possibly My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic.

I beta read for the Ducks fandom.

That's all folks.
