Hi!
I enjoy art...(more classic than contemporary), great/controversial/noncontroversial interesting literature, and music (which has grown to be my greatest source of everything for the past four years.) I listen to almost anything (that captivates my mind). Artists that I listen to almost everyday (ie. "addicted to") include Coldplay (sweet instruments and the very essence of melancholy, cool lyrics, absolute fav. song"In My Place"), The Black Eyed Peas (fun!! always manages to make me ^_^, unforgettable lyrics, "Where's the Love?"), The Beatles (weird nostalgia from someone who's not from their generation - they just about influenced everybody! yaay!), Linkin Park (haunting, provocative angst, "Faint"), The Strokes ( unforgettable tunes, provocative & catchy, "Someday"), Good Charlotte (our generation boy-rock band fresh) Sarina Paris (fun! music that motivates - techno 4 ever, "Look at Us Now"), Kitaro (new age music rocks!), Enya (something that sounds like somewhere faraway, ect.), and others who are very much worth noting but unfortunatley escape my memory right now...music motivates and inspires me to be creative - write, paint, ect.

I really appreciate L.M. Montgomery's writing--have read almost all of her books. Other cool authors are Jane Austen, Sherryl Jordan, Donna Jo Napoli, and many others who I forget right now! I like anything from classical lit. to mild sci-fi...Orwell's 1984 is a favorite (ending was depressing, but book still resonates message today)

I like humor and comedy in everything (except some super serious stuff) and honestly believe that "life is funny" - because it really is. It's hysterically so.
(Giggles helplessly)

(Contemplative silence)
Hope you like my "stuff". Enjoy : ) : D : )
Please don't forget to review. They really do sustain me.
~_^ Thanks. ^_~

Go check out my original poems & stories at www.fictionpress.com.

Also check out my friend Zanx's original poems & stories while you're there! Very cool & inspiring! (sighs & wipes tears)
