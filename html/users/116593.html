I think my time on ff.net is up.

This site has happily consumed almost 5 years of my life and it's been the bestbestbest ever. Sure, I've failed a lot of tests because of all the time I blow on this site when I should be studying but I still loved it. I've met the best people here, been in flame wars and been blown away and inspired by the amount of talent many authors have.

The update for Solitude's Song is well overdue and I went to write on it yesterday but I just couldn't do it. I wrote about a line or two before exiting it and going to my original stories. I just don't have the same passion I did for writing on my anime stories that I did 2 years ago. If you had asked me 2 years ago when I was leaving ff.net I would have replied "never" if you ask me now I'd say "soon"

I'm still deciding whether to delete my stories off or not. I've always appreciated every review and email I get but if I write on something that I just couldn't care less about, I'll ruin it. And I'd rather have them deleted than ruin them.

But like I said, I'm still deciding. Even if I do pack up my fics I'd still read off this site. But more and more of my time is being devoted to fictionpress.

If you seriously want me to continue my stories, drop me a line. Either at my livejournal (above) or my email (xdaylightdancerx@hotmail.com) otherwise, thanks ff.net for being my soul since 2001 :D
