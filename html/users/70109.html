I'm mostly into anime, although I'm still quite new to much of it. Some of the series I'm digging through right now areDigimon (01/02), Love Hina, Kodocho no Omocha/Child's Toyand Cowboy Bebop.

As for games, I usually, albeit always, frequent the Call of Duty and Medal of Honor series, as well as real-time strategy games like StarCraft and Command Conquer: Generals.

Oh yeah, and I'm also quite a Cold War fan. Gosh, I'm beginning to fall in love (orwhatever else it is to that extent) with the whole thing. All the politics and history involved with it intrigues me to no end. I blame all those 70s and 80s-era Soviet military parade movies for introducing me to that 46 year extravaganza. Those things can inspire anyone if youobserve them long enough, I swear they do. Maybe I'll pick up a couple Tom Clancy novels while I'm at it. I know they're not exactly Cold War-oriented, but I really can't pass up a cool political suspense/thriller.

Anyway, yeah, that's all I can think of posting onto here right now. Not really much, huh? Oh well...
