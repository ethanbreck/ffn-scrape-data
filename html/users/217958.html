Hello one and all and welcome to the world and mind known as well.. me. I'm a college graduate with an associates in the arts (theatre). Though most want to be onstage, I actually prefer being backstage even though I have been onstage a few times. Being backstage is always a lot of fun for me and oddly enough, is where I do some of my best writing. But before we get into the world and mind of my alter ego Dodger, lets see what else I have to write about. Um.. well one of my best friends is a guy who gives me plenty of laughs and helps inspire some of my characters as I have begun to write again after a long stall on production. If you know who Kid Blink is in the Newsies, he kinda looks like him. Just needs the eye patch. The friends I'm around have sparked even more interesting characterizations and will likely be included in stories that are much in the works at the moment. As I look at it, my best friends are all in different cities going from Toronto to Milwaukee down to Champaign.

Now onto my stories. I won't even mention as to how many are in process of writing. I seem to get so far and then just get stuck and stop. As of right now, I've got like 14 stories going on though only some are listed. My favorite movies are dun,dun,duh! the Newsies (obviously), Little Women (I relate to all 4 girls), Mighty Ducks (Portman is HOT!), Harry Potter (I swear, my brother looks like him in his grade8 graduation pictures), The Labyrinth, Who Framed Roger Rabbit and A Muppet Christmas Carol. I could go on more with them... but that list would take awhile. Favorite T.V shows are something along the lines of CSI and Cold Case... detective style shows though I haven't been able to watch any recently due to lack of access to the television. More recently I've become addicted to Lost Girl, Once Upon a Time and GCB... my Sunday line up. Oh.. and cannot for Glee. I am proud to say I am a Gleek.

My favorite actors are Christian Bale and Slater, Gabe Damon, Kevin Costner, Sean Connery, Ryan Renolds, Ryan Gosling, Neil Patrick Harris and the actresses are Sandra Bullock, Julia Roberts, Megan Follows, Jamie Lee Curtis, Anne Hathaway, Kate Hudson, Goldie Hawn, Emma Stone, Amy Adams, Isla Fisher.
