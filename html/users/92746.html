I write BtVS/AtS fan fiction, centered largely around Spike and lately I've written a bit of Ethan. My stories often deal with friendship issues. I also write a lot of m/m slash. I am best known for my S/X stories - which are too naughty to be posted here.

I'm a full subscriber to ffnet, so if you want to know when I update (and if you are registered) you can turn on the author alert. I haven't posted anything here in donkeys years, because I was busy with RL and mostly posted in my livejournal. I will try to be more active here in the future.

For those of you who left feedback and never got a reply: My sincere apologies. All my notification mails disappeared in the Nirvana of a discontinued email account... :( So here's my belated THANK YOU!
