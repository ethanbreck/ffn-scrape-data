Author has written 3 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Rurouni Kenshin.

12/29/2008

I've gotten the urge to write again, but instead of writing crap like my previous fics (I tried to reread them and cringed. The story ideas are nice, but the writing is awful. I could barely get two chapters in before giving up.), I want to focus more on characterization and writing style rather than just an interesting plot.

I considered deleting all of my old stories because of how embarrassed I am over their quality, but instead I think I'll leave them as a reminder to myself that I need to grow, and seeing these will give me quick motivation to do so.

I have a few story ideas (mainly one shots), so we'll see what comes of it. My current fixations are: One Piece, Twilight (it's a guilty pleasure), and Fairy Tail (if you haven't read it, get over to OneManga.com and check it out. Now.)

Also, I'm a huge fan of puzzles. Crack this and I'll give you... Well, nevermind. I can't really give you anything over the internet, but... I'll be your friend? Not much of a reward, but really, who could leave a puzzle unsolved?
