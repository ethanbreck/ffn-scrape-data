Author has written 10 stories for Rival Schools, Serial Experiments Lain, Baldur's Gate, and League of Legends.

There is little to say about myself. I'm an average person, of average looks and acumen. I write yuri/femslash with a lot of angst and a bit of awkward sex.

LATEST NEWS:

SAVE TWISTED TREELINE!

I've uploaded 'Faith', a sequel to 'Gemini'. It's the length of a short novel and an honest-to-god fantasy adventure, though it's still neurotic and thorny because that's my thing. Quinn is back on the scene, both salvaging and disrupting Ashe's relationship with Sejuani.

It took a long time to compose. Life intruded at every turn. I never stopped writing but, sometimes, all I could do was scratch out a sentence in my pad when time allowed. I got there in the end, and it's nice to have it off my chest.

COMPLETED FICS:

'Nowhere' is my attempt at writing a popular story for a mainstream pairing: Vi x Caitlyn from League of Legends.

'Infinity', 'Dreams', and 'Vengeance' are the start of a sequence about a more off-kilter pairing: Sejuani x Ashe from League of Legends. The idea was that I kept each fic short so ideas could take priority over craft. 'Infinity' is lofty and rapturous - 'Dreams' is tense and domestic - 'Vengeance' is dark and perilous.

'Prey' is the follow-up to 'Vengeance', a lengthy diversion that I never anticipated writing. It focuses on Ashe x Quinn.

'Gemini' is the follow-up to 'Prey', focusing again on Ashe x Sejuani, it's a long, tortuous tale of sexual identity, power-play and catharsis.

'Faith' is the follow-up to 'Gemini', an adventure to the Shadow Isles with a lot of self-doubt and longing. Quinn's back in Ashe's life, complicating her tentative accord with Sejuani.

'Go Back' is the labour of love, a crack pairing from a more obscure game: Female PC x Imoen from Baldur's Gate.

'After The Flood' and 'Sunday' were written over ten years ago. They're rickety but I've left them up for nostalgia's sake. I wouldn't bother reading them unless you require evidence that I didn't spring from the womb fully-formed.

I try to respond to each favourite/review/follow that I receive.

If you'd rather contact me outside of this website, my email is FFWSubstance at gmail dot com. I rarely do social media but I'm not anti-social. I'm also on Archive Of Our Own. I have an account on Wattpad under the pseud 'FFWSubstance'

Thank you.
