Hi there,

So I'm a teacher in Canada. I am usually into either reading, writing or drawing and my interests tends to circulate between these three. I do have a bad habit of not updating in a consistent manner and leave my stories unattended for months at a time. I usually get back to them eventually but I am just being honest. I have loads of fandoms I am interested in but don't write for all of them.

Oh, one thing I can't understand is why people who don't like a certain writing style leave angry reviews but continue to read on. If you don't like the story just stop reading it. That being said I am always open to positive criticism and PM's. Enjoy.

Oh if you're interested check our my Deviant Art

Also, due to some of the censorship issues that seem to be plaguing this site some of my work is moving to Adultfanfiction.net. My user name is the same so check me out there if anything goes missing from here.
