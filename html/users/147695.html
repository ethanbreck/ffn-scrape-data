Once upon a time there was a girl named Ember. Ok so that wasn't her REAL name, but she liked that name a lot better than her real name.
So anyway, Ember loved to write fanfictions about animes and books. Her specialty was Yaoi/Shounen-ai/Slash, mainly because those where the only types of stories she had any interest in writing. Her downfall in writing tho, appeared to be that no matter how hard the poor girl tried, she could never finish a chapter fiction.
Never.
Now, this not only angered the people reading her stories, but also the characters being written about.
"I want nookie and DAMNIT, I want it now!" Sirius Black exclaimed, stomping about and throwing a fit.
"Calm down luv," his lover Remus Lupin declared, "I'm sure she'll finish one between you and I sometime soon."
"But Re! She hasn't even started one!"
"What about that lovely Downfall one?"
"It's more Jamie-head and Lily than me and you."
"Oh…well that is rather upsetting then."
"Upsetting? UPSETTING?!?! It's damned offensive!" The black haired man raged, stomping some more, then settling down to pout.
Eagle and Lantis, who were off in a corner cuddling, heard the man's plight and readily agreed with him.
"She loves the two of us together," Lantis whispered as to not disturb the peaceful air around he and his lover, "and she hasn't written a story for us either."
"Sure she has!" the blond exclaimed, almost laughing, "she's written lots about us!"
"No, koibito, she's READ lots, not written lots."
The young prince looked aghast.
"The bitch!" he declared, getting to his feet, "I say we off her!"
"I'm with you!" Sirius agreed, grabbing up a pitchfork.
A few other couples, Ryo and Shin, Trowa and Duo, Gingetsu and Ran, and Schuldich and Omi appeared with various sharp objects and torches, and the whole lot of them admonished to kill the poor author who was currently cowering under her computer desk.
"But wait!" cried Shuichi, jumping in front of the mob, "she has written a fic about you all!"
"You're just saying that 'cause you're on her bitch list!" Duo accused.
"No! She really has! THIS ONE."
The mob stopped short. They saw that the pink haired boy was right. Ember was currently typing out a fiction with all her favorite couples that she hadn't written fictions for.
He was right.
For once.
"So…wanna go get tacos?" Ryo offered, and the group murmured in agreement.
Shuichi smiled and nodded, proud to finally be right. Yuki snuck up behind him, placing an arm around his waste and a light kiss on his check.
"You're being cute right now…" he whispered.
And the two wondered off to have nookie.

- End
