Hello All,

I'm a 25 years old french Canadian girl from Montréal working has a Mecanical Engineer. Writting fanfiction is my way to improve my english and get ride of those annoying plot bunnies wandering in my head. Most of the stories here are still only in the bunny stage and probably will never get pass that stage. If you ever find one of them adorable enough to adopt fell free to do so and drop me a line to tell me how they grown up. I'll even Beta for you althought don't expect miracle in term of grammatical correction.

I like reading and writting slash. First because it's boy on boy and being a red blooded girl...well...who wouldn't like to picture two of the opposite sex in bed ? And second because most of the good show/cartoon/movie seem to have men only has major characters.

I like Batman and Cie (most specifically Robins although I seem to be able to writte mostly about Tim), Magnificient Seven, X-men ( Remy is pretty much the only one who get stuck in my head), Harry Potter and The Sentinel. I have a weakness for Adams Family fic (general or not), King Arthur, Chronicle of Riddick and threesome POTC althought them seem to be far in between. I've also been known to read Stargate, Supernatural and Orignal work.
