Author has written 12 stories for Phantom of the Opera, Labyrinth, and Harry Potter.

I haven't updated this in ages. Longtime reader but not much for writing, until now.

I'm a huge fan of David Bowie (obviously), Labyrinth, Harry Potter (I ship Snape/Hermione), and Phantom of the Opera (more stage production than movie).

What else? I'm one of those never-completely-satisfied-with-her-works kind of people, and I'll go back and reread my stuff- finding issues (even if I run them through a beta). I unknowingly completed an Associates in English (took a lot of writing classes as electives and they added up), but that doesn't mean that I'm a perfect writer. I still have issues with commas, semicolons, and hyphen. I am trying to get better with them.

I've got a few plot bunnies who have decided to attach themselves to me. Let's see what they produce.

Unless noted, my works are self edited/betaed. If anybody is interested in helping, PM me!
