Hi! My name is Nisha, I'm female and I'm English. I'm an obsessed fan of a lot of things, mostly Charmed although I've obviously honed in on a lot of other shows as well. I used to be a fan of Smallville but have lost interest in the last couple of years, although I am still writing fics for it.

I hope you enjoy my stories- the older ones aren't all that great, but I'd like to think my writing skills have improved over the years. Constructive criticism is always welcome and I'm always happy to hear when my work is appreciated.

Please visit my homepage- it's a Charmed website created by myself and my brain twin Chiara, initially a memorium but will have to be updated now that we have S9 in form of graphic novels.
