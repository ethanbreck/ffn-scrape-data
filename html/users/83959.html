Author has written 1 story for Harry Potter.

Hmm... about three years ago I promised new fics, a few rewrites, and more updates. Whoops! I'm in college now, and the last time I logged in was the summer before my senior year! Now that the books have all come out I feel kind of lost. So I turned back to fanfiction and it's been like walking into the welcoming arms of a dear old friend. Certain rewrites have been finished (actually were years ago, I just never typed them up!), and my perspective has matured somewhat. I feel like I'm finally ready to be a really dedicated fanfic writer! (Let's just hope that I keep it up!)

My favorite character is, without a doubt, Ginny Weasley! (obviously)

I've really come to appreciate Harry/Ginny as a decent ship since the seventh book release. So I'm a fairly loyal H/G shipper!

My first true fanfic loveship was, and probably always will be Draco/Ginny!

I like George/Luna, and I want to write a fic centering around those two in the very near future!

I'm toying with the idea of stepping into the Nineteen Years Later generation... we'll see about that one!

It's definitely already been done but I would like to do a Harry Potter/Buffy crossover of sorts. I'm not sure if I would bring in Buffy characters or not, but I really like the idea of having a vampire slayer at Hogwarts. It would be the best of both worlds for me! It would take a near total reworking of the Harry Potter universe and would be very demanding so I'm not sure if I want to add that to the list of future fics... but I would sure love to try!

(If any of you have read my sonfic 'If I Was the One' look forward to a rewriting of it hopefully VERY SOON!)
This comment was posted 3 years ago.. and I promise that it will be up by the end of January!
