About Me:

I'm a shipper, first and foremost. I don't even watch a show unless there is a couple I can root for. My all time favorite 'ships:

-The X-Files (Mulder & Scully)

-JAG (Harm & Mac)

-Bones (Booth & Brennan)

-Harry/Hermione (Harry Potter)

-The Office (Jim & Pam)

-Gilmore Girls (Rory & Jess)

-Grey's Anatomy (Meredith & Derek)

-Elliot/Olivia (Law & Order SVU)

I don't do plot very well. I write cute, romantic fluff with a little bit of drama thrown in. I'd like to try something plot driven sometime (hopefully in the form of a crossover (I love colliding fandoms), but we'll see. I have ideas, I'm just not sure about the logistics.

Hope you enjoy my work!
