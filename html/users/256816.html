It's been a while... actually, I think it's been at least two years since I've written any fan fiction. That's actually kind of depressing, since I really do miss writing fan fictions. Right now, I'm trying to focus on writing my own stories, but I still have tons of fan fic ideas in my head that I would love to type out. One of these days (I hope sooner than later), I'm going to have to sit down and type something out.

I'm eighteen now, and I still live in Canada. Go CANADA!

Charmed - hm... ever since the show took on a more (you know what, I'm going to say this bluntly) pathetic take, I've lost my interest in writing fan fics about the TV show. I still watch it, but it's more of a habit thing than anything else.

My current interest... well, they say that as you grow older, you grow more immature, and I love it! My current fan fiction interest in Kim Possible. I hope it lasts long enough for me to get a story out. Good luck, to myself!
