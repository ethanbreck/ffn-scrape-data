Recommended Book Reading

It might seem a step back to many, but a book still has its advantages. Published works, almost without exception, have proper grammar and spelling; qualities that are often lacking in many online fanfics. So go to the library. Patronize a Barnes & Noble. Pick up that dusty book hiding underneath your bed.

Read and learn.
