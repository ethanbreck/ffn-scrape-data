Hoooookay, so, hare is de earth...

Yes, I've been very inactive. I've actually moved from the east to west coast and am now in about 30K debt. And apparently in AZ, when you speed, they send your parents a ticket saying you ran a red light? Which is interesting because my parents have nothing to do with me. Oh well, somebody has to fund the post office. Why send something from Tucson to Tucson when you could mail it from Tucson to DC to Tucson? lol.

ANYWAY, most likely you're here interested in, "Okay, you, you promised a MKT rewrite THREE years ago." And I make excuses and promise it in a few months. Well, here it is. I will update...eventually. To be honest, I've written a lot...but nothing that resembles an intelligent storyline. The storyline wasn't that intelligent to start with. lol. but now I'm just writing the parts that interest me. aka, I've written all about Sess and Kag falling in love, and a little of the war, but nothing about Naraku, or how Kagome ends up turning demon, or how she ends up leaving Inu and the rest. In fact, now I've written love stories for all of Sess and Kag's children, though in the actual story, they haven't even met.

The problem is that I'm too bored with the beginning chapters. I have three paragraphs of chapter 1, and whenever I open it, determined to finish it, I just stare at it for ten minutes then open up something else.

I have iTunes playlists for everyone, too. lol.

SO, in short, send me any ideas you have to make the first 5 chapters exciting for me, otherwise I don't know when the rewrite will make it out, or if the masses of pages I've written will just remain personal entertainment.

Also, I was FLABERGHASTED by the response to In Plain Sight. I'm so pleased! I thought it'd get 10 views, tops, not 500. And 10 favourites...I'm curious how many views were from people who added me to their author alerts back from 2000 on, because I just can't imagine how I got so many views. Kiss kiss!
