Author has written 33 stories for Sherlock Holmes, Poltergeist: the Legacy, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Star Wars, Misc. Books, Matrix, Van Helsing, Stargate: Atlantis, and Doctor Who.
Thought this deserved an update. I' m a 22 year old postgraduate student of clinical criminology, living in Oxford, UK, and I write as a hobby, in these fandoms:

- Van Helsing: my current pet project is a sequel to my first fic 'Possessions'; it's called 'Strange Gifts' and there are two chapters posted so far.
- Harry Potter: Many stories in various genres, all Snape-centred. 'Penance' is the one I'm most happy with.
- Lord of the Rings: Silly paraodies only, featuring Boromir as the main focus. Yes, even after he's dead.
- The Matrix: I'm slowly working on a Smith-focussed prequel to the movie series, followed by an equally Smith-focussed sequel to the prequel, set after the movies. Make sense?
- Sherlock Holmes: Parodies featuring my own creation, dim-witted detective Sebastian Domesday, plus a semi-humorous fluffy SH/JW slash tale
- Star Wars: Mostly daft comedies, featuring Obi-Wan or Mace Windu as the main characters. Also a WIP involving Obi and young!Anakin.

Feel free to contact me on MSN; *BAMF* is the handle I use (long story). Or email me! at K_L_Schofield@hotmail.com. I'm happy to chat with anyone about fanfic, fandoms, writing, or anything else:-)
