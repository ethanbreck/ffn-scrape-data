Author has written 2 stories for Lord of the Rings.

No one ever reads these things, but here goes nothing: I live Nearish-to-Toronto, Ontario with my wonderful husband and two beautiful daughters. I have a degree in teaching ESL, as well as a minor each in English Literature and Psychology. Most of my free time is spent homeschooling my girls, taxiing the girls to their various and sundry activities, reading, writing, crocheting and general 'mom' stuff. I have been known to beta, and if I'm asked nicely enough. I love well-written fiction, and some of my favourite authors are: Tolkien, Gabaldon, Rowling, Card, Milton, Shakespeare, Dumas, Donne and Chaucer.

I have to admit, I haven't spent a great deal of time working with fanfiction lately (read, 'none at all') - all my creative time has been pressed into an original work of epic proportions. If it ever gets close to being published, I'll certainly let you know.
