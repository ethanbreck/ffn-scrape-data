Permanent Post:
Note for readers: Haven't really felt the writing bug bite on any of my ongoing projects = writing for them is harder than ever. There are ideas for other fandoms though. And polishing off some old NARUTO fic ideas to be posted, mostly one-shots, a few longer ones.

"Prolific is a word that is currently not in my dictionary." -personal motto

In college and like many, I aspire to be a professional writer. How I wish. Hopefully college will bring only good things- like better writing style. The best authors, I've noticed, don't rely on sensationalism and instead get to the core of the subject and their feelings. Kudos to those who can successfully achieve this. And for the constant updaters- you rock! Me, I have to wait for the writing fit to fall on me and then I can write for about a few hours and then, it's gone. Sigh, I'm too easily distracted and there are so many good stories to read.

Current obsession: Blood+ = an awesome anime that starts as your average anime, then explodes into amazingness! It's that good. There isn't much love for a certain pairing I love, which I plan to rectify. Also, Suikoden III = An amazing game that needs more fictions. The history and plot lend so many great fictions...although I'm not well versed at all, having only played the third one.

Specialities: I love puns and equivocations so watch out for them. I also place humor in the oddest places. And angst is the mode set for the time being, although I read in my psychology book that being upset or angry all the time will shorten your life...great, that's just what I need!

Sand-sized grain of wisdom: The thing about writing fanfiction is that if you don't do any writing in a long time, it's hard to pick up a story line again. It's equally hard when you're writing in a different fandom. Getting into character is one of the harder things to do, maybe we don't give actors/actresses the credit they're due. Maybe. So write more! Even if it's crap...I'm kidding of course, although thoughtful crap is okay...um...alright, getting off that tangent.
