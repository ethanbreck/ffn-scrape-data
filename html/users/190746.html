Author has written 21 stories for Sonic the Hedgehog, Saiyuki, Phantom of the Opera, NiGHTS, Twilight, Invader Zim, Ever After High, Steven Universe, and Gravity Falls.

Formally known as The Magic Pickle Fairy.

So... I'm writing fanfiction again. I will not be updating often, but I am doing one shots and Reala Again.

My name is Chai, I'm female and in my mid 20's. I enjoy writing and had practiced plenty to be somewhat decent at it.

So I've been writing fanfiction since the early 2000s. It has really improved on my writing skills and I am writing original stories and nonfiction to this day. I was once so serious about writing that I majored in Creative Writing, but found out that I'm not very good compared to my classmates. So I go to writing groups where I write random stuff that eventually turn into stories. Refer to Donkey Cullen for one of them (it's satire, I promise). I have a blog on Tumblr with much shorter stories called Pickle's Chocolate Sample Stories. (Yet I mostly reblog NiGHTS into Dreams fanart and equal right stuff.) For my blog about anime stuff go to: A Lady with a Taste for Anime.

I like NiGHTS into Dreams a lot! It's a really inspiring game for me back when I was graduating high school, and it's still is while I'm living in the real world. Five years of college has gotten in the way from my writing, but I hope to have more time to write since I don't have to go home to hours of homework.

I stopped writing fanfiction years ago due to a lack of literary connection to fanfiction. I'm not continuing any fanfiction that was last updated in 2011. My fanfiction now have a lot more meaning than my fanfiction then. College is what happened.

I hope you enjoy.

Notes about the Fanfiction posted: NiGHTS: Journey on Earth is the first and NiGHTS: Back to Earth is the second. King of Dreams, Heorine of Reality, and Tyrant of Insanity are a trilogy, and an unfinished one at that. Once Again, it's Jackle and Reala Again are brother/sister fanfictions. You don't have to read one without the other, but they're in the same universe and reference things from each other. (Think Wand of Gamelon and Faces of Evil, but with less Zelda and bad acting.)
