ok...you wanna know all about me!

well..im just a bit obsessed with harry potter...just a bit. i mean its not like i covered my arms in the harry potter band aids or have a wall dedicated to it or read all the books a billion times or spend all my free time reading fanfiction or writing my own. yuppers...

i love ron and hermione. there is simply no other way to put it. i eat, drink, and sleep ron and hermione. my parents dislike being around me because i talk about it so much. my friend steven tends to put his fingers in his ears or quickly sign off whenever i say, "That reminds me of Ron and Hermione!"
i tend to walk around singing songs about Ron and Hermione. *We were strangers...* i tend to analyze everything and break it down into ron and hermione format (you're either a ron...or youre a hermione). i tend to start lecturing random people about r/hr. ive gotten into quite a few arguments about h/hr (ITS NOT GOING TO HAPPEN!) including quite a few arguments with family members. when being introduced to new people i often ask their name, what school do they go to, whats their favorite subject, do they embrace the truth and think that ron and hermione are madly in love...k, enough with my r/hr ramblings...

I LOVE RON. he is the BEST CHARACTER in the books. if he and hermione werent so in love, id so do him. hes such the cuteness!!

i love the entire weasley family, if truth be told. and since i cant marry ron, ive gone for my other favorite weasley male, THE WONDERFUL FRED WEASLEY!!

beyond harry potter, my other favorite books are anything by jane austen (especially Pride and Prejudice), walk two moons, a walk to remember, stephen king books, and the angus, thongs and full frontal snogging series.

my fave movies are, BEAUTY AND THE BEAST!, a walk to remember, Pride and Prejudice with colin firth (AHHH--he MELTS MY BUTTER!), the harry potter films, dude! where's my car? and my big fat greek wedding.

i dont watch a ton of television, but i do tend to watch everwood and gilmore girls religiously.

i do love music... my cousin calls me the emo kid...i love dashboard confessional--the lyrics are fantastic! i also LOVE the wallflowers!!!! i just saw them in concert with my homes cristina!! i also enjoy the aquabats and catch 22 (great ska bands). i like this band that no one has heard of called midtown and i also love EVERCLEAR. i love classic rock like the rolling stones (saw them in concert) the eagles and the doors. right now im really into the who and led zeppelin...i dunno why i hate good charlotte... my guilty pleasures include all american rejects and *gasp* john mayer--hahaha--hes all random--but seriously! i love his music! and saw him in concert!!!
thats basically all my interests...yuppers!!

well...here are...summaries of my stories

AND THE GREATEST OF THESE: It's been five years since one horrible day broke up Hogwart's infamous trio until one terrible incident throws them back together. In a race to save the future, can Ron and Hermione overcome their past in order to stop an ancient evil from using the magic of three powerful books and find out just what power is the greatest of these...R/Hr =D

THE TRUTH: Pointless R/Hr fluff that is quite a bit out of date...seeing as the fifth book already came out...oh well.

please read and review my stories!! plus check out my favorites lists!! love ya lots!! XOXO!
