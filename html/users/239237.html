I'm fifteen and I am a horrible author. I'm the sort of person who starts a story and never finishes it. Generally this is because I get to whichever chapter and realise that I'm just not that interested anymore. Or I'm still interested, but I'm interested in the-story-as-I-would-have-written-it-now instead of the-story-as-I-actually-did-write-it. So recently I wisened up and decided not to post any new stories unless they're complete (this doesn't affect my already-been-posted fics). It's beginning to look like I'm never going to post anything again at all, ever... but hopefully I'll get past that... To date I have 19 WIPs, including the ones I've posted. Scary, huh?
