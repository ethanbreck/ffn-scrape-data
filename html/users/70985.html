A quick note about the definition of "drabble" since certain people seem not to get it. A drabble is a ficlet of, ideally, exactly 100 words. My most recent crop of postings are, therefore, intended to be short. Feel free to ask me to develop the stories you liked, but please, please, don't tell me my drabbles are too short. It took considerable effort to get them that way, and that is how I want them.
