Notes about my writing...

I have decided to write down which order to read the LotR Goes (vs!) Series so things will make the most clarity (such as you'll see how we killed someone and then brought them back). So here it is:
1. LotR Goes Camping!
2. LotR Vs. The Lake
3. LotR Goes Hiking!
4. LotR Goes to Band Practice!

Any story that you may read down towards the bottom was likely written in middle school or early high school. I am now 26 and I have improved much. Therefore those ones suck. Eventually I will rewrite them, but at this point, I am working on other things or feel they might be beyond hope but I don't want to remove them (as I am a pack-rat and hate getting rid of things...editing stories is one thing but deleting entire scenes/plots/whatnot is a different thing...especially when published...in any format).

Regarding A Visit Home. I love you people. I really really do. Which is the only reason why I wrote more. I'm done. I don't feel that that was very good, nor entertaining. What I had written (and now deleted) was that awful sequel that movie producers make to satisfy people (and mostly make more money.) Sorry.
