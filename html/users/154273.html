Time for a new bio! Wheeeee!

Okay, first of all you can all call me Cheery, since that is the name I generally go by in cyber-space. Next, I'm a Lord of the Rings writer. I try and write as much in-canon as I can, but I can't get my hands on a copy of History of Middle-earth or Letters From JRR Tolkien so my canon can be stretched and whatever at times. A beta is always willingly accepted!

Next: I am a complete Gimli fanatic. Dwarves rule, no less. So all stories I write will have Gimli in them, and he will not be insulted (by me) in any way. Elves and people may be a bit mean but that's just them.

My list of favourite books is as follows: Tolkien (EVERYTHING Tolkien), Terry Pratchett (check out the email!), Douglas Adams, JK Rowling and Philip Pullman. I am, as you can see, a complete sucker for science fiction and fantasy.

On this site, the authors I read most are: Thundera Tiger (I kiss the cyber-ground this gal walks on, almost...), Ithilien (*sob sob* poor Gimli! Poor Legolas! If you can read her stories and remain unmoved I'll give you a pound and say you have a heart of stone), Camilla Sandman (great stories: she has everything I like! So funny *wipes tear from eye*), The Nightrunners (Classic story, Brothers in Arms. Classic), Gecco (What can I say? She is the best author on this site, with the best story! Sorry to everyone else but she is!) and last but not least, Legolas and Gimli (can you say FUNNY!? They can!)

Alright: I don't WRITE slash, though I've been corrupted by the Axe-Bow site (though I've not as of yet posted there) and now can see the canonical could-be-slash relationship of Legolas and Gimli: now I can't see them being paired with anyone else. Cheers *rolls eyes*.
And I detest the word fic. Ugh.

A final thankyou to Legolas for all the great ideas, and Gimli for posing questions at Axe-Bow till I work up the courage to ask myself!

Cheers, peeps! ~DtT~
