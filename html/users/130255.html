Reading is my favorite thing to do in the whole wide world and I recently decided to try writing when I got INCREDIBLY bored in some of my college lecture classes. (I still can't believe I pay ridiculous amounts of money to be bored to death...) Love Story is my first fanfiction and I really have a lot of fun writing it. I know it's not amazing and I'm not the greatest writer, but it has kept me sane during classes and therefore I love it!

My favorite author is Jane Austen. Some of my favorite books include the Bible, Pride and Prejudice, Harry Potter Series, I Am America and So Can You!, One Thing, The Shopaholic Series, Romeo and Juliet, Christy, The Good Earth, Mandy, Dating Mr. Darcy, Twilight, New Moon, Eclipse, Breaking Dawn, The Chronicles of Narnia books, Speak. I just finished reading The Host (finally!) by the great S. Meyer and it was awesome! I pretty much cried the ENTIRE TIME I was reading it but I will do it again as soon as I get more kleenex.
