Hi there! Thanks for taking the time to visit my profile.

I'm Kase, born 4th of March 1986 (you do the math) in Europe, the Netherlands. I started writing fanfiction when I was 13 (I even have a fic submitted here from that time... hopefully you'll be able to tell I've improved) and I'm relieved my passion for writing hasn't dwindled overtime. Also, I'm an avid shonen-ai/seinen-ai fan, so if that's not your cup of tea or you don't know what shonen-ai entails, I suggest you either run for the hills right now or find your favorite search-engine and look up the term.

My interests regarding fanfiction are anime- and game-based. A list of my favorite fandoms/character/couples can be found down below and for all of you wondering: I intend to write a fic for every single one of them. Eventually. _ I'm always moving at a snail's pace when it comes to updating. Coming up with ideas is a cinch, but writing it all down on paper... that's the tough part. Maybe I should stick to one-shots from now on. =P

Fanfic pet peeves include: portmanteaus (aka names combined to form a mutilated whole: Zelloyd, Kraine, Roiai, etc), excessive use of epithets (a term used as a descriptive substitute for the name or title of a person), bad spelling (self-explanotary), Mary Sue/Original Characters who hog the place (no explanation neccesary, I'd think) and Anti-fic/Bash-fic (a story where the author abuses the cast/a specific character of a show he/she dislikes). I'm such a partypooper, no?

Well, that's it for my rambling. Below, you can find linkage and additional info. To all readers and reviewers, a big thanks! Love you guys. "Chu!"
