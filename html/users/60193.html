Author has written 62 stories for Star Wars, Harry Potter, Gundam Wing/AC, Saiyuki, Discworld, Supernatural, Good Omens, Sherlock, Chronicles of Narnia, X-Men: The Movie, and White Collar.

I'm a fan of manga, fantasy, sci-fi and old(er) Disney movies. I tend towards humour in my writing, or at least witty banter. My heroes are Terry Pratchett, because he is a genius, Neil Gaiman, because his dark sense of humour appeals to my morbid side, and Philip Pullman, for writing His Dark Materials. My favourite books are Perfume: Story of a Murderer by Patrick Süskind and Weaveworld, by Clive Barker.

My other hobby is art. I seem to be torn between wanting to go into realistic painting and continuing the manga style, which has my poor hand quite confused. My preferred tool is Photoshop CS and a tablet. My favourite artists include Marta Dahlig, Lara Yokoshima, CLAMP and Kazuya Minekura.

My LiveJournal, the place where you can read the spur-of-the-moment ficcage and sketchiness, as well as anything that's on my mind at the moment. Which, more often than not, is fandom. XD
