Author has written 6 stories for Buffy X-overs, Roswell, and Buffy: The Vampire Slayer.

I love to write and I use fanfic as a way of keeping my writing skills oiled (and to experiment with different styles) until I can finally figure out my style for a book.

I'm Welsh (for those who don't know, that means I'm from Wales, U.K - NOT Wales, England. That's like calling Canada part of America. It's just not true.)

I love anything that provokes thought and creates a sense of it's own beauty. Anything that celebrates life. I love anything that gives me a new experience or a way of looking at the world.

I removed quite a few of my fanfics, mostly because they had been started when I was 12 and they need a total makeover.

I have lost track lately of writing, theres been a lot going on in my life, but I'm finally pretty much settled so hopefully I will be updating more often. However, even if I've left a fanfic for 2-3 years, I won't ever scrap it completely. Even after 3 years, I still write new parts and post them as if the fic had been started yesterday. My damn Muse is just a little Lazy thats all :D

On fanfic boards like Candy is Dandy, Majik Moon and Roswellian Ramblings, I used to post as Maria Alejandra (Kat)- if you knew me, drop me a line sometime :)

At the moment, the only fics I WILL be currently working on are:

Coming Home (roswell high)

Into The Abyss (roswell high)

and Two Times Two (btvs)

These I feel take less effort to get the ball rolling. But I will update the others when I get around to it :) Anyway thanks for reading, all criticisms, suggestions and comments are fully welcome.
