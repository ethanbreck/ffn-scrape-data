I think a writer's favorite authors tell you a lot about their writing style, or at least what they hope for their writing style to be. Authors I find inspirational in terms of style include Brandon Sanderson (!!), Kristin Cashore, Suzanne Collins, and Rick Riordan. I'm attracted to a fantastic world and characters that could walk off the page. And I want desperately to write something that inspires in others the same beautiful mess of emotions my favorite stories inspire in me.

I truly appreciate reviews and feedback, both positive and negative. Drop me a line! And if you want to exchange reviews or critiques, definitely let me know.

Please note: Of my 7 incomplete works, I am only updating the Legend of Zelda and Star Wars ones. The others are marked as discontinued.
