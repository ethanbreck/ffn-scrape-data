Hello! My name's Willow, I'm 16 years old and slightly insane. My muses are called N'hrive and Elear (though you don't see much of them, they appear to have abandoned me right now.)

I am a fan (read: obsessively huge lover) of anime, LOTR, Buffy, Star Wars, Star Trek, Thunderbirds and X-Men. I've recently become a gamer as well, and have now completed FFX finally and started on Kingdom Hearts and FFX-2.

Animes are mainly: Digimon, Gundam Wing, Sailor Moon, Akira, Dragonball Z, Shinzo (does anyone know where I can buy Shinzo DVD/videos?), and recently, Beyblade.

I recently watched the Thunderbirds movie, and I really enjoyed it. It has awoken my long hidden obsession with Thunderbirds and hopefully shall lead to some Thunderbirds stories being written. However, I have not seen the series itself for years, literally, and am writing currently from what I can remember...

I am a slash fan, yes, but I also love stories where two characters (who could be considered a couple) are just very close friends - Aragorn and Legolas for example, I love them as a couple, but I love them just as much (if not more) portrayed as they are in The Mellon Chronicles (by Cassia and Sio).

Well, all I ask is that y'all keep reviewing!
