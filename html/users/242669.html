Author has written 7 stories for Lord of the Rings, Spider-Man, Tuck Everlasting, Harry Potter, and Diana Wynne Jones.

October 2006, Friday the 13th:

Short story: I'm in university. I'm doing a double degree in English and something else. I have no time to live, let alone eat sleep and write. Strangely enough, I'm still here. Now I just need to find enough time to both finish Squibbiness AND learn how to play the guitar. Squibbiness HAS NOW BEEN UPDATED. Finally. If you can't remember the plot anymore (as I know one unfortunate reader mentioned) -- it's about a girl who's a Squib and doesn't want anybody to know that she's a Squib. Actually, I can't even remember what it's about anymore. Something to do with the Chudley Cannons.

Works in Progress:

The Word -- based on Diana Wynne Jones' Chrestomanci novels, kind of WW. My question: what would happen if somebody remembered?

The Dark Forest -- an experiment in writing sappy romantic fiction. Lord of the Rings-based.

Squibbiness -- about a squib in Harry Potter's universe. May or may not feature canon characters.

Everything else you see is either complete or defunct.

And that's pretty much it. I like cheese, reviews, sleep, Neil Gaiman, DWJ, Robin McKinley, Thomas Hardy, Jane Austen, W. B. Yeats, E.E. Cummings, Alice Munro, Margaret Atwood, Michael Ignatieff, Peter Mackay, Brendan Benson, The Greenhornes, The Raconteurs, Matchbox Twenty, John Mayer, Peter Cincotti, Michael Kaeshammer, Art Tatum, The White Stripes, Stabilo (Boss), Keane, Travis, James Blunt, Ron Sexsmith, The Beatles, The Yardbirds, Eric Clapton, Santana, The Zombies, The Guess Who, Snow Patrol, Franz Ferdinand, Jack Johnson, Sarah Vaughan, Terrain, and reviews.

Recommended Things:
Book = Runaway, by Alice Munro. Fantastic short stories. Try "Silence."
CD = The Alternative to Love, Brendan Benson. Amazing. Haven't heard anything like this in ages. Modern '60s pop-rock.
