Author has written 9 stories for Ranma, Peter Pan, and Sailor Moon.

You have stumbled upon my little corner of the web were I post my fanfiction doodling. Generally speaking I write angst, with great big ol' heaping of romanticism thrown in. The main universe I write in is the Sailor Moon universe specifically the one connected to the Shitennou.

I hang out at the Shitennou Forum and on LJ Shitennou_ai. Lots of wonderful INSANELY talented people there.

I also do my fair share of fanart (frequently Sailor Moon) which can be found here.

Let's see what else...I do not write in linear fashion so generally speaking everything I write takes for freaking ever. I apologize for that but it is what it is lol.

Currently working on:

The World Ends in Fire: Sailor Mar's final moments in the Silver Millennium. The second piece in "The World Ends" quartet and the last to be written.

i am the shadow: part 2 & 3 this story may or may not get a new name. I'm almost finished with the second part which shows the other side of the story (i.e. what's happening with Makoto) and then will have a third piece topper that should salve all the angsty wounds brought up by the first two or at least some of them.

That's about all I got to talk about. Please feel free to drop me a line I love hearing from people!
