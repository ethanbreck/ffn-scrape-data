Well, hello there!

What is there to say about me...? Not a lot. I'm a girl, I'm English, I'm obsessed with all things Gundam Wing - that pretty much sums it up.

What I would like to do here, is thank my ever wonderful Beta Readers - Chris, Iriana & Callisto. I'd be lost without all of them and my writing would be so much weaker without all their help and advice.

I'm also putting my standard disclaimer here, because I have a terribly bad habit of forgetting to stick it at the the top of my documents!!

I don't own Gundam Wing, or anything to do with it (except the one or two original Characters that crop up from time to time) and I make no money from this. It is purely a work of my disturbed and overactive imagination and no infringement is intended.

It should be noted that most of my work contains both Slash/Yaoi, Het and Yuri content, and scenes that are unsuitable for children in may ways, not least because of language, violence and adult situations. I stick within the ratings allowed by FF.net, but that's it. You have been warned!

Those things which are not FF.net safe are at AO3, under Ginnybag, as are all my works now. Things may update faster there than here, sometimes!

Oh, and I love getting reviews! To those of you who have reviewed so far, a thousand thankyou's. It makes my day getting the notice, it really does!

Note - I write to music, always. It can be anything, as long as it catches my attention, and I have 'image songs' for each character for each series to help me jump between them. I do, however, need new stuff to keep me moving forward, so... if anyone would care to throw suggestions at me by PM or review, I'll use my Amazon account to download it and hopefully it'll prompt more writing. I'll credit anyone who does offer anything of course!

Thanks!

Sept 14 - I've had a couple of people leave really lovely reviews, but they aren't giving me a reply option, so I just thought I'd drop this in here: I read every single one, and I love getting them. I wish I had more time to reply - I try to, but don't always manage it!! So, thanks, to each and all of you - you really do make my day.
