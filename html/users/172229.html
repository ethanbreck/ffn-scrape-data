Hello! The name is Famksantiago. You can call me Fammy. It's been awhile since I've written anything-- probably ten years or so. I figure I could get back into writing by writing fan fiction.

I love to write. My first writing project was a script, of 34 pages. It was Digimon Originated. The main characters of that “novel” were Hikari and Takeru. It had taken place between season one and season two. It had also featured the other six Digi-Destined Children. After that project I started on one of my own named Witches: The Power Within. I drew experiences from my everyday life. It wasn’t spectacular, or anything. I just wanted to try to write a story, and see if I could have any fans.
