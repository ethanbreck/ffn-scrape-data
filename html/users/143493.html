Author has written 4 stories for Labyrinth, and Avatar: Last Airbender.

I had no intention of ever logging back on to this site. I don't really know what happened, but I was not pleased with any of my work. I was taking too much influence from outside sources. I was relying too much on inspiration from other works I had read. I wasn't thinking for myself and it left a bitter taste in my mouth every time I tried to continue one of my fictions. I have been working on my own original stories for quite sometime, but I feel it's only right that I go back to where I started. "The way forward is sometimes the way back." So, knowing that my readers and followers are most likely no longer on this site or even watching my feed, I announce a new project. A new Labyrinth fiction. It won't be what I'm used to writing, but that's the good thing about being an Author. You have to challenge yourself to make new things or you'll let your imagination die. Please be kind. I haven't posted my work online in YEARS.

Love,

Kryhs
