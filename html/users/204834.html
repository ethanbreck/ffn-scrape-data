UPDATE AS OF 08/15/06

I now have a new homepage, where I will be posting up information about my fanfiction as I write it, and where you can respond. Visit it at PhallenFoenix.livejournal.com.

Yay! I'm back, after only a year of not writing! And with an unfinished story! How could I? SORRY!

I'm going to flatter myself and think that I've been missed. (If I'm wrong, please don't tell me) So, as of 8 of 06, I'm back to stay, baby! YEAH!

What? You want to know about me? Well, I guess I'll tell you who I am then.

I'm a 20 year old male russian immigrant attending Earlham College in Richmond, Indiana. I am a chemistry major. I speak four languages fluently, and conversationally speak six more.

Hobbies: I watch a ton of anime, and play a bunch of computer games. Obviously, I enjoy writing, or I wouldn't be here. I am a third degree blackbelt in Tae Kwon Do, and I play Magic: the Gathering professionally to help pay for college (yeah right, more like feed my terrible hobby of cardboard crack)

Favorite Pairings... Well, I have my own favorite pairings for just about every book, game, show, and anime I've ever read/played/watched. I tend to stick to them pretty religiously, too...
My absolute favorites are below:

Shinji/Rei from Evangelion - The godfather of all pairings.

Raven/Robin from Teen Titans - Just look at what I write my fics about. Duh

Naruto/Hinata Hyuuga from Naruto - This is the only possible pairing in that show that obligates my sense of justice. Hinata deserves to end up with him, becuase she's the only one who actually appreciates him.

Hibiki/Meia Gibson from Vandread - Like Rob/Rae, another hardcore non-cannon pairing that I will defend to the death.

Cloud/Tifa from FFVII... and if you didn't know that, you probably should be shot... - Tifa could take Aerith anyday. Final Heaven FTW.

BB/Terra from Teen Titans - Again, look at my fics. This only works in the show, though. In the comic, Terra isn't a tragic character like she is in the show, and it's less meaningful.

Arsenal/Donna Troy from Teen Titans - This one will be appearing in my fic at somepoint, I promise. wink

Yoko/Rakashun from 12 Kingdoms - What a cute pairing. They deserve each other.

Drizzt/Catti-Brie from R.A. Salvatore books - Soooo awsome together, even when Drizzt is being super angsty.

Artimis Entreri with anyone, so long as it's written well - From R.A. Salvatore book, just becuase the character is so interesting.

And last but definately not least...
Spiderman/Kitty Pryde from Ultimate Spiderman - The coolest new idea for a pairing ever. I just know he's going to end up back with MJ, but omg, this idea makes me so happy
(In case anyone cares why, in the original x-men comics, Kitty (shadowcat) was by far my favorite character... she wasn't lame like they usually make her in shows... she was a badass child prodigy hacker genious. And soooo cool)

I have hundreds more, but those are the primary ones.
