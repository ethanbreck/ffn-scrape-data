Hello!

This is Altair speaking.

I've been away from ff.n for quite a while now. So I'd say a little update on the profile is more than overdue.

So what have I been up to in these past years. Well, I'm married, have two little sons and I was almost cured from the whole fandom sickness but then I stumbled back here, started reading and am hooked once again.

What has also changed are the fandoms I follow. It used to be just Harry Potter. Nowadays I'm actually even more into the Twilight books and the Skip Beat! manga. I'm still fond of the Harry Potter fandom, too, though. So lately I've been considering whether to pick up the abandoned "For the Love of an Enemy" project again.
