Author has written 4 stories for Card Captor Sakura, Tsubasa Chronicle, and Sailor Moon.

NOTICE BOARD

Anyone who's bothered to keep up with me will know that I have not updated in almost a year. This can be blamed with my latest obsession with Prince of Tennis and my immersion into LiveJournal.

I never wanted to be one of those authors who start things that they can't finish, but I've grown a lot since then to know that I should stop while I'm ahead and write when I can. I write to improve and to enjoy my fandom with other fans. Upon reading back on the stories I have posted here, I cringe and feel very, very embarrassed.

You know those young newbie writers whose fics are absolutely horrible, absolutely mary sueish, absolutely deserving of being burnt and shredded to pieces? Yeah. I was like that once and those fics down there fall under that category. I'm by no means a great writer now, only a better one since the time I started, and still striving to improve the quality of my fics. Too many of my CCS fics are headdesk-worthy (containing mary sues, OOC, typical teen writing style, you name it, basically every flaw that is listed in my Peeves forum) and hurts my brain too much to go back over again and again or to continue.

My point is that anything in this account will remain as you see it now. If I am ever motivated then I will write. If not, then no. Chances are these WIPs will remain incomplete forever because I am no longer as motivated/as Sakura/Syaoran crazy as I used to be. I have another account, for my fics in my current fandoms. If you're interested just PM me.

Big thanks to lithtys for translating "Him" into Italian. It can be found at MangaNet.it Fanfiction and EFP.

Shoutouts to P-chan and Ebony. For making my ff.n life fun and happy and worth coming back. Like -stretches arms out- this much. Even when I'm not around. Thanks so much guys!

X3 And let's not forget Apelles, Majah, Leaves, Lord Archive, Draconis, tread, finding and CR.
