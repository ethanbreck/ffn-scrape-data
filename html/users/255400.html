Hey there.

I should just come out and say it: I don't really update my stories anymore because I don't really like them. Tragic, isn't it? Truth be told, I started writing these when I was young. One is a blatant Mary Sue, and I wish I could read it and see the humor in the earliest chapters, but it just makes me cringe to think that at one time, I thought I was brilliant. The other... not much better.

But in a weird morbid sense, I have grown quite attached to my characters and to the vague plots that I inserted them in. Come on; who doesn't have a special place in their heart for their first fanfic? I tried to keep updating, editing later chapters more to my tastes, but the longer it went on, the less frequent my updates became, until I can bear no longer to add to anything that started out so horribly.

At present, I am trying to re-work both stories, in hopes that something can be salvaged. Please note that these days I'm much busier, and my writing has become distracted. I don't like writing fanfiction as much as I used to, so I'm usually working on original things, which I feel suit my creative endeavors a bit more. For the time being, I keep them posted for sentimental purposes, but don't expect me to continue directly on either of these early versions. But don't think I'm abandoning them, either. With a little luck and determination, Kalika and Stella just might weasel their way back into FFN, and raise some hell.

sono toki made, genki de, ne?
