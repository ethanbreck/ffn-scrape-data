Hey! I am now 16 and a junior in high school. Yes I do have my liscense! Anyways, I love to read and write fanfiction (though I enjoy reading more since I don't have to work much for it!). Actually I just love reading in general. I also play soccer and softball. And for those of you who don't know, I am female (that means girl).

Favorite movies: Pirates of the Caribbean, 10 Things I Hate About You, and Monty Python (and the Holy Grail)

Favorite books: this is hard, but my favorite genre is science fiction/fantasy!

Favorite foods: chicken curry, chocolate, and right now scrambled eggs for some strange reason.

Favorite HP pairings: Ginny/Draco! (if you must pair anyone else... I also like Ron/Luna and Harry/Hermione, though one of my fav authors did a nice Blaise/Harry one...)

Right that's enough about that. On to my fanfiction...

~Coming soon if not here already~ The Raven's Prophecy, a G/D story of course! Will prob also be Blaise/Harry though not sure. Summary: The only one who can defeat Voldemort is Voldemort himself, but how is there another Voldemort in the world? The answer: there's not, but four people possess parts of Voldemort that when put together just might do the trick. Enter: The blood, the soul, the power, and the mind. Will these four be able to defeat the Dark Lord?

I will continue with Lily's Tale, and I am so sorry for those of you who are eager for an update! I am currently suffering writer's block something awful on this story! I know, we've all had it once or twice!

The other two... Chased by Airheads and Harry Potter and the Deadly Summer are finished, so read and enjoy!

~Irish
