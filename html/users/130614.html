Stories:

"Girl from the Past": Draco Malfoy had a best friend ever since he was little. Overtime, they had most things in common and would be seen everywhere. This best friend was a girl. What would happen if Draco did something incredible stupid that would possible end their friendship forever? He is now in his seventh year and there's a new girl at Hogwarts. What will his reaction be and how will he act towards her after five long years???

Poems:

"Tears": My best poem yet...I believe. Thanks to Joni for helping me find my inspiration and Something Corporate's "Forget December". I owe a lot of my feelings and poems to their songs. This is inspired by a real life situation that happened to me, back in December 2002, hence my inspiration and love from "Forget December". It's not totally the experience, but I think you'll get my drift.

"Mazes": I have always thought life could compare to mazes. Twists and turns, sometimes easy, sometimes not. Everyone's own life or maze is different and some people make it through easily and unscarred. Others...

Well...if you don't mind, could you please read and review my creations (preferably "Girl from the Past" and "Tears")? I'd really appreciate it ^_^ ...thanx!
