Author has written 13 stories for Dark Angel, Pretender, and TV X-overs.

Wrote my first poem at the age of 12. My first fanfiction (long since lost) at age 14. (The first two fanfics written as a teen would have been for "Man from Atlantis" and "Battlestar Galactica." (Original BG mind you! LOL) Who knew that decades later I'd want to take up writing about characters from TV shows again!)

Most of my stories are about my absolutely all time favorite show, which has been cancelled, The Pretender. I do, however, have a couple fics written for other fandoms.

I've been a fan of the Pretender since the pilot episode aired on NBC back in September 1996. Jarod, Miss Parker, Sydney and the Centre gang captured my attention and imagination in a big way.

"I have a good imagination." - Jarod, the Pretender
