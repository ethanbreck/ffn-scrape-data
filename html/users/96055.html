Hello! I'm updating my profile for the first time in several years. I left fanfiction for a while but I stumbled upon this site one day when I was suddenly flooded with nostalgic memories of all the fun I had on this site. So I immediately picked up where I left off. I am so amazed at the talent on this site. (Check out my favorites lists)

I also haven't written fan fiction in such a long time. I finally started again. I reread what I wrote serveral years ago and decided I definately needed to rewrite the story which is exactly what I am doing right now. (I need a beta by the way!)

I am working on "Black Rose Waiting" but it will be a slow process because I don't have a lot of free time. So bear with me!

I am a major D/G shipper, though I wasn't in the beginning of my time here. But reading so many great stories writen by these amazing authors slowly convinced me into this ship. Now I can't get enough! But I still love Harry Potter fan fiction of any ship.

If anyone would like me to read their stories or have any suggestions, predictions, or questions about my stories, feel free to email me with the address listed above. I also have MSN Messenger with my email address.

Hope to hear from you guys! Please Read and Review!
