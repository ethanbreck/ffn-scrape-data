Author has written 5 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Rurouni Kenshin, Inuyasha, Ouran High School Host Club, and Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir.

Ah, how I've missed the fanfiction world! Now that I'm a stay at home mom/freelance writer and artist, I have a little time to get back into my work! Well, what time I catch in between regular household chores, my motherly duties, and various art and writing endeavors will be devoted to writing some more fanfiction.

"Catharsis" is a Ladybug and Cat Noir fanfic. It is my newest project, and I already have 5 of the 6 outlined chapters completed. My boys started watching this kids show and it sucked me in as much as any anime would... and now I am here, writing fanfiction about it. And to be honest, I am so glad to know I am not the only one in this fandom older than the intended target audience. It also allows for some great Mother-Sons bonding time, so woo hoo! =)

"Golden Eyed" is a little Inuyasha story I thought of after reading Twilight. It's not a piece where characters from the two worlds are thrown together, but simply one where Kagome happens upon reading the book for herself... The novel is very lightly mentioned, so you don't need to have read it to get my story. It sort of took on a life of it's own apart from the original concept. =) I'm still planning on finishing it just as soon as my husband recovers my documents with the last chapters.

"Intervention" will have an update as soon as I'm done with my other fics. I really want to finish my story... and hopefully people will still find it interesting. I am currently editing and revamping it, and I hope to have it done by the end of this year. This fic is my baby... I really want to give it a proper ending.

Hopefully you'll enjoy my stories... Take care and God bless! =)
