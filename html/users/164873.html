The NEWS

October 5, 2003
Two fucking months. DAISAKU IS BACK IN BUSINESS, BABY!!!

Alright, let's see. Just Another Cold Night will probably be discontinued until I can dig up some inspiration. Don't expect another update 'till...like... the end of the year. Obstinacy will probably NOT be discontinued, as my love for Big O has been reinspired; lookit all the damn waff!!! o_o Alriiight! Let's see, what else. Expect more Final Fantasy fanfiction, mostly insane humor or romance, and as for Big O, the remainder of the fanfics I'll post there will be vignettes and one-shots.

Expect to see Angelic Layer fanfiction featuring the canon yet not-so-popular duo, Tamayo and Koutarou-chan! O_o

My main focus, however, will be Big O, X-men: Evolution, and Final Fantasy VII. As for couplings, R/D is an absolute MUST; I've been leaning towards Rietros as well. Tifa/Rufus seems to have become my main pairing, but that's only one fanfic.. gawd, the fandom possibilities. But, nonetheless, I'm alive and kickin'.

Grins*
