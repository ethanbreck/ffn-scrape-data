Thought I'd best update my bio seeing as I haven't in such a long time. I've always wanted to be on stage doing something but this is the first time I've gone for it, being a little self-conscious and sensible before. (It's funny what age does for you!) I also love writing and my ambition would be to star in something that I've written, whether it be situation or dramatic comedy, which I love doing. I'm also a sucker for period dramas.

I've have one final module to complete for the degree I'm working towards and as creative writing is a part of it, I am now beginning to start writing a few original stories. I currently have one story at fictionpress, my profile is gally2.

Anyway, have been a member here for sometime and normally only write sappy stuff that whiles away my boredom, and generally about a story that I feel some kind of connection with, (generally down to the great performance skills of actors when they take on great roles - nudge, nudge, wink, wink.) Sometimes I even write smut, but I don't think I'm particularly good at that.

My current healthy outing - obsessions, fantasies - are centred around the whole Life On Mars/Ashes to Ashes idium. I admit, I came to Life on Mars quite late on - it's actually a favourite of hubby's - but when Ashes to Ashes was screened I were 'ooked. Gene and Alex's sexual tension is always uppermost in my thoughts as I write, I love the way they interact with one another and I think that both Phil and Keeley do a wonderful job portraying that to us. Like the storylines too. I'm also currently attempting to finish all those that I've started and am starting with my Never Been Kissed story.

So, enough of my ramblings, sit back and enjoy (hopefully) some of my musings.

Update - I am busy with life in general at the moment. Be rest assured that I will get around to writing more chapters to those stories I need to finish off but this will probably be towards end of May 2011.

Please see each individual story for further details. Stories will also be posted on Livejournal (gallygaskins) when I have a spare moment so you can add me there too or on Archive of Our Own under the same penname.
