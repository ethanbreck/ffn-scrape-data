UPDATE September 2009

Wow, what a blast from the past. Thought it was time to make my profile a little more current and a lot less cluttered. A little editing is long overdue (I haven't been a teenager in years!)

THANK YOU to everyone who has given me reviews. I get much joy out of them all :-)

I am a poet trying to learn to do short stories and fan fics.
I like Neil Gaiman, J.R.R. Tolkien, C.S. Lewis, and E.A. Poe.
Music I like includes Danny Elfman, Chopin, Oingo Boingo, The Beatles, The White Stripes, Rasputina, The Dresden Dolls, Amanda Palmer, Enya, Bond

A Bit About my Stories:

Look at the dates they were published and remember I was a teenager when I wrote them. Please.

I think I've been writing "Amy's Bones" since I was seven or eight and first saw The Nightmare Before Christmas. I always wondered why the little boy was so freaked out by Jack. I mean, I can understand being a little kid and feeling disapointed in getting a shrunken head for Christmas, but if I had woken up and found Jack in my livingroom, even if I didnt know who he was I would be curious. I dont think I'd really be scared, per say, until I opened the gifts. (and actually I would be delighted to get a shrunken head for Christmas these days ;-)) So, "Amy's Bones" came out of that.

"Looking After Himself" came from browsing around this site and not being able to find many fanfics about Peter. He's my least favorite character in Harry Potter. I loath him for the obvious reason (he's a traitor) and also because I dont like people who follow around "Popular" people. I unconciously did that when I was little. Good thing I grew out of it. But even though I loath him, he would camp out in my mind a lot. There must have been some happy times in his Maurauding years. I wondered if he ever thought about the "Good ol Days" before he lied to all his friends, got one killed, and another imprisoned...

"Mutters of the Mad" came from the Haiku at the beginning. I was up really late with insomnia one night (actually, most nights, but I'm thinking of one particular one ;-)) thinking about Sirius (whom I love) and his years in Azkaban. Even with his knowledge of innocence it couldnt have been easy. Maybe I have an obsession with bad dreams or somethin...
