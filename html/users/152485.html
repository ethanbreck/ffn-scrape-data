Oh, hello! I'm Holly, an 22-year-old vegetarian/animal rights activist and wannabe actress who lives in Michigan. I'm extremely obsessed with Harry Potter, and I'm quite the Harry/Hermione 'shipper. I also have an addiction to Daria, and I'm a Daria/Trent 'shipper. At one time, I wished to animate myself just so I could kill Tom for hooking up with Daria. Then I realized I was a nutball, and I'm now over it. I'm also into Lifehouse, and... well... Jason Wade is hot. About four years ago, I started writing a lot of Daria fan fictions... and I never finished one. About two months ago, I decided to give up on my Daria fan fics and work on a Harry Potter one. I don't know if I'll ever finish it, but I'm going to try not to procrastinate so much this time. So here's the summary for "I'll Have the Veela." What do we know about Cho Chang? She’s a beautiful girl who plays Seeker for Ravenclaw’s Quidditch team. She seems polite enough, and she dated the late Cedric Diggory. She’s also Harry Potter’s crush… and recently, Cho seems to be crushing back. Unbeknownst to him, someone else has a crush on Harry… his best female pal, Hermione Granger. Despite her feelings for Harry, Hermione can’t help but like Cho. After all, the girl has read Hogwarts: A History, loves homework, and likes hanging out with Harry and Ron. But when the bubbly, gorgeous new Defense Against the Dark Arts professor warns Hermione not to trust Cho, Hermione and Ron pair up to solve the mystery of Cho Chang, and what she wants with their best friend. Um... it kind of sounds like a Ron/Hermione fic, but it's not. Whispers loudly It'll be Harry/Hermione! I'm also writing a fic called "Harry Potter--The Bachelor", which is just what it sounds like: a take-off on the reality show, The Bachelor. Again, whispers loudly This'll be Harry/Hermione too.
