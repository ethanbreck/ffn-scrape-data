10/14/2019

Wow, it has been a long time.

I actually wrote something and posted it today. I haven't written anything in so long. I've wanted to but there's been a block. I've been trying to write lately. It's been bad. But hopefully I'm getting closer to being back and being better than before. I don't know if I will go back or finish any of the fics I have written before. I'm sorry about that. I hate abandoning the fics but I didn't write anything for close almost seven years, so I'm not going to try to force myself to write something and stop again.

I'm mostly going to be at AO3 and on Tumblr. I haven't put any of my older fics on the AO3 account and I'm not sure I will, but they will be here.

Thank you to everyone who has read my stuff and everyone who has continued to review or message me throughout the years.

05/22/15

I know that anyone who is reading this is probably waiting for me to update the fics I have. I used to say that I wasn't going to abandon any of the stories I was in the middle of and that I would one day finish them but the truth is that I am probably not going to. I'm not saying for sure that I will never get the urge to write and continue my fics but since I haven't been able to write anything for the last few years I'm not sure that I ever will again either. I hope someday soon I'll start writing again, I miss it so much.

For now all I can say is that I am sorry and I hope you enjoy what I have already written.

Thank you so much.

Thanks for reading my stories, hope you like them.
