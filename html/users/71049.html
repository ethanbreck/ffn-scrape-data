Hello, I'm Luni Sedai, the writer who has very little to show for her five years on this site. Yes, I'm properly ashamed; diligence is not in my nature, though I'm trying to remedy that. It is now summer, the most opportune season for me to be productive for once. But before I start resuming my writing, I want to sort of recap all of my other fanfiction:

Back With Raistlin: This is one of my earliest works, one I wrote when I read Test of the Twins by Margaret Weis and Tracy Hickman the first ten times. At the time, I was writing for heterosexual pairings, so this fanfiction is CrysaniaxRaistlin. However he doesn't come back from the dead or anything; it's just a little one-shot about how Crysania felt after his death, and her reaction to it all. It's coupled with the song "Never had a Dream Come True" by S-Club 7. Try to be listening to that when/if you read this fanfiction.

Without Tears: Another "how does the girl react to her boyfriend dying" fanfiction. I tried to keep the pairing a mystery as much as possible =/ Written after Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. Believe it or not, I was inspired by a Britney Spears song =O. It's a het pairing though, just so you know.

Scoffer of Love Society: This was just a funny little idea, a Draco/Ginny fanfiction. Basically, Draco Malfoy has created a club of sorts, one for everyone who is bitter or skeptical about "love". Ginny Weasley has a column focused on the romance in Hogwarts. They're bitter enemies because of this, and I was planning to later start bringing them together somehow. However, since I'm much more into slash (yaoi) now, this fanfiction has been DISCONTINUED

Death Becomes Her: I don't know where I got the idea for this; I was sort of in a haze when I wrote it O.o It's a general fic, just reflecting on Draco Malfoy's relations with his family. Something like that. I don't even really remember writing it. Hmmn...

Realistic D/G Fic: This was my big, major project. As you can see, it is also Draco/Ginny, and it's the longest fanfiction I have written. I got lazy right before everything started happening. I still remember where I was going with it, so there's some hope of me continuing this some day. But probably not a day too soon. The synopsis is that Ginny Weasley finds a new best friend, a girl who came from America. Later on, we find out that the girl is Draco Malfoy's half-sister, and they have an amicable relationship. So, she wants Ginny to become his friend too, and they could all be happy together. Romance ensues. _ I wrote this in seventh grade.

Try To Take: A little ficlet inspired by Linkin Park's A Place for my Head. It's a different view on Harry and Dumbledore's relationship (don't worry, it's not romance O_O). Written before Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix.

Last Chance: An abortive attempt at a Yami BakuraxRyou Bakura fanfiction from the anime Yugioh. I can't remember what I was thinking when I wrote it, it was just this displaced little thought..er...paragraph? Yeah. Don't expect to see a continuation.

gomen ne, tenshi: Yes, it's lowercase purposefully. This is a Yami BakuraxRyou Bakura fanfiction, inspired by Linkin Park's With You, the remix from Reanimation. I'm almost done with the last installment, so this will be my first finished fanfiction! Written not too long ago, in my freshman year, I think? (I'm going to be a junior... =/)

A Letter: Just a Seto KaibaxJounouchi Katsuya fanfiction. I will continue it, I've got it planned out. Seto Kaiba writes a letter to Jounouchi. (Yeah, very original title, right?) And, that's it_ Read on readers, and I shall struggle against procrastination to write on. =D Wish me luck!
