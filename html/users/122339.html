Okay. So I've been gone a while.

I'm really sorry. I abandoned all my little babies. Here is a status update!

Dead Stories

UMM - the original plan was that Em and I were going to write a sequel where we ended up in the Buffyverse, but that idea died years ago.

HAPPY LITTLE VEGEMITES - Wow this story was odd. I think it's dead. It never had a plot, so I'd say it was suicide.

Works in Progress

LOVE OR LOSE - as of 4 Aug 2007, the first six chapters have been rewritten and submitted. In the next week I plan to have chapters 7 and 8 fixed up, and then there's only two action-packed chapters to go!

THE ONCE AND FUTURE DRAGON KING - ahem. Yeah. I'll finish it. Sorry that it's completely insane. But I actually have a smidgeon of plot brewing in my brain now. Once Love or Lose is finished, I'll try and revive this baby.

DRABBLE ON - wow I gave up on this one pretty darn fast. Sorry! Once I get some of my D/G stories fixed up, I'll rewatch the 9th doc and finish the story.

Unposted Stories

LET GO - Yes. I know. I know I know. It got taken away. But I'm editing it, removing the Avril Lavigne lyrics that the story centered around so that it is safe for posting here, and bringing it back to life. I'll have the first couple of chapters up by the end of August if I know what I'm doing. Which I don't.

HOUSE OF CRACK - A Heroes series, starting from the already posted 'Accident'. No plot that I can tell, just ridiculous events and a bit of fluffiness. These will turn up every now and again as one-chapter posts.

GINEVRA WEASLEY AND THE PUREBLOOD PRINCELING - I started this one before HBP, but haven't really had a chance to sink my teeth into it. Ginny Weasley is hit by the Killing Curse and survives. After a couple more attempts on her life reveal that she is apparently invincible, she teams up with the ex-Death Eater Draco Malfoy and a portrait of Albus Dumbledore, and sets out on a journey to save the world. No Cheerleader necessary.

(UNNAMED FIREFLY SERIES) - a series of River/Jayne drabbles in the style of 'Drabble On': moments we missed.

(UNNAMED ROSWELL SERIES) - a post-Roswell Isabelle/Kyle story I started on a while ago, but never came up with an ending.

MADNESS AND LOVE - I'm still considering posting a sequel series to "You call it Madness, but I call it Love." Unfortunately it'd be completely AU, and since I'm working on eight different stories as well as my dozen novels as well as university as well as my fanvids... It may never happen. You never can tell with bees, though.

Here's looking at you, kid.
