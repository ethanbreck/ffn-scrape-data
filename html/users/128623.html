Updated as of May 15th, 2012.

Greetings everyone. Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Jonathan and I've been writing fanfic now close to twelve years. I started back in 2001, and have been sporadic with postings fics. I usually go on a run at writing a number of chapters at a time. But, there are times when I'll stop writing for short periods of time. It usually either depends on what's happening in my real life or the loss of a muse. But, my muse does resurface, so when it does, I start writing again.

The majority of my stories have taken place in Buffy: the Vampire Slayer. It's actually how I first started, and I found myself writing for Xander Harris. I seem to have allot in common with him. My shipping for that show has mainly been Buffy/Xander. I seem to love the relationship. And ever since I saw the first episode and the spark between them, I fell in love with the relationship. It's just that good to me, and 12 years later, I still love it.

Currently, I've been experimenting in the fandom of Victorious. Ever since I was a kid, I watched all the Dan Schneider shows: All That, Keenan and Kell, The Amanda Show, Drake and Josh, Zoey 101, iCarly, and now Victorious. I even remember watching Dan Schneider back during the days of Head of the Class. So, I got a lot of respect for the guy.

And when you see that my favorite ship of Buffy is Buffy/Xander, then you can see my interest in Robbie/Tori. Both have allot of simulates and are allot of the same spark. So, falling in love with that ship is very canon for me.

I've also been known to dive into Faith/Xander (Buffy) and Jade/Robbie (Victorious), so you can see some similarity there as well.

Currently, I've got two main Projects. One is "Silverback Pride." It's my most known story to date. The idea was taken from a challenge in which Xander was possessed by another animal, rather than the hyena. So, I went with a gorilla which brought in the aspect of Tarzan. The story is going rather well, and it focuses on the Buffy/Xander relationship. You can find the story here, and I'm very proud of it.

However, something interesting happened while writing Silverback Pide. I often find myself watching random shows on television, and I even had some random thoughts about writing fanfics based on the shows that I pass, but I usually talk myself out of the idea. Knowing that I don't want to branch out and try something new. I know, trying something new is something people should do, but I didn't want.

Against my better judgement, I decided to do a one-shot based on an episode of 'Victorious,' and when I posted it, I was pleasently surprised on how well it did. I never got that much feedback during my Buffy writing, and I wanted to continue to see how far I could go. I wrote another one-shot to follow my first attempt, and I even later found out that it could fit the criteria for a one-shot RADE contest. I sent it in at the last second, and when I found out that I came in 2nd, I knew I was doing something right.

So, I decided to push further and try something multi-chapter. Enter "Bound Bound actually was old idea I had with Buffy. Now, the thing is, I never got the motivation to try it out with Buffy. But I then wondered if it could work with Victorious. Two completely different genre of shows. But it's working. After re-seeing "Locked Up." It clinched it. And, "Bound"was born. The story is new and it seems to be going well. The idea was what happened if Robbie and Tori got recaptured after escaping Yerba. The fic spins off from there. It's also a very emotionally driven story and it grows gradually dark. I encourage anyone to read it. I'm having a blast writing it.

With "Bound" finished, I wanted to try and go and do more stories.

First and foremost, "Unbound,"happened. This is actually the sequel to "Bound," and 'Oh my God!' I am amazed on how well this story is doing. I never believed the story could top "Bound," but it looks like it's happening. During the halfway point of writing Unbound, something in my mind sparked. I soon began to realize that Bound and Unbound can become a launching ground for a universal series. I believe there is enough character growth and emotion to drive it. So now that Unbound is done, a sequel was born.

"Rebound," is the product of the next story. And this is where it gets interesting. Not only does it expand on the RORI relationship, but it is also a bridge that could lead to other avenues. Currently Rebound is just one fic in production, but I am more than willing to spread out the universe. I already got a few ideas for future stories if people want to try and write into this series. I am more than willing to allow this to happen. Just Private Message, IM me, or e-mail (ways to do that are at the end of this profile). I'd love to talk future plans and create fics if anyone is interested. Let me know.

In case you're interested to know, the order of Boundverse fics goes as follows:

Bound
Unbound
Unknown Potential
Rebound
Inbound
Outbound

Outside of this universal series, you can find my other Victorious fics here too. First is , "Survival Instinct,"which is an idea I got after watching the movie, '127 Hours.' If anyone hasn't seen the movie of or hasn't heard of Aron Ralston, I seriously suggest you go watch the movie at least. It's a tragic story, and its based on true events. It actually happened. So, then I thought how would it related to "Victorious." This fic is the product of it. Currently, its my second live fic in production. While it isn't being updated as much as I want, I do come to it every now and then.

Now, there is, "Robbie Shapiro: Hollywood Stuntman." I won't say much about this story, because it's new and I am keeping it a mystery. It isn't meant to be as long as my other stories, but it isn't a short one either. The way I'm thinking, I think this fic will be seven chapters long, but don't quote me on that.

A project I just completed is the Everything Trilogy. These are fics that take place after episodes of Victorious and focuses on the RORI relationship. These fics being: Everything Wrong, Everything Right, and Everything Beautiful.

A new fic just started too. It's actually a crossover, and a weird one at that. If anyone knows my Buffy work, then you can see me trying something like this. If not, please, give it a chance. The idea is what if Jade West was the daughter of Ash Williams, of Evil Dead, Evil Dead II, and Army of Darkness. For some reason I can see Jade being the daughter of Ash. It just seems to fit. The fic is called A Father's Love. It's early still, but it seems to be going ok. If you can, please give it a shot.

These three fics are currently in production, and there are going quite well. I do have more ideas in the future... But, let's keep that unknown for right now.
