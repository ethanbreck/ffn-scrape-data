Hello,my fans, Moonraker One here. Time for a little news update.

My Deviantart

The more I think about it, the more it hits me; I have 65 stories posted on this website. Some of them were written when I first started writing thirteen years ago when I was prepubescent, and please, ignore those. They suck balls. A lot of them are Dragonball, because it will always be my favorite manga series of all time. After a while, I branched out, and lately, I seem to be on something of a superhero kick. Don't worry; it'll pass if you don't like superheroes.

I am one of the senior members of fanfiction and I've seen just about every major incarnation of this website. Not to be a braggart or arrogant, but I daresay I am one of the better writers on this site. If you don't believe me, email me, and I will give you pointers. I do certainly hope I can get published soon. After all, if Stephenie Meyer can get published, by God, so can I.

Take a gander, if you want. But please, leave me a review.
