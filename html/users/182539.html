Names Kandy been doing the fandom thing since early 2000s. I started with Dragon Ball Z, wrote a story a chaptered story that was 400 words and was ultra proud of it. I've since realized that something that short is a drabble.

Since that silliness eons ago, I've written several stories and a heap of 'drabbles' exclusively for Harry Potter. I'm not huge on cross-posting, so idk if anybody who wants to read them will read them here. :D

Other than that I ship Draco/Hermione (OTP) and Spock/Uhura (pretty much my soul). I've also dabbled in bit of Smallville, Angel, BtVS, Roswell and of course Dragon Ball Z.

I'm attracted to strong female characters and the belligerent males that they love. I, however, do not think it is a woman's prerogative to 'fix' them, I'm just saying that they're in a better place than their counterparts.
