You live, you learn, you get the fuck over it. I'm still not happy about what they did, but at the same time, I have some understanding after seeing some of the crap that turns out with other people who break the rules.

Probably won't write anymore, still don't particularly like the administration, but it's probably the best place for fanfics, so I stick around. Got rid of all my old fics, too, makes me feel sick looking at them. I recall being 14 and 15 when I wrote them. At that age, you do a lot of stupid shit. I can't say I've matured since then (I'm 18 and still giggle when I hear "Titicaca" XD) , but if I'm going to share something, I want it to be something I can feel proud of, and I wasn't proud of those.

-Jen
