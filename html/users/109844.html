It's been a long time, FanFiction.net.

My name is Kimberly. I enjoy many things, so many its hard to really make a list. Lately my passion of writing has started to dwindle down to occasional RP sessions with friends. However- I know it can release a lot of stress if I can just buckle down and write anything. My passion for anything pirates, including real life ones and famous ones made famous from popular movies and books; has made me wish I wrote more on the matter. I have two stories from PotC that I never got around to finishing and I feel so awful about it. While the Hector Barbossa story could be updated I feel the Sparrabeth one (Three Favors) is going to be forever unfinished. Perhaps someday I'll take up arms to work on this- but for now that's that.

Disney anything. Send a request, I bet I can write it.

I plan on being on here more often. Feel free to send me a message or two.

World's still the same. There's just less in it.
