Hello everyone! I'm Brittany; I'm a 22-year-old graduate student; and I've been writing fanfics for almost 10 years now. Basically, I'll just go ahead and say that everything posted on here before 2003-2004ish, I'm extremely embarrassed about. But, I won't take them down because it documents my growth as a writer, and that I'm proud of. :3 Anyway, I'm definitely not as active in the fanfiction community as I used to be, but I pop in every once in a while.

As you can see, I've written for many different animanga series/video games and I almost always write romance. I usually write one-shots. If you are or ever have tried to follow my chapter fics, you probably know why. I'm the slowest person alive when it comes to updating. u_u

Also, you can find me on tumblr if you'd like! (kuroneko-sama.tumblr.com) That's where I spend most of my time... heh. Tumblr has a pretty awesome anime community, so you should check it out if you haven't already! I also run the tumblr blog Fuck Yeah Bulma Briefs (fuckyeahbulmabriefs.tumblr.com), which is obviously a blog dedicated to the one and only Bulma from DBZ, as well as a Zoids fan blog (planet-zi.tumblr.com).

Well, I hope you all enjoy my fanfics! Feel free to send me a message about anything, and of course, reviews are always loved. =)

Those are the select few that I think are actually fairly decent, if you can get past my ridiculous author's notes at the beginning, that is.
