I'M ALIIIIIIIIIVE!

But not kicking much, as you my have noticed. Again, I've not been working at all on these stories. It's a pity to give up a brainchild, you know? But seems...it can't be helped. I've honestly tried to get my creative juices flowing to write...or revise...or rewrite...whatever comes out screams MELODRAMATIC CRAP.

So! I suppose this is an apology - I have to cut the promise I made and say that there's no real possibility that every story will be finished or that I'll post regularly and whatnot. Sorry! For the most part, I'll do a bang-up job of keeping quiet. Otherwise, posting will probably be irregular and arbitrary, noting that my brain's a chaotic jumble of nothing. =)

Thanks for dropping by! Your attention is much appreciated!
