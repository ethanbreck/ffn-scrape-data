Author has written 8 stories for Jhonen Vasquez, Furi Kuri: FLCL, and Dungeons and Dragons.

Just a few notes... I'm trying to get back into writing fanfics after years and years of stagnance... and man, is it tough. What used to come easily to me, I really have to struggle for now. But I'm trying! Isn't that the main thing? Hopefully, I'll have some new(ish) stuff up soon. ^_^

Fandoms (including, but not limited to)

Johnny the Homicidal Maniac/I Feel Sick/Squee, Invader Zim, Veronica Mars, Glee, Being Human, Scott Pilgrim, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, FLCL, Death Note, and Pirates of the Caribbean.

Pairings (who, where, and sometimes why)

JtHM/IFS/Sq: Nny/Devi and Nny/Tess, simply because I like seeing Johnny with a strong woman, and because we all wanted to see Nny and Devi get back together after her revelations about him in IFS. I love crazy!Nny, but I always wanted to see how they might interact in the future, when he's mellowed out a bit.

IZ: Zim/Gaz. They're both so angry... and I want them to work that out together, pretty much exclusively in the context of futurefics.

VMars: Logan/Veronica (LoVe, how cute is that), Dick/Mac. LoVe is one of my favorite OTPs because I seriously have the hots for Logan Echolls. The punch first, ask questions later attitude he displays at several points in the series (while "saving" Veronica from JTT at the Camelot, to beating the crap out of Piz at the end of the series for allegedly leaking the sex-tape) is incredibly appealing to me. There's a lot of raw passion mixed into his love for Veronica, and I love that. Dick/Mac is more of a quirk for me, it doesn't make too much sense since she sort of hates him in the show and he was pretty awful to her as well, but I always thought their mutual trauma over Cassidy's suicide was something they could bond over, take solace and whatnot.

Glee: Puckleberry for life. Finchel and Quick make me want to light myself on fire. 'Nuff said.

Being Human: George/Annie! Why is there none of this anywhere? All I ever see is Mitchell/Annie, which I find overdone and I don't even really like Mitchell that much anyway. George and Annie are always together! They have cute moments, like when Sykes was teaching her how to taste and she put her hands on George's head and he said that it tinglied, he liked it. Or when she had that ghost baby for a day, and George made him stop crying and they talked about having children. Tons of moments like this! I'm going to start a campaign, and it looks like I'm going to have to kick-start the George/Annie movement here on FF.net

SP: Knives/Young Neil. I just thought they were both sweet, neglected, and a little bruised. They deserved a little fluff.

BtVS: Spuffy forever. He was so much more worthy than Angel. She almost didn't deserve him, but made up a lot of ground at the end of season 7. AU is a must here, since Joss had season 8 all planned out in the comics and they don't really ever reunite the way I wanted them to.

FLCL: Mamimi/Canti. What? I like robots. (shifty eyes)

DN: L/Misa. Because Light was a douchebag, and L would have taken much better care of her.

PotC: Jack/Elizabeth. He's Captain Jack Sparrow- need I say more? But I will anyway. Their chemistry was so much better than Will/Elizabeth- that final sword-fight/marriage ceremony notwithstanding. W/E barely had any chemistry to speak of... he just seemed angry most of the time anyway.
