I am a little on the pretentious side. I am also Uber!Nerd due to the depth of my geekiness in several different areas. Graphic novels, music, art history, literature, science fiction/fantasy, dog breeding, underwater basket-weaving, you name it, I probably know just enough to bullshit comprehensive knowledge on the subject. I am an intellectual dilettante.
