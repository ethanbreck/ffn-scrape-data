G'night. *Snooooore* --Fanfiction Biography-- Hiyas! In a previous life I was know as Mimi's Pet Chameleon, and you can take that literaly. So. Yes. FanFiction. What a hellish idea that is, but in spite of that we all love it, ne? My personal FanFictioning days begun around two and a half years ago, it was merely a wee lad of 14 that had also recently discovered anime and manga. It was also that time Digimon 02 was kicking in. And I liked it, I loved the whole Digimon concept. And so I've kept it with me over a couple of years and I still see that I love Digimon very much. And honestly I don't care what others may think. So the very first Fiction I read was one I found on a link from Lelola. The site was called Digital Sushi, if my memory serves me. It was a nice little site dedicated to Digimon couples. That is where I found it, the very first Fic I ever read. It was a Mimato, written in Matt's POV. Right before Mimi left for the U.S., she meets up with T.K. and Matt, they have ice cream and find out that Mimi has to leave, Matt confeffes to her on the airport and give her his beloved jacket. I loved it so much, it was so descriptive, almost poetic. After that I craved for more and went to read a Takari I found in that same site. T.K. and Kari go on a date set up by their respective brothers. They end up ice skating and later meet up with Davis, T.K. then leaves heartbroken, but Kari catches up to him, and they confes to each other and they kiss. It was oh so very nicely done. After that I just HAD to have my own fic, so I began gathering ideas and thinking up a nice plot and all. Now my deepest secret that I shall share with you, it's something i have never told anyone at all: My very first fic was planned to be a Mimato...*GASP* Yes indeedy. But I later realized I didn't like Mimato, instead I placed Taichi in there, they just clicked together, and that's when I discovered my one true purpose for writing fanfiction: Michi/Taimi. So I began writing as fast as my rookie hands would type. It took me an entire week of constant writing to finish it. Plus I was very exited, so writting just flowed like...something that flows a lot. Anyway by the time I was ready to send it to Digital Sushi,I found out it had been closed...that is what can be considered my low point, after a while of trying to find a site to upload my story to, I couln't find anything. That is of course until I heard the magical phrase: Fanfiction.net, my home. Now I have eight "In progress stories" (I am a VERY slow writer...not to mention lazt) and guess what? Yes, all fo them are Michis. Well this is as far as my Fanfictioning biography goes. Hoped you enjoyed it. --Advertising Corner-- This is where I advertise about...well...myself. To keep the tiny flame that is the Michi loving comunity, I have opened a Dev Art acount with a few...you guessed it, Taimi art.
