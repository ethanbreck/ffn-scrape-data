So all of my stories have been on hold since I became preoccupied in High school. Sadly, they will remain unfinished until further notice.

So, some info. I'm a 26 year old senior graduate of Kansas State University. I majored in English-Creative Writing and Literature (emphasis on early British Lit) and minoring in Geography (focus: Cartography) and French (focus: Literature & Film). I read a lot (5-8 books per week).

I don't know if I'll be writing more fanfictions, as I'm strictly working on three of my own novels at the moment. Right now I'm admiring the work of others, and thoroughly enjoying being a fan!

It's been a long time since I've been on, and I'm trying to catch up in some fandoms. When I began this journey, I was only a fan of anime. But now I have so many fandoms that it's hard for me to keep track of them all.

Off the top of my head, a few fandoms I dabble in include: CardCaptor Sakura, Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicle, InuYasha, Trigun, Twilight, Harry Potter, etc.
