5/2/16: Updated the FAQ

FAQ:

- Can I translate (Insert Fic Name Here) to my language?

Yes! In fact, I'm super honored you'd even consider these very old fics. All I ask is that you credit me as the creator and send me a link so I can put it here. :)

- What happened to the porn?

I've been getting this question. A lot. In fact when I checked my messages after my long hiatus more than 50% of them were "What happened to the lemon scene in X fic?" This website actually doesn't allow those sort of scenes. People post them anyway, and that's cool, but to me it's not worth the risk of being reported and having my stuff removed without warning. Almost all of my fics are archived on my LiveJournal, even fics that used to be on here or weren't completed.

- Plagiarism

It's happened to me a few times and honestly? It's not something I'm overly concerned with. I would like to encourage writers to write from the heart, however. It's okay to be inspired by something you've read or watched, it's even ok to incorporate certain elements into your own story. But cutting and pasting other people's works is lazy and doesn't help you improve. No one starts out a great writer, you just have to keep at it and you'll naturally get better. Take an idea and make it better.

-Are you coming back from your hiatus?

Probably not.

- Will you be writing anymore SasuHina fics?

No. I've written novels worth of material, and from the messages I've been getting, they still entertain and make people happy. That's honestly all I really wanted when I started posting fics: To share my ideas with those of like mind. To those that still like/love them, great! Those who don't, that's fine, it just means I wasn't writing for them.

- Would you like to enter/be a part of X fanfic contest, publishing opportunity, other promotional stuff?

Apologies, but no. I don't really write much anymore, and what projectsI am working for are all original works. I am not looking to become a part of a publishing house or be published right now.
