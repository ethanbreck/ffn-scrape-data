Author has written 15 stories for Harry Potter, Smallville, Supernatural, and Dark Angel.

About Me: I'm just trying to figure everything out. This coming fall I will be a college senior... scariest thought ever! My main objective is to not think about it... hence why I write!

My Muse: My muse's name is Frella Framboozalled Peabody. She goes by Frella or Peabody depending on her mood. She is quite insane and loves to constantly overwhelm me with different story ideas. Frella Peabody says I should be thanking her for supplying ideas and not giving me writers block so "Thank You Frella!" She is quite vocal and likes to add her opinion to other peoples stories on occasion. If she offends you I apologize. Don't take it personally, she offends most everyone, myself included.

Books & Authors: Jane Austen. Janet Evanovich (Stephanie Plum). Stephen King. JK Rowling (Harry Potter).

TV Shows: Dark Angel. Everwood. Gilmore Girls. Smallville. Supernatural.

Fav. Movies: I'm a HUGE movie buff. I love anything from the box office big hits like the Pirates triolgy to the small indie films like Donnie Darko. I love dry humor and sarcastic comedies like Igby Goes Down and Ferris Bueller's Day Off... not so much slapstick unless its monty python style. I am a horror/thriller movie freak! I love to be scared out of my mind or be kept on the edge of my seat. Twists in plot lines are amazing! The inner girly chic in me loves romantic comedies and the classic movies from the 40's and 50's like Breakfast at Tiffany's and Philadelphia Story. Basically, I will watch anything at least once. If I love it, I'll watch it again and again, if not... we move on.

Currently: My computer crashed late last spring causing me to lose everything. Finally, after multiple reformats and crashes I think I am good to go. Sadly some of my WIP like Borrowed Hearts & Playing Dress Up got lost in the mess. I'm sure they're somewhere but until I sort through everything just bear with me. I'm sorry about that. Until then however, I plan to write a few short comedies and such in hopes of getting back into the writing groove. Its been a while so just bear with me.

My story, Never Give a Man a Drill won runner up in the best category at . The story is awesome (if I do say so myself) and the website is a great place to check out other amazing Supernatural stories. Check it out if you have time!
