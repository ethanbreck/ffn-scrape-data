Author has written 60 stories for Lord of the Rings, Silmarillion, and Hobbit.

OK. I was born in 1954 - which makes me old. I'm from the UK and I've been a LOTR fan for many years but until March 2002 the only writing I had ever done started with Dear Sir and ended with Yours faithfully. I discovered Lord Of The Rings when the Ralph Bakshi movie came out and, imperfect as it was, it made me go out and buy the trilogy. I read all three books over the course of one weekend, pausing only to eat and grab a few hours sleep. For years I was a closet fan, unaware of the numbers of other fans there were out there until I found the internet. The arrival of the Peter Jackson movies provided me with the means to find all those others and I have never looked back.

The occasional, "Thank you," or "I enjoyed reading that," makes my day, however I don't tolerate trolls. If you don't like my stories just don't read them. Your world will not come to an end because you feel you wasted five minutes of it here.

Thank you for taking the time to find out a little more about me.
