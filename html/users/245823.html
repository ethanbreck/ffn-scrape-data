Author has written 19 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Yu Yu Hakusho, and Harry Potter.

Formerly on this site as M.E. (Magnificent Entity).

Most of my writing on this site is angsty and introspective. Both Cages and The Cloverchain Chronicles are abandoned stories; I'm not taking them down because they will always hold special places in my heart, but I haven't worked on either in over a decade.

I'm a graphic designer in Los Angeles who's been writing fanfic for over half her life. The stories on this website were all written just after I finished high school and while I was in college, and that is definitely reflected in the quality of the writing. Personally, I feel a lot of it is mediocre and inferior compared to my more recent writing, but since the majority of my more recent stuff was either written for a Harry Potter RP I was involved in at one time (99% original characters, also the majority of it fairly raunchy) or for bandom (RPF), it can't be shared on this site.

If you are interested in my recent stuff (again—a lot of bandom, a little DC, some miscellaneous other stuff), check out my AO3 account. I'm also on tumblr, but I'm old and crotchety and can't be bothered to tumblr properly.

I may or may not post the DC stuff I've been writing here. On the one hand, ff.net now allows easy username changes so I can finally move away from the mildly-embarrassing pseudonym I adopted when I was 14. On the other hand, I rarely remember to even check the email attached to this account, let alone sign in to check for PMs (which I'm only not turning off because I think readers should be able to contact a writer privately).

Tl;dr: My stuff here is old and at this time I don't post here anymore. I rarely ever check the email address attached to this account, so if you send me a PM or leave a review, chances are I won't get back to you for 2–3 years, if ever.
