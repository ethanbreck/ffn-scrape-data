Author has written 42 stories for Farscape, Sookie Stackhouse/Southern Vampire Mysteries, and Once Upon a Time.

I'm an old fandom lady from way back before some of you were even born. While I have been around for a while, I started writing fic during Farscape. I'm not terribly prolific because something really has to catch my imagination, but once it does, I don't let go easily. As I said, I've written in the Farscape universe as well as Rome, True Blood/Sookie Stackhouse, and these days I'm digging Once Upon a Time. While I'm definitely a Captain Swan fan, I'm really here for Hook. No matter the fandom, I stick to Scapeartist as my nickname, so you can find/follow me on Tumblr, and AO3 under that name as well. Thanks for reading!
