Okay, so Kanojo is the worst author ever, and she knows this.

I would like to let the world know that, if you hadn't already assumed it, this account was abandoned long ago. I should have told the world, but I sadly did not.

But fear no more. You have to understand that when I wrote these stories, I was a a bit of a wee one (my DBZ fic began when I was how old...twelve? And it's STILL not finished?), and I was definately not one to finish things. But that's going to hopefully change.

Since the last time I logged in here (I couldn't remember my password it's been so long), I've done a bit of growing up, and my writing has matured right along with me. Does this mean that I won't be finishing any of my fics? Probably, with a minor exception.

I plan on doing a bit of re-writing to "Save Your Tears," and then completing it. This won't happen soon (I'm a big girl now, and I have many school-related things to do), but eventually I will get off of my lazy behind and get something done.

I think you'll notice the change. In four years, that tends to happen.

All my love, Kano.

UPDATE:

I've edited both chapters of SYT and plan to write the third chapter as soon as I've finished two of my school reports. Give them a read, for they have changed for the better!
