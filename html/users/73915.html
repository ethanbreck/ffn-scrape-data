Hey guys, CA here. Or Jurion--depending on how well you know me.

I'm 17 and have been reading and writing fanfiction on this site since 2001. I am proud to be one of the few male boyslove authors in the fanfiction world and I do my best to stay in touch with the community and be helpful, even if my life leads me away from the computer more often than not.

I like many fandoms but my three favorite pairings of all time are:

Taichi x Yamato -- Digimon

Sasuke x Naruto -- Naruto

and last, but certianly not least

Duo x Heero -- Gundam Wing

I've written mostly Taitos in my day, some of them posted, some of them not. I don't really like many of the stories posted here because I outgrow my writing often... it happens in leaps and bounds, I think.
