O.o well, since ive rediscovered ff.net again, i kinda hope i might put some more chapters and stories up.

sorry for the wait you , what?, 3 people who read my stories.

ugh...i hate profiles. hmmm...now to ramble on about myself for as long as i can...which isnt that long...but...

first off...i am a LotR FREAK! whenever i see anything that relates to lord of the rings i get pulled to it and i have to be dragged away from it. frodo and pippin and legolas and gollum are the awsome-ly-est characters. I SAW THE MOVIE! i saw it 5 times to be exact...and one more time to see the preview for the two towers. I WANT TO SEE THAT ONE SOOOO BAD!!!!

next...REDWALL! yes...redwall. i love little creatures that are thrown into a medieval setting with lots of weapons. i especially love the squirrels. and martin, but i havent met one person who loves redwall but doesnt like martin. most of the baddies are awsome too...i cant wait till triss comes out...i hope its good...

now...harry potter...sirius is my favorite. severus snape gets the credit for being my second favorite. i want harry to die a horribly slow death by being impaled by a blunt object...like a whifflebat. or a chair. i saw the movie the first day it came out and i thought it was good and not-so-good at the same time.

sherlock holmes is one of my favorite collections of books. he is an awesome crack addict! well, hes not really an addict but oh well...

Muh hahaha!

anime! i love gundam wing. 5 suicidal guys sent on a mission to save the space colonies...hee hee hee! duo!!! hes so cool...alright...escaflowne...i only saw the first few episodes of it then they took it off fox philadelphia. but i got hooked on it. damn fox channel. and NOW they took gundam wing off the cartoon network! god im perturbed...might as well just buy the damn dvds.

i am a crazy child. everybody tells me that...well...almost everybody. small children are scared of me. they scream and run in the opposite direction. its kinda funny. my dad thinks i need mental help. i dont think i need mental help. do you? no. i didnt think so.

hmmm...is that all thats on my mind at the moment? i think so. well i dont. you shut up. ok. i think that my fellow squirrels in my hair (and i) have caused enough damage here.
