dancing to the beat of my zoe heart

I haven't had time to write in the last 2-3 years as my life took a giant upheaval and then recovering. Short telling of it is I was kicked out for standing by my beliefs, survived through winter in a paper thin RV and drove through several States before settling down. That was the first year, winter 2016 - spring 2017.The rest of it has been... recovery. Seriously, do not be ashamed of needing to go to a therapist.

Finally, as of late 2019, I feel I am finally getting my creative mind back and feeling the oh so wonderful itch to write. Wrong Number is at the top of the priority list but don't be surprised to see any Marvel stories crop up.

Like any honest writer, I love to hear from my readers from their reviews of my story - no matter how short! Even if you don't leave a review though, thank you for stopping by to read my stories (or just my profile?)!

While stories on my favorite list are usually the ones I've read, the list of ffnet communities are usually just a placeholder until I can mine them for stories.

I am never too old to love cartoons and as such I find myself enjoying and even preferring shows such as Ninja Turtles, Kim Possible (Ron and Kim's relationship is awesome) and so many more.

Have questions or just want to chat? Send me a PM!
