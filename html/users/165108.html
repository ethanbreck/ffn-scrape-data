Walking in stardust
hide bio
Follow . Favorite
Joined 02-03-02, id: 165108
Author has written 4 stories for Les Miserables, and Troy.
Well, let's see... I'm a 21 year old girl with the gift of imagination... and a pen. Unfortunatly, my other obligations have provented me from writing right now. I have so many ideas floating around in this little head of mine... so many stories started, none finished. I am taking a creative writing class next semester... maybe that'll speed things up... or slow them down. Man, I do so much...
I'm currently a senior in college with a major in Theatre Performance. I have an odd attraction to Shakespeare and Greek tragedies. I've yet to tackle a greek play in full (I played Andromache in a scene from The Trojan Women), but I'm becoming a pro at community/college theatre Shakespeare. I've been First Fairy in A Midsummer's Night Dream, Margaret in Much Ado About Nothing, Juliet and Tybalt (figure that one out because I can't) in Romeo and Juliet, two versions of Rosalind in As You Like It, and Cordelia in King Lear. Musicals are my next love- I'd kill to play Cinderella in Cinderella, Cathy in The Last Five Years, The Baker's Wife in Into the Woods, and Christine in The Phantom of the Opera. Right now I really only have Audrey from Little Shop, "Mary Magdeline figure" (sang Bless the Lord and Turn Back O Man) from Godspell, and two reincarnations of Zaneeta in The Music Man under my belt for roles beyond chorus.
I've only wrote some Les Miserables stories to date. I've toyed with Harry Potter, Romeo and Juliet, Pirates of the Caribbean and The Phantom of the Opera. Some are still worthy of continueing... other I've given up on. But one day, I will finish a story again.
Until that day, happy reading!
Love from,
Ali
