Welcome to my little chunk of FF.net. It isn't much since everything is getting trashed and new things put back in place. I'm a fantasy nut and most my writing focus is on an original series. You may or may not have heard about my current rejection of writing LotR fan fiction and most forms of fan fiction based on books. I love to read and I'm addicted to movies (I practicly live at the cinema). I have a thing for anime, manga, and American comic books. I also luv fighting and rpg video games (actually I enjoy most forms of rpg). I am far from normal and most people would classify me as a mix of punk and goth. I'm also Wiccan and no I never went to Hogwarts (resit urge to hate Harry Potter).

Current addictions: Anne Rice novels (oohh... vampires), Cowboy Bebop (aawww... space cowboys), Godsmack (mmmm... Sully), Beat the Geeks (I could kick the Movie Geek's ass!), Stand up comedy (the only humor that appeals to me nowadays), Coal Chamber (music... good!), and Shakespeare (Romeo and Juliet wasn't very good but Macbeth is more of my tastes)

Long Time Addictions: Anything and everything Tolkien, Anything dragon related (now if only I could find one), King of Fighters (best fighting game ever!), Sailor Moon (I know; it's kiddy but I can't get enought of it!), Outlaw Star (This got me addicted to sci fi), Marvel comics (DC sucks, nuff said), Movies (from horror to action, I like it all!), and books (I'm such a nerd sometimes).

Things I want to try so warn me against it if it sucks: Hamlet and Titus (I have a new thing for Shakespeare's tragedies), Terry Brooks books (I heard some good thing about this), It and The Stand (Steven King books... creepy), Harry Potter (I never read a single line from those books in my entire life... am I missing out?), and Trigun (anime needs more space cowboys!).
