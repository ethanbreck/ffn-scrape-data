Name: Katt Monroe
Species: Humanoid Feline
Occupation: Unknown
Current Associates: Bill Grey, Falco Lombardi, the CDF.
Enemies: Andross
Family: None. Had a former relationship with Falco Lombardi, became somewhat close to Bill Grey but could not bring herself to have a relationship with him.
Quote: "Make way for Katt!"
Stories in chronological order: StarFox: The Search for the StarFox Team
Info: Her past is primarily shrouded in mystery, but all that is known is that prior to The Lylat Wars she had a brief relationship with Falco Lombardi of Star Fox fame. While the relationship didn't last, Katt cared enough about him to come to his aide both on Zoness and in Sector Z along with Macbeth. Following the success of the war against Andross, Katt found herself in charge of the cleanup on Zoness. For the next three years she would find herself facing delays and problems of all kinds yet she found herself enjoying it, something she hadn't felt in a long time. However she would be torn from her duties when the Star Fox team vanished under mysterious circumstances. General Pepper of the CDF assigned her to work with Bill Grey of Katina to try and locate them. After getting off to a bad start and suffering through a series of adventures, both found themselves facing a massive attack force composed of former Venomian Units poised to enter a modified wormhole to Corneria. While unable to stop them from entering, she and Bill entered the wormhole and warped to Corneria where they did their best to fight off the fleet with the CDF. Succesfull, Bill and Katt left Corneria each feeling newfound respect for the other one. Though both split ways never expecting to see the other again, the next day Mantia was freed from a rockslide outside of Corneria City and returned with a vengeance. Going on a systemwide tour of destruction, Katt and her small complement of CDF forces were quickly overwhelmed by Mantia and his bioweapons. Nearly facing defeat, Katt was saved by the timely arrival of Bill Grey along with Husky Squadron. Katt was both surprised and impressed with his selflessness and returned with Bill and his squadron back to Katina to assist Bulldog Unit in facing Mantia yet again. Though Mantia once again reigned victorious, he was soon defeated by ICBMs launched from Macbeth. After Mantia's defeat and the destruction of Sector Z, Bill and Katt found themselves working closely in once again trying to repair the new damage caused by Mantia's path of destruction. The two found themselves growing close despite themselves, but Katt couldn't bring herself to engage in a relationship with him after being burned by Falco as well as being fearful that a relationship with Bill could be rewarding. Abruptly fleeing to space far beyond the Lylat System under the guise of searching for Falco, a couple months later she ran into Bill again who was searching for rumors of a powerful military presence lurking beyond the Lylat System's borders. Agreeing that this was a cause worth investigating(as it may have had something to do with Falco's disappearance), Katt and Bill ventured forth to discover this potential new threat as well as try and sort out their feelings of what they felt of the other. Only time will tell what their future holds...

The above is a teaser for the StarFox: The Adventures of Katt and Bill, coming soon!
