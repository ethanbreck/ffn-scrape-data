Greetings

It all began with Harry Potter and the books/fanfiction has been a constant companion these many years.

I've had a few brief encounters with other books as well, but nothing has lasted as long as Twilight (Harry Potter not taken into account), which I am currently taking an interest in as well.

Interests…

Harry Potter
- Fighter!Harry
- Intelligent!Harry
- Harry/Snape MENTOR fics
- Slytherin!Harry
- Random Ron Bashing, Draco/Hermione etc…

I love a nice flow in the story, above all else, and so I don't mind if there are no pairings.

Luna Lovegood. I like the idea of her, but I don't get why most people seem to write her as this all-knowing seer-like character, who sees through any kind of ruse and knows what's really going on in her own, weird sense.

Twilight
- Bella and Edward - ONLY
- Clever Bella
- Fighting Bella
- Funny stories
- Slightly AU stories

I don't get AH stories and so you won't find any in my favorites or my community.

Jane Austen
