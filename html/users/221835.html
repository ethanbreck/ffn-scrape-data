Oh my gosh my golly! I have been away for a looooong time! Well, since my departure back in what... 09 or 08? Many many many things have happened to me. I now have a fancy degree from college. Its hanging on my wall all sparkly like! And I am a stay at home mother of a beautiful baby girl... much to my degrees annoyance lol.

The computer I was using, and had all my stories saved on... died... it went to compuheaven. We had a funeral, it was quite sad. I'm not sure if I will EVER get around to finishing any of my older stories. The me that wrote them has grown up a bit, and every time I try to reread them I cringe on the inside a little haha. I may finish NFNA in the future, if for no other reason than to give my faithful followers (are any of you even still around? lol) some closure. :)
