UPDATE

All of my stories are on indefinite hold. I don't know when I'll be able to update any of them.

"The Eye of Destiny" will be taken down in the near future. It will be edited extensively and absorbed into my new Harry Potter/Charlie and the Chocolate Factory/various-other-fandoms ultimate crossover. The story will re-appear under the new title "Charlie Bucket and the Eye of Destiny". I have HUGE plans for that one.

Just so you all know, here are my five stories, arranged by priority, with the highest priorities at the top:

1) The Eye of Destiny: Will disappear, but will be brought back as Charlie Bucket and the Eye of Destiny. This is my highest priority story.

2) PotC: The Leap for the Black Pearl: I'm desperately hoping to work on this soon, and it's high on my priorities of fanfics I'd like to work on, but I can't guarantee anything. I do hope to have it finished before next summer, just in case the new canon that comes about via "Dead Man's Chest" makes the story AU upon the arrival of the new film.

3) Quantum Leap: The Mirror Shattered: I'm still dedicated to this fic, but I have no clue if I'll be able to write anything more on it. I'm still trying to decide whether to pursue changing it into prose form, or if I'll revert the first few parts back to script form and continue writing the story in script form. If I do that, though, I don't know if this will be able to remain on ff.net. Anyway, lately, I've become a bit... bored... in the QL fandom, as things are getting a bit stale with no new news about the new QL. So I've found myself lacking motivation to write this story. But, I do still want to.

4) Enterprise: Secrets and Sacrifices: I don't know if I'll ever continue on this story. I have a general idea of the plot and how I'm intending it to go, but I'm just not motivated to write on it, and I don't know that I'll ever find that motivation.

5) I'll Be Home For Christmas: This one is now gone. It was horribly written, badly plotted, and just downright bad. I kept the reviews, and I appreciated them very much. If I ever find the time and motivation, I may revisit my initial seedling of an idea and write a whole new story. We'll see...

So, that's it for now. I might give some more info on Eye of Destiny later, to get people pumped about it. But don't be surprised when it disappears.
