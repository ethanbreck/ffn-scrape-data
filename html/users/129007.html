Watashi wa Anim8ed desu! I'm 18 years old, and a Second year Uni student. I'm a Creative Writing Major who takes Elementary Japanese on the side. I'm currently obsessed with PGSM, having watched it for the first time with my roommate on my birthday.

I like to read fanfiction more than I like write it. I love Gundam Wing/Sailor Moon crossovers, my favourite (biased) pairing being Minako/Heero. I also don't really "hate" Usagi, I just strongly dislike the way many of the writers portray her in their stories. If Takeuchi Naoko intended for her to be 'perfect' then I suppose she would have made Usagi perfect. She isn't that much of a crybaby! Sure, she has self doubts, but she can be just as good a leader as anyone else.

There's an article (or rather a series of articles) that I think fandom authors should read. It's called Loving Your Characters Too Much. You know, when you have a favourite character (in my case Minako, which I'm trying to break out of -_-;) and you want everything good to happen to her: i.e. you create a situation (say Betrayal in the case of an astounding number of Usagi stories), make the character go to another dimension, fall in love, everyone loves the character, the character excels at EVERYTHING, and her personality changes drastically then goes back to her home dimension and acts like a bitch to all those who 'betrayed' her. Come ON. Make your characters complex... give them weaknesses.. self doubts... no one really likes to read about a Mary Sue. (see Interview With A Mary Sue)

I love the coupling Usagi/Mamoru. They just work so well together. However, if you're into Usagi/Someone-other-than-Mamoru, that's your thing. I prefer to see her with Mamoru. Another thing, then I'm done, every character has flaws. I'm not asking for a Usagi that resembles the manga version, because every author/authoress changes every characters' personalities slightly, but please, don't Mary-Sue-ize Usagi. Don't make Minako too boy-crazy or Rei too bitchy.

Last thing: People/Characters who are too much alike: ie Perfect Soldier Usagi x Perfect Soldier Hiiro. 1. Usagi was never made to be "Perfect Soldier" mode, although she was quite determined when it came to saving her friends, I really don't believe she would be properly labelled a "Perfect Soldier". and 2. Hiiro does have his emotions. (See Gundam Wing is a Victim of Fan Rape.) In general, people who are too much alike tend to hate each other. That is most likely why siblings don't get along.

I may post a fic soon, but as soon as I say something, college work crops up. Uni work comes before everything else. Please consider the above mentioned tips before, after and during the writing process.

- Ja ne.
