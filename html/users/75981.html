ON WRITING

The fanfiction that you see here is not a prime example of my work, but I have left the various pieces in order for a very specific reason. Rather than represent the pinnacle of my creative achievement, I have chose the leave them in an order, the order in which they were written, that reflects where I was, as a writer and also as a person, at that particular period in time. One of the most interesting things I find about fanfiction is how much they can reflect the creator in a number of different ways on which I will not attempt to elaborate. However, as you may have noticed, some pieces are strongly linked to one another; others have been rendered uncanon; both of these I will not really attempt to 'fix'.

Once of the main points that I am trying to make is that although I have not produced a piece in a considerable amount to time, I have not ceased to come up with ideas, or anything like that. It is, similarly, not an issue of time to write (though I do like taking a very long time to write and muse over a piece :). I am not a writer or an artist, if not from the fact that I have many obvious flaws with my writing that I do not have the consuming need to express my ideas.

This long dissertation (-_-*) has not been so much written out of need, rather from I wish for you, gentle reader, to understand where I am coming from, which is why I have earlier mentioned why the pieces which are posted are here.
