Honest truth? I don't see myself continuing any of this in the near future. I've fallen out of liking anime/manga and just generally am not inspired to write any form of fiction for any fandom. Along with the fact that the project I was working on, I realized was something a little too mature for the obvious standards on this site. At this point, the only thing I'd probably add onto is Darkness is Golden. Simply because - to be blunt - thats more mythologically related than truly, deeply Bible related, and I've been studying Demonology and Angelology for a pretty long time so it's of personal interest. By all means, if you're interested in continuing any of the unfinished work here (excluding Of Crows and Doves and Raven Dream for personal reasons) just send me an e-mail to let me know and it's yours. I wouldn't mind seeing them finished, but I'm not gonna be the one to do it. Don't get me wrong, I love reading and writing on this site, but I'm just too busy with work, night school, family, friends and the like. I wanna thank all my reviewrs for their support, they're a chunk of what lead me to continue writing. Of course, I will be on every once in a while to read other people's work and say 'hello', just don't expect anything to be added. Again, thanks for all of your support over this span of time, none of it would've ever happend without readers.

Emily
