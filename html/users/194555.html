Where should I start?Well,I started writing a few years ago out of boredom and I slowly got better and better.I'm a BIG fan of Dragonball/Z/GT(Actually,I haven't seen GT yet)and the Final Fantasy video game series.Most of my stories are Action/Adventure with a little romance,drama,and humor mixed in.And I usually write Fanfiction with some of my original characters.Take my Dragonball Z fics for instance.They're basically about Future/Mirai Trunks getting pulled into a different dimension after telling kids that it's okay to play again becasue the Androids are gone.He was pulled to the dimension because of a burst of energy from a guy that Trunks thinks may have gone Super Saiyan,but it's something totally different.It'll be called:A New World to Protect,and the sequel is Secret of a Mad Scientist. There are more(five in the entire series).Those that have already read them think they're good,so check it out.
In case you're wondering about the name,it's a character I created for a Dragon Ball Z fic(the fused form of Gohan and Mirai Trunks).I'm not going to post it since it wasn't very good.
