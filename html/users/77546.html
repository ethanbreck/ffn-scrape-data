Uh...yeah. It's been a long time since I've written any fanfiction, and I probably should've updated this bio a long time ago(although I doubt anyone is really going to read it anyway). I just want to say that I don't write for this site anymore. There's just too much damn censorship here. This used to be a really cool place for me to have fun and screw around making fun of video game characters and such. But at some point, ff.net's catchphrase went from "Unleash your imagination and free your soul" to "You write what we tell you to write". I'm just sick of constantly wondering if the next thing I upload will get my account deleted or some stupid shit like that. It's already happened to some people I know.

Anyway, there's another site that's a lot cooler than this one. It's called With some support, it can be as cool as ff.net used to be. I would suggest anyone reading this to make an account over there and do your writing there from now on. You can find some of my stuff there. To be honest, I don't know if I'll be doing any writing at all anymore, but I suppose I'll have to if inspiration strikes. So anyway, thanks to anyone who's reading this and made it to the end of this rant. Don't forget to check out soupfiction.net.

Peace, love, and rock n roll!
