Author has written 14 stories for Naruto, Death Note, Hellsing, Batman the Animated Series, Ninja Turtles, Batman, and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

I love to read and review. Piece of advice: Don't take poor reviews to heart. But anyways: a little on me :) My name is Minya.

I'm an artist and a writer working towards publishing a novel someday.

Some favorites of mine:

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (in their older years - 20s and 30s, sometimes 40s)

Batman

Costuming

Charity Work

I love romance, angst, mystery, and a good plot. I read a lot of books besides manga and love to use my imagination. A good movie to accompany the book is also welcome! :)

My biggest pet peeve on Fanfiction.net- People who DEMAND reviews! I will not leave a review for someone who claims they will not post another chapter of a story until they receive said number of reviews! It is highly unprofessional to hold a story hostage and it irritates the hell out of me! You write stories because you want to, because you LOVE to, not for an ego boost! If your story touches me or I have an emotional attachment to it, I will leave you a review or critique.

Other peeves of mine is Censorship in the fandoms. Art is art in every way, shape, or form. One of the reasons why I despise DeviantArt these days.
