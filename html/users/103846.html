What's on (or IN) my mind?

FMA: I love the EdWin relationship. Love it. Can't get enough. I explore AlWin too. Don't get me on a rant about RoyEd (Ed's a freakin' UNDERAGE teen in the manga/FMA:B)-so many wrongs there. NAMBLA, (shakes her fist) I'm looking at you! MAAAYYYBEEEE this is an ok relationship in the anime, well, after Ed emanci-freakin-pates (not a word, I know). RoyAi? Hmmm, I haven't gone there yet, I might-dunno. Elricest? Gack! Cough! Hairball!

Angst: Bring it! Loads of it, covered in chocolate and filled with razorblades! Got a "ship" to tout, do it, just make sure to throw a major character death/hurt/illness/something to give me shivers.

Lemons: Ok, ok, ok. Don't get me wrong. I LOVES ME SOME LEMONS-good lemons. I might write a few but keep them squirreled away for myself. Naughty. Truthfully, when I get to a place in something that just screams: "Write the SEX stuff here!" I just feel silly. Oh, but I will allude to it. I think that's just tasty, but I have an active imagination. Others may not and NEED the writer to, well, spell it out for them. That's fine. So, for now, my stuff may be a little "lemon pepper."

O.C.'s: Unless they are the maid, the policeman, the helpful old man who give them a ride, they don't have any place for me in FAN fiction. I have read a FEW works that have a major character as an OC and ended up liking them. I really do applaud the few writers on ff.net that have been able to create lovely OC's. I am envious of that talent! If I could create an original character I could become obsessed with, I could actually write some regular fiction I'd be proud of. Instead, I'm honing my writing with someone else's OC. Sad.

Self Insertion: I do not want to get trapped in the world of FMA. I don't want to get stuck back in feudal Japan. So, I don't want to read YOUR adventure with your favorite characters either. Sorry. Have fun writing that though.

You can find me as buggyness101 on tumblr and BuggyNess on archive of our own.
