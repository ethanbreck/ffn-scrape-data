What I write, and why I write it:

I write SI, plain and simple. I know that Self-Inserts are viewed by a whole lot of people as annoying, but it's how I've been writing for the past four or so years, so I'm not stopping anytime soon.

The main reason people seem to dislike SI's so much, is because of the prevalence for these characters to turn into the dreaded "Mary-Sue". In my first two fics published on this website (Evolution Slash and The Will Of The Medafighter) I'll plead guilty to my characters being what constitutes one of these: They replaced the main character completely, were pretty damned unbeatable and always ended up beating the bad guy on their own. I see it as the mistakes any fanfic writer can make while learning our craft. But I'll also say this: Those two fics got pretty good reviews, and it's what convinced me to carry on writing.

Well, why do I write SI? Simple - I enjoy the shows and books my fanfics are based on, and isn't the fan's ultimate fantasy to star in these shows? I've also tried writing non-SI fics, and... Well, let's just say that I've never deleted a fic in my life, except for those.

My opinion on SIs and Author Created Characters? They're not the evil, creative black holes that people make them out to be. If they're done well, and with a reasonable amount of balance, they just add that much more to a fic.

Thanks for having enough interest in me to read this.
