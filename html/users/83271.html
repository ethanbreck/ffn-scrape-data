Hey, I'm Dan. I write fanfics just for the hell of it. What I have here is kinda weak, but I'm getting better. My main focus is to keep things in-character since I love trying to figure out how people think and what makes them that way. It's just a lot more fun :) All in all I simply adore literature.

I don't post much. I'm more of a reader than a creator. Over the years, I've created my own archive of collected fics. Someday I'll open my own recs page. Until then, I'm just an eighteen year old boy in Canada. Next year I'll either be taking a course in Animation or Engineering. Geh, well anyway, thanks for dropping by.
