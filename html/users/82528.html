Oi! As you can see I have kinda dropped off the face of the planet, writing has become non-existent in my world. However, I'm still the fun-loving bookworm as always.Writing has been on the back-burner due to lack of time, and the addictions have mellowed out somewhat, but the pile of favorites keep growing. I find myself gravitating towards writing more and more so maybe the hiatus is over?

I guess you could say I'm nice... once you get past the anti-social, must warm up to you, not to mention sarcastic attitude. Funny and weird may also be good choice words, but as me da says, I am proof evil does exist. I have an idea of what direction I'd like to go in, but then again, I've always been terrible with directions. =9

All along the path I have taken over the years, I have gotten beaten by errant branches, stumbled and fallen, and I have gotten lost numerous times.

Er... thats about it. Drop a line sometime and I'll respond.
