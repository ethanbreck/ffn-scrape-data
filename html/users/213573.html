Favourite characters (Good guys and bad guys)
1. Megatron (BW)
Why: He's cunning, ruthless, heavily armed, extremely dangerous, his motto: "yessss", he was a purple T-Rex, metal T-Rex and a bigassed dragon, AND he's polite. If you disagree with this, then see the episode "Before the Storm".

2. Sephiroth (FF7)
The best videogame villian ever. He's powerful, fast, has a bigassed sword, has black clothing and he's confused. He does it all for his Mother, Jenova. Who is really an alien menace that manipulates Sephiroth. I felt really sorry for him when I learned the truth about him. (Hint: Get Vincent, then you'll see what I mean)

3: Phantom Renegade (Medabots)
Some say that he's a Tuxedo Mask ripoff, but he's cool, a thief, and has a tragic past. And not to forgot a silent Medabot who will kill you when he only snaps with his fingers.

4. Waspinator (BW)
He's a wasp. He can fly. He talks in third perspective. He's funny. And he gets blown up all the time. What more do you want?

5. Big Convoy (BW Neo)
I saw a Japanese episode of Beast Wars Neo, and this guy immediately got my interest. He can turn into a mammoth, has a gun that's the size of the Titanic, is armed from tip to toe (LITERALLY), he's a loner and his motto is: "I'm gonna blast ya a new hole", but then in Japanese. His downsides? His crew is a bunch of cowardly weaklings.

Well, no other favourite characters...

What I truly appreciate in a story, is the plot. For example in FF7. First, they only want to stop a corporation from draining the natural resources, but then they discover the true nature of Mako energy and go out to stop a crazy madman who wants to combine himself with the spirit flow. Or Beast Wars. At first, the Maximals thought that Megatron stole the Golden Discs to find a planet full of Energon. But then they find out the TRUE meaning of Megatron's plans.
