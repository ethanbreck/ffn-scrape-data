Hello. If you have made it this far, you obviously are at least mildly curious about my writing. This makes me happy and surly like an elf at the same time. I don't know why the surliness is there. It just is.

As implied by the fanfictions I write, I am a fan of many, many things. I like all of the Nightmare on Elm Street movies. I like Freddy krueger and the man who plays him, Robert Englund. I like Monty Python. I have memorized the entire "Knights of the Round Table" song. I like Edward Scissorhands. I like The Matrix. I like Rage Against the Machine/Audioslave, and would give anything to make Tom Morello my personal love slave. I like Trigun. I like Cowboy bebop. I like swinging dead cats over my head in the light of the full moon while completely naked. I like the Evil Dead/Army of Darkness series. I like all sorts of comics by Jhonen Vasquez and his sidekick Roman Dirge. I like Brak. I like repeating myself. I like repeating myself. I like drawing weird comics. I like Stephen King novels. I like Nirvana. I like the Nightmare Before Christmas. I like Johnny Depp (what teenage girl DOESN'T like him, may I ask?) I like Invader Zim. I like puppies and marshmallows and decapitations. I like Animal Crossing. I like Don Quixote. I like playing God and zapping villagers with lightning. I like BONE. I like Calvin and Hobbes. I like griping that the Boondocks doesn't have a catagory on ff.net. I like flying piggies who crash into giant concete peanuts. I like chocolate. I like Psycho. I like writing stories about Jason Voorhees mutilating cute, fluffy animals. I like smacking babies against walls. I like the Spongmonkeys of rathergood.com. I like listening to System of a Down while jumping on the bed until I hit the ceiling and crack my head open. I like Blo-pops. I like Halloween, the holiday. The movie was okay. I like Tim Burton. I like my Israel. I like writing fan fictions and giggling as people tell me how awful I am.
