uPdAtE - 10/07/08

I am officially on permanent hiatus.

My life has been pretty upside down these past few years. University has not been treating me well. I have lost my motivation to write. The passion is still there and I know if I just throw away everything and just sit down and write, I would be able to continue. It's just so much easier sleeping away my time or sitting myself down and numbing my brain away with TV.
I've re-read some of my previous work and it is embarrassing at how immature and badly developed or expressed some of that crap is. I still like the original idea, I'd love to have it rewritten. Hazukashii...but I guess it's my growth.
I would like to say that no, I have not given up. I don't know if anyone cares. But hopefully, one day, I'll at least be able to finish what I started.

Thanks to anyone who even read this junk of a blurb. I apologize to and thank anyone who has ever read any of my stories.
