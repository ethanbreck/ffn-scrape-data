I'm never any good at these things but here it goes

I'm 22, I live in Canada, and I have both my parents and a brother & sister. And have a very large, yet very close knit family :) I love them! (as crazy as they may be)

I LOOOOVE the Twilight Series (but you probably guessed that by my username :P). When my friend first told me about the series she also showed me a bunch of pics of Tom Sturridge, and she was calling him Edward. So when the casting stuff came out for the movie I was kind of confused at first cause I thought that it already was a movie and Tom was Edward. No offence to Rob, he did an amazing job in the movie, but as far as I'm concerned Tom is always the one I pictured as Edward. He's just got that look about him. Case in point... my display pic... look at that crooked smile, this is exactly how I pictured Edward in the 30's... well except for the gorgeous blue eyes (this I believe is from Being Julia, or at least it looks to be about that era)

After a while I really got into some of Tom's work, and as it stands now I currently own Murderous Intent (or Like Minds, which I personally think is a better title), Being Julia (Tom is sooooo adorable in this movie), and Vanity Fair (although Tom's part is only about 5 minutes long :P)

My other 'celebrity crush' is Shane West. (the other being Tom... no offence to Rob, I just don't find him all that "hot"... but more 'cute' :P) I have been a fan of Shane's for a while. I currently own quite a few of his movies (A Walk To Remember, League of Extraordinary Gentlemen, as well as his newer movies The Lodger & Red Sands) I also LOOOOOVED him on ER and am soooo glad we all got the Roomies reunion we wanted!! _

Some of my favourite shows are:
CSI (original, Miami, NY... in that order :P)
ER (it's not on anymore cries)
Without A Trace
Criminal Minds
Law & Order

My musical intrests are completely random... seriously! If you took a look at my music library on iTunes you'd understand. I can go from listening to Evanescense, to the Beatles, to Debussy (although I only have 2 of his songs), to Paramore. I have a very wide range of music. I was just brought up like that, my dad loves the 'oldies' stuff; and I share a lot of his musical taste :)
