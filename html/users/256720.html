Gabi L. mostly writes cheesy romance and humor fiction. She loves making up bizarre scenarios for Pirates of the Carribean, Lord of the Rings, Angel or X-Men, although for our sake most stay in her head. Yes, we often question her sanity. The great author in her is currently working on tons of projetcs at the same time, including X-Men and King Arthur stories. Gabi L enjoys reading online comics, drawing and surfing aimlessly on the Internet. Her addiction to video games and romance fics is reportedly under control with medication. We did mention the whole 'questionning sanity' part, right?

Gabi L enjoys calling herself a beta-reader, although it's never been all that official. Don't be afraid to contact her, she doesn't bite anymore. At least, that's what the therapist said when he woke up in the hospital. Hm.
