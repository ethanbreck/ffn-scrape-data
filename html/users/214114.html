All I do is read fics, anymore...well, I write, but not fanfics, generally, and certainly not ones that I finish enough to post.

...Sorry, world.

Er, anyway, I guess...

I was born in 1988 easier than fixing my age every year, I have dreams about actually being a GOOD writer someday, and I posted whatever is on here I don't even know anymore when I was much younger, more naive, and didn't write well at all. Maybe, someday, I will post something, and by some chance it will be decent. Huzzah and kudos to that. Most likely, I'll just get drunk and write something awful and angsty, and you'll hate your life for multiple reasons after reading it. I want to live a profound life, and if I reviewed something of yours, I meant it. Flame or rave. Bold words, maybe, but why SHOULDN'T I mean it? You're just a bunch of random people on the intarnets, and I'm just some random American who don't speak not good Inglish most of the time, whether or not it's my first language.

I like good books, so if you want to suggest one for me, feel free. Unfortunately, I'll probably buy it for thirteen expensive dollars and read it in a day, but maybe it'll turn out to be something I really like in the end.

I also like good fics, so if you want to suggest one of those, also feel free.

I like: Naruto, One Piece, Final Fantasy have played VII - X-2, so I'm not familiar with characters before then...and I've played I+II, but I only read those if they're good parodies, Kingdom Hearts, Harry Potter on an increasingly rare basis, considering the sheer volume of craptastic fanfics out there, Xenosaga, Fire Emblem, and Artemis Fowl or Artemis fowl, fanfiction.net for the win on that one, for starters. I've read/viewed more than that, obviously, but I can't remember anything else that has a big ole catagory worth reading on here. I also like the gay because I AM rather gay. Or half gay. Ambi. Anyway, I'm an open-minded little brat, which means I'm up for basically ANY pairing/quadring/trioette why yes, yes, I DID make up those words! and MORE. A well-written only stipulation romance involving the entire crew of the Going Merry One Piece, for the uninformed in a giant relationship? Awesome. I'll likely enjoy it more than a solo romance, to be honest, because my experience is that half of the time people writing duets are rather biased towards only one pairing or sexuality or concept, and I'm really not.

If you haven't stopped reading yet, kudos, champ! This is the end anyway, so it doesn't really matter if you're still looking at this, but I guess I appreciate it in some vague way.

By the by, flames? Please do. I like reading them, even about myself. Keeps me entertained, and I'll probably agree, masochist that I am.

Later.
