I'm a young professional and I love to read & write. I'm faithful to several fandoms - Alias (Vaughn & Sydney); The West Wing (Josh & Donna); Friends (Monica & Chandler); The X-Files (Mulder & Scully); Bones (Booth & Brennan); Twilight (Edward/Bella); NCIS (Tony/Ziva); Castle (Beckett/Castle). I'm a faithful & passionate fan of the Yankees & The University of Connecticut men's & women's basketball team (hence the pen name). I've posted a few fanfics for Friends & several for Alias (including the novel-length "Coming to Terms", "Raw Precision" & "It Goes On" as well as the WIP's "The Lightkeeper" & "Trying Normal". I love feedback - live for it. Feel free to drop me a line.
