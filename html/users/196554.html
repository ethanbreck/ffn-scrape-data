Author has written 7 stories for Dragon Ball Z, Rurouni Kenshin, Justice League, and Thor.

More television shows should have giant robots, phenomenal swordsmen, post-apocalyptic settings, really big guns, demons, inadvertent (or in some cases, just plain lecherously advertent) perverts, and/or muscular men beating the shit out of each other.

As far as the romance writing goes, I treat my pairings like I treat my academic work. I enjoy pairings that:

1. Possess evidence for their existence

2. Make sense in terms of disposition, personality, and power-play dynamics

3. Have something interesting to say about human relationships in general

That said, I tend to focus on relationships with high dialogue-content since that's what I like to write. And no, I do not believe in OTPs. I like exploring relationships and alternative pairings, but if they don't fulfill my 3 categories, forget it.
