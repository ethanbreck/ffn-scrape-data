Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

Got a review on a story the other day and realized that with as much time as I spend on this site (ALL the freaking time), I really ought to get around to updating my profile. So here 'tis as of 8/11/08.

I'm a 22 year old college girl who is really kind of becoming a hermit. Isn't college supposed to do the opposite of that?

I'm a fan of too many things to mention. The current obsessions are Hikaru no Go (drawn characters who AGE and change CLOTHES! Not to mention the PLOT!) and Life with Derek (which I used to bash horribly. Don't tell anybody that I've changed my mind and now worship the kinky stepbrother/stepsister dynamic. GAH.)

Fanfics: Well, I have two HP ones that are old and filled with AN's written by a depressed and attention starved girl. I am happy to announce that that description no longer applies! (Except for the girl part. That's still true.) However... the AN's are still there. So, if you decide to read them, please just remember that they are outdated and if I wasn't too lazy to do so, I'd replace all the chapters so they weren't there.

I have pretty much given up on writing fan fiction. For now, and the forseeable future, I'm just going to be a reader. At least I didn't leave any unfinished, which is more than some authors do. That's my consolation.

(Not all authors do that!! And I realize it's not always possible, don't hate me -_- Though, I do think that if you are going to leave them unifished, you should post a short description of what you WOULD have done if you HAD finished it. Then people wouldn't get as upset, at least they'd have SOME kind of closure. Just my 2 cents.)

~Teva AKA Sara~
