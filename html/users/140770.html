I don't have much to say here, people! One thing that I must point out is that I started writing Harry Potter and the Crystal of Gryffindor after the 4th Book came out. Therefore, things we found out in Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix and Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince are irrelevant to my story.

And a note on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince... if anyone would like to discuss it, feel free to Email me or IM me! It was so excellent, but I have so many things I want to talk about with people! I would love to hear from you! Hell, I'd love to hear from you even if you don't want to talk about that and want to talk about something different... like musicals, or the weather, or toaster ovens or anything like that. I'm a rather bored individual sometimes.

Here are some things that you should always remember:

~Before you criticize someone, walk a mile in their shoes. That way when you criticize them, you're a mile away and you have their shoes.~ ~Never underestimate the power of a spoon.~

~"One day you're going to wake up and I'm not going to be here... because I had to go to the store to get more bread because you fed it all to the ducks!"~

~Anything is possible~

~"Life's funny like that"~
