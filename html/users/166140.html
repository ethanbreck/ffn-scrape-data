I use the name kalisgirl for my fan fiction and other fannish activities. I'm in my 30's, so I've been reading and lurking since the late 90's and began writing in fandom in 2002. There was a very, very long hiatus for life chaos and grad school, and now I mostly lurk, read, and post a fic every now and then.
I started in HP but I write for fests now to push my comfort zone. I love small fandoms and rare pairs. That means there's not much fandom repetition in my writing. Hope you enjoy what you see here.
I have a livejournal and an AO3 account as kalisgirl - that's where you'll find my more mature fics, if you're old enough for that stuff.
