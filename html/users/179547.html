I'm female, obvious Scottish descent with massive shoulders (never needed shoulder pads in the 80's), red hair, and pale pale pale... practically translucent skin. Married to the man of my dreams for 11 years this coming up June ('08). We have two darling, yet devious children.

If ya wanna know more... or just open to abuse possibilities, the website link goes to my livejournal. Email me if you think you wanna be a part of my friend's list

I enjoy reading the some incredibly talented people on this website. I'm embarrassed to post my stuff alongside them. I need to update my favorite authors, because it doesn't begin to cover some of the wealth and richness that people bring to this site.

As far as my stories are concerned... Usually real life causes me to delay posting stuff. RL Stuff gets me off track and I really have to focus myself to write, which usually requires blunt objects...

Thanks for reading! Let the good times role!
