Well, it was time to change of profile, don't you think? I'll just introduce myself quickly, and get on with thing that might actually interest you. Let's see… My name is Aelys, I'm 17 and I live in Belgium. Obviously, English is not my native language, but it is the language in which I love to write. I love quite an amount of anime and mangas… And others books, movies and games of course.

On to the writing… I currently amuse myself a lot with Draco/Ginny fics, from Harry Potter. Don't ask why, I just feel they're a very special couple… And they're fun to write.

I know, I haven't written anything in quite some time... Shame on me.
