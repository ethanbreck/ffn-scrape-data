Author has written 10 stories for Matrix, Star Wars, Harry Potter, Yu Yu Hakusho, Naruto, Detective Conan/Case Closed, Avatar: Last Airbender, Doctor Who, Ouran High School Host Club, and Sherlock.

Current interests: I enjoy reading, writing, singing (acapella particularly), art and bookbinding.

About me: I'm 25 going on 26 as of Sep 4. I've been reading fanfiction ever since stumbling onto Tamora Pierce's RPG site Steelsings when I was about maybe fifteen or so. Since then I branched out to Star Wars, Harry Potter, and then reading anime-based fanfiction, which I only started watching when I almost nineteen. My book reading interests are varied but include Fantasy, Sci-fi, classic novels, and the occasional mystery. I love adventure books and despise vampires with a passion. The only vampire stories I have found worth reading are Bram Stoker's "Dracula" and Barbara Hambly's "Those Who Hunt By Night." On the other hand, I DO like werewolves. Don't ask me why, or I will explain in detail how vampires are to elves as werewolves are to dwarves in fantasy books.

I was home schooled from kindergarten to twelfth grade and started drawing when I was sixteen. I am currently trying to achieve a BFA in Painting, minoring in Art History on the side.

After you're done reading my stories, please check out my favorites list! These are really good authors. Plus, I have a Harry Potter C2 Community with excellent fanfiction I've read at least once, if not more. The stories are that good, I promise.

If you aren't interested in any of my fanfiction and are just visiting my account because I have reviewed something you have written, please consider visiting my gallery at deviant art instead. The link for my gallery is posted as my webpage. I accept anonymous reviews, although if you're from this website let me know and give me your penname. :-)

Standard disclaimer for all: I own nothing, I am not making any profit by my stories. These are just for fun and killing time.
