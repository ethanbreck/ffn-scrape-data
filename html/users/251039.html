Born February, 15th 1982 in a little old hospital which became a library later. Life can be strange, sometimes. Me, a bookworm, born in a library.
Like you might have noticed I love books and reading in general, and also Mangas and Animés.

Some of my favourite Authors: Tamora Pierce, Lilian Jackson Braun, Gabriele Beyerlein, Susan Cooper, Andreas D. Hesse, Philip Pullman ...

Grammar & Vocabulary Mistakes: Sorry, I'm not a native speaker. I'm from Germany. I hope you can understand my stories.

I've started two series at the moment, which I so hope to finish any time soon.
My first fic ever (my baby) is 'A Lady's Destiny', but due to my lack of writing skills back then I will rewrite all the old chapters, and make chapters of equal length out of them.
And I've decided to split the fic into three parts and make it a series (titles might change):

THE WANDERER SERIES:
(1) A Lady's Destiny
(2) Woodland Spirits
(3) The Oak

The idea for the second series came to me while sorting out my books. I noticed how many of the adventure stories are about people bringing important messages or items to their kinds and so on. I really liked to read those stories. So I at first just wanted to write some thing like ‘Green Rider’ from Kristen Britain or ‘The Letter for the King’ from Tonke Dragt. But my crazy little mind has come up with so much possibilities to make the story more complex and mysterious that I have to make a series with four parts out of it.

FOUR ELEMENTS SERIES:
(1) The Message
(2) In the Wild Forests
(3) Dark Legacy
(4) Raging Elements

The names I use are from a baby name book, I don't copy from others. That goes as well for my stories. If there are similar ideas like mine in other fanfics, it's only pure chance.

At last I want to warn you. I’ve just started studying at the University of Munich and therefore I’m veeeery busy. I have hardly ever time to write, so there might/ will be gaps between the updates. I can’t help it, sorry.
