Author has written 6 stories for Rurouni Kenshin, Fairy Tales, Hana Yori Dango, and Saiyuki.

Wanna find the chance to practice writing... for the challenge (and fun) of it. So here I am!

And I finished my first lengthy fanfiction (Romance of the Seventh Star: Gaiden) on 04 Feb 2014, after a period of three years! I'm so pleased I put up a fresh profile image!

Currently in July 2017... I'm producing a second edition of Romance of the Seventh Star: Gaiden. Quite a lot of parsing, condensing and restructuring to be done in the hindsight of the first edition. I'm aiming for a more fluid style and succinctness of expression and content. If I'm successful, this redux will definitely be a more compact and emotionally satisfying read.

Meanwhile... my other project - The World Goes Round, has to suffer a lag in updates. For anyone who cares, my apologies!
