In Love and War

7/13/17

Alright, due to hits for ILW skyrocketing and people contacting me about the story already, I figured I'd post an update.

Zodiac Age just came out 2 days ago, and I've been hard at work on playing through it. It's even more gorgeous than before, and the remastered music is AMAZING. Of course, my inspiration for ILW was back up for a little while before it, and now it's in full-force. I hope something has changed this time around, because the fact that I STILL have not gotten the rewritten portions of the story up yet are killing me and my motivation. Sometimes I wonder if it will ever get up. And it needs to be up before a new chapter ever comes out. You might ask why, but the only thing I can say is that it'll make perfect sense when the current chapters are rewritten and done.

The Facebook page for ILW is likely going to be taken down and replaced with a Tumblr blog. After many years of swearing it off, I caved earlier this year and made one for myself, which is mostly shitposting about different fandoms, but I think I'm going to make a side blog just for ILW. I'll post a link here when it's up; which may be later tonight, if I can tear myself away from the game. If you're interested and have a Tumblr of your own, come on over and chat. You don't have to worry about me posting any kind of political stuff on my pages; I try to keep that as far away from social media as possible, especially when I use the platform for fandom more than anything else. EDIT: That was quicker than anticipated. I named the blog 'Invitation to Heresy', after that one area in the Zertinan Caverns.

Check out the awesome render of Gabranth and Aralenne I had commissioned! It completely took my breath away. The artist's page is here: It's under the name 'Gabranth'.

In Love and War: The Dalmascan Years (THE PREQUEL)

Details the events from the time Aralenne came to Dalmasca as a child up until Ashe and Rasler's wedding. Includes manga events. YES, there IS a FFXII manga, I urge you all to google it and check it out!

In Love and War: The Archadian Years (THE SHORT STORIES)

Not a full-fledged story. More like a novella. Details the two years Aralenne spent in The Empire. Each chapter would be a separate anecdote of life in Archadia, in no particular order. Goes into more depth on her relationship with the Solidor family. Also goes more into her relationship with the judges, namely Gabranth and Drace. Focuses mainly on Lenne and Larsa. I'll be looking to readers and reviewers for ideas they want to see come to life in this one.

In Love and War: The Sequel?

I'm confident that this will happen one day... I have plenty of ideas for it, but by the time ILW ends, I'll be looking for readers on what they'd want to see in a sequel. I've never played Revenant Wings, so that's not really an option as far as sequel ideas. The only for sure thing I have in mind is that Rozarria will be heavily involved.


FINAL FANTASY XII

In Love and War comes first, but I've been nursing a plot bunny about another potential FFXII story for a few months now, involving Vayne this time around. It will be... different, to say the very least.


FINAL FANTASY XV

Yes, I have caught the XV bug. Yes, parts of the plot were not perfect, but it was full of feels and the main characters were so engaging for me, and... Ugh. I love this game. It definitely ranks up there with VII and XII for me. I've got an OC thought up and a story planned out. ILW will be taking priority though right now.

Title: The Whips of God
