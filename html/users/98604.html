Author has written 25 stories for West Wing, Sookie Stackhouse/Southern Vampire Mysteries, True Blood, Due South, and Twilight.

I've been writing since 2000. I discovered fanfiction my first summer off after becoming a teacher. My family nearly staged an intervention. After meeting some of the greatest writers, I was urged to try my hand. I was seduced by Due South...Woo'ed by the West Wing...Glamoured by the Sookie Stackhouse Series and now Tantalized by Twilight. Ten years later, here I am. Not everything I've written is on fanfiction.net so if you like a certain fandom I've written for, check out my web link. I'll try to keep it updated.

I hope you enjoy the fic. :)
