Author has written 4 stories for Final Fantasy VIII, and Fallout.

The last time I updated this page I was 16. I sit here now at 25.

What can I say... I grew out of writing Final Fantasy fanfiction and though I knew there was potential for Checkered Destinies, I admittedly will NOT be finishing the piece. Having grown up a little, that is a writing style and a type of character I can no longer work with. That chapter in writing is over. Still, none the less amusing to keep the stories up for nostalgia's sake.

I am in the middle of working through a Fallout: New Vegas fanfiction. I admit; I have a deep affection for Charon from Fallout 3 and Craig Boone from New Vegas. The piece I Forgot to Remember will be a work in progress.

Gaming and entertainment interests: Band of Brothers, Fallout, Resident Evil, Borderlands, Assasin's Creed, Dragon Age... anything by Tarantino or Miyazaki. Kitties. Ancient Rome.

A/S/L: 25 year old elusive femalian hailing from the distant Canadialand.
