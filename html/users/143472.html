I'm an average person who is absolutely infatuated with...anime/manga! I'm a huge CLAMP fan (Tokyo Babylon mainly, X/1999, CCS, MKR, Clover ain't bad), but I also like a variety of other anime/manga including Slayers (woo hoo!), Gundam Wing, Cowboy Bebop, Ruroni Kenshin, Neon Genesis Evangelion, Fushigi Yuugi, Angel Sanctuary, Vision of Escaflowne, Princess Mononoke, and others that, frankly, I can't think of at the moment. My fav PS2 game is Tekken Tag Tournament b/c the music is, like, cool, ya know. Fav N64 game: the two Legend of Zelda games. (Link!)
I'm weird, so I like yaoi couples. Yukito/Touya (CCS), Kamui/Fuuma(X/1999), Zelgadis/Xellos, Xellos/Valgaav (Slayers), Wufei/Treize (GW)Kenshin/Sanosuke (RK), Shinji/Kaoru, Shinji/any other guy (NGE), Kira/Setsuna, Rosiel/Katan (AS), Hwoarang/Jin, Kazuya/Jin, Hwoarang/Baek, Jin/any guy, Hwoarang/any guy, just to name a few. The girl that I don't mind being yuri is Hokuto (Tokyo Babylon). She's allowed to b/c she's the only female character in anime I like (I like Lina (Slayers), but Lina's not yuri at all while Hokuto is :ahem: nevermind). My absolute favorite couple is...and I'm sure many will agree...Subaru Sumeragi and Seishiro Sakurazuka from Tokyo Babylon and X/1999. :sigh: I have some fics about that, don't you worry! About my fics...I like writing humorous fan ficitons, but you'll sometimes see a serious fic or an original work. Anyway, enjoy!
