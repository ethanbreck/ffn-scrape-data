Allright, here's the thing. Fanfiction.net used to be my numba one stop in my internet surfing and fanfiction used to be my ultimate creative outlet. But it's not anymore. Fictionpress.com doesn't cut it either. Frankly, I just found something better to do. It happens and I'm sorry. I'm not ragging on fanfiction. Some of it is still great and it's a good thing to do if you're talented and need a little inspiration. But I've got a good hold on originals now and I only read the occasional fanfic once in a blue moon. M-O-O-N. That spells moon. So don't come sniffing around expecting updates except for maybe once a year, if that. Keep on keeping on, folks and best of luck to ya.
