July 14, 2013
I update my profile, and the next thing I know, four and a half years go by with no further word. Well, that's just not sustainable, is it? I really need to start bringing some stuff up to the foreground. I've been writing (a lot) but as I intend for most of that material to be published at some unspecified date, I can't put it here. So I guess I'd best try to figure out what I'm going to use this account for, because it really is time for me to get active in the fanfiction community again. I need to write, every day, and if I'm not writing stuff to be published, I guess that leaves fanfics as my fallback.

Let the writing commence anew!
