I'm a 28 years old Brazilian girl who is completely CRAZY about watching movies and reading books. My favorite movie series is STAR WARS, and therefore the first time I ever read a fanfic was in the SW fandom. Later, I also read fics in dozens of other fandoms. So, pretty soon I was addicted to fanfiction. Eventually I ventured writing my own fanfics, starting in the SW fandom. Eventually I moved on to the Harry Potter universe, but never giving up or denying my roots in the SW fandom. Anyway, I hope you enjoy my work! But even if you don't, PLEASE, leave a review! I can deal with CONSTRUCTIVE CRITICISM! Just one more thing before I go: I posted a LOTR (sort of) fic with my dear friend and fellow nut Claudia. You can find the fic under TRMJandMaram68 :) I support the following ships: SW: Luke/Mara; Han/Leia (Well, DUH! That's obvious, LOL) HP: Ron/Hermione; Harry/Ginny; Bill/Fleur MAY THE FORCE BE WITH YOU, ALWAYS! Other fandoms I like: BBT, BSG, Farscape, Superman, Legend of the Seeker, SG-1, Supernatural, HIMYM, Chuck, Castle, Friends, GLEE (latest obsession as well as the pairing KURT/KAROFSKY)!
