Hey all you readers out there, Miss Chang Po here! I'm just your average fanfic writer Extraodinaire... well, not really. I really do like writing fanfictions, but I also love writing in general. I do it all the time... but you guys probably already guessed that.

My fave Anime titles include Sailor Moon, Ronin Warriors, Gundam Wing, Outlaw Star, Dragon Ball Z, Magic Knight Rayearth, Card Captor Sakura, Escaflowne, The Big O, Revolutionary Girl Utena, Voltrons: Defenders of the Universe, and Hana Yori Dango, Cowboy Bebop, Rurouni Kenshin, Trigun, Inu Yasha, Kikaider, Witch Hunter Robin, Wolf's Rain, Samurai Champloo, Fullmetal Alchemist... and I have to admit to enjoying Naruto as well. (blushes heavily)

My fave pairings include...

Usagi x Mamoru (duh!), Senshi x Shitennou; Heero x Relena (ha! take that, Amataru-chan!), Wufei x Sally, Wufei x Relena (yah, I know it's weird, too), Duo x Relena; Kenshin x Kaoru; Vash x Meryl; Faye x Spike; Inu Yasha x Kagome, Sesshomaru x Kagome, Sesshomaru x Kagura; Jin x Fuu... among many many others.

I consider myself to be a non-yaoi fan, but I have enjoyed reading a few shounen-ai fics in the past (tastefully done of course, I'm aprude at heart if nothing else...) I'm for the usual pairings, but I like it when an author can convince that a non-canon pair will actually work. (Really now... 5xRP? Who would have thought...!)

My favorite manga include Trigun Maximum, Tsubasa, Rurouni Kenshin, and Saiyuki. My favorite books include 'The Eight' by Katherine Neville, 'The Lion, The Witch, and the Wardrobe' by C.S. Lewis (yes, I'm a dork), 'A Wrinkle in Time' by Madeline L'Engle, 'The Princess Bride' adapted by William Goldman, 'Angels and Demons' and the 'Da Vinci Code', 'Howl's Moving Castle' by Diane Wynne Jones (who would have thought that Christian Bale could sound so sexy as a cartoon character :drool:) Ahem, anyways...
