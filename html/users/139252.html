Okay, here's the deal, I work the best off of critiques in any way shape or form. Reviews/comments on my stories give me more reason to write, because I know there's at least someone out there to read. If there's no audience, then why write in the first place. And I know you're saying, "Well there's a count of how many people visit the story." Yes, I know, but I want to know YOUR thoughts on it. C'mon, most of the time people don't want you're opinion. I'm askin' for it! Throw me a bone here, ya know?

I used to talk to a lot of my readers. I like having that little, miniscule bond. I like knowing what you guys think. So leave reviews, it makes me update quicker.

"I was reading my story A Sussuration of Immaturity, and now that I think about it, everyone seems really bipolar... it's quite amusing."

I have a certain way of doing things. If the story is old, it stays old. I don't like changing things and revising them because they won't have the same crazyness (is that even a word? Did I spell that right?) that they had when I first wrote them. Each story I have up here gives me some sense of nostalgia when I read over it. I suppose it's another one of those sentimental value things that are mine and mine alone. The stories are also a reminder to myself of how far I've come as a writer, and I hope to surpass what is here with flying colors.
