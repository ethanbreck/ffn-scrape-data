I am an insane, anime-obsessed girl. What more can you ask of me? I love anime, in particular as many can tell from my list of fanfiction, Weiss. I do love Yami no Matsuei, Kenshin, Fruits Basket, Gravitation, Vampire Princess Miyu, FY and many many more.

The list is just too long!!!

All of what I post here is fanfiction! Meaning I don't own any of the characters! They all have wonderful authors who I adore because they came up with these. I'm only borrowing them for a moment and a couple words because or else... my mind would never leave me be! I hope you enjoy these stories... either way let me know!

Now I know I've started a lot of fics and many have yet to be finished. All I can say is that I'm really sorry... life is keeping me busy. But I haven't given up on ANY of my stories and do plan on completing them. I have notes on many of them on where I want to go with the plot line and etc. In any case, I hope you can be patient with me!

Thank you to everyone who has given me support and feedback throughout the years. It means a great deal to me.
