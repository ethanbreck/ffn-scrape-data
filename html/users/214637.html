Hi there!

Welcome to my humble abode. I'm a 27 (soon to be 28) year old, San Joaquin valley gal hailing from the middle-of-nowhere California. When I'm not working at a bank, I am here in some capacity.

To everyone who's taken the time to read and review my work, THANK YOU! I always enjoy hearing people's positive reactions to my work as well as constructive criticism (cough, cough hint! cough).

I've got a few stories in the works right now. Work has been crazier than usual (we are working one man short and I've been having to pull some serious overtime) so, while not completely on hold, my writing is definitely slower than usual. Here's a quick run-down:

The Logistics of Being Superman (always in process): Chronicles (somewhat) the more amusing aspects of being a spandex-clad superhero. There are so many good (and hilarious) topics out there, its hard to pick one to expand upon. If there's a particular subject you'd like to see chronicled, let me know. I'm always open to suggestions!

The Window Washer: What happens when Superman takes his surroundings for granted? Note: I had finished this piece however, due to some unexpected backlash from my readers, I have removed the fifth (and supposed final) chapter pending review. Some of the comments made were, in my opinion, uncalled for. I had the entire fic completed the way I wanted before I began posting so chapter 5 was not a cop-out due to writer's block. Anyways, chapter 5 of WW will be reposted at such a time as I finish writing the second & third parts. I apologize for any inconvenience.

A Love Less Perfect (in process): An AU that follows Clark's life should certain events in S:I not happen the way they were supposed to. I began writing this a year ago and have well over 100 pages written but only 5 of an estimated 30 chapters completed. It is currently on hold as my muse is focusing on WW at this time.

Updates:

1/11: Happy New Year everyone!

~VictorianSuperman
