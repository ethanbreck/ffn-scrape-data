People say you live a million lives through books. They never said anything else. Un-explanatory. Then again, it is just a logo to try and get people to read. I wonder if it worked.

I found this in a journal of mine from a long time ago, it read: "I don't hate life, my school, my dad, nor breathing. As for hating me? I'll never know. I don't think I'll ever truly understand, me."

It's been about 3 years since I wrote that. And I still haven't found the answer.

I don't own anything except for my own ideas- so don't sue and plagiarism is a crime (commited by laziness)...

Many of these BtVS fics have been beta'd by Maleila. Mally is lovely, and I couldn't breathe without her. She's been the one true person I could cling to in my life. She knows it, but I love you, Maleila.
