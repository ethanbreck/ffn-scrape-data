[Updated March 8, 2014]

Oh. My gods.

I apologize deeply to what people have followed me in more recent years, but my 'fanfic' these days is more collaborative works / roleplay for WoW rather than those samples of my writing style of over a decade ago, I pretty much haven't even looked at this account in years, and I frankly am not likely to start again. If I ever get back into fanfic I'll probably be posting over on AO3 anyhow. Maybe I'll remember to come back and slap a link up. Probably I won't. I've got the WoW collaborative / roleplay stuff and a novel that I'm supposed to be working on but it's slow-going.

Happy fanficcing, folks. Without the people who can actually get fired up to write it, I wouldn't have any to read, and so thank you. Fanfic gave me some of the most shining moments of my early teenage years and showed me that I didn't need to get published to be a writer, and frankly, without that, I wouldn't be working on something I'd like to get published now.

And by the hells, I apologize even more deeply to anyone who had to read that screed of a profile my 14-year-old self wrote, although I stand by the fact that anyone who gets outright hostile at a thirteen-year-old over them not being great at writing yet deserves to fall through to the earth's core.

-Blancheflor-
