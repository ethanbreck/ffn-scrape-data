I love to read, I love to write, I love to draw. I love Legacy of Kain, Captain Hook, and Middle-Earth. Probably not in that order, but who could put those things in order, eh? Lately, owing to real life and job things like commuting, I don't have half as much time to read and review as I would like. Trust me, even if you think I'm not reading, I probably am. If you think I've missed out on something good you've written while I've been inactive, just e-mail me and let me know.

I am also registered as a beta reader, but I'm afraid I'm a bit busy to take on new beta'ing projects at the moment.

Thanks for visiting my page, please drop by again. If you want to see any of my LoK artwork (and other pics) drop by my gallery (that website link thing)
