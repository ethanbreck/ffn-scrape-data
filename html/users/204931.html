I've decided to maybe start writing again... here is the line up..maybe.

On The Drawing Board

The Next Level - Ranma gains the interest of some old and powerful people who wish to teach him the original secret of Martial Arts and Magic.

A Different Fate 2- Continuation of A Different Fate. I really don't want to mar the "completed" story directly by adding chapters. I skim through my reviews time to time in my email, and I've gotten so many requests over the years to continue my most popular story... it's humbling. Allow me to entertain you some more. I may have to take a "refresher" read of Love Hina first, before continuing. I have to get the characters' personalities back into my head.

Bonds of Slavery- This may or may not be the final title. I've got the idea kicking around in my head to make some lovely ladies accidentally Ranma's slaves by mystical means. Full service DearS style.

Alive and Kickin Stories
Eezoon, Dragons Ring (Ch 7 LEMON)

Has a Pulse

To Become a God- This one has Unique potential, so it may be continued.

Stories in Limbo
Birth of a Matriarch - This one has potential, but I have no idea where i'm going to go with it. I didn't start this with the end entirely in mind.

Addicted to Your Embrace- This may be revived. I have been away from both Love Hina fiction and the manga for a while. I have to re-read it before I can think of where to go from here. I've forgotten alot of characterization.

Dead
Clear Blue Sky- Maybe an alternate, less OOC version may be created, but this one in this form is most likely dead for good.

Hina 1/2- This may have a comeback one day, but I generally don't like alterverses, and they are extremely annoying to write properly. You technically should have all the major events and people show up throughout the series that aren't influenced by the change you made. This means I'd have to follow through the Ranma Manga, and Hina Manga and keep major events in order, although there will be different responses to them.

NEVER

When Things Happen - Probably never at any rate. There are better similar stories already out there, and I'd be feeding too much off of them. Like, Ships in the Night. And the Ranma version of the same title.

Happosai's Revenge- It comes off to me as a great short, entertaining story that does not have the feel of an uncompleted plot. Sure it'd be fun to see where it goes after this...but that's up to the reader's imagination and would ruin the story to take that away. I refuse to become one of those who write a story with no intention of wrapping it up, or dragging it on forever. A story has a beginning, middle and end, just like life does. Plus I want to have at least one No-Sh*t completed story.

Complicated Feelings - This story is my first venture into writing, and is somewhat painful for me to read. Not to mention how stupid I was with the community back then...getting into a flame-fest with the then Eva "Big Boys". Though to this day I still think the "Big Boys" writing sucked worse than I realize this sucks now. Though it should not be that much a surprise. The Eva Fanficdom has an averagely lower quality than Ranma. Though, in honesty, some of them have gotten decent compared to their old days. They probably were just 13 or something when they started.
