Bio

I'm a twenty-seven year old girl now who no longer wishes to keep up with how old she is. I enjoy writing and drawing. Eventually I hope to publish some stuff. Heh... if I can ever get myself to actually finishing a book. Yeah... I'm hopeless... ;p

Music, role playing (not D&D, more like I pick some characters, someone else has some and we write what they do/say), video games (usually of the rpg genre, can't help it, I love stories), and being out in nature are my other passions.

Also... being odd, hyper, and random are the norm for me.

Story Progress

Digital Hell - This story has been on hiatus for a long time now. Over 5 years! Dang... I am going to begin writing it again since it's still very much my baby and I've got so much planned for it! (Crazily enough, that includes a sequel and a prequel.) With all the time that has passed however, I have found that what I had originally planned as far as Dante's and Virgil's pasts is... bleh. Honestly, rereading what I'd had for chapter 3... it was kinda dumb and sounded very amateurish. I've grown a lot, learned a lot, and as a result have changed a lot as both a DMC fan and a writer. I will be rewriting parts of chapter 3 to fit my new direction. I'll more than likely be rewriting parts of the earlier chapters (especially the little summary at the beginning of chapter one. Makes me wanna gag!) at a later time but rest assured I will continue this story!

Ch 2 is officially completed on June 26th 2009; Ch 3 is 50 percent completed as of Nov 9 2009
