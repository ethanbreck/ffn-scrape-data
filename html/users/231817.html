Author has written 3 stories for Lord of the Rings.

Currently a gap year student filling time and keeping my brain working by writing fanfiction! I love Eomer/Lothiriel- I've just started a story on here about them called 'The Clod and the Pebble' (which has drawn at least some inspiration from the William Blake poem).

The other two stories on here I wrote when I was 15, so the style is a little different (but depressingly I think a little better). Read, enjoy, review. I've love constructive criticism because it really helps alter my thinking patterns for the better.

Oh, and if you happen to be an admissions tutor for International Relations or History... Go on, you know you want me!
