Author has written 20 stories for Dragon Ball Z.

Hello, I'm Wind2. I like to write and I like anime, hence the stories on here. I realize they're all Dragonball Z, but I used to have quite a love for that show, and it seems to be the only world I can write fics in. There's a good 20 of them, so take your pic, and don't be afraid to tackle some of the longer ones, my chapters are pretty short, I promise.

What I really recommend you read: The Real Folk Blues, Blue Skies, Broken Hearts...Next 12 Exits, and Killer Queen

We Blame Canada and Behind Blue Eyes will probably never be finished. I know, I know, I bowed out of WBC on the last chapter. I'm sorry. BBE has no plot going forward, it's just a failed fic, I should probably just take it off.

The other fics are good, give them all a read, too.

Shout out goes to Goku's Daughter. This girl is probably the only reason I have done any writing at all. I envy her talent. Six years of friendship, all thanks to this website...

UPDATE! 8/26/08: So, the initial comeback sorta failed, but have no fear! Goku's Daughter and I are trying to get Blood On The Throne back on track! Chapter 3 should be up either tonight or tomorrow, and we're going to get the plot all sorted out and our characters down (trust me, it was painful rereading some of our chapters...we're doing a lot of editing). So head over to her profile (you can find her under my fav. authors) to check it out

I still want to do a sequel to Killer Queen, but I don't know if I'll ever get over the writer's block...

I know I haven't updated in ages, and it's nice to see they're still getting the occasional hit, so please leave me a pretty review so I know you still appreciate my work and I know I totally haven't failed as a writer. Thanks! :-)
