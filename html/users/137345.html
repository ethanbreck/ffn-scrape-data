Hello everyone!

Just a few words about me - I'm Canadian though I'm currently in the US finishing my second MA. Most days I feel like I'm likely the most over-educated person in the world - eleven straight years of university will do that to a person.

For anyone who's ever tried to follow me knows that my updating is sporadic at best, and I'm notorious at leaving people hanging...forever. Though with all your wonderful reviews on my Castle fics, I've been much MUCH better at not giving up on my stories. But I warn you - my muse is a fickle, fickle creature.

:hangs head in shame:

Story Status: Basically, don't hold your breath on anything that wasn't a one-shot to begin with. I'm actually incapable of truly finishing anything I start - mostly cause life catches up with me and by the time I can get back to it, it's been so long, I can't pick up the thread again.

Right now, truly loving me that Casketty goodness. Again - my dear friends - enjoy my works, but don't get too attached - or else be prepared to exact a review bombing campaign of such a magnitude that I actually finish something that I start...

update: I have now finished not one, but TWO multi-chapter fics! My god - never thought I, or any of you, for that matter, would ever see the day! And it's all b/c of you! Keep those reviews coming! You'll make a story-finisher out of me yet!
