Hello everyone!

My (nick)name is Munsje and I'm an eighteen year old girl from the Netherlands. I work at the local theatre, behind the bar mind you, and I hope to start working in a new restaurant soon and if they don't hire me I'm going to start working for a catering service that works on party boats. I don't go to school, but I am taking several at-home courses so my brain has to function every once in a while.
To tell you a little something about me, here's a short list of my favourite things :) (when the dogs bite, when the bees stings)

stories I'm working on

Currently I'm very busy writing (and hopefully finishing) Expecto patwhatta? a story about Lily Minevra Potter, the daughter of Ginny and Harry, and her time at Hogwarts. I finally plotted the whole thing! And I've always wanted to write a story about Petunia Dursley. Yeah, go figure. But just things from her point of view, so I think I'm going to write about her after I've finished expecto, and I think it's better to wait until I've read the seventh book anyway, because we're supposed to learn a whole lot more about her.

I was writing a whole story about Davey Jones (from PoTC, for those who're wondering) but quit after I found out about his connected with Tia Dalma (I had introduced an Original Character to be the one that broke his heart) and everything else is just doodles, basically.

Anyhow, I'm signing off, thanks for viewing my profile page and read and review some of my stories, if you're up to it!

Greets,
