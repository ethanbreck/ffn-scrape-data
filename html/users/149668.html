Being ScoobyGal55 is only her night job. Anybody who knew the true identity of the Super!Writer! ScoobyGal55 would know that day SG55 and night SG55 were two completely different people. But if you didn't know her, you may assume that SG55 was always the Super!Hero!Writer!OtherAdjectives! that she portrays herself to be.

Alyssa, mild mannered, C average student, hid between her peers, secretly laughing at their stupidity. How could people not tell that she and SG55 were one and the same? Well, I'll tell you. It was her genius disguise.

See those glasses on her face. Ha! SG55 doesn't wear glasses! Genius, right?

Some ask, how did she come up with the Super!Hero!AwesomePerson!Writer! name? Some say it came to her in a dream. Some say it is a homage to Buffy The Vampire Slayer or Scooby Doo. Those people are wrong. Really it because, uh, she, uh, had to make an, er, aol screen name and lacked, what'dya call it, creativity.

But, uh, no matter! She was... is great! Behold the greatness! SG55 to the rescue!
