Author has written 11 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Naruto.

I am born in June of 1984, and married to Lewis Sammons. I am double majoring in psychology & nursing BSN in college with a minor in biology. I love to write, from fanfics to original stories to poetry. I love music, all music except rap and pop. My fav is hard rock/ death metal. My fav band is Tool. I play the clarinet and saxaphone.

My original pen name on here was Tenkuh, so if you read reviews where people call me that, that is why.

Fav mangas are:

Lovesless, Vampire Game, Demon Diary, Judas, Anima, Death Note, Rurouni Kenshin, Chobits, X 1999, etc... but pretty much anything by Clamp.

Fav animes are:

Naruto, Dragon Ball Z (not GT), Ninja Scroll, Inu Yasha, Gundam Wing, Full Metal Alchemist, Princess Mononoke, Ghost In The Shell, Armitage, Cowboy Beepop, etc.

Fav story I've written so far is:

Whispered Hell In Her Eyes, but not because it is my best fanfic cause in my opinion it is not. It was my first fanfic, and it took me years to write, literally, due to college & illness & having to take a leave of absence from fanficing due to my illness. When I read the story, I can see my writing skills and style progress and change. That's why it's my fav.

My writing style tends to be morbid... yeah... after I'm done w/ Somewhere In Time there will be only two "happy" fics in my categories. Yaoi does not bother me, as you can tell from my stories, although I do not believe guyXguy relationships are 'right'. When I write my stories, I like to pair characters with the most conflict between them without regards to their gender in order to create the best angst in my stories. I like to write stories about the character relationships that interest me the most and which will create the most tension to provoke character development. This is why most of my stories have Uchihacest in them and stuff like that, cause I like to write messed up angsty stories. But please take care to notice my reasons for writing this are true, cause unlike some stories that's just sex without any real story line- My stories actually have a real plot line instead of just typewritten screwed up porno. :)

Ill-Gotten Gain (DBZ)- future, sequel to Heaven Foresake Me, Gohan won't appear til Summer of 09 due to college
