Technically Active Fics (It may not look like it sometimes, but I'm trying to write, I really am!)

Harry Potter, the Child-Who-Lived: Harry Potter A/U, no pairings

Premise - Instead of Harry being a boy (Harry James Potter) Harry as a girl (Harlequin Carlotta-James Potter). Starts where Philosopher's Stone/Sorcerer's Stone begins.

Notes on the Fic - I wanted to see what would happen if Harry was a girl instead of a boy. That's a very small change, considering, so the first fic with Harley!Harry is going to stick fairly close to the book canon. Any future fics, of course, will be increasingly divergent from the canon as the alterations in the story due to my change build up.

Status of Fic - About 75-80 percent complete. As soon as I move this story onto my laptop, I'll be able to work on it far more often - the laptop is slightly more portable then that clunky old PC it's been sitting on, you know.

Clockwork Nightingale: Yu-Gi-Oh A/U, no pairings

Premise - Extreme divergence from Yu-Gi-Oh canon storyline. Some members of cast are part of 'Ban-Ile' race, some are part of the 'Demon' race. Elements from Manga!canon and Anime!canon are present. Features version of Disability!Yuugi.

Notes on the Fic- I was totally inspired by a story known as 'An Angel in my Closet', by Hot Ice. However, I was also determined not to rip Hot Ice off. Thus, instead of angels, I have Ban-Ile, which is an angel-like race (angel-like in apperance ONLY - read the fic if you want specifics). This has since grown into an epic-looking adventure, during which many characters have secrets, some of which not even they know they have...
