About Me

What's there to say really? I'm 30, from Scotland, Ayrshire to be exact in the west of it. Currently back at college doing an art course after finishing three years of computing, how time flies. Hobbies include games, writing, drawing, reading, listening to music. Mostly.

Games wise...I'm a big fan of Silent Hill series, Legacy of Kain, Oblivion, Morrowind, the Witcher (yes I know it was a book long before it became a game.), Assassin's Creed and on and off the Final Fantasy series. Though I haven't played any of them since 10.

Writing wise...On here I USED to have a couple of Legacy of Kain fics which ended up horribly neglected and taken down as I wasn't likely to finish them anytime soon. The only one that's left is the one about Malek only because it was actually finished. Though could probably do with a rewrite.
For a while there, I didn't do any fanfiction at all, at least until I discovered Oblivion and Morrowind and that was me. Soo currently most of my Oblivion fics are at my DeviantArt account and may or may not be shifted over here. Except the Dark Brotherhood story, it's staying at DA for now.

Not a lot else I can say here. I'm always glad for a bit of feedback, lets me know where I'm going wrong and what to watch out for.

And that's about it really.
