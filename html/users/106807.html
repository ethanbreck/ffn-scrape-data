Author has written 14 stories for Doctor Who, Once Upon a Time, and Blackpool.

I've been in various fandoms for many, many years. I'm currently most interested in The Vampire Diaries and Once Upon a Time, but I still have a deep fondness for Harry Potter and Doctor Who. I can be found various places on the internet such as Twitter, though I rarely tweet anything; Live Journal, which I've had for nearly 10 years and rarely update anymore; and most recently Tumblr, which is where I am most active at the moment.

I am a shipper at heart, and love a good romantic fic.

I am in the process of re-uploading a number of fan fics I wrote in 2006-07 back during my Doctor Who days. These are all ones that were either posted to ff.net or live journal and were removed for personal reasons. However, I have decided I will be re-uploading these fics once again.
