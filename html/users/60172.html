I am 15 (5.18.87), tall (5.7), brunette, and my name is Kacie E. I am AMERICAN. I am a WRITER. I am WOMAN. I have one older sister (Kristy-16), one younger sister (Emmy-3), and three younger brothers (Andy-13, Matt-10, Adam-7). There are six kids in my family, plus my mom and dad.
