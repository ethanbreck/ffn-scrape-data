Author has written 12 stories for Naruto, Bleach, Rurouni Kenshin, StarTrek: The Original Series, Claymore, Avatar: Last Airbender, and Mass Effect.

I'm currently in college. Writing is something that still interests me, once in a while.

I like watching/reading Naruto, Bleach, Death Note, Elfen Lied, Rurouni Kenshin, 20th Century Boys, Black Lagoon, GTO, and Monster. I've written a couple of Naruto fanfics and I'll probably write a few more on the other series above.

I like writing one-shots mostly because I don't have the confidence to create a real complicated plot for a multi-chapter story that doesn't look bad compared to the actual series. Mostly I write character's introspections, because I like exploring the way my favorite characters think.

-Team Dysfunctional FINISHED!. This is my first attempt to actually write something that isn't a one-shot. I plan to cover the entire Wave Arc and start a sequel to cover the Chuunin Exams if people like it enough. Enjoy!

-Protecting the Future is done. And so ends my first step into the RK world. It's one of my best works to date, I think. Hm, I seem to have picked up a disturbing trend of alternating between funny(and perverted) fics and serious ones. I wonder what I'll write next...

-The Quest For High Quality Fabric is done. Another story similar to Catch That Pervert, where I borrow some characters and put them in hilarious situations. Read and review.

-Is Pride A Sin?, my first Bleach fanfic is done. I've wanted to write Kuchiki Byakuya for a while now and thought that recent events in ch. 247, plus his backstory, and Rukia's backstory would be material to make a great story about his feelings toward Rukia. And so, enjoy. And expect more from me about Byakuya.
