Author has written 3 stories for Final Fantasy VII, Fairy Tales, and Song of the Lioness.

If you are here for my stories, well, please note that they were written during a large part of my adolescence when my writing was quite...unpolished. But who am I to say that my writing is polished even now? I have nothing recent below...I keep the stories up for anyone to stumble upon and the curious visitor. So here is your fair warning, even I wrinkle my nose at them. But by all means...read away.

Can't quite think of any other reason you are here other that the fact that I might have reviewed a story of yours. And that, I must say, is a feat. These days, I hardly think to review a work unless you, the author, has made me believe your story.

In this sense, you've made me believe an established relationship in the story, made me believe in characters that are completely OOC, or pairing a crack!couple together. I compare everything I read to the actual canon, so if you can convince me that the whatever you are writing can fluidly be part of the original work, then...well, you've earned more than one more review on your story. You, dear writer, are growing in skill.

And that has its own merits, don't you agree?

Until next time.

"No thinking - that comes later. You must write your first draft with your heart. Then you rewrite it, with your head. The first key to writing, is to write... not to think."
