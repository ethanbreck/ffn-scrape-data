Young Dan grew up in the boondocks of Siberia, where he came upon a frozen goat-shaman who had been entombed for almost 6 months. Nursing him back to health, young Dan learned the ways of the goat from the shaman, who promptly died as soon as he was back to full health. Dan became so depressed that he travelled to Long Island in New York, killed a teenage boy who was wandering through a dark street, and, using the powers of the goat, assumed the shape of the child's body. He now poses as this teenager, coincidently also named Dan, and waits for the opportunity to unleash his goat powers upon the world.
