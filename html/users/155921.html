About Edgewind

I'm bored most of the time and I sometimes use that time to write. I work 6 days a week and I don't always have much time to devote to my writing. If I go a few months without updating a story, it's no biggie. If I go more than a year, then I may not be coming back. I am fueled by good reviews, especially from new readers. Point being, I have to have a desire to write or I don't write. I can find about anything else to do besides write and if I don't get any satisfaction from posting it here, then I probably won't continue to post. Even if you don't want to type out a review, at least put in something to let me know you read the thing. I at least enjoy when I know someone is reading the stories I post and if no one is, then why post it here when I can just send the text files to my friends for them to read. If you read a story, send a review. That's all I ask.
