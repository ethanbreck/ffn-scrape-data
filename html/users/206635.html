Author has written 12 stories for General Hospital, Doctor Who, Torchwood, Harry Potter, and James Bond.

I write mostly General Hospital fan fiction. I'm a fan of the Jones family and the old WSB days, my favorites being Frisco, Georgie and Anna, so almost all of my fics are Frisco-centric. :)

I also write/read in the Torchwood/Doctor Who, Stargate, NCIS, and Harry Potter fandoms.

UPDATE 3-13-17

I'm not dead!

Okay, I didn't know the links weren't working. I very rarely actually login anymore (still around, just not logged in). I updated with names this time, if the links fail again. If you want a reply, you should probably try me on another site.

While I am still writing, very little is getting finished before the muse moves on to something else. I have lots of half-finished stuff in notebooks I'm trying to get into a publishable state, so I might finally give in and do one of those scrap bit posts. I do have a James Bond/Veronica Mars crossover that's almost done. Have another Phoenix-verse story I was holding on to (it was planned as the last story), but as what was supposed to be next in the series turned into a bit of a plotting mess, I may go ahead and post that one.

UPDATE 12-8-12:

Paying The Price: On permanent hiatus. It's marked as complete, but it is not finished, nor will it ever be. Chapter 56 is the last complete chapter; Chapter 57 is the last of what I have written. :-(

A New Life: Almost finished, actually. I won't start posting again until it's completely written, hopefully next year.

Just Between Friends: Still going. I'm still writing, slowly but surely; unfortunately, most of it's not for the next chapter. It probably won't be updated anytime soon, but I do intend to finish, even if it takes me another decade.

Phoenix!verse: Next up is a sequel to Bonfire Night; I'm hoping to have it up by the end of the year.
