Hello! My name is Dreamsweeper (okay, not really), and I am a 26 year-old teacher (fine, fine... almost a teacher). I love snickers, rolos, and watching reruns of Animaniacs and the HBO series "Band of Brothers." AND I love Grey's Anatomy and Heroes (hmmm... Sylar...hehe... oops, ahem, well...). My little sister, Ducky is not only seven years younger than me, but she's also my beta! I'm glad my parents let me keep her when I found her our doorstep. Hehe, just kidding Ducky.
My favorite book is Gone with the Wind by Margaret Mitchell, and its also my favorite movie. My second favorite book is Princess Bride by S. Morgenstern (or so we think) and its my second favorite movie. I love Harry Potter, but sadly, it isn't my favorite. Neither is Twilight, but still... I love the series.
I've been formulating Saving Grace for quite a while now, and have been writing down different chapters in my notebook since my sophmore year of college (instead of taking notes). I'm glad its received what is a generally good reception, and I hope to make everyone happy by finishing it... eventually.
Unfortunately, I must make this disclaimer: I have a real life that consists of me not focusing on SG for long periods of time, and when (if ever) I receive downtime I can work on it, work on it I shall. I found out long ago that nobody pays anyone for writing fanfiction... darn it. So in between my hectic schedules of being a teacher-in-training, working at Macy's and being apart of my family, I'll work on it whenever I can... promise!

I love criticism, so if you have any words of wisdom, please, leave them. This includes any constructive criticism you have. I LOVE CRITICISM!! Seriously, if its well-thought and well-said, I'll take it!

Okay, so any questions? Leave 'em in a review!

Thanks!
