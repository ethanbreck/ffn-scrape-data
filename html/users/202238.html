Wow! I can't believe I just took the only story I've ever had on here off. It feels weird, but now that I look back on it I don't really like what I did with that story. Oh, well, I was young and didn't know any better. Then again, now I'm older and I can't seem to write anything. Oh, well, I'll figure something out eventually. I hope. According to about.com I need to find my muse. I think I lost her in that room my mom keeps telling her to clean. At this rate maybe I'll find her sometime this summer. Well, if anyone has an idea for a story, original or not, please tell me. I'm desperate as much as I hate to admit it. Well, I'm off to go rack my brain for an idea. I hate writer's block. Well, farewell, the next time you hear from me I'll be free of my braces and hopefully I'll have an idea for a story! Bye!
