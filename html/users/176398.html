Hi all. . .finally a small update to my bio.

"Why?" I hear you all ask.

Why?? Well. . . Because my last bio told of how I was never going to write fan fiction and only write original stories. This is. . .well anyone who has read "The Art of Teleportation". . .this is no longer true.

I have succumbed to the insanity that is Harry Potter fanfiction and now cannot stop. There are however a few random plot bunnies in the making from the LotR fandom too, as well as some stories I have finished but just have not got round to uploading because I'm lazy.

Something amusing: I am randomly sitting watching TV with Chad and Pendragon, and suddenly get hit by the most descriptive plot bunny that I have ever "seen". Had to run to get my pad and furiously scribble notes until it was finished.

Original story of course. Those are the ones that hit me. . .the fanfic ones I know the ending before the beginning and just see where the story goes. . .but the originals. . .geez they just come out of nowhere.

Anyway who has ever read any of my original stories will know that I am physically incapable of writing "nice" original stories. So yes, this amazing story that is brewing...it will be angst, there will be death, everyone will be crying at some point, oh yes. *giggles evily*
