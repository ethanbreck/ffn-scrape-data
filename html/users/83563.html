There. I just deleted my old profile. I haven't been active on fanfiction.net or fictionpress.com since high school... so here is my new information.

My name is Emily, 23, currently working as a law clerk and going to university. I am recently married. That's the boring information ;)

What inspires my writing? My emotions, my feelings and my life happenings. I am also obsessed with vampires, especially the relationship that occurs after a blood bond, or if a vampire takes a father figure type role in a human's life. I also have this habit of making Hunters just as evil as the vampires. Some of my vampire fiction can be found on fictionpress.com

I used to do alot of "bible" fanfiction, that's not to say I still don't, but not as publically and not as much. I also have some Sailor Moon fanfiction (actually I have alot on my laptop... but it's so old it's HORRIBLE. I'll spare you) However, I wrote alot of that fanfiction in high school so don't expect high quality writing ;)

My new fanfiction passion is a childhood favourite: the Baby- Sitters Club.

I do my best to edit my stories, but I'm a horrible speller so please be patient with me.

I appreciate any reviews and any feedback on my stories. I want to improve and I won't unless you tell me how!

Author Notes

Stoneybrook High- thank you to all those who have reviewed. I am still working on this story so don't be discouraged if I am temporarily distracted by something else. I do have intentions to finish it, and possible do a sequel, should my muse allow.

Gone- this is my current distraction. I'm trying techniques in this story I haven't tried before... so it's the piece I'm most concerned about.

Responsible- thank you to all those who have reviewed and requested that I continue with it. While, much to my suprise, actually enjoy writing Kristy- centric stories, I really have no further plot ideas for this fic, and therefore, at this point in time, have no intention to make it a multi- chapter fic. Thank you, again, for those who reviewed.

Sunny Side Up- thank you to all those who have reviewed and requested that I continue with it. Much like the previously mentioned story, I intend to keep this as a one shot. However, a lot of the major ideas are being shown in Stoneybrook High. I would call this one shot an AU of Stoneybrook High. Thank you, again, for those who reviewed.
