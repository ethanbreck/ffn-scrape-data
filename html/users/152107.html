NEW NOTE: September/07

I haven't read Deathy Hollows, or whichever, yet, but I plan on it after Igo through and read the other books again to refresh my memory. I still severely dislike Half-blood Prince but I shouldn't let that discourage me from the fandom.

With that said; I will be rewriting Salamander Scales.

It is rather old and outdated and I'd like to think my writing has improved somewhat. I'm not sure if I'll be posting it on this account or my more recent ff.net account, but whatever the case, I will be writing it over and completing it. So if anyone is still interested (it has been a long, long time after all) please look forward to it! When I get it started I will update with a new notice :D

Notice: After reading Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince whenit came out... whenever that was, my interest in the Harry Potter fandom has severely waned. In fact, I find that I dislike the series much more than I like it. My tolerance for the latest book and the series in general is very low.

So, on that note, I will try and finish Salamander Scales because of all the people who have been reviewing and adding it to their favourites and alert lists. I do not know when I'll get around to it as I'm currently working on some original fiction and putting more focus on fanart, rather than fanfiction. But I'll try my best to pique my interest enough to finish the story. I am honestly and truly sorry. But when something lets you down as hard as that book did me, you can't help when the inspiration to continue won't come.
