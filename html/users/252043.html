Author has written 4 stories for Mega Man.

Well, I suppose I should write something here. Uh, I enjoy reading fanfiction about Megaman, Mario, Sonic, and lots of other video game series. I'm currently working on Megaman X: Denique Fatum, which is probably one of the more serious concepts I've come up with.

One of my biggest inspirations on this site is Erico and his Megaman stories. They were what inspired me to do DF in the first place. I also enjoy the stories that Magus523 has written that novilize Megaman games.

Well, that's really all that's interesting about me. If you want to know more, I guess you could always email me. Oh, and I'd love more feedback on MMX: DF, I really want to know what people think about it.

Update 11/11/2008

Well, yesterday I finished my spinoff to Denique Fatum; Redemption of the Misjudged. I think I put a lot more feeling into RotM then I did with DF, but then again, I needed to to make the main character sympathetic, since he is usually portrayed as a very unsympathetic character. Well, as of yet, I don't really know what I'm going to be working on, but I was tossing around the idea of writing more about the events that take place between the X and Zero series. Maybe some sort of novilization of the ideas already put forward in the Zero series. But before I get to that, I'm probably going to take a bit of a sabbatical from writing to read all the books that have piled up on my list. And then I need to actually play the Zero games. Oh well, it'll happen eventually, until then, keep your busters charged...
