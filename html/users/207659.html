Hmmmm, what to say. I suppose i could fill a few lines writing a basic bio, boring you to distraction in the process of course, like the fact i'm 28 and 'fae' Scotland and writings my hobby and... You see my point...

The fact is I'm not a great writer and i am terribly unreliable. If you are suicidally bored and end up taking a peek at what I've written you'll no doubt notice that the large majority of my various tales are incomplete. Shame on me. I do however plan to finish them at some point, as hard as that may be to believe considering the start date on some of the dustier ones. The problem is I'm very easily distracted. I no sooner become interested and borderline obsessed with one particular character or notion when another one comes along! (Sigh) Its such a dilemma... ;)

Anyway, i tend to gravitate, in writing lets be clear, towards dark and broody characters. I also like mentor/parental type fics, both to read and write.

I'm always interested to hear from fellow writers. As my 'sometimes-hobby' is known only by myself, its refreshing to speak to like-minded folk about it. In other words, drop me a line if you fancy a chat.

Well, thats about all i can think of to say at the moment. Not true of course, there are an abundance of subjects i could blather on about, but as i don't want you to start bleeding from your eyes I'll leave it here because thats the kind of person i am...:)
