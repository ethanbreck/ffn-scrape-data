Author has written 25 stories for Harry Potter, and King Arthur.
I write HP fanfiction. For other readings (you may well see the influences in my writing) I like Voltaire, Tolkien, C. S. Lewis, and Welsh mythology (that's Welsh, not Celtic), as well as various books normally used as school textbooks. I also read normal fiction such as Pratchett, Gaimon, Brooks, Asimov (okay, that's fantasy/scifi, but as normal as I get).

I am also a Uni student, so if my time schedule seems odd or strange, that should be enough to put your fears at rest. And, yes, even Uni students like Harry Potter. As one of my hallmates recently stated, "But everything has to do with Harry Potter!"

I own nothing, but the plotlines and the characters I create. Harry Potter and his various cohorts belong to J. K. Rowling and her various cohorts. Clearly, if I was making money off of this, I wouldn't be letting people read it for free.

Fictions on Hiatus:
Party on Privet Drive: Amazingly this does have a plotline and will be restarted once Three O'clock is done. There is a plot! It will be completely overhauled and be rather unrecognizable to most.

MidWinter Sun: I'm posting this as a WIP (fear not, DNoS is also being written). Midwinter Sun (MS) is about a very serious, very real subject. Some of the things that will happen in it may seem absurd and unreal to the casual eye. These are very real. Normally, I try not to take myself too seriously, but I would like my readers to understand that MS deals with a very serious subject. (I wonder how many times I can fit the words "serious" and "real" into this description?) The problems that both Professor Snape and Harry Potter deal with come from real life. Indeed, they come from my real life. Nearly all of Harry's symptoms, with a few adjustments, are based upon my own. I will admit here and now that if people laugh at this or mock it in anyway, I will become upset. What can I say? I'm sensitive. If you don't want to read a serious fic, don't read Midwinter Sun.

Right now, I actually have a Real Life that comprises of more than just reading Harry Potter fanfiction. I have social and academic obligations. Therefore, I make no promises as to when DNoS will be posted, only that the chapters are being written when I find time for them. The first six chapter are finished. DNoS is being written in a different pattern than TOM was, so expect changes! It is a sequel/prequel.

If you're really eager, I will be posting updates and teasers on my livejournal. Some teasers and plot devices are already up.

And I'm developing carpal tunnel, the doctors say. Or at least, I showing symptoms. So DNoS might be a bit longer in coming...

Lastly - and this time I'm very serious - DO NOT POST MY FICTION ON YOUR SITE WITHOUT ASKING ME. Ask me and chances are that I'll say yes, but I'm sick and tired of checking out a site and suddenly finding my work on it, when I know I never put it there. This is still stealing my work, even if you attach my name to it. Don't make me Google my name or my fics and find your site. I will be greatly displeased.
