Hello, kiddos. Name's Stabitha, but I also go by MarthVader, Mothra, and a bunch of other ridiculous things.

I enjoy writing, and reading, and cartoons. I want to write a book someday (but who doesn't? Seriously.)

Fantasy is my favorite probably. I love Robert Jordan, David Eddings, Ray Bradbury (if you have not read Something Wicked This Way Comes, do it now. It's amazing.), J. K. Rowling, Steven King, Michael Crichton, Jacqueline Carey, Anne Rice, Anne Bishop, Neil Gaiman, Tanith Lee, George R. R. Martin, and Orson Scott Card, among others. I'm a huge comic book fan, and an even bigger Batman fan (I'd marry him on the spot if I could... Alas...) Really. Batman is my hero, and I'm in love with all his villains. Talk about actual characters.

I love cartoons, even though some would say I'm too old for them (fuck off, kindly, if that is you.) Batman the Animated Series, Batman Beyond (seeing a theme here?) are some oldies but goodies, though I am a big fan of Avatar: the Last Airbender. It's one of the better current cartoons (hurrah) and I love Heroes as well. Think it's great, keep wishing I'll discover my powers, too.

Love most music, aside from country, and love to draw, a ton. I am always up for a new book to read, so if you know any terrifically good ones, tell me and I'll check it out. Or comic books. You know, anything I can read. My AIM is moonstringsx. Feel free to talk if you aren't going to babble on and on. :)

Later kids. 3
