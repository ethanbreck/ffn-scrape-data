I have a lot of hobbies but the main ones are listening to music, reading, playing sport and of course writing. update

26/03/2004 I am sorry for the huge gaps in updating at the moment (and in general) I suffer from writers block in a big way and also college is taking up a huge amount of my time at the moment leaving me with very little time to update. i am planning on finishing all my fics though and I do have a two week break (seems like forever doesn't it?) for easter beginning this friday so expect a few updates to appear. Thanks for being patient, the emails and reviews do help honestly end update

Update
- July 2008

I am back writing again. This will include updates on some of my older fics as well as new fic. It feels like forever since I've written anything and in a way it kind of is - not counting drabbles which never really made it this far. If you're interested in those try my livejournal (link above) - they may all make it here eventually.

I also think I should update my personal information (I'm leaving the old updates for nostalgia). I am now a 21 year old university student studying Psychology, Geology and Anthropology. Fandom and I had a falling out - with no wank involved whatsoever - but I missed it and I'm back.

I'm always looking for new friends who like similar things so feel free to drop me a message.
