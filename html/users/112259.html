Now I'm doing genealogy, learning Duetsche, travelling a lot and trying to hook a man that won't find my obsession over Benedict Cumberbatch threatening...that deep sexy voice is so yummy.

Anyway, all stories have been discontinued with no plans to pick them back up. They're all a little childish and no longer my cuppa. If anyone wants to expand on one of my stories I just ask that you let me know so I can go read it! ;P
