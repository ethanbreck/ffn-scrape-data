Deeper Stuff
I'm a follower of the gothic culture. This does not mean I dress all in black and wear scary make-up while praising satan. This means I appreciate and understand the darker, more morbid side of things, and am truly diverse in my interests. I am a lover of forgein language and culture, an avid reader and writer, and I truly love most any genre of music. I'm not much for rap, pop or country, but I will listen to select songs if I find them to my liking. I'm also a believer in the mystics and supernatural, the night world.

I have been through a lot in my short life, and I am a strong and street smart person because of it. I enjoy intelligent conversation and I do not have much tolerance for people who are ignorant of how the world works, are closed minded, or think they know everything. Consider it a warning- if you lack common sense, you'll do better to just stay away from me. I don't want to lower anyone's self-esteem. But, I will not hesitate to do so if you make me.
