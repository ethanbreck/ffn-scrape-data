Hi! I'm the author formerly known as Dan (No-one can ever find me on the author list so I've changed my pen-name), I'm a 22 year old guy from Wales in Uni(somehow still hanging on in there )with an OTT imagination, a thing for Pokemon (which everyone in my family either hates or ignores ;( ) and a Final fantasy fanatic. I also own the FFVI, VII, VIII and IX soundtracks on import, but hold a grudge against square for not releasing Xenogears or Chrono Cross in europe. I also enjoy watching anime series, my favourite being Chrono Crusade (which I advise anyone andeveryone towatch, since it's something really special).Evangelion, Martian Successor Nadesco and Tenchi are also among my faves.

The rest of the time I spend playing cricket or reading/writing fanfiction. Although recently it's been mostly reading since I am having little luck in actually putting words on paper... Anyhow, if you have the chance to read any of my writing, hope you enjoy reading as much as I enjoyed writing it. (I hope that makes sense). And don't forget to review!

I suppose I should update this thing sometime soon. But then again, I haven't changed much. At least this profile now isn't still wishing everyone a merry christmas and a happy new year. It's taken me until the end of March to actually get around to removing the message ^_^; (And it's taken me until September to notice this is still here...)

And people still wonder why I don't update quicker!

Well, I guess that's all for now!
