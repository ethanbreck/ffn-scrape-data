Author has written 25 stories for Smallville, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Star Wars, Law and Order, StarTrek: Voyager, Robin of Sherwood, Sons of Anarchy, and Arrow.

I'm currently working on a crossover between the Sons of Anarchy (FX TV) and Arrow (CW). Starting with "Not One of Them". Latest update: 2019/09/07

If/when I do continue other fan fiction, only Star Wars "Honour or Death" and Robin of Sherwood "A Fool's Errand" stories may have updates.

I enjoyed writing Smallville or Star Trek:TNG stories -- but there are no guarantees and no plans in future. If/when I do, it would follow the internal continuity that I've already established.

Robin of Sherwood: Nothing is forgotten! England is at war with Philip Augustus of France, one of Christendom's great monarchs. Gisburne is sent to fight in Normandy, but will he find glory or an early grave? As harsh levies are raised to pay for King John's campaigns, the new Robin struggles to shed his past as an earl's son -- and evade the Sheriff's schemes to hunt him down. Make ready your arrows for more of "A Fool's Errand".

Smallville universe: Lex Luthor has known Bruce Wayne since they were schoolmates. As Clark approaches his destiny, Bruce and Lex attempt to mend their own strained friendship. Will they succeed, or will their rivalry descend into chaos and ensnare Clark, Lana, Chloe and the others? The line dividing friend and foe becomes blurred. There is a deadly conspiracy afoot (which emerged in the stories "Inquisitor" and "Nightshade") -- one that will test their relationship beyond the breaking point. Bruce has his own secrets in the shadows: keeping them hidden may demand too high a price ...

Read about Clark's introduction to a few major characters in Gotham City and the disintegration of Bruce and Lex's once-grand alliance! "Sever" - the story of Bruce Wayne in the Smallville universe. Note: You can consider these short stories as a follow-up to my previous one, "Nightshade".

If you like the series about Bruce Wayne in Smallville, you can read the stories in chronological order:

Full length (episodic) stories:

"Trophy" - Clark, Lex and friends cross paths with a deadly anarchist -- one who shares a mysterious past with both Bruce and Lex.

"Archer" - Oliver Queen in Smallville, written three years before his appearance on the show! An untold story about Lionel Luthor's attempt to take over Wayne Enterprises ... and Ollie has to choose sides.

"Vigilante" - Tensions between Bruce and Lex surface, as a student teacher with a bloody past exposes a rift among friends and ignites a dirty mob war in the streets of Metropolis.

Short stories:

"Comfort and Joy" - A Christmas tale during the 'Jason Teague era'. Bruce visits Smallville during the holidays. Can Lex and Bruce remain friends?

"Regret" - Clark and Lois confront the aftermath of Jonathan's death, as they reflect on Bruce's involvement in Jonathan's senate campaign.

"Inquisitor" - Metropolis rebuilds from Dark Thursday, as Bruce and Lex set aside their differences. A mysterious figure from Clark's past threatens to reveal everything about the Last Son of Krypton.

"Nightshade" - Alfred is missing, as Clark and Chloe begin to investigate a malevolent conspiracy. Lionel reveals truths about the Waynes of Gotham.

From the archives: Star Trek: TNG / Star Trek Voyager cross-over, the 3-part Undertow Trilogy.

1. "Adapt" - Voyager's homecoming and an unexpected reception for Seven of Nine

2. "Pledge" - Riker unearths a conspiracy within Starfleet Command

3. "Decision" - With the Federation on the brink of civil war, Q chooses to intervene ... for good or bad?
