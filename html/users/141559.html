Remus comes in as a close second. They are both awesome! Lessee, if I went to Hogwarts I'd be in fourth year and I'm an honors student, not that that's important. I'm slightly on the short side with long, dark hair and dark eyes. People with blue-grey eyes rock! Hey, that means I rock! Cool! Hmm, I really like reading fantasy books. I'm also a fan of musicals.
