OK, so original Reunion? Dead. New Reunion? Finished! Now marked complete with no more forthcoming chapters.

So I finally found a comprehensive definition of what a Mary Sue is and I must admit, reluctantly, that The New Chick technically is a Mary Sue (and you have no idea how much is pains me to admit it.) I always thought thata Mary Sue was the author's way of working him or herself into their story. I am DEFINITELY NOT Jule, nor do I wish to be. But Jule is a made up character who intrudes upon the characters of a pre-existing work, so technically is a Mary Sue. But I would like people to see it the way I have come to see it: I'm not trying to include myself in Harry Potter land. I have absolutely no desire to do that because I find it tacky. If I wanted to write about myself it wouldn't really be fanfiction, would it? I hate reading stories where other authors put themselves in the books. But Jule is not an original part of Harry Potter land and, thus, a Mary Sue. Just don't go thinking Jule and I have anything in common except that I am from New York (I only wrote she was from here because I don't know much about any other place on Earth) and I am kind of bi-curous, because other than that we really don't. I just happen to know a bajillion Jule-esque people and I thought it might be interesting to introduce Hermione to that kind of personality. Oh well. JULE IS NOT ME AND I DO NOT LIVE VICARIOUSLY THROUGH HER! If anything, I think of myself as more of a Hermione character.

A different definition of Mary-Sue which I found on the Petulant Poetess said a Mary-Sue is an author's way of living through someone to enter the HP world. As I've said, I've little in common with Jule, so hah!
