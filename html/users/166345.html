More a reader than a writer now, after a length absence from ff.n. Seems all I do nowadays is learn languages, study history books, and make clothing. I suppose that's what I get for being an entrepreneur and a university student.

I don't know if I'll be posting anything new anytime soon. I'm in a Weiss Kreuz mood lately, so if there's anything, it'll prolly be that. Still a big fan of LOTR, Star Wars, BSG, and anime, but they come in leaps and bounds, I'll like one thing for a ywhile, and then move on to something else.

Recently changed my penname, I was once called "blue dragoness", so if you see that name in "Tall Tales", (which was called "Amusingly Disturbing" for a while), then don't worry: you've got the right fic. Sorry for the confusion ahead of time.

If you liked my Legolas fic, or have a passion for any of the things listed above feel free to drop me a line, my email's up there somewhere.

There may be more in the future, but you should never count your chickens before they hatch.
