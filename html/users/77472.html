Ok here goes.

I am a lover of all things fanfic. Well that's not really true, I'm not a fan of slash, or twincest or any of that weird stuff, but I'm not one to judge people who are. I currently have a Cowboy Bebop fic sort of in progress called Midnight Blues and I hope to eventually get into the practice of writing more. Unfortunately, now that I'm about to start my second year at the University of Washington (yeah and I'm taking summer quarter classes, too) I haven't had nearly as much time as I'd like to continue that particular story. And when I say, "haven't had as much time" I mean no time at all. Well, that's what I get for majoring in Philosophy.

I live in Seattle, have my whole life, andamcurrently attending theUniversity of Washington, if you hadn't already noticed. I have three cats: Bon Bon, Meesh Meesh (means apricot in Hebrew) and Ichiro. Why should you care? Well, because I said so.
