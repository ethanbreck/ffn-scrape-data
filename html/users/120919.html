Author has written 29 stories for Record of Lodoss War, Lord of the Rings, Inuyasha, Slayers, Ranma, Valdemar universe, Rurouni Kenshin, Utena, Love Hina, Labyrinth, and Crossfire series, Sylvia Day.

So, I am writing fan-fiction again after a very long time. That does not necessarily mean much for my existing stories. A lot of them may never see another update, though it's hard to say what the future may hold. In regard to unfinished stories, here's a little idea of what you can expect.

Currently Active):

Forbidden Fruit: This story is brand new and completely active. Because it's a gift, I feel compelled to work on it and finish it, so you can probably expect semi-regular updates.

Elven Rites: This story is one chapter from the end. Yes, I intend to finish it. I'm not completely sure when, but it gets to have an ending. All bets are off on the sequel that was supposed to follow though, since all those notes . . . actually, I may still have those, so we'll have to see.

Potentially Active:

Fairy Tale One Half: I'm pretty proud of what I was going for here, and I intend to finish it some day. It's reasonably likely that I'll dust it off some time in the next year or so and post a chapter.

Rurouni Kenshin: Wandering Sword-dancer: It's likely that this will catch my fancy again, especially since I'm planning to reread the books it was based on some time soon. However, it's unlikely to see any work until I've finished one of the active projects.

Balancing Fire and Water: I'll be honest: given how long this project was originally planned for, it's unlikely I would ever finish it. However, it's very likely to get a few chapters published at some point, maybe even within the next year, depending on my mood.

Probably Inactive:

Oaths for Eternity: This won't see any activity within the next year unless something changes drastically. I would like to eventually work on this project again, however, since it's got a lot of potential.

Second Best: I'm unlikely to do much work on this, though I do think I have another chapter written somewhere in my files, and that might get posted some time soon.

Inactive:

King Kashue and the Holy Grail: I suppose I might change my mind and work on this again, but once the initial joke was made I'm not sure how much humor is left in the concept. Also there isn't much scope for creativity in finishing this project.

Slayers Gothic: I'm pretty sure this one's dead. A lot of my initial enthusiasm for due to a class I was taking at the time, and that time was over ten years ago.
