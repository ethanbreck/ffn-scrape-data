In brief:

People describe me as a cross between Hermione Granger and Bernadette from the Big Bang Theory.

I'm a busy person who occasionally procrastinates by writing fanfiction. Hence the sporadic writing. I don't read as much as I want to, hence the possibly unusual style.

Contact me if you like. I promise I really am quite amiable.
