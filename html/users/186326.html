ABOUT ME

I've been reading fanfic since good ol' 2001, I started writing before joining this site and as a result have a number of LotR and HP fics that never have and never will be published up here. I joined (as you can see) in early 2002 and initially posted my first fic, "The Transfer Students," (Now known as "They Came From Over There"), at some point shortly before "To Change or Not to Change." After receiving some fairly soul and esteem crushing reviews from a single reviewer that just kept on coming, I lost my nerve. Well, at least when it came to posting my fics, until now. I spent my off years not posting, but continuing to read and learn more about writing and taking note of ways to improve.

~Your most cheerful panda, Marah
