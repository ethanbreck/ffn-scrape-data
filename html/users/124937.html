December 31. 2005

20 more minutes before 2006. Happy New Year everybody! It's been...uh... quite a while since I last visited this site. Gomen, people. Ugh, I feel terrible. I know I have a lot of unattended fics over the past years... Erk! Sorry again! I really am planning to work on them. Right now. Yeah. So, thank you for nagging me. Although things can get pretty scary. nervous laughter I really enjoy writing fanfics because I write them purely for myself. Sometimes, I wish I never became an editor in the first place. Heehee. Yes, I would rather write yaoi fics (and on rare moments, straight fics) than edit. Unfortunately, I had to prioritize my staff and this responsibility first.

A few minutes later... Yikes, my editor-in-chief just greeted me a happy new year. Huhu, I wonder if said senior editor knows I've just been talking about paper stuff. Uwah... Fireworks are really showing off... cough cough Smoke is slipping through the windows... Gah!

September 26, 2005

Dammit. There's really something wrong with this thing. I just typed a pretty heartfelt message about updating "The Doll Factory" and before I knew it, it was gone. :0 Sigh... Well, I'm lazy. I don't want to write everything again so I'll just say the key points. I'm going to work on that Card Captor fic first because it has stayed a single chapter for too long. thank you to all those who reviewed and nagged me constantly. Your ways have roused me from being a lazy ass. Well... sort of. I'm still kinda... okay-okay. I'll get to it. I'm downloading the fic into my PC as I write. Cheerios, minna-san.

Septemer 9, 2005

For the first time in months, I checked my del_chan account. I didn't realize how many people reviewed my fics! To those readers, thank you very much. I feel very guilty. Somehow, I have neglected everything about ff.net... My stories... Sigh... But I will stay true to my word. A lot of things have happened to me, ya see... changes... But I'm ready to finish everything. Stay tuned, loyal readers. I'm sorry for being such a lazy ass. :) I am now pursuing the path to literary success. Yikes! Hehe, it's been so long. Years since I last updated old fics. You'll find that I have somewhat improved. My style has changed. And no more grammatical errors. shudder I might re-do most of the earlier chappies but the plot will more or less remain the same. :)
