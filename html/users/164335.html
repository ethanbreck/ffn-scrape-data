That one came from the bizzare roleplay in the SM universe, which crashed into the Dragoverse, and made Oak Chiba, second daughter to the Neo Queen, Commandor of the Royal Guard, and the Leader of the Second Generation Senshi.

(Don't ask about some of those nicknames. Their history is quite prosaic - first three were given to me by my classmates. Some boys, until I accepted that because of my surname I will be teased through my life, or, at least, through the primary (8yrs) and the secondary school(4yrs), got their *not* respective asses kicked. Hard. I was a tomboy, I admit it willingly, and even now, when the chance of a friendly sparring exists, I take it up. It's *fun*.)

I was always a strange one, doodling and daydreaming in my classes, while still, amazingly, managing very good grades in primary school. Secondary was not so easy, but I passed the national exam pleasingly well (not counting the science - I got 3 (1 - the worst, 6 - the best note avaiable). Obsessed with fantasy and all along, living in unreal world. Hell, I *still* live in an unreal world, and proud of it!

I really want to be a writer.

I'm going to take the FCE exam this year.

My ever favourite movie is 'Blade Runner', and book, 'The Master and The Margaret' by Bulhakov.
