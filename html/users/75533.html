Author has written 8 stories for Final Fantasy: Tactics, Escaflowne, Harvest Moon, Corpse Bride, Girl who Leapt Through Time, and Dune.

A young theatre artist from Canada. I work long hours in between studying for a degree, which leaves me little enough time for writing as it is. But some of my own prominently one shot meanderings are posted here for your enjoyment. I realize I often have issues in my writing with tense changes or verb agreements, but it's a skill I'm slowly working on as I keep writing.

Most of my writing is rooted in romance, or drama of some sort. You'll find I'm very much a fan of traditionalist pairings, and perhaps some very unconventional ones too. I do write CloudxAeris, and Ramza/Agrias. If either of those pairings bug you, well then don't read. I also tend to write Van/Hitomi because I hate Allen...grin. As far as other fics go, especially tv and such I am wierd in that I enjoy creating my own reality for the characters instead of following the 'distinct' cannon. Characterization remains fairly consistent however and is something I strive for.

Of late I've become something of a 'moments' writer, canonizing specific moments in the story of movies, television or games to build layers of character. Because of this, many of my stories tend to be one-shots or vignettes.

Oh and to all my shattering friends, I do realize it's been almost 4 years in the running, and I am working on it, but don't hold your breath, it might take another year for a new chapter! I've actually been thinking of rewritting the whole thing with my new writing talents of late.

In any case, do read my fics, please review and comment on them, it is much appreciated. Thanks!
