Well, let's see here. I'm an avid bookworm who constantly pesters dad for the credit card so I can order a book from amazon that no one else even knew exsisted. Umm... I've read Phantom of the Opera and I hate Raoul. I recently scared my little sis by recreating the punjab lasso scene from the musical with her Barbies. I think what disturbed her most was the fact that the Raoul doll was naked and the Christine and Meg dolls were laughing maniacally at his nakedness. I'm in the middle of Les Mis, and I've just read Love of Seven Dolls by Paul Gallico. It's a fairly short book, my copy is 90 pages, but a very beautiful story nonetheless.
