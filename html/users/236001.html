Hi folks!

This is a little bio of me, Hotaru Vador. Vador is French for Vader and I think Darth Vader is the greatest movie character ever. Tragic, but I like him.
Hotaru is the character from Sailor Moon... ggeeee...

I write coz I love it. I write about two characters from Sailor Moon (got me right, that childish TV- series from Japan), to be exact Hotaru and Yaten. They have a great potential which wasn't used in the series.
Next to Y/H I write about Star Wars, Indiana Jones and X-Files .
Drama, romance and adventure are my favourite genres.

I like stars (Star Wars, Starlights, Mercedes Benz... ^_^ ), shopping, Batman + Catwoman (ever heard of 'Birds of Prey'?), Charmed, Harry Potter, The Labyrinth (the movie with David Bowie), Merlin, Adam Sandler...

And: I love to get reviews! Think about that you get fiction for free. Provide the author with your opinion. It's his payment.
