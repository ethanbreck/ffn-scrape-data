Jamaica is still writing! Yes, she is. Yes, she will finish her DGM fics, and write many, many more. But she's working on that.

Newer bio:

Jamaica has other accounts around the net, and she usually likes to keep them somewhat separate, but now she has pretty much delved into a tiny fandom with a specific, tiny pairing, it's counterproductive to not crosspost fics. So she will. Her AO3 name is solitaryjane (archiveofourown.org/users/solitaryjane/works) and her tumblr handle is sparklymirror (sparklymirror.tumblr.com).

(P.S. She can't write uke!Kanda worth shit. Like, she tries but it just never clicks, unlike for the majority of the fandom. It's probably because she actually is a rabid Lavi fan, and Kanda is just her chosen vessel to show it.)

Old bio:

Jamaica is a crazy writer who cannot for the life of her keep up a good schedule with writing. The Fayths thinks it's funny to keep her inspirations dormant until she either has absolutely no time or no place or neither to write, and then creativity hits full force. By the time she finds the place/time, the stories go straight back to sleep. Jamaica would like to argue with the Fayths, but considering she couldn't find them and they don't respond, she is pretty much stuck.

She is still writing, despite all contrary evidences. She simply does not want to post something that she may not finish, again, and put everyone in the misery of asking her twenty times to continue it. She feels extremely guilty for not living up to people's expectations. Nevertheless, the writing marches on.
