Here it is! I'm tired of always writting disclaimers when posting fics so I'm doing this for all my current and future fics : I do not own anything except my original characters (which are currently existant only in Conspiracy).

Also, I apologize for any grammar or vocabulary mistakes in my stories. I'm not patient enough to wait for a beta reader proof reading my fics and English isn't my first language. Please be kind.

For all those who right in French in their fics and are not sure of what they're writing, you can always e-mail me those. I can help you and I want to (I've read poorly French translated sentences).

For those who can't help but wonder what I'm like, well, I am from Montreal, Quebec. I am a university student in literature and I am 19 years old. I have a passion for reading, writing, drawing and do role-playing games with my friends.

For now, I'm only a BtVS fanfiction writer (most of them X-Overs with various themes), but I am also an avid reader of Harry Potter fics. I am a Willow/Spike shipper even if I tend to pair up Willow with someone else in X-Over. As for HP, I'm Hermione/Draco or Hermione/Snape shipper.

NOTE : When I *really* like more than one fic from an author, I put his ID in favorite authors, not favorite stories.

As of that, go ahead and read my fics. Enjoy!
