November 18, 2009

Okay, so apparently I'm obsessed with writing Buzz and Jessie fanfiction at the moment. There are still so many new ideas floating around in my head and if I had the time the sit around and write all day, I definately would! So because I'm such a huge Buzz and Jessie fan, I've decided to create a Buzz and Jessie fan blog, so I can obsess over them anytime, no matter how busy I am!

November 6, 2009

I haven't even looked at this account in over 5 years, let alone logged in. I was re-directed to this site when I was reading a story two days ago, and it brought back so many memories of past hobbies and interests, as well as friends I made on this site when I was a frequent visitor. Obviously, I used to adore the Power Rangers and write a lot about them. It's been years since I've written a Power Rangers story, and I honestly don't know enough about them now to do it again, but it's been nice reminiscing about my childhood and also re-reading some of my favorite stories. There are a lot of really talented authors on here, and I know there are tons more whose stories I have yet to read. So even though my interests differ and I don't have quite as much time to write as I used to, I will still visit more often. At least I can never say I have nothing to read. =0)

Also, even though I'm a lot older now and when I do get to writing again my style will likely be at least a little different, I've decided to leave my old stories on here. They were a part of who I am, and I'm proud to have written them.
