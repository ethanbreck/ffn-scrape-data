Author has written 4 stories for Harry Potter.

I am 18 and definitely too old to be on this website, but I'm here anyway. I am a freshman at University of Oregon. I like SS Leather and Libraries and yes, I am aware that the pairing will never happen in the books. I have a few stories up and it is unlikely that I will write more. Most of the things I write are from spur of the moment inspiration. Please don't ask for updates as I can only write when something pops into my head. "Oh my god, update please" does not generally inspire me.

I will not be updating Dream Boy, but I will leave it up. If anyone has the desire to take it and make it their own, just let me know and give me credit for the first few chapters.

Encounter in the Hallway is not actually supposed to be a Harry Potter fic. It was a poem written in story format for my own pleasure. It so happens that the story relates to Hermione and Draco.

Dirty Beautiful is a poem about Draco and Hermione, but I have changed the names to David and Samantha because I think it sounds better in the story. Please do not tell me you thought this was a Draco/Hermione story; it quite obviously is, otherwise I would not have submitted it in the Harry Potter section. Also, I mention in the summary and author's note about the name changes, so if you missed one of those, you should learn to read a bit more carefully.

If you wish to get in contact with me, e-mail me at GoldynGyrl@earthlink.net. I will usually respond, but as this is not my primary e-mail address, I do not check it on a regular basis.

Reviews are always appreciate. Thanks!
