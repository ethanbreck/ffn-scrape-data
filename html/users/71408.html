Information on Updates! (June 25, 2006) I am officially retired as a fanfiction author. There may be one or two little things to come out in the future, but don't expect much. Please enjoy what is out here right now, and keep sending me reviews. I enjoy hearing from you. :)

(March 4, 2010) Well, it's almost four years since I last posted a story, and I'm impressed to see that not only are people still reading my stuff, I'm still getting wonderful comments. I just wanted to say thank you so much. It means a lot to me to still be hearing from readers after all this time. :)

Information on Me!
Name: Jen
Hobbies: Japanese anything, music
Favorite Music: Dave Matthews Band, Jimmy Eat World, Coldplay, Do As Infinity
Stories written: Some Harry Potter stuff, some X-Files. Not all of either is posted here.

Story Info(Chronological Order... sorta)

Scarlet Woman: A story that my muse attacked me with. Ginny is a prostitute, Draco was almost a customer. The story follows their progress in their relationship and in dealing with the past. Draco/Ginny FINISHED

Burning: Another not-so-happy piece. I wrote it when I had free time (also known as not now) and uploaded it almost six months later. Oh well. It's one of my favorite personal works.FINISHED

Back To You: The only hopelessly fluffy story I've ever written. Trust me, it's a lot of cotton candy, bunnies, and long-haired Draco. Fluff to the max. D/G FINISHED

Grace Is Gone: Definitely not fluff. An angsty song-fic that I made me cry while writing it. Another D/G one. FINISHED

Finality: An angsty Mulder/Scully story I wrote a very long time ago. Based on the episode This Is Not Happening. Short. FINISHED

Not Doing That Again: My requisite Drunk!Mulder story. The first part of it is rather amusing, but the second part was written when I wasn't in a giddy mood, so it's not so much... FINISHED

The Tale of Aphrensia: Trojan War fanfiction. I know, I didn't even knew it existed until I looked here. I wrote it for an English project freshman year. Again, another old one, as I'm now a college student. FINISHED
