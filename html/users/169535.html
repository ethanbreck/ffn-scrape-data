Let's see, the facts are: I'm in my thirties. I have a significant other. I don't write all that much. I have very varied interests. Said interests run the gamut of television, video games, cartoons, anime, books, movies and whatever else I can zero in on.

I like all sorts of fanfiction. This can and will include: gen, slash, het, yuri, yaoi, shonen ai, what have you. There really is no rhyme or reason to what I will like at any given time. I also write any and all of the above. However, I do typically give a warning at the beginning of my fics and in the summaries of said fics. That way people can avoid reading something they don't like. If they read it anyway, knowing they won't like it, that's their own fault.

Otherwise, I'm mostly just a hermit due to circumstances. About five years ago now, I got very sick. And unfortunately it's been an ongoing thing since then. Because of that my interest in just about anything waxes and wanes with whatever state my health is in. Will I get back to any of my unfinished series? I honestly don't know. I used to have plans for them, but everything kind of fell to the wayside. If you like my stuff, then that's great! If you like one of my unfinished series, then I'm very sorry for leaving you in the lurch. I haven't given up hope in finding interest in them again, but at the same time, I can't make any promises. It is what it is.
