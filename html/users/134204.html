Author has written 10 stories for Lost World, Doctor Who, and NCIS.

I love television. Some of my earliest memories are of watching Saturday afternoon scifi which I was too young to really understand. I think most people who become enamored of certain television shows have moments where they wish a scene had played out differently. Despite the fact that for me this was a frequent occurrence (usually involving LOTS more hurt/comfort scenes), the thought of sitting down and writing my own stories just never dawned on me until my television obsession focused on a show called Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's The Lost World. This show, and the talented fans I soon became friends with, opened up a world of creative ideas that I had never previously explored. All of a sudden, the idea of writing my own stories didn't seem like such an impossibility. The show was a playground filled with all manner of potential, unexplored storylines and my good friends provided the moral support (and Beta expertise) that allowed me to close my eyes and dive in. I enjoy writing but am extremely lazy...the hardest part being sitting down in front of an empty computer screen and just getting started. Once I can tackle the huge roadblock of typing that first sentence, I usually enjoy the process...just not enough to battle that roadblock on a more frequent basis.

If you do decide to take a chance on reading my work, I really hope you enjoy it. Feedback, both positive and constructively negative, is always appreciated.

-Ry
