Author has written 6 stories for Animaniacs, and Jimmy Neutron.

I'm 34 years old, and I've been a very big fan of the Jimmy Neutron franchise for many, many years. The show is basically my life. My ongoing mission for the past 6 years has been to try to convince Nickelodeon to revive the series. With other Nickelodeon properties being revived, I feel that now is the time. There are so many leftover cliffhangers from the third season that need to be resolved including what becomes of Jimmy and Cindy's relationship, which was quite rushed towards the end of the series. There are also many adventures left to be told, and many life lessons left to be taught.

I started writing fanfics in my 20s. One for Animaniacs, and several for Jimmy Neutron. I'm what you might call the Professor Calamitous of fanfiction in that I have not finished any of the stories I have written. Sometimes, I'll sit in front of an empty screen on Microsoft Word and try to write again, but each time I attempt to do so, I either write complete mindless randomness or nothing at all. I have been suffering from depression for quite some time and I think that may be one reason I have not had much inspiration to write these days.

I have my sights set on attempting to write something for Jimmy Neutron again at some point. My other stories, however, will remain on hiatus for the foreseeable future.

For those of you who remember Animaniacs, Dot was the cute one, hence the reason for my username Cutie5. I've had this username for 15 years and it's sort-of grew on me.
