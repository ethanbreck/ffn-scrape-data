It's been a while, eh?

Sorry about the lack of updating. I've run into a horrib bout of writer's block while trying to jump start my writing career. My original stories have taken over my agenda, of course. However, I'm having trouble finishing any of them. To top it all off, I'm in a awful fangirl phase, which commands I write sappy fics about Harry Potter characters snogging in broom closets. ((SS/DM is at the top of the list at them moment. Currently in the works, a sappy story about them snogging in a clearing.))

I have also decided to challenge myself and take up the writing of one of my oldest Harry Potter fanfiction writing projects. Damned Without Redemption. I seriously doubt anyone on this site will remember this story. It's over three years old, and never made it past the second chapter. Well, chapter two is already halfway done, and I haven't even re-uploaded the first! Wewt! I'm proud of myself.

I shall leave you with this, and hope to hell that my story gets read... So I can know I'm writing it for more than just myself. And that people will take me more seriously than they do when I post crap like "Harry Potter and the Horrible, Awful, Terrible, Atrocious, Ghastly, Repulsive, Very Bad Plot".

Thank you.
