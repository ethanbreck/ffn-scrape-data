RANDOM NOTES:

I don't watch dubbed anime. Never do. I watch only subbed. The only time when I did watch dub, and that was definitely not USA dub, I watched the german dub, which in MOST anime stayed true to the original.The only reason why I do use YGO dub things: like Shadow Realm, Shadow Power, Shadow Games and Shadow Masters is because I find it comfortable and reasonable. First of all, let's not forget I am originally a Sailor Moon fan. And the original YGO anime's reason to Zorc's creation, well, I don't quite like it. Well more like, I wish it would be more crafted out. So... in ANY of my stories that deal with Yu-Gi-Oh!:
1. Shadow Realm for me is like a parallel dimension. The Kul Elna massacre, when to gain unimaginable power 99 people were sacrificed, caused a rift in the space-time continuum (here ya go, told ya I'm an SM fan) creating an opening, with which the Millennium Items connected to that parallel dimension.
2. Concerning Zorc. Either: a) he's a spirit created by the massacre, and then, in my continuities, as a newborn demon went to Shadow Realm to harvest the dark powers, and then returned and corrupted Bakura; or b) he's a demon that dwelled in the Shadow Realm for eternity (not its creator), but he dwelled there for a long time.
3. Shadow Master is an appropriate name for someone who can summon the Ancient Egyptian monsters, use the magic of Millennium Items, or draw magic from the same realm, as for where the monsters originate. Meaning: Shadow Master is anyone who can access the Shadow Realm. At point 1 you may read what the Shadow Realm is in my stories.
4. I dislike using random japanese in my stories. So obviously characters will not be declaring Yami no Game and so on in their dialogues. They will be declaring Shadow Games or Games of Darknesses.
5. No, the looser of the Shadow Game won't be banished to the Shadow Realm. They will die, have their soul shredded, suffer for all eternity in a horrible illusion, get their sanity stripped, be fed to the pets of the Shadow Masters, have their eyes removed, be fed to shadows (meaning they don't resurrect. They are not in the shadow realm. No, they were fed to the angry spirits/demons/'whatever else exists in my stories in the Shadow Realm'.
