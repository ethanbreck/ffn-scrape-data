Author has written 4 stories for Moulin Rouge, Legend of Zelda, Phantom of the Opera, and Heathers.

I'm a 20-year-old college student, majoring in English and/or Creative Writing; I haven't quite decided yet. Plus, I'm not sure if writing is truly my passion in life. We'll see.

I have started and worked on a countless number of fanfiction stories for a lot of different fandoms over the years, but I have been unable to finish ANY of them. If you look up writer's block in the dictionary, my picture would be beside it.

I fell in love for the first time in the summer of 2006, but now he's gone from my life forever and I fear that he has taken a large part of my soul with him. Forgive me if this sounds melodramatic, but I have never been the same since, and I find it incredibly difficult to be optimistic about anything in life anymore. This whole past year has been like a dream. I fell into the shithole they call anorexia, and have spent this entire summer recovering from it. I'm happy to say that I'm doing very well with recovery with the help of a therapist.
