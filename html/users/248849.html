Author has written 3 stories for Digimon.

After years of not writing fanfiction and not being able to access this account, I've decided that maybe it's time to come back to writing fanfiction... except not in the disgusting fashion that I did in years past. Five years is a long time for a teenager, and by the grace of some deity above, I've improved my writing skills more than you could even begin to imagine. (Also, I'm not as insane as I was then, either. Proper grammar and sanity are amazing things for a person.)

Someday, I will have some new stories. First, I need to get back into a fandom, more than likely being Digimon. (Okay, scratch that. I've already managed to write a Digimon ficlet.) I've left two of my older works, just as a reminder to my younger teen-self. (The Daichan Mystery makes me laugh... because it's that badly written. I was so ridiculous.)

Sorry for my fourteen-year old insanity. Please welcome a new and improve, nineteen year old Kemata.

ABOUT ME

The name's Kellye, and I'm a nineteen year old freshman in college. I currently live in Arkansas, as I have my whole life; I promise you, the stereotypes don't always apply to everyone. ;P Anyway, I've always loved Digimon, especially the first season. I wrote (horrible) fanfiction and roleplayed as much as I possibly could, trying to become a better writer. Something that I'm unsure of happened somewhere along the way, and I stopped writing. I'm now, hopefully, back into the scene. I ship so many pairings that it's hard to keep up with, but more about that later.

I'm a computer geek. I love programming robots and various programs. I used to manage a website back in the day; I would now if I could only figure out what to do with it. If that wasn't enough, I'm a Computer Science major, and as far as I know, I'm the only freshman girl in the program. I enjoy messing around with Photoshop and taking pictures... Yeah.
