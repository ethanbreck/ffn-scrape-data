As of November 2015

I entered into the world of fanfiction about seventeen years ago. I started out reading Buffy fanfiction (I was so obessed with that show; it was sad really lol). I would devour dozens of stories within a week. Then from there whenever I had a favorite show/book/movie/show ... whatever I would read its fanfiction. Then in 2002, I started watching the Tribe and I was inspired to write my first story (Love with Trust, which I recently went back and read. I cringed. But it was nice to see how much my writing has evolved over the past eleven years). I had never really considered writing for enjoyment before that. I have always loved telling stories, but I had never put them to paper. As time went on, through the world of Fanfiction I discovered Role Playing. I had written a Higher Ground fic and I was looking for a board to share it on; the search lead me to Healing Waters: A Higher Ground RPG. After I got fully immersed into the world of rpging, writing became my solace. And now here we are...
