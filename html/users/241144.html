Author has written 11 stories for Legacy of Kain, and Lord of the Rings.

Influences on my writing include Clarke Ashton Smith (unparallelled in my opinion), Edgar Rice Burroughs and Robert E Howard. Favourite authors are Anne McCaffery and Graham Masterton.

Loves: my bloke the self- confessed 'Moth' (part metaller, part goth, all sweetless and night), my quadcore, my Chrysler PT Cruiser, my swords and other weapons (up to 15 at last count).

Hates: pedestrians with no sense of direction or urgency; pedestrians with buggies; pedestrians with umbrellas; injustice (really gets my goat).

Obsessions: The Darkness, Legacy of Kain, cheese spread.

Favourite Quote: "So utterly at a variance is destiny with all the little plans of men!" - H.G. Wells.

Recommendations: Since I only really skulk around the Legacy of Kain section, that's the only subset I can make recommendations on, so: Dragonseer's Tomb Raider/Legacy of Kain Crossovers are highly entertaining, everything Vladimir's Angel has ever done is worth a read, and MikotoTribal's 'The Soul Reaver and the Spirit Shield' is an overlooked masterpiece. So go away and read them!
