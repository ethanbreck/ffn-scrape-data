Nothing really. Well, okay, maybe I'll just go into the usual "favorite/hated characters" and "pairings I do/don't support" bit. But before I do, I'll have you know that I love "King of Fighters" (and "Fatal Fury", for that matter) even though I think it would've been best had it ended with either '97, '98, or even R-2 (on the Neo Geo Pocket), and "Fatal Fury" with "Real Bout 2".

Favorite females (in no particular order): Athena Asamiya (though I don't like any of her post-'98 hairstyles), Mai Shiranui, King, Shermie, Chizuru Kagura, and Blue Mary

Favorite males (again, in no particular order): Andy Bogard, Terry Bogard (with his cap and ponytail), Shingo Yabuki, Yashiro Nanakase, Chris, and Iori Yagami (as long as he's not wearing that ridiculous disguise he had in "Gals Fighters")

Pairings I support: Athena+Kensou, Athena+Shingo, Athena+Iori, Mai+Andy, Shermie+Yashiro, Chizuru+Iori, Mary+Terry, King+Ryo, Benimaru+most any female (it has happened in at least one fic), Yuri+Robert, and Yuki+Kyo

Least favorite characters: Chang, Choi, Geese Howard, Rock Howard, B. Jenet, Jhun, Angel, Whip, May Lee, Kula Diamond, Hinako, Foxy, Li Xiangfei, and anyone introduced in 2003 or Maximum Impact (or beyond).

Pairings I don't support: any involving the aforementioned "least favorite characters" (especially if the "least favorite character" is paired with a "favorite character"), most crossover pairings (though I actully do like a FEW of these), Athena+Kyo, Shermie+Iori Mai+Eiji, Shermie+Ryuji (though that's not exactly a "pairing", per se), Mai+Iori (can you tell I'm fed up with all these random Iori pairings?), Chizuru+Shingo, Mai+Geese, Mary+Iori, all self-insert pairings, Mai+anyone except Andy really (I don't like how Andy's almost always a total bastard and Mai's almost always a brain-dead whore (if not a Mary Sue) in those fics),and any slash/yaoi/yuri/shounen-ai/shoujo-ai pairings (due mainly to the numerous Iori+Kyo and Mai+random female fics out there)

Other series I like: "Slayers", "Urusei Yatsura", "Rurouni Kenshin", and "Fighting Vipers" (that last one's an obscure fighter on the Sega Saturn, with a sequel on the Sega Dreamcast).

As you can tell by my "favorite stories" list, though I do read other kinds of stories as well, I'm partial to romances, even lemons (so long as they are devoid of rape, bestiality, or tentacles and no more than two people are involved at once).

If I ever write any fics myself, they more than likely will be romances involving a pairing I support, though I doubt I'll write any lemons.
