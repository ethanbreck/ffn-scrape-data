New UPDATE: March 25, 2008

Wow, how long has it been since I've been on here? Way too long. I don't know if anyone is reading this or not, but if there are still people out there waiting for updates, I'm so sorry for the long (long) wait. As far as fanfiction goes, I'm not much for it these days. I still like to read it and write something out every now and again, but its not as important as it was once upon a time. I've spent the last hour looking over all my old fics all the while covering my eyes and shaking my head in denial. Wow, those were interesting!

As far as Desert Tales and Fathers & Sons goes, someday I do want to update those. I don't know when, but maybe my muse will return and I can finish those up. If not, oh well. They were fun while they lasted. I am going to put up some new stuff however. I got into the Role Playing world a couple years ago and in result to a lot of storylines I took part in, I started putting them into story form and so I may be posting those, see how they fly on here. As well as a story I've had in progress for years, which was going to be my sequel to "Sunnydale High" but that story pooped out and I'm going to pretend it doesn't exist. I do want to post the other one though.

Hopefully, this will be the start of my return to this lovely site. I have so many good memories here.

UPDATE 8/20 Still working on Desert Tales. Will finish that, even if it takes me forever.

~SUNNYDALE HIGH IS OFFICIALLY SHELVED UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE

I am a total BtVS and AtS freak!! You really can't call me obsessed, as that stage is already passed. I love to read and write fanfic, and some of you have even told me how good my writing is. Buffy is a show unlike others. It has made me cry, laugh, yell at my TV, and keep me in shock that sometimes lasts days. I wanna thank Nick Brendon for being on the show, he is the reason I started to watch in the first place, (former stutterer like me). Anyways, my absolute fave characters are Xander, Spike, Anya, Tara, Wesley, Fred, Cordelia, and Angel. I love all the others too (with the exception of Warren, Justine, and Caleb)but these are my fave.

Couples:

I have to say a big thank you to the cast and crew of Buffy the Vampire Slayer for a wonderful 7 seasons. This show will live on in all our hearts(and DVD's too!) forever! Thank God for Joss Whedon and his genius mind. You rule Joss!!
