I am a simple girl whose biggest desire is simply to walk through life instead of being dragged through it.

July 13th - I'm baaaack. Foreign Affairs is finally updated, after a long absence. My apologies! I hope you can forgive me!

I draw; I paint; I write things occasionally. [Sometimes, people even read them.] The mind boggles.

I have too many fandoms to count, really. My writing is focusing intently on Avatar: The Last Airbender for now, but who knows what subject my brain will coerce me into scribbling about next. I try to never leaving you hanging for too long with my updates. Hopefully, you'll get as much out of reading my fabrications as I do writing them. *shrug*

Anyway... Cheers to obsessive storytellers, artists and muses with too much time on their hands!
