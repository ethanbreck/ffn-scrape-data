*blows cobwebs off poor neglected bio*

Oh dear. I've suddenly realised that I've been here for yonks and have not posted a darn thing for well over two years. Maybe even three. I've lost count.

Sadly, it is beginning to look doubtful that I will ever finish 'A Mirror Up To Life'. Partly because I am not the huge Tenchi Muyo fan I once was, partly because I have done no work on the fic for the past two years (*cringes*), but mostly because I just plain lost interest in the story. To anyone who read what I'd done so far, I'm really sorry. I know how annoying it can be to read a WIP that never gets finished.

At any rate, at least I learned one thing from the 'Mirror' fiasco, which is: never post _anything_ of a chaptered fic until at least 3/4 of the entire fic is already completed. Since my projects tend to be loooooong, and I suffer from writers' block almost constantly, I'll probably lose interest in any fic I attempt right after finishing the first three chapters. So it looks increasingly doubtful that anything of mine will ever make it onto this site. :D Oh well. Such is life.

UPDATED 10/09/04* Wow, wouldja lookit that? I actually posted something. This is a minor miracle - I'm actually rather stunned. It's the first chapter of a Pokemon fic, imaginatively entitled 'Guys, Dolls and Pokemon'. It's just a silly, fluffy thing, based on the plot of the musical 'Guys and Dolls'. (If anyone reading this hasn't yet been to see 'Guys and Dolls'... well, what are you waiting for? Go, go, go! Or at least rent the movie or something.) Anyhoo, any and all reviews would be appreciated and squeed over. ^_^ It's Contestshippy and Pokeshippy, for all you romance-philes out there.
