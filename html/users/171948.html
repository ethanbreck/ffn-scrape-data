I am an aspiring writer, but find that writing fan fiction is not only a lot easier, it also helps with writer's block. I've been writing seriously since my sophomore year in high school (about ten years ago). I shall try to update at least once a month with a story or two.

I also am an avid Brit humor fan. I think that the Brits have the best sense of humor in the entire world and have a way with words that is incomparable with any other culture.

I usually leave reviews, and if you leave me one I shall try to write you back. Constructive criticism and praise are always welcomed.
