Hi Everybody!

I'm a me! Panda!

I write what I know, family, weed, and food. As I get to know more things in this vast space we call a world I will write about them.

But seriously I write about food, and kitchens a lot. In the home there is often a central room, where important memories happen, good and bad, and the kitchen seems to be it for most of the families and homes I belong to.

I love to write, as you can probably tell, though I can't really promise to update consistently, because of life, and those periods of time a writer goes through where they look at anything they've written and go, "Oh god no, this is awful. How did I write this shit, I think I'm going to be sick." that happen from time to time.

Most of my stuff at the moment involves spanking, though not all. Please don't think this means I think real life people should be spanked, not without their consent anyway. I certainly don't believe it's appropriate for children in rl or fiction(older teens/young adults and adults are a different story in fic). Nothing like that will appear on my page. Also please do not ask me to read or review anything involving that. I will gladly review other works(not that I'm an expert at constructive criticism, but I can try :) but not that.

If any of that bothers you, there is a very simple answer-don't read it!
