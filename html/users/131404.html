A word (or words, should I choose to be wordy) from the author:

To be a prolific writer was a dream I once cherished and held very dear to my heart. My early attempts at such remain on this profile like tombstones; Projects that could have been great had I the skill to relate the story I was attempting to tell. I will not remove these, as they will serve as testaments to the improvements I have developed over the past several years and as reminders of a happier time when writing as a singular author was something I made a point to do every day, rain or shine, class or no class.

As I feel my endeavors of being a renowned scientist begin to suck away what little blot of creativity I have left in this predominately math and code filled brain, I reach out again to FF.net in attempt to revive a dream nigh forgotten. I hope, in submitting what is sure to be a long and involved story, that my writing has improved over the past few years that I have been absent.

In the way of updates, I cannot assure a steady update pattern as creativity hits me at random blips throughout my day and most of my life is siphoned away by the PhD dissertation and research that I am currently struggling to complete. It is because of this dissertation that I return to this community, as scientific writing is, on the whole, dry and unimaginative.

Hobbies: Reading, writing, playing video games, chastising my computer when it refuses to run my model for research, throwing said computer through walls.

Orientation: Bi
