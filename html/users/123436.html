I write things. Some of those things are in English. Some of that ends up here.

These days I usually read Harry Potter. Before that it was Star Trek, and before that, The X-Files. I ship Harry/Hermione, Trip/T'Pol, and Mulder/Scully (among many lesser pairings), but my OTP is and will always be Julian/Jadzia.

A lot of stuff in this account is from when I was a teenager. I leave it up because I hate deleting things, not because I think you should actually read it. (Although I'm a bit proud of Turbolift, to tell the truth.)
