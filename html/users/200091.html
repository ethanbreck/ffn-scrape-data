"When I was ten, I read fairy tales in secret and would have been ashamed if I had been found doing so. Now that I am 50, I read them openly. When I became a man, I put away childish things--including the fear of childishness and the desire to be grown-up." -C.S. Lewis

To all of you waiting for the novel-length version of In Brightest Day, thank you for your interest. I am over 25,000 words into the story. However, a combination of moving, a new job, and a thrashed PC have left me unable to work on it since November of '08. But now that everything is beginning to calm down and I'm taking an extended break from work, I've been working on it with renewed fervor. In the meantime, I'll be posting a few one-shots and short stories.

About Me:

My name is Emily and I write crossovers. Every now and then you might find a single-fandom story amongst my fics, but it won't happen often. My two favorite fandoms to cross are DC Comics and Harry Potter, though I don't mind dabbling in others.

Fandoms:

1.) DC Comics – especially Batman, Nightwing, Robin, Batgirl (Cass Cain), Superman, Wonder Woman, J'onn J'onnz, Jason Blood, and John Constantine. (The comic version, not the movie. Keanu Reeves will never be Constantine to me.)

2.) Harry Potter - Harry himself is my favorite character.

3.) Supernatural - I'm still fairly new to this fandom, but I think I'm in love.

4.) Discworld - Terry Pratchett is a genius.

5.) Transformers - Mostly movieverse.

6.) Psych - It's too adorable not to love.

7.) The Fast and the Furious movies - Gorgeous cars and hot guys. Really, what more can I ask for in a fandom?

Favorites:

Favorite Books: Anything by Terry Pratchett or Neil Gaiman, but especially Good Omens and Gaiman's American Gods, and Pratchett's Reaper Man and Soul Music. Romance of the Three Kingdoms by Luo Guanzhong, The Art of War by Sun Tzu, A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens, Kindred by Octavia Butler, and Lucky Jim by Kingsley Amis

Favorite Video Games: The Gears of War series, Batman: Arkham Asylum and Arkham City, Borderlands, Elder Scrolls: Skyrim and Morrowind, Kingdom Hearts I & II, Final Fantasy XII.

Favorite Movie: Snatch

Favorite TV Shows: Archer, Supernatural, The Daily Show with Jon Stewart, The Colbert Report.

Favorite Songs: Over the Hills and Far Away and Ramble On by Led Zeppelin and Highway to Hell by AC/DC are my absolute favorite songs. Others include Breathing by Lifehouse, Such Great Heights and The District Sleeps Alone Tonight by The Postal Service, Son of a Preacher Man by Dusty Springfield, Always on My Mind and Suspicious Minds, both of which are by Elvis.
