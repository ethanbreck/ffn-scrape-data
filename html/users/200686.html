Personal Info...

I'm a huge Final Fantasy fan with a soft spot for all things CloTi and Gippikku. I don't get much time to write these days as I'm always stuck in work, but when I do find the time I like to write cheery romance fics and dumb parodies. Most of the fics I write never make it onto Fanfiction.net as they tend to really suck. I am working on one fic at the moment though which I'm quite pleased with and should be making it's debut from the end of January. For more info, see the coming soon post below.
