Hello all! I'm a fifteen year old cowgirl from Oklahoma, and I love writing! I swear it, ever since I was eight I've known i was gonna be a writer. My aunt was Carol Fenner, she was a writer, any of y'all heard of her? well, anyways, she was purty cool and influenced me towards writing.

I'm a rabid fanatic of fantasy stuffa, 80's movies (the Brat Pack is wicked awesome!), and Harry Potter. Isn't it great to think that there's a whole world out there that us muggles are just too stupid to notice?!

Er, i play volleyball (kinda suck, but that's all righty), and I'm on the competitive acting squad at my high school. it's fun i guess, but since i'm a lowly sophomore and it's my first year it's super-scary. writing is my vent, what can i say?

if you wanna IM or email me, go ahead, i'm no where near shy or afraid to meet new people. I'm the most talktative person on the face of the planet. WARNING: I'm weird. Very much so.

;) SO yah, have fun reading my stuffa, and don't forget to review! I love people who review! HINT HINT!
