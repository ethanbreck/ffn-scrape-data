Hmm... I have many likes, dislikes, and hobbies. Writing is one of them. Kendo is another. Good luck.

Took... a number of years, but I finally have a number of ff worthy of actually posting without running into the oh-so-dreaded bottle-neck. Enjoy it.

Naruto Stories:

What Else? - Still a work-in-progress. Ran into some SERIOUS plot continuation issues in trying to decide whether to make a move into a CrossOver with A) Diablo, B) Kingdom Hearts, C) a number of other series. I haven't completely abandoned the crossover idea, but as of now it will not be. Though Yakshma will probably be ENTIRELY based on Alucard from Hellsing. That's cause he's friggin BAD-A.

Interestingly enough, I've noticed that nearly 1/3 of all people that 'glance' at my story never make it past Chapter 1. I'm wondering whether its time to run a reboot... Not a rewrite mind you, just a clean-up/spiff-up to improve background, character development, and relationship developments. Thoughts? (Send me a PM and/or Review.)

Uzumaki Naruto and the Demon's Plan - (New) - (Working Title) - In need of a full beta-review (an earlier work). Over 100k story along similar premises to how/why Cloak ended up around in the Deviated Nexus story. The inspiration for Deviated Nexus honestly. I fuggin LOVE this particular story because of creative chakra usage. However, that's also why the story is semi-permanently 'under-development' because Naruto ended up being WAY overpowered. Time-Travel ReWrite or not, gotta keep SOME believability. Though I have considered going the route of the 'Sealed Kunai' instead.

Some of the issues with this story have been resolved as I've rewritten a great deal of it, however I'm still running into the problem of "get on the with the story already" and whether to introduce the Demonic Elements prior to the Wave Arc. We'll see how it turns out.

Harry Potter Stories:

HP and the Deviated Nexus - The hardest story I have ever written in my entire life. Possibly going to be renamed 'Puppet Masters' at some point because a great deal of the actions/inactions within the story revolve around mind-altering spells and abilities being used to influence and not influence everyone BUT Harry (protected as he is via Lily's essence) and one other person who hasn't been identified yet. I have nearly 45k unposted for this story at the moment, but I am STILL rewriting much of the unposted sections to maintain the plots and counter-plots. Might be a while till you see the next chapter.

Unpublished Stories:

A Little More Familiar, A Little More Surreal - (Working Title) - This HP story is 'almost' ready for posting at a hefty 50k pre-written (six chapters), though I'm still correcting off/on through the first few chapters. Its an EXTREMELY convoluted story (like most of my writing) where Harry slowly looses his sanity via many Fae encounters (Not that he KNOWS or the story even indicates in early chapters that the Fae are in any way involved). This story will be my first true attempt at a multi-part story. Part I = Up through Second Task; Part II = 2011 & The American Rebellion (Long story, details later); Part III = Back to the Past;

Naruto / Harry Potter Crossover - The Oddest Wizard - (Working Title) - Currently unpublished with only about 15k words. Fun ideal about a world-dropped Naruto with amnesia and a penchant for pranking being 'mentored' by Harry around Infant Levels. This ends badly for pretty much everyone trying to control the Boy-Who-Lived. Interference from 'Odd Thomas' (though, amusingly, I learned a LOT about Bleach because of it. Haha.). Probably going to be abandoned, though I might post it in a "Stuff" folder for other authors to draw inspiration from alongside some of my old HP stuff like 'Dobby's Boot Camp'.
