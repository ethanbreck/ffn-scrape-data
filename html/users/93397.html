I am a University student from Queensland, Australia. My dearfriend put me onto this website about 4 years ago, thanks Farseeker. I am really into Tamora Pierce, Harry Potter and His Dark Materials and I hope you enjoy my Fanfictions!

I realise that most of my works aren't fantastic, and I get bored with them and leave them in the middle. However, I do hope that I can finish 'The Sorceress of Alois', because I quite like the idea. However, as an update to that, I'm very sorry it's taken me such a long time to update on The Sorceress - I'm so very slack and had terrible writers block!

Please R&R, I don't care if you say bad things about me, it's just constructive critism in the long run (and aside from that, I love getting the emails that tell me that I have a new review!)
