Hey folks. The name's Pierre, but I prefer being called Pac in most places online. I'm a young man in my twenties living in Florida.

I've been doing fanfiction since I was a child, though I've pretty much slacked off on that end in recent years. Partially because I've accepted my lack of ability to do long term projects very well, partially because of moments that have killed inspiration. I'm a major fan of professional wrestling, as well as various anime such as Dragon Ball Z, Tenchi Muyo, to a lesser extent a lot of the other Toonami classics, and in terms of more moden works Fullmetal Alchemist (especially Brotherhood which follows the same story as the manga), Kill la Kill, and to a lesser extent today's "Shonen Big 3" of Bleach, Naruto, and One Piece. As for live action shows, I am currently following Agents of SHIELD and the shows of CW's Arrowverse, and have had a love/hate relationship with Charmed for the past ten years. I also got rap skills, hence the "Dizzle" involved in some old pen names I used to have, but I don't shine those out too much anymore.

I used to have one of the longest bios in the history of this site, and I even think I competed with a friend to see whose bio could be longer. But after not having it updated for over two years at one point, I decided it was too much work to keep up. So I decided I'd start from scratch and jot down a few notes for you peeps later on. Over time I've ended up adding in, fixing, and changing things. What you see below is what you see below. Here goes.

I deleted a few of my old wrestling fanfics back in late July, early August 2013, when I was going through my stories and trying to sort out what pictures I wanted to put on them. But that was more of an old shame thing. My understanding of the characters and concepts I was writing were very flawed. I'm talking 2002-2003 stuff here, where I had Shane McMahon become a regularly active wrestler and a member of Team Xtreme; his real-life wife become on-screen talent; factions like DX, the nWo, and even the Ministry of Darkness completely bastardized; and even a couple stories where I threw in Amy Lee, as in the Evanescence singer Amy Lee, as well as CM Punk before I had any idea who or what he was about other than his look. Not to mention a cheesy idea in one of those stories with Amy Lee and Punk where Undertaker kills bin Laden. YES. IT WAS THAT BAD. No matter how people took to those stories or not at the time, no matter how anybody who remembers them or went through them wants to slice it, I just wanna state this for the record right now. I. Fucking. Sucked. I wasn't even 15 years old yet at the time, but still.

Aside from that, I don't really any more deletions planned anytime soon. Not even the one where I had John Cena as a main protagonist when I was still a fan of the guy. (Nowadays you couldn't get me to like him if you pointed a gun to my head.) Well, except for possibly the Charmed ones, but only because I've got entirely different ideas for my Edge-based OC now that are nowhere near as cheesy or at risk for Gary Stu-dom. What's holding me back from deleting those is some sort of nostalgic "let's see where I started" type deal, as well as the fact that I probably won't be writing anything on this site for my newer ideas. Anyway, that's not directly wrestling-related, so I digress.

Speaking of stuff that's not really wrestling-related, I might put up some lists for favorites in other fandoms. When you'll get to see them, I don't know. I need to find a good enough window of time and control to be able to do it, though. Which is especially tough for me since I'm always finding something else to do and my time management sucks.

As for right now, enjoy my fanfics (probably more than I'd enjoy looking back at them). Any questions, don't hesitate to ask. Peace, everybody. One love.

-Pac.
