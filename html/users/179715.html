Ohayo. Konnichiwa. Hola. Como estas? I would like nothing better to do than to write Fan Fics all day. I like writing lemons and ones so funny you'll laugh your ass off. I'm 15 years old and a freshman. And as the moonsaiyanprincess said "If I hear shit about my fics being shit just cause my age I'll find you and shove my keyboard up your virtual ass!" Anyway my fics are good and please don't put me down because of my age or call me names.

And even though I've just started doing this FF.NET I am very addicted to it. Before I go to school I check for updates & reviews,when I come home from school at 2:30, and from about 8 to "freaking bizarre hours into the night."

I love Ranma 1/2, Dragonball/Z/GT, Sailor Moon, Gundam Wing (Endless Waltz), Ronin Warriors, Utena, Tenchi Muyo (and all the others), Outlaw Star, Ogre Slayer, Mermaid's Scar and countless others.

Basically, I'll watch mostly anything. I love Heero, Duo and Quatre (although he's kinda a sissy). Goku, Gohan, Vegeta and Trunks are awesome. And I can't forget Ranma, Ryouga and Mousse. And I must mention that Mamoru (Darien) has a head that's way to small for the rest of his body and that the same thing must have happened to him as my dog: he was neutered at too young an age. LOL no offense intended I just want to make you people laugh.

Music is my soft spot (next to writing of course). I love Blink 182, Linkin Park, Limp Bizkit, 69 Boyz, M2M, Dream, Faith Hill, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Sugar Ray, Savage Garden, Madonna, No Doubt, Creed, and don't hate me for these: Jessica Simpson, Mandy Moore, Cristina Aguilera, and (don't laugh) Britney Spears. I know she doesn't use her real voice in songs sometimes but they're addicting (like Madonna's) and are great to dance to (another thing I like).

My parents threw their bands at me since I was little so Creedence Clearwater Revival, the Doors, the Rolling Stones, the Beatles, Natalie Merchant, Fleetwood Mac, Aerosmith, and since my mom keeps stealing my Blink 182 CD's I'll just assume that she likes them too.

I write a lot of PG-13 fics because I don't think my lemons should be R because they aren't that level. I do have works in progress that are that level though. I read nothing below PG-13 unless it's funny funny funny because the one thing I love as much as lemon fics are funny fics.

Please read my stories. I think they're good except for a few I'm re-writing because they were the first ones I had written so I'm making them better now that I don't have the mind of an elementary kid. Bear with me since I only have a few up.

If you want really good fics look under my Fav Authors and my Fav Stories because I have good taste in fics (moonsaiyanpprincess rocks!).

The most inportant thing is that none of my fics ARE LOST CAUSES. Just because I haven't updated in a LONG WHILE doesn't mean that I've quit working on the story. It just means I haven't had the time on my hands because my "creative juices" (as my mother calls them) are always flowing with new ideas and stories. So bear with me people.

Please review my stories if you have read them. And please e-mail me if you have anything super-duper negative to say about my fics because I don't think people who actually like my stuff and are waiting for the next chapters to come out need to hear about how horrible YOU may think they are. My website can be found at That's where all my works (and those of many others) are published. And if you would like to join my mailing list then please e-mail hellsangel013-subscribe@yahoogroups.com alrighty.

I know my name is a little odd but that's because my friend insists that I'm an angel from hell, why he would say that I have not the slightest clue. Anyway that's enough about me. Please, please, please R&R my stories, e-mail me, IM me, anything. Ja ne peeps.
