Author has written 10 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, Castlevania, Silmarillion, Lord of the Rings, and Escaflowne.

As you can see from the list of fics below, I have a tendency to write about couples where the male has long blonde/silver hair... Blame it on my archetype couple, Celeborn and Galadriel from The Lord of the Rings﻿. My muses have changed every so often over the years (more often when I was younger and had the time for these things).

Presently, the reigning couple is Lucius and Narcissa Malfoy from Harry Potter (of all things...) D: However, they have yet to produce a decent plot bunny for me.

April 17, 2011 The Likeness of Twilight Lost is now complete!

July 22, 2010

I extremely dislike ff.net's auto formatting. It's mangling a lot of my old stories and depriving them of much needed whitespace between scene changes... Only here for maintenance, for now.

NB: Some of the fics I've uploaded are actually a few years old. The original date I wrote them is still stated at the beginning of each story. For example, my oldest Alucard fic waswritten in 1998. Phew! Hopefully, my writing skills have significantly improved since then (I was 15 then, you do the math). I still liked the older ones enough to post, mainly because I'm halfway between being shameless and being addicted to reviews.
