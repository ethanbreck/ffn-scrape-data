Author has written 29 stories for StarTrek: Enterprise, TV X-overs, Final Fantasy X, and Harry Potter.
I change my biography so often that it isn’t funny. I’ve decided to update it again (5.12.04).

I don’t think anyone ever reads these things (or at least not mine), but I will add some personal details for the fun of it. I am twenty-three years old, American, and female. I have a degree in psychology and am currently attending graduate school for the same. I love science fiction (Star Trek, Babylon 5) and video games (Final Fantasy X).

I don’t write ‘Enterprise’ anymore. I have retired because I ran out of plot ideas and because I have become obsessed with another fandom. My old Enterprise ships: T/T, H/M, and C/P. I still enjoy reading though. "Consider it Shore Leave" has been removed because it was awful.

I also sometimes read fan fiction from the following fandoms: Final Fantasy X, Babylon 5, X-Men: The Movie (K/O, L/M), and Darkwing Duck (when I can find good stories!).

I love the Harry Potter books and the fan fiction (check out my favorite stories' list). It is my latest and continuing obsession.

Favorite characters: Severus, Hermione, Lucius, Gilderoy Lockhart, Filch, and Alastor Moody.

Ships: Albus and Minerva (see note at the end of bio), Severus and Hermione, Remus and Ginny, and a lot of rather rare slash ships.

Note about stories: “Another World”, “Another Time”, and "Another Story" share a universe; all other HP stories I have written stand alone. "A Little Knowledge" is AU from "Another Time" and shares a few minor elements and characters.

"A Werewolf's Clothes" is a one-shot (single chapter fic); also, please read the warnings on that one in the notes section.

Archiving? I'm flattered! If you want to archive anything I have written, please just send me an email and give credit where credit is due (to the original author, to the owners, to the fic writer (me)).

Anyone who is interested in the AD/MM ship is welcome to join or to email me for info.
