Author has written 28 stories for Witchblade, Buffy: The Vampire Slayer, Secret Circle series, Underworld, Harry Potter, Moon Child, Crossing Jordan, Bleach, Leverage, and Glee.

Writing is my passion...

However, writing is one of those things... when muses are fickle, it sometimes takes a lot longer than you would have liked to get things done. So, if I go on for long periods without updating, I beg forgiveness now my muses are oft unpredictable and prone to wandering off at the oddest times.

I started writing Buffy and Witchblade fics. I still have those up here. However, I have since branched out.

Now my writings span not just shows, but movies and books as well. I hope you all enjoy them.

I welcome comments and constructive criticism, however I do not appreciate uneducated flaming. If you do not like a story, you do not need to leave rude, scathing comments. Just don't read it again... forget it exists. If you can't say something nice or helpful then please, do not say anything at all. Thank you.

Just a note for any that are interested in my writing style, I have original work posted on www.fictionpress.net and my id is the same there. Thank you once again!
