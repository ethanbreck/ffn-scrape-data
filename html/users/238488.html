Me, I'm just your typical nerdy fantasy reader. I love my cats and I love biology (especially compared to physics).

Just wanted to say thanks to all reviewers. Sorry for the delays. I've actually been in Australia this past semester and this current semester is shaping up to be a doozy.

Finally updated! Boy was there a dilemma with what to do in the story. I ended up writing one scene three times completely differently each time. This middle part is always the hardest part of a story I find. I know how I want the story to end but figuring out what happens between now and then is a challenge.

Thanks to all those who've read and enjoyed my story even when my updates don't occur as frequently as they should.
