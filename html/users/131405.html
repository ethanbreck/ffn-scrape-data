Joined 11-25-01, id: 131405, Profile Updated: 02-07-17
Author has written 9 stories for Ranma, Sailor Moon, Naruto, White Wolf, Haruhi Suzumiya series, X-overs, Touhou Project, and Familiar of Zero.

Most of my writing interests are Anime/Manga and Video Games of various sorts. There are a few things I'm considering/planning that are more western culture than eastern, but that's neither here nor there, nor even likely to be gotten to all that soon.

My father, Larry Huss, passed away January of last year (2016). Before this I had been proofreading my father's forays into the world of fanfiction, and his own profile on this site holds a number of his stories: they are over at http://www.fanfiction.net/u/2062884/Larry_Huss

Feel free to visit and give them a look-over. I occasionally check on the reviews and PMs that the account receives, so if you have any questions or such on his stories I will eventually get to answering them as best I am able.

Status of my fics

Stuff I've at least gotten started:

Yugasa no Kaze - Permanent Hiatus. I might revisit this, but as it's been quite a while since I worked on it I've lost a decent amount of interest and track of what the planned plot was.

The Tendos' Brother - Permanent Hiatus. Fun while I was first writing it, but with everything else I've been doing I just don't feel the same way about it anymore.

Wolf in the Snow and Insane Magic: Version 2 1/2 - Both of these are on permanent hiatus. I might some day revisit one or both of these, but as it stands what you see is all you're going to get.

Vanilla Ice Cream - Completed One-Shot. I might revise it or clean it up at some point. Otherwise, this one is a done deal.

Wounded Bird - Permanent Hiatus. Unfortunately, while I like the character of Blind Thrush, he's actually pretty boring to write about on his own...

Exciting Investigation of Eastern Mysteries - The SOS-dan has come to Gensokyou. Will either survive the encounter intact? Physically, probably. Mentally? Chapter 9 released from captivity.

Clutter From the Attic - Sweeping out the collection of packed away, forgotten, or just random story concepts from my mind. Part 5 has come forward, despite possibly being sideways itself.

Parlor Tricks - Started on Spacebattles and reposted here, a crossover between Zero No Tsukaima and Exalted, wherein Louise summons a man with more than a few peculiarities.

Fics in the works: who knows when/if these will ever get done. These have been hanging around the twisted depths of my brain, along with other ideas, but they're the most thought out as of yet

Currently Untitled Bubblegum Crisis crossover fic - A crossover with the game Aberrant by White Wolf Publishing. Ripped from his own universe by an out-of-control Nova, Greg Callahan finds himself in the BGC universe in the year 2032 trying to keep himself from getting in trouble. He's not having that easy a time doing so.

Currently Untitled Dirty Pair fic -Someone has done what was thought to be impossible, and hacked the 3WA mainframe, stealing important codes and ciphers. These programs have to be recovered, and unfortunely, the Dirty Pair has been called onto the case.

Virtues of the Father (Ranma 1/2 & The Incredibles crossover) - A oneshot I'm currently in the process of writing. It's an otherwise peaceful day at the Tendo Dojo, but some old secrets are discovered about the older generation.

Currently Untitled Ranma fic - The Nerima Wrecking Crew is on the forefront of any major weirdness that goes on in said town, while everyone else watches from the sidelines. Due to an accident one day, one of these watchers ends up with a closer look than he expected.

Please read my fics, and review them. Flames will be ignored via my mighty powers of obliviousness.

If there's a fic I've started, or one from the list of ones that are still in the pre-written stage from the lists above, that you would like to see in particular, drop me an e-mail or mention it in a review. Can't promise much, but if I get enough interest in a particular one I'll do my best to focus and get to that one done quickly.
