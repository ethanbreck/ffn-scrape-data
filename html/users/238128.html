Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter.

So I started writing The Mistake They Can't Deny the summer before I would be a junior in high school. Its now currently the summer before I'll be a junior in college. My, how the time flies. I can't believe it. And since the time I first started this fic, I think (I hope anyway) that my writing has greatly improved. I've grown as a person, including creatively and its really funny to read back over the fics I have posted here. Sometimes I cringe when I read the first few chapters lol, but many people have seemed to enjoy it, and for that I am thankful.

I just wanna say thanks to anyone who's ever reviewed/put me on their faves list, its much appreciated. I still love reading fanfiction, and I read amazing stories on here and think "how could I possibly compare?" lol. Hopefully one day I'll work up the motivation though. I am one of the world's biggest procrastinators. For some reason I just haven't been able to come up with any good ideas. Everything just seems so overdone. I did really like my idea for DYBIM, but I don't really know where that one would go, especially since it was started way before OOTP and HBP.

I hope one day I'll work up the energy/time to go back and revise my fics and make improvements and such...and to finally finish TMTCD, but lately I've been feeling like my fanfiction creative juices have been drained. I'll blame all the papers I have to write in college.

I won't give up hope that inspiration will hit some day, but for now I think I'll just be reading all the great fics I've come across -
