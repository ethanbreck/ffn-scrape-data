Hi, my name is Kristina Leigh. I'm a theatre major at the University of Alabama at Birmingham in Birmingham, AL USA. I am studying to be a Costume Designer & Costume Historian in Film, Theatre, and TV. I'm a big Romantic. I enjoy film, theatre, opera, costuming, designing, romance novels, history, pop culture, writing, and being a professional student. You can visit my costume portfolio online at kristinaleigh.com or visit my facebook here.

You may have noticed I have some pretty old fanfics under my userID. I started this account back in 2002 when I was still in high school. (Seems like forever ago) I've decided to start writing again, so hopefully I have gotten somewhat better.

I'm currently working on finishing my Superman fic "From This Moment On" and my Iron Man fic "Passionate Dreams". I'm also planning on starting Star Trek Spock/Uhura shippers soon. :) I'm excited!
