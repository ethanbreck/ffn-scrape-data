So, sorry. I'm on a fanfiction story writing hiatus... but it's been wayyyy too long. After a couple of years I am finally starting to feel some buzzing in my head. Probably some Hart of Dixie coming in the near future. Stay tuned!~1/9/16

The name's Danielle...

My favorite ships:

Life with Derek—

Derek and Casey ( Dasey )-- Dasey just = LOVELOVELOVELOVELOVE! I think they call it my OTP? hehe

Harry Potter—

Draco and Hermione (Dramione)-- My explanation would be too long for them. So here:

Harry and Hermione (Harmony?)-- As I've read in a few other profiles..."JK fudged this one up..." I agree.

Gilmore Girls—

Jess and Rory (Literati)-- Literati= true love --their characters were meant to be. However, if Jess and Rory can't be together—

Tristan and Rory (Trory)-- They kicked him off the show before they could become official, but there was definitely something there. Jess basically took over from where Tristan left off. If Chad hadn't gone and starred in his own show, there would have been no Jess and Trory would have happened!

Marty and Rory (Mary..haha I just made that one up right now)-- OKay...so, what exactly was the point of Marty's character if it wasn't for him to be in love with Rory and for her to love him back? Well...once again, the series messed it up. It ended too soon. The show was going in the direction of a Rory/Marty pairing (FINALLY), but then it ended. In my opinion, since Tristan and Jess left the picture, Marty was it. Rory and Marty were meant for each other, the way the show was going. UGH! Where's the happy ending?

Overall, though: Literati. It just makes sense. To be as canon as possible, I would choose Marty for her, just to go along with the show, and Tristan is just for fun since he left so soon. Oh man...this little part was supposed to be me choosing my absolute favorite, but it's too hard…

So, basically…I love all the couples that most people don’t…or the couples that don’t really have a chance in canon (but are oh SO good in fanfiction…).
