Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, Bleach, Puella Magi Madoka Magica/魔法少女まどか★マギカ, and Independence Day.

Crawling out of my cave and dusting this account off to actually post fic again. Hell has frozen over. I've been too shy for a long time. Thank or blame kurgaya for this LOL.

I tend to name-drop and insert crossover characters as placeholders for random walk-on parts instead of coming up with OCs. It amuses me. Said cameos may or may not be entirely in character. They may be no more than convenient cardboard cutouts or they may be important. Depends on what I need.

My stories run long because I like character development and worldbuilding.

I don't have any absolute OTP. I'm likely to read or write damn near any pairing that is written in a way that makes sense and doesn't outright squick me. I actually like shipping characters with various different characters. It's a nice change of pace sometimes.

I'm a woman in my thirties. I have aging parents with health issues. I am completely in charge of health/home care for one of them. I have health issues myself and a collection of odd jobs. Please understand that writing fic is something I do for fun. If someone in my family has a health crisis or other daily life ridiculousness exhausts me, fic gets shoved to the back burner. Sorry, but that's how life goes.
