My name is Sam. Sam Proops. And I'm a senior in High School.

Lots of fandoms, and I somehow manage to balance them all with my insane school schedule.

I'm always busy, so taking time to sit down and get into my stories is rather difficult, but I try.

School-wise, I am on the girls volleyball team, I am School & Community Editor, as well as a Photographer on my school newspaper. I am in Creative Writing Club, Student Council, Outdoors Club, International Club, and I work out 3-5 times a week. I also assist the boys volleyball team in the spring. Needless to say, I'm pretty busy.

I love music, and I am always gaining new songs, albums, and placing them into my itunes/ipod. I am always listening to music and without it, I would probably die faster than if I went without oxygen.

I like to write, but sometimes the vision I have in my mind never comes out quite right in my stories.

And I'm always reading something, too. I'm a big fan of diving into a novel and escaping.

Now, onto the fandoms.

House (HouseWilson (MM or just Angst) or Chase!Angst), Strange Days at Blake Holsey High (JosieZ DaughterDad), Firefly/Serenity (Anything but Tam!Cest), Harry Potter (I'm a sucker for a good Severitus fic), Boy Meets World (anything Shawn + Angst), Whose Line is it Anyway (anything not involving Wayne ughhh), Supernatural (No Win!Cest, thanks... mostly just Angst) uhh and probably more... mostly its just ANGSTANGSTANGST and adorable mentorish fics...

If you actually read this, that's pretty darn cool. I was really bored when I wrote this, haha oops.
