Author has written 9 stories for Valdemar universe, Lord of the Rings, Smallville, X-Men: The Movie, Chronicles of Narnia, and Rookie Blue.

My life is very busy with work and creating curriculum. The only writing I do is when I get inspiration or someone bugs me really hard about something. (Though Yuletide is awesome and gets me to write at least one fic each year, and I'm slowly trying out other challenges and exchanges.)

I do not post on this site any more (I haven't done so since 2010); I find Archive of Our Own much easier to use. You can copy text! And download epubs and pdfs of the fics! For someone with an e-reader, that is an imperative feature.

To find my new works (and any of the old that are good), go to my AO3 works list (put slashes where I put commas and spaces): ArchiveofOurOwn, users, Doranwen, works

You can still get a hold of me with a PM if need be, but if you want to follow my writing, AO3 is the place to do it!

Anam Cara is on permanent hiatus; it needs a complete overhaul, but I have no inclination to work on it. It is here as a reminder of where I came from. (There's a few other fics that are good reminders too; I was an awful writer when I started! Hopefully I'm improved now.)

I've used various betas for different fandoms; some of the early fics didn't have betas. My betas for X-Men fanfiction have been judosas, elshadye, and beansideirae, all from LiveJournal. They are excellent, and I have them to thank for the quality of the "Four Ways" fic. Newer fics are all on AO3, with credit given to betas in the fics' author notes.

General note to everyone: please post fics on the appropriate site (and category)! Original fics belong at FictionPress, fanfiction belongs here. Read the guidelines and follow them, please! :)
