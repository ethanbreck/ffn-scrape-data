I'm finally back online! nearly took a year. Literally the longest I have been without a computer since 1993. It was weird.

Works In Progress (WIP)

More than one way to protect you: Jaune Arc thought he always wanted to be a Hero. Eventually, he learned he only wanted to make a difference. The story starts when he is a young teen and moves will eventually move up to events in VOL1. AU. Each chapter is its own self-contained one-shot in a series of one shots.

A walk alone: Ash starts his journey late and starts looking to try and complete his journey with just his pokemon. He makes friends on the way but can he earn enough badges to enter the Indigo League Conference in time?

Completed Stories

A Complicated Sky: A one shot submission for a contest back in June by the deviantart artist, Perfilles-Studio. A Complicated Sky won third place! Here is the image the submission was based on. www dot deviantart dot com / perfilles-studio / art / CONTEST-ANNOUNCEMENT-Sunset-Yang-x-Jaune-RWBY-748293271

A great thank you for everyone that voted for my story.

Unintended Consequences: Blake centric BlakexJaune story where she is really too curious for her own good and starts to really look around her.

The story was based off a discord conversation of what makes a good Blake story. I never wrote for her and wanted to try.

Everything to gain: JaunexWeiss story. A misunderstanding comedy based on how everyone could think one way and the reality was very different.

The original story was originally based off a reddit prompt. I decided the original was kinda too sad and wanted to write a version that was way more light-hearted.

Cafe Hinata: This is a Love Hina Story taking place two years before Keitaro is requested to visit his grandmother at the Hinata Inn. Keitaro is roped into running the Beach Cafe Hinata in an attempt to save him from being scolded by his father for failing the Todai Exam right after secondary school. When Haruka asks for help from around the new Girls Dormitory, Aoyama Motoko honorably volunteers. AU

Brothers of the Sand: This is a Naruto fanfic that was originally based on a rejected Harry Potter story of Harry leaving the Dursleys to live in London working on his own as a child. The story is completely AU and revolves around the friendships of Gaara and Naruto as well as the group of Naruto, Hinata, and Sasuke as children. The summary of the actual story speaks for itself.

Harry Potter and Voldemorts' Bane: Harry Potter suffered from the curse when he laid Voldemort to rest. He exiles himself to keep his friends safe as well as leaving the wizarding world he is disgusted with. Albus Dumbledore finds him several years later in hopes for Harry to guide a troubled girl. The story circles around Harry's acceptance of the world that he denied himself.

Harry Potter and the Prisoners Predictions: is a sequel to Harry Potter and Voldemorts Bane. This post-Hogwarts story continues the story of Harry living his life after his self-imposed exile and now crippled magic. His worst fears come to pass as both his curse activates and the wizarding world falls apart on itself.

Path of the Hokage: Is a really rough one shot of Naruto's death had he allowed himself to be captured in exchange for the seemingly pointless murder of his teammate Sasuke. The story picks up right at the point where the Akastuki tracked Naruto down to a hotel while Naruto and Jiraiya were on their mission to acquire a new Hokage in canon. The story is a one-shot that really is meant to be a ten to fifteen chapter story condensed into 10k words. The story helped me get over writers' block for my other Naruto story Brothers of the Sand.

Hive Freedom: This story was inspired by the episode of TeenTitans, "deception" where Cyborg had to go undercover to find out what the H.I.V.E. Academy was up to. I started writing it literally after I saw the episode and is the first story I have ever written solely for the purpose of seeing a pairing.

Abandoned:
I started writing in 2000. this was the first story I posted on FF.net and I resubmitted it almost two years later after accidentally deleting it. It is not completed, but I thought I should leave it up here since it was the first thing I published online.

GG's New Member: This Jet Grind Radio story was about the troubled times after the end of the game. The story centers around a blader named Z-ro. Ironically I never really thought of this as a self-insertion fic, and had actually written it before I chose a name as a writer on ff.net. I liked the name and just slapped it on when I signed up. Go figure.

Harry Potter and the Book of Persephone: It is the sixth year story of the Voldemort's Bane series that started after Hogwarts. The story follows Harry and how he became the man we found in Harry Potter and Voldemort's Bane. *update on status* Unfortunately I had lost several chapters of this story. As I was already struggling to keep this one alive, the fact I had to start those chapters again really killed this one. I am not sure if I will move this one to the abandoned section or not.
