I'm writing again...much thanks goes out to SendInTheClowns who found me haunting the Supernatural section and invited me to participate in the Summer of Sam Love fic exchange...I somehow managed to wind up getting the chance to write for her, and the fic for that is posted here. I DO have plans to write another Supernatural fic-real life tends to get in the way here and there...it's like a light switch you know? One minute it's on and everything's running smoothly and going well...and after awhile it's off and everything is completely chaotic. But meh, that's life...right?? I'm currently almost done watching season 03 of Superntural--I feel so far behind sniffles I'm determined to play catch up so I can watch season 05 and read more of the wonderful fics out there! I fell in love with the Sam/Dean brotherly love and I'll sheepishly admit that I'm also one of the limp/sick/angsty Sammy lovers too. I can't help myself! LOL.

As for my other fics…? I’m honestly not sure what’s going to happen to them…for right now they’re on hold. I've been told some people are disappointed in me for this and have gotten some...umm...I'll call them interesting emails for now. I really do apologize...I want to continue them and not give up completely on them since I've come so far with them...maybe it was my wording in the past but I don't WANT to abandon those fics. I'll sheepishly admit that it IS refreshing working on some new stuff for a new fandom-a new way of letting out my creativity in a different way and who knows, maybe by doing that I'll get motivation to finish working on my other fics that're up and running as well. A lot of authors explore writing in other fandoms and that's kind of what I'm doing at this point in time. What I really want to do with them is get that spark back for them and finish them all completely and totally and upload everything at once so you guys can consume it all to your hearts content. Sometimes though, I don't always get what I want...but I can attempt to try.

I do have an LJ…and I plan to eventually get that up and running sometime in the near future (and here I was thinking I was internet savvy...apparently not when it comes to LJ! Heh...), I’m hoping to post fics there as well as here. I’ll put my LJ link on my bio once it’s ready to go…and who knows, maybe I’ll even post some actual stuff that I’ve been working on that isn’t fanfiction…

But for right now I plan to clean up my profile/favorites and whatnot here a little bit and start posting some new stuff…and for those who are wondering, yes, I'm a yaoi fan. I like male/male relationships and reading and writing about them. I don't have yaoi pairings in every fandom that I like though...like Supernatural or Thunderbirds for instance...I'm also a big fan of just sheer brotherly love that both of those fandoms provide. But other fandoms like Digimon, Harry Potter, Kingdom Hearts and some of the other anime/video games I've gotten hooked on?? Bring it on. You can usually see the pairings or the type of fics I like either by what I write or what I read (aka favorites section)
