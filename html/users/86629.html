Permanently out of here. Go throw a party.
Why? Because the rules are stupid. As you may have noticed, the staff took my Marauder MST down. That means that someone reported it, and you know what? I refuse to come to a site that places restrictions one my creativity. I'm moving to FictionAlley ( and taking the MST with me.
I was going to rewrite You're Only Young Twice. I will do that, and upload it, but after that I'm gone from the FFN authors. I'll come here to read and review work, but not to write.
If you don't like FictionAlley, check the `My Website` link above. I'm going to be slowly posting my work up there, too. And if anyone tells me what I can and can't post on my own website... well, then I guess I'll stop writing all together.
The moral of this, Fanfiction.net, is; to `unleash our imagination`, we need a supportive environment in which to do it. Not somewhere where we are only allowed to unleash our imagination in certain ways.
- Admiral Albia
Afternote - Fanfiction.net has now enforced the removal of `bloopers` as well. This means that all my best work is on my website. Go figure.
