Welcome to those who've dare to venture into my personal profile. I am the Lady of The Library of Works. Mainly my own works. To those who do not know me, I am merely Artemis. But my closest of companions I am the entity known as Moony.

To know me is certain death and only few have come out victoriously. But who is to say you who have ventured here will not be defeated.

I am a creature that loves solitude,like many of the people I love to chase. Legolas Greenleaf, Harry Potter, Severus Snape and Lucius Malfoy. I am not a creature that can be soiled by evil or swayed by good. I am but a middle person. Opting for either side for my own personal gain.

But underneath that cold and antisocial attitude is someone very different. That is Moony. For I am so sweet and very kind. Happy to assist and annoying twit that will not leave anyone alone until I can get a smile. And much like my personality, I love people who are like me. Fred and George Weasley, Will Turner, Jack Sparrow and many others.

You will find that for both of personalities, I am very happy to announce my favorites are Lord of The Rings, Harry Potter and Pirates of the Caribbean.

Thank you for visiting my profile.
