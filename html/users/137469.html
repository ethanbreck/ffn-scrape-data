Hello!

I used to write fanfics waayyy back in school, but after a while I slowly stopped. I came to this huge writers block that has lasted about...15 years. Don't get me wrong I still love writing and I've tried to get back in to it several times. I have lots of unfinished drafts that haven't gone anywhere. They just still quietly in my computer...it's a little sad. I'm just not good with writing plot. I can think of scenes and dialogues that seem pretty amazing but when I comes down to writing something with a plot it just doesn't...happen. Maybe one day it will all come back to me. That's why I keep coming back to FF.net, you wonderful writers out there are a great inspiration! I hope to be joining you all once again.

-Xypop
