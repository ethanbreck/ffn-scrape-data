Author has written 7 stories for Gilmore Girls, and Everwood.

i haven't written anything substantial in years, but i'm trying. my grammar is pretty terrible technically but my writing has always been based a lot more on feeling and style then what is considered proper. you can love it or hate it, i don't care either way. i've left all my stories up (even the horrendous early works) because i think development as a writer is important and it just goes to show that you can come a long way with practice.

my personal belief is that no one has ever put a woman's desire into words more perfectly than miss carrie bradshaw when she said:

"I am looking for love. Real Love. Ridiculous, Inconvieniant, Consuming, Can't-live-without-each-other love."

people inspire me. writers inspire me. artists and music and so many things inspire me. i think the world has so much more potential than we allow it to demonstrate and that breaks my heart. i think that's why i love fiction so much, why i love living in a world where you can find everything that you need in one person, where people follow their dreams and live to the fullest of their potential.

it gives me hope that every day is another chance to turn everything around.

lucas/peyton. rory/jess. are pretty much my super couples. i've never understood how people can't root for them, but then again i so rarely understand people.
