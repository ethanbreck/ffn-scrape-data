Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter.

I'm honored that you have taken the time to read my profile. I am a huge fan of Snape and think that J.K Rowling should have at least given him the honor of knowing he was cleared before killing him...but on the other hand...if you rip the epilogue out of the book nothing is written in stone!

I like stories With any of the following people: Bellatrix/Snape, Lucius/Narcissa, Snape/ofc, Draco and sometimes Hermione or Ginny. I am definitely a dark character lover when it comes to fanfiction. I love writing Snape with ofc's because I just don't believe that J.K. Rowling made a character that is perfect for him and as I relate to him on so many levels I believe he has the potential for so much more!

For those of you reading The Other Child, I would like to apologize that it has been so long since it was updated, during the writing of this story I suffered the loss of a child and took a leave from writing. I will get back to it, its just going to be tough seeing as in the story that was about to happen to a character.

The Mage is complete, but there is a potential to add more if there is a demand for it.

Also looking for someone willing to draw up some fanart for the Mage or at the very least a cover for it.
