Author has written 3 stories for Ranma, and Dragon Ball Z.

A former college student who writes too little and reads too much - the sheer volume of fanfiction I've read scares me at times. I seem to be a PoV snob of some sort. I find that odd...but I just can't read certain things like poorly established first person, especially the kind that jump around and denote who is viewing this point each time. Crazy. Also, I have more than 535 people on alert. And I wonder why I don't write enough?

I had been working on beating thegrum for slowness of writing, but then I went and updated (and it only took 3 years)! Oh well, let's see if it motivates me (hint: 1 year after I updated, I haven't been very motivated).

If anyone's interested (and I'd be surprised if they were), HGtM chapter 2 is WIP as of Jan '09, as well as another new thing. Projected posting date is around late 2011 at my current rate of writing. If anyone does care, I promise to write if I am contacted! I'm not expecting to be, but I wanted to try and make this profile more interesting.
