About me

I'm in a college rite now. In the center of Malaysia, KL. Big city, busy life. Taking up TESL course. Of coz, TESL means majoring in English. My life is so far so good rite now... Nuthin' much to say.

Recently, I stumbled upon a manga by Matsuri Akino-sensei, Petshop of Horrors. I told my friends about it, and how ashamed I am to find out that it is a well-known manga... How can I not know about this great manga? Since then, I tried my best to find Matsuri Akino's other mangas... So far I've read Night Exile, Shin Petshop of Horrors and Elixir... Ooh... I've fallen for her style... (Alex is uber cool! But maybe Cain is cooler .~)

Another manga that I'm crazy about rite now is Kouryu Shuurei's Alichino... I know... Other people already knew about it, and I also knew about it since 2000 smth, i think... But only recently that I had managed to get my hands on the manga... The art is sooooo beautiful!

Of course, I would never forget my all time favorite mangaka, Kaori Yuki sensei...One of her latest manga, Blood Hound is sooo cool! It's about vampires... But nothing can challenge my Cain! That's why rite now I'm dedicating myself to write Count Cain's fics and forgetting my other unfinished fics -_-;;; Saiyuki, Fushigi Yuugi and Gravitation can wait... Haha! Another thing, for some weird reason,I kept using H.I.M's song to inspire me to write Cain's fic. Dunno why... I currently am listening to Gackt, Hyde and Laruku's song... But the lyrics didn't fit the image of my Cain. sigh

Oh.. Yesterday I finished reading Yuu Watase's Imadoki and Alice 19th and it's sooooo funny! I don't usually read mushy shoujo-manga, butI have to let these pass... Not like Watase-sensei's other manga, the endings are not that crappy... (no offense to Watase-sensei's fan...)
