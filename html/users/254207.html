Existing Works
Mr. Anime Bishie- A Miss America type pageant, it’s a contest to see which bishounen is most desirable. The reviewers picked 35 bishies from different animes, and 5 get voted off per round. Autobiography of an Elf – In the wonderful works of Tolkien, elves are immortal. What if Legolas was so much more than just a fictional character, and had been around for years? Not like every other LotR story you read with a similar plot! This one is actually developed and interesting!

Rabid Fan girl- Posted this one soon after I vowed not to work on any more fics while working on Autobiography of an Elf. A rabid fan girl gets sucked into the world of Yugioh, and invades Yami’s privacy. I’m particularly happy with this attempt at humor, and the few reviews I’ve gotten have been complimentary, so I assume it’s good.. It’s my own character, but she’s not a Mary-Sue. Quite the contrary, actually. You could say it’s a Mary-Sue bashing fic…
