Author has written 5 stories for Slayers, Yu-Gi-Oh, Yu Yu Hakusho, and Gundam Wing/AC.

Wooo, eleven years at FF.net! It's hard to believe I've been a part of the community for that long and yet still haven't explored it to it's full potential...

I'd love to give you a biography, but I'm afraid you'd be bored to death, which I can accomplish in far fewer words. Suffice it to say I'm still an aspiring novelist at the not-quite-tender age of twenty-four, working one full time and one part time job, using much of my free time to immerse myself in all that is Supernatural and trying to enjoy life despite the curve balls it tends to throw me. Also, I have a twisted and dirty sense of humor; I am not afraid to employ it.

You can find some of my other stuff under the same penname at Lunaescence.com, where a lot of reader-insert fanfiction can be perused. It's my venue of choice, but I still love FF.

I leave you with my favorite quote of the moment, via 'shitmydadsays' on Twitter: ""Sometimes life leaves a hundred dollar bill on your dresser, and you don't realize until later that it's because it fucked you."

Adieu.
