Notable Story Ideas

The following are a few notable story ideas that I have had over the years. I am posting them here so others can enjoy them and maybe find a true home. Just please PM or Email me to ask to adopt an idea and I shall only ask in return about giving me credit for the idea. Earth Alliance Having Mobile Suits Before The G-Project

This idea sports a few sub-ideas which all basically have the Earth Alliance sporting mobile suits before the start of the G-Project. However, the thing with these machines is that they are not as good as the Coordinator GINN or just that Coordinators are better Mobile Suit pilots then Naturals. Still basically these mobile suits would be basically mobile suits taken from another Gundam series like Gundam Wing, or Gundam X. However, other than that the story pretty much remains the same as the series itself.

Mobile Suit Gundam SEED: The White Lotus

Basically, an idea where Kira Yamato becomes a mercenary mobile suit pilot and thanks to losing his family to some event he is a bit more jaded than his canon counterpart. That said, he arrives at Heliopolis with his crew for some much-needed leave and soon after ZAFT attacks the colony dragging the mercenaries into the battle. They then join up with the Archangel where they become the ship's mobile suit force with Kira now piloting the Strike, but with more experience, he becomes a larger threat to ZAFT then canon Kira. From there the story changes due to the introduction of this new version of Kira.

Robotech: A New Ride

This idea basically has Scott Bernard's Resistance Cell struggling to keep supplied since thanks to only having a small truck they can't carry the needed supplies to keep their small force going for much longer. They then stumble onto a crashed Horizon-T Dropship that carries a larger vehicle that will allow them to carry far more supplies which allow their Mecha to be kept in the fight longer. Other than that there is no real difference in the story just replacing Lunk's small truck with a larger vehicle that can carry more supplies to keep their fight going on longer.

Harry Potter: Knight of Macross

After the Battle in the Ministry of Magic Atrium between Lord Voldemort and Headmaster Albus Dumbledore, Harry Potter is briefly possessed by Voldemort and Harry manages to defeat him by using his Magical Core to destroy him. After this Harry is drummed out of the magical world and is left to wonder the Muggle World. After a while, he starts to finish his education before going on to join the RAF. As he finishes the Academy the Visitor arrives and soon after Lieutenant James Potter Jr. the name Harry takes after his exile, joins the new Variable Fighter Program. Here he rises in rank till he becomes a squadron commander under Commander Roy Fokker the new CAG of the SDF-1 Macross. What new adventure awaits the former Boy-Who-Lived along the stars?

Sword Art Online: The Swordsman and the Valkyrie

What if Kirito met Asuna at the start of the game's Tutorial instead of Klein where Kirito helps train MMO Newbie Asuna? When the Tutorial ends after Kayaba's speech Kirito drags Asuna with him to start powerleveling to prepare for the climb up Aincrad. They then become a powerful duo of players through the rest of the game while becoming ever closer as the story goes on. This idea basically follows a similar story-line to that of SAO Progressive just with Kirito and Asuna being partners from the start of the game.

Sword Art Online: The Moonlight Black Cats

An idea that has Kirito already being a member of the Black Cats at the start of the game. Basically, he had been a member of the same Computer Club the other members were part of before the start of the game. It is only Kirito's experience in the Beta that does see the others of the Club to join the game dragging Kirito kicking and screaming to form a Guild with them. Thanks to having his friends in the game with him he joins Klein staying behind in the Town of Beginning to get them trained up. From there the story follows a similar line just with the Black Cats being Kirito's Guild.

Sword Art Online: A Mother's Love

After the death of the Moonlight Black Cats, Kirito's friends try to help him but nothing is working. At least till Argo finds out that one of her contacts who helps her with the Daily Argo Newspaper is actually Kirito's Real Life Mother. Midori Kirigaya had been invited to SAO by her job so she could write an article about the Nerve Gear and the game itself for their Magazine. Therefore she is trapped in the game along with the rest of the players, but since this is pretty much her first time playing such a major game she mainly stays behind in the Town of Beginning and it is only when she becomes a contact of Argo that she starts helping the other players to Clear the Game. After passing on the data on Nicholas The Renegade to Kirito Argo is shocked that he is going to try to solo the Event Boss and soon after contacts Midori after figuring out she is Kirito's mother. Mother and son then reunite thanks to Argo and Midori consoles Kirito on what had been happening to him since the start of the game.
