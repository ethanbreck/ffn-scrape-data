Author has written 14 stories for Dragon Ball Z, and Dragon Ball.

Hello, all.

As far as my writing goes, I'm very much into drama and action. You'll see both quite a bit in my fics. Nevertheless, I've been in a bit of an experimental mood for the past few months and have been trying to branch out a bit genre-wise. I've already done some experimentation in the suspense and humour genres (the latter of which I've been in quite the mood for recently) and I want to try and tackle a romance sometime in the future. Just to see if I could write a decent one.

You'll probably also notice my affection for the Mirai Timeline. I still do love it and have a couple of other stories I would love to set within it. Only time will tell.

December 2007

Well, what do you know? I'm back! I had all these unfinished fics sitting on my hard drive, so I finally got down to business getting a few of them done. Mostly one-shots that have yet to see the light of day. If anyone is out there who remembers my in-progress stories Inversion of Shadows and Thought Processes (which I doubt, honestly, but meh), I can't promise anything with either of them. I'd like to get both of them finished, but I don't know whether or not it will happen.
