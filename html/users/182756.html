It's been nearly ten years now since I joined this site...

Hello! I am Jaha Canon and I have a few confessions:

My posts here are far from regular. If I start a story I WILL stick with it to the end.. eventually... probably.

Oh, and I often have some trouble formatting my stories on this site. Sorry, I'm old school (point of reference- my join date) and I'm still pretty used to how formatting used to work. Sooo... if you see some odd formatting, it's not because I'm stupid. Much, anyway. :)

I am 26 years old and I was born and raised in California. I have a BA in Japanese and an MA in International Policy Studies. I've also lived in Japan for two years. On an uncool note, I've been job hunting for way too long now.

There's really no reason to go into this, but I've had experiences where people have been surprised that I am, in fact, female. I didn't know "Jaha" sounded like a guy's name, but I guess it doesn't sound very feminine either, I suppose.

One of my definite weaknesses is that I never bother to go through what I write to find typos and errors. I usually find them WEEKS later and then I don't care enough to fix them. Also- I am a bit too ff.net "old school" to want to do the whole "beta reader" thing. I guess I just can't bring myself to take fanfiction seriously enough?

Hopefully you'll be able to look past silly little errors. Remember I'm not paid to try to create this content and enjoy it for what it is. :)

Enjoy my work here, but don't count on it to be frequent. I will not be held responsible for any maniacs who decide to hold their breaths.

これから、よろしくお願いします

-Jaha Canon

p.s. In case you are wondering what happened to about half of my older stories (from the first 9 months I was here), they were deleted due to the script format. I don't know if anyone remembers, but someone had a problem with me for some reason and decided to report my stories for rules that didn't even exist at the time they were written/posted. (I know some people won't agree with me on this, but good riddance, I say!)

Aahhh, geeze, I've been on this site too long.
