Notes about my interpretation of the Ranma 1/2 Universe:

Doctor Tofu is a real doctor and a chiropractor?! In strict manga canon, Tofu is only shown to practice first aid, chiropractic and folk/alternative medicine. He treats Ranma's bruise after Akane nails him (accidentally) with a baseball, treats Akane’s twisted ankle, and has patched Akane up after a fight (when she was younger). In the anime “Akane Goes to the Hospital”, Akane breaks her leg and Tofu puts her in a cast. In the US and in Japan, only medical doctors are allowed to do that. So, I choose to believe that he is a medical doctor as well. Although manga canon does not show this, but it doesn’t prohibit this, either.
