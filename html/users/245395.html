This account is essentially inactive, it's been like 5 years since I've been on FF.N (my last profile said I was 16 and in Grade 11... haha). I'm now 21. I live in Canada and enjoy reading, playing video games, shopping, writing and painting. I'd like to hope that my writing skills have improved since the last time I was here. I know that my awareness of reality has matured so now I can at least know if what I write makes logical sense. Looking back on the stories that I wrote back then, there are pieces in them that really do not make logical sense. When I read my older stuff I cant help but think "What the hell, this would never happen in real life!" sigh

My primary fandom on here used to be Beyblade (obviously). I have since moved to other fandoms, mostly video game related. I'm much less "fangirl-ish" than I used to be. Which is good, believe me.

This account will likely be deactivated in the near future. If I ever finish writing a fic that I can put on FF.Net, I'll just make a new account. Until then, I'll continue to lurk and read stories from various fandoms... vanish
