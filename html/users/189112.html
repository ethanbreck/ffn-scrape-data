Hello all! I'm really into TV fanfiction, but I can't promise you that I'm good at it! I specialize in mostly Buffy The Vampire Slayer fiction (the majority dealing w/ the character of Spike). I also hope to eventually come up with story ideas and join the Whosers of Whose Line Is It Anyway? (American version), since that's my other favorite show. My writings have a tendency to get lengthy, so consider yourself warned! Feedback and reviews are *very* welcomed and appreciated, but to quote a very talented WLIIA author, "flamers will be boiled in oil". =)~~

Update: Because of the ban on actor fanfic, I've deleted my James and Aly story (really innocent and nothing smutty mind you- pure friendship, nothing more), "Discussions of a recovering Witch and reformed Vamp". I don't have my own site, but if anyone's interested *snicker*... just e-mail me and I'll see if I can send it to you either directly within a couple of e-mails or as an attachment.
With friends and by myself I have authored over a dozen Buffy stories, and yet after the ban there was only a small handful that I feel truly comfortable in posting. Which is ironic, because I didn't post as much before the ban because I was paranoid of what people would think of my writing. This has nothing to do with anything- I'm just letting people know that I actually wrote much more then what's posted under this pen name.
