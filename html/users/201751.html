Hi! I am eighteen years old and a Freshman in college. :pouts: Just when I get to the top of the school chain, I'm right back down at the bottom. :sulks:
I am a superb rambler, as you will be more than able to tell if you ever read through this!! Once I get started, it's pretty hard for me to stop, though. '^_^
I am a loyal Anne Bishop fan and am somewhat of an otaku. But I don't know half as much as I want to!! I love Vegeta and his badass attitude, so naturally, I'm a B/V fan. Mostly for a/u where Bulma's more than a snivling woman. (I hate weak women) As is true for most loving b/v fans, I despise Yamchua and wish him an eternity of pain. Sorry Yamchua fans, he was in the way of potential b/v happiness.
I've written a lot for my sister, and am now subjecting my work to the public.
