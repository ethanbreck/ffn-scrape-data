I like (in no particular order) Sailor Moon, Ronin Warriors/Yuroiden Samurai Troopers, Gundam Wing, Legend of Zelda, Undertale, Star Wars, Rurouni Kenshin, Ouran High School Host Club, Cute High Earth Defense Club LOVE!, Mercedes Lackey, The Wheel of Time, Cardcaptor Sakura, and lots of more stuff, but I either can't remember them at the moment or they're not really fanfic type things (like Stardew Valley or FTL).

I like to read, write, play video and board games, and play the piano. There's not much else interesting about me.
