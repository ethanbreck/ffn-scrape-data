I'm Dash Trudeau. I'm 16 and a male of Homo Sapiens Sapiens, about 5'9, 158 lb 175 cm, 72 kg. I'm straight, fairly pale in complexion and of medium build.

I'm interested in Genetics/Biotechnology, History, Philosophy, Politics, Military, Religon, Writing, Music, andDreaming.

My occupation is 'Student', as I'm in Grade 11. I'm taking in the order of time, Math, Law, History, Biology. I'm hoping to be able to pass everything, and I have awards to prove my excellence in History and Biology.My average in all my subjects at the moment is about a 70.

In politics, and related govermental matters, I'm an anarchist first, and a socialist on a secondary train of thought. In my anarchy, you do what you want unless it involves me, because it involves me, I get some say. In the socialism side of things, the goverment should look after the people, and make sure we live good lives with freedom.

My religous beliefs are a mix of Levayan Satanism and Basic Christianity. It took my life of experiences and genetic make up to come to this set of beliefs, and I'm going to try and explain a bit. I'm a firm believer in revenge and not turning the other cheek , enjoying my life, and don't complain about shit you can change. I believe Christ died for my sins, There is one god, and that you should love others.

As a writer, I usually write in short paragraphs. It has a tendancy towards an essay format, with each paragraph discussing something almost totally new. You can see it in my fanfics or in this profile.

Swimming over to another subject, I'm a vegetarian. Our ability to reason beyond "I'm Hungry" and "I need to pee" give us the ideas of morality so we don't need to end the lives of animals for our consumption.Added there's the enviromental factor of it taking 2.1 hectares to produce a tonne of Animal-based foods versus the 1.7 it takes for a tonne of Crop-based foods.

I like industrial Music. Bands like Skinny Puppy:Wumpscut: and KMFDM are really good to my ears, even if they aren;t the favorites of others, or seen on MTV actually a bonus. The lyrics are extremely enjoyable and the songs pretty catchy.

My dreams are often vivid in colour and emotion. They involve me changing the world. Like if were the Anti-Christ. Or if
