Hey here FanFiction traveler's, welcome to my humbel abode. Did I spell that right? Probably not... I'm not a big spelling type of person. My pen name was Pip because Pippin from LOTR's is a freakin' awesome dude. But now it's QueenC, taken from the license plate of Cordelia Chase's car (from Buffy for all of you that don't get it... I pity you if you don't) I'm very big on the Buffy. The name suited me, because seeing that my birth given name is Cailin(and I'm and Junior from Colorado who enjoy's long walks on the beach...wait a minute!) it all works out mighty fine. I'm very down with the Harry Potter maddness as well as the LOTR's and Buffy. I'm in love with Harry Potter, but not JK at the moment. The lack of new material is really digging on my brain. "Beware the Jabberwock my son!!!" Bwahahaha!

So, in the absense of her material, I create my own (my, what a fabulous idea!) All I have right now is a Harry Potter fic (beware, it requires a tissue box) and then the revised edition of a previous origonal that if I find my love for it again, I'll continue with it. And I just got hit with inspiration (AH!!! :Choir of angels sing triumphantly:) , and I started a story all my own. I call it Poob for short, but it's a fantasy about how story telling got lost of the years, and really should have been paid more attention too, because now all of the people who forgot about it, are screwed. I'm pondering possibly putting up the revised edition of another Harry Potter story up, (one I actually finished eons ago) but that requires revising it, and that takes effort I most certainly do not have.

Beware: I am suffering from a large case of missing creativity (you know how Austin lost his mojo... it's a kinda similar case here) So, bare with me until I figure out how to get my damn Muse back. Muchos Gracias citizens of fanfiction-dom. Callooh, Callay!
