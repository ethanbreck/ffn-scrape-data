Author has written 3 stories for El Hazard, Sakura Wars, and Evangelion.

A legend in my own mind, lover of drug and booze humor, and the only fic author (that I know of) who's ever written himself in a fic as the villian, I present for your scorn the results of me having too much free time. Some people accuse me of having low self esteem, I can't figure out why. It is not I who am crazy it is I who am mad. How many more nights and weird mornings can this terrible stuff go on? Did I really come here to work on a story or am I just wandering around in a frenzy of some kind? Anyhow expect most of my works to be crossovers involving at least two of the following, El Hazard, Evangelion, Warhammer, Warhammer 40k (though at the moment I'm keeping my attempts at that underwraps), Ranma 1/2 and She Hulk (or similar things), along with the occasional semi-orginal fic.
