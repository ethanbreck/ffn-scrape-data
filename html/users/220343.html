Author has written 31 stories for Mutant X, Firefly, Andromeda, Fastlane, and Supernatural.

I love to write fanfic and have done so for... decades... (cough).

Perhaps it's the British in me, but I usually find myself writing for characters that I perceive as a bit of an underdog. Like Jesse in MX, or Rhade in Andromeda. I like writing plotty stuff mostly, some angst and occasionally humour. I don't usually write romance or shippy types unless it absolutely has to be there for the plot. Quite a lot of what I write is pretty hokey, but you know what? If I get a kick from writing it, I don't care. And if someone else gets a kick from reading it, then all the better.

I also love to read fanfic, pretty much anything involving my fave characters. Except romance and MarySue. A good plot with angst, h/c and plenty of action/adventure will suck me in every time.
