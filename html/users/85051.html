UPDATE: August 28, 2019

BRIEF UPDATE: Summer is my busy season at work and it's been kicking my butt. I am behind on updates, but I'm still working--just verrrrrry slowly due to burn out.

About Me: Hi! I'm Blue Kat. I have been writing on FF.net for more than half of my life, which is kind of crazy and a weird thing to think about. As a result, the fics on my account are from various points in my fanfic career.

You can also find me on Archive of Our Own and Wattpad as AKABlueKat/Blue Kat and HarryPotterFanfiction.com as Blue Kat. Currently, only two of my fics (Playing With Fire and Delicate) are on AO3 and HPFF. Anything I publish from March 2018 and onward should go on FF.net, AO3, and HPFF. Only the Playing with Fire is on Wattpad, though I'll eventually add Delicate. I'm also on tumblr and Twitter as @akabluekat.

Whenever I post a new chapter, I generally try to include an estimated timeframe for the next update. Occasionally, real life can interfere or a chapter goes longer than I expected or is just more difficult to write. I try to update my profile with this info, but my tumblr is also a good place to check, as I sometimes forget to update my profile as regularly as I should.
