CELL FANFIC UPDATE NOTE (Jan 20): Because some people are asking - I'm STILL working on In Ten Day's Time! Srsly, I am. xD I friggin LOVE Cell too much and am way too obsessed to give up on a fanfic of him. I just stopped updating every single month because it was putting too much of a strain on things with everything else I have going on (e.g. health problems and whatnot) New chapters will come as they come, I proms. x3 Besides, given the fact that lots of the stories I've read seem to only update every half year or so, I think I'm on a rollllllll. (if you don't believe me, I will say THIS - I already have a sequel plotted out xD So yes, I'm not giving up on anything)

NOTE: I am mainly on DeviantArt under the same username.

I am Mouka, 29 years old and still fangirlin' like a high schooler, and this is... well you're not going to find much here :P I'm working on it though! ...since I joined this site in 2001, I've apparently been "working on it" for quite some time I guess. It just took me 14 years to go from reader to writer, no big dealio.

I love Dragon Ball Z! Though mostly Cell... to the point of obsession? Hey, I collect Cell and Cell-related accessories! And I will read pretty much any Cell/OC fic you throw at me. I'm not too much a fan of Cell paired with canon characters though. But when it comes to canon characters, I seriously love me some Vegebul.

I am also a puppyshipping fan and this site is my Kaiba/Jou crack dealer. Thank you, YGO yaoi writers. I love you guys.

At some point, I'd like to try my hand at writing a Kaiba/Jou fic, maybe once I'm finished with In Ten Day's Time. I have an idea floating around in my head for one, but Kaiba is a seriously difficult guy to write I think. After that I maybe might write a sequel to In Ten Day's Time. I do a lot of RPing of Cell and Yuki post-fanfic, so I've already got characters and ideas fleshed out for it, as well as a general timeline of shenanigans they get up to. So... yeah, that might happen.

Let's see... things I enjoy other than all this stuff... I guess entomology and art museums? And making things out of beads. By things I mean giant plastic Cells D: If you wanna see lotsa Cells made of plastic beads, you can see them on my DA account. You know, go there and have a giggle over the sad ways I choose waste my free time.
