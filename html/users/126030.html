I quit writing fan fiction some time ago and am no longer active on this site. Due to RL, family, and time constraints, I have had to give up writing. There simply has to be time, peace, and space available for it, and there really isn't any way to make any anymore. If God wants me to write, He's going to have to step in and help. Until then, it's simply not going to happen.

If you're looking for Midnight, it is no longer available on the net. It was just time to move on. The thing had really had its day. Some people wanted their own copy, and saved it during its last month up. If you wanted a copy and missed it, please feel free to email me. It still exists on my computer, and I'm happy to share it. Just give me proper credit if anyone else reads it.
