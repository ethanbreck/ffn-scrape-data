Hi everyone!

I thought that I might actually want to put up a profile, as I've been a member of ff.net for a few years now. I guess that I first began to write fanfic way back in 6th grade as part of my English class, and that was "fixing" or morphing that "Mandie" book series by Lois Gladys Leppard. I never really thought that much about it being fanfic until much later. While I was in college, I came across, what I thought of a something new, these stories that someone had posted online continuing the TV Series from the 1990's "The Young Riders." Well, I was hooked, but again kind of forgot about it for a year or two. Then, after I graduated college, while I was trying to find a job using online search engines, I stumbled across some "Star Trek: Voyager" fanfic and from then on (around early 2001) I was hooked.

I have since written several stories, though I have published none, just because I refuse to publish a story until it it's finished and I have made my final revisions. So, since I wasn't getting my stories written soon enough for my taste, I decided to help out other authors and become a beta reader. So, I am now listed on a few beta-reading sites and would take some beta work, if someone would like to send me a copy, but I may take a week or two to get back to you depending on my schedule at the time. So, in changing the subject...I have a few authors listed as my fav's and some stories listed there as well, so check them out & you may find the jewel of a story you've been looking for, or at least a good read.

Bye for now and happy reading,
