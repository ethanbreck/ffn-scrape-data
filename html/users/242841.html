Author has written 8 stories for SSX Tricky, Sonic the Hedgehog, Final Fantasy X, Final Fantasy VIII, Inuyasha, and Degrassi.

Name: You can just call me Starlight

Age: At the moment I'm 18

Occupation: I tend to switch jobs a lot, but for now I'm a hostess at a restaurant and a full time college student.

Contact Me: My livejournal is always available to contact me for any reason should need be.

How did I get into fanfiction writing: Well, it started a long time ago at the young age of eleven that I even found myself interested in anime. I found myself attracted to Dragonball Z yet I didn't like how some of the storylines went and I loved thinking of the history of these characters where there was no explaination about them. I eventually wrote my own versions of how the show should go, not knowing that people actually did this and took it seriously. I found this site, Fanfiction.net (maybe you've heard of it?) and began uploading some stories. The first one I was actually proud of was "Foolish", an SSX Tricky fanfiction. Keep in mind, this was around the age of twelve and thirteen. As I get older, I tend to put forth more effort into my writing and I cringe at older stories I've written. I leave them up to see how far I've come though. That's the whole story. .

Likes: I love my school and I thought I'd never say that. I also love sleeping, music, shopping, tanning, talking on the phone, driving endlessly with the stereo blaring so loud my ears hurt, working at my job (when it's not stressful), writing, reading, sociology, laughing, and photographs. I put God ahead of everything. He's been my Savior through everything. I have a family who I'm super close with and a boyfriend I can never get enough of (when we're not always fighting _).

Dislikes: People (which I find ironic since people are what my major is focused on), traffic, fear, jean shopping, and sitting still too long.

Projects Update
Well for now I'm putting "Eyes on Me" on hiatus because the story has really bored me to tears. I hate the writing style, the lack of flair, and the fact I feel it's going nowhere. I'll get back to it eventually but for now my new project is my baby. Titled "Kings and Queens", it's a Seftis that I had the sudden urge to start after playing Final Fantasy VIII once again. I haven't forgotten "Rescued", my Degrassi story. I'll return to that one . . . someday.
