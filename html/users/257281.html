Bio last updated...April 23rd, 2005

I'm a high school student teetering precariously on the tip of a mountain in Colorado. I've got a strong dislike of Mary-Sues, constant silly grammatical/spelling errors, freshmen who claim to be 'punk-rawker-goth!11!', abuse of the exclamation point, and cigarettes.

I love writing Harry Potter fanfiction,and prefer to stick to the darker characters, because they really are the most fun to write. Recently I finished the Malfoy Code of Conduct, although it was deleted off of ff.net due to...lack of story or some such thing. So that is being revised, and several other stories centered on the Malfoys are being written, as well. (Because I feel that it's my responsibility to be Keeper of the Malfoy Family Ego. It's getting rather big, but I love it, and accept the challenge.)

While I like to write Harry Potter fanfiction, I enjoy reading good Aragorn & Legolas friendship stories in the Lord of the Rings fandom. The angst! The torture! I'm drawn to it with morbid fascination. If Aragorn is mortally wounded at some point and about to die, I'm there!

Apart from that, I enjoy acting, haunting the local cinema,causing general chaos with The Them,reveling in my complete and utter dork-dom via actually sneaking into our school auditorium like the drama geeks we are, climbing things, buying new scarves, and stand-up comedy.

currently working on...

The Malfoy Code of Conduct - Chapter 5 Draco Malfoy had a little too much fun with rewriting the Code of Conduct. Still, he did it for the amusement of his own son. He couldn't have Ethan dying of boredom, now could he? Next up: Section 5: On Enemies, or some such thing., in which portraits argue and Ethan's interest inthe life and death of his mysterious grandfather sparks.

In the Phoenix's Eyes - Chapter 14
There's only so much death that Harry can take before he loses it. We are told that those who die never truly leave us. So...where exactly does the Veil lead and why does the Ministry of Magic have access to it? Also, our dear Troika is back. Theytheorized before that maybe it would be bestnot to mess with Lucius Malfoy. Obviously they didn't learn the lesson well enough.

on hiatus...

Switching Adversaries
I've realised it's much easier to focus on one story as opposed to several at one time. This one is on hiatus until I've finished with In the Phoenix's Eyes and The Malfoy Code of Conductthen I'll take up to finishing this one up for you all. Maybe.I'm terribly sorry about the long wait!

Keep on the lookout for more news!

All the best,
