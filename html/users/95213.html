Author has written 5 stories for Digimon, Final Fantasy VII, and Harry Potter.

Since I am venturing once again into fanfiction I have decided to update this long ago written profile.

My most recent fics are Just How Much and Angelus. The reviews I got for Just How Much have really motivated me to write again.

Angelus is an experiment. It is a fanfic I have had in my head for a while and have finally posted. It's a long piece. I'm not sure when it'll be done. My updates are random and infrequent, but if you can handle that, please take the time to read it.

There are other one-shots I have in mind. There is one Draco/Hermione one-shot I have hanging around on my computer, waiting to be edited and finished, but for now, what little writing I do will be focused on Angelus.

June 12, 2012

I am alive. I'm happy to have finally updated Angelus.
It is a longer chapter so I hope you enjoy.

Thank you to all those who have left reviews. It really motivates me to know there are people who are still interested in this story as I am a poor author in terms of keeping in touch with fans.

Thank you again.

I cannot promise when the next chapter will be out, but Angelus is something I continually work on even if it gets neglected from time to time.

July 26, 2012

I have finally uploaded a Hermione/Draco oneshot. It's been in writing for a while, so I'm happy to finally have it up.

It's kind of a woman's story. I wrote it over a long period of time from the course of heartbreak to finding new love. It's kind of written as a cycle, like a progression through seasons. I was inspired quite a bit by the work of "inadaze22" who wrote "Broken" & "Seven Days in April" among other fanfics. She really is a wonderful Hermione/Draco fanfic writer with some Harry/Pansy. If you have not checked her out, you really should.

I would like to have a beta for this work, so if are interested, please leave a review.

Thank you all.

-Bubblemilktea
