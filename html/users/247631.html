Author has written 6 stories for Harry Potter, Batman Begins/Dark Knight, and Gundam Wing/AC.

The name you get for me is Larissa. I've been in a lot of fandoms, but my current favourite is Batman of the Christopher Nolan movies variety. I'm not too into slash, and my current favourite pairing is Joker/Rachel, and since I'm being nice enough to not rag on you and your kinks, I'll thank you very much to not rag on me and mine. I don't suffer fools at all, so criticism of the non-constructive variety will invariably make me yawn and then I'll correct your spelling and/or grammar, grade it, and send it back to you. Trust me, it will look better my way.

Feedback is always a good thing, and I love it. I work hard on everything I write, and I like people to notice the effort; you don't have to like it, but if you don't, either don't comment or leave an intelligent reply to whatever you don't care for.

I tend to incorporate real places and mention other fics in my stories; if you see a reference that you think is from a story you wrote, please believe me that I'm not doing it maliciously. I either really like your story, or I found part of it amusing and I'm poking the good kind of fun.

If you want to contact me, I'm CuteGirlGretch on AIM. Just let me know right off that you're a reader, or else I won't reply.
