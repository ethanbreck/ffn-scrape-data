Author has written 4 stories for Xenosaga, Xenogears, and Naruto.

I'm just a nerd, standing in front of other nerds, asking them to read my fanfictions.

Been on hiatus for...quite a while now, and looking back am somewhat embarrassed by my old writing. Good lord...can you say emoticon abuse? I suppose I shall have to blame it on the fact that I was a lovesick kid at the time with the attention span of a hummingbird on speed. I'll leave some up as a reminder to myself (a shrine to inexperience?), but any oldies will either be cast aside in shame or re-written with less nauseating author's notes (and borderline indicators of dissociative identity disorder) in what is, hopefully, a more mature and wiser voice.

Heh...but if there's an opportune chance for being silly, I might backslide a touch!

I am a scientist by trade and a writer by plot bunny infestation. No real training...I take many liberties with pauses by way of interjection and ellipses, and I've been known to intentionally toss in fragments or write in vernacular. Basically...I enjoy writing how I speak, with all of the idiosyncrasies therein.

I am always looking for new 'fics to read and new genres to try. I will likely try reading any writing that shows an honest effort (ie, not song lyrics with one original line afterwards, nor a 200-word run-on sentence about "I luff yoo's").

I do not write nearly as often as I get ideas, as most of my creative energies are drained at work/school. Time-permitting, I'd like to be more active again and once more find my voice in prose.

If you've ever gotten annoyed by those "If You Ever" things, do NOT copy this into your profile. Nyah.
