I WRITE WHATEVER THE VOICES IN MY HEAD TELL ME TO!

Ahem. Hello there. Well, for those who care, I'm a twenty-something escaped mental patient named Moria, and I live in a rinky dink town no one's ever heard of, in the boonies of northern Arizona, with an assortment of invisible friends. I'm in my senior year of college, so I haven't had a lot of time to write lately. I'm obsessed with Les Miz, Phantom, SP, and pretty much every other musical ever written. I love my RPGs, especially Baldur's Gate, Dragon Age, Fallout 3, and pretty much anything else by Bioware or Bethesda. I also love anything and everything Star Wars, and have an embarrasing fondness for Disney cartoons. My latest obsession is Once Upon a Time (Rumbelle forever!). I generally get my kicks by brutally mocking the above, but it's all in good fun.
