The Apology
I'm so touched by the loyal fans of Exposed but as you can see, I haven't updated that fic in -months- and dare I say a -year-. And being only human, I have simply -forgotten- where I wanted to go with that story. I'm not going to take it down because I still adore it, yet I just...don't know what to do with it. I hate deleting things and maybe someday I'll remember what I wanted to do with it and it'll continue. But for now, Exposed is definitely PAUSED aka not-being-updated. Once again, thank you so much for all your support. I don't think I would be back here without you guys!

Blurb
I'm a sixteen year old girl residing in Canada. I have an unplanned future and is envious to those that do know where they're going ;) I love web design and just having absolutely nothing to do (only if I know that I'm not procrastinating by doing nothing). I'm a great worrier and I have a habit of asking really silly questions. Hey, someone has to ask the stupid questions right? Anyway, I use to be a big fan of CCS, but that's sort of faded and my current obsession is Haruka and Michiru. I'm slowly running more and more out of time for my usual indulgences, so I rarely have time to watch anime anymore. On a lighter note, I also love meeting new people. If you have any further questions/comments or wish to contact me about something, just e-mail at winged.ephemera@gmail.com =)
