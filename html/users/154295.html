Author has written 24 stories for Star Wars, Fanfiction Poetry, Pitch Black / Riddick, X-Men: The Movie, Movie X-overs, Mummy, and Lord of the Rings.
I am married and have one daughter. I do dishes, noses, laundry, vacuuming, and milk mustaches. I also draw pretty pictures, contribute to church classes, and delude myself into thinking I can write. I was born in 1974, but am sometimes mistaken as being older (not sure what to think about that one!) I live just north of Dallas, TX, and am a Unitarian Universalist (one of those weird free-thinking faiths.) My favorite fiction authors include Anne McCaffrey, Charles de Lint, and Mercedes Lackey. In the nonfiction field, Joseph Campbell, Huston Smith, and Desmond Morris are always good for an intellectual drop-kick. (You don't want to see what happens to me after reading Carl Jung.)

Warning: I confess that I am guilty of writing indeterminate endings. Wherever my characters wind up, they generally go on without me--the story doesn't necessarily end where you'd expect it to. So if you like your endings tied up neatly in a bow, go somewhere else: happily-ever-afters rarely happen here.
