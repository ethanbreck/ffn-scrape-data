/+10.14.04+\ *sigh* Sorry everyone, but...since you don't see any updates anyway...I'm terminating this account. Or putting an end to it, in other words. I'm sorry...I'm sorry if you actually enjoyed my work and stuff...I really appreciate that, I do, but...I'm not going to update or continue any of my fics. And its not because I don't have too much time nowadays since I'm in high school (freshmen) its really because I have a passion about another pairing--Seifer/Rinoa. And obviously all my fanfics were Squall/Rinoa before but I don't like the couple anymore...I mean I do but not as much. Not NEARLY as much. I should explain why I like Seifer/Rinoa though but...I guess I'll do that later. On another account. I need to move on, forget about the f*ckin past already... My fics weren't that great anyway in fact barely good at all so yeah whatever. I'll still leave one of them here (other one is not mine--i had a note there but FF.N took it off O_o). So yeah...just so you guys know. If I will ever write fanfictions again...they will be 1) ONLY Seifer/Rinoa, nuthin else. And 2) on another account like I said before. And also please don't ask me to ever continue any of these fics because I won't. Period. I simply cannot write about a couple that I'm no longer attached to or whatever (and this didn't happen in just a day or week by the way).

Well so I guess that's it then. Thank you everyone, I'll never forget you guys =)

My time on this abandoned account is done now...

Peace.

And oh yeah I really don't give a damn sh!t if you hate this pairing...I'm passionate about them, and if you're not a fan then oh well.
