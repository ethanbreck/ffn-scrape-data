Stuff 'bout me!-
I'm a left-handed, butt-kickin', anime lovin', country singin', egotistical, feminist, goddess and you SO don't wanna mess with me!

On a lighter note: I'm from the oh-so boring town of Hesperia that has no sidewalks, no street lamps, no mall, and appears to be Mars in every aspect save that the air is more breathable on Mars. It's in the High Desert in California! My peers think that I'm smart, but only compared to them, since most of them are stupid. Sorry! ^-^
