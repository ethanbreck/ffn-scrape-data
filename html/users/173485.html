News:

Well here I am again. Everyone who used to write with me is gone. There used to be hundreds of new stories every day, and now? Now there are very few people left. I got a little discouraged last time I was here because of how few people are here, but I am determined to finish up my stories, so here I am again!

My name is Mysti, but since I first started this site, I've had two alias'. I was first Sarah McKatie, but then took on the Mystical Starlight name...I've stuck to this one :) I would love to change it but unfortunately I fear that people won't be able to find me, if they're looking )=

I've been here since I was 10/11 and am now 26-years-old. Long time huh? Yes, I am one of the few who have been here from the beginning that actually stayed.

My ULTIMATE favorite ship is D/G but I like Hermione/Sirius and recently Hermione/Snape a bit too, as well as Ginny/Snape... I've sort of taken a shining to Scorpius/Lily. I don't HATE many ships, but I can honestly say H/Hr makes me gag.

Some of the newest stories that I'm working on and have yet to get all the kinks out of are Hermione/Snape, Ginny/Sirius and Scorpius/Lily. I'm rather excited :)

Also, a little note to those of you reading my lovely D/G work:

I will likely refer to Ginny as Virginia. If you notice this it is because most of these stories began before she was referred to as Ginevra in the books. Keep in mind the year that the story was published. On that same token, I like the name Virginia way better than Ginevra.

Also, this is fanfiction. If her name is Virginia, or there is a larger age gap between a set of people, or if someone's eye color has changed, it is FANFICTION. If I want to give Ginny blue hair and polka dotted skin, I have that right. Leave me alone lol.

I love Sirius, Dumbledore, Dobby, Fred and in some cases, Lily and James Potter. If they make guest appearances in some of my stories, so be it. I love them and they are awesome lol.
