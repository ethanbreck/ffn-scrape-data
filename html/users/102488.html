Thanks to everyone who has read for your interest and support in this story; for your comments, positive, negative, inquisitive; and for the time you've spent playing in Sookie's world with me. I have moved on to work on original fiction as well as occasional dalliances with other fandoms. If discipline and inspiration conspire to help me finish this story, you'll be second to know, just after my brilliant beta-reader. For now, the discordant cliffhanger of chapter 30 is all I have to offer. If any writers out there are stricken with the crazy notion to see the story through, please do so with my blessing and great interest. This is fan fiction after all. It's what we do.
