Hmm. I'm actually on here and writing again after well over a decade. Watching Resurrection F gave me some inspiration for writing. If I ever find some more of my very old Gatchaman stuff I may post it.

My writing style has changed a whole lot since I last wrote anything. It seems to be better. If you told me back then I would be able to write something with any length, I'd have laughed. Yet here we are now, and even the shortest modern story I've written dwarfs the old ones.

It looks like 'Blood of the Moon' will be on a bi-weekly update schedule for now. I'm trying to keep the chapters at least 2000 words. I'd make longer chapters, but that would slow down the updates. I have a full outline of what is going to happen in the story and much has been written for future chapters. I'm not sure how far I'm going to take it. So far to the end of Cell but we will see. It is AU so things will be happening a bit different than the main cannon timeline.

(2000?! Boy did I underestimate when I typed that. It's averaging ~4750)
