Hi! I really like to write, and usually I fall into these writing funks where I hide in my room like a recluse for days on end and just write and write and write. So you should expect a big long story every once in a while. I like to write and read. I like good romances, I am a sucker for Romances...sigh. (But not kind that you buy at the checkout counter at the supermarket with the guy on the cover with his shirt off. You know, those stupid novels with dumbass names like, "Her rugged man" or some crap like that? I hate those.) I am a movie freak. I like too many movies to name, but some are: Moulin Rouge, Pride and Prejudice (COLIN FIRTH!), Amelie, Chocolat, Monty Python and the Holy Grail, My Big Fat Greek Wedding, That Thing You Do!, Bridget Jones's Diary (Again, how I love Colin), Romy and Michelle's High School Reunion, and Much Ado About Nothing. Ok, with ome of these movies we're not talking cinematic landmark genius, but that doesn't mean they're good. My favorite food is nachos. Mmmm...nacho cheese. I like lots of books too. Some of my favorite books are Pride and Prejudice, The Scarlet Pimpernel, Emma, Sense and Sensibility, Sisterhood of the Travelling Pants, The Gospel According to Larry, A Tree Grows in Brooklyn, The Anne of Green Gables Series (Anne of the Island is the best one), and these books by Lloyd Alexander with this girl named Vesper Holly who is really cool. I am in love with Sean Biggerstaff! Go Sean! (In case anyone didn't know, Sean Biggerstaff plays Oliver Wood in Harry Potter. I LOVE SEAN!!!) But they SO did not show enough of him in Chamber of Secrets. I am Sean-deprived! Favorite Quotes: "Whatever you do may seem insignificant, but it is most important that you do it." -Gandhi "Whenever you go into someone's house, and they leave you alone in a room, do you look in the drawers? I do. I'm not trying to steal anything; I just like to know where everything is." "Griddle Cakes, pancakes, hotcakes, flapjacks: Why are there four words for grilled batter and only one word for love?" -Last two quotes taken from funny times, a newspaper. Anyway, so these are my annoyingly long stories...read and please REVIEW!!!
