MadamGrandAdmiral here, self-proclaimed commander of the ASC Loveboat...

The New Year brings about a change in my profile: I felt it deserved an update. I never know what to say on these things...

I have been writing fanfiction since I was 13 years old, albeit terrible, as well as attempting to draw my way through life.

Having been through a number of fandoms, particularly enjoy reading Labyrinth, Star Wars, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings and Pirates of the Carribean

Currently studying Politics and History, I spend my life reading, planning fanfiction, revising a lot and thinking about whatever fandom is my forte at that moment (Star Wars at present). My essay results are incredibly promising, and I finally have a job. Woohooo!

My hatred of all things Rebel-based, especially those damnable X-Wing Rogues, expands further than anyone could possibly imagine...

Whilst my adoration of the Empire just keeps growing...

Imperials Never Give Up. Ever.

Fanfiction:

Inertia Part One: Concluded! The first and hardest part of the whole epic saga ended with Dania forgetting, Court plotting and plenty of upset...

Inertia Part Two: Three-quarters done, the climax will be a a gut-wrenching, heart-stopping, pain-ridden showstopper to end all of SW fanfiction forever...possibly. Depending on your perspective, of course...But it will be worth watching

Inertia Part Three: Marriage, Pregnancy, Funerals and Mental Instability...Its gonna be one hell of a blast...

If you have any questions, comments, requests or criticisms, feel free to PM me...

And don't forget to check out my c2 community, dedicated to the one, the only...Grand Admiral Thrawn!
