You really wanna know? *Gasp* Okie dokies, you asked for it!!

Yes, I'm another one of those Middle-Earth freaks - have been for years, having studied the whole kit and caboodle throughout my life, from the Silmarillion to the Unfinished Tales and so on and so on. Although I am not worthy enough to kiss the hallowed ground beneath the late great Professor Tolkien's feet.

And yes, I am another Orlando Bloom fan. So sue me - he shouldn't have come on to Stephen Fry in Wilde, then I'd have been blissfully ignorant of his presence on this earth over the past six years or so.

Head to www.orlandomultimedia.net and see what a star the webmistress is!! She updates almost every day. Jas rawks!! (tell her Aarien sent ya!)

Oh, and the major thing you might wanna know about me is that I'm a little crazy! My friends will tell you, just ask 'em. (Actually, don't. They're still reeling in shock from my profound statement made a while ago: I'm an Elf, dammit!) I'm learning Quenya and Sindarin (slowly). I get too many ideas and never have enough time to write them all down, so apologies if my updates get a bit screwed from time to time. And I like writing OC fics (and I ain't talkin' Mary-Sue's, neither).

Told you I was mad...

Actually, that ain't the half of it!! I also go a little kerazee over the WWF - oops, I mean WWE. Must. Stop. Doing. That. Most of them centre on the Hardy Boyz - I know, I know! - and I don't have anywhere to post 'em now 'cept here.
