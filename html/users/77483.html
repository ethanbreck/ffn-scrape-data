Author has written 16 stories for Harry Potter, and Silmarillion.

I'm a total fan fic addict who has decided to finally try her hand at this. Here's how my stories fit together. Most of them are A/U now, because they were written before OOP came out. From Across the Great Divide, On Her Own and Ghost Story are all part of the same A/U.

The Price of Victory is in a world all its own, and is not what it appears at first. It has also been abandoned.

Ginny's Gift and The Long Road Home form another A/U which is entirely separate from the Great Divide "world".

The Only Ones He Ever Feared is a parody, and has nothing to do with anything except my own warped sense of humour. Through the Glass Darkly is a short missing moment from OOP. Once Upon a Time was written for a H/G fic challenge on Live Journal. It's a one-shot, as is "Through the Glass Darkly".

Finally, In Absentia is based on post-OOP canon, and it features some different ships than what I normally write.

Note to all my readers: Thanks for all the support you've shown me, and thanks to those who continue to read and review. I've had someone ask if I'm planning on writing any more stories in the GG/LRH world. The answer to that is no. I feel that I've written all there is to be written in that storyline. I don't see the point of writing a third story just for the sake of having a trilogy. I've seen too many good series go downhill because the author really didn't have anything new to say, and I don't want to fall into that trap.

Updated: October 21, 2010: Wow, I haven't been over here in a while. A recent review reminded me, however, that I perhaps ought to update my profile. I'm sorry to say that any unfinished fic of mine on this site will, sadly, have to remain unfinished. Book seven pulled me back into writing fanfic, but I soon got the urge to write my own characters. For two years, now, I've been concentrating on writing original fiction in hopes of one day seeing something of mine in print.

Yet another update on the off chance anyone comes across my profile. July 26, 2011: Another message to say that any of my abandoned fics will remain abandoned. I'm sorry for leaving people hanging in some cases, but I have moved on from writing fanfiction, and I don't feel I'll ever return to it. I have been writing original historical romance for three years now, and, as of this spring, have acquired an agent who has sold one of my manuscripts. My debut will be out in late February, 2013. If you'd like more details, including my penname and a link to my author site, PM me.

Thanks to everyone who has read and reviewed my stories over the years. I wouldn't even know that I could write without your encouragement.
