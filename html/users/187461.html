Ok let's start off with the basics:

My name is Lindsey and everyone calls me that. Some select few call me Lin and even fewer call me Linny or LynnseyChynnsey or even "That smart girl".

I have brown hair with natural auburn and blonde highlights. My eyes are brown also, but more of a lighter brown because I'm always happy. They turn dark when I'm upset or angry.

I'm kind of pale but this year I promised myself to get a nice tan. I probably will get one too. I'm about 5'7".

I have a boyfriend. His name is Will. I believe he is my one and only. I know I'm in love.

My pen name WAS DeAtH2aLlPrEpZ but I have decided thats a little mean. My name now reflects my chinese horoscope and my favorite Hogwarts house.

How about some information about my preferences?

Music: I really like all types of music. From classic rock to Motown, J-Pop to boybands, metal to 90's dance, I like it all. I dont like gangster rap that much, especially rappers that rap about killing people. It's not necessary

T.V.: I watch a lot of T.V., but not really series or reality TV, sorry but I can't stand shows like that. I watch CSI. Thats about the only 'real person' TV I watch. That and Whose Line is it Anyway and Mad TV. I watch a whole load of cartoons, too. Mostly anime.

Books: I love to read. My favorite books are the Harry Potter Series and Agatha Christie. Oh, and I just read Angels and Demons and The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown.

Ships: Hermione/Draco, Hermione/Snape, Hermione/Fred/George, Sesshomaru/Kagome.
