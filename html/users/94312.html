Okay, after a long hiatus, I've decided I'm leaving fanfiction.net. I'm just tired of all the hyper-critical people that seem to lurk in the shadows here. I might start posting elsewhere, but it won't be here. If I do find a site, I'll put it up here in case anyone is interested in abandoning fanfiction.net with me. Its been fun while it lasted, but I think I'm through.

For anyone who is interested at all, I took down my Ariana series because people were criticizing it YEARS after I'd finished it, and I was sick of having to read their reviews. I haven't posted it anywhere else yet, and I doubt I ever will.

Feel free to read my stories anyway if you like.

Don't like it? Megabyte me.
