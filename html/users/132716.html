Penelope has been updated once more! And once more, I dangle shippiness in front of my loyal readers only to yank it away again at the last second. Oh, well. Morgan has contacted me and requested a one-shot based on the parting scene between Penelope and Odysseus right before Odysseus leaves for Troy. I'm still currently in the "Begging Thalia For Inspiration" stage, and as I leave for college (W&M!) in two days, it might be a while before poor Morgan gets her prize, which makes it even longer for the rest of yous. If I even have time to think about the Odyssey between orientation and figuring out registering for classes online, I'm lucky. Meanwhile, go read some stories by Rosethorn, my beta (even though she hasn't updated since way before the last time I updated grrgrowlmuttermuttergrumble). Or if you're looking for some good Odyssey fics, check out (off the top of my head, sorry if I've forgotten somebody) AphroditeIncarnate , Idun03, or Alyx Bradford(who hides it on fictionpress.com, Heaven knows why).

As for my other stories...everything else is a oneshot except for my Labyrinth fic, and it'll probably take the second coming of Christ to resurrect that doozie.

Current Plotbunny: Arthur Weasley enrolled in a muggle Physics class. Just imagine it for one second, and you'll see why I'm in love with the idea. Think about it. Really. Other than that, the chances of me doing a non-Homer oneshot in the future is slim to none.
