I'M BACK! Maybe? Hah.

Time for reflection and excuses.

First of all, ff.net isn't the site that I originally stumbled upon. My tastes have changed, and so have the tastes of authors on this site.

Most of my favorite authors are gone. Many of my favorite fics remain unfinished and/or deleted. I'm having a hard time finding quality fics of my old favorite Sailor Moon Crossovers domain. This isn't to say that I don't love SM anymore, and I sincerely hope that I will finish my ffs. Comments help keep me inspired, and if you feel so inclined, drop me a line.

On a shorter note, other things that are probably dead:

CCS fics - the only thing I'm looking forward to is L-chan finishing Shadows, and there's not much CCS left to analyze

aSoUE - I love my Violet/Klaus, but with nothing new coming, nothing inspires

Xenosaga: no idea what's happening with this, cuz I'm not playing, wish I could just watch the cut scenes, still love MOMO/Jr

Looking forward, you might expect some more Narnia fics. I have a love/hate relationship with Narnia (mostly because of end treatment to Susan) that I'm trying to just detach myself from.

I want to write, believe me, I do.

But I'm in college now. And I have other commitments.

And actually, if I ever get good enough, I'd love to make manga/doujinshis of these instead. And also do a few originals.

If you have a strong opinion on me finishing anything - TALK TO ME, we'll work something out.
