I am a 30 something stay at home mom of three. I've been writing fanfiction for...a long time now. I want to say somewhere around 10-15 years but a lot of that time I had complete writer's block.

I started writing again when Dragon Age:Origins came out. Unfortunately I had to remove a story I loved but just couldn't finish. Currently obsessed with Mystic Messenger (Seven...be still my heart. It's always the ginger that gets me.). Working on a few stories at once as inspiration strikes. I have a toddler so I can't always find the time I want to dedicate to my passion but if you enjoy what I write I hope you stick with me.

Watch this space for upcoming works!
