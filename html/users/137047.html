Hullo! Lets see, who am I? I am now an out of work History and Theatre person... looking for a job sucks, by the way, especially with the economy... eh, lets not get into that. Anyways... I specialize in freaking people out. It's a fun pasttime and I'm firmly convinced that more people should try it out. People that scare me: My brother manages quite nicely, and uh... many a politician...

My obsessions are:

Sherlock Holmes (Holmes/Watson), Star Trek (Jones), Tin Man (Cain/Glitch), Harry Potter (SS/HP) Stargate SG-1 (J/D) A-Team (F/M) Star Wars (Luke and Mara, look Ma, a straight couple!) Reboot (Bob/Dot, or Bob/Hex, depending on the mood and story.) MASH (BJ/H) Naruto (SasuNaru) Saiyuki (8338, Sanzo/Hakkai) FFVII (Seph/Vin, yes, I am crazy) and Due South (Fraser/RayK)

"Never miss a good chance to shut up."

"Have you ever walked in snowy woods on a moonlit evening? Have you ever sat on the beach with only the stars to see by as the waves crash around you? Have you sat on a rock, sunbathing in the middle of a lake, listening to the sound of the waves crashing near you? Have you sat outside in the freezing cold in February just to see an eclipse? Have you done these things alone, or did you bring a friend? Did you and your friend not speak, simply enjoyed the silence of the company you love so well? Or did you talk of subjects you never thought you could mention to them? Did you talk of God, politics, philosophy, and NOT argue, just accepted someone's view?

If you have never done any of this, and if you ignore everything else I say, please pay attention to this:

It is not how much money you make, it's how many stars you can count in the night sky. It's not how many friends you have, it's how many you can't live without. It's not how fast you live your life, it's how often you look around at the beauty around you. Watch the sun rise, watch it set, and know you accomplished something that day. Look at the stars, and see the beauty of nature. True beauty, true peace is around you, please, just look at it."
