Author has written 4 stories for Inuyasha.
Thanks for all the reviews! I was impressed with the responses I've gotten so far. I know the fics I have are short, but I hope you all enjoy them anyway. So far all I have had are short bursts of inspiration, but I will try to mull over some ideas for longer pieces. In the meantime, there is other content on my webpage if you are absolutely starving for something to read. I'm a 4th year English major, and my college schedule keeps me very busy, so updates both here and there come very slowly. At any rate, enjoy what is here. I try to return at least a couple of reviews from each story -- I only have a limited amount of time and I don't usually review stories if I'm not familiar with the fandom it's based on, but I do my best.

Status of Beyond Blood:

2/23/03
Chapter 6 has been uploaded - I have ideas and a few bits and pieces for Chapter 7, but nothing too concrete yet, so - it will come eventually, I just don't know when. I have midterms to get through first!

In other news, I'm thinking about starting another Inuyasha fic called Wolfsong, but it would involve an original character and I don't know how enthused anyone would really be. But it's interesting me and I might play with it, and if I do, I might post it. But hopefully I will finish the next Beyond Blood chapter before I do that.
