You're actually making a difference in someone's life.

Ah, sorry for the confusion! I'm using the Japanese names for the Dark Kingdom generals wherever I use Japanese names for the other characters; so they might look weird but they're correct since I looked them up just to double-check (and for the most part, their names get abbreviated for the length of the story usually).

Nephrite = Nefuraito
Jadeite = Jedaito
Zoisite = Zoisaito
Kunzite = Kunzaito

'i' is the letter E except in 'ai,' which is always pronounced 'aye'; 'e' is the letter A; 'to' is 'toe'

Also, honorifics are a pain to write so if they don't show up at the beginning they probably won't in the rest of the story unless it is meant as an endearment or some other interesting connotation. This is only a handful of the ones that exist but these are the most popular and will probably be the only ones that I use.

-chin – cute way to call a close girl friend (not used with guys ever)
-chan – used with close friends/relations
-none – used with people close to you; interchangeable with -chan; can also be rude if you drop appropriate suffixes
-kun – used on close male friends; often used by younger children when they address each other regardless of their sex and sometimes by bosses to address professional subordinate women who work for them
-san – those who are older than you or who you don't know
-sama - those who have an elevated status (often nobility, may be the head of a corporation) or who you want to put on a pedestal
-papa/-mama – for people who take care of you and you feel a bond with but aren't your parents
-baka – Usagi's all-time favorite suffix for Mamoru; and if you can use this, think of all the other words you could add . . .
-sensei - added after a teacher's last name; also used for doctors, lawyers, and other highly regarded professions

Careful of the fics I added to the list of favorites. I think one or two have adult content but I added them cuz I absolutely loved the writing style. Also a few don’t start off well but definitely do get a lot better.

I’ll prolly remain a BSSM fanfic author for a while . . . unless I get inspired or something. I doubt I can do sequels on request, so don’t ask. Reviews wanted. Criticism welcomed. Flames will only piss me off.

I’m looking for one or two people who would like to beta my chapters as they come out. So, basically check for plot and character consistency, let me know if something sounds weird, and general comments. Please e-mail me if you are interested.
