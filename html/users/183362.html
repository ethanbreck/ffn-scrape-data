Author has written 2 stories for Sailor Moon, and Naruto.

Hiya, I’m a 30 something that enjoys reading fanfics, manga, fantasy, watching anime. At least those are the things that I do when I’m not at work, or playing with my kid.

Right now the series that I’ve been doing a lot with is Naruto. I’ve been watching what was on CN, and reading the manga. I’m constantly looking at spoilers. sigh And I’ve been watching subs of Shippuden on Crunchy Roll.

My favorite characters tend to be the older ones. Don’t get me wrong though I still love Naruto. I wish I could have a kid just like him. But when it comes right down to it I am in love with Jiraiya, Tsunade, and Kakashi.

The other anime that I love to read/write about is InuYasha. I might eventually do a fic about it, I’ve started one before, but there was just something about it that I couldn’t do it justice.

My favorite part about writing fics is the idea that you might be able to portray some emotion that wasn’t in the anime or manga.
