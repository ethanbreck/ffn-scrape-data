so I leave for a few years and come back, and ff.net is all fancy-like. that's nice. I wish they'd cut out some of the wRitInG liK DiS, but that's unavoidable. and here's me, one foot out of high school and still looking back at the stories of my childhood and laughing, gently, because despite all, they're still good. and I sound like I'm 40, 60 years old and cynical and wise to the world but I'm really not, I'm as naive as I was in grade school pouring my heart out in these writings, but I have learned one thing: that even as you grow older, there will be generations following in your footsteps, pouring their hearts out over the same subjects, with the same overused phrases and with the same underappreciated innocence of youth. and that's nice.
