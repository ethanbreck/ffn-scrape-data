Update: 9-10-19

Happy Birthday Voltron! And me, too!

I've been back from a 10 day road trip to Texas less than 48 hours. Went all the way to South Padre Island, up the coast to Galveston and Houston, and then home. It was an adventure! Understandably I had to stop writing while I was away. And the kiddos at work went back to school while I was gone. Now we're all adjusting to the change in our routine from summer crazy to school order AND I'm neck deep in unpacking and cleaning the camping gear, etc. Plus it's my birthday with all the usual fanfare, so I won't likely get back to writing for a few more days.

I think I've found a beta reader finally! If anyone else is interested, I could always use another opinion!

Update: 8-7-19

I'm not apologizing for another year-long absence. Nope. Stuff happens. (My MMORPG days are long over- at least that type is. No more games that glue me to them 24/7. If I can't skip logging in for the weekend, I'm not interested! But I don't regret doing it once. It was kind of cool being a queen of sorts.)

I've been working on my multi-chapter Voltron story all summer, and I've made great headway! I'm not finished, but I've already begun writing what will be the final sequence. A few more chapters to go, some of which is already written. That said...

I could really use a beta reader. If you've read any of my work, you already know I have a good handle on spelling, grammar, etc. I still make mistakes. I found one in an already published story just today. You're instead of Your. (Insert facepalm emoji here.) What I need is for someone to do a read through of my story and look at pacing, making sure everything makes sense, and other concerns I've made notes of throughout my process. PM me if you're interested!

Update: 6-17-18

So, it's been over a year since updating my profile. 0_o Sorry! A lot of months ago (more than half a year, less than one year) I got sucked into a MMORPG. I'm still playing. I somehow wound up a guild leader. xD It's been quite a ride, let me tell you! I managed to put together a really great group of players and they keep me on my toes. With the exception of one player that decided it would be fun to fake her death. (Yes, that really happens in real life.) It took us three days to first realize things weren't ringing true, and then to prove it was a lie. We did, though. Let me tell you, investigating isn't easy! Somehow all of it will translate into experiences I can use in my writing. When I get around to writing again, that is! When you are immersed in a real time game, you don't really have free time for anything but the game. The writer in me has been getting restless, though, so I'm bound to start opening some Word documents sometime soon.

Sapphire

FY Info: I officially have a completed manuscript draft of the OFG trilogy! It's undergone a lot of work and changes over the years, so I've begun to re-upload my previously published chapters. This will be a long process (I've been known to go years between updates, so take that as you will). I recently read all the reviews my readers have so kindly left me over the years and compiled all the critiques I found in them into a 'To-Do' list, and added some things I wanted changed or improved, too. Thus far, the prologue and chapters 1-28 of Suzaku's Oracle have been updated with the new version. If you were thinking of re-reading Oracles, you might want to read along with the updates. Once I finish replacing all the previously published chapters with updates, I'll move on to posting new chapters. Hang in there! A complete OFG is within sight! *Note I assume that readers have seen the end of FY. If you haven't, we're talking spoilers for the anime and second OAV (First OAV and Eikoden excluded).

JQ Info: I made my very first attempt at fan fiction for JQ: TRA. (Not on FFN, but a fan site no longer in existence.) Please don't flame my JQ work. I am well aware it isn't the best (or even my best). I posted it because I spent so much time working on it that it's a shame to let it sit around on my hard drive collecting e-dust. (Not to mention I'm still writing the AU arc!) So far, I've posted all of "Industry of Intrigue", "Back from the Dead", "Outlaws", "Continental Divide", and "Day of Reckoning". I've finished writing two more installments, "Revelations" and "Saving Sara", which will eventually follow. I'm working on two more installments, and I'm playing with the chronological order of the non-posted installments. This is not my main project, so there is no telling when anything JQ related gets done.

Voltron Info: Voltron and I share a birthday! I've been a fan since 1984. The show stuck with me all these years even though I hadn't seen an episode since it's original run until spring of 2014- nearly 30 years! I think that says something! My Voltron fan fiction is based off the original 1984 series. While I thoroughly enjoyed 2011's Voltron Force (the only other Voltron incarnation I have seen), it didn't get me the way the original did. I mean, come on. Allura is an only child, but Larmina is her niece? How, exactly, does that work? I do want to see Voltron: Legendary Defender, but I am holding off until I finish my current project. I'm afraid I'll love VLD and things from that universe will start to bleed into my DOTU fanfiction, which is what happened after watching VF! (Trivia: My version of Hunk is actually a mishmash of DOTU Hunk and VF Hunk.)
