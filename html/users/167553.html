I am Rachel. I am 26 years old and have been writing on and off on here since I was a teenager. I've just recently came back to this site to start up my creativity again. In addition to writing I also love spending time with my family. I'm married and have two sons. They are my whole life and have definitely changed my view of life. In my spare time when I'm not being a parent, I'm attending university as a student. Between being a full time mom, student, and working part time, it's amazing I have time for much else. But I still always try to find time to read or write some fanfiction!

I like several different fandoms. Mostly I like reading about unlikely romantic pairings and trying to portray them as "in character" as you can. Some of my favorite fandoms include Harry Potter, Queer as Folk, Pirates of the Caribbean, Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, Gravitation, and Lord of the Rings. My favorite things to read are fictions depicting Draco and Harry. That is and forever will be my OTP! Thank you for taking the time to read this! :)
