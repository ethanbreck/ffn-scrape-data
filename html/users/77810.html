Author has written 24 stories for Superman, Beauty and the Beast, Mummy, Twilight, Supernatural, and Arrow.

A little about me. I've been writing fanfic since before this sight was started. I started with notebooks, and at the time Beauty and the Beast was still on TV, After they killed Catherine off, I met a friend who had these wonderful written works by fans, called Fanzines. She had some about Catherine living, and the story went on from there. I began writing not long after that and she used to have two boxes full of stories notebook form.

From there, once Fanfiction.net started up, I had moved on to writing Fanfiction for The Mummy movies, Rick and Evelyn and their life together between The Mummy and The Mummy Returns. Those stories can be found on my Friend's site, Movie Fanfic Chains .Those were some of my early adventures in writing fanfics, so if there are some inconsistencies, that would be my reason which I shall stick too.

I'm not sure if I'll ever get around to branching outside of writing fanfiction. There is something about being able to connect with you the reader that is a really nice part of writing stories. I love people who leave the long reviews because often times I get an idea of how to improve what I'm doing and also when something isn't working. I also like the short reviews so either way feedback is good and if there is something that doesn't seem right or if you're mad at a character let it out. Just be constructive about it, no flames please. Also if it's towards the beginning of a story and a character seems to be floundering a little, it may be where I want them to be. I always try and build on the subsequent chapters.

So there we go. I little bit about how I began in this.
