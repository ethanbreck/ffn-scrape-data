Author has written 2 stories for Lord of the Rings, and Babylon 5.
Mostly I like to read and write commentary fanfiction. This means (usually) gap-fillers, missing scenes and what-ifs, for the purpose of exploring the origin text in greater detail, and making comments on what I liked or what I needed to work through to better understand. If you have any comments, please post!

Currently I'm focusing on JRR Tolkien, I've also written B5 fanfiction; I've been a JRR Tolkien fan 35 years, since I first read Lord of the Rings when I was in my early teens. For lots of good JRRT fanfiction (many authors) see:

In Real Life I am Julie Watkins and I work in a university library; my life has always been full of books.
