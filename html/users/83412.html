I actually don't KNOW what I'm doing here in ff.net. It's not like I'm comfortable enough with words to actually practice the grand art of storytelling. No. These are mere fumblings on the blank screen of the computer, dusting off favorite cliches, copybook exercises. It approaches the state of 'all right, that will do' if caffeine circulates in my veins instead of blood.

Sadly, this isn't one of those times.

The trail of my murdered darlings is short but telling: Harry Potter, Bleach, Hikaru no Go, St Seiya, Yu-Gi-Oh, Tennis no Oujisama, Yami no Matsuei, Katekyo Hitman Reborn, Tokyo Babylon, Naruto, Slam Dunk,and Sherlock Holmes.
