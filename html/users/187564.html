The two sweethearts in the picture are Cat Toy and Mouse Trap, CT is the white one, MT is the black one. They were the first mice that I owned, both of them passed away years ago, but I still love my babies.

INFO ON STORY UPDATESI am currently in college taking both undergrad classes and graduate classes, I work as much as I can to try to avoid getting buried in student loans. Because of this, my updates are very sparatic at best. If you are reading one of my stories and notice that "Hey, this hasnt been updated in two months!" do not assume that I have abandoned it. The CSI fanfic I actually have fully written out, just not typed up. Unless I make an author's not saying otherwise, I have not abandoned the fanfic. I just have a large amount of work to get done in a short period of time, so I cannot devote the amount of time that I would like to my fanfiction. It is still very important to me, and I would like to spend more time writting and post more often. As a reader, I know that nothing is more frustrating than an author who posts their stories very slowly, but I honestly do not have any other choice. Thank you for reading my stories (or at least my bio), and again, I am sorry.

Thank you to everybody who has read anything that I wrote, even if you never reviewed. For those who did review, thank you very much for reviewing. For anyone who said something "mean" to me in your review, thank you for being honest. I'd rather get one review telling me how to improve a story than ten telling me how amazing it is.
