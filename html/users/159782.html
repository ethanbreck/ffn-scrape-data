Author has written 3 stories for Harry Potter, and Fallout.

female 30ish I write mostly apocalyptic stuff or stuff with a massive death rate, plagues and what not. For fanfiction I'm working on Snape oriented stuff and also some Fallout 3 stuff. I have tried some World of warcraft fics, but they never seem to come out.

Don't be afraid to send email just mention fanfiction.net and I will respond. Otherwise I might think that you are spam mail. :)

Ive been of the gothic type for so long that I think more like one then I dress like one. I work too much and now also have a child so pardon any delay on story updates. Time is such a commodity these days that it is just ridiculous.

Stories I am working on and their expected fate:

Pray for Daylight - The first of my fanfics. I need to reread the books, at least the last ones, if I am to continue this one, but I do intend to. I like the flow and the character interaction so do not think that this one will not be updated, though it may be a while until I can.

Dukav's Daughter - This will need a major overhaul before I can start considering it again. I just don't like the way it is coming out yet for some reason so I started a different fic with no in game character connections.

Scanning - The newest of mine and I see some future for it. It can almost be considered a rewriting of Dukav's Daughter, but you will quickly only notice a single similarity. The parts that would have coincided you have never seen and had never before left my head. So now I let them come out for you and leave my head as I need all the memory space my head can allow.

Periodically I get a message saying something about an error 2... well I found online somewhere a while ago... All you needed to do was change the word "properties" in the URL of the story editor to "content."
