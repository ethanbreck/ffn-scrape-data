A bio? Well, all right. If you insist. My name is Victoria, but on here I'm generally called by my pen name and Vicki by most of my friends. I'm 17 years old and live in Georgia with my mom, brother, and step-father. I really love anime. Fruits Basket, Escaflowne, Slayers, InuYasha, Kaleido Star, and Ruroni Kenshin are some of my favorites. I'm a great writer and cook and I especially enjoy learning and philosophy. Right now I'm struggling with juggling all of the factors in my life, which = teenage drama. I apologize to those of you who read my work and have stuck with me for not posting. I've been through so much lately, including losing my father to cancer, moving away from my best friend, having a step father come into my life who is like a room mate who tells you to do various chores everyday, and much more. I'm also pitifully short of my dream in life, which I will most likely not achieve because it depends on an easily swayed human heart. On the bright side, I've got a lot to live for and do. If you have any questions or what not e-mail me at shiiraweigi@yahoo.com !
