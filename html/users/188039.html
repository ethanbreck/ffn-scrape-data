I didn’t make up my penname I got it from a book (kudos to those who know what book.)

Birth date: A warm and sultry night in late spring, the heavy clouds hung ominously low in the blotted-out sky. Lightning flashed, thunder rolled in its deep bass, and the windows shook in fear.

Height: From the floor to my head.

Writing speed: Dependant on my thoughts, mood, and, I hate to admit this, sometimes the weather.

Typing speed: Sufficient for my needs but no one would construe it as fast.

Source of plots (or if you prefer stories): My pet writing bug’s first born child. When my bug went into a comatose state I hade to use something, and the evil plot bunnies, though quite amusing and willing to help in any way they could, seemed to specialize in diabolical schemes not story plots.
