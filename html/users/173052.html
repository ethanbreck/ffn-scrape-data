Author has written 4 stories for Teen Wolf.

Okay, since I've been getting back into writing pretty regularly, I feel like I should actually put something with substance here.

I'm Ryan, twenty-nine years young, and I have an affinity for shiny things and delicious men.

I write because it's what I love. I'm not the most gifted writer ever, but I consider my prose to be strong enough to be worth a read. Most of what I write ends up being terribly depressing, which is odd. Although I do have mild depression, I'm actually quite a cheery person and love happy endings. But my stories gravitate toward being darker for whatever reason. While this may net me fewer views, I actually enjoy breaking away from the norm and writing what I feel I need to write, not what everyone wants written.

I'm a sucker for reviews. They validate me as a writer. So please, let me know how I'm doing. And be constructive, by all means. You don't have to flatter me. If there are parts that need improving, tell me. The only way we grow is by making mistakes and learning from them.

Thank you for reading, and I only hope you enjoy these stories as much I enjoyed writing them.
