Author has written 4 stories for Pirates of the Caribbean, and Mysterious Cities Of Gold.

Who am I? Well, I am a history and social studies teacher. I have a Bachelors degree in History and another in Secondary Education. My specialties are pre-Colombian and colonial Latin America as well as the Classics, particularly ancient Rome. I minored in and can teach Latin, Anthropology, and ESOL. I have not ruled out going on to graduate school, but plan to teach for a while first. As far as hobbies, I would like to say that fan fiction and fan art are hobbies, but I work on them too rarely for that so I call them pursuits. I read a lot, maintain my website, and collect things. I love to travel.

Some of my favorite things include:

Movies - Pirates of the Caribbean, The Lord of the Rings, Star Wars, Phantom of the Opera, Labyrinth, Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea, Treasure Island (Disney's version with Robert Newton), and many more. I love movies!

Books - The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkien, Harry Potter by J.K. Rowling, The King's Fifth by Scott O'Dell, Sherlock Holmes by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Blossom Culp Stories by Richard Peck, the Enola Holmes series by Nancy Springer and too many more to list. I am also a fan of Victorian novels so I really enjoy books like Dracula, Phantom of the Opera, Frankenstein, Les Miserables and almost anything by Jules Verne. I love books even more than movies. :D

Cartoons – The Mysterious Cities of Gold, Avatar: The Last Airbender, Gargoyles, Voltron, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, X-Men (old school), He-Man (I particularly enjoyed the 200x version).

Anime - Fullmetal Alchemist, Slayers, Magic Knight Rayearth, Trigun, Sailor Moon, and DragonBall Z.

TV - Star Wars Rebels, Game of Thrones, BBC Sherlock Holmes (I actually don't watch a lot of TV anymore. Hmm.)

Music - Bastille, Marina and the Diamonds, Of Verona, Fallout Boy, Loreena McKennitt, Breaking Benjamin, Michael Crawford, Evanescence, Enya, Sarah McLachlan, and Dido.

Games – Dragon Age (which has taken over my entire writing life!), Star Wars: The Old Republic, Legend of Zelda, The Sims, Bubble Bobble, Wizardry series (Crusaders of the Dark Savant is my fav)
