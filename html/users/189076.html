Hi and welcome to anyone reading this!

My name is Beki and I'm a twenty-something living in London, UK. I first forayed into the wonderful world of FanFiction as a teenager and re-discovered it about five years ago. I am an avid HGSS shipper and have uncovered some of the best writing I've ever encountered on here. My passions in life are good grammar, chocolate and Alan Rickman and am lucky enough to have a wonderful Mr in my life that accepts my need to listen to Mr Rickmans's incomparable voice :)

I've got extensive plans (and plots!) for my own piece of FanFiction but-alas!- RL has yet to give me enough time to get started although it is my hope to begin publishing again soon. I have written before for other types of FanFic and would describe my style as being very ecclective...no idea what it will be like now though!

Sooo...until I actually have some of my own work published, why not check out some of my favourite stories/ authors on the tabs below? I promise you'll not be disappointed!

Take Care
