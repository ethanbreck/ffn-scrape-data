Greetings. I am Zenaku and welcome to my world.

I supposed I should say a little about myself. I've always had a great imagination and enjoyed writing even as a child. Unfortunately, most of my writings were self-inserts of myself and my relatives, none of which are fit for publication. Hopefully, my writing has improved sense then.

One of the obstacles I have with writing, is forcing my ideas out of my head, and onto the paper. I probably have hundreds of possible storylines in my mind, I just can't bring myself to bring them to life on paper or the computer. Another problem I have, is with planning. While I may have ideas for parts of a story, rarely are they in order, and rarely do I know how I want it to end.

I would just like to say a little bit about my writing, and then I will stop for now.

First, NO YAOI! I am sickened by the amount of good animes out there, ruined by having practically nothing but yaoi fics written about them. There will be no shounen-ai, no mm, no mpreg in any of my fics (Only exeption will be well written omakes and possibly some practical jokes, but none of the male characters will fall in love with each other.)

Second, while I will accept and indeed welcome criticism, I do not tolerate flames. If you don't like what I write, that is fine. You have a right to your own opinion. I write because I enjoy it, and I would like to share that joy. However, if you are going to bitch about me being a sucky writer, go someplace else. If you are going to say something bad about my work, at least give a reason why. If you think it can be improved, tell me how. I may not use it, but I will appreciate it

Well, thats all. Please enjoy
