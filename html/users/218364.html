Story Status:

(1) Four of a Kind: finally UPDATED after 5 1/2 yrs! yay! I've also decided that it WILL NOT be a prequel to All Just a Game. I want to use some different characters for this one.

(2) Changing Seasons: after rereading it, I truly do believe I need to rewrite this story. It will be based more to the actual events of Avatar, but Aang will still be evil. I just need to tweek it up a bit. As for when I'll be able to do this... I really don't know sorry. So I've taken down the story to be edited and put up and a later time. HIATUS

(3) All Just a Game: ahhh... my most successful story so far. Hopefully I'll get some humor back into my writing so I can continue this... but it is sort of hard when you're writing something like Four of a Kind. So, until then, it will be put on HOLD.

(4) The Avatar's Roadside Guide: Coping with Hitchhikers: Dang that title is long! This is my second time uploading this story. I've decided to try it again, but it'll probably be put on the back-burner later if I decide to pick up Four of a Kind (which is the story that currently holds most of my interest).
