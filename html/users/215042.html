Author has written 28 stories for Andromeda, Terminator: Sarah Connor Chronicles, Tron, and Underworld.

There's nothing really interesting about me: I like Sci-Fi in general and few years ago my favorite TV show was Andromeda due to certain AI :-) Lately I've found a special interest in Terminator: Sarah Connor Chronicles, which became my favorite Sci-Fi TV show (and still remains on top of my list!). Later yet, I finally managed to watch Van Helsing, Tron: Legacy and the complete Underworld series and all these movies became my favorite movies. I enjoy reading sappy, fluffy stories with lots of Jameron, happy Selene/Michael, Quorra/Sam and Anna/Van Helsing pairing. (I can't help, I seem to always fall for some advanced mechanical or inhuman beauties ... ROFL). I also like stories about Legend of the Seeker/Sword of Truth. I even watched the ill fated show The Cape, which I noticed exclusively due to Summer Glau. And to "fill the bucket" there're also Firefly fics (again due to Summer Glau). I admit that Underworld and Van Helsing caught my eye because of certain British beauty, going by the name Kate Beckinsale, as well as I noticed Tron due to an American/Irish Olivia Jane Cockburn (commonly known as Olivia Wilde).

Few uninteresting facts about me
Nationality: Slovene
Writing languages: German and English.
Personal achievements in fanfiction area: twice voted "Author of the year" on German Andromeda Board; twice voted "Story of the year" (Snowboarding Andromeda & Android or Human?) on same board - unfortunately the board has been closed for quite a while now.

I've been writing fics before for Andromeda, but after the show ended, also my writing career took a temporary hiatus, which was ended with my increased interest in TSCC. The result: some new stories for this fandom. Tron stories followed more like a 'hiatus' from writing a difficult story for Terminator. Underworld added as the last fandom.

Some of my stories are written in series and should also be read in certain order to make sense.
