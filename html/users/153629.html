Author has written 5 stories for ER, Gilmore Girls, and Cagney and Lacey.

Over the years I have been into so many different shows/movies/books. Among those that gave rise to the most serious of obsessions were (not necessarily in chronological order): M*A*S*H, Remington Steele, Gone with the Wind, Gilmore Girls, Lost, The West Wing, Cagney & Lacey, and Cheers.

Other shows that I love(d): E.R., Mad Men, Boardwalk Empire, Big Love, The Wire, The Mary Tyler Moore Show, Who's The Boss?, Roseanne, 30 Rock, Orange Is the New Black, GLOW, The Walking Dead.

Every now and then I get it into my head that I want to take up writing fan fiction again and then I start and there are probably bits and pieces of half a dozen stories somewhere on my computer. Maybe one of these days I'll muster up the discipline and inspiration to finish and share some of them with all of you.

(My pen name used to be 'waterlilies'n moonlight' until 2013.)
