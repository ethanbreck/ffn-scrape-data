Author has written 10 stories for Harry Potter, Shakespeare, and Avengers.

Previously known as simply Dark Fairy or "that deranged lunatic" (that last one wasn't my pen-name). Avid Harry Potter reader until JK Rowling threw in her lot with the fluffers (needed an eyewash after reading the Wild Shipping Chapter). University student (studying Geology). Socially inept. Considers sarcasm a virtue. Reads Ambrose Bierce and Oscar Wilde. Would love to be a Slytherin. Loves Severus Snape, Sirius Black, Lucius Malfoy, Tom Riddle, Draco Malfoy and any combination of the above. Writes/reads slash. Is altogether weird (this last statement sums up all the others).

I can be suddenly attacked and savaged by plot-bunnies, to the great danger of my readers' sanity (if there is such thing). I hope that if you happen to be reading this, it means that you are bored enough to go and read all my stories too, and review them :hopeful smile: Of my work, I specially reccomend you "Family matters", "Ramblings of Unrequited love" and the one-shots "Complication" and "Run to you". Thanks and good hunt!

ETA: I have abandoned "Family Matters", short of one or two chapters to the end... I know what you think of me because of this, but believe me that the emotional strain of writing all those murders caught up with me. The fic has been finished, is being beta'd, and will be reposted here and at fictionalley.org, in due time. I apologise to all readers left hanging (sometimes literally).
