January 30, 2006: Hard as it may be to believe, I am still alive and I am still writing. College and work and the bazillion things associated with both have not contributed well to any leisure writing.

I'm 22, I recently completed a Web Design & Multimedia degree, and am now working on an English Writing degree. I am a chronic night owl who's addicted to j-rock, techno-industrial music, and Photoshop. I more or less live in front of computers (by trade, education, and hobby), and my ailing PS2 typically goes untouched for long stretches, which it does not like as this was not always the case. My feeble PS can attest to many obscenely lengthy gaming marathons lasting until the wee hours of the morning.

Anybody who's kept up with my writing knows that my updates have an unpleasant tendency to take way too damn long, and I wholly agree with them. They do take way too damn long. Even I don't like that. However, RL is generally uncaring of whether I like it or not. RL consisting of, among other things, school, work, family, and non-writing hobbies. Just know that in some way or another, I'm probably working on an update or...something.

Otherwise, enjoy, and be sure and snoop around. There are plenty of decent fics around here to keep you occupied.

Final Note: Everything original is...well, mine. If you didn't see it in the game, it belongs to me. If you did see it in the game, it doesn't belong to me. If I actually owned characters from a real video game, I certainly wouldn't be here, would I?
