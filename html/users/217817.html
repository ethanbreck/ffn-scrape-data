Author has written 4 stories for Ronin Warriors, and D.Gray-Man.

I am a grammar-fiend and a bit of an angst-whore -- Hey, great emotional tensions make for great reading. My main fandoms include (but are not limited to): A lot of anime series that would take up their own chapter just to write about, Teen Titans, Pirates of the Caribbean, Harry Potter (mostly the Marauders), and lots and lots of fantasy. Someday I hope to make writing my profession, but until then, enjoy my fanfiction. (After that, um, buy my books? Please?) And please, PLEASE comment. I LIVE to hear what other people think about my writing. Flames will be mocked to death on their way to the trash can, but, hey, thanks for taking the time to say SOMETHING.

I feel the need to say this here. Unless implicitly stated, my D. Gray-Man fanfiction (as of 05/20/08) mainly consists of stories for my 50 themes claim. My claim is for my OC Exorcist char Gwen Flail that I made up for a now-defunct DGM role-playing game. I enjoy writing her; she's a fun character. Not your cup of tea? I totally understand, but it is my hope that you'll give mystories a try anyway. Any questions or comment can, of course, be addressed in either PMs or reviews.
