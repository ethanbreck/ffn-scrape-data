I'm a writer.. Mostly I write drabble and one shots, but I'm working on a few longer sorts. With ships, I have a few favourites (Draco pairings have a special place in my heart). If you want to know more about me, points up go read my livejournal. If you want a quickie, here it is. I'm an INFP; I'm as old as I want to be and as young as I act. I am drawn to debauchery and make a hobby of being odd. I've got an addictive personality (in many respects); Harry Potter and fanfiction are two of the few that have lasted. I yearn to go back home to the Greater Toronto Area, and am unable to do so for a long while due to circumstances beyond my control. I'm a Euro-mutt, but when people ask I'll tell them, "I'm Canadian." You won't get more than that. At least not yet. Much love.
