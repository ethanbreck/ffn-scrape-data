31/05/08

Have you ever had one of those sleepless nights where you toss and turn, hoping for sleep to swoop in like a knight in shining armour? Only to realise that there is no prince, but insomnia is courting you.

So you search for ways to relieve the loneliness, and turn to your almost-forgotten ff.net account. You remember all the details, and sadly, once again, your email address is lost to the graveyard of the vast cyberworld.

Hello world. I am alive, I am cognizant, and I am sorry.

I have become one of those horrible writers who don't finish their stories and make lame excuses about artistic integrity and life stumbling into fanfiction world. There is no excuse so frankly, I won't even stretch my limited mental resources to try come up with a half-decent lie.

Perhaps one day, when I don't have university exams, and insomnia swings the ball in my court, I shall finish my incomplete stories.

In the meantime, I bid adieu. May our paths cross again someday.

29/12/04

Before I go on, I would like to wish everyone a (belated) Merry Christmas and an (early) Happy New Year's! Hope this year's winter holiday rocked! Now, on to the grovelling:

No amount of sorries will be sufficient to convey how I feel right now - I am a failure as a writer. A bit harsh, yes, but... true nonetheless. I feel so much guilt for not being able to complete my fanfiction that I will take a moment to wallow in self-despair. takes a moment. Hopefully, I will be up soon to finish off my old fics that really do need finishing off and adding new fics.

I'm just any girl-next-door. Seriously. And I write when I feel like it. So, probably not any old girl-next-door... but I'm a really nice person. I swear!
