Greetings again to anyone who bothers to read profiles. I don't have a beta and I'm not really worried about it. I look forward to posting new works here as well. I hope you do, too.

Now, a little about myself. Hmm, where to start. Basics, basics, I guess. I like purple, hence my pen name. How I can up with 'Purple Mist' I haven't a clue. Purple I can understand, but the Mist part. No idea. I'm pretty much a slash author 'cause it's what I like and what I mostly write, but to go back in time I can remember writing non-slash fanfics of Ramna1/2, Sailor Moon, and Ronin Warriors during study hall in highschool. Ah, memories. Then came along Gundam Wing and I can still remember my first slash fanfic that started it all. A Duo/Quatre pairing found on some website probably long gone 'cause firecity or 'something like that, hosted the page. Goes to show how far back I go as a reader and writer of the slash kingdom. I can still trace my journey of venturing into the world of slash, but that's a tale for another time and full of boring details I won't bother with.

I am a huge manga and anime fan, but non-animes have been making and made their way into my heart. Avatar, Xialong Showdown, Kaapa Micky, Danny Phantom, Buffy and Angel but for only a few seasons because I start to hate the crap that's been written. I do love slash and B.L. but I also love cheesecake. And, yes, I do refer to Hinata as my wife. If Naruto doesn't want her, I'll take her. Female version of Naruto is hot, too. Does my semi-sorta lesbi wants scare you? Booga-booga!

I've also got a twin sister, Shuuichi Minamimo, who posts (or used to) and reads here as well. She's a slasher, too. I love her character Raven, whose an extension of herself, in a way. My sis doesn't really beta my work, she just likes to read it. Claims my stories with Angel are the only one's she'll bother to read because she doesn't really care for the character, but somehow she enjoys him in mine. Mostly 'cause I happen to write an AU Angel or before he becomes a huge ass. Or I make him suffer. Mwa-ha-ha-ha!!

I also enjoy writing crossover fanfics as well and I like to think I do a good job with coming up with hopefully believable crossovers. I've notived it's been Sailor Moon/something else crossover. Like Gundam Wing, Ranma1/2, D.N Angel, Naruto, Inu Yasha, and Digimon. Only a few of those crossovers have been posted. Either here or mediaminer.org or adultfanfiction.net. All works in progress. Sorry. I like to pair Usagi with someone other then that prick Darien. God, I'll stop myself before I start in on my Sailor Moon rant. Yes, your welcome. SM ranting will be save for author notes in my SM fics.

As of late, I really into Hetalia and Fullmetal Alchemist. Dear God, do I love FMA. Sorry, but it's bumped Sailor Moon from my #1 anime love spot as of right now. Hetalia might have been #1 if I had never gotten into FMA. Yeah, I came late to the party, but I don't care. Oh, and Bleach and One Piece is good too. I know, I know, late to the party.

Well, laters.

And laters it is. New fandom loves; BBC's Sherlock. Dear God, do I love Johnlock. I wasn't sure about a modern setting for the series, but I am now hooked. And Teen Wolf. Holy FUCK does that show have some pretty, pretty boys. Just finished Season 1. I have an AO3 account now so I'll probably post my more rate M stuff.
