Author has written 2 stories for Harry Potter, and Doctor Who.

Hello! How are you? I've been a member of this website for at LEAST 10 years. A long time ago, I tried to start a fanfic involving the Harry Potter world. I never finished it -- I'm terrible, I know! I mean...It's not as though it was terribly good (and it was also probably a Mary Sue) but I did get favourable reviews. I just lost interest. I may finish that story later on. It was a Founders of Hogwarts fic, and I know there aren't too many of those floating around.

As far as books go, I like to read fantasy and science fiction, although I'll read anything if it has a favourable review from other people. I also enjoy fan fiction! Right now I am absolutely hooked on Doctor Who fan fiction. I started watching the show with the 9th Doctor although not when it first aired. Me and my husband watched the series from beginning to the last Christmas special that was aired. And of course, we've watched the new episodes as they've aired.

Other interests include pixel art (when I was young, I used to chat on The Palace Chat program, and was an avid pixel artist/cartoon doll maker), soap making, reading other people's zines, and surfing the internet for random things.

I'm currently saving all the random websites I save to my bookmarks on a monthly basis, and plan to make a zine collection after an entire year is done with. I currently have 6 months (or 7 if you count October now) of bookmarks saved, so I'm hoping I continue this to completion.

Oh, also? Well it's a pretty big thing, actually...I love The Rocky Horror Picture Show. So much, that both my husband and I are in a RHPS cast based out of Hobart, IN.
