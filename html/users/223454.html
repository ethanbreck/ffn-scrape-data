Author has written 13 stories for Robotech/Macross, Max Payne, Homeworld, EVE Online, Dragon Age, Bleach, Metal Gear, League of Legends, Contra, and Dota 2.

I'm just one of many fanfiction writers out there. I enjoy writing the more... bizarre... types and have an unfortunate-fortunate interest for female characters just like any other guy out there. I enjoy writing safe for work - but also not safe for work material, as these are quite challenging and fun.

Science fiction as well as fantasy are my favorite topics as these allow one to explore creative ideas while bending the restricting rules of reality, but I'm never limited to these genres. The world out there tends to become narrow-minded and incapable of thinking outside of the box at times - Fanfictions and such on the other hand offer salvation and freedom.

My major focus is set upon a semi-original fanfiction of the award-winning Homeworld(tm) series, but I always enjoy writing for new topics and challenges.

As usual, my life and this profile are an ongoing WIP.
