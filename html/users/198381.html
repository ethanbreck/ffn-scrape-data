I like to read. I especially like to read long fics. The topic of what I like to read changes frequently and I generally go through 'phases'. Returning favourites of mine are 'What Ifs?', survival adventures and darkfics.

I don't review very often. Even on something I really enjoy I'll probably just say "I liked this". However, if someone asks I'll try my best to give a longer review - just please keep in mind I'm really not a writer and I don't have the best understanding of sentence structure or story structure. Writing really isn't a strong point of mine. I much prefer to draw.
