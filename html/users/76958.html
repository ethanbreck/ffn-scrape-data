Author has written 7 stories for Dark Angel.

I'm pretty much all about Marvel these days - Avengers, Agents of SHIELD, Daredevil, and occasionally X-Men. I'd like to write fic to answer all the "What happened in between those scenes or pages?" questions bouncing around in my head, but I don't have the time or ability to think up good answers and explore them well.

My fic-reading habits tend to cycle between "More, more, more!" and "I have to stay away from fic to focus on real life." This explains why I leave reviews in spurts. I do have lots of fics bookmarked for reading later.

I generally prefer canon pairings, though I don't mind reading other pairings if they're done well and don't involve slash or cheating.
