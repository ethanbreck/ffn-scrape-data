Why are you still reading this?

Video Games genre's I prefer are trun-based stratetgy, grand strategy and 4x, over RTS games, not much of a fan of micromanagement. FPS's I don't really care about, barring the tribes series, as I got caught up in the whole earth/starsiege and cyberstorm universe by Dynamaix, and I love the crazy jetpacks.

If you're looking for an obscure strategy game, I reccomend trying Legend of Cao Cao, which plays like Final Fantasy Tactics/Tactics Ogre/Fire Emblem/Vandal Hearts, set in Romance of the Three Kingdoms, with Cao Cao as the Protaganist.
