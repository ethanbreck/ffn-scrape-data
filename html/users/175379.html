Author has written 5 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Dragon Ball Z.

The female known as Fallen Angel or more affectionately to her mates, Krys, began her obsession with anime at the tender age of 9 when watching Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind. Now, 13years later, (she's getting old...)she has decided that immitation is the highest form of flattery and gone out on a limb to write fanfiction. Her personal favourite animes include Cowboy Bebop, Inuyasha, Fullmetal Alchemist, Gundam Seed and Gundam Wingand one day she wishes she had the skill to actually produce manga/anime herself and thus release the torrent of ideas that seem to appear in her head at approximately 3.20 am most mornings (unless, of course, it is the end of exams and she is drunk and snoring with her mouth open. In which case she is lucky if anything is actually passing into her mind, as opposed to out). Her favourite anime character is Faye, who she feels she resembles in personality and wishes she resembled physically.

As a general disclaimer, she would like to state that officially she owns none of the above animes and all writing is for entertainment purposes. Well, hers at least but she hopes someone reads and reviews...someone...anyone?

Her fanfiction is to be consumed at least 3 times a week and preferably with food. Some side effects may occur. Consult your health care professional if symptoms persist.
