July 24, 2007

Hey there people! I originally had not planned to do anything with this account or any of these stories. However, I have been looking at Desert Rose recently. And I would like to say, to anyone whom may actually take the time to read this bio after so long, that I have decided the fate of Desert Rose. I will be rewriting/editing/continuing the story to the best of my ability. In the beginning, way back when I wrote it, I had planned the story out to be a trilogy. This first part, Desert Rose, obviously takes place in Ancient Egypt. From what I remember, part 2 was to be in Present-Day Japan, and part 3 was to be the prequal to part 2 in Ancient Egypt. A new plan is now in order, seeing as I have no idea where the files these other chapters are! But, lucky me, I've been getting ideas.

The new story will be posted on my Kreyana account, and the rewrite on my Livejournal Writing account. All stories will actually be posted on the livejournal account, so feel free to check it out.

Thank you everyone! And Thank you for over 1800 hits on Desert Rose!

The one and only,
