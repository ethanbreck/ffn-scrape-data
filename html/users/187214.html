Author has written 11 stories for Gundam Wing/AC, and Cowboy Bebop.

Hey guys...well, after like three years of nothing, I've decided to start writing again. Big step for me.

Some of my stories have been removed, because I lost my train of thought on those that were unfinished. I've decided to start anew.

My latest fic is anothe Cowboy Bebop fic called "Whiskey Cowboy". Check it out and let me know what you think. I'm also working on another CB fic with a friend of mine, mariamcardoso, called Deja Vu. Click here to check it out.

Cowboy Bebop is one of my favorite animes, and I'm trying to keep everyone in character, although it is dificult. The only pairing I write for that series is Spike and Faye. The other possible combinations seem weird to me.

As far as Gundam Wing goes, I, a former Relena-hater, am now a Relena-putter-upper-wither. I'm beginning to understand more and more where she's coming from in the series. Check out "Dressed to Kill" and tell me what you think! I have no particular pairing preferences among the characters of GW, but am more inclined to write stories with hetero pairings than yaoi. Who knows. I might get back into yaoi.

If you have any requests for GW or CB stories, bring it on! I'll take any pairing at all into consideration!

I'll also be working on my account at fictionpress.com, so check it out if you have some time.

While I realize that I am only just getting back into the world of fanfiction, I would like to point out that I have spent a great deal of time and energy producing each fic that you see here, and I'd like to thank all of you who actually take the time to leave a review.

Those of you who don't review obviously either do not realize how much work I put into my fiction or think you have nothing of value to say, which is untrue - every review counts. I think I speak for all authors when I say that it's easy to read a story, but writing it is a big job, made worthwhile mainly by support and suggestions from the readers. Please review!
