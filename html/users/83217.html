Other stuff about me

I'm not really a good writer of fiction, nor was I ever, and I do much better writing non-fiction. I have a really hard time coming up with ideas, and an even harder time putting it on paper in the form of words. It takes a huge amount of effort to put out what I can, but I always try my best. If I have a multichapter fic, just be patient with the release of subsequent chapters as it may be a very long time before the next chapter comes out. In fact, it's been years since I last updated anything. In fact, the one bit of fanfic I've worked on since my last update was started back in 2005, and I'm still not done. This is mainly because of two things 1) lack of ideas and/or time to write (many a time I've thought of some good material, only to forget it by time I was able to write it down or use the computer), and 2) most of my time online has been devoted to participating in online forums, reading other people's fanfics, and working on other non-fanfic projects.

I currently have a Love Hina fanfiction in the works, with only a couple of pages done. It will be a Keitaro/Motoko pairing, and will likely consist of only one or two chapters. I do plan on continuing my fics "After the End" and "Bonds" sometime in the future, and I may even revise them. As to the latter, I will probably start writing down ideas for it once I watch season 3 of the Tenchi OVA. OVA 3 wasn't even produced yet in Japan when I first published "Bonds," but now that it's out I plan on incorporating elements of it into chapter 3 (which will be the final chapter). As for "After the End", I haven't really been able to come up with any good ideas for the next chapter(s), but once I do, I'll be sure to update the story.

I normally read fanfiction that derives from Evangelion, Tenchi Muyou, Love Hina, Negima, and various CLAMP series, though I'll read stories based on just about any anime I like. Romance is my preferred genre. Favorite genres are drama and romance. I also enjoy shoujo-aifics from quite a few series and shounen-ai fics (mostly) from DNAngel, Gundam Seed, Last Exile, and Kyo Kara Maou, and various CLAMP works. Hey. Shoujo-ai, shounen-ai, het—it's all good, 'cause I'm just a sucker for a good romance fic. Note that I don't normally like fics that have original characters inserted as main characters (unless the story is really good).

Whenever I review fics, I tend to always point out any and all typos, including spelling, punctuation, and grammar errors. I've been an avid reader since I was really little, and I easily notice such things. I'm kind of a perfectionist when it comes to this, and I even go over everything I write on a regular basis to check for errors of any sort. So, if I ever point out anything to an author about spelling, punctuation, grammar, style, and so forth, I'm not harping on you. Just trying to help you make a better fic. It's not everyday that I actually review a fic, so whenever I do, that means I've taken a particular interest in your fic. If you're a native or otherwise fluent English speaker but can barely write the language, I probably won't bother to read your fic, especially if the title and/or description is riddled with errors.

Current favorite anime: Unfortunately, for a couple of years I hadn't had the money to buy much in the way of anime DVDs, but I'm finally starting work on my collection again. I'm currently watching Ergo Proxy, Fafner, FMP: The Second Raid, and Samurai 7, and they're all pretty good so far. I'm also enjoying Bleach and Code Geass on Adult Swim, and Ouran Host Club on Funimation On Demand.

All-time favorite anime: Evangelion, Escaflowne, Rahxephon, Fullmetal Alchemist, Last Exile, Elfen Lied, Voices of a Distant Star, Tenchi Muyou OVA, Ai Yori Aoshi, Kino's Journey, Please Teacher, .hack//SIGN, Cowboy Bebop, Akira, GitS, and many others.

Favorite manga: Negima, just about anything by CLAMP (X/1999, xxxHolic, Tsubasa, & Legal Drug in particular), Angel Sanctuary, DNAngel, and Hellsing (much better than the anime) are among my current favorites. Dragonball, Love Hina, and Lament of the Lamb are my all-time fav manga.

Favorite anime/manga pairings: Too many to list right now. I'll get to this later.
