Author has written 37 stories for Silmarillion.

I'm a long-time reader of Tolkien, with a special passion for the Elder Days. These stories and poems are intended to honor his work, and perhaps even enhance your enjoyment of Tolkien's masterwork - The Silmarillion. I hope that you enjoy them. While you're here, be sure to check out the authors and stories I've listed under my Favorites sections. You won't be disappointed! And when you're done, try out the Silmarillion Writers' Guild Archive, the Henneth Annun Story Archive, and the Tolkien Fan Fiction Archive for even more high-quality Tolkien fanfics.

I have also written a few more Silmarillion fanfics which are not posted here on ff.net for ratings reasons. You can find them, if you are interested, at my Dreamwidth fanfiction journal or my fanfiction LiveJournal.
