Heh, it's been awhile hasn't it?

Many of my stories are going to be discontinued because I have new views and styles of writing. I will mark those stories accordingly. I want to make my opinions on reviews perfectly clear: I am solely interested in perfecting my writing ability. I will not beg for reviews or delete any that you leave me, flames included. If you can help me in my quest by giving me your honest opinion and explaining it then let me thank you in advance. Any questions you ask will be answered via the message sending provided by fanfiction.net and not by chapter. Author notes will be limited only to clarifying the story and giving disclaimers due to the character biases. The opinions expressed by my characters are not necessarily my own. I apologize to those that I had been involved with and who's stories will never be finished due to my lack of interest.

Thanks in advance and it's good to be back.

March 23, 2016: So I'm trying to get back into writing. As for the stories that don't have DISCONTINUED on them I'll try to finish them but from now on I have a rule. I'm not going to publish a story that I haven't completely finished. I'll publish it chapter but chapter but not until the rough draft is complete. It just isn't fair to my readers otherwise. I'm sorry.
