Hi, I'm back. Sorry about that. Anyway, now that I'm here, I may add some stories -- or add to some stories. Fun, fun. This was always my favorite penname anyway. It is a remodeled version of Paysheri Alleme.

Here's a short history:

My name is Marianna.

Some people call me Grace, Iluv, Lluv, Oy, Maria, Ria, Barf Girl, Marianne, Angie, Mari, Pepsi Girl, Gabby or Nyx.

I am thirteen years old and in the eighth grade. School can be good. School is mostly bad.

I am very vocal about certain subjects when they mean a lot to me (well, duh).

My life is better than for what I give it credit... Sometimes. Other times, I'm pretty OK with acknowledgments.

I have always wanted to be a writer when I grow up, or sooner, but I have frequent bouts of self doubt. I'm still improving on myself and my art. I should accept that and appreciate what I've accomplished so far. There's always more to come. No one ever stops learning. And if I repeat that to myself a thousand times, I may begin to believe it.

My diary is more of a bio, so I'd suggest going there. To paraphrase, I'm really into romantic crap, living life to its fullest, seeing places (even if it's just down the street or something), art and finding balances between self consciousness and confidence. But my literary and musical interests are all in there, so... Are ya' interested?

One day I woke up and realized that I had friends. Not only do I know them very well but I can relate to them. Even though we trip over ourselves sometimes, we think and we're genuine... I think, heh. They are very cool people. I'm lucky to have them in my life. Talk about brushes with insanity, too...

"He lives inside of me,
He smokes his cigarette in me,
He is my G-d, he is me,
As a man inside of me."
-- Gabby, A Short Poem About Mookie

Also, send shoutouts to the Punky Monkeys, kids from BCreek, Christian's hat, Usdaners, CTYers, people I've met on boards and ff.net (whether we talked often or we gave each other a few friendly reviews). I may be forgetting people, so this is sort of dangerous, but I have all this beautiful space...
