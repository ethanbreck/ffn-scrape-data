Author has written 12 stories for Ranma, Yu Yu Hakusho, My Little Pony, Digimon, Pokémon, Fire Emblem, and Undertale.

I am Yuki Ryu. I am an avid writer and artist whose poor health causes sporadic updates. I enjoy romance, comedy, drama, and lovely readers who leave insightful reviews like you. Please enjoy my stories.

Below the news break is a comprehensive listing of the stories co-written by Mouko (http://www.fanfiction.net/u/45935/) and me. Please pardon the messy links, as they are left visible due to this site sometimes forbidding direct links in profiles.

Series

These are stories that exist in a unified continuity. They will be listed here in chronological order.

Dreaming To Reality (Digimon) - The path to discover the difference between dreams verses reality and Chosen verses Tamers.

Dreaming To Reality: Fragments (https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11578251/1/) - Before joining the Odaiba group, Ken Ichijouji traveled the Digital World with another group of Chosen Children.

Dreaming To Reality: Oneiromancy (https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12911141/1/) - Immediately following the events of 02 Remix, the war continues on.

Dreaming To Reality: Snapshots (https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11435436/1/) - A compilation of short stories of small events that happen between the pages of the main series.

Keterburg (Tales of the Abyss) - Foreboding yet wonderful, tragic yet golden - the bond of the four children who grew up in the silver village Keterburg is too powerful to be broken.

Dedicated (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3836788/1/) - Jade, Nephry, snowball fights, and the bond of the Balfour siblings.

Anxiety (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3910098/1/) - The first day at Professor Nebilim's school and the first time Jade and Nephry encountered Saphir.

Curiosity (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3965531/1/) - The beginning of the most infamous friendship in Tales of the Abyss.

Unwanted (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/5360918/1/) - An introspective on Jade and his actions.

Warm (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/3597611/1/) - A moment during the "Golden Era" of Dist's childhood.

Pokémon Remix (Pokémon) - There are many tales of Pokémon, in worlds that vary greatly. This is a series of tales from a world with things found in other worlds with a hint of aspects of our own, mixed together into its own unique universe. Here, anything can happen, and things, at time, can be a bit darker when reality sets in.

Pokémon Remix: Pay Day (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/8673473/1/) - It's rarely a wise decision to cross Team Rocket.

Pokémon Remix: Shiny Hunt (https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9759995/1/) - Everyone wants a rare Pokémon, and even relatives to the illustrious Professor Okido are not immune to greed.
