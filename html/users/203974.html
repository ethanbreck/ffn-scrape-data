Okay, I'm keel hauling my profile, since it's old and out dated. It's been basically the same for nearly three years now, so I have decided to trash it and start again.

I've been writing on this site since I was old enough to register, and quite possibly slightly before I was old enough to register, which means that my love affair with fanfiction.net, and fanfiction in general has lasted a grand total of 7 years!! I've gone through a lot of usernames over the years too, since my tastes etc. have changed too. I've been SilverMilleniumPrincess, Golden Darkness, Crazy Freaked Out Lollipop and Loveless Soul. Loveless Soul in particular has served me well, but now I've decided to move on. I hope that you will not lose me in my most recent name change, as I love all my reviewers and fans and wish to keep you all entertained with my fanfiction for as long as possible!

I've written a lot of stories in my time here, and it's pretty scary to see how different and more polished my writing has become over the years! Some of my older stuff will never be worked on again, and it's likely that I will be removing them slowly from my profile. I was only thirteen or fourteen when I wrote them, and since I am now nearing twenty one, I feel its time to let them go gracefully. I am grateful for all the writing knowledge my early works gave me, but it's time for them to rest in peace. All my completed works are staying up though, like my one shots etc.

Okay, so the stories I intend to continue working on are:

Forest of Faeries - my current Harry/Draco foray over in the Harry Potter section. I am really excited about this one, since I've currently got a small (aka HUGE) obsession with faeries and all things fey!

The Seer's Endeavor - my as yet unposted sequel to Siren's Song, set directly after the first story finished, I'm currently working on chapter one, with hopes to post it soon. I still have a few plot kinks to work out in the middle section though. Wish me luck!

Of Darkess and Light - my newly posted sequel to my Harry/Lucius Starting Over!! I suddenly realised what a brilliant sequel I posessed in their son James, and have decided to go ahead with it. I still need to complete some more research, so that I know exacly where it's going in later chapters, since it appears to be getting more and more complicated plot wise, but it is well on its way to being my favourite idea yet! Please keep an eye out! It's also a semi-crossover with Melissa Marr's Wicked Lovely, since I'm borrowing her characters and some tiny snippets of her plot line. It's a really good book by the way!

Learning to Live - my Ritsu/OC story over in the Fruits Basket section! I really love this story, and am looking forward to posting more chapters!

Understanding Riki - my Ai no Kusabi/Taming Riki fic, which I completely adore, but am having a little trouble with right now. Writer's block is not a pleasent thing, is it? I hope that you will all be patient with me, I will get there eventually.

Of Life and Love - This is a Fullmetal Alchemist fic that I think requires one more chapter. Its not a high priority, but it will happen eventually.

Little Devil - another Harry/Draco that was originally a one shot, but I plan to put up a second chapter dedicated to Blaise and Neville. It will take a while, since this isn't really a priority either.

Light of Hope - this is an original that is posted both on fictionpress.com and adultfanfiction.net, and I hope you'll all check out my venture into original fiction! my username is Spring Witch at Adult Fanfiction, and Loveless Soul at Fiction Press.

I'm not planning to delete all of my older stories, I plan to keep a hold of some of them, because I love them too much to let them go. I'm definately planning to keep Not So Different, Remembering the Past, The Dark Witch Sagas and Artemis Fowl: Together at Last up there, since I'm thinking that I might like to edit and repost them to my current level of work. These are some of my favourites and I think if I fixed them they could be really awesome! I hope you'll all keep an eye out. Unfortunately I probably won't be fixing these until I have finished up all the others in the list.

I'm also now starting up a LiveJournal under the username whyte_tigress88. I plan on posting update announcements etc. there, and I hope you'll all subscribe to my blog! Lets all be friends! :D I also write for lunch.com under the name White Tigress. Its a site where you can leave reviews for anything you want, and I'd love to see you all get out there and sign up, since I know that many of my reviewers are also great writers themselve! I was lucky, and got invited to write for them not long after the site launched about a month ago, so go and sign up, its an awesome site!!

All right, I think that's all from me for now! I look forward to reading your reviews in the future!!
