I'm glad to say I'm not the only one guilty of not updating their profile nearly often enough. Although 5+ years may be pushing it on my part.

I admit, I'm not so wonderful a writer but I'm an avid reader and editor. My dream has always been to help people put their stories out into the world, not necessarily create them myself. Doubt that'll ever happen as a career unfortunately.

I'll attemp to write again sooner or later, if only to see how far I've come from the raw teenaged writer I was when I sprung up on FFN. Can you pick out the words to be emphasized in the last sentence?

Cheers! You might check out my favorite stories and authors though. I'm finally putting the damned features to use now and keeping track of what pleased me the most. I only wish we had the opportunity to comment on those favorites; why we like them and so on.
