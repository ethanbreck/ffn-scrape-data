Author Bio:

I am a Navy wife and use my BA in English Lit and MA in Mass Communication to teach ballet at the YMCA and take care of my kiddo, Gabriel. All that schooling was so useful for those things, wasn't it?

Music: Muse, Led Zeppelin, The Black Keys, Jack White (in all his forms), John Butler Trio, Radiohead, Rage Against the Machine, Incubus, Solas, The Medieval Baebes, Bach, Vivaldi, Flogging Molly, Nickel Creek (including everything Chris Thile, Sarah Watkins, and Sean Watkins have done on their own)...

Books: Epic poetry (such as Edmund Spenser and Homer), T.S. Elliot, J.R.R. Tolkien, C.S. Lewis, Brent Weeks, Kristen Britain, Stephen Lawhead, Patrick Rothfuss

I've dabbled in languages and music my whole life, learning a smattering of Latin, Greek, French, and Spanish; a decent chunk of German; and piano, guitar, and flute (gag, flute, I know, my mom forced me as a preteen). Next language on my list-of-things-to-do-before-I-die: Gaelic.

Um, favorite color: Green

Favorite message-board-signature-tag: I guess surrealism isn't your cup of tuna.

Fact: I'm too old to be obsessed with fanfiction, I know. But I like watching baby authors (myself included) mature their styles.
