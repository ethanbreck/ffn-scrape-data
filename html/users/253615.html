A Little Bit About Me!

I started reading Fanfiction when my friend introduceed it to me. It was after a fight we had and it helped to ease us back into a friendship. Even though we are no longer friends I still love Fanfiction. I am a Trory and JavaJunkie. I only write my stories as such.

If I had to pick a character I am most like I would say Rory. I know you are all thinking I must be loopy. However here are the facts: I Love Coffee. I Love to read. I always have and always will.The classics are a favorite of mine and while I have read and own all but one Jane Austen bookmy favorite is Jane Eyre by Charlotte Bronte.I also bring a book with me almost where ever I go. Finding a purse big enough can be hard. I am also very driven when it comes to school.My mom is crazy much like Lorelai. We are friends. And she loves the snow. She once woke me up at 6 in the moring to go eat breakfast in a town about an hour away just because she heard it was snowing.And last but not least and most important for all you trory fans,I had a Tristan of sorts. No he didn't call me Mary. He also may not have liked me, I didn't like him either. But boy could we banter. Anyway there you go, probably waaaaaaaaay more info then you would ever want, but hey what can I say I love to share.

On another note I have Paris tendenices. Only when I work in a group though. We had a project in English kind of like their Romeo and Juliet thing. Except it was Macbeth. Anyway I was picked as the director. Boy did I take it seriously. In fact I could have given Paris a run for her money. I did get an A though. My group was horrible though so whatever. Ok so thats plenty for you to know.
