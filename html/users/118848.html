Okay, so here's the situation:

I really am sorry for all my readers and reviewers out there, but after long, long thought I've finally decided that my stories are pretty much going to be permanently unfinished. I've sort of lost interest (my own fault) and to tell you all the truth, my life has been so unbelievably busy and hectic that writing is so difficult for me right now. I'm hoping to maybe post a few short stories, but these would be post-OotP stories if I get around to it. As I'm sure at least some of you can understand, writing about L&J as friends first just isn't fun now that we know James was a 'bullying toerag' to use Lily's words. So I'm really very sorry for those of you who've waited for updates, but I just feel that my writing style has changed, my interests have changed, and that there are so many better stories out there for you all to read that have been updated. Like I said, I have hopes that one day I can get back into the updating-mode, but as of now, I need to concentrate on more important things. Namely school, unfortunately, but come summer- who knows? Thanks again to all you readers and reviewers, it was a lot of fun reading all your reactions and I hope if I post again at least some of you will give me another chance! In the meantime, if you need a good read, check out my favorite stories and favorite authors' stories, they're all fabulous and have my full recommendations!

Happy reading wherever you may venture,
Britz
