I sincerely apologize to all of the readers (few though they may be) that have read my older stories. Lack of time and enthusiasm for my older stories has prolonged their continuation.

I also fear that, basically, the majority of the readers who have taken the time to read my older stories have lost any spark they might have held for them. I hate it when I lose a spark for a good story because the author failed to post, and I'd hate it even more if I caused someone else to lose his or her spark for one of my own stories. So, regardless if my older stories are fabulous from this day forward, everyone who read them and liked them will no longer hold the same "spark" for the plot and the story itself.

I apologize profusely again, dear readers.
